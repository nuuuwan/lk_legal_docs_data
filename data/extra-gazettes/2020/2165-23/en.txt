THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: uy$b$w;a /CEB/385.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the Land Acquision
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a Public
Purpose:
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
The land described below, situated in the village of
Gangodawila (within the Municipal Council Limits of
Maharagama) in the Grama Niladhari Division of 526/B
Gangodawila South, Divisional Secretary's Division of
Maharagama in the District of Colombo, Western Province
and depicted in the PPC 9875 prepared by the Surveyor
General and contains in Extent 0.1161 Hectares.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Millagaha Kumbura alias
Millagahadeniya; Present land use:Barren land and
abandoned paddy field; Claimant: State;Original Claimant:
U.D. Janaka Priyantha, No. 95, Old Kesbewa Road,
Gangodawila, Nugegoda;Extent: 0.1161 Hectares.
All persons interested, in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretary Office
Maharagama on 28th
  March, 2020  at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify me in writing  (in duplicate) on or before
the 20th March, 2020 the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount. You
should Present relevant deeds and Extracts thereon and other
Documents to prove the ownership to the land on the Inquary
date.
                              M.M.K.D
ILRUKSHI WALPOLA,
                            Divisional Secretary,and Acquiring officer
Maharagama.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
