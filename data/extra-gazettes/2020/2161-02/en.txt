THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No.: KDS/HAR/4/2/1/41.
Land  Ministry's  Reference No.: 4-3/09/2015/WS$16.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquistion Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following lot of land for the
construction of the entrance road to the new service reservoir
of Madawala Service Station of the Water Supply Scheme
for Kandy and adjacent areas.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
The Lots No. 01 and 02 with an extent of 0.0075 Hectare.
depicted in the basic plan No. 6958,  surveyed and prepared
by the Chief Surveyor Suprintendent of Kandy District,
situated in Medawala Village in Kulugammana Siyapattuwa
Minor Division in the Harispattuwa Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kandy District in the Central Province.
1. Lot No. 01 & 02
Name of the Land: Galpadigawa Watte; Owner of the
Land: Mr.A. B. G . Nevil Priyantha Bulukumbura, No. 212,
Angoda, Medawala; Extent: 0.0075 Hectare .
All persons interested, having rights or possessions in
the aforesaid land are required to appear before me personally ,
or by agent duly authorized in writing, at Divisional Secretariat
Harispattuwa, on 01st April 2020   at 10.00 a.m. They are hereby
requested to notify  me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 01st April 2020 the nature of their interests in the land and
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the aforesaid land, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
MOHAN D HARMADASA,
Divisional Secretary,
Harispattuwa.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Harispattuwa, Nugawela,
30th  January , 2020.
