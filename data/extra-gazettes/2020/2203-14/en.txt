The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    WATTEGAMA    URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Wattegama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Dunusinghe
Mudiyansele Gedara Ranaweera Banda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that No. 09 Pitiyegedara of Wattegama Urban Council  who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Yaddehi Gedara Nandana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer  of  the Wattegama
Urban Council , acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Yaddehi Gedara Nandana was elected as the member of  the  No. 09 of Pitiyegedara of  Wattegama Urban Council.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Wattegama Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
23rd  November, 2020.
