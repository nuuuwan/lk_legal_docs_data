 2ND   AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Animal Production and Health Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Animal Production and Health Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1840/51 dated 13th of December 2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2015/4 dated 17th of April 2017 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is
amended as follows and these amended provisions will be made effective from 26th of February 2019, the date on
which the Cabinet of Ministers made the policy decision.
                                                           By Order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. D
AYA SENARATH ,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
13th December 2019.
