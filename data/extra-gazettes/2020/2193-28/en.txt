FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kalmunai Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the National Front of Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Thajutheen Mohammathu Mufaris, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of the National Front of Good Governance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Noorul Ameen Muhammathu Asam to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of   Kalmunai Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Noorul
Ameen Muhammathu Asam was selected as the member of  the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
15th  September, 2020.
