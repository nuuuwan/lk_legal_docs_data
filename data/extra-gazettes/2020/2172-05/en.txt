Order No. 63
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no importer, producer, distributor, or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display for
sale the item listed below, above the Maximum Retail Price given here under :-
Item Maximum Retail Price
Turmeric Powder Rs. 750/- per 1 kg
This Order shall come into effect from 21st April, 2020.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
21st April, 2020.
