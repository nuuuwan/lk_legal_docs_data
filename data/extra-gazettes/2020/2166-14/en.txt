 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A  of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
the Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, having obtained a report under Sectioin 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the Institute of Chemistry, Ceylon, established under the Institute of Chemisty, Ceylon, Act, No. 15 of
1972, do by this Order and subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the Institute of Chemistry,
Ceylon as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the
Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in
Column II of the Schedule II hereto :-
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
1. Institute of Chemistry, Ceylon shall conduct the degree programmes relevant to the Degree specified in Schedule
II hereto, only at Adamantane House, Kotte Road, Welikada, Rajagiriya.
2. Institute of  Chemistry, Ceylon is allowed to offer the Degree specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to the
programme re-review to be conducted by the Specified Authority.
