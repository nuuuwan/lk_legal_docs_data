FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAHARAGAMA URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return a member of  Maharagama  Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of death;
AND  WHEREAS  the secretary  of the  United  National  Party, the  recognized  political party  to  which  Ranhalu  Hewage
Chandrasiri  the  member  belonged,  was  required  by  me under Section 66A (1)   of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hettiya Meregngna Ayesha Nelum Renuka Senarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the Maharagama Urban
Council acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Hettiya Meregngna Ayesha Nelum Renuka Senarathna was selected as the member of the Maharagama Urban Council.
S. D. S. A . Rupasinghe,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
3rd  February, 2020.
