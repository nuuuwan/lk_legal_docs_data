THE SOCIETIES ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksha, 
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do by this Notiﬁ cation;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “WANA  ARANA  FOUNDATION” situated at No. 16/3,
Kesbewa Road, Boralesgamuwa as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be
extended; and
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid Society by exempting it from the provision of Section 5(4) of
that Ordinance.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSHA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, 
Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
The Secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01.
