Greetings !
As you have been appointed as members of the Presidential Task Force to build a Secure Country, Disciplined,
Virtuous and Lawful Society, established by the Presidential Directive dated 02nd June, 2020 by exercising the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
And whereas, the necessity of representation of the Department of Prisons has been recognized in addition to
the above members in view of  the scope and duties of the Task Force.
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers
vested in me by  Article 33 of the Constitution of Sri Lanka, appoint the Commissioner General of Prisons, H. M. T. N.
Upuldeniya Esquire as a member of the Presidential Task Force to build a Secure Country, Disciplined. Virtuous and Lawful
Society, which had established in terms of the aforesaid Presidential Directive, reposing great trust and confidence in his
prudence, ability and fidelity.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 30th day of June Two
Thousand and Twenty.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P.  B.  J
AYASUNDERA,
 Secretary to the President.
