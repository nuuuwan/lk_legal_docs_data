DECLARING   THE   EXTENT   OF   SACRED   LAND    BELONGING   TO   THE   HISTORICAL
OLABODUWA    SRI   JAYAWARDHANARAMA   RAJAMAHA    VIHARAYA   AS   A   SACRED   AREA
Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2153/12 on Tuesday 10 th December 2019
IN accordance to the announcement made by HIS EXCELLENCY GOTABHAYA RAJAPAKSA, President of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka by Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2153/12 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka. published on Tuesday 10 th December 2019 and in accordance with the subject of Sacred Areas Conservation and
Development assigned to me as Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs do herby
declare the land specifically mentioned in the Schedule herein as a Sacred Area for longevity conservation and the said
Area could be known as the “ Historical Olaboduwa  Sri Jayawardhanarama Rajamaha Viharaya Sacred Area”.
M
AHINDA  R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs.
On the 04th day of May 2020,
Ministry of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs
No. 135, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
