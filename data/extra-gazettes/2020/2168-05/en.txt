Greetings!
Whereas, I, in the exercise of powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, have established a Task Force for Poverty eradication and Livelihood Development by the Presidential
Directive dated 24
th January, 2020 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2159/64 of January 25, 2020.
And whereas, the aforesaid Task Force must provide financial, education, health, food, sanitation and other infra-
structure facilities within a short period of time, the basic needs that must be met to improve the living conditions of the rural
and low income people.
And whereas, it is identified that necessity of access to the general public by its activities to achieved the
desired objectives from the Task Force,
And, it is expected to complete the tasks of this Task Force within a year.
Now, therefore, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby
appoint S.B. Divarathne Esquire, Retired Deputy Secretary to the Treasury and Sumedha Perera Esquire, Retired Major
General, as the Co-Chairmen of the Task Force for Poverty Eradication and Livelihood Development, as established by the
said Presidential Directive.
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this  24
th day of March, Two
thousand and Twenty.
By order of His Excellency,
P. B. JAYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
