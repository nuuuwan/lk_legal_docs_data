FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RAJGAMA     PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Koralage Nirmalee
De Silva,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kahingalage  Chandrawathee to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kahingalage  Chandrawathee
was selected as the member of  the  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
14th  December, 2020.
