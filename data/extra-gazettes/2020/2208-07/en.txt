and
Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 500, T.B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10. of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
1. Whether unjust has been caused to qualified employees of the Mahaweli Authority by recruitment of employees from
outside the extablishment in the recruitment/ filling of vacancies of Security Officers, and if so, to what relief the are
entitled.
2. Whether of the Officers in Grade I of the Security Guards service have been caused unjust by not being promoted and
having to retire in their earlier posts as a result, and if so  to what relief they are entitled.
3. Whether the qualified Security  Officers have been caused unjust by others  being promoted, violating  the accepted
promotion procedures, and if so, to what relief the qualified Officers  are entitled.
4. Whether the qualified Security Officers of Grade 1 who have retired from service, have been caused unjust by not
been promoted their retirement and if so, to what relief  they are entitled.
