Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance

DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT - NORTHERN   PROVINCE
Notice for meeting under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue of powers vested to me
under Sub Section No. 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, hereby notify to convene the
meeting for the election to fill the vacant of Chairman of Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha in Northern Province on
2020.12.16 at 10.30 am at Council Hall of the Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha.
M
ARIYATHAS PATRICK DIRAJAN,

Northern Province.
