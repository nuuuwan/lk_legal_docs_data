Greetings!
As you have been appointed as members of Presidential Task Force for build a Secure Country, Disciplined,
Virtuous and Lawful Society established by the Presidential Directive dated 02
nd June, 2020 by exercising the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka,
And, as a new Commander of the Navy has been appointed following the retirement of the former Commander of
the Navy, Admiral Piyal de Silva, who was a member of the aforesaid Task Force,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of Sri Lanka, appoint new Commander of Sri Lanka Navy Vice Admiral
Nishantha Ulugethenna as a member of the Presidential Task Force for build a Secure Country, Disciplined, Virtuous and
Lawful Society, for former Commander of the Navy,  reposing great trust and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka on the 19th day of August Two
thousand and Twenty.
By His Excellency’s  Command,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
  Secretary to the President.
