Greetings!
As  You  have  been  appointed  as  members  of  the  Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management in the
Eastern Province established by the Presidential of Directive dated 01st June, 2020 by exercising the powers vested in me by
Article 33 of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And whereas, the necessity of representation, of the Civil Security Department has been recognized in addition to the
above members, in view of the scope and duties of the said Task Force,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa,  President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of Sri Lanka, appoint the Director General of  Civil Security Department,  Rear
Admiral  Ananda  Peiris Esquire, as a member of the Presidential Task Force Archaeological Heritage Management in the
Eastern Province, established in terms of the aforesaid Presidential Directive, reposing great trust and confidence in  his
prudence, ability and fidelity.
Given  at Colombo under the Seal of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 30
th day of June Two
Thousand and Twenty.
By  His  Excellency’s  Command,
P.  B.  JAYASUNDERA,
 Secretary to the President.
