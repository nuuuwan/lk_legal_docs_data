NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A  (1) ( a) OF THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kalutara Urban Council
I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kalutara Urban Council do hereby declare
under Section 10A (1) (a) of  the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Mohamad Firaus Mohamad
Faslan of the Kalutara Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that Urban Council as he has ceased to be a member
of the United National Party.
