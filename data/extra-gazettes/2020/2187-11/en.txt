THE  ANNUAL  FEAST  OF  OUR  LADY’S   RAGAMA    LANKA    BASILLICA    CHURCH    2020
Gampaha District
IT is announced for the general public to inform that The Annual Feast of the Ragama Thewatta Lanka Basillica Church 2020
(Feast of the Patients) will be held on 30th August in 2020 and from 22.08.2020 to 31.08.2020 as the duration of the Annual
Feast.
1. This will be brought to the attention of the general public on the regulations published in Gazette No. 1953/43
dated 12th February, 2016 under the Pilgrimages Ordinance.
2. Arrangements have been made to hold this year’s festival with the participation of a limited number of people in
accordance with the quarantine laws on the instructions of the health sector under the program to control the
spread of Covid 19 epidemic disease in the country.
3. These publicity issued laws will be valid during the period of this ceremony.
S
UNIL JAYALATH,
District Secretary/Government Agent,
Gampaha.
