PART IV (A) - PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  
 
 
Provincial Councils Notifications 
 
WESTERN PROVINCIAL COUNCIL  
 
The Inquiry being conducted under Section 184(1A)(a) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of 
the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 
 
 
 
I, Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of the Western Province do hereby announce that the period of in vestigation of 
single person Inquiry Board comprise of retired Judge of the Court of Appeal Hon. Chandradasa Nanayakkara, appointed 
by Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.  2152/14 dated 03rd December, 2019 to 
inquiry whether the offences stipulated under sub -section 184(1) of the aforesaid Urban Councils Ordinance have been 
committed by Wattala-Mabola Urban Council and by the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council while functioning 
in his office and submit a report within 03 months shall be extended until 03rd December, 2020 accor ding to the powers 
vested in me in terms of Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of the Provincial                 
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
