PART III - LANDS  
 
Title Registration 
 
REGISTRATION  OF  TITLE ACT, No.  21 OF  1998  
 
Declaration  of  Determination  of  the  Commissioner  of  Title  Settlement  under  Section  14 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 14 of the Registration of Title Act, No.21 of 1998, I, the undersigned, hereby de-
clare my determination as set out in the Schedule appended hereto in regard to the  title to parcel of Land Nos. 10, 15, 16, 19, 26, 32, 33, 
37, 51, 53, 58, 106 and 109 of Block 01, contained in the Cadastral Map No. 830061, situated in the Village of Saddhatissapura New 
Town within the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 76 - Saddhatissapura New Town in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Lunugam-
wehera, in the District of Hambantota, in the Province of Southern, referred to in Notice No. 83/0316 calling for claims to land parcels 
which was duly published in the Gazette No. 2134/07 of 30th July, 2019 in terms of Section 12 of the Registration of Title Act, No.21 
of 1998.
