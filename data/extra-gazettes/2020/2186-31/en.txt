Regulations 
 
The first paragraph of 5(I) in part II of the Regulations No. 01 of 2016 of the National Association of Sports published in 
the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th October 2016 which was amended by the Regulations of the National 
Association of Sports published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2006/13 dated 13th February 2017, the Regulations of 
the National Association of Sports published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2137/85 dated 23rd August 2019, the 
Regulations of the National Association of Sports published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2166/9 dated 10th March 
2020 and the Regulations of the National Association of Sports published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2177/8 
dated 27th May 2020 is amended by inserting the new section as follows: 
 
5(1). Every National Association of Sports should appoint a Committee made up of a number of office bearers specified 
in their constitution including the below-mentioned office bearers as well. The members appointed for the said executive 
Committee should be with at least twenty percent (20%) of female representation, and twenty percent (20%) of youth 
representation. (Youth representation herein means the persons over 18 years and under 40 years)  
 
This regulation is effective from 01.01.2021.
