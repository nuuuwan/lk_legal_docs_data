SRI   LANKA  ELECTRICITY ACT,  No. 20  OF  2009
Order under Section 52
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 52 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009 and upon the
recommendation made by the Minister assigned the subject of Power, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, being of the opinion that -
(a) the land specified in the Schedule hereto is urgently required for the construction of a hydropower plant as a
power generation project; and
(b) the said power generation project being a public purpose would serve for the general welfare and benefit of
the public,
            do by this Order declare that the said land is required for the above purpose.
G
OTABAYA  RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
17th September, 2020.
