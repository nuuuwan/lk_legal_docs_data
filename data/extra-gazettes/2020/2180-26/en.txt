THE   MOTOR   TRAFFIC    ACT   (CHAPTER   203)
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Transport Services Management under Section 237 read with Section 126 of the
Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203).
MAHINDA  AMARAWEERA,
Minister of  Transport Services Management.
Colombo,
16th June,   2020.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving
License) Regulations No. 03 of 2020.
2. Validity period of Every Driving License, issued by the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic under the Motor
Traffic Act,
(a) which expires within the period of 16th March, 2020 to 30th June, 2020 shall be extended by six months
period from the expiry date of such Driving License,
(b) which expires within the period of 1st July, 2020 to 30th September 2020, shall be extended by three months
period from the expiry date of such Driving License.
