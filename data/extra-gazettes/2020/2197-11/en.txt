No. 2197/11 –  TUESDAY, OCTOBER  13,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF  
1964 (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
        Land Ministry’s Reference  No.: 4-3/11/2015/TR/85. 
             My Ref. No.: KIDS/LND/01/ACQ/6. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose:  
 
Public purpose: - For the Construction of Kiriella Vidatha 
Resource Center. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired : 
 
The  allotment of land depicted as Lots 742, 743, 744 
in sheet No. 36 supplement No. 39 of F. V. P. 182 
prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys Rathnapura 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Idangoda in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kiriella of 
Kuruwita Korale Uthuru Uda Paththuwa in Rathnapura 
District of the   Sabaragamuwa Province and morefully 
described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 742 
Name of the land: Idangoda Watta;  Claimant: 
Balangoda Plantation Company Ltd, Land Reform 
Commission, State Plantation Corporation of Sri Lanka; 
Extent: 0.0026 Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 743 
Name of the land: Idangoda Watta;  Claimant: 
Balangoda Plantation Company Land Reform 
Commission, State Plantation Corporation of Sri Lanka; 
Extent: 0.1497 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 744 
Name of the land: Idangoda Watta;  Claimant: 
Balangoda Plantation Company Land Reform 
Commission, State Plantation Corporation of Sri Lanka; 
Extent: 0.0050 Hectare. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Kiriella   
Divisional Secretariat, on 17.11.2020 and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 10.11.2020 the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.  
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 
   
 J. L. C. KEERTHI  JAYASINGHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kiriella. 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Kiriella, 
05th October, 2020.
