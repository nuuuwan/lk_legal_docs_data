EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under  Section 07 
 
 
My No.  : 6/3/7/15/8/2. 
 Land Ministry's Ref. No. : LD/05/2007/MV/155. 
 
I, hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that               
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for Moragahakanda & Kaluganga 
Development Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired : 
 
Public purpose means the Land Acquisition for the 
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project. 
 
An allotment of land described below, shown in 
Surveyor General's Final Village Plan No. 532, 
Supplement No. 09 Sheet No. 9, situated at Randawela in 
Rajawela Grama Niladhari Division Gangala Pallesiya 
Pattuwa of Naula Divisional Secretary's Division, Matale 
District, Central Province.  
01. Lot No. 311 
Name of the Land: Kudumirissawatta; Description: 
Chena;  Claimant: The State, M. G. Athula Kumara 
Wijerathna of Kongahawela, Rajawela; Extent: 0.2045 
Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at 9.00 a.m. on 28th 
October, 2020 at the Naula Divisional Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before 21st October, 2020 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the  computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
 CHINTHAKA ILANGASINGHE, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Naula. 
 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Naula. 
21st September, 2020.
