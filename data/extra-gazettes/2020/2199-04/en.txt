FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NAULA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of  Naula  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Pitiye
Waththegedara Sunilshantha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Silpessara Patabedige Athula Weerawardhana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Naula  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Silpessara
Patabedige Athula Weerawardhana  was selected as the member of  the  Naula  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Naula  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matale,
22nd  October, 2020.
