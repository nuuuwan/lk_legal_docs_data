SOUTHERN    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
The Order given in accordance with the Article 247 of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that
should be read with Clause 2(1)(a) of Authority 255 Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province, order you, Wedaarachchi Patabendige Ravindu
Dilshan, the Chairman of Tangalle Urban Council to proceed as follows under the powers vested on me in accordance
with the Article 247 of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2(1) (a) of
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I do hereby order you, with effect from 01st of January 2021, to incur only the Recurring Expenses and not to
spend on any of Capital Expenditures included in the proposed estimated expenditures in the Budget 2021 of Tangalle
Urban Council submitted by you for approval on 07.12.2020 as per instructions given vide my letter No. G/SP/4/7/16/4/
7 and dated 13.12.2020 under the heading, “Failure to adopt the Budget of Tangalle Urban Council in accordance with
the constitution” until such time you or the council acts accordingly, or pending further order by me by way of a
Gazette  Notification.
D
R. W. W. GAMAGE ,
Governor,
Southern Province.
Southern Province Governor’s Secretariat,
Lower Dickson Road,
Galle.
