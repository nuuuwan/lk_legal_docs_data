The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   MADE   UNDER   SECTION   66C   READ   WITH   SECTION   66G   OF   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No. 22 OF 2012
I, Kasun Epa Senevirathne, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the powers entrusted
to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of selecting a new Mayor for the
vacated Mayor’s position of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council of Western Province, will be convened on
September 15 of 2020 at 9.30 a. m. at the Municipal Council Chamber of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council
located at No. 06, Nawala Road, Rajagiriya.
K
ASUN EPA SENEVIRATHNE,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,
at No. 204,  Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla,
20th August, 2020.
