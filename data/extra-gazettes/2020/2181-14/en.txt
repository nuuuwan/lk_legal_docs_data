PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
35th Higher Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”) of Sri Rohana Maha Sanga Sabha of
“Shyamopaliwanshika Maha Nikaya”
IN terms of the Regulation mentioned in the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175), No. L.D.B 11/60 dated 14th
October, 1991, it has been published that the 35th Higher Ordination (“Upasampada Ceremony”) of Sri Rohana Maha Sanga
Sabha of Kamburupitiya Divisional Secretariat, Matara District will be held from 24th, 25th, 26th and 27th of July, 2020 at the
“Seema Malaka” of Rohana Sanga Sabha Headquarters of Urapola Siri Rathana Jothi Pirivena Viharaya and the devotees are
requested not to bring plastic and polythene based products, and also it is hereby informed that the standing orders and
regulations published by the Government Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be valid within this time
period of the Higher Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”)
C
HINTHAKA ABEYWICKRAMA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kamburupitiya.
at Divisional Secretariat,
Kamburupitiya,
15th  June, 2020.
