FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    COLOMBO     MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return  member of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the United Peace Alliance, the recognized political party, to which Cadar Mohideen
Mohamed Nizamdeen the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned member of United Peace Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Salahudeen Mohamed Musammil to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Salahudeen
Mohamed Musammil was selected as the member of  the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City,
24th November, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 25.11.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    GALGAMUWA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which Herath
Mudiyanselage Herath Jayathissa Bandara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  the  member of that 12-Govigampura of Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Jayasuriya Arachchige Hemantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jayasuriya
Arachchige Hemantha was elected as the member of  the 12-Govigampura of Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
24th November, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    LAHUGALA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return  member of  Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party, to which Wiragamwela
Mudiyanselage Hitihami Sarathmahinda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of the People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ravindra Kumara Gunawardhana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Ravindra Kumara Gunawardhana was selected as the member of  the Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
24th November, 2020.
