  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice  under Section 10A(1)(a)
RAJGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Wadu Mesthrige Thilak Pushpalal of the Rajgama Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a Member of the Janasetha
Peramuna.
