PORTS   AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT   LEVY  ACT,   No.   18  OF  2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance do hereby this Order, amend the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2152/32 of December 05, 2019, as amended, by the addition of the following Sub-paragraph immediately after
Sub-paragraph (m) of Paragraph 2.
(n) Pharmaceutical machinery and equipment including accessories and spare parts for pharmaceutical machinery and
equipment imported by the pharmaceutical manufacturers for the manufacture of pharmaceuticals,  on the
recommendation of the Secretary to the State Ministry of Production, Supply and Regulation of Pharmaceuticals,
subject to the approval of the Director General of Customs.
                                               This Order shall come into effective from October 24, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance,
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
23rd October, 2020.
