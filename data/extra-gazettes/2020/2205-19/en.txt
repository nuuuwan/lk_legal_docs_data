THE  AIR  FORCE  ACT
MOD/DEF/HRM/SLAF/REG/02.
REGULATION made by the President under Section 155 of the Air Force Act (Chapter 359) read with Article 44(2) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
29th October, 2020.
Regulation
THE Sri Lanka Volunteer Air Force Regulations of 1978, published in Gazette No. 304 of February 10, 1978 are hereby
amended by insertion immediately after Regulation 19 thereof, of the following Regulation:–
“19(A) An airman who is eligible for such transfer by reason of service, shall be transferred to the Volunteer
Reserve with the approval of the Commander of the Air Force, upon his reaching the age of Fifty-five years:
Provided however, that the Commander of the Air Force may, if he is of opinion that the services of such
airman should be so extended in the interest of the Volunteer Force and on the recommendation of the Commanding
Officer of the Volunteer Air Force, extend by one year at a time but not exceeding the aggregate of three years
beyond the prescribed age limit for a Corporal and above, the period of service of an airman in the Volunteer
Force who according to the preceding provisions of this regulation has to be transferred to the Volunteer Reserve.”
