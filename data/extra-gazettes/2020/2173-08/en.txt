FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   DOMPE  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending three consecutive
meetings of the council without permission ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the  United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wijesingha
Adhikari Appuhamilage Niluka Harsani the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ruwani Damayanthi Thisera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Dompe Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Ruwani Damayanthi Thisera was selected as the member of the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
29th April, 2020.
