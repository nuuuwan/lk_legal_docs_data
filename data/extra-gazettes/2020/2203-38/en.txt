THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below 
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 An allotment of land depicted in the Advanced Tracing 
No.  Ma/YTT/2018/458 dated of 19.03.2019 prepared by the 
Surveyor General, situated in the Village called Walawela in 
the Grama Niladhari Division of Walawela (E 339 B) in the 
Minor Division of Asgiri Udusiya Pattuwa in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Yatawatta in Matale District of Central 
Province and bounded as follows:
 0 1. Lot ‘A’ – Walawela Kumbura  alias Maligawe 
Kumbura – Extent: 0.0607 Hectare
 North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Moragaspitiya Watta 
claimed by T. W. A. Badralatha; East: Moragaspitiya Watta 
claimed by T. W. A. Badralatha and Polwatta claimed by 
Jayatissa Eramudugolla; South: Polwatta claimed by Jayatissa 
Eramudugolla and Canal; West: Maligawekumbura claimed 
by Jayatissa Eramudugolla.
