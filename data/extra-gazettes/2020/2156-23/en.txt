FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAWANELLA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party
to which Karapane Mahaarachchilalage Chandrasiri Bandara the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66
A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Muhammadu Rafik Muhammadu Nausad to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammadu
Rafik Muhammadu Nausad was selected as the member of  the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
01st January, 2020.
