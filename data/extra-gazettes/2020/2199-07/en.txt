The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE    IBBAGAMUW A   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)    AS   AMENDED BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT, No.  16   OF   2017
WHEREAS the office of Chairman in Ibbagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  elected as a  member of
Parliament ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Udukumburage Sumith Weerasinghe the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party  who shall be
declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Warnakulasooriyalage Ajith Kumar Wickramasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Warnakulasooriyalage Ajith Kumar Wickramasinghe was
elected as the Chairman of  the  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
20th  October, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE    IBBAGAMUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)    AS   AMENDED BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT, No.  16   OF   2017
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman of  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman
elected as the Chairman;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Warnakulasooriyalage Ajith Kumar Wickramasinghe the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section
66B(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such
party  who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Alawaththage Dharmakeerthi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare that Alawaththage Dharmakeerthi  elected as the Vice Chairman
of  the  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
20th  October, 2020.
