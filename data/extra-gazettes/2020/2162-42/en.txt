FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KORALAIPATTU NORTH
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Koralaipattu North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to
be member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Balasingam Muralitharan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon   returned member of  Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kathiramalai Visuvanathan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the  Koralaipattu North Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that
Kathiramalai Visuvanathan was selected as the member of the  Koralaipattu North Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning officer,
Koralaipattu North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
12th of February, 2020.
