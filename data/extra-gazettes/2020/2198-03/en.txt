REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 read with Section 126 of the Motor Traffic Act

GAMINI LOKUGE,

Regulation
The Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving License) Regulation No. 03 of 2020 published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2180/26 of June 17, 2020, is hereby amended in the Paragraph (b) of  regulation 2 thereof by the
substitution for the words “to 30th September 2020” of the words “to 31st December 2020.”.
