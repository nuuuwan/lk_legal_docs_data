CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Announcement  by Hon. Governor, Central Province
DELEGATION   OF   POWER   UNDER   THE   CENTRAL   PROVINCIAL
COUNCIL   CO - OPERATIVE   SOCIETIES  CHARTER
No.  10  OF  1990,  AMENDED BY  THE  CENTRAL   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL  COOPERATIVE   SOCIETIES
(AMENDMENT)   CHARTER  No.  4  OF  1993  AS   PER  THE   ARTICLE  154 (C)  OF   CONSTITUTION  OF DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI  LANKA
AS the Govenor of the Central Provincial Council Lalith U. Gamage, according to the powers vested in me conferred by the
Article 154(c) of the constitution  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, as per the sub Section 2 and 4 of Section
2 of the Central Provincial Council Co - operative Societies Charter No. 10 of 1990, amended by the Central Provincial Council
Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Charter No. 04 of 1993, I hereby declare that powers referred in column III will be vested
with effect  from the date specified in Column II  to the Assistant  Commissioner of  Co - operative Development of the Central
Provincial Council (Acting) - (Sri Lanka Administrative Service) referred to in column No. 1 below.
