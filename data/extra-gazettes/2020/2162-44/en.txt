FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   NAMALOYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group Number 01 to which Thalabandaralage Jayathilaka, the
member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon   returned
member of  Independent Group Number 01 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Dewage Arunasiri Indrawansa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the  Namaloya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
that Hewa Dewage Arunasiri Indrawansa was selected as the member of the Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
ILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning officer,
Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
12th of February, 2020.
