The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    SIYAMBALANDUWA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which Konara
Mudiyanselage Ajith Pushpa Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that No. 03 Kotagoda of Siyambalanduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance   and who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of Thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Rathnayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Rathnayaka was elected as the member of  the   No. 03 Kotagoda of Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA GAYAN PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Monaragala,
23rd  November, 2020.
