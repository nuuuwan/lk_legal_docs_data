No. 2185/2 –  MONDAY, JULY  20,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION  7 
 
 
                
My No.: BIYA/3/1/1//510LA. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964, that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose: 
 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired : 
 
The land in total extent of about 1.6479 Hectares, 
depicted as Lot Nos.  1 and 2 in Primary Village Plan                    
No. 4206 and prepared by the Senior Surveyor 
Superintend dated 29.05.2020 situated in the Village 
called Walgama West in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Biyagama of Gampaha District. Two (2) lots. 
 
 1. Lot No. 1  
 
 
Name of the land : Aluwinne Paddy land and Kidei 
Ismaththa; Present use of the land: Marshy Land; 
Claimant: Untraceable; Extent: 1.6178 Hectares (3 A,               
3 R, 39.6 P ) 
 
 2. Lot No. 2  
 
 
Name of the land : Aluwinne Paddy land and Kidei 
Ismaththa; Present use of the land: Road; Claimant: 
Untraceable; Extent: 0.0301 Hectare (0 A, 0 R, 11.9P). 
   
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby  required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorised in 
writing, in the following dates and times before me at 
Biyagama  Divisional Secretariat. 
 
Date  Lot No.  Time 
 
12.08.2020 Lot 1and 2 9.30 a. m. 
 
 And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th day of 
August, 2020 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for the compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
  S.A.  CHANDIMA SOORIYAARACHCHI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
  Biyagama. 
 
 
  
16th  July, 2020 
Biyagama Divisional Secretariat.
