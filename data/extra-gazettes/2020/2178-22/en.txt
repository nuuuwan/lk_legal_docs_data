L.D.B 104/44(v)
TRUST  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS  the Trustees of “the Ginigathena Sri Mangala Vinayagar Temple Trust” namely -
1. Somasundaram Pillai Rajamanickam
2. Somasundaram Pillai Sundaralingam
3. Somasundaram Pillai Mahalingam
have applied to the Minister of Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms for their incorporation under Section 114 of the
Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87) ;
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Nimal Siripala de Silva, Minister of
Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms do by this Order authorize  the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and
declare that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
Deed of Trust bearing No. 2161 of January 25, 2014 attested by Kandiah Neelakandan, Notary Public of
Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style
of “the Trustees of the Ginigathena Sri Mangala Vinayagar Temple Trust” ;

(b) the said Trustees may, under the said name and style, purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of  any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust ; and
(c) the said Trustees shall, by the said name and style, be able and competent in law to sue and to be sued, to
answer and to be answered, to defend and be defended, in any court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and
actions arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time  to be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
