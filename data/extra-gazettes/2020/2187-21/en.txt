My No.: WGSWD&EIP/ACQ/KB/25.

I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
State has acquired the land described below, located in 
between Colombo - Horana Main Road to marshy land 
adjacent to Pasal Mawatha, Bokundara for canal 
development, townscape development activities under 
Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage and Environment 
Improvement Project. 
 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of 0.2611 
hectare, depicted as lot Nos. 126, 127 and 128 in Block 
No. 02 of the Cadastral Map No. 521211 dated 
10.07.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
the Grama Niladhari Division No. 575 of Bokundara in 
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kesbewa in 
Colombo District of the Western Province and bounded as 
follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 126 
Name of the Land: Parasthawala Kumbura, assessment 
No. 369/6, Colombo Road; Description: Home Garden, 
four permanent buildings and a cement constructed well 
available; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Mr. Neel 
Fernando, No. 12 Francis Road, Wellawaththa; Extent: 
0.2079 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 127 
Name of the land: Parasthawala Kumbura, 
Assessment No. 369/4, Colombo Road; Description: 
Home Garden, three permanent buildings, a tempoary 
building and a cement constructed well available; 
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Mr. R. A. Piyasena, 
No. 369/4, Colombo Road, Bokundara, Piliyandala ; 
Extent: 0.0384 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 128 
Name of the land: Godaparadeniya, assessment No. 
369/C, Colombo Road; Description: Home Garden; 
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Heirs of late Mr. K. 
P. D. Somasiri, who was living at No. 369/C, Colombo 
Road, Bokundara, Piliyandala ; Extent: 0.0148 Hectare. 
 
 
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
on which a servitude  is to be acquired are hereby  
required to appear  personally, or by agents duly 
authorised in writing, before me at Land Acquisition 
Office located in the Head Office of the Sri Lanka Land 
Reclamation and Development Corporation No. 03, Sri 
Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on 
24.09.2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
17.09.2020, the nature of their interests in the land/lands, 
the particulars of their claims for  compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
