The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    VALIKAMAM   NORTH  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Nallaiya
Puradshithasan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sanmugavadivel Sellakanthan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the V alikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sanmugavadivel
Sellakanthan was selected as the member of  the  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
06th  October, 2020.
