I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends  to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Under mentioned land the shown in the Surveyor
General’s plan of F. V . P . 162/11/11, situated in the Village of
Gunadaha in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 372
Gunadaha in Thumpane Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Kandy District of Central Province.
1. Lot No. 386
Name of the Land: Madagodawaththa,
Nagashenawaththa, Kaudalla Nagashinna; Description:
Garden; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:
Wilbagedara Ihala Walawwe Gamini Waththawa; Extent:
0.5318 Hectares.
2. Lot No. 387
Name of the Land: Madagodawaththa, Nagashenawaththa,
Kaudalla Nagashinna; Description: Garden; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Wilbagedara Ihala Walawwe
Panduka Waththawa; Extent: 0.0758 Hectares.

servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at  Thumpane Divisional Secretariat, on 09th  November 2020,
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before   26th October, 2020 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
computation for the said land/servitude the amount of
compensation the details of the computation of such amount.
