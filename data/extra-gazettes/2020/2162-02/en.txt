EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, K. A. Vimalenthirarajah, Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 10th February,  2020, all  duties
of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2161/1 of
03rd  February,  2020 is  hereby rescinded.
K. A.  VIMALENTHIRARAJAH,
Acting  Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
07th February, 2020.
