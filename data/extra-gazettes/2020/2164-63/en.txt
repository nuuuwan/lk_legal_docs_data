THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby declare, under  Sub - section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that  the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be required to acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The Allotments of land describe below, depicted in Surveyor
General's Advance Tracing No. Ma/LGG/2015/403 of 09.05.2016,
situated at Guruwela village (within Topo.P.P.13) in Laggala Pallesiya
pattuwa minor Division of Laggala Pallegama Divisional Secretary's
Division, Matale District, Central Province and bounded as follows;
01. Lot 'A' – Illagahalanda – Extent: 1.1673 Hectares
North: Meeriyakanda ela; East: Meeriyakanda forest Claimed by
the State; South: Ela and Nikawathura Oya; West: Nikawathura
Oya.
02. Lot 'B' – Illagahalanda – Extent: 0.8039 Hectare
North: Ela; East: Ela and Meeriyakanda forest Claimed by the
State; South: Meeriyakanda Idama Claimed by the E. M.
Muthubanda; West: Lot C.
03. Lot 'C' – Illegolla – Extent: 0.5787 Hectare
North: Nikawathura Oya and Ela; East: Lot B; South:
Meeriyakanda Idama Claimed by E. M. Muthubanda; West:
Nikawathura Oya.
