The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a)
of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Amuldas Jesintha of the Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated her office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as she has ceased to be a member of the Ceylon Workers
Congress.
P. S. P
ATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
