THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 13/97/FH/523.
My Reference No : 05/31.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired :
An allotment of land Paratholla, Modarawattha in total
extent of  0.3496 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 01 and 02 in the
Preliminary Plan No. 3016 dated 28.03.2001 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village called Dodanduwa
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 51 of Dodanduwa in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Hikkaduwa in Galle District
of the Southern Province and morefully described as follows:
Lot No. 01 - Name of Land: Paratholla, Modarawattha;
Extent: about 0.3068  Hectare.
Boundaries: North: Sea Coast, Pinwattha and custom  road
possessed by Rev. Darmasena Thero; East: Custom Road,
Lot No. 2, Paratholla, Modarawattha possessed by A. M. T.
Arsakulasooriya; South: Paratholla, Modarawattha possessed
by A. M. T. Arsakulasooriya and Janthungawattha possessed
by K. G . Sugathadasa and Others; West: Sea coast.
Lot No. 02 - Name of Land: Paratholla, Modarawattha;
Extent: about 0.0428  Hectare.
Boundaries: North: Lot No. 01 and Custom Road; East:
Custom Road; South: Lot No. 01; West: Lot No. 01.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agents  duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Hikkaduwa Divisional Secretariat, on
the 28th October, 2020 at 02.00 p.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the nature
of their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said  lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. All deeds and documents in support of the claims
should be produced before me at the inquiry, 21st October,
2020.
A. M. A. U. G. KARIYAWASAM,
Divisional Secretary,
Hikkaduwa.
18th September, 2020
Divisional Secretariat,
Hikkaduwa.
