THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978 
 
 Order under Section 20(4)(b) 
 
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by section 20(4)(b ) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G. L 
Peiris, Minister of Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi 
Ayurveda institute have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed to restore 
normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Senior Professor Janitha Abeywickrama Liyanage to be the Competent 
Authority with effect from September 13, 2020 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of the 
Director of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function respectively which has been 
conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-
law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder. 
 
 Professor G. L. PEIRIS,  
 Minister of Education. 
 
Colombo, 
14th September, 2020.
