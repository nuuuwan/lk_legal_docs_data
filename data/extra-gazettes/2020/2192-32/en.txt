THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7 (Amendment)
My Ref. No.: 4/1/La/Acquisition/88/2016(12/2019).
Ministry of Land and Lands Development Reference
No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/325.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/Servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
The allotments of Land, containing in extent of 0.0451
Hectare, Lots 321-322 (2 lots) under mentioned land shown
in the Surveyor General’s Cadastral Map No. 420204 dated
19.02.2020 Block No.01, Sheet No.13, situated in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. 715, Havana, in Mallawapitiya
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kurunegala District of North
Western Province.
1. Lot No. 321
Name of the Land: Havanakumbura; Description of Land:
Paddy field; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Taken
over under Section 38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act.
Possessed by Panawennage Upul Priyantha resident in
Havanawatta (262 sq.m); Extent: 0.0262 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 322
Name of the Land: Havanakumbura; Description of Land:
Paddy field; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Taken
over under Section 38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act.
Possessed by Muthunayake Durayalage Padmini Mallika
resident in Havanawatta (189 sq.m); Extent: 0.0189 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired by are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Mallawapitiya Divisional Secretariat, on the day of
the 23.09.2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
16.09.2020 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for computation for the said land/
