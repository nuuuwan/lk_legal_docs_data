FILLING  OF  A   VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  KOTIKAW ATTA  MULLERIYAWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  66 A (1) (a) OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the Office of a Member of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not
attending three consecutive meetings without permission;
And Whereas the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamad Nilamdeen
Mohamad Nisthar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,  to decide
upon the member of that Ward No. 15 Megoda Kolonnawa of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall
be declared elected as a Member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Wahab Mohomed Shihan to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that  Abdul Wahab Mohomed Shihan was elected as the member of the Ward No. 15 Megoda Kolonnawa of
Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
29th June, 2020.
