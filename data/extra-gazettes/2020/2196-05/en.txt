CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Imposing Entertainment Tax at Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Lalith U. Gamage, Governor of Central Province, hereby declare that I have approved the below mentioned resolution as
per the powers vested in me by the Subsection 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) decided under decision
No. 05.01 of the General Assembly held on 12.03.2020 at the Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
in accordance with the powers vested in it by Sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the aforesaid Entertainment Tax Ordinance.
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE (Attorney-at-Law),
 Governor,
Central Province.
At the Governor’s Office,
Central Province,
26th day of August, 2020.
RESOLUTION
I suggest levying
an entertainment tax on all recreational activities carried out in the Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale
Pradeshiya Sabha area in terms of the Entertainment Tax Amendment Act, No. 37 of 1984 and Subsection (1) of Section 2 of
the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 and when that entertainment activity is
(a) a screening of a film, to levy an amount equal to 7.5% of the amount charged from the person who is admitted to
the show ; and
(b) any other entertainment activity, to levy an amount equal to 10% of the amount charged from the person admitted
for such activity.
