SRI    LANKA    PORTS   AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.  51   OF   1979
Order under Section 25(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25(1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 as amended by Act,
No. 07 of 1984, Act, No. 35 of 1984 and Act, No. 02 of 1992, I, P. Rohitha Abeygunawardhana, Minister of Ports and Shipping
do by this Order approve under Section 25(1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act, the acquisition of the lands specified in
Schedule Nos. 01 to 03 hereto being required for the purposes of the business of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority a Statutory
Body established by the aforementioned Act to be acquired under the Land Acquisition Act and transferred under the Act
to the Sri Lanka Ports Authority.
P. R
OHITHA ABEYGUNAWARDHANA,
Minister of Ports and Shipping.
At Colombo,
25th  September, 2020.
SCHEDULE   No. 01
All that allotments of lands marked Lot 01 depicted in Plan No. S/Mis/510A dated 11.08.2020 made by S. R.
Ileperumachchi, Registered Licensed Surveyor situated in village Kankasanturai West at Grama Niladari Division No. J/233,
Kankasanthurai West Divisional Secretariat of Valikaman North, District of Jaffna, Northern Province and bounded on the
North by : Reservation of Sea, East by : Lot 02 & Kovil, South by : Kovil & Road (RDA) and on the West by : Road to Pier
containing in extent 6.536  Hectares or Sixteen Acres and Twenty Four Perches (16A., 0R., 24P.).
