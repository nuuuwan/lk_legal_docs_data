THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: KDS/HAR/4/2/1/49. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/09/2015/WS/131. 
 
I, hereby given notice under Sub Section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intends to acquire the following lot of the 
land for the construction of the water reservoir of 
Uduwawala service station of the Kandy North 
Pathadumbara joined Water Supply Scheme :- 
 
Description of the Land  to be Acquired: 
 
The Lot No. 76 in Folio No. 01 and Zone No. 02 with 
an extent of 0.0813 Hectare, depicted in the Cadastral 
Plan No. 320320, surveyed and prepared by the Chief 
Surveyor Superintendent of Kandy District, situated in 
Uduwawala village in Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor 
Division in the Harispattuwa Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Kandy District in the Central Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 76 
Name of the Land: Usketehena; Owner of the Land: 
Mr. J.A. Wimalasena, No. 24/A, Gothatuwe watta, 
Uduwawala, Katugastota; Extent: 0.0813 Hectare. 
All persons interested, having rights or possessions in 
the aforesaid land are required to appear before me 
personally, or by an agent duly authorized in writing at 
Divisional Secretary’s Office of Harispattuwa, on the 
26.02.2020, at 10.00 a.m. 
 
They are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of February 2020 
the nature of their interests and rights in the land and the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the aforesaid land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the compensation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 MOHAN DHARMADASA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Harispattuwa Divisional Secretary’s Division. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat of Harispattuwa, Nugawela.  
03rd of January, 2020.
