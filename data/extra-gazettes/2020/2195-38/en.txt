FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    HORANA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Horana  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Atigala
Pathirage Suranjan Sajeewan Premathilaka, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the  member of that No. 29 - Vevala of Horana  Pradeshiya Sabha   who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wanniarachchige Nevil Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  Horana  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Wanniarachchige Nevil Kumara was elected as the member of  the No. 29 - Vevala of Horana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Horana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
29th  September, 2020.
