THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.:  DIVIB/LND/LADAQ/CEP/05/2015/03.
Ministry of Land and Parliamentary Reforms
Reference No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/263.
I,  hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7
of the Land Acquisition Act, No.28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
The allotments of Land, containing in extent of 0.1070
Hectare, Lots 356-357 (02 lots) under mentioned land shown
in the Surveyor General’s FVP No. 1157 dated 30.12.2019
Supplement No.5, Sheet No.5, situated in the village of
Ihala Walpola in the Grama Niladhari Division of No.510,
Medagedara, in Ibbagamuwa Divisional Secretary’s
Division in Kurunegala District of North Western Province.
1. Lot No. 356
Name of the Land: Kudaoya Watta; Description of Land:
Open wasteland; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:
P .P 5072 Lot No. 15, P.P . 6485 Lot No. 4. required for the
Central Expressway Project - Phase IV (891 sqm); Extent: 0.0891
Hectare.
2. Lot No. 357
Name of the Land: -; Description of Land: Rathwita Canal;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: P .P 5072 Lot
No. 22, Half of the Canal. required for the Central Expressway
Project - Phase IV (179 sqm); Extent: 0.0179 Hectare.
      All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired by are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Ibbagamuwa Divisional Secretariat, on the day of the  2020
July 10 at 09.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 2020 July 03 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for computation for the said land/servitude the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D. P . S. KUMARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ibbagamuwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Ibbagamuwa.
