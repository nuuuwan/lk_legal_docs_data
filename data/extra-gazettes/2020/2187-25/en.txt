SPORTS   LA W No. 25  OF  1973
Regulations under the Sections 32 and 33
I, Dullas Alahapperuma, Minister of  Education, Sports and Youth Affairs, hereby cancel the Extraordinary Gazette
No.   2113/59 issued on 07.03.2019 under the Sections 32 (b) and 33 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973 and authorize the Director
General of the Department of Sports Development as the competent authority with immediate effects to call and conduct the
Elections of the Sri Lanka Bodybuilding and Fitness Federation and the Sri Lanka Powerlifting Federation as well as to
continue the activities therein until the Election is held.
D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA (M. P .),
Minister of Education, Sports and Youth Affairs.
At Colombo,
31st July, 2020.
