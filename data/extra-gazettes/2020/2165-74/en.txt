FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PANNALA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Hettiarachchige Demiyan Jayalal Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the returned member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Jayamaha Hitihamilage Kasun Sanjeewa Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Jayamaha
Hitihamilage Kasun Sanjeewa Kumara was selected as the member of the  Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
06th March, 2020.
