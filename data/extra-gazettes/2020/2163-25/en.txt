THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No. 40  OF  2008
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No.40 of 2008, I, Johnston Xavier 
Fernando, Minister of Roads and Highways, do by this Order, designate the “Southern National Highway (Kottawa-
Mattala)” which was designated as an “Expressway” by Order made under Section 9 of the aforesaid Act and   published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No.2163/15 of February 18, 2020 and described in the Schedule to this Order, to be an 
“User Fee National Highway”.  
         The Orders made under Section 11 of the aforesaid Act published in -
 1. Gazette Extraordinary No.1731/2 of November 08, 2011 designating Southern National Highway as a 
User Fee National Highway;
 2. Gazette Extraordinary No.1850/33 of February 19, 2014 designating Galle-Matara National Highway as a 
User Fee National Highway;
 3. Gazette Extraordinary No. 2148/62 of November 09, 2019 designating Godagama - Kasagala National 
Highway (Section of Southern Expressway) as a User Fee National Highway; and
