THE  CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Delegation of Powers by the National Police Commission in terms of Article 155 J
BY virtue of the powers vested in the National Police Commission in terms of Article 155J of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the said National Police Commission delegated some of its powers to the
Inspector General of Police and Police Officers as specified in the Government Gazette Extraordinary bearing No. 2016/6
dated April 24, 2017.
The Schedule of the Said Gazette Notification is replaced by the following Schedule with effect from January 29,
2020.
By order of the National Police Commission,
K.W.E. K
ARALLIYADDA,
Chairman,
National Police Commission.
