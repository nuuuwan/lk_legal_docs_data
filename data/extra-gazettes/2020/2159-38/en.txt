THE  CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Assigning of Duties and Subjects in terms of the Provisions of Section 44(5) of the Constitution
ON the concurrence and approval of the Cabinet of Ministers received at the the cabinet meeting dated 10.12.2019 for the
Cabinet Memorandum No. 19/3391/201/005 submitted by H. E. the President to assign the duties and subjects for the
Non-Cabinet State Ministers, hereby notified that the following subjects, duties, departments, state corporations and
statutory bodies should be under the Hon. Anura Priyadarshana Yapa, State Minister of Internal Trade and Consumer
Welfare in terms of the 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
C
HAMAL  R AJAPAKSA ,
 Minister of Internal Trade,
Food Security and Consumer Welfare.
07th January, 2020.
