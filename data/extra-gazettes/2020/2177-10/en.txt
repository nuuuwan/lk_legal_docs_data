THE   SOCIETIES    ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Prime 
Minister and Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do by this notification :
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “PRECIOUS  CARGO  CLEARING  AND  FORWARDING
AGENTS  SOCIETY   LIMITED ” situated at National Gem and Jewellery Authority, No. 25, Galle Face Terrace,
Colombo 03 as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
The Secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01,

 THE   SOCIETIES   ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Prime 
Minister and Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do by this notification :
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “SRI   LANKA   WURTHEEYAWEDINGE   SANSADAYA”

that Ordiance.
