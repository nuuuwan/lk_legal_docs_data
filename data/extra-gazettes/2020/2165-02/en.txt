WIMAL W EERAW ANSA,

Colombo,
28th February, 2020.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Gem and Jewellery (License fee) Regulations No. 1 of 2020.
2. The National Gem and Jewellery Authority shall levy a fee for the issuance, renewal; extension or amendment
of any license, certificate, permit or approval or other services rendered by the National Gem and Jewellery Authority to
any person carrying on the gem industry.
3. The fees specified in column II of the Schedule hereto shall be levied in respect of matters specified in the
corresponding entry in Column I of that Schedule.
