THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978 
 
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21  
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula  
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the U niversity 
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made Under Section 21 of the sa id Act, 
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 420/25 of September 26, 1986 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette 
Extraordinary, No. 2002/35 of January 18, 2017), establishing the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, by the addition 
immediately after entry ''11. Department of Philosophy and Value Studies" appearing in Column II of the Schedule 
which corresponds to the item ''Faculty of Arts and Culture" appearing in Colum I of the Schedule of  the following new 
entry  :- 
 
 ''12. Department of English Language Teaching". 
 
 Dr. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
 Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
