THE   PUBLIC   SECURITY   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   40)
Order
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 12 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
President, do by this order call out with effect from January 22, 2020, all the members of the Armed Forces specified in the First
Schedule hereto, for the maintenance of Public Order in the areas specified in the Second Schedule hereto.
GOTABAYA R AJAPAKSA,
 President.
Colombo,
January  20, 2020.
