The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KARAITIVU   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group Number 01 to which Arumukam Poopalareththinam  the
member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member
if Independent Group Number 01 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated
his decision to nominate Sathasivam Sasikumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sathasivam
Sasikumar  selected as the member of   the Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
19th June, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KARAITIVU   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group Number 01 to which Rasaiya Mohan the member belonged,
was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of Independent
Group Number 01 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated
his decision to nominate Kandasamy Kumarasiri to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kandasamy
Kumarasiri  selected as the member of   the Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Karaitivu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
