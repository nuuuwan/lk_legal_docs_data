THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 22
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) read with Section 22 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr.
Bandula Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 22 of the aforesaid Act, in
relation to the establishment of the Vavuniya Campus of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, published in the Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 968/6 of March 26, 1997, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2050/
7 of December 18, 2017) by the addition immediately after the item “Faculty of Applied Science” appearing in Column I
and in its corresponding entries appearing in Column II thereof, of the following new item and entry :-
Column I Column II
“Faculty of Technological Studies Department of Information and Communication
Technology”.
Dr. B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
29th January, 2020.
