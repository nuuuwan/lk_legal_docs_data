 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION   ACT (CHAPTER    460)
Order  under  Section 49A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 49A of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) read with Article 44 of
the Constitution, I, Prasanna Ranatunga, Minister of Industrial Export & Investment Promotion of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, declare that the land specified in the Schedules hereto are required by the Board
of Investment of Sri Lanka being a public corporation established by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law, No. 4 of
1978.
P
RASANNA  R ANATUNGA ,
Minister of Industrial Export & Investment Promotion.
Colombo,
22nd February, 2020.
