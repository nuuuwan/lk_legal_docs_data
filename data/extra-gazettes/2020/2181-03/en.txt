FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PATHADUMBARA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which
Shihabdeen Mohamed Irshad the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hassim Mohammed Azmy to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of the Pathadumbara
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Hassim Mohammed Azmy was selected as the member of the  Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
19th June, 2020.
