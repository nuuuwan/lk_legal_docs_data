THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: 6/3/7/15/11/15.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/MV/117J.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intend
to acquire the following land which is required for Moragaha-
kanda and Kaluganga Development Project.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Public purpose means the land acquisition  for the
Moragahakanda and Kaluganga Development Project.
The allotments of land described below, shown in TopoPP
No. 18, Supplement No. 29 Sheet No. 62, dated 29.01.2018,
approved by Surveyor General, situated at Elagamuwa Grama
Niladhari Division Gangala Pallesiya Puttuwa Division of
Naula Divisional Secretary's Division, Matale District, Central
Province;
01. Lot No. 2936
Name of Land: Ambagahadewolaheneyaya; Description:
Forest; Claimant: The State (Department of Forest
Conservation); Possessed by: Mr. Heenbanda Kulugammana
of No. 14, Kulugammana, Kandy and Mr. Wattegedara
Duggannaralalage Ekanayaka Bandara of Ja-Ela Road,
Kirindiwita, Gampaha;  Extent: 13.8891 Hectares.
02. Lot No. 2937
Name of Land: Ambagahadewolaheneyaya; Description:
Forest; Claimant: The State (Department of Forest Conservation);
Possessed by: Mr. Heenbanda Kulugammana of No. 14,
Kulugammana, Kandy and Mr. Wattegedara Duggannaralalage
Ekanayaka Bandara of Ja-Ela Road, Kirindiwita, Gampaha;
Extent: 21.3617 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents before me at 9.00
a.m. on 21st July, 2020 at the Naula Divisional Secretariat.
