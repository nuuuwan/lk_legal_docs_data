The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   ARACHCHIKATTUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being
ceased to be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Adhikari
Mudiyanselage Iranganie Pushpalatha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Dhanapalasingam Wijayanirmala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Dhanapalasingam Wijayanirmala  was selected as the member of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
29th  January, 2020.
