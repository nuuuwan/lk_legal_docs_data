The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   GALLE   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Galle Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Panangala Liyanage Deshapriya the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Nawurunna Guruge Methsiri Alexander De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the  Galle Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Nawurunna
Guruge Methsiri Alexander De Silva was selected as the member of the Galle Municipal Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning officer,
Galle Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Galle,
03rd March, 2020.
