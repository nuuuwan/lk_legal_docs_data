FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PANDUWASNUWARA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member in Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased
to be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United  People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Ushettige Don Janaka Thilakasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member if United  People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid Recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Agare Gedara Durayalage Ranjith Premalal to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Agare
Gedara Durayalage Ranjith Premalal was selected as the member of   the Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
21st June, 2020.
