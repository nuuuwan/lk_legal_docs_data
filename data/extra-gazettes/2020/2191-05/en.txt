FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United Peace Alliance the recognized political party to which Mohamed
Pradeep Cadar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of United Peace Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid Recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Imthiyas Ahmad Kaleelur Rahman to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Imthiyas
Ahmad Kaleelur Rahman was selected as the member of  the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
28th August, 2020.
