Notice
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers  vested in me by section 44(5)  of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. I hereby delegate following duties and functions of the Ministry of  Health and Indigenous
Medical Services to the State Minister of indigenous Medical Services.
P
