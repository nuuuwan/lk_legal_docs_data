3RD   AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Education Administrative Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Education Administrative Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1928/28 dated 21st of August 2015 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1956/16 dated 01st of March 2016 and No. 2141/6 dated 16th of September 2019 of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended as follows. This amendment should take effect from the 17th of April, 2015.
                                          By order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Office of the Public Service Commission.
17th of January, 2020.
10.3.2 Method of Promotion
The officers who have fulfilled the qualifications in 10.3.1 above will be promoted to Special Grade for the number of
vacancies in approved number of posts in Special Grade as at the 01st of January and 01st of July every year by the Public
Service Commission according to the seniority.
i. A number of officers not less than thrice the number of vacancies from among those who are in Grade I as at the
date of the calculation of vacancies will, according to the order of their seniority, be called for the interview.
