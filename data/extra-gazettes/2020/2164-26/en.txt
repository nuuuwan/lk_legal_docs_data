DECLARING ThE SACRED LAND bELONGING TO hISTORICAL RANkOThmALu PuRANA 
RAjAmAhA VIhARAYA Of WAGEGODA, TANGALLE AS A SACRED AREA
Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette No.2153/12 of friday, 10th December  2019
IT is hereby declared that the extent of land specifically mentioned in the Schedule below as a sacred area for its long 
standing protection  and will be known as “historical Rankothmalu Purana Rajamaha Viharaya of Wagegoda” as 
per the Notification made by His ExcEllEncy Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka in the Extraordinary Gazette No.2153/12 of friday, 10th December 2019 and according to the powers vested to 
me as Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs as per the subject of protection and 
Development of Sacred Areas.
 
MaHinda Rajapaksa,
