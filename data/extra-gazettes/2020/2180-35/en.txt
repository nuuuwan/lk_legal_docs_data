Government Notifications
MINUTES  ON  PENSIONS
NOTIFICA TION under section 51 of the Minutes on Pensions amended on 05th February 1934 by the Minutes on Pensions
(revision) Act bearing No. 13 of 1948.
Notification
The provisions No. 02 and 17 of the Minutes on Pensions amended time to time is further amended with the insertion of
the under mentioned subsection immediate after the said provisions.
“The compulsory retirement age of the medical officers of the public service is extended up to sixty-one (61) years”.
The amendment made by this notification to the Minutes on Pensions shall come into effect form 20.05.2020.
