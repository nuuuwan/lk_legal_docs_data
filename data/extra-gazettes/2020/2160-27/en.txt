The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
DEPARTMENT   OF   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT - EASTERN   PROVINCE
Notice on Summon Meeting  under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
BY virtue of  power vested on me under Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections as amended by Act, No. 22 of 2012,
I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to
fill the vacancy arises as a result of vacating the post of Deputy Mayor of the Kalmunai Municipal Council, Ampara
District Eastern Province at 10.00 a. m. on 2020.02.12 at Kalmunai Municipal Council Hall for the purpose of electing
the new Deputy Mayor.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
