PART I : S EC. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 18.12.2020
1A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 3 and 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance do by this notification—
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “GUWAN THOTUPALA SANCHARAKA KAULU
MARGOPADESHAKA SAHA RIYADURU SANGAMAYA” situated at No. 238/4, Veyangoda Road,
Andiambalama as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended; and
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of section 5(4)
of that Ordinance.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
The Secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01,
18th December, 2020.
