The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  NINTHA VUR  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Athamwawa
Rifana member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned
member of All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mahroof Faththima Nushha to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina lshan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mahroof
Faththima Nushha was selected as the member of the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
09th October, 2020.
