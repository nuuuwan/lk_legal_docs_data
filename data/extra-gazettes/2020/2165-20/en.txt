PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1  OF  1981
Notice under Section 6(1)
IN the exercise of the powers vested in the Election Commission under Section 6(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, I do hereby appoint the Assistant Returning Officers mentioned by designation in Column II of the Schedule
here for Electoral District in the corresponding entry in Column I of that Schedule.

SAMAN S RI R ATHNAYAKE,
Commissioner General of Elections.
