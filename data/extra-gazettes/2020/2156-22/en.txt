NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the
Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, has been graciously pleased to award President’s Truncheon and the Regimental
Truncheon to the Sri Lanka Sinha Regiment at a parade held in the Regimental Center of the Sri Lanka Sinha Regiment,
Ambepussa on thirtieth day of January in the year Two Thousand and Seventeen.
                        By His Excellency’s Command,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
30th Day of December, 2019.
