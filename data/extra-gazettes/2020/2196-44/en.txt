The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  V ALIKAMAM EAST  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Ilayavan
Mailvahanam the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kunaradnam Gayendran to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning officer of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kunaradnam
Gayendran was selected as the member of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna.
08th October, 2020.
