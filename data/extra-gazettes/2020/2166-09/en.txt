SPORTS   LAW   No.   25   OF   1973
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sports under the Section 41 and read with the Section 31 of the  Sports Law
No.25 of 1973.
DULLAS  A LAHAPPERUMA,
Minister of Education, Sports and Youth Affairs.
At Colombo,
On  28th  day of  February, 2020.
Regulations
01. The procedure of appointing the Selection Committee of National Association of Sports specified in the
instructions issued on 25.02.2017 and the sections (7) (XIV) (a), (b), (c) and (d) of the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 2006/13 dated 13.02.2017 which was substituted for the item (XIV) of 7 in Part III of the Regulations No.1990/23
issued on 27.10.2016 are repealed and the following item is inserted instead.
No.7 (XIV)
(a) In respect of selecting the members for Technical Committee, Finance Committee and Disciplinary
Committee for the official term as set out in the Constitution, the Executive Committee shall inform all
affiliated member institutes, call for nominations and make appointments as per the qualifications immediately
after the Executive Committee is appointed by the election of the association.

05. The section (XVll) of 7 in Part Ill of the Regulations No.1990/23 issued on 27.10.2016 and the section 7 (c); as
a revision thereto, of the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2006/13 dated 13.02.2017 and the (a) and (b) of the sections No.
(01) 7 (XVII) of the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2128/ 13 dated 18.06.2019 are repealed and the following sections are
inserted instead.
(a) Name list of the qualified officers entitled to be elected as office bearers shall be finished and submitted by
the Election Committee to the affiliated institutes at least 30 days prior to the election of the National
Association of  Sports indicating the reasons for the rejected nominations as well.
(b) Submit a notice at least 14 days prior to the Annual General Meeting to its affiliated institutes including the
list of the members entitled to be present and to vote at the Annual General Meeting, a copy of it certified by
the President, Secretary and Treasurer of the relevant financial year.
(c) If the Financial Statements of the year has been submitted before conducting the General Meeting to the
Auditor General for the audit within 02 months after the end of financial year and if the Audited Financial
Statements have not been received after completing the audit to be presented to its members 14 days prior to
the date of election, the Election and the General Meeting can be held without presenting the Audited Financial
Statements and  immediately  after  the Financial Statements audited by the Auditor General are given to the
Federation, the said Financial Audit Report and the Financial  Statements  shall be certified by the President,
Secretary, Treasurer and referred to the affiliated institutes through the present Executive Committee 14
days before the Special General Meeting and adopted in a Special General Meeting wherein the President,
Secretary, Treasurer and the present Executive Committee are participating.

8. (2) (a) An appeal in respect of a decision given by the Election Committee of National Association of
Sports registered by the Minister in charge of the subject of Sports shall be submitted to the Minister
within a period of 07 days from the date of communication of the said decision and the Minister
shall refer such appeals to the Appeal Consultancy Committee and the Appeal Consultancy Committee
shall inform its recommendation in writing to the  Minister  in charge of Sports for the purpose of
reviewing within a period of a week (07 days) from the date of the reference of the  appeal.
(b) The appeals regarding financial administration of a National Association of Sports or the
appointments to the Executive Committee can be submitted to the  Minister at any time.
(c) All the appeals [except the appeals in relation to the elections and appeals specified in the above
section (b)] shall be presented to the Minister within 14 days from the date of decision given or
action taken by the said National Association of Sports.
(d) Except for the appeals on the selection of players, a non-refundable sum of Rs.5000.00 for all
other appeals shall be paid to the Shroff counter of the Ministry of Sports and the receipts shall be
affixed to the appeal. The aforesaid sum will be refunded only if he is attested to be correct as per
the appeal or if the objection presented is proved.
(e) The Appeal Consultancy Board; appointed by the Minister after considering all the appeals presented
to the Minister in charge of the subject of Sports, should be a committee of three persons consisted
with retired judges of Supreme Court/Appeal Court or retired senior officers in the government.
