MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,

1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 11 of 2020.”
2. The Regulations prescribed in this Gazette shall only be applicable to importation of goods which have the date
of Bill of Lading/ Air Waybill on or after 25th December, 2020.
