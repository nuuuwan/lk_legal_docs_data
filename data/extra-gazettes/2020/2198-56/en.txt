EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Announcement
I,  Anuradha Yahampath, Governor of the Eastern Province, do hereby amend the Sections referred below in the Regulations
published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 2184/9 dated 13th July
2020 in relation to allowances for Councilors of Municipal Council, Urban Council and Pradeshiya Sabha in the Eastern
Province. The amendments shall be effected from 23rd June, 2020.
A
NURADHA YAHAMPATH ,
 Governor,
Eastern Province.
Governor’s Secretariat,
Eastern Province,
21st September, 2020.
Regulations
01. Municipal Council
Sub-section 1.1 of the Regulations for Municipal Council published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2184/9 dated 13th July 2020 is hereby repealed and replace with the following
sentences.

1. Fuel Limits to Mayor of Municipal Council for official visits :
1.1 If a vehicle of Municipal Council is used within or outside of the municipality limit, monthly fuel consumption of
diesel/petrol limit for such purposes shall be approved by the Council and paid from the Council funds.
02. Urban Council
Sub-section 1.1 of the Regulations for Urban Council published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2184/9 dated 13th July 2020 is hereby repealed and replace with the following sentences.
1. Fuel Limits to Chairman of  Urban Council for official visits :
1.1 If a vehicle of Urban Council is used within or outside of the Urban Council limit, monthly fuel consumption of
diesel/petrol limit for such purposes shall be approved by the Council and paid from the Council funds.
03.  Pradeshiya Sabha
Sub-sections 1.1, 1.2 and 1.3 of the Regulations for Pradeshiya Sabha published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2184/9 dated 13th July 2020 is hereby repealed and replace with the
following sentences.
1. Fuel Limits to Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha for official visits :
1.1 If a vehicle of  Pradeshiya Sabha is used within or outside of the Pradeshiya Sabha limit, monthly fuel consumption
of diesel/petrol limit for such purposes shall be approved by the Pradeshiya Sabha and paid from the Sabha funds.
