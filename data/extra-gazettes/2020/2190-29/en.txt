Notice  under  Section 07 
 
 
My No. : UDU/2/5/2/12. 
Land Ministry No.  : 13/97/FR/254. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for the conservation of Dumbara mountain range. 
 
Description of the Land / Servitude to be Acquired  : 
 
An allotment of land called "Winchfield Park Watta" 
bearing an extent of about 0.3743 Hectare mentioned in 
P.P. Maha 1930 Supplement (Lot No. 23) of the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Karambakatiya, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province. 
 
Name and Address of Claimants  : Mr. T. M. P. G. 
Loku Banda, No. 59/2, Udawela, Kahataliyadda. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
on which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 18th 
November, 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
11th November, 2020 nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Udadumbara. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Udadumbara 
21st  August, 2020.
