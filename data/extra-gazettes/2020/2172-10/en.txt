PORTS  AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT    LEVY   ACT,   No.   18   OF   2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do hereby this Order, amend
the  Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2152/32 of 05.12.2019, as amended, by addition to the said
Schedule of the articles specified in the Column III corresponding to the HS Headings and HS Codes specified in the Column
I and II of the Schedule hereto, effective from April 23, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
