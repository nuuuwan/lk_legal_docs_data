THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER  203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport Services Management under Section 237 read with Section 126 of the
Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203).
MAHINDA  AMARAWEERA,
  Minister of Transport Services Management.
Colombo,
17th March, 2020.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the V alidity Period of Driving
Licence) Regulations No. 01 of 2020.
2. Validity period of Every Driving Licence, issued by the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic under the Motor
Traffic Act, which expires within the period of 16th March 2020 to 15th April 2020, shall be extended till 30th of
June 2020.
