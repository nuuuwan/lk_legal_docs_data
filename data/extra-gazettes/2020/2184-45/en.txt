THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2009/FR/239.
My Reference No.: 3/3/6/2/13.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following Lands which are required for a
Conservation of Catchment Area.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1 and 2 allotments in total extents of about 5.6869
Hectares of land in Preliminary Plan No. Ma. 1812 prepared by
the surveyor superintendent of Matale situated at Imbulpitiya
village in Imbulpitiya Grama Niladhari Division of Ukuwela
Divisional Secretary Division in the District of Matale Central
Province.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of Land: Hunnasgiriya Watta; Original Claimant:  Land
Reform Commission; Land extent: 0.8424 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of Land: Hunnasgiriya Watta; Original Claimant:  Land
Reform Commission; Land extent: 4.8445 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid/lands are hereby
requested to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Ukuwela on
30.09.2020 at 10.00 a.m.
And are hereby requested to inform me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 22.09.2020. The nature of their
interest in the land, the particulars of their claim for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
S. N. K. SERASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Ukuwela.
At Ukuwela Divisional Secretariat,
13th July , 2020.
