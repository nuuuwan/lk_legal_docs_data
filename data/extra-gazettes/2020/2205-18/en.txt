FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE POOJAPITIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Poojapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
D. G. H. Dilshan Rupasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate A. D. Vimukthi Daraka Edirisinghe to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of the
Poojapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) do hereby declare that A. D. Vimukthi Daraka Edirisinghe was selected as the member of the Poojapitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A.  C.  N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
08th  December, 2020.
