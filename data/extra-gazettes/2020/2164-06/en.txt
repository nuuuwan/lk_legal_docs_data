FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   AKURANA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending for council
meetings for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohomed
Faizal Mohomed Ashkar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of  that Number 10  Bulugohothenna if Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abdul Rasak Muhammadu Zarook to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer  of  the  Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do
hereby declare that Abdul Rasak Muhammadu Zarook was elected as member of the Number 10 - Bulugohothenna of
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning officer,
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
20th February, 2020.
