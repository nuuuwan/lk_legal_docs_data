Greetings!
As you have been appointed as members of the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs, established by the
Presidential Directive dated 31st March, 2020 exercising the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And whereas, I have, in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, appointed
new Cabinet of Ministers, State Ministers, Secretaries to Ministries, and Secretaries to State Ministries following the
Parliamentary Election held on 05th August, 2020,
And whereas, the Ministries of Higher Education, Technology, Innovation and Skills Development, Employment
and Labour Relations, no longer exists and the scope of these Ministries has been allocated to the Ministry of Education
and the State Ministry of Women and Child Development, Pre-School and Primary Education, School Infrastructure and
Education Services, the State Ministry of Education Reforms, Open University and Distance Learning Promotion, the
State Ministry of Skills Development, V ocational Education, Research and Innovation and the State Ministries of Dhamma
Schools, Bhikku Education, Piriven and Buddhist Universities which are newly established under the said Ministry of
Education and whereas I am of the view that the new Secretaries of the Ministry of Education and these State Ministries
should be appointed as members of the aforesaid Task Force,
Also, whereas, new Vice Chancellors have been appointed in place of Acting Vice Chancellors to the Universities
of Sri Jayewardenepura, Kelaniya, Jaffna, and Wayamba and a new Vice Chancellor has been appointed for the University
of Moratuwa,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the
powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, appoint the
below mentioned Secretary to the Ministry, Secretaries to State Ministries and Vice Chancellors of Universities as members
of the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs, established under the aforesaid Presidential Directive in place of the
members of the said Task Force referred to at 1, 2, 3, 7, 8, 9, 10 and 17, by reposing great trust, and confidence in their
prudence, ability and fidelity.
1. Prof. Kapila Perera Esquire
Secretary to the Ministry of Education
2. Mrs. K.M.D.S. Jayasekara
Secretary to the State Ministry of Women and Child Development, Pre-Schools and Primary Education,
School Infrastructure and Education Services
