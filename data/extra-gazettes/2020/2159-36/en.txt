Amended Sections:
10.3.1     VI. Should be an officer holding the post of Senior Superintendent of Survey.
11.2
Post Qualifications
Will be appointed by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Appointment of the next senior most officers in Special Grade of the
Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service other than the Surveyor General  to the
posts of Additional Surveyor General according to the existing number
of vacancies will be made by the Public Service Commission.
Appointment of the next  senior most officers in Special Grade of the
Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service other than the Surveyor General and the
Additional Surveyor Generals to the posts of Senior Deputy Surveyor
General according to the existing number of vacancies  will be made
by the Public Service Commission.
Appointment of the officers from among those in Special Grade of
the Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service to the post will be made by the Public
Service Commission.
Appointments of the senior most officers holding the posts of
Superintendent of Survey from among those in Grade I of the Sri Lanka
Surveyors’ Service to the posts of Senior Superintendent of Survey
according to the existing number of vacancies will be made by the
Public Service Commission (When appointments are made, priority
will be accorded to the officers who have been given acting/attending
to duties appointments.
Appointment of the next senior most officers in Grade I or Grade II of
the Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service other than the Senior Superintendents
of Survey to the posts of Superintendent of Survey according to  the
existing number of vacancies will be made by the Public Service
Commission.
(a) Surveyor General
(b) Additional Surveyor General
(c) Senior Deputy Surveyor General
(d) Deputy Surveyor General /
Provincial Surveyor General
(e) Senior Superintendent of Survey
(f) Superintendent of Survey
