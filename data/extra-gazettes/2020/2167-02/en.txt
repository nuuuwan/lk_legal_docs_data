PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS ACT, No 1 of 1981
Notice under Section 7 (4)(b)
THE list of Political Parties entitled to be recognized for the purpose of Elections is given in the Schedule A below in
terms of Section 7 (4)(b) of the Parliamentary Elections Act No.l of 1981.
Owing to the disputes regarding the office of the secretary, parties that cannot submit nominations as recognised
political parties for the Parliamentary Election scheduled for 25th April, 2020 are listed under Schedule B.
MAHINDA DESHAPRIYA N. J. ABEYESEKERE S. RATNAJEEV AN  H. HOOLE

SCHEDULE  B
The following recognised political parties that have disputes for the office of the secretary are not treated as recognised
political parties for the Parliamentary Election scheduled for 25th April, 2020.
68. Eksath Lanka Podujana Pakshaya
69. Eksath Lanka Maha Saba Party
70 Sri Lanka Progressive Front
The office of the secretary of the recognised political parties All Ceylon Makkal Congress listed under 4 and Eelavar
Democratic Front listed under 6 above are valid only for the Parliamentary Elections scheduled for 25th April, 2020.
