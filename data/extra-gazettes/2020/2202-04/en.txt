SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rate specified in the corresponding
entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  06 months commencing from  November 18, 2020, subject to provisions of
Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2197/12 of  13.10.2020 in respect of Canned Fish is rescinded with effect from November 18, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
17th November, 2020.
