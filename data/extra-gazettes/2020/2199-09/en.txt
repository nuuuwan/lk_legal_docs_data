CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification of  Hon. Governor of the Central Province
BY  virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 184(1)(1A) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) to be read
with Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage, Attorney-at-Law
and Governor of the Central Province do hereby appoint retired judicial officer Mrs. Sarojini Kusala Weerawardena to inquire
and report within 3 months time from the dated, whether the Chairman, Urban Council Thalawakele Lindula has committed
any misdeeds described in Section 184(1) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255).
This officer shall have the Powers of a Commission appointed under the Inquiry of Commission Act, pertaining to
this inquiry. ·
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE ,
Attorney-at-Law,
 Governor,
Central  Province.
Kandy,
26th October, 2020.

Notification of  Hon. Governor of the Central Province
The Order made under Section 184(1) (1B) (a) (i) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255), to be read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989.
By virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 184(1) (1B) (a) (i) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter
255), to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consquential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage,
Attorney-at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby temporarily suspend Mr. Anagipura Ashoka Sepala,
Chairman of the Thalawakele - Lindula Urban Council from holding his post with immediate effect.
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 184(1) (1B) (a) (i) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter
255), to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage,
Attorney-at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby direct Mr. Lechchuman Bharathidasan presently the Vice
Chairman of the Thalawakele - Lindula Urban Council, Nuwaraeliya District, to perform the duties and use of powers of the
Chairman of the Thalawakele - Lindula Urban Council, Nuwaraeliya District of the Central Province with immediate effect.
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE ,
Attorney-at-Law,
 Governor,
Central  Province.
Kandy,
26th October, 2020.
