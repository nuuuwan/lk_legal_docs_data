THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Revocation order under Section 39 (1)
BY the Order made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the
Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) and published in the
Gazette Extraordinary  No.  2028/46  dated 20.07.2017  and the
land specified more fully in the Order No. 169 of 2017 dated
17.07.2017  amended by the correction paper published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2033/5  dated 21.08.2017 was vested
in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. But the
possession of the said land has not yet been taken for and on
behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Therefore, I  Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 39 (1) of the land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke
the land specified morefully in the aforesaid Order No. 169 of
2017  dated 17.07.2017  published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 2028/46  dated  20.07.2017.
S.M. Chandrasena,
Minister of  Lands.
