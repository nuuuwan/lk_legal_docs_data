FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   DELFT   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a Member of
the Party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Philippu
Pattric  Roshan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that 04 - Delft 04 of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Michel Pillei Anthonippillei to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Delft Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Michel Pillei
Anthonippillei was selected as the member of the 04 - Delft 04 of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
22nd  May, 2020.
