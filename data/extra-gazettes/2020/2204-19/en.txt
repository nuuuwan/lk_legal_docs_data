Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - G  33302— 30 (12/2020)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A(1)(a)   OF  THE  LOCAL AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262)  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Selvaratnam Chandrakumar of the Muthur Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the Tamil United
Liberation Front.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
