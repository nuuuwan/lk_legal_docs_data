WHEREAS the office of return member of Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Kanthasamy Rony Prinson the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abraham George Pillei to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abraham George Pillei was
selected as the member of the Batticaloa Municipal Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
07th December, 2020.
