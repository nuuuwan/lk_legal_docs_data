PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local  Authority Elections Ordinance Notices
1A - PG 5227— 25 (12/2020)
NOTICE  UNDER  THE  SECTION 66(C) TO BE READ WITH SECTION (G) OF THE
AMENDED  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  OF  THE  LOCAL
GOVERNMENT  ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)  ORDINANCE  N O.  22 OF  2012
I, M. M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province hereby announce that in accordance with the
powers vested in me under the Section 66(c) to be read with Section (G) of the Amended Local Government Elections
Ordinance of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Ordinance No. 22 of 2012, the meeting to elect a new Vice
Chairman to fill the vacant post of Vice Chairman of the Haldumulla Pradeshiya Sabha located in Uva Province, will be
convened on the 08th of January 2021 at 10.30 am at the Haldumulla Pradeshiya Sabha Council Chamber, Haldumulla.
M. M. W
IJENAYAKA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Uva Province.
At the Department of Local Government,
Martin Silva Mawatha,
Badulla,
24th December, 2020.
