The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Dimbulagala  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a)
of the (chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Herath Mudiyanselage Lahiru Priyadarshana of the
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be
a member of the United National Party.
P
IUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
29th October, 2020.
