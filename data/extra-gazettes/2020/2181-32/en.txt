FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   V A VUNIYA   NORTH   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the T amil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Thureirasa Thamilchelvam the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Paramasivam Barath to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya North  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Paramasivam Barath  was selected as the member of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH  ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
26th June, 2020.
