FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Colombo Municipal  Council has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member
of the party ;
AND  WHEREAS  the secretary  of the  United  National  Party, the  recognized  political party  to  which  Nobert Nerius
Fernando the  member  belonged,  was  required  by  me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon the  member of that No. 09 - Bluemendhal of Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  elected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mohomed Prem Naseer Mohomed Irfan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Mohomed Prem Naseer Mohomed Irfan was elected as the member of the No. 09 - Bluemendhal of Colombo
Municipal  Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
06th February, 2020.
