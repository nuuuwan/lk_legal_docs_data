FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KURUNEGALA    MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kurunegala Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Lakmini Priyadarshika Wickramasinghe,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Disanayake Mudiyanselage Nadeeshani Sugandhika to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala Municipal Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Disanayake
Mudiyanselage Nadeeshani Sugandhika was selected as the member of  the  Kurunegala Municipal Council.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
3rd December, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 04.12.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     TRINCOMALEE    URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Trincomalee Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which  Mohamed
Mustaffa Mohamed Mukthar,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of  All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Raufu Faizer to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Trincomalee Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Raufu Faizer
was selected as the member of  the  Trincomalee Urban Council.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
03rd December, 2020.
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MEERIGAMA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Gamaralalage
Kanthi Sunethra Samarakoon,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of  Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Karunarathna Pathiranage Samankumari Jayathilaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Meerigama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Karunarathna Pathiranage Samankumari Jayathilaka was selected as the member of  the  Meerigama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
03rd  December, 2020.
