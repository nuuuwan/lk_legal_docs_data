FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   ERA VUR   PATTU
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member in Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Vijayarethinam Jeyaganesh the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member if Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Nagenthiran Mohanarajan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Nagenthiran
Mohanarajan was elected as the member of the  Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning officer,
Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
06th March, 2020.
