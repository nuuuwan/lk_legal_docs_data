SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT,  No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, i, Mahinda 
rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and policy Development of the Democratic socialist republic of sri lanka, do 
by this order impose in respect of the commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy 
at the rate specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
this Order shall be valid for a period of 03 months commencing from April 10, 2020 subject to provisions of sub section 
(3) of section 2 of the special Commodity levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
the Order made under section 2 of the special Commodity levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2160/63 of 01.02.2020 in respect of Vegetable oils (HS Headings 15.07, l5.11, 15.12, 15.13) are 
rescinded with effect from April 10,  2020.
 
Mahinda Rajapaksa,
 Minister of Finance, Economic and policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and policy Development,
Colombo 01,
April 09, 2020.
