No. 2197/26 –  FRIDAY, OCTOBER  16,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
                                                                                                
Reference  No.:  04/07/31/08. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The Allotment of land containing in extent of 8.0323 
Hectare, of Lot 1 at the Preliminary Plan No. 4745 of 
Superintendent of Survey Kalutara, situated in the Village 
called Raigam Estate in Raigam Korala Udugahapaththu 
coming withing the area of Ingiriya Divisional Secretariat 
in the District of Kalutara, Western Province and  
bounded as follows: 
 
Lot No. 1 
Name of the land: Raigam Estate; Description: Scrub; 
Name of Claimant: State; Extent: 8.0323 Hectares. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/Lands  on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Ingiriya on 
the 17th day of December, 2020 at 10.00 a. m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 11th day of December, 2020  the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount,  
 
 
   
 IRANGANIE  WEERASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ingiriya. 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Ingiriya, 
13th October, 2020.
