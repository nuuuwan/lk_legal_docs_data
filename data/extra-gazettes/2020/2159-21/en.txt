Notification
IT is hereby notified that the subjects, functions, Departments, Public Corporations and Statutory Institutions assigned to
the state ministers of the Ministry of Environment and Wildlife Resources in terms of the Article 44(5) of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka will be as mentioned in the schedule given below from the date of this
notification.
