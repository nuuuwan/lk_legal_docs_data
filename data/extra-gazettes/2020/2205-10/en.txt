LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands do
hereby give notice that it intends to acquire the following lands that
necessary for a public purpose under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act.
Details of Allotment of following lands shown descriptively by
names and extents in Surveyor General’s Advance Tracing No.
Ma/LGG/2012/239 dated 30.04.2013 situated at Attanwala village in
the Laggala Udasiyapattu Minor Division of Laggala Pallegama DS
Division, Matale District of Central Province and bounded as follows:–
01. Lot A – Name of the Land - Old Thelgamuwa Watta -
Extent: 7.4386 Hectares
North : Thelgama Oya and Canal; East : Canal and Forest claimed
by the Government; South : Road and Lot B; West : Lot B and
Thelgama Oya.
02. Lot B – Name of the Land - Old Thelgamuwa Watta -
Extent: 10.2085 Hectares
North : Thelgama Oya and Lot A; East : Lot A and Road; South:
Road, Canal and Forest claimed by the Government; West : Thelgama
Oya.
S. M. C
HANDRASENA,
Minister of Lands.
