THE  CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified by me, as the Cabinet Minister in charge of the subject of Transport Services Management, in terms
of the Article 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that assignment of subjects and
functions for State Minister of Transport Services Management and State Minister of Railway Services, appointed under
the Article 44(5) of the Constitution, shall come into effect immediately under my supervision and guidance as listed in
the following Schedule I and II respectively.
M
AHINDA  A MARAWEERA,
 Minister of Transport Services Management.
08th January, 2020.
