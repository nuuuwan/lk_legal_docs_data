CONSUMER    AFFAIRS    AUTHORITY   ACT,  No. 09 OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no Medical Test Laboratory shall charge, or levy any charges or fees directly or indirectly for
conducting of the Laboratory Tests specified hereunder, above the Maximum charge or fee specified therein :
Type of Laboratory Test Maximum
Charge/Fee
NS1 Antigen Test for Dengue Rs. 1,200/- per
Test and Report
Full Blood Count (FBC) Test Rs. 400/- per
Test and Report
This Order shall come into effect from 29th July, 2020.
                                                                              By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
