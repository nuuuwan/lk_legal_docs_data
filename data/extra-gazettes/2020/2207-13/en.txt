IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Monetary Board established under the Monetary Law Act
(Chapter 422), in terms of Section 30 of the Debt Recovery (Special Provisions) Act, No. 02 of 1990,  the Monetary Board
has, having regard to the current rates of bank interest, determined that with effect from 01st January 2021, the Market Rate
of Interest for the purpose of this Act, shall be ten point one two per cent (10.12%) per annum.
Prof. W. D. LAKSHMAN,
Chairman of the Monetary Board and Governor,
Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
Colombo,
23rd December, 2020.
