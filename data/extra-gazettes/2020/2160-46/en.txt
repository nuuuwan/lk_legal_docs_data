The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BINGIRIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which W. P.
Karunarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of Peoples Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate R. L. T. Bhashana Abewardhana to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of   Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that R. L. T.
Bhashana Abewardhana was selected as the member of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
31st  January, 2020.
