FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PANNALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Pathirannehelage Chandrapala the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of
Independent Group  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the  aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Wickramasinghe Mudiyanselage Chaminda Wickramasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities  Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wickramasinghe
Mudiyanselage Chaminda Wickramasinghe was selected as the member of   Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
1st July, 2020.
