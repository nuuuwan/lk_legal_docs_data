No. 2203/28 -  THURSDAY  NOVEMBER  26,  2020
Notification to be made under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment T ax Ordinace bearing
No. 12 of 1946 which shall be read together with the Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act bearing No. 12 of 1989
I, the Governor of the Western Province, by virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of the provisions of the Section 2 (2) of
the Entertainment Tax Ordinance bearing No. 12 of 1946 which shall be read together with the Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act bearing No. 12 of 1989, do hereby notify that the proposal, passed by the Colombo
Municipal Council on 30.07.2020 as per the provisions mentioned in the Section 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance
bearing No. 12 of 1946 to levy for the years of 2021 and 2022 having imposed as is described in the following Schedule the
percentage which shall be charged on the Tax from the admission fee chargeable for entertainment activities declared in the
Entertainment Tax Ordinance bearing No. 12 of  1946 amended by the Entertainment Tax (amendment) Act bearing No. 37 of
1984, was approved.
