SPORTS   LAW   No.   25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Sports under the Section 41 and read with the Section 31 of the Sports Law No. 25
of 1973.
NAMAL   RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of Youth and Sports.
At Colombo,
on 05th day of November, 2020.
Regulations
Regulations No. 01 of 2016 of the National Association of Sports published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 1990/23 dated 27th October 2016 amended by the regulations of the National Association of Sports published in the
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2173/13 dated 30.04.2020 are amended as follows only in relevance to the year 2020 :-
01. “31st day of May” under the provisions related to the constitution of the National Associations/ Federations of
Sports in the Section (a) of the Schedule III published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th
October 2016 is repealed and the “30th day of November ” is inserted ;
02. The Section (a) of the Schedule III under the provisions related to the constitution of the National Associations/
Federations of Sports published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th October 2016 come into
force will again be effective from the 1st day of January, 2021 ;
03. The Extraordinary Gazette No. 2173/13 dated 30.04.2020 is hereby cancelled.
