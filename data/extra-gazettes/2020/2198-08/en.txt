DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  -  EASTERN   PROVINCE
Notice on summon meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment)
Act, No. 22 of 2012
By virtue of  power vested on me under Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections as amended by Act, No. 22 of 2012,
I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting
to fill the vacancies arose as a result of resigning the post of Vice Chairman of the Trincomalee Town & Gravets
Pradeshiya Sabha, Trincomalee District, Eastern Province at 10.00 a. m. on 2020.11.03 at Trincomalee Town & Gravets
Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall for the purpose of elect the new Vice Chairman.
N. M
ANIV ANNA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, V arothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
20th of October, 2020.
