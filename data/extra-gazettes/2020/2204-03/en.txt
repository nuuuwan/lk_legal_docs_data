THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
AS AMENDED BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/76.
Land Ministry's  Ref. No.:  4-3/9/2018/WS/48.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for the construction of Rattota Water
Purification Project of Grater Matale Water Supply Project.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :
Allotments of land, described in bellow in Plan No. Ma
1824, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of Surveys
situated in Maussagolla Village, Maussagolla Grama
Niladhari Division, in the Rattota Divisional Secretariat
Division, in Matale District, in Central Province.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Esweddume Watta; Description of  Land:
Open waste land; Claimant: Mr. D. G. P . Samaranayake &
Others; Extent: 0.0681 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Esweddume Watta; Description of
Land: Open waste land; Claimant: Mr. D. G . P. Samaranayake
& Others; Extent: 0.0531 Hectare.
''All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat, Rattota on the
28th December 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th
December 2020 the nature of their interests in the Land the
particulars of their claims for compensation for acquisition of
the said land, the amount of compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount".
H. M. N. D
INIPRIYA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rottota.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Rattota.
12th November, 2020.
