BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 63 of the Coconut Development Act, No. 46 of 1971, I, Ramesh Pathirana, Minister of
Plantations, do by this Order, specify that Kithul shall be a palm for the purpose of Section 63 and any reference in Sections 20, 22, 23, 30, 44,
45, 46, 48, 49, 50, 51 and 62 to Coconut shall be construed as including a reference to Kithul.
RAMESH PATHIRANA,
Minister of Plantations.
Ministry of Plantation,
Battaramulla,
07th December, 2020.
