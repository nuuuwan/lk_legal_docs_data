 1. I do hereby grant permission unto the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the 
Province to spend Rupees Thirty Three Billion Six Hundred and Sixty Six Million One 
Hundred and Eighty Two Thousand  only, (Rs.33,666,182,000/ -) and which estimated for the 
services of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the one year duration begining on 01 st 
January, 2020  and ending on 31 st December, 2020, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
section 27(A)  of Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987 as the term of the  Sixth 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council ended on 26th September, 2017. 
 
 2. (1).  Without prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council which estimate will be Rupees Thirty Three Billion Six Hundred and 
Sixty Six Million One Hundred and Eighty Two Thousand  only, (Rs.33,666,182,000/ -)  
for the service of the period begining on  01 st January 2020 and ending on 31 st 
December, 2020, in this order referred to as the “one year” shall be met. 
 
 (a). For payments which are hereby authorized to be made out of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council fund or monies of or advance at the disposal of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council and, 
 
 (b). For the proceeds of loans which are hereby authorized to be raised, within the 
province of Sabaragamuwa for and on behalf of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council.  The sum of Rupees Thirty Three Billion Six Hundred and Sixty Six 
Million One Hundred and Eighty Two Thousand  only, (Rs.33,666,182,000/ -)  
here in before referred to may be duly expended as specified in the first schedule 
to this order. 
 
 (2). The provision of subsection (1) of this section shall have effect without prejudice to the 
provisions of any other written law authorizing the raising of loans for and on behalf of 
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council. 
 
 3. (1). The receipts of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council during the financial year 2020 
from each activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall be 
credited to the account of such activity, the aggregate of receipts so credited shall be not 
less than the minimum limit specified  in the corresponding entry in column  (vi) of that 
schedule. 
  
   
  
 4. The expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, during the financial year 
2020 each activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall be paid 
out of the receipt of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council from such expenditure shall not 
exceed the maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry in column (v) of that 
schedule. 
 
 5. The debit balance, outstanding at the end of the financial year 2020 of any activity specified 
in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall not exceed the maximum limit 
specified in the corresponding entry in column (vii) of that schedule, and the total liabilities of 
that activity  at the end of that financial year shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in 
the corresponding entry column (viii) of that schedule. 
 
 6. Whenever at any time during the financial year 2020, the receipts of that Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council from any activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this 
order are insufficient to meet the expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council on such activity, the governor may, from time to time, by order direct that such sums 
may deem necessary to meet such expenditure shall be payable by way of advances out of 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council fund or any other fund or monies of or at the disposal of,

I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council to spend a sum of Rupees Thirty Three Billion Six Hundred and Sixty Six Million One Hundred and Eighty 
Two Thousand  only, (Rs.33,666,182,000/-) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund, which is estimated for 
the expenses of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the one year period that starts on 01 st of January, 2020  and 
end  on 31st of December, 2020,  by virtue of powers vested in me  under Section  27 (a)  of the Provincial Council Act 
No. 42 of  1987,  as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri 
Lanka ended on  26th September, 2017. 
 
This permission will be valid  till  31 st of December, 2020  or  upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of 
summoning  the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  or  from the date  re-starts the functions of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council  which occurs first. 
 
This estimate has been prepared based on the amount authorized on 28 th May, 2020 by the Hon. Governor of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the expenses of the first eight month of the year 2020, allocations received from 
the general treasury for the expenses from 01st September, 2020 to 31st December, 2020, estimated revenue for the year 
2020 and the first supplementary estimate of the year 2020. 
 
I also emphasize that the expenditure incurred by the revenue expenditure estimates authorized by me  on 28 th May 
2020 for the expenses of the first eight months of the year 2020 and the first supplementary estimat e authorized by me 
on 19th August 2020, should be covered by the provisions set forth in this permission. 
 
 
 TIKIRI KOBBEKADUWA, 
 Honourable Governor – Sabaragamuwa Province 
 
29 August, 2020.
