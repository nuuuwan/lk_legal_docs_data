FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MUTHUR   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Muhammadu Masthan Aalim Muhammadu Nazeer the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member if National Front for Good Governance  who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Latheef Muhammadu Ahsan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Latheef Muhammadu
Ahsan  was selected as the member of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee
22nd May, 2020.
