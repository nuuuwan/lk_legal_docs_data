LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
Divisional Secretary Ref. No.: Hangu/Div.Sec/3/2/1/495C II.
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.: 13/97/E/456.
I hereby inform, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the land/servitude described below which is
required for the purpose of a development activity of CP/HU/
Diyathilaka Central College.
Description of the Land dependence to be acquired:
An allotment of land called as Pallemaluwa in Extent of 02
Acre., 02 Rood and 18.7 Perches (1.0591 Hectare) depicted
as Lot No. 01 in Preliminary Plan No. P.P .N. 3398 situated
in the village called Hanguranketha in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 495C Pallemaluwa of Diyathilaka Korale in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Hanguranketha of
Nuwara Eliya District described as follows :-
Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Pallemaluwa; Names and address of the
claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 02 Acre, 02 Rood,
18.7 Perches.
All those who make claims or other connections to the
above-named land, must be present before me by themselves
or by the persons who have been authorized in writing at the
Hanguranketha Divisional Secretariat Office at 9.30 a.m. on
the day of 24th July 2020.
Further, they must communicate to me in duplicate the nature
of their title/ownership to this land, the details of claiming
for compensation for this land, the amount claimed, and the
method of calculating such amount, before 15.07.2020. The
deeds for claims have to be forwarded to at the interview.
I. M. S
ENANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Hanguranketha.
Divisional Secretary,
Hanguranketha,
01st July, 2020.
