The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is

THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
(1956  REVISED  EDITION)
     M r .  P . D. P. Sisila Kumara, No. B-12, 500 Houses - Scheme, Kiriwandala, Puwakpitiya, Avissawella.
 and
      Ceylon Electricity Board, No. 644, Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Ethul - Kotte.
STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. P. D. P. Sisila Kumara, had been caused injustice b y being the only person not to be given relief from his 
punishments, when 6 out of the 7 persons who worked in the Ceylon Electricity Board against whom a disciplinary inquiry 
was conducted with regard to the death of employee S. V . S. Rathnasiri and accident of employee Hemal Jagath Kumara 
that had occurred while being engaged in a 33,000 high voltage maintenance duty in the Customer Service Centre area of 
Ingiriya on 31.01.2013 had been given relief following their appeal against the punishments given and if such injustice had 
been caused, to what relief he is entitled.
 A. Wංආൺඅൺඐൾൾඋൺ,
 Commissioner General of Labour.
