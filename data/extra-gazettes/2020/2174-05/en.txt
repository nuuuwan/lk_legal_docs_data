PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA - 06.05.2020
3A
Greetings !
As you have been appointed as the members of the Task Force for Economic Revival and Poverty
Alleviation, established by the Presidential Directive dated 21st of April 2020 in terms of powers vested in me by Article
33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And, while considering about the facts presented by Mr. Mahesh Amalean it is appropriate to appoint a person
with vast experience in working with public sector to represent his field in the member composition, in view of the role
assigned to this Task Force,
 
I appoint MAS Holdings Vice Chairman Mr. Sherad Amalean as a member of the task force which had been
established in terms of the aforesaid Presidential Directive instead of MAS Holdings Chairman Mr. Mahesh Amalean who
had appointed as a member of said task force previously.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 06
th day of May Two
Thousand and Twenty.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P. B. JAYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
