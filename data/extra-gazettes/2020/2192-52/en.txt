FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KALUTARA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Rammuni
Samarasena Silva, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the returned member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ranasinghe Arachchige Sameera Madushanka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Kalutara
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Ranasinghe Arachchige Sameera Madushanka was selected as the member of  the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
09th  September, 2020.
