FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   BADALKUMBURA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Badalkumbura Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
three consecutive meetings without permission  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Attanayaka
Mudiyanselage Udeshika Madhushani the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of United National Party, who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudiyanselage Somawathi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Badalkumbura Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Herath Mudiyanselage
Somawathi was selected as the member of  the Badalkumbura Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA GAYAN PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Badalkumbura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Monaragala,
07th July, 2020.
