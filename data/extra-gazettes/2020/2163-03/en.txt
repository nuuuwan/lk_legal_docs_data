FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   HARISPATTUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which
Rankoth Gedara Aruna Janitha Amarasinghe the  member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon   the member of  that Number 01 Medawala of Haripattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Denigama Sumangala Thero to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the
Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262)  do hereby declare that Denigama Sumangala Thero was elected as the member of the Number 01 Medawala of
Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning officer,
Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
13th of February, 2020.
