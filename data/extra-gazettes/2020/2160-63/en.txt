SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do by this order impose in respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy at the rates specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from February 02, 2020 subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Orders made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2147/74 of 01.11.2019 in respect of B'Onions (HS Code 0703.10.20), the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2135/54 of 08.08.2019 in respect of Maize (HS Code 1005.90), the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2148/37 of 08.11.2019 in respect of Dates (HS Code 0804.10), Oranges (HS Code 0805.10.10), Apples
(HS Code 0808.10) and Vegetable oils (HS Headings 15.07,15.11,15.12,15.13) are rescinded with effect from February 02, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and
Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
February 01, 2020.
