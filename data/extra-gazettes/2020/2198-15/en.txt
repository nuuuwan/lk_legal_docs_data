FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NUWARAELIYA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ahangama
Ramanayakalage Ajith Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of that No. 09 Magastota of Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council  who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Keppetigodage Vindhya Hansani to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pulasthi Serasingha Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Keppetigodage Vindhya Hansani was elected as the member of  the  No. 09 Magastota of Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council.
P
ULASTHI SERASINGHA PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
19th  October, 2020.
