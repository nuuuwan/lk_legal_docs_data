FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   DAMANA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Kande Gedara Sudeera Wijayathilaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Numer 3 Padagoda of Damana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  elected  as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Meragal Pedige Nimal Jayarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Meragal Pedige Nimal Jayarathna was elected as the member of the No. 3 Padagoda of Damana Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Damana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
27th January, 2020.
