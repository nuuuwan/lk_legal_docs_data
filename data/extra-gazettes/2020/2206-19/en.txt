STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Project Act, No. 14 of 2008
as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, further to the Order published under Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/22 dated 19
th December 2012, as amended by the subsequent Orders published under
Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/7 dated 04th July 2016, No. 2049/59 dated 15th December
2017 and No. 2062/47 dated 16th March 2018 in respect of SINOLANKA   HOTELS  &   SPA   (PRIV ATE)   LIMITED, do by this
Order declare that,
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/22 dated 19th December 2012, as amended
by the subsequent Orders published under Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinay No. 1974/7
dated 04th July 2016, No. 2049/59 dated 15th December 2017 and No. 2062/47 dated 16th March 2018 is hereby further
amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct and operate a 475 roomed city hotel with 90 serviced Apartments at No. 116,
Galle Road, Colombo 03 in the Western Province” shall commence commercial operations within a period of one
hundred and twenty five (125) months from the date of the Project Agreement signed with the Board of Investment
of Sri Lanka on the 19
th of July 2012 and ending on the 31st December 2022.”
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
18th December, 2020,
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01.
