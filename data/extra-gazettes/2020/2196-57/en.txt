FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    YATAWATTA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member of  Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which Yatawara Gedara Wijitha Bandara Dorakumbura  the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of
Independent Gorup  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Siththaiya
Saundaranayagam to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Yatawatta Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Siththaiya Saundaranayagam was selected as the member of  the Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
09th  October, 2020,
District Election Office,
Matale.
