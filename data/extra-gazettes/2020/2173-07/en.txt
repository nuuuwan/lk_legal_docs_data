Greetings!
As you have been appointed as Members of the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs, established by
Presidential Directive dated 31 st March, 2020 by exercising the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And as it is necessary to carry out primary, secondary and tertiary education continuously without any disruption
in the current situation of Covid-19 pandemic, and also to identify and implement required strategies for innovations in
the field of education,
And further considering the need for Information Technological interventions, especially for appropriate subject
syllabuses in school education, examinations etc. and for distance learning strategies,

PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA - 28.04.2020
3A
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, appoint Dr. Sunil Jayantha
Navaratne Esquire, Director General of National Institute of Education, B. Sanath Pujitha Esquire, Commissioner
General of Examinations and P.N. Ailapperuma Esquire, Director (Information Technology) of Ministry of
Education as Members of the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs which has been established in terms of the
aforesaid Presidential Directive with great devotion and trust in their prudence, excellence and confidence by exercising
the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of Sri Lanka.
 
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 27th day of April Two
Thousand and Twenty.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
