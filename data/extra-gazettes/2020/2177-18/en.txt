LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: DHO/LAN/ASECURITYN/A/2/44.
Lands and Lands Development Ref.  No.:
4-3/13/2010/HA/241.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act that government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Allotments of land described below and depicted in
Preliminary plan No. K/3269 and dated 18.01.2019 situated in
the Village of Eriyagolla in Grama Niladhari Division of
No. 119 B, Dehiowita Minor Division of Atulugam Korale in
Divisional Secretary's Division of Dehiowita in Kegalle District
in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded ;
01. Lot No. 1
Name of Land: Chariot Gardens alias  Hettiyawatta ;
Description of Land; A garden, 06 Permanent buildings and a
mixed plantation; Claimants: Land Reform Commission;  Extent:
0.7699 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby, required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Dehiowita on day of 30th of April, 2020 at 11.00 a.m.
Further, it is hereby notified me in writing (in duplicated)
before 16th day of April, 2020 regarding the nature of their
interests in the land/lands, particulars of their claims for
compensation for acquisition of the said land/servitude, the
amount of compensation and the details of computation of
such amount. Deeds and other documents for claims should
be submitted at the examination date.
U. P. CHANDANA ANURUDDHA PIYADASA,
Divisional Secretary,
Dehiowita.
At Divisional Secretariat, Dehiowita,
11th March, 2020.
