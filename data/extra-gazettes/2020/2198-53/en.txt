THE    STATE    AGRICULTURE   CORPORATIONS    ACT,    No.   11   OF   1972
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972, I, Mahindananda
Aluthgamage, Minister of Agriculture, with the concurrence of the Minister to whom the Subjects of Finance and planning
are assigned, do by this Order, amend the Order made under the aforesaid Section and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 302/12 of  01st February, 1, 1978, as last amended by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2026/45 of July 7, 2017, by the repeal of
paragraph (5) thereof and the substitution therefor of the following paragraph :
“ (5) declare that the Principal Place of Business of the corporation shall be Kandy.”
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
