SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by
this order impose in respect of the  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at
the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  06 months commencing from  June 17, 2020, subject to provisions of Sub
section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
June 16, 2020.
