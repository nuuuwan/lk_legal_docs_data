FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     CHA V AKACHCHERI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which  Kandaiya
Thurkka,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Niranjan Kalaivani to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Niranjan
Kalaivani  was selected as the member of  the  Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
24th  December, 2020.
