THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.

indicated in the Progressive Tracing No. CO/HMG/2019/388 and
dated 26.08.2019 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Magammana in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 590/A of
Magammana West in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Homagama in Colombo District in Western Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ambagahalanda, Atambagahalanda and
Moragahalanda – Extent : 0.0368 Hectare
North: Cemented ditch and Ambagahalanda, Atambagahalanda
and Moragahalanda claimed by P. A. S. Kumarathilaka; East:
Road (R.D.A.); South: Lot B; West: Lot B and Cemented ditch.
