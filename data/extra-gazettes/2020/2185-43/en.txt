FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MEDADUMBARA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Medadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Subramaniyam Mohan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sihabudeen Aleem Sahib Ubaidulla to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal  Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the Medadumbara
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Sihabudeen Aleem Sahib Ubaidulla was selected as the member of  the Medadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Medadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Kandy,
21st July, 2020.
