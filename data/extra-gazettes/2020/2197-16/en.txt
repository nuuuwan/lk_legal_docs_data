STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mrs. P. Seethadevi who had worked at Perth Estate owned by Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation had been caused
injustice by termination of her employment from 31.12.2017 without compensation, in spite of the written agreement made by
the Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation to pay a compensation of Rs. 260,000 for termination of employment and if such
injustice had been caused, to what relief she is entitled.
