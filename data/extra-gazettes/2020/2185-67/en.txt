1A- PG  5070 — 217  (07/2020)
Direction No. 72
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.   09   of   2003
Direction under Section  10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no manufacturer, trader or distributor  shall sell, expose or offer for sale, store,
transport, distribute or buy or purchase any locally produced rice or paddy directly for the manufacture of animal feed or
otherwise as an ingredient for the manufacture of animal feed.
This Direction shall come into effect from 23rd July, 2020.
                                                                        By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
M
AJ. GEN. SHANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
23rd July, 2020.
