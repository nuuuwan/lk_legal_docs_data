FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PUTTALAM   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Puttalam Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Front For Good Governance, the recognized political party to
which Ahamad Kabeer Siththi Saleema the member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  Puttalam Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Hameed Neina Marikkar Mohamad Sifak to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Puttalam Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hameed Neina Marikkar
Mohamad Sifak was selected as the member of   the Puttalam Urban Council.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
08th July, 2020.
