THE  ORDER  MADE  UNDER   THE   SECTION  10 OF  THE  ASSISTED   SCHOOLS  AND   TRAINING
COLLEGES  (SUPPLEMENTARY  PROVISION)  ACT,  No.  8  OF  1961
J/Kaithady No. 02 Lanka Sabha Mixed Tamil School
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Section 10 of the Assisted Schools and Training Colleges (Supplementary
Provision) Act, No. 08 of 1961, I, Dullas Alahapperuma, Minister of Education, do hereby repeal the land area described
in the Vesting Order No. 893 published  in the Ceylon Government  Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 12,979 dated 09.03.1962
as only up to an extent of 04 Latchams is applied thereto with effect from 01st February, 2020.
D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA ,
Minister of Education.
