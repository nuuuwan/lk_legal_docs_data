UV A  PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments by the Governor of Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32 (1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Raja Collure -
Attorney At Law, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me:
01. Mr. W. M. M. G . Abeysinghe Banda, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as the
Secretary to the Ministry of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure, Co - operative
Affairs and Tamil Education of Uva Province, with effect from the date 27.12.2019.
02. Mr. W. M. M. G. Abeysinghe Banda, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as the Acting
Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel & Training) of Uva Province, in addition to the duties of his permanent post
with effect from the date 27.12.2019.
03. Mr. S. E. R. T. M. S. P. Bandara, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Deputy Chief
Secretary (Personnel & Training) of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 29.01.2020).
04. Mrs. V . I. P. Ranasiri, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, as Director (Planning) of the office of
Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning) of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 10.02.2020).
