PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
G 31781 - 17  (2020/01) 
LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  AS  AMENDED  BY  
LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)   
ACT  No. 28  OF  1964  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini's Reference No.: PLDS/LND/04/ACQ/92. 
  
My Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/362. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following 
land. 
 
An allotment of land depicted as Lot No. 1198 in sheet 
No. 56 of supplement No. 59 of F.V.P 34 situated in the 
Grama Niladhari Division No. 170 of Panawenna of 
Nawadunkorale Udapattuwa in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Pelmadulla in Ratnapura District of 
Sabaragamuwa Province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
Lot No. 1198 - Extent: 13.3546 Hectares (Thirteen 
point three five four six). 
 
 North: Lot No. 71M; East: Lot No. 71M and Road 
(Pradeshiya Sabha); South: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) Lot 
No. 71M, Lot No. 107, Lot No. 102, Lot No. 71G; West: 
Lot No. 71M, Lot No. 107, Lot No. 102 and Lot No. 
71G. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Pelmadulla 
Divisional Secretariat on 13th May, 2020 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 13.05.2020 the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
  
 B. S. G. RUWANSIRI, 
 Divisional Secretary/Acquiring officer, 
 Pelmadulla. 
  
Divisional Secretariat,  
Pelmadulla, 
08th January, 2020.
