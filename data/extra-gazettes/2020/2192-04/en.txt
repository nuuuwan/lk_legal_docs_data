The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE SRI JAYAWARDHANAPURA KOTTE
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION 66A (1) (a)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Elected
as a member of Parliament ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Ovitigala
Vithanage Madhura Vithanage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that No. 06 - Bandaranayakepura of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal
Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Madhuka Weerasingha Gunasekara to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, returning officer of the Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Madhuka Weerasingha Gunasekara was elected as the member of the No. 06 - Bandaranayakepura of
Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE
Returning Officer,
Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City.
07th September, 2020.
