FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   AMBALANTOTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council  meetings period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Miyuru
Sampath Rajapaksha the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Parana Liyanage Pemasiri to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Parana Liyanage
Pemasiri was selected as the member of the Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
A VINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambantota,
10th June, 2020.
