The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MINIPE    PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of the
party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  Egoda
Walawwe Sanath Bandara Ekanayaka,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudiyanselage Nilantha Kumara Wijebandara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer  of  the Minipe
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Herath Mudiyanselage Nilantha Kumara Wijebandara was selected as the member of  the  Minipe Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
30th  November, 2020.
