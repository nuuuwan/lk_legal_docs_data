The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KEBITHIGOLLEWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kebithigollewa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Dodanduwa Waduge Ananda Wijesiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member if  People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kotanage Jayamini Kumarathunga to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Kebithigollewa Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
that Kotanage Jayamini Kumarathunga was selected as the member of the Kebithigollewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning officer,
Kebithigollewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
17th February, 2020.
