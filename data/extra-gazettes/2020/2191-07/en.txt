ESTABLISHMENT  OF   DEPARTMENT   OF   INDUSTRIAL   DEVELOPMENT   (NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE)
THE Department of Industrial Development (North Western Province) is established since 01.05.2019 by amalgamating
the Department of Small Industries (North Western Province) and Department of Textiles (North Western Province) on
adoption of the resolution by the North Western Province Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated
03.08.2018, and on approval of the Hon. Governor of North Western Province dated 10.08.2018.
02. There shall not be prejudicial effect over any legal activity prevailed in the name of Department of Small Industries
(North Western Province) and Department of Textiles (North Western Province) since 01.05.2019, the effective date of this
imposition.
A. J. M. M
USAMMIL,
Hon. Governor,
(North Western Province).
09th July, 2020.
