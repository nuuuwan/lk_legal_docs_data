FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NIVITIGALA     PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the  member of  Nivitigala  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which
Madurasingha Mohottalage Rathnathilaka Nilame the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that  14  Delwala of  Nivitigala Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Watupolagedara Inoka Priyadharshani Dayananda to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Nivitigala  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Watupolagedara Inoka Priyadharshani Dayananda was elected as the member of  the 14  Delwala of Nivitigala  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Nivitigala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
11th  November, 2020.
