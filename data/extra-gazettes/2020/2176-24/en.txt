PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - G  32257— 27 (05/2020)
 NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A(1)a OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of the Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Sundaram Wijendran
of the Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a
member of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at Present).
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
