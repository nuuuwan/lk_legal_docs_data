SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the
commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rate specified in the corresponding
entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  04 months commencing from  August 15, 2020, subject to provisions of Sub
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2176/18 of 21.05.2020 in respect of potatoes (HS Code 0701.90) is rescinded with effect from August
15, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
August 14, 2020.
