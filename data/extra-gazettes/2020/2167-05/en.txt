NOTICE
I do hereby in the exercise  of the  powers vested  in me by Section 4  of the  Commission of  Inquiry Act
(Chapter 393) enlarge  by six months from  20th March 2020 to 20th September 2020 the six months period ending
20th March 2020 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission established
by the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 2  of the said Commission of Inquiry
Act on 20th September 2019 by Notification published in the Gazette  Extraordinary  No. 2141/88 dated  21st
September 2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  to Investigate, inquire into and report on or take
necessary actions in relation to the Bomb Explosions that occurred on 21st 
April 2019.
GOTABAY A RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
13th March, 2020.
