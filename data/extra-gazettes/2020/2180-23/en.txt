THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
Raference No.: 3/2/3/10/2018.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following Land or Servitude which is required
for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The block of the land is shown in the plan No. 4194 prepared
by the Officer of Servey of Gampaha Division on behalf of the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Mudagamuwa, in
No. 324/A, Mudagamuwa Grama Niladhari Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of the Attanagalla in the District
of Gampaha, Western Province and bounded as follows:
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Ambagahalanda; Description:  Gardan
10 years old 16 coconut trees, 20 years old 13 jack trees,
20 years 5 Mahogany trees and 20 years old 6 thekka trees;
Claimant: Jayakody Arachchige Dayawathi; Extent: 0.2942
Hectare.
All personal interested in the aforesaid land/Lands on which
a servitude is be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Attanagalla Divisional Secretariate on the 03rd day of July,
2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (induplicate) on or before the 26th day of June, 2020
the nature of there interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
S. P. GUNAWARDANA,
Divisional Secretary,
Attanagalla Division.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Attanagalla, Nittambuwa,
03rd June, 2020.
