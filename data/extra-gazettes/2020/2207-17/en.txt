FILLING OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  NAWALAPITIYA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Nawalapitiya  Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front at present)  the
recognized political party to which Komathi Nayagam Raja Wijayaragawan the member belonged, was required by me
under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Nawalapitiya Urban Council who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thangaiah Sendur Kumar  to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of the
Nawalapitiya Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare that Thangaiah Sendur Kumar was selected as the member of the Nawalapitiya Urban Council.
T. A.  C.  N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Nawalapitiya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
23rd  December, 2020.
