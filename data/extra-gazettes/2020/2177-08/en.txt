SPORTS  LAW  No.  25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS made by the  Minister of Sports under the Section 41 and read with the Section 31  of the Sports Law No.25
of  1973.
DULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA (M.P),
Minister of Education, Sports and Youth Affairs.
At Colombo,
On 26th  day of May 2020.
Regulations
1. The amended regulations of the National Association of Sports published in the Extraordinary Gazette No.2166/9
dated 10th March 2020 are amended only in relevance to the year 2020.
“Within 02 months after the end of the financial year” under Nos. l and III in the Section 5(4) (v) of the Item  No. 04
is amended as “Within 06 months after the end of the financial year” only in relevance to the Financial Statements
of the year 2019 that shall be presented in the year 2020.
“Within 30 days from the date of appointing the above committee” of the ltem 09 is amended as “75 days prior to the
due date of election” only in relevance to the year 2020.

Also, the aforesaid amendments will be negated with effect from  01st January 2021, and again from 01st January
2021 all the National  Associations  of Sports shall present the Financial Statements certified by their Executive
Committee to the Auditor  General  within  02 months  after  the end of each  financial  year and   the Election
Committee shall finish the activities related to the name list of the voters (affiliated institutes entitled to vote) and
inform it to all the members of the National Association of Sports within 30 days from the date of appointing the
Election Committee.
2. “Within a period not less than 02 months from the date of appointment” in the   Section 3 (d) of the regulations
issued by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2089/56 dated 22nd September 2018 is amended as “the election should be
called 55 days prior to the due date of election’’ only in relevance to the year 2020. The Section No.3 (d) in the
regulations  on  the  National Association  of  Sports  published  in  the Extra-ordinary Gazette No.2089/56 dated
22nd  September 2018 will again come into effect from   01st January 2021.
