 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: ATU/LAD/DO 45.
 Land Ministry's Ref. No.: 4-3/2/05/2016/HA 90.
I, hereby give notice, under Section 7 of the Land Acquisition
Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act,
No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land/servitude which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land/servitude to be acquired :
The allotment of Land described below depicted in
supplementary plan No. 4 of  F.V .P . No. 73 prepared by surveyor
General situated at Hawpe village Divisional Secretary's
Division of Athuraliya in the District of Matara.
Lot No. 42
Name of  the Land: Galketiyagoda, Yakdeliovita
Heenagamageyaddeliyagewatta; Particulars of the land:
Garden, Mixed Cultivation and Permanent Building; Claimant:
Chief Incumbent, Sri Bodirukkaramaya, Hawpe; Extent:
0.0264 Hectares.
   All person interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing to appear
before me at Athuraliya Divisional Secretariat on the 21st day
of September 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of
September, 2020 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
N
IROSHA S. GAMAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Athuraliya.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Athuraliya,
17th August, 2020.
