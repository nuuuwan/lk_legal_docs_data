FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KATTANKUDY   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Thamby
Lebbe Theenul Jawfer the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamed Saly Mohamed Mohideen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohamed Saly Mohamed
Mohideen was selected as the member of   the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
19th June, 2020.
