The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Colombo Municipal Council
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Umachandra Pragash of the Colombo
Municipal Council has vacated his office of  member of that council as she has ceased to be a member of the United People’s
Freedom Alliance.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City,
27th of April 2020.
