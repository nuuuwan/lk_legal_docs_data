THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Schedule published by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) of
Article 43 and paragraph (1)(a) of Article 46 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2153/12 of December 10, 2019 and amended from time to time, is further
amended with immediate effect, in the manner set out in the schedule below:
GOTABAYA R AJAPAKSA,
 President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo-01,
13th March, 2020.

2A
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the Heading, No. (1) “Minister of Defence” of the said  notification, as follows:-
(a) In Column I thereof, by insertion the following items immediately after item 35;
36. Matters relating to Immigration and Emigration
37. Matters relating to Sri Lankan Citizenship
(b) In Column I thereof, by re-numbering the items from 36;
(c) In Column II thereof, by insertion the following item immediately after item 31;
32. Department of Immigration and Emigration
(d) In Column III thereof, by insertion the following items immediately after “Sri Lanka Disaster Management
Act,  No. 13 of 2005”;·
• Immigrants and Emigrants Act, No. 20 of 1948
• Citizenship Act, No. 18 of 1948
• Grant of Citizenship to Persons of Indian Origin Act, No. 35 of 2003
• Grant Citizenship to Stateless Persons Act, No. 5 of 1986
• Grant of Citizenship to Stateless Persons (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1988
(2) With reference to the Heading, No. (24) “Minister of 
Power & Energy ” of the said  notification, as follows:-
(a) In Column I thereof, item 2 as –
2. “Investigating, planning, monitoring and developing the activities relating to the generation of electricity
and other energies by  utilization of power sources, such as solar, water, heat, coal, wind &  waste”
(b) In Column I thereof, item 4 as –
4. “Management of demand to ensure energy efficiency, and  development of renewable power”
(c) In Column I thereof, by insertion the following items immediately after item 10 :
11. Implementation of Solar Power Projects,
12. Rural Electrification,
13. Expedite implementation of Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) Power and Coal Power Constructions Projects,
identified as special priorities.
(d) In Column I thereof, by re-numbering the items from 11;
(e) In Column II thereof, by insertion the following items immediately after item 8 :
9. Lanka Electricity Company,
10. Lanka Coal Company (Pvt.) Ltd,
11. Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority.
(f) In Column III thereof, by insertion the following item immediately after “Petroleum Resources
Development Act,  No. 26 of 2003”;
 “Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of  2007”
