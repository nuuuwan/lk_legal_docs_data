THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.   16   OF   1978
Order  under  Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No.16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said University
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Emeritus Prof. Kathirkamanathan Kandasamy to be the
Competent Authority with effect from February 7, 2020 for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu
of the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, any power, duty or function  conferred, imposed on or
assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor under the Act or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule
made the said Act.
D
R. B ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation
Colombo,
18th February, 2020.
