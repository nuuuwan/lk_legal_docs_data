UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No.   16   OF   1978
Order under  Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No.16 of 1978, I, Prof. G. L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Benedict XVI Catholic
Institute of Higher Education (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007
and bearing Registration Number PV 115017 and located at No. 495, Minuwangoda Road, Bolawalana, Negombo, do by this
Order and subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the Benedict XVI Catholic Institute of Higher
Education (Private) Limited as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading
to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the
corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule II hereto
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
1. Benedict XVI Catholic Institute of Higher Education (Private) Limited shall conduct the degree programme relevant
to the Degree specified in Schedule II hereto, only at No. 495, Minuwangoda Road, Bolawalana, Negombo.
2. Benedict XVI Catholic Institute of Higher Education (Private) Limited is allowed to offer the Degree specified in
Schedule II hereto, subject to the programme re-review to be conducted by the Specified Authority.
