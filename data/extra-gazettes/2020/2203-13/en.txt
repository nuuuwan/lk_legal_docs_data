FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    TRINCOMALEE    URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return  member of  Trincomalee Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Leader of  the Independent Group,  which Thillainathan Pavithran the member belonged, was
required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of Independent Group who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and
who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Kanagaratnam Thiruchelvam to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Trincomalee Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kanagaratnam Thiruchelvam
was selected as the member of  the Trincomalee Urban Council.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
23rd  November, 2020.
