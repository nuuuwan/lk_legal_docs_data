ORDER  UNDER   SECTION  49(A)  OF  THE   LAND  ACQUISITION   ACT
I,  Prof. G . L. Peiris, Minister of Education hereby order under Section 49(A) of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land
described in below Schedule is needed to be acquired for the proposed building to be constructed in the Department of
Electrical and Information Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, University of Ruhuna.
Prof. G. L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
No. 18, Ward Place,
Colombo 07,
29th  September, 2020.
Schedule
An allotment of land in extent of 02 Roods, 10 Perches depicted as Lot No. A in Plan No. 5355 of Authorized Surveyor
Mr. H. L. R. Jayasundera situated in Peonaratchiwatta alias Mulanegewatta land in Niladeniya Village of No. 123A Niladeniya
Grama Niladhari Division of Bope-Poddala Divisional Secretary’s Division in Galle District, Southern Province of which land
the boundaries are as follows,
North : Faculty of Engineering Premises of the University of Ruhuna ;
East : Faculty of Engineering Premises of the University of Ruhuna and Lot No. B and C of Plan No. 5355 ;
South : Lot No. J, K and L of Plan No. 5355 ;
West : Faculty of Engineering Premises of the University of Ruhuna and Lot No. 03.
