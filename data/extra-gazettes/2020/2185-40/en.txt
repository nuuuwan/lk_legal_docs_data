THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: NYGM/LAN/7/53.
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/9/2014/WS/172.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose:-
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired :
An allotment of land described below and depicted in
F.V .P .592, Supplementary No. 28, Sheet No. 29, land detailed
list page 154 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in
30B Pitigala North Grama Niladhari Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of  Niyagama in Bentota Walallavita
Korale South, Galle District.
Lot No. 1033
Name of  land: Halgahaovita; Current usage of land: Crown;
Claimants' Name and Address: Mr. R. A. Karunarathna,
Mr. W. D. M. Pradeep Kumara, No. 235, Thotupola Road,
Pitigala; Extent: 0.0101 Hectare.
   All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby requested to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Niyagama Divisional
Secretariat on the 14.10.2020 at 10.30 a.m. and are requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08.10.2020
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount in duplicate.
