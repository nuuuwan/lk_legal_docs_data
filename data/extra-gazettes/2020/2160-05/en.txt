PART III - LANDS  
 
Title Registration 
 
AMENDMENT  
 
REGISTRATION OF TITLE ACT, NO. 21 OF 1998 
 
(Section 14) 
 
In regard to the title to parcel of land No. 01 of  Block 06 contained in the Cadastral Map No. 810012 situated in the village of Magalla 
within the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 99 - Magalla in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Homagama in the District of Co-
lombo in the Province of Western published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1576/9 of 17th November, 2008. 
 
       “Parcel of land No. 01 of block 06, contained in the cadastral map No. 810012” mentioned in the declaration of determination 
of the Commissioner of Title Settlement is amended as “Parcel of land no.01 of Block 01, contained in the cadastral map No. 810012”.
