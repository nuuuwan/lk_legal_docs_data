THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No.  16   OF   1978
Order  under   Section  25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of the CINEC Campus (Private)
Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number
PV 17745, do by this Order, amend the Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2157/3 of January 6, 2020 by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Business Management Honours in
Banking and Finance” appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding entry in Column II thereto, of the entries specified under
Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-

II hereto in respect of the Master of Business Administration by course work (SLQF Level 9), adhere to the minimum entry
qualification of ;

UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G . L. Peiris,
Minister of Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda
Institute have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed to restore normal conditions,
do by this Order, appoint Senior Professor Janitha Abeywickrama Liyanage to be the Competent Authority with effect from
December 13, 2020 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of the Director of Gampaha Wickramarachchi
Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the
said Director under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
Prof. G . L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of  Education.
