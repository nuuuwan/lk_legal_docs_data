The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KAMBURUPITIYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Lalith Alwis Samarasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of  that  2 - Narandeniya of Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Haliela Gamage Upul Priyantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the  Kamburupitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do
hereby declare that Haliela Gamage Upul Priyantha was elected as the member of the 2 - Narandeniya of Kamburupitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning officer,
Kamburupitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
13th March, 2020.
