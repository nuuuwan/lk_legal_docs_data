 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: DS/GFG/L&L/5/98.
Ref. No. of Ministry of Lands and Land
Development: 4-3/11/2017/UD/170.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the allotment of land described below required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Allotments of land in total extent of 0.0018 Hectare depicted
as Lot No. 231 below mentioned name in Sheet No. 06 of Block
No. 03 of the Cadastral Map No. 810023 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the village called Madawalamulla
South in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 98 of Madawalamulla
South within the Municipal Council limit of Galle in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Galle Four Gravets in Galle District of
the Southern Province and bounded as follows:
Lot No. 231
Name of  the Land: Wellambagalaaddara Owita; Description:
Home Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: N. B.
Ekanayaka; Extent: 0.0018 Hectare.
   All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Galle Four Gravets Divisional Secretariat on 24.08.2020 at
9.00 a.m.
Further, the nature of their interests in the land/lands, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount should be
notified to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 17.08.2020.
I. HIMALI RATHNAWEERA,
Divisional Secretary,
Four Gravets - Galle.
At Galle Four Gravets Divisional Secretariat,
04th August, 2020.
