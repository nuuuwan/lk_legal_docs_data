PUBLIC    SERVICE    COMMISSION
Delegation of Powers by the Public Service Commission in terms of Article 57(1) of the Constitution
1. By virtue of the powers vested in terms of paragraph 1 of Article 57(I) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, the powers concerning the officers of the Sri Lanka Police Service and those who are serving
under the Department of Police stated in Column III of the table below are delegated to the public officers specified
in Column II of such table by the Public Service Commission.
2. The Public Service Commission hereby specifically orders that the Notice in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2161/32
dated 06.02.2020 stands rescinded without prejudice to any action taken or purported to have been taken under the
Delegation of Powers made by the National Police Commission by such Gazette Notice.
By order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. D
AYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
Office of the Public Service Commission,
No. 1200/9, Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
19th November, 2020.
