FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  CHA V AKACHCHERI  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  66A (1)  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Chavakachcheri Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Social Democratic Party of Tamils, the recognized political party to which
Kandaiah Sivarasa the member belonged, was required  by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Chavakachcheri Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rasa Rasakumar to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Chavakachcheri Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasa Rasakumar
was selected as the member of the Chavakachcheri Urban Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ.
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri Urban Council.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  RATHNAPURA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  66A (1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Rathnapura Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Gamini Dayananda
Ramadasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Ward No. 9-Godigamuwa of Rathnapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Nirosh Nalaka Jayathunga Nindawatte to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Rathnapura Municipal Council , acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nirosh Nalaka
Jayathunga Nindawatte was selected as the member of the Ward No. 9-Godigamuwa of  Rathnapura Municipal Council .
S
URANGA AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Rathnapura Municipal Council.
