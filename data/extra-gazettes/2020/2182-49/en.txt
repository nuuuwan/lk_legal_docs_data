PART  IV(A) : PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  NOTIFICATIONS
SOUTHERN PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Cooperative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 Amended by the Acts, No. 32 of 1983 & 11 of 1992
ORDERS  ON DELIGATION OF POWERS
I, WILIYAM WIJESINGHE GAMAGE, the Governor of the Southern Provincial Council hereby assign all the
powers depicted in the schedule III in the following and have already been assigned to the Registrar of
the Cooperative Societies in terms of the rules enacted in accordance with the Act, of Cooperative
Societies No.05 of 1972 amended by the Act, No.32 of 1983 & Act, No.11 of 1992 and under thereof,
to the assignee whose name is depicted under the 1st column of the following schedule and has already
been appointed to assist the Commissioner of the Southern Provincial Cooperative Developments and
Registrar of the Cooperative Societies, as I have already been vested with the such provisions laid down
by the Sec.l54(C) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka due to a
circumstance in which the Southern Provincial Council is already dissolved at the release of this
notification and the such provisions which had also been assigned to the Minister of the Cooperative
Developments in terms of the Sec.2(2) of the Cooperative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 amended by Acts,
 No.32 of 1983 and
Act, No.11 of 1992 in accordance with the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989.
DR. W. W. GAMAGE,
Governor,
At Galle, Southern Provincial Council.
16th March, 2020.
