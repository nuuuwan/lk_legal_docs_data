NOTICE  UNDER  THE  SECTION  66C  TO  BE  READ  WITH  SECTION 66G  OF  THE

(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 22 OF  2012
I,  M. M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with the
powers vested in me under the Section 66C to be read with Section 66G of the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, the meeting to elect a new Chairman to fill the vacant post of
Chairman of the Haldummulla Pradeshiya Sabha located in Uva Province, will be convened on the 21st  December, 2020
at 9.30 am at the Haldummulla Pradeshiya Sabha Council Chamber, Haldummulla.
M. M. W
IJENAY AKA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Uva Province.
Department of Local Government,
Located at Martin Silva Mawatha,
Badulla.
11th December, 2020.

NOTICE  UNDER  THE  SECTION  66C  TO  BE  READ  WITH  SECTION  66G  OF  THE
 LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  NO.  22  OF  2012
I,  M. M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with the
powers vested in me under the Section 66C  to be read with Section 66G of the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, the meeting to elect a new Chairman to fill the vacant post of
Chairman of the Ella Pradeshiya Sabha located in Uva Province, will be convened on the 22nd December, 2020
at 9.30 am at the Ella Pradeshiya Sabha Council Chamber, Ella.
