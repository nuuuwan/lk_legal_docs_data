CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC    SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of Article 44(5) of  Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka and in compliance with the Cabinet Approved Memorandum No. 19/3391/201/005 submitted on 10.12.2019 by
His Excellency the President on “Assignment of Subjects and Functions to the Non-Cabinet Ministers”, I, Minister of
Fisheries and Aquatic Resources delegate to the State Minister of Fisheries and Inland Fisheries the Duties and Functions,
the Departments, Statutory Institutions & Public Co-operations and Law and Ordinance to be Implemented, as specified
in the Schedule hitherto.
D
OUGLAS D EV ANANDA (M.P),
 Minister of Fisheries & Aquatic Resources.
