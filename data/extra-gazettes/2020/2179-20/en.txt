The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Panduwasnuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ushettige Don Janaka Thilakasiri of the
Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of  the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased
to be a member of the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
10th June, 2020.
