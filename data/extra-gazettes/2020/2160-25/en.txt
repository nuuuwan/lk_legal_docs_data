FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V A VUNIYA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Selvaradnam Mathiyalagam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Prabakaran Yaanujan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Prabakaran Yaanujan was selected as the member of the Vavuniya Urban Council.
M. G . Lalith Ananda,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
27th January, 2020.
