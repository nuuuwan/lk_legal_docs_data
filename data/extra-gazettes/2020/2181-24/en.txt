SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby make an order to waive-off Rs. 440 per kg. from the applicable Special Commodity Levy on V egetable fats and oils and
their fractions, classified under the HS Code of 1516.20 and Rs. 400 per kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy on
Other (Margarine) classified under the  HS Code of 1517.90 listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2180/10 of 16th June, 2020.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  06 months commencing from  June  27, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
June 26,  2020.
