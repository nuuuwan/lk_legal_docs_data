THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: Kadu/Land/02/135/UDA. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude, which is required for a public purpose :- 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
An allotment of land in extent 0.3717 Hectare 
described below and depicted as Lot No. 01 in 
Preliminary Plan Co. 9847 dated 07.10.2019 prepared by 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Thalangama 
North (within the Municipal Council limits of Kaduwela) 
in 477A, Thalangama North ‘‘B” Grama Niladari 
Division, Divisional Secretariat Division of Kaduwela, 
Colombo District, Western Province.  
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Dombagahawatta; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Dinesha 
Perera, K. A. R. D. B. Wijerathne and others; Extent: 
0.3717 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Kaduwela on 29.01.2020 at 10.00 a.m and 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 22.01.2020, the nature of their interests in 
the land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the compensation of such amount. All deeds 
and other documents in support of the claims should be 
produced at the inquiry. 
 
 A. D. Y. ANANDANI,  
 Divisional Secretary , 
 Kaduwela. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Kaduwela. 
31st December, 2019.

THE AMENDMENT 
 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: EP/LD/9/4/5. 
 
THIS refers the Extra - Ordinary Gazette Notification No. 
434/4 dated 29.12.1986 of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka. The Notice is hereby given under 
the sub-section (01) of section 7 of the Land Acquisition 
Act (Amended) No. 28 of 1964 as the government intends 
to acquire the below mentioned land for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The following land allotment depicted at the additional 
No. 06 of the Finalized Village Plan bearing No. 582 
prepared by the Surveyor General over the land allotment 
located at the Ella Thanabaddegama Village, within the 
Grama Niladhari Division 26 of the Divisional Revenue 
Officer’s Division of Bentota Walallavita Korala (East) in 
Galle District. 
 
01. Lot No. 470 
Name of the Land: Yakbatekanda Addara alias 
Gohakoladeniya; Description: 10 years old 03 Jack Fruit 
trees, a temporary shelter made of clay together with the 
roof of bricks and 02 temporary wells have been existed; 
Extent: 02. Acres/00 Roods/00 Perches. 
 
The all claimants and people who have ownership or 
other relation to the aforementioned land themselves or 
the people who are authorized by their documents should 
be appeared before me at 10.00 a.m. on 18th February, 
2020, at the Divisional Secretariat– Elpitiya. 
 
Further, the nature of their ownership related to the 
mentioned land, details on the facts for claiming 
compensation regarding the acquiring that land, the 
amount of compensation and the way it is calculated shall 
be received to me by duly completed copies prior to the 
10th of February, in 2020. Further, relevant title deeds and 
other particulars must be brought on the date of 
examination in order to confirm the ownership over the 
land. 
 
 S. K. WIMAL SISIRA KUMARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Elpitiya. 
 
On 31st December, in 2019, 
At the Divisional Secretariat, Elpitiya.
