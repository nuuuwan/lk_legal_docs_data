FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   NA VITHENVELI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of Navithenveli Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council meetings period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which  Sithambaram Vasanthi, the member belonged, was
required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of Independent Group
who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Ramalingam
Yokesvari to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Navithenveli Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ramalingam
Yokesvari was selected as the member of  the Navithenveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Navithenveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
20th August, 2020.
