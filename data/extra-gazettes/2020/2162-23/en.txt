PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNME NT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KEGALLE  PRADESHIYA 
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political par ty to 
which Mohomed Imitiyas Cad ar the member belonged, was required by me under  Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide  upon   returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary o f the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated  his decision to 
nominate Hapan Pedige Nishal Sameera Thilakarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance ( Chapter 262)  do hereby declar e that Hapan  
Pedige Nishal Sameera Thilakarathna was selected as member of the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
