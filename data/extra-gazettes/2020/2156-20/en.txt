FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MADURAWALA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Madurawala   Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Palle Watta
Kankanamge Eranga Sandaruwan  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Haputhanthrige Don Ravinath Manuraja  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Madurawala
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Haputhanthrige Don Ravinath Manuraja was selected as the member of the Madurawala Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAY AWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Madurawala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
01st  January, 2020.
