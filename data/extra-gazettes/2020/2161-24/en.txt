PREVENTION   OF  OFFENCES  RELATING  TO  SPORTS  ACT,   No.  24  OF  2019
Order made under Section 21
I, Dullas Alahapperuma, Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs by virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 21 of the
Prevention of Offences relating to Sports Act, No.  24 of 2019 do hereby appoint a Special Investigation Unit comprising
of the following members for a period of three (3) years with effect from 15.02.2020, in order to implement the legal
provisions of the Act and direct that Unit to perform its duties according to the powers and functions referred to in that
Act.
I. Mr. W. A. J. H. Fonseka, SSP - Director/Chief
II. Mr. S. M. J. Samaranayake, SP - Deputy Director
D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA (M. P.),
Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs.
Colombo,
03rd February, 2020.
