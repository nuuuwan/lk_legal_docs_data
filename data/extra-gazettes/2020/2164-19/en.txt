The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MANMUNAIPATTU
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Ponnan Kunasekaram the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of  Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Maanikkam Kandhasamy to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the  Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Maanikkam
Kandhasamy was selected as the member of the Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning officer,
Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
25th February, 2020.
