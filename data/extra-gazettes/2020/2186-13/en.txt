THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: ME/ACQ/F 819.
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/8/2014/PC/373.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisitions
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands for the widening and
development of Kavinda Place in Kirulapone.
Description of the Land to be acquired :
Two (02) allotments of lands described in following name
and total extents of 0.0379 Hectare depicted as Lot Nos. 1 and 2
in the Preliminary Plan No. CO 9820 dated 28th February , 2020
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village called
Kirulapone, in the Thibirigasyaya Divisional Secretariat
Divisions in Colombo District, Western Province within the
Colombo Municipal Council limit and morefully described as
follows:
1. Lot No. 01
Name of  the Land: High Level Road, Assmt. No. 200 (Part);
Description: Garden; Claimant: C. G . De Alwis Seneviratna;
Extent: 0.0202 Hectare.
     2. Lot No. 02
Name of  the Land: Assmt. No. 5 (Part), Kavinda Place;
Description: Garden; Claimant: U. M. Silva; Extent: 0.0177
Hectare.
   All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally, or by agents  duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Municipal Commissioner's Office,
Colombo on the 27th August, 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 20th August, 2020 the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
ROSHANIE D ISSANAYAKE,
Attornery-at-Law,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council and Land
Acquiring Officer Colombo Municipal
Council Limit.

Town Hall, Colombo 07.
