FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER OF THE ATTANAGALLA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  66A  (1) OF THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resigning.
And whereas the secretary of the, National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Muhammadu Mansoor Shiras Mohamed the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of National Front for Good Governance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohomad Naseer Mohomad Nawsar to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, returning officer of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Mohomad Naseer Mohomad Nawsar was selected as the member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
31st August, 2020.
