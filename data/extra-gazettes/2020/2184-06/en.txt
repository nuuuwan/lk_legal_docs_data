THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Ambako/3/4/A/137.
Land Ministry Reference No.: LD/05/2004/PC/200.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following Land/Servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The land called Bogawana Estate (Bridwel Division) of total
extent about 0.0712 Hectares, shown lot No. 01 in P .P . Nu.2838
dated 20.12.2012 prepared by the Surveyor General situated
in the village of Bagawanthalawa in the 319 E, Bagawanthalawa
Grama Niladhari Division of the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Ambagamuwa Koralaya, in the Distric of Nuwara Eliya.
Lot No. "01" – Name of Land: –; Claimant's Name and
Address: Bagawanthalawa Plantation Company Limited,
No. 540, Thimbirigasyaya, Colombo 05; Extent: 0.0712 Hectares.
Boundaries:
North : Bogawana Estate (Bridwel Division) ; East :
Bogawana Estate (Bridwel Division); South: Bogawana Estate
(Bridwel Division) and Estate Road; West: Estate Road and
Bogawana Estate (Bridwel Division).
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Ambagamuwa Koralaya on the 14th
day of October 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 28th Day of
September 2020, the nature of their interests in the land, the
perticulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said Land/ servitude, the amount of Compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
SITHARA RUWINI GAMAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Ambagamuwa Koralaya.
Divisional Secretariat Ambagamuwa Koralaya,
09th July , 2020.
