THE   JUDICATURE   ACT,   NO.   2  OF  1978
Order under Section 3
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I, M.U.M. Ali Sabry. Minister of Justice,
in consultation with the Chief Justice and the President of the Court of Appeal, do by this Order, amend the Schedule of the
Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1679/40 of November 10, 2010, as last amended by Order published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2182/38 of July 2, 2020 as follows:-
(i) by the substitution for the entry “1. Kalmunai” appearing in Column II under the heading “  District” and its
corresponding entry in Column IV under the heading “Area of Jurisdiction” appearing against corresponding
Judicial Zone of Kalmunai under Column I of the following entries respectively :-
“1. Kalmunai The Judicial District of Kalmunai shall comprise the
Judicial Divisions of Kalmunai and Samanthurai.”;
(ii) by the repeal of all the entries relating to the Divisional Secretary’s Divisions of Karathiv, Ninthavur and Samanthurai
(Muslim) appearing in Column IV under the heading “Area of Jurisdiction” appearing against corresponding
Judicial Division of Kalmunai under Column III, appearing against corresponding Judicial District of Kalmunai
under Column II and appearing against corresponding Judicial Zone of Kalmunai under Column I;
