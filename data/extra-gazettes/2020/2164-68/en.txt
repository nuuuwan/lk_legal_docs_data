FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PUTHUKKUDIYIRUPPU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi the recognized political party to which Valarmathi
Subakaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  the member of  that No. 03 Udayarkaddu of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Arumugam Shanthi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that
Arumugam Shanthi was selected as the member of the No. 03 Udayarkaddu of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning officer,
Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mullativu,
28th February, 2020.
