FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PANADURA  URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Panadura Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Waduge
Sumanadasa Fernando the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of that Ward No. 10 - Nalluruwa of Panadura Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Murukkuwadura Padmasiri Silva to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  Panadura
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Murukkuwadura Padmasiri Silva was elected as the member of the Ward No. 10 - Nalluruwa of Panadura
Urban Council.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
14th May, 2020.
