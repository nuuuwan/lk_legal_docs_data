SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.   48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY  virtue of the powes vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby make an order to waive-off balance amount
after collecting Rs. 10 per kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 40 per Kg on importation of Salt (Sodium
Chloride BP), classified under the HS Code of 2501.00, listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2195/29 of September 30, 2020 by any pharmaceuticals manufacturing enterprises registered with the
National Medicines Regulatory Authority (NMRA) for the purpose of manufacturing pharmaceuticals, not for commercial
purpose, on the recommendation of the Secretary, State Ministry in charge of subject of Production, Supply and Regulation
of Pharmaceuticals.
This Order shall be valid until September 30, 2021.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
16th  December,  2020.
