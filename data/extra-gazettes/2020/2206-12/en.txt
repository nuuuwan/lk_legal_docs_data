(CHAPTER  262)  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notification under Section 66g
IT  is hereby announced that the first meeting to elect the Mayor of the Matale Municipal Council in the Matale District of the
Central Province as per the powers vested in me under Section 66g of the amended Local Government Elections Ordinance
of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 shall be held at the specific time, on the date specified
