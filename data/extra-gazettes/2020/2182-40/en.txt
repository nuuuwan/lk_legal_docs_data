THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.   16   OF   1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities  Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do
by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1482/26 of February 2, 2007 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2160/40 of January 30, 2020), which specifies the Faculties and
the Departments of the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of section 139 of the
aforesaid Act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after the entry
“8. Department of Zoology” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Sciences”
appearing in Column I of that Schedule of the following new entry :-
“9. Department of Environmental and Industrial Sciences”.
D
R. BANDULA GUNA WARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
02nd July, 2020.
