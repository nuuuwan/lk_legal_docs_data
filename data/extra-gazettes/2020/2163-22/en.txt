THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF 1978
Order  under  Section 27(1)  read with  Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  Dr. Bandula  Gunawardana,
Minister  of Higher  Education, Technology  and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1469/38 of November 03,
2006 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2037/18 of September 21, 2017), which
specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka which is deemed by virtue of the
provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, by the
addition immediately after the item “Faculty of Business” appearing in the Column I of the Schedule and the corresponding
entries in Column II of the Schedule, of the following:-
Column I Column II
“Faculty of Medicine 1. Department of Anatomy (Gross and Clinical Anatomy)
2. Department of Biochemistry and Clinical Chemistry
3. Department of Physiology
4. Department of Pharmacology
5. Department of Microbiology and Parasitology
6. Department of Community Medicine and Family Medicine
