L.D.- B. 21/78(xiv)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said
University have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Emeritus Professor Weerakoon Mudiyanselage
Abeyrathna Bandara to be the Competent Authority with effect from March 13, 2020 for the purpose of  exercising,
performing or discharging in lieu of the Vice-Chancellor of the University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri Lanka,
any power, duty or function, conferred, imposed on or assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor under the Act or under any
Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made the said Act.
Dr. B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
13th May, 2020.

THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute  have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Senior Professor Janitha Abeywickrama Liyanage to be the
Competent Authority with effect from March 13, 2020 for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu
of the Director of  Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function conferred, imposed on or
assigned to the said Director under the Act or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made the said
Act.
Dr. B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
