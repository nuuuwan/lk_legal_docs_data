THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDMENT  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: Thimbi/1/8/3/61 Ath.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose :
SCHEDULE
An allotment of land depicted as Lot No. 2 an extent of
0.0479 Hectares in Preliminary Plan No. Co. 9889 prepared by
Surveyor General situated at Borella Village Division No. 33
Grama Niladari Division of Borella North within the limits of
Thimbirigasyaya Divisional Secretariat area in the Colombo
District, Western Province.
Lot No. 02
Land name : Asst. No. 265 (part), Pelangasthuduwa Road
Land use : Garden
Land extent : 0.0479 Hectare
Claimant : Unknown
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Thimbirigasyays on the 19.06.2020 at 10.0 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (with duplicates) or before
19.06.2020 the nature of them interested in the land, the
praticular of their claimed for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
PRIYANTHA D ISSANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Thimbirigasyaya.
Divisional Secretary,
