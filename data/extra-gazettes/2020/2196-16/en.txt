FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    AMBALANGODA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Group Leader of the Independent Group 01, to which Dampella Gamage Janaka Deepthi Kumara
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member
of Independent Gorup 01  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Group Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group 01 has communicated his decision to nominate
Raththaran Handi Ravinda Thushara De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Raththaran Handi
Ravinda Thushara De Silva was selected as the member of  the  Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
05th  October, 2020.
