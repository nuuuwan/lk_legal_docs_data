FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   IPALOGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
council meetings period of three months without permission  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha, the recognized political party to which Kristhogu
Baduge Chamari Nilupa the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member if United National Party, who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kaluthotage Sandya Dhilhani Kaluthota to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kaluthotage
Sandya Dhilhani Kaluthota was selected as the member of  the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
03rd July, 2020.
