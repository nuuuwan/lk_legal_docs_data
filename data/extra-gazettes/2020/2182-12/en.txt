L.D.B 15/2014
THE   CONSTRUCTION  INDUSTRY  DEVELOPMENT   ACT,  No.  33  OF   2014
RULES made by the Construction Industry Development Authority under Section 57 of the Construction Industry Development
Act, No. 33 of 2014, read with Section 40 of the aforesaid Act.
PROF. N. T. SOHAN WIJESEKERA,
Chairman,
(for and on behalf of the) Construction Industry Development Authority.
Colombo,
19th June, 2020.
Rules
1. These Rules may be cited as the Construction Industry Development (Monitoring System of Contractors) Rules
of  2020.
2 There shall be a Monitoring System for the monitoring of contractors registered under the Construction Industry
Development Act, No. 33 of 2014.
3. Every contractor registered under the said Act shall comply with the Monitoring System of the Construction
Industry Development Authority established under rule 2, which shall be maintained as a physical, manual or web based
Monitoring System.
4. Every contractor registered under the said Act shall facilitate the officers of the Construction Industry Development
Authority to physically monitor their activities by visiting the sites during day time.

5. Every contractor registered under the said Act, every consultant or engineer, as the case may be, of a contract and
the  relevant employer shall be entitled to access the web based Monitoring System of the Construction Industry Development
Authority.
6. The Monitoring System shall use all inputs, views, feedback of all parties to a contract, including the eomployer,
the consultant, the engineer or the contractor. Such inputs, views, feedback shall be collected through the manual, physical
and web based monitoring system of the Construction Industry Development Authority.
7. The performace of a contractor shall be evaluated annually, on the basis of -
(a) quality of work ;
(b) time taken to complete the project ; and
(c) cost management.
for the purpose of arriving at a rating for a contractor.

L.D.B  15/2014
THE   CONSTRUCTION  INDUSTRY  DEVELOPMENT   ACT,  No.  33  OF   2014
RULES made by the Construction Industry Development Authority under Section 57 of the Construction Industry Development
Act, No. 33 of 2014, read with Section 55(3)(i) of the aforesaid Act.
P
ROF. N. T. SOHAN WIJESEKERA,
Chairman,
(for and on behalf of the) Construction Industry Development Authority.
Colombo,
19th June, 2020.
Rules
1. These Rules may be cited as the Construction Industry Development (Power to Instruct and Request Information)
Rules of  2020.
2. The Construction Industry Development Authority (hereinafter in these rules referred to as the “Authority”) shall
require a local authority or a statutory body to provide information on quarterly basis on-
(a) project for which approval for permit or licence hava been sought ;
(b) on-going projects ; and
(c) completed projects.
3. Every such construction project referred to in rule 2 shall be categorized under the following sectors :-
(a) buildings ;
(b) water supply and sewerage ;
(c) highways ;
(d) bridges ;
