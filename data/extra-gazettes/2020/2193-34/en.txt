THE   SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance do by this notification;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “Lanka Internet Society Limited” situated at 230 A,
Dawulagala Road, Pilimathalawa as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to
be extended.

THE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance do by this notification;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “Dehiwala Galkissa Sulu Kukulu Mas Viyaparikayange
Sangamaya” situated at No. 103/5, Sudarshani Mawatha, Kalubowila, Dehiwala as a purpose to which the
powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended; and
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of section
5(4) of that Ordinance.
MAHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
The Secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01,
17th September, 2020.
