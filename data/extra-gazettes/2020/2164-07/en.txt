Direction No. 58
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority hereby orders that no producer, importer, trader or supplier shall sell, offer or display for
sale, or expose for sale the following item above the Maximum Retail Price given below.
Item Maximum Retail Price (Rs.)
Big Onions (B. Onions) 190.00 per kg.
This Order shall come into effect from 23rd February, 2020.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order remove the item ‘B.Onions’ in Order No. 39 published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1975/68 dated 14.07.2016.
                                                      By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. G
EN. SHANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Rtd.)
Chairman.
Colombo,
23rd February, 2020.
