L.D.B. 21/78(XV)(A)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, Minister of
Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order
made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2126/21 of June 04, 2019), establishing the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, by
the addition immediately after the entry “4. Department of Human Resource Management”appearing in Column II of the
Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Management and Finance” appearing in Column I of that Schedule of the
following new entry.
“5. Department of Business Management”.
Prof. G . L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
08th September, 2020.
