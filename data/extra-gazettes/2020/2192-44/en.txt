ADVERTISEMENT   OF  PETITION
Winding -up of   Central Investments & Finance Limited (PB 858) subject to the supervision of court in terms of the
Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 read with Sections 32 and 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011
Therefore, subject to express provisions of Section 37(5) of the Finance Business Act,
Notice is hereby given that a Petition for the winding up of the above-named company subject to the supervision of
the Commercial High Court of Colombo, was on the 3rd day of December 2019, presented to the said Court by
Weerappulige Ranaweera, then Director, Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions, Central Bank of
Sri Lanka, Colombo 01. And that the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 30th day of
November, 2020 ; and any creditor or contributory of the said company desirous to support or oppose the making of
an order on the said petition may appear at the time of hearing in person or by his counsel or proctor for that
purpose ; and a copy of the petition will be furnished to any creditor or contributory of the said company requiring
the same by the undersigned on payment of the prescribed charge for the same.
Director,
Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions,
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
No. 30, Janadhipathi Mawatha,
Colombo 01.
Note.- Any person who intends to appear on the hearing of the said Petition must serve on or send by post to the
above-mentioned Director of Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institution notice in writing of his
intention so to do. The notice must state the name and address of the person, or, if a firm, the name and address of
the firm, and must be signed by the person or firm, or his or their proctor (if any) and must be served, or if posted,
must be sent by post in sufficient time to reach the above-named not later than 1.00 p. m. of the 28th day of
November 2020.
