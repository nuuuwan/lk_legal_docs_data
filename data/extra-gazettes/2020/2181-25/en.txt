AN ORDER UNDER SECTION 3 OF THE ACT No. 23 OF 1979 OF MAHAWELI  AUTHORITY OF SRI  LANKA
I Chamal Rajapakse, the Minister of Mahaweli, Agriculture, Irrigation and Rural Development, in pursuant to the
powers vested in me under section 3 of the Act No.23 of 1979 of Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka, with the
concurrence of His Excellency the President of the Democratic Socialistic Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby declare the
Moragahakanda-Kaluganga Mahaweli ‘F’ zone as a sustainable development area made through organic agricultural
products, for the functions prescribed in the said Act and for the purpose of achieving the following objectives and
the said functions.
Functions and objectives:-
(a) to promote organic vegetable crops, fruit crops and other  field crops in this area as a  green  organic zone
with free from use of agro chemical and pesticides.
(b) to ensure sustainable   usage of  soil through soil conservation method of setting stones bunds  and
biological hedges etc.
(c) to promote   water conserve farming for the preservation of irrigated water and its efficient use.
(d) to promote integrated farming system by integrating  poultry,cattle, goat and pasture cultivation to ensure
food & nutritional security of the farmer  families living in  this zone and  to promote the manufacturing  of
organic fertilizer.
(e) It is expected to manufacture the required organic fertilizer within this zone. The farmers will be provided with
necessary fertilizer by government in pursuant to the suitable method until such time arises.

(f) to implement the programs under public and private partnership in order to supply the direct and value added
certified organic products to local and foreign markets from this zone.
(g) to supply  such products to local and foreign markets under the brand of ‘Mahaweli Knuckle’s Organic
Products’.
(h) to promote the cultivation of vegetable crops and under-utilized fruit crops in  home gardens of these farmer
families and thereby Conservation  such crops from their nonexistence and encourage bee keeping.
(i) to promote integrated pest management practices without using agro chemicals to control such pests and
diseases affecting organic cultivation.
(j) No permit shall be granted to maintain the outlets in this zone which sell pesticides and weedicides.
(k) to develop this organic crop cultivation zone as an agro tourism and a model zone where knowledge about
organic plants can be gained.
(l) to take action not to provide the irrigation water of this zone for any  industrial activities without a special
permission.
(m) to develop a necessary management operational mechanism through the Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka with
the technological assistance of the Department of Agriculture in order to provide necessary agricultural
instructions and recommendations to relevant farmer organizations.
