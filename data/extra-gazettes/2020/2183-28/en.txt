THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: 6/3/7/15/7/7.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/MV/118.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following Land which is required for
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Public purpose means the Land Acquisition for the
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project.
The allotments of land described below, shown in Final
Topo PP No. 17, Supplement No. 37 Sheet No. 95 of dated
27.04.2020, prepared by Surveyor General, situated at
Medapihilla Grama Niladhari Division No. E 407 C Gangala
Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor Division of Naula Divisional
Secretary's Division, Matale District, Central Province.
01. Lot No. 3772
Name of Land: Wandurumulleyaya; Description:  Estate;
Claimant: The State; Minneriya - Girithale Wildlife Reservation;
Encroached by: Batagalle Gedara Rathnayake of Paranagama
Road, Kumbiyangahaela, Ambana Junction; Extent: 0.5951
Hectare.
02. Lot No. 3773
Name of Land: Wandurumulleyaya; Description:  Estate;
Claimant: The State; Minneriya - Girithale Wildlife Reservation;
Encroached by: Batagalle Gedara Rathnayake of Paranagama
Road, Kumbiyangahaela, Ambana Junction; Extent: 0.9748
Hectare.

a.m. on 12th day of August, 2020 at the Naula Divisional
Secretariat.
