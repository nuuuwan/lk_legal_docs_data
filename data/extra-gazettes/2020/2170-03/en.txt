NOTIFICATION
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, do hereby in the exercise  of the
powers vested  in me by Section 4  of the  Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) enlarge  by six months from  09th
April, 2020 to 09th October, 2020 the three months period ending 09th April, 2020 for rendition of the final report on
completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission established by me under Section 2 of the said Commission of
Inquiry Act on 09th January, 2020 by Notification published in the Gazette  Extraordinary No. 2157/43 dated  09th
January, 2020 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  to “Investigate and inquire into the alleged
wrongdoing, irregularities and malpractice in connection with ETI Finance Ltd. (ETI), a Finance Company which was
regulated by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka”.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
08th April, 2020.
