THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of  Lands do
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of  Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is  needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An  Allotment of land called "Kanahampitiye Kumbura" in extent
of about 0.1746 Hectares, depicted as Lot "A" in the Advance Tracing
No. Maha/UDD/2017/319 prepared by the Superintendent of
Surveys Kandy dated 31.07.2017, situated in Madiwaka Village in
the Gampaha Batahira Korale in Udadumbara Divisional Secretary's
Division in Kandy District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A'  - Kanahampitiye Kumbura - Extent:
0.1746 Hectare
North: Kanahmpitiye Kumbura claimed by E. W. W. M. A. B.
Gangoda, Kanahampitiye Watta claimed by M. S. U. Kuldu and

Kanahampitiye Chena claimed by I. I. Abdul Rasak and Road; South:
Road and Kanahampitiye Kumbura claimed by Y . W. M. M. K.
Egodawela; West: Kanahampitiye Kumbura claimed by Y . W. M.
M. K. Egodawela, Kanahampitiye Kumbura claimed by M. A. M. E.
