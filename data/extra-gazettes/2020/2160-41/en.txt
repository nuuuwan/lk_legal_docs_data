THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978 
 
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula  
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the U niversity 
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under section 21 of the sa id  Act, and 
published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 896/2 of November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette 
Extraordinary, No. 2115/6 of March 18, 2019) establishing the Raja Rata University of Sri Lanka, as follows :- 
 
 (1) by the addition immediately after entry ''4. Department of Human Resources Management" appearing in 
COLUMN 2 of the Schedule which corresponds to the item ''Faculty of Management Studies" appearing in  
COLUMN 1 of the Schedule of the following new entry :- 
 
             ''5. Department of Information Systems"; and 
 
 (2) by the addition immediately after the entry ''6. Department of Computing" appearing in COLUMN 2 of t he 
Schedule which corresponds to the item '' Faculty of Applied Sciences "in COLUMN 1 of that Schedule,  of 
the following new entry :- 
 
             ''7. Department of Chemical Sciences". 
 
 Dr. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
 Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation. 
 
Colombo.
