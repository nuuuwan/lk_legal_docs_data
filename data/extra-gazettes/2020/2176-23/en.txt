THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Schedule published by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) of Article
43 and paragraph (1)(a) of Article 46 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the
Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2153/12 of December 10, 2019 and amended from time to time, is further amended with effect from
May 21, 2020 in the manner set out in the schedule below:
GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
 President.
21st May,  2020,
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.

(a) In Column II thereof , by omitting item No. 13 ;
(2) With reference to the Heading No. (1) “Minister of Defence” of the said notification, as follows:-
(a) In Column I  thereof, by addition of the following item  immediately after item 35;
36. Enhancing the  skills of low income recipient  persons with lower educational  qualifications through a

implementing the law relevant  to their professions.
40. Providing an education on strategies and methods for the three Armed Forces and civil officers.
