THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: DIK/LAN/02/30/48.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following  land/servitude which is required for
the public purpose mentioned below.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Following allotments of lands depicted in Preliminary
Survey Plan No. MR/2812 prepared by Senior Survey
Superintendent of Matara District, situated in the villages of
Babarenda Central and Babarenda South, in Grama Niladari's
Division of Rathmale in minor division of Wellabada Pattu
(East) in Divisional Secretary's Division of Dickwella, Matara
District.
01. Lot No. 01
Land name: Diyaheliyaboralessa; Description: Open barren
land; Claimant: Goigoda Gamage Manjula Pathmakanthi,
Boralessa, Rathmale, Dickwella; Extent: 0.0925 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Land name: Boralessa; Description: Open barren land;
Claimant: Wasantha Kumudini Samarasekara, Boralessa,
Rathmale, Dickwella; Extent: 0.0066 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Land name: Dampotha; Description: Open barren land;
Claimant: Ariyawathi Wickramarathna Bandaranayake,
Boralessa, Rathmale, Dickwella; Extent: 0.0242 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
