1A- G 32043—57 (02/2020)
NOTICE   UNDER   THE PROVISIONS OF THE   REGISTRATION   OF   ELECTORS  ACT,  No.  4  OF   1980
AS AMENDED BY THE REGISTRATION   OF   ELECTORS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  10  OF   2017
ELECTORAL   DISTRICT   NUMBER   AND    NAME :  11-V ANNI
IN terms of Section 4 of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of Electors
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 2017, it is hereby notified that the supplementary register for the year 2019 containing
the  names of the internally displaced persons in the aforesaid electoral district has been certified and is open for inspection
during office hours at Mannar, Vavuniya and Mullaitivu  Election Offices.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Assistant Registering Officer,
Electoral District No. 11-Vanni.
At the Election Office Vavuniya.
On this 15th day of February 2020.
