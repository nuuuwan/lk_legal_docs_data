THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
My Ref. No.: DS/VN/LA.A/THURKKAPURAM.

I hereby give Notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land

servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
  
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Province : Northern 
District : Jaffna 
Divisional Secretariat Division : Valikamam North, 
    Tellippalai 
Grama Niladhari Division : Thurkkapuam (J/229)

Purpose : to construct a Building

    Office (Village 
     Secretariat) - 
      Thurkkapuram (J/229) 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Thotharai; Description: Garden 
Contains one Permanent building, One Tube well, One 
Coconut tree (10 -15 years), One Margosa tree                   
(10-15 years), and three Palmyrah trees (10 -15 years); 
Name of Claimant: K. Kaneshamoorthysarma and wife 
Srimathy; Extent: 0.0497 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Tellippalai on the 
05th day of May 2020 at10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 28th day of April 2020 the nature of their 
interests in the land the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
