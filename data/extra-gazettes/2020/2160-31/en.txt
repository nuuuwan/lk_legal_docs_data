EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   YATINUWARA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Kurunduwaththe Randunuge Dayananda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Number 15 Haliyadda of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abeykoon Mudiyanselage Chandra Kumari Abeykoon to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talanagama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that Abeykoon Mudiyanselage Chandra Kumari Abeykoon was elected as the member of the
Number 15 Haliyadda of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
27th  January, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 29.01.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   YATINUWARA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending consecutive
meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Damith Chathuranga Bandara Herath the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Number 26 Embilimeegama of Yatinuwara
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Herath Pathirajage Jayawardana Walgampaya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talanagama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namala Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that Herath Pathirajage Jayawardana Walgampaya was elected as the member of the Number 26
Embilimeegama of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
27th  January, 2020.
