L.D- B.  21/78(XIV).
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.   16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G. L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa
have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed to restore normal conditions, do by
this Order, appoint Dr. Chamindalal Sujeewa Lewangamage to be the Competent Authority with effect from October 01, 2020
for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of the Director of the Institute of Technology, University of
Moratuwa, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said
Director under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
Prof. G. L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
30th September, 2020.
