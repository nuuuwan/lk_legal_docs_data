Notices under the Local Authorities  Elections Ordinance
I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Retuning Officer of the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Mr. Suriya Arachchige Arvin Uditha of
the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been
ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
