THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My No.: KDS/KGK/4/9/2018/01/LA.
Land Min's Ref. No.: 4-3/11/2018/UD/147.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the State has acquired
the land described below: for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired :
An allotment of land in total extent of 0.0051 Hectare of the
land bearing Assessment No. 185 at Sirimovo Bandaranaike
Mawatha depicted as Lot 01 in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha
6996 dated 30.04.2020 prepared by the Senior Superintendent
of Surveyors-Kandy District, situated in the village called
Katukele, Ward No. 18 of Katukele (within the Municipal
Council Limit of Kandy) in the Grama Niladhari Division No.
255 of Katukele, of Gangawata Korale Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawata Korale in Kandy District of Central Province and
morefully described as follows:
Lot No. 01
Name of  the Land: Assessment No. 185 at Sirimavo
Bandaranaikei Mawatha; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
Could not be traced; Extent: 0.0051 Hectare.
   All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Divisional Secretariat of Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawata Korale on 16th of September, 2020 at 10.00 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 09th of September 2020, the nature
of their interests in the land/lands, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
U. J. M. S. S. B. JAYASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale.
Divisional Secretariat, Kandy Four
    Gravets and Gangawata Korale,
