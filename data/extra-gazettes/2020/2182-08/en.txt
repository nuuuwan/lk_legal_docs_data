THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.   16   OF   1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities  Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said University have failed to
restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Emeritus Prof. Kathirkamanathan Kandasamy to be the Competent
Authority with effect from May 7, 2020 for the purposes of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu  of the Vice-
Chancellor, University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka any power, duty or function  respectively which has been conferred or imposed on,
or assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor under the said Act, or  any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule
made thereunder.
D
R. BANDULA GUNA WARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
25th June, 2020.
