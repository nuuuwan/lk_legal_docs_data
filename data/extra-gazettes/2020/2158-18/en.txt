THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SLCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified by me, as the Cabinet Minister in Charge of the Subject of Power and Energy, in terms of the Article
44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that assignment of subjects and functions
for State Minister of Power and State Minister of Energy, appointed under the Article 44(1) of the Constitution, shall
come into effect immediately under my supervision and guidance as listed in the following Schedule.
M
AHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Power and Energy.
14th January, 2020.
