FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM   NORTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Valikaman North  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not
attending three  consecutive meeting without permisson ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Tamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Arulambalam Suntharesan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member of Ahila Ilankai Tamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Subramaniyam Sarveswaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the  Valikaman North Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Subramaniyam Sarveswaran was selected as the member of the Valikaman North  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam North  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
24th January, 2020.
