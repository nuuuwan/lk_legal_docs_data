SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do by this order impose in respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity
Levy at the rate  specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from March 08, 2020, subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2140/20 of 10.09.2019 in respect of Black gram (HS Codes 0713.31.22 and
0713.31.29) is rescinded with effect from March 08, 2020.
MAHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic
and Policy Development.
Ministry of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
