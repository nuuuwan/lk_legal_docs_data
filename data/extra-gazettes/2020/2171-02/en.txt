SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do by this order impose in respect of the commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 02 months commencing from April 17, 2020 subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Orders made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2124/2 of 21.05.2019 in respect of Dried Grapes (HS Code 0806.20), the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2143/3 of 30.09.2019 in respect of Fresh Grapes (HS Code 0806.10), the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2148/25 of 07.11.2019 in respect of Dried Oranges, Pears, Other Cherries and
Plums and soles (HS Codes 0805.10.20, 0808.30, 0809.29 and 0809.40), and  the  Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2160/63 of 01.02.2020 in respect of Fresh Oranges and Fresh Apples (HS Codes 0805.10.10 and 0808.10),  are  rescinded
with effect from April, 17, 2020.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
April 16, 2020.
