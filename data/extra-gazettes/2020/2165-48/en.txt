PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 05.03.2020
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER SECTION 39A(1)
THE allotments specified in the Order No. 117 of 2016 made under
the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act
(Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 1972/39 dated 23rd June, 2016 is vested absolutely in the State
and the possession of which has been taken for or on behalf of the
State under Para (a) of Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by  the
virtue of powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section  39(a)
(inserted by Act, No. 8 of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act. I,
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development, do hereby divest only that allotment.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/6/2015/HW/293.
