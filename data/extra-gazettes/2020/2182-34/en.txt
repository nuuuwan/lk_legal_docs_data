Government Notifications 
 
L.D.B.21/78 (xiv) 
 
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978 
 
Order Under Section 20(4)(b) 
 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  Dr. Bandula                   
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the ad ministration of 
the affairs of the Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities 
of the said Institute have  failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Prof. S. M. A. Nanayakkara  to be  
the Competent Authority with effect from July 01, 2020 for the purpose of exercising performing and discharging in lieu 
of the Director of the Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa, any power, duty or function respectively which 
has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, 
Statute, By law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder. 
 
 
       Dr. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
                                                                                                   Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation. 
 
Colombo. 
30th June, 2020.
