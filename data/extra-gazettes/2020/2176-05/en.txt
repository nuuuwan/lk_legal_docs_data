NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No.  02  OF  1974
Order under Section 64(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 64(1) of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 02
of 1974, I Mahinda Rajapaksa, the Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Housing Facilities do by this order,
embody in the schedule hereto the provision of the voluntary transfer scheme approved by me under Section 63(1) of the
aforesaid law in respect of water supply undertakings carried on by the Bandarawela Municipal Council.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Housing Facilities.
22nd of April, 2020,
Ministry of Urban Development, Water Supply and Housing Facilities,
No. 35, “Lakdiya Medura”,
New Parliament Road,
Pelawatta,
Battaramulla.

and supply of water services and payments to obtain such services will be vested to the Board.
(d) The assets of these facilities valued at Rs. 7,320,850.00 (Rupees Seven Million Three Hundred and Twenty
Thousand and Eight Hundred and Fifty) will be provide as a grant to the Board by the government.
(e) All agreements entered into by the Municipal Council in connection with water supply will also be continued
and honored by the board.
The above transfer will be effective from the Date of Publication of this Notice.
