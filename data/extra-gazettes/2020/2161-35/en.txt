2A
“4A. (1) Upon receipt of the information specified in Schedule III forwarded by a trustee in relation
to an express trust under  Regulation 4, the Registrar of Lands shall forward such information to the District
Secretary of the area within which the trust property is situated for the purpose of -
(a) verifying such information ; and
(b) ascertaining whether the creation of the trust contravenes any provisions of any written law.
(2) The District Secretary shall cause the information specified in Schedule III to be verified and ascer-
tained through the Divisional Secretary of the relevant Divisional Secretary’s Division with the assitance
of the Grama Niladhari of the area within which the trust property is situated.
(3) The Divisional Secretary shall conduct such inspections, examinations or enquiries as may be necessary
for the purposes specified in paragraph (a) and (b) of sub-regulation (1), and forward his report to the
District Secretary for making his observations on the relevant express trust.
(4) Upon receipt of the report under sub-regulation (3), the District Secretary shall forward his observations
in relation to such trust to the Registrar of Lands, who shall refer such observations to the Registrar
General.
(5) Where the Registrar - General is satisfied, based on the observations received by him under sub-regulation
(4) that -
(a) such express trust may be lawfully registered, the Registrar - General shall direct the Registrar of
Lands to register such trust ; or
(b) there is sufficient grounds to suspect that any aspect of such trust contravenes any provision of
any written law, the Registrar - General shall refer all information relating to such trust to relevant
law enforcement agencies for necessary action. ” ;
(3) in Sub-regulation (1) of regulation 5, by substitution for the words and the figure “express trusts received under
Regulation 4.” of the words and the figure “express trusts registered under Regulation 4A.”
