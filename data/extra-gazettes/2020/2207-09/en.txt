NOTICE  UNDER  THE  SECTION  66C  TO  BE  READ  WITH  SECTION 66G  OF  THE AMENDED
  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  OF THE  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  NO. 22  OF  2012
I, M. M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with the
powers vested in me under the Section 66C to be read with Section 66G of the  amended Local Government Elections Ordinance
of the Local Government Elections (Amendment)  Act, No. 22 of  2012, the meeting to elect a new Chairman to fill the vacant
post of Chairman of the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha located in Uva Province, will be convened on the 30th December, 2020
at 11.00 a.m. at the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha Council Chamber, Mahiyangana.
M. M. W
IJENAY AKA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Uva Province.
