FILLING OF   A   VACANCY   IN   THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   VALIKAMAM   SOUTH    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of V alikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the T amil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Rasathurei Kuneswaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sellappa Vijayanandan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sellappa
Vijayanandan  was selected as the member of   the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
V alikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
15th July, 2020.
