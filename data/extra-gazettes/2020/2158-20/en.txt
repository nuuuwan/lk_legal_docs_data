The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Galle Municipal Council
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Galle Municipal Council, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Panangala Liyanage Deshapriya of the Galle Municipal
Council has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a member of the United
People’s Freedom Alliance.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Galle Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
14th January, 2020.
