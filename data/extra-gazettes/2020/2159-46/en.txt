THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
D.S.'s Reference No.:  ELDS/LND/08/ACQ/2017/04.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.:  4-3/15/2017/VE/584.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land  to be acquired:
The allotment of land situated in the village of Millawitiya in
the Grama Niladari Division of Samangama (188 B), in
Pallepaththuwa in Nawadun Korale of the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Elapatha, in Ratnapura District,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
Plan No :- F.V .P . 63, Extra No. 12
01. Lot No. 175
Name of land: Millawitiya Wattha; Claimant: Balangoda
Plantations Company, P .O. Box 06, Palmgarden, Ratnapura;
Extent: 2.2995 Hectares.
G 31877 - 17 (2020/01 )
North: Lot Nos. 20, 21 and Road; East : Lot No. 21 and
Road; South: Road and Lot No. 20; West: Lot Nos.20 and 21.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby required to appear personally or by agents  duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Elapatha on 18th March, 2020 at 10.30 a.m.
Further, they should notify me in  writing (in duplicate)
before 11th March, 2020 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount. Documents
should be submitted to me on the same day to prove the
interests.
S.A. DILRUK,
Divisional Secretary,
Elapatha.
