Greetings!
Whereas, I have, in the exercise of powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, appointed you as members of the Task Force for Economic Revival and Poverty alleviation established
by the Presidential Directive dated 21st April, 2020,
And whereas, the high degree of attention is focused on the subject of regional development in the context of the
functions assigned to this Task Force and the need for further strengthening the efficient implementation of the relevant
tasks at regional level has been identified,
I do hereby appoint Mr. D.L. Nihal, Additional Director of the Regional Development Department of the Central Bank
of Sri Lanka as a member of the aforesaid Task Force in place of Dr. Nandalal Weerasinghe, Deputy Governor of the Central
Bank of Sri Lanka, who was appointed member of the said Task Force,
And whereas, I have, in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined the
number of Ministries of the new Cabinet of Ministers, Cabinet Ministers, State Ministers, Secretaries to Ministries and
Secretaries to State Ministries, following the Parliamentary Election held on 05 th August, 2020 and there has been and
alteration in the designations of the Ministries,
And, whereas, Major General (Rtd.) Sumedha Perera Esquire has been functioning as Secretary to the Ministry of
Agriculture,
And, taking into account the aforementioned alternations, I do hereby remove the said name given under item
number 23 and effect the alteration of the designations under item number 30 as indicated below.
Secretary to the Prime Minister, Secretaries to the Ministries of Finance, Defence, Highways, Public Services,
Provincial Councils & Local Government, Plantation, Industries, Power, Energy, Education, Agriculture, Irrigation, Urban
Development & Housing, Water Supply, Trade and Foreign.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the  16th day of September, Two
Thousand and Twenty.
                                                                                                                     By His Excellency’s Command,
P .B.  JAYASUNDERA,
 Secretary to the President.
