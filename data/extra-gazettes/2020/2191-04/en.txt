THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
                                                                                                                   
Reference No. 4/2/1/94. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No. 4-3/2/2018/CEB/10. 
 
IT is hereby notified  under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that was amended by  Land 
Acquisition (Amendment)  Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intend  to obtain the following Land/
accessibility for a public purpose. 
 
Land/accessibility to be obtained: 
 
Block of  land given under the following name and 
extent depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Gam 4207 
made by the Surveyor General situated in the Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 401C, Namaluwa in the Village of 
Namaluwa in the Divisional Secretary Division of Dompe 
in the Gampaha District of Western Province. 
 
P. P. Gam 4207- Description of the Block: 
 
Block No. 1 
Name of the Land: Halgahawatta; Description: 
Household Land; Person claiming original ownership:  
M. D. Kularathna, No. 226, Namaluwa, Dekatana; 
Claimant: The Government; Extent: 0.0127 Hec.  
 
Those who are claiming entitlement, ownership or any 
other relationship to the land/accessibility mentioned 
above or authorized persons by written documents from 
them should meet me at the Divisional Secretariat of 
Dompe at 10.00 a. m. on 02nd day of the month of 
December in the Year 2020. 
 
Further, I should be informed in writing with 
document consisting of two copies giving the amount of 
compensation and how such compensation is being 
computed and reasons for claiming compensation in 
obtaining the Land/Accessibility with regard to the nature 
of entitlements regarding the Land/Lands before the date 
of 25th in the month of November in the Year 2020. 
   
 N. A. A. S. NISSANKAARACHCHI 
           Divisional  Secretary,         
                         Dompe.  
 
On this 26th day of August 2020, 
At the Divisional Secretariat of Dompe.

Notice Under Section 7 
                                                                                                                    
Ref. No. DEV/LND/02/ACQUI/04-25. 
 
I  hereby give notice  under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as  amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment)  Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intend to acquire the following Land/
servitude which is required for the public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land bearing Lot No. 01 (Extent About 
0.8805 Hec.) depicted in the preliminary survey plan 
bearing No. MR/2839, situated in the Village of 
Kapugama, Grama Niladhari Division of Kapugama East 
in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara, Matara 
District, Southern Province. 
 
Lot No.  1 
Name of the Land: Gedarawatta alias 
Gallegedarawatta, Julgahakoratuwa alias Julgahawatta, 
Ihalawatta; Extent: 0.8805 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands   on 
which a servitude is to be  acquired are hereby  required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat, of 
Devinuwara on the 24th day of September 2020 at 10.00 
a. m. and are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 10th September 2020  the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for  compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
  K. P. G. SUMITH  SHANTHA, 
 Acquiring Officer/Divisional Secretary-Actg., 
 Devinuwara.
