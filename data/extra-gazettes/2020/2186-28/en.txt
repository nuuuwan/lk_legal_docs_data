Government Notifications
L.D.B.21/78(XV)(A)
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do
by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2160/42 of January 30, 2020), which specifies the Faculties and
the Departments of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the
aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition imme diately after the entry
“19. Department of Anesthesiology and Critical Care” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item
“Faculty of Medicine” appearing in Column 1 of that Schedule of the following new entry:
“20. Department of Medical Technology”.
DR. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
30th July , 2020.
