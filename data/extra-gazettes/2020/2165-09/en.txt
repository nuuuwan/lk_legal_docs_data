THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref. No. : ෙදʏ/2-1/2/අƮ/748. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for the Dehiwala Super Market 
Development. 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of lands shown below in Plan No. 
P.P.CO.9882 prepared by the Surveyor General 
containing in extent of 0.0605 Hectare, situated in the 
village of Dehiwala, within the Municipal Council of 
Dehiwala - Mt. Lavinia, in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Dehiwala, in the District of Colombo, 
Western Province. 
P.P.CO.9882 
 
01. Lot No. 01

0.0079 Hectare.  
 
02. Lot No. 02

0.0007 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
Name of the Land: Bogahawatta; Description : Garden, 
There is a permanent building; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimants: Rev. Kuttigala Chandasumana Thero, Sri 
Subodharama Raja Mahaviharaya, Dehiwala; Extent : 
0.0519 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Dehiwala.
