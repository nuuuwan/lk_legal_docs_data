FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   HALDUMMULLA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Haldummulla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Mirisse Gam Hewage Gamini Jayantha the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member if
Independent Group  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Periyasami
Sudaharan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  the Haldummulla Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Periyasami Sudaharan
was selected as the member of   the Haldummulla  Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Haldummulla Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
12th July, 2020.
