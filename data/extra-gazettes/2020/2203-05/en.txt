NOTICE OF APPROV AL OF THE AMENDMENT TO THE CITY OF COLOMBO
DEVELOPMENT  PLAN 1999 AND  2008 (AMENDMENT)
Prepared under the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the Urban
Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban
Development & Housing by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8H of the Urban Development Authority Law
No. 41 of 1978, have approved the proposed amendment to be included to the City of Colombo Development Plan 2008
(Amendment).
I hereby approve the amendment to Sections 4.5 & 5 of the City of Colombo Development Plan 2008 (which is an
amendment to the approved City of Colombo Development Plan 1999), specified hereunder, by virtue of the powers vested
in me under Section 8H of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978.
1. Section 5 of the City of Colombo Development Plan (referred to as the principle City of Colombo
Development Plan 1999 and 2008 (Amendment)) is further amended by the substitution of the
following provisions :
i. Change the zoning of the area bounded on the North by Flower Terrace & Walukarama Road
on the East by up to 1
st lot facing western side of Cumarathunga Munidasa Mawatha and Sir
Earnest Silva Mawatha, on the South by Queen’s road and up to 1st lot facing western side of
Ried Avenue, on the West by 27th Lane, 37th Lane and up to 1st lot facing eastern side of R.A.
De Mel Mw from “Special Primary Residential Zone” to “Mixed Development Zone”.
