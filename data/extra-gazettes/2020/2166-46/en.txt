UNIVERSITY  OF  VOCATIONAL  TECHNOLOGY  ACT,  No. 31  OF  2008 
 
Order under section 8(1)(b) 
  
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8(1)(b) of the University of Vocational Technology A ct, No. 31 of 
2008, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that 
the administration of the affairs of the University of Vocational Technology, Sri Lanka, have been s eriously dislocated 
and that the authorities of the said University have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this  Order, appoint Senior 
Professor Wachcha Patabendege Ranjith Premalal de Silva to be the Competent Authority with effect fr om March 17, 
2020 for the purpose of exercising performing and discharging in lieu of the Vice -Chancellor of the University of 
Vocational Technology, Sri Lanka, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on, or 
assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or 
Rule made thereunder. 
 
 DR. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
 Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation. 
 
Colombo, 
13th March, 2020.
