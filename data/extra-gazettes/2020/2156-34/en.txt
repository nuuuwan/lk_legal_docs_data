The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE CHAIRMAN  OF   THE   BUTTALA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS   AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)
ACT, No. 16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka  Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Herathmudiyanselage Chinthaka Sampath Bandara  the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section
66 B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party who shall  be declared elected  as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Wickrama Arachchige Ranjith to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Wickrama Arachchige Ranjith elected as Vice Chairman of Buttala
Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA G AYAN P ATHIRANA,
Returning officer,
Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Moneragala,
03rd January, 2020.
