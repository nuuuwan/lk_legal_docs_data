FIREARMS  ORDINANCE
Declaring a period of time for Firearms without Licence to be Handed over
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 30(1) of the Firearms Ordinance, as amended by Ordinances No. 06 of
1920, No. 12 of 1945, No. 07 of 1947 and Acts No. 18 and 22 of 1955, No. 38 of 1975, No. 08 of 1980, No. 50 of 1981 and No. 22
of 1996 (Chapter 182), I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President do hereby declare a period of time as mentioned in the following
Schedule to be enforced island wide for firearms without licence to be handed over to the government.
Further, when firearms without licence are handed over within the declared period of time, it is ordered that the person
handing over the firearm should not be fined or punished for an offence committed contrary to Section 22 of the Firearms
Ordinance with regard to keeping in custody, possession or utilization of the firearm before the date of handing over.
G
OTABAYA R AJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
31st January, 2020.
SCHEDULE
01. Places to handover firearms without licence
Police Station in the area of residence
02. Duration of time
From 5th to 12th February, 2020
