FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    MANMUNAI   PATTU
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  the  resignation;
And whereas the  Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Adamlevvei Muhammadu Rahumaththullah, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the returned member of National Front for Good Governance  who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammadu Aliyar  Muhammadu Siyath to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammadu
Aliyar  Muhammadu Siyath was selected as the member of  the Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
17th  September, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KATTANKUDY   URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  the resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Paikkeer Muhaideen Muhammadu Irzath, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the returned member of National Front for Good Governance  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammadu Musthafa Abdul Rahuman to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammadu Musthafa
Abdul Rahuman was selected as the member of  the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
17th  September, 2020.
