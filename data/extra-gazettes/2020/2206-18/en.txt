STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008
as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, further to the Order published under Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11
th July  2011, as amended by the subsequent Order  published under
Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/15 dated 05 th July 2016,   in respect of SHANGRI-LA
HOTELS   LANKA (PRIV ATE) LIMITED, do by this Order declare that,
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11th July  2011, as amended by the
subsequent Order published under Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinay No. 1974/15 dated 05th
July 2016,  is hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the
Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct  a luxury hotel as Mixed Use Complex including High Quality Residencies
and a High-End Shopping Mall, in Fort, Colombo 01, in the Western Province” shall commence commercial operation
on 30
th June 2020”.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
18th December, 2020.
