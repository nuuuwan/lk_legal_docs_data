FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   VA VUNIYA  NORTH  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending three
consecutive sabha meeting without permission ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Adhikarigedara
Nimal Rohana the member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Basnayaka Mudiyanselage Indrawansa Samantha Dilan Basnayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Basnayaka
Mudiyanselage Indrawansa Samantha Dilan Basnayaka was selected as the member of   the Vavuniya North  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North  Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
20th August, 2020.
