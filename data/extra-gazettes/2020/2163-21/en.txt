THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF 1978
Order  under  Section 27(1)  read with  Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  Dr. Bandula  Gunawardana,
Minister  of Higher  Education, Technology  and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15 of October 09, 2007
(as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2139/12 of September 02, 2019), which specifies
the Faculties and  Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of
section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of the item “Faculty of Arts” appearing in Column I of the Schedule and its corresponding entries
in Column II thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following item and entries :-
Column I Column II

2. Department of Geography
3. Department of Philosophy
4. Department of Linguistics and English
