FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   TRINCOMALEE  TOWN  &
GRA VETS   PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased
to be member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Kanthasami Koneswaranathan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that No. 07 - Singhapura of Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Amartha Valli Ramaiya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the  Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby
declare that Amartha Valli Ramaiya was selected as member of the No. 07 - Singhapura of Trincomalee Town & Gravets
Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. S
UTHAKARAN ,
Returning officer,
Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
12th of February, 2020.
