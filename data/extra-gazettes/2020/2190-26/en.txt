(iv) Should have obtained the full membership of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka or full membership

10.3.2 Method of promotion
Promotion of officers who completed the qualifications in 10.3.1 to the Special Grade will be made by the Public
Service Commission according to their seniority as per vacancies existing as at 01st of July and 01st of January each
year.
Note :-
1. (i) When promotions are made to the vacancies existing in a category stated in the Service Minute, it is only
a number of officers who have completed the qualifications in 10.3.1 not exceeding twice the number of
relevant vacancies will be invited for interview according to the seniority of officers in Grade I of such
category as at the date of computation of vacancies.
and
(ii) When promotions are made to the vacancies existing in common posts for several categories, it is only a
number of officers who have completed the qualifications in 10.3.1 not exceeding twice the number of
existing vacancies as at the date of computation of vacancies will be invited for the interview according to
the common seniority of officers in Grade I of such categories as at the date of computation of vacancies.
2. The Board of interview will carry out only the scrutiny of basic qualifications and seniority of the officers invited
for the interview.
3. The Public Service Commission, in accordance with the recommendations by a Board of interview appointed by
the Public Service Commission in terms of Rule 70 of the Procedural Rules of the Public Service Commission
following the scrutiny of qualifications, will promote a number of officers equal to the number of vacancies in the
Special Grade as at the date of computation of vacancies according to the seniority order of officers in Grade I as
at the date of computation of vacancies.
11.3 Method of selection.
(a) As it is
(b) As it is

(d) Approved posts in Special Grade.
The Public Service Commission will make appointments to the posts according to the recommendations
made by a Board of Interview appointed in terms of Rule 70 of the Procedural Rules of the Public Service
Commission following the scrutiny of seniority and special or expert knowledge in engineering subjects of
officers as per a Scheme approved by the Public Service Commission after inviting applications from
Special Grade Officers upon publishing the vacant posts by the Administrative Authority for filling up of
existing vacancies in the Special Grade.
