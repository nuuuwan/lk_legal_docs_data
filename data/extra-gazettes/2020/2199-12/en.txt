THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF 1978
Order under Sub-section (1) of Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,
Professor G . L. Peiris, Minister of Education, do hereby revoke with effect from 28th February, 2021 the Gazette
Extraordinary,  No. 859/12 of February 23, 1995 which contained the Order made under Section 24B read with Section

(a) establish with effect from March 01, 2021, a University, for the purpose of providing, promoting and
developing Higher Education in the Branches of learning of Indigenous Medicine, Indigenous Health Sciences
and Technology and Indigenous Social Sciences and Management Studies. The University shall be a body
corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal ;
(b) assign such University the name and style of “The Gampaha Wickramarachchi University of Indigenous
Medicine, Sri Lanka” ;
(c) specify the location which shall be the seat of such University within the administrative District of Gampaha ;
(d) specify that the University should develop courses connected to a wide range of related disciplines with the
aim of providing the students with a well-grounded education with potential to develop specialization in
chosen disciplines ;
(e) specify that the University should place greater emphasis on educating students on creating value within the
local resource base ; and
(f) specify that the University shall comprise the Faculties and the Departments of study set out in Colomn I and
