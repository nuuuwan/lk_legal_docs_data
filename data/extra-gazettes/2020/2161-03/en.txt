I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends  to    acquire

ground reservoir, water tower, pump house and caretaker
quarters.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
The allotment of land, described below, depicted as Lot No.
726, sheet No. 32, supplementary No. 36 of Final Village Plan
No. 302 prepared by Senior Superintendent of Survey,
Hambanthota on behalf of Surveyor General, in total Extent of
about 0.2023 Hectares situated in Giruwapaththuwa South,
village of Nakulugamuwa Siyambalagasara, in the Grama
Niladari Division of No. 375, Nakulugamuwa North, in
Divisional Secretaries Division of Beliaththa in Hambanthota
District, Southern Province.
Lot No. 302 F.A.P . 726 – Name of the Land: Kosgahahena,
Siyambalagas Kumbura and Weralagehena of Wawehena;
Existing use of the land: Garden; Original Claimant: Tissa
Weeratunga.
Boundaries of the Land:
North: Lot No. 54 and 52; East: Lot No. 52 and 56; South:
Lot No. 56 and 55; West: Lot No. 55 and 54.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude rights/
claims are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Beliaththa on the 04.03.2020 at 10.00 a.m.
