SPECIAL  COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
the  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rate specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  06 months commencing from  October 27, 2020, subject to provisions of Sub
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2186/32 of 31.07.2020, the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2191/26 of 03.09.2020 in respect of
Fish and the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2184/53 of 18.07.2020 in respect of Maldive Fish are rescinded with effect
from October 27, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
October 26, 2020.
