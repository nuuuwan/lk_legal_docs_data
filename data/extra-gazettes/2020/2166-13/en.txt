WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
The inquiry being conducted under Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of
the Provincial Councils (Conseqential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Wijepala Sri Narayana Wasala Bandaranayaka Mohottalage Seetha Kumari Arambepola, the Govenor of Western Province
do hereby announce that the period of investigation of single person Inquiry Board comprise of retired judge of the Court
of Appeal Mr. Chandradasa Nanayakkara, appointed by Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka No. 2152/14 dated 03rd December 2019, to inquire whether the offences stipulated under Subsection 184(1)
of the aforesaid Urban Councils Ordinance have been committed by Wattala-Mabola Urban Council and by Chairman of
Wattala-Mabola Urban Council while functioning in his office, and submit a report within 03 months shall be extended
until 03rd June 2020 according to the powers vested in me in terms of Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
