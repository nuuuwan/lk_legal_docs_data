PRADESHIYA  SABHA  -  BENTOTA
Entertainment Tax
I, William Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection 2 of
Section 2 of  the Entertainment Tax Ordinance to be read with Clause A of Sub Section 1 of Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Consequentital Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, in my capacity as the Governor of the Southern Province, do
hereby declare that I endorsed the following resolution adopted by Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha in the district of Galle, under
the Council Resolution No. 5.6.2. (x), at the meeting of the council held on 16th day of September 2019, by virtue of powers
vested in the Council by Sub Section 1 of Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267).
W
ILLIAM WIJESINGHE GAMAGE,
Governor of Southern Province.
25th day of February, 2020,
At the Office of the Governor, Galle.
RESOLUTION
Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha by virtue of the powers vested in, by Sub Section 1 of Section 2 of the Entertainment
Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) resolves to impose and levy a tax on payments for admission to entertainments held in the area
within the administrative limits of the Council to the value similar to 10 per centum of such payments and the same resolution
shall come into operation with effect from the date of publication of this Gazette. The Council further proposes that the
organizer or organizers of such an entertainment shall duly pay the respective tax to Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha before the
date of such entertainment.
