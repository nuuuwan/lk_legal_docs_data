 THE    MOTOR   TRAFFIC   ACT   (CHAPTER   203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport Services Management under section 237 read with section 126 of the
Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203).
MAHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Transport Services Management.
Colombo,
13th May 2020.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving Licence)
Regulations No. 02 of 2020 and shall be deemed to be valid and effective from 16th April 2020.
2. Validity period of Every Driving Licence, issued by the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic under the Motor
Traffic Act , which expires within the period of l6 th April 2020 to 31 st May 2020, shall be extended till 31 st of
July 2020.
