No. 2189/51 –  FRIDAY, AUGUST 21,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
Notice under Section  7 
 
 
                
My No.: UDU/2/5/2/56-B. 
Land Ministry No.: 13/97/FR/151-B. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the  
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose (the conservation of 
Dumbara mountain range) 
 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be Acquired : 
 
An allotment of land called “Winchfield Park Watta” 
bearing an extent of about 10.7406 Hectares mentioned in 
Maha 6645 (Lot Number1) Supplement of the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Karambakatiya, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province. 
 
Name and Address of Claimants: 
Mrs. T.G. Podi Manike, 
No. 100, 
Kande Watta, 
Matale. 
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
on which is to be acquired are hereby  required to appear  
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara on the 11th 
November 2020  at 10.00 a. m. and are hereby requested 
to notify  me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
04th November 2020  the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for  compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
  SUJANI  R. WIJETUNGA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Udadumbara. 
  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Udadumbara 
18th August 2020.
