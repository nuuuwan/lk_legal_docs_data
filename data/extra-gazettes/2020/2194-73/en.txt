Order No. 69
CONSUMER    AFFAIRS    AUTHORITY   ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, distributor or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale, or display
for sale the following item above the Maximum Retail Price given hereunder.
Item Maximum Retail Price
Coconut (Circumference over 13 inches) Rs. 70/- per nut
Coconut (Circumference  between 12 - 13 inches) Rs. 65/- per nut
Coconut (Circumference below 12 inches) Rs. 60/- per nut
This Order shall come into effect from 25th September, 2020.
                           By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
25th September, 2020.
