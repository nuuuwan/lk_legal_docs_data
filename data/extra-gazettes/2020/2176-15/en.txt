FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MANMUNAI   SOUTH  WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of   member in Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  the
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group,  to which Thiraviyam Sansajan, the member elected to
Ward 10 - Thanthamalai of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha, belonged was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the  member of  that Ward 10 - Thanthamalai of Manmunai South West
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Pakiyarasa Paskaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Pakiyarasa
Paskaran was elected as the member of the  10 - Thanthamalai of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
20th May, 2020.
