The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   YATAWATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Mapa Gunathilake Mudiyanselage Priyampathi Saliya Gunathilake the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Herath Mudiyanselage Udagedara Hesika Bandara Wijesinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarinie Dasanthi, Returning Officer of the Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do
hereby declare that Herath Mudiyanselage Udagedara Hesika Bandara Wijesinghe was elected as the member of the  Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning officer,
Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
06th March, 2020.
