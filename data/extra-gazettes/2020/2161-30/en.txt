CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the power vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Mahinda
Amaraweera, Minister of Power & Energy do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition of the land required by the
Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Power & Energy.
04th February, 2020.
Schedule
The land situated in the village of Lewala, in Grama Niladharie’s Division of 129A Lewala in the Secretary’s Division of
Bulathkohupitiya, within the Pradeshiya Sabha Limits of Bulathkohupitiya in the Administrative District of Kegalle, in the
Province of Sabaragamuwa, Sri Lanka as shown in part of field 08 (Tea) in the Urumiwella Estate plan, containing in extent
of 0A., 0R., 20P. (Lot No. 1) as to the survey plan No. 12013 dated 28.05.2019 of M. S. M. Ajward, Licensed Surveyor  &
Leveller, bounded as follows :
North by : Remaining portion of Urumiwella Estate Tea Field No. 8 ;
East by : Earth Drain ;
South by : Remaining portion of Urumiwella Estate Tea Field No. 8 ;
West by : Remaining portion of Urumiwella Estate Tea Field No. 8 ;
