KALMUNAI    MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Notice pertaining to the Amendments for the Levy for Solid Waste Disposal Management Service provided within the
limits of Kalmunai  Municipal  Council
IT  is  hereby informed to the public that, the table  in the Extraordinary Gazette  notification   No: 2113/61 dated 08.03.2019
regarding solid waste disposal management service tax is amended as follows.
S.no. Category of the service beneficiary Levy for the Solid Waste Disposal
Service (Rs)
01 Residential House or Commercial Shop (except 3rd
category mentioned below) Rs.  50.00 per  week
02 Factories Rs.  20.00 per day
03 Canteens, Food Stalls, Banana Stalls, Beef Stalls,
Mutton Stalls, Chicken Stalls, Fish Stalls Rs. 5.00 per Kg
04 Used king coconut husks (it should be cut into
two halves and handed over) Rs. 01.00 each
