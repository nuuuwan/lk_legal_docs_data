FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KORALAIPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  the resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Kalamayil Kamaleshwaran the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that ward 14 - Morokkottanchenai of  Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Samiththambi Manisegar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Samiththambi
Manisegar was elected as the member of the Ward 14 - Morokkottanchenai of Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
18th June, 2020.
