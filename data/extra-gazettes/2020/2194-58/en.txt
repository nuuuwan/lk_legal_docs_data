THE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Prime Minister and Minister of Finance do by this Notification ;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “Podujana Kala Paura” situated at No. 1019/A,
Nagahamulla Road, Pelawaththa, Baththaramulla as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that
Ordinance ought to be extended ; and
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of Section
5(4) of that Ordinance.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
The Secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01.
24th September 2020.
