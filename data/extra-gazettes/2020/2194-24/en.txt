FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NALLUR    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which   Mervin Rosan, the member belonged, was
required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of Independent Group
who  belongs to such Independent Group, and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Vinayagamoorthy Gnanenthiran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Vinayagamoorthy
Gnanenthiran  was selected as the member of  the  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Nallur  Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
21st  of September, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NALLUR    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which  Nagarasa Elimohan, the member belonged, was

terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Yulish Appuhami
Sauntharanathan  was selected as the member of  the  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
