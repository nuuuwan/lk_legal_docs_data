2A
1. I do hereby grant permission unto the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the
Province to spend Rupees Twenty One Billion Three Hundred and Forty-eight Million Six
Hundred and Twenty Nine Thousand Eight Hundred only (Rs. 21,348,629,800) and which
estimated for the services of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the eight months
duration begining on 01st January, 2020 and ending on 31st August, 2020, by virtue of powers
vested in me under Section 27(A) of Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 as the term of the
Sixth Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council ended on 26th September 2017.
2. (1) Without prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council which estimate will be Rupees Twenty One Billion Three Hundred
and Forty Eight Million Six Hundred and Twenty Nine Thosuand Eight Hundred only
(Rs. 21,348,629,800) for the service of the period beginning on 01st January 2020 and
ending on 31st August, 2020, in this order referred to as the “eight months duration”
shall be met.
(a) For payments which are hereby authorized to be made out of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council fund or monies of or advance at the disposal of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council ; and,
(b) For the proceeds of loans which are hereby authorized to be raised, within the
province of Sabaragamuwa for and on behalf of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council. The sum of Rupees Twenty One Billion Three Hundred and Forty Eight
Million Six Hundred and Twenty Nine Thousand Eight Hundred only
(Rs. 21,348,629,800) here in before referred to may be duly expended as specified
in the first Schedule to this order.
(2) The provision of Subsection (1) of  this Section shall have effect without prejudice to
the provisons of any other written law authorizing the raising of loans for and on behalf
of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
3. (1) The receipts of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council during the first eight months of
the financial year, 2020 from each activity specified in Column (iv) of the second
Schedule to this order shall be credited to the account of such activity, the aggregate
of receipts so credited shall be not less than the minimum limit specified in the
corresponding entry in Column (vi) of that Schedule.
4. The expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council during the first eight months
of the financial year 2020 each activity specified in Column (iv) of the Second Schedule to
this order shall be paid out of the receipt of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council from
such expenditure shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry in
Column (v) of that Schedule.
5. The debit balance, outstanding at the end of first eight months duration of the financial year
2020 of any activity specified in Column (iv) of the second Schedule to this order shall not
exceed the maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry in Column (vii) of that Schedule,
and the total liabilities of that activity at the end of that financial year shall not exceed the
maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry in Column (viii) of that Schedule.

6. Whenever at any time during first eight months of the financial year 2020, the receipts of that
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council from any activity specified in Column (iv) of the second
Schedule to this order are insufficient to meet the expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council on such activity, the minister may, from time to time, by order direct that
such sums may deem necessary to meet such expenditure shall be payable by way of advances
out of Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council fund or any other fund or monies of or at the disposal
of, the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, so however, that the aggregate of the sums so
advances does not exceed the maximum limit of entry in Column (v) of that Schedule. Any
sums so advanced in respect of such activity shall be refunded to the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council fund in such manner as the Governor may by order direct.
7. (1) Any money which, by virtue of the provisions of the first Schedule to this order, have
been allocated to recurrent expenditure under any programme appearing under any head
specified in that Schedule but have not been expended or may likely to be unexpended,
may be transferred to the allocation of recurrent expenditure or capital expenditure under
any other programme within that head by order of the Chief Secretary to the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or any other officer authorized by him.
(2) Any money allocated to capital expenditure under any programmes appearing under
any head specified in the first schedule to this order shall be transferred out of that
programme or project only by the Chief Secretary of Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
8. (1) That the receipts from taxes and other sources will be less than the amount anticipated
to finance authorized expenditure ; or
(2) That amount originally appropriated for a particular purpose or purposes are no longer
required ;
He may with the   approval of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, withdraw in whole,
or in part, any amounts previously released for expenditure under the authority of a
warrant issued by him from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council fund or from any other
fund or monies of or at the disposal of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
9. The Governor may, on or before 31st May, 2021, by order, vary or alter.
(a) Any of the maximum limits specified in Column (v), Column (vii) and Column (viii) of
the second Schedel to this order.
(b) The maximum limits specified in Column (vi) of the second Scheduel to this order.
No order made under this section shall have effect unless it has been approved by the
Governor.
Any such order shall, if so expressed therein be deemed to have had effect from such
date prior to the date of making such order as may be specified therein.
10. The Governor may amend the second Schedule to this order, by adding to the appropriate
columns of that schedule any activity and
(a) All or any of the maximum limits relating to such activity.
(b) The minimum limit relating to such activity.

Authorization
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
spend a sum of Rupees Twenty One Billion Three Hundred and Forty Eight Million Six Hundred and Twenty Nine Thosuand
Eight Hundred only (Rs. 21,348,629,800) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund, which is estimated for the expenses
of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the eight months period that starts on 01st Januay, 2020 and end on 31st of
August, 2020, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the
term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September,
2017.
This permission will be valid till 31st August, 2020 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-starts the functions of the Sabaragramuwa
Provincial Council which occurs first.
This estimate has been prepared by way of a vote on account based on the amount authorized by the Hon. Governor
of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council on 22nd April 2020, the allocation received from the Treasury for the expenses
during the period from 01st June, 2020 upto 31st August, 2020 and the expected revenue for the first eight months of the
year 2020.
I also emphasize that the expenditure incurred in the revenue expenditure estimates authorized on 22nd April, 2020,
to cover the expenses for the first five months of the year 2020, should be covered by the provisions set forth in this
permission.
T
IKIRI KOBBEKADUW A,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
28th May, 2020.
Authorization
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
bear the expenses stated in the Second (II) Schedule, which is estimated for the Advance Account Activities of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the eight months period that starts on 01st of January, 2020 and end on 31st August,
2020, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the
06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
Accordingly, I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council to bear the expenses subject to those limits.
This permission will be valid till 31st August, 2020, or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which occurs first.
