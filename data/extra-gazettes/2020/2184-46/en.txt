No. 2184/46 –  FRIDAY, JULY  17,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
Notice under Section  7 
 
 
                
Reference No.: MTR/LND/L1/ACQUI/3998. 
Reference No.: L.A.: 4-3/9/2017WS/459. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude  which is required for a public purpose. 
 
 
The following land situated in the village of 
Hungangoda in No. 427, Nawimana South Grama 
Niladhari Division in the Matara Divisional Secretary’s 
Division in Matara District in Southern Province and 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in Plan No. P. P. MARA/2835 has 
been acquired as additional land for the  construction of 
salt barrier across the Nilwala River under IVth stage of 
Water Drainage System. 
 
Schedule 
 
 1. Lot No. 1  
 
 
Name of the land : Paramaghghoketiya; Description: 
Open Waste Land; Claimant: Can’t find the Original 
Claimant; Extent: 0.113 Hectare.   
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a servitude  is to be acquired are hereby  required 
to appear  personally, or by agents duly authorised in 
writing, before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on 
12th day of August, 2020 at 10.30 a.m. and  are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 05th  day of August, 2020 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for  
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 
  DEEPIKA  K. GUNARATHNA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
  Matara. 
 
 
  
14th  July, 2020. 
At Divisional Secretariat, Matara.
