FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    AKKARAIPATTU   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Muhammathu Muhaiyadeen Muhammath Thaiyar, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the returned member of National Front for Good Governance  who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sulaima Levvai Muhammathu Irfan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathne, Returning Officer  of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sulaima
Levvai Muhammathu Irfan was selected as the member of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
02nd  September, 2020.
