 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: 3/1/81/2014.
 Land Mini's Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2014/A/194.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude*
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired :
Two allotments of land depicted as 514 and 515 Sheet
No. 03 Block No. 03 of the Cadastral Map No. 420151 prepared
by Superintendent of Survey Kurunegala situated in the village
called Lihinivehera in the Gramaniladari Division No. 553 of
Lihinivehera in Ihalavisideka Negenahira Korale in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Rideegama in Kurunegala
District of North Western Province and morefully described
as follows:
01. Lot No. 514
Name of  the Land: Holongolla Waththa; Name and Address
of Claiment: Kurunegala Plantation Company; Extent: 1.5046
Hectares.
Boundaries :
North: Lot No. 515, 82 and 83; East: Lot No. 83; South: Lot
No. 94, 92 and 88; West: Lot No. 88, 86 and 515.
     02. Lot No. 515
Name of  the Land: Holongolla Waththa; Name and Address
of Claiment: State; Extent: 0.1192 Hectare.
Boundaries :
North: Kudaoya; East: Lot No. 82, 83 and 514; South: Lot
No. 514 and 88; West: Lot No. 86.
   All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Rideegama Divisional Secretariat
on the 10th day of September, 2020 at 9.30 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on
or before the 03rd day of September, 2020 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
Divisional Secretary/Acquiring Officer,
Rideegama Division.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Rideegama,
30th July, 2020.
