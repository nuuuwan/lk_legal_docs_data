I, hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by, Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
The allotments of  land described below and depicted 
in Plan No. 3772 prepared by Surveyor General, situated 
in the Village of Navagamuwa in Grama Niladhari Division 
of No. 10A, Bathabure in Divisional Secretary's Division 
of Rambukkana in District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 1
 
Name of the Land: Hende Kumbura Kamatha; 
Description: Watta; Claimant : Government; Original 
Claimant: Arambawattage Nevil Arambawatta; Extent: 
0.0155 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Rambukkana on the 09th October, 2020 at 
10.00 a.m. 
 
Further, it is hereby notified me in writing (in duplicated) 
before 06th October, 2020, regarding the nature of their 
interests in the land/lands, particulars of their claims for 
compensation for acquisition of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of compensation and the details of 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
 W. L. R. I NDIKA LIYANAGE, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Rambukkana.
