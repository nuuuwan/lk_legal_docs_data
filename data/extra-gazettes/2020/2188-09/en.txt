The Order 
 
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3.  of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by Acts, 
No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I , S. M.

day of July, 2020.
