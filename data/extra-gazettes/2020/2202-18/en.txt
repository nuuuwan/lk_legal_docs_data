PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
G 33186 - 15 (2020/11) 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: DS/VW/LA/PO/02. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition Amendment No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
  
Description of the Lands to be Acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan: Ya 2978 
 
Situation  : Village: Chankanai East J/178 
DS Division  :  Valikamam West, Chankanai 
District  :  Jaffna 
Province  :  Northern 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
  Name of the Land: Pattankadavai; Description of 
Land: Permanent Building 01; Name of the Claimant: 
Not Known; Extent:0.1517 Hectare . 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid Land/Servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at Valikamam 
West, Chankanai Secretariat, on the day of the 30th 
December 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify to  me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
16th December 2020 the nature of their interest in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the  Acquisition of the land, the amount of compensation 
and  the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 All deeds and documents in support of claim should be 
produced before me at the inquiry 
 
 PREMINI PONNAMPALAM , 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Valikamam West, Chankanai.  
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Valikamam West, Chankanai, 
28th October, 2020.
