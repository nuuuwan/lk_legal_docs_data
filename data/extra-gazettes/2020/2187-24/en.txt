Notice under Section 7
      Reference No.: WGSWD&EIP/ACQ/KB/01.
Ref. No. of the Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the allotments of lands situated in either side of the
canal from Andiris Mawatha to Old Kesbewa Road for
Developing Katu Ela under Weras Ganga Storm Water
Drainage and Environment Improvement Project.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Allotments of land described in below numbers and depicted
in the Preliminary Plan No. 9481 dated 18.03.2015 (as amended
on 13.10.2016) prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in
the villages called Boralesgamuwa, Divulapitiya in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 533A of Raththanapitiya in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kesbewa in Colombo District
of Western Province and morefully described as follows:
