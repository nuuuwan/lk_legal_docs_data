FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KALUTARA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Kalutara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of the
party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamd
Ferows Mohamed Faslan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of that Ward No. 10 - Kuruduwatta of Kalutara Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Edirimuni Chaminda Suranga De Zoysa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  Kalutara Urban
Council,  acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Edirimuni  Chaminda Suranga De Zoysa was elected as the member of the Ward No. 10 - Kuruduwatta of Kalutara Urban
Council.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
11th June, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KALUTARA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Kalutara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be member of the
party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamed
Maharuf  Mohamed Jawfer the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of that Ward No. 08 - Mahawatta of Kalutara Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Prasantha Samanmal Warakagoda Vithanage to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  Kalutara Urban
Council,  acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Prasantha Samanmal Warakagoda Vithanage was elected as the member of the Ward No. 08 - Mahawatta of Kalutara
Urban Council.
K
ALINDRA JAYA WEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
