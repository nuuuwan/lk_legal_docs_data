The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    AMBALANTOTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of    member of  Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which Jayasooriya
Arachchi Patabedige Sunil Shantharathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon  returned member if Sri Lanka  Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Desa Wijesekara Bandaranayaka Kasun Rohana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Desa Wijesekara
Bandaranayaka Kasun Rohana  was selected as the member of  the Ambalantota  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
A VINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambantota,
19th November, 2020.
