PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
G 32261 - 22  (2020/06) 
AMENDMENT 
 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
My Reference No.: BD/HE/LND/05/64. 
 
Reference No. of Ministry of Lands and Lands 
Development: 4-3/4/2017/LO/57. 
 
I hereby inform under Section 7 Sub Section (1) of the 
Land Acquitition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire possession of the following land/lands for a 
public purpose. 
  
Details of the lands expected to be acquired : 
 
The allotment of land called Diyabolathenna depicted 
as Lot No. 955 in F.V.P. 197 Supplement 33 Sheet No. 
38, prepared by the Surveyor General and situated in 
Etampitiya Village in 55 Etampitiya Grama Niladari 
Division in Dehivini Korale within the limts of Haliela 
Divisional Secretariat in Badulla District Uva Province 
and containing in extent of 0.0051 Hectare and bounded 
as follows: 
 
North : by Lots No. 887, 888 and 9 
East : by the Road (Divisional Council) 
South  : by the Road (Divisional Council) and Lot No. 9  
West : by Lot No. 9 
 
All Persons interest in the aforesaid land should appear 
personally or by their agents on the dates and times as 
stayed hereunder, at the Office of the Divisional 
Secretary of Haliela. 
 
 Date of inquiry  Time  Lot No. 
  
 30.06.2020 10.00 a.m. 955 
 
Further, hereby request to notify to me in writing in 
duplicates, the nature of their claims on the said lands, 
detailed facts on application for compensation on 
acquisition of those lands, amout of the compensation, 
and as to how it was calculated, prior to 24.06.2020 
Deeds and other Claims of the said lands/land must be 
adduced. 
 
  H. P. A. G. ANURUDDHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Haliela.
