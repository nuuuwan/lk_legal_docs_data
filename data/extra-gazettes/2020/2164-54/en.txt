THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT, No. 16  OF  1978
Order under section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
the Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under  the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number  PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the Schedule II to the
Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2156/10 of December 30, 2019, by the
addition immediately after the entry  “Bachelor of Science Engineering Honours in Mechatronic Engineering” appearing
in Column I and it’s corresponding entry in Column II thereto, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively
of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
D
R. B ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
24th February, 2020.

SCHEDULE   I
CONDISTIONS
1. National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme relating to
the degree specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Business Management in Tourism, Hospitality and
Events, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of -
(a) three (3) simple passes (‘S’) in commerce subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced
Level) Examination or equivalent ; or
(b) three (3) simple passes (‘S’) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level)
Examination with a bridging course approved by the Specified Authority.
2. National School of Business Management Limited is recognized to offer the degree specified in Schedule II hereto
subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.

SCHEDULE   I
CONDITIONS
1. KAATSU - Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) shall, when admitting students
for the degree programme relating to the degree specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Master of Business
Administration in Management Strategy degree, by course work and research component (SLQF Level 10), adhere to the
minimum entry qualification of -
(a) a Bachelor’s degree including 30 credit passes in Business Administration, Public Administration, Commerce,
Human Resource Management, Marketing, Accountancy, Finance, Estate Management and Valuation, Business
Economics, Operations Management, Management Information System or Economics from a recognized
University or Higher Education Institute ; or
(b) a qualification of SLQF Level 6 or above in Business Administration, Public Administration, Commerce, Human
Resource Management, Marketing, Accountancy, Finance, Estate Management and Valuation, Business Economics,
Operations Management, Management Information System or Economics ; or
(c) a professional qualification equivalent to SLQF Level 6 or above ; or
(d) completion of NVQ Level 7 with a minimum GPA of 3.0 at a scale of 0-4, as determined by the Specified
Authority for admission in that field of specilization.
2. KAATSU - Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) is recognized to offer the degree
specified in Schedule II hereto subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.
