ESTABLISHMENT OF  DEPARTMENT OF MULTIPURPOSE DEVELOPMENT TASK FORCE 
 
 
IT is hereby notified to the Public that on 15 th January of 2020 the “ Department of Multipurpose Development Task 
Force” was established and its operation commenced under the scope of the Ministry of Defence, by Cabinet d ecision 
No. 19/3388/201/004 dated 19 th December 2019 issued based on Cabinet Note No. PS/SP/Sec./Cab.P dated                         
09th   December 2019 submitted by His Excellency the President Gotabaya Rajapaksa for the  purpose of build ing a new,               
economically effective workforce through formal professional training, comprising 100,000 minimally educated, low 
income, poor among the poor Sri Lankan citizens with the objective of raising the economic standard of Sri Lankan             
citizens suffering from poverty in keeping with the President’s concept of “Vistas of Prosperity and  Splendour.”    
 
Priority functions fulfilled by the workforce 
 
 1. Assist the government agencies for state property maintenance and civil works. 
 
 2. Assist the  schools and hospitals as caretaker works, maintenance and other work. 
 
 3. Assist in the  Programmes to control the Dengue and other communicable diseases and environmental 
  cleaning programmes. 
 
 4. Act as a support force to safeguard villages, properties, agrarian resources and livelihoods etc, wh ere 
  wildlife threats are present. 
 
 5. Assist the activities of mitigating natural disasters.
