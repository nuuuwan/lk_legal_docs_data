FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Muhandiram Ralalage Dammika Udayangani Pushpakumari the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abayarathna Herath Mudiyanselage Sriyani Sumithra Kumarihami Abayarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Abayarathna
Herath Mudiyanselage Sriyani Sumithra Kumarihami Abayarathna was selected as the member of the Mawanella Pradeshiya
Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
26th February, 2020.
