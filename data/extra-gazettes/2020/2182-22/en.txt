SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT   ACT,  No.  40  OF  1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 14(2) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Prasanna
Ranatunga, Minister of Industrial Export and Investment Promotion, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance,
Economic and Policy Development, do by this Order rescind the amount of CESS specified in the corresponding entry in
Column IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2047/2 dated November 27, 2017, as amended by the
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2178/21 dated June 03, 2020, in respect of the article specified in the Schedule hereto, with effect
from July 01, 2020.
P
RASANNA R ANATUNGA,
Minister of  Industrial Export and Investment Promotion.
Ministry of  Industrial Export and Investment Promotion,
Colombo 01,
30th June,  2020.
