SRI   LANKA   EXPORT   DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  40  OF  1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14(2) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I,
Prasanna Ranatunga, Minister of Industrial Export and Investment Promotion, with the concurrence of the Minister of
Finance, Economic and Policy Development, do by this Order rescind the amount of CESS specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2047/2 dated 27th November 2017 in respect of
the article specified in the Schedule hereto, with effect from 14th January, 2020.
P
RASANNA  R ANATUNGA ,
Minister of Industrial Export and
Investment Promotion.
Ministry of Industrial Export and Investment Promotion,
Colombo 01,
13th January, 2020.
