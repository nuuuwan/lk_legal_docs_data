FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE NINTHAVUR PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which
Mahroof Faththima Nushha member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kanthaiyapillai Suthamathi to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kanthaiyapillai Suthamathi was selected as the member of the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
04th  December, 2020.
