SRI Jayawardenapura, Kotte Municipal Council in accordance with the provisions of Section 2 (1) of the entrance act
imposing a 10% entry fee on entertainments levied for recreational purposes published in the entertainment tax ordinance
under 37 of 1984 on amended tax ordinance No. 12 of 1946 I, the governor of the Western Province, hereby announce that I

 WESTERN    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Notification to be made under Section 2 (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance bearing No. 12 of 1946 which shall be
read together with the Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act bearing No. 12 of 1989
I, the Governor of the Western Province, by virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of the provisions of the Section 2 (2)
of  Entertainment Tax Ordinance bearing No. 12 of 1946 which shall be read together with the Section 2 of Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act bearing No. 12 of 1989, do hereby notify that the proposal, passed by the Horana Urban
Council on 11.02.2020 as per the provisions mentioned in the Section 2 (1) of the said Ordinance itself to levy an Entertainment
tax of 7.5% having imposed on the admission fee chargeable for entertaining activities declared in the Entertainment Tax
Ordinance bearing No. 12 of 1946 amended by the Entertainment (Amendment) Act bearing No. 37 of 1984, was approved.
R
OSHAN GUNATILLEKE,
