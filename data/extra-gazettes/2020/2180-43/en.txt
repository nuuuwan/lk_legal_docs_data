FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PUTHUKKUDIYIRUPU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Puthukkdiyirupu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Earampu Raththinavadivel the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kanapathippillai Vijayakumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the Puthukkdiyirupu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kanapathippillai
Vijayakumar  was selected as the member of   the Puthukkdiyirupu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Puthukkdiyirupu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mullaitivu,
19th June, 2020.
