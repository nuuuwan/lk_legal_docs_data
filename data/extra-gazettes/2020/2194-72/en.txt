UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  NO. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No.16 of 1978, I, Prof. G. L. Peiris, the
Minister of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the International
College of Business and Technology Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing Registration Number PB 1437 and located at No. 36, De Krester Place, R.A.De Mel Mawatha,
Colombo 04, do by this Order and subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the International
College of Business and Technology Limited as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Educa-
tion therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation
is set out in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule II hereto.
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
l. International College of Business and Technology Limited shall conduct the degree programme relevant to the
Degree specified in Schedule II hereto, only at No. 36, De Krester Place, R.A.De Mel Mawatha, Colombo 04.
2. International College of Business and Technology Limited is allowed to offer the Degree specified in Schedule II
hereto, subject to the programme re-review to be conducted by the Specified Authority.
