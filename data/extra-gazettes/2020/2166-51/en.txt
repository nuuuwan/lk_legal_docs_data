ORDER  UNDER SECTION 10 (1) (a)
Of  The Holidays  Act,  No. 29 of  1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10 (1) (a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon,
Minister of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and Provincial  Councils & Local Government , do by this order declare
Monday, 16th of March, 2020 a Public and Bank special  Holiday to support quarantine process on Corona virus. This Holiday
will be extending if necessary.
J
ANAKA BANDARA TENNAKOON,
  Minister of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and
Provincial  Councils & Local Government.
Ministry of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and Provincial  Councils & Local Government ,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
