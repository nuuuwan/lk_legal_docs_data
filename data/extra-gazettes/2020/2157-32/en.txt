NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order of Delegating Power
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009 of North Central Provincial
Council, Professor Thissa Vitharana Governor of Governor’s Office of North Central Province do hereby delegates all
powers mentioned in the Column III of the Schedule to the persons mentioned in the Column I who has been appointed to
perform the duties and functions of the post of Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development (Departmental) to
assist the North Central Provincial Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar, from the date mentioned
in Column II in terms of the powers under Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009 that shall be read with Co-
operative Societies Amendment Statute No. 01 of 2011 of North Central Provincial Coucil.
Professor T
HISSA V ITHARANA,
Governor,
North Central Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Province.
