Promotion to Special Grade from Grade I
Method of Promotion-
A number of officers not more than thrice the number of vacancies in
Special Grade will be called for the interview in accordance with the first
schedule in the order of the seniority of officers in the Grade of the
stream/ streams relevant to the vacancy. The existing number of vacancies
will be filled after examination of the above qualifications and seniority
by a board of interview appointed by the Public Service Commission.
Selection will be made to the posts of Director, District Director of
Agriculture, Additional Director, Registrar of Pesticides Control and
Principal Agriculture Scientist and Principal Agriculture Specialist after
calling for applications from the officers in Grade I of the Sri Lanka
Agriculture Service having, at an  interview, awarded marks approved by
the Public Service Commission in the order of merit of marks they obtain.
Selection will be made to the posts of Deputy Director and Deputy
Registrar of Pesticides Control after calling for applications from the
officers in Grade I and II of the Sri Lanka Agriculture Service after having,
at an interview, awarded marks to all candidates in accordance with a
marking scheme approved by the Public Service Commission in the order
of merit of marks they obtain.
