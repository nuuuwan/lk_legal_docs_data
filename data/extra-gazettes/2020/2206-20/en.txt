BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor
G. L. Peiris,  Minister of Education, in consultation with the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the
Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South Eastern University of Sri Lanka,
published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996, as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 2126/10 of June 03, 2019, by the addition immediately after the entry “(d) Department of Chemical
Sciences” appearing in Column II of the Schedule II to such Order, which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Applied
Sciences” appearing in Column I of that Schedule of the following new entry:–
“(e) Department of Computer Science”.
Professor  G. L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
29th October, 2020.
