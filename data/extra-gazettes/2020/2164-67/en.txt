FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   DEWINUWARA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Dewinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party the recognized political party to which Athula
Alahakoon the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Achira Kalhara Ilangamge to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the Dewinuwara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
that Achira Kalhara Ilangamge was selected as the member of the Dewinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning officer,
Dewinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
28th February, 2020.
