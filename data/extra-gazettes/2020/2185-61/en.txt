THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                     My Ref. No. : DSS/LND/03/AQURE/26.
Land Ministry Ref. No.:4-3/6/2018/HW/100.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Lot No. E 4598 and E 4599 (02 Lots)  allotments of  land in
Total Extent of about 0.1730 Hectare, in F. T. P . No. 4 ,
Supplement No. 325, Sheet No. 720 and dated 20.06.2019,
prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of Surveys of
Hambantota District on behalf of the Surveyor General,
situated in the Village of  Viharagala in the Grama Niladhari
Divisions of  No. 112, Baddewewa  in  Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Sooriyawewa in Hambantota District in the
Southern Province.
01. Lot No. E 4598
Name of land: Thuneyaya; Description: Garden, Contains a
Mixed cultivation; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Patabadi
Pathiranage Thilakarathna in Viharagala, Sooriyawewa  under
the Land Development Ordinance on Grant No. H/UV/Soo/
Pr/1044(Wari) . (Lot No. D 9710 in F. T. P . 4) (To finalise the
Land Acquisition); Extent: 0.0457 Hectare.
02. Lot No. E 4599
Name of land: Thuneyaya; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Sri Lanaka Mahaweli
Authority ( Cultivated by Pahalage Upul Dhammika in
Viharagala, Sooriyawewa  under the Land Development
Ordinance on Licence No. H/UV/Soo/A/7791 . (Lot No. D 9746
in F. T. P . 4) (To finalise the Land Acquisition); Extent: 0.1273
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of  Sooriyawewa
on the  13.08.2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
06.08.2020, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
