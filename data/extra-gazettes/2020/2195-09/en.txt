THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Order Under Section 39A (1)
The allotment specifie in the Order No. 181 of 2016 made under the
Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)
and published in the Gazette  Extraordinary No. 1981/84 dated 25th
August 2016 is vested absolutely in the State and the possession of
which has been taken for or on behalf of the State under Para (a) of
Section 40 of the aforesaid  Act. But by the virtue of powers vested
in me by Sub section (1) of Section 39 (a) (inserted by Act, No. 8 of
1979) of the Land Acquisition Act, I Samarakoon Mudiyanselage
Chandrasena, Minister of Lands do hereby divest only the allotment
described in the schedule below.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands.
