THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 (as
last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2121/21 of April 30, 2019) which specifies the Faculties
and Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of
Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the repeal of entry “3.
Department of Engineering Technology” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty
of Technology” appearing in Column I thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following new entries :-
“3. Department of Materials and Mechanical Technology
4. Department of Civil and Environmental Technology
5. Department of Science for Technology”.
Dr. B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
