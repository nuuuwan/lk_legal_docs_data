The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    YATINUW ARA PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason the post has been ceased by
the governor under Article 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Amukotuwe
Gedara Sapumal Deshappriya Amukotuwa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance to decide upon the member of that number 05 Dodanwala of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Bambarandage Anuruddha Shantha Bandara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, returning officer of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Hewa Bambarandage Anuruddha Shantha Bandara was elected as the member of the number 05 Dodanwala of  Yatinuwara
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
15th  of October, 2020.
