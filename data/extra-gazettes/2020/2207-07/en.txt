Regulations
The National Associations of Sports Regulations, No. 01 of 2016, published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1990/23 of
October 27, 2016 as amended, from time to time, are hereby amended as follows:—
(1) by the repeal of Regulation 8 thereof, and the substitution therefore of the following regulation:—
“8. (1) (a) Any person who is aggrieved by any decision or action of a registered National Association
of Sports may appeal in writing to the Minister within seven days of the receipt of such decision or action.

(b) Such appeal shall be accompanied by all relevant material to support the appeal and the receipt of
the non-refundable payment of rupees five thousand (except for the appeals on the selection of players) paid
in favour of the Director General of Sports.
(2) Upon receipt of an appeal, the Minister may refer such appeal to the Appeal Advisory Committee
referred to in sub regulation (3) of this regulation.
(3) (a) The Minister may establish an Appeal Advisory Committee consisting of seven members to
consider the appeal and to make recommendations thereon to him. The Minister shall not be bound to by any
of the recommendations given by the members of such Committee.
(b) The Appeal Advisory Committee (hereinafter referred to as the “Committee”) shall consist of a panel
of a retired Judge of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka, persons who have wide knowledge and experience in the
field of law and persons who have contributed for the development of the sports of Sri Lanka.
(c) The retired Judge of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka shall be the Chairman of the Committee and in
the absence of the Chairman the members shall elect a member to preside at the meeting.
(d) Where the Minister refers an appeal to the Committee, the Chairman shall appoint at least three
members, from among the members of the Committee, to consider and make recommendations on such appeal
to the Minister within fourteen days from the date of the reference of the appeal.
(4) The Minister shall, on consideration of all relevant facts on the appeal inform his decision with the
reason therefore to the appellant and the relevant National Sports Association within one month of the receipt
of such appeal.”.
(2) In regulation 10, by the insertion immediately after the expression “corruption” of the following expression:—
“ “Director General of Sports” means, the Director General of Sports appointed under Section 3 of the
Sports Law;”.
