TOWN   AND   COUNTRY   PLANNING    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER    269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I,  Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development and Housing, do by this Order-
(a) declare that the area of land called the “Polonnaruwa Solosmasthana Rajamaha Vihara Sacred Area” specified in the
Schedule hereto shall be an urban development area for the purposes of aforesaid Ordinance; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Polonnaruwa Solosmasthana Rajamaha Vihara Sacred
Area”, which is declared above to be an urban development area.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Urban Development and Housing.
Colombo,
06th November, 2020.
