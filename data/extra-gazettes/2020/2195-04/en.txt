The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NINTHA VUR     PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which
Muhammadu Musthafa Samsudeen,  member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of  All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Seeni Muhammadu Adamwawa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina lshan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Seeni
Muhammathu Adamwawa was selected as the member of  the  Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
25th  September, 2020.
