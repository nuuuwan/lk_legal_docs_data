FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     DOMPE   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)  AS   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES    ELECTION    (AMENDMENT)
 ACT,   No.   16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman of  Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  elected as Parliament
member ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Vithana
Pathiranage Milan Sajith Jayathilaka,  the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party  who shall
be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kariyapperumage Piyasena to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Dompe Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare that Kariyapperumage Piyasena elected
as Chairman of  the  Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st  December, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE     MANTHAI   WEST
PRADESHIYA    SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Mallika
Amirthanadan,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Manjula Jesudasan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, J. Jenittan, Returning Officer  of  the Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Manjula Jesudasan
was selected as the member of  the  Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. J
ENITTAN,
Returning Officer,
Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mannar,
02nd  December, 2020.
