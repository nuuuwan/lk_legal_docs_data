THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I , Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena,  Minister of
Lands, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The  allotments of  land depicted in Advance Tracing
No.  GA/HKK/2015/352 and dated  26.02.2016, prepared by
the  Survey General, situated in the Village of
Kekillamandiya  in the Divisonal Secretariat Hikkaduwa, in
the District of  Galle, Southern  Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot  'A' -  Suwappukanda - Extent: 0.0010  Hectare
North: Suwappukanda claimed by Mary Nimal; East:
Suwappukanda claimed by Mary Nimal and Senapathi
Wijethunga; South: Lot  B and of the same  advance tracing;
West: Kekillamandiya  claimed by T.D. Sunil Shantha and
Kekillamandiya Circular Road.
02. Lot  'B' -  Suwappukanda - Extent: 0.0113  Hectare
North: Lot A of the   same Advance Tracing  and
Suwappukanda claimed by Mary Nimal; East: Suwappukanda
claimed by Senapathi Wijethunga; South: Lot  C of the same
advance tracing and Road; West: Kekillamandiya  claimed by
T.D. Sunil Shantha.
03. Lot  'C' -  Suwappukanda - Extent: 0.0073  Hectare
North: Lot B of the same Advance Tracing  and
Suwappukanda claimed by Senapathi Wijethunga; East and
South: Suwappukanda claimed by T.D. Sarath Premawansha;
West: Kekillamandiya  claimed by Circular road and road.
04. Lot  'D' -  Kekillamandiya - Extent: 0.0003  Hectare
North: Kekillamandiya claimed by T.D. Sunil Shantha and
Road; East:  Road; South: Kekillamandiya Circular Road; West:
Kekillamandiya  Circular road and  Kekillamandiya claimed by
T.D. Sunil Shantha.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands
My No.: 4-3/8/2014/PC/170.
