THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 30.07.2018 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2083/16 dated 09.08.2018 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified
in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour dated 25.07.2018.
Mr. H. K. Nimal, R G 4, Soyzapura, Moratuwa.
and
State Engineering Corporation of Sri Lanka, No. 130, W. A. D. Ramanayaka Mawatha, Colombo 02.
was referred to Mr. Obadage Leelarathne for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Dinesh Gunawardhana, Minister of Skills
Development, Employment and Labour Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings
be taken upon the said Order dated 30.07.2018.
D
INESH GUNAW ARDHANA,
Minister of Skills Development, Employment and Labour Relations.
Colombo.
13th March, 2020.

and
State Engineering Corporation of Sri Lanka, No. 130, W. A. D. Ramanayaka Mawatha, Colombo 02.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. H. K. Nimal who joined the State Engineering Corporation of Sri Lanka as a Computer Operator in 1981 and works
as a Systems Assistant at present has been caused injustice by not been given the post of Systems Analyst/Programmer from
1996, the arrears of salary, overtime payments and other allowances relevant not being paid the allowance for covering duties
in the post of Data Entry Supervisor from 01.01.2006 and the deduction of Rs. 15/- from salary as per the 1991 salary revision
and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo, this 02nd day of February, 2020.
