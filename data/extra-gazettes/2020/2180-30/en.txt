Appointments  & c., by the President
 No.  215/1  of  2020
 P .S. No.  PS/CSA/00/1/6/2.
IT is hereby notified that HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT has been pleased, under paragraph (3) of Article 47  of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Hon. Mahinda Rajapaksa, Prime Minister, Minister of Finance,
Economy and Policy Development, Minister of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs & Minister of Urban Development,
Water Supply and Housing Facilities, as the Minister of Community Empowerment and Estate Infrastructure Development,
with effect from June 01, 2020.
             By Order of His Excellency,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
