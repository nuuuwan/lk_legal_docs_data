PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS ACT, NO. 1 OF 1981
SPECIAL notice issued in connection with the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2165/45 dated 05th March, 2020-
The Election Commission hereby orders that 
 01.  the symbols set out in the Schedule (A) of the aforesaid Gazette namely,
Flower
Rabbit
Diamond
Pair of Spectacles
are removed from the symbols allotted to the political parties set out in Schedule (A) and set out as the symbols which are 
not allotted to the political parties referred to in Schedule (B) of the Gazette and
02. the symbols set out in the Schedule (B) of the aforesaid Gazette namely,
Pigeon
Fish
Compass
Conch-shell
are as the symbols allotted to the political parties referred to in the Schedule (A) of the aforesaid Gazette
  M
