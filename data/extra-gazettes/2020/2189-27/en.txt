THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice  under  Section 07 
 
 
My Number: DS/MG/LND/03/02/07. 
Ministry of  Lands No.  : 4/3/5/2014/HW/244. 
 
AS the Government intends to acquire the following 
lands for public use, I hereby notify you under  
Sub-section 7 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act as amended 
by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964). 
 
Land details Expected to Acquire : 
 
The final village maps prepared by the Badulla 
District Survey Superintendent at 22nd Karandagahamada 
Grama Niladhari Division 22 9 Karandagahamada Grama 
Niladhari Division in the Oyapalatha Minor Korale in the 
Meegahakiwla Divisional  S ecretariat Division in the 
Badulla District of the Uva Province.  Boundaries for 
plots of land with the following names and sizes: 
 
01. Lot No. 68 
Name of the land: Pettah Yaya; Description: Shurb; 
Original Claimant : Ratnay ake Mudiyanselage Kanchana 
Kumari, Karandagahamada; Extent: 0.0850 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 70 
Name of the land: Pettah Yaya; Description: Shurb; 
Original Claimant : Ratnay ake Mudiyanselage Kanchana 
Kumari, Karandagahamada; Extent: 0.0805 Hectare. 
03. Lot No. 72 
Name of the land: Pettah Yaya; Description: Shurb; 
Original Claimant : Ratnay ake Mudiyanselage Kanchana 
Kumari, Karandagahamada; Extent: 0.262 Hectare. 
 
All  persons claiming ownership, rights or other 
affiliation to the aforesaid land are entitled to submit their 
rights, entitlements or affiliations by  themselves or by 
their own documents on 12th October 2020 at 2.30 p.m.  
at the Meegahakiwla Divisional Secretariat. 
 
Further, before October 05, 2020 , they should inform 
me in writing of the nature of their rights regarding those 
lands, the details of the claim for compensation for the 
acquisition of those lands and the amount of 
compensation and the amount calculated.
