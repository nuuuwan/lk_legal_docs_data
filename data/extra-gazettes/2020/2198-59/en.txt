THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice Under Section 7
My Reference No.: V ADA/N/LA/DS.
Land Min.  Ref. No.:  4-3/8/2016/HA/368.
THE Statement in the page No.2A of the Gazette Extraordinary
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No: 2194/57
and dated 24th September 2020;
"All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on
which a servitude is to be acquired  are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Divisional Secretariat, V adamaradchy North,
Point Pedro on the 10th July 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify  me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 06th July 2020 the nature of their interests in the land the
particulars of their claims for compensation for acquisition of
the said land/servitude. The amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount" should be
revised as:
''All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Divisional Secretariat, V adamaradchy North, Point Pedro
on the 30th December 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify  me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 23rd December 2020 the nature of their interests in the
land the particulars of their claims for compensation for
acquisition of the said land/servitude. The amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount".
