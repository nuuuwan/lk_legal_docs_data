Statement of matter in dispute
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is:
Whether Mr. R. P . Ananda Sagara, who had worked as a Security Guard of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority from 10.02.1987 and
found with unauthorized goods in the van owned by him in which he was travelling with 03 others after the days work on
05.05.2008, following which 01 person had been released and an internal disciplinary inquiry had been conducted against 03
after which 02 of them were reinstated in service and only the complainant was terminated from employment, had been caused
injustice by being the only peson not to be reinstated in employment even after all the 03 accused persons were acquitted and
discharged in a case filed at Magistrate Court, Colombo regarding the same incident and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
