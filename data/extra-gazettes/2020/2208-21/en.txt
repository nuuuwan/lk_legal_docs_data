NOTIFICATION  MADE  UNDER  CLAUSE  154(C) READ  WITH  CLAUSE 154(F) OF THE
CONSTITUTION
I, do hereby announce that the Schedule relating to enforcement of Entertainment Tax adopted by the Colombo Municipal
Council on 30.07.2020 as per provisions stated under Sub-section 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
amended by Entertainment Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 1984, the Schedule of which was approved by me as per powers
entrusted in me according to the provisions stated under Subsection 2 (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and the notification of such approval
having been published in the Gazette Paper No. 2203/28 dated 26.11.2020 and included into the notification dated 18.11.2020
will be amended as stated in the Schedule hereto in compliance to the powers assigned to me under Clause 154 (c) of the
Constitution read with Clause 154(f).
I would further announce that the above mentioned relevant amendments will be carried out for the public welfare
based on the matters stated under regulations required to be followed when entertainment affairs are conducted; the
regulations of which have been enforced in compliance to the provisions of The Quarantine and Prevention of Diseases
Ordinance to control Covid - 19 epidemic owing to the impact caused by Covid - 19 epidemic to the area of authority of
Colombo Municipal Council.
