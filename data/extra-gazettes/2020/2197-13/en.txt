FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KOLONNAWA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Kolonnawa Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kalandar Mohideen
Rawuththar Nijamdeen the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that Ward No. 08 Salamulla of Kolonnawa Urban Council who belongs to such party  is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Weerasinghe Arachchige Don Regee to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Kolonnawa Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Weerasinghe Arachchige Don Regee was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 08 Salamulla of Kolonnawa Urban Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kolonnawa Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
13th  October, 2020.
