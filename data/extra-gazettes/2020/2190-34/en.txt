PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
 REGULATIONS   MADE  UNDER   SECTION  204  OF  THE  INTELLECTUAL  PROPERTY  ACT,  No.  36  OF  2003
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 204 of the Intellectual Property Act, No. 36 of 2003, I, Bandula Gunawardena,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation do hereby amend the effective date in respect of the Intellectual
Property Regulations No. 02 of 2019 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2146/37 of 27 October 2019 for the revision
of fees of the National Intellectual Property  Office as 27 November, 2019.
Dr. B
ANDULA GUNAWARDENA (M. P .)
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Ministry of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation,
Colombo,
04th August, 2020.
