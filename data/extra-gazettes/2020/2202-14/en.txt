PROMOTION   AND   APPOINTMENT   APPROVED   BY   HIS    EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT  has been pleased to promote Air Vice Marshal SK Pathirana, WWV
and Bar, RWP and Bar, RSP and three Bars, VSV , USP, MSc (MOA) USA, MSc (Def Stu) in Mgt, M Phil (Ind), MIM (SL), ndc
(Ind), psc, qfi, to the rank of Air Marshal and to appoint him under Article 61E(1)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka Air Force, with effect from November 02, 2020.
By  His Excellency’s  Command,
P. B.  J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
