CEYLON  ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Mahinda Amaraweera,
Minister of Power and Energy do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of the lands required by the Ceylon
Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto.
