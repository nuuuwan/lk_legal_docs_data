THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,   AS  AMENDED  BY
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDEMENT)
ACT,  No.  28  OF  1964  (CHAPTER  460)
 Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: THI/LND/L2/ACQVI/136.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
to obtain the servitude rights of the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land/servitude to be acquired:
The allotment of land described below and depicted as Lot
No. 1 in the Preliminary Plan MARA 2827 and dated 03.12.2019
prepared by senior superintendent of Matara District for
Surveyor General, in total extent of about 0.1074 Hectare in
the Village of Horonduwa in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 334, Yatiyana in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Thihagoda, in Matara District.
Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Thannahena or Pathinagoda Kandehena
and Ilukhenegodalla or Elhena; Description: Open wasteland
Water tower under construction; Extent: 0.1074 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude rights/
claims are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Thihagoda on the 25.03.2020 at 10.00 a.m.
And are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 18.03.2020 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
UMESHA SURANGI MATARAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Thihagoda.
Divisional Secretariat,
Thihagoda,
19th February , 2020.
