THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
 
Notice under Section  7 
 
 
                
My Reference No.: GA/IMA/LAN/05/02/10. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government has 
intends to acquire the following land which is required for 
a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
One allotment of land called Indurambavila 
Atanikitha Division in extent about 2.0047 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in P. P. N. 4286 and dated 
05.12.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
Grama Niladhari Division of Atanikitha in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Imaduwa in Galle District of the 
Southern Province morefully Described as follows: 
 
Lot No. 01 - Indurambavila/Induregoda -Extent: 
2.0047 Hectare; Claimed by Upali Wickramasekara, 
Cheri Wickramasekara, Lional Upali de Silva, Kusum de 
Silva, Rejinold Abeweera. 
 
North: Lot No. 3, 2 of P. P. N. 3604 and Lot Nos. 27, 
26, 25 of P. P. N. 2537 and Indunil Pura Tsunami 
Village; 
 
East: Indunilpura Tsunami Village,Road (R. D. A.) 
and Atanikitha Training and Research Center; 
 
South: Atanikitha Training and Research Center, Lot 
No. 03 and 01 of P. P. N. 2542 and Lot No. 11, 03, 09, 08 
and 03 of P. P. N. 3604. 
 
West: Lot No. 01 of P. P. N. 2542 and Lot No. 11, 03, 
09, 08, 03, 02 of P. P. N. 3604, Lot No. 27, 26, 25 of P. P. 
N. 2537.
