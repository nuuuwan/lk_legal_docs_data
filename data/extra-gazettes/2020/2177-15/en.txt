THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER SECTION 39A(1)
THE allotments specified in the Order No. 67 of 1995 made under
Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act
(Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 881/25 dated 25th July, 1995 is vested absolutely in the State
and the possession of which has been taken for or on behalf of the
State under Para (a) of Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by  the
virtue of powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section  39(a)
(inserted by Act, No. 8 of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act. I,
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development do hereby divest only the allotment described in
the Schedule I below.
