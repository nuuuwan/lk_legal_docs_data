THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: DIK/LAN/02/14.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following  land/servitude which is required for
the public purpose mentioned below.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Following allotment of land depicted in Preliminary Plan
No. P .P . 2821 prepared by Senior Survey Superintendent of
Matara District, situated in the village of Indigasara in Grama
Niladari's Division of Walasgala East in Minor Division of
Wellabada Pattu (East), Divisional Secretary's Division of
Dickwella, Matara District.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Dikhena; Description: Home garden, two
permanent buildings which are being constructed and a tomb;
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.5944 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of  Dickwella on the 18th day of March
2020, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of  March, 2020
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for  compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
SUSANTHA ATHTHANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Dickwella.
Divisional Secretariat,
Dickwella,
11th February 2020.
