SPECIAL   COmmODITY   LEvY   ACT,   No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, mahinda 
rajapaksa, minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect 
of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the 
corresponding entries in Column II of that schedule.
this Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from October 01, 2020 subject to provisions of sub 
section (3) of section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
 Mahinda Rajapaksa,
 minister of Finance.
