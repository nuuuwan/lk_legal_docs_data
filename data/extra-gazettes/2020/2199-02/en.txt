PORTS   AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT   LEVY  ACT,   No.   18  OF  2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance do hereby this Order, amend the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2152/32 of December 05, 2019, as amended, by the addition of the following Sub-paragraph immediately after Sub-
paragraph (n) of Paragraph 2.
(o) Medical instrument and equipment including test kits required for the provision of health services, imported or
imported and supplied by any agency, on the recommendation of the Secretary to the Ministry of Health subject to
the approval of the Secretary to the Treasury.
               This Order shall come into effective from October 27, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance,
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
October 26, 2020.
