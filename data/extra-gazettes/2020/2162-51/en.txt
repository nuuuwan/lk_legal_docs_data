NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION   66H  OF  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTION   ORDINANCE  AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,   No.  22  OF  2012
I, A. S. J. Godallawatta, Commissioner of Local Government, Sabaragamuwa Province do hereby announce that by virtue of
powers vested in me under Section 66H of Local Authorities  Election Ordinance as amended by Local Authorities Election
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 ; I, summon the meeting of the Local Authority located in the Ratnapura District which
name is stated in the Schedule hereof for the purpose of electing the vacant Deputy Chairman of that local authority at the
time, date and venue shown in the corresponding column of the Schedule below.
A. S. J. G
ODELLAWA TTA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
