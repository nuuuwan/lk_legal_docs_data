The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  VELANAI  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Aarumugam
Kobalakrishnan the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Visuvalingam Rasanayagam to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning officer of the Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Visuvalingam
Rasanayagam was selected as the member of the Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna.
08th October, 2020.
