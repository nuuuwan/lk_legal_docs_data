FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   JA-ELA  URBAN   COUNCIL   UNDER
SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Ja-Ela Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which Deekirikege Don Nuwan Sirikumara the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of
Independent Group, who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Pathagamagamage Kamal Krishantha Samarasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Ja-Ela Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Pathagamagamage Kamal Krishantha Samarasinghe was selected as the member of  the Ja-Ela Urban Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-Ela Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
3rd July, 2020.
