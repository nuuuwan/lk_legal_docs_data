FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  MAHAWA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group  to which Athapaththu Konara Mudiyanselage Sujeewa Sampath
Athapaththu the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Independent Group who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Weerasooriya Mudiyanselage Gnanawathi Kumarihami to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Weerasooriya
Mudiyanselage Gnanawathi Kumarihami was selected as the member of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala.
25th November, 2020.
