PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, H. P.  Jayananda, Additional Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 06th  January,  2020, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid
and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2156/1 of
30th  December,  2019 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 12th January, 2020.
H. P . JAY ANANDA,
Additional  Director General of Customs
for Director  General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
03rd January, 2020.
