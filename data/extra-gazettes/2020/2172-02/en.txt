IN  ADDITION  TO  THE  RELEV ANT  DIRECTIVES  ISSUED  BY  THE  ELECTION  COMMISSION  IN  THE
GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY  No.  2165/70  DATED  06.03.2020
DURING the election period, officers of all Government Ministries, Departments, Statutory Boards, Provincial Councils and
Local Authorities should avoid engaging with any candidate contesting the Parliamentary Election in facilitating or directing
the distribution of government relief to citizens, as it would amount to promoting the parties’/groups’ or candidates’.
