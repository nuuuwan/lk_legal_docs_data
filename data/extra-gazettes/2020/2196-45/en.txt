The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  DERANIYAGALA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resigning;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Nathawitharamalayi
Malani Nanda Hamine the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Madappuli Arachchilage Ushani Nithin Sri Tharaka Fernando to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning officer of the Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Madappuli
Arachchilage Ushani Nithin Sri Tharaka Fernando was selected as the member of the Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kegalle.
08th October, 2020.
