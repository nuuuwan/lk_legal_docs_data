EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT AS 
AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION

(CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 07 
                                                                                                
Land Ministry’s Reference  No.: 4-3/8/2017/PC/63. 
My Ref. No.: DIVPANN//LND/ACDEP/1/4/12. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land/servitude which is required for a public 
purpose:  
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
An allotment of land in total Extent of 0.5939 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 130 in the Supplement No. 8, Sheet 
No. 8 of Final Village Plan dated 21.07.2020 prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called the 
Ingaradavula of No. 341 Medapattu Korale -West in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pannala in Kurunegala 
District of the North Western Province and morefully 
described as follows: 
Lot No. 130 
Name of the land: Indigahawelawatta; Description: 
Home Garden, a mix cultivation of 23 coconut trees of 
about 35 -40 years, 8 coconut plants, three permanent 
buildings and a cement constructed well, available; 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.5939 
Hectare. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land on which 
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear  
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Pannala  Divisional Secretariat, on 25.11.2020 
at 10.00 a. m.  and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before 18.11.2020 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount,  
 
 
   
 R. M. S.  WICKRAMARATHNA BANDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pannala. 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Pannala. 
07th October, 2020.

Two  allotments of land called Mulhalkele Estate in

prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village

Lot No. 1 
Name of the land: Mulhalkele Estate; Claimants: 
State; Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission; 
Land Use: garden, Temporary Mixed Cultivation; Extent: 
0.0322 Hectare. 
 
 
Lot No. 2 
Name of the land: Mulhalkele Estate; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Land Use: 
Home Garden, Permanent Three Buildings and 
Temporary Mixed Cultivation; Extent: 0.4124 Hectare.
