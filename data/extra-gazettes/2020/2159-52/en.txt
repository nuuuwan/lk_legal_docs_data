THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER SECTION 39a(1)
THE allotment specified in the Order No. 185 of 2001 dated
22.05.2001 made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land
Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra
Ordinary No. 1185/12 dated 23.05.2001 is vested absolutely in the
State and the possession of which has been taken for or on behalf of
the State under Para (a) of Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by
virtue of powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 39a
(inserted by Act No. 8 of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act.
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development, do hereby divest the allotment depicted in the
Advance Tracing KA/MTG/01/56 dated 20.02.2001, described in
the schedule below:
