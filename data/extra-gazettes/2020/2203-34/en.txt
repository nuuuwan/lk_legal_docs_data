L.D. B. 277/1940(III)
THE  ANTIQUITIES   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   188)
Notification under Section 33
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 33 of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188), I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Senarath Bandara Dissanayake, Director General of Archaeology, being satisfied that the provisions of the aforesaid section
have been complied with, do by this Notification declare the area of state land specified in the Schedule to this Notification,
shall deemed to be an “archaeological reserve” for the purposes of the aforesaid Ordiance.
S
ENARATH DISSANAYAKE,
Director General of Archaeology.
Colombo,
27th November, 2020.
SCHEDULE
Area comprising the Delft Island Ruin Monastry Complex Archaeological Reserve
All that divided and defined allotment of land called the Delft Island Ruin Monastry Complex Archaeological Site depicted as
Lot No. 1 in the Final Village Plan depicted in the Topography Preliminary Plan No. Ya 1416 dated 29th December 1977
surveyed by the Superintendent of Surveys of Colombo, in the place of the Surveyor General containing in extent 1 Acres, 2
Roods and 21.07 Perches situated in the Delft West No. J01 Grama Niladhari Division in the Delft Divisional Secretary’s
Division in Jaffna District in Northern Province.
