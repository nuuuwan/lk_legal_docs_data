EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
                                                                                                 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following                      
land/servitude which is required for a public purpose:  
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The allotments of land called Hynfordwatta depicted 
& prepared by Senior Superintendent of Surveyor in 
Kandy District in Preliminary Plan No. P. P. Maha 6999 
dated 07.09.2020 situated in the Village of Hynford 
Colony in Grama Niladhari Division of Kendopitiya,               
No. 1097 in Divisional Secretary Division of Pasbage 
Korale in Kandy District, Central Province. 
 
01. The Boundary's of the land called 
Hynfordwatta, Lot No. 01 in extent of 2.5326 Hectares 
 
 
North: Road (Road Development Authority); East: 
Road (Road Development Authority) & Lot No. 02; 
South: Lot No. 02 & Road to Watta; West: Road to Watta 
& Road (Road Development Authority). 
 
 
02. The Boundary's of the land called 
Hynfordwatta, Lot No. 03 in extent of 3.6548 
Hectares. 
 
 
North: Lot No. 02 & Road (Road Development 
Authority); East: Road (Road Development Authority); 
South: P. P. A 1120 in Lot No. 34, Hynfordwatta claim 
by Land Reclamation Commission Sabha & Road to 
Watta; West: Road to Watta & Hynfordwatta claim by 
Land Reclamation Commission Sabha & Lot No. 02. 
 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Land/Lands  
on which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of Pasbage 
Korale, Nawalapitiya on the 20th day of January 2021 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of 
February 2021 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
   
 C. K. AMARAKOON, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pasbagekorale, Nawalapitiya. 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Nawalapitiya, 
11th November, 2020.
