Notice under Section  7 
 
 
                
Land Ministry’s Reference No. 4-3/8/2016/HA/220. 
Divisional Secretary’s Reference  No: Ben/2/7/3/27. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land named as Mahagedarawatta in 
total extent of 0.0165 Hectares and marked as Lot No. 01 
in Plan No. 4492 dated 13.12.2019 made by Surveyor 
General situated in the village of Kaikawala, in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Kaikawala, No. 09 in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Bentota District of 
Galle, Southern Province. The details of the allotment of 
land are as follows: 
 
Lot No. 1  
Name of the land: Mahagedarawatta; Land Use: Home 
Garden, permanent building; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Chairman of Relief Services Society, 
Kaikawala Induruwa. 
 
    
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands 
which servitude is to be acquired are  hereby  required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Bentota on 
the 29 day of July 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 22nd day of July 2020, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the said land/servitude 
the amount of compensation  and  the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
  W.W. K.  VITHANAGE, 
 Divisional Secretary 
  Bentota. 
 
 
 
  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Bentota 
11th June 2020.
