The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    HOMAGAMA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which Dehiwala
Liyanage Oliver Jayathilake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon  the member of that No. 15 Kiriberiyakele of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mathara Achchi Vidanalage Donal Nilantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Homagama Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Mathara Achchi Vidanalage Donal Nilantha was elected as the member of  the   No. 15 Kiriberiyakele of Homagama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
23rd  November, 2020.
