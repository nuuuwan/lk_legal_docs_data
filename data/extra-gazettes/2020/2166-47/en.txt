THE   SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123) I, Mahinda Rajapaksha Prime
Minister and Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do by this notification;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as "CEYLON  CINNAMON  GEOGRAPHICAL  INDICA TION

that Ordinance.
