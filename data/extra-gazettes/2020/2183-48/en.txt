UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of  Uva Province
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 154C. and 154F. of the 13th Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Raja Collure, (Attorney at Law) Governor of Uva Province,
do hereby announce that I have made the following appointments.
01. Mr. Dayananda Rathnayake, Secretary to the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural
Affairs and Textiles and Small Industries of Uva Province, has been appointed as the bearing Member of the
Director Board of the Road Passenger Transport Service Authority of Uva Province, in accordance with the
Section 6(C) (1) of the Road Passenger Transport Service Authority Statute No. 01 of 2001 of the Uva Province
Provincial Council, from the date 27th May, 2020.
02. Mrs. G . A. M. S. P. Ambanwala, Secretary to the Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education
(Except Tamil School), Local Government, Land, Power and Energy, Construction and Rural Infrastructure
Development of Uva Province, has been appointed to perform the duties of the Chairman of the Library Service
Board of Uva Province, in temporary basis, in accordance with the Section 13.5 of the Uva Provincial Library
Services Statute No. 05 of 2000 of the Uva Province Provincial Council, with effect from the date 11th March, 2020
till appoint a permanent Chairman.
