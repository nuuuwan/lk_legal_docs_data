FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    MUTHUR  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group - 02, which Puththisigamini Jeyaseelan the member belonged,
was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Independent Group
02  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter
262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group - 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Visuwalingam Anbalahan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Visuwalingam Anbalahan
was selected as the member of  the  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
05th  October, 2020.
