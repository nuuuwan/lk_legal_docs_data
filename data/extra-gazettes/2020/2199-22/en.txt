The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1) (a) OF THE  LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
WELIKANDA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Welikanda Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a) of
the (chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Suraweera Mudiyanselage Anil Pushpa Kumara of the
Welikanda Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
P
IUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Welikanda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
29th October, 2020.
