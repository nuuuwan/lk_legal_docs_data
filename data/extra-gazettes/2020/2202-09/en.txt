PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - G 33212 — 41 (11/2020)
NOTICE    UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE     LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS     ORDINANCE
Anuradhapura  Municipal  Council
I, Mohideen  Bawa Mohomed Sufyan Returning Officer of the Anuradhapura Municipal Council do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Rashmika Gamaarachchi of the Anuradhapura
Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a Member of the United
National Party.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
16th November, 2020.
