FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to
which Abdul Rahim Mahomed Safeen the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member of  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Rahman Mohamed Nawasdeen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Abdul Rahman Mohamed Nawasdeen  was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
20th May, 2020.
