The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    ALAYADIVEMBU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the T amil United Liberation, the recognized political party to which
Kanakareththinam Perinbarasa, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the  member of that No. 02 - Akkaraipattu - B of Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya Sabha   who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sivalingam Kanakareththinam to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Sivalingam Kanakareththinam was elected as the member of  the No. 02 - Akkaraipattu - B of Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
08th  September, 2020.
