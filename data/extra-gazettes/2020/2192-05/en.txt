FILLING   OF   A VACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  RAMBUKKANA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  66A (1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by the reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Perumbadage
Manjula Bogahawaththa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Number 2 - Hewadiwela of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Gedara Lionel Sarath Kumara to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Herath
Gedara Lionel Sarath Kumara was elected as the member of the Number 2 - Hewadiwela of Rambukkana Pradeshiya
Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kegalle.
07th of September, 2020.
