UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the  Governor of Uva Province
BY virtue of  powers vested in me, under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, A. J. M. Muzammil,
Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me.
01. Engineer Mr. M. T. G . A. N. Lakmal, Grade II officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, has been appointed to
act as the Provincial Mechanical Director of the Uva Province, for one year from the date 07.09.2020.
02. Mrs. L. H. P. Nilmini, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Agriculture Service, to act as the Deputy Director of
Agriculture (Extention) of Badulla District, in addition to the duties of her permanent post, with immediate effect
(from the date 15.09.2020)
03. Mr. G. G. N. Gunarathna, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, appointed as the Secretary
to the Governor of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 21.09.2020).
04. Mrs. G . A. M. S. P. Ambanwala, re-appointed Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, on contract
basis as the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education (Except Tamil Schools)
Local Government, Power and Energy, Construction, Rural Infrastructure Development and Land of  Uva Province,
has been extended the contract period from the date 09.10.2020 up to 08.10.2021.
