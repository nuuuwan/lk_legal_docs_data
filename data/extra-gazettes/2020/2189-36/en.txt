The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM    EAST   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation
;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which  Selvakumary Selvaruban the member belonged, was
required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member if Independent Group
who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Niththika
Saththiyaseelan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Selection 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Niththika
Saththiyaseelan was selected as the member of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
17th August, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 20.08.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM    EAST   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which  Vanithaamani Jegatheesvaran the member belonged,
was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member if Independent Group
who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Udayatheepan Latha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Selection 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Udayatheepan
Latha was selected as the member of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
17th August, 2020.
