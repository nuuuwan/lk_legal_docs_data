THE    SOCIETIES     ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Prime
Minister and Minister of Finance do by this Notification.
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “SEEMASAHITHA  WAYAMBA  PALATH  PALANA
MANTHREE  SANSADA  SAMITHIYA” situated at Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabhawa, Bohingamuwa, Kuliyapitiya
as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended.
