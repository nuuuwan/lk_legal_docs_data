THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Survey plan No. B/HLD/2003/850 at 2003/10/26 of Surveyor-
General situated in Kandapalla Korale Minor Haldummulla town in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Haldummulla in Badulla District
in Uva Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thawalanmandiya – Extent: 0.0481 Hectare
North and East: The remainder of the land claimed by M. A.
Gamini Jayantha; South: Land claimed by A. H. T. Helan de Silva,
Lot B and land claimed by M. A. Gamini Jayantha; West: Land
claimed by A. H. T. Helan de Silva, Lot B and Land claimed by M. A.
Gamini Jayantha.
