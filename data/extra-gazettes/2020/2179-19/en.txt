FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KORALAIPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council  meetings period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Seenithambi
Manimekaladevi the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ponnaiah Kalaivani to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ponnaiah Kalaivani was
selected as the member of the Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Koralaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
09th June, 2020.
