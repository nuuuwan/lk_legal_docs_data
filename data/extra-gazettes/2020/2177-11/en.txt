CENTRAL   BANK   OF  SRI   LANKA
Finance Business Act,  No. 42 of 2011
CANCELLATION  of a licence to carry on finance business under Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of
2011.
Prof. W. D. L AKSHMAN,
Chairman of the Monetary Board and
the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
Colombo,
28th May, 2020.
The public is hereby informed that in terms of Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, the
Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka has decided to cancel the licence issued to The Finance Company PLC
under the said Finance Business Act with effect from 22nd May, 2020. Accordingly, The Finance Company PLC is not
permitted to carry on finance business with effect from 22nd May, 2020.
