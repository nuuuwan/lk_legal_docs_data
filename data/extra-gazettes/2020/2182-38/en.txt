THE JUDICATURE ACT, No. 2  OF 1978
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I, Nimal Siripala De Silva, Minister of
Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms, in consultation with the Chief Justice and the President of the Court of Appeal, do
by this Order, amend the Schedule of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1679/40 of November 10, 2010 as
last amended Gazette Extraordinary No. 2165/46 of March 5, 2020 as follows:
(1) by the substitution for the entry “1. Batticoloa” appearing in Column II under the heading of “District” and its
corresponding entry in Column IV under the heading of “Area of Jurisdiction” appearing against corresponding
Judicial Zone of Batticoloa under Column I of the following entities respectively.
1. Batticoloa The Judicial District of Batticoloa shall comprise the Judicial
Divisions of Batticoloa, Eravur and Kaluwanchikudy.
(2) by the repeal of all the entries relating to Divisional Secretary’s Divisions of Eravur Pattu, Eravur Town, Poratheeva
Pattu, Manmunai South and Eluvil Pattu in Column IV under the heading “Area of Jurisdiction” appearing against
corresponding Judicial Division of Batticoloa under Column III appearing against corresponding Judicial District of
Batticoloa under Column II appearing against corresponding Judicial Zone of Batticoloa under Column I.
