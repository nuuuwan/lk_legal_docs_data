No. 2196/4 –  MONDAY, OCTOBER  05,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
The Land Acquisition Act as amended by Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 
(Chapter 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
                                                                                                
Land Ministry’s Reference  No.: 4-3/16/2017/PT/270. 
My Ref. No.: DIV/MW/LND/3/1/2-7. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land to be acquired : 
 
An allotment of land in total Extent of 0.0127 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Plan No. F. V. P. 1107 
dated 20.05.2020 prepared by the superintendent of 
Surveys Kurunegala in the Village called Paragahadeniya 
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 653 of 
Paragahadeniya of Gandahaya Korale Minor Division in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mawathagama in 
Kurunegala District of the North -Western Province and 
morefully described as follows: 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the land: Kanatigollawaththa -
Badahelakotuwawatta; Description: Homegarden; 
Claimant: Sadikul Munavvara Azees Nizam; Extent: 
0.0127 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby  
required to appear  personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Mawathagama 
Divisional Secretariat, on 11.11.2020 at 10.00 a. m.  and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                         
(in duplicate) on or before 04.11.2020 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount,  
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
  D. P. S.  KUMARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretary’s Division, 
 Mawathagama. 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Mawathagama. 
30th September 2020.
