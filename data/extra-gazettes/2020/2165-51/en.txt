ANTIQUITIES   ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Sub-section 39“a” of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) as amended by the
Act, No. 24 of 1988, I, Minister of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs, do by this Order, appoint the members
referred to in the Schedule hereto the Advisory Committee on Archaeology as effective for a period of 01st of February
2020 to 31st of January 2022.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Buddhasasana, Cultural and Religious Affairs.
Ministry of Buddhasanasa, Cultural and Religious Affairs,
24th February, 2020.
