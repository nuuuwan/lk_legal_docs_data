 Seal GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA
 PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE   PRESIDENT
KNOW YE, that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President, do by this Proclamation summon Parliament to meet in the Chamber of
Parliament, Sri Jayawardenepura, on the Twentieth day of August, Two Thousand and Twenty at 09.30 a.m.
Given at Colombo, on this 13
th day of August, Two Thousand and Twenty.
                                                                                                                               By Order of His Excellency,
P.  B.  JAYASUNDERA,
 Secretary to the President.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING,  SRI LANKA.
