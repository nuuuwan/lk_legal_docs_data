The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  MINUWANGODA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Rathnayake
Mudiyansele Shehan Udith Rathnayaka the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Andavitharanage Wimalasiri Rodreego to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Andavitharanage Wimalasiri Rodreego was selected as the member of the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha.
08th of October, 2020.
