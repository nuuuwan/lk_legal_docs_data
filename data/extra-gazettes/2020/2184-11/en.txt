FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KARANDENIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the Office of  a  return member of Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending
three consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Thommaya Hakuru Nirosh Sanjeewa Ranasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kirahandi Thilakarathna De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kirahandi
Thilakarathna De Silva was selected as the member of  the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
13th July, 2020.
