SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do hereby make an order to waive off the balance amount after collecting Rs. 25 per kg from the applied Special Commodity
Levey of 10% or Rs. 150 per kg on importation of fish to use as fish bait, classified under the HS Heading of 03.02 and
03.03 (Other than the Mackerel fish coming under the HS Code 0303.54), listed out in the Schedule of the Order published
in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2165/12 of 03rd March, 2020, subject to confirmation of the import
having shipped on or before March 04, 2020, by the Bill of Lading (B/L) and recommendation of the Ministry of Fisheries
and Aquatic Resources.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
March 13, 2020.
