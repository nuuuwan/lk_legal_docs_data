FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    VENGALACHEDDIKULAM
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of V engalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Santhakumari
Mahendralingam, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  Sri Lanka Freedom Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Nikkalasraja Pushpalatha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Nikkalasraja Pushpalatha was elected as the member of  the  Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G . L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
25th  September, 2020.
