The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a)
of the (chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Sujith Rathnaweera of the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the United
National Party.
PIUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
29th October, 2020.
