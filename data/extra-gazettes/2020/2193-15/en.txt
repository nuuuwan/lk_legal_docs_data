Divisional Secretary Ref. No.: Hangu/Div.Sec/3/2/1/119.

I hereby inform, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the land/servitude described below which is
required for the construction of Bridge No. 40/2 in
Rikillagaskada - Ragala (B - 413) Road, Thennekumbura.

Secretary's Division of Hanguranketha of Nuwara Eliya
District, described as follows:

Perch (0.0098 Hectare).

the Claimant: Masjithul Anyar Jumma Mosque, No. 144,
