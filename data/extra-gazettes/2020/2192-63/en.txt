L.D.B. 21/78(XV)(A)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, Minister of
Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1482/26 of February 02, 2007 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2182/40 of July 03, 2020), which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Peradeniya,
Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established
under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after the entry  “17. Department of Psychology ”appearing in
Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Art” appearing in Column I of that Schedule of the
following new entry.
“18. Department of English Language Teaching”.
Prof. G . L. P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
08th September, 2020.
