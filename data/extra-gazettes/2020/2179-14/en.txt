No. 2179/14 –  WEDNESDAY, JUNE  10,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
 
Notice under Section  7 
 
 
                
My No.: La/Div.Sec./3/3/7/3. 
Reference No. of Ministry of Lands: 13/96/FR/153. 
 
I do hereby give notice,  that the  Government intends to 
acquire the following land which necessary for a public 
purpose under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, which was amended by the Amended 
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964. 
 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
 
Acquiring of land for Knuckles Conservation Zone is 
denoted as a public purpose in this effort. 
 
 
Details of Allotments Nos. 1492 and 1493 of lands 
called Kalupahana Watta shown in Surveyor General’s 
approved Plan No. F. V. P.  21, Supplement 07, Sheet  
No. 35 and dated 10.09.2019 situated in Walpolamulla 
Village in the No. E -386 Etanwala GN divisions, in 
Laggala Divisional Secretariat in Matale District of 
Central Province, Total extent of allotments contain 
20.2343 Squre Hec. 
 
1. Lot No. 1492 
 
Name of the Land: Walpolamulla; Description: Paddy 
Field (Abundant); Name and Address of the Claimant:              
K. A.  Ellepola (Late) who resided at No. 10, Sappuwatta, 
Palapathwela, Matala; Extent: 1.8970 Squre Hec. 
 
2. Lot No. 1493 
 
Name of the Land: Walpolamulla; Description: 
Forest; Name and Address of the Claimant: K. A.  
Ellepola (Late) who resided at No. 10, Sappuwatta, 
Palapathwela, Matala; Extent: 18.3373  Squre Hec. 
    
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land  are 
hereby  required to appear  personally, or by agents 
before me at 10.00 a. m. On the 08 day of July at 
Divisional Secretariat of Laggala. 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority)
