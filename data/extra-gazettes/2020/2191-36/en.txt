FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    V A VUNIYA   SOUTH  TAMIL
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Vavuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Pathmanathan
Saththiyanathan , the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Ward No. 4 Sasthirikoolankulam of Vavuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Perumal Ramakrishnan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the V avuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Perumal Ramakrishnan was elected as the member of  the ward No. 4  Sasthirikoolankulam of  Vavuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya
Sabha.
M. G . L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd  September, 2020.
