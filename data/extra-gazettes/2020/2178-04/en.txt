EXCISE  ORDINANCE 
 
Excise Notification No. 03/2020 
 
FOOLPROOF  STICKER  AND  STICKER  MANAGEMENT  SYSTEM 
  
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32 read with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Exci se Ordinance 
(Chapter 52), as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development , do hereby 
amend the rules in the Excise Notification No. 04/2019 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2128/30 dated June 
20, 2019, as last amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2164/72 dated March 01, 2020, as follows.  
 
 1. In rule 3, 
 
 (a) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the first proviso, for the words and figures "prior to Sept ember 01, 
2020" of the words and figures "prior to December 31, 2020", and  
 
 (b) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the second proviso, for the words and figures "prior to Sep tember 01, 
2020" of the words and figures "prior to December 31, 2020". 
 
 2. In rule 13, by the substitution for the words and figures "347 days" of the words and figures "561 days".
