PART I : S EC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 10.08.2020
THE   AWARD
The  Honourable Minister of Labour and Labour Relations
by virtue of the powers vested in him by Section 4(1) of the
Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative
Enactments of Ceylon (1956 revised edition) as amended by
Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with
Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968
appointed me as Arbitrator by his order dated 07th February,
2019 and referred the disputes following  to me for settlement
by Arbitration.
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties are :-
Whether Mr. W. A. S. S. Kumara who worked as a Wichman,
Grade ii of Bogasla Graphite Lanka Limited had been caused
injustice by being removed from the post of Winchman
without a proper inquiry saying that he had done some
mistake and being engaged in underground work and if such
injustice had been caused, to what reliefs he is entitled.
Appearance :
Mr. Kithsiri Muhandiram, Human Resources Manager,
appeared for the Party of the Second Part.
There was no appearance by the Part of the First Part.
Parties filed their 1st Statements.
The Party of the First Part failed to appear in 3 consecutive
occasions wherefor the matter was dismissed.
In the circumstances I make no order.
K
APILA M. SARA THCHANDRA,
Arbitrator.
14th  March, 2020.
