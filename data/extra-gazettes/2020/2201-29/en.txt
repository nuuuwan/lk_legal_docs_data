NORTH    CENTRAL    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Announcement  of  the  Honourable  Governor
IN accordance with the provisions of Sub-section 3(8) of the North Central Provincial Road Development Authority Act,
No. 1 of 1998 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1159/37 dated 24.11.2000, I am the Governor of the North Central
Province Mahiepala Herath. The following persons, who have been appointed to the post of the Road Development Authority,
have been removed from their posts on the date on which their names are indicated. As the Governor of the North Central
Province I make this announcement.
Chairman - Mr. Darshana Anuradha Nanayakkara - Since  14/1 1/2020
Member - Mr. I.M. Hettiarachchi (Lawyer) - Sin ce  14/11/2020
Member - Mr. I.M.M.B. Ilangasinghe - Since  14/11/2020
M
AHIEPALA HERATH,
Governor of the North Central Province.
On 14th day of November, 2020
At the Governor’s Office of the North Central Province.
