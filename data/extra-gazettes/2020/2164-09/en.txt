 THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
Order  under  Article 98(8)
BY virtue of the powers vested in the Election Commission under paragraph (8) of Article 98 of the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election Commission does by this Order certifiy that -
The number set out in Column II of the Schedule  hereto be the number of members which each electoral
district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the
Proclamation under Article 97 and under Article  98.
