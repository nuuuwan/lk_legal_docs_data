1A-  G  32465— 67 (07/2020)
REGISTRATION   OF   PERSONS   ACT,   No.   32   OF   1968
Order under Section 34
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016, I, The
President do by this Order, alter the date of expiration published in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the
period of operation of Section 6, 9, 14, 16 and 17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made
under the aforesaid Act (other than the Registration of Persons Regulations 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of
November, 20, 1990) to be 31.12.2020, until the necessary infrastructure, arrangements and technological methodologies are
made to.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
22nd June, 2020.
