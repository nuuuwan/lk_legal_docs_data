Greetings!
Whereas there have been difficulties in the procurement of geological resources for development priorities and to
meet livelihood needs;
And whereas those engage in mining and transporting identified such resources are confronted with difficulties in
the existing process;
And whereas there has been an increase in the cost of construction and provision of services due to the creation of
shortage of supplies of such items compared to the demand;
And whereas my attention is focused on the fact that difficulties have arisen in maintaining the sustainability of the
development process and that the damages have been caused in the conservation of environment and that there are various
irregularities caused;
Having given attention to the need for the methodologies to the identified in maintaining the continued conservation
of environment and development activities in the construction industry without a setback;
Now therefore I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka reposing great trust
and confidence in your prudence and ability as those directly responsible for formulating the National Plan in the context of the
collective responsibility of this role do, by these presents appoint you, the said:

to be members of the Presidential Task Force to make a study of the exploitation of Geological Resources for
development priorities and self-employment and to meet smallish economic livelihood needs of those engaged in traditional
vocations and the sources and methodologies of extraction of such resources and accomplish the tasks detailed below :
1. To identify separately development priorities utilizing such Geological resources like metals, sand and earth as main
raw materials and assess the quantities of supply required on a yearly basis.
2.  To identify and remark areas and places throughout the Island giving priority of attention to the environmental
conservation where the above mentioned raw materials could be obtained the said development priorities.
3. To identify scientific and simple methodologies in carrying out mining in such places and study the methodolo-
gies adopted for securing raw materials with prior approval.
4. To obtain views and suggestions of the persons traditionally resident in the neighbourhood of the localities where
geological resources such as metal, sand and earth available as well as of those producers exploiting the such resources and
those engaged in the construction industry.

5. To review the existing legal provisions in respect of the supply mining and transportation requested as basic
construction raw materials, minerals resources such as metal, sand and earth in order to service the needs of the development
projects, self-employment and Traditional vocations as well as smallish economic livelihood and introducing and implement
simple and efficient methodologies integrating charges and state levies in force.
6. To review the existing methodology of licenses issuing  the said purposes and revisit the out dated rules and
regulations, institutional and administrative structures in place and make proposals for reforms in order to update them as well
as proposals to appropriately update complex administrative systems and circulars now in force.
7. To introduce a methodology facilitating the grant of all approvals through a single institutions structure in order to
avoid unnecessary delays being experience in obtaining approvals required for construction work.
8. To identify division wise agricultural lands where various crops are commercially cultivatable, inland fisheries
farms including prawn farming, cattle farming and poultry farming, animal husbandry farms industry and investment zones,
container yards and stores complexes, mining  sites of geological resources such as sand ,earth,  metal etc. Urban development
and housing schemes, information technology zones, tourist zones and centers highways and urban facilities, play grounds
and parks, forests and places of natural scenic beauty etc. to be prioritized as national pogramme and also identify jointly with
the divisional secretaries and other officials by carrying out environmental assessments, approvals and assessments by the
government valuer the places where infrastructure projects that meet public requirements could be launched and make
recommendations to publish such places in the government gazette with the prior approval.
And, I do hereby appoint, J.J. Ratnasiri Esquire, Secretary to the Ministry of Public Services, Provincial Councils and
Local Government as the Chairman of the said Task Force.
And I do hereby appoint, Mr. K. R. Gangadhara, Senior Assistant Secretary to the President as the Secretary of the
said Task Force.
And I do hereby authorize the said Task Force to make such inquiries and issue such instructions as required for the
purpose of executing the tasks entrusted.
And I do hereby authorize you, the members of the said Task Force to present to me reports from time to time.
And you are further required to report to me within 45 days in relation to the places and methodologies that could be
prioritized in the accomplishment of this task.
And I do hereby require and direct all public officers and other persons to whom the said Task Force may issue
instructions or from whom assistance for provision of services may be required, to comply with all such instructions, render all
such assistance and furnish all such information as may be properly complied with, rendered and furnished on that behalf.
And I do hereby direct the said Task Force to report to me, all cases of delay or default on the part of any Public Officer
or Officer of any Ministry, Government Department, State Corporation or other similar institution in the discharge of duties and
responsibilities assigned to such public officer or such institution.
