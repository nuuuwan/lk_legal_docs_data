TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS  the Trustees of  the “Sree Narayana Dharma Sangam Colombo Trust” namely -
1. Krishnan Jayakumar,
2. Ven. Dr. Madampagama Assagi Thero,
3. Dr. Ampalam Kandasamy
4. Thoppil Sathyapalan Prakash,
5. Abiramipillai Kailasapillai,
6. Rienzie Anicetus Fernando Arsecularatna, P. C.,
7. Anthony Randev Dinendra John.
have applied to the Minister of Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms for their incorporation under Section 114 of the
Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87) ;
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Nimal Siripala de Silva, Minister
of Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms do by this Order authorize  the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and
declare that-

(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
Declaration of Trust bearing No. 169 of July 27, 2019 and August 5, 2019 attested by Saravanan Neelakandan,
Notary Public of Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpectual succession and a common seal under the
name and style of  the “Sree Narayana Dharma Sangam Colombo Trust ” ;
(b) the said Trustees may, under the said name and style, purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of  any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust ; and
(c) the said Trustees shall, by the said name and style, be able and competent in law to sue and to be sued, to
answer and to be answered, to defend and be defended, in any court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and
actions arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time  to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
N
IMAL SIRIPALA DE SILV A,
Minister of Justice, Human Rights and Legal Reforms.
