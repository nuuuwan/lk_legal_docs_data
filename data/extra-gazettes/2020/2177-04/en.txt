1A- PG  5020 — 567  (06/2020)
PORTS  AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT    LEVY   ACT,   No.   18   OF   2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do hereby this Order, amend
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2152/32 of December 05, 2019, as amended, by the addition of the following Sub-
paragraph immediately after Sub-paragraph (i) of Paragraph 2.
(m) Raw materials and packing materials imported by the pharmaceutical manufacturers for the manufacture of
pharmaceuticals, on the recommendation of the Secretary to the Ministry of Health and Indigenous Medical
Services, subject to the approval of the Director General of Customs.
            This Order shall come into effective from May 26, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
