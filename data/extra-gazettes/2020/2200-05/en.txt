PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - PG  5210 — 65 (11/2020)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
Negambo Urban Council
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Negambo Urban Council do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Welappuge Don Lalith Tensil of the
Negambo Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that Negambo Urban Council as he has been ceased to be a
member of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Negambo Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
04th November, 2020.
