FOREIGN  EXCHANGE  ACT,  No. 12 OF 2017
Order under Section  22
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 22 of the Foreign Exchange Act, No. 12 of 2017 (the Act), I,
Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance do by this Order, extend the period of validity of the Order under Section 22 of the
Act published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2182/37 dated 02nd July, 2020, for further six months from
02nd January, 2021.
