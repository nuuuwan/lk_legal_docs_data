The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KALMUNAI MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kalmunai Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the T amil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Kaththamuththu Ganesh the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned  member of Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Vadivetkarasu Chandiran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of   Kalmunai Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Vadivetkarasu Chandiran was selected as the member of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
07th  January, 2020.
