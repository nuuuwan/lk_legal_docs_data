SUPREME  COURT  RULES  –  2018
Notification under Rule 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Rule 1 of the Supreme Court Rules 2018, I, Jayantha Chandrasiri Jayasuriya,
Chief Justice, do by this notification appoint 15th of October, 2020 as the date on which Rules 1, 3 and 4 of the Supreme
Court Rules of 2018 shall come into force.
J. C. JAYASURIYA,
Chief Justice.
