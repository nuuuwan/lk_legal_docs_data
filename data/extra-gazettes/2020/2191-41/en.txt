Government Notifications
SPORTS LAW No. 25 OF 1973
Regulations made under the Section 32
I, Namal Rajapaksa, the Minister of Youth and Sports as per the powers vested in me under the Section 32 (c) of the Sports
Law No. 25 of 1973 cancel the registration of the Ballroom Dancers Federation of Sri Lanka which is the National Federation
for the Sport of Ballroom Dancers with immediate effect.
NAMAL RAJAPAKSA (M. P.), 
Minister of Youth and Sports.
At Colombo,
On 31st day of  August, 2020.
