SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto the Special Commodity Levy at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 03 months commencing from October 14, 2020 subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2186/32 of 31.07.2020 in respect of B'Onion and the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2176/
18 of 21.05.2020 in respect of Lentils, Canned Fish and Sugar are rescinded with effect from October 14, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
October 13, 2020.
