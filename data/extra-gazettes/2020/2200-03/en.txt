Order No. 70
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no Producer/Miller shall sell, expose or offer for sale or display for sale the varieties of rice listed
below, above the Maximum Prices given hereunder :
Variety Maximum
Price
White/Red Samba - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Suduru Samba) Rs. 94/- per kg
White/Red Raw Samba Rs. 94/- per kg
White/Red Nadu - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari) Rs. 92/- per kg
White/Red Raw Rice Rs. 89/- per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 04th November, 2020.
                                                      By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA  DISSANAYAKE (Retd.)
Chairman.
Colombo,
04th November, 2020.
