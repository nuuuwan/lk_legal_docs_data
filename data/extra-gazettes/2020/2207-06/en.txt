CODE  OF  CRIMINAL  PROCEDURE   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT   No.  2   OF   2013
Order  under  Sub-section  (2) of Section 7
By  virtue of the powers vested in me under Subsection (2) of Section 7 of the Code of Criminal Procedure (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 2013, I, M. U. M. Ali Sabry, Minister of Justice, do by this Order extend for a further period of
two years the operation of the provisions of the aforesaid Act, with effect from February 2, 2021.
M. U. M. A
LI SABRY,
