PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1   OF  1981
Notice under Section 25 (3)
NOTICE is hereby given that :
(a) The death occurred on June 10, 2020 of Mr. Jayawardhanage Sunil Perera, who was a candidate nominated by
the Independent Group 26 for the Electoral District No. 1 - Colombo at the General Election to be held on
August 5, 2020 ;
(b) Notwithstanding such death, the nomination paper submitted by Independent Group 26 for that electoral district
with the omission of the name of the said Mr. Jayawardhanage Sunil Perera and the inclusion of the name of
Mrs. Awilikara Galappaththige Priyanthi  Aruna  Kapukotuwa who has been nominated by the Independent
Group 26 under Sub-section (2) of Section 25 of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 is valid, and that
the poll in such Electoral District shall be taken on August 5, 2020 as specified in the notice published under
Section 24 of the aforesaid Act.
