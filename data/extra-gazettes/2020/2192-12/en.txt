THE  HOLIDAY  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
Order under Section  11(1)(b)
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11(1)(b) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Chamal Rajapaksa,
State Minister of Internal Security, Home Affairs and Disaster Management, do, by this Order declare that the half
days specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an additional Bank Holidays for the purpose of the Act, in addition to
the days for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to that Act.
 C
HAMAL RAJAPAKSA,
State Minister of Internal Security,
Home Affairs and Disaster Management.
