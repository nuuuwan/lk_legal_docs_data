SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY   ACT,   No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do hereby make an order to waive off ;
the balance amount after collecting Rs. 25 per kg from the applied Special Commodity levy of 10% or Rs. 100
per kg on  the importation of fish, classifiable under the HS Heading of 03.02 and 03.03 (Other than the Mackerel
fish coming under HS Code 0303.54), listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2143/3 of 30th September, 2019.
In terms of this Order, a Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 25 per Kg on importation of fish (HS Heading of 03.02
and 03.03, (other than the Mackerel fish coming under HS Code 0303.54) shall be collected for a period of 3 months
commencing from February 07th, 2020.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and
Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
06th February 2020.
