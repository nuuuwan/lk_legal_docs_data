The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MANMUNAI   SOUTH   WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of the
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Sellaih
Nahuleswaran the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of that Ward 01 - Ambalanthurai of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kumarasingam Weerasingam to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kumarasingam Weerasingam was elected as the member of   the Ward 01 - Ambalanthurai of Manmunai South West
Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
1st July, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 03.07.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MANMUNAI   SOUTH   WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a  return member of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of the
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Kanapathipillai Punniyawathi the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member if Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kanahasooriyam Kokilamalar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kanahasooriyam Kokilamalar was elected as the member of  the Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
1st July, 2020.
