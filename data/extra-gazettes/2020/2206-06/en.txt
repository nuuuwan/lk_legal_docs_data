Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING OF A VACANCY IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER OF  THE PALLEPOLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)

WHEREAS the office of return member of Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Yodage Nila
Shanthi Kumari Jayathissa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of United National Party, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Elwala Gedara Sithara Dilrukshi Nissanka to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer of the Pallepola
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Elwala Gedara Sithara Dilrukshi Nissanka was selected as the member of the Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
P.A.K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
10th December, 2020.
