NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTIONS   7(4)  AND  7(5)  OF
THE   PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1   OF  1981
IN terms of the powers vested in the Election Commission under Sections 7(4) and 7(5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No.
1 of 1981, it is hereby notified that the political parties named below have the right to be treated as Recognized Political Parties
for the purpose of elections, with effect from the 13th day of October 2020.
Name of the Political Party Symbol allotted to the Political Party
Arunalu Janatha Peramuna Water Tap
People’s Servant’s Party Guitar
New Democratic Marxist Leninist Party Kettle
Pivithuru Hela Urumaya Flower
Samaththuva Kachchi Shield
Sinhala Rawaya Diamond
