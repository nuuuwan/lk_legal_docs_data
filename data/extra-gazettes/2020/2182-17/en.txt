FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KATTANKUDY    URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the Office of  a  return member of Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Muslim National Alliance, the recognized political party to which Ahamathulebbe
Ali Akbar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of Muslim National Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Asanarlebbe Deen Fairoos to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Asanarlebbe Deen Fairoos was
selected as the member of  the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
29th June, 2020.
