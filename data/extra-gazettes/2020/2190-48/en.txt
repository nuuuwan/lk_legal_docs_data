VA VUNIYA    SOUTH    SINHALA    PRADESHIYA    SABHA
Approving Standard By - Laws which are to Northern Province Pradeshiya Sabha
LOCAL  AUTHORITY  (STANDARD  BY -  LAWS)  ACT   No. 06  OF 1952
ACCORDING to the resolution made on 24.10.2019 under No. VSSPS/2019/10/24/165 by the Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya
Sabha Council in the monthly meeting. I do hereby informed that the Standard by Law under Act, No. 12 of 1989 read with
section 2 sub section (1) of Act, No. 06 of 1952 approved to implement in the Northern Province (standard by - law) Act,
No. 06 of 1952 Standard by-Law from chapter 07 to chapter 09 which are published in the (Extraordinary) Gazette Notification
No. 2092/6 dated 08.10.2018 and which are implement in the limit of the V avuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha situated at
V avuniya District from the date which are published which are applicable the Pradeshiya Sabha in the Northern Province were
prepared by the Minister of Local Government  of Northern Province according to the virtue of powers vested to the Minister
of Local Government under Act, No. 6 of 1952 read with  para 1 sub section (1) Section 2 of Provision of Provincial Council
Act,  No. 12 of 1989 (inter direct provisions of result).
S. M. K
ASUN THAYAGA SRI ALAGALLA,
Chairman,
Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha.
