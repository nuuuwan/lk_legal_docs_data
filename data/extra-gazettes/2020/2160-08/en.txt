FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   EMBILIPITIYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka  Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasinghe Gama Arachchige Don Dayananda Weerasinghe the  member belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that No. 15 Thunkama of  Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and
who shall  be declared elected  as member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Weerasingha Liyanagamage Ranjith to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that
Weerasingha Liyanagamage Ranjith was elected as the member of the No. 15 Thunkama of Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning officer,
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
24th of January, 2020.
