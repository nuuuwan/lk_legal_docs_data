NOTIFICATION   UNDER   THE   FINANCE  ACT,  No.  35  OF  2018
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Section 51 read with Section 19 of the Finance (Luxury Tax on Motor
Vehicle) Act, No. 35 of 2018, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Developmet, do by this
Order amend the Regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2147/72 dated October 31, 2019,
by the substitution for the paragraph (b) of the Regulation 4 thereto, of the following.
“(b) A petrol motor vehicle of which the cylinder capacity not exceeding 1800 cc or a diesel motor vehicle of
which the cylinder capacity not exceeding 2300 cc or an electric motor vehicle of which the motor power of the
engine not exceeds 200 kW, imported into Sri Lanka  based on a Letter of Credit established within the period from
March 06, 2019 to October 31, 2019 subject to no amendment is made to the description or quantity or technical
specifications of such motor vehicle in the said Letter of Credit and value or expiry date of the said Letter of Credit
and released from the Sri Lanka Customs on or before May 31, 2020, shall not be subject to the Luxury Tax on
Motor Vehicles.”
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic, and
Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
February 20, 2019.
