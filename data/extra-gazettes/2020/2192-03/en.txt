FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN  THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF   THE   POLONNARUWA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION   66A (1) (a)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three
consecutive meetings without Permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Pakkeer Mohidin
Sahul Hameed the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that No. 11, Kaduruwela of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Udapala Gamarallage Rathnasiri Gamage to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Polonnaruwa Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Udapala Gamarallage
Rathnasiri Gamage was elected as the member of the No. 11 Kaduruwela of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council.
P
IUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Polonnaruwa Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
07th September, 2020.
