TOWN  AND   COUNTRY  PLANNING   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER  269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Mahinda Rajapakse, Minister of Urban Development and Housing, do by this Order :-
(a) declare that the area of land called the “Kolawenigama Raja Maha Vihara Area” specified in the Schedule hereto shall
be an urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance ; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Kolawenigama Raja Maha Vihara Area” which is
declared as above to be an urban development area.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSE, M. P.
Minister of Urban Development and Housing .
Colombo,
13th November , 2020.

SCHEDULE
The Area of land comprising the Kolawenigama Raja Maha Vihara Area
The area of land containing in extent of 2.2419 Hectares shown by the Surveyor General’s Tracing No. MR/KTP/
2019/382 dated 8th July 2019 and situated in the village of Kolawenigama within the Minor Division of Morawak Korale in the
Kotapola Divisional Secretary’s Division in the District of Matara in the Southern Province and bounded as follows :
Lot No. A
North : Paddy land and Gin Ganga ;
East : Gin Ganga and Paddy land ;
South : Paddy land ;
West : Paddy land and Road (RDA).

The Area of land comprising the Bingiriya Devagiri Raja Maha Vihara Area
