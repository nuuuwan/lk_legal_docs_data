FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Umachandra Pragash the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kamani Nalika Serasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kamani
Nalika Serasinghe was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
14th May, 2020.
