THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: 6/3/526.
IT is hereby notified under Subsection (1) of Section 7 of the
land Acquisition Act that the government intends to acquire
the land detailed hereunder, required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land intended to be acquired:
The land marked as lot 1,2 in the basic plan of survey general
bearing ( uq'ms'l') 4763 dated 24.12.2019 in extent 4.0469
Hectares and situated in Ovitiyagala village, in Horana
Divisional Secretary's Division in the District of Kalutara, in
Western Province.
01. Lot  No. 1
Name of  land: Ellakanda watta; Description: Estate, (watta);
Claiment: Government;  Extent: 0.0422 Hectare.
G 32132 - 27 (2020/03)
02. Lot  No. 2
Name of  land: Ellakanda watta; Description: Estate;
Claiment: Government;  Extent: 4.0047 Hectare.
All those who are claiming ownership or any other
connection with the aforesaid land or those who are
authorized by their documentary factors should meet me at
10.00 a.m. on 25 March 2020, at Divisional Secretariat Horana.
The facts too for requesting compensation regarding the
acquisition of the aforesaid land in keeping with the nature of
the ownership claimed for the aforesaid land and the
compensation amount and the way it was calculated should
be informed to me in writing with two copies thereof before
18th March 2020.
