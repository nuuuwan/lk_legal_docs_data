PRADESHIYA  SABHAS   ACT,  No.  15  OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Sections 2, 4, 6 and 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be
read with Section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Public
Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils & Local Government do by this Order, further amend the order bearing
No. 03-807/6 under Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2061/4 of 05th March 2018 by
substitution of the words “Agarapathana Pradeshiya Sabha, Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha and Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya
Sabha” for the words “Agarapathana Pradeshiya Sabha and Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha”.
J
ANAKA B ANDARA T ENNAKOON ,
Minister of Public Administration,
Home Affairs, Provincial Councils
& Local Government.
Colombo,
06th January, 2020.
