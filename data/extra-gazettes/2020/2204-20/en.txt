FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     DIVULAPITIYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)  AS   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,   No.   16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Sakalasooriya
Appuhamilage Nanda Kumara Sakalasooriya,  the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thambadiya Teshan Indika Jayasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Thambadiya Teshan Indika Jayasinghe  elected
as Chairman of  the  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st  December, 2020.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 03.12.2020
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     DIVULAPITIYA
PRADESHIYA    SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)  AS   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES    ELECTION
(AMENDMENT)    ACT,   No.   16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  elected as
Chairman ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Thambadiya
Teshan Indika Jayasinghe,  the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party  who shall be
declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Munasinghe Arachchige Ranjith Munasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Munasinghe Arachchige Ranjith Munasinghe
elected as Vice Chairman of  the  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st  December, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF    MEMBER   OF   THE     DIVULAPITIYA
PRADESHIYA      SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the Party ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Warnakulasooriya Ayesha Madhushani Warnakulasooriya Fernando,  the member belonged, was required by me under
Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  People’s Liberation Front,  who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dhammika Kalyani Nanayakkara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
Dhammika Kalyani Nanayakkara selected as the member of  the  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st  December, 2020.
