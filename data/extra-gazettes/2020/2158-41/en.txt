CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IN terms of Paragraph (5) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, His
Excellency the President has directed to assign the subjects and functions to Non Cabinet Ministers.
It is now hereby notify that subjects, functions, departments, public corporations and statutory institutions for
the State Minister of Urban Development, the State Minister of Water Supply Facilities and the State Minister of Housing
have set out in Schedule given below from the 06th January, 2020.
Dr. (Eng.) P
IYATH B ANDU WICKRAMA,
 Secretary,
Ministry of Urban Development,
Water Supply and Housing Facilities.
Ministry of Urban Development, Water Supply and Housing Facilities,
Battaramulla,
13th January, 2020.
