SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby make
an Order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 6 per kg from the applied Special Commodity Levy of 10% or
Rs. 150 per  kg whichever is higher on Jack and horse mackerel (Trachurus spp. classified under the HS Code 0303.55) under
HS Heading 03.03 listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2165/12 of
03rd March 2020, on importation by any canned fish producers registered with the Ministry of Fisheries and Aquatic
Resources on the recommendation of the Ministry of  Fisheries and Aquatic Resources.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  03 months commencing from  May 22,
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
21st May, 2020.
