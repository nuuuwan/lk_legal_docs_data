 and
      Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05.
STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. R. M. P. Nihal Rajapakse who worked as a Foreman of the Sri Lanka Transport Board had been caused injustice 
by termination of his employment, following the interdiction on 10.06.2005 and a disciplinary inquiry conducted even though 
he had been acquitted and discharged later from Attanagalle Magistrate Court Case No. 74664/pc in 2013 regarding the same 
matter and if such injustice had been caused, to what relief he is entitled.
 A. Wංආൺඅൺඐൾൾඋൺ,
 Commissioner General of Labour.
