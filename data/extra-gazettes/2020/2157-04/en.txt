THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: KHDS/LND/03/ACQ/08.
Ministry of Land and Parliamentary Reforms Ref. No.:
LD/05/2007/FR/362.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, (Amendment), No.28 of 1964 that
the Government intends to acquire the following land which
is required for a Public Purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Allotment of land in total extent about 12.9373 Hectare
depicted Lot No. 839 in the Preliminary Plan F.V .P . No. 701
Supplement No. 35 Sheet No. 35 and dated 12.09.2019
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village of
Pannila Minor Division of Pannil Pattu and Atakalan Korale,
Divisional Secretary of Kahawatta in Rathnapura District
Sabaragamuwa Province:
1. Lot No. 839
Name of the Land: Trinkowatta, Pannilkanda Mukalana;
Description: Forests; Claimant: A.A.Manel Perera, No. 81,
Rosmid Place, Colombo; Extent: 12.9373 Hectare.
All persons interested, in the aforesaid land/lands on
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kahawatta on 12th
February , 2020  at 10.00 O' clock.
 and are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before  the 05th February, 2020 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
                                                     B.T.GAYANI I.KARUNARATHNA,
                                                                Divisional Secretary,
Kahawatta.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Kahawatta.
30th December, 2019.
