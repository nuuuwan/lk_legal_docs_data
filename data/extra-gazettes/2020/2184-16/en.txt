THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of  Lands and
Land Development, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of  Section
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below in the
column I is  needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under
the provisions of that Act. The land of which  the said servitude is
acquired is described in the column II.
1. Description of the Servitude: Acquisition of its servitude to
use the private access road from 120 Papiliyana bus route to the
allotment Acquired to the Government for developing the Nugegoda
canal under Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage and Environment
Improvement  Project.
