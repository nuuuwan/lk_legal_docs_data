CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC    SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
I, hereby declare that I endue power to Hon. Kanaka Herath, Minister of State of Port Development Affairs to monitor and
fulfill specific functions listed in the following schedule with my concurrence in terms of the provisions of Article 44(5)
of the constitution, the powers and responsibilities vested in me as the Minister of Ports and Shipping of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister of State will still have the opportunity to investigate, intervene and
coordinate all functions within this scope.
J
OHNSTON  F ERNANDO ,
 Minister of Ports and Shipping.
Ministry of  Ports and Shipping,
Colombo 01,
20th January, 2020.
