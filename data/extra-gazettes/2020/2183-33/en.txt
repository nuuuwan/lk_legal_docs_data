SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
2019
Fourth Supplementary Financial Statement
GRANTING   PERMISSION  BY  VIRTUE  OF  POWERS  VESTED  IN  ME  UNDER  SECTION  27(A)  OF  PROVINCIAL
COUNCIL  ACT,  NO. 42  OF  1987  AS  THE  TERM  OF  THE  SIXTH  SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF   SRI  LANKA  ENDED  ON  26TH  SEPTEMBER,  2017
2019
1. THIS permission may be cited as "Permission to disburse under Fourth Financial Statement 2019 of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council" and come in to operation from 12.12.2019.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Four Billion  Fifty Seven
Million Eight Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,057,806,197.24)
for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2019 and ending 31st December, 2019, which is specified as
"Financial Year 2019" in the Budget Estimate 2019, that was approved on 10.04.2019 and amended by First, Second and
third Supplementary Financial Statement 2019, is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Four Billion Three Hundred and
Ninety Seven Million one Hundred and Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Five and Cents Twenty Four
Only (Rs. 34,397,173,645.24) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers
to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
3. Accordingly the first schedule of the said Gazette Notification  is amended as the first schedule hereof and grant
permission to disburse the same.
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk

AUTHORIZA TION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
disburse as per the Fourth Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2019, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Four Billion  Fifty Seven
Million, Eight Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,057,806,197.24)
for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2019 and ending 31st December, 2019, which is specified in annual
financial statement 2019 and amended by third supplementary estimate 2019 is hereby amended as, Rupe es Thirty Four
Billion Three Hundred and Ninety Seven Million, One Hundred and Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Five
and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,397,173,645.24) and grant permission to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be expired on 31st of December, 2019.
TIKIRI KOBBEKADUWA,
Honourable Governor - Sabaragamuwa Province.
12th day of December, 2019.
AUTHORIZA TION
I do hereby approve to amend the advance account limits stated in the fifth Schedule of the Fourth Supplementary Financial
Statement for the year 2019, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987,
as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th
September, 2017.
Accordingly, I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council to bear the expenses subject to those limits.
This permission will be expired on 31st of December, 2019.
TIKIRI KOBBEKADUWA,
Honourable Governor - Sabaragamuwa Province.
12th day of December, 2019.
