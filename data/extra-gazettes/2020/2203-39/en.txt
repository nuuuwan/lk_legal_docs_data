SRI    LANKA    PORTS    AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.    51   OF   1979
Order under Section 25(1)
Order made by the Minister of Ports and Shipping under Section 25(1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 as
amended by Act, No. 07 of 1984, Act, No. 35 of 1984 and Act, No. 2 of 1992.
ORDER
The Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2195/26 of 29th September, 2020 on this matter is hereby amended by
the substitution of Schedule Nos. 01 to 03 thereof by the following new Schedule Nos. 01 to 03. The said allotments of lands
specified in new Schedule Nos. 01 to 03 hereto being required for the purposes of the business of the Sri Lanka Ports
Authority a Statutory Body established by the aforementioned Act to be acquired under the Land Acquisition Act and
transferred under the Act to the Sri Lanka Ports Authority.
P. R
OHITHA ABEYGUNA WARDHANA,
Minister of Ports and Shipping.
At Colombo,
24th November, 2020.
