The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER OF THE MATARA PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  66A  (1) OF THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Matara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation.
And whereas leader of the Independent group, to which Makawita Liyanage Pathum Priyantha the member belonged,
was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Independent
group who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance
(chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the leader of the aforesaid Independent group has communicated his decision to nominate Nammuni
Kankanam Vidana Mahadurage Ajith Hemantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, returning officer of the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Nammuni Kankanam Vidana Mahadurage Ajith Hemantha was selected as the member of the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara District,
21st August, 2020.
