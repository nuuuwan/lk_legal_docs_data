THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
             My No.: UDU/2/5/2/8.
Land Ministry No.: 13/97/FR/158.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition Amendment Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
for public purpose (the conservation of Dumbara mountain
range.)
Description of the Land / servitude to be Acquired:-
An allotment of land called "Liyankobulla watta" bearing
an extent of about 16.2420 Hectare mentioned in P. P. Maha
7002 Supplement (Lot No. 1) of the Surveyor General, situated
in the Grama Niladhari Division of Karambakatiya, in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Udadumbara of Kandy
District, Central Province.
Name and Address of Claimants:–
Mr. S. M. Z. K. Abdeen, 70, Egadahena, Borupana,
Rathmalana; Mr. A. S. A. Siddik, No. 92, Kandy Road,
Madawala Bazzar, Madawala; Mr. A. S. S. Firdawuse, No. 92,
Kandy Road, Madawala Bazzar, Madawala; Mr. A. S. M.
Mafaas, No. 92, Kandy Road, Madawala Bazzar, Madawala;
Mr. A. Z. M. Manaaseer, No. 92, Kandy Road, Madawala Bazzar,
Madawala.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Udadumbara, on the 25th
November 2020 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 18th November 2020
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
S
UJANI R. WIJETUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Udadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat,
