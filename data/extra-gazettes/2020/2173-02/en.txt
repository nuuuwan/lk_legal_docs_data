The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   COLOMBO MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the  United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which
Charitha Maithree Gunarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon returned  member of United National Freedom Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Oshada Malshan Rathnaweera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the  Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Oshada Malshan Rathnaweera was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
27th April, 2020.
