WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
The order made under Section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of Provincial
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Roshan Gunathilaka,  the  Governor   of  Western   Province,   by  virtue   of   powers  vested   in  me  as   per provisions
stated in Sub-section 185 (2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, do hereby appoint Retired Judge of Court of Appeal Hon. Chandradasa
Nanayakkara as a Single-person Board  of  Inquiry  to investigate whether the Mr. A. N. K. Sakalasooriya, the Chairman of
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, while functioning in his office has committed the offences stipulated under Sub-section 185
(1) of the above    mentioned Pradeshiya  Sabha  Act  on  account  of the  following  mentioned  matters/  allegations  and
present  a  report within 03 months.
Whereas, the matters/ allegations to be investigated in relation to Mr. A. N. K. Sakalasooriya, the Chairman of
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha :-
I. While functioning as the Chairman of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, due to the action taken to purchase dhal and
tinned fish (Salmon) from a private trading place at a price higher than the Government controlled  price by spending
the advance  money obtained from the General Fund  of  Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya  Sabha  during the  period  of
30.03.2020  to  08.04.2020  or  a period adjacent to such and thereby committing an offence under Section/ Sections
185(l)(c) and/ or 185 (1) (e) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987.

II. While functioning as the Chairman of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, due to the action taken to sell tinned fish (Salmon)
and dhal, purchased by spending money from the Fund of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, to consumer public at a
price higher than the Government controlled price during the period of 30.03.2020 to 08.04.2020 or a period adjacent to
such and thereby committing an offence under Section/ Sections 185 (l)(c) and/ or 185 (l)(e) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987.
Ill.      While functioning as the Chairman of Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha, due to the action taken to sell food items to the
public of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha area of authority at a higher price exceeding the Government controlled price
contrary  to  the  instructions  of Circular No. LGD/ 2A/General dated 24.03.2020 issued by the Commissioner of Local
Government of Western Province in relation to “Providing Necessary Consumer Goods to the Public to Suppress
Covid - 19 Virus” and thereby committing an offence under Section 185(1) (d) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987.
Further, according to the provisions stated in Sub section 185(2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with Section
2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I hereby announce that this Single - person Board
of Inquiry is entitled to the powers entrusted to  a Commissioner appointed under Commissions of Inquiry Act and the
following schedule includes written and oral evidences identified as applicable when examining the above mentioned matters.
Moreover,according to the powers vested in me under Sub-section  185 (3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read
with Section 2 of  Provincial Councils (Consequential  Provisions) Act,  No. 12 of  1989, I hereby announce  that subject to
the  above  mentioned  inquiry, the  Chairmanship of the  Chairman  of Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya  Sabha,
S.A.N.K. Sakalasooriya  will  be suspended  with  effect from  20th  of  May 2020.
Furthermore, conforming to the  provisions  stated further  under  Sub-section  185(3)  of the  Pradeshiya Sabha Act,  No. 15
of 1987 read with Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential  Provisions) Act,  No. 12 of 1989, I hereby announce
further that the Deputy Chairman of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha T. T. lndika Jayasinghe  is  hereby  ordered  by  me to
operate  the  powers and  perform functions  of the  said Chairman’s Office suspended thus, in Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha.
