THE  HOLIDAY  ACT,  No. 29 OF  1971
Order  under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971,  I,  Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of   Public Administration, Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government, do by this order, declare that,
as regards the year 2021, each of the days specified in the Schedule hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of
this Act.
