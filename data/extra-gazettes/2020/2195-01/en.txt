FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    V A VUNIYA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Badurdeen
Muhammadu Faiz, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  Sri Lanka Freedom Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Jakkariyas Salistan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jakkariyas
Salistan was selected as the member of  the  Vavuniya Urban Council
M. G . L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
25th  September, 2020.
