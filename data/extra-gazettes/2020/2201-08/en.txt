Order No. 71
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority hereby orders that no Importer, Distributor, Supplier or Trader shall sell, expose, offer, or display for
sale or supply for sale the following item above the Maximum Price given below :
Item Maximum Retail Maximum Retail Importer’ s Maximum
Price Price Wholesale
(Packeted) (Unpacketed) Price
White Sugar Rs. 90/- per kg. Rs. 85/- per kg. Rs. 80/- per kg.
This Order shall become operative with effect from 10th November, 2020.
                                                      By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. General S
HANTHA  DISSANAYAKE (Retd.)
Chairman.
