 NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Announcement  of the Honourable Governor
IN accordance with the provisions of Sub-section 8 of the North Central Provincial Road Development Authority
Act, No. 1 of 1988 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1159/37 dated 24.11.2000, I am the Governor of the
North Central Province Tissa Vitharana. The following persons, who have been appointed to the post of the road development
authority, have been removed from their posts on the date on which their names are indicated. As the Governor of the
North Central Province I make this announcement.
Chairman - Mr. M. Herath Banda - Since 2020.01.20
Member - Mr. D. M. S. Dissanayaka - Since 2019.12.18
T
ISSA VITHARANA,
Governor of the North Central Province.
at the Governor's Office of the North Central Province,
On 20th day of January 2020.
