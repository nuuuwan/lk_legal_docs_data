FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   WELIVITIYA - DIVITHURA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being
ceased to be a member of othe party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Janasetha Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Dammullage
Rohana Wijayalal the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Janasetha Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Loku Enderage Sandun Rathnayaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Loku
Enderage Sandun Rathnayaka was selected as the member of  the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
19th June, 2020.
