NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Badulla Municipal Council
I, H. I. R. Hathurusinghe, Returning Officer of the Badulla Municipal Council, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a)
of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Balage Athar Silva of the Badulla Municipal Council
has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a member of the Sri Lanka Podujana
Peramuna.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
