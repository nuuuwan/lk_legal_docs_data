The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   BANDARAGAMA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Abesinghe Korale Arachchige Nimal Abeysinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that Ward No. 15 - Mahabellana of Bandaragama Pradeshiya
Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Praneeth Welivitage to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of    Bandaragama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do
hereby declare that Praneeth Welivitage was elected as the member of the Ward No. 15 - Mahabellana of Bandaragama
Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA J AYAWEERA F ERNANDO,
Returning officer,
Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
03rd March, 2020.
