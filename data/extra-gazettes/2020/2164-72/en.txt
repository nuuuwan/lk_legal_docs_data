 EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 01/2020
FOOL PROOF   STICKER   AND   STICKER   MANAGEMENT   SYSTEM
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32 read with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance
(Chapter 52), as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development, do hereby
amend the rules in the Excise Notification No. 04/2019 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2128/30 dated June
20, 2019 as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2147/75 dated November 01, 2019, as follows :-
1. In rule 3,
(a) by the repeal of the first proviso and substitution therefore of the following :
“Provided however,
(i) an importer, prior to September 20, 2019, and
(ii) a licensed manufacturer, prior to September 01, 2020,
shall take steps to affix, the existing stock of excisable articles referred to in Schedule II with a sticker”. and
(b) by the repeal of second proviso there to and the substitution therefore of  following :
