The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE    RUWANWELLA     PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66B (2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
(AMENDMENT)   ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  the  Chairman of  Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being elected to
Parliament ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which
Lokuhelapitiyage Sudath Manjula  the former Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as Chairman  within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Narangoda Ralalage Manjula Priyashantha Nawarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66B (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Narangoda Ralalage Manjula Priyashantha Nawarathna  elected as
Chairman of  the   Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
18th  November, 2020.
