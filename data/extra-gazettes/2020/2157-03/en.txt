THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16 OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
the Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid,
Act, in respect of the Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist Studies (Pvt.) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri
Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number PV 94125, do by this Order, amend the
Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and published in  Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1944/2 of December 7,
2015, by the addition immediately after the entry “Master of Philosphy in Buddhist Studies” appearing in Column I and it’s
corresponding entry in Column II thereto, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II
hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
Conditioins
1. Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist Studies (Pvt.) Limited is recognized to offer the degree specified in
Schedule II hereto subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.
2. Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist Studies (Pvt.) Ltd shall, when admitting students for the degree programme
specified in Schedule II hereto, adhere to one of the following minimum entry qualifications as prescribed in the Sri Lanka
Qualification Framework (SLQF) : -
(a) the succesful completion of a Master’s Degree in Philosophy in any recongnized University or Higher
Education Institution in the relevant area of study ;
(b) the succesful completion of a Master’s Degree in any recongnized University or Higher Education Institution
in the relevant area of study ;
(c) the succesful completion of the SLQF Level 6 in the Bachelor’s Honours Degree in any recongnized University
or Higher Education Institution in the relevant area of study with a minimum GPA of 3.0 at a scale of 0-4 ;
(d) the succesful completion of the SLQF Level 6 in the Bachelor’s Honours Degree in any recongnized University
or Higher Education Institution in the relevant area of study with valid registration to follow a Master of
Philosophy (MPhil.) at Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist Studies (Pvt) Limited, which may be
upgraded to Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) level after a minimum period of one year, where the research
competencies of such person are of exceptional merit ; or
(e) the succesful completion of the SLQF Level 5 in the Bachelor’s Degree in any recongnized University or
Higher Education Institution in the relevant area of study with a minimum GPA of 3.0 at a scale of 0-4 and the
successful completion of a qualifyin examination conducted after completion of 30 credits equivalent to
SLQF Level 6, with valid registration to follow a Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) at Nagananda International
Institute for Buddhist Studies (Pvt) Limited, which may be upgraded to Doctor of Philosophy (Phd) level
after a minimum period of one year, where the research competencies of such person are of exceptional merit.
