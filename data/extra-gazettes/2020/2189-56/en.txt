The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   VACANCY   THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN OF THE  PUTHUKKUDIYIRUPPU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA UNDER SECTION 66B (2) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTION (AMENDMENT)
ACT, No. 16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of Chairman in Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Sellaiyah
Premakanth the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under section 66B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Arulanantham Thavakumaran to fill such Vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan Returning Officer of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha acting in
terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Arulanantham Thavakumaran elected as chairman of the
Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Mullaithivu,
21st August, 2020.
