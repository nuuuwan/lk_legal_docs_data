UNIVERSITY   OF   VOCATIONAL   TECHNOLOGY   ACT,   No.   31   OF   2008
Order under Section 8(1)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8(1)(b) of the University of V ocational Technology Act, No. 31 of 2008,
I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the
administration of the affairs of the University of V ocational Technology, Sri Lanka, have been seriously dislocated and
that the authorities of the said University have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Senior
Professor Wachcha Patabendege Ranjith Premalal de Silva to be the Competent Authority with effect from June 17, 2020
for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of the Vice-Chancellor of the University of V ocational
Technology, Sri Lanka, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on , or assigned to the
said Vice-Chancellor under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
D
R. BANDULA GUNA WARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
16th June, 2020.
