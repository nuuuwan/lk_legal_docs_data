SPORTS   LAW   No.   25   OF  1973
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sports under the Section 41 and read with the Section 31 of the Sports  Law No.
25 of 1973.
DULLAS A LAHAPPERUMA  (M.P.),
Minister of Education,  Sports  and Youth Affairs.
At Colombo,
On 29th day of April 2020.
Regulations
01. Regulations No. 01 of 2016 of the National Association of Sports published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 1990/23 dated 27 th October 2016  are  amended  only  in relevance to the year 2020.
- “Thirty first day of May” in the Section  (a)  of the  Schedule  III  under  the provisions related to the constitution
of  the  National  Associations / Federations of Sports  is repealed and the “Thirty first day of August” is inserted.
(The Section (a) of the Schedule III issued under the provisions related to the  constitution of the National
Associations /  Federations  of  Sports  published  in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27 th October
2016  come  into effect yet again with effect from the first day of January  2021)
02. Regulations of Selection Committees related to the Section No. 01 (b) of the amended Regulations of the
National Association of  Sports  published in the  Extraordinary  Gazette No. 2166/9 dated 10 th March 2020 will come
into effect from the 01st day of January 2021.
