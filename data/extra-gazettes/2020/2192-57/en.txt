BINARA  MAHA  PERAHERA  ORDERS  OF  GALVILA  PURANA  RAJAMAHA  VIHARAYA
I, Kaushalya Galappatty Divisional Secretary of Hambanthota hereby adjucate the time of regulation under the
Pilgrimages Ordinance as Binara Maha Perahera festival orders of  Galvila Purana Rajamaha Viharaya. Which belongs
to No. 121 Pahala Beragama Gramaniladhari Division at Divisional Secretary Division of Hambanthota Magampatthu of
Hambanthota District.
Binara Maha Perahera Festival 2020, will be held from 22.09.2020 to 01.10.2020.
K
AUSHALYA GALAPPATTY,
Divisional Secretary,
Hambanthota.
