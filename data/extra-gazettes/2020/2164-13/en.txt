20. Interim Provisions
As per the approval received from the Cabinet decision No. wum/19/0672/112/040-1 dated 03.07.2019 of the
Secretary to the Cabinet of Ministers, the interim period is extended from 31.12.2015 to 31.12.2020 provisions of the
new service minute are intergratly effective from 01.01.2021
20.2.2. Promotion to Grade II from Grade III
Nursing Officers who were appointed to the post of Nursing officer in grade III on or before 04.09.2014, will be
promoted to grade II with effect from the date the officer complete an active service period of 05 years in grade III during
the interim period or the date the officer completed all other qualifications required in terms of the old Nursing Service
Minutes, whichever occurs later. Officers who completed qualifications after the expiry of the interim period will be
given promotions in terms of the provisions of the new Service Minute. Accordingly, service period in segment II A under
the old Service Minutes will be reckoned as the service period in grade III of the New Service Minute.
