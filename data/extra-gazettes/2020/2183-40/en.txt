The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of the Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kanakarethinam Perinparasa of the
Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office  of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he has  ceased to be a member of the
Tamil United Liberation Front.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Alayadivembu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
