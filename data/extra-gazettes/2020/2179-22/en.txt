FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   PANWILA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be member of the
party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front present), the
recognized political party to which Sundaram Wijendran the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that 01 - Kelebokka if Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muththusamy Sasikumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of the Panwila
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Muththusamy Sasikumar was selected as the member of the 01 - Kelebokka of  Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
11th June, 2020.
