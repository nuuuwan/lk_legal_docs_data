PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1   OF  1981
Notice under Section 25(3)
NOTICE is hereby given that
(a) the death occured on May 26, 2020 of Mr. Arumugan Thondaman, who was a candidate nominated by the
Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna Party for the Electoral District No. 6 - Nuwara-Eliya at the General Election to be
held on August 5, 2020 ;
(b) notwithstanding such death, the nomination paper submitted by Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna Party for that
Electoral District with the omission of the name of the said Mr. Arumugan Thondaman and the inclusion of the
name of Mr. Jeevan Thondaman who has been nominated by the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna Party under
Sub-section (2) of Section 25 of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 01 of 1981 is valid, and that the poll in such
Electoral District shall be taken on August 5, 2020 as specified in the notice published under Section 24 of the
aforesaid Act.
