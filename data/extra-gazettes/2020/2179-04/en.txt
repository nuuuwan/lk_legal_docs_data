PUBLIC   SERVICE   COMMISSION
Appointment of Committees in terms of Articles 56(1) and 56(2) of the Constitution
ON the direction of the Public Service Commission, following members are hereby appointed to the Committee for the
Education Service Sector constituting three members for a period of one year in terms of Article 56(1) of the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka with effect from 1st of March Two Thousand Twenty (2020).
I. Mr. M. M. N. D. Bandara (Chairman)
II. Mr. E. H. M. P. Elkaduwa (Member)
III. Mr. K. L. Mohomed Thamby (Member)
