FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NUWARAGAM   PALATHA  EAST
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return  member of  Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party, to which Bogolle
Kadawathale Ashoka Wimalasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon  returned member of People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kiribandage Harisens to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kiribandage Harisens was selected as the member of  the   Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
10th  November, 2020.

 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   the return member of  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party, to which Kande
Kankanamge Munasingha, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon  returned member of the   People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under

Konara Mudiyanselage Ariyarathna to fill such vacancy ;

terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Konara
Mudiyanselage Ariyarathna was selected as the member of  the   Damana Pradeshiya Sabha.
