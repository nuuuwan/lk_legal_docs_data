FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    WARAKAPOLA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the  member of  Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party, to which Samarasinghage
Sena Samarasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  member of that Ward No. 23 - Pahala Lenagala of Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Agampodige Ruwani Tharanga Wimarshani to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Agampodige Ruwani
Tharanga Wimarshani was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 23 -  Pahala Lenagala of Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
06th  November, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    PORATHIVU    PATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   the member of  Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the reccognized political party, to which
Thamotharam Manoharan, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon  member of that  Ward No. 09 Sinnawatta of Porativupattu Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforsaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kopalasingam Vavikaran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I,  Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kopalasingam Vavikaran
was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 09  Sinnawatta of Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
06th  November, 2020.
