THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.   16   OF   1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities  Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa, have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Dr. Nawaratnage Sunil Jayantha Nawaratne to be the
Competent Authority with effect from April 01, 2020 for the purposes of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of the
Director of the Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa, any power, duty or function  respectively which has been
conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director under the said Act, or  any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law,
Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
D
R. BANDULA GUNA WARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
25th June, 2020.
