PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1   OF  1981
Notice under Section 25 (3)
NOTICE is hereby given that :
(a) The death occurred on July 05, 2020 of Mr. Ashoka Wadigamangawa, who was a candidate nominated by the
Samagi Jana Balawegaya party for the Electoral District No. 16 - Puttalam at the General Election to be held
on August 5, 2020 ;
(b) Notwithstanding such death, the nomination paper submitted by Samagi Jana Balawegaya party for that electoral
district with the omission of the name of the said Mr. Ashoka Wadigamangawa and the inclusion of the name
of Mr. Abeysinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Gamini Ashoka Wadigamangawa who has been nominated by the
Samagi Jana Balawegaya party under Sub-section (2) of Section 25 of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of
1981 is valid, and that the poll in such Electoral District shall be taken on August 5, 2020 as specified in the
notice published under Section 24 of the aforesaid Act.
