The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   NAVITHANVELI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Thavaraja
Kaleiarasan, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of that No. 03 - Navithanveli of Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muththukkumar Vickneswaran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Muththukkumar Vickneswaran was elected as the member of  the No. 03 - Navithanveli of  Navithanveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
28th August, 2020.
