FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   UDAPALATHA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wijesinghe
Arachchilage Priyangika Kumari Senewirathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Gayani Sriyamala Athugalpe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of the Udapalatha
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Gayani Sriyamala Athugalpe was selected as the member of the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
15th June, 2020.
