Article 154P of the Constitution) (Temporary Provisions) Rules, 2020.

Appeal (Procedure for appeals form High Courts established by Article 154P of the Constitution) Rules, 1988 published
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 549/6 of March 13, 1989-
