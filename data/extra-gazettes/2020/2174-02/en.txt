FIXING   A   CERTIFIED   PRICE   TO   PURCHASE   16   CROPS   AS   PER   THE   CABINET   DECISION
No. 20/0737/217/036-I DATED 23.04.2020
I, Neel Bandara Hapuhinna , Secretary to the Ministry of Mahaweli, Agriculture, Irrigation and Rural Development of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby impose farmgate prices for crops specified in column I of the
appendix based on proposed proportions specified in column II of the appendix in terms of the  Cabinet Decision
No. 20/0737/217/036-I dated 22.04.2020 by this order.
This Order shall be effective from 23 April 2020 and shall remain valid as long as the Cabinet of Ministers
amends it.
NEEL BANDARA HAPUHINNA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Mahaweli,  Agriculture, Irrigation  and
Rural Development.
04th May, 2020,
80/5, “Govijana Mandiraya”,

Battaramulla,
