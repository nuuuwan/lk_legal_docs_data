2020 - FIRST  SUPPLEMENTARY  FINANCIAL  STATEMENT 
 
Granting  Permission By Virtue Of Powers Vested In Me Under Section 27(A)  Of Provincial Council Act No. 42 
Of 1987 as the term of the  Sixth Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  
 Sri Lanka ended on  26th September, 2017 - 2020 
 
 1. This permission may be cited as “Permission to disburse under first financial statement -2020 of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Counicl”  and  come in to operation from 19.08.2020. 
 
 2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Twenty One Billion  Three 
Hundred and Forty Eight Million  Six Hundred and Twenty Nine Thousand Eight Hundred Only  
(Rs. 21,348,629,800.00)  for the service of the period beginning on 01 st of January, 2020 and ending 31 st August 
2020, which is specified as “Eight months duration” in the Budget Estimate prepared for the first ei ght months of 
the year 2020, that was approved on 28.05.2020,  is  hereby amended as, Rupees Twenty Three Billion  Five 
Hundred and Eighty Million  Three Hundred and Ninety Nine Thousand  Eight Hundred  Only  
(Rs. 23,580,399,800.00 ) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Acco unting 
Officers  to  disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.   
 
 3. Accordingly the first schedule of the said gazette notification is amended as the first schedule hereof and grant 
permission to disburse  the same. 
 
 4. This permission will be valid  till  31 st  of August, 2020  or  upto the date that expires  three months (03) from the 
date of summoning  the Seventh (07th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  or  from the date  re-start the functions of 
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  which occurs first. 
 
(Published by Authority)
