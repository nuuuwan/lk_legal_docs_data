ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  10 (1) (a)
of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10 (1) (a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon,
Minister of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government, do by this order declare 17th,
18th and 19th, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday of March, 2020 as special Public Holidays to support quarantine process on
Corona virus. This Holiday will be extending if necessary.
J
ANAKA BANDARA TENNAKOON,
  Minister of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and
Provincial  Councils & Local Government.
Ministry of  Public Administration, Home Affairs and
Provincial  Councils & Local Government ,
