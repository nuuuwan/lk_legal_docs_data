L.D.B.21/78(XV)(A).
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do
by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order pubished in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2164/53 of February 27, 2020), which specifies the Faculties and
the Departments of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section
139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, as follows:
(1) by the addition immediately after the entry “16. Department of Medical Education and Health Sciences” appearing in
Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Medical Sciences” appearing in Column 1 of
that Schedule of the following new entry:
“17. Department of Immunology and Molecular Medicine”; and
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
