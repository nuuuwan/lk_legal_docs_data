PARLIAMENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  1   OF  1981
Notice under Section 24(1)(d) and 127 B(1)
GENERAL   ELECTION   OF   MEMBERS   OF   THE   PARLIAMENT
THIS refers to the Gazettes  Extraordinary No. 2179/7 dated 09.06.2020 and 2179/17  dated 10.06.2020 issued by the Election
Commission.
Whereas the Election Commission has accepted the application made by the registered electors of the Vanni
Electoral District No. 11, who reasonably fear due to conditions prevailing in the area within which their polling station is
situated, that they are unable to cast their vote at such polling station, in terms of Section 24(1)(d) and 127 B(1) of the
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 ; and
Whereas the Election Commission is empowered to allow such electors to cast their vote at another polling station
determined by the Commission, in its absolute discretion ;
It is hereby notified that the situation of the polling stations in the electoral district of Puttalam No. 16, reserved for
the electors registered in the electoral list of the Vanni District who reasonably fear that they are unable to cast their votes at
the polling station reserved for them, to cast their votes shall be as follows :
