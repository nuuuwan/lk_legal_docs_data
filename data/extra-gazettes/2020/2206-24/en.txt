IT is hereby announced that the Annual Feast of St. Sebastian’s National Shrine, Kandana will be held on 20th January, 2021 and the duration
of the Feast will be from 02.01.2021 to 25.01.2021 for the public to know.
1. The attention of the general public is drawn to the Regulations published in Gazette Notification No. 2135/34 dated 06th August, 2019
under the Pilgrimage Ordinance 175 in this regard.
2. Arrangements have been made to hold this year’s festival with the participation of a limited number of people in accordance with the
quarantine laws on the instructions of the health sector under the program to control the spread of Covid 19 epidemic disease in the
country.
3. These publicity-issued laws will be valid during the period of this ceremony.
SUNIL JAYALATH,
District Secretary/ Government Agent,
Gampaha.
District Secretariat,
Gampaha,
18th December, 2020.
