SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do by this order impose in respect of the commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 31 days  commencing from February 25, 2020 subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and
Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
February 24, 2020.
