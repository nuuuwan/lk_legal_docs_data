Authorization
I do hereby grant Permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
spend a sum of Rupees Thirteen  Billion Two Hundred and Eight Million Two Hundred and Ninety Six Thousand only
(Rs. 13,208,296,000/-) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  fund, which is estimated for the expenses of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council for five months period that  start on 01st of January, 2020 and end  on 31st  of  May 2020, by virtue of powers
vested in me under Section  27 (a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th  Sabaragamuwa
Provincial  Council  of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended  on 26th September, 2017.
This permission will be valid till 31st of May, 2020 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of summoning  the
07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-starts  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which
occurs first.
This estimate has been prepared by way of a vote on account based on the amount of received imprests upto  05th  May
2020, out of the allocation made by the vote on account passed by the Parliament for the first four months of the year 2020, the
allocation for the next three months made by the His excellency the president by  virtue of powers vested in  him under  the sub
article (3) of Article 150 of the Constitution  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri   Lanka and the expected revenue for the
first five months of the year 2020.
I also emphasize that the expenditure incurred in the revenue expenditure estimates authorized by me on November 29, 2019,
to cover the expenses for the first four months of the year 2020, should be covered by the provisions set forth in this
Permission.
T
IKIRI KOBBEKADUWA,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
22nd April, 2020.
