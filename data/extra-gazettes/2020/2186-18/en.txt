PILGRIMAGE   ORDINANCE
Annual Festival of Mother’s Church, Matara
IT is hereby noticed under Section 02 of the regulations framed under Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) and published in
the Gazette No. 687 of 01.11.1991 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that the Annual Festival of Mother’s Church,
Matara in the Division of Matara in Matara District will be conducted from Sunday, 30th of August to Sunday, 6th of
September 2020.
D
EEPIKA K. GUNARATHNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara Four Gravets.
At Divisional Secretariat,
on 24th July, 2020.
