SRI    LANKA    EXPORT    DEVELOPMENT  ACT,    No.   40   OF   1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14 of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Bandula
Gunawardana, Minister of Trade, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the rate of CESS
specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2047/2 dated
27.11.2017, in respect of the articles described under the H. S. Codes as specified in the Schedule hereto, with effect
from October 14, 2020.
B
ANDULA GUNAWARDANA,
Minister of Trade.
Ministry of Trade,
Colombo 02,
13th  October, 2020.
