SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do by this order impose in respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity
Levy at the rates specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of six month (06 months) commencing from March 04, 2020 subject to
provsions of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2143/3 of 30.09.2019 and the order make under Section 5 of the Special Commedity Levy Act,
No. 48 of 2007 and  published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No.  2161/34 of 06.02.2020 in respect of  Fish
(HS Heading of  03.02, 03.03 and other than the Mackerel fish coming under HS Code 0303.54.00), are hereby rescinded
with effect from March 04, 2020.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic
and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
03rd  March, 2020.
