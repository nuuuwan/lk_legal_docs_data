The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE    UNDER    SECTION  10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Padaviya  Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Mohideen Bawa Mohomed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Election Ordinance that Mr. Sesath Gedara Rohana Dharmasena of the
Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he  has ceased to be a member of
the People’s  Liberation Front.
M. B. M. Sufyan,
Returning Officer,
Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
05th  October, 2020.
