CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the subjects, functions, Departments, Public Corporations and Statutory Institutions assigned to
the State Ministers of the Ministry of Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils and Local Government
in terms of the Article 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka will be as mentioned
in the Schedule given below from the date of this Notification.
J
ANAKA B ANDARA TENNAKOON (M.P.),
 Minister of Public Administration, Home Affairs,
Provincial Councils and Local Government.
