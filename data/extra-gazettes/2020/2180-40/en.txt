FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   AKKARAIPATTU   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the Office of  a  member of Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Congress, the recognized political party to which Muhammathu
Aabdeen Siththi Noorul Iyen the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of that Number 11 Grand Mosque of Akkaraipattu Municipal Council who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Fahizer Fathima Sameeha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Fahizar
Fathima Sameeha was selected as the member of  the Number 11 Grand Mosque of Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
19th June, 2020.
