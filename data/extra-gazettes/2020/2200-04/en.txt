PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - PG  5209— 30 (11/2020)
DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  -  EASTERN   PROVINCE
Notice on Summon Meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment)
Act, No. 22 of 2012
BY virtue of the power vested on me under Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections as amended by Act, No. 22 of 2012,
I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to fill
the vacancies arose as a result of resigning the post of Chairman of the Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha, Trincomalee District,
Eastern Province at 10.00 a. m. on 17.11.2020  at Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall for the purpose of elect the new
Chairman.
N.  M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
05th November, 2020.
