Land Acquisition Notices 
G 31841 - 32  (2020/01) 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
My Reference No.: Ana/E2/LA 4609. 
 
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 03J/85/1R/43. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition act, that the Government intends to acquire 
the following lands which are required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Eight (8) allotments of land in total extent of about 3.24 
Hectares, depicted in Surveyor General's plan No. 3230 
situated in the village of Mawathagama in Minor Division 
Karamba Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary's Division 
of Anamaduwa in Puttalam District. 
 
 01. Lot No. 21 - Name of Land: Halabagahayaya; 
Extent: 0.240 Hectare.

25 2/2; East: Lot Nos. 17, 25 2/2 and 25 1/2; South: Lot
