L.D.B 24/1951(XV)
THE  MOTOR   TRAFFIC   ACT,  (CHAPTER   203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport Services Management under Section 237, read with Section 143 of
the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203).
MAHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Transport Services Management.
Colombo,
24th  January 2020.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as Motor Traffic (Restriction of Motor Vehicles) Regulations, No. 01 of 2020.
2. A person shall be restricted from using certian vehicles along a section of the road which extends from Thotalanga
Junction to Kohilawatta Junction, the Colombo - Hanwella (AB 010) road.
3. The vehicles restricted by regulation 2 shall be as follows :-
(a) all vehicles transporting containers ;
(b) other vehicles with a height of over 3.2 meters from the ground level ; and
(c) vehicles with a load height of over 3.2 meters from the ground level.
