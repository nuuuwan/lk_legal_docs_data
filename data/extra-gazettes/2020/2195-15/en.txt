PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
1A -  G 33015— 30 (09/2020)
Kolonnawa Urban Council
I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Kolonnawa Urban Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kalandar Mohideen Rawuththar
Nijamdeen of the Kolonnawa Urban Council has vacated his office of the member of that Urban Council as he has been
ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kolonnawa  Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
23rd  September, 2020.
