The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NAGODA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Nagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which  Bodara
Gamage Aruna Shanthi, the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of United National Freedom Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kariyawasam Hewamanage Nadeeka Darshanie to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Nagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kariyawasam Hewamanage
Nadeeka Darshanie was selected as the member of  the Nagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Nagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
05th  October, 2020.
