 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: MN/DS/L/DI/AQ/WPP/44.
 Land Ministry No.: 4-3/5/2019/CEB/41.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired :
The lands allotment called Thoddavelikani, in total extent
of about 2.1310 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan
No. MN 836 and prepared by Surveyor General situated in the
village called Thoddaveli in the Grama Niladari Division
No. 62 of Thoddaveli, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Mannar Town of Mannar District in Northern Province.
Plan No.: MN 836
Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Thoddavelikani; Present use of the land:
Garden contain 16 Palmyra trees; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: CEYLEX Engineering (Pvt) Ltd. Parkland 17th Floor,
33 Park Street, Colombo 02; Extent: 2.1310 Hectares.
The dates and the time of the inquiries :
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands, on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.00 a.m. on the 21st day of September 2020 at Mannar
Town Divisional Secretariat.
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of September
2020 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of
their claims for the compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
M. PIRATHEEP,
Divisional Secretary,
Mannar Town Division,
Mannar.
Divisional Secretariat of Mannar Town,
11th August, 2020.
