The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   NUWARAELIYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the Party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ceylon Worker ’s Congress (P. Wing), the recognized political party to
which Mrs. Amuldas Jesintha the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of  that  15 - Kirimetiya if Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya  Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Subramaniyam Gowry to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the  Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that
Subramaniyam Gowry was selected as the member of the 15 - Kirimetiya of Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P ATHIRANA ,
Returning officer,
Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
11th March, 2020.
