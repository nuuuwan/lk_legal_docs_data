THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: BD/WM/LND/2/5/AQUIS/29.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/8/2017/mSiS/518.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, The Government intends to
acquire the following land for a public purpose:
Description of Land to be acquired:
In the Province of Uva, District of Badulla, Divisional
Secretariat of Welimada, Minor Division of Udapalatha Korala,
Grama Niladhari Divisional of 50A Ambewela, situated in the
Village of Hewanakumbura, Prepared by Badulla Survey
General P .P .N. B 736 and dated on 23.12.2019 base plot lot
number 621, the total extent of 0.9284 Hectares, the details
follow:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Ambewelawatha (Tawana Division); Land
Details: Home Garden mixed cultivation about 15-20 years and
two permanent buildings; Claimant: Land Reform Commission;
Extent: 0.9284 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Office, Welimada on the date and
time relevant to each land lot as writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 02nd day of March 2020 the nature of their interests
in the land the particulars of their claims for compensation for
the acquisition of the said land, and the amount of computation
and the details of the compensation of such amount.
