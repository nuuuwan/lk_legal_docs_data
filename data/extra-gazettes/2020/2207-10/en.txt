2A
Naming  a  Tourist  Zone  in Ella  Divisional  Secretary’s  Division  in  Uva  Province
By virtue of powers vested in the Section 223 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read along with the Section 02 of
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, A. J. M. Musammil, the Governor of Uva Province hereby
inform that the below mentioned areas are named as the Ella Tourism Zone.
I, name the Grama Niladari Divisions of—
1. 68 B Ella;
2. 69 G Hettipola;
3. 68 F Idamegama;
4. 69 E Kithal Ella;
5. 68 C Maduragama;
6. 68 J Newburg;
7. 69 F Rawana Ella;
8. 68 E Yahalewela,
which belong to Ella Divisional Secretary’s Division as the Tourist Zone in order to achieve the following objectives:—
OBJECTIVES
1. The sustainable development of tourism industry in Ella Tourist Zone.
2. Making Ella Tourist Zone an internationally standardized tourist destination by developing infrastructure facilities.
3. Regulation and close supervision of tourist service providers in the objective of improving the quality of tourism
industry.
4. Ensuring the security and improving tourist satisfaction of local and international tourists visiting Ella Tourist Zone.
5. Involving to provide support for tourism industry service providers during emergencies/pandemics.
6. Providing solutions for environmental and social issues which have occurred with the development of tourism
industry.
