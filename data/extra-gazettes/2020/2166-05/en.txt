  The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Provincial Councils Notifications
CENTRAL PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
I, Lalith U. Gamage, Governor of the Central Province, hereby make following regulations by virtue of the powers vested in
me by Section 188(i) m.m. and Section 289 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) which should be read with
Section 2(1)(a) of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989. This Order shall be effective from
19th of October, 2019.
REGULATIONS
Payment of an allowance to the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and a Member of the Kandy Municipal Council of th e Central
Province for participating in monthly Standing Committees and Sub-Committees from the Kandy Municipal Council fund.
These allowances shall be in addition to the regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1391/25 of 06.05.2005
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Sub-Committee Allowance - Rs. 1,000.00
Standing Committee Allowance - Increased up to Rs. 1,500.00
LALITH U. GAMAGE (Attorney-at-law),
Hon. Governor,
Central Province.
At the Governor's Office,
18th December, 2019.
