PART IV (A) - PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  
 
 
Provincial Councils Notifications 
 
WESTERN PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL  
 
WESTERN PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC MANAGEMENT ASSISTANT SERVICE MINUTE-AMENDMENT No. 4 
 
I, hereby, amend Western Provincial Public Management Service Minute published in Special Gazette 1898/5 and dated 
19.01.2015 which is amended by Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic special Gazette No. 2050/41 and dated 
22.12.2017 and Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic special Gazette No. 2114/61 and dated 15.03.2019 and            
Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic special Gazette No. 2151/8 and dated 25.11.2019 as follows. 
 
1. Instead of the service Minute Name which is “Western Provincial Public Management Assistant Service               
Minute” is replaced as follows. 
 
 “Western Provincial  Public Management Service Officer Service Minute” 
 
 
2. Afterwards every applications mentioned in the relevant service minute as “Western Provincial Public             
Management Assistant Service” to be amended as  “Western Provincial Public Management Service              
Officer Service  and every applications of “Western Provincial Public Management Assistant” to be 
amended as “Western Provincial Public Management Service Officer” as well as it should be considered 
where  mentioned as “Officer” as Managment Service Officer.
