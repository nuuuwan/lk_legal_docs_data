FILLING  OF  A   V ACANCY  IN THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  BATTICALOA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS  the office of member in Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death :
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Santhiyappillai Elatin Chandrakumar, the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the  member of that ward No. 11 Periyauppodai of Batticaloa Municipal Council   who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sachchithanandam Kamalaruban to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sachchithanandam
Kamalaruban was elected as the member of  the ward No. 11 Periyauppodai of Batticaloa Municipal Council.
R. S
ASEELAN
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
08th  September, 2020.
