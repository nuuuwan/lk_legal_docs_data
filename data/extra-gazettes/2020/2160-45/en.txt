THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, establishing the Uva Wellassa
University of Sri Lanka and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1395/15 of June 01, 2005 (as last amended by Order
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2116/44 of March 27, 2019) by the addition immediately after the entry “2.
Department of Computer Science and Informatics” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item
“1. Faculty of Applied Sciences” in Column I thereof, of the following new entry :-
“3. Department of Applied Earth Sciences ”.
Dr. B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
29th January, 2020.
