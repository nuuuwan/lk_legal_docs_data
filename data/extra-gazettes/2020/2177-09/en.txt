Order No. 66
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS    AUTHORITY  ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Order  under Section 20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no importer, producer, distributor, or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display for sale the
varieties of rice listed below, above the Maximum Retail Prices given here under.
V ariety Maximum
Retail Price
Keeri Samba Rs. 120/- per kg
White/Red Samba - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Suduru Samba) Rs. 98/- per kg
White/Red Raw Samba Rs. 98/- per kg
White/Red Nadu - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari) Rs. 96/- per kg
White/Red Raw Rice Rs. 93/- per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 28th May, 2020.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 62 published in Gazette  Extraordinary
No. 2170/7 on 10.04.2020.
                                                                    By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
28th May, 2020.
