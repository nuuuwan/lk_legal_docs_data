THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in statute 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and
52(1) of the Constitution, the Secretary to the Ministry of Information and Communication Technology is acting as the
Chief Accounting Officer and do hereby notify that the activities and functions of the following Schedule pertaining to the
Departments, Statutory bodies and State Corporations vested in me as the Minister of Information and Communication
Technology, in terms of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2153/12 of 10th December 2019, are herewith entrusted to the
State Minister of Information and Communication Technology, Mr. Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena with effect from the date
mentioned herein this notification.
D
R. B ANDULA G UNAW ARDANA,
Minister of Information and Communication Technology.
