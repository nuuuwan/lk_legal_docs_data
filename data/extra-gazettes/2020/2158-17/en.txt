THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
Delegation of Functions of the Ministry of Mahaweli, Agriculture, Irrigation and Rural Development  to
Hon.  Minister of Agriculture, to Hon.  State Minister of Irrigation and Rural  Development and to
Hon. State Minister of Mahaweli
IT is hereby declare that out of powers and functions vested in me in my capacity as the  Minister of Mahaweli, Agriculture,
Irrigation and Rural Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, power and functions specified in
the following Schedule are delegated to Hon. State Minister of Agriculture - Vidura Wickramanayaka, to Hon. State
Minister of Irrigation and Rural Development - Mahinda Yapa Abeywardana, to Hon. State Minister of Mahaweli - Roshan
Ranasinghe in terms on provisions of the Article 44(5) of the Constitution and these State Ministers have the opportunity
of inquiring, mediating and co-ordinating all functions coming within the scope of work of this Ministry.
