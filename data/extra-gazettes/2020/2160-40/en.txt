THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978 
 
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula  
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the U niversity 
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1482/26 
of February 02, 2007 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2015/46 of April 19, 2017), 
which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of 
the provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said Act, by the  
addition immediately after the entry "16. Department of Radiology" appearing in Column II  of the Schedule which  
corresponds to the item ''Faculty of Medicine'' appearing in Column I thereof, of the following new entry :- 
 
 ''17. Department of Medical Education". 
 
 Dr. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
 Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation. 
 
Colombo, 
29th January, 2020.
