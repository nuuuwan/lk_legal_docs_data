The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council
I, Pulasthi Serasingha Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ahangama Ramanayakalage Ajith Kumara
of the Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council has vacated his office of the member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased
to be a member of the United National Party.
PULASTHI SERASINGHA PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
17th September, 2020.
