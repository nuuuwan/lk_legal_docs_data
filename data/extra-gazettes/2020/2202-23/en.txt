No. 2202/23 –  FRIDAY, NOVEMBER  20,  2020 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
        My  Reference  No.: LLD/DO(J)/LA/CH/16. 
             Min. Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2017/LO/573. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose:  
 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired : 
 
Preliminary Plan  : PPYA 2977 
Situation Village : Kodikamam 
GN Division  : Kodikamam Centre (J/327) 
District  : Jaffna 
Province  : Northern 
 
 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Kaddaipariththan; Description: 
Garden contains four Permanent Buildings; Name of the 
Claimant: Sivasubramaniyam Pavalarani; Extent: 0.2410 
Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Thenmaradchchi on 06.01.2021 at 10.00 a. m. and 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 23.12.2020 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
 
   
  
 S. USHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretariat. 
 Thenmaradchchi, 
 Chavakachcheri.
