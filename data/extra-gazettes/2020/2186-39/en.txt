THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCATION UNDER SECTION 39(1)
BY the Order under Section 38(a) of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter
460) and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary No. 1539/7 dated
03.03.2008, the land specified more fully in the Order No. 52 of
2008 dated 27.02.2008 was vested in the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka.
But the possession of the said land has not yet been taken for and
on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore,
I Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands and
Land Development, by virtue of powers vested in me under 39(1) of
the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke aforesaid Order No. 52
of 2008 dated 27.02.2008 published in the Gazette Extra-Ordinary
No. 1539/7 dated 03.03.2008.
S.M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
