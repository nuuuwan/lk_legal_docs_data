FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KADUWELA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kaduwela Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Y . P. I.
N. G . Senarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  returned member of  People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate D. G. S. Karunarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the Kaduwela Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby
declare that D. G. S. Karunarathna was selected as the member of the Kaduwela Municipal Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning officer,
Kaduwela Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
11th February, 2020.
