FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER  OF   THE   YATAWATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group  to which Asarabdin Mohamad Nawas the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member if  Independent
Group  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Yatawara Gedara Wijitha Bandara Dorakumbura  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Prathapasinghe  Arachchige Kumarinee Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the  Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Yatawara Gedara Wijitha Bandara Dorakumbura  was selected as the member of the Yatawatta Pradeshiya
Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
24th February, 2020.
