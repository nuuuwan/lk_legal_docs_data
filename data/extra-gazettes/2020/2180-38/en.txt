FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   ALAYADIVEMBU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council meetings period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Seenithamby
Wevi the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned
member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thavaraja Sharanya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramaratne, Returning Officer  of  Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thavaraja
Sharanya was selected as the member of   Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
08th June, 2020.
