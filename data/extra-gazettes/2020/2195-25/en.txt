The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KUNDASALE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front at Present), the
recognized political party to which  Sellambaram Withneswaran, the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s
Front at Present) who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance  and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Savundara Raja Wijekumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the Kundasale
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Savundara Raja Wijekumar was selected as the member of  the  Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
28th  September, 2020.
