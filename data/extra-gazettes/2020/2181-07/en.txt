FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KARAITIVU  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Muthulingam
Kandeepan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kandasami Jeyathasan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kandasami
Jeyathasan was selected as the member of   the Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA  WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
22nd June, 2029.
