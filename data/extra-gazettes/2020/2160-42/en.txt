THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978 
 
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of  1978, I, Dr. Bandula  
Gunawardana, Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the U niversity 
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 
of June 09, 2006 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2139/11 of September 02, 2019), 
which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deem ed, by virtue of 
the provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said Act, by the  
addition immediately after the entry ''7. Department of International Business "appearing in Column II of the Schedule 
which corresponds to the items ''Faculty of Management and Finance'' in Column I of that Schedule, of the following 
new entry :- 
 
 ''8. Department of Hospitality and Leisure Management". 
 
 Dr. BANDULA GUNAWARDANA, 
 Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation. 
 
Colombo. 
29th January, 2020.
