FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   TANGALLE PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resign ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Yakdehige
Ajith Nishantha the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  returned member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Fransisku Baduge Sanath Sandaruwan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I, Ravindra Subhashana  Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that
Fransisku Baduge Sanath Sandaruwan was selected as the member of the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
A VINDRA SUBHASHANA  W EDAGE,
Returning officer,
Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambantota,
24th February, 2020.
