NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Welivitya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare  under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Dammullage Rohana Wijayalal of
the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to
be a member of the Janasetha Peramuna.
K. U. C

Returning Officer,
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
