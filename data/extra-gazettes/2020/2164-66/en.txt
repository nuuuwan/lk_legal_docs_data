THE   URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY
Law No. 41 of 1978 order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of powers vested with me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978,
I, Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Housing Facilities do by  this Order declared that the land fully
described in the Schedule I below which as owned by the Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council and vested with the Urban
Development Authority under the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 602/15 dated 21st March, 1990 and part of
the said land fully described in Schedule II below is released to the Land Commissioner General’s Department enabling
vest the same to the Ministry of Defence and retain the balance land described in the Schedule II hereto with the Urban
Development Authority for its development purposes.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA (M.P.),
 Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply
& Housing Facilities.
Ministry of Urban Development, Water Supply & Housing Facilities,
17th & 18th Floors,
Suhurupaya,
Battaramulla,
24th February, 2020.

SCHEDULE  I
The allotment of land called Upper lake Road depicted as Lot 01 in Preliminary Plan No. P. P. Nu. 1378 prepared by the
Survey General situated in Moon Plains, Ward No. 07, Park Road within the Municipal Council Limits of Nuwara Eliya in
Nuwara Eliya Assistant Government Agent’s Division in Nuwara Eliya District, Central Province containing in extent of
2.192 Hectare bounded by :
North by : Lands  belonging to Huejay International (Pvt.) Ltd, and the Nuwara Eliya Municipal
Council ;
East by : Upper Lake Road and land belonging to the Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council ;
South by : Land belonging to Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council & Lot 2 in Preliminary Plan
No. P. P. Nu. 1378 ;
West by : Lot 02 in Preliminary Plan No. P. P. Nu. 1378 and land belonging to Huejay International
(Pvt.) Ltd.
SCHEDULE  II
The allotment of land in Northern Boundary of Lot 1 in Preliminary Plan No. Nu. 1378 prepared by the Survey General
situated within the Municipal Council Limits of Nuwara Eliya in Nuwara Eliya Assistant Government Agent’s Division in
the Nuwara Eliya District, Central Province containing in extent of 0.0572 Hectare bounded by :
North by : Land belonging to Huejay International (Pvt.) Ltd, and  Nuwara Eliya Municipal
Council ;
East by : Upper Lake Road and land belonging to  Nuwara Eliya Municipal Council ;

West by : Land belonging to Huejay International (Pvt.) Ltd.
SCHEDULE  III
