Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 12 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
President, do by this Order call out with effect from December 22, 2020 all the members of the Armed Forces specified in the
First Schedule hereto, for the maintenance of public order in the areas specified in the Second Schedule hereto.
Colombo, GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
December  17, 2020. President.
FIRST SCHEDULE
The Sri Lanka Army
The Sri Lanka Navy
The Sri Lanka Air Force
