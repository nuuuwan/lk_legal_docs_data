CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification of Hon. Governor of the Central  Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 277(1A) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252) to be
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage, Attorney-
at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby appoint Mrs. Sarojini Kusala Weerawardena to inquire and report
within 3 months time from the dated, whether the Mayor Municipal Council Matale has committed any misdeeds described
in Section 277(1) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252).
This office shall have the powers of a Commission appointed under the Inquiry of Commission Act, pertaining to
this inquiry.
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE,
Attorney-at-Law,
Governor, Central Province.
Kandy,
24th  August, 2020.

2A
Notification of Hon Governor of the Central Province
THE Order made under Section 277(1B)(a)(i) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252), to be read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989.
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 277(1B)(a)(i) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter
252), to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage,
Attorney-at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby temporarily suspend Mr. Daljith Nandalal Aluvihare, Mayor
of the Matale Municipal Council from holding his post with immediate effect.
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 277(1B)(a)(i) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter
252), to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith U. Gamage,
Attorney-at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby direct Mr. Sandanam Prakash presently the Deputy Mayor
of the Matale Municipal Council, Matale District, to perform the duties and use of powers of the Mayor of the Matale
Municipal Council, Matale District of the Central Province with immediate effect.
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE,
Attorney-at-Law,
Governor, Central Province.
Kandy,
24th  August, 2020.
