SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do  hereby make an order to waive off the balance amount after collecting Rs. 195 per kg from the applied Special
Commodity Levy of Rs. 295 per kg on importation of Coconut oil classified under the HS Codes of 1513.11.11, 1513.11.19,
1513.11.21, 1513.11.29, 1513.19.10, 1513.19.90 listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2170/5 of 09th April, 2020.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 03 months commencing from April 17, 2020, subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of 2007.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
