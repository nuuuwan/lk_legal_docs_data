THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT, No. 16  OF  1978
Order under section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009
(as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2160/44 of January 30, 2020) which specifies the
Faculties and Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions
of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for
all the entries appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Humanities and Social
Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the following:-
D
R. B ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
25th February, 2020.
