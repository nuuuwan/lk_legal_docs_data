AUTHORIZATION 
 
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council to disburse as per the fifth Supplementary Financial statement for the year 2019,  by virtue  of powers vested in 
me  under Section  27 (a)  of the Provincial Council Act. No. 42 of  1987,  as the term of the 06 thSabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka ended on  26th September, 2017. 
 
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Four Billion   Three 
Hundred and Ninety Seven  Million, One Hundred and Seventy Three Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Five  and Cents 
Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,397,173,645.24)for the service of the period beginning on 01 st of January, 2019 and ending 
31st December, 2019, which is specified in annual financial statement -2019 and amended by fourth supplementary 
estimate-2019  is  hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Four Billion  Four Hundred and Eighty Eight Million, Four  
Hundred and Forty Nine Thousand Five Hundred and Sixty Five and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,488,4 49,565.24)
and grant permission to  disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.   
 
This permission will be expired on 31st  of December, 2019.
