Local Government Notifications
Pradeshiya  sabha - Panduwasnuwara
To be read with section 24 of the Pradeshiya sabha (local councils) Act, No.15 of 1987.In accordance with the work 
assigned to the Pradeshiya sabhas (local councils) of the North Western Province in the series of "orders on the preparation 
of plans and listing of roads and narrow paths maintained by the Pradeshiya sabhas (local councils) of the North Western 
Province" published by the Honorable Governor of the North Western Province in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 
and dated July 30, 2021 and the gazette  announcement dated 27.12.2022 No. 2312/10. In terms of section 9(3) of the said 
local council act,it is hereby announced for the public's knowledge that it has been resolved at the decision No. 177 on 
20.04.2023 that all the roads and narrow paths maintained by Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya sabha that are stated in the sub 
section given below should be declared as roads and narrow paths owned by Pradeshiya sabha (local council).
   If it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or narrow path included in the said schedule 
passes through any land or enters into any part of any land,a suit in a competent court be filed before the expiry of a period 
of three months from the date of this notification to prove his right to such land,it is hereby announced that the assignment 
shall be acted upon.
   It is further announced that every road and narrow path included in the said schedule for which no such case has 
been assigned before the expiry of the said three month period shall be deemed to be a road or narrow path owned by the 
Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya sabha(local council).
                    H. A. G. N
ishshaNka, 
 secretary,
 Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya sabha.
At Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya sabha,
Head Office,
