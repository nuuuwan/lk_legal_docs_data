      The Food (Control of Import, Labelling and Sale of Genetically Modified Foods) Regulations 2006, published in 
the 
gazette Extraordinary No. 1456/22 of August 03, 2006 is hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 13 thereof and

      “13. Food which contains or has genetically modified organisms or materials less than nought decimal nine per

  Provided  that  the  presence  of  such  genetically  modified  organisms or  materials  are  considered  technically
