of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2287/12 
dated 05.07.2022, the allotment of land specified 
morefully  in the Order No. 113 of 2022 dated 30.06.2022

Republic of Sri Lanka.
