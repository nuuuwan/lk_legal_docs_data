SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No. 48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2 and Section 5
 1. BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies of  the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose in respect of the commodity speciﬁed in Column I of the Schedule 
hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate speciﬁed in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
