NORTh  wESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notifications of Hon. Governor in the North Western Province
By virtue of powers vested under Section 326 (1) of Municipal Council Ordinance Chapter 252 to be read with                        
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Karannagoda, 
the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province do hereby issue the following order.
 Admiral of the Fleet 
 Wasantha Karannagoda,
 Governor,
 North Western Province.
At the Hon. Governor's Office, 
in the North Western Province,
21st january, 2023.
Order
I, hereby order that the powers, activities, execution of performance of the position of the Mayor shall be entrusted 
to the deputy Mayor since the post of Mayor of Kurunegala Municipal Council is vacant, in terms of Section 14 (6) of the 
Municipal Council Ordinance, Chapter 252 as per the Local Government election (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 with 
effect from 31.12.2022, until the vacancy is filled, as the budget document for the year 2023 has not been passed at the Council 
which was presented by the Mayor of the Municipal Council Kurunegala belonging to the North Western Province in terms 
of Section 215(a) of the Municipal Council Ordinance Act, 252, as amended by Section 7 of the Local Authorities (Special 
Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and/or since the budget had not been compiled or enforced in terms of Municipal Budget 
Compilation and enforcement Orders 2020 published by the Honourable Governor in the North Western Province by the 
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2199/15 of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka and dated 29.10.2020.
