THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below 
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.

the Advance Tracing No. KI/PNK/2023/201 dated 28.06.2023 
prepared by the Survey General situated in the village called 
Gowtharimunai in the Grama Niladhari Division No. KN/68 
of Gowtharimunai in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Punakari of Kilinochchi District in Northern Province and 
bounded as follows :
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Koiltivoe  – Extent:  1.0610 Hectares
 North: Koiltivoe and Path; East: Path; South: Path and 
Koiltivoe; West: Koiltivoe.
 02. Lot ‘B’ – Koiltivoe  – Extent:  1.1634 Hectares
 North: Path; East: Path and Koiltivoe; South: Koiltivoe; 
West: Koiltivoe and Path.
