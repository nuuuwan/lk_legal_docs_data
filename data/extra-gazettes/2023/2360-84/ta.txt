Local Government Notifications
  WennappuW a  pradeshiya  sabha 
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that it has been decided under the Resolution No. 61 in the book of 
resolution dated 09.11.2023 that out of roads and narrow roads considered to be belonging to the Wennappuwa Pradeshiya 
Sabha in the schedule included in the Notification published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2342/31 dated 27.07.2023, 
roads and narrow roads for which no case has been filed in a competent court prior to the prescribed date of the said 
notification, and all roads and narrow roads, for which a case had been instituted in a competent court prior to such date 
and which have been confirmed to be belonging to the Pradeshiya Sabha in accordance with verdict given by such court, 
shall be deemed to be roads and narrow roads belonged to the Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha under the directive made by 
the series of "Orders on publication of notices stating the preparing plans and listing of roads and narrow roads maintained 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the Northwestern Province" declared by the Hon. Governor of the Northwestern Province in 
the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 and under the directive made by Section 24(1) of the 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
 
   It is further notified that name list regarding such roads and narrow roads has been included in the Schedule hereto 
and every road and narrow road included in the Schedule, shall be deemed to be a road and narrow road belonged to the 
Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha under subsection (5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
                    W. D. L
ucian , 
 Secretary,
 Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
At the Head Office of Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, 
On 09th November, 2023.
