EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Order under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the 
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989
I, Anuradha yahampath, the Governor of the eastern Province, do hereby make Order as described in the schedule hereto 
by virtue of power vested in me under section 223 of the Pradeshiya sabhas Act No. 15 of 1987 read with section 2 of the 
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989.
AnurAdhA YAhAmpAth,
 Governor,
eastern Province.
Governor's secretariat,
Orr's hill,
trincomalee,
30th January, 2023.
Order
the Chairman of Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha in trincomalee district within the Province of the east has submitted and failed 
to get adopted the budget of the year 2023 as the power vested in him under section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya sabha 
Act No. 15 of 1987. thus the said Chairman of the said Pradeshiya sabha not complied, as provided under section 17 of the 
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman 
effective from 2023.01.28 and that the office of the Chairman of that Pradeshiya Sabha become vacant effective from that date.
