FOREST   CONSER vATION  ORDINANCE   
The  Order
by virtue of powers vested in me by section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) of act, No. 13 of 
1966 as amended by acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1998, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I Pavithra 
Wanniarachchi, the Minister of  Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation do by this order amend the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 1779/15 dated 10.10.2012 by which the Madu Road Forest declared as the Madu Reserve Forest, by removing the word 
Manthei West divisional secretarys division in the first paragraph under the order in the page 9A of the Sinhala print of the 
Gazette notification and substituting Madu and Manthei West Divisional Secretaries Division thereof.
 
 Pa vithra Wanniarachchi,
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation.
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation,
Rajagiriya,
07th day of august, 2023.
