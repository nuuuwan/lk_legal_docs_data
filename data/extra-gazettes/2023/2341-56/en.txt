PRADEShIYA  SABhA  - KuLIYAPITIYA
iN terms of Section 9.3 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, it is hereby notified for public information that it has been 
adopted under resolution No. 2023/705 moved at the General Conference held on 05 July in 2023 at Pradeshiya Sabha 
Kuliyapitiya   that the roads and thoroughfares  included in the schedule below should be declared as roads and thoroughfares  
owned by Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha,  according to the purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West 
Province by the series of "Orders on the preparation of plans and publishing  notices specifying the list of names of the roads 
and thoroughfares  maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province", published by Extraordinary Gazette 
Notification No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 and No. 2317/87  dated 03rd  February 2023 by the Hon. Governor in the 
North Western Province,  to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
 
 It is hereby further notified that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare included 
in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be filed in the competent Court 
to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of 03 months from the date of this notification.
 It is further notified that all the  roads and thoroughfares, against which a legal action had not been taken before the expiry 
of three months,  mentioned in that schedule, shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Kuliyapitiya.
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                     S.M.N.K. G
uNathilaKe,
                                 Secretary and Officer of Executing Powers and Duties,
                                                                             Pradeshiya Sabha Kuliyapitiya.
Pradeshiya Sabha Office Kuliyapitiya,
