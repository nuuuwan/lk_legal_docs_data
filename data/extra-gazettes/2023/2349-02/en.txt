TOuRISm  ACT,  No.  38  Of  2005
Order under Section 53
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the subsection (1) of section 53 of the tourism act, No. 38 of 2005, I, merrennage 
priantha Fernando, Chairman of the sri Lanka tourism development authority on the recommendation of the authority 
do by this order fix -
(a) the prices specified in Schedule I hereto which shall be the minimum prices that shall be imposed, levied, demanded 
or recovered from each occupant in respect of charges for the occupancy of a room and for meals which may be 
provided to such an occupant in a tourist hotel which is categorized as a tourist service under the tourism act 
within the limits of the area of Colombo municipal Council; and
(b) the fees specified in Schedule II hereto which shall be the minimum fees that shall be levied from a tourist hotel 
in respect of re-registration and licensing of such a tourist hotel of which a license has been cancelled due to 
noncompliance of the prices specified in Schedule I hereto.
The prices and the fees specified in Schedule I and II hereto shall come into effect from October 1, 2023.
