KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Butchers Ordinance
AS, nobody in the Kalmunai Municipal limit expressed objections against the persons mentioned in the schedule 01 and 02 below 
in terms of the notice published under Section  7(2) of the Butchers Ordinance, I hereby inform that those persons were given trade 
licenses and Butchers license to slaughter bulls and goats for meat and to run their businesses at the addresses mentioned in the 
Schedule 01 and 02 in terms of the conditions under Section 4(2) of the said Ordinance for one year with effect from 01.01.2023.
I also hereby inform the persons mentioned in the schedule No. 03 were given trade licenses and Chicken Stall license 
to run Chicken stalls at the addresses mentioned in the Schedule 03 under the Local Government Statutory Ordinance fo r 
one year with effect from 01.01.2023.
Further, I inform that the licenses of those who violate the conditions and laws will be cancelled under the Municipal 
Councils  Ordinance, Local Government Statutory Ordinance No. 06 of 1952 and Butchers Ordinance.
 A. M. R AKEEBRR , Attorney - at - law,
Mayor,
