PRADESHIYA SABHA - ANAMADUWA    
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed to the public that it has been passed under the decision book number 2082 held on 22nd November 2023 
that the roads and lanes from among the roads and lanes published in the schedule of the Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2341/42 dated 
20th July 2023 under the provisions of ''Orders regarding the preparation of the plans and for the roads and lanes maintained 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and the publication of advertisements showing the names of roads 
and lanes'' declared by the Hon. Governor of the North Western Province by Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2238/35 
dated 30th July 2021 and the provisions of Section 24 (1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act,. No. 15 of 1987, for which cases have 
not been ﬁ led in a proper court of law before the date assigned in the notiﬁ cation and the roads and lanes for which cases have 
been ﬁ led in a proper court of law and were declared to be considered as roads and lanes owned by the Maho Pradeshiya Sabha.
 It is further provided that a list of the said roads and narro w roads is included in the sub-register and every road and narrow 
road included therein shall be deemed to be the roads and narrow roads owned by the local council under Sub-section (5) of 
Section 24 of the Local Council Act. will be announced.

Resolution
Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha proposes that following the decision book number 2082 held on 22nd November 2023 under 
the provisions of the series of ''Orders regarding the preparat ion of the plans and for the roads and lanes maintained by the 
Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and the publication of advertisements showing the names of roads and lanes''
published by the Hon. Governor of the North Western Province in the Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2238/35 dated  
30th July 2021 and the Provisions of Section 24(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act. No. 15 of  1987, the roads and lanes included 
in the schedule of the Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2341/42 dated 20th July 2023, for which cases have not been ﬁ eld in a proper 
court of law and are included in the following schedule, should  be aﬃ  rmed as roads and lanes owned by the Anamaduwa 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
