SPECIAL   COmmODITY   LEvY  ACT,  No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, ranil 
wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist republic 
of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose the Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 10 per Kg on importation of Maize, classified 
under the Hs Code of 1005.90, on the recommendation of the secretary, Ministry in charge of the subject of agriculture for 
the purpose of manufacturing of animal feeds and arrived such consignments of Maize at the Ports/ airports in sri Lanka 
on or before january 15, 2023.
This Order shall be valid from january 26, 2023 to February 25, 2023 subject to provisions of sub section (3) of 
section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
