ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I,           
dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid act 
in respect of the Horizon College of Business Technology (Private) limited, a company incorporated in sri lanka under the 
Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 62877, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, 
do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the Order made under the said act and published in  Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2273/43, of March 30, 2022, by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Management Honours in 
Accounting and Finance” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified 
under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I 
hereto.
sCHedule I
Conditions
 1. Horizon College of Business Technology (Private) limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme 
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours in Information Technology, 
complete with a minimum of 120 credits (SLQF level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (3) 
‘simple passes’ (s) in any subject stream, in one and the same sitting at the GCe (advanced level) examination 
conducted by the department of examinations of sri lanka or equivalent.

ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid 
act in respect of the sri lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) limited, a company incorporated in                           
sri lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number Gl  24, being recognized as a degree 
awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend the schedule I to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2216/39, of February 07, 2021, by the repeal of the conditions 1 in schedule I and the substitution 
therefor of  the following:-
 “1. In respect of the course of study titled, the Bachelor of science Honours in Financial Mathematics and applied 
Statistics, complete with a minimum of 120 credits (SLQF Level 6), the minimum entry qualifications for admitting 
a student shall be three (3) simple passes (s) in any subject stream including Combined Mathematics as a main 
subject, in one and the same sitting at the GCe (advanced level) examination conducted by the department of 
Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.”.
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA (m.P.),
 Minister of education.
