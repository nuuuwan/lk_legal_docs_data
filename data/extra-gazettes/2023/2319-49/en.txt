 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of member of Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Samarakoon 
Mudiyansele Rupasingha Bandara Samarakoon the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward Number  7 - Idamegama of Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha Who 
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Udagedara Sri Nishshanka Maravanagoda to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Udagedara 
Sri Nishshanka Maravanagoda was selected as the member of the No. 7 - Idamegama of Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha.
