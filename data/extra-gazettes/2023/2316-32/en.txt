SECuRITIES  AND  EXChANGE  COmmISSION  Of  SRI  LANkA   ACT,  NO.  19  Of  2021
Regulation under Section 155 (1)
By Virtue of the powers vested in me by section 155(1) of the securities and Exchange Commission of sri Lanka act, No. 19 
of 2021, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic stabilization and National Policies, do by this regulation, 
specify in the schedule hereto, the rate of Cess to be charged and levied on every repurchase transaction in Corporate debt 
securities carried out on the Over the Counter Platform of the Colombo stock Exchange.
                  this regulation shall come into effect from January 31, 2023.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
  Minister of Finance, Economic
 stabilization and National Policies.
Ministry of  Finance, Economic stabilization and National Policies,
Colombo 01,
January 26, 2023.
Schedule
Item Security Rate
1
Every repurchase transaction in Corporate debt securities issued 
by Companies, which are carried out on the Over the Counter 
Platform of the Colombo stock Exchange
0.006% of the value of the 
repurchase transaction
