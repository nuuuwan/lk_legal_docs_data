TRuST   ORDINANCE   (ChAPTER   87)
Order under Section 114
WHereas the Trustees of the “anjalee and priyanjith Weerasooriya Charitable Trust” namely-
 1. rajinda priyanjith Weerasooria;
 2. Thusharie anjalee Thilothama de silva Weerasooria; and
 3. budrika Tarusha de s. Weerasooria,
   have applied to the minister of Justice, prison affairs and Constitutional reforms for their incorporation under 
section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87);
   Now Therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in by section 114 of the aforesaid Ordinance, i, dr. Wijeyadasa 
rajapaksha, minister of Justice, prison affairs and Constitutional reforms do by this Order authorize the incorporation of 
the said Trustees and direct and declare that –
 (a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the 
deed of Trust bearing No. 495 of July 30, 2020 attested by sembukutti arachchige dona avanthika rukshani 
senaratne, Notary public of Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal 
under the name and style of the “anjalee and priyanjith Weerasooria Charitable Trust”;
