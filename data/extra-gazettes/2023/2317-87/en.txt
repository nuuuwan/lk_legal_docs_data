Local  Government Notifications
Pradeshiya  sabha  act,  No. 15  of  1987
I, Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Karannagoda, being the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province do hereby publish 
the following amendment to the Sections 3(1) of the Gazette No. 2238/35 - 2021 July 30 by virtue of powers vested under 
Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Incidental Provisions) 
Act, No. 12 of 1989.
 Wasantha Karannagoda,
 Admiral of the Fleet,
 Hon. Governor in the North Western Province. 
At Kurunegala,
30th January, 2023.
3.(1) By virtue of powers vested under Subsection (1) of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 
of 1987, hereinafter referred to as “Act” to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Incidental 
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, permission is hereby granted to each Pradeshiya Sabha in the North 
Western Province, hereinafter referred to as “Sabha” to extend the required time period up to an additional 
period of twenty four months from effective date of these orders are coming into force, under Section 24 
of the Act.
