Notice under  Section 07 
 
 
My Reference No.  : KSWD&EIP/ACQ/DWC/02. 
Land Ministry’s  Reference No.  : 4-3/4/2020/LRB/181. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No . 28 of 1964, that               
the Government has acquired the allotment of land to 
create a lake in the allotments of marshy lands called 
Maha Kumbura and Kiththampahuwa located adjacent to 
Vivekarama Mawatha, Wellampitiya under Kolonnawa 
Storm Water Drainage and Environment Improvement 
Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired : 
 
Three (03) allotments of Land in total extent of 1.4323 
Hectares, depicted as Lot Numbers 01, 02 and 03 in the 
preliminary plan No. Co. 10036 dated 08.03.2023 
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in village 
called Wellampitiya in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 
508 of Kiththampahuwa in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Kolonnawa in Colombo \District, Western 
Province and bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the land: Maha Kumbura and 
Kiththampahuwa; Description of the land: Home Garden 
and Marshy Land; Claimant : State; Original Claimant : 
Seylan Bank (Private Company), No. 90, Galle Road, 
Colombo; Total Extent: 0.6966 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the land: -; Description of the land: Canal; 
Claimant : State; Original Claimant : Could not be trade; 
Total Extent: 0.0498 Hectare. 
(Published by Authority)
