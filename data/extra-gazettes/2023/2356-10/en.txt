REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS
Notice  that  the  main  Electoral  Register  for the Year 2023  has  been certified  under  the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
uNder Section 20(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, notice is given on behalf of the Registering Officer of each 
electoral district that the 2023 main electoral register has been certified on 31st October 2023 in terms of the Registration 
of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980; that the electoral districts mentioned in Column I of the following schedule is certified by 
the Assistant Registering Officer in the corresponding entry in Column II thereof; and that inspection may be made during 
normal office hours at the District Elections Office/Offices mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column III thereof.
 
 
Saman Sri ratnayake,
 commissioner General of elections,
 election commission.
Elections Secretariat, Rajagiriya,
On 31st October, 2023.
