Regulations
   the Crown Lands regulations, 1948 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 9912 of october 15, 1948 are 
hereby amended  by the repeal of sub-regulation (1) of regulation 4 thereof, and the substitution therefor, of the following 
sub-regulation:-
    “(1). The period to be prescribed for the purposes of Section 22 of the Ordinance shall be fifty years:
   Provided however, the period for a lease of a land for commercial and agricultural activities of investment projects 
approved by  the Board of Investment of sri Lanka shall be ninety-nine years. the tax for such lease shall be revised in every 
five years at the rate of twenty per centum.”.
