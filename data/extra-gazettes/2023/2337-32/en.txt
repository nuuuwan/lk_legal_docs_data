Declaration of names and Details of Roads
IN terms of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as per the decision dated173/19.12. 2019 of Maritimepattu 
Pradeshiya Sabha in Mullaitivu District by virtue of powers vested in Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha as per Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act,roads (1 to 1103) under mentioned Schedule, published as the roads belong to Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
 
  It is hereby noticed that in terms of Section 24(2) Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987 if anyone is to be the owner of 
the land of the roads of which are identified by Pradeshiya Sabha shall raise his or her objections to me with the provable 
documents within one month from the date of this notice published  in the Gazette or institute action in the appropriate court 
with in three months from the date of this notice published in the Gazette with one-month prior notice to establish his or her 
ownership.
 K. 
ShanmugathaSan
                      Secretary,
 Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
