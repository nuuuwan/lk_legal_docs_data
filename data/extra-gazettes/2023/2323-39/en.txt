DECLARATION  uNDER  Sub  SECTION  (1)  Of  SECTION  3A Of  ThE  LOCAL  
AuThORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER  262)
By, virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 3a  of the Local authorities Elections Ordinance 
(chapter 262) of Legislative Enactments of  Sri Lanka, read with Section 4 of the Interpretations Ordinance (chapter 12) 
of said Enactment, I, dinesh Gunawardena Prime minister and minister of Public administration, home affairs, Provincial 
Councils and Local Government, do hereby declare that the term of office of the National Delimitation Committee on 
demarcation of Wards for Local authorities, consisting of the following members appointed with effect from 01st November 
2022 up to 28th February 2023 is extended up to 31st march 2023.
   mr. mahinda deshapriya (chairman)
   mr. Jayalath R. V . dissanayake (member)
   mrs. W. m. m. R. adikari (member)
   mr. K. Thavalingam (member)
   mr. I. a. hameed (member)
 
Dinesh GunawarDena (M.P),
