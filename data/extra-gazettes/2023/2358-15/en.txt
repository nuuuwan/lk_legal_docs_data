APPOINTMENTS  MADE  UNDER  SECTION  2(C)  OF  THE  WELFARE  BENEFITS  ACT,  No. 24  OF  2002
BY virtue of powers vested in me under the Section 2 (c) of the Welfare Benefits Act, No. 24 of 2002, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, 
minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do 
hereby, to implement the objectives of the said act, appoint all district secretaries as additional Welfare Commissioners and 
all Divisional Secretaries as Deputy Welfare Commissioners. District Secretary and Divisional Secretaries include officers 
appointed on acting basis to perform and cover the duties of that post. I declare these appointments shall came into force 
with effect from November 01, 2023.
   the duties for additional Welfare Commissioners and deputy Welfare Commissioners will be assigned by the 
Welfare Benefits Board in terms of Section 8(1) of the Welfare Benefits Act, No. 24 of 2002.
     
 R
anil WickRemesinghe,
 minister of Finance, economic stabilization and
 National Policies.
