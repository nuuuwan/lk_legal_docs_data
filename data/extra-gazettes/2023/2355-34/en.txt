CONSTITuTION Of ThE DEmOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPubLIC Of SRI LANkA
Order under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution
IN terms of the provisions of the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amendment by the registration of electors 
(Amendments) Act, No. 22 of 2021, it was notified in the extraordinary Gazette No. 2349/38 dated 15.09.2023 that the 
supplementary electoral register 2023 (1) pertaining to the period commencing from the first day of February 2023 to the 
thirty first day of May 2023 was certified on the fifteenth day of September 2023.
   02. Main register 2022, supplementary electoral register 2022(1), supplementary electoral register 2022(2), 
supplementary electoral register 2022(3) and supplementary electoral register 2023 (1) remained in force as of 15th 
september 2023 and accordingly in pursuance of the powers vested in the election commission under article 98 (8) of 
the constitution of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, the election commission by this Order certify that the 
number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members which each electoral district specified in the 
corresponding entry in column I of the schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation under article 97 
and article 98.
