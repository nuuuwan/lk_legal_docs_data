THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No. : GCF/ACQ/NG/50/LR.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : L/03J/94/LRB/160.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government has
acquired the below mentioned lands to retain water under
Greater Colombo Flood Control and Environmental
Improvement Project.
Description of the Lands to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of 0.5784 hectare
depicted as lot numbers 01 and 02 in the preliminary plan No.
Co. 9709 dated 22.11.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in village called Welikada in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 514 A of Welikada West the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte in Colombo district
of the Western province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Arachchiya Kumbura; Description: Open
waste land and marshy land, a temporary building and parts
of 17 temporary buildings are available; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: -; Extent: 0.0510 hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Arachchiya Kumbura; Description: Open
waste land and marshy land, 23 temporary buildings and parts
of 16 temporary buildings are available; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: Ven. Batugampola Indrasiri Thero; Extent:
0.5274 hectare.
