TRuSTS   ORDINANCE   (ChAPTER   87)
Order under Section 114
wHeReas the trustees of the "Joan de Mel trust" namely -
 1. Romesh de Mel;
 2. Nalini sirima ellawala; and 
 3. shaila amalean,
have applied to the Minister of Justice, Prison affairs and Constitutional Reforms for their incorporation under section 114 
of the trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87)
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, dr. wijayadasa Rajapaksha, 
Minister of Justice, Prison affairs and Constitutional Reforms do by this Order authorize the incorporation of the said 
trustees, and direct and declare that-
 (a) the trustees of the said trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the 
deed of trust bearing No. 7304 of March 16, 1999 attested by shelton ernest abeysuriya, Notary Public of   
Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style of 
the "Joan de Mel trust";
