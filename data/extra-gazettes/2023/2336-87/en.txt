PILGRImAGES ORDINANCE
It is hereby notified that the annual Sri Maha Bodhiraja Procession of Sri Shasanalankararama Ancient Temple in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of Udugama Divisional Secretary’s Division of Nagoda, District of Galle, Southern Province, will be 
held from 03rd to 11th of July 2023 as per the regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1956/11 
dated 01.03.2016 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance 
(Cap. 175).
