ACT  No. 23  Of 1979  (AmENDED)  mAhAwELI  AuThORITY  Of SRI  LANkA  
By virtue of powers vested in me by under sections 54 (1) & 54(2) of act No. 23 of 1979 mahaweli authority of sri Lanka, 
I, Roshan Ranasinghe minister of Irrigation do hereby order to publish the Regulation pertaining to the carrying out and 
empowering of principles and provisions of this act which will come into operation on the date of its publication
 Roshan Ranasinghe,
 minister of Irrigation.
 
ministry of Irrigation,
on this 08 day of august 2023.
   a  learned advisory committee is hereby appointed to advice and guide the Hon. minister to whom assigned the 
subject of Irrigation for the purpose of investigation, direction, supervision and giving effect to the powers, duties, principles 
and provisions of this act;
 (1)	 Two	officers	from	all	island	services	who	are	having	subject	knowledge	(retired)
	 (2)	 An	University	Lecturer	who	is	having	expertise	knowledge	in	field
	 (3)	 An	Officer	who	is	having	expertise	knowledge	in	finance
 (4) an Engineer who is having subject knowledge
 (5) an experienced Lawyer
