PRADESHIYA  SABHA  GALGAMUWA
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed for public information that it has been adopted under resolution No. 1136 of the Secretary of the Management 
Committee held on 11.07.2023 at Pradeshiya Sabha Galgamuwa that all the roads and thoroughfares for which legal action 
had not been ﬁ led in a proper Judicial Court before the date speciﬁ ed or roads and thoroughfares conﬁ rmed as the roads and 
thoroughfares that should be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha Galgamuwa as per the Decree of a Judicial Court in which a case 
had been ﬁ led before the said date and not mentioned in the following sc hedule from among the roads and thoroughfares 
given in the list of the schedule of the notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2331/ 02 on 08.05.2023, shall 
be conﬁ rmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Galgamuwa in terms of orders made by the series of 
“ Orders on publication of notiﬁ cations specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the 
Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province” published by Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/ 35 and dated 30th July 
2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, 
No. 15 of 1987.
  
 It is further notiﬁ  ed that a list of such roads and thoroughfares is presented in this schedule and every road and thoroughfare 
speciﬁ ed therein shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares vested in the Pradeshiya Sabha Galgamuwa under Sub-Section 
(5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
