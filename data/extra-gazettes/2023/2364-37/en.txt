PORTS   AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT   LEVY   ACT,  No. 18  OF  2011
Order under Section 3
by virtue of the powers vested in me by sub-section (3) of section 3 of the Ports and airports development Levy act,            
No. 18 of 2011, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do hereby, 
amend the Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2312/67 dated december 31, 2022 as last amended 
by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2350/45 dated september 21, 2023 by  addition to the schedule IV thereto of 
the Articles specified in Column III corresponding to the HS Headings and HS Codes specified in the Column I and II as 
exempted in column IV of the schedule hereto.
This Order shall come in to effect from January 01, 2024.
