THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of land depicted in Advance Tracing No. Ka/
MAT/2021/448 dated 12.11.2021 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Grama Niladari Division of No. 805 
Mathugama East, Village of Mathugama in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Mathugama of Kalutara District in 
Western Province and bounded as follows:
 0 1 .  Lot ‘A’ - St. George Estate - Extent: 0.0203 Hectare
 North: St. George Estate claimed by Sri Lanka Transport 
Board and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya 
Sabha) and St. George claimed by Land Reform Commission; 
South: St. George claimed by Land Reform Commission and 
Lot B of this plan; West: Lot B and St. George Estate claimed 
by Sri Lanka Transport Board.
 02. Lot ‘B’ - St. George Estate - Extent: 0.1195 Hectare
 North: St. George Estate claimed by Sri Lanka Transport 
Board, Lot A of this plan and St. George claimed by Land 
Reform Commission; East: Lot A, St. George Estate claimed 
by Land Reform Commission; South: St. George Estate 
claimed by Land Reform Commission; West: St. George 
Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission and St. George 
Estate claimed by Sri Lanka Transport Board.
