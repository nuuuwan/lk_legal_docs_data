Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No.  11/0425 ANURADHAPURA DISTRICT 
  
IT is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner 
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 110301 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to th e village of Panankaniya (Block 
01) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari 
Division of No.349 Nelawagama in the Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Nochchiyagama in the District of Anuradhapura in the 
Province of North Central shoul d submit his claim to the 
undersigned before 19th of June 2023 In the event of failure  to 
submit any claim before the above -mentioned date, action will be 
taken to hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and 
accordingly publish such determination in the Government 
Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act. 
The Cadastral Map No. 110301 refe rred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office  of the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office. 
 
Further information may be obt ained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
