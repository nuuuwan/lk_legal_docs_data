	4.	 Notwithstanding	the	Regulation	No.	3	above,	importation	of	goods	specified	therein	shall	be	allowed	for	the	Custom	
Clearance subject to an Import Control License (ICL) on the following instances;
(a) Goods, which have the shipped on Bill of Lading date on or before june 14, 2023, and arrived on or before 
june 30, 2023. 
(b) Goods, which have been imported based on the Letter of Credit, established on or before june 14, 2023, and 
arrived within 60 days from the date of these Regulations
(c) Goods, which have been imported based on an advance Payment effected on or before june 14, 2023, and 
arrived within 60 days from the date of these Regulations.
5. To achieve the objectives of these Regulations, the Controller General of Imports and exports shall issue “Operational 
Instructions” to the director General of Customs, licensed banks or any other relevant authorities. 
6. Final decision shall be made by the Controller General of Imports and exports on any issues arising in relation to these 
Regulations.
7. Import Control License fee, which shall be payable on the following category of goods, shall be inserted into the
