Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2023”. 
2. The Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2278/21 
dated May 06, 2022, as amended is hereby further amended.
3. Importation of goods, classiﬁ  ed under the HS Codes 0402.21 (Milk powder) and 0713.40.11 (Red whole lentils) shall 
be exempted from the Regulation No. 6 of the Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, referred 
above.

4. The Director General of Sri Lanka Customs is hereby authoriz ed to release any cargo / Consignments of milk powder 
or red whole lentils, referred in Regulation No. 3 above, impor ted under the Document Against Acceptance (DA) or 
Document Against Payment (DP) terms and arrived at any Port / A irport of Sri Lanka during the period of January 19, 
2023, to April 05, 2023, without being endorsed the Proforma invoice of such goods by the relevant licensed banks prior 
to the shipped on board date of Bill of Lading / Airway Bill of the same from Sri Lanka Customs subject to a Penalty of 
Rs.10,000 per Bill of Lading or Airway Bill.
5. To achieve the objectives of these regulations, the Controll er General of Imports and Exports shall issue “Operational 
Instructions” to the Director General of Customs, licensed banks, or any other relevant authorities.
