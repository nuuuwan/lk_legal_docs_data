sabha  under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF    the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  member of Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which 
Muhammaduththambi Fausulla the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon  member of that No. 03-Sinnapalli of Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ahamathu Abdul Cader Muhammathu Nizam to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning officer of the Sammanthurai 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Ahamathu Abdul Cader Muhammathu Nizam was selected as the member of the No. 03-Sinnapalli of 
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
