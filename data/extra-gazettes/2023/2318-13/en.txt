In the uva Provincial High Court of monaragala
In the presence of the additional High Court Judge, R.S.a. dissanayake
Case Number - election Petition 01/2018
date :  13.09.2021
Report of the Election Judge in terms of section 82X of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
The determination of the trial of the election petition which was presented to the Provincial High Court in monaragala under 
the aforesaid case number was given on 13.09.2021.
The Report of the election Judge in respect of the facts disclosed and proved in that trial is presented as follows:-
The reasons for the order given by me upon the conclusion of the trial have been described in such order in detail. 
a copy of that order has been attached to this report. accordingly, this report is presented by summarizing the facts proven 
and unproven in that trial as follows:-
The proven and unproven facts in respect of the corrupt or illegal practices alleged to have been committed by 
dissanayake mudiyanselage Tharanga Harshaka Priya Prasad dissanayake who had secured the highest preferences in the 
election held on 10.02.2018 in respect of maduruketiya electoral area for monaragala Pradeshiya Sabha as follows :- 
 (a) It has not been proved at the trial that this candidate, dissanayake mudiyanselage Tharanga Harshaka 
Priya Prasad dissanayake or his representative has provided, cash, material and equipment to the voluntary 
organizations and new domestic electricity connections to certain houses as specified in paragraphs 12(i) (ii) 
of the election petition.
 (b) It has not been proved at the trial that the alleged offences, providing cash to maduruketiya ekamuthu Funeral 
aid Society and providing material support to oil Farm Ranamayura Sports Club and Kalugala Funeral 
Aid Society as specified in paragraph 13 of the election petition have been committed by the candidate 
dissanayake mudiyanselage Tharanga Harshaka Priya Prasad dissanayake or his representative.
 (c) It has been proved that the offence of offering bribes by providing new water connections to certain houses 
in Maduruketiya electoral area on or around 28.11.2017 as specified in paragraph 12(iii) of the election 
petition, has been committed by Siriyarathne anura Shantha Kumara bearing national identity card number 
812811401V , a representative of the candidate dissanayake mudiyanselageTharanga Harshaka Priya Prasad 
dissanayake with the knowledge and consent of such candidate.
