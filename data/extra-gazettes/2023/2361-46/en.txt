EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No. : MOR/LAN/01/FI/206. 
Ministry of Tourism and Lands No. : 4-3/4/2017/D/124. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for location of Electrical and Mechanical 
Engineering Base Workshop of Sri Lanka Army. 
 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land in extent of 3.3897 Hectares depicted in 
Sheet 2 of the 10th Block of 20 Blocks of the Cadastral Map 
No. 520210 dated 15.08.2022, prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the village called Lakshapathiya (Within 
the Municipal Council of Moratuwa) in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Moratuwa in Colombo District of the 
Western Province described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 307 
Name of the Land : Kutteriyawalawatte, Velonawatte; 
Claimant : Sri Lanka Transport Board; Extent: 3.3531 
Hectares. 
 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority)

compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
