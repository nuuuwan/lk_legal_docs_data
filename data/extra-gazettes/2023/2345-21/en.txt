REvISION  Of  ELECTORAL   REGISTERS - 2023 
Notice of the Election Commission  on the Display of  'A' and 'B' Lists, Claims and Objections
Definition
 01. “Act”   -  Registration of electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of electors 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2021
 02. “Valid electoral Register” - The main register of the year 2022 and 2022(1), 2022(2) and 2022(3) supplementary 
electoral Registers
 03. “A'  List”  -  List of persons whose names are included in the 2022 electoral Register, who have been 
disqualified due to death or leaving the residence or for any other reason, namely those 
who are recommended for removal in the year 2023
 04. "B' List" - List of persons who are not included in the 2022 electoral Register but are eligible to be 
included in the 2023 electoral Register on completion of 18 years of age or residency or 
for any other reason.
Exhibition Centers of Electoral Register (or part thereof) valid for the year 2022 and "A" and "B" Lists
 Exhibition Center Electoral register and lists or parts thereof kept for display
 01. Each District Election Office Full copy of the Electoral Register valid for the year 2022 in the relevant electoral 
district (district) and copies of the relevant "A" and "B" lists
 02. e ach divisional secretariat  A copy of the 2022 valid electoral Register section showing all the polling districts 
in the relevant divisional secretariat and copies of the relevant "A" and "B" lists

  Instructions
01. It was announced today (August 15, 2023) that the Registering Officers in each electoral district have completed the
preparation of Lists "A" and "B" in accordance with section 13 of the Registration of electors Act.
02. Every person who is qualified in terms of Section 14 of the Registration of Electors Act to have his own name or someone
else's name entered in the said electoral register applicable to each electoral  district and whose  name has been omitted
from the electoral register and from List "B" or whose name has been included in List "A" may claim to have his name
entered in such register. Claims to have his or someone else's name entered or retained in the register may be made by
himself or by someone else on his behalf, as the case may be, and they may apply substantially in form “A” and “B” set
out in the Schedule to above Registration of Electors Act so as to reach the registering officer of each electoral district
on or before 11 
september 2023, within 4 weeks from the date of the publication of this notice in the Gazette of the
democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka  i.e. from 15 August 2023.
03. Any person whose name appears in the electoral or in list "B" for any electoral district may object to the entry of his
own name or to the entry or retention of the name of any other person appearing in such register or list "B". every such
objection shall be made in duplicate and shall be substantially in form "C" set out in the schedule to the above Act and
shall be made so as to reach the registering officer of the Election Office. All objections to the inclusion of the name
in the register should be sent to the registering officer of the District Elections Office on or before 11 September 2023,
within 4 weeks from the date of the publication of this notice in the Gazette of the 
democratic socialist Republic of
sri Lanka i.e. from 15 August 2023.
04.  every objection to the entry of the name of any claimant in the electoral register shall be made in duplicate and shall
be substantially in form “ d” set out in the schedule to the above Act and shall be made so as to reach the registering
officer within 14 days from the date on which the notice containing the names of claimants is exhibited at the
District Election Office. This notice will be published in the District Election Offices on 01 September 2023 and
15 
september 2023.
05. Form ‘A’ ‘B’ required for submission of claims and Form ‘C’ ‘D’ required for submission of objections can be obtained
from the District  Election Offices, Divisional Secretariats, Local Authorities or Grama Niladhari Offices.
R. M. A. L. RAthnAyAke,
Chairman,
 election Commission.
15th August, 2023,
At the elections secretariat.
