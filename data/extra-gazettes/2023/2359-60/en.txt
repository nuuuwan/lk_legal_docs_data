STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. G.H.Udaya Saman working at the Sri Lanka Standards institution is entitled for the below - mentioned promotions 
as per the amended recruitment procedure approved in 2021 and whether he had been caused injustice by not getting them 
and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
 1. MA(Tec) - Grade iii from 1994-2004 (in 10 years)
 2. MA(Tec) - Grade iii from 2004-2009 (in 05 years)
 3. Post of JM in 2009
 4. After 05 years in the post of JM ie, in 2014 - the post of Assistant Director.
 B. K. P

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mrs. R.M.P.R.P. Rajapaksa who had been appointed to the Post of Conﬁ dential Secretary to the General Manager  
on 01.04.2012 had been caused, injustice by not being given the previledges enjoyed by the person appointed to the Post of 
Conﬁ dential Secretary to the Chairman 03 months prior to 01.04.2012 and by changing the grade MM 1-1 given to her as 
JM 1-1 as per the new recruitment procedure implemented after the approval of the employee estimate and if she had been 
caused injustice, to what relief she is entitled.
