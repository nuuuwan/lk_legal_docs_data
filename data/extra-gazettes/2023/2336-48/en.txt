ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) read with section 21 of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I,                
dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants Commission, do 
by this Order, amend the schedule to the Order made under section 21 of the said act, published in Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 420/25, of september 26, 1986 (as amended from time to time) establishing the eastern university, sri Lanka, by the 
addition immediately after the entry “2. department of Multidisciplinary studies” appearing in Column II of the schedule 
which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Technology” appearing in Column I of the Schedule (specified in the Order 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2002/35 of january 18, 2017) of the following entry :-
“3. department of Information and Communication Technology”.
 
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA (m.P.),
 Minister of education.
