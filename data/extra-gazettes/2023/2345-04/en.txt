THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : KES/DS/LND/AC/8/5/22.
Ministry of Land Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2019/HW/627.
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot No. 1 (Lots 01) allotments in total extent of about 0.0067
Hectare, of land in Preliminary Plan No. CO 9976 and dated
29.08.2022 prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of Surveys
of Colombo District on behalf of the Surveyor  General, situated
in Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 582B, Pelenwatta East in
the village of  Pelenwatta (Within the boundaries of  Kesbewa
Municipal Council) in Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kesbewa in the Colombo District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land:  Delgahakanaththa, Assmt. No - 29/2,
Maththegoda Road; Description: Contains a garden, parts of
the 2 permanent buildings; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
Janak Priyantha Gamage, Maththegoda, (to finalize the land
acquisition process); Extent: 0.0067 Hectare.
          All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat  of Kesbewa on
02.10.2023 at 10.00 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to
me inwriting (in duplicate) on or before the 25.09.2023 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
K. P . P
EMADASA,
 Divisional Secretary ,
  Kesbewa.
