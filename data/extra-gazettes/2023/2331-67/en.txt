NATIONAL  ZOOLOGICAL  GARDENS  ACT  
With effect from 02.05.2023, the fees charged for providing food for the animals, taking photos with the animals and 
providing internal transportation facilities in the Zoological Gardens of the department of National Zoological Gardens will 
be set as follows in accordance with the powers vested in the minister under section 13 of the National Zoological Gardens 
act, No. 41 of 1982,
   Pa vithra Wanniarachchi, attorney-at-Law,
   minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation.
   
02nd may, 2023.
