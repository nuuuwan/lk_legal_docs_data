NOTICE No : 83/0433 HAMBANTOTA DISTRICT  
  
IT is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner 
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 830151 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Hangamuwa (Block 
04) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari 
Division of No. 318 Dammulla West in the Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Beliatta in the District of Hambantota in the Province

Section 14 of the above Act.

District Survey Office, Office  of the Commissioner of Title
