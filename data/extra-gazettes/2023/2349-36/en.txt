PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Central Bank of Sri Lanka Notices
FINANCE  BUSINESS  ACT,  No. 42  OF 2011
CANCELLATION of the licence issued to carry on finance business under Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 
of 2011. 
 d r. P. NaNdalal WeerasiNghe,
 Chairman of the Monetary Board and the
 Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
13th September 2023,
Colombo.
  The public is hereby informed that in terms of Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, the Monetary 
Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka has decided to cancel the licence issued to Bimputh Finance PLC under the said 
Finance Business Act with effect from 01st September 2023. Accordingly, Bimputh Finance PLC is not permitted to carry 
on finance business with effect from 01st September 2023.
