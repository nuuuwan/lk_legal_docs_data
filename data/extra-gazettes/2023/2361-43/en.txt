PRADESHIYA  SABHA - NATTANDIYA
IT is hereby notiﬁ  ed for the public information that it has been adopted under the resolution No. 2454 moved at the General 
Conference held on 04.12.2023 at Pradeshiya Sabha Nattandiya that all the roads and thoroughfares for which legal action 
had not been ﬁ  led in a proper Judicial Court before the date speciﬁ  ed or roads and throughfares conﬁ  rmed as the roads and 
thoroughfares that should be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha Nattandiya as per the Decree of a Judicial Court in which a case has 
been ﬁ  led before the said date and not mentioned in the following Schedule from among the roads and thoroughfares given 
in the list of the schedule of the notiﬁ  cation published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2341/55 and dated 21.07.2023 shall 
be conﬁ  rmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Nattandiya in terms of orders made by the series of 
"orders on publications of notiﬁ cations specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the 
Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province" published by Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 
2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the Order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, 
No. 15 of 1987.
 It is further notiﬁ  ed that, a list of said roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule here to and all the roads 
and thoroughfares mentioned in that schedule shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Nattandiya under Sub Section (5) of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
K. P. Chandana Kumara,
Secretary,
At Nattandiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
 04th  December, 2023.
Resolution
 Nattandiya Pradeshiya Sabha proposes that the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions had not been ﬁ  led in a proper 
Judicial Court and not mentioned in the following Schedule from among the roads and thoroughfares given in the list of Schedule 
of the notiﬁ  cation published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette dated 21.07.2023 according to the resolution No.2454 made at the 
Pradeshiya Sabha Conference held on 04.12.2023, shall be conﬁ  rmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Nattandiya in terms of orders made by the series of " Orders on publication of notiﬁ  cations specifying preparation of 
plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within North Western Province" published by 
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms 
of the Order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
