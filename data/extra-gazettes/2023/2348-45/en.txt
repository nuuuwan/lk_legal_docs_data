THE  UTTAMA  SEV A  PADAKKAMA
IT is hereby notified that the President of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka being the Commander in Chief of 
the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to award the Uttama Seva Padakkama for the year Two Thousand Twenty-one 
in terms of regulation four of the Presidential Warrant in instituting, constituting and creating the Uttama Seva Paddakkama, 
published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 156/5 of 01st September1981, to the persons named in the Schedule 
hereto.
