I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the land intended to be acquired: 
 
The following allotment of land marked Lot No. 01 
depicted in Sheet No. 27 of Zone No. 05 of the Cadastral 
Map No. 320301 dated 30.12.2022 prepared by Senior 
Superintend of Surveys of Kandy on behalf of the 
Surveyor General, situated at the Village called 
Kirimetiya in the Grama Niladhari Division (278) of 
Gurudeniya West in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale in the District 
of Kandy, Central Province and containing in extent 
0.1299 Hectares. 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority)

I hereby give notice to the public that every person 
interested in the said land/servitude which is to be 
acquired is informed to appear personally or by agent 
duly authorized in writing before me on 04.04.2023 at 
10.00 a.m at the Divisional Secretariat of Kandy Four 
Gravets and Gangawata Korale. 
 
The nature of their interests in the land/lands, the 
particulars of their claim for compensation, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount should be notified to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 27.03.2023.
