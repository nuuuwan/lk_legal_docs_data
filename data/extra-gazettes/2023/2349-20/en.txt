NOTIfICATION
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry act (Chapter 393), I, do hereby enlarge 
once again the period of time ending 21st July, 2021, which has been enlarged from time to time by the said powers, up to 
30th September, 2023 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission of Inquiry 
established in terms of section 2 of the aforesaid act on 20th January, 2021 by Proclamation published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2211/55 dated 21st January, 2021.
