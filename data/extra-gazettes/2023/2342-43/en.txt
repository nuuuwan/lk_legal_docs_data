Local Government Notifications
Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama 
IT is hereby notified for the public information that it has been decided under Recommendation No. 21 and Resolution  
No. 182 of the Management Committee Meeting held on 14.07.2023 that the missed roads and thoroughfares included 
in the schedule below should be declared as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha, in addition to the 
Gazette Notification No. 2307/17 and dated 24.11.2022, in terms of the purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the 
North-West Province by the series of "Orders on the preparation of plans and publishing notices specifying the list of 
names of the roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province", published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province 
to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
   It is hereby further notified that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare 
included in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be filed in the competent 
Court to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of three months from the date of this notification.
   It is further notified that every road and thoroughfare included in the said schedule, against which a legal action has 
not been taken before the expiry of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or a thoroughfare which has 
been vested in the Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
                   M.W. L. S
oMachandra ,
 Secretary and Officer of Executing Powers and Duties,
 Pradeshiya Sabha - Nawagaththegama.
