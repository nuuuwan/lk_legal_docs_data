uPGRADING  Of  PROvINCIAL  GENERAL  hOSPITAL  -  BADuLLA  AS  A TEAChING  hOSPITAL 
accordiNG  to the Budget Speech 2022, it has been proposed to establish a faculty of medicine in Uva Wellassa 
University of Sri Lanka, with the objective of expanding the medical education in Sri Lanka. The ministry of Education has 
issued the Gazette Notification No. 2324/12 and dated 20.03.2023, in order to notify the establishment of the medical faculty 
in Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka by revising the order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 of the University 
act,    No. 16 of 1978.
The Provincial General Hospital (PGH), Badulla has been selected to facilitate the clinical training for students of 
the said new faculty. accordingly, the Provincial General Hospital, Badulla is upgraded and re-categorized as a Teaching 
Hospital with immediate effect to develop the required facilities for under-graduate and post-graduate training.
