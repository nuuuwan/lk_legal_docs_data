PRADESHIYA  SABHA - NARAMMALA
" IT  is hereby notified for public information that it has been passed under the Decision No. 709 of the Managing Committee held 
on 31.08.2023, that by virtue of the orders published by the Hon. Governor of the North Western Province in the "Preparation 
of Plans for he Roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and publication 
of advertisements stating the name list" in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 and the order 24(1) of 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, all the roads and thoroughfares for which a case has not been filed in the proper court 
before the date specified in the said notification and the roads and thorougthfares which have been confirmed to be owned by 
the Pradeshiya Sabha in accordance with the judgment given by that Court, for which a case has been filed before that date,  
among the roads and thoroughfares, considered to be owned by the Pardeshiya Sabha Narammala in the Schedule contained in 
the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette  Paper No. 2334/45 dated 02.06.2023 should be considered as the roads 
and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Narammala.
 It is further informed that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road and 
thoroughfare included in it should be considerd as a road and thoroughfare owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Narammala under 
Sub-section (5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.     
 
 D. D. Wickramasinghe  
 Secretary,
 (Officer of executing powers, functions and duties)
 Pradeshiya Sabha Narammala.
Head Office of Pradeshiya Sabha Naramamala,
01st September 2023.

Decision

mentioned in the list included in the following schedule should be considered as the roads and thoroughfares maintained by 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Narammala in terms of the orders of seris on zz preparation of Plans for the Roads and thoroughfares

July 2021 and the order made under Section 24(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
