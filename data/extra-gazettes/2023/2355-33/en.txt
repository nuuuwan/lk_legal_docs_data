Kurunegala   municipal   council 
 
Budget for the year 2024
Notice is hereby given, in terms of Section 212 (b) of the Municipal council ordinance (chapter 252) that the Budget of 
the Kurunegala Municipal Council, for the financial year 2024 containing the estimates of the available Municipal revenue 
and details of the proposed expenditure of the Kurunegala Municipal council, will be kept open for public inspection at the 
Kurunegala Municipal Council front Office and Public library, Kurunegala for seven days commencing from 25th october 
2023.
   Approved by the powers conferred according to the Municipal Council Ordinance Act under section 286 (a).
