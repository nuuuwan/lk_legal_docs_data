uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section  25A
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section  25a  of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  dr. a. d. susil 
Premajayantha, the minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid  act, in respect of  
“The Lyceum campus (Private) Limited”, a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the companies act, No. 7 of 2007 
and bearing registration Number PV 130972 and located at No. 10, raymond road, Nugegoda do by this Order subject 
to such conditions as specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize “The Lyceum Campus (Private) Limited” as a Degree 
Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in  
column I of the schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in column II of the 
schedule II hereto :-
schedULe I
Conditions
 1.  The Lyceum campus (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “institute”) shall, when admitting students for the 
degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours in Primary Education 
with a minimum of 120 Credits (SLQF Level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of ;
 (a) at least three (3)  simple Passes (s) in any stream in one and the same sitting at the G.c.e. (advance Level) 
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
