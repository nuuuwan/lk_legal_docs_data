I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No.28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired:- 
 
The below-described allotment of land with an extent of 
0.0059 Hectares shown in Preliminary Plan No. 10015, 
Prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village 
of Depanama in the Maharagama Divisional Secretariat 
Division, in the District of Colombo in Western Province. 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Not known; Land use: Road; 
Original Claimant: Paramadhamma Temple, Moraketiya, 
Pannipitiya; Claimant: The Government; Extent: 0.0059 
Hectare. 
All persons claiming right, title or any other interest in 
the aforesaid land/ servitude are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of 
Maharagama on the 06.09.2023 and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 
04.09.2023 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/ servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 M. M. K. Dilrukshi Walpola,  
 Divisional Secretary,   
 Maharagama. 
 
On 16th day of August, 2023,
