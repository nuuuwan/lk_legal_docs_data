Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: DS/KU/LND/ACQUS/1/5. 
Ministry of Tourism and Land No.: 4-3/14/2020/CEB/82. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 
of the land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964. that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired:- 
 
Allotment of land called in extent 0.0334 of Hectare 
shown as Lot 01 in Cadastral Map No. CM 260225 dated 
17.07.2023 prepared by the Surveyor General, Situated in 
Kumpurupiddi South Grama Niladhari Division, 
Divisional Secretariat Divi sion of Kuchchaveli in 
Trincomalee District and bounded as follows.  
Lot No. 01 - Extent: 0.0334 Hectare. 
 
North: Road (PS); East: Part of Kumpurupiddi South 
Village (TP 346434); South: Part of Kumpurupiddi South 
Village (TP 346434); West: Part of Kumpurupiddi South 
Village (TP 346434). 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretariat Trincomalee on the 
21st day of November 2023 at 10.00 o'clock. And you 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 07th day of November 2023 
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in  support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry.
