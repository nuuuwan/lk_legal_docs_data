EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
My No: Thiprale/1/8/3/60 Ath. 
Ministry of Land’s Ref. No: 4-3/6/2016/HW/322. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
State intends to acquire the following land/servitude 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
Land Acquisition for Baseline Road Extension Project 
- Phase III 
 
One lot of Land having the following name and 
extents, about 0.0175 Hectares show as Lot Nos. 1 in the 
Advanced Tracing No. Co 10009 and dated 13.09.2022 
prepared by the surveyor Gener al, situated in Kirulapona 
Village (Division Pamankada East) in the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Thimbirigasyaya, Colombo 
District, Western Province, Description of the above Land 
as follows. 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Assmt. No. 10 (Part) Balapokuna 
Purana Vihara Road; Present Usage of the Land: The 
garden has part of a permanent building; Name and 
address of the Claiments: Dayananda Hewakuruppu, 
Kandedola tea factory, Pitabaddara; Extent: 0.0175 
Hectare. 
 
 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid Land/Lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby acquired to 
appear personally or by agent duly authorized in writing, 
befor me at Thimbirigasyaya Divisional Secretarial on the 
24th of April 2023 day of 10.00 o’clock and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 10th of April, 2023 the nature of their interest in 
the land the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land Servitude the amount 
of compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount.
