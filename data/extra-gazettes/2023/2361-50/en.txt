CHILAW URBAN COUNCIL
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed for the information of the public that it has been passed as per the resolution book No. 1382 dated 13.11.2023 
that out of roads and narrow roads considered to be belonging to the Chilaw Urban Council in the Schedule included in the 
Notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2341/53 dated 21.07.2023 roads and narrow roads for which no case 
has been ﬁ led in a competent court prior to the prescribed date of the s aid notiﬁ cation and all roads and narrow roads, for 
which a case had been instituted in a competent court prior to such date and which have been conﬁ rmed to be belonging to the 
Chilaw Urban Council in accordance with verdict given by such court, shall be deemed to be roads and narrow roads belonged 
to the Chilaw Urban Council by virtue of powers vested in the Urban Councils under the Section 46 of the Urban Ordinance in 
accordance with the orders of the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2317/87 dated 03rd February, 2023 in furtherance to the Order 4 
(i) of the series of Orders published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July, 2021 by the Hon. Governor of 
the Northwestern Province.
 It is further notiﬁ ed that name list regarding such roads and narrow roads has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road 
and narrow road included in the Schedule shall be deemed to be a road and narrow road belonged to the Chilaw Urban Council.
