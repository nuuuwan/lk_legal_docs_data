SPORTS  LAw No.  25  Of 1973
Regulations made under the Section 32(b) and  Section 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973
i, roshan ranasinghe, the Minister of Sports and youth affairs by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32(b) and 
33 of the Sport law No.25 of 1973, suspend the registration of the Sri lanka Karate-do Federation, which is the National 
association for the Sport of Karate-do, with immediate effect and making interim arrangements to appoint the following 
committee as a temporary operational procedure in order to further maintain the functions of the said association. 
 1. C. a. G. de Zoysa - President
 2. dr. Jayalath illangakoon - Secretary
 3. Mr. S. C. Medawatta - Member
 4. dr. Jayanthi a. Nanayakkara - Member
 5. Mahilini Maiyuran - Member
 6. K. M. C. Kumara - Member
 7. r. deva Kumara - Member
 8. a. K. Shiromi Fonseka - Member
 9. a. r. M. iqbal - Member
   Furthermore, i hereby grant authority to call a special general meeting of the said federation as per the Sports law  
No. 25 of 1973 and valid Sport regulations in order to appoint an election committee at the said meeting by the director 
General of  the Department of Sports Development, and to call and conduct the election of office bearers by the election 
committee under the supervision of the director General. 
 
Roshan Ranasinghe (M. P.),
 Minister of Sports and youth affairs.
at Colombo,
28th July, 2023.
