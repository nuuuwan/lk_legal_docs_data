It is hereby notified that the Gazette No. 2312/33 dated December 28, 2022 is hereby amended and since the political parties 
recognized for election purpose under Section 7 of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 01 of 1981 are considered as political 
parties recognized for the purpose of Local Authorities Elections in accordance with Section 27A of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 31 of 2017, the 
following Schedule shows the names of the recognized political parties that are considered as recognized political parties in 
the upcoming Local Autorities Elections.
