 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Gurunge 
Udaya Prasad Thilakarathna the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha Who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Adhikari Mudiyanselage Anura Adhikari to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe, Returning Officer of 
the Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance                  
(Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Adhikari Mudiyanselage Anura Adhikari was selected as the Member of the Rambukkana 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
