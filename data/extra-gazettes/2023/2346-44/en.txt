IN terms of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, It is hereby declared that following roads listed under the 
schedule given below belongs to Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha of district of Colombo under the Western Province Council.
 If any of the roads or Lanes marked under this schedule crosses over any private land and the owners of the such property  
wish to raise objection. He/She should file under Section 24:2 of No.15 of 1987 in a proper court of low, after end of three 
months and before the termination of six months from the date of publication of this notice in the 
Gazette  and established their 
ownership.
 It is hereby declared for the information of general public that these roads and lanes, listed in this schedule will be accepte d

stipulated period.
