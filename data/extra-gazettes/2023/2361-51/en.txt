PILGRImAGES   ORDINANCE
IT is hereby notified that  the scheduled date for the Annual Duruthu Maha Festival and Procession of Pilana Purana Sri 
Maha Temple in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 152 Pilana in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Akmeemana, 
District of Galle, Southern Province, published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2359/04 dated 20th November, 2023 as 
per the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance, is hereby amended and will be held from 26th December 2023 to 25th 
January 2024.
    
Shantha WeeraSinghe,
   District Secretary/Government Agent,
   Administrative District, Galle
   
At the District Secretariat, Galle,
04th december, 2023.
