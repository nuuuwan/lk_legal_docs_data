2a
Regulations
   the regulations made under section 61 of the Judicature act, No. 2 of 1978, read with section 60 of that act and 
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 43/4 of July 2, 1979, as amended from time to time, is hereby further amended in 
the schedule to that regulations as follows:-
 (1) in Column II corresponding to the entry relating to “the high court of the republic of sri Lanka held in the Judicial 
Zone of colombo” in Column I thereof –
 (a) by the repeal of item 1 and the substitution therefor of the following:-
 “1. all prosecutions, proceedings or matters in respect of offences under Parts ii, iii and iV of the anti-
Corruption Act, No. 9 of 2023, committed in any Judicial Zone in Sri Lanka which are specified to be heard 
by the high court, including all prosecutions, proceedings or matters in respect of offences under that  
Act, in respect of which no court is specifically nominated.”;
 (b) by the repeal of item 4 and the substitution therefor of the following:-
 “4.  all prosecutions, proceedings or matters in respect of offences under s ection 120 of the Penal code (chapter 
19) committed in any Judicial Zone in Sri Lanka.”; and
 (c) by the repeal of item 5; and
 (2) in Column II  corresponding to the entry relating to “the magistrate’s court of colombo” appearing in Column I 
thereof –
 (a) by the repeal of item 3 and the substitution therefor of the following:-
 “3.  all prosecutions, proceedings or matters in respect of offences under Parts ii, iii and iV of the anti-corruption 
Act, No. 9 of 2023, which are specified to be heard by the Magistrate’s Court.”; and
 (b) by the repeal of items 4, 5 and 6.
