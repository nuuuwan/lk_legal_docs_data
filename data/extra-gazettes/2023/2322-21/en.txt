SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order  under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 5 of the special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, ranil 
Wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies  of the democratic socialist republic 
of sri Lanka, do hereby make an Order to waive-off balance amount after collecting rs. 1 per Kg from the applicable special 
Commodity Levy of Rs. 200 per Kg on importation of Dates, classified under the HS Code of 0804.10.10 and 0804.10.20 
levied by Order, published the Gazette Notification No. 2308/17 dated November 30, 2022 to be received as donation or gift 
on no foreign exchange basis from any foreign governments, organizations including business entities, volunteer associations 
and well - wishers on the recommendation of the secretary, ministry of Buddhasasana, religious and cultural affairs.
   this Order shall be valid from march 08, 2023 to until further notice subject to provisions of sub section (3) of 
section 2 of the special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
Ranil WickRemesinghe,
minister of Finance, economic stabilization and 
National Policies.
