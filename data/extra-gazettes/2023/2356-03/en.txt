No. 2356/03 –  MONDAY, OCTOBER 30, 2023 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)   
 
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
Ref No.: KDS/DEL/4/4/10/12. 
Land Min. No:-  LD/05/2004/PC/106. 
 
I here by give notice, under Sub - section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amentment) Act, No 28 of 1964, that the 
government intends to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
 
 
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
 
An Allotment of land described below in extent 
0.0824 Hectare situated in the village of Galaha Deltota 
Division Kandy district and shown as lots 1 in plan No. 
ක.සි. 320706 prepared by the Survey General. 
 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Galahawaththa; Name of 
Claimant : Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.0824  
Hectare. 
North : L.R.C. Claimant by Galahawaththa; East: 
Road (RDA) and foot path; South : Foot path and Road 
(Pradeshiya Sabha); West : Road; 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional 
Secretary's office Deltota on the 18th day of December 
2023 at 10.30 am and are here by requested to notify to 
me in writing on or before the 30th day of November 
2023 the nature of their interests in the land the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the said land  the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  Please produce deeds or any other documents in 
support of your claim to the land. 
 
 
    D.R.K. H
ENNAYAKA, 
    Divisional Secretary, 
    Deltota. 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office 
Deltota.
