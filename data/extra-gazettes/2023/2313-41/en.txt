  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
The Inquiry Conducted under Section 184(1a) of Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 02 of 
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989 and under Section 2 of the Powers of 
Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province 
read with clause 154 c of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution 
I, roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of western Province, by virtue of powers vested in me as per provisions stated in 
section 184 (1a) of urban Councils Ordinance read with section 02 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act 
No. 12 of 1989 and under section 2 of the Powers of supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities statute No. 04 
of 1991 of the western Province read with clause 154 c of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution, do hereby appoint 
retired judge of Court of Appeal Hon. Chandradasa Nanayakkara as a single - person Tribunal to investigate whether the 
urban Council of Maharagama, in its functioning, and Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, while functioning in his 
office    has committed the offences stipulated under Section 2 of the powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local 
Authorities statue   No. 04 of 1991 of the western Province and offences stated under section 184 of the urban Councils 
Ordinance (Chapter 255) on account of the following mentioned matters, and present a report within 03 months.
Allegations/matters under investigation against the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council:-
 
 01. while functioning as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, whether he has improperly exercised the powers  
conferred on him under section 184 of urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 225) as the Chairman of Maharagama

  The above mentioned projects,
 I. development of the end-section of Talapathpitiya delgahawatta by laying carpet - rs. 1,900,000.00

 03. while functioning as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, whether he has improperly exercised the powers 
conferred on him as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council under the written law due to action taken by him to 
issue a statement to media on 23.11.2021 or date adjacent to it, regarding illegally accessing the main power supply 
line external to the electricity meter to obtain electricity to the private property located at No. 365, shanthi Mawatha, 
Makumbura, Pannipitiya, and in which statement, making a false remark implying, as the Chairman of Maharagama 
urban Council, powers have been granted to him under urban Councils Ordinance to acquire electricity without 
permission, and thereby misleading the general public and/or bringing the Maharagama urban Council into disrepute.
 04. while functioning as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council whether he has improperly exercised the powers 
conferred on him as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council under section 184 of the urban Councils Ordinance 
(Chapter 255) due to the action taken by him to consider the expenses under item No. 5.1.12 of future Council Agenda 
as expenses adopted by the Council, when the said expenses have been included in the Agenda to be adopted by the 
urban Council at its general meeting held on 24.09.2021.
 05. while functioning as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, whether he has improperly exercised the powers 
conferred on him as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council by acting contrary to the provisions of section 
40 of urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255), due to the  action taken by him to award to contract relating to 
construction of the Crematorium at Makumbura, estimated at Rupees Thirty-five Million Seven Hundred Thousand 
(rs. 35,700,000/-) or an amount equivalent to that, on advertising without calling for tenders.
 06. while functioning as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, whether he has improperly exercised the powers 
conferred on him as the Chairman of Maharagama urban Council by acting contrary to the provisions of section 39 of  
urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255), due to the action taken by him to enter, external to the Council approval, 
into a contract with Methsith Citizens' society and sumedha senior Citizens' society with regard to the contract to 
construct the Crematorium at Makumbura located within Maharagama urban Council Authority area.
