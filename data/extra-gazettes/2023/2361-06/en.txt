ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 1, dr. Suren raghavan, State 
Minister of Higher education, on the recommendation of the University Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the 
Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 9, 2006 (as last amended by Order published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2319/22 of February 13, 2023) which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the 
University of colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a 
University established under Section 21 of the said act, as follows;-
(1) by the substitution for the words “Faculty of Indigenous Medicine” appearing in column I of that Schedule of the 
words “Faculty of Indigenous Medicine”; and
(2) by the substitution for the item “6. department of Moolika Siddhanta and Shareera Vignana (department of basic 
principles, ayurveda anatomy and Physiology)” appearing in column II of that Schedule of the item “6. department 
of Maulika Siddhanta and Shareera Vignana (department of basic Principles, ayurveda anatomy and Physiology)”.
 dr. Suren ragha v an,
 State Minister of Higher education.
colombo.
