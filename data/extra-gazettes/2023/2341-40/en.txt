PART  IV (B) – LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Government Notiﬁ cations
PRADESHIYA  SABHA - IBBAGAMUWA.
IN addition to the Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2289/45 and dated 22 July 2022, it is hereby notiﬁ ed for public information that it has 
been adopted under resolution No. 368 moved at the General Conference held on 13.07.2023  at Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa 
that all the roads and thoroughfares as set out in the following schedule should be declared as the roads and thoroughfares owned 
by Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa  in terms of orders made by the series of “ Orders on publication of notiﬁ cations specifying 
preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province” 
published by Extraordinary  Gazette  No. 2238/35 and dated 30th  July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province 
and in terms of the order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 0f 1987.
 It is hereby noti ﬁ ed that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare included in the 
said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be ﬁ led in the competent Court to prove 
his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of three months from the date of this notiﬁ cation.
 It is further noti ﬁ ed that all the  roads and thoroughfares, against which a legal action had not been taken before the expiry 
of three months,  mentioned in that schedule, shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Ibbagamuwa.
 
 S.T.D. W
IMALASENA. 
        Secretary and Ofﬁ cer of executing   
 17th July, 2023. powers and duties,   
Head Ofﬁ ce of the Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa.                Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa.
                                                                                                                                            
  
Decision 
Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa  hereby decides, that  the roads and thoroughfares  set out in the following schedule which have 
been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2317/87 dated 03rd February 2023 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western 
Province should be declared as the roads and thoroughfares owned by Pradeshiya Sabha Ibbagamuwa  in terms of orders made 
by the series of “ Orders on publication of notiﬁ cations specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares 
maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province” published by Extraordinary  Gazette Paper No. 2238/35 
and dated 30th  July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order by Section 24(1) of 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
