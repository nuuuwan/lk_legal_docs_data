Rules
PaRT  1 :  PRelIMINaRy
1.	 These rules may be cited as the Rules of Census of Population and Housing 2024.
2.	 In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires, “Ordinance” means the Census Ordinance (Chapter 143); 
‘superintendent’ means the superintendent of Census, ‘Commissioner’ means the Commissioner of Census, 
‘deputy Commissioner’ means the deputy Commissioner of Census, ‘assistant Commissioner’ means the 
assistant Commissioner of Census (superintendent of Census and Commissioners of Census has been published 
by extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2264/8 dated 25th January 2022.) In addition to that, the officers 
involved in the Census duties are also meant as ‘Census Officer’.

PaRT II: GeNeRal
1. The listing stage of the Census of Population and Housing – 2024 shall be conducted as follows.
Process       Time Frame
     Numbering all buildings and Census units and paste the within 3rd and 4th quarters in 2023
     same numbered labels on every Census units and collect 
     information about those units and obtaining positional 
     coordinates of locations of buildings using a digital device.   
     
     
2. Every person should supply the information relevant for the listing stage to the Census officer appointed for 
the relevant area by as the superintendent or Commissioner 
3. Particulars entered in the listing schedule should be checked by supervisors who are appointed by the 
superintendent or the Commissioner.
PaRT III: duTIes OF CeNsus OFFICeRs
1. any power or duty delegated or assigned on the superintendent by the Ordinance or any other rule made 
thereunder, the general or special direction of the superintendent, may be exercised or performed by a deputy 
superintendent of Census throughout the Island or by a deputy / assistant superintendent of Census in the 
relevant area prescribed for the said deputy / assistant superintendent of Census. 
2. any power or duty delegated or assigned on a Commissioner by the Ordinance or any other rule made thereunder, 
may be exercised or performed by a deputy / assistant Commissioner within the area for which he/she is 
appointed, under the general or special direction of the Commissioner of that area.
3. Every Census Officer  should  attend for instruction classes or inspections on such dates and at such times and 
places as directed by  the superintendent or Commissioner or the deputy / assistant Commissioner of the area 
for which he/she is appointed as a Census Officer.
4. A Census Officer employed for the purpose of listing duties should not, except in connection with the particular 
service generally, divulge or communicate to any other person, without lawful authority, any information obtained 
by him for the purpose of completing a schedule.
5. A Census Officer employed for the purpose of listing duties should not disclose the information contained in 
the tablet computer relating to the listing duties, schedules or other documents to any person not authorized to 
do so until those are in his custody.
6. Every Census Officer employed for the purpose of taking any Census duties should make and sign the following 
oath or affirmation.
