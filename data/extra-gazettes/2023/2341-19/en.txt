MINISTRY  Of  PLANTATION  INDuSTRIES
National Institute of Plantation Management, Sri Lanka 
aNNouNcemeNt of ProfessioNal PlaNters iN sri laNka
the National institute of Plantation management (NiPm), ministry of Plantation industries, sri lanka, incorporated by 
the Parliament act of “National institute of Plantation management act No. 45 of 1979” as amended in 1981 by act                   
No. 76, in 1987 by act No. 05 and in 2003 by act No. 38 is the sole body empowered with the responsibility of enhancing 
the Professionalism of the Plantation industry in sri lanka. the Professional planters in sri lanka those who have met 
the necessary criteria, qualifications or contribution required for the membership is herewith listed for public notice. 
Honorary Members of National Institute of Plantation Management are entitled to use designatory letters as a qualification               
“FIPM-Sri Lanka”, Fellow Members are entitled to use designatory letters as a qualification “FIPM-Sri Lanka” and 
Associate Members are entitled use as a qualification “AIPM-Sri Lanka” after their names. As per the sub section 15F of 
section 05 of act No. 05 of 1987 “No person shall take or use the above title unless he has been enrolled as such by the 
institute” and as per the sub section of 15G of section 05 of act No. 5 of 1987 “every person who neglects or fails to comply 
with the provisions of 15f of this act shall be guilty of and offence an on conviction after trial before a magistrate be liable 
to imprisonment of either description for a period not exceeding six months or to a fine not exceeding one thousand five 
hundred rupees or to both such imprisonment and fine”
 dr. Prasad dharmasena,
 director/ceo,
 National institute of Plantation management,
 ministry of Plantation industries.
17th July, 2023.
