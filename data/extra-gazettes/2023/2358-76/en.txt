ORDER   mADE   uNDER   SECTION   52  Of  ThE  SRI  LANkA  ELECTRICITY   ACT,   
No.  20 Of  2009
It being my opinion, by virtue of the powers vested in me under and in terms of the Provisions of Section 52 of the Sri Lanka 
electricity act, No. 20 of 2009, and on the recommendations of the minister of the Subject of Power that;
(A) Allotments of land marked Lot Nos. 01 to 111 morefully described in Items 1 to 111 of Schedule No. 01 situated 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mannar in the District of Mannar, and  Items No. 01 to 65 of Schedule  
No. 02 in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pooneryn in the District of Kilinochchi, attached hereto, that have 
been declared as ‘Development Areas’ by the Minister of the Subject of Power in accordance with Section 12 of 
the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007, are urgently required for the implementation and 
operation of a Wind Power Project; and
(B)	The	said	Wind	Power	Project	is	a	public	purpose	and	for	the	welfare	and	benefits	of	the	public;
I, ranil Wickremasinghe, the President of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, hereby declare by this order 
that the Lands described herein are required for the purpose of the aforesaid provisions.
	 	 Ranil	Wickremasinghe,
  President.
on this 16
th day of  November,  2023,
Colombo.
