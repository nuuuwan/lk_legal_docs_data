The rules for sale of postage stamps and stamped/printed stationary, published in the Gazette Notification  No. 7,654 dated 
13th July 1928 and revised from time to time, are hereby further revised as follows :
 (1) omission of rule No. 1 of the above and substitution of the following instead ;
“1. The Postmaster General can issue a license to sell postage stamps, stamped/printed stationery to the 
value of rs. 110/- and less than that any individual and can cancel any such license so issued at any time.”
 (2) omission of the rule No. 4 of the above and substitution of the following instead ;
“4. a licensee is eligible to purchase postage stamps or stamped/printed stationery not exceeding the value 
of	Rupees	110/-	without	exceeding		a	total	amount	of	Rupees	10,000/-	at	one	time	from	a	post	office.	A	licenses	
should be granted a proportionate commission of 2%”
