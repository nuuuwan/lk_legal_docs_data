ThE  NATIONAL  ENvIRONmENTAL   ACT,  No. 47  Of  1980
Order under Section 23W(1)
By vIrtue of the powers vested in me by section  23W(1) of the National environmental act, No. 47 of 1980,  as 
amended by act, No. 56 of 1988, I, Naseer ahamed, Minister of environment, do by this Order, prohibit the use of the 
following plastic items and the use of the said items in any process, trade or industry for in country use, with effect from 
October 01, 2023.
 (i) single-use drinking-straws and stirrers.
 (ii) single-use food containers, plates, cups, spoons, forks and knives, except in air travels.
 (iii) Garlands.
 (iv) string hopper trays.
For the purposes of this Order-
  “cups” includes drinking cups but does not include yoghurt cups;  
  “food containers” means the meal boxes manufactured from expanded polystyrene for in country use;
