WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna  the recognized political party to which Thennakoon 
Mudiyanselage Anura Sudarshana the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall 
be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Thelikada Palliya Guruge Anusha Nilmini Senevirathna  to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore I, Ratnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayaka, Returning Officer of  Wariyapola 
Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended 
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Thelikada Palliya Guruge Anusha Nilmini 
Senevirathna elected as Vice Chairman of the  Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha.    
 R. M. H. B. R
