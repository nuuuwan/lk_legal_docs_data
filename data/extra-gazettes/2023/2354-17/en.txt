I,  dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid 
act, in respect of the benedict XVI catholic International Institute of Higher education, a company incorporated in sri 
Lanka under the companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number PV 115017 and located at No. 495, 
Minuwangoda road, bolawalana, Negombo, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the 
schedule II to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2343/03, of July 31, 2023, by 
the addition immediately after entry “bachelor of education Honours in early childhood education” appearing in column I 
and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule 
II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto:-
scHedULe I
Conditions
 1. benedict XVI catholic International Institute of Higher education shall, when admitting students for the degree
