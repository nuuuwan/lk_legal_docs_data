by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section  27(1)(b) read with section 25a of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978,

Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number Pv  2268 and located at No. 591, Gall road, Colombo 
06 and being recognized as a degree awarding institute, do by this order, amend the Schedule ii to the order made under  
the said act and  published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2208/49 of January 01.2021, by the addition immediately after 
the entry “bachelor of  business management (Honours)”appearing in Column i and its corresponding entry in column 
II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto  subject to the 
conditions  specified  in Schedule I hereto. 
      SCHedULe  i
Conditions
1. business management School  (Private) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme 
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Business Management, complete with a minimum of 
90 credits (SLQF Level 5 ), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of at least three (3) Simple Passes (S) in 
any subject stream in one and same sitting at G. C. e. advanced Level examination conducted by the department 
of examination of Sri Lanka or equivalent.
