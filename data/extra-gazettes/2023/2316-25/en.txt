ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 27(1) (b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) (b) read with section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 
1978, I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the 
aforesaid act, in respect of the Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist studies (Pvt.) Limited (NIIBs), a company 
incorporated in sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 94125 and 
located at Manelwatta, Bollegala, Kelaniya, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend 
the schedule II to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2308/29 of december 
1, 2022, by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of arts Honours in applied Communication and Media 
Technology” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under 
Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
sCHeduLe I
Conditions
 1. Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist studies (Pvt.) Limited (NIIBs) shall, when admitting students for the 
degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Arts Honours in Applied Archaeology,  
complete with a minimum of 120 credits (SLQF Level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (3) ‘simple 
pass’ (s) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCe (advanced Level) examination conducted by the 
department of examinations of sri Lanka or equivalent.
