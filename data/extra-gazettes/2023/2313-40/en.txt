PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF   a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF   member  oF   the karachchi pradeshiya 
sabha under  section  66 a(1)  oF   the  local  authorities  elections  
ordinance  (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending council 
meetings for a period of three months without permissions ;
And whereas the Leader of Independent Group to which Sivakumar Merisdela Romitha the member belonged, was 
required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Karachchi Pradeshiya 
Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Leader of Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Kalyani Thayaparan 
to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Kandiah Vimalaruban, Returning Officer of the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kalyani Thayaparan was selected 
as the member of the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K. Vimalaruban,
 Returning Officer,
 Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Kilinochchi,
03rd January, 2023.
