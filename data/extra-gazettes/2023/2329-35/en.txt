rules  (revisioN  of  the  registrAtioN  fee  for  BusiNess  plAces)
i, wiliyam wijesinghe gamage, the governor of the southern provincial council hereby revise the fee amounts for the  
registration of Business places depicting in the section 04 of the Gazette Notification No. 2143/58 dated 04.10.219 relevant 
to the rules laid down by the statute on the registration of Business places No. 04 of 2019 under the southern provincial 
council, as per the powers vested upon me by the section 154(c) of the constitution of the democratic socialist republic 
of sri lanka since the said provincial council has already been dissolved and on behalf of the powers that had been vested 
upon subject Minister of Agriculture, Agrarian development, irrigation, water supplying and drainage, food supplying 
and distribution, trade and co-operative development in accordance with the scetion 19 of the statute on the registration 
of Business places No. 04 of 2019 under the southern provincial council.
   04. Fees should be charged in terms of the Section 18(1) of the Statute on the Registration of Business Places                 
No. 04 of 2019 under the Southern Provincial Council for the issuance of a certificate or statement in respect of the each of 
the following activities as per the amounts depicting in compliance with each.
   in this, the higher value out of the capital or turnover amount being presented at the registration of Business place 
should be taken in to consideration.

4.5 charging late payment fee in the registration of a Business place
An application should be made by the specimen prescribed in section 02 in order to register the Business place registration 
within 30 days after the initiation of Business place. in case of exceeding the said 30 days, the late payment fees should be 
charged as follows.
For 31 days - 90 days, Prescribed Fee for the Registration of the Business and a penalty fee of Rs. 2875/= should be charged 
in addition as a late payment.
No Business place which has exceeded 91 days without registration after the initiation of such a Business is entitled to be
