Local  Government  Notifications
PRADEShIYA  SABhA  - ARAchchIkATTuwA
It is hereby notified for public information that I have decided under the Decision No. 595 of Decision Book dated 
01.11.2023 that the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions have not been taken in a proper court before the date 
specified in the said notification, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been confirmed to be deemed to be owned by 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Arachchikattuwa in accordance with the decree announced by that Court, from among the roads and 
thoroughfares considered to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Arachchikattuwa in the Schedule set out in the Notification 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2342/10 dated 25.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the 
series of “Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas 
in the North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North 
Western Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021, and the order 
made under Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been inlcuded in the Schedule hereto and 
every road and thoroughfares included therein shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Arachchikattuwa under Sub-section (5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
 
B. A. A. M. ShirAni ABeySinghe,
 Secretary and Officer who executes powers,
 Duties and Functions, 
 Pradeshiya Sabha - Arachchikattuwa.
 ,
Head Office of Pradeshiya Sabha Arachchikattuwa,
16th of November, 2023.

Decision
By virtue of powers vested in me under Section 9/3 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, 1, B. A. A. M. Shirani Abeysinghe, Secretary to the Pradeshiya Sabha Arachchikattuwa 
hereby decide under Decision No. 595 of Book dated 01.11.2023, that the roads and thoroughfares among the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the list set out 
in the Schedule of the Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2342/10 dated 25.07.2023, for which a case has not been filed in the proper court 
and are included in the Schedule below should be considered as the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Arachchikattuwa, by virtue of powers 
vested under the Orders in the series of “Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained  by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the 
North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has been published 
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July, 2021, and order made under Section 24(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
