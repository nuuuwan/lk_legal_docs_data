SPECIAL   COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT,  No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist republic 
of sri lanka, do by this Order, rescind the validity period of the special Commodity levy, imposed on importation of Maize 
(hs Code of 1005.90.00) under section 2  and waiver of the special Commodity levy, imposed on importation of Maize  
(hs Code of 1005.90.00) for the purpose of production of “thriposha” by M/s, sri lanka thriposha ltd, by the Order 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2332/14 of May 17, 2023 under the special Commodity levy act, No. 48 of 
2007, effective from July 14, 2023, without any change to the remainders in that Order.
