 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Horana Urban Council has fallen vacant by reson of ceased to be a member of 
the party;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Hettige Prasangika 
Damayanthi Perera the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Horana Urban Council Who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the 
local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of              
thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Muthu Krishnan Wijayalatha to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Horana Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Muthu Krishnan Wijayalatha was selected as the member of the Horana Urban Council.
