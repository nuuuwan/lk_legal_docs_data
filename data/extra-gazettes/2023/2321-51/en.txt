ThE   UNIvERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27 (1) read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the university Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 21 of the said act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15 of October 09, 2007 (as 
amended from time to time) which specifies the Faculties and Departments of Study of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, 
which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a university established under section 
21 of the said act, by the addition immediately  after the entry “3. department of Medical Laboratory science” appearing 
in Column II of the said Schedule (specified in the said Gazette Extraordinary No. 2139/12 of september 2, 2019) which 
corresponds to the item “Faculty of Allied Health Sciences” appearing in Column I thereof, of the following new entry:-
 “4. department of human Biological sciences”
 Dr. 
 A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 Minister of Education.
