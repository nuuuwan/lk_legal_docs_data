Local Government Notifications
  WennappuWa  pradeshiya  sabha 
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that it has been decided under the recommendation of the management 
meeting held on 14.07.2023 and resolution No. 338 dated 27.07.2023 that roads in the following schedule herein should be 
declared as abandoned roads and narrow roads in furtherance to the Gazette Notification No. 2308/38 dated 02.12.2022 as 
per the function assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha of the Northwestern Province from the series of "Orders on publication of 
notices stating the planning and listing of roads and narrow roads maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha in the Northwestern 
Province" declared by the Governor of the Northwestern Province in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated 
30.07.2021 to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
   If the party claiming the said land is of the opinion that any road or narrow road included in the said Schedule passes 
through any land or belongs to any part of any land, it is hereby notified that he/she shall file a case in a competent court prior 
to the lapse of three months from the date of this notification in order to prove his/her right to the said land. 
   It is further notified that every road and narrow road included in the said Schedule for which no such case has been 
instituted prior to the lapse of three months, shall be deemed to be a road or narrow path belonging to the Wennappuwa 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
                    W. D. L
ushion, 
 Secretary,
 Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
At the Office of Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, 
27th July, 2023.
