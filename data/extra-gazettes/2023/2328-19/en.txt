MEDIATION  (SPECIAL  CATEGORIES  Of  DISPuTES)   ACT,  No. 21 Of 2003
Order under Section 8
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8  of the Mediation (Special Categories) act, No. 21 of 2003, i, Wijeyadasa 
rajapakshe, Minister of Justice, prison affairs and Constitutional reforms, do by this Order –
 (1) amend the Order made under Section 8 of the Mediation (Special Categories of disputes) act, No. 21 of 2003 in 
paragraph 3 and published in Gazette extraordinary No. 2259/11 of december 21, 2021, by the substitution for the 
words and figures “shall be less than Rupees five hundred thousand (Rs. 500,000/-)” of the words and figures “shall 
be Rupees One million (Rs. 1,000,000/-) or less than Rupees One million (Rs. 1,000,000/-)”; and
 (1) declare that this Order shall not prejudice the mediations which have already commenced under the aforementioned 
Gazette and anything done thereunder.                        
  dr.
 Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe,
 Minister of Justice, prison affairs and 
 Constitutional reforms.
