CuSTOmS  NOTIfICATION
 The Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
Order uNder sectiON 10a
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 10a of the customs Ordinance (chapter 235) as amended by act             
No. 83 of 1988, i, ranil wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the 
democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do by this Order to amend the Order, issued under Extraordinary Gazette     
No. 2308/16 dated 30.11.2022, to impose a surcharge on importation of goods specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto 
at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the said schedule on the Customs Import Duty, both General 
and Preferential basis, effective from january 05, 2023 for a period of 05 months.
 R
anil WickRemesinghe,

 stabilization and National Policies.

and National Policies.
