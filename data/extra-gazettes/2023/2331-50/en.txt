ThE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE  
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), i, dr. ramesh Pathirana, 
Minister of Ministry of Industries do by this notification ;
 01. authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “Employee Share Holders association of UL” situated       
No. 275, Hapugoda, Kandana as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be         
extended; and
 02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of Section 5(4) of 
that Ordinance.
