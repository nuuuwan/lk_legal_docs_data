EXCISE ORDINANCE
Excise Notiﬁcation No. 01/2023
AMENDMENT  OF  REGULATIONS  ON  NON  POTABLE  SPIRITS  IMPORTED  IN  TO  SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Sections 2, 12, 22 and 25, read with the Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance 
(Chapter 52), I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies hereby amend the 
Excise Notiﬁ cation No. 07/2018 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2059/32 dated February 21, 2018 as amended 
by the Rules in Excise Notiﬁ cation No. 10/2018 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2075/23 dated June 13, 2018, 
with effective from June 17, 2023 as per Rules set out hereto that is applicable only for 2. Propyl Alcohol/ Isopropyl Alcohol 
in Schedule I.
