in terms of Subsection 38(3) of the Local Authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) as amended to be read with 
Subsection 38(1)(c) of that ordinance, it is hereby notified that-
the elections to the Local Authorities mentioned in the Schedule below will not be held on 09.03.2023 as stipulated 
in the Gazette Extraordinary no. 2317/11 dated 30.01.2023 published by me under Section 38(1)(c) of the Local Authorities 
elections ordinance (chapter 262) as amended;
upon consultations, the election commission advised me that the date of the elections has to be postponed due to 
unexpected and unavoidable reasons beyond the control of the election commission; and 
the 25th of April 2023 shall be the new date on which the elections to the said Local Authorities will be taken.
