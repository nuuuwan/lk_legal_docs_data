EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of 
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, the 
Government intends to acquire the following land s/
servitudes which are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired : 
 
The portion of Land, in extent with 0.0197 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 235 and 236 (Two Lots) in sheet No. 
11 dated 31.08.2022 and Zone No. 01 of the Cadastral 
Map. No. 420214 prepared by the Surveyor General, 
which situated in Village called Thorayaya belongs to 
Grama Niladhari Division No. 725 Narampola, Minor 
Division of Mahagalboda Megoda Korale, Mallawapitiya 
Divisional Secretariat Division in Kurunegala District in 
the North Western Province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 235 
Description of land: Deduru Oya; The Prossessor: The 
State; Original Claimants (Possessors): Portion of the land 
depicted as Lot No. 01 was acquired under Section 38(A) 
of the Land Acquisition Act, Original Claimants 
(Possessor): Kumarapeli Arachchige Stephan, It is 
required to protect the water source of Deduru Oya water 
supply project; Extent : 0.0160 Hectare.
