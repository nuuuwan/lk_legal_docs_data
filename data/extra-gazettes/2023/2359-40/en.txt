STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. S. M. Fawmi is entitled for an acting allowance from 10.10.2016 to 20.02.2020, the period during which

it and if so, to what relief  he is entitled.
