No. 2326/15 –  MONDAY, APRIL 03,  2023 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under  Section 07 
 
 
My Reference No.  : Bu/Ln/1/1/15. 
 Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/2/2017/VE/353. 
 
I, hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No . 28 of 1964, that               
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired : 
 
Boundaries are as follow for the land Called, 
Mirishenawtta in extent of 0. 8094 hectares mentioned in 
Cadastrial Map 530431 block No. 01, lot of number 01 
prepared by survey General for Athura Village of 
Bulathsinghala Divisional Secretariat Division in 
Kaluthara District in Western Province. 
 
Lot No.: 01– Mirishenawatte - Extent 0.8094 hectre 
 
North :  Athura (Part) 
East : Athura (Part) and Road) Pradesheeya Sabha) 
South : Road (Preadesheeya Sabha) and Athura (part) 
West : Athura (Part) 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a  servitude is to be acquired to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at  
Divisional Secretariat, Bula thsinghala April 26th on the 
the day 2023 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
26th April 2023, the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.

 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Bulathsinghala.
