SOUThERN  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Statute of the Registration of Trading Places No. 04 of 2019 in the Southern Provincial Council
rules (AmeNdmeNt of the specimeN form)
i, Wiliyam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of the southern provincial council hereby do amend the followings in terms 
of the section 19 of the statute of the registration of trading places No. 04 of 2019 in the southern provincial council as 
per the provisions laid down by the rule No. 2.2 and 2.4 of thereof, by removing the words “if the owner is known by any 
other Name such Name” and inserting the words National identity card Numbers in the section 07 of the specimen form 
of Business Name registration (s.p.) r 02 and by removing the words “if the owners are known by any other Name, such 
Name” and inserting the words “National identity card Numbers” in the specimen form of corporative Business Name 
registration (sp) No. r 04 as i am vested with the power in terms of the section 154(B) of the constitution of the democratic 
socialist republic of sri lanka since the southern provincial council is dissolved and thereby the delegation of powers that 
had been assigned to the minister of Agriculture, Agrarian development, irrigation, Water supplying & drainage, food and 
supplying & distributions and trade of the southern provincial council.
dr. W. W. GamaGe,
 the Governor,
southern provincial council.
At Galle,
19th december, 2022.
