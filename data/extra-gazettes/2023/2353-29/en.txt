THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (01) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act:
 An allotments of land morefully depicted in following 
names and extent in the Advance Tracing No. MN/
MNN/2022/311 dated 28.02.2023 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Village called Thoddaweli in the 
Grama Niladhari Division No. MN/62 of Thoddaweli in the 
Divisional Secretary's Division  of Mannar Town of Mannar 
District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot 'A' – Kurusadikani – Extent:  0.9117 Hectare
 North: Kurusadikani (Claimant not known); East: Lots B 
and C; South: Lot C and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: 
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Kurusadikani (Claimant not 
known).
 02. Lot 'B' – Kurusadikani – Extent:  0.8787 Hectare
 North: Lot A and Kurusadikani (claimant not known); East: 
Kurusadikani (Claimant not known); South: Kurusadikani 
(Claimant not known), Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot C; 
West: Lot C and A.
 03. Lot 'C' – Kurusadikani – Extent:  0.7729 Hectare
