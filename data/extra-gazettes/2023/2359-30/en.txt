PRADESHIYA  SABHA   - KARUWALAGASWEWA 
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed for public information  that I have decided under the   Decision No. 381 of Decision Book dated  01.11.2023 
that the roads and thoroughfares for  which legal actions have not been taken  in a proper court before the date speciﬁ ed in the 
said notiﬁ cation, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been conﬁ rmed to be deemed to be owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Karuwalagaswewa in accordance with the decree  announced by that Court, from among the roads and thoroughfares 
considered to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Karuwalagaswewa in the Schedule set out  in the Notiﬁ cation published in 
the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2342/14 dated 25.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders 
with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares  maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in  the North Western 
Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" by Hon. Governor in the  North Western Province which 
has been  published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th  July 2021, and the  order made under Section 24 (1) 
of  Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
 It is further notiﬁ  ed that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule here to and every road and 
thoroughfares included there in shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Karuwalagaswewa 
under sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.

Decision
By virtue of powers vested in me under Section 9/3 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, K. B. Jagath Kumara,   Secretary of the   Pradeshiya 
Sabha Karuwalagaswewa  hereby decide under  Decision No. 166 of Book  dated  14.07.2023, that  the roads and thoroughfares 
among the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the list set out in the Schedule of the Notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2342/14 dated 25.07.2023, for which a case has not been ﬁ led in the proper court and are included in the schedule 
below should be considered as  the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Karuwalagaswewa, by virtue of 
powers vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares 
maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" 
by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 
30th July 2021, and order made under Section 24 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
