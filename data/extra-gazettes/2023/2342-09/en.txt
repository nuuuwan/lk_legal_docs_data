SPORTS LAw No. 25 Of 1973
Regulations made under the Section 32, Section 33 and Section 41 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973
I, the minister of Sports and youth affairs, Roshan Ranasinghe, hereby appoint the following committee to carry out the 
administrative and other required activities of the Football Federation of Sri lanka and to convene and conduct the relevant 
elections, in accordance with the powers vested in me under the Sections 32, 33 and 41 of the Sports act, No. 25 of 1973 
and as its amendments, with effect from 24th july, 2023.
 (1) mr. a. G. C. deshapriya  - Chairman
 (2) mrs. N. K. Kapurubandara - Secretary
 (3) mr. K. a. R. C. Kahandawala - Treasurer
Further, in terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32, 33, and 41 of the Sports act No. 25 of 1973 and as its 
amendments, I hereby direct that the following will be applicable to the Football Federation of Sri lanka under the National 
associations of Sports Regulations, No. 2 of 1974 and its amendments.
	 1.	 The	Officer	bearers		will	be	elected	for	a	four-year	term.
	 2.	 The	Post	of	General		Secretary/CEO	will	be	filled	by	appointment.
 3. a  new executive committee will be elected in terms of the Football Federation of Sri lanka 
  Constitution adopted on 22nd September, 2022                   
 
Roshan Ranasinghe (M.P.) 
 minister of Sports and youth affairs.
Colombo 
24th july, 2023.
