Revocation under Section 39 (1)
BY the Order made under Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the 
Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) and published in the 
Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1117/21 dated 03.02.2000 the 
allotment of land specified morefully in the Order No.07 of

Republic of Sri Lanka.  But the possession of the allotment of

Section 39(1) of the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke 
the aforesaid Order No.07 of 2000 dated 12.01.2000 published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1117/21 dated 03.02.2000 
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
