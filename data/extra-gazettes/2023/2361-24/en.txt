I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the land intended to be acquired : 
 
The following allotment of land marked Lot No. 01 
depicted in the Sheet No. 01 of Zone No. 01 of the 
Cadastral Map No. 320434 dated 27.06.2023 prepared by 
Senior Superintend of Surveys of Kandy on behalf of the 
Surveyor General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division 
(261) Deiyannewela (division No. 4, Bahirawakanda within 
the Kandy Municipal Council Limit) in the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Kandy Four Gravets and 
Gangawata Korale in the District of Kandy, Central 
Province and containing in extent 0.0058 hectare. :

amount should be notified to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 16.01.2024.
