WHEREAS the office of return member of Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member 
of the party ;
And whereas the secretary of the, United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which 
Somawathi Weerasingha the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 
9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Egodagedara Poorna Nilanthi Darmadasa to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Hettighe Asiri Eranga Pushpakumara, Returning Officer of the Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Egodagedara 
Poorna Nilanthi Darmadasa was selected as the member of the Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha.
