District of the North Central Province, it is hereby announced that the road described in the below schedule declared as owned

publication in the Gazette.
 If there is no objection were raised to this period. I do hereby announced to the public that the roads mentioned in the schedule 
will be owned and maintain by the Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha.
