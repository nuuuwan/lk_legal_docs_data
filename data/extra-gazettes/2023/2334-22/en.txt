Regulations
Previously amended regulations No. 01 of 2016 of the National association of sports published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th october 2016 are hereby amended as follows only in relevance to the year 2023.
	 01.	 “Thirty	first	day	of	May”	in	the	Section	(a)	of	the	schedule	III	under	the	provisions	related	to	the	constitution	of	
the National associations/Federations of sports published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th 
October	2016	is	repealed	and	the	“Thirty	first	day	of	October”	is	inserted.
 02. the section (a) under the provisions related to the constitution of the National associations/Federations of 
sports in the schedule iii of the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th october 2016 come into force 
again with effect from the 1st day of January 2024.
