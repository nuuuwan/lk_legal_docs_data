SPORTS  LAw  No.  25  Of  1973
Order under Section 32 and 33 of the Sports Law
I, roshan ranasinghe, being the minister of sports and youth affairs, by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 32 
of the sports Law No. 25 of 1973, I hereby suspend the registration of sri Lanka Cricket, which is the National association 
of sport of Cricket with effect from 05.11.2023 in view of the corruption, malpractices, irregularities, misconducts and for 
failing to carry out its duties and functions as discovered by the auditor General report issued on the inquiry of T 20 (men) 
World Cup matches in 2022, and also as it is necessary to conduct an independent, transparent and trustworthy inquiry into 
such conduct and to take remedial actions.
I, roshan ranasinghe, being the minister of sports and youth affairs, by virtue of the powers vested in me 
under section 33 of the sri Lanka sports Law, No. 25 of 1973, hereby appoint an Interim Committee consisting of the 
following members for the purpose of exercising the powers, functions and duties of sri Lanka Cricket which is the National 
association of sport of Cricket until an election is held or until further notice is issued and the interim board among other 
things is empowered for the effective functioning of sri Lanka Cricket and,
 1. To inquire into and to take appropriate action in relation to the recommendations contained in the audit report 
dated in 11th september, 2023 issued by the auditor General,
 2. To exercise the powers, functions and duties of sri Lanka Cricket in an appropriate manner until remedial actions 
are taken by the Interim Committee by conducting the relevant inquiries independently, transparently and trustfully,
