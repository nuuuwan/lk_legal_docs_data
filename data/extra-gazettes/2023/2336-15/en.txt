I, hereby give notice , under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government has acquired the lands located in between 
Dehiwala Maharagama (119) Bus Route to Maharagama 
Moratuwa Bus Route  (192) are required to create a water 
reserve to temporally collect storm water in Attidiya, 
Bellantara paddy field under Weras Ganga Storm Water 
Drainage and Environment Improvement Project.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired :  
 
An allotment of land, depicted as Lot No.1 in the 
Preliminary Plan No. 9920 and dated 21.12.2020 prepared 
by the Surveyor General situated in the village called 
Bellanthara in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 535A of 
Bellanwila in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kesbewa in Colombo District, Western Province and 
bounded as follows : 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Bellanwila Attidiya Sanctuary; 
Description: Reserve. A permanent building, parts of two 
permanent buildings, and parts of two temporary 
buildings are available. A section of the canal is available; 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
