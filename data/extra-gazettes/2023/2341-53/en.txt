Local Government Notifications
Chilaw  Urban  CoUnCil 
BY virtue of power vested in urban council by section 46 of the urban cuncil, and the Gazette announcement dated 23.12.2022 
No. 2311/49, it is hereby announced for the public's knowledge that is has been resolved under Decision Book No. 685, and 
13.07.2023 that road and lanes maintained by Chilaw Urban Council that are stated in the Sub-section given below should 
be declared as road and lanes paths owned by Chilaw Urban Council.
   When opinion of the party claimed to the ditto land is that roads in the schedule below included through any land 
or any portion of the land, it is do hereby notified that an  action should be taken to be filed a case in the court prior to 
completion of three months from the date of notice to clarify rights to the ditto land.
   Further it is notified that every road not filed a case in the schedule below will be considered as roads entitled to the 
chilaw urban council. 
 M. A. S. S. Maarasinghe,
 Secretary,
 Urban Council, Chilaw.
At Chilaw Urban Council Office.
