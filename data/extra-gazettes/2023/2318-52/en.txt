I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of  section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act. No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government has acquired the allotment of marshy land 
located between Piliyandala-Moratuwa bus route and 
Temple Road for canal development, creating a water 
tank, townscape development and other developments 
under Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage and 
Environment Improvement Project.
