ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dr. a. d. Susil 
premajayantha,  minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants commission, do by this order, 
amend the Schedule to the order published  in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1454/37 of July 22, 2006 (as amended from time 
to time),which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue 
of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said act, by the 
addition immediately after entry “10. department of English language Teaching” appearing in column ii of the Schedule, 
which corresponds to the item “ Faculty of Humanities” appearing in Column 1 of the Schedule  (specified in the Order 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2005/13 of February 08,2017) of the following new entry:-
“11. department of drama, cinema and Television”
 
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 minister of Education.
