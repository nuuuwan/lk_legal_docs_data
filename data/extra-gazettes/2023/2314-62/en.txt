Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS the office of return member of Moratuwa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three 
consecutive meetings without permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Ranhaluge Krishani 
Fernando the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned  member of Moratuwa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hemba Geekiyanage Anuruddhika Nilmini Silva  to fill such vacancy; 
Therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, returning Officer of the Moratuwa Municipal Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hemba 
Geekiyanage Anuruddhika Nilmini Silva was selected as the member of the Moratuwa Municipal Council.
