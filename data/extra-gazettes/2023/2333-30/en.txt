The Land Acquisition for Conservation of  
Mahaweli River  
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the land acquisition act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
  Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:   
 
 
The allotments of land called Mahawila Watta in total 
extent of about 2.5000 Hectares in Lot No. 2 depicted & 
prepared by Surveyor General in Cadastral Map No. 
320040(01) 2 situated in Grama Niladhari Division of 
Kalugalhinna No. 1189 in Divisional Secretary Division 
of Udapalatha in Kandy District, Central Province. 
 
The dates & the time of the inquiries of Compensation 
 
Order No.: 1; Cadastral Plan No.: 320040(01)2; Lot 
No. 02; Extent: 2.5000 Hectares; Date of inquiries of 
compensation: 19.07.2023; Time: 10.00 a.m; Place: 
Divisional Secretariat, Udapalatha, Gampola. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secr etariat of Udapalatha on the 
19th day of July 2023 at 10.00 o’ clock, and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplilcate) on or 
before the 05th day of July 2023, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
