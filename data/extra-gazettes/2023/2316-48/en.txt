Proclamations & C., by the President 
PROCLAmATION  bY  ThE  hON.  PRESIDENT
KNOW yE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the democratic Socialist republic 
of Sri Lanka, i, ranil Wickremesinghe, President, do by this Proclamation, prorogue Parliament with effect from midnight 
of the Twenty Seventh day of January, Two Thousand and Twenty Three and hereby fix the Eighth day of February, Two 
Thousand and Twenty Three, at 10.00 a.m. for the commencement of the next session and summon Parliament to meet in the 
Chamber of Parliament, Sri Jayewardenepura.
 Given at Colombo, on this 27 day of January, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
        
      By Command of the Hon. President,
              
 e. m. s. B. ekanayake,  
 Secretary to the President.
