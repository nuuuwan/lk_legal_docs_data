CONSTITuTION Of ThE DEmOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPubLIC Of SRI LANkA
Order under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution
IT was notified in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2352/04 dated 02.10.2023 that the supplementary electoral register  
2023 (2) for the period starting from the first day of June 2023 to the thirtieth day of September 2023 was certified on  
second day of October 2023 in terms of the provisions of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the 
Registration of Electors (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2021.
   02. WHEREAS 2022 main electoral register, 2022 (1) supplementary electoral register, 2022 (2) supplementary 
electoral register, 2022 (3) supplementary electoral register, 2023 (1) supplementary electoral register and 2023 (2) 
supplementary electoral register remained in effect as on October 02, 2023, in pursuance of the powers vested in the Election 
Commission under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election 
Commission by this Order certify that the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members 
which each electoral district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by 
virtue of the Proclamation under Article 97 and Article 98.
