THE SOCIETIES ORDINANCE
By virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 3 & 4 of the societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, dr. ramesh Pathirana 
Minister of Ministry of Industries do by this notification;
01. authorize the purpose for which the society known as “Ceylon safety Match Manufactures association” 
situated C/o, sun Match Company (Pvt) Ltd, Bamunupolawatte, Kundasale as a purpose to which the powers 
and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended; and 
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exemption it from the provision of section        
5 (4) of that Ordinance.
 Dr . Ramesh PathiRana,
 Minister of Industries.
