 LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No. GM/DS/2016/2/2/1/141. 
Land Min. Ref. No.: 4-3/8/2016/HA/404. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 
that the Government intends to acquire the following 
land/servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired:- 
 
An allotment of land in extent of 0.0152 Hectares of the 
land called Kebellagaha Kumbure Kotasa and Delgaha 
Kumbure Kotasa depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. 
4169 dated 05.07.2019, prepared by the Superintendent of 
Survey-Gampaha District for the Land called 
Bogahalanda, situated in the village called Bollathe in the 
Grama Niladhari Division No. 209 A of Kudabollathe in 
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Gampaha in 
Gampaha District of the We stern Province and described 
as follows. 
 
Lot No. 1 
Name of the land: Kebellagaha Kumbure Kotasa and 
Delgaha Kumbure Kotasa ; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimants: K. D. Kalana Ch athuranga Kahatapitiya (For 
completion of acquisition activities) Extent: 0.0152 
Hectare. 
North: Balance Portion of this land; East: Balance 
Portion of this land and Delgaha Kumbure Kotasa Land 
claimed by K. D. L. B. Laxman; South: Delgaha 
Kumbure Kotasa Land claimed by K. D. L. B. Laxman 
and Road; West: Road and Balance portion of this land.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Gampaha Divisional 
Secretariat, at 10.00 a.m. on the days herein under given, 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 27th October, 2023 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
