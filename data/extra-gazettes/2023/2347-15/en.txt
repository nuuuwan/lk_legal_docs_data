Appointing under Section 32(1) of the Western Provincial Council Act,  No. 42 of 1987
I, roshan goonetileke, the governor of the Western Province, according to the powers vested upon me under the section 
32(1) of the Western Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, hereby notify that with effect from 24.08.2023, Mr. Massenna 
Ampitiyawaththe Priyawansa Jayakodi of the sri Lanka Administrative service Class I, has been appointed for the post of 
Commissioner of the Co-operative development and registrar of the Co-operative societies Western Province.
