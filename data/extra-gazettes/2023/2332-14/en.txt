SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT, No. 48  Of  2007
Order under Sections 2 and 5
               
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ranil 
wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist Republic 
of sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special 
Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule; and
 
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 5 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, do 
hereby make an order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 25 per kg from the applicable special Commodity 
Levy of Rs. 75 per kg on importation of maize, listed out in the schedule below, for production of “Thriposha” by sri Lanka 
Thriposha Limited. 
This order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from may 18, 2023 subject to provisions of                 
sub section (3) of section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
The order made under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No.48 of 2007 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2284/03 of June 15, 2022, and the order made under section 5 of the special 
Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the  Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2322/21 of march 07, 
2023 for duty waiver on importation of dates (Hs Code 0804.10.10 and 0804.10.20) are hereby rescinded with effect from 
may 18, 2023.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 minister of Finance, economic stabilization 
 and National Policies.
