 THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  AS

Notice under Section 7
                                           My Ref. No. : W a/DS/1/3/4/2018/2.
Tourism and Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2018/HW/07.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
  Allotments of  02 Lots  in  Total Extent of about 0.0091 Hectare,
depicted in Cadastral Plan No. 512022, Block No. 04, Sheet No. 1
and dated 22.06.2023 prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of
Survey of  Gampaha District on behalf of the Surveyor General,
situated in the Village of  Kurukulawa in the Grama Niladhari Division
of  No. 250A, Kurukulawa South in the  Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Wattala in Gampaha District in Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden, Contains a
part of Permanent Building; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
Seemanneru Pathiranage Keshan Sameera, No. 310/A, Kurukulawa,
Ragama (Acquired under Land Acquisition Act.); Extent: 0.0048
Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of  land: Koongahawatta; Description: Garden, Contains a
part of  Permanent Building; Claimant: State; Original Claimant :
Nawalage Pantraliyan Nishantha Kure, No. 309/B, Kurukulawa,
Ragama (Acquired under Land Acquisition Act.); Extent: 0.0043
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby requir ed to
appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of  Wattala on the  13th September, 2023 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 13th September, 2023, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
P . D. T . C. R
AJIKA,
