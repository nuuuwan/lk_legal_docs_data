EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I hereby, give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
State intends to acquire the following land/servitude 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired: 
 
Plan No.: cadastral map No. 410257 
Extent: 0.0379 Hectare 
 
Discription: Prepared by the surveyor General at 
Pulichchakulama Village, No. 593 Pulichchakulama 
Grama Niladhari Division, Mundel Divisional Secretariat, 
Puttlam District. Kiriyankalliya which is 0.0379 Hectare 
out of the toatal area shown in detail in the Cadastral Map 
No. 410257 (2022.11.09) 
Boundaries: Lot 01 - Kiriyankalliya - Extent: about 
0.0379 Hectare. 
 
 North: Road (Road devcelopmen t authoriya) and part 
of Pilichchakulama; East: Part of Pilichchakulama; South: 
Part of Pilichchakulama; West: Part of Pilichchakulama 
and Road (Road devcelopment authority) 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me on 10th July 2023 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 10.07.2023. 
 
 The nature of their interests in the land, the particulars 
of their claims for compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
