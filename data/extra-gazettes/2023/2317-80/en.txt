NORTh   WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province
by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 277(1a) of the amended Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) to 
be read with Section two of the Provincial Councils (incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, i. Admiral of the fleet 
Wasantha Karannagoda the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province do hereby notify that the investigation period for 
submitting the report of the one-man investigation commission composed of Mr. ekanayake Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Lalith 
Ekanayake, retired Judicial Officer appointed by the Extraordinary Gazette Paper of democratic Socialist republic of Sri 
Lanka No. 2305/12 and dated 09th November 2022 in order to submit a report within three (03) months after investigating 
whether any of the offences referred to in Section 277(1) of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252) have 
been committed by the Mayor of the Municipal Council Kurunegala, will be extended by one month from 09th february 
2023 to 09th March 2023.
Admiral of the fleet,
W
asantha Karannagoda,
 Governor,
North Western Province.
Governor's Office, 
North Western Province,
Kurunegala,
02nd february, 2023.
