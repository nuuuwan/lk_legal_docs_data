NATIONAL  zOOLOGICAL  GARDENS  ACT
IN accordance with the powers assigned to the Minister under Section 13 of the National Zoological Gardens act, No. 41 of 
1982, the fees charged in relation to the ticket fees and additional activities of the Zoological Gardens of the department of 
Zoological Gardens are amended as stated below with effect from 13.01.2023.
 Mahinda aMaraweera,
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources 
 Conservation.
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest resources
Conservation,
13th january, 2023.
ORDERS
With effect from 13.01.2023, entry ticket fees and additional activities will be charged from domestic and foreign visitors to 
dehiwala Zoo, Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, Pinnawala Zoo and ridhiagama Safari Park under the National Zoological 
department as follows.
