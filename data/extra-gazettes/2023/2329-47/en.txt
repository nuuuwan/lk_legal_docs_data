Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25a of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, 
dr. a. d. Susil premajayantha, the Minister of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70c of the aforesaid act, 
in respect of the royal institute of colombo (private) limited, a company incorporated in Sri lanka under the companies 
act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number pV 9729, being recognized as a degree awarding institute, do by this 
Order amend the Schedule ii to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2208/49 of 
January 01, 2021, by the addition immediately after entry “Bachelor of Business Honours in international Economics and 
Finance” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I 
and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.

 1.  Royal Institute of Colombo (Private) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme specified

‘Simple passes’ (S) in any subject stream including English as a one Main Subject, in one and the same sitting at 
the GcE (advanced level) Examination conducted by the department of Examinations of Sri lanka or equivalent.

ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25a  of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
i, dr. a. d. Susil premajayantha, the Minister of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70c of the aforesaid 
act, in respect of the Sri lanka institute of information Technology (Guarantee) limited, a company incorporated in Sri 
lanka under the companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number Gl  24, being recognized as a degree 
awarding institute, do by this Order amend the Schedule ii to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2312/47 of december 29, 2022, by the addition immediately after entry “Master of philosophy in 
Business Administration” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified 
under column i and ii respectively of the Schedule ii hereto, Subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
ScHEdUlE  i
C
onditions
 1.  Sri lanka institute of information Technology (Guarantee) limited shall, when admitting students for the degree 
programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Master of Education, Complete with a minimum of 
60 credits (SlQf level 10), and Master of Education with course work, complete with a minimum of 30 credits 
(SLQF Level 9), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of -
 (a) a Bachelor of Education degree obtained from a University recognized by the Ministry of Higher 
Education or University Grants commission;
 (b) a Bachelor’s degree with a postgraduate diploma in Education (pGdE) from a University recognized by 
the Ministry of Higher Education or University Grants commission; or
 (c) a Bachelor’s degree obtained from a University recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education or 
University Grants commission with at least two years’ relevant work experience.
