THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under  Section 07 
 
 
Reference No.  : බිය 3/1/1/503. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No . 28 of 1964, that               
the Government intends to acquired the following land 
for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired : 
 
Four allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0132 
Hectares, depicted from lot No. 01 to 04 in the 
Preliminary Plan PP. GAM 4263 and dated 10.01.2023, 
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys, 
situated at the village of Heiyanthuduwa, in Biyagama 
Grama Niladhari Division, i n Gampaha District, and 
bounded as follows. 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the land: Delgahawatta; Present land usage: 
Land reserved for the Express way; Original Claimant : 
Road Development Authority; Extent: 0.0026 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the land: Delgahawatta; Present land usage: 
Homestead; Original Claimant : W. D. Deepal Rodrigo, 
No. 168/3, Biyagama; Extent: 0.0081 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
 
Name of the land: Delgahawatta, Assessment No. 
0215/063, Sri Dewamitta Road; Present land usage: 
Homestead; Original Claimant : Chamith Gayan 
Kannangara, No. 349/13, Dewamitta Place, 
Heiyanthuduwa; Extent: 0.0021 Hectare. 
 
04. Lot No. 04 
 
Name of the land: Not mentioned; Present land usage: 
Road; Original Claimant : Cannot be found; Extent: 
0.0004 Hectare.
