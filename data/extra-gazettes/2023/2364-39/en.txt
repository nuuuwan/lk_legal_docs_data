RoAds  and lanes by Local Councils in sabaragamuwa Province published by the Governor of sabaragamuwa Province in 
Extraordinary  Gazette No. 2316/20 and dated 24th January 2022 to be read with section 24 of  Pradeshiya sabha  No. 15 of 
1987 sabaragamuwa from the series "orders on the preparation of road plans and publication of notices showing the list of 
names". In accordance with the work assigned to the  Local Councils of the province, it is hereby decided under number 45 
on 07.06.2023 that the roads and lanes included in the following schedule should be declared as roads and lanes owned by the 
Kolonna Pradeshiya sabha.
 Any road or lane included in the said sub-register through thirty lands or to any part of any land, it is hereby announced that 
if the party claiming the said land is of the option, they should take action to file a case in the competent court before the expiry 
of a period of three months from the date of this announcement published in order to map their right to the said lane.
 It is further announced that every road and lane included in the said sub-document, where no such case has been assigned 
before the expiry of the said three months period, shall  be deemed to be a road or lane owned by the Kolonna Pradeshiya sabha.
 
 K. V . P. Irangani, 
 Secretary and Powers, Functioning Officer,
 Pradeshiya sabha Kolonna.
