by virtue of power vested in me under sub section (1) of section 134 of Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, being the Assistant

Proposal
   “by virtue of power vested in to the Pradeshiya sabha under sub section (1) of section 134 of Pradeshiya sabha Act, 
No. 15 of 1987, the Pallepola Pradeshiya sabha do hereby propose to declare the Koholanwela area, within the jurisdiction 
of Pallepola Pradeshiya sabha, map attached to the schedule and detailed its boundaries, and to propose the said Proposal to 
be forwarded for the approval of the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Matale District and to be assessed the 
annual value of the immovable and movable assets and to impose annual Assessment Tax based on the annual valuation, and 
to make request to approve the Proposal by the Hon, Governor of Central Province, in terms of sub section (1) of section 
134 of Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read along with section 2 of the Provincial Councils Act, (Consequential 
Provisions) No. 12 of 1989".
