THE  CIVIL  A VIATION  ACT,  No. 14  OF  2010
Order under Section 23
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 23 of the Civil aviation act, No. 14 of 2010,1, Nimal siripala de silva, 
Minister of Ports, shipping and aviation, do by this Order, repeal the schedule to the “sri Lanka aerodromes” (Zoning) 
Regulations, 1975. Order No.l of 1975. Katunayake published in Gazette No. 160 of april 18, 1975 and substitute the 
schedule set out below to-
(a)	 redefine	the	metes	and	bounds	of	the	area	of	land	declared	as	a	protected	area;	and
(b) comply with the requirements set out in the instructions, directives and procedures issued by the director General 
relating to the creation of Protected Zones and the construction of any buildings or other structures or the making 
of alterations to any existing buildings or structures within the Protected area.
“SCHEDULE
annex 1-  area of land bounded by the line joining the geographical coordinates represented by the points 0 to 215 situated 
on the circumference of a circle with a radius of 16, 905 m which is centered on the midpoint of the runway of 
the Bandaranaike International airport
annex 2- details of the Protected area
annex 3- List of Geographical Coordinates”
 N
imal Siripala de Silv a,
 Minister of Ports, shipping and aviation.
Colombo,
04th May, 2023.
