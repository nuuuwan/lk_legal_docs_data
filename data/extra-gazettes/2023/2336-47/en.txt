ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 22
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 22 of the aforesaid act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1187/15 of june 06, 2001, 
establishing the Trincomalee Campus of the eastern university of sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after the item 
“Faculty of applied science” appearing in Column I of the schedule which corresponds to the entries in the Column II of 
the schedule of the following item and entries:-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of siddha Medicine department of Moola Thaththuvam (Basic Philosophy) 
department of Gunapadam (siddha Pharmacology)
department of Noinaadal Chikitsai (Pathology and Clinical sciences)”
