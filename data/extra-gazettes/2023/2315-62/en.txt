DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT — NORTHERN  PROVINCE
Notice under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012 under Section 66(G)
THE elected mayor wasn't declared in the meeting for election of New Mayor of jaffna Municipal Council convened 
on 19.01.2023 at 10.00 a.m at jaffna Municipal Council, under the sub section No. 66(G) of Local Authorities election 
(Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012, such meeting was postponed. However, under the sub section No. 66(d)(5) of the above 
Mentioned Act, i hereby declare that Mr. immanuel Arnold was selected as new mayor of jaffna Municipal Council in the 
meeting convened on 19.01.2023 at 10.00 a.m at jaffna Municipal Council.
