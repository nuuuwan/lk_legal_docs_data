PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
38th Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”) of Sri Rohana Maha Sanga Sabha of  
“Syamopaliwanshika Maha Nikaya”
IN terms of regulations mentioned in the section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175), No. L. D. B 11/60 dated 14th 
October 1991, it has been published that the 38th Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”) of Sri Rohana Maha 
Sanga Sabha of Kamburupitiya Divisional Secretariat, Matara District will be held from 13th, 14th, and 15th of May 2023 at 
the “Seema Malaka” of Rohana Sanga Sabha Headquarters of Urapola Siri Rathanajothi Piriven Viharaya and the devotees 
are requested not to bring plastic and Polythene based products. And also it is hereby informed that the standing orders and 
regulations published by the Government Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be valid within this 
time period of the Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”).
 K
USALAKA NANAYAKKARA,
 Divisional Secretary,
 Kamburupitiya.
