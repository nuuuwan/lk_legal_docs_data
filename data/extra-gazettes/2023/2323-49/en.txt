WHEREAS the office of a return member of Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the, United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which 
Alagiyahandi Dayarathna De Silva the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Withanage Nevil Dhammika Perera Wickramarathne to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Withanage 
Nevil Dhammika Perera Wickramarathne was selected as the member of the Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
