REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS
Notice  that  the  Supplementary  Elactoral  Register  2023(2)  has  been certified  under  the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
NotIce is given under sub-section 20A (10) of the Registration of Electors Act for the Registering Officer of each electoral 
district that the 2023(2) Supplementary electoral register containing the names of persons who attained the age of 18 years 
on a certain date within the period from 01 June 2023 to 30 September 2023 was certified on 02 October 2023 in terms of 
the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of electors (amendment) act, No. 22 of 
2021; the said registers have been certified by the Assistant Registering Officer in the corresponding entry in Column II for 
the electoral districts mentioned in column I of the schedule below; and that the said registers can be inspected during the 
normal office hours of the District Election Office/offices mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column III thereof.
 
 
Saman Sri ratnayake,
 commissioner General of elections,
 election commission.
at the elections Secretariat, rajagiriya,
on 02 october, 2023.
