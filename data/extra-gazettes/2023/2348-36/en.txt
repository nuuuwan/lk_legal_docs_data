ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, 
dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of Education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid act, 
in respect of the aquinas College of higher studies, being recognized as a  degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, 
amend the schedule I to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2116/11 of March 
26, 2019, by the appeal of the condition in schedule I and the substitution therefore of the following :-
 1. aquinas College of higher studies shall, when admitting students for the degree programme Bachelor of Education 
Honours	in	English		(SLQF	Level	6),	adhere	to	the	minimum	entry	qualification	of	-
 (a) Three (03) simple (s) passes in any subject stream (‘English’ shall be one of main three subjects,) in one and 
the same sitting at the G. C. E. a/L Examination or equivalent ; or
 (b) Three simple (s) passed in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the G. C. E. a/L Examination or 
equivalent with a credit pass (C) for English at the G. C. E. O/L Examination or equivalent and being a holder 
of diploma in English Language and Literature or diploma in English Teacher Education offered by aquinas 
College of higher studies.
 2. aquinas College of higher studies is recognized to offer Bachelor of Education honours in English subject to the 
five	(5)	year	re-review	conducted	by	the	Specified	Authority.
