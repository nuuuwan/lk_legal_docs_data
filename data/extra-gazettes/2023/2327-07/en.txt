SPORTS LAw No. 25 Of 1973
Regulations under the Section 32 and 33
I, Roshan Ranasinghe the Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32 (b) and 
33 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973, suspend the registration of the Rugby Federation of Sri Lanka; which is the National 
Association for the sport of Rugby, with immediate effect and appoint a Stabilization Committee as a temporary operational 
procedure in order to further maintain the functions of the said association for a period of 06 months.
