SPORTS  LAW  No. 25  OF  1973
Regulations made under the Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973
i, The Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs, Roshan Ranasinghe, hereby temporarily suspend the registration of the  
Sri Lanka Football Federation which perform on behalf of the Sport of Football in Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers 
vested in me under the Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973 and as its amendments, and hereby appoint 
the Director General in the Department of Sports Development as the competent authority to carry out the administrative 
and other required activities of the said National sports Federation and to convene and hold the relevant elections and 
perform duties in a realistic manner until then, with effect from 04th April, 2023
 Roshan Ranasinghe (M.p.)
 Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs.
At Colombo,
04th april, 2023.
