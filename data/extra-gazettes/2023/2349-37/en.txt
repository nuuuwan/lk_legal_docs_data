Central Bank of Sri Lanka Notices
THE  FINANCE  BUSINESS   ACT,  No.  42  OF  2011
CaNCEllaTioN of licence issued to Kanrich Finance ltd, due to amalgamation with Nation lanka Finance PlC under

 d r. P. NaNdalal WeerasiNghe,
 Chairman of the Monetary Board and the
 Governor of the Central Bank of Sri lanka.
  The public is hereby informed that, due to the amalgamation of Kanrich Finance ltd with Nation lanka Finance PlC as 
part of the Masterplan for Consolidation of Non-Bank Financial institutions Sector, the Monetary Board of the Central Bank 
of Sri lanka, in terms of the Finance Business act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the licence issued to Kanrich Finance ltd 
of No. 69, Ward Place, Colombo 07, to carry on finance business, with effect from 31.07.2023.
