ThE  CuSTOm  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 19A
by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 19a of customs ordinance (chapter 235), as amended by the customs 
(amendment) Law No. 35 of 1974 and the customs (amendment) act, No. 83 of 1998, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister 
of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies do by this Order exempt the goods specified in Column V of the 
Schedule hereto, imported by or consigned to, person or institutions specified in the corrresponding entry in Column II of 
that  Schedule from payment of the Customs Import Duty, leviable thereon as specified in the corresponding entry in Column 
VIII of that Schedule subject to such conditions.
   However, any importer or person by whom or on whose behalf any goods, specified and imported under this Order 
are subsequently to be sold or disposed of contrary to the conditions specified, such importer or person shall obtain approval 
or the director General of customs and pay the customs Import duty payable on such goods at the time of sale or disposal 
to the director General of customs in terms of Section 19 (a) (3). any goods sold or disposed of in costravention of such 
Section shall be liable to be forfeited.
 Ranil Wickremesinghe,
 minister of Finance, economic Stabilization &
 National Policies.
ministry of Finance, economic Stabilization & National Policies,
colombo 01,
october 27, 2023.
