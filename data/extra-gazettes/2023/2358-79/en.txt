Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Reference No. : KDS/AKU/4/2/1/60.

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460), as amended by 
the land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, 
that the Government intends to acquire the following lots 
of land for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Lots of land situated within following boundaries and 
numbered as A with a total extent of 0.0372 Hectares, 
named as Saiston Estate, depicted in the preliminary plan 
No. K. C. 320672, surveyed, prepared and approved by the 
Surveyor General on 20.07.2023 and situated in 
Balakaduwa Village in No. 585 - Balakaduwa Village 
Officer’s Division Akurana Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Kandy District in the Central Province : 
 
01. Lot No. A 
Name of the Land : Saiston Estate; Owners of the Land : 
1. H.M.G. Welagedara, Saiston Bangalow, Alawathugoda. 
2. M. N. Welagedara, Saiston Bangalow, Alawathugoda; 
Extent: 0.0372 Hectare (14.7 Perches) 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority)

acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of compensation of such amount.
