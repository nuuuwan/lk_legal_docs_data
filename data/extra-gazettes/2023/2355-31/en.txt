11. appointment to the Post
Press Administrator – An officer holding a Forman post in Class 2 Grade I will be appointed by the Commissioner General 
of examinations on the basis of seniority.
Note
When an officer with prescribed qualifications is not available, an officer who has fulfilled the combined service period of 
10 years in Class 3 Grade I as per the old Service Minute and Class 2 Grade III as per this Service Minute and has working 
experience of 10 years in the post of Forman, will be appointed to the post of Press Administrator by the Commissioner 
General of examinations on the basis of seniority.
