Part I : Sec. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 29.08.2023
2a
REGuLATIONS  
1.	 These Regulations may be cited as the  “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 13 of 2023”.
2.	 Imports and exports (Control) Regulations No. 10 of 2023, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2336/45 dated 
June 14, 2023, is hereby rescinded without prejudice to the validity of any actions performed thereunder.
3.	 The director general, sri Lanka Customs is hereby authorized to release any Cargo/ Consignment of goods, which were 
regulated by the Imports and exports (Control) Regulations No. 10 of 2023, referred in the Regulation No. 2 above, 
during the period of June 14, 2023, to august 28, 2023, as per provisions of the Customs Ordinance without demanding 
an Import Control License effective from August 29, 2023.
4.	 These Regulations shall enter into force with effect from August 29, 2023.
