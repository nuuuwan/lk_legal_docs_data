PRADEShIYA  SAbhAS  ACT  No.  15  Of 1987
Order
by virtue of the powers vested in me under Paragraph (b) of sub-section (2) of section 5 of Pradeshiya sabhas act No. 15 
of 1987 to be read with section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 12), I, dinesh Gunawardena, Prime Minister 
and  Minister of Public administration, Home affairs, Provincial Councils and local Government, hereby extend the term 
of office of the members of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha which had been commenced in terms of the order published in Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2151/23 dated 26th November, 2019, up to 04th November 2024.
 Dinesh GunawarDena,
 Prime Minister, 
 Minister of Public administration, Home affairs, 
 Provincial Councils and local Government.
Colombo,
On 18th July, 2023.
