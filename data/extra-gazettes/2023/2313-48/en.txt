ORDER
Since the Budget for the year 2023 has not been passed till 31.12.2022 due to the vacant position of the Chairman, 
Urban Council Point Pedro, I do hereby authorize Mathini Nelsan, Vice Chairman of the Point Pedro Urban Council, to make 
only the following expenditure, within their proposed estimates for the year 2023, until such time the Budget for 2023 is passed.
 (01)  Salaries and Allowances
 (02)  Travelling Allowances
 (03)  Transportation, Communication and Utility Services
 (04)  Supplies and Requisites
