Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  kegalle  urban  council 
under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF    the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  the member of Kegalle Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member 
of the Party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Warnakulage Ishara 
Randhika Harischandra the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon  member of that 10 - Pitihuma of Kegalle Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sirimewan Palitha Wijewardena to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe, Returning Officer of the Kegalle 
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Sirimewan Palitha Wijewardena was elected as the member of the 10 - Pitihuma of Kegalle Urban Council.
   
 K. H. M. S
ajitH P. KaStHuriSingHe,
 Returning Officer,
