PRADESHIYA  SABHA  - PANDUWASNUWARA
IT is hereby notified for public information  that it has been decided under  Decision Book No. 1539 dated  09.11.2023 
that all the roads and thoroughfares for  which legal actions had not been filed  in a proper Judicial Court before the date 
specified or roads and thoroughfares confirmed as the roads and thoroughfares that should be  owned by Pradeshiya Sabha 
Panduwasnuwara as per the  Decree of a Judicial Court  in which a case had been filed before the said date  and not mentioned in 
the following schedule from among the roads and throughfares given in the list of the schedule of the notification  published in 
the  Extra ordinary Gazette paper No. 2338/22 and dated 27.06.2023 shall be confirmed as roads and thoroughfares  owned by 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Panduwasnuwara in terms of Orders made by series of “orders on publication of notifications specifying 
preparation of plans  and lists for roads and thouroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within  the North Western 
Province" Published by Extraordinary Gazette  Paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon Governor in the North 
Western Province and in terms of the Order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
 It is further notified that a list of said roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and all the roads 
and thoroughfares mentioned in that  schedule shall be consideres as roads and  thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Panduwasnuwara under Sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
                 
    
    H.A.G. Nishshanka, 
 Secretary,
 Pradeshiya Sabha - Panduwasnuwara.
At Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Head office.
14th December, 2023.

 2A
Decision
Under Section 9(3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, I, H.A.G. Nishshanka,   the Secretary of Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha 
decided that  the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions had not been filed in a proper Judicial Court and  not  mentioned 
in the  following Schedule from among the roads and thoroughfares given in the list of the Schedule of the notification 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette paper  No. 2338/22 and dated 27.06.2023 according to the  Decision Book No. 1539 
dated 09.11.2023, shall be confirmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Panduwasnuwara in terms 
of Orders made by the series of “Orders on publication  of notifications specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and 
thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province” published by Extraordinary Gazette  
paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order by 
Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
