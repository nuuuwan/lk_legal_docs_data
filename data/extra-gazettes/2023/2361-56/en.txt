 PRADEShIYA  SABhA  -  mAwAThAGAmA
It is hereby notified for public information that it has been decided under the Decision 1568 dated 15.11.2023 that the 
roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions have not been taken in a proper court before the date specified in the said 
notification, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been confirmed to be deemed to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Mawathagama in accordance with the decree announced by that Court, from among the roads and thoroughfares considered 
to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Mawathagama in the Schedule set out in the Notification published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2341/48 dated 20.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of “Orders with regard to 
the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province 
and publication of advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has 
been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 which was amended by Section 3 (1) of the 
Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2317/87 and dated 03rd February 2023, and to be read with the order made under Section 
24 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road and 
thoroughfares included therein shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Mawathagama 
under sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
                                                                                                                                       
 r.m.t.K. Rathnayake,
 Secretary and officer who executes powers,
 Duties and functions
 Pradeshiya Sabha Mawathagama.
Head Office of Pradeshiya Sabha, Mawathagama,
05th december 2023.

2aDecision
In accordance with the Decision No. 740 dated 19.07.2023, I hereby decide that the roads and thoroughfares among the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the 
list set out in the Schedule of the Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2341/48 dated 20.07.2023, for which a case has not been filed in the 
proper court and are included in the schedule below should be confirmed as the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Mawathagama by virtue of 
powers vested under the Orders in the series of “Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in 
the North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has been 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No, 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021, which was amended by Section 3 (1) of the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2317/87 
and dated 03rd February 2023, in terms of the order made under Section 24 (1) a of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
