FIREARmS  ORDINANCE
Declaring  a  period  to hand  over  Unlicensed  Guns
by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 30(1) of the firearms ordinance No. 33 of 1916, as amended by ordinances 
No. 06 of 1920, No. 12 of 1945, No. 07 of 1947 and acts No.18 and 22 of 1955, No. 38 of 1975, No. 08 of 1980, No. 50 of 
1981 and No. 22 of 1996 (Chapter 182) to be read with article 44(3) of the Constitution of the democratic Socialist republic 
of Sri Lanka, I ranil Wickremesinghe minister of defence do hereby declare a period mentioned in the following Schedule 
to be enforced Island wide for unlicensed guns to be handed over to the government.
further, when unlicensed guns are handed over within the declared period of time, it is ordered that the person handing 
over the gun should not be fined or punished for an offence committed contrary to Section 22 of the Firearms Ordinance with 
regard to keeping in custody, possession or utilization of the said gun before the date of handing over.
    
 
Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 minister of defence.
Colombo,
06th february 2023.              
SCHeduLe
 01. Place to handover unlicensed guns:-
  Nearest Police Station
 02. declaring a period to hand over unlicensed guns:-
  from 06th february to 15th march 2023
