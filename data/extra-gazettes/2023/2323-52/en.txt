PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
filling  of  a  v acancy  in  the  office  of  member  of  the  baddegama  Pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66a(1)  of   the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chaPter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the, United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Janak 
Priyantha Ilesingha the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 
of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Withanage Nihal Sisira Waidyasekara Weerasingha to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Withanage 
Nihal Sisira Waidyasekara Weerasingha was selected as the member of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 W. H. R. W
ijaya kumaRa,
 Returning Officer,
 Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Galle,
