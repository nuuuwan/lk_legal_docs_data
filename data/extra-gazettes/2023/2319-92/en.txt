WHEREAS the office of the return member of Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the secretary of the, United National Party the recognized political party to which Udalawaththa 
Gamage Nanda Wijekoon the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Mallika Jayanthi Menike to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Poojapitiya Pradeshiya 
Sabha,  acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Mallika Jayanthi Menike was selected as the member of the Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
