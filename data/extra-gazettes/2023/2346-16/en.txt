2. By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, do hereby make 
an Order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 1 per Egg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of 
Rs. 50 per Egg on importation of Eggs, classified under the HS Code of 0407.21 listed out in the Schedule of the above 
by the Sri Lanka State Trading (General) Corporation on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry of Trade, 
Commerce and Food Security.
3. This Order shall be valid for a period of december 31, 2023 commencing from august 21, 2023 subject to provisions 
of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
