CORRECTION PAPER
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964
Order under Proviso (a ) to Section 38
Order No. 48 of 2023
   My Ref. No.  : 4-3/18/2020/WS/248.  
 Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No. : GAL/LAN/ACQUIS/               
                                                          A13/68.  
  
The Order No. 48 of 2023 under Proviso (a) to Section 
38 appeared in all three languages in the page 3A of the      
Gazette Extraodinary  No. 2331/06 dated 08.05.2023 of 
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka has been 
erroneously published. Therefore, I hereby rescind the 
said Order No. 48 of 2023 in terms of the provisions of 
Section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance. 
    
 Harin Fernando,
