Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE MADE UNDER SECTION 66 C READ WITH SECTION 66 G OF LOCAL AUTHORITIES 
ELECTIONS ORDINANCE AMENDED BY LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS (AMENDMENT)
ACT, NO. 22  OF 2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the           
powers entrusted to me by section  66 c read with Section 66 g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local 
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of selecting a new chairman 
for the vacated Chairman's position of Seethawakapura Urban Council of Western Province, will be convened on February 08th of 
2023 at 10.00 a.m.at the Urban Council Chamber of Seethawakapura Urban Council located at Awissawella.
