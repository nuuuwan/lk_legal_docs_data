Local Government Notifications
Pradeshiya  sabha  rideegama
It is hereby notified for the public information that it has been decided under Resolution No. 601 of the Resolution Book  
No. 02  and dated 10.07.2023 that the roads and thoroughfares included in the schedule below should be declared as roads 
and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Rideegama, in addition to the Gazette Notification No. 2297/64 and dated 
16.09.2022, in terms of the purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province by the series of "Orders 
on the preparation of plans and publishing  notices specifying the list of names of the roads and thoroughfares maintained by 
the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province", published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2238/35 dated 
30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha  
Act, No. 15 of 1987.
   It is hereby further notified that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare 
included in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be filed in the competent 
Court to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of three months from the date of this notification.
   It is further notified that every road and thoroughfare included in the said schedule, against which a legal action has 
not been taken before the expiry of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or a thoroughfare which has 
been vested in the Rideegama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
