SRI  LANkA  TEA  bOARD   LAw   No.  14  Of  1975
Order under Section 13(1)(b)
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 13(1)(b) of the sri lanka tea Board law, No. 14 of 1975, I, Ramesh 
Pathirana, Minister of Plantation Industries, do by this Order, vary the cess imposed under section 13(1)(a) of that law, from 
“rupees three hundred for every one hundred kilograms” to “rupees four hundred for every one hundred kilograms”. 
 Ramesh  PathiRana,
 Minister of Plantation Industries.
Colombo,
21st June, 2023.
