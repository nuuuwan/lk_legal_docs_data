ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  76A  OF  
THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER   262)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in the Election Commission by Section 76A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
(Chapter 262), it is hereby notiﬁ ed that, in addition to the matters mentioned in Section 39B of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance, a limited number of ofﬁ cers and employees of the institutions mentioned in the Schedule below, will be treated as 
postal voters for the purpose of the local authorities election, since their service are essential for the purpose of the election 
and they are expected to be deployed in essential duties on the day of the poll.
 NIMAL G. PUNCHIHEWA, S. B. DIV ARATNE, M. M. MOHAMED,

SCHEDULE

Kala Weva, Udawalawa Reservoir, Bowathenna Reservoir, Maduruoya Reservoir, Ulhitiya/Ratkinda Reservoir,

the city of Colombo such as the Port, VIP quarters, Major Government Institutions, Major Media Organizations 
such as the Rupavahini Corporation, The Broadcasting Corporation, and the Centers where polling and counting 
are carried out, the employees deployed at power plants and power exchange and maintenance centers of the 
Ceylon Electricity Board who are engaged in providing the said essential services ;

who are required to maintain continuous services for 24 hours ;

be maintained ;
 5.  Employees performing Security duties at fuel depots at Kolonnawa, and Muthurajawela of the Ceylon Petroleum
