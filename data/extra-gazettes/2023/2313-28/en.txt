ERRATA
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 92 of the National Water supply and drainage Board act, No. 02 of 
1974, I, dr. Keheliya rambukwella, the Minister of Water supply, published an announcement with a schedule on page 2a 
under No. 03-0768 in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 2269/77 and dated 04.03.2022 of the democratic socialist 
republic of sri Lanka in which mentioned as “acquisition of the immobile properties of the land described in the following 
schedule as it is required for the Laying of 315mm Pipe Line from dekinda road to Bawwagama of Galaboda ella Water 
supply Project”, which I hereby declare should be amended to read as “acquisition of servitude right over the land described 
in the following schedule as it is required for the Laying of 315mm Pipe Line from dekinda road to Bawwagama of Galaboda 
ella Water supply Project.”.
