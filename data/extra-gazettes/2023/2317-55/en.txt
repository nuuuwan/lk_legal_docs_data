Revision of Electoral Registers
Notice that the Supplementary Electoral Register 2022(3) has been certified under the Registration 
of Electors Act
Notice is given under s ub-section 20A (10) of the Registration of Electors Act for the Registering Officer of each 
electoral district that the 2022(3) supplementary electoral register containing the names of persons who attained  
the age of 18 years on a certain date within the period from 01 October 2022 to 31 January 2023 was certified on 01 
february 2023 in terms of the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of electors 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2021; the said registers have been certified by the Assistant Registering Officer in the 
corresponding entry in column ii for the electoral districts mentioned in column i of the schedule below; and that the  
said registers can be inspected during the normal office hours of the District Election Office/offices mentioned in the 
corresponding entry in 
column iii thereof.
