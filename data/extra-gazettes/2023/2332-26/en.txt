It is hereby notified that, the hon. President has, by virtue of powers vested in him under the Article 154B (2) of the 
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, removed the below mentioned Governors from their offices 
with effect from May 15, 2023.
 01. Mrs. Anuradha Yahampath
  Hon. Governor of the Eastern Province
 02. Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Kumar Jayadewa Karannagoda
  Hon. Governor of the North Western Province
 03. Mr. Jeevan Thiagarajah
  Hon. Governor of the Northern Province
