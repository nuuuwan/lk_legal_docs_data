PREvENTION  Of  OffENCES  RELATING  TO  SPORTS  ACT,  No.  24  Of  2019
Regulation made under the Section 21
I,  Roshan Ranasinghe, minister of sports and youth affairs, appoint a special Investigation unit consisting with following 
members for a period of three (03) years to be effective from 15.02.2023 as per the section 21 of the Prevention of offences 
relating to sports act, No. 24 of 2019 for the practical execution of the legal provisions of this act, and hereby order the said 
unit to proceed in line with the powers and functions assigned by this act. 
1. mr. W. a. Jayanath Wanasinghe, assistant superintendent of Police - director/Head    
     
 Roshan Ranasinghe (M. P.),
 minister of sports and youth affairs.
