by virtue of the powers vested in me  by section 2a of the Value added tax act, No. 14 of 2002, as amended by act,                
No. 06 of 2005, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do by this 
Order, amend the order published in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2282/26 dated May 31, 2022 by the substitution 
for the sub paragraph (b) of paragraph 1, of the following sub paragraph, with effect from January 01, 2024.
“(b) On the import and/or supply of goods or supply of services, other than the import of goods as referred to 
under Sub-paragraph (a) of this paragraph, at the rate of eighteen per centum (18%). ”
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2295/08 dated August 31, 2022 will be hereby rescinded with effect 
from January 01, 2024.
