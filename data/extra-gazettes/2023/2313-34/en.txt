SPECIAL   COmmODITY   LEvY  ACT,  No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, ranil 
wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist republic 
of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose, in respect of the commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special 
Commodity Levy, at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule which shall be valid for a 
period of one year commencing from january 05, 2023 subject to provisions of sub section (3) of section 2 of the special 
Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2284/03 of june 15, 2022 in respect of sugar (Hs Heading of 17.01) and the Order 
made under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette 
Notification No. 2308/17 of November 30, 2022 in respect of Yogurt (HS Code of 0403.10) and Canned fish (HS Code of 
1604.14 - Tunas, skipjack and bonito (Sarda Spp)) are hereby rescinded with effect from january 05 2023.
 R
