I, hereby give notice , under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose :

All that divided and defined allotments of land, 
mentioned in the attachment, depicted in Preliminary Plan

in the village of Pilimathalawa in Arambegama West 
Division of the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Yatinuwara in the District of Kandy, Central Province 
containing in extent of 1.0813 Hectares.
