REGULATIONS
 1. These regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 09 of 2023”. 
 2. The imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 02 of 2023, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2312/78 
dated January 01, 2023, as amended is hereby further amended.
	 2.1	 Goods,	classified	under	the	HS	Codes	as	described	in	the	Schedule	below,	which	is	an	integral	part	of	this	
regulation, together with their corresponding entries shall be deleted from the Schedule I of the imports and 
exports (Control) regulations No. 02 of 2023, as referred in the regulation No. 1 above.
