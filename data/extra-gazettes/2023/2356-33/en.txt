SRI   LANKA  PORTS  AUTHORITY   ACT  N o. 51  OF  1979
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section  2(1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act No. 51 of 1979 as amended by Act 
No. 7 of 1984, Act No. 35 of 1984, Act No. 2 of 1992 and Act No. 23 of 2023, I, Nimal Siripala de Silva of Minister of Ports, 
Shipping and Aviation, do by this Order declare that on and after the 01st of November 2023, the Port of Mannar shall be a 
Port to which the aforesaid Act shall apply.  
 
 Nංආൺඅ Sංඋංඉൺඅൺ ൽൾ Sංඅඏ ൺ,
