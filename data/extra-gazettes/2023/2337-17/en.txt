Provincial  Councils  Notifications
WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL           
The Inquiry Conducted under Section 184(1a) of Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 02 of Provincial 
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and under Section 2 of the Powers of Supervision of the 
administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province read with Article 154 c of the 
Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution
I, roshan Gunathilaka,the Governor of Western Province, by virtue of powers vested in me as per provisions stated in section 
184(1a) of urban Councils Ordinance read with section 02 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 
1989 and under section 2 of the Powers of supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities statute No. 04 of 1991 of 
the Western Province read with Article 154 c of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution, do hereby announce that the 
inquiry period of the single-person Tribunal consisting of retired judge of Court of Appeal hon, Chandradasa Nanayakkara 
appointed by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2313/41 dated 04th january 2023 to investigate and present a report on whether 
the urban Council of Maharagama, in its functioning, and Chairman of Maharagama urban Council, while functioning in his 
office has committed the offences stipulated under Section 2 of the aforesaid Powers of Supervision of the Administration of 
Local Authorities statue No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province and offences stated under section 184 of the urban Councils 
Ordinance (Chapter 255), will hereby be extended up to 30th june 2023.
