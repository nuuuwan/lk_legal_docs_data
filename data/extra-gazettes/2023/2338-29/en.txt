THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL   ACT,  No. 47  OF  1980
Order under Section 23W(1)(a)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section  23W(I)(a) of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 I, Naseer 
Ahamed, Minister of Environment, do by this Order, amend the Order made under the aforesaid Section and published in 
the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2211/51 dated January 21, 2021 as follows  :-
 (a) by the repeal of Sub-paragraph (i) of Paragraph (b) thereof, and the substitution therefor, of the following :-
 “(i) Sachets having less than or equal to a net volume of content 20ml/net weight of 20g (except for packing food, 
medicines and agrochemicals approved by the Registrar of Pesticides).”
 (b) by the insertion, immediately after the de ﬁnition of expression “process” thereof, the following new deﬁnition :-
  “Registrar of Pesticides” means the Registrar of Pesticides appointed under Section 3 of the Control of Pesticides   
                      Act, No. 33 of 1980.”
 
 N
ASEER AHAMED,
 Minister of Environment.
Colombo,
