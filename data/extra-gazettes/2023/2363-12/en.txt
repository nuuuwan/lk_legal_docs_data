Local Government Notifications
    Pradeshiya   sabha  -  KalPitiya
It  is hereby notified for public information that I have decided under the Decision No. 595 of Decision Book dated 01.11.2023 
that the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions have not been taken in a proper court before the date specified in the 
said notification, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been confirmed to be deemed to be owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Kalpitiya in accordance with the decree announced by that Court, from among the roads and thoroughfares considered 
to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha,Kalpitiya in the Schedule set out in the Notification published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2341/45 dated 20.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders with regard to 
the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha in the North Western Province 
and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has 
been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021, and the order made under Section 24 (1) of 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987. 
   It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road 
and thoroughfares included therein shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya 
under sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
                    R. M
angala  RaManayaka , 
 Secretary,
 Pradeshiya Sabha, Kalpitiya.
 Head Office At Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
06th December, 2023.

 Decision
