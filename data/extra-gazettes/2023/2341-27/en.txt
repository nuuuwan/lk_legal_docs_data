Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Partition (Rates payable for surveying) Regulations.
2. The rates set out in schedule I hereto, shall be the rates applicable in estimating the costs of the preliminary survey in a 
partition action in terms of the Partition Law, No. 21 of 1977.
