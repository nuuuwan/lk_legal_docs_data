Appointing under Section 32(1) of the Western Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987
I, Roshan goonethileke, the governor of the Western Province, according to the powers vested upon me under the section 
32(1) of the Western Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, hereby notify that with effect from 08.08.2023, Mrs. Handuwalage 
dulashini Asinsala senevirathne of the sri Lanka Administrative service Class II, has been appointed for acting duties to 
the post of Western Province Commissioner of the Co-operative development and Registrar of the Co-operative societies.
 Roshan Goonethileke,
 RWP and Bar VsV usP, Phd (Hons.) FIM (sri Lanka), ndc, psc,

 governor of the Western Province.

Battaramulla,
