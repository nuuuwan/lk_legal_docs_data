SOUThERN PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL           
Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Acts, No. 32 of 1983 and No. 11 of 1992 
ORdERs ON dELEgAtION OF POWERs
As the southern Provincial Council has been dissolved the powers vested upon the Minister in charge of the subject of  
Co-operatives of the southern Province by section 2(4) of Co-operative societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Acts, 
No. 32 of 1983 and No. 11 of 1992 as per Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, have been 
assigned to me by article 154 (C) of the Constitution of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka and in terms of the 
such powers I, William Wijesinghe gamage, the governor of the southern Province hereby assigned all powers depicted in 
Column III of the said schedule, assigned to the Registrar of Co-operative societies under Co-operative societies Act,  
No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Act, No. 32 of 1983 and Act, No. 11 of 1992 and the regulations enacted thereunder to the 
person mentioned in the Column I of the following schedule and who has been appointed to assist the Commissioner of 
Co-operative development and the Registrar of Co-operative societies of southern Province from the date mentioned in the 
Column II of the said schedule.
 dr
. W. W. GamaGe, 
 governor of southern Province.
At galle,
On this 28th day of July, 2023.
