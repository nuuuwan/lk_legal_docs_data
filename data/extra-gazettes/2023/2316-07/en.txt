Order under Section 326 of the Municipal Council Ordinance read with Section 2(1)(A) of the Provincial 
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act. No. 12 of 1989
I, jeevan thiagarajah, Governor of the Northern Province, upon the Power vested in me, do hereby make the under mentioned

21st january, 2023.
order
Since the Budget for 2023 has not been passed before 31.12.2022, I do here by authorize the mayor of the Jaffna 
Municipal Council, to make only the following expenditure within the proposed estimates for the year 2023 for a period 
of fourteen days with effect from 20.01.2023 or the date of passing the budget for the year 2023 by the council whichever 
comes first.
 (01) Salaries and Allowances
 (02) Travelling Allowances
 (03) Supplies and requisites
 (04) Transportation, Communication and utility Services
 (05) repairs and maintenance
 (06) Interest Payment
 (07) Council Pension Payment
 (08) BoC Leasing
