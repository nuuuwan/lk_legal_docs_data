CIvIL  PROCEDuRE   CODE
Notification under Section 192
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Central Bank of Sri Lanka established under the Central 
Bank of Sri Lanka Act, No. 16 of 2023, in terms of Section 192 of the Civil Procedure Code, as amended by the Civil 
Procedure Code (Amendment) Act, No. 06 of 1990, the Central Bank has, having regard to the current rates of bank interest, 
determined that with effect from 01st January 2024, the Legal Rate of Interest shall be fifteen point one five per centum 
(15.15%) per annum.
    dr. P. Nandalal Weerasinghe,
   Chairman of the Governing Board and Governor,
   Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
