Filling of a Vacancy Under Paragraph (13) (b) of Article 99 of the Constitution of 
The Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
WHEREAS, the Secretary General of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under Section 64 (1) of the 
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Ninth Parliament by reason 
of ceasing to be a Member of Parliament of Mr. Ahamed Zainulabdeen Nazeer, who was declared elected as a Member of 
Parliament for the Electoral District No. 12 - Batticaloa under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act;
And whereas the Returning Ofﬁ cer, for the Electoral District which returned the said member was directed by the 
Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 to ﬁ ll this vacancy as provided 
for under paragraph (13) (b) of Article 99 of the Constitution;
And whereas, the said Returning Ofﬁ cer acting under Section 64(2) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 
and in terms of paragraph (13) (b) of Article 99 of the Constitution has made a return in form "M" set out in the ﬁ rst Schedule  
to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Ali Zahir Moulana Seiyad was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the 
said Electoral District and has submitted the report to the Election Commission;

to ﬁ ll the vacancy caused by reason of ceasing to be a Member of Parliament of Mr. Ahamed Zainulabdeen Nazeer.
