IN fulfilment of Section 26 of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka Act, No. 16 of 2023 (hereinafter referred to as the “Central Bank 
Act”), the Minister of Finance and the Central Bank of Sri Lanka enter into this monetary policy framework agreement, on 
this 03rd day of October, two thousand and twenty-three (2023).
      Now it is hereby agreed and declared as follows:
 1. In this agreement unless the context otherwise requires:
 (a) “CCPI” means the Colombo Consumer Price Index published by the Department of Census and Statistics, 
Sri Lanka.
 (b) “Quarterly headline inflation rate” refers to the simple average of the year-on-year percentage changes in 
the monthly CCPI for the three months of the corresponding quarter.
