WHEREAS the office of  return member of Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending for  
Pradeshiya Sabha meetings period of three months without permission.
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Mahajana Pakshaya  the recognized political party to which Adhikari 
Mudiyanselage Saman Bandara Adhikari the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mannapperu Mudiyanselage Saranathissa Dharmapriya Bandara  to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of the Wariyapola 
Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Mannapperu Mudiyanselage Saranathissa Dharmapriya Bandara was selected as the member of the  Wariyapola 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
