Whereas the Budget of the year 2023 of the Koralaipattu Pradeshiya sabha in the district of Batticaloa and Navithanveli 
Pradeshiya sabha in the district of Ampara within the limits of eastern Province Provincial Council was failed to adopt as 
required under Section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987. Thus the offices of the Chairmen of the 
Koralaipattu Pradeshiya sabha and Navithanveli Pradeshiya sabha hereto became vacant in accordance to section 17 of 
the Local Authorities (special Provisions) Act No. 21 of 2012 and hence the Chairmen be appointed to that vacant position 
incompliance to Local Authorities elections (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit a Budget for 
the year 2023 to the Koralaipattu and Navithanveli Pradeshiya sabha hereto. As such I, authorize Assistant Commissioner 
of  Local Government of the Batticaloa district to Koralaipattu Pradeshiya sabha and Assistant Commissioner of Local 
Government of the Ampara district to Navithanveli Pradeshiya sabha to grant approval to the following proposals proposed 
in the aforesaid draft Budget during the period commencing from 30.12.2022 and 31.12.2022 till such a date on which a 
Chairmen are appointed to that vacant positions of the Koralaipattu and Navithanveli Pradeshiya sabhas.
Proposals
 01. salaries and Wages
 02. t ravelling Allowances
 03. stationaries 
 04. recurrent expenses
 05. LdsP and CdLG Project expenses
 06. Provincial Specific Development Grant and Criteria Base Grant
