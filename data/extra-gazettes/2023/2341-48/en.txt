 PRADEShIYA  SABhA  -  mAwAThAGAmA
IT is hereby notified for public information that it has been adopted under resolution No.740 moved at the General 
Conference held on 19 July in 2023  at Pradeshiya Sabha Mawathagama  that the roads and thoroughfares  included in the 
schedule below should be declared as roads and thoroughfares  owned by Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha,  according to the 
purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha of the North-West Province by the series of “Orders on the preparation of plans 
and publishing  notices specifying the list of names of the roads and thoroughfares  maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas of 
the North-West Province”, published under Section 3(1) of Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 
2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province   which has been amended by Extraordinary Gazette Notification  
No. 2317/87  dated 03rd  February 2023 to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987. 
   It is hereby further notified that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare 
included in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be filed in the competent 
Court to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of 03 months from the date of this notification.
   It is further notified that all the  roads and thoroughfares, against which a legal action had not been taken before the 
expiry of three months,  mentioned in that schedule, shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Mawathagama.
                                                                                                                                             
 R.M.T.K. 
Rathnayake,
 Secretary and Officer of Executing Powers and Duties,
  Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Pradeshiya Sabha Office,
Mawathagama,
