PRADESHIYA  SABHA   - KOBEIGANE 
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed for public information  that I have decided under the   Decision No. 604 of Decision Book dated  30.11.2023 
that the roads and thoroughfares for  which legal actions have not been taken  in a proper court before the date speciﬁ ed in the 
said notiﬁ cation, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been conﬁ rmed to be deemed to be owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Kobeigane in accordance with the decree  announced by that Court, from among the roads and thoroughfares considered 
to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kobeigane in the Schedule set out  in the Notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2341/28 dated 19.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders with regard to 
the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares  maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in  the North Western Province 
and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" by Hon. Governor in the  North Western Province which has 
been  published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th  July 2021, and the  order made under Section 24 (1) of  
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
 It is further notiﬁ  ed that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road and 
thoroughfares included there in shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kobeigane under 
sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.

 Secretary,

Decision
By virtue of powers vested in me under Section 9/3 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, I, A. S. M. Fernando,   Secretary of the   Pradeshiya 
Sabha Kobeigane  hereby decide under  Decision No. 604 of Book  dated  30.11.2023, that  the roads and thoroughfares among 
the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the list set out in th e Schedule of the Notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2341/28 dated 19.07.2023, for which a case has not been ﬁ led in the proper court and are included in the schedule 
below should be considered as  the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kobeigane, by virtue of powers 
vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" by Hon. 
Governor in the North Western Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 
2021, and order made under Section 24 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
