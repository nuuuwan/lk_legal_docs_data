STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. W. G. Nimal Attanayaka who had worked in the Sri Lanka Transport Board as a driver from 11.07.1993 and had 
gone abroad on 26.03.2003 before the conclusion of the disciplinery inquiry against him with regard to an accident and been 
cosidered to have vacated his post and then appointed as a contract basis worker by letter dated 12.03.2007 after appealing 
had been caused injustice by,
• the conduct of a disciplinery inquiry after re- appointment
• suspension of employment from 01.12.2014 to 30.04.2015 as a punishment after the disciplinery inquiry and 
• deprival of salaries for the period 
and if so, to what relief he is entitled and whether he could be considered to have had a continuous service period from 
11.05.1993 upto now.
 B. K. P
RABATH CHANDRAKEERTHI,
 Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the Ofﬁ ce of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, on this 16th day of December, 2022.
