JuDICATuRE   ACT,  No. 2 Of 1978
Order under Subsection (2) of Section 29 A
by virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (2) of section 29a of the Judicature act, No. 2 of 1978, i, dr. Wijeyadasa 
Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice, Prison Affairs and Constitutional Reforms, do by this Order, fix the monetary value of all 
actions specified in the Seventh Schedule of the aforesaid Act as a sum of rupees two million or not exceeding a sum of 
rupees two million, excluding interest with effect from 01st May, 2023.
                        
  dr.
 Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe,
 Minister of Justice, prison affairs and 
 Constitutional reforms.
Colombo,
17th april, 2023.
