SPORTS  LAw No.  25  Of 1973
Regulations made under the Sections 32 and  33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973
I, the Minister of sports and youth affairs, Roshan Ranasinghe, as per the powers vested in me under the sections 32 and 
33 of the sports Law No. 25 of 1973, the registration of sri Lanka automobile sport which is the National association for 
Motor sports is suspended with immediate effect and the following committee is appointed to continue the functions of that 
association as a temporary scheme.
 1. Mr. Mahesh gammanpila - Chairman
 2. Major general Indunil Ranasinghe - secretary
 3. Mr. Pubudu Wickrama - treasurer
 4. Mr. Rameesh Mahamur - Member
 5. Mr. Nishan Weerasuriya - Member
 6. Mrs. Champika amarasekara - Member
 7. Mr. sajad suhair - Member
   Further, as per the powers vested in me under the sports Law No. 25 of 1973 and the valid sports regulations, I delegate 
the power to the director general of the department of sports development to appoint an election committee for conducting 
the sri Lanka automobile sports election after convening a special general meeting of the association and the election will 
be convened and conducted by the election committee under the supervision of the director general, dr. shemal Fernando.
 
 Roshan Ranasinghe (M. P.),
 Minister of sports and youth affairs.
Colombo,
09th august, 2023.
