PRADESHIYA  SABHA - BINGIRIYA
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 9 (3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, I, as the Secretary to the 
Pradeshiya Sabha, Bingiriya, hereby notify for public information that I have decided under Decision No. 2023/712 on 29.06.2023 
which has been recommended by Management and Steering Committee under No. 2023/06/218 held on 26.06.2023 that the 
roads and thoroughfares set out in the following schedule should be published as the roads and thoroughfares owned by the 
Pradeshiya Sabha, Bingiriya, in terms of the purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha in the North Western Province by the 
series of "Orders on the preparation of plans and publishing notices specifying the list of names of the roads and thoroughfares 
maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha of the North-Western Provinc e", published in the Extraordinary Gazette  Notiﬁ cation 
No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province, to be read with Section 24 of Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
   It is hereby further notiﬁ  ed that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said l and that any road or thoroughfare 
included in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be ﬁ led in the competent 
Court to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of 03 months from the date of this notiﬁ cation.
   It is further notiﬁ  ed that all the roads and thoroughfares, against which a legal  action had not been taken before the 
expiry of three months, mentioned in that schedule, shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha, Bingiriya.
