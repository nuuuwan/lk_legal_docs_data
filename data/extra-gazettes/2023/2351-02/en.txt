INTERPRETATION  ORDINANCE
Notice of cancellation of the name of “Sri Lanka indigenous medical council” under Section 18 of interpretation ordinance.
   i, dr. ramesh pathirana, the minister of industries do hereby inform that as it is observed that the ayurvedic 
medicine council established by the ayurveda act, No. 31 of 1961, under the powers vested in me by Sections 3 and 4 of 
the Societies ordinance (chapter 123) being prejudiced due to the “Sri Lanka indigenous medical council” published and 
registered in the Gazette No. 2317/70 - 2023 as per the powers conferred on me by Sections 3 and 4 of the Societies ordinance 
(chapter 123), the said “Sri Lanka indigenous medical council” has been struck off from the register of Societies and the name 
of the Society has been canceled pursuant to the powers vested in me under Section 18 of the said interpretation ordinance.
 Dr. Ramesh PathiRana,
 minister of industries.
