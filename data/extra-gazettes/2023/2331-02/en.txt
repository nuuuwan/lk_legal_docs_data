TO be read with Section 24 of the Pradesheeya Sabha (Local Councils) Act No. 15 of 1987, In accordance with the work 
assigned to the Pradesheeya Sabhas (Local Councils) of the North Western Province in the series of "Orders on the preparation 
of plans and listing of roads and narrow paths maintained by the Pradesheeya Sabhas (Local Councils) of the North Western 
Province" published by the Honourable Governor of the North Western Province in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 
and dated July 30, 2021 and the Gazette announcement dated 06.12.2022 No. 2309/12, It is hereby announced for the 
public's knowledge that it has been resolved at the Council Meeting No. 5.1.2 held on 14.02.2023 that all the roads and 
narrow paths maintained by Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha that are stated in the sub-section given below should be declared 
as roads and narrow paths owned by Pradesheeya Sabha (Local Council).
If it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or narrow path included in the said schedule passes 
through any land or enters into any part of any land, a suit in a competent court be filed before the expiry of a period of three 
months from the date of this notification to prove his right to such land, it is hereby announced that the assignment shall be 
acted upon.
It is further announced that every road and narrow path included in the said schedule for which no such case has been 
assigned before the expiry of the said three month period shall be deemed to be a road or narrow path owned by the 
Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha (Local Council).
 
A.B.NimAl RAthNAyAkA,
 Sectretary,
 Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.

Resolution
     From the series of "Orders on the preparation of plans and listing of roads and narrow paths maintained by Pradesheeya 
Sabhas (Local Councils) of the North Western Province" published by the Honourable Governor of the North Western Province 
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated July 30, 2021 Pursuant to the provisions made and the powers received 
from Section 24(01) of the Local Council Act No. 15 of 1987 by the Honourable Governor of the North Western Province 
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2317/87 and dated February 03, 2023, The Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha resolves that the 
roads and narrow paths included in the following schedule be considered as roads and narrow paths owned by the Galgamuwa 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
