Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF   a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF   the  rambukkana   pradeshiya   
sabha  under  section  66 a(1)  oF   the  local  authorities   elections  
ordinance  (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of return member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which Nawarathna Mudiyanselage Gamini Nawarathna Bandara 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned  member 
of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Muhamdiram 
Ralalage Muhamdiram Udaya Bandara to fill such vacancy; 
Therefore I, Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe, returning Officer of the 
Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) 
do hereby declare that Muhamdiram Ralalage Muhamdiram Udaya Bandara was selected as the member of the Rambukkana 
Pradeshiya Sabha. 
   
 Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage 
 Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe,
 Returning Officer,
 Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
12th February, 2023.
