ThE  PERSONAL   DATA  PROTECTION   ACT,   No.  9  Of  2022
Order under Subsection (5) of Section 1
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (5) of Section 1 of the Personal data Protection act, No. 9 of 2022, 
read with paragraph (3) of article 44 of the Constitution of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri lanka, i, Minister of 
Technology, ranil Wickremesinghe, do by this Order, appoint July 17, 2023 as the date on which the provisions of Part V 
of the aforesaid act shall come into operation.
 
 
Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 Minister of Technology.
Colombo,
July 19, 2023.
