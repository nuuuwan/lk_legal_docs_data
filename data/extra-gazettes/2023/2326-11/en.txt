ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dr. a. d. Susil premajayantha,  
minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this order, amend the Schedule 
to the order published  in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 420/25 of September 26, 1986 (as amended from time to time),which 
specifies the Faculties and Departments of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of 
Section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a university established under Section 21 of the said act by the addition immediately 
after entry “14. department of information Technology” appearing in Column ii of the Schedule which corresponds to 
the item “Faculty of Arts and Culture” appearing in Column I of the Schedule (specified in the Order published in the         
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2290/13 of July 26,2022) of the following entry:-
                     “15. department of Economics and Statistics”
 
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 minister of Education.
