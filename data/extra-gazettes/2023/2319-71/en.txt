 (chaPter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Jaela Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased be a member 
of the Party.
And whereas the secretary of the, United National Party the recognized political party to which Wadiya 
Pathirannehelage Aneshka Minoli Jayaweera the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Jaela Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hetti Arachchige Srimathi Devika Thisera to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Jaela Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hetti 
Arachchige Srimathi Devika Thisera was selected as the member of the Jaela Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K. J. S. M
adhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Jaela Pradeshiya Sabha.
