Notice of Calling for Nominations to appoint  persons to be Mediators
NoTice made in terms of sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 4 of the mediation (special categories of disputes) act, No. 
21 of 2023 by the mediation boards commission established under the mediation boards act, No. 72 of 1988 to appoint 
panels of mediators for each special mediation board area in pursuance of the order published in the Gazette Extraordinary  
No. 2259/11 dated 21.12.2021 by the minister under sections 2 and 3 of the mediation (special categories of disputes) act, 
No. 21 of 2023.
   Nominations are called for the selection and appointment of eligible persons to be chairman and members of the 
panels of special mediation board areas (Finance) of.
▪	 administrative district, colombo
▪	 administrative district, Gampaha
▪	 administrative district, Kandy
▪	 administrative district, monaragala
▪	 administrative district, polonnaruwa; and
▪	 Administrative	District,	Anuradhapura

05. specimen Form applicable for nominations:
 (a) Nominations submitted by individual should be in specimen Form ‘a ’.
 (b) Nominations submitted by any board, organization or establishment should be in specimen Form ‘b’.
 (c) Nominations submitted by district secretaries / divisional secretaries should be in specimen Form ‘c’.
 each nomination should be in a separate specimen Form in writing accompanied by the recommendation of the Nominator 
as	specified	hereinafter	in	the	next	paragraph.		
06. The recommendation of the Nominator should not be of absence of grounds based on which such recommendation is 
given.	The	specific	grounds	based	on	which	such	recommendation	is	given	by	the	Nominator	should	be	indicated	very	
clearly	enabling	the	Mediation	Boards	Commission	to	firmly	ascertain	as	to	the	eligibility	or	ineligibility	of	the	Nominee	
suggested to be selected and appointed to the  panel of special  mediation board area.   
 
 Illustration : a clear description should be incorporated very clearly material to the Nominee’s present of past employment, 
the period of service during which the Nominee has been engaged in such employment, any position held by the Nominee 
in trust and responsibility and any other particulars in respect of  any position held by the Nominee attached to any social 
and welfare organization, place of religious worship, charity, any such other body or group of persons or a board etc .  
07.  any individual, board, organization or establishment should as far as refrain from submitting more than three nomination 
to	a	same	Special		Mediation	Board	Area.	However,	this	requirement	of	limit	is	not	applicable	in	case	of	nominations	
suggested by district secretaries / divisional secretaries. 
08.  all nominations should be sent by registered post to reach the secretary, mediation boards commission, No. 80, ministry 
of Justice New building, adhikarana mawatha, colombo 12 on or before 31st october, 2023.
09. The interview board, upon scrutinizing the basic requirements of the Nominees at the interview, will recommend the 
eligible persons and refer them to a Training course to be conducted on mediation skills and strategies and Laws relating 
to the Field of mediation. Those who will be successfully completing the Training course, will be appointed to be 
mediators on the order of merit to be secured at Training course at the discretion of the mediation boards commission. 
One	of	the	qualified	Mediators	will	be	appointed	as	the	Chairman	of	the	Panel	of	Special	Mediation	Board	Area	based	
on the recommendation being given by the mediation boards commission.
10.				The	decision	of		the	Mediation	Boards	Commission	in	respect	of	any	fact	not	provided	for	in	this	notice		shall	be	final	
and conclusive.
