Notice  under  Section 53(1) (b)  of  the  Statute  of  Central  Province  Co-operative  Societies
No. 10 of 1990  as  amended  by  the  Statute  No. 04 of 1993
ALL members officers employees and non-members who have done business with one of the following Co-operative  
Societies in the Matale District of the Central Province where the registration has been cancelled and the liquidation proceedings 
are being carried out are being called in relation to the money owed to the insitutions accordingly the submission of claims 
should be made through an application form prepared in the following format and one application form should be sent to 
the liquidator by registered mail on or before 25.12.2023.Alternatively you can meet and deliver at the following address 
25.12.2023, I further note that the claim submitted after 25.12.2023 will not be considered.
 Amila Navaratne,
 Commissioner of Co-operative Development & Registrar,
 Central Province.
Department of Co-operative Development,
