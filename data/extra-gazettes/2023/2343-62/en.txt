(SESRIP—Package 1). It is hereby notified under 
Subsection 1of Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act.

o f  t h e  C a d a s t r a l  P l a n  N u m ber 720284, which has been

01. Lot No. 01

Claimant: Shiran Wasantha Samarakoon Wijesinghe and 
Ravindra Athula Samarakoon Wijesinghe, Ella Road,

 All those  who claim title, rights or other relations to 
the above indicated land, must meet me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Wellawaya by themselves or through those 
who have acquired authority from them through 
documents, at 10.00 a.m. on the 13th day of the month of 
October of 2023. 
 
Furthermore, before the 21st day of the month of 
September of 2023, it must be notified to me through a 
document in duplicate, the nature of their rights regarding 
the said land, details of the facts which are available to 
request compensation regarding the acquisition of said 
land, the amount of compensation and how the said 
amount was calculated.
