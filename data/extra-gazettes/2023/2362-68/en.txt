PRaDEShiya SaBha - PuTT aLam
IT is hereby notified for public information that I have decided under the Decision No. PUPS/2023/12/554 of Decision 
Book dated 12.12.2023 that the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions have not been taken in a proper court before 
the date specified in the said notification, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been confirmed to be deemed to be 
owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Puttalam in accordance with the decree announced by that Court, from among the roads 
and thoroughfares considered to be owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Puttalam in the Schedule set out in the Notification 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2341/57 dated 21.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the 
series of "Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas 
in the North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list" by Hon. Governor in the North 
Western Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021, and the order 
made under Section 24 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
 It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every road 
and thoroughfares included therein shall be deemed as roads and  thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Puttalam 
under sub-section (5) of section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
 
 A.M. Mangalika Senevirathne,
 Secretary and officer who executes powers,
 Duties and functions
 Pradeshiya Sabha Puttalam.
Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha head Office,
12 th  December 2023.

Decision
By virtue of powers vested in me under Section 9/3 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, A.M. Mangalika Senevirathne, Secretary of 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Puttalam hereby decide under Decision No. PUPS/2023/12/554 of Book dated 12.12.2023, that the 
roads and  thoroughfares among the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the list set out in the Schedule of the Notification 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2341/57 dated 21.07.2023, for which a case has not been filed in the proper court 
and are included in the schedule below should be considered as the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Puttalam, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of "Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads 
and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the North Western Province and publication of advertisements 
specifying the name list" by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has been published in the  Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021, and order made under Section 24 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
