Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  wariyapola  pradeshiya 
sabha  under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF    the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  the member of Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Thennakoon 
Mudiyanselage Anura Sudarshana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 21 - Ethanawaththa of Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Pothuwewe Nayidelage Jayawardana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning officer of the Wariyapola 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Pothuwewe Nayidelage Jayawardana was elected as the member of the 21 - Ethanawaththa of Wariyapola 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 R. M. H. B. R
