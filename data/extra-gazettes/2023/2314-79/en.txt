ThE NAvY ACT (ChAPTER 358)
Order under Section 21
By virtue of the power vested in me by Section 21 of the Navy act (Chapter 358), i, ranil Wickremesinghe, President, 
having considered necessary in the national interest order the members of the Sri Lanka Navy Specialized in Hydrography              
and Nautical Cartography to perform such hydrographic activities as non-naval duties provided for in the Merchant  
Shipping (Safety of Navigation) regulations, No. 06 of 2021 made under Section 321 of the Merchant Shipping act, No. 52 
of 1971 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2261/54 of january 06, 2022, and in paragraph (ee) of Section 4 of 
the National aquatic resources research and development agency act, No. 54 of 1981.
 R
anil WickRemesinghe,
 President.
Colombo.
09th january, 2023.
