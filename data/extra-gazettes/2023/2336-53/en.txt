Regulations
 The Medicines (Ceiling on Prices) Regulations, 2019 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2123/35 of May 15, 2019, 
as amended by regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2241/43 of August 19, 2021 and regulations published in 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2271/23 of March 15, 2022 and regulations published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2277/55 
of April 29,2022 are hereby further amended as follows:

 (1)   by the repeal of regulation 6 thereof and the substitution therefor, of the following: -
“6. (1) A manufacturer or importer who sells any Scheduled Medicine at a Maximum Retail Price speci ﬁ ed 
in Column V of the Schedule to the Medicines (Ceiling on Prices) Regulations of 2019 published in 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2123/35 of May 15, 2019 as last amended by regulations published  in 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2277/55 of April 29, 2022 shall not sell such medicine at a price more than 
the Maximum Retail Price speciﬁ ed in Column V of the Schedule to these regulations.
(2)  A manufacturer or importer who sells any Scheduled Medicine at a revised retail price which is less than 
the Maximum Retail Price speciﬁ ed in Column V of the Schedule to the Medicines (Ceiling on Prices) 
Regulations of 2019 as last amended by regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2277/55 
of April 29, 2022, shall reduce proportionately such revised retail price by a total sixteen per centum 
(16%).  
(3)  Every trader, distributor, pharmacist, medical practitioner, dentist, veterinary surgeon, medical institution 
including a private medical institution, pharmacy or person who or which is in possession of the Scheduled 
Medicines for the purpose of sale shall maintain the price of the Scheduled Medicines at the maximum 
retail price or revised retail price whichever is less.”.
