Delegation  of  powers  under  co-operative  societies  statute  of  the 
 western provincial  council  No.  03 of 1998
I, Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of the Western Province, according to the powers vested upon me under the section 
02 (2) of the Co-operative Societies Statute of Western Provincial Council No.03 of 1998: that shall be read with the  
Act, 154 (c) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby notify that powers specified under 
the Column III of its schedule will be delegated to the post holder mentioned in Column II, corresponding to the name of 
Column I in the following schedule, that are appointed to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development of Western 
Province and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies of the Western Province with effect from 19th June 2023.
 Roshan Goonetileke,
 RWP and Bar VSV USP, PhD (Hon.) FIM (SL), ndc, psc,
 Marshal of the Sri Lanka Air Force,
 Governor of Western Province.
At the Office of the Governor of Western Province,
Located at the Western Provincial Council Building,
Battaramulla,
13th day of July, 2023.
