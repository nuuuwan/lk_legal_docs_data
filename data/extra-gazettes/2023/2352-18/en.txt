I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land/
Servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/Servitude to be acquired : 
 
  Allotments of lands depicted as the Lot Nos. 346, 347 in 
the Supplementary No. 10, Sh eet No. 10 of the Final 
Village Plan No. 219 dated 30.12.2022 of the land called 
"Kovulwewa Farm" in total ex tent of 2.0233 Hectares, 
situated in the village called Digandeniya in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 1076 of Dambagirigama West in 
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Narammala in 
Kurunegala District of the North Western Province and 
bounded as follows:  
Lot No. 346 
Name of  the land:  Kovulwewa Farm; Name and 
Address of the Claimant: National Livestock 
Development Board; Extent: 1.9216  Hectares. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents  
duly authorized in writing before me at Narammala 
Divisional Secretariat on 30th October, 2023 at 10.00         
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                       
(in duplicate) on or before 3rd October, 2023 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said  land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
