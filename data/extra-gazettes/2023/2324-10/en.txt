WHEREAS the office of member of Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member 
of the party ;
And whereas the secretary of the, United National Party, the recognized political party to which Guththila Kaviraja 
Mudiyanselage Gamini Thilakarathne the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that ward Number 6  Kehelwarawa of Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs 
to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who 
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Henarath Mohottalalage Chithra Kalyanavathi to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, Returning Officer of the Ambagamuwa 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Henarath Mohottalalage Chithra Kalyanavathi was elected as the member of the ward Number 6  Kehelwarawa 
of Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
