 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Minuwangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rajasekara Waduge 
Nalaka Rajasekara the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward Number  05 - Minuwangoda East 1 of Minuwangoda Urban Council Who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Wasala Mudalige Lasantha Wimalaweera to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Minuwangoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Wasala Mudalige Lasantha Wimalaweera was elected as the Member of the Ward Number  05 - Minuwangoda East 1 of 
Minuwangoda Urban Council.
   
 K. J. S. M
adhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Minuwangoda Urban Council.
