PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the KattanKudy urban council  
under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF   the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Mohammadu 
Musthafa Abdul Latheef the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ward No. 01 - Badhuriya of Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mohammadu Musthafa Jazeema to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohammadu 
Musthafa Jazeema was elected as the member of the Ward No. 01 - Badhuriya of Kattankudy Urban Council.
   
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
 Returning Officer,
  Kattankudy Urban Council.
