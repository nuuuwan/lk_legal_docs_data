ESTABLISHMENT  OF  NATIONAL NEPHROLOGY  SPECIALIZED  HOSPITAL,  POLONNARUWA
THE National Nephrology Specialized Hospital, Polonnaruwa which is newly built with 200 beds as a grant from the 
People’s Republic of China was declared open on 11th June 2021. According to the new hospital re-categorization, this 
hospital has categorized as a specialized hospital.
   Accordingly, I hereby inform that the National Nephrology Specialized Hospital, Polonnaruwa is functioning under 
the administration of the Ministry of Health and it was established to provide healthcare facilities to the kidney related 
diseases. This Hospital is located at the below mentioned address and the head of the institution is designated as the Director.
Address: National Nephrology Specialized Hospital, Polonnaruwa
Telephone No. 027 2030300
