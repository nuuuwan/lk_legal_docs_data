 1. These Rules shall be enacted to allocate provisions with regard to determining fees for issuing a Certificate of Business 
Names or Issuing a declaration and levying fees for inspecting documents including matters incidental thereto under 
the Western Provincial Business Names statute of trade, Corporations and Non Incorporated trade Institutions No. 05 
of 2011 and to allocate provisions to repeal the Rules made with regard to the said matter under the Western Provincial 
Business Names statute of trade, Corporations and Non Incorporated trade Institutions No. 05 of 2011 and published 
in the Extra-ordinary Gazette of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka No. 1829/32 dated 25.09.2013 and 
Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 2118/57 dated 11.04.2019.
 2. these Rules shall be cited as Rules to determine fees to be charged under the western Provincial Business Names statute 
of trade, Corporations and Non-Incorporated trade Institutions No. 05 of 2011.
 3. under sub-section 32(1) (a) of the Western Provincial Business Names statute of trade, Corporations and Non 
Incorporated Trade Institutions No. 05 of 2011 on behalf of issuance of a certificate or statement on each task stated 
below and inspecting documents, a fee, as stated corresponding to each such task shall be charged.
 (a) Fees to be charged for issuing a Certificate for Registration of Business Name of an Individual Business or a 
Partnership Business shall be as follows.
Initial Capital Normal fees Fees for One day Service
Initial Capital up to Rs. 299,000.00 1,500.00 5,000.00
Initial Capital From Rs. 300,000.00 to Rs. 499,999.00 2,500.00 5,000.00
Initial Capital From or above Rs. 500,000.00 3,500.00 5,000.00
 (b) The fee to be charged for issuing a Certificate related to declaration of alterations to a registration of Business 
Names shall be Rs. 3,000.00 whereas fees to be charged for one day service for the same shall be Rs. 5,000.00.
 (c) The fees to be charged for issuing a copy of a Certificate of Registration of Business Names shall be Rs. 1,500.00.
 (d) the fee to be charged for checking the Register of Business Names shall be Rs. 500.00.
 4. The Rules for charging fees for issuing a Certificate of Business Name or issuing a Declaration under the Western 
Provincial Business Names statute of trade, Corporations and Non-Incorporated trade Institutions published in the 
Extra-ordinary Gazette of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka No. 1829/32 dated 25.09.2013 and Extra-
ordinary Gazette No. 2118/57 dated 11.04.2019 shall be repealed hereby with effect from the day on which these rules 
shall come in to effect.
 5. In the event of any inconsistency between Sinhala, Tamil and English tests of this document, the Sinhala text shall 
prevail.

mentioned in the third schedule below (Form BNR – 03).

Names shall be in the manner mentioned in the eighth schedule below (Form BNR – 08).
