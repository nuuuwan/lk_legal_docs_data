ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
I,  dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid 
act, in respect of the saegis Campus  (Private) limited, a company incorporated in sri lanka under the Companies  
act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 72548 and located at No. 135, s. d. s. jayasingha Mawatha, 
Kohuwala, Nugegoda, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the 
Order made under the said act, and published in  Gazette Extraordinary No. 2301/19, of October 10, 2022, by the addition 
immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Business Management Honours in tourism and Hosptality management” appearing in 
Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively 
of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto:-
sCHedule I
Conditions
 1. The Saegis Campus (Private) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme specified in 
schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Business Management Honours in logistics and supply Chain 
Management, with a minimum of 120 Credits (SLQF level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of 
three (3) ‘simple passes’ (s) in any stream in one and the same sitting at the GCe (advance level) examination 
conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
