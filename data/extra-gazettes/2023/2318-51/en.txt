Regulations
 01. These regulations will be enacted to allocate provisions relevant to granting housing aid to low income earners under 
the Housing development fund in the Western Province and to repeal the regulations related thereon, published 
in the Gazette No. 1552, dated 30th May 2008 and under the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1916/9 dated 27th of May 
2015 of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka.
 02.  These regulations are referred to as regulations on Granting Housing Aid to Low-income-earners in the Western 
Province.
 03. Housing Aid provided by the Western Provincial Housing development fund to Low-income-earning families 
should accord to the below mentioned categories in the corresponding value.
