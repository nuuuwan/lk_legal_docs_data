WHEREAS the office of return member of Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And whereas the secretary of the, United National Party the recognized political party to which Welimaha Sarukkali 
Patabendige Jayantha Premadasa the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Priyanthi Renuka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Namaloya 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Priyanthi Renuka was selected as the member of the Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha.
