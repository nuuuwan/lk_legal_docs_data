WHEREAS the office of member of Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which 
Samarawickrama Jayawardhanage Chandana Hemantha Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under section 
66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Number  21 - Degaldoruwa of Kundasale Pradeshiya 
Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 
262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Uduwarage Don Dharmasinghe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Uduwarage Don Dharmasinghe was elected as the member of the Number 21 - Degaldoruwa of Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
