 02. By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, do hereby 
make an Order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 200 per Kg from the applicable Special Commodity 
Levy of Rs. 1300 per Kg on importation of Frozen Whole Egg Liquid, classified under the HS Code of 0408.99 listed 
out in the Schedule above on the recommendation of the Secretary, ministry of Trade, commerce and food Security 
subject to approved quantity and applicable rules and regulations.
 03. This Order shall be valid for a period of two (02) months commencing from 18th march,2023 subject to provision of 
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
