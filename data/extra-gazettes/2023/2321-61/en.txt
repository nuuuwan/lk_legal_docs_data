I, roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of the Western Province, according to the powers vested upon me under the section 
02 (2) of the co-operative societies statute of Western Provincial council No.03 of 1998: that shall be read with the Act, 
154 (c) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby notify that powers specified under the       
column III of its schedule will be delegated to the post holders mentioned in column II, corresponding to the names of
