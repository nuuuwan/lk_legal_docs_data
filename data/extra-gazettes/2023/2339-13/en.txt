It is hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following lands for the widening 
and development of Ishwari Road Pamankada within the 
Colombo Municipal Council Limit.  
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
Sixteen (16) allotments of lands described in following 
names and Total Extents of 0.1031 Hectares depicted as 
Lot Nos. 1 - 16 in the Preliminary Plan No. 9873 dated 
13.05.2020 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in  
the Village called Pamankada, in the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Thimbirigasyaya in Colombo 
District, Western Province within the Colombo Municipal 
Council Limit and morefully described as follows:
