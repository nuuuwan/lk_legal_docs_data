 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Jaffna Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And whereas the secretary of the, Eelam People's Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which 
Mudiyappu Remidius  the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Jaffna Municipal Council, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of 
the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Anthonippillai Kilapodasias to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Anthonippillai Kilapodasias 
was selected as the member of the Jaffna Municipal Council.
