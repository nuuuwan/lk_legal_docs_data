I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by land Acqusition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the below mentioned land which is 
needed to be acquired for the construction of the proposed 
Yahalathenna Karaduwawala Pump House and the Water 
Tank. 
 
  Description of the lands to be acquiried:   
 
 
All that allotment of land in total extent of about 
0.0552 Hectare, depicted as  Lot No. 01 in the Cadastral 
Map No. 320490, Surveyed and prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys of Kandy District, situated at 
the village of Karaduwa wala Yahalathenna, in 
Karaduwawala Upper Grama Niladhari Division, No. 477 
in Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor Division, in the 
Divisional Secretay’s Division of Hrispattuwa in Kandy 
District, Central Province and bounded as follows: 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Kalugalathennawatta; Claimant:   
State; Original Claimant: Abdul Kafoor Mohomed 
Wazeer, of No. 03, 2nd Lane, Subadrarama road, 
Nugegoda; Extent: 0.0552 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons  who claim ownership, interest or any other 
connection to the aforesaid land/servitude are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me on 21.02.2024 at 10.00 
a.m. at the Divisional Secretariat, Harispattuwa. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing in duplicate before 14.02.2024, nature of their 
interests in the land/servitude, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
