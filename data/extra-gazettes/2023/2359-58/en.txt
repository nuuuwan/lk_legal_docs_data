I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964 that the Government  
has acquired the allotment of lands required for the 
construction of a maintenance and monitoring office to 
protect and maintain the water ponds, canals and water 
reserves constructed under Weras Ganga Storm Water 
Drainage and Environment Improvement Project.
