WHEREAS the office of member of Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of been ceased to be member 
of the Party;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Lanka Geeganage 
Mahindadasa the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that No. 01- Bambarakele of Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kalyani Satharasinghe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, Returning Officer of the Nuwaraeliya 
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Kalyani Satharasinghe was elected as the member of the No. 01 - Bambarakele of Nuwaraeliya Municipal Council.
