01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land :Mulhalkele Watta;  Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant:- Land Reforms Commission;

02. Lot No. 02

Land Usage: Open barren land; Extent : 0.0006 Hectare.

of such amount.
