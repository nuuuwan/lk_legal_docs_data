WHEREAS the office of return member of Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the secretary of the, United National Party the recognized political party to which Alawathupitiye 
Gedara Tikiri Banda the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of United National Party, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the 
local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Thilakarathna Mudiyanselage Shriyani Sagarika Kumari Thilakarathna to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, Returning Officer of the Hanguranketha 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Thilakarathna Mudiyanselage Shriyani Sagarika Kumari Thilakarathna was selected as the member of the 
Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha.
