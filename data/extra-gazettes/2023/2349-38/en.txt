REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS
Notice  that  the  Supplementary  Electoral  Register  2023(1)  has  been certified  under  the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
NOTICE is given under sub-section 20A (10) of the Registration of Electors Act for the Registering Officer of each electoral 
district that the 2023(1) Supplementary Electoral Register containing the names of persons who attained the age of 18 years 
on a certain date within the period from 01 February 2023 to 31 May 2023 was certified on 15 September 2023 in terms 
of the Registration of Electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of Electors (amendment) act, No. 22 
of 2021; the said registers have been certified by the Assistant Registering Officer in the corresponding entry in Column II 
for the electoral districts mentioned in column I of the schedule below; and that the said registers can be inspected during 
the normal office hours of the District Election Office/offices mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column III thereof.
 
 
Saman Sri ratnayake,
 Commissioner General of Elections.
at the Elections Secretariat, Rajagiriya,
September 15, 2023.
