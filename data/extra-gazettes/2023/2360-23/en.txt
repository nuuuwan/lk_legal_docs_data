Notification
       P.S. No. :  PS/CSa/00/1/13/13
It is hereby notified that Honourable President, by virtue of powers vested in him under Article 47(3)(a) of the Constitution 
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has removed Honourable Anuruddha Ranasinghe Arachchige Roshan, 
Member of Parliament, from the following Ministerial Portfolios with effect from November 27, 2023.
 i. Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs
 ii. Minister of Irrigation
