Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOcAL   AuthOritiEs   ELEctiONs  OrdiNANcE  (chAptEr  262)
Notice under  section 38(3)
whereas notifications calling for nominations for the Election to Local Authorities mentioned in the Schedule below 
were published on the 04th of January, 2023 by me in terms of Section 26 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
(Chapter 262) upon discussions had with the Election Commission and nominations were received from 18th January until 
12.00 noon on 21st January, 2023;
Whereas Gazette Extraordinary No. 2317/10, dated 30th of January, 2023 was issued by me thereafter declaring the  
09th of March, 2023 as the day of the poll in terms of Section 38(1) of the said Ordinance;
Whereas due to unexpected circumstances that inability of the General Treasury to release the necessary funds for 
the conduct of the said Election on the aforesaid date of the poll, the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2322/36, dated 08th of 
March, 2023 was issued by me declaring the 25th of April, 2023 as the alternative date of the poll in terms of Section 38(3) 
of the said Ordinance after consulting the Election Commission; and
Whereas the issue involving the release of the necessary funds for the Election by the General Treasury remains 
unresolved and as a result, the conduct of the poll on the specified date has become impossible compelling the postponement 
of the date of the poll again.
