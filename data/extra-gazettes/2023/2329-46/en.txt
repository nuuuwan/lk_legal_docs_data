 RANIL WICKREMESINGHE,

Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Passengers’ Baggage (Exemption – Inward) Regulations No. 01 of 2023”. 
2. These Regulations shall be in force effective from May 01, 2023 until further notice.
