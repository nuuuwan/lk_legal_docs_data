ThE  ANTIquITIES  ORDINANCE (ChAPTER 188)
Notice  under Section 19
by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 19 of the antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188), I, Vidura Wikramanayaka, 
minister of buddhasasana, religious and Cultural affairs, do hereby give notice that I intend to make an Order under Section 
18 of the said Ordinance declaring the ancient monuments specified in the Schedule hereto as “protected monuments” for 
the purposes of the said Ordinance.
   Objections, if any, to the making of the aforesaid Order shall be sent to the director- General of archaeology, Colombo, 
on or before 10th October, 2023.
   every objection shall be in writing and shall contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is made.
 
Vidura Wikramanayaka,
 minister of buddhasasana,
 religious and Cultural affairs.
Colombo,
11th august, 2023.
