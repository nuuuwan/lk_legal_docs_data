PRADEShIYA SABhA mAhO
to be read with section 24 of the Pradeshiya  sabha (local Council) act, No.15 of 1987, in accordance with the work 
assigned to the Pradeshiya sabhas ( local Councils) of the North Western Province in the series of “Orders on the preparation 
of plans and listing of roads and narrow paths maintained by the honorable Governor of the North Western Province in the 
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 and dated July 30, 2021 and the Gazette announcement dated 14.11. 2022 No. 2307/19. 
It is hereby announced for the public’s knowledge that is has been resolved No. 2023/347 held on 12. 07. 2023 that all the 
roads and narrow paths  maintained by Maho Pradeshiya sabha that are stated in the sub-section given below should be 
declared as roads and narrow paths owned by Pradeshiya sabha (local Council).
   
      It is further notified that, if it is the opinion of the claimant party that any road or lane included in that Schedule will 
pass through any land or part of a land, action should be taken to file a case in a proper court within three months from the 
date of this notification is published in order to confirm his ownership of that land. 
      It is further notified that all the roads and lanes included in the schedule for which cases have not been filed in the Court 
before the expiry of the said three months period will be considered as roads and lanes owned by the Maho Pradeshiya sabha.
 R.D.K.Amarajeewa,
 secretary, 
 Maho Pradeshiya Sabha Head Office.
at Maho Pradeshiya sabha,
19th of  July 2023.
