ThE  CENSuS   ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER  143)
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Census Ordinance  (Chapter 143), I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, 
Minister  of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies, direct that the second stage of the Census of Population 
and Housing - 2024, inclusive of numbering of all buildings, numbering of all census units and listing of all census units of 
democratic socialist Republic of sri lanka be taken in the end part of the year 2023.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 Minister of Finance, economic stabilization
 and National Policies.
Colombo,
28th June, 2023.
