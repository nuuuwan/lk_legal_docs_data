PRaDEShiya  SaBha - aLawwa
IT is hereby notified for the public's information that it has been decided under Decision Book No. 816 dated 18.07.2023 that 
the roads and thoroughfares included in the schedule below should be declared as roads and thoroughfares   owned by the 
local council, in terms of the purposes assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province by the series of "Orders 
on the preparation of plans and publishing  notices specifying the list of names of the roads and thoroughfares  maintained 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhas of the North-West Province", published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2238/35 
dated 30th  July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province to be read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
It is hereby further notified that, if it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or thoroughfare included 
in the said schedule passes through any land or a part of any land of that party, a case should be filed in the competent Court 
to prove his right to the said land before the expiry of a period of 03 months from the date of this notification.
It is further notified that every road and thoroughfare included in the said schedule, against which a legal action has not been 
taken before the expiry of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or a thoroughfare which has been 
vested in the Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
     
J.M.P. J ayasekara , 
     Secretary (Officer of executing,   
                                                            Powers and duties),
     Pradeshiya Sabha Alawwa.
