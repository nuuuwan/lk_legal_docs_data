PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE KURUNEGALA MUNICIPAL 
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE 
 (CHAPTER  262)  
WHEREAS the ofﬁ ce of member of Kurunegala Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be member 
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Anuradha 
Gamini Peramunage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that 6 - Gettuwana of Kurunegala Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁ ed 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Thanga Udayar Abdul Saththar to ﬁ ll such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Ratnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayaka, Returning Officer of the Kurunegala 
Municipal  Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Thanga Udayar Abdul Saththar was elected as the Member of the 6 - Gettuwana of Kurunegala Municipal  
Council.  
RATNAYAKARR  MA UDIYANSELAGE HARENDRA
 BANDARA RA ATNAYAKAR ,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Municipal Council. 
District Elections Ofﬁce,
Kurunegala,
15th March, 2023.
