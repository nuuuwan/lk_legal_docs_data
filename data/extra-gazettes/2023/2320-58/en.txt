SPORTS  LAw No. 25  Of 1973
Order made under the Section 41 and read with the Section 39 (3) of the Sports Law
No. 25 of 1973
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 39 (3) of the sports Law No. 25 of 1973, I appoint the committee consisting 
of following members to investigate the matters related to financial control, administration and sports promotion of the Football 
Federation of sri Lanka and to submit a complete report to me containing relevant observations and recommendations prior 
to the expiry of two (02) months from the date of printing this Gazette Notice.
 i. Mrs. sarojini Kusala weerawardena - Retired High Court Judge (Chairman of the Committee)
 ii. Mr. Kingsley Fernando - Former secretary to the Ministry (Committee Member)
 iii. Mr. sudath Nagahamulla - Retired deputy Inspector General of Police (Committee Member)
 iv. Mr. Ananda  Peiris - Retired Rear Admiral (Committee Member)
 v. Mr. susil Rohan Ramanayake (Committee Member)
 vi. Miss. shalini Fernando (Attorney at Law), Committee secretary
  Mrs. w. M. Irangani wijeratne, stenographer.
The committee shall pay attention on the following points :
 1. delaying the preparation of new constitution by the Football Federation since 2012.
 2. Financial control of the Football Federation from the year 2018.
 3. Administration of the Football Federation.
 4. Norminal football clubs established with the determination of obtaining votes.
