 1. this permission may be cited as ''Permission to disburse under First supplementary financial statement - 2023 of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council" and come in to operation from 19.10.2023. 
 2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Fifty One Billion One Hundred 
and Nineteen Million (Rs. 51,119,000,000.00) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2023 and ending 
31st December, 2023, which is specified as ''Financial Year 2023" in the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue - 
Expenditure Estimates prepared for the year 2023, that was approved on 30.11.2022, is hereby amended as, Rupees 
Fifty One Billion Two Hundred and  Ninety Two Million Seven Thousand One Hundred Forty Six and Cents Thirteen 
(Rs. 51,292,007,146.13) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers to 
disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
 3. Accordingly the first schedule of the Annual Financial statement and revenue - expenditure estimates - 2023, is amended 
as the first schedule hereof and grant permission to disburse the same.

Authorization  
I, do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamua Provincial Council 
to disburse as per the first Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2023, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of 
the Democratic  Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Fifty One Billion One Hundred 
and Nineteen Million (Rs. 51,119,000,000.00) for the services of the period  begining on 01st January, 2023 and ending 31st 
December 2023, which is specified in the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue Expenditure Estimates for the year 2023, 
is amended as Rupees Fifty One Billion Two Hundred and Ninety Two Million Seven Thousand One Hundred Forty Six 
and Cents Thirteen (Rs. 51,292,007,146.13) and grant permission to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
council Fund. 
This permission will be valid till 31st December 2023, or upto the date that expires three months (03) from the date of 
summoning the Seventh (07th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council which occurs first.
