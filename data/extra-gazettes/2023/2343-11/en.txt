 02. By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 5 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, do hereby 
make an Order to waive-off;
 (a) Balance amount after collecting Rs. 10 per Kg from the applicable special Commodity Levy of Rs. 40 per Kg on 
importation of Salt (Sodium Chloride BP), classified under the HS Code of 2501.00, listed out in the Schedule  of 
the above, by any pharmaceuticals manufacturing enterprises, registered with the National Medicines Regulatory 
authority (NMRa) for the purpose of Manufacturing pharmaceuticals, not for commercial purpose, on the 
recommendation of the secretary, Ministry in charge of subject of health.
 (b) Balance amount after collecting Rs. 275 per Kg from the applicable special Commodity Levy of 10% or  
Rs. 400 per Kg on importation of fish, classified under the HS Heading 03.03 imposed by the Order published 
in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2301/40 of October 13, 2022 in respect of fish verities, classified

 03. This Orders made under section 2 or section 5 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in 
the foll owing Extraordinary Gazette Notifications are here by rescinded with effect from July 31, 2023.
 (i) The Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2292/41 of August 10, 2022,
 (ii) The Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2298/51 of September 22, 2022,
 (iii) The Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2337/16 of June 22, 2023 in respect of duty waiver on importation 
of sail Fish, Marlin Fish and Queen Fish classified under the HS Code of 0303.49 and 0303.89.90.
 4.  The Order in the Paragraph No. 2 (b) of the above shall be valid for a period of 3 months and the Orders in the Paragraph 
Nos. 1 and 2 (a) of the above shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from July 31, 2023 subject to provisions 
of sub-section (3) of section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
