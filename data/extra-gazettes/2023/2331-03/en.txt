NORTh  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification  by  Governor  of  North  Western  Province
It has been reported  to me that thushara Sanjeewa Witharana has been found guilty for 11 charges out of 18 against him in 
the inquiry conducted by one member investigation committee appointed by the order, given by me, the Admiral of the fleet  
Wasantha Karannagoda, the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province, published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 2305/12 
dated 09.11.2022 by virtue of powers vested under article 154 (c) of the democratic Socialit Republic of Sri Lanka, and under 
Section 277 (1a) in municipal Council ordinance, the Chapter 252 to be read with Section 2(1)(a) of Provincial Council 
(Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 to investigate and report whether any of the offences referred to in Section 277 (1) 
of municipal Council ordinance, the Chapter 252,  while he was functioning as the mayor of Kurunegala municipal Council.
   Also, the said Thushara Sanjeewa Witharana has been deemed to have resigned from his office with effect from 
31.12.2022 since the implementation of the provisions in budget compilation and enforcement orders of the municipal 
Council budget 2020 published in Section No. 215a of the said municipal Council ordinance and the Extraordinary Gazette         
No. 2199/15 dated 29.10.2020 in the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, while the said commission was conducting 
the investigation.
   Further on intensive inspection and studying all the documents regarding the charges and conclusions mentioned in the 
said report, I am satisfied that there are sufficient facts to decide that the said Thushara Sanjeewa Witharana has committed 
one or more offences mentioned in Section 277 (1) of municipal Council ordinance, the Chapter 252, while functioning as 
the mayor of Kurunegala municipal Council.

   Taking into consideration all the facts, I, the Admiral of the fleet Wasantha Karannagoda, the Hon. Governor in the 
North Western Province, by virtue of powers vested under article 154(c) of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, 
and under Section 277 (1a) in municipal Council ordinance, the Chapter 252 to be read with Section 2(1) (a) of Provincial 
Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, do hereby order that the said thushara Sanjeewa Witharana shall be 
deemed to have been dismissed from the position of mayor and a member of Kurunegala municipal Council with effect 
from 31.12.2022.
 Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Karannagoda,
 Governor of North Western Province.
At Kurunegala,
