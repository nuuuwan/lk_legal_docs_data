REGISTRATION   Of  PERSONS    ACT,  No. 32  Of  1968
Notification under Section 36(2)
by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 36(2) of the registration of Persons act, No. 32 of 1968, I, Tiran alles, 
minister of Public  Security, hereby notify that a maximum fee of four hundred rupees (LKr 400.00) should be charged by 
a registered photographer, with respect for uploading to the e-photo Studio Software System of department for registration 
of Persons, the Icao standard compliance digital image of a person who applies to obtain an identity card or duplicate of 
an identity card after registration, and thereafter for issuing the Photographic reference Slip to the applicant.
The notification published in Gazette No. 1965/33 of 05th may, 2016 is hereby rescinded.
    Tiran Alles,
   minister of Public Security.
   
ministry of Public Security,
