ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Sections 25A and 70C
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid 
act, in respect of the Kaatsu - Highly advanced Medical technology training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIu), a company 
incorporated in sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 66705, which is 
recognised as a Degree Awarding Institute, and since under the certificate dated May 26, 2023 issued by the Registrar 
general of Companies the said Kaatsu - Highly advanced Medical technology training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIu) has 
been incorporated as the KIu Campus (Private) Limited, do by this Order, amend the Order made under the said act, and 
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1896/22 of January 7, 2015, by the substitution for the words “Kaatsu-Highly 
advanced Medical technology training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIu)” wherever those words appear in the said Order, of 
the words “KIu Campus (Private) Limited” and every reference to the “Kaatsu-Highly advanced Medical technology 
training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIu)” in any Order, notice, contract, communication or other document made in terms of the 
aforesaid sections shall be read and construed as a reference to the “KIu Campus (Private) Limited”.
   Further, any power exercised, or any duty or function performed by the said Kaatsu-Highly advanced Medical 
technology training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIu) prior to the date of coming into effect of this Order in pursuance of any 
power conferred on it by the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1896/22 of January 7, 2015 shall be deemed for 
all purposes to have been validly exercised and performed by the KIu Campus (Private) Limited.
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 Minister of education.
