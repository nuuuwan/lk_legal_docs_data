 02. Importation of Maize, Black gram and green gram as described above shall be allowed on recommendation of the 
Ministry of agriculture.
03. the Orders under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the following 
Extraordinary Gazette Notifications are hereby rescinded with effect from august 18, 2023. 
 (i) Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2321/76 of March 04, 2023 in respect of importation of Black gram (hs 
Code 0713.31.22 and 0713.31.29)
 (ii) Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2301/40 of October 13, 2022 in respect of importation of green gram 
(hs Code 0713.31.19)
 4.  the Order of the above  shall be valid for a period of 6 months  commencing from august 18, 2023 subject to provisions 
of sub-section (3) of section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
