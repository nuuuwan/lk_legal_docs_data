THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice  under  Section 07  
 
 
 
File No. of the Divisional Secretary  : BOP/4/4/1-89. 
File No. of the Ministry of Lands: 4-3/3/2021/PI/153. 
 
 
IT is hereby informed that under Sub-section (1) of 
Section 07 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by 
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, 
that the government intends to acquire the following 
lands required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Lands to be Acquired: 
 
The plots of land described below depicted from Lot 
No. 01 to 03 of Folio No. 03 of Division No. 01 in the 
Cadastral Map No. 810169 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in Meepawala Village of Meepawala 
Grama Niladhari’s Division N o. 117 A in D.S. Division 
Bope Poddala in Galle District, Southern Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the land: Wakkattiyawela; Description of the 
land: Paddy Field; Claimant : Mahesh Piyantha 
Kalansuriya, Holuwagoda, Uluvitike, Galle; Extent: 
0.2857 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the land: Cannot be found; Description of the 
land: Canal; Original Claimant : Fist owner cannot be 
found; Extent: 0.0086 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
 
Name of the land: Usgodawila; Description of the 
land: Paddy Field; Claimant : Lalitha Kalansuriya and 
others, Narawala, Poddala; Extent: 0.3677 Hectare. 
 
All  persons themselves who claim title, rights or 
other relations to the above-mentioned land or authorized 
persons should meet me at Divisional Secretariat, Bope 
Poddala on 17th May 2023 at 10.00 a.m.
