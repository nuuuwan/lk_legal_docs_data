No. 2326/51 –  THURSDAY, APRIL 06,  2023 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) 
 
Notice under  Section 07 
 
 
Reference No.  : RAT/LAN/10/FI/07. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No . 28 of 1964, that               
the Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude right which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land / Servitude right to be 
Acquired : 
 
All that allotment of land in total extent of about 
0.0275 Hectares, depicted as lot 207 in the Cadastral Map 
No. 521003/09/46/1 and dated 05.10.2022, prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated at Vihara Grama Niladhari 
Division, No. 546 B, within Dehiwala Mount Lavinia 
Municipal Council Limits, in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Ratmalana in Colombo District, Western 
Province and bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 207 
 
Name of the land: Bulugaha Watta, Assessment No. 
129/8, Rajamawatha; Description of the land: Homestead; 
Claimant : Government; Original Claimant : Sathkoralage 
Nanda Dissanayake and Sathkoralage Sarath 
Dissanayake; Total Extent: 0.0275 Hectare. 
 
All  persons who claim ownership, interest or any 
other connection to the aforesaid land are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.30 a.m. on 15.06.2023 at the 
Divisional Secretariat located at No. 175, Galle road, 
Ratmalana. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing in duplicate on or before the 06.06.2023, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. Deeds and other documents 
shall be produced at the inquiry.
