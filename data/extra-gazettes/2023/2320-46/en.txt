	 2.	 The Imports Control regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary                         
No. 2278/21 dated May 06, 2022 as subsequently amended by the Imports Control regulations on Payment Terms 
No. 10 of 2022 and Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 12 of 2022, is hereby further amended as follows:
	 3.	 regulation No. 4(e) is hereby inserted immediately after the regulation No. 4(d) of the Imports Control regulations 
on Payment Terms No. 10 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2285/19 dated June 24, 2022 and 
read as follows:
 “4(e) Terminal Operators, who are settling their payments for importation of goods, which are essentially required 
to maintain and operate their Terminals at any Ports / Airports in Sri Lanka to provide specified terminal 
services, from the foreign exchange earned by such terminal operators”. 
Importation of such goods shall be imported by the terminal operators on the prior recommendation of the  
secretary to the Ministry in charge of subject of Ports on or before arrival of such goods at any Ports / airports 
in sri Lanka.”
	 4.	 regulation No. 5 (i) of the Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 12 of 2022, published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2294/29 dated august 23, 2022 is hereby deleted and replaced by a new regulation No. 5 (i) and 
read as follows:
 “5 (i) request for a prior approval submitted by any Government organizations or development projects approved 
by the board of Investment of sri Lanka or development projects, awarded by any Government organizations 
exclusively for the purpose of direct usage may be considered under the Imports Control regulations on 
Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022 as amended subsequently, by the Controller General, department of Imports 
and exports Control;
	( a)	On the recommendation of the secretary to the Ministry in charge of the subject of Irrigation or Power or 
water or road development, obtained prior to arrival of such goods at any Ports / airports in sri Lanka, 
case by case basis. 
	( b)	In all other cases, except the subjects specified in (a) above, on the recommendation of the Secretary to 
the Treasury obtained prior to arrival of such goods at any Ports / airports in sri Lanka, case by case 
basis.”
	 5.	 The Controller General, the department of Imports and exports Control shall issue required “Operating Instructions” 
to the director General, sri Lanka Customs and licensed banks for effective implementation of these regulations.
 6. The director General, sri Lanka Customs shall consult the Controller General, department of Imports and exports 
Control on any importation made in violation of the regulation No. 5 (i) of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2294/29 
dated august 23, 2022, as amended above.
