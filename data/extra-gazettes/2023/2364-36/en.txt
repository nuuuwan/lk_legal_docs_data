EXCISE (SPECIAL PROvISIONS) ACT, No. 13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of the excise (special Provisions) act, No. 13 of 1989, i , ranil 
Wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do by this Order declare that :-
	 1.	(a)	 The	 Excise	Duty	on	every	article	specified	in	Column	III	of	the	Schedule	1	hereto	shall	be	payable	at	the	rate	
specified	in	the	corresponding	entry	in	Column	IV	of	the	Schedule	I;
	 	 (b)	 The	 concessionary	Excise	Duty	on	excisable	articles	specified	in	Column	II	of	the	Schedule	II	hereto	shall	be	
payable	at	the	rate	specified	in	Coloum	III	of	the	Schedule	II	wherever	applicable;	and
	 	 (c)	 wherever	 two	rates	are	specified	in	Column	IV	of	the	Schedule	I	and	Coloum	III	of	the	schedule	II	hereto,	the	
applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the excise duty payable shall be the rate which yields the higher 
amount payable as excise duty.
 2. The Orders made under section 3 of the excise (special Provisions) act, No. 13 of 1989 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notifications No. 2312/68 of 31.12.2022, No. 2334/23 of 31.05.2023, No. 2336/69 of 
16.06.2023 and No. 2338/58 of 30.06.2023, are hereby rescinded.
