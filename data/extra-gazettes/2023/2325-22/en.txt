I hereby inform that the Sri Lanka Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a pubic 
purpose under sub-section (1) of the Land Acquisition 
Act as amended by the land Acquisition (Amendment) 
Act, No. 28 of 1964. 
 
  Details of lands to be acquiried:   
 
 
The portion of land 0.0253 Hectare in extent, depicted 
as lot No. 526 in F.V.P. 2 47 situated in Bambaragama 
Grama Seva Niladhari Division No. 70, Bandarawela 
Asst. Govt. Agent’s Division, Badulla Distict, Uva 
Province. 
 
Boundaries : 
 
North: Bambaragama Ampitigodawatta claimed by 
Land Reform Commission; East: Road (Provincial 
R.D.A); South: Bambaragama Ampitigodawatta claimed 
by Land Reform Commission and Road (Provincial 
R.D.A); West: Bambaragama Ampitigodawatta claimed 
by Land Reform Commission. 
 
Claimant : Chairman Land Reform Commission 
Gregory road, Colombo. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
of Bandarawela on the 05th day of May 2023 at 9.00 
a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of March 2023, 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
