WHEREAS the office of member of Dehiaththakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Marakkala 
Manage Ajith Rohana Silva the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that Number  19 Nawamedagama of Dehiaththakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to 
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ekanayaka Mudiyanselage Ranjith Ekanayaka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Dehiaththakandiya 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Ekanayaka Mudiyanselage Ranjith Ekanayaka was elected as the member of the Number 19 Nawamedagama 
of Dehiaththakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
