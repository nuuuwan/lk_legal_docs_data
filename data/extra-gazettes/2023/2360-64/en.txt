REGISTRATION  Of  PERSONS   ACT,  No.  32  Of  1968 
Order under Section 34
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of Persons (amendment) act, No. 8 of 2016, I, Tiran 
alles, Minister of Public Security of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, alter the date of 
expiration published in Gazette No. 1979/40 of august 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of Section 6, 9, 14, 16 
and 17 of the Registration of Persons act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the aforesaid act (other than the 
Registration of Persons Regulation 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6  of  November, 20, 1990) to be 31.12.2024, 
until necessary infrastructure, arrangements and technological methodologies are made to.
 Tiran alles,
 Minister of Public Security.
