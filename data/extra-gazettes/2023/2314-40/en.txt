WHEREAS the office of  member of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Thuraisamy 
Nadarajasingam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward No. 11 Kanthapuram of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Punniyamoorthy Vijayakumaran to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Jesuthasan Jeniton Returning Officer of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Punniyamoorthy 
Vijayakumaran was elected as the member of the Ward No. 11 Kanthapuram of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.
