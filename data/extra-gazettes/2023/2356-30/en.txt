by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section  25a  of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i,  dr. a. d. Susil 
Premajayantha, the minister of education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid  act, in respect of 
the Gateway College (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and 
bearing registration Number Pv 7161 and located at No. 185, Koswatta road, rajagiriya do by this order subject to such 
conditions as specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize Gateway College (Pvt) Ltd as a Degree Awarding Institute for the 
purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule 
ii hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in Column ii of the Schedule ii hereto :-
SChedULe i
Conditions
 1.  The Gateway College (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “institute”) shall, when admitting students for the 
degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours with a minimum of 
120 Credits (SLQF Level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of ;
 (a) at least three (3)  Simple Passes (S) in any stream in one and the same sitting at the G.C.e. (advance Level) 
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
