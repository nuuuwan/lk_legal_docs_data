Local Government Notifications
Pradeshiya  sabha -  Giribawa 
It is hereby notified for the public information that it has been adopted under the resolution number 504 of the Secretary 
of the Pradeshiya Sabha held on 06. 07. 2023 at Pradeshiya Sabha Giribawa that all the roads and thoroughfares for which 
legal action had not been filed in a proper judicial Court before the date specified or roads and thoroughfares confirmed 
as the roads and thoroughfares that should be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha Giribawa as per the Decree of a Judicial Court 
in which a case had been filed before the said date and not mentioned in the following schedule from among the roads 
and thoroughfares given in the list of the schedule of the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 
2326/53 on 07.04.2023, shall be confirmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Giribawa in terms of 
orders made by the series of "Orders on publication of notifications specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and 
thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province" published by Extraordinary Gazette 
Paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order 
by Section 24(1) of  Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
   It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares is presented in this schedule and every road and 
thoroughfare specified therein shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares vested in the Pradeshiya Sabha Giribawa 
under Sub-section (5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act. 
                    H. M. S. H
eratH, 
 Secretary and Officer of executing Powers and Duties,
 Pradeshiya Sabha Giribawa.
Pradeshiya Sabha, Giribawa, 
06th July, 2023.
