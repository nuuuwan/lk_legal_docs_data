RuLes
 1. these rules will be enforced to amend the rules governing procurement affairs relevant to the Western Provincial  
Council Fund.
 2. these rules shall be cited as the Rules for Amendment of Procurement Affairs relevant to the Western Provincial  
Council Fund.
 3. section 12 of the Rules governing the Procurement Affairs relevant to Western Provincial Council Fund included in the 
Extraordinary Gazette of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka No. 2186/26 and dated 30th July 2020 will 
hereby be amended, numbered as sub-section 12(1) and substituted consequently with the following sub-sections.
 (ii) An appeal relevant to a Procurement decision presented under this section, when forwarded to the governor 
of Western Province by the appellant, the relevant appeal should be forwarded after depositing money to 
the governor's secretary of Western Province or to the Procurement entity relevant to the said procurement 
according to the value of the procurement which accord to the following mentioned values, together with a 
photocopy of the receipt relevant to it.
Value of Procurement Deposit Amount
Over Rs. 250 Million Rs. 100,000.00
Rs. 200-250 Million  Rs.  75,000.00
Rs. 100-200 Million  Rs.  50,000.00
Rs.  25-100 Million  Rs.  25,000.00
Less than Rs. 25 Million  Rs.  15,000.00
 (iii) unless the appeal has been forwarded with a copy of the relevant receipt obtained after duly depositing the 
deposit money, the relevant appeal should not be considered as an appeal made properly under this section and 
no action should be taken thereon.
 (iv) Where the decision of the Procurement Committee relating to the relevant procurement activity changes by 
an appeal forwarded under this section, measures should be taken to release the relevant deposit money to 
the appellant, whereas, if the appellant's appeal is rejected, relevant deposit money should be credited to the 
Provincial Council Fund.
 4. should any inconsistency arise among sinhala, english and tamil texts of these rules, sinhala text shall prevail.
