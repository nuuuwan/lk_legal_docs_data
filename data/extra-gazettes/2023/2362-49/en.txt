SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  Of  1973
I,  Harin Fernando, the minister of sports and youth affairs, under section 32 read with section 33 of sports Law, No. 25 
of 1973, do hereby revoke, rescind and cancel the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2356/43 dated November 05, 2023, with effect 
from december 12, 2023, and thereby the interim committee appointed for the purpose of exercising the powers, functions 
and duties of sri Lanka cricket shall stand dissolved.
 H
arin Fernando (M. P.),
 minister of sports and youth affairs.
at colombo,
