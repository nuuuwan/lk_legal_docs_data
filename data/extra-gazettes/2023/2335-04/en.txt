i, Lalith u. Gamage (Attorney-at-Law), Governor of Central Province, as per the powers vested in me under Sub-section 2(2) 
of the entertainment tax ordinance to be read with Section 2(1)(a) of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, 
No. 12 of 1989, hereby announce that the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2135/38 dated 06.08.2019 
of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, adopted by the Kandy municipal Council on 20.12.2018 as per the 
provisions mentioned under Sub-section (i) of Section 2 of the entertainment tax ordinance No. 12 of 1946 (Chapter 267) 
as amended  by the entertainment tax (Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 1984 and approved by me, is amended as mentioned 
in the Schedule hereto according to the resolution passed under decision No. 8(24) at the General meeting of the Kandy 
municipal Council held on 31.10.2022.
Furthermore, i hereby announce that this percentage shall be considered as the entertainment tax Percentage for all 
activities mentioned in this Schedule and for every chargeable activity, until 31st december 2023, till a new order is made 
in this regard.
 LaLith U. GamaGe, Attorney-at-Law, 
 Governor,
 Central Province.
on this 03rd day of march, 2023,
