PILGRImAGES  ORDINANCE 
It is hereby notified that the Annual Esala Festival 2023 of Historic Welle Dewalaya in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Yaddehimulla, Divisional Secretary's Division of Habaraduwa, District of Galle, Southern Province, will be held during 
the period from Tuesday the 1st of August, 2023 to Monday the 7th of August, 2023 as per the order published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2125/24 dated 28.05.2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms 
of the regulations of the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175).
 
Shantha WeeraSinghe,
 District Secretary/Government Agent,
 Administrative District, Galle.
At the District Secretariat, Galle,
