IMPORTS AND EXPORTS CONTROL  ACT, No. 1 OF 1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-Section 4(1) and Section 14 of the Imports and 
Exports (Control) Act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by Act, No. 48 of 1985 and Act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, 
the Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies promulgate following Regulations.

REGULATIONS
1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 03 of 2023”. 
2. The Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2278/21 
dated May 06, 2022 as amended is hereby further amended.

3. The Regulation No. 4 of the Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 15 of 2022 published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2297/79 dated September 16, 2022 and the Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 
19 of 2022 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2304/04 dated October 31, 2022, are hereby repealed without 
prejudice to importation of goods under the said Regulations.
4. Date of arrival of import consignments of Wheat Flour ( HS Code of 1101.00.10) at the Port/Airport in Sri Lanka under 
the Open Account or Consignment Account Payment terms on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry of Trade, 
Commerce and Food Security as authorized by the Regulation No. 4 (d) of the Imports Control Regulations on Payment 
Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette No. 2278/21 dated May 06, 2022, as amended by the Imports Control 
Regulations on Payment Terms No. 10 of 2022 published in the Gazette No. 2285/19 dated June 24, 2022, is hereby 
extended until January 31, 2023.
5. Importation of Wheat Flour, classi ﬁ ed under the HS Code of 1101.00.10  in the Schedule I to the Imports Control 
Regulations on Payment Terms No. 10 of 2022 as amended, shall also be exempted from the Regulation No. 6 of the 
Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette No. 2278/21 dated May 06, 
2022, until January 31, 2023.
6. The Director General, Sri Lanka Customs shall consult the Secretary, Ministry of Trade, Commerce and Food Security 
on any importation made in violation of the Regulation No. 4 above.
7. Controller General of Imports and Exports Control is also hereby authorized to issue necessary “Operational Instructions” 
to the Sri Lanka Customs and Licensed Banks for implementation of these Regulations to achieve the objectives of 
these Regulations.
