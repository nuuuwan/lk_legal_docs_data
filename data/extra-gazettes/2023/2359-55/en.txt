No. 2359/55 –  FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 24,  2023 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section 7 
 
 
Ministry of Land’s Reference No.: 4-3/7/2020/hw/212. 
My Reference No. : 3/6/2019/9/AD1/1. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends  to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
The allotment of land depicted in the Preliminary Plan 
No. Ku 2527 dated 30.11.2022 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, having the name and the extent as mentioned 
below, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 888 
of Dewatagedara, in the Divi sional Secretary’s Division 
of Polgahawela, in Kurunegala District, in North Western 
Province, described as follows : 
  
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Pinkumbura; Original Claimant: 
Algama Sri Sangaraja Rajamaha Viharaya; Extent: 
0.0922 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforementioned land/
servitude on which servitude is to be acquired are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat,  
Polgahawela, on the day of 27. 12. 2023 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Moreover, it is hereby requested to notify me in 
writing  (in duplicate) on or before 13.12.2023, the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
computation of such amount.
