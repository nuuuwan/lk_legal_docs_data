WHEREAS the office of member of Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Ganesasundaram 
Kulamani the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that Number  10 - Valathappitty of Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Seeniththamby Rajeswary to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Sammanthurai 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Seeniththamby Rajeswary was selected as the member of the Number 10 - Valathappitty of Sammanthurai 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
