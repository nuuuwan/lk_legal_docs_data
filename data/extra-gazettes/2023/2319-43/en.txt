All persons whom interested in the aforesaid lands/
servitudes already acquired, whether their names stated in 
the Gazette or not are hereby required to appear 
personally, or through an agent duly authorized in 
writing, (in duplicate) before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Weerabugedara on the day of 24.04.2023 with the nature 
of their claims as deeds for compensation for the said 
lands/servitudes, the amount of compensation and 
computation details of such amount in writing. 
 
Further the person should appear for an inquiry under 
section 09 of the Act, commence from 24.04.2023 
onwards. The date of inquiry and the relevant documents 
to be submitted at the inquiry such as deeds may duly 
informed by me. 
 
If an agent present at the inquiry, original claimant of 
the land must be duly authorized in writing. Without such 
a letter statement or evidence may not be recorded from 
such an agent.
