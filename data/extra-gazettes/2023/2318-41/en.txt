of powers vested under Section 223 and 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 2 of Provincial

ORDERS
 1. These orders are published to authorize Pradeshiya Sabhas i n the Uva Province to spend further time 
to take action pursuant to Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act and to prescribe the procedure to publish 
notiﬁ cations stating the name list of roads and paths in terms of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act and 
to make provisions on the matters related or incidental thereto considering the special circumstances 
that roads and paths which shall be deemed to be vested in Pradeshiya Sabhas in terms of Section 23 
of Pradeshiya Sabha Act should be permanently marked and demarcated and designs of such roads and 
paths should have been prepared in terms of Section 24 of the said Act within three years of coming into 
eﬀ ect of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, that is before 11th May 1990, and even though it has 
been ordered to publish notices on them as prescribed in the orders, and since necessary orders have not 
been formulated yet and the time that the notices should be made is already over.

Order 5 (1) 
Allocation of Code Numbers for Roads
The following matters should be included into the Code assigned for a certain road deemed as to be vested in the Sabha:
Code: Province/ Pradeshiya Sabha/ Base line Road / road owned by th e Local Government Institution/ By road
  Example : UP/BP/B247/04/00 or 08
 * “UP” Should be used for the code assigned in respect of the province
 *  The code prescribed for the Pradeshiya Sabha should be assi gned under the directions of the Commissioner of Local 
Government
 * Base line road means a road owned by the Road Development Au thority or Provincial Road Development Authority. 
The number assigned to that route in relation to that particular road in a notiﬁ cation published in the Gazette by that 
particular institution should be used for this purpose.
