Order
 01. These regulations shall be enforced to provide for determining the fees applicable for issuing a license to register 
a business of a pawnbroker and to determine the security deposits payable for a business of a pawnbroker under the 
Western Provincial Pawnbrokers' Statute No. 01 of 2015 and to repeal the Western Provincial Pawnbrokers' rules No. 
01 of 2015 published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka No. 1937/20 
dated 22nd October, 2015.
 02. These regulations shall be cited as the regulations regarding the businesses of the Pawnbrokers' No. 01 of 2023.
 03. The fees and deposits to be charged for the purposes stated in the Section 07 (03) of the Western Provincial P awnbrokers' 
Statue No. 01 of 2015 shall be as mentioned below.
Purpose Amount
a.   registration of a pawnbrokers' business rs.   50,000.00
b.   Annual License fees for a pawnbrokers' business rs.   25,000.00
c.   Security deposit payable to carry out the pawnbrokers' business rs. 500,000.00
 04. The amount to be deposited under Section 02 above shall be deposited only to the state owned banks, i.e., People's 
bank, bank of ceylon and National Savings bank.
 05. Western Provincial pawnbrokers' rules No. 01 of 2015 published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the democratic 
Socialist r epublic of Sri Lanka No. 1937/20 dated 22nd October 2015 shall be repealed from the date these r egulations 
shall come into effect, without prejudice to the actions taken under those rules.
 06. In the event of any inconsistencey between Sinhala, English and Tamil Texts of these orders, the Sinhala text shall 
prevail.
