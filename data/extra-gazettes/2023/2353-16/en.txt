ImPORTS  AND  EXPORTS  CONTROL  ACT,  No. 1  Of  1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-Section 4(1), Section 6 and Section 14 of the 
Imports and exports (control) act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by act, No. 48 of 1985 and act, No. 28 of 1987, I, ranil 
Wickremesinghe, the minister of Finance, economic Stabilization and National Policies promulgate following regulations.
 Ranil Wickremesinghe,
 minister of Finance, economic   Stabilization and National Policies.
ministry of Finance, economic Stabilization and National Policies,
colombo 01,
october 09, 2023.
REGULATIONS
1. these regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 14 of 2023”. 
2. the Imports and exports (control) regulations No. 02 of 2023, published in the Gazette No. 2312/78 dated January 
01, 2023, as amended subsequently is hereby further amended.
