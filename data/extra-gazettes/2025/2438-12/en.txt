bANk Of CEYLON  ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER  397)
Order under Sub-section (2) of Section 7
By virtue of the powers vested in me under paragraph 3 of Article 44 of the Constitution of Sri Lanka read with  
Subsection (2) of Section 7 of the Bank of Ceylon Ordinance (Chapter 397), I, Anura Kumara Dissanayake, President of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order determine that the paid-up capital of the Bank of Ceylon be 
increased from Rupees Twenty-Five Thousand Million (Rs. 25,000,000,000) to Rupees Twenty-Five Thousand and Seven 
Hundred and Thirty Million. (Rs. 25,730,000,000).
	 Anura	Kumara	Dissanayake,
 President.
 
Colombo,
May 27, 2025.
