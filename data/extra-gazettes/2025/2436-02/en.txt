Customs  Ordinance  (Chapter  235)
REGULaTION  UNdER  SEcTION 101
determination of customs Value (Price) for Motor Vehicles
IN pursuance of the powers vested in me by Section 101 (1) (h) of the customs Ordinance (chapter 235), I, anura Kumara 
dissanayake, Minister of Finance, Planning and Economic development, do hereby further amend the Regulation promulgated 
and published in the Gazette Notification No. 1971/10 dated June 14,  2016 to prescribe a basis for calculation of the customs 
Value of Motor Vehicles classified under HS Headings of 87.02, 87.03, 87.04, 87.05 and 87.11 by insertion of the below-
mentioned clause (1) (c). This Regulation shall be effective from May 16, 2025.
 (1) (c) For the purpose of calculation of applicable duties only at the time of importation in respect of the customs Value 
of a finished motor vehicle classified under HS Headings of 87.02, 87.03, 87.04, 87.05 and 87.11, which has been 
manufactured/ assembled with the use of new motor vehicle components under a Manufacture in Bond facility provided 
under Section 69 of the customs Ordinance, the domestic value addition relating to manufacturing/ assembling of the 
said motor vehicle recommended by the Minister in charge of the Subject of Industries as a percentage value should 
be excluded.
