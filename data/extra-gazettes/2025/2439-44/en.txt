Local Government Notifications
SECTION  66 C OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE,  CHAPTER (262)
Notice of the First Meeting of the Council
I, Erandi Umanga Mendis, the Commissioner of Local Government, Southern Province, do hereby inform that by virtue of 
the powers vested in me by Section 66C of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance Chapter 262, as amended by the Local 
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, the first meeting for the purpose of electing the Mayor/Deputy 
Mayor, Chairman/Vice Chairman for the Local Authorities listed in the Schedule below within the Southern Province, 
is scheduled to be held at the specified time on the date correspondingly indicated under the schedule below at the place 
mentioned therein.
	 Erandi	Umanga	Mendis,

 Southern Province.
