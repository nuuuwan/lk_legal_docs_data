FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  V ALIKAMAM  WEST  
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Eelam People's Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which Sivaguru 
Balakrishnan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of 
the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sellamuthu Selvakkumar to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Rajendra Saseelan, returning officer of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sellamuthu Selvakkumar 
was selected as the member of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 R. Saseelan,
 Returning Officer,
 Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
24th July, 2025.
