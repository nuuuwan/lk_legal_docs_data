PART IV (B) — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  THE MEMBER  OF    
MANMUNAI  PATTU  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Under Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
WHEREAS the Office of the member of Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi the recognized politial party to which Sellathurai 
Manikkarasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha of Ward Number 04 Arayampathy South who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS  the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sujeetha Rajaravitharma to fill such a vacancy ;
THEREFORE , I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that ward Number 
04 Arayampathy South was elected as the member of the Sujeetha Rajaravitharma of Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
 M. B. M. Sufyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
31st July, 2025.
