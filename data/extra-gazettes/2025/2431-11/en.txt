FILLING OF A V ACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13(B) OF ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF 
THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
WHereAs, the secretary General of parliament has informed the election Commission, under section 64(1) of the 
parliamentary elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the tenth parliament by reason 
of the death of Mr. Hapu Arachchige Kosala Nuwan Jayaweera, who was declared elected as a Member of parliament for 
the electoral district No. 22-Kegalle under section 62 of the aforesaid Act, 
	 WHEREAS,	 the	Returning	Officer	for	the	electoral	district	which	returned	the	said	member	was	directed	by	the	
Election	Commission	under	Section	64(1)	of	the	Parliamentary	Elections	Act,	No.	1	of	1981,	to	fill	this	vacancy	as	provided	
for under sub Article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution: 
	 AND	 WHEREAS,	the	said	Returning	Officer	acting	under	Section	64(2)	of	the	Parliamentary	Elections	Act,	No.	1	of	
1981, and in terms of sub Article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution has made a return in Form "M" set out in the First schedule 
to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Samantha Ranasingha was declared elected as a 
Member of parliament for the said electoral district;
