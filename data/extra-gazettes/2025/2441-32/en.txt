FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF THE BALANGODA PRADESHIYA  
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION   66B(2) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTION (AMENDMENT) ACT N o. 16 OF 
2017
WHEREAS the office of Chairman in Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Jathika Jana Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Durayalaya Ranjith 
Udaya Kumara the former chairman belonged, was required by me u nder Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who b elongs to such party who shall be declared elected as 
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has c ommunicated his decision to nominate 
Gama Ethige Ariyadasa to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Suranga Ambagahathenne Returning Officer of Balangoda Pradeshiy a Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Ch apter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Gama Ethige A riyadasa elected as Chairman of the Balangoda 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
