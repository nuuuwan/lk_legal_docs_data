I do hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:  
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:   
The allotment of land described below depicted in 
sheet, No. 02 of block 02 of Surveyor General's Cadastral 
Map No. 820493 situated at the Village of Lenama in 
Grama Niladhari Division of Lenama South in Weligam 
Korale North, Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Akuressa in the District of Matara in the Southern 
Province. 
 
Lot No. 1 - Name of the Land: 
Kirimatithudallewatta - Extent: 0.0341 Hectare.   
North: Lot No. 194A of F.V.P. 535; East: Lot No. 
194A of F.V.P. 535; South: Lot No. 194A of F.V.P. 535; 
West: Lot No. 194A of F.V.P. 535.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before 
me at Divisional Secretariat Akuressa on the 02nd day of 
April 2025 at 9.30 a.m. 
 
As well, It is hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of March 
2025 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
