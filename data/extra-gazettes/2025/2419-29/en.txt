Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of Indigenous Medicine (Amendment) Ordinance,                  
No. 01 of 2025 and shall come into operation with effect from 15th January 2025.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of Indigenous Medicine Ordinance No. 05 of  2017, published in Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2048/46 of december 08, 2017 is hereby amended as follows :
 (1) in paragraph 9 thereof -
  (a) by the repeal of sub-subparagraph (e) of subparagraph (1) and the substitution therefor of the following:-
   “(e) the commissioner-General for ayurveda;”;
