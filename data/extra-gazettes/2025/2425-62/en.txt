LOCAL  AuThORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER 262)
Order under Section 76A
THe election Commission, in the exercise of the powers vested in it by Section 76a  of the Local authorities elections 
Ordinance, hereby clarifies that the provision in Section 28 (2A) (b) of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, 
No. 30 of 2003, which states “not less than fifty per centum of the total number of candidates nominated in the Additional 
Nomination Paper”, shall be interpreted to mean “Not less than fifty per centum of the candidates in the second nomination 
paper shall consist of women candidates.”
