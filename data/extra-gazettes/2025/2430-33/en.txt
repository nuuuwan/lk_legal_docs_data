THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of 
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial 
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 17.02.2023 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2320/53 dated 22.02.2023 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter speciﬁ ed 
in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour dated 31.01.2023.
     Mr. W.A.D.P. Perera, T/5, Diyawanna Garden, Thalangama - South, Battaramulla.  
 and
      National Water Supply and Drainage Board, No. 14, Galle Road, Rathmalana.
was referred to Mr. Kumarasingha Amaranath Perera Rajakaruna Attorney-at-law for settlement by arbitration. 
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, due to Mr. Kumarasingha Amaranath Perera 
Rajakaruna Attorney - at - Law, the Arbitrator informing that he is resigning from this dispute, Dr. Anil Jayantha Fernando, 
Minister of Labour do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said 
Order dated 17.02.2023.
 Dr. A
NIL JAYANTHA FERNANDO,
 Minister of Labour.
Colombo.
25th March 2025,

Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter speciﬁ ed in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour

     Mr. W.A.D.P. Perera, T/5, Diyawanna Garden, Thalangama - South, Battaramulla.  
 and
      National Water Supply and Drainage Board, No. 14, Galle Road, Rathmalana.

4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended 
by Acts Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 
hereby appoint.

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. W.A.D.P. Perera working as a Driver in the National Water Supply and Drainage Board has been caused injustice 
by being transferred as a punishment, before the concusion of the disciplinary inquiry conducted against him and if so, to 
what relief he is entitled.
