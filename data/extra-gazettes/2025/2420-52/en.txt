No. 2420/52 –  THURSDAY, JANUARY 23,  2025 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: OPDS/LND/08/ACQ/01/01. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4/3/5/2018/HA/80. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
  
Described following Lot of land containing in extent 
of about 0.9385 Hectare, Showing Lot No. 01 of 
Cadastral Map No. 620665 in Extra No. 01 and Sheet  
No. 01, Hunuwalawaththa in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of Hunuwala North in Helapalle Paththuwa in 
Meda Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Opanayaka of Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
 
Schedule 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Hunuwalawaththa; Claimants of 
the Land: 01. Basnayaka Nilame, Sabaragamuwa Maha 
Saman Devalaya, Rathnapura, 02. Superintendent, 
Kahawaththa Plantation, Hunuwala, 03. State Plantation 
Corporation, 11th Floor, Sethsiripaya Second Stage, 
Battaramulla, 04. Land Reforms Commission, Good Shed 
Road, New Town, Embilipitiya; Extent: 0.9385 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secret ariat of Openayaka on the 
02nd day of June in 2025 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 26th day of May, in 2025, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
 H. Dඁൺආආං඄ൺ Sൺඍඁൺඋൺඌංඇ඀ඁൺ, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Opanayaka. 
 
On 21st of January, 2025, 
At the Divisional Secretariat, Opanayaka.
