08. Efficiency Bar Examinations and Departmental Tests :
 8.4 Departmental Tests
   I.  the details of the departmental test relevant to each post shall be included in the scheme of Recruitment 
of the respective Post.
   II.  the respective departmental test shall be conducted by the relevant department and shall consist of at least 
two technical subjects and a subject related to departmental rules and regulations, acts and procedures. All 
departmental examinations shall be written and practical, and each written question paper of not less than 
three hours in duration shall be provided for each subject. the time limits applicable for the practical tests 
shall be determined by the respective departments.
	 	 	III.		 Every	 officer	shall	complete	both	the	first	and	the	second	Departmental	Tests	within	not	more	than	two	
sittings to pass the departmental tests. A minimum of 40% shall be obtained for each question paper relevant 
to the technical subjects of the departmental test while the average score for those subjects shall be 50% 
or higher. A minimum of 50% shall be obtained to pass the subject related to departmental regulations.
	 	 	 IV.		 An	officer	who	possesses	a	technical	qualification	relevant	to	the	post	mentioned	in	Section	7.2.1.3	(b)	
shall	be	exempt	from	the	technical	subjects	of	the	departmental	test.	Such	an	officer	should	pass	the	subject	
relevant to the departmental regulations in order to pass the departmental test.
       10.1.2.  Upon passing the departmental test
      10.1.2.1. Qualifications to be fulfilled.
	 	 	 I.	 	Should	have	been	confirmed	in	the	Service.
	 	 	 II.	 Should	 have	completed	active	and	satisfactory	period	of	service	of	at	least	five	(05)	years	in	Grade	
III	of	the	service	and	earned	five	(05)	salary	increments.	
   III. should not have subjected to any disciplinary punishment in terms of the Public service Commission 
Circular No. 01/2020.
	 	 	 IV.	 Should	have	acquired	proficiency	in	the	other	official	language	at	the	respective	level.
	 	 	 V.	 Should	have	passed	the	first	efficiency	bar	examination	on	the	prescribed	date
	 	 	 VI.	 Should	have	passed	the	first	Departmental	 Test.
     10.1.2.2. Method of promotion :
	 	 	 1.	 Each	officer	who	has	completed	three	years	of	active	service	shall	sit	for	the	first	departmental	test.
	 	 	 2.	 An	 officer	who	passes	the	first	departmental	test	within	the	first	two	consecutive	sittings	held	after	
fulfilling	the	requirement	mentioned	in	(1)	above	shall	be	promoted	to	Grade	II	from	the	date	on	
which	he	fulfills	the	qualifications	specified	in	10.1.2.1.	above.
            10.2.2. Upon passing the departmental test
     10.2.2.1 Qualifications to be fulfilled
   I. should have completed a minimum of four (04) years of active and satisfactory service in Grade II 
of the service and earned four (04) salary increments.

15. Interim provisions :
 the facts mentioned under No. 15 shall be renumbered as 15.1
 15.2	 These	interim	provisions	shall	apply	only	to	the	officers	who	are	in	service	on	the	effective	date	of	this	
amendment.
	 15.2.1	 An	officer	in	Grade	III	who	passes	the	first	departmental	test	within	the	first	two	sittings	of	the	tests	
conducted after the effective date of this amendment will be promoted to Grade II, effective from the 
date	either	from	the	effective	date	of	this	amendment	or	the	date	of	fulfillment	of	the	qualifications	
mentioned in section 10.1.2.1, whichever occurs later.
	 15.2.2.	 The	active	and	satisfactory	service	period	in	Grade	III	of	an	officer	promoted	to	Grade	II	as	specified	
in	sub-section	15.2.1	above	either	from	the	date	of	fulfillment	of	the	qualifications	mentioned	in	
10.1.2.1. from the effective date of this amendment or from 01.06.2018, whichever occurs later, 
to the effective date of this amendment, shall be considered as an active and satisfactory period of 
service in Grade II, only for the promotional purposes.
	 15.2.3.	 An	officer	in	Grade	II	who	passes	the	second	departmental	test	within	the	first	two	sittings	conducted	
subsequent to the effective date of this amendment will be promoted to Grade I, effective from either 
the	effective	date	of	this	amendment	or	the	date	on	which	the	qualifications	mentioned	in	section	
10.2.2.1 are completed, whichever occurs later.
	 15.2.4.	 An	officer	in	Grade	II	who	passes	the	first	and	second	departmental	tests	within	the	first	two	tests	
conducted subsequent to the effective date of this amendment will be promoted to Grade I, effective 
from	either	the	effective	date	of	this	amendment	or	the	date	on	which	the	qualifications	mentioned	
therein,	other	than	the	qualification	in	section	10.2.2.1	(1)	are	completed,	whichever	occurs	later.
 15.2.5. In terms of the section 15.1 of the service Minute of the sri Lanka technological service published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1930/12 dated 01.09.2015 of the democratic socialist Republic 
of sri Lanka the interim provisions were in effect until 1
st	of	June	2018,	but	the	officers	who	were	
unable to sit any of the departmental tests due to the departments not conducting departmental tests 
shall grant two attempts under the implementation of the new amendments of service minute and 
the	officers	who	were	unable	to	sit	one	of	the	departmental	tests	shall	grant	one	opportunity	under	
the implementation of the new amendments of the service minute.
