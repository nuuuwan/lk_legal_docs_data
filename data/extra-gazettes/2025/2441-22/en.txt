SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
First Supplementary Financial Statement - 2025  
GRANTING PERMISSION BY VIRTUE OF POWERS VESTED IN ME UNDER SECTION 27(A) OF PROVINCIAL 
COUNCIL ACT, No. 42 OF 1987 AS THE TERM OF THE SABARAGAMUWA PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF THE 
DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA ENDED ON 26TH SEPTEMBER, 2017
2025
1. This permission may be cited as ‘‘Permission to disburse under the First supplementary Financial Statement - 2025 of 
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council” and come in to operation from 04.04.2025.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Sixty One Billion Seven Hundred 
Thirty Eight Million One Hundred Thirty Eight Thousand only (Rs. 61,738,138,000.00) for the service of the period 
beginning on 01st of January, 2025 and ending 31st December 2025, which is specified as ‘‘Financial Y ear 2025” in 
the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue - Expenditure Estimates prepared for the year 2025, is hereby amended 
as, Rupees Sixty Two Billion Two Hundred Eight Million Fifty Five Thousand Eighty Two and Twelve Cents  only 
(Rs.62,208,055,082.12) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers to 
disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
3. Accordingly the first schedule of the Annual Financial Statement and revenue- expenditure estimates - 2025, is amended 
as the first schedule hereof and grant permission to disburse the same.

AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council 
to disburse as per the First Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2025, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
Section 27 (a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of 
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secratary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council to spend a sum of Sixty Two Billion Two Hundred Eight Million Fifty Five Thousand Eighty Two and Twelve 
Cents only (Rs. 62,208,055,082.12) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund, which is estimated for the 
expenses of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the one year period that starts on 01st of January, 2025 and end on  
31st of December, 2025.
