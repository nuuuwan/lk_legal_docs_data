THE  CENSUS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  143)
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Census Ordinance (Chapter 143), i, Anura Kumara Dissanayake, 
Minister of finance, Planning and Economic Development, direct that an Economic Census covering Agriculture, industry, 
Trade and Services sectors of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka be taken in years two thousand twenty five/  
two thousand twenty six.
