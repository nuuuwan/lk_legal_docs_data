Revocation Under Section 39 (1)
THE  land specified morefully in the Order No. 308 of 2014 
dated 31.10.2014 made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 
of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1887/41 dated 07.11.2014
