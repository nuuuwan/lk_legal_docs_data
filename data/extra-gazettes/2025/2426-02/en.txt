IN exercise of the powers vested in the Election commission under Section 26 of the Local authorities Elections ordinance 
(chapter 262) and subject to Section 3 of the Local authorities Elections (Special Provisions) act, No. 1 of 2025, nomination 
notice for the election of members to Local authorities in each administrative district, situated within the administrative 
District specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto and specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the said 
Schedule, is hereby issued on 03rd March 2025.
It is hereby announced that deposits will be accepted from the day of the issuance of the nomination notice, to the day before 
the last date for receipt of nominations, i. e. 12.00 noon on 19.03.2025 and (during this period, deposits will  not be accepted 
on the Poya Day of 13.03.2025 and on Saturdays and Sundays of 08.03.2025, 09.03.2025, 15.03.2025 and 16.03.2025.
