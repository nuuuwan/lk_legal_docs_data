ISSUANCE  OF  LICENSES
THE Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka, exercising its powers under Section 13(1) of the Sri Lanka Electricity  
Act, No. 20 of 2009 (as amended), has extended and issued new licenses to the Ceylon Electricity Board for the following 
activities. These new licenses will be valid for a period of 15 years, effective from October 08, 2024. The terms and 
conditions of these licenses are available on the Commission’s official website at this link -
https://www.pucsl.gov.lk/electricity/licensee/energy-efficiency/.
