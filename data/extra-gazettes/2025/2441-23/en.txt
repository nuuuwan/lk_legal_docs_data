Regulations
   The regulations being amended time to time made under section 15 of Sri Lanka Railways Welfare Society Act and 
published in Gazette Notification No. 10493 dated 6th of February 1953 are further amended as follows:
Regulation 33 :  Increment of loan facility Rs. 100,000/- which being paid at now for members who are in membership 
more than 1 year by Rs. 200,000/- with effective from 22.11.2024.
9(3) a : If the membership period in the society is less than 18 years, pension bonus Rs. 20,000/- which being paid 
at now is increased upto Rs. 25,000/- with effective from 22.11.2024.
