LOcAL AuThORITIES ELEcTIONS - 2025
Notice under Section 27A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262)
It is hereby notified that in accordance with the decision taken by the Election Commission on 11.03.2025 in terms of 
Section 27E of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), Mr. Sirisena Rajapaksa has been accepted as the 
Secretary of the Sri Lanka Progressive Front Party, as stated in Gazette No. 2426/10 dated 03 March 2025 issued by the 
Election Commission, which was a recognized political party that have disputes for the office of Secretary, for the 2025 
Local Authorities Elections only.
