PART IV (B)  — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
Notice under Section 76A
WHEREAS the Local Authorities Elections have been concluded and the res ults released, it is required that each party 
or group shall, without delay, notify the Election Commission o f the names of the members to be elected to that party or 
group, including the women members, however, although 21 days have now passed since the conclusion of the election, the 
prescribed number of members to be elected has not yet been nominated and submitted to the Election Commission:
Whereas, by virtue of the Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2424/02 dated 17.02.2025, issued by the Minister in  charge of Public 
Administration, Provincial Councils and Local Government, it has been ordered that the term of oﬃ  ce of the Local Authorities 
shall commence on 02.06.2025:
And whereas, in compliance with the directions of the Minister,  the names of all elected and nominated members shall be 
published in the Gazette prior to the commencement of the term of oﬃ   ce, and therefore each party and group is hereby 
directed to submit the names of all such members to the Returni ng Oﬃ  cer of the relevant Local Authority on or before 
midnight (12:00 a.m.) on 30.05.2025.
