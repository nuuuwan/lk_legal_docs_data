 THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub - Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose.  
 
Description of the land to be  acquired:  
 
The allotments of land depicted in Sheet No. 1, Block No. 
01, in the Cadastral Map No. 321032 and dated 
30.12.2024, prepared by the Su rveyor General, situated at 
the Village of Kadiyanlena, in Kadiyanlena Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 1081, in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Pasbage Korale in Kandy District, Central 
Province and bounded as follows : 
 
Boundaries of the land Bowhill Estate Lot No.  1 in total 
extent of about 1.1665 Hectares. 
 
North by : Baharanda Oya and Lot No.  1 in P. P. Maha 
1849; East By : Lot No.  1 in P. P. Maha 1849; South by : 
Lot No.  1 in P. P. Maha 1849; West by : Baharanda Oya. 
 
Boundaries of the land Bowhill Estate Lot No. 2 in total 
extent of about 0.0190 Hectare. 
North by : Lot No.  1 in P. P. Maha 1849 and Baharanda 
Oya; East by : Baharanda Oya; South by : Baharanda Oya 
and Lot No.  1 in P. P. Maha 1849; West by : Lot No.  1 
in P. P. Maha 1849 
 
All persons who claim ownership, interest or any 
other connection to the aforesaid land/servitude hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me on 03.04.2025 at 9.00 
a.m. at the Divisional Secretariat, Pasbage Korale 
Nawalapitiya. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing in duplicate before 30.03.2025 the nature of 
their interests in the land/lands, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.
