REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS   
Notice that the Supplementary Electoral Register 2024(3) has been certified under the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
IN terms of Section 20 A (10) of the Registration of Electors Act, notice is given on behalf of the Registering Officer of 
each electoral district that the supplementary electoral register 2024(3) including the names of registered electors, who 
has attained the age of 18 years on a specified date within the period from 01st October 2024 to 31st January 2025 has 
been certified on 01 February 2025 in accordance with the Registration of Electors Act No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the 
registration of electors (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2021 ; that the electoral register for the electoral districts mentioned 
in column I of the schedule below has been certified by the Assistant Registering Officer in the corresponding entry in  
column II thereof; that inspection may be made during normal office hours at the District Election Office/offices mentioned 
in the corresponding entry in Column III thereof.
 Saman Sri Ratnayake,
 Commissioner General of  elections,
