The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
 
The notification, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1535/12 dated February 08, 2008 issued in addition to the National 
Honours awarded to the non-Sri Lankans, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 627/8 dated September 12, 1990; 
  “Nature of the Award will be a citation and a Silver Medal studded and adorned with nine 
 kinds of Sri  Lanka Gems (Nawaratna) with the symbols of a Lotus, the globe, Sun, Moon 
 and sheaves of rice”
is hereby revised by the substitution of the words,
 “Nature of the Award will be a citation and a Silver Medal studded and adorned with nine 
 kinds of Sri Lanka Gems (Nawaratna) with the symbols of a Traditional Grain Vase, the 
 Globe, Sun, Moon and Sheaves of Rice”
       By Order of the Hon. President,
                Dr. N.S. Kumanayake,  
               Secretary to the President.
