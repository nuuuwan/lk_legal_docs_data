RAmbukkAnA   Pradeshiya  Sabha  Gazette  Notification.  Read  with  section  24  of  the  Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No 15 
of 1987, published by the Hon. Governor of the Sabaragamuwa province in the  special  Gazette  No.2316/29  and  dated 24th  
January  2023  to  publish  notices  showing  the preparation of plans and list of names of the roads narrow paths maintained 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhas  of  Sabaragamuwa  province.  In  accordance  with  the  work  assigned  to  the  Pradeshiya Sabhas 
of Sabaragamuwa province in the series of orders herein it is hereby announced for the public’s knowledge that it has been 
decided under secretary’s decision No.2024/12/30/63 dated 30.12.2024  that  the  roads  and  narrow  roads  included  in  the  
following  schedule  should  be declared as roads and narrow roads owned by the Rambukkana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
 If it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or narrow path included in the said schedule passes 
through any or part of land, to file a suit in the competent court  before the expiry of three months from  the date if this 
notification to prove his/her right to the said land.
 And it is further announced that every road and or narrow path included in the said schedule for which no case has been 
assigned before the expiry of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or narrow path owned by the 
Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha. 
 R. G. S. Niroshan,
 Secretary and implementation officer,
 Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha.
At Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha,
Rambukkana.
