DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT - EASTERN  PROVINCE
Notice on summon meeting of under the Local  Authorities  Elections  (Amendment)  Act,  No.  22  of  2012
BY virtue of power vested on me under Section 66(C) read with Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
amended by Local Authorities Elections  (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Adam Lebbe Mohamed Azmi, the Commissioner 
of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to fill the vacancy arose to the post of Chairman 
of the Manmunai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, Batticaloa District, Eastern Province at 10.00 a.m. on 07.08.2025 at Manmunai 
Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha Hall for the purpose of elect the new Chairman.
 A. L. M. Azmi,
 Commissioner of Local Government,
 Eastern Province.
