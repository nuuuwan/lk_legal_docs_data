THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: DIVUD/LND/ACQUI/13/4-6. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2021/BOI/173. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired:   
The 02 allotments of lands, referred in sheet No. 01,  
Block 01 of Cadastral Map No. 421353, prepared by the 
Surveyor General, situated in Wathuwaththa Village in 
No. 1484 Wathuwaththa Grama Niladhari Division in 
Udubaddawa Divisional Secretary's Division in 
Kurunegala District of North Western Province. 
 
 
 
Lot No. 1  
Name of the Land: Pansal Watta, Palugahapitiyawela, 
Kanagonpitiyekumbura, Wathuwaththa, Gangoda, 
Thalgahawela; Claimed by: The Government; Original 
Claimant:Suriya Pathirannahalage Lalithasiri Pathirathna; 
Extent: 0.0834 Hectare. 
Lot No. 2  
Name of the Land: Hiriyaddahena; Claimed by: The 
Government; Original Claimant: Suriya Pathirannahalage 
Sumanawathi, has been issued a Jayabhumi grant letter 
under the Land Development Ordinance; Extent: 0.0035 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat on 
11.06.2025 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 05.06.2025 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
