LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Number: MON/WEL/LND/16/02/2023/03. 
Land Ministry Number: 4-3/4/2013/D/342. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for Uva Kuda Oya 
Commando Regiment Training School. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:   
The allotment of land of approximately 2.0000 
Hectares as described below as Lot No. 1, in inset No. 02, 
Sheet No. 4 of Cadestral Map No. 720266, prepared by 
the Superintendent of Surveys of Monaragala, situated in 
the Village of Kuda Oya in Ethiliwewa Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 150 J, Wellawaya Divisional Secretary's 
Division, Monaragala District, Uva Province: 
 
01. Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Kalukanda; Claimant: Government; 
Original Claimant: Forest Conservation Department,              
Mr. Arangwala Vakishta Arachchige Sirisena has been in 
possession with a license; Extent: 2.0000 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Wellwaya on the 22nd day of May 2025 at 
10.00 a.m. 
 
Further are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 30th day of April, 2025 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 K. K. Rൺඏංඇൽඋൺ Mൺඅൺඇං, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Wellawaya. 
 
At Divisional Secretariat, Wellawaya, 
On the 25th day of March 2025.
