Government Notifications
SPORTS  LAW,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations made by the Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports under the Section 39 of Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973 as 
Amended
I, Sunil Kumara Gamage, Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 39 of the 
Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973 and its amendments hereby direct the WORKING TASK FORCE appointed by the Extraor-
dinary Gazette Notification bearing No. 2437/32  published on 22nd of May, 2025 to adopt the Constitution of Sri Lanka 
Rugby as recommended by the Constitutional Review Group appointed by the World Rugby, and agreed upon by  all the 
stakeholders, and which was published along with the notice issued by the Director General of Sports ( Competent Author-
ity) of Sri Lanka Rugby bearing No. DSD/NSA/COM/01/2024, dated 24.06.2024.
   Sunil Kumara Gamage (M.P.),
Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports.
at Colombo,
On this 22nd day of May, 2025.
