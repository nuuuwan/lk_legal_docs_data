THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Kuragamage Don Lal Kantha, Minister of Agriculture, 
Livestock, Land and Irrigation do hereby declare, under 
Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that 
the land described below is needed for a public purpose and 
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 An Allotment of land  in total extent of about 0.0177 Hectare 
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. B/ELL/2022/231 dated 
21.12.2022, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the village called “Edamegama (F.V .P. 184)” in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of Hettipola (69G) in the Minor Division 
of Kumbalwela Koralaye in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Ella in Badulla District in Uva Province and 
bounded as follows:
 01.  Lot ‘A’ – Maldarawatta – Extent: About 
0.0177 Hectare
 North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha), Hettipola Estate claimed

Bandara, Road, Hettipola Estate claimed by R. M. Karunarathna 
and Maldarawatta claimed by H. M. N. Wijerathna; South: 
Maldarawatta claimed by H. M. N. Wijerathna; West: 
Maldarawatta claimed by H. M. N. Wijerathna and Road 
(Pradeshiya Sabha).
