uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 25a of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. harini amarasuriya, the 
minister of education, higher education and V ocational education having obtained a report under section 70c of the 
aforesaid act, in respect of the “sri Lanka Institute of Tourism and hotel management”, established by the Tourism act, 
No. 38 of 2005, and located at No. 78, Galle Road, Colombo 03 do by this Order subject to such conditions as specified in 
schedule I hereto, recognize the “sri Lanka Institute of Tourism and hotel management” as a degree awarding Institute 
for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the 
schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in column II of the schedule II hereto:-
schedULe I
Conditions
1. sri Lanka Institute of Tourism and hotel management (hereinafter referred to as the “institute”) shall, when admitting 
students for the degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Hotel and Resort 
Management Honours with a minimum of 120 Credits (SLQF Level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of;
 (a) Three (3) simple passes (s) in any stream in one and the same sitting at G.c.e. (advance Level) examination 
conducted by the Department of Examination of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
