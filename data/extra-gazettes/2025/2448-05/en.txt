PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  PANADURA  PRADESHIYA  
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sarvajana Balaya, the recognized political party to which Wannakuwaththa Waduge 
Neel Sunethra Lal Fernando the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 
9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Maddumage Sujeewa Nilanga Fernando to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, returning officer of the Panadura Pradeshiya 
Sabha acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Maddumage Sujeewa Nilanga Fernando was selected as the member of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
 Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
31st July, 2025.
