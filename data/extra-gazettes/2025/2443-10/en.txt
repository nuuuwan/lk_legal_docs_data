THE  TOWN  AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21 (2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Anura Karunathilaka, Minister of Urban Development, Construction and Housing, do, by this order, -
 (a)  declare that the area of land called the “Sankapitti Purana Vihara Area” specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an 
urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid ordinance; and
 (b)  direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Sankapitti Purana Vihara Area”.
Anura Karunathilaka,
Minister of Urban Development, Construction and Housing.
Colombo
28th of June, 2025.
