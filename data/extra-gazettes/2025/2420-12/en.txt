Notice under Section 7 
 
Ministry of Agriculture, Lands, Livestock, Irrigation and 
Aquatic Resources Ref. No.: 4-3/18/2021/WS/23. 
My Ref. No.: DSWA/LND/4/ACQUI/F1/45. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/Servitude intended to be 
acquired: 
  
Description of the allotment of land depicted as Lot 
No. 01 in the Cadastral Map No. 830457, situated at 
Pahalawatta Grama Niladhari Division No. 554, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Walasmulla, in 
Hambantota District, Southern Province and bounded as 
follows: 
 
Lot No. 1 - Cadastral Map No. 830457 
Name of the Land: Welangahakumbura; Current 
Usage of the Land: Home garden, two water tanks and a 
pump house are situated; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0379 
Hectare. 
 
All persons who claim ownership, interest or any other 
connection to the aforesaid land/servitude are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at 10.00 a.m. on 
16.06.2025 at the Divisional Secretariat, Walasmulla. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notified me 
in writing in duplicate on or before 02.06.2025, the nature 
of their interests in the land /servitude, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.  
 
 Kඎආඎൽඎ C. Lඈ඄ඎඁൾඐൺ඀ൺආൺ, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Walasmulla. 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, Walasmulla.
