Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha
I hereby give notice for information of General Public that as per Secretary's Decision No. 12 and dated 19.12.2024, roads and 
paths included in this Schedule should be declared as roads and paths belonged to Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha, in terms 
of task assigned to Pradeshiya Sabhas in Sabaragamuwa Province by the series of orders with regard to publishing notices 
indicating register and preparation of plans for roads and paths maintained by Sabaragamuwa Province having been declared in 
Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2316/20 dated 24th of January, 2023 by Hon. Governor in Sabaragamauwa Provincial 
Council read with Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,  No. 15 of 1987.
If opinion of party claiming to a land that is any road or a path included in the said Schedule is inclusive in any partition of land, 
I hereby notify that action should be taken to institute an action in the appropriate court before expiration of a period of three 
months from this Notice for proving own possession for the said land.
Further, I notify that every road and path included in the said Schedule which hasn't been instituted an action before expiration 
of the said period of three months should be considered as a road or a path belonged to Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
