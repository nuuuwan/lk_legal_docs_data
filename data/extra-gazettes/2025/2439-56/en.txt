THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Revocation undeR Section 39 (1)
 THE allotments of Land specified more fully in the Order 
No. 317 of 2012 dated 18.12.2012 made under Proviso (a) to 
Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act and published in the 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/30 dated 19.12.2012 was

for and on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri

Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the Order only in respect 
of the land specified in the Schedule of Order No. 317 of 2012
