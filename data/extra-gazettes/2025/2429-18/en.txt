THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Kuragamage Don Lal Kantha, Minister of Agriculture, Livestock, 
Land and Irrigation, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude described below 
is  required for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act. Column 11 details The land from which the 
said condition is achieved.
 1. Description of the Servitude .–  Acquiring the Right of 
Servitude of a portion of land for Thalatuoya Service Reservoir of 
Kundasale Haragama Water Supply project and access road of the 
pump house and plumbing.
 2. Description of the Servitude.– Boundaries of the allotments 
of land consist with following names and extents shown as in detail 
in the Advanced Tracing No. Maha/PTH/2020/679 dated 02.02.2021, 
prepared by the Surveyor- General and situated in the Kadakula 
Village in Grama Niladhari Division of Bootawatta North (984) in 
the Pathahewaheta Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy District 
in Central Province:
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal 
Watta – Extent: 0.0548 Hectare 
 North: Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed 
by B.G.Warnakulasooriya and others and the road (R.D.A.); 
East: Road (R.D.A.) and Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal 
Watta claimed by G.M.H.V . Ajantha Lakshman and others; South: 
Naranka duwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed by 
G.M.H.V . Ajantha Lakshman and others, Narankaduwa Watta alias 
Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed by D.A. Perera; West: Narankaduwa 
Watta alias Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed by B.G.Warnakulasooriya 
and others.
 02. Lot ‘B’ – Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal 
Watta – Extent: 0.0133 Hectare 
 North: Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed 
by B.G.Warnakulasooriya and others and the road; East: Road 
and Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed by 
D.A. Perera; South: Narankaduwa Watta alias Kaliyapperumal 
Watta claimed by D.A. Perera; West: Narankaduwa Watta alias 
Kaliyapperumal Watta claimed by B.G.Warnakulasooriya and others.
