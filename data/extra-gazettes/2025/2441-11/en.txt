PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
filling  of  a  v acancy  in  the  office  of  member  of  the  Weligama  Urban  coUncil 
Under  section  66a(1) ( a) of   the  local  aUthorities  elections  ordinance 
 (chapter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of member of Weligama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Jathika Jana  Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Mohamed 
Mohamed Mufallal the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that No. 3 Galbokka of Weligama Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Umarul Jiffry Mohammed Afras to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore Pulasthi Serasingha Pathirana Returning Officer of the Weligama Urban Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Umarul Jiffry 
Mohammed Afras was elected as the member of the No. 3 Galbokka of Weligama Urban Council.
   
 P. S. P
athirana ,
 Returning Officer,
 Weligama Urban Council.
