Notice under Section 27A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262)
It is hereby notified that the political parties currently recognized as political parties for the purpose of elections under 
Section 7 of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, are also considered recognized political parties for the purpose 
of Elections to Local Authorities as per Section 27A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended 
by the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 24 of 1977, the names of political parties recognized as political 
parties for the purpose of elections to Local Authorities are set out in the following Schedule.
