No. 2439/57 – THURSDAY, JUNE 05, 2025
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Revocation under Section 39 (1)
THE allotment of land specified morefully in the Order  
No. 313 of 2012 dated 18.12.2012 made under the Proviso 
(a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act and published 
in the  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/29 dated 19.12.2012 
was vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. 
The possession of the land depicted below has not yet been 
taken for and  on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka. Therefore, I, Kuragamage Don Lal Kantha, 
Minister of Agriculture, Livestock, Land and Irrigation, by 
virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 39(1) of 
the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke order made in 
respect of the the lands specified in the Schedule of the Order 
No. 313 of 2012 dated 18.12.2012 published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 1789/29 dated 19.12.2012.
