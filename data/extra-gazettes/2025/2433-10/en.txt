ANNUAL PERAHARA OF BADULLA MUTHIYANGANA RAJAMAHA VIHARAYA
IN accordance with the powers vested in me by Gazette notiﬁ cations No. 9902 of 10th September 1948, No. 10283 of 17th 
August 1951 and No. 14778 of 25th March 1955, and under the powers conferred on Divisional Secretaries by Act, No. 58 
of 1992, and according to the constitution published in No. 1710 Gazette Notiﬁ cation of 10.06.2011 on Annual Perahara of 
Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya, I, Thushari Anuradha Nanayakkara, the Divisional Secretary of Badulla do hereby 
make the following orders in connection with the Annual Perahara of Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya, 2025.
 01. I do hereby notice that, the Annual Perahara of Badulla Muthiyangana shall begin with inner Perahara on 11th of 
May 2025 and proceeding along the streets on 12th and 13th days of May 2025, and end after having returned to the 
Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
 02. Route of the Perehara
  Inner Vihara Perahera ceremony will be on 11th of May 2025
  The Perahara shall start from Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya premises on 12th of May 2025 and proceed 
along Station road, Anagarika Dharmapa ła Mawatha, Lower King's Street, King's Street and to Lower Street along 
Library Street and back to Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya from the end of Lower Street.

  The Perahera shall start from Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya premises on 13th of May 2025 and proceed 
along Station road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Cocowatte Road, Udayaraja Mawatha, King's Street and to Lower 
Street along Bazzar Street and back to Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
 03.  Cooking and selling articles of trade are prohibited in and around Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya compound.
 04. Private vehicles are prohibited to park in the main roads during the Perahara Season. All types of vehicles, including 
bicycles and carts are prohibited to enter the Vihara area without a permit issued by me or the Trustee Chief Incumbent 
of Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
 05.  The permanent buildings and the buildings currently being constructed along the Perahera route under a formal approval 
given by Badulla Municipal Council must conform to the instructions of the Health Department. At the end of the 
Perehara, after having dismantled the temporary sheds, the lessees shall dispose of all polythene and other garbage 
lying along the route of Perahara and Viharaya premises as instructed by the Public Health Inspectors.
 06. Construction of any temporary hut or building in and around the sacred area shall not be done without a permit obtained 
from me thereto.
 07. No person shall construct any temporary hut or building of any form, or effect any extensions to any existing building 
on a street or on a land reserved for a street or in the sacred area without a permit obtained from me or from the Badulla 
Municipal Council.
 08. Burning of crackers within the area is prohibited.
 09. No person shall engage in selling tickets, ﬂ ags or engage in any other task meant for collecting subscriptions, within 
the camping site indicated in clause No.03, without the prior approval of an Authorized Ofﬁ cer of Divisional Secretary.
