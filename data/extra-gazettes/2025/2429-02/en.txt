WELfARE  BENEfITS   ACT,  No.  24  Of  2002
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 09 of the Welfare Benefits Act, No. 24 of 2002, I, Anura Kumara Dissanayake, 
minister of Finance, Planning and Economic development of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby 
declare the Aswesuma Welfare Benefit Payment Scheme as specified in the following schedules shall come into force with 
effect from the date on which the approval of Parliament of this payment scheme.
   The Notification published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 2415/66 dated 21.12.2024 is hereby rescinded.
 Anura Kumara Dissanayake,
 minister of Finance, Planning and
 Economic development.

   The Aswesuma Welfare Benefit Payment Scheme, published in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2415/66 
dated December 21, 2024, is hereby rescinded, however, payments related to Schedule 1 and Schedule II shall be made to 
the beneficiaries whose payments have been delayed due to various reasons and to the applicants who will subsequently 
be entitled for this scheme upon their eligibility for selection from those who have already forwarded their appeals and 
investigations are still underway in accordance with the applicable Welfare Benefit Payment Scheme in force at that time, 
notwithstanding the cancellation of the said Gazette notifications.
