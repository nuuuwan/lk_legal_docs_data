PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE HORANA URBAN COUNCIL 
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE 
 (CHAPTER  262)  
WHEREAS the Office of return member of Horana Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sandanam Jonson 
Paul the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned 
member of Horana Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days,
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rajeewa Wasula Gunasekara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Wannakuwatta Wadugu Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, returning Officer of the Horana Urban Council 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rajeewa 
Wasula Gunasekara was selected as the member of the Horana Urban Council.
   
 Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
 Horana Urban Council.
 
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
31st  July, 2025.
