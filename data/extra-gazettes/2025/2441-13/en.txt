PART IV (B)  — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Local Government Notifications
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  KARUWALAGASWEWA 
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A  (1)  ( a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
AND WHEREAS  the Secretary of the Jathika Jana Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Ilandari Purage 
Wikramapala the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that No. 1 - Pahala Puliyankulama of Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is 
not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS  the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Nawaratna Muththalage Neel Nawaratna to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE  Sirinivasage Lakshitha Ruwan Jayanayake returning officer of the Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of selection 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Nawaratne Muththalage Neel Nawarathna was elected as the member of the No.- 1 Pahala Puliyankulama of 
Karuwalagaswewa  Pradeshiya Sabha. 
S. L. R. Jayanayake,
Returning Officer,
Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
17th June 2025,
District Election Office,
Puttalam.
