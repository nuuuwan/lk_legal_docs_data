PAWNBROKERS ORDINANCE N o . 13 OF 1942 
IN Terms of Section 46 (a) of  the Pawnbrokers Ordinance No. 13 of 1942 (99th Authority) read with Section 23 (a) (1) of 
the said Ordinance and Provincial Council Act, No. 12 of 1989 (Subsidiary Regulations) by virtue of the powers vested in me 
under Section 2(2) of thre Act, I, the Governor of North Western Province, Tissa Kumarasiri Warnasuriya, hereby order that 
every licensee, who runs a business of pawnbroker in the province of the north western, shall deposit a security deposit of 
Two Hundred Thousand Rupees (Rs. 200,000) in the custody of the license issuing authority in accordance with Section 3(1).
 Tissa Kumarasiri Warnasuriya,
 Governor of the North Western Province,
Colombo.
