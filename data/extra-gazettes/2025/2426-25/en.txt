SERvICE   MINuTE  Of  ThE  MANAGEMENT  SERvICE  OffICERS'  SERvICE  -  REvISION  No. 04
The Public service commission has ordered to include the revision mentioned here as revision No.04 in substitution for the 
revision published in Gazette Extraordinary  of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka  No. 2383/06 dated 06 may 
2024 as revision No.04 of the Minute of the Management Service Officers' Service published in  Gazette Extraordinary of 
the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka No.1840/34 dated 11 december 2013, revised by Gazettes Extraordinary of 
the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka in the following manner. 
revision No. 01 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2032/26 dated 16 august 2017
revision No. 02 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2052/26 dated 04 January 2018
revision No. 03 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2140/04 dated 09 september 2019
revision No. 04 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2383/06 dated 06 may 2024*
*The following revision is substituted for this revision.
Paragraph 1.7 should be revised in the following manner.
 7.2.2.1 Educational Qualifications
 (a) shall have passed the G.c.e. (Ordinary Level) examination in six (06) subjects in one sitting with credit 
passes for 04 subjects, including Language (sinhala / Tamil / english) and mathematics or passed in one 
sitting an examination recognized by the commissioner General of examination as equivalent to the G.c.e. 
(Ordinary Level) examination with the passes and credits to the subject in a similar combination.
