Local Government Notifications
KURUNEGALA MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
Alternative routies that can be used during the 100th Sri Dalada Poson Perahera at the 
Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya in Kurunegala
In terms of Section 04 of the “By-law on Halting and Handling of Vehicles” of Kurunegala Municipal Council Published in 
the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1921 dated 26 th June 2015 and by virtue of the powers 
vested in me by Chapter 252 Municipal Council Ordinance and Sri Lanka Law Statutes Code, I hereby announce and publicize 
that, whereas, the annual Sri Dalada Poson Perahera Pageant will be held at the Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya in Kurunegala 
on the 23rd, 24th and 25th of June 2025, vehicular traffic on the following roads will be restricted during the period of the said 
Perahera Pageant and since vehicle traffic may be restricted on the following routes where the Perahera Pageant is taken place 
during that period, the following alternative road facilities have been provided for uninterrupted travel during that period.
Perahera Pageant Route
Dates : 23rd and 24th June 2025 (Perahera Pageant starts at 4.00 PM)
Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya – Rajapihilla Road – Holy Family School Road – Mihindu Road – Puttalam Road – Clock 
Tower Junction – Kandy Road – Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya.
25th June 2025 (Perahera Pageant starts at 7.30 PM)
Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya – Rajapihilla Road – Police Station – Clock Tower Junction – Puttalam Road – D.B.Welagedara 
Mawatha (Main Street) – Parakumba Street – Dambulla Road – Clock Tower Junction – Kandy Road – Athkanda Rajamaha 
Viharaya.
