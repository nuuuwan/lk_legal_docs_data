PART IV (B)  — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Local Government Notifications
NOTICE UNDER  SECTION  66C.(4)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE, 
AS  AMENDED  BY  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  N o. 22  OF  2012
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 66C of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, as amended by the Local 
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012,1, Erandi Umanga Mendis the Commissioner of Local Government 
of the Department of Local Government, Southern Province, do hereby inform that the meeting that was convened and 
postponed on 27.06.2025 for the purpose of electing a Chairman and a Vice Chairman for a Local Authority within the 
Southern Province named in the schedule below, will be reconvened in terms of the section 66C.(4) of the said Ordinance at 
the specified time on the date correspondingly indicated under the schedule below at the venue mentioned therein .   
Erandi Umanga Mendis,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Southern Province.
Department of Local Government of Southern Province,
6th Floor, Galle District Secretariat Building.
On this 08th day of July 2025.
