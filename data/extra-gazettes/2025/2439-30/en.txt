Local Government Notifications
NOTIFICATION   MADE  UNDER   SECTION  66C   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   AMENDED   BY  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  
(AMENDMENT )  ACT, No. 22  OF 2012
AS per the powers entrusted to me under Section 66c of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by the Local 
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012, I, Sarangika Kalhari Jayasundara, the Commissioner of Local 
Government in the Western Province do hereby announce that the first meeting held for the purpose of electing Mayor/
Chairman, Deputy Mayor/ Deputy Chairman for the Local Government Authorities of the Western Province stated in the 
schedule hereto under will be held on the date correspondingly set out under the same schedule at the time and place specified 
therein.  
 Sarangika Kalhari Jayasundara,
 Commissioner of Local Government,
 Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,
located in the Western Provincial Council Building at No. 204, 
Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla,
