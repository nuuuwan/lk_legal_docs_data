STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
   Whether Mrs. S.D. Nayana Ranasinghe working as the Manager (Finance) of the Janatha Estates Development Board 
had been caused injustice by not receiving the below- mentioned privileges and if so, to what relief she is entitled.
 
1.  not being placed in the post of Manager (Finance) from 27.09.2011, if entitled
2.  only one person out of the two officers in the post of Accountant receiving privileges  relevant to the post.
