LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No. : Bu/Ln/1/1/22. 
Min. of Lands Ref. No. : 3-43/2019/FR/165 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
  
Boundaries are as follows  for the land called, 
Idadeniya and Palagalamukalana extent of 0.8599 Hectare 
mentioned in Block No. 04, Sheet No. 02, Lot Number I 
in Cadastral Map No. 530426, Prepared by Surveyor 
General situated in Udugalakanda Village of 
Bulathsinghala Divisional Secretary's Division in 
Kalutara District, in Western Province. 
Lot No. 01 - Idadeniya and Palagalamukalana - 
Extent: 0.8599 Hectare. 
 
 
North: Lot No. 1936 of F.T.P. 03; East: Lot No. 1936 
of F.T.P. 03; South: Lot No. 1936 of F.T.P. 03; West: Lot 
No. 1936 of F.T.P. 03. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on the 
which a servitude is to be acquired to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Divisional Secretariat Bulathsinghala on 30th April, 2025 
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the April 30th on the 
day of 2025, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 B. A. Sංඍඁൺඋං Uൽൺඒൺඇ඀ൺඇං, 
 Acting Divisional Secretary, 
 Bulathsinghala. 
 
07th April, 2025,
