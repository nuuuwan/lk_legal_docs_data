THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Kuragamage Don Lal Kantha, Minister of  Agriculture, 
Livestock, Land and Irrigation do hereby declare under  
Sub- section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that 
the lands described below are needed for a public purpose and 
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 Allotments of land depicted in below names and extents 
in the Advance Tracing  No. Gm/JAE/2023/221 dated 
21.09.2023 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
the Village called Peralanda in No. 181D, Peralanda Grama 
Niladhari Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Ja- Ela in Gampaha District of the Western Province and 
bounded as follows:
 01.  Lot ‘A’ - Delgahawatta - Extent: 0.0159 Hectare
 North: Railway reservation and Delgahawatta land claimed 
by Shamalee Resairo and others; East: Railway reservation 
and Lot B (Ela); South: Lot B (Ela) and road; West: Road and 
Delgahawatta land claimed by Shamalee Resairo and others. 
 02.  Lot ‘B’ - Not known - Extent: 0.0017 Hectare
 North: Lot A and Railway reservation; East: Lot C and 
Railway reservation; South: Lot C and Road; West: Road and 
Delgahawatta land claimed by Shamalee Resairo and others. 
 03.  Lot ‘C’ - Delgahawatta - Extent: 0.0241 Hectare
 North: Lot B (Ela) and Railway reservation; East:

reservation; South: Delgahawatta land claimed by H.D. 
Sudaraka and Road; West: Road and Lot No. B (Ela).
