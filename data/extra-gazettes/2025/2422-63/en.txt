PRADESHIYA  SABHA - WELIGEPOLA
Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act  No. 15 of 1978 should be read with the Honorable Governor of Sabaragamuwa 
Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2316/20 and dated 24th of January, 2023 according to the 
task assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabhas of Sabaragamuwa Province by the series of directives about preparing the plans of roads 
and narrow paths and publishing notices showing the list of names, it is hereby decided under Resolution No. 10 on 21.01.2025 
that the roads and narrow paths included in the following schedule should be declared as the roads and narrow paths owned by 
the Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
That any road or narrow path included in the said schedule passes through any land or part of any land, if in the opinion of the 
party claiming the land, it is hereby announced that a case should be ﬁ led in the competent court before the expiry of a period 
of three months from the date of this announcement in order to assert their right to the land. It is further announced that every 
road and narrow path included in the said schedule for which no such case has been assigned before the expiry of the said three 
month period shall be considered as a road or narrow path owned by the Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
