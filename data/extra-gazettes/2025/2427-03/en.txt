No. 2427/03 - MONDAY, MARCH 10, 2025 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No. : LND/ACQUS/ARHAM/D-2/01. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No. : 4-3/13/2019/PC/596. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
Servitude which is required for KM/AK/AL - Arham 
Vidyalaya, Addalaichanai. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land in total extent of 0.1652 Hectare in the 
Preliminary Plan No. - CM 280356 dated 31.12.2024, prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated at the Village called No. 7 
Addalachchenai in Addalaichanai Grama Niladhari Division No. 
07 at Addalaichenai Divisional Secretary’s Division in the 
Ampara District of Eastern Province.   
 
The Boundaries of the above said Lands are as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
North by : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East by : Lot - 2; South 
by : TP178340; West by : TP178340 & Lot - 1 in pp/am/3634. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
North by : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East by : Lot - 3 & Lot - 4; 
South by : TP178340 & Lot 1; West by : Lot 1. 
(Published by Authority) 
 
PART III  — LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
03. Lot No. 03 
 
North by : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East by : Road (Pradeshiya 
Sabha) Lot 4;  South by : Lot 4 & Lot 2; West by : Lot 2. 
 
04. Lot No. 04 
 
North by : Lot - 3; East by : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);  South 
by : TP178340; West by : Lot 2. 
 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which a 
servitude is to acquired are hereby acquired to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Addalaichenai on the 26th of May 2025 at 10.00 a.m 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 21st of May 2025 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
Acquisition of the said land/servitude amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 P. T. M. Irfan, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Addalaichenai. 
 
 
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Addalaichanai,
