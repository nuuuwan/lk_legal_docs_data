TOWN AND COUNTRY PLANNING ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Anura Karunathilake, Minister of Urban Development, Construction and Housing do by this Order –
 ( a) declare that the area of land called as “Dikwella Deegawalukarama Rajamaha Viharaya” speci ﬁed in the 
Schedule hereto shall be an Urban Development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance; and
 ( b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Dikwella Deegawalukarama Rajamaha Viharaya”, 
which is declared as above to be an Urban Development area. 
 ANURA KARUNATHILAKE,
