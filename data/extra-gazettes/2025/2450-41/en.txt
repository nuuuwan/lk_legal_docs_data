L.D.B. 21/1978(X)(D)
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  N o. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(l)(b) read with Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, 
1, Dr. Harini Amarasuriya, the Minister of Education, Higher Education and Vocational Education, having obtained a report 
under section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the SRI LANKA TECHNOLOGY CAMPUS (PVT) LIMITED, a 
company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 101137, 
being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education leading to the award 
of the Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Civil Engineering by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2210/18 of January 13, 2021 and amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2308/29 of December 1, 
2022, do hereby further amend the said Order by the substitution for the words “minimum entry qualifications specified 
herein” appearing in proviso to Condition 2 in Schedule I thereof, of the following:-
“minimum entry qualifications specified herein and shall be eligible to be awarded the Bachelor of Science Honours in 
Engineering in Civil Engineering, to be effective from 07.05.2015, upon the successful completion of the said degree 
programme.”.
 Dr. Harini Amarasuriya,
 Minister of Education, Higher Education and
 V ocational Education.
Colombo,
August 06, 2025.

THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  N o. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(l)(b) read with Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, 
1, Dr. Harini Amarasuriya, the Minister of Education, Higher Education and Vocational Education, having obtained a report 
under section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the SRI LANKA TECHNOLOGY CAMPUS (PVT) LIMITED, a 
company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No.07 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 101137, 
being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education leading to the award of 
the Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Mechatronics Engineering by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2157/3 of January 6, 2020 and amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2308/29 of December 1, 
2022, do hereby further amend the said Order by the substitution for the words “minimum entry qualifications specified 
herein.” appearing in proviso to paragraph (ii) of Condition 1 in Schedule I thereof, of the following:-
“minimum entry qualifications specified herein and shall be eligible to be awarded the Bachelor of Science Honours 
in Engineering in Mechatronics Engineering, to be effective from 07.05.2015, upon the successful completion of the 
said degree programme.”.
 Dr. Harini Amarasuriya,
 Minister of Education, Higher Education and
 V ocational Education.
Colombo,
August 06, 2025.
