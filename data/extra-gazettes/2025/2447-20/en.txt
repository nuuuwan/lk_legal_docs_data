THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Order under Section 39(a)(1)
The allotments specified in the Order No.299 of 2013 made 
under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition 
Act (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra ordinary 
No. 1830/51 dated 4th October 2013 are vested absolutely in 
the State and the possession of which has been taken for or 
on behalf of the State under Paragraph (a) of Section 40 of 
the aforesaid Act. But by the virtue of powers vested in me 
by Sub section (1) of Section 39 (a) (inserted by Act No.8 
of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act, I, Kuragamage Don 
Lal Kantha, Minister of Agriculture, Livestock, Land and 
Irrigation, do hereby divest only the allotments specified in

THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Revocation Order under Section 39(1)
The allotment of land specified morefully in the Order 
No.299 of 2013 dated 30.09.2013 made under the Proviso 
(a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act, and published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1830/51 dated 04.10.2013 
was vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka. The possession of the land depicted below has 
not yet been taken for and on behalf of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore, I, Kuragamage 
Don Lal Kantha, Minister of Agriculture, Livestock, Land 
and Irrigation, by virtue of the powers vested in me under 
Section 39(1) of the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke 
the Order in respect of the land specified in the Schedule of 
the Order No. 299 of 2013 dated 30.09.2013 published in the 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1830/51 dated 04.10.2013.

 Minister of Agriculture, Livestock,
 Land and Irrigation.
