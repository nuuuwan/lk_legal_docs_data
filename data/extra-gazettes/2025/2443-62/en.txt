NOTICE  UNDER  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Declaration of  appointment  made by  virtue  of  the  statutory responsibility vested in me under the  
Section No. 66c of and No. 66d of Chapter No. 262 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  
amended by No. 22 of 2012 of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act
I, Adam Lebbe Mohamed Azmi, Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province, by virtue of the statutory 
responsibility vested in me under the Section No. 66c of and No. 66d of Chapter No. 262 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance amended by No. 22 of 2012 of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, do hereby notify that Mayor/
Chairman, Deputy Mayor/Vice Chairman have been appointed on the date depicted in the Column No. IV of the corresponding 
chart of the Schedule here, as depicted in the Column No. III of the corresponding chart of the Schedule here, for each Local 
Government Authorities as depicted Column No. II in the corresponding chart of the Schedule here, located in the specified 
Administrative District as depicted Column No. I of the corresponding chart of the Schedule here situated in the Eastern 
Province.
 A. L. M. Azmi,
 Commissioner of Local Government,
 Eastern Province. 
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar, 
Trincomalee,
On this 04th day of July, 2025.
