EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: DIVPANN/LND/ACBOA/1/7/21. 
Ministry of Lands Ref No.: 4-3/18/2019/WS/236. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:   
All that allotment of land in total extent of about 
2.3340 ha., depicted in sheet No. 01, Block No. 02, Lot 
No. 307 in the plan No. Cad. Map 420593 and dated 
17.01.2013, surveyed and pr epared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the village of Badabedda, in 
Medapattu West Korale, in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Pannala in Kurunegala District, North 
Western  Province and bounded as follows. 
Lot No. 1  
Name of the Land: Kandetiya; Description of the 
Land: Estate; Claimant: Jude Deepal Iranga Peiris; 
Extent: 2.3340 Hectare. 
 
All persons who claim ownership, interest or any other 
connection to the aforesaid land/servitude hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me on 30th April, 2025 at 10.30 a.m. at 
the Divisional Secretariat, Pannala. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 23rd April, 2025 
the nature of their interests in the land/lands, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
