Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 Of 1987
order
by virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 2, 4 and 5 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas act, No.15 of 1987 and Section 3d 
of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262), I, a. H. m. H. abayaratne, minister of Public administration, 
Provincial Councils and Local Government, do by this order; 
 (1) Constitute a Pradeshiya Sabha for the area specified in the Schedule mentioned in the Order published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No.453/26 dated 12.05.1987, as amended by the order published in the Gazette extraordinary No.2043/56 
dated 02.11.2017 and assign the following name and the designation for the said Pradeshiya Sabha.
“Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha”
 (2)  I order that the Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of a number of members elected and returned as per paragraphs 
i, ii and iii below.
        The Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of;
 i. Nine members elected as per paragraph (a) of Sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas act, No. 15 
of 1987
 ii. Six members elected and returned as per paragraph (b) of Sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas 
act, No.15 of 1987 and:
 iii. Such number of members determined by the Commissioner of elections under paragraph (a) of Sub-section 4 of 
Section 65b of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) as per paragraph (c) of Sub-section (1) 
of Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas act, No.15 of 1987.
 (3) The Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of wards specified in the Schedule of the Order published under No.08 of 
Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha No.23 of monaragala district in the 
Gazette extraordinary  No. 1928/26 dated 21.08.2015.
 (4) The term of office of the Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha shall commence on 02nd of June 2025.
 Dr . A. H. M. H. Abayaratne,
 minister of Public administration, 
 Provincial Councils and Local Government.
in Colombo,
on 17th day of february 2025.
