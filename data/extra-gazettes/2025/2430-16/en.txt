TObACCO   TAX   ACT,   No.  8  of  1999
Imposition of Tobacco Tax on beedi under the Tobacco Tax Act, No. 8 of 1999
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the tobacco tax act, No. 8 of 1999, as amended,  i, anura Kumara 
dissanayake, Minister of Finance, planning and economic development do by this Order:
 1. repeal regulation 2 of the “regulations for levying the tobacco tax for Beedi” published in the Extraordinary Gazette 
No.2312/71 dated december 31, 2022; and
 2. determine the tobacco tax at three rupees (rs. 3/-) for every beedi produced in sri lanka.
this Order shall come in to effect from april 02, 2025.
 AnurA KumArA DissAnAyAKe,
 Minister of Finance, planning and
 economic development.
