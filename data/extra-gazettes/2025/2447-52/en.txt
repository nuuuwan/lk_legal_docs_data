PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  CHILAW  URBAN  COUNCIL 
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  
(CHAPTER  262)  
WHEREAS the office of return member of Chilaw Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Samagi Jana Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Mirisage Don 
Ajanthi Nilusha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Chilaw Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local 
authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) 
days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Chakrawarthige Chamarika Dilhani Fernando to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Siriniwasage Lakshitha Ruwan Jayanayake, returning officer of the Chilaw Urban Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Chakrawarthige 
Chamarika Dilhani Fernando was selected as the member of the Chilaw Urban Council.
   
 S. L. R. Jayanayake,
 Returning Officer,
 Chilaw Urban Council. 
District Elections Office,
Puttalam,
31st July, 2025.
