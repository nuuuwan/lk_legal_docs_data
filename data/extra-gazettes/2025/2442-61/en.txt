NOTICE  UNDER  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Declaration of  appointment  made by  virtue  of  the  statutory responsibility vested in me under the  
Section No. 66c of and No. 66d of Chapter No. 262 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  
amended by No. 22 of 2012 of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act 
I, W. M. Ruwan Wijesinghe, Commissioner of Local Government of North Central Province, by virtue of the statutory 
responsibility vested in me under the Section No. 66c of and No. 66d of Chapter No. 262 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance amended by No. 22 of 2012 of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, do hereby notify that Chairmen 
have been appointed on the date depicted in the Column No. IV of the corresponding chart of the Schedule here, as depicted 
in the Column No. III of the corresponding chart of the Schedule here, for each Local Government Authorities as depicted 
Column No. II in the corresponding chart of the Schedule here, located in the specified Administrative District as depicted 
Column No. I of the corresponding chart of the Schedule here situated in the North Central Province.
 W. M. Ruwan Wijesinghe,
 Commissioner of Local Government,
 North Central Province.
     
   
At Department of Local Government,
North Central Province, 
On 23rd June 2025.
