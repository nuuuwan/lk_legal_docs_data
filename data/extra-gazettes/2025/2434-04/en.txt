Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,  
I, Anura Kumara Dissanayake, Minister of Finance, Planning and Economic Development do by this Order amend the Order 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2418/43 dated January 10, 2025, as last amended by the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2421/42 dated January 31, 2025, as follows:
 1.  repeal of HS Codes 8703.80.71 and 8703.80.72 specified in Column II and the corresponding articles specified in 
Columns III and IV of the Schedule thereto; and
 2. addition of the rate of Excise duty specified in the Column IV applicable on corresponding articles specified in 
Column I, II and III of the Schedule hereto.  
This Order shall come into effect from April 29, 2025.
 Anura Kumara Dissanayake,
 Minister of Finance, Planning and  Economic Development.
Ministry of Finance, Planning and  
Economic Development,
Colombo 01,
April 28, 2025.
