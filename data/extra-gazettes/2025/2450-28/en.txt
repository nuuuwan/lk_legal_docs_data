PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & C., by the President
No. 581/1 of 2025
                     
P.S. No. : PS/CSA/00/7/IX/4/02.
IT is hereby notified that the Honourable President, in terms of Article 41C. (1) and 61E. (b) of the Constitution of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has appointed Mr. Weerasooriya Liyana Arachchige Saman Priyantha, as the 
Inspector General of Police, with effect from August 14, 2025.
By order of the Honourable President,
 Dr. N.S. Kumanayake,
 Secretary to the President.
