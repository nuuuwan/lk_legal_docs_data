Regulations
the Sri Lanka Air Force Women's Wing (regular and regular reserve) regulations, 1987, published in Gazette  
Extraordinary No. 539/15 of January 7, 1989 are hereby amended as follows:-
	 (1)	 by	 the	substitution	in	sub-regulation	(4)	of	regulation	20,	for	the	words	“age	of	forty	five	years	or	on	completion	
of	a	maximum	period	of	twenty	years	of	service,	whichever	occurs	earlier.”,	of	the	words	and	figures	“age	of	fifty	
five	(55)	years	or	on	completion	of	a	period	of	twenty	(20)	years	of	service.”;
	 (2)		by	 the	substitution	in	regulation	21,	for	the	words	“age	of	fifty	years,”	of	the	words	and	figures	“age	of	sixty	(60)
years.”;	and
 (3)  by the substitution in sub-regulation (3) of regulation 35, for the words “maximum period of twenty years whichever 
occurs earlier.”,	of	the	words	and	figures	“period of twenty two (22) years.”.
