THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 40)
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 12 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, Anura Kumara 
Dissanayake, President, do by this order call out with effect from June 27, 2025 all the members of the Armed Forces specified 
in the First Schedule hereto, for the maintenance of public order in the areas specified in the Second Schedule hereto.
