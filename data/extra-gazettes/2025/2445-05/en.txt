THE  COLOMBO  PORT  CITY   ECONOMIC  COMMISSION   ACT,  No.  11  OF  2021
Order  under  section   53
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by section 53 of the Colombo Port City Economic Commission Act, No. 11 of 
2021, I, Anura Kumara Dissanayake, Minister of Finance, Planning and Economic Development, having considered the 
recommendations of the Colombo Port City Economic Commission and upon the approval of the Cabinet of Ministers, do 
by this Order specify that-
(a) the Cabinet of Ministers, by its decision No. 25/1123/804/134 dated, 01.07.2025 has approved the designation 
of the business of the authorized person specified in Schedule I hereto as a Primary Business of Strategic 
Importance;
(b) the rationale for considering such business as a Primary Business of Strategic Importance is as specified in 
Schedule II hereto;
(c) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments shall be as specified in Schedule III hereto ; and
(d) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments specified shall commence and cease to be operative 
on the dates specified in Schedule IV hereto.
.
 Anura Kumara Dissanayake, 
 Minister of Finance, Planning and 
 Economic Development.  
Colombo, 
July 12, 2025.

SCHEDULE II
Rationale for the consideration of the Primary Business of Strategic Importance
The objectives of the Colombo Port City Economic Commission, as stipulated in section 5 of the Colombo Port 
City Economic Commission Act, No. 11 of 2021, have been considered in designating the business as a Business 
of Strategic Importance, in order to ensure the success of establishing the Colombo Port City, having regard to the 
national interest or the advancement of the national economy as provided for in section 53(5) of the said Act.
ICC Port City (Private) Limited. is a company established by the International Construction Consortium (Private) 
Limited (ICC), a recognized leader in Sri Lanka’s construction industry with the aim of investing in premier 
residential development within the Colombo Port City (CPC). ICC is a distinguished tier one general contract 
and property developer with a strong legacy in delivering high-quality construction and real estate projects with 
over 40 years of experience in executing a range of projects including residential, commercial, industrial and civil 
engineering work.
The primary goal of this establishment is to develop a luxury residential project within the CPC on plot 1-02-05 
spanning an area of 13,945 sqm. The proposed development will feature two luxury apartment towers housing high 
end residential units and five luxury villas with state-of-the-art facilities offered to residents.
ICC Port City (Private) Limited. seeks to carry out the objectives of residential development, driving investment 
in the CPC, expansion of ICC's existing project portfolio, catering to international markets, conduct sales and 
marketing activities to sell apartments and to engage with foreign investors with the goal of attracting required 
foreign investment. ICC Port City (Private) Limited. will also offer the core services of luxury apartments, luxury 
villas and commercial space for sale or rent as well as serviced apartments and management services extending to 
rental management services and condominium management services.
ICC Port City (Private) Limited. aims to target a comprehensive range of buyers focused on high-net-worth 
individuals, expatriates and foreign professionals, investors, families, retirees, business owners, tourists, short 
term renters, digital nomads, marina living experience seekers, yacht enthusiasts and business and financial hub 
professionals.
ICC Port City (Private) Limited. will invest USD 66,000,000 and LKR 13,800,000,000 to commence the business 
being carried out from Colombo Port City. This amount includes expenses for the land and development cost of the 
project.
In engaging in this business, ICC Port City (Private) Limited. will recruit 8 direct employees employed locally in 
the first year and this is to increase to 12 direct employees by the fourth year of operations. ICC Port City (Private) 
Limited. will also recruit 343 indirect employees in the first year and this is to increase to 862 indirect employees by 
the fourth year to engage in design, engineering and construction.
The project's alignment with the Port City’s growth potential and its favourable business climate enables ICC Port 
City (Private) Limited. to position itself as a premier player in Sri Lanka’s evolving luxury real estate market 
enhancing both the company’s and the country’s global market presence.
