THE  UNITED NATIONS ACT, NO. 45 OF 1968
Order under regulation 5 of the United Nations Regulations No. 1 of 2012
I, Hadigalage Sampath Sajeewa Thuyacontha, Competent Authority appointed for the purpose of the United Nations Regu-
lations No. 1 of 2012, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1758/19 of May 15, 2012, acting in terms of the powers 
vested in me by regulation 5 of the said Regulations, do by this Order, freeze all funds, other financial assets and economic 
resources belonging to or owned or held by any natural or legal person, group or entity designated and listed under regulation 
4 of the above Regulations and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1854/41 of March 21, 2014, as amended from time 
to time and last amended by the amendment to the list published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2438/47 of May 30, 2025.
The Orders made under regulation 5 of the United Nations Regulations No. 1 of 2012 published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2335/31 of June 10, 2023, Gazette Extraordinary No. 2387/03 of June 3, 2024 and Gazette Extraordinary No. 2424/52 
of February 20, 2025 are hereby revoked without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
   
  Air Vice Marshal Sampath Thuyacontha (Retd),
