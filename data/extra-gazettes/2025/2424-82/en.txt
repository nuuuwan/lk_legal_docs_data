PILGRImAGES   ORDINANCE  
IT is hereby notified that the Annual Sri Maha Bodhiraja Procession of Sri Shasanalankararama Ancient Temple in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of Udugama in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Nagoda, District of Galle, Southern Province, 
will be held from 7th of March 2025 to 13th of March 2025 as per the regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette 
No. 1956/11 dated 01.03.2016 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance 
(Chapter 175).
 W. A. Dharmasiri,
 District Secretary/Government Agent,
 Administrative District, Galle.
 
At the District Secretariat, Galle,
18th of February, 2025.
