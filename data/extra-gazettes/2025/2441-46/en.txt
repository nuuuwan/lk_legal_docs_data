Notice of Calling for Nominations for the Appointment of Mediators
The Mediation Boards Commission, by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 4(1) of the Mediation (Special 
Categories of disputes) act, No. 21 of 2003, invites nominations for the appointment of Chairman and Mediators in respect 
of resolving all disputes relating to State Lands and Private Lands subject to the provisions contained in the Extraordinary                       
Gazette Notification No. 2261/68 dated 06.01.2022 for the following Panel of Special Mediation Board who is to vacate post 
in terms of sections 1 to 5 in the Schedule of the Mediation (Special Categories of disputes) act No. 21 of 2003.
 Administrative District Special Mediation board Areas Date of Vacation of Post
 Gampaha Gampaha district 30.09.2025   
  
 02. The Mediation Boards Commission hereby gives notice calling nominations upon (a) the individuals, (B) the Bodies, 
Organizations and Institutions of non political nature, and (C) the District Secretaries/ Divisional Secretaries specified 
hereinafter under 02 (ii) to submit names of person who are eligible under 02 (i) for appointment as Mediators to the 
Panel of Mediators for the said Special Mediation Board area:
 (i) The following Persons are entitled to be appointed to the Panel of a particular Special Mediation Board area 
whose names may be so submitted for appointment.
