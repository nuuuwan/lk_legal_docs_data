is required for public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:   
Acquisition of land for Moragahakanda/Kaluganga 
Development Project. 
 
Allotment of land depicted in sheet No. 01 in Block 
No. 02 in Cadastral Map No. 310226 seconded on 
14.02.2025 by Surveyor General, situated in Nawaneliya 
village in Galporugolla Grama Niladhari Division No. 
E401A, in Naula Divisional Secretary's Division in 
Matale District in Central Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 1  
Name of the Land: Maha Kumbura, 
Karakolagahamula Mukalana; Use of land at present: 
Shrub Jungle; Claimed by State; Occupied by A. G. 
Leelawathi; Extent: 1.2140 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 2  
Name of the Land: Use of land at present: Road (Old); 
Claimed by State; Extent: 0.0219 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Naula Divisional Secretariat on 08th April, 2025 at 
10.00 a.m.  
 
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
with 2 copies on or before 03rd April, 2025 the nature of 
their interests in the land/lands, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
