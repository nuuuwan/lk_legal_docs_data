Regulation
The Food (Iodization of Salt) Regulation 2023, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2332/03 of May 15, 2023, as amended 
by regulation published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2364/27 dated of  December 27, 2023 and Gazette Extraordinary  
No. 2416/53 dated of  December, 2024 are hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 1 thereof, and the substitution thereof 
the following regulation:
 “1.   These regulations may be cited as Food (Iodization of Salt) Regulations, 2023 and shall come into operation with 
effects from January 01, 2026.”
