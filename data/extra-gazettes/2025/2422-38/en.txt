Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Civil aviation (imposition of Charges for aerodrome facilities) regulations 
No. 01 of 2025.
2.	 The	 Service	Provider	specified	in	Column	(I)	of	the	Schedule	hereto	who	engages	in	the	provision	of	aeronautical	
services	in	Aerodromes	specified	in	Column	(II)	of	that	Schedule	in	terms	of	section	8	of	the	Civil	Aviation 
Act,	No.	14	of	2010	shall	pay	to	the	Director	General	of	the	Civil	Aviation	Authority	the	charge	specified	in	Column	
(iii) of that schedule for the usage of such aerodromes and the land appertaining to such aerodrome.
