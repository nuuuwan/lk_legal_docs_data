ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section  27(1) read with Section 21
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) read with Section 21 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, 
1, Harini Amarasuriya, Minister of Education, Higher Education and vocational Education on the recommendation of the 
University Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said 
Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1093/8 of August 17, 1999, (as amended from time to time) establishing the 
Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, by the substitution for the entry “Department of Applied Nutrition” appearing in column 
2, which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Livestock, Fisheries and Nutrition” appearing in column 1 of the Schedule of 
the following entry :-
“Department of Nutrition and Dietetics”.
 
Harini amarasuriya,
 Minister of Education, Higher Education and
 vocational Education.
colombo,
