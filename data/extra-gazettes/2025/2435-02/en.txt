ImPORTS  AND  EXPORTS  (CONTROL)  ACT,  No.  1  Of  1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-Section 4(1) and Section 14 of the Imports 
and Exports (Control) act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by act, No. 48 of 1985 and act, No. 28 of 1987, I, anura Kumara 
dissanayake, the minister of Finance, Planning and Economic development promulgate following Regulations.
  AnurA KumArA DissAnAyAKe,
  minister of Finance, Planning and Economic development.
ministry of Finance, Planning and Economic development,
Colombo 01,
may 03, 2025.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 05 of 2025”. 
2. The Regulation Nos. 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 13 and 15 of the Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 02 of 2025, published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2421/44 dated January 31, 2025, shall be applicable for importation of vehicles 
described in the Schedule A below, as appropriately.

3.  Special Import License Regulations No. 01 of 2023, published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2312/77 dated January 01, 
2023, as amended by Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 04 of 2025, published in the Gazette Extraordinary  
No. 2434/12 dated april 29, 2025 is hereby amended.
3.1 Goods, classified under the HS Code of 8703.80.72 together with its corresponding entries, described in the 
Schedule I of the Special Import License Regulations No. 01 of 2023, referred to above, are hereby deleted. 
3.2 Goods described in the Schedule B below together with their corresponding entries shall be inserted into the 
Schedule I of the Special Import License Regulations No. 01 of 2023 and Schedule B of the Imports and 
Exports (Control) Regulations No. 04 of 2025, referred to above.

4. To achieve the objectives of these Regulations, the Controller General of Imports and Exports shall issue “operational 
Instructions” to the director General of Customs, licensed banks or any other relevant authorities.
5. Final decision shall be made by the Controller General of the Imports and Exports on any issues arising in relation to 
these Regulations.
6. These Regulations shall enter into force with effect from may 05, 2025.
