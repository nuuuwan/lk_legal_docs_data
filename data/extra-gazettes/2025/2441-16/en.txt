uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25a  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. Harini amarasuriya, 
the Minister of education, Higher education and V ocational education having obtained a report under section 70C of the 
aforesaid act, in respect of sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in 
sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number GL 24, being recognized as a degree 
Awarding Institute, do by this Order and subject to the conditions specified in schedule I hereto, recognise the branch located 
at Balagolla, Kengalla, Kandy (Kandy Branch) of sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, to 
offer the degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding 
entry in Column II of the schedule II hereto, for the purpose of developing Higher education therein :-
sCHeduLe  I
Conditions
 1. sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited - Kandy Branch, shall be recognized to offer the 
Degrees specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to the five (5) year review conducted by the Specified Authority from 
the date of publication of this Order.
2. sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited - Kandy Branch, shall conduct the degree programmes 
specified in Schedule II hereto, only at Balagolla, Kengalla, Kandy.
