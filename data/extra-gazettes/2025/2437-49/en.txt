TOWN AND COUNTRY PLANNING ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 269)
Order under section 6(2)(b) and section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 6(2) (b) and section 21(2) (b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Anura Karunathilake, Minister of Urban Development, Construction and Housing do by this Order -
(a)  declare that the area of land called as " Asgiriya Raja Maha Viharaya " speciﬁ ed in the Schedule hereto shall be an 
urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance; and
(b)  direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for  the "Asgiriya Raja Maha Viharaya ", which is declared as 
above to be an urban development area.
