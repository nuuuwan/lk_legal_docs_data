THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No. :09/03/01/2021/03'
Minister of Land Ref.  No. :  4-3$11/2021/CEB/338'
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land for a public
purpose:
Description of the Land   to be Acquired :
The land below mentioned as Lot 3 and 4 as per folio 6 and
boundary plan No. 510021(Zone 2) dated 23.12.2024 made by
the Surveyor General which situated at Madithiyawala Village
in No. 46 Madithiyawala (Lower) Grama Niladhari Division in
Divulapitiya Dovisional Secretary's Division in the District of
Gampaha in Western Province.
01. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land : Madithiyawelawatta; Description: Home
Garden;Claimant: The Government;Original Claiment: Mr.
Thenahandi  Nandana Mendis, No.68, Temple Road,
Negombo.(For the purpose of finalizing the land acquisition
process) Extent : 0.0207Hectare
01. Lot No. 04
Name of the Land : Madithiyawelawatta; Description: Home
Garden;Claimant: The Government;Original Claiment:
Poddiwala Kankanamge Pathmanatha, No. 511/07, R.A.
Fernando Mawatha, Eeriyawetiya, Kiribathgoda, Kelaniya.(For
the purpose of finalizing the land acquisition process) Extent
: 0.0893Hectare
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of Divulapitiya
on 24.03.2025 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby  requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) before the 14.03.2025, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,  the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
