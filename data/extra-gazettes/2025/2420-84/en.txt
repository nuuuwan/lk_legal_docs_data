DISTRICT 
  
IT is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner 
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 110386 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Kanugahawewa 
(Block 08) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama 
Niladhari Division of No.28 - Kanugahawewa in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Kebithigollewa in the District of 
Anuradhapura in the Province of North Central should submit his 
claim to the undersigned before 3rd of February 2025 In the event 
of failure  to submit any claim before the above -mentioned date, 
action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to 
the land and accordingly publish such determination in the 
Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 110386 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s Office. 
 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
