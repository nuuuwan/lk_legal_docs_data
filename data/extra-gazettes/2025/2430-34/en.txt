STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr.K.B.S.Ekanayaka who had worked at the Kandy- South Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board and had been 
paid salary increments from February - April 2004, had been caused injustice by the non- payment of salary increments for
