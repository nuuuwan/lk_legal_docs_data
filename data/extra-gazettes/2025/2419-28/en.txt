ArAnA y AkA  Pradeshiya Sabha Gazette Notification. Read with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act,  No. 15 of 1987, 
published by the Hon. Governer of the Sabaragamuwa Province in the special Gazette No. 2316/29 and dated 24th January 
2023 to publish notices showing the preparation of plans and list of names of the roads and narrow paths maintained by the  
Pradeshiya Sabha of Sabaragamuwa Province. In accordance with the work assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha of Sabaragamuwa 
Province in the series of orders herein it is hereby announced for the public's knowledge that it has been decided under 
Secretary's decision No.40 dated 30.12.2024 that the roads and narrow roads included in the following schedule should be 
declared as roads and narrow roads owned by the Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
 If it is the opinion of the party claiming the said land that any road or narrow path included in the said Schedule passes 
through any or part of land, to file a suit in the competent court before the expiry of three months from the date of this 
notification to prove his/her right to the said land.
 And it is the further announced that every road and narrow path included in the said Schedule for which no case has been 
assigned before the expiry of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or narrow path owned by the 
Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
 P. G. U. Jayawikrama,
 Secretary and implementation officer,
 Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
