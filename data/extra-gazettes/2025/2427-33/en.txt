PART IV (B) - LOCAL  GOVERNMENT  
 
 
Local  Government  Notifications 
 
Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha 
 
Declaration as a Development Area 
 
AS the Honourable Governor of Sabaragamuwa Province has publish  in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2316/20 dated 
2022 January 24 to be read with the Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sa bha Act, No. 15 of 1987, "Orders on the preparation of 
plans and posting of notices showing the list of names of the r oads and lanes maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha of 
Sabaragamuwa Province" according to the assigned to the Pradesh iya Sabha of Sabaragamuwa Province and it is hereby 
published for the public that it has been decided under Decisio n No. 16 of 16.01.2025 and as Section 9(3) of Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act No.15 of 1987, that all said road and lanes mentioned  in the schedule below shall be owned by Pelmadulla 
Pradeshiya Sabha. 
 
If the party claiming the land considers that any road or lane mentioned in the said schedule is entering in any land or 
part of any land it is hereby announced that action should be taken to file a case in the competent court before expiry of a 
period of three month from the date of the said notification in order to prove right to the land. 
 
It is further informed that every road and lane mentioned in th e said schedule, for which no such case has been filed 
before the expiry of the said period of three months, shall be deemed to be a road or lane owned by the Pelmadulla 
Pradeshiya Sabha.

 carrying out the functions,
