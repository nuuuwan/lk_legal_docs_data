PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE 
IT is hereby notified that the Annual Esala Festival and Procession at the Historic Seenigama Sri Devol Maha Devalaya 
Situated  in the Grama Niladhari Division of Seenigama, Divisional Secretary's Division of Madampagama of the District of 
Galle, Southern Province, will be held during the period from Thursday, 21st of August 2025  to Thursday, 28th of August 
2025 as per the orders made under the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1965/39 dated 06.05.2016 of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175).
 W. A. Dharmasiri,
 District Secretary/Government Agent,
 Administrative District, Galle.
 
At the District Secretariat, Galle,
03rd of July, 2025.
