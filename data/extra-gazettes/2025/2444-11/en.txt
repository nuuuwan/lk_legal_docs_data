PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Sri Siththira Velayutha Swami Temple Annual Festival - 2025
DIVISIONAL  SECRETARIAT  OF  THIRUKKOVIL  DIVISION   IN  AMPARA  DISTRICT
IT is hereby notified for the information of the pilgrims who attened the above festival and others concerned that the above 
festival commences on 07.07.2025 and terminates on 24.07.2025 with water cutting ceremony.
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the above festival and others concerned is drawn to standing regulation 
published in the Government Gazette No. 10247 dated 11th May 1951 which will be in force during above festival.
The camp of the above has been enlarged as folows :
 North  : Maha Vidyalaya Road, Thambiluvil
 East : Fisheries Road, Thirukkovil,
 South : Beach Road, Thirukkovil,
 West : Main Road, Thirukkovil.
