DERANIYAGALA PRADESHIYA SABHA
It is hereby made a public notiﬁ cation to the public that, pursuant to the functions assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha of the 
Sabaragamuwa Province by the “Regulations on the preparation of plans and publication of notices indicating the names of the 
Roads and Lanes   maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha of the Sabaragamuwa Province” series published by the Hon. Governor 
of the Sabaragamuwa Province in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2316/20 and dated 24th January 2023, read with Section 24 of 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, it has been decided that the roads and lanes in the Schedule hereto shall be declared 
as roads and lanes belonged by the Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha, under Secretary decision No. 06 dated 30.12.2024.
 
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that if any party claiming ownership of any road or lane included in the said Schedule passes through any 
land or part of any land that someone belonged and the person shall be required to ﬁ le a case in the appropriate court to prove 
the ownership of the said land before the expiry of a period of three months from the date of this notiﬁ cation.
It is further notiﬁ ed that every road and lane included in the said Schedule for which no such case has been ﬁ led before the expiry 
of the said period of three months shall be deemed to be a road or lane owned by the Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
