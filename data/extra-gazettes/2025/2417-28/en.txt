04. It is informed to the public that the Election Commission has decided to revise the electoral register in the year 2025 
as follows.
05. The consolidated registers will be prepared for the enumeration of electors for the year 2025 by consolidating the main 
electoral register 2024 certified on 01.07.2024, supplementary electoral register 2024(1) certified on 07.08.2024, 
supplementary electoral register 2024(2) certified on 01.10.2024 and supplementary electoral register 2024(3) which 
is to be certified on 01.02.2025.
06. The Grama Niladhari of each division will act as the enumerator of that division. Special enumerators may also be 
deployed opportunely.
07. It is informed that the house-to-house visits of the enumerators to enumerate the electors will commence from 
01.01.2025, subject to paragraph 08 below.
08. The enumerator will not visit every household and will visit only in cases where an amendment to the 2024 consolidated 
electoral register is required, i.e.; in the event of a name or names being removed from the 2025 consolidated electoral 
register or a name or names not mentioned in the 2025 consolidated electoral register being added. Even if the 
enumerator does not visit the household, if there is an addition or deletion of a name/names under his/her household 
and if there is a need for an amendment to the name, National Identity Card number, or household number mentioned 
in the valid register, it is the responsibility of the chief occupant to meet the enumerator and act as instructed in this 
notice. If there are no amendments to be made, the elector will be registered under the address registered in the 2024 
valid electoral register. There is no need for the chief occupant to complete any form for this.
09. If an elector registered in 2024 is not eligible for registration in 2025 due to death or change of residence or any other 
reason, inform to enumerator. Those who are thus disqualified will be listed 'A' and removed from the valid register. 
10. If a person was not registered in 2024 or is registering as an elector for the first time upon reaching the age of 18 years, 
or due to a change of residence or any other reason, complete Form 2025/ER and submit it to the enumerator to register 
as an elector in 2025. Further details are given in Form 2025/ER itself. Those who are not included in the valid register 
but who are eligible to get their name included in the register under a qualifying address will be included in the List 
'B' and included in the 2025 register.
11. Those who complete 18 years of age as on 01.02.2025 i.e. born on or before 31.01.2007 are eligible to register in the 
main register of 2025.
12. Notices regarding the exhibition of 'A' and 'B' lists, receiving of claims and objections will be published on 02.05.2025, 
in terms of the Registration of Electors Act. Claims and objections should be submitted within 28 days from the date 
of publication of the notice.
13. Information is collected through YC forms for the following 06 supplementary electoral registers to be certified in the 
future. The exhibition of supplementary lists, receiving of claims and objections will be notified in future public 
notices.
