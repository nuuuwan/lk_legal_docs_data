EXTRAORDINARY 
The Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: WGSWD & EIP/ACQ/KB/41. 
Ministry of Agriculture, Land, Livestock and  
Irrigation Ref. No.: 4/3/10/2016/LRB/504. 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government has 
acquired the allotments of the lands -for the widening and 
development of Ambalan Ela which runs across the Bus 
Route No. 120, under Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage and 
Environment Improvement Project.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
  
An allotment of land depicted as Lot No. 01 in Sheet 
No. 06, Block No. 03 of the Cadastral Map No. 521239 
dated 10.04.2025 prepared by the Surveyor General, 
situated in the Village called Katuwawala (Ward No. 09 
of Katuwawala, within the Urban Council limits of 
Boralesgamuwa) in the Grama Niladhari Division  
No. 578 A of Katuwawala in Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Kesbewa in Colombo District of the Western 
Province and bounded as follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Muththettuwa Wela Kumbura;  
Description: Home garden, a permanent building and a 
well is situated;  Claimant: State; Original Claimant:   
Mrs. Anoja Kumari Bulathsinghala, No. 75/74, Sandun 
Uyana, Mabulgoda, Pannipitiya; Extent: 0.0325 Hectare. 
 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
on which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Land Acquisition Office located in 
the Head Office of the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and 
Development Corporation, No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura 
Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on 28.05.2025 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 21.05.2025, the nature of 
their interests in the land/lands, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 H. Thilakawardhana, 
 Acquiring Officer,  
 Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage 
 & Environment Improvement Project. 
 Divisional Secretary’s Division - Kesbewa.
