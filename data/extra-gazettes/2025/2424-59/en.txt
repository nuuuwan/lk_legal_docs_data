Order under Section  27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
by virtue of powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
I, dr. harini amarasuriya, the Minister of education, higher education and Vocational education having obtained a report 
under section 70C of the aforesaid act, in respect of the sri Lanka Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited, 
a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number GL 24, 
being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the Order made under the said 
act and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2415/20 of december 16, 2024, by the addition immediately after entry 
“bachelor of science honours in Psychology” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, 
of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in 
schedule I hereto :-
