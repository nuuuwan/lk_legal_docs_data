THE Sri Lanka Ports Authority (hereinafter referred as ‘SLPA’) acting by virtue and in terms of powers vested under the 
Section 42A(1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act No. 51 of 1979 as amended by Sri Lanka Ports Authority (Amendment) 
Act No. 7 of 1984 hereby certifies that ABCC Phoenix Shipping (Pvt) Limited, (Company Registration No. PV 92715) 
incorporated under the laws of Sri Lanka having it’s registered office at No. 56, St. Lucia’s Street, Colombo 13, being the 
Shipping Agent of G P Shipping Services Ltd (Manning Agent) of Greenhill Road, Peterhead, AB42, ADQ, Scotland has 
refused, failed and/or neglected to pay the charges due to SLPA, as morefully described in the schedule appended to this 
certificate and the respective charges are now due and owing from the aforementioned ABCC Phoenix Shipping (Pvt) 
Limited, to the SLPA amounting to a sum of Sri Lankan Rupees Nine Million Eight Hundred Eighty Thousand Seven 
Hundred Forty Nine and Fifty Three cents. (Rs.9, 880,749.53) as at 31.12.2022.
