THE  PERSONAL  DATA  PROTECTION  ACT,  No. 9  OF  2022
Order under Subsection (3) of Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (3) of Section 1 of the Personal Data Protection Act, No. 9 of 2022, read 
with paragraph (3) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, i, President, Anura 
Kumara Dissanayake do by this Order, amend the Order made under Subsection (3) of Section 1 of the aforesaid Act and 
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2366/08 of January 08, 2024 by the repeal of paragraph (b) thereof. 
 ANuRA KuMARA DISSANAyAKE,
