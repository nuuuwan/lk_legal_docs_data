NOTICE No. : 82/0325 Matara District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the 
ownership or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels 
of lands reflected in the Cada stral Map No. 820015 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Isadeen Town 
(Block 08) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama 
Niladhari Division of No.414 C - Isadeen Town in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Matara in the District of Matara in the 
Province of Southern should submit his claim to the undersigned 
before 21st of July 2025. In the event of failure  to submit any 
claim before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to 
hold an ex parte inquiry to determine title to the land and 
accordingly publish such determination in the Government 
Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 820015 refe rred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretary's, 
District Survey Office, Office  of the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s Office. 
 
Further information may be obt ained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
