Co-operative Societies Statue, No. 06 of 2019 of the Southern Province Provincial Council Orders on 
Delegation of Powers
SINCE the Southern Provincial Council has been dissolved, the powers vested in the Minister in charge of the subject of 
Co-operatives of the Southern Provincial Council by  Section 2. (4) of the Co- operative Societies Statute No. 06 of 2019 of 
the Southern Province Provincial Council, have been assigned to me by Article 154 (C) of the Constitution of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and in terms of such powers, I,  Madduma Kankanamalage Bandula Harischandra, the 
Governor of the Southern Province, hereby delegate all powers mentioned in Column III of the Schedule below, which are 
vested in the Commissioner Registrar of Co-operative Societies in terms of the Co-operative Societies Statue No. 06 of 2019 
of the Southern Province Provincial Council and the rules made there under, and all powers mentioned in Column III that 
are not inconsistent with the Co-operative Societies (amendment ) Act, No. 05 of 1972 read with the preamble of the said 
Co-operative Societies Statute, to the person mentioned in Colu mn I of the Schedule below to assist the Commissioner of 
Co-operative Development of the Southern Province, from the date mentioned in Column II of the said Schedule.
