SRI LANKA TELECOMMUNICATIONS ACT, No. 25 OF 1991 
RULES  made by the Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of Sri Lanka under Section 68 read with Section 17 B  
of the Sri Lanka Telecommunications Act, No. 25 of 1991 as amended by Sri Lanka Telecommunications (Amendment) Act, 
No. 27 of 1996 and Sri Lanka Telecommunications (Amendment) Act, No. 39 of 2024 with the approval of the Minister of

 Chairman,
 Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of  
 Sri Lanka.
Colombo,
On this 18th July, 2025.
Rules
The Provider License Rules, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2395/34 of 31 st July, 2024  is hereby amended as set 
out below: -  
1. The following paragraph shall be inserted immediately after item 4.1 of Schedule 4 as 4.1 (a) 
 4.1 (a) The licensee is authorized to provide the following infrastructure services:-
4.1.1  Line, Submarine Cable, Distribution Point services
4.1.2 Tower, Mast, Pole, Antenna, Structure services
4.1.3  Duct, Pit, Tunnel, Manhole services
4.1.4  Active network used for the purpose of providing telecommunication service
2. The following paragraph shall be inserted immediately after item 4.18 of Schedule 4 as 4.18 (a) 
4.18  (a)   The licensee is authorized to provide following telecommunication services :-
4.18.1 V oice Services
4.18.2 Data/Broadband Services
4.18.3  Value Added Services
4.18.4 Television/Audio Services
3.  To add clause 4 after clause   3 to the format of the license set out in Schedule 2 as follows:-
 4. The licensee shall pay during the currency of the license on or before 31st January of each year following the issue 
of the license, a levy of Cess imposed under Section 22G of the Act, 
4.    The following paragraph shall be added immediately after item 4.17 of Schedule 4 as 4.17 (a)
 4.17 (a) The licensee shall not assign or lease the Infrastructure Services License or part  thereof to any third party 
issued under these rules. 
5.    The following paragraph shall be added immediately after item 4.39 under Schedule 4 as 4.39 (a)
 4.39 (a) The licensee shall not assign or lease the Telecommunications Service License or part thereof to any third party 
issued under these rules.
6.   The following paragraph shall be added immediately after item 4.58 under Schedule 4 as 4.58 (a)
 4.58 (a) The licensee shall not assign or lease the Cable Landing Station Facility License or part thereof to any third 
party issued under these rules.
