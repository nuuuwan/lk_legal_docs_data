UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  N o. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Harini Amarasuriya, 
the Minister of Education, Higher Education and V ocational Education having obtained a report under section 70C of the 
aforesaid Act, in respect of "Sri Lanka Institute of Textile and Apparel", established by the Sri Lanka Institute of Textile and 
Apparel Act, No. 12 of 2009 and located at Kandawala Estate, No. 02, General Sri John Kotalawala Mawatha, Ratmalana, 
do by this Order subject to such conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize "Sri Lanka Institute of Textile and 
Apparel" as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of 
the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in  
Column II of the Schedule II hereto:-
SCHEDULE  I
Conditions
 1. Sri Lanka Institute of Textile and Apparel (hereinafter referred to as "institute") shall, when admitting students for the 
degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science in Textile and Apparel Studies 
with a minimum of 90 credits (SLQF Level 5), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of;
 (a) At least three (3) simple passes (S) in any stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE Advanced Level 
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification And
