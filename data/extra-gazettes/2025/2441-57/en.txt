Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Anura Karunathilake, Minister of Urban Development, Construction and Housing do by this Order –
 ( a) declare that the area of land called as “Purana Athkanda Rajamaha Viharaya” speci ﬁed in the Schedule hereto
