LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: DIVPE/LND/ACQUI/5/2/1. 
Land Ministry's Ref No.: 4-3/18/2019/WS/485. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for the construction of a 2000 cubic 
meter underground reservoir at Rambawewa and a 450 
cubic meter water tower for the proposed Katupotha - 
Bamunakotuwa - Panduwasnuwara Water Supply Project. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
  
Land depeicted in the Sheet No. 1 Block No. 02 of the 
Cadastral Map No.  420849 of prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys-Kurunegala, situated in the 
Village called Paathamulla in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 1362 of Gallehepitiya in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of  Panduwasnuwara East in 
Kurunegala District of the North Western Province and 
described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 1  
Name of the Land: Dangahamulahena; Current use of 
the land: Estate; Claimant: Government; First Owner:  
Mr. Wijesingha Aarachchilage Sunil Wijesingha, 
Ranaviru Pradeep Mawatha, Paathamulla, 
Kanaththewewa; Extent: 0.20 18 Hectare. (1rood 39.8 
perches) 
 
02. Lot No. 2  
Name of the Land: --; Current use of the land: Estate; 
Claimant: Government; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare (0.2 
perches). 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Panduwasnuwara East 
Divisional Secretariat on 20th of August in 2025 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requeste d to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 20th of August in 13th of 
August in 2025 the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their clai ms for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
