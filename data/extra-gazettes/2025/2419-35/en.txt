Western  Province  Provincial  council 
Notification  made  under  section  3(8)  of  the  Road  Passenger  Transport  Services  Statute,  
No. 01  of  1992  of  the  Western  Provincial  Council
As the requirement for restoration and reappointment of members to the Board of Management of the Western Provincial Road 
Passenger Transport Authority has arisen, as per the powers entrusted to me under Article 154 c and 154 f of the Constitution 
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and  under Section 3(8) of the Road Passenger Transport Services Statute 
No. 01 of 1992 of the Western Provincial Council, as the Governor of  Western Province, I do hereby announce that the 
following mentioned persons will be removed from the membership of the Board of Management of the Western Provincial 
Road Passenger Transport Authority. 
1.  Mr. Indika Hapugoda
2.  Mr. Mahendra Wickramaarachchi
3.  Mr. H. G. Koralearachchi
4.  Mr. H. M. Lakindu Gajasen Herath
 Hanif Yusoof,
 The Governor,
 Western Province.
At Governor’s Office - Western Province,
Located in the Western Provincial Council Building,
Battaramulla,
On the 20th day of December, 2024.
