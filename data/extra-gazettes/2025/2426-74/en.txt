2a
Appointment of a fellow member 
01(a) 
in the event of appointment as a fellow member to the council, the individual must have maintained continuous and active 
membership in the category of Member of Sri Lanka Council of Archaeologists (MSLCA) for a period exceeding five years; 
and 
 
 The individual must provide evidence of having functioned as a professional in the field of archaeology for a minimum period 
of ten years after obtaining their first degree.
