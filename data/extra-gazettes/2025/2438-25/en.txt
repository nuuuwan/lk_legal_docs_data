UPGRADING  TYPE “A”  DIvISIONAL HOSPITAL  –  BANDARAwELA TO A  TYPE  “B”  BASE HOSPITAL
divisioNal Hospital - Bandarawela, located in the Badulla district and under the administration of the uva Provincial 
Ministry of Health, has a long-term need to expand its healthcare services in order to provide secondary healthcare not only 
to the residents of the Bandarawela area but also to local and foreign tourists. in response to this need, the Ministry of Health 
and Mass Media has decided to upgrade the Type “a” divisional Hospital – Bandarawela to a Type “B” Base Hospital with 
immediate effect.
   Accordingly, this Hospital will be developed as a Type “B” Base Hospital, offering the basic specialist treatment 
services specified in General Circular No. 01-18/2020 dated 03.03.2020 – namely, Medical, Surgical, Obstetrics & 
Gynaecology, Paediatrics, Anaesthesia, Psychiatry, Eye Surgery, Radiology, Pathology (including Histopathology), 
Dermatology, and Accident and Emergency Care. In addition, Integrated Ayurveda Wellness facilities will  also be provided.
   Furthermore, the Hospital will continue to be administered under the Uva Provincial Council, and the head of the 
Hospital will be designated as the Medical superintendent. 
   dr. Anil Jasinghe,
   secretary,
   Ministry of Health & Mass Media.
