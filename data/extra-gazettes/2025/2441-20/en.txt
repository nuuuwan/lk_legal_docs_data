filling  of  a  v acancy  in  the  office  of  member  of  the  balangoda  Pradeshiya

 (chaPter  262)  
WHEREAS the office of member of Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Jathika Jana  Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Durayalaya 
Ranjith Udaya Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ward Number 02 - Rassagala of Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Gama Ethige Ariyadasa to fill such vacancy;
therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Gama Ethige Ariyadasa 
was selected as the member of the Ward Number 02 - Rassagala of Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
