EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE
SAMMANTHURAI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member of the Eastern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognised political party  to which Ibrahim
Mohamed Mohamed Mansoor  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person as a  member of that
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of
thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr.  Ismalebbe Najeem being
an eligible person to fill vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare Mr. Ismalebbe Najeem elected as  a member of   the Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
25th March, 2013.
