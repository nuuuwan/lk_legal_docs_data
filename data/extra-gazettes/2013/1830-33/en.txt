ELECTORAL  DISTRICT  NO. 01 - C OLOMBO
NOTICE  is hereby given under Section 13(2) of  the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, that —
(1) List “A” containing names of persons in the register of electors who are dead or have become
disqualified ; and

and copy of the relevant  “A” and “B” lists.
