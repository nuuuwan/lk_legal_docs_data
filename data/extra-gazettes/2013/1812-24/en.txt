Order No. 24
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20 (5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader or distributor shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display for sale Broiler
Chicken Meat with skin above the Maximum Retail Price specified below ;-
* Maximum Retail Price of 1kg of Broiler Chicken Meat with skin (whole chicken or any parts) - Rs. 380
sold with a Brand Name
* Maximum Retail Price of 1kg of Broiler Chicken Meat with skin (whole chicken or any parts) - Rs. 370
sold without a Brand Name
                                                                By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
30th May  2013.
