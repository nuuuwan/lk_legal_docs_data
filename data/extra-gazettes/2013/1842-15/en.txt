PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a V acancy  in  the  Western  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS, the secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western Province Provincial
Council by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which  the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated  Mr. Abeygunawardena
Pahalage Piyal Jayantha to fill such vacancy :
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapirya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare  Mr. Abeygunawardena Pahalage Piyal Jayantha, elected
as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Kalutara in respect of which such vancancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Rajagiriya,
26th  December, 2013.
