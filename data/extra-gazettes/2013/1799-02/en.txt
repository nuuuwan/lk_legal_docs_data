THE  NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No. 02 OF 1974
Order under Section 64(1)
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under Section 64(1) of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 02 of 1974.
I, Dinesh Chandra Rupasinghe Gunawardene, the Minister of Water Supply and Drainage, do by this order, embody in the
Schedule hereto the Provisions of the voluntary transfer scheme approved by me under Section 63(1) of the aforesaid Law,
in respect of the Water Supply undertakings on by the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
D
INESH CHANDRA RUPASINGHE GUNAW ARDENE,
Minister of Water Supply and Drainage.
Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage,
No. 35, New Parliament Road,
Lakdiya Medura,
Pelawatta - Baththaramulla,
17th January, 2013.
SCHEDULE
Contents of V oluntary Transfer Scheme Section 61 (1)
(a) The following movable and immovable property to be transferred to and vested in the National Water
Supply and Drainage Board to wit ;
All trunks and distribution mains with servitude for water mains, spares standby equipment and stores
accessories to operate and maintain the Water Supply Scheme situated within the limits of Matara Pradeshiya
Sabha area mention in the following schedule ;
(b) The rights, obligations and liabilities of the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha together with any contract the council
has entered in to in connection with design, supply, installation, constructions, operation and maintenance
with respect of the aforesaid property will also be transferred to the Board ;
(c) All agreements entered in to with water consumers with regard to conditions of the use and supply of water
services and payment to obtain such services will also be transferred to the Board ;
