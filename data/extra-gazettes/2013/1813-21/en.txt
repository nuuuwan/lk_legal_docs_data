No. 1813/21 – THURSDAY,  JUNE 06, 2013
My No.: IR/10/03/2012.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between–
Mr. K. P. Wickramanayake, C/O Somaratne  Kade, Polgahawela  Road, Panakawa, Kegalle of the one part
AND
Kelani Cables Ltd, No. 14, Wewelduwa, Kelaniya of the other part,
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4(1)
of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts Nos. 14
of 1957, 4 of 1962,  and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial disputes-Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. S. M. S.
Jayawardane, No. 213,  1st Lane, Egodawatta, Boralesgamuwa to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by
arbitration.
G AMINI  LOKUGE ,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
03rd May, 2013.
Colombo.

Mr. K. P . Wickramanayake, C/O Somaratne Kade, Polgahawela Road, Panakawa, Kegalle of the one part
AND
Kelani Cables Ltd. No. 14, Wewelduwa, Kelaniya of  the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether  Mr. K. P. Wickramanayake, who had worked for Kelani Cables Limited as a Fitter since 25.11.1988 had been caused
injustice by been suspended from service from 23/11/2005,  and being expelled from service with effect from 24.11.2005 after the conduct
of a disciplinary inquiry on 19.10.2006, and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
V . B. P . K. WEERASINGHE ,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 10th day of April, 2013.
