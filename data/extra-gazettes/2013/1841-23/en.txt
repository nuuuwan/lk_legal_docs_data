SRI  LANKA  ELECTRICITY  ACT,  No.  20  OF  2009
Order under Section 52
BY virute of the powers vested in me by Section 52 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20  of 2009 and upon the recommendations
made by the Minister in charge of the subject of Power and Energy I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka being of the opinion that -
(a) the land specified in the Schedule hereto is urgently required for the construction of a Power Transmission
Project ; and
(b) the said Power Transmission Project being a public purpose would serve for the general welfare and benefit of the
public .
do by this order declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
18th December 2013.
