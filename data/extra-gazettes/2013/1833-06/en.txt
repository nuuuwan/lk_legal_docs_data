EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  HINGURAKGODA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Hingurakgoda   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  R. M. Rambanda the   recognized  political party  to which United People’s
Freedom Alliance  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Hingurakgoda   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Disanayake Mudeyanselage
Dhanuka Ishara Rathnayake   being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Indika Haththurushingha  Returning Officer  of   Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha,   acting  in  terms
of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Disanayake Mudeyanselage
Dhanuka Ishara Rathnayake  elected as  a member of   the Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
INDIKA HA THTHURUSHINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
12th  October, 2013.
