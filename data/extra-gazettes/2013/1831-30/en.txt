THE WAGES BOARDS ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES BOARD FOR THE ENGINEERING TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Section 29 (3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that the decisions of the Wages
Board for the Engineering Trade made under Section 30 of that ordinance and Specified in the Schedule hereto have been
approved by the Honorable Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decisions shall come into force on the 01st of 11 2013.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo 05,
03rd of October, 2013.

PART IV
Weekly Holiday (Section 24)
The following new section is hereby inserted immediately after section 2(b) of wages board decisions of Engineering
Trade.
''In accordance with the provision made by the section 25 of the Wages Boards Ordinance, when a public holiday
falls on such weekly holiday the public holiday shall be granted on that day and the deprived weekly holiday shall be
allowed on a day within the six days succeeding next before reaching the weekly holiday."
