No. 1803/25 – THURSDAY, MARCH 28, 2013
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Four (04)  allotments of land described  below in total extent of
about 6807.0 sq.m. depicted in the Advance Tracing No. 158 dated
24.02.1982, situated in the Village of Chavakachcheri in the Assistant
Government Agent's Division of Thenmarachchi in Jaffna District of
the Northern Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot No. 01 – Kadatkaraikiraivayalum, Thalaiyadium –
Extent: About 841.8 sq.m.
North: Path;  East: Lot No. 02 of this plan belonging to Samoon
Sukura Ummah; South: Lot No. 04 of this plan belonging to Kanthan
Nagarajha and some others; West: Thanankilappu Road.
02. Lot No. 02 – Kadatkaraikiraivayalum, Thalaiyadium –
Extent: About 385.6 sq.m.
North: Path;  East: Lot No. 03 of this plan belonging to S. Karalirajha;
South: Lot No. 04 of this plan belonging to Kanthan Nagarajha and
some others; West: Lot No. 01 belonging to Sivakolunthu, wife of
Shanmugasundaram and Singan Sivarasa.
03. Lot No. 03 – Kadatkaraikiraivayalum, Thalaiyadium –
Extent: About 491.4 sq.m.
North: Path;  East: State land and land of Kaliappan Ramu, wife of
Sothippillai; South: State land and Lot No. 04 of this plan belonging
to Kanthan Nagarajha and some others; West: Lot No. 02 of this plan
belonging to Samoon Sukura Ummah.
04. Lot No. 04 – Kadatkaraikiraivayalum, Thalaiyadium –
Extent: About 5088.2 sq.m.
North: Lot Nos. 01, 02 and 03 belonging to Sivalokunthu, wife of
Shanmugasundaram and some others;  East: State land; South: State
land and Tank; West: Thanankilappu Road.
