AMENDMENT
THE   COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Amendment for the Notice Published in Extra ordinary Gazette No. 1816/1  of 26.06.2013
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “Sirimath  R. G . Senanayake
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of
the subject of Local Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which
shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
