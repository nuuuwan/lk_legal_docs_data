THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Twelve Lots of Land  containing in extent of about 5.2072 Hectares,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No.: R/ELP/2010/0021 of
30.06.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Niriella in Minor Division of Palle Paththuwa in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Elapatha of Ratnapura District in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' –  Extent: 1.8948 Hectares
North: Pradeshiya Sabha Road, Labuwel Kanaththa occupied by
D. A. Karunarathna, Horagaha Kanaththa occupied by Pabilis
Appuhamy Labuwel Mandiya and Lot B occupied by A. H. Nimal
Premasiri and others; East: Labuwel Kanaththa occupied by D. A.
Karunarathna, Horagaha Kanaththa occupied by Pabilis Appuhamy,
Labuwel Mandiya and Lot B occupied by A. H. Nimal Premasiri and
others, Bulathgewatta occupied by N. V . Kularathna, Lots C and D;
South: Bulathgewatta occupied by N. V . Kularathna, Lots C, D, E, N
and F;West: Lots E, N, F and Pradeshiya Sabha Road.
02. Lot 'B' –  Extent: 0.1371 Hectare
North: Labuwel Mandiya, occupied by A. H. Nimal Premasiri and
others and Pradeshiya Sabha Road; East: Pradeshiya Sabha Road
and Bulathgewatta occupied by N. V . Kularathna; South:
Bulathgewatta occupied by N. V . Kularathna and Lot A;West: Lot A
and Labuwel Mandiya, occupied by A. H. Nimal Premasiri and
others.

Lots D and A.
04. Lot 'D' –  Extent: 0.0372 Hectare
North: Lots A and C; East: Lots A and C, Bulathgedola and Lot E;
South: Bulathgewatta and Lot E;West: Lots E and A.
05. Lot 'E' –  Extent: 0.1009 Hectare
North: Lot A; East: Lots A  and D, Bulathgedola and Lot N; South:
