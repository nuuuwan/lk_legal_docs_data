THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON

Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between,

arbitration.

and
Affno (Pvt.) Ltd., No. 56, Kynsey Road, Colombo 07 of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTERS IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
01. Whether the non reimbursement of Travelling and Maintenance Expenses and Fuel Allowance of Motor vehicle to Mr. E. P. R. A. Emil,
who served as the Business Development Manager of Affno (Pvt) Ltd., from June 2009 to May 2010 is just, and if not so, to what relief he
is entitled.
and
02. Whether Mr. E. P. R. A. Emil is entitled for wages for the unutilized leave of year 2009 and 2010, and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
