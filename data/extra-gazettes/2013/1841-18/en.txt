THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a Public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 An allotment of called Arulmeehu Sri Sangarnathar
Mahakanapathipillaiyar Kovil land depicted as Lot 'A' in the Advance
Tracing No. Ya/KYT/ 2012/ 77 dated 27.04.2012 prepared by the
Surveyor -General,  in the Village of Analathivu in the Divisional
Secratary's Division  of Kayts, Jaffna  District, Northern Province
and containing in total extent of 0.0500 Hectare and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘‘A’’ - Arulmeehu Sri Sangarnathar
Mahakanapathipillaiyar Kovil land  - Extent : 0.0500 Hectare
North: Land called Panaivalavu Garden claimed by Shanmugam
Rajarathinam  ; East: Land claimed by Arulmeehu Sri Sangarnathar
Mahakanapathipillaiyar Kovil land, Garden property of Arulmeehu
Sri Sangarnathar Mahakanapathipillaiyar Kovil ; South : Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha) from houses to Jatty; West: Land called
Panaivalavu Garden claimed by Ponnambalam Makandu.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
 Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Ref. No.: 4/3/9/ 2011/ WS/ 214.
