Notice under Section 33
My No.: GCF/ATH/CB/38/LR.

The public are hereby notified that the compensation payment in
respect of allotments of land of which the claims were not presented
and compensation was refused, as depicted in Schedules 01 to 02
below and included in Preliminary Plan No. 8160 prepared on
26.05.2000 by Surveyor General that have been vested in the
Government through Proviso 38(a) No.89 of 1993 published in the
Gazette Notification No. 770/08 dated 09.06.1993 under Greater
Colombo Flood Control and Environment Improvement Project have
been deposited in the District Court, Colombo under case numbers
depicted in the same schedule under Section 33 of the Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotments mentioned below could obtain the compensation payable
to the allotment concerned after submitting necessary documents to
prove their ownership to the District Court, Colombo.
