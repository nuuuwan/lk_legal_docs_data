EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  TRINCOMALEE  URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Trincomalee Urban Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of  the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front   the recognized  political party  to which P. W. Susantha
Jayalath    the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person
eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that  Trincomalee Urban Council   who belongs to  such group  who
shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. K. B. Udayasiri an eligible
person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nalaka Rathnayake,   Returning Officer  of   Trincomalee Urban Council ,   acting  in  terms of Section
65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. K. B. Udayasiri, elected as  a member
of   the Trincomalee Urban Council.
N
ALAKA RATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
22nd   November, 2013.
