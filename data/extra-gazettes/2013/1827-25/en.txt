PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2   OF  1988
Notice under Section 125(A)
Polling Stations which are located outside of the Administrative District where the voters are registered
ACCORDING  to the Section No. 125(A) of  Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, Commissioner of Elections has
determined following polling stations to allow to cast his vote of any registered elector, who is compelled by conditions
prevailing in the area within which his polling station is situated to reside outside such areas and who reasonably fears that
he could be unable to cast his vote at such polling station and made an application to the Commissioner of Elections on the
above matter.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat, Rajagiriya,
13th  September, 2013.
