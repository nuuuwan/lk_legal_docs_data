URBAN   COUNCILS
Southern     Province   Provincial   Council
I, Gustingnawadu Shan Wijayalal de Silva, the Chief Minister and the Minister of  Finance, Planning, Law and Order,
Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine and Local Government
of the Southern Province Provincial Council,  do hereby rescind the regulations made by me relevant to the travelling
expenses of Chairmen, Vice Chairmen and Members of Urban Councils and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1780/20 dated 18.10.2012 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Further, under the powers vested in me in terms of Section 193(2)(h) of the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255)
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, hereby impose the following
regulations made by me pertaining to the travelling expenses of Chairmen, Vice Chairmen and Members of the Urban
Councils. These regulations shall take effect from 01.01.2012.

02. A Chairman, Vice Chairman or member of an Urban Council who uses his own vehicle or any mode of public
transport on his official functions as a people's representative or on any functions assigned to him by the council, may be

by him.
03. If the motor car/three wheeler or the motor cycle so used is not his own, it could be treated as own vehicle if the
following documents relating to the motor car/three wheeler or the motor cycle concerned, are produced.
(i) Certified copy of the certificate of registration ;
(ii) Certified copy of the annual revenue licence ;
(iii) Certified copy of the valid insurance certificate ; and
(iv) An affidavit sworn by the owner of the vehicle authorising the use of the motor car/three wheeler or the
motor cycle concerned.
04. The fuel bills or if the travelling was undertaken by public transport, the relevant bills, shall be submitted when
claiming the travelling allowance.
05. The approval of the council shall be obtained before the payment of the said travelling allowance and financial
provision shall have been provided for the purpose in the annual budget of the council.
