Government Notifications
THE PILGRIMAGE ORDINANCE
SITTANDY  Sri - Sithira  Velayutha  Suwamy  Temple Festival -  2013
IT is hereby notified  for the information of the pilgrims who attend the above festival and of the others concerned that the
above festival commences on 05th August, 2013 with the ceremony of hoisting the flag and  terminates on the 20th of
August, 2013.
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the above festival and others concerned is drawn to the regulation
published in the government Gazette No.  9886 of 16.07.1984 which will be inforce during the period of the festival.
U. U
THAYASHRITHER,
Divisional Secretary.
Divisional Secretariat,
Eravur Pattu,
Chenkalady.
