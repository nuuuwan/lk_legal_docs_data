PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the   Secretary-General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (1) of the Parliamentary  Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  Seventh Parliament
by reason of Resignation of  Mr. Dayasiri Jayasekara, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral
District No. 15 - Kurunegala under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act.
And whereas the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed
by me under Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of Article 99
of the Constitution ;
And whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return
in Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr.  Nalin Nilantha Bandara Wijerathne
Jayamaha Hitihamilage was declared elected as a  Member of Parliament for the said electoral district ;
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested
in me by Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. Nalin Nilantha Bandara Wijerathne
Jayamaha Hitihamilage has been  declared elected as a member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District
No. 15 - Kurunegala  to fill the  vacancy caused by reason of  resignation of Mr. Dayasiri Jayasekara.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
30th July, 2013.
