THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 04/03/01/09.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: LD/05/2010/WS/272.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three allotments of land called Kalemodara Ovita and Pitakattiya
in total extent 0.3379 Hectare depicted as lot No. 1, 2 and 3 in
Preliminary Plan No. P .P.Ka 4393 prepared by the Surveyor-Gen-
eral situated in the village of Pahalagoda in Grama Niladhari Division
of 815B Pahalagoda in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Madurawala in Kalutara District Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Kalemodara Ovita and Pitakattiya; Own-
ers: A part of the land Depicted as block No. 12 of the P.P.A. 889
original claimant is not to be found. Possession of the land is under
Mr. Premarathne Withana living in Pahalagoda-Nahalla; Extent:
0.1919 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Kalemodara Ovita and Pitakattiya; Owners:
Part of the Kuda Ganga; Extent: 0.010 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land: Kalemodara Ovita and Pitakattiya; Own-
ers: A part of the land Depicted as block Nos. 11, 12, 13, 16, 17, 18,
21, 22, 23 and 24 - Part of Gam Sabha Road Orginal Claiment is not
to be found; Extent: 0.1353 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be acquired are
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat Madurawala on the
02nd day of September, 2013 at 10.00 o'clock, and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
26th day of August, 2013 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of the compu-
tation of such amount.
M
ENAKA PRIYANTHA ABEYRATHNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Madurawala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Madurawala.
03rd July 2013.
