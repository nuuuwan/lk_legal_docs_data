SERVICE  MINUTE  OF  THE  INLAND  REVENUE  SERVICE  OF  SRI  LANKA
APPROV AL of the Public Service Commission was received to make following amendments to the Gazette Notification of the
Service Minute of the Inland Revenue Service of Sri Lanka which was published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 1804/50 dated 5th Firday, April 2013.
Paragraph Proposed Amendment
No.
19.3 (i) Should have completed thirteen (13) years of active and satisfactory service which aggregates the period
of service completed in Class II Grade II, Class II Grade I as per the previous service minute and Grade II as per
this service minute. An active and satisfactory service period of three years (03) in Grade II should be included
into this period of service.
Note :
For the clause "An active and satisfactory service period of there years (03) in Grade II" in the above paragraph ;
1. three years (03) of an active and satisfactory service period in Class II Grade I under the previous service
minute ; or
2. three years (03) of an active and satisfactory service period in Grade II under the current service minute;
or
3. an aggregate of three years (03) of an active and satisfactory service period of above (1) and (2)
would be considered.
19.3 (ii) Should have Passed First and second Departmental at examinations as stated in previous service minute
which was prevailed prior to this service minute.
19.4 Officers who have completed 25 years of active service in Grade II-II and above of the Sri Lanka Inland
Revenue Service and who are eligible to be granted promotions to the Special Grade according to the provisions
specified in above paragraph 19.4 will be released from acquiring the post graduate requirement stipulated by
Section 11.3 (i) of the minute. The concession will be in force from 01.10.2012 to 31.12.2015.
