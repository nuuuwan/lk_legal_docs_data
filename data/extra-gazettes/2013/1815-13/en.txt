Reference No.: lem%df,a/w;a 3/1/75.
I, hereby inform that the government has intended to obtain the land
mentioned below for a public purpose under Sub-section (1) of
Section 07 of Land Acquisition Act as amended by Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964.
Public purpose required :
Acquiring land to implement Orizon Power Project in a land of 12
acres in garbage yard premises of Karadiyana.
  Details of land which is supposed to acquire:
Lot No. 1 and 2 mentioned in plan No. PPCo 9338 situated in
Thumbowila and Werahara villages of 576A Thumbowila North
Grama Niladari Division of Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat Division
of Colombo District, Western Province. (Details have been annexed)
Everyone who claim ownership, right or other relations to the
land mentioned above should meet me by their own or by persons
authorized by them in write on 17th July, 2013 at 9.30 a.m. at
Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat.
Further, the nature of their right, details with regard to matters to
claim compensations for obtaining the said land, amount of the
compensation and the way that amount is calculated should be
informed to me by a document accompanied with two copies.
