EXTRAORDINARY
1A
I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of  the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare  under
Section 10A(1)(a) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ajith Anura Talangamaarachchi of the Bandaragama
Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the People’s
Liberation Front.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Bandaragama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
13th  March, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   DAMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of  a Vice Chairman in  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Jagoda Arachchige Mangala Mahinda Gunawardana,  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me  to decide upon the
member of that Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as   Vice Chairman within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
