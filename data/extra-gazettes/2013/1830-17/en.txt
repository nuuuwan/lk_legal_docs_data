THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Five (05) allotments of land containing in total extent of about
0.0034 Hectare, depicted as Lots ‘A ’, ‘B’, ‘C’, ‘D’, ‘E’ in Advance
Tracing No. R/RTN/2013/225 dated 29.07.2013, prepared by
Ratnapura District Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Ethoya in the Minor Division of Uda Pattuwa in Kuruwita Korale in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ratnapura in Ratnapura District
in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Gamagewatta – Extent: 0.0015 Hectare
North: Gamage Watta claimed by K. A. L. Weerasuriya and Road;
East: Road; South: Road and Gamage Watta claimed by K. A. L.
Weerasuriya; West: Gamage Watta claimed by K. A. L. Weerasuriya.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Pussallewatta – Extent: 0.0009 Hectare
North: Road; East: Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Upasena; South:
Pussallewatta claimed by P . A. Upasena and Lot C; West: Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Pussallewatta – Extent: 0.0003 Hectare
North: Road and Pussallewatta claimed by P . A. Upasena; East:
Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Upasena and Pussallewatta claimed
by P. A. Malani; South: Lot D and Road; West: Road.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Pussallewatta – Extent: 0.0003 Hectare
North: Lot C, Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Upasena and
Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Malani; East: Pussallewatta claimed
by P . A. Malani; South and West: Road.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Pussallewatta – Extent: 0.0004 Hectare
North and East: Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Malani;  South:
Pussallewatta claimed by P. A. Malani and Road; West: Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No.: LD/05/2010/PC/07.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
