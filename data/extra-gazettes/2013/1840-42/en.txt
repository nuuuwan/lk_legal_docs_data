EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  MATALE  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Matale Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the Central  Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Muttusami Pillei Sivagnanam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Matale Municipal Council who belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated. Mr. Rafaideen Mohomed
Safarullah being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Matale Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Rafaideen
Mohomed Safarullah elected as a member of the  Matale Municipal Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Matale Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Matale,
06th December, 2013.
