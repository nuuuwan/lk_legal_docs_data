URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No.  41  OF  1978
Order under Section 3
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development being of opinion that the area specified in Column I of Schedule II
hereto and having the metes and bounds specified in corresponding entry in Column II of  Schedule II except the area already
declared as Urban Development Area and set out in Schedule I hereto are suitable for development and do hereby this order
declare such areas for the purpose of the said section.
I do hereby declare that, the already declared areas set out in Schedule I together with the other areas decalred as afore said,
shall be included within the limits of the expanded Bandarawela Urban Development Area set out in Column II under
Schedule II hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development
Ministry of Defence and Urban Development,
No. 15/5,
Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
14th October 2013.
