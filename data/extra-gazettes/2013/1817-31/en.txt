THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  40)  ORDER
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 12 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, do
by this order shall call out all the members of the Armed Forces specified in the First Schedule hereto, for the maintenance of
public order in the areas specified in the Second Schedule hereto.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
03rd July, 2013.
