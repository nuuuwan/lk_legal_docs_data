THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
THE  COCONUT  GROWING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Coconut Growing Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance, and specified in the Schedule hereto have been
approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decisions shall come into force on the 1st of December, 2013.
W. J. L. U. W
IJEWEERA,
Secretary,
