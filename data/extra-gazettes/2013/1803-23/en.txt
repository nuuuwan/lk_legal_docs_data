No. 1803/23 – WEDNESDAY, MARCH 27, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: NCP/TK/9/1/7/3/11.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Seventeen (17) allotments of the land described below are in extent
of 2.7298 Hectares, depicted from Lot Nos. 645 to 679 in the Original
Plan No. 7497 dated 11.01.2013 prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Pattiyadamana in the Minor Division of
Meda Pattu in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Thamankaduwa
in Polonnaruwa District, North Central Province.
01. Lot No. 645
Name of land: Pattiyadamana; Description: Paddy land; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: Bamunusinha Liyanage Mahindadasa (Grant
Nos. PL/G/6195 and PL/G/3109); Extent: 0.0788 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 648
Name of land: Pattiyadamana; Description: Paddy land; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: Bamunusinha Liyanage Kumarasiri (Grant
No. PL/G/6195); Extent: 0.1596 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 651
Name of land: Pattiyadamana; Description: Paddy land; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: Cultivated by Bamunusinha Liyanage
