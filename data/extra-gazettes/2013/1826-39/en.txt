THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The lands depicted as Lots 'A' and 'B' in Advance Tracing
No. Nu/KTM/2012/113 dated 18.09.2012, prepared by the
Surveyor-General and total extent of  about 0.0156  Hectare situated
in Gongalla Village in Thispane Korale Minor Division in Kotmale
Divisional  Secretary's Division in Nuwara-Eliya District in Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Kolapatana Estate – Extent: 0.0120 Hectare
North: Kolapatana Estate (T.P. 48346); East: Kolapatana Estate
(T.P. 48346) and Lot B; South: Kolapatana Estate (T.P. 48346), Lot
B and Kolapatana Estate;West: Kolapatana Estate (T.P. 48346).
02. Lot 'B' – Kolapatana Estate – Extent: 0.0036 Hectare
North: Lot A and Kolapatana Estate (T.P. 48346); East and South:
Kolapatana Estate (T.P. 48346) and Road (Estate); West: Kolapatana
Estate (T.P. 48346) and Lot A.
