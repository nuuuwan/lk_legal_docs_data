THE  NATIONAL   THOROUGHFARES   ACT,  No. 40  OF   2008
Order under Section 42
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 42(1) of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Article 42(2)
of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this order declare the building limit alongside of the national
highway gazetted under the Gazette No. 1469/6 dated 30.10.2006 and specified in the Schedule hereto, to be twenty five (25)
meters from the centre of the carriageway of the national highway.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
22nd August, 2013.
