No. 1793/11 – WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 16, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: KE/Prale/A TH3/1/63.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a Service Facility Centre at Mahalwarawa.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An Allotment of the land depicted as Lot No. 2 in Preliminary
Plan No. Co. 9182 situated at No. 581E Mahalwarawa Grama Niladari
Division in the Village of Mahalwarawa in the Divisional Secretary’s,
Division of Kesbewa in Colombo District in Western Province and
described as follows:
Name of land: Pelengahawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant:
State; Original claimant: R. A. S. Hemapala, No. 57, Athurugiriya
Road, Homagama, P. D. Piyasena, No. 337, Highlevel Road,
Maharagama, Pannipitiya, M. P. Peiris, No. 97, Mahalwarawa,
Pannipitiya and others; Extent: 0.1642 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary’s Office at Kesbewa on the 28th day of
February, 2013 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 21st day of February, 2013
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claim
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, and the details
of the computation of such amount. All deeds and the relevant
documents in support of the claims should be provide at the inquiry.
L. A. Kalukapuarachchi,
Divisional Secretary,
Kesbewa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kesbewa.
