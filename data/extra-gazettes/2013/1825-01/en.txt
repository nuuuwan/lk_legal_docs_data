THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Gorokkotuwawatta in extent of 1.2141
Hectare, mentioned as Lot No. 01 in Advance Tracing No. Gam/Att/
2012/332 of 26.12.2012 prepared by Surveyor-General situated in
the Village of Karasnagala in Udugaha Pattuwa Minor Division, in
Divisional Secretary’s Division at Attanagalla, in the District of
Gampaha in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 –  Gorokkotuwawatta – Extent: About 1.2141
Hectare
North and East: Remaining portion of this land; South: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha) West: Remaining portion of this land.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
