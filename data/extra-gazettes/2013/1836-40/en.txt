THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT  is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “Mihindu Senpura Mawatha” by
the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the subject of
Local Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read with
the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J. M. B
ADHRANI  JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
01. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
02. District - Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street - A name has not been assigned
04. New Name of the Street - “Mihindu Senpura Mawatha”
05. Road Description - This road is runs between Assesment numbers 566 and 568 of
Danister de Silva Mawatha (Government Land) of the Dematagoda
Municipal ward.
