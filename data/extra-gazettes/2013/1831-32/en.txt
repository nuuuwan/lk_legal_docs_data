PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
YATINUWARA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman  Sri Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of the Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha ,  do hereby declare
under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Herath Pathirajage  Jayawardhana Walgampaya
of the Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he  has ceased to be
a member of the United National Party.
SAMAN SRI  RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha .
Elections Office,
Kandy,
07th October, 2013.
