ORDER   UNDER  SECTION  10(1)(A) and 11(1)(A)   HOLIDAYS   ACT,   NO. 29  OF  1971
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10(1)(a) and 11(1)(a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J.
Senevirathne, Minister of Public Administration and Home Affairs, do by this  order declare  Monday, 15th April, 2013 a Public
Holiday on account of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year 2013.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRA THNE,
Minister of Public Administration and Home Affairs.
