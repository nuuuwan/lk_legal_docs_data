No. 1800/20 – WEDNESDAY, MARCH 06, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Five (05) allotments of land called Lower Cobowatta in extent of
about 1.6211 Hectare, depicted as Lots A, B, D, E, H shown in the
Advance Tracing No. B/ACQ/SRN/2012/454 dated 30.10.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Lower
Cobowatta in 25A, Kuttiyagolla Grama Niladhari Division in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Soranathota in Badulla District of
Uva Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Lower Cobowatta – Extent: 0.0454 Hectare
North: Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC
and Estate road; East: Estate road; South: Lot B; West: Lot B and
Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Lower Cobowatta – Extent: 0.2180 Hectare
North: Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC,
Lot A and Estate road; East: Lot A, Estate road and Lower Cobowatta
claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC; South: Estate road, Lower
Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC and Lot C; West:
Lot C and Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC.
03. Lot ‘D’ – Lower Cobowatta – Extent: 0.9734 Hectare
North: Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC,
Lots C, H and G; East: Lots C, H, G and Lower Cobowatta claimed
by Balangoda Plantation PLC; South: Lots H, G , Lower Cobowatta
claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC, Lots F and E; West: Lower
Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC, Lots F and E.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk.
04. Lot ‘E’ – Lower Cobowatta – Extent: 0.1808 Hectare
North: Lower Cobowatta claimed by Balangoda Plantation PLC
and Lot D; East: Lot D; South: Lot D and Lower Cobowatta claimed
by Balangoda Plantation PLC; West: Lower Cobowatta claimed by
Balangoda Plantation PLC.
05. Lot ‘H’ – Lower Cobowatta – Extent: 0.2035 Hectare
North: Lots D and C; East: Lot C and Lower Cobowatta claimed
by Balangoda Plantation PLC; South: Lower Cobowatta claimed by
Balangoda Plantation PLC, Lots G and D; West: Lots G and D.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Reference No.: 4/3/9/2012/IR/253.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
