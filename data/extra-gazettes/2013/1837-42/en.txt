THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Allotments of land depicted in Advance Tracing No. Ma/UKW/
2007/407 and dated 29th April, 2008, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, Matale District, situated in the Village of Elkaduwa in Minor
Division of Udasiyapattuwa of Matale in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Ukuwela, Matale District in Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Elkaduwa Watta – Extent: 0.0036 Hectare
North: Elkaduwa Watta claimed by Thissa Gunapala and
Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road; East: Wattegama-Kandenuwara
Road; South: Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road, Lot B and Elkaduwa
Watta claimed by H. M. Ekanayake; West: Elkaduwa Watta claimed
by Thissa Gunapala.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Elkaduwa Watta – Extent: 0.0017 Hectare
North: Elkaduwa Watta claimed by Thissa Gunapala, Lot A and
Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road; East: Wattegama-Kandenuwara
Road; South: Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road, Lot C and Elkaduwa
Watta claimed by W. B. Jayathilaka; West: Elkaduwa Watta claimed
by H. M. Ekanayake.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Elkaduwa Watta – Extent: 0.0057 Hectare
North: Elkaduwa Watta claimed by H. M. Ekanayaka, Lot B and
Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road; East: Wattegama-Kandenuwara
Road; South: Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road and Elkaduwa Watta
claimed by W. B. Jayathilaka; West: Elkaduwa Watta claimed by
W. B. Jayathilaka.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Elkaduwa Watta – Extent: 0.0091 Hectare
North: Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road and Elkaduwa Watta
claimed by Elkaduwa Plantation Company; East: Elkaduwa Watta
claimed by Elkaduwa Plantation Company; South: Elkaduwa Watta
claimed by Elkaduwa Plantation Company and Wattegama-
Kandenuwara Road; West: Wattegama-Kandenuwara Road.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Elkaduwa Watta – Extent: 0.0389 Hectare
North: Elkaduwa Watta claimed by I. S. D. Gunathilaka and Canal;
