THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two allotments of land depicted as Lot Nos. 01 to 02  in Senior
Survey Superintendents Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 3895 dated
2013.02.20 situated in the Village of Burullapitiya in No. 126/2,
Grama Niladhari's  Division in the Minor Division of Dasiyapattuwa
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Minuwangoda, in Gampaha
District in Western Province .
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Moonamalgahakumbura; Description: Open Barren
land; Claimant:R.W. H. Rajapaksha, Adiyagara, Nilpanagoda; Extent:
0.0384 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Moonamalgahakumbura; Description: Garden,
contain a permanent building and a temporary building; Claimant:R.W.
H. Rajapaksha, Adiyagara, Nilpanagoda; Extent:  0.0121 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Minuwangoda on the 08th day of  May,
2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 24th day of April, 2013 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
C. P . W. Gunathilaka,
Divisional Secretary(Acting),
Minuwangoda.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Minuwangoda,
04th, April, 2013
