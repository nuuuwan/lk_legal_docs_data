EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
MANTHAI  WEST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi the recognized political party to which
James Suthakaran  the member belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible as a member of that Manthai West
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Santhampillai Pathinathan
being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana, Returning Officer of  Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha,
do hereby declare Mr. Santhampillai Pathinathan elected as  a member of   the Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. A. S
UMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
Manthai West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Mannar,
28th June 2013.
