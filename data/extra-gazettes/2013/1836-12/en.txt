PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
KOTMALE   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kotmale  Pradeshiya Sabha ,  do hereby declare
under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rauf Mohomed Hilmi  of the Kotmale
Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he  has ceased to be a member of the
United National Party.
H. A. S. FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kotmale  Pradeshiya Sabha .
Elections Office,
Nuwara- Eliya,
