L.D – B 127/32.
BUDDHIST  TEMPORALITIES  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Buddhist Temporalities Ordinance (Chapter 318) as amended by Act, No.
18 of 1981, I. D. M. Jayaratne, Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, do by this order, exempt the Ruhunu Maha Katharagama Devalaya from the application of the provisions “so however
that the aggregate of such period shall not exceed two years”, specified in paragraph (b) of the proviso to section 12 of that
ordinance.
D. M. J
AYARA TNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
15th March, 2013.
