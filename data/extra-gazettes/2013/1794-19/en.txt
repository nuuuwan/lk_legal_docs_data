THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
W AGES   BOARD  FOR  THE  RUBBER  GROWING  AND  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Mr. Lional Dasanayaka has been appointed
as the Nominated Members Member the Wages Board for the Rubber Growing and Manufacturing Trade in place of
Mr. Wimal Kariyawasam by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 9 of the Wages Boards
Ordinance (Chapter 136). This Appointment is valid only Up to 01.01.2015.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Labour Secretariat, Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.

15th January, 2013.
