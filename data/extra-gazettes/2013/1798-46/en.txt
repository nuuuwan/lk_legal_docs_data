THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                 My Ref. No.: Thi/DS/1/8/3/36Ath.
Reference No. : LD/05/2010/HW/162.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a construction of road from Ramakrishna
Road to Fraser Avenue of Merindrive Road.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Lot Nos. 8578, 8579, 8580, 8581, 8582, 8583, 8584, 8585 and
8586 (Lots 09) allotments of land in total extent of about
0.3852 Hectare, in Preliminary Plan No. Co: 9307 and dated
08.01.2013  prepared by the Surveyor- General situated in the Village
of Wellawaththa South in the Grama Niladhari Division of  Division
No. 18,  Wellawaththa South within the limits of Municipal Council
of Colombo, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Thimbirigasyaya in Colombo District in Westen Province.
01. Lot No. 8578
Name of land: Mahawellawaththa; Description: Open waste land;
Claimant: State;  Extent: 0.0122 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 8579
Name of land: Mahawellawaththa, premises bearing part of Assmt.
No. 61, Ramakrishna Road; Description: Garden, part of permanent
building; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0205 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 8580
Name of land: Mahawellawaththa, premises bearing part of Assmt.
No. 61 A, Ramakrishna Road; Description: Garden, part of
permanent building; Claimant: State;  Extent: 0.0221 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 8581

05. Lot No. 8582

07. Lot No. 8584

08. Lot No. 8585
Name of land: Mahawellawaththa, premises bearing Assmt.

09. Lot No. 8586

All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary’s Office, Thimbirigasyaya on the 12th day of
March 2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of March, 2013,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
