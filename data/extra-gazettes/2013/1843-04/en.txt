WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Order under Section 247 of the Urban Councils Oridnance  (Authority 255) read with Clause 02 of the
Provincial Councils (Consequential) Provisions Act, No. 12 of 1989
I,  Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief  Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Trade, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion
of the Western Province, do hereby make the following announcement according to provisions stated in Section 247 of the
Urban Councils Ordinance (Authority 255)  read with Clause 02 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential) Provisions Act,
No. 12 of 1989.
P
RASANNA RANA TUNGA,
Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning,
Law and Order, Education, Land, Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Trade, Electricity and Power,
Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council.
On the 30th date of December 2013,
Shrawasthi Mandiraya,
Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
ORDER
1. This order shall be named as the Order to enforce the budget documents for year 2014 of the Urban Councils  of
the Western Provincial Council.
2. Where the budget document relevant for year 2014 of a certain Urban Council within the Western Province was
not adopted according to the provisions stated in Section 178 of the Urban Councils Ordinance (Authority 255), and as per
Clause 12 of the Local Government Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 when the Chairman’s Office of the
relevant Urban Council has become vacant wherein action has not been taken as per provisions of the Local Government
Authorities Elelctions (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 to fill the Chairman’s office that has become vacant or when a
Chairman newly appointed to the vacant position requires to present a proposal to the Council in relation to adoption of a
budget document for year 2014, however, lacks the minimum days to present such proposal to the Council in compliance to
the written law relevant to that, in such case, it is legal to execute certain budget document prepared for year 2014 by the said
Urban Council  under the approval of the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Authorities of the distrcit where the
relevant Urban Council is located.
3. All the provisions stated in this order shall be put into effect according to the provisions of the Local Government
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and Local Government Authorities Elelctions (Amendment) Act, No. 22
of  2012.
