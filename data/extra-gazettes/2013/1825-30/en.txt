The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
MINISTRY  OF  CIVIL   AVIATION
Civil  Aviation Authority  of  Sri Lanka
NOTICE  BY  THE  DIRECTOR  GENERAL  OF  CIVIL  AVIATION  UNDER  THE  AIR  NAVIGATION  REGULATION  196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that this office has received on 21st June, 2013 an application dated
21st  June, 2013 from F-Airways  Limited., at  The  fairways, 100, Buthgamuwa Road, Rajagiriya, seeking an Air Operator
Certificate to engage in Domestic private and charter air service operations in compliance with Civil Aviation Act and Regula-
tions made thereunder. Business name of  the company is F-Airways Limited with the trade name as “F-air”. The Directors of
the F-Airways Limited are declared as follows.
Mr. Hemaka de Alwis
Mr. Shivanka De Zoysa
Mr. Saminda Jayasekara
Ms. Neluka de Alwis
F-Airways Limited has nominated Capt. Lushan Fernando to be the Accountable Manager in charge of the proposed
operations of air services.
A copy of the  aforementioned application is kept at the Technical Library, Ground Floor of Headquarters of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 04, Hunupitiya Road, Colombo 02, for the perusal of the members of the public, during
office hours of any working days.
Members of the Public are hereby informed that this office would proceed with the certification process of this
application after 20th September, 2013 as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant’s compliance with
the applicable Legislative provisions, Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended Practices speci-
fied by ICAO and associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe,
secure, and  efficient operation of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, and
Charter License together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
