No. 1796/23 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 08, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Maha/3/312/Ath.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in extent of about 0.0147 Hectare, depicted
as Lot No. 01in the  Preliminary Plan No. Co 9303  prepared by  the
Colombo District Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Kottawa
East, in the Minor Division of Salpiti Korale, in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Maharagama, in the  Colombo District in
Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Horahenawatta; Description: Access road;
Claimant: Unknown; Extent: 0.0147 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Maharagama Divisional Secretariat on the 20th day of March,
2013 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 13th day of March, 2013, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Buddi Tharanga Karunasena,
Divisional Secretary,
Maharagama.
Divisional Secretariat,
Maharagama,
07th February,  2013.
