UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (ICASL) established by the Institute of Chartered
Accountants Act, No. 23 of 1959, do by this Order, grant the Degree awarding status to the Institute of Chartered Accountants
of Sri Lanka (ICASL) for the purpose of developing higher education leading to the conferment of Degrees specified in
Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the abbreviations are set out in the corresponding entries respectively in Column
II of the Schedule.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Programmes Abbreviations to the Degrees
Degree of Bachelor of Science in Applied Accounting (General) BSc AA (Gen.) ICASL
Degree of Bachelor of Science in Applied Accounting (Special) BSc AA (Spl.) ICASL
DISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA BANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
