UV A   PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made   by  the  Governor   of   Uva   Province
I  hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province.
01. Mr. C. Dissanayake, Retired officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, Reappointed on contract basis as
Accountant to the Department of Road Development, from the date 01.01.2013 till the appointment of a Permanent
officer or Up to 31.12.2013,
02. Mr. W. M. M. G . Abesinghe Banda, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to
the Ministry of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Consumer Affairs and Co-operative and Food
Supply and Distribution of the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 24.01.2013
to 31.01.2013, till the return of Mr. M. A. B. Daya Senarath, to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of
Road Develpment, Housing, Water Supply, Consumer Affairs and Co-operative and Food Supply and Distribution
of the Uva Provincial Council,
03. Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara Mapa, Officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, to perform the Duties of the
Assistant Director to the Institution of Management Development Training for Uva, in addition to his permanent
post, with effect from the date 01.01.2013,
04. Mr. Sri D. Abeyakoon, Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, Reappointed on
contract basis as Deputy Director to Education of Education Department of Uva Province, from the date 10.02.2013
till the appointment of  a Permanent Officer or Up to 09.02.2014,
05. Mr. H. S. Yapa, Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, Reappointed on contract
basis as Deputy Director to Education (English) of Education Department of Uva Province, from the date
10.02.2013 till the appointment of a Permanent Officer or Up to 09.09.2013,
