EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  EMBILIPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of Vice Chairman in Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Amil Meegasdeniya Kangkanamge  the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me to  decide upon the member of that
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period
of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Chandrasiri Munasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera, Returning Officer of  Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Chandrasiri Munasinghe elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
12th March, 2013.
