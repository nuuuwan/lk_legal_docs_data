EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
V ALVETITHURAI  URBAN  COUNCIL
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Valvetithurai Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending more than three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Thillaiyampathy Jegathees the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of that
Valvetithurai Urban Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Konalingam
Karunanantharasa  being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Valvetithurai Urban Council, do hereby
declare Mr. Konalingam Karunanantharasa elected as  a member of   the Valvetithurai Urban Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valvetithurai Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna.
12th April  2013.
