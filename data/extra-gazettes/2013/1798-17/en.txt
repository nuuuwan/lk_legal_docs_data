No. 1798/17 – TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My No.: 4/1/2/8.
I, R. M. R. Rathnayake, the Divisional Secretary to the Divisional
Secretariat, Kurunegala of Kurunegala District do hereby give
notice, under Section 33 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)
that a sum of Rs. 4,500,000.00 payable as compensation in respect
of the Acquisition of land described in the Schedule  below has been
deposited in the District Court of Kurunegala under the Case No.
LA/7862/12, as the land owner refused the compensation.
R. M. R. Rathnayake,
Divisional Secretary,
Kurunegala.
18th February, 2013.
SCHEDULE
The allotment of land situated in Wehera in Ward No. 6 in the

North Western Province.
Plan No. : Ku. 1691
Lot No. : 01
Name of land : Kahatagahamulla Watta, Dangahamula Pillewa
alias Karadagahamula Kumbure Kanatiya
Extent : 0.304 Hectare
SCHEDULE II
Reason for depositing the compensation in the District Court:
It was decided to deposit the Compensation in the Judiciary as
the land owner refused to obtain the compensation payable in respect
of the land acquired to the State.
