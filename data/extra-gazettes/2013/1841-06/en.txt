EXTRAORDINARY
1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KULIYAPITIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER SECTION 65A(2) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which   Senarathna
Y . M.  the member  belonged, was required by me, under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who
shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mrs. Dayapathirajage
Manjula Kumari Karunathilaka, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning  Officer of Kuliyapitiya
Pradeshiya  Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mrs. Dayapathirajage Manjula Kumari Karunathilaka, elected as  a member  of   the  Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala.
02nd December, 2013.
