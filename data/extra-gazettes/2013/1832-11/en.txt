Regulations
The Fishing (Import and Export) Regulations, 2010 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1665/16 of August 04,
2010 are hereby amended by the repeal of item 4 of Schedule II thereof and the substitution therefor of the following item -
"4. A fee of Ten Rupees (Rs. 10/=) shall be charged for a kilogram of Beche-de-mer. This sum shall be paid when
applying for a license for the export of boiled and dried (processed) Beche-de-mer."
