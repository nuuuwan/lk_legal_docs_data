EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT, N O. 2  OF  1980
Order  under  Section 2
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 1980,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the subject of
Defence and Urban Development, being of opinion that the lands specified in the Schedule hereto are urgently required to
implement a Financial cum Institutional Urban Development Project do by this order, declare that the said lands are required for
such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo.
26th of  February  2013.
SCHEDULE
Two (2) allotments of lands bearing Assessment Nos. 300 and 294 Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Rajagiriya shown in Lot Nos.
A and B in Advance Tracing No. Co/KTT/2008/400 situated within the Municipal Council limits of Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte
in Grama Niladari Division No. 514/B Rajagiriya and Divisional Secretariat Division of Sri Jayawardenapura in District of
Colombo, Western Province.
The said allotments of lands are bounded as follows :
Lot No. A - Extent - 1.2072 Hectares
North : Royal Garden Road, Lot B and Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha ;
East : Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha and Kolonnawa Ela ;
South : Kolonnawa Ela and Masonry Drain ;
West  : Masonry Drain, Royal Garden road and Lot B.
