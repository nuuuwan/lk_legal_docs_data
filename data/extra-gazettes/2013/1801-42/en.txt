THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: LEI-CDP/SD/R1(ADD).
I,  hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0017  Hectare,
depicted as Lot Nos. 673 and 674 in the  Cadastral Map No: 521003
(Zone No. 5 sheet No. 2) dated 31st  December 2012 prepared by
the Surveyor-General   situated in Ratmalana Village, Ratmalana
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Colombo District, Western  Province
and morefully described as follows:
01. Lot No. 673
Name of  Land: Kajugahawatta alias Kottambagahawatta and
Dombagahaowita alias Geritteowita; Description: Road; Original
Claimant: Mahinda Wijeratne, 38/1, Gamini Lane, Ratmalana; Extent:
0.0013 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 674
Name of  Land: Kajugahawatta alias Kottambagahawatta and
Dombagahaowita alias Geritteowita; Description: Lunawa Main
Canal; Original Claimant: Mahinda Wijeratne, 38/1, Gamini Lane,
Ratmalana; Extent: 0.0004 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands  are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me on 10th April, 2013 at Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage,
‘‘Lakdiya Medura’’ , New Parliament Road, Palawatta, Battaramulla
at the time intimated to them in due course and are  hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 03rd April, 2013
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars  of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land , the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
S. Weerabahu,
Land Acquiring Officer.
Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage,
‘‘Lakdiya Medura’’, New Parliament Road,
Palawatta, Battaramulla.
14th March, 2013.
