WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Financial Statute No. 06 of 1990 Order Under Section 76(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section 76(2) of the Financial Statute of the Western Province, No. 06 of 1990,
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local
Government, Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment, Economic Promotion, Power and Energy, Trade of the
Western Province being satisfied that no stamp duty is chargeable in the Embassy of the People’s Republic of China case of
any instrument relating to the transfer of any immovable property executed by or on behalf of or in favour of Government of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby declare that the exception granted by Sub-section (4) of Section 39
of this statute shall apply with effect from the date of publication of this order in the Gazette to the purchase of Land and
premises bearing assessment No. 112, Horton Place, Colombo 07. By or in favour of or on behalf of Embassy of the People’s
Republic of China.
P
RASANNA RANA THUNGA,
