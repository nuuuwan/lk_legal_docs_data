1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF CHAIRMAN OF  THE  MAWANELLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION 65A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS a vacancy has occurred in the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha  due to Chairman ceasing to hold office by reason of
an Order of a competent Jurisdiction;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  Korale
Gedara Piyathissa,  the Chairman belonged, was required by me, under section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Chairman
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  failed to communicate his decision within
the period specified ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha,  Returning  Officer of  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.P. P. Wickramasingha who
secured the next highest number of preferences at the election held on 17th March 2011 to   Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha
elected as Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
G
AMALA THGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle.
23rd September, 2013.
