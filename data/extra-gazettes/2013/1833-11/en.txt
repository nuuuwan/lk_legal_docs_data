THE  MINES  AND  MINERALS  ACT,  No.  33  OF  1992
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Environment and Renewable Energy under Section 64 read with Section 34 of the

Minister of Environment & Renewable Energy.
Colombo,
20th September 2013.
Regulation
The Mining (Licensing) Regulation, No. 1 of 1993 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 794/23 of November 26,
1993, as amended from time to time are hereby further amended in Regulation 33 by the substitution for paragraph (4) thereof
of the following :-
"(4) Royalties charged from sales or determined market value of mine output shall, unless otherwise specified in the
Mineral Investment Agreement, be a percentage of that value, as following :
Precious metals - 7 per cent
Industrial minerals (not exported) - 6 per cent
Industrial minerals (exported) - 7  per cent
Building materials - 4 per cent
Dimension stone (exported) - 7 per cent
Base and other metals - 6 per cent".
