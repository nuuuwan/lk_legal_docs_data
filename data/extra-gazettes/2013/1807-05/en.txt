1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
MEDIRIGIRIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a Chairman in Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member of the North  Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Wickramaarachchige Sampath Sri Nilantha Wickramaarachchi  the Chairman belonged, was required by me to decide upon the
member of that Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Chairman within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Thembuwaththe Gamaralalage Ananda Sri Dharmasena  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hathurusinghalage Indika Ranjan Hathurusinghe,  Returning Officer of  Medirigiriya Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Thembuwaththe Gamaralalage Ananda Sri Dharmasena elected as  Chairman of   the Medirigiriya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
8th April, 2013.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
MEDIRIGIRIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the North  Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Wickramaarachchige Sampath Sri Nilantha Wickramaarachchi  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person
eligible as a  member of that Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a
member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr.  Udahamullamudiyanselage
Samith Chandra Udahamulla being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hathurusinghalage Indika Ranjan Hathurusinghe,  Returning Officer of  Medirigiriya Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Udahamullamudiyanselage Samith Chandra Udahamulla elected as  a member of   the Medirigiriya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
8th April, 2013.
