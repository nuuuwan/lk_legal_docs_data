THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
Order under Section 11(1)(b)
BY virtue of the Powers vested in me by Section 11(1)(b) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971 I,  W. D. J. Senevirathne, Minister
of Public Administration and Home Affairs, do, by this order declare that the day of Tuesday, 15th of April 2014 and additional
Bank Holidays for the purpose of the Act, in addition to the days for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to that
Act.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRA THNE,
