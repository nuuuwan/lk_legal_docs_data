ORDER  PERTAINING  TO  ASSIGNING  THE  POWERS  OF  WESTERN  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC  SERVICE  UNDER
CLAUSE  32  OF  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ACT,  No.  42  OF  1987  AMENDED  BY  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1990
I, Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province, as per powers entrusted in me under Clause No. 32 of
Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990, subjected to
enforcing by myself the powers related to appointment, transfer, removal from post or dismissal from service and disciplinary
control (to the extent of relevance) of officers in posts of the Western Provincial Public Service that have been referred by the
schedule stated below, shall hereby assign ; having exempted the aforesaid powers and without prejudice to the powers
assigned to the Public Service Commission regarding all-island-services ; powers pertaining to appointment, transfer dismissal
from service and disciplinary control of all the other posts of the Western Provincial Public Service to the Western Provincial
Public Service Commission with effect from 15th of June 2013.
Further, power and authority shall be assigned to the Western Provincial Public Service Commission to entrust
those powers to the Chief Secretary of the Western Province or other official as it deems fit by the Western Provincial Public
Service Commission in its functioning with regard to powers assigned thus as stated under Sub-clause 32(2) (a) in the
Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990 unless any order
is specifically made by me. Equally, it shall hereby be prescribed and declared that any party dissatisfied with the implementation
of powers assigned thus, by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, has the right to appeal to the Western
Provincial Public Service, Commission, whereas, any person dissatisfied by a decision taken by the Western Provincial
Public Service Commission in relation to Western Provincial Public Service, has the right to appeal to the Governor of the
Western Province.
In addition to that, orders pertaining to assigning of powers related to Western Provincial Public Service under
Clause 32 of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Council (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990,
which has been enforced prior to assigning of these powers, shall hereby be repealed without any prejudice to actions taken
under said orders.
S. Alavi Mowlana,
Governor of the Western Province.
Secretariat of the Governor Western Province,
No. 98/4,

Colombo 05.
