HAMBANTOTA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Programmed Budget - 2014
IT is herey notified that under section 2012 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) the programme budget for the
year 2014 of Hambantota Municipal Council is open for public inspection at this office during working hours from 22nd
November, 2013 to 02nd December, 2013 at the Hambantota Municipal Council Office. (except on Sundays and Public
Holidays).
