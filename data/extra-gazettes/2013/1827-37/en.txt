ANTIQUITIES  ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of Powers vested in me under Sub-section 39 "a" of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) as amended by the
Act, No. 24 of 1998, I, Minister of National Heritage, do, by this Order, appoint the members referred to in the Schedule hereto
the Advisory committee on Archaeology as effective for a period the 02nd of September 2013 to 02nd of September 2015.
Dr. JAGATH BALASURIYA,
Minister of National Heritage.
Battaramulla,
11th September 2013.
