URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No. 41  OF  1978
Order under Section 03
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 03 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development being of opinion that the area specified in Column I of Schedule II
hereto and having the metes and bounds specified in corresponding entry in Column II of Schedule II, except the areas
already declared as Urban Development area and set out in Schedule I hereto are suitable for Development and do hereby this
order declare such areas to be Urban Development areas for the purpose of the said Section.
Therefore,  I do hereby declare that the already declared areas set out in Schedule I together with the other areas
declared as aforesaid, shall be included within the limits of the Dehiowita Urban areas set out in Column II under Schedule II
hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development.
Ministry of Defence and Urban Development,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
06th February 2013.
