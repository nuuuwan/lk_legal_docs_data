NOTICE No : 82/0031 Mathara District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-

lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 820032 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Pamburana (Block 
03) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Divi-

2013 In the event of failure  to submit any claim before the above
-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to 
determine title to the land and accordingly publish such determi-
nation in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the 
above Act.

at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat,
