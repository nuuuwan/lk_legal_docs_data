1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  KANDY  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kandy  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Sanjeewa Hulangamuwa,  the member  belonged, was required by me,  to nominate a person   eligible  as  a member of  that
Kandy Municipal Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Herath Mudiyanselage
Upali Vijitha  Jayasekara  being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of Kandy Municipal Council,  do
hereby declare Mr. Herath Mudiyanselage Upali Vijitha Jayasekara , elected as  a member  of   the Kandy Municipal Council.
S
AMAN SRI RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kandy  Municipal Council.
Kandy ,
19th  April 2013.
