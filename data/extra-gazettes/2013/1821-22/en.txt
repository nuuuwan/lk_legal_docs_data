EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
TISSAMAHARAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front the recognized political party to which
Abeysingha Liyana Aarachchige Kumara Rupasingha the member belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible
as a member of that Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Rubasinge Rajith
Priyadarshana being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of  Tissamaharama Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Rubasinge Rajith Priyadarshana elected as  a member of   the Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Tissamaharama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
23rd  July  2013.
