EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  V A VUNIYA NORTH  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Vavuniya  North  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the   United People’s Freedom Alliance  the recognized  political party  to which
Thevarasa Thillairajh   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Vavuniya  North    Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Nagalingam  Dharmarajah
being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan   Returning Officer  of   Vavuniya  North  Pradeshiya Sabha,   acting  in
terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Nagalingam
Dharmarajah  elected as  a member of   the Vavuniya  North  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya  North Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
15th  October, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  V ALIKAMAM WEST  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Valikamam West   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  IIankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi   the   recognized  political party  to which  Veerasingam
Sivakumar   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person
eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Valikamam West   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Sanmugalingam Sabesan
being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam  Achchuthan  Returning Officer  of   Valikamam West  Pradeshiya Sabha,   acting
in  terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Sanmugalingam
Sabesan elected as  a member of   the Valikamam West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
23rd  October, 2013.
