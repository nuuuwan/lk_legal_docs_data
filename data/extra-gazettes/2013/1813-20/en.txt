1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ERAVUR URBAN COUNCIL
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Eravur Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending more than three
consecutive meetings without permission;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which  Akeel Arshath
K. L.,  the member  belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible as a member of that Eravur Urban Council who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Jamaldeen Mohamed
Musthafa, being an eligible person to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayake Ralalaga Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer  of Eravur Urban
Council, do hereby declare Mr.  Jamaldeen Mohamed Musthafa, elected as  a member  of   the  Eravur Urban Council.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa.
03rd  June, 2013.
