NOTIFICATION UNDER THE BOTANIC GARDENS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER  446)
BY virtue of power vested in the Section 2(2) of the Botanic Gardens Ordinance (Chapter 446) No. 31 of 1928 as amended
by the Act, No.  32 of 1973, I, declare the Dry Zone Botanic Garden at Mirijjawila, Hambantota which is described in the
Schedule, as a national botanic garden with effect from 14th of November 2013.
J
AYARATHNA HERATH,
Minister of Botanical Gardens and
Public Recreation.
Ministry of Botanical Gardens and Public Recreation,
6th Floor,
No. 21, Rakshana Mandiraya,
Vauxhall Street,
Colombo 02,
6th November 2013,
