NOTICE
NOTICE under Section 53(1)(a) of the statute of Central Province Co-operative Societies No. 10 of 1990 as amended by the
Statute No. 04 of 1993.
The under mentioned Co-operative Societies are in the process of Liquidation under Section 53 of the statute of
Central Province Co-operative Societies No. 10 of 1990 as amended by the statute of no. 04 of 1993.
Therefore the members and employees of the said societies, and institutions and persons who have connections to
the assets of said societies are hereby informed to submit their requests and claims for deposits, loans, arrears or any out
standings due from the said societies to them.
All the requests and claims should be submitted to the liquidation on or before 31.12.2013 and the requests and
claims which are submitted after that date shall not be considered.
W. M. P. K. W
