1A
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATIONS
The Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  10(A)
THE surcharge of 100% on Customs Import Duty payable on imported Gold as specified in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1815/15, 20th June 2013, is removed with effect from 22nd November 2013 by virtue of this order and the aforesaid Gazette
is hereby recinded.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
(Minister of Finance and Planning).
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
21st November, 2013.
