PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF  1988
Filling  of  a Vacancy in the Western  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western  Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western  Province Provincial
Council by reason of being elected  as a member of  the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the  aforesaid  Act,  to  nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Uthuwan Pathirannehelage
Chandrasoma Saranalal  to fill such vacancy  ;
I, Waduge  Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in  terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Uthuwan Pathirannehelage Chandrasoma Saranalal ,
elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Gampaha  in respect of which such
vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
05th June, 2013.
