EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
HIKKADUWA   URBAN  COUNCIL   UNDER  SECTION 65A(2)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Hikkaduwa  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Nanayakkara Kandambige Douglas  the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the ordinace for Election as a member of that Hikkaduwa  Urban Council who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as a Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Galhena Kankanamge
Gunadasa being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Hikkaduwa Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby  declare Mr. Galhena Kankanamge
Gunadasa, elected as  Member of   the Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Galle,
01st  October, 2013.
