THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land shown as Lot Nos. 1 - 4  in extent of
about 0.0623 Hectare, indicated  in Advanced Tracing No. CO/HNW/
2012/1125 and dated 28.02.2013 prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Brandigampala in the Minor Division of
Medapattu in Divisional Secretary's Division of Seethawaka
(Hanwella) in the Colombo District in Western Province and bounded
as follows:
1. Lot No. 1 - Jambugahawatta -Extent: 0.0019 Hectare
North: Road (Road Development Authority); East: Road (Road
Development Authority); South: Lot No. 2 and Jambugahawatta
claimed by W.K. Gamini; West: Jambugahawatta claimed by W.K.
Gamini and Jambugahawatta claimed by H.P . Weerasiri.
2. Lot No. 2 - Jambugahawatta -Extent: 0.0126 Hectare
North: Lot No. 1 and Road (Road Development Authority); East:
Road (Road Development Authority) and Lot No. 3; South: Lot
No. 3, Jambugahawatta claimed by S.D. Berty Gamini Sethunga and
Jambugahawatta claimed by R.A. Jayapala; West: Jambugahawatta
claimed by R.A. Jayapala, Jambugahawatta claimed by W.K. Gamini
and Lot No. 1.
3. Lot No. 3 - Jambugahawatta -Extent: 0.0384 Hectare
North: Lot No. 2 and Road (Road Development Authority); East:
Road (Road Development Authority) and Jambugahawatta claimed
by S.D. Berty Gamini Sethunga; South: Jambugahawatta claimed by
S.D. Berty Gamini Sethunga; West: Jambugahawatta claimed by
S.D. Berty Gamini Sethunga, Jambugahawatta claimed by R.A.
Jayapala and Lot No. 2.
4. Lot No. 4 - Makulugaha Owita alias Kandana Owita -
Extent: 0.0094 Hectare
North: Bund of Lake claimed by State that the Land Name Not
Found and Makulugaha Owita  alias Kandana Owita claimed by
S.A.D.P. Sendanayake; East: Makulugaha Owita alias Kandana Owita
claimed by S.A.D.P. Sendanayake; South: Road (Road Development
Authority); West: Road (Road Development Authority) and Bund
of Lake claimed by State that the Land Name Not Found.
