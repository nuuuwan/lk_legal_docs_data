THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Allahenawaththa alias Katumatiyawa
containing in extent of about 0.8938 Hectare shown as Lot Nos.
1,2,3,4 in the plan No. 2004/42  prepared by the District Secretariat's
Surveyor H. W. Ashoka Jayalal situated in the Village of
Siyambalawehera in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Ibbagamuwa in the District, of Kurunegala of the North Western
Province and the  land is bounded as follows:
Lot Nos. 1,2,3,4 – Allahena Waththa alias Katumatiyawa  –
Extent: About 0.8938 Hectare
North: Land claimed by Ajith Gamini and H. D. Gunawardhana;
East: The land claimed by A.G . Dingiriya and others;  South: The
land claimed by S. D. Gunasekara and Alawdeen and others;
West: Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
