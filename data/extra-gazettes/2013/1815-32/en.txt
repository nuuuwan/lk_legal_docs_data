THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
                                                             Reference No.: W/E/2/2066.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The Lot No. 2524 in extent of about 0.0025 Hectare, of land in
Final Village Plan No. 112 prepared by the Surveyor-Superintendent
situated in the village of Pelawatta in  Grama Niladhari Division of
850-Pelawatta and Divisional Secretary's Division of Walallawita in
Kaluthara District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 2524
Name of land: Demodara Gankanda alias Demodara Pitakattiya;
Description of Land: Open Waste Land; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Hettiarachchige Don Weerarathna, Wele Gewatta, Pitigala
Road, Pelawatta; Extent: 0.0025 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which  a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally,
or by agents duly authorised in writing, before me at Divisional
Secretariat, Walallawita of the 29/07/2013 at 10.00 a.m., and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing [in duplicate] on or
before the 23/07/2013, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
M.L.D
ASHITHA NIROSHANA
    Divisional Secretary,
          Walallawita.
Divisional Secretariat,
Walallawita.
