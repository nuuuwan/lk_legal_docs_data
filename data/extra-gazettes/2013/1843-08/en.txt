Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance and Liquidity Support Scheme Regulations
Amendment to the Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance Scheme Regulations, No. 1 of 2010
1.1 These Regulations shall be cited as Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance and Liquidity Support Scheme
Regulations, No. 1 of 2013.
2.1 Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance Scheme Regulations, No. 1 of 2010 published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1673/11 of 28th September, 2010 is hereby amended as follows :-
2.2 In regulation 3.1 thereof, by the substitution for the words “This Scheme shall be titled Sri
Lanka Deposit Insurance Scheme” of the words “This Scheme shall be titled Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance
and Liquidity Support Scheme (hereinafter referred to as “the Scheme”)”.
2.3 In regulation 7.1 thereof, by the substitution for the words “The Scheme shall have a fund titled
“Deposit Insurance Fund” ” of the words “The Scheme shall have a fund titled “Sri Lanka Deposit Insurance
and Liquidity Support Fund” ”.
