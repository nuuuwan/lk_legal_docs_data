EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  ALAWWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of Vice Chairman in Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the North Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which B. R.
Rohana Chandra Kumara Basnayake the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as a Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his dicision to nominate
Mr. Pathiraja Mudiyanselage Punchi Bandara Pathiraja Weththewa to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Alawwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Pathiraja Mudiyanselage Punchi Bandara Pathiraja Weththewa elected as Vice Chairman of the  Alawwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Alawwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala District,
16th  November, 2013.
