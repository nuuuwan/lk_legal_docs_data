DECLARATION  OF  THE  OPERATION  OF  SRI  LANKA  NURSING  COUNCIL
THIS is to inform the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that as per the provision of the Sri Lanka
Nursing Council Act, No. 19 of 1988 and amended by Act, No. 35 of 2005, the Sri Lanka Nursing Council has been established
as per section 2 of the said Act with effect from 12.05.2011 with such members who have been elected from the election held
in this respect on 09.04.2011 as per section 3 of the ex-officio members. All of whom are as listed below :
01. President : Director General of Health Serv ices
