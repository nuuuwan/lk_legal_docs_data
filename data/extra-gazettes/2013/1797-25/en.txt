THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : Soori/Land/01/01/07.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4/3/3/2010/WS/270.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two  (02)  allotments of  land in total extent of  0.4244 Hectare
depicted in Preliminary Plan No. F.T.P . 4 Supplement No. 152 Sheet
No. 456  prepared by the Surveyor-General situated in the Village of
Buruthakanda in Gramaniladhari Division of Wediwewa in
Magampattu Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Sooriyawewa in Hambantota District Southern Province:
01. Lot No. C-6030
Name of  land: Wedigamawewa; Description:  Chena with
Temporally mixed crops cultivation; Claimant: Lokuprathapa
Sayakkarage Pentis, Koggalla; Extent: 0.3280 Hectare.
02. Lot No. C-6031
Name of  land: Wedigamawewa; Description:  Chena with
Temporally mixed crops cultivation; Claimant: Deemanpatabedige
Piyadasa, Wediwewa; Extent: 0.0964 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby  required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Sooriyawewa  on the 24th day of April,
2013  at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 17th day of April, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the lands, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
All the Documents and deeds which are relevant to prove the
ownership should be forwarded at the inquiry.
N. K. R. Pathirana,
Divisional Secretary,
Sooriyawewa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Sooriyawewa,
12th February,  2013.
