EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & C., by the President
  PS No.:  CSA/5/9/1.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Sub - Sections  (1) and (2) of section 19 of the
Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 to appoint the
under mentioned Lawyer members and Valuer members of the Board of Review for the purpose of hearing appeals in the
manner therein provided, for a period of three years with effect from 23rd November 2013 and to appoint Justice S. I. Imam and
Mrs. M. M. Liyanage as Chairman and Vice Chairperson respectively of the said Board of Review under sub section (3) of
section 19 of the aforesaid Act.
Lawyer members Valuer members
1. Mr. S. I. Imam 1. Mr. W. D. P. Rupananda
2. Mrs. M. M. Liyanage 2. Mr. J. M. S. Bandara
3. Mr. Upali Samaraweera 3. Mr. R. W. M. S. B. Rajapakse
4. Mr. Saman Gunasekara 4. Mr. H. A. Jayarathna
5. Mr. T. K. Azoor 5. Mr . Pasupathy Santhirasegaram
6. Mr.  A. K. Chandrakantha 6. Mrs. S. R. Amarasingha
7. Mr. K. Y .  Abeypala 7. Mr. P. P. Ekanayake
8. Mrs. J. M. W. Jayakody 8. Mr. L. H. Lickson
      By  order of  His  Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.

Colombo 01,
30th December 2013.
