THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: Bala2/1/3104/72.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2006/HW/482.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
The land  called Sapugahawaththa in extent  about   0.0012 Hectare,
depicted as Lot Nos. 01 and 02 in the Surveyor General's Plan of
P.P.G . 3756 of 18th May, 2010 situated at Paragahathota Village in
Balapitiya Divisional Secretary’s Division of Galle District.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Sapugahawaththa; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Sapugahawaththa; Extent: 0.0007 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Balapitiya Divisional Secretariat on 14th day of August, 2013 at
02.30 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of August, 2013 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. K. Sarasi S. Gunasekara,
Divisional Secretary,
Balapitiya.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Balapitiya.
09th May, 2013.
