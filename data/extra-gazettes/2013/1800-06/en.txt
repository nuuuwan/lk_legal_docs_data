L.D.B. 21/1978 (VIII)
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under  Section 25 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,    I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, the  Minister of Higher Education,  having obtained in respect of  the  National School of
Business Management Limited incorporated under the  Companies Act,  No. 7 of  2007 and  having company  number
PB 4833, a   Report in accordance with the  provisions of  section 70 C of  the aforesaid  Act, do  by   this order,  grant  the degree
awarding status,  for the  purpose of  developing higher  education leading to  the conferment of  the Degree Programmes and
Postgraduate Diploma Programmes specified in   Column I of the Schedule hereto of  which  the abbreviations are  set out in  the
corresponding entries in  Column II of  the Schedule respectively, to the National School  of  Business Management  Limited
(NSBM), which is owned by the National Institute of  Business Management (NIBM) established by Law, No.  23 of 1976.
