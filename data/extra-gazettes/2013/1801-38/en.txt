CIVIL AVIATION ACT, NO. 14 OF 2010
REGULA TIONS made by the Minister of Civil Aviation by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 117 (q) of the Civil
Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010.
PIYANKARA JAY ARATNE,
Minister of Civil Aviation.
Colombo 02,
14th March, 2013.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as Aeronautical Service Charges at Mattala Rajapaksha International Airport.
2. Unless otherwise exempt by Director General of Civil Aviation in writing,
(a) every aircraft operating International commercial air services into and out of Mattala Rajapaksa International
Airport shall pay the following Landing charges and Parking Charges to the Statutory Service Provider.
01 Landing Charges United State's Dollars 4.00 per Metric Tonne of the Maximum
Take-off Weight of the aircraft or part thereof, subject to a
minimum charge of United State's Dollars 250 per landing.
02 Parking Charges 10% of the applicable Landing charge for a period of 06 hours or
part thereof in excess of 1st three hours of parking after landing.
(b) every aircraft operating domestic commercial air services into and out of Mattala Rajapaksa International
Airport shall pay the following charges to the Statutory Service Provider.
01 Landing Charges Rs. 200.00 per Metric Tonne of the Maximum Take-off Weight
of the aircraft or part thereof, subject to a minimum of 2,500 per
landing.
02 Parking Charges 10% of the applicable Landing charge for a period of 06 hours
or part thereof in excess of 1st three hours of parking after
landing.
