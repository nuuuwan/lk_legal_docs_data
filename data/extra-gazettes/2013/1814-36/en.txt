EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
NUW ARA-ELIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Murugaiah Yogeswary the recognized political party to which
Ceylon Democratic Unity Alliance the member belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible as a member of that
Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of
thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Atputharaj Perumal being
an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of  Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Atputharaj Perumal elected as  a member of   the Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara-Eliya.
07th June 2013.
