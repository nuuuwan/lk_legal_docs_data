SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT  ACT, No. 40  OF  1979
Order Under Section 14
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 14 (1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Rishad
