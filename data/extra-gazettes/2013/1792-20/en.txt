EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MANNAR URBAN COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Mannar Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending more than three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Nilamutheen Nahusheen  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Mannar Urban Council who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Nilamutheen Nahusheen
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana, Returning Officer of  Mannar Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Nilamutheen
Nahusheen elected as  a member  of   the  Mannar Urban Council.
K. A. SUMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Mannar Urban Council.
Mannar,
31st December  2012.
