WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
THE  WAGES  BOARD FOR THE  ENGINEERING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, 1971 that Mrs. R. N. Gamlath has been appointed
as Employer Representative of the Wages Board for the Engineering Trade in place of Mr. R. A. U. Perera and Miss. K. K. A.
D. C. de Kumarasinghe and Mr. R. A. P. K. Ranathunga has been appointed as Employee Representative for the above Trade
in place of W. Upul Priyashantha, repetitively by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 09 of the
Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) This Appointment is valid only Up to 30th June 2014.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Labour Secretariat, Ministry of Labour & Labour Relations.

31st May, 2013.

WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD FOR THE  MOTOR TRANSPORT  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of
the Wages Board for the Motor Transport  Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under
Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 16th of
February 2013.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour & Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
25th June, 2013.
