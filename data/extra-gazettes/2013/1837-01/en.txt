I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose for                              
Ra/Godakumbura Miyanawita Maha Vidyalaya.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.3330 
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 109 in the Preliminary Plan 
No. F.V.P. 35 dated 29.07.2013 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Village called Miyanavita in 
Pannilpaththuwa Minor Division in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Kahawaththa in Rathnapura 
District of the Sabaragamuwa province and morefully 
described as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 109 
 
Name of the Land: Dummalehena; Description: Tea 
plantation and mixed crop cultivation; Claimant: R. A. 
Somaratna; Total Extent: 0.3330 Hectare.   
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Kahawaththa 
Divisional Secretariat on 06th January, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 03rd January, 2014 the nature of 
their interest  in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 B. A. C. P. BAMUNUARACHCHI,
