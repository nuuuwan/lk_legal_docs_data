EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    VICE   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
KANDY   GRA VETS  AND  GANGAWATA   KORALE    PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF
THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a Vice Chairman in Kandy Gravets  and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by
reason of Vice Chairman has been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Delkandura Arachchige Dharmasena  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Saddhathissa Dasanayake to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Saddhathissa Dassanayake  elected as  Vice Chairman of the Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale
Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
29th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    MEMBER  OF  THE
RIDIGAMA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Ridigama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of
the North Western Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  theUnited National Party, the recognised political party  to which  M. S. Perera the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under
the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Ridigama Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Mahamadu Ismile Seiyadu
Kaleel being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalagama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Ridigama Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Mahamadu Ismile Seiyadu Kaleel elected as a member of the Ridigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ridigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
15th  November, 2013.
