Notice under Section 7

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
An allotment of land mentioned in Plan No. Gam. 3352 prepared
by the Superintendent of Surveys in Gampaha instead of Surveyor
General situated in  the Village of Maduwegedara in Ranpokunagama
Grama Niladhari Division No. 349/A, in Divisional Secretary's
Division at Attanagalla, in the District of Gampaha  in Western
Province  and described as follows :
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Pokunawalewatta; Description: Pansalwatta;
Claimant: State;Original claimant: National Housing Development
Authority,  Srimath Chiththampalam A Gurdner Mawatta, Colombo
02;  Extent: 0.3484 Hectare.

writing (in duplicate) on or before the 17th day of July, 2013 the
nature of their interests in the land the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
