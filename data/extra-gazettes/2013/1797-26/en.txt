Government  Notifications
1A
THE  PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
In terms of section 02 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka dated 23.07.2010, it is hereby notified that annual religious festival of Thumbawula Purana Rajamaha Vihara of
Mologgamuwa in Pasgoda, Matara District, Southern Province for the year 2013 will be held from the Sunday 20th February
2013 to Sunday 27th February 2013.
K. N. K
AUSHALYA KUMARI,
Divisional Secretary,
Pasgoda.
At Divisional Secretariat of Pasgoda,
31st  January 2013.
