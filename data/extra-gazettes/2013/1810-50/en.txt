NOTICE CALLING FOR CLAIMS TO 
LAND PARCELS 
 
Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No : 51/0555 Gampaha District  
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 511201 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Adiambalama   
(Block 02) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Nilad-
hari Division of 155 - Adiambalama Western  in the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Katana in the District of Gampaha in the 
Province of Western should submit his claim to the undersigned 
before 17th June, 2013 In the event of failure  to submit any claim 
before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an 
exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly pub-
lish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 511201 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals’ office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Land Title Set-
tlement.
