PANWILA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
By-law relating to parking of hiring Cars and Motor Vehicles
I, Sarath Ekanayake, as Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of the Central Provincial Council, do hereby
declare, according to powers vested in me under Sub-section (1) of Section 123 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, to
be read with Sub-section (1) (a) of Section 2 of Provincial Council Act No. 12 of 1989 (Consequential Provisions), that,  I have
approved the deletion of By-law No. 03 of By-laws relating to parking of hiring cars and Motor Vehicles within the Pradeshiya
Sabha Authority Area of Panwila, according to the notification published in the Govt. Gazette No. 1510/42 dated 17th August
2007, and has approved the substitution of the under mentioned By-law No. 03 framed by the said Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha
by virtue of powers vested under Sub-section (vii) (c) of Section 126 to be read with Sub-section (1) of Section 122 of the said
Pradeshiya Sabha Act and that all other By-laws therein shall remain in force unchanged and the said By-law shall be
effective within Pradeshiya Sabha Authority Area from the date of publication of this notice in the Govt. Gazette.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
The Chief Minister of the Central Provincial Council and the
Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government.
Office of the Chief Minister,
Central Provincial Council,
Kandy,
08th January, 2013.
BY-LAW  RELATING  TO  PARKING  OF  HIRING  CARS  AND  MOTOR  VEHICLES
Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha
03 No motor vehicle shall be parked for the purpose of hiring in any place other than in the places mentioned in the 1st
Schedule shown herein, to be notified by the chairman after approval of the Pradeshiya Sabha and the said Motor Vehicle
parking places shall be amended from time to time upon a resolution passed by the Council.
