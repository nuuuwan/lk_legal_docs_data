Direction No. 45
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority directs all Incense/Joss Sticks Manufacturers, Importers and Traders that they shall mark the Maximum
Retail Price, Batch Number, Expiry Date, Net Weight and the name and address of the Manufacturer and/or the Importer on
the pack or on the container or on the wrapper of all Incense/Joss Sticks manufactured/imported by them.
This Direction shall come into effect from 1st July 2014.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,

Direction No. 47
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority directs that no Manufacturer, Distributor or Trader shall manufacture, distribute, transport, store, sell,
expose for sale or offer for sale in wholesale or in retail any locally manufactured Mosquito Coils or Mosquito Mats unless
the SLS Product Certification Mark issued by the Sri Lanka Standard Institution is legibly printed in a conspicuous place on
each and every pack of such Mosquito Coils and Mosquito Mats.
Sri Lanka Standard Specification for Mosquito Coils SLS 453
Sri Lanka Standard Specification for Mosquito Mats SLS 930
This Direction shall come into effect from 1st December 2014.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Direction rescind Direction No. 10 published in Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 1484/16 dated 15th February 2007.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
