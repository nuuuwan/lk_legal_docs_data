WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD FOR THE  BISCUIT  &  CONFECTIONARY  (INCLUDING  CHOCOLATE  MANUFACTURING)
MANUFACTURING TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of
the Wages Board for the Biscuit & Confectionary (Including Chocolate Manufacturing)  Manufacturing Trade, by virtue of
the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of
1941, for a period of three years commencing on 16th February 2013.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour & Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
10th July, 2013.
