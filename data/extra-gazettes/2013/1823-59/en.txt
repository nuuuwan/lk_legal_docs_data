FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  BADDEGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of a member in Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party.
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Guniyangodage
Leelarathna Gunawardhana, the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person elegible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that Baddegema Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of Thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Nambukarage Gunapala,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith de Silva, Returning Officer of Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Nambukarage
Gunapala, elected as a member of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. 
DE SILV A.
Returning Officer,
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
05th August, 2013.
