THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: DS/D/LA/PU/02.
Land Ministry's  No.: L/D/05/2009/WS/63.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An Allotment of land in total extent about 0.0016 Hectare
described as Lot No.  90 in CM 320021 prepared by the Surveyor-
General situated in the Gampolawatta Colany in Gampolawatta
Grama Niladari Division in Kandukara Pahala Korale Minor Division
in Divisional Secretary's Division of Doluwa in the Kandy District,
Central Province.
Lot No. 90
Name of land: Gampolawatta; Original claimant: Korala Gedara
Podimenike; Extent: 0.0016 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat Doluwa, on 24th day of June, 2013 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 17th day of June, 2013, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
B. M. P. G. P . V . Bandara,
Divisional Secretary,
Doluwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Doluwa.
29th April, 2013.

CORRIGENDA
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. 4/7/45-11.
Land Ministry's File  No.: 03/1/J/91/UDA/184.
The notice issued in accordnace with section 7 of the Land Acquisition
Act (Chapter 460)  and published in the Gazette Extra-Ordinary No.
1151/28 of 29.09.2000, based on the ownership inquiry held on
27.10.2000 and the notice issued under section 10 (1) (a) of the said
Act. and whereas the claim to the acquired land referred to in plan B/
508 of the F.V .P. happened to be not duly established and the
anticipated compensation was not properly received by the land
owners in spite of the matter was referred to the Court for a ruling,
upon the demise of the first owner of the said land, and in accordance
with the appeal made subsequently by the claimants for
compensation, I do hereby give notice to the public that the inquiry
made into the Right of Ownership held in terms of Section 18 of the
Interpretation Ordinance, the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460),
is cancelled, and the notice under Section – 07 thereof is amended as
mentioned hereunder.
The two blocks of lands described below in extent of 0.1449
Hectare stated in plan No. B/508 dated 10th April, 2013, prepared
by Badulla Senior Superintendent of Surveys and superimposed in
the preliminary plan No. B/508 dated 27.10.1999, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in Kendalagama Village, No. 10 Badulla-
Central Grama Niladhari Division (within Badulla Municipality),
coming under Badulla Divisional Secretary's Division of Badulla
District.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Pandithagedarawatte and Radalawatte Assmt. No.
37, 39, 39/1, 39/2, 39/3, 39/4, 39/5, Post office  Road; Description:
Garden, a part of  a permanent building; Claimant: State; Extent:
0.0228 Hectare.
