SPORTS  LAW, No. 25 OF 1973
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sports under Section 41 and read with Section 31 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973.
MAHINDANANDA  A LUTHGAMAGE,
 Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
30th August, 2013.
Regulation
The National Associations of Sports Regulations, No. 1 of 2013, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1793/3 of
January 15, 2013 is hereby amended by the insertion immediately after the regulation 54(2) of the Following new regulation –
‘‘(3) Notwithstanding anything in any of the preceding regulations, the Minister may direct the Secretary, Additional
Secretary, or Director General of Sports, of the Ministry of Sports, to convene and conduct for such purpose and in such
manner as may be determined by the Minister, Special General Meeting, General Meeting or Committee Meeting of any
National Association. Any such meeting so convened and conducted shall be deemed to be a duly constituted meeting of
that National Association.’’
