I, hereby give Public notice, under Sub-section (1) of 
section  7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The land called Kachcheriwatta, in an extent of 
Hectares 0.0130 depicted as  lot No. 639 advance tracing 
No. H/AMB/2013/188 prepared by the surveyor General, 
situated in FVP 466 Thawaluwila village in Puhulyaya 
Grama Niladari Division in  East Giruwapattuwa (sulu 
division) in Ambalantota Divisional Secretary’s Division 
in Hambantota District in Southern Province. 
 
 
North: Lot No. 128 in H/AMB/2013/188; East: Lot 
No. 128 and Walawa River in H/AMB/2013/188; South: 
Walawa River & Lot No. 142 in H/AMB/ 2013/188; 
West: Lot No. 142 and Lot No. 128 in H/AMB/2013/188.   
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at  Divisional Secretariat Ambalantota 
on the 03
rd day of March, 2014 at 10.00 a. m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 17th day of February,  2014  the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
 
 H. T HILAKAWARDANA,
