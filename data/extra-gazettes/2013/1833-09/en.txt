THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in extent of about 0.1177 Hectare and the
name mentioned below depicted in Advance Tracing No. CO/THI/
2012/738 dated 03.10.2012 prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Kollupitiya (within the limits of Colombo
Municipal Council, Division Number 3, Kollupitiya), in
Thimbirigasyaya Divisional Secretary’s Division, Colombo District
in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Assmt. No. 174, R. A. de Mel Mawatha – Extent:
About 0.1177 Hectare
North: Kollupitiya Police Station and the President Security
Women Police Division; East: R. A. de Mel Mawatha and
Muhandiram Road; South: Muhandiram Road; West: Kollupitiya
Police Station, Assmt. No. 59, Muhandiram Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
