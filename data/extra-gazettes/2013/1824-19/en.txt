EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    MEMBER  OF  THE
BINGIRIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Arachchi Mudiyanselage Munidasa Premachandra the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Bingiriya Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Bingiriya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Nalin Chaminda Thilakarathna Muthunayakege who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the
member declared elected at the election held on 17th March 2011 to Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
21st  August, 2013.
