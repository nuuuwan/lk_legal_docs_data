UV A  PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of Uva Province
I hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province.
1. Mr. R. M. Jayasekara, Retired officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, Reappointed on contract
basis as Zonal Director to the Education of the Bandarawela Zonal Education, from the date 20.11.2012 till the
appointment of a permanent officer or Up to 30.04.2013,
2. Mr. S. M. Jayasundara officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the Director to Road for
Uva Province, from the date 24.11.2012 to 03.12.2012, till the return of Mr. A.A.P.S.K. Karunarathne, to the Island
who is the Director to Road for Uva Province,
3. Mr. W.M.M.G . Abesinghe Banda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service to perform the Duties of the
Secretary to the Council of the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 28.11.2012
to 07.12.2012, till Mr. K. D. Sirisena resumes his duty after sick leave, who is the Secretary to the Council of the Uva
Provincial  Council,
4. Mr. M.T.G.A.N. Lakmal officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the Director to Provincial
Mechanical for Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 11.12.2012 to 18.12.2012, till the return
of Mr. S.T. Thennakoon, to the lsland who is the Director to Provincial Mechanical for Uva Province,
5. Dr. Mrs. J.C.M. Thennakoon, to perform the Duties of the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province,
in addition to her permanent post, from the date 10.12.2012 to 15.12.2012, till the return of Dr. Mrs. M.K.D. Senanayake
to the Island who is the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province,
6. Dr. Mrs. J.C.M. Thennakoon, to perform the Duties of the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province,
in addition to her permanent post, from the date 26.12.2012 to 31.12.2012, till the return of Dr. Mrs. M.K.D. Senanayake
to the Island who is the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province.
