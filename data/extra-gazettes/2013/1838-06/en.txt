1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF A MEMBER OF  THE  NUWARA-ELIYA  MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION 65A(2) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Nuwara-Eliya Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the Central Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Radhakrishnam Rajaram, the recognized political party to which
United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Nuwara-Eliya Municipal
Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Mari Ramanathan
Balakrishnan, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando,  Returning  Officer  of  Nuwara-Eliya Municipal
Council, acting in  terms of    Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Mari Ramanathan Balakrishnan, elected as  a member  of   the  Nuwara-Eliya Municipal Council.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara-Eliya Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Nuwara-Eliya.
20th November, 2013.
