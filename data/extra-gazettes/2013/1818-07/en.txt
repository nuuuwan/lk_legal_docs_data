UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY Virtue of powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the Colombo International Nautical and Engineerig College (Private) Limited (CINEC) incorporated under
the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 and having company Number PV 17745, do by this order, recognize the aforesaid Colombo
International Nautical and Engineering  College (Private) Limited (CINEC) as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of
developing Higher Education leading to the conferment of Degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the
abbreviations are set out in the corresponding entries respectively in Column II of the Schedule.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Programmes Abbreviations to the Degree
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Maritime Sicience BSc (Hons) Maritime Sicience
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Marine Engineering BSc (Hons) Marine Engineering
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Logistics & Transportation BSc (Hons) Logistics & Transportations
D
