FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF CHAIRMAN  OF THE  PUTTALAM  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of    Chairman in Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Rankiri Pathirage Diluk Susira Pathirage  the Chairman   belonged, was required by me  to   decide upon the   member of  that
Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as   Chairman  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Bamunu Arachchige Nimal Bamunuarachchi,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana,  Returning  Officer of   Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha,  do
hereby declare  Mr.  Bamunu Arachchige Nimal Bamunuarachchi, elected as   Chairman  of   the Puttalam   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
K.A. SUMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
 Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam
28th May, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   PUTTALAM  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of   Death ;
 And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which  Rankiri
Pathirage Diluk Susira Pathirage   the  member  belonged, was required by me  to   nominate a person eligible  as a  member  of
that Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared  elected as a member within a  period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. Arachchilage Subasinghe
Surath Isuru Chaminda,  being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana,  Returning  Officer of   Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha,
do hereby declare  Mr. Arachchilage Subasinghe Surath Isuru Chaminda, elected as   a  member  of   the Puttalam   Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K.A. SUMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
 Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam
28th May, 2013.
