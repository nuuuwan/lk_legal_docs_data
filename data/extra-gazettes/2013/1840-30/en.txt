ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  8(1)  OF  THE  TOWN  AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  No.  13  OF  1946
(AS  AMENDED  BY  ACT,  NO.  49  OF  2000)
Declaration of Regional Development Area
I, Wimal Weerawansa, Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing and Common Amenities by virtue of powers
vested in me under Section 8(1) of the Town and Country planning Oridnance No. 13 of 1946 as amended by Act, No. 49 of
2000 do hereby declare that the areas given in the following schedule to be a regional development area for the purpose of
preparation of a Regional Physical Plan.
WIMAL W EERAWANSA,
Minister of Construction, Engineering Services,
Housing and Common Amenities.
Ministry of Construction, Engineering Service, Housing and Common Amenities,
"Sethsiripaya",
Battaramulla,
11th December 2013.
SCHEDULE
The area comprising the administrative boundary of the North Central Province as given in the 8th schedule of the
13th amendment to the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  21(2)(a)  OF  THE  TOWN  AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  No.  13  OF
1946  (AS  AMENDED  BY  ACT,  NO.  49  OF  2000)
Order to prepare a Regional Development  Plan for North Central Province
I, Wimal Weerawansa, Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing and Common Amenities by virtue of powers
vested in me under Section 21(2)(a) of the Town and Country planning Oridnance No. 13 of 1946 as amended by Act, No. 49
of 2000 do hereby direct, that a Regonal Physical Plan for the Regional Development Area of North Central Province be
prepared within 12 months from the date hereof.
