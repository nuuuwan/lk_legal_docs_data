WHEREAS an Industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the termiation of  Mr. A. D. A. Amarasinghe  who worked as a Security Officer of Kantale Sugar Industries  Limited at the
age  of 53 years, depriving his opportunity of working up to the age of 57 years is just,  and if not so, to what relief he is entitled.
