THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: BGM/DS/2/2/43.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land mentioned below situated in the
Village of Ganegama East in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Baddegama in the District of Galle, Southern Province depicted in
the Preliminary Plan No. GA/3953 dated 18.05.2012 prepared by
the Superintendent of Surveys, Galle District:
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Sarappugewela; Description: Paddy field; Extent:
0.3296 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Sarappugewela; Description: Paddy field; Extent:
0.0751 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of  Baddegama, on the 31st day of July,
2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 24th day of July, 2013,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
D. K. S. Jayalakshmi,
Divisional Secretary,
Baddegama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Baddegama,
20th  May, 2013.
