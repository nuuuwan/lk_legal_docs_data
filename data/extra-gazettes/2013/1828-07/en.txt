THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 9(1) (a) of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Article
44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order designate the ''Colombo - Katunayake National
Highway" which was declared a National Highway by Order published  in the  Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1823/ 6 of August
12, 2013 and specified in the Schedule  to this Order, to be an Expressway.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
10th  September, 2013.
