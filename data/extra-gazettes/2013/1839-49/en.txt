No. 1839/49 –  FRIDAY, DECEMBER 06, 2013 
EXTRAORDINARY 
 NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
Reference No.: KDS/PDB/1/6/1/196. 
 
I, do hereby give notice, that the following land has been 
acquired by the Government for a public purpose, under 
Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act 
(Chapter 460) as Amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  
 
Description of the acquired area : 
 
Two allotments of land, with extent of about 0.0082 
hectares, depicted in the preliminary plan Maha/5400, 
surveyed and prepared by the superintendent of surveyors 
of Kandy district and situated in Madawala village in 
Pathadumbara Pallegampaha Korale minor division of 
Pathadumbara division in Kandy District, Central 
province. 
 
 
01. Lot No. 59 
 
Name of the Land: Hitinaw atte; Claimant: A. R. 
Badurunniza, No. 95, Wattegama Rd, Madawala;              
Extent: 0.0057 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 60 
 
Name of the Land: Hitinaw atte; Claimant: A. R. 
Badurunniza, No. 95, Wattegama Rd, Madawala; Extent: 
0.0025 Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are  
required to appear before me personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, at the Divisional Secretariat 
Pathadumbara, on the 05th day of March 2014 at 10.00          
o' clock in the morning, and notify to me in writing with 
duplicate before the 26th day of February 2014, the 
nature of their interests in the aforesaid land and the 
amount and the particulars of their claims for 
compensation. 
 
I do, hereby, further declare that, whether the persons 
interested as foresaid do app ear as required above or not, 
I shall proceed to hold an inquiry to determine the 
compensation payable for the acquisition of the aforesaid 
lands.
 
 
 L. A. S UJATHA WIJESINGHE, 
  Divisional Secretary, 
 Pathadumbara. 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
03rd December, 2013.
