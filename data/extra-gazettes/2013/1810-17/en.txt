EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   BANDARAGAMA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Bandaragama  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the  recognized political party to which
Ajith Anura Talangamaarachchi  the member  belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible  as a  member of that
Bandaragama   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of
thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Wilegoda Wickramage
Pathmasiri,  being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning  Officer of  Bandaragama   Pradeshiya Sabha,
Mr. Wilegoda Wickramage  Pathmasiri , elected as  a member  of   the Bandaragama   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
2rd  May  2013.
