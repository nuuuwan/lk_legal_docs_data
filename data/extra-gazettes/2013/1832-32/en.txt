and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05. of the other prt
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
01. Whether the non-payment of arrears of salary to Mr. D. Wimalasena, in accordance with the promotion made on 01.01.2002,
by the Sri Lanka Transport Board is just, and if not so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
02. Whether the salary increments first being paid on two occassions to Mr. D. Wimalasena on 18.09.2002 and 21.10.2012 and the
same being cancelled and recovered through circular No. 2004/04 is just, and if not so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
V . B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.

Colombo, this 06th day of September, 2013.
