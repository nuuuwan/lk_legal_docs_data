EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  KANDY  GRA VETS  AND  GANGAWATA
KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER SECTION  65A  (1)
OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman  in Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason
of  being elected as a member of the Central Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which Gamini
Wijaya Bandara the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Delkandura Arachchige Dharmasena to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.
Delkandura Arachchige Dharmasena elected as Chairman of the  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy
05th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  KANDY  GRA VETS  AND
GANGAWATA  KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)
OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of a Member  in Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason
of  being elected as a member of the Central Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which Gamini
Wijaya Bandara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mrs. Hetti Arachchilage
Sunethra Kumarihami being and elegible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs.
Hetti Arachchilage Sunethra Kumarihami elected as a member of the  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale
Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy
05th  November, 2013.
