Provincial  Councils  Notifications
UV A   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Amendment to Section 4 of the Gazette Notification  Extraordinary No. 1793/6 dated
16th January, 2013
SECTION  4 of the Gazette Notification Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1793/6 dated 16th January 2013 is amended as
follows on 01.08.2013.
As the functions of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District have been suspended under Subsection 3(a)iii
of 2011 of Charter No. 02 of 2011 on Power of Administration and supervision of the Local Government Institutions of Uva
Province the appointment of Mr. Maley Pathiranage Nishantha Mohan Wickramasinghe the Commissioner of Local Government
of Uva Province to execute and perform the duties is hereby cancelled and order Mr. Ratnayake Mudiyanselage Jayawardena,
Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Badulla District to execute the powers and carry out the tasks thereof.
S
HASHEENDRA RAJAPAKSA,
The Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning,
Law and Order,  Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport
Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural
Infrastructure Development and Construction.
