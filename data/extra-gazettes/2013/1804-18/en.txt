Government  Notifications
1A
IMPORT  AND  EXPORT  (CONTROL)  ACT,  No. 1  OF  1969
Regulations made by the President under Section 20 read with Section 14 and Subsection 3 of 4 of the Import and Export
(Control) Act, No. 1 of 1969 and paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
31st  March  2013.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Import and Export (Control) Regulation, No. 02 of 2013.
2. Ethyl alcohol under H. S. Code which was banned to import to this country by the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1799/15 dated 01.03.2013, is permitted to import subject to the license issued under the Import and Export
(Control) Act No. 1 of 1969.
3. As set out in the above regulations, license shall be issued to the importers subject to the following rules and
conditions.
i. Excise permit shall be obtained under the Excise Ordinance.
ii. The importers shall confirm that they have duly paid other duties imposed time to time including Excise
Duty, Value Added Tax (V AT), and Income Tax.
iii. Shall Subject to rules time to time made by Commissioner of Excise with regard to local purchase.
iv. Ethyl alcohol imported shall be in conformity with accepted standards.
4. Regulations 02 and 03 above shall apply to the business entities established under the Board of Investment Law,
No. 4 of 1978.
