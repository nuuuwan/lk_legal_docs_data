Provincial  Councils  Notifications
SOUTHERN     PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 277 OF THE MUNICIPAL COUNCILS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 252) READ WITH SECTION 2
(1) AND SECTION 4 OF THE POWERS OF SUPERVISION OF THE ADMINISTRATION OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT
AUTHORITIES STATUTE NO. 04 OF 1990 AND SECTION 2 OF THE PROVINCIAL COUNCILS (ANCILLARY
PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 12 OF 1989.
I, Shan Wijayalal de Silva, the Minister in Charge of the subjects of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Transport, Water
Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local Government of the
Southern Province Provincial Council, acting under the powers vested in me in terms of Section 277 of the Municipal
Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local
Government Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, did cause an inquiry to be held by a retired judicial officer appointed by
me to inquire into and find out whether Mr. A. A. Methsiri Alexander de Silva, the Mayor of Galle Municipal Council had, in
the performance of his functions as the Mayor of  Galle Municipal Council committed any acts of offence falling under
Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) and section 2(1) of the Powers of  Supervision of the
Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council. On the
basis of the findings of the said inquiry I am satisfied that the said Mr. A. A. Methsiri Alexander de Silva, the Mayor of  the
said Galle Municipal Council  had committed such offences falling under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 252) and Section 2(1) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute
No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council. As such in terms of Section 277 (i) (1) of the Municipal Councils
Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2(1) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government
Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I remove Mr. A. A. Methsiri Alexander de Silva, the Mayor of Galle Municipal
Council from the Office of  Mayor and membership of the Galle Municipal Council.
S
HAN WIJAYALAL DE SILV A,
Minister of Finance and  Planning,
Law and Order,  Transport, Water Supply and Drainage,
Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine
and Local Government of the Southern Province.
17th January 2013.
