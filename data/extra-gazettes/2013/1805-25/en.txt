No. 1805/25 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 10, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 My No.: MO/D S/E D/03/ACQ/01.
 Land Min. No.: 3/J/91/E/81.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for Monaragala Technical College.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Allotments of land in F.V .P. No. 172, Supplement No. 260, Sheet
No. 301 prepared by the Survey Superintendant in Monaragala,
situated in Muppane Village in the Monaragala Divisional Secretary’s
Division of the District of Monaragala in Uva Province described as
follows.
1. Lot No. 3263
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 0.4146 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 3264
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 12.1899 Hectares.
3. Lot No. 3265
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 0.7209 Hectare.
4. Lot No. 3266
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 1.5910 Hectares.
5. Lot No. 3267
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 0.0302 Hectare.
6. Lot No. 3268
Name of land: Monarakalewatta; Original claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 2.2822 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Monaragala Divisional Secretariat on the 13th day of July, 2013 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 06th day of July, 2013 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
J. M. U. K. Jayasekara,
Divisional Secretary,
Monaragala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Monaragala.
18th March, 2013.
