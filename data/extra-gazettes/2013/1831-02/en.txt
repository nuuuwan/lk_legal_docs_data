PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
SPORTS  ACT, No. 25 OF 1973
Direction  under  Section  54 (3) of National  Sports Association  Regulations
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 54 (3), Regulations of National Sports Associations, published in  Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1825/38 of 30th August 2013, made under Section 41 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, do hereby direct :–
I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, the Minister of Sports do by this Order direct that Mrs. Maharage Duggannaralalage
Ranjani Jayakodi, the Director General of the Department of Sports Development of the Ministry of Sports to convene and
conduct the meetings of the following Sports Associations to elect new office bearers.
1. Sri Lanka Gymnastics Association
2. Sri Lanka Shooting Federation
3. Sri Lanka Body Building Federation
4. Cycling Federation of Sri Lanka
M
AHINDANANDA  A LUTHGAMAGE,
 Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
04th of October, 2013.
