MINUTE  OF   THE   SRI  LANKA   INLAND    REVENUE   SERVICE
THE Public Service Commission has granted approval to make  following amendments to the  Gazette Notification of the
Minute of the Sri Lanka Inland Revenue Service which was published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1804/50 dated Friday,
April 5th, 2013.
Paragraph Proposed Amendment
No.
19.3 (i) Should have completed thirteen (13) years of service which aggregates the periods of service completed in
Class II Grade II, Class II Grade I as per the previous Service Minute and in Grade II as per this Service Minute.
Such period of service should  include  an active and satisfactory period of service of three years (03) in Grade II.
Note :- For the purpose of the active and satisfactory period of service of three years (03) in Grade II hereof,
1. three years (03) of an active and satisfactory period of service in Class II Grade I under the previous Service
Minute; or
2. three years (03) of an active and satisfactory period of service in Grade II under the new Service Minute; or
3. the aggregate of three years  (03) of an active and satisfactory period of service of (1) and (2) above  would be
considered.
19.3 (ii). Should have passed first and second departmental examinations as set out in previous  Service
Minute  which existed prior to coming into force of this Service Minute.
Note of  19.4  :-  For promotion to the Special Grade  under the interim provisions set out in paragraph 19.4 above, officers who
have completed 25 years of active service in Grade II-II and above of the Sri Lanka Inland Revenue Service will be exempt from
