FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    MEMBER  OF  THE
UDUBADDAWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Koranees De
Koralayalage Sanjeewa Prasanna Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Koranees De Koralayalage
Sanjeewa Prasanna Kumara being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Udubaddawa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr. Koranees De Koralayalage Sanjeewa Prasanna Kumara elected as a member of the Udubaddawa Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
21st  October, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    MEMBER  OF  THE
VELANAI   PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Velanai  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
Council for a period of three months without premission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Sothilingam Gobilakshan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Kayilasapillai Sriskanthan
who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election held on 23rd
July, 2011  to Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy
occurred.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
V elanai Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
22nd  October, 2013.
