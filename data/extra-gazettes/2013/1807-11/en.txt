WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Financial Statute, No. 06 of 1990
ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  76(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section 76 (2) of the Financial Statute of the Western Province, No. 06 of 1990,
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local
Government, Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment, Economic Promotion, Power and Energy Trade of the
Western Province being satisfied that no stamp duty is chargeable in the Government of United States of America in the case
of any instrument relating to the transfer of any immovable property executed by or on behalf of or in favour of Government
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby declare that the exception granted by Sub-section (4) of Section
39 of this Statute shall apply with effect from the date of publication of this order in the Gazette to the purchase of Land and
premises bearing assessement No. 148, Galle Road, Colombo 03. By or in favour of on behalf of the Government of the United
States of America.
P
RASANNA RANA THUNGA,
The Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
 Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
 Manpower and Employment, Economic Promotion Power and Energy,
and Trade of the Western Province Provincial Council.
