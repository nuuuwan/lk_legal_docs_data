No. 1798/43 – WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 20, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 3/2/1/7/253/04.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2002/D/183.
I hereby give Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Abagahawatta in extent of
0.0023 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Plan No. P.P .Gam. 3477
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys, Gampaha situated
in the Village of Uthuru Kurana, Division No. 02, Katunayaka within
the limitation of Katunayaka - Seeduwa Urban Council in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Katana in Gampaha District in
Western Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Abagahawatta; Description: Open abandoned land;
Claimant: S. Shanthi Danapala, Kandegoda, Baddegama road,
Gonapinuwala;  Extent: 0.0023 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Katana on the 30th day of April, 2013
at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 16th day of April, 2013, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
K. G . H. H. R. Kiriella,
Divisional Secretary,
Katana.
Divisional Secretariat, Katana.
06th February, 2013.
