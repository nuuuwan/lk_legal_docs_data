L. D. B. 9/2011.
FINANCE ACT, No. 11 OF 1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 72 read with section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963 as amended from
time to time, read with Article 44 (2) of the Constitution. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order, vest in the
People’s Bank established under the People’s Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from, 30 th July two thousand thirteen, the
properties described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
The Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
30th day of July  2013.
 SCHEDULE
1. All that divided and defined allotment of amalgamated land marked as Lot 4 of Bandarawatte, Beligahawatte and
Ambalammullewatte situated at Anwarama in Colombo–Kandy road, within the town Council Limits of Mawenella,
Uthuwankanda Electorate in Egodapatha, Thanipperu Pattu of Galbode Korale in the District of Kegalle, Sabaragamuwa
Province and bounded on the North and East by Lot 3 of plan No. 3090 dated 02.06.1973 made by L. A. Beddewela, Licensed
Surveyor, on the South by said Lot 3 and on the West by Lot C in plan No. 150 and containing in extent Three Decimal Two
One Perches (0A. 0R. 3.21P) together with the building bearing Assessment No. 404 and everything else standing thereon.
The said land is registered in folio C 330/269 in the District Land Registry, Kegalle.
2. All that undivided  one thirty second (1/32) share an allotment or allotments of lands partitioned as per the final
judgment in Case No. 17112 in the District Court of Kegalle, the land called Bangalatanne Henewatta situated at Nelundeniya
in Othara Pattu of Beligal Korale in the District of Kegalle, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded on the North by main road,
on the East by land belong to Appuhamigamarachchi, on the South by boundary stones of Batuwanayage Watta and on the
West by ditch, containing in extent of one amuna of paddy together with everything else standing thereon.
The said land is registered in folio E 1072/206 in the Land Registry, Kegalle.
