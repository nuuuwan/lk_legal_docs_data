Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No: W/18/55/2001. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired :

0.7983 Hectares, shown in Preliminary Plan No. GAM. 
3884, prepared by Survey Superintendent of Gampaha, 
situated at Mabola and Kerawalapitiya villages in Minor 
Division of Ragam Pattuwa in  the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Wattala in Gampaha District in the Western 
Province.
