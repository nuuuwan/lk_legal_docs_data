PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual Festival Mother's Church Matara
IT is hereby noticed under Section 02 of the regulation framed under Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) and published in the
Gazette No. 687 of  01.11.1991 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri lanka. That the Annual Festival of Mother's Church
Matara in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Matara in the Matara District will be conducted from Thursday the 6th
September to Sunday the 08th September 2013.
B. RANAWEERA,
Divisional Secretary.
Matara.
At Divisional Secretariat Matara,
12th of June 2013.
