THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry’s No.: 4/3/4/2011FH/355.
My Ref. No.: BER/4/5/2/26.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired :
The Lot Nos. 01 and  02 Surveyor-General's Plan No.Mu.Pi.Ka.4370
is situated at Grama Niladhari’s Division of 744A Duwegoda in
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Beruwala in Kalutara District,
Western Province:
Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Duweowita; Description: Land acquistion for
Construction of Halkandavilla fair under Fisheries Tourism
Programme; Claimant: H. V . H. Karunarathne, No. 325, Galle Road,
Beruwala; Extent: 0.4684 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of  land: Duweowita; Description: Land acquistion for
Construction of Halkandavilla fair under Fisheries Tourism
Programme; Claimant: D. J. Biyanka Jayalath, Muhandiramwatta,
Halkandavilla, Payagala; Extent: 0.0304 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat,  Beruwala on the 09th day of July, 2013 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 23rd day of July, 2013 the nature of their
interests in the lands, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
S. Janaka Sri Chandraguptha,
