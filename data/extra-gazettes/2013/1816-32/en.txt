THE  MINUTE  OF  THE  SRI  LANKA  PLANNING  SERVICE  PUBLISHED  IN  THE  GAZETTES
(EXTRA ORDINARY) Nos. 1670/32  AND  1783/37
SECTION 10.3.1. of the Minute of the Sri Lanka Planning Service published in the Gazettes (Extraordinary) Nos. 1670/32
dated 10.09.2010 and 1783/37 dated 09.11.2012 will be amended as follows :
10.3.1 Appointment to special grade will be done only on promotion of Grade I Officers who have fulfilled the
following requirements as approved by the Public Service Commission.
(i) Should have obtained a Post Graduate Degree in the relevant field (As mentioned in the Appendix "e") ;
(ii) Should have completed five (05) years of active and satisfactory service in Grade I of the Executive category
and should have earned five increments after promotion to Grade I as at the date of gaining eligibility for
promotion ;
(iii) Should have completed not less than 18 years of active service period in the Executive Grade of related service
category/posts as at the date on gaining eligibility for promotion ;
(iv) Should have attained an annual performance level at satisfactory or above within the period of immediately
preceding 05 years as at the date of gaining eligibility for promotion ;
(v) Should have completed 05 years of satisfactory service and should not have been subjected to disciplinary
punishment within the immediately preceding 05 years as at the date of gaining eligibility for promotion.
Method of Promotion :
Officers will be promoted depending on the number of vacancies available and having considered the seniority and
the merit at an interview conducted by a board of interview approved by the Public Service Commission.
