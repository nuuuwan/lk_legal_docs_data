No. 1808/1 – MONDAY, APRIL 29, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The land called Pansalwatta in extent of  0.0438 Hectare, (0 Acre
0 Rood 17.3 Purches) depicted as Lot No. 1 in the Preliminary Plan
No. P. P. G . 3891 prepared by Surveyor-General situated in the
Village of Gonapinuwala in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Gonapinuwala in Galle District.
North: Lot No. 54 of the Prelimiary Plan No. G3857 and the
Road; East: Pansalwatta claimed by the Chief Incumbent of
Nigrodharama Maha Viharaya; South: Pansalwatta claimed by the
Chief Incumbent of Nigrodharama Maha Viharaya, Ampitiya
Batadombagahawatta claimed by H. W. Piyal and the Road; West:
Ampitiya Batadombagahawatta claimed by H. W. Piyal and the
Road and Lot 54 of the Preliminary Plan No. G3857.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Gonapinuwala Divisional Secretariat on the 03rd day of July,
2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 3rd day of July, 2013 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Nimal Gunawardana,
Divisional Secretary,
Gonapinuwala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Gonapinuwala.
11th March, 2013.
