EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
KUNDASALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Kundasale  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  People’s Liberation Front , the recognised political party  to which Muthupatin
Gedara Sunimal Pushpa Kumara  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of that
Kundasale  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. D. K. Nishantha
Dasanayake  being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Kundasale  Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. D. K. Nishantha Dasanayake  elected as  a member of   the Kundasale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
 SAMAN SRI RA THNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
10th  June, 2013.

NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Weeraketiya Pradeshiya   Sabha
I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the  Weeraketiya Pradeshiya   Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Wellahenage Chandrapala of the
Weeraketiya Pradeshiya   Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya  Sabha as he  has ceased to be a
member of the People’s Liberation Front.
W. H. R. W
IJAY A KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
 Weeraketiya Pradeshiya   Sabha.
