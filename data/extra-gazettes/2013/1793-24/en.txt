THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460 )
Notice under Section 7
I,  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1144, Allotments of land called "Godakele" in total
Extent of 0.0765 Hectare, depicted in Final Village Plan No. 3216 in
Extra Paper No. 20, prepared by Surveyor General, situated in the
Village of Thammannawetiya in the Minor Division of Kirimetiyawa
in Divisional Secretary's Division of 'Nawagaththegama in (Puttalam
District in North Western Province as follows:
01. Lot No. 1144 (Part of Lot No. 758)
Name of Land: Godakele; Claimant: State; Original claimant:
B. Ukkuwa, Welewewa, Nawagaththegama  – Extent: 0.0765 Hectare
All persons interested in the aforesaid land to be acquired are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing
before me at Divisional Secretary's Office of Nawagaththegama on
the 06th day of March 2013 at 11.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day of
February, 2013 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
R. P. G. Podineris,
Divisional Secretary,
Nawagaththegama.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Nawagaththegama,
08th December, 2012.
