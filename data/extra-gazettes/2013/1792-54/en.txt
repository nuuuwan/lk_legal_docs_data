NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order by virtue of the power vested in me under the Provisions of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)
Act, No. 12 of 1989, read with the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 and as amended under the
Provisions of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983
Athula Sarath Kumara Wijesinghe Chief  Minister  and the Minister-in-charge of the subject  of Finance and Planning, Law
and Peace, Local Government and Manpower,  Education and Cultural Affairs, Lands, Transport,  Environment, Tourism,
Investment Co-ordinate,  Co-operative and Food Supply and Distribution of North Western Provincial Council, do hereby
delegate the power vested with the Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western
Provincial Council, under the Provisions of the Co-operative   Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended under the Provisions
of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the rules framed there under,
thereafter as per Column III and from the date as per Column II to the persons named as per Column I, to assist the
Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western Province.
A
THULA  SARATH  KUMARA  WIJESINGHE,

and Planning, Law and Peace, Local Government and Manpower,
Education and Cultural Affairs, Lands, Transport,  Environment,
Tourism, Investment Co-ordinate,  Co-operative and
Food Supply and Distribution of North Western Province Provincial Council.
