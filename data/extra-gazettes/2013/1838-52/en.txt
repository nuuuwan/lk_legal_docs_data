EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
IRAKKAMAM    PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of  Chairman in Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognised political party  to which Mohamed
Ismail Naizer  the chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a chairman within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Aliyar Umarkatha Jabeer  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Aliyar Umarkatha
Jabeer elected as chairman of the Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
15th November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
NAULA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of  Chairman in Naula Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of the
Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which Thilak
Bandara the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Naula  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a Chairman within a
period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Walliwela Gedara Chaminda Sanath Dasanayaka  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Naula Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Walliwela
Gedara Chaminda Sanath Dasanayaka elected as Chairman of the Naula Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Naula Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
24th November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
BIYAGAMA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be  a member
of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Amarasinghe
Arachchillage Amal Udayaruwan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Biyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated Mr. Cader Meera Sahibu
Mohamed Rafeek being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne,  Returning Officer of  Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Cader Meera
Sahibu Mohamed Rafeek, elected as a member of the Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
14th November, 2013.
