PART I : S EC. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 10.12.2013
THE  AW ARD
The Honourable Minister of Labour Relations and
productivity promotions by virtue of the powers vested in
him by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Dispute  Act No. 43 of
1950 Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(1956 revised edition) as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4
of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 appointed me as the Arbitrator
by his order dated 10th November 2010 and referred the
following disputes to me for settlement by arbitration.
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is ;-
(1) Whether Messrs D. Jayasena de Silva and L. D.
Ratnapala who were attached to the Galle Depot which
is managed under the Sri Lanka Transport Board are
entitled to receive arrears of salary for the period of
interdiction of their service from 20.10.2004 to
01.06.2005 and if so entitled, what should be the
quantum of payment so due and ;
(2) Whether any injustice was caused to Messrs. D.
Jayasena de Silva and L. D. Ratnapala due to transfer
of them to the Elpitiya Depot from the Galle Depot is
justified and if not justified, to what releif each of them
is entitle.
Appearances : Mr. K. D. S. Jayawickrema appeared for the
Applicants Mr. Janaka Samarasinghe, State Counsel
instructed by Nishani Thenabadu Attorney-at-Law
appeared for the Respondent.
The First Statement and an additional statement were filed
on behalf of the Respondent Sri Lanka Transport Board and a
Replication was also filed on behlaf of the Applicants and the
parties were given time if they could arrive at an amicable
Settlement.
The Parties were not in a position to arrive at a Settlement
and the matter was fixed for Inquiry where the Applicants got
the opportunity to give evidence and was cross examined and
a relevant official of the Respondent gave evidence and was
cross examined and on behalf of the Parties certain documents
were marked.
Having considered the evidence and marked documents
presented by the Parties and being satisfied with the
Applicants’ portion it is ordered that the 01st and 02nd
Applicants Messers D. Jayasena de Silva and L. D. Ratnapala
are entitled to receive arrears of Salary for the period of
interdiction of their services from 20.10.2004 to 01.06.2005 ie.
Rs. 59409.23 for the 01st Applicant D. Jayasena de Silva and
Rs. 56509.23 for the 02nd Applicant L. D. Ratnapala and the
same shall be settled completely within Fourteen (14) days
from the Publication of this Order in the Government Gazette.
And also it is ordered to cancel the transfers from Galle
Depot to Elpitiya Depot of the said Applcants with immediate
effect if the transfers are still in force.
J
AY ANTHA DE SILV A,
Arbitrator.
