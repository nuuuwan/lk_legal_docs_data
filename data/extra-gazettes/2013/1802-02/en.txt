Government  Notifications
1A
SPORTS  ACT  No. 25, OF 1973
I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports, on the powers bestowed upon me under section 39(1) of the Sports Act
No. 25, of 1973 do hereby issue the following special directives.
(1) The term of office of the current Sri Lanka Cricket Executive Committee, elected at the 2012 election, is hereby
extended up to 16th April 2013.
(2) To postponed the Annual General Meeting and Election of Office Bearers/Executive Committee of Sri Lanka
Cricket scheduled to be held on 30th March 2013, till 16th April 2013.
MAHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
15th  March  2013.
