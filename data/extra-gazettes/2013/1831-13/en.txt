PROCLAMATION  BY  THE  GOVERNOR  OF  THE  NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 154(b) Section 08, Sub-section(a) of the Thirteenth Amemdment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tissa Reginold Balalla, Governor of the North Western
Province, do hereby summon the Sixth Provincial Council of the North Western Province to meet at 9.30 a.m. of the Eleventh
day of October 2013, in the “Sassri” Hall of the Provincial Council Complex of Kurunegala.
Given in Kurunegala this seventh day of October Two Thousand Thirteen.
T
ISSA R. BALALLA,
Governor,
North Western Province.
