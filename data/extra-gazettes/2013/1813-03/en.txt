PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
FILLING OF A V ACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13 (B) OF ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE
DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary - General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No.
1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Seventh Parliament by
reason of death of Mr. Jayalath Jayawardhana, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District
No. 02 - Gampaha under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act,
And whereas the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by me
under Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13 (b) of Article 99 of the
Constitution;
And whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return in Form
“M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. A. Ajith Kumara Mannapperuma was declared
elected as a Member of Parliament for the said electoral district;
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested in me by
Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. A. Ajith Kumara Mannapperuma has been
declared elected as a member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District No. 02 - Gampaha to fill the vacancy caused
by reason of death of Mr. Jayalath Jayawardhana.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
03rd June, 2013.
