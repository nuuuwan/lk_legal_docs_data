CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the Governor of the Central Province
BY virtue of  powers  vested in me by Section 32 (1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I,  Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa,
Governor of Central Province hereby notify that the appointments indicated in the schedule below were done by me with
immediate effect.
I. Mr. M. S.  Premawansha, - As the Secretary of  Central Province Chief  Ministry and Provincial
Ministry  of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Manpower,
Education and Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Lands, Trade and
Commerce, Food Supplies and Distribution Affairs and
Investment Co- ordination and the subjects which are not taken
under other Ministries.
II. Mr. K. Kekulandara - As the Secretary of  Central Province the  Ministry of Health,
Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Child Care
Services.
III. Mr. B. M. Nimal Balasuriya - As the Secretary of  Central Province  Ministry of  Agriculture,
Minor Irrigations, Animal Production and Development,
Agrarian Development, Fisheries, Enviromental  Affairs, Hindu
Cultural and Estate Infrastucture Facilities.
IV . Mr. P. B. Wijayaratne - As the Secretary of  Central Province   Ministry  of  Road
Development, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and
Construction.
V. Mr. A. M. U. D. Alahakoon - As the Secretary of  Central Province   Ministry of Sports, Youth
Affairs, Women Affairs, Rural Development and Industries.

IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUW A,
