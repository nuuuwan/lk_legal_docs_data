Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council to inquire and submit a report whether the
Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha, in its functioning, has made the offences stated under Section 2(1) of the aforementioned
Statute.

WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute, No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land,  Local
Government, and Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council do hereby appoint the retired Judge of the Court of Appeal Hon. B. L. K.
Wimalachandra as Single Person Tribunal of Inquiry as per the Powers entrusted to me by the  Provisions of Section 2(2) of
the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province
Provincial Council to inquire and submit a report based on the following matters/allegations within 03 months, whether the
Councilor of Maharagama Urban Council ; Hewa Viruduge Nishantha Wimalachandra, while functioning in his office has
made the offences stated under Section 2(1) of the aforementioned Statute.
Matters/allegations to be inquired about :
1. You, the bearer of the abovementioned name, while functioning as a Councilor in the Maharagama Urban Council,
committed the offence stated in Section 2(1)(d) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities
Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province, by taking measures to obtain prices from Cliftex Industries (Private) Limited
of No. 18, Welimuna Road, Hekitta, Wattala to purchase Buddhist flags for the Maharagama Urban Council on 06th of June
2011 or on a date adjacent to it when the Maharagama Urban Council has not assigned such Authority by way of adopting
a proposal.
2. You, the bearer of the abovementioned name, while functioning as a Councilor in the Maharagama Urban Council,
committed the offence stated in Section 2(1)(a) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities
Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province, by causing to purchase Buddhist flags for the Maharagama Urban Council
under a value of One Hundred Forty Four Thousand Two Hundred and Twenty Eight Rupees (Rs. 144,228), subsequent to
presenting the prices obtained external to the Tender procedure on 06th of June or on a date adjacent to it.
