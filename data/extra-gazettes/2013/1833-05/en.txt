PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
PADDY  HULLING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Paddy Hulling Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance, and specified in the Schedule hereto have been
approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decisions shall come into force on the 1st of November, 2013.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo 05,
05th October, 2013.
SCHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Paddy Hulling Trade and set out in, the Schedule to the notification
published in Gazette No. 14950  of  19th March, 1971 as varied from time to time, and last varied by the notification published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1660/35 of 30th June 2010, should be further varied by the Substitution of the following new
part for Part II of that Schedule.
PART  II
WAGES
The minimum rate of wages for time work applicable to a worker shall be Rs. 750.00 per day
