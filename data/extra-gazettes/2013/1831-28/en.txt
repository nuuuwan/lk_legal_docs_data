THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Ten (10) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.1231 Hectare
shown as Lot Nos. 1 to 10 in Advance Tracing No. Co/KLN/2012/
158 of 30.05.2012 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys of
Colombo, situated in the Village of Halbarawa within the Kotikawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha, Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kolonnawa in
Colombo District, Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0028 Hectare
North: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . Nimal Santha; East:
Ela; South: Ela and Lot B; West: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G.
Upul Kumara and G . Nimal Santha.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0018 Hectare
North: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . Upul Kumara, G . Nimal
Santha and Lot A; East and South: Ela; West: Halbarawa Kumbura
claimed by G . Upul Kumara.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0003 Hectare
North: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . Upul Kumara; East:
Ela; South: Ela and Lot D; West: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G.
Gnanathilaka and G . Upul Kumara.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0047 Hectare
North: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . Gnanathilaka and G .
Upul Kumara and Lot C; East and South: Ela; West: Halbarawa
Kumbura claimed by G . Gnanathilaka.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0074 Hectare
North: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by W. Sunil Kumara and K.
Karunadasa; East: Ela; South: Ela and Lot F; West: Halbarawa
Kumbura claimed by G . G . Gunadasa and W. Sunil.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Halbarawa Kumbura – Extent: 0.0381 Hectare
North: Ela, Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . G . Gunadasa and
W. Sunil and Lot E; East: Ela and Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G.
G . Gunadasa; South: Halbarawa Kumbura claimed by G . G. Gunadasa
and W. A. Kumara and Lot G; West: Lot G and Ela.
