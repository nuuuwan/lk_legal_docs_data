EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  THANAMALWILA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the Party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation front, the recognized political party to which
W. M. Karunadasa,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. S. P. Sirisena  being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of  Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. S. P. Sirisena elected as  a
member  of   the  Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
SURANGA AMBAGAHA TENNE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
Moneragala,
08th October  2013.
