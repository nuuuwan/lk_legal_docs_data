THE  ANTIQUITIES  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 188)
Notice under Section 19
I,  Jagath Balasuriya, Minister of National Heritage do hereby give Notice that it is intended to make an Order under Section
18 of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) declaring the Monument specified in the Schedule to this Notice, to be a
"Protected monument" for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance.
Objections to the making of the aforesaid Order should be forwarded to the Director General of Archaeology,
Colombo, on or before 23rd October 2013.
Every such objection shall be in writing and contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is made.
D
R. JAGATH BALASURIY A,
Minister of National Heritage.
Colombo,
08th October 2013.
SCHEDULE
M
ONUMENT DECLARED AS A PROTECTED MONUMENT
The ancient image house and preaching hall (Darmashalawa) situated within the premises of the Daladawaththa
Purana Viharaya in the village of Talpitiya in the Grama Niladhari Division of Talpitiya- South (GND No. 697) in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Panadura, of the District of Kalutara, Western Province.
