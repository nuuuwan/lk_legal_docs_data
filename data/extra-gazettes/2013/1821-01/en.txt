EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified in the Column II and
III of the schedule hereto, correspoding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Headings mentioned in the
Column I of the schedule of the Extraoridinay Gazette Notification No. 1783/19 dated 2012.11.08 issued under the section 3
of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect
from 31.07.2013.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
 Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
29th July 2013.
