Power Delegation Order
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Co-operative Societies Amendment Statute No. 01 of 2011 read with Section 2.2(5) of
Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009, I, Kidagalegama Hethuhamige Nandasena, North Central Provincial Minister
of Co-operative, Trade and Food, Small Industries, Textile Industries, Rural Development and Tourism, delegate all powers
mentioned in Column III of the Schedule given below to the person mentioned in Column I who has been appointed to
perform the functions of the post of the (departmental) Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development to assist the
North Central Provincial Co-operative Development Commissioner and Registrar in accordance with the aforementioned
statute, to be effective from the date mentioned in Column II.
K
IDAGALEGAMA HETHUHAMIGE NANDASENA,
Minister of Co-operative, Trade and Food, Small Industries,
Textile Industries, Rural Development and Tourism,
North Central Province.
