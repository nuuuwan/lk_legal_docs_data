EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  WATTALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Wattala   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United People’s Freedom Alliance , the recognised political party  to which
Chandrasiri   Gunawardana  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Ranaweera Arachchige
Neil Indika  being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting  in
terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ranaweera
Arachchige Neil Indika   elected as  a member of   the Wattala   Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S.  SENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha
26th  August, 2013.
