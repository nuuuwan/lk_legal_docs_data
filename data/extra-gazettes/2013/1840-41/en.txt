1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN OF  THE  V A VUNIYA  SOUTH  SINHALA
PRADESHIYA SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of Vice Chairman in Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being
elected as a member of the Northern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Ajayathilaka Vanni Hamighe,  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of  that V avuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who
shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision  to nominate
Mr. Vimala Senage Dharsana Prasad Vimalasana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of  Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Vimala
Senage Dharsana Prasad Vimalasana elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the  Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. KUGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, V avuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha.
Vavuniya,
27th November  2013.
