THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land shown as the Lot in extent of about 0.0036
Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. KU/WRM/2011/162,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Gepallawa in the Minor Division of Rekoopaththuwa, in the
Weerambugedara  Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Kurunegala
District in North Western Province and bounded as follows:
Maguruoyawatta - Extent: 0.0036 Hectare
North: Road (RDA); East: Maguruoya; South: Balance portion
of same land; West: Maguruoyawatta claimed by I.K. Pathiraja.
