PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & c., by the Governors
1A
NORTH   CENTRAL    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made   by  the  Hon. Governor   of   North  Central   Province
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32(i) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Karunaratna Divulgane,
Governor of the North Central Province hereby notify that the officers mentioned below are appointed to the respective posts
mentioned.
01. Mr. K. B. Hettiarachchi - SLAS Class I as the Secretary to the North Central Provincial Ministry of Cooperative,
Trade and Food, Small Industries, Textiles Industries, Rural Development and Tourism, with effect from 09.01.2013
until further notice.
02. Mr. P. B. Dayaratna - SLAS  Class I, as the Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning) of the North Central Provincial
Department of Planning and Monitoring with effect from 01.07.2010, until further notice (Amendment to serial No.
05 of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1739/8 dated 03.01.2012)
03. Ms. W. T. A. Manel, SLAS - Class I as the North Central Provincial Commissioner of Local Government, with
effect from 14.02.2013, until further notice.
04. Ms. M. G . N. Dilrukshi, SLAS - Class III to perform the functions of the post of the North Central Provincial
Commissioner of Cooperative Development and Registrar with effect from 14.02.2013 until further notice.
05. Mr. R. M. K. R. B. Ratnayaka, SLAS - Class III to perform the functions of the post of the North Central Provincial
Director of Rural Development, with effect from 16.01.2013, until further notice.
K
ARUNARATNA DIVULGANE,
Governor,
North Central Province.
Governor's Office,
North Central Province,
14th February 2013.
