PROVINCIA L COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
Central  Province Provincial Council
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10
NOTICE  is hereby given under Section 10 of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, (hereinafter referred to as
the Act) that elections are to be held for the purpose of electing members to the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
Districts situated within the Province, and specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto.
02. The number of candidates to be nominated for each such Administrative Districts as is specified in Column I, by
a recognized political party or an independent group, shall be as specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the
Schedule hereto.
03. Nomination Papers will be received by the respective Returning Officers at the places mentioned in the
corresponding entry in Column III of the Schedule hereto, at any time during office hours during the "nomination period"
which will commence on July 25th 2013 and expire at 12.00 noon on August 01st 2013.
04. The attention of any group of persons intending to contest as independent candidates is drawn to Section 14 of
the Act under which the group leader of each independent group or any person authorised by him must deposit with
Returning Officer between the date of publication of this notice and 12.00 noon on the day immediately preceding the end of
the nomination period specified above (i.e. July 31st 2013) a sum calculated at the rate of Two Thousand Rupees (Rs. 2,000)
per candidate.
05. Nomination Papers in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Returning Officer free of charge, before the
expiry of the nomination period.
