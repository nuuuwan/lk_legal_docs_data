No. 1817/33 – WEDNESDAY, JULY 03, 2013
My No.: IR/15/28/2011.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between–
Mr. S. S. K. S. Wijewardane, No. 373, Siyambalagoda, Danture  of the one part.
AND
Ceylon Petroleum Storage Terminals Limited, Nimawa, Kolonnawa  of the other part.
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section  4(1)
of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts Nos. 14 of
1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes-Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. S. Wirithamulla,
No. 128, Pirivena Road, Boralesgamuwa to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
GAMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo.
30th April, 2013.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether Mr. S. S. K. S. Wijewardane, who has served the  Ceylon Petroleum Storage Terminals Limited,has been caused injustice by working
there for 16 years without any promotion, and if so, to what relief he is entitled .
