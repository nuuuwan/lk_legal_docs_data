CUSTOMS  NOTIFICATIONS
1A
Order under Section 69 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 69 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) to be read with Article 44 (2)
of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this notification order and declare that the
premises of Mattala  Rajapaksa International Airport of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be a bonded area
and its respective boundaries shall be as set out in Schedules ‘‘A’’, ‘‘B’’ and ‘‘C’’. There should be a legal airport within such
boundaries for loading and unloading of air passengers, air mail items and other cargo, storing in the bonded warehouses,
importing, exporting and re-exporting of goods, issuing of goods stored in bonded warehouses and extent of such airport
shall be as set out in the Schedules ‘‘A’’, ‘‘B’’ and ‘‘C’’ within the Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
(Minister of Finance and Planning)
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
24th day of July  2013.
 Above Schedules are described as ‘‘A’’, ‘‘B’’ and ‘‘C’’
