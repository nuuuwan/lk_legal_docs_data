PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR THE  ICE,  AERATED  WATERS,  FRUIT  JUICE  AND  JAM  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified Section 29(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that the decisions of the Wages Board for
the Ice, Aerated Waters, Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance, and specified in
the Schedule here to have been approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The Decision shall come into force on the 1st of September, 2013.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations,
Colombo 05,
08th August, 2013.
SCHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Ice, Aerated Waters, Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing Trade and
set out in, the Schedule to the notification published in Gazette no. 13535 of 27th February, 1963 as varied from time to time,
and last varied by the notification in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1664/24 of 30th July 2010, shall be further varied in Part II of
that Schedule by the substitution for the existing part of the following new part.
PART  II
COLUMN
Class of workers
Grade I.
Head Ice cream, Fruit Juice, Jam makers (by adding natural and artificial essences), Kanganies, Head syrup makers,
Operaters of water-purifiers, Can makers, Carpenters, Fillers (Automatic), Checkers.
