THE WAGES BOARDS ORDINANCE
Plumbago Trade
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Plumbago Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance and specified in the Schedule hereto have been approved by
the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decisions shall come into force on the 01st of  January 2013.
W. J. L. U. WIJAY AWEERA,
Secretary,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.

21st December, 2012.

Wages Board for the Batik  Trade
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) read with Section 33(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), that the
determination relating to the minimum rate of wages for time work in respect of workers in the Batik Trade, specified in the
Schedule hereto made by the Commissioner of Labour under Section 33(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, in pursuance of the
authority conferred on him by an order by the Minister of Labour under Section 33(1) (a) of the Ordinance and published in
Gazette No. 48 of  03rd August, 1979 has been approved by the Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The determination shall come into force on the 1st January, 2013.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Minister of Labour. and Labour Relations.
Colombo 05,
21st December, 2012.
