THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection (1) of Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I , Dissanayake
Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, do by this Order, on the recommendation
of the University Grants Commission, amend the Order establishing the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, which order is
deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said Act,
(as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1787/27 of December 05, 2012) in the Schedule thereto as
follows :-
(1) By the repeal of the item “Faculty of Management Studies and Commerce” in Column I and it’s corresponding
entries in Column II of such Schedule appearing immediately after the item “Faculty of Arts” in Column I and it’s
corresponding entries in Column II of the Schedule; and
(2) In the item “Faculty of Management Studies and Commerce” appearing immediately after the item “Faculty of
Medicine” in Column I and it’s corresponding entries in Column II of such Schedule, by the addition at the end of
it’s corresponding entries in Column II thereof, of the following new item :-
“5.  Department of Commerce”.
D
ISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA BANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
17th July, 2013.
