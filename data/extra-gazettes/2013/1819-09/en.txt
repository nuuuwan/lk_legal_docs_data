THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Kakunagahakumbura Lot ‘A’
containing in extent of 4.2267 Hectares, depicted in Advance Tracing
No. Co/Mo/KSB/2004/15 dated 23.06.2004, prepared by Surveyor-
General situated in the Village of Piliyandala in Minor Division of
Salpitikorale in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kesbewa in the
District of Colombo, Western Province and bounded as follows:
Lot ‘A’ – Kakunagahakumbura – Extent: 4.2267 Hectares
North: Road (RDA and Halgahapokuna Road); East:
Halgahapokuna Road and Assmt. No. 165/1, Horana Road; South:
Assmt. No. 165/1, Horana Road (RDA) and Assmt. No. 149, Horana
Road; West: Assmt. No. 149, Horana Road and Kakunagahakumbura
claimed by K. Ranjith, P. Satharasinghe and others.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
