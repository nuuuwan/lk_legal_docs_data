THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land indicated in the Advance Tracing No.
H/TNG/2009/60 dated 12.08.2009 prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Ranna (F.V .P. 328) in Minor Division of
Giruwapattuwa East in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Tangalle
in Hambantota District in the Southern Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Katangahakoratuwa Pallameathmaga and
Pallama – Extent: 0.0058 Hectare
North: Lot No. 79; East: Lot No. 79 and Road (R.D.A.); South:
Road (R.D.A.); West: Canal.
