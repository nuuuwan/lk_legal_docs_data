THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Kammalpitiyawatta shown as the
Lot "A" in extent of about 0.0085 Hectare, indicated  in Tracing
No. CO/HNW/2010/231 and dated 25.08.2010, prepared by the
Surveyor General, which is situated in the Village of Puwakpitiya in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Seethawaka in Colombo
District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
Lot  " A " – Kammalpitiyawatta - Extent: 0.0085 Hectare
North and East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Kammalpitiyawatta
claimed by A.D.N. Abeysinghe and A.D.P .Wikramathunga and
Madatiyawatta claimed by S.Rupasinghe and others; West: Road
(R.D.A.).
