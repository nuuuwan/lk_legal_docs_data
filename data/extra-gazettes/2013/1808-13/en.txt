No. 1808/13 – TUESDAY, APRIL 30, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land (Lot No. 2859)  in extent of 0.234 Hectare
depicted in the Final Village Plan No. 109 Supplementary No. 28
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the village of
Meegahathenna of Pasdun Korale-South Minor division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Walallawita, Kalutara District,
Western Province and described as follows:
Name of land: Medawatta; Description: Cemetery and Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M. A. D. Josalin Kularathna,
Maddegama, Meegahathenna; Extent: 0.234 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Walallawita on 26th day of June, 2013 at
10.30 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 19th day of June, 2013, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
M. L. Dashitha Niroshana,
Divisional Secretary,
Walallawita.
Divisional Secretariat" Walallawita.
12th April, 2013.
