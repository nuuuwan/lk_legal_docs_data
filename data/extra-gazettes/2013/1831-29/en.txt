1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  AKURANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of a member in Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death :
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress,  the recongnised political party to which Ahamed
Mohamed Mohamed Muhuseen  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the  Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Diganahena Gedara
Sameen Mohamed Mihurar being and eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy.
Now therefore I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka,  Returning Officer of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Diganahena
Gedara Sameen Mohamed Mihurar  elected as a member of the Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
03rd September, 2013.
