UV A    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment  made  by  the  Governor of  the Uva Province
BY virtue of  powers vested to me under Section 154 F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of  the Democratic
Socialist  Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Caluwadewagey Nanda Mathew,   Governor of the Uva  Province, do hereby announce that
I have made the  following appointment :–
Hon.  Shashindra Rajapaksha,
C. NANDA MA THEW,
Governor,
Uva Province.
Office of the Governor,
King's  Street,
Badulla,
03rd  June 2013.
07 - 100/ 1
Chief Minister and Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and
Peace, Education, Local  Government, Cultural Affairs,
Transport, Land,  Irrigation,   Economic Promotion and
Rural Infrastructure Development and  Construction of the
Uva Province Provincial Council.
As Acting  Minister of Youth Affairs, Sports, Social Welfare,
Estate Infrastructure Development, Power and Energy, Textiles
and Small Industries, of the  Uva Province  Provincial Council,
from the date 03rd June  2013,  till the return to Sri Lanka of
Hon. Minister  M. Sendil Thondaman, who is the Minister of
above Ministry.
