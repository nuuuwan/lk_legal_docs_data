THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My Ref. No. GCF/ATH/CB/41/LR.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. L03J/93/LRB/74.
The public are hereby notified that the compensation payment in
respect of allotments of land of which the claims were not presented,
as depicted in the Schedule below and included as Lot Nos. 01 to 05
in Preliminary Plan No. 8163 prepared on 31.12.1999 by Surveyor-
General that have been vested in the Government through
Proviso 38(a) No. 45 of 1994 published in the Gazette Notification
Extra Ordinary No. 821/25 dated 03.06.1994 under Greater Colombo
Flood Control and Environment Improvement Project have been
deposited in the District Court, Colombo under case numbers
depicted in the same schedule under Section 33 of the Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotments mentioned could obtain the compensation payable for
the allotment concerned after submitting necessary documents to
prove their ownership to the Court of Justice, Colombo.
C. W. Abesooriya,
Acquiring Officer.
No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha,
Welikada, Rajagiriya.
