THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Five (5) allotments of land called portion of Thennahena of
Dankoluwa Estate in extent of about 7.9749 Hectares, as Lots A, B,
C, D, E in Advance Tracing No. MR/PTB/2012/85, dated 31st May
2012 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys in the Matara
Division situated in the  Village of Pitabeddara Gramaniladari Division
of Pitabeddara Morawakkorale Minor Division of Pitabeddara
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Matara District and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate –
Extent: 1.4797 Hectare
North, East, South and West: Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa
Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate –
Extent: 0.9127 Hectare
North: Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate claimed by
Land Reform Commission and Road; East: Malpudanalla Deniya
claimed by G. Sanjeewa Kumara and others; South: Road and
Malpudanalla Deniya claimed by G. Sanjeewa Kumara and others;
West: Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate –
Extent: 3.2237 Hectares
North: Road and portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate
claimed by Land Reform Commission; East: Road; South: Portion of
Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate claimed by Nagodawithanage
Hemalatha and Road (R.D.A.); West: Portion of Thennahena of
Dankoluwa Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission and
Malpudanalla Deniya claimed by G. Sanjeewa Kumara and others
and Road.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate –
Extent: 2.3104 Hectares
North: Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate claimed by
T. G. Sumathipala and Auction land of Tambipille Estate and Road;
East: Road and portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate claimed
by T. G. Sumathipala and Auction land of Tambipille Estate; South:
Road (R.D.A.) and Road; West: Road.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Portion of Thennahena of Dankoluwa Estate –
Extent: 0.0484 Hectare
North: Road; East and South: Auction land of Tambipille Estate
and Road ; West: Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
