NORTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 154(B), 8(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, I, Gammanpila Arachchige Chandrasiri, the Governor of the Northern Province, do by this Proclamation, summon
the First Session of the First Northern Provincial Council, to meet at 9.30 a.m. on the Twenty Fifth day of October, Two
Thousand and Thirteen at the Provincial Council Assembly Hall of Northern Provincial Council Building, Jaffna (Kaithady).
G . A. C
HANDRASIRI,
Governor,
Northern Province.
Governor’s Secretariat of the Northern Province,
Jaffna,
15th October 2013.
