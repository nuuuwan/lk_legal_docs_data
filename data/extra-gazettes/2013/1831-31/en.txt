THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Article 44(2)
of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order designate the Section of the"Colombo - Katunayake
National Highway" described in the Schedule to this order, which was designated as an "Expressway" by  order made under
Section 9 of the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1828/7 of September 16, 2013, to be an "User Fee
National Highway.".
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
10th October, 2013.
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHW AY
The stretch of road being 23.980 kilometres long and 60.0 metres broad, situated between Peliyagoda (1.820 km.) and
Katunayake (25.80 km.) of the Expressway, designated as the Colombo - Katunayake National Highway.
