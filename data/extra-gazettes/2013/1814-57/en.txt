WAGES  BOARD FOR THE  HOTEL  AND  CATERING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of
the Wages Board for the Hotel and Catering Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under
Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 16th
February 2013.
H
ERATH YAPA,
Actg. Secretary,

Labour Secretariat,

WAGES  BOARD FOR THE  JENITORIAL  SERVICE  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations,  1971 that Mr Mohomed Jeffree have been
appointed as Employer’s Representatives of the Wages Board for the Janitorial Service Trade in place of Mrs. Manel
Wanigasooriya by the  Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance
(Chapter 136). This Appointment is valid only up to 30th November 2015.

ERATH YAPA,
