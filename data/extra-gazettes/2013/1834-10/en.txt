MINUTE  OF  GOVERNMENT  LIBRARIANS'  SERVICE - REVISION  No.  01
IT  has been ordered by the Public Service Commission to revise the Minute of Government Librarians' Service in the
following manner, which was published in the Gaxette Extraordinary No. 1620/22 dated 24.09.2009.
(i) A new qualification is included in the following manner to Section 6.3.1.1 (d) as No. (iii).
(iii) Shall have possessed a higher qualification than the qualifications mentioned in (i) and (ii) above on Library
Science. (Such qualifications shall be determined by the Director General of Combined Services on
consultation with the university authorities and Sri Lanka Library Association).
