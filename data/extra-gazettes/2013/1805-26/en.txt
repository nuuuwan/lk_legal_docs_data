THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My Ref. No. : GCF/ATH/DL/12/LR.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 13/99/LRB/278.
The public are hereby notified that the compensation payment in
respect of allotments of land for which the claims were not presented,
as depicted in Schedules 01 to 07 below and included in Preliminary
Plan No. 8421 prepared on 14.01.2002 by Surveyor General that
have been vested in the Government through Proviso 38(a) No. 319
of 2000 published in the Gazette Notification No. 1163/24 dated
20.12.2000 under Greater Colombo Flood Control Project have been
deposited in the District Court Mount Levinia, under case numbers
depicted in the same Schedule.
The General Public is hereby informed to obtain there compensations
entitle to them after  submitting necessary documents to prove their
ownership to the above mentioned allotments, to the Court of Justice.
C. W. Abesooriya,
