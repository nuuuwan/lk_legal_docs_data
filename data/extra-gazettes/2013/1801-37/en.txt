THE  CIVIL  AVIATION  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2010
Order under Section 6
APPOINTMENT  OF  SERVICE  PROVIDERS
BY virtue of powers vested in me as the Minister of Civil Aviation under Section 6 of the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010,
I, Piyankara Jayaratne, do hereby appoint, subject to the terms and conditions stipulated in the Schedule I hereto, Sri Lankan
Airlines Ltd. which is hereinafter referred to as the Service Provider for providing the Services as specified in the
Schedule II.
P
IYANKARA JAYARATNE,
Minister of Civil Aviation.
12th March 2013.
SCHEDULE I
TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF APPOINTMENT
1. In providing the Services specified in Schedule II, the Service Provider shall conform to the applicable legal provisions
in the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 and Regulations, Rules or Implementing Standards made thereunder or any
other written law ;
2. The Service Provider shall comply with any general or special directions given by the Minister in writing from time to
time as to the discharge of functions of the Service Provider ;
3. The Service Provider shall maintain in good order all immovable properties which are being sub let by the statutory
service provider and movable properties for the wellbeing and development of air transport industry and shall not
give such immovable property on lease to any third part ;
4. Every contract entered into by the Service Provider after the date hereof in respect of any Aeronautical Service
provided by the Service Provider, shall unless there is express provision to the contrary in the terms of such contract,
be deemed to be a contract entered into by the Service Provider on his own behalf and not on behalf of the State and
shall not bind the State in any manner ;

5. On demand by the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister in writing, the Service Provider shall furnish to the
Secretary, with all relevant information concerning any administrative, operational, technical or financial matters
pertaining to the aeronautical services provided ;
6. The Service Provider shall comply with the applicable requirements stipulated by the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri
Lanka or Director General of Civil Aviation as the case may be and obtain requisite licenses or certificates as applicable,
for provision of aeronautical services specified in the Schedule II ensuring that such services are maintained in
conformity with the applicable international standards and recommended practices ;
7. The Service Provider shall not alter, suspend or stop provision of any service mentioned in Schedule II without
approval from the Minister in writing ;
8. It is the duty of the Service Provider to arrange a sufficient insurance cover to meet the potential liabilities or
compensations that may arise during its operations and the State will not be liable for such liabilities or
compensation ;
9. The change of corporate identity, share capital or name of the Service Provider requires prior approval of the Minister
in writing ;
10. The Service Provider shall not transfer or sublease provision of any of the Services specified in Schedule II without
written approval by the Minister.

CHEDULE II
SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED BY THE SERVICE PROVIDER
1. The provision of assistance or equipment for dispatch of aircraft on the apron, including aircraft cleaning services ;
2. The provision of ground handling facilities or services to aircraft ;
3. The provision of Catering Services to aircraft at –
(i) Bandaranaike International Airport, Colombo in the Western Province,
(ii)Mattala Rajapaksa  International Airport in the Southern Province,
(iii)Colombo Airport, Ratmalana in the Western Province
and any other aerodrome to be specified by the Minister in terms of an Order published in the Gazette from time to
time.
