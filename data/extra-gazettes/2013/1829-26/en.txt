PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  40  OF 1979
Order under Section 14
BY  virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Rishad
Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this
order declare that a cess shall be charged, levied and paid on exports of goods, which shall be based on the F. O. B. (Free on
Board) value of the exports or any other specified rate on every article specified in the Column II and III of the Schedule hereto,
corresponding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Headings mentioned in the Column I of the Schedule of
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1783/20 dated 08.11.2012 issued under the Section 14(1) of this Act, shall be
payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from 25.09.2013.
R
ISHAD BA THIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of Industry and Commerce,
Colombo,
24th September, 2013.
