THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
My Reference No.: DSB/L/1/2/125. 
Ref. No. of Land Ministry.: 4-3/13/2012/E/307.  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition Act (amended) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following  I and/
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired :  
The allotments of land in extent about 0.0893 Hectare, 
depicted in C. M. No. 620075 3rd Zone From Zonal 4 In 
Sheet No. 05 Plan prepared by the Surveyor General 
situated at Gorokgahamada Village, No. 255 B, 
Dehigastalawa Grama Niladhari Division, Balangoda 
Divisional Secretariat, Rathnapura Disrict, and 
Sabaragamuwa Province: 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
 1. Lot No.170- Name of the land:Dunumadalagahawatta; 
claimant: A. M. Izad,No.  182/10, Gorakgahamada, 
Balangoda; Extent: 0.0893 Hectare. 
 
All person interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Balangoda Divisional Secretariat on 
29th day of January at 10.00 O' Clock and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 22nd day of January 2014 of the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude* the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
