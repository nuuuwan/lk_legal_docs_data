CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Jagath Premalal Wijeweera,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 30th  December, 2013, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1842/4  of
23rd  December, 2013 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 05th  January,  2014.
J
AGATH P.  WIJEWEERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
27th  December  2013.
