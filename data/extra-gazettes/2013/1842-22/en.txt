FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE   KOTHMALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Kothmale   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member  of  the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Rauf Mohomed
Hilmi  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person
eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Kothmale  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who
shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. R. W. Rajapaksha  being
an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando,  Returning Officer of  Kothmale  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section   65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  R. W. Rajapaksha  elected as  a member of   the Kothmale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kothmale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara- Eliya,
19th  December 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  KINNIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Kinniya  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Mohamed Ali
Mohamed Siraj  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Kinniya  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Kasudeen Munawwer
Khan  being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nalaka Rathnayake ,  Returning Officer of  Kinniya  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section
65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Kasudeen Munawwer Khan  elected
as  a member of   the Kinniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
N
ALAKA RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kinniya   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
23rd  December 2013.
