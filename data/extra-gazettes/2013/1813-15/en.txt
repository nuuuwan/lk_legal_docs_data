Colombo,
05th June 2013.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Exports (Standardization and Quality Control) Regulations.
2. Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 1808/6 dated 29th April, 2013 is hereby cancelled and the said notification is published
again in this Gazette.
3. No exporter shall export the articles setout in Column I of the Schedule hereto unless they conform to the
applicable Sri Lanka Standard setout in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
4. Every exporter shall in respect of each article exported by him, furnish a certificate of conformity with the Sri Lanka
Standard stipulated for that article issued by either laboratory accredited for the testing of the particular article or
a laboratory recognized by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution.
5. Every exporter in respect of each article exported by him shall obtain a certificate of clearance issued by the
Director General of Sri Lanka Standards Institution by providing conformity certificate obtained from either
laboratory accredited for the testing of the particular article or a laboratory recognized by the Sri Lanka Standards
Institution.
6. Every exporter who exports any article setout in the Schedule shall (before Customs Clearance) make available to
the Director General of Customs with Certificate of Clearance issued by the Director General of Sri Lanka Stan-
dards Institution with all documents relating to the articles and samples from each consignment as may be
required by them
