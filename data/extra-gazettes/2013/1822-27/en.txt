2. The Sub-section 5(2) of the Rent Statute No. 05 of 2010 of Uva Province, hereinafter
referred to as “The Principal Statute” is hereby amended as follows ;
“The regulations made by the minister currently in force shall be deemed as made by the
Provincial Minister, when enforcing the relevant acts in the province, it is legitimated to
act according to the regulations enforced or to be enforced in due course by the Provincial
Minister under the relevant acts. Also the Provincial Minister shall be able to abolish the
current order, alternate, make amendments or make new regulations.”
3. In the event of inconsistency between the Sinhala and English or Tamil text of this statute,
the Sinhala text shall prevail.
