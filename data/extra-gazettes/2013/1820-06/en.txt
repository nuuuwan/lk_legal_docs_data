PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
TRINCOMALEE   URBAN   COUNCIL
I, Nalaka Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the  Trincomalee   Urban Council, do hereby declare under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. P. W. Susantha Jayalath of the Trincomalee   Urban Council has vacated
his office of the member of that   Council as he  has ceased to be a member of the People's Liberation Front.
NALAKA RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
 Trincomalee   Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
15th July, 2013.
