 THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference Number: BD/4/7/113. 
 
Reference Number of the Ministry: LD/05/2009/PC/208. 
 
I, hereby give notice to the public under Subsection (1) of 
the Section (7) of the Land Acquisition Act, the land 
described below where the new Provincial Council 
building is situated is required by the government and the 
Government intends to acquire the said land. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired : 
 
The land of 0.2127 Hectares in extent stated as block 
No. 1 and 2, in the final village plan of No. B/644, 
prepared by the Senior Superintendant of Survey, situated 
in Kendelagama village of 78 B Badulla East Grama 
Niladhari Division coming under Rilpola Korale in 
Badulla Divisional Secretary Division of Badulla District 
in the Province of Uva. 
 
Details of the Land: 
 
Plan Number: B/644; Name of the Land: Biththara 
Amuna; Present land use: Open wasteland, home garden, 
10 permanent Building and a water tank; Name of the 
Claimant of the land: State (Block Number 1) Badulla 
Municipal Council (Block Number 2); 
 
Boundaries of the Land: 
 
01. Block Number 01 
 
North: Block Number 01 of preliminary plan 2649; 
East: Block Number 01 of preliminary plan 2649; South: 
Bailey Road claimed by Veeraiya Wardharajan and Block 
Number 02; West: Block Number 02 Biththara Amuna 
claimed by State and Block Number 01 of preliminary 
plan 2649. 
 
02. Block Number 02  
 
North: Biththara Amuna claimed by state, Block 
Number 01, Block Number 01 of preliminary plan 2649, 
Bailey Road claimed by Veeraiya Wardharajan; East: 
Block Number 01, Block Number 01 of preliminary plan 
2649, Bailey Road claimed by Veeraiya Wardharajan and 
R. H. Gunawardhana Mawatha; South: R. H. 
Gunawardhana Mawatha, Biththara Amuna claimed by 
State; West: Biththara Amuna claimed by State. 
 
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other 
connection to aforesaid land, should appear personally or 
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at 
Divisional Secretariat, Badulla on 19th of December, 
2013 at 2.30 p.m. In addition, hereby requested to notify 
to me in writing with two copies of the nature of their 
interests in the land the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of computation 
of such amount before 12th of December, 2013. 
 
 
 G AMINI MAHINDAPALA JOPHEUS 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Badulla. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Badulla.
