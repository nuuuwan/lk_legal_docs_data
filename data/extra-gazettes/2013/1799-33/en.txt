THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
The land called Hapugahapathana in extent of 1.7185 Hectares
stated in Lot Nos. 686, 736 and 737 depicted in the Preliminary Plan
No. F.V .P. 80, Supplement No. 54 and F.V .P. 37, Supplement No. 46
dated 13.02.2013, prepared by the District Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Rambukpotha, 80-H Glen Alphin Grama
Niladhari Division, coming under Rilpola Korale in Badulla Divisional
Secretary's Division of Badulla District:
Lot No. 686
North: Lot No. 33 and F.V .P. 37 (Rambukpotha); East: F.V .P. 37
(Rambukpotha); South: F.V .P. 37 (Rambukpotha) and Lot No. 3;
West: Lot No.  33.
Lot No. 736
North: F.V .P. 80  (Hanwella) Lot Nos. 11 and 737; East: Lot Nos.
11 and 737; South: Lot Nos. 737 and 11;  West: Lot No.  11 and
F.V .P. 80 (Hanwella).
Lot No. 737
North: Lot Nos. 736 and 11 ; East: Lot No. 11; South: Lot Nos.
11 and 736;  West: Lot No.  736
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other connection
to aforesaid land should appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat, Badulla on
8th of March, 2013 at 10.00 a.m.
In addition, hereby requested to notify to me before 5th of March
2013 in writing with two copies of the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and the
details of computation of such amount.
Gamini Mahindapala Jopheus,
