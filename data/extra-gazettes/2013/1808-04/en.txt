FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
THE Order under Section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as amended by Acts, No. 13 of 1966,
No. 56 of 1979,  No. 13 of 1982,   No. 84 of 1988,  No. 23 of 1995 and   No. 65 of 2009.
Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts. No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of  1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Susil
Premajayantha, Minister of Environment and Renewable Energy do by this order release the part of 12900 hectares Padaviya
Reserved Forest bounded by limits set forth in the Schedule given below which was declared, as a Reserved Forest by the
Exraordinary Gazette No. 1793/21 dated 18th January 2013 form its Reserved status from 25th day of May 2013.
S
USIL PREMAJAYANTHA,
Minister of Environment
and Renewable Energy.
Ministry of Environment
and Renewable Energy,
Battaramulla,
26th day of April 2013.
