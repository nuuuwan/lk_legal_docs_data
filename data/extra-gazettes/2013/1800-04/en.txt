1A
THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT , No. 02 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and  Minister-in-charge of the subject
of Defence and Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for
the  Maharagama Town Development Project do by this Order, declare that the said land is required for the aforesaid purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
 Presidential Secretariat,
 Colombo 01,
26th February, 2013.
SCHEDULE
The  land  shown  in Plan No. 3696 dated 06.05.1990 prepared by Mr. C. H. G. Fernando, Registered Licensed Surveyor
containing in extent of 0A - 1R - 08.10P situated at village of Maharagama in the Grama Niladari Division of Maharagama West
within the limits of Maharagama Divisional Secretariat in the District of Colombo Western Province.
Boundaries of the said land  is  as follows:
North : Railway reservation ;
East : Premises bearing Asst. No. 161, Old Road ;
South : Old Road, Maharagama ;
West : Temple Road.
