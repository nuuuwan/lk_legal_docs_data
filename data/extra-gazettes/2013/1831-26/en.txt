EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PATHADUMBARA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER  SECTION 65A(2)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meeting
of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognised political party  to which D. R. B. Rathnayake
the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible
under the ordiance for Election as a member of that Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall be
declared  elected as  Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Arachchi Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Sidney Rathnayake being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Pathadumbara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby  declare
Mr. Arachchi Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Sidney Rathnayake, elected as  Member of   the Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Pathadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
30th  September, 2013.
