EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE  MEDAWACHCHIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of  Chairman in Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mr. Hettiarachchige Kumudu Chandra Mendis,  the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of  that Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who
shall be declared  elected as  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision  to nominate
Mr. K. N. Peris to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of  Medawachchiya Pradeshiya
Sabha , acting in  terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. K. N. Peris elected as  Chairman  of   the  Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Anuradhapura,
30th December  2013.
