THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in extent about 0.0253 Hectare depicted as
Lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No. H/CGR/BLA/2012/107 dated
28.03.2012 prepared by Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Dedduwawela Gramaniladari Division, of Dedduwawela - East in
Beliatta Divisional Secretary’s Division in Hambantota District:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Meegahakumbura – Extent: 0.0253 Hectare
North: Canal; East and South: Meegahakumbura; West: Road and
Canal.
