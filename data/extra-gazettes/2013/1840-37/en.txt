I hereby give public notice, under sub-Section (01 of 
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land for 
a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired :—  
 
The land depicted in the surveyor General’s 
Preliminary Plan No. 9381 dated 04.11.2013 as Lot Nos. 
1-04 having an extent of 8.6845 Hectare, situated in the 
village of Makumbura Minor Division of Pallepattuwa 
Maharagama Divisional Secretary’s Division, Colombo 
District in the Western Province. 
 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; Description: 
Abandoned paddy field; Ownership: State; Claimants: 
Makumburage Chandradasa Perera, Makumburage 
Wimalasiri Perera, Makumburage Nihal Perera, Gamage 
Sarath Perera, Chithra Kusumalatha Perera and Indra 
Irangani Suraweera; Extent: 5.2627 Hectares.  
 
Lot No. 02  
Name of Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; Description: 
Abandoned paddy field; Ownership: Not known; 
Claimants: Not known; Extent: 0.0089 Hectare. 
Lot No. 03  
Name of Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; Description: 
Paddy field; Ownership: Not known; Claimants: Not 
known; Extent: 0.0206 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 04  
Name of Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; Description: 
Paddy field with permanent building; Ownership: State; 
Claimants: Makumburage Chandradasa Perera, 
Makumburage Wimalasiri Perera, Makumburage Nihal 
Perera, Gamage Sarath Perera, Chithra Kusumalatha 
Perera and Indra Irangani Suraweera; Extent: 3.3923 
Hectares.  
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby 
required to appear personally or through their agents, 
dully authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Maharagama on the 10th January, 2014 at 
9.00 a.m. and all hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before 31st December, 2013 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation, and the details of 
the compensation of such amount. 
 
All Deeds and relevant documents is support of claim 
should be provided at the inquiry. 
 
 
 B UDDHI THARANGA KARUNASENA, 
 Divisional Secretary/Land, 
 Acquiring Officer - Maharagama. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,
