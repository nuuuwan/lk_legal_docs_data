THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land in total extent of about 2578.4475 Hectares
(6317A. 01 R. 15 P.) depicted as Lot Nos. 01 to  72 in Advance
Tracing No. Ya/TLL/2013/111 of 24th April 2013 prepared by the
Surveyor-General  situated in the Villages of  Kankasanthurai West,
Kankasanthurai Central, Wimankamam South ,Theiyadi South, Palali
South, Ottapulam and Walali in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Walikamam North, Walikamam East in Jaffna District and bounded
as follows:
North: Indian Ocean; East and South: Earth Bund;West: Land of
the Ports Authority land claimed by Cement Corporation and Earth
Bund.
