Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the charges for the use of aerodrome at the Mattala Rajapaksa International
Airport and come into effect on the date of issue.
2. By these Regulations the Regulations published by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1801/38 dated 15th
March 2013 shall stand cancelled.
3. Unless otherwise exempted by Director General of Civil Aviation in writing.
Every aircraft landing at the Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport shall pay the flowing Landing and Parking
Charges to the Statutory Service provider.
1. Landing Charges Four (4) United States Dollars for every one thousand kilo grammes
or part thereof the maximum take-off weight of the aircraft
2. Parking Charges Ten (10) per centum of the applicable Landing Charge for each
period of six (6) hours or part thereof in excess of the first three (3)
hours of parking
4. Notwithstanding the Landing and Parking Charges specified in paragraph “3” above, the following limited catego-
ries of operators will be afforded with promotional discount rates stated below with regard to the applicable
Landing and Parking Charges with immediate effect.
