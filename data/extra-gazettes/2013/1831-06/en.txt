NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification  made  by  the  Governor North Western Province
Tissa Reginold Balalle being the Governor of the North Western Province in terms of power vested in me under Sub - section
(4) and (5) of Section 154 (f) of Amendment Act, No. 13 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
do hereby proclaim that New Mini Cabinet of the North Western Province was sworn in/affirmed before me at Colombo on
this  03rd October, 2013 on the following posts in terms of Section 165 and Section 157 (a) 7 of the Constitution of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1. Hon. Minister J.P.Dayasiri  Padmakumara Jayasekara - Chief Minister of Finance and Planning , Law and Peace,
Local Government and Provincial Administration, Human
Resource, Education and Cultural Affairs, Land,
Environmental, Tourism, Investment Coordination, Co-
operative Development and Food supply and Distribution of
the North Western Province ;
2. Hon. Minister H. M. D. B. Herath - Minister of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Sports, and Youth
Affairs of the North Western Province ;
3. Hon. Minister R. P. D. Gunadasa Dehigama - Minister of Road Development, Transports, Electricty and
Housing and Construction of the North Western Province ;
4. Hon. Minister K. Sanath Nishantha Perera - Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries, Animal Products and
Development, Minor Irrigation and Agrarian Development of
the North Western Province ;
5. Hon. Minister K. R. M. Sanadhiya Samantha  - Minister of Social Welfare, Probationary and Childcare,
Women’s Affairs, Rural Industries Development and Village
Development of the North Western Province ;
Proclaimed at Kurunegala on this Fourth day in October of Two Thousand Thirteen.
T
ISSA R. BALALLE,
The Governor of the North Western Province.
