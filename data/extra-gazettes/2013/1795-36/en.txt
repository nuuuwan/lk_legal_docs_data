THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
My Reference No. 4/3/9/2011/WS/141.
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Land and Land  Development  hereby declare, under Sub section (1) of  Section
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land called ''Gal Ange hena alias Kande Watta alias Pamunuwa Watta" in total extent of about
0.0024 Hectares, shown in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/YN/2012/381p, dated 08.05.2012, surveyed and prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the village of Gannoruwa West in Ganga Palatha Korale Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Yatinuwara in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows.
1. Lot No. ''A" - Gal Ange hena alias Kande Watta alias Pamunuwa Watta  – Extent  about 0.0024 Hectares.
North : Road ;
East : Road and Gal Ange hena  alias  Kande Watta  alias Pamunuwa Watta claimed by G. A. Somarathne ;
South : Gal Ange hena alias Kande Watta claimed by G. A. Somarathne and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) ;
West : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha)
