EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
HARISPATTUWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Harispattuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Rangbandi Katugahapihillege Wimalarathne  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a
member of that Harispattuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Dambaladeniyage Asanka
Priyanath Karunarathne being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka,  Returning Officer of  Harispattuwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Dambaladeniyage Asanka Priyanath Karunarathne  elected as  a member of   the  Harispattuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha.
 S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Harispattuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
02nd  May, 2013.
