TRUST   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS  the Trustees of the “Redemptories Fathers” namely -
1. Rev. Fr. Francis Neil Dias Karunarathne,
2. Rev. Fr. Ambagahage Sanath Kumara Fernando,
3. Rev. Fr. Gampolawaduge Anton Shiran Fonseka,
4. Rev. Fr. Waranakulasooriya Rufus Ivan Fernando,
5. Rev. Bro. Patrick Joseph Emanuel Perera,
6. Rev. Fr. Philip Benetius Anthony Malaviaratchi,
7. Rev. Fr. Shanti Gerard Racstan Abeyasingha,
8. Rev. Fr. Desmond Vimal Anthony Tirimanna,
9. Rev. Fr. Amarasinghe Arachchige Christopher Rohan Perera,
10. Rev. Fr. Podipili Arachchige Chandana Sanjeeva Kumar Perera,
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trust Ordinance (Chapter 87)
Now therefore, by virtue of powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of Justice, do
by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that -
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of Trust bearing No. 2489 of October 28, 2011 and attested by Segu
Fareed Mohamed Zavahir Notary Public of Kandy/Matale shall be a body corporate with perpetual succes-
sion and common seal under the name and style of “Redemptorist Fathers” ;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust ; and
