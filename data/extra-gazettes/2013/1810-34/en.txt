UVA    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made   by  the  Hon. Governor   of   Uva  Provincial   Council
I,  hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province :
01. Mr. Gundasa Samarasinghe,  Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government for Uva Province, from the date 22.03.2013 to 03.04.2013, till the
return of Mr. M. P. N. M. Wickramasinghe, to the Island, who is the Provincial Commissioner of Local Government
for Uva Province.
02. Mr. Gunadasa Samarasinghe, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to the
Ministry of Finance, Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land,
Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction of the Uva Provincial
Council, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 01.03.2013 to 05.03.2013, till the return of Mr. L. L. Anil
Wijesiri, to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance, Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local
Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure
Development and Construction of the Uva Provincial Council.
03. Dr. Mr. M. D. G . L. Basil Karunarathne, retired officer of the Sri Lanka Ayurvedic Medical Service, Re-appointed
on contract basis as Medical Officer of the Bibila Medagama District Ayurvedic Hospital, from the date 27.02.2013
till the appointment of a Permanent Officer or up to 26.02.2014.
04. Mr. G . K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to the Ministry
of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation, Childcare and Women's Affairs of the Uva Provincial Council, in
addition to his permanent post, from the date 13.03.2013 to 17.03.2013, till the return of Mrs. G. A. M. S. P.
Ambanwela to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation,
Childcare and Women's Affairs of the Uva Provincial Council.
05. Dr. Mr. Sunil Udunuwara, to perform the duties of the Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala, in
addition to his permanent post, from the date 20.03.2013 to 23.03.2013, till the return of Dr. Mr. P. D. K. Adhikari,
to the Island who is the Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala.

of  Finance Management for Uva Province.
