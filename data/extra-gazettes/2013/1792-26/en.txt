No. 1792/26 – THURSDAY, JANUARY 10, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02) Allotments of  land called Dahanwatta as shown as Lot
Nos. 1 and 2 in total extent of about 0.0077 Hectare, depicted in
Advance Tracing No.  MAHA/TMP/ 98/959 and dated 17.03.1999.
Prepared by the Survey Superintendent in Division of Kandy, situated
in Marawanagoda Village, in  the Minor Division of Medasiya Pattuwa
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Thumpane in Kandy  District
and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01 - Dahanwatta - Extent : 0.0069 Hectare
North: Highway; East: Dahanwatta claimed by Thissa Subasinghe;
South : Dahanwatta claimed by Thissa Subasinghe & Dahanwatta
claimed by E. G . Premawathi;West: Highway.
02. Lot No. 02 - Dahanwatta - Extent : 0.0008 Hectare
North: Lot No. 01; East: Dahanwatta claimed by E. G . Premawathi;
South and West: Highway.
