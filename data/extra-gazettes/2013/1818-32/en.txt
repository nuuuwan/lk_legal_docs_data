THE  NATIONAL  HOUSING  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  ACT  No.  17  OF 1979
(Certificate  under Section 6)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by  Section 6 of the National Housing Development Authority Act, No. 17 of 1979 I, Wimal
Weerawansa  Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing & Common Amenities do, hereby certify that the land
described in the schedule hereto should be acquired by the Government for the purpose of being made available for carrying
out of any housing objects within the meaning of Section 4 of the Act.
WIMAL WEERAW ANSA,
Minister of Construction, Engineering Services,
Housing & Common Amenities.
Colombo,
27th June, 2013.
