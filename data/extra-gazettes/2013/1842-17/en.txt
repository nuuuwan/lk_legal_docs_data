EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
My Reference No: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/01(LA/3529. 
Land Min. Ref. No.  4/05/2010/E/291. 
 
I, hereby notify  sub-section  7 of  the Land Acquisition 
Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendments) 
Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land/servitude which is required for 
a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Allotment of the land situated at Kotapola village in 
the Grama Niladhari's Division Kotapola-North (242) in 
the Divisional Secretariat, Kotapola in the District of 
Matara in the Southern Province, depicted as lot No. 2294 
of Supplementary Sheet No. 87 of Sheet No. 115 in the 
FVP 45 made by Survey or General, containing in extent 
about 0.8094 Hectare and boundaries of the land given 
below. 
 
Person who claiming over-Jagath Chnandana 
Abeygunawardena resided at Kurunduwatta of Kotapola. 
 
Lot No. 2294 containing in extent about 0.8094 
Hectare. 
 
On the North by- Lot No. 515 and 1761; On the East 
by: Not No. 1766; On the South by: Dola which was not 
applied lot Number and Lot No. 515; On the West by: 
Dola which was not applied Lot Number and Lot No. 515 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at   the 
Divisional Secretariat Kotapola on 11th day of February  
2014 at 10.00 o' clock, and are hereby requested to notify 
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 4th day of 
February 2014  the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.  
 
  G ANESHA AMARASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kotapola. 
  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kotapola 
24th December, 2013.
