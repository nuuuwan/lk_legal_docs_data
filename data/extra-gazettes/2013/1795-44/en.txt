Land Acquisition Notices
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02) allotments of  land in extent of 0.3673 Hectare , indicated
in the Progression Tracing bearing number B/BDL/2008/404 dated
24th May, 2011 prepared by the Surveyor-General, which is situated
in the Village of Telbedda coming under the 81B Telbedda Grama
Niladhari Division of Badulla Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Badulla District in Uva Province .
01. Lot ‘A’ – Telbedda Estate  – Extent: About 0.2851 Hectare
North: Road;  East: Telbedda Estate and Lot B; South: Lot B and
Road; West: Road.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Telbedda Estate  – Extent: About 0.0822 Hectare
North: Lot A and Telbedda Estate;  East: Telbedda Estate and
Road; South: Road; West: Road and Lot A.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
