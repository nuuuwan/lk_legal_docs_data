No. 1795/19 – TUESDAY,  JANUARY 29, 2013
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460 )
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/14.
Land  Ministry's No.: 03/J/87/IM/47.
I,  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Allotments of the land situated at the Village of Kotapola in the
Minor Division  of Morawak Korale in the Divisional Secretariat of
Kotapola in the District of Matara depicted in Supplementary Sheet
No. 74 of the Sheet No. 102 (F.V.P. 45) prepared by the Surveyor-
General and containing in extent about 3.2666 Hectares and boundaries
of that lands given below:
01. Lot No. 2178 – Hangaran kanda Warallawatta  alias
Yajlandi Watta – Extent: About 2.3053 Hectares
North: Lot No. 595; East: Lot Nos. 595 and 596; South: Lot No.
596 and Road; West: Road and Lot Nos. 596 and 595.
02. Lot No. 2179 Koodaluhena Deniya – Extent: About 0.9035
Hectare
North: Main Road and  Lot No. 597; East:  Lot No. 597 and Road;
South: Road and Lot Nos. 597 and 2180; West:  Lot Nos. 2180, 597
and Road.
03. Lot No. 2180 – Andaramullawatta, Koodaluhenadeniya,
Denipillehena – Extent: About 0.0578 Hectare
North: Lot No. 2179 and Road; East:  Lot No. 597 and Road;
South: Lot No. 597; West:  Lot Nos. 597 and 2179.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me at
Divisional Secretariat Kotapola on the 22nd day of April, 2013 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before 16th day of April, 2013 the nature of their
interests in the lands, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
D. D. K. Wickrama Arachchi,
Divisional Secretary,
Kotapola.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Kotapola.
24th January, 2013.
