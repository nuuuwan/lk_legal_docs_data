No. 1839/10 –  TUESDAY, DECEMBER 03, 2013 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT NO. 28 
OF 1964  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No: Medu/3/1/2/71/21. 
 
Lands & Land Development Ministry Reference No.: 
13/96/FR/499. 
 
I hereby give notice under sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964, (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Public Purpose: Acquisition of land for the 
conservation of Dumbara mountain range. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
An Allotment of land called Galbokka Galapita Watta 
in total extent of 0.6914 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in 
preliminary plan No. Maha 5531, situated in the village 
called Mangoda in the Grama Niladhari Division 
Mangoda of Udasiyapaththuwa minor Division in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Madadumbara in 
Kandy District of the Central province and more fully 
described as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
   
Lot Number: 01 
 
Name of the land: Galbokka Galapita Watta; 
Claimant: H. M. D. G. Muthumenika, Mangoda, 
Madamahanuwara; Plan Number: Maha 5531; Extent: 
0.6914 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Medadumbara 
Divisional Secretariat on the 29th January, 2014 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before 22nd January, 2014 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 J. M. J. K. JAYASUNDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Medadumbara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Medadumbara, 
24th August, 2013
