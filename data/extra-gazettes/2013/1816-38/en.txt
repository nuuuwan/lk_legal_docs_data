WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
NOTIFICATION
Wages   Board   for  the    Hotel   and   Catering    Trade
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of
the Wages Board for the Hotel and Catering Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under
Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27  of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 16th
February 2013.
H
ERATH YAPA,
Actg. Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
05th June 2013.
Nominated Members :
01. Mr. Nadaraja Manmadarajan
02. Mr. Chandrasena Nanayakkara
03. Mr. A. Dissanayake
Employers' Representatives :
01. Mr. R. L. P. Peiris
02. Mr. Jayantissa Kehelpannala
03. Mr. V . Ellapola
04. Mr. H. W. Bandula Perera
05. Ms. Fathima Ishara Naufal
06. Mr. Wasula Wejegunawardhana
07. Mr. L. H. Jayasinghe
