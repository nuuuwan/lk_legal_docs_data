PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Provincial  Councils  Notifications
1A
UV A  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
THE notification made under Section 2(2), subsection 3(a) and 3(a) iii of Charter No. 02 of 2011 on power of supervision and
administration of Local Government Institutions of Uva Provincial Council.
I, Shasheendra Rajapaksa, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education,
Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure Development
and Construction, under the powers vested in me, have appointed Mr. Kotuwila Kamkanamalage Gamini Franzis Perera, the
retired high court judge of investigate and report on the matters shown under subsection 2(1) (c) and 2(1) (e) of Charter
No. 02 of 2011 on the power of supervision and administration of the Local Government Institutions of Uva Provincial
Council.
I, Shasheendra Rajapaksa, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education,
Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure Development
and Construction, under the powers vested in me, and under the powers vested in me under Section 3(a) (iii) of Charter
No. 02 of 2011 on power of supervision and administration of the Local Government Institutions of Uva Provincial Council,
do hereby suspend the functions of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District, with immediate effect.
As the functions of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District have been suspended under subsection 3(a) (iii)
of 2011 of Charter No. 02 of 2011 on power of supervision and administration of the Local Government Institutions of Uva
Province, I do hereby order Mr. Maley Pathiranage Nishantha Mohan Wickramasinghe, the Commissioner of Local Government
of Uva Province to execute the powers and carry out the tasks thereof.
S
HASHEENDRA  RAJAPAKSA,
The Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and
Planning, Law and Order, Education, Local Government,
Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and
Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction.
