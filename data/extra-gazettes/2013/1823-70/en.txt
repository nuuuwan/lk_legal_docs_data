Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (1)(a) of Article
44 of the Consitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1681/3  of November 22, 2010 and amended from time to time is further amended with effect from August 16, 2013 in the
manner set out in the Schedule below.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
16th August, 2013.
