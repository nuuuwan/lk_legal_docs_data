EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this Order declare that -
(a) The Excise duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule hereto shall be payable at the rate
specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule.
(b) Wherever an ad valorem rate and a unit rate are specified in Column IV of Schedule hereto, the applicable rate
for the purpose of calculating the Excise duty payable shall be that rate the application of which results in a
higher amount being payable as Excise duty.
(c) The orders made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1251/25 of 30.08.2002 and No. 1783/19 of 08.11.2012, No. 1821/1 of
29.07.2013 are hereby rescinded.
(d) This order shall come into effect from 22.11.2013.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
 Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01
21st November 2013.
