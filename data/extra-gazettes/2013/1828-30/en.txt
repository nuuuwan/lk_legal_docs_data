THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
This acquisition is made for the distribution of lands among persons
selected by land Kachcheries under Village widening programme as
per the circular No. 2008/04.

extent of 0.5015 Hectare, depicted as Lot 'A' in the Advance Tracing
No. MR/DVN/2013/128 dated 28th March, 2013, prepared  by the

Naotunna, in Minor Division of Wellabada Pattu West in Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara, Matara District, Southern
Province:

– Extent: 0.5015 Hectare
North: Godellawatte claimed by Tissa Dissanayaka and Welipatha
alias Mahagedarawatte claimed by Balage Sirisena; East: Welipatha
alias Mahagedarawatte claimed by Balage Sirisena, Wandigehena
claimed by Tissa Dissanayaka and Pitakongaha Koratuwa claimed
by Manikkuwadurage Chandradasa; South: Wandigehena claimed by
