THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 
 
 
 
 Reference No.: ව/18/67/2003(2)-වත්/අත්/221. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose.  
Description of the land to be acquired :  
An allotments of land in extent about 0.0059 Hectares, 
shown in Preliminary Plan No. GAM. 3904 prepared by 
Survey Superintendent of  Gampaha, situated at 
Thelangapatha village in Grama Niladhari Division of 
175-Thelangapatha, Minor Division of Ragam Pattuwa in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wattala in 
Gampaha District in the Western Province. 
 
 
1. Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Kahatagahawatta; Description: 
Garden, part of a permanent building; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant : R. D. S. D. Amarasooriya,                         
R. D. D. N. Amarasooriya, R. D. D. I. Amarasooriya, No. 
172, Thelangapatha Road, Wattala; Extent: 0.0059 
Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Wattala on the 20th day of December, 2013 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before th e 17th December, 2013, the 
nature of their interests in the land,  the particulars of 
their claims for compensatio n for the acquisition of the 
said land the amount of compensation the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 C HANDIMA DISSANAYAKE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Wattala. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Wattala.
