CERTAIN persons have been internally displaced as a result of any actions of a terrorist militant or other group during the
recent past :
02. In terms of Section 2 of the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 27 of 2013 any person,
2.1 who is or had been an internally displaced,
2.2 whose name appeared in the register of electors for any electoral district in the Northern Province or Eastern
Province for any year, until the end of the year 2009 ; and,
2.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009,
shall on proof of the matters specified in above paragraphs and on production of a certificate issued by the
Grama Niladhari of the area in which the applicant is presently residing, to the effect that the applicant is or the
applicant had been an internally displaced person, be entitled to apply to the registering officer of the
electoral district within which the applicant was permanently resident prior to 18th May 2009 to be registered
in the register of electors of such electoral district.
03. In terms of the Section 3 of the Special Provisions Act, children of Sri Lankan citizen mentioned in paragraph 2
above,
3.1 who had not attained the age of eighteen years on the date on which such citizens became an internally
displaced and have attained the age of eighteen years or more on the date on which the revision of the
operative register commenced, at the time in which the application is made,
3.2 who were born after such citizen became an internally displaced person and have attained the age of eighteen
years or more on the date on which the revision commenced in respect of the register in operation, at the time
in which the application is made,
3.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009,

04. It is hereby notified that in terms of the powers vested in me under 2(1) of the Registration of Electors (Special
