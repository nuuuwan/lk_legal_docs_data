NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a)
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the  Kundasale Pradeshiya   Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Muthupatin  Gedara Sunimal
Pushpakumara of the  Kundasale Pradeshiya   Sabha  has vacated his office of the   member of that  Pradeshiya   Sabha as he
has ceased to be a member of  the  People's Liberation Front.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
 Kundasale Pradeshiya   Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
25th March, 2013.
