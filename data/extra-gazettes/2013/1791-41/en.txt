THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.

Division of Salpitikorale of Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat Division
of Colombo District in Western Province and indicated as lot No.
''A" mentioned in progressive tracing No. CO/KSB/12/07 dated
09.03.2012 prepared by surveyor General of Colombo District.
01. Lot A - Karadiyana Thumbowila alias Kadole Kumbura

Kumbura; East: ownership unknown Karadiyana Kumbura alias
