EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  KESBEWA   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  chairman in Kesbewa  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Lakshman Perera Polwaththage  the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the  member of that  Kesbewa  Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Ranweera Don Sumana Viviyan to fill such  vacancy :
Now therefore I, Niruka Dananga Laththuwahandi,  Returning Officer of   Kesbewa  Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ranweera Don Sumana
Viviyan  elected as a Chairman of the  Kesbewa  Urban Council.
N
IRUKA DANANGA LATHUWAHANDI,
Returning Officer,
Kesbewa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
23rd  December, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  PELIYAGODA    URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  chairman in Peliyagoda  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Ananda Nihal Perera Duwage   the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the  member of that  in Peliyagoda  Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Amila Nishantha Kumarasinghe  to fill such  vacancy :
Now therefore I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne,  Returning Officer of   Peliyagoda  Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Amila Nishantha
Kumarasinghe   elected as a Chairman of the Peliyagoda  Urban Council.
 P. A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Peliyagoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
24th   December, 2013.
