SRI  LANKA  ELECTRICITY  ACT,  No. 20 OF 2009
Order under item 3 of Schedule I
BY  virtue  of the powers vested in me by item 3 of Schedule I of Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009, I, Patalie Champika
Ranawaka, Minister of Power and Energy, upon the recommendation of the Public Utilities Commission established under
the Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka Act, No. 35 of 2002 do by this Order :-
(a) approve that the land specified in the Schedule to this Order is required by the Ceylon Electricity Board
established under the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No 17 of 1969, for a public purpose ; and
(b) may be acquired under the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and transferred to the Ceylon Electricity
Board.
P
A TALIE CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Power and Energy.
Colombo,
10th January, 2013.
