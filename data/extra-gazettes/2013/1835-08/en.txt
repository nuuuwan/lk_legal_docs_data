PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
W ARIYAPOLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of the Wariyapola  Pradeshiya Sabha ,  do
hereby declare under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Dasanayaka Mudiyanselage
Gamini  Dasanayake of the Wariyapola  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as
he  has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
T.A. C. N. TALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Wariyapola  Pradeshiya Sabha .
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
26th October, 2013.
