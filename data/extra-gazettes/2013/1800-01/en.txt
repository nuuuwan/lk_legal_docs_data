THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No. 41  OF  1978  OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence & Urban Development do by this order vest with the Urban Development Authority, with
effect from today the land described in the schedule hereto belongs to the Kalutara Urban Council.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence & Urban Development.
Ministry of Defence & Urban Development,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
26th February 2013.
SCHEDULE
The land called Rathmahara containing in extent of 1.5061 Hectares (03A. 02R. 35.48P.) depicted as Lot Nos. E, G , K
and M in Tracing No. Ka/KLT/10/414 dated 01.12.2010 made by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys of Kalutara District, in
the village of Desastra Kalutara, in Divisional Secretariat Division of Kalutara within the Urban Council limits of Kalutara, in
the District of Kalutara, Western Province.
The details and boundaries of the land are as follows :
1. Lot No. : E ;
Land Name : Rathmahara ;
Extent : 0.2232 Hec. (0A., 2R., 08.25P.)
North : Lot No. D and Arbroo Road ;
East : Arbroo Road ;
South : Lot No. F and Sea ;
West : Sea and Lot No. D of this Tracing.
