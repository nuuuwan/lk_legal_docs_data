NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7TH OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
As Amended by the land Acquisition (Amendment) 
Act No. 28 of 1964  
Reference No: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3930. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section               
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The land called Mannehewatta depicted as lot 01, 02 
in the plan No. MR/2569 situated in the village of Nupe 
Kadeweediya in the Kadeweediya-East Grama Niladari 
Division of the Divisional Secretary at Division of 
Matara, Matara District, Southern province, has land 
acquired for the expansion of Anagarika Dharmapala 
Mawatha. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Mannehegewatta; Current land use: 
Pavement; Claimant: S. F. Waseena, No. 130A, 
Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Matara; Extent: 0.0002 
Hectare.   
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of Land: Mannehegewatta; Current land use: 
Pavement; Claimant: M. A. M. Kasali, Anagarika  
Dharmapala Mawatha, Matara; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 
28th December, 2013 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 15th December, 2013 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
 Y. W ICKRAMASIRI, 
 Acting Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Matara, 
27th November, 2013.
