APPROV AL  OF  THE  DEVELOPMENT  PLAN  FOR  THE  ATTANAGALLA  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT AREA
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development do hereby approve the Development Plan for the Urban
Development Area of Attanagalla, having considered the recommendations made by the Board of Management of the Urban
Development Authority on 06th July 2011 by the virtue of the powers vested with me under Section 8F of the Urban
Development Authority Amendment Act, No. 4 of 1982.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development
