ARTICLE  154 B  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph (8) of Article 154 B of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the Central Province do hereby dissolve the Central Provincial
Council with effect from midnight July 05, 2013.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUWA,
Governor of the Central Province.
Office of the Governor of the Central Province,
05th July, 2013.
