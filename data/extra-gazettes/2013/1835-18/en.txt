Notice by the  Governor  of  the  Northern  Province

KNOW ye that  by virtue of powers vested by the article 154(b) : (10)(a) of the thirteenth amendment to the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Gammanpila Arachchige Chandrasiri, Governor of the Northern Province, do
hereby summon  to meet the Council and I will address at Second Session of the First Council at Assembly Hall at the Northern
Provincial Council Complex at Kaithady, Jaffna on Monday the 11th November, Two Thousand Thirteen at 09.30 a. m.
G . A. C
HANDRASIRI,
Governor of the Northern Province.
Given at Governor's Secretariat,
Northern Province,
