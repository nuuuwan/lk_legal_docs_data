THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No. 41 OF  1978  OF THE  NATIONAL  STATE   ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of  the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development do by this order, to vest with the Urban Development Authority with
effect from today the land described in the Schedule hereto belongs to the Kalutara Urban Council.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development.
Ministry of Defence and Urban Development,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
26th  February  2013.
SCHEDULE
The land called Calido Rest House, Sea beach road containing in extent of 0.14164 Hectares (0A., 1R., 16.00P.) shown in the Plan
No. 2535 dated 15.03.1998 made by Licensed Surveyor Jayasiri Kodikarage situated in the village of Desastra Kalutara, in
Divisional Secretariat Division of Kalutara within the Urban Council limits of Kalutara, in the District of Kalutara, Western
Province. The said land is bounded as follows :
North : Lot No. 1A of Tuduwewatta bearing Assessment No. 102, Sea Beach Road ;
East : Sea Beach Road ;
South : Sea Beach Road ;
West : Sea Beach Road.
