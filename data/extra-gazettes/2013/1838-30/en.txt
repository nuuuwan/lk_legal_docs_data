Appointments & C., by the President
No. 355/1 of  2013

IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under article 44(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to appoint Hon. Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, MP as Minister of
Special Projects, with effect from November 23, 2013.
