EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  WEERAKETIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Weeraketiya  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  People’s Liberation Front , the recognised political party  to which Wellahenage
Chandrapala  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Weeraketiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Palliya Gurugei Don
Lakshman Chandrarathna Abeywardana   being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Weeraketiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Palliya Gurugei Don Lakshman Chandrarathna Abeywardana  elected as  a member of   the Weeraketiya   Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R.  W
IJAY A  KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Weeraketiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
05th  August, 2013.
