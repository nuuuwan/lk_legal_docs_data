THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
needed  for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
The land called Mahabadullawaka Kumbura in extent of
0.3764 Hectare, depicted in Progression Tracing No. B/BDL/
2009/173 dated 19.08.2010, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Badulupitiya in Badulupitiya Grama
Niladhari Division in Rilpola Korale Minor Division in Badulla
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Badulla District in Uva Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mahabadullawaka Kumbura – Extent:
0.3606 Hectare
North: Streamlet and Badulu Oya; East: Mahabadullawaka
Kumbura Home garden claimed by Richard Jayawardhana; South:
Green Lane Drive and Cement Drain; West: Cement Drain and
Streamlet.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Mahabadullawaka Kumbura – Extent:
0.0158 Hectare
North and East: Cement Drain and Streamlet; South: Green
Lane Drive; West: Mahabadullawaka Kumbura Home garden claimed
by D. M. Jayantha Thilaka.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No.: LD/05/2009/E/30.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
