EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  PUTTALAM  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of    Vice  Chairman in Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of Vice Chairman  has been
appointed as Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Bamunu Arachchige Nimal Bamunu Arachchi  Vice Chairman   belonged, was required by me  under Section 65 A  (1) of  the
aforesaid Ordinance, to  decide upon the   member of  that  Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall
be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Poruthotage Sunil Fernando,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana,  Returning  Officer of   Puttalam  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting  in terms  of  Section  65 A (1) of  the Local Authorities  Elections  Ordinance  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.
Poruthotage Sunil Fernando, elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Puttalam   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
K.A. S
UMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
 Puttalam   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
15th August, 2013.

 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice Under Section 10A (1) (a)
THANAMALWILA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of the  Thanamalwila  Pradeshiya   Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10
A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. W. M. Karunadasa  of the T hanamalwila
Pradeshiya   Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya  Sabha as he  has ceased to be a member of the
People’s Liberation Front.
SURANGA AMBAGAHA TENNE,
Returning Officer,
 Thanamalwila  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
Elections Office,
Moneragala,
