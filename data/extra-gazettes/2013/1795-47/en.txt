No. 1795/47 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 01, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 3/2/6/4/1/25.
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4/3/12/2011/CEB/125.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for Reconstruction of the access road to adit
No. 04 which has been decayed by an earthslip, under Upper Kotmale
Hydro Power Project.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of 0.1337 Hectare,
shown in the Preliminary Plan No. PPNu 2826 and dated 03rd
January, 2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
village of Kumbaloluwa in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 470,
Niyangandara of Udapane Korale Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kotmale in Nuwara Eliya District.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Choisy Estate; Description: –; Claimant: Land
Reform Commission, Nuwara Eliya; Extent: 0.1219 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Choisy Estate; Description: –; Claimant: Land
Reform Commission, Nuwara Eliya; Extent: 0.0118 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Kotmale on the 07th day of March,
2013 at 1.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 28th day of February, 2013,
the nature of their interests in the lands, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
H. M. M. U. B. Herath,
Divisional Secretary,
Kotmale.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kotmale.
29th January, 2013.
