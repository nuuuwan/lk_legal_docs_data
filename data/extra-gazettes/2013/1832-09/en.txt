THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An  allotment of land called Godakumbura depicted  in Advance
Tracing No. R/GDK/2005/781/ of  22nd November, 2005 prepared
by Rathnapura Surveyor General in the Village of Godakawela, in
Godakawela Grama Niladhari Division in Medapattuwa Minor
Division in Godakawela, Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Rathnapura District. Allotment ‘A’ containing in extent of
0.502 Hectare and boundaries as follows:
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Godakumbura – Extent: 0.502 Hectare
North: Godakumbura, Godakumbura watta; East and South: Lot
B and Godakumbura, Iramedakella;  West: Road and Godakumbura.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
