No. 1834/42 – FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 01, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land described
below under the 1st paragraph is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes under
the 2nd paragraph from which land, that the servitude will be
acquired.
1. Description  of the Servitude .– Acquisition of servitude
over the land for the Access Road to Pothgoda Water Tank and
Laying of Pipes, of the Marassana Water Supply Scheme.
2. Description of the Land .– The allotment of land called
"Galapita Hena" in total extent of about 0.0054 Hectare, depicted in
the Advance Tracing No. Maha/PTH/2011/384P, dated 13.06.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Pothgoda
in Hewawissa Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Pathahewaheta in Kandy District, Central Province and
bounded as follows;
01. Lot No. 1 – Galapita Hena – Extent: About 0.0054 Hectare
North: Galapita Hena claimed by Soma Kulasekara; East: Galapita
Hena claimed by Soma Kulasekara and Galapita Hena, belongs to
National Water Supply and Drainage Board; South: Galapita Hena
claimed by J. S. M. Leelawathie;West: Galapita Hena claimed by
J. S. M. Leelawathie and Road (R.D.A.).
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
