NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORIDNANCE
Thamankaduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Indika Hathurusingha Returning Officer of the Thamankaduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section 10(A)(1)(a)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ituwala Kankanamlage Wijerathne of the Thamankaduwa Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his Office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the People’s Liberation
Front.
I
