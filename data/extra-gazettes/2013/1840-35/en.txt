PUTTALAM  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Declaring  as  Developed  Areas
IT is hereby notified that, I, R. M. D. Jayawardane, Assistant Commissioner of Local Government for Puttalam Administrative
District, by virtue of powers vested in me, have approved the following resolution adopted by Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha by
Resolution No. 7of the Council’s Monthly Meeting held on 30.10.2012, under and virtue of powers vested in the Council by
Sub-section (1) of the Section 134 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
R. M. D. JAYAWARDANE,
Divisional Asst. Commissioner of Local Government,
Puttalam Administrative District.
Office of the Asst. Commissioner of Local Government,
Puttalam District,
02nd December 2013.
RESOLUTION

Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha resolves that the areas described in the schedule below that come
under the limits of Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha be admitted as developed areas, and
Action be taken to obtain the approval of the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government for the Administrative
District of Puttalam for the resalution.
