No. 1829/35 – THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 26, 2013
My No.: IR/10/65/2010.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between –
Mr. K. C. De Silva, No. 27 A, D. W. J. Perera Mawatha, Uyankelle, Panadura, of the one part
     AND
Road Development Authority, "Sethsiripaya", Battaramulla of the other part
NOW THEREFORE  I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4(1)
of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of
1957, 62 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 [read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968], hereby
appoint  Mr. R. S. Ariyapperuma, No.  03, Araliya Mawatha, Sirimal Uyana, Ratmalana  to be the Arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute
to him for settlement by arbitration.
GAMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo.
20th  September, 2013.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether  the punishments given to Mr. K. C. De Silva who works as a Technical Officer of the Road Development Authority,
following a disciplinary inquiry held against him by the Road Development Authority are just, and if not so, to  what reliefs he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
