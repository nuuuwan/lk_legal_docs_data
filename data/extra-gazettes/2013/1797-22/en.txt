THE  NATIONAL  AUTHORITY  ON  TOBACCO  AND  ALCOHOL  ACT  No. 27 OF 2006
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Health under section 30 read with section 34 of the National Authority on Tobacco
and Alcohol Authority Act, No. 27 of 2006.
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
Minister of Health.
Colombo,
15th February  2013.
Regulations
The Tobacco Products (Labelling and Packaging) regulations, No. 01 of 2012, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1770/15 of August 8, 2012 are hereby further amended as follows :–
1. in regulation 5 thereof, by the substitution for the words on ‘‘shall be printed both sides of every cigarette
packet, package or carton containing cigarette and shall cover an area of not less than eighty per centum (80%) of the total
area of a packet, package or a carton.’’ of the words ‘‘shall be printed on the top surface area of both front and back sides of
every cigarette packet, package or carton containing cigarette and shall cover and area of not less than eighty per centum
(80%) of the top surface area of both front and back sides of every cigarette packet, package or a carton.’’;
2. in regulation 6 thereof, by the substitution for the words ‘‘contained in such packet, package, carton or label
including formaldehyde and other toxic contents if any.’’ of the words ‘‘contained in such packet, package, carton or label
including its nicotine and tar.’’;
3. in regulation 7 thereof, by the substitution for the words ‘‘year of production thereof. ’’ of the words ‘‘year of
production and the date of expiry thereof.’’;
4. by the adding of a new regulation after regulation 10, of the following regulation :–
‘‘11. Every importer of any tobacco product shall comply with these regulations by affixing stickers in accordance
with the requirements specified in these regulations within three months from the date of publication of Gazette. ’’
