THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for  the constuction of water tank  of Sakaran
Kotuwa Mount Tempal  Water Scheme of  Kandy South.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The land called ‘Mount Tempal Watta’ in total extent of about
0.0039 Hectare in Cadastral Map  No. 320052 (3)14 shown as Lot
No. 123 Surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor-General in Grama
Niladhari Division of Mount Tempal in Kandukara Ihala Korale in
Divisional Secretary's Division of Udapalatha in Kandy District,
Central Province.
01. Lot No. 123
Name of land: Mount Tempal Watta; Description: -; Claimant:
A. A. Podiralahami, Thusara Stores, Kadugannawa road, Gampola;
Extent:  0.0039 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby  required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at  Divisional Secretariat  Udapalatha on the 24th day of June, 2013
at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 17th day of June, 2013, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
B. M. Nimal Balasooriya,
Divisional Secretary,
Udapalatha-Gampola.
My No.: DS/UP/3/1/LAQ/ Sakaran. tank/40.
Your Ref. No.: 4/3/3/2010/WS/249.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Udapalatha-Gampola.
04th April, 2013.
