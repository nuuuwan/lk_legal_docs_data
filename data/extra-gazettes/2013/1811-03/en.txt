EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PELMADULLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings  of
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Bowaththe Indrarathana Thero the member belonged, was required by me, to nominate a person eligible of that Pelmadulla
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Senanayaka Isilige Sunil
Shantha Senanayaka being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera  Returning Officer of  Pelmadulla Pradeshiya
Sabha, Mr. Senanayaka Isilige Sunil Shantha Senanayaka elected as  a member of   the Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura.
15th May 2013.
