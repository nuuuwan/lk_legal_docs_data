CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Jagath Premalal Wijeweera,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 26th   August, 2013, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1824/01 of
19th  August, 2013 is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 01st  September,  2013.
J
AGATH P.  WIJEWEERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
23rd   August  2013.

UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
THE columns I and II of the Schedule of the English version of  the Notice published by the Ministry of Higher Education,
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1818/ 7 of 09.07.2013 under the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 - Order under section 25 A
was printed incorrectly as -
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Programmes Abbreviations to the Degree
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Maritime Sicience BSc (Hons) Maritime Sicience
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Marine Engineering BSc (Hons) Marine Engineering
Bachelor or Science (Honours) in Logistics  Transportation BSc (Hons) Logistics   Transportations
It should be corrected as-
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Programmes Abbreviations to the Degree
Bachelor of  Science (Honours) in Maritime Science BSc (Hons) Maritime Science
Bachelor of  Science (Honours) in Marine Engineering BSc (Hons) Marine Engineering
Bachelor of  Science (Honours) in Logistics  Transportation BSc (Hons) Logistics  Transportation
