Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  Under  Section 10A(1)(A)
BIYAGAMA   PRADESHIYA SABHA
I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senarathne, Returning Officer of the Biyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Amarasinghe Arachchillage Amal Udayaruwan of the
Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his Office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of
the United National Party.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
26th September, 2013.
