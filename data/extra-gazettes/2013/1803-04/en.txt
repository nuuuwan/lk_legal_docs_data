FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   DELFT   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
WHEREAS the office of  a Vice Chairman in  Delft Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Subramaniam Kobalasingam,  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me  to decide upon the member of the Delft
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as   Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30)
days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Sebasthi Pethuru Arulappu to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan ,  Returning  Officer  of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
Mr. Sebasthi Pethuru Arulappu,   elected as  Vice Chairman of   the  Delft Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
14th March, 2013.
