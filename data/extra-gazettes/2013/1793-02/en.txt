NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 8(b) of Section 154(b) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Karunaratna Divulgane, Governor of the North Central Province declare hereby that the first session
of the Sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Provincial came to an end on mid-night of November 28th, 2012, and
By virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 3(b) of Section 154(a) of the aforesaid Constitution, declare
hereby that the second session of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province shall meet at 9.15 a.m. on 17th
January, 2013, at the Conference Hall of the North Central Provincial Council.
K
ARUNARATNA DIVULGANE,
Governor,
North Central Province.
Governor's Office,
North Central Province,
11th January, 2013.
