THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3927.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.

The following mentioned lands depicted in the Preliminary Plan
No. MR/2544 as Lot Nos. 01, 02, 03, 04, 05 situated in the Village of
Wallamadama in Madda Watta South Grama Niladhari Division in
Matara Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matara District, Southern
Province has been proposed for acquired for the construction
of Faculty of Fisheries and Marine Sciences of the University of
Ruhuna.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Madapittaniya alias Borallahena Watta; Description:
Garden, 55 coconut trees 10-50 years old, 04 cadju trees, 04 mango
trees and mixed cultivation; Extent: 0.6302 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Madapittaniya alias Borallahena Watta; Description:
Garden, 40 coconut trees 10-50 years old, abandoned permanent
building, part of a toilet and a well; Extent: 0.5490 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of land: Pahala Pothuwil Kumbura; Description: Wetland
(Carr); Extent: 0.0864 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 04
Name of land: Pahala Pothuwil Kumbura; Description: Paddy
field; Extent: 0.1003 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 05
Name of land: Pahala Pothuwil Kumbura; Description: Garden,
mixed cultivation; Extent: 0.0119 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Matara on the 17th day of May, 2013 at
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of May, 2013, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
