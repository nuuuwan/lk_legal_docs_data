L.D.B. 21/1978 (xii).
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Subsection (1) of  Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Subsection (1) of Section 27   of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,  I, Dissanayake
Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake,   Minister of Higher Education, do by  this Order, on the recommendation
of  the University  Grants Commission, amend  the  Order establishing the  University of  Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which Order is
deemed, by virtue of   the  provisions of  Section  139 of  the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said
Act, (as last amended by Order published in   Gazette Extraordinary, No, 1606/ 26 of June 18, 2009) in the Schedule  thereto in
the item ‘‘Faculty of Architecture’’ appearing in Column I of such Schedule, by the addition at the end of its corresponding
entries in Column II thereof, of the following new item:-
‘‘ 4. Department of Integrated Design’’.
D
ISSANAY AKE  MUDIY ANSELAGE  SUMANAWEERA  BANDA  DISSANAY AKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
18th  July, 2013.
