EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  PUTTALAM  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the Party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Hettiarachchilage Nimal Ekanayaka Perera,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Hettiarachchilage Nimal
Ekanayaka Perera  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe, Returning Officer of  Puttalam Pradeshiya
Sabha , acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Hettiarachchilage Nimal Ekanayaka Perera elected as  a member  of   the  Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. A. S. P. JAYASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Puttalam Pradeshiya Sabha.
Puttalam,
29th November  2013.
