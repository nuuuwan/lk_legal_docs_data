WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute, No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and the Minster of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council do hereby appoint the retired Judge of Court of Appeal Hon. B. L. K. Wimalachandra
as Single Person Tribunal of Inquiry as per the powers entrusted to me by the Provisions of Section 2(2) of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council, to
inquire and submit a report within 03 months based on the following matters/allegations, whether the Chairman of the
Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha, Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera, while functioning in the said office has made the offences
stated under Section 2(1) of the aforementioned statute.
Matters/allegations to be inquired about ;
(a) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has acted contray to Clause 174 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act,  No. 15 of 1987 and has committed the offence/offences stated under Section/
Sections 2(1) a and/or 2(1) c of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute
No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by assigning the industry of “digging ditches in
the land named Walauwatte in the part that stretches from the place of commencement of the land up to the
main road” within the area of Authority of Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha and by causing the said task to be
accomplished by the person named Y . A. Arjuna Dharmapriya residing at No. 72, Walbolana, Pallewela
without calling tenders properly ;
(b) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has acted contray to Clause 173 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and has committed the offence/offences stated under Section/Sections
2(1) a and/or 2(1) c of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by assigning the industry of “digging ditches in the land
named Walauwatte in the part that stretches from the place of commencement of the land up to the main
road” within the area of Authority of Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha and by causing the said task to be
accomplished by the person named Y . A. Arjuna Dharmapriya residing at No. 72, Walbolana, Pallewela
without reaching into a proper contract ;

(c) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within

Walauwatte in the part that stretches from the place of commencement of the land up to the main road”

Pradeshiya Sabha without proper adoption by the Pradeshiya Sabha ;
(d) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has committed the offence/offences
stated under Section/Sections 2(1) a and/or 2(1)c  of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by assigning the
contract for leveling of soil and removal of waste from the land belonging to the Meerigama Pradeshiya
Sabha where the new library of Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha in proposed to be built to the person named
Y . A. Arjuna Dharmapriya residing at No. 72, Walbolana, Pallewala, without calling tenders properly.

the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has acted contray to Clause 173 of the

2(1)a and/or 2(1)c of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council having assigned and by causing accomplishment of the
contract for leveling of soild and removal of waste from the land  belonging to the  Meerigama Pradeshiya
Sabha where the new library of  Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha is proposed to built, to the person named Y . A.
Arjuna Dharmapriya residing at No. 72, Walbolana, Pallewela without reaching into a proper contract ;
(f) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has committed the offence stated under
Section 2(1) d  of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991
of the Western Province Provincial Council by making a payment of Rupees Three Hundred Thirty Five
Thousand Eight Hundred and Thirty (Rs. 335,830) or an amount equal to it from the General Fund  of the
Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha having assigned  and by causing accomplishment of the contract for leveling
soild and removal of waste from the land belonging to the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha where the new
library of Meerigama Pradeshiya Sahba is proposed to be built, to the person named  Y . A. Arjuna Dharmapriya
residing at No. 72, Walbolana, Pallewela without proper adoption by the Pradeshiya Sabha ;
(g) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has acted contrary to Caluse 174 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and has committed the offence/offences stated under Section/Sections
2(1) a and/or 2(1)c  of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by obtaining the service of a Motor Grader machine from
the institution of “Imashi Constructions” for maintenance of roads within the area of Authority of  Meerigama
Pradeshiya Sabha without calling tenders properly.
(h) Mr. Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera acting as the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha within
the time period from 04.04.2011 or a date adjacent to it up to present has acted contray to Clause 173 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and has committed the offence/offences stated under Section/Sections
2(1) a and/or 2(1) c of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of
1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by deploying a Motor Grader machine from the institution
of “Imashi Constructions” for maintenance of roads with in the area of Authority of Meerigama Pradeshiya
Sabha without reaching into a proper contract ;
