MINISTRY  OF  CIVIL  A VIATION
Civil  Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka
NOTICE BY THE DIRECTOR GENERAL OF CIVIL AVIATION  UNDER THE AIR NA VIGATION  REGULA TION 196
MEMBERS  of the public are hereby notified that this office has received on 18th February 2013 an application dated 15th
February 2013 from Richy Skylark (Pvt.) Ltd, at No. 409, Walawwa Road, Homagama, seeking an Air Operator Certificate to
engage in domestic scheduled operations, charter operation, private operation and aerial work air service operations in
compliance with Civil Aviation Act and Regulations made thereunder. Business name of the Company is Richy Skylark
(Private) Limited. The Directors of the Richy Skylarlk (Pvt.) Ltd are declared as follows :
Mr. Wirantha Harischandra Hettiarachchi
Ms. Sunanda Damayanthi Hettiarachchi
Mr. Uwin Windula Hettiarachchi
Richy Skylark (Pvt.) Ltd has nominated Lt. Col. PPSJ Pathirana to be the Accountable Manager in charge of the
proposed operations of air services.
A copy of the aforementioned application is kept at the Technical Library, Ground Floor, of  Head Quarters of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 04, Hunupitiya Road, Colombo 02, for the perusal  of the members of the public, during
office hours of any working days.
Members of the public are hereby informed that this office would proceed with the certification process of this
application after 29th July 2013 as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant's compliance with the
applicable legislative provisions, Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended Practices specified
by ICAO and associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure
and efficient operation of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Charter Licence
and Aerial Work Licence together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
Any person may, not less than 10 days before the aforementioned date in the penultimate paragraph, make represen-
tations to me in writing, setting out the specific grounds on which such representations are made in respect of this application,
