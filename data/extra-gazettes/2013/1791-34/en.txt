No. 1791/34 – THURSDAY, JANUARY 03, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref No. : 04/ 03/ 01/ 10.
Land Ministry Ref. No .:  LD/ 05/ 2010/ WS/ 32.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of  land called ‘Ellamullahena’ in total  extent of
0.2040 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No. PP . Ka. 4367
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Kandana
in Grama Niladhari's Division of 632 B Kandana-South in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of  Madurawala in Kalutara District,
Western Province.
Name of  Land: Ellamullahena; Claimant : Dammi Karunarathne,
No. 5, Kolamunna, Piliyandala; Extent: 0.2040 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat Madurawala on the 15th  February 2013
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 08th day of February 2013 and the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the aquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Menaka Priyantha Aberathna,
Divisional Secretary,
Madurawala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Madurawala,
04th January, 2013.
