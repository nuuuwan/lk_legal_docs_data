FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  WENNAPPUWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of a Member  in Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the North Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which Trewin
Fernando the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of that Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Karunanayaka Liyanage
Chaminda Kumara Karunanayaka being an eligible person under th aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Wennappuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr. Karunanayaka Liyanage Chaminda Kumara Karunanayaka elected as a member of the  Wennappuwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
J. A. S. P. J
AYASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Wennappuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
13th  December, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  PACHCHILAIPALLI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of a Member  in Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  death :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which Vayiran
Thiyagarasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of that Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Kanthan Rasathurai being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kanthan Rasathurai
elected as a member of the  Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
25th November, 2013.
