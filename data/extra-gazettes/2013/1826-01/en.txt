THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
THE  COCONUT  GROWING  TRADE
IN accordance with the provisions of Section 28 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), the Wages Board for the
Coconut Growing Trade hereby notifies that the Board by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid
Ordinance proposes to vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that
Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 24th
September 2013.
Every such objection must be made it writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
V . B. P. K.  W
EERASINGHE,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Coconut Growing Trade.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
16th August 2013.
