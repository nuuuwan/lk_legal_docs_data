UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972
I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Kumarasiri Rathnayaka,  Minister in charge of  subject of  Housing of Uva  Province Provincial
Council,  by virtue of powers vested in me by section 5 (1) of Rent Statute No. 05 of 2010 of the Uva Province Provincial
Council which should be read along with the Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972, am hereby announcing that Mr. Rajarama Brahmanalage
Nayana Ranjith Priyashantha of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service was appointed as the ''Authorized officer" for Badulla
and Monaragala districts with effect from 2013.01.01 to accomplish duties and tasks demised from the Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972.
R. M. K
UMARASIRI RATHNAYAKA,
Minister of  Road Development, Housing,
Water supply, Consumer Affairs and
