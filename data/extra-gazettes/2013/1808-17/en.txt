EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
KELANIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which Kelum
Hasitha Madawala  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of that Kelaniya
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr.  Bolandage Indika
Nishantha Gunarathna being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare Mr. Bolandage Indika Nishantha Gunarathna  elected as  a member of   the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
23rd  April, 2013.
