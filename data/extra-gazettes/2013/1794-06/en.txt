No. 1794/6 – MONDAY, JANUARY 21, 2013
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
expansion of the village and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of  about 8.050 Hectares
depicted as Lot  No. 01 in Advance Tracing No. KE/MWN/99/27
and  dated 08th July, 1997, prepared by Survey Superintendent of
Kegalle, situated in Aluthnuwara Village in Minor Division of
Medapattuwa in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mawanella in
the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa  Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot No. 01  – Newlandwatta  – Extent:  About 8.050 Hectares
North: Darande Kumbura and Newlandwatte; East: Newlandwatte
and Road of Watte; South: Newlandwatte, Wewa Kumbura and
Atugedara Kumbura; West: Kahatange Watte alias Yakwariya,
Panchiyawatta Kumbure Pillawa, Agala Kotuwa Hena and
Dombagollewatte.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
