STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are.–
1. Whether unjust had been caused to Mr. Ranjith Liyanage who served as Manager (Supplies) Grade III of the State Gem Corporation, by
his compensation being computed in accordance with the Bulumulla Compensation Scheme whereas he had given his consent to leave his
employment under the General Compensation Scheme, at the time of the conversion of the Coroporation to an authority in 1994,
and
2. Whether Mr. Ranjith Liyanage is entitled for the fifty months – compensation for his 22 years of service, since his Employees, Provident
Funds (EPF) have already been released before reaching the age, considering his as an excess employee based on the surplus of employees
and to what other reliefs he is entitled to.
V. B. P. K. Weerasinghe,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 03rd day of December, 2012.
