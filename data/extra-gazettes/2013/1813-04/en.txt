No. 1813/4 – MONDAY, JUNE 03, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref.  No.: Hapu/Acq/1/68.
Land Ministry's  No.: 4/3/8/2011/PC/219.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two allotments of land called Diyathalawe Pathana in total extent
of about 0.1203 Hectare depicted as Lot Nos. 1359 and 1360  in the
Town Survey Preliminary Plan No. 28 Supplement No. 55 Sheet
No. 60 dated 24.04.2013 situated in the Village called Diyathalawa
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 63 B of Diyathalawa in
Mahapalatha Korale in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Haputale in Badulla District of the Uva Province and more fully
described as follows.
Lot No. 1359
Name of land: Diyathalawe Pathana; Extent: 0.0242 Hectare.
Lot No. 1360
Name of land: Diyathalawe Pathana; Extent: 0.0961 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Haputale Divisional Secretariat, on the 17th day of July, 2013 at
9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 10th day of July, 2013, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
T. P . Anura Hemakumara,
Divisional Secretary,
Haputale.
Divisional Secretariat,
Haputale.
29th May, 2013.
