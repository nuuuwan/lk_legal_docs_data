EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PUTTALAM  URBAN  COUNCIL
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Puttalam Urban council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the group Leader of  the independent group No. 08  to which A. R. M. Mahir  the member belonged, was
required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of that Puttalam Urban council  who belongs to  such group  who
shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group  has nominated  Mr.  M. M. Rasmi  being an eligible person to fill
vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana,  Returning Officer of  Puttalam Urban Council, do
hereby declare Mr. M. M. Rasmi elected as  a member of   the Puttalam Urban Council.
K. A. SUMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
01st April, 2013.
