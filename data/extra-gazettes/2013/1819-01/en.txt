THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The following land in extent  of 1.1372 Hectare, depicted as Lot  A
in Advance Tracing No. CO/COL/2012/1047 dated 11.01.2013
prepared by Surveyor General  situated in the Village of  Suduwella
(Ward No. 24 Suduwella within the limits of the Colombo Municipal
Council) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, of Colombo in the
District of Colombo, Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Parts of land bearing Assmt. Nos. 300 and 320,
D. R. Wijewardena Mawatha, Part of land Assmt. No not known
and Johnsonwatta – Extent: 1.1372 Hectare
North: Lands bearing Assmt. Nos. 294 and 300, D R Wijewardena
Mawatha, D R Wijewardena Mawatha and Lands bearing Assmt.
Nos. 320, 340 and 340/1, D R Wijewardena Mawatha; East: D R
Wijewardena Mawatha and Lands bearing Assmt. Nos. 320, 340 and
340/1, D R Wijewardena Mawatha; South: Lands bearing Assmt.
Nos. 340 and 340/1, D R Wijewardena Mawatha and Beira Lake;
West:  Beira Lake, Lands bearing Assmt. Nos. 294 and 300, D R
Wijewardena Mawatha.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
