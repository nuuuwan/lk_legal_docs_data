Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
KAYTS  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of a member in Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Kanthagnani Katheessan the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member of that Kayts
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Johnpillai Anton Charles
Jansan being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of Kayts Predeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
Mr. Johnpllai Anton Charles Jansan elected as a member of the Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
13th March, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
V ALIKAMAM  EAST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS the office of a member in Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  the recognised political party to which Tharumalingam
Krishnarasa the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member of that Valikamam East
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.

an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of Valikaman East  Predeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare Ms. Thambirasa Siyama elected as a member of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
