NORTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Cancellation
I, Governor of the Northern Province do hereby cancel the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka No. 1832/20 of 15.10.2013 consisted of the appointments made by the virtue of the powers vested on me under the
Section 154F(1), (4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
G. A. C
HANDRASIRI,
Governor,
Northern Province.
NORTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of the Northern Province
I, Gammanpila Arachchige Chandrasiri, Governor of the Northern Province by virtue of powers vested in me under the
Section 154F (1) , (4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby announce that I have made the following appointments.
Hon. Kanagasabapathy Visvalingam Wigneswaran - Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Lands, Road
Development, Electricity, Housing and Construction, Water Supply,
Co-operative Development, Social Service and Rehabilitation,
Women’s Affairs, Industries and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local
Government and Provincial Administration and Food Supply and
Distribution of the Northern Province with effect from 11th October
2013
Hon. Ponnuththurai Ainkaranesan - Minister of Agriculture and Agrarian Services, Animal Husbandry,
Irrigation and Environment of the Northern Province with effect from
11th of October 2013
