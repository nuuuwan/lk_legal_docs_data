THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
This acquisition is made  for distributing lands among persons
selected by land Kachcheries as per the circular 2008/04.
Boundaries for the portion of  land containing in extent  of 0.8321
Hectare depicted  as Lot  'A' in the Advance Tracing No. MR/DVN/
2013/133 dated 23.03.2013  made by Survey Superintendent of
Matara and  situated  in the Village   of Wawwa in  Minor Division of
Wellabada Pattu West,  Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Devinuwara,  Matara District in Southern Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kandegodellawatte – Extent:  0.8321 Hectare
North: Egodawatte claimed by P. P. Peter, others and  Kandegodella
claimed by K. G . Jagath Priyantha Kumara; East: Kandegodella
claimed by K. G . Jagath Priyantha Kumara and Road (P. S.); South:
Road (P. S.) and Kandegodella claimed by D. M. Dharmasena; West:
Kandegodella claimed by D. M. Dharmasena and Egodawatte claimed
by P. P. Peter and others.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: 4/03/ 15/2012/VE/156.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
‘‘Mihikatha Medura’’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
28th August, 2013.
