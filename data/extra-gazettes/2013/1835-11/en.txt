Notice under Section 7  
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the lands described in the 
schedule given below for the third Phase of Kegalle 
Alternate HighWay.  
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Ninety allotments of land described in the scheduled 
attached below depicted in Preliminary Plan No. 
KE/3445, P.P.K. 3446, P.P.K. 3447 and P.P.K. 3448 
prepared by Surveyor General situated in the Villages of 
Golahela, Nilmalgoda, Meepitiya and Pallewela Grama 
Niladhari Divisions (Within boundaries of Kegalle Urban 
Council and Pradeshiya Sabha boundaries) in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Divisi on in Kegalle District of 
Sabaragamuwa Province.
