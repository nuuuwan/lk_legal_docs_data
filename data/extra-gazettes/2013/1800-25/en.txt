PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
CUSTOMS    NOTIFICATION  OF   THE   GOVERNMENT
Order  under   Section 111 of  the  Customs  Ordinance  (Chapter  235)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 111 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) to be read with Article 44(2)
of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, subject to Air Navigation Regulations No. 09 of 1938, I,
Mahinda Rajapaksha, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this notification order and declare that
the village of Maththala in Hambanthota District in the Southern Province to be an Airport of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka and its respective boundaries shall be as set out in the Schedule “A”, I do further declare the places setout in the
Schedule “B” to be a legal Airport within such boundaries for loading and unloading of air passengers, air mail items and other
cargos and declare the boundaries and extent of such Airport shall be as set out in the Schedules “A” “B” and “C” within the
Maththala Rajapaksa International Airport.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
(Minister of Finance and Planning)
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
07th March 2013.
Above Schedules are described as “A”, “B”, “C”
SCHEDULE   A
All that divided and defined allotments of lands marked Lots A 1408, 1409 and 1411 depicted in the National grid
reference field sheet No. E. D. M. H 2825, E. D. M. H 2950, final topographic plan No. 03 interim plan No. 369 sheet No. 405, and
plan No. L/519 and Lot 177 depicted in the Plan No. 575 of the final village plan of Additional Field Sheet No. 575 made in March
2012 by the Surveyor General of the Land called Kurudana Kelle Inginiyangahahena, Jambugahahena and Palekurudana
Welkatuwa of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka situated at the village of Thammennawa Kurudana of
Thammennawa, Ketanwewa Grama Niladari Division in the minor division of Magam Pattu of Hambantota Divisional Secretary's
Division in the District of Hambantota, Southern Province and containing in extent Two Hundred and Eighty Eight Decimal
Seven Three Five Two Hectares (288.7352 ha.)
