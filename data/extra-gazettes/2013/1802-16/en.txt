NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10(A) 1 (a)
GAMPAHA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning officer of the Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare Under
Section 10(A) 1 (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) that Mr. Kumarapeli Arachchige Indika Saman
Kumarapeli of the Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased
to be a member of the People’s Liberation Front.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Gampaha  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Gampaha,
12th March, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  IPALOGAMA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of  a member in Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Muhammadu Unusu Abdul Latheebu the member  belonged, was required by me to  noaminate a person eligible as a member
of that Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as a member  within a period
of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Heenbanda Chandara
Rathna Bandara Rajakaruna being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha,
do hereby declare Mr. Heenbanda Chandara Rathna Bandara Rajakaruna elected as member of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Ipalogama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Anuradhapura,
13th March, 2013.
