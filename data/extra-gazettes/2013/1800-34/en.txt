THE  NATIONAL  HOUSING  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1979
Certificate under Section - 6
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 6 of the National Housing Development Authority Act, No. 17 of 1979, I, Wimal
Weerawansha Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing and Common Amenities, do hereby certify that the
land describe in the Schedule hereto should be acquired by the Government for the purpose of being made available for
carrying out of any housing object within the meaning of Section 4 of that Act.
W
IMAL WEERAW ANSHA,
Minister of Construction, Engineering Services,
Housing and Common Amenities.
Colombo,
28th February 2013.
