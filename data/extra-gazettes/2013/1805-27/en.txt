No. 1805/27 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 10, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: SIYA/DS/ED/5/2/L/4/ LA.
Land Ministry's No.: 03/J/78/P/412.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for access road to 174/ 8 bridge of
Colombo - Rathnapura, Wallawaya - Batticaloa Road.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of  land in F. T. P 54 A, insect No. 24  prepared by
the Survey Superintendent of Monaragala, situated in the Village of
Ulvita in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Siyambalanduwa in
Monaragala District, Uva Province.
01. Lot No. 250
Name of land: Pahalaweleyaya; Claimant: Rev. Maallagama
Somananda Thero, Kotiyagoda Temple, Dombagahawela; Extent:
0.0313 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Siyambalanduwa on the 08th day of May,
2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day of May, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
A. M. Nandasiri,
Divisional Secretary,
Siyambalanduwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Siyambalanduwa.
