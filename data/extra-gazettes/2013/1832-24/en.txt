CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice by the Hon. Governor  of  the  Central  Province
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
KNOW ye that  by virtue of powers vested by the article 154(b) : (10)(b) of the thirteenth amendment to the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the Central Province, do hereby summon
the council to meet at the Central Province Provincial Council Meeting Hall at Pallekelle, Kundasale on Thursday the 17th
October, Two Thousand Thirteen at 10.00 a. m.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUW A,
Governor of the Central Province.
Given at Governor's Secretariat,
Kandy,
07th October, 2013.
