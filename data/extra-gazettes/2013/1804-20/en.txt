No. 1804/20 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 03, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Divisional Secretary’s
Reference No.: KDS/KGK/4/2/2009/02/24.
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/05/2009/WS/292. 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
servitude rights of land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the servitude to be acquired:
One (01) allotment of land called Pukra Estate  alias
Mapanawathura Estate in extent of about 0.0764 Hectare, depicted
in the P.P.MAHA 6241 and dated 06.03.2013 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Mapanawathura Ward
No. 6, Asgiriya (within the limits of Kandy Municipal Council) in
the Grama Niladhari Division of Wattaranthanne in the Minor
Division of Gangawata Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in Kandy District, Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Mapanawathura Estate alias Pukra Estate;
Original Claimant: Manager, Reyelit Estate Developers (Pvt.) Ltd.,
No. 403/1, Church Road, Colombo 01; Extent: 0.0764 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Kandy and Gangawata Korale on the
29th day of May, 2013 at 2.30 p.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of
May, 2013, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D. M. N. G . G . T. Karunarathne,
Divisional Secretary,
Kandy and Gangawata Korale.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kandy and Gangawata Korale,
28th March, 2013.
