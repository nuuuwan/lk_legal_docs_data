THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0253 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. B/ELL/2012/424 of 31.10.2012
prepared by the Surveyor-General of Badulla District. The Land
called Nethervalley Division in the Minor Division of Kumbalwela
Korale in the Village of Halpe (F.V .P 36) in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Ella in Badulla District, Uva  Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot 'A' –  Nethervalley Division – Extent: 0.0253 Hectare
North: Estate Road and Demodara Estate owned by
Udupussellawa Plantation; East and South: Demodara Estate owned
by Udupussellawa Plantation;West: Demodara Estate owned by
Udupussellawa Plantation and Estate Road.
