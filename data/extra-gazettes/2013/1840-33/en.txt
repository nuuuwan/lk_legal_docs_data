FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  WENNAPPUWA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a Vice Chairman  in  Wennappuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as
a member of the  North Western Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United People’s Freedom Alliance , the recognised political party  to which Trewin
Fernando  the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (1) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of  that  Wennappuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Vice
Chairman   within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Anuruddha Kachchakaduge Namal Siriwardana Fernando to fill   such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Wennappuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section  65 A (1) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  Anuruddha Kachchakaduge Namal Siriwardana Fernando  elected as  Vice Chairman of   the Wennappuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. A. S. P. JAYASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Wennappuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
29th   November 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  NATTANDIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the  North Western Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Asoka Priyantha
Ranpati Devage  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Herath Pathirannehelage
Athula  Priyantha Herath  being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Nattandiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section  65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Herath Pathirannehelage  Athula Priyantha Herath  elected as  a member of   the Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. A. S. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
29th   November 2013.
