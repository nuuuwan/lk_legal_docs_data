THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF 1971
Order  under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J. Senevirathne, Minister of
Public Administration & Home Affairs, do by this order declare that, as regards the year 2014, each of  the days specified in the
Schedule hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
W. D. J. SENEVIRA THNE,
  Minister of Public Administration
 & Home Affairs.

Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
28th  March, 2013.
