THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No.  1  OF  1972  OF  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 27A
BY virtue of the powers  vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as amended
by the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby -
(a) Amend the Order, made under Sub-section (1) of Section 27A of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 344/5 of April 09, 1985 relating to the land called and known as Yatawahara alias Yatawehera morefully
described in the First Schedule to this order and which had vested in the Janatha Estate Development Board established by
the State Agricultural Corporation Act, No. 11 of 1972, by revoking on grounds of non-compliance with such terms and
conditions relating to consideration for the vesting in such Board, such portion of the said Order as relates to the extent of
land called Yatawahara alias Yatawehera specified in the Second Schedule ; and
(b) State that upon revocation, such extent of land as specified in the Second Schedule to this order, shall re-vest in
the Land Reform Commission.
