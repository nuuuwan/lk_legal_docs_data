No. 1805/3 – MONDAY, APRIL 08, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. : Ath/Kd/81/HW.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An  allotment of land in extent of about 0.0094 Hectare, depicted
as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary Plan No. Co.9290  prepared by
the Colombo District Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Hewagama, Grama Niladhari Division of 474 Hewagama
in the Minor Division of Palle Pattuwa, in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Kaduwela, in Colombo District in Western
Province.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Hedawakagahalanda; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K. A. D. Pathmasiri
No. 139, Kothalawala, Kaduwela ; Extent: 0.0094 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Kaduwela Divisional Secretariat on the 29th day of May, 2013
at 10.00.a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of May, 2013 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
M. S. P . Suriyapperuma,
Divisional Secretary,
Kaduwela.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kaduwela,
04th April, 2013.
