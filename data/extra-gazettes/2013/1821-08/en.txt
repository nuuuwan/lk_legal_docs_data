MUNICIPAL  COUNCILS  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 10 (2) (b)
By virtue of the powers vested in me by  Paragraph (b) of Subsection (2) of the Section 10 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 252), I, A. L. M.  Athaullah, Minister of   Local  Government and Provincial Councils, do by this order appoint the date
of termination of the term of  office of the members of Jaffna Municipal Council, upto 31st August 2014 in place of 31st August
2013 and extend the term of office of the members of the said Municipal Council.
A. L. M.  A
THAULLAH,
Minister of  Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
25th  July, 2013.
