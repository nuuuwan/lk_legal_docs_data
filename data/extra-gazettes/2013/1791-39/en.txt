THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: DS/UP/3/1/LAQ/Ego.tank/37.
Your Reference No.: LD/05/2009/WS/292.
Laying of pipe line to reservoir of Egoda Kalugamuwa
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The land called Galkaduwahena, Palliyagawahena, Galdettahena
in total extent of about 0.0381 Hectare in cadastral map No. 320081
(2) 3 shown  as Lot No. 38 surveyed and prepared by Surveyor
General in Grama Niladhari Division of Egodakalugamuwa in
Kandukara Pahala Korale in Divisional Secretary's Division of
Udapalatha in Kandy District Central Province.
01. Lot No. 38
Name of land: Galkaduwahena, Palliyagawahena, Galdettahena;
Claimant: H. M. L. Munivar, No. 418/A, Egoda Kalugamuwa,
Gelioya; Extent: 0.0381 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Udapalatha on the 13th day of February,
2013 at    9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 6th day of February, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
B. M. Nimal Balasooriya,
