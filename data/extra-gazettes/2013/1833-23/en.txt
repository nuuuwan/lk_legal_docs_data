EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  V ADAMARADCHI  SOUTH  WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of a member in Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not
attending meetings of the Council for a period of three months without permission :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recongnised political party to which Thurai
Piratheepan  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible under the  Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified :
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan ,  Returning Officer of  Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Inthirarasa
Thuseekaran who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the
election held on 23rd July, 2011 to Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the Vadamaradchi
South West  Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
17th  October, 2013.
