AMENDMENT
Marine  Environment  Protection  Authority
(Ministry  of  Environment)
THE Marine Pollution Prevention Act published under I:I - General - Government Notifications in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1816/37 of 28 June 2013 should be corrected as follows.
SUSIL PREMAJAYANTHA,
 Minister of Environment and
Renewable Energy.
Colombo,
27th September, 2013.
REGULATIONS
‘‘I. These regulations may be cited as the Marine Environmental Protection (Issuance at Permits for Dumping at Sea)
Regulations No. .................. 2013, ’’
Should be corrected as
‘‘I. These regulations may be cited as the Marine Environmental Protection (Issuance at Permits for Dumping at Sea)
Regulations No. 01/2013. These regulations shall be deemed to have come into effect from the 12 day of June 2013.’’
