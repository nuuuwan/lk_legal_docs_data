FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
GAMPAHA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a Member in Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the Party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognised political party  to which Kumarapeli
Arachchige Indika Saman Kumarapeli the Member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a Member of
that Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as a Member within a period of
thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr.  Manawaduge Sumathipala
being an elgible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare Mr. Manawaduge Sumathipala elected as  a Member of   the Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
10th  May, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
MAHARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a Member in Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the Party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognised political party  to which Uchira Sankalpa
Kasthuriarachchi the Member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a Member of that Mahara
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr.  Pepiliwala Gamaralalage
Wijegunawardana being an elgible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare Mr. Pepiliwala Gamaralalage Wijegunawardana elected as  a  Member of   the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
10th  May, 2013.
