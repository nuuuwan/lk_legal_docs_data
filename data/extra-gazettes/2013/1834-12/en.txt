EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  GALGAMUWA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Galgamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the   the  United People’s Freedom Alliance  the recognized  political party  to which
the Benthara Arachchilage Thilakarathna   member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Galgamuwa    Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. S. Bandara  being an
eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama  Returning Officer  of   Galgamuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha,   acting  in  terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr. S. Bandara  elected as  a member of   the Galgamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. THALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Galgamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
22nd  October, 2013.
