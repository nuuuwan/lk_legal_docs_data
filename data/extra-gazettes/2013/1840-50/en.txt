WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 read with Section
2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and
the Minister in-charge of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government, Provincial Administration,
Manpower and Employment, Economic Promotion, Power and Energy of the Western Province Provincial Council, do by this
order declare that I have approved to impose and charge ; a 10% Entertainment Tax on entrance fee for entertainment
activities stated in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 37
of 1984, within the limits of the Pradeshiya Sabha, the proposition of which has been passed by the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, on 2013.07.30 as per provisions stated Section 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief Minister and the Minister in-charge of Finance Planning,
Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government,
Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment,
Economic, Promotion, Power and Energy
of the Western Province Provincial Concil.
