Notice under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PACHCHILAIPALLI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS, the office of a member in Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognised political party to which Thiyagarajah
Piratheepan  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member of that  Pachchilaipalli
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Krishnan Veeravaguthevar
being an eligible person to fill such vacancy.
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare Mr. Krishnan Veeravaguthevar elected as a member of the Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. ACHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
04th July, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PACHCHILAIPALLI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS, the office of a member in Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognised political party to which Arulpirakasam
Wilson Paul  the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member of that  Pachchilaipalli
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Subramaniam Suren being
an eligible person to fill such vacancy.
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare Mr. Subramaniam Suren elected as a member of the Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,

Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
