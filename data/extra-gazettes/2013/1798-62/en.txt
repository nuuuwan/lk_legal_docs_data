UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  N O. 16 OF  1978
Order  under  Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy (SIBA) a company duly incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 and havaing Company Number GA 2232, do by this Order, subject to the condition specified in
the Schedule hereto, recognizes the aforesaid Academy as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher
education leading to the conferment of the Degree Programme specified in Column I and its abbreviation appearing in Column
II as follows :
Column I Column II
Degree Programme Abbreviation to the Degree
Bachelor of Arts Special Degree in Buddhist BA (BL) Special SIBA
Leadership
SCHEDULE
1. The minimum qualification for admission to the Degree Programme shall be three passes in the G. C. E. Advanced
Level Examination.
2. In the case of Sri Lankan students, in addition to the minimum qualification it is required that the students admitted
to the Degree Programme should have obtained at least thirty per centum (30%) for the Common General paper  at
the G . C. E. (Advaned Level) Examination.
3. In the case of foreign students applicants shall be required to send along with their application a letter obtained
from the Examination Board concerned certifying that their educational qualifications are equivalent to the Sri
Lanka G . C. E. (Advanced Level) Examination or acceptable by the admission authority in their own country, as
sufficient qualifications for an undergraduate course of study leading to a Bachelor's Degree.
