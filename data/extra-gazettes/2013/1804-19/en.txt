EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
KOLONNAWA  URBAN  COUNCIL
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Kolonnawa Urban council has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which
Subaweerage Chandrasiri Dias the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of that
Kolonnawa Urban council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Morawakage Chamila
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Niruka Dananga Laththuwahandi,  Returning Officer of  Kolonnawa Urban council, do hereby
declare Mr. Morawakage Chamila elected as  a member of   the Kolonnawa Urban council.
N
IRUKA LA THTHUWAHANDI,
Returning Officer,
Kolonnawa Urban council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
18th March, 2013.
