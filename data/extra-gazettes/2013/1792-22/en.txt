Notification
W AGES  BOARD FOR THE BAKING  TRADE
IN accordance with the provisions of Section 28 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) the Wages Board for the
Baking Trade hereby notifies that the Board by virtue of  powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance
proposes to vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 01.02.2013.
Every such objection must  be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
V . B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Baking Trade.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
13th December, 2012.
