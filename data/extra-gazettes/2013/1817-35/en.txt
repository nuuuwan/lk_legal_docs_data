EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notice Under the Lcoal Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  TANGALLE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS, the office of a member in Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recongnised political party to which Kaluhennadige
Darshana Pradeep the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member of that  Tangalle
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Ananda Sarath Upali
Palihakkara being and eligible person to fill such vacancy.
Now therefore I, Wewelawala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara Returning Officer of Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare Mr. Ananda Sarath Upali Palihakkara elected as a member of the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. WIJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota
27th June, 2013.
