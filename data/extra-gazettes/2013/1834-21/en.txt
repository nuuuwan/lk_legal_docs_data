STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No. 14  OF  2008
Revocation of Notifications under Section 3(2) and Order under Section 3(4)
I,  Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena, being the Minister in Charge of the subject of Investment and Promotion, declare by this
notification that the following Gazette Notification and the Order shall hereby be rescinded :-
1. the Notification published under Sub Section (2) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of
2008, in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1819/30 dated 18th July, 2013; and
2. the Order published under Sub Section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008,
in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1829/23 dated 23rd September, 2013.
LAKSHMAN YAPA ABEYWARDENA, M.P.
Minister of Investment and Promotion.
Colombo ,
29th day of October, 2013.
