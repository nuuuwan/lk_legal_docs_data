No. 1805/28 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 10, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: PTB/LND/ Acqui / 7/ 23.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called ‘‘Murukkandola Hena’’ in total extent
of 0.0051 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 400 in the F.V .E. No. 6,
Supplement No. 17, Sheet No. 18,  and dated 11.03.2013, surveyed
and prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of
Pitabeddara Minor Division of Morawakkorale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Pitabeddara in Matara District Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
Lot No. 400
Name of land: Murukkandola Hena; Description : Garden, a
permanent building; Claimant : State; Original Claimant : W. H. Lalith
Mahindasiri Chandana, Gorakawela, Pitabeddara; Extent: 0.0051
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Pitabeddara Divisional Secretariat on the 10th day of June, 2013
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 3rd day of June, 2013, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
K. G . R. Jayanath,
Divisional Secretary,
Pitabeddara.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Pitabeddara.
