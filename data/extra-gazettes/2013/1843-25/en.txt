PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  NO. 2  OF  1988
Filling  of a Vacancy  in the  Southern  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforeasid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Bandu Ranawaka to fill
such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Bandu Ranawaka, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Matara in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
30th  December, 2013.
