In the matter of an Industrial Dispute between
Mr. K. Senthivel, Brinthavanam, Thunnali North, Karaveddy
of the One Part
Case No. A3410
and
1. Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industries of Sri Lanka Federation House, No. 53, Vauxhall Lane, Colombo 02.
2. Chamber of Commerce and Industries of Yalpanam, No. 175, Point Pedro Road, Anaipanthi, Jaffna
of the Other Part
AWARD
01. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in him by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act,
Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 4 of 1962
and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968  appointed me as Arbitrator by his order dated
18.10.2011 and referred the dispute between the aforesaid parties to me  for  settlement by arbitration.
02. The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is:–
“To   identify the employer of Mr. K. Senthivel, who served as the Chief Executive Officer of Chamber of Commerce and Industries of
Yalpanam from 01.11.2004 and whether his termination on 20.02.2006 is justified, if not to what relief he is entitled”
03. Appearances:
Workman was present in person no one appeared for him.
Mr. Isuru Lakpura Attornery-at-Law appeared for the 1st Respondent. Mrs. D. Malka Sudarshini Programme Co-ordinator was present
on behalf of  the 1st Respondent.
Mr. K. V. S. Ganesharajah,  Attornery-at-Law appeared for the 2nd Respondent. Mr. S. Yogarajasingham, Chief Executive Officer-
Chamber of Commerce and Industries of Yalpanam was present on behalf of  the 2nd Respondent.
04. Negotiations ensued for sometime and it was finally suggested by me that the case be settled with a payment of (06) months salary. Both
parties accepted my suggestion and accordingly the case was settled on 07.11.2012 before me.
05. Payment was made to the workman on 22.11.2012 as follows:-
Commercial Bank of Ceylon at Borella cheque No. 670138 dated 22.11.2012 and Seylan  Bank PLC at Jaffna Branch cheque No.
193062 dated 22.11.2012 for Rs. 69,000/= each. This was a full and final settlement in respect of all claims of the workman against the
Respondents in relation to  the reference before me. It was agreed by both parties that this settlement will not be cited as a precedent in
any other forum whatsoever. This settlement being full and final the workman cannot pursue any claim in any forum whatsoever. Both
parties signed the case record and the workman accepted the two cheques issued to him before me.
06. I consider this settlement just and equitable and make award accordingly.
V. Vimalarajah,
Arbitrator.
06th day of December 2012.
