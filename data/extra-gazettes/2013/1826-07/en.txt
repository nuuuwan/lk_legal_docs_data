EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  MEDIRIGIRIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of    Vice  Chairman in  Medirigiriya    Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of Vice Chairman  has
been appointed as Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Thebuwaththe Gamaralalage Ananda Sri Dharmasena the  Vice Chairman   belonged, was required by me  under Section
65 A  (1) of  the  aforesaid Ordinance, to  decide upon the   member of  that  Medirigiriya    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Widana Arachchilage Padmasiri Hemachandra,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hathurusinghalage Indika  Ranjan Hathurusinghe,  Returning  Officer of  Medirigiriya    Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting  in terms  of  Section  65 A (1) of  the Local Authorities  Elections  Ordinance  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Widana Arachchilage Padmasiri Hemachandra, elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Medirigiriya    Pradeshiya   Sabha.
H.I.R. H
A THURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
 Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
29th August, 2013.
