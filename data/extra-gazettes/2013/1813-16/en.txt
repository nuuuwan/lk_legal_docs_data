CO-OPERATIVE  EMPLOYEES  COMMISSION  STATUTE  No. 01 OF  1994  OF  THE  WESTERN  PROVINCE
Appointment to the Western Province Co-operative Employee’s Commission
I, H. M. Upali Kodikara, Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs, Art and Cultural Affairs, Co-operative Development,
Food supplies and Distribution and Rural Development of the Western Province by virtue of Provisions vested in by Section
4(2) of the Co-operative Employees Commission Statute No. 01 of 1994 of the Western Province do by this order appoint the
following as members of the Co-operative Employees Commission of the Western Province to be effective from 06th of  June,
2012 :
1. Thalgamuwe Gedara Lional Weerarathne ;
2. Peduru Arachchige Ariyapala.
Further, A by virtue of powers vested in me by Section 7(1) of the said Co-operative Employees Commission Statute
No. 01 of 1994 of the Western Province do hereby appoint the under mentioned member as Chairman of the Co-operative
Employees Commission of the Western Province to be effective from 06th of June, 2012.
1. Ariyadasa Thanthrige.
H. M. U
PALI KODIKARA,
Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs,
Art and Cultural Affairs, Co-operative Development,
Food supplies and Distribution and
Rural Development of the Western Province.
