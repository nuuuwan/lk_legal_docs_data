My No.: Kot/Lnd/Acqvi/01/02(La/3530).

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of the land called Atambagahahena situated at
Kotapola Village of Kosmodara Grama Niladhari’s Division
(242E) at the Divisional Secretariat of Kotapola in the District of
Matara in the Southern Province, depicted as Lot No. 2292 of
Sheet No.  113 of the Supplementary Sheet No. 85 (F.V .P. 45)
prepared by the Surveyor-General, containing in extent about
0.0488 Hectare and described as follows:
Lot No. 2292
Name of land: Atambagahahena; Claimant: Rev. Thalangalle
Pugnasara, Sri Subhodharamaya; Extent: 0.0488 Hectare.

appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of  Kotapola, on the 15th day of July, 2013
at  10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 10th day of July, 2013, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
Ganesha Amarasinghe,
Divisional Secretary,
