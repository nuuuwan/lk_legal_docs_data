WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Orders published under the road passenger carriage services statute No. 1 of 1992 which has been amended by the
Statute of No. 3 of 1993 and No. 07 of 1995 of Western Province Provincial Council
ACCORDING to the authority and provisions delegated to the Western Province Passenger Transport Authority by the
Article No. 6.6(a) of the Statute of Road Passenger Transport Services No. 01 of 1992, the order drafted by the Authority and
published in the Gazette No. 1777/12 on 24.09.2012 is notified as amended as given below.
Order 1. The fee of Rs. 100,000.00 which is charged at present for transferring the ownership of the route permits issued
by the Western  Province Passenger Transport Authority is amended as Rs. 70,000.00 and charged for the period
from 26.12.2012 to 25.03.2013
Order 2. Only the rates under the items numbered 01, 06 and 15 of the orders of the Passenger Transport Services
notifications published under the schedule 4 of the Gazette No. 1777/12 on the 24th September 2012 are amended
and charged for the period from 26.12.2012 to 25.03.2013.
1. Passenger service permit per bus Rs. 3,500
6. Monthly service fee per bus (normal service) Rs. 750
15. Monthly service fee per bus (luxury service) Rs. 750
Futher, the rates published in the Provincial Council Notification on 24th September 2012 in the Gazette No. 1777/12
will be charged with effect from 26.03.2013.
