1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  V ALVETITHURAI  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Valvetithurai Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the Northern  Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  the recognised political party to which Kanagalingam
Sivagilingam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Valvetithurai Urban Council who belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated. Mr. Shakthivel Pratheepan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Valvetithurai Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Shakthivel Pratheepan
elected as a member of the  Valvetithurai Urban Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
V alvetithurai Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
13th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Jaffna Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission  :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Murugiah Komahan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Jaffna Municipal Council who belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Karthigesu Thillaiambalam
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy.
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Jaffna Municipal  Council, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Karthigesu Thillaiambalam
elected as a member of the  Jaffna Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal  Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
13th  November, 2013.
