UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Announcement  made  by  the  Hon. Governor
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987, Section 32, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of
the Uva Province, do hereby announce that I have created these Uva Provincial Council Public Service procedural rules on
appointment, promotion, transfer, dismissal of Uva Provincial Council Public Service and to provide for matters connected
therewith and incidental thereto. These procedural rules shall come into force with effect from 01st January, 2013. This Code
is Published in Sinhala and Tamil Languages.
C. N
ANDA MaTHEW,
Governor of Uva Province.

King’s Street,
