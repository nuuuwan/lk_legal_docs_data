PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
LAND  REFORM  LAW  No.  01 OF  1972
Order  under  Section 22 (1) (f)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph (f) of Subsection (1) of Section 22 of Land Reform Law, No. 01 of 1972 as
amended from time to time, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order declare that :
(a) the agricultural land specified in Column II to this Order shall vest in the person whose name is specified in
Column I of the Schedule to this Order ;
(b) the agricultural land specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule to this Order was owned
by the person whose name appears in Column I of the Schedule to this Order and that such agricultural land was
included in the extent of land deemed to be owned by the head of the family of which such person was a
member ;
(c) the person whose name appears in Column I of the Schedule to this Order was under eighteen years of age at the
time of the imposition of the ceiling on agricultural land in terms of the aforesaid Law ;
(d) the agricultural land specified in Column II of the Schedule hereto has not been alienated to any person under
Section 22 of the said Law ; and
(e) no compensation has been paid in respect of such agricultural land under Section 28 of the said Law.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
February 20, 2013.
