1A
THE  WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Wages  Boards  for  the  Prawn  Culture  and  Exports   Trade
It is hereby notified under Section 29(3) of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chaper 136), that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Prawn Culture and Exports Trade made under Section 30 of the Ordinance and specified in the Schedule hereto have
been approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decision shall come into force on 01st of January, 2013.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
21st December, 2012.
SCHEDULE
The decision made by the Wages Board for the Prawn Culture and Export Trade  set out  in   Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1073/26  dated   04th  April, 1999,  and last  varied by the Schedule  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1664/24 dated
30th July, 2010, shall be varied by the  inclusion of the Part below.
The monthly minimum consolidated wages for time work applicable to workmen mentioned under different
categories in Part I who are engaged in the Prawn Culture and Exports  Trade shall be the rate set out in the
corresponding entry in Part II.
