ARTICLE 154 B OF THE CONSTITUTION  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST REPUBLIC  OF SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Paragraph (8) of Article 154B of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka I, Tissa Reginold Balalle, Governor of the North Western Province do hereby dissolve the North Western
Provincial Council with effect from midnight July 05, 2013.
TISSA REGINOLD BALALLE,
Governor of the North Western Province.
Office of the Governor of the North Western Province,
05th  July,  2013.
