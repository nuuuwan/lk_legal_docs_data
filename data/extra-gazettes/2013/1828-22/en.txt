I Hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of the Land 
Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of the land called Neelawatta in total 
extent of about 0.5581 hectares, depicted as Lot No. 1095 
of Final Village Plan No. 115 suppliment No. 46 Sheet 
No. 47 surveyed and prepared  by the Surveyor General, 
situated in the village of Morahenagama in Dagampitiya 
Grama Niladhari Division in Ambagamuwa Korale 
Divisional Secretary's Divisi on in District of Nuwara 
Eliya, Central Province and bounded as follows. 
 
Lot No. 1095 - Extent of Land - 0.5581 Hectares  
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 1 and 1085; East: Lot No. 1085 and 13; 
South: Lot No. 13 and 1090; West: Lot No. 1090 and 1. 
 
Claimant and Address of the claimant : Mrs. G. M. 
Heenmenike, Pallewatta, Pitawala  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/Lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambagamuwa Korale, on the 05th day of November 
2013 at 3.00 p.m., and are hereby requested to notify  me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th day of 
October 2013. The nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims  for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
 
 
 H. M. C. K. H ERATH, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Division Ambagamuwa Korale. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambagamuwa Korale, 
29th August 2013.
