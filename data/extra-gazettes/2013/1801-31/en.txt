No. 1801/31 – THURSDAY, MARCH 14, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.:  LD/05/2005/PC/481
4/1/5/13.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land’s which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.5908 Hectare,
depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. P.P.Ku 2284 dated
24th December, 2012, prepared by the Superintendent of
Surveys-Kurunegala District, and, situated in Ward No. 12 – Wehera
in the Municipal Council Limit of  Kurunegala in Thiragandahe Korale
of Minor Division, Divisional Secretary's Division  of Kurunegala in
Kurunegala   District of  North Western Province and more fully
described as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Pahala Ketha; Description: V ehicle Park of the
Public Fair; Claimant: State;Original Claimants: D.G .Jayathunga,
Kumburegedara, Haloluwa,  A.M.P. Adhikari, Gammedda road,
Malkaduwawa, S. Walgampaya, Wilgoda Circuler Road, Kurunegala
and Wijewardhana, Kenston Gardens, Colombo 04; Extent: About
0.5908 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at the Kurunagala Divisional Secretary's Office on the
22nd day of May, 2013 at  09.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing   (in duplicate) on or before the 08th day of
May, 2013, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
produced before me at the inquiry.
R. M. R. Rathnayaka.
Divisional Secretary,
Kurunegala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kurunegala.
08th February, 2013.
