THE  AIR  NA VIGATION  REGULATION  OF  1955
Notice   Under   Regulation  126
COMMISSIONING   OF  MATTALA   RAJAPAKSA   INTERNATIONAL   AIRPORT
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport located at Mattala in the administrative
areas of Hambantota and Lunugamvehera Divisional Secretariats in Hambantota District of the Southern Province, will be
commissioned on 18th March 2013 for operation of domestic and international air transport services. Accordingly aircraft
arriving in Sri Lanka or departing to overseas destinations may land at or depart from Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport
respectively and such aircraft may disembark and embark, passengers cargo and mail as applicable on such flights at the
airport.
Every aircraft operating into and out of Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport shall conform to the applicable
national laws, regulations, rules and implementing standards and also the established procedures relating to entry and
departure of aircraft, which are published in the Sri Lanka Aeronautical Information Package, Published by the undersigned.
Every departing and arriving passenger shall conform to the applicable national laws, regulations, rules and
implementing standards and also the established procedures relating to entry and departure of persons and baggage and
they may be subject to the applicable screening processes that will be performed by the respective government authorities
in charge of customs, immigration and emigration, aviation security, public health and plant and animal quarantine.
All inbound or outbound cargo including mail and other articles shall be  in conformity with the applicable national
laws, regulations, rules and implementing standards, and also the established procedures relating to entry and departure of
cargo, mail and other articles which are published in Sri Lanka Aeronautical Information Package and such cargo, mail and
other articles may be subject to the applicable screening processes that will be performed by the respective government
authorities incharge of customs, aviation security, public health and plant and animal quarantine.
