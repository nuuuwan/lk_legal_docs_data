EXTRAORDINARY
1APRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MAWANELLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION 65A(2) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  Korale
Gedara Piyathissa,  the member  belonged, was required by me, under section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. R. P. Noyel Dasantha
Steewan, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha,  Returning  Officer of  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. R. P . Noyel Dasantha
Steewan, elected as  a member  of   the   Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle.
12th September, 2013.
