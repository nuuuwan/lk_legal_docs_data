THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub - section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Katuwahena depicted in the Advance
Tracing Number MO/BDL/2012/15 dated 10th August, 2012 made
by the Surveyor-General situated in the village of Pussellawa (F.V.P.
245) in Kadukara Korale Minor Divison in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Badalkumbura in Monaragala District Uva Province and
containing of total extent of about 0.0379 Hectare and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Katuwahena – Extent: About 0.0379 Hectare
North: Katuwahena land claimed by D. M. Gunapala, Road
(R.D.A) and balance portion of the same land claimed by Pathiranage
Roslin Perera; East and South: Balance portion of the same land
claimed by Pathiranage Roslin Perera;West: Balance portion of the
same land claimed by Pathiranage Roslin Perera and Katuwahena
land claimed by D. M. Gunapala.
