WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Rules under Sections 7(1) - (2) and 30 of the Western Province Provincial Road Passenger Transport Services  Statute
No. 1 of 1992 and Sections 5-6 of the Western Province Provincial Road Passenger Transport Services
(amendment) Statute  No. 3 of 1993
ACCORDING to the authority and Provisions delegated to me by the 7(1) - (2)  and 30 of the  Western Province Provincial
Road Passenger Transport  Services Statute No. 1 of 1992 and Sections 5 and 6 of the Western Province Provincial Road
Passenger Transport Services  (amendment) Statute No. 3 of 1993, and in order to provide more efficient services to the
passengers who are travelling in the private buses that are in the transportation services within the Western Province, I
notify that the regulations formulated are as follows.
H. M. U
PALI KODIKARA,
Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs,
Art and Cultural Affairs, Co-operative Development,
Food Supply and Distribution and Rural Development.
01st November, 2013.
The following regulations are introduced and implemented within the Western Province from 01st of November 2013
on the usage of modern technology in order to regulate the transportation services of private buses that are in services with
the Western Province.
ENFORCEMENT  OF  REGULATIONS
* Facilities shall be provided to use electronic ticket issuing machines, which have obtained the approval of
the Authority, in the buses, in order to facilitate the usage of prepaid cards by the passengers.
* GPS technology, which has obtained the approval of the Authority, shall be fixed in the buses enabling the
substitution of modern technology in order to facilitate efficient bus transportation services.
* The driver and the conductor of the bus (employees) shall be included to a social security pension scheme
which is approved by the Authority,
