THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0119 Hectare
described as following lot in Advance Tracing No. NU/NWE/2013/
59 of 08.05.2013 prepared by Surveyor-General situated in the Village
of Nagasena, Dimbula Korale Minor Division, Nuwara Eliya
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Nuwara Eliya District in Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Tillcoultry Estate – Extent: About 0.0119 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and H. P . 67360 Tillcoultry Estate; East:
H. P . 67360 Tillcoultry Estate; South and West: Road (R.D.A.).
