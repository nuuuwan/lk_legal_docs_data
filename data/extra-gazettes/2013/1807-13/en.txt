FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
BALAPITIYA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a Vice Chairman in Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which Ajantha
Gunarathna De Silva  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me to decide upon the  member of that Balapitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30)
days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Waradana Sarath Samarajeewa De Silva  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare    Mr. Waradana Sarath Samarajeewa De Silva elected as  Vice Chairman of   the Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Balapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
17th April, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE
BALAPITIYA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which Ajantha
Gunarathna De Silva  the Member belonged, was required by me to nominat  a person eligible as a  member of that Balapitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated Mr.  Agampodi Cyril De Silva
to being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare   Mr. Agampodi Cyril De Silva  elected as  a Member of   the Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Balapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
17th April, 2013.
