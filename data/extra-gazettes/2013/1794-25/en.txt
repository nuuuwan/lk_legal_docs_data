THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been named as “Reverend Muruththettuwe
Ananda Nahimi Mawatha” by the  Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable
Minister in charge of the Subject of Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947
(Chapter 252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing
No. 12 of 1989.
J. M. B. J
AYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
1. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
2. District - Colombo
3. Present Name of the Street - Timbirigasyaya Road
4. New Name of the Street - ‘‘Reverend Muruththettuwe Ananda Nahimi Mawatha’’
5. Road Description - This road Starting from between assessment numbers 01 and 02
Thimbirigasyaya Road and runs through Thimbirigasyaya Municipal
Ward, Havelock Town Municipal Ward, Kirula Municipal Ward and ends
between assessment Nos. 521/15 and 542 Thimbirigasyaya Road.
