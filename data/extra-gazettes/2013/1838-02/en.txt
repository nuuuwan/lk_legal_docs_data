Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  KATUWANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Katuwana Pradeshiaya Sahba  has fallen vacant by reason  of Death :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Frdeedom Allinace, the recognised political party to which
Ariyadasa Weerasinghe Gamaarachchige the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Katuwana Pradeshya Sabha
who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated. Mr. Sarambage Sugathadasa
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sarambage
Sugathadasa elected as a member of the  Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
15th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  KATUWANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman in Katuwana Pradeshiaya Sahba  has fallen vacant by reason of Death :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which Ariyadasa
Weerasinghe Gamaarachchige the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
decide upon the member of that Katuwana Pradeshya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has  Communicated his decision nominate
Mr. Imbulahitiya Gamachchi Mahinda to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Imbulahitiya
Gamachchi Mahinda elected as Chairman of the  Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
15th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  RATTOTA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman in Rattota Pradeshiaya Sahba  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Central Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Frdeedom Allinace, the recognised political party to which
Thalgaswatte Don Jayatissa the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that Rattota Pradeshya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. S. K. Jaynatha Pushpakumara  to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. S. K.
Jaynatha Pushpakumara elected as a member of the  Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
18th  November, 2013.
