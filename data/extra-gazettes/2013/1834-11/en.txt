PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy  in the North Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS  the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Central Province
Provincial Council by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate and eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Punchi Banda Disanayaka
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Punchi Banda Disanayaka, elected as a member of the
above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Anuradhapura in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
24th October 2013.
