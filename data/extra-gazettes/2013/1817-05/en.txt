EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
PADA VISIRIPURA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Padavisiripura  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of three months without permission  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United People’s Freedom Alliance , the recognised political party  to which
Muthunayaka  Gedara Nimal Thissa the member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a  member of
that  Padavisiripura  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period
of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Ranthirange Sarath
Heman Marasinha   being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nalaka  Rathnayaka,  Returning Officer of  Padavisiripura Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
Mr. Ranthirange Sarath Heman Marasinha  elected as  a member of   the Padavisiripura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
 NALAKA RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Padavisiripura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
24th  June, 2013.
