FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  BADULLA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Badulla  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Lekamlage Supun Pushpa Kumara Dasanayake,  the member  belonged, was required by me,  to nominate a person     as  a
member of  that  Badulla  Municipal Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Weerasingha Mudiyanselage
Rathnapala  being an eligible person  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of  Badulla Municipal
Council,  do hereby declare Mr. Weerasingha Mudiyanselage Rathnapala, elected as  a member  of   the Badulla Municipal
Council.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Badulla  Municipal Council.
Badulla ,
28th  May 2013.
