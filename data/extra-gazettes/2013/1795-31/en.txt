THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,    No. 2   OF   1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of  the  powers vested in me under Section 2 of  the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2  of
1980,  I,  Mahinda Rajapaksa,  President of  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and  the  Minister- in - charge of  the
subject of Urban Development, being of  opinion that the lands specified in the schedule  hereto is urgently required for the
Development of  Delkanda  Pola, do by this  Order declare that the said land  is required for such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
 28th January 2013.
SCHEDULE
One  allotment of land shown as in  Plan No. 2012/155 dated 07.12.2012 prepared by the  Licensed Surveyor
I. T. Madola containing in extent  of  01A. 03R. 29.51P  situated in the village of Gangodawila in the  Grama Niladari Division of
526 D Jambugasmulla, within the limits of  Maharagama Divisional Secretariat in the District of   Colombo, Western Province.
Boundaries of the said  land are as  follows :
North by - Road  way to the houses ;
East by - Old Kesbewa Road ;
South by - Land claimed by A. Dayananda
West by - Road and land claimed by  Mr. Rajapakse V . S. Perera, Y . Sisirakumara and P. Munasinghe.
