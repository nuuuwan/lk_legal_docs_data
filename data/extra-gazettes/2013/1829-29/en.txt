L. D. B. 5/2007.
ELECTRONIC TRANSACTIONS ACT, No. 19 OF 2006
Order under Section 1(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 1(2) of the
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Telecommunication and
Information Technology do by this Order, appoint 23rd of
September, 2013 to be the date on which the provisions of
Section 18 and Section 20(1) of the said Act shall come into
operation.
R
ANJITH SIY AMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of  Telecommunication and
Information Technology.
Colombo,
23rd September, 2013.

L. D. B. 5/2007.
ELECTRONIC TRANSACTIONS ACT, No. 19 OF 2006
Order under Section 18
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 18 of the
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Telecommunication and
Information Technology, being in charge of the subject of
Information and Communication Technology, do by this Order
designate the Information and Communication Technology
Agency of Sri Lanka (ICTA) registered under the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and recognized under the Information and
Communication Technology Act, No. 27 of 2003, as the
Certification Authority for the purposes of Act, No. 19 of
2006 referred to above.
R
ANJITH SIY AMBALAPITIYA,
Minister of  Telecommunication and
Information Technology.
Colombo,
23rd September, 2013.
