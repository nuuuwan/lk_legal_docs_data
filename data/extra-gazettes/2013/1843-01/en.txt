THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0340 Hectare,
depicted  in the Advance Tracing No. KU/WRY/2010/269  of
21.11.2011 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Thalakolawewa (F.V .P 2692), within Sub Division of Medagandahe
Korale in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wariyapola in
Kurunegala  District in North Western  Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ambagahamula Waththa – Extent:  0.0303
Hectare
North: Canal; East: Balance portion of this land; South:Road;West:
Tank and Tank embankment Lots  ‘C’, ‘B’,  Ambaghamula Waththa
claimed by G . H. Samara and Balance portion of this Land.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Ambagahamula Waththa – Extent:  0.0037
Hectare
North: Ambagamula Waththa claimed by G . H. Podiya and Lot
‘A’; East: Lot ‘C’; South: Lot ‘C’, Tank and Tank embankment and
balance of portion of this land; West: Balance portion of this Land.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: LD/05/2009/IR/252.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: WARI/3/12/5/3.
