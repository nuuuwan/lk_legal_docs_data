THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: bvï/ 01/17.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Allotments of below name, land in total extent of about 1.9760
Hectares, shown in Final Village Plan No. 520, Additional No. 27,
Paper No. 29,  prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Survey-
Hambantota Division on behalf of Surveyor - General, situated in
the Village of Palleberagama in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 123
- Mirijjawila in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Hambantota in
Hambantota District in Southern Province:
01. Lot No. 1677
Name of Land : Palleberagamakele; Description: Garden, contains
five Wormwood trees; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Gunapala
Muthumala, No. 19, Wijepara, Medaketiya, Tangalle; Extent: 1.8661
Hectares.
02. Lot No. 1678
Name of Land : Palleberagamakele; Description: Garden, contains
one Wormwood tree; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Sri Lanka
Mahaweli Authority; Extent: 0.0598 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 1679
Name of Land : Palleberagamakele; Description: Garden, contains
one Wormwood tree; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Sri Lanka
Mahaweli Authority; Extent: 0.0501 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at  Divisional Secretary’s Office Hambantota on the 12th day of
June, 2013 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of June, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. S. N. De Z Siriwardana,
Divisional Secretary,
Hambantota.
 Divisional Secretariat,
Hambantota.
09th April, 2013.
