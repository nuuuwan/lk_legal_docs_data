PROCLAMATION  BY  THE  GOVERNOR  OF  THE  CENTRAL  PROVINCE
KNOW Ye that by virtue of powers vested by the article 154 (b):(8):(b) of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the Central Province, do hereby
this Notice prorogue the First Session of the Sixth Council of the Central Province with effect from 06th December, Two
Thousand Thirteen.
  By virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph 8(a) of the said Article, I, do hereby summon the said Council
to meet at the Central Province Provincial Council Meeting Hall at Pallekelle, Kundasale on Wednesday the 29th January, Two
Thousand Fourteen at 10.00 a.m.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUW A,
Governor of the Central Province.
Given at Kandy, this 05th day of December, Two Thousand Thirteen.
