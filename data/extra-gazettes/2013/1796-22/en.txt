Regulations
The Motor Traffic (Fees) Regulations 2011, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1722/27 of September 09, 2011
are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of paragraph (a) of regulations 3 thereof and the substitution therefore of the following :-
“3. (a) Where an application for the first registration of a motor vehicle referred to in regulation 2 is made after
the expiration of ninety calendar days or more from the date of —
1. importation of the motor vehicle ;
2. purchase of motor vehicle manufactured or assembled or manufactured and assembled locally, as the
case may be, of such motor vehicle ;
3. auction of the unregistered vehicles belonging to the State or to a Semi-Governmental Organization or to
the Sri Lanka Customs ; or
4. sale of motor vehicle by the dealers,
the fee payable for the registration of that motor vehicle when it belongs to such category as specified in Column
I of Schedule II to these regulations, shall, depending on the period of the delay as referred to in the corresponding
entry in Column II thereof, be the fee specified in the corresponding entry in Column III of that Schedule.”;

(2) by the repeal of regulation 6 thereof and the substitution therefore of the following :-
“6. (1) The fee payable for the registration of a person as a new owner of a motor vehicle, on normal, priority
or one day basis for the categories specified in Column I of Schedule III to these regulations, shall be as specified
in the corresponding entry of Column II, III and IV of that Schedule ;
(2) Where an application for registration is not submitted within fourteen days of the date of change of
possession of the motor vehicle, on normal, priority or one day basis, an additional charge of one hundred rupees
for each foregone day shall be charged along with the fee payable ;
(3) Upon the death of a registered owner of the motor vehicle an application for registration shall be submitted
on normal basis within one hundred and eighty days after the change of possession of the motor vehicle. An
additional sum of one hundred rupees for each foregone day shall be charged along with the fee payable ;
(4) Upon the sale of vehicle belonging to the State or to a Semi-Governmental Organization or to the Sri Lanka
Customs an application for registration shall be submitted on normal basis within one hundred and eighty days
after such sale. An additional sum of one hundred rupees for each foregone day shall be charged along with the
fee payable.”;
(3) by the repeal of regulation 8 thereof and the substitution therefore of the following :-
“8. The fee payable for -
(a) the entry in the register of the name of any person as absolute owner on -
(i) normal basis shall be thousand rupees ;
(ii) priority basis one thousand five hundred rupees ; and
(iii) one day basis two thousand rupees ; and
(b) the deletion from the entry in the register of the name of an absolute owner on -
(i) normal basis shall be five hundred rupees ;
(ii) priority basis seven hundred and fifty rupees ; and
(iii) one day basis one thousand two hundred and fifty rupees.”.
(4) by the repeal of regulation 11 and the substitution of following :-
“11. The annual revenue license fee payable in respect of -
(a) motor cycles shall be seven hundred rupees ;
(b) motor tricycle and motor tricycle vans, shall be seven hundred and fifty rupees ;
(c) vehicles propelled by electrical means, solar energy, liquid petroleum gas or alternative fuel shall be fifty
per centum of the fee payable for a petrol motor vehicle of a similar class and of similar weight as
specified in Columns I and II of Schedule V to these regulations ; and
(d) vehicles propelled by a petroleum product together with any electrical, solar energy, liquid petroleum
gas or alternative fuel means, shall be the same as the annual revenue license fee payable for a motor
vehicle of similar classes and of similar weight as specified in Schedule V to these regulations.”;
