2A
 THE   CONSTITUTION   OF    THE    DEMOCRATIC    SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC    OF    SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (1)(a) of
Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1681/3 of November 22, 2010 and amended by time to time is further amended with effect from October 15, 2013 in the
manner set out in the Schedule below
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo 01,
Presidential Secretariat,
15th  October 2013.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the heading, Minister of Ports and Highways (No. 3) of the said Notification, as follows :-
(a) the heading as “(3) Minister of  Highways, Ports and Shipping” ;
(2) With reference to the heading, Minister of Co-operatives and Internal Trade (No. 47) of the said Notification, as
follows :-
(a) in column III thereof, by the addition immediately after the item which read as “Paddy Marketing Board
Act” of the following item :-
“Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (Incorporation) Act, No 41 of 1980 ” ;
(3) With reference to the heading, Minister of Telecommunication and Information Technology (No. 61) of the said
Notification, as follows :-
(a) in column III thereof, by the addition immediately after the item which read as “Information and Communi-
cation Technology Act, No. 27 of 2003” of the following item :-
“Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006”
