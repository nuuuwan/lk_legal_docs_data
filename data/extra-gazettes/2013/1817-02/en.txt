1A
THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT , No. 02 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka and  Minister-in-charge of the
subject of  Urban Development, being of the opinion that the lands specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for
Multi Story Car Park cum Commercial Urban Development Project, do by this Order, declare that the said land is required for
such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
 Presidential Secretariat,
 Colombo 01,
26th June, 2013.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land depicted as Lot 1 in PP Co. 5257 dated 21.03.1980 prepared by Surveyor General, containing in
extent of  00A.02R. 14.78P situated in the Ward No. 20 in Fort within Colombo Municipal Council, Colombo Divisional
Secretariat in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
