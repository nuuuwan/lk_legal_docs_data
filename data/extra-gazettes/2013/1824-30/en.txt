WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute,
No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council do hereby announce that the inquiry period will be extended from 09th August to
9th November of the Single Person Tribunal comprising retired Judge of  Court  of Appeal Hon. B. L. K. Wimalachandra
appointed on 21st May 2013 by the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No.1811/4 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
as per the powers entrusted to me by the provisions of Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council to inquire and submit a report whether
the  Councilor of the Maharagama Urban Council, Hewa Viruduge Nishantha Wimalachandra,  while  functioning in the said
office, has made the offences stated under Section 2(1) of the aforementioned statute.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief Minister and  the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Education,  Land,  Local Government, Provincial Administration, Electricity and
Power,  Manpower and Employment, Economic Promotion
of the Western Province Provincial Council.
Western Province.
Chief Ministry of the Western Provincial Council,
