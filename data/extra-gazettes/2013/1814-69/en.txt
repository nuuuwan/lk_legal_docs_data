Notice Under the Lcoal Authorities Elections Ordinance
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
THISSAMAHARAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Abeysingha Liyana Aarachchige
Kumara Rupasingha of the Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his Office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as
he has ceased to be a member of the People’s Liberation Front.
W. H. R. Wijaya Kumara,
Returning Officer,
Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota
05th June, 2013.
