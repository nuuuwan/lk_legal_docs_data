EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  KANTALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Kantale  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of vacation of membership;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Hetiarachchilage Chandana  Hettiarachchi  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Kantale   Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage
Wijerathna  being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nalaka Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Kantale  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section
65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.  Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage
Wijerathna elected as  a member of   the Kantale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
N
ALAKA RATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Kantale   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
22nd  November 2013.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  PANNALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in  Pannala  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member of
the North Western Province Provincial Council;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Asanka Sudhira
Perera Mallawa Arachchige   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A (2) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible  under the ordinance for Election as a  member of that Pannala   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated  Mr. Mohomad Sali Mohomad
Anas  being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Pannala  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting  in  terms of Section 65 A (2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Mohomad Sali Mohomad Anas elected as  a member of   the  Pannala   Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pannala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
21st  November 2013.
