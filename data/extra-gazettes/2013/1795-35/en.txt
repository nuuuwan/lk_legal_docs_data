L.D.B. 24/51(III).
THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT (CHAPTER 203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 read with Section 19 of the Motor Traffic Act, (Chapter 203).
KUMARA WELGAMA,
Minister of Transport.
Colombo,
30th January, 2013.
Regulations
1. The Motor Traffic (Constructions of Vehicles ) Regulations 1983, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 248 of 03rd June,
1983 are hereby amended as follows :–
(a) by the renumbering of regulation 57 as regulation 58 thereof ;
(b) by the addition of the following regulation immediately after regulation 56 thereof -
"57. The body of the vehicles listed below shall be painted as follows :–
(1) Public Passenger Transport Vehicles used for the carriage of passengers for a fee or reward shall –
(a) be painted in Light Blue using PANTONE COLOUR CODE No. 2707 C ; and
(b) display the name and the address of the owner in font size 60x35mm on the right side and left side rear end of
the body and it shall be dispalyed 500 mm above the bottom end of the body and end at 1,000mm from the rear
end of the body ;
(2) Omnibuses operated and maintained by the Sri Lanka Transport Board shall be painted in Red using PANTONE
COLOUR CODE No. 185 C ; and
(3) Vehicles used for School Transportation for a fee or reward shall be painted in Yellow using PANTONE COLOUR
CODE No. 1375 C.".
2. These regulations shall come into operation from 01st February, 2013 for unregistered vehicles and from 01st January, 2014
for registered vechicles.
