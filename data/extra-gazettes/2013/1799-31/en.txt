THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act
No. 37 of 1968 dated 08.03.2012 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist  Republic  of Sri Lanka Extra-ordinary
No.1750/06 dated 19/03/2012   the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter  specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated
28/02/2012  between,
Ceylon Estates Staff Union, No. 06, Aloe Avenue, Colombo 03 of the one part
and
1. Sri Lanka State Plantations Corporation, No. 28, Mudalige Mawatha, Colombo 01 and 2. Superintendent, Galpihilla State
Plantation, Panwila of the other part
was referred to Mr. M. D. K. Kulatunga for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do
hereby revoke the said post Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated  08/03/2012.
GAMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo,
18th February, 2013.
