The Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: BD/4/7/104 
Reference Number of the Ministry.: LD/05/2007/D/220. 
 
I, hereby give notice, to the public under Sub-section  
(1) the of section (7) of the Land Acquisition Act, the 
land described below is required by the government and 
the government intends to acquire the said land for 
constructing the official residence for the Superintendent 
of Police of Badulla. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired : 
 
The land of 1.4348 hectares in extent stated as block 
No. 842, in the final village plan of No. B/170. prepared 
by the Senior Survey Superintendent, situated in the 
Wadiguna village of 80 H Glen Alpin Grama Niladhari 
Division, coming under Rilpola Korale in Badulla 
Divisional Secretary Division of Badulla District in the 
Province of Uva. 
 
Details of the Land:  
 
Plan Number: B 170; Name of the Land: Narangolla 
Pathana, Galkande Pathana and GlenAlpin Estate Field Number 
11; Present land use: Scrub land, abandoned foot path and 
eleven Turpentine trees around 15-25 years old; Claimant of the 
land: Balangoda Plantation Limited.   
Boundaries of the Land: 
 
 North :  Block Number 88 and 14A 30;  
 East :   Block Number 14A 30;  
 South :  Block Number 88 and 14A 30, 89;  
 West :  Block Number 89 and 88.  
 
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other 
connection to aforesaid land, s hould appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Badulla on 12th August 2013 at 2.30 p.m. In 
addition, her of by requested to notify to me in writing with two 
copies of the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars 
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
computation of such amount before 06th of August, 2013. 
  
                         GAMINI MAHINDAPALA JOPHEUS, 
                       Divisional Secretariat, 
                      Badulla. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Badulla.
