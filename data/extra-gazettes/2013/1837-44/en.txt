EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  ALAWWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the North Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which B.R.
Rohana Chandra Kumara Basnayake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of that Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Patabandi Wasam Baduge
Gamini Wijayananda Senadheera being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Alawwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Patabandi Wasam Baduge Gamini Wijayananda Senadheera elected as a member of the  Alawwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Alawwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala District,
12th  November, 2013.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  RATTOTA CPRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the Central Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Thalgaswatte Don Jayathissa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of that Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Ajith Kumara Bandara
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Rattota  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ajith
Kumara Bandara elected as a member of the  Rattota  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Rattota  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale District,
14th  November, 2013.
