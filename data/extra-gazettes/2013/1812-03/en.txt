Rescinding again the statutory determination No. 4324 published under Section 19
THE Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1181/19 dated 25th April 2001, by which the
statutory determination published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 527/13
dated 14th October 1988 under No. 4324 in relation to the declaration of unique No. Ra/106 of Mr.Hatanachchi Mohottiralalage
Mudiyanse residing at Malwatta, Godawela has been rescinded, is hereby rescinded.
I do hereby declare that the statutory determination published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 527/13 dated 14th
October 1988 under No. 4324 shall be valid with the cancellation of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1181/19 dated 25th April
2001.
