EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
GODAKAWELA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
WHEREAS  the office of a Member in Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which L. H.
Dharmasiri  the Member belonged, was required by me to nominate a person eligible as a member  of that Godakawela
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated Mr.  Medagangodage
Senarath Dimuthu Gunarathna being an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning Officer of  Godakawela Pradeshiya
Sabha, do hereby declare Mr. Medagangodage Senarath Dimuthu Gunarathna elected as a  Member of   the Godakawela
Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
30th  April, 2013.
