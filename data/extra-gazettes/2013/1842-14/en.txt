FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  MEERIGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of Chairman  in Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason Resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Appuhamilage Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Meerigama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Ranatunga Arachchige Sarath Nandasiri Ranatunga fo fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne,  Returning Officer of  Meerigama  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ranatunga
Arachchige Sarath Nandasiri Ranatunga elected as Chairman of the  Meerigama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Meerigama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
24th  December, 2013.
