THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three (3) allotments of land  in total extent of about 0.6496  Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MAHA/HRS/2012/551, dated
26th February, 2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in
the Village of Kondadeniya in Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor
Division in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Harispattuwa in
Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dingiriyadeniya Kumbura – Extent: About
0.2692 Hectare
North: Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by State, Dingiriyadeniya
Kumbura claimed by Walala Raja Maha Viharaya and Ela; East: Ela;
South: Ela, Lot B and Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by State;West:
Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by State and Dingiriyadeniya Kumbura
claimed by Walala Raja Maha Viharaya.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Dingiriyadeniya Kumbura – Extent: About
0.2559 Hectare
North: Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by State, Lot A and Ela;
East: Ela and Lot C; South: Ela, Lot C and Dingiriyadeniya Hena
claimed by State;West: Lot C and Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by
State.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Dingiriyadeniya Kumbura – Extent: About
0.1245 Hectares
North: Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by State, Lot B and Ela;
East: Ela and Lot D; South: Ela, Lot D and Dingiriyadeniya Hena
claimed by State;West: Lot D, Dingiriyadeniya Hena claimed by
State and Lot  B.
