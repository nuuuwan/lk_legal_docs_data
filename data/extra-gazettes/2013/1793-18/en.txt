No. 1793/18 – THURSDAY, JANUARY 17, 2013
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: Meega/3/Ath/155.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired: PP Co 9125
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of about
0.0133 Hectare, depicted as Lot Nos. 1, 2, 3 in the Preliminary Plan
No. Gam 3888 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys, Gampaha
District for the Surveyor-General, situated in the village called
Basiyawatta within Negombo Municipal Council  Limit in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Negombo in the District of
Gampaha in Western Province and described as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Kadolgahawatta, a part of Assmt. No. 30, Thalahena
Road; Description: Home Garden; Claimant: M. Raman Fernando,
Basiyawatta, Thalahena, Negombo; Extent: 0.0021 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of Land: Kadolgahawatta, a part of Assmt. No. 35, Thalahena
Road; Description: Open waste land; Claimant: Joseph Cashiyas
Fernando, Basiyawatta, Thalahena, Negombo; Extent: 0.0107 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of Land: Kadolgahawatta, a part of Assmt. No. 30A,
Thalahena Road; Description: Open waste land; Claimant:
David Fernando, Basiyawatta, Thalahena, Negombo; Extent:
0.0005 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Negombo, on the 21st day of March,
2013 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing  (in duplicate) on or before the 13th day of March, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
A. K. R. Alawatta,
Divisional Secretary,
Negombo.
Divisional Secretariat,
Negombo.
16th January, 2013.
