Provincial  Councils  Notifications
WESTERN     PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Inquiry held under the Section 2 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities
Statute, No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council do hereby appoint the retired Judge of High Court Hon. Dudley Karunarathne as
Single Person Tribunal of Inquiry as per the powers entrusted to me by the provisions of the Section 2(2) of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council, to
inquire and submit a report within 03 months based on the following matters/allegations, whether the Kelaniya Pradeshiya
Sabha, in the course of its functioning, has made the offences stated under Section 2(1) of the aforementioned Statute.
Matters/allegations to be inquired about :
(a) In the course of its functioning, the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha committing the offences stated in Section
2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(d) of the Powers of Suprevision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute, No.
04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by allowing the weekly fair of Kiribathgoda owned
by the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha to be maintained without a contract to the person named K. G. D.
Thilakasiri of No. 162/2, Dalupitiya, Kadawatha for the time period of 01st August, 2011 to 31st December
2011 and thereby acting contrary to Section 173 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
(b) In the course of its functioning the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha committing the offence/offences stated in
Section 2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(d) of the Powers of Suprevision of the Administration of Local Authorities
Statute, No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by adopting on 29th July 2011 Rupees
Four Hundred Thousand as the minimum bid to a sales outlet at the Trade Complex of Hunupitiya owned
by the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha without calling tenders to  the previous adoption by the same Pradeshiya
Sabha adopted on 30th November 2010 wherein it had been adopted that the minimum bid for a sales
outlet at the aforesaid market complex to be Rupees One Million Two Hundred Thousand.
(c) In the course of its functioning, the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha committing the offence/offences stated
in Section 2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(d) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities
Statute, No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council by taking action to call tenders having
adopted without proper estimation Rupees Four Hundred Thousand as the minimum bid per one sales
outlet applicable for sixteen such sales outlets of Hunupitiya Trade Complex owned by the Kelaniya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
