EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A    MEMBER   OF  THE
JAFFNA   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Jaffna Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of the
Northern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognised political party  to which Karthigesu
Nadarasa Kanagarathinam  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Jaffna Municipal Council  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Subramaniyam Sothilingam
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Subramaniyam Sothilingam
elected as a member of the Jaffna Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
26th  November, 2013.
