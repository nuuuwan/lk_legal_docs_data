No. 1797/24 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 15, 2013
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The land called Kudamuththettuwa in extent of about
0.1288 Hectare, stated as Lot No. 01in the  Preliminary Plan
No. B/637 dated 21.01.2013 prepared by  the District Surveyor-
General, situated in the Division No. 07 Welekade coming under
Badulla Municipality in Badulla Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Badulla District in Uva Province.
North: Kudamuththettuwa claimed by V . K. Siwasubramaniyam;
East: Kudamuththettuwa claimed by V . K. Siwasubramaniyam and
Lot Nos. 01 and 02 of  Preliminary Plan No. 229, Badulla; South: Lot
Nos. 01 and 02 of Preliminary Plan No. 229, Badulla and Road;
West: Keppetipola Road.
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other connection
to aforesaid land should appear personally or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Badulla on
the 06th day of March, 2013 at 2.30 p.m.
 In addition hereby requested to notify to me before the 26th day
of February, 2013,  in writing with two copies of the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
Gamini Mahindapala Jopheus,
Divisional Secretary,
Badulla.
My Ref. No.: BD/04/07/109.
Divisional Secretariat,
Badulla,
11th February,  2013.
