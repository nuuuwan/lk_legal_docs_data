EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE   UV A - PARANAGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Uva - Paranagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which  H. M. Chamika Aruna
Kumara the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for  Election as a member of that Uva - Paranagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such  party
who shall be declared  elected as a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has nominated Mr. T. D. Nimal Jayarathna
being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of  Uva - Paranagama Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of    Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare    Mr. T. D.
Nimal Jayarathna elected as a member of the Uva - Paranagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
G. D
ILIP NISHANTHA DE SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Uva - Paranagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Badulla,
26th  December, 2012.
