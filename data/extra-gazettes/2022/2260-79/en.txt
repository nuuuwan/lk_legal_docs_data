EXCISE ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 02/2022
ANNuAL LIQuor LICENsE FEEs
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25, read with section 32 of the Excise ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended 
from time to time, I, Basil Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Excise Notification No. 1004, published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2049/4 dated December 11, 2017 by substitution of item No. 7, 8, 10, 13 and 15(ii) of 
Schedule I of that Notification with the following Schedule with effect from January 01, 2021 to March 31, 2022. 
The Excise Notification No. 02/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2208/35 dated January 01, 2021 is hereby 
rescinded.
