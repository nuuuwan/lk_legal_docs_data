WHEREAS, the office of member in Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Thennakoon 
Mudiyanselage Mahanamage Ajith Prasanna Thennakoon the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)
(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that ward No. 07 - Kede Kallanchiya of Rambewa Pradeshiya 
Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and 
who shall be declared  elected as a member within a period of Thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Saman Kumara Dissanayaka to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer of the Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dissanayaka 
Mudiyanselage Saman Kumara Dissanayaka was elected as the member of  the ward No. 07 - Kede Kallanchiya of Rambewa 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K. M
