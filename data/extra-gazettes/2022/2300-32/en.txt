WHEREAS, the office of  return member of  Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Meegoda Kankanamge 
Dona Sitha Lalitha Meegoda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mohamed Haris Noor Haseema to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Beruwala Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Mohamed Haris Noor Haseema was selected as the member of  the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
