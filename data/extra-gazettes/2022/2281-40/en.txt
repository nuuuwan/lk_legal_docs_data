Issuance of media Accreditations 2022/2023/2024 (Revision)
The  Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2259/12 dated 21st December 2021 of the Democratic Socialist republic of 
Sri Lanka has been issued under the caption “Department of Government information - issuance of media accreditation 
2022/2023/2024”. The following Sections of the said Gazette Notification will be revised as follows.
01. Basic criteria and required documents
 (ii) media accreditations issued to journalists working on contract basis for a recognized media institution are 
valid only for the year 2022. The validity period of such accreditations will end on December 31, 2022.
 (x) media accreditations issued for Websites are valid for two years (2022/2023) only. The validity period of 
those accreditations will end on December 31, 2023.

 (ii) Provincial Journalist
