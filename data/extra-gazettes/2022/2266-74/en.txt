FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   GANGA IHALA   KORALE   
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (1a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)  
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of return member of  Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being 
ceased to be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Aluth Gamage Ashara 
Sewwandi Wickramarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁed 
under Section 9 and is qualiﬁed under Section 8 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Navaratne Mudiyanselage Nandawathie to ﬁll such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Ofﬁcer  of  the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Navaratne Mudiyanselage Nandawathie was selected as the member of the  Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Dr. H. D. N. SAMPATH FERNANDO,
Returning Ofﬁcer,
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Ofﬁce,
Kandy,
10th February, 2022.
