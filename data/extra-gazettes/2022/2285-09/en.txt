ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27 (1)  of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. d. susil Premajayanth, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order published in Gazette Extraodinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 (as amended from time to time) which 
specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue 
of the provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said Act, by the 
addition immediately after the entry “Faculty of Urban and Aquatic Bioresources” appearing in Column 1 of the Schedule 
and its corresponding entries in Column 11 of that Schedule, (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2267/35 of February, 17, 2022) of the following :
 “Column I   Column II
 Faculty   Department
 Faculty of Computing 1. Department of Scientific Computing
  2. department of Information systems e ngineering and Informatics
  3. Department of Knowledge Engineering and Communication ”.
