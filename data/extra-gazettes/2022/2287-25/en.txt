Rule 11 - Minimum and Fractional Charges
 
 11(a) - The minimum distance in calculating the charges for the conveyance of goods will be 100km.
 11(b) - Calculating the minimum charge for wagon.
 11(b) (i) - rs. 3,000.00 for a four-wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 13.20 MT.
 11(b) (ii) - rs. 4,000.00 for a four - wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 13.20 MT.
 11(b) (iii) - rs. 5,000.00 for a four - wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 13.20 MT.
 11(b) (iv) - rs. 6,000.00 for a four - wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 13.20 MT.
Rule 11(c)
 (i) The minimum weight is 10 MT for an eight-wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 13.20 MT,
 (ii) The minimum weight is 20 MT for an eight-wheeled wagon with the loading capacity not exceeding 26.40 MT,
 (iii) The minimum weight is 25 MT for an eight-wheeled wagon with the loading capacity exceeding 26.40 MT and not 
exceeding 39.60 MT,
 (iv) The minimum weight is 35 MT for an eight-wheeled wagon with the loading capacity exceeding 39.60 MT,
Rule 23 - Demurrage on Wagons
Rule 23(a)
  The loading of wagons at all the railway stations should be completed within eight hours from the time on which they 
are supplied. any consignor who orders a wagon and fails to load within the prescribed time or any consignor who fails to 
furnish the consignment notes even he completed the loading is liable to a demurrage charge and should pay a demurrage 
charge of  rs. 2,500.00 per a four-wheeled or eight-wheeled wagon pera working hour or a part thereof.
