WHEREAS the office of member of Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the, Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Amarasinghe 
Arachchige Sudath Chandrasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 16 - Koholwila of Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate Bu-
lathwelage Ashoka Kumara Rodrigo to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Bulathwelage Ashoka Kumara Rodrigo was elected as the member of the Ward Number 16-Koholwila of Kelaniya Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
K. J. S. Madhawa,
Returning Officer,
