WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Muncipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  
being ceased to be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kengalle Gedara 
Dhammika Chandrarathna the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as 
a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Konara Mudiyanselage Herath Bandara Medagama to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, returning officer of the Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte 
Municipal Council, acting in terms of section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Konara Mudiyanselage Herath Bandara Medagama was selected as the member of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte 
Municipal Council.
