NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT, No. 2 OF 1974
Order Under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 2 of 
1974, I, Dr. Keheliya Rambukwella, Minister of Water Supply, do hereby approve the acquisition of the servitude right of 
the allotments of land described in the following schedule as it is required by the said Board, for Transmission Pipe Lines 
running from Akurana Storage Tank and Pump House up to Dippitiya Pump House of Kandy North Pathadumbara Integrated 
Water Supply Project.
 Dr. K
EHELIYA RAMBUKWELLA,
