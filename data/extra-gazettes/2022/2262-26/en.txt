Order 
1. As per section 169 of Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 amended by Local Government Authorities (special 
Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, Mr. A. s. ranathunga who functioned as the Chairman of Horana Pradeshiya sabha 
shall be considered as resigned from his respective office of Chairmanship; as Deputy Chairman of the said Pradeshiya 
Sabha shall execute powers and duties of the said vacant office of Chairman in terms of provisions of Sub-section 8(4) 
of the Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, and to prevent whatever impediment to act by Pradeshiya sabha on behalf 
of public welfare of Horana Pradeshiya sabha Authority area, this written order is made to determine the written law 
relating to it since budget document for year 2022 has not been adopted by the said Pradeshiya sabha.
2. According to provisions stated under section 169 of the Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 amended by Local 
Government Authorities special Provisions Act, No. 21 of 2012, as budget document for year 2022 has neither been 
subjected to final consideration by the said Pradeshiya Sabha nor been adopted by 31st December, 2021, it shall be 
lawful to maintain by the said Pradeshiya Sabha income and expenditure relating to maintenance of services for public 
welfare according to a budget document for the financial year 2022, prepared on behalf of the said Pradeshiya Sabha by 
the secretary of the respective Pradeshiya sabha and approved by the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government of 
Kaluthara district in which the said Pradeshiya sabha is located until the date a budget document will be prepared by the 
relevant Pradeshiya sabha as per provisions of the written law.
3. All the Provisions stated in this order shall be executed subject to provisions under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, 
No. 15 of 1987 amended by Local Government Authorities (special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012.
4. In case any inconsistency between the Sinhala, English and Tamil text of this order should rise, the Sinhala text shall 
prevail.
