WHEREAS, the office of member of Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Ahangama 
Vithanage Sarath Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that No. 09 Andugoda of Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Angulugaha Gamage Tenisan Jayathilaka to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, returning officer of the Imaduwa Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Angulugaha Gamage Tenisan Jayathilaka was elected as the member of No. 09 Andugoda of Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
 W. H. R. W
ijaya KumaRa,
