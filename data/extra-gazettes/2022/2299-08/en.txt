EXTRAORDINARY 
AMENDMENT  
 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No: - Wa/D.S./1/3/4. 
  
THE Notice published in the Extraordinary Gazette  of 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 1949/32 
on 13th January, 2016, Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 3964 
stated as Lot No. 40. It should be amended as Lot Nos . 
135 and 136.  
  
01. Lot No. 135 
Name of the Land: Muthurajawela; Description: 
Muddy land; Claimant: State;  Original Claimant: Need 
for Outer Circle Road Developement; Extent : 0.0361 
Hectare.  
 
02. Lot No. 136 
Name of the Land: Muthurajawela; Description: 
Muddy land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: I. A. D. 
Rexy Jerad Susith, No. 378/52, Kerawalapitiya, Hendala, 
Wattala; Extent : 0.0329 Hectare.  
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, Lot Nos. 
135 and 136 are hereby required to appear personally or 
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at 
Divisional Secretariat, Wattala on the 07th of October 
2022 at 10.00 a.m. and  are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th October 
2022 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land.,  the amount of compensation 
and the datails of the computation of such amount.  
 
 
 P.D.T.C. R
AJIKA, 
 Divisional  Secretary, 
 Wattala. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Wattala Divisional Secretary's Office,
