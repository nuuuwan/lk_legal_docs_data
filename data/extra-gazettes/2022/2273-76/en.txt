   2. The validity period of all  Driving Licences issued by the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic under Part VII 
of the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203) -
 (a) Which is due to expire, on a particular date which shall commence from April 1, 2022 and end on June 30, 
2022 as specified is such Driving Licence, shall hereby be extended for six (6) months from the date of such 
expiry ;
 (b) which is due to expire, on a particular date which shall commence from July 1, 2022 and end on September 
30, 2022 as specified in such Driving Licence, shall hereby be extended for three (3) months from the date of 
such expiry.
   3. These Regulations shall not apply to a Driving Licence of which the validity period has already been extended by 
the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving License) Regulations No. 1 of 2021 published in 
Gazette Extraodinary No. 2221/57 of April 1, 2022, as amended by Regulations published in Gazette Extraodinary No.2247/17 
of September 29, 2021.
