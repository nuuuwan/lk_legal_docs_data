STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the request made by the Trade Union to increase the salaries of the employees of Ceylon Leather products 
Manufacturers Ltd by 20% for 2018, 15% for 2019 and 15% for 2020 and to pay them arrears of salaries is just and if so, to 
what relief, in what percentage, from which date are they entitled..
