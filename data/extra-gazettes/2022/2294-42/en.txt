the sri Lanka Ports authority acting by virtue and in terms of powers vested under the section 42a (1) of the sri Lanka 
Ports Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 as amended by Act, No. 07 of 1984 hereby certifies that S. S. Shipping (Private)

from the said s. s. shipping (Private) Limited, to the said sri Lanka Ports authority a sum of rupees twenty Million Four 
hundred twenty two thousand and Nine hundred and eight and cents seventy One (rs. 20,422,908.71) only.
