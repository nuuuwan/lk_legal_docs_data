LAND  REFORM  COMMISSION
 Land reforms act no. 01 of 1972 
ORDER UNDER SECTION 27A(1) 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27A (1) of the Land Reforms Act No. 01 of 1972 amended by the Act 
Nos. 39 of 1975, 14 of 1981, 39 of 1981 and 18 of 1986, I, Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and Lands, do hereby 
alienate allotments of agricultural land which are vested in the Land Reforms Commission under the provisions stipulated 
in the aforesaid act, to the Urban Housing Development Authority established by the Act No. 36 of 2008 by way of sale in 
accordance with the Section 22(1) bb of the said Land Reforms Act.
 (1) Land scheduled below are vested in the said Urban Housing Development Authority for a sum of Rs. 60,000,000 
(Rupees Sixty Million) as the monetary consideration for the aforesaid sale.
