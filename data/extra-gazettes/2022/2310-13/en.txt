uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978 
 
Order under section 27(1) read with section 21 and section 139
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,  I,  dr. A. d. susil Premajayantha, 
minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule to 
the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as amended from time to time), which specifies 
the Faculties and the departments of the university of ruhuna, sri Lanka which is deemed by  virtue of the provisions of 
section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately 
after the entry “4. department of mechanical and manufacturing engineering” appearing in column II of the schedule, which 
corresponds to the item “Faculty of Engineering” appearing in Column I of that Schedule (specified in Order published in 
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1567/23 of september 19, 2008) of the following new entry : -
“5. department of marine engineering and Naval Architecture”
