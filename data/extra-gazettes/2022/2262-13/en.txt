Department  of   local    Government,   northern   province
notice for meeting under the local authorities elections (amendment) act, no. 22 of 2012     
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue of powers vested to me 
under Sub section 66(G) of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, hereby notify to convene 
the meeting for the election to fill the vacancy of Vice Chairman of Karainagar Pradeshiya Sabha in Northern Province on 
26.01.2022 at 10.00 a. m. at Hall of the Karainagar Pradeshiya Sabha.
