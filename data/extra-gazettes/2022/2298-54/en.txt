The Esala Perahera Festival of historic Sankhapala Raja maha Viharaya - 2022
atteNtiON of the General public is drawn to the regulations relating to pilgrimages in the Gazettes of the democratic 
socialist republic of sri Lanka bearing  No. 2153/15 of 12th december, 2019, No. 2193/27 of 16th september, 2020 and              
No. 2153/15 of 01st October, 2021 published. i hereby notify the following particulars relating to those regulations.
 1.  Duration of conducting the Perahera :
 •  I hereby notify the General Public that the Esala Perahera Festival of Historic Sankhapala Raja Maha 
Viharaya will start on the 26th day of september, and end on the 13th day of October, 2022.
 * the kap planting ceremony 26.09.2022
 * pirith Chanting of thirty hours 05.10.2022
 * alms giving and worship of Chethya at night 06.10.2022
 * Kumbal perahera Festival 07.10.2022
 * salu perahera Festival 08.10.2022
 * randoli perahera Festival 09.10.2022,
 * Ginisilu perahera Festival and
  the festival of fire-walking 10.10.2022
 * maha perahera Festival 11.10.2022
 * the Cutting of Water and Gammadu shanthikarma 12.10.2022
 * alms giving to the deity at dawn
  and the end of the esala perahera Festival 13.10.2022
 2. The Camp Area :
 •  The camp area where the Esala Festival to be conducted shall be the area bounded to the North by the 
sankhapala peak, to the south by the ‘rakwana’ river, to the east by perahera mawatha and to the West by 
perahera mawatha.
 3. Vehicles :
 3.1 No vehicle, including motor vehicles, bicycles, and carts , without my permission or a permission given by 
an authorized officer, shall park or enter any place or festival premises except the area reserved for parking.
 3.2 entering vehicles to the sacred area through the gateway erected facing to ratnapura - embilipitiya main road 
is strictly prohibited.
 4. Construction of Buildings :
 4.1 No person shall construct or build any shelter or structure whether temporary or permanent or add a new part 
to such building which is already exist within the camp area during the period of time from the 26th September 
to the 13th October 2020, without my written permission or such permission obtained from an authorized 
officer.
 4.2 Whereas, it shall be permitted to enter any land or place situated in the camp area in order to implement any 
regulation, among the regulations imposed by me or any authorized officer and to erect a pillar or to conduct a 
construction or a building on any land or place within the camp area in order to display any notice or signboard.

5. Health :
5.1 No person who is suffering from any contagious or infectious disease shall enter the camp area. every person
shall thoroughly heed to follow the health instructions currently operative in the country.
5.2 Providing health services before and during the Perahera shall be entrusted to the Office of the Medical 
Officer of Health- Udawalawa, District Hospital- Embilipitiya, Base Hospital - Udawalawa and the Provincial 
Directorate of Health Services.
6. Trade :
6.1 No person shall, within the camp area, sell lotteries, flags or collect contributions for any other purpose without
my permission or the permission given by an authorized officer and sale and consumption of tobacco products 
and every kind of drugs are strictly prohibited in the premises and within the 
devalaya boundary, during the 
period of conducting the perahera.
6.2 Boutiques which are currently selling flowers, puja items within the camp area and either side along the main 
road shall thoroughly adhere to the health guidelines prevailing in the country during the period of conducting 
the 
perahera.
6.3 No person shall establish, maintain any permanent or temporary boutique within the sacred area or do business 
therein except selling flowers, puja items in the boutiques already exist either side of the access roads.
6.4 d ustbins shall be placed in every boutique and building and all refuse should be deposited therein.
6.5 All processed foods for sale shall be kept with covered so as to protect them from flies.
7. Beggars :
• No person shall beg or solicit alms, by way of money or other method, within the camp area except such places 
as may be appointed for the purpose outside the sacred area.
8. Power Supply Facilities :
• Ceylon Electricity Board and Pradeshiya Sabha- Embilipitiya Shall be responsible for the installation of street 
lamps and extra lamps on the temple premises and the Perahera route.
9. Water :
• Installation of additional water tanks providing water for bowsers and providing drinking water facilities to 
the devotees is entrusted to the National Water Supply and Drainage Board,Udawalawa, Pradeshiya Sabha, 
embilipitiya, and to the divisional secretariat embilipitiya.
10. Security :
• All security arrangements and handling traffic on the road without obstructing the Perahera and the devotees 
shall be assigned to the security forces and to the police.
