reGuLAtIoNs
1. these regulations may be cited as the Medicines (Pricing of Paracetamol tablets/Capsules 500mg) regulations, 2022.
2.	 The	maximum	retail	price	of	Paracetamol	Tablets/	Capsules	500mg	as	a	generic	name	of	the	strength	of	five	hundred	
milligrammes (500mg) whether dosage form is tablet or capsule shall be two rupees and thirty cents. (rs. 2.30).

3. the maximum retail price of Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg shall be applicable to all the brand names of the 
generic name of the Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg.
4. A manufacturer, importer, trader, distributor, pharmacist, medical Practitioner, dentist, veterinary surgeon, medical 
institution including a private medical institution, pharmacy or person who or which is in possession of Paracetamol 
tablets/ Capsules 500mg, for the purpose of sale shall not sell such Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg above the 
maximum	retail	price	specified	in	regulation	2	or	revised	retail	price	as	may	be	determined	in	accordance	with	the	
provisions of regulation 6.
5.  every person or institution referred to in regulation 4, who sells or offers for sale Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg, 
shall upon such sale, issue a receipt clearly indicating the appropriate brand name and the price of the Paracetamol 
tablets/Capsules 500mg.
6.  (1) on the date of publication of these regulations, a manufacturer or importer who sells the Paracetamol tablets/ 
Capusles 500mg at a revised retail price under the Medicines (Ceiling on Prices) regulations of 2019 published 
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2123/35 of May 15, 2019 as amended by the regulations published in Gazette 
Extraordinary	No.	2241/43	of	August	19,	2021	which	is	less	than	the	maximum	retail	price	specified	in	regulation	
2	may	increase	proportionately	such	revised	retail	price	by	thirty-five	per centum (35%).
   Provided however, such increase of revised retail price of Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg shall not exceed 
the	maximum	retail	price	specified	in	regulation	2.
 (2) every trader, distributor, pharamacist, medical practitioner, dentist, veterinary surgeon, Medical institution 
including a private medical institution, pharmacy or person who or which is in possession of Paracetamol tablets/ 
Capsules 500 mg for the purpose of sale shall maintain the price of Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg at the 
maximum retail price or revised retail price whichever is less.
7. Where any dosage form or strength of the Paracetamol and the maximum retail price of such dosage form or strength 
of	Paracetamol	are	not	specified	in	regulation	2,	the	National	Medicines	Regulatory	Authority	shall	fix	the	price	for	the	
Paracetamol in such dosage form or strength.
8. Any Manufacturer or importer shall print or mark on the commercial package or label of Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 
500mg	the	maximum	retail	price	specified	in	regulation	2	or	the	revised	retail	price,	whichever	is	less,	in	the	stock	
manufactured or available in the market as expeditiously as possible.
  Provided however, the maximum retail price or the revised retail price whichever is less shall be printed or marked in 
the	commercial	package	or	lable	of	Paracetamol	Tablets/	Capsules	500mg	before	the	expiry	of	a	period	of	forty-five	days	
from the date of publication of these regulations.
9. Any person who contravenes the provisions of these regulations commits an offence and shall be triable under section 
131 of the National Medicines regulatory Authority Act, No. 5 of 2015.
10. It shall be the duty of every manufacturer, importer, trader, distributor, pharmacist, medical practitioner, dentist, veterinary 
surgeon, medical institution including a private medical institution, pharmacy or person who or which is in possession of 
Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg for the purpose of sale, to display at every retail outlet the maximum retail price 
of the Paracetamol tablets/ Capsules 500mg or the revised retail price whichever is less.
11. the Medicines (Ceiling on Prices) regulations of 2019 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2123/35 of May 15, 
2019 as amended by the regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2241/43 of August 19, 2021 are hereby 
amended in the schedule thereof by the repeal of item 32 thereof.
