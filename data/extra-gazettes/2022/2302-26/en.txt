THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The land named as Gorakaduwawatta in total extent of 0.0006
Hectare (A.0, R.0, P.0.2) depicted as Lot No. 01 in P .P .G.No.
4627 made by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of
Gonapinuwala in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Gonapinuwala in the District of Galle, Southern Province. The
boundaries of the land is as follows :
Boundaries :
North - Batapolagewatta claimed by W. M. D. S. M.
Prasanna and Lot No. 61 depicted in P.P.G. 3857,
East - Lot No. 61 depicted in P.P.G . 3857 and Road,
(R.D.A.),
South - Road (R.D.A.) and Lot No. 62 depicted in P.P .G .
3857,
West - Lot No. 62 depicted in P .P .G . 3857, Batapolagewatta
claimed by K. T. Lalitha de Silva and
Gorakaduwawatta claimed by H. L. Somalatha.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Gonapinuwala on the 26th day of
December, 2022 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day
of  December, 2022 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation and the details of
the said land/ servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
L. N. P
IUMALIE LIYANAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Gonapinuwala.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Gonapinuwala,
On this 14th day of October, 2022.
