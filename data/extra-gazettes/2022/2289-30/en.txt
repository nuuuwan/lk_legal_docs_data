Acquisition of Land for the construction of the play 
ground of Dharmadutha National School - Badulla  
I hereby give notice to the public under Sub-section (1) of 
the Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by 
Land Acquisition Act (Amendment) No. 28 of 1964, the 
land described below is required by the government and 
the Government intends to acquire the said land/servitude 
for public purpose.  
Description of the Land/Servitude  to be Acquired:  
The boundaries to the block of land mentioned in 
Schedule 01 with the extent of 0.9181 Hectare of the 
preliminary plan No. B 748 dated 23.11.2020, prepared 
by the District Survey General of Badulla on behalf of the 
Survey General which is situat ed in the GS Division of 
78B - Badulla East in the Village of Kendalagama 
[division 04] of Badulla Municipal area in Badulla 
Divisional Secretariat Divisi on of Badulla District, Uva 
Province. 
Lot No. 1  
Name of the land: Koskanuwa; Present land use: 
Paddy land - abandoned; Claimant: Purana Rajamaha 
Vihara of Andeniya; Other Matters: Required to be 
acquired by the government under the Land Acquisition 
Act; Extent: 0.9181 Hectare. 
 
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other 
connection to aforesaid land, should appear personally, or 
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at 
Divisional Secretariat Badulla on 10th August, 2022 at 
10.00 a.m. 
 
In addition, hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
with two copies of the nature of their interests in the land/
lands, the particulars of thei r claims for compensation for

amount before 03rd August, 2022.
