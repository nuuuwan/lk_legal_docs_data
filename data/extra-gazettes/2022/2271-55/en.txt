Whereas the office of member of Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for council 
meetings period of three month without  permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Muththukkumar 
Vickneswaran, the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that No. 03 Navithanveli of Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Yogarasan Tharsan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Mohaideen Bawa Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of  Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Yogarasan Tharsan 
was elected as the member of the No. 03 Navithanveli of Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. S
