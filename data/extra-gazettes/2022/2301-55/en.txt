ThE  CONSTITuTION  Of  ThE  DEmOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPubLIC  Of  SRI   LANkA
Notification 
IT is now hereby notified that from the subjects of functions and Departments, Public Corporations and Statutory Institutions  
assigned to me and laws and ordinances set out in my charge by the Hon. President by Notification published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2289/43 dated 22nd of July 2022 in the exercise of powers vested in me in terms of Sub-paragraph 
(1)(a) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka the subjects and functions and 
Departments, Public Corporations and Statutory Institutions and Laws and Ordinance to be implemented set out in the 
Schedule given below are assigned to the Hon. State Minister of Health and Hon. State Minister of Indigenous Medicine 
with effect from 28.09.2022 as per the powers vested in me in terms of Article 45(4) of the Constitution.
    
 Dr.
 Keheliya RambuKwella,  
 Minister of Health.
On 05th October, 2022.
