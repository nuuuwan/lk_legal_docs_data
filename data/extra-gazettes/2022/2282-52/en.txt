WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kuhajini 
Balakrishnan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Velupillai Naharani to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Velupillai Naharani  
was selected as the member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council.
