WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Pachchilaippalli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilanki Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Kirushnan 
Veeravaguthevar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon  the member of that No. 05 Mullaiyadi of Pachchilaippalli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Yogeshwary Pradakaran to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Winson Gunalan Winson Gnanathayalan, Returning Officer of the Pachchilaippalli Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Yogeshwary Pradakaran was selected as the member of the No. 05 Mullaiyadi of Pachchilaippalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 W. G. W. Gnanathayalan,
 Returning Officer,
 Pachchilaippalli Pradeshaya Sabha.
