Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  kandy  municipal  council   
under  section  66a (1) oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present), the recognized 
political party to which Nagaraja Vimalendran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Weeran Keshawan Kumareshan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Weeran Keshawan Kumareshan was selected as the member of  the Kandy Municipal Council.
