FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE   OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE  POINT PEDRO PRADESHIYA SABHA 
UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)  
WHEREAS  the  ofﬁ ce of return member of  Point Pedro Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason  of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Eelam People's Democratic Party,  the recognized political party to which  Kalavathi
Parameswaran the member  belonged was  required by me  under Section 66 A (1) of the  aforesaid  ordinance to decide upon
returned member  of Point  Pedro Pradeshiya Sabha  who  belongs to such  party  and is not  disqualiﬁ ed under Section  9 of 
the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member  within a period 
of thirty (30) days; 
And  whereas the secretary of the  aforesaid recognized political party has  communicated his  decision to nominate
Sivabalasingam Vimalanantharani to ﬁ ll such  vacancy; 
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Ofﬁ cer of the  Point Pedro Praseshiya Sabha, acting  in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sivabalasingam
Vimalanantharani was selected as the  member of the Point Pedro Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. AMALRAAJ,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Point Pedro Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Ofﬁce,
Jaffna.
10th  March, 2022.
