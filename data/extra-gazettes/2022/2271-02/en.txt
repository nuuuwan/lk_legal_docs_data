notice under section 66 g 
I hereby announce in terms of the powers vested by me under Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

Talawakele Lidula Urban Council, Nuwara Eliya District, Central Province, will be held on 23.03.2022 at 10.00 a.m. at the 
conference room of Talawakele Lidula Urban Council.
