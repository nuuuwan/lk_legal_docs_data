2a
Regulations
 1. These Regulations shall be cited as the Regulations for Registration and Licensing of authorised Person (Fees), 
No. 01 of 2022 
 2. The fees for application of authorised Person under the act and such other services provided by the Commission 
shall be as set out in the schedule hereto.
 3. For the purpose of these Regulations;
 (a) “act” shall mean the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 of 2021.
 (b) A term that is used in these regulations which is not defined above and is defined in the Colombo Port City 
economic Commission act, No.11 of 2021 will have in these regulations, the same meaning as it has in the 
Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No.11 of 2021.
 (c) In the event of any inconsistency between the sinhala and Tamil texts of these Regulations, the sinhala text 
will prevail.
