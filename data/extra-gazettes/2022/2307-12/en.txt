2a
Regulations
1.	 These regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 21 of 2022”. 
2.	 These regulations shall enter into force with effect from November 23, 2022.
3.	 Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 13 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2294/30 dated 
august 23, 2022 as amended by Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 14 of 2022 published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2296/30 dated september 09, 2022, is hereby further amended.
4.	 Goods, described in the Schedule B of this Gazette shall be deleted from the schedule I of Imports and exports (Control) 
regulations No. 13 of 2022 referred above.
5.	 as per provisions of the Customs ordinance read together with the Government revenue Protection (special Provisions) 
act, No. 01 of 2006, the director General, sri Lanka Customs is hereby authorized to release any Cargos / Consignments 
of goods, 
(i)  specified in the Schedule A of Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 14 of 2022, published in the 
Gazette extraordinary No. 2296/30 dated september 09, 2022 and arrived at any port / airport of sri Lanka 
during the period of august 24, 2022 to september 08, 2022, 
or
(ii)  specified in the Schedule B of this Gazette and arrived at any port / airport of sri Lanka during the period of 
august 24, 2022 to November 22, 2022.
6.	 Licensed banks are hereby authorized to release shipping documents and effect the payment for the goods referred in 
regulation No. 5 above.
