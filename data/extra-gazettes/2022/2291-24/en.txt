Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  boralesgamuwa  urban  
council  under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of  Boralesgamuwa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wickramasinghe 
Arachchige Lasantha Panchali Wickramasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Boralesgamuwa Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kuruppu Appuhamilage Sharoja Sujeewani to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Boralesgamuwa Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kuruppu Appuhamilage Sharoja Sujeewani was selected as the member of  the Boralesgamuwa Urban Council.
   
 M. M. S. K. B
andara Mapa,
 Returning Officer,
 Boralesgamuwa Urban Council. 
District Election Office,
Colombo,
01st August, 2022.
