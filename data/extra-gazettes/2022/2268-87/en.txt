Order under Section 25A
bY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, i, Dinesh Gunawardena, the 
Minister of education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the National School of 
business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing 
registration Number Pb 4833, do by this Order amend the Schedule ii to the Order made under said Act, and published in 
Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2224/26 of April 22, 2021 by the addition immediately after  entry “bachelor of Science Honours 
in Public Health and Nutrition” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereto, of the entries specified 
under Columns I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
