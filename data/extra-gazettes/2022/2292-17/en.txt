AMENDMENT 
 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460),   
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: PUT/LND/01/01/17. 
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2015/WS/210. 
 
THE advertisement published under Section III of the 
Extraordinary Gazette notification of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2226/27 of 04th May 
2021 in respect of the land described below under Section 
07 of the Land Acquisition Act is amended and published 
as follows: 
 
I hereby inform under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land situated in the Village of 
Senakudirippuwa in GN Division of 616, 
Senakudirippuwa in the Divisional Secretariat Division of 
Puttalam in the District of the Puttalam in North Western 
Province and marked No. 3 Survey General Plan                  
No. 410112 dated 12.02.2020. 
01. Lot No. 124 
Name of the Land: Mudaliya Senai Estate; 
Description: Land with a Coconut cultivation; Claimant: 
Government; Original Claimant: I.N.M. Mohamed 
Cassim Marikkar; Size: 1.2197 Hectares. 
 
All  persons claimed ownership or other affiliation of 
the aforesaid land should contact me personally or by 
agent duly authorized in writing on 10th October 2022 at 
10.00 a.m. at the Divisional Secretariat, Puttalam. 
 
And, before 26th September 2022, must be notified in 
writing of the nature of their rights to the land, the details 
of the claim for the acquisition of the land, the amount of 
compensation and the amount to be calculated in two 
copies. 
 
 S. A. Y. R. Jayathilake, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Puttalam. 
 
03rd August, 2022, 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Puttalam.
