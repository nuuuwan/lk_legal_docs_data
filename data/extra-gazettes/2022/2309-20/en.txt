NOTIFICATION  UNDER THE  CUSTOMS  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 19A
BY Virtue of the power vested in me under Section 19(A) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), as amended by the 
Customs (Amendment) Law No. 35 of 1974 and the Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 83 of 1988, I Ranil Wickramasinghe, 
Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies do by this Order exempt the goods speciﬁ ed in Column 
V of the Schedule hereto, imported by or consigned to, person or institutions speciﬁ ed in the corresponding entry in Column 
II of that Schedule from payment of the Customs Import Duty, le viable thereon as speciﬁ ed in the corresponding entry in 
Column VIII of that Schedule subject to such condition. 
However, any importer or person by whom or on whose behalf any goods, speciﬁ ed and imported under this Order 
are subsequently to be sold or disposed of contrary to the cond itions speciﬁ ed, such importer or person shall obtain prior 
approval of the Director General of Customs and pay the Customs Import Duty payable on such goods at the time of sale or 
disposal to the Director General of Customs in terms of Section  19(A)(3). Any goods sold or disposed of in contravention 
of such Section shall be liable be liable to be forfeited.
