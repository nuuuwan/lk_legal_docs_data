UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice of the Hon. Governor of Uva Province
by virtue of powers vested on me through the section 154(c) of the constitution of democratic socialist republic of sri 
Lanka, inconsistent with the section 185 of the pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, should be read with the section 2 of 
the provincial Council (Consequential provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the section 
2(3) (a) (I) of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding the powers to monitor the administration, identified as the Statute No. 2 
of 2011 of the uva provincial Council imposed to make  provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor 
administration in Local Government Authorities established in uva province and matters relate and incidental thereto I, A. 
J. m. muzammil, the Governor of the uva province temporarily suspended the functions of Katharagama pradeshiya sabha 
with effect from the midnight of 9th of may 2022 through the Special Gazette No. 2279/10 dated 09th may 2022 of the 
democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka.
Further, through the Special Gazette No. 2279/10 dated 09th may 2022 of the democratic socialist republic of sri 
Lanka. I appointed Mr. H. M. R. Anura Kumara Judicial officer, retired justice to investigate and report to me whether any 
fact in sections (b) (c) and (d) of the subsection 2(1) of the said statute has been done by the Katharagama pradeshiya sabha 
including the hon. Chairman of Katharagama pradeshiya sabha.
I have personally read and satisfied myself with the relevant investigation report of Judicial Officer, retired justice 
and hereby inform that it has been decided to dissolve the Katharagama pradeshiya sabha from midnight of 22.09.2022.
