CEYLON  PETROLEum  CORPORATION  ACT,  No.  28  Of  1961  
Order under Section 66
As per the powers vested upon me by the section 66 of ceylon Petroleum corporation Act, No. 28 of 1961, i, Gamini Lokuge, 
minister of Energy, in the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do hereby revise the fuel prices to be implemented 
from 12th march, 2022 as follows :

Petrol 92 rs. 177.00 rs. 254.00
Petrol 95 rs. 207.00 rs. 283.00
Auto diesel rs. 121.00 rs. 176.00
super diesel rs. 159.00 rs. 254.00
 Gamini LokuGe,
