Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS the office of  member of Hali-ela Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending Pradeshiya 
Sabha meeting for a period of three months (90 days) without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Ekanayaka 
Mudiyanselage Nilmini Renuka Damayanthi the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Hali-ela Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Weligamage Wajira Amarasena to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, K. Kantheepan, Returning Officer of the Hali-ela Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Weligamage Wajira Amarasena 
was selected as the member of the Hali-ela Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K. K
antheepan ,
 Returning Officer,
 Hali-ela Pradeshiya Sabha.
