THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Lands and 
Tourism hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that  Act. Description of the Land or servitude 
to be acquired.
 Allotments of land shown in the Plan No: CO/THI/2016/646 
dated 18/06/2019, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated 
at Borella village (Division No 40, within the Limits of 
Colombo Municipal Council) , in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Thimbirigasyaya, Colombo District, Western 
Province and bounded as follows. Limits and extent of the 
above land are as follows:
 1. Lot 01 – Assmt. No. 228 Lake Drive – Extent : About 
0.0509 Hectare
 North: Lake Drive and Assmt. No. 228 and Lake Drive;

Lot No. 02, Lake Drive and a land of which Claimant is not 
found; West: Lake Drive and a Land of which Assmt. No. and 
Claimant are not found and Lake Drive and Assmt. No. 228.
    2. Lot 02 – Assmt. No. 228 Lake Drive – Extent : About 
0.0084 Hectare
 North: Lot No. 01; East: Heen Ela; South: Heen Ela, Lake
