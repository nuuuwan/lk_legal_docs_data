ThE  CONSTITuTION  Of  ThE  DEmOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPubLIC  Of  SRI  LANkA
Notifications
whereAs in the exercising of powers vested in the president by the Constitution of the democratic socialist republic of 
sri Lanka, subjects and functions and departments, state Corporations and statutory Institutions have been assigned to the 
Ministers by Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2289/43 on 22.07.2022,
It is now hereby notified in terms of Article 45(4) of the constitution, being the Minister of Ports,Shipping and 
Aviation that the subjects and functions given in the schedule herewith are assigned to the state minister of ports and Aviation 
operating under  purview of my ministry, with immediate effect and the schedule is given below.
 Nimal Siripala De Silv a, 
 minister of ports, shipping and Aviation.
ministry of ports, shipping and Aviation,
Colombo 01,
september 23, 2022.
