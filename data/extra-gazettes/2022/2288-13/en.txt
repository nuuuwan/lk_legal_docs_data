NOTICE  Of  APPROvAL  Of  ThE  DEvELOPmENT  PLAN  fOR  ThE  uRbAN  COuNCIL  
LImIT Of  mAhARAGAmA
Notice is hereby given to the General Public of the democratic socialist Republic of sri lanka under section 8 (G) of the 
urban development authority law No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the act, No. 4 of 1982 that I, Prasanna Ranathunga, the 
Minister in charge of the subject of urban development & Housing, by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 8 (F) 
of the said act, No. 4 of 1982 have approved the development Plan for the urban Council limit of Maharagama, prepared 
under section 8 (a) of the said act, on the day of 04th july 2022.
 Prasanna ranathunga (M. P.),
 Minister of urban development & Housing.

on 04th july, 2022, by Hon. Prasanna Ranathunga, Minister of urban development & Housing by virtue of powers vested
