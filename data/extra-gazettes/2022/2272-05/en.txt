WHEREAS the office of Chairman in Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by the reason of dismissal by the North 
Western Provincial Governor;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Dissanayake 
Mudiyanselage Thilakarathne Bandara Dissanayake the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) 
of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who 
shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Kiriya Durayalage Lionel Premathilaka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Ratnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayaka, returning officer of the Wariyapola Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local 
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Kiriya Durayalage Lionel Premathilaka elected 
as Chairman of the Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
 Ratnayaka Mudiyanselage 
 Harendra Bandara Ratnayaka,
 Returning officer,
 Wariyapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
