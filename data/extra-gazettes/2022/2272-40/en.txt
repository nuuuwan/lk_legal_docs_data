Regulations
the regulations of the National sports Associations published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1990/23 
dated 27th october, 2016 which was last amended  by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2207/7 of 23rd December, 
2020 are further amended as follows :
 01. regulation 8(1)(a) thereof
  Anyone who is discontented with any decision or action of a registered National sports Association and any other 
committee, including the elections committee appointed by the National sports Association, may appeal in writing 
to the minister in charge of sports within seven days of receipt of that decision or action.
