PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  muthur  pradeshiya  sabha  
council  under  section  66 a (1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262) 
WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which 
Thillaiyambalam Harieston the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon  member of  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of 
the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Arumugam Nesathurai to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section                  
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Arumugam Nesathurai was selected 
as the member of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 P
iumii AttygAlle,
 Returning Officer,
 Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
