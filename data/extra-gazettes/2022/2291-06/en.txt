   THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : DSM/PL/LAND/Aku/Playground/ Acqu.         
 
I, do hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a public 
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
 Land called Dawulghamulla Pillewa in extent of 0.0203 
Hectare (08 perches) that depicted as Lot No. 521 which 
situated at Akuramboda Village of Udugoda Udasiya Pattu 
Minor Division of Pallepola Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Matale District in Central Province that shown in 
Supplementary No. 24, Sheet No. 20 of Surveyor General’s 
approved plan F.V .P. No. 32.
 
 Boundary Scheduled
 North: Lot No. 336 and 250; East: Lot No. 250; South: Lot 
Nos. 250 and 251B; West: Lot Nos. 251B and 336.
 Public Purpose in this eﬀ ort is denoted as:
Acquiring of land for the Playground of CP/Ga/Pa/
Akuramboda Junior School.
Claimant: Unanimous.
    All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agent duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 a.m on the day of 31.08.2022 at 
the Pallepola Divisional Secretariat.
 And further yor are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the day of 24.08.2022 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation of such amount.
