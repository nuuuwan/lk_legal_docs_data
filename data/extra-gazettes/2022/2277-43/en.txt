WHEREAS, the office of member of  Peliyagoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member 
of the party;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Solanga Arachchige 
Thamith Nihal Dias the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that ward Number 01 Paliyagoda Watta of Paliyagoda Urban Council, who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Biyanwilage Don Peter Antony to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Paliyagoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Biyanwilage Don Peter Antony was elected as the member of the Ward Number 01 - Paliyagoda-Watta of  Paliyagoda Urban 
Council.
K. J. S. Madhawa,
Returning Officer,
Paliyagoda Urban Council.
