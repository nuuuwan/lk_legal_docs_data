SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT, No. 48 Of 2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, i, M. U. M. Ali 
sabry, Minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the com-
modities specified in Column I of the schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding 
entries in Column ii of that schedule.
The Order made under section 2 of the special Commodity levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2225/1 of April 26, 2021 is rescinded with effect from April 14, 2022.
This order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from April 14, 2022 subject to provisions of Sub Section 
(3) of section 2 of the special Commodity levey Act, No. 48 of 2007.
 M. U. M. Ali SAbry,
 Minister of Finance.
