Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  vengalacheddikulam

WHEREAS, the office of  return member of  Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Thangavelu 
Thiyagarajah the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  
the member of  Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Raththinakumar Kokilan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Vengalacheddikulam  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Raththinakumar Kokilan 
was selected as the member of the Vengalacheddikulam Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 J. J
