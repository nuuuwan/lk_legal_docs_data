2. According to provisions stated under Section 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 amended by Local 
Government Authorities Special Provisions Act, No. 21 of 2012, as the budget document for year 2022 has neither 
been subjected to final consideration by the  said Pradeshiya Sabha nor been adopted by 31st December 2021, it shall 
be lawful to maintain by the said Pradeshiya Sabha income and expenditure relating to maintenance of services for 
public welfare according to a budget document for the financial year 2022, prepared on behalf of the said Pradeshiya 
Sabha by the Secretary of the respective Pradeshiya Sabha and approved by the Assistant Commissioner of Local 
Government of Gampaha district in which the said Pradeshiya Sabha is located until the date a budget document will 
be prepared by the relevant Pradeshiya Sabha as per provisions of the written law.
3. All the provisions stated in this order shall be executed subject to provisions under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha 
Act, No. 15 of 1987 amended by Local Government Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012.
4. In case any inconsistency between the Sinhala, English and Tamil texts of this order should rise, the Sinhala text shall 
prevail.
