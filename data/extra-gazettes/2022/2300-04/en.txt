Notice under Section 7
             My. No. : L4/Acquisition/288/V olume 2'

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of the
Land Acquisition Act 24 of 1964, as amended by the Land
Acquisition(Amendment) Act, that the Goverment intends to
acquire the following lands/servitudes  which are required for
a public purpose :
Description of the Lands/servitude  to be Acquired :
Three allotments of land mentioned in the below list by the
names, extents and in total extents about 1.9862 Hectares, in
PP No. 18 Sheet No. 01 Supplement No. 1 in dated 14.08.2022
prepared by the Surveyor General and situated at the village
of Wadugamayaya in the Minor Division of Magul
Madagandahaya Western Korale in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Nikaweratiya in the District of Kurunegala in North
Western Province:
PP - 18
01. Lot No. 307
Name of the Land :Wadugamayaya;  Discription: Field;

Extent : 1.0793 Hectares.

03. Lot No. 309
Name of the Land :Wadugamayaya;  Discription: Field;

Extent :0.8532 Hectare.
 All person interested in the aforesaid lands/ servitude are
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Nikaweratiya on 15.11.2022 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested  to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
15.11.2022 the nature of their interests in the land/lands the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the land/servitude the amount of  compensation and the
details of the  computation of such amount.
