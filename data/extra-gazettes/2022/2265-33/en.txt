THE  AWARD
 1. The Honourable Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations  by virtue of the power vested in him by 
Section  4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as 
amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 62 of 1957,  4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, 
No. 37 of 1968 appointed me by his order dated 03rd May, 2019 a nd referred the dispute between the aforesaid 
parties to me for settlement by Arbitration.
 2. The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is:–
  “Whether Mr. P. D. Somasiri who was working at the Wennappuwa  Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board had 
been caused injustice by being retired without the salaries at the age of 55 with eﬀ ect from 19.10.2014 and if so, to 
what reliefs he is entitled.”
 3. Mr. A. A. Sahabandu, Attorney-at-Law appeared for the appli cant and State Attorney, Miss Malmi Abeysundara, 
assisted by Mrs. Dilrukshi Liyanage, Legal Oﬃ  cer, appeared on behalf of the respondent organization.
 4. Parties were summoned for learning of the case on 26th Nov.  2019, 17 Dec. 2019, 11th February, 2020, 30th June, 
2020 and 14th July 2020. The applicant was present only on 06th  Nov. 2019 and failed to appear, or inform the 
courts in advance, explaining his absence on the 4 days ﬁ xed thereafter.
 5. Since the applicant takes no interest, I dismiss his applic ation and award no relief. This is just and equitable order.
 P. Nൺ ඏ ൺඋൺඍඇൾ,
 Arbitrator.
