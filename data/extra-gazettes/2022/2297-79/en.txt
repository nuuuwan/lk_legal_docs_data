3. the date of arrival at ports / airports of essential food commodities imported on the recommendation of the secretary, 
ministry of trade, Commerce and food security in terms of the regulation No. 4 (d) of the imports Control regulations 
on payment terms No. 07 of 2022, as amended by the imports Control regulations on payment terms No. 10 of 2022 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2285/19 dated June 24, 2022, shall be extended as follows;
i.	 Goods	 Specified	under	the	HS Codes of 0402.21.00 and 0713.40.11 of the Schedule I to the imports Control 
regulations on payment terms No. 10 of 2022 as amended, shall be allowed to import under the Open account/ 
Consignment account payment terms subject to recommendation of the secretary, ministry of trade, Commerce 
and food security, obtained prior to arrival of such goods to the ports/ airports of sri Lanka on or before October 
31, 2022.
ii.	 Goods	 specified	in	the	Schedule I to the said Regulations, except goods referred in 3 (i) above, shall be allowed 
to import under the Open account/ Consignment account payment terms subject to recommendation of the 
secretary, ministry of trade, Commerce and food security, obtained prior to arrival of such goods to the ports/ 
airports of sri Lanka on or before september 30, 2022.
4.	 Importation	 of	goods	specified	under	the	HS Codes of 0402.21.00 and 0713.40.11 of the Schedule I to the imports 
Control regulations on payment terms No. 10 of 2022 as amended, shall be exempted from the regulation No. 6 of 
the imports Control regulations on payment terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2278/ 
21 dated may 06, 2022.
5. importation of maize (HS Code of 1005.90.00) and Soya Bean meal (Hs Code of 2304.00.00) by registered poultry 
feed manufacturers are hereby allowed under the Open account/ Consignment account payment term subject to 
recommendation of the secretary, ministry in charge of subject of agriculture, obtained prior to arrival of such goods 
to the ports / airports of sri Lanka on or before October 31, 2022 for the purpose of manufacturing of poultry feed.
6. the director General, sri Lanka Customs shall consult the secretary, ministry of trade, Commerce and food security 
on any importation made in violation of the regulation No. 3 above and the secretary, ministry in charge of subject 
of agriculture on any importation made in violation of the regulation No. 5 above.
