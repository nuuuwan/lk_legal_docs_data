DECLARATION  UNDER  SUB SECTION (1)  OF  SECTION  3A  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES 
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 3A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) 
of Legislative Enactments of Sri Lanka, read with Section 4 of the interpretations Ordinance (Chapter 12) of said Enactment, 
I, Dinesh Gunawardene, Prime Minister and Minister of Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils and Local 
Government, do hereby declare that the National Delimitation Committee on Demarcation of Wards for Local Authorities, 
consisting of the following members, will be appointed with effect from 01st of November 2022 up to 28th of February 2023.
Mr. Mahinda Deshapriya (Chairman)
Mr. Jayalath R. V . Dissanayake (Member)
Mrs. W. M. M. R. Adikari (Member)
Mr. K. Thavalingam (Member)
Mr. I. A. Hameed (Member)

INESH GUNAWARDENA (M. P.),

 Affairs, Provincial Councils and Local
