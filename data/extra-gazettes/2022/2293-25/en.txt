I,  Dr. a. D. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education,  having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid

1972, and having recognized the said institute as a Degree awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education 
leading to the award of the Bachelor of science Honours in Chemical science by the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary                
No.  2166/14 of March 11, 2020, subject to the conditions stipulated in the schedule I to the said Order, do hereby amend the 
said Order by the insertion immediately after the condition (3) specified in the said schedule I, of the following conditions :-
 
 “4.  Institute of Chemistry, Ceylon shall, when admitting students by lateral entry from the graduateship programme 
in Chemistry (gIC) for the degree programme relating to the degree specified in schedule II hereto in respect 
of -
 (a) the Level 1 and Level 2 of the graduateship programme in Chemistry (gIC), adhere to the minimum 
entry qualification of, three (3) simple passes ('S') in biological science or physical science subject
