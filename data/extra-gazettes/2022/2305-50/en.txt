WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman in Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending Council 
meetings for a period of three months ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the, Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to, which Manikka 
Waduge Vajira Darshana De Silva the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the aforesaid 
ordinance to decide upon member of  that Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared 
elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days:
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Priyantha Upul Hewage to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Priyantha Upul Hewage elected as the member of the Kahawatta 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
