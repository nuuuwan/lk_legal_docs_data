                                  GOTABAYA  RAJAPAKSA
PRESIDENTIAL    DIRECTIVE
Whereas, a Presidential Task Force for “One Country, One Law” has been established by the Presidential Directive
dated 26th October, 2021 in terms of the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka,
And whereas, the time granted to accomplish the functions and responsibilities assigned to the aforesaid Task
Force is to end on 28th February, 2022,
And whereas, it is necessary to seek the views of the public and the panels of experts covering all the provinces
of the island,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of   Sri Lanka, do hereby enlarge the time
period of the Task Force by three (03) months from 28th February, 2022 to complete the task assigned to the aforesaid Task
Force efficiently within an appropriate action plan.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of   Sri Lanka on the 28th day of February,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
By His Excellency’s Command,
G
AMINI  SENARATH,
 Secretary to the President.
