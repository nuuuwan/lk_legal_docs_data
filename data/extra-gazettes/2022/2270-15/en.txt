WHEREAS, the office of member of  Hambantota Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Eraj 
Ravindra Fernando the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that 07 - Mirijjawila of Hambantota Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Wickramage Don Upul Kumara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer of the Hambantota Municipal Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wickramage Don 
Upul Kumara was elected as the member of the 07 - Mirijjawila of Hambantota Municipal Council.
