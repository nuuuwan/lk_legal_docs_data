WHEREAS, the office of member of Ambalangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of expelling by Hon. Governor 
of Southern Province;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  
Gange Sarath Srilal De Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that 06 Kariththakanda of Ambalangoda Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kasthuri Arachchi Pinnawalage Jeevan Supavithra to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of  the Ambalangoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kasthuri Arachchi Pinnawalage Jeevan Supavithra was elected as the member of 06 Kariththakanda of Ambalangoda Urban 
Council.   
 W. H. R. W
ijaya KumaRa,
