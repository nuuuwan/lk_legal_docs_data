ThE uNIvERSITIES ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dinesh Gunawardena, Minister 
of Education, on the recommendation of the university Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule to the 
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1482/26 of February 02, 2007 (as amended from time to time) which specifies 
the Faculties and departments of the university of Peradeniya, sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of 
section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately 
after the entry "17. department of Medical Education" appearing in column II of the schedule which corresponds to the item 
"Faculty of Medicine" appearing in Column I of the Schedule (specified in order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 
2160/40 of January 30, 2020) of the following entry :
 "18. 
department of Family Medicine"
Dinesh GunawarDena,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
23rd February, 2022.
