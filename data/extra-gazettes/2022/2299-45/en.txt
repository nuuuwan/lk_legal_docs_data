STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. A. Hewagama working in the Southern Provincial Road Development Authority who appeared for Arbitration 
inquiry No. A 3609 as the employees’ representative, personally  and as the Secretary of the Freedom Trade Union of the 
Southern Provincial Road Development Authority has been caused injustice by the days on which he had appeared for the 
case being decided as leave without pay by the institution even though he had made a request to approve leave for those days 
and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
