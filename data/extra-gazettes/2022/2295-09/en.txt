CASINO  buSINESS  (REGuLATION)  ACT,  No.  17 Of  2010
Order under Section 2
By, virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Casino Business (Regulation) act, No. 17 of 2010, I, Ranil 
wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do by this Order -
	 (a)	 designate	the	specific	area	to	engage	in	the	business	of	a	Casino	shall	be	the	place	or	premises	on	or	at	which	
the	business	of	a	Casino	is	carried	on	as	specified	in	the	licence	issued	under	Section	2	of	the	Casino	Business	
(Regulation) act, No. 17 of 2010; and
 (b) declare that this Order shall come into operation from september 01, 2022.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 Minister of Finance, economic stabilization  
 and National Policies.
Colombo, 
On 31st august, 2022.
