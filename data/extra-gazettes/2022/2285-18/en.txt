Direction No.  84
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.   09   of   2003  (Amended)
Direction under Section  10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no importer, manufacturer, trader or distributor  shall import, sell, expose or offer for
sale, store,   transport, distribute or buy or purchase any  rice or paddy directly for the manufacture of animal feed or otherwise
as an ingredient for the manufacture of animal feed.
This Direction shall come into effect from 24th June, 2022.
Direction No. 72 published in Government Extraordinary No. 2185/67 on 23rd July 2020 is hereby rescinded.
