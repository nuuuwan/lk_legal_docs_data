whereas the office of  Chairman in Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Nihal 
Thilakawardhana the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as 
Chairman within a  period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hemantha Chandha Kumara Rathwaththa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Kangatharan Mugunthan returning Officer of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Hemantha Chandha Kumara Rathwaththa elected as Chairman of the 
Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
 K. Mugunthan,
 Returning Officer,
 Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
08th  March, 2022.
