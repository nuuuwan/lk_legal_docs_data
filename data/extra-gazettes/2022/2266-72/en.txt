14.  The following sentence will be inserted as Section ii (e) under Absorption into the Sri Lanka Ayurvedic Medical Service.
(e) Officers serving in Supra Grade for a period of 03 years or more as at the effective date of this Service Minute will be 
absorbed into Administrative Grade i and those who are serving in Supra Grade for a period less than 03 years as at the 
effective date of this Service Minute will be absorbed into Administrative Grade ii of the service.
15. The following section will be inserted under interim Provisions.
15.3 The officers who have been promoted to Supra Grade of the Ayurvedic Medical Service as per the previous service 
minute shall be reckoned as those who have fulfilled the qualifications for appointment to the approved posts under Admin-
istrative Grades of the Ayurvedic Medical Service mentioned in Appendix V until the officers are absorbed into the Admin-
istrative Grades of the Ayurvedic Medical Service as per the new service minute. furthermore, when computing the period 
of service in Ayurvedic Medical Administrative Grade under the new service minute, the period of service in Supra Grade 
as per the previous service minute will also be taken into account.
