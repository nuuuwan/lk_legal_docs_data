WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Adamwawa 
Abdul Wakidu the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of All Ceylon Makkal Congress who  belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kalandarlebbe Rafeek to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Ninthavur 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Kalandarlebbe Rafeek was selected as the member of  the Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha.
