SRI   LANkA   EXPORT   DEvELOPmENT   ACT,   No.  40  Of  1979
Order under Section 14
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 14(2) of the sri Lanka export development act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democraatic socialist Republic 
of sri Lanka, do by this Order from august 10, 2022, insert the new Paragraph as (xx), of annexure a, as further amend the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2202/6 dated November 17, 2020 as follows;
 
(xx)  Importation of essential goods, to be received from any foreign states (governments), organizations including 
business entities, volunteer associations and well-wishers by any government entities, government approved entities 
and entities working on relief activities in direct collaboration with any government entities as aid/donations to 
provide relief measures or facilitate continuation of essential public services subject to ;
(a)  Recommendation of the Secretary (Chief Accounting Officer), Ministry in Charge of donation recipient 
government entity or government approved entity or the directly collaborating government entity with 
the donation and relief measures, where ; (i) The Secretary shall provide recommendation for exemption 
of CESS only after his satisfaction on the purpose and target beneficiaries of the donation ; and (ii) Such 
donated goods shall be consinged to the Secretary (Chief Accounting Officer), who issued the letter of 
Recommendation referred above ; case by case basis;
