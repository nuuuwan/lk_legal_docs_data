UNIvERSITIES ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dinesh Gunawardena, the 
minister of Education having obtained a report under Section 70c of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the SLT campus (Private) 
Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number  
PV 101137, do by this order amend the Schedule II to the order made under said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, 
No. 2210/18 of January 13, 2021 by the addition immediately after entry “Bachelor of music honours” appearing in 
Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the  
Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
SchEdULE I
coNdITIoNS
1 . SL T Campus (Private) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto
in respect of the Bachelor of Science in Textile and Clothing Technology, adhere to the minimum entry qualification
of three (03) simple passes (S) in approved subjects in Physical Science or Biological Science Stream or Technology
Stream in one and the same sitting at the G
cE (Advanced Level) Examination conducted by the department of
Examination of Sri Lanka or equivalent.
