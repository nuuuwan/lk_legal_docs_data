WHEREAS, the office of return member of Balangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Mulgama 
Mudalihamige Raweendra Saman Kavirathna the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Balangoda Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate  
Sundaram Arulappu George to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Balangoda Urban Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sundaram Arulappu 
George was selected as the member of  the Balangoda Urban Council.
