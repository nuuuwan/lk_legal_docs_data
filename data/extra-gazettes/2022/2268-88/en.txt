Order under Section 25A
bY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, i, Dinesh Gunawardena, the 
Minister of education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Saegis Campus 
(Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration 
Number PV 72548, do by this Order amend the Schedule ii to the Order made under said Act, and published in Gazette 
Extraordinary, No. 2211/69 of January 22, 2021 by the addition immediately after the entry “bachelor of business Management 
Honours  in Marketing” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under                  
Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
