Notice  uNder  SectioN  10A(1)(a)  of  the  LocAL   AuthoritieS   eLectioNS   ordiNANce
Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby 
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Walimuni Ravi Nalin 
Kumara De Silva of the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha as 
he has been ceased to be a member of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna Party.
