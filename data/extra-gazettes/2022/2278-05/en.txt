ThE PILGRImAGE ORDINANCE
The Annual Feast Pilgrimage of our Lady of holy Rosary Shrine,
Periyapullumalai - 2022
THe, annual feast and the Pilgrimage of our Lady of Holy Rosary shrine, Periyapullumalai 2022.
IT is hereby for the information of the Pilgrims who attend the above feast and the others concerned that the annual feast 
for the year 2022 begins on the 06th of May (06.05.2022) with hoisting of flags, and ends on the 15th of May with the 
concelebrated eucharistic celebration.
The special event of the feast would be a Pilgrimage which begins on 13th of may at 3.30 p.m from st. mary's Cathedral, 
Puliyanthivu and proceed through Chenkalady, Karadiyanaru and to Periyapullumalai.
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the above feast and others concerned is drawn to the regulation published in the 
Government Gazette No. 9886 of 16.07.1984 which will be inforce during the period of the feast.
