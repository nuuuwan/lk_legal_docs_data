WHEREAS, the office of return member of Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to 
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which 
Banneheka Mudiyanselage Upali Vijayapala Bandara Banneheka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 
66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Janguge Don Lalith Aloka Sampath Somarathna to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Ratnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayake, Returning Officer of the Kobeigane 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Janguge Don Lalith Aloka Sampath Somarathna was selected as the member of  the Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha.
