WHEREAS the office of member in Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Nihal 
Thilakawardhana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward Number 08 - Kagama of  Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Disanayaka Mudiyanselage Sumith Disanayaka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, returning officer of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Disanayaka Mudiyanselage 
Sumith Disanayaka was elected as the member of the Ward Number 08 -  Kagama of Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
 K. M
