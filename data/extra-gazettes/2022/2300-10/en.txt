WHEREAS, the office of   Member of Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three consecutive 
meetings  without permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Dadallage 
Sangeeth Rangana Siri Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
do decide upon the member of that No. 16 - Malwatta of Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a 
member within a period of  thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Muhandiram Arachchilage Rohitha Dharmasiri to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Muhandiram Arachchilage Rohitha Dharmasiri was elected as the member of  the No. 16 - Malwatta of the Kandy 
Municipal Council.
