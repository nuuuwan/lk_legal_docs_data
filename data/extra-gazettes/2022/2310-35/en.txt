WHEREAS the office of the member in Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Pushparaj 
Rubaraj the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of  that Ward No. 07 Mamangam of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ann Yukchristiya Vulththasar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ann Yukchristiya 
Vulththasar was elected as the member of the Ward No. 07 Mamangam of  Batticaloa Municipal Council.
