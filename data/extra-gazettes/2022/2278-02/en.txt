CONSUMER AFFAIRS AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003 (Amended)
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no Supplier, Manufacturer, Producer, Distributor or Trader shall Sell, Supply,
Expose or Offer for sale, Display for sale the Local varieties of Rice listed below, exceeding the Maximum Retail Prices
given hereunder.
Variety Maximum Retail Price
White/ Red Nadu - Steamed/ Boiled - Local Rs. 220 per Kg.
(excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari)
White/ Red Samba - Steamed/ Boiled - Local Rs. 230 per Kg.
(excluding Suduru Samba)
  Keeri Samba (Local) Rs. 260 per Kg.
