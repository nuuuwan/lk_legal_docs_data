WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the United Peoples Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Teranse 
Chamupathi Sisira Kumara Isurumuni the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Yatikindage Indrani Thamara Kumari to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Yatikindage Indrani 
Thamara Kumari was selected as the member of the Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
Suranaga ambagahathenne, 
Returning Officer, 
Weligepola Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
