THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 Ministry of Lands' Reference No.: 4-3/8/2020/CEB/04. 
My Reference No.: 3/6/4/5.  
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands/ 
servitudes which are required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of Land  to be Acquired : 
 
The allotment of land depicted in the Preliminary Plan 
No. P. P. Ku. 2502 dated 28th February, 2022 prepared 
by the Surveyor General, having the name and the extent 
as mentioned below, situated in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 909 of Imbulgoda, in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Polgahawela, in Kurunegala 
District, in North Western Province, described as 
follows : 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Urawekumbura; Original 
Claimant: Chief Incumbent, Weherapitiya Temple, 
Polgahawela; Extent: 0.0379 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforementioned lands/ 
servitudes on which servitude is to be acquired are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Polgahawela, on the day of 23.09.2022 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Moreover, it is hereby requested to notify me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or b efore 09.09.2022, the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/ servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of computation of such amount.
