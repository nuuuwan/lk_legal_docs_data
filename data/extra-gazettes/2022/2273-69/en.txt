Whereas the office of member of  Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sahan Udesh 
Bopitiya  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the 
member of that 10 Kirimetithenna of Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Jawaddanage Premadasa to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of  
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jawaddanage Premadasa 
was elected as the member of the 10 Kirimetithenna of Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 S
uranga ambagahathenne,
 Returning Officer,
 Balangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
