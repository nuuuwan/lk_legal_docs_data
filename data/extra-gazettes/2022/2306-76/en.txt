WHEREAS the office of  return member of Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be 
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress the recognized political party to which Atham 
Levvei Muhammad Amsa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Meeralevve Muhammadu Riyas to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Addalachchenai 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Meeralevvei Muhammadu Riyas was selected as the member of the Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha.
