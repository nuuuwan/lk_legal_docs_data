CANCELLATION  Of  LICENCE  NOTIfICATION 
CaNCellaTioN of licence issued to sinhaputhra Finance PlC, due to amalgamation with Commercial leasing and 
Finance PlC under the Finance Business act, No. 42 of 2011. 
 Dr. P. NaNDalal WeerasiNghe,
 Chairman of the monetary Board and the Governor of the  
 Central Bank of sri lanka.
The public is hereby informed that, due to the amalgamation of sinhaputhra Finance PlC with Commercial leasing 
and Finance PlC as part of the masterplan for Consolidation of Non-Bank Financial institutions, the monetary Board of 
the Central Bank of sri lanka, in terms of the Finance Business act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the licence issued to 
Sinhaputhra Finance PLC of 11, Hill Street, Kandy, to carry on finance business, with effect from 21.03.2022. Sinhaputhra 
Finance PlC has not accepted deposits from the public with effect from 21.03.2022.
