WHEREAS the office of return member of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three 
consecutive meetings without obtaining permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha Mohottige 
Don Yasarathna Chandradasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Liyana Athukoralalage Madhura Sanjeewa Athukorala to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, returning officer of the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Liyana 
Athukoralalage Madhura Sanjeewa Athukorala was selected as the member of the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
 K. J. S. Madhawa,
 Returning officer,
 Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.

Gampaha.
