under  section  66a(1) oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  (chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Nagoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which Kariyawasam 
Hewamanage Nadeeka Darshanie, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the returned member of Nagoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Priyangika Dilrukshi Ekanayaka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Nagoda Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Priyangika 
Dilrukshi Ekanayaka was selected as the member of the Nagoda Pradeshiya Sabha. 
W. H. R. Wijaya KumaRa,
Returning Officer,
Nagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
