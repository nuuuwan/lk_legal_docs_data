NOTIfICATION
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by 
section 4 of the  Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby once again enlarge the time up to 21st september, 2022 
for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry for appraisal 
of the findings of previous Commissions and Committees on Human Rights and the way forward established under Section 
2 of said Act on 20
th January, 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette  Extraordinary  No. 2211/55 dated  21st january, 
2021 of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka, having enlarged of time ending on 21 st july, 2021 by six months 
from 21st july, 2021 up to 21st january, 2022 by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2238/6 dated 27 july, 2021 and again enlarged 
from 21st january, 2022 up to 21st june, 2022 by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2263/4 dated 18th january, 2022, in the exercise 
of powers vested in me. 
         Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
                  President.
Presidential secretariat,
