WHEREAS, the office of   Member of Gampaha Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized Political Party to which Appuacharige 
Don Nilantha Dilruk Priyadarshana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon  member of that ward Number 15 - Yakkala West of Gampaha Municipal Council who belongs to 
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared Selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Narangoda Appuhamillage Wasantha Ranjith Samarasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Gampaha Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Narangoda Appuhamillage Wasantha Ranjith Samarasinghe was Selected as the member of  the ward Number 15 - Yakkala 
West of Gampaha Municipal Council.
  
 K. J. S. M
adhawa,
