WHEREAS the office of return member of  Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of non - attendance 
for consecutive three terms for a period of three months;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which M. M. Somathilaka 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member 
of Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
E. H. S. Edirisinghe to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, returning officer of the Polgahawela 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that E. H. S. Edirisinghe was selected as the member of the Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
 R. M. H. B. Rathnayaka,
 Returning officer,
 Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election office, 
Kurunegala.
02nd of March 2022.
