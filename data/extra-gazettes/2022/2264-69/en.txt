Whereas the office of member of  Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha 
Mudiyanselage Wijerathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that 04 - Kanumale of Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Walgama Ariyakiththi Thero to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer  of  the Polgahawela 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Walgama Ariyakiththi Thero was elected as the member of the   04 - Kanumale of Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
