PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 01.12.2022
2A
REGULATIONS
 These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 22 of 2022”. 
 The Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2278/21 
dated May 06, 2022 as amended by the Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 15 of 2022, published in 
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2297/79 dated September 16, 2022 is hereby further amended.
 Date of arrival of import consignments of  Maize (HS Code of 1005.90.00)  and Soya Bean Meal (HS Code of 
2304.00.00) at the Port / Airport in Sri Lanka under the Open Account / Consignment Account Payment terms, by 
registered poultry feed manufacturers on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry in charge of subject of 
Agriculture, for the purpose of manufacturing of poultry feed as authorized by the Regulation No. 5 of the Imports 
Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 15 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2297/79 dated 
September 16, 2022, is hereby extended until December 31, 2022.
 The Director General, Sri Lanka Customs shall consult the Secretary, Ministry in charge of subject of Agriculture on 
any importation made in violation of the regulation No. 3 above.
