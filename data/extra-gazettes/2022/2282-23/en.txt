SPECIAL COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT, NO. 48 OF 2007
Order under Section 2 
BY virtue of the powers vested  in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I , Ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the commodities speciﬁed in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special 
Commodity Levy at the rate speciﬁed in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
   This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from June 01, 2022 subject to provisions of Sub 
Section (3) of Section 2 of the special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
  The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notiﬁcation No. 2270/19 of March 09, 2022 and the Order made under section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy 
Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁcation No. 2273/01 of March 28, 2022 are rescinded 
with effect from June 01, 2022.
  
 R
ANIL WICKREMESINGHE,  
 Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization 
 and National Policies.
Ministry of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies,
Colombo 01,
31st May, 2022
