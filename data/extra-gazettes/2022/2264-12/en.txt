(AmENDmENT )  AcT,  No.  16  OF  2017    
whereas the office of  Chairman of Embilipitiya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of removal of the President 
from his post by the Governor under Section 184 of the Urban Council Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  
Wawulanbokka Gamage Lalith Dharmakeerthi the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of 
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of Embilipitiya Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be 
declared elected as Chairman within a  period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate 
Subhasingha Pathiranage Dinesh Madushankha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne returning Officer of the Embilipitiya Urban Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Subhasingha Pathiranage Dinesh Madushankha elected as Chairman 
of the Embilipitiya Urban Council.
 Suranga Ambagahathenne,
 Returning Officer,
 Embilipitiya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
26th  January, 2022.
