THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 My No.: 3/2/3/10/272. 
Ministry of Land Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2017/HW/418. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following lands which are required for             
a public purpose. 
 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired: 
 
 
Lot No. 1 (1 Lot) allotment in total extent of about 
0.0391 Hectare, of land in Cadastral Map No. 510610, 
Block No.03, Sheet No. 1 and dated 22.04.2022 prepared 
by the Senior Superintendent of  Surveys of Gampaha 
District on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of No. 328A, Thalgasmote in 
village of Thalgasmote in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Attanagalla in Gampaha District in the  
Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 312 
Name of the Land: Koongahawatta; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Kurugamage 
Ariyawansha,No. 49/2, Nittambuwa Road, Veyangoda, 
(to finalize the land acquisition process); Extent: 0.0391  
Hectare. 
 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agent duly authorized 
in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat of 
Attanagalla on the from 15.06.2022 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to  me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 07.06.2022 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
  S.P. G UNAWARDHANA, 
  Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer, 
                                                Attanagalla. 
 
  
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Attanagalla,
