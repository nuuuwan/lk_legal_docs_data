Whereas the office of member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending three 
consecutive meetings without permissions ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamed Usanar 
Mohamed Niflar the member elected belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that ward No. 16 Manchanthoduwai of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Abdul Carder Mohamed Latiff to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Carder 
Mohamed Latiff was elected as the member of the Ward No. 16 Manchanthoduwai of Batticaloa Municipal Council.   
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
