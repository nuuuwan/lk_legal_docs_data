I, M. M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with the powers 
vested in me under the Section 66C to be read with Section 66G of the Amended Local Government Elections Ordinance of the 
Local Government Elections (Amendment) Ordinance No. 22 of 2012, the meeting to elect a new Chairman to fill the vacant post 
of Chairman of Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha in Uva Province, will be convened on the 20th of December 2022 at 11.00 a.m. at the 
Council Chamber, Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha, Lunugala.
