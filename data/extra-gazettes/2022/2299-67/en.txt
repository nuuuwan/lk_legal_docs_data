ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16 Of   1978  
Order  under  Section 27(1) read  with  Section 21
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dr. a. d. susil premajayantha, 
minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 21 of the said act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 896/2 of November 07, 1995 
(as amended from time to time) establishing the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, which specifies the Faculties and 
departments of study of the sabaragamuwa University of sri Lanka, by the repeal of the entry "department of Family 
Medicine" appearing in Column II of the said Schedule, (Specified in the Order Published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 
2082/32 of august 01, 2018) which corresponds to the item "Faculty of medicine" appearing in Column i of the schedule, 
and the substitution therefor of  the following entry :-
                        "Department of Primary Care and Family Medicine".
 d r. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 minister of education.
Colombo.
