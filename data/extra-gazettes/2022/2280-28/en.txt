UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of Uva Province
BY virtue, of powers vested in me under Section 32 (1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, A.J.M. Muzammil 
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me.
 01. Mr. Gamini Mahindapala Jopias, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, appointed as the 
Secretary to the Uva Provincial Council Public Service Commission, with effect from the date of 01.07.2021.
 02. Dr. Mr. A. M. T. K. Atthanayake, Sri Lanka Deputy Medical Administrative Service, appointed to act as the Regional 
Director of the Health Service of Monaragala, in addition to the duties of his permanent post, for a period of one 
year, with effect from the date 01.01.2022.
  
 03. Eng. Mr. K. A. B. Senevirathne, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, appointed as the Deputy 
Chief Secretary (Engineering Services) with effect from the date 17.01.2021.
 
 04. Dr. Mrs. M. P. S. Karunarathne, Assistant Ayurveda Commissioner, to Coverup the duties of the Ayurveda 
Commissioner of Uva Province with effect from the date 31.01.2022 till, Ayurveda Commissioner of Uva Province, 
P. G. D. S. Premathilaka resumes her duty who is under going for treatment for her illness.
 05. Engineer. R. M. E. Lakmal Mechanical Engineer, to Coverup the duties of the Provincial Mechanical Director of Uva 
Province, with effect from the date 02.02.2022 till, Provincial Mechanical Director Eng. Mr. M. T. G. M. Lakmal, 
resumes his duties who is under going for treatment for his illness.
 06. Mr. W. M. P. P. Kumara, Grade III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the 
Municipal Commissioner of Bandarawela Municipal Council, in addition to his duties of his permanent post and 
acting post from the date 28.02.2022 till the return to the Island of T. Gajendran Kumar Municipal Commissioner 
of the Bandarawela Municipal Council, who is abroad from the date 28.02.2022 to 02.06.2022.
    A. J. M. M
uzAMMil,
   Governor, 
   Uva Province.
