I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for a public purpose.  
 
 
Description of the land or servitude  to be acquired: 
 
 
Undermentioned allotments of lands in total extent of 
0.0251 hectares shown as lot No. 776,  in the plan of 
F.V.P. 166, supplement No.35, sheet No.37 dated 
29.08.2022 prepared by Surveyor General situated in the 
village of Radawela, Kithsiripura Grama Niladhari 
Division, Mulatiyana Divisional Secretary's Division, 
Matara District, Southern Province. 
 
 
01. Lot No. 776 
Name of the Land: Pansalawatta alias egodakoratuwa; 
Description: Garden, A 20-30 years old mixcrop 
Permanent building where the Divinaguma Prajamula 
Bank located and watertank; Claimants: Chef incumbent, 
Kirthi Sri thejowararamaya; Extent: 0.0251  Hectare. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretariat Mulatiyana on the 01st 
day of March 2023 at 10.30 am and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (induplicate) on or before the 24th 
day of February 2023 the nature of their interests in the 
land the particulars of their claims for computation for the 
said land/ servitude the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.
