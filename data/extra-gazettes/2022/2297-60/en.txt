Provide an Opportunity for Public Consideration of the Draft Budget Statement for the Year 2023
Pursuant to the powers available in section 184 of the Provincial Council act, no. 15 of 1987 read with section 02 of the 
Provincial Council (subsidiary Provisions) act, no. 12 of 1989 and section 223 of the regional Council act, no. 15 of 1987, on 
29.10.2020 through the Governor of uva Province, special Gazette no. 2199/25 of the “2020 Pradeshiya sabha Budget Formulation 
and Implementation act” promulgated by 10(2)(b) the draft budget statement proposed to be presented by the Haputale Pradeshiya 
sabha for the year 2023 will be submitted to the public consideration from 19.09.2022. this paper hereby announces that an 
opportunity has been set up in the appropriate at Public Library Diyatalawa.
 
 B. K
andasamy,
 Chairman.
Haputale Pradeshiya sabha,
