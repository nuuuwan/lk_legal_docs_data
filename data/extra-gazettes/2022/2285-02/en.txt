WESTERN  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
The inquiry being conducted under Section 184(1A) of the urban Council ordinance and with Section 2 of the Provincial 
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989.
I, Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of Western Province do here by announce that the period of investigation of 
single person inquiry Board comprise of retired judge of the Court of Appeal hon. Chandradasa Nanayakkara, appointed by 
extra-ordinary Gazette of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2152/14 dated 3rd december, 2019 to inquiry 
whether the offences stipulated under sub Section 184(1) of the aforesaid urban Councils ordinance have been committed 
by Wattala-Mabola Urban Council and by Chairman of Wattala - Mabola Urban Council while functioning in his office and 
submit a report within 03 months shall be extended until 03rd September, 2022 according to the powers vested in me in 
terms of Section 184(1A) of the urban Council ordinance read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential 
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
