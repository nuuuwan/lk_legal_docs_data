PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
PRESIDENTIAL   ELECTIONS  (SPECIAL  PROvISIONS)  ACT, No. 2 Of 1981
Notice in terms of Section 17
I, dhammika dasanayake, secretary - General of Parliament, do hereby notify in terms of section 17 of the Presidential 
elections (special Provisions) act, No. 2 of 1981, that the Hon. Ranil wickremesinghe, Member of Parliament, has been 
declared elected by Parliament on wednesday, the 20th of july 2022 as the eighth executive President of the democratic 
socialist Republic of sri lanka, in terms of section 11 of the aforesaid act.
 Dhammika Dasanayake,
 secretary - General of Parliament.
at sri jayewardenepura, Kotte, 
wednesday, the 20th of july 2022.
