PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  MUSALI PRADESHIYA  SABHA  
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1) (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE 
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of Member of  Musali Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Jabarullakhan Fayes  
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member 
of that ward 07 - Potkerny of Musali Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁ ed under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sathakkaththulla Abdul Rakuman to ﬁ ll such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, K. Vimalaruban, Returning Ofﬁ cer of the Musali Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Selection 66A(1)
(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sathakkaththulla Abdul Rakuman was 
elected as the member of  the ward 07 - Potkerny of Musali Pradeshiya Sabha.  
K. VIMALARUBAN,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Musali Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Mannar,
19th August, 2022.
