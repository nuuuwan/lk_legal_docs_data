MEDIATION  BOARDS  COMMISSION
Notice Calling for Nominations for the Appointment of Meditators
THe mediation boards Commission, by virtue of the power vested in it by section 4(1) of the mediation (special Categories 
of disputes) act, No. 21 of 2003, invites nominations for the appointment of Chairman and mediators in respect of resolving 
all disputes relating to state Lands and Private Lands subject to the provisions contained in the Extraordinary Gazette 
Notification No. 2261/68 dated 06.01.2022 for the following Panels of Special Mediation Boards who are to vacate post in 
terms of sections 1 to 5 in the schedule of the mediation (special Categories of disputes) act, No. 21 of 2003.
Administrative District Special Mediation Board Areas Date of Vacation of Post
Anuradhapura - Anuradhapura District - 26.03.2019
Jaffna - Jaffna District - 27.05.2019
Kilinochchi - Kilinochchi District - 27.05.2019
Batticaloa - Batticaloa District - 29.07.2019
Trincomalee - Trincomalee District - 29.07.2019
Mannar - Mannar District - 01.12.2021
Vavuniya - Vavuniya District - 01.12.2021

2. The mediation boards Commission hereby gives notice calling  nominations upon (a) the individuals, (b) the bodies, 
Origanizations and Institutions of nonpolitical nature, and (C) the District Secretaries/Divisional Secretaries specified 
hereinafter under 02 (II) to submit names of person who are eligible under 02 (I) for appointment as mediators to the 
Panel of mediators for the said special mediation board areas;
 (I) The following Persons are entitled to be appointed to the Panel of a particular special mediation board area 
whose names may be so submitted for appoinment.
 (a) a  person eligible to be appointed as a member of the Panel of mediators in respect of the special 
mediation boards shall be between 35 to 70 years of age and;
 (b) Has any professional qualification obtained from a recognized professional body established by or under 
any written law;
 (c) other than the existing mediators, the nominations,
 (i) a degree recognized by the University Grants Commission or equivalent qualification,
or
 (ii) who have succesfully completed GCe (o/L) or GCe (a/L) and hold 5 years of experience relating 
to Land as a Public Officer.  
 (II) (a) The following Individuals are entitled to submit nominations other thn themselves for appointment to the 
Panel of a particular special mediation board area: 
 (i) A Public Officer or a Provincial Public Officer serving as the Head of a Department or as the Regional 
Head of a Department in an office located withing the administrative District at which that particular 
special mediation board area is situated: 
 (ii) a  Head of a Place or religious worship or of school situated in that particular special mediation board 
area;
 (iii) a  retired Head of the department or school principal residing in that particular special mediation 
board area;
 (iv) Chairman of Panel of special mediation board.
 (B) Any board, Organization or Institution which has been in existence at least for a period of five years and 
which is engaged in carrying out or doing anyone or more of the under mentioned objectives;
 (i) The enhancement of educational, religious, moral or spiritual, advancement of the community.
 (ii) The enhancement of social welfare and eradicaton of poverty.
 (iii) The enhancement of rural or community development.
 (iv) The enhancement of culture of sports. 
 (v) The enhancement of projects, programmes and activities intending to provide and supply basic needs 
of the peoples such as health, food and shelter etc.
 (C) The respective district secretary/divisional secretaries are entitled to submit nominations for appointment 
to the Panel of a particular special mediation board area:-
3. (a) Individual and boards, organization and Institutions referred to in Paragraph (02) above may submit nominations 
of such Public Officers as are specified in Paragraph 2(a) and 2(b) above. An individual may not nominate his 
or her name for nominations.

4. Form of Nominations:
 (a) Individuals should submit nominations substantially in Form “a” below;
 (b) boards, organizations and Institutions should submit nominations 
  substantially in Form “b” below; and.
 (c) district secretaries, divisinal secretaries should submit nominations
  substantially in Form “C” below; 
 each nomination should be in a separate Form and should without fail contain the recommendation of the nominator 
as indicated in the next paragraph.
5. The recommendation of the nominator should not be a recommendation without reasons being assigned. It required 
to set out specific facts and circumstances to enable the Commission to draw its own reference to the suitability or 
otherwise of the person nominated for appointment to the Panel of mediators, such as, for instance, his or her present 
or past occupation, the period of service or engagement in such occupations, any position or trust responsibility held by 
him or her and the office, if any, held by him or her in any social service/ religious/ charitable Organization, Society or 
body.
6. an individual or a body, organizations or Institution should as far as possible, refrain from making more than three 
nominations in respect of the same special mediation board area. However, this limitation will not apply to the district 
secretaries, and divisional secretaries.
7. all nominations should be forwarded to reach Secretary, Mediation Board Commission, No. 80, Ministry of Justice's 
New Building, Courts Road, Colombo 12 on or before 01
st of December  2022.
8. The Mediation Boards Commission will, based on the basic qualifications to be checked at interview, select eligible 
candidates to be offered a preliminary training on mediation skills and methods. based on the recommendations, the 
mediation boards Commission will appoint the mediators on merit basis. The selected mediators to be offered a basic 
training on Legal and Procedural aspects relating to Land, and a training on Gender equality.
9. Final decision in connection with any matter which is not being prevailed by the regulation stipulated in this notice is 
the decision made by mediation boards Commission at any time.
