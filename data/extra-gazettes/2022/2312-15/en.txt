EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Order under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the 
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Anuradha yahampath, the Governor of the eastern Province, do hereby made Order as described in the schedule hereto 
by virtue of powers vested in me under  section 223 of the Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with section 2 of the 
Provincial councils (consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Anuradha Yahampath,
Governor,
eastern Province.
Governor's secretariat,
Orr's Hill,
Trincomalee,
22nd of december, 2022.
Order
The chairman of Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha in Trincomalee district within the Province of the east has submitted 
and failed to get adopted the budget of the year 2023 as the power vested in him under section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya 
sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987. Thus the said chairman of the said Pradeshiya sabha not complied, as provided under section 
17 of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the 
Chairman effective from 16.12.2022 and that the office of the Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha become vacant effective 
from that date.

Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 171(b) of the Pradeshiya sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, I, Anuradha 
yahampath, Governor of the eastern Province, do hereby make the following rule for the administration of fund and property 
and for the matters connected therewith and incidental thereto enforceable during the period as described in the rule hereof.
Anuradha Yahampath,
Governor,
eastern Province.
Governor's secretariat,
Orr's Hill,
Trincomalee,
22nd of december, 2022.
rule
whereas the budget of the year 2023 of the Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha in the district of Trincomalee within the limits 
of eastern Province Provincial council was failed to adopt as required under section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya sabhas 
Act, No. 15 of of 1987. Thus the offices of the Chairman of the Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha hereto became vacant in accordance 
to section 17 of the local Authorities (special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and hence the chairman be appointed to that 
vacant position incompliance to local Authorities elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit 
a budget for the year 2023 to the Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha hereto. As such I, authorise Assistant commissioner of local 
Government of the Trincomalee district to Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha to grant approval to authorize the expenses for the 
remaining period in 2022 and the following proposals proposed in the aforesaid draft budget during the period commencing 
from 16.12.2022 till such a date on which a chairman is appointed to that vacant positions of the Kinniya Pradeshiya sabha.
Proposals
 1. salaries and w ages
 2. Travelling Allowances
 3. stationaries
 4. recurrent expenses
 5. ldsP & cdlG Project expenses
 6. Provincial Specific Development Grant & Criteria Base Grant
