Order under Section 14A
by, virtue of the powers vested in me under section 14A read with section 11 of the V oluntary social services Organizations 
(registration and supervision) Act, No. 31 of 1980 and Paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the constitution, I, Tiran Allas, the 
minister of the Public security do by this Order, appoint an Interim board of management to administer the affairs of the 
community Trust Fund, bearing No. L.63615 registered under section 05 of the aforesaid act for a period of two years 
commencing from the date of publication of this Order or till such period as may be necessary to ensure that financial 
management of that Organization is restored.
The members of the Interim Board of Management shall be as specified in the Schedule hereto.
 
Tiran allas,
 minister of Public security.
colombo,
23rd November, 2022.
