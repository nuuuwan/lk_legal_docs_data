FILLING  OF  A  V AcANcY  IN  ThE  OFFIcE  OF  mEmBER  OF 
 ThE  KESBEWA  URBAN  cOUNcIL  UNDER  SEcTION  66A (1) (a)  OF  ThE  
LOcAL  AUThORITIES  ELEcTIONS  ORDINANcE  (chAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Kesbewa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to, which Porage 
Don Kamal Ranjana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon  the member of that Ward No. 05 Bokundara of Kesbewa Urban Council, who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Yapa Mudiyanselage Jayarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Kesbewa Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Yapa Mudiyanselage Jayarathna was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 05 Bokundara of Kesbewa Urban Council.
 M. M. S. K. B
anadara Mapa,

FILLING  OF  A  V AcANcY  IN  ThE  OFFIcE  OF  mEmBER  OF 
 ThE  ANURADhAPURA  mUNIcIPAL  cOUNcIL  UNDER  SEcTION  66A (1) (a)  OF  ThE  
LOcAL  AUThORITIES  ELEcTIONS  ORDINANcE  (chAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Anuradhapura Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three 
Consecutive General Meetings of the Council permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to, which Mahampa 
Delgoda Kankanamalage Vinodani Wijesooriya the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that  01 - Pethispura of Anuradhapura Municipal Council, who belongs 
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who 
shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Jayasiri Mudiyanselage Manisha Roshini Bandara to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer of the Anuradhapura Municipal Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jayasiri Mudiyanselage 
Manisha Roshini Bandara was selected as the member of  the  01 - Pethispura of Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
 K. Mugunthan,
