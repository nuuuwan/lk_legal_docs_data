LAND REFORMS ACT, NO. 01 OF 1972 OF THE NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 27 a (1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by the Section 27 a (1) of the Land Reforms Law No. 01 of 1972 amended by the Act, 
Nos. 39 of 1975, 14 of 1981, 39 of 1981, and 18 of 1986, I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands, 
do hereby alienate allotments of agricultural land which are vested in the Land Reforms Commission under the provisions 
stipulated in the aforesaid act, to the Road Develpoment Authority established by the Act, No. 73 of 1981, by way of sale in 
accordance with the Section 22 (1) bb of the said Land Reforms Act.
1).   Lands Scheduled below are vested in the said Road Development Authority for a sum of Rs. 26,200,000/- 
(Rupes Twenty Six Million Two Hundred Thousand) as the monetary consideration for the aforesaid sale.
