THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 40)
Curfew Order under Section 16
WHEREAS, I consider that it is necessary to maintain public order in the areas specified in the Schedule hereto, I, Gotabaya 
rajapaksa, president, by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 16 of the public security Ordinance (Chapter 40) 
do by this Order direct that no person shall be on any public road, railway, public park, public recreation ground or other 
public ground or the seashore in such areas, from 18.00 hour of april 02
nd, 2022 to 06.00 hour of april 04th, 2022, except 
under the authority of a written permit granted by secretary, Ministry of Defence or secretary, Ministry of public security 
or Inspector-General of Police or any officer authorized by them.
 
Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
         president.
Colombo, 
april 02, 2022.
