THE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) Read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, a. d. susil Premajayanth, 
Minister of education,  on the recommendation of the university grants Commission, do by this order, amend the schedule 
to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinery No. 1518/15 of October 09, 2007 (as amended from time to time), which 
specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, Which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of 
section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said act, by the addition immediately 
after entry “4. department of Inter - disciplinary studies” appearing in Column II of the schedule, which corresponds to 
the item “Faculty of Engineering” appearing in Column I of that Schedule, (specified in the Order published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 1787/27 of december, 5,2012) of the following entry :-
 
 “5. department of Mechanical engineering”.
 
A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnth,
 Minister of Education.
Colombo,
