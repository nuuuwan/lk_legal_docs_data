WHEREAS, the office of member of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Rasenthiran 
Yanaki the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that ward 08 - Kaithadi South of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of Thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Vanniyasingam Indrani to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Vanniyasingam 
Indrani was elected as the member of the ward 08 - Kaithadi South of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
