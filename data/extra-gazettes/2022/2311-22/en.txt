WHEREAS the office of  member of Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Sivananthan 
Meharaj the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of  that Ward No. 06 - Karuveppankerny of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Vairamuththu Thineshkumar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Vairamuththu 
Thineshkumar was elected as the member of the Ward No. 06 - Karuveppankerny of  Batticaloa Municipal Council.
   
 M. B. M. Sufyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Batticaloa Municipal Council.
