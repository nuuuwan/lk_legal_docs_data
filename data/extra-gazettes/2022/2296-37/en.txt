PRADEShIYA  SAbhA  ACT,  No. 15  Of  1987
Order
by virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraphs (a), (b) and (c) of section 9a of pradeshiya sabha act, No. 15 of 1987, 
i, dinesh Gunawardena, prime minister and minister of public administration, Home affairs, provincial Councils and Local 
Government, do hereby dissolve, trincomalee town and Gravets pradeshiya sabha constituted by the order published in 
Gazette Extraordinary, No. 453/20 of 12th may 1987, with effect from 19th march 2023 in order to constitute new trincomalee 
town and Gravets pradeshiya sabha replacing and having amended the limits and number of members.
 Dinesh GunawarDena,
 prime minister and 
 minister of public administration, Home  
 affairs, provincial Councils and Local  
 Government.
Colombo,
05th september, 2022.
