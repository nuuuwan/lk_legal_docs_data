THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No. : 3/2/1/140.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 4-3/8/2018/PC/468.
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
the public purpose:-
Description of the Land/servitude to be Acquired :
Allotment of Land in extent of 0.1624 Hectare described below
and depicted as Lot 1 in the Preliminary Plan No. GAM 4278 dated
28.12.2021 prepared by Senior Superintendent of Survey for the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village called Ginihimulla in Grama
Niladhari Division No. 107 of Ginihimulla in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Minuwangoda in Gampaha District of the
Western Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Paragahawatte; Description: Home Garden, 16
coconut trees of 20-40 years; Claimant: State, S.P.A. Arunasiri
Samarakoon residing at the address of Dewalapola, Watinapahawatte;
Extent: 0.1624 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing
before me at Minuwangoda Divisional Secretariat on the 27.04.2022
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 20.04.2022 the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
U. W. T. U. RAJAKARUNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Minuwangoda.
03rd March, 2022.
At Divisional Secretariat - Minuwangoda.
