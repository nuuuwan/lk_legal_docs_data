AMENDMENT
Land Development Ordinance - Regulations
I  do hereby declare that “Regulations made by the Minister of Lands under Sections 155 and 156 of the Land Development
Ordinance (Chapter 464), as amended by Act, No. 19 of 1969, Act, No. 27 of 1981, Act, No. 22 of 1993, Act No. 09 of 1995 and
Act No. 20 of 1996” which appeared in my notice published in Sinhala and Tamil languages in page 1A of the Gazette Extra
Ordinary No. 2251/48 dated 28.10.2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be corrected as “Regulations
made by the Minister of Lands under Sections 155 and 156 of the Land Development Ordinance (Chapter 464), as amended
by Act No. 16 of 1969, Act, No. 27 of 1981, Act, No. 22 of 1993, Act, No. 09 of 1995 and Act, No. 20 of 1996.”
S. M. CHANDRASENA,
Minister of Lands.
