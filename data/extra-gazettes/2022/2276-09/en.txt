Order
the Chairman of lahugala pradeshiya sabha in Ampara district within the province of the east has submitted and failed 
to get adopted the Budget of the year 2022, as the power vested in him under section 168 and 169 of the pradeshiya sabha 
Act No. 15 of 1987, thus the said Chairman of the said pradeshiya sabhas not complied, as provided under section 17 of the 
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman 
effective  from 01.04.2022 and that the office of the Chairman of that Pradeshiya Sabha’s become vacant effective from that date.

EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Pradeshiya Sabhas Act No. 15 of 1987
By Virtue, of powers vested in me under section 171 (b) of the pradeshiya sabhas Act No. 15 of 1987, i, Anuradha 
yahampath Governor of the eastern province, do hereby make the following rule for the administration of fund and property 
and for the matters connected therewith and incidental thereto enforceable during the period as described in the rule hereof. 
       
    AnurAdhA YAhAmpAth,
   Governor, 
   eastern province.
Governor’s secretariat,
Orr’s Hill,
trincomalee,
19th April, 2022.
rule
Whereas the Budget of the year 2022 of the lahugala pradeshiya sabha in the district of Ampara within the limits of eastern 
province provincial Council was failed to adopt as required under section 168 and 169 of the pradeshiya sabhas Act No. 
15 of 1987. Thus  the officers of the Chairman of the Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha’s hereto became vacant in accordance to 
section  17 of the local Authorities (special provisions) Act No. 21 of 2012 and hence the Chairman be appointed to that 
vacant position incompliance to local Authorities elections (Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit 
a budget for the year 2022 to the lahugala pradeshiya sabha hereto, as such i, authorize Assistant Commissioner of local 
Government of the Ampara district to lahugala pradeshiya sabha to grant approval to the following proposals proposed in 
the aforesaid draft budget during the period commencing from 22.01.2022 till such a date on which a Chairman is appointed 
to that vacant positions of the lahugala pradeshiya sabha’s.
proposals
 01.  salaries and Wages
 02.  travelling Allowances
 03.  stationaries
 04.  recurrent expenses
 05.  ldsp and CdlG project expenses
 06.  Provincial Specific Development Grant and Criteria Base Grant
