THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
                               My Ref. No. :  Mahara/De/La.Acq/2/
 Kadawatha Town Dev./Bypass Road.
 Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2014/HW/98.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the  Government
intends to acquire the following lands which are required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Allotments of  Lot No. 01 in Total Extent of about 0.0053
Hectare, depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 4285 and Dated
07.03.2022 prepared by the Surveyor General,  situated in the
village of Pahala Karagahamuna in the Minor Division of
Adhikari Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Mahara in Gampaha District in Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Alubogahawatta; Description: Land
Acquisition for construction of Kadawatha Interchange;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: D. A. S. Silva ; Extent: 0.0053
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secrtariat  of Mahara on the
25.07.2022 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate)  on or before the 11.07.2022, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the asquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
G. S. I. Wimalasena,
Divisional Secretary and
Land Acquisition Officer,
Mahara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Mahara
