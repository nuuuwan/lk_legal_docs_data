WHEREAS, the office of return member of Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Hitihami 
Mudiyanselage Nalaka Sampath the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon the returned member of Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dhiyagu  Arachchilage Prasad Prasanna Gunawardhana to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer of  the Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dhiyagu Arachchilage 
Prasad Prasanna Gunawardhana was selected as the member of the Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
  
K. Mugunthan,
Returning Officer,
Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Anuradhapura.
