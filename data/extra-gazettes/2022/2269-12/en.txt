PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
LOcAL GOVERNmENT NOTIfIcATIONS
Notice under Section 66G to be read with Section 66C of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as 
amended by the Local Authorities Elections ordinance (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Dulip Somirathna, Commissioner of Local Government, Sabaragamuwa Province do hereby announce by virtue of powers 
vested in me under Section 66G to be read with section 66C of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as 
amended by Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, that the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha 
will be convened at 10.00 a. m. on 04th day of March, 2022 at the Auditorium of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha located at 
the address of Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, Mawanella, for the purpose of electing a new Chairman for the vacant chairman 
post of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha situated within the Sabaragamuwa Province.
Dulip Somirathna,
Commissioner of Local Government, 
Sabaragamuwa Province.
