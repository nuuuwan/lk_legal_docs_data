Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the   oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  tangalle  pradeshiya  
sabha  under section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return member of  Tangalle  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of been ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sayakkarage Kithsiri 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member 
of Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ariyachandrage Kodithuwakku Chanaka to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer of the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ariyachandrage 
Kodithuwakku Chanaka was selected as the member of  the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
