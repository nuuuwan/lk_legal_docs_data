WHEREAS the office of member of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which Thiyagarajah Thevaranjan the member belonged, was 
required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of Manmunai South 
and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Group Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  communicated his decision to nominate  
Thangarasa Raveendran to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Thangarasa Raveendran was selected as the member of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
