WHEREAS, the office of member of Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of the resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which 
Kopalasingam Vavikaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon member of that Ward No. 09  Sinnawatta of Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sivapadam Thayananthan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sivapadam 
Thayananthan was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 09  Sinnawatta of  Porathivu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. S
