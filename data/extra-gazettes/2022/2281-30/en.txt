WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Matale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Don 
Lasantha Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Matale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dilan Ravindra Liyanaarachchi to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dilan Ravindra Liyanaarachchi 
was selected as the member of the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
