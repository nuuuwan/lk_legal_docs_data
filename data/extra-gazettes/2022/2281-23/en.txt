Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
FILLING   OF   A  V AcANcY   IN  ThE OFFIcE OF  mEmBER  OF  ThE   BATTIcALOA  mUNIcIPAL 
cOUNcIL  UNDER  SEcTION 66 A (1) OF  ThE LOcAL  AUThORITIES  ELEcTIONS ORDINANcE  
(chAPTER 262)     
whereAs the office of return member of Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which Sivarajah 
Sivaraj the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned 
member of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30)
 days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Raveendran Hariprasath to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Raveendran 
Hariprasath was selected as the member of Batticaloa Municipal Council.
M. B. M. Sufyan,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
24th May, 2022.
