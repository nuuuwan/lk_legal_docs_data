regulations of sri Lanka Olympic committee Published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 241/3 dated 30th November 1976 
and amended by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 385/7a dated 20th January, 1986 and the Extraordinary Gazette No. 773/7 
dated 29th June, 1993 are further amended by inserting following new regulations immediately after its 09th regulation.
 
 10. The aggregate of maximum service period of any position out of three positions; President, secretary and Treasurer 
of the National Olympic committee or the aggregate service period of the said two positions is shall be two (02) 
terms; that is eight (8) years.
	 	 However,	 in	respect	of	the	officers;	who	have	been	appointed	by	the	election	as	at	the	effective	date	of	these	
regulations, the aforesaid regulation shall not be an obstacle to hold the said positions until the end of the term to 
which they have been appointed.
