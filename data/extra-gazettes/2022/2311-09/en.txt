ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE  (chAPTER  262) 
WHEREAS the office of   member of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Gnanamuthu 
Yoganathan the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of  that Ward No. 04 Kaluthavalai South of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who 
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and 
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kanapathippillai Thevamanokaran to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Kanapathippillai Thevamanokaran was selected as the member of the Ward No. 04 Kaluthavalai South of the Manmunai 
South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
