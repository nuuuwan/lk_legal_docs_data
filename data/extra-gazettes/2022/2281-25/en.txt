SPORTS  LAw,  No. 25  Of  1973
Regulation made by the Minister of Sports under the Section 32 and 33 of Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973
 I, roshan ranasinghe, the minister of sports and youth affairs, as per the powers vested in me under sections 32(b) and 
(33)of the sport Law No. 25 of 1973 suspend the registration of the muaythai association of sri Lanka Which is for the 
sports of muaythai with immediate effect and ; as a temporary procedure, in respect of the administrative and other necessary 
functions of the Muaythai Association of Sri Lanka as well as for calling and holding relevant elections and fulfilling the 
duties duly until then, mr. amal Edirisuriya ; the director General, department of sports development, is hereby appointed 
as the competent authority with effect from 01.06.2022.
 
Roshan Ranasinghe (M.P.)
 minister of sports and youth affairs.
at Colombo, 
On 24th day of may, 2022.
