WHEREAS the office of return member of Poojapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Hurigolle Gedara 
Champika Suchithra Kumari Hurigolla the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Poojapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Batagalla Konage Inoka Niroshani to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Poojapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Batagalla 
Konage Inoka Niroshani was selected as the member of the Poojapitiya Pradeshiya  Sabha.

WHEREAS the office of  member of Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three consecutive 
general meetings without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Abdul Azees 
Mohomad Faris the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide

disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mohamad Hafish Marikkar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohamad 
Hafish Marikkar was elected as the member of the Ward Number 02 - Senkadagala of the Kandy Municipal Council.
