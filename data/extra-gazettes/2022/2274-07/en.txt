STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. W. G. I. M. Amarasinghe who Worked as a Sales Representative of Metro Auto Mart (Pvt) Ltd is Entitled For,
 - Commissions and Telephone charges for the period from January 2017 to February 2018, 
 - Vehicle allowance for the two months of August and September 2017, and 
 -The commission of 1% deducted from the commission by the establishment during his period of service, 
and if so, to what relief he is entitled for the injustice caused by not receiving them.
 
 B. K. P
