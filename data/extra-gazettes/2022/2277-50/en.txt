WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
a member of the party;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Abesekarage 
Chaminda Kelum Abesekara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the returned member of Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Abesingha Mudiyanselage Senavirathna Banda to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer of the Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abesingha Mudiyanselage 
Senavirathna Banda was selected as the member of the Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha.
