Notice of the Election Commission on the Display of ‘A’ and ‘B’ Lists, Claims and Objections
Definition
01.	 “Act”		 	-		 Registration	of	Electors	Act,	No.	44	of	1980	as	amended	by	the	Registration	of 
	 	 Electors	(Amendment)	 Act,	No.	22	of	2021
02.	 “	‘A’	List”	 	-		 List	of	persons	whose	names	are	included	in	the	2021	Electoral	Register,	who	have	 
	 	 been	 disqualified	due	to	death	or	leaving	the	residence	or	for	any	other	reason,	namely	 
	 	 those	who	are	recommended	for	removal	in	the	year	2022
03.	 “	‘B’	List”	 	-		 List	of	persons	who	are	not	included	in	the	2021	Electoral	Register	but	are	eligible	 
	 	 to	 be	included	in	the	2022	Electoral	Register	on	completion	of	18	years	of	age	or	 
	 	 residency	or	for	any	other	reason.
Exhibition	Centers	of	Electoral	Register	(or	part	thereof)	for	the	year	2021 and			“A”	List	and	“B”	Lists
 Exhibition Center Electoral register and lists or parts thereof kept for display

	 electoral	district	(district)	and	copies	of	the	relevant	“A”	and	“B”	lists
02.		Each	Divisional	Secretariat	 A	copy	of	the	2021	Electoral	Register	section	showing	all	the	polling	 
	 districts	in	the	relevant	Divisional	Secretariat	and	copies	of	the	relevant

Instructions
01.		It	was	announced	today	(June	15,	2022)	that	the	Registering	Officers	in	each	electoral	district	have	completed	the	
preparation	of	Lists	“A”	and	“B”	in	accordance	with	Section	13	of	the	Registration	of	Electors	Act.
02.		Every	person	who	is	qualified	in	terms	of	Section	14	of	the	Registration	of	Electors	Act	to	have	his	own	name	or	someone	
else’s	name	entered	in	the	said	electoral	register	applicable	to	each	electoral	district	and	whose	name	has	been	omitted	
from	the	electoral	register	and	from	List	“B”	or	whose	name	has	been	included	in	List	“A”	may	claim	to	have	his	name	
entered	in	such	register.	Claims	to	have	his	or	someone	else's	name	entered	or	retained	in	the	register	may	be	made	by	
himself	or	by	someone	else	on	his	behalf,	as	the	case	may	be,	and	they	may	apply	substantially	in	form	“A”	and	“B”	set	
out	in	the	Schedule	to	above	Registration	of	Electors	Act	so	as	to	reach	the	registering	officer	of	each	electoral	district	
before	12.07.2022,	within	4	weeks	from	the	date	of	the	publication	of	this	notice	in	the	Gazette	of	the	Democratic	Socialist	
Republic	of	Sri	Lanka	i.e.	from	15	June	2022	.	
03.		Any	person	whose	name	appears	in	the	electrol	register	or	in	list	“B”	for	any	electoral	district	may	object	to	the	entry	of	
his	own	name	or	to	the	entry	or	retention	of	the	name	of	any	other	person	appearing	in	such	register	or	list	“B”	Every	
such	objection	shall	be	made	in	duplicate	and	shall	be	substantially	in	form	“C”	set	out	in	the	schedule	to	the	above	Act	
and	shall	be	made	so	as	to	reach	the	registering	officer	of	the	Election	Office.	All	objections	to	the	inclusion	of	the	name	
in	the	register	should	be	sent	to	the	registering	officer	of	the	District	Elections	Office	before	12.07.2022,	within	four	
weeks	from	the	date	of	the	publication	of	this	notice	in	the	Gazette	of	the	Democratic	Socialist	Republic	of	Sri	Lanka	
i.e. june 15, 2022.
04.		Every	objection	to	the	entry	of	the	name	of	any	claimant	in	the	electoral	register	shall	be	made	in	duplicate	and	shall	be	
substantially	in	form	“D”	set	out	in	the	schedule	to	the	above	Act	and	shall	be	made	so	as	to	reach	the	registering	officer	
within	fourteen	days	from	the	date	on	which	the	notice	containing	the	name	of	such	claimant	is	exhibited	at	the	District	
Election	Office.	This	notice	will	be	published	in	the	District	Election	Offices	on	1st	July	2022	and	19th	July	2022.
05.		Form	‘A’	‘B’	required	for	submission	of	claims	and	Form	‘C’	‘D’	required	for	submission	of	objections	can	be	obtained	
from	the	District	Election	Offices,	Divisional	Secretariats,	Local	Authorities	or	Grama	Niladhari	Offices.
 Nimal G. PuNchihewa,
	 Chairman,
	 Election	Commission.
