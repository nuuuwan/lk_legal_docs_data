PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance  
UDUNUwARA PRADEShIYA SABhA    
Notice  under  Section  10A(1) (a)  of  the  Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Udunuwara Pradeshiay Sabha, do hereby declare 
under Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage 
Bandula Kumara Senevirathne of the Udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya 
Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
 Dr. 
H. D. N. SampatH FerNaNDo,
 Returning Officer,
 Udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
