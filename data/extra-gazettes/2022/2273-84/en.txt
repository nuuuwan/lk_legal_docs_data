THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ministry of Land Ref. No. :4-3/05/2018/HA/454'

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land   to be Acquired :
An allotment of land herein under described as
'' Bandarawatta, Lunukerewa and Warahena Watta alias
Mahabandarawatta" in total extent of  0.7141 hectare (01  Acre
03 Roods, 2.3 Perches) depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary
Plan No. GA 4617 dated 31.12.2021 prepared by the Surveyor
General,  situated in the  village called " Warahena" in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. 02 A of Warahena in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Benthota in Galle District of
the Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land :Bandarawatta, Lunukerewa and
Warahena Estate alias Mahabandarawatta; Current Land
usage: Home Garden, a mixed cultivation is available; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant:Mangalika Amarasinghe, No. 03, Police
Park Place, Colombo -05. Lakshmi Shiranthanee Abhayakoon
and some others, No. 306 /2, Unit 04, 06
th floor, Kotte Road,
Nugegoda.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of Benthota on
27.04.2022 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby  requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) before the 20.04.2022, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,  the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
