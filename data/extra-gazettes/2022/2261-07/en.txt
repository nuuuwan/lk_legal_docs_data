Whereas the office of member of  Niyagama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member 
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Thotagoda Gamage 
Terie Hemal Jayaweera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ward Number 09, Niyagama of Niyagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Athukorala Don Chamara Nishantha Wijegunawardhana to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Niyagama Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Athukorala Don Chamara Nishantha Wijegunawardhana was elected as the member of the Ward Number 09, Niyagama of 
Niyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
