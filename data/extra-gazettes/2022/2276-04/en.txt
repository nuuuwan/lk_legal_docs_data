ThE UNIvERSITIES ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
 order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dinesh Gunawardena, 
minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this order, amend the Schedule 
to the order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 9, 2006, (as amended from time to time), which specifies 
the Faculties and the departments of the University of Colombo, Sri lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of 
Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately  
after entry ‘‘11. department of English language Teaching’’ appearing in Column ii of the Schedule which corresponds to the 
item ‘‘Faculty of Arts’’ appearing in Column I of the Schedule, (specified in the order published in the 
Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2025/39 of June 30, 2017) of the following new entry :-
‘‘12. department of information Technology”.
 
     
 Dinesh GunawarDena,
 minister of Education.
08th of  April, 2022.
Colombo.
