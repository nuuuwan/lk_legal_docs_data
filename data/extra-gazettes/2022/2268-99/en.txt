1. this permission may be cited as “Permission to disburse under Fourth Supplementary Financial Statement -2021 of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council”  and  come into operation from 31.12.2021.
2. the expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated rupees Forty billion  One Hundred 
and eighty three Million  Four Hundred and Ninety thousand eight Hundred and three and Cents Sixty eight Only  
(rs. 40,183,490,803.68) for the service of the period beginning on 01 st of January, 2021 and ending 31 st december 
2021, which is specified as “Financial Year 2021” in the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue- Expenditure 
estimates  prepared for the year 2021, amended by First Supplementary Financial Statement- 2021, that was approved 
on 09.08.2021,  is  hereby amended as, rupees Forty billion  One Hundred and Ninety One Million  Four Hundred and 
Ninety thousand eight Hundred and three and Cents Sixty eight Only (rs. 40,191,490,803.68) and grant permission 
to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers  to  disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council Fund.
