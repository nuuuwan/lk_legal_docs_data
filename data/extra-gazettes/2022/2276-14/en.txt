WHEREAS the office of return member of Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the leader of the Independent Group 1, to which Seinul Abdeen Mohamad Aswarkhan the member belonged, 
was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Wanathawilluwa 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorites 
elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Komalathambiliya 
Godage Dilshan Dinuka Jayawardhana to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Siriniwasage Lakshitha Ruwan Jayanayake, returning officer of the Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Komalathambiliya 
Godage Dilshan Dinuka Jayawardhana was selected as the member of the Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
