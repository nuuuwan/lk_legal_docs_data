THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude described below 
in Paragraph 1 is required for a public purpose and will be 
acquired under the provisions of that Act. The land over 
which the said servitude is to be acquired is given in 
Paragraph 2. (The Declaration 5 dated on 26.07.2013 is 
herewith cancelled.) 
 1. Description of the Servitude .– Acquisition of access 
rights to the housing scheme. Access roads.
 2. Description of the Land.– An allotment of land which 
name and extent given below is depicted in the Advance 
Tracing No. GM/KE/99/252 and dated 25.01.2000, prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Polhena 
in the Minor Division of Adhikaripattuwa in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Kelaniya in Gampaha District of 
Western Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot No. 01 – Obanwatta or Polhenawatta – Extent: 
Approximately 0.0266 Hectare 
 North: The land belongs to Somawathi Perera, Assmt. 
No. 23, Polhena, Second Lane, the land belongs to Dayawathi 
Wijethilaka, Assmt. No. 29, Polhenawatta, Second Lane 
and the land belongs to National Housing Development 
Authority; East: The land belongs to National Housing 
Development Authority; South: The land belongs to 
National Housing Development Authority and Road; West: 
Road and the land belongs to Somawathi Perera, Assmt. 
No. 17, Polhena, Second Lane.
