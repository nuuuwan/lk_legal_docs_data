Direction No.  85
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.   09   of   2003  (Amended)
Direction under Section  10(1)(a)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs  all Importers, Producers,  Manufacturers, Store-owners, Distributors, Packers, Online
Traders and Traders shall not  Produce, Manufacture, Store, Distribute, Pack, Repack, sell or display for sale, expose for sale
or offer for sale unless the following details are legibly printed or non-removable labeled in a conspicuous place of the pack,
wrapper or container of the end product of the commercial goods purchased by the consumer at the retail price.
1. Maximum Retail Price
2. Weight or volume
3. Date of Manufacture and Date of packing if re-packaging
4. Date of Expiry/Best before use/Active period (except for the goods which does not expire)
5. Batch/Code Number
6. Name, Registered address and Contact Number/ web-site of Manufacturer
7. If it is imported name of  Country, Name, Address, Contact number/web-site of issuer or importer
This Direction shall come into effect from 28th July, 2022.
                                                                                               By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
