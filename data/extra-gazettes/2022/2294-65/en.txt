Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS, the office of the return member of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  
W. M. Susantha Karunathilake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the returned member of United People's Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Bandula Rathnayake to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the Rideegama 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Bandula Rathnayake was selected as the member of  the Rideegama Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
