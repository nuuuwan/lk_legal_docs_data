Revision  of  electoRal  RegisteRs
Notice issued by the Registering Officers in each Electoral District that the Supplementary Electoral Register 
2022(2) has been certified under the Registration of Electors Act 
Definition :
01. Registration of Electors Act - Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of 
Electors (Amendment) Act,    No. 22 of 2021
02. Supplementary Electoral Register -  Supplementary Electoral Register 2022(2) certified on 15th November 2022; 
containing the names of persons who attained the age of 18 years on a certain date 
within the period from 01st June 2022 to 30th September 2022
Notice is hereby given under sub-section 20A (10) of the Act that the 2022(2) Supplementary Electoral Register of the  
No. 01 - Colombo Electoral District has been certified and the said list has been kept at the Colombo District Election Office 
for inspection during office hours.
 P.A.S. Senaratne, Assistant Registering Officer, Electoral District no. 01 – Colombo
 On 15
th November 2022, At the Colombo District Election Office.
