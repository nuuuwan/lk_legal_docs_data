THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister 
of Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of 
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described 
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired

MA/LGG/2019/151 dated 19.08.2010, prepared by the
