ThE  UNIvERSITIES  AcT, No. 16 Of 1978 
Order under Section 27(1) read with section 21 and section 139
by, virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. A. d. Susil Premajayantha, 
minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants commission, do by this order, amend the Schedule to 
the order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 9, 2006, (as amended from time to time), which specifies 
the Faculties and the departments of the University of colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of 
section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately 
after entry "12. department of Information Technology" appearing in column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the 
item "Faculty of Arts" appearing in Column I of the Schedule, (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No, 2276/04 of April 18, 2022) of the following new entry :-
"13. department of communication and creative Arts."
 
Dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA (m.P.),
 minister of education.
colombo,
05th december, 2022.
