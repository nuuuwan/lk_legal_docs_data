According to the letter bearing No. MC/ADM/2/By-Law, addressed to Secretary of
the Ministry of Mass Media on 03.05.2022 -
As the powers vested  in pursuant  to the Section 3(4)  of Local Authorities (Standard
By-Laws) of No. 06 of 1952  and Sections 267, 268 of the Municipal Council Ordinance
relating to publication of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2269/70, are overridden by
the  executive powers vested on the Governor under the Thirteenth Amendment of
the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 42 of 1987.
Thereby such publication of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2269/70  becomes null
and void. Hence the Government Printer does not possess  legislative power for
publication of  such Gazette Extraordinary and therefore it is hereby revoked
