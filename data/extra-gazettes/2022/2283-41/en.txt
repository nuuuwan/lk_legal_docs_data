WHEREAS, the office of member of  Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Thampippillai 
Sivasubramaniyam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that ward 10 - Chankanai East and Chankanai South of  Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs 
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who 
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Aarumugam Sivakumar to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aarumugam 
Sivakumar was elected as the member of the ward 10 - Chankanai East and Chankanai South of Valikamam West Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
