WHEREAS the office of return member of Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present) the recognized 
political party to which Pichchei Selwapuwaneswari the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present) 
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 
262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate Dewaraja
Pushparani to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka  Nalin Sampath Fernando, returning officer of the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dewaraja 
Pushparani was selected as the member of the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. D. N. Sampath Fernando,
Returning officer,
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
