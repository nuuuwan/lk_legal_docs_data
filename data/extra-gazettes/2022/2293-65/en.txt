PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  MATALE PRADESHIYA SABHA  
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return Member of  Matale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Ceased to be member 
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which 
Lolugaswala Gamaralage Indrani Kumari Lolugaswala  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of 
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Matale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualiﬁ ed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Munasinha Arachchilage Sriyani Munasingha to ﬁ ll such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Of ﬁ cer of the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Selection 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Munasinha Arachchilage Sriyani
Munasingha was selected as the member of  the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha. 
PULASTHI PATHIRANAPP ,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Ofﬁce,
Matale District,
18th August, 2022.
