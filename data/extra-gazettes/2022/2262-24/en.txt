 (4) by the insertion immediately after rule 37, of the following:- 
 
“(B) RE-REVIEW FEES 
 
   38. Institutional Re-review and Subject Re-review shall be conducted after five (5) years from the 
date of the first Institutional Review and Subject Re view and consecutively at the end of every five 
years thereafter. The processing fees applicable for the Re-reviews are set out in Schedule IV. 
 
   39. (1) Where a Degree Awarding Institute on the day immediately proceding the date of coming 
into operation of this rule, - 
 
   (a) has completed five (5) years or more from the date of the Institutional Review and 
the Subject Review, shall within six (6) calendar months from the date of coming 
into operation of these rules;  
 
   (b) has not completed five (5) years since the date of the Institutional Review and the 
Subject Review, shall at the earliest time from the date of completion of the said 
five (5) years but not later than two calendar months; 
 
submit to the Specified Authority, an application consisting of Part I or Part II or both, containing 
particulars set out in the guidelines for the Re-review of the said Institution in the manner set out in the 
guidelines provided in Schedules I and II. 
 
 (2) Any amendments or variance to the previo usly approved Review application, shall be 
clearly indicated and distinctly marked by the Degree Awarding Institute when submitting the relevant 
application for Re-review, Proof of payment of the a pplicable processing fees, set out in Schedule IV 
shall be attached to the application. 
 
 40. Where a Degree Awarding Institute has not complied with the requirements set out in the 
guidelines for an Institutional Re-Review and the Subject Re-Review during the specified time period 
referred to in rule 39 (1) (a) or (b) as the case may be, the Specified Authority shall permit, a further 
period of three months after the date for submitti ng the said application fo r Re-Reviews, for which a 
late fee of 50% of the amounts as set out in Schedule IV shall be charged on a pro rata basis, in 
addition to the fees specified in Schedule V. 
 
 41. Where the Degree Awarding Institute fails to submit an application for Institutional Re-
Review and the Subject Re-Review, the Specified Authority shall submit a report to the Minister 
regarding such non-compliance. A copy of the same shall be forwarded to the Degree Awarding 
Institute.". 
 
 (5) by the insertion immediately after rule 41, of the following:- 
 
             "(C) CHARGES APPLICABLE FOR AMENDING THE DEGREE PROGRAMME 
 
 42. Where any Degree Awarding Institute proposes any amendments to the curriculum, entry 
qualifications to any degree programme, or amendments to upgrade or standardize any study 
programme, such Institute shall submit an application containing the relevant particulars to the 
Specified Authority, Proof of payment of the processing fees as set out in Schedule V shall be attached 
to the application.".
