Whereas the office of return member of  Chilaw Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Randunu Dewage 
Kingsli Ananda Wickramasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Chilaw Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party,  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Herath Mudiyanselage Wathsala Sriyarathne Herath to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Siriniwasage Lakshitha Ruwan Jayanayake, Returning Officer  of  the Chilaw Urban Council, acting 
in terms of  Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Herath 
Mudiyanselage Wathsala Sriyarathne Herath was selected as the member of the Chilaw Urban Council.
   
 S. L. R. J
ayanayake,
 Returning Officer,
 Chilaw Urban Council. 
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
21st of March, 2022.
