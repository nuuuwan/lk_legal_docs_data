WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman of Horana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Office of the Chairman shall 
be deemed to have been resign under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by No. 21 of 2012 
(Special Provisions ) Local Authority Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Anil Asoka 
Ranathunga the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of  that Horana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dilip Pushpakumara Weerakkody to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of Horana Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Dilip Pushpakumara Weerakkody elected as Chairman of the 
Horana Pradeshiya Sabha.
