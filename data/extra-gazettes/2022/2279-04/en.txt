WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kelle Gamage  
Gnanathilaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the returned member of Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kankanamgamage Nandasena to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kankanamgamage 
Nandasena was selected as the member of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
