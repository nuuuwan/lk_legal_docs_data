ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 420/25 of september 26, 1986 (as amended from time to time), which 
specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions 
of section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a university established under section 21 of the said act by the addition immediately 
after the entry “5. Department of Physics” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of 
Science” appearing in Column I of the Schedule (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1523/16 
of November 14, 2007) of the following entry :–
 “6. department of computing”.
