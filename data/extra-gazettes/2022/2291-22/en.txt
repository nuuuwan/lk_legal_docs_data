Notice under Section 7

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for the public purposes. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
The public here is the acquisition of land for the North 
West Province project related to the Bowathena tunnel 
and the built institution.

1304 and1306-1316, shown in the Survey General's 
F.V.P. No. 252, Sup No. 43, Sheet No. 43, dated 
21.06.2022, situated in Meegolla village in the Grama 
Niladari Division of No. E 415G, Meegolla in Naula 
Divisional Secretary's Divisi on in Matale District of 
Central Province.
