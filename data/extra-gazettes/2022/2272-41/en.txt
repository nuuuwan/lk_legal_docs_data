Divisional Secretary's Reference No.: DSM/YT/LAND/5/8.

I hereby give notice, under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/ servitude
which is required for Quarters of the Divisional Secretary,
Yatawatte and that of the Assistant Divisional Secretary.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
An allotment in extent of 0.3062 hectare of the land called
Dangan Watte depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. MA 1855
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village called
Deevilla in the Grama Niladhari Division No. E 337B of  Dangan
Place in Asgiri Pallesiya Pattu Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Yatawatte in Matale District of the
Central Province.
Lot No. 1
Extent - 0.3062 Hectare,
Name of the Land - Dangan Watte,
Description - Acquisition of an allotment of land for
Quarters of the Divisional Secretary,
Yatawatte and that of the Assistant
Divisional Secretary,
