WHEREAS, the office of return member of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which  
Mohomed Naseer Mohomed Nawsar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of National front for Good Governance, who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Muhammed Naleer Mohamed Rizwan to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of  the Attanagalla Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Muhammed Naleer Mohamed Rizwan was selected as the member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.   
 K. J. S. M
