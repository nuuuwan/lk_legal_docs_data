SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972
I, tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the sabaragamuwa Provincial council, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
the provisions of section 154 (c) of the constitution read with sub section 2 (A) of the Provincial council (consequential 
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and in accordance with the provisions of section 2 (1) (B) of the rent Act, No. 7 of 1972, 
make the following orders by this notification.
(A)  I, amend the areas mentioned under schedule No. 2 of the announcement Published in connection with the rent 
Act, No. 7 of 1972, relevant to sabaragamuwa Provincial council under the Gazette Notification dated 07.07.1995 
as follows, the areas where the said rent Board shall be implemented.
(B) I, order the date of publication of this notice in the Gazette as the date on which this notice shall be valid.
            Tikiri Banda koBBekaduwa,
 Governor,
 sabaragamuwa Provincial council.
At Governor's Office of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
On February 24, 2022.
