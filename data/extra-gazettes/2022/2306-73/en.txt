PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
IT is hereby notiﬁed that the Annual Duruthu Maha Procession of Pilana Purana Sri Maha Temple in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of No. 152, Pilana, Divisional Secretary's Division of Akmeemana, District of Galle, Southern Province will be 
held from 7th of December 2022 to 6th of January 2023 as per the regulations published in the Extra Ordinary Gazette 
Notiﬁcation No: 2146/18 dated 22.10.2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of the Section 2 of 
the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Cap. 175).
SHANTHA WEERASINGHE,
District Secretary/Governement Agent,
Administrative District, Galle.
At the District Secretariat, Galle.
