NOTIfICATION
IT is hereby notified that Assignments of subjects and functions, Departments, Government Corporations and Statutory 
Institutions and Rules and Regulations to be implemented are assigned to the Hon. State Ministers mentioned in the said 
Schedule in terms of the powers vested to me as per the Article 45(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka will come into force with effect from 17.11.2022 in the manner set out in the below schedule which 
are assigned by the His Excellency of President to me as the Minister of Industries in terms of the amendments made to it 
from time to time and by the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification bearing No. 2289/43 and 22nd 
of July 2022 dated.
Further, it is notified that the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2301/32 dated 11th October 2022 published by me is 
hereby rescinded.
    
    
 dr. Ramesh PathiRana,
 Minister of Industries.
17th November 2022.
