WHEREAS the office of return member of Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the Party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Karuna 
Dewayalage Ananda Kumarathunga the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Sunil to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Nagalingam Regan , returning officer of the, Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Sunil was 
selected as the member of the Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha.
 Nagalingam Regan,
 Returning Officer,
 Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office, 
Monaragala.
12th August, 2022.
