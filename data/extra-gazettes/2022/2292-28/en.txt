PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  RATHNAPURA MUNICIPAL 
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return Member of Rathnapura Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to 
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kirimudiyanselage
Navarathna  Seetha Kumarihami the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Rathnapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed under 
section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of Thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dehiwala Liyanage Sumudu Sewwandika Chandrasiri to ﬁ ll such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, returning of ﬁ cer of the Rathnapura Municipal Council, acting in terms 
of section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities  Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Dehiwala Liyanage
Sumudu Sewwandika Chandrasiri was selected as the member of the Rathnapura Municipal Council.
SURANGA AA MBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Ofﬁcer,
Rathnapura Municipal Council.
District Election Ofﬁce, 
Rathnapura,
05th August, 2022.
