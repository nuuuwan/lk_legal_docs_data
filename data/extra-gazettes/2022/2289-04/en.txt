WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Perumal 
Ramakrishnan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward  No. 04 Sasthrikoolankulam of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to 
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Anthony Thaveethu to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Anthony Thaveethu 
was elected as the member of  the Ward No.  04 Sasthrikoolankulam of  Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.
