THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 My Reference No.: WGSWD&EIP/ACQ/KB/35.

I  hereby  give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government has acquired the allotment of land for the 
development of the Canal from No. 15/25, 2nd Lane, 
Pepiliyana Mawatha, Kohuwala to Depa Ela located 
adjacent to the house at No. 327/2, Ranasinghe Mawatha 
Pepiliyana Boralesgamuwa and for the construction of the 
maintenance path of the canal under Weras Ganga Storm 
Water Drainage and  Environment Improvement Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
Allotments of land, depicted in Lot numbers below in 
the preliminary plan No. 9741 dated 23.07.2019 prepard 
by the Surveyor General, situated in Village called 
Pepiliyanna (within the Urban Council Limit of Kesbewa)

Colombo District of the Western Province and bounded 
as follows:
