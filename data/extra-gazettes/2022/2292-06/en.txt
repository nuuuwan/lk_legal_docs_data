Statement of matter in dispute
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the below - mentioned 14 employees of Independent Television Network Limited inclusive of Mr. D. A. Anil 
Nishantha are entitled for the claims made by them,
 01. to place them in the appropriate grades as per Circular MSD 25,
 02. to place them in salary step MM1 - 1 of Circular 30/2006 and 
 03. pay the arrears of salaries and other previledges in line with it
and if so, what relief they are entitled for the injustice caused.
 01. Mr. D. A. Anil Nishantha
 02. Mr. P. Bandula Sri Padmalal de Silva
 03. Miss I. Shyama Priyadarshani Jayasinghe
 04. Mr. M. Thayaparan
 05. Mr. K. Amaradasa
 06. Mr. K. W. Kithsiri Kumara
 07. Mr. K. B. V . S. Kalyanapriya
