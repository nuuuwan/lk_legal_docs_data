THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 Description of the land: bounded by the allotments of land 
with following names and extents as shown in the Tracing No. 
GA/BPP/2021/468 dated 29.10.2021 prepared by Surveyor 
General, situated at Meepawala village of Meepawala Grama 
Niladhari's Division, No. 117A in D.S. Division" Bope 
Poddala in Galle District, Southern Province;
 01. Lot 'A' – Usgodawila – Extent: 0.2412 Hectare
 North and East: Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and 
Others; South: Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and 
Others, Ela and Lot B (Ela) in this Tracing; West: Lot B (Ela) 
in this tracing, Ela and Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya 
and Others.
 02. Lot 'B' – Not mentioned – Extent: 0.0241 Hectare
 North: Ela, Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others 
and Lot  A  in this Tracing; East: Lot A in this Tracing, 
Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others and Ela; South: 
Ela, Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others and Lot 
C in this Tracing; West: Lot C in this tracing, Usgodawila 
claimed by T. T. Punya and Others and Ela.
 03. Lot 'C' – Usgodawila – Extent: 0.0786 Hectare
 North: Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others, Ela 
and Lot  B  in this Tracing; East: Lot B (Ela) in this Tracing, 
Ela and Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others; South: 
Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others, Usgodawila 
claimed by Upulron Kalansooriya and Lot D in this Tracing;

Kalansooriya, Usgodawila claimed by T. T. Punya and Others.
