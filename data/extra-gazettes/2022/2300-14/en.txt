WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to, which Bulath 
Veloge Indika Thusitha Rupasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon  member of that ward number 28 - Dalupitiya of Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Arachchi Appuhamilage  Saman Kumarasiri to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Arachchi Appuhamilage Saman Kumarasiri was elected as the member of  the ward number 28 - Dalupitiya of Mahara 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
