WHEREAS the office of return member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member 
of the Party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rajashilpa Acharige 
Madumane Gedara Nelson Karunathilaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Weerasekara Mudiyanselage Priyantha Chularatne Weerasekara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, returning officer of the, Walapane Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Weerasekara Mudiyanselage Priyantha Chularatne Weerasekara was selected as the member of the Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha.
