WHEREAS the office of  return member of Minuwangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three 
consecutive meetings of the council without Permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Midellawala 
Hettiyagodage Nimanthi Mala the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of  Minuwangoda Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Jayakodi Arachchige Ramani Shanthilatha Jayakodi to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Minuwangoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jayakodi 
Arachchige Ramani Shanthilatha Jayakodi was selected as the member of the Minuwangoda Urban Council.
   
 K. J. S. Madhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Minuwangoda Urban Council. 
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
