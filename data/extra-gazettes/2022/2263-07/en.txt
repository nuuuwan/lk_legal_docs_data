NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE 
I, Maheepala Herath, Governor of the North Central Province, hereby announce the following orders by virtue of powers vested 
in me under the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential 
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
  M
AHEEPALA HERATH,
 Governor of the North Central Province.
 
Governor Ofﬁ ce of the North Central Province,
Provincial Council Complex,
Dharmapala Mawatha, 
Anuradhapura.
18th January, 2022.
Order
Since the post of Chairman of the Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha situated within the District of Anuradhapura of 
the North Central Province remains vacant due to failure to pass the budget prepared for the year 2022, in terms of Section 
168 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and the Section 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended 
by Section 17 of Local Government Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, I hereby inform that the powers, 
performance, and functions related to this post is vested to the deputy Chairman in terms of Section 8 (4) of the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act from the date of the vacancy and until the date of the vacancy is to be ﬁ lled as per Local Government Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
Further, I hereby direct the Deputy Chairman of the Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha to bear the essential expenses 
related to special projects currently in progress and the recurrent expenditure of the Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha for the 
year 2022, with the approval of Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Anuradhapura Administrative District, prepared 
by the Secretary of the Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha to ensure the continuation of the activities of the Horowpothana 
Pradeshiya Sabha for a period of time until the new Chairman presents the budget for the ﬁ nancial year 2022 to the Sabha 
and approves it properly.
EOG 01-0184/2
