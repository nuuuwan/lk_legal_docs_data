LAND  REFORMS  ACT,  No. 1  OF  1972  OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY  
Order under Subsection (4) to Section 27A
bY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27 A (4) of the Land reforms Law No. 01 of 1972 of the National State 
Assembly amended by the Land reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 I, Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism 
and Lands, do by this order.
 (a) amend the order made under the Section 22 a(1) of the Land reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 517/20 dated 05 August 1988 relating to the estate land called and depicted as Delthota (Gallantenna 
Lower and Upper bellwood), more fully described in the First Schedule of this order, vested in Janatha estates 
Development board established under State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972, by revocation of the such 
portion of the said order as relates to the extent of the land called as Delthota Watte, Delthota Group (Gallantenna 
Division) specified in Second Schedule hereto by reason of failure to adhere to the terms and conditions relating to 
the consideration for vesting that land in the said board; and
 (b) declare that upon this revocation, the extent of land specified in the Second Schedule of this order shall revest in the 
Land reform Commission.
