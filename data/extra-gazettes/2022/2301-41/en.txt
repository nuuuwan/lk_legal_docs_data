REGISTRATION   OF   PERSONS   ACT,   No. 32  of  1968
Notification Under Section 9(2)(bb) (i)
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 9(2)(bb)(i) of registration of Persons act, No.32 of 1968, I, tiran alles, 
Minister of Public security hereby inform that a fee of two hundred rupees should be paid in respect of an application 
forwarded under aforesaid Section 9, for registration and obtain a National Identity Card for the first time with effect from 
01.11.2022.
I hereby cancel the notification published in this regard in Gazette No. 2083/25 of 09th august 2018.
    
 Tiran alles,
 Minister of Public security.
Ministry of Public security,
on 12th october 2022.

REGISTRATION   OF   PERSONS   ACT,  No.  32  of  1968
