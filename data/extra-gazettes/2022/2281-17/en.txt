The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. K. P. Pemasiri (Chief Cashier) and Mr. B. A. Dharmasena Jayantha (Office Aid) who had worked at the Royal 
Colombo Golf Club had been caused injustice by the non - payment of the exgratia Allowance paid to some employees who
