IMPORTS  AND  EXPORTS  (CONTROL)  ACT,  No.  1  OF  1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Section 4 of the Imports and Exports (Control) Act, 
No. 1 of 1969 as amended by Act, No. 48 of 1985 and Act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Basil Rajapaksa, the Minister of Finance, 
promulgate following Regulations.
 B
ASIL RAJAPAKSA,
 Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance, 
Colombo 01,
13th January, 2022.
Regulations
 1. These Regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 04 of 2022”.
 2. These Regulations shall enter into force with effect from January 13, 2022.
 3. The following goods and their corresponding entries described in the Serial No. 17 of the Schedule I of the Special 
Import License Regulations, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2044/40 dated 09th November, 2017, as 
amended, are hereby deleted.
