ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT, No. 16 Of 1978 
 
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dinesh Gunawardena, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as 
amended from time to time), establishing the University of ruhuna, sri lanka, by the addition immediately after entry “9. 
department of english language Teaching” appearing in Column ii of the schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of 
Humanities and Social Sciences” in Column I of that Schedule, (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2079/10 of July 9, 2018) of the following new entry:-
“10. department of information Technology”.
 Dinesh Gunawardena,
 Minister of education.
