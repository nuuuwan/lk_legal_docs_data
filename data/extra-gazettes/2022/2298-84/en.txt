2a
6a.  (a)  the selection of the participants in Common Wealth sports, asian Games and other similar international Contests 
to represent sri lanka shall be done by a National sports selection Committee (Hereinafter referred to as "National 
sports selection Committee" in this regulation) subject to the prior written approval of the minister in Charge of 
the subject of sports.
 (b) National sports selection Committee shall consists of ––
 i. Five members who have done a distinctive service to the sports as opined by the minister in charge of the 
subject of sports, or who have achieved a unique status, and appointed by the minister in charge of the 
subject of sports.
 ii. a  representative of National sports Council, and
 iii. a  representative of the ministry in charge of the subject of sports.
 (c) the president and the secretary of the National sports selection Committee shall be appointed by the minister in 
charge of the subject of sports.
