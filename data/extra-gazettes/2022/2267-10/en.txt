PILGRImAGES  ORDINANCE
IT is hereby notified that the annual lenten season of Hiniduma Calvary Shrine (St. Ann’s Church) located within the two 
Grama Niladhari Divisions of Hiniduma South and Malhathawa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Thawalama in the 
District of Galle, Southern Province, will be held only to perform rituals during the period from 2nd March 2022 to 17th 
April 2022 as per the regulations laid down under the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2057/33 dated 09.02.2018 in 
terms of the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175).
 
Shantha WeeraSinghe,
 District Secretary/
 Government Agent,
 Administrative District,
 Galle.
