3A
Greetings!
As you have been appointed as members of the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs, established by the
Presidential Directive dated 31 st March, 2020 exercising the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And whereas,new Vice Chancellors have been appointed to the Universities of Eastern and Rajarata,
And whereas, Acting Vice Chancellor has been appointed to the University of Wayamba,
And whereas, new Principals have been appointed to St. Bridget’s Convent, Ananda College, Visakha Vidyalaya,
Dharmaraja College and Rahula College,
Also, whereas, a new Commissioner General of Examination has been appointed,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in the exercise of powers vested
in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, appoint the below-mentioned
members in place of the members referred to at15, 17, 19, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27 and 30, in the Presidential Directive published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2242/2 dated 24.08.2021 to the Task Force on Sri Lanka’s Education Affairs, established in terms
of the aforesaid Presidential Directive,having reposing great trust, and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity.
1. Prof. V . Kanagasingam Esquire
Vice Chancellor, Eastern University
2. Prof. (Ms.) G .A.S. Ginigaddara
Vice Chancellor, Rajarata University
3. Dr. Sanjeewa Bowaththa Esquire
