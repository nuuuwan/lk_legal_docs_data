Whereas the office of return member of  Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Rathnayaka 
Mudiyanselage Pradeepa Darshani Rathnayaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is 
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ekanayeka Mudiyanselage Amitha Kumari Ekanayeka to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer  of  the Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ekanayeka Mudiyanselage Amitha 
Kumari Ekanayeka was selected as the member of the Hingurakgoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
