3. Notwithstanding the regulation No. 8 of the imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 10 of 2021, referred above, 
importation of electric motor vehicles, classified under the HS Codes of 8702.40.19, 8702.40.39, 8702.40.59, 8703.80.31, 
8703.80.32, 8703.80.33, 8703.80.34, 8704.90.31, 8704.90.32, 8704.90.33, 8704.90.34, or 8711.60.10, listed out in the 
Schedule I of  the said regulations by any eligible person, who has obtained a "Vehicle import permit" as per the Circular 
No. 02/2022 dated august 31, 2022 of the ministry of Labour and foreign employment, as amended, shall be allowed 
by the Director General, Sri Lanka Customs on compliance to terms and condition specified in the above said Circular 
including a valid Letter of Credit (LC) established prior to the date of airway bill/ bill of Lading.
4. Notwithstanding the regulation No. 8 of the imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 10 of 2021, referred above, 
importation of electric motor vehicles, classified under the HS Code of  8702.40.19, 8702.40.39, 8702.40.59, 8703.80.31, 
8703.80.32, 8703.80.33, 8703.80.34, 8704.90.31, 8704.90.32, 8704.90.33, 8704.90.34 or 8711.60.10 listed out in the 
Schedule I of the said regulations by any authorized agent / authorized distributor of the manufacturer and registered 
in sri Lanka, who has obtained a "Vehicle import permit as sample for demonstration of exhibit purpose" as per the 
Circular No . 02/2022 dated august 31, 2022 of the ministry of Labour and foreign employment' as amended , shall be 
allowed by the Director General, Sri Lanka Customs on Compliance to terms and conditions specified in the above said 
Circular including a valid Letter of Credit (LC) established prior to the date of airway bill/bill of Lading. as per this 
regulation, maximum number of samples of three (03) electric motor vehicle models shall only be allowed to import 
by an authorized agent or authorized distributor referred above.
5. the director General, sri Lanka Customs shall grant approval for Customs clearance of an electric motor vehicles 
referred in the regulation No. 3 and 4 above, subject to the prevailing rules and regulations applicable on importation 
of motor vehicles, classifiable under the HS Chapter 87.
6. director General, sri Lanka Customs shall take action for violation of these regulations as per provisions of Customs 
Ordinance and related legislations.
7. any meaning or interpretation of terms and conditions of the Circular No. 02/2022 of the ministry of Labour and foreign 
employment shall be obtained from the secretary, minister of Labour and foreign employment, if any.
8. director General, sri Lanka Customs shall submit a monthly summary report including information on the name and 
address of the permit holder, vehicle imports permit number, type of motor vehicle, Cost-insurance-freight value and 
date of importation of such electric motor vehicle (s) under these regulations to the secretary, ministry of Labour and 
foreign employment with a copy to the Controller General of imports and exports.
9. these regulations will only be in force until december 31, 2023
