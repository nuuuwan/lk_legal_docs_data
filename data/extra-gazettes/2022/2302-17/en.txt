Order under Section 27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a  of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, 
dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of education having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid act, 
in respect of the sri Lanka Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in sri Lanka 
under the companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number GL 24, being recognized as a degree awarding 
Institute, do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the Order made under the said act and published in Gazette Extraordinary , 
No. 2293/25 of august 17, 2022 by the addition immediately after the entry “bachelor of education Honours in Information 
Technology” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column 
I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, Subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
scHedULe  I
Conditions
1. the sri Lanka Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited shall, when admitting student for the degree 
programmes specified in schedule II hereto - 
 (a) in respect of bachelor of science Honours in computer systems engineering, with a minimum 120 credits 
[Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) Level 6], adhere to minimum entry qualification of two (02) 
‘credit passes’ (‘c’) and one (01) ‘simple pass’ (‘s’) in Physical science stream, in one and the same sitting 
at the Gce (advanced Level) examination conducted by the department of examination of sri Lanka.
 (b) in respect of doctor of Philosophy in business administration, adhere to one of the following minimum 
entry qualifications as specified in Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) -
 (i) A Master of Philosophy Degree in Business or any other related field; or
 (ii) A Master's Degree of Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) Level 9 and 10 or equivalent 
in Business or any other related field with evidence of research competencies in the field; or
 (iii) A Bachelor's Honours Degree of Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) Level 6 in Business 
or any other related field with a minimum GPA of 3.7 at a scale of 0 - 4.0 and with evidence of 
research competencies in the related field; or
 (iv) A Bachelor's Honours Degree of Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) Level 6 or equivalent 
held by a person who has registered to follow an MPhil degree in Business or any related field which 
may be upgraded to PhD level after a minimum period of 18 months of confirmed registration of 
MPhil degree provided that his research competencies are of exceptional merit.
2. The Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited shall be recognized to offer the degree specified 
in schedule II hereto subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.
