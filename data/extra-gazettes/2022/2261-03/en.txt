2A
ORDER
1. This order is known as the order regarding the implementation of the Budget for the year 2022 in the Pradeshiya 
Sabhas of the Central Province.
2. The Chairman of Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha in Nuwara Eliya district is considered to have left the post with 
effect from the midnight on 31.12.2021 and above post is considered to be vacant, due to the failure to pass the Budget 
prepared by the Chairman of Hang uranketha Pradeshiya Sabha for the year 2 022 in terms of Section 168 of th e 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and presented to the Council for final consideration under Section 168 (2) of 
the Pradeshiya Sabha Act and Non-compliance with Section 17 of the Local Government Institutions (Special 
Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012.
3. Since, the post of Chairman of Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha in Nuwara Eliya districts in the Central Province 
remain vacant as per Section 17 of the Local Government Institutions (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 due to failing to 
pass the budget for the year 2022 in accordance with the requirements of Sections 168, 168 (2) of the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act, I hereby inform that the powers, performance and functions related to this post is vested to the deputy 
Chairman in terms of Section 8 (4) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act from the date of the vacancy and until the date of the 
vacancy is to be filled as per Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
4. When a budg et for the year 2022 for a particular  Pradeshiya Sabha in the Central Province is not passed in  
accordance with the provisions of section 168 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and in terms of Section  
17 of the Local Government Institutions (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, When the Post of Chairman of the 
relevant Pradeshiya Sabha becomes vacant, in case of non-compliance with the provisions of the Local Government 
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 when filling that vacancy or when a chairman appointed for a vacant 
post is not left with the minimum number of days to submit a proposal to pass the budget for the year 2022 as per the 
relevant written law, it is legal for the relevant Pradeshiya Sabha to implement for the year 2022 a budget prepared for 
the year 2022 by the said Pradeshiya Sabha with the approval of the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government in 
the District where the relevant Pradeshiya Sabha is located.
5. All the provisions cotained in these regulations shall come into force in accordance with the Provisions of the Local  
Government Institutions (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and the Provisions of the Local Government Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.

ORDER
1. This order is referred to as the order regarding the Enforcement of Budgets for the Year 2022 in the urban Councils 
of the Central Province.
2. When a budget for the year 2022 for a particular urban Council in the Central Province is not passed in accordance 
with the Provisions of Section 178 of the urban Council ordinance (Chapter 255) and in terms of Section 17 of the 
Local  Government Institutions (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, when the Post of Chairman of the relevant  
urban Council becomes vacant, in case of non-compliance with the provisions of the Local Government Elections  
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 when filling that vacancy or when a chairman appointed for a vacant post is not left 
with the minimum number of days to submit a proposal to pass the budget for the Year 2022 as per the relevant written 
law, it is legal for the relevant urban Council to implement for the Year 2022 a budget prepared for the year 2022 
by the said urban Council with the approval of the Assistant Commissioner of Local Governement in the District 
where the relevant urban Council is located.
3. All the provisions contained in these regulations shall come into force in accordance with the provisions of the Local 
Government Institutions (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and the Provisions of the Local Government Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
