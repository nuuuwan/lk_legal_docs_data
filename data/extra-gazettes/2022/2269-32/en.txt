WHEREAS, the office of return member of Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which Lide Gedara Lionel Ariyadasa the member belonged, 
was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the returned member of Yatawatta 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  has communicated his decision to nominate  Jambu 
Gahawatte Gedara Shantha to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A 
(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jambu Gahawatte Gedara Shantha was 
selected as the member of the Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 P
ulasthi Pathirana,
