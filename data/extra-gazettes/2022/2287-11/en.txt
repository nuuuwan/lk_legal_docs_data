REvISION  Of  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS 
Notice of the Election Commission  regarding the Display of Supplementary Lists, and the Submission of Claims 
and Objections as per the Registration of Electors Act.
Definition
 01. “Act”   -  Registration of electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of electors 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2021
 02. “supplementary list”  -  list of persons who will complete18 years of age on any given day during the period 
from 1st February 2022 to 31st May 2022.
Exhibition Centers of Supplementary Register

authority area

  Instructions
01.		It	was	announced	today	(July	05,	2022)	that	the	Registering	Officers	in	each	electoral	district	have	completed	the	
preparation of supplementary lists in accordance with the Registration of electors Act.
02.		Every	person	who	is	qualified	in	terms	of	the	Registration	of	Electors	Act	to	have	his	own	name	or	someone	else’s	name	
entered in the supplementary electoral register applicable to each electoral district and whose name has been omitted 
from	the	supplementary		list	and	who	claims	for	himself	or	for	someone	else	to	have	his	or	other	person’s	name	entered	as	
the case may be, in the supplementary electoral  register shall make available the application forms substantially in form 
“A” and “B” set out in the schedule to above Registration of electors Act or by a yC form introduced for enumeration 
of	electors	together	with	a	copy	of	the	birth	certificate	or	National	Identiry	Card	so	as	to	reach	the	registering	officer	of	
each electoral district  within 10 days from july 05, 2022 i.e. on or before july 14, 2022. 
03.  Any person whose name appears in the supplementary list for any electoral district may object to the entry in such 
register of his own name, the name of any other person appearing in such register or the name of any claimant. every 
such objection shall be made in duplicate substantially in form “C” set out in the schedule to the above Act and shall be 
made	available	so	as	to	reach	the	registering	officer	of	the	Election	Office.	All	objections	to	the	inclusion	of	the	name	in	
the	supplementary	list	should	be	sent	to	the	registering	officer	of	the	District	Elections	Office	within	10	days	from	the	
date of this notice in the Gazette of the democratic socialist Republic of sri lanka i.e. july 05, 2022.
04.  A notice containing a list of  all objections to the entry of the name of any claimant in the supplementary electoral register 
and	a	list	of	claimants	shall	be	submitted	to	the	Registering	Officer	in	duplicate	substantially	in	form	“D”	set	out	in	the	
schedule to the above Act within 7 days from the date on which the notice containing the names of such claimants is 
exhibited	at	the	District	Election	Office.	This	notice	will	be	published	in	the	District	Election	Offices	for	a	period	of	07	
days from july 19th, 2022.
05.		Form	‘A’	‘B’	and	YC	required	for	submission	of	claims	and	Form	‘C’	‘D’	required	for	submission	of	objections	are	
available	at	the	District		Election	Offices,	Divisional	Secretariats,	Local	Authorities	or	Grama	Niladhari	Offices.
 N
imal G. PuNchihewa,
 Chairman,
 election Commission.
