Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the manmunaipattu  pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of the Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal the recognized Political Party to which 
Ponnuthurai Murugamoorththy the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the returned member of Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sanmuganathan Kokulanathan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sanmuganathan 
Kokulanathan was selected as the member of  the Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 M. B. M. S
ufiyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Manmunaipattu Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
