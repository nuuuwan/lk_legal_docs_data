THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, as the  Minister of Tourism 
and  Lands, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of  
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, the land described 
herein as mentioned below, required to be used for a public 
purpose, is  acquired as per the  provisions of the said Land 
Acquisition Act.
 The  allotment of land depicted in the Advanced Tracing
