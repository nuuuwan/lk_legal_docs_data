Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Eravur Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending council 
meetings for a period of three month without permissions;
And Whereas the Secretary, of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal Party, the recognized political party to which 
Kaneshan Pirapakaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Eravur Urban Council who belongs to such party, and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rasasingam Satheeskumar to fill such vacancy ;
 Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Eravur Urban Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasasingam Satheeskumar 
was selected as the member of the Eravur Urban Council.
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Eravur Urban Council.
