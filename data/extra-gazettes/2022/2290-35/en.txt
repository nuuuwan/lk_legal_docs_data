PROCLAMATION  BY  THE  HON.  PRESIDENT
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Proclamation,
prorogue Parliament with effect from midnight of the Twenty Eighth day of July, Two Thousand and Twenty Two and hereby
fix the Third day of August, Two Thousand and Twenty Two, at 10.30 a.m.for the commencement of the next session and
summon Parliament to meet in the Chamber of Parliament, Sri Jayewardenapura.
Given at Colombo, on this 28th  day of July,Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
      By Command of  the Hon. President,
E. M. S. B. E
KANAYAKE,
Secretary to the President.
