Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOticE  uNdEr  SEctiON  66A(1)(a)  Of  thE  LOcAL  AuthOritiES   ELEctiONS  OrdiNANcE 
(chAptEr  262)
Weligama  urban council
WHEREAS the office of member of Weligama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  been  ceased  to be a member of the 
party.
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which Madarasinghe 
Matarage Hemachandra the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that 01 Kohunugamuwa of Weligama Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local Authorities Elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Wathiyage Shelton Pemananda to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Weligama Urban Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66(A)(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wathiyage 
Shelton Pemananda was elected as the member of the 01 Kohunugamuwa of Weligama Urban Council.
T.W. PoshiTha Kaumadi,
Returning Officer,
 Weligama Urban Council.
District Elections Office, 
Matara,
19th July, 2022.
