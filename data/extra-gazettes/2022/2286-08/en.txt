ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  of  1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 A  of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. d. Susil Premajayanth, 
minister of education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Lanka Nippon 
BizTech Institute (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and 
bearing Registration Number PV 109910 and presently located at No. 278, High Level Road, maharagama, do by this order, 
subject to such conditions as specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the Lanka Nippon BizTech Institute (Private) Limited, 
as a degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher education therein, leading to the award of the degree 
specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in Column 
II of the Schedule II hereto.
SCHeduLe I
Conditions
 1. The Lanka Nippon BizTech Institute (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “Institute”) shall, when 
admitting students for the degree programme relevant to the degree specified in the Schedule II hereto in respect of 
the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Software Engineering, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of : -

 (a)  three (3) simple passes (‘S’) in Physical Science subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCe

 (b) three (3) simple passes (‘S’) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCe (Advanced Level)

approved by the Specified Authority.
 2. The Institute shall offer the Degree specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to a subject re-review conducted by the 
Specified Authority every five (5) years from the date of publication of this Order.
 3. The Institute shall conduct the degree programme in relation to the degree specified in Schedule II hereto, only at 
No. 278, High Level Road, maharagama, Sri Lanka.
 4. The Institute shall, within five (5) years from the date of publication of this Order, submit the evidence of the 
establishment of the institute sought to be recognized as a degree Awarding Institute under this order, in a location 
registered under the name of the institute to the specified Authority.
 5. The Institute shall enter into a supplementary agreement to the existing agreement with the Board of Investment of 
Sri Lanka established by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law, No. 4 of 1978 (hereinafter referred to as the 
“BoI”) which stipulated conditions for the company to comply with the proposed investment within the proposed 
time period and the BOI shall have the power to monitor the investment flow, the performances, and compliances 
of the company with such stipulated conditions.
