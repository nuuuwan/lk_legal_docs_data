WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which Asanar 
Fasmeer, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ahamadumeerkan Iyyoobkhan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ahamadumeerkan Iyyoobkhan 
was selected as the member of the Kuchchaveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
