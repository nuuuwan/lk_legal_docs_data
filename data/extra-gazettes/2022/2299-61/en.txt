THE  ANTIQUITIES  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 188)
Order under section 18
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by section 18 of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188), I, Vidura Wikramanayaka, 
Minister of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs, do by this order declare that the monuments speci ﬁ ed in the 
Schedule to this Order to be “Protected Monuments” for the purposes of the aforesaid Ordinance.
The Notice in respect of the protected monuments speciﬁ ed in the Schedule to this Order has been published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2011/6 of March 20, 2017 in terms of section 19 of the aforesaid Ordinance.
VIDURA WIKRAMANAYAKA, 
Minister of Buddhasasana,
Religious and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
22nd September, 2022.

SCHEDULE
List of monuments declared as protected monuments 
 1. The hillock of ancient Chaitya and three places containing ruins of buildings at the premises called Kovilkanda No. 
03 of Dikwela Road belonging to Himbudawa village situated in Grama Niladhari Division No. 1605, Thambakanda 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Pannala in Kurunegala District in North Western Province.
 2. The rock plain with hillock of ancient ruins in the land called Kongahamolahena Weheragawahena alias 
Kadurugahamulawaththa belonging to Pandawa village situated in Grama Niladhari Division No. 1352, Diggalagedara 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Panduwasnuwara (West) in Kurunegala District in North Western Province.
 3. The Drip ledged cave at the premises of Kuruwalena Rajamaha Vihara belonging to Yatiwilla village situated in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 647, Bandaragala in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Mawathagama in Kurunegala District 
in North Western Province.
 4. The Molamure cemetery belonging to Makandura village situated in Grama Niladhari Division Makandura in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Godakawela in Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
 5. Hillock of ancient ruins and Bodhi Prakara on the rock plain called belonging to Kotagama village situated in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 09, Kotagama in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Rambukkana in Kegalle District in 
Sabaragamuwa Province.
 6. Two drip ledged caves belonging to Dimbulgamuwa village situated in Grama Niladhari Division No. 14B, 
Dimbulgamuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Kegalle in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
 7. Drip ledged cave at the premises called Mothajjewaththa belonging to Nawandala village situated in Grama Niladhari 
Division  Heenatipana in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Mawanella in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 8. Ancient Awasa (Dwelling) at the premises of Iddamalpana Rajamaha Vihara belonging to Harigala village situated 
in Grama Niladhari Division Kinigama in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Galigamuwa in Kegalle District in 
Sabaragamuwa Province.
 9. Drip ledged cave at the Namaldeniya village situated in Grama Niladhari Division No. 146/B, Bodhimaluwa in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Kuruwita in Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
 10. Ancient Shrine the premises of Jayasingharama Purana Vihara belonging to Karaputugala village situated in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 324, Karaputugala in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Kamburupitiya in Matara District 
in Southern Province.
 11. Ancient Shrine with ancient paintings at the premises of Keerthi Shri Thejowararama, Kithsiripura situated in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 279 B, Kithsiripura in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Mulatiyana in Matara District in 
Southern Province.
 12. The Dutch reformed Church and premises with ancient Sohon Puwaru (Grave Monument Slab) at the place of Rate No. 
12 of Church Street in Galle Fortress situated in Grama Niladhari Division No. 96D, Galle Fortress in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division, Kadawath Sathara Galle in Galle District in Southern Province.
