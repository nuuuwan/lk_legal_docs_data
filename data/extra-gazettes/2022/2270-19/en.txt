SPECIAL COmmODITY LEvY ACT, NO. 48 Of 2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil Rajapaksa, 
Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the commodities 
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto the Special Commodity Levy at rates specified in the corresponding entries in 
Column II of that Schedule.
This order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from March 10, 2022, subject to provisions of Sub Section 
(3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The order made under Sections 2 and 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2240/20 of August 11, 2021 in respect of Butter and other related products (HS Codes 0405.10, 
0405.20, 0405.90), the Orders made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in
