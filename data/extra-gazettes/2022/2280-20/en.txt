EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
AMENDED  BY  LAND  ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT. No. 28 OF 1964  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: DSB/LND/03/F1/38. 
Ref. No. of Ministry of Lands: 4-3/9/2017/WS/211. 
       
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Acquisition of an allotment of land for the construction of 
Water Source under Beliatta Kudapihilla Rural Water 
Supply Scheme. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of  land, in total Extent of about 0.0136 
Hectare, from Lot No. 617 in  sheet No. 17 supplement 
No. 19, in the final village Plan No. 300 dated 
17.12.2020, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
surveys of Hambantota District  of behalf of the Surveyor 
General, situated at the village of Ambala, in Kudapihilla 
North Grama Niladhari Division, No. 368, in the 
Divisional Secretaries Division of Beliatta in Hambantota 
District, Southern Province and bounded as follows: 
FVP 300 Lot No. 617  
Name of the Land: Rukattanahena; Present land usage: 
Exists a permanent building of pump house; Original 
Claimant: Randumathi Mendis Jayasinghe.  
Boundaries: 
North: Lot No. 64; East: Lot No. 64; South: Lot               
No. 64; West: Lot No. 64.  
All persons who claim ownership/interest or any other 
connection to the aforesaid land/servitude are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agent  duly 
authorized in writing, before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 
22nd day of June, 2022 at th e Divisional Secretariat, 
Beliatta. and further, you are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08th day of 
June, 2022 the nature of their interests in the land/
servitude, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.      
 J. G
ANGA RUWAN PATHIRANA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Beliatta. 
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Beliatta.
