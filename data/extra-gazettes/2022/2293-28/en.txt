THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : KDS/KGK/4/2(2)/2018/02/04'
Reference No.  Ministry  0f Lands: 4-3/12/2018/WS/29'
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
the  Land Acquisition Act that the Goverment intends to
acquire the following land  which is required for a public
purpose :
Description of the Land  to be Acquired :
The following allotment of land depicted in Sheet No. 27 of
Zone No. 06 of the Cadastral Map No. 320385 dated 30.06.2022
prepared by Senior Superintendent of Survey of Kandy on
behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called
Mullepihilla, Alupathakada in the  Grama Niladhari Division
(274) of Wewathenna in the Divisional Secretariat Division  of
Kandy Four  Gravets and Gangawatta Korale in the District of
Kandy, Central Province and contain in extent 0.0440 hectares.:
01. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land :Alupathakadahena;  Claimant: State;
Original Claimant:- Kalyana probodha Dissanayake, No. 305,
Alupathakada, Pallegama.; Extent : 0.0440 Hectare.
 I hereby give notice to the public that every person
interested in the said land/ servitude which is to be acquired
is informed to appear personally or by agent duly authorized
in writing before me  on 28.09.2022 at 10.00 a.m. at the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata
Korale.
 The nature of their interests in  the land/lands, the particulars
of their claim for compensation, the amount of compensation
and the details of  the computation of such amount should be
notified to me in writing (in duplicated) on or before 21.09.2022.
U.J.M.S.S.B.J
AY ASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawata Korale.
