EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT,  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
Land Ministry . Reference No : 3/J/79/E/462. 
Divisional Secretary Ref.  No.: කඹු/ඉඅ/3281. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude  to be acquired:- 
 
The Allotment of the land mentioned below depicted 
in MR/1873, situated in the No. 330-B Magamure Grama 
Niladhari Division, in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Kamburupitiya in Matara District in the Southern 
Province.     
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Kadawathagewattha; Description: 
Hena, Cinnamon cultivation about 15 years old; Name 
and Address of the land claimed by: Jinadasa Wellappili, 
Narandeniya, Kamburupitiya; Extent: 0.783 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 02 
Name of Land: Kadawathagewattha; Description: 
Hena, Cinnamon cultivation about 15 years old; Name 
and Address of the land claimed by: A. S. Jayawardhana, 
Medagoda, Kamburupitiya; Extent: 0.527 Hectare.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired  are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Kamburupitiya on 
the 14th day of February 2022 at 10.30 a. m. and are 
hereby  requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate )  
on or before the 07th day  of February 2022, the nature of 
their interests in the land,  the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
