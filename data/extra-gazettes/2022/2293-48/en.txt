WHEREAS, the office of member of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Mahenthirarani 
Ponnampalam, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that ward 15 - Kachchai - Allarai of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate  
Vanithamani Somasuntharam to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Vanithamani 
Somasuntharam was elected as the member of  the ward 15 - Kachchai - Allarai of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
