Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of office of the Chairman shall 
be deemed to have been resign under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987 as amended by No. 21 of 2012 
(Special Provisions) Local Authority Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Munidasa 
Gamage the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman 
within a period of Thirty (30) days;  ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Maliduwa Rajapaksha Jagath to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendment) act No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Maliduwa Rajapaksha Jagath elected as Chairman of the 
Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 T. W. P
oshiTha Kaumadi,
 Returning Officer,
 Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha.
