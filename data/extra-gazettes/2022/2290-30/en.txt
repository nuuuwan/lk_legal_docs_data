L. D. B. 21/78 (XV) (B) II
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. D. Susil Premajayanth, 
Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule 
to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1469/38 of November 03, 2006 (as amended from time to time), which 
speciﬁ es the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the 
provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition 
immediately after the entry "4. Department of Integrated Design" appearing in Column II which corresponds to the item 
"Faculty of Architecture" appearing in Column I of the Schedule (speciﬁ ed in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 1819/46 of July 19, 2013) of the following entry:-
   "5. Department of Facilities Management"
