WHEREAS the office of   member of Chavakachcheri Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Kanagasingam 
Darshan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon   
member of that ward 11 -  Kovilkudiyiruppu - A (Land side) of Chavakachcheri Urban Council who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Nallaiya Sundaralingam to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Rajaendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Chavakachcheri Urban Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nallaiya Sundaralingam 
was elected as the member of the ward 11 - Kovilkudiyiruppu - A (Land side) of  Chavakachcheri Urban Council.
