THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 D.S. Ref No.: DSM/YT/LAND/5/74. 
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.: 4-3/18/2019/WS/164. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for the construction of 
Yatawatta Pump House under Greater Matale Water 
Supply Project. 
 
 
Description of the land/ servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
The allotment of land called "Hunuwelakumbura alias 
Nisaru Pela", in total extent  of about 0.0388 Hectare, 
depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Ma 1853 of the 
Surveyor General, situated at the village of Nikagolla, in 
Mediyapola Grama Niladhari Division, in Asgiri 
Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor Division, in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Yatawatta in Matale District, 
Central Province and bounded as follows: 
 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Hunuwelakumbura alias Nisaru 
Pela; Description: For acquisition of an allotment of land 
for the construction of Yatawatta Pump House under 
Greater Matale Water Supply Project; Name of the claimant: 
Samunge Mohamed Lahir, No.21/1, Pamunudeniya, Yatawatta; 
Extent: 0.0388  Hectare. 
 
 
All  persons who claim ownership/ interest or any other 
connection to the aforesaid land/servitude are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized    
in writing. before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 22nd day of 
August 2022 at the Divisional Secretariat, Yatawatta. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day of August  2022, 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority)
