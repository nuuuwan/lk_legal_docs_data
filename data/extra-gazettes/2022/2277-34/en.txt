WHEREAS, the office of return member of Ampara Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation  ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group  to which Teral Berenjar the member belonged, was required 
by me under Section 66A (1)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Ampara Urban Council who 
belongs to such Independent Group and is not  disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Samarakoon 
Mudiyanselage Jayasundara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Ampara Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section  66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Jayasundara was selected as the member of the Ampara Urban Council.
