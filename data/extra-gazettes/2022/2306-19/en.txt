Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  akurana  pradeshiya  sabha

ordinance  (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be the 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha the recognized political party to which Mohamed 
Ramsan Faththima Safani the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of  All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Seyadu Ahamed Sithy Rozan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Seyadu 
Ahamed Sithy Rozan was selected as the member of the Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
