meeting for the purpose of electing the vice chairman for the Rabewa Pradeshiya Sabha of Anuradhapura District in North Central

in me, under the provisions of section 66 (C) that should be read with section 66 (G) of Local Government Election (amendment) 
Act, No. 22 of 2012, as amended by Local Authorities Election Ordinance (Chapter 262).
