THE  uNIvERSITIES ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 27(1) Read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 21 of the aforesaid act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 420/25 of  september 26, 
1986 (as amended from time to time) establishing the eastern university, sri lanka, by the addition immediately after the 
entry “13. Department of Tamil Studies” appearing in Column II of the schedule (specified in the Order published in the 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2220/19 of March 22, 2021) which corresponds to the item “Faculty of arts and Cultur” appearing 
in Column 1 of the schedule (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1523/16 of November 14, 
2007) of the following new entry:-
“14. department of Information technology”.
A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
Minister of Education.
