EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: W/D.S./1/3/4/2021/01. 
       
I hereby notify under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the 
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
The lot of land as described below with a total extent 
of 0.1258 Hectare, and depi cted as Lot No. 01 of the 
preliminary plan dated 21.04.2022 and P.P. No. G 4302, 
prepared by the Superintendant of Surveyor, and located 
in Dickowita village in the Minor Division of Ragam 
Pattu within Wattala Divisional Secretariat Division in 
Gampaha District of the Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of  the land: Muthurajawela Watta; Description: 
Home Garden, two permanent building and mixed crop 
cultivation; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: G. L. 
Iresha Shyamalie, No. 37/1, N. H. S. Hekitta, Wattala; 
Extent: 0.1258 Hectare. 
 
All those who make claims, ownership claims and 
claims by any other or those who are authorized to 
represent others for the aforesaid lands should meet me 
personally carrying with them the related documents at 
10.00 a.m. on the 27th June 2022 at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Wattala. 
 
Futher, they have to keep me informed by a document 
with two copies before the 27th June 2022 giving the 
nature of claims, the reasons for making a claim for 
compensation, amount of compensation and how it was 
computed.  
 P. D. T. C. R
AJIKA,
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Wattala. 
 
17th May, 2022, 
At the Wattala Divisional Secretariat Office.
