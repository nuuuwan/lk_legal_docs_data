 (a) the master of science in Network engineering, complete with a minimum of 60 credits (sLQF Level 10), 
adhere to the minimum entry qualification of -
 (i) a degree of bachelor of science in engineering obtained from a university recognized by the university 
Grants Commission ; or
 (ii) a degree of bachelor of science in engineering obtained from the sri Lanka Institute of Information 
Technology (Guarantee) Ltd. ; or 
 (iii) any Professional Engineering qualification equivalent to the qualilfications specified under (i) or (ii) 
above, with minimum one year of experience in a related field. The recognition of such Professional 
Engineering qualification and the relevance of the field shall be determined by the relevant faculty and 
approved by the relevant senate.
 (b) the Master of Philosophy in Business Administration, as prescribed in Sri Lanka Qualification Framework 
(SLQF) Level II, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of -
 (i) a Bachelor’s Honours degree of SLQF Level 6 in a related field, complete with a minimum of 30 credits 
and a minimum GPa of 3.7 at a scale of 0 - 4.0 with evidence of research competencies in the related 
field as acceptable to the faculty.
 (ii) a Bachelor’s Honours degree of SLQF Level 6 in a related field, complete with a minimum of 30 credits 
and a successful completion of a qualifying examination.
 (iii) a Bachelor’s degree of SLQF Level 5 in a related field, complete with a minimum of GPA of 3.0 at the 
scale  of 0 - 4.0 and successful completion of a qualifying examination which will be conducted after 
completion of 30 credits equivalent to SLQF Level 6 in the same field.
 (iv) SLQF Level 7 or above in the related field ; or
 (v) the completion of NVQ Level 7 with a minimum GP a of 3.0 at the scale of 0 - 4.0 and the successful 
completion of a qualifying examination which will be conducted after completion of 30 credits equivalent 
to sLQF Level 6 or 7, as determined by the academic authority of the sri Lanka Institute of Information 
technology (Gurantee) Limited (sLItt).  
 
 (2) sri Lanka Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited (sLItt), is recognized to offer the degrees 
specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.
