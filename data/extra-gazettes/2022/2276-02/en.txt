Notice on Summon meeting  under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Amendment) 
Act, No. 22 of 2012
By virtue of power vested on me under Section 66(C) read with Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of 
Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to fill the vacancy arose to the Post of Vice Chairman 
of the Eravur Urban Council, Batticaloa District, Eastern Province at 10.00 a. m. on 28.04.2022 at Eravur Urban Council 
Hall for the purpose of elect the new Vice Charirman.
