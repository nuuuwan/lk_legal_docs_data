I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land of 
Undugoda-Yatideriya Estate which is required for 
resettling the people displ aced due to the Landslide 
occurred in 2016 in Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kegalle: 
 
  Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired:  
 
An allotment of the land depicted in the plan No. F.V.P. 
Undugoda Ke/3853 prepared by the Superintendent of 
Survey - Kegalle, and situated in the village of Pahala 
Yatideriya in the Grama Nila dhari Division of No. 56B 
Undugoda in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kegalle in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province 
and bounded as follows : 
Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land : Yatideriya Estate Undugoda 
Division; Names and address of the Claimant: Kegalle 
Plantations Company, No. 310, Nawinna, Maharagama. 
Land Reforms Commission, No. 475, Kaduwela Rd, 
Battaramulla; Extent: 0.0506 Hectares. 
 
North: Yatideriya Estate claimed by the Kegalle 
Plantations company; East: Yatideriya Estate claimed by 
the Kegalle Plantations company and Road; South: Road 
and Yatideriya Estate claimed by the Kegalle Plantations 
company; West: Yatideriya Estate claimed by the Kegalle 
Plantations company. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretariat of Kegalle on 28.09.2022 at 
10.00 a.m.
