ThE fAuNA AND fLORA PROTECTION ORDINANCE (ChAPTER 469)
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Fauna and Flora protection Ordinance (Chapter 469), I, mahinda 
amaraweera, minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation, do by this Order –
(1) declare under Sub-section (4) of that section, that the area of land described in the schedule hereto shall, with 
effect from the date of publication of this Order, cease to be a part of the sanctuary called and known as the 
“Gal Oya Valley North-eastern sanctuary”; and
(2) declare under Sub-section (2) of that section, that the remaining area of land after exclusion of the area 
described in the schedule hereto, from the area of land called “Gal Oya Valley North-eastern sanctuary”  
containing in the extent about 48 square miles and described in paragraph (c) of the schedule to the Order  
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 10640 of February 12, 1954, to be called and known as the “Gal Oya 
Valley North-eastern sanctuary” 
Mahinda aMaraweera,
 minister of Wildlife and Forest 
 resources Conservation.
