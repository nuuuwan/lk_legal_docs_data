PART I : SECTION (I)  — GENERAL 
 
Government  Notifications 
1A - G 35940 — 15 (01/2022)  
REGISTRATION OF PERSONS ACT, No. 32 OF 1968 
 
Order under Section 34 
 
BY virtue of the Powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016, I, 
Gotabaya Rajapaksa, Minister of Technology of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do by this Order, alter 
the date of expiration published in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of 
Section 6, 9, 14, 16, and 17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the 
aforesaid Act (other than the Registration of Persons Regulation 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of November, 
20, 1990) to be 31.12.2022, until the necessary infrastructur e, arrangements and technological methodologies are made 
to.  
 G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA, 
 President and Minister of Technology. 
 
24th January, 2022.
