Whereas the office of  return member of Horana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political pary to which Degambada 
Arachchige Nandawathi the member belonged was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Horana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Samarakkodige Vindya Shrenika Perera to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Horana Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Samarakkodige Vindya Shrenika Perera was selected as the member of the Horana Pradeshiya Sabha. 
  
Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, 
Returning Officer,
Horana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
