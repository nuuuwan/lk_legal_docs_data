ThE UNIvERSITIES ACT, NO. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dinesh Gunawardana, 
the minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid Act in respect of the horizon college 
of Business and Technology (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the companies Act, No. 7 of 
2007 and bearing registration Number PV 62877, do by this Order amend the schedule II to the Order made under the said 
Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2208/49 of January 01, 2021 by the addition immediately after the entry 
''Bachelor of science honours in Biotechnology" appearing in column I and its corresponding entry in column II thereof, 
of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in 
schedule I hereto. 
schedULe I
CONDITIONS
1.  h orizon college of Business and Technology (Private) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme 
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Management Honours in Accounting and Finance, adhere 
to the minimum entry qualifications of -
(a) three (03) simple passes (s) in commerce stream in one and the same sitting at the Gce (Advanced Level) 
examination conducted by the department of examinations of sri Lanka or equivalent; or 
(b) three (03) simple passes (S) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) 
examination conducted by the department of examinations of sri Lanka with a bridging course approved by 
the Specified Authority.
