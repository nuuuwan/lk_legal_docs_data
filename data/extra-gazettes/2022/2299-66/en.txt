ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16 Of   1978  
Order  under  Section 27(1) read  with  Section 21 and Section 139
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, dr.  a. d. susil 
premajayantha, minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, 
amend the schedule to the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as amended from 
time to time), which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of  the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, which is deemed 
by virtue of the provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a University established under section 21 of the said act 
by the substitution for the item "3. department of agricultural engineering" appearing in Column ii of the schedule which 
corresponds to the item "Faculty of Agriculture" appearing in Column I of the Schedule (Specified in the Order Published in 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1567/23 of  
september, 19, 2008) of the  following entry :-
"3. department of agricultural engineering and environmental technology"
 d r. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
