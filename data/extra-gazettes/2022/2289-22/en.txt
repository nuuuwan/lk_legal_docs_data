Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Muththuppillai 
Muralitharan the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward No. 08 Sittandy East of Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sivanandan Vavananthan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sivanandan 
Vavananthan was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 08 Sittandy East of Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
