Second Supplementary Financial Statement
GrANtiNG PermiSSioN by virtue of PowerS veSted iN me uNder SectioN 27(A) of ProviNciAl

2022
1. this permission may be cited as “Permission to disburse under the second supplementary financial Statement - 2022 of 
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial council” and come in to operation from 29.06.2022.
2. the expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial council which is estimated rupees thirty eight billion five hundred 
and Ninety two million one hundred and twenty four thousand and Seventy two and cents Seventy four only 
(38,592,124,072.74) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2022 and ending 31st of december 2022, 
which is specified as “Financial Year 2022” in the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue - Expenditure Estimates 
prepared for the year 2022, that was approved on 29.11.2021 and amended by the first supplementary budget 2022, is 
hereby amended as, rupees thirty Nine billion Six hundred and eight million three hundred and Sixty five thousand 
Seven hundred and Seventy two and cents Seventy four only (rs.39,608,365,772.74) and grant permission to the 
Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
council fund.
3. Accordingly the first Schedule of the Annual Financial Statement and revenue - Expenditure Estimates - 2022, is amended 
as the first schedule hereof and grant permission to disburse the same.
