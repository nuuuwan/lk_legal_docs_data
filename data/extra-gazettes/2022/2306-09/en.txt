Greater dambulla Urban development area of municipal Council of dambulla and Pradeshiya Sabha Limit of dambulla

NotICe is hereby given to the General Public of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8(G) of the 
Urban development authority Law No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the act, No. 4 of 1982 that I,  Prasanna ranathunga, the 
minister in charge of the subject of Urban development & Housing, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8(F) 
of the said act, No. 4 of 1982 have approved the development Plan for the Greater dambulla Urban development area of 
municipal Council of dambulla and Pradeshiya Sabha Limit of dambulla and Pradeshiya Sabha Limit of Galewela and Part 
of the Pradeshiya Sabha Limit of Naula and Part of the Pradeshiya Sabha Limit of Palagala and Part of the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Limit of Kekirawa prepared under Section 8(a) of the said act on the day of 31st october 2022.
