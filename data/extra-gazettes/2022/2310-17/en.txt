Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  matugama  pradeshiya

WHEREAS the office of  member of Matugama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings of 
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Adikariwattage 
Priyantha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that No. 10 - Keeranthidiya of Matugama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kamburawala Kankanamge Mothilal Santhapriya Ranaweera to fill such vacancy ;
therefore I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Matugama Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Kamburawala Kankanamge Mothilal Santhapriya Ranaweera was elected as the member of the No. 10 - Keeranthidiya 
of Matugama Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K
alindra Jayaweera Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
