EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 Reference No.: DEV/LND/02/ACQI/02/109.  
 
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for the public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
  Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired:   
 
 
Allotment of land bearing Lot No. 01 (Extent: About 
0.0378 Hec.) depicted in the preliminary survey plan 
bearing No. MR/2899, situated in the Village of Gandara, 
Grama Niladhari Division of Gandara West in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara, Matara 
District, Southern Province. 
 
Sub-schedule of the land description 
 
01. Lot  No. 1 
Name of the Land: Hompala pittaniya watta alias 
Jagodawatta; Description: There is a mixed cultivation 
and two permanent buildings; Extent: 0.0378 Hectares. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of 
Dewinuwara on the 28 day of February, 2022 at 10.00 
a.m and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 14 day of February, 2022 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land/servitude, the amount of the compensation and 
the details of computation of such amount.
