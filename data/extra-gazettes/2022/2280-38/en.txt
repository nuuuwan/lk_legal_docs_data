Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the Pannala Pradeshiya sabha 
under section 66a (1) oF the local authorities elections ordinance (chaPter 262)
WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending Council 
meetings for a period of three month without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which K. S. M. Sapran 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the returned 
member of Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
M. S. M. Rilwan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the Pannala Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
M. S. M. Rilwan was selected as the member of the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
