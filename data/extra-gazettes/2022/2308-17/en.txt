SPECIAL   COmmODITY   LEvY  ACT,  NO.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007, I, ranil 
wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies of the democratic socialist republic 
of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose in respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special 
Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from december 01, 2022 subject to provisions of 
subsection (3) of section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
The order made under section 2 and 5 of the special Commodity Lvey act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2298/51 of september 22, 2022 in respect of b’ onions (Hs Code 0703.10.20) and 
the order made under section 2 of the special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2280/31 of may 18, 2022 in respect of Canned Fish (Hs Heading 16.04) are rescinded with effect 
from december 01, 2022.
