The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
I, Gotabaya rajapaksa, President of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by 
Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry act (Chapter 393), having enlarged the time ending on 30th May, 2021 for the 
rendering of the final report on completion of tasks assigned to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to investigate and 
inquire into various allegations and alleged corruption in Sri Lanka Customs established under Section 2 of the said act 
on 24th February, 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2216/ 14 dated 24th February, 2021 of 
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by five months from 30th May, 2021 to 31st October, 2021 by Notification 
published in the Gazette Extraodinary No. 2229/10 dated 29th May, 2021 of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, 
and again, under the said powers, enlarging the time up to 28th February, 2022 by Notification published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2251/ 49 dated 29th october, 2021 of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby once 
again, under the said powers enlarge the time up to 31st March, 2022.
  G
otabaya Rajapaksa,
  President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
28th February, 2022.
