Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF   the  dodangoda  pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66 a (1)  oF   the  local  authorities   elections  
ordinance  (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending 
meetings of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the group leader of the Independent Group, the recognized independent group to which Ambawalage 
Dalsan Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under 
section 9 of the local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Group Leader of the aforesaid recognized independent group has communicated his decision to 
nominate Samaraweera Mudalige Don Nandana to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Dodangoda Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Samaraweera Mudalige Don Nandana was selected as the member of the Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K
alindra Jayaweera Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
 Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
