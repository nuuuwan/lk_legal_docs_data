Whereas the office of  member of  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Eelam People’s Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which Murugan 
Iyathurai the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  
the member of that ward 10 - Kollankallady of Valikamam North  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sandirakumar Rajanidevi to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam North  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sandirakumar 
Rajanidevi was elected as the member of the ward 10 - Kollankallady of Valikamam North  Pradeshiya Sabha.
