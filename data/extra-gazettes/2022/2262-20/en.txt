SRI LANkA ExPORT  DEvELOPmENT  ACT, No. 40 Of 1979
Order under Section 14
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 14 of the sri Lanka export development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, dr. 
Bandula Gunawardana, Minister of Trade, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the rate 
of CESS specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2202/6 dated 
17.11.2020, in respect of the articles described under the H. S. Codes as specified in the Schedule hereto, with effect from 
12th January, 2022.
 Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
 Minister of Trade.
Ministry of Trade,
Colombo 03,
11th January, 2022.
