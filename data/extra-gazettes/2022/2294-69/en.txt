Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Kesbewa Urban coUncil  
Under  section  66a(1) (a) oF  the  local  aUthorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of Member of  Kesbewa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation; 
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Lalith 
Rohana Liyanage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward No. 04 Pelenwatta of Kesbewa Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Piyumathara Liyanage to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Kesbewa Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Piyumathara Liyanage was elected as the member of the Ward No. 04 Pelenwatta of Kesbewa Urban Council.
   
 M. M. S. K. B
andara Mapa,
 Returning Officer,
 Kesbewa Urban Council. 
District Election Office,
Colombo,
