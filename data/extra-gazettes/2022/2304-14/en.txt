Appointments & C., by the President
No. 744/1 of 2022
                        P. S. No. : PS/cSA/00/9/1/4/1
It is hereby notified that the Hon. President has been pleased to appoint the following persons as Commissioners of the 
Mediation Boards Commission, with effect from October 22, 2022 under the provisions of Section 2(1) of the Mediation 
Boards Act, No. 72 of 1988.
 1. Justice Hector Yapa
  (Retired Judge of the Supreme Court)
 2. Justice Nimal Dissanayake
  (Retired Judge of the Supreme Court)
 3. Justice Chandradasa Nanayakkara
  (Retired Judge of the Court of Appeal)
 4. Mr. Vivekananthan Puvitharan, PC
 5. Mr. J. W. S. Siriwardana 
02. The Hon. President has appointed Justice Hector Yapa, retired Supreme Court Judge under the above-mentioned 
provisions, as the Chairman of the said Commission.
                 By the Hon. President’s Command,
    
 E. M. S. B. EkanayakE,  
 Secretary to the President.
