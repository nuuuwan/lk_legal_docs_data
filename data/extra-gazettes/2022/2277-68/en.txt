THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below, is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act. 
 An Allotment of land described as following names and 
extent in the Advance Tracing No. HA/AMB/2020/126 dated 
on 08.10.2020 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at 
the village of Thawaluwila (FVP 466), in Giruwa Pattuwa 
Minor Division East, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Ambalantota in Hambantota District, Southern Province and 
bounded as follows. 
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Southern Park – Extent: 0.0212 Hectare
 North: Southern Park claimed by K. M. Mallika and Road; 
East: Walawe river; South: Walawe river and lot B; West: 
Southern Park claimed by K. M. Mallika and Road.
 02. Lot ‘B’ – Southern Park – Extent: 0.0061 Hectare

Walawe river; South: Walawe river and lot C; West: Southern 
Park claimed by K. M. Kamalawathie, Southern Park claimed 
by K. M. Mallika and Lot  A.
