Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  chairman oF  the  imaduwa  Pradeshiya sabha  
under  section  66b(2)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  (chaPter  262) 
as amended  by  local  authorities  elections (amendment) act, no. 16 oF 2017
WHEREAS, the office of  Chairman in Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And Whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Ahangama 
Vithanage Sarath Kumara the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance 
to decide upon the member of that Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as 
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Jayawickrama Kankanamge Darmarathna Jayawickrama to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended  by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Jayawickrama Kankanamge Darmarathna Jayawickrama 
elected as Chairman of the Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 W. H. R. Wijaya KumaRa,
 Returning Officer,
 Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Galle.
09th August, 2022.
