CONSumER AffAIRS AuThORITY ACT, No. 09 Of 2003
Direction under Section 10(1) (a)
actiNG under the powers vested in it by section 10 (1) (a) of the consumer affairs authority act, No. 09 of 2003, the 
consumer affairs authority directs all Manufacturers, producers, store owners, suppliers, distributors or traders shall have 
a receipt or bill or invoice in writing or printing or electronic for the goods in their possession, custody or under their control, 
shall not sell, store, collect, distribute, expose or offer for sale, display for sale, unless they have following information is in 
the form of a receipt or bill or invoice for the goods they have purchased, issued by the Manufacturer, producer, store owner, 
supplier, distributor or trader, stored in a container, warehouse, silo, place of business or any other places. 
1. Name and address of the supplier
2. date of purchase
3. purchased price
4. t ype of goods
5. Quantity
6. Batch number (if any)
this direction shall come in to effect from april 27th, 2022.
 By Order of the consumer affairs authority,
 
Maj. Gen. Shantha DiSSanayake (Retd.),
