Whereas the office of return member of  Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Hapu Arachchige 
Dona Danushka Nadeeshani Karunarathna the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as 
a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Deepani Kandana Arachchi to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Bandaragama 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Deepani Kandana Arachchi was selected as the member of the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha. 
  
Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando,
Returning Officer,
Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
