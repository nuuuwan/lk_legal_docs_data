By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2A of the Value Added tax Act, No. 14 of 2002, as amended by Act, No. 
06 of 2005, I, Ranil wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do by this Order, 
amend the order published in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2282/26 dated May 31, 2022 by the substitution for the 
sub paragraph (b) of paragraph 1, of the following sub paragraph, with effect from september 01, 2022.
“(b) On the import and/or supply of goods or supply of services, other than the import of goods as referred to under 
sub-paragraph (a) of this paragraph, at the rate of fifteen per centum (15%).”
