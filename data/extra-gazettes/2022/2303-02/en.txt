THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 My Reference No.: 4-3/9/2018/WS/236. 
 Divisional Secretary's Ref. No.: DS/VS/ACQ/WT/J-199/01. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intended to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for a public purpose : 
 
Description of the Land/ Servitude to be Acquired : 
 
Preliminary Plan No. : PPYA 3006, 
Village : Chunnakam, 
G.N. Division : J/199 Chunnakam Town West, 
D.S. Division : Valikamam South, Uduvil, 
District : Jaffna, 
Province : Northern, 
Purpose : To Construction of Chunnakam 
Water Tower under Jaffna -
Kilinochi Water Supply and 
Sanitation Project. 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Sonawattekkaladdy, Sonawatte 
and Theviranai; Land use: Garden; Owner: Murugesu 
Sunthararajan and Wife Lingeshwary; Extent: 0.0689 
Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretariat, Valikamam South, Uduvil on 
22nd day of February, 2023 at 10 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in three copies) on or 
before the 23rd day of January, 2023. The Nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ 
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
