ThE sri Lanka Ports authority acting by virtue and in terms of powers vested under the section 42a (1) of the sri Lanka 
Ports Authority Act No. 51 of 1979 as amended by Act No. 07 of 1984 hereby certifies that S. S. Shipping (Private) Limited, 
(Company registration No. PV5012) incorporated under the laws of sri Lanka having its registered office at No. 10-1/1, 
10th Lane, Colombo 03 being the shipping agent has refused, failed and neglected to pay the charges due to the said 
authority, as more fully described in the schedules to this  certificate hereinafter and that there is now due and owing from 
the said s. s. shipping (Private) Limited, to the said sri Lanka Ports authority a sum of rupees Four Million Two hundred 
Forty seven Thousand and Nine hundred and sixty six and cents Fifty seven (rs. 4,247,966.57) only.
