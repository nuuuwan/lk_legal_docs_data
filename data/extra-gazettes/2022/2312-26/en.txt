i, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice, Prison Affairs and Constitutional Reforms, do by this Notification, fix with

Notification made under Paragraph (1) of the First Schedule to the High Court of the Provinces (Special Provisions) 
act, No. 10 of 1996 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2112/33 dated 28th february, 2019 is hereby rescinded 
without prejudice to anything duly done thereunder.
