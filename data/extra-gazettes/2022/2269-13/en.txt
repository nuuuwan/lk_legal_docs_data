WHEREAS, the office of member of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Mylvahanam Elango the 
member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that ward No. 07 
Kurumanvelly of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate Raththinaiah 
Venuraj to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Raththinaiah 
Venuraj was elected as the member of the ward No. 07 Kurumanvelly of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. Sufyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
