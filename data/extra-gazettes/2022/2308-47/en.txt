WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kanapathippillai 
Puvaneswary the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Kalmunai Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Perinparasa Manoranjini to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Kalmunai Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Perinparasa Manoranjini was selected as the member of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
