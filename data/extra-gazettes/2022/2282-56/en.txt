THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : KDS/PHT/4/6/1/11'
Ministry of Lands Ref. No. :4-3/18/2018/WS/465'
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Goverment intends
to acquire the following lands which are  required for a public
purpose.
Description of the Land  to be Acquired :
Three allotments of land called Kiribowa and Pinnagolla,
in total extent of 0.0486 Hectare, depicted as Lot Nos. 1, 2 and
3 in Preliminary Plan No. Maha 7255 dated on 24.02.2022,
surveyed, prepared and approved by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys, Kandy on behalf of Surveyor
General, situated in the village of Butawatta in Grama Niladhari
Division of Pinnagolla, No. 986 in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Pathahewaheta in Kandy District, Central Province
and bounded as follows,
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land : Kiribowa; Extent : 0.0041 Hectare
(1.6 Perches); Name and address of the claimants: Mr. H.G.
Somarathne, No. C/A/7/13, Ranpokunagama, Nittambuwa
and others.
Boundaries of the land:
North By: Kiribowa claimed by H.G .Somarathne and
others and Road; East By: Lot No. 62 in Preliminary Plan No.
2432; South By:Lot No. 62 in Preliminary Plan No. 2432;West
By:Kiribowa claimed by H.G.Somarathne and others.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land : Pinnagolla; Extent : 0.0192 Hectare
(7.6  Perches); Name and address of the claimants: Mr. H.A.
Sisira Hettiarachchi, No. 37, Pinnagolla, Thalathuoya.
Boundaries of the land:
North By: Lot No. 63 in Preliminary Plan No. 2436; East
By: Lot No. 1 in Preliminary Plan No. 6857; South By:Lot No.
61 in Preliminary Plan No.2436;West By:Lot No. 62 in
Preliminary Plan No.2436.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land : Kiribowa; Extent : 0.0253 Hectare
(10 perches); Name and address of the claimants:
Mr. K. Dhanaraj, Kiribowa, Thalathuoya.
Boundaries of the land:
North By: Kiribowa claimed by K. Dhanaraj; East By:
Kiribowa claimed by K. Dhanaraj; South By:Danewatta claimed
by M.G. Premarathne;West By:Lot No. 1 in Preliminary Plan
No.6857 and Lot No. 63 in Preliminary Plan No.2436.
