EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
 My No: La/Div. Sec./3/3/5/2/17.   
 Land Ministry No.: 13/96/FR/380. 
 
I do hereby give notice to acquire the following Land by 
the government which necessary to use for public purpose 
under the section 7 (1) of Lands Acquisition Act amended 
by the Land Acquisition (Amended) Act No. 28 of 1964. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired : 
 
Acquiring of land to the government for Knuckles 
Conservation Zone  
 
Total extent of Plot of land called Gonamadawatta 
depicted as Lot No. 01 is 2.0234 Hec. situated at Pitawala 
Village of E -385 Pitawala Grama Niladhari Division of 
Laggala Divisional Secretary's Division of Matale District 
in Central Province that shown in Surveyor Generals' 
approved Plan No. FVP  1843 and dated 22.07.2021. 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Gonamadawatta; Land Use: Forest; 
Name and Address of Claimant: Mr. Rohan Nalin 
Elikewela at No. 71/23C, Dodamwala Mawatha, 
Asgiriya, Kandy; Extent: 2.0234 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents before 
me at 10.00 a.m. on the day of 11.05.2022 at the Laggala 
Divisional Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04.05.2022 the 
nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.   
            
 S. VIJAYAKUMAR, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Laggala. 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Laggala,
