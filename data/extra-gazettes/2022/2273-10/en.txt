WHEREAS the office of member of  Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Yusup Mohamad Thaufeek 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member 
of that ward number 10 - Muriyakadawala of Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Tikiri Bandara Wanasinghe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, returning officer of the Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Tikiri Bandara Wanasinghe 
was elected as the member of the ward number 10 - Muriyakadawala of Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha.
 K. Mugunthan,
 Returning officer,
 Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election office, 
Anuradhapura.
