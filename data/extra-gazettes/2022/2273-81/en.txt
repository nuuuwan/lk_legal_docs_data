NOTICE Made under Section 24 (1) (wd) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987. This is to announce that the Galnewa 
Pradeshiya Sabha in the Anuradhapura District of the North Central Province in accordance with Section 24(1) (wd) of the 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 declares the roads and str eets shown below in the Schedule as the roads and streets 
owned by the Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha under decision No. 93/2020 taken at the General Meeting of the Galnewa Pradeshiya 
Sabha held on 23rd of September 2020.
   In case of any objection by the general public of the area o r the owners of the lands pertaining to the roads, any action 
may be taken in accordance with Section 24(2) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to prove their ownership within 
one month from the date of publication of this Gazette will prompt you to do so.
   This is make the public aware that if there is no protest ag ainst this announcement during this period, the streets 
and roads mentioned in the Schedule will also be recognized and controlled as the streets and roads owned by the Galnewa 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
