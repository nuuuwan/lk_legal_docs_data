WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to, which Moragal 
Pedige Thilak Rohana Jayawickrama the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon  member of that 09 Wilegoda of Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hapanthilage Tilakarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hapanthilage Tilakarathna 
was elected as the member of the 09 Wilegoda of Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
