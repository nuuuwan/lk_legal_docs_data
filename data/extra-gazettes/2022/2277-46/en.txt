In Accordance with the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Delegation of power under the Co-operative Societies Statute No. 10 of 1990 of the Central Provincial 
Council as amended by the Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Statute No. 4 of 1993 of the Central 
Provincial Council 
I, Lalith U. Gamage, as the Governor of the Central Provincial Council, in accordance with the powers vested in me by the 
Article 154(c) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in terms of Sub-sections 2(2), 2(4) of 
the Co-operative Societies Statute No. 10 of 1990 of the Central Provincial Council as amended by the Cooperative Societies 
(Amendment) Statute No. 04 of 1993 of the Central Provincial Council, I hereby declare that powers referred in Column 
III will be delegated to the Deputy Commissioner of Co-operative Development (Sri Lanka Administrative Service) of the 
Central Provincial Council referred to in Column No. 1 below with effect from the date specified in Column II.
 L
aLith U. GamaGe,
 The Governor of Central Provincial Council.
