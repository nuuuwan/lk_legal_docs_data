Notice under Section 7 
 
My  No. : 9/3/1/2017/02. 
Ref. No. of Ministry of Lands : 4-3/9/2017/WS/322. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude right which is required for a public purpose.
 
 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude right to be acquired : 
 
All that allotment of land in total extent of about 
0.0956 Hectare, depicted as lot 25 in sheet 1 and (block 2) 
in the Cadastral Plan No. 510045 and dated 20.06.2022, 
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at the village 
of Kotadeniyawa, in Kotadeniyawa Grama  Niladhari 
Division, No. 54B in the Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Divulapitiya in Gampaha District, Western Province 
and bounded as follows.   
 
01. Lot No. 25 
 
Name of the land: Etaluwahena and Thekkalanda; 
Description: Homestead; Claimant: Government; Original 
Claimant : Cannot be found, taken over under Sub section 
38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act. To finalize land 
acquisition process; Extent : 0.0956 Hectare.  
 
 
 
All  persons who claim ownership, interest or any 
other connection to the aforesaid land are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 a.m. on 28.12.2022 at the 
Divisional Secretariat, Divulapitiya. 
 
And further, you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing in duplicate on or before the 19.12.2022, the 
nature of their interests in the land/servitude, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
