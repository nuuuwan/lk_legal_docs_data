PETROLEum  PRODuCTS  (REGuLATION  AND  CONTROL  Of  SuPPLIES) ACT, No. 34 Of 1979 
Order under Section 1
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Petroleum Products (Regulation and control of supplies) Act,                     
No. 34 of 1979. I, Kanchana Wijesekera, Minister  of Power and Energy, do hereby this Order determine the day, 10th of june, 
2022, as the date on which the provisions of Parts II, III, IV , V , VI and VIII of the aforesaid Act, shall came into operation.
 Kanchana WijeseKera,
 Minister of Power and Energy.
At Colombo,
10th june 2022.
