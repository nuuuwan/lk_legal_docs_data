EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref.No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/101/4001. 
Ministry of Land's Ref. No.: 4-3/12/2008/WS/280. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act , as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude  which is required for a public purpose. 
 
A land part from the following  Land situated in the 
village Hiridenikanda in the 428A Ruwanella Grama 
Niladhari Division in Matara Divisional Secretariat 
Division in Matara District, Southern Province and 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in Primary Plan Mara 2889 has 
been acquired to build a new water tank for Maligathenna 
old water tank for developing Matara -Mirissa Water 
Supply Project. 
Schedule  
Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Maligathenna; Description: Water 
Tank in the Garden; Claimant: IIandarige Andrayas; 
Extent: 0.0051 Hectare.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in  
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Matara on 
the 25th   of November 2022 at 10.30 a. m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on  or before  04th  of November, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the  particulars of their claims for 
compensation  and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
   K. N. KAUSHALYA KUMARI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara.  
At Divisional Secretariat, Matara, 
On the 22nd  day of September 2022.
