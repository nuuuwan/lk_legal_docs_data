THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
  My No. : BD/BD/LND/03/05/19/1370.
I hereby give notice, to the public under Sub - section (1) of  
the Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by 
Land Acquisition Act (Amendment) No. 28 of 1964 that the 
land described below is required by the government and the 
government intends to acquire the said land/servitude for 
public purpose. Acquisition of Land for Badulla-Chenkaladi 
Road Development Project.
 Description of the Land / Servitude to be Acquired:
 The boundaries to the block of land mentioned in Schedule 
01 with the extent of 0.0019 Hectare and stated as block No. 
1370 of the ﬁ nal village plan dated 31.12.2015 and bearing 
FVP 170, supplement 62 and sheet No. 57 prepared by the 
Survey General, situated in Badulla-Glen Alphin 80-H, 
Udawela 80-D, Wewessa 80-J of Vediguna village of Rilpola 
Korale Minor Division of Badulla Divisional Secretariat 
Division in the District of Badulla, Uva Province.
 All those who claim ownership, interest and any other 
connection to aforesaid land, should appear personally, or 
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Badulla on 18.05.2022 at 2. 30. p.m.
 In addition, hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
with two copies of the nature of their interests on the land/
lands, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and 
the details of computation of such amount before 11.05.2022.
