SPECIAL  COmmODITY LEvY ACT, No. 48 Of 2007 
Order under Section 2 
By  virtue of the powers  vested in me under  section 2 of the special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007,I, Basil rajapaksa, 
Minister of  Finance of the democratic  socialist republic  of sri Lanka, do by this order  impose in respect  of the Commodities  
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding  entries 
in Column II of  that  schedule.
This Order  shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing  from  January  12, 2022  subject  to provisions 
of  sub section (3) of  section 2 of  the special Commodity Levy Act, No.48 of 2007.
The Order made under  section 2 of the special Commodity  Levy Act, No.48 of 2007 and published  in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2249/3 of October  11, 2021, the Order made under  Section 5 of the Special  Commodity 
Levy  Act, No.48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary  Gazzette Notification No. 2252/25 of  November 02,2021 in  
respect of rice  ( Hs Codes 1006.30.10,1006.30.20 and 1006.30.30) and the Order  made  under section  2 of the  special  
Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published  in the Extraordinary Gazzette Notification No. 2249/16 of  October 
13, 2021 in respect  of  sugar (Hs Heading 17.01 ) are rescinded  with  effect  from  January 12, 2022.    
    
 Basil Rajapaksa,   
 Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of  Finance
Colombo 01,                                          
11th January, 2022.
