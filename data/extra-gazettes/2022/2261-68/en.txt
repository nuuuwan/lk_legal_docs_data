THE  MEDIATION  (SPECIAL  CATEGORIES  OF  DISPUTES)  ACT,  No. 21  OF  2003 
 
Order under Sections 2, 3 and 8 
 
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 2, 3 and 8 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act,  
No. 21 of 2003, I, Mohammed Ali Sabry, Minister of Justice, do by this Order- 
 
(1) determine that the categories of disputes to which the pr ovisions of the aforesaid Act shall apply in regard to the 
settlement through mediation, shall be- 
 
(a) all disputes relating to State lands; and  
 
(b) all disputes relating to private lands, other than- 
 
(i)  disputes under Section 66 of the Primary Court’s Procedure Act, No. 44 of 1979 which  have been 
referred to Primary Courts; 
 
(ii)  disputes with regard to the recovery of premises given on rent or lease; and 
 
(iii) disputes relating to the rights of third parties or co-owners who were not parties to mediation in  
co-ownership disputes. 
 
(2) specify all Administrative Districts of Sri Lanka as Sp ecial Mediation Board Areas within which the settlement of 
such disputes shall be carried out; 
 
(3) specify that the monetary value of the subject matter of the category of disputes determined under paragraph (1) in 
regard to the settlement through mediation arising wholly  or partly within such Special Mediation Board Areas 
Specified in paragraph (2) shall be less than Rupees one Million (Rs. 1,000,000/-);
