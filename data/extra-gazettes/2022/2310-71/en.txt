Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Weligama  Pradeshiya 
sabha  under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
(chaPter  262)  
whereas, the office of member of  Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending council 
meetings for a period of three months without permission.
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Vindika 
Ipalawaththa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of the No. 25 Thalaramba Ward of Weligama  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Saliya Nanayakkara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi returning officer of the Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Saliya 
Nanayakkara was elected as the member of the No. 25 Thalaramba of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
