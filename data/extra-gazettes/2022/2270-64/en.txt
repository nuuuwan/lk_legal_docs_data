WHEREAS the office of member of Hikkaduwa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recoginzed political party to which Vinie 
Kariyawasam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of  that 07 Katukoliha of Hikkaduwa Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Aluthgedara Wedaralalage Wilashini Thakshila to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Hikkaduwa Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Aluthgedara Wedaralalage Wilashini Thakshila was elected as the member of 07 Katukoliha of Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
