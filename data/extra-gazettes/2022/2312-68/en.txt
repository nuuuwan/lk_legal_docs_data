EXCISE (SPECIAL PROvISIONS) ACT, No. 13 of 1989
Order under Section 3
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of the excise ( special Provisions) act, No. 13 of 1989, i , ranil 
Wickremesinghe, minister of Finance, economic stabilization & National Policies do by this Order declare that :-
1. (a) The 	Excise	Duty	on	every	article	specified	in	Column	III	of	the	Schedule	I	hereto	shall	be	payable	at	the	rate	
specified	in	the	corresponding	entry	in	Column	IV	of	the	Schedule	I;
(b) The 	concessionary	Excise	Duty	on	excisable	articles	specified	in	Column	II	of	the	Schedule	II	hereto	shall	be
payable	at	the	rate	specified	in	Column	III	of	the	Schedule	II	wherever	applicable;	and
(c) Wherever 	two	rates	are	specified	in	Column	IV	of	the	Schedule	I	and	Column	III	of	the	Schedule	II	hereto,	the
applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the excise duty payable shall be the rate which yields the higher
amount payable as excise duty.
