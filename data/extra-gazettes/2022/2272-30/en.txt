under  section  66a(1)  of  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance (chapter 262)  
WHEREAS  the  office of return member of  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
  And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front the recognized political party to which Wijayasinhe 
Mudiyanselage Roshanee Shiromala Amarasena the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of Thirty (30) days; 
And  whereas the secretary of the  aforesaid recognized political party has  communicated his  decision to nominate 
Tanthriwattage Rohinee Bandara to fill such vacancy; 
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer of the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Tanthriwattage Rohinee 
Bandara was selected as the member of the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha. 
Ravindra Subhashana Wedage,
Returning Officer,
Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office, 
Hambantota.
