WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Ilangaran 
Pedige Amila Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Garumuni Arachchillage Royanda Silva Sampath to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Garumuni Arachchillage Royanda Silva Sampath was selected as the member of  the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
