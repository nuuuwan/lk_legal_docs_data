Filling  oF   a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF   member  oF   the  walallawita  pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66 a  (1) oF   the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
( chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of return member of Walallawita Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which Munigoda 
Hewage Hemasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Walallawita Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Devamuni Adikaramage Sanath Chaminda to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Walallawita 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Devamuni Adikaramage Sanath Chaminda was selected as the member of  the Walallawita Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
 Walallawita Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
06th July, 2022.
