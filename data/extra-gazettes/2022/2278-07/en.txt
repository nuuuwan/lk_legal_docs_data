WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Akamad Lebbei 
Rafeek the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned 
member of Kalmunai Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days ;

Bathurudeen Sarmil Jahan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Kalmunai Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Bathurudeen Sarmil Jahan was selected as the member of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
