ThE uNIvERSITIES ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 27 (1) read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27 (1) of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. d. susil 
Premajayantha, Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the university grants Commission, do by this Order, 
amend the schedule to the Order made under section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 
1395/15, of June 1, 2005 (as amended from time to time) establishing the uva Wellassa university of sri Lanka, by the 
addition immediately after the entry "2. department of Biosystems technology" apperaing in Column II of the schedule 
which corresponds to the item "Faculty of Technological Studies" appearing in Column I of the Schedule, (specified 
in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2015/44 of April 19, 2017) of the following new entry:-  
 "3. department of Information and Communication technology".
  A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA, m. P.,
 Minister of Education.
