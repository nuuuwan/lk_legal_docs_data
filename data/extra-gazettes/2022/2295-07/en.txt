WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of the not attending 
more than three consecutive meetings of the council without permission :
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which 
Mohamed Musthafa Abdul Rahuman the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Muhammadu Ibrahim Muhammadu Kamaldeen to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammadu 
Ibrahim Muhammadu Kamaldeen was selected as the member of  the Kattankudy Urban Council.
