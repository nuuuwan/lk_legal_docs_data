ThE  fAuNA  AND  fLORA  PROTECTION  ORDINANCE (ChAPTER 469)
 Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Fauna and Flora protection Ordinance (Chapter 469), I, mahinda 
amaraweera, minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation, do by this Order –
 (1) declare under subsection (4) of that section, that the area of land described in the schedule hereto shall, with 
effect from the date of publication of this Order, cease to be a part of the sanctuary called and known as the 
“madampawila sanctuary”; and
 (2) declare under subsection (2) of that section, that the remaining area of land after exclusion of the area described in 
the schedule hereto, from the area of land called “madampawila sanctuary” containing in extent approximately 
1217.75 hectares and described in the schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1515/21 of 
september 21, 2007, to be called and known as the “madampawila sanctuary”.
 
 
Mahinda aMaraweera,
 minister of Wildlife and Forest resources,
 Conservation.
