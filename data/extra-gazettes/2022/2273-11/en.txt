WHEREAS the office of return member of  Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Dissanayaka 
Mudiyanselage Sanath Nandana Dissanayake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Pihimbuwalage Gedara Sunil Priyantha Kumara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, returning officer of the Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Pihimbuwalage Gedara Sunil Priyantha 
Kumara was elected as the member of the Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
 M. Mugunthan,
 Returning officer,
 Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office, 
Anuradhapura.
