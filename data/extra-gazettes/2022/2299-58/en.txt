Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Kalawana   pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66a(1)(a)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  Member of Kalawana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Disanayaka 
Mudiyanselage Sunil Chandrasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that 05 Hangarangala of Kalawana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Elabadage Sampath Niroshan Dharmadasa to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Kalawana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Elabadage Sampath 
Niroshan Dharmadasa was elected as the member of  the 05 Hangarangala of Kalawana Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 S
uranga ambagahathenne,
 Returning Officer,
 Kalawana Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
27th September, 2022.
