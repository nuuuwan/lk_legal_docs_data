DECLARATION Of REGIONAL DEvELOPmENT AREA
Order under Section 8 (1) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance No. 13 of 1946 (as amended by 
Act No. 49 of 2000)
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of urban development and Housing by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 
8 (1) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance No. 13 of 1946 as amended by Act, No. 49 of 2000 do hereby declare 
that the areas given in the following schedule to be a regional development area for the purpose of preparation of Regional 
Physical Plan.
 
Mahinda Rajapaksa (M. p),
 Minister of urban development and Housing.
Ministry of urban development and Housing,
“suhurupaya”,
Battaramulla.
25th February, 2022.
sCHedule
 The area comprises of the administrative boundary of Central Province as given in the 8th schedule of the 13th 
amendment to the constitution.
