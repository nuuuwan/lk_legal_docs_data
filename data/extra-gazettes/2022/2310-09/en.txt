KALMUNAI  KADATKARAI  MOSQUE  NAHOOR  ANDAHAI  DHARHA  SHARIEF   
201st Annual Flag Hoisting Festival  - 2022
IT is hereby notified for the information of the General Public that the aforementioned Kalmunai Kadatkarai Mosque Nahoor 
Andahai Dharha Sharief 201st Annual Flag Hoisting Festival will commence on 24th December 2022 and terminates on 
05th January, 2023.
The attention of the General Public is drawn in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimage Ordinance to the Standing 
Regulations published in the Government Gazette No. 13529 of 22nd February, 1963 which will be in force during the 
pilgrimage season.
