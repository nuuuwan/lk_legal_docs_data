GOTABAYA  RAJAPAKSA
Presidential Directive
WHEREAS, a Presidential Task Force for “One Country, One Law” has been established by the Presidential Directive
dated 26 th October, 2021 in terms of the powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And whereas, the time granted to accomplish the functions and responsibilities assigned to the aforesaid Task
Force is to end on 27 th May, 2022,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby enlarge the time
period of the Task Force by three (03) weeks from 27th May, 2022 to complete the task assigned to the aforesaid Task
Force.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka on the 27th day of May, Two
Thousand and Twenty Two.
By His Excellency’s Command,
G
AMINI SENARATH,
Secretary to the President.
