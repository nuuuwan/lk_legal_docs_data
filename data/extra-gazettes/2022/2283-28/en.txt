eXCIse  dUTY  ON  eTHYL  ALCOHOL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 32 read with section 22 of the excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as amended 
from time to time, I, Ranil wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies do hereby 
this Order, amend the Schedule I of the Excise Notification No. 988 of Gazette extraordinary No. 1956/19 of March 02,
