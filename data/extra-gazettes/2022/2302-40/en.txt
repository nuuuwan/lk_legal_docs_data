Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Wattala  Pradeshiya  sabha

(chaPter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member 
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Senarathna 
Arachchige Nilantha Leshan Sanjeewa Senasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Suriya Bandarage Don Krishan Shanaka to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Suriya 
Bandarage Don Krishan Shanaka was selected as the member of  the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
 K. J. S. M
adhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
21st October, 2022.
