WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to 
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Muhammdu 
Sareefdeen Asraf Ali the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of  Udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mohamed Ajwad Mohamed Fayas to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Udunuwara Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Mohamed Ajwad Mohamed Fayas was selected as the member of  the Udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
