ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
minister of education, on the recommendation of the university Grant commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under section 21 of the aforesaid act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 896/2 of November 07, 
1995 (as amended from time to time) establishing the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, which specifies the Faculties 
and departments of study of the sabaragamuwa university of sri Lanka, as follows :-
 (1) by the addition immediately after the item “Faculty of Medicine” (specified in the Order published in  
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2082/32 of august 01, 2018) appearing in column I of the schedule and its 
corresponding entries in column II of the schedule of the following new item and entries :-
 “Column I Column II
 Faculty of Computing 1. Department of Software Engineering
  2. department of data science
  3. Department of Computing and Information Systems.”.
