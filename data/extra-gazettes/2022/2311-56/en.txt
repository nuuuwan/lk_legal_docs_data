Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  harispattuwa   pradeshiya

WHEREAS the office of  return member of Harispattuwa Pradeshiya  Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to 
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ranaraja 
Mudiyanselage Lalith Ruwan Ranaraja the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Harispattuwa Pradeshiya  Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dulwala Galapitagedara Athula Kumara Alwis to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Harispattuwa Pradeshiya  
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Dulwala Galapitagedara Athula Kumara Alwis was selected as the member of the Harispattuwa Pradeshiya  Sabha.
   
 Dr. H. D. N. S
