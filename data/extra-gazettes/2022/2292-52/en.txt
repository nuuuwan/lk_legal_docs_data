WHEREAS, the office of return member of Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Muhammad 
Sarifdeen Muhammed Hayas the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Muhammadu Arifdeen Mujeebur Rahman to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Ratnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayake, Returning Officer of the Kurunegala 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Muhammadu Arifdeen Mujeebur Rahman was selected as the member of  the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
