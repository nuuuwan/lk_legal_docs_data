REGuLATIONS   uNDER   ThE  ImPORTS   AND  EXPORTS   CONTROL  ACT,  No.  01  Of  1969
IN Terms of the powers vested in me by section 20 read together with sub - section 4(1) and section 14 of the Imports and 
exports (Control) act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by act, No. 48 of 1985 and act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, 
the Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies, promulgate following Regulations.
 Ranil Wickremesinghe,
        Minister of Finance, economic   
 stabilization and National Policies.
Ministry of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies.
Colombo 01,
May 31, 2022.
Regulations
 1. These Regulations may be cited as the "Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 08 of 2022".
 2. Imports and exports (Control) Regulations No. 05 of 2022, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2270/18 dated 
March 09, 2022 and the Imports and exports (Control) Regulations No. 06 of 2022, published in the  Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2274/42 dated april 09, 2022 are hereby rescinded without prejudice to the validity of anything done thereunder 
during the periods in force.
 3. director General, sri Lanka Customs is hereby authorized to release any Cargos/ Consignments of goods, which were 
regulated by the Imports and exports (Control) Regulations No. 05 of 2022 or No. 06 of 2022 as referred in the Regulation 
No. 2 above during the period of March 10, 2022 to May 31, 2022, as per provisions of the Customs Ordinance without 
having an Import Control License effective from June 01, 2022.
 4.  These Regulations shall enter into force with effect from June 01, 2022.
