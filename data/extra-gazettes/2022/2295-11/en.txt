REGISTRATION  Of  ELECTORS  ACT,  No. 44 Of 1980 AS AmENDED  bY 
ThE  REGISTRATION  Of  ELECTORS (AmENDmENT)  ACT, No. 22  Of  2021 
Electoral District No. : 1 - Colombo
NOtICe is hereby given under subsection 20 a (10) of the Registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the 
Registration of electors (amendment) act, No. 22 of 2021 that the supplementary electoral register for the period from the 
first day of June 2021 to the thirty-first day of January 2022 in the above Electoral District has been certified and has been 
kept at the Colombo District Elections Office for inspection during office hours. 
 P.A.S. Senaratne,
 Assistant Registering Officer,
 electoral district No. : 1 – Colombo.
On 31st august 2022,
at the Colombo District Elections Office.
