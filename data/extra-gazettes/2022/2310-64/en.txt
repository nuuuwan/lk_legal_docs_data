WHEREAS the office of return member of Minuwangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of been ceased to be 
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Mathugama 
Arachchige Kalana Lakmina Mathugama the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Minuwangoda Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Suriya Pathirannahalage Upul Nilantha to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Minuwangoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Suriya 
Pathirannahalage Upul Nilantha was selected as the member of the Minuwangoda Urban Council.
