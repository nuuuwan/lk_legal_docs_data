PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the muthur  pradeshiya  sabha  
under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  (chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the secretary of the National Congress the recognized Political Party to which Yubunu Suhood 
Mohammadu Zihan the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sadik Sadath to fill such vacancy ;
And Therefore, I, Piumi Attygalla, Returning Officer of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sadik Sadath was selected as the 
member of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.  
 P
iumi AttygAllA,
 Returning Officer,
 Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
