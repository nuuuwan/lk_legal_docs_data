Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  ambanganga  Korale  
pradeshiya  sabha  under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  
ordinance  (chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of  Ambanganga Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not 
attending Council meetings for a period of three months without permission.
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Matiwala Thanne 
Gedara Bandula Wijekoon  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Ambanganga Korale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Nekath Gedara Nilmini Nirosha Wijesuriya to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Ambanganga Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nekath Gedara Nilmini 
Nirosha Wijesuriya was elected as the member of  the Ambanganga Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 P
ulasthi  Pathirana,
 Returning Officer,
 Ambanganga Korale Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Matale,
