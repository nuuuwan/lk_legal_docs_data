ADvERTISEmENT Of PETITION 
Winding -Up of The finance Company PLC alias The finance Company Limited (PQ 1) subject to the supervision 
of court in terms of the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 read with Sections 32 and 37(5) of the finance Business 
Act, No. 42 of 2011
Therefore, subject to the express provisions of Section 37(5) of the finance Business Act,
NOtice is hereby given that a Petition for the winding up of the above named company subject to the supervision of 
the commercial High court of colombo, was on the16 th day of February 2021 presented to the said court by Jayakodi 
arachchige don saman Jayanath Nanayakkara, then director, department of supervision of Non-bank Financial 
institutions, central bank of sri Lanka, colombo 1. and that the said Petition is directed to be heard before the court on 
the 08
th day of december 2022; and any creditor or contributory of the said company desirous to support or oppose the 
making of an order on the said petition may appear at the  time of  hearing  in person or by his counsel or proctor for that 
purpose; and a copy of the petition will be furnished to any creditor or contributory of the said company requiring the 
same by the undersigned on payment of the prescribed charge for the same.
