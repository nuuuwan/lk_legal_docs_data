 1. this permission may be cited as ''Permission to disburse under First supplementary financial statement - 2022 of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council" and come in to operation from 31.03.2022. 
 2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Seven Billion One Hundred 
and Eighty Nine Million Only (Rs. 37,189,000,000.00) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2022 
and ending 31st December 2022, which is specified as ''Financial Year 2022" in the Annual Financial Statement and 
Revenue - Expenditure Estimates prepared for the year 2022, that was approved on 29.11.2021, is hereby amended 
as, Rupees Thirty Eight Billion Five Hundred and Ninety Two Million One Hundred and Twenty Four Thousand and 
Seventy Two and Cents Seventy Four Only (Rs. 38,592,124,072.74) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of 
this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.

2022 
 1. This permission may be cited as ''Permission to disburse under First supplementary financial statement - 2022 of the 
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council" and come in to operation from 31.03.2022. 
 2. The limits of the advanced accounts No. 80401, 80701 and 80502 stated in second schedule of Revenue and Expenditure 
Estimates of the year 2021, which were amended by first, second, third and fourth Supplementary estimates for the 
year 2021, is hereby amended as first schedule of this notification. 
Authorization - Advanced Accounts
I, do hereby approve to amend the limits of the Advanced Accounts for the year 2021 that stated in the 7th Schedule of the 
First Supplementary Financial Statement for 2022, by virtue of Powers in me under Section 27(A) of Provincial Council Act 
No. 42 of 1987 as the term of the 6th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka 
ended on 26th September, 2017. 
Accordingly, I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and other accounting officers of the Sabaragamuwa  Province 
to disburse necessary expenditure from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund subject to those limits. 
 
tikiri kobbekaduwa,
 hon. governor,
 sabaragamuwa Provincial council.
31st March, 2022.
Authorization 
I, do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council 
to disburse as per the first Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2022, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987 , as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of 
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017. 
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Seven Billion One Hundred 
and Eighty Nine Million Only (Rs. 37,189,000,000.00) for the services of the period beginning on 01st January, 2022 and 
ending 31st December, 2022, which is specified in the Annual Financial Statement and Revenue Expenditure Estimates for 
the year 2022, is amended as Rupees Thirty Eight Billion Five Hundred and Ninety Two Million One Hundred and Twenty 
Four Thousand and Seventy Two and Cents Seventy Four Only (Rs. 38,592,124,072.74) and grant permission to disburse 
the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund. 
This permission will be valid till 31st of December, 2022 or upto the date that expires three months (03) from the date of 
summoning the Seventh (07th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council which occurs first.  
 
tikiri kobbekaduwa,
