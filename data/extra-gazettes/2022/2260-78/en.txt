EXCISE ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 01/2022
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25, read with section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended 
from time to time, I, Basil Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Excise Notification No. 986, published 
in the Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1947/42 of December 31, 2015 in order to exempt payment of Authorization Fee until 
March 31, 2022 to (i) 3 Stars and 4 Stars Classes Hotels and (ii) 5 Stars Hotel and above Stars Classes/Boutique Hotels: 
Boutique Villas/Heritage Hotels, specified in the PART II of the above Notification.  
Basil Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
january 01, 2022.
