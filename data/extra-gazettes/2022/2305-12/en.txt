NORTH WESTERN  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL 
Notiﬁcation of Hon. Governor of the North Western Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 277(1A) of the amended Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) to be 
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha 
Karannagoda Governor of the North Western Province do hereby appoint Mr. Ekanayake Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Lalith 
Ekanayake the retired Judicial Of ﬁcer, to inquire and report to me within a period of three months from the date of this 
notiﬁcation, whether the Mayor of Kurunegala Municipal Council has committed any offences referred to in Section 277 (1) 
of the amended Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252).
This ofﬁcer has the authority of a commission appointed under the Commission of Inquiry Act in relation to this inquiry.
A
DMIRAL OF THE FLEET WASANTHA KARANNAGODA,
Governor of the North Western Province.
09th of  November, 2022,
At Kurunegala.
