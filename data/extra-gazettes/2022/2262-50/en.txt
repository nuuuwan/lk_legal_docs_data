2A
 (3) Following paragraphs will be attached in place of Paragraphs "a", "b", "c" and "d" of Land development regulations 
2013 prepared on 03rd january 2041. i. e. -
 (a) "Peasant" means a person who is willing and is able to cultivate a land by himself or to have it cultivated by 
any member of his family, and who is in need of the land for the sustenance of himself and his family, of which 
the annual income not exceeds three Hundred thousand  rupees (rs. 3,00,000/=) even if engaged in any other 
economic activity related to agriculture.
 (b) "Educated youth" means a child of a peasant or a person belonging to the lower income group, who is unmarried, 
whose age is not less than 18 years and not more than 30 years and  who has been qualified up to General 
Certificate Examination - Ordinary Level.
 (c) "Lower income group person" means a person whose total income for the year immediately  preceding :
 (i) does not exceed Four Hundred and Eighty thousand rupees (rs. 480,000/=), if he or she is unmarried; 
or
 (ii) does not exceed six Hundred thousand rupees (rs. 600,000/=), if he or she is married.
 (d) "upper income group person" means a person whose total income for the year immediately  preceding :
 (i) Is more than Four Hundred and Eighty thousand rupees (rs. 480,000/=), but not exceeding six Hundred 
and sixty thousand rupees (rs. 660,000/=), if he or she is unmarried; or
 (ii) Is more than six Hundred thousand rupees (rs. 600,000/=), but not exceeding seven Hundred and twenty 
thousand rupees (rs. 720,000/=), if he or she is married;
* In this case the middle class persons are also categorized into lower income and upper income groups.
