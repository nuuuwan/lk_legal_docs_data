WHEREAS, the office of  Vice Chairman in Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman being 
appointed as Chairman ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Jayawickrama 
Kankanamge Darmarathna Jayawickrama, the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) 
of the aforesaid Ordinance to decide upon the member of that Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who 
shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days,
And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Velandagoda Pathiranage Ajith Lasantha to fill such vacancy ;
 Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Velandagoda Pathiranage Ajith Lasantha elected as Vice 
Chairman of the Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
 W. H. R. W
ijaya KumaRa,
