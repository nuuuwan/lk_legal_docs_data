WHEREAS, the office of  member of Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Nanayakkara 
Agarage Nimal Dias the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the  member of  that Number 04 Muwangala of  Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is 
not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kudadurayalage Nishantha Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Namaloya Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kudadurayalage Nishantha Kumara was elected as the member of the Number 04 Muwangala of Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha.
