PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Government Elections Ordinance
Notice under the Section 66C to be Read with Section 66G of the Amended Local Government Elections Ordinance 
of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Ordinance No. 22 of 2012
I, M.M. Wijenayaka, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with the powers 
vested in me under Section 66 C to be read with Section 66G of the Amended Local Government Elections Ordinance of 
the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Ordinance No. 22 of 2012, that meeting to elect a new Chairman to fill the 
vacant post of Chairman of Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha in Uva Province, will be convened on the 08th of December 2022 
at 10.30 am at the Council Chamber, Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha, Soranathota.
   
 M. M. W
ijenayaka,
 Commissioner of Local Government,
 Uva Province. 
At the Department of Local Government located at,
Martin Silva Mawatha, 
Badulla,
28th November, 2022.
