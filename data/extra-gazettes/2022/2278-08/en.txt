THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Divisional Sec's Ref. No. : ELDS/LND/04/ACQ/2014-08.         
Ministry of Land Ref. No. : 4-3/8/2016/HA/283.
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land/Servitude to be Acquired :
 An alloment of land called Galkoratuwatta, Kovilawatta 
alias Magurugoda Mukalana (high jungle) in extent of 0.0471 
hectare, situated in the village called Magurugoda of Grama 
Niladhari Division No.183B of Magurugoda in Nawadun 
Korale Palle Paththu Minor Division of the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Elapatha in Rathnapura District of 
the Sabaragamuwa Province.
 Plan No.: F.V .P. No. 88, Supplement No. 2, Sheet No. 4
Extent: 0.0471 hectare
 01. Lot No. 27
 North, East, South and West: Lot No. 2; Claimant: 
Nekathkiyana Hamilage Piyasena and some others, Magurugoda, 
Rathnahangamuwa, Rathnapura; Extent: 0.0471 Hectare.
 All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Elapatha Divisional 
Secretariat on 25.07.2022 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 
18.07.2022 the nature of their interest in the land/lands, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition 
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and 
the details of the computation of such amount. All deeds and 
documents in support of the claims should be produced before 
me at the inquiry. 
 S.A. Dංඅඋඎ඄,       
 Divisional Secretary. 
 
Elapatha Divisional Secretariat,
