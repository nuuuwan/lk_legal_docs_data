REvISION  Of   ELEcTORAL   REGISTERS  - 2022
iN pursuance of  section 12(1) of the registration of electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of 
Electors (Amendment) Act,No. 22 of 2021, it is hereby notified that the revision of registers of electors for the year 2022 has 
commenced, on 01st February 2022. 
02. Copies of registers of electors 2021 now in force or portions thereof will be exhibited by Registering Officers 
in their offices and following offices. 
Offices where Copies of Registers or Portions thereof 
will be exhibited
Registers or Portions thereof that will be exhibited
Each District Election Office Complete copy of the Electoral Register relating to the 
Electoral District
Each Grama Niladhari Office Portion of the Electoral Register containing names of all 
electors residing in the Grama Niladhari Division
 
 03. Thus, every person who is eligible to be an elector in a particular electoral district can verify his name from the 
electoral register displayed in the above places.
04. Due to the Covid epidemic, distribution of enumeration forms to every household and their re-collection will 
not be done this year.

05. If your name is registered in the Electoral Register 2021, action will be taken to register for the year 2022 under 
the address where you are registered in the 2021 electoral register. Note that you do not need to complete any form for that. 
If your name is not registered in the year 2021 or you are registering as an elector for the first time on attaining the age of 
18 or you need to change the registered address in the year 2021 then complete the 2022/er form and submit to the Grama 
Niladhari to register as an elector in the year 2022. Further details are given in the 2022/ER form itself.
06. inform your Grama Niladhari when an elector registered in the year 2021 is not eligible for registration in the 
year 2022 due to death or change of residence or any other reason.
07. information required to prepare supplementary electoral registers will be collected as soon as 18 years of age 
are completed. Accordingly, the details of those who have completed 18 years of age during the period from 01st February, 
2022 to 31st January, 2024 can also be given to the Grama Niladhari.
08. Grama Niladharis have been informed to give notice to every household on how to enumerate electors in the 
year 2022. If you have not received this notice before 28th February 2022, inform your Grama Niladhari.
