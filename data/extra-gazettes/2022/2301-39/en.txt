WHEREAS, the office of  Member of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending council 
meetings for a period of three months without permissions ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Subramaniyam 
Sivabalan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of  that ward – 17 Urumpirai South of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a  member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kumarasamy Kuneswaran to fill such vacancy ;
I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kumarasamy Kuneswaran was 
elected as the member of the ward 17 – Urumpirai South of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
