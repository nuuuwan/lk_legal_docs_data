PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Notice made under Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the 
powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended 
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of 
selecting a New Chairman for the vacated Chairman's position of Katana Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province, will be 
convened on March, 18 of 2022 at 10.00 a. m. at the Pradeshiya Sabha Chamber of Katana Pradeshiya Sabha located at 
Demanhandiya.
A. D. P. I. PrAsAnnA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,

Battaramulla,
