Filling of a Vacancy in the Office of Member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha  
under Section 66A(1)of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)   
whereas, the office of return member of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
and whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Mohamed 
Thawfeek Mohamed Irshan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the returned member of  Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
and whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Mohomad Husen Mohamad Nasar to fill such vacancy;
THEREfORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohomad 
Husen Mohamad Nasar was selected as the member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 K. J. S. M
adhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha. 
22nd of february 2022,
District Elections Office,
Gampaha.
