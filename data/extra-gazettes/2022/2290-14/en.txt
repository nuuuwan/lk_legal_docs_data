NATIONAL   MEDICINES   REGuLATORY   AuThORITY   ACT,  No.  05  Of  2015
Appointment of Authorized Officers
In terms of section 124 (1) of the National Medicines Regulatory authority act, No. 05 of 2015, I, dr. Keheliya Rambukwella, 
Minister of Health hereby appoint the following categories of officers as Authorized officers with effect from 01st of July 
2022 for the purpose of the said act. :-
   In case of new recruits, the date of appointment of the officer concerned shall be considered as the date of appointment 
as authorized officer under the said Act.
 1. any Provincial director of Health services 
 2. any Regional director of Health services
 3. Any Medical Officer of Health
 4. any divisional Pharmacist
 5. any Food and drugs Inspector
 6. any drugs Inspector
 7. any Pharmacist attached to the National Medicinal Regulatory authority
The officers are assigned to their respective designated areas. This appointment includes the covering up appointments as well.
 
 dr. 
Keheliya R ambuKwella, 
 Minister of Health.
