I, Nupe Hewage Roy Nishantha, the Commissioner of Local Government of North Central Province do hereby notified that the meeting for 
the purpose of the electing the Chairman for the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha of Polonnaruwa District in North Central Province, will

the Provisions of Section 66(c) that should be read with Section 66(G) of Local Government Election (amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, 
as amended by Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262).
