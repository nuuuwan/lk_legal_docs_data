1. i do hereby grant permission unto the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Province 
to spend rupees thirty seven billion one hundred and eighty Nine million only, (rs. 37,189,000,000) 
and which estimated for the services of the sabaragamuwa Province Provincial council for the one year 
duration beginning on 01st January, 2022 and ending on 31st december, 2022, by virtue of powers vested in 
me under section 27(A) of Provincial council Act, No. 42 of 1987 as the term of the sixth sabaragamuwa 
Province Provincial council ended on 26th september, 2017.
2. (1) without prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure of the sabaragamuwa 
Province Provincial council which estimate will be rupees thirty seven billion one 
hundred and eighty Nine million only, (rs. 37,189,000,000) for the service of the period 
beginning on 01st January 2022 and ending on 31st december 2022, in this order referred 
to as the “one year duration” shall be met.
 (a) for payments which are hereby authorized to be made out of the sabaragamuwa 
Province Provincial council fund or monies of or advance at the disposal of the 
sabaragamuwa Province Provincial council and,
 (b)  for the proceeds of loans which are hereby authorized to be raised, within the province 
of sabaragamuwa for and on behalf of the sabaragamuwa Province Provincial council. 
the sum of rupees thirty seven billion one hundred and eighty Nine million only, 
(Rs. 37,189,000,000) here in before referred to may be duly expended as specified in 
the first schedule to this order.
 (2) The provision of subsection (1) of this section shall have effect without prejudice to the 
provisions of any other written law authorizing the raising of loans for and on behalf of 
the sabaragamuwa Provincial council.
3. (1) The receipts of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council during the financial year, 
2022 from each activity specified in column (iv) of the Second Schedule to this order shall 
be credited to the account of such activity, the aggregate of receipts so credited shall be 
not less than the minimum limit specified in the corresponding entry in column (vi) of that 
schedule.
4.  t he expenditure incurred by the sabaragamuwa Province Provincial c ouncil, during the 
financial year 2022 each activity specified in Column (iv) of the Second Schedule to this 
order shall be paid out of the receipt of the sabaragamuwa Province Provincial council from 
such expenditure shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry in 
column (v) of that schedule.
5.  The debit balance, outstanding at the end of financial year 2022 of any activity specified in 
column (iv) of the Second Schedule to this order shall not exceed the maximum limit specified 
in the corresponding entry in column (vii) of that schedule, and the total liabilities of that 
activity at the end of that financial year shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in the 
corresponding entry column (viii) of that schedule. 
6.  Whenever at any time during the financial year 2022, the receipts of that Sabaragamuwa 
Province Provincial Council from any activity specified in Column (iv) of the Second Schedule 
to this order are insufficient to meet the expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa Province 
Provincial council on such activity, the minister may, from time to time, by order direct that 
such sums may deem necessary to meet such expenditure shall be payable by way of advances 
out of sabaragamuwa Province Provincial council fund or any other fund or monies of or at the 
disposal of, the sabaragamuwa Provincial council, so however that the aggregate of the sums 
so advances does not exceed the maximum limit of entry in column (v) of that schedule. Any 
sums so advanced in respect of such activity shall be refunded to the sabaragamuwa Province 
Provincial council fund in such manner as the Governor may by order direct.
