FILLING   OF   A   V ACANCY   UNDER   PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE 
CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS, the Secretary Gen eral of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under Section 64(1) of the 
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Ninth Parliament by reason 
of death of Mr. Amarakeerthi Athukorala, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District  
No. 18 – Polonnaruwa under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act; 
 And whereas, the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by the 
Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, to fill this vacancy as provided 
for under Sub Article 99(13)(B)  of the Constitution; 
 And whereas, the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 
1981, and in terms of Sub Article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution has made a return in Form “M” set out in the First Schedule 
to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr.  S.  Jagath Samarawickrama was declared elected as a Member of Parliament 
for the said electoral district;
Now therefore, the Election Commission, in the exercise of the powers vested in the Election Commission by Section
