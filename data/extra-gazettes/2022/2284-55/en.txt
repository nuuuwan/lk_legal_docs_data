WHEREAS, the office of Chairman in Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Narangoda 
Ralalage Manjula Priyashantha Nawarathna the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of 
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who 
shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to make a nomination within the 
above mentioned time period specified;
Therefore I, Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe Returning Officer of   
Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 66B (3) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 
262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Hewayalage Saman 
Kulasooriya who had obtained the highest percentage of votes No. 16 - Amithirigala ward contested at the election held on 
10th February, 2018 to Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha elected as Chairman of the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
