Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  bentota  Pradeshiya  sabha  
under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  (chaPter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of  Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group 2,  to which Lanka Wirinduge Rathnapala the member belonged, 
was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the returned member of Bentota 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Metiwalage 
Jagath Niroshan Munasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Metiwalage 
Jagath Niroshan Munasinghe was selected as the member of  the Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 W. H. R. W
ijaya KumaRa,
