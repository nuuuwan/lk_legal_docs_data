3. the charges for the use of aerodromes at the bandaranaike international airport (bia), Mattala rajapaksa international 
airport (Mria) and ratmalana airport (rMa) regulations, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1869/33 dated 
July 02, 2014 as last amended by regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2192/21 dated september 08, 
2020 is hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything previously done thereunder.
4. in these regulations, unless the context otherwise requires –
 “aerodrome” shall have the same meaning as is assigned to it under the civil aviation act, No. 14 of 2010 ;
 
 “break–off on Final Approach” means after an aircraft approaches to land at the airport discontinuing the flight without 
landing ;
 “Circuit and Landing” means aircraft flying a predetermined polygonal path around the airport and landing at the airport 
(this procedure is repeated several times for training);
 “director General” shall have the same meaning as is assigned to it under the civil aviation act, No. 14 of 2010 ;
 “instrument approach” means aircraft using ground and aircraft instruments' guidance to land at an aerodrome ;
 “New scheduled international airline” means an airline authorized to operate scheduled international air services. the 
waiver under regulation 2 shall not automatically be applicable to existing scheduled international airlines ;
 “Operator of the aerodrome” shall have the same meaning as is assigned to it under the civil aviation act, No. 14 of 
2010 ;
 “statutory serviec Provider” means the statutory service Provider appointed under section 6 (3)(a) of the civil aviation 
act, No. 14 of 2010 ; and
“Touch and Go” means aircraft flying circuit and landing touching down on the runway and continuing to take off 
without making a full stop landing.
