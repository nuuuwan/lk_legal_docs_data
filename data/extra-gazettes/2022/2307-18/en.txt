TOWN  AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 269)
Order under Section 6(2) (b) and Section 21 (2) (b)
BY Virtue, of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Prasanna Ranatunga, Minister of Urban Development and Housing, do by this Order-
 ( a) declare that the area of land called “Kotahena Sri Paramananda Purana Maha Viharaya” speci ﬁ ed in the Schedule 
hereto shall be and Urban Development area for the purposes of the aforesaid Ordinance; and 
 ( b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Kotahena Sri Paramananda Purana Maha Viharaya” 
which is declared as above to be an Urban Development Area.
PRASANNA RANATUNGA,
                                                                                                  Minister of Urban Development and Housing.
At Colombo.
