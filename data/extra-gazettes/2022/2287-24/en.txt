2a
regulatioNS
the shipping agents (licensing) regulations 1991, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 684/9 of October 17, 1991, 
as amended by the regulations published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 763/8 of april 21, 1993 is hereby further amended 
in sub-paragraph (b)(ii) of paragraph (1) of regulation 5 thereof, by the substitution for the words “by citizens of sri lanka.”, 
of the following: -
“by citizens of sri lanka:
          Provided that, no public or private company shall be eligible for a licence under this sub-paragraph, where –
(a) the shares are owned by a citizen of a foreign country or,  public or private company incorporated or 
registered  in a foreign country; and 
(b) such foreign country prohibits by the law of that country the citizens of sri lanka  to own the shares 
of a public or private company incorporated or registered in that country:
Provided however, that the Minister may, upon the consideration of national interest and exceptional circumstances, 
waive this requirement.”.
