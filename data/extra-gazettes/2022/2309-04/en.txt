mINuTES  ON  PENSIONS
Notice under Section 51 of the minutes on Pensions dated 05th February 1934 as amended by the minutes on Pensions 
act, No. 13 of 1948 (amendment).
Notice
the minutes on Pensions, which is amended from time to time is further amended by removing Section 17 entirely 
and substituting the following new Section.
17. “any Government employee may be directed to retire from Government Service on or after attaining the age 
of fifty-five years. All civil officers must retire compulsorily on attaining the age of sixty, except those officers whose age of 
compulsory retirement is fixed by the Constitution or by any other law, unless otherwise decided by a competent authority.”.
this order shall come into effect from January 01, 2023.
