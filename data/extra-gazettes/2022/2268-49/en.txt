Filling of a Vacancy in the Office of Member of the Valvettithurai Urban Council Under 
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Odinance (Chapter 262)   
whereas, the office of member of Valvettithurai Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
AnD WhereAs the secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which 
Ponnambalam sivagnanasundaram the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that ward 05 - Valvettythurai of Valvettithurai Urban Council who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AnD WhereAs the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Makalingam Mayooran to fill such vacancy;
TherefOre, I, rajendram Christy Amalraaj, returning Officer of the Valvettithurai Urban Council, acting in 
terms of section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Makalingam 
Mayooran was elected as the member of the ward 05 - Valvettythurai of  Valvettithurai Urban Council.
