WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Eelam People's Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which Arumugam 
Sivanesan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kanagasabai Devendran to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Kandiah Vimalaruban, Returning Officer of the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kanagasabai Devendran 
was selected as the member of  the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. Vimalaruban,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
