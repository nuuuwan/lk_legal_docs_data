WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Peliyagoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party  the recognized political party to which Kalahe 
Kankanamlage Nandadewa Hemapriya the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Peliyagoda Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ganga Dilrukshi Disanayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Peliyagoda Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ganga 
Dilrukshi Disanayaka was selected as the member of the Peliyagoda Urban Council.
