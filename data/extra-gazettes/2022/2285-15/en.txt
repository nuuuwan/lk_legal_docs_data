ThE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
By virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 3 and 4 of the societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, ranil Wickremesinghe 
Minister of Ministry of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policy do by this notification.
 01. Authorize the purpose for which the society known as “samastha Lanka sancharaka sewa sapayannange sangamaya” 
situated No. 210, Council road, dehiwala as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought 
to be extended ; and
 02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of section  5(4) of 
that Ordinance.
