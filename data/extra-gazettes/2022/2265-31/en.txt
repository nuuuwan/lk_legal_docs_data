Whereas the office of member of  Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Jasinge Chandralatha 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned member 
of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections 
ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kariyawasam Katukoliha Gamage Sumethra to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Katuwana  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kariyawasam Katukoliha Gamage Sumethra was selected as the member of the  Katuwana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
