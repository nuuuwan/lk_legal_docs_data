Amendment to the Service Minute of the Teacher Advisors' Service - Amendment No. 01
the service Minute of the teacher advisors' service published in Gazette ExtraOrdinary No. 2182/36 of 01.07.2020 of 
the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka is amended as follows. other matters and conditions therein will remain 
unchanged.
 by order of the Public service commission,
M. A. B. DAyA SenArAth,
        secretary,   
Public service commission.
27th day of september, 2022.
14.4 (a) All officers in Service as at 02.01.2022 and who have given their consent prior to that date to be absorbed to the 
teacher advisors' service, will be absorbed to sri Lanka teacher advisors' service with effect from 02.01.2022 in 
terms of the provisions under section 14.1 of this service Minute.
(b) The absorption made from 01.07.2020 of the aforesaid officers, who will be absorbed with effect from 02.01.2022
in terms of sub-section (a) above, shall be cancelled.
(c) However, upon a request from an officer absorbed to the service from 01.07.2020, the Secretary to the Ministry
may consider 01.07.2020 as the absorption date of the respective officer.
