FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  BATTICALOA   MUNICIPAL 
COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  66A(1) (a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE 
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the of ﬁ ce of member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Iyathurai
Sritharan the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward No. 02 Thiraimadu of Batticaloa Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualiﬁ ed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Munusamy Vasantharaj to ﬁ ll such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Ofﬁ cer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Munusamy
Vasantharaj was elected as the member of the Ward No. 02 Thiraimadu of Batticaloa Municipal Council.
M. B. M. SUFYAN,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Batticaloa Municipal Council. 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Batticaloa,
31st May, 2022.
