Order
 The Chairman of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha of the North Central Province, Herath Hamige Senarathna 
is considered to have resigned from the post with effect from 31.12.2021 and the above post is considered to be vacant since 
that date due to failure to pass the budget prepared by the Chairman of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha for the year 
2022, in terms of Section 168 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 
15 of 1987 as amended by Section 17 of Local Government (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and non-compliance 
with the said Section No. 169.

NORTh CENTRAL PROVINCE
i, Maheepala Herath, Governor of the North Central Province, hereby announce the following orders by virtue of powers vested 
in me under the Pradeshiya Sabha, Act, No. 15 of 1987, to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential 
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
 M
aheepala herath,
 Governor of the North Central Province.
Governor Office of the North Central Province,
Provincial Council Complex,
dharmapala Mawatha, 
Anuradhapura.
21st day of january 2022.
Order
   Since the post of Chairman of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha situated within the district of Anuradhapura of 
the North Central Province remains vacant due to failure to pass the budget prepared for the year 2022, in terms of Section 
168 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and the Section 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended 
by Section 17 of Local Government Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, i hereby inform that the powers, 
performance, and functions related to this post is vested to the deputy Chairman in terms of Section 8 (4) of the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Act from the date of the vacancy and until the date of the vacancy is to be filled as per Local Government Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
 
  further,i hereby direct the deputy Chairman of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha to bear the essential expenses 
related to special projects currently in progress and the recurrent expenditure of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha for the 
year 2022, with the approval of Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Anuradhapura Administrative district, prepared 
by the Secretary of the Medawachchiya Pradeshiya Sabha to ensure the continuation of the activities of the Medawachchiya 
Pradeshiya Sabha for a period of time until the new Chairman presents the budget for the financial year 2022 to the Sabha 
and approves it properly.
