FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
THe Order under section 4(2) that has to be with section 64(1)of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as 
amended by Acts, No. 13 of 1966(Reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 
2009.  
Order
   By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 4 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by 
Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995, and No. 65 of 2009, I, Wimalaweera 
dissanayake,Minister of Wildlife & Forest Resources Conservation do by the release  27.3684 ha. of Rajawaka Reserved 
Forest located within below-mentioned schedule 1(0.5716ha.), schedule  2 (19.3426ha), schedule 3(4.1712 ha.), schedule  
4 (2.8381 ha.) and schedule  5(0.4449ha.) was declared as a Reserved Forest by the Gazette Extraordinary Notification 
No.1577/5 dated 25th November 2008, from the reserved forest state from 27th day of  April, 2022.
 W
imalaWeera Dissanayake,
 Minister of Wildlife & Forest Resources Conservation.
Ministry of   Wildlife & Forest Resources Conservation,
Rajagiriya,
27th day of April 2022.
