EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Orders issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance by virtue of the power 
vested in him by the Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, and published as the Excise Notifications, 
were passed by Parliament as follows.
 
 (i)  No. 10/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2249/50 dated October 16, 2021 by amending the 
Excise Notification No. 07/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2216/16 dated February 25, 2021 
as last amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2241/3 dated August 16, 2021 was passed by Parliament on  
April 20, 2022.
 (ii)  No. 01/2022 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2260/78 dated January 01, 2022 by amending the Excise 
Notification No. 986 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1947/42 dated December 31, 2015 was passed 
by Parliament on April 20, 2022.
 (iii)  No. 02/2022 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2260/79 dated January 01, 2022 by amending the Excise 
Notification No. 1004 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2049/4 dated December 11, 2017 was passed 
by Parliament on April 20, 2022.
