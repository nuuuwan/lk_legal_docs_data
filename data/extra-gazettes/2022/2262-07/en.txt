THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                                 My No.: HRP/LND/ACQSN/12/15.
                        Land Ministry No.: 4-3/9/2018/IR/348-D.
I hereby notify under Sub-section (1) of the Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendments
Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands/servitude which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Boundaries to the land lots of 1.2049 Hectares with below mentioned
names and extents shown in Cadastral Map dated 2020.08.25
prepared for alloments of lands 740 in pages 107 of the list of land
description of sheet No. 02, zone No. 02, No. 1566 of Survey General
situated in the village of Mawathawewa in the Grama Niladhari's
Division of the No. 146 Maradanmaduwa in the Minor Division of
Hurulu Palatha Central of the Divisional Secretariat of Horowpothana
in the District Anuradhapura in the North Central Province.
Lot No. 740
Name of the land: Pullearemoragoda, Mawathawewa Mookalana;
Description: Chena; Claimant: State, Unauthorized cultivation has
been done by Nandasenage Athula Somasiri resident in
Mawathawewa, Kapugollawa. Need for Yan Oya Reservoir; Extent:
1.2049 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a servitude
to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Horowpothana on each of the below mentioned dates and times.
Date Time Lot No.
2022.02.07 9.30 am 740
Further, hereby it is requested to notify me in writing in duplicate on
or before the 2022.02.07, the nature of their rights in the land
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
M. K. G. MORAWAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Horowpothana.
On 09th January 2022,
At the Horowpothana Divisional Secretariat.
