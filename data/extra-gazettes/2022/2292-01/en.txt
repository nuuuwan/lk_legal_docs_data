The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
DGC A877/PL 305.
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, P. B. S. C. Nonis,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 08th August,  2022, all  duties of Customs as
well as other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The Notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette No. 2291/01 of  01st August 2022 is  hereby
rescinded.
P. B. S. C. Nonis,
 Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
05th August, 2022.
