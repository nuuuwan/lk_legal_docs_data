I, Senaka Palliyaguruge, Commissioner of Local Government, Department of Local Government, Southern Province, hereby 
give notice, under Section 66G of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance. As amended by the Local Authorities Election 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, that Mr. Kaburugamuwa Gamarachchige Gamini Sri Ananda was elected as the Mayor 
of Hambantota Municipal Council at the meeting held in the Assembly Hall of Hambantota Municipal Council at 10.00 a. 
m. on 23.03.2022.
