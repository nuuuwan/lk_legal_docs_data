Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance the recognized political party to which 
Abdul Rahaman Mohamed Nawasdeen the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sundaram Mahendran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sundaram 
Mahendran was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
