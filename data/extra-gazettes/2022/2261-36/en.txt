Whereas the office of member of  Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member 
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Weligama 
Arachchige Saliya Shanthilal Gunasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Guruge Madhawa Dilanka Perera to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Bandaragama Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Guruge Madhawa Dilanka Perera was selected as the member of the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
