THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of 
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial 
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 30.03.2021 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2224/32 dated 23.04.2021 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter speciﬁ ed 
in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour dated 25.03.2021.
     M r .  W .  A .  Wijedasa, ''Nilmini Niwasa", Nagolla, Kuttiyagolla, Badulla.
 and
      Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05.
was referred to Mr. Panwatte Gedara Sarath Premalal for settlement by arbitration. 
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Nimal Siripala De Silva, Minister of Labour do 
hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 30.03.2021.

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. W. A. Wijedasa of the Sri Lanka Transport Board who had  been retired on 05.10.2013 at the completion of 57 
years of age and reinstated in service with eﬀ ect from 24.04.2015 had been caused injustice by the non-payment of salaries 
for the period from 05.10.2013 to 24.04.2015 and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
