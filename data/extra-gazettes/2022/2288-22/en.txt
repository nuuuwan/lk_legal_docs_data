THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  40)
Curfew Order under Section 16
WHEREAS, I consider that it is necessary to maintain public order in the areas speci ﬁed in the Schedule hereto, I, Ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Prime Minister appointed to act in terms of Article 37(1) of the Constitution, by virtue of the powers 
vested in me under Section 16 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40) do by this Order direct that no person shall 
be on any public road, railway, public park, public recreation ground or other public ground or the seashore in such areas, 
from 12.00 hour of July 14th, 2022 to 05.00 hour of July15th, 2022, except under the authority of a written permit granted 
by Secretary, Ministry of Defence or Secretary, Ministry of Public Security or Inspector-General of Police or any of ﬁcer 
authorized by them.
R
ANIL WICKREMESINGHE,
Prime Minister,
Appointed to act in terms of Article 37(1) of the Constitution.
Colombo, 
July14th, 2022.
