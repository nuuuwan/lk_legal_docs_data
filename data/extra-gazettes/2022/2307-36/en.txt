FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  V A VUNIYA  SOUTH (TAMIL)  
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the of ﬁ ce of  return member of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of 
Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Rasaiya
Suventhira the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed unde r 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rahunathan Juliyat to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rahunathan Juliyat was 
selected as the member of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. JENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE HANGURANKETHA  
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262) 
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return member of Hanguranketha  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Gorandiye Gedara 
Hemantha Pradeep Kusumsiri  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of  Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Paththini Hannedige Amith Abeyrathna to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, Returning Officer of the Hanguranketha  
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Paththini Hannedige Amith Abeyrathna was selected as the member of the Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha.
