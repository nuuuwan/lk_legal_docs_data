ThE CONSuLAR fuNCTIONS ACT, No. 4 Of 1981
Notification under Section 8
I, mohamed Uvais mohamed ali sabry, minister of Foreign affairs, in terms of the powers vested in me under section 8 of 
the Consular Functions Act, No. 4 of 1981, do by this Notification specify that, with effect from January 01, 2023-
 (a) the Fees set out in Column II of Schedule I hereto shall be levied in respect of the Consular Functions specified in 
Column I of that schedule, which are performed by the Consular division of the ministry of Foreign affairs and its 
Regional Officers: and 
 (b) the Fees set out in Column II of Schedule II hereto shall be levied in respect of the Consular Functions specified in 
Column I of that schedule, which are performed by the sri Lanka missions or Posts abroad.
 The Notification specifying Fees in respect of the Consular Functions performed by the Consular Division of the Ministry 
of Foreign affairs published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2044/39 of November 09, 2017 and the Notification specifying 
fees in respect of the Consular Functions performed by the sri Lanka missions or Posts abroad published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2057/35 of February 09, 2018, are hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
