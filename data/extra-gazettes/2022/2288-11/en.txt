THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
               Land Ministry's Ref' No.: LD/05/2006/PC/239.  
Ref. No.: KMB/LAN/ACQUI/2/59. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following Land/
servitude which are  required for the public purposes. 
 
Description of the Land/servitude  to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of Land depicted in the Sheet No. 10, 
Supplement No. 10 of the Plan No. F.V.P. 182, situated in 
the Grama Niladhari Division No. 320-A of Gathara East 
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kamburupitiya 
in Matara District of the Southern Province and described 
as follows:  
 
01. Lot No. 97 
Name of Land: Ihalahirideniya alias 
Kadurugahadeniya; Description: Barren land; Claimant: 
Hewa Kodithuwakku Manamala Arachchige Somapala; 
Extent: 0.2725 Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 98 
Name of Land: -; Description: Stream; Claimant: 
Could not be traced; Extent: 0.0388 Hectare.  
03. Lot No. 99 
Name of Land: Ihalahirideniya alias 
Kadurugahadeniya; Description: Barren land, a bus stop 
has been constructed; Claimant: Hewa Kodithuwakku 
Manamala Arachchige Somapala; Extent: 0.0053 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Kamburupitiya 
Divisional Secretariat on 05th September 2022 at                
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 29th August, 2022 
the nature of their interest in the lands the particulars of 
their claims for compensatio n for the acquisition of the 
said lands the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.    
 K
USALAKA NANAYAKKARA, 
 Divisional Secretary,  
 Kamburupitiya. 
at the Divisional Secretariat, 
Kamburupitiya, 
06th July, 2022.
