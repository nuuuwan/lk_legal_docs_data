WHEREAS the office of  member of Kaduwela Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Raigama Arachchige 
Prema Ranjith Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ward No. 21 Jayawadanagama of Kaduwela Municipal Council who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as member within a period of Thirty (30) days;  ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kapurubandage Gayan Saminda to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of Kaduwela Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
Kapurubandage Gayan Saminda was elected as the member of the Ward No. 21 Jayawadanagama of Kaduwela Municipal 
Council.
