EXCISE ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 03/2022
ExcisE  duty  ON  BOttLEd  tOddy
By Virtue of the powers vested in me by sub-section (1) of section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (chapter 52) as amended from 
time to time, i, M. u. M. Ali sabry, Minister of finance do by this Order direct the following, with effect from May 04, 2022;
 (1) there shall be levied on bottled toddy manufactured in and issued from any licensed manufactory established in 
sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of rupees fifty (rs. 50/-) per litre;
 Provided that such duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid bottled toddy which is exported 
out of the island; and
  (2) Excise Notification No. 03/2021 Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2209/42 of January 07, 2021 is hereby 
rescinded. 
 M. U. M. Ali  SAbry,  PC
 Minister of finance.   
      
Ministry of finance,
colombo 01,
