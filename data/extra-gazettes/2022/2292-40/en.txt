WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Pillayankutty 
Thevanesarasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward No. 08 Kokadicholai of Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kanthaiya Raththinakumar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai South West Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Kanthaiya Raththinakumar was elected as the member of  the ward No. 08, Kokadicholai of the Manmunai South West 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. B. M. S
ufyan,
