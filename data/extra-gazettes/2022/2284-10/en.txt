Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No : 12/0312  Polonnaruwa District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels 
of lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 120151 made by the Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of 
Title Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to th e Village of Sudukanda Nikawewa (Block 04) or any part thereof, situated within 
the Grama Niladhari Division of No.46 Sudukanda Nikawewa in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Hingurakgoda in 
the District of Polonnaruwa in the Province of North Central should submit his claim to the undersigned before 6th of July 
2022. In the event of failure  to submit any claim before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte 
inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 120151 referred to above may be perused at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional 
Secretariat, District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari , the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement.
