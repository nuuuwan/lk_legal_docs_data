WHEREAS the office of member of Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a member 
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Minsar Mohomad the 
member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of 
that 10 -  Kasamaduwa of Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Wannihamige Thilakarathna Bandara  to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, returning officer of the, Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wannihamige Thilakarathna 
Bandara was elected as the member of the 10 -  Kasamaduwa of Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha.
