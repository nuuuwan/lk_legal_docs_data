PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF THE WESTERN PROVINCE
NOTIFICATION under Section 5 of the Cemeteries and Burial Grounds Ordinance to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial 
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989.
   Based on the recommendation of the Kaduwela Municipal Counci l, which is the competent authority under 
Section 03 of the Cemeteries and Burial Grounds Ordinance pertaining to the Kaduwela Municipal Council area, in terms of 
the powers vested in me as the Governor of the Provincial Council of the Western Province under Section 5 of the Aforesaid 
Ordinance to be read with Section 02 of the Provincial Councils  (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, I hereby 
order that the land described in the Schedule hereto be used as a public cemetery that has been allocated for the purpose of 
burial or cremation of dead bodies.
