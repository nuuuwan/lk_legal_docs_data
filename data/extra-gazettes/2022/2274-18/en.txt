WHEREAS the office of member of Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of being ceased to be a 
member of party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which M. S. Muhammadhu 
Jawfer the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that Ward No. 05 -Iyankeny Muslim of Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Aliyar Rahuma Bebe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, returning officer of the Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aliyar Rahuma Bebe 
was elected as the member of the Ward No. 05 -Iyankeny Muslim of Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
 M. B. M. Sufyan,
 Returning officer,
 Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
