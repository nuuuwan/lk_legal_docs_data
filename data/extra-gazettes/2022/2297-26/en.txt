THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                        My Ref. No. : BD/ELL/LND/3/AQUSN/32'
Reference No.  Ministry  0f Lands: 4-3/10/2019/MV/394'
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
the  Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act  No. 28  of 1964 that
the Goverment intends to acquire the following land /servitude
which is required for a public purpose :
Description of the Land /Servitude to be Acquired :
Allotment of land (01 Lot ) called " Galhinne Pathana'' in
total extent of 4.7344 Hectares described below and depicted
as Lot 1570 in the Final Village Plan No. 27, Supplementary
117, Sheet 116 dated 25.01.2022 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the village called Udukumbalwela in Grama
Niladhari Division No. 69B of Heeloya in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Ella in Badulla District of  the Uva
Province:
01. Lot No. 1570
Name of the Land :Galhinne Pathana; Extent : 4.7344
Hectares.
Boundaries:
North by : Lot No. 13CY; East By : Lot Nos. 13CY,
1571,580,579,577,576,574,566 and 1572; South by : Lot
Nos. 13CY , 1571,580,579,577,576,574,566 and 1572 and 1573(T.P.
385700) West by: Lot No. 1573 ((T.P .385700) and 13CY
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ servitude are
hereby required  to appear personally or by agents duly
authorized in writing before me at Ella Divisional Secretariat
on 23.11.2022 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in  duplicate) on or before 17.11.2022 the
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
