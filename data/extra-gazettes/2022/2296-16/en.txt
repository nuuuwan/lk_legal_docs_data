WHEREAS the office of  member of Lunugamvehera Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the Party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Hewa Batagodage Nimal 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of 
that 09 - Agbopura of Lunugamvehera Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Sumith Sudusinghe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, returning officer of the, Lunugamvehera Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sumith Sudusinghe 
was elected as the member of the 09 - Agbopura of Lunugamvehera Pradeshiya Sabha.

WHEREAS the office of member of Mannar Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending more than three 
consecutive ordinary meeting without permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Poornanantha 
Eswarasarma Ingarasarma the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that 01 -  Eluthoor of Mannar Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Nagalingam Ganesalingam to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kandaiya Vimalaruban, returning officer of the, Mannar Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nagalingam Ganesalingam was elected 
as the member of the 01 - Eluthoor of Mannar Urban Council.
