Whereas, the office of the return member of Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized Political Party to which Chandrasekarage 
Chandrasena the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate Pannala 
Gedara Kusumavathi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, acting in 
terms of section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Pannala Gedara 
Kusumavathi was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P.	A.	S.	Senaratne,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
