PILGRImAGES ORDINANCE
37th Ordination ceremony (“Upasampada Ceremony”) of Sri Rohana maha Sanga Sabha of “Syamopaliwanshika 
maha Nikaya”
iN terms of the regulations mentioned in the section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (chapter 175), No. L. d. B. 11/60 dated 
14th October 1991, it has been published that the 37th Ordination ceremony (“Upasampada ceremony”) of Sri rohana maha 
Sanga Sabha of Kamburupitiya divisional Secretariat, matara district will be held from  07th, 08th and 09th of may 2022 at 
the “Seema malaka” of rohana Sanga Sabha headquarters of Urapola Siri rathanajothi Piriven Viharaya and the devotees 
are requested not to bring plastic and polythene based products. And also it is hereby informed that the standing orders and 
regulations published in the Govenrment Gazette of democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka shall be valid within this 
time period of the Ordination ceremony (“Upasampada ceremony”).
