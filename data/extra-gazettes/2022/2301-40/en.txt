 2. by  virtue of the powers vested in me by section 5 of the special commodity Levy act No. 48 of 2007, do hereby 
make an order to waive-off balance amount after collecting rs. 6 per kg from the applicable special commodity Levy 
of 10% or rs. 400 per kg on importation of Mackerel (scomber scombrus, scomber australasicus, scomber japanicus) 
classified under the HS Code of 0303.54 and Jack and horse mackerel (Trachurus spp.) classified under the HS Code 
of 0303.55 listed out in the schedule of the above, classified under HS Heading of 03.03 for production of canned fish 
by local canned fish manufactures on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry in charge of subject of Fisheries.
 3. t he Order made under section 2 of the special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2269/56 of March 03, 2022 in respect of Mackerel (Scomber Scombrus, Scomber australasicus, 
Scomber Japanicus) classified under the HS Code of 0303.54 and Jack and horse mackerel (Trachurus spp.) classified 
under the hs code of 0303.55 are rescinded with effect from October 14, 2022.
 4. this Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from October 14, 2022 subject to provisions of sub 
section (3) of section 2 of the special commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
