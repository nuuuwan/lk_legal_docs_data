Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Benadict 
Yakkoppillei the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the 
Local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Peter Annekenat Sulakshana to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Kandiah Vimalaruban, Returning Officer of the Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Peter Annekenat Sulakshana was 
elected as the member of the Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K. V
imalaruban,
 Returning Officer,
 Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Mannar,
04th November, 2022.
