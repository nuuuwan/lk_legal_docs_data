SOUThERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification of Hon. Governor of Southern Province
The order made vide Article 184(1) of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that should 
be read with Clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
uPoN being convinced that there is adequate evidence to prove that Gange Sarath Sri Lal de Silva, Councilor of Ambalangoda 
urban Council have caused misdemeanours cited in Article 184(1) of urban Council ordinance;
As per the final report dated 10.02.2022 submitted to me by the One Man commission appointed by me vide Extra 
Ordinary Gazette Notification No. 2260/47 dated 29.12.2021 to investigated the alleged charges against Gange Sarath Sri Lal 
De Silva, Councilor of Ambalangoda Urban Council and whether he had caused any misdemeanours cited in Article 184(1) 
of Urban Council Ordinance and to report me within a period of 03 months on consideration of documentary evidence and 
written submissions of the Plaintiff and the respondent;
I, Dr. Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province do hereby expel Gange Sarath Sri Lal De 
Silva, Councilor of Ambalangoda urban Council from the position of Council member with effect from 11.02.2022, under 
and by virtue of powers vested on me vide Article 184(1)(1a) of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that 
should be read with Clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Further I order Southern Province Commissioner of Local Government and Secretary of Ambalangoda Urban Council to 
intiate actions according to Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by various other Acts.
