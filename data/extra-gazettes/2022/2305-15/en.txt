WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason 
of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which 
Tharmarethinam Suthaharan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of  Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kanthasamy Jeishankar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kanthasamy Jeishankar was selected as the member of the Manmunai South and Eruvil Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
