THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: Thi DS/1/8/3/55 Ath.
Tourism  &  Land Ministry Ref.No.: 4-3/11/2015/UDA/227.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following lands which are required for
the public purpose.
Description of the Land :
Acquisition of the land bearing Asst. No. 181 (part), Aramaya
Place to Urban Development Authority.
An allotment of land depicted as Lot No. 1 an extent of
0.0600 Hectares in Preliminary Plan No. Co.9859 prepared by
Surveyor General situated at Dematagoda village Division No.
29 Grama Niladhari Division of Dematagoda Colombo
Municipal Council limits within the limits of Thimbirigasyaya
Divisional Secretariat area in the Colombo District, Western
Province.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Asst. No. 181 (part) Aramaya Place;
Land Use:Garden and a part of Housing Complex;
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0600 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Thimbirigasyaya on the 30th November, 2022 at 10.00 a.m and
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (with duplicates)
on or before 23.11. 2022, the nature of their interest in the land,
the particular of their claimed for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
P
RIYANTHA DISSANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
     Thimbirigasyaya.
Divisional Secretariat at Thimbirigasyaya,
18th October, 2022.
