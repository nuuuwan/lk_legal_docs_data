Appointment of Commissioner-General of Essential Services
By virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph (1) of Regulation 8 of the emergency (miscellaneous Provisions and 
Powers) Regulations, No. 1 of 2022, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President, do hereby appoint mr. K. d. s. Ruwanchandra, a 
Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service to be the Commissioner-General of Essential Services for the 
whole of Sri Lanka to execute and coordinate all activities relating to the supply and maintenance of essential services with 
immediate effect.
Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
 President.
