ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) read with section 21 and section 139 of the Universities Act, No. 16 
of 1978, I, dinesh Gunawardena, Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by 
this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraodinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 (as amended 
from time to time) which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka, which 
is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of 
the said Act, by the addition immediately after the item “Faculty of dental Sciences” appearing in Column 1 of the Schedule 
and its corresponding entries in Column 11 of the Schedule, (specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2257/28 of december, 8, 2021) of the following :
 “Column I   Column II
 Faculty   Department
 Faculty of Urban and Aquatic bioresources 1. department of Urban bioresources
  2. department of Aquatic bioresources
  3. department of Multidisciplinary Studies ”.
  
 
Dinesh GunawarDena,
 Minister of Education.
