SRI LANKA EXPORT DEVELOPMENT ACT, NO. 40 OF 1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14 of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979,

Stabilization and National Policies, do by this Order amend the rate of CESS speciﬁ ed in the corresponding entry in Column 
IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2202/6 dated 17.11.2020, in respect of the articles described under the
