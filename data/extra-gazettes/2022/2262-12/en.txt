Whereas the office of member of  Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Seiyad Mohomed 
Mohommed Nazar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon the member of that Ward Number 20 - Guruthalawa of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is 
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rajapaksha Wasala Mudiyanselage Wijerathna Bandara Mirahawaththa to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  the Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rajapaksha Wasala 
Mudiyanselage Wijerathna Bandara Mirahawaththa was elected as the member of the Ward Number 20 - Guruthalawa of 
Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha.
