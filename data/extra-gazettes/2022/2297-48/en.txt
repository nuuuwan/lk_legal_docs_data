under section 66a(1) of the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262)
Whereas the office of return member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation.
And whereas the Secretary of the, People's Liberation Front the recognized political party to which Herath 
Mudiyanselage Senanayaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared Selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political Party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Seewali Saman Bandara Rathnayake to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kasthurisinghe Herath Mudiyanselage Sajith Priyankara Kasthurisinghe, returning officer of 
the Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance                           
(Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Seewali Saman Bandara Rathnayake was selected as the 
member of the Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha.
