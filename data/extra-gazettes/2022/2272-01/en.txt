The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
DGC A877/PL 305.
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, Major General  (Retired) G. V .  Ravipriya Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 21st March,
2022, all  duties of Customs as well as other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance
(Chapter 235), shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The Notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette No. 2271/1 of  14th March, 2022 is
hereby rescinded.
G. V .  R
AVIPRIYA Major General (Retired),
 Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
18th March, 2022.
