WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of 
resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha 
Appuhamilage Sandya Namali Gunarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kanewela Arachchige Roshini Perera to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kanewela Arachchige Roshini Perera was selected as the member of the Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
