It is hereby notified that the Hon. President, having consulted with the Hon. Prime Minister under Article 44(3) of the Con-
stitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has determined that the ministerial portfolios indicated below 
should be in his charge.
1.	 Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies
2.	 Minister of Technology
3.	 Minister of Women, Child Affairs and Social Empowerment
4.	 Minister of Investment Promotion
