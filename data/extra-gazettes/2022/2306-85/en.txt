  PRADEShIYA  SABhA  IPALOGAmA 
The Notice under the Section 24(1) (B) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987
It is hereby announced, in accordance of the Section 24(1) (b) of the Pradeshiya Sabha act No. 15 of 1987. that the Ipalogama 
Pradeshiya Sabha in anuradhapura district of North Central Province, the thoroughfares listed in the following schedule will 
be declared as thoroughfares owned by the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha; under the General assembly decision No. 451 held 
on 19.07.2021 of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
  It is notified that, if the public of the area or owners of the thoroughfares lands make any objection, they shall proceed in 
accordance of the section 24(1)(b) of the Pradeshiya Sabha act No. 15 of 1987 to prove their right within one month from 
the date of the publication of this notice in the Gazette.
 It is hereby announced for the information to the public that if there no any objection raised within this period the thoroughfares 
mentioned in the schedule will be recognized and controlled as thoroughfares owned by the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha.
HemantHa CHandra Kumara ratHwatHtHa, 
Chairman,
Pradeshiya Sabha Ipalogama.
At the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha Office.
26th of october, 2022.
