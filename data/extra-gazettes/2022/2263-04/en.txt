I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President  of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by 
section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby once again enlarge the time up to 21st June, 2022 for 
rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry for appraisal of 
the findings of previous Commissions and Committees on Human Rights and the way forward established under Section 2 
of said Act on 20th January, 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2211/55 dated 21st January, 
2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, having enlarged of time ending on 21st July, 2021 by six months 
from 21st July, 2021 up to 21st January, 2022 by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2238/6 dated 27th July, 2021 in the exercise of  
powers vested in me.
