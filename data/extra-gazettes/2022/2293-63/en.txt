THE UNIvERSITIES ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order under Section 27 (1) (a) read with Section 21
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27 (1) (a) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the university grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the schedule 
to the Order made under 21 of the said act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1400/24 of July 08, 2005 (as amended 
from time to time), establishing the university of the Visual and Performing arts, by the addition immediately after the entry 
“Department of English Language Teaching” appearing in Column II of the Schedule (specified in the Order published in 
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2217/2 of March 01, 2021) which corresponds to the item “faculty of dance and drama” 
appearing in Column i of the schedule, of the following new entry:-
 “department of information technology”.
 
A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
 Minister of Education.
Colombo,
17th august, 2022.
