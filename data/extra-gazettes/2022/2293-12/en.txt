No. 2293/12 – MONDAY, AUGUST  15,  2022 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/6/2019/HW/467. 
My Ref. No.: THI/LND/L2/ACQVI/160. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act , as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired :  
 
 
Allotments of Lot No. 01 and 02 (02 Lots) in Total 
Extent of about 0.0084 Hectare, depicted in Preliminary 
Plan No. Mara 2876 and dated 23.12.2021 prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated in the village of 
Kadawedduwa in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Uduwa East in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Thihagoda in Matara District in Southern Province. 
  
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Bathalawelkoratuwa; Description 
of Land: Garden; Extent: 0.0020 Hectares. 
 
01. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Henawatta alias Aluthwatta; 
Description of Land: Garden; Extent: 0.0064 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands  are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents dully 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
office of Thihagoda on the 21.09.2022 at 10.00 a. m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                    
(in duplicate) on or before 19.09.2022 the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particular of their claims for 
compensation for the  acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
  
                                                UMESHA MATARAGE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Thihagoda. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Thihagoda. 
09.08.2022.
