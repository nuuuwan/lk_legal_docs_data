LAND REFORM ACT, No. 1 OF 1972 OF NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY
BY this Order, I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, The Minister of Lands of the Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27a (1) of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 which is amended 
time to time by the Land Reform Commission (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 which is read in conjuction with 
Sub  - section(2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
1)    Owned by the Land Reform Commission which is established under Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 of National 
State Assembly in terms of Section three of the said Act, the agricultural land parcels which is stated deﬁ nitely by the 
schedule of this Order is vested by selling in terms of Section 22 (1) b b of the said Land Reform Act, to the Board of 
Investment of Sri Lanka, which is established by the Act, No. 04 of 1978.

Investment of Sri Lanka for the scheduled lands below as the ﬁ nancial pledge for the aforesaid sale.
