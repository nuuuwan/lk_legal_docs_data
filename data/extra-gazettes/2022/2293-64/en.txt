PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  MATALE MUNICIPAL COUNCIL  
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return Member of  Matale Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Ceased to be member 
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Ahamed 
Lebbe Iruphan Ali  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Matale Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed under Section 9 of 
the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Seyiyad Yashin Mohamed Saleemdeen to ﬁ ll such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Of ﬁ cer of the Matale Municipal Council, acting in terms of Selection
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Seyiyad Yashin Mohamed 
Saleemdeen was selected as the member of  the Matale Municipal Council.
PULASTHI PATHIRANAPP ,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Matale Municipal Council. 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Matale District,
18th August, 2022.
