WHEREAS, the office of  member of  Pottuvil  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Muhammadu Sarifu 
Abdul Wahid the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of that Number 3 Hidayapuram of Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Nadarasa Jegan to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Pottuvil Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Nadarasa Jegan was elected as the member of  the Number 3 Hidayapuram of Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha.
