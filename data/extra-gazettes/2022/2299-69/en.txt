uNDER SubSECTION (4) Of SECTION 27 A
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the subsection (4) of section 27 a of the Land reform act, No. 01 of 1972 of the 
National state assembly amended by Land reform (special provisions) act, No. 39 of 1981, which should be read with sub 
article (2) of article 44 of the Constitution of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, i, Harin fernando, minister 
of tourism and Lands do hereby :-
 (a) amend the orders made under the subsection (4) of section 27 a of the Land reform act, published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 1101/2 dated 11 October 1999 relating to the estate land called and known as Heyyanthuduwa 
Watte, morefully described in the first schedule of this order, alienated to National youth service Council 
established under the act, No. 69 of 1979 as per the provisions stipulated in the said amended Land reform Law, 
by revocation of the such portion of the said order as relates to the extent of the land called as Heyyanthuduwa 
Watte specified in Second Schedule hereto by reason of failure to adhere to the regulations or conditions pertain 
to the consideration for alienating the said land to the National youth service Council.
