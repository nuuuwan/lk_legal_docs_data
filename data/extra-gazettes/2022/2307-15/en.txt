PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  VAVUNIYA  NORTH  
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66 A (1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return member of Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Kanagarasa 
Pratheepan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Arumugam Akileswaran to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Arumugam Akileswaran 
was selected as the member of the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha
.
J. JENITON,
 Returning Officer,
 Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Ofﬁce,
Vavuniya,
21st November, 2022.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE AKKARAIPATTU  PRADESHIYA  
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66 A(1)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  
(CHAPTER  262) 
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of  return member of Akkaraipattu  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of been ceased to 
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Muhammadu 
Maiyatheen Raheem  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of  Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁ ed under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Atham Levvei Najeed Meera Levvei to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Akkaraipattu  
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Atham Levvei Najeed Meera Levvei was selected as the member of the Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 K
ASUN SRINATH ATHTHANAYAKA,
 Returning Officer,
 Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Ofﬁce,
Ampara,
21st November, 2022.
