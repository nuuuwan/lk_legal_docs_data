Notice under section 10a(1) (a) of the Local authorities elections ordinance
I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby 
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Indika Prasanna Kumara 
Koralage of the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been 
ceased to be a member of the United People's Freedom Alliance.
  
 M. M. S. K. B
andara Mapa,

 Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
