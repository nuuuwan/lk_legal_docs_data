sri Lanka under the companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number PV 94125, and located at manelwatta, 
bollegala, Kelaniya, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the 
Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2301/19 of October 10, 2022, by the addition 
immediately after the entry “bachelor of science honours in Information technology” appearing in column I and its 
corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule 
II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
scheduLe  I
Conditions
 1. Nagananda International Institute for buddhist studies (Pvt.) Limited (NIIbs) shall, when admitting students for 
the degree programmes specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Arts Honours in Anthropology 
and bachelor of arts honours in applied communication and media technology, complete with a minimum of 120 
credits (SLQF Level 6) adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (3) ‘simple passes’(S) in any subject stream 
in one and the same sitting at the Gce (advanced Level) examination conducted by the department of examinations 
of sri Lanka or equivalent.
 2. Nagananda International Institute for buddhist studies (Pvt.) Limited (NIIbs) is recognized to offer the degree 
specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted by the Specified Authority.
