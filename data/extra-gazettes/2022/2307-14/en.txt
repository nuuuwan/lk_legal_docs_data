PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  KULIYAPITIYA  URBAN COUNCIL  
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1) (a)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the ofﬁ ce of return member of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending for more
than three council meetings without permission;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Banneheka
Mudiyanselage Indika Mahesh Banneheka  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  member of that 03-Dandagamuwa of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualiﬁ ed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Vidhanalage Wilfred De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the Kuliyapitiya Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Vidhanalage Wilfred De Silva was elected as the member of the 03 - Dandagamuwa Town of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council.
R. M. H. B. RATHNAYAKER  ,
 Returning Officer,
 Kuliyapitya Urban Council. 
District Elections Ofﬁ ce,
Kurunegala,
21st November, 2022.
