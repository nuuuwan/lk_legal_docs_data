SAbARAGAmUwA  PROVINCE
I, tikiri Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the sabaragamuwa Province, hereby announce the following orders by virtue of 
powers vested in me under the Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, to be read with section 2 of the Provincial Council  
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.   
Tikiri kobbekaduwa, 
Governor,
 sabaragamuwa Province.
13th day of January, 2022,
Governor Office,
sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
New town, 
ratnapura.
Order
since the post of Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya sabha situated within the district of Kegalle of the sabaragamuwa 
Province remain vacant due to failure to pass the budget prepared for the year 2022, in terms of section 168 of Pradeshiya 
sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and the section 169 of the Pradeshiya sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by section 17 of 
Local Government Authorities (special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of  2012, I hereby inform that the powers, preformance and 
functions related to this post is vested to the deputy Chairman in terms of section 8 (4) of the Pradeshiya sabha Act from 
the date of  the vacancy and until the date of the vacancy is to be filled as per Local Government Elections (Amendment) 
Act, No. 22 of 2012.
Further, I hereby direct the 
deputy Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya sabha to bear the essential expenses related to 
special projects currently in progress and the recurrent expenditure of the Mawanella Pradeshiya sabha for the year 2022, 
with the approval of Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Kegalle Administrative district, prepared by the secretary 
of the Mawanella Pradeshiya sabha to ensure the continuation of the activities of the Mawanella Pradeshiya sabha for a 
period of time until the new Chairman presents the budget for the financial year 2022 to the Sabha and approves it properly.

Order
the Chairman of the Balangoda urban Council of the sabaragamuwa Province, Chamika Jayamini Wimalasena, is considered 
to have resigned the post with effect from 14.01.2022 and the above post is considered to be vacant since that date due to 
failure to pass the budget prepared by the Chairman of the Balangoda urban Council for the year 2022, in terms of section 
178 of urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) and section 178(A) of urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) as amended 
by section 17 of Local Government (special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and non- compliance with the said section 
No. 178(A).
SAbARAGAmUwA  PROVINCE
UrbAn COUnCiL OrdinAnCE (ChAptEr 255)
I, tikiri Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the sabaragamuwa Province, hereby announce the following orders by virtue of 
powers vested in me under the urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) to be read with section 2 of  the Provincial Council 
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.   
 
Tikiri kobbekaduwa, 
 Governor,
 sabaragamuwa Province.
13th day of January, 2022,
Governor Office,
sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
New town, 
ratnapura.
Order
since the post of Chairman of the Balangoda urban Council situated within the ratnapura district limits of the sabaragamuwa 
Province remain vacant due to failure to pass the budget prepared for the year 2022, in terms of section 178 of urban Council 
Ordinance (Chapter 255) and the section 178(A) of the urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) as amended by section 12 
of Local Government Authorities (special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, I hereby inform that the powers, performance 
and functions related to this post is vested to the deputy Chairman in terms of section 19(6) of the urban Council Ordinance 
(Chapter 255) from the date of the vacancy and until the date of the vacancy is to be filled as per Local Government Elections 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
Further, I hereby direct the deputy Chairman of the Balangoda urban Council to bear the essential expenses related to special 
projects currently in progress and the recurrent expenditure of the Balangoda urban Council for the year 2022, with the 
approval of Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, ratnapura Administrative district, prepared by the secretary of 
the Balangoda urban Council to ensure the continuation of the activities of theBalangoda urban Council for a period of time 
until the new Chairman presents the budget for the financial year 2022 to the Council and approves it properly.
