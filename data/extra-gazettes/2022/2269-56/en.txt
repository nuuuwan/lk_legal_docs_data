SPECIAL COmmODITY LEvY  ACT, No. 48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the special commodity Levy Act No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil rajapaksa, 
minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the commodities 
specified in Column (i) of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding entries 
in Column (ii) of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from March 04, 2022 subject to provisions of Sub Section (3) 
of section 2 of the special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
