Part I
 REGISTRATION 
2. a Company which has been issued a Letter of approval in Principle under the regulations for registration, 
Licensing, authorisation and other approvals of authorised Persons No. 01 of 2022 published in the Gazetteextraordinary 
No. 2299/46 of september 28, 2022 and which intends to be registered as an offshore company to engage in business in 
and from the area of authority of the Colombo Port City shall make an application substantially in Form I set out in the 
Schedule hereto accompanied by the documents, statements, information and non-refundable processing fee as specified 
in section 41 (3) of the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 of 2021 (in these regulations referred to 
as the “act”).
3. upon receipt of an application,the Commission shall, if such application and documents are acceptable to the 
Commission, recommend to the registrar-General of Companies to proceed to register such company as an offshore 
company in terms of Part XI of the Companies Act and issue a Certificate of Registration to the applicant company as an 
offshore company permitted to engage in business in and from the area of authority of the Colombo Port City. 
4. (1) the registrar-General of Companies shall take all necessary steps to register such company within a period 
of three days as an offshore company permitted to engage in business in and from the area of authority of the Colombo 
Port City and forward to the Commission a Certificate of Registration in the name of that company substantially in Form 
II set out in the schedule hereto, to be issued to the applicant company.
   (2)  the registrar-General of Companies shall maintain a register known as register of offshore Companies-
Colombo Port City Commission (in these regulations referred to as the “register”) and allocate a registration number to 
such company.
5.  Upon the issue of a Certificate of Registration under regulation 4, the Certificate of Registration shall be deemed 
to exempt such company to which the Certificate of Registration is issued to engage in business in and from the Area of 
authority of the Colombo Port City, from having to comply with the provisions of such Companies act.
6. (1) an offshore company registered under regulation 4 shall, within a period of thirty days, inform the Commission 
in Form III set out in the schedule hereto, whenever-
(a) any change or alteration occurs with regard to the particulars provided to the Commission in the 
application made under regulation 2; or
(b)  any of the following occurs relating to the company, that is: -
(i) a merger or amalgamation with another company or legal entity;
(ii) a reduction of share or stated capital;
(iii) inability to pay debts as it falls due or the commencement of a winding up;
(iv) change of the status; 
(v) change of the name;
(vi) the acquisition or an agreement for the acquisition, whether contingent or not, of assets 
of a value which is greater than half the value of the assets of the company before the 
acquisition or agreement for acquisition;
(vii) the disposition or an agreement for the disposition, whether contingent or not, of the whole 
or more than half of the value of the assets of such company;

           (2) the Commission shall notify the registrar-General of Companies the information received by it under 
subregulation (1) without delay.
7. (1) Upon receipt of a notification under regulation 6, the Registrar-General of Companies shall cause the information 
in the register to be updated in such manner as may be necessary.
    (2) updating of information in the register shall be in addition, and without prejudice, to any changes to other 
records maintained by the registrar-General of Companies in relation to such offshore company.
8. an offshore company registered under regulation 4 which intends to continue its business shall, in terms of section 
41(9) of the Act, produce to the Registrar-General of Companies proof of payment of the annual fee as may be specified 
in the regulations for registration and Licensing of authorised Person (Fees), No. 01 of 2022 published in the Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 2299/47 of september 28, 2022 in Form Ivset out in the schedule hereto.
Part II
CANCELLATION OF REGISTRATION
9. (1) Where an offshore company registered under regulation 4 intends to cease carrying on its business, such 
offshore company shall send a notice to that effect in Form V set out in the Schedule hereto, to the Commission. 
    (2) Upon the receipt of notice under subregulation (1), the Commission shall cancel the licence issued to such 
offshore company as an authorised person and forward such notice to the Registrar-General of Companies to strike off 
the name of the offshore company from the Register.
10. For the avoidance of doubt, it is hereby declared that a company incorporated under the Companies Act which 
is registered under Part VII of the Act as an offshore company shall, notwithstanding the cancellation of its registration 
and striking off of its name from the Register, continue to be a body corporate, registered and incorporated under the 
said Companies Act. 
Part III
INTERPRETATION
11. In these regulations -
“Commission” means the Colombo Port City economic Commission established under the act; and
“Companies act” means the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007.
