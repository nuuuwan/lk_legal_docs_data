Notice made under section 66(c) that should be read with section 66(G) of the Local Authorities 
Election Ordinance amended by the Local Government Election (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Nupe Hewage Roy Nishantha, the Commissioner of Local Government of North Central Province do hereby notified that the 
meeting for the purpose of electing the Vice Chairman for the Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha of Anuradhapura District in North 
Central Province, will be held in the conference hall of Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha at 10.00 a.m. on 08.07.2022, in terms of the 
powers vested in me, under the provisions of section 66(C) that should be read with Section 66(G) of Local Government Election 
(Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, as amended by Local Authorities Election Ordinance (Chapter 262).
