WHEREAS, the office of return member of Eravur Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of party ;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Meeralebbe

returned member of Eravur Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty

Meerashahibu Abdul Gafoor to fill such vacancy;

of Section  66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Meerashahibu Abdul 
Gafoor was selected as the member of the Eravur Urban Council.
