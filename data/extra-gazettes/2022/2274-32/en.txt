WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending  Council 
meetings for a period of three month without permission ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group Number 01  to which  Sehumathar Pahurudeen the member 
belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Pottuvil 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Abdul 
Gaffoor Iqbal to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I,  Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Kasun Srinath Aththanayaka, Returning Officer of the Pottuvil Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section  66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Abdul Gaffoor Iqbal  was selected as the member of the Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha.
