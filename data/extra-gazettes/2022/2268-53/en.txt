Filling of a Vacancy in the Office of Chairman of theVerugal Pradeshiya Sabha Under Section 66B(2) 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorites Elections 
(Amended) Act, No. 16 of 2017  
whereas, the Office of Chairman in Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Office of the Chairman shall 
be deemed to have been resign under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by No. 21 of 2012 
(Special Provisions) Local Authority Act;
And Whereas  the Secretary  of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu  Kadchi, the recognized political party to which 
Kathirkamaththampi Suntharalingam, the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of  Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be  
declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Thevarasa Vijayarahuvaran to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, San
garapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer of the Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Election (amendment) 
Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Thevarasa Vijayarahuvaran elected as Chairman of the Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha.
Sangarapillai Suthakaran,
Returning Officer,
Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha. 
23rd of February 2022,
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee.
