Land Acquisition Notices 
1A - G 36300  - 17 (03/2022) 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDED) ACT, NO.  28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
My Reference No.: BAL/2/1/10(ii).   
Land Ministry’s No. : 03J/94/D/107.   
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:- 
 
Land called Atuwala Edama Uthuru Kotasa Punchi 
Liyadda and Podi Saneege Kotuwa and 
Aththanawalawatta land acquisition for Police of 
Kosgoda, extent about 0.4675 hectares depicted as lot 01 
in the Survey General’s Amended Plan of P.P.G. 3943 
and 31.01.2022 dated and situated in 17 A Kosgoda 
Grama Niladari Division in Balapitiya Divisional  
Secretary’s Division in Galle District : 
   
01. Lot No. 01—North portion of Athuwala Idama 
Punchi Liyadda and Podi Saneege Kotuwa and 
Aththanawalawatta; Extent : 0.4675 Hectares 
North by: Bakinigaha Liyadda claimed by Yagawa 
Somawathi De Silva & Others, Road and Road (RDA); 
East by: Road (R.D.A.) and Road; South by: Road and 
Thambithotapadinchiwatta claimed by D. A. Perera; 
West by: Thambithotapadinchiwatta claimed by D. A. 
Perera, Alan Mendis padinchi watta claimed by Kaal 
Weerawardana & Bakinigaha Liyadda claimed by 
Yagawa Somawathi De Silva and others
