POINT PEDRO URBAN COUNCIL
Declaration of Names and Details of Roads
IN terms of Section 44, 46 and 49 of Urban Council Ordinance Number 61 of 1939 (Chapter 255), out of the details of roads 
situated within the jurisdiction of Point Pedro Urban Council in the Jaffna District, roads of No. 01 – 129 published in the 
Gazette No. 2052 dated 29.12.2017 and No. 130 – 167 published in the Gazette No. 2132 of 12.07.2019  and the Gazette of 
Council Road details published before 21.09.2022 are hereby cancelled.
 In terms of the resolution  No: C26/2022 dated 25.02.2022 and in terms of power vested to this Urban Council, I hereby 
announce  that the roads of  No. 01 -184 given in the schedule below are the roads own or belong to Point Pedro Urban Council.
 I hereby announce to the public that if anyone claims ownership for the land of these roads that are identiﬁ ed in terms of 
Section 44, 46 and 49 of Urban Council Ordinance Number 61 of 1939 (Chapter 255), he /she may apply to us, his objections 
with relevant documents that prove his /her ownership, within one month from the date published in the Gazette or he/ she 
shall ﬁ le a case in a suitable court within three months from the date of publication in the Gazette, in order to establish his/ 
her ownership, with one month prior notice to Point Pedro Urban Council.
JOSEPH IRUTHAYARAJAH,
Chairman,   
Point Pedro Urban Council.
Ofﬁ ce of the Point Pedro Urban Council, 
Point Pedro.
