WHEREAS, the office of  Member of  Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party.
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Ameen 
Farees the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the  
member of that No. 03 - Arafathnagar of Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Abdul Rasak Mujeebu Rahuman to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Rasak Mujeebu Rahuman 
was elected as the member of  the No. 03 - Arafathnagar electoral ward of Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
