NOTIfICATION
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 of the Commission of inquiry act (Chapter 393), i do hereby enlarge once 
again the period of time ending 21st July, 2021, which has been enlarged from time to time by the said powers, up to 31st 
December, 2022 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission of Inquiry established 
in terms of Section 2 of the aforesaid act on 20th January, 2021 by Proclamation published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2211/55 dated 21st January, 2021.
