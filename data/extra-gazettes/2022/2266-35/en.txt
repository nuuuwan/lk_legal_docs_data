THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  Reference No. : KDS/KGK/4/2(2)/2017/02/05.
Ministry of Land Ref. No. : 4-3/12/2018/WS/252.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land for a public purpose:
Description of the Land  to be Acquired :
The following allotment of land marked Lot No. 244 depicted
in Sheet No. 06 of  Zone No. 01 of  the Cadastral Map No.
320390 dated 27.11.2021 prepared by Senior Superintend of
Surveyor of Kandy on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated
in the Village called Meekanuwa, in the Grama Niladhari
Division (269) of Ampitiya Udagama - North in the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Kandy Kadawathsathara and
Gangawata Korale in the District of Kandy, Central Province
and contain in extent 0.0467 hectares:
01. Lot No. 244
Name of the Land : Tennakumbura Watta; Claimant:
State;Original Claimant:-M.P.W.N.K.G.Peiris, No.
194,Katugastota Road, Kandy;Extent : 0.0467 Hectare.
I hereby give notice to the public that every person
interested in the said land / servitude which is to be acquired
is informed to appear personally or by agent duly authorized
in writing, before me  on 29.03.2022 at 10.00 a.m. at the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Kandy Kadawathsathara and
Gangawata Korale.
The nature of their interests in the land/lands, the particulars
of their claims for compensation, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount should be
notified to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 22.03.2022.
U.J.M.S.S.B.J
AY ASINGHE,
Divisional Secretry,
Kandy Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale.
On this 02nd of February 2022.
At Divisional Secretariat,
Kandy Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale.
