L.d. b. 277/40
ThE  ANTIquITIES  ORDINANCE (ChAPTER 188)
Notice  under Section 19
i, Vidura Wikramanayaka, minister of buddhasasana, religious and Cultural affairs, do hereby give that i intend to make an 
Order under section 18 of The Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) declaring ancient monuments specified in the Schedule 
to this notice as “protected monuments” for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance.
Objections, if any, to the making of the aforesaid Order shall be sent to the director General of archaeology, Colombo, on 
or before 21.10.2022.
every objection shall be in writing and shall contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is made.
 
Vidura Wikramanayaka,
 minister of buddhasasana,
 religious and Cultural affairs.
Colombo,
21st september, 2022.
