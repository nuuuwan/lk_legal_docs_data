WHEREAS, the office of  return member of Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of 
Death;
And Whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Thammitage Sujeewa 
Priyadharshana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance to decide upon 
returned member of Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party, and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dhammika Dananjaya Ranaweera to fill such vacancy ;
 Now Therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer of the Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Dhammika Dananjaya Ranaweera was selected as the member of the Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
