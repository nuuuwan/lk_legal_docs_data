PRADEShIYA  SABhA  NAwAGAThThEGAmA 
It is hereby notified for public information that it has been adopted under resolution No. 529 moved at the General 
Conference held on 13.10.2022 at Pradeshiya sabha Nawagaththegama that all the roads and thoroughfares for which legal 
action had not been filed in a proper judicial Court before the date specified or roads and thoroughfares confirmed as the 
roads and thoroughfares that should be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama as per the Decree of a Judicial Court 
in which a case had been filed before the said date and not mentioned in the following schedule from among the roads and 
thoroughfares given in the list of the schedule of the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2287/06 
and dated 04.07.2022 shall be confirmed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama in 
terms of orders made by series of “Orders on publication of notifications specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads 
and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within the North Western Province” published by Extraordinary 
Gazette Paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of 
the order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
It is further notified that a list of said roads and thoroughfares has been included in the schedule hereto and all the 
roads and thoroughfares mentioned in that Schedule shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Nawagaththegama under Sub Section (5) of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
 S. M. I. S
enadhIpathI,
 Chairman,
 Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama.
At the office of Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama,
26th October, 2022.

Resolution
Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama proposes that the roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions had not been 
filed in a proper Judicial Court and not mentioned in the following Schedule from among the roads and thoroughfares given 
in the list of the Schedule of the notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2287/06 dated 04.07.2022. 
according to the resolution No. 529 made at the Pradeshiya Sabha conference held on 13.10.2022, shall be confirmed as roads 
and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama in terms of orders made by the series of ‘Orders on 
publication of notifications specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya 
sabha within the North Western Province’ published by Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 
by the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, 
No. 15 of 1987.
