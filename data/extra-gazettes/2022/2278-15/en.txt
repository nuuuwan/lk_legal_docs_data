THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE 
Notiﬁ cation
WAGES BOARD FOR THE BATIK TRADE
IN pursuance of the authority conferred on me by the Minister of Labour by an order made by him under Section 33(1) (a) 
of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) and published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 48 of 03rd August 1979, I. B. K. 
Prabath Chandrakeerthi, Commissioner General of Labour hereby notify under Section 28 of the aforesaid Ordinance, and I 
propose to determine under Section 33(2) of that Ordinance the minimum rate of wages for time work set out in the Schedule 
hereto in respect of the workers in the Batik Trade.
            Objections to the proposed determination will be received by me until 12.00 noon  on 18th May, 2022.
   Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is taken.
 B. K. P
RABATH CHANDRAKEERTHI,
  Commissioner General of Labour.
Department of Labour,
''Mehewara Piyesa"
Colombo 05.
29th April, 2022.

of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) and published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 1120/3 of 21st February 2000, I.
