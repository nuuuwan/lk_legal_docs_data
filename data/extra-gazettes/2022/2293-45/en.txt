atteNtION of the general Public is drawn to the regulations related to the pilgrims published in the Gazette No. 2151/21 
of 26th November 2019, Gazette No. 2184/49 of 17th July 2020 and Gazette No. 2243/8 of 30th august 2021. I hereby 
notify the following particulars relating to those regulations.
1. Duration of conducting the Perahera
• It is hereby informed that the esala Festival of sabaragamu Maha saman devalaya will commence on the 26th 
August and end with Water Cutting Ceremony and Day Procession on the 11th September 2022.
* All night Pirith Chanting Ceremony 26.08.2022
* Alms Giving Ceremony for Maha Sangha 27.08.2022
* Kap Planting Ceremony 28.08.2022
* auction of stalls 28.08.2022
* Kumbal Perahera Festival From 28.08.2022 to 31.08.2022
* street Parade of the Perahera From 01.09.2022 to 05.09.2022
* randoli Perahera Festival From 06.09.2022 to 10.09.2022
* Water Cutting Ceremony 11.09.2022
2. The Camp Area
• the area where the esala Festival to be conducted shall be the area bounded to the North by the public resident 
lands of Paraveni Officers, vehicle parking area, cement channel wich demarcates sales stalls area and the entrance 
road to the vehicle park, to the south by thennagewaththa and the “Kalu” river, to the east by ratnapura - Panadura 
road and to the West by Maha uyanwaththa belonged to the devalaya.
3. Vehicles
 3.1 No vehicle, including motor vehicles, bicycles and carts, without my permission or a permission given by an 
authorized officer, shall park or enter any place or festival premises except the area reserved for parking.
 3.2 entering vehicles to the sacred area through the gateway erected facing to ratnapura - Panadura main road is 
strictly prohibited.
4. Construction of Buildings
 4.1 No person shall construct or build any shelter or structure whether temporary or permanent or add a new part to 
such building which is already exist within the camp area during the period of time from the 26th August to the 
11th September 2022, without my written permission or such permission obtained from an authorized officer. 
 4.2 Whereas, it shall be permitted to enter any land or place situated in the camp area in order to implement any 
regulation, among the regulations imposed by me or any authorized officer or to erect a pillar or to conduct a 
construction or a building on any land or place within the camp area in order to display any notice or signboard.
5. Offering Puja for the Perahera
 5.1 Stopping between Perahera in order to offer puja for the Water Cutting Perahera will be decided depending on 
the situation.

6. health
 6.1 No person who is suffering from any contagious or infectious disease shall enter the camp area. every person 
shall thoroughly heed to follow the health instructions currently operative in the country.
7. Trade
 7.1 No person shall, within the camp area, sell lotteries, flags or collect contributions for any other purpose without 
my permission or the permission given by an authorized officer and sale and consumption of tobacco products 
and every kind of drugs are strictly prohibited in the premises and within the devalaya boundary, during the 
period of conducting the Perahera.
 7.2 Stalls which are currently selling flowers and other sacred items in the camp area and along road sides starting 
near Kotaambe up to the entrance to Ketaliyanpalla shall thoroughly adhere to the health guidelines prevailing 
in the country during the Perahera season.
 7.3 Whereas, in case it decided to isolate or close the country on health guidance, all boutiques in the area indicated 
in section 7.2 shall be closed.
 7.4 No one shall build a shop or sell anything in the sacred area, except for the flowers and puja items that have been 
erected on both sides of the entrance road to the camp site. 
 7.5 each of the buildings and stalls shall be placed with a garbage container and garbage shall be disposed in such 
containers. 
 7.6 All processed food items for sale shall be kept in covered boxes as to protect against flies. 
8. Beggars 
•  No person shall beg or solcit alms and give or distribute anything by way of cash or otherwise within the camp 
area, except such places as may be appointed for the purpose outside the sacred area.
