THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. KDS/TUM/4/1/6/6-2020/05.
Ministry of Land Reference No.: 4-3/7/2018/HW/367.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends  to    acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Under mentioned land shown in the Surveyor General’s
Plan of F. V. P. 192, Supplement No. 06, Sheet No. 06, situated
in the village of Pubbiliya in the Grama Niladari Division of
No. 322, Barandara in the Minor Division of Uda Palatha Korale
Batahira in Thumpane Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Kandy District of Central Province.
1. Lot No. 377
Name of the Land: Betwarth Estate; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Epitawaththe Gedara
Sumanabanda; Extent: 0.0281 Hectares.
2. Lot No. 378
Name of the Land: Betwarth Estate; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Epitawaththe Gedara
Sumanabanda; Extent: 0.0153 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Thumpane Divisional Secretariat, on the day of the 01st
August 2022, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th July 2022
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for  computation for the said land/servitude the amount
of compensation the details of the computation of such
amount.
S. M. C. G
UNARATHNA,
Divisional Secretary,
     Thumpane.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Thumpane,

th May, 2022.
