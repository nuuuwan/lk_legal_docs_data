CONSTITuTION   Of  ThE  DEmOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPubLIC   Of  SRI  LANkA  
Order  under  Article  98(8)  of  the  Constitution
whereAs  the first Supplementary Electoral Register for the year 2022, including electors who will complete the age 
of eighteen within the period commencing on the first day of February 2022 and ending on the 31st day of the May 2022, 
prepared under Section 10 of the Registration of Electors (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2021 was certified on 08 November 
2022; in pursuance of the powers vested in the Election Commission under Article 98(8) of the Constitution of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election Commission by this Order.
Certify that the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members which each electoral 
district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation 
under Article 97 and Article 98.
 
 
 Nimal G. Punchihewa, S. B. Diwarathne, M. M. Mohamed,
 Chairman, Member, Member,
 Election Commission. Election Commission. Election Commission.
  K. P. P. Pathirana,  P. S. M. Charles,
  Member,  Member,
  Election Commission.  Election Commission.
 
Elections Commission,
Rajagiriya,
08th December, 2022.
