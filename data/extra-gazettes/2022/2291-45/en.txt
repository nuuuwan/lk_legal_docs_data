PART  IV (A) — PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
Provincial Council Notifications
UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice by the Governor of Uva Province
uvA Provincial Council Notice of the Hon. governor of uva Province inconsistent with the section 185 of the Pradeshiya 
sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, should be read with the section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, 
No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the section 2(3) (a) (i) of the statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding 
the powers to monitor the administration, identified as the Statute No. 2 of 2011  
of the Uva Provincial Council imposed 
to make provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor administration in Local government Authorities 
established in uva 
Province and matters relate and incidental thereto i, A.J.M. Muzammil, the governor of the uva Province, 
temporarily suspended the function of the Katharagama Pradeshiya sabha  with effect from the midnight of  09th  May, 2022  
and through the Gazette 
No. 2279/10 of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka  dated 09th May 2022, appointed                                                     
Mr. H. M. R. Anura Kumara Judicial Officer, retired Justice to investigate and report to me.
   Considering the practical issues in implementing the activities of the investigation commission, i hereby inform to 
extend the time period from 09th August 2022 to 09th september 2022 to submit the investigation report.
A. J. M. MuzAMMil,
 governor of uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor’s Office,
04th of August of the year 2022.
