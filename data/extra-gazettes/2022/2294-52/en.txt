UNIvERSITIES  AcT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 27(1) (b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25A of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, 
dr. A. d. susil Premajayantha, the Minister of Education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in 
respect of the National school of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the Companies 
Act, No. 7 of 2007, bearing registration Number PB 4833, and being recognized  as a degree Awarding Institute, do by this 
Order, amend the schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2268/87 of 
February 25, 2022 by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of science honours in data science” appearing in 
Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Columns I and II respectively of 
the Schedule II thereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
