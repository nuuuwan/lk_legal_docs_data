Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Kamburupitiya  pradeshiya

WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the group leader of the Independent Group  to which Gunaneththige Uditha Dasun the member belonged, 
was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Kamburupitiya 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the group leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sisira Wijesinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sisira 
Wijesinghe was selected as the member of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 T. W. P
oshiTha Kaumadi,
 Returning Officer,
 Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
