Regulations
Previously  amended regulations No. 01 of 2016 of the National association of sports published in the Extra-ordinary 
Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th october 2016 are hereby amended as follows only in relevance to the year 2022.
 
 01. “Thirty first day of May” in the Section (a) of the Schedule III under the provisions related to the constitution of 
the National associations /federations of sports published in the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th 
October 2016 is repealed and the “Thirty first day of August” is inserted.
 02. the section (a) under the provisions related to the constitution of the National associations/federations of sports 
in the schedule iii of the Extra-ordinary Gazette No. 1990/23 dated 27th october 2016 come into force again 
with effect from the 1st day of January 2023.
