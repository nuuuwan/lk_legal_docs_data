SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT, No. 48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
               
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy act No. 48 of 2007, I, Ranil 
Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka, do by this order imposed in respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special 
Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding entiries in Column II of that Schedule.
 
  This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from June 15, 2022 subject to provisions of Sub Section 
(3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
  The Orders made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy act, No.48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2260/72 of December 31, 2021, Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2262/15 of January 11, 
2022 and Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2236/66 of July 18, 2021 are rescinded with effect from June 15, 2022.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization 
 and National Policies.
Ministry of Finance, Economic Stabilization 
and National Policies,
Colombo 01. 
10th June, 2022.
