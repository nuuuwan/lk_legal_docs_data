Whereas the office of return member of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Indika 
Prasanna Kumara Koralage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kankanige Nimal Wijayasena Perera to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Homagama Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Kankanige Nimal Wijayasena Perera was Selected as the member  of the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
 
M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,
 Returning Officer,
 Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Colombo,
24th  March, 2022.
