WHEREAS the office of return member of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by  reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ranjith Bandara Wijesinghe 
Liyanage Welikumbura the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Thuninchidevage Gunasinghe to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thuninchidevage 
Gunasinghe was selected as the member of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
 Dr. H. D. N. Sampath Fernando,
 Returning Officer,
 Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office, 
Kandy.
28th of March 2022.
