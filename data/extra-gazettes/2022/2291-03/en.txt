THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalak Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and Lands, 
do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 5 of the 
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed 
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions 
of that Act.
 The land name, extent and the boundaries mentioned below 
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/UDP/2018/112 of 
15.08.2018 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
the village of Mahawilawatta in Grama Niladari Division of 
Kalugalhinna (1189) in Minor Division of Kandukara Ihala 
Korale in Divisional Secretary Division of Udapalatha in 
Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows:  
 1. Lot A– Mahawilawatta - Extent : 2.5000 Hectares.
 North: Mahawilawatta claimed by Land Reforms 
Commssion Sabha; East: Mahawilawatta claimed by Land 
Reforms Commission Sabha and Estate Road; South: 
Estate Road and Mahawilawatta claimed by Land Reforms 
Commission Sabha; West: Mahawilawatta claimed by Land 
Reforms Commssion Sabha.
 Harin Fernando,
