WHEREAS, the office of  member of Eravur Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Muslim National Alliance (United National Alliance at present) the recognized 
political party to which Abdul Saly Muhammadu Riyal the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 
66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 06 Palliady of Eravur Urban Council who 
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and 
who shall be declared elected as member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kalideen Ismath Ifthikar to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, returning officer of the Eravur Urban Council, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kalideen Ismath Ifthikar 
was elected as the member of the Ward No. 06 Palliady of Eravur Urban Council.
