IMPORTS AND EXPORTS CONTROL ACT, NO. 1 OF 1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-section 4(1) and Section 14 of the Imports and 
Exports (Control) Act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by Act, No. 48 of 1985 and Act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, 
the Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies promulgate following Regulations.

3. Date of arrival of import consignments of Wheat Flour (HS Code of 1101.00.10)  at the Port / Airport in Sri Lanka 
under the Open Account or Consignment Account Payment terms on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry of 
Trade, Commerce and Food Security as authorized by the Regulation No. 4 (d) of the Imports Control Regulations on 
Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette No. 2278/21 dated May 06, 2022, as amended by the Imports

Imports Control Regulations on Payment Terms No. 07 of 2022, published in the Gazette No. 2278/21 dated May 06, 
2022.

on any importation made in violation of the Regulation No. 3 above.
6. Controller General of Imports and Exports Control is also hereby authorized to issue necessary “Operational Instruc-
