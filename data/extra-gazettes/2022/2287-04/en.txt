ThE  hOLIDAYS  ACT,  No.  29  Of  1971
Order under Section 4
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, dinesh Gunawardhana, 
Minister of Public Administration Home Affairs, Provincial Councils and Local Government do by this order determine 
that, for the purpose of this Act, the days specified in the Schedule hereto shall be the Public and Bank Holidays for 
the year 2023.
