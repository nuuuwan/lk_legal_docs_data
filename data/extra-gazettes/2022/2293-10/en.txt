Award
 1. the honorable minister of Labour and trade union Relations by virtue of the powers vested in him by section 4 (1) of 
the Industrial disputes act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative enactments of Ceylon (1956 revised edition) as amended by 
acts Nos. 14 of 1957, 62 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with industrial disputes (special Provisions) act No. 
37 of 1968 appointed me by his order dated 07th october, 2019 and referred the dispute between the aforesaid parties 
to me for settlement by arbitration.
 2. the matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is -
“Whether Injustice had been caused to mr. senaka maithri de Livera who was employed as senior manager 
(Food and Beverages) of the Royal Colombo golf Club situated at No. 223, model Farm Road, Colombo 08, 
by the non - payment of the bonus paid to other employees from 2008 and non - payment of the salary increase 
given to other employees from January, 2009, and if so, to what relief he is entitled.”
 3. our behalf of the applicant, mrs. sachintha gunaratne, attorney-at-law, followed by mrs. Biyanka anandappa, aaL, 
mrs. shalini Fernando aaL, and mr. R. d. de. silva, aaL, appeared on different dates.
  Whereas on behalf on the respondents, messers anurudda Perera, shehan Liyanage, thenura samarasuriya and Nuwan 
Premadasa all attorneys at Law appeared on different dates.
