ThE   UNIvERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under section 27(1) read with section 21 and section 139 
by vritue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dr. a. d. Susil Premajayantha, 
Minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants commission, do by this order, amend the schedule 
to the order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1469/38 of November 03, 2006 (as amended from time to time) 
which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of 
the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said act, by the 
substitution for the item “12. department of Transport and Logistics Management” appearing in column II of the schedule 
which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Engineering” appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the following entry:-
  “12. department of Transport Management and Logistics engineering”
 dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA (M.P),
 Minister of education.
