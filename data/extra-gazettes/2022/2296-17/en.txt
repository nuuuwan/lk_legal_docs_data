WHEREAS the office of member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member of 
the Party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Karunadipathi 
Mudiyanselage Nandasiri Banda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 07 Liyanwala of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Gardiyawasam Lokuveligamage Mallika Padmalatha to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, returning officer of the, Walapane Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Gardiyawasam Lokuveligamage Mallika Padmalatha was elected as the member of the Ward Number 07 Liyanwala of 
Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha.
 Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru
 Shanaka Kalugampitiya,
 Returning Officer,
