THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
My No.: B/E/3/MARGA/SAN-5/Acquisition. 
Land Ministry No.: 4/3/7/2018/HW/463.  
 
IT is hereby notified under Section 7 (1) of the Land 
Acquisition Act that the Government intends to acquire 
the following lands required for the Improvement of the 
additional Land Acquisition for Peradeniya-Badulla-
Chenkalladi Road. 
 
  Description of the Land to be Acquired:   
 
 
Allotments of land in F.V.P. 573 Supplement 104 
Sheet No. 105 prepared by the Superintendent of 
Surveyor in Monaragala, situated in Bibile Village in 
Bibile Divisional Secretary’s Division in the District of 
Monaragala in Uva Province described as follows : 
 
Total Extent : 0.0093 hectares.  
 
01. Lot  No. 1526 
Name of the Land: Buruthak etiya waththa; Claimant: 
Private; Possessor: D. M. Gunarathna; Extent: 0.0073 
Hectare. 
02. Lot  No. 1527 
Name of the Land: Buruthaketiya waththa; Claimant: 
Private; Possessor: Am ara Damayanthi Menike 
Weerasekara; Extent: 0.0015 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot  No. 1528 
Name of the Land: Buruthaketiya waththa; Claimant: 
Private; Possessor: K. R. Siyaneris Pranandu; Extent: 
0.0005 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby  
require to apper personally or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at Bibile Divisional Secretariat on  
25th of April  2022 at 9.30 a.m. and requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 28th of March  
2022 the nature of their interests in the Land, amount of 
compensation and the details of the compensation of such 
amount.
