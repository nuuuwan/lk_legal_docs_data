Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  chilaw  pradeshiya  sabha  
under  section  66a(1)  oF   the  local  authorities   elections  
ordinance  (chapter  262) 
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending  for 
Pradeshiya sabha  meetings period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Asurappulige 
Dumindra Ayesh Rathnasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon returned member of Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Pilippan Ge Nihal Ashoka Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Siriniwasage Lakshitha Ruwan Jayanayake, Returning Officer of the Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Pilippan Ge 
Nihal Ashoka Silva was selected as the member of the Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 S. L. R. J
ayanayake,
 Returning Officer,
 Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
29th November, 2022.
