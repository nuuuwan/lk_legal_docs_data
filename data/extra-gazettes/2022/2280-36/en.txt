CANCELLATION  Of  LICENCE  NOTIfICATION
CaNCellatioN of licence issued to Commercial leasing & Finance PlC, due to amalgamation with lolC 
Finance PlC under the Finance Business act, No. 42 of 2011. 
 Dr. P. NaNdalal WeerasiNghe,
   Chairman of the monetary Board and the
 Governor of the Central Bank of sri lanka.
 
 the public is hereby informed that, due to the amalgamation of Commercial leasing & Finance PlC with lolC Finance 
PlC as part of the masterplan for Consolidation of Non-Bank Financial institutions, the monetary Board of the Central 
Bank of sri lanka, in terms of the Finance Business act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the licence issued to Commercial  
Leasing & Finance PLC of 68, Bauddhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 04, to carry on finance business, with effect from 31.03.2022. 
Commercial leasing & Finance PlC has not accepted deposits from the public with effect from 31.03.2022.
