WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Kattankudy Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of the Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Muslim National Alliance (United National Alliance at present), the recognized 
political party to which Asanarlevve Deen Fairoos the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance to decide upon the returned Member of Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party, and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a Member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Thambi Maraikkar Thoufeek to fill such vacancy ;
 Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thambi Maraikkar 
Thoufeek was selected as the Member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
