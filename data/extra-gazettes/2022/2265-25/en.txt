SOUThERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
The order given in accordance with the Article 248 of Urban Council Ordinance (Amended) which is the 
authority 255 that  should be read with Clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) 
Act, No. 12 of 1989
I. Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province do hereby order you, manimel Wadu Aruna Pradeep, 
Attorney-at-Law, the Chairman of Ambalangoda urban Council, to incur only the recurring Expenses and in case of the need 
for any Capital Expenditure included in the estimate for proposed financial expenditure in the Budget 2022 submitted for the 
approval of Ambalangoda urban Council on 22.12.2021, such move should be executed following a council adaptation for 
the purpose of management of financial requirements for the implementation of the activities of Ambalangoda Urban Council.
