Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS, the office of  member of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Hewagamage 
Sumudu Rukshan Pinto the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of that Ward No. 08 Ambathale of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance and who shall be declared elected as 
a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ihala Gamage Upali Priyantha Weerasekara to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, returning officer of the Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Ihala Gamage Upali Priyantha Weerasekara was elected as the member of the Ward No. 08 Ambathale 
of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. M. S. K. B
andara Mapa,
 Returning Officer,
 Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office,
Colombo,
11th November, 2022.
