Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Notice made under Section 66c read with Section 66g of 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local Authorities Elections 
(Amendment) Act No. 22 of 2012
I,  Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the powers 
entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendemnt) Act No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a  meeting in view of selecting a new Vice Chairman for the 
vacated Vice Chairman's position of Peliyagoda Urban Council of Western Province, will be convened on January 12 of 2023 at 
10.00 a.m. at the Urban Council Chamber of Peliyagoda Urban Council located at Kandy road, Peliyagoda.
 A. D. P. I. PRASANNA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
 Western Province.
Department of Local Government,
Western Province,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla.
on the date of 29th of December, 2022.
