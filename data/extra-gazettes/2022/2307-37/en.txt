Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  Kandy  municipal council

WHEREAS the office of return member of Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Samudra 
Jalini Dayananda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon  returned member of Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days ;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Samarasinghe Arachchilage Sudarma Geethanjalee Samarasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Hewage Danushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Samarasinghe 
Arachchilage Sudarma Geethanjalee Samarasinghe was selected as the member of the Kandy Municipal Council.
