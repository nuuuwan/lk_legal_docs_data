THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT, No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1) read with section 21 of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978,                   
I, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, minister of education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do 
by this order, amend the schedule to the order made under section 21 of the said act, published in Gazette Extraordinary, 
No. 1395/15, of June 1, 2005 (as amended from time to time) establishing the Uva wellassa University of sri Lanka, by the 
addition immediately after the entry “2. department of export  agriculture” appearing in Column II of the schedule which 
corresponds to the item “Faculty of animal science and export agriculture” appearing in Column I of the schedule, of the 
following new entry:-
 “3. department of Food science and Technology”.
Dr. A. D. SuSil PremAjAyAnthA,
