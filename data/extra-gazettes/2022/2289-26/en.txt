notice made  under   section  66 (c)  that  should  be  read  with  section  66 (G)  of

I, Nupe Hewage Roy Nishantha, the commissioner of Local Government of North Central Province do hereby notified that 
the meeting for the purpose of electing the chairman for the Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha of Anuradhapura District in North 
central Province, will be held in the conference hall of Pradeshiya Sabha, Rambewa at 10.00 a.m. on 29.07.2022, in terms of 
the powers vested in me, under the provisions of Section 66(c) that should be read with Section 66 (G) of Local Government 
Elections (amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, as amended by Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262).
