WHEREAS the office of  return member of Horana Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member

And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Gamage Don Somasiri

Sandanam Johnson Paul to fill such vacancy ;

acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sandanam 
Johnson Paul was selected as the member of the Horana Urban Council.
