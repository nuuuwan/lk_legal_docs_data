Notices Under the Local Authorities Election Ordinance
UhANA PRADEShIYA  SABhA
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamad Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section 
10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Nimal Bandara 
of the Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the  
Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
 M. B. M. Sufyan,
 Returning Officer,
 Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha.
 
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
21st of February, 2022.
