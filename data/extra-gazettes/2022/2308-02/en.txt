PRADESHIYA  SABHA  -  KOBEIGANE
IT is hereby notiﬁ ed for public information that it has been adopted under resolution No. 5/x.   moved at the General Conference 
held on 10.11.2022 at Pradeshiya Sabha kobeigane  that all the roads and thoroughfares for which legal action had not been 
ﬁ led in a proper  judicial Court before the date speciﬁ ed  or roads and thoroughfares conﬁ rmed as the roads and thoroughfares 
that should be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha kobeigane as per the Decree of a Judicial Court in which a case had been ﬁ led 
before the said date and not mentioned in the following schedule from among the roads and thoroughfares given in the list of 
the schedule of the notiﬁ cation published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 2282/02 and dated 30.05.2022 according 
to the resolution No. 05/4 made at the Pradeshiya Sabha conference held on 20.10.2022, shall be con ﬁ rmed as roads and 
thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha kobeigane in terms of orders made by the series of “ Orders on publication of 
notiﬁ cations specifying preparation of plans and lists for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha within 
the North Western Province” published by Extraordinary  Gazette Paper No. 2238/35 and dated 30th July 2021 by the Hon. 
Governor in the North Western Province and in terms of the order by Section 24(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 0f 1987.  
 It is further notiﬁ ed that alist of said roads and thoroughfares has been included in the schedule hereto and all the roads and 
thoroughfares mentioned in that schedule shall be considered as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
kobeigane  under Sub Section (5) of Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act.   
 W. M. S
URESH KUMARA WARNASOORIYA, 
   Chairman, 
 Pradeshiya Sabha kobeigane. 
At Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha,                                             
25th of November 2022.
