STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the salary of Mrs. M. D. Seetha Ranjani who worked at the State Printing Corporation can be adjusted from 
01.11.2018 and if the salaries can be adjusted whether she is entitled for the salary increments bonuses and arrears of salaries 
for 2018/2019 and 2020 and if she is entitled for these whether she has been caused injustice by not being given them and 
if so, to what relief she is entitled.
