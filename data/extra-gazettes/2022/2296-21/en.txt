already acquired, Whether their names stated in the Gazette

compensation and computation details of such amount in
writing.

of inquiry and the relevant documents to be submitted at the
inquiry such as deeds may duly informed by me.

land must be duly authorized in writing. Without such a letter
statement or evidence may not be recorded from such an
agent.
