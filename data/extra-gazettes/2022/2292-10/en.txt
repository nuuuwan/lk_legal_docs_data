WHEREAS, the office of return member of Morawewa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a 
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rajakaruna 
Mudiyanselage Suramya Wijesingha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Morawewa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ranghawadi Gedera Kanchana Gunasekara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer of the Morawewa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ranghawadi Gedera Kanchana 
Gunasekara was selected as the member of  the Morawewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
