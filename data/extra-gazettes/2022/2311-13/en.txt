PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF   a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF   member  oF   the  rideegama  Pradeshiya 
sabha  under  section  66a(1)( a)  oF   the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance 
(chaPter  262)  
whereas, the office of member of 12- Iriyagolla of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death.
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Rajasinghe 
Dewage Wedakara Gedara Rahula Sarath Munasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of 
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that No. 12- Iriyagolla of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs 
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who 
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Gamage Anura Ranjith Perera to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, returning officer of the Rideegama Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Gamage Anura Ranjith Perera was selected as the member of the No.12- Iriyagolla of Rideegama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
