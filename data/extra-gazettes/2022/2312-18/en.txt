WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which Weerasooriya Mudiyanselage Gnanawathi Kumarihami 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member 
of Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days ;
 And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Liyana 
Pedige Kumudini Munasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of the Mahawa 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Liyana Pedige Kumudini Munasinghe was selected as the member of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
