ImPORTS AND EXPORTS CONTROL  ACT, NO. 1 Of 1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-section 4(1) and Section 14 of the Imports and 
exports (control) act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by act, No. 48 of 1985 and act, No. 28 of 1987, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, 
the Minister of Finance, economic Stabilization and National Policies promulgate following regulations.
Ranil WickRemesinghe,

National Policies.

colombo 01,

Regulations
1. these regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 20 of 2022”. 
2. the Special Imports License regulations, published in the Gazette No. 2044/40 dated November 09, 2017, as amended 
time to time, are hereby further amended.

3. Importation of following goods, classified under the HS Heading of 88.03 and listed out in the Schedule I of the  
Special Imports License regulations referred above, by any Approved International Airline Operators for the 
purpose of Repairs/maintenance of Aircrafts within the approved Customs Bonded Area, on the recommendation 
of the chairman or director General of the civil aviation authority of Sri Lanka, shall be exempted from the Imports 
control License (IcL) requirement.

4. The recommendation of the Civil Aviation Authority, as specified in the Regulation No. 3, shall be submitted to;
(a) the Sri Lanka customs for customs clearing purpose, and
(b) their licensed bank to release shipping documents and/or establish Letter of credit (Lc) and for effect the payment.
5. the controller General, department of Imports and exports control shall issue required “operating Instructions” to 
the director General, Sri Lanka customs and licensed banks for effective implementation of these regulations.
