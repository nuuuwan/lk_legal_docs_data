NOTICE NO : 53/0271 KALUTARA DISTRICT 
  
IT is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner 
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 530200 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Wewala (Block 04) 
or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Division 
of No.615G Wewala East in the Divisional Secretariat Division of 
Horana in the District of Kalutara in the Province of Western 
Province should submit his claim to the undersigned before 9th of 
March 2022 In the event of failure  to submit any claim before the 
above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte 
inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such

of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 530200 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office.

the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
