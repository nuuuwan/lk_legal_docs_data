Notice under  Section  7 
 
D. S. Ref. Number.: DSM/YT/LAND/5/17. 
Ministry of Lands Ref No.: 4-3/17/2019/LO/162. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 
that the Government intends to acquire the following 
land/ servitude which is requir ed for establishment of the 
Police Station, Yatawatte. 
 
 
Description of the land/ servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
An allotment in extent of 0.6847 Hectares of the land 
called Yatawatte Estate depicted as Lot No. 01 in the 
Preliminary Plan No. MA 1859 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the Village called Yatawatte in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Maligathenna in Asigiri 
Pallesiya Pattu Minor Division in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Yatawatte in Matale District of the 
Central Province.  
 
 
 Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Yatawatte Estate; Description: 
Acquisition of an allotment of land for establishment of 
the Police Station, Yatawatte; Claimant: Udupussallawa 
Plantations Pvt Ltd; Extent: 0.6847  Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Yatawatte on 22.08.2022 at 9.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing in duplicate on 
or before 01.08.2022 the nature of their interest in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
