WHEREAS the office of return member of Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party of Sri Lanka, the recognized political party to which Kiththambahu 
Appuhamilage Kumuduni Jinadasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under 
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within 
a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate                          
Karadana Lekamlage Rathnawali Karadana to fill such vacancy;
Therefore I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, returning officer of the Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Karadana Lekamlage Rathnawali 
Karadana was selected as the member of the Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
Suranga Ambagahathenne,
Returning Officer,
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Elections Office, 
Ratnapura.
