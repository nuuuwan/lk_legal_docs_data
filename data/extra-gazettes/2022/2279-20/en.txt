WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Alager 
Selvakumaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Devit Jesudasan Kingsley Prasanna Kumara to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Devit 
Jesudasan Kingsley Prasanna Kumara was selected as the member of the Kandy Municipal Council.
