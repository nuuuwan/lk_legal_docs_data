Order No. : 83
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF 2003  (Amended)
Order under Section 20(5) 
aCTiNG under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer affairs authority act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer 
affairs authority orders that no Manufacturer, Producer, Supplier, Distributor or Trader shall Sell, Supply, expose or Offer 
for sale, Display for sale the variety of rice listed below, exceeding the Maximum retail Price given hereunder. 
Variety Maximum  Retail Price 
White/red raw rice (Local) rs.210 /- per kg
This Order shall come in to effect from 10th june, 2022.
