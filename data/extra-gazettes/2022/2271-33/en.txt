THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of below mentioned lands, depicted 
in Advance Tracing No. GA/DOM/2020/608 and dated 
19.03.2021, prepared by the Surveyor, General situated in the 
villages of  Kapugoda and Malinda in the Grama Niladhari 
Divisions of  No. 418A, Kapugoda and No. 407 B, Malinda East  
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Dompe  in Gampaha 
District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot  'A' -  Kahatagahawatta - Extent: 0.0065 Hectare
 North: Kahatagahawatta claimed by I. W. M. P. 
Wickramathilaka and Road (Road Development Authority); 
East: Road (Road Development Authority); South: Road 
(Road Development Authority) and Lot B; West: Lot B, 
Galabodawatta claimed by I. W. M. P. Wickramathilaka and 
Kahatagahawatta claimed by I. W. M. P. Wickramathilaka.
 02. Lot  'B' -  Galabodawatta - Extent: 0.0047 Hectare
  North: Galabodawatta claimed by R. P. K. Deshappriya,  
Kahatagahawatta claimed by I. W. M. P. Wickramathilaka  and 
Lot A; East: Lot A and Road (Road Development Authority); 
South: Road (Road Development Authority) and Galabodawatta 
claimed by R. P. Siriyawathie; West: Galabodawatta claimed 
by R. P. Siriyawathie and Galabodawatta claimed by R. P. K. 
Deshappriya.
