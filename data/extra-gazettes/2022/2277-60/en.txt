under  Section  66b(2)  of  the  local  authorities  elections ordinance  (chapter 262)  as  amended  by  
local  authorities  elections  (amendment) act,  no.  16  of  2017  
whereas, the Office of Vice Chairman in Hikkaduwa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman been 
appointed as Chairman ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Minal 
Sembakutti, the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B (2) of the aforesaid ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of  that Hikkaduwa Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be  declared elected as 
Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Waduge Priyantha Kumara De Silva to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Hikkaduwa Urban Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities 
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Waduge Priyantha Kumara De Silva elected as Vice Chairman 
of the Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
W. H. R. Wijaya KumaRa,
Returning Officer,
