WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress the recognized political party to which Ariyanayakam 
Jegatheeswary the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rasakulam Lalithavathy to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasakulam 
Laithavathy was selected as the member of the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. AmAlRAAj,
Returning Officer,
