Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  thunukkai    pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66a (1)(a) oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of member of Thunukkai Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending council 
meetings for a period of three months without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which 
Kanapathippillai Niruban the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon  member of that Ward No. 08 Bharathinagar of Thunukkai  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Subramaniyam Raththinasingam to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Thunukkai Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Subramaniyam 
Raththinasingam was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 08 Bharathinagar of Thunukkai Pradeshiya Sabha.
