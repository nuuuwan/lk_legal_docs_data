ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 25A
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,  I,  dr. A. d. susil Premajayantha, 
the minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the sri Lanka 
Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited (sLIIt), a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the companies 
Act,  No. 7 of 2007, and bearing registration Number GL 24, being recognized as a degree Awarding Institute, do by this 
Order and subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognise the branch located at 5th Floor, No. 24, E. H. 
cooray tower, Anagarika darmapala mawatha, matara (matara branch) of sri Lanka Institute of Information technology 
(Guarantee) Limited, to offer the first two years of the degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which 
the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in column II of the schedule II hereto, for the purpose of developing 
Higher Education therein : -
SCHEduLE I
Conditions
1. s ri Lanka Institute of Information technology (Guarantee) Limited (sLIIt) - matara branch, shall be recognized to
offer the first two years of the degree specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to the five (5) year re-review conducted
by the Specified Authority from the date of publication of this Order.
