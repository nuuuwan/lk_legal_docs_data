Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  pitabeddara    pradeshiya  
sabha  under  section  66a (1) oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of return member of  Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, to which Gigummaduwa Kankanamge Ajith 
Wasantha Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a 
period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Munidasa Kandambige to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Munidasa Kandambige was selected as the member of  the Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha.
 T. W. P
oshiTha Kaumadi,
 Returning Officer,
 Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Matara District,
