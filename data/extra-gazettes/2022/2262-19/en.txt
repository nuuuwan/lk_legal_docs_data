PORTS  AND  AIRPORTS  DEvELOPmENT  LEvY  ACT,  No. 18  Of 2011
Order under Section 3
By virtue of powers vested in me by sub-section (3) of section (3) of section 3 of the Ports and airports development 
Levy act, No. 18 of 2011, I, Basil rajapaksa, Minister of Finance do hereby this Order, amend the Order published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2152/32 dated December 05, 2019 by,
 I. Inserting to Schedule I of said Order, of the articles specified in Column III corresponding to the HS Headings and 
HS Codes specified in Columns I and II and Concessionary Rate specified in Column IV of the Schedule I hereto ; 
and
 II. Inserting to Schedule III of said Order, of the articles specified in Column III corresponding to the HS Headings and 
HS Codes specified in Columns I and II and Exemptions specified in Column IV of the Schedule II hereto.
This Order shall come in to effect from January 12, 2022. 
 B
asil Rajapaksa,
 Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
11th January, 2022.
