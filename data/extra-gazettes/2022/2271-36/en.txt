WHEREAS the office of return member of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Sivakumaran 
Kajen the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned 
member of Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local 
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty 
(30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rasenthiram Suthahar to fill such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasenthiram 
Suthahar was selected as the member of the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
