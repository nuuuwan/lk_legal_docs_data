I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare 
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Pathirana Hewage Champika Kushan 
Gunasinghe of the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased 
to be a member of the United People's Freedom Alliance.
