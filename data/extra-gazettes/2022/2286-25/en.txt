Cancellation of Licence issued to Prime Finance PLC, due to amalgamation with HNB Finance PLC under the Finance 
Business Act, No 42 of 2011.
 dr. P. NaNdalal WeerasiNghe,
 Chairman of the Monetary Board and the
 Governor of the Central Bank of sri Lanka.
THe public is hereby informed that, due to the amalgamation of Prime Finance PLC with HNB Finance PLC as part of the 
Masterplan for Consolidation of Non-Bank Financial Institutions, the Monetary Board of the Central Bank of sri Lanka in 
terms of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the Licence issued to Prime Finance PLC of No. 61, d. s. 
Senanayake Mawatha, Colombo 08, to carry on finance business, with effect from 12.05.2022, Prime Finance PLC has not 
accepted deposits from the public with effect from 12th May, 2022.
