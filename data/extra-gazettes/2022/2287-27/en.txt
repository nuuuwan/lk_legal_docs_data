Delegation of Powers under the Co- operative Societies Statute of Western Provincial 
Council No. 03 of 1998
I, Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of Western Province, according to the powers vested upon me under the Section 2(2) 
of the Co-operative Societies Statute of Western Provincial Council No. 03 of 1998; that shall be read with the Act, 154 (c) 
of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby notify that powers specified under the Column 
III of its Schedule will be delegated to the post holders mentioned in Column II, corresponding to the names of Column I in 
the following Schedule, that are appointed to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative 
development of Western Province 
and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies of the Western Province with effect from 04th july 2022. 
Roshan Goonetileke,
RWP BarVSV USP
PhD (HON), FIM(Sl), ndc, psc, 
Marshal of the Sri lanka Air Force 
Governor of Western Province.
At the Office of the Governor of Western Province,
located at the Western Provincial Council Building,
Battaramulla.
04th july, 2022.
