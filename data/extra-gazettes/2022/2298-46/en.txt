PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
Filling  oF  a  v acancy  in  the  oFFice  oF  member  oF  the  nivithigala  pradeshiya 
sabha  under  section  66a(1)  oF  the  local  authorities  elections  ordinance  
(chapter  262)
WHEREAS, the office of  return Member of Nivithigala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to 
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Polwathtalage Piyasena 
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member 
of  Nivithigala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities 
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ranasinghe Arachchillage Ajantha Sisira Kumara Ranasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Nivithigala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ranasinghe Arachchillage 
Ajantha Sisira Kumara Ranasinghe was selected as the member of  the Nivithigala Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 S
uranga ambagahathenne,
 Returning Officer,
 Nivithigala Pradeshiya Sabha. 
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
21st September, 2022.
