FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  YATINUWARA PRADESHIYA 
SABHA  UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE 
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS, the ofﬁ ce of return member of  Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front the recognized political party to which Neel
Wickramarachchi the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disquali ﬁ ed under section 9
of the local authorities  elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
R. Y . G. N. Wimalasena to ﬁ ll such vacancy;
Therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, returning ofﬁ cer of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of section 66A(1) of the local authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that R. Y . G. 
N. Wimalasena was selected as the member of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha
.
 Dr . H. D. N. SAMPATH FERNANDO,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Kandy,
25th of May, 2022.
