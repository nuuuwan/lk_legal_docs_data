ORDER
   The Chairman of Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha in Trincomalee district and Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha in Ampara district 
within the province of the East has submitted and failed to get adopted the Budget of the year 2022, as the power vested in 
him under Section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, thus the said Chairman of the said Pradeshiya 
Sabha not complied, as provided under Section 17 of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. No. 21 of 2012, is 
deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman effective from 01.01.2022 and that the office of the Chairmen 
of that Pradeshiya Sabhas become vacant effective from that date.

RULE 
   Whereas the Budget of the year 2022 of the Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha in the district of Trincomlaee and Lahugala 
Pradeshiya Sabha in the district of Ampara within the limits of Eastern Province Provincial Council was failed to adopt as 
required under Section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987. Thus the officers of  the Chairmen of the 
Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha and Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabhas hereto became vacant in accordance to Section 17 of the Local 
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and hence the Chairmen be appointed to that vacant position incompliance 
to Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit a Budget for the year 2022 
to the Kinniya and Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha hereto. As such I, authorize Assistant Commissioner of Local Government 
of the Trincomalee district to Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha and Assistant Commissioner of Local Government of the Ampara 
district to Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabha to grant approval to the following proposals proposed in the aforesaid draft Budget 
during the period commencing from 01.01.2022 till such a date on which a Chairmen are appointed to that vacant positions 
of the Kinniya and  Lahugala Pradeshiya Sabhas.
Proposals
1.Salaries and Wages
2. Travelling Allowances
3. Stationeries
4. recurrent Expenses
5. LdSP & CdLG Project Expenses
6. Provincial Specific Development Grand & Criteria Base Grand

ORDER
The Chairman of Kinniya urban Council in Trincomalee district within the Province of the East has submitted and failed 
to get adopted the Budget of the year 2022, as the power vested in him under Section 178 and 178A of the urban Council 
ordinance (Chapter 255) thus the said Chairman of the said urban Council not complied, as provided under Section 12 of the 
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman 
effective from 29.12.2021 and that the office of the Chairman of that Urban Council become vacant effective from that date.
