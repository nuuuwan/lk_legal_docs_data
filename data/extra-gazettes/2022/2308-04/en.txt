CONSTITuTION   Of  ThE  DEmOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPubLIC   Of  SRI  LANkA   
Order  under  Article  98(8)  of  the  Constitution
IN terms of Section 18 of the registration of electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, the main electoral register for the year 2022 
related to the revision of the Electoral Registers for the year 2022 was certified on 31st October 2022. 
02. In pursuance of the powers vested in the election Commission under Article 98(8) of the Constitution of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election Commission by this Order certify that the number set out in Column II of the 
Schedule hereto be the number of members which each electoral district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of 
the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation under Article 97 and Article 98.
