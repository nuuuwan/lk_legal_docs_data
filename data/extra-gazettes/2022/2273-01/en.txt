SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT,  NO. 48 Of 2007
Order under Section 5
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil rajapaksa, 
minister of Finance of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby, make an order to waive-off  balance amount 
after collecting rs. 1.00 per Kg from the applicable Special commodity Levy of rs. 200 per Kg on importation of dates, 
classified under the HS Codes of 0804.10.10 and 0804.10.20, levied by the Gazette Notification No. 2270/19 dated march 
09, 2022 under Section 2 of the Special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
This order shall be valid for a period of 05 months effective from march 28, 2022 subject to provisions of Sub Section 
(3) of Section 2 of the Special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Basil Rajapaksa,
minister of  Finance.
ministry of Finance,
