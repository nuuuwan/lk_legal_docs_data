ThE UNIvERSITIES  ACT, No. 16 Of 1978
Order Under section 25a
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25a of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, dinesh Gunawardena, the 
Minister of education, having obtained a report under section 70c of the aforesaid act, in respect of the sri Lanka Institute 
of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the companies act, No. 7 of 
2007 and bearing registration number GL 24, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order, amend 
the schedule II to the Order made under the said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2216/39 of February 
27, 2021, by the addition immediately after the entry "Postgraduate diploma in education" appearing in column I and its  
corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule 
II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
SChEDULE I
Conditions
  sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Gurantee) Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree                  
programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours in Social Sciences, adhere to 
the minimum entry qualification of three (3) 'simple passes' (S) in Commerce subject stream or Arts subject stream in one 
and the same sitting at the Gce (advanced Level) examination or equivalent.
