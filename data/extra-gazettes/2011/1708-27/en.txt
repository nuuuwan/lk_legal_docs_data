PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW  No.  1  OF  1972
Order   under   subsection  (4)  of  Section  27A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (4) of section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No.1 of 1972 as amended by
the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby –
(a) amend the Order made under subsection (2) of section 27A of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 815/10 of April 21, 1994 relating to the land called and known as Bentota alias Bentota Watta
morefully described in the First Schedule to this Order and which had vested in the Sri Lanka State Plantations
Corporation established by the Sri Lanka State Plantations Corporation Act, No. 4 of 1958, by revoking on
grounds of non-compliance with such terms and conditions relating to consideration for the vesting in such
Corporation, such portion of the said Order as relates to the extent of land called Atakohata Watta specified in
the Second Schedule hereto which is part of the land called and known as Bentota alias Bentota Watta;
(b) state that upon revocation, such extent of land shall re-vest in the Land Reform Commission;
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
31st May, 2011.
