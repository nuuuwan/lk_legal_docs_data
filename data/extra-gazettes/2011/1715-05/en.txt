THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.:DS/Ak/Adhi/Athi/40.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land

2. Lot No. 3172
Name of   land: Mahaliyaddagodakanda; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State;Original Claimant: R.H. Siripala, No. 400/2,
Agalathollawatta, Dorape, Agulugaha; Extent: 0.0303 Hectare.
