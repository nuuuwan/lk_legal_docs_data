ORDER  UNDER  THE  MERCHANT  SHIPPING  ACT,   No.  52  OF  1971
IT is hereby notified for general information that Hon. Minister of Ports and Highways, in exercising powers vested in him
under Section 323 of the Merchant Shipping Act, No. 52 of 1971, has exempted the vessels MT ‘‘Ocean Pioneer’’ - IMO
No. 9571129 and  MT ‘‘Ocean Perfect’’ - IMO No. 9571131 registered under Tuvalu Flag, which are being chartered by
Sri Lanka Ports Authority, for assisting in Berthing of Vessels in Port of Colombo and Magampura Mahinda Rajapaksa Port,
from the provisions of  Section 114 (1) of the said Act, effective from 01st August 2011 to 29th February, 2012.
S
UJATHA COORAY,
Secretary,
Ministry of Ports and Highways.
Colombo,
29th August,  2011.
