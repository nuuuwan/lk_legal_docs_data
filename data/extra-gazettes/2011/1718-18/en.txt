PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS   ACT,   No. 2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  the
Central  province  Provincial  council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central Province Provincial Council
by reason of Death.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. K.M.G . Thushara Sampath
Swarnathilake to fill such vacancy;
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. K.M.G . Thushara Sampath Swarnathilake, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Kandy in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09th August, 2011.
