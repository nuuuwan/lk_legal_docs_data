Colombo,
08th August, 2011.
Regulation
1. “This regulation may be cited as the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 6 of 2011”.
2. Notwithstanding anything in any other written law for the time being in force, and for so long and so long only as
this regulation is in force, the term of office of Councilors or Members as the case may be, of the Municipal
Councils, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya Sabhas, specified respectively in Schedule I, Schedule II and Schedule III
to these regulations, shall deemed to cease with effect from August 10, 2011.
3. The Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 03 of 2011 published in Gazette Extra
Ordinary No. 1699/35 of March 30, 2011 is hereby rescinded.
