NATIONAL  HOUSING  ACT,  No. 37 OF 1954
Order under Section 25(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 25(2) of the National Housing Act, No. 37 of 1954 and it subsequent
amendments I, Wimal Weerawansa, Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing and Common Amenities do by
this order declare that, with effect from 14th December 2010, the Provisions of Part XII and Part XIII of the Companies Act,
No. 07 of 2007 shall apply to Nations Building Society Limited (formerly Known as Ceylinco Building Society Limited), duly
incorporated under the National Housing Act, No. 37 of 1954 and that this order shall remain in force until rescinded.
