EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ANURADHAPURA    MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Anuradhapura Municipal  Council has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Anuruddhika Dissanayake,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Anuradhapura Municipal Council  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   falied to make a nomination within
the period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I,  Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,   Returning Officer of  the Anuradhapura
Municipal Council,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.   Ranjith Chandrakumara Rajakaruna who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the
member declared elected at the election held on 30th March, 2006 to Anuradhapura Municipal Council,  elected as a member of
the Anuradhapura Municipal  Council in respect of which such vacancy occured.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Anuradhapura Municipal  Council.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura.
27th  May 2011.
