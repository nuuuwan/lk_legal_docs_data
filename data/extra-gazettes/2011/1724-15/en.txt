THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: PE/DS/LA/2/Ath/16.

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the follow-
ing land which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three (03) allotments of  land in total extent of 2.0231 Hectares,
depicted in additional No. 10 in final village plan No. 13 prepared by
the Survey-Superintendent at Rathnapura District, situated in the
village of Gonakumbura in Sub-Division of Nawadun Korale
Medapattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division Pelmadulla in
Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and described as
follows:
Lot No. 134
Name of land: Lansdaunwatta; Description: Scrub; Claimant:
Hapugasthenna Ltd. PLC; Extent: 1.1865 Hectare.
Lot No. 136
Name of land: Lansdaunwatta; Description: Scrub; Claimant:
Hapugasthenna Ltd.; Extent: 0.5904 Hectare.
Lot No. 137
Name of land: Lansdaunwatta; Description: Scrub; Claimant:
Hapugasthenna Ltd.; Extent: 0.2462 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretary’s office, Pelmadulla on the 2nd day of
November, 2011 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th  day of  November,
2011 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Rohana Senarathna,
