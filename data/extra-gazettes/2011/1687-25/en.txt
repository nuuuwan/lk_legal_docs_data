LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections by virtue of powers vested in me by section 27(1) of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Act, No. 24 of 1987, read with Section 4(1) of the said Act, do
hereby appoint the officer specified in Column III of the schedule hereto as Returning Officer of each Local Authority
described in the corresponding entry in Column II of the said Schedule, situated in the Administrative Districts described in
the corresponding entry in Column I of the said Schedule, for the purpose of election of members of the Local Authorities in
the said Administrative District.
