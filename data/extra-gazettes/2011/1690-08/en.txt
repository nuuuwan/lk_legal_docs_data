Government  Notifications
1A
Direction No. 34
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT  No  09 OF  2003
Direction  under  Section  12 (1)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(1) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority directs that no Manufacturer, Distributor or Trader shall manufacture, distribute, store or sell or display for
sale, expose for sale or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any item of locally manufactured Clay Roofing Tiles classified below
unless it bears the SLS Product Certification Mark issued by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution;
i. Flat Roofing Tiles - SLS 2:1975 (2006)
ii. Ridge Tiles - SLS 2:1975 (2006)
This Direction shall come into effect from 1st August, 2011.
By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
25th January 2011.
