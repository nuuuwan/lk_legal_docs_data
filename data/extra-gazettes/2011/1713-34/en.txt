EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   CHIRMAN OF  THE   PORATHIVUPATTU  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Iraththinasingam Mahendiran,   the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the member   of that  Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as a Chairman within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Vinayagamoorthi Siridaran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,   Returning Officer of  the Porathivupattu
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.   Vinayagamoorthi Siridaran elected as Chairman of the Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
27th  June, 2011.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHIRMAN OF  THE   PORATHIVUPATTU

ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in  Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Vinayagamoorthi Siridaran,   the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the member   of that  Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as  Vice Chairman within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Seeralasingam Yogeswaran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,   Returning Officer of  the Porathivupattu
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.   Seeralasingam Yogeswaran elected as Vice Chairman of the Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha.

(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Iraththinasingam Mahendiran,   the memeber  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr. Gunaratthinam Yoganadan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,   Returning Officer of  the Porathivupattu
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.   Gunaratthinam  Yoganadan elected as a member of the Porathivupattu Pradeshiya Sabha.

SANKA RA THNAY AKA,
