EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KANDY  GRA VETS  AND
GANGAWATA KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL
AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kandy  Gravets  and  Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason
of  being ceased to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Surendra  Bandara,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kandy  Gravets  and
Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period
of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.  Nishantha Kumara Ruwan
Pathirana,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  U. Amaradasa,   Returning Officer of  Kandy  Gravets  and  Gangawata Korale   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.   Nishantha Kumara Ruwan Pathirana,  elected as a member of the Kandy Gravets  and  Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets  and  Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
06th  May, 2011.
