No. 1705/20 – TUESDAY,  MAY 10, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.:Kara/7/1/92B/1.
I hereby give notice, under sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 The  allotment of land in total extent of  0.0219 Hectares, depicted
as Lot No. 1 in the Preliminary Plan No.GA3754 and dated
31.03.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the  Village
of Mandakanda, in  the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Karandeniya
in the District of  Galle  in Southern Province morefully described as
follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Mandakanda; Description: Cinnamon Cultivation;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: T. H. Wickramarathne of
No. 886, Mandakanda, Karandeniya; Extent: 0.0219 Hectare.
All perosns interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Karandeniya Divisional Secretariat on the  12th  July, 2011 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the  5th  July, 2011 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
                        H.N.S.T.K. De Silva,
                    Divisional Secretary,
               Karandeniya.
