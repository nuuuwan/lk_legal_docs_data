TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order   under   Section  114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the ‘‘Oru Paanai Trust’’ namely, –
1. Dr. Danial Wijeyarajah Muthuveloe ; and
2. Mr. Somasunderam Skandakumar,
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance
(Chapter 87).
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of
Justice, do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that –
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of Trust bearing No. 1933 of July 26, 2010 attested by Kandiah
Neelakandan, Notary Public of Colombo and shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common
seal under the name and style of the ‘‘Oru Paanai Trust’’;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold take on loan or otherwise acquire,
sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of movable or immovable property of any description
on behalf of the said Trust; and
(c) the Trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and to
be answered to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all causes and actions
arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,
Colombo 01, Minister of Justice.
02nd June, 2011.
