THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose - to acquire the land called Walankande Mukalana
to be maintained as a reservation - and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land shown as Lots ‘A ’ and ‘B’ of land called
Walankande Mukalana in total extent of about 2.8690 Hectares,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. R/KLW/97/246 and dated
28th January, 1999 in final village Plan 943, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Rambuka, in Minor
Division of Uda Pattu in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kalawana, in the District of Ratnapura, Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’ – Walankande Mukalana – Extent: 2.3540 Hectares
North: Lot No. 2 in F. V . P. 943 and F. V . P. 701; East: F. V . P. 701;
South: Lot No. 10 in F. V . P. 943;West: Lot No. 2 in F. V . P . 943.
2. Lot ‘B’ – Walankande Mukalana – Extent: 0.5150 Hectare
North: Lot No. 10 in F. V . P. 943; East: F. V . P. 701; South and
West: Lot No. 2 in F. V . P. 943.
