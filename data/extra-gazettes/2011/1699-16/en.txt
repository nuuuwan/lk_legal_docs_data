EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MAYOR OF  THE  MATARA   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A (1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Mayor in Matara Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary  of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Mr. Upul Nishantha Subawickrama Mallika Vidanaarachchige  the Mayor  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1)
of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Matara Municipal Council who belongs to such party  who
shall be declared  elected as Mayor within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated  his decision to nominate
Mr. Deegoda Gamage Yasarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning Officer of the Matara Municipal Council, acting
in  terms of   Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. Deegoda
Gamage Yasarathna elected as Mayor  of the Matara Municipal Council.
C. A. S
UNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Matara,
21st March, 2011.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MATARA  MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a Member  in Matara Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary  of the Mr. Upul Nishantha Subawickrama Mallika Vidanaarachchige the recognized
political party to which  United People's Freedom Alliance the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid  Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Matara Municipal
Council who belongs to such party  who shall be declared  elected as a Member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Welikada Durage Jayan
Chaminda de Silva  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning Officer of  Matara Municipal Council, acting in
terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. Welikada Durage
Jayan Chaminda de Silva elected as a member of the Matara Municipal Council.
C. A. S
UNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Matara,
21st  March, 2011.
