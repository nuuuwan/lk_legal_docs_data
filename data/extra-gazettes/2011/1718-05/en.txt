THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been re-named as “Deshmanya N. W. J. Mudalige
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the
Subject of Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall
read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J.M. B. JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07,
01st August, 2011.
Schedule
1. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
2. District - Colombo
3. Present Name of the Street - Mudalige Mawatha
4. New Name of the Street - Deshmanya N. W. J. Mudalige Mawatha
5. Road Description - The above road commences from Assesment No. 07 and 49
 and terminates at assesment No. 12 and 67 of the Mawatha
 (Fort Municipal Ward).
