THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 3/2/3/10/244.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose (For Welameda road of
Katuwasgoda road in Veyangoda).
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of land mentioned in Plan No. Gam 3767, prepared by
Survey Superintendent, Gampaha instead of Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Katuwasgoda, in No. 321A Katuwasgoda
Grama Niladhari Division, in Attanagalla Divisional Secretary’s
Division in the District of Gampaha in Western Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Dabugahakubura; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: R. P . Nimal Premasiri, No. 05, Sriyawasa, Katuwasgoda,
Veyangoda; Extent: 0.0054 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Dabugahakubura; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: R. P . Sisira Senarathna, No. 90/1, Katuwasgoda; Extent:
0.0057 Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Dabugahakubura; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: R. P . Nimal Premasiri, No. 05, Sriyawasa, Katuwasgoda,
Veyangoda; Extent: 0.0048 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Attanagalla on the 19th day of September,
2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of September, 2011,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Sunil Jayalath,
