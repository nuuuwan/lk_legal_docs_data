THE MUNICIPAL COUNCILS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 252)
Amendment of Order under section 277(2) (a)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Paragraph (a) of subsection (2) of section 277 of the Municipal Councils
Ordinance (Chapter 252), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby amend
the Order published in Gazette  Extraordinary  No. 1686/ 29 of December 29, 2010, by the substitution for the words and
figures ''March 31, 2011'' of the words and figures ''June 30, 2011''.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
February 28th, 2011.
