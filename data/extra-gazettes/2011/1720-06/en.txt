THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been named as “Kettarama Temple  Road” by the
Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the Subject of Local
Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read with the
Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J.M. B. JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07,

Schedule

 at the Dr. Brittor Babapulle Place.
