  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Eastern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern  Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Death;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which the
member   belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Donsiman Ralalage
Shantha Premarathna   to fill such vacancy ;
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Donsiman Ralalage Shantha Premarathna, elected as a member of the above
Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Ampara  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
03rd  May, 2011.
