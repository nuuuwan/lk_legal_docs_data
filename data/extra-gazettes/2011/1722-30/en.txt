EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KELANIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings of
the  Council for a period of  three months without permission  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Maligaspe Koralage Osanda Dinesh   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Kelaniya   Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.   Alexandar Witharana
Nadeeka Sampath,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning  Officer of  Kelaniya  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.   Alexandar Witharana Nadeeka Sampath,   elected as  a member  of   the Kelaniya    Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
02nd  September, 2011.
