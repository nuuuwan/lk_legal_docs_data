1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  BATTICALOA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Batticaloa  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without  permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance , the recognized political party to which
Piragasam Sahayamani,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Batticaloa   Municipal Council   who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   failed to make a nomination within the
period specified ;
Now therefore,  I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer of  Batticaloa   Municipal Council ,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Chandiresan Johnson
Dilipkumar who had secured the highest number  of  preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election
held on 10th of March 2008 to Batticaloa Municipal Council  elected as a member of the Batticaloa Municipal Council  in respect
of which such vacancy occurred.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa  Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa ,
28th March 2011.
