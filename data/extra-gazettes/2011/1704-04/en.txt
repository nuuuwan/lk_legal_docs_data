EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A MEMBER OF  THE  KANDY GRA VETS   AND   GANGAWATA
KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION
65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of    a member in Kandy Gravets Gangawata Korale    Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of
Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Gamini Wijayabandara     the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person   eligible under the  Ordinance for  Election as a   member of that  Kandy Gravets Gangawata Korale
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as  member   within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr. Goigoda Liyanage
Ravipriya Chamikara being an eligible person under the a foresaid Ordinance   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  U. Amaradasa,  Returning  Officer  of    Kandy Gravets  and Gangawata Korale    Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Goigoda Liyanage Ravipriya Chamikara  elected as  a member  of   the   Kandy Gravets Gangawata Korale    Pradeshiya
Sabha.
U. AMARADASA,
Returning Officer,
 Kandy Gravets Gangawata Korale    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
21st April, 2011.
