THE   PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 40)
REGULATION made by the President under section 5 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40).
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Colombo, President.
20th  June,  2011.
Regulation
1. This regulation may be cited as the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 4 of 2011.
2. Notwithstanding anything contained in the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 03
of 2011 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1695/4 of 28.02.2011, with effect from the dates specified in Column II of the
Schedule to this regulation till the date on which an election is held for the election of members respectively to the Urban
Council and  the Pradeshiya Sabhas specified in Column I of the  Schedule to this regulation under the Urban Councils
Ordinance (Chapter 255) and the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No 15 of 1987, the administration of the affairs of the aforesaid
Council and Sabhas shall be deemed to have been vested in, and be exercised, performed and discharged by, the persons
whose names are specified in Column III of the Schedule to this regulation in place of the officers whose names are specified
in Column IV of such  Schedule.
