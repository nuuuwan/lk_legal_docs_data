THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT,  No.  15  OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Paragraph (b) of section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, I, A. L. M.
Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do hereby amend the Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 453/9 of May 12, 1987 relating to the constitution of the Harispaththuwa Pradeshiya Sabha —
(1)   By the repeal of the word ‘ten’ in the fourth paragraph of the said Order and the substitution thereof, of the word
       ‘seventeen’ ;
(2)  The above Order shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 2011.
A. L. M. ATHAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils.
Ministry of Local Government and Provincial Councils,
Colombo,
 3rd January, 2011.
