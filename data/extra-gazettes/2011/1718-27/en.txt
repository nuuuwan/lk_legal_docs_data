Municipal Councils Ordinance
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2, 3, 5 and 9 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) I, A. L.
M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial councils, do by this Order–
(1) Declare the area specified in part - I of the Schedule hereto, to be a Municipality for the purposes of that Ordinance;
(2) Define the limits of the Municipality to be the limits set out in Part - II of that Schedule;
(3) Constitute a Municipal Council for the Municipality declared under paragraph (I) of this Order and assign the
folowing name and designation to such Council:
"Hambantota Municipal Council"
(4) Prescribe that the Hambantota Municipal Council shall consist of the Mayor, the deputy Mayor and ten other
councilors;
(5) Appoint the first day of November 2011, as the date on which the term of Office of the Hambantota Municipal Council
shall commence.
A. L. M. ATHAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial councils.
Colombo,
11th August, 2011.
