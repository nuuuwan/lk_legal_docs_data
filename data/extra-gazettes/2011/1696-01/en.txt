CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATE  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Walawage Sudharma Karunaratne,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 07th March, 2011, all  duties
of  Customs as well as  other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the   Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235),
shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1695/03 of
28th  February, 2011 is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 14th  March,  2011.
S
UDHARMA KARUNARATNE,
 Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 01,
04th March  2011.
