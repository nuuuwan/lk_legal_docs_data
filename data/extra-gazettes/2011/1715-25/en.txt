Regulation
1. This regulation may be cited as the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 5 of 2011.
2. Notwithstanding anything contained in the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 03
of 2011 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1695/4  of 28.02.2011, with effect from the dates specified in Column II of the
Schedule to this regulation till the date on which an election is held for the election of members to the Pradeshiya Sabhas
specified in Column I of  the  Schedule  to this regulation under the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No 15 of 1987, the administration
of the affairs of the aforesaid Sabhas shall be deemed to have been vested in, and  be exercised, performed and discharged
by, the persons whose names are specified in Column III of the Schedule to this regulation in place of the officers whose
names are specified in Column IV of such  Schedule.
