DRUG   DEPENDANT   PERSONS  (TREATMENT  AND  REHABILITATION)
 Act, No. 54 of  2007
Order  under  Section  2
BY VIRTUE  of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Drug Dependant Persons (Treatment and Rehabilitation) Act, No.
54 of 2007, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the National Dangerous Drug Control Board,
designate the place specified in the Schedule hereto to be a Treatment and Rehabilitation Centre for the purpose of providing
care and rehabilitation to drug dependant persons.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo ,
30th  August 2011.
