EASTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Transfer of Court Fines and Fees Statute No. 01 of  2011 of the Eastern Province Provincial Council
A Statute to provide for transfer of Courts fines and fees imposed by courts and collected under items 36.8 and 36.14 of List
1, Nineth Schedule to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka and the provision under the Eastern
Province Finance Statute No. 1 of  2008 and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidential thereto.
Be it enacted by the Provincial Council of Eastern Province of Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka as follows:
1. This Statue may be cited as “ the Transfer of Court Fines and Fees Statute No. 01 of  2011” and
shall come into effect on receiving the assent of the Governor.
2. (1) The Court fines and fees imposed by Courts of Lw having jurisdiction in the Province shall
be collected by such Courts and credited to the Provincial Fund of the Province in terms of
Chapter XXXI, Part 7 of the Eastern Province Finance Statute No. 01 of  2008.
(2) The Provincial Council shall transfer such fines and fees, imposed by Courts having
jurisdiction within the administrative limits of  local authorities, as provided hereinafter, to
such local authorities, on a claim as prescribed in Annexure I hereof, made in writing by the
Administrative Officer of such Local Authority to the Minister within a period of two years
commencing the first day of January, after the year in which such fines and fees had been
collected.
(3) Any claim under Sub-section (2) of section 2 shall not be valid unless and otherwise such
claim is in conformity with Annexure II hereof and accompanied with the schedule as
prescribed in Annexure I hereof and duly certified by the Registrar of by any other person
named and appointed by the Court.

(4) In this Statute, unless the context otherwise requires,
“Administrative officer” means:-
(a) the Municipal Commissioner in relation to a Municipal Council; and
(b) the Secretary in relation to an Urban Council or a Pradeshiya Sabha;
“Local Authority” means any Municipal Council, Urban Council or a Pradeshiya Sabha and
includes any Authority created and established by or under any law to exercise,
perform and discharge powers, functions and duties corresponding to or similar to
the powers, duties and  functions  exercised, performed and discharged by any such
Council or Sabha;
“Provincial Council” means the Provincial Council established for the Eastern Province
under Article 154 A(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka;
“Minister” means the Provincial Minister in charge of the subject of Finance in the Eastern
Province.
“Fees” means and includes the stamp fee and Court fee remitted as provided in item 36.14
of List I of the Nineth Schedule to the Constitution of  Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka;
“Fines” means and include any fine, pecuniary forfeiture or compensation adjudged upon
any conviction for any crime or offence or for the breach of any enactment by any
Courts as provided under section 2 of the Code of Criminal Procedure;
“Provincial Fund” means Provincial Fund established for the Eastern Province in accordance
with Section 19 of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987.
