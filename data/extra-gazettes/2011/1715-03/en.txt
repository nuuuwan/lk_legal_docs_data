Library Statute No. 04, 2009 of North Central Provincial Council
This statute is implemented as an Amendment to the Library Statute No. 04, 2002 of North Central Provincial
Council.
01. The Phrase, “is the objective of this Statute” is added to the end of the section (b) of No. 02 of
the Original Statute.
02. Sub Section (iv) of section No. 03 of the original statute is deleted.
Part 1
03. Deleting sub sections (e), (f) and (g) of section No. 05 of the original statute and substituting
them with Sub section (e); the sub sections (i), (j), (h), (k), (l), are emended respectively as (f), (i), (j), (g)
and (h).
(L) Maintenance of a high standard of information service through collection and
conservation of printed media including books, newspapers and periodicals and audio visual media and
computerized information, documents, and manuscripts, documents and ola leaf books which are of special
significance to the North Central Province, and providing assistance to the numerous development projects
implemented by the North Central Provincial Council by providing them with the necessary data and
information.

04. The following sections are added under “The functions of the library Services Board” under
No. 06 of the original statute and amended accordingly.
6.1 Training of library staff,
6.2 Counselling Services,
(a) Planning of library buildings and furniture;
(b) Library interior planning;
(c) Policy making;
(d) Application of new technology to libraries;
(e) Counselling to conserve library materials and counselling to establish conservation
centres;
(f) Helping to resolve other professional problems.
6.3 Provision of library resources.
(a) Furniture and equipment;
(b) Books;
(c) Other (audio visual media and electronic medium);
6.4 Making of policies and rules and regulations,
(a) Making library-specifications and policies;
(b) Making rules and regulations;
(c) Classification of libraries into different grades;
(d) Assisting to make schemes of recruitment.
6.5 Detailed services,
(a) Holding exhibitions of books depending on the requests received by other libraries in
the province;
(b) Assisting to conduct lectures, seminars and workshops.
6.6 Other services,
(a) Provision of implements such as registers index cards, book labels etc. to libraries;
(b) Giving instructions to obtain internet facilities;
(c) Activating publication aid projects to encourage the writers in the province.
