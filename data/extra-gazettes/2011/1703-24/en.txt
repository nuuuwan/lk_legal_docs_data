No. 1703/24 – THURSDAY,  APRIL 28, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. : DO/L/1/ACQ/21/SUB/110.
I hereby give notice under  Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired  the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land  acquired:
One (01) allotment of land in total extent of 0.0022 Hectare,
depicted in the P.P. K. 4166 and dated 04.01.2011 prepared by
Superintendent of Surveys, Kalutara District, situated in the village
of Dodangoda in Dodangoda Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Kalutara District in Western Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Batadombagahawatta; Description: Open waste
land; Claimant: State (Original claimant K.K.D.E. Ranaweera,
Thudugala Junction, Dodangoda); Extent: 0.0022 Hectare.
All the persons interested  in the aforesaid land  are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing , before
me at Dodangoda Divisional  Secretary’s  office  on the 29th of June,
2011 at 10.00 A.M. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd of June, 2011 the nature
of their interests in the land,the particulars of their claims for
compensation  for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation  and details of the computation of such amount.
H.W.T.R Wettasinghe,
Divisional Secretary,
Dodangoda.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Dodangoda,
02nd February, 2011.
