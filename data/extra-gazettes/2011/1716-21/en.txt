Name of land: Thalgahawela and Thibirigahamula Waththa;
Description: Paddy field; Claimant: Ronald Norbat Fransis, Gurugoda
Waththa, Malagane; Extent: 0.201 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 156

land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
