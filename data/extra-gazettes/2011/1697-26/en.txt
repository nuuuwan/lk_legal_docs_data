WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance, No. 12 of 1946, read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, I, Prasanna Ranathunga, the Chief
Minister and the Minister in-charge of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of the Western
Province Provincial Council do by this order declare that I have approved to impose and charge : a 10% Entertainment Tax on
entrance fee for entertainment activities stated in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance, No. 12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment
Tax (Amendment) Act No. 27 of 1984, within the limits of the Urban Council, the proposition of which has been passed by
Boralesgamuwa Urban Council on 30.09.2009 as per provisions stated in Section 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No.
12 of 1946.
P
RASANNA RANATHUNGA,
Chief Minister and the Minister in-charge of
 Finance and Planning, Law and Order,  Land,
Education, Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power,
Manpower and Employment and Economic Promotion of
the Western Province Provincial Council.
Srawasthi Mandiraya,
Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
16th March, 2011.
