BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2
of 1980. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-charge of the subject of Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is
urgently required for the purpose of Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation Activities do by this order
declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
