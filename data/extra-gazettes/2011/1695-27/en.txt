THE URBAN  COUNCILS  ORDINANCE
Order
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Paragraph (d) of Section 14 of the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255), I, A. L.
M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do hereby dissolve with effect from First day of July,
2011 the Hambantota Urban Council constituted by the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 329/8 of 10th
August, 1987 in Order to constitute the Hambantota Municipal Council.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government
and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
01st March 2011.
03-519/1
THE  MUNICIPAL  COUNCILS   ORDINANCE
Order
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 9 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252), read with section 18
of the Interpritation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do
hereby amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1639/12 of 02nd February 2010 relating to the Constitu-
tion of Hambantota Municipal Council, by the repeal of the words “First day of April 2011” and the substitution thereof of the
words “First day of July 2011” in paragraph (5) of the said Order.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government
and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
01st March 2011.

THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT,  No. 15  OF  1987
Order
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Paragraphs (a) of  Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with
section 18 of the Interpritation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial
Councils, do hereby amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1421/26 of First December 2005, relating to
the Order in respect of revision of administrative limits of Hambantota Pradeshiya Sabha by the repeal of the words “First day
of April 2011” and the substitution thereof of the words “First day of July 2011” in paragraph (02) of the said Order.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government
and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
01st March 2011.
