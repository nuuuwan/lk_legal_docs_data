REVISION  OF  STATUTORY  DETERMINATION  No.  3596  PUBLISHED UNDER SECTION  19  OF  THE
LAND  REFORM  ACT,  No.  01  OF  1972
STATUTORY  Determination published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
No. 52/5 dated 06th December 1979 under Statutory Determination No. 3596 is hereby cancelled, and portions of lands
indicated in following schedule are hereby revised in order to take action as per the final verdict of the Case No. 13/2004 at
District Court, Kandy which was heard in respect of the estate of Mr. Kattandi Devage Nandasiril Premaratne, who demised
whilst residing at No. 38 Colombo Street, Kandy and demised.

STATUTORY  DETERMINATION  UNDER  SECTION 19  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  ACT,  No.  01  OF  1972
Mr Dharmadasa Nalina Keerthi Welikala residing at Somikandura Waththa, Kalawana has made a statutory determination
under section 18 of the Act, and since at present he has demised, by virtue of the powers vested in the Land Reform
Commission under section 19 of the Act, a statutory determination has been made by the Commission in connection to his
estate determining the portion of agricultural land which the statutory Tenant should be allowed to keep. The portion of
agricultural land which the Tenant was allowed to keep is indicated in the following schedule.
N
IMAL G.  PUNCHIHEWA, ATTORENY-AT-LAW,
Chairman,
No. C 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Land Reform Commission.
Colombo 07,
20th July, 2011.
Schedule
(1) The land of Lot No. 285, 286 in extent 16A, 00R, and 35P out of the land called ‘Kaluwala Thennehena’ depicted in
Final Village Plan No. 243, additional No. 04 prepared by Survey General and situated in the Village Pedikanda in
Minor Divison Medapaththuwa, in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Kukulu Korale in the District of Rathnapura
in Sabaragamuwa Province, and other trees and buildings standing thereon.
(2) The land of Lot No. 287 in extent 26A, 02R, and 00P out of the land called ‘Somikandura Waththa’ depicted in Final
Village Plan No. 243, additional No. 05 prepared by Survey General and situated in the Village Pedikanda in Minor
Division Medapaththuwa, in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Kukulu Korale in the District of Rathnapura in
Sabaragamuwa Province, and other trees and buildings standing thereon.
(3) The land of Lot No. 447 in extent 06A, 00R, and 19P out of the land called ‘Ambagalu ellegoda’ depicted in Final
Village Plan No. 244, additional No. 03 prepared by Survey General and situated in the village Kalawana in Minor
Division Medapaththuwa, in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Kukulu Korale in the District of Rathnapura in
Sabaragamuwa Province, and other trees and buildings standing thereon.
(4) The land of Lot No. 01 in extent 00A, 03R, and 29P out of the land called ‘Morandugalawila Kumbura’ depicted in
preliminary plan No. Co/1791 prepared by Survey General and situated in the Village Horagala in Minor Division
Medapaththuwa, in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Hanwella in the District of Colombo in Western Porvince,
and other trees and buildings standing thereon.
(5) The land of Lot No. 01 in extent 00A, 03R, and 16P out of the land called ‘Polonkanaththa’ depicted in preliminary
plan No. 1792 prepared by Survey General and situated in the Village Horagala in Minor Division Medapaththuwa,
in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Hanwella in the District of Colombo in Western Province, and other trees
and buildings standing thereon.
