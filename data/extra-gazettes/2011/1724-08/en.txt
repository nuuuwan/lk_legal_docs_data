UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made  by  the  Governor of  the Uva  Province
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province :
01. Mr. Sumith Suraweera, Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Council of the Uva
Provincial Council, with immediate effect,
02. Mr. K. D. Sirisena, Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry of Youth Affairs,
Sports, Social Welfare, Estate Infrastructure Development, Power and Energy, Textile Industries and Small
Industries of Uva Province, with immediate effect,
03. Mr. K. D. Sirisena, Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Acting Secretary to the Council of
the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, till Mr. Sumith Suraweera assumes his duties,
who has been appointed as Secretary to the Council of the Uva Provincial Council,
04. Mr. Gunadasa Samarasinghe, Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Acting Provincial
Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 07.08.2011
to 29.10.2011, till the return of Mr. M. P. N. M. Wickramasinghe, to the Island who is the acting Provincial
Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province,
05. Mrs. N. C. Hennayake, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Acting Senior Assistant
Secretary to the Ministry of Finance, Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs,
Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction of
the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to her permanent post, from the date 07.08.2011 to 29.10.2011, till the
return of Mr. M. P . N. M. Wickramasinghe, to the Island who is the Senior Assistant Secretary to the above
Ministry of Uva Province,
