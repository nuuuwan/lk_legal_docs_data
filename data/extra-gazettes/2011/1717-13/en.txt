MUNICIPAL  COUNCILS  ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 2, 5 and 9 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) I, A. L. M.
Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils do by this Order –
(a) Constitute a Municipal Council for the municipality, the area of which is specified in the Schedule to the Order
published in Gazette Extra Ordinary  No. 329/8 of August 10, 1978, and assign the following name and
designation to such Council :–
‘‘Colombo Municipal Council’’;
(b) prescribe that the Colombo Municipal Council shall consist of the Mayor, the Deputy Mayor and fifty one
other Councillors ;
(c) appoint the First day of November 2011 as the date on which the term of office of the Colombo Municipal
Council shall commence.
A. L. M. ATHAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
02nd of August,  2011.
