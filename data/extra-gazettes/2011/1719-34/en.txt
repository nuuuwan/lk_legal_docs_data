PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Provincial  Councils  Notifications
1A
SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice of confirmation the by-law by Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which was prepared by Kuruwita Pradeshiya
Sabha and approved by the Minister assigned the Subject
THIS is to notify in terms of  Sub section (3) of Section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989
read with Section 125 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, that the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council at its meeting held on 10.06.2010
has confirmed the by-law regarding the Removing Garbage, Regulating and maintenance, by-law regarding regularization,
maintenance and controlling of industries related to Gravel, Rocks, Cement, Sand, by-law regarding the regularization and
control of public libraries and by-law regarding minimization and control of the damages due to stray animals, which were
prepared by the Kuruwita Pradeshiya Sabha and adopted by the resolution No. 05(06) dared by virtue of the power vested in
it under Sub section (1) of Section 122 read with Section 126 of  Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987 and approved by the
minister who is assigned the subject of Local Government of the Provincial Council of  Sabaragamuwa Province by virtue of
the power vested in him under the Sub section (II) of Section 123 read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential
Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989 and published in the Section IV(A) of the Gazette No. 1541/11 dated 19.03.2008 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
H
ERA TH P. KULARATNE,
Secretary,
Chief Ministry,
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
27th July, 2011.
