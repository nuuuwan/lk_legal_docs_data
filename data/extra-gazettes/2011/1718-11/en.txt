ORDER  UNDER  THE  MERCHANT  SHIPPING  ACT,   No.  52  OF  1971
IT is hereby notified for general information that Hon. Minister of Ports and Highways, in exercising powers vested in him
under section 323 of the Merchant Shipping Act, No. 52 of 1971, has exempted the vessels MT ‘‘Osren Tide’’ - IMO No.
9527972, MT ‘‘Netherland Tide’’ - IMO No. 9476898 and ‘‘Bourbon Alienor’’ - IMO No. 9390070 registered under foreign
flags, which are being chartered by Cairn Lanka (PVT) Ltd, for petroleum exploration in the Gulf of Mannar, Sri Lanka, from
the provisions of section 114 (1) of the said Act, effective from 01st July 2011 to 30th June 2012.
S
UJATHA COORAY,
Secretary,
Ministry of Ports and Highways.
Colombo,
08th August,  2011.
