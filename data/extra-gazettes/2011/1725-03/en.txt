MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE  IN  CRIMINAL  MATTERS  ACT,  No. 25 OF 2002
Order under Section  2
BY  VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters Act,
No. 25 of 2002, I, Rauf Hakeem, Minister of Justice, do by this Order declare that the provisions of the aforesaid Act shall
apply in respect of the Agreement between the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the
Government of the Kingdom of Thailand made at Bangkok on the thirtieth day of July Two Thousand and Four. This
Agreement made between the Government of Sri Lanka and the Government of Thailand, which is a Non-Commonwealth
Country, deals with mutual legal assistance in criminal matters and concerning inter alia the improvement of effectiveness
on the part of both parties to the Agreement in the investigation, prosecution and prevention of crime through co-operation.
A
BDUL RAUF HAKEEM,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
22nd September,  2011.
