   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice under  Section 10 A  (1)  (a)
ANURADHAPURA MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
I,  Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning  Officer of the Anuradhapura Municipal Council, do hereby
declare  under  Section  10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Anuruddika Dissanayake of  the
Anuradhapura Municipal Council  has vacated her office of  the member of  that  Council as  she has  ceased  to be a  member
of the United  People’s Freedom Alliance.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
  Returning Officer,
Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
