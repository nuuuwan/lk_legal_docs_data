SECURED   TRANSACTIONS  ACT, No. 49 OF 2009
Order under Section  1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 1 of the Secured Transactions Act, No. 49 of 2009, read with paragraph (2)
of Article 44 of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do by this
Order appoint 01st August, 2011, to be the appointed date of for the purposes of coming into operation of the provisions of the
said Act.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
23rd June, 2011.
