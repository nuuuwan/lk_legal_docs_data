THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No.1  OF  1972
Order under Section 22(1)(f)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by paragraph (f) of Subsection (1) of  Section 22 of  the Land Reform Law, No. 01 of
1972 as amended by the Land Reform (Special Provision) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President do by this Order vest in the
Person whose names appears in  Column I of the Schedule hereto and who owned  the said agricultural lands specified in the
corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule hereto, who were under eighteen years of age at the time of the imposition
of the ceiling on agricultural land, the agricultural lands specified in Column II of the Schedule hereto which have not been
alienated to any person under section 22 of the said Act and in respect of which no compensation has been paid under
section 28 of the said Act.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
The Presidential Secretariat, President.
Colombo 01,
28th January  2011.
