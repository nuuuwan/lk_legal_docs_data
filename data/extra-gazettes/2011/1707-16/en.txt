EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   KANDY GRA VETS   AND
GANGAWATA KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason
of  being ceased to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Surendra Bandara the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Akuretiyage Nimal Chandrasiri to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, U. Amaradasa, Returning Officer of   Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Akuretiyage Nimal Chandrasiri, elected as  Chairman  of   the Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
13th May, 2011.
