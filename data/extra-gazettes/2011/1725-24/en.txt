EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   WALAPANE   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Walapane  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  failing to make and
subscribe an oath or affirmation in  terms of the Constitution of the  Democratic Socialist Rebublic of Sri Lanka   ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mr.  Sisira Pushpa Kumara   Pathirana the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Walapane    Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Annady Perumal
Rajendram,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning  Officer of  Walapane  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   Annady Perumal Rajendram,
elected as  a member  of   theWalapane     Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Walapane   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara- Eliya,
21st  September, 2011.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   NIKAWERATIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Nikaweratiya   Pradeshiya Sabha    has fallen vacant by reason of  failing to make and
subscribe an oath or affirmation in terms of Seventh Schedule of  the Constitution of the  Democratic Socialist Rebublic of Sri
Lanka    ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Aruna  M. P. Jayasinghe  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Nikaweratiya    Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. E. M. Jayathilaka,  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Ananda Lokubandara Rathnayake,  Returning  Officer of  Nikaweratiya
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr.   E. M.  Jayathilaka,   elected as  a member  of   the Nikaweratiya    Pradeshiya   Sabha.
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Nikaweratiya    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
