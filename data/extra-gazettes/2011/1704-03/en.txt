EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  DEPUTY / MAYOR  OF  THE   MATARA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Deputy Mayor  in   Matara   Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Deputy Mayor  been
appointed as Mayor ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognised    political party to which
Deegoda Gamage Yasarathna   the Deputy Mayor  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,   to  decide upon the   member of  that  Matara   Municipal Council   who belongs to  such party  who shall be
declared  elected as    Deputy Mayor   within  a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   Communicated his  decision to nominate
Mr.  Chathurasiri  Jayasinghe Karunarathna to    fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Candauda Arachchige  Suneth Lochana ,  Returning Officer of  Matara   Municipal Council , acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Chathurasiri
Jayasinghe Karunarathna   elected as  Deputy Mayor  of   the Matara  Municipal Council.
C. A. SUNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Matara  Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo,
27th  April  2011.
