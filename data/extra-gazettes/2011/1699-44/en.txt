DEFAULT  TAXES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 16  OF  2010
Order under Section – 1
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Default Taxes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 16 of 2010 read with
article 44 (2) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby
appoint the 01.04.2011 as the date on which the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
