I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.

No. 8, Sheet No. 10, dated on 26th August, 2011, surveyed and

North Central Province with following names and bounded as follows:

All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to

at the Divisional Secretary’s Office at Ipalogama, on the 16th day of
November, 2011 at  2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 09th day of November,
2011, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
