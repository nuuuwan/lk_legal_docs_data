SPORTS  LAW,  No.  25  OF  1973
Order Under section 28
ORDER made by the Minister of Sports under section 28 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973.
MEETHALAWE M AHINDANANDA ALUTHGAMAGE,
Minister of Sports.
Ministry of Sports,
Colombo,
28th February, 2011.
ORDER
The Order made under section 28 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 110/3 of 07th May, 1974 is hereby amended as follows by adding the following new subject at the end of the schedule
amended from time to time.
“All disciplines of Sports Shooting Recognized by International Shooting Sports Federation (ISSF) and
International Olympic Committee (IOC)”.
