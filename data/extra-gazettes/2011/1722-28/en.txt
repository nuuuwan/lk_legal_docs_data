FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  V A VUNIYA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Vavuniya Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Democratic People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Kanthar Thamotharampillai  Linganathan, the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Vavuniya Urban Council  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr. Suwampillai Thevakingsly
Rajeswaran  being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarappillai Suthakaran,  Returning  Officer  of   the V avuniya Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Suwampillai Thevakingsly
Rajeswaran elected as a member of the  Vavuniya Urban Council .
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
01st September, 2011.
