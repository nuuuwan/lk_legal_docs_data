EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KOLONNA WA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kolonnawa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason  of  resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Aruna Nishantha Perera,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kolonnawa Urban Council  who belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.   Morawakage
Chamila,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I,  Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,   Returning Officer of  the Kollonnawa
Urban Council,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.   Morawakage Chamila,  elected as a member of the Kolonnawa Urban Council.
J. A. S. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Kolonnawa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo,
28th  March 2011.
