THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LA W  NO. 41  OF  1978
Order under section 15(1)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under section 15 (1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence de by this Order, vest with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from the
date of this notice the land described in the Schedules hereto, belongs to the Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Ministry of Defence, Minister of Defence.
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,

SCHEDULE
The land is containing an extent of 0A-0R-19.17P (0.0485 Ha.) depicted as Lot No. 19 in P Plan Co.7032 dated 08.07.1991
prepared by Survey General situated at No. 3, Welikada Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Sri Jayawardenapura
Kotte, within the Muncipal Council limits of Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte, in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
The Land is bounded as follows :
North - Lot No. 18 in P Plan No. Co. 7032 ;
East - Lot Nos. 15 and 16 in P Plan No. Co. 7032 ;
South - Lot No. 20 in P Plan No. Co. 7032 ;
West - Sarana Mawatha.
