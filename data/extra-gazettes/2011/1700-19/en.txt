Regulations
The Landing of Fish Regulations 1997 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 972/4 of April, 21st, 1997,  as amended
last by Regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1555/13 of June, 26, 2008, are hereby further amended by the
repeal of paragraph (2) and paragraph (3) of Regulation 2 thereof, and the substitution therefore of the following paragraphs
:
“(2) Any fish taken outside Sri Lanka waters under the authority of the landing permit issued under paragraph (1),
shall not within the Sri Lanka waters be transferred, reloaded or transhipped to any local or foreign fishing boat:
Provided however, the above provisions shall not apply to a transfer, reloading or transhipment of fish from local or
foreign fishing boats by mother vessels duly authorized for such purpose by Director General.
(3) Ten percent (10%) of the total weight of the tuna fish landed at any Commercial Harbour, Fishery Harbour,
Anchorage or Fish Landing Centre in Sri Lanka under the authority of a landing permit issued under paragraph (1) shall, at the
time of landing, be made available to the Ceylon Fisheries Corporation for purchase at a price to be agreed by such corporation,
The aforesaid ten percent of the total weight of  tuna fish shall consist of five percent (5%) of fresh tuna and five percent (5%)
frozen tuna”
