THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:4/3/1/129.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land

 Two (2)  allotments of  land in total extent of  0.0083 Hectares, as

25.02.2011 prepared   by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the
Village  of  Dodammulla, in the Minor Division of Kalutara Thotamuna

1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Batadombagahawatta; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State;  Original Claimant: G. D. Hemathunga Perera of
Dodammulla, Galpatha; Extent: 0.0082 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Batadombagahawatta; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State;  Original Claimant: A. D. S. Shanthi of “Anura
Storse”, No. 893, Panapitiya, Waskaduwa; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare.
All perosns interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Kalutara Divisional Secretariat on the 22nd  July 2011 at 10.00
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the  15th  July 2011 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars or their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
Sirisoma Lokuwithana,
