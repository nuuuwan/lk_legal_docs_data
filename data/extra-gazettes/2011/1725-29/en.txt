PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No. 41  OF  1978  OF  THE
NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order  under Section  15
BY  VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under Section 15 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 of the
National State Assembly, I Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence, do by this Order, vest with the Urban Development
Authority, with effect from 29.09.2011, the land situated in Cinnamon Garden ward within the limits of the Municipal Council
of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land belonging to the Colombo Municipal Council
excluding the area presently being used for the Public Library and the area reserved for the monument and its functions.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence.
Ministry of Defence,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
29th  September, 2011.
SCHEDULE
Land in extent of Fifty-four (54) Acres (approximately,) excluding the area presently being used for the Public
Library and the area reserved for the monument and its functions, shown as Lot No. 1 in Crown Land Plan No. 2 in Cinnamon
Garden Ward dated 18.02.1948 prepared by the Surveyor General situated within the Cinnamon Gardens Ward in the Colombo
Municipal Council limits in the District of Colombo, Western Province, bounded on the North by Dharmapala Mawatha
(Turret Road) and F R Senanayake Mawatha ; East by Dr. C. W. W. Kannangara Mawatha ; South by Ananda Kumaraswami
Mawatha; West by Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha.
