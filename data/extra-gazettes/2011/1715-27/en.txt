EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   KANDY GRA VETS   AND
GANGAWATA KORALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by
reason  of  Vice Chairman has been appointed as Chairman ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Akuretiyage Nimal Chandrasiri  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  65A(1) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Ehalamalpe Mudiyanselage Jayatissa Bandara Ehalamalpe to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, U. Amaradasa, Returning Officer of   Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of  Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Ehalamalpe Mudiyanselage Jayatissa Bandara Ehalamalpe, elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Kandy Gravets and Gangawata
Korale  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
15th July, 2011.
