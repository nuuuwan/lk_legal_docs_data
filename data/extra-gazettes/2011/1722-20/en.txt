UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Proclamation   by the Governor of  Uva Province
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me under  the  13th  Amendment and under  section 154 b (8) (b) of  the
Constitution of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I,  Caluadewagey  Nanda  Mathew, the Governor of  Uva
Province, do  hereby  this proclamation  prorogue  the First  Session of  the  Fifth Uva  Province  Provincial Council  with  effect
from midnight of  08th  September, Two Thousand Eleven.
C. NANDA MA THEW,
Governor of  the Uva Province.
Governor’s Office,
Badulla,
08th  September, 2011.

UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Proclamation   by the Governor of  Uva Province
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in  me under  the  13th  Amendment and under  section 154 b (8) (a) of  the
Constitution of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I,  Caluadewagey  Nanda  Mathew, the Governor of  Uva
Province, do  hereby  this proclamation  summon the  Second  Session  of  the  Fifth Uva Province  Provincial Council  to meet
at 10.00 a. m. on the 22nd day of   September, Two Thousand Eleven, at the Provincial Council   meeting Hall,  Badulla.
C. NANDA MA THEW,
Governor of  the Uva Province.
Governor’s Office,
Badulla,
08th  September, 2011.
