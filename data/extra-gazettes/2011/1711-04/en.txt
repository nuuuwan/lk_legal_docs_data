FISHERIES  AND  AQUATIC  RESOURCES  ACT,  No.  02  OF  1996
REGULA TIONS made by the Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development under section 61 of the Fisheries
and Aquatic Resources Act, No. 02 of 1996.
DR. RAJITHA SENARATNA,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development.
Colombo,
13th June 2011.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Nilwella Fisheries Regulations No. 1 of 2011.
2. The Fishing gear to be used by any person who engages in fishing activities within the Nilwella fisheries
harbour area to catch fish, shall be restricted to only the rod and line fishing gear.
3. Any person who contravenes the provisions of these regulations shall be guilty of an offence.
4. In these regulations –
‘‘Nilwella fisheries harbour area’’ means the area of one point five (1.5) nautical miles in the territorial water, between
Nilwella point or Berawagala situated in Dugahawella up to Hanwella in Nilwella area.
