WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Service Minute  of  the  Western   Province   Public  Management  Assistant    Service
AMENDMENT No.  03
THE Service minute of  the Western  Province  Public Management Assistant Service which  has  been  published in the
Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1385/10 is decided to be amended as follows :
The following   sub-section  will be inserted subsequent to the sub- section  14 :3:4
(a) Employees  who have got  recruited to the Western  Province Public  Management Assistant  Service prior
to the date 23.03.2005 and are  to retire before 01.07.2012, will be  exempted from the  completion   of  the
second language requirement enforced in their service minutes.
(b) All the others shall accomplish the second language requirement stated in the service minute of the
Western Province  Public Management Assistant  Service.
(c) Officers  of  the Western  Province  Public Management Assistant  Service shall be given  the right to earn
their increments and to obtain relevant  promotions  without completing the second language requirement
only till 01.07.2012.
