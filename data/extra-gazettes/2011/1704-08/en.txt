PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Order No. 16 .
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09 OF 2003
Order  under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003,
the  Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader, importer  or distributor shall sell,  expose or offer
for  sale, display for  sale  any  Full Cream  Milk Powder above  the  maximum  retail prices given below.
Maximum Retail Price
400 g pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 264.00
1 kg pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 647.00
All other pack sizes of Full Cream Milk Powder below 400g shall not be sold for more than the proportionate price of
Rs. 66.00 per 100g and pack sizes above 400g shall not be sole for more than the  proportionate price of Rs. 64.70 per 100g.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 13 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1658/39 dated 20th June, 2010 issued in respect of the Maximum Retail Price of  Full Cream Milk  Powder.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
 Chairman.
Colombo,
02nd May, 2011.
