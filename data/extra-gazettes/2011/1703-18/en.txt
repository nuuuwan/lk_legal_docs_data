Local Authority Notification
IT is hereby notified that the draft by laws on offensive or dangerous and offensive and dangerous trades, itinerant vendors,
parking vehicles in the Pradeshiya Sabha area, destruction of mosquitoes and disease causing insects within the Pradeshiya
Sabha area, decorations on roads and thoroughfares within Pradeshiya Sabha area and lodging and rests in the Pradeshiya
Sabhas of the North Western Province, made by Hon. Minister of Local Government and published in the Gazette  No. 1663
dated 16th July 2010 of  Democratic Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of powers vested in him, had been passed by the
North Western Province Provincial Council held on 18th January 2011 at North  Western Province Provincial Council, in terms
of Sub section (1) of Section (2) of Local Authorities (Standard by- laws) Act No. 06 of 1952 (Chapter 261) to be read with Sub
Section (1) of Section (2) of Provincial Councils (incident Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
W. G. D
