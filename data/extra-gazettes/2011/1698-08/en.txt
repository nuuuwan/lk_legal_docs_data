PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Direction No. 35.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09 OF 2003
Direction  under Section 10(1)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10 (1) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003,
the   Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Manufacturers and Traders that the-
(a) The maximum retail price
(b) Batch Number
(c) Expiry Date
(d) Date  of Manufacture
(e) Net Weight/ V olume
of the product be marked on the article, on the pack or on the container or the wrapper of such articles specified in the
schedule hereto.
This Direction shall come into effect from 1st September 2011.
SCHEDULE
1. Hair Oil,  Hair Gel, Hair Cream, Shampoo, Lotion, Talcum Powder.
2. Dates, Plums sold in packs or containers.
3. Food Colouring, Food Flavours, Food Essences sold in packs or containers.
4. Instant Food sold in packs or containers.
5. Dish wash Bar sold in packs.
6. Car Wash, Windscreen Cleaner sold in Containers.
7. Ayurvedic Products sold in packs or containers.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
 Chairman.
Colombo,
16th March, 2011.
