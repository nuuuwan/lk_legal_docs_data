EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of the powers  vested in me under Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)  Act, No. 12 of
1989, and Section 184 (4A)(a) of the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255) I,  Sivanesathurai Chandrakanthan, the Chief
Minister and the Minister of Local Government of the Eastern Provincial Council do hereby  revoke the previous order
published in the Gazette Notification bearing the No. 1666/28 dated 11th August, 2010, suspending Mr. Shanmugarasa Gowri
Mugunthan the former Chairman Urban Council, Trincomalee with immediate effect.
S
IV ANESATHURAI CHANDRAKANTHAN,
Chief Minister and the Minister of Local Government,
Chief Minister’s Secretariat, Eastern  Province.
Eastern  Province,
Inner Harbour Road,
Trincomalee,
04th day of January, 2011.
