SRI  LANKA  ELECTRICITY  ACT, No. 20 OF 2009
Order under Section 52
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under  Section 52 of the Sri Lanka  Electricity  Act, No. 20 of  2009, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
President upon the  recommendation of the Minister-in-charge of the subject of Power and Energy, do by this Order, declare
that the land specified in the Schedule to this Order –
(a) is in my opinion urgently required for the construction of a power transmission project; and
(b) is required for the construction of the aforesaid power transmission project which is in my opinion, a public
purpose, which would serve the general welfare and benefit of the public.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo.
31 st March, 2011,
