Notification  Under  Section  31 (3)
I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and labour Relations, do hereby give notice that  –
(1) The Hazardous Ocupations Regulations, 2010 were Gazetted in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1667/41 August
20, 2010, in terms of subsection (3) of Section 31 of the aforesaid Act ;
