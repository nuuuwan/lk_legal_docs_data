No. 1719/29 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 18, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land Develop-
ment hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a Village
Extension and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Six (06) allotments of land in total extent of about 32.3739 Hect-
ares, depicted in Advance Tracing No. MO/WLL/2010/396 of 27th
July, 2010, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the village
of Wallawaya, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wallawaya
in Monaragala District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Extent: 7.7050 Hectares
North: Land of Village's Ownership; East and South: Lot F; West:
Galamota Ara.
02. Lot ‘B’ –  Extent: 7.8900 Hectares
North and East: Lot F; South: Kirindi Oya; West: Lot F.
03. Lot ‘C’ –  Extent: 5.4909 Hectares
North and East: Lot F; South: Road and Galamota Ara; West:
Galamota Ara.
04. Lot ‘D’ –  Extent: 5.8373 Hectares
North: Lot F and Galamota Ara; East: Galamota Ara; South: Road
and Lot F; West: Lot F.
05. Lot ‘E’ –  Extent: 4.1167 Hectares
North and East: Lot F; South: Road; West: Land of Village's Own-
ership.
06. Lot ‘F’ –  Extent: 1.3340 Hectares
Boundaries do not mention.
