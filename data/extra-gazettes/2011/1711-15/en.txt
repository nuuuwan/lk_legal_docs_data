No. 1711/15 – THURSDAY,  JUNE 23, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: ath/ KD 108/ WMA.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 of the original plan No. co 9138 prepared by the
Survey superintendent of the Colombo District situated in the 488
Grama Niladari Division of Korathota of Minor Division of Palle
Paththuwa of Hewagama Sub Division of Kaduwela Divisional
Secretary's Division of Colombo District of the Western Province,
which is about 2.0238 Hectares by size and boarders to the lots
mentioned bellow:
Lot No. 01 of the Original Plan No. co. 9138
1. Lot No. 1
Name of  land: Mikewatta or Korathota mount; Description:
Open barren land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant : Unknown ;
Extent: 2.0238 Hectares.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING, SRI LANKA.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary's Office, Kaduwela, on the 21st day of  July,
2011 at 09.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of July, 2011, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
M. S. P . Suriyapperuma,
Divisional Secretary,
Kaduwela.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kaduwela,
