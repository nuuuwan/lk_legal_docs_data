No. 1692/22 – THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 10, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
  My No.: PB/L/Aq/1/20.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotment of land from depicted Lot No. 1652 total extent 0.5909
Hectare, shown in f.v.p. 10 sheet No. 98 supplement 84 MR/PTB/
2010/261 of 27.09.2007 prepared by the Surveyor-General situated
in the village of Weliwa Sub Division of Morawakkorale in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Pitabeddara in Matara District:
Lot No. 1652 – Badahala Liyadda – 0.5909 Hectare
North: Lot No. 154; East: Lot Nos. 154, 156; South: Lot Nos.
154, 156 and Road; West: Lot No. 154.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretary's Office, Pitabaddara  on the 21st
day of March, 2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of March,
2011, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
K. G. R. Jayanath,
Divisional Secretary,
Pitabaddara.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Pitabaddara.
3rd February, 2011.
