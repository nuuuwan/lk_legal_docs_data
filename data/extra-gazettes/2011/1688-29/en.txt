THE Order Numbers 08 (a) and (b) which have been composed by the Minister of Cooperatives, Trade, Food, Water Supply,
Electricity, Sports, Youth, Women's, Cultural and Arts Affairs of the Western Province Provincial Council under Paragraph 04
(9) (b) of the Western Province Sports Statute, No. 01 of 1993 amended by Western Province Sports (Amendment) Statute,
No. 01 of 1998 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1236/13 of 15th May 2002, shall be amended as follows.
These orders shall come into operation from the date of their publication in the Gazette Paper.
H
EWA MATHARAGE UPALI KODIKARA,
Minister of  Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs,
 Cultural and Arts Affairs, Cooperative Development,
Food Supplies and Distribution Affairs of the Western Province.
On the date 14th of January, 2011,
Colombo.
ORDERS
(a) Applying for the registration of Sports Societies a new can be made at any day of the year.
(b) For the annual renewal of registration of the registered sports Societies, applications should be forwarded
along with the following documents within One calendar month from the date of the termination of the
said annual registration.
(i) Four certified copies of the reports of the General Meeting conducted to appoint Office Bearers for
the oncoming year.
(ii) Four copies of the audited Report of Accounts for the previous year.
(iii) Names and addresses of the board of Office Bearers elected for the oncoming year.
(iv) D etails related to the participation in sports competitions, training camps and seminars conducted
by the Divisional Sports Officer.
(v) A list of members including their names and addresses.
