FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  V A VUNIYA  URBAN COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in V avuniya Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai  Tamil   Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
S. N. G.  Nathan , the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Vavuniya Urban Council  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has failed to  make a  nomination  within the
period specified ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarappillai Suthakaran,  Returning  Officer  of   the V avuniya Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ponnaiah Sellathurai  who
had secured the highest  number  of   preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election held on 08th
August 2009 to Vavuniya Urban Council elected as a member of the Vavuniya Urban Council in respect of which such Vacancy
occurred.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
19th August, 2011.
