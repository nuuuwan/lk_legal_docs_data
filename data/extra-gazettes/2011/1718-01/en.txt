No. 1718/1 – MONDAY,  AUGUST 08, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of land to be acquired:
An allotment of Land in total extent of 2.2016 Hectares marked as
Lot No. 01 depicted in B.P .G. No.3826 of  10.11.2010, prepared by
Survey General situated in the Village of Panagamuwa in Grama
Niladhari Division of 134D  Bambaragoda in  the  Divisional
Secretary's Division of Akmeemana District of Galle, Southern
Province.
Name of land: Walahanduwa watta (5 Acres); Description: Garden,
41 permanent buildings and a shrine room; Claimant: State;Original
Claimant: Chairman, Land Reclamation Commission; Extent: 2.2016
Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Akmeemana on the 10th day of October,
2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 03rd day of October, 2011, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
and the details of the said land, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
Damayanthi Paranagama,
Divisional Secretary,
Akmeemana.
Divisional Secretariat,
Akmeemana.
03rd August, 2011.
