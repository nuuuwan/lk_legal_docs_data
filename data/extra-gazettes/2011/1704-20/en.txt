No. 1704/20 – FRIDAY, MAY 06, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: KE/DS/AC/3/1/65.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
To construct the Western Head Office of the Industrial Development
Authority and Industrial Development Service Centre.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.1136 Hectare,
depicted  in  Preliminary Plan No. CO 9128 situated in village called
Mampe, of Palle Pattuwa Minor Division of the Divisional
Secretary’s Division Kesbewa of Colombo District in Western
Province.
 Description: Home garden, four permanent buildings and a
permanent well. Industrial Development Authority runs a carpentry
school; Claimant: State; Extent:  0.1136 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat, on the 16th day of June, 2011 at
9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 08th day of June, 2011, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
L. A. Kalukapuarachchi,
Divisional Secretary,
Kesbewa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kesbewa.
02nd  May, 2011
