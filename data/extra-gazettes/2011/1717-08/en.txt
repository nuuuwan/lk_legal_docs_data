1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE   PUTTALAM  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  in  Puttalam Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the group Leader of the independent group 8 to which M.A.R.M. Jafris the member belonged, was
required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance, to nominate a Person Eligible under the Ordinance for
Election as a member of that  Puttalam Urban Council  who belongs to  such group  who shall be declared  elected as a member
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group has  nominated  Mr. M. H. M. Farzeen being an eligible person under
the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Abdul Offoor Muhammad Nafeel, Returning Officer of  Puttalam Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. M. H. M. Farzeen elected as
a  member  of   the Puttalam Urban Council.
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
27th July, 2011.
