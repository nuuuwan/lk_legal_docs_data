THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (4) of section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, do by this Order –
(a) on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously
dislocated, with effect from July 21, 2011, appoint Mr. Nimal Ranjith Arthenayake to be the Competent  Authority in
respect of the aforesaid University for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging, the powers, duties and
functions which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to, the Council of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka,
in terms of the aforesaid Act or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder ;
and
(b) rescind the Order made by me under Sub section (4) of Section 20 and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1715/13
of  20th July,  2011.
D
ISSANAYAKE  MUDIYANSELAGE  SUMANAWEERA  BANDA  DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
21st  July, 2011.
