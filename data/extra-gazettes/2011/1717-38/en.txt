No. 1717/38 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 04, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The  allotment of the land called Viharagama  depicted as Lot 'A',
'B' and 'C' in Advance Tracing No. Ku/BNG/2010/179 dated
21.08.2010, made by the Superintendent of Surveys in Charge of the
Division of  Kurunegala and situated in the village of Viharagama in
Keeniyama  Korale Minor Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Bingiriya, Kurunegala District  and containing in total extent of 1.0935
Hectares and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Viharagama – Extent:  About 0.0992 Hectare
North, East, South and West: Deduru Oya.
02. Lot 'B' – Viharagama – Extent:  About 0.6945 Hectare
North and East: Viharagama claimed by Bingiriya Devagiri Rajamaha
Viharaya; South: Viharagama claimed by Bingiriya Devagiri Rajamaha
Viharaya and Lot C;West: Deduru Oya.
03. Lot 'C' – Viharagama – Extent:  About 0.2998 Hectare
North: Lot  B; East:Viharagama claimed by Bingiriya Devagiri
Rajamaha Vihara; South: Viharagama claimed by Bingiriya Devagiri
Rajamaha Viharaya;West: Pradesiya Saba Road and Viharagama
claimed by Bingiriya Devagiri Rajamaha Viharaya.
