Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Schedule to the Notification published by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under
paragraph (1)(a) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1681/3 of  November 22, 2010 and amended by the Notifications published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1697/25 of March 17, 2011 and No. 1704/17 of May 06, 2011, is further amended with effect from May 10, 2011 in the manner
set out in the Schedule below.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
