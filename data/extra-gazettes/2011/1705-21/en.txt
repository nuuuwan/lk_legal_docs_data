2009,P.P.Gam. 3669 stated as Lot No. 111 and 112. It should be

Name of land: Hapalandagahawata; Description:Garden; Claimant:

Kadawatta ; Extent: 0.0464 Hectare.
Lot No. 176
Name of land: Hapalandagahawata; Description:Garden; Claimant:

All persons interested in the aforesaid land, Lot No. 175-176 are

in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Biyagama on the
10th day of  June, 2011, at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of
June, 2011, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. Deeds and extracts in support of claims should be produced
at the inquiry.
