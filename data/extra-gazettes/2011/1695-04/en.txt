THE   PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 40)
REGULATION made by the President under section 5 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40).
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
February 28th, 2011.
Regulation
1. This regulation may be sited as the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 3 of 2011.
2. Notwithstanding anything contained in the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 02
of 2011 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1687/41 of 06.01.2011, with effect from the dates specified in Column II of the
Schedule to this regulation till the date on which an election is held for the election of members to the Pradeshiya Sabhas
specified in Column I of the said Schedule under the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No 15 of 1987, the administration of the affairs
of the aforesaid Sabhas shall be deemed to have been vested in, and exercised, performed and discharged by, the persons
whose names are specified in Column III of the Schedule to this regulation in place of the officers whose names are specified
in Column IV of such  Schedule.
