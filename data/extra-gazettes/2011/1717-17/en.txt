THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.:Do/L/1/Ath/Adhi/88.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three (3)  allotments of  land in total extent of  0.0461 Hectare,  as
Lot Nos. 1- 3 depicted in preliminary plan No. Ka 4234  and dated
20.07.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the Village
of  Pahanwatta,  in  the Divisional   Secretary’s Division of Dodangoda
in the District of  Kalutara  in Western Province morefully described
as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Pahanwatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: K. K. D. K. Ranaweera, Eladuwawatta,
Mathugama; Extent: 0.0202 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Pahanwatta; Description: Paddy; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: W. D. S. L. De Mel  of  No. 144/1, Kotagedara
Road, Madapatha, Piliyandala; Extent: 0.0139 Hectare.
    3.Lot No. 3
Name of   land: Pahanwatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: Kitinan Selwadore, Eladuwa, Mathugama; Extent:
0.0120 Hectare.
All perosns interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Dodangoda Divisional Secretariat on the 10th October, 2011 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 3rd October, 2011 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
                      H. W. T. R. Weththasinghe,
