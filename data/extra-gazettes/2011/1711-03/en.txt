LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCDE   (CHAPTER 262)
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections by virture of powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Act, No. 24 of 1987,  read with Section 4(1) of the said Act, do hereby appoint
the officer specified in Column III of the Schedule hereto as Returning Officer of each Local Authority described in the
corresponding entry in Column II of the said Schedule, situated in the Administrative Districts described in the corresponding
entry in Column I of the said Schedule, for the purpose of the election of members of the Local Authorities in the said
Administrative District.
