THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. No.: LD/05/2001/HW/243.
My No.: HIK/DS/LND/INV/05.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition  Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for rebuild the Seenigama bridge in Colombo
to Galle road, village at Werellana and Seenigama.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Six (06) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0396 Hectare
depicted in P .P.G. 3866 prepared by the Surveyor - General situated
in the village of No. 63, Werellana in Hikkaduwa Divisinal Secretary’s
Division of Galle District and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Arachchigewatta; Extent: 0.0025 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Arachchigewatta; Extent: 0.0040 Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Welegewatta; Extent: 0.0012 Hectare.
Lot No. 04
Name of land: Welegewatta; Extent: 0.0016 Hectare.
Lot No. 05
Name of land: Welegewatta; Extent: 0.0017 Hectare.
Lot No. 06
Name of land: Kalugurugewatta; Extent: 0.0286 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Hikkaduwa Divisional Secretariat, on the 14th day of Sepetmber,
2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of October, 2011,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
produced before me at the inquiry.
U. G. Vidura Kariyawasam,
Divisional Secretary,
Hikkaduwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Hikkaduwa.
