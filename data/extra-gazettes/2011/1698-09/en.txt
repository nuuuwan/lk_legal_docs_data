THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Kal/06/Land/669.
Land Ministry's Reference No.: LD/05/2007/E/03.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition  Act, that the  Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of  land called Viduwalawi Kani depicted in Plan No.
pu. 4709 dated 31st August, 2010  prepared by the Surveyor-General,
in extent 0.1285Hectare situated at the village of Kalpitiya, 630C,
Mandalakudawa Grama Niladari Division, Kalpitiya Divisional
Secretary’s Division in Puttalam District.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Viduwalawi Kani ; Description: Plan No. pu. 4709
Lot No. 1;  Original claimant: M. E. Walakisumma and A. Aleesha
Thumma; Extent: 0.1285 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby  required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Office of the Divisional Secretariat Kalpitiya on the 26th
May, 2011 at  9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of  May, 2011, the
nature of their interests in the land the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
M. H. M. Riyaldeen,
Divisional Secretary,
Kalpitiya.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kalpitiya.
03rd March, 2011.
