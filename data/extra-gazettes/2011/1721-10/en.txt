THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 3/6/02/15.
Lands  and Land Development Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2008/

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called "Chundankadu alias Sooriyapura" in
total extent of about 0.8085 Hectare, depicted as Lot Nos. 9368 in
the FTP No. 02, Supplement No. 167, Sheet No. 504 dated 13th
December, 2010, surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Sooriyapura in Sooriyapura Minor, Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kanthale, in Trincomalee District of Eastern
Province and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 9368
Name of land: Chundankadu alias Sooriyapura; Description: -;
Claimant: Mr. A. R. K. Banda,  C/o Mrs. H. M. Podimanike,
Jayasekara Stores, Pottankadu Junction, Kanthale and Others;  Extent:
0.8085 Hectare.
North: Lot No. 9369 and Lot No. 9369 Road (RDA); East: Lot
No. 9369 Road (RDA) and Lot No. 9369 (Bund); South: Lot No.
9369 (Bund) and Lot No. 9369; West: Lot No. 9369.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretary's office, at Kanthale, on the 07th day of
October, 2011 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of September,
2011, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
U. J. M. S. S. B. Jayasinghe,
Divisional Secretary,
Kanthale.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kanthale.
