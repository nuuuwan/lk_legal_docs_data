AND
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether unjust has been caused to Mr. D. K. Dodangoda, who was served the Sri Lanka Transport Board form 01.01.1963 and
retired on 02.06.2006 as the Principal of the Central  Training College, Kalutara by not receiving the 03 months leave with total pay given
to Executive Officers of the Sri Lanka Transport Board at retirement due to the needs of service or not receiving a payment for the same and
not receiving a payment for the balance of annual leave and the annual leave earned, and  if so, to what relief to what extent he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. WEERASINGHA ,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour, Colombo, this 24th day of August, 2011.
