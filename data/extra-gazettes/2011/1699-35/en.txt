THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  40)

MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,

Colombo,
30th March  2011.
Regulation
01. This regulation may be cited as the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 03 of 2011.
02. Notwithstanding anything in any other written law for the time being in force, and for so long and so long only
as this regulation is in force, the term of office of councilors or members as the case may be of the Municipal Councils, Urban
Councils and Pradeshiya Sabhas specified respectively in Schedule I, Schedule II and Schedule III to this regulation shall
expire, on 31st December, 2011.
3. The Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 02 of 2011 published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1689/ 12 of  January 18, 2011 is hereby rescinded.
