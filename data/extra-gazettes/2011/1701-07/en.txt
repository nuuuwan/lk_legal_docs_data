PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual  Higher  Ordination  of  Sri  Lanka  Rohana  Sanga  Sabhawa
IT is hereby informed that the 26th higher ordination ceremony of the Sri Rohana Maha Sangha Sabhawa is to be held on
26th, 27th, 28th and 29th of April, 2011 at Mangala Seemamalakaya of Ran Dupatha Sri Rohana Uposathagara Maha Vihara,
Kotuwegoda, Matara and that the pilgrims should refrain from carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials and that
the Standing Orders and Regulations under section 02 of the pilgrimage ordinance (Chapter 175 – LDB 11/60) declared on
14th October, 1991 will be in force during the period the ceremony is held.
T
HUSITHA P. WANIGASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.

Matara,
01st  April 2011.
