PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE  SRI  LANKA  PORTS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 51  OF  1979
Order  under  Section  2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Subsection (1), (2) and (3) of Section  2  read with Paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President do by this Order :–
(a) declare that the limits of the Port of Trincomalee being a specified port to which the aforesaid Act applies, has
hitherto been the limits which were the limits of such port on the day immediately preceding the coming into
operation of this Act ;
(b) determine that by this Order, the limits of the Port of Trincomalee shall with effect from the date of the making of
this Order, excluding the water area defined in Schedule II of such Order.
SCHEDULE   I
L
IMITS  OF  THE  PORT  OF  TRINCOMALEE
(a) commencing from high water mark at Coral point at postition
Latitude : 08 0 29.8 North
Longitude : 081 0 21.2 East;
(b) thence, from Coral point draw a straight line due North up to a point ‘A’ at
Latitude : 08 0 33.0 North
Longitude : 081 0 21.2 East;
(c) from point ‘A’ above draw a straight line in a direction 301 (True) up to High Water Mark at Elizabeth point at
Latitude :  08 0 37.65 North
Longitude : 081 0 13.6 East;
(d) thence along the high water mark (along the coast) from Elizabeth point to Coral point encompassing the Trincomalee
Harbour, Thambalagamam Bay, Koddiyar Bay, Trincomalee Bay and Back Bay.
