EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  SRI  JAYAWARDANAPURA  KOTTE
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Sri  Jayawardanapura  Kotte  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of
Resignation;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Weerasooriya Arachchige Chandima Nayanajith   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Sri  Jayawardanapura
Kotte   Municipal Council   who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within  a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Weerasooriya
Arachchige Chandima Nayanajith   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  A. Senanayake,  Returning Officer of  Sri  Jayawardanapura  Kotte   Municipal Council , acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Weerasooriya
Arachchige Chandima Nayanajith  elected as a member  of   the Sri  Jayawardanapura  Kotte   Municipal Council.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Sri  Jayawardanapura  Kotte   Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo,
10th  January  2011.
