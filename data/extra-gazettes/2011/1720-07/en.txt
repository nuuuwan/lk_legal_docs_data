NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry conducted under Section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Provisions stated in section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read
with section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Hettiachchi Mudiyanselage Athula Sarath
Kumara Wijesingha, Chief Minister of the North Western Province and Minister of the Subject of Local Government do
hereby appoint Retired High Court Judge, O. W. O. B. Weerasena esquire as a Single Person Commission of Inquiry to inquire
into whether the offences mentioned in section 185 were committed by the Chairman Senanayake Mudiyanselege Bandula
Pushpakumara Senanayake, while holding his office as the Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha, Maho on the following grounds
/ allegations in terms of section 185 and a report may be submitted within a period of 03 months.
Allegations to be inquired into
ALLEGATIONS :–
(i) During his tenure of office as Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha, Maho from on or about 01st of April, 2011 has
committed the offence/offences stated in section/sections 185 (1) (a) and / or 185 (1) (b) and / or 185 (1) (e) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by taking measures to Pay a sum of Rupees Fourty  Six Thousand two
Hundred and Seventy Five  (Rs. 46,275.00) or and amount close to that amount by the payment voucher No. 624
to the institute of ‘‘Sesath Electricles Company’’, No. 68,  Maliban Street, Colombo 11 from the General Fund
without a formal resolution by the Pradeshiya Sabha on or about 2011.06.23 for the supply of street lamp
accessories for the repair of Street Lamps within the area of authority Pradeshiya Sabha, Maho.
(ii) During his tenure of office as Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho from on or about 01st of April, 2011  has
committed the offence/offences stated in section/sections 185 (1) (a) and / or 185 (1) (b) and / or 185 (1) (e) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by  disturbing to the duty of Acting Secretary, Management Assistant
Mrs. I. M. Swarnathilaka of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho on 26.07.2011.

Company’’, No. 68, Maliban Street, Colombo 11 from the General Fund without a formal resolution by the
Pradeshiya Sabha on or about 2011.06.13 for the supply of street lamp accessories for the repair of Street Lamps
within the area of authority Pradeshiya Sabha, Maho.
Further this retired high court Judge Oliver Weerasena esquire is entitled to the powers of a Commissioner appointed
under commission of inquiry Act in terms of the provisions more fully described in section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987 to be read with section 2 of Provincial Council (incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1987 and you are further
informed to conduct this inquiry and submit a report including the findings of the inquiry within a period of 3 months from the
date of this appointment.
I further inform you that the written and oral evidence which are identified as suitable to be used in making inquires
into the above facts are given in the following schedule.
