THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS an Industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between –
Mr. R. M. Nimal Ariyawansa Ratnayake, No. 318/3, Usgala, Siyabalangamuwa, Galgamuwa.
AND
Mahaweli Livestock and Agro Enterprises (Private) Limited, No. 260/20, Baseline Road, Colombo 09.
NOW THEREFORE  I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4(1)
of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of
1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968), hereby appoint
Mr. T. E. Santharajan, No. 181/1 - 503, W. A. Silva Mawatha, Colombo 06 to be the Arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for
settlement by arbitration.
G

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the non granting of reemployment to Mr. R. M. Nimal Ariyawansa Ratnayake who had been interdicted with effect from
03.10.2005 while he was serving as a Casual Driver attached to the Niravia Farm which is managed under the Mahaweli Livestock and Agro
Enterprises (Private) Limited is justified and if not justified to what relief he should be granted.
V. B. P. K. WEERASINGHE ,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 21st day of January, 2011.
