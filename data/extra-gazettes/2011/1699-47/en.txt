MUNICIPAL COUNCILS   ORDINANCE
Order Under Section 10 (1) (b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 10 (1) (b) of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252), read with
Section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial
Councils, do by this Order appoint the 01st day of April, 2011, as the date on which the term of  office of the members of each
of the Municipal Council specified in the Schedule hereto shall commence.
A. L. M.  ATHAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
01st  April,  2011.

1. Dambulla Municipal Council
2. Bandarawela Municipal Council
