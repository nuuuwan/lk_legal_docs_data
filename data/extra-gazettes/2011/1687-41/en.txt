Regulation
1. This regulation may be sited as “the Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 1 of 2011.”
2. Notwithstanding anything in any other written law for the time being in force, and for so long and so long only as
this regulation is in force, with effect from January 06th 2011 and ending on the date on which and election is held for the
election of members to the Urban Councils constituted under the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255) and specified in
Column I of Schedule I to this regulation and the Pradeshiya Sabhas constituted under the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of
1987 and specified in Column I of Schedule II respectively to this regulation, are constituted, the administration of the affairs
of the aforesaid Councils and Sabhas shall be vested in and exercised, performed and discharged by, the persons whose
names are specified in Column II of the respective Schedules.
