APPOINTMENT  OF  A  SECRETARY  TO  THE  BOARD  OF  REVIEW  INLAND  REVENUE
Mr. Dharmadasa Rangalla, Deputy Commissioner of Inland Revenue, had been appointed as the Secretary to the Board of
Review, Inland Revenue, in addition to his present duties, with effect from 07th January, 2011by the Honourable Minister of
Finance and Planning in terms of Section 118(2) of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 28 of 1979, Section 137(2) of the Inland
Revenue Act. No. 38 of 2000 and Section 166(2) of the Inland revenue Act, No. 10 of 2006.
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo,
13th January 2011.
