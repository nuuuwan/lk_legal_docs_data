BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sub-section (1) of section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, do by this order, on the

of Community Medicine”  appearing in the corresponding entry to such item in Column II thereof, of the following item  :—
“Department of Community and Family Medicine”
