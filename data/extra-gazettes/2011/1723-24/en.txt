1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  NAWALAPITIYA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Nawalapitiya  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  failing to make and
subscribe an oath or affirmation in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Amadoru Galappaththige Jagath Lakshman  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Nawalapitiya  Urban Council
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Meera  Sahib Mohamad
Asmi   being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, U. Amaradasa,  Returning  Officer  of   the Nawalapitiya  Urban Council,  acting in  terms of   Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Meera  Sahib Mohamad Asmi  elected
as a member of the  Nawalapitiya  Urban Council .
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Nawalapitiya  Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
01st September, 2011.
