No. 1710/15 – THURSDAY,  JUNE 16, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Hak/5/1/1/93.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land, depicted in Additional No. 10 in Final Village
Plan No. 185 prepared by the Survey Superintendent of Matara
District,  situated at Muruthamuraya Village in Sub Division of
Kandabadapatthuwa East in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Hakmana in Matara District in Southern Province:
01. Lot No. 229 - Kopiwatta - Extent: 0.0010 Hectare.
North: Lot No. 130 B; East:  Lot No. 130 B and Un Numbering
Road; South: Lot No. 130 B; West: Lot No. 130 B.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Hakmana Divisional Secretariat on the 29th June, 2011 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 29th June, 2011 the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
Chinthaka Abeyvikrama,
Divisional Secretary,
Hakmana.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Hakmana,
31st May, 2011.
