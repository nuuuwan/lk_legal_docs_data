PART III — LANDS
Land Reform Commission  Notices
LAND REFORM COMMISSION
Notice under Section 29 of the Land Reform Law No. 1 of 1972 as amended by Land Reform (Special Provi-
sions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 and Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 14 of 1986
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 29 of the Law, I hereby direct every person who is interested in any
agricultural land or lands described in the Schedule hereto immediately before the date on which such land or lands have been
vested in the Land Reform Commission to make, within one month from the 10.09.2011 a written claim in triplicate to the whole
any part of the compensation payable under the law in respect of such land or lands and to specify in the claim.
(a) His name and address;
(b) The nature of his interest in such land/ lands;
(c) Particulars of his claim; and
(d) How much of such compensation is claimed by him.
All claims should  be sent by registered post, addressed to the Chairman, Land Reform Commission.
N
IMAL G.  PUNCHIHEWA,
Attorney-at-Law
Chairman, Land Reform Commission.
Office of the Land Reform Commission.
No. C 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
