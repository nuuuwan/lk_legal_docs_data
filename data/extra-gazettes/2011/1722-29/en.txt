EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MINUWANGODA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member   in  Minuwangoda  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to  which Asurappulige
Sunil Jayarathne, the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of  that Minuwangoda    Pradeshiya Sabha,  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  Thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr.  Andavitharanage
Wimalasiri Rodrigo, being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning  Officer  of   Minuwangoda
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in   terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr.  Andavitharanage Wimalasiri Rodrigo,  elected as  a member  of   the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
26th  August, 2011.
