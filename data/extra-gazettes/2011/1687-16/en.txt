L. D. B  69/ 49
THE   IMMIGRANTS   AND   EMIGRANTS    ACT   (CHAPTER 351)
Order under Section 3
BY virtue  of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the  Immigrants and Emigrants Act, (Chapter 351) read with paragraph
(2) of Article 44 of  the Constitution  of  the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda  Rajapaksa, President, do
by this order declare that the Ports specified in the Schedule to this order, shall be ''approved Ports of Entry and Departure''
for the purposes of this Act.
SCHEDULE
Approved Ports of Entry and Departure
1. The Port of Magam Ruhunupura Mahinda Rajapaksa.
2. The Port of Norachcholai.
3. The Port of Oluvil.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
29th December, 2010.
