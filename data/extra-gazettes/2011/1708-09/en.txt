1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KURUNEGALA   MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kurunegala Municipal  Council has fallen vacant by reason  of  resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Jayapathma Wanninayake,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kurunegala Municipal Council  who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.   Upali Jayawardena,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I,  Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Ananda Lokubandara Rathnayaka,   Returning Officer of  the
Kurunegala Municipal Council,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare  Mr.   Upali Jayawardena,  elected as a member of the Kurunegala Municipal  Council.
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Municipal  Council.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala.
24th  May 2011.
