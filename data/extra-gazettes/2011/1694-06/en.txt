THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been re-named as “ Sir Razik Fareed Mawatha” by
the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the Subject of
Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read with
the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J.M. B. JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.

1. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
2. District - Colombo

4. New Name of the Street - Sir Razik Fareed Mawatha
5. Road Description - This road which starts from in between Assessment No. 3
and 4, ends from in between Assessment No. 40 and 41
