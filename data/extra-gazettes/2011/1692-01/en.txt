PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the   Secretary-General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (1) of the Parliamentary  Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  Seventh Parliament
by reason of  Mr. Lakshman Nipuna Arachchi, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District
No. 01 - Colombo under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act by absenting himself from the sitting of Parliament during a
continuous period of three months without the leave of Parliament first obtained ;
And whereas the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed
by me under Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of Article 99
of the Constitution ;
And whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return
in Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr.  Ketagoda Gamage Jayantha Perera was
declared elected as a  Member of Parliament for the said electoral district ;
Now therefore, I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested
in me by Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. Ketagoda Gamage Jayantha Perera
has been  declared elected as a member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District No. 01 - Colombo  to fill the
vacancy caused by reason of  Mr. Lakshman Nipuna Arachchi, absenting himself from the sitting of Parliament during a
continuous period of three months without the leave of Parliament first obtained.
D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
07th February, 2011.
