Regulations
“Authorized Representative Regulations No. 1 of 2006” published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1487/3 dated March 05,
2007 are hereby amended in item 04 thereof by the substitution for the paragraph (e), of the following paragraph :
“(e) (i) has obtained one of the following qualifications/status :
(a) Licentiate/Intermediate certificate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants ;
(b) Professional Stage Part I of the Institute of Cost and Management Accountants/Intermediate
Examination of the Chartered Institute of Cost and Management ;
(c) Professional Examination Section 1 of the Chartered Association of Certified Accountants ; or
(d) Membership of the Association of Accounting Techinicians ; and
(ii) has post qualification experience of at least ten years in accounting.”
