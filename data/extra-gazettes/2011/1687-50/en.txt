THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT N O . 15 OF 1987
Order
BY VIRTUE  of  the  powers vested in me by sections 2, 4,  6  and paragraph (b) of subsection (5) of  section 185  of the
Pradeshiya Sabhas Act. No. 15 of 1987,  I,  A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do  by
this order -
(1) declare the area specified in the Schedule hereto, to be a Pradeshiya Sabha for the purpose of that Act;
(2) define the administrative limits of that area to be the limits as set out in that Schedule;
(3) constitute the Pradeshiya Sabha for the area declared under paragraph (1) of this order and assign the following name
and designation to such Pradeshiya Sabha:-
                                 "Udapalatha  Pradeshiya  Sabha"
(4) determine that the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of the Chairman, Vice Chairman and Twenty One other
elected members;
(5) appoint  the First day of April, 2011 as the date on which the term of office of the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha shall
commence.
(6) rescind the Order with effect from first day of April 2011, which has published, in the Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 1637/24 of  Twenty Second day of January 2010, by the Minister in-charge of the subject of  Local Government
and the Chief Minister of Central Province in relation to the dissolution of Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. L. M. A
