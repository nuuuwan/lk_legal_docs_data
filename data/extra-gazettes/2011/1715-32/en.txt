Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the University of Colombo School of Computing (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 1 of
2011.
2. The University of Colombo School of Computing Ordinance, No. 1 of 2002 published in Gazette Extraordinary No.
1262/32 of November 15, 2002, is hereby amended in paragraph 18 thereof, by the repeal of sub-paragraph (1) of that paragraph
and the substitution thereof of the following sub-paragraph :-
“(1) There shall be Centres established in respect of the following :-
(a) Advanced Digital Media Technology ;
(b) E-Learning ;
(c) External Degrees ;
(d) Computing Services ;
(e) Professional Development ; and
(f) Digital Forensics.”.
