THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)
ACT  No. 2 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY VIRTUE of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No. 2 of 1980.
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Minister-in-Charge of the subject of urban
development, being of opinion that the lands specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the purpose of relocation
of Defence Ministry do by this order declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
28th February 2011.
