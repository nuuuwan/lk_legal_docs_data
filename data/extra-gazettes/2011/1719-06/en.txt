NATIONAL  HOUSING  ACT,  No. 37 OF 1954
Revocation of Order under Section 25(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 25(2) of the National Housing Act, No. 37 of 1954 and its subsequent
amendments I, Wimal Weerawansa, Minister of Construction, Engineering Services, Housing and Common Amenities do
hereby  declare   that the Order  published  in Part I, Section (1) of the  Gazette Notification dated 28th January, 2011 by which
it was declared that the provisions of Parts XII and XIII of the Companies Act, apply to the Nation's Building Society
(formerly known as Ceylinco Building Society Limited) shall be revoked, rescinded and cancelled  with effect from 3rd
August 2011.
