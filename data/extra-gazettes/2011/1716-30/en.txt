THE  PRADESHIYA    SABHAS   ACT, No.  15   OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Paragraph (d) of Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with
Section 18 of  the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2) I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial
Councils, do hereby amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1639/11 of 02nd February 2010, relating to
the dissolution of the Kaduwela Pradeshiya Sabha by replacing of the words “01st day of April 2011” and the substitution
thereof the words “01st day of August 2011” of the said Order :-
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister  of Local Government
and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
30th July, 2011.

MUNICIPAL   COUNCILS   ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 9 of the Municipal  Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252), read with Section 18
of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2) I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do
hereby amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1639/11 of 02nd February 2010, relating to the constitution
of the Kaduwela Municipal  Council by replacing of the words “01st day of April 2011” and the substitution thereof the words
“01st day of August 2011” in paragraph (5) of the said Order :-
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister  of Local Government
and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
30th July, 2011.
