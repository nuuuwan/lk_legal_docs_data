THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: Dehi/Land 1/2/28(II).
Land Ministry No.: 13/97/E/597.
It is hereby informed to the all persons who have the rights that the
rights inspection date and the due date that to be forwarded
compensation claims documents mentioned in the 2nd page under
section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act of the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Dehiovita in the District of Kegalle published in the
pages of 1A and 2A of the notice of land acquisition under part III -
Lands bearing No. 1686/10 and 27th Monday of December 2010 of
the Gazette (Extraordinary) of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka shall be amended as mentioned  below :
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Deheowita on the 08th day of June, 2011 at
1.30 p.m. and further all are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th day of May, 2011, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. T. R. Nalika Piyasena,
Divisional Secretary,
Dehiovita.
