Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of about 6.9180
Hectares, depicted in P.P .G. 3801, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Thotawila Divisional Secretary’s Division
of No. 48A Thotawila in Galle District, Southern Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Sirikandurawatta; Extent: 0.9280 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Sirikandurawatta; Extent: 0.0050 Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Sirikandurawatta; Extent: 5.9310 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Hikkaduwa Divisional Secretary's Office, on the 24th day of
October, 2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 17th day of October, 2011,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims  should be produced
before me at the inquiry.
