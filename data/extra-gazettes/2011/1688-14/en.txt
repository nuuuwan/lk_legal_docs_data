No. 1688/14 – WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 12,  2011
Registration of Title Act, No. 21 of 1998
Making an Order under Section I as an Area coming under the Registration of Title Act
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, the Minister of Lands and Land Development, in persuance of the powers conferred on me by Section I of the
Registration of Title Act, No. 21 of 1998, do hereby declare that the following administrative districts as areas coming under the Registration
of Title Act with effect from 01.12.2010:
1. The Mullaitivu District in the Northern Province
2. The Kilinochchi District in the Northern Province
3. The Vavuniya District in the Northern Province
4. The Jaffna District in the Northern Province
5. The Mannar District in the Northern Province
Janaka Bandara Tennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Colombo,
01st December, 2010.
