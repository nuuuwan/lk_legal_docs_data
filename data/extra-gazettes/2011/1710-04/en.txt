THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: Kela/7/Laq/3/218.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotments of land in total extent of  about  0.0031 Hectare
depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 3754 and  dated 2010.07.20
prepared by the Survery General,  situated in the Villages of
Thalawathuhenpita in the Minor Division Adikari Pattuwa in the  in
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kelaniya in Gampaha District in
Western Province. .
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Assmt. No. 164, Kandy Road; Description: Garden;
Claimants: State; Orginal claimants: I. B.  Nihal Wijesiri, No. 164,
Kandy Road, Mahara Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0031 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands  are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at the Divisional Secretary's office of Kelaniya on the 18th
day of July, 2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of July, 2011,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
