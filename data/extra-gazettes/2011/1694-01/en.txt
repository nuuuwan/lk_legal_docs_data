No. 1694/1 – MONDAY,  FEBRUARY 21,  2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Bala/2/1/24.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: LD/05/2005/E/160.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for  G/Randombe Damnada Junior School.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Land called Kahatagawatta in extent about 0.2045 hectare depicted
as Lot Nos. 1, 2, 3 and 4 in Surveyor-General's Plan No. P .P.G. 3859
at 28th December, 2010 dated, situated in 88B, Randombe (North)
in Balapitiya Divisional Secretariat.
Lot No. 01 – Kahatagahawatta– Extent: 0.0025 Hectare
North: Suchithrarama Vihara Road; East: Lot No. 3 and Cemetery
Road; South: Lot No. 03; West: Suchithrarama  Vihara Road.
Lot No. 02 – Kahatagahawatta– Extent: 0.0010 Hectare
North: Suchithrarama Vihara Road; East: Lot No. 3; South: Lot
No. 03 and Lot H of 197 P .P.G plan; West: Lot 11 of 197 P.P.G. Plan
and Suchithrarama Road.
Lot No. 03 – Kahatagahawatta alias G/Damnada School
Premises – Extent: 0.1977 Hectare
North: Lot No. 2, Suchithrarama Vihara Road and Lot No. 11 of
Cemetery Road; East: Cemetry Road, Suchithrarama Vihara Road
and Lot No. 04; South: Suchithrarama Road, Lot No. 4 and Lot No.
11 of 197 P.P.G Plan's; West: Lot No. 2 and Lot No. 11 of 197 P.P.G.
Plan's.
Lot No. 04 – Kahatagahawatta alias G/Damnada School
Premises – Extent: 0.0033 Hectare
North: Lot No. 03; East and South: Suchithrarama Viharaya; West:
Lot No.03.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Balapitiya, on the 06th day of April, 2011
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 30th day of March, 2011, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
R.H. Kamal Ranasinghe,
Divisional Secretary,
Balapitiya.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Balapitiya.
10th February, 2011.
