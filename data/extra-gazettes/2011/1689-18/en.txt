THE  DEBT  CONCILIATION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  81)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Justice under sections 6 and 10 read with section 63 of the Debt Conciliation
Ordinance (Chapter 81).
RAUF  HAKEEM,
Ministry of Justice. Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
30th December, 2010.
Regulations
The Debt Conciliation Regulations, 1942, published in Gazette No. 8979 of July, 31st 1942 as amended from time to
time are further amended as follows :—
(1) by the substitution for regulation 3 thereof, of the following regulation :—
“3.(1) Every member of the Board or of a Branch Board who attends a meeting of the Board or of a Branch Board
shall be paid a fee of Rupees One Thousand in respect of each sitting.
 (2) The Chairman of a Board who attends a meeting of the Board shall be paid a fee of Rupees One Thousand
Five Hundred in respect of each sitting.” ;
(2) in regulation 4 thereof —
(a) in paragraph (1) of that regulation by the substitution for sub-paragraph (ii), of the following
sub-paragraph :—
“(ii) where a member uses a motor vehicle to attend a meeting, Rupees Five Hundred per meeting and in the
case of the Chairman, Seven Hundred and Fifty per meeting.” ;
(3) by the substitution, for paragraphs (2) and (3) of that regulation, of the following paragraph :—
“(2) In addition to any sum payable under paragraph (1), the Chairman and every member of the Board or Branch
Board shall in terms of section 12 of Public Administration Circular 06/2006, be paid a combined allowance of
Rupees Five Hundred when he is engaged in official duty away from his residence.”.
(4) the amendments herein made shall be deemed to have come into force on August 1st, 2010.
