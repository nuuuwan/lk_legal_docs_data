THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection (4) of section 20
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by subsection (4) of  section 20 of  the Universities Act, No. 16 of  1978,  I,
Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, on being satisfied that the
administration of the affairs of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated, do by this Order, appoint
with effect from January 21, 2011, Nimal Ranjith Arthenayake to be the Competent Authority in respect of the aforesaid
University for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging, the powers, duties and functions which have been
conferred or imposed on, or assigned to, the Council of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, in terms  of the aforesaid Act or any
Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
D
ISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA BANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
21st January 2011.
