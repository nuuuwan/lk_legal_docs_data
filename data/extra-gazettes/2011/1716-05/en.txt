Appointment  made  by  the  Governor of  the Uva  Province
I  hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province :
01. Dr.  Mr. U. S. P . Udunuwara, to act as Acting Deputy Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala, in
addition to his permanent post, from the date 06.05.2011 to 20.05.2011, till the return of Dr.  Mr. P. D. K. Adhikari,
to the Island who is the Regional Director  to the Health Service of Monaragala.
02. Mrs. N. C. Hennayake, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Acting Commissioner of
Badulla Municipal Council, in addition to her permanent post, with effect from the date 05.06.2011 to 02.07.2011,
03. Mrs. E. M. Sumithra De Silva, Class I of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, as Director of the Uva Provincial
Council (Accounts & Payment), with effect from the date 01.06.2011,
04. Mr. R. S. C. W. M. A. B. N. Wijayathunga, Class II of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service as Deputy Director to the
Agriculture of Uva Province (Distribution), from the date 10.06.2011,
05. Mr. R. D. Siripala, Class I of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, as Deputy Director to the Agriculture of Uva
Province (Training), with effect from the date 10.06.2011,
06. Mrs. N. C. Hennayake, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Acting Provincial Commissioner
of Local Government of Uva Province, in addition to her permanent post, from the date 19.06.2011 to 25.06.2011.
07. Mr. M. P. N. M. Wickramasinghe, Class II of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Senior Assistant Secretary
to the Ministry of Finance, Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport,
Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction of the Uva
Provincial Council, with effect from the date 01.02.2010
