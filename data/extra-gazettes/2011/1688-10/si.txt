Statement of matter in dispute
The Matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is -
Whether Miss  G . D. Wijewickrama who was in the
service of the Ceylon Electricity Board as the Accountant
(Project) is entitled to receive a project allowance for the
period of  January, 2001 to October, 2007 in respect of the
project duties performed by her directly related to that
post in terms of the Board's Circular No. 2002/GM/91/Policy
dated 12.04.2002 and based on the subsequent decisions
and the precedences taken to pay project allowances for
the non engineering staff and if so entitled, what quantum
of allowance Should be paid to her.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Commissioner General
 of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 13th day of August, 2010.
