THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been re-named as “ Sri Sambudhatwa Jayanthi
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the
Subject of Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall
read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J. M. B. JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07,
4th April 2011.
Schedule
1. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
2. District - Colombo
3. Present Name of the Street - Havelock Road
4. New Name of the Street - Sri Sambudhatwa  Jayanthi Mawatha
5. Road Description - This road which starts from in between Assessment No. 3
and (Thimbirigasyaya Municipal Ward) Assessment No. 2
(Milagiriya Municipal Ward), ends from in between
Assessment No. 649 and 844 (Pamankada East  Municipal
Ward).
