Government  Notifications
1A
NOTIFICATIONS
President’s Office No. : CSA/5/7/1.
IT  is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under the provisions of sub-sections (1) and (2) of
Section 19 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964
to appoint Messrs. L. H. G. Weerasekara, S. T. Gunawardena, Dr. Mohamed Sheriff Jaldeen, J. C. Boange, Upali Samaraweera,
Jayantha Liyanage, Meera Sahib Azmi as Lawyer Members and Messrs. P. M. B. Edmond, W. Panditharathna, H. M.
Premathilake, M. D. E. S. Gunathilake, P. P. T. Mohideen, U. G. Wijerathna as Valuer Members of the Board of Review for the
purpose of hearing appeals in the manner therein provided, for a period of three years with effect from 23rd November, 2010.
His Excellency has also been pleased, under the provisions of sub-section (3) of Section 19 of the aforesaid Act, to
appoint Messrs. L. H. G . Weerasekera and S. T. Gunawardena respectively as the Chairman and the Vice Chairman of the said
Board of Review with effect from 23rd November, 2010.
By His Excellency’s order,
L
ALITH WEERA TUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
11th March, 2011.
