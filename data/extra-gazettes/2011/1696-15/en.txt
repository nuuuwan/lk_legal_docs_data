Government   Notifications
Determination of areas of Authority of Agrarian Development Council under section 51 of
Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000
BY virtue of powers vested in me under section 15 of Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000, I,  Raveendra Hewavitharana,
Commissioner  General of Agrarian  Development determine that the area of the Authority of Agrarian Services Committee
No. 1/6, Kotte in the Nugegoda, Dehiwala/Mount-Lavinia, Colombo, Maharagama Divisional Secretary's Divisions in the
Electorates of  Kotte, Dehiwela/Rathmalana, Borella, Colombo-Central, Colombo West, Colombo East and Maharagama in the
Colombo District, the areas of the Authority of Agrarian Services Committee No. 2/14, Nittambuwa and No. 2/15 Urapola in the
Attanagalla Divisional Secretary's Division in the electorate of Attanagalla in the Gampaha District, the areas of the Authority
of Agrarian Services Committee No. 4/38 Delpitiya and No. 4/39 Medapitiya in the Udupalatha Divisional Secretary's Division
in the electorate of Gampola in the Kandy District, the areas of the Authority of Agrarian Services Committee No. 61/10
Rikillagaskada and No. 6/12 Hanguranketha in the Hanguranketha Divisional Secretary's Division in the Nuwara Eliya District
published in the Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 1099/19 dated 30th September, 1999 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka should be amended as referred to in the following Schedule :-
