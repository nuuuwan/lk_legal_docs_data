Notification
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me in terms of sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) of Article 44 of the Constitution, I
Mahinda Rajapaksa, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka;
(a) having determined the number of Ministers of the Cabinet of Ministers in terms of the aforesaid Atricle;
(b) having assigned the subjects and functions to the said Ministers in the manner specified in the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1681/3 of November 22, 2010;
(c) do hereby notify that the Schedule to the aforesaid Notification has been amended in the manner set out in
the Schedule below.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Presidential Secretariat, President.
Colombo 01,
28th February 2011.
