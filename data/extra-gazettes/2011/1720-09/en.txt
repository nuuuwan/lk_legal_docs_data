Provincial  Councils  Notifications
NOTIFICATION  MADE  UNDER  SECTION  185 OF  PRADESHIYA  SABHA   ACT,  NO. 15 OF 1987 TO BE READ
WITH    SECTION 2   OF   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  (INCIDENTAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, NO.  12 OF 1989
BY virtue of powers vested in me under subsection 185, 3(a)i of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section
2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Wijesingha Hettiachchi Mudiyanselage Athula Sarath
Kumara Wijesingha, the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of the North-western
Provincial Council do hereby suspend the office of Chairmanship of Mr. S. M. Bandula Pushpakumara of Pradeshiya Sabha
Maho with immediate effect.
Further I hereby order the Vice Chairman Rohana Madagedara to implement the powers of the office of the suspended
Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha, Maho and perform his duties in terms of the provisions of sub section 185 3(a)i of Pradeshiya
Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 2 of aforesaid Provincial Council (incidental provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
