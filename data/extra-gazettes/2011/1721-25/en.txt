PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
ANNUAL PERAHARA  OF  BADULLA  KATHARAGAMA  DEV ALAYA
IN accordance  with the powers vested in me by  Gazette notifications No. 9902 of 10.09.1948, No. 10283 of 17.08.1951 and
No. 14778 of  25.03.1955 published under chapter 365 of the Pilgrimage Act, and under the   Powers  conferred on Divisional
Secretaries by Act No. 58 of 1992,  and by the statute dealing with  Devalaya published in No. 1622 of 02.10.2009, I, Gamini
Mahindapala Jopheus, the  Divisional Secretary  of  Badulla do hereby make the following orders in connection with the
Annual Perehara of Badulla Katharagama Davalaya - 2011
1. Annual Perehara of Badulla Katharagama Davalaya shall begin on 30th of August 2011, and end in the afternoon
on 14th September 2011 after having returned to the  Devalaya.
2. Route of  Perehara
The Perehara  begins at Badulla  Katharagama Davalaya premises and proceed along Badulla Lower Street,
Passara Road, (Muthiyangana Roundabout), Longden Road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Cocowatte Road,
Udayaraja Mawatha, king's Street, Bazar Veediya, Lower Street and back to Badulla Katharagama Devalaya.
3. Cooking and selling articles of trade are prohibited in and around Badulla  Katharagama Davalaya compound and
in the compound where the throne of the Devalaya is located.
4. Private vehicles are prohibited to park in the main roads during the Perehara Season. All types of vehicles
including bicycles and carts are prohibited to enter the  Devala area without a permit issued by me or by the Nilame
In- charge of the Devalaya.
5. The permanent and buildings currently  being constructed under a formal approval given by Badulla Municipal
Council along the route of  Perehara must conform to the instructions of the Health Department. At the end of the
Perehara, after having dismantled the temporary sheds, the lessees shall dispose of all polythene and other
garbage lying along the route of Perehara and Devala premises as instructed by the Public Health Inspectors.

6. Construction of temporary buildings in and around the sacred area shall not be done without a permit is obtained

7. No one shall construct a temporary hut or a building of any form of effect any extensions to an existing building
