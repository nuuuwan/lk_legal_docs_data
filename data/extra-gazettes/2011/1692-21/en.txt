TOWN AND COUNTRY PLANNING ORDINANCE (CHAPTER  269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning
Ordinance (Chapter 269), I, Wimal Weerawansa, Minister of  Construction, Engineering  Services, Housing and  Common
Amenities, do by this order :-
(a) declare that the area of land specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an Urban Development Area for the
purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance and shall be called “Maligavila Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area” ; and
(b) direct that an outline scheme shall be prepared for the area declared to be the “Maligavila Raja Maha Vihara
Sacred Area”.
W
IMAL WEERAWANSA,
Minister of  Construction, Engineering  Services,
Housing and  Common Amenities.
Ministry of  Construction, Engineering  Services,
Housing and  Common Amenities,
Colombo,
28th January 2011.
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE MALIGA VILA RAJA MAHA VIHARA  SACRED AREA
The entire area of land containing 10.1172 hectares in extent comprising the two blocks of land specified in Part I, Part
II and Part III below, as depicted in the Survey-General’s Final Village Plan No. 54H, dated 19.02.1976, situated in the village of
Maligavila, in the Sulu Division of the Buttala Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Buttala, in the Monaragala
District in the Uva Province.
