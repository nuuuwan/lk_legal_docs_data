NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION  16  2(b)  OF  THE  COAST  CONSERV ATION  ACT, No. 57 OF  1981
Proposed 380 Rooms 16 Storied Environmental Impact Assessment Report Hotel Project
Mosque Lane, Kammalthota, Negombo
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed 380 Rooms 16 Storied Hotel
Project, submitted by the Greener Water Ltd., No. 27-2, East Tower, World Trade Centre, Echelon Square, Colombo 01, as
required by the Section 16(1) of the Cost Conservation Act, No. 57 of 1981 will be available for inspection by the public at the
following institutions between 8.30 a.m. and 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 01.06.2011 (Please note that these offices
are closed on weekends and public holidays.)
1. Ministry of Defence, Public Security, Law and Order, No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha, Colombo 03.
2. Coast Conservation Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor, Maligawatte, Colombo 10.
3. Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, ‘‘Parisara Piyasa’’ Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha Battaramulla.
4. Urban Development Authority, 6 and 7th Floors, Sethsiripaya, Battaramulla.
5. District Secretariat, Gampaha.
6. Divisional Secretariat, Negombo.
7. Municipal Council, Negombo.
8. District Fisheries Office, Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, Negombo.
9. Regional Office, Coast Conservation Department, Custom Road, Negombo.
Any member of the public may within 30 days from 01.06.2011 submit their comments in writing on the above
Environmental Impact Assessment to the Director General, Coast Conservation Department.
