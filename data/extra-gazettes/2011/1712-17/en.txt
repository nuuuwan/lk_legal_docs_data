THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. : Udu/2/5/2/56A.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. :13/97/FR/151A.
I hereby give notice under  Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An  allotment of land  called  “Winchfield Park Watta”  mentioned
in Plan No.  Maha 6142 depicted as Lot No. 1 bearing an extent of
about 0.1473 Hectare,  Lot No. 2 bearing an extent of about 1.8683
Hectares, Lot No. 3 bearing an extent of about 4.8861 Hectares and
lot No. 4 bearing an extent of about 8.0879 Hectares, with a total
extent of 14.9914 Hectares, prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Karambaketiya, in  the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Udadumbara of Kandy District
in Central Province.
Name  and address of  Claimant : H.  M. T. G. Podimenike,
Kandagama, Udadumbara.
All persons interested  in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 03rd August, 2011
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 03rd August, 2011 the nature of their
interests in the land,the particulars of their claims for
compensation  for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation  and details of the computation of such amount.
W. M. M. G. Abeysinghe Banda,
Divisional Secretary,
Udadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Udadumbara,
14th June, 2011.
