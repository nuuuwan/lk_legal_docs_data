CUSTOMS  NOTIFICATION
Order   under  Section  111 of  the Customs  Ordinance  (Chapter  235)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under Section 111 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), read with Article 44(2) of
the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order declare and appoint Puttlam in the North Western Province to be a port of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and its respective limits as set out in Schedule ‘‘A’’. I do further declare and
appoint the places set out in the Schedule ‘‘B’’ to be the legal quay within such limits for the lading and unlading of goods
and declare the bounds and the extent of such quay to be as set out in the Schedule ‘‘B’’ within the Port of Puttlam.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
Ministry of Finance and Planning, (Minister of Finance and Planning)
Colombo 01,
21st April 2011.
SCHEDULE  A – PORT LIMITS OF THE PORT OF PUTTLAM
North by : Line drawn East from a Position ‘‘A’’ in Latitude 080 02' N and Longitude 0790 38' E to the High
Water Mark on the Sea Coast marked by a position B.
East by : From Position ‘‘B’’ above to position ‘‘C’’ Southward situated in Latitude 07 0 58'.0 N and 0790
44' E Longitude along the High Water Mark on the Coast.
South by : Line drawn due East from a position ‘‘D’’ in Latitude 07 0 58' N and Longitude 0790 38' E to the
High Water Mark on the Sea Coast Marked by above position ‘‘C’’.
West by : A straight line connecting position ‘‘A’’ and ‘‘D’’ above.
SCHEDULE  B – DESIGNATED LEGAL QUAYS OF THE PORT OF PUTTLAM
Forefront direction of the Quay of the Port as defined in schedule A is 2530 9'.10'' to the North and the Distance from
the abutment in the Land to the furthest edge of the Quay is 511.72 m
