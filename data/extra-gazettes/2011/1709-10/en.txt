PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE FINANCE ACT, No. 11 OF 2006
Order  under  Section 2(1)(b)(i) and (ii)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subparagraphs (i) and (ii) of paragraph (b) of subsection (1) of section 2 of the Finance
Act, No. 11 of 2006, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this order determine that,
1. the category of motor vehicles which constitutes a “permitted motor vehicle” for purpose of sub-paragraph (ii) of
paragraph (b) of subsectioin (1) of Section 2 of the aforesaid Act, shall be a Motor Car, the cylinder capacity of which does not
exceed 1600cc and,
2. the motor vehicle concessionary levy to be charged, shall be such amount as is calculated at the rate of seventy five
percentum of the aggregate amount of taxes payable by such person in terms of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) the
Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 and the Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002.
in this order “motor vehicle concessionary levy” means the levy imposed in terms of sub-paragraphs (i) or (ii) of
paragraph (b) of subsection (1) of subsection 2 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2006.
The order made under paragraph (b) of subsection (1) of Section 2 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2006 and published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1680/21 of November 19, 2010 is hereby rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
06th  June,  2011.
