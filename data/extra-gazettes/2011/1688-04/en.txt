PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
Direction No.: 33.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Special Direction under Section 10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING under  the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(b)(ii) of the Consumer  Affairs  Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, The
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Producers, Transporters, Distributors and Traders of locally produced vegetables and
fruits that they shall use containers made out of plastic, hard paper or wood at the time of plucking, collecting, storing,
transporting and displaying, offering for sale of the locally produced vegetables and fruits and that the maximum net weight
of the vegetables or fruits shall not exceed 25 kgs.
The Direction shall come in to effect immediately.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Consumer Affiars Authority,
Colombo,
10th January 2011.
