ERA VURPA TTU  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning  Officer of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby

Jeewarangan of  the  Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha as he has ceased  to be a
member of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal Party.
ASANKA RATHNAYAKA,
