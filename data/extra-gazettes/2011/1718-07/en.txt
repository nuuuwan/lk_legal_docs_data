THE   PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 40)

MAHINDA RAJAP AKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
08th August, 2011.
Regulation
1. This regulation may be cited as “The Emergency (Administration of Local Authorities) Regulation, No. 7 of 2011”.
2. Notwithstanding anything in any other written law for the time being in force, and for so long and so long only as this
regulation is in force, with effect from 10th August, 2011 and ending on the date on which and election is held for the election of members
to the Municipal Councils constituted under the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) and specified in Column I of Schedul e I
to this regulation, Urban Councils constituted under the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255) and specified in Column I of Schedule
II to this regulation and the Pradeshiya Sabhas constituted under the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 and specified in Co lumn I
of Schedule III respectively to this regulation, are constituted, the administration of the affairs of the aforesaid Councils and Sabhas shall
be vested in and exercised, performed and discharged by, the persons whose names are specified in Column II of the respective Schedules.
