EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   V A VUNIYA  URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Vavuniya Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
S. N. G . Nathan,   the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that Vavuniya Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman whithin a
period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Iyaththurai Kanagiya to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Sangarappillai Suthakaran,   Returning Officer of  the V avuniya Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.   Iyaththurai Kanagiya
elected as Chairman of the Vavuniya Urban Council.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
V avuniya Urban  Council.
Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
28th  June 2011.
