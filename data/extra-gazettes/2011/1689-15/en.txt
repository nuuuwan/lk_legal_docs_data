THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called ''Winchfield Park Watta'' bearing an
extent of about 4.282 Hectares, depicted as Lot 'A' in the Advance
Tracing No. MAHA/UDD/ACQ/95/550 dated 18th April, 1997,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Meemure,
in the Minor Division of Gandeke Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province is
bounded as follows:
North: Winchfield Park Watta claimed by M. Abeyratne,
Otunnabadde Gala and Winchfield Park Watta claimed by A. V . S.
Abayagunasekara and others; East: Winchfield Park Watta claimed
by A. V . S. Abayagunasekara and others; South: Winchfield Park
Watta claimed by A. V . S. Abayagunasekara and others, Maha Oya
and Winchfield Park Watta claimed by M. Abeyratne;West:
Winchfield Park Watta claimed by M. Abeyratne.
