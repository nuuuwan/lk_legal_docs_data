THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I,Janaka Bandara Tennakoon,  Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Viharagama depicted  as Lot 'A' in the
Advance Tracing No. KU/BNG/2010/268 dated 16.09.2010 made
by the Superintendent of  Surveys and situated in the village of
Viharagama in the Kiniyama Korale  Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Bingiriya, Kurunegala District,  containing in
extent of 2.0239 Hectares and bounded as follows:
North and East: Viharagama claimed by Devagiri Rajamaha
Viharaya, Bingiriya; South: Road owned by the Department of
Irrigation;West: Viharagama claimed by Devagiri Raja Maha Viharaya,
Bingiriya.
