EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE  PALLEPOLA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman  in  Pallepola   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Disanayaka  Mudiyanselage Jagath Wasantha Kumara Disanayaka,     the   Chairman  belonged, was required by me under
Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to   decide upon the member of that  pallepola  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as  Chairman  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Channa Indika Herath  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning  Officer  of   Pallepola   Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Channa Indika Herath elected as  Chairman of   the  Pallepola   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C. N.  T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pallepola  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
23rd December 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   YATINUWARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Jathika Hela Urumaya, the recognized political party to which
Gannoruwa Kangaramulle Gedara Thushara Sampath Swarnathilaka,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section
65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Yatinuwara    Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Welipele Gedara
Jayaratna,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  U. Amaradasa,  Returning Officer of   Yatinuwara    Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section
65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare     Mr.  Welipele Gedara Jayaratna, elected
as  a  member  of   the   Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
U. A
MARADASA,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office,  Y atinuwara   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kandy,
06th December, 2010.
