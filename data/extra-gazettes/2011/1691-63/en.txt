No. 1691/63 – THURSDAY,  FEBRUARY 03, 2011
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition  Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Eight lots of land called Heerassamulla Godella alias Magalayamulla
bearing an extent of about 0.0205 Hectare, out of the total extent
shown as Lots "A", "B", "C", "D", "E", "F", "G" and "H"  depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. MR/ KMB/2008/ 103 dated 29.10.2008
prepared by the  Survey Superintendant of Matara district, situated
in the village of  Ullala East No. 329 in the Minor Division  of
Gangabada Patthuwa North  in  the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Kamburupitiya in Matara District and bounded as below:
01. Lot  “A” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0017 Hectare.
North: Land of S. G. L Chandrasena and Land of H. K. Nandasiri;
East: Lots  B and J; South: Lot J and Road (Pradeshiya Saba); West:
Land of S. G. L. Chandrasena.
02. Lot  “B” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0075Hectare.
North: Land of  H. K. Nandasena; East: Land of H. K. Nandasena
and Land of P. M. Kusumawathi ; South: Lot J ; West: Lots J, and A
and Land of S.G. L. Chandrasena.
03. Lot  “C” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0037 Hectare.
North: Lot J ; East: Lot J and  Land of P. Piyasena ; South: Land
of P. Piyasena ; West: Lot J.
04. Lot  “D” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0001 Hectare.
North: Lot J ; East: Land of  Allawela Gamage Sunil ; South: Land
of P. Piyasena ; West: Lot J.
05. Lot  “E” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0046 Hectare.
North: Lot J ; East: Lot J and  Land of Allawela Gamage Sunil ;
South and West: Land of Allawela Gamage Sunil.
06. Lot  “F” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0002 Hectare.
North: Land of P . M. Kusumawathi ; East and South: Lot J ; West:
Lot B and Land of  H. K. Nandasiri.
07. Lot  “G” - Heerassamulla Godella - Extent: 0.0009 Hectare.
North: Land of P. M. Kusumawathi ; East: Land of Gamage
