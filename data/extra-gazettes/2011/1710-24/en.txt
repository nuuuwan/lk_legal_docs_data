EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KORALEPATTU  NORTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Seeniththamby Perinbarasa the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated Mr.  Nagamani Kamalanadan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer of  Koralepattu North
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  Nagamani Kamalanadan elected as a member  of  the  Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
08th June, 2011.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  KORALEPATTU  NORTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in  Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased
to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Seeniththamby Perinbarasa the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the member of that Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  a  Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Vadivel Sachchithanandam to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer of  Koralepattu North
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  V adivel Sachchithanandam elected as Vice Chairman  of  the  Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A
SANKA RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha.
Batticaloa,
08th June, 2011.

ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Navaretnam Ruvendiran the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated Mr.  Nagan  Ilayathambi  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer of  Koralepattu North
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  Nagan Ilayathambi elected as a member  of  the  Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A
SANKA RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha.
