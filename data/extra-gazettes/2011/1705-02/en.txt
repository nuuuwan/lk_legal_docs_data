MINUTE  OF  THE  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC  MANAGEMENT  ASSISTANTS’   SERVICE
Amendment No. 03
CENTRAL Provincial Public Service Commission has ordered to amend the Central Provincial Public Management Assistants’
Service Minute published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1381/18 dated
25th February 2005 as follows :
Following new subsections shall be included at the end of the sub section 14.3.4 :
(a) Those who joined the Central Provincial Management Assistants’ Service before 24.12.2004 and are due to
retire before 01.07.2012, shall be exempted from completing the requirement of the second language stipulated
in the service Minute.
(b) Rest of the officers shall satisfy the requirement of possessing proficiency in the second language prescribed
in the Central Provincial Management Assistants’ Service Minute.
(c) All the officers in Central Provincial Management Assistants’ Service shall be granted the entitlement to
earn their salary increments and promotions without satisfying the requirement of possessing the proficiency
in the second language up to 30.06.2012.
