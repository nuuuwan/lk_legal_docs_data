SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25 OF  1973
Regulations Under Sections 32 and 33
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32 and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage,
Minister of Sports, shall hereby inform that the appointments made in terms of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1686/2 dated
27.12.2010, to the National Association of Cricket which was earlier known as Board of Control for Cricket, to carry out the
functions of Sri Lanka Cricket until an election is held or until further notice, were cancelled on 30.06.2011 and the names of
persons appointed on 01.07.2011, to carry out the functions of Sri Lanka Cricket until an election is held or until further notice,
are as follows :-
Mr. H. K. Upali Dharmadasa – Chairman ;
Mr. Prakash Schaffter – Secretary ;
Mr. Nimal Perera – Treasurer ;
Mr. Sidath Weththamuny – Member.
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
1st July, 2011.

SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25 OF  1973
Regulations Under Sections 32 and 33
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32(C)  and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Mahindananda
Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports, suspend with effect from 01.06.2011 the registration  of the Netball Association of Sri
Lanka which is the National Association for Netball, and do hereby inform that the list of name of the persons appointed as
an interim measure with effect from 01.06.2011 to continue with the activities of Sri Lanka Netball Association until and
election will be held in due course, will be as follows :–
(1) Ms. Tamara Dharmakeerthi Herath – President ;
(2) Ms. Kumuduni Gomes – Secretary ;
(3) Ms. Vigitha Rathnayaka – Treasurer/Member ;
(4) Ms. Chandrani Pathiraja – Member ;
(5) Mr. Kishan Goerge – Member.
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
1st June, 2011.

SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25 OF  1973
Order Under Sections 32 and 33
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32(C)  and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Mahindananda
Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports, suspend with effect from 01.05.2011 the registration  of the National Shooting Sports
Federation of Sri Lanka which is the National Association for Shooting Sports, and do hereby inform that the list of names
of the persons appointed as an interim measure with effect from 26.05.2011 to continue with the activities of National
Shooting Sports Federation of Sri Lanka until an election will be held in due course, will be as follows :–
(1) Commodore Shemal Fernando ;
(2) Lt. Col. Dushan Wijewickrema ;
(3) Mr. Waruna Deepal Liyanarachchi ;
(4) Brig. K. W. Edima ;
(5) Air Commodore K. Yahampath ;
(6) Mr. U. K. Dissanayake ;
(7) Mr. Zal Chitty ;
(8) Mr. Elmore Rodrigo ;
(9) Mr. Hemasiri Fernando ;
(10) Mr. S. A. Milroy Peries.
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
1st June, 2011.
