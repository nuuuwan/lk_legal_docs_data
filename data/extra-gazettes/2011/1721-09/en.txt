THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land described
below in the 1st column is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in the 2nd
column from which land that the servitude will be acquired.
1. Acquisition of servitude over the land for the construction and
maintenance of Transmission Main from Atalis Intake of Ginigathhena
Water Supply Scheme of the Nuwara Eliya District Group Water
Supply Project.
