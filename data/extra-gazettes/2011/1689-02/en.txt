1A
THE  FINANCE  ACT, NO. 11  OF  2002
Order  under  section  2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of  2002, as last amended by Finance
(Amendment) Act No. 13 of 2009, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance & Planning, do by this Order declare that, the
Dairy Animals for Breeding Purposes under H. S. Codes 0102.10 and 0104.20.10 and Milking Machines Machinery and  Parts
under H. S. Codes 8434.10, 8434.20 and 8434.90 imported into Sri Lanka shall be exempted from the payment of Port & Airport
Development Levy with effect from 18th January, 2011.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance & Planning.
Ministry of Finance & Planning,
Colombo 01,
17th January 2011.
