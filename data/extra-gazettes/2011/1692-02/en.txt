CORRECTION  PAPER
SRI  LANKA  LAND  RECLAMATION  AND  DEVELOPMENT  CORPORATION  ACT  No. 15  OF  1968  AS
AMENDED  BY  SRI  LANKA   LAND  RECLAMATION  AND  DEVELOPMENT
CORPORATION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT  No. 35  OF  2006   AND  ACT  No. 52  OF  1982
AND ACT No. 27 OF 1976
Order  under  Section  2(b) 1
THE low land, marshe, barren or muddy Land areas situated in Colombo, Kaluthara, and Gampaha districts of the Western
Province, described in the relevant schedules and published in Part I Section (1), General Government Notifications in the
Gazette No. 1545/10 dated 17 April 2008 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka by Hon. Dinesh Gunawardhana,
subject Minister of Urban Development and Sacred Area Development has determined with the concurrence of the subject
Minister of Land and has been declared under the above Order. I insert below mentioned terms and conditions prescribed by
the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation to the Order under Section 2(b)1 of the said Act, I hereby
order to adhere those terms and conditions in carrying out any activity in this area.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence.
Ministry of Defence,
15/5,
Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03.
TERMS  AND  CONDITIONS
1. The written approval of the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation shall be obtained for filling or
developing a portion of Land located within any of these declared areas through a request made using an application
issued by the Corporation for that purpose.
2. The approval would be granted in terms of the recommended Terms and Conditions by a Planning Committee
comprising  the officers of the Urban Development Authority, Central Environmental Authority, Department of
Agrarian Services and following officers of the Sri Lanka Reclamation and Development Corporation , Chief Executive
officer / General Manager, Additional General Manager, Deputy General  Manager (Special Projects), Deputy General

6. The recommendations of the other institutions concerned to be obtained for the respective development purpose of
the land proposed for development.
7. Any individual authorized by the Chief Executive Officer of the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development

disturbs such access could be convicted guilty under the provisions of the Act.
8. Every individual who does not adhere to the terms and conditions published through this Gazette Notifications, is
guilty under the Corporation Act.

future shall be adhered to.
