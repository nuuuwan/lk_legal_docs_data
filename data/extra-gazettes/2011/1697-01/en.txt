TRANSFER OF STAMP DUTY STATUTE OF THE EASTERN PROVINCE, No. 06 OF 2010
A Statute to provide for the transfer of Stamp Duty collected under the Finance Statute No.0l of 2008 of the Eastern
Province to the local authorities in the Eastern Province, remittance of administrative cost to the Provincial Fund and
incidental thereto –
Be it enacted by the Provincial Council of the Eastern Province as follows :
Short Title
Transfer of
Stamp Duty
01 This Statute may be cited as, the Transfer of Stamp Duty Statute of the Eastern Province,
No. 06 of  2010.
02 (1) Where the Provincial Council has charged and collected, in terms of the Finance Statute of
the Eastern Province No. 01 of 2008, any Stamp Duty (hereinafter referred to as the Duty)
on any instrument affecting to a transfer of any immovable property situated within the
administrative limits of any Local Authority in the Eastern Province and paid to the provincial
Fund of the Eastern Province, the Provincial Council may, on a claim made in writing as
provided under Annexure I of this Statute by the Administrative Officer of that Local Authority
to the Minister in charge of the Subject of Finance of the Eastern Province within a period

 (2) Any Duty not claimed by a Local Authority or Local Authorities within the period as described
under sub section (1) and at the expiry of the period of 2 years as described the Duty as on
the first day of January of the following year shall be deemed to have been transferred to
the Provincial Fund Provided however that the Duty of the local authority whose Duty has
been transferred to the Provincial Fund, may submit late claim to the Minister and if the
Minister after having satisfied with the late claim, direct the authority to release such funds
as provided under Sub Section (1).
(3) Under subsection (1), in the event of the transfer of the Duty to such local authority a sum
equivalent to one per centum  (01%) thereon shall be deducted by the Provincial Council
and credited to the Provincial Fund of the Eastern Province being the expenditure of every
description incurred by the Provincial Council in connection with the collecting and transfer
of the Duty.
This provision shall not apply to the years prior to this statute coming into existence.
03. No. claim under Sub Sections (1) and (2) of Section 2 shall be valid unless accompanied a Schedule
perpared in the manner as prescribed under Annexure II to this Statute for the transfer of the
Duty specified in that claim and certified by the Registrar General or any officer authorized by
him in that behalf.
04.  In this statute, unless the context otherwise requires–
"Administrative Officer" means :-
(a) In relation to Municipal Council, the Municipal Commissioner; and
(b) In relation to an Urban Council or a Pradeshiya Sabha, the Secretary thereof :
“Instrument’’ means the same meaning  as provided in the Finance Statute of the Eastern Province
No.01 of 2008 ;
“Local Authorities” means any Municipal Council, Urban Council and Pradeshiya Sabha and includes
any Authority created and established by or Under any law to exercise, perform and discharge
powers, functions and duties and functions corresponding to or similar to the powers, duties
and functions exercised, performed and discharged by any such Councilor Sabah ;
“Provincial Council ” means the Provincial Council established for the Eastern Province under and by
virtue of Article 154 A(l) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
“Minister” means the Provincial Minister in charge for the Subject of Finance in the Eastern Province ;
“Duty” means the Stamp Duty provided under Chapter 10 of Section 37 of Sub Section (a) of the Finance
Act, No. 1 of 2008.
05. In the event of any inconsistancy between the Sinhala and Tamil text of this Statute, the Tamil
text shall prevail.
