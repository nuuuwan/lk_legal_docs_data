MINISTRY  OF  CIVIL  AVIATION
Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka
NOTICE BY THE DIRECTOR GENERAL OF CIVIL AVIATION UNDER THE AIR NA VIGA TION REGULATION 196
MEMBERS of the Public are hereby notified that I have received on 07th February, 2011 an application dated 02nd February,
2011 from Breeze Liner (Pvt.) Ltd. at No. 663, Kerangapokuna, Mabole, Wattala, seeking from this office an Air Operator
Certificate/Airline License to engage in Domestic Scheduled Operations, Charter License to engage in Domestic Charter
Operations and Aerial Work License engage in Domestic Aerial Work Operations in pursuance of the Air Navigation Regulations
of 1955. The Members of the Board of Directors of Breeze Liner (Pvt.) Ltd. is declared to be as follows :—
Mr. Elegodage Prashanth Wijedasa,
Mr. Wellapunidalage Don Adolf   Leo
Breeze Liner (Pvt.) Ltd. has nominated Mr. Rienzie Fernando to be the Accountable Manager in-charge of the
proposed operations of air services.
A copy of the complete application of the aforementioned applicant is kept at the Technical Library, Ground Floor of
Headquarters of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 64, Galle Road, Colombo 03, for the perusal of the Members of
the Public  during office hours of any working day.
Members of the Public are hereby informed that, I have decided to commence after 23rd March 2011, the Certification
process of this application as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant's compliance with the
applicable Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended Practices specified by ICAO and associated
local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure, efficient operation of
proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Airline License, Charter License and Aerial
Work License together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
