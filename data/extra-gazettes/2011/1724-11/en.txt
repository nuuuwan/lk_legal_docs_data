THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 3/2/6/4/1/23.
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/05/2008/CEB/479.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for the Construction works of Switch Yard,
under Upper Kotmale Hydro Power Project.
Description of the land to be acquired :
The land in total extent 0.1970 Hectare, shown  in the Preliminary
Plan No. PP Nu 2751 and dated 15.08.2011, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Niyangandora, Udapane
Korale Minor Division, Kotmale Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Nuwara Eliya District.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Watawana kumbura; Claimant: U. G. Punchibanda;
Extent: 0.1970 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Kotmale on the 29th day of November,
2011 at  9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of November, 2011, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. M. M. U. B. Herath,
Divisional Secretary,
Kotmale.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kotmale,
16th September, 2011.
