THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 115/13/01/13.
 Ministry of Land  Ref. No.: 13/99/VE/257.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for  a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
An allotment  of  land depicted as Lot Nos. 01, 02, and 03 in plan
P.P.K. 2779 Surveyed and made by Surveyor General and situated
in  the Village of Balathgamuwa, in the Minor Division of
Thumpalatha Paththuwa East  in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Mawanella  in the District of Kegalle.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Ambuluwawa Watta; Claimant: Land Reconstruction
Commission; Original Claimant: -; Extent: 20.124 Hectares.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Ambuluwawa Watta;  Claimant: Land
Reconstruction Commission; Original Claimant: -; Extent: 0.247
Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Ambuluwawa Watta; Claimant: H. M. M. Raseek,
Ambulawawa Watta, Sinhapitiya, Gampola; Original Claimant: -;
Extent: 2.462 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Mawanella, on the 21st day of February,
2011 at 8.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of February, 2011, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
A. W. Rukmani Ariyarathna,
