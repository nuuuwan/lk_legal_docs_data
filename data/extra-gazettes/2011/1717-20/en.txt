THE  FINANCE  ACT,  No. 11 OF  2004
Order   under  Section 26
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 26 read with Section 22 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2004 and Article
44 (2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, the President of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order, repeal the regulation 9 published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1620/5 of 22nd September, 2009 and substitute of the following hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Ministry of Finance and Planning, President,
Colombo 01, Minister of Finance and Planning.
27th July, 2011.
REGULATIONS
9. Each Domestic PSTN Operator may claim two-thirds of the TDC funds for the development of their
telecommunication networks in unserved and underserved areas of Sri Lanka as may be determined by the Commission. Each
Domestic PSTN Operator shall be entitled to claim only the TDC payments made in their individual name. Such operator shall
be eligible to claim the two-thirds of the TDC of the particular year, within a period of three years, provided that for any period
stipulated in Column II of the Schedule below, such operator shall be eligible to claim up to the corresponding date as
stipulated in Column III of the Schedule.
