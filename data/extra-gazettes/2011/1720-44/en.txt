THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Hak/5/1/1/94.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.

The allotment of land, depicted in Additional No. 10 in Final
Village Plan No. 187, prepared by Survey Superintendent of Matara
District, situated at Kongala village in Sub-division of
Kandabadapattuwa East in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Hakmana in Matara District in Southern Province:
Lot No. 195 – Kongalayaya – Extent: 0.0002 Hectare
North: Road; East and South: Lot No. 196; West: Lot No. 30.
Lot No. 196 – Kongalayaya – Extent: 0.0049 Hectare
North: Road; East: Lot No. 167 and Main Road; South: Lot Nos.
167, 170 and 197; West: Lot No. 30.
Lot No. 197 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0196 Hectare
North: Lot No. 30; East and South: Lot Nos. 196, 167, 170 and
Canal; West: Lot No. 30.
Lot No. 198 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0138 Hectare
North: Lot No. 30 and Canal; East: Canal and Lot No. 175; South:
Lot No. 175; West: Lot Nos. 199 and 30.
Lot No. 199 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0047 Hectare
North: Lot No. 30; East: Lot Nos. 198, 175 and 185; South: Lot
Nos. 185 and 200; West: Lot Nos. 200 and 30.
Lot No. 200 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0076 Hectare
North: Lot No. 30; East: Lot Nos. 199, 185 and 186; South: Lot
Nos. 186 and 184; West: Canal and Lot No. 30.
Lot No. 201 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0006 Hectare
North: Canal and Lot No. 184; East: Lot Nos. 184 and 187; South:
Lot Nos. 187 and 189; West: Lot No. 202.
Lot No. 202 – Motamure – Extent: 0.0014 Hectare
North: Lot No. 30; East: Canal and Lot Nos. 201, 187 and 189;
South: Lot Nos. 201, 187 and 189; West: Lot No. 30.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Hakmana, on the 28th day of September,
2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 28th day of September, 2011,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
