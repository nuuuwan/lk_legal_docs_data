NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  U. Amaradasa , Returning  Officer of the Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare  under
Section  10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Surendra Bandara of the
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, has vacated his office of  the member of  that  Pradeshiya Sabha as
he  has  ceased  to be a  member of the United National Party.
U. AMARADASA,
  Returning Officer,
Kandy Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
16th  March,  2011.
