SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2(1) and Section 4 of
the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 and Section 2
of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Gusthinnawadu Shan Wijayalal de Silva, the Minister in charge of the subjects of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local
Government of the Southern Province Provincial Council, acting under the powers vested in me in terms of Section 277 of the
Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, did cause an inquiry to be held by a retired judicial officer
appointed by me to inquire into and find out whether Mr. Subhawickrema Mallika Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha, the Mayor
of Matara Municipal Council had, in the performance of his functions as the Mayor of Matara Municipal Council committed
any acts of offence falling under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) and Section 2(1) of the
Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern
Province Provincial Council. On the basis of the findings of the said inquiry I am satisfied that the said Mr. Subhawickrema
Mallika Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha, the Mayor of the said Matara Municipal Council had committed such offences
falling under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) and Section 2(1) of the Powers of Supervision
of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council. As
such in terms of Section 277(i)(1) of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2(1) of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial
Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, I remove Mr. Subhawickrema
Mallika Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha, the Mayor of Matara Municipal Council from the office of Mayor and membership of
the Matara Municipal Council.
G
USTHINNAW ADU SHAN WIJAY ALAL DE SIL V A,
Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity,
Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and
Local Government, Southern Province.
31st December, 2010.
