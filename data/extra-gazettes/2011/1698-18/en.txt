PROMOTION AND APPOINTMENT APPROVED BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, to promote Air Vice Marshal Harsha Duminda
Abeywickrama, RWP, RSP, USP Rcds psc to the rank of Air Marshal and to appoint him under Article 61E (a) of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka Air Force, with effect from February 28, 2011.
     By  His Excellency's command,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
President's Office,
Colombo 01,
21st  March,  2011.
