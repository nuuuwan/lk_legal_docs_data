STATE   INDUSTRIAL   CORPORATIONS  ACT, No. 49 OF 1957
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of Section 2 of the State Industrial Corporation Act, No. 49 of 1957, I,
Rajitha Senaratne Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development, amend the Order establishing the Corporation
known as the Ceylon Fisheries Corporation made under Section 2 of the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 14186 of October 1, 1964, by the substitution for item (V) of such Order of the following item :
“(v) shall carry out the following activities :-
(a) engaging in fishing operation within the Exclusive Economic Zone and beyond in accordance with accepted
rules of international law ;
(b) the processing of fish ;
(c) marketing of fish at foreign and local markets ;
(d) where in appears to be so required, to carry out such activities as are necessary for the import and export
of fish and fish products.
(e) to enter into joint venture agreements with local and foreign investors, for the carrying out of any of the
above activities ;
Provided, that such agreements should not in anyway have an adverse impact on the national
interest of Sri Lanka in engaging in activities with foreign investors, to ensure supply of fish to the
consumers at a reasonable price, in keeping, with Government Policy and local and international market
rules ; and for this purpose to obtain the approval of the External Resources Department, and the Department
of Plan Implementation of the Ministry of Finance ;
