NOTICE  UNDER  THE  EXCHANGE  CONTROL  ACT  (CHAPTER  423  OF  THE  CLE)
Permission  in terms of Section 5 and 7 of the Exchange  Control  Act
Permission is hereby granted to Sri Lankan Citizens who have proceeded outside Sri Lanka for educational purposes to
borrow from banks, financial institutions, universities or educational institutions in foreign countries and to repay such
loans by remitting funds from Sri Lanka through authorized dealers.
