EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   ERA VURPATTU  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  chairman in Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha   has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Mr. Samiththamby Jeewarangan, the Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  decide upon  the  member of  that  Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha   who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected
as   Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision  to  nominate
Mr.  Sidambarapillai Vinoth,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Rathanyaka Ralalage Asanka  Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning    Officer of  Eravurpattu
Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of Section 65A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr. Sidambarapillai Vinoth elected as Chairman of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha
A
SANKA RA THNAY AKA
Returning Officer,
 Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha .
Elections Office,
Batticoloa,
18th August 2011.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ERA VURPATTU  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha   has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Samiththamby Jeewarangan, the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election  as a  member of  that Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member   within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated    Mr.  Kandiah Mahalingam
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance    to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,   Rathanyaka Ralalage Asanka  Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning  Officer of  Eravurpattu
Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of Section 65A (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr. Kandiah Mahalingam  elected as  a member  of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha
A
SANKA RATHNAY AKA
Returning Officer,
 Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha .
Elections Office,
Batticoloa,
18th August 2011.
