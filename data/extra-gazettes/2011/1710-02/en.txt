THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 3/2/6/1/6.
Land Ministry  Reference No.: 13/96/E/614.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for Pundaluoya Sinhala Maha Vidyalaya.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Land total extent of about 0.272 Hectare, shown in the preliminary
Plan No. PPNu 2007 and dated on 17th November 1999,prepared
by Surveyor-General, situated in Kadadorapitiya Ihalagama village,
Udapane Korale minor division, Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kothmale in Nuwara-Eliya District:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Hawanwewa Deniya - Pundaluoya South;
Description: Land needed for Pundaluoya Sinhala Maha Vidyalaya;
Claimants: 1. W. Amarasinghe Appuhami, School Road, Pundaluoya,
2. K. G. Punchimenike, School Road, Pundaluoya, 3. A. G.
Bisomenike, North, Pundaluoya; Extent: 0.272 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Kothmale on the 23rd day of August 2011
at the 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of August, 2011, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. M. M. U. B. Herath,
Divisional Secretary,
Kothmale.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kothmale,
6th June, 2011.
