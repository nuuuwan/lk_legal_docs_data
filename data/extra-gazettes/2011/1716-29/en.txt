 A Statute to make provisions to provide domestic and Mobile telephone facilities for official
duties to Members of Pradeshiya Sabha of the Western Province.
Be it enacted as follows by the Western Province Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka :-
1. This statute is cited as Statute to Provide Domestic and Mobile Telephone Facilities
Pertaining to official Duties to the Members of Pradeshiya Sabha of the Western Province,
No. 10 of 2010 and it shall be effected from January 01st of the Calendar year followed by the
date  on which the signature of the Governor is placed.

2. It is legal to bear expenditure from the Pradeshiya Sabha Fund. by a Pradeshiya Sabha, for
the provision of residential and mobile telephone facilities for the official duties of the
Members elected to the Pradeshiya Sabha and maintenance of them.
3. Maximum expenditure limits in lieu of the settlement of residential and mobile telephone
bills for official duties of the Members of Pradeshiya Sabha shall be the amounts regulated
by the rules enacted under this statute by the Minister.
4. The Minister can formulate rules with regard to the tasks mentioned in this statute.
5. (i) The rules composed by the Minister shall be published in the Gazette paper and
subsequent to publishing thus ; they shall be referred to the Provincial Council for approval
as immediately as possible.
(ii) Subsequent to receiving the approval by the Provincial Council regarding every rule
composed by the Minister, it shall be published through a Gazette Paper whereas, that
approved notification shall be enacted from the date it is published in the Gazette paper,
6. Unless other meaning is required regarding words, in this statute, the ''Minister in charge of
