THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: MR/Div./Sec./Athu/4/4/01.
Land Ministry’s No.: 13/2000/E/31.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
One (01) allotment of land called Karagahawaththa alias
Kandegewaththa also called Meddegedarawaththa in total extent of
about 0.7800  Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 12  in the (F.V .P. No. 60)
Preliminary Plan made by the Superintendent of  Surveys Matara
situated in the Village of Gadippala, in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Athuraliya, in Matara district in Southern Province is
bounded as follows.
North: Nilwala River, Panadugama, F.V .P. 70; East: F.V .P. 70,
Panadugama, Lot Nos. 3 and 6;South: Lot Nos. 3 and 6, Nilwala
River; West:   Nilwala River.
All persons interested in the afore said land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretariat  Athuraliya, on 11th day of May,
2011 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th day of May, 2011, the
nature of their interests in the land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
I. S. Samarakoon,
Divisional Secretary,
Athuraliya.
Divisional Secretariat,
Athuraliya.
