REGISTERED  STOCK  AND  SECURITIES  ORDINANCE
WHEREAS a loan for Two Million and Four Hundred Thousand Rupees (Rs. 2,400,000) was raised in the form of Registered
Stock in terms of an Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1478/18 dated 04 January 2007 under the provisions of
Section 4 of the Registered Stock and Securities Ordinance (Chapter 420) as amended;
And whereas the date of redemption for the said Registered Stock was specified in the said Order as the 01st day
of  October, 2014 but an option for the early redemption of  the said Registered Stock at par at a date subsequent to the 1st
day of  October, 2010 as may be appointed by the Minister of Finance by a Notification published in the Gazette not less than
six months prior to such date, was retained in the said Order in terms of the provisions of subsection 3 of  section 4 of the said
Ordinance;
And whereas the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka has decided to exercise the said
option;
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister  in charge of the subject of  Finance do hereby appoint the 30th Day of August 2011
as the date for redeeming the said Registered Stock.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Ministry of Finance and Planning, Minister of Finance and Planning.
Colombo 01,
25th February  2011.
