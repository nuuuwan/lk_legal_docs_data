EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KOTIIKAWATTE  -  MULLERIYAWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  deth  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Kaluthanthreege Kemathissa the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa  Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated Mr.  Thilakamuni Ajith
Gunasekara Soyza being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,  Returning  Officer of  Kotikawatte -
Mulleriyawa  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare  Mr.  Thilakamuni Ajith Gunasekara Soyza elected as a member  of  the  Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
J. A. S. P. J
AYASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
20th June, 2011.
