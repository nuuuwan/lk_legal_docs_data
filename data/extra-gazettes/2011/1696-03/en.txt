I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
 Three (03)  allotments of land depicted in Surveyor General's
Preliminary Plan No. Co.  9116 dated 14th October, 2010 as Lot No.
1-3 with the total extent of 0.1479 Hectare,  situated in the village of
Godagama  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Homagama in
Colombo District in Western Province and described as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Assmt. No. No. 54/1 (part) of Borella Road;
Description: Garden, contains mixed  cultivation; Claimant: State;
Original claimant: Chamath Nishantha Liyanage of  No. 134, Dencil
Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Homagama and Others; Extent: 0.0529
Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Assmt. No. No. 54/1 (part) of Borella Road;
Description: Path; Claimant: State; Original claimant: Chamath
Nishantha Liyanage of  No. 134, Dencil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Homagama and Others; Extent: 0.0140 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of land: Assmt. No. No. 54/1 (part) of Borella Road;
Description: Garden, contains mixed  cultivation,  a permanent
building part of a permanent building and parts of two temporary
buildings; Claimant: State; Original claimant: Chamath Nishantha
Liyanage of  No. 134, Dencil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Homagama
and Others; Extent: 0.0810 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Divisional Secretary's office of Homagama, on the 16th
day of May, 2011 at  10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 09th day of May,
2011, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
producet at the inquary.
A. J. Karunarathna,
Divisional Secretary,
