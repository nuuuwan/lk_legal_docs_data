EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  KANDY  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kandy  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Sadasiwam Sasikumar   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Kandy  Municipal Council   who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within  a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Thumbane Thriboovana
Keerthisinghe Dunuweera   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  U. Amaradasa,  Returning Officer of  Kandy Municipal Council , acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Thumbane Thriboovana Keerthisinghe
Dunuweera elected as a member  of   the Kandy  Municipal Council.
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
03rd  January  2011.
