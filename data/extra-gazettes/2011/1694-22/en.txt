1A
A STATUTE TO AMEND THE APPROPRIATION STA TUTE NO. 06 OF 2009.
BE it enacted by the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as
follows :—
1. This Statute may be cited as “The  Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council Appropriation (Amendment)
Statute No. 05 of 2010, and shall come into operation on the date of receiving the assent of the Governor of the Sabaragamuwa
Province.
2. Paragraph 2 of the Appropriation Statute No. 06 of 2009, the expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial
Council which it is estimated Rupees Thirteen Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty Eight Million Seven Hundred and Sixty
Nine Thousand only (Rs. 13,828,769,000) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2010 and ending 31st
December, 2010 in this Statute referred to as the financial year 2010, schedule I as specified in below shall be met is amended
as the expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated will be Rupees Fourteen Thousand Two
Hundred and Eighty Six Million Two Hundred and Twenty Five Thousand Nine Hundred and Cents Forty only
(Rs. 14,286,225,900.40) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2010 and ending 31st December, 2010.
