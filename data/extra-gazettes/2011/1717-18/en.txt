NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry conducted under Section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
IN terms of the powers vested in me under sub section 185 3(i) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with section
2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Athula Wijesingha, Chief Minister of the North Western
Province and Minister of Local Government to hereby appoint Retired High Court Judge, O. W. O. B. Weerasena esquire as
a Single Person Tribunal of Inquiry to inquire into whether the offences mentioned in section 185 were committed by the
Chairman S. M. Bandula Pushpakumara while holding his office as the Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho on the following
grounds / allegations in terms of section 185 and a report may be submitted within a period of 03 months.
Facts and charges to be inquired into
CHARGES
(i) During his tenure of office as Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho from on or about 01st of April, 2011 up
to now, has committed the offence/offences stated in section/sections 185 (1) (a) and / or 185 (1) (d) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by violating the directions of C.L.G . / Circular No. 1, 2010 dated 27.07.2010
of the Commissioner of Local Government in the North Western Province, by taking measures to recover a sum
of Rupees One Thousand Nine Hundred and Fifty Five (Rs. 1,955.00) by Receipt No. 79488 of Pradeshiya Sabha
Maho from M. T. Jaleel of Atawarala, Maho on or about 03.05.2011 for the issue of street lines certificate in
approving the building Application in respect of the buildings constructed within the area of authority of
Pradeshiya Sabha Maho.
(ii) During his tenure of office as Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho from on or about 01st of April, 2011 to
on or about 24.06.2011, has committed the offence/offences stated in section/sections 185 (1) (a) and / or 185 (1)
(b) and / or 185 (1) (e) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by violating the section 174 of Pradeshiya Sabha
Act, No. 15 of 1987 by taking measures to make payments a sum of Rupees Twenty Two Thousand Two
Hundred and Forty Five (Rs. 22,245.00) by paying voucher No. 435 from the General fund of the Pradeshiya
Sabha to K. M. Aruna Pushpakumara of Maho on or about 21.04.2011 for the supply of stationeries required for
the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho without a formal resolution of Pradeshiya Sabha.

(vi) During his tenure of office as Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho from on or about 01st of April, 2011 up

(e) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by taking measures to pay sum of Rupees Thirty Eight Thousand

Company, No. 68, Maliban Street, Colombo 11 on or about 25th of April of 2011 for the supply of street lamp

to now, committed the offence/offences stated in section/sections 185 (1) (a) and / or 185 (1) (b) and / or 185 (1)
(e) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, by taking measures to pay a sum of Rupees Seventy Six Thousand
Four Hundred and Seventy Five (Rs. 76,475.00) to Mr. R. M. L. K. Rajapaksha on or about 26.04.2011 without a
formal resolution of the Pradeshiya Sabha for the preparation of plans and estimates for the Public Library and
Solid Waste Management Project, Balalla of the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho.
