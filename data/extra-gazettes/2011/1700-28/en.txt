SRI  LANKA   ELECTRICITY  ACT,  No.  20  OF  2009
Order under Sub Section (1) of the Section 46
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested  by Sub Section (1) of the  Section 46 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009, the
Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka does hereby impose and annual levy in such amounts as are set out in Column II of
the Schedule hereto, to be recovered from those licensees holding such licenses as are specified in the corresponding entry
in Column I of that schedule. The previous Gazette Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1605/19 on
Wednesday 10th June, 2009 is hereby revoked.
