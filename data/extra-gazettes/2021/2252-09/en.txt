SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order Under Section 5
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 5 of the special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, basil rajapaksa, 
minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do hereby make and order to waive-off the balance 
amount after collecting rs. 1 per Kg from the applicable special Commodity Levy of rs. 25 per Kg on importation of maize 
(classified under the HS Code of 1005.90) by Gazette Notification No. 2228/3 dated May 17,2021 under Section 2 of the 
special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 for production of "Thriposha", by the Government owned sri Lanka Thriposha 
Limited up to a total of 2000 mT's.
   This Order shall be valid for a period of 01 month commencing from November 03, 2021 subject to provisions of 
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007. 
 
 Basil Rajapaksa,
 minister of Finance.
ministry of Finance,
