PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34073—35 (03/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Point Pedro Urban Council
I,  Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Point Pedro Urban Council, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Nadarasa Nirainjan of  the Point Pedro Urban
Council has vacated his office of member of that  Point Pedro Urban Council as he has ceased to be a member of the Tamil
United Liberation Front.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Point Pedro Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
25th  March, 2021.
