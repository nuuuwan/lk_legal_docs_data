PART IV (A) - PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  
 
 
 
PROCLAMATIONS & C.,  BY THE GOVERNORS 
 
Southern Province Provincial Council 
 
The Order given in accordance with the Article 248 of Urban Council Ordinance which is the 
Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2(1) (a) of Provincial Councils                        
(Ancillary  Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989. 
 
I, Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province, do hereby pronounce that the orders given to the 
Chairman of the Urban Council of Tangalle, published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2240/14  dated 10.08.2021 of 
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sr i Lanka are forthwith amended as stated below by virtue of authority vested on 
me vide the Article 247 of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2(1) (a) 
of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989. 
 
I do hereby Order, Warnakulasooriya Patabendige Ariyadasa, the Chairman of the Urban Council of Tangalle, that he 
should spend only on recurring expenses from proposed estimate of expenditure included in the Financial Statement for 
2021 submitted for the approval of the council on 07.12.2020 for the purpose of management of financial obligations in 
executing the activities of the Urban Council of Tangalle and is obliged to obtain the approval of the council in case of 
need for expenses of any nature on Capital Expenditure. 
 
  Dr. W. W. GAMAGE, 
 Governor, 
 Southern Province. 
 
On this 29th day of September 2021, 
Southern Province Governor's Secretariat, 
Upper Dickson Road, Galle.
