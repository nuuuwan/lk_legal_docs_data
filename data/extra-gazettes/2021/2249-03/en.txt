SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF 2007
Order under Section 2 and Section 5
1. By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose in
respect of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the  rate
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule

2. By virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I do hereby
make an order to waive-off balance amount after collecting 25 Cents per kg from the applicable Special Commodity
Levy of Rs. 65 per kg on importation of Rice, listed out in the Schedule of the above, by Sri Lanka State Trading
(General) Corporation on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry of Trade.
3. This Order shall be valid for a period of 4 months commencing from October 12, 2021 subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
 Basil Rajapaksa,
