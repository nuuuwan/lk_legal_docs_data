FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KUNDASALE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group number 1 to which Wannisinghe Mudiyanselage
Jayathilaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such Independent Group number 1 and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Group Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group number 1, has communicated his decision to
nominate Prandul Madathenne Gedara Piyathissa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Kundasale
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Prandul Madathenne Gedara Piyathissa was selected as the member of  the Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election  Office,
Kandy,
02nd  July, 2021.
