CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION
THE  CUSTOMS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  235)
Order under Section 10(A)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 10(A) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended by Act,
No. 83 of 1988, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order
levy on imported goods specified in Column I of  the Schedule hereto, a surcharge at the rate specified in the  corresponding
entry in Column II on Customs Duty, both General and Preferential, from February  02, 2021 to February 23, 2021.
The Order made under Section 10(A) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended by Act, No. 83 of 1988 and
published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notifiction No. 2203/1 of 23.11.2020 is rescinded with effect from February 02, 2021.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
February 01, 2021.
