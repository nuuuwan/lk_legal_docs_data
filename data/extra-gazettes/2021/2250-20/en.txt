 FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   mEmbER  OF   ThE  kANThALE  pRADEShIYA  
SAbhA    uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1)  OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE     
(chApTER   262)    
WhereAs the office of  return member of  Kanthale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group-02,  which Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Muthubanda the  member  
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of Kanthale 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group-02 has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kobbekaduwe Dehialawatte Anoma Jayanthi to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Kanthale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Kobbekaduwe Dehialawatte 
Anoma Jayanthi was selected as the member of the Kanthale Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 
S. Suthakaran,
 Returning Officer,
 Kanthale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
18th October, 2021.
