FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF   THE    MAHARAGAMA
URBAN   COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,   No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Maharagama Urban Council h as  fallen vacant by reason of  death  ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group No. 02 to which  Rajapaksha Mohottige Karolis the former
Vice Chairman, belonged was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of  that Maharagama Urban Council who belongs to such Independent Group  who shall be declared elected as
Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Galkanda
Arachchige Sunil Piyarathne to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Galkanda Arachchige Sunil Piyarathne  elected
as Vice Chairman of the Maharagama Urban Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
23rd September, 2021.
