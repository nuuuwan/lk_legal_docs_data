UNIVERSITIES ACT, No.16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No.16 of 1978,1, Prof. G. L. Peiris, the
Minister of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the International
Institute of Health Science (Pvt) Ltd., a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No.7 of 2007,
bearing Registration Number PV 66370 and located at No.700, Negombo Road, Maththumagala, Ragama, do by this
Order and subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the International Institute of Health Science
(Pvt) Ltd, as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of
the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry
in Column II of the Schedule II hereto.
SCHEDULE  I
CONDITIONS
1. International Institute of Health Science (Pvt) Ltd, which has been recognised by this Order as a Degree Awarding
Institute may offer the Degree specified in Schedule II hereto, subject to a subject re-review conducted by the
Specified Authority every five (5) years from the date of publication of this order.
