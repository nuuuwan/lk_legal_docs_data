SPORTS   LAW   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulation made by the Minister of Sports under Sections 32 and 33 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973
REGULATION  UNDER  SECTIONS  32  AND 33
I, the Minister of Youth and Sports, in accordance  with the powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Law, No. 25 of 1973 and its amendments, temporarily suspend the registration of the National Sports Associations/
Federations for the following sports and appoint Mr. Amal Edirisuriya, Director General of the Department of Sports
Development, as the competent authority with effect from 01st July 2021 in respect of administrative and other required
proceedings and to call for and hold the relevant elections as well as to perform the activities of the said National Sports
Associations / Federations until then as a temporary procedure.
01. Netball Federation of Sri Lanka
02. Sri Lanka Judo Association
03. Scrabble Federation of Sri Lanka
04. Surfing Federation of Sri Lanka
05. Sri Lanka Jiu-jitsu Federation.
 N
AMAL RAJAPAKSA (M. P .),
Minister of Youth and Sports.
Colombo,
On 08th day of July, 2021.
