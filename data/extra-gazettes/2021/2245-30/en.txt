PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local  Government  Notifications
COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Standard By-Laws of Colombo Municipal Council
THIS is to bring to the public attention that the following proposal has been put forward by the Colombo Municipal
Council in the Monthly General Meeting convened on the 06th day of September 2021, under the etermination No. 2445.
M. T. M. IQBAL,
Deputy Mayor/Acting Mayor.
At  Colombo Municipal Council,
on the 17th day of September 2021.
Proposal
Colombo Municipal Council hereby proposes to accept and implement the following by-laws in the limits of the
Colombo Municipal Council. Those by-laws are :-
By-laws of the Part I and the contents of the Part II ;
1. Part III - Monetary Order
2. Part V - By-laws relating to Contracts
which billed by the Hon. Minister of Local Authorities, Housing and Constructions and published in the Special
Gazette No. 541/17 dated 20.01.1989 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and confirmed by the Western Province
Council and published in the Special Gazette No. 663/5 dated 21.05.1991 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
under the power vested in pursuant to Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws), No. 6 of 1952.
