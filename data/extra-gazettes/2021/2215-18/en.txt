FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  MAHARA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending Three
consecutive Sabha meetings without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the, United National Party the recognized political party to which Subhasinghe
Nishshanka Aruna Sujeewa Nishshanka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muthugamage Sisira Jayamanna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Mahara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Muthugamage Sisira Jayamanna was selected as the member of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha.
10th February, 2020.
