THE LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,

Notice under  Section  7 
 
Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 4/3/5/2018/HA/156. 
My Ref. No. : 9/3/5/40. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Govenment intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for the Grama Niladhari Office, Walpola. 
 
 Description of the Land / Servitude to be acquired:   
 
An allotment of land in total Extent of 0.0379 Hectare 
from the land called Yatawatta Watta depicted in the 
Preliminary Plan No. මා1831 prepared by the 
Superintendent of Survey, situated in the village called 
Yatawatta Watta in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 
E334D of Maligatenna in Asgiri Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Yatawatta in Mathale District of the Central Province and 
morefully described as follows: 
 
Lot  No. 01                               
Name of the Land: Yatawatta Watta; Description: For 
acquisition of a parcel of land for maintaining Grama 
Niladhari Office, Walpola; Claimant: State Plantations 
Corporation; Extent: 0.0379 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents 
duly authorized in writing before me at the Yatawatta 
Divisional Secretariat on 22.03.2021 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 01.03.2021 the nature of their 
interest in the land/lands, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 D. R. K. H ENNAYAKE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Yatawatta. 
  
  
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Yatawatta, 
20th January, 2021.
