FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     V A VUNIYA    URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Kanthaiya
Rajalingam the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of  that Ward No. 6 Bazaar Street of Vavuniya Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Antony Arumairajan Renuka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Antony Arumairajan Renuka was
elected as the member of the Ward No. 6 Bazaar Street of Vavuniya Urban Council.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
21st September, 2021.
