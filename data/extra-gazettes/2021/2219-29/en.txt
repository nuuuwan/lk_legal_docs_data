EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Notifications under Section 66 g
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Section 66g of the Local Government Elections Ordinance as amended by the Local
Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I,  hereby announce that the Meeting to elect the Chairman of
Talawakele Lindula Urban Council in the Nuwara Eliya District of the Central Province shall be held under Section 66C (4) of
the aforesaid Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, on 31.03.2021 at 10.00 a.m. at the Lindula Urban Council
Meeting Hall, Talawakele.
H. M. M. U. B.  H
ERATH,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Department of Local Government,
Central Province.
15th  March 2021,
Provincial Council Complex,
Pallekele,
Kundasale.
