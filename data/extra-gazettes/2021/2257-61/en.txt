 Forest Conservation Ordinance 
 
The Order 
 
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) that has to be read 
with the notification published in Gazette Extraordinary No.2187/27 dated 09th August 2020 by the President according 
to the constitution 43(1), 44(2) and 46(1)(a), (b) of the constitution of Democratic Socialist Repub lic of Sri Lanka 
respectively, I do by this order declare the state land named Elamaradankulam of 429.17 hectares bounded by the 
limits set forth in the schedule here to as a Reserved Forest from the 30th  day of November 2021. 
  
  
     
 WIMALAWEERA DISSANAYAKE, 
 State Minister of Wildlife Protection, Adoption of Safety Measures  
 including the Construction of Electrical Fences and Trenches 
  and Reforestation and Forest Resources Development. 
  
  
30th day of November 2021, 
Battaramulla, 
State Ministry of Wildlife Protection, Adoption of Safety Measures  
including the Construction of Electrical Fences and  
Trenches and Reforestation and Forest Resources Development.
