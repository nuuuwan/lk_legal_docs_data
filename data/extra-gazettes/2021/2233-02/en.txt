Order under section 9(1)

read with section 10 of the aforesaid Act,  I, Johnston Xavier Fernando, Minister Highways, do by this Order amend the

by the repeal of the Schedule thereto and the substitution therefor of the Schedule set out hereto, in order to provide for the 
addition of the Kapuduwa interchange on the Southern Expressway.

 minister  of  Highways.
