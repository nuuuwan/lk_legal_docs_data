LAND REFORMS ACT No. 01 OF 1972 OF THE NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY 
Order under Section 27 a (4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27 a (4) of the Land Reforms Law No.01 of 1972 amended 
by the Act, Nos. 39 of 1975, 14 of 1981, 39 of 1981, 14 of 1986 and 18 of 1986 and as per the request of the 
Land Reforms Commission, I, S.M.Chandrasena, Minister of Lands of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka, do hereby:-
 (a) Amend the order made under the Section 27 a (l) of the Land Reform Act, published in the  Gazette                             
Extraordinary No.468/21 dated 28 August 1987 relating to the estate land called and known as 
Imbulgaswadiya Watta, morefully described in the First Schedule of this order, alienated to Janatha Estate 
Development Board established under State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972 as per the 
provisions stipulated in the said amended Land Reform Law, by revocation of the such portion of the said 
order as relates to the extent of the land called as Imbulgaswadiya Watta specified in Second Schedule 
hereto by reason of failure to adhere to the regulations and conditions pertain to the consideration for 
alienating the said land to Janatha Estate Development Board; and
