NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL ACT, No. 47 of 1980 
 
Order under Section 24C and 24D 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 24C and 24D of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 , I, 
Mahinda Amaraweera, Minister of Environment do by this Order, amend the Schedule II and Schedule III  to the Order 
made under the aforesaid sections in respect of the area called "Lake Gregory Environmental Protecti on Area" and 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1487/10 of March 5, 2007 as follows :- 
 
 (1)  by the addition immediately after item 15 of the Schedule II, of the following : 
 ''16.  Cable Car Project from Nanu Oya to Nuwara Eliya." 
 
 (2)  by the addition immediately after item 15 of the Schedule III, of the following : 
 ''16.  Construction, Operation and maintenance of Cable Car Project from Nanu Oya to Nuwara Eliya, shall no t 
have adverse impact on the Environmental Protection Area." 
 
 
 MAHINDA AMARAWEERA, 
 Minister of Environment. 
Colombo.
