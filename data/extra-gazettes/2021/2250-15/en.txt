FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NEGOMBO   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Negombo Municipal Counil has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Arachchige Don
Antony Calistar Jayakodi the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  that Ward Number 29 - Kepungoda of Negombo Municipal Counil  who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who
shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Matara Kiriliyanage Liyo Frank Rathanasiri Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Negombo Municipal Counil,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Matara Kiriliyanage Liyo Frank Rathanasiri Fernando was elected as the member of the Ward Number 29 - Kepungoda of
Negombo Municipal Counil.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Counil.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
18th October, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     BALAPITIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABAHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE

Silva the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
