MINUTES OF PENSIONS
Notification under Section 51 of the  Minutes on Pensions dated 05th February, 1934 as amended by
Minutes on Pensions (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICA TION
THE  provisions of Sections 02 and 17 of the Minutes on Pensions amended from time to time, further amended by removing
the clause “The age limit for compulsory retirement of medical officers in the public service will be extended to 61
years” and subsequently adding ;
“The compulsory retirement age for medical officers of all grades in the public service will be extended to 63 years”.
The amendment made to the Minutes on Pensions by this notification shall be treated as effective from 20.04.2021.
