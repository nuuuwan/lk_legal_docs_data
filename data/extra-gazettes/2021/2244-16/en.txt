THE   PUBLIC   SECURITY   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   40)
Notice under Section 2(7)
IT is hereby informed that the Proclamation made by His Excellency the President under Section 2 of the Public Security
Ordinance (Chapter 40), bringing into force throughout Sri Lanka from August 30, 2021 the Emergency Regulations
made in terms of Section 5 of the said Ordinance for the supply of essential food items enforcing the Provisions of Part
II of the aforesaid Ordinance was approved by a resolution of Parliament on September 06, 2021.
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
Colombo,
September 08, 2021.
