FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     LUNUGALA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Obbe Ariyadasalage
Lolitha Priyanka the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abekoon Mudiyanselage Jayaweera Abekoon to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  the Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abekoon Mudiyanselage
Jayaweera Abekoon was selected as the member of  the  Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Lunugala Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Badulla,
09th  April, 2021.
