FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     SAMMANTHURAI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member of  Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Velli Jeyachandran
the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  member of
that Number 10 - Valathappitty of Sammanthurai  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ganesasundaram Kulamani to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Ganesasundaram Kulamani was elected as the member of  the  Number 10 - Valathappitty  of Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Ampara,
26th  March, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KINNIYA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of Kinniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance,  which Umar Ali Ranees the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member of Kinniya
Urban Council  who belongs to such  party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Majeedu Muhammadu Nazik to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Kinniya Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Majeedu Muhammadu
Nazik was selected as the member of  the Kinniya Urban Council.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Kinniya Urban Council.
District Elections  Office,
Trincomalee,
26th March,  2021.
