Greetings !
As you have been appointed as members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management
in the Eastern Province established by the Presidential Directive dated 01.06.2020 by exercising the powers vested in me by
Article 33 of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And, whereas, it has been identified that the Surveyor General, W.T.M.S.B. Tennakoon Esquire, has been retired
from the public service,
And, whereas, it has been identified that the composition of the membership of the said Task Force should be further
widned,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby appoint below-
mentioned officers as the members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management in the Eastern
Province established under the aforesaid Presidential Directive by reposing confidence in their knowhow, wealth of experience
in the related field and their fidelities.
1. Ariyarathna Dissanayaka Esquire
Surveyor-General
2. A. Pathinadan Esquire
Retired Chief Secretary
3. Mufhisal  Abubakkar Esquire
Lecturer
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka on the 24th day of November,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P.  B.  J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
