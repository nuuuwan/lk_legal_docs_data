Greetings !
Whereas, a Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry was appointed by Warrant dated January 28, 2021 in accordance
with the provisions of Section 2 of the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Act, No. 7 of 1978, as amended by the
Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry (Special Provisions) Act, No. 4 of 1978,
And whereas, Hon. Ratnapriya Gurusinghe Esquire, Judge of the Court of Appeal, who is the member of the said
Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry, has resigned his office,

of Section 3 of the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Act, No. 7 of 1978, as amended by the Special Presidential
