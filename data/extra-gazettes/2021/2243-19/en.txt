FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     V ALVETTITHURAI    URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Valvettithurai Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Koonalingam
Karunanantharasa the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of  that Ward  06 - Sivankoviladi of Valvettithurai Urban Council who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Arumairaththinam Satheeswaran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Velvettithurai Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Arumairaththinam Satheeswaran  was elected as the member of the Ward  06 - Sivankoviladi of Valvettithurai Urban
Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valvettithurai Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
02nd September, 2021.
