SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the
commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rate  specified in the corresponding
entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from February 12, 2021, subject to provisions of Sub
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
This Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and  published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2199/1 of 26.10.2020 in respect of Sprats and Dried Fish and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2206/3 of 14.12.2020 in respect of Potatoes are rescinded with effect from February
12, 2021.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance,
Ministry of  Finance,
Colombo 01,
February  11,  2021.
