PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notice  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - G 33594— 45 (01/2021)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10(A)(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Eelctions Ordinance that Mrs. Gajanayaka
Mudiyanselage Premila Janakee of the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, has vacated her office of member of that
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha as she been ceased to be a member of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
26th January, 2021.
