EXPLOSIVES ORDINANCE (AMENDMENT)
BY the powers vested in me under Section 33 of the Explosives Ordinance No. 21 of 1956, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President
and Minister of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, make the following orders.
GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
Ministry of  Defence.
November 29, 2021,
Colombo.
Order
The following new schedule will replace the schedule specifying the relevant fees for clearance permits for dangerous
cargo items given in paragraph 08 in the fifth schedule to the 1979 Explosives Ordinance, published in the Extraordinary
Gazette No. 23/ 5 dated 13.02.1979, as amended by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1985/ 37 dated 23.09.2016.
