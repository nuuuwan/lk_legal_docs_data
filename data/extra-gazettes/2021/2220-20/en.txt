L.D.B. 21/78 (XV)(A)
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 22
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) read with Section 22 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor
G. L. Peiris, Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order,
amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 22 of the aforesaid Act, in relation to the establishment of the
Vavuniya Campus of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 968/6 of March 26,
1997 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2160/43 of January 30, 2020) by the repeal of
the item “Faculty of Business Studies” appearing in Column I of such Schedule and its corresponding entries appearing in
Column II thereto, and the substitution therefore of the following :-
Column I Column II
“Faculty of Business Studies 1. Department of Finance and Accountancy
2. Department of English Language  Teaching
3. Department of Project Management
4. Department of Human Resource Management
5. Department of  Marketing Management
6. Department of Business Economics
7. Department of Management & Enterpreneurship”.
Prof. G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
