Declaration  of  Determination  of  the  Commissioner  of  Title  Settlement  under  Section  14  
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 14 of the Registration of Title Act, No.21 of 1998, I, the undersigned, 
hereby declare my determination as set out in the Schedule appended hereto in regard to the  title to parcel of Land No. 17, 
18, 19, 22, 23, 24 of Block 02, contained in the Cadastral Map No. 320267, situated in the Village of Daskara within the 
Grama Niladhari Division of No. 61 - Daskara in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Udunuwara, in the District of 
Kandy, in the Province of Cental, referred to in Notice for claims to, land parcels which was duly published in the Gazette 
No. 2221/21 of 05th April, 2021 in terms of Section 55 of the Registration of Title Act, No.21 of 1998.
