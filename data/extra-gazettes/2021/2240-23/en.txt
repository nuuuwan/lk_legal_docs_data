THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Order under Section 39A (1)
THE allotments specified in the Order No. 318  of  2014 made
under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition
Act (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette  Extraordinary
No. 1887/43  dated 07th November, 2014 are vested absolutely
in the State and the possession of which has been taken for or
