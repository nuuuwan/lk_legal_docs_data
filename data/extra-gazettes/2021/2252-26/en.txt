THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Johnston Xavier 
Fernando, Minister of Highways, do by this Order, designate the “Meerigama-Kurunegala National Highway (Section of 
Central Expressway)” which was designated as an “Expressway” by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2251/38 
of October 28, 2021 and speciﬁ ed in the Schedule to this Order, to be an “User Fee National Highway”.
J
OHNSTON XA VIER FERNANDO,
Minister of Highways.
Battaramulla,
02nd November, 2021.
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHWAY
The stretch of road being 40.910 kilometers long and 80.000-100.000 metres wide, situated between Meerigama 
(36.981km) and Kurunegala (77.891km) of the Expressway, designated as the Meerigama-Kurunegala Expressway (Section 
of Central Expressway).
