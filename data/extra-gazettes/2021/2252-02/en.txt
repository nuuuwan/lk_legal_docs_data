Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007
Order under Section 12 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 12 (1) of the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007, 
I, Duminda Dissanayake, State Minister of Solar, Wind and Hydro Power Generation Projects Development, in consultation 
with the Ministers in charge of the subjects of Fisheries, Land, Public Services Provincial Council and Local Government, 
Irrigation, Agriculture, Environment, Water Supply, Forest, Wildlife, Mahaweli Development, Religious and Cultural Affairs, 
Palmyrah Development, Coconut Cultivation, Road Development, Archaeology, Urban Development, Coast Conservation, 
Waste Disposal and Public Sanitization and the relevant Ministers of North Western Provincial Council to whom the subject 
of do by this Order declare the areas of lands, described in the Schedule hereto as Energy Development Areas in the Puttalam 
District for the purposes of the aforesaid Act.
 
 D
UMINDA DISSANAYAKE,
 Minister of State Ministry of Solar, Wind and  
 Hydro Power Generation Projects Development.
28th October, 2021.
Colombo.
