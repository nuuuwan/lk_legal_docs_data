FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF   THE     POONAKARY
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)   AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,   No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Sivakumaran
Sriranjan the former Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and  who shall be declared elected as
Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mudiyappu Emiliyampillai to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Winson Gunalan Winson Gnanathayalan, Returning Officer  of  the Poonakary Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017, do hereby declare  Mudiyappu Emiliyampillai  elected as
Vice Chairman of  the Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. G. W. G
NANATHAYALAN,
Returning Officer,
Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Kilinochchi,
07th  May, 2021.
