Order  under  Section  25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,1, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Sri Lanka International
Buddhist Academy, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration
Number GA 2232, being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order made
under the said Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1979/21 of August 9, 2016, by the addition immediately after
the entry “Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology and Communication” appearing in Column I and its abbreviation
appearing in the corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively in the
Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in the Schedule l hereto:-

SCHEDULE     I
Conditions
1. In respect of the course of study titled the Bachelor of Science Honours in Data Science (SLQF level 6), Bachelor of
Science Honours in Cloud Computing (SLQF level 6), Bachelor of Science Honours in Software Engineering (SLQF
level 6) and Bachelor of Science Honours in Cyber Security (SLQF level 6), the minimum entry qualification for
admitting a student shall be -
(i) three (03) simple passes (S) in the Physical Science subject stream in one and the same sitting at GCE (Ad-
vanced Level) Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualifica-
tion; or
(ii) three (03) simple passes (S) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advanced Level)
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka with a bridging course approved by the
Specified Authority.
2. In respect of the course of study titled the Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Civil Engineering (SLQF
level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (03) simple passes (S) in Physics, Chemistry and Combined
Mathematics in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advanced Level) Examination conducted by the Department of
Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
3. In respect of the course of study titled the Bachelor of Music Honours (SLQF level 6) adhere to the minimum entry
qualification of three (03) simple passes (S) in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advanced Level)
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
4. Every five (5) years from the date of publication of this Order a Subject Re-review shall be conducted by the Specified
Authority in order to offer the degrees specified in the Schedule II.
