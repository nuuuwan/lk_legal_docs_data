Intelligence Test: This paper which is limited to one hour, has been prepared to assess the logical state of 
thinking of the candidate, analytical competence of his mind and his decision making power. 
This question paper will consist of multiple choi ce questions and short an swer type questions. 
Candidates should answer all questions. 
General Knowledge: This is a general question paper which is limited to one hour to test the knowledge of the entire 
gamut including political, social, cultural and economic an d environmental state of Sri Lanka 
and the scientific, technologi cal and technical development as well as nationally and 
internationally important contemporary matters. Candidates should answer all questions.
