FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     TRINCOMALEE   TOWN   AND
GRA VETS    PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Trincomalee Town and Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, which  Elieser Gnanayagam Gnanagunalan,  the
member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member
of    Trincomalee Town and Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rasathurai Sivaraja to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Trincomalee Town and Gravets Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Rasathurai Sivaraja was selected as the member of  the   Trincomalee Town and Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Town and Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Trincomalee,
02nd  March, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VALVETTITHURAI   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Valvettithurai Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which  Premathas Gnaneswary,  the member belonged, was
required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member of   Valvettithurai Urban
Council  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Sunthareswary Kavignan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valvettithurai Urban Council , acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sunthareswary Kavignan
was selected as the member of  the Valvettithurai Urban Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
V alvettithurai Urban Council.
District Election  Office,
Jaffna,
02 nd March,  2021.
