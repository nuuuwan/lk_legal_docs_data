CONSUMER AFFAIRS AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Order under Section 18
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 18 of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, I, Lasantha
Alagiyawanna, State Minister of Co-operative Services, Marketing Development and Consumer Protection, do by this
Order, exclude “Milk Powder (Full Cream Milk Powder,Non Fat Milk Powder and Infant Milk Powder)” , “Liquefied
Petroleum Gas” from the list of “Specified Goods” that were prescribed by Gazette Extraordinary No. 1302/24 on 20th
August, 2003, “Wheat Flour ” from the list of  “Specified Goods” that was prescribed by Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1342/11 on 26th May, 2004 and “Cement of all types” from the list of  “Specified Goods” that was prescribed by Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1468/16 on 26th October, 2006.
L
ASANTHA ALAGIYAWANNA,
State Minister of Co-operative Services, Marketing Development and Consumer Protection.
