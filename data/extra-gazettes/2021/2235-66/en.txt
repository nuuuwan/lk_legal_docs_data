FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NUWARAGAM   PALATHA
CENTRAL   PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
being ceased to be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Jeinul Abdeen
Musadik the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thammitage Sujeewa Priyadharshana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer  of  the Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Thammitage Sujeewa Priyadharshana was selected as the member of the Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. M
UGUNTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
05th July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     PALAGALA     PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Suranga Rathnayaka the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that Ward Number 10 - Dambuluhalmillewa of Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
and  who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Jayasundara Herath Mudiyanselage Anura Kumara Jayasundara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer  of  the Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jayasundara
Herath Mudiyanselage Anura Kumara Jayasundara was elected as the member of the Ward Number 10 - Dambuluhalmillewa
of Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. M
UGUNTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Palagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
05th July, 2021.
