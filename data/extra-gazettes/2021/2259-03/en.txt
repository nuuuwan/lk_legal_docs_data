Whereas the office of return member of  Kadugannawa Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized political party to which Kotaligoda 
Jayasinghage Mahinda Rathne Jayasinghe the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Kadugannawa Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Dissanayaka Pathirana Gajanayakage Indunil Gunasena to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando , Returning Officer  of  the Kadugannawa Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Dissanayaka Pathirana Gajanayakage Indunil Gunasena  was selected as the member of the Kadugannawa Urban Council.
