PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Election Ordinance Notices
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1) (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Wattala   Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Matarakirige Duminda
Deshapriya Fernando of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha
as he has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha.
14th of June, 2021.
