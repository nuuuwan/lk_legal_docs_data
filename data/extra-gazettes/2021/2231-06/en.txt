FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE  DEHIWALA - MT. LA VINIA
MUNICIPAL COUNCIL UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of  Dehiwala-Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of
Death.
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized Political Party to which Colomba
Patabendige Laxmi Prasila Fernando  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of United National Party  who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Waga Arachchige Sunitha Violet Gunathunga to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the  Dehiwala - Mt.Lavinia
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Waga Arachchige Sunitha Violet Gunathunga was selected as the member of the Dehiwala - Mt. Lavinia
Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City.
08th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    KALUTARA URBAN
COUNCIL UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kalutara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending for more
than three Council meetings  without permission ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front,  the recognized Political Party  to which Samarasinghe
Hewage Danuj Niroshan Somarathna  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon  returned member of  People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Samarasinghe Hewage Danuj Niroshan Somarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Kalutara
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Samarasinghe Hewage Danuj Niroshan Somarathna was selected as the member of the  Kalutara Urban
Council.
K
ALINDRA  JAYAWEERA  FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara.
08th June, 2021.
