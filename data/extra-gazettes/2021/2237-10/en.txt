1A - G 34684 - 25  (07/2021) 
 
THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY LAW No. 41 OF 1978 ENACTED BY THE 
NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY  
 
Order under Section 15 (1) 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15 (1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 
enacted by the National State Assembly,  I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development & Housing do by this 
Order vest with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today where the part of land "Thekkawatta" situated 
at Municipal Council of Minuwangoda declared morefully in schedule hereto, being the land called Old Town Council 
Building & Central Market Building. 
 
 
 MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M.P.),  
 Minister of Urban Development & Housing. 
Ministry of Urban Development & Housing, 
17th and 18th Floors, 
“Suhurupaya”,

Battaramulla.
