by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32 (1) of the Provincial council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, A. J. m. muzammil 
Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me.
01.	 Engineer	Mr.	M.	T.	G.	A.	N.	Lakmal,	Grade	II	officer	of	the	Sri	Lanka	Engineering	Service,	to	act	as	the	Provincial	
mechanical director of Uva Province, on full time basis with effect from the date 07.09.2021.
02.	 Dr.	Mr.	L.	A.	A.	H.	K.	De	Silva,	Deputy	Medical	Administrative	Grade	Officer,	to	act	as	the	Regional	Director	of	the	
Health Service of badulla, in addition to the duties of his permanent post, for period of one year with effect from the 
date 28.09.2021.
03.	 Mrs.	G.	A.	M.	S.	P.	Ambanwala,	Retired	officer	of	the	Sri	Lanka	Administrative	Service,	on	contract	basis	as	the	
Secretary to the ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and order, education, Local Government, Land, Power and 
energy, construction and rural Infrastructure development of Uva Province, has been extended the contract period 
from	the	date	09.10.2021	till	appointing	a	permanent	officer	or	up	to	31.12.2021.
