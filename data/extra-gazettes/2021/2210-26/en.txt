PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance Notices
1A - G 33509— 35 (01/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of the Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Seenimuhammed Kamaaldeen of  the Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya
Sabha as vacated his office of member of that  Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the
Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
12th January, 2021.
