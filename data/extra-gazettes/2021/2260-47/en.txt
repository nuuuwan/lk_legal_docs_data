SOUTHERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notifications of Hon. Governor of Southern Province
THE announcement made under the provisions of the Article 184(1)(1b)(a)(II) of Urban Council Ordinance which is the
Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I, Dr. Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province, under and by virtue of powers vested on me
vide Article 184(1)(1b)(a)(II) of Urban Council Ordinance which is the Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2 of
Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, do hereby suspend the functioning of Gange Sarath Sri Lal De
Silva, Councilor of Ambalangoda Urban Council effective immediately.
Dr. W. W. G
AMAGE,
Governor,
Southern Province.
On this 29th day of December, 2021,
Southern Province Governor’s Secretariat,
Upper Dickson Road,
Galle.

SOUTHERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notifications of Hon. Governor of Southern Province
I, Dr. Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of Southern Province, nominate Retired Judicial Officer Sarojani Kusala
Weerawardana, under and by virtue of powers vested on me vide Article 184(1)(1a) of Urban Council Ordinance which is the
Authority 255 that should be read with Clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, to
investigate whether Gange Sarath Sri Lal De Silva, Councilor of Ambalangoda Urban Council have caused, any misdemeanours
cited in Article 184(1) of Urban Council Ordinance and to report me within a period of 03 months since the date of issue of this
notification.
The respective official has the powers of a commission appointed under the provisions of Commissions of Inquiry
Act, as regard to this inquiry.
Dr. W. W. G
AMAGE,
Governor,
Southern Province.
On this 29th day of December, 2021,
Southern Province Governor’s Secretariat,
Upper Dickson Road,
Galle.
