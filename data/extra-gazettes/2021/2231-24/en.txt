1A-  PG  5413— 225 (06/2021)
Direction No. 80
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Special Direction under Section 10(l)(b)(ii)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(l)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no importer, manufacturer, store owner, collector, distributor or whole seller
who has in his possession, custody or under his control the following products mentioned here.n in any warehouses,
stores, containers and any other place unless such warehouse, store, container and any other place is registered with the
Consumer Affairs Authority.
1 Sugar (Brown sugar and White sugar)
2. Milk Powder (Full cream, Low fat, Non fat and Infant)
Further, the Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no importer, manufacturer, store owner, collector, distributor
or whole seller shall keep above products under his possession without being registered with the Consumer Affairs
Authority and shall forthwith furnish information on demand to the Consumer Affairs Authority on the stock of product/
products in bulk or in any other form in such warehouse, store, container and any other place.
