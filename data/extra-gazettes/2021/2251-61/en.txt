FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     ALAWWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)  AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION   (AMENDMENT)
 ACT,  No.  16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna,  to which Madapali Muhandiramlage Sandya
Kumara the  former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the  member of  that Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be declared elected as  Chairman
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Gamlathralalage Padma Kumari Waththewa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer  of  the Alawwa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Gamlathralalage Padma
Kumari Waththewa  elected as Chairman of the Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. M. H. B. R
ATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
28th October, 2021.
