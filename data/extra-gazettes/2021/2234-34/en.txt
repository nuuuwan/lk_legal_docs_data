FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     WELIGAMA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Weligama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rehan Dhanushka
Wijerathne Jayawickrama   the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Weligama Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamad Ibrahim Mohomad Nizar to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the Weligama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Mohomad Ibrahim Mohomad Nizar was selected as the member of the Weligama Urban Council.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Weligama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Matara,
30th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KIRINDA   PUHULWELLA

WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Bandula
Wickramaarachchi Mohottala   the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not

Kahandagamage Neraj Sampath to fill such vacancy ;

Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare  that Kahandagamage Neraj Sampath was selected as the member of the Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya
Sabha.
