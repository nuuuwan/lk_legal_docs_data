I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition

acquired the allotment of land to develop Katu Ela from

Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage & Environment
Improvement Project, without interrupting the future
development in Kelani Valley Railway.

Eighteen allotments of land in total extent of 0.0793 hectare,
depicted as Lot No. 01 to 18 in the Preliminary Plan No. 9837

situated in village called Gangodawila in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 519 C of Pagoda Eastin the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte in Colombo District
of the Western Province and bounded as follows:
