SRI   LANKA   ELECTRICITY   ACT,   No.  20  OF  2009
Order under Section 52
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by  Section 52 of the Sri Lanka Electricity  Act, No. 20 of 2009, and upon the
recommendation made by the Minister assigned the subject of power, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka being of the opinion that -
a) the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the construction of a 220/132 kV Switching
station as a power transmission project; and
b) the said power transmission project being a public purpose would serve for the general welfare and benefit of the
public,
do by this Order declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
16th December, 2021.
