REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS - 2020
Special Notice to Obtain Information for Registration of Electors in Areas where Resettlement has
partially been done or not done
THE list of names of persons who have been registered in the Electoral Register for 2019 and will be removed from the
Electoral Register for 2020, residing in the following Grama Niladhari Divisions in the Northern Province where resettlement
has partially been done or has not been done, will be displayed at the Grama Niladhari Offices in those areas, and at Jaffna,
Kilinochchi and Mannar District Election Offices and on the Election Commission’s official website www.elections.gov.lk
from 16.04.2021 and ;
Those who are alive but are not recommended for registration for the year 2020 should inform the respective District
Election Offices by the prescribed form before 27.04.2021, it is hereby informed that this form can be obtained from the
exhibition places referred to above paragraph or be downloaded from the Election Commission’s website www.elections.gov.lk.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
