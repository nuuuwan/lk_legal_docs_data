PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  BINGIRIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being Ceased to be
a member of the Party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Appuhamilage
Premathilaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ranpati Devayalage Nimal to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ranpati
Devayalage Nimal was selected as the Member of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala.
01st July, 2021.
