EASTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Order under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Anuradha Yahampath, the Governor of the Eastern Province, do hereby make Order as described in the Schedule hereto
by virtue of power vested in me under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
A
NURADHA YAHAMPATH,
 Governor,
Eastern Province.
Governor’s Office,
Eastern Province,
31st December,  2020.
ORDER
The Chairman of Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha within the Province of the East has failed to get adopted by and re-submitted
to the said Pradeshiya Sabha, a Budget of the year 2021, as the power vested in him under Section 168 and 169 of the
Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, thus the said Chairman of the said Pradeshiya Sabha, as provided under Section 17
of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the
Chairman effective from 31.12.2020 and that the office of the Chairman of that Pradeshiya Sabha become vacant effective from
that date.

Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 171 (b) of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987,1, Anuradha Yahampath
Governor of the Eastern Province, do hereby make the following rule for the administration of fund and property and for the
matters connected therewith and incidental thereto enforceable during the period as described in the rule hereof.
A
NURADHA YAHAMPATH,
 Governor,
Eastern Province.
Governor’s Office,
Eastern Province,
31st December, 2020.
RULE
Whereas the Budget of the year 2021 of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha in the district of Batticaloa within the limits of
Eastern Province Provincial Council was failed to adopt as required under Section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act,
No. 15 of 1987, thus the office of the Chairman of the Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha hereto became vacant in accordance to
Section 17 of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and hence the Chairman be appointed to that
vacant position compliance to Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit a
Budget for the year 2021 to the Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha hereto, as such I, authorize Assistant Commissioner of Local
Government of the district Batticaloa to grant approval to the following proposals proposed in the aforesaid Budget during
the period commencing from 01.01.2021 till such a date on which a Chairman is appointed to that vacant position of  the
Eravurpattu Pradeshiya Sabha .
Proposals
1. Salaries and Wages
2. Travelling Allowances
3. Stationaries
4. Utility Expenses
5. LDSP Project Expenses.
