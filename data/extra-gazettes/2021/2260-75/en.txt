Whereas the office of the member of  Kegalle Urban Council has  fallen vacant by the reason of Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Karandana 
Acharige Lalith Jayashantha the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that 05 - Olagama of  Kegalle  Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kurumba Pitiyalage Lal Ananda Santha Kumara to fill such vacancy;
therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Kegalle  Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kurumba Pitiyalage Lal Ananda 
Santha Kumara was elected as the member of the 05- Olagama of the  Kegalle Urban Council.

And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Konara

disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Goda Amune Vithanage Gunarathna to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Nalin Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Kandy  Municipal Council, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Goda 
Amune Vithanage Gunarathna was selected as the member of the Kandy Municipal Council.
