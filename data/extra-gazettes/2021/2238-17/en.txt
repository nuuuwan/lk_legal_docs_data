1A – G34724 – 17 (2021/07)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotment of lands in total extent of about 1.7497 Hectares
depicted as Lots A and B in the Advance tracing No. R/EHL/
2017/733 dated 09.05.2018 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveyors - Rathnapura District, situated
in the village called Vilegoda (FVP 666) in the Eheliyagoda
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kuruwita Korale, Palle
Paththuwa Minor Division of Rathnapura District in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  ‘A’  -  Eheliyagodawaththa– Extent: 1.2141 Hectares
North: Road and Eheliyagodawaththa belongs to
Pussellawa Plantation; East: Road, Eheliyagodawaththa
belongs to Pussellawa Plantation and Lot B of this trace; South:
Lot B of this trace and Road; West: Road.
02. Lot  ‘B’  -  Eheliyagodawaththa– Extent: 0.5356 Hectare
North: Lot B of this trace and Eheliyagodawaththa belongs
to Pussellawa Plantation; East: Road, Eheliyagodawaththa
belongs to Pussellawa Plantation and dead river; South:  Road;
West: Road and Lot A of this trace.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands.
