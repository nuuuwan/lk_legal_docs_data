EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 04/2021
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Excise Notification No. 03/2018, published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 2054/41 dated January 18, 2018 in order to revise the opening and closing hours of Toddy Taverns, specified in the Item
No. 02 of Schedule of that Notification, by substituting the opening and closing hours specified in the Schedule below with
effect from January 07, 2021.
The Excise Notification No. 11/2018 published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2075/24  of June 13, 2020 is hereby rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPASKA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
January 07, 2021.
