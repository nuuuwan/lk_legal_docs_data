THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : BD/HP/LND 2/LAQ/01.
Ministry of Land Ref. No. : 4-3/11/2018/UDA/178.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired :
Allotments below name land in Total Extent of about 0.0269,
shown in Town Survey Plan No. B 754 and dated 21.12.2020,
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys, Badulla
District on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the
Town of Haputale in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. (63A)
Haputale and in the Division of Haputale, in the District of
Badulla in Uva Province.
01. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land : Sherwood Waththa; Land Use: Estate,
2 years old mixed farming and there is a part of a permanent
building; Claimant : Agarapathana Plantations, Baron
Jayathilaka Mawatha, Colombo 01. (The Land has been leased
by the Reform Council); Extent : 0.0269 Hectare.
Boundaries : - North : Lot No. 2 and Sherwood Waththa,
Assessment No. 25, Colombo Road, Teagarden (part of
H.P .45774); East : Sherwood Waththa, Assessment No. 25,
Colombo Road, Teagarden (part of H.P.45774); South :
Sherwood Waththa, Assessment No. 25, Colombo Road,
Teagarden (part of H.P .45774); West :  Sherwood Waththa,
Assessment No. 25, Colombo Road, Teagarden (part of
H.P .45774), Lot No. 4 of  Mu.P .2137 and Lot No. 129 of
Mu.P .B.593.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land / Lands on
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing
before me at the Divisional Secretariat Haputale of 29th of
March, 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 22nd of March,
2021 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
K. B. A. M. S. ABEKOON,
Divisional Secretary,
Haputale.
Divisional Secretariat,
Haputale.
