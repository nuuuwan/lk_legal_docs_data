EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
Reference No.: Ma/LA/4/1568. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude 
which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:- 
 
The alloments of land in total extent of 4.0469 Hectare 
as Lot Nos. 1038, 1039 & 1040 depicted in the 
Preliminary Plan No. 69 and dated 21st January 2021, 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village 
of Welipenna, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Matugama in the District of  Kalutara, Western Province 
morefully described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 1038 
Name of Land: Nagodamukalanagankanda alias 
Mountperlwatta; Description: Open barend land; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission ; 
Extent: 3.6209 Hectares.  
02. Lot No. 1039 
Name of Land: Ellakanda alias Mountperlwatta; 
Description: Open barend land; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.3884 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 1040 
Name of Land: Ellakanda and Nagodamukalanagan -
kanda alias Mountperlwatta; Description: Road; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission ; 
Extent: 0.0376 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Matugama Divisional Secretariat on the 16th 
day of June, 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby request to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
04th day of June, 2021 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land/servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 SHAYAMA P. KARIYAWASAM, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Matugama. 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office,  
Matugama, 
01st April, 2021.
