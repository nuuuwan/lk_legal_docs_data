PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE   UNDER    SECTION   10 A(1)(a)    OF   THE   LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Sri  Jayawardanapura  Kotte  Municipal  Council
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of  the Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Sarukkalige Dhanushka
Kushal Silva of the Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council
as he has ceased to be a member of the United People's Freedom Alliance..
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer.
Sri Jayawardanapura  Kotte Municipal Council.
District Elections Office, 
Colombo City,
09th November 2021.
