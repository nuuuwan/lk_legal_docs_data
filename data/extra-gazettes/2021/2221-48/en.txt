North Central Province Provincial Council
NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION  185(1)  OF  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  ACT,  15  OF  1987  THAT  SHOULD BE READ
WITH   SECTION  2  OF  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  (INCIDENTAL  PROVISION)  ACT,  No. 12  OF  1989 OF
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
I,  Mahiepala Herath, Governor of North Central Province have received the report submitted by Mr. Saman Wickrama
Wickramarachchi retired High Court Judge, appointed according to Section 185(2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987
in order to satisfy that Mr. Piyathissa Gamage Priyantha Kavindu Abeysooriya, Chairman of Walikanda Pradeshiya Sabha
has committed one more or all the offences as per the Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and published
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2208/48 and dated 01.01.2021. According to the said inquiry report, the offences have been
committed in terms of Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and after the satisfaction of the same, by virtue
of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989 of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in the exercise the powers vested under Section 185(1) i of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987 as the Minister in Charge of the Subject of Local Government,  I do hereby notify that Mr. Piyathissa Gamage Priyantha
Kavindu Abeysooriya, of Walikanda Pradeshiya Sabha of Polonnaruwa District of North Central Province is removed from
the post of Chairman of Walikanda Pradeshiya Sabha with immediately effect in terms of the powers vested under Section
185(1) i of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
M
AHIEPALA HERATH,
The Governor of North Central Province.
On 31st March, 2021,
At Anuradhapura.
