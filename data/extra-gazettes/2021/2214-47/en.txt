SPORTS   LAW,   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations made by the Minister of Sports under Sections 32 and 33 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973
REGULATION  UNDER  SECTIONS  32  AND  33
I, the Minister of Youth and Sports, in accordance with the powers vested in me under Sections 32(b)  and 33 of the Sports
Law, No. 25 of 1973 and its amendments, temporarily suspend the registration of the National Sports Associations/Federations
for the following sports and appoint Mr. Amal Edirisooriya, Director General of the Department of Sports Development, as the
competent authority with effect from 05th February 2021 in respect of the administrative and other required proceedings and
to convene and hold the relevant elections as well as to perform the activities of the said National Sports Associations/
Federations until then as a temporary procedure.
01. Sri Lanka Hockey Federation
02. Carom Federation of Sri Lanka
03. Handball Federation of Sri Lanka
04. Sri Lanka Golf Federation
N
AMAL RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of  Youth and Sports.
Colombo,
On the day of 05th  February, 2021.
