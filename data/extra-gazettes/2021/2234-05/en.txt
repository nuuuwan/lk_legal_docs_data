The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART  IV(A) : PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Provincial Council Notification
Uva Provincial Council
Notification of Hon. Governor of Uva Province
Notification under Section 2(3)(b) of the Statute No. 2 of 2011
regarding Powers to Monitor Administration in Local
Government Authorities Established in Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested on me through the Section 2(2) of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding powers  to monitor
administration in Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province, appointed Mr. H. M. R. Anura Kumara Judicial
Officer, retired Justice to  investigate and report to me whether any fact in Sections (b) and (c) of the Subsection 2(1) of the said
Statute has been done by the Council including the Hon. Mayor of the Badulla Municipal Council, considering the observations
and recommendations of that report I hereby cancel the notification by me through the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2214/28 dated
09.02.2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka relevant to Badulla Municipal Council under the Section 2(3)(a) of
the Statute No. 2 of 2011 to monitor administration in Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province, according to
2(3)(b) Section of that Statute with effect from 29.06.2021.
A. J. M. MUZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor's Office.
28th June, 2021.
