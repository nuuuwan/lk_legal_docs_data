10.3 Recruitment to the Special Grade
10.3.1 Percentages of recruitment
Stream Percentage
Experience of Service and Merit 40%
Seniority 60%
Note: When recruitments are made to the Special Grade, the number of vacancies should be calculated as at 31st of December 
of the previous year.
10.3.2  Recruitment under Experience of Service and Merit
 10.3.2.1  Qualiﬁ  cations to be fulﬁ lled
I. Shall have completed an active and satisfactory period of service of at least six (06) years in Grade I of
the service category and earned all six (06) salary increments.
II. Shall not have been subjected to a disciplinary punishment in terms of the provisions in Public Service
Commission Circular No. 01/2020 and circulars incidental thereto.
III. Shall have demonstrated a performance at satisfactory level or above during the period of six (06) years
preceding the date of promotion as per the approved performance appraisal procedure.
IV . Shall have passed the third Efﬁ ciency Bar Examination on the due date
o r
Shall have passed the Senior Technological Examination in subjects of categories I and II in the ﬁ rst half 
and parts (a) and (b) of category II in the second half.
10.3.2.2  Method of Recruitment
Promotions will be made to the Special Grade on the order of merits of the marks given for experience 
of service and merit as mentioned in Schedule 07 by a board of interview approved by the Public 
Service Commission after calling applications from Engineering Assistants who have fulﬁ lled the above 
qualiﬁ cations for the vacancies available in the Special Grade as at 31st of December. 
10.3.3  Recruitment under Seniority
 10.3.3.1  Qualiﬁ  cations to be fulﬁ lled 
I.  Shall have completed an active and satisfactory period of service of at least six (06) years in Grade I of
the service category and earned all six (06) salary increments.
II. Shall not have been subjected to a disciplinary punishment in terms of the provisions of Public Service
Commission Circular No. 01/2020 and circulars incidental thereto.
III. Shall have demonstrated a performance at satisfactory level or above during the period of six (06) years
preceding the date of promotion as per the approved performance appraisal procedure.
IV . Shall have passed the third Efﬁ ciency Bar Examination on the due date.
o r
V . Shall have passed the Senior Technological Examination in subjects of categories I and II in the ﬁ rst half
and parts (a) and (b) of category II of the second half.
