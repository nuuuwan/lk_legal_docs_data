Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE
KALMUNAI MUNICIPAL COUNCIL UNDER SECTION  66A (1)(A) OF THE LOCAL
AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three consecutive
meeting without permissions,
And whereas the leader of Independent Group No. 04 to which Maharan Waseer the member belonged, was required
by me under Section 66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Number 19 Ward 19 of Kalmunai
Municipal Council who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
