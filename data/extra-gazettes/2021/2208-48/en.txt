NORTH    CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Notification of Honourable Governor of North Central Province
ORDER MADE UNDER SUB SECTION 185 (3) OF  PRADESHIYA SABHA ACT, NO. 15 OF 1987 THAT SHOULD
BE READ WITH SECTION 2 PROVINCIAL COUNCIL (INCIDENTAL PROVISION) ACT, NO. 12 OF 1989.
I, Mahiepala Herath, Governor of North Central Province do hereby suspend Mr. Piyathissa Gamage Priyantha
Kavindu Abesooriya, to hold the office of Chairman of Welikanda Pradeshiya Sabha, in terms of the powers vested in me,
under Sub Section 185 (3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 Provincial Council
(Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Further, I order Mr. Junaid Mohomad Ameen, Assistant Commissioner of Local Government (Polonnaruwa) to
execute and exercise duties and powers the office of Chairman of Welikanda Pradeshiya Sabha in Polonnaruwa District of
North Central Province with immediately effect in terms of the powers vested in me, under Sub Section 185 (3) of Pradeshiya
Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
M
AHIEPALA HERATH,
Governor of North Central Province.
On 01st of January 2021,
At Anuradhapura.

2A
NORTH    CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Notification of Honourable Governor of North Central Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Sub Section 185 (2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read
with Section 2 Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Mahiepala Herath, Governor of North
Central Province do hereby appoint Judicial Officer, retired High Court Judge, Mr. Saman Wickrama Wickramarachchi to
investigate and report whether Mr. Piyathissa Gamage Priyantha Kavindu Abesooriya. Chairman of Welikanda Pradeshiya
Sabha had committed any offence specified under Sub Section 185 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987, within
the period of Three (03) Months from the date of this notification.
Judicial Officer, retired High Court Judge, Mr. Saman Wickrama Wickramarachchi has the powers of Commission of
Inquiry Act to conduct this inquiry.
M
AHIEPALA HERATH,
Governor of North Central Province.
On 01st of January 2021,
At Anuradhapura.

NORTH    CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Notification of Honourable Governor of North Central Province
ORDER MADE UNDER SUB SECTION 185 (3) OF PRADESHIYA SABHA ACT, NO. 15 OF 1987 THAT SHOULD
BE READ WITH SECTION 2 OF PROVINCIAL COUNCIL (INCIDENTAL PROVISION) ACT, NO. 12 OF 1989.
I, Mahiepala Herath, Governor of North Central Province do hereby suspend Mr. Thennakoon Mudiyanselage
Mahanamage Ajith Prasanna Thennakoon, to hold the office of Chairman of Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha, in terms of the
powers vested in me, under Sub Section 185 (3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2
Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Further, I order Mr. Salibuge Thaibu, Vice Chairman of Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha to execute and exercise duties
and powers the office of Chairman of Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha in Anuradhapura District of North Central Province with
immediately effect, in terms of the powers vested in me, under Sub Section 185 (3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987
that should be read with Section 2 Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
M
AHIEPALA HERATH,
Governor of North Central Province.
On 01st of January 2021,
At Anuradhapura.
