NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION 16  SUB - SECTIONS (2D)  OF  THE  COAST  CONSERV A-
TION AND  COASTAL  RESOURCE  MANAGEMENT
ACT, No. 57 OF 1981
Proposed Fishery Harbour at Haraspola, Balapitiya in Galle District
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT
IT is hereby notified, that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed fishery harbour at Haraspola,
Balapitiya in Galle District submitted by the Ministry of Fisheries, New Secretariat, Maligawatta, Colombo 10 as required
by the Section 16 (1) of the Coast Consvervation and Coastal Resource Management Act, No. 57 of 1981 as amended by
the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following institutions from
8.30 a.m. to 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 01.11.2021. (Except weekends and public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official web site (www.coastal.gov.lk) of the Department of
Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management.
(1) Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor,
Maligawatta, Colombo 10.
(2) Pradeshiya Sabha, Balapitiya.
(3) District Secretariat, Galle.
(4) Divisional Secretariat, Balapitiya.
(5) Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, District Fisheries Office, Galle.
(6) Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa” Densil Kobbakaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla.
