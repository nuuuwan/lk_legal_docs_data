SPECIAL COMMODITY LEVY ACT, No. 48 OF 2007 
 
Order under Section 2 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007,  I, Basil 
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order i mpose in respect of 
the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate sp ecified in the 
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. 
 
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from October 14, 2021 subject to prov isions of Sub 
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007. 
 
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notification No. 2225/13 of April 27, 2021 in respect of Potatoes (HS Code 0701.90) is resci nded with effect 
from October 14, 2021.
