LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
My No. : 4-3/1/Appointment of Acquisition Officers/2021
HON. Minister of Lands has appointed the below named officer in terms of the provisions of the Land Acquisition Act, as
Acquiring Officer to function under the directives of the Divisional Secretary concerned to expedite the land acquisitions
pertaining to the project implemented under the Ministry of Highways, i. e. Mattakkuliya Bridge Access Road, Orugodawatte
- Ambhathale Road, Kelanimulla-Mulleriyawa-Koswatte Road.
