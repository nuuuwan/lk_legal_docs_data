 Seal GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA
To :
1. Hon. A.H.M.D. Nawaz Esquire
Judge of the Supreme Court
2. Chandra Fernando Esquire
Retired Inspector-General of Police
3. Nimal Abeysiri Esquire
Retired District Secretary
Greetings !
Whereas, in pursuance of the provisions of Section 2 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), a Presidential
Commission of Inquiry on Human Rights Violations has been established by the Directive dated January 20, 2021,
Whereas, it has been recognized that the tasks assigned to the above Commission need to be fulfilled more
expeditiously and efficiently,
BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  GOTABAYA  RAJAPAKSA,  PRESIDENT  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA

And whereas, taking into consideration the necessity of reflecting the pluralistic character of Sri Lankan Society
and Gender Equity,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, in accordance with the provisions
of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, appoint Ms. Yogeswari Patkunarajah, as a Commissioner of the Presidential
Commission of Inquiry established in terms of the aforesaid Directive, reposing great trust and confidence in her prudence,
ability and fidelity.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka on this  11th  day of February,
Two thousand and Twenty one.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P.  B .  J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
