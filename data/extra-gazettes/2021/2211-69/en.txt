THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Saegis Campus (Private)
Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV
72548 being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order made under the
Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2141/81 of September 20, 2019 by the addition immediately after the entry
“Bachelor of Business Administration” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries
specified under Column I and II respectively in the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I
hereto :-
SCHEDULE   I
