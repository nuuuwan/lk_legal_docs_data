THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Order under Section 39A (1)
THE allotments specified in the Order No. 140 of  2014 made
under Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act

No. 1868/50  dated 25th June, 2014 are vested absolutely in
