FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KORALAIPATTU  WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Uduma Labbe
Ahamathu Labbe the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of  Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohammathu Thamby Mohammathu Fairoos to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Koralaipattu West  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Mohammathu Thamby Mohammathu Fairoos was selected as the member of  the Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
19th  July, 2021.
