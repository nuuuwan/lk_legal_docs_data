SOUTHERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
CO-OPERATIVE   SOCIETIES   ACT,   No.   05   OF   1972   AS   AMENDED   BY   ACTS   No. 32 OF
1983   AND   No. 11  OF 1992
Orders on Delegation of Powers (Amendment)
AS the Southern Provincial Council has been dissolved the powers vested upon the Minister in charge of the subject of
Co-operatives of the Southern Province by section 2/2 of Co-operative Societies Act No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Acts,
No. 32 of 1983 and No. 11 of 1992 as per Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions ) Act No. 12 of 1989, have been
assigned to me by article 154 (c) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and in terms of the
such powers I, William Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of the Southern Province hereby assigned all powers depicted
in the column III of the said schedule, assigned to the Registrar of Co-operative Societies under Co-operative Societies
Act. No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Act No. 32 of 1983 and Act No. 11 of 1992 and the regulations enacted there under to
Piyadigoda Liyanage Bhagya Chathurani Liyanage who has been appointed as the Deputy Commissioner of Co-operative
Development to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies of
Southern Province and the approval which I have given on 16.03.2020 for the assignment from 01.01.2020 as mentioned
in the column II of the said schedule has been published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2182/49 of 03.07.2020 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and I hereby amend the said empowerment be effective from 03.01.2020.
Dr. W.W. G
AMAGE,
Governor of Southern Province.
At Galle,
On this 16 day of February, 2021.
