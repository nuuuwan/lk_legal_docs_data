Regulations
The Immigrants and Emigrants Regulations 1956, published in the Supplement to the Ceylon Government Gazette 
No. 10,896 of February 24, 1956 are hereby amended as follows:
1. In regulation 8 -
(1) In paragraph (1) by the substitution for the words “furnish such number of copies of the photograph or of the 
finer-prints	or	of	both,”	of	the	words	“furnish	such	number	of	copies	of	the	photograph	or	of	the	bio	metrics	
or	of	both,”;
(2) In	paragraph	(2),	by	the	substitution	for	the	words,	“or	of	the	finger-prints	furnished”	of	the	words,	“or	of	the	
bio	metrics	furnished”.
