PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - PG 5486— 25  (07/2021)
DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT   -  EASTERN   PROVINCE
Notice on summon meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
BY  virtue of  power vested on me under Section 66(C) read with Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of
Local Government of Eastern Province, do hereby announce that the recalling meeting to select a new Vice Chairman, for the
vacated Vice Chairman’s position of Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha, Batticaloa District of Eastern Province, will be
convened on  19.07.2021 at 10.00 a. m. at the Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
