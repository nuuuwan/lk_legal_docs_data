CEYLON ELECTRICITY BOARD ACT, No. 17 OF 1969
Order under Section 57
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57  of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Dullas Alahapperuma,
Minister of Power do by this Order, approve the proposed acquistion of the lands required by the Ceylon Electricity Board,
described in the Schedule hereto.
D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA,
Minister of Power.
25th March, 2021.
Schedule
The land situated in Field No. 1A of Uva Ben Head Estate, in the Grama Niladhari’s Division of Landegama (52B) and
Kabillegama (52D) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Welimada, in the Udapalatha Korale, in the Admnistrative District
of Badulla in Uva Province of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 04 Acres, 0 Roods, 0 Perches, shown as Lot 1 in the Sketch
No. CEB/CEATP/TE/51 dated 03.03.2021 prepared by the Ceylon Electricity Board, bounded as follows :
01. Lot No. 01 - Land in extent of 04 Acres, 0 Roods, 0 Perches
North : Welimada - Nadungamuwa Road (15 feet wide) ;
East : Part of the same land (Uva Ben Head Estate, Filed No. 1A) ;
South : Gravel Road (8 feet wide) ;
West : Gravel Road (8 feet wide) ;
Area : 4 Acres, 0 Roods, 0 Perches
Claimed by : Janatha Estate Development Board and Land Reform Commission
