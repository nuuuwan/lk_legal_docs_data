PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of the member of Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamed Anwar
Mohamed Dilshad the  member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that number 21 Kochikade South of Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rathamba Ralalage Chandana Pushpakumara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Rathamba Ralalage Chandana Pushpakumara was elected as the member of the number 21 Kochikade South of Colombo
Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
21st June, 2021.
