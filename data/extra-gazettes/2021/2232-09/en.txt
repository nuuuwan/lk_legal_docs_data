PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Election Ordinance Notices
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Ja-Ela  Urban Council
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Ja-Ela Urban Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Uswatta Liyanage Thushantha
Kumara Perera of the Ja-Ela Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that Ja-Ela Urban Council as he has been
ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-Ela Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha.
14th of June, 2021.
