THE   URBAN    DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY   LAW   No.   41   OF  1978, ENACTED BY THE  NATIONAL
STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order Under Section  15(I)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of  the  Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, enacted
by the National State Assembly, I,  Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development and Housing by this Order to vest
with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today, the land situated at Kapparathota Village within the limits of
the  Urban Council of Weligama described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land of Weligama Rest House
belonging to the Urban Council of Weligama.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of  Urban Development and Housing.
Ministry of Urban Development and Housing,
17th and 18th Floors,
“Suhurupaya”,
Battaramulla,
04th January, 2021.
