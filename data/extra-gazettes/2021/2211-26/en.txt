NOTICE UNDER THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
BY  virtue of the Powers vested in me under the section (1) of 66(C) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and the
provision of section 66(G) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, I do hereby informed that t he names mentioned
below were elected on the days of 15.01.2021 at 9.30 a.m. and at 2.30 p.m,. at the meeting held in the council hall of Trincomalee
