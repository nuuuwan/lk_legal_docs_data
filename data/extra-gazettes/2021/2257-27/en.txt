ThE NATIONAL ThOROuGhfARES ACT, No. 40 Of 2008
Order under Section 86
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 86 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Johnston Xavier 
Fernando, minister of Highways, do by this Order, restrict access by the public to or from the Galle Port Access road which 
was declared a National Highway by order published in Gazette extraordinary No. 1469/6 of October 30, 2006, or any part 
of such highway other than from the access roads specified in the Schedule hereto, for the reasons stated below :-
 (1) to prevent unauthorized reclamation of Lands, constructions and vehicle parking alongside the Galle Port Access 
road; and
 (2) to improve the mobility of the Galle Port Access road.
 
 Johnston Xa vier Fernando,
 minister of Highways.
