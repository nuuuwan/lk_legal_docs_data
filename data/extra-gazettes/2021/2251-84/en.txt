THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: 9/3/5/50.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. .: 4-3/5/2018/HA/105.
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
for constructing ''Sewa Piyasa" Building in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Walawela.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in extent of 0.0607 hectares of the land
called Walawela Kumbura alias Maligawe Kumbura depicted
in the Preliminary Plan No. MA 1844 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the village called Walawela in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. E 3348 of Walawela in Asgiri Udasiya
Pattu Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Yatawatte in Mathale District of the Central Province.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: W alawela Kumbura alias Maligawe
Kumbura; Description: Acquisition of an allotment of land for
constructing "Sewa Piyasa" building in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Walawela; Original Claimant: Walawela Pamunuwa
Farmers' Association;  Extent: 0.0607 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Yatawatte on
15.12.2021 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 01.12.2021 the nature
of their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D.R.K.H
ENNAY AKE,
Divisional Secretary's Division - Yatawatte.
27th October, 2021,
At Divisional Secretary's Division - Yatawatte.
