FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     SORANATHOTA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sandanam Joniklas
Arokeyanathan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that 08 - Kandegedara, if Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harsha Milinda Udayajeewa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  the Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) (a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Harsha Milinda Udayajeewa  was selected as the member of  the   08 - Kandegedara of Soranathota Pradeshiya
Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Badulla,
10th  March, 2021.
