Order
The chairman of Verugal Pradeshiya sabha in Trincomalee district within the eastern Province has submitted and failed to 
get adopted the budget of the year 2022, as the power vested in him under section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya sabha act, 
No. 15 of 1987, thus the said chairman of the said Pradeshiya sabha, as provided under section 17 of the Local Authorities 
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman effective from 
30.11.2021 and that the office of the Chairman of that Pradeshiya Sabha become vacant effective from that date.

eastern Province Provincial council was failed to adopt as required under section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya sabhas

submit a budget for the year 2022 to the Verugal Pradeshiya sabha hereto. As such I Authorize Assistant commissioner of 
Local Government of the district Trincomalee to grant approval to the following expenses in the existing budget for 2021
