Order No. 76
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,  NO.  9  OF  2003
Order under Section  20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority hereby orders that no Importer, Manufacturer, Distributor, Supplier or Trader shall sell,
supply, expose, offer, or display for sale or supply for sale the following items above the Maximum Price given below :
Item Importer’ s Maximum Maximum Retail Price Maximum Retail Price
Wholesale Price (Unpacketed) (Packeted)
White Sugar Rs. 116/- per kg. Rs. 122/- per kg. Rs. 125/- per kg.
Brown or Red Sugar – Rs. 125/- per kg. Rs. 128/- per kg.
                This Order shall come into effect from 2nd September, 2021.
                                                                By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. General S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (RETD.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
02nd September, 2021.
