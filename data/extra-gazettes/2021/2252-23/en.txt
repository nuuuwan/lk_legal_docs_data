FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   mEmBER   OF   ThE   WELIGAmA  uRBAN  
cOuNcIL   uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE     
(chAPTER   262)      
Whereas the office of  member of Weligama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohammad Lafeer 
Muhammad Siyam  the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to 
decide upon the member of  that No. 02 Aluthweediya of Weligama Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Sandara Durage Krishan Lasanthaka Jayasooriya Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the Weligama Urban Council, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sandara 
Durage Krishan Lasanthaka Jayasooriya Fernando was elected as the member of the No. 02 Aluthweediya of  Weligama 
Urban Council .
  
 T. W. P
oshiTha Kaumadi,
 Returning Officer,
 Weligama Urban Council 
District Election Office,
Matara,
