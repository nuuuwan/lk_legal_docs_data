PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A -  PG 5321— 30 (02/2021)
DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT - EASTERN  PROVINCE
Notice on Summon Meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment)
Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Nagarasa Manivannan the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province, hereby noticed vide Gazette Notification
(Extraordinary) No. 2212/55 dated 29th January, 2021,  announced that the meeting scheduled to be held on 11.02.2021 at  2.30
pm for the selection of New Chairman of Manmunai Pradeshiya Sabha in Batticaloa District will not be held as per the interim
order of HCB/WRIT/635/21 by the Provincial High Court of Eastern Province holden at Batticaloa.
