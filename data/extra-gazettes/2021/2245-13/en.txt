FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MAHARAGAMA    URBAN    C OUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(1a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Maharagama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group No. 02 to which Rajapaksha Mohottige Karolis, the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(1a)  of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  the member of  that
Ward No. 12, Kottawa East of Maharagama Urban Council who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified
under Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Galkanda Arachchige Sunil Piyarathne to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of   Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Galkanda Arachchige Sunil Piyarathne was elected as the member of the Ward No. 12, Kottawa East of Maharagama Urban
Council.
W. H. R.  W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
14th September, 2021.
