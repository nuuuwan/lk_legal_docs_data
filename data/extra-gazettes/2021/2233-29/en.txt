PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A  (1) (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Ja-Ela  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Ja-Ela Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A (1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ethmadalage Asanka Devruwan
Perera of the Ja-Ela Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Ja-Ela Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been
ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-Ela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
18th June, 2021.
