PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34113— 45 (04/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Millaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Udawatthage Upul
Priyadharshana Perera of  the Millaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as he
has   ceased to be a member of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
30th  March, 2021.
