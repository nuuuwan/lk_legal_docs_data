No. 445/2 of 2021

IT  is hereby notified that H IS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT has been pleased, under Article 52(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Ms. R. M. C. M. Herath as Secretary to the State Ministry of
Canals and Common Infrastructure Development in Settlements in Mahaweli Zones, which has been established under
Article 45(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with immediate effect on April 28,
2021.
