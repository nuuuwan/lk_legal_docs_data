THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 Allotments of Lands depicted as Lot Nos. 1 and 2 in   
Advance Tracing No. CO/MHR/2019/318 dated 02.09.2019 
prepared by Surveyor General, situated in Village of Mirihana 
in No. 523, Mirihana - North Grama Niladhari Division 
within Divisional Secretariat Division of Maharagama in 
the District of Colombo, Western Province and bounded as 
follows:
 01. Lot  ‘A’ – Pelengahawatta – Extent: 0.0388 Hectare
 North: Rajamaha Vihara Mawatha and Cement Drainage; 
East: Cement Drainage and Pelengahawatta claimed by UDA; 
South: Pelengahawatta claimed by UDA and Lot B; West: Lot 
B and Rajamaha Vihara Mawatha.
 02. Lot  ‘B’ – Pelengahawatta – Extent: 0.0146 Hectare
 North: Rajamaha Vihara Mawatha and Lot A; East:  Lot A 
and Pelengahawatta claimed by UDA; South: Pelengahawatta 
claimed by UDA and Dawatagahawatta claimed by H. B. 
Dayapala; West: Dawatagahawatta claimed by H. B. Dayapala 
and Rajamaha Vihara Mawatha.
 S. M. Chandrasena,
 Minister of Lands.
