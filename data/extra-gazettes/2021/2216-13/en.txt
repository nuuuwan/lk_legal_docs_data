NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Chamal Rajapaksa, State Minister of  National Security, Home Affairs and Disaster
Management has, under Article 47(2)(b) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, relinquished
office of the State Minister with effect from February 18, 2021, in order to assume office of the new State Minister.
                                                                                            By Order of  His Excellency,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA ,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
February 22, 2021.
