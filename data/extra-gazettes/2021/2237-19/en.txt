1A-  G  34693— 25  (07/2021)
SPORTS   LAW  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under the Section 32
I, the Minister of Youth and Sports, as per the powers vested in me under Section 32(c) of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973,
cancel the registration of the Sri Lanka Powerboat Association, the National Association for the sport of Powerboat and
the Sri Lanka Unifight  Federation, the National Association for the sport of Unifight with immediate effect.
N
AMAL RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of Youth and Sports.
Colombo,
On the day of 19th July, 2021.
