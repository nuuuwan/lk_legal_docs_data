THE  PETROLEUM  RESOURCES  ACT,  No. 21  OF  2021 
 
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Energy under section 48 of the Petroleum Resources Act No. 21 of 2021 read 
with sections 5(3)(b) and 30(3) of that Act. 
  
 
UDAYA GAMMANPILA, 
Minister of Energy. 
Colombo, 
14th October, 2021. 
 
 
 
 
REGULATIONS 
 
 1.  These regulations may be cited as the Petroleum Resources (Minimum Qualifications of the Appointed M embers 
and the Director General) Regulations, No. 1 of 2021. 
 
 2.  The minimum qualifications of an appointed member of the Board of Directors under section 5(3)(b) of  the Act 
shall be as follows:-

 (a)  be an Attorney at Law of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka having at least twelve years of experience; 
 
 (b) be a Geologist or Geophicist or Geochemist or Petro -Physicist having at least twelve years of practical 
experience in the field of petroleum exploration methodologies; 
 
 (c) be a Professional of an accredited investment, project finance, financial management or accounting o r a 
chartered financial analyst, having at least twelve years of institutional experience; 
 
 (d) be an Engineer having at least twelve years of experience in the field of petroleum industry; 
 
 (e) be a senior Corporate Leader with an exemplary track record in the field of corporate governance; or  
 
 (f) be an Economist from an independent research and policy background, having at least twelve years of 
professional experience. 
 
 3.  The minimum Qualifications and experience of the Director-General of the Petroleum Development Authority of 
Sri Lanka appointed under section 30(1) of the Act shall be as follows :- 
 
 (a) a degree in physical science or engineering granted by any recognized university specially in the field of oil 
and gas, but not limited to the fields of petroleum geology, explorative geophysics, petroleum engin eering, 
reservoir engineering, blast and production engineering or petroleum economics; 
 
 (b) at least fifteen years experience in the field of petroleum exploration, development and production activities 
with international involvement including at least five years experience in the field of offshore ope rations; 
and 
 
 (c) at least three years experience at senior level in the fields of administration and project management. 
 
 4.  In these regulations :- 
 
  "Act" means the Petroleum Resources Act, No. 21 of 2021.
