REGULATIONS 
 
 1.  These regulations may be cited as the specific pathogen free post larvae production regulations, 2021. 
 
 2.  These regulations shall apply to Brackishwater Shrimp Hatcheries in Sri Lanka. 
 
 3.  A person operating a Brackishwater shrimp hatchery in Sri Lanka shall engage in breeding Brackishwater shrimp 
by only using specific pathogen free Broodstock. 
 
 4.  A person operating a Brackishwater Shrimp hatchery unit which is engaged only in developing the shrimp from the 
Nauplii stage to the post larvae stage of the shrimp, shall obtain only the Nauplii of the shrimp which is free from 
specific pathogen and shall develop such Nauplii of the shrimp to the post larvae stage. 
 
 5.  The Director-General of the National Aquaculture Development Authority of Sri Lanka or any officer authorized 
by him in writing, for the purpose of conservation and development of Aquaculture, shall have the power to seize 
and destroy any Broodstock, eggs, larval stages including Nauplii and post larvae in shrimp hatcheries engaged in 
breeding shrimps other than those which are specific pathogen free. 
 
 6.  In these regulations - 
 
"Brackishwater shrimp hatchery" means the place where Brackishwater shrimp post larvae or Nauplii are 
produced; 
 
"Nauplii" means the first larval stage of a shrimp; 
 
"Post larvae" means the shrimp larvae that are produced for sale; 
 
"specific pathogen free" means the Broodstock produced without the pathogen that infect shrimps specified in 
these regulations by selective breeding in a center specially designed for broodstock development. These 
shrimps shall be certified as specific pathogen free by an Institution recognized by the National 
Aquaculture development Authority of Sri Lanka.
