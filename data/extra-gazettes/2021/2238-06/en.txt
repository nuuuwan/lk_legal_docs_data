I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by
Section 4 of the  Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby enlarge  the time ending 21st July, 2021 by Six
months from 21st July, 2021 to 21st January, 2022 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to
the Commission of Inquiry established on 20th January, 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2211/55 dated  21st January, 2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of Section 2 of the
aforesaid Act “for appraisal of the findings of previous Commissions and Committees on Human Rights and the way
forward”.
 G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
    President.
