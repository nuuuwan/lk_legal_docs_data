THE land specified morefully in the Order  
No. 307 of 2017 dated 22.11.2017 made under the Proviso 
(a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) 
and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary No. 2049/5 dated 
11.12.2017 was vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka. But the possession of the said land has not yet been 
taken for and on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka. Therefore, I Samarakoon Mudiyanselage 
Chandrasena, Minister of Lands, by virtue of powers vested 
in me under Section 39 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act, 
do hereby revoke the aforementioned order No. 307 of 2017 
dated 22.11.2017 published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary  
No. 2049/5 dated 11.12.2017.
