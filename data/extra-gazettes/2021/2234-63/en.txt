FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     JA-ELA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Ja-ela Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wasala
Mudiyanselage Athula Jayawardhana the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Ja-ela Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Suranga Niroshan Ramanayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Ja-ela Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Suranga Niroshan Ramanayaka  was selected as the member of  the Ja-ela of  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-ela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
02nd July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     JA-ELA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Ja-ela Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of
the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Uswatta Liyanage
Thushantha Kumara Perera the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Ja-ela Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mahamalage Sugath Prasantha Perera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasiinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Ja-ela Urban Council
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Mahamalage Sugath Prasantha Perera was selected as the member of the Ja-ela Urban Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-ela Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
