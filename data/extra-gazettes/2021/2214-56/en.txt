1A- PG 5323 — 500  (2021/02)
IMPORTS  AND  EXPORTS  (CONTROL) ACT, No. 1 OF 1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-Section 4(1), Section 6 and Section 14 of the Imports
and Exports (Control) Act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by Act No. 48 of 1985 and Act No. 28 of 1987, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, the
Minister of Finance, promulgate following Regulations.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
11 February, 2021.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 03 of 2021”.
2. The Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 02 of 2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2213/8 is
hereby repealed. By this means, “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 04 of 2020”, published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2184/21 dated 16 July 2020 and “Special Import License Regulations”, published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2044/40 dated 09 November 2017 are in force without any effect of amendments made by the
repealed “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 02 of 2021 ”, published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2213/8 dated 02 February 2021.
