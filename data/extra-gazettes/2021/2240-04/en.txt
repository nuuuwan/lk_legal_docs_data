THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The Land of below mentioned, depicted in Advance 
Tracing No. H/BLA/2019/409 and dated 2019.07.17 
prepared by the Survey General, Situated in the  village 
of   Gatamanna in the Minor Division of Giruwa Pattuwa 
(Southern) in  the Divisional Secretary Division of Beliatta 
in Hambantota District in  Southern  Province and  bounded 
as follows:
 01. Lot  'A' - Wawekalla - Extent: 0.0070 Hectare
 North: Wawekalla claimed by H. P. Saman; East: 
Madangahahena claimed by Jayasinghearachchi Sujith; South: 
Lot B in this Tracing; West: Wewekalla claimed by 
B. P. Gunawathi and Wawekalla claimed by H. P. Saman.
 02. Lot  'B' - Wawekalla - Extent: 0.2173 Hectare
 North: Wawekalla claimed by B. P. Gunawathi, Wawekalla 
claimed by H. P. Saman and Lot  A in this Tracing; East: 
Madangahahena claimed by Jayasinghearachchi Sujith; South: 
Madangahahena claimed by Jayasinghearachchi Sujith and 
The Land which Acquired to Southern Highway; West: Lot  
C in this Tracing.
 03. Lot  'C' - Not mentioned- Extent: 0.0074 Hectare
 North: Canal, Wawekalla claimed by B. P. Gunawathi and 
Lot  B in this Tracing; East: Lot  B in this Tracing; South: Lot  
B in this Tracing and The Land which Acquired to Southern 
Highway; West: Lot D in this Tracing, Madangahahena 
claimed by B. P. Gunawathi and Others and Canal.
 04. Lot  'D' - Wawekalla - Extent: 0.0048 Hectare
 North: Madangahahena claimed by B. P. Gunawathi and 
Others; East: Lot C in this Tracing; South: Lot C in this Tracing 
and The Land which Acquired to Southern Highway; West: 
Madangahahena claimed by B. P. Gunawathi and Others.
