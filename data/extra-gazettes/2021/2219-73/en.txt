FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RATTOTA     PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present), the recognized
political party to which Wasudewan Randeep the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ramachandran Chandrabose, to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Rattota Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Ramachandran Chandrabose was selected as the member of  the   Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Matale,
19th  March, 2021.
