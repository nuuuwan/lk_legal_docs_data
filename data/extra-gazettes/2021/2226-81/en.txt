UV A PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Notice of the (Hon) Governor of Uva Province
INCONSISTENT with the Section 277 of the Municipal Council Ordinance, Chapter 252 should be read with the Section 2 of
the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the Section
2(2) of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding powers to monitor the administration, identified as the Statute No. 2 of 2011 of the
Uva Provincial Council imposed to make provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor administration in
Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province and matters relate and incidental thereto, I, A.J.M. Muzammil, the
Governor of the Uva Province do hereby appoint Mr. H.M.R. Anura Kumara Judicial Officer, retired Justice to investigate and
report to me within a duration of three months from the date of this notice whether any fact in Sections (b) and (c) of the
Subsection 2(1) of the said Statute has been done by the Council including the Hon. Mayor of the Badulla Municipal Council.
Considering the current situation and the request made by Mr. H.M.R. Anura Kumara Judicial Officer, retired Justice to
extend the time period as the present time period given to submit the relevant investigation report is not sufficient, it is hereby
informed that it has been decided to extend the time period to 31.05.2021 to submit the above investigation report.
A.J.M. M
UZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor’s Office,
08th of  May of the year 2021.
