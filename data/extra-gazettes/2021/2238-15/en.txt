The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     HABARADUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Walpitagamage
Lal the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned
member of  Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Godagama Gamage Thakshina Ravishan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Godagama Gamage
Thakshina Ravishan was elected as the member of  the Habaraduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
22nd July, 2021.
