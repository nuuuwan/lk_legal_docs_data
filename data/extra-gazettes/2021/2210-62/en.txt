FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DIVULAPITIYA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Sakalasooriya
Appuhamilage Nanda Kumara Sakalasooriya,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of   that 20 - Divulapitiya  of Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kodikarage Hema Kusumalatha Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa , Returning Officer  of  the Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kodikarage Hema Kusumalatha Silva was elected as the member of  the  20 - Divulapitiya  of Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Gampaha,
15th  January, 2021.
