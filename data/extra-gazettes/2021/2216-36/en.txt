FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE      ERA VUR   PATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Seenimuhammed
Kamaaldeen,  the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of   that Ward No. 04 Michnagar of  Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdhul Majeeth Makeer to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdhul Majeeth Makeer
was elected as the member of  the   Ward No. 04 Michnagar of Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
23rd  February, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VA VUNIYA   NORTH   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which  Kanagar
Kanapathippillai,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of   Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kanagarasa Pradeepan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kanagarasa Pradeepan was
selected as the member of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Vavuniya,
23rd  February, 2021.
