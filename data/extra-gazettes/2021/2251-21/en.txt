REGISTRATION   OF  DEATHS  (TEMPORARY  PROVISION)   ACT,  No.  19  OF  2010

Regulation
The given effective period of operation of the provisions of Registration of Deaths (Temporary Provision) Act,
No. 19 of 2010, will be extended by another two years from 09th of December 2021 to 09th of December 2023.
