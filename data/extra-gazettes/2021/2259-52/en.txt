FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KALUTARA   URBAN  
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     
(CHAPTER   262)  
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of  member of Kalutara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Pathirana 
Arachchige Wasantha Silva  the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon the  member  of that Ward No. 11  Katukurunda - Kalapuva of Kalutara Urban Council who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualiﬁed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Gulavitage Rangana Sumith Kumara to ﬁll such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Of ﬁcer  of  the Kalutara 
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Gulavitage Rangana Sumith Kumara was elected as the member  of the Ward No. 11  Katukurunda - Kalapuva 
of Kalutara Urban Council.
KALINDRAKK  JA AYAWEERA FA ERNANDO,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Kalutara Urban Council . 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Kalutara,
24th December, 2021.
