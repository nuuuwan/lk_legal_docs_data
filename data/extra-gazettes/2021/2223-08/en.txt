PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34181— 65 (04/2021)
 NOTICE UNDER SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby  declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections ordinance that Mr. Subramaniyam
Wasudevan of the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has
been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
09th April, 2021.
