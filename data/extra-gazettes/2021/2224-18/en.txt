UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the  Governor of Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987,  I, A.J.M.Muzammil
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me.
1. Mr. N.M.S.P.K. Nishshanka, Grade II officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, appointed as the Commissioner
of Probation and Childcare Service of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from  the date 11.11.2020).
2. Mrs. L.H.P. Nilmini, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Agriculture Service, appointed to act as the Deputy Director of
Agriculture (Extention) of Badulla, in addition to the duties of her permanent post, with effect from the date
12.05.2020.
3. Mr. B.M.K.G . Bandara, Grade II officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, appointed as the Director of Social
Services of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 13.01.2021).
4. Mr. D.M.N.P. Dissanayake, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, appointed to act as the Director
(finance) of the Deputy Chief Secretary (Finance Management) office, with immediate effect (from the date 13.01.2021).
