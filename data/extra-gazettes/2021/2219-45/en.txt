THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Number : DIVGW/LAND/LA/POLKG/3/5/429/2017.
Land Ministry File Number : 4-3/4/2017/LO/216
I hereby inform that the Government intends to acquire the
following land required for a public purpose under Section
7(1) of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964.
Details of the land expected to be acquired at Pothana
Village, 429 Kumbukgettee Grama Niladhari Division,
Thitthaweli Gandahaya Korale, Ganewattha Divisional
Secretariat, Kurunegala District. North Western Province Lots
of land with the following names and sizes in the basic Lot
No. 137 of the Page No. 2 Paper No. 2 and Additional No. 2 of
the Plan No. 2754 containing 0.4052 Hectare is bounded.
01. Lot No. 137 – Name of the Land: Helambagahamulagala,
Meegahamulahena and Pinkumburepillewa; Description: Open
Wasteland; Claimants: Kasagala Temple Viharadipathi; Extent:
0.4052 Hectare.
North by: Lot No. 38 and 103; East by: Lot No. 103 and 24;
South by: Lot No. 38; West by: Lot No. 38 and 103 of the plan
No. 1754.
All persons claiming ownership rights or other affiliation to
the aforesaid land property can apply to themselves or their
authorized persons on 02.06.2021 and they can meet me at the
Ganewattha Divisional Secretariat at 10.30 a.m.
Further before 19.05.2021 they should inform me in a duplicate
document of the nature of their ownership of the land and the
details of the claim for the land possession and the amount of
compensation and the amount calculated.
R. N. N. L. KARUNARATHNE,
Divisional Secretary,
Ganewattha.
At the Ganewattha Divisional Secretariat,
15th March, 2021.
