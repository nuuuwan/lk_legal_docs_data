2. By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, do hereby make 
an order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 10 per kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 40 
per kg on importation of Salt (Sodium Chloride BP), classiﬁ ed under the HS Code of 2501.00, listed out in the Schedule of 
the above, by any pharmaceuticals manufacturing enterprises, registered with the National Medicines Regulatory Authority 
(NMRA) for the purpose of manufacturing pharmaceuticals, not for commercial purpose, on the recommendation of the 
Secretary, State Ministry in charge of subject of Production, Supply and Regulation of Pharmaceuticals.
3. This Order shall be valid for a period of  one year commencing from August 12, 2021 subject to provisions of Sub Section 
(3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
4. The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2195/29 of September, 30, 2020 and the Order made under Section 5 of the Special Commodity 
Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 2206/10 of December 16, 2020 is 
rescinded with effect from August 12, 2021.
