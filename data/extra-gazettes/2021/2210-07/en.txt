 NORTHERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
BY the power vested in me in terms of Provincial Councils Act, No. 12 of 1989 read with Section 2(1)(A), I, Piencia Sarojinidevy
Manmatharajah Charles, Governor of Northern Province do hereby notify that since the Budget for 2021  in respect of Jaffna
Municipal Council, Vengalachettikulam Pradeshiya Sabha and Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha have been rejected for the 2nd time
by the respective Council and Sabhas, and assuming that Emmanual Arnold, Mayor of the Jaffna Municipal Council,
Aseevatham Antony Chairman of the Vengalachettikulam Pradeshiya Sabha and Thamotharampillai Thiyagamoorthy Chairman
of Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha have vacated their posts, in accordance with Section 215(A) of the Municipal Council Ordinance
and Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, I give below the details of the newly elected Mayor and Chairman
at the meeting held to fill the vacant posts in terms of amended Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment)
Act, No. 22 of 2012.
P. S. M. C
HARLES,
 Governor,
Northern Province.
Governor’s Secretariat,
Northern Province,
31st December,  2020.

Order under Section 326 of the Municipal Council Ordinance read with Section 2(1)(A) of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989

ORDER
Since the Budget for 2021 has not been passed before 31.12.2020, I do hereby authorize Visvalingam Manivannan,
Mayor of the Jaffna Municipal Council, to make only the following expenditure within their proposed estimates in the
aforesaid Budget, until such time the Budget for 2021 is submitted at the Council and accepted by the Council :
(01) Salaries and Allowances,
(02) T ravelling Allowances,
(03) Transportation, Communication and Utility Services,
(04) Supplies and Requisites.
