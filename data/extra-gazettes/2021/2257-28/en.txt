THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dinesh Gunawardena, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009, (as amended from time to time), which specifies the

provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition
