EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  No. : KE/LAN/ACQ/A3/01/101. 
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/9/2015/WS/189. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1)  of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
for the constrction of a distribution water tank under 
Kegalle Water Supply Scheme in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of Dikella in the Divisional Secretary's Division 
of Kegalle. 
 
 
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land in total extent 0.0198 Hectare 
depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Ke. 3665 dated 
31.03.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
the village of Ambanapitiya in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 53D of Dikella in Paranakooru Korale 
minor divison in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Kegalle  of the Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as 
follows; 
01. Lot No. 01
 
 
Name of the land : Dikellawaththa; Claimant :  
H. R. Nandawaththie, No. 44, Dikella, Kegalle; Extent : 
0.0198 Hectare.  
 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Land/servitude 
on which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat  Kegalle 
on 18 August 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 11 August 2021 the nature of their interests in 
the land/lands, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for  the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
 
  
   
 K. G. S. N ISHANTHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kegalle. 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat Kegalle, 
02nd July,  2021.
