THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of the that Act.
The allotment of  land described  below the following names
and extent, depicted in Surveyor General's  Advance Tracing
No. MA/PLL/2017/268  dated 30.06.2017 situated  at
Akuramboda Village (within F.V .P. 32) in Udugoda, Udasiya
Pattuwa minor Division of Pallepola Divisional Secretary's
Division, Matale District   Central Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot   ‘A’  - Wedeniya Pillewa   –  Extent: 0.0228  Hectare
North: Government owned Akuramboda Kanishta Vidyalaya
located at Wedeniya Pillewa and Wedeniya Pillewa claimed
by Upali Herath Ranawarna; East: Wedeniya Pillewa claimed
by Upali Herath Ranawarna; South: Wedeniya Pillewa  claimed
by Upali Herath Ranawarna, Lot B and Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha); West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and  Government
owned Akuramboda Kanishta Vidyalaya located at Wedeniya
Pillewa.
02. Lot  ‘B’  - Wedeniya Pillewa   –  Extent: 0.0033  Hectare
North: Lot A and Wedeniya Pillewa  claimed by Upali Herath
Ranawarna; East: Wedeniya Pillewa  claimed by Upali Herath
Ranawarna; South: Wedeniya Pillewa claimed by Upali Herath
Ranawarna, and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha) and  Lot A.
