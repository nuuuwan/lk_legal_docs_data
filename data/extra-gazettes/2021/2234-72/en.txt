FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NAWALAPITIYA  URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Nawalapitiya Urban Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of the
party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Hewa Waduge
Sasanka Sampath Sanjeewa the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon  the member of  that Ward Number 09, I. D. H. Mahakumbura of Nawalapitiya Urban Council  who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kadiravelu Thure Ratnam to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Nawalapitiya
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Kadiravelu Thure Ratnam was elected as the member of the Ward Number 09, I. D. H. Mahakumbura of
Nawalapitiya Urban Council.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Nawalapitiya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kandy.
02nd  July, 2021.
