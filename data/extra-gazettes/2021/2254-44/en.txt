THE   JUDICATURE  ACT, No. 2 OF  1978
Order under Section 13
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 13 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I, Mohammed Ali Sabry,
Minister of Justice, do by this Order-
(a) empower the Judge of the High Court sitting in the judicial zone of Hambantota to exercise Admiralty Jurisdiction
over the territorial limits of the Administrative District of Hambantota and its territorial waters including -
(i) the land and water area limits of the Hambantota Port as specified in the Order made under Subsection (3) of
Section 2 of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2038/15 dated September 25, 2017 ; and
(ii) the sea area limits as specified in the Order made under Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Sri Lanka Ports
Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2133/7 dated July 22, 2019 ; and
(b) revoke the Order made under Section 13 of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2239/51 dated August 6, 2021, without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
M. U. M. ALI SABRY,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
05th November, 2021.
