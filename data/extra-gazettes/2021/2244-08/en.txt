SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of commodity
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto the Special Commodity Levy at rate specified in the corresponding entry in
Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from September 08, 2021 subject to provisions
of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2223/2 of April 13, 2021 is rescinded with effect from September 08, 2021.
BASIL RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
07th September, 2021.
