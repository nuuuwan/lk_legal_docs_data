FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE   CHAIRMAN  OF   THE
BORALESGAMUWA   URBAN   COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTION
(AMENDMENT)   ACT,  No. 16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Boralesgamuwa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Pudujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Liyanage
Udaya Srilal Sigera the former Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Boralesgamuwa Urban Council who belongs to such party  who shall be
declared elected as a Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Omaththage Mervin Nandasiri Perera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya  Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Boralesgamuwa
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66B (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017, do hereby declare  Omaththage Mervin Nandasiri
Perera  elected as Vice Chairman of the Boralesgamuwa Urban Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Boralesgamuwa Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
07th  May, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE    EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 11.05.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     BORALESGAMUWA
URBAN   COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Boralesgamuwa Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Subaweerage Swarnalatha Dias the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 02 Divulpitiya of Boralesgamuwa Urban Council who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sujeewani Priyangika Gamlath to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya  Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Boralesgamuwa
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Sujeewani Priyangika Gamlath was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 02 Divulpitiya of
Boralesgamuwa Urban Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Boralesgamuwa Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
07h  May, 2021.
