FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE     HORANA    URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Horana Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ranpati Pathirage
Sarathchandra Samarakoon the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon  returned member of   Horana Urban Council, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as  a member within a period
of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ranawaka Arachchige Saranga Mangalanath Senanayaka to fill such vacancy ;
THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Horana Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Ranawaka Arachchige Saranga Mangalanath Senanayaka was selected as the member of the Horana Urban Council.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Horana Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
29th December, 2021.
