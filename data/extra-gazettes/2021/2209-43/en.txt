EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 05/2021
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 and 12 of  the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do by this Order direct that with effect from the date of publication of this Notification -
(1) declare that from and after the said date, the limit of sale by retail, with respect to the whole Island and as regards
purchases generally, shall be three (03) litres in the case of toddy  ;
(2) prescribe generally for the whole Island, three (03) litres as the quantity in excess of which no toddy shall be
transported from and after the said date, except under a pass issued under Section 13 of the said Ordinance ; and
(3) rescind Excise Notification No. 745 published on August 14, 1985.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo,
January 07, 2021.
