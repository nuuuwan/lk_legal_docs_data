CIVIL   PROCEDURE  CODE  (CHAPTER  101)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Justice under Section 840 of the Civil Procedure Code (Chapter 101) read with
Section 214 of the said Code.
M. U. M. ALI  SABRY,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
23rd June 2021.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Civil Procedure (Costs) Regulations, 2021 and shall come into operation on 07th
July, 2021.
2. All bills of costs, whether between a party and parties, or between a registered attorney and a client, shall be taxed by
the Registrar of the court at the rates as set out in the Schedule hereto.
3. The Civil Procedure (Costs) Regulations, 1997 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 994/7 of September
24, 1997 are hereby rescinded.
