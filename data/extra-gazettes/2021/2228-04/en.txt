THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.

No. Ku/IBB/2020/152 - A  dated 24.07.2020  prepared by the

Division,  in the Divisional Secretary's Division of

and boundaries as follows:

Livestock Development Board, Road and Lot No. 2; West:
Lot No. 2, Road and Medamulla claimed by National Livestock
Development Board.
