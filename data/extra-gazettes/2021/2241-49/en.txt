FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   MAWATHAGAMA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER
262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which W. G . Sarath
Upali Somasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
S. M. Sunil Samarakoon to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage  Harendra Bandara Rathnayake Returning Officer  of the Mawathagama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby
declare that S. M. Sunil Samarakoon was selected as the member of the  Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. M. H. B. R
A THNAYAKE ,
Returning Officer,
Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
20th August, 2021.
