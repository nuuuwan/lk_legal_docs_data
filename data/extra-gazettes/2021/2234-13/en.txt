FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     AMPARA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDED)  ACT,   No.  16  OF
2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Ampara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  office of the Chairman shall
be deemed to have been resign under Section 178(a) of Urban Council Ordinance  (Chapter 255) as amended by  No. 21
of 2012 (Special Provisions) Local Authority Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Manodara
Acharige Chaminda Sugath, the  former Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of the Ampara Urban Council who belongs to such party  who shall be declared
elected as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kalthotage Kelum Kumara Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Ampara Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as ameded by Local Authorities
Election (amended) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Kalthotage Kelum Kumara Fernando  elected as Chairman of
the Ampara Urban Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Ampara Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
29th  June, 2021.
