No. 486/3 of 2021
P.S. No.: PS/CSa/00/1/8/2
It is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 52(1) of the Constitution of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. K. W. Ivan de Silva as Secretary to the State Ministry of Tanks, 
Reservoirs and Irrigation Development Related to Rural Paddy Fields, which has been established under Article 45(1) of the 
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with immediate effect on May 25, 2021.
                                                                             By order of His Excellency,
P. B. Jayasundera,
Secretary to the President.
