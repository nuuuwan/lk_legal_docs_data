PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 33902— 30 (03/2021)
NOTICE   PUBLISHED  UNDER   SECTION   66(G)  OF  THE  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
AMENDED   BY   THE  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  (AMENDMENT)   ACT,   No.  22  OF  2012
I, I. M. I. Ilangakoon, the Commissioner of Local Government, North Western Province by virtue of powers vested in me
under Section 66(G) of the Local Government Elections Ordinance amended by the Local Government Elections (amend-
ment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 do hereby notify that the meeting for the purpose of electing the Deputy Chairman for the
Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha, will be held in the conference hall of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha at 10.00 a. m. on
31.03.2021.
I. M. I. I
LANGAKOON,
Commissioner of Local Government,
North Western Province.
Department of Local Government,
North Western Province,
2nd Floor, B Gate,
