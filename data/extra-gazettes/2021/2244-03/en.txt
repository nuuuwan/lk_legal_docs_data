STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 3
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No.
14 of 2008 as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Economic Policies and Plan Implementation, further to the
Order published under Sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No.1847/36 dated January 30, 2014, as
amended by the subsequent Order published under sub-Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2084/18
dated August 14, 2018 in respect of Waterfront Properties Private Limited, do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.1847/36 dated January 30, 2014, as amended
by the subsequent Order published under sub-section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2084/18
dated August 14, 2018 is hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to
implement the project for a further period of Fourteen (14) months and twenty (20) days from October 12,
2021:-
“(4) The aforesaid “Project to set up a project for the development, construction, equipping, commissioning,
owing, managing, operating, selling, leasing and renting luxury multi business including a luxury hotel,
convention centre, entertainment with associated facilities, international standards shopping mall, luxury
condominiums, serviced apartments and office space (“the Integrated Resort”) in two phases at Glennie
Street, Colombo 02 in the Western Province” shall commence commercial operation on the December
31, 2022".
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA, MP,
Minister of Economic Policies and Plan Implementation.
Ministry of Economic Policies and Plan Implementation,
06th September, 2021.
