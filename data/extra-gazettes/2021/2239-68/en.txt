(175TH   COMMISSION)   PILGRIMAGE   ORDINANCE
The Annual Esela Feast of 2021
KOTABOWA   KUDA   KATHARAGAMA   DEV ALAYA   OF   UV A   WELLASSA
I, herewith declaring to aware the public that Annual Feast of “Kotabowa Kuda Katharagama Devalaya of Uva Wellassa” will

PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 07.08.2021
2A
THE ANNUAL ESELA FEAST OF 2021
KOTABOWA    KUDA   KATHARAGAMA    DEVALAYA   OF   UVA   WELLASSA
I, herewith declare the Pilgrimage Ordinance of No. 14117 of Government Gazette that published on 24th July 1964 for the
public attention and announcing the regulations and below mentioned substance.
01. Camp Area :
The area covers from so called “Dahamal Oya” of inner Kotabowa ancient ford within half mile of quasi diameters.
02. Constructing Buildings :
Constructing permanent or temporary building prohibited inner the camp area in between the period of 08th
August 2021 to  23rd August 2021 without a written permission of me or an accredited officer of me.
03. Beggers :
Begging, obtaining & providing dime and distributing are prohibited in the sacred and the camp area.
04. Bathing & Washing :
Bathing, washing, washing animals or washing vehicles are prohibited in the area so called “Dahamal Oya” of
inner Kotabowa ancient ford with in half of mile of quasi diameter.
05. Vehicles :
05.01 Vehicles such as automobile, bicycle, bullock carts or any are prohibited to enter to the sacred area
(to “Dahamal Oya” east) without  my permission or an accredited person of mine.
05.02 Prohibited to park vehicles except the reserved parking area of the market in the camp.
06. Kavadi Shop :
Prohibited to establish any kavadi shop in the area without my permission or the accredited officer of mine.
07. Business :
07.01 Prohibited to auction or lease any temporary shops in the camp area without my written permission or an
accredited person and no one will be permitted to do as such in the prohibited area.
07.02 Construction & extending buildings, making tents, encroaching the riverbeds in camp area or in the sacred
area are prohibited.
07.03 Selling foods items, ritual things & trays of fruit, ice cream and holy ash are prohibited to sell except the
reserved area which permitted by me or an accredited officer of mine.
07.04 A trash bin of 4 1/2 cubic feet has to be placed at every buildings and the constructions where business or
trade take place and the bins have to be kept closed by lids when they are not in use and must kept outside
the premises in time for  elimination as convenient to the workers of health.
07.05 All the food items and beverages that for selling and consumption have to be arranged in containers and to
be protected by the flies.
