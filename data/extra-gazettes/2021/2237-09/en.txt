PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
36th Higher Ordination Ceremony (Upasampadha Ceremony) of Sri Lanka Rohana Maha Sanga Sabha of
Shyamopali Wanshika Maha Nikaya
IN terms of the regulation mentioned in the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175) No. L. D. B. 11/60
dated 14th October, 1991, It has been published that the 36th Higher Ordination (Uapasampadha Ceremony) of Sri Rohana
Maha Sanga Sabha of Kamburupitiya Divisional Secretariat, Matara District will be held from 15 ,16, 17, 18 July 2021 at
the “Seema Malaka” of Rohana Sanga Sabha head quarters of Urapola Sri Rathana Jothi Piriven Viharaya and the devotees
are requested not to bring plastic and polythene based products. And also it is hereby informed that the standing orders and
regulations published by the Government Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be valid within this
time period of the Higher Ordination Ceremony (“Upasampadha Ceremony”)
 C
HINTHAKA ABEYWICRAMA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kamburupitiya.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Kamburupitiya,
07th July, 2021.
