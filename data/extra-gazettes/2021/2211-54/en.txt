THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, S.M. Chandrasena, Minister of Lands, do hereby declare, under
Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the
land described below is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. CO/
THI/2019/668 dated 18.10.2019, prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of  Dematagoda in the
Village called Dematagoda ( within the Limit of  Colombo Municipal
Council) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Thimbirigasyaya
in Colombo District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  No. 1 - Assessment No.752/5,  Elvitigala Road - Extent:
0.0063 Hectare
North: Dematagoda Canal; East: Dematagoda Canal; South: The

The land bearing assessment No. 752/5 in Elvitigala Mawatha.
02. Lot  No. 2 - Dematagoda Canal - Extent: 0.1348 Hectare
North: Road; East: Road and Dematagoda Canal; South: Dematagoda
Canal and  Road and Lot No. 3; West: Lot No. 3, Road  and
Dematagoda Canal.
