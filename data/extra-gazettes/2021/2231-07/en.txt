FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    WARAKAPOLA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which Gamlath Arachchillage Wasantha Sisira Kumara
Samarathunga  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  returned member of  Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Gunawardana Lekamge Wijitha Gunawardana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Gunawardana
Lekamge Wijitha Gunawardana  was selected as the member of the Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle.
08th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    MADULLA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office member of  Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death.
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized Political Party  to which
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Wimal Bandara Dissanayaka  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A
(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that No. 10, Dambagalla of Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such Party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Neela Nuwan Rathnayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the  Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Rathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Neela Nuwan Rathnayaka was selected as the member of the No. 10, Dambagalla of  Madulla Pradeshiya
Sabha.
I
NDIKA GAYAN PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Madulla  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Monaragala.
08th June, 2021.
