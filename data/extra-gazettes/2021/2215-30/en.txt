SPORTS ACT, No. 25 OF  1973
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 39-(1) of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Namal Rajapaksa, Minister of
Youth and Sports do hereby prescribe the following special direction.
1. I, hereby extend the term of office of the Officers of the Executive Committee of Sri Lanka Cricket, duly elected and
currently in office for the year 2019/2021, to be extended effective up to the 05th of April, 2021.
NAMAL RAJAPAKSA, (M.P),
Minister of Youth and Sports.
at Colombo,
On this 15th day of February, 2021.
