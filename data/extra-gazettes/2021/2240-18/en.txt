ORDINAN cE     (chAPTER    262)    
Whereas the office of  return member of  ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being 
ceased to be a member of the Party;
and whereas the secretary of the united National Party, the recognized political party to which ranasinghe 
Mudiyanselage sumudu udayangani Perera the  member  belonged, was required by me under section  66 a(1) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of that ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be 
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
and whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rajakumar  Vidhushani to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Ganga Ihala Korale  
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare  that 
rajakumar Vidhushani was selected as the member of the  ganga Ihala Korale  Pradeshiya sabha.
Dr. H. D. N. Sampath Fernando,
Returning Officer,
 ganga Ihala Korale  Pradeshiya sabha.
