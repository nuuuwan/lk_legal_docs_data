FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MAHARA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Nalawathage
Ranil Chaminda Kumar Pinto the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Nuwara Passa Hewayalage Priyantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Mahara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Nuwara Passa Hewayalage Priyantha was selected as the member of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
08th October, 2021.
