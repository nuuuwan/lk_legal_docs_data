1A -  PG 5436—  30 (05/2021)
WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
I hereby approve the rules for amending the Administrative Manual of the Western Provincial Road Development Authority,
which had been approved by the board of directors of the Western Provincial Road Development Authority on 24.12.2020,
for the purpose of amending “the Section 16.6 of volume 1 of the Administrative Manual” in connection with operational
management of the Western Provincial Road Development Authority by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 15
of the charter No. 04 of 1989 of the Western Province Provincial Road Development Authority that has to be read with
Section “154 c” of the Constitution. The rules so amended shall be valid from this day, the date of this approval.
W
ELLARACHCHIGE DON ROSHAN MAHESH JAMES GOONATHILAKE,
RWP and Bar, VSV , USP,
PhD, FIM (Sri Lanka), NDC, PSC,
Marshal of the Sri Lanka Air Force
Governor of the Western Province.
Office of the Governor of the Western Province,
Western Provincial Council Building,
Battaramulla.
On the 07th of the month of May of the year 2021.

2A
Rules
1. These rules have been promulgated for amending the administrative manual of the Western Provincial Road
Development Authority.
2. These rules shall be known as the rules for amending administrative manual of the Western Provincial Road
Development Authority.
3. Allotted by the Minister in charge of the subject of roads of the Western Province for determining the administrative
manual of the Western Provincial Council, and effective from 01.01.2004, the sub paragraph 16.6 of the 16 th
paragraph under of the rules of “Administrative Manual of Western Provincial Road Development Authority” has
been removed, and the following paragraph has been incorporated in its place.
Amounts of subsistence allowances are as follows
Basic Salary (monthly) Subsistence Allowances (For 24 hr period)
01. Up to Rs. 32,799/- Rs. 700/-
02. From Rs. 32,780 to 43,479/- Rs. 800/-
03. From Rs. 43,480/- upwards Rs. 1000/-
