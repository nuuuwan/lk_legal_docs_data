SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT,  No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 5
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 5 of the special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Prof. G. L. 
Peiris, Acting minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do hereby make an order to waive-off 
balance amount after collecting rs. 10 per kg from the applicable special commodity Levy of rs. 25 per kg on importation 
of Maize (Classified under the HS Code of 1005.90) by Gazette Notification No. 2228/3 dated may 17, 2021 under section 
2 of the special commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 for production of “animal feeds” by the manufactures, who have 
obtained approval/recommendation of the Department of Animal Production and Health up to a total of 40,000 metric tones.
this Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months effective from december 22, 2021 subject to provisions of sub 
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
   Prof G. L. Peiris,
 Acting minister of Finance .
