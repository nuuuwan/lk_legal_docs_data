Part II
18.1 Higher diploma in Surveying Science conducted by the Institute of Surveying and Mapping or a postgraduate diploma
in Surveying Science or a course in Surveying Science equal to or higher than these courses are accepted as alternative
qualifications to those prescribed in (V) of 10.2.1 or (I) of 10.3.1 of this Service Minute, during the interim period.
Note
1. An officer qualifying for promotion to the Special Grade as per the qualifications above should submit a written
dissertation on a subject area on which he is in agreement with the Surveyor General to a committee appointed by
the Surveyor General.  The opportunity to submit a written dissertation accordingly is given only until 24.12.2023,
the date on which the implementation of the interim provisions expires.
2. The increment of salaries of the officers who are promoted to the Special Grade based on the qualifications stated
under 18.1 will temporarily be suspended until the approval of the respective committee is obtained after
submission of a written dissertation as stated in I above.
