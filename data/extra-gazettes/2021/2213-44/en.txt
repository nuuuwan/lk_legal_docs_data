PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - G 33653— 35 (02/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTIOIN   10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
Kesbewa Urban Council
I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Kesbewa Urban Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Vithanage Sujeewa Nishanthi
Chandrasiri of the Kesbewa Urban Council has vacated her office of the member of that Urban Council as she has been
ceased to be a member of the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kesbewa Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
02nd February, 2021.
