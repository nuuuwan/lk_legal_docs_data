THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection (1) of Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of  Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor
G . L. Peiris, Minister of Education, in consultation with the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the
Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the University of the Visual and Performing
Arts, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1400/24 of July 08, 2005, as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 2126/11 of June 03, 2019, as follows:-
(1) in the item ‘‘faculty of Dance and Drama” appearing in Column I by the addition immediately after the entry “Department
of History and Theory of Dance and Drama” appearing in the corresponding Column II which corresponds to the
item "Faculty of Dance and Drama" appearing in Column I thereof of the following new entry :–
“Department of English Language Teaching”
(2) by the repeal of the item “Faculty of Music” appearing in Column I of the Schedule and its corresponding entries
appearing in Column II thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following item and entries
