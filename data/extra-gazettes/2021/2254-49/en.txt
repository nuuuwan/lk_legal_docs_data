Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING OF A V ACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE NINTHA VUR PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER SECTION 66A (1) (1a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Kithuru
Mohamadu Jezeema Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (1a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon Returned member of Ninthavur Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
and is qualified under Section 8 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamed Ismail Siththi Fasfiha to fill such vacancy;
