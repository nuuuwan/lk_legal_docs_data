SECTION 72 OF THE MEDICAL ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 105)

Services were approved by the parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 3rd of November 2020.
1. Regulation on Medical (Maintenance of Minimum Standard of Medical Education) Regulation No. 01 of 2018
published in Extra Ordinary Gazette No. 2055/54 of January 26, 2018.
