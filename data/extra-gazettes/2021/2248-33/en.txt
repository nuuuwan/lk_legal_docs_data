I,  alwala dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to 
the powers entrusted to me by section 66 c read with section 66 g of the Local authorities elections ordinance amended 
by Local authorities elections (amendment) act No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of selecting 
a new chairman for the vacated chairman's position of Kotikawatte Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya sabha of Western Province 
will be convened on october 22 of 2021 at 9.30 a.m. at the Pradeshiya sabha chamber of Kotikawatte Mulleriyawa 
Pradeshiya sabha located at Gothatuwa New town.
