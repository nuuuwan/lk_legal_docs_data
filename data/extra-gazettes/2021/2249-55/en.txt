WhereAs the office of   member of  Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Hewa Juwan 
Kankanamge Gnanathilaka Wijerama the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that  03 - Debarawewa of Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Parana Liyanage Chali Gunarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Parana 
Liyanage Chali Gunarathna was elected as the memer of 03 - Debarawewa of Thissamaharama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
