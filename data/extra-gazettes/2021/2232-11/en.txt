PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Election Ordinance Notices
NOTICE  UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (A) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Valikamam  North  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Vanniyasingam Pirabakaran of
the Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha as he
has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna.
14th of June, 2021.
