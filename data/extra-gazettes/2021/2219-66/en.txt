THE  AWARD
   The Honourable Minister of Labour Relations and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in him by 
Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of th e Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), 
as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 r ead with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, 
No. 37 of 1968 appointed me as Arbitrator by his order dated 28th February 2019 and referred the following dispute to me 
for settlement by Arbitration.
   The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties are:–
   Whether Mrs. H. W. S. C. K. Wijayawardena who worked as an A ssistant Bank Manager of the Bank of Ceylon had 
been caused injustice by being terminated on 15.04.2007, the decision taken by the Bank not to pay her pension and reduction 
of part of her Bank of Ceylon Provident Fund and if so, to what relief she is entitled.
Appearance:
There was no appearance for the Applicant.
Mr. Chamil Perera, Attorney-at-Law of Employers’ Federation and Legal Oﬃ  cer Ms. Anusha Chandrasekara, appeared 
for Bank of Ceylon.
Parties ﬁ led their ﬁ rst Statements.
At the inquiry the Applicant did not appear.
By her letter dated 26.07.2019, the Applicant informed the Registrar as she has to go overseas, requested the Court 
to lay by her case.
Further the Applicant, by her e-mail dated 05.01.2021 informed the courts that she has no intention to continue with 
case as she has gone overseas. Therefore case was dismissed.
In the circumstances I make no Award.
