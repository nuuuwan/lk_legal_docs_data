THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
   My  No. :DSO/HOM/LAC/124.
Land Ministry Ref. No.:4-3/5/2019/CEB/110.
I, hereby inform that the Government intends acquiring the
lands as given below for a public purpose under Sub-section
(1) of Section 7 of the Acquisition of Lands Act:
Description of the Land  expected  to be acquired:
Lots No 585 and 586 of the Cadaster map number
520004 -04-67 dated 31.03.2021 prepared by the Surveyor
General and located in the village of Magammana in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Number 590 A Magammana West within
Homagama Divisional Secretariat Division, Colombo District
of the Western Province, Total extent of the land  0.0664 Hectare
comprising the two Lots within the borders as given below:
01. Lot No. 585
Name of  land:  Ambagahalanda; Description: Home garden
claimed by the Government, Original claimant: Araliya Land
Homes (Private) Ltd; Extent: 0.0354 Hectare.
02. Lot No.  586
Name of  land:  Ambagahalanda; Description: Home garden
claimed by the Government, Original claimant: Sumith
Kotalawela resident No. 323/4/C, 4th Lane,  Kulasirikumarage
Mawatha, Katuwana; Extent: 0.0310 Hectare.
All those who have a claim, rights or other connections to
the aforesaid land have to meet me on 28th January, 2022  at
10.00 am along with the related documents and with the
authorized persons at the office the Homagama Divisional
Secretariat.
Further, the nature of their rights as regards the land facts
pertaining to the compensation sought for the land, as well as
a thorough description of the  compensation sought and the
way the amounts of money have been computed have to be
submitted to me by a document with two copies before 20th
day of January, 2022.
B.D.A. CHINTAKA,
Divisional Secretary ,
Homagama.
At  Divisional  Secretariat,  Homagama.
06th of  December, 2021.
