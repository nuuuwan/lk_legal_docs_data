NORTHERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Co-operative Societies Law No. 5 of 1972 as amended by Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and
the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 read in conjunction with Provincial Council (Consequential
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
CONFERMENT  OF  POWER
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Co-operative Societies Law No. 5 of 1972 as amended by the Co-operative
Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992, read in
conjunction with Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Mrs. P. S. M. Charles, Governor of
Northern Province, do hereby confer Mr. Muthulingam Nanthagopalan has been appointed as the Commissioner of Co-
operative Development and Registrar of Co-operative Societies of the Northern Province to exercise such powers of the
Registrar under the Co-operative Societies Law No. 5 of 1972 as amended by Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act,
No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and the rules their under in respect of the
Northern Province, with effect from 04.03.2021 to be excercised by the aforesaid during their tenure of office.
P. S. M. C
HARLES,
Governor,
Northern Province.
04th  March,  2021.
