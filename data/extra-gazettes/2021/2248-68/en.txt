FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE  GALLE  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Galle Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group 02,  to which Mahamada Arachchige Sirimal the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the returned member of
Independent Group and  is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Group Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Thilak Wijebandara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Galle Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Thilak Wijebandara
was selected as the member of the Galle Municipal Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Galle Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Galle,
08th October, 2021.
