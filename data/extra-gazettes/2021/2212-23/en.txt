ORDER
The Chairman of Ampara Urban Council within the Province of the East has submitted and failed to get adopted  the Budget
of the year 2021, as the power vested in him under Section 178 and 178A of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)
thus the said Chairman of the said Urban Council, as provided under Section 12 of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 21 of 2012, is deemed to have been resigned from the office of the Chairman effective from 31.12.2020 and that the
office of the Chairman of that Urban Council become vacant effective from that date.

RULE
Whereas the Budget of the year 2021 of the Ampara Urban Council in the District of Ampara within the limits of Eastern
Province Provincial Council was failed to adopt as required under Section 178 and 178A of the Urban Council Ordinance
(Chapter 255), thus the Office of the Chairman of the Ampara Urban Council hereto became vacant in accordance to Section
12 of the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and hence the Chairman be appointed to that vacant
position incompliance to Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 and is to propose and submit a Budget
for the year 2021 to the Ampara Urban Council hereto, as such I, authorize Assistant Commissioner of Local Government of
the District Ampara to grant approval to the following proposals proposed in the aforesaid Draft Budget during the period
commencing from 01.01.2021 till such a date on which a Chairman is appointed to that vacant position of  the Ampara Urban
Council.
Proposals -
1. Salaries and Wages ;
2. Travelling Allowances ;
3. Stationaries ;
4. Recurrent Expenses ;
5. LDSP Project Expenses.
