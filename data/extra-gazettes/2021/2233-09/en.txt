PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (a) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Kegalle Urban Council
I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Kegalle Urban Council, do hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a)
of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Luxman Dissanayake of
the Kegalle Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that Urban Council as he has been ceased to be a member
of the United National Party.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kegalle Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
17th  June, 2021.
