The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN  OF   THE     POONAKARY   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262) AS   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITY  ELECTION   (AMENDED)  ACT,   No. 16   OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   office of the Chairman shall
be deemed to have been resign under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by No. 21 of 2012
(Special Provisions) Local Authority Act  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  the recognized political party to which Arunasalam
Iyampillai  the former Chairman  belonged was required by me under section 66B(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  the member of  that Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be declared elected as Chairman
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sivakumaran Sriranjan to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Winson Gunalan Winson Gnanathayalan, Returning Officer  of  the Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Sivakumaran Sriranjan  selected as   Chairman of the Poonakary
Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. G. W. G
NANATHAYALAN,
Returning Officer,
Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
29th December, 2021.
