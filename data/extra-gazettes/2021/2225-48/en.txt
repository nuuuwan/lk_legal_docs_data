1A-  G  34168— 385  (05/2021)
I,   Gotabaya   Rajapaksa,   President   of   the   Democratic    Socialist   Republic   of  Sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in
me by Section 4 of the Special Presidential Commissions of Inquiry  Act, No. 7 of  1978 National  State Assembly  which  was
amended by Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Act (Special Provisions) No. 4 of  1978  of do hereby enlarge the three
months period of time ending  28th April, 2021 by six months from 28th April, 2021 to 28th October, 2021 for rendition of the
final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the  Special  President  Commission  of  Inquiry  established  on 28th
January 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2212/53 dated 29th January 2021of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of the provisions  of  Section 2 of  the aforesaid Act.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
April 28, 2021.
