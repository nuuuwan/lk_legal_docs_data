THE   FAUNA   AND  FLORA   PROTECTION   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   469)
Order Under  Subsection  (2)  of  Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of  Section 2 of the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance (Chapter
469), I, Wimalaweera Dissanayaka, State Minister of Wildlife Protection, Adoption of Safety Measures including the
Construction of Electrical Fences and Trenches and Reforeststation and Forest Resource Development, do by this Order
declare the area of land described in the Schedule hereto, excluding the area of land comprising 866 Hectares marked as the
"Airport" in the map set out in such Schedule, to be a Managed Elephant Reserve for the purposes of the aforesaid
Ordinance and further declare that such Managed Elephant Reserve shall be called and known as the "Hambantota Managed
Elephant Reserve".
W
IMALAWEERA DISSANAYAKA,
State Minister of Wildlife Protection,
Adoption of Safety Measures including the
Construction of Electrical Fences and
Trenches and Reforeststation and Forest
Resource Development.
Colombo,
09th April, 2021.
