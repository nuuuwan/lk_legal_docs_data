PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   DOMPE  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)(a)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Jayalath
Pathiranage Nishama Dimutu Kumari Pathirana, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 05 Mithirigala of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wijethunga Loku Pitumpage Gnanawathi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Dompe Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Wijethunga Loku Pitumpage Gnanawathi was elected as the member of the Ward Number 05 Mithirigala of
Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
20th July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   DOMPE  PRADESHIYA SABHA UNDER
SECTION 66A (1)(a)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of elected as Parliament member ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Withana
Pathiranage Milan Sajith Jayathilaka, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 06 Kiridiwela of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to

shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Withana Pathirannahelage Ganga Kumari Jayawardana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Dompe Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Withana Pathirannahelage Ganga Kumari Jayawardana was elected as the member of the Ward Number 06
Kiridiwela of Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
