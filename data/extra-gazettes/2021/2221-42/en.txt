NOTICE No : 81/0220 Galle District 
  
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the ownership or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of lands re-
flected in the Cadastral Map No. 810748 made by the Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title Act, No. 21 of 
1998 relating to the village of Igala (Block 08) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 26/D Igala East 
in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Elpitiya in the District of Galle in the Province of Southern should submit his claim to the un-

hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 810748 referred to above may be perused at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, Dis-
trict Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Land Title Settle-
ment.
