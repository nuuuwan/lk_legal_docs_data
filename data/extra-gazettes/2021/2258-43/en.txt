PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE   UNDER    SECTION   10 A(1)(b)    OF   THE   LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer of  the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(b) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Mrs. Ranhoti Pedi Gedara
Santhilatha, a candidate whose name appears in the nomination paper submitted by United National Party for Ganga Ihala
Korale Pradeshiya Sabha at the Local Authorities Election 2018 has forfeited her rights to have her name retained in the
nomination paper of the United National Party as she has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
Dr. H. D. N. S
AMPATH FERNANDO,
Returning Officer.
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
15th December 2021.
