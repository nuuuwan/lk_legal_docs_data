THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                                     My No. : Miri/DS/L/3/2019/04.
           Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2018/HW/439.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
 03 Allotments in total extent of about 0.0817 Hectare, of
land  in Cadastral Map No. 510276, Block No. 02, Sheet No. 7
and dated 15.12.2020 prepared by the Senior - Superintendent
of Surveys of Gampaha District on behalf of the Surveyor
General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 31,
Pahala Bokalagama in Village of  Ambalanwatta in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Mirigama in Gampaha
District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 287
Name of  land: Watakeyapetha Kumbura; Description:
Paddy Field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: C. M. P . R.
Chandrasekara, Walawwa, Indiparape, Mirigama, (to finalize
the land acquisition process); Extent: 0.0150 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 288
Name of  land: -; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabhawa, (to finalize the land
acquisition process); Extent: 0.0494 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 290
Name of  land: Watakeyapetha Kumbura; Description:
Paddy Field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: C. M. P . R.
Chandrasekara, Walawwa, Indiparape, Mirigama, (to finalize
the land acquisition process); Extent: 0.0173 Hectare.
          All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat  of Mirigama on
10.07.2021 at 10.00 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02.07.2021 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
                                                        W. P . PRASAD INDIKA,
