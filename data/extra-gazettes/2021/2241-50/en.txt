FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     GALNEWA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of  Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Jayantha Kumara returned member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Heenkenda Mudalige Don Alfred Lionel Jayasinghe  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer  of  the Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Heenkenda Mudalige Don
Alfred Lionel Jayasinghe  was selected as the member of  the  Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. M
UGUNTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Galnewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anurathapura,
10th August, 2021.
