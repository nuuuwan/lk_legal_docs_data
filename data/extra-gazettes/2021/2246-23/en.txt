PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
DEPARTMENT   OF   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  -  EASTERN   PROVINCE
Notice on summon meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections  (Amendment)
Act, No. 22 of 2012
BY virtue of  power vested on me under Section 66(C) read with Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner
of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to fill the vacancy arose as a result of resigning
the post of Vice Chairman of the Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha, Trincomalee District, Eastern Province at 10.00 a. m. on
11.10.2021 at Kinniya Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall for the purpose of elect the new Vice Chairman.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of  Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
24th  September, 2021.
