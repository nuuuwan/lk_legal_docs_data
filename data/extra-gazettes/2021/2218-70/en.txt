THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No.2 of 1980,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-charge of the subject of Urban Development & Housing, being of the opinion that the land morefully described
in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the purpose of implementation of Athurugiriya new town development project
do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
March  12 , 2021.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land namely DawatagahaKumbura, MillagahaOwitaKumbura, GorokgahaDeniya, NallawalaKumbura
depicted as from Lot 1 to 7 in the sketch plan No. 04/2020 dated 04.12.2020 prepared by D K Wickramasekara, Land Manager
of Urban Development Authority, containing in extent of about 80 Acres situated in Pore, Gowinnagodagama, Boralugoda
and Athurugiriya villages within the Grama Niladari Divisions of 489A - Boralugoda, 490B – Taldiyawala and 490 - Athurugiriya
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kaduwela in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
