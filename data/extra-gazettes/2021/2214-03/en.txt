THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY,   LAW   No.  41  OF  1978   ENACTED   BY  THE  NATIONAL   STATE
ASSEMBLY
Order  under  Section  15(1)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 enacted
by the National State Assembly, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development & Housing do by this Order to vest
with the Urban Development Authority, which effect from today, the land situated within the limits of the Municipal Council
of Kurunegala described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land of Kurunegala Rajapihilla Rest House belonging to
the Municipal Council of Kurunegala.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of  Urban Development & Housing.
Ministry of Urban Development & Housing,
17th & 18th Floors,
“Suhurupaya”,
Battaramulla,
January  05, 2021.
