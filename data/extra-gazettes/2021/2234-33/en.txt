PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  KADUWELA  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Kaduwela Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Ceased to be a member
of the Party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Payagalage
Susanthi Iththapana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Subasinghe Gamachchige Dulmi Kaushalya Kulathunga to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Kaduwela Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Subasinghe Gamachchige Dulmi Kaushalya Kulathunga was selected as the Member of the Kaduwela Municipal
Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kaduwela Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo.
30th June, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 01.07.2021
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE  OF MEMBER  OF  THE   KOTAPOLA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1) OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Kotapola Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kaluthanthrige
Mahandarathna the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Kotapola Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muthukutti Kankanamge Ajith Prasanna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Kotapola Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Muthukutti Kankanamge Ajith Prasanna was selected as the Member of the Kotapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kotapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matara.
30th June, 2021.
