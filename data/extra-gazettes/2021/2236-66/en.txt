SPECIAL COMMODITY LEVY ACT, No. 48 OF 2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Basil
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto the Special Commodity Levy at rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from July 19, 2021 subject to provisions of Sub
Section (3) of  Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
B
ASIL RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
July 17, 2021.
