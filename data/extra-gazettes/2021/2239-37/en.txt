No. 2239/37 –  THURSDAY, AUGUST   05, 2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISTION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 Land Ministry's No.: 4-3/11/2019/H/25. 
My Reference No.: BER/4/5/2/82. 
 
I, hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by The Land  
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public  purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or servitude to be acquired: 
 
One allotment of land in total extent of 0.0230 
Hectare known as Lot No. 1 in Preliminary Plan No. 
ක 4834 dated 22.06.2021 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated at Grama Niladhari's Division of 
748 A -Polkotuwa, Divisional Secretariat Division of 
Beruwala  in Kalutara District, Western Province, 
Morefully described as follows.  
 
Lot No. 1 - Name of land: Dummalamodara, 
Thuduwewatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: Railway Reservation; 
Extent: 9.1 Perches. 
 
North: Illeperumachchiyewattha vs Ileperumachchiyawatta 
claimaed by D. Surantha and others; East: Lot No. 01 (Road) 
(PS) and Lot 02 (District Hospital, Beruwala) of PPA 1071; 
South: Lot 2 (District Hospital, Beruwala) of PPA 1071; West: 
Illeperumachchiyewattha vs Ileperumachchiyawatta claimed by 
D. Surantha and others.  
 
 
 
All  persons interested in the land/lands on which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear  
personally, or by agents, duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretariat of Beruwala on the 08th day of 
September, 2021 at 10.00 a. m. O' clock, and are hereby 
requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 
the 01st day of September 2021, the nature of their interest 
in the lands the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
                                        M. RANJAN P. PERERA, 
                                              Divisional Secretary, 
                                                      Beruwala. 
   
At the  Divisional Secretariat, Beruwala 
19th July 2021.
