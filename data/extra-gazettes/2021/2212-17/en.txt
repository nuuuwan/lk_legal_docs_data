THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : MUL/LND/02/ACQ/91.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/15/2016/VE/506.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends  to acquire the following lands which are required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Under mentioned allotments of lands in total extent of 1.9867
Hectares shown as lot No. 81, 82 in the plan of F.V .P . 89,
supplement No. 07, Sheet No. 09 and dated 16.09.2020 prepared
by Surveyor General situated in the village of Galatumba,
Galatumba Grama Niladhari Division, Mulatiyana Divisional
Secretary's Division, Matara District, Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 81
Name of  land: Kalugalabadda and Kalugahahena;
Description: Garden, 20-25 years old Cashew trees, 40 Attoniya
trees, 20-25 years old Garcinia trees (Goraka),  20-25 years old
Cinnamon cultivation and mixed crops; Claimant:  Government;
Original Claimant: - Abesirinarayana Wanigarathna Nandasiri
and others; Extent: 1.9804 Hectares.
02. Lot No. 82
Name of  land: Kammalgoda Waththahenyaya; Description:
Garden; Claimant:  Government;  Original Claimant:
Abesirinarayana Wanigarathna Nandasiri and others; Extent:
0.0063 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary of Mulatiyana on the day 17th March,
2021  at  10.30 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th March, 2021 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
K. N. KAUSHALYA KUMARI,
Divisional Secretary,
Mulatiyana.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Mulatiyana.
04th January , 2021
