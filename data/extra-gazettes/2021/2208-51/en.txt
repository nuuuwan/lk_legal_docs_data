FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MUTHUR    PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Selvaratnam
Chandrakumar,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thillaiyambalam Harieston to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thillaiyambalam Harieston
was selected as the member of  the  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Trincomalee,
28th  December, 2020.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VALIKAMAM    SOUTH
PRADESHIYA    SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which  Kanthasami
Sothimalar,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon

of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ariyanayakam Jegatheeswary to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam South  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ariyanayakam
Jegatheeswary was selected as the member of  the  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
28th  December, 2020.
