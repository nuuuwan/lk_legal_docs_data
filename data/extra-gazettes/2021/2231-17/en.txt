1A-  PG 5467— 495 (06/2021)
IMPORTS  AND  EXPORTS  (CONTROL)  ACT, No. 1 OF 1969
 
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 20 read together with Sub-Section 4(1) Section 14 of the Imports and Exports
(Control) Act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by Act, No. 48 of 1985 and Act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, the
Minister of Finance, promulgate following Regulations.
 
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
 
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
11 June 2021.
 
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as “Imports and Exports Control Regulations No. 09  of  2021”.
2. These Regulations shall be in force effective from 11 June 2021.
3. No exporter shall export the goods specified in the Schedule I without the recommendation of the National Medicines
Regulatory Authority, Sri Lanka.
4. Final decision on any issues arising in relation to implementation of these Regulations shall be made by the Controller
General of Imports and Exports considering the interest of national economy, public health and environmental
security.
