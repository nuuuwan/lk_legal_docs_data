CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Notification of Honourable Governor of  Central Province
THE  ORDER   MADE   UNDER  SECTIONS  184(1)(I),  184(1B)(b)  OF  THE   AMENDED   URBAN    COUNCIL
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  255)    TO  BE  READ   WITH   SECTION   2   OF  THE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
(CONSEQUENTIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  12  OF 1989
AS per the final report handed over to me on 25.01.2021 with observations and conclusions made by conducting an inquiry
and considering evidence, documents and written submissions of the complainant and respondent by the Commission of
Inquiry, appointed by me by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2199/9 dated 27.10.2020 to inquire allegations made against
Mr. Anagipura Asoka Sepala, Chairman of the Urban Council Talawakele-Lindula by a council member, and to report within
3 months whether he has committed any misdeed described in Section 184(1) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance
(Chapter 255) as the Chairman of the Urban Council Talawakele-Lindula.
And since I am satisfied that there is sufficient proof of misdeeds referred to in Subsections 184 (1)(d) and (e) on the
part of Mr. Angipura Asoka Sepala, Chairman of the Urban Council Talawakele-Lindula.
By virtue of  powers vested in me by Sections 184(1)(i) and 184(1B) (b) of the amended Urban Council Ordinance
(Chapter 255), to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Lalith
U. Gamage, Attorney-at-Law and Governor of the Central Province do hereby remove Mr. Anagipura Asoka Sepala, Chairman
of the Urban Council Talawakele-Lindula from office with effect from 29.01.2021.
Further, I order the Commissioner of Local Government, Central Province and Secretary of Urban Council, Talawakele-
Lindula to act according to the provisions of the amended Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262).
L
ALITH U. GAMAGE,
Attorney-at-Law,
Governor,  Central Province.
29th January 2021,
