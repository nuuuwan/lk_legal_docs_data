FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     ANURADHAPURA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Anuradhapura Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Rashmika
Gamaarachchi,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of   Anuradhapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Dharmasena to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Anuradhapura Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thennakoon
Mudiyanselage Dharmasena was selected as the member of  the  Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
District Election  Office,
Anuradhapura,
27th  January, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 29.01.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     PADA VIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Menik
Ralage Nimal Weerakkody,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of   that Ward No. 02 - Parakramapura of Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Chandrasekara Mudiyanselage Nadeesha Lakmini Jayawardhana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Chandrasekara
Mudiyanselage Nadeesha Lakmini Jayawardhana was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 02 - Parakramapura of
Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Padaviya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election  Office,
Anuradhapura,
27th  January, 2021.
