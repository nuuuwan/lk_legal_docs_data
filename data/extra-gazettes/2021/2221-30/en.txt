 THE Notice published under Part III of the Extra 
Ordinary Gazette notification No. 1154/18 and dated 19th 
October, 2000 in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka regarding the land described below under Section 
07 of the Land Acquition Act, is amended and published 
as follows : 
 
 I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1)  of 
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for a public purpose. 
Description of  Lands to be Acquired :  
The allotments of lands in Preliminary Plan No. Pu. 
3777 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveyor of 
Puttalam District, situated in the villages of Chilaw 
Division No. 2 Aluthwatta, Division No. 2 Ichchampitiya 
(within limits of Urban Council of Chilaw) in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Chilaw in Puttalam 
District, in the North Western Province.
