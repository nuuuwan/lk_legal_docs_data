CEYLON  PETROLEUM  CORPORATION  ACT,  NO.  28 OF 1961
ORDFR  UNDER  SECTION 40(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 40(1) of the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation Act, No. 28 of 1961 as
amended by the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation (Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 1963, I, Udaya Gammanpila, Minister of
Energy, do hereby approve the proposed acquisition of the property described in the schedule hereto, which is required
for the business affairs of the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation.
 U
DAYA GAMMANPILA,
Minister of Energy.
26th June, 2021.
SCHEDULE
All that allotment of paddy land depicted in the sketch made by Ceylon Petroleum Corporation together with the building,
trees, plantations and everything else standing thereon situated at Heiyanthuduwa Village within the No. 275/C,
Heiyanthuduwa West Grama Niladhari Division in the Biyagama Divisional Secretariat area within the Pradeshiya Sabha
Limits of Biyagama in Adhikari Pattuwa of Siyane Korale in the District of Gampaha, Western Province and which said
land bounded on the North by Heiyanthuduwa West Village, on the East by Heiyanthuduwa Temple Road, on the South by
Heiyanthuduwa West Village and the West by land claimed by Ceylon Petroleum Corporation and containing in extent
Eighteen Acres (A:18-R:00-P:00) according to the said sketch.
