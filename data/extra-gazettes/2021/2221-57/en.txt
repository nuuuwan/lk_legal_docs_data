THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER   203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport under section 237 read with section 126 of the Motor Traffic Act
(Chapter 203).
GAMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Transport.
Colombo,
30th March, 2021.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving
License) Regulations No. 1 of 2021.
2. The validity period of every Driving License issued by the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic under Part VII
of the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203), which is due to expire within the period commencing from 1
st of April, 2021 and ending
30th of September, 2021 as specified in such Driving License, shall hereby be extended for six (06) months from the date of such
expiry.
3. These regulations shall not apply to a Driving License of which the validity period has already been extended by
the Motor Traffic (Temporary Extension of the Validity Period of Driving License) Regulations No. 03 of 2020 published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2180/26 of June 17, 2020 (as last amended by Regulation published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2211/24 of January 18, 2021).
