12.2   Training and Development: 
12.2.1		 	 All	officers	recruited	to	Grade	III	should	complete	the	training	course	in	Basic	Teaching	Methods	over	a	minimum	
period	of	four	(04)	weeks	(160	hours)	before	engaging	in	service.	A	certificate	will	be	issued	after	evaluating	the	
teaching	skill	of	the	officers	at	the	end	of	that	training	programme.
  this training will be conducted by the ministry in charge of the subject or any other advanced training or higher 
education institute as decided by the Secretary of the ministry.
 
12.2.2 	 	 All	officers	in	Grade	II	of	the	Service	should	complete	a	training	course	in	Institutional	Regulations,	Financial	
regulations and National and International Labour market over a period of 100 hours and they should have 
obtained	this	training	certificate	for	promotion	to	Grade	I.

3. objectives of the criteria:- 
 I. objective and Approach 
	 	Evaluate	the	successful	clarification	of	the	objective	of	the	lesson	and	the	ability	of	approaching	the	lesson.	
 II. Personality and voice Control 
  Should have a good personality as a teacher and the ability of proper voice control in teaching is evaluated.
 III. Clarity in Communication
	 	Evaluate	the	ability	of	effective	communication	of	relevant	matters	in	the	process	of	learning	and	teaching.
 Iv. time management
  evaluate the skill in achieving the objective within the given timeframe.
 v . Application of Presentation techniques
  evaluate the ability in proper usage of presentation techniques to achieve the objective.
