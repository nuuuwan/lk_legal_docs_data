PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 33822— 45 (03/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Aluthgamage
Ramani Namananda Aluthgamage of  the Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that
Pradeshiya Sabha  she has  been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
25th  February, 2021.

NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Kandy Municipal Council
I,  Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Kandy Municipal Council, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Marimuththu Pillei
