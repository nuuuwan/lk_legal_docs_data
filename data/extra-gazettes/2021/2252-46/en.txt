Whereas the office of member of Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Kanthan 
Raththinam the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of  that ward No. 13 - Karanawai of Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
elected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kalaivany Balakrishnan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Kalaivany Balakrishnan  was elected as the member of the ward No. 13 - Karanawai of Vadamaradchi South West  
Pradeshiya Sabha.
