PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - PG  5304— 35 (02/2021)
NOTICE  MADE UNDER  SECTION  66c READ  WITH  SECTION  66g OF LOCAL
AUTHORITEIS  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT) ACT,  No.  22  OF  2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the
powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by  Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment)  Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a recalling meeting in view of selecting a
new Chairman for the vacated Chairman’s position of Katunayake Seeduwa Urban Council of Western Province, will be
convened on February 22nd of 2021 at 9.30 a. m. at the Urban Council Chamber of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council
located at Seeduwa.
A. D. P. I. P
RASANNA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla,
05th February, 2021.
