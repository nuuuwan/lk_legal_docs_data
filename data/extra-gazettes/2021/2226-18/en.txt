NOTICE   UNDER   REGISTRATION   OF   ELECTORS   ACT,   No.   44   OF   1980   AS   AMENDED
BY   THE   REGISTRATION   OF    ELECTORS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No. 10  OF  2017
WHEREAS certain persons have been internally displaced as a result of any actions of a terrorist militant or other group
during the recent past;
02. Whereas in terms of Section 2 of the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 2017, any
citizen of Sri Lanka:
2.1  who was or had been an internally displaced person;
2.2 whose name appeared in the register of electors for any electoral district for any year, until the end of
the year 2009; and
2.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009,
shall on proof of the above matters and on production of a certificate issued by the Grama Niladhari of the area in which
he is presently residing to the effect that he is or had been an internally displaced person, be entitled to make an application
to the registering officer of the electoral district within which he was permanently resident prior to May 18, 2009, to be
registered in the register of electors of such electoral district;
03. And whereas in terms of Section 2(3) of the aforesaid Special Provisions Act, the children of a citizen of Sri
Lanka referred to in paragraph 2 above:
3.1 who had not attained the age of eighteen years on the date on which such citizen became an internally
displaced person and have attained the age of eighteen years or more on the date on which the revision
commenced in respect of the register in operation, at the time in which the application is made;

04. In terms of the powers vested in the Election Commission under the Registration of Electors (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 2017, I do hereby notify that the receipt of applications for the preparation of a supplementary
register of electors for the year 2020 including the names of those who meet the requirements specified in paragraphs 02
and 03 above will commence on 04

forms could be obtained from the offices of the registering officers of all electoral districts in Sri Lanka; and that duly
filled applications shall be submitted to the relevant registering officers.
05. I further notify that it is expected to finalize the preparation of the supplementary register referred to in
paragraph 04 above on 11
h day of May 2021 and the notice thereon to be issued on the same day and that it is also expected
to allocate additional time period of 14 days for those who fulfill the requirements specified above and whose names are
not entered in the said supplementary register to make appeals to enter their names in the supplementary register.
