EXCISE (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that :-
(i) the rate of Excise duty specified in the Column IV applicable on corresponding articles specified in Column
I of the Schedule A hereto, shall amend the rate specified in Column IV on the corresponding articles of the
Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2113/9 dated March 05, 2019, as amended issued
under the Section 3 of this Act with effect from April 23, 2021;
(ii) amend the Schedule II of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2113/9 dated March 05, 2019, as   amended
issued under the Section 3 of this Act as set out in the Schedule B with effect from April 23, 2021.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01.
April 22, 2021.
