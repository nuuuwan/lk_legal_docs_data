My No.: 9/3/3/1/2014/III.
I, U.W.T.U. Rajakaruna, Divisional Secretary, Divulapitiya of 
Gampaha District, in Western Province do hereby give notice, 
under Section 33 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)

the following schedule acquired by the state, under the Land 
Acquisition Act, the lands for which no claimant appeared 
and unclaimed lots of the lands, amount of the nominal/full 
valuation, I hereby inform you that it has been credited to 
the Attanagalla District Court to case numbers given herein.  
 U. W. T. U. Rajakaruna,
 Divisional Secretary,
