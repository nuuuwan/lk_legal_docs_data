FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PELIYAGODA   URBAN  
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE     
(CHAPTER   262)  
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of  member of Peliyagoda Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And whereas the Secretary  o f the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which 
Hewaduwaththa Gamage Sudesh  the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon the  member  of that No. 08 - Peliyagoda Gagabada East of Peliyagoda Urban Council who belongs 
to such party and is not disquali ﬁed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who 
shall be declared selected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ilucksooriya Arachchilage Sumith Ranasingha to ﬁll such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Of ﬁcer  of  the Peliyagoda Urban 
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Ilucksooriya Arachchilage Sumith Ranasingha was selected as the member  of the Ward Number  08 - Peliyagoda 
Gagabada East of Peliyagoda Urban Council .
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Ofﬁcer,
Peliyagoda Urban Council .
