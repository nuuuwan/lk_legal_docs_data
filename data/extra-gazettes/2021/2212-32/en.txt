1A - G 33601  - 28  (2021/01) 
LAND REFORMS ACT NO. 01 OF 1972 OF THE NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY 
 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27 a (1) of the Land Reform Law No. 01 of 1972 amended from 
time to time, which should be read w ith Article 44 (2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka and as per the request of the Land Reform Commission, I, S. M.  Chandrasena, Minister of Lands of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby :- 
 
 1.  Alienate allotments of agricultural land which are ve sted in the Land reform Commission established under the  
Land Reforms Act, No. 01 of 1972 of the National State Assembly in accordance with Section 3 therein and 
specified in the Schedule of this Order to the Board of Investments of Sri Lanka established by the Act, No. 04 
of 1978 by way of sale in accordance with the Section 22(1) b of the said Land Reforms Act. 
 
 2.  Rs. 200,000,000.00 (Two Hundred Million Rupees) has been paid to the Land Reform Commission as the 
monetary consideration for the aforesaid sale by the Board of Investments of Sri Lanka for  the land scheduled 
below.
