PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - PG 5493— 25 (07/2021)
NOTICE  MADE  UNDER  SECTION  66c  READ  WITH  SECTION  66g  OF  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   AMENDED   BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   (AMENDMENT)
ACT, NO. 22  OF 2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to
the powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66 g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of selecting a
new Vice Chairman, for the vacated Vice Chairman’s position of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council of Western Province,
will be convened on July 27 of 2021 at 9.30 a. m. at the Urban Council Chamber of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council
located at Seeduwa.
A. D. P. I. P
RASANNA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla.
07th July, 2021.
