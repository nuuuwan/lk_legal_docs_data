FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     ATTANAGALLA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Democratic National Movement, the recognized political party to which
Kamal Banda Rathnayake the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of  Democratic National Movement who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Athukorala Udumullage Kapila Ganesh Karunarathne to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Attanagalla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Athukorala Udumullage Kapila Ganesh Karunarathne  was selected as the member of  the   Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Gampaha,
16th  March, 2021.
