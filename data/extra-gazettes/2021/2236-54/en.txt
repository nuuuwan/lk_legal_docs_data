FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     YATAWATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Siththaiya Saundaranayagam the  member  belonged,
was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Linde
Gedara Lionel Ariyadasa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Yatawatta Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Linde Gedara Lionel Ariyadasa was selected as the member of the Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
05th of July, 2021.
