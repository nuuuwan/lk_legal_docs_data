THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 My No. : 11/3/3/1/26A.
Land Ministry's Ref No.:  LD/05/2010/MV/91.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following lands which are
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Lands to be acquired :
Public purpose means the land acquisition for the
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project.
The lands described below, containing in extent of 3.6397
Hectares shown in Topo PP 20  of  Supplement No.163 , Sheet
No.270,  approved by Surveyor General dated on 12.03.2021,
situated at E402 G Kandepitawala Division of Laggala
Pallesiya Pattuwa, Laggala Divisional Secretary’s Division,
Matale District, Central province.
