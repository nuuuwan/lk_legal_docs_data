STA TEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether  injustice had  been caused to Mr. A. L. Wijesundara who worked at Lumiere Textiles  Limited, (Lumiere Textiles Private
Limited later) by the non payment of driving allowance, transport  allowance and fuel allowance and to Mrs. S. P. A. K. Lanka
Samarakoon by the  non payment of transport allowance and fuel allowance and if such injustice had been caused  to what
relief  both of them are entitled.
B.K.  PRABATH  CHANDRAKEERTHI,
Commissioner General of  Labour.
