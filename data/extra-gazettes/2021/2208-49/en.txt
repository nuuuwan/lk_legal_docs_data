UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G .L. Peiris, Minister of
Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Royal Institute Colombo
(Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration
number PV 9729 and located at No. 92, Sunethra Devi Road, Kohuwala 10250, Nugegoda do by this Order and subject to such
conditions as specified in the Schedule I hereto, recognize the Royal Institute Colombo (Private) Limited as a Degree
Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in
Column I in the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in Column II in Schedule
II hereto.
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
1. Royal Institute Colombo (Private) Limited, shall conduct the course of study titled Bachelor of Business Honours in
International Economics and Finance, only at No. 92, Sunethra Devi Road, Kohuwala 10250, Nugegoda.

Order under Section  25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Horizon College of Business
and Technology (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing
registration number PV 62877, being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the
Order made under the said Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2053/38 of January 11, 2018 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2164/54 of February 27, 2020) by the addition immediately after the entry
“Bachelor of Education Honours in English” appearing in Column I and its abbreviation appearing in the corresponding
entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and  Column II respectively of Schedule II hereto, subject
to the conditions specified in the Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE    I
C
ONDITIONS
1. The minimum entry qualification for admitting a student shall be three (03) Simple Passes (S) in Biological Science
Stream or Bio-system Technology Stream in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advanced Level) Examination conducted
by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
