PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34274— 55  (05/2021)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a)
of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Upali Dissanayaka of
the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
27th April, 2021.
