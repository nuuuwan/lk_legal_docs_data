FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MADULLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Tisawalangu
Mudiyanselage Jayasundara the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dhanapala Mudiyanselage Amarasena to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Dhanapala Mudiyanselage
Amarasena was selected as the member of the Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA GAYAN PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Madulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Monaragala,
02nd July, 2021.
