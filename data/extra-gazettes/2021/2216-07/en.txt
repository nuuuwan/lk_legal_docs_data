FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     GALEWELA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   dead ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Rathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Wijethunga Banda,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of   Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Demuni Niwanthaka de Zoysa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Galewela Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Demuni Niwanthaka de Zoysa  was selected as the member of  the   Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Matale,
18th  February, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RATHNAPURA    MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Rathnapura Municipal Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Assala Arachchige
Laki Saman Kumara Alwis,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of   Rathnapura Municipal Council   who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kalupahanage Anushka Dilhara Kalupahana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Rathnapura Municipal Council , acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kalupahanage
Anushka Dilhara Kalupahana was selected as the member of  the   Rathnapura Municipal Council.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Rathnapura Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Rathnapura,
18th  February, 2021.
