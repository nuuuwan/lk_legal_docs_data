FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KURUNEGALA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Abegoda Liyanage
Nilan Indika Samarasinghe the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of  United National Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamed Shahabdeen Mohamed Mowjood to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Mohamed
Shahabdeen Mohamed Mowjood was selected as the member of the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
05th July, 2021.
