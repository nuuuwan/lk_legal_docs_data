FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE     PUTTALAM   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Puttalam Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress  recognized political party to which Kamardeen
Abdul Baiz, the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the  member of that No. 01 Puttalam Central of Puttalam Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ahamadu Neina Marikkar Jaufar Marikkar to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Puttalam Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  that Ahamadu Neina
Marikkar Jaufar Marikkar  was elected as the member of the No. 01 Puttalam Central of Puttalam Urban Council.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
29th  June, 2021.
