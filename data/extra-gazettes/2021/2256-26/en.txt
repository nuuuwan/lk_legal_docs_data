Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE
KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION 66A (1) OF THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)

WHEREAS  the Office of return member of Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And Whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which Noorul
Ameen Muhammadu Asam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of Kalmunai Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30 days;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammadu Ibrahim  Rajabdeen to fill such vacancy;
 Therefore  I,  Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, returning officer of the Kalmunai Municipal Council acting in terms of
selection 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammadu Ibrahim
Rajabdeen was selected as the member of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
M.B.M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council,
District Election Office,
Ampara.

PART IV (B) : GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 30.11.2021
FILLING   OF   A  V ACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE   OF   MEMBER    OF   THE
HATTON   DICKOY A   URBAN  COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION  66A (1)  OF THE
LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS  the Office of return member of Hatton Dickoya Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Dead;
And Whereas the Secretary of the Communist Party of Sri Lanka, the recognized  political  party to which Palaniyandy
Subramaniyam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  the  Hatton Dickoya Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30 days.
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Subramaniyam Bresnav to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore  I , Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, returning officer of the Hatton Dickoya
Urban Council, acting in terms of selection 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Subramaniyam Bresnav was selected as the member of the Hatton Dickoya Urban Council.
K
ALUGAMPITIYA APPUHAMILAGE LAHIRU SHANAKA KALUGAMPITIYA,
Returning Officer,
Hatton Dickoya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Nuwaraeliya.
