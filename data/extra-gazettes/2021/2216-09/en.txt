SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Implementation of New Financial Rules of the Southern Provincial Council
IN terms of powers in regard to Provincial Council Fund vested in me under Section 19(5) and 20(3) of the Provincial Council
Act, No. 42 of 1987 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Wiliam Wijesinghe Gamage, Governor of the Southern
Province, having considered the powers and functions Conferred on me under article 154(c) of the Con stitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Section 2(1) and 2(2) of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)
Act, No. 12 of 1989, Section 3(2) of Transfer of Powers (Divisional Secretaries) Act, No. 58 of 1992, do hereby abolish the
Financial Rules issued in 2007 with regard to payment of Money out of the Provincial Council Fund and issue new financial
rules which shall come into operation on 01.03.2021. Furthermore, I do hereby require that these rul es shall come into
operation for the Provincial Public Service of the Southern Province exceeding the Government Financ ial Regulations
adopted for the financial control of the Provincial Council Fund or any other orders relevant thereto issued by me. And I do
hereby require that until any procedure with regard to financial control of that fund is issued in c ompliance with the
provisions of the statute of the relevant institutions established under a statute of the Southern P rovincial Council, these
rules shall also apply to those institutions.
Dr. W. W. GAMAGE,
Governor,
Southern Province.
Southern Provincial Governor's Secretariat,
Southern Provincial Council,
Lower Dickson Road,
Galle.
22nd of February, 2021.
