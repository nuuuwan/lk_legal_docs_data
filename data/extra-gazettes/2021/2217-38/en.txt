L.D.B 5/2005
THE MEDIATION (SPECIAL CATEGORIES OF DISPUTES) ACT, No. 21 OF 2003
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act, No. 21 of 2003,
I, Mohammed Ali Sabry, Minister of Justice, do by this Order, specify the areas set out in the Schedule hereto, as Special
Mediation Board Areas within which the settlement of disputes relating to the possession or ownership of land as determined
by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1901/8 dated February 10, 2015 shall be carried out, under the provisions
of the said Act.
M
OHAMMED ALI SABRY PC,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
04th March, 2021.
SCHEDULE
Administrative Districts of Colombo, Gampaha, Ratnapura, Kandy, Hambantota, Kurunegala and Polonnaruwa.
