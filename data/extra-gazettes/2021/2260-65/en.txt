On 30th december, 2021
ORDER
As the post of mayor of the badulla municipal council has become vacant under section 7 of the Local Authorities (special 
Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 due to the failure to pass the budget report for the year 2022 of badulla municipal council 
in the badulla district of the uva Province in accordance with the requirements of sections 215 and 215A of the municipal 
councils Ordinance, I hereby inform that the powers, duties, functions of the above post is handed over to the deputy mayor 
in accordance with section 14 (6) of the municipal councils Ordinance, in order to perform and implement the above from 
the date of vacancy until the vacancy is filled as per the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
Further, I hereby order the deputy mayor of the badulla municipal council on the recommendation of the municipal 
commissioner of the badulla municipal council and prior approval of the commissioner of Local Government to incur the 
expenditures relevant to the recurrent expenditure for the year 2022 and the essential expenditures relevant to the ongoing 
projects including the L.d.s.P. project to ensure the continuation of the activities of the badulla municipal council until the 
newly appointed mayor presents the budget for 2022 to the council and pass the budget.
