MOTOR   TRAFFIC   ACT  (CHAPTER   203)
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 of the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203) read with
Section 213B of the aforesaid Act.
Minister of Transport.
Colombo,
10th September, 2021.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Road Safety Fund) Regulations No. 1 of 2021 and shall
come into force on October 01, 2021.
2. Every insurer or person who issues a policy of insurance or security, as the case may be, in respect of third
party risks, under Part VI of the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203) shall contribute two per centum (2%) of the premium to the
fund.
3. The Road Sefety Fund Regulations No. 01 of 2004, published in Gazette Extraordinanry No. 1368/18 of
November 24, 2004 is hereby rescinded, without prejudice to anything previously done thereunder,
4. For the purpose of these regulations -
“Fund” means the Road Safety Fund established under Section 213B(2) of the Motor Traffic Act
(Chapter 203).
