NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No. 02 OF  1974
Order under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 02
of 1974, I, Vasudeva Nanayakkara, Minister of Water Supply, do hereby approve the acquisition of the immovable
properties of the land described in the following Schedule as it is required by the said Board, for the construction of
Water Treatment Plant of Kiriella New Water Supply Project.

ERRATA
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 02
of 1974, I Vasudeva Nanayakkara, Minister of Water Supply, published an announcement on page 1A and page 2A
under No. 07-329 in the Government Extraodinary Gazette  No. 2181/28 dated 26.06.2020 of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka in which the proposed activity for the acquisition of the land mentioned as acquisition of an
allotment of land for the construction of the access road to the proposed land for resettlement of the people affected by
the Basnagoda Reservior under Gampaha, Aththanagalla and Minuwangoda Intergrated Water Supply Project which I
hereby decalre should be amended to read as acquisition of a part of the road used for the access road to the proposed
land for resettlement of the people affected by the Basnagoda Reservior under Gampaha, Aththanagalla and Minuwangoda
Intergrated Water Supply Project.
