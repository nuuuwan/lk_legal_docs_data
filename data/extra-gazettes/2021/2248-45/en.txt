FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     HORANA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Horana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sumith Wasantha
Wanigathunge the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
renturned member of  Horana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Gamini Weerakoon to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Horana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Gamini Weerakoon was selected as the member of the Horana Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Horana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
06th  October, 2021.
