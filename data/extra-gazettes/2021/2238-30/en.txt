EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance by virtue of the
powers vested in him by the Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, and published as the Excise
Notification No. 04/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2209/44 dated January 07, 2021 by amending the
Excise Notification No. 03/2018, published in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2054/41 dated January 18, 2018 in order
to revise the opening and closing hours of Toddy Taverns, specified in the Item No. 02 of Schedule of that Notification, by
substituting the opening and closing hours specified in the Schedule below with effect from January 07, 2021, was passed
by Parliament on May 04, 2021.
S. R. A
TTYGALLE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
July 29, 2021.
