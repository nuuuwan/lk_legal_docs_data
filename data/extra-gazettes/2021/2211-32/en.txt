REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT, NO. 32  OF  1968
Order under Section 34
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016, I, Gotabaya
Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, alter the date of expiration published
in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of Sections 6, 9, 14, 16 and 17 of the
Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the aforesaid Act (other than the Registration
of Persons Regulation 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of November, 20, 1990) to be 31.12.2021, until the necessary
infrastructure, arrangements and technological methodologies are made to.
GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
18th January, 2021.
