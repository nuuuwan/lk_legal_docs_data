THE  JUDICATURE  ACT,  NO.  2  OF  1978
Order under Section  13
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me under Section 13 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I, Mohammed Ali Sabry, Minister
of Justice, do by this Order, empower the Judge of the High Court sitting in the judicial zone of Hambantota to exercise
Admiralty jurisdiction over the -
(a) territorial limits of the Administrative District of Hambantota and its territorial waters ; and
(b) land and water area limits as specified in the Order made under Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Sri Lanka Ports
Authority Act, No. 51 of 1979 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2038/15 dated September 25, 2017.
M
OHAMMED ALI SABRY PC,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
26th July, 2021.
