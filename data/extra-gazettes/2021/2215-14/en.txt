THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of Lands,
do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
The  allotments of land described in below names and extent
in Advance Tracing No. Co/KDW/2018/621 dated 14.11.2018
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village of
Pore (within the Municipal Council limits of Kaduwela) in Grama
Niladari Division 489 A, Boralugoda in the Divisional Secretariat
Division of Kaduwela in Colombo District in Western Province
and boundaries of lands as follows:
01. Lot  'A' – Saibaraduyaya – Extent: About 1.5426
Hectares
North: Ela, Dawatagaha kumbura of which claimant could
not be traced and Malabe - Godagama Road; East: Road; South:
Road, Dawatagahakumbura claimed by N. Premathilaka and
Ela; West: Ela and Road.
02. Lot  'B' – Saibaraduyaya – Extent: About 1.6449
Hectares
North: Dawatagahawatta claimed by B. J. Kure and Others
and Malabe - Godagama Road; East: Dawatagahawatta claimed
by Nisako (Pvt) Ltd, Ela and Dawatagahakumbura of which
claimant could not be traced; South: Dawatagahakumbura of
which claimant could not be traced and Ela; West: Ela, Road
and Dawatagahawatta claimed by B. J. Kure and Others.
