THE  CUSTOMS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  235)
Customs Notification No. 2/2021 - Bonded Warehouses
NotificAtioN  mAde  bY  the  director  GeNerAl  of  cuStomS  iN  termS  of  SectioN  69  of  
the  cuStomS  ordiNANce
iN pursuance of the power vested in me by Section 69 of the customs ordinance (chapter 235), i, Ganwarige Vijitha 
ravipriya, director General of customs, do, with the approval of the minister of finance granted by virtue of the powers 
vested in him by that Section, by this notice publish in accordance with his direction, approve and appoint warehouses 
and places of security specified under Column 03 of the Schedule I & II annexed hereto, as Public bonds, Manufacture 
- in -bonds, duty free shops, feeder bonds and Private bonds, in which the goods specified under Column 06 of the said 
Schedules imported by the importer/owners of such warehouses and places of security may be warehoused, kept and secured 
without payment of duty on the first entry thereof.
 Major General (Retired) G.V .
