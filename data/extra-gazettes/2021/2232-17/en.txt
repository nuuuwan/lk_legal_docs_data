PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kothmale Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Kothmale Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that
Mr. Herath Mudiyanselage Nishshanka Gedara Sanjeewa Dananja of the Kothmale Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office
of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
PULASTHI SERASINGHE PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Kothmale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
14th June, 2021.
