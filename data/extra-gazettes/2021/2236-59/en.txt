PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)(a)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Murukesu
Sivagnanasuntharamoorthi, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 18 Udayanagar of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Arulanantham Jesurajan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Winson Gunalan Winson Gnanathayalan, Returning Officer of the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Arulanantham Jesurajan  was elected as the member of the Ward Number 18 Udayanagar of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. G. W. G
NANATHAYALAN,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kilinochchi,
13th July, 2021.
