NORTH      CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification under Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 of
Provincial Council (Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 989 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
I, Mahiepala Herath, Governor of North Central Province have received the report submitted by Mr. Saman Wickrama
Wickramarachchi retired High Court Judge appointed according to the Section 185(2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987
in order to satisfy that Mr. Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Mahanamage Ajith Prasanna Thennakoon, Chairman of Rambewa
Pradeshiya Sabha has committed one, more or all the offences as per the Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha  Act, No. 15 of
1987, published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2208/48 and dated 01.01.2021, powers vested to Minister of Portfolio under
Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, as powers vested to me in terms of Section 2 of Provincial Council
(Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, after scrutinizing the inquiry report
above, I am in the opinion that Mr. Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Mahanamage Ajith Prasanna Thennakoon is fit to be
reinstated in the post of Chairman of Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha and thereby notify the same.
M
AHIEPALA  HERATH,
 Governor,
North Central Province.
At Anuradhapura,
On 01st April,  2021.
