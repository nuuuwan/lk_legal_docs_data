FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DIMBULAGALA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  returned member in Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Sujith Rathnaweera,
the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member
of   Dimbulagala  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wengappuli Arachchige Sampath Priyantha Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer  of  the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wengappuli Arachchige
Sampath Priyantha Kumara was selected as the member of  the  Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
IUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
13th  January, 2021.
