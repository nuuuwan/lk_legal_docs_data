PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT,  NORTHERN  PROVINCE
Additional Notice for meeting under the Local Authorities (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue powers vested to me
under Sub section No. 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, hereby notify to convene
the meeting again for the election of Chairman of Valvettithurai Urban Council in Northern Province on 22.09.2021 at
10.00 a.m. at the hall of the Valvettithurai Urban Council.
