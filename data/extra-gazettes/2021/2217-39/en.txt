THE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Prime
Minister and Minister of Finance do by this notification ;
01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “ Sri Lanka Music Composers and Directors Association”
situated at No. 190/53H,  Weera Mawatha, Depanama, Pannipitiya as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of
that Ordinance ought to be extended ; and
02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of Section 5(4) of that
Ordinance.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance.
