PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  OF  THE  SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE
Notification of the Honourable Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province
I, Tikiri Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do hereby extend the three months (03) period stated in
Gazette Notification No. 2210/50 dated 15.01.2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, for further three months
from 15.04.2021, which I appointed, Judicial Officer, Herath Mudiyanselage Rohana Anura Kumara, Retired High Court Judge,
as a single person tribunal of inquiry by virtue of powers vested in me by the provisions of Sub-section 184(1A) of the Urban
Council Ordinance, Chapter 255 read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (consequential provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, in
order to inquire and submit me a report within three (03) months from the date of that notification whether any offenses have
been committed under Section 184(1) of the Urban Council Ordinance, Chapter 255, by the Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban
Council, Mr. Wawulanbokka Gamage Lalith Dharmakeerthi.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUWA,
 Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
At the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
15th April,  2021.
