EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE BOPE-PODDALA PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER SECTION 66A(1)(a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Bope-Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being  ceased to be
a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamad Nayim
Hussain Kiyas  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that Ward No. 07 - Kurunduwaththa of Bope-Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thellambure Hettige Kularathne to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Bope-Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Thellambure Hettige
Kularathne elected as the member of the Ward No. 07 - Kuruduwaththa of Bope-Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Bope-Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
01st July, 2021.
