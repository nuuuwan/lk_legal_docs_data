EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance by virtue of the
powers vested in him by the Section 25, read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from
time to time, and published as the Excise Notification No. 06/2021 in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2210/2 dated
January 11, 2021 by amending the Excise Notification No. 986, published in the Extraordinary Gazette, No. 1947/42 of
December 31, 2015 in order to exempt payment of Authorization fee only for year of 2021 to (i) 3 Stars and 4 Stars
Classes Hotels and (ii) 5 Stars Hotel and above Stars Classes/Boutique Hotels: Boutique villas/Heritage Hotels, specified
in the PART II of the above Notification, was passed by Parliament on May 04, 2021.
S. R. A
TTYGALLE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
July 29, 2021.
