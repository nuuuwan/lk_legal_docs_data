PENSIONS   MINUTE
Notification under Section 51 of the Pensions Minute dated 05 February 1934 amended by
Pensions Minute (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICATION
Section 02 and 17 of the Pensions Minute amended from time to time shall further be amended with the inclusion of the
following provision immediately after the above Sections.
“The compulsory age of retirement of the registered medical officers of the government, who serve in public service,
shall be extended up to 61 years of age.”
The amendment made to the Pensions Minute by this Notification shall be treated as effective from 12.10.2020.

and Local Government.
