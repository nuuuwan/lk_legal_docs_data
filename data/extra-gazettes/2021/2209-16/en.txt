FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MATALE    MUNICIPAL     COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Matale Municipal  Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of  Dead ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Jayathunga
Sarath Mahinda Ruberu,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of  that Number 07 Sangamiththa Pedesa of Matale  Municipal Council who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mapa Gunathilaka Mudiyanselage Rohana Kumara Atipola to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Matale Municipal
Council , acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Mapa Gunathilaka Mudiyanselage Rohana Kumara Atipola was elected as the member of  the  Number 07 Sangamiththa
Pedesa of  Matale Municipal  Council.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Matale Municipal  Council.
District Election Office,
Matale,
04th  January, 2021.
