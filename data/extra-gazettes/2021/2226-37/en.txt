PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - PG   5431— 30  (05/2021)
 DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT,  NORTHERN   PROVINCE
Notice for meeting under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue of powers vested to me
under Sub-section No. 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, hereby notify to convene
the meeting for the election to fill the vacant of Chairman of Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha, in Northern Province on
19.05.2021 at 10.30 a. m. at Council Hall of the Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
M
ARIYATHAS PATRICK DIRANJAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Northern Province.
Department of Local Government,
Northern Province,
Elders Home Complex,
A9 Road, Kaithady,
Jaffna,
