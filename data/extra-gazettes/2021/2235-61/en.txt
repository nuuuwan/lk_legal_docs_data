FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NAWAGATHTHEGAMA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to
be a member of party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Herath
Mudiyanselage Ajith Kumara Gunathilake the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that Ward No. 05  Kuruluwewa of Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Udugampalage Chamari Nilangika Thushari to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Udugampalage
Chamari Nilangika Thushari was elected as the member of the Ward No. 05  Kuruluwewa of Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Nawagaththegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
30th of June, 2021.
