THE   FINANCE   ACT,   No. 11  OF  1963
Order under section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 72 read with section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963,1, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do by this Order, vest in the People’s Bank established under the People’s Bank Act, No. 29
of 1961 with effect from 08th March,  two thousand twenty one the property described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
 Minister of Finance.
 Colombo,
08th March, 2021.
SCHEDULE
All that divided and defined allotment of land marked as Lot 1 A being a resurvey of the land called Liyanamuttagewatta alias
Ambagahawatta depicted in Plan No. 803 dated 10.02.2000 made by S.P. Weerasuriya, Licensed Surveyor situated at Kadurupe
Boossa in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 43, Kendala Kadurupe in the Pradeshiya Sabha limits of Rathgama in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Hikkaduwa in the District of Galle in Wellabada Pattuwa in the Southern Province and
bounded on the North by Lot 2, on the East by Road, on the South by Lot 1 B of the same land and on the West by the
remaining part of the same land and Nikathsudu Maradana Kurunduwatta containing in extent ten perches (0A., 0R., 10P.)
together with the fruits, plantations, house and everything standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio L 24/32 in the Land Registry, Galle.
