FILLING   OF   A   V ACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NAMALOYA    
PRADESHIYA    SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)    
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of  return member of  Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kevitiyagalalage 
Dammika Priyantha the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Karuna Pedige Sunil Shantha to ﬁll such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Of ﬁcer  of  the  Namaloya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Karuna 
Pedige Sunil Shantha was selected as the member of the  Namaloya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Namaloya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Ofﬁce,
Ampara,
30th  July, 2021.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN   THE  OFFICE  OF   MEMBER OF  THE  SOORIYAWEWA PRADESHIYA    
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE     
(CHAPTER   262)    
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of  return member of  Sooriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedon Alliance, the recognized political party to which Hetti Thanthrige 
Chandana Kumara the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁed under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hewa Hakuruge Subodha Sanjeewa to ﬁll such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Ofﬁcer  of  the  Sooriyawewa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Hewa Hakuruge 
Subodha Sanjeewa was selected as the member of the  Sooriyawewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
