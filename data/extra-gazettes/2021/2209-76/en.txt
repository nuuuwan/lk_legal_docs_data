THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena  Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare that under Sub-section (1) of Section
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under
the provisions of that Act.
Two (02) allotments of  land depicted in Advance Tracing
No.  Maha/PNW/2017/491  and dated 22.09.2018, prepared
by the  Surveyor - General, situated in the Village of
Udakumbura, in Panwila Grama  Niladhari Division (729) in
Palle Gampaha East minor division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Panwila in Kandy District in Central Province, and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' -  Galpihillawatte - Extent: 0.4047  Hectare
North: Galpihillawatte land owned by the State Plantation
Corporation and Lot B; East: Lot B; South: Lot B, stairs and
Deniye paddy field owned by R. Y . Abeysinghe and others;
West: Deniye paddy field owned by R. Y . Abeysinghe and
others.
02. Lot  'B' -  Galpihillawatte - Extent: 0.0299  Hectare
North: Galpihillawatte land owned by the State Plantation
Corporation and Pradeshiya Sabha Road East: Pradeshiya Sabha
Road; South: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and stairs ; West: Lot A.
