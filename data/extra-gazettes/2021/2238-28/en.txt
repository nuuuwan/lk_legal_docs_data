Excise Notification
FOOLPROOF  STICKER  AND  STICKER  MANAGEMENT  SYSTEM
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance by virtue of the
power vested in him by the Section 32 read with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), by
amending the Excise Notification No. 04/2019 published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2128/30 dated June 20,
2019, as last amended by the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2178/4 dated June 01, 2020 and published as the Excise
Notification No. 01/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2208/34 dated January 01, 2021 was passed by
Parliament on April 20, 
2021.
1. In rule 3,
(a) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the first proviso, for the words and figures "prior to December 31,
2020" of the words and figures "prior to June 30, 2021", and
(b) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the second proviso, for the words and figures "prior to December 31,
2020" of the words and figures "prior to June 30, 2021".
