Where As the office of member of Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the secretary of the Sri lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Godagampala 
Dewage Sampath Sri Nayanajith, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Number 17 - Amunugoda of Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local 
Authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Pothuwila Samaraweera Kankanamge Ayantha Shanjith to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Gampaha Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare 
that Pothuwila Samaraweera Kankanamage Ayantha Shanjith  was elected as the member of the Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. Madhawa,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
30th September, 2021.
