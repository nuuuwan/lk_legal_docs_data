SPECIAL   COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2 and 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the
commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the corresponding
entry in Column II of that Schedule.
However, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy No. 48 of 2007, do hereby
make an order to waive-off balance amount after collecting Rs. 1 per kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 300
per kg on importation of Desicated Coconuts - Other (classified under the HS Code of 0801.11.90), by the Government owned
B. C. C. Lanka Limited up to a total of 13,000 MT’s per month, on the recommendation of the Secretary, Ministry of Industries.
This Order shall be valid for a period of three (03) months commencing from April 28, 2021.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
April  27, 2021.
