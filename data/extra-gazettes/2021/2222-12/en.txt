PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34134— 35 (04/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Kamburupitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Darshana Jathunga
Abeysiriwardhana of  the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha
as he has been  ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
T
HANANTHIRI WITHANAGE POSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matara,
31st  March, 2021.
