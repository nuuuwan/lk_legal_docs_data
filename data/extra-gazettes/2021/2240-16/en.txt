THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 Allotment of Land called Mahagasthota Upper Division 
described as following Lots in Advance Tracing No. NU/
NWE/2021/177 of 17.05.2021, prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in Nuwara Eliya Oya Palatha Korale Minor  
Division, Nuwara Eliya Divisional Secretary’s Division,  
Nuwara Eliya District in Central Province and bounded as 
follows:
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Mahagasthota Upper Division – Extent: 
About 10.2171 Hectares
 North: Road (Estate) and Mahagasthota Upper Division 
claimed by Kalanivally Plantation PLC; East: Mahagasthota 
Upper Division claimed by Kalanivally Plantation PLC and 
Road (Estate); South: Road (Estate); West: Road (Estate) 
and Mahagasthota Upper Division claimed by Kalanivally 
Plantation PLC.
 02. Lot ‘C’ – Mahagasthota Upper Division – Extent: 
About 0.2989 Hectare
 North, East, South and West: Lot A.
