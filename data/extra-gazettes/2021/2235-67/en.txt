FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VA VUNIYA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Thamizh United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Baskaran
Jeyavathany the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of  Vavuniya Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Niruba Sachchithanandam to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Niruba Sachchithanandam
was selected as the member of the Vavuniya Urban Council.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
V avuniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd of July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     THIRAPPANE
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wijesiri
Wimalasurendra the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of  that Ward Number 08 Walagambahuwa of Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thilakarathna Mudiyanselage Thilina Rasanga Thilakarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer  of  the Thirappane  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Thilakarathna
Mudiyanselage Thilina Rasanga Thilakarathna was elected as the member of the Ward Number 08 Walagambahuwa of
Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. M
UGUNTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Thirappane  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
05th of July, 2021.
