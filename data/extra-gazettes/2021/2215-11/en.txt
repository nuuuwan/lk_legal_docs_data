DEPARTMENT  OF  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT  -  EASTERN  PROVINCE
Notice on Summon meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
BY virtue of the Powers vested in me under the Section 66 (G) of the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act as
amended by Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Nagarasa Manivannan the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do
hereby summon the Meetings to be held in the following date and time to elect the new Chairman and Vice Chairman to
fill the vacancies for the post of Vice Chairman of Thambalagamam Pradeshiya Sabha in the Trincomalee District and in
Chairman of Manmunai Pradeshiya Sabha Batticaloa District in the Eastern Province.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
16th February, 2021.
