DEPARTMENT   OF  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  -  NORTHERN  PROVINCE
Notice for meeting  under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of  2012
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue of powers vested to me
under Sub-section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of  2012, hereby notify to convene the
meeting for the election to fill the vacancy of Chairman of Velvettithurai Urban Council in Northern Province on 15.12.2021 at
10.00 a.m. at hall of the Velvettithurai Urban Council.
