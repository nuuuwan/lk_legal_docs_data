PART IV (B) - LOCAL  GOVERNMENT 
 
Notices Under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance 
 
 
Notice made under Section 66c read with Section 66g of Local  Authorities Elections 
Ordinance amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 
 
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to 
the powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section  66g of the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended 
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of  2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of 
Selecting  a new Vice Chairman for  the vacated Vice Chairman's position of  Kotikawatte Mulleri yawa Pradeshiya 
Sabha of  Western Province, will be convened on December 15th of 2021 at 9.30 a.m. at the Pradeshiya  Sabha Chamber 
of Kotikawatte  Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha located at Gothatuwa New Town.  
 
 A.D.P.I. PRASANNA, 
 Commissioner of Local Government, 
 Western Province. 
 
 
 
 
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province, 
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Battaramulla. 
01st December, 2021.
