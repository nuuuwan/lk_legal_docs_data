G 35082 —  70 (10/2021) 
 
UPGRADING OF DISTRICT GENERAL HOSPITAL - KALUTARA AS A TEACHING HOSPITAL 
 
AS per the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers made at the Cabinet meeting held on 05.04.2021, the District General 
Hospital - Kalutara had been identified to establish professorial units to facilitate clinical training of the students by 
upgrading it to a Teaching Hospital. For additional clinical training, Base Hospitals, Horana and Pa nadura have been 
selected. 
 
Accordingly, the District General Hospital - Kalutara is upgraded and re -categorised as Teaching Hospital - 
Kalutara, to provide clinical training for the students of the newly established Faculty of Medicine , University of 
Moratuwa with immediate effect. All these three hospitals will be further developed in order to facilitate clinical training 
of the medical students attached to the Faculty of Medicine, University of Moratuwa. 
  
 
Dr. S. H. MUNASINGHE, 
Secretary,
