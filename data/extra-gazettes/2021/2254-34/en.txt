FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER OF  THE  KUCHCHA VELI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress,  the recognized political party to which Soosaipillai
Mohanathas the member belonged was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ponnaiah Vaiththilingam to fill such vacancy ;
I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Ponnaiah Vaiththilingam was selected
as the member of the Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
17th November, 2021.
