Declaration under Section 5
I , Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena,  Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below
is  needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.

Division of Agalawaththa in District and bounded as follows:

Weerakoon and road; South: Road and Dalkith Estate
(Udawela) claimed by Kotagala Plantations; West: Delkith
Estate (Udawela) claimed by Kotagala Plantations.
