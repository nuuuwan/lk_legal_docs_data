I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.7107 Hectare
depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary Plan No. GA 4503
dated 19.02.2020 prepared by the Superintendent of Survey,
Galle District, situated in the village called Pilana in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 135A of Meegoda in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Akmeemana in Galle District of the
Southern Province and morefully described as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Walahanduwa Watte, Keranketiya Watte
Land; Description: Homestead, A mixed crop cultivation, 13
Permanent buildings and a cemented well are available;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:  Chairman, Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 0.7107 Hectare.

hereby required to appear personally or by agents  duly
authorized in writing, before me at the Akmeemana Divisional
Secretariat on 17.05.2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 03.05.2021
the nature of their interest in the land/lands, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
