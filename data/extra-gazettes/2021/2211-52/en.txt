NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION  16  SUB - SECTIONS  (2D) OF  THE  COAST  CONSERVATION  AND
COASTAL  RESOURCE  MANAGEMENT  ACT,  No. 57  OF  1981
Proposed project on Mining of Heavy Mineral Sand along the Akkaraipattu to Komari Coastal stretch
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Heavy Mineral Sand along the
Akkaraipattu to Komari Coastal stretch has been submitted by the Damsila Export (Pvt.) Ltd., No. 07,  Flower Avenue,
Colombo 07, as required by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Act, No. 57 of
1981 as amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following
institutions from 8.30 a.m. to 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 21.01.2021 (Except weekends and public holidays).
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official website (www.coastal.gov.lk.) of the Department of
Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management.
(1) Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor, Maligawatte,
Colombo 10.
