NOTIFICATION
P.S. No.  PS/CSA/00/1/13/12.
IT is hereby notified that the below-mentioned Ministers ceased to hold the portfolios previously held by them with
effect from 16th August, 2021, following the alterations of the subjects and functions and the change effected in the
composition of the Cabinet of Ministers by His Excellency the President under Article 44(3) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Name Previous Portfolio
1. Hon. G. L. Peiris, M. P. - Minister of Education
2. Hon. Pavithra Devi Wanniarachchi, M. P. - Minister of Health
3. Hon. Dinesh Gunawardena, M. P. - Foreign Minister
4. Hon. Gamini Lokuge, M. P. - Minister of Transport
5. Hon. Keheliya Rambukwella, M. P. - Minister of Mass Media
6. Hon. Dullas Alahapperuma, M. P. - Minister of Power
By Order of His Excellency,
 P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
September 08, 2021.
