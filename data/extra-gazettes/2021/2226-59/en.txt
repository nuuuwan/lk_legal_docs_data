1A-  PG  5432— 125  (05/2021)
Direction No. 77
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Special Directon under Section 10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority hereby directs all importers, refiners, manufacturers, stockists, packers, distributors and
traders of edible Coconut oil sold in bulk or in retail in bottles or packs or containers that such edible Coconut oil shall
not be blended with any other oils/oil.
This Direction shall come into effect from 07th May, 2021.
                                            By  Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAY AKE (Retd.),
Chairman,
Consumer Affairs Authority.
Colombo,
07th of May, 2021.
