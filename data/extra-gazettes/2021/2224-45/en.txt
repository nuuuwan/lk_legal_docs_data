 My Ref. No.: EHE/DS/LND/05/ACQ/Village-Ex/03.

I, do hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(amendment) that the Government intends to acquire  the 
following lands which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Land Details: 
 
The Land in total extent of 3.5314 Hectares depicted 
as Lot No. 453 in Sheet No. 09 Supplement No. 09 in the 
Final village Plan No. 703 dated 2020.06.18 prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called 
Bopeththa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Eheliyagoda in Kuruvita Korale Pallepattuwa in 
Rathnapura District of Sabaragamuwa Province and 
morefully described as follows: 
 
Boundaries: 
North: Lot Nos. 201, 452, 450, 451, 240 and 241; 
East: Lot Nos. 201, 452, 450, 451, 240, 241 and 454; 
South: Lot Nos. 201, 454, and F. V. P. 702 Beruwana; 
West: F. V. P. 702 Beruwana and Lot No. 201. 
 
Original Claimant: Pussellawa Plantation Company. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear  personally, or by agents,  duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Eheliyagoda 
Divisional Secretariat on 28th July 2021 at 10.00                     
a. m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before 14th July 2021 the  nature of 
their interests in the land. The particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. All deeds documents in 
support of the claims should be produced before me at the 
inquiry. 
 
 
   H. C. N. .  DHARMAPALA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
  Eheliyagoda.
