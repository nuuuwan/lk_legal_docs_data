of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by land

Government intends to acquire the following                      
lands/servitudes which are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  Lands to be acquired :  
The allotments of land depicted in the F.V.P. 911 
situated in the village of Buthkanda in the Minor Division 
of Kolonna Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Kolonna, in Ratnapura District, Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
 
F.V.P. 911, Extra No. 4  Sheet No. 04  
 
01. Lot No. 325  
Name of the land: Naigalapalleyaya and Bathalahengoda; 
Claimant: The Government; Extent: 6.5876 Hectares. 
 
02. Lot No. 327  
Name of the land : Kolonna Waththa, Keenathenna 
Waththa, Keenathenna Kumbura; Claimant: Individual 
owner not found;  Extent : 27.6894 Hectares. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat, 
Kolonna at 3.30 p.m. on the day of 30th June 2021. 
 
Further, they should notify \me in writing and in 
duplicate before the day  of 21st June, 2021, the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. Documents should be 
submitted to me on the same date to prove the interests.
