FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     EMBILIPITIYA   URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Embilipitiya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Amila Prabhath
Abenayake,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the
returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Indika Wijesiriwardhana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Embilipitiya Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Indika Wijesiriwardhana
was selected as the member of  the   Embilipitiya Urban Council.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya Urban Council.
District Elections  Office,
Ratnapura,
18th  January, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 19.01.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     GALLE   MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Galle  Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Mohamad
Saukat Mohamad Iyas,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the returned member of  Galle Municipal Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohomad Farsan Hamd to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Galle  Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohomad Farsan Hamd
was selected as the member of  the   Galle  Municipal Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Galle  Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Galle,
18th  January, 2021.
