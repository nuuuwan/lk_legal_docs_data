FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     HOROWPOTHANA      PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Sellage Asoka
Irangani,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of   Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Udukumbure Anusha Priyanganee Senadheera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Udukumbure Anusha Priyanganee Senadheera was selected as the member of  the   Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha.
M.  B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
11th  February, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MINUW ANGODA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Minuwangoda  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   ceased to be
a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Gajanayaka
Mudiyanselage Premila Janaki,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of   Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Madabavitige Siriyawathee to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Minuwangoda  Pradeshiya
Sabha , acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Madabavitige Siriyawathee was selected as the member of  the   Minuwangoda  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda  Pradeshiya Sabha
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
16th  February, 2021.
