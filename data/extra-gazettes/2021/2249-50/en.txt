EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 10/2021
SECURITY  FEATURES  AND  SECURITY   FEATURES  MANAGEMENT  SYSTEM
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 32 read with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance
(Chapter 52), as amended, I, Basil Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do hereby amend the rules in the Excise Notification
No.  07/2021 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2216/16 dated February 25, 2021, as last amended by the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2241/3 dated August 16, 2021 as follows ;
In rule 3,
(a) by the substitution of the first proviso, for the words and figures “after October 15, 2021” of the words and
figures “after January 03, 2022”, and
(b) by the substitution of the second proviso, for the words and figures “before October 01, 2021” of the words
and figures “before April 01, 2022”.
