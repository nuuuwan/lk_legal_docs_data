PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34163— 55 (04/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Pasbage  Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Uyenage Ganga
Thushari de Silva of  the Pasbage  Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as
she has   been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
08th  April, 2021.
