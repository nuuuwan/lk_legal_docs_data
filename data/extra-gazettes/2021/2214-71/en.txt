LAND  DEVELOPMENT  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  320)
Under Section 6(1) of the Land Development Ordinance,
Mr. Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Naveen Harshapriya
Thennakoon is appointed as a Land Officer for the Districts
of Colombo, Kaluthara and Gampaha with effect from
07.09.2020 in addition to the duties of his post. This
appointment will be in effect until he serves in the said post
and if he had been previously appointed as a Land Officer for
another province or District/s, it shall be thereby cancelled.
G. D. KEERTHI GAMAGE,
Land Commissioner General,
Land Commissioner General's Department.
Land Commissioner General's Department,

Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
