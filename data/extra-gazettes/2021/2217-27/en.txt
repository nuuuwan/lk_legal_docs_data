WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
The Inquiry being conducted under Section  184(1A) of  the Urban Councils  Ordinance read with   Section 2   of  the
Provincial Councils  (Consequential  Provisions) Act, No.  12  of 1989
I,  Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of the Western Province do hereby announce that the period of investigation of single
person inquiry board comprise of retired Judge of the Court of Appeal Hon. Chandradasa Nanayakkara, appointed by
Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2152/14 dated 03rd December 2019 to inquiry
whether the offences stipulated under Subsection 184(1) of the aforesaid Urban Councils Ordinance have been committed
by Wattala-Mabola Urban Council and by Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council while functioning in this office and
submit a report within 03 months shall be extended until 03rd June 2021 according to the powers vested in me in terms of
Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)
Act, No. 12 of 1989.
R
OSHAN  GOONETILEKE,
RWP and  BAR VSV USP PHD FIM (Sri Lanka) NDC  PSC,
