FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DIVULAPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Dangallage
Wasantha Lakshman Appuhami the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Malladewage Anil Deshapriya Weerawardhana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Malladewage Anil Deshapriya Weerawardhana was selected as the member of  the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st July, 2021.
