FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     ANAMADUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to be
a member of  party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ladduge Anushka
Priyashantha the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudhiyanselage Dipthi Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Herath Mudhiyanselage
Dipthi Kumara  was selected as the  member of the Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
30th of June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     ANAMADUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to
be a member of  party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Jayakodi
Arachchilage Don Asela Premalal Karunarathna the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of   Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rathnamalala Irugal Bandaralage Rathnamalala to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Rathnamalala Irugal
Bandaralage Rathnamalala was selected as the member of  Anamaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Anamaduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,

Rendage Anton Dilruks Fernando to fill such vacancy ;

66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Rendage Anton Dilruks
Fernando was elected as the member of the Number 01 Munneshvarama of  Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha.
