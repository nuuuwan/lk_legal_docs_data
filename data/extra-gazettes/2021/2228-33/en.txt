1A-  PG 5446— 545 (05/2021)
RECORD  KEEPING  REGULATIONS
The Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of  2002
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 75 of the Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Regulations published for the purpose of Section 64 of the Act, in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2042/21 dated October 26, 2017.
Paragraph 8 of the Regulations is hereby amended by the insertion of following new subparagraphs immediately
after subparagraph (e) :-
“(f) Schedule 06 (Schedule for Export of  Goods)
(g) Schedule 07 (Schedule for Export of Services)”
This Order shall come into effect from April 01, 2021.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
20th May, 2021.
