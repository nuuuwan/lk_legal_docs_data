PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34205— 35 (04/2021)
 NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Devaraj Moni of the Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the People’s Liberation
Front.
P
ULASTHI SERASINGHE PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaraeliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
19th April, 2021.
