PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that
Mr. Appuhamilage Premathilaka of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya
Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
GAMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
14th June, 2021.
