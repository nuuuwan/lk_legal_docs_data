DEPARTMENT  OF   LOCAL  GOVERNMENT — NORTHERN   PROVINCE
Notice for meeting under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Mariyathas Patrick Diranjan, Commissioner of Local Government, Northern Province by virtue of powers vested in me
under Sub Section No. 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, hereby notify to convene the
meeting for the election to fill the vacant of Chairman of  Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha in Northern Province on 22.04.2021,
10.00 am at Council hall of the Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
