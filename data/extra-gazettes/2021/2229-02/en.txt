STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   ACT,   NO.  14  OF 2008
Order under Sub Section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sub section (4) of section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No 14 of
2008 as amended, I Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, further to the order published under sub- section (4) of
Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1782/28 dated October 31, 2012, as amended by subsequent Order published
under sub-Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/7 dated July 04, 2016 in respect of
WelcomHotels Lanka (Private ) Limited, do by this order declare that,
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1782/28 dated October 31, 2012, as amended by
subsequent Order published under sub Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/7 dated July
04, 2016 is hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the project for
a further period of Eighteen (18) months from January 01, 2022:-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct, build and operate a Mixed Utility Complex including a luxury hotel
at Fort, Colombo 01 in the Western Province shall commence commercial operations on June 30, 2023
within the time period from the date of approval of the Mixed Use Project as a Strategic Development
Project under the said Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008 as amended.”
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA, MP,
Minister of Finance.
