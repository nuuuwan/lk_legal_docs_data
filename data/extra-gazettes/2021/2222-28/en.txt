THE  PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
The Annual Feast Pilgrimage of Our Lady of Holy Rosary Shrine, Periyapullumalai - 2021
THE Annual feast and the Pilgrimage of Our Lady of Holy Rosary Shrine, Periyapullumalai 2021.
It is hereby for the information of the pilgrims who attend the above feast and the others concerned that the annual
feast for the year 2021 begins on the 23rd of April, (23.04.2021)  with hoisting of flags, and ends on the 2nd of May with the
concelebrated eucharistic celebration.
The special event of the feast would be a pilgrimage which begins on 30th of April at 3.30 p. m. from St. Mary’s
Cathedral, Puliyanthivu and proceed through Chenkalady, Karadiyanaru and to Pullumalai.
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the above feast and others concerned is drawn to the regulation published
in the Government Gazette No. 9886 of 16.07.1984 which will be inforce during the period of the feast.
N. V
ILV ARETNAM,
