Statement of matter in dispute
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is.
Whether Mr. H. K. Nimal who joined the State Engineering Corporation of Sri Lanka as a Computer Operator in 1981 and

in the post of  Data Entry Supervisor from 01.01.2006 and the deduction of Rs. 15 from salary as per the 1991 salary revision
and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
