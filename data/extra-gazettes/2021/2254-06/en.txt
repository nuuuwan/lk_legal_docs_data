 3. (1) The driver of every vehicle using the User Fee National Highway, shall pay a user fee in respect of the use of 
such User Fee National Highway by such driver, at such rates as specified in Part A of the Schedule to these regulations. 
 
  (2) The user fee shall be charged in respect of the w hole distance, or part thereof, of the User Fee National 
Highway. 
 
  (3) Different user fees shall appl y for different categories of vehicles. 
 
  (4) The User fee shall be paid manually by cash. 
 
 4. (1) Every driver entering the User Fee National Highway from an interchange prior  to being permitted to proceed 
along the User Fee National Highway, shall, obtain a ticket from the teller on duty, at such interchange. 
 
  (2) Every driver who has obtained a ticket under sub regulation (1) of this regulation shall retain such ticket 
securely in his possession until he reaches the paying station of the interchange through which he intends to exit such User 
Fee National Highway.  
 
  (3) Where a vehicle, arrives at a paying station of an interchange for the purpose of exiting such User Fee 
National Highway, the driver of that vehicle shall surrender the ticket obtained at the interchange through which he entered 
the User Fee National Highway, to the Teller. 
 
  (4) The Teller shall inform the driver of a vehicle th e amount of the fee payable and the driver shall pay such 
amount, prior to being permitted to pass through such interchange  and the Teller shall issue a receipt in respect of such 
payment. 
 
  (5) Where the driver is unable to surrender the ticket to the Teller the driver shall pay the fee for the entire 
distance of the User Fee National Highway from the furthest possible point of entry to the actual point of exit and shall be 
charged a user fee at the rates specified in part A of the Schedule to these regulations. 
 
 (5) (1) The Road Development Authority, established under the Road Development Authority Act, No. 73 of 1981, 
(hereinafter referred to as “the Authority”) shall ensure that, for the purpose of implementation of the provisions of these 
regulations, sufficient number of gates are positioned at the required places along the User Fee National Highway. 
 
  (2) The gates so positioned along the User Fee National Highway shall be owned, operated and maintained by the 
Authority, and each gate shall be manned by a Teller. 
  
  (3) The Teller shall be responsible for the collection of th e user fee at every gate referred to in paragraph (2), at 
such rates from the categories of vehicles specified in Part A of the Schedule to these regulations from every driver using 
such User Fee National Highway. 
 
   Provided that, the provisions of these regulations shall not apply to a driver who is driving a vehicle of a 
category specified in Part B of  the Schedule to these regulations, which is exempt from such payment. 
 
 6. The Teller shall at such times as are specified, hand over the day’s collection of the fees to the officer authorized 
in that behalf by the Authority and obtain a receipt in respect of such amount. 
 
 7. The money so collected as user fee shall be credited to the Fund of the Authority and be used for the purpose of 
developing and maintaining the User Fee National Highway to which these regulations apply. 
 
 8. For the purpose of these regulations - 
 
  “ Teller” means an officer who is  manning the gates at an interchange; 
  “ User Fee National Highways” means the Meerigama - Kurunegala User Fee National Highway (Section of 
Central Expressway) from Meerigama  (36.981 km) to Kurunegala (77.891 km).

Regulation 5 
 
VEHICLES EXEMPT FROM PAYMENT OF USER FEE ON THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHWAYS 
 
  1. All vehicles which form part of the Presidential Convoy at any given time for so long as such vehicle is driven 
along the User Fee National Highway. 
 
  2. All Vehicles which form part of the Prime  Ministerial Convoy at any given time for so long as such vehicle is 
driven along the User Fee National Highway. 
 
  3. All Ambulances using the User Fee National Highway.
