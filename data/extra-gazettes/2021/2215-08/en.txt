LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
BY virtue of the Powers vested in me under the section (1) of 66 (C) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and the
provision of section 66 (G) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, I do hereby informed that the names mentioned
below were elected on 10.02.2021 at 09.00 am at the meeting held in the council hall of Thabalagamam Pradeshiya Sabha
in Trincomalee District, on the day of 11.02.2021 at 09.00 am and at 12.00 pm at the meeting held in the Council hall of
Koralaipattu North Pradeshiya Sabha and Eravur Urban Council in Batticaloa District respectively and on the day of
12.02.2021 at 10.00 am and at 02.30 p.m. at the meeting held in the Council hall of Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha and
Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha in Ampara District respectively and declared by me as the new Chairman for the respective
Sabha.
N. M
