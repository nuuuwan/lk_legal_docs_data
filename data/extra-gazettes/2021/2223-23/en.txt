No. 2223/23 –  SATURDAY, APRIL  17,  2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDENT) ACT, No. OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: 3/2/3/10/229. 
 
I, hereby given notice under Sub -section of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the land 
Government intends to acquire the following land/or 
servitude which is required for the St. Mary's College, 
Veyangoda.  
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The block of the land is shown in the plan No. 3732 
prepared by the officer of survey of Gampaha Division on 
behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the village of 
Thalgasmote in No. 328/D Thalgasmote East Grama 
Niladhari Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division 
of the Attanagalla in the District of Gampaha Western 
Province and is bounded as follows. 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Gorakagahalanda ; Description:                 
A well made with cement 20 - 40 years Old Coconut trees, 
20 - 35 Years of age 07 jack trees and mixed cultivation ; 
Claimant: Church of England, 368/3, Bhaudhaloka 
Mawatha, Colombo 07; Extent: 1.2298 Hectare. 
 
All personal interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Attanagalla Divisional Secretariat, on the 
07th day of May, 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 23rd April, 2021 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land/servitude the amount 
of compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount.  
 
 S. P. GUNAWARDENA, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Attanagalla Division. 
 
Attanagalla Divisional Secretariat,  
Nittambuwa,  
08th April, 2021.
