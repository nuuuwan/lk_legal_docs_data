THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 Declaration Under Section 7

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Seven (7) allotments of land in total extent of about 
4.5173 Hectares, depicted in F.V.P. 1359, Sheet No. 26, 
Supplement No. 22 and Lots 260 -267 situated in Nelawa 
in 452 Nelawa Grama Niladhari Division of, 
Mahagalboda Egoda Korale, in Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Ganewatta, Kurunegala District in the North 
Western Province and Bounded as follows: 
  
01. Lot No. 260 - Estate - Kahatagahamulaheena, 
Pinwattahena or Getakulalanda Waththa; Claimant: 
State and Kurunegala Plantation Limited; Extent: 
1.9663 Hectare.  
North: Lot No. R 992 and W 991; East: Lot No. R 
992, 116, Z991, 111 and 112; South: Lot No. 112, 266, 
261, 267 and 265; West: Lot No. R 992 and V 991. 
 
02. Lot No. 261 - Way - Kahatagahamulaheena, 
Pinwattahena or Getakulalanda Waththa; Claimant: 
State; Extent: 0.0014 Hectare.  
North: Lot No. 260; East: Lot No. 266 and 265; 
South: Lot No. 265; West: Lot No. 265 and 267. 
 
03. Lot No. 262 - Way - Kuda Aluthgama Waththa; 
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0132 Hectare.  
North: Lot No. 107; East: Lot No. 107; South: Lot No. 
187 and 263; West: Lot No. 265 and 266. 
 
04. Lot No. 263 - Estate - Yalagamayagamaheena, 
Pinwattahena or Getakulalanda Waththa; Claimant: 
State and Kurunegala Plantation Limited; Extent: 
0.0050 Hectare.  
North: Lot No. 265 and 262; East: Lot No. 187 and 
230; South: Lot No. 230 and 264; West: Lot No. 264.
