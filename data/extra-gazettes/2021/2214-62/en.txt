PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
(CHAPTER  262)  LOCAL GOVERNMENT  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notifications under Section 66 g
IT is hereby announced that the first meeting to elect the Chairman of the Thalawakelle Lindula Urban Council in the
Nuwaraeliya District of the Central Province as per the powers vested in me under Section 66 g of the amended Local
Government Elections Ordinance of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 shall be held at the
Thalawakelle Lindula Urban Council Meeting Hall at 10.00 a.m on 05
th March 2021 at the venue mentioned therein.
H. M. M. U. B. HERATH,
Commissioner,
Department of Local Government,
Central Province.
Provincial Council Complex,
Pallekele,
Kundasale,
