FOREST CONSERVATION ORDINANCE 
The Order 
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) that has to be read  
with the notification published in Gazette Extraordinary No.2187/27 dated 09th August 2020 by the President according 
to the constitution 43(1),44(2) and  46(1)(a),(b) of the  constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka 
respectively ,I do by this order declare  the state land named Sinnakulam  of  56.86 hectares bounded by the limits set 
forth in the schedule here to as a Reserved Forest  from the 26th day of November 2021. 
WIMALAWEERA DISSANAYAKE,  
State Minister of Wildlife Protection,  
Adoption of Safety Measures including the  
Construction of Electrical Fences and Trenches and 
Reforestation and Forest Resources Development.   
Battaramulla, 
State   Ministry   of Wildlife   Protection,  
Adoption of Safety Measures including the  
Construction of Electrical Fences and Trenches and 
Reforestation and Forest Resources Development.   
26th day of November 2021,
