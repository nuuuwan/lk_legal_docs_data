THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 Reference No. : MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/53.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.:  4-3/9/2017/WS/379.
IT is hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7,
of  the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land or servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Below mentioned land depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No.
PPMR/2855 situated in the Village of Katuwangoda, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division in Matara District
in Southern Province, was acquired for the construction of
the Thuluman Linda and broadering of the access road to
water pumping station under the Matara 4th step of water
supply scheme.
 Schedule
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land:  Aralugaha Koratuwa; Description of
the land: Open bare land; Initial claimants of the land:
01. W. A. K. Sarathchandra, Gemattige Watta, Katuwangoda,
Palatuwa; 02.  Nalani Hatharasingha, Aralugahakoratuwa,
Katuwangoda, Palatuwa; Extent: 0.0026 Hectare.
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a land/servitude is to be acquired personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at Malimbada
Divisional Secretariat on the 25th day of February, 2021 at
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 18th day of February, 2021
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
K. G. R. J
AY ANATH,
Divisional Secretary,
Malimbada.
Divisional Secretariat,
Malimbada,
26th day of January, 2021.
