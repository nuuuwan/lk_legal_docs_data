THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor  G. L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 420/25 of September 26,
1986 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2160/39 of January 30, 2020) establishing the
Eastern University, Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after entry “12. Department of English Language Teaching”
appearing in Column II of the Schedule, which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Arts and Culture” appearing in Column
I of that Schedule of the following new entry : -
“13. Department of Tamil Studies ”.
Professor G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
18th March, 2021.
