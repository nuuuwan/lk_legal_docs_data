THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor  G . L. Peiris, the
Minister of Education having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the National School of
Business Management Limited, a Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing
Registration Number PB 4833, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2164/54 of February 27, 2020 by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of
Business Management in Tourism, Hospitality and Events” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column
II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions
specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
Conditions
1. National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programmes specified
in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science Honours in Biomedical Science (SLQF 6) and Bachelor of
Science Honours in Public Health and Nutrition (SLQF 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of three (3)
simple passes (S) for Biology, Chemistry and Physics or Agricultural Sciences in one and the same sitting at the
GCE (Advanced Level) Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent
qualification ;
