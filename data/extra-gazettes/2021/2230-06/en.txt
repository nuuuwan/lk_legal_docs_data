THE  REGISTRATION OF ELECTORS ACT, No.44 OF 1980
IN pursuance of Section 12(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, it is hereby notified that the revision of
registers of electors for the year 2021 has commenced, on 01st June 2021.
02. Copies of registers of electors now in force or portions thereof will be exhibited by Registering Officers in their
offices and following offices.

04. Details of persons eligible to be registered as electors for the year 2021 are based on the 2020 electoral register,
and as in previous years, enumerators do not issue BC forms to chief occupants due to the COVID-19 epidemic.  Action will
be taken to obtain the details of persons who have to be deleted from the 2021 electoral register, from the electoral register
operated as of 01st June 2021 and the persons who are to be included in the 2021 electoral register in a special format. These
special forms can be obtained from the exhibition points mentioned in the second paragraph above.
