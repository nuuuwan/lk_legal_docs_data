THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon  Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of Lands, do
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of the land called, "Galhinne pathana", (F.V .P. 27)
in total extent about 4.8562 Hectares depicted in Advance Tracing
No. B/ELL/2019/428 of   14.10.2019 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the village of "Udukumbalwela" in 69B Heeloya
Grama Niladhari Division of "Kumbalwela Korale" in Ella Divisional
Secretary's Division in Badulla District at Uva Province, and
boundaries are follows:
01. Lot  'A' -  Galhinne Pathana - Extent: About 4.8562
Hectares
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Road, Galgodahinne pathana and
Road; South: Road, Galgodahinne pathana, Road and Galhinne
pathana (Remainng part of this land); West: Galhinne pathana
(Remaining part of this land) and Road (R.D.A.)
.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands.
