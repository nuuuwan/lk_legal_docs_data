muNIcIPAL cOuNcIL uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1) OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS

Whereas the office of  return member of Bandarawela Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group Number 02, to which Gungamuwe Yaddehi Appuhamillage 
Sarathchandra Padma Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, 
to decide upon returned member of Bandarawela Municipal Council who belongs to such Independent Group Number 02 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared 
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group Number 02, has communicated his decision to nominate 
Korawage Nimmi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer of the Bandarawela Municipal Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Korawage Nimmi was 
selected as the member of the Bandarawela Municipal Council.   
 
H. I. R. HatHuRusIngHa,
