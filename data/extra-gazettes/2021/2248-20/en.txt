FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NARAMMALA      PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Kithalawa
Arachchige Nalin Chandima Sugathadasa the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that 03 -  Narammala of Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Chandrasekara Mudiyanselage Ranbanda  to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, Returning Officer  of  the Narammala
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Chandrasekara Mudiyanselage Ranbanda was elected as the member of the 03 - Narammala of  Narammala
Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. M. H. B. R
ATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
04th October, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RIDEEGAMA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Hammadu
Muhammadu Assar the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamed Nazeer Mohamed Azad  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer  of  the Rideegama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Mohamed Nazeer Mohamed Azad was selected as the member of the  Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ATHNAYAKA MUDIYANSELAGE HARENDRA BANDARA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
09th  September, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KOTAGALA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kotagala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resigning ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ceylon Workers’ Congress (P. Wing), the recognized political party to which
Marimuththu Nagulesvary the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of  Kotagala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Annasi Sandiravadani  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kalugampitiya Appuhamilage Lahiru Shanaka Kalugampitiya, Returning Officer  of  the Kotagala
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Annasi Sandiravadani was selected as the member of the  Kotagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALUGAMPITIYA APPUHAMILAGE LAHIRU SHANAKA KALUGAMPITIYA,
Returning Officer,
