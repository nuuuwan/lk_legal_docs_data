NOTIFICATION UNDER SECTION 16 SUB - SECTIONS (2D) OF THE COAST
CONSERV ATION AND COASTAL RESOURCE MANAGEMENT ACT No. 57 OF 1981
Proposed Recreational Beach Area/Sea Water Front Development Project - Phase 1 from Colpetty to Dehiwala 
Canal Outlet
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on Proposed Recreational Beach Area/Sea 
Water Front Development Project - Phase 1 from Colpetty to Dehiwala Canal Outlet has been submitted by the Urban 
Development Authority, Sethsiripaya, Battaramulla, as required  by the Section 16 (1) of the Coast Conservation and Coastal 
Resource Management Act No. 57 of 1981 as amended by the Act No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for 
inspection by the Public at the following institutions from 8.30 a.m. to 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 15.12.2021 
(Except weekends and public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official web site (www.coastal.gov.lk.) of the Department of 
Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management.
