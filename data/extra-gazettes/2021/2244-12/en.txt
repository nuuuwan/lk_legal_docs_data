THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT  No.  2  OF  1980
 Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provision) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development and Housing, being of the opinion that the land described more
fully in the Schedule hereto are urgently required to implement Urban Development Project under the Thalawathugoda
Town Development Plan do by this order, declare that the said lands are required for such purpose.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
August 06 ,2021.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land called Mahawela at Kalalgoda in extent of 2.7837 Ha. shown as Lot Nos. 01 to 08 in Advanced
Tracing No. CO/MHR/2018/775 dated 05.02.2019 prepared by Surveyor General situated in a village of Kalalgoda within
Grama Niladhari Division 493 Kalalgoda in Divisional Secretariat Division of Maharagama in the District of Colombo,
Western Province.
