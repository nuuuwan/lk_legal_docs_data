FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE  PELIYAGODA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  retrurn member of  Peliyagoda Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kavindha Ranganatha
Ranatunga the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  that Number 05 - Rohana Vihara Place of Peliyagoda Urban Council who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Pahindara Dewage Nalin Chandima to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Peliyagdoa Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Pahindara Dewage Nalin Chandima  was selected as the  member of the ward Number 05 - Rohana Vihara
Place of Peliyagoda Urban  Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Peliyagoda Urban  Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
08th October, 2021.
