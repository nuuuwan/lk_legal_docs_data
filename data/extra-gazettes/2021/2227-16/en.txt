THE  HOLIDAY  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
Order under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Chamal Rajapaksa, State
Minister of Home Affairs, do by this order declare that, as regards the year 2022, each of the days specified in the
Schedule hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
 C
HAMAL RAJAPAKSA,
State Minister  of Home Affairs.
State Ministry of Home Affairs,
“Nila Medura”,
Elvitigala Mawatha,
Narahenpita,
Colombo 05,
