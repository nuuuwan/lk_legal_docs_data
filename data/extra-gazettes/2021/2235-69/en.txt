CUSTOMS NOTIFICATION
Notification made by the Director General of Customs in terms of Section 51 of the Customs Ordinance
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 51 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), i, Ganwarige Vijitha ravipriya, 
director General of Customs do hereby notify that the declaration (hereinafter called and referred to as Value declaration) 
to be made by the importer or his agent as stipulated by Section 51 of the Customs Ordinance, shall be on a form as set 
out in the Schedule hereto on white colour paper of 210mm x 297mm (a4) in dimensions and 80 grams per square meter  
in density.
The value declaration shall be the affirmation of the value of each such article stated in the Bill of Entry delivered 
to the director General of Customs in relation to such articles as required by Section 47 of the Customs Ordinance and the 
particulars furnished in such Value declaration shall be supported by such relevant documents.
The value declaration may also be delivered electronically to the director General of Customs through the automated 
Customs Clearance System provided by the director General of Customs. However, until a paperless clearance system is 
introduced, a duly signed copy of such Value declaration printed through the said automated Customs Clearance System 
shall be delivered to the Director General of Customs along with the relevant Bill of Entry.
This Notification will come into effect from 12
th July 2021.
The Notification made by the Director General of Customs in terms of the Section 51 of the Customs Ordinance 
and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1260/16 of 07th January 2003 is hereby rescinded.
 Major General (retired) G. V . Ra vipriya, 
 director General of Customs.
Sri lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
08
th July 2021.
