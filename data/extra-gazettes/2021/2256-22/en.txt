FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE 
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI LANKA
WHereas, the secretary General of Parliament has informed the e lection Commission, under section 64(I) of the  
Parliamentary elections act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Ninth Parliament by reason 
of resignation of Mr.  Mahinda Buddhadasa Samarasinghe, who was declared elected as a member of Parliament for the 
electoral district No. 3 – Kalutara under section 62 of the aforesaid act; 
	 And	 Whereas,	the	Returning	Officer,	for	the	electoral	district	which	returned	the	said	member	was	directed	by	the	
Election	Commission	under	Section	64(1)	of	the	Parliamentary	Election	Act,	No.	1	of	1981,	to	fill	this	vacancy	as	provided	
for under sub article 99(13)(b)  of the Constitution; 
	 And	 Whereas,	the	said	Returning	Officer	acting	under	Section	64(2)	of	the	Parliamentary	Election	Act,	No.	1	of	
1981, and in terms of sub article 99(13)(b) of the Constitution has made a return in Form “m” set out in the First schedule 
to the aforesaid act, to the effect that Mr. Wadduwage Manju Lalith Varna Kumara was declared elected as a member 
of Parliament for the said electoral district;

Now therefore, the election Commission, in the exercise of the powers vested in the election Commission by section 
64 (2) of the Parliamentary elections act, No. 1 of 1981, does hereby notify  that Mr. Wadduwage Manju Lalith Varna 
Kumara has	been	declared	elected	as	a	Member	of	the	Ninth	Parliament	for	the	Electoral	District	No.	3	–	Kalutara	to	fill	the	
vacancy caused by reason of resignation of Mr.  Mahinda Buddhadasa Samarasinghe.
