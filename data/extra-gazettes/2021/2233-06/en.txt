FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   mEmBER   OF   ThE    PANDuwASNuwARA 
PRADEShIYA SABhA uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1)(a) OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS   
ORDINANcE    (chAPTER   262)    
Whereas the office of  member of Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha 
Pediyadurayalage Sunil Jayaweera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 2 - Kevitiya of Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party 
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared 
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate 
Delana Mudiyanselage Ajith Chandrarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that                             
Delana Mudiyanselage Ajith Chandrarathna was selected as the member of the 2-Kevitiya of Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya 
Sabha.   
 
GamalathGe Dilip NishaNtha,
 Returning Officer,
 Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
18th  June, 2021.
