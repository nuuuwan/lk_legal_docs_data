The  Award
The Honourable Minister of Labour Relations and Trade
Union Relations, do by virtue of the powers vested in him by
Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of
the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 revised edition)
as amended by Acts Nos. 14 of 1957,  4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968
read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37
of 1968 appointed me as Arbitrator by his order dated 7th
February, 2019 and referred the following disputes  to me for
settlement by arbitration.
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is -
“Whether the transfer, non-payment of salary for March,
2016, 1/2 the salary of April, May and June months of the
same year and the salary increment for year 2016 to Mrs. L. G .
D. Gunathilaka, an employee of the Spring Valley Estate, under
Balangoda Plantation PLC following the disciplinary inquiry
against her are just and if not so, to what relief she is entitled.”
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA
Appearence :
Mrs. L. G. D. Gunathialake appeared on her behalf.
The Superintendent of Spring Valley Estate, Mr. Brandly
Mayura Amunugama appeared for Spring Valley Estate.
Party of the 1st Part filed 1st Statements
At the inquiry the Parties agreed for the following
settlement.
The Respondent, Spring Valley Estate paid a sum of
Rupees Seventy One Thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety
(Rs. 71,790/-) to Mrs. L. G. D. Gunathilake, the Party of the
Frist Part as full and final payment.
In the circumstances I make no order.
KAPILA MANAWASINGHE SARATHCHANDRA,
Arbitrator.
28th  September, 2020.
