Whereas the office of  return member of Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Yaman 
Gedara alias Mudunkoth Gedara Wijitha Sugathadasa the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1) of 
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and 
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared 
selected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Parakrama Harishchandrage Wathsala Sanjeewani Karunarathne to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Hewage Dhanushka Naleen Sampath Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Kundasale Pradeshiya 
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Parakrama Harishchandrage Wathsala Sanjeewani Karunarathne was selected as the member of the Kundasale Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
