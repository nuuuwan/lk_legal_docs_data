THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
File No. of the Land Ministry. : 4-3/5/2018/HA/56.
File No. of the Divisional Secretary.:  Ben/2/7/3/30.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land named as "Morakatiyawatta alias
Millagahawatta" in total extent of 0.0253 (10 Perches) Hectare
depicted as Lot No. 01 in Plan No. G 4588 of 31.12.2020 made
by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of "Marumulla"
in the Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 12F - Viyanduwa in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Bentota, District of Galle,
Southern Province. The name and extent of the allotment of
land is as follows:-
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: "Morakatiyawatta alias Millagahawatta";
Current Land Use: Home Garden, temporary cultivation and a
permanent building; Claimant: Galbada Arachchige Redin of
No. 281/A, Viyanduwa, Haburugala.
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Bentota on the 21st day of April,
2021 at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of April, 2021
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
BANDULA PEETHIRAJ ABEYSEKARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Bentota.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Bentota,
10th March, 2021.
