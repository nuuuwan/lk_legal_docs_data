Department of Local Government -Eastern Province
NOTICE   ON   SUMMON   MEETING   OF  UNDER   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  22  OF  2012
BY virtue of power vested on me under  Section 66(G) of Local Authorities  Elections as amended by Act, No. 22 of 2012,
I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby summon the meeting to fill
the vacancies arose as a result of resigning the post of Vice Chairman of the Koralaipattu West  Pradeshiya Sabha,  Batticaloa
District,  Eastern  Province  at   10.00  a.m.  on   29.04.2021  at Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall for the purpose
of elect the new Vice  Chairman.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
