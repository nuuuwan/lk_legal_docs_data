PART  IV(A) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIS T REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 02.11.2021 
Rules 
 
1. These rules shall be imposed to amend the Rules on Procurement Affairs relating to Western Provincia l Fund, 
announced by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2186/25 dated 30th July, 2020 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka. 
 
2. These rules shall be called as Amended Rules on Procurement Affairs relating to Western Provincial Fund. 
 
3. The words; "to Ten Million" stated under first Sub-section in Section 7 of the Rules on Procurement Affairs relating 
to Western Provincial Fund announced under Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2186/25 dated 30th July, 2020 of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be amended hereby by substitution of the words "to Fifteen 
Million". 
 
4. In case any inconsistency between the Sinhala, English and Tamil texts of these rules should rise, t he Sinhala text 
shall prevail.
