FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KANDY   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Kandy Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   being ceased to be  a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive  Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present), the recognized
political party to which Marimuththu Pillei Wigneswaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at
present) who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Nagaraja Vimalendran to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Kandy Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Nagaraja Vimalendran was selected as the member of  the   Kandy Municipal Council.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Kandy,
21st  April, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RAJGAMA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of    member of  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Thotavaththage Kamal Shantha Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section   66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that No. 09 - Katudampe of  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Deegoda Gamage Tyurin to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Deegoda Gamage
Tyurin  was selected as the member of  the  Ward No. 09 - Katudampe of Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Galle,
20th  April, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AKURANA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of    member of  Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Zeyyad Ibrahim
Mohomed Fuwad the member belonged, was required by me under Section   66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Ward Number 6 Niralla of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ulugedera Seiyadu Mohammadu Mohammadu Hussain to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Akurana  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Ulugedera Seiyadu Mohammadu Mohammadu Hussain was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 6  Niralla of Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
