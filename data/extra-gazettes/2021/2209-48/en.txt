FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MATALE   PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  returned member in Matale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  elected as Parliament
Member ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which  Nilantha
Nalaka Bandara Kottegoda,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of  that Ward Number 10 Dorakumbura of  Matale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudiyanselage Lansakara Gedara Kithsiri Bandara Herath to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Matale Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Herath Mudiyanselage Lansakara Gedara Kithsiri Bandara Herath was elected as the member of  the  Ward Number 10
Dorakumbura of  Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Matale,
07th  January, 2021.
