FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AKURANA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Muhammadu Ismail
Mohommak Mansoor the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that Ward No. 12 - Pallewelikatiya of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudiyanselage Shantha Seneviratne to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Akurana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Herath Mudiyanselage Shantha Seneviratne was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 12 - Pallewelikatiya  of
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election  Office,
Kandy,
10th  May, 2021.
