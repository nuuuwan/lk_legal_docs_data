PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance Notices
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (A) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Beliaththa   Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer of the Beliaththa Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kotte Hewage Janaka Krishantha of the
Beliaththa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
R
A VINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Beliaththa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota.
14th of June, 2021.
