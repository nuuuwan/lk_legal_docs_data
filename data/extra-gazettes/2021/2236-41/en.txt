EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDMENT BY THE LAND ACQUISTION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964  
 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 
 Reference No.: IMDS/LND/05/ACQ/UNI/SAB(1).    
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub-section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the Government 
intend to the following land/servitude which is required 
for a public purpose.  
 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :- 
Acquire the land for the hostel of the University of 
Sabaragamuwa. 
 
The allotment of land in extent about 1.3150 Ha. Lot 
424 supplementary No. 17 in F. V. P. 571 situated at 
Muththettuwegama Village, Muththettuwegama G. S. 
Division Talapitagampattuwa, Kadawatha Korale, 
Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat, Rathnapura District and 
Sabaragamuwa Province. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land / land on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at the Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat on 
23.08.2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby request to notify 
to me in writing (in duplicate)  on or before the 
29.07.2021 the nature of their interest in the land/
servitude, the amount of claims for compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
 B. M. B. M. A. Batugedara, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
                                                Land Acquiring Officer. 
 Imbulpe. 
 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Imbulpe. 
09th July, 2021.
