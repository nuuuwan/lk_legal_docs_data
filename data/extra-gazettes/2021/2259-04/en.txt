THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7

I, do hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be Aquired : 
 
An allotment of Land in total extent of about 0.3280 
Hectare depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 4265 
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Survey 
Gampaha District on behalf of the Surveyor General, 
situated in the Village called Bandiyamulla in the Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 234 of Bandiyamulla North on 
Meda Pattu Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Gampaha in Gampaha District of Western 
Province and morefully described as follows: 
 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
 Lot  No. 01  
Name of the Land: Alubogahawatte; Description: 
Home Garden, a permanent building and a Masonry Well 
are available; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 
Prasanna Athulathmudali, No. 194/02, Rayyadoluwa 
Watte, Uggalboda, Gampaha; Extent: 0.3280 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Gampaha at 10.00 as per the date given below,  
 
Date of Inquiry 
 
Lot No.                               Date 
 
01                            20.01.2022

compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
