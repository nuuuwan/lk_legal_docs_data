THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY  THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO.  28 OF 1964  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 
My No.: Kadu/Land/03/145/UDA. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No. : 4-3/11/2016/UDA/452. 
 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude, which is required for a public purpose.  
 
 
Description of the Lands to be Acquired:- 
 
 
The Allotment of land in extent of 0.0276 Hectare 
described below and depicted as Lot No. 1 in Preliminary 
Plan Co. 9904 prepared by the Surveyor General situated 
in the village of Thalangama North in 477, Thalangama 
North A Grama Niladhari Division, Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Kaduwela, Colombo District, Western 
Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: cannot be found; Description: Garden; 
Claimant: State; Original Cl aimant: Claimant cannot be 
traced; Extent: 0.0276 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Kaduwela on 27th October, 2021 at 9.00 a.m.  
 
And are hereby requested to  notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate)  on or before the 20th October 2021, the 
nature of their interests in the land/servitude, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. All deeds and other documents in support of the 
claims should be produced at the inquiry. 
 
 
  
 H. Chatura Mihidum, 
    Divisional  Secretary, 
       Kaduwela. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Kaduwela.
