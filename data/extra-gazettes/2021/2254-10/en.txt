Whereas the office of member of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Niriella 
Thanapathige Samantha Rangajeewa Jayasingha the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of 
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that ward No. 06 Kudabuthgamuwa of Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa 
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Kalubowila Appuhamilage Don Vipula Nanayakkara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer  of  the Kotikawatta 
Mulleriyawa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 
262) do hereby declare that Kalubowila Appuhamilage Don Vipula Nanayakkara  was elected as the member of the ward     
No. 06 Kudabuthgamuwa of  Kotikawatta Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
   
 M. M.  S. K. B
