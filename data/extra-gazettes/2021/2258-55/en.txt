PART IV (A) - PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  
PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  NOTIFICATION 
 
ANNOUNCEMENT  MADE  BY  HON.  GOVERNOR,  CENTRAL  PROVINCE 
 
In Accordance with the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka 
 
Delegation of power under the Co-operative Societies Statute No. 10 of 1990 of the Central Provincial Council as 
amended by the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Statute No. 4 of 1993 of the Central Provincial Council. 
 
I, Lalith U. Gamage, as the Governor of  the Central Provincial Council, in accord ance with the powers vested in me by 
the Article 154 (c) of the Cons titution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in terms of Sub-section 2 (2),  
2 (4) of Co-operative Societies Statute No. 10 of 1990 of the Central Provincial Council as amended by the Co-operative 
Societies (Amendment) Statute No. 04 of 1993 of the Central Provincial Council, I hereby declare that powers referred in 
Column III will be delegated to the Deputy Commissioner of  Co-operative Development (Sri Lanka Administrative 
Service) of the Central Provincial Council referred to in Column No.I below with effect from the date specified in 
Column II. 
 
  LALITH U. GAMAGE, 
 The Governor, 
 Central Province.
