FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NALLUR   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  retrurn member of  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Yulis Appuhamy Sowntharanathan the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Nallur
Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated her decision to nominate Viyajakumar
Sinthuja  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Chrisity Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Viyajakumar Sinthuja was
selected as the member of the Nallur  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
04th October, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NALLUR   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Vinayakamoorthi Gnanenthiran the  member  belonged,
was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Nallur Pradeshiya
Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Ravithas
Komathy  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Chrisity Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Ravithas Komathy was
selected as the member of the Nallur  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NANATTAN  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Nagoor Meera
Razik Pareethu the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of  Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Benadict Jackopillai to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, K. Vimalaruban , Returning Officer  of  the Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A
(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Benadict Jackopillai was selected as the
member of the  Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. V
IMALARUBAN,
Returning Officer,
Nanattan Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mannar,
04th October, 2021.
