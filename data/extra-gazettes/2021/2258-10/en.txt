ANNuAL   FEAST OF  ST.  SEbASTIAN’S  NATIONAL  ShRINE,  KANDANA - 2022
Gampaha  District
IT is hereby announced that the Annual Feast of St. Sebastian’s National Shrine, Kandana will be held on 20th January 2022 
and the duration of the feast will be from 03.01.2022 to 25.01.2022 for the public to know.
 1. The attention of the general public is drawn to the regulations published in Gazette Notification No. 2135/34 dated  
06th August 2019 under the Pilgrimage ordinance 175 in this regard.
 
 2. Arrangements have been made to hold this year’s festival with the participation of a limited number of people in 
accordance with the quarantine laws on the instructions of the health Sector under the program to control the spread 
of Covid 19 epidemic disease in the country.
 
 3. These publicity-issued laws will be valid during the period of this ceremony.
 
 
PradeeP Yasarathne,
 district Secretary/Government Agent (Act.)
 Gampaha. 
At district Secretariat - Gampaha,
