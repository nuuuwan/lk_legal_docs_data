No. 2222/63 –  FRIDAY, APRIL  09,  2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Declaration under  Section  7 
 
My Reference No.: KHDS/LND/03/ACQ/Other/16. 
Ministry of Land Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2015/L/223. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act (Amendment) No. 28 of 1964 
that the Government intends to acquire the following lands 
which are required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired:- 
 
The Allotments in total extent of about 0.2074 Hectare 
of land in Preliminary Plan No. 12 Supplement No. 30, 
Sheet No. 28 and dated 31.12.2020, prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the village of Wellandura of 
Pannilpattu Minor Division of Atakalan Korale of the 
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kahawatta in 
Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province : 
 
01. Lot No. 697 
Name of Land: Mahayaya ; Description: About 20 years 
old a Teak tree, there are also temporary crops, a two story 
Building and there is a temporary car park ; Claimant: 
Kahawatta Plantation Company Ltd, No. 52, Maligawatta 
Road, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.1881 Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 698 
Name of Land: Mahayaya ; Description: There are also 
temporary crops; Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation Company 
Ltd, No. 52, Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10 ; Extent: 
0.0193 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before 
me at Divisional Secretariat, Kahawatta on the 19th day 
of May 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
12th May 2021 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
 B. T. GAYANI I. KARUNARATHNA, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Kahawatta. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Kahawatta,  
05th April, 2021.
