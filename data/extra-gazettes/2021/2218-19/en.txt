THE  BOARD  OF  INVESTMENT  OF  SRI  LANKA   LA W,  No.  4  OF  1978
Order under Section 22A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 22A of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law, No. 4 of 1978 as
amended , I  Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order declare the
area specified in Schedules A and B hereto to be a Licensed Zone for the purposes of this Law.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
23rd  February 2021,
Colombo.
