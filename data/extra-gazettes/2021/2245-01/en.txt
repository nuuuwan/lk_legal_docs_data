PART IV (A) : PROVINCIAL COUNCIL 
 
Provincial Council  Notifications 
PG 5542 —  25 (09/2021) 
PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  OF  THE  NORTHERN  PROVINCE 
 
Notification of the Honourable Governor of the Northern Province 
 
Order under Sub section 185(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the  Provincial 
Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 
 
According to the inquiry report dated 02.08.2021 which was handed over to me with its finding, having considered the 
evidence and documents of the prosecution after an independent inquiry by Mr. Kandiah Ariyanayagam, Retired 
Judiciary Officer, who was appointed by me under Sub section 02 of Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 
1987 in order to inquire and submit to me a report whether there is any proof of any Disqualifications as per Section 185
(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 committed by the Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha of  Mannar,  
Mr. Sahul Hameed Mohamed Mujahir, in his capacity as the Chairman and a member of the Pradeshiya Sab ha, Mannar 
by virtue of powers vested in me by the provisions of Sub section 185(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act , No. 15 of 1987 
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989; 
 
As I am satisfied that there is sufficient proof of the disqualification as stated in Sub section 18 5(1) (c) (a) of the 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 including abuse and misuse of powers of office against the Chai rman of 
|Pradeshiya Sabha, Mannar, Mr. Sahul Hameed Mohamed Mujahir in discharge of the duties and responsib ilities of the 
post of  Chairman of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha,  
 
I, Mrs. Piencia Sarojinidevy Manmatharajah Charles, Governor of  the Northern Province do hereby dismiss Mr. Sahul 
Hameed Mohamed Mujahir from the posts of Chairmanship and the membership of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sa bha with 
effect from 14 of September 2021, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section 185(1) of the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989: 
 
 PIENCIA SAROJINIDEVY MANMATHARAJAH CHARLES, 
 Governor of the Northern Province. 
  
Governor's Secretariat, 
Old Park, 
Chundikuli, 
Jaffna. 
13th September, 2021.
