TRUSTS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “Chithambara College P. S. A. Trust ” namely -
01. Siththiravelu Kurukulalingam,
02. Kanthasamy Ponnambalam,
03. Sinnadurai Sivanesan,
04. Sinnadurai Sriskandarajah,
05. Thiruchittampalam Yogasabapathipillai,
06. Dr. Tharmalingam Koneswaran,
07. Tharmalingam Karunakaran,
08. Thillaiampalam Murugathasan.
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87);
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, M.U.M. Ali Sabry, Minister of Justice,
do by this Order authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees, and direct and declare that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
Declaration of Trust bearing No. 170 of August 22, 2019 attested by Saravanan Neelakandan, Notary Public of
Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style of the
“Chithambara College P.S.A. Trust”;
