THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G . L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this  Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act,  establishing the South Eastern University of Sri Lanka published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996, by the addition immediately after the  entry “(g) Department of Sociology”
appearing in Column II of the Schedule II to such Order, which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Arts and Culture”
appearing in Column I of that Schedule to such Order, of the following new entry :-
“(h) Department of Information Technology”
Prof. G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
25th February, 2021.
