PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34404— 45  (06/2021)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Kariyawasam Dewarakkottage Janaka Prasanna of the
Bentota  Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of
the United National Party .
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
21st May, 2021.

NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Talawakelle Lindula Urban Council
I, Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Talawakelle Lindula Urban Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Mr. Milvaganam Paramanadan of the

member of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna.

Returning Officer,
Talawakelle Lindula Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
