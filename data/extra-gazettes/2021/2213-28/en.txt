PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 33642— 35 (02/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Colombo Municipal Council
I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Thennakon Rusiripala of  the Colombo
Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that  council  as he has  ceased to be a member of the United People’s
Freedom Alliance.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City,
02nd February, 2021.
