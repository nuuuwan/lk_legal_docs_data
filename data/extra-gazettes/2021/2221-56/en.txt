SPORTS ACT, No. 25 OF  1973
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 39 - (1) of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, 1, Namal Rajapaksa, Minister of
Youth and Sports do hereby prescribe the following special direction.
1. The term of office of the officers of the Executive Committee of Sri Lanka Cricket extended as per the Gazette
Extraordinary dated 17th February 2021 bearing No. 2215/30  and in terms of Section 39 (1) of the Sports Act,
No. 25 of 1973 ends on April 05, 2021.
2. Until the Sri Lanka Cricket Elections scheduled to be held on 20th of May, 2021 and until the elections are duly held
on the due date, in order to carry out the necessary administrative and necessary activities, 1 notify that the
following persons have been appointed to function and operate as a Management Committee with effect from the
06
th of April, 2021 to the 20th of May, 2021.
3.
i. Prof. Arjuna de Silva (President)
ii. Mr. Ashley de Silva (Secretary)
iii. Mr. Sujeewa Mudalige (Treasurer)
iv. Mr. Uchitha Wickramasinghe AAL (Committee Member)
v. Mr. Amal Edirisooriya (Observer)
4. While the above Management Committee is not empowered to incur any expenditures except for those essential for
administrative development activities already planned up to the 20th May, 2021, I do hereby direct and order that
prior written approval of the Secretary to the Ministry of Youth and Sports should be obtained if such a need arises
for any such expenditure.
N
AMAL RAJAPAKSA, (M.P),
Minister of Youth and Sports.
at Colombo,
0n this 01st day of April, 2021.
