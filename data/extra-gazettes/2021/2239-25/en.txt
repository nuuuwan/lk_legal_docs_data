Part II
10. Grade Promotion
10.1. Promotion to Grade II from Grade III
10.1.1. Under the Average Performance
10.1.1.1. Requirements to be satisfied
i. Should have been confirmed in the appointment in Grade III of the service. (A letter of confirmation in
the post should have been issued)
ii. Should have completed an active and satisfactory service period of five (05) years in Grade III of the
service category and earned five (05) increments prescribed for the period.
iii. Should have proved a performance of the satisfactory level or above that according to the approved
scheme of performance appraisal during the five (05) years immediately prior to the promotion.
iv. Should have passed the first Efficiency Bar Examinations on the prescribed date.
v .  Should have acquired the prescribed level of proficiency in the other official language.
vi. Should have completed an active and satisfactory service period of five (05) years immediately prior to
the date of promotion.
10.2.  Promotion to Grade I from Grade II
10.2.1. Under the Average Performance
10.2.1.1. Requirements to be satisfied
i. Should have completed an active and satisfactory service period of seven (07) years in Grade II of the
Nursing Service as at the date of becoming eligible for promotion and earned   seven (07) increments
prescribed for the period.
ii. Should have proved a performance of the satisfactory level or above that according to the approved
scheme of performance appraisal during the seven (07) years immediately prior to the promotion.
iii. Should have passed the second Efficiency Bar Examinations on the prescribed date.
iv. Should have completed an active and satisfactory service period of five (05) years immediately prior to
the date of promotion.
