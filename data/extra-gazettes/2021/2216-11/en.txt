L.D.B. 21/78 (XV) (A)
THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G . L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this  Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006, which specifies the Faculties and the Department of
the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the Provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be
a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the entries “1. Department of Anatomy” and
“6. Department of Microbiology ” appearing in Column II thereof, which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Medicine”
appearing in Column I, thereof of the entries “1. Department of Anatomy, Genetics and Biomedical Informatics” and “6.
Department of Medical Microbiology and Immunology” respectively.
Prof. G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
18th February, 2021.

L.D.B. 21/78 (XV) (A)
THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G.  L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this  Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2186/27 of July 30, 2020) which specifies the Faculties and the Department of the University of Sri
Jayawardenapura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the Provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a
University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the repeal of item “Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences”
and the substitution therefor of the item “Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences”.
Prof. G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
18th February, 2021.
