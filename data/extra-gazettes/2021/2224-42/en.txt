THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under 
the above provisions of the said Act. The particulars of the 
acquired land are described in paragraph 2.
 1. Description of the land. – Acquiring the servient 
right of an allotment of land to build the entrance road of 
the Yahalatenna Service Reservoir under the Kandy North 
Pathadumbara combined water supply project.
 2. Description of Servitude. – An allotment of 
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/HRS/2018/401 
of 19.02.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the Village Ruwanpura in Grama Niladhari Division No. 460 
Ruwanpura in Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor Division 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Harispattuwa in 
Kandy District of Central province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot  ‘A’ – Extent: About 0.0038 Hectare
 North: Delgasmuduna claimed by C. Jayadevi, A. Kalyani 
and Lot B; East: Lot B and Road; South: Road and Ruwanpura 
Colony claimed by T. A. C. H. Thenuwara; West: Ruwanpura 
Colony claimed by T. A. C. H. Thenuwara.
