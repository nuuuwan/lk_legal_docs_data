1A-  G  34317— 30  (05/2021)
THE   URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provision) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development and Housing, being of the opinion that the lands described more
fully in the schedule hereto are urgently required to implement Mixed Development Project do by this order, declare that
the said lands are required for such purpose.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
May 05, 2021.
SCHEDULE
Allotments of land called Waljambugahawatta at Pannipitiya in extent of 0.7899 Ha. shown as Lot Nos. A, B & C in Plan
No. 3018 dated 06.04.2005 prepared by M.L.N. Perera , Licensed Surveyor situated in the village of Pannipitiya within
Grama Niladhari Division 531 Pannipitiya - North in Divisional Secretariat Division of Maharagama in the District of
Colombo, Western Province.
