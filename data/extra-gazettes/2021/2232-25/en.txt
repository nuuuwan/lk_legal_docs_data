PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Bibila Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Bibila Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  that  Mr. Marakoon Mudiyanselage Tissa Gunasinghe of the
Bibila Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
INDIKA GAYAN PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Bibila Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Monaragala,
14th June, 2021.
