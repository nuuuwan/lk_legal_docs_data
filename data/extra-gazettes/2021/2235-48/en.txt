THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the government intends to
acquire under the provisions of the Land Acquisition Act, the
following  lands which are required for a public purpose:
The Allotments of land depicted in  the Advance Tracing No.
Maha/UDN/2017/679 and dated  23.04.2018 prepared by the
Surveyor General situated  in the Village of Balumgala,
Kandupalatha Korale  (Udunuwara) Minor Division, in the
Divisional  Secretary's Division of Udunuwara, in Kandy
District, Central Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot  ‘A’  - Balumgalawatta  – Extent: About 0.0036
Hectare
North: Road and the remaining portion  of the said land;
East: Remaining portion of the said land; South: Lot B and
remaining portion  of the said land; West: Remaining portion
of the said land and road.
02. Lot  ‘B’  - Balumgalawatta  – Extent: About 0.0271
Hectare
North: Remaining portion  of  the said land and Lot A; East,
South and  West: Remaining portion of the said land.
