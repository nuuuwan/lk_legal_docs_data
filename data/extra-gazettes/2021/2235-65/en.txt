FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     WARAKAPOLA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mathota Ralalage
Priyantha Jayakody the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wanisekara Bandaranayaka Wasala Mudiyanse Ralahamilage Inok Dammika Aluwihare to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Wanisekara
Bandaranayaka Wasala Mudiyanse Ralahamilage Inok Dammika Aluwihare was selected as the member of the Warakapola
Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
28th of June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KEGALLE  URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kegalle Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Laxman Dissanayaka the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dasanayaka Mudiyanselage Lakpriya Punchi Bandara Mirihella to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Kegalle Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Dasanayaka
Mudiyanselage Lakpriya Punchi Bandara Niriella was selected as the member of the Kegalle Urban Council.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kegalle Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
28th of June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MATALE   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Matale Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Thambi
Mudalige Mohomad Yusup Mohomad Nilaf the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Matale Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ratala Kotuwe Durayalage Dharmarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanti, Returning Officer  of  the Matale Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Ratala Kotuwe Durayalage Dharmarathna was selected as the member of the Matale Municipal Council.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Matale Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Matale,
