FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN  OF   THE   WELIGAMA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)  AS   AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Weligama Urban  Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to be a member
of the party;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Rehan
Dhanushka Wijerathne Jayawickrma the  former Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that Weligama Urban  Council who belongs to such party  who shall
be declared elected as  Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate  Gardiya Punchihewage Yamuna Kanthi to fill such vacancy ;
THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withange Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of Weligama Urban  Council, acting
in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as  amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Gardiya Punchihewage Yamuna Kanthi  elected as Chairman
of the Weligama Urban  Council.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Weligama Urban  Council.
District Election’s Office,
Matara,
22nd July, 2021.
