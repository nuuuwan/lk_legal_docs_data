SOUTHERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Acts, No. 32 of 1983 and No.  11 of 1992
ORDERS  ON  DELEGATION  OF  POWERS
AS the Southern Provincial Council has been dissolved, the powers vested upon the Minister in charge of the subject of Co-
operatives of the Southern Province by Section 2(4) of Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended by Acts, No.
32 of 1983 and No. 11 of 1992 as per Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, have been assigned
to me by Article 154(c) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and in terms of the such powers
I, William Wijesinghe Gamage, the Governor of the Southern Province hereby assigned all powers depicted in the Column III
of the said Schedule, assigned to the Registrar of Co-operative Societies under Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as
amended by Act, No. 32 of 1983 and Act, No. 11 of 1992, and the regulations enacted there under to the person mentioned in
the Column I of the following Schedule and who has been appointed as the Deputy Commissioner of Co-operative Development
(Perform Duties) to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies of
Southern Province from the date mentioned in the Column II of the said Schedule
D
R. W. W. GAMAGE,
Governor of Southern Province.
On this 07th day of July, 2021,
At Galle.
