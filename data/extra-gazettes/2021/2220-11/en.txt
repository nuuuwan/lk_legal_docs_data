THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of Lands, do
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the government intends to acquire the following lands
which are required for a public purpose:
The allotment of land known as the name and in extent given
below fully described in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/MHN/
2018/619 dated 18.10.2019 made by the Surveyor General, situated
in the village of Mullepihilla, Alupathakadahena in the Grama Niladhari
Division of the Wewatenna (274) in the Divisional Secretariat Division
of Kadawath Sathara and Gangawata Korale in the District of Kandy,
Central Province is bounded.
01. Lot  'A' - Alupathakadahena and Oruwehena - Extent:
About 0.0506 Hectare
North: Alupathakadahena and Oruwehena claimed by Kalyana
Prabhoda Dissanayake; East: Alupathakadahena and Oruwehena
claimed by Kalyana Prabhoda Dissanayake; South: Alupathakadahena
and Oruwehena and road claimed by Kalyana Prabhoda Dissanayake;
West: Road and Alupathakadahena and Oruwehena claimed by
Kalyana Prabhoda Dissanayake.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands.
