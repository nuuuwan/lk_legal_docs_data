No. 2255/26 –  THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 25, 2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) Act No. 28 0f 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
My Reference No.: BOP/4/4/1-87 
Ministry of Land  Ref. No.: 4-3/19/2020/F/265. 
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land  which 
is required for a public purpose: 
 
  
Description of  the Land  to be acquired: 
 
 
The Lot No. 01 depicted in the basic Plan                      
No. GA/4620 surveyed and prepared by the Chief 
Surveyor Superintendent of Galle District, situated in the 
village called Niladeniya in the Grama Niladhari Division 
No. 123 A of Niladeniya in the Bope -Poddala Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Galle District in the Southern 
Province. 
 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Piyanarachchiwatta or 
Mulanagewatta; Description: Land, Mixed cropping in 
the age group of  10 -15 years; Claimant: D. K. 
Ponnamperuma, Hapugala, Wackwella; Extent: 0.1955 
Hectare. 
 
  All persons interested, having rights or possessions in 
the aforesaid lands are required to appear before me 
personally, or by agents dully authorized in writing, at 
Divisional Secretariat  Bope -Poddala, on 13 th December 
2021 at 10.00 a. m.  
 
 They  are hereby requested to notify to me in writing             
(in duplicate) on or before the 06 th December  2021,  the 
nature of their interests in the land and the  particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
aforesaid land, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
All original deeds and all documents  in the 
applicant's possession, copies of the relevant extracts of 
the title, shall be presented at the time of inspection. 
 
                      PRABHASHI C. VIDANAPATHIRANA, 
 Bope-Poddala Divisional Secretary, 
 Galle District. 
  
 
Bope-Poddala Divisional Secretariat, 
Date : 24th November 2021.
