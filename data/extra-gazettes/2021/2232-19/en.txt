PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Election Ordinance Notices
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A (1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Eheliyagoda   Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of the Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Gallage Ranjith Wikramasinghe of the
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATENNE,
Returning Officer,
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
14th June, 2021.
