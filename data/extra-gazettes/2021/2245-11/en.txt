I, roshan Gunathilaka, the Governor of Western province, in terms of the powers vested in me as per section 326 (Chapter 
252) of the municipal Councils Ordinance read with section 02 of the provincial Councils (Consequential provisions) act 
No. 12 of 1989 do hereby order that all the municipal Councils of Western province in their functioning shall, to the relevant 
degree, accord to procedures described under the Financial regulations of democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka on 
decision making, functioning or defaulting with regard to financial control of Municipal Council Fund until provisions be 
allocated by a written law relating to any matter not provided for by provisions under municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 
252) on determining the procedure for utilization of the money of municipal Council Fund; or not provided for by orders 
enforced by section 289 of the said Ordinance or by by-laws enforced under section 267 read with section 272 of the same 
Ordinance or by a relevant by-law made under section 03 of the Local Government authorities (standard by-laws) act 
No. 06 of 1952.
