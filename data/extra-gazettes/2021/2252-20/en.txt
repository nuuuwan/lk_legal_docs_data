Whereas the office of  return member of  Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased 
to be a member of the party  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Tharumaraththinam 
Supashini  the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member  within a period 
of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Wairaiya Chandrakalarani to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Badana Mudiyanselage Indunil Sanjeewa Lasantha Bandara, Returning Officer  of  the 
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 
262) do hereby declare that Wairaiya Chandrakalarani was selected as the member of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
