2.2 Qualifications for recruitment of the internal applicants b y a limited competitive examination and an interview 
to examine the qualifications. 
 
2.2.1 For the Posts of Assistant Government Printer (Production ), Assistant Government Printer 
(Maintenance) and Assistant Government Printer (Printing Editor): 
 
(a) Should be an officer in Supervisory Management Assistant (Tec hnological) Service Category 
in the Printing Service with a period of service of not less th an 15 years in a Departmental Post 
(Management Assistant Technological Service Category) in the Department of Printing. 
 
(b)  Should have obtained certificates after following 02 courses  in Printing in the Sri Lanka 
Printing Institute or a recognized institution. (Duration of a course should not be less than one 
year). 
 
(c) Should not have been subjected to disciplinary punishment as per the provisions set out in 
Public Service Commission Circular No. 01/2020 and the circulars incidental thereto.
