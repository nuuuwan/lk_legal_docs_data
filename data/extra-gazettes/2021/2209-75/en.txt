NOTICE
THIS is to inform that, Professor G.L.Peiris the Hon. Minister of Education, having cancelled the Order relating to the
Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute which has been published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.859/12 dated 23rd
February, 1995, with effect from 28. 02. 2021 published a new Order in the Government Gazette Extraordinary bearing
No. 2199/12 dated 28.10.2020 to establish a new University under the name and style of “The Gampaha Wickramarachchi
University of Indigenous Medicine, Sri Lanka” with effect from 1
st March, 2021.
Therefore, the Students who are registered at the said Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute for courses of
study leading to undergraduate degrees awarded by the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka as at 28 th February, 2021 will be
treated as duly registered students for the same courses of study leading to the Degrees awarded by “The Gampaha
Wickramarachchi University of Indigenous Medicine, Sri Lanka”.
Further the Students who have marked preferences in the applications for University admissions for the academic
year 2019/2020 for admission to the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute based on the results of G.C.E. (Advance
Level) Examination held in 2019 under old and new syllabus are henceforth treated as applicants who have applied for
Bachelors Degrees awarded by “The Gampaha Wickramarachchi University of Indigenous Medicine, Sri Lanka”.
Senior Prof. S
AMPATH AMARATUNGE,
Chairman,
University Grants Commission.
Colombo,
30th December 2020.
