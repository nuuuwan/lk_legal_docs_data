FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     POONAKARY   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Vikkineswaran
Jeyakanthan the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of  that No. 02 Gnanimadam of Poonakary  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Loorththamma Adaikkalarasa to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Winson Gunalan Winson Gnanathayalan, Returning Officer  of  the Poonakary  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Loorththamma Adaikkalarasa  was elected as the member of  the No. 02 Gnanimadam of Poonakary  Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. G. W. G
NANATHAYALAN,
Returning Officer,
Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
28th July, 2021.
