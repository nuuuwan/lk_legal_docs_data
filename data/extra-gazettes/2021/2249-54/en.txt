ORDINANcE     (chApTER   262)    
WhereAs the office of  return member of  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending 
three consecutive meeting without permissions ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Velmurugan 
Srivathsan the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
returned member of  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period 
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Raman Senathirasa to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Raman 
Senathirasa was selected as the member of the Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 
R. C. AmAlRAAj,
 Returning Officer,
 Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
