FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   KALPITIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Abdul
Rahuman Muhammadu Jeseem  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abuthahir Mohamed Aslam to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of the Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Abuthahir Mohamed Aslam was
selected as the member of the  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
20th August, 2021.
