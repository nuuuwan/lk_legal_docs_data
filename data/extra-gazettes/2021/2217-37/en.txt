WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD  FOR  THE  TEA   GROWING   AND  MANUFACTURING   TRADE
It is hereby notified that the decision adopted by the Wages Board for the Tea Growing and Manufacturing Trade and
approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour as per provisions of Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) is to be varied as
depicted in the following schedule as per provision Section 29(3) of the said ordinance.
These decisions should be valid with effect from 05th March 2021.
M. P. D. U. K. M
APA PATHIRANA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour.
Mehewara Piyasa,
Colombo 5,
03.03.2021.
