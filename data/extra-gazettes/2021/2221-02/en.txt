THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of  Lands,
do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
Allotments of land called by the following names and extents
shown in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/PTH/2019/322 dated
28.10.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village
of Pinnagolla in Grama Niladhari division of Pinnagolla (986) of Gam
Dahaya Korale Minor Division in the divisional Secretary's Division
of Pathahewaheta in Kandy district, Central Province and bounded
as follows.
01. Lot  'A' - Kiribowa - Extent: About 0.0041 Hectare
North: Kiribowa claimed by H. P . Somarathna; East and South:
Lot No. 62 of P.P. Maha 2436; West: Kiribowa claimedd by H. P.
Somarathna.
02. Lot  'B' - Pinnagolla - Extent: About 0.0192 Hectare
North: Lot No. 63 of P .P. Maha 2436; East: Lot No. 01 of P .P.
Maha 6857 and Lot No. 61 of P.P. Maha 2436; South: Lot No. 61 of
P.P. Maha 2436; West: Lot No. 62 of P .P. Maha 2436.
03. Lot  'C' - Kiribowa - Extent: About 0.0253 Hectare
North: Lot No. 63 of P .P. Maha 2436 and Kiribowa claimed by
K. Dhanaraj; East: Kiribowa claimed by K. Dhanaraj;  South: Dana
Watta claimed by M. P . Premarathna; West: Lot No. 01 of P .P. Maha
6857.
