ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Winding -Up of The Standard Credit Finance Limited (PB 123) subject to the Supervision of 
court in terms of the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 read with Sections 32 and 37 of the Finance 
Business Act, No. 42 of 2011
NOTICE is hereby given that a Petition for the winding up of the above-named company subject to the supervision 
of the Commercial High Court of Colombo, was on the 7 th day of October, 2020, presented to the said Court by 
Jayakody Arachchige Don Saman Jayanath Nanayakkara, the Director, Department of Supervision of Non-Bank 
Financial Institutions, Central Bank of Sri Lanka, Colombo 1. And that the said Petition is directed to be heard 
before the Court on the 11
th day of October 2021; and any creditor or contributory of the said company desirous 
to support or oppose the making of an order on the said petition may appear at the time of hearing in pereson 
or by his counsel or proctor for that purpose, and a copy of the petition will be furnished to any creditor  or 
contributory of the said company requiring the same by the undersigned on payment of the prescribed charge 
for the same.
Director,
Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions.
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
No. 30, Janadhipathi Mawatha,
Colombo 1.
