PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 25.06.2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCA TION ORDER UNDER SECTION 39 (1)
THE allotments of land specified more fully in the Order No.
01 of  2016 dated 30.12.2015  made under the Proviso (a) to
Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1948/37  dated
07th January, 2016  are vested in the Democratic Socialist
Republic  of Sri Lanka.But the possession of the said land has
not yet been taken for and on behalf of the Democratic
Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka.Therefore, I Samarakoon
Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of  Lands, by virtue of
powers vested in me under Section 39(1) of the Land
Acquisition  Act, do hereby revoke the order No. 01 of 2016
dated 30.12.2015 published in the Gazette Extra-Ordinary No.
1948/37 dated 07.01.2016.
S. M. Chandrasena,
Minister of Lands.
