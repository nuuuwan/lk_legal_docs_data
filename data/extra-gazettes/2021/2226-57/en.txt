I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendments) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for a public  purpose, Knuckles Conservation.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :
The following block of land which are located within the
below mentioned boundaries, and this block of land constitute
19.4734 Hectares from a distinct portion of Y ahangalawaththa
depicted as lot number 310 and 311 of main plan numbered as
F.V .P . 320, Supplement No.2, sheet No.2 drown after having
being surveyed by Survey General, and situated in Kanda
Pahala Korale Minor Division, Udagaladebokka Grama Niladari
Division, Udagaladebokka Village which comes under the
purview of Divisional Secretary's Division Minipe, Kandy
District in Central Province.
01. Lot Nubmer 310 Yahangalawaththa:- (Extent : 19.4734
Hectares)
To North:-Lot Nuber 301: To East: 301, 311and 312: To South-
Lot Number 312 Pallegala Debokka (F.V .P 319) Udailuka (F.V .P
486) and lot Nubmer 173:- To west:- Lot Number 173 and Lot
Number 308
02. Lot Nubmer 311 Yahangalawaththa:- (Extent : 4.1399
Hectares)
To North:- Lot Number 301 and 313: To East: Lot Number
Pallegala Debokka (F.V .P . 319) : To South-Pallegala Debokka
(F.V .P . 319) and Lot Number 312:- To West:- Lot Number 310
and 301
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretary's Office, at Minipe on the 26th of
July 2021, 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th of June 2021 the
nature of their interests in the land. The particulars  of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
