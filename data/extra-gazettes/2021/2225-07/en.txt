DEPARTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT — EASTERN PROVINCE
Notice on Summon Meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner of Local Government of Eastern Province do hereby inform that the meeting,
which was declared by me through the Gazette Notification (Extraordinary) No. 2223/4 dated 15.04.2021 and scheduled to be
held on 29.04.2021 of at 10.00 a. m. at meeting hall of Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha, to elect the new Vice Chairman of
Koralaipattu West Pradeshiya Sabha, Batticaloa District, will not take place due to the Covid 19.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Eastern Province.
