THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Divisional Secretary Reference No. : BOP/4/4/1-78.
Land's Ministry Reference No. : 4-3/11/2018/H/480.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of  land depicted as Lot No. 01 in Preliminary
Plan No. G/4524 situated in the Village of Godakanda in No.
126, Godakanda Grama Niladhari's Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Bope Poddala in Galle District Southern
Province and described as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Karapitiyawaththa alias Obaddege
Waththa; Description: Garden; Claimant : Dilusha Duminde
Bope Weerathunga, No. 274, Milco Road, Digana, Rajawella,
Kandy; Extent: 0.1418 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Divisional Secretary's Office, Bope Poddala on the 31st day
of March, 2021 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 23rd day of
March, 2021 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
All the Deeds, Documents and extract which are relevant
prove the ownership should be forwarded at the inquiry.
PRABASHI C. WIDANAPATHIRANA,
Divisional Secretary,
Bope Poddala.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Bope Poddala,
17th February , 2021.
