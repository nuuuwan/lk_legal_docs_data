PILGRIMAGES ORDINANCE
IT is hereby notified that the Annual Duruthu Maha Procession of Pilana Purana Sri Maha Temple in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Akmeemana, District of Galle, Southern Province, will be held only to perform rituals from 18th
December 2021 to 17th January 2022 as per the order published in the Extra Ordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2146/18
dated 22.10.2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of the Regulations of the Section 2 of the
Pilgrimages Ordinance (Cap.175).
