FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VALIKAMAM     SOUTH
PRADESHIYA    SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which  Abiramy
Vamadevan,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sasikumar Shiyamala to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the V alikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Sasikumar
Shiyamala was selected as the member of  the  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
V alikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election  Office,
Jaffna,
07th  January, 2021.
