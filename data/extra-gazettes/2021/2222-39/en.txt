FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     NIVITIGALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Nivitigala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   ceased to be member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Dhanasiri
Rajapakshage Dileep Chaminda Rajapaksha the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Nivitigala Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Madduma Hewage Chaminda Kumara Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Nivitigala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Madduma Hewage
Chaminda Kumara  Fernando was selected as the member of  the   Nivitigala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Nivitigala  Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Ratnapura,
06th  April, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     BALANGODA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Balangoda Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   elected as a member
of the Parliament ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Ekanayaka
Rajapaksha Basnayaka Mudiyanselage Akila Saliya Ellawala the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 10 - Ellepola of Balangoda Urban Council  who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ilandari Arachchilage Mangalika Ilankoon to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Balangoda Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ilandari Arachchilage
Mangalika Ilankoon  was elected as the member of  the  10 - Ellepola of Balangoda Urban Council.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Balangoda Urban Council.
District Elections  Office,
Ratnapura,
05th  April, 2021.
