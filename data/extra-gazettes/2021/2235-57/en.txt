FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KALPITIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Uvais Marikkar
Mohamad Akmal  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon  the member of  that Ward No. 02 Anawasal of Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Warnakulasooriya Jude Ivon Kingsley Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Warnakulasooriya Jude
Ivon Kingsley Fernando was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 02  Anawasal of Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Kalpitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
30th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KALPITIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Abdul Kareen
Salahudeen the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that Ward No. 07 Ettala of Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Asan Kuddus Abdul Abdeen to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Asan Kuddus Abdul Abdeen
was selected as the member of  the Ward No. 07  Ettala of Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Kalpitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,

WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased
to be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Sooriyaarachchige
Arvin Uditha  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  Angunakolapelessa  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Samarappuli Madduma Arachchige Somasiri to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha ,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Samarappuli  Madduma Arachchige Somasiri was selected as the member of  the  Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha.

AVINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
