I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the 
Government has acquired the lands described below to 
create a water reserve in the abandon paddy field located 
between the cross canal stretches from Maha Ela located 
in Bokundara, Piliyandala to Piliyandala Gangarama road 
and Wewala Karadiyana road, under Weras Ganga Storm 
Water Drainage & Environment Improvement Project.
