THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
   My  No. : kd/b/1/w;a/2000/2.
Land Ministry Ref. No.:4-3/8/2016/PC/81.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No.28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
An allotment of  Land depicted in the Preliminary Plan No.
P.P.PU 5451 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the
Village called Dunkannawa in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 505/B of  Dunkannawa in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Naththandiya in Puttalam District of the Wayamba
Province and morefully described as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land:  Paranahenawatte; Claimant: P .W.C. Mallika
Fernando ; Extent: 1.0487 Hectares.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby
required to apear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat , Naththandiya on
the 23rd day of March 2022 at 10.00 a.m  and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the16th day of March 2022  of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of such amount. All deeds and documents in support of the
claims should be produced before me at the inquiry.
                                       C
HA THURAKA JAYASINGHE,
                                             Divisional Secretary ,
                                                 Naththandiya.
At  Divisional  Secretariat, Naththandiya.
20th of  December, 2021.
