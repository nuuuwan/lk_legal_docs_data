THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY  LAW,   No.  41  OF  1978   ENACTED  BY  THE
NATIONAL  STATE ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 enacted by
the National State Assembly, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development & Housing do by this Order vest with
the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today where the land is situated at Suduwella Ward No. 24 within the
limits of the Municipal Council of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land at Norris Canal Road
belonging to the Municipal Council of Colombo.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of  Urban Development & Housing.
Ministry of Urban Development & Housing,
17th & 18th Floors,
“Suhurupaya”,
Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
22nd  February, 2021.
