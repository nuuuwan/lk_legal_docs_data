Direction No. 75
CONSUMER AFFAIRS AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING, under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no importer, manufacturer, distributor or trader of Hand Sanitizers shall import,
manufacture, store, distribute or display for sale, expose or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any Hand Sanitizers unless such
Hand Sanitizer is registered with the National Medicines Regulatory Authority (NMRA) and the Registration Number is
displayed in a conspicuous place of the pack or container of such Hand Sanitizer.
This Direction shall become operative with effect from 01st April, 2021.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Direction, rescind Direction No. 74 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2208/27 dated 31st December, 2020.
                                                                         By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
25th February, 2021.
