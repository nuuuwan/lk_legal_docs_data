FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MINUWANGODA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Minuwangoda Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohomad Munas
Mohomad Rifas the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon  member of that ward number 06  Minuwangoda Middle  of Minuwangoda Urban Council who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kekulandala Liyanage Sumudu Dilanga to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Minuwangoda Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Kekulandala Liyanage Sumudu Dilanga was elected as the member of  the ward number 06  Minuwangoda
Middle  of Minuwangoda Urban Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st July, 2021.
