PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that  Mr. Wedikkaralage Suresh Sanjeewa of the
Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has been ceased to be
a member of the United National Party.
SAJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
14th June, 2021.
