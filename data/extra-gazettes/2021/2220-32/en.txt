BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G. L. Peiris, 
the Minister of Education having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Benedict XVI 
Catholic Institute of Higher Education (Private) Limited, a Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, 
No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 115017, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order made 
under the said Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2201/4 of November, 09, 2020 by the addition immediately 
after the entry “Bachelor of Business Management Honours” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry in Column 
II thereof, of the entries speci ﬁ ed under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions 
speciﬁ ed in Schedule I hereto.
