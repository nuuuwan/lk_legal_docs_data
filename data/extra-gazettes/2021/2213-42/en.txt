THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
 My Ref. No. : BD/WM/LND/2/5/AQUIS/28.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : LD/05/2005/H/398.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, the
Government intend to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
In the Province of Uva, District of Badulla, Divisional
Secretariat Division of Welimada in the Minor Division of
Udapalatha Korala, Gramaniladhari Division of No. 50A,
Ambewela, situated in the village of Hewanakumbura,
prepared by Survey General P.P.N.B. 740 and dated on
30.06.2020 base lot number 01, The total extent of 0.7452
Hectares.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Worwick Estate; Details: Home garden, mixed
cultivation about 20-25 years and six Permanent buildings
and part of Permanent building; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission Sabawa; Extent: 0.7452 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me on the day 22nd February , 2021 at 10.00 a.m.
at the Divisional Secretariat Welimada.
Futher request to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 22nd February, 2021 the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
W. M.C. WEERASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Welimada.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Welimada.
