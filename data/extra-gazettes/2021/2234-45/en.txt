PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  AMBAGAMUWA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)(a)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Nakandalage Don
Kapila Nandana Nakandala the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that ward number 05 Jambuthenna of Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Batahena Wedaralalage Ramyasiri Batahena to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana, Returning Officer of the Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Batahena
Wedaralalage Ramyasiri Batahena was elected as the Member of the ward number 05 Jambuthenna of Ambagamuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI SERASINGHA PATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
30th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  RIDEEGAMA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A (1)  OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be a
member of the Party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Edirisinghe
Devage Lional Chandrawansha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Yapa Hetti Pathirannehallage Indika Kavisigamuwa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Yapa Hetti
Pathirannehallage Indika Kavisigamuwa was selected as the Member of the Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
01st July, 2021.
