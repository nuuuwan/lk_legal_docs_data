Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN   THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF  THE   VAVUNIYA   SOUTH   (TAMIL) 
PRADESHIYA    SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A (1)   OF  THE   LOCAL    AUTHORITIES     ELECTIONS 
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by r eason of 
Resignation ;
And whereas the secretary of Ahila Ilan kai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to wh ich Aiyakannu 
Selvanayagam the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide up on 
returned member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Sectio n 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a memb er within a period 
of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rasiah Suventhera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in 
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasiah Suventhera 
was selected as the member of the Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. J ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South Tamil Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
21st June, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 23.06.2021
FILLING   OF   A  VACANCY   IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER  OF  THE  BENTOTA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA UNDER
SECTION 66A (1) OF  THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return member of Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kariyawasam
Dewarakkottage Janaka Prasanna the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Aswin Manuranga Samaranayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aswin Manuranga
Samaranayaka was selected as the member of the Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Bentota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
23rd June, 2021.
