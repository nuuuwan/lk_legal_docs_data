FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MATALE    MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member in Matale Municipal  Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   removal of the Mayor from
his post by the Governor under Section 277 of the Municipal Council Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Daljith Nandalal
Aluvihare,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of   that Ward Number 01 Aluviharaya Nagaraya of Matale Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Chilton Stefan Berenjer to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Matale Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Chilton Stefan Berenjer  was elected as the member of  the Ward Number 01 Aluviharaya Nagaraya of  the Matale
Municipal  Council.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Matale Municipal  Council.
District Election  Office,
Matale,
07th  January, 2021.
