SRI LANKA EXPORT DEVELOPMENT ACT, No. 40 OF 1979
Order under Section 14
BY Virtue of the Powers vested in me by Section 14 of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Bandula
Gunawardena, Minister of Trade, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the rate of CESS
specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule to the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2202/6 dated 17.11.2020,
in respect of the articles described under the H. S. Codes as specified in the Schedule hereto, with effect from March 16, 2021.
Dr. B
ANDULA GUNAW ARDANA,
Minister of Trade.
Ministry of Trade,
Colombo 02,
15th March, 2021.
