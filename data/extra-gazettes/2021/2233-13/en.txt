CEYLON  PETROLEum  CORPORATION ACT, No. 28 Of 1961  
 
Order under Section 40 (1)
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 40 (1) of the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation Act, No. 28 of 1961 as amended 
by the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation (Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 1963, I, udaya Gammanpila, Minister of energy, do hereby 
approve the proposed acquisition of the property described in the schedule hereto.
 Udaya Gammanpila,
 Minister of energy.
12th May, 2021.
sChedule
All that allotment of land marked lot 1 depicted in Plan No. 1059 dated 31st August, 2017 made by K. A. Perera, licensed 
surveyor of the land called “Palleha Watta alias Adikari Gedara Watta” together with the building, trees, plantations and 
everything else standing thereon bearing Assmt. No. 07, Pragathi Mawatha situated at uda Iriyagama within the No. 152, 
uda Iriyagama West Grama Niladhari division in the yatinuwara divisional secretariat area within the Pradeshiya sabha 
limits of yatinuwara in Gangapalatha Patthuwa of yatinuwara Korale in the district of Kandy, Central Province and which 
said lot 1 is bounded on the North by Road from houses and Pragathi Mawatha, on the east by Pragathi Mawatha, on the 
south by Pragathi Mawatha and Polgasdeniya Road and on the West by Assmt. No. 7/1, Pragathi Mawatha and containing 
in extent Thirty-five decimal Two Eight Perches (0A., 0R., 35.28) or 0.08923 Hectare according to the said Plan No. 1059.
