PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34215— 35 (04/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer of the Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Samarasinghe Arachchige Sujeewa of
the Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that  Thissamaharama  Pradeshiya Sabha  as she
has   been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
R
A VINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Thissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
20th  April, 2021.
