FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DAMBULLA   MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Dambulla Municipal Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Payin Gamuwe
Gedara Siriwardhana,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of   Dambulla Municipal Council   who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Rupasinghe Don Piyathilaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Dambulla Municipal
Council , acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Rupasinghe Don Piyathilaka was selected as the member of  the   Dambulla Municipal Council.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Dambulla Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Matale,
02nd  February, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 05.02.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VA VUNIYA   NORTH   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of failing to make
and subcribe an oath or affirmation in terms of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Basnayaka
Mudiyanselage Indrawansa Samantha Dilan Basnayaka,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of   Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wijekoon Mudiyanselage Erandi Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wijekoon Mudiyanselage
Erandi Kumari was selected as the member of  the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd  February, 2021.
