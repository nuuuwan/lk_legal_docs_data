 FOREST CONSERVATION ORDINANCE 
 
 
The Order 
BY virtue of powers vested in me by section 3(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) that has to be read  
with the notification published in G azette Extraordinary No.2187/27 dated 09 th August 2020 by th e President according 
to the constitution 43(1),44(2) and  46(1)(a),(b) of the  constitu tion of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka       
respectively ,I do by this order declare  the state land named Siratikulam of  6365.63 hectares bounded by the limits set 
forth in the schedule here to as a Reserved Forest  from the 30th day of November 2021.
