No. 2254/45 –  FRIDAY, NOVEMBER  19, 2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 Ref. No.: 4/2/1/91. 
Land Ministry's of  Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2017/LO/213.  
 
I, hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 
that the Government intends to acquire  the following  
lands which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land depicted in below name and 
extent in the Preliminary Plan No. P. P. GAM 4241 dated 
18.05.2021 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the village called Meddegama in the  Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 383 of Meddegama in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Dompe in Gampaha District of the 
Western Province. 
 
P. P.  GAM 4241 Description of the Allotment  
Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Wekawatta alias Maligawatte; 
Description: Homestead, there are 2 temporary buildings; 
Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 
5.2444 Hectare 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents, duly authorized 
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of Dompe 
on 09.02.2022 at 10.00 a. m. and  are hereby requested to 
notify to  me in writing  (in duplicate) on or before 
02.02.2022 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 
        N. A. A.  S. NISSANKAARACHCHI, 
                       Divisional Secretary,  
 Divisional Secretary's Division- Dompe, 
 
 
 
On 15th November, 2021,   
At Divisional Secretary's Division - Dompe.
