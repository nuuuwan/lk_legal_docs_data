THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No.  2   OF   1980
Order under Section  2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No.2 of 1980,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-charge of the subject of Urban Development & Housing, being of the opinion that the land more fully described
in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the purpose of implementation of Athurugiriya New Town Development
Project do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
March  12 , 2021.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land namely Dawatagaha Kumbura,Dawatagaha Watta depicted as from Lot 1 to 5in the sketch Plan
No. 05/2020 dated 16.12.2020 prepared by D. K. Wickramasekara, Land Manager of Urban Development Authority, containing
in extent of about 2 Acres situated in Pore Village within the Grama Niladari Division of 489A - Boralugoda in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kaduwela in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
