FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KALUTARA    PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Keraminiyage
Teksan Samararathna,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of   Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Anura Shantha Hettimulla to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Kalutara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Anura Shantha Hettimulla was selected as the member of   the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election  Office,
Kalutara,
08th  January, 2021.
