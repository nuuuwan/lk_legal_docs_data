Whereas the office of  return member of  Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Hasanve 
Muhammadu Siraj  member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon the  member of  that No. 05 - Minhaj of  Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Ibralebbe Muhammad Rafeek to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ibralebbe 
Muhammad Rafeek was elected as the member of the No. 05 - Minhaj of Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha.
