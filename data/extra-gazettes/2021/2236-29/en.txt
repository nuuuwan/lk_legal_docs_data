FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KURUNEGALA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Mohomad
Munas Mohomad Museen the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammad Sarifdeen Muhammed Hayas to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammad
Sarifdeen Muhammed Hayas was selected as the member of the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
09th  July, 2021.
