FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RAMBUKKANA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group to which  Senadirannehelage Pathmasiri Herath the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of
Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Nawarathna Mudiyanselage Gamini Nawarathna Bandara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nawarathna
Mudiyanselage Gamini Nawarathna Bandara was selected as the member of  the  Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Kegalle,
30th  April, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     WANANTHAWILLUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Wananthawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Nagur Pakeer
Abdul Munap the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Number 01 - Ralmaduwa of Wananthawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wepola Pathirannehelage Diksan Kumarasiri to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Wananthawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wepola
Pathirannehelage Diksan Kumarasiri was elected as the member of  the  Number 01 - Ralmaduwa of Wananthawilluwa
Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Wananthawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Puttalam,
30th  April, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     IBBAGAMUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the member of  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   elected as a
member of the Parliament ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Pudujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Udukumburage  Sumith Weerasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 13 - Ambanpola of Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Attanayaka Mudiyanselage Senarath Jayanada to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Attanayaka
Mudiyanselage Senarath Jayanada was elected as the member of  the  13 - Ambanpola of Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Kurunegala,
30th  April, 2021.
