NOTIFICATION
Appointment of a Chairman and Members of the Board for approval of Credit Agencies under the
Mortgage Act, No. 06 of 1949
IT is hereby notified that the Hon. Minister of Trade by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 114(1) of the
Mortgage Act, No. 06 of 1949, has appointed Mr. Jayantha De Silva to be the Chairman and Mr. J. R. C. Jayathilake and Ms.
Kanthi Gunawardana to be the other members of the Board constituted for the purpose of the above Act, for a period of 03
years effective from 15th March 2021 to 14th March 2024.
It is further notified that the previous Board constituted in terms of Sri Lanka Government Gazette No. 2030/34 of
02.08.2017, ceased to be in operation.
J. M. B
HADRANIE JAYAWARDHANA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Trade.
Ministry of  Trade,
Colombo 02,
22nd March, 2021.
