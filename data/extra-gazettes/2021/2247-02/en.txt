(Chapter 262) Local Government Elections Ordinance
Notifications under Section 66 g
IT is hereby announced that the first meeting to elect the Vice Chairman of the Kotagala Pradeshiya Sabha in the Nuwaraeliya
District of the Central Province as per the powers vested in me under Section 66g of the amended Local Government
Elections Ordinance of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 shall be held at the Kotagala
Pradeshiya Sabha Meeting Hall at 10.00 a.m. on 21st October 2021 at the venue mentioned therein.
H. M. M. U. B. H
ERATH,
Commissioner,
Department of Local Government,
Central Province.
Provincial Council Complex,
Pallekele,
Kundasale,
