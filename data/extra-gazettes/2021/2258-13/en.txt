ThE CuSTOmS ORDINANCE (ChAPTER 235)
Customs Notification No. 3/ 2021 - Unaccompanied Passenger Baggage Bonded Warehouse
IN pursuance of the power vested in me by section 69 of the customs, ordinance (chapter 235), I Ganwarige Vijitha ravipri-
ya, director General of customs, do, with the approval of the minister of Finance granted by virtue of the powers vested in 
him by that section, by this notice published in accordance with his direction, approve and appoint warehouses and places 
of security specified under Column 03 of the Schedule annexed hereto, as an Unaccompanied Passenger Baggage Bonded 
Warehouse in which such goods may be warehoused, kept and secured without payment of duty.
 
Major General (retired) G. V . raVipriya,
 director General of customs.
