MINUTES OF PENSIONS
Notification under Section 51 of the Pensions Minutes dated 05th February, 1934 amended by
Pensions Minutes (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICA TION
THE  Pension Minutes are hereby amended with the removal of the last sentence “However the gratitude in no case shall
exceed Rs. 3,000” of Section 44 of the Pension Minutes amended by the Gazette Notification No. 304 dated 16.06.1984 and
with the inclusion of the following sentence and then a new section as 44 a in the following manner.
“However the ex gratia gratuity shall not case exceed Rs. 50,000 in any case”
44a - However no gratuity shall be granted under this section to the officers and their dependents, who have been
deprived of the pension due to the facts in Section 20(1) of the Pension Minutes.
This decision shall be treated as effective from 06.01.2009.
