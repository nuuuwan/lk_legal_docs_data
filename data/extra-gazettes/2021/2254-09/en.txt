Whereas the office of member of  Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Failure to attend meetings 
of the council for a period of three months without permission  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana  Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Bamunu 
Mudiyanselage Amila Indheewara Chandrapala the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the 
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  that 14 - Alawwa of Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected 
as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Hewanan Arachchige Chandima Nilu Gunarathna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer  of  the Alawwa  
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do 
hereby declare that Hewanan Arachchige Chandima Nilu Gunarathna  was elected as the member of the 14 - Alawwa of 
Alawwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
