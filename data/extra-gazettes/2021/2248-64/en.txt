Order  No. 79
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.  09   OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 57 in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2155/34 dated 27th December, 2019 and
Order No. 74 in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2237/40 dated 25th July, 2021 and do by this Order remove “Wheat Flour”from
Order No. 39 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1975/68 on 14th July, 2016.
This Order shall come into effect from 08th October, 2021.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
MAJ. GEN. SHANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
08th October 2021.
