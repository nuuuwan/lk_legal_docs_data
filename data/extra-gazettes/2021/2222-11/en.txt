FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     POINT    PEDRO    URBAN
COUNCIL    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Point Pedro Urban  Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of   Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Francis
Vincentdipol the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  that Ward 03 - Munai of Point Pedro Urban  Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mikketpillai Antonbenik Robinson to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Point Pedro Urban  Council,  acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mikketpillai Antonbenik
Robinson was selected as the member of  the   Ward 03 - Munai of Point Pedro Urban  Council .
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Point Pedro Urban  Council.
District Elections  Office,
Jaffna,
30th  March, 2021.
