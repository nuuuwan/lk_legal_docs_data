INDuSTRIAL  DISPuTES  ACT  (ChAPTER 131)
I, Gotabaya rajapaksa, the President of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of the powers vested in me 
in terms of Section 22 of the 43 of 1950 Industrial disputes Act, which Chapter 131 of the list of Legislations of Sri Lanka 
(revised edition 1956), as amended by the Industrial disputes (Amendments) Act Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957 and shall be read 
together with the Industrial disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1968.
(1) d o hereby appoint the persons whose names are indicated in the scheduled attached hereto as the members of
the panel where from person should be selected for the constitution of an Industrial Court,
(2) Prescribe	that	the	term	of	office	of	the	said	members	shall	be	a	period	of	three	(03)	years	with	effect	from
01.11.2021.
Gotabaya Rajapaksa,
