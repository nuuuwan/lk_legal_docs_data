SPECIAL COMMODITY LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order Under Section 5
by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 5 of the special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, basil rajapaksa, 
minister of Finance of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do hereby make and order to waive-off the balance 
amount after collecting of 25 Cents from the applicable special Commodity Levy of rs. 65 per Kg on importation of rice, 
calssified under the HS Codes of 1006.30.10; 1006.30.20 and 1006.30.30, imposed by the Order Published in the Gazette 
No. 2249/3 dated October 11,2021 under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 for a period of six 
(06) months effective from 02nd November, 2021.
 Basil Rajapaksa,
 minister of Finance.
ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
02nd November, 2021.
