SPORTS  LAw  No. 25 Of 1973
Regulations under the Section 32
I, the minister of youth and sports, as per the powers vested in me under sections 32 (c) of the sports Law No. 25 of 1973 
cancel the registration of the sambo Federation of sri Lanka, the National Association for the sport of sambo, the sri Lanka 
Jujitsu Federation; the National Association for the sport of Jujitsu and the scrabble Federation of sri Lanka, the National 
Association for the sport of scrabble with immediate effect.
 Namal Rajapaksa (M.P.),
 minister of youth and sports.
One the day of 24th November, 2021,
Colombo.
