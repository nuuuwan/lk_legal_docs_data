FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     V ALIKAMAM   WEST
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas of the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Kunabalakrishnan Kirubakaran the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of
Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Kathiravelu Mathanagobalakrishnan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Kathiravelu Mathanagobalakrishnan was selected as the member of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
19th July, 2021.
