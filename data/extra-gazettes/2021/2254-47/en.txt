FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna,  the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha
Mudiyanselage Viraj Milinda Rajapaksha the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as  a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kirigampamunuwage Hareendra Dilip to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore  I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kirigampamunuwage Hareendra Dilip was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
19th November, 2021.
