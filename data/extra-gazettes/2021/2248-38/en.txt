Whereas, in terms of Article 7(2) of the aforesaid Act, the Commission has made a request that it requires
additional powers in order to effectively accomplish the task assigned ;
And, I do hereby further assign the Commission, powers specifically set out in  Sub section (f) of Section 7(2) of
the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Law No. 7 of 1978, as amended by the Special Presidential Commission of
Inquiry (Special Provisions) Act, No. 4 of 1978 ;
Now therefore, reposing great trust and confidence in your integrity, prudence, ability and fidelity do direct you
to take every step to implement the above functions set out in the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry, established by
the aforesaid Warrant ;
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 06th day of October,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
                                                                                                 By  Order of His Excellency,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
