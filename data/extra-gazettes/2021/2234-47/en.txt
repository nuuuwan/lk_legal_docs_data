FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AMBALANGODA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Hiranya Kosala
Wijewickrama the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Gallage Lasantha De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Gallage
Lasantha De Silva was selected as the member of the Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
01st of July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     TANGALLE   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Tangalle Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  been ceased to be a member
of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wedaarachchi
Patabendige Ravindu Dilshan the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon  the member of  that No. 03 Medaketiya of Tangalle Urban Council who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abeysinghe Kodippili Patabendige Vijith Chandima to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Tangalle Urban Council  acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Abeysinghe Kodippili
Patabendige Vijith Chandima was elected as the member of the No. 03 Medaketiya of Tangalle Urban Council.
R
AVINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
01st of July, 2021.
