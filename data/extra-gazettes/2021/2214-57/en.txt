THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of  Lands, do
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of  Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is  needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Boundaries for the an allotments of land bearing following names
and extents fully depicted in the advance tracing No. H/ANG/2017/
06 dated 10.01.2018 made by Surveyor General and situated in
Weeragaswewa (F.V .P. 364) in minor division of Giruwapattu East,
Divisional Secretary's Division of Angunakolapelessa, Hambantota
district, Southern Province.
01. Lot 'A' – Name of land Muruthawela Y aya – Extent:
0.0483 Hectare
North: Muruthawela Yaya enjoyed by Kodithuwakku Arachchige
Dayawathie and road; East: Road and Muruthawela Yaya enjoyed
by Mohottige Sriyalatha; South: Muruthawela Yaya enjoyed by
Mohottige Sriyalatha and Canal; West: Canal and Muruthawela Yaya
enjoyed by Kodithuwakku Arachchige Dayawathie.
02. Lot 'B' – Name of land Muruthawela Y aya – Extent:
0.0383 Hectare
North and East: Lot A; South: Lot A and Canal; West: Canal and
Lot A.
