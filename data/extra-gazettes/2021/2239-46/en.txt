THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under 
the provisions of that Act.
Eight (08) allotments of land depicted in the Advanced 
Tracing No. Aa/NPE/2020/1011 dated 28.12.2020 prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called Anu-
radhapura Town (FTP A 2) in  the Grama Niladhari Division 
No. 248,  Kanadara Korale Minor Division in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Nuwaragampalatha East in Anurad-
hapura District of the  North Central  Province and bounded 
as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’  – Nuwarawewa Kale – Extent: 0.0262 Hectare
North: Nuwarawewa Kale occupied by Regional Archeo-
logical Oﬃ  ce; East: Nuwarawewa Kale occupied by Regional

canal; West: Nuwarawewa spill canal and Malwathu Oya.

North: Nuwarawewa Kale occupied by Regional Archeo-

and Nuwarawewa spill canal; South: Nuwarawewa spill canal; 
West: Road and Nuwarawewa Kale occupied by Regional

3. Lot ‘C’ – Nuwarawewa Kale – Extent: 0.0649 Hectare

spill canal.
