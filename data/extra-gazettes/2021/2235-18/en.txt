FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   mEmBER   OF   ThE    JA  ELA   PRADEShIYA 
SABhA   uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1) OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE    
(chAPTER   262)    
Whereas the office of  return member of Ja ela Pradeshiya Sabha has   fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a 
member of the Party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ethmadalage Asanka 
Devuruwan Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide 
upon returned member of Ja ela Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the 
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has communicated his decision to nominate 
Agampodi Sanjaya De Soysa  to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Ja ela Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Agampodi 
Sanjaya De Soysa was selected as the member of the Ja ela Pradeshiya Sabha.   
 
K. J. S. Madhawa,
 Returning Officer,
 Ja ela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha.
05th  July, 2021.
