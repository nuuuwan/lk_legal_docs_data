Greetings !
As you have been appointed as members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management
in the Eastern Province established by the Presidential Directive dated 01.06.2020 by exercising the powers vested in me
by Article 33 of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And, whereas, it has been identified that the officers who have been appointed as the members of the said Task
Force, such as Surveyor General and the Director General of the Civil Security Department, have retired or been trans-
ferred,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the
powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby appoint
below mentioned officers as the members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management in the
Eastern Province established under the aforesaid Presidential Directive in place of the members of the said Task Force
referred to at 11 and 16, by reposing great trust, and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity.
1. W. T. M. S. B. Tennakoon Esquire
Surveyor General
2. Major General (Retired) N. R. Lamahewage Esquire
Director General of Civil Security Department
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 18th day of August
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P.  B .  J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
