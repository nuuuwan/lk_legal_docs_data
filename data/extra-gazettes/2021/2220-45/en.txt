THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An  allotment of land in extent about 0.4047 Hectare depicted
Advance Tracing No. NU/NWE/2017/370 of  03.05.2018
prepared by the Surveyor - General, situated in Kandapola
Village in Oya Palatha Minor Division in the Nuwara - Eliya
Divisional Secretary's Division in the Nuwara - Eliya  District
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' -  Park Estate - Extent:  0.4047  Hectare
North:  Park Estate claimed by Udapussellawa Plantation
(Pvt. ) Ltd; East and South: Road  (RDA);  West: Stream and
Park Estate claimed by Udapussellawa Plantation (Pvt) Ltd.
S. M. Chandrasena,
