PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - PG 5462— 35  (06/2021)
NOTICE  MADE UNDER  SECTION  66C  READ   WITH    SECTION   66 g   OF     LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  NO. 22  OF 2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to
the powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a recalling meeting in view of
selecting a new Chairman, for the vacated Chairman’s position of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council of Western Province,
will be convened on June 28 of 2021 at 9.30 a. m. at the Urban Council Chamber of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council
located at Seeduwa.
A. D. P. I. P
RASANNA,
Commissioner of Local Government,
Western Province.
At the Department of Local Government of Western Province,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla.
11th June, 2021.
