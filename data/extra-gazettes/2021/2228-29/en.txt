FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AMBALANGODA   URBAN
COUNCIL    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Ambalangoda Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Maduwage
Dhanasena the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Egodage Ajith Susira Shantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Ambalangoda Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Egodage Ajith Susira
Shantha was elected as the member of the Ambalangoda Urban Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Urban Council.
District Elections  Office,
Galle,
18th  May, 2021.
