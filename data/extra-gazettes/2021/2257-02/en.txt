 Governor of the central bank of Sri lanka
  
   the public is hereby informed that, due to the amalgamation of arpico Finance company Plc with associated motor 
Finance company Plc as part of the master Plan of Financial Sector consolidation, the monetary board of the central 
bank of Sri lanka, in terms of the Finance business act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the licence issued to arpico Finance 
Company PLC of 146, Havelock Road, Colombo 05, to carry on finance business, with effect from 31.03.2021. Arpico 
Finance company Plc has not accepted deposits from the public with effect from 31.03.2021.
