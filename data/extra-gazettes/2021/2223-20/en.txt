ADVERTISEMENT  OF  PETITION
Winding-Up of The Standard Credit Finance Limited (PB 123) subject to the supervision of court in
terms of the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 read with Sections 32 and 37 of the Finance Business
Act, No. 42 of 2011
NOTICE  is hereby given that a Petition for the winding-up of the above-named Company subject to the supervision of the
Commercial High Court of Colombo, was on the 7th day of October, 2020, presented to the said Court by Jayakody Arachchige
Don Saman Jayanath Nanayakkara, the Director, Department of Supervison of Non-Bank Financial Institutions, Central Bank
of Sri Lanka, Colombo 01. And that the said Petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 20
th day of July 2021 ; and
any creditor or contributory of the said company desirous to support or oppose the making of an order on the said petition
may appear at the time of hearing in person or by his counsel or proctor for that purpose ; and a copy of the petition will be
furnished to any creditor or contributory of the said company requiring the same by the undersigned on payment of the
prescribed charge for the same.
Director,
Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions,
Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
No. 30, Janadhipathi Mawatha,
Colombo 01.
Note.- Any person who intends to appear on the hearing of the said Petition must serve on or send by post to the above-
mentioned Director of Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions notice in writing of his intention so to
do. The notice must state the name and address of the person, or, if a firm, the name and address of the firm, and must be
signed by the person or firm, or his or their proctor (if any) and must be served, or if posted, must be sent by post in sufficient
time to reach the above-named not later than 6.00 p. m. of the 19
th day of July 2021.
