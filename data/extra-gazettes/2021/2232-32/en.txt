PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A -  PG 5468— 22  (06/2021)
DEPARTMENT   OF  LOCAL   GOVERNMENT  -  EASTERN  PROVINCE
Notice on summon meeting of under the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of  2012
BY virtue of power vested on me under Section 66(C) read with Section 66(G) of Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, Nagarasa Manivannan, the Commissioner
of Local Government of Eastern Province, do hereby announce that the recalling meeting to select a new Chairman, for
the vacated chairman’s position of Seruwila Pradeshiya Sabha in Trincomalee District of Eastern Province, will be convened
on 02.07.2021 at 10.00 a. m. at the Seruwila Pradeshiya Sabha Council Hall.
N. M
ANIV ANNAN,
Commissioner of Local Govenment.
Eastern Province.
Office of the Department of Local Government,
Eastern Province,
Kanniya Road, Varothaya Nagar,
Trincomalee,
17th June, 2021.
