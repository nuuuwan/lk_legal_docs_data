THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The land of below mentioned, depicted in Advance Tracing
No. GA/BPP/2020/910 and dated 15.01.2021 prepared by  the
Surveyor General, situated in the village called Niladeniya in
the Grama Niladhari Division No. 123 A of Niladeniya in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Bope - Poddala in Galle
District of the Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  "A"  - Piyanarachchiwatta or Mulanagewatta  –
Extent: 0.1955 Hectare
North: Faculty of Engineering of University of Ruhuna;
East: Faculty of Engineering of University of Ruhuna,
Piyanarachchiwatta or Mulanagewatta claimed by D. K.
Ponnamperuma, and Piyanarachchiwatta or Mulanagewatta
claimed by Erangi Ponnamperuma; South: Piyanarachchiwatta
or Mulanagewatta claimed by D. K. Ponnamperuma, and
Piyanarachchiwatta or Mulanagewatta claimed by Erangi
Ponnamperuma and Piyanarachchiwatta or Mulanagewatta
claimed by Kamal Kariyawasam; West: Piyanarachchiwatta or
Mulanagewatta claimed by Kamal Kariyawasam and Faculty
of Engineering of University of Ruhuna.
