CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD   ACT,   No.   17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Dullas
Alahapperuma, Minister of Power, do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition of the lands required by the Ceylon
Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto.
 D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA,
26th July, 2021. Minister of Power.
Schedule
The land situated in Madatiyawela Estate, in the Poththegedara village, in the Pahala Madatiyawela, Grama Niladhari’s
Division No. 46 of Divlapitiya Divisional Secretary’s Division in the Hapitigama Korale in the Gampaha Administrative
District of Western Province of Sri Lanka, containing in extent of 0.11 Hectares (01 Roods & 4 Perches) shown at Lot A in
sketch CEB/PR/PD/PSRSP1/TE/DRG/001 dated 05th July, 2021 prepared by the Ceylon Electricity Board bounded as
follows :
North : Kotadeniyawa Proposed Land for 220kV Grid Substation - Lot B ;
East : 12 feet wide private gravel road ;
South : Kande Kaalla Gravel Road (P. S.) ;
West : Part of the same land of Lot A claimed by Manori Mendis.
Claimed by : Manori Mendis.
