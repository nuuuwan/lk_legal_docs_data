Government  Notifications
REF. DGC A877/PL 305.
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, Retired Major General  G. V .  Ravipriya, Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 13th  September,  2021,
all  duties of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette No. 2244/2 of  06th September, 2021 is  hereby
rescinded.
Retired Major General  G. V .  RA VIPRIYA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
10th September, 2021.
