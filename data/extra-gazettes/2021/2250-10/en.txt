PART I : SECTION (I)  —  GENERAL 
 
 
 
THE SOCIETIES ORDINANCE 
 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 3 & 4 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Basil Rajapaksha, 
Minister of Finance do by this notification; 
 
 01. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “SRI LANKA PARAMPARIKA VAIDYA 
SANGAMAYA” situated No. 123, Wataddara Purana Viharaya, Wataddara, Veyangoda as a purpose to 
which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended; and 
 
 02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of Section 5(4) 
of that Ordinance. 
 
 
 
 BASIL RAJAPAKSHA, 
 Minister of Finance. 
 
Ministry of Finance, 
The Secretariat, 
Lotus Road, 
Colombo 01.
