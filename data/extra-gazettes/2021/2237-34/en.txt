FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AKKARAIPATTU
MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Mohammed Ismalebbe Risreena the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Akkaraipattu Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammadhu Musthafa Siththy Jaleela to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that
Muhammadhu Musthafa Siththy Jaleela was selected as the member of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
20th July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     AKKARAIPATTU

WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Sulaima Lebbe Muhammathu Irfan the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid

acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Abdul Cader Muhammadhu Nawfer was selected as the member of  the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
