1A-  PG  5459— 225  (06/2021)
Direction No. 78
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Special Direction under Section 10(1)(b)(ii)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(l)(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority hereby directs all manufacturers and traders of Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) that they shall
ensure that adequate quantities of the domestic cylinders weighing 12.5kg containing Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) shall be
available for sale at all LPG sales outlets island wide.
Further, no trader who has in his possession or custody or under his control the domestic cylinders weighing 12.5 kg
containing Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) for purpose of trade within Sri Lanka shall refuse to sell the domestic cylinders
weighing 12.5 kg containing Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) or in any manner directly or indirectly compel consumers not to
purchase  such cylinders.
This Direction shall come into effect from 09th June, 2021.
                                            By  Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
09th of June, 2021.
