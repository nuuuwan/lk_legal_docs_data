EMERGENCY  (ESSENTIAL  FOOD  SUPPLY)  REGULATIONS,  No.  1 OF  2021
Appointment of Commissioner General of Essential Services
By virtue of powers vested in me by paragraph 1 of Regulation 9 of the Emergency (Essential Food Supply) Regulations,
No. 1 of 2021, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby appoint Major
General Niwunhellage Don Senarath Piyasiri Niwunhella, RWP RSP PGD – CPS, to be the Commissioner General of Essential
Services for the whole of Sri Lanka to execute and co-ordinate all activities relating to the aforesaid Regulations.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo
August 30, 2021.
