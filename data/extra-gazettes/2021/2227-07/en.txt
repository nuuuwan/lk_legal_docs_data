FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     VA VUNIYA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Prabakaran
Yaanujan the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of Vavuniya Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Shaththithas Thanuskanth to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Shaththithas Thanuskanth was
selected as the member of  the   Vavuniya Urban Council.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
District Election  Office,
Vauniya,
10th  May, 2021.
