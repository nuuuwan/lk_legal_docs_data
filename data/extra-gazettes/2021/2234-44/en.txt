FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   mEmBER   OF   ThE    WALAPANE PRADEShIYA
SABhA uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1) OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE    
(chAPTER   262)    
Whereas the office of  return member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Ceased to be a 
member of the Party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Hiranya Deepthi 
Dulanjani Manike Herath Ranaweera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified 
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate 
Wijekoon Mudiyanselage Susanthi Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana returning Officer of the Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of 
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Wijekoon Mudiyanselage 
Susanthi Kumari was selected as the member of the Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha. 
 
Pulasthi serasingha Pathirana,
Returning Officer,
Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwaraeliya,
30th  June, 2021.
