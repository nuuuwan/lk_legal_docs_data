NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION 16  SUB - SECTIONS (2D)  OF  THE  COAST
CONSERV ATION AND COASTAL  RESOURCE MANAGEMENT ACT, No. 57 OF 1981
Proposed project on Mining of Heavy Mineral Sand along the Kokilai - Kokkuthoduwai Coastal stretch and
Establishment of Mineral Sand Processing Plant at Kokilai in Mullaitivu District
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT   ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Heavy Mineral Sand along the
Kokilai - Kokkuthoduwai Coastal stretch and Establishment of Mineral Sand Processing Plant at Kokilai in Mullaitivu has
been submitted by the Lanka Mineral Sand Ltd., No. 341/21,Sarana Mawatha,Rajagiriya, as required by the Section 16 (1) of
the Coast Conversation and Coastal Resource Management Act, No. 57 of 1981 as amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and
No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following institutions from 8.30 a.m. to 4.15 p.m. for a period
of 30 days from 23.02.2021 (Except weekends and public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official web site 
(www.coastal.gov.lk.) of the Department
of Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management
