FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     GAMPAHA   MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Gampaha Municipal Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Wanasundara
Arachchilage Samantha Lakmali,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of   Gampaha Municipal Council   who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dona Vajirani Gnanapriya Samaraweera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Gampaha Municipal
Council , acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Dona Vajirani Gnanapriya Samaraweera was selected as the member of  the   Gampaha Municipal Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Gampaha,
03rd  February, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MUTHUR   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation  ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group - 01,  which  Muhammadu Ismail Naufaaldeen ,  the member
belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of   Muthur
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group - 01 has communicated his decision to nominate
Kameedu Bawa Nismi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kameedu Bawa Nismi was
selected as the member of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Trincomalee,
03rd  February, 2021.
