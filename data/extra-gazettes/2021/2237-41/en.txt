1A-  PG 5416— 120  (07/2021)
Direction No. 82
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.  09   OF  2003
 Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no manufacturer, distributor, dealer or trader of Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG)
Cylinders shall manufacture, store, distribute or display for sale, expose or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any domestic
Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) Cylinders unless the weight in Kilograms is marked in a conspicuous place of such
Domestic Cylinders.
This Direction shall become operative with effect from 25th July, 2021.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
25th July, 2021.
