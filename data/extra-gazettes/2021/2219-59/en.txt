THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the schedule hereunder has been named as “Chinmaya Lane” by the
Honourable Governor of Western Province under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No.29 of 1947 (Chapter
252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council's Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No.12 of 1989.
