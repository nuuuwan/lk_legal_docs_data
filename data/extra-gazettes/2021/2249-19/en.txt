THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: KHDS/LND/03/ACQ/VIL/5-1.
Ministry of Lands Ref  No.: LD/05/2003/VEP/524.
I do hereby give notice, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act (Amendment) No. 28 of 1964 that
the Government intends to acquire the following lands which
are required for public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of land in total extent of about 9.949 hectares
depicted as a Lot No. 309, 311 in the Preliminary Plan F.V .P . No.
27 Supplement No. 17, Sheet No. 20 and dated 16.01.2006
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village of
Yatagara, Minor Division of Pannil Pattu and Atakalan korale,
Divisional Secretary of Kahawatta in Rathnapura District
Sabaragamuwa Province.
1. Lot No. 309
Name of the Land: Hedawakahena, Thippolahena,
Kuduruppehena and Kirimaduve hena; Description: Three
years old mixed Cultivation; Claimant: Agalawatte Plantation
Limited, No. 10, Gnanartha Pradeepa Mawatha, Colombo 08;
Extent: 6.144 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 311
Name of the Land: Hedawakahena, Thippolahena;
Description: There is a temporary Cultivation One Years old;
Claimant: Agalawatte Plantation Limited, No. 10, Gnanartha
Pradeepa Mawatha, Colombo 08;  Extent: 3.8050 Hectare.

All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
