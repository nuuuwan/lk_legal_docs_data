Notice under Section 7   
My Ref. No. : 6/3/7/22

I, hereby give notice, under Sub section 1 of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land

the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for Moragahakanda & Kaluganga 
Development Project.

Province. 
 01. Lot No. 1189

Kolongolle Gedara Tikiri Banda (deceased) of Melpitiya, 
Matale and Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Kolongolle 
Gedara Heenbanda of Melpitiya, Matale; Extent: 0.2430

 All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
