FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MANTHAI    EAST   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Manthai  East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which  Rajeswary
Siritharan,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of   Manthai  East Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Election Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kandaiah Parameswary to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the Manthai  East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kandaiah Parameswary
was selected as the member of  the  Manthai  East Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Manthai  East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Mullaitivu,
09th  March, 2021.
