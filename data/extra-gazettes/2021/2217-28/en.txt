PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 33840— 45 (03/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer of the Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Wasudewan Randeep of  the
Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as he has  been ceased to be a member
of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present)
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matale,
02nd  of March, 2021.
