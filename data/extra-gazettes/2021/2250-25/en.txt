NATIONAL WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD ACT, NO.2 OF 1974  
Order under Section 64(1) 
 
 
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 64(1) of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 2 
of 1974 of the National State Assembly, I, Vasudeva Nanayakkara, Minister of Water Supply, do by this order, embody 
in the Schedule hereto the provisions of the voluntary tr ansfer scheme approved by me under Section 63(1) of the 
aforesaid Law, in respect of the water supply undertakings on by the Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha.

Schedule 
 
Contents of the Voluntary Transfer Scheme Section 61(1) 
 
 (a) The following movable and immovable property to be transferred to and vested in the National Water Supply 
and Drainage Board to wit, 
 
 (i) All water sources, service reservoirs, water treatme nt plants and balancing tanks which belong to the 
Punchi Rattota Water Supply Scheme of the Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha and all lands, structures, water 
mains and installments of all the mechanical, electri cal plant and machinery to operate and maintain the 
service reservoirs, pumping stations together with all spares, stand by equipment and stores required for 
the maintenance of such machinery. 
 
 
 (ii) All water sources which belongs to the Punchi Ra ttota Water Supply scheme of the Rattota Pradeshiya 
Sabha, and all service reservoirs, water treatment pl ants and balancing tanks established in the place 
mentioned in Schedule I and all lands, structures, wa ter mains and installments  of all the mechanical, 
electrical plant and machinery to operate and maintain  the service reservoirs, pumping stations together 
with all spares, stand by equipment and stores required for the maintenance of such machinery. 
 
 (b) The assets of these facilities valued at Rs. 13,037 ,626 (Rupees Thirteen Million Thirty Seven Thousand Six 
Hundred Twenty Six) will be provided on a grant to the Board by the Government. 
 
 
 
 The  above transfer will come into effect from the date of publication of this notice.
