Greetings !
As it has been recognized the necessity of an active and viable mechanism to identify and address the fundamental
problems of the grass-roots community in order to meet the aspirations of the people for access to people-centered economic
development empowered by the rural economy as mentioned in the government’s policy statement, “Vistas of Prosperity and
Splendour”,
And, While considering about the active phases of the government’s leading program, “Gama Samaga Pilisandara”
(Dialogue with the Village) which aims at rural economic development, by identifying local geological, environmental,
cultural, social and economic disparities at the grass-roots level and creating new opportunities for the rural areas that are
being disadvantaged due to those disparities are being launched all over Sri Lanka,
And, observing that directing and monitoring the said programme towards aimed, definite results while coinciding
with the goals of the Government and the needs of the people an essential thing,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of   Sri Lanka, hereby appoint aforesaid you,
1. Hon. Namal Rajapaksa Esquire, M.P.

as the members of the Presidential Task Force for “Gama Samaga Pilisandara” (Dialogue with the Village) rural development,
in terms of powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, while
keeping the great devotion and trust on their discretion, success and reliability to perform the following duties :
1. Direct all the officers at the divisional level as per the service requirements, including the District Secretary,
Divisional Secretary and all other Heads of Institutions, to expedite the implementation of my advice and directives
on the issues and problems raised by the people in the “Gama Samaga Pilisandara” (Dialogue with the Village)
Program,
2. Take necessary steps to make the said programs efficient in a manner that meets the expectations of the people
while continuously monitoring the progress of rural development activities at the regional level focused the
“Gama Samaga Pilisandara” (Dialogue with the Village),
3. Make arrangements to deploy Grama Niladharis, Development Officers (State Ministry of Home Affairs), Agricultural
Research Assistants (Agrarian Services Department), Samurdhi Development Monitors and Officers (Department
of Samurdhi Development), Family Health Officers (Health Department) who have appointed covering each and
every village to provide Residential Level Projects,  technical and consulting services,
4. Accelerate projects such as roads, drinking water supply, electricity, telecommunications, playgrounds, schools,
hospitals, vocational training institutes, urban universities, land transfers, banks and financial facilities, markets,
stores and infrastructure implemented by national level government agencies for implementation in the Pradeshiya
Sabha and Divisional Secretariat areas implemented under the 2021-2023 budget allocations,
5. Accelerate the expeditious implementation of all projects implemented by local and foreign funds under the
Public Investment Program within the targeted time frame and with the standards,
6. Strengthening the residential economy by cultivating vegetables, fruits, coconuts, jackfruits and breadfruits in
residential lands and setup housing and sanitary facilities in those lands,
7. Direct and provide necessary instructions for the budgetary management along with the Secretary to the Treasury
or a Deputy Secretary to the Treasury authorized by him to coordinate with the Budget Department and the
Treasury Operations Department to make the necessary allocations systematically,
8. Since the Department of Management Services has approved staffing requirement for the provision of employment
to low income recipients by the Multipurpose Development Task Force. The Deployment of labour productively
from relevant areas by the Multipurpose Development Task Force being the staff required to development work
at grass-root level for rural development, clearance work of schools, hospitals, rural roads, Pradeshiya Sabhas,
weekly fairs, making elephant fences and maintenance, instruction for supervision and guidance,
And, I do hereby appoint, Hon. Namal Rajapaksa Esquire, Minister of Youth and Sports as the Chairman of said Task
Force.
And, I do hereby require and direct all public officers and such other persons to whom  the Task Force may issue
instructions or apply for assistance  for information, to  comply with such instructions, render all such assistance and  furnish
all such  information  as may properly be  complied  with, rendered and furnished, in that behalf.
And, I do hereby require and direct the said Task Force to report to me all instances where any Government
employee or an officer in any Ministry, Government Department, State Corporation or any such Institution who delay the
performance of duties and fulfillment of responsibilities or fail to perform such duties and responsibilities to be entrusted by
the said Task Force.
Additional Secretary to the President, Ms. Chandima Wickramasinghe will coordinate the necessary activities by
providing all the necessary information related to “Gama Samaga Pilisandara” (Dialogue with the Village).
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 09 day of April Two
Thousand and Twenty One.
By  His Excellency’s Command,
 P. B. J
AYASUNDARA,
 Secretary to the President.
