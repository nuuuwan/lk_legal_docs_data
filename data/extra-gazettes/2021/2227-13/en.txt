FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MINUWANGODA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Pathiranage
Yasarathne the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the returned member of that number 28 - Asgiri Walpola of Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohamed Mashahir Mohamed Suhaider to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Minuwangoda
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Electioins Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare  Mohamed Mashahir Mohamed Suhaider was elected as the member of the number 28 - Asgiri Walpola  of
the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
11th  May, 2021.
