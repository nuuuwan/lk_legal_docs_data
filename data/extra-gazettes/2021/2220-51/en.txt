FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KESBEWA     URBAN    C OUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Kesbewa Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of being  ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Vithanage
Sujeewa Nishanthi Chandrasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon returned member of  Kesbewa Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Pahalawattage Dona Yashodara Lakmini to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Kesbewa Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Pahalawattage Dona Yashodara Lakmini was selected as the member of  the   Kesbewa Urban Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kesbewa Urban Council.
District Election  Office,
Colombo,
24th March, 2021.
