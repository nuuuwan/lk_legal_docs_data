FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     CHILAW   URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A (1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Chilaw Urban Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of   death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Warnakulasooriya
Emmanuel Fernando,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of   that 04 - Weralabada North of Chilaw Urban Council   who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muthunayaka Gamlathge Nihal Sumanadheera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Chilaw Urban Council  , acting in terms of Section
66A (1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muthunayaka Gamlathge Nihal
Sumanadheera was elected as the member of  the   04 - Weralabada North of Chilaw Urban Council.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Chilaw Urban Council.
District Election  Office,
Puttalam,
01st of  March, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     CHILAW   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending for Council
meetings period of three months without permission  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Kurukulage Nilanthi
Fernando,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of  that Number 01 - Munneshvarama of Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
John Peterge Udeni Pushpa Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Chilaw  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A (1) (a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that John Peterge Udeni Pushpa
Kumari was elected as the member of  the Number 01 Munneshvarama of Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Chilaw  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election  Office,
Puttalam,
01st March,  2021.

(30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Jayasinghe Arachchilage Wasanthi Mangalika Jayasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Puttalam Urban Council , acting in terms of Section
66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Jayasinghe Arachchilage Wasanthi
Mangalika Jayasinghe was selected as the member of  the Puttalam Urban Council.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
District Election  Office,

WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Valvettithurai Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;

Thamotharampillai Sathasivam,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,

a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Sanmugampillai Sanmugathas to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valvettithurai Urban Council , acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Sanmugampillai
Sanmugathas  was selected as the member of  the Valvettithurai Urban Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valvettithurai Urban Council.
District Election  Office,
Jaffna,
