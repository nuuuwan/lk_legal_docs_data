FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     V A VUNIYA   SOUTH  (SINHALA)
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya South  (Sinhala)  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased
to be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Punchi  Appuhamige
Karunadasa the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of  Vavuniya South (Sinhala)  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Lalith Padmasiri Siriwardena to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton Returning Officer  of  the Vavuniya South (Sinhala) Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Lalith Padmasiri
Siriwardena was selected as the member of the  Vavuniya South  (Sinhala) Pradeshiya Sabha.
 J.  J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South (Sinhala) Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd July, 2021.
