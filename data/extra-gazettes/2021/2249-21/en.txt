 FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE  KALPITIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Mohamed
Mohidin Fathima Mujifa  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ulam Kader Siththi Sakeena to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Badana Mudiyanselage Indunil Sanjeewa Lasantha Bandara, Returning Officer  of  the Kalpitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare  that Ulam Kader Siththi Sakeena  was selected as the member of the Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
B. M. I. S. L. B
ANDARA,
Returning Officer,
Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
12th October, 2021.
