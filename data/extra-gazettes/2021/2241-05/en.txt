UV A  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notiﬁ cation of Hon. Governor of Uva Province
NOTIFICATION UNDER SECTION 2(3)(b) OF THE STATUTE No. 2 OF 2011 REGARDING POWERS TO 
MONITOR ADMINISTRATION IN LOCAL GOVERNMENT AUTHORITIES ESTABLISHED IN 
UV A PROVINCE 
BY virtue of powers vested on me through the Section 2(2) of the Statute  of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding powers 
to monitor administration in Local government Authorities established in Uva Province,  appointed  Mr. H.M.R. Anura 
Kumara, Judicial Ofﬁ cer to investigate and report to me  whether any fact in Sections (b) and (c) of the Sub-section 2(1) of 
the said Statute has been done by the Pradeshiya Sabha including the  Hon. Chairman of the Kandaketiya Pradeshiya Sabha 
Considering the observations and recommendations of that report I hereby cancel the notiﬁ cation by me through the special 
Gazette No. 2217/45 dated 05.03.2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka relevant to Kandaketiya Pradeshiya 
Sabha under the Section 2(3)(a) of the  Statute No. 2 of 2011 to monitor administration in Local Government Authorities 
established in Uva Province, according to 2(3)(b) Section of that statute with effect from 16.08.2021.
A. J. M. M
UZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor's Ofﬁ ce,
16th of August, 2021.
