SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
2020 Second  Supplementary  Financial  Statement
GRANTING   PERMISSION   BY   VIRTUE   OF   POWERS   VESTED   IN  ME   UNDER   SECTION   27(A)    OF
PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL   ACT    No.   42   OF   1987   AS   THE   TERM   OF   THE    SIXTH   SABARAGAMUW A
PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI    LANKA   ENDED   ON  26TH
SEPTEMBER,   2017, 2020
1. This permission may be cited as “Permission to disburse under second supplementary financial statement -2020 of
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council”  and  come in to operation from 02.12.2020.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Three Billion  Six Hundred
and Sixty Six Million One Hundred and Eighty Two Thousand Only  (Rs. 33,666,182,000.00) for the service of the
period beginning on 01st January, 2020 and ending 31st December 2020, which is specified as “financial year 2020” in
the Budget Estimates,  prepared for the year 2020, that was approved on 29.08.2020  is  hereby amended as, Rupees
Thirty Four Billion  Seventy One Million  Five Hundred and Sixty Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Eleven and cents
Seven  Only (Rs. 34,071,569,611.07 ) and grant permission to the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting
Officers  to  disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
3. Accordingly the first schedule of the Annual financial statement and revenue- expenditure estimates- 2020,  is
amended as the first schedule hereof and grant permission to disburse  the same.

AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby approve to amend the advance account limits stated in the Sixth Schedule of the Second Supplementary
Financial Statement for the year 2020, by virtue of powers vested in me  under Section  27 (a)  of the Provincial Council Act
No. 42 of  1987,  as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
ended on  26th September, 2017.
Accordingly, I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council to bear the expenses subject to those limits.
This permission will be valid  till  31st  of December, 2020  or  upto the date that expires  three months (03) from the
date of summoning  the Seventh (07 th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  or  from the date  re-start the functions of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  which occurs first.
TIKIRI KOBBEKADUW A,
 Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
02nd day of December, 2020
AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
disburse as per the second Supplementary Financial statement for the year 2020 ,  by virtue of powers vested in me  under
Section  27 (a)  of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of  1987,  as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka ended on  26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Three Billion  Six
Hundred and Sixty Six Million One Hundred and Eighty Two Thousand Only  (Rs. 33,666,182,000.00 ) for the services of the
period beginning on 01st January, 2020 and ending 31st December, 2020, which is specified in the  estimates for the year 2020
is amended  as Rupees Thirty Four Billion  Seventy One Million  Five Hundred and Sixty Nine Thousand Six Hundred and
Eleven and cents Seven  Only (Rs. 34,071,569,611.07 ) and grant permission to  disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be valid  till  31st  of December, 2020  or  upto the date that expires  three months (03) from the
date of summoning  the Seventh (07 th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  or  from the date  re-start the functions of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  which occurs first.
