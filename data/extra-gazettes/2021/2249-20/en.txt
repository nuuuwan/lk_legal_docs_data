FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MAHARA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Lanka Chanda
Wickrama Arachchi the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Suduwa Dewage Indika Ruwan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Mahara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Suduwa Dewage Indika Ruwan was selected as the member of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
12th October, 2021

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KATTANKUDY  URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  the resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which Mohamed
Saly Mohamed Mohideen the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the returned member of  Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Subaideen Mohamed Sabry to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Subaideen
Mohamed Sabry was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
M. B. M. S
UFIYAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
