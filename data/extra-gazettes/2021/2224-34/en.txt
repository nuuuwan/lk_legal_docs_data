THE LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 07
 
Ref. No. of the Divisional Secretary: KDS/KGK/4/9/2015/CO-op
Land Ministry No. :  4-3/8/2014/PC/429.
 
I do hereby give notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 07 of
the land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the land described below  which is required
for public purpose.
Description of the Land which is expected to be Acquired :
The following allotment of land marked Lot No. 1 containing
in extent 0.0114 hectares depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Maha
7020 dated 31.12.2020 prepared by the Senior Superintendent
of Surveys of the Kandy District on  behalf of the Survey
General, situated in the Village of Udaperadeniya in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Udaperadeniya (249) in the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Kandy Kadawathsathara and
Gangawata Korale in the District of Kandy Central Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Gedarawatte;  Claimant: S. S. H. Liyanage,
No.24/1, Kekunawatte, Udaperadeniya; Extent:
0.0114 Hectare
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude which
is to be acquired to appear personally or by agent duly
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale on 24.05.2021 at
10.00 a.m.
 
 Further, it shall be notified to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before 17.05.2021 the nature of their interests in the
land/lands, the particulars of their compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
