FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     GALIGAMUWA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of the  member of  Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by the reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Wedikkaralage
Suresh Sanjeewa the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that 05 - Edurapotha of Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Lekamalage Sunil Herath to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of
Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Lekamalage Sunil
Herath  was elected as the member of  the 05 - Edurapotha of Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
28th June, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     RAMBUKKANA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of  Rambukkana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased
to be a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Thennakoon
Mudiyanselage Tikiribanda the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  United National Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hiti Rallage Gamini Herath Bandara Jayasekara to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the Rambukkana  Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Hiti Rallage Gamini
Herath Bandara Jayasekara was selected as the member of  the  Rambukkana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Rambukkana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
28th June, 2021.
