SPECIAL   COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the
commodity specified in the Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the
corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of  06 months commencing from April 27, 2021.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2214/58 of February 11, 2021 in respect of Potatoes (HS Code 0701.90) is rescinded with
effect from April 27, 2021.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
April  27, 2021.
