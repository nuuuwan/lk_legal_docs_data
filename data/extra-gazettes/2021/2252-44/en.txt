THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
My No.: 11/3/3/1/6B.  
Land Min. Ref. No. : LD/05/2007/MV/214B. 
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
is required for a public purpose.  
  
Description of the lands to be acquired:- 
   
Public purpose means the land Acquisition for the 
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project. 
 
The lands described below, containing in extent 
2.0196 hectares shown in Topo PP 13 of supplement  
No. 156, sheet No. 323, approved by Surveyor General 
dated on 15.07.2021, situated at E 389A Gangahenwala 
Division of Laggala Pallesiya pattuwa, Laggala 
Divisional Secretary's Division, Matale District, Central 
Province. 
01. Lot No. 11041 
Name of Land:  Kokgalawalapitiyehena; Description 
of Land : bush; Claimant: The State; Encroached by: 
Konara Mudiyanselage Pahala Gedara Tennakoon Banda 
of Gangasirigama, Perakanaththa ; Extent: 1.0764 
Hectare. 
  
02. Lot No. 11042 
Name of Land:  Kokgalawalapitiyehena; Description 
of Land : bush; Claimant: The State; Possessed by: 
Konara Mudiyanselage Pahala Gedara Tennakoon Banda 
of Gangasirigama, Perakanaththa possessed under L.L. 
permit No. 18; Extent: 0.8094 Hectare. 
  
03. Lot No. 11045 
Name of Land:  Kokgalawalapitiyehena; Description 
of Land: bush; Claimant: State; Encroached by: Konara 
Mudiyanselage Pahala Gedara Tennakoon Banda of 
Gangasirigama, Perakanaththa; Extent: 0.1338 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents before me at 
9.00 a.m. on the 30 day of November 2021 at the Laggala 
Divisional Secretariat.
