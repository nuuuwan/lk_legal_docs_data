FILLING OF A V ACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE RIDEEGAMA PRADESHIYA 
SABHA UNDER SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE 
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the ofﬁce of  return member of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be 
member of the party ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the The Liberal Party, the recognized political party to which Appukutti Dewayale 
Manoja Bishanthi Malawika Karunadasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid 
ordinance, to decide upon returned member of Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualiﬁed 
under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member 
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Boraluwe Gedara Devage Sujeewa Nilmini Dharmadasa to ﬁll such vacancy ;
Now Therefore, I, Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayaka, Returning Ofﬁcer  of  the Rideegama 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Boraluwe Gedara Devage Sujeewa Nilmini Dharmadasa was selected as the member of the Rideegama Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
  
 R
ATHNAYAKA MUDIYANSELAGE HARENDRA BANDARA RATHNAYAKA,
 Returning Of ﬁcer,
 Rideegama Pradeshiya Sabha.

02nd November, 2021.
