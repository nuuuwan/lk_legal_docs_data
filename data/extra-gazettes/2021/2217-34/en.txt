FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     SEETHAWAKA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Rajapaksha
Pathirage Yasaru Dewanmina Rajapakshe  the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dolawaththa Appuhamilage Don Upul Chandana to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer  of  the Seethawaka Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Dolawaththa Appuhamilage Don Upul Chandana was selected as the member of  the   Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Elections  Office,
Colombo,
01st  March, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KAYTS     PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  the recognized political party to which
Kanagasuntharam Jeyakumar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned member of that ward 03 - Naranthanai North of Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such  party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ambalam Kanakaiya to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of
Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ambalam Kanakaiya
was selected as the member of  the ward 03 - Naranthanai North of Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election  Office,
Jaffna,
03rd March,  2021.
