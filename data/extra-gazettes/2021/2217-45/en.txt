UV A PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Notice of the Hon. Governor of Uva Province
INCONSISTENT with the Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, should be read with the Section 2 of the
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the Section 2(3)
(a) (I)   of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding the powers to monitor the administration, identified as the Statute No. 2 of 2011
of the Uva Provincial Council imposed to make provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor administration
in Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province and matters relate and incidental thereto I, A.J.M. Muzammil,
the Governor of the Uva Province do hereby  temporarily suspend the function of the Hon. Chairman of Kandeketiya
Pradeshiya Sabha of Badulla District in Uva Province with effect from the 8th of March 2021
Further, I appoint and order Mr. Sowkenda Mudiyanselage Senevirathne, Hon. Deputy Chairman of Kandeketiya
Pradeshiya Sabha to implement powers and perform functions of  Kandeketiya Pradeshiya Sabha of Badulla District in Uva
Province with effect from 08th March 2021.
A. J. M. M
UZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor’s Office,
05th  March, 2021.

2A
UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice of the Hon, Governor of Uva Province
INCONSISTENT with the Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, should be read with the Section 2 of the
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the Section 2(2)
of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding the powers to monitor the administration, identified as the Statute No. 2 of 2011 of the
Uva Provincial Council imposed to make provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor administration in
Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province and matters relate and incidental thereto I, A.J.M. Muzammil, the
Governor of the Uva Province do hereby appoint Mr. H.M.R. Anura Kumara Judicial officer, retired justice to investigate and
report to me within a duration of 03 months from the date of this notice whether any fact in Sections (b)  (c)  and (d)  of the
Subsection 2(1) of the said statute has been done by the Hon. Chairman of Kandeketiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
The said judicial officer has the powers of a Commissioner appointed under the Investigation Commission Act
regarding the said investigation.
A.J.M. M
UZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor’s Office,
05th  March, 2021.
