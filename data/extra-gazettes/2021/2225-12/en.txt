FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     COLOMBO    MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of the  return member of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front at present), the recognized
political party to which Maradappan Bala Suresh Kumar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Aarumugam Udayar Shiva to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aarumugam
Udayar Shiva was selected as the member of  the   Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections  Office,
Colombo City,
27th  April, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 27.04.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DIMBULAGALA      PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of    member of  Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Henakaralalage
Ananda Wijayaraja the member belonged, was required by me under Section    66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Ward No. 03 - Aralagangwila Electoral Ward of  Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Samarawickrama Gamachchige Dipal Asanka Virosan to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Piumi Attygalle, Returning Officer  of  the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Samarawickrama
Gamachchige Dipal Asanka Virosan was elected as the member of  the  Ward No. 03 - Aralagangwila Electoral Ward of
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
IUMI ATTYGALLE,
Returning Officer,
Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Polonnaruwa,
23rd  April, 2021.
