THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : THI/LND/L2/ACQVI/119.
Ref. No. of the Ministry of Lands : 4-3/9/2014/WS/02.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (I) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude right which is required
for a public purpose:
Description of the land/servitude right to be acquired :
Two allotments of land in total extent of about 0.1396
Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 01 and 02 in the Final Village
Plan No. P .P . Mara 2808 dated 19.02.2021 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Kithalagama,
Kithalagama Central Grama Niladhari Division, No. 349B, in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Thihagoda in Matara
District, Southern Province and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 01
Name of  the land: Badulukele alias Medawattahena;
Description of the Land: Homestead, contains a part of a
ground reservoir which is being constructed by the
Government and mixed crop cultivation; Extent: 0.1373 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of  the land: Badulukele alias Medawattahena;
Description of the Land: Open waste land, contains a part of a
ground reservoir; Extent: 0.0023 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary's
Office, at Thihagoda on 03.05.2021 at 10.00 a.m.  and are hereby
requested to notify me in writing in duplicate on or before
30.04.2021, the nature of their interests in the land/servitude,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
UMASHA M ATHARAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Thihagoda.
Divisional Secretariat,
Thihagoda,
30th March, 2021.
