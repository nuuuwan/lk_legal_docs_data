I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by
Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby once again enlarge the time up to 28th February,
2022 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to
investigate and inquire into the various allegation and alleged corruption in Sri Lanka Customs, established under Section
2 of the said Act on 24th February, 2021 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2216/14 dated 24th
February, 2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, having enlarged of time ending on 30th May, 2021 by
five months from 30th May, 2021 up to 31st October, 2021 by Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2229/10 dated 29th May,
2021 in the exercise of powers vested in me.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.

Colombo 01,
