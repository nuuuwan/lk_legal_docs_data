FILLING OF A VACANCY IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER OF  THE  AYAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION 66A(1) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)

WHEREAS the office of return member of Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Demuni
Chandrasiri Wickramanayake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Madalagamage Pradeep Niroshana to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Madalagamage
Pradeep Niroshana was selected as the member of the Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
19th January, 2021.
