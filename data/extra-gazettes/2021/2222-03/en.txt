EXCISE   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13 OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended, I,
Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance do by this Order declare that,
(i) replace Item No. 2 of the Schedule II of the Order made under Section 3 of the above Act and published by the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2113/9 dated March 05, 2019, by substitution of the following Schedule A
hereto ; and
(ii) replace the Schedule III of the Order made under Section 3 of the above Act and published by the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2113/9 dated March 05, 2019, by substitution of the following Schedule B hereto.
        This Order shall come into effect from April 06, 2021.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
