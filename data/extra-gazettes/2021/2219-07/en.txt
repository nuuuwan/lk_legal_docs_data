PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNME NT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MANTHAI    WEST    PRADESHIYA 
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Iyathurai 
Jeganathan the member belonged, was required by me u nder Section  66A(1) of th e aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  
returned member of  Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of 
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of 
thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Rasathurai Rajendren to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, J. Jenittan, Returning Officer  of  the Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 
66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasathurai Rajendren was selected 
as the member of  the Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
