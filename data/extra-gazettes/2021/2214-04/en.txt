THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the CINEC Campus (Private)
Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number
PV 17745 being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order made under
the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2208/1 of December 28, 2020 by the addition immediately after the
entry “Master of Business Administration by course work” appearing in Column I and its abbreviation appearing in the
corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively in the Schedule II
hereto, subject to the conditions specified in the Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE   I
