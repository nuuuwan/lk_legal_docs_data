Order under Section 4(1)

by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes-Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the employees of Grade 8.1, 8.2 and 9 of Sri Lankan Air lines Limited have been caused injustice in the 
payment of salary increments for year 2016 and 2018, the Travel ling Allowance and Professional Allowances 
(Professional Installment Allowance, Allowance on Professional Demand, In-charge Management Allowance and 
Consultant Allowance), Holiday Incentives, Salary for the 13th month and in the issue of free Airline Tickets and if 
such injustice had been caused to what relief the employees are  entitled and futher, whether the salary scales of these 
employees should be increased in comparison with the other desi gnations of the institution, and if so, in what amount.
