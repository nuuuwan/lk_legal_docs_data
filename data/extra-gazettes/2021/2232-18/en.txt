Local Authorities Election Ordinance Notices
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A (1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Karuwalagaswewa   Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer of the Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A
(1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Chandi Prabodha
of the Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as she has been
ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
N
AGALINGAM REGAN,
Returning Officer,
Karuwalagaswewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam.
