Notification
The provisions of section 02 and 17 of the Minutes on Pensions amended from time to time, further amended by removing 
the added clause “The age limit for compulsory retirement of medical officers in the Public Service will be extended to 63 
years” and subsequently adding.
“The compulsory retirement age of the officers serving in the following government pensionable posts has been amended 
as follows :–
 Post Age limit for compulsory retirement Effective Date
Medical Officers 63 05.04.2021
Dental Surgeon Officers 63 05.04.2021
Nursing Officers 63 14.06.2021”
