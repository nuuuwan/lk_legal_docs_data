LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (b)
WELIGAMA URBAN COUNCIL
I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi Returning Ofﬁcer of the Weligama Urban Council, do hereby declare under Section 
10A (1) (b) of the Local Authorities Elections ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mrs. Muhammadu Sahidu Siththi Mashhura a 
candidate whose name appears in the nomination paper submitted by United National Party for Weligama Urban Council at 
the Local Authorities Election 2018 has forfeited her rights to have her name retained in the nomination paper of the United 
National Party as she has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
 T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
 Returning Ofﬁcer,
 Weligama Urban Council. 
District Election Ofﬁce,
Matara,
02nd November, 2021.
