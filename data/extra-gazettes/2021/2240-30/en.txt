CONSumER AffAIRS AuThORITY ACT, No. 09 Of 2003
Order under Section 20(5)
acTiNg  under the powers vested in  it by section 20(5) of the consumer affairs authority act, No. 09 of 2003, the 
consumer affairs authority Orders that no Medical  Test Laboratory of a hospital or a Medical Test Laboratory center shall 
charge, or levy any charges or fees directly or indirectly for conducting of the Laboratory  Tests specified hereunder, above 
the Maximum charge or fee specified herein.
Type of Laboratory Test Maximum Charge/Fee
Polymerase chain reaction (Pcr) rs. 6,500 per Test and report
rapid antigen Test (ra T) rs. 2,000 per Test and report
This Order shall come into effect from 12th august, 2021.
