Order No. 77
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,  NO.  9  OF  2003
Order under Section  20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no Importer, supplier, producer, distributor,  or trader shall sell, supply, expose, or
offer for sale, display for sale the varieties of rice listed below, above the Maximum Retail Prices given hereunder.
Variety Maximum Retail
Price
Keeri Samba Rs. 125/- per kg.
White/Red Samba - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Suduru Samba) Rs. 103/- per kg.
White/Red Nadu - Steamed/Boiled (excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari) Rs. 98/- per kg.
White/Red Raw Rice Rs. 95/- per kg.
