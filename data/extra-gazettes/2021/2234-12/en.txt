7.1 Ratio of recruitment to Grade III
Serial No. Section No. How to amend

An officer who holds a post of Agricultural Instructor and has completed at
least ten (10) years active and satisfactory period of service in Sri Lanka
Technological Service or Provincial Technological Service.
An officer of the Associate Officer Service category who has fulfilled the
qualification prescribed in 7.2.1.1 above and has completed five (05) years
active and satisfactory period of service in the Department of Agriculture or
Provincial Departments of Agriculture directly involving in or involved in the
duties/ responsibilities of the Department of Agriculture or Provincial
Departments of Agriculture.
An officer of the Associate Officer Service category who has fulfilled the
qualification prescribed in 7.2.1.1 above and has completed five (05) years
active and satisfactory period of service in the Department of Agriculture or
Provincial Departments of Agriculture directly involving in or involved in the
duties/ responsibilities of the Department of Agriculture or Provincial
Departments of Agriculture.
An officer who holds a post of Research Assistant, Soil Surveyor or Land
Evaluation officer and has completed a minimum period of ten (10) years
active and satisfactory service in Sri Lanka Technological Service.
An officer of the Associate Officer Service category who has fulfilled the
qualification prescribed in 7.2.1.1 above and has completed five (05) years
active and satisfactory period of service in the Department of Agriculture or
Provincial Departments of Agriculture directly involving in or involved in the
duties/ responsibilities of the Department of Agriculture or Provincial
Departments of Agriculture.
