PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections  Ordinance
1A - PG 5292— 28 (02/2021)
(CHAPTER  262)   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notification under Section 66 g
IT  is hereby announced that the first meeting to elect the Deputy Mayor of the Matale Municipal Council in the Matale
District of the Central Province as per the powers vested in me under Section 66 g of the amended Local Government
Elections Ordinance of the Local Government Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 shall be held at the Matale
Municipal Council Meeting Hall at 10.00 a. m. on 16th February 2021 at the venue mentioned therein.
H. M. M. U. B. H
ERATH,
Commissioner,
Department of Local Government,
Central Province.
Provincial Council Complex,
Pallekele
Kundasale,
27th January, 2021.
