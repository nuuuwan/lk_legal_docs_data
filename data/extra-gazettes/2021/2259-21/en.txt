Whereas the office of return member of  Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which 
Wijesinghe Mudiyanselage Lasantha Wijesinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as 
a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Quvintas Ciril Keegal to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Ratnayake, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala 
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby 
declare that Quvintas Ciril Keegal was selected as the member of the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
