THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage  Chandrasena, Minister of Lands,
do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
SCHEDULE
The allotments of below mentioned land, depicted in Advance
Tracing No. CO/DSO/2017/524 and dated 20.06.2019, prepared
by the Survey General, situated in the village of Welikada
(Ward No. 3, Welikada, within Municipal Council limits of Sri
Jayawardhanepura Kotte) in the Grama Niladhari Division of
514B, Rajagiriya in the Divisional Secretary Division of Sri
Jayawardhanepura Kotte in Colombo District in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' -  Assmt. No. 1062/1 (a part) Old Kotte Road -
Extent: About 0.0004  Hectare
North:  Old Kotte Road and Lot No. 131 in Preliminary Plan
No. Co. 9646; East: Lot No 131 in Preliminary Plan No. Co.
9646 and The Land which Assmt. No. 1062/1 (a part) in Old
Kotte Road; South: Lot No. 131 in Preliminary Plan No. Co.
9646 and The Land which Assmt. No. 1062/1 (a part) in Old
Kotte Road; West: Old Kotte Road.
S. M. Chandrasena,
