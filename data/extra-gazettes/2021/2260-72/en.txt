SPECIAL  COmmODITY  LEvY  ACT,   No.  48  Of  2007
Order under Section 2
by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, i, Prof. G. L. 
Peiris, Acting minister of finance of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose in respect 
of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the rate specified in the  
corresponding entry in Column ii of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from January 01, 2022 subject to provisions of 
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the 
Extraordinary Gazette  Notification No. 2249/16 of October 13, 2021 in respect of Potatoes (HS Code 0701.90) and 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2244/8 of September 07, 2021 in respect of B’Onions (HS Code 0703.10.20) are 
rescinded with effect from January 01, 2022.
 
 Prof
