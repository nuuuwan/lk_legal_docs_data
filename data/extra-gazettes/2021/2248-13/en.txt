FILLING   OF   A   VAcANcY   IN    ThE   OFFIcE   OF   MEMbER   OF   ThE   VALIkAMAM  
EAST  PRADEShIYA  SAbhA   uNDER   SEcTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   ThE   LOcAL   AuThORITIES   
ELEcTIONS   ORDINANcE     (chAPTER   262)      
WhereAs the office of  Member of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three 
consecutive meeting without permissions  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which Elakunathan 
Senthuran the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon 
the member of  that ward 16 - Kopay North of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not 
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected 
as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Valli Sivagnanam to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting 
in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Valli 
Sivagnanam was elected as the member of the ward 16 - Kopay North of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
  
 
R. C. AmAlRAAj,
 Returning Officer,
 Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
30th September, 2021.
