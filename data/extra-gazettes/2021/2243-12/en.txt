WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
The Inquiry being conducted under Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989
I,  Roshan Goonetileke, the Governor of the Western Province do hereby announce that the  period of  investigation of
single person inquiry Board comprise of retired Judge of the Court of Appeal Hon. Chandradasa Nanayakkara, appointed
by Extraordinary  Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 2152/14 dated 3rd December, 2019 to
inquiry   whether  the offences stipulated under Sub section 184(1) of the aforesaid Urban Councils  Ordinance have been
committed  by Wattala - Mabola Urban Council and by the Chairman of Wattala - Mabola Urban Council while functioning
in his office  and submit a report within 03 months shall be extended until 03rd December, 2021 according to the powers
vested in me in terms of Section 184(1A) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.

OSHAN  G OONETILEKE,
