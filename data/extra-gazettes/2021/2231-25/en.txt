1A-  PG  5414— 225 (06/2021)
Direction 81
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Special Direction under Section, 10(l)(b)(ii)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10( 1 )(b)(ii) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 ol 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no maize collector, store owner, distributor or whole seller, who has in his
possession, custody or under his control the maize in any warehouses, silos, stores, containers and any other place unless
such warehouse, silo, store, container and any other place is registered with the Consumer Affairs Authority.
Further, the Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no maize collector, store owner, distributor or whole seller
shall keep maize under his possession without being registered with the Consumer Affairs Authority and shall forthwith
furnish information on demand to the Consumer Affairs Authority on the stock of maize in bulk or in any other form in
such warehouse, silo, store, container and any other place.
The Consumer Affairs Authority directs all existing maize collectors, store owners, distributors or whole sellers
shall register within 07 days of this Direction and shall furnish information forthwith on the stock of maize in their
possession in bulk or any other form.
This direction shall not apply to maize cultivators in respect of their maize harvest from their cultivation.
This Direction shall come into effect from 11th June 2021.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Maj. Gen. S
HANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman.
Colombo,
11th June, 2021.
