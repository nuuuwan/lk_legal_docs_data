THE   UNIVERSITIES    ACT,   No. 16    OF   1978
Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Professor G . L. Peiris, Minister
of Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this  Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as last
amended by Order publishied in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2192/64 of September 11, 2020) establishing the University of
Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, by the repeal of the item “Faculty of Management and Finance” appearing in Column I of such Schedule
and its  corresponding entries appearing in Column II thereto and the substitution therefore of the following : -
Column I Column II
Faculty Departments
“Faculty of Management and Finance 1. Department of Management and Entrepreneurship
2. Department of Marketing
3. Department of Human Resource Management
4. Department of Business Management
5. Department of Accountancy
6. Department of Finance”
Prof. G .  L.  P
EIRIS,
Minister of Education.
Colombo,
25th February, 2021.
