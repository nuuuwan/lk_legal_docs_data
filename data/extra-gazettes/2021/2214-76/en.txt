EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
Ref. No. of Ministry of Lands: 4-3/11/2016/UDA/524. 
Ref. No. of Divisional Secretary: Ben/2/7/3/22.  
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
  Description of the Land or Servitude to be  acquired:  
 
 
Two Allotments of land described below named as 
"Baliwala Kumbura" and "Andryawela" in total extent of 
1.4907 Hectares (03 A., 02 R., 29.4 P.) and marked as lot 
No. 01 and 02 in basic plan No. 4530 dated 31.08.2020 
made by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of 
'Pahurumulla' in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 01 
Pahurumulla in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Bentota, District of Galle, Southern Province. The details 
of the land are as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: "Baliwala Kumbura"; Current land 
use: Open Barren Land; Claimant: Government; Original 
Claimants: J. P. Rosalin Nona, of Pitaramba Bentota,                
A. D. Amarasekara of No. 149, Galle Road, Bentota,               
H. W. Upali of Pitaramba Bentota, K. G. Adlin 
Ariyawathie of Pitaramba Bentota, A. M. Mangalika 
Jayasekara of Pitaramba, Bentota, W. Wijesekara of No. 
51, Thotupala Road, Kalutara North, T. Jemis Singho, L. 
Gnanawathie, Shelton Asularachchi, I. T. Karunawathie, 
Dulsi Indra Jayasinghe and Karunawathie Abeysundara 
of Angoda, Bentota. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
Name  of the Land: "Andrayawela"; Current Land 
Use: Open Barren land; Claimant: Government, enjoyed 
by the Pradeshiya Sabhawa Bentota.
