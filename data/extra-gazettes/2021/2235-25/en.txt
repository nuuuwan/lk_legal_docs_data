I, G.A.M.S.P.Ambanwala, Commissioner of Local Government, Uva Province, hereby announce that, in accordance with

to ﬁll the vacant post of Chairman of the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha located in Uva Province, will be convened on the 
14
