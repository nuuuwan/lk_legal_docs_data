Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the National Environmental (Plastic Material Indentification Standards)
Regulations No. 01 of 2021.
2. Any manufactured plastic item shall be marked clearly in accordance with the Plastic Material Identification
Standards specified in the Schedule hereto.
3. A plastic item manufactured using the material specified in Column I in the Schedule hereto, which is abbreviated
in Column II, shall bear either one of the three symbol options specified in the corresponding entry in Column III of that
Schedule.
