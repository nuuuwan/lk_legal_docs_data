PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL 
Imposing Entertainment Tax at Kadugannawa Urban Council  
IT is hereby published that, I, Lalith u. Gamage, Governor of Central Province, have approved the below mentioned reso-
lution as per the powers vested in me by the subsection 2 of the entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) decided under 
decision No. (e) 2 (II) of the General Assembly held on 23.07.2020 at the Kadugannawa urban Council in accordance with 
the powers vested in it by sub-section (I) of section 2 of the aforesaid entertainment Tax Ordinance.
 
LaLith U. GamaGe (Attorney-at-Law)
 Governor,
 Central Province.
At the Governor’s Office,
Central Province,
On this 18th day of January, 2021.
ResOLuTION
In accordance with the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 2 of the entertainment Tax Ordinance (Au-
thority 267), I suggest that the Kadugannawa urban Council shall levy an equivalent tax of 10% on any payment (excluding 
entertainment tax) made for entering a certain recreational activity as specified in the said Ordinance in areas within the 
administrative limits of the Urban Council and I also propose that this resolution shall be implemented from the first day of 
the month following the month in which it is published in the Gazette.
