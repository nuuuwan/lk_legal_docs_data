PART IV (B) - LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices Under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY   IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  GALGAMUWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  66A(1)  OF THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)

WHEREAS  the Office of return member of Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death.
And  Whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Udagedara
Sisira Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter  262) and who shall be declared Selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30 days.
And  Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Herath Mudiyanselage Jayantha Herath to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Harendra Bandara Rathnayake, returning officer of the Galg amuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Herath Mudiyanselage Jayantha Herath was selected as the member of the Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. M. H. B. RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Galgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
29th November 2021.
