FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     PASBAGE  KORALE
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   ceased to be
a member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kobewatta
Arachchige Rashika Thushani Kobewatta the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  that Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Napana Mudiyanselage Susila Damayanthi Menike to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa , Returning Officer  of  the Pasbage
Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare  that Napana Mudiyanselage Susila Damayanthi Menike was selected as the member of the Pasbage
Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
29th of June, 2021.

Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare  that Karahadungala Ovita Gedara Kusumlatha was elected as the member of the Ward Number 08
