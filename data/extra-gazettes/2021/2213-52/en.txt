NOTICE   BY   THE   DIRECTOR   GENERAL   OF   CIVIL   AVIATION   UNDER   THE   AIR   NA VIGA TION
REGULATION    No.  196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that this office has received on 26th January, 2021 an application dated 26th
January, 2021 from Airways of Colombo (Pvt) Ltd, 23B, No.07, Perera Mawatha, Rajagiriya, seeking an Air Operator Certificate
and Airline Licence to engage in International Charter operations carrying Cargo, Mail and Private Operations in compliance
with Civil Aviation Act and Regulations made thereunder. Business name of the company is Airways of Colombo (Pvt) Ltd
with the trade name as “Air Colombo”. The Directors of the Airways of Colombo (Pvt) Ltd are as follows.
Mr. Benedict Sirimanne
Mr. Rajitha Ariyaratne
Airways of Colombo (Pvt) Ltd has nominated Mr. Rajitha Ariyaratne to be the Accountable Manager in charge of
the proposed operations.
A copy of the aforementioned application is kept at the Technical Library, 04th
 Floor, of Head Quarters of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 152/1, Minuwangoda Road, Katunayake, for perusal of the members of the public, during
office hours of any working days of the week.
