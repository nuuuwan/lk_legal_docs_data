FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBERS  OF   THE     KATTANKUDY   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(1a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Front for Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Kachchi Muhammadu Ummu Salma member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(1a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of  Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of the local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be
declared selected as  a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Abdul Munaf Fahmiya to fill such vacancy ;
THEREFORE, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Munaf
Fahmiya was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
23rd December, 2021.
