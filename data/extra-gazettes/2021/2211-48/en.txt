PART IV (B) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 08.12.2020
1A
WHEREAS the office of a return member of Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party.
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Janasetha Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Wadu Mesthrige
Thilak Pushpalal the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Hendawasam Thushara Prasantha to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hendawasam Thushara
Prasantha was selected as the member of the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K.U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
19th January, 2021.
