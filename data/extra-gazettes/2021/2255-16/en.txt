   The starting point is the junction of the southern and western boundaries of the land bearing Lot No. 32 of Tracing No. HTSS 
2004/841 of the Survey General.

   A distance of 552 metres respectively to the Northeast and Southeast along the Northern boundary of the aforesaid land Lot
