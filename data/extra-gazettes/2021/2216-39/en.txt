THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. G . L. Peiris, the Minister
of Education having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Sri Lanka Institute of
Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007
and bearing Registration Number GL 24, being recognized as a Degree Awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the
Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2120/6 of April 23, 2019 by the
addition immediately after the entry ‘’Bachelor of Science Honours in Biotechnology" appearing in Column I and its abbreviation
appearing in the corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively in the
Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :–
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
1. In respect of the course of study titled the Bachelor of Science Honours in Financial Mathematics and Applied
Statistics (SLQF Level 6), the minimum entry qualification for admitting a student shall be three (03) simple passes (S)
in the Physical Science subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) Examination conducted
by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
