Order No. 72
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.  09  OF 2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 71 in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2201/8 dated 10th November, 2020 and
do by this Order remove “ Dried Chilies - Neither crushed nor ground ” from Order No. 39 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1975/68 on 14.07.2016, “Potatoes (imported) ” from Order No. 40 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2003/44 on
27.01.2017 and “Keeri Samba” from Order No. 66 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2177/9 on 28.05.2020.
This Order shall come into effect from 08th February, 2021.
                                                                          By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
MAJ. GEN. SHANTHA DISSANAYAKE (Retd.),
Chairman,
Colombo,
08th February, 2021.
