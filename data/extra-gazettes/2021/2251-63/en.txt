Regulations
The Motor Traffic (Driving Schools, Driving Instructors and Assistant Driving Instructors) Regulations No. 1 of 2015
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1939/4 of November 2, 2015 are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of regulation 8 and the substitution therefor, of the following:-
“8. Where a person has completed the training at a Driving School, a Certificate in the form as may be
determined by the Commissioner General shall be issued to such person to the effect that the training
has been successfully completed.”.

(3) by the repeal of items (iii) and (iv) of paragraph (c) of regulation 14, and the substitution therefor of the following:-
“(iii) Driving Instructor class “C”
Driving Licence for classes “B”, “Bl” and “A”:
Provided that, an applicant who possesses a valid Driving Licence, obtained at least five years prior to
the date of application may be issued a class ‘C’ Instructor’s Licence, authorizing such person to be an
instructor for any one or more Driving Licence classes classified as ‘B’, “B1’ or ‘A’ for which he possesses
qualifications.
(iv) Assistant Driving Instructor
Driving Licence for classes “B”,  “B1”  and “A”:
Provided that, an applicant who possesses a valid Driving Licence, obtained at least five years prior to
the date of application may be issued an Assistant Driving Instructor’s Licence, authorizing such person to
be an Assistant Instructor in any one or more Driving Licence classes classified as ‘B’, ‘B1’ or ‘A’ for which
he possesses qualifications.”.
(4) in regulation 16, by the repeal of paragraph (a), and the substitution therefor of the following:-
(a) “Any person who applies for a Driving Instructor’s Licence shall -
(i) possess a minimum qualification of having passed the G.C.E. (Ordinary Level) Examination in six
subjects including Mathematics and Sinhala or Tamil language obtaining two credit passes in not
more than two sittings or an equivalent qualification; or
(ii) possess the Certificate of NVQ Level III recognized by the Tertiary and V ocational Education
Commission; or
(iii) have experience as an Assistant Driving Instructor for five years prior to the date of application.”.
