Whereas the office of  member of  Embilipitiya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of removal of the president 
from his post by the Governor under Section 184 of the Urban Council Act  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Wawulanbokka 
Gamage Lalith Dharma Keerthi the member belonged,  was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid 
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 03 Kalagediara of Embilipitiya Urban Council who belongs to 
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall 
be declared as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate 
Jagoda Arachchige Kelumchandra to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Embilipitiya Urban Council, acting in terms 
of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jagoda Arachchige 
Kelumchandra  was elected as the member of the Ward No. 03 Kalagediara of the Embilipitiya Urban Council.
