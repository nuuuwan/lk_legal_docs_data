FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MUTHUR   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignationa ;
And whereas the Secretary of the National Congress,  the recognized political party to which Sehuthampi Sahufar
the member belonged was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member
of  Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities
Elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as  a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Yubunu Suhooth Mukammadu Zihan to fill such vacancy ;
THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Y ubunu Suhooth
Mukammadu  Zihan was selected as the member of the Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
ANGARAPILLAI  S UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Muthur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
24th December, 2021.
