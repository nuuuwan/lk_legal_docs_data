NOTIFICATION UNDER SECTION 16 SUB - SECTIONS (2D) OF THE COAST CONSERVATION AND 
COASTAL RESOURCE MANAGEMENT ACT, No. 57 OF 1981 
 
Proposed 652 Roomed Marino Tower City Hotel Project - No. 594, Galle Road, Colombo 03 
 
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT 
 
 
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed 652 Roomed Marino 
Tower City Hotel Project has been submitted by the Damro Leisure (Pvt.) Ltd, No. 361, Kandy Road, Nittambuwa, as 
required by the Section 16 (1) of the Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Act No. 57 of 1981 as 
amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following 
institutions from 8.30 a.m. to 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 24.11.2021 (Except weekends and public holidays). 
 
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official web site (www.coastal.gov.lk) of the Department 
of Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management. 
 
 (1) Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor, 
Maligawatte, Colombo 10. 
 (2) Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa” Denzil Kobbakaduwa Mawatha, 
Battaramulla.
