I. District	Election	Office	
Colombo district

  2.		 Where	a	person	who	is	qualified	in	accordance	with	the	said	Registration	of	Electors	Act	to	have	his	name	entered	
in the register of electors for the above Electoral district and whose name has been omitted from such register and from 
List “B” or whose name has been included in the List “a” and who claims to have his name inserted or retained as the case 
may	be,	in	the	register,	that	person	or	any	other	person	on	his	behalf	may	make	a	claim	in	writing	to	the	Registering	Officer,	
Election	Office,	Colombo	substantially	in	From	“A”	and	“B”	as	the	case	may	be	specified	in	the	Schedule	to	the	said	Act.	
Every	such	application	should	reach	the	Registering	Officer,	Colombo	within	four	weeks	from	the	date	of	publication	of	this	
notice in the Gazette of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka. (From 05th january 2021 to 01st February 2021)
  3.  any person whose name appears in the register of Electors or the List “B” for the above Electoral district may object 
to the inclusion in such register of his own name or the name of any other person appearing in such register or List “B” or to 
the	insertion	or	retention	of	the	name	of	any	claimant	in	the	register,	in	duplicate	to	the	Registering	Officer,	Election	Officer,	 	
Colombo substantially	in	Form:	“C”	or	“D”	as	the	case	may	be	specified	in	the	Schedule	to	the	said	Act.	Every	objection	to	
the	inclusion	of	any	name	in	the	register	should	reach	the	Registering	Officer,	Electrions	Office,	Colombo
  within four weeks 
from the date of publication of this notice in the Gazette of the democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka, Every objection 
in	the	insertion	or	retention	of	the	name	of	claimant	in	the	register	should	reach	the	Registering	Officer	within	fourteen	days	
from	the	date	on	which	a	notice	containing	the	names	of	claimants	is	exhibited	at	the	Elections	Office,	Colombo.
	 	 	This	notice	will	be	exhibited	at	the	Elections	Office	Colombo	on	19th	January	2021	and	02nd	February	2021.
  4.  Forms for the purpose of making any claim or objection to a claim made by a person to the insertion or retention of 
the	name	of	any	other	person	in	the	register	may	be	obtained	from	the	above	Elections	Office,	each	Divisional	Secretariat,	
each	Grama	Niladhari’s	Office	and	office	of	each	Local	Authority	in	the	Administrative	District	of	Colombo.
