UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice of the Hon. Governor of Uva Province
INCONSISTENT with the Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, should be read with the Section 2 of the 
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, by virtue of powers vested on me through the Section 
2(3) (a) (I) of the Statute No. 2 of 2011 regarding the powers to monitor the administration, identi ﬁ ed as the Statute No. 2 
of 2011 of the Uva Provincial Council imposed to make provisions with regard to implementation of powers to monitor 
administration in Local Government Authorities established in Uva Province and matters relate and incidental thereto 
I,  A. J. M. Muzammil, the Governor of the Uva Province temporarily suspended the function of the Hon. Chairman of 
Kandeketiya Pradeshiya Sabha of Badulla District in Uva Province with effect from the 08th of March 2021 and appointed  
Mr. H. M.R. Anura Kumara Judicial Ofﬁ cer, retired Justice to investigate and report to me through the notiﬁ cation No. 2217/45 
dated 05.03.2021 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Considering the request of the Investigation Ofﬁ cer to extend the investigation period, I hereby inform that, the period 
of submission of the investigation report has been extended to 15.07.2021 considering the travel restrictions in accordance 
with the current situation of the Covid pandemic in the country and the imposed health guidelines. 
A. J. M. M
UZAMMIL,
Governor of Uva Province.
At the Uva Province Governor's Ofﬁ ce,
28th of May, 2021.
