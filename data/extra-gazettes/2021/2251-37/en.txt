I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of powers vested in me by
Section 4 of the Special Presidential Commissions of Inquiry Act No. 7 of 1978 of the National State Assembly which was
amended by Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Act (Special Provisions) No. 4 of 1978, do hereby once again enlarge
the period of time up to 28th April, 2022 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Special
Presidential Commission of Inquiry established under Section 2 of the said Act on 28th January 2021 by Notification
published in the Gazette  Extraordinary No. 2212/53 dated  29th January 2021 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, having enlarged the three months period of time ending on 28th April, 2021 by six months from 28th April, 2021 up to
28th October, 2021 by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2225/48 dated 29th April, 2021 in the exercise of powers vested in me.
Gotabaya  Rajapaksa,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
October 28, 2021.
