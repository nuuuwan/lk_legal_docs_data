And whereas, Hon. R.M. Sobhitha Rajakaruna Esquire, Judge of the Court of Appeal, who is the member of the said
Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry, has relinquished from the post of Member of the said Commission,
And, whereas, the need to fill the vacancy thus caused has been identified,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of   Sri Lanka, in accordance with the
provisions of Section 3 of the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry Act, No. 7 of 1978, as amended by the Special
Presidential Commission of Inquiry (Special Provisions) Act, No. 4 of 1978 appoint Hon. Balendren Sasi Mahendren Esquire,
Judge of the Court of Appeal, as a Member of the Special Presidential Commission of Inquiry established in terms of the
aforesaid Warrant, reposing great trust and confidence in his prudence, ability and fidelity.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka on this 05th day of November,
Two thousand and Twenty one.
By His Excellency’s Command,
 P. B. J
AYASUNDERA.
