THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/6/2016/HW/446.
 My Ref. No. : Wa/La/02/Acq/769.
Wa/La/2/2796
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that  the Government  intends  to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
The allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0065 Hectare,
in Final Village Plan No. 112, Suppliment No. 120, Sheet
No. 140 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the
Village of Pelawatta in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Walallawita in Kalutara District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 2746
Name of  land: Hettigodawatta; Description: Waste Land;
Claimant:  State;  Original Claimant: - I. W. M. H. K.
Wickramathilaka; Extent: 0.0065 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of Walallawita on
the 22nd March, 2021  at  10.30 a.m  and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 15th
March, 2021 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
M. RANJAN P. PERERA,
Divisional Secretary,
Walallawita.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Walallawita.
