FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     PANADURA    URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Panadura Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of the
party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kalugalage Anura
Vijayapala the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of  that Ward No. 05 - Walapala Pattiya of Panadura Urban Council  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ranasinghe Arachchilage Harsha Mihirani Ranasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Panadura
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare  that Ranasinghe Arachchilage Harsha Mihirani Ranasinghe was elected as the member of the Ward No. 05
- Walapala Pattiya of Panadura Urban Council.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
05th July, 2021.
