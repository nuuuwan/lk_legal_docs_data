RESOLUTION
In accordance with the powers conferred by Sub section (1) of section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Authority 267),
I suggest that the Medadumbara Pradeshiya Sabha should levy an equivalent tax of  10% on any payment (excluding
entertainment tax) made for entering  a certain recreational activity as specified in the said Ordinance in areas within the
administrative limits of the Council and I also propose that this standard should be implemented from the first day of the month
following the month in which it is published in the Gazette.

CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Imposing Entertainment Tax at Panwila  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Lalith U. Gamage, Governor of Central Province, hereby declare that I have approved the below mentioned resolution
as per the powers  vested in me by the Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) decided under decision
No. e (01) iii  of the General Meeting  held on 19.08.2020 at the Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha in accordance with the powers
vested in it by Sub - section (I) of Section 2 of  the aforesaid Entertainment Tax  Ordinance.
LALITH U. GAMAGE, (Attorney - at -Law)
Governor,
Central Province.
On this 13th day of November, 2020,
At the Governor's Office,
Central Province.
Resolution
In accordance with the powers vested on me by  Section 8 (1) of  Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, I suggest that the
Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha  shall  levy an entertainment tax of 10% of the ticket price for all public shows except religious,
educational and charitable activities held in the Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha area as per entertainment  Tax amendment Act,
No. 37 of 1984 and Section 2(1) of  Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 ( Chapter 267).
