SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto the Special Commodity Levy at  rates specified in the corresponding
entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from April 14, 2021 subject to provisions of Sub
section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
April  13, 2021.1A-  PG 5385— 550  (04/2021)
