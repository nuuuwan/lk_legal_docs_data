PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34402— 35  (06/2021)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Mr. Loku Ederage Sandun Rathnayaka
of the Welivitiya - Divithura  Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has
been ceased to be a member of the Janasetha Peramuna .
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
07th June, 2021.
