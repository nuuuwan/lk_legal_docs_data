FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE    RAJANGANAYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kamburapola
Gamlathge Nandasiri the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the returned member of Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Liyanna Waththage Sunny Silva to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kangatharan Mugunthan, Returning Officer  of  the Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Liyanna
Waththage Sunny Silva selected as the member of the  Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. M
UGUNTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
11th  May, 2021.
