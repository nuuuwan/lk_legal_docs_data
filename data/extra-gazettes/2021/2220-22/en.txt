PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34012— 35 (03/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha
MOHIDEEN Bawa Mohomed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kamburapola Gamlathge Nandasiri of
the Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as he has  ceased to be a
member of the People’s Liberation Front.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Rajanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
19th  March, 2021.
