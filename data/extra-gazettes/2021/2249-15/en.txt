THE  MINES  AND  MINERALS  ACT,  No.  33  OF 1992
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Environment under section 64 of the Mines and Minerals Act, No. 33 of 1992 read 
with section 34 of the aforesaid Act.
                                         Minister of Environment.
Colombo,
28th September 2021.
Regulations
The Mining (Licensing) Regulations, No. 1 of 1993 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 794/23 of November 26, 1993 
as amended, from time to time, are hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) in paragraph (6) of regulation 3, by the substitution for the words ''be valid for not more than one year" of the 
words “be valid for a minimum period of one year and a maximum period of three years based on available 
mineral reserves” ;
(2) in paragraph (3) (b) of regulation 5, by the substitution for the words "be valid for a period of not more than 
one year" of the words “be valid for a minimum period of one year and a maximum period of three years based 
on available mineral reserves.”.
