Greetings !
As you have been appointed as members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management in the
Eastern Province established by the Presidential Directive dated 01.06.2020 by exercising the powers vested in me by Article
33 of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And, whereas, Prof. Kapila Gunawardena, who is attached to the Medical Faculty of University of Peradeniya has
been appointed as the Secretary to the Ministry of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs,
And, whereas, it has been identified that the officers who have been appointed as the members of the said Task
Force, such as Director General of Archaeology, the Land Commissioner General, the Land Commissioner of the Eastern
Province and the Director General of the Civil Security Department, have retired or been transferred,
I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in accordance with the powers
vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby appoint below
mentioned officers as the members of the Presidential Task Force for Archaeological Heritage Management in the Eastern
Province established under the aforesaid Presidential Directive in place of the members of the said Task Force referred to at
8, 9, 12, 14 and 16, by reposing great trust, and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity.
1. Prof. Kapila Gunawardena Esquire
Secretary, Ministry of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs
2. Senior Prof. Anura Manathunga Esquire
Director General of Archaeology
3. Keerthi Gamage Esquire
Land Commissioner General
4. Major General (Retired) Nandana Senadheera Esquire
Director General of Civil Security Department
5. Ms. H. D. Asinsala Seneviratne
Provincial Land Commissioner, Eastern Province
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the 26 day of January Two
Thousand and Twenty One.
By His Excellency’s Command,
P.  B .  J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
