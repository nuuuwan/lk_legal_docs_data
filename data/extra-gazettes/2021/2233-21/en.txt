PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DEHIW ALA - MT.  LA VINIA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  the member of Dehiwala-Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kurukulasuriyage
Maldeva Suneetha Perera the  member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that number 04 Kalubowila of Dehiwala-Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Pattiyage Sarath Kumara Peiris to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Dehiwala-Mt. Lavinia Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Electioins Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Pattiyage Sarath Kumara Peiris was elected as the member of the number 04 Kalubowila of Dehiwala-
Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala-Mt. Lavinia Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
21st June, 2021.
