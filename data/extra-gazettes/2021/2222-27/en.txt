 NOTIFICATION   MADE  UNDER   LOCAL  GOVERNMENT   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  AS  AMENDED  BY  THE
(LOCAL  GOVERNMENT)  AMENDMENT  ACT,   No.  22  OF  2012
