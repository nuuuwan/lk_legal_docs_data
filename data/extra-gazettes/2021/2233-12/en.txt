THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 An allotments of lands named and extents as given below  
depicted in the Surveyor General’s Advance Tracing No. 
GA/BDD/2019/494 of 11.07.2019 situated in the Village of 
Halpathota in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Baddegama 
in Galle District, Southern Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Fedruwatte – Extent: 2.6689 Hectares
 North: Road; East: Road and Fedruwatte claimed by 
Irrigation Department; South: Fedruwatte claimed by 
Irrigation Department, Lot B of this plan, Fedruwatte 
claimed by M. K. Sriyani,  Fedruwatte claimed by Irrigation 
Department and Road; West: Fedruwatte claimed by Land 
Reform Commission and Road.
 02. Lot ‘B’ – Fedruwatte – Extent: 0.0258 Hectare
 North: Lot A of this plan; East: Lot A of this plan, 
Fedruwatte claimed by Irrigation Department and Fedruwatte 
claimed by M. K. Sriyani; South: Fedruwatte claimed by M. 
K. Sriyani; West: Fedruwatte claimed by M. K. Sriyani and 
Lot A of this plan.
 S. M. Chandrasena,
