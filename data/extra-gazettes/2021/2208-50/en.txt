FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN    OF   THE     HAPUTHALA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262) AS   AMENDED   BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION   (AMENDMENT)
ACT,   NO.  16  OF   2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman of Haputhala Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And whereas the  Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  Lama
Hewage Sampath Kumara Lamahewa,  the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that Haputhala Urban Council who belongs to such party and  who shall
be declared elected as  Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Upul Amarajeewa Dissanayaka to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of   Haputhala Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment)
Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Upul Amarajeewa Dissanayaka  elected as Chairman of  the  Haputhala Urban Council.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Haputhala Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Badulla,
31st  December, 2020.
