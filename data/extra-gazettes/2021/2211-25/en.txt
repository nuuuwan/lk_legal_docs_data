Local Government Notifications
NOTICE  MADE  UNDER  SECTION  66c  READ  WITH  SECTION 66g  OF  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  NO.  22  OF  2012
I, Alwala Dewage Prabath Indika Prasanna, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province, according to the
powers entrusted to me by Section 66c read with Section 66g of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, do hereby announce that a meeting in view of selecting a new
Chairman for the vacated Chairman’s position of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council of Western Province, will be convened
on February 01 of 2021 at 10.00 a.m. at the Urban Council Chamber of Katunayaka Seeduwa Urban Council located at No. 06,
Nawala Road, Rajagiriya.
A. D. P . I. P
RASANNA,
Commissioner of  Local Government,
Western Province.

at No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla
