1A-  G  34381— 35  (05/2021)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Order under Section 49 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 49 A of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) read with Article 44 of the
Constitution, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order,
declare that the land specified in the Schedule hereto is required by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka being a public
corporation established by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law, No. 4 of 1978.
G
OTABAYA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
10th May, 2021.
SCHEDULE
An allotment of land with an extent of 0A 0R 35P from out of the land in extent of 0A 3R 0P with the following
boundaries, claimed by Mr. A. P. Lalithasiri Pathirathna, situated within the Wathuwatta Grama Niladhari Division of
Udubaddawa Divisional Secretariat in Yagampattu Korale in Kurunegala District of North Western Province.
North : Kirimetiwala Canal and Paragahapiti Road (Ranawiru Sarayan Nilantha Wijeratna Mawatha)
East : Paragahapiti Road (Ranawiru Sarayan Nilantha Wijeratna Mawatha)
South : Land claimed by S P Sumanawathie
West : Land claimed by Wijayanthi Janaki
Extent : 0A., 0R., 35P.
