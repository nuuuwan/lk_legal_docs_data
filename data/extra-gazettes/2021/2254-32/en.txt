FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     BINGIRIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which
Muthunayaka Pedige Hemachandra Kularathna the member belonged was required by me under section 66A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon returned member of  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared
selected as  a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ilangaran Pedige Amila Kumara to fill such vacancy ;

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KATTANKUDY URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE     (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the Office of  return member of Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  the resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Muslim National Alliance,  the recognized political party to which Ahamadulevve
Muhammadu Marzook the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the returned  member of  Kattankudy Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as  a member within a period
of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized  political  party has communicated his decision to nominate
Peer Muhammadu Ameer Ali to fill such vacancy ;
I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Peer Muhammadu
Ameer Ali was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
16th November, 2021.
