No. 2240/7 –  MONDAY, AUGUST   09, 2021 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 
 Ref.  No.: MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/57. 
Ref.  No. of Land Ministry: 4-3/9/2017/WS/34. 
 
I, hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land  
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public  purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired :  
 
Part of land bearing Lot 01 depicted in the 
preliminary survey plan bearing No. MR2856 situated in 
the village of Katuwangoda, in Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Malimbada, Matara District, Southern 
Province is acquired for widening the entrance road to 
the land of Deep well and Pump Station under Matara 
Water Supply Level iv.  
 
Schedule  
 
01. Lot No. 1 - Name of land: Hompala Hena; 
Description: Home Garden; The Original Claimant:                  
Mr. K. M. Gunadasa; Extent: 0.0013 Hectare. 
 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear  personally, or by agents, duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat of Malimbada on the 
17th day of August, 2021 at 10.30 a. m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 10th day of August 2021, the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
                                              K. G. R. JAYANATH, 
                                             Divisional Secretary, 
                                                         Malimbada. 
   
30 day of August, 2021. 
At  Divisional Secretariat,  
Malimbada.
