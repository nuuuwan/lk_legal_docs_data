CARRIAGE BY AIR ACT, No. 29 OF 2018
Order under Section 3 (2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 (2) of the Carriage by Air Act, No. 29 of 2018, I, Prasanna Ranatunga,
Minister of Tourism, do by this Order notify that the States and territories of the States specified in the Schedule hereto shall
be the States and territories of the States Parties to the Convention for the Unification of Certain Rules for International
Carriage by Air.
P
RASANNA R ANATUNGA,
Minister of Tourism,
Colombo,
06th January, 2021.
