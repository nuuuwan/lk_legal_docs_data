 PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING,  SRI LANKA.
STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   ACT,  No.  14   OF   2008
Order under Sub Section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub Section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14
of 2008 as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, further to the Order published under Sub Section (4) of
Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No.1867/5 dated June 16, 2014, as amended by the subsequent Order published
under Sub-Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2051/38 dated December 29, 2017 in respect of
Avic International Hotels Lanka Limited, do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No.1867/5 dated June 16, 2014, as amended by
the subsequent Order published under Sub-Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2051/38
dated December 29, 2017 is hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to
implement the Project for a further period of Nineteen (19) months from March 25, 2020
“(4) The aforesaid “Project to undertake Mixed Development Project with an apartment complex at No. 418,
420 and 422, R.A. De Mel Mawatha, Colombo 03 in the Western Province” shall commence commercial
operation on October 24, 2021 without extending the duty free facility for the construction related
items”.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA, MP,
Minister of Finance.
