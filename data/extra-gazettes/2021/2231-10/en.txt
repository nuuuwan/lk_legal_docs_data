FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     WENNAPPUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And whereas the Leader of the Independent Group 1, to which Hetti Achchige Wimal Rohitha Susantha Perera the
member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon  returned
member of  Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  has communicated his decision to nominate
Iddamalgoda Bandaranayaka Sem Harishnath Sarathchandra to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Electioins Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Iddamalgoda
Bandaranayaka Sem Harishnath Sarathchandra  was selected as the member of the Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Wennappuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
08th June, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE    EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 09.06.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     MILLANIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Udawattage
Upul Priyadharshana Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon  returned member of  Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Malavige Jagath Thushara Hemantha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the Millaniya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Electioins Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare  that Malavige Jagath Thushara Hemantha was selected as the member of the Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
08th June, 2021.
