The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN   OF   THE     PADIYATHALAWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDED)
ACT,   No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Padiyathalawa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  office of the
Chairman shall be deemed to have been resign under Section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by
No. 21 of 2012 (Special Provisions) Local Authority Act ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Herath
Mudiyanselage Lalantha Sumith Senevirathna, the  former Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of  Padiyathalawa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as  Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Konara Mudiyanselage Dinesh Parakrama to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the Padiyathalawa Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as ameded by Local
Authorities Election (amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Konara Mudiyanselage Dinesh Parakrama
elected as Chairman of the Padiyathalawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Padiyathalawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
29th June, 2021.
