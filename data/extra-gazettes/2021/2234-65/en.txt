FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     KELANIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Dona Anne
Kamalie Maduragoda the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon returned member of  Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Bulathwelage Kalani Wijethunga to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Kelaniya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Bulathwelage Kalani Wijethunga was selected as the member of the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
02nd July, 2021.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     BELIATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a
member of the party;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kotte Hewage
Janaka Krishantha the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned member of  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Gamage Dinesh Anurudda to fill such vacancy ;
Therefore, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms
of Section 66A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  that Hewa Gamage
Dinesh Anurudda was selected as the member of the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
AVINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambantota,
02nd July, 2021.
