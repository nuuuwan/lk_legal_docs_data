Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A(1)(a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
MAHARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Nalawattage Ranil Chamindha
Kumar Pintu of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has Vacated his office of member of that Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha as he has
been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
19th July, 2021.
