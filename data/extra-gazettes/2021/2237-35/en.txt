THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 An allotment of Land called Martinwatta depicted as Lot 
‘A’ in the Advance Tracing No. PU/ARA/2012/204 dated 18th 
of October 2012 made by the Superintendent of Surveys in 
charge of the Division of Puttalam and situated in the village 
Siththa Madama village in Anavilundan Minor Division in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Arachchikattuwa, 
Puttalam  District of North Western Province and containing 
to total extent of 0.2028 Hectare and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot  ‘A’ – Martinwatta – Extent: 0.2028 Hectare
 North, East and South: Martinwatta claimed by Land 
Reform Commission; West: Road (R.D.A.).

 Minister of Lands.
