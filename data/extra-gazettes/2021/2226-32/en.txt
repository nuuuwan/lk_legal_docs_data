REVISION  OF  GAZETTE  NOTIFICATION No.  2026/25   DATED  05.07.2017
Condominium Management Authority Act, No. 10 of 1973 for Condominium Properties of the
National State Assembly
TABLE No. 3 of the Schedule of the Order made by the then Minister of Housing and Construction on 27.06.2017 under
Section 24 of the Condominium Property Management Authority Act, 1973/10 of the National State Council should be
amended to include the following items.
The above Section 3 is hereby amended to include the following items after the charges mentioned in the items
therein.
i. Urban Development Authority
ii. National Housing Development Authority
iii. Urban Settlement Development Authority
iv. Ocean View Development Private Limited
v. New Villages Development Authority for Plantation Region
I. No charges for housing projects classified as low-income earners built by the above institutions.
II. A fee of Rs. 250/-  per sq. m. will be charged for housing projects (over 5001 sq. m.) classified as middle
income earners built by the above institutions.
III. The amendments mentioned in above item (I) and (II) do not apply to housing projects and mixed projects
built in collaboration by government agencies and private property development companies (PPP - Private
Public Partnership)
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),
Minister of Urban Development and Housing.
