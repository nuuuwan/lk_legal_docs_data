Notification  of  Honourable Governor of Eastern Province
 ORDER   MADE   UNDER   SECTION   184(1B)   OF   THE   URBAN   COUNCIL   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   255)
READ   WITH   SECTION   2   OF    THE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS    (CONSEQUENTIAL    PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.12   OF  1989
I, Anuradha  Yahampath,  the Governor  of the  Eastern Province, in terms  of the powers vested in me under Sub Section
184(1B) of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255 ) read with Section 2 of the Provincial  Councils (Consequential
Provisions) Act, No.  12 of 1989, has initiated action to conduct an inquiry on the matters  arose at the Ampara   Urban
Council.   Until  the aforesaid inquiry  is concluded,  I  do hereby temporarily  suspend  Mr. Manothara  Acharige Chamintha
Sugath to hold the office of Chairman  of Ampara Urban Council and authorized to Mr.Dulip Laal Kumanayaka, Vice Chairman
of Ampara Urban Council, to exercise duties and powers of the office of Chairman of Ampara Urban Council.
Further, I authorize Assistant Commissioner of Local Government of the district  of Ampara to grant approval to the
following proposals proposed. in the 2021 draft budget during the period commencing from 01.01.2021 till such a date on
which the aforesaid inquiry  is concluded.
Proposals :
01. Salaries and wages
02. Travelling Allowances
03. Stationeries
