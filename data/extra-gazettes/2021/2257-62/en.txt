 FOREST CONSERVATION ORDINANCE 
THE ORDER 
BY virtue of powers vested in me by section 3(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) that has to be read  
with the notification published in Gazette Extraordinary No.2187/27 dated 09th August 2020 by the President according 
to the constitution 43(1),44(2) and  46(1)(a),(b) of the  co nstitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  
respectively ,I do by this order declare  the state land named  Mahilankulam   of  65.4  hectares bounded by the limits 
set forth in the schedule here to as a Reserved Forest  from the 30th day of November 2021.  
                                                      
 
 W IMALAWEERA DISSANAYAKE,

 Adoption of Safety Measures including the
