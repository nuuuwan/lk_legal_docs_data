PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Nawalapitiya Urban Council
I, Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Nawalapitiya Urban Council, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Hewa Waduge
Sasanka Sampath Sanjeewa, the Chairman of the Nawalapitiya Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that
Urban Council as he has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
M. M. S. K. BANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Nawalapitiya Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
14th June, 2021.
