FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    NEGOMBO    MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Negombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Dinayadura
Pushpa Kamini de Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of  People’s Liberatioin Front, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kurukulasooriya Selvi Iwotny Anjala Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the Negombo Municipal
Council , acting in terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Kurukulasooriya Selvi Iwotny Anjala Fernando was selected as the member of  the   Negombo Municipal Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Council.
District  Elections  Office,
Gampaha,
09th  March, 2021.
