Regulations
1. These Regulations shall be cited as Land Development Regulations 2021.
2. The minimum unit of subdivision stated in the conditions of a permit issued under Section 25 of the Ordinance and a
grant issued under Section 27 of the Ordinance is amended as follows.
I. Highlands - Minimum unit of Subdivision in the area within the administrative limits of the respective Local  Authority
where the land is situated.
II. Lands with irrigation facilities - 01 acre
III. Lands alienated for commercial crops - 01 acre
IV . Lands of the middle class - 2 1/2 acres
V. Rain fed highlands - 01 acre
3. The minimum fraction shall be determined in correspondence with this amended minimum unit of subdivision.
4. In the instances where requests are submitted to amend the minimum unit of subdivision and minimum fraction of a
permit or grant already issued, the said amendment can be made with an endorsement placed by the Divisional Secretary
in the said permit or grant, The said endorsement should be substantially made in the Specimen ‘A’.
5. Whereas the disposition of the Land issued by a grant or any part thereof has been made by a notarial deed upon the
prescribed prior written consent, if requested to amend the minimum unit of subdivision and minimum fraction of the said
notarial deed, action should be taken as per the above Regulation No. 4. The endorsement used thereby should be
substantially made in the Specimen ‘B’.
