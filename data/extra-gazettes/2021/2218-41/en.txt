FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     POINT  PEDRO   URBAN    COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of   return member of  Point Pedro Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Bamathy
Kathiramalai the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  Point Pedro Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kumuthini Nesarasa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Point Pedro Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kumuthini Nesarasa
was selected as the member of  the   Point Pedro Urban Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Point Pedro Urban Council.
District Elections  Office,
Jaffna,
08th March, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 09.03.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE    MARITIMEPATTU    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front,  the recognized political party to which
Manikkavasakar Subaskar the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned member of Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such  party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected as a member within
a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mohanarajasingam Vikna to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohanarajasingam
Vikna was selected as the member of  the Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections  Office,
Mullaitivu,
08th March,  2021.
