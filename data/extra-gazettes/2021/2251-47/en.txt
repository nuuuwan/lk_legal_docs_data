NOTICE   UNDER    SECTION   10A (1) (a)   OF   THE   LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Arachchikattuwa  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Badana Mudiyanselage Indunil Sanjeewa Lasantha Bandara, Returning Officer of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha,
do hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that
Mr. Warnakulasooriya Sujith Fernando of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that
Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
B. M. I. S. L B
ANDARA,
Returning Officer,
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
27th October, 2021.
