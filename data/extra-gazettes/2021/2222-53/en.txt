LAND  REFORM   COMMISSION
Notice under Section 29 of the Land Reform Law No. 1 of 1972 as amended by Land Reform
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 and Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 14 of 1986
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 29 of the Law, I, hereby direct every person who is interested in any
agricultural land or lands described in the Schedule hereto immediately before the date on which such land or lands have
been vested in the Land Reform Commission to make, within one month from 09.05.2021 a written claim in triplicate to the
wholy any part of the compensation payable underthe law in respect of such land or lands and to specify in the claim.
(a) His name and address ‘
(b) The nature of his interest in such land/lands
(c) Particulars of his claim and
(d) How much of such compenation is claimed by him.
All claims should be sent by registered post, addressed to the Chairman, Land Reform Commission.
NILANTHA WIJESINGHA,
Attorney-at-Law,
Chairman/Land Reform Commission.
