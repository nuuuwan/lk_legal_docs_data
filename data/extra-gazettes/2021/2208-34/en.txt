EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 01/2021
FOOLPROOF    STICKER    AND    STICKER   MANAGEMENT   SYSTEM
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32 read with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter
52), as amended, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do hereby amend the rules in the Excise Notification No. 04/2019
published in the Gazette Extraordinay No. 2128/30 dated June 20, 2019, as last amended by the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2178/4 dated June 01, 2020, as follows.
1. In rule 3,
(a) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the first proviso, for the words and figures “prior to December 31, 2020” of
the words and figures “prior to June 30, 2021”, and
(b) by the substitution in paragraph (ii) of the second proviso, for the words and figures “prior to December 31, 2020”
of  the words and figures “prior to June 30, 2021”.
2. In rule 13, by the substitution for the words and figures “561 days” of the words and figures “742 days”.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo,
January 01, 2021.
