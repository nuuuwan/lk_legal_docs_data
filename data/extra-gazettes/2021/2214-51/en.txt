PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 33706— 35 (02/2021)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Mapa Mudiyanselage Sena Kumarasiri Bandara Mapa, Returning Officer of the Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Wadiwel Ravi of  the
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  he has  been ceased to be a member
of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present).
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabbha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
08th February, 2021.
