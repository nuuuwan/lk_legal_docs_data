Regulation
The Sri Lanka V olunteer Air Force Regulations, 1978, published in Gazette No. 304 of February 10, 1978 as amended
from time to time are hereby further emended by the insertion immediately after  paragraph 18(b) thereof of the Regulation 18,
the following paragraphs :-
“(c)  is an Aircraftman or a Leading Aircraftman whose service is absolutely essential for the Sri Lanka V olunteer Air
Force owing to the service exigencies in the opinion of the Commander of the Air Force ; and
(d) has been recommended for extension by the Commanding Officer of the Sri Lanka V olunteer Air Force or any other
officer acting on behalf of Commanding Officer of the Sri Lanka V olunteer Air Force.”
