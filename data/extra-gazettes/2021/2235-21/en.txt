THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of 
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below 
are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of land in total extent of about 
0.0075 Hectare, depicted as Lot Nos. 01 and 02  in the Advance 
Tracing No. CO/THI/2020/156 dated 25.09.2020, prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called 
Polhengoda (Ward No. 41 - within the Municipal Council 
Limit of Colombo) in the Grama Niladhari Division of Kirula 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Thimbirigasyaya 
in Colombo District of Western Province and bounded as 
follows:
 0 1. Lot No. 1 – Assmt. No. 30/13 (a part), Kalinga 
Mawatha – Extent: 0.0008 Hectare
 North: Road and Railway reservation; East: Railway 
reservation and Lot No. 2; South: Lot No. 2 and lands bearing 
Assmt. Nos. 30/14 and 30/13 at Kalinga Mawatha; West: Land 
bearing Assmt. No. 30/13 at Kalinga Mawatha and Road.
 0 2. Lot No. 2 – Assmt. No. 30/14 (a part), Kalinga 
Mawatha – Extent: 0.0067 Hectare
 North: Lot No. 1 and Railway reservation; East: Railway 
reservation and canal; South: Canal, land at Kalinga Mawatha
