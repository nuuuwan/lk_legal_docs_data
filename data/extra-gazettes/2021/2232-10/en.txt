PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Mohammadu Naleer
Mohammadu Thahir Fassy of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya
Sabha as he has been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
KALINDRA JAYAWEERA FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
14th June, 2021.
