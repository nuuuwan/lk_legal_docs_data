Granting 
Permission
 1. I do hereby grant permission unto the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Province 
to spend rupees thirty seven Billion four hundred and twelve million only, (rs. 37,412,000,000) 
and which estimated for the services of the sabaragamuwa Provincial council for the one year 
duration beginning on 01st January, 2021  and ending on 31st december, 2021, by virtue of powers 
vested in me under section 27(A)  of  Provincial council Act No. 42 of 1987 as the term of the  sixth 
sabaragamuwa Provincial council ended on 26th september, 2017.
 2. (1)  w ithout prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure of the sabaragamuwa 
Provincial council which estimate will be rupees thirty seven Billion four hundred and 
twelve million only, (rs. 37,412,000,000) for the service of the period beginning on  01st 
January, 2021 and ending on 31st december, 2021, in this order referred to as the “one year” 
shall be met.
 (a) for payments which are hereby authorized to be made out of the sabaragamuwa Provincial 
council fund or monies of or  advance at the disposal of the sabaragamuwa Provincial 
council and,
 (b) for the proceeds of loans which are hereby authorized to be raised, within the province of 
sabaragamuwa for and on behalf of the sabaragamuwa Provincial council.  the sum of 
rupees thirty seven Billion four hundred and twelve million only, (rs. 37,412,000,000) 
here in before referred to may be duly expended as specified in the first schedule to this 
order.
  (2) the provision of subsection (1) of this section shall have effect without prejudice to the 
provisions of any other written law authorizing the raising of loans for and on behalf of the 
sabaragamuwa Provincial council.
 3. (1) The receipts of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council during the financial year 2021 from 
each activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall be credited 
to the account of such activity, the aggregate of receipts so credited shall be not less than 
the minimum limit specified  in the corresponding entry in column  (vi) of that schedule . 
 
 4. The expenditure incurred by the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, during the financial year 2021, 
each activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall be paid out of the 
receipt of the sabaragamuwa Provincial council from such expenditure shall not exceed the maximum 
limit specified in the corresponding entry in column (v) of that schedule.
 5. The debit balance, outstanding at the end of the financial year 2021 of any activity specified in 
column (iv) of the second schedule to this order shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in the 
corresponding entry in column (vii) of that schedule, and the total liabilities of that activity  at the 
end of that financial year shall not exceed the maximum limit specified in the corresponding entry 
column (viii) of that schedule.
 6. Whenever at any time during the financial year 2021, the receipts of that Sabaragamuwa Provincial 
Council from any activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule to this order are insufficient 
to meet the expenditure incurred by the sabaragamuwa Provincial council on such activity, the 
governor may, from time to time, by order direct that such sums may deem necessary to meet such 
expenditure shall be payable by way of advances out of sabaragamuwa Provincial council fund or 
any other fund or monies of or at the disposal of, the sabaragamuwa Provincial council, so however  
that the aggregate of the sums so advances does not exceed the maximum limit of entry in column 
(v) of that schedule.  Any sums so advanced in respect of such activity shall be refunded to the 
sabaragamuwa Provincial council fund in such manner as the Governor may by order direct.
