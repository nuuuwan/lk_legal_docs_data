PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance Notices
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A (1) (A) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
Colombo Municipal Council
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Mohamed Sarook Mohamed
Riza of the Colombo Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that Council as he has been ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City.
14th of June, 2021.
