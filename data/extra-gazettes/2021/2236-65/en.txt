PILGRIMAGES   ORDINANCE
GENERAL  public is hereby notified that Annual Esala Festival of historic Welle Devala being functioned ceremonially in
Grama Niladhari Division, Yaddehimulla, Divisional Secretariat, Habaraduwa, District of the Galle, Southern Province,
scheduled to be held during the period from Friday the 23rd of July 2021 to Sunday the 25th of July 2021 only to perform
rituals as per the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chap. 175) in terms of the regulation laid down by the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2125/24 dated 28.05.2019 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

HANTHA W EERASINGHE,
