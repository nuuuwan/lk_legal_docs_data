THE  FELLING  OF  TREES  (CONTROL)  ACT  (CHAPTER  452)
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Felling of Trees (Control) Act, (Chapter 452), I, Mahindananda
Aluthgamage, Minister of Agriculture, do by this Order amend the Felling of Trees (Control) Order, 1962 by -
(a) the repeal of paragraph 4 thereof ; and
(b) the insertion immediately after item 3 of the Schedule thereof of the following new item -
         “4. Coconut tree (Cocos nucifera). ”.
MAHINDANANDA ALUTHGAMAGE,
Minister of Agriculture.
Battaramulla,
17th of June, 2021.
