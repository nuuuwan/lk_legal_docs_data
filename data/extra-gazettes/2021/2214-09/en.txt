THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. No. : LD/05/2003/E/614.
My Ref. No. : Hal/3/2/1/55.
I hereby inform that the Government intends to obtain the
following land required for the construction of the playground
of B/Kalupahana Maha Vidyalaya, West, under Sub-section
7(1) of the Land Acquisition Act.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Uva Province, Badulla District, Haldummulla Divisional
Secretariat, Kandapalla Korale Minor Division, Uvatenna -
158C Grama Niladhari Division, located in Welegama Village,
Final Village Plan No. 287 Sup. No. 13 Sheet No.15 dated
21.12.2020 prepared by the Superintendent of  Surveys a Lot
of land with extent about 0.4047 Hectare.
01. Lot No. 714
Name of  the land: Nelligoda Hena or Diganegedara Estat
Hena; Description: A scrub land; Claimant: Being possessed
by Mrs. Kusuma Nalini Kannangara, No. 26, Priyadarshanarama
Road, Dehiwala; Extent: 0.4047 Hectare.
Details of Boundaries :
North : Lot No. 72; East : Lot No. 72; South : Lot No. 72;
West :  Lot No. 470.
All  persons claiming ownership, rights or other affiliation
of the aforesaid land should contact me or their authorized
persons at the Divisional Secretariat, Haldummulla on 03rd
March, 2021 at 10.00 am.
Hereby requested to notify me in writing (in 2 duplicate) on
or before the 24th February, 2021 and the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
A. P. SUNETH GINENDRASENA,
Divisional Secretary,
Haldummulla.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Haldummulla.
