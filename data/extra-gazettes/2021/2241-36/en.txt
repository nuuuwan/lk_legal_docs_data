THE   RUBBER   REPLANTING   SUBSIDY   ACT (CHAPTER 437)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Plantation under Section 8 of Rubber Replanting Subsidy Act (Chapter 437).
RAMESH PATHIRANA,
Minister of Plantation.
Colombo,
06h August, 2021.
REGULATIONS
1. These regulations may be cited as the Rubber (Replanting) Subsidy Regulations No. 01 of 2021 and shall come
into operation on 01st January, 2019.
2. Any proprietor of a rubber estate or a rubber small holding who has been issued a permit under Section 7 of the
Rubber Control Act (Chapter 436) for replanting rubber on any land (hereinafter referred to as the “permit holder”) may
submit an application to the Rubber Controller in writing for the purpose of obtaining a subsidy for replanting of rubber
plants in such estate or small holding.
3. Every application for a subsidy under regulation 2 shall be made in the form as may be determined by the
Rubber Controller.

4. The Rubber Controller may upon the receipt of such application, grant a subsidy to the applicant from the
Rubber Replanting Subsidy Fund established under section 2 of the Rubber Replanting Subsidy Act (Chapter 437) and in
accordance with the provisions of these regulations.
5. The amount of subsidy payable for replanting of rubber in any specified part of a rubber estate or a rubber
small holding which is exclusively of private ownership and in respect of the year 2019 or thereafter shall be rupees three
hundred and fifty thousand (Rs. 350,000) for each hectare of the area and shall be paid in eight installments in the following
manner:-
(a) (i) the first installment of Rupees Sixteen Thousand (Rs. 16,000) for each Hectare off an area to be
replanted shall be paid after the eradication of the existing rubber trees and other vegetation on
such area;
(ii) the Rubber Controller may pay an amount not exceeding fifty per centum (50%) of the first
installment as a part payment to the permit holder of the rubber estate or the rubber small holding
on being notified that the eradication of the existing rubber trees and other vegetation on such land
has been completed.
(iii) the Rubber Controller may authorize to remit an additional remuneration after completion of digging
holes for rubber replanting when the Rubber Controller considers such authorization is necessary.
(b) (i) the second installment of Rupees Eighty-six Thousand (Rs. 86,000) for each hectare shall be paid
after the completion of replanting;
(ii) the Rubber Controller may pay an amount of money not exceeding fifty percentum (50%) of the
second installment as a part payment to the permit holder on being notified that the replanting has
been completed;
(iii) where it is found at the inspection that all requirements for the payment of the second installment
has not been fulfilled, payment shall be made after satisfactory completion of the following activities
in the following manner:-

6. Where the Rubber Controller is satisfied that progress specified in paragraphs (c) to (h) of regulation 5 has
been achieved prior to the periods, specified therein, the Rubber Controller may at this discretion release the particular
installment at an earlier date.
7. No installment of subsidy shall be paid later than seven years after the completion of replanting or after the
commencement of tapping in such clearing other than in accordance with paragraphs (a) and (b) of regulation 17.
8. The date on which trees were planted in a field shall be considered as the date of replanting.
9. Where the area to be replanted is less than one Hectare in extent or where such area includes a part which is
less than one Hectare, the amounts specified in regulation 5 of these regulations shall be paid in respect of that extent or
part of that area in the proportion to that extent or part of the area bears to one Hectare, in computing the amount of
subsidy payable.
10. An incentive payment amounting to Rupees Fourteen Thousand (Rs. 14,000) per Hectare may be paid for
replanting rubber estates or small holdings which has a minimum 2/3 from the required number of trees per Hectare at the
end of the fifth year of replanting which have reached the minimum girth of fifty decimal point eight naught (50.80)
centimeters in circumference at the height of one hundred and twenty one decimal point nine two (121.92) centimeters
above the ground and on the receipt of a certificate from an officer authorized in writing in that behalf by the Rubber
Controller to that effect.
11. The permit holder shall open and maintain either a current account or a savings account in a branch of the
Government Bank or any Private Commercial Bank in order to receive the installments of subsidy and effect recoveries
from the installments of subsidy payments.
12. It shall be the duty of every permit holder to whom the first installment of the subsidy has been paid under
regulation 5 to comply with the directions issued and conditions imposed by the Rubber Controller by notice in writing
under regulation 19 and to maintain the replanted area in good agricultural condition.

13. A permit holder to whom one or more installments of subsidy are due under regulation 5 in respect of an area
for which a replanting permit has been issued, shall not sell or otherwise dispose of part or whole of such area without the
written approval of the Rubber Controller.
14. A permit holder to whom the first installment has been paid under regulation 5 shall within one year or within
such further time as may be given by the Rubber Controller eradicate the existing vegetation and clear the area to be
planted shall -
(a) complete the replanting of such area with rubber plants belonging to such variety or varities approved
by the Rubber Controller;
(b) when required to do so by the Rubber Controller by notice in writing, remove from such area all
plants other than rubber plants and leguminous ground cover plants;
(c) construct in such area, such drains or terraces as the Rubber Controller may require by notice in
writing;
(d) plant leguminous ground cover crops in such area or take such other measures as are required for
the prevention of soil erosion in such area, as the Rubber Controller may require by notice in
writing; and
(e) construct a fence around such area, if requested so to do by the Rubber Controller by notice in
writing.
15.(1) Where any such work specified in regulation 14 has not been carried out by the permit holder the
Rubber Controller may employ such people and utilize required materials as the Rubber Controller considers necessary
to complete such work.
(2) The Rubber Controller shall deduct the expenses incurred in that connection from the subsidies payable
to such permit holder.
16. Every permit holder to whom a subsidy or a portion thereof has been paid in respect of an extent of land shall
ensure that the minimum number of rubber plants to be replanted in each hectare of that area shall conform with the
following requirements: -
(a) (i) 516 plants per hectare at the time of replanting, if planted with rubber clones (RRIC, RRISL)
which are of Rubber Research Institute of Sri Lanka (hereinafter referred to as “RRISL” ) and varieties
of other high yield bud rubber plants approved by the Rubber Controller;
(ii) 516 plants per hectare at the end of the first and second years after replanting;
(iii) 475 plants per hectare at the end of the third and fourth years after replanting;
(iv) 450 plants per hectare for the next two consecutive years after replanting;
(v) 450 plants per hectare for the next three consecutive years after replanting in accordance with
paragraph (b) of regulation 17.

18. No permit holder who is a recipient of a subsidy in full or any portion thereof in repsect of any area, shall tap
or cause to be tapped any rubber plant until at least Sixty per centum (60%) of the rubber plants have reached a minimum
girth of Fifty decimal point Eight Naught (50.80) centimeters at a height of one Hundred and Twenty-one decimal Nine
Two centimeters (121.92) when measured from the ground.
19. Where a subsidy is payable or has been paid under regulation 5 to any permit holder, such permit holder shall,
within such period of time as the Rubber Controller may, by notice specify in writing, comply with such directions and
requirements as the Rubber Controller deems necessary for the purpose of carrying out the replanting of a rubber estate
or a rubber small holding in a satisfactory manner.
20. Where the Rubber Controller is of the opinion that any permit holder to whom a subsidy or any portion
thereof has been paid has contravened regulations 12,13,14,15,16,17, or 18, the Rubber Controller may by notice in
writing, require such permit holder to refund the amount of the subsidy which has been paid to him in respect of an area
either forthwith or within such time as may be specified in the notice, and no permit holder on whom notice is served
under this regulation shall fail to comply with such notice.
21. (1) Whenever any permit holder fails to repay the subsidy paid under these regulations such sum of
money shall be recovered as a fine imposed by a court of law.
(2) The submission of a certificate issued by the Rubber Controller to the effect that a person after
having obtained one or more installments of subsidy has failed to repay such subsidy and has
abandoned a rubber estate or rubber small holding shall be prima facie evidence of such fact.
22.  Notwithstanding the provisions of the Rubber Replanting Subsidy Regulations published in the Gazette
No. 10594 of 13th October, 1953, as amended from time to time, the following instalments of subsidy payable on any
replanting permits issued in the respect ot the years 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019 respectively and
where all the installments- due on such permits have not been paid at the time of operation of these regulations such
installments shall be paid at the rates specified in regulation 5 in the following manner:-
Year 2013 - the eighth installment in terms of regulation 5 (h);
Year 2014 - from the seventh installment onwards in terms of regulation 5 (g) ,
Year 2015 - from the sixth installment onwards in terms of regulation 5 (f) ,
Year 2016 - from the fifth installment onwards in terms of regulation 5 (e) ;
Year 2017 - from the fourth installment onwards in terms of regulation 5 (d),
Year 2018 - from the third installment onwards in terms of regulation 5 (c) ;
Year 2019 - from the first and second installments onwards in terms of regulation 5 (a) and 5 (b).
23. In these regulations: -
“Proprietor”, shall have the same meaning assigned to it in the Rubber Control Act (Chapter 436);
“Estate” shall have the same meaning assigned to it in the Rubber Control Act (Chapter 436);
“Replanting” shall have the same meaning assigned to it in the Rubber Replanting Subsidy Act (Chapter
437);
“Re-planting permits” means a permit issued under Section 7(1) of the Rubber Control Act (Chapter
436) ; and
“Rubber Controller” shall have the same meaning assigned to it in the Rubber Replanting Subsidy Act
(Chapter 437).
