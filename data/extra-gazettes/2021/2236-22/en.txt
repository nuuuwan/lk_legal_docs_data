APPROVAL OF THE  DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA OF  
PART OF MAHO PRADESHIYA SABHA AREA 
 
 
 I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Urban Development and Housing do hereby approve the Development plan for the 
Urban Development Area of  part of Maho Pradeshiya Sabha  Area, having considered the recommendation made by the 
Board of Management of the Urban Development Authority on 24 th September, 2020 by virtue of the powers vested in 
me under Section 8 "F" of the Urban Development Authority (Amendment) Act, No, 4 of 1982. 
 
 
 MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA (M. P.),

NOTICE OF APPROVAL OF THE  DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT  
AREA OF A  PART OF  PRADESHIYA SABHA LIMIT OF MAHO 
 
 
 NOTICE  is hereby given to the General Public of the De mocratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8 (G) 
of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982 that I, Mahinda 
Rajapaksa, the Minister in charge of the subject of Urban Development & Housing, by virtue of the powers vested in me 
under Section 8(F) of the said Act, have approved the Development Plan for the Urban Development Area for a part of 
Pradeshiya Sabha limit of Maho, prepared under Section 8(A) of the said Act, on the 15
th day of April, 2021.
