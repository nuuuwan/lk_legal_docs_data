PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 25.03.202 1
In the Matter of Industrial Dispute
Between
Mr. S. G . J. Barbutt,
No. 7/6, Alubogahawatta,
Anderson Road,
Dehiwala.
And
Life Insurance Corporation Lanka Limited,
Case No.: A/07/2017 Sharnell Building,
No. 29/2, Visaka Road,
Colombo 04.
A ward
1. The Honourable Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations, by virtue of the powers vested in him by Section 4(1)
of the Industrial Dispute Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactment of Ceylon (1956 revised edition) as amended
by Acts No. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968,
appointed me by his order dated 29 December and referred the dispute between the aforesaid parties for settlement
by arbitration.
2. The matter in dispute is as follows –
“Whether the non payment of money for the petrol allowance of Rs. 200.00 agreed to be paid as a cond ition of
service to Mr. S. G . J. Barbutt who worked as a Sales Manager at Life Insurance Corporation Lanka Limited for the
period from July to 14th September, 2014 is just and if not what reliefs he is entitled to.”
3. Applicant looked after his own interest, while respondent organization was represented by Mrs. Kasun Jayaweera,
Attorney At Law.
4. Applicant simultaneously filed action in labour tribunal, which heard and judgment delivered per Cas e No. 01/02/
2014 dated 30th June 2020, where the respondent had been ordered to pay a sum of Rs. 34,84,800 - as compensation.
Respondent had deposited the sum on 06th August 2020, with com. of labour (Colombo North).
A ward
In view of this judement, I am not required to hear the present application, in term of Sec. 31 B (5) of the Industrial
Disputes Act which precludes an applicant to seek relief from another legal remedy. No relief granted.
This is just and a fair award.
P. NAVARATNE,
Arbitrator.
02nd March, 2021
