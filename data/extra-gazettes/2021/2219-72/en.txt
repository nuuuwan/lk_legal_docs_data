WESTERN PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Amended Rent Act, No. 07 of 1972, amended by the Amendment Acts No. 26 of 2002
and No. 55 of 1980
IN terms of the powers vested in me by Section 44 of the amended Rent Act bearing No. 07 of 1992, amended by the
amendment Acts bearing No. 55 of 1980 and No. 26 of 2002, which should be read with Section 02 of  Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, bearing No. 12 of 1989, I, hereby notify that the names of the persons mentioned in the below
Schedule will be appointed as the authorized officers for the functions of the Rent Act, No. 07 of 1972 amended by the
aforesaid Amendment Acts bearing No. 26 of 2002 and No. 55 of 1980 to the area or areas mentioned correspond to the names.
R
OSHAN GOONETILEKE,
RWP and BAR, VSV , USP Phd (Hon.),
FIM (Sri Lanka), ndc, psc,
Marshal of the Air Force,
The Governor,
Western Province.
At Battaramulla,
on 16th  March, 2021.
