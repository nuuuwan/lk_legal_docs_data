ThE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 and 4 of the societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa 
Prime Minister and Minister of Finance do by this notification ;
 01. Authorize the purpose for which the society known as “Kadawatha Metro shop/Apartment Owners Association” 
situated at No. M 9, Metro shopping Complex, Kirillawala, Kadawatha as a purpose to which the powers and 
facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended ; and
 
 02. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of section 5 (4) of 
that Ordinance.
 
Mahinda Rajapaksa,
 Prime Minister and Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
The secretariat,
Lotus Road,
Colombo 01,
23rd june, 2021.
