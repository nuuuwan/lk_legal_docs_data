FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF   THE     KAMBURUPITIYA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B (2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELLECTION
(AMENDMENT) ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the Office of  Vice Chairman in Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Lalith
Alwis Samarasinghe the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section  66B (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the  member of that  Kamburupitiya Prad eshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Bambarandage Duminda Pushpa Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B (2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Hewa Bambarandage Duminda Pushpa
Kumara  elected as Vice Chairman of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
08th June, 2021.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE    EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 09.06.2021
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE     DAMBULLA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER   262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Dambulla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Warnakulasuriya Mudiyanselage Yasaratna Warnasuriya the member belonged, was required by me under Section  66A
(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the  member of that No. 06 Pallegama of Dambulla Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Pinnapolage Sarathchandra to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the Dambulla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  that Hewa Pinnapolage Sarathchandra was elected as the member of  the No. 06  Pallegama of Dabmulla  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Damublla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,  
Matale,
08th June, 2021.
