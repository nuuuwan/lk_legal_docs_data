1A – G34303 – 15 (2021/05)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Chandrasena, Minister of
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the below mentioned land is
required for a public purpose and is acquired under the
Provisions of Land Acquisition  Act.
Boundaries of the allotments of land, with below mentioned
names and extents depicted as Advance Tracing No.
MA/MLM/2019/349 and 10.07.2019 which was prepared  by
the Surveyor  General, situated in the village of Sulthanagoda
East, in Weligam Korale  South Minor Division, in Malimbada
Divisional Secretariat Division in Matara District, in Southern
Province.
01. Lot ‘A’  – Madidhdhuwa alias Madidhdhuwawila –
Extent: 0.0459 Hectare
North and East: Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by
A.L. Abeysinghe; South: Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed
by Chaminda Weerasekara and Others and Lot B of that
tracing; West:   Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by A.L.
Abeysinghe.
02. Lot ‘B’  – Madidhdhuwa alias Madidhdhuwawila –
Extent: 0.0338 Hectare
North: Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by A.L.
Abeysinghe and Lot A of this Tracing; East: Madidhdhuwawila
which is as claimed by Chaminda Weerasekera;South:
Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by  Sunil Hemantha Silva
and by Lot C of that tracing; West:   Madidhdhuwawila which
is claimed by Chaminda Weerasekera and Others.
03. Lot ‘C’  – Madidhdhuwa alias Madidhdhuwawila –
Extent: 0.0254 Hectare
North: Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by Chaminda
Weerasekera and Others and Lot B of this Tracing; East, South
and West: Madidhdhuwawila which is claimed by Sunil
Hemantha Silva.
