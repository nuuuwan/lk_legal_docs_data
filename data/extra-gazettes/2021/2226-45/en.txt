PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices  under  the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance
1A - G 34301— 65  (05/2021)
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authority Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Loku Kumaralage Dona
Bageshwari Madushika Gunasekara of the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that Pradeshiya
Sabha as she  been ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHA WA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
04th May, 2021.
