PART IV (A) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 30.08.2018
NORTH WESTERN PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
A Statute to Amend North Western Province Environmental Statute No 12 of 1990
Amended by the Amendment Statute No 02 of 2011
BE it passed by the Provincial Council of the North western Province of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka as follows:
(1) This statute may be cited as the North western Province Environmental (Amendment) Statute
No ……… of   2018
(2) The Section 03 of North Western Province Environmental  Statute No 12 of 1990 amended by
Amendment statute No 02 of 2011( herein after referred to as principal statute) is repealed and the
following section is substituted.
3.1. The Provincial Authority shall consist of 7 members appointed by the Chief Minister of the
North Western Province in Consultation with the Minister in Charge of the Subject.
(a) One of whom shall be the Chief Secretary of the North Western Provincial Council or the
officer discharging the duties of that office.
(b) One of whom shall be the Secretary of Chief Ministry of the North Western Provincial
Council or the officer discharging the duties of that office.
(c) One of whom shall be the Commissioner of Local Government of North Western Province
or the officer discharging the duties of that office.
(d) One of whom shall have adequate expertise and qualifications in the subject of the
environment ; and
(e) One of whom shall have suitable administrative skill and experience in environmental
management or a dominant active figure in an organization committed to environmental
protection; and
(f) One of whom shall have adequate expertise and qualifications in the subject of Industry.
(g) One of whom shall be a professor or a senior lecturer for Science subjects in a government
university of Sri Lanka.
3. 2 The Chief Secretary of the North Western Provincial Council or the officer discharging the
duties of that office shall be the chairman of the North Western Environmental Authority.
3. 3 The director for the time being of provincial Authority shall be appointed as the Secretary of the
said Authority by the Chairman of the Provincial Authority.
3. 4 The quorum for any meeting of the Authority shall be 3 members.
(3) Section 10 of Amendment Act No 02 of 2011 is hereby repealed and section 63 is inserted immediately
after section 62 of the principal statute.
63. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts in this Statute, the
Sinhala texts shall prevail.
