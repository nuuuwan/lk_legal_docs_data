1A  - PG 4078 — 107  (06/2018)
RULES   ON   RECRUITMENT,   APPOINTMENT,   TRANSFER, PROMOTION   AND   DISCIPLINARY
CONTROL   OF   POLICE   OFFICERS
RULES made by  the National Police Commission by virtue of  the powers vested in it under Article 155 FFF of  the
Constitution.
P. H. MANATUNGA,
Chairman,
National Police Commission.
Colombo,
11th June, 2018.
Rules
1. These rules may be cited as the Rules on recruitment, appointment, transfer, promotion and disciplinary
control of Police Officers, approved by the Commission on May 08, 2018.
2. The National Police Commission when appointing chairperson/s and members to the interview panels for
the recruitment/ appointment/ selection of candidates to the posts of Police Officers may appoint a
chairperson/ s to chair such Panels.
3. The Rule in (2) above shall apply to the recruitment and appointment of Police Officers, notwithstanding any
existing rules or rules of procedure to the contrary.
