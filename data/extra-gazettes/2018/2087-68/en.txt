Declaration  of  Determination  of  the  Commissioner  of  Title  Settlement  under  Section  14

hereby declare my determination as set out in the Schedule appended hereto in regard to the  title to parcel of Land Nos. 
2367, 2368, 2369, 2370, 2371, 2372, 2373 and 2374 of Block 01, contained in the Cadastral Map No. 110073, situated in

Niladhari Division of No. 421-Nallachchiya, 424-Thammannagama, 425-Konwewa, 427-Thelhiriyawa in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Thambuttegama, in the District of Anuradhapura, in the Province of North Central, referred to in
