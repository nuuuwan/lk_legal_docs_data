 MINUTE  OF   SRI   LANKA   ARCHITECTS ’   SERVICE
FOLLOWING  amendments are made to the  Minute of Sri Lanka Architects' Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1877/28 dated 28.08.2014 as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  No. 1882/18 dated 01.10.2014, No. 1952/20 dated 02.02.2016 and
No. 1994/68 dated 24.11.2016, and the other matters and conditions thereof  remain unchanged.
(1)  7.3.2.1. Educational/ Professional Qualifications and Experience
I. Officers who have completed the qualifications in 7.2.2.1. who have completed an active and satisfactory
period of service of 05 years in the Public or Provincial Public Service and who draw Salaries under the
Salary code  of  MN 2 or above referred to in the Public Administration Circular 06/2006
II. Officers who have passed the Standard Course - Part I at Sri Lanka Institute of Architects and who have
completed 10 years of active and satisfactory service in the Public Service/ Provincial Public Service
And
III. Draughtsman in Special Class of  the Sri Lanka Technological Service with an immediately preceding 05
years of satisfactory period of Service
(2)  7.3.2.3. Other
II. Should have completed a satisfactory period of  Service during the immediately preceding 05 years
IV . Should have confirmed in the Service.
(3)  10.2.1  Qualifications to be fulfilled
I. Should have completed a five (05) years of active and satisfactory period of Service in Grade II of  the
category of Service and should have earned five (05) salary increments that fall due.
