No. 2061/9 – MONDAY, MARCH 05, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of land called “Pallemaluwa” in extent of about
1.064 Hectare, shown in the Advance Tracing No. NU/UDH/97/163
and dated 26.12.1997, prepared by the Surveyor-General situated in
the Village of Hanguranketha in Diyathilaka Korale Minor Divison
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Udahewahete in Nuwara
Eliya District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Pallemaluwa – Extent: About 1.064 Hectares
North: Road; East: Road and Pallemaluwa claimed by Land Reform
Commission; South: Lot Nos. 2, 3, 4 and Road; West: Road.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
