FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  RAJGAMA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Rajgama   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National  Party, the recognized political party to which Kandage
Sharmi Anushka Kumari De Silva   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned member if  United National  Party   who belongs to such  party  and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the local authorities election ordinance and who shall be declared selected as  a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Siriyawathi Suwaris Abeysekara  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Siriyawathi Suwaris Abeysekara  was selected as the member of the Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G .  L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
08th  August, 2018.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  MANMUNAI    WEST

WHEREAS the office of  member of  Manmunai West  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the IIankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Ariyarethinam Mahesarajah  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid

in terms of Section 66A(1) (a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Alagaiah Amirthalingam   was selected as the member of the Manmunai  West Pradeshiya Sabha
