THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.3376 Hectare,
depicted as Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. Ya/VDN/2016/314
dated 28.02.2017 prepared by the Senior superintendent of Surveys
Jaffna District, situated in the Village called Puloly Centre in the
Grama Niladari Division No. J/409 in the Vadamaradchi North in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Jaffna District in Northern Province
and bounded as follows:
North: Name of land not known, claimed by Sri Lanka Transport
Board, Name of land not known, claimed by Rukkumani wife of
Sankarappillai and Name of land not known, claimed by
Thankavelautham Anparasan; East: Name of land not known, claimed
by Rukkumani wife of Sankarappillai, Name of land not known,
claimed by Thankavelautham Anparasan and Road (R.D.A.); South:
Road (R.D.A.) and Name of land not known and claimed by Sri
Lanka Transport Board; West: Name of land not known and claimed
by Sri Lanka Transport Board.
