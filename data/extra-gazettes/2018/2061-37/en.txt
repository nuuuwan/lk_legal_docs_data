THE  LAND  ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
(Notice Under Section 7)
I hereby give notice, under sub section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, That the government intends
to acquire the following land /Servitude* which is required for a
public purpose:-
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Description of the said area – Allotments of land from No. 01
(Described in the Schedule) in Plan Ma 949 , Situated in
WelamitiyawaVillage in Welamitiyawa GramaNiladhari Division No.
E440D in Wagapanaha Pallesiya Pattuwa in Dambulla Divisional
Secretariat division in Matale District in Central Province.
01. Lot No. . 1
Name of the Land: Walamitiyawa; Description : Chena & Water;
Name of the owner : Inamaluwe SumangalaThero in Rangiri
Danbulu Temple ; Amount: 10.022ha.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally,
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Dambulla
Divisional Secretariat 03rdApril 2018 at 10.30 a.m., and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing ( in duplicate) on or before the
29th March 2018 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of
the said land/servitude*, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
H.P .P.A.HEWAPATIRANA,
Divisional Secretary - Dambulla.
Divisional Secretary’s Office
