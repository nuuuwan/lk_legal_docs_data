FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE  NUW ARAGAM   PALATHA  EAST
PRADESHIY A   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION  66A  (1)  (a) OF THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of Member of Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Ranaweera
Arachchilage Pradeep Nilaweera the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 4- Vijayapura if Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabh a who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the local authorities elections ordinance and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Basnayaka Adhikaram Gunarathna Mudiyanselage Kumuduni Geethamala Gunarathna Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, returning officer of the Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting  in terms of section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities  Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Basnayaka Adhikaram Gunarathna Mudiyanselage Kumuduni Geethamala Gunarathna Kumari was elected as the member of
the 4- Vijayapura of Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. SUFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
25th September 2018.
