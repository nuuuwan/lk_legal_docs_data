FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
THE Order under Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as amended by Acts, No. 13 of 1966,
No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and  No. 65 of  2009.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I,  Maithripala
Sirisena, Minister of Mahaweli Development and Environment do by this order declare the Sasthraweli (Mangrove Forest)
Forest of 9.9807 Hectares  bounded by the limit set forth in the Schedule 01 hereto as  Sasthraweli (Mangrove Forest)
Reserved Forest from 05th day of  June, 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
Minister of Mahaweli Development and  Environment.
Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment,
Battaramulla,
05th day of June, 2018.
