THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is required for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of Land
Acquisition Act.
Boundaries of the portion of land in extent of  0.0126 Hectare
depicted as Lot A, in the Advance Tracing No. R/ELP/2017/III dated
18.05.2017, prepared by the  Surveyor General and situated in the
Village Kahawaththa (F. V . P. 58), in the Minor Division of Palle
Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Elapatha in
Rathnapura District of  Sabaragamuwa Province :
01. Lot A – Gangagawawaththa – Extent: 0.0126 Hectare
North: Gangagawawaththa possessed by K. M. Subasinghe and
the Kalu River ; East: The Kalu River and Gangagawawaththa
possessed by K. M. subasingha; South:  Gangagawawaththa
possessed by K. M. Subasingha and Road (R.D.A); West: Road
(R.D.A) and Gangagawawaththa possessed by K. M. Subasingha.
