EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7   
 
My   Reference No.:  KDS/YNU/4/1/JALA/14. 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/141. 
 
I  hereby Notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of the 
Land Acquisition Act,  (Chapter 460) as amended by the 
Land Acquition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 was 
given by me that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land for a public purpose. 
 
 Discription of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The  land called Galange Watta Kande Watte or 
Pamunuva Waththa mentioned as No. 01 in Total 
Pamunuva Watta mentioned as No. 01 in total extend or 
Hectare 0.0024 depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. 6564 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at Gannoruva 
West Village at Yatinuwara Ganga Palatha Korale Minor 
Divisin in the Yatinuwara Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Kandy District. 
 
 
Lot No. : 01 
Name of the land : Galange Watta Kande Watte or 
Pamunuva Waththa : Extent 0.0024 Hectare. 
Boundaries 
 
To North :  Road (Pradeshiya Sabhawa) 
 
To East :  Road (Pradesshiya Sabhawa) and  
  Galange Watta Kande Watte or  
  Pamunuva Waththa claimed by J. A. 
  Somarathna. 
 
To South :  Claimed by J. A. Somarathna Galange 
  Hena or Kande Watte or Pamunuva 
  Waththa 
 
To West :  Road (Pradeshiya Sabhawa) 
 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing at Divisional Secretary’s  Office Yatinuwara on the 
15th May 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the day 
of 9th May 2018 the nature of their interests in the land and 
the perticulars of there claims for compensation for the 
acquition of the aforesaid land. The amount of 
compensation  and the details of the computation such 
amount. 
 
 
 
                    U. P. INDIKA ANURUDDHA PIYADASA, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
                                          Yatinuwara. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Yatinuwara, 
21st day of February, 2018.
