 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under  Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Kabir Hashim, Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order,
amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1454/37 of July 22, 2006 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2023/33 of June 14, 2017) which specifies the Faculties and the Department
of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act,
to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the entry “Department of Sanskrit”
appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Humanities” appearing in Column I of
the Schedule of the following entry :-
“6. Department of Sanskrit and Eastern Studies”.
K
ABIR H ASHIM,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
20th April, 2018.
