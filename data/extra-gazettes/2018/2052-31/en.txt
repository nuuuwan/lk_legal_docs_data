THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ACT (CAP. 262)
Directive issued under Section 76 A
IN the event of any potential disruption to the delivery of postal voting material, official poll cards or any other documents
related to the poll by post, at the Local Authorities Elections 2018 Scheduled to be held under  Chapter 262 of Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance, as amended by the Amendment Acts Nos. 9 of 1963, 24 of 1977, 24 of 1967, 25 of 1990 and
22 of 2012 , it is hereby notified that it is lawful for the Elections Commission to get such activities carried out through a
government institution or government officers or servants or through any other method adopted and it is the responsibility
of such officer to assist the Commission as directed.
This directive is issued under the powers vested with the Elections Commission, by the Article 104B (2) of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and provisions in the Ordinance mentioned above.
MAHINDA D ESHAPRIYA, N. J. A BEYSEKERA, S. R ATNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE,
Chairman. Member. Member.
