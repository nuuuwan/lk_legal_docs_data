THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub section (1) of Section 5 of the
land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of  land described in names and extents in the
Advance Tracing No. KE/GLG/2014/44 of 31/05/2017  prepared by the
Surveyor General situated in the Villages of Apalathotuwa and
Batupitiya in the Grama Niladhari Divisions  of  No. 65, Pindeniya and
No. 64 A, Arandara Grama Niladari Divisions in Kandupita Pattuwa
South Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Galigamuwa in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province are bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.7600 Hectare
North: Sawaneya Watta and Atala Watta; East: Atala Watta; South:
Atala Watta, Canal and Canal holding the Lot  B; West: Canal holding
the Lot B, Canal and Sawaneya Watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.0385 Hectare
North: Lot C, Sawaneya Watta, Canal and Sawaneya Watta and
Lot C; East: Lot A; South: Lot A, Atala Watta, Canal, Atala Watta
and Lot  C; West: Lot C.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 1.1094 Hectare
North: Sawaneya Watta, Canal holding the Lot B; East: Lot B,
Canal and Atala Watta; South: Atala Watta, Road holding Lot D;
West: Road holding Lot D.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.1360 Hectare
North: Road, Milands Watta, Canal, Sawaneya Watta and Lot C;
East: Lot C, Atala Watta and Road; South: Atala Watta, Lots E and F;
West: Lot G and Canal.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.5988 Hectare
North: Lot D and Road; East: Atala Watta; South: Midellagahamula
Watta and Road; West: Lot F.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.0260 Hectare
North: Road holding Lot D; East: Lot E and Midellagahamula
Watta; South: Road and Midellagahamula Watta; West: Lot G .
07. Lot ‘G’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 1.6839 Hectares
North: Canal and Road holding the Lot D; East: Road holding the
Lot D and Road holding the Lot F; South: Midellagahamula Watta
and Milands Watta; West: Milands Watta, Canal holding Lot H,
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Canal.
08. Lot ‘H’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.0124 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot G; East: Lot G; South:
Lot G , Milands Watta, Canal, Milands Watta and Lot J; West: Lot J.
