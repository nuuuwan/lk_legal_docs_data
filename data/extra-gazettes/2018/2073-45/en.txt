THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
             ReferenceNo.: NE/3/1/8/Acquisition/162 
            
I hereby  give  notice under Sub - section (1) of the Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intended to acquired.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
   Allotment of  land called in Total Extent about 0.1389 
Hectare described as Lot 01 in Preliminary Plan No. 
P.P.Nu 3147 of 01.08.2017 prepared by the Surveyor 
General situated in Scrb Estae in West NuwaraEliya 
Grama Niladhari’s Division in Oya Palatha Korale Minor 
Division in Nuwara Eliya Divisional Secretary’s 
Division, in Nuwara - Eliya District in Central Province 
and bounded as follows:   
01.  Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Scrub Estate; Details: Garden; Contains a 
permanent building Claiment: Kelani Valley Plantations Ltd, 
No. 400, Deans Road, Colombo 10; Extent: about 0.1010 
Hectatres. 
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at the 
Divisional Secretariat  on the 04th  July,  2018 at 11.30 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of June, 2018. 
The nature of their interests in the land. The particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation  of such amount. 
 
 
 
  
 S. P. K. BODHIMANNA, 
 Divisional Secretary , 
 Nuwara - Eliya. 
 
 
Nuwara Eliya Divisional Secretariat.
