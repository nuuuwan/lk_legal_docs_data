FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   JA- ELA    URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER    SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Ja- Ela  Urban Council   has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the Independent  Group,  to  which Jayasundara Arachchige Chamal Lakshitha   the
member  belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,   to decide  upon returned
member of Independent Group who belongs to such Independent  Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the aforesaid  Independent  Group has  communicated his decision to nominate
Jayakodi Arachchige Noel Deckster Perera  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Ja-Ela  Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Jayakodi Arachchige Noel Deckster Perera  was selected as the member of the Ja- Ela  Urban Council.
K. J.S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Ja- Ela  Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
16th  October  2018.
