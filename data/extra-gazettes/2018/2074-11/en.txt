Greetings !
Whereas the Government is desirous of establishing a Task Force to -
review the development projects so far launched in the Northern and Eastern Provinces in Sri Lanka since
the end of the 26 year long conflict.
accelerate the development process under way in the said provinces.
provide necessary guidance and advice in relation to the development activities launched by the Government
in the said provinces,
And whereas it is considered that the development process is essential to ensure National Unity and Integration
and creation of a balanced socio-economic and income growth ;
Now, Therefore, I, Maithripala Sirisena, in pursuance of the provisions of Article 33 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and reposing great trust and confidence in your prudence, ability and fidelity,
do by these presents, appoint you the said :

as members of the Presidential Task Force chaired by me to direct, coordinate and monitor the Northern and Eastern
provincial development and direct you to take all such steps as may be considered necessary to proceed with the
implementation of the tasks as set out at (a) to (g) below :
(a) To review status of progress of various sectoral plans, programmes and projects now being implemented in the
Northern and Eastern Provinces.
(b) To initiate and formulate new plans, programmes and projects targeting sectors and groups that need priority
attention, and facilitate finances for projects/programmes and other actions.
(c) To intervene and direct as necessary, in matters relating to all development activities in the Provinces.
(d) To encourage and enlist the support and participation of the private sector, by-lateral and multilateral development
institutions, for investing and implementing development projects and programmes in the two Provinces.
(e) To coordinate work between the Government and Provincial administrations and between Public and Private
Sectors and non-governmental and civil society sections.
(f) To carry out overall monitoring of the implementation of the development activities in the regions.
(g) To identify any constraints which impede expeditious implementation of development programmes in the regions
and advise the relevant authorities on speedy solutions to overcome them.
And, I, further appoint Mr. V . Sivagnanasothy as Secretary to the Task Force
AND I, do hereby authorize the Task Force which is established to issue instructions made such inquiries and
investigate such matters as may be considered necessary in relation to the execution of the tasks the said Task Force is
hereby entrusted with ;
AND I, do hereby require and direct all public officers and such other officers to whom the Task Force which is
established to direct, coordinate and monitor the development activities in the Northern and Eastern Provinces may issue
instructions or apply for assistance for information to comply with such information, render all such assistance and furnish
all such information as may be properly complied with, rendered and furnished in that behalf.
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the day of 05th of June in
the year Two Thousand and Eighteen
                                                                   By  His Excellency's command,
S
UMITH ABEYSINGHE,
Acting Secretary to the President.
