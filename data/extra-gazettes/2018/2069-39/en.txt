THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE  NOTIFICATION
Wages Board for the Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing and Re- Building), Plastic and Petroleum Resin Products
Manufacturing Trade
IN accordance with the provisions of Section 28 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) the Wages Board for the
Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing and Re-Building), Plastic and Petroleum Resin Products Manufacturing Trade hereby
notifies that the Board by virtue of powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance proposes to vary in the manner
set out in the schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 25th of May 2018.
Every such objection must be made in writting and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection
 is taken.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing
and Re- Building), Plastic and Petroleum
     Resin Products Manufacturing Trade.
Department of Labour
Colombo 05.
1st May, 2018
