CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha (Divisional Council of Pujapitiya)
IMPOSING   OF  ENTERTAINMENT   TAX
IT is hereby informed, according to sub section (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance Chapter 267, that the following
proposal which is decided by the Secretary of the Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha (Divisional Council of Pujapitiya), in terms
of the provisions of Section 2(1) of the above Ordinance, has been accepted by the Minister in-charge for the subject of
local governments of the provincial council of the central province, in terms of the provisions of sub section (2) of the
said ordinance.
R. M. G. S. R
ATHNAYAKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Local
Government Ministry,
Central Province.
At Pallekale,
12th October 2018.
PROPOSAL
I have decided to levy an Entertaiment Tax of 10% from the ticket fares (except the entertainment tax) within the
limits of the Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha (Divisional Council of Pujapitiya), in terms of the powers vested to me according
to Section 9(3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and in terms of provisions of Section 2(1) of the Entertainment
Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 further amended by the Entertainment Tax Act, No. 37 of 1984.
