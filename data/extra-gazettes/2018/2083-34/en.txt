FINANCE  BUSINESS  ACT,   No.  42   OF   2011
CANCELLATION  of a licence to carry on finance business under Section 37 of  the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011.
DR. INDRAJIT COOMARASWAMY,
Chairman of the Monetary Board and the Governor,
of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
The public is hereby informed that in terms of Section 37 of  the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, the
Monetary Board of  the Central Bank of  Sri Lanka, has decided to cancel the licence issued to The Standard Credit
Finance Limited under the said Finance Business Act with effect from 25
th July 2018. Accordingly, The Standard Credit
Finance Limited is not permitted to carry on finance business with effect from 25 th July 2018.
