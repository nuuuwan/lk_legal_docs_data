NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  3(1)  OF  THE  REGISTRATION   OF  ELECTORS   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,
No.  10  OF  2017
Supplementary  Register  of Electors - 2018
FOR THE SRI LANKAN CITIZENS WHO HA VE BEEN INTERNALLY DISPLACED AS A RESULT OF ANY ACTIONS OF
A TERRORIST MILITANT OR OTHER GROUP DURING THE RECENT PAST AND WHOSE NAMES HA VE NOT BEEN
REGISTERED AS ELECTORS ELSEWHERE IN THE ISLAND
THE calling for applications in respect of the preparation of the Supplementary Register on the above matter has
expired on 01.11.2018. Any person who is eligible under the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act to be
registered in the supplementary register but whose name does not appear in the said register, may appeal in writing to the
Registering Officer of the respective Election Office on the format set out in the aforesaid Registration of Electors Act,
in order to have his/her name entered or retained as the case may be in the register. Every appeal in this regard should be
made so as to reach the Registering Officer of the respective District Election Office within two weeks (until 15th
November 2018) from the date of this notice published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Any person whose name is included in the supplementary register relating to the aforementioned electoral district
may object to the entry or retention of his/her own name or that any other person in the register. Every objection should be
made in writing in duplicate and should be substantially in Form “C” set out in the aforesaid Act and be submitted to the
Registering Officer of the respective District Election Office within two weeks (until 15th November 2018) from the date of the
respective notice published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Forms to make a claim, to enter or retain a name in the register or to submit objections to any claim to enter or retain
any name in the register may be obtained from the Election Office of the respective administrative district.
By order of the Election Commission.
H. M. T. D. H
ERA TH,
Secretary,
Election Commission.
At the Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
on this 02nd day November, 2018.
