FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Muhammadu
Sali Muhammadu Sareepu   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A(1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of that 23- Madulbowa of Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Muhammadu Lareef Muhammadu Mansoor to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Muhammadu Lareef Muhammadu Mansoor was elected as the member of the 23- Madulbowa of Mawanella Pradeshiya
Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
19th October  2018.
