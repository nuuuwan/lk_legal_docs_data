THE   MOTOR  TRAFFIC  (CHAPTER  203)
Regulations made by the Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation under Section 237 read with Section 143 of the Motor
Traffic Act (Chapter 203)
NIMAL SIRIPALA DE SIL V A,
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Colombo,
15th January, 2018.
Regulations
1. The Motor Traffic (Expressway) Regulations, No. 2 of 2011 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1717/14 of
August 3, 2011 are hereby amended by the insertion immediately after item 6 of the Schedule thereof, the following item
which shall have effect as item 7 thereof :-
“7. Quadricycle”.
2. The provisions of these regulations shall come into force on January 20, 2018.
