1A   G   28919 —17  (11/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  KALUTARA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Janage Don Shantha Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member if United People’s Freedom Alliance who  belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Suduhakuruge Ketin to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pahala Ralalage Nalaka Hemantha Rathnayake, returning officer of the Kalutara Pradeshiya
Sabha acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Suduhakuruge Ketin was selected as the member of the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
N
ALAKA R A THNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
08th November, 2018,
