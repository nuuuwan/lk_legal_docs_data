THE  SPORTS  LAW,  No.  25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sports under Section 41 read with Section 31 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973.
FAISZER M USTHAPA,
President’s Councel,
Minister of Provincial Councils,
Local Government and Sports.
Colombo,
25th  October, 2018.
Regulations
Whereas by  Gazette Notification bearing No. 2073/15 dated 31.05.2018, I Faiszer Musthapa, PC, Minister of Provincial
Councils, Local Government and Sports appointed Mr. Hewa Thondilage Kamal Pathmasiri, Secretary of the Ministry of
Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports as the Competent Authority of Sri Lanka Cricket with effect from
01st June 2018 to continue with administration and perform any such other functions necessary for the due administration
of Sri Lanka Cricket.
And whereas Sri Lanka Cricket has consequently undertaken to the International Cricket Council, the international
Governing body for the Sport of Cricket to appoint an advisory committee to assist and advise the said competent authority
in carrying out the administration and perform any such other functions necessary for the due administration of Sri Lanka
Cricket until such time an election is held to elect office bearers to Sri Lanka Cricket.
Accordingly, I Faiszer Musthapa, PC Minister of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports, in terms of
Section 41 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973 as amended hereby appoint -
(a) The Secretary to the Ministry of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports as the Competent
Authority of Sri Lanka Cricket with effect from 01st October 2018 to continue with the administration
and perform any such other functions necessary for the due administration of Sri Lanka Cricket.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk

(b) I hereby also appoint five persons of repute as an advisory committee to assist the said competent authority

of Sri Lanka Cricket.
(c) The said advisory committee shall continue to function until such time an election is held to elect office

(d) On the death, resignation, removal or other incapacity of a particular member of the said advisory committee,
