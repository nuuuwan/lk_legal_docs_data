No. 2102/24 –  TUESDAY, DECEMBER,  18,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Declaration under Section 07 
 
My Ref. No.: WA/18/02/2014. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2016/D/91. 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the  following Lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The land allotments of land described below in total 
extent of 0.0671 Hectares, shown as Lot 1 in the 
preliminary plan dated 03.12.2018 for Gam 4121 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in Village of 
Nawammahara, the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 179, 
Elapitiwela the minor division of Ragam Paththuwa in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wattala in the District 
of Gampaha, in western province and bounded as follows: 
     
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Kajugahakumbura; Description of 
Land : Open Marshy Land; Claimant: Padmika Gayan 
Ruwanpura, No.79/2, Temple Lane, Papiliyana Road, 
Nedimala, Dehiwela; Original Claimant: -; Extent : 
0.0671  Hectare.  
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid is hereby required 
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in 
writing before me at Divisional Secretariat Wattala on the 
20th Day of March 2019 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 20th Day of March 2019 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
    
 P. D. T. C. RAJIKA, 
 Divisional Secretary , 
 Wattala. 
 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Wattala. 
 
17th December, 2018.
