1A   G   28944 —17  (11/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  GALLE  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Galle Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kariyawasam
Thelikada Gamage Thisara Amarathunga the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member if People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Keerthi Kumara Seneviratne to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Piyaleel Jayasinghe, returning officer of the Galle
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Keerthi Kumara Seneviratne was selected as the member of the Galle Municipal Council.
J. A. S. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Galle Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Galle,
09th November, 2018,
