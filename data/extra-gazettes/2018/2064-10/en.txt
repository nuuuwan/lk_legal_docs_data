EXTRAORDINARY
WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification made under Section 66c of Local Government Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by
Local Government Authorities Electoins (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012
I, Chandrani Samarakoon, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province as per powers entrusted to me
under Clause 66C of Local Government Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local Government Authorities Elections
(Amendment) Statute No. 22 of 2012 do hereby announce that the recalling meetings of the Local Government Authorities
of Western Province, names of which mentioned in the Schedule hereunder to be held for the purpose of selecting Mayor,
Deputy Mayor, will be convened on the date, time and place correspondingly stated in the aforementioned schedule.
C
HANDRANI  S AMARAKOON ,
Commissioner of Local Government
Western Province.
At Western Provincial Department of Local Government,
No. 02, Cambridge Terrace,
Colombo 07,
On the date of 26th March 2018.
