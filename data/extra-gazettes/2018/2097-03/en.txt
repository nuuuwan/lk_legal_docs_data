EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every
article specified in the Column I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from November 12, 2018.
The order made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2079/58 of July 12, 2018 is hereby recinded with effect from November 12, 2018.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
November 12, 2018.
