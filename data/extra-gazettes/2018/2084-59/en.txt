PENSION MINUTE
Notification under Section 51 of the Pensions Minute dated 05th February 1934 revised by
the Pension Minute (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICATION
THE Pension Minute is further revised with the inclusion of new Sections mentioned below with the removal of the
revision which has been made including Sub Section I by the Gazette notification of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka No. 1750/1 dated 19th March, 2012, after Section 02 of the Pension Minute dated 05.02.1934, which has been
revised from time to time. Provisions of the Minute under Section 2 and 3 shall further remain unchanged.
01. (a) When the pension of the retired Government Officers is revised re-employing them on contract basis or
exigency of service on or after 19th March, 2012, the total gratuity paid to them previously shall be recovered
from the new award granted to them.
(b) When the pension of Officers who have been re-employed before 19th March, 2012 is revised the balance of
the gratuity excluding the value of the gratuity recovered up to  the relevant date from the gratuity paid
according to the award made to them previously  should be deducted from the gratuity which is entitled after
the revision and if there is any amount to be paid back it should be paid after making necessary adjustments.
