1A – G28484 – 14 (2018/09) PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of  land called Pussellagehenedeniya in total extent
of  about 0.4351 Hectare depicted as Lot A in the Advanced Tracing
No. R/EHL/2015/994 dated 17.06.2016  situated in the Village called
Kendangamuwa Town (F.V .P. 691) in Kuruwita Korale Palle Pattuwa
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's  Division of Eheliyagoda
in Rathnapura District of the Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pussellagehenedeniya – Extent: About 0.4351
Hectare
North: Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha Premises and Pussellagehene
Watta claimed by Ra/Eheliyagoda Central College; East:
Pussellagehene Watta claimed by Ra/Eheliyagoda Central College
and Lot B; South: Lot B, Pussellagehenedeniya claimed by L. R.
Dharmasiri and some others and Road (R.D.A.); West:
Pussellagehenedeniya claimed by L. R. Dharmasiri and some others
and Road (R.D.A.) and Pussellagehene Watta in which the
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha is located.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
