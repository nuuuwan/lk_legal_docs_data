Order pertaining to the delegation of powers of the Western Provincial Public Service under Section 32(2a)
of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 as amended by the Provincial Councils (Amendment)
Act No. 28 of 1990
WHEREAS the Governor of the Western Province by his Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2046/8 dated 26.03.2018, delegated to the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, the power of appointment,
transfer,promotion, dismissal from service and disciplinary control of officers of the Western Provincial Public
Service;From and out of the powers so delegated, the powers to the extent provided for in each Schedule, from Schedules
01 to 07 hereto, are hereby delegated to the officers specifically mentioned in column I of each of the said Schedules,
being powers pertaining to the appointment, transfer, promotion, dismissal and disciplinary control as are specified in
corresponding column III of the Schedules, in respect of the categories of public officers as specified in column II of the
Schedule thereto, in conformity with the provisions in clause 32 (2a) of the Provincial Councils Act No. 42 of 1987 as
amended by the Provincial Councils Act, No. 28 of 1990.
Provided however, in the event of any question arising in respect of any delegation or as to the implementation of
the delegation hereof, made by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, the decision of the said Commission
shall be final and conclusive. The Commission hereby directs that in the course of implementing the powers hereby
delegated to the Provincial Chief Secretary or to any officer by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, all
the decisions taken by such officer shall be implemented subject to the provisions in clause 32 (2a) and 32 (2b) of the
Provincial Councils Act No. 42 of 1987 as amended by the Provincial Councils (Amended) Act, No. 28 of 1990.
The said Commission specifically directs hereby that whoever officer to whom the aforementioned powers have
been delegated, they, at all times of implementing the powers so delegated to them, shall act in accordance with the
procedural rules of the Western Provincial Public Service made by the Governor of the Western Province acting in terms
of clause 32 (3) of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 and also in conformity with the Establishment Code and:
any conditions rules, regulations and other procedures decided upon from time to time by the Governor of the Western
Province and the Western Provincial Public Service Commission.
