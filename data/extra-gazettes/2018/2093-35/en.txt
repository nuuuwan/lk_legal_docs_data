THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of lands in total extent of about 1.2141 Hectare
depicted as Lot A and in the Advance Tracing No. BT/KTT/2017/20
dated 31.03.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the
Village called New Kattankudy ward No. 6 within the Urban Council
Limits of Kattankudy, in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 167/B,
New Kattankudy East in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kattankudy in Batticaloa District of Eastern Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Alenodey – Extent : About 0.0821 Hectare
North: Water Hole and Beach Road; East: Beach Road and Lot B;
South: Lot B; West: Lot B, Alenody Garden claimed by U. L. Suhara
Ummah and others and Water Hole.
