FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF THE MANTHAI WEST PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER SECTION 66A(1) (a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party the recognized Political Party to which Abdul
Hameedu Sanoos the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 04, Vidataltivu of  Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary  of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Saveriyan Ferdinant to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I. Samarasinghe Appuhamilage Darsha Praveena, Returning Officer of the Manthai West
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that Saveriyan Ferdinant was selected as the member of the Ward No. 04, Vidataltivu of
Manthai West Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A. D
ARSHA P RA VEENA,
Returning Officer,
Manthai   West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mannar,
21st June, 2018.
