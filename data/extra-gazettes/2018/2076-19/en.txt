FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  66A(1)  (1a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 292)
WHEREAS the office of Member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which MAYINATHTHUGE Rasika Udeni the Member elected
to Ward No. 07 Pamunuwa of Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 07 Pamunuwa of Maharagama Urban Council who belongs
to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is Qualified under section 8 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as member within a period of thirty                               (30)
days;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Heenatigala Madinage Rasika to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) d o hereby declare that
Heenatigala Madinage Rasika was elected as the Member of the Ward No. 07 Pamunuwa of Maharagama Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. RUPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
20th June 2018.
