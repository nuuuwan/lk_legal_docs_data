STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT    PROJECTS  ACT,  No. 14  OF 2008
Revocation  of  Notification  under  Section 3(4)
I, Malik Samarawickrama, being the Minister in charge of the subject of Development Strategies and International Trade
declare by this notification that the following Gazette Notification shall hereby be rescinded :
1. The Notification published under Sub-section (4) of the Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects
Act, No. 14 of 2008 as amended, in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1703/46 dated 29.04.2011.
MALIK S AMARAWICKRAMA , MP,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
20th August, 2018.
