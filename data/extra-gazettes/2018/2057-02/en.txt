No. 2057/2 – MONDAY, FEBRUARY 05, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 5.2970 Hectares
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/UDP/2017/407 and dated
09.10.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Yatipiyangala in the Grama Niladhari Division of Galatha in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Udapalatha in Kandy District,
Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Devita Maddeliyahena – Extent: 5.2970 Hectares
North: Mahaweli River and Atabage Oya; East: Atabage Oya and
Kothmale tail race; South: Kothmale tail race, Atabagewatta claimed
by State and Mahaweli River; West: Mahaweli River.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
