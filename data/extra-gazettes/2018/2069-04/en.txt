THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: MO/ED/03/ACQ.
Land Min. No.: 4/3/8/2015/PC/87.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a Monaragala Mahanama Primary College.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of land  in  F.V .P. No. 172, Supplement No. 393, Sheet
No. 434, prepared by the Superintendent Surveyor in Monaragala
situated in Muppane Village in the Monaragala, Divisional Secretary's
Division of the District of Monaragala in Uva Province described as
follows:
Name of the land: Pallearawewattha alias Helahenewetawattha; Total
extent: 0.3661 Hectare.
01. Lot No. 3449
Name of land: Misiyaketumulleroda; Claimant: State; Prior
Claimant: 1. Wijeweera Gunawardhana Mahawidanalage Elbert Silva,
2. Wijeweera Gunawardhana Mahawidanalage Gebbrial, 3. Wijeweera
Gunawardhana Mahawidanalage Elan Nona, 4. Wijeweera
Gunawardhana Mahawidanalage Sopi Nona; Extent: 0.3661  Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Moneragala Divisional Secretariat
on the 29th day of May, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 15th day of
May, 2018, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
G . U. K. Algewaththage,
Divisional Secretary,
