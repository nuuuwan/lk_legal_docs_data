THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. : PTB/LAND/ACQVI/07/38.
Land Ministry No. : 4-3/15/2016/PC/432.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
servitude which is required for a Public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The following allotment of land is situated at villages of
Waliwa in Divisional Secretary's Division Pitabaddara in
the District of Mathara Southern Province and marked in
Surveyor General Plan No F.V .P . 10, Sheet No. 102 and
Supplement No. 88 Servey Requisition No. MR/PTB/2018/
164 and Date 15/03/2018. Containg in Extent 2.0234 Hectares.
Lot No. - 1671
Name of the Land: Morawaka Waththa (Radagewaththa
and Katuanagewaththa); North: Lot Nos. 81 and 76; East:
Lot Nos. 76 and 81; South and West: Lot No. 81; Extent:
2.0234 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly  authorized in writing,
before me at the Divisional Secretariat - Pitabeddara on the
20th February 2019 of at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested
to notify to me in   writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13th
February 2019 the nature of their interests in the Land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude*, the amount of compensation and
the details of the Computation of such amount.
K. G. R. JAYANATH,
Divisional Secretary,
Pitabeddara.
Divisional Secretary's Office
05th December 2018.
