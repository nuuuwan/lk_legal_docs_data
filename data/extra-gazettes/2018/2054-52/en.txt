1A – G 26968 – 12 (2018/01)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatileka, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Kuttapitiya Watta Kirindiella Division
in total extent of about 0.4173 Hectare depicted as Lot 'A' in Advance
Tracing No. R/PLM/2015/886 and dated 06.04.2016 situated in the
Village called Kuttapitiya in the Minor Division of Navadun Korale
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Pelmadulla in Ratnapura
District of the Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' – Kuttapitiya Watta Kirindiella Division –  Extent:
0.4173 Hectare
North: Kuttapitiya Watta Kirindiella Division claimed by Ven.
Bengamuwe Dammadinna Thero; East: Kuttapitiya Watta Kirindiella
Division claimed by Ven. Bengamuwe Dammadinna Thero and Road;
South: Road and Kuttapitiya Watta Kirindiella Division claimed by
Ven. Bengamuwe Dammadinna Thero; West: Kuttapitiya Watta
Kirindiella Division claimed by Ven. Bengamuwe Dammadinna Thero.
