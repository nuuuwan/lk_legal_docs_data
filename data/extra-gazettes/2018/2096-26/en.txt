THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
WHEREAS, I have, in terms of paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, determined the ministry and  the subjects and functions assigned  to the State Minister of Petroleum Resources
Development appointed  by me as a  Minister who is not a member of the Cabinet of Ministers in the exercise  of powers
vested in me under paragraph (1) of Article 44 of the  Constitution, it  is  hereby  notified  that the  subjects and functions
of the aforesaid  Ministry  and the Departments, State Corporations and Statutory Institutions pertaining  to the said
Ministry  shall be as set out  in the  schedule  hereto from  November 07, 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 07, 2018.
