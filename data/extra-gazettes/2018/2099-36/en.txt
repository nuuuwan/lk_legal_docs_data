No. 2099/36 –  FRIDAY, NOVEMBER,  30,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 07 
 
My No.: 11/3/3/1/32B 
Land Ministry’s No.: LD/05/2010/MV/54. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under sub section (1) of Section 7 of  
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition  (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intended to acquire the following land which 
is required for public purpose:- 
 
      Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Public purpose means the land Acquisition for the 
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project. 
 
The lands described below, containing in extent 0.1723 
Hectare shown in Topo PP 13 of supplement No. 98, 
sheet No. 249, approved by Surveyor General dated on 
14.11.2018, situated at E 389 Rambukoluwa Division of 
Laggala Pallesiya Pattuwa, Laggala Pallegama Divisional 
Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central Province. 
     
01. Lot No. 9578 
Name of the Land: Galwangediya Kumbura; 
Description of the Land : Paddy field; Claimant : Private; 
Possessed by: Ihala Gedara Punchi Banda of 
Rambukoluwa, Laggala Pallegama;  Extent : 0.1723 
Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents before me at 
…….. a.m. on the 04 day of  Januray 2019 at the Laggala 
Divisional Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27 day of 
December 2018, the nature of their interests in the Land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
    
 
 S. D. P. JAYASOORIYA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Laggala Division. 
 
 
 On the 26 day of November, 2018, 
At Laggala Divisional Secretariat.
