PILGRIMAGES   ORDINANCE
GENERAL Public is hereby notified that Annual Esala Festival of Seenigama Sri Devol Maha Devala being functioned
ceremonially in Grama Niladhari Division - Seenigama, Divisional Secretariat Division - Hikkaduwa of the Galle District,
Southern Province, scheduled to be held during the period from Thursday the 13th of September, 2018 to Thursday the
20th September, 2018 as per the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175) in terms of the regulations laid
down by the Extraordinary Gazatte Notification No. 1965/39 dated 06.05.2016 of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka.
S
OMARA THNA V IDANAPATHIRANA ,
District Secretary/Government Agent,
Administrative District, Galle
At the District Secretariat,
Galle,
31st July, 2018.
