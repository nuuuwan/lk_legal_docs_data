EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 as amended,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that, the Excise Duty on every
article specified in the  Column I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from September 18, 2018.
MANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
 Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
17th September, 2018.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
