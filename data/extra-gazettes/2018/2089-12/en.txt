THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER  460)
Order under Section 49A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 49A of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) read with Article 44 of the
Constitution, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, declare that the land specified in the Schedule hereto is required by the
Board of Investment of Sri Lanka being a public corporation established by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law,
No. 4 of 1978.
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
14th September, 2018.
SCHEDULE
The allotment of land from Lot 4 depicted in Plan No. S/Mis/450 dated 22.01.2016 made by S. R. Ileperumachchi,
Registered Licensed Surveyor, which is 77.36 ha. (191A., 00R., 25P.) in extent and situated at Arabokka village of Hambantota
Divisional Secretariat Division in Magam Pattu in Hambantota District of Sourthern Province of which the boundaries are as
follows :
Lot No. 4
North : Lot 3, Road (Highway) & Lot 5 ;
East : Lot 5 ;
South : Lot 5, Sri Lanka Ports Authority Land & Lot 3 ;
West : Lot 3.
Extent - 77.36 ha. (191A., 00R., 25P.)
