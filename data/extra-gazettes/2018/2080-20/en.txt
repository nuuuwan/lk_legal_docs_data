for the Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing and Re-Building), Plastic and Petroleum Resin Products Manufacturing Trade
made under Section 30 of that Ordinance and specified in the Schedule hereto have been approved by the Hon. Minister of
Labour and Trade Union Relations.
