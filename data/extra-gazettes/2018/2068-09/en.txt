EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 as amended,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that the rate of Excise duty
charged, levied and collected on imporation of motor vehicle bearing Chassis No. MR0KB8CC401054677 and Engine
No. 2GD-0416778, consigned to and cleared by General (Rtd) Hamilton Wanasinghe, holder of the National Identity
Card No. 332310097 V shall be 35% of the payable Excise duty as specified in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017. The imported motor vehicle should registered in the name of the consignee and
should not be transferred for the period of 05 years from the date of registation. The above concessionary rate of Excise
duty is granted as per the approval of the Cabinet of Ministers.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
24th April 2018.
