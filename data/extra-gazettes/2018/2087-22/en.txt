FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF   THE  KEGALLE PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Kalu
Arachchilalage Janaranjana Saman Kumara Kalu Arachchi the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a)
of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 14-Meedeniya of Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and  who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Dapanage Nisala Sanjeewa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Dapanage
Nisala Sanjeewa was elected as the member of the No. 14 - Meedeniya of Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
04th September, 2018.
