THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 My  No.: GODS/LND/05/ACQ/39. 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/9/2012/WS/193. 
 
I,  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following lands which are required for  
public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired:  
Allotments of land, depicted as Lot No. 661, 663, 664 
in page No. 19 of additional No. 19 in Plan No. A. G. P. 
697 prepared by Survey General situated in the village of 
Masimbula in No. 237, Masimbula Grama Niladari 
Division in Divisional Secretaries Division of 
Godakawela in sub division Madapaththuwa, Atakalan 
Korale, Rathnapura District Sabaragamuwa Province and 
bounded as follows: 
 
Schedule 
 
 01. Lot No. 661 
 Name of the Land: Kudagodahena; Claimant of the 
land: Government; Primary Claimant: Mrs. N. M. P. 
Jinadasa, Kudagodawaththa, Masimbula, Godakawela; 
Extent: 0.0041 Hectare.    
 02. Lot No. 663 
 Name of the Land: Galkotuwa; Claimant of the land: 
Government; Extent: 0.0129 Hectare. 
 
 03. Lot No. 664 
 Name of the Land: Galkotuwa; Claimant of the land: 
Government; Extent: 0.0039 Hectare. 
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid  Lands are  
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Godakawela on  18th October 2018 at 9.00 a. m.  
 
And are  hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 03rd October 2018 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. In order to proof the 
ownership relevant documents and deeds are submitted 
on the date of inquiry. 
 
   B. S. G. RUWANSIRI,

 Godakawela.
