THE  LAND  ACQUISITION    ACT    (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Mini's Ref.  No. : KDS/GIK/4/1/3/19.
My Ref. No.:4-3/15/2013/VE/95.
I hereby give notice to  under Sub- section (1) of the Section
(7), of  the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment)
that the Government intends to acquire the following land
which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three allotments of land in total extent of 10.1171 hectares,
depicted as lot No. 01, 02 and 03 in the Perliminary Plan No.
Maha 6639 dated 18.05.2017 situated in the Village called
Pellapitiya in the Grama Niladhari Division of Malwattagama
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Ganga Ihala Korale in
Kandy District of the Central Province and more fully described
as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name  of  the land: Kurunduwatta; Description: -; Claimant:
Robert W. Hali; Total Extent: 9.8685 Hectares.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the land: Kurunduwatta; Claimant: Robert W . Hali;
Extent: 0.1107 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of the land: Kurunduwatta; Claimant: Robert W . Hali;
Extent: 0.1379 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Ganga Ihala Korale Divisional
Secretariat on 21.02.2018 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 15.02.2018
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
K. G . Lanerolle,
Divisional Secretary,
Ganga Ihala Korale.
At Divisional Secretariat, Ganga Ihala Korale,
03rd of January 2018.
