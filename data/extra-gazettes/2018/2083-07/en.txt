No. 2083/7 – TUESDAY, AUGUST 07, 2018
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Matale Pradeshiya Sabha
ENTERTAINMENT   TAXATION
ACCORDING to the powers conferred in (Chapter 267) second part (I) Sub-section of Entertainment Tax Ordinance, under-
mentioned resolution determined by the Secretary of Matale Pradeshiya Sabha as the powers specified in Section II of the
Ordinance on Entertainment Taxation is approved by the Minister in charge for Local Government, Central Provincial Council
as per the second Sub-section of above ordinance is hereby published based on its second sub section.
R. M. P. S. RATHNAY AKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Local Government Ministry,
Central Province.
At Pallekele.
14th February, 2018.
RESOLUTION
On the powers conferred in section 9 (3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, when it is held to conduct the entertainment
programs in the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha Authority it has been determined to impose an entertainment tax of 15% of the fee
for cinematic view and 20% of the fee for all other entertainment programs from the persons who organize the above as per the
Section (2)1 of No. 12 of 1946 Tax Ordinance and Section 37 of the 1984 Tax Amendment Act.
