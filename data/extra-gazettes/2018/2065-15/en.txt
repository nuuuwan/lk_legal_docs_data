1A   G   27412 —17  (04/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  WARD  11  DIKELLA  OF
DEHIOWITA  PRADESHIYA   SABAH   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Kankanamalage Gamini Karunaratna, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward 11 Dikella if Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Hewaralalage Yashoda Lalanga Padmasiri to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, returning officer of the Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Hewaralalage
Yashoda Lalanga Padmasiri was elected as the member of the Ward 11 Dikella of Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
