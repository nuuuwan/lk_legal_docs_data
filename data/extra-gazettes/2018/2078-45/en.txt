THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Allotments of land indicated in following names and extents
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KE/YTY/2015/101 and dated
2016.06.30 prepared by the Superintendent of Survey of Kegalle
District, situated  in the Village of Mahabage  (Dunukedeniya) in
Western Minor Division of Patha Bulathgama of the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Yatiyantota in Kegalle District of the
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follow:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Lelwalathennawatta – Extent: 0.0336 Hectare
North: Lelwalathennawatta and road; East: Lelwalathennawatta;
South: Girankiththeoya, Kithulgalawatta and Lot B; West:
Lelwalathennawatta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kithulgalawatta – Extent: 0.1060 Hectare
North: Kithulgalawatta, Lelwalathennawatta and Lot No. A; East:
Kithulgalawatta and Girankiththe Oya; South: Girankiththeoya,
Kithulgalawatta Stream and Lot C; West: Kithulgalawatta.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Extent: 0.0006 Hectare
North: Stream, Kithulgalawatta and Lot B; East: Kithulgalawatta
and Stream; South: Stream, Kithulgalawatta and Lot D; West: Lot D,
Kithulgalawatta and Stream.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Kithulgalawatta – Extent: 0.0855 Hectare
North: Kithulgalawatta, Stream  and Lot C; East: Kithulgalawatta
and Girankiththe oya; South: Kithulgalawatta, Bulukanuwawatta
and Lot  E; West: Bulukanuwawatta and Kithulgalawatta.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Bulukanuwawatta – Extent: 0.0336 Hectare
North: Bulukanuwawatta, Kithulgalawatta and Lot D; East:
Bulukanuwawatta; South: Bulukanuwawatta and Lot F; West: Lot
F and Bulukanuwawatta.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Bulukanuwawatta – Extent: 0.0117 Hectare
North: Bulukanuwawatta; East: Lot E; South: Bulukanuwawatta;
West: Lot G (Stream).
07. Lot ‘G’ – Extent: 0.0006 Hectare
North: Stream; East: Lot F; South: Stream; West: Lot H.
08. Lot ‘H’ – Bulukanuwawatta – Extent: 0.0217 Hectare
North: Bulukanuwawatta; East: Lot G; South: Bulukanuwawatta,
Girankiththe Oya and Lot G; West: Bulukanuwawatta.
