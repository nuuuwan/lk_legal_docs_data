FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  WANATHA VILLUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Wanathavilluwa  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the leader of the Independent Group 01, the Independent Group  to which Senadheera Dumunnage
Douglas Indika Senadeera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon returned member if Independent Group 01 who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group, has communicated his decision to nominate
Katuwala Kande Gamladdalage Pradeep Aruna Kantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE N. Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Wanathavilluwa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Katuwala Kande
Gamladdalage Pradeep Aruna Kantha was selected as the member of the Wanathavilluwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Wanathavilluwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
12th June, 2018.
