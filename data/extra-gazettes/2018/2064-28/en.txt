THE  LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under section 7
       Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/LI/3978.
Land Ministry Reference No.:  4-3/11/2015/NRD/217.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 Government intends to
acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose:
The following Land situated in the village of Thalpavila East
in No. 430A Thalpavila North Grama Niladhari division in the
Matara Divisional Secretariat division, Matara District,
Southern Province and depicted as Lot No. 01 in Primary Plan
Mara 2725 has been acquired to the renovation of houses due
to the construction of Matara-Katharagama new railway line
and acquisition of houses on the technical requirements of
the site.
SCHEDULE
01. Lot No. 1
Name of Land: Hill Crest Watta; Description: Garden,
permanent building, Toilet and Well; Claimant: Priyanthi
1A - G 27376 - 17(2018/03)
Gunaweera, Y asa Isuru, Thalpavila North, Kekanadura; Extent:
0.0479 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 27th April, 2018 at
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 20th of April the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
compensation of such amount.
