1A   G   28169 —17  (07/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   WELIMADA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member in Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Wijesekara
Mudiyanselage Gunawardhana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon  returned member People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Dissananayake Mudiyanselage Ajith Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Hathurusinghalage Indika Ranjan Hathurusinghe, returning officer of Welimada  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Dissananayake Mudiyanselage Ajith Kumara was selected as the member of the Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Badulla,
24th July, 2018,
