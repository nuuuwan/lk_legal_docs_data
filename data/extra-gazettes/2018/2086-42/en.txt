THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref.No.: Ambako/3/4/A/89.
Land Ministry of Lands and Parlimentary Reforms Reference
No.: 13/99/E/61.
I hereby give notice,  under Sub section (1) of section 7 of the
land Acquisition Act, as amended by the  Land Acquisition
(Amendment)Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the Government
intends to acquire the following Land/ servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land or servitute to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 of Hatton Estate below name in total extent about
0.8077 Hectare, shown in P .P . No. 3099 and dated 26.02.2018
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveyor
NuwaraEliya District, situated in the Hatton Dickoya Urban
Limits  in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Ambagamuwa,
in the Distric of Nuwara Eliya.
01. Lot No. 01 - Claimants: Land Reform Commission,
475, Kaduwela Road, Battaramulla; Extent:0.8077 Hectares;
Boundaries:
North: Stream; East: Stream and Hatton Estate; South:
Hatton Estate, P .P . Maha 763 Lot No. 01 and Hatton Estate;
West: Hatton Estate and Stream.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Ambagamuwa on 17.10.2018 at
2.00 p.m.  and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 10.10.2018, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for com-
pensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
R.T.P. SUMANASEKARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ambagamuwa Koralaya.
Divisional Secretariat,
Ambagamuwa.
28th August, 2018.
