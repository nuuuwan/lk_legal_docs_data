PART IV (A) – PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Provincial  Councils  Notifications
CENTRAL PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Gampola Urban Council
ENTERTAINMENT TAXATION
ACCORDING to the powers conferred in (Chapter 267) Second Part (1) Sub-section of Entertainment Tax Ordinance,
under-mentioned resolution determined by the Secretary of Gampola Urban Council which was approved by the Minister in
charge for Local Government, Central Provincial Council as per the powers specified in Section II of the Ordinance on
Entertainment Taxation is hereby published based on its second Sub-section.
R. M. P. S. R
ATHNAY AKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Ministry of Education and Local Government,
Central Province.
At Pallekele,
14th of February, 2018.
RESOLUTION
On the powers conferred to me in Section 27(3) of Urban Council Act, when it is held to conduct the entertainment programs
in the Gampola Urban Council Authority as specified in Section III of the Ordinance on Entertainment Taxation, it has been
determined to impose an Entertainment tax of 10% when the entrance fee is between Rs. 1.00 to Rs. 200, 12% when the entrance
fee is between Rs. 201 to Rs. 500, 15% when the entrance fee is over Rs. 501 and 7.5% of the value of the ticket when on
cinematic view as per the Section (2) 1 of No. 12 of 1946 Tax Ordinance and Section 37 of the 1984 Tax Amendment Act.
