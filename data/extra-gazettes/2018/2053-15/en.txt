2. Insertion of new regulation 7.4.2 (2) (c) to the regulations published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 2041/18 of
October 17, 2017.
Regulation 7.4.2 (2) (c)
In the event of inability of the Immediate Past President to carry out the functions of the office as Immediate Past
President due to ill health, death or being unavailable for a period exceeding six (6) months, a member of the College
of Past Presidents who does not hold a formal office at the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects shall be appointed by
the Council as the “Past President in Council” and shall hold office for the unexpired period of term of office of the
Immediate Past President whom he succeeds.
