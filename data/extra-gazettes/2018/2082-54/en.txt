THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 dated 10.04.2018 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2068/20 dated 25.04.2018 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in
the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 22.03.2018.
Mr. T. Nelson A. Fernando, No. 608/1, Galle Road, Panadura. of the one part
AND
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo - 05.of the other part
was referred to Mr. Nandasena Bedigama for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and
Trade Union Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said
Order dated 10.04.2018.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
24th July, 2018.

( 1956 REVISED EDITION)

AND
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo - 05.of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether Mr. T. Nelson A. Fernando who had worked as a Grade V Officer (Court Officer) of the Kalutara Regional Office of the
Sri Lanka Transport Board and worked in the same post on contract basis from 02.05.2011 to 28.10.2015 after retirement had
been caused injustice by the non-payment of salary relevant to the post, annual increments and increased allowances for the
services rendered during the period and if such injustice had been caused, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo this 16th day of July, 2018.
