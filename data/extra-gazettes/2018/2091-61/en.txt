NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  7  OF  THE  LAND
ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1964
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for a public purpose :-
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
Allotments of land called in extent 3.3712 of Hectares,
0.0166 of Hectare, 0.0632 of Hectare shown as lot 1379,
1896, 1897 in  FVP .27, FVP . 29 dated 10.08.2018 prepared
by the Surveyor General. Situated in with in the Pradeshiya
Sabha limits of Trincomalee No. 243, Uppuveli Grama
Niladhari Division, Divisional Secretariat Division of Town
& Gravets, Trincomalee District and bounded as follows.
Details Attached.
Lot No. 1379
North Lot: Lot 2; East Lots: Lots 104, 1898 and FVP 29;
South Lots: Lot 2, Lots 104, 1898, 1897, 102, Lot 2, Lots
1896 and 102 in FVP 29; West Lots: Lot 2, Road(Road
Development Authority) and Lot 2; Extent: 3.3712 Hectares.
Lot No. 1896
North Lot: Lot 1378, 2 in FVP 27 and Lot 102; East Lot:
Lot 102; South Lot: Lot 102; West Lots: Lot 102, Lots 2 and
1378 in FVP 27; Extent: 0.0166 Hectare.
Lot No. 1897
North Lots: Lots 1378, FVP 27 and Lot 1898; East Lots: Lots
1898, 1899 and Lot 102; South Lots: Lot 102; West Lots: Lots
2 and 1378 in FVP 27; Extent: 0.0632 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional secretariat Trincomalee on the 29th day of
October 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and you are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08th day
of October 2018, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
    All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
J.S. ARULRAJ,
Divisional Secretary,
District-Trincomalee.
26th September, 2018.
