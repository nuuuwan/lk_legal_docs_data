1A  - G 28195 — 22  (07/2018)
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President  of  the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of  the powers vested in
me by Section  4 of  the Commission of  Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby enlarge the Six Month period of time ending
31st July 2018 by five months from 31st July 2018 up to 31st December 2018 for the rendering of  the final report on
completion of  the said tasks of the Commission of Inquiry established on 31 st January 2018 by Notification published
in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2056/41 dated 02nd February 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
in terms of  the provisions of Section 2 of the aforesaid Act to investigate into alleged irregularities in connection with
Sri Lankan Airlines Limited, Sri Lanka Catering Limited and Mihin Lanka (Private) Limited during the period 01st January,
2006 to 31st January, 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
30th July, 2018.
