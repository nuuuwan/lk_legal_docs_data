PART IV (A) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 03.09.2018
 Statute No.……… of ……… Pawnbrokers (Amendments) of the
Southern Province Provincial Council
BE it passed by the Provincial Council of the Southern Province of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka as follows:
1. This statute may be cited as the Pawnbrokers Statute (Amendment) No. ………… of 2018 of the
Southern Province.
2. The word ‘‘consistence’’ depicted as ‘‘This Statue is consistence with the provisions of pawnbrokers
Ordinance No. 13 of 1942’’ in the first page of the pawnbrokers Statute No. 01 of 2013 of the Southern
Province shall be repealed and amended as ‘‘Inconsistence’’.
3. The new section mentioned below is hereby included to the main statute and subsequent to thereof
and the said section shall be come into force as II (1) of the main statute.
II (1) An interest incurred in respect of the deposit of  pawnbroker  business if any, should be credited
to the Provincial Council Fund.
4. In the event  of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this statute, the Sinhala text
shall prevail.
