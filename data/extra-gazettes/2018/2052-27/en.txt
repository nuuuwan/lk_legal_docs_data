PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Western Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under Section
65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Krishantha Yogadas
Pancharathnam Ram to fill such vacancy;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988,
read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby declare
Mr. Krishantha Yogadas Pancharathnam Ram, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Colombo in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
Mahinda Deshapriya - Chairman - Election Commission
N. J. Abeysekere - Member - Election Commission
S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole - Member - Election Commission
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
04th January, 2018.
