Greetings :
WHEREAS, I, Maithripala Sirisena, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka have, in pursuance of
the provisions of Section 2 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), established by Warrant dated 31st January
2018, a Presidential Commission of Inquiry to investigate and inquire into alleged irregularities committed in respect of
SriLankan Airlines Limited, Sri Lankan Catering Limited and Mihin Lanka (Pvt.) Limited
AND WHEREAS, the said Commission has requested that they be granted additional powers in order for them to
effectively accomplish the task entrusted to them in conducting inquiries and investigations ;
