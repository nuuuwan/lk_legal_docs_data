FOOD  ACT,  No.  26  OF  1980
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under subsection (2) of section 17 of the Food Act, No. 26 of 1980, I, Rajitha Senaratne,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine, do by this notification hereby declare, Mr. Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Bhathiya Nissantha Dissanayake, to be the Additional Approved Analyst for the Administrative area of
Kandy Municipal Council for the purposes of the said Act.
Dr. R
AJITHA SENARATNE,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine.
Colombo,
16th April 2018.
