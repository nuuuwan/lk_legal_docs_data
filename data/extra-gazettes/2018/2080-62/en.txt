PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
Statutory Determination No. 4642
Unique No. RA/275
STATUTORY  DETERMINATION  UNDER  SECTION  19  OF  LAND  REFORM  LAW  No. 1 OF  1972
SINCE, Mrs. Shyamini Tennakoon Adhihetty Gunarathna, the daugther of late Asanka Anoma Wijewardena Tennakoon
Adhihetty, at official Quarters of Gampola district judge, has made a late statutory declaration under Section 18 of the
Law with regard to the legacy of Asanka Anoma Wijewardena Tennakoon Adhihetty, when Land Reform Law came into
effect, she and her brothers and sisters were minors and her father was alive, the Land Reform Commission, as per the
powers vested in it under Section 19 of the Law, the said Commission has affected a part of statutory determination on
Walter Dayaratne Adhihetty's legacy. As he demised, specifying the portion of Agricultural Land owned by Walter Dayaratne
Adhihetty who is statutory lessee, which shall be allowed to retain, the agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the
statutory lessee is described in the Schedule below.
S
AMPATH S UBASINGHE  ARACHCHI ,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
No. 475,
Kaduwela Road,
Battaramulla,
17th July, 2018.
Schedule
An allomtment of land marked Lot Nos. 545, 548, 549, 552, 553, 554, 556, 558 and 559 of the land called Hakamuwa
Watta, situated in the village Hakamuwa, in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 180, Lower Hakamuwa, of Divisional
Secretariat Rathnapura in the District of Rathnapura Sabaragamuwa Province containing in extent 22 Acres, 3 Roods,
19.68 Perches (Hectares 9.2563) depicted in the final village plan No. 18 Supplement No. 34 sheet No. 34 made by the
Surveyor General, together with the plantation and buildings thereon.
