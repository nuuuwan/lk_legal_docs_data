FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PUTTALAM    URBAN
COUNCIL UNDER    SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Puttalam Urban Council   has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the Independent  Group 01 , the Independent  Group to  which  Nizamdeen
Mohamed Fazil  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,   to decide
upon returned   member if   Independent  Group 01  who belongs to such Independent  Group and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the aforesaid  Independent  Group, has  communicated his decision to nominate
Selladurai Naguleshwaran  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the  Puttalam Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Selladurai Naguleshwaran
was Selected as the member of the Puttalam Urban Council.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
09th  October  2018.
