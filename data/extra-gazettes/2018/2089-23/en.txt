THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms ministry, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.

about 0.0051 hectare depicted in advance tracing No. B/ACQ/HLE/
