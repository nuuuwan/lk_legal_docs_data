LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
             My No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/75.
Land Min's Ref .No.: 4-3/15/2017.
I , hereby notify , under Sub-section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendments) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for public purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of the land called Bogathugahahena or
Bokutugahahena situated at the Village of Morawaka in
the Grama Niladhari's Division of Morawaka (257) in the
Divisional Secretariat of Kotapola in the District of Matara
in the Southern Province, bearing lot No. 699 of sheet
No. 30 of Supplementary sheet No. 26 in the Final Village
Plan No. 4-40 prepared by the Survey General Containing
in extent about 0.2017 Hectares and boundaries of the land
as follows.
Lot No. 699
Land Called Bogathugahahena or Bokutugahahena
containing in extent about 0.2017 Hectares.
Owner of the Land : Ridiyagamage Nandawathie resided
nearby Fairy Mount Tea Factory of Morawaka.
On the North by:- Lot Nos. 698, 82F3 and 700
On the East by:- Lot Nos. 700 and 82E
On the South by:- Lot Nos. 82E and 698
On the West by:- Lot Nos. 698 and 82F3
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude is to
be acquired are hereby to appear personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional
Secretariat, Kotapola on 08.08.2018 at 10.00 o' clock.
Further, I hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 01.08.2018 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of compensation of such
amount.
GANESHA AMARASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Kotapola.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Kotapola.
03rd, July 2018.
