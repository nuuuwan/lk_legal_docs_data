1A – G 26960 – 14 (2018/01)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose of Conservation (Damba Mountain Ranges) and will
be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Gonawala Watta in total extent of
about 2.0234 Hectares depicted as Lot A in the advanced Tracing
No. Maha/MDD/2010/317 and dated 28.10.2010, prepared by the
Surveyor General and situated in the Village called Galabodawatta in
Palispattuwa East Korale Minor Division, of the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Medadumbara in Kandy District of the Central Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' –  Gonawala Watta –  Extent: 2.0234 Hectares
North and East: Gonawala watta claimed by Lands Reforms
Commission; South: Road; West: Road, Gonawala watta claimed by
Lands Reforms Commission.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: LD/05/2001/FR/139.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
"Mihikatha Medura",
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla,
