Regulation “D”
RESERVE  REQUIREMENTS
Regulation “D” made by the Monetary Board and published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1805/39 of 12th April, 2013  as amended by the notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1816/13 of 26th June 2013, 1947/39 of 31st
December 2015 and No. 2089/ 28 of  19th September 2018  is hereby further amended as follows with effect from 16th
October 2018.
In regulation 2(i)(a), by the deletion of the figure and words “100 per centum of   margins against letters of
credit specified in the Circular No. 35/01/005/0010/ 20 dated 19th September  2018 on ‘ Margin Requirements
against Letters of Credit for importation of  Motor Vehicles’ ” and the substitution thereof the figures and the
words ‘‘100 per centum of  margins against  letters of  credit specified in -
(i) Circular No. 35/01/005/0010/20 dated 19th September 2018 on ‘Margin Requirements against Letter of
Credit for importation of Motor Vehicles’ and/ or
(ii) Circular No. 35/01/005/0010/21 dated 29th September 2018 on ‘Margin Requirements against Letter of
Credit for importation of Motor Vehicles and Non Essential Consumer Goods’,
as the case may be.’’
In regulation 9, by replacing the Schedule A with the following Schedule A
