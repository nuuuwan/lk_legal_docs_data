Two Block of lands are called Udaviharatenna and 
Nindulekumburehena about 2.0679 Hectares in extent

District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Udaviharatenna; Description: Land

Acquisition Act. H. P. Thilakarathna who is a resident of 
D1/6 Dampahena, Ruwandeniya, Dewanagala is a 
preliminary claimant; Extent: 1.2121 Hectares for the 
completion of acquisition activities. 
 
Lot No. 03 
Name of the Land: Udaviharatenna; Description: Land 
has been acquired under Section 38(A) of the Land 
Acquisition Act. H. P. Thilakarathna who is a resident of 
D1/6 Dampahena, Ruwandeniya, Dewanagala is a 
preliminary claimant; Extent: 0.0223 Hectares for the 
completion of acquisition activities. 
 
Lot No. 05 
Name of the Land: Nindulekumburehena; Description: 
Land has been acquired under Section 38(A) of the Land 
Acquisition Act. N. Senarathna who is a resident of 
Ruwandeniya, Dewanagala and others are preliminary 
claimants; Extent: 0.8335 Hectares for the completion of 
acquisition activities.

which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to

on the 19th day of April, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are
