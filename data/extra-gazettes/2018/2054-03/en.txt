I  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land, which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
 
The allotment of land called “ Kongahamula Watta 
and Asliyadda” in total extent of about 0.0316 Hectares, 
depicted as Lot No. 220 in Cadastral Map No. 420214, 
Block No. 01 out of Block No. 02, Sheet No. 5, dated on 
30.12.2017, surveyed and certified by the Surveyor 
General, situated at the Village of Idangapola, in 
Narampola Grama Niladhari Division, No. 725, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mallawapitiya, in 
Kurunegala District, North Western Province and 
bounded as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No.  220 —  Name of Land : Kongahamula 
Watta and Asliyadda —  Extent of the Land : 0.0316 
Hectare. 
 
Boundaries : 
 
 
North by: Land Lot No. 187; East by : Land Lot No. 1; 
South by: Land Lot No. 189; West by: Land Lot No. 219  
 
Names and Address of the claimants :  
 
1. Kristogu Baduge Karunarathne 
    No. 94, 
     Maha Veediya, 
     Kanthale. 
 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at the  Divisional Secretary’s Office  
at Mallawapitiya on 28th February  2018  at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 21st day of  February 2018, the 
nature of their interests in the land, particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of  such amount.
