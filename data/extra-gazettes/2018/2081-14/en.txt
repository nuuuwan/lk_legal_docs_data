THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
W AGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  NURSING HOME TRADE
In accordance with the provisions of Section 28 of thw Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) the Wages Board for the
Nursing Home Trade, hereby notifies that the Board by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid
Ordinance, proposes in vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto. such decisions of the Board as are specified in that
Schedule.
Objections to the proposed decisions will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 15th of August 2018.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is made.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
CHAIRMAN,
Wages Board for the Nursing Home Trade.
