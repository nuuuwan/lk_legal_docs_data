L.D.B 21/78 (xiv)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20 (4) (b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 20 (4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on being satisfied that the administration of  the affairs of
the Postgraduate Institute of Archeology have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have
failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Prishantha Gunawardhana to be the Competent
Authority with effect from January 24, 2018 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of  Director
of Postgraduate Institute of  Archeology, any power, duty or function which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned
to the said Director under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By- Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
D
R. WIJAY ADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
28th June, 2018.
