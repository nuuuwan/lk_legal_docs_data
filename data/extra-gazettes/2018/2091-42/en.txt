THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The Allotments of land ‘A’  in total extent of about 0.0818 Hectare
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GA/HKK/09/527 dated 30th
December, 2009 prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys,
Galle District, situated in the Village called Godagama in the Divi-
sional Secretary’s Division of Hikkaduwa of Galle  District in the
Southern Province and called Pingaha Watta  as  follows.
