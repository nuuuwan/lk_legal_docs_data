SABARAGAMUWA    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
AUTHORIZA TION
I do hereby approve the amendments of the limits of the Advanced Accounts that are stated in the Sixth schedule of the Fourth
Financial Statement for the year 2017 of this Provincial Council, by virtue of powers vested in me under section 27 (a) of the
Provincial Council  Act No. 42 of 1987, as the term of  the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanaka ended on 26th September, 2017.
Accordingly, the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council are hereby granted
permission to bear the necessary express subjected to those limitations.
This permission will be terminated on 31st of  December, 2017.
MARSHAL P ERERA,
Honourable Governor - Sabaragamuwa Province.
08th of December, 2017.

Grant  Permission  by  virtue  of  powers  vested  in  me
under  section  27(A)  of  Provincial  Council  Act  No.  42  of  1987
as  the  term  of  the   sixth  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial  Council  of  the
Democratic  Socialist  Republic  of  Sri  Lanka  ended  on  26th  September,  2017.
1. This Permission may be cited as the "Permission to spend as per Fourth Supplementary Financial Statement of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council" and will be effective from 08.12.2017.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Two Billion Four Hundred
and Twenty Million, Six Hundred and Seventeen Thousand Three Hundred and Seventy one and Cents Ninety Nine
only (Rs. 32,420,617,371.99) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2017 and ending 31st December,
2017 in this statute referred to as the "Financial Year 2017" which is specified in 2nd paragraph of the Appropriation
Statute No. 04 of 2016 as amended by Appropriation statute No. 01 of 2017, Appropriation Statute No. 02 of 2017, and
Appropriation Statute No.  04 of  2017 the first schedule is hereby amended as the expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council which is estimated, Rupees Thirty Two Billion Nine Hundred and Eighty Six Million, One Hundred
and Forty One Thousand and Nineteen and Cents Ninety Nine Only (Rs. 32,986,141,019,99) for the service of the
period beginning on 1st of January , 2017 and ending 31st December, 2017 in this statute referred to as the "Financial
year 2017" as the First Schedule hereof and permission is hereby granted unto the Chief Secretary of the Province and
to the Accounting Officer to spend that amount out of the Provincial Council Fund.
