The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   SEETHAWAKAPURA
URBAN   COUNCIL UNDER    SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Seethawakapura Urban Council   has  fallen vacant by reason of  death;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political  party to  which
Wannakuwaththewaduge Don Ajith Lakshman Perera  the member  belonged, was required by me under
Section 66 A (1) (a)   of the aforesaid ordinance,   to decide  upon the member of that Ward No. 01 Kiriwandala North of
Seethawakapura   Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the local authorities
Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the secretary  of  the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to
nominate Diddeni Gamage Janaka Roshan Pushpakumara  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the  Seethawakapura
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Diddeni Gamage Janaka Roshan Pushpakumara  was elected as the member of  the Ward No. 01
Kiriwandala North of Seethawakapura Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Seethawakapura Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
30th  October  2018.
