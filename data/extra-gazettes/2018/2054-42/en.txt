EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 04/2018
REVISION   OF   THE   CONDITIONS   SET   OUT   IN   THE   EXCISE   NOTIFICATION   NO.  666
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25 of  the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to time, I,
Mangala  Samaraweera, Minister of  Finance and Mass Media do by this Order amend the schedule in the Excise Notification
No. 666 of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 69/5, dated 31 December 1979 as amended by Excise Notification No.
02/2018 of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2053/26, dated 10 January 2018, by -
(1) repealing sub-paragraph (v) of paragraph 5 and substituting thereof with following sub-paragraph,
“(v) male persons under the age of eighteen years and female persons of any age. Provided however, that the
Excise Commissioner may in his discretion permit the employment of female persons above the age of
eighteen years, only as waitresses in licensed premises specifically approved by him for the purpose; ”
(2) by insertion immediately after sub-paragraph (c) of paragraph 11 thereof the following new sub-paragraph
which shall have effect as sub-paragraph (d) thereof,
“(d) to a woman within the premises of a tavern, or”
MANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
18  January 2018.
