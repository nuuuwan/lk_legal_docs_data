EXCISE    (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13 OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended, I,
Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every
article specified in Column I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from November 02, 2018.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
01st November 2018.
