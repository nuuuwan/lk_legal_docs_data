EXCISE   ORDINANCE
EXCISE  NOTIFICATION  No.  12/2018
Excise Duty on Import of Non-Potable Sprits
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 2, 12, 22 and 25, read together with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance
(Chapter 52), as amended from time to time, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do by this Order
amend the Paragraph (b)  of the Excise Notification No. 07/2018 published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2059/32 dated
February 21, 2018, by the substitution of the  following  new Paragraph;
‘‘(b)any export oriented manufacturing company, which has entered into an agreement with the Board of Investment
of Sri Lanka under Section 17 of the Board of Investment  of Sri Lanka Law, No.04  of 1978 (Hereafter called ‘‘BOI
approved manufacturing company’’), and exports 100% of its products, manufactured by using the items in the
Schedule I of Excise Notification No. 07/ 2018 published in the  Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2059/32 dated
February 21, 2018 as its raw materials and such export is certified by the Director General of  the Board of
Investment of Sri Lanka, Excise duty on importation of such raw materials shall not be levied with effect from
October 04, 2018.
Provided that, if any such BOI approved manufacturing company sells a part of its product in the local market as
approved by the BOI, such company shall pay duty as prescribed in Paragraph (a) of aforesaid Excise Notification
No. 07/2018, proportionately on such part of  the raw materials as attributable to the products sold in the local
market.’’
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
