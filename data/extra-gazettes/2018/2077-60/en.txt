THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
B/ACQ/BDL/2017/161 and dated 31.07.2017 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Kendelagama (Division
No. 03) (within Badulla Municipality) in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Badulla East (78B) in the Minor Division of Rilpola in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Badulla in Badulla District,
Uva Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thotupola Arawa Dodangaha Arawa – Extent:
About 0.0126 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Thotupola Arawa Dodangaha Arawa
claimed by Paththini Devalaya; South: Badulu Oya; West: Thotupola
Arawa Dodangaha Arawa claimed by Paththini Devalaya.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/514.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
