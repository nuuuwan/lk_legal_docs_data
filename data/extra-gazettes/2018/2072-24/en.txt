1A
My No.: IR/COM/02/2017/182.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which
accompanies this order exists between,
United Tea Rubber and Local Produce Workers’ Union, (on behalf of 18 Employees), No. 513-2/1,
Elvitigala Mawatha, Colombo 05. of the one part
and
01. Finlays Colombo Limited, No. 186, Vauxhall Street, Colombo 02.
02. Finlay Properties (Pvt.) Limited, No. 309/6, Negombo Road, Welisara. of the other part
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested
in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised
Edition), as amended by Acts Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial disputes-Special Provisions) Act
No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. S. Kariyawasam, No. 75, Melpitiya, Matale to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute
to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
15th May, 2018.
