1A   G   27613 —17  (05/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  ATTANAGALLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resigning ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which   Ranasinghe
Mudiyanselage Seelawathi Menike, the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon returned member if Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to  such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Rajapaksha Welhenage Sudharshani to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, returning officer of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that  Rajapaksha Welhenage Sudharshani was elected as the member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ARUNASINGHA  J AYARATHNAGE S AMPATH M ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
