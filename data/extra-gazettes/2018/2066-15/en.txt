THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the above provisions
of that Act.
1'1'1'1'1' Description of the purpose '– For the construction of Access
Road to the  Rambekealla reservoir of North, integrated Water supply
of Kandy project:
2'2'2'2'2' Description of the Land '– An allotment of, Land depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/AKR/2015/542 and dated
27.01.2015 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village
of Rambukela at Udagampaha Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Akurana in Kandy District in Central Province
and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A'– Ankumbura Estate – Extent: About 0.0114
Hectare.
North: Ankumbura Estate claimed by M.H.M.Nawshard and Road
(Pradesiya Sabawa); East: Road (Pradesiya Sabawa) and Ankumbura
Estate claimed by M.H.M.Nawshard, Ankumbura Estate claimed
by S.L.M.Subair; South Ankumbura Estate claimed by
M.H.M.Nawshard, Ankumbura Estate claimed by S.L.M.Subair &
Lot No. B;  West: Lot No. B and Ankumbura Estate claimed by
M.H.M.Nawshard.
02. Lot 'B'– Ankumbura Estate – Extent: About 0.214
Hectare.
North: Ankumbura Estate claimed by M.H.M.Nawshard and Lot
No. A; East: Lot No. A and Ankumbura Estate claimed by
S.L.M.Subair; South : Ankumbura Estate claimed by M.Y .Naseem;
West: Ankumbura Estate claimed by M.Y .Naseem & Ankumbura
Estate claimed by M.H.M.Nawshard.
