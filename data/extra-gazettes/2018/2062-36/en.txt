THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land in extent of about 1.8525 Hectares, depicted
as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing No. MR/KMB/2017/43 and dated
11.08.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Mapalana in the Minor Division of Gangabada Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kamburupitiya in Matara District
in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Giragahahenawatta – Extent: 1.8525 Hectares
North and East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); South and West: Channel.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land,  described
below in the 1st Paragraph, is needed for a public purpose and will
be acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in the 2nd
Paragraph from which land that the servitude will be acquired.

the rebuild of drain system of Hokandara Water Supply Scheme.

North and East: Canal and name unidentified land Ovita claimed
by W. Albert Perera; South: Road; West: Guruge Watta claimed by
Premarathna and A. K. C. Padmalatha.
