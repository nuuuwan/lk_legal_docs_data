Colombo,

Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Sri Lanka Nurses Council (Post-graduate Qualifications of the Council Members)
Regulations No. 01 of 2018.
2. (a) For the purposes of subsections (ii), (iii), (iv) and (v) of Section 3 of the Act, the term “prescribed post-
graduate qualification” shall mean a post-graduate diploma, master's degree or doctoral degree (Ph.D) granted by any
university in Sri Lanka or outside, recognized by the University Grants Commission of Sri Lanka, in the relevant field of
nursing.
(b) Where no person with such post-graduate qualification is available, the Sri Lanka Nurses Council may
accept a Diploma in teaching and supervision or a Diploma in public health nursing or a Diploma in ward management issued
by the Post Basic School of Nursing or any other equivalent qualification recognized by the Sri Lanka Nurses Council as the
