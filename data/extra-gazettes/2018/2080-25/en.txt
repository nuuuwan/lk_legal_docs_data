Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  PRINTING  TRADE

PART I: S EC. (I) – GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRA TIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 16.07.2018
SCHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Printing Trade and set out in the Schedule to the notification published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 9436/19 of 21st July, 1945 as varied from time to time and last varied by the notification
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/19, of 18th December 2012, shall be further varied by the substitution of the
following new part for Part II of that Schedule:
PART II
Class of  Worker
Class ''A'' Workers
Process Artists, Process Camera Operators, Process Etcher, Process Hands, Litho Artists, Litho and Offset Machine Minders,
Rotary Machine Minders, Photographer and Rotagrano Machine Minders, Tin Plate Printers, Film setters, Lino-type Operators,
Mono-type keyboard operators, Lithow Operators Lino-type Mechanics, Printing Mechanics process Engravers and Mounters,
process Printers Down, Litho Transfers, Litho provers, Proof Readers (other than those employed in the production of
newspapers)
Class ''B'' Workers
Mono-type caster, Operators (Attendants), super Caster Attendants, Elrod Machine Operators, Compositors, Stone Hands
and Imposing Hands, pressmen, Letter press Cylinder Machine Minders and Feeders, Silk Screens Machine Operators,
Rubber Stamp Makers, Relief Stamp and Dye Stamping Machine Operators, Binders, Binding Machine Operators, Sewing
Machine Operators, Folding Machine Operators, Carton cutting and pasting Machine Operators, cutters, (Hand and machine),
Guillotine Machine Operators, Guilding Machine Operators, and Guilding Rulers (Hand and machine) stereo typers mangle
man , Foundry Plate Casters, Type Casters, Planten Machine Minders (Operating fully automatic Machine), Roller casting
(using the Roller casting gun) platen machine Minders (Hand feed), Roller caster (Hand).
Class ''C '' Workers
Foundry plate chippers, Foundry laborers, Rotary laborers, packers, counters and checkers.
Class ''D'' Workers
Unskilled worker not  under 18 years of age.
Class ''E'' Workers
Unskilled worker under 18 year of age.
Class ''F'' Workers
Watchers.
