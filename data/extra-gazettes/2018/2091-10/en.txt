THE LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
  My Ref. No.:DS/GFG/L&L/5/89.
 Ministry of Land and Parliamentary reforms
Ref. No.: 4-3/11/2017/UD/72.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment)
that the Government intends to acquire the following land
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Four (04) allotments of land in total extent 0.0847 Hectare
depicted as lot no. 232, 233, 236 & 237 in the Sheet No. 09
of Block No. 01 in Cadastral Map No. 810019 prepared by
the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Maligaspe
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 101E of Maligaspe,
within Municipal Council limit of Galle Divisional
Secretary's Division of Four Gravets, Galle District of Galle,
Southern Province. The names, extent and boundaries of
the said allotments of land are as follows:
01. Lot No. 232
Name of the Land: Not Stated; Description: Scrub jungle;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: National Housing
Development  Authority; Extent: about 0.0269 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 233
Name of the Land: Not Stated; Description: Scrub jungle;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: National Housing
Development  Authority; Extent: about 0.0258 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 236
Name of the Land: Not Stated; Description: Road; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant:Could not be traced; Extent: about
0.0008 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 237
Name of the Land: Not Stated; Description: Scrub jungle;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: National Housing
Development  Authority; Extent: about 0.0312 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Four Gravets Galle, Divisional
Secretariat on 08.10.2018 at 09.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 03.10.2018, the nature of their interests in the lands, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said  Lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
    All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
I. HIMALI RATHNAVEERA,
Divisional Secretary (Acting),
Divisional Secretariat - Four Gravets Galle.
