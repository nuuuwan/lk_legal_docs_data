THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460) AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND   ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT, No. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended  by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land /servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land depicted as Lot Nos. 01 and 02 in Surveyor
General's preliminary Plan No. G 3734 and situated in the Village of
Nugethota in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Welivitiya-Divithura in Galle District, Southern Province.
01.  Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Polgahavila Group Thuththudoova ; Description:
There are 42 buildings with gardens; Extent: 1.9609 Hectare.
02.  Lot No. 02
Name of Land: Kohiladeniya Kumbura; Description:
Open bare land; Extent: 0.0912 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land /lands on
which servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorised in
writing, before me at The Divisional Secretariat
Welivitiya - Divithura on the 08th day of February 2018
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day of
February 2018, the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the Computation of such
amount.
A. M. A. U. G . KARIYAVASAM,
Divisional Secretary,
Welivitiya-Divithura.
Divisional Secretariat,
Welivitiya - Divithura,
12th January, 2018.
