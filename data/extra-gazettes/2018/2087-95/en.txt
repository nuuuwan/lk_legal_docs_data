1A  G 28481 — 22  (09/2018)
Amending clauses of  the Central Province Road Development Authority Statute No. 02 of year
2003 published in Gazette No. 1 2007, 510/ 43 of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
by the Provincial Council Central Province
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of  Road Development, Tranport, Power and Energy, Housing and  Construction of  the
Central Provincial Council hereby announce under the powers vested in me by the clause No. 22 (1) of  the Section V of the
Central Provincial  Road Development Authority Statute No. 02 of  year  2003 of  the  Central Provincial Council, clause 10 (4)
and clause 10 (1) of part III of  the Central Province Road Development  Authority Statute No. 02, 2003 published on the
Gazette published on 17 th August 2007 by Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka will come into force with effect
from 01.09.2018 as mentioned below :-
Clause Existing clause A General Manager for the Authority should be appointed for the Authority
10(1) with the approval of  the Minister by the Authority
To be amended as A person fulfill required qualifications should be appointed as  the General
Manager according to the approved  Recruiting Procedure and an approval
of   the Director Board
Clause Existing clause  The General Manager should not be removed from the post   without a   prior
10(4) written approval from the Minister
To be amended as Clause  4 will be removed
E
DIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of  Road Development, Tranport, Power and
Energy, Housing and  Construction
Central Province.
