CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No. 17 OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
11th April 2018.
Schedule
The land called Muththettulanda situated in the village of Bangalawatta in Grama Niladari's Division of 75A, Walagama in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Galigamuwa, in the Gamdolaha Pattu of Beligal Korale, in the Administrative District
of Kegalle, in the Province of Sabaragamuwa of Sri Lanka containing in extent of approximately 83.68 Perches shown in
sketch drawing No. CENEIPP2/LotC/TKL/LEA02(Rev 2) dated 20.03.2018 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board and
depicted as Lot No. 01 and bounded as follows :
North : Gravel Road and land claimed by L. K. Kalupahana ;
East : Kegalle-Polgahawela Main Road ;
South : Balance portion of Muththettulanda claimed by W. A. R. B. Wickramasinghe and land claimed
by W. A. D. P. Weerasuriya
West : Land claimed by R. M.  Premakanthi and land claimed by J. M. Dasanayaka Menike ;
Claimed by : W. A. R. B. Wickramaisnghe.

Order under Section 57

Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
11th April 2018.
Schedule
The land called Muththettulandewaththa situated in the village of Bangalawatta in Grama Niladari's Division of 75A,
Walagama in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Galigamuwa, in the Gamdolaha Pattu of Beligal Korale, in the
Administrative District of Kegalle, in the Province of Sabaragamuwa of Sri Lanka containing in extent of approximately
58.16 Perches shown in sketch drawing No. CENEIPP2/LotC/TKL/LEA01(Rev 2) dated 20.03.2018 prepared by Ceylon

West : Gravel Road ;
Claimed by : W. A. S. Wickramaisnghe.
