FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   TOWN & GRA VETS
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Town & Gravets  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  Termination of
Party membership;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Eelam People’s Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which
Kirubairajah Naguleshwaran   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member if   Eelam People’s Democratic Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared returned  as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Shanmugam Balaganeshan  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the  Town & Gravets  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Shanmugam
Balaganeshan was selected as the member of the Town & Gravets  Pradeshiya Sabha.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee  Town & Gravets  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
07th  November  2018.
