THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: CO/HMG/2017/536.
I hereby give notice, under sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of 0.0443
Hectares, as Lots depicted in Sheet No. 05, Block No. 01 in
Cadastral Plan No. 520030 and dated 08.05.2018 prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated in Grama Niladhari Division
593, Wethara in the Village of Wethara in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Homagama in the District of Colombo
in Western Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 190
Name of land: Kahatagahawatte Kanaththa alias
Kahatagahawatte Kattiya; Description: Garden, Contain a
permanent building & Part of a permanent building; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: Dona Deepani Abeysinghe, No. 183,
Wethara, Polgasowita; Extent: 0.0307 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 193
Name of land: Kahatagahawatte Kanaththa alias
Kahatagahawatte Kattiya; Description: Garden; Claimant:
1A - G 27806 - 17 (2018/06)
State; Original Claimant: Dona Geethani Abeysinghe &
Kumbukage Lalith Jayasiri, No. 183, Wethara, Polgasowita;
Extent: 0.0106 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 194
Name of land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.0030 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Homagama Divisional Secretariat on the
17th June, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th
June, 2018 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
or their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
            BUDDHI THARANGA KARUNASENA,
                Divisional Secretary,
            Homagama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Homagama,
30th May 2018.
