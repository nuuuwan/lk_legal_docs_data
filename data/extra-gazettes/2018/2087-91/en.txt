No. 2087/91 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 07, 2018
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha
ENTERTAINMENT  TAXATION
ACCORDING  to the powers conferred in (Chapter 267) second part (I) Sub - section of Entertainment Tax Ordinance, under-
mentioned resolution determined by the Secretary of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha which was approved by the Minister in
charge for Local Governments, Central Provincial Council as per the powers specified in Section II of the Ordinance on
Entertainment Taxation is here by published based on its second Sub section.
R. M. P. S. RATHNAY AKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Local Government Ministry,
Central Province.
At Pallekele.
14th February, 2018.
RESOLUTION
On the powers conferred to me in section 9(3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, when it is held to conduct the entertainment
programs in the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha Authority, it has been determined to impose as entertainment tax of 10% from the
ticket fee as per (2) 1 of No. 12 of 1946 Tax Ordinance and Section 37 of the 1984 Tax Amendment Act.

PART IV (A) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 07.09.2018
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha
ENTERTAINMENT  TAXATION
ACCORDING to the powers conferred in (Chapter 267) second part (I) Sub - section of Entertainment Tax Ordinance, under-
mentioned resolution determined by the Secretary of Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha which was approved by the Minister in
charge for Local Governments, Central Provincial Council as per the powers specified in Section II of the Ordinance on
Entertainment Taxation is here by published based on its second Sub section.
R. M. P. S. RA THNAY AKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Local Government Ministry,
Central Province.
At Pallekele.
14th February, 2018.
RESOLUTION
On the powers conferred to me in section 9(3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, when it is held to conduct the entertainment
programs in the Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha Authority, it has been determined to impose an  entertainment tax of 7.5% of the
ticket fee for cinematic view and 20% of the entrance fee for all other entertainment programs as per the Section (2) 1 of No. 12
of 1946 Tax Ordinance and Section 37 of the 1984 Tax Amendment Act.
