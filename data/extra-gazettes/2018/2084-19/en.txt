FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF  MEMBER OF MAHARAGAMA URBAN COUNCIL
 UNDER SECTION 66 A (1) (1a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Maharagama Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation:
And Whereas the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Nuwan Madhushanka Fernando the member appointed
to Ward No. 18 Maharagama South of Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under Section  66 A (1) (a)
of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 18 Maharagama South of Maharagama Urban Council
who belongs to such  independent group and is not  disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a period of thirty (30)
days.
And Where as the Leader of aforesaid independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nomin ate
Angoda Liyanage Pradeep Chaminda  to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore  I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of  Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66 A (1)  (1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Angoda Liyanage Pradeep Chaminda  was elected as the member of the Ward No. 18 Maharagama South of Maharagama
Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. RUPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo.
