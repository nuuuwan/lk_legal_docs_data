THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice  under Section 7
      My No .: 3D/HD/LND/3/1/NWS &D3/2.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose:-
    Description of the land  to be acquired :
The allotments of land fin Total Extent about 0.2605 Hectare,
under mentioned extent and named lots of land in
Walhaputhenna Grama Niladari Division Walhaputhenna
Village shown in the tracing prepared by the Surveyor General
No. F. V . P . 295 B/ HLD/ 2018/ 198 dated 10th August 2018
situated in Kandapalla Korale in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of  Haldummulla in Badulla District in Uva Provinces.
The public purpose for which land acquired to : Construction
Walhaputhenna Water Pump House and the office under the
Haldummulla Beragala Water Supply Scheme.
Lot No. 280 - Name of the Land: Walhaputhenne waththa;
Claimant: W. Rasika Damayanthi, Digaredda Kubura,
Kahagolla, Diyathalawa.
Boundaries:
North: Lot Nos. 38 and 281; East: Lot No. 281; South: Lot
No. 38;West: Lot Nos. 38 and 42.
Lot No. 281 - Name of  Land: Walhaputhenne Pathana;
Claimant: State.
Boundaries:
North: Lot Nos. 280, 38, 215, 218, 220, 221, 222 and 223; East:
Lot Nos. 218, 220, 221, 222, and 223; South: Lot Nos. 282, 38
and 280;West: Lot Nos. 282, 38, 42 and 280.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Haldummulla Divisional Secretariat, on
the 31st of October 2018 at 10 .00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 17th of
October 2018, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the   computation of such amount.
J. M. C HAMILA INDIKA JAYASINGHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Haldummulla.
Divisional Secretary's Office, Haldummulla,
On 19th September, 2018.
