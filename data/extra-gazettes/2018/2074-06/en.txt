THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 7
Land Ministry No.: 13/99/VE/115.
My No.: DS/3/LAQ/139/(old No.).
KDS/DOL/4/4/5/1/10 (new).
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intended to acquire the following land for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of land in total extent about 5.0824 Hectares
described as Lots 01, 02, 03, 04 of in Preliminery plan
No. Maha 5108, prepared by the Surveyor General situated in
the Pitawalagama Village in the Pitawala Grama Niladhari
Division in Kandukara Pahala Korale Minor Division in
Divisional Secretariat Division of Doluwa in the Kandy District,
Central  Province.

0.7223 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02 of P .P. No. 5108
Name of the Land: Levelan watta; Original Claimant: Janatha

All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional
Secretariat on the 11th July 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 04th day of July 2018.
The nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
