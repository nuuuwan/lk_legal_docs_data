No. 2061/41 – THURSDAY, MARCH 08, 2018
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Thattakaikadu in extent of 4.9455
Hectares depicted as Lot A in  Advance Tracing No.
TRI/TWG/2014/198 and dated 24.02.2016, prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys Trincomalee, situated in Varothayanagar
Village in Kattukulam Pattu minor Division, Pradeshiya Sabha Limits
of  Trincomalee  in the Varothayanagar Grama Niladhari Division
No. 243N, in  Divisional Secretary's Division of  Trincomale Town
and Gravets, Trincomalee District in Eastern Province and is bounded
as follows:
01. Lot A – Thattakaikadu – Extent: 4.9455 Hectares
North: Thattakaikadu and Road (P.S); East: Road (P. S) and
Thattakaikadu; South and West:  Thattakaikadu.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
