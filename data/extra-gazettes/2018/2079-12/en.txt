THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the said Act,  published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1395/15  of June 01, 2005  (as last amended by Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary ,
No. 2015/ 44 of  April  19, 2017),  establishing of the Uva Wellassa University of  Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately
after the entry ‘‘2. Department of  Public Administration’’ in Column II of  the Schedule which corresponds to the item
‘‘ Faculty of Management’’ in  Column I of that Schedule, of  the following entry :-
‘‘3. Department of  English Language Teaching’’.
Dr. W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
06th  July  2018.
