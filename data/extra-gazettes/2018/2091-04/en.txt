UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa Rajapakshe,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in
respect of the KAATSU-Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU), a company incorporated
in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 66705, do by this Order, amend
the Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1961/34 of April 08,
2016 by addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Management Honours in Accounting” appearing in Column I
and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II  respectively of the
Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule  I hereto.
SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS
 KAATSU-Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) shall, when admitting
students for the degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours in
Physical Science, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of three (03) ‘S’ passes among the subjects Combined
Mathematics or Higher Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Information & Communication Technology and Mathematics
in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advance Level) Examination or equivalent.
