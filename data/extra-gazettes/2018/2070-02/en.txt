SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Eran
Wickramaratne, Acting Minister of Finance and Mass Media,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this
order impose in respect of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from May 08, 2018 subject to provisions of Sub
section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2044/26 of 08.11.2017 in respect of Sprats and Dried Fish is rescinded with
effect from May 08, 2018.
E
RAN W ICKRAMARA TNE,
Acting Minister of Finance amd Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
04th May, 2018.
