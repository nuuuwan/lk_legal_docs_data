PENSION MINUTE
Notification under the Section 51 of the Pension Minute dated 05th February 1934, amended by the
Pension Minute (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICATION
The interim section 2-17 of the Pension Minute as amended from time to time is hereby further amended by the insertion of
the following sub section immediately after the aforesaid section.
“Compulsory age limit of retirement  of the Specialist Doctors shall be extended up to 63 years”
The amendment made to the Pension Minute by this Notification deemed as coming into force  with effect from
06.06.2017.
R. M. R
ANJITH MADDUMABANDARA,
