ELECTIONS   COMMISSION
Notice of  the Election Commission
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS - 10TH   FEBRUARY   2018
IT is hereby informed that the results of the counting in each wards related to each of the 340 local authorities, of which
the election was held on the 10th February, 2018, are being exhibited at relevant District Election Offices, Local Authorities,
Grama Niladhari offices relevant to each ward, and in the website of the Elections Commission.
Further, it is informed that these results sheets have also been issued to the political parties contested.
