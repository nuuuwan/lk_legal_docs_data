Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964
(Chapter 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                             My Ref. No.: DS/GFG/L&L/5/90.
       Ministry of Land and Parliamentary
  Reforms Ref. No.: 4 - 3/11/2017/UD/77.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act No.28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
One (01) allotment of land in total extent 0.0066 Hectare
depicted as lot No. 176 in the Sheet No.05 of Block No. 01 in
Cadastral Map No. 810022 prepared by the Surveyor General
situated in the village of Deddugoda North in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 101C of Deddugoda North in the
Municipal Council limit of Galle in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Four Gravets Galle in Galle District of the Southern
Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot No. 176
Name of the land : Pansalaaddara ovita, Addara ovita,
Karmantha duwa, Arachchige Kumbura, Karakumbura and
Dostaragewatta; Description : Open barren land; Claimant :
State; Original Claimant: Urban Development Authority Galle;
Extent :  About 0.0066 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Four Gravets Galle, Divisional
Secretariat on 20.08.2018 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 15.08.2018
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
produced before me at the inquiry.
                                            I.HIMALI RATHNAVEERA,
                                            Divisional Secretary,
                                                     Divisional Secretariat - Four
                                        Gravets Galle.
