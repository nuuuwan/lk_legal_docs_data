THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in extent and named as follows,  depicted  in
the Advance Tracing No. Maha/TMP/2017/121 dated 13.06.2017
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Ban Anga
in the Minor Division of Udapalatha Korale East in the  Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Thumpane in Kandy District in Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Gallenawattha – Extent: 0.0202 Hectare
North and East: Gallenawaththa claimed by W. G . Ramyalatha;
South: Gallenawaththa claimed by W. G . Ramyalatha and
Gallenawaththa claimed by the Government; West: Gallenawaththa
claimed by the Government and Gallenawaththa claimed by W. G .
Ramyalatha.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
