THE   LAND   ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)   ACT
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER   460)
Notice  under Section 7
My  Ref.: KDS/MDB/4/2/1/79/12.
Land Ministry's  Ref.  No.: 13/97/FR/327.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that
the Government intends to acquire the following land which
is required for a public purpose.
     Purpose of Acquisition: Knuckles Range Reservation:
An allotment of land called Kandhewaththa in total extent
of about 2.8448 hectares depicted as lot 01 in the Parliminary
Plan No. Maha 6734 dated 08.09.2017 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys - Kandy District, situated in the
village called Mangoda in the Grama Niladhari Division No.
806 of  Mangoda in the Udasiyapattuwa Korale Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Medadumbara in
Kandy District of the Central Province and more fully described
as follows.
 01. Lot No.  01
Name of the Land: Kandhewaththa; Description: Scrub;
Claimant:Y . M. Muthumenika, Y . M. Heen Banda, Y . M.
Abeyrathna, Mangoda, Medhamahanuwara; Extent: 2.8448
Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at the Medadumbara Divisional
Secretariat on the 28 .02. 2018 at 02 .00 p.m. and are hereby
requested  notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
21.02.2018, the nature of their interests in the aforesaid land/
servitude, the particulars of their claims for compensation
and the details of  computation of such amount.
A. M. WICKRAMARACHCHI,
Divisional Secretary,
Medadumbara.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Medadumbara,
On 28th December, 2017.
