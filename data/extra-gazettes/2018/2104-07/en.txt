THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: NAG/LAND/5/1/73.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2008/FR/351.
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of  the land/servitude to be acquired :
Four allotments of land described below and depicted in
the Plan No. FVP . 528 Prepared by the Surveyor General
Situated in 219A Homadola in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Nagoda, Gangabada Pattuwa North, Galle District.
Lot No. 1743
Name of land: Homadola Mukalana; Description: Jungle;
Claimants's Name and Address: Land Reform Commission;
Extent: 264.3042 Hectares.
Lot No. 1744
Name of land: Kondagaldolahena Bataketiya Mukalana;
Description: Jungle; Claimants's Name and Address: Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 2.7019 Hectares.
Lot No. 1745
Name of land: Kondagaldolahena Bataketiya Mukalana;
Description: Jungle; Claimants's Name and Address: Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 68.3888 Hectares.
Lot No. 1747
Name of land: Homadola Mukalana; Description: Jungle;
Claimants's Name and Address: Not known; Extent: 3.9815
Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly
authorized in writing before me at Nagoda Divisional
Secretariat on the 30th January, 2019 and are requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 23rd
January, 2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of  the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
C. P. RAJAKARUNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Nagoda.
Divisional Secretariat,
Nagoda,
20th December, 2018.
