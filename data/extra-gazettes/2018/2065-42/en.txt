PORTS  AND  AIRPORTS  DEVELOPMENT  LEVY  ACT,  No. 18  OF  2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Sub-section 3 of Section (3) of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do hereby this Order, amend the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2049/16 of 13.12.2017 by,
1. The addition of the following Sub Paragraph immediately after Sub Paragraph (b) of Paragraph 1 :-
(c) “Ports and Airports Development Levy, shall be charged and levied on importation of each article specified
in Column III corresponding to the H. S. Headings in Column I and H. S. Code in Column II of the Schedule
III hereto, a concessionary rate of 2.5%  in its corresponding entry in Column IV , on the recommendation
of the Secretary, Ministry of Industry and Commerce based on a set of predetermined criteria.”
2. The addition of Schedule hereto as Schedule III, immediately after the Schedule II of the said  Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2049/16.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
April 5th 2018.
