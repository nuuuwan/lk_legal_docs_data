SPORTS  ACT,  No.   25  OF  1973
Regulations under Section 32 and 33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera the Minister of Sports, in pursuance of powers vested in me under Sections 32 and 33 of the Sports
Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby inform that the interim committee to be appointed with effect from 04.01.2018 is comprised
of the following officers to proceed with the affairs of the Sri Lanka Badminton Association which is the National
Association for Badminton of which registration has been suspended at present until such time a lawful election is held,
as an interim arrangement.
1. N. D. De S. Jayasinghe - President
2. Clarence Homer - Secretary
3. G . G.  Mangala - Treasurer
4. Rohan De Silva - Vice President
5. Subanie Udawatta - Member
D
AYASIRI JAYASEKERA (Attorny-at-Law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
04th January, 2018.
