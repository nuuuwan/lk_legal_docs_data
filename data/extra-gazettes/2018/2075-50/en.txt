Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  BALANGODA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of Member of Balangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Sugath Indrajith Wadudurage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that 10 Ellepola if Balangoda Urban Council  who belongs to  such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of  aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ekanayaka Rajapaksha Basnayaka Mudiyanselage Akila Saliya Ellawala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Raveendra Subhashana Wedage, returning officer of the Balangoda Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ekanayaka
Rajapaksha Basnayaka Mudiyanselage Akila Saliya Ellawala was elected as the Member of the 10 Ellepola of Balangoda
Urban Council.
R
A VINDRA S UBASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Balangoda Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
13th June, 2018.
