PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2 OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Western Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under Section
65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Death ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which the member belonged
was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Janaka Kuruppu to fill
such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988,
read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby declare
Mr. Janaka Kuruppu, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Gampaha in
respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA, N. J. A BEYSEKERE, S. R A TNAJEEV AN H. HOOLE,
