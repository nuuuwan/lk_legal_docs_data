1A  - G   27471—12  (04/2018)
SPORTS   ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sec. 32 and 33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera, Minister of Sports in terms of power vested in me, as an interim arrangement until the election of
the Sri Lanka Badminton is Lawfully held, do the hereby appoint the undermentioned officers as two positions in the
Interim Committee of the Sri Lanka Badminton have fallen vacant due to resignation of
1. Clarence Homer - Secretary
2. Rohan de Silva - Vice President
who were appointed by Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 2053/6 dated 08.01.2018.
Following new officers to be appointed with effect from 07.04.2018 to the Committee.
1. Kariyawasam Gonapeenuwala Gamage Ranjan Mohan Wijesinghe - Secretary
2. Wasantha Kumara - Vice President
D
AYASIRI J AY ASEKERA,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
07th April 2018.
