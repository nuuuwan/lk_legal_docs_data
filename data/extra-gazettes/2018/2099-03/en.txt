I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of  Section (7) of
the  Land Acquisition  Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
     Description of the land to be  Acquired:
An alloment of land is called Kobekatuwehena about
0.3284 Hectare in extent depicted as Lot 01, in P .P.K. Plan
Number 3615 and dated 08.10.2018 of  preliminary plan made
by the Senior Superintendent of Survey and situated in the
village of Thambarawa, in the Grama Niladhari Division of
No. 44A, Habalakkawa, in the Minor Division of Galbata
Korale (West), in the Divisional Secretariat Division of
Aranayaka, in the Distric of Kagalle, in Sabaragamuwa
Province. Boundaries are as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land:Kobekatuwehena;  Extent: 0.3284 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North: Kobekatuwehena and boundary of Bakulwala; East:
Lot No.4 in preliminary plan K 3573; South :
Aluthdurayalagehena; West: Aluthdurayalagehena and
Kobekatuwahena.
Other particulars: Land has been acquired under Section
38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act. M.M.K. Dayarathna who
is preliminary claimant and resident of No. F 07 Demalahiriya,
Aranayaka for the completion of acquistion activities.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Aranayaka Divisional Secretariat on 20th December, 2018
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writting (In duplicate)on or before 13th December, 2018  the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and details of computation of
such amount.
Z.A.M.FAISAL,
Divisional Secretary,
Aranayaka.
At Divisional Secretariat, Aranayaka,
23rd  November, 2018.
