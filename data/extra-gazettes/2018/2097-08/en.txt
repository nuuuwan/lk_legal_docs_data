The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
 NOTICE   UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe, Returning Officer of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha do
hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Punyarathna
Wickramasinghe of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has
ceased to be a member of the United National Freedom Front.
J. A. S. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
09th November, 2018.
