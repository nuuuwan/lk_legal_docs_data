CARRIAGE  BY  AIR  ACT,  No.  29  OF  2018
Order under Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Carriage by Air Act, No. 29 of 2018, I, Nimal Siripala De Silva,
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation, do by this Order, appoint 02.11.2018, as the date on which the aforesaid Act shall
come into operation.
