PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the North Western Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Western Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission
under Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership
of the North Western Province Provincial Council by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
former member Ananda Chandralal belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid
Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Dingiri Banda Herath
Herath Mudiyanselage to fill such vacancy;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Dingiri Banda Herath Herath Mudiyanselage, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council
from the Administrative District of Kurunegala in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
