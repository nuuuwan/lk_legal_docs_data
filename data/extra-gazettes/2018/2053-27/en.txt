THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation  of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS  by order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts, No. 14 and 62  of 1957, 14 of  1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes(Special Provisions)
Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 05.09.2016 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extra-Ordinary No.
1983/39 dated 09.09.2016 the Industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement  of the Commissioner of Labour dated
11.08.2016.
Mr. R.U.M.S.Rajakaruna, No. 100/08, Dharmaraja Mawatha, Kandy  of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05. of the other part
was referred to Mr. P. Danansooriya for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, W.D.J. Senevirathna, Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order
dated 05.09.2016.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and
Sabaragamuwa Development.
Colombo,
29th  December, 2017.
