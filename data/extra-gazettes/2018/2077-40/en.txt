STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether  Mr.  K.W.N. Perera, working at the Ceylon Fishery Harbours Corporation  had been caused injustice by not been given th e
promotion entitled as at 01.01`.2014,  non - approval of Accident leave for the accident which had occured on 25.06.2015 while coming to
work, cutting off 22 leaves from his leave entitlement, considering the 57 balance leave as no - pay leave and recovering it from his salary, as
well as Suspending his Salary increments and if such injustice  had been caused, to what reliefs he is entitled.
