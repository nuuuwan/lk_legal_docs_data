FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF   THE  ADDALACHCHENEI
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of a member of  Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which
Muhammathu Ameer Ansil the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon returned member of that No. 05 - Minhaj, of Addalachchenei Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and  who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Hasanbay Mohmethu Siraj to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of the Addalachchenei Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that  Hasanbay Mohmethu Siraj was elected as the member of the No. 05 - Minhaj, Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILANA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
03rd September 2018.
