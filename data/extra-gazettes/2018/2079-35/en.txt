THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25 A
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa Rajapakshe,
the Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in
respect of the Saegis Campus (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing registration number PV 72548, do by this Order and subject to the conditions specified in the Schedule
I hereto, recognize the Saegis Campus (Private) Limited as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing
Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the
abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in  Column II of the Schedule II hereto :-
SCHEDULE   I
Conditions
Saegis Campus (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the ‘‘Institute”) shall when admitting students for the
following Degree Programme specified in the Schedule II hereto, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (3)
‘S’ passes in any stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) Examination or equivalent, in complying
with University Grants Commission Circular No. 995 dated 28
th  September 2012.
