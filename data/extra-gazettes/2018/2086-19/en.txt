RULES
The Specified Authority (Powers relating to Recognition of Institutes as Degree Awarding Institutes) Rules No. 1 of 2013
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1824/21 dated August 22, 2013 as last amended by Gazette Extraordinary No.
1891/9 dated December 02, 2014 are hereby further amended as follows :-
1. by the repeal of rule 2 and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“2. The guidelines set-out in the Schedule I and Schedule II to these rules shall be applicable for
the recognition of any State or Non-State Institute as a Degree Awarding Institute and for the obtaining
of power to grant Degree or Degrees by such institute, on the payment of a processing fee as provided
under Schedule III to these rules.”.
2. by the repeal of rule 7 and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“7. Any Institute which intends to obtain recognition as a Degree Awarding Institute and obtains the
power to confer a degree or degrees (hereinafter referred to as “the applicant”) shall submit to the
Specified Authority, an application consisting of Part I and Part II, containing particulars given in the
guidelines as set out in the Schedule I and Schedule II and on the payment of the processing fee as set out
in the Schedule III, for the Institutional Review and the Subject Review.”.

3. by the repeal of rule 14 and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“14. The applicant may re-submit the application returned at the first review, together with the
required information, to the Specified Authority within a period of three months from the date of such
return. No processing free shall be charged for the first re-submission of the application. The guidelines
set out in  the Schedule I shall be complied with. The Specified Authority shall forward the re-submitted
application to the SCAQA for reconsideration.”.
4. by the repeal of rule 20 and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“20. The applicant may re-submit the application for review with the required information, to the
Specified Authority within a period of three months from the date of such return, and in accordance with
the requirements specified in the Schedule II to these rules. No processing fee shall be charged for the
first re-submission of the application.”.
5. by the insertion immediately after the rule 34 of the following new part as Part IV :-
“P
ART IV
Processing Charge
35. Institutional Review and Subject Review shall be subject to a processing fee as set out in the
Schedule III to these rules.
36. Any Degree Awarding Institute which does not comply with the requirements at the second or
subsequent reviews shall be subject to a payment of fifty percent (50%) of the processing charges
specified in item (b) of the Schedule III.
37. Where the study programme is conducted at a different location, a payment of twenty percent
(20%) of the processing charges for the Institutional Review shall be additionally charged for evaluating
such different location.”.
6. by the renumbering of rule 35 as rule 38.
7. in the renumbered rule 38 the definition of the expression “Specified Authority” shall be repealed and the
following substituted therefor :-
“ “Specified Authority” means the person appointed as a Specified Authority under Section 70B of
the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 ;”.
