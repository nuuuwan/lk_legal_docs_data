LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Notice under Section 66 (2)
ONLY the Particulars relating to Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha in the Gazette Notice No. 2062/15 of Friday 13 March
2018, which published the names of members elected and members returned for the Poojapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha in the
Administrative District of Kandy, are hereby cancelled in order to change the date of the Gazette Notice.
02. Accordingly, it is hereby declared that the names of the members elected and members Returned for the Poojapitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha in the Kandy Administrative District are specified in a Column II of the Schedule.
03. It is also declared that no any change has been effected to the names of the members indicated in the Gazette
Notice.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA. N. J. A BEYSEKERE. S. R A TNAJEEV AN H. HOOLE.
Chairman, Member, Member,
Elections Commission. Elections Commission. Elections Commission.
29th March, 2018.

Rajagiriya.
