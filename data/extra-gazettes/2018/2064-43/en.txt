LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
Notice under Section 66(2)
ONLY the Particulars relating to Kekirawa Pradeshiya Sabha in the Gazette Notice No. 2061/42-20 of Friday 09 March
2018, which published the names of members elected and members returned for the Kekirawa Pradeshiya Sabha in the
Administrative District of Anuradhapura, are hereby cancelled in order to change the date of the Gazette Notice.
02. Accordingly, it is hereby declared that the names of the members elected and members returned for the Kekirawa
Pradeshiya Sabha in the Anuradhapura Administrative District are specified in a Column II of the Schedule.
03. It is also declared that no any change has been effected to the names of the members indicated in the Gazette notice.
Mahinda Deshapriya N. J. Abeysekere S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole
Chairman Member Member
Elections Commission Elections Commission Elections Commission
