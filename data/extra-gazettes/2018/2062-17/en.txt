THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT CHAPTER 131 OF
THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
(1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by order made under Section 4 (1) of the
Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative
Enactments of Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956) as amended
by Acts. No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968
read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act
No. 37 of 1968 dated 15.03.2016 and published in the
Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Extraordinary No. 1958/20 dated 17.03.2016 the Industrial
Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement
of the Commissioner of Labour dated 29.02.2016.
13 employees including Mrs. W. L. P. Imali
Maheshani,
No. 130/13/1, Elizabeth Perera Mawatha,
Mahara-Kadawatha
and
                                                        of the one part
Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority,
No. 80, Galle Road,
Colombo 03.
                                                     of the other part
was referred to Mr. T. E. Santharajan for settlement by
arbitration.
And whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said
Order be revoked, I, W. D. J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour,
Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamuwa Development do
hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no
proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 15.03.2016.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development.
Colombo,
08th March, 2018.

Compensation Scheme being used in the calculation of
compensation and if such injustice had been caused, to what
reliefs they are entitled, and

on their service periods since their Employees’ Provident
Funds had already been released on the basis of surplus of
employees."
