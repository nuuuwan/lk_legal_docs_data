1A   G   27802 —17  (06/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   NORWOOD  PRADESHIYA
SABAH   UNDER   SECTION  66B(2) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No. 16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of Chairman in Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ceylon Workers Congress (Political Wings), the recognized political party
to which  Thangaraj Kisokumar, the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be declared
elected as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kanapathy Kulandevel Ravi to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pulasthi Pathirana, returning officer of the Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendement)
Act, No. 16 of 2017  do hereby declare Kanapathy Kulandevel Ravi  elected as  Chairman of the Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P ATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
