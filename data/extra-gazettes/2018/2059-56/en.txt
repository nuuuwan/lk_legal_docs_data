FOREST   CONSERV ATION   ORDINANCE
THE order under Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as amended by Acts, No. 13 of 1966,
No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by
Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009,  I, Maithripala Sirisena,
Minister of Mahaweli Development and Environment do by this order declare the Himbiliyakada Forest (2nd Step) of
532.8034 hectares bounded by the limit set forth in the Schedules 01 and 02 hereto as Himbiliyakada Reserved Forest (2nd
Step) from 15 day of February 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
Minister of Mahaweli Development and Environment.
Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment,
Battaramulla,
15th February 2018.
