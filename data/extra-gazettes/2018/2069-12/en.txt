EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Ministry Reference No. : 4-3/2/2012/HA/243. 
Divisional Secretary Ref. No.: KMB/LAN/ACQUI/02/65. 
 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  as Amended by the land 
acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land or servitude  to be acquired : 
 
The allotment of the land mentioned below depicted in 
PP MR 2719, situated in the No. 330, Kamburupitiya 
Grama Niladhari Division, in the Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Kamburupitiya in the Matara District in the 
Southern Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Ratalankawattha, Kiragewila; 
Description: Home Garden; Name and Address of the 
land claimed by: Not found ; Extent: 0.0037 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Ratalankawattha; Description: 
Home Garden; Name and Address of the land claimed by: 
Not found ; Extent: 0.0029 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
Name of the Land: Ratalankawattha; Description: 
Home Garden; Name and Address of the land claimed by: 
Land Reform Commission ; Extent: 0.0321 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear,  personally,  or  by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary’s Office 
Kamburupitiya on the 6th day of June, 2018 at 9.30 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 30th day of May, 2018 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
             N. H. M. W. W. N. HERATH, 
                                 Divisional Secretary, 
                                                     Kamburupitiya.
