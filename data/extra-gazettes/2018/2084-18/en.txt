STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,   No.  14  OF  2008
Order under  Sub-section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects, Act, No. 14
of 2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the
Minister in charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub-section (4) of
Section 3 under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1847/36 dated 30th January, 2014 do by this Order declare that-
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1847/36 dated 30th January, 2014 is hereby
amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid Project shall commence commercial operations within ninety-two (92) months from the date
stated in the Projet Agreement with the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka.”.
M
ALIK S AMARAWICKRAMA, M. P,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
14th August, 2018.
