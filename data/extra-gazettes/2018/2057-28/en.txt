THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land in total extent of about 2.2150 Hectares,
depicted as Lots ‘A ’, ‘B’ and ‘D’ in the Advance Tracing No. AM/
NIN/2016/358 and dated 08.11.2016 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys - Ampara District, situated in the
Village called Nintavur in the Minor Division of Nintavur Pattu in
the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Nintavur in Ampara District
in Eastern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kokaddi Ilavasam – Extent: 0.1911 Hectare
North: Kokaddi Ilavasam claimed by Aboobucker Maraikar and
A. L. Safeer; East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Lots B and D; West:
Kokaddi Ilavasam claimed by M. H. M. Yaqub Hasan.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kokaddi Ilavasam – Extent: 1.2145 Hectares
North: Lot A; East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Kokaddi Ilavasam
claimed by S. M. Buhary, M. L. Achchi Mohamed and M. N. Nahoor;
West: Kokaddi Ilavasam claimed by M. L. Achchi Mohamed, M. N.
Nahoor and Lot D.
03. Lot ‘D’ – Kokaddi Ilavasam – Extent: 0.8094 Hectare
North: Lot A; East: Lot B; South: Kokaddi Ilavasam claimed by
M. L. Achchi Mohamed, M. N. Nahoor, M. M. Kabeer and his wife;
West: Kokaddi Ilavasam claimed by M. H. M. Yaqub Hasan.
