NOTIFICATION
Cancellation  of  the  Gazette  Notification  (Extraordinary)  No.  2068/25  dated  26.04.2018   issued  under  the
 Sports  Act  No. 25 of  1973.
I, Faiszer Musthapa, the Minister of  Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports do hereby can cel the Gazette
Notification  No. 2068/25 dated 26.04.2018 by which the registration of the Archery Association (which is the Nat ional
Association for Archery) was revoked with effect from 18.05.2018 under Sec. 32 (b) and 33 of the Sports Act No. 25 of 1973
and order that re - registration there of be confirmed as National Association.
I do hereby further direct the Sri Lanka Archery Association to appoint impartial Election Committee  to convene the
election to elect new office bearers for 2018 - 2020.
 FAISZER MUSTHAPA (M. P),
President Counsel.
Minister of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports
At Colombo.
