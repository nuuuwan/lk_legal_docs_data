CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No. 17 OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required to the Ceylon Electricity Board described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
18th October 2018.

Order under Section 57
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto.

18th October 2018.
Schedule
Boundaries of lands respectively 3.7757 hectares and 4.9286 hectares in extent, depicted as  Lot A and Lot B, in the
Survey Plan No. BHP- 05 dated 04.09.2018, prepared by the Ceylon Electricity Board  situated in Millagahamula village,
of Grama  Niladhari Division No. 316/ E, and  Pitawala  Village of  Grama Niladhari  Division No. 316/ D, of  Ambagamuwa
Divisional Secretary's Division of  Ambagamuwa Korale of  Nuwaraeliya administrative district of the Central Province
of the land of Sri Lanka, as described in the Schedule hereto :
01. Lot No. A - The land in extent of 3.7757  Hectares
North : Main Road ;
East : Delpath Oya;
South : Kelani River and Delpath Oya;
West : Rambukpath Oya.
Land ownership : Not known.
02. Lot No. B - The land in extent of 4.9286  Hectares
North : Delpath Oya and Main road ;
East : Main Road and the boundary up to the Kelani River from the 11th k.m. Post;

West : Delpath Oya .
