Notice under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment land containing in extent of about 5.2768 Hectares,

bounded as follows:

5.2768 Hectares
North: Thibbatuhenawatta claimed by H. M. V . Agnas Mebal De

land claimed by Kahawatta Plantation; East: Kandeththewatta claimed
by H. A. Sirisena, Rest of this land claimed by Kahawatta Plantation,
Imbulththenna Playground claimed by Kahawatta Plantation and
Imbulththenna claimed by G. L. Chandra Landani; South:
Imbulththenna Playground claimed by Kahawatta Plantation, Rest
of this land claimed by Kahawatta Plantation, Imbulththenna claimed
by G. L. Chandra Landani, M. G . Heenmahaththmaya, K. Nalani
Latha Gunawardhena, H. F. Priyanka Fonseka, T. L. Wijepala and

Liyanapathirana and T. W. Gunarathna, Wekadakumbura claimed by
Jayantha Liyanapathirana, Udahagedarawatta claimed by H. A. Upali
and K. H. Indika Jayasundara, Aluthgedharawatta claimed by W. M.
Indrani Premalatha W. R. M. Peeris, G. L. Chandra Landani, G . L.
Maheepala and G . L. Mudiyanse, Pahalagalawatta claimed by K. D.
Jayasiri, Thibbatuhenawatta claimed by H. M. V. Agnas Mebal De
Silva, Kandeththewatta claimed by H. A. Sirisena.
