EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by Honourable Minister of Finance and Mass Media, by virtue of the
power vested in him by Section  25, read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as amended, and published in
the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2065/57 of 06.04.2018 to do by this Order amend the Existing   Notification No. 1004, published
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2049/4 dated December 11, 2017 in order to revise existing the license fee for bottling
Toddy, specified in the Item No. 03 of Schedule I of that Notification by substituting the license fee,  with effect from April 01,
2018 was passed by Parliament on June 21, 2018.
R. H. S. S
AMARA TUNGA
Secretary
Ministry of  Finance and Mass Media
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
July 09, 2018.
