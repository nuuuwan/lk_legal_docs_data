NOTIFICATION   MADE   UNDER   SECTION   66C   OF   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   AUTHORITIES   ELECTOINS
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 22 OF 2012
I, Chandrani Samarakoon, the Commissioner of Local Government of Western Province as per powers entrusted to me
under Clause 66C of Local Government Authorities Elections Ordinance amended by Local Government Authorities Elections
(Amendment) Statute No. 22 of 2012 do hereby announce that the first meetings of the Local Government Authorities of
Western Province, names of which mentioned in the Schedule hereunder to be held for the purpose of selecting Mayor/
Chairman, Deputy Mayor/Deputy Chairman, will be convened on the date, time and place correspondingly stated in the
aforementioned schedule.
C
HANDRANI  S AMARAKOON ,
Commissioner of Local Government
Western Province.
At Western Provincial Department of Local Government,
No. 02, Cambridge Terrace,
Colombo 07,
on the date of 15th March 2018.
