1A   G   29155 —17  (12/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   MAWATHAGAMA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of Member of Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Weththige Upul Priyantha Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon  returned  member if United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a Member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Farook Muwad Farook to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Farook
Muwad Farook was selected as the Member of the Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
