PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY   THE  PRESIDENT
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70(4) of the Chapter XI of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Proclamation summon Parliament to meet at
the Parliament Hall, Sri Jayawardenepura, on the Eighth day of May, Two Thousand and Eighteen at 02.15 p.m.
Given at Colombo on this Twenty Third day of April, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
                                                                                               By  Order of His Excellency,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
