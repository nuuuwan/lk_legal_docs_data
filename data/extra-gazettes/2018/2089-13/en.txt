V ALUE  ADDED TAX  ACT, No. 14 OF 2002  AS  AMENDED BY ACT,  No. 06 OF 2005
Order under Section  2A
BY virtue of the powrs vested in me by Section 2A of the V alue added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002, as amended by Act, No. 06 of
2005, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that the V alue Added Tax shall
be charged on the importation of goods specified under H.S. Codes and descriptions in Column I and I I of the Schedule
hereto, at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column III, of such Schedule.

Colombo 01,
