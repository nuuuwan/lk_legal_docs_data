Provincial Councils Notifications
 NORTH   CENTRAL    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of North Central Provincial Council
By virtue of the power vested in me, under section 32( a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987, I Punchi Banda
Dissanayaka, the Governor of the North Central Province, do hereby notify that the officers, names mentioned below have
been appointed for the posts North Central Provincial Council Services.
01. Mr. S. M. S. Bandulasena, Officer in special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Secretary of the Ministry of
the Provincial Health, Ayurvedic, Social Welfare Services, Probation and child care services and Provincial Affairs with
effect from 22.03.2017 until further an order is made.
02. Mr. W. A. R. Wimalasiri, Officer in special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as Deputy Chief  Secretary (Personnel
and Training) of North Central Provincial Council with effect from the date 07.04.2017 to 27.08.2017.
03. Mr. H. M. Thilaka Bandara, Officer in special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Deputy Chief Secretary
(Personnel and Training) of North Central Provincial Council with effect from 28.08.2017 until further an order is made.
04. Mr. W . A. R. Wimalasiri, Officer in special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Secretary of the Public Service
Commission of  North Central Provincial Council with effect from 28.08.2017 until further an order is made.
05. Mrs. W. T. A. Manel, Officer in Grade I of Sri Lanka Administrative Service for acting as the Chief Secretary of North
Central Provincial Council with effect from 29.09.2015.
06. Mr. S. S. M. S. R. Dharmadasa, Officer in Grade I of  Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Land Commissioner North
Central Provincial Council with effect from 22.06.2017 until further an order is made.
