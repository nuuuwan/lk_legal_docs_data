THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land extent of about 0.4957 Hectares depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. MR/MLM/2017/133 and dated
03.05.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
of Malimbada in Weligam Koralaya South Minor Division, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division in Matara District in
Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Nakandagodahena – Extent: About 0.4957
Hectare
North: Cement Channel; East: Nakandagodahena claimed by
National Water Supply and Drainage Board;  South and West:
Nakandagodahena claimed by V . S. Kumarage.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
