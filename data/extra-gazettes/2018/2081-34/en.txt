No. 2081/34  –  WEDNESDAY JULY 25, 2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No. : BD/RM/LND.2/PMORP-III 
 
Land  Min. No.: 4/3/9/2012/I R/351  
I hereby give notice, under Sub - Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
Morana Tank Project. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Schedule I  
 
Allotments of Land in TOPo.P.P. No.: 72, Insert No. 
12, Sheet No. 165 prepared by the superintend surveyor 
in Badulla, situated in Yalwela village, in the 
Redeemaliyadda Divisional Secretary’s Division, of the 
district of Badulla in Uva Province described as follows. 
 
01.  Lot No. 2147 
Name of the Land: Neluwa; Claimant: K. H. M. 
Keerthisena Bandara; Extent : 0.5059 Hectare.  
02.  Lot No. 2148 
Name of the Land: Neluwa; Claimant: State; 
Extent : 0.2204 Hectare. 
 
03.  Lot No. 2149 
Name of the Land: Neluwa; Claimant: State; 
Extent : 0.0225 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Redeemaliyadda Divisional Secretariat on the 14th day of 
August 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 28 
day of July 2018 the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars or their claims for compensation for the 
said land the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 D. A. K. WANASINGHA, 
 Divisional Secretary,  
 Redeemaliyadda. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
20th July, 2018.
