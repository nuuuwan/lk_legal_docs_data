CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD   ACT,   No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
05th March 2018.
SCHEDULE
The land situated in the village of Thoddaveli in Grama Niladari’s Division of Thoddaveli in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Mannar, in the Mannar-West Pattu of Mannar Korale, in the Administrative District of Mannar, in the Province
of Northern of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 2.0398 hectares shown in the sketch No. CEB/MWPP/ACQLOT4/01
dated 26.01.2018 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board and demarcated by the coordinates A(9
01'32.33''N   79051'56.76''E),
B(901'30.16''N   79 051'59.6''E),  C(9 01'25.26''N   79 051'56.26''E),  D(9 01'27.92''N   79 051'53.29''E) bounded as follows:
North : Premises of Four Teess Rest Inn ;
East : Thoddaveli - Thalvupadu Road ;
South : Manthukudiyiruppu Road ;
West : Balance part of the same land ;
