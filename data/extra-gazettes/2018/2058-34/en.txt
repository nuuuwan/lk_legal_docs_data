EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  NO.  13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order,
a. amend Item No. 1(b) and 1(c) of the Schedule II of the Order made under that Section and published by the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017 by substitution of the following
Schedule A hereto ; and
b. amend Item No. 02 and 03 of the  Order made under that Section and published by the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2047/25 dated November 28, 2017 by substitution of the following Schedule B hereto.
This Order shall come into effect from February 16, 2018.
MANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
15th February 2018.
