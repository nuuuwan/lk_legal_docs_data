PUBLIC   SERVICE  COMMISSION
Appointment of Committees in terms of  Article 56(1) and 56(2) of  the Constitution
01. ON the direction of  the Public Service Commission, following members are hereby appointed to the Committee
constituting three members of  the Health Sector in terms of the Article 56 (1) of  the Constitution of  the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka with effect from the 01
st of  November Two Thousand Eighteen (2018) until further
orders.
I. Mr. Ranjith Maligaspe (Chairman)
II. Mrs. W. Sudharma Karunarathne (Member)
III. Mr. Sinnathamby Shanmugam (Member)
02. Accordingly, the contents stated under subsection 03 of the Gazette  Notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1990/ 25 dated 27th  October 2016 are amended
with effect from the 01 st of November Two Thousand Eighteen (2018).
By Order of  the Public Service Commission.
M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
Office of  the Public Service Commission,
No. 1200/9,
 Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla,
29th October 2018.
