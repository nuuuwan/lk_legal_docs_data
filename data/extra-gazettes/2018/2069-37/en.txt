Direction No. 65
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY   ACT,   No. 09  OF  2003
Direction under Section  12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs that no Manufacturer, Importer, Packer, Distributor or Trader shall manufacture,
import, distribute, pack, store or sell or display for sale, expose for sale, or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any skin
creams and lotions unless such skin creams and lotions shall conform to the permissible maximum limits for heavy
metals when tested in accordance with ISO/TR 17276 as specified in SLS 743 issued by the Sri Lanka Standard Institution.
Heavy Metal Permissible Maximum
Limit
Lead (as Pb) 10 mg/kg
Arsenic (as As) 3 mg/kg
Mercury (as Hg) 1 mg/kg
Cadmium (as Cd) 3 mg/kg
Skin Creams and Lotions include ;
(1) makeup creams and lotions
(2) cleansing creams and lotions
(3) moisturizing creams and lotions
(4) hair removal creams and lotions
(5) sun screen creams and lotions

Direction No. 66
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY   ACT,   No. 09  OF  2003
Direction under Section  10(1)(a)
ACTING undet the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Manufacturers, Importers, Packers, Distributors or Traders of skin creams and
lotions that the following information shall be legibly and indelibly marked on each and every bottle, pack or container of
skin creams and lotion. However, a separate leaflet containing such information may be issued for skin creams and lotions
of pack sizes of 50g/50ml or less.
(1) Name of the Product
(2) Name and address of the Manufacturer
(3) Name and address of the importer and/or distributor in Sri Lanka
(4) Country of Manufacture
(5) Brand name
(6) V olume/weight
(7) Batch or code or lot identification number
(8) Date of manufacture
(9) Expiry date or Best before date
(10) Maximum Retail Price
(11) List of Ingredients
(12) Instructions for use
(13) W arning statements, if any
Skin creams and lotions include ;
(1) makeup creams and lotions
(2) cleansing creams and lotions
(3) moisturizing creams and lotions
(4) hair removal cream and lotions
(5) sun screen creams and lotions
