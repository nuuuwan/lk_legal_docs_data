THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28
OF  1964   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
DIK/LAN/02/46.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Details of  the land to be Obtained :
Following land mentioned in the preliminary plan No. 2754
of Surveyor General and situated in Village of Walasgala
Grama Niladhari Division of Dickwella North in Dickwella
Divisional Secretariat Matara District.
Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Willapaththinigahawaththa alias Mahahena;
Description: Home Garden, Mixed Cultivation is there; Claimed
by: M. M. Leelarathna, Dambagasara, Walasgala, Dickwella;
Extent: 0.2914 Hectare.
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing before me at Dickwella Divisional Secretary's Office
on 09th January, 2019 at 10.30 a.m. are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 30th
December, 2018 the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of there claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount. All deeds and documents
in support of the claims should be produced before me at the
inquiry .
B. S. RANJITHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat Dickwella.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Dickwella,
30th November, 2018.
