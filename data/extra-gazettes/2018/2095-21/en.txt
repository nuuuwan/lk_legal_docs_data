SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of  Finance and Economic Affairs of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of list of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 03 months commencing from November 02, 2018, subject to provisions
of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Orders made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2075/31 of 14.06.2018 in respect of Black gram classifiable under the HS
Code 0713.31.29 and Gazette Notification  No. 2070/6 of 09.05.2018 in respect of lentils classifiable under the HS
Codes 0713.40.11, 0713.40.12, 0713.40.21, 0713.40.22 and Gazette Notification  No. 2087/76 of 07.09.2018 in respect
of Chickpeas classifiable under the HS Codes 0713.20.10, 0713.20.20 are rescinded with efffect from November 02,
2018.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
