THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
As Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. : GAL/LAN/AQUIS/A13/34.
Land Ministry Ref : 4-3/9/2016/WS/70.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the lands/servitude
described in the Schedule given below for a public purpose:
Description of the Lands/Servitude to be acquired:
Allotment of land in extent about 0.0766 Hectare
depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Ke/3626 dated 11/10/2018
prepared by Surveyor General situated in the villages of
Kobbewela in 71 Kobbewela Grama Niladhari Division
subdivision of Kandupita Pattuwa North in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Galigamuwa in the District of Kegalle
in Sabaragamuva Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Kandahena; Name of the Claimant:
S. M. Nimal Karunathilaka, No. 569/108, Bandaranayake
Place, Kalagedihena, Thihariya; Extent: 0.0007 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Kandahena; Name of the Claimant:
S. M. Nimal Karunathilaka, No. 569/108, Bandaranayake Place,
Kalagedihena, Thihariya; Extent: 0.0756 Hectare.
Dates for Inquires:
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be
acquires are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents
duly  authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional
Secretariat, Galigamuwa on the 14/11/2018, at 10.00 a.m.
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
with duplicate of on or before 07/11/2018  the nature of their
interests in the Land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the Computation of such
amount.
H.M.C. SUSANTHA HERATH,
Divisional Secretary,
Galigamuwa.
Divisional Secretariat Office,
Galigamuwa.
31st October, 2018.
