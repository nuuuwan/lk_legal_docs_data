REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Ports and Shipping under Section 10 read with Section 3 (5) of the Licensing of

Colombo,
May 04th, 2018.
Regulations
The Shipping Agents (Licensing) Regulations, 1991, published in Gazette Extraordinary  No.684/ 9 of October
17, 1991 are hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 7 thereof and the substitution therefor, of the following-
''7. A Shipping Agent shall levy any charge from a Principal for all services rendered in terms of  the tariff
recommended by the Ceylon Association of  Shipping Agents (CASA) and approved by the Minister in charge of
subject of Ports and Shipping.''.
