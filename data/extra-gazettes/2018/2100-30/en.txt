PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL

CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD   ACT,   No.  17   OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
29th November, 2018.
SCHEDULE
The land called Ataheraliyagahakumbura situated  in the village of  Malabe West  in Grama Niladhari’s Division of
476A Malabe West  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Kaduwela, in the Palle Pattu of  Hewagam Korale, in the
Administrative District of Colombo, in the Province of  Western of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 30 Perches  shown in the
rough sketch No. CEB/ NTDND&EIP/P2/ L1/ L3 Road  prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board , for 30ft wide access road. 30ft
wide access road  bounded as follows :
North : Ataheraliyagahakumbura in Plan No. 4259 and Water Canal ;
East : Chandrika Kumarathunga Mawatha ;
South : Ataheraliyagahakumbura in Plan No. 4259 and Water Canal ;
West : Portion of Ataheraliyagahakumbura acquired to Ceylon Electricity Board
(Trancing No. CO/ KDW/ 2016/ 410.)
Claimed by  : H. Lilawathie Silva, No. 144/ 1, Kurundu Watta, Thalahena, Malabe, H. Ariyawathi Silva,
No. 28, Thudalla South, Ja- Ela, H Premawathi Silva, No. 148/ 4, Sethsiri Mawatha, Thalahena
Malabe and S. L. Suriyaarachchi, Pore, Athurugiriya.
