WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Rent Act of 1972
ACCORDING to the powers entrusted to me as per 13th Amendment to the Constitution of 1978 and under Section 43 of the
Rent Act of 1972, I, Kejitan Lalith Wanigarathne, the Minister of Provincial Roads, Transport, Cooperative Development and
Trade, Housing and Construction, Estate Infrastructure Facilities, Industries and Rural Development of Western Province do
hereby make the following order as per Section 44 of the said Act.
K
EJITAN LALITH WANIGARATHNE,
Minister of Provincial Roads, Transport, Cooperative Development
and Trade, Housing and Construction, Estate Infrastructure
Facilities, Industries and Rural Development of
Western Province.
Battaramulla,
January 02, 2018.
ORDER
For the purposes of Rent Act No. 07 of 1972, I, Kejitan Lalith Wanigarathne, the Minister of  Provincial Roads, Transport,
Cooperative Development and Trade, Housing and Construction, Estate Infrastructure Facilities, Industries and Rural Devel-
opment of Western Province do hereby announce the under-mentioned officer as the Authorized Officer for implementing the
Rent Act of Western Province with effect from 02.01.2018. The areas subject to implementation of powers and duties assigned
to the officer under the Act have specifically been mentioned in front of the officer's name.
Name of the Authorized Officer, Areas,
Muhandirange Thushari Rodrigo. Western Province consisting the districts of
Colombo, Gampaha and Kalutara.
