EXCISE  ORINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by Honourable Minister of Finance and Mass Media, by virtue of the
power vested in him by Section  25, read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as amended, and published in
the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2066/2 of 09.04.2018 to do by this Order with the following license fees for toddy tapping with
effect from April 16, 2018 was passed by Parliament on June 21, 2018.
