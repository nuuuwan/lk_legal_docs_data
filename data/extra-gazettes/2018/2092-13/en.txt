THE   LAND    ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: HAK/LAN/1/14.
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.:
4-3/ 9 /2014/WS /232'
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of  Section (7) of
the  Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/ servitude
which is required for  public purpose mentioned below.
 Description of the land to be  Acquired:
Allotment of land depicted in paper No. 8 of Final Village
Plan 497 prepared by Senior Surveyor Superintendent of
Matara District, situated in the Village of Udupeellagoda East
No. 294 in Minor Division of Kandabada Pattu East in
Kandabada Pattu Division in Divisional Secretary's Division
of Hakmana, Matara District, Southern Province.
Lot No. 137 –  Land Name: Diwela, Ganewatta,
Ellalayakumbuwa; Name of Land Owner: Chief Incumbent
of Sri  Sudharmarama Purana Viharaya, Udupeellagoda;
Extent of the Land: 0.0044 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North by: Canal and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East by:
Lot Nos.14, 4 and 13A; South by: Lot Nos. 13A and 4; West
by: Lot No. 4.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Hakmana Divisional Secretariat on 12th November, 2018 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writting
(In duplicate) on or before 05th November, 2018  the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/  servitude,
the amount of compensation and details of the computation
of such amount.
KUSALAKA N ANAYAKKARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Hakmana.
At Divisional Secretariat, Hakmana.
03rd  October, 2018.
