NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE
Notice
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in the Minister in charge of the subject, under Section 289(1) of Municipal Council
Ordinance (Chapter 252) that should be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of
1989, I,  Marappulige Jayasinghe, Governor of the North Central Province, do hereby make regulation pertaining to the
following allowances to the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Member of Municipal Council. These regulations shall be effected
from 01st of July 2018.
M
ARAPPULIGE  J AYASINGHE,
Governor of North Central Province,
North Central Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Province,
13th June,  2018.
ORDERS
01. Fuel limits to Mayor of Municipal Council for official visit of Municipal Council :
I. If vehicle of Municipal Council is used and for a Municipal Council comprise of one Divisional
Secretariat, not more than 400 liters diesel for a period of one month, or
III. In the event of non-avaliability of Municipal Council vehicles, personal vehicles could be utilized
under the approval of Commissioner of Local Government,  subject to the said fuel limitation and
expenditure could be incurred under Municipal Council Fund.

02. Travelling expenses and subsistence :
I. A sum of  Rs. not more than 500.00 subsistence for one day can be paid for Mayor of the Municipal
Council subjected to the Provisions of Establishment Code and these allowances can be paid maximum
10 days for a month.
II. Subsistence allowances are paid only duties out of Municipal Council limit. These allowances should
be paid incompliance with the provisions of Establishment Code.
III. These allowances will not be paid for travelling within the Municipal Council limits.
IV . Commuted travelling allowances and subsistence are allowed only for the official visit of respective
Municipal Council. Except, having an official invitation for function seminar or meeting. It is not
entitled to obtain travelling expenses or subsistence under Municipal Council fund.
03. Refreshment Allowances :
I. A monthly allowances of Rs. 3000.00 can be paid to the Mayor of Municipal Council  as refreshment
allowance. The expenditure Head should be submitted for the purpose.
II. A monthly allowance of Rs. 750.00 can be paid to the Deputy Mayor of Municipal Council as
refreshment allowance. The Expenditure Head should be submitted for the purpose.
III. For the Members of Municipal Council participation for meeting of Municipal Council each member
for each meeting could be spent Rs. 100.00 under Municipal Council Fund.
04. Telephone Allowance :
I. Permission will be given to pay following allowances to the Mayor and Deputy Mayor for the residence
telephone under Municipal Council Fund.
II. To the Mayor for a month Rs. 4000.00
III. To Deputy Mayor for a month Rs. 2500.00
05. Attendence allowances for Municipal Council and Committee Meetings
I. An allowance of Rs. 500.00 can be paid to the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Members of Municipal
Council for the attendence of one meeting under Municipal Council Fund.
06. Allowancs to personal staff of the Mayor of Municipal Council :
I. The Mayor of the Municipal Council can appoint a Personal Assistant and a maximum amount of
Rs. 15,000.00 could be paid for him/her under Municipal Council Fund.
II. The Mayor of the Municipal Council can appoint a Public Relation Officer and a maximum amount of
Rs. 10,000.00 could be paid for him/her under Municipal Council Fund.

Notice
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in the Minister in charge of the subject, under Section 184(1)(c)(d)(f) of Pradeshiya
Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of
1989, I, Marappulige Jayasinghe, Governor of the North Central Province, do hereby make regulation pertaining to the
following allowances to the Chairman, Vice Chairman amd Members of Pradeshiya Sabha. These regulations shall be
effected from 01st of July 2018.
M
ARAPPULIGE  J AY ASINGHE,
Governor of North Central Province,
North Central Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Province,
13th June,  2018.
REGULATIONS
01. Fuel limits to Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha for official visit of Pradeshiya Sabha :
I. If vehicle of Pradeshiya Sabha is used and for a Pradeshiya Sabha comprise of one Divisional
Secretariat, not more than 400 liters diesel for a period of one month, or
II. The expenses may be incurred for not more than 500 liters diesel for one month for a Pradeshiya
Sabha comprise of two Divisional Secretariats.
III. In the event of non-availability of Pradeshiya Sabha vehicles, personal vehicles could be utilized under
the approval of Minister incharge of the subject within the said fuel limitation and expenditure could
be incurred under Pradeshiya Sabha Fund.
02. Travelling expenses and subsistence :
I. A sum of  Rs. not more than 500.00 subsistence for one day can be paid for Chairman of the Pradeshiya
Sabha subject to the Provisions of Establishment Code and these allowances can be paid maximum 10
days for a month.
II. Substistence allowances are paid only duties out of Pradeshiya Sabha limit. These allowances should
be paid incompliance with the provisions of Establishment Code.
III. These allowances will not be paid for travellings within the Pradeshiya Sabha limits.
IV . Commuted travelling allowances and subsistence are allowed only for the official visit of respective
Pradeshiya Sabha. Except, having an official invitation for function seminar or meeting. It is not entitled
to obtain travelling expenses or subsistence under Pradeshiya Sabha fund.
03. Refreshment Allowances :
I. A monthly allowances of Rs. 2000.00 can be paid to the chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha as refreshment
allowance. The expenditure Head should be submitted for the purpose.
