EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: DEV/LND/ACQUI/04-24. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
Servitude which is required for a Public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
Description of the land   to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land bearing Lot 01 (Extent : 2.8578 
Hec.) depicted in the preliminary Survey Plan  
No. MR/2728, situated  in  the Village of Naotunna, 
Grama Niladhari Division of Talalla North in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara, Matara District, 
Southern Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land:  Godaudahena, Galkattawehena alias 
Galkattawa; Description: 7 Jak trees aged about 20 -25 
years, Teak trees aged 15 -20 years, Mixed cultivation 
with cinnamon trees; Extent 2.8578 Hectares. 
   
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at the Divisional Secretariat of Devinuwara  on 
the 25th day of April, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and  are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 09th day of April, 2018 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 
        RANJITH YAPA, 
                             Acquisition Officer/Divisional 
           Secretary, 
          Devinuwara. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Devinuwara, 
25th January 2018.
