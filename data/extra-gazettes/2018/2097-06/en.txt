No. 2097/6 – MONDAY, NOVEMBER 12, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MA/
NUL/2016/442 and dated 18.04.2017 prepared by the Surveyor-
General situated in the Village of Medapihilla (F.V .P. 17) in the Minor
Division of Gangala Pallesiya Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Naula in Matale District, Central Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Wandurumulleyaya – Extent: 0.5168 Hectare
North: State Forest; East: State Forest and State canal; South:
State canal and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and State Forest.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Wandurumulleyaya – Extent: 0.8094 Hectare
North: State Forest and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.),
Lot C and State canal; South: State canal and State Forest; West:
State Forest and State Stream.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Wandurumulleyaya – Extent: 0.1115 Hectare
North: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.),
Lot C and State canal; South: State canal and Lot B; West: Lot B.
