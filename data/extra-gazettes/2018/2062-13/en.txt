THE  STATE   INDUSTRIAL  CORPORATION   ACT,   No.  49  OF  1957
Vesting of State Land with the State Development and Construction Corporation under Section 25
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 25 of the State Industrial Corporations Act, No. 49 of 1957, I, Sajith
Premadasa, Minister of Housing and Construction with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance and Minister of Land
do hereby vest in the State Development and Construction Corporation, the State land described in the schedule hereto.
SAJITH  PREMADASA ,
Minister of  Housing and Construction.
Ministry of Housing and Constructions,
Sethsiripaya,
Battaramulla,
06th March 2018.
