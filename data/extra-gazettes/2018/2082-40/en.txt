1A - G 28201 — 17 (2018/08)
FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF HORANA URBAN COUNCIL UNDER
SECTION 66A (1) (a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member in Horana Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized Political Party to which
Balage Don Vipula Rohana Kulasiri the  member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 03 - Munagama West of Horana Urban Council who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Perumpulle Mudiyanselage Ajith Munasingha to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of Horana Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Perumpulle
Mudiyanselage Ajith Munasingha was elected as the member of the Ward Number 03 - Munagama West of Horana Urban
Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Horana Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara.
31st July, 2018.
