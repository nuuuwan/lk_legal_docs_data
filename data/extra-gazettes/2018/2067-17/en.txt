UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I Kabir Hashim, the Minister of
Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of the National
School of Business Management Limited, a Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and
bearing Registration Number PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under th e said Act, and
published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2053/40 of January 11, 2018 by the addition immediately after the entry "Bachelor of
Science Honours in Computer System Engineering" appearing in column I and it's corresponding entry in Column II respectively
of the Schedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of th e Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
                                                                                                                                                                KABIR HASHIM,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo ,
18th   April  2018.
    SCHEDULE    I
CONDITIONS
National School of Business Management Limited, shall, when admitting  students for the degree programmes specified in
Schedule II hereto in respect of :
(a) Bachelor of Interior Design, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (03) 'S' passes in any subject
stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advance Level) Examination or equivalent.
