1A   G   27976 —17  (07/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  ALAWW A   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Patabandi Wasam Baduge Gamini Wijayananda Senadheera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 4 - Nungamuwa if Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Karuna Hewage Chaminda Weerawansha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, returning officer of the Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Karuna
Hewage Chaminda Weerawansha was elected as the member of the 4 - Nungamuwa of Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Alawwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
