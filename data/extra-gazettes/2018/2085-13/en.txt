1A   G   28332 —17  (08/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  KARANDENIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Ranjith Donald Ranaweera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon  the member of that No. 05 Magala in Karandeniya  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Bodiya Baduge Lalith Rathnakumara Gamini Perera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, returning officer of the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Bodiya Baduge Lalith Rathnakumara Gamini Perera was selected as the member of the Ward No. 05  Magala of Karandeniya
Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
21st August, 2018,
