My No.: 3/6/2016/7/2(2018).

I do hereby give notice, under sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, That the government intends 
to acquire the following lands/ servitudes which  required 
for a public purpose:- 
 
Schedule of the Land Acquired: 
 
Two (02) Allotments of land in extent of 0.0205 hectare 
depited as Priliminary plan No. ý 2395 of 25.06.2018 
(lots 1 to 2) prepared by the senior Superintendent of 
surveys, Kurunegala District on behalf of the Surveyor 
General Situated in the villages of Nillakkegama in 888 
Dewategedara Grama Niladari Divisions of Polgahawela 
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kurunegala District, 
North western province. 
     
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Waragaspitiyahena, Aswedduma; 
Land Use : Garden, Temporary Plantation; Claimant: 
State; Original claimant: K.P.H. Daya Menike Herath, 
No. 135/5, Diggale watta, Colombo Road, Potuhera; 
Extent : 0.0068 Hectare.    
02. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Elhena, Ambagahamulahena; Land 
Use : Garden, Temporary Plantation; Claimant: State; 
Original claimant: G. Sriyani Pushpa Kumari, 
Nillakkegama, Lindakumbura, Mahapitiya, Potuhera; 
Extent : 0.0137 Hectare. 
 
All persons whom interested in the aforesaid lands/
servitudes is to be acquired, are hereby required to appear 
personally, or through an agent duly authorized in 
writing, before me at divisional secretariat Kurunegala on 
the day of 19.07.2018, in nature of their claims for 
compensation for the said lands/Servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of compensation of such 
amount, further the person should appear for an inquiry 
under section 09 of the act Commence from 08.08.2018 
on wards. The date of inquiry and the relevant documents 
submit at the inquiry such as deeds may duly informed by 
me. 
 
If an agent may present at the inquiry, Original claimant 
of the land must be duly authorized in writing, Without 
such a letter statement or evidence may not be recorded 
from such an agent. 
    
 D. ANOMA  DISANAYAKE, 
 Divisional Secretary & Land Acquiring Officer, 
 Polgahawela.
