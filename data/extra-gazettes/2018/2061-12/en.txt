Rules
The rules published in Gazette No. 5147 dated 17th June 1892 as amended as from time to time and last amended
by Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1777/ 38 dated 28th September 2012 are hereby further amended as follows :-

therefore :-

requested to set off every loan or advance obtained under the rules No. 12 and 13 and any balance of
the property loan obtained under Chapter II. This shall consist of total contribution fee, the interest
