FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAWATHAGAMA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Kankani
Arachchi Appuhamilage Disna Chamani Janaranjana the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  returned member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Karunarathna Appuhamilage Ajantha Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  Gamalathge Dlip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of the Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Karunarathna Appuhamilage Ajantha Kumari was selected as the member of the Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D LIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
29th November 2018.
