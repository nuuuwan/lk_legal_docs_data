THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
  Reference No.  NE/3/1/8/Acquisition/143. 
 
I,  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land in total extent about 0.4947 
Hectare described as Lot 01 in preliminary Plan No.  
Nu 3151 of 03.10.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General 
situated in Dayagama West (Division No. 1) Village in 
Sandiriham 476 R Grama Niladhari Division in Oya 
Palatha Korale Minor Division in Nuwara Eliya 
Divisional Secretary’s Division, in Nuwara Eliya District 
and bounded as follows : 
 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Dayagama West (Division No. 1); 
Details: Tea Cultivation; Claimant: Agarapatana Plantations 
Ltd, Voxhalla Street, Colombo 02;  Extent about: 0.4947 
Hectare. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands are to be 
acquired hereby required to appear personally, or by agents 
duly authorized in  writing, before me at  Divisional 
Secretariat on the 25th day of April 2018 at 10.30 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 18th day of April 2018. The 
nature of their interests in the land. The particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
 
 
                                    S. P. K. BODHIMANNA,

                                               Nuwara Eliya.
