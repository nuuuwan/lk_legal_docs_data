1A   G   27977 —17  (07/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  KURUNEGALA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Boyagane Dewage Jagath Manjula Wijerathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 25 - Katupitiya if Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ranasinghe Arachchilage Asanka Pradeep Ranasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, returning officer of the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ranasinghe
Arachchilage Asanka Pradeep Ranasinghe was elected as the member of the 25 - Katupitiya of Kurunegala Pradeshiya
Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
