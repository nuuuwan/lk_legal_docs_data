AN order to provide for the services of the financial year 2018, to authorize the raising of loans within the
province of  Sabaragamuwa for the purpose of such service, to make financial provisions in respect of
certain activities of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council during that financial year, to enable the payment,

Lanka as follows.

to spend Rupees Twenty Nine Billion Six Hundred and Thirty Six Million One Hundred and Forty
Thousand (Rs. 29,636,140,000/-) which estimated for the services of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council for the period beginning on 01st January, 2018 and ending on 31st December, 2018, by
virtue of powers vested in me under section 27(A) of Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987 as the
term of the Sixth Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council ended on 26th September, 2017.
