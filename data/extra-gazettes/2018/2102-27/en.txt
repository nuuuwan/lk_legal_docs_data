THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
HAVING been duly directed by the Minister of Lands and
Parliamentary Reforms under sub-section (1) of section 2 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, I hereby give
public notice as required by sub-section (1) of the section 7
that land in the area described below is required for a public
purpose.
Description of  land/servitude to be acquired :
The allotment of land called Walagamawaththa having
Extent of 0.0126 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 3516 as per the
survey plan of Padiyathalawa Topo.PP 49, Supplement
No. 54 surveyed by the surveyor General, situated in the
village of Meegaswatta with in the minor Division of
Binthenna Paththu South under the Padiyathalawa Divisional
Secretariat in Ampara District in the Eastern Province.
Boundaries of the Land
North: Lot No. 3517; East: Lot No. 3517; South: Lot No.
3517 and 1622; W est: Lot No. 1622.
The owner who claim ownership or have any other relation
to the above described land/ servitude or any person acting
under their written directions should meet me at Divisional
Secretariat Padiyathalawa on 09.01.2019 at 10.00 a.m. Further,
the nature of ownership to the above land/ servitude details
of the reason to request compensation for land acquisition,
the amount of compensation and the mode of calculation of
the compensation should be submitted in written document
with two copies on or before 09.01.2019.
R. M. N. WIJAYATHUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Padiyathalawa.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Padiyathalawa.
