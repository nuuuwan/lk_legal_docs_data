No. 2087/12 – MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 03, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land described as Lots ‘A’, ‘B’, ‘C’, ‘D’, ‘E’,
‘F’ and ‘G’ in total extent of about 84.8065 Hectares, depicted in
Advance Tracing No. R/KLW/2005/99 and dated 08.09.2009,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Iliumbakanda (F.V .P. 932) in the Uda Pattuwa Minor Division in
Kukulu Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kalawana in
Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 60.5629 Hectares
North: Lot No. 659; East: Lot No. 75; South: Lot No. 413; West:
Lot Nos. 75, 95 and Lot No. 70 (T P. 105552).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 16.3817 Hectares
North: Lot No. 659; East: Lot No. 70 and (T P . 99544) and (T P .
99545) Lot No. 76; South: Lot Nos. 76 and 93; West: Lot No. 75.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 0.3926 Hectare
North: Lot No. 76; East: Lot No. 70 (T P. 99544); South: Lot
No. 92; West: Lot Nos. 92 and 76.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 0.5579 Hectare

06. Lot ‘F’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 0.0053 Hectare
North: Lot No. 94; East: Lot No. 75; South: Lot Nos. 75 and 74;
West: Lot No. 74.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Moradolahena – Extent: About 2.2657 Hectares
