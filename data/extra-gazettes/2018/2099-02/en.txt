THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is required
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in extent of 0.2200 Hectare, depicted as
Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. R/KLN/2014/319 dated 05.02.2015,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village called
Kahawatta (F.V .P. 901) in the Minor Division of Kolonna
Gampattuwa of Kolonna Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kolonna in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pathale Hene Watta – Extent: 0.2200 Hectare
North: Lot No. 215; East: Lot Nos. 215 and 203; South: Lot
No. 203; West: Lot Nos. 203, 452 and 215.
