CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, N. K. G . K. Nemmawatta,  Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from  12th  February, 2018, all
duties of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter
235), shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2057/1 of
05th February,  2018 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 18th  February, 2018.
N. K. G . K. N
EMMAW ATTA,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
09th  February,  2018.
