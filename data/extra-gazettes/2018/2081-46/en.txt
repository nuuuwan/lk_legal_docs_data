NATIONAL  AUDIT  ACT,  No. 19  OF 2018
Order  under  Section 1(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1(2) of the National Audit Act, No. 19 of 2018, I, Maithripala Sirisena,
President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do by this order, appoint August 01, 2018 to be the date on
which the Provisions of Parts I, II, III, IV , V , VI, VII,  VIII and IX of the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
July 25, 2018.
