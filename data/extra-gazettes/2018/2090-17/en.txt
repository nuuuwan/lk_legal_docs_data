L.D.B. 9/2001
MUTUAL   ASSISTANCE  IN  CIVIL   AND   COMMERCIAL   MATTERS   ACT,  No.  39  OF  2000
Order under Section 4
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 of the Mutual Assistance in Civil and Commercial Matters Act,
No. 39 of 2000, I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Order declare that the provisions
of Part II of the aforesaid Act, shall apply in respect of the countries specified in the Schedule hereto, being countries
which are parties to the Hague Convention on the Service Abroad of Judicial and Extrajudicial Documents in Civil and
Commercial Matters of November 15, 1965.
The Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1309/2 of October 06, 2003 is hereby rescinded without
prejudice to anything previously lawfully done thereunder.
T
HALA THA ATUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
25th September, 2018.
SCHEDULE
Parties to the Hague Convention on the Service Abroad of Judicial and Extrajudicial Documents in Civil and
Commercial Matters of November 15, 1965
Albania Kazakhstan
Andorra Korea (Republic of)
Antigua and Barbuda Kuwait
Argentina Latvia
Armenia Lithuania
Australia Luxembourg
Bahamas Malawi

MUTUAL   ASSISTANCE  IN  CIVIL   AND   COMMERCIAL   MATTERS   ACT,  No.  39  OF  2000
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 9 of the Mutual Assistance in Civil and Commercial Matters Act, No. 39
of 2000, I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Order declare that the provisions of
Part III of the aforesaid Act, shall apply in respect of the countries specified in the Schedule hereto, being countries which
are parties to the Hague Convention on the Taking of Evidence Abroad in Civil and Commercial Matters of March 18,
1970.
