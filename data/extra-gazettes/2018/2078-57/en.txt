GENERAL  SIR   JOHN  KOTELAWALA  DEFENCE  UNIVERSITY
(SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 17 OF 2018
Order  under  Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (Special Provisions),
Act, No. 17 of 2018, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, Minister of Defence do by this Order declare 6th July 2018 as the date on which the provisions of this
Act shall come into operation.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
Minister of  Defence.
Colombo,
6th July, 2018.
