Order  No.  52
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF   2003
Order  under  section  20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority hereby orders that no trader or supplier including a distributor, shall sell, expose, offer,or display for sale,
or supply for sale the following item above the Maximum Retail Price given below:
Item Maximum Retail Price (Rs)
White Sugar (Unpacketed) 100.00 per kg
White Sugar (Packeted) 105.00 per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 12th October, 2018.
                                            By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
A
NURA B. MEDDEGODA,
Chairman.
Colombo,
12th October, 2018.

Order  No.  53
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF   2003
Order  under  section  20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority hereby orders that no importer shall sell, expose, offer or display for sale, or supply for sale the following
item above the Importer’s Maximum Wholesale Price given below:
