THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS

(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
construction of jungle training camp and armory for Sri Lanka
Navy.
Description of  land/servitude to be acquired :
The under mentioned allotments of land situated in
Katuwanna Korale sub Division in Maho Divisional
Secretary's Division in Kurunegala District, North Western
Province.
Grama Niladari's Division: 206 Pothuwewa; Village:
Pothuwewa; F.V .P . No.: 2378; Date of Plan: 29.10.2018; No. of
Lots: 02.
01. Lot No. 124
Name of land: Moragoda Mukalana; Land Use: Land;
Claimant: Senanayaka Senarath Bandaralage Priyanka
Bandara Manike; Extent: 0.8094 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 125
Name of land: Moragoda Mukalana; Land Use: Land;
Claimant by: Senanayaka Senarath Bandaralage Priyantha
Bandara; Extent: 0.8094 Hectare.
Total: Extent: 1.6188 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at 10.00 a.m. at Divisional Secretariat, Moho on 06.02.2019
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing on or
before 07.01.2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of the compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. M. J. M. HERATH,
Divisional Secretary/Acquisition Officer,
Maho, Kurunagala District.
Divisional Secretariat,
Maho,
