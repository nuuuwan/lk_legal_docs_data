TOURISM  ACT,  No.  38  OF  2005
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 70 of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005, I, John Antony Emmanual
Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs, do hereby order in line with the Section
49(a) of the Land Acquisitions Act, that the land mentioned in the Schedule below is required to be acquired for the
purpose of development of tourism by Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
J
OHN ANTONY  E MMANUAL  AMARATHUNGA ,
Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs
Colombo,
12th July, 2018,
Schedule
The land 145.4 hectares (359 Acre., 1Rood, 06 Perches) in extent depicted as Lot Numbers A of Surveyor
General's Tracing No. MA/DSO/2017/126, situated in the village of Pavilupaddankaddykudiyeruppu, in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Thullukudiyiruppu in Mannar town Divisional Secretary's Division in Mannar District of Northern Province.
North : Suvamithotam (part of same land) and Road (R. D. A.) ;
East : Suvamithotam (part of same land) ;
South : Suvamithotam (part of same land) and Coastal Reservation ;
West : Suvamithotam (part of same land).
