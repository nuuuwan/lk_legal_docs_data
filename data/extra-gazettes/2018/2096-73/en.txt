02. The Government Agent/District Secretary of Jaffna will be the Returning Officer for Jaffna Electoral District
which comprises of Jaffna and Killinochchi Administrative Districts while the Government Agent/District Secretary of Vavuniya
District will be the Returning Officer of Vanni Electoral District which comprises of Mannar, Vavuniya and Mullative
Administrative Districts. The Returning Office of Digamadulla Electoral District will be the Government Agent/District
Secretary of Ampara Administrative District. Additional District Secretary (Development) Kandy Administrative District will
be the Returning Officer of the Mahanuwara Electoral District. The Government Agents/Districts Secretaries of the remaining
Administrative Districts will be of the Returning Officers of the respective Eelectoral Districts.
03. An Independent Group shall, between the date of the publication of the Notice under Section 10 of the
Parliamentary Elections Act requiring the holding of the election and the submission of the nomination paper of that
group, deposit in legal tender with the Returning Officer a turn calculated at two thousand rupees in respect of each
candidate nominated by such group, on working weekday during office hours.
04. Arrangements for receiving of deposits are made at the relevant District Secretariat or the District Eelctions
Office.
