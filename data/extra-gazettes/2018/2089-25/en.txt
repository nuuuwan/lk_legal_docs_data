SABARAGAMUW A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification in the Gazette that the By-Laws made by the Pradeshiya Sabha Pelmadulla and approved by the
Minister in charge with the portfolio were passed in the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
IT is hereby notified that the By-Laws namely ; By-Law for control of parking of motor vehicles within the Pradeshiya
Sabha area, by providing parking places and collecting prescribed fee for parking vehicles in such places and By-Law for
regulating and control of Public Libraries, having made by the Pradeshiya Sabha Pelmadulla by virtue of powers vested in
the Pradeshiya Sabhas by Section 126 read with Sub-section (1) of Section 122 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987 haivng passed at the monthly meetings of the said Pradeshiya Sabha held on 25.07.2013, and having approved by
virtue of the powers vested in the Minister of Provincial Local Government, Sabaragamuwa Province and notified in Part
IV(a) of the Gazette Notification of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka bearing No. 1863 dated 30.05.2014
were read and passed in and by the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council on 26.09.2017 in terms of Section 125 of the
aforementioned Pradeshiya Sabha Act read with Sub section (3) of Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions)
Act, No. 12 of 1989.

RIYANI P ADMALA THA,
