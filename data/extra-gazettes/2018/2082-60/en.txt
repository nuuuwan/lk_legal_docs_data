LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   (ADMINISTRATIVE   AREAS)   ORDINANCE   CHAPTER   265
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Paragraph (a) of Section 2 of Local Government (Administrative Areas) Ordinance,
Chapter 265 to be read with Paragraph (1) (a) of Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of
1989, I, Dassanayake Gunarathne Basnayake Mudiyanselage Dharmasiri Dassanayake the Chief Minister and Minister in
Charge of the subject of Local Government in the North Western Province, hereby notify that the following administrative
districts shall be the administrative areas of authority for the above purpose with effect from 06.07.2018.
It is further notified that the notification made under Section 2 of Local Government (Administrative) Ordinance
No. 57 of 1946 amended by the Act, No. 22 of 1955 to be read with Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No.
12 of 1989 which was published by Hon. Gamini Jayawickrama Perera the Chief Minister and the Minister of Education,
Local Government and Industries in the North Western Province in Section IV(a) of the Gazette Paper of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 760 dated 26.03.1993 is hereby repealed.
D. G. B. M. D
HARMASIRI DASSANAYAKE,
Chief Minister and Minister of Finance,
Engineering Services, Law and Order, Education,
Local Government and Local Administration,
Drainage and Tourism in the North Western Province.
Kurunegala,
06th July 2018.
Schedule
1. Administrative District Kurunegala
2. Administrative District Puttlam
