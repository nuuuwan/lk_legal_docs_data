THE  CEYLON  ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT  BEARING  No.  17  OF  1969
Order made in terms of Section 57
IN pursuance of the power vested with me as Minister of Power and Renewable Energy by virtue of the Ceylon Electricity
Board Act bearing No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya, hereby grant my approval to acquire the land which needs to
be acquired by the Ceylon Electricity Board and which is described in the Schedule hereto.
RANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewaable Energy.
30th July, 2018.
SCHEDULE
All that divided and defined allotment of land marked Lot No. 1 depicting in Plan No. 879 dated 28.10.2016 made by a
Licensed Surveyor, of the land situated at Batuwangala in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 233- Batuwangala within the
Divisional Secretariat - Neluwa in the Administrative District of Galle Southern Province, and bounded in terms of the
above plan as follows :
On the North by : Land owned S. D. L. Albert ;
On the East by : Remaining portion of the main land that comes under this land ;
On the South by : Remaining portion of the main land that comes under this land ;
On the West by : Road reservation of Neluwa - Dellawa Road.
