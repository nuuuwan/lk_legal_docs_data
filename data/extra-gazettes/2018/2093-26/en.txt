AMENDING   THE    NAME   OF   THE   INSTITUTE   OF   POST   HARVEST   TECHNOLOGY
THE name of the Institute of Post Harvest Technology established in terms of Gazette Extraordinary No. 1137/10 dated
19 th June 2000 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under State Agricultural Corporation Act “No. 11 of
1972 ” and being operated under the purview of the Ministry of Agriculture is hereby amended as “National Post Harvest
Management Institute”  as per approval granted on 21.05.2018 by the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka.
B. W
IJAYARATHNE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Agriculture.
October 12, 2018.
