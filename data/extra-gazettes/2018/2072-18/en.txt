THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Koongahawatta alias
Kamathgodellewatta or Godellawatta’’ in total extent of about 0.0104
Hectare depicted as Lot ‘A’ in the Plan No. Gam/Gam/2017/121
dated 26.05.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the
Village called Moragoda in Medapattuwa Minor Division, in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Gampaha of Gampaha District of
the Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Koongahawatta alias Kamathgodellewatta or
Godellawatta – Extent: 0.0104 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Koongahawatta alias
Kamathgodellewatta or Godellawatta claimed by P . M. Sriyalatha
Perera; South: H. P . W. Perera Mawatha; West: Balance portion of
this land.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
