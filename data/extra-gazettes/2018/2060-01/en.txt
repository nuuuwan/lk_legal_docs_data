CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, N. K. G. K. Nemmawatta,  Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from  26th  February, 2018, all  duties
of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2059/1 of
19th February,  2018 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 04th  March, 2018.
N. K. G . K. N
EMMAWATTA,
 Acting  Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
23rd  February,  2018.
