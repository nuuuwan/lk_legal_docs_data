No. 2053/17 – TUESDAY, JANUARY 09, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act,that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotment of  land situated at Morawaka village (f.v.p.
169) of Morawak Korale Minor Division in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Kotapola of Matara District in the
Southern Province, depicted as lots No 'A' in Survey General's
tracing No. MR/KTP/2017/439 and dated 18.09.2017,
containing in extent about 0.2023 Hectares and the bounded
as follows:
01. Lot  'A' – Bokutugahahena – Extent: 0.2023 Hectare
North: Bokutugahahena land claimed by Ramaswami Rani
and Ridiyagamage Nandawathi; East: Bokutugahahena land
claimed by Ridiyagamage Nandawathi and Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha); South: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Bokutugahahena
claimed by the Feyari Mount Tea factory; West:
Bokutugahahena claimed by the Feyari Mount Tea factory
and Bokutuhena claimed Ramaswami Rani.
Gayantha Karunathilaka,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
