ORDER  UNDER   INTERPRETATION   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 2)   AND  SPORTS   ACT,   No  25  OF  1973
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 33 of the Sports  Act, No. 25  of 1973 to be read with  Section 18 of the
Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 18), I, Faiszer  Musthapha, Minister of Provincial Councils,  Local Government and Sports,
do by this  Order,  Substitute of  the name and words '' Herath Mudiyanselage Buwaneka Padmakumara Herath, who is
appointed As Acting'' for the name ''Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardhana Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha
Bandara'' published in the extraordinary Gazette No. 2053/ 7 of  08th January 2018.
F
AISZER M USTHAPHA ,
