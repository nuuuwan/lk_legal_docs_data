EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 as amended,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance & Mass Media, do by this Order declare that :-
01. the rate of Excise duty applicable on importation of motor vehicle by a member of the first Northern Provincial
Council under the Concessionary Motor Vehicle Permit Scheme of the Local Government and Provincial Council
Circular No. 01/2017 dated April 21, 2017 with a Letter of Credit (LC) opened on or before November 09, 2017,
and cleared from Sri Lanka Customs on or before April 30, 2018 shall be, on every article specified in Column III
of the Schedule hereto, 35% from the payable Excise duty at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in
Column IV of the Schedule.
02.  the rate of Excise duty specified in the Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017 shall not be
applicable for the importation of such motor vehicles described in Paragraph 01 above.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01.
12th April 2018.
