EXCISE   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 13 OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 read together with Section 3A of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.
13 of 1989 as amended, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that, a
method for determination of payable Excise duty on sale or disposal of motor vehicles, imported or locally purchased on
100% duty/tax free basis by Diplomats or Diplomatic Organizations is hereby established.
Fiscal levies payable at the time of sale or disposal of a motor vehicle, imported or locally purchased on 100%
duty/tax free basis by a Diplomat or a Diplomatic Organization, will be a percent of payable Excise duty on such vehicle
at the time of importation, established on the basis of period of usage of such vehicle, as specified in the Schedule below.
Period between the date of first registration and date of sale or disposal of a vehicle shall be taken for computation
of period of usage of that vehicle. This Order should be applicable on sale or disposal of such motor vehicle, imported or
locally purchased and registered on the name of a Diplomat or a Diplomatic Organization, on November 21, 2015 or later.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
15th May, 2018.
