THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Kankaniyage Kumbura Wanatha in
total extent of about 0.0008 Hectare, depicted in the Advanced Tracing
No. Maha/PTD/2017/596 of 29.11.2017 prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village called Walala  in the Minor Division
of Pallegampaha Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Pathadumbara in Kandy District of Central Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kankaniyage Kumbura Wanatha – Extent:
0.0008 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.) and Kankaniyage
Kumbura claimed by W. M. P. N. Wahindara; South: Kankaniyage
Kumbura claimed by W. M. P. N. Wahindara and Kankaniyage
Kumbura Wanatha claimed by K. A. N. W. Nanayakkara; West:
Kankaniyage Kumbura Wanatha claimed by K. A. N. W. Nanayakkara
and canal.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/11/2017/UD/172.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
