1A  G  27734  — 17  (05/2018)
REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT,  No.  32  OF  1968
Order under Section 34
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016, I,
S. B. Nawinna, Minister of Internal Affairs and Wayamba Development  do by this Order, alter the date of expiration
published in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of Section  6, 9, 14, 16 and
17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the aforesaid Act (other than the
Registration of Persons Regulation 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of November, 20, 1990) to be 30.06.2019,
until the necessary infrastructure, arrangements and technological methodologies are made to.
S. B. N
AWINNA,
Minister of Internal Affairs and Wayamba Development.
Battaramulla,
23rd May  2018.
