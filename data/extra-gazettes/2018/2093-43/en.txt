I hereby give notice under Sub Section 1 of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act as amended by Land Acquisition (Amended) Act
No. 28  of 1964, that the government intends to acquire the
following land/servitude which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: for a Ranaviru
Village
Following lots described in sheet 15 in Supplementary No. 15
of F.V .P . No. 226 situated in the village of Thembiliyana within Uda
Pattuwa minor division in the Divisional Secretariat of Kuruwita,
District of Ratnapura, Sabaragamuwa Province.
01.  Lot No. 199
Name of Land: Paradise Estate; Original Owner: Land Reform
Commission, No. 475, Kaduwela Road, Battaramulla; Present
Claimant: State; Extent: 1.309 Hectare.
02.  Lot No. 200
Name of Land: Paradise Estate; Original Owner:
Land Reform Commission, No. 475, Kaduwela Road,
Battaramulla; Present Claimant: State; Extent: 0.187
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing before me at Divisional
Secretariat, Kuruwita on 12th November, 2018, at
11.00 a.m.
And are hereby required to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 29th October, 2018, the
nature of their interest in  the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of
the said land, the amount of compensation and details
of computation of such amount.
