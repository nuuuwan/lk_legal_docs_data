No. 2087/93 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 07, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Aliyawatta in total extent of about
0.0177 Hectare, depicted as Lot ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing No.
Maha/MDD/2017/356 and dated 31.07.2017, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village called Alliyadda in the Minor
Division of West Palispattuwa Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Medadumbara in Kandy District in Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Aliyawatta – Extent: 0.0177 Hectare
North and East: Balance portion of same land claimed by S. K.
Wijeratne; South: Stream, Road and Aliyawatta claimed by N. T.
Anoma Wineethamala; West: Aliyawatta claimed by N. T. Anoma
Vineethamala and Balance portion of same land claimed by S. K.
Wijeratne.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
