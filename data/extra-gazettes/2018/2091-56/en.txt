THE  LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460),
AS  AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT
(AMENDMENT) NO. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
Acquiring Land for the Development of Badulla City
Ref. No. of the Ministry of
Land Development - 31/91/UDA/184
My No . BD/BD/LND/03/05/1/i
I hereby give notice to the public under subsection (1) of
the Section (7) of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended
by the Land Acquisition Act (amendment) No. 28 of 1964
the land/servitude described below is required by the
government and the government intends to acquire the
said land for public purpose :-
Description of the land/ servitude to be acquired:
Schedule - 01 including the block of land and boundaries
described below for an extent of 0.0119 Hectares according
to the supplementary tenement list relevant to the
preliminary plan B 508 dated 24.08.2018 and to the
preliminary plan B 508 dated 27.10.1999, prepared by the
Badulla District Senior Superintendant of Survey for the
Surveyor General, situated in Badulla Central village
(within Badulla Municipality), Division No. 10,
Kendelagama coming under 78 B Badulla East Grama
Niladhari Division, of the Minor Division of Rilpola
Korale, in Badulla Divisional Secretary Division of
Badulla District in the Province of Uva.
Block No. 04
Name of the land: Pandithagedara watte and Radalawatte,
Assessment Number 3A, Post Office Road; Description of
the land: Road(part); Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 4th
block of land of P.P.B. 508. Verified after clearing the
boundaries. Acquired under the Section 38(A) of the Land
Acquisition Act. Original claimant is Lilian Peiris of No. 17,
Peiris Mawatha, Kalubowila, Dehiwala. For the completion of
acquiring; Extent: 0.0119 Hectare.
All those who claim ownership, interest and any other
connection to aforesaid land, should appear personally, or
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional
Secretariat, Badulla on 05.11.2018 at 10.30 a.m.
In addition, hereby requested to notify to me in writing with
two copies of the nature of their interests in the land/lands,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of computation of such amount before 22.10.2018.
N.M. GNANAWATHI,
Divisional Secretary,
Badulla.
At Divisional Secretariat, Badulla,
01st October, 2018.
