L. D. B. 5/ 2005
MEDIATION (SPECIAL CATEGORIES OF DISPUTES) ACT, No. 21 OF 2003
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act,
No. 21 of 2003, I  Thalatha Athukorale, Minister of Justice, do by this Order Specify the area set out in the Schedule
hereto, as Special Mediation  Board Area within which the settlement of disputes relating to the possession or ownership
of lands as determined by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1901/ 8 of  February 10, 2015 shall be
carried out, under the provisions of such Act.
SCHEDULE
Each Administrative District of Mullaitivu, Vavuniya, Mannar and Ampara.
T
HALATHA  A THUKORALE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
08th February  2018.
