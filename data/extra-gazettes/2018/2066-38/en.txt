1A  - G   27468—12  (04/2018)
THE  WAGES   BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD FOR  THE  COCONUT  GROWING   TRADE
IT hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, 1971, that Mr. S. K. L. Obeysekara and Mr.
Albert Peiris have been appointed as the Employers' Representatives of the Wages Board for the Coconut Growing Trade
in place of Mr. Malin Gunathilake and Mr. Sunil Poholiyadda by the Hon. Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and
Sabaragamuwa Development under Section 9 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136). This appointment is valid
only upto 16.02.2019.
S. A. N. S
ARANA THISSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour, Trade Unions Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
09th April 2018.
