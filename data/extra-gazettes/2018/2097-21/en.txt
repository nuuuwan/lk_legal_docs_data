I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act(Chapter 460), as amended by the
land Acquisition(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, was given
by me that the Government intends to acquire the following
land for the purpose of constructing Kurundugahamada under
water reservoir under Kandy North-pathadumbara integrated
water supply project.
     Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired:
The Lot No. 1 of the land in total extend of hectares 0.0184
depicted in the preliminary plan No. Maha/6773, prepared by
the Kandy District Senior Surveyor General situated at
Ruwanpura Village of Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor
division in the Harispattuwa Divisional Secretary's Division
of Kandy District in the Central Province.
  G 28952 - 17 (2018/11)
01. Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Yahalatanne Watte Colony; Claimant:
B.G .Kalayanawathy;  Extent: 0.0184 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are required to
appear before me personally, or by agents duly authorised in
writing at Divisional Secretary's Office, Harispattuwa on the
23rd January, 2019 at 10.00 a.m.  and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing [in duplicate] on or before the 23rd
January, 2019, the nature of their interests in the land, and the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of thea fore said land the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
