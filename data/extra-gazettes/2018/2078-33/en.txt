THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation  of  Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956) as amended by Acts. No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 dated 30.10.2017 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2044/38 dated 09.11.2017 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the
statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 21.09.2017. –
Mr. S. Maduraweeran, No. 65, Jana Udana Village, Batugampola, Handapangoda  of the one part
and
Bank of Ceylon Head Office, No. 01, Bank of Ceylon Mawatha, Colombo 01  of the other part
was referred to Mr. P . Nawaratne for settlement by arbitration.
AND, whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said order be revoked, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and
Trade Union Relations do hereby revoke the said order and further make order that no proceedings be taken upon the said
order dated 30.10.2017.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
25th June, 2018.

STA TEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the deprival of the pension entitlement of Mr. S. Maduraweeran who had been an employee of the Bank of Ceylon
from 27.08.1981 , recruited to the permanent cadre from 03.10.2001 and retired on 04.12.2004 due to completaion of proper age,
is unjust as he had not completed the due permanent service period and if such injustice had been caused to what reliefs he is
entitled.
