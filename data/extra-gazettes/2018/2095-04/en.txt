No. 829/1 of 2018
P.S. No.  PS/CSA/00/1/3/1
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under paragraph (2) of Article 43 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint the following Ministers, with effect from October 29,
2018.
Name Portfolio
1. Hon. Mahinda Rajapaksa, MP - Minister of Finance & Economic Affairs
2. Hon. Nimal Siripala de Silva, MP - Minister of Transport & Civil Aviation
3. Hon. (Dr.) Sarath Amunugama, MP - Minister of Foreign Affairs
4. Hon. Mahinda Samarasinghe, MP - Minister of Ports & Shipping
5. Hon. Mahinda Amaraweera, MP - Minister of Agriculture
6. Hon. Ranjith Siyambalapitiya, MP - Minister of Power & Renewable Energy
7. Hon. (Dr.) Wijayadasa Rajapaksa, MP - Minister of Education & Higher Education
8. Hon. Vijith Wijayamuni Zoysa, MP - Minister of Fisheries & Aquatic Resources Development and
Rural Economic Affairs
9. Hon. Faizer Musthapa, MP - Minister of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports
10. Hon. Douglas Thevaanantha, MP - Minister of Resettlement, Rehabilitation, Northern Development
and Hindu Religious Affairs
11. Hon. Arumuga Thondaman, MP - Minister of Hill Country New Villages, Infrastructure &
Community Development
12. Hon. Wasantha Naresh Parakrama - Minister of Tourism & Wild Life
Senanayaka, MP
By order of His Excellency,
U
DAY A R S ENEVIRATNE
Secretary to the President
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
October 29, 2018.
