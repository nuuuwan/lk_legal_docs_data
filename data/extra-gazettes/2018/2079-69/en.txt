CONSULAR  FUNCTIONS  ACT,  No.  04  OF  1981
Notification under  Section 8
I, Tilak Marapana, Minister of  Foreign  Affairs, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 8 of the Consular Functions
Act, No. 04 of 1981, do hereby notify that, with effect from  1st August 2018, Sri Lankan refugees living in India as at 31st
July 2018 shall be exempted from the Consular fees applicable to registration of births and deaths, as mentioned in the
Column II of the Gazette notification No. 2057/ 35 dated 09 th February 2018.
TILAK M ARAPANA,
Minister of  Foreign Affairs.
Colombo,
4th July, 2018.
