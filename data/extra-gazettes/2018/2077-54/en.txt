No. 2077/54 – SATURDAY, JUNE 30, 2018
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Waththegama Urban Council
IMPOSING   ENTERTAINMENT   TAX
IT is hereby informed, according to sub section (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance chapter 267, that the following proposal
which is decided by the secretary of the Waththegama Urban Council, in terms of the provisions of Section 2 (1) of the above
Ordinance, has been accepted by the Minister in-charge for the subject of local governments of the provincial council of the
central province, in terms of the provisions of sub section (2) of the said ordinance.
R. M. G . S. RA THNAYAKE,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Local Government Ministry,
Central Province.
At Pallekele.
14th February, 2018.
PROPOSAL
I have decided to levy an Entertainment Tax of 20 % from the ticket fee (with out entertainment tax) within the limits of
the Waththegama Urban Council, in terms of the powers vested to me according to chapter 255, section 27 (3) of the Urban
Council act and in terms of provisions of section 2 (1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 further amended by
the entertainment tax act No. 37 of 1984.
