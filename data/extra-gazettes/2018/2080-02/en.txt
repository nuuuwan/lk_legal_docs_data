1990     SUWASERIYA   FOUNDATION   ACT    No. 18 OF 2018
Order  under  Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the 1990 Suwaseriya Foundation Act No. 18 of 2018, I, Maithripala
Sirisena President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do by this order, appoint July 16, 2018 to be the date
on which the provisions of Parts I, II, III, IV and V of the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
13th July, 2018.
