FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  66A(1)  (1a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of Member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Sarath Abegunawardana the Member appointed to
Ward No. 05 Udahamulla of Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1 ) (a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance,to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 05 Udahamulla of Maharagama Urban Council who belongs
to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is Qualified under section 8 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Membr within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate Yakalle
Kankanange Thilaka Premalal Weerasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (1a) of the Local Auhtorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) d o hereby declare that
Y akalle Kankanange Thilaka Premalal Weerasinghe was elected as the Member of the Ward No. 05 Udahamulla of Maharagama
Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. RUPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
03rd July  2018.
