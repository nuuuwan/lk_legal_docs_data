SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council - 2018
FOURTH   SUPPLEMENTARY   FINANCIAL   STATEMENT
GRANTING PERMISSION BY VIRTUE OF POWERS VESTED IN ME UNDER SECTION 27(A) OF PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL ACT, NO. 42
OF 1987 AS THE TERM OF THE SIXTH
SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF
SRI  LANKA  ENDED  ON  26 th SEPTEMBER,  2017- 2018
1. This permission may be cited as ''Permission to disburse under fourth  financial statement - 2018 of  the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial  Council'' and come  in to operation from 13th, November 2018.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Billion Five
Hundred and Seventy Nine Million, Five Hundred and Fifty  Four Thousand Six Hundred and Eighty One
Cents Eighty Seven Only (Rs.30,579,554,681.87) fot the service of the period beginning on 01 st January
2018 and ending on 31st December, 2018, which is specified in 2 nd paragraph of Gazette Notification No. 2052/ 15
dated 03.01.2018, which is amended by third supplementary financial statement for the year 2018 and assented
on 12.09.2018, is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Two Billion Eight Hundred and Thirty Four Million, Four
Hundred and Eight Thousand One Hundred and Twelve and Cents Thirty Five Only (Rs. 32,834,408,112.35) and
grant permission to Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
Accordingly the first Schedule of the said Gazette Notification is amended as the first schedule hereof and
grant permission to disburse the same.
3. Advanced accounts limits which is specified in second schedule of Gazette Notification  No. 2052/ 15 dated
03.01.2018 is hereby amended as second  schedule of this notification and grant permission to Chief Secretary
of this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council Fund.
4. This permission will be valid till 31 st of December, 2018 or upto the date that expires three months (03) from
the date of  summoning the Seventh (07 th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the
functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which occurs first.

AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
to disburse as per the Fourth Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2018, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Billion Five Hundred
and Seventy Nine Million, Five Hundred and Fiffty Four Thousand Six Hundred and Eighty seven only (Rs.
30,579,554,681.87) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2018 and ending 31st December, 2018,
which is specified in annual financial statement - 2018 (amended) is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Two Billion Eight
Hundred and Thirty Five Only (Rs. 32,834,408,112.35) and grant permission to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st of December, 2018 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which occurs first.
