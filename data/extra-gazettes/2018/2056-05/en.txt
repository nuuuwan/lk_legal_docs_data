 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 896/2 of November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1954/45 of February
19, 2016) which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, by the addition
immediately after the item “Facultry of Geomatics” appearing in Column I of the Schedule and its corresponding entries
in Column II of the Schedule of the following item and entries :-
Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology 1. Department of Engineering Technology
2. Department of Biosystems Technology
