THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Two allotments of land  depicted as Lot A and B in Advance
Tracing No. R/EHL/2017/734 of  13/11/2017 prepared by the
Surveyor - General situated in the Village called Divurumpitiya
(F.V .P. 709) in Kuruwita Korale Palle Paththuwa Minor Division in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of  Eheliyagoda in Rathnapura
District of  Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Divurumpitiya watta – Extent: 0.3090 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Rangpura Village claimed by
Land Reform Commission; East: Rangpura Village claimed by Land
Reform Commission and Divurumpitiya  Watta claimed by Pussellawa
Plantation Company; South: Divurumpitiya Watta claimed by
Pussellawa Plantation Company and land allocated for high-tension
electricity cable system claimed by Pussellawa Plantation Company;
West:  Land allocated for high-tension electricity cable system claimed
by Pussellawa Plantation Company and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Divurumpitiya Watta – Extent: 2.5558 Hectare
North and East: Road, Land allocated for high-tension electricity
cable system claimed by Pussellawa Plantation Company and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); South and West:  Road.
