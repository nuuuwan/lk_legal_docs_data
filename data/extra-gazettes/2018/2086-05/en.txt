LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No. : 4/1/1/32/H/147 
Land Ministry No.: 4-3-7/ 2016/HW/180 
 
I do hereby given notice under Sub Section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following lands/servitudes which 
required for a public purpose to information of public. 
 
Description of the land acquired : 
 
Two (02) lots of in extent of 0.4899 hectares depicted 
as Lot No. 267 and 268 in Preliminary plan No. ý 2356 
of 24.07.2018 prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Surveys, Kurunegala District on behalf of the Surveyor 
General Situated in the Village of Ganegedara, in No. 830, 
Madithiyawela Grama Niladari Divisions of  Kurunegala 
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kurunegala District to 
North Western Province and further described as follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 267 
Name of the Land: Pilalandewatta or Hitinawatta; 
Land Use : Mix plantation and Garden, parts of 
Permanent building and a masonary Well; Claimant: Sate; 
Original Claimant: N. J. D. Ganededara, “Siriniwasa”, 
Ganegedara, Boyagane; Extent : 0.2948 Hectare.  
2.  Lot No. 268 
Name of the Land: Pilalandewatta or Hitinawatta; 
Land Use : Mix Plantation and Garden, a Permanent 
building; Claimant: Sate; Original Claimant: N. J. D. 
Ganededara, “Siriniwasa”, Ganegedara, Boyagane; 
Extent : 0.1951 Hectare. 
 
All persons whom interested in the aforesaid lands/
servitudes is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or through  an agent duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kurunegala 
on the day of 10.09.2018. The nature of their claims for 
Compensation for the said lands/servitudes the amount of 
compensation and the details of the compensation of such 
amount. Further the person should appear for an inquiry 
under section 09 of the Act commence from 25.09.2018 
on wards. The date of inquiry and the relevant documents 
submit at the inquiry such as deeds may duly informed by 
me.  
If an agent may present at the inquiry, original 
claimant of the land must be duly authorized in writing. 
Without such a letter, statement or evidence may not be 
recorded from such an agent.  
 
 E. M. M. S. EKANAYAKA,  
  Divisional Secretary,  
 And Land Acquiring Officer, 
 Kurunegala. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kurunegala
