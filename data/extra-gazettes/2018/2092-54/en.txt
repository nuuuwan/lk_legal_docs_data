NOTICE   IN  TERMS  OF  SECTION  7  OF THE
LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT No.  28  OF  1964
(AMENDMENTS)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LAND
ACQUISITION  ACT
(460 CHAPTER)
No. Sri Jpura/la/76/Defence
Reference No. of Land Ministry : 4-3/4/2017/D/140
NOTICE is hereby given in terms of Section 7(1) of sub
Section that the Government has decided to acquire the
following land for a matter that is necessity to the Public.
Description of the land which is decided to acquired:
The Lot No. 01 which is described hereunder according
to the prime Plan No. 9778 situated in the Village of Pitakotte,
within the Municipal limits of Kotte, in the division of Sri
Jayawardenapura Divisional Secretary in Colombo District
Western Province.
The boundaries for the said Lot No. 01 described
hereunder
Name of the Land: Madatiyagahawatta; Description of
the Land: Compound, consists of two permanent buildings
and a two permanent buildings with a three storey building;
Claimed by: Cannot be traced; Extent: 0.1317  Hectare.
All those who claimed, servitude, rights, and/or any other
connections to the above land either by themselves or by
their documents and/or authorized personnel must meet me
on 08.11.2018 at 10.00 a.m. at the Divisional Secretariat in
Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte.
Further the nature of their rights to the said land/lands, the
details regarding the matters of their request for
compensations, the amount of compensation and how they
have calculated the said compensation must be informed by
producing a copy document in duplicate before the
30.10.2018. The necessary Deeds and documents in
confirmation of the Title must be produced at the inquiry.
Divisional Secretary,
Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte.
On 03 day of  October, 2018.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte.
