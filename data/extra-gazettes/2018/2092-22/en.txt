PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor of Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Ariya B. Rekawa,
Governor of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mr. D. M. N. P. Dissanayake, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, to perform the duties of the
Revenue Commissioner of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent post, with effect from the date
25.06.2018.
2) Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to
the Governor of Uva Province, with immediate effect, (from the date 09.07.2018)
3) Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary
to the Uva Provincial Council Public Service Commission, in addition to his duties of permanent post, with
immediate effect (from the date 09.07.2018)
4) Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Director
to the Institute of Uva Management Development, in addition to his duties of permanent post, with immediate
effect (from the date 24.07.2018).
5) Mr. W. M. M. G. Abesinghe Banda, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as
Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel & Training) of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent post,
with immediate effect (from the date 13.08.2018).
6) Mr. Gamini Mahindapala Jopheus, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary
to the Uva Provincial Council Public Service Commission, on full time basis with effect from the date
15.08.2018.
