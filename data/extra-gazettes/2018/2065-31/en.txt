THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
       My No : 47/2017, KDS/KGK/4/2/3/8/-Common.
 Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2016/HW/151.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot Nos. 1 - 3 (Lots 03) allotments in total extent of about
0.0449 Hectare, of  land  in Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6747
and dated 17.01.2017 prepared by the Senior - Superintendent
of Surveys of  Mahanuwara  District  on behalf of  Surveyor
General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of  Mahaiyawa
(239) in the village of Mahaiyawa Division No. 20, Mahaiyawa
(Within the Mahanuwara Municipal Council Limit) in Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Mahanuwara Kadawathsathara  and
Gangawata Korale in Mahanuwara District in the Central
Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of  land:  Assmt. No. 161, Katugasthota Road;
Description: Contains a garden, a part of the permanent
building and a part of the temporary building; Claimant:  State;
Original Claimant: - (Encroached by M. M. Gunathilaka,
No. 5A, Dharmashoka Mawatha, Mahanuwara, a part of the
Lot No. 459 in P . P . No. 7428, (reserved land for Railway) and a
part of lot not numbered); Extent: 0.0313 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land:  Assmt.  No. 159, Katugasthota Road;
Description: Contains a garden, a part of the permanent building;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: - (Encroached by M. M.
Gunathilaka, No. 5A, Dharmashoka Mawatha, Mahanuwara,
a part of the Lot No. 459 in P . P . No. 7428, (reserved land for
Railway) and a part of lot not numbered); Extent: 0.0114
Hectare.
03. Lot No. 3
Name  of  land:  Assmt. No. 161, Katugasthota  Road;
Description: Contains a garden, a part of the permanent
building; Claimant:  State; Original Claimant: - (Encroached by
M. M. Gunathilaka, No. 5A, Dharmashoka Mawatha,
Mahanuwara, a part of  lot not numbered in P . P . No. 7428);
Extent: 0.0022 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at  Divisional  Secretariat  Office of
Mahanuwara  Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale on
the from 27.04.2018 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing in duplicate) on or before the 20.04.2018
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of  their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Mahanuwara Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale.
Mahanuwara Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale,
Divisional Secretary’s Office.
02nd  April, 2018.
