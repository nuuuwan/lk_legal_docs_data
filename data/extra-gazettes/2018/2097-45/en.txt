 LAND  ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)  ACT  NO 28
OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: DIK/LAN/02/47.
I, here by give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition  Act. No. 28 of 1964 (amendment) that
the Government intends to acquire the following Land which
is required for a public purpose.
Details of the land to be obtained :
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary Plan No. 2743
of surveyor General Matara and situated in Village of
Batheegama Grama Niladhari Division of Batheegama West
in Dickwella Divisional Secretariat Matara District.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Wanniarachchikankanamgegedarawatta,
Iththabenewatta, Rathuralagewatta, Ihalaweralugahawatta,
Damaniyagahawatta and Diyawarakagahakoratuwa;
Description: Scrub; Claimed by: W. D. Sujeewa Prasanna,
145/48, Lesly Ranagala Road, Borella, Colombo; Extent: 0.9603
Hectare.
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the  Dickwella Divisional Secretary's
Office on 19th December, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 12th December, 2018 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. All deeds and documents in support of the claims
should be produced before me at the inquiry.
B. S.  RA
NJITHA ,
