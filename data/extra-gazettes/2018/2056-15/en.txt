NOTIFICATION BY MR. SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Tuesday,
06th February, 2018 at 10.30 a.m.
I , Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order No. 14 of the Standing
Orders of Parliament, I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Tuesday the 06th of February, 2018 at 10.30 a.m.
having been requested so to do by the Hon. Prime Minister.
KARU  J AYASURIY A,
Speaker.
Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte,
30th January, 2018.
