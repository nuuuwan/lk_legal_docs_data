FILLING  OF  A   VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  66 A (1) (1a) OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the Office of Member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of failing to make and subscribe
an oath or affirmation in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
And Whereas the  Leader of Independent Group No. 02 to which Ranadheerage  Lakmali Dilrukshi the  Me mber
appointed to Ward No. 08 Thalawathugoda of Maharagama Urban Council  belonged, was required by me under Section 66A
(1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance,  to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 08 Thalawathugoda of Maharagama Urban
Council who belongs to such Independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is Qualified under Section 8 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordiance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a period of
thirty (30) days;
And Whereas the leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate Harshinie
Kemanganee Sepalage to fill such vacancy;
Now Therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A (1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do  hereby declare that
Harshinie Kemanganee Sepalage was elected as the member of the Ward No. 08 Thalawathugoda of Maharag ama Urban
Council.
S. D. S. A. RUPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
12th July , 2018,
Colombo.
