THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I,Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land depicted in the Advanced Tracing
No. R/AYG/2017/318 and dated 15.07.2017 prepared by Surveyor
General, for the village called Udugala (F.V .P. 239), in the Palle Pattu
Minor Division of Kukulu Korale in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Ayagama in Ratnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Bubulehenewatta Sirikandurewatta
(Pinehillwatta)  – Extent: 3.6588 Hectare
North: Lot 71 P, Remaining portion of this land, Estate road and
Lot 71M; East: Remaining portion of this land, Lot 71M and Estate
Road; South: Estate Road; West: Estate Road, Lot No. 71P and
Remaining portion of this land.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/481.
