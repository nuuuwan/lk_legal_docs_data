1A  G  27475 — 12  (04/2018)
PILGRIMAGE ORDINANCE
33rd Higher Ordination Ceremony of Sri Rohana Maha  Sangasabha of  Syamopali
Wanshika Maha Nikaya
IT is hereby informed that the 33 rd Higher Ordination Ceremony of Sri Rohana Sanga  Sabha in Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kamburupitiya, Matara District  will be held on 22nd and 23rd of April 2018 at Rohana Sanga Sabha Headquarters
of Siri Rathanajothi Temple of Urapola and pilgrims should refrain from carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials
and standing orders and regulations under Section 2 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175 - L. D. B. 11/ 60) declarade on
14th October 1991 and published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, will be in force during the
period in which the ceremony is held.
N
ILMINI H ERA TH,
Divisional Secretary,
Kamburupitiya.
At Divisional Secretariat of Kamburupitiya,
10th  April  2018.
