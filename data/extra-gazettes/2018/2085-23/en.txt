RULES made with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance, by Minister of Transport of Virtue of powers vested in him
by Sections 03 and 04A of the Railway Ordinance (Chapter 200)
NIMAL SIRIPALA DE SILV A,

Colombo 10,
24th August 2018.
Rules
The rules for the conveyance of passengers pracels, horses, vehicles and dogs, goods and livestock, traffic and
rates and classification of goods published in the Supplement to Gazette No. 7514 of 26th February 1926 amended from
time to time are further amended with effect from October 1st 2018.
“Under heading Passengers”
Rules 17 - Rates
Includes sub para-
The rates livable from every railway station and or sub railway station as per following zonal table are revised as under
Any starting railway station Rates per Passenger KM
1st Class 2nd Class 3rd Class
Zone 01 from 0 Km - 10 Km 4.20 2.30 1.30
Zone 02 from 11 Km - 50 Km 3.80 2.10 1.20
Zone 03 from 51 Km - 100 Km 3.30 1.80 1.00
Zone 04 from 101 Km - 200 Km 2.50 1.40 0.80
Zone 05 from 201 Km and above 1.90 1.10 0.60
