PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 08.03.2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land, called  Holongolla Estate in total extent of
about 1.6253 hectares depicted as lot A in the Advance Tracing No.
KU/RDG/2015/471 dated 15.11.2015 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the village called Lihiniwehera in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 553 of Lihiniwehera in Ihala Visideke Korale
(East) Minor Division in the Divisinal Secretary's Division of
Rideegama in Kurunegala District of North Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot zzzzzAZZZZZ – Holongolla Estate – Extent: 1.6253 Hectares
North: Kuda Oya and Holongolla Estate claimed by B.H.Fernando
& B.A. Fernando; East: Holongolla Estate claimed by B.H. Fernando
& B.A. Fernando, Holongolla Estate claimed by Kurunegala
Plantation Company and Road; South: Holongolla Estate claimed by
Kurunegala Plantation Company and Road; West: Holongolla Estate
claimed by Kurunegala Plantation Company and Kudu Oya.
