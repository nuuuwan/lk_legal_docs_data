THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY   LA W   No.  41 OF 1978  ENACTED  BY  THE  NATIONAL
STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development
do by this Order to vest with the Urban Development Authority which effect from today, land situated in Bloemendhal
within Municipal Council Limits of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto being the land belonging to
Colombo Municipal Council.
