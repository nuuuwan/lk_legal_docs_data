CONTROL OF PESTICIDES ACT, No. 33 OF 1980 
Order Under Section II 
 
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the Control of Pesticides Act, No. 33 of 1980,  I, Jayakody 
Arachchige Sumith, Registrar of Pesticides, do by this Order, in the interest of the public and on t he advice of the  
Pesticide Technical and Advisory Committee, modify every licence issued in respect of pesticides con taining the active 
ingredient bearing the Chemical Abstract Service Registry number specified in the Schedule I hereto,  in the manner 
specified in the Schedule II hereto. 
  
  Registrar of Pesticides. 
 
Peradeniya. 
  
Schedule I 
 
  
 Chemical Abstract Service Registry Active Ingredient 
 Number  
     
 1071-83-6 Glyphosate 
 
 
Schedule II 
 
Every pesticide containing the active ingredient bearing the Chemical Abstract Service Registry 
number specified in the schedule I hereto shall be devoid of the adjuvant specified in the table 
hereto and shall be used only for tea and rubber plantations. 
 
 Chemical Abstract Service Registry Adjuvant 
 Number  
     
 61791-26-2 polyoxyethylene amine
