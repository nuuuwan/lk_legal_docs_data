THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the said Act,  published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 281/7  of January 24, 1984  (as last amended by Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary ,
No. 2037/ 16 of  September 21, 2017),  establishing the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately
after the entry ‘‘8. Department of  English and Linguistics’’ in Column II of  the Schedule which corresponds to the item
‘‘ Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences’’ in  Column I of that Schedule, of  the following entry :-
‘‘9. Department of  English Language Teaching’’.
Dr. W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
03rd  July  2018.
