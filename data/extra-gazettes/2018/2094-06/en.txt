NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
My Ref. : DHO/LAN/ACQTN/A1/2/57
Land and Parliamentary Reforms Ministry's Reference No. :
4-3/2/2016/VE/267
I hereby give notice under Sub Section (1) of Section (7) of the
Land Acquisition Act 1964 that Government intends to acquire
the following lands which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The following allotments of land from Lot No. 01 to Lot No.
02 depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. K/ 3559 dated
20.06.2018 prepared by the Surveyour General, within the
boundaries shown below situated in the Village of Atalugama in
the Grama Niladhari Division No. 116C Kanangama in the
Western Minor Division in the Atalugam Korale in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Dehiovita in the District of Kegalle in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
01.  Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Not indicated ; Description of the Land: Road;
Claimant: Government; First Claimant: Kelani V alley Plantations
Company, No. 100, Deans Road, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.0513
Hectare.
02.  Lot No. 02
Name of Land: Dewalakanda Estate - Akkaramalaya
Division; Description of the Land: Estate, Rubber
Cultivation aged 15-20 years; Claimant: Government;
First Claimant: Kelani V alley Plantations Company,
No. 100, Deans Road, Colombo 10; Extent: 1.7049
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Dehiovita Divisional
Secretariat on the 27th day of December 2018, at 10.00
a.m.
Further,  you are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before 13 day of  December,
2018, the nature of their interests in  the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation
and details of computation of such amount.
U. G. CHANDANA ANURUDDA P IYADASA,
Divisional Secretary/Land Acquisition Officer,
Dehiowita.
At  the Divisional Secretariat,
Dehiowita,
17th October 2018.
