THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARDS  FOR  THE  HOTEL  AND CATERING TRADE
It is hereby notified under Section 29(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Hotel and Catering Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance and specified in the schedule hereto have  been
approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
The decision shall come into force on 01st of August 2018.
S. A. N. S
ARANA TISSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo 05.
26th July, 2018.
SCHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Hotel and Catering Trade and set out in the Schedule to the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 34 of 27th April, 1979  as varied from time to time and last varied by the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/19, of 18th December, 2012, shall be further varied as follows:
The minimum monthly rates of  wages for time work applicable to the Workers of the below-mentioned classes of the Hotel
and Catering Trade shall be the rates set out in Table I.
