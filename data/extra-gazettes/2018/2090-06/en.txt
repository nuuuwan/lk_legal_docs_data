THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0036 Hectare,
depicted as Lot ‘A’ in the Advanced Tracing No. Ya/JFF/2017/112
and dated 29.09.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the Village called Vannarpannai Southwest, Ward No. 20 within
the Municipal Council Limit of Jaffna in the Grama Niladhari Division
of No. J/81 Fort in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Jaffna in
Jaffna District of Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Marithondikadu, Assmt. No. not available
Karaithivu Veethy – Extent: About 0.0036 Hectare
North: Property claimed by Jaffna Municipal Council; East: Land
claimed by Sinaathurai Pushparaja; South and West: Property claimed
by Jaffna Municipal Council.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/9/2017/WS/111.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”, Land Secretariat,
Rajamalwatta Avenue,
