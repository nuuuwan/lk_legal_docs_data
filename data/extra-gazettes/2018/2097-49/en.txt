SPORTS   ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Order  under  Section  32  and  33
I, Faiszer Musthapha, the Minister of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports in pursuance of powers vested in me
under Section 32(b) and 33 of Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect the registration of the
Sri Lanka Aquatic Sports Union which is the National Association for Aquatic Sports and appoint the following Interim
Committee as a provisional arrangement to continue activities of the said Association.
1. Mr. S. R. W. M. R. P . Sathkumara - (Ch airman)
2. Mr. Ashane Fernando - (Member)
3. Mr. W. Piyal Hewage - (Member)
Moreover, I do hereby appoint Director General of the Department of Sports Development as the Competent Authority with
effect from 16th November 2018, to appoint the Election Committee and make necessary arrangement to conduct the election
in terms of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2089/56 dated 22nd September 2018 published in order to convene and conduct the
election of the Sri Lanka Aquatic Sports Union.
F
AISZER MUSTHAPHA (President’s Counsel),
Minister of Provincial Councils,
Local Government and Sports.
Ministry of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports,
Colombo,
16th November 2018.
