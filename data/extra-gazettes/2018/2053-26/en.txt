EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 02/2018
REVISION  OF  THE  CONDITIONS  SET  OUT  IN   THE   EXCISE   NOTIFICA TION   No. 666
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to time,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do by this Order amend the Schedule in the Excise
Notification No. 666 of the  Extraordinary Gazette  dated 31 December 1979, by-
(1) repealing sub-paragraph (v) of paragraph 5 and substituting thereof with following sub-paragraph,
“ (v) Any persons under the age of eighteen years.”,
(2) removing sub-paragraph (c) of paragraph 11, and
(3) renumbering sub-paragraph (d) as sub-paragraph (c) in paragraph 11.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
