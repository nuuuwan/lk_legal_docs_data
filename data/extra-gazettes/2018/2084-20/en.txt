FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF  THE
VALIKAMAM   EAST   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66 A (1)  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  V alikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen V acant by reason of  resignation:
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the All Ceylon Tamil Congress the recognized  political party to which Shanthirasekaram
Piratheepan  the member belonged, was required by me under section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinan ce, to decide upon
returned member of All Ceylon Tamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of Thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Ilayavan Mayilvakanam to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE Thanabalasingam Akilan, returning Officer of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms
of selection 66 A (1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ilayavan Mayilvakanam
was selected as the member of the V alikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
         T. AKILAN,
                Returning Officer,
              V alikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna.
