No. 2092/63–  FRIDAY, OCTOBER,  12,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 Reference No.: Biya/3/1/3/01/2017. 
 
I,  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of section 7 
of the land acquisition (amendment) act, No. 28 of 1964 
that the government intend to acquire the following lands 
which are  required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land  intend to acquire: 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0354 
hectare depicted Lot No. 01 in the Advance Tracing              
No. Gam/4101 dated 13.08.2018 prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys of Village called Makola in 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Biyagama in Adikari 
Pattuwa of Gampaha Disstrict of Western Province. 
 
 Allotment No. 01 
 Name of the Land: Diualagahawatta; Description: Croft, 
Temporary Building, Water Tank, A well and inter  
cropping; Owners: D. I. Wickramarathne, No. 203/4, 
Makola South, Makola; Extent: 0.0354 Hectare.   
     
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Lands are    
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, for me at Divisional Secretariat of 
Biyagama on and at the following mentioned dates and 
times: 
 
Date  Allotment Number         Time 
 
2018.11.07             01                    10.a.m. 
 
Further claimants should inform me with two copies 
of detailed descriptions of how they have computed the 
compensation and the points of how they are eligible for 
the said compensation, before 31st of October 2018. 
 
  
 S. A. CHANDIMA SOORIYAARACHCHI, 
                                      Divisional  Secretary,  Biyagama. 
  
  
10th of October 2018, 
Sapugaskanda, Makola, 
At Divisional Secretariat, Biyagama.
