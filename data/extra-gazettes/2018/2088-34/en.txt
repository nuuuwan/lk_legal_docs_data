No. 2088/34 – WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of below mentioned land depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. CO/MHR/2016/553 and dated 17.06.2017, prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Rukmale (within
the Maharagama Municipal Council) in the Minor Division of
Pallepattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Maharagama
in Colombo District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Welihenpitalanda Watta – Extent: 0.0199 Hectare
North: Welihenpitalanda Watta claimed by W. A. D. Sunil and
others; East: The land which land name and claimant cannot be
found; South: The land which land name and claimant cannot be
found and Thummodara Kumbura claimant cannot be found; West:
Thummodara Kumbura claimant cannot be found.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
