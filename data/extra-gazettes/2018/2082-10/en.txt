EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that ;
(i) The rate of Excise duty applicable on every article specified in the Columns I, II and III of the Schedule A hereto,
shall replace corresponding every article specified under the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017, issued under the Section 3 of this Act, as specified in the corresponding
entry in Column IV of the Schedule A with effect from August 01, 2018.
(ii)  However, the rate of Excise duty applicable on importation of motor vehicles specified in Columns I, II and III of
the Schedule B hereto, imported based on Letters of Credit (LC) established on or before August 01, 2018 and
cleared from Sri Lanka Customs on or before January 31, 2019, shall be as specified in the corresponding entry
in Column IV of the Schedule A or B, which ever is lower, hereto.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01.
July 31, 2018.
