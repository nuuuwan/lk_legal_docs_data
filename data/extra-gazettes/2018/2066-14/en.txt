THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are need for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of Land containing in extent 0.0986 Hectare
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MR/WLG/2015/597 and  dated
10th February, 2016 prepared by the Surveyor-General and situated
in the Village of Walliwala, in Minor Division of Welilgam Korale in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Weligama in Matara District
in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ekala Kapparatota Road, part of Assessment No.
118 alias Ekala South West Lot – Extent: 0.0986 Hectare
North: Lot No. 7 of P.P. MR 181; East: Lot Nos. 7,3,4 of P.P. MR
181, Alenegodaha Watta to which claimants are not known and Ekala
South West Lot claimed by Chandrasiri Alawatta; South: Ekala South
West Lot claimed by Chandrasiri Alawatta; West: Ekala South West
Lot claimed by Chandrasiri Alawatta, Walliwalawatta to which
claiments are not known and Lot Nos. 9,10 and 7 of P .P. MR 181.
