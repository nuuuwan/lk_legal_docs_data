THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
My  Ref. No:  YAK/LAND/01/03/05.
Land  Ministry  No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/321.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land/servitude* whic is required for a public purpose :
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The allotments of land in total extent of 0.0123 Hectare  depicted
as Lot No. 963, 964, 965, 966 in Sheet No. 22, Additional No. 19 of
F.V .P . No. 645 made by Surveyor General situated in the Village of
Walpola in the Grama Niladhari Division, No. 181, Walpola within
area of authority of Local Government Y akkalamulla in the Divisional
Secretariat Y akkalamulla, Thalpe Paththuwa, North, District of Galle,
Southern Province. The names and extents of the allotments of
land are as follows:-
Lot  No.  963
Name of the Land: Kalukandawatta; Claimant: State; Land Use:
Open Barren Land; Original Claimant: W. K. Karunapala, Ihala
Walpola, Imaduwa; Extent: 0.0013 Hectare.
Lot  No.  964
Name of the Land: Pathiranagewatta; Claimant: State; Land Use:
Home Garden; Original Claimant: M. K. P . Kalyanawansha; Extent:
0.0076 Hectare.
Lot  No.  965
Name of the Land: Pathiranagewatta; Claimant: State;
Land Use: Home Garden; Original Claimant: M. K. P .
Kalyanawansha, "Jayanthi", Ihala Walpala, Imaduwa;
Extent: 0.0014 Hectare.
Lot  No.  966
Name of the Land: Kongasgodawatta, Katigalokanda,
Beraliyamullegoda, Millagahawatta, Depelegodawatta;
Claimant: State; Land Use: Home Garden, A part of a
cement wall; Original Claimant: M. K. Leelawathie,
"Jayanthi", Ihala Walpala, Imaduwa; Extent: 0.0020
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands*
on which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Y akkalamulla Divisional Secretariat,
on the day of  the 27th August, 2018 at 10.00 am and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the 20th day of August,  2018 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for  compensation and the details of the said land/
servitude the amount of  compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
CHANDIMA C. MUHANDIRAMGE,
Divisional Secretary,
