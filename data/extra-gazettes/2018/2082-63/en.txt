The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  KALUTARA   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Kalutara Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Kurukulasooriya
Patabandiarachchiralalage Laxman Henry Silva  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned   member if   People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate
Samarasinghe Hewage Danuj Niroshan Somarathna  to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer  of  the Kalutara Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Samarasinghe
Hewage Danuj Niroshan Somarathna was elected as the member of the Kalutara Urban Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
03rd August  2018.
