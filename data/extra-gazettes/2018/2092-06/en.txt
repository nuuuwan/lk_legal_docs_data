NORTHERN PROVINCE PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952
APPROVED STANDARD BY LAWS FOR LOCAL AUTHORITIES IN NORTHERN PROVINCE
STANDARD Draft By-Laws Chapter VII to IX made by me, C. V . Wigneswaran, as Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance
and Planning, Law and Order, Lands, Electricity, Housing and Construction, Tourism, Local Government, Provincial
Administration, Rural Development, Road Development, Motor Traffic and Transport of Northern Province by virtue of
powers vested in the Minister under whose purview the subject of Local Government of Provincial Council fall under Sub
section 1 of Section 2 of Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952 read with paragraph (a) of Sub section 1 of
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 are published hereby.
1. Standard Draft By-Laws of (Chapter VII to Chapter IX) Municipal Councils, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya Sabhas in
the Northern Province the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2070/11 of 09.05.2018.
It is hereby declared that the above Standard Draft By-Laws for Municipal Councils, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya
Sabhas in the Northern Province have been submitted at the Northern Provincial Council and approved by the Northern
Provincial Council on 11.09.2018.
