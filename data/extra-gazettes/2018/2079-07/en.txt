PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
 UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of  Uva  Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987,  I, Ariya B, Rekawa,
Governor of Uva  Province,  do hereby notify that the following  appointments have been  approved by me.
1. Mrs. D. M. Vineetha Dissanayake, Class II officer of  the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Director
to the Rural Development of Uva Province, in addition to her duties of permanent post, with effect from the
date 02.05.2018.
2. Assistant Commissioner of Ayurvedict Mrs. M. P. S. Karunarathne, to perform the Duties of the Provincial
Commissioner of  Ayurvedict of Uva Province, in addition to her duties of permanent post, from the date
07.06.2018 to 22,06.2018, till the return to the Island of Mr. P. W. W. Dissanayake, who is the Provincial
Commissioner of  Ayurvedict of Uva Province.
3. Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Special  Grade officer of  the Sri Lanka Administrative Service to act as
Secretary to the Governor of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent post, with effect from the
date 10.06.2018.
4. Mr. Dayananda Rathnayake, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Provincial Commissioner
of Land of the Department of  Land of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 04.06.2018).
A
RIYA B. REKAWA,
Governor of Uva Province.
Governor’s Office,
Uva Province,
04th July 2018.
