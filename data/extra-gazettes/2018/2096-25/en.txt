THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that whereas the subjects and functions and departments, state  corporations and statutory institutions
to be assigned to the Ministers appointed under Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka have been determined;
The subjects, functions, departments, state corporations and statutory institutions under the charge of the said
Ministers shall be as set out in the Schedule given below with effect from the date of this Notification.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 07, 2018.
