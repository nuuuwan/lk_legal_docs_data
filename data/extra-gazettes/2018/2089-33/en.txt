THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired :
Allotment of land called in extent 0.6395 of Hectare, 3.2362
of Hectare shown as Lot 1 in Tri. 535 dated 07.07.2018 prepared
by the Surveyor General. Situated in with in the Pradeshiya
Sabah limits of Trincomalee No. 243F Thirukadaloor Grama
Niladhari Division, Divisional Secretariat Division of Town
and Gravets, Trincomalee District and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 320 –  Extent of the Land: 0.6395 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North by: Lots 26 and 25; East by: Lots 25, 26, 25, 322 and
321; South by: Lot 321 and North Coast Road; West by: North
Coast Road and Lots 26 and 25.
1A - G 28559 - 37 (2018/09)
Lot No. 323 –  Extent of the Land: 3.2362 Hectares.
Boundaries:
North by: Lots 321, 322, 31 and 33; East by: Lots Lots 31,
33, 328, 327, 326 and 324; South by: Lots 328, 327, 326, 324
and North Coast Road; West by: North Coast Road and Lots
321 and 322.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Divisional secretariat Trincomalee on the 22nd day of
October, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and you are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day
of October, 2018 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for comensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
J. S. ARULRAJ,
