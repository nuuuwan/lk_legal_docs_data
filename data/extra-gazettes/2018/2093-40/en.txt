THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Following allotment of land in extent 1.3281 hectare depicted as
Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. MR/THG/2018/127 dated
23.04.2018 made by Surveyor General and situated in the village of
Komangoda in Minor Division of Gangabada Pattu in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Thihagoda, Matara District, Southern
Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thiraniyawatta – Extent: 1.3281 Hectare
North: Thiraniyawatta claimed by H. N. Manie, Thiraniyawatta
claimed by Mapalane Wimalawansa Thero, Kumburammulla claimed
by H. P . Eramanis, Road and Gorakavitiyalahena claimed by L. P.
Nilanthi; East: Kumburammulla claimed by H. P. Eramanis,
Gorakavitiyalahena claimed by L. P . Nilanthi and Thiraniyawatta
claimed by Mapalane Wimalawansa Thero; South: Thiraniyawatta
claimed by Mapalane Wimalawansa Thero, Canal and Road; West:
Canal, Road, Thiraniyawatta claimed by H. N. Manie and
Thiraniyawatta claimed by Mapalane Wimalawansa Thero.
