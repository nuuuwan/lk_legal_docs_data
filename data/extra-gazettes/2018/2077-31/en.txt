THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Five (5) allotments of land depicted  and  Advanced Tracing No.
GA/GFG/2017/778 dated 26.12.2017, parepared  by the Surveyor -

District of Southern Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot ‘A’ - Could not be traced – Extent: 0.0054  Hectare

claimed by T. B. Sepali Dammika Danthanarayana.

Thalgahawatta  alias Lansiyawatta claimed by K. A. Priyankara;
West:  Duwewatta, Thalgahawatta  alias Lansiyawatta claimed by

03. Lot ‘C’ - Aluth Aswedduma – Extent: 0.0008  Hectare

Moda Ela.
