LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
             My Ref. No.: DSAN/LND/08/LA/03.
             Land Min's Ref .No.: 3 J/81/E/53.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7,
of the Land  Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment),
that the Government intends to acquire the following land
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Siyambalagahawatta and
Koswattahena in total extent of 0.9890 hectare depicted as
lots 65, 66, 67, 68 and 69 in Sheet No. 04, supplement No.
03 of the Final Village Plan No. 254 situated in the village
called Indigetawela in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Indigetawela in Giruwapattuwa East in the Divisional
Secretariat's Divisional of Angunakolapelessa in
Hambantota District of the Southern Province and more
fully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 65
Name of the Land : Siyambalagahawatta; Description :
Paddy field; Claimant : Chief Incumbent, Kotawaya Purana
Maha Viharaya, Hakuruwela; Extent : 0.1090 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 66
Name of the Land : Siyambalagahawatta; Description : Home
garden, a mix cultivation available; Claimant : Chief Incumbent,
Kotawaya Purana Maha Viharaya, Hakuruwela; Extent : 0.0160
Hectare.
03. Lot No. 67
Name of the Land : Siyambalagahawatta; Description : Paddy
field; Claimant : Chief Incumbent, Kotawaya Purana Maha
Viharaya, Hakuruwela; Extent : 0.0048 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 68
Name of the Land : Siyambalagahawatta; Description : Home
garden, Part of a permanent building and a mix cultivation
available. The building has been constructed by the Education
Department; Claimant : Chief Incumbent, Kotawaya Purana
Maha Viharaya, Hakuruwela; Extent : 0.0299 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 69
Name of the Land : Koswattahena; Description : Home
garden, Parts of two permanent building, two toilets and a mix
cultivation available. The building has been constructed by
the Education Department; Claimant : Gamini Jayasinghe,
Thalamporuwa, Hakuruwela; Extent : 0.8293 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Angunakolapelessa Divisional
