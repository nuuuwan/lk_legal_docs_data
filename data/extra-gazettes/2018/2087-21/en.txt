2018
SECOND SUPPLEMENTARY FINANCIAL STATEMENT
Authorization
I, do hereby grant Permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
disburse as per the second supplementary financial statement for the year 2018, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27 (a) of the provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial  Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended  on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Twenty Nine Billion Six Hundred and
Seventy Million Nine Hundred and Forty Thousand Only (Rs. 29,670,940,000.00) for the service of the period beginning on
01st of January, 2018 and ending 31st December, 2018, which is specified in annual financial statement - 2018 is hereby amended
as, Rupees Thirty Billion Five Hundred and Forty Two Million Two Thousand only (Rs. 30,542,002,000.00) and grant permission
to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st of  December, 2018 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of summoning
the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
which occurs first.
N
ILUKA EKANAYAKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
24th day of July, 2018.
