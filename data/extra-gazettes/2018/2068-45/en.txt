PRADESHIYA    SABHAS  ACT,  No. 15 OF 1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, Faiszer Musthapha,
Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government do by this Order, repeal the paragraph 2 of the Order related to
Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2043/56 of 02nd November 2017 and all the
amendments subsequently made thereto, by virtue of the powers vested in me under sub section (1) of Section 2, Section
4 and Section 6 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with the Section 18 of interpretation Ordinance.
F
AISZER M USTHAPHA ,
Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government.
Colombo,
25th April 2018.
