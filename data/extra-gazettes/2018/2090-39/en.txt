FINANCE  ACT,   No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with  Section71  of the   Finance  Act, No. 11 of 1963,  I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order, vest in the People's Bank established under the
People's Bank Act, No. 29 of  1961, with effect from, 28 th September, two Thousand and eighteen, the property described in
the Schedule hereto.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Colombo 01,
28th September, 2018.
SCHEDULE
All that divided and defined allotment of  land marked as Lot 17 called as '' Kekunagahalanda'' depicted in plan No.
8996 dated 15.08.1990 made by licensed surveyor G .. L. B. Nanayakkara, situated in Grama Niladhari Division of
Balummahara bearing No. 239 in Divisional Secretary's Division of  Gampaha in Meda Pattu in Siyane Korale in the
District of  Gampaha in Western Province and bounded on the North by Lot 18, on  the East by Lot 15 and 16, on the South
by Lot 48, on the West by Lot 07 containing in extent of  sixteen perches decimal seven naught (A:00 R:00 P : 16.70)
together with the house bearing No. 278/ 17, soil, trees and everything standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio E 355/ 212 in the Land Registry, Gampaha.
