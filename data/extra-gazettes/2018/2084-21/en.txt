Filling of a Vacancy in the Office of  Member of the
Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha
under Section 66 A (1) of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen V acant by reason of  resignation:
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the United National  Party the recognized political party to which Hewa  Ulawaduge
Susantha the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned
member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party had communicated his decision to nominate
Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Jayaweera Rathnayake to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE  R. Sasilan, returning Officer of the Uhana  Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of section 66 A (1) of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Jayaweera Rathnayake was
selected as the member of the Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha.
                     R. SASILAN
                           Returning Officer,
                            Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha.
13th August, 2018
District Elections Office,
Ampara.
