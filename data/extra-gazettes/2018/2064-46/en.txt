FINANCE  BUSINESS  ACT,  No.  42  OF  2011
CANCELLATION of a licence to carry on finance business under Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011.
DR. I NDRAJIT C OOMARASWAMY,
Chairman of the Monetary Board
and the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
The public is hereby informed that in terms of Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, the
Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka, has decided to cancel the licence issued to Central Investments and
Finance PLC under the said Finance Business Act with effect from 5
th March 2018. Accordingly, Central Investements
and Finance PLC is not permitted to carry on finance business with effect from such date.
