FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  AMBALANGODA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Ambalangoda  Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front the recognized political party to which Kukulege
Padmasiri Dayananda Silva  the  Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon returned member if People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party  and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the  Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Magage Champa Sumudu Kumari  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, returning officer of  the Ambalangoda Urban Council acting in terms
of Selection 66A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Magage Champa
Sumudu Kumari  was selected as the member of the Ambalangoda Urban Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda  Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
