ELECTIONS   COMMISSION
Notice of the Election Commission
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS - 10TH   FEBRUARY   2018
IT is hereby informed that the results issued by Returning Officers of each Local Authority giving the number of votes
received by each recognized political party and independent group contested for 340 Local Authorities, of which the
election was held on the 10th February 2018, the number of members from each recognized political party/independent
group elected at ward level and the total number of members to be returned by each recognized political party/independent
group, are being exhibited at relevant District Election Offices, local authorities and in the website of the Elections
Commission.
Further, it is informed that these results have also been issued to each political party and independent group.
