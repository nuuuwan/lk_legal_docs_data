TOURISM   ACT,  No. 38  OF 2005
RULES made by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority under Section 62 of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005 read
with Sub- section (8) of Section 48 of  the aforesaid Act and approved by the Minister of  Tourism Development and
Christian Religious Affairs.
Director - General,
Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
Colombo,
02nd  May, 2018.
Rules
1. These rules may be cited as the Tourism (Licensing Tourist Enterprises and Tourist Services) Rules,
No. .......of 2018.
2. Every application for a  license to carry on a tourist enterprise or  a tourist service shall be made to the
Director- General along with a detailed report setting out the capacity and the ability of  the tourist enterprise or tourism
Service. The Director - General may, where necessary require an applicant to furnish further information.
3. Upon the receipt of an application under rule 2, the Director - General shall authorize an inspector nominated
for that purpose, to carry out an inspection of  the tourist enterprise or tourist Service to which the application relates.
The Director - General shall when appointing a  person as an inspector, have regard to  the fact as to whether such person
possesses sufficient qualification or experience in the relevant field.
4. The inspector shall, upon completion of his inspection submit a report to the Director - General, setting out all the
relevant details on the inspection carried out by him of  the specified tourist enterprise or tourist service.
