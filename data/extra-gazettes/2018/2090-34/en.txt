NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1974
Order Under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of The National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No.2 of 1974,
I, Rauff Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning and Water Supply, do hereby approve the acquisition  of the immovable
property, described in the following schedule, as this additional land is required by the National Water Supply and Drainage
Board laying Pipe Lines to carry untreated water under the Kundasale - Haragama Water Supply Project.
