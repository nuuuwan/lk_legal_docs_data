No. 2078/43 – WEDNESDAY, JULY 04, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted as Lots ‘A ’ to ‘H’ in the Advance
Tracing No. KE/GLG/2014/169 and dated 29.08.2017 prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Atala in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. 65B, Boyagoda in the Minor Division of
Kandupita Pattuwa South in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Galigamuwa in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dikkhena – Extent: 1.2788 Hectares
North: Dikkhenawatta, Jambugahamula Hena Watta and Galauda
Hena Watta; East: Road holding Lot C; South: Road holding Lot C
and Dikkhenawatta; West: Dikkhenawatta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Dikkhena – Extent: 0.5219 Hectare
North and East: Road holding Lot C; South: Dikkhenawatta; West:
Dikkhenawatta and Road holding Lot C.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Extent: 0.3917 Hectare
North: Lot A and Galauda Hena Watta; East: Road holding Lots E,
F, Lot G and Dikkhenawatta; South: Lot D, Dikkhenawatta and
Lot B; West: Dikkhenawatta and Lot A.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Dikkhena – Extent: 0.9988 Hectare
North and East: Road holding Lot C; South: Road holding Lot C
and Dikkhenawatta; West: Dikkhenawatta  and Road holding Lot C.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Dikkhenawatta – Extent: 0.4691 Hectare
North: Galauda Hena Watta and Dikkhenawatta; East: Road holding
Lot F; South: Road holding Lots C and F; West: Road holding Lot C
and Galauda Hena Watta.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Extent: 0.1014 Hectare
North: Lot E and Dikkhenawatta; East: Dikkhenawatta and
Lot G; South: Lot G; West: Road holding Lot C, Lot E and
Dikkhenawatta.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Dikkhena – Extent: 1.1479 Hectare
North: Road holding Lot F and Dikkhenawatta; East:
Dikkhenawatta and Canal; South: Canal and Dikkhenawatta; West:
Road holding Lots C and F.
08. Lot ‘H’ – Dikkhena – Extent: 0.7624 Hectare
North: Canal and Dikkhenawatta; East and South: Dikkhenawatta;
West: Dikkhenawatta and Canal.
