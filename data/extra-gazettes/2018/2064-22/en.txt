Appointments & C., by the Governors
 UVA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointment made by the Governor of Uva Province
BY Virtue of powers vested to me under section 154F. (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, M.P . Jayasinghe, Governor of Uva Province, do hereby announce that I have made the following
appointment for the Provincial Council of Uva Province, with effect from the date 26th March, 2018.
Hon. Don Arthur Chamara Sampath Dassanayake  - As Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and  Peace,
Chief Minister of  Uva Province Education (Except Tamil Schools), Local Government, Power
and Energy, Construction and Rural and Infrastructure
Development and Land of Uva Province.
                                                                                                                                                                               M.P. JAYASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province.
At office of the Uva Governor,
Badulla.
26th  March, 2018.
