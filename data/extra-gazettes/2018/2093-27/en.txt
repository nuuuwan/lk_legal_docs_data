The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE
EHELIYAGODA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  in  Eheliyagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  Termination of Party
Membership ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Socialist Party of Sri Lanka, the recognized political party to which
Kelaniyage Ratnapala   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,   to
decide  upon returned   member if   Socialist Party of Sri Lanka  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Wathawana Vithana Wasam Pemachandra  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Suranga  Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the  Eheliyagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Wathawana Vithana Wasam Pemachandra was Selected as the member of the  Eheliyagoda  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
16th  October  2018.
