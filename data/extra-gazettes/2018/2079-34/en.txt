FORTS  AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT   LEVY  ACT, No. 18 OF  2011
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy, No. 18
of 2011, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do hereby this Order, amend the Schedule III of
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2049/16 of 13.12.2017 as amended by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2065/42 of 06.04.2018, by the addition at the end of such Schedule III of the articles specified in Column III corresponding
to the HS headings and HS codes specified in the Column I and II of the Schedule hereto, on the recommendation of the
Secrtary, Ministry of Industry and Commerce.
