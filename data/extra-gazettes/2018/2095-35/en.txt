THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Revocation Under Section 39 (1)
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, in terms of the provisions of Section 18 of the Interpretation
Ordinance (Chapter 12) as amended by the Interpretation
(Amendment) Law No. 18 of 1972 and 29 of 1974, do hereby cancel
the Revocation Order under Section 39(1) of the Land Acquisition
Act published in Page 3A of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2084/8
dated 14.08.2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
