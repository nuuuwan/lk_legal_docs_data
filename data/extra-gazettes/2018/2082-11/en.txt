EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance & Mass Media, do by this Order declare that the rate of  Excise duty
applicable on every article specified in the Columns I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, shall replace correspoding every
article specified under the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette  Notification No.2044/32 dated November 09, 2017,
issued under the Section 3 of this  Act, Shall be  as specified in the corresponding entry in Column  IV of  the Schedule
with effect from August 01, 2018.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01.
31st  July  2018.
