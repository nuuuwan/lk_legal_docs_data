FINANCE  ACT  No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order, vest in the People's Bank established under the
People's Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 06th July Two Thousand and Eighteen, the properties described in the
Schedule hereto.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Colombo,
July 06, 2018.
SCHEDULE
All that divided one sixths share (1/6), one sixteenths share (1/16), and one eights share (1/8) from and out of the
land called, “Panvile Kumbura” depicted in the Title Plan No. 414393 situated in Gramasewa Division of Ambana, No. 94,
Divisional Secretary's Division of Elpitiya in Benthara Walallavita Korale in the District of Galle in the Southern Province
and bounded on the North by : foot path, on the East by : the road, lands depicted in the Title Plans No. 331300, 327968
and 390496 and Lot No. 122 1/2, on the South and West by : Beligasyaya Canal containing in extent of one acre, two roods
and sixteen perches (01 A., 02 R., 16 P.) together with everything standing thereon.
Lot No. 121
D2 depicted in the Plan 2919 B and dated 27.08.2007 made by the licensed surveyor M. Ariyasena for
the aforesaid undivided land contains in extent of 90.66 perches.
This land is registered in folio B 539/169 in the Land Registry, Balapitiya.
