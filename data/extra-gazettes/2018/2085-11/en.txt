Regulations
The United Nations (Sanctions in relation to Iran) Regulations No. 1 of 2018 published in the Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 2080/34 of July 17, 2018 are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) in regulation 3 thereof -
(a) by the repeal of paragraph (a) thereof and the substitution therefor of the following paragraph :-
“ (a) every person in Sri Lanka and every citizen of Sri Lanka outside Sri Lanka ; ” ;
(b) by the insertion, immediately after paragraph (a) thereof, of the following new paragraph :-
“ (aa) a body corporate incorporated under a law of Sri Lanka wherever located ; ” ; and
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk
