STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
"Whether the claim made by Mr. H. M. Ariyapala who works as a Grade V officer of the Mahiyangana Depot of the Sri Lanka
Transport Board requesting the salary and other allowances deprived from 01.06.2013 to the date the desciplinery order was
implemented, the period during which he had been interdicted without pay is just and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled, and
Whether the claim made by him that he had been caused injustice by the transfer to the Ambalantota Depot from 25.02.2014 and
interdiction without pay from 01.08.2014 to 31.01.2015 in accordance with the disciplinery/ appeal order is just and if so, to what
reliefs he is entitled".
