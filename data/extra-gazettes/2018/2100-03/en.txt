EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs, do by this Order declare that, the rate of Excise duty
applicable on every article specified in the Columns I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, shall replace corresponding every
article specified under the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017
and Schedule of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2097/3 dated November 12,2018, issued under Section 3
of this Act, shall  be as specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule, with effect from December
04, 2018.
The order made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2097/3 dated November 12,  2018 is hereby rescinded with effect from December 04, 2018.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
03rd December, 2018.
