THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
WHEREAS, I have, in terms of paragraphs (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, determined the ministry and the subjects and functions assigned to the State Minister of National Unity & Co-
existence appointed by me as a Minister who is not a member of the Cabinet of Ministers in the exercise of powers vested
in me under paragraphs (1) of Article 44 of the Constitution, it is hereby notified that the subjects and functions of the
aforesaid Ministry and the Departments, State Corporations and Statutory Institutions pertaining to the said Ministry shall
be as set out in the schedule hereto from May 11, 2018.
 M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
 President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo - 01,
May 28, 2018.
