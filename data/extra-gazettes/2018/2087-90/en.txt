I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act No.28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired :
Two allotments of land called Navinnapitiye hena in total
Extent about 0.0820 Hectares depicted as Lot Nos. 1 and 2 in
PP Maha 6679 Surveyed  and prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the village of  Navinnapitiya in Waldeniya
Grama Niladhari Division No. 504 in Galasiyapattuwa Minor
Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Pujapitiya
in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows,.
01. Lot No. 1 – Name of the Land: Navinnapitiye Hena;
Extent of the Land: 0.0161 Hectare.
