THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL  ACT,  No. 47  OF  1980
Order under Section 24C and Section 24D
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 24C and Section 24D of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980,
read with Section 51 of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Order, declare-
(a) that the area of land, the limits of which are described in Schedule hereto shall be an Environmental
Protection Area for the purposes of the aforesaid Act, and shall be called the “Pansalathenne-Maussawa
Environmental Protection Area” ; and
(b) that for so long as this Order is in force :-
(i) any planning scheme or project  within the aforesaid Pansalathenne-Maussawa Environmental
Proection Area which is in conflict with the provisions of the aforesaid Act, shall cease to operate
from the date of the making hereof ;
(ii) no person other than the Authority shall exercise any power or discharge any function within the
limits of the aforesaid Pansalathenne-Maussawa Environmental Protection Area ; and
(iii) the use of any land area within the aforesaid Pansalathenne-Maussawa Environmental Protection
Area for any activity, shall be totally prohibited.
(c) a Monitoring Committee consisting of the members set out below shall monitor the Pansalathenne-
Maussawa Environmental Protection Area and make recommendations to the Authority on conservation
and sustainable management thereof :-

SCHEDULE
Limits of the total land area comprising the Pansalathenne-Maussawa Environmental Protection Area containing in extent
103.4614 ha depicted in survey plan No. ud 1620 of Sri Lanka Survey Department dated 17.06.2014 which is situated
within Dombagasdeniya-E345A and Halangoda-E347A Grama Niladhari Divisons of the Divisional Secretary's Division
