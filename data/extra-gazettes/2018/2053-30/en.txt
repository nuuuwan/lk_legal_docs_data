THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No.  41  OF  1978  ENACTED  BY  THE  NATIONAL
STATE   ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Magapolis and Western Development
do by this Order vest with the Urban Development Authority which effect from today where the land is situated in Ward
No. 03 Welikada within the Municipal Council Limits of Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte described morefully in the Schedule
hereto being the land belonging to Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA M. P.,
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development,
17th & 18th Floors,
“Suhurupaya”
Battaramulla.
05th January, 2018.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land depicted as Lot 01 in PP Co. 8943 dated 27.06.2008 prepared by Surveyor General in extent of
0.3616 Ha. (0A., 3R., 22.96P.) in the village of Welikada in Ward No. 03 of Welikada within the Municipal Council limits
of Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte in Divisional Secretariat Division of Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte in Colombo District, Western
Province.
The boundaries of said allotment is as follows :
North - The claimant not known land namely Kadurugastuduwa and Lot No. 02 in PP Co. 8337 ;
East - Lot No. 01 in PP Co. 7801 ;
South - Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha and Cement Drain ;
West - Cement Drain and the claiment not known land namely Kadurugastuduwa.
