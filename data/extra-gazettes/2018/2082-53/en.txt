THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 05.04.2016 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 1961/29 dated 07.04.2016 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the
statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 10.02.2016.
Inter Company Employees Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Talangama.
(On behalf of Mr. Chaminda Stouter)
AND
Avery Dennison Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd., Block B, Export Processing Zone, Biyagama.
was referred to Mr. S. M. S. Jayawardena for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and
Trade Union Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said
Order dated 05.04.2016.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
24th July, 2018.

THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between–
Inter Company Employees Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Talangama.
(On behalf of Mr. Chaminda Stouter)
AND
Avery Dennison Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd., Block B, Export Processing Zone, Biyagama.
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers
vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised
Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 62 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special
Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. R. S. Ariyapperuma, No. 03, Araliya Mawatha, Sirimal Uyana, Ratmalana to
be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
24th July, 2018.
My No.: IR/21/05/2013.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON

Inter Company Employees Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Talangama.
(On behalf of Mr. Chaminda Stouter)
AND
Avery Dennison Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd., Block B, Export Processing Zone, Biyagama.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether  the claim made by the Inter Company Employees Union that Chaminda Stouter has been caused injustice by Avery
Dennison Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd. Block B, Export Processing Zone, Biyagama at the performance Evaluation of employees in year
2011 is justified, and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo this 16th day of July, 2018.

Edition), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act,

My No.: IR/COM/03/2016/143.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mrs. Seetha Wijesooriya, ‘Shanthi’, Thalawitiya, Parakaduwa. of the one part
AND
National Gem and Jewellery Authority, No. 25, Galle Face Terrace, Colombo 03.of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  Mrs. Seetha Wijesooriya who had worked at the State Gem Corporation and consented on voluntary retirement
under the General Compensation Scheme before reaching the due age being considered an extra employee when the corporation
was made an Authority in 1994, had been caused injustice by the Bulumulla Compensation Scheme being used in the calculation
of compensation and if such injustice had been caused, to what reliefs she is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo this 16th day of July, 2018.
