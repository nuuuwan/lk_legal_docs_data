Notice  under Section 7

I, hereby inform that under Sub section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
Act, No. 28 of 1964, the government decided to acquire the
land described below for a public purpose.
Required public purpose :
Acquisition of the land for the purpose of constructing Official
Quarters for the Kesbewa Divisional Secretary.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot No. 160 of sheet No. 1 in Block No. 10 out of 18 of
Cadastral Map No. 521202 situated  in the village of
Thumbowila at Thumbowila - West in Grama Niladari Division
No. 576 of Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat in the Colombo
District, Western Province.
All of them who claim title, owership, or interest to the said
land should come and meet me at the Kesbewa Divisional
Secretariat on 08th June 2018 at 9.30 a.m. either by themselves
or their representatives authorized by the documents.
Futher I should be informed in writing (in duplicates) before
6th June 2018 indicating the nature of the owership, reasons
for claiming compensation in respect of the land, the amount
of compensation and method of calculating the said amount.
Y. K. S. JEEWAMALA,
Divisional Secretary and Land Acquisition Officer,
Kesbewa.
Divisional Secretariat
Kesbewa.
