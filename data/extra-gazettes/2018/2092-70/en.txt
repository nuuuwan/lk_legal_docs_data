1A – G28729– 16 (2018/10) PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotments of  land in total extent of  about 2.0234 Hectares
depicted as Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. MU/MLL/2015/288
of 24.10.2015 Prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the
Village of Alampil in the Grama Niladhari  Division, No. Mul/87 of
Alampil South Grama Niladhari Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of  Maritimepattu, Mullaittivu District of Northern Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – OA Thoddam – Extent: 2.0234 Hectares
North: Road (RDD) and Road (RDA); East: Road (RDA) and OA
Thoddam Alampil Roman Catholic Vidyalayam; South: OA Thoddam
Alampil Roman Catholic Vidyalayam and OA Thoddam Puvaneswary
Sivanathan; West: OA Thoddam Puvaneswary Sivanathan and Road
(RDD).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2014/D/322.
