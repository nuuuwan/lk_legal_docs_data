SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 6  months commencing from November 24,  2018, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2087/76 of 07.09.2018 in respect of Peas (Watana) classifiable under the HS
Codes 0713.10.10 and 0713.10.20 is rescinded with effect from November 24, 2018.
MAHINDA  R AJAPAKSA ,
Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
23rd  November, 2018.
