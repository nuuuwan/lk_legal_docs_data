SPORTS  ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Orders under Sections  32  and  33
I, Faiszer Musthapha, the Minister of Sports by virtue of the powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the
Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect, the registration of the Sri Lanka Archery Association,
which is the National Association for Archery and as an interim arragement, appoint Mr. Herath Mudiyanselage Buwaneka
Padmakumara Herath, Director General (Acting) of the Department of Sports Development as competent authority with
effect from 26.04.2018 to continue its functions and further order that the election be convened and held to elect new
office - bearers to Sri Lanka Archery Association for 2018/2020.
F
AISZER MUSTHAPHA (M. P.),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
25th April 2018.
