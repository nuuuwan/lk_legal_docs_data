No. 2060/20 – WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 28, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Mu/
MLL/2017/113 and dated 27.11.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village called Kumarapuram in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Maritimepattu in Mullaitive District,
Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Puliyadiyil Kadu – Extent: About 0.5371 Hectare
North: Road (RDA) and Puliyadiyil Kadu claimed by
Thamotharampillai Nadarasapillai and others; East: Puliyadiyil Kadu
claimed by Thamotharampillai Nadarasapillai and others and
TP 226583; South: TP 226583 and Lot B; West: Lot B and
Road (RDA).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Puliyadiyil Kadu – Extent: About 1.9469 Hectares
North: Road (RDA) and Lot A; East: Lot A and TP 226583;
South: TP 226583 and Lot C; West: Lot C and Road (RDA).
03. Lot ‘C’ – Puliyadiyil Kadu – Extent: About 2.5645 Hectares
North: Road (RDA) and Lot B; East: Lot B and TP 226583;
South: TP 226583 and Lot D; West: Lot D and Road (RDA).
04. Lot ‘D’ – Puliyadiyil Kadu – Extent: About 2.4453 Hectares
North: Road (RDA) and Lot C; East: Lot C and TP 226583;
South: TP 226583 and Puliyadiyil Kadu claimed by Kumaraiyaha
Vijayakumary; West: Puliyadiyil Kadu claimed by Kumaraiyaha
Vijayakumary and Road (RDA).
