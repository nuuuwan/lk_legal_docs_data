KNOW YE, that by virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph (3)(i) of Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,  I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Proclamation summon Parliament to meet
at the Chamber of Parliament, Sri Jayawardenepura, on the Fourteenth day of November, Two Thousand and Eighteen at
10.00 a.m.
Given at Colombo, on this 04th day of November, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
 By Order of His Excellency,
U
DAYA R. S ENEVIRA TNE,
Secretary to the President .
