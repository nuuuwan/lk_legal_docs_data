EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 Reference No.: Ma/LA/4/1564. 
 
I,  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act,  No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude* which is 
required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land  for servitude to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of  land in total extent of 0.0519 hectare, as 
lot No. 1037 depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. 69 and 
dated 31st August 2018, prepared by the surveyor  
-General situated in the village of Welipenna in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Matugama in the District of 
Kalutara-in Western Province morefully described as 
follows. 
 
 01. Lot No. 1037 
 Name of the Land: Pimburawatta, Dippitawatta; 
Description: Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 
Arrumapperuma Arachchige mala, No. 399/b, Samagi 
Mawatha, Panagoda, Homagama; Extent: 0.0519 Hectare.   
     
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Land/Lands*  
on which a servitude is to be acquired are  hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Matugama  Divisional 
Secretariat on  the 14th day of November at 10.00 a. m. 
Clock and are  hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before 01st day of November the   
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude* the amount of compensation and the 
details of  the computation of such amount. 
 
  
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Matugama Division 
  
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Matugama, 
21st September 2018.
