THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW,  No.  41  OF  1978
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978, I, Patale
Champika Ranavaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development being of the opinion that the area described in
Column I of the Second Schedule appended hereto and specified in the corresponding metes and bounds entry in Column
II of the same Schedule, excluding the area described as already declared Urban Development Areas in the First Schedule
appended hereto do by this Order declare such area to be Urban Development Area.
P
A TALEE C HAMPIKA R ANA V AKA (M. P.),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,
17th and 18th Floor,
“Suhurupaya”, Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
30th July, 2018.
FIRST  SCHEDULE
The areas already declared as Urban Development Areas under the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 by
the following Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
* The areas under the jurisdiction of Town Council of Polonnaruwa declared by the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 38/16 of Friday, 01st June 1979.
* The Urban Development Area of Thammankaduwa by Gazette No. 1397/1 of Monday 13th June, 2005.
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk
