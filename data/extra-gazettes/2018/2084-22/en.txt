FILLING   OF  A   VACANCY   IN   THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF  THE
POTTUVIL   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66 A (1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen V acant by reason of  resignation:
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party the recognized political party to which Kirishnapillai
Kirishnaveni the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A (1) of the aforesaid ordinanc e, to decide upon
returned member of Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Muhammathismail Kairunnisa to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE   R. Sasilan, returning Officer of the Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of section 66A (1) of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Muhammathismail Kairunnisa was selected as
the member of the Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha.
                    R. SASILAN,
                     Returning Officer,
                        Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Amapara,
13th August, 2018.
