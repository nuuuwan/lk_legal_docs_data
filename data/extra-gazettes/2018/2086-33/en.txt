No. 2086/33 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 30, 2018
Order by virtue of the Power vested in me under the Provisions of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)
Act, No. 12 of 1989, read with the Provsions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 and as amended under the
Provisions of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983.
PIYASIRI RAMANAYAKA, Provincial Minister of  Co–operative Development & Trade, Land,Electricity & Energy, Sports
& Yourth Affairs, Cultural & Arts Affairs and  Information Technology of North Western Provincial Council, do hereby
delegate the power vested with the Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western
Provincial Council, under the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended under the Provisions
of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 & Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the rules framed there under,
thereafter, as per column III  and from the date as per column II to the persons named as per column I, to assist the
Commissioner and the Registrar  of Co-operative Development of the North Western Province.
P
IY ASIRI RAMANAYAKE,
Provincial Minister of Co – operative Development & Trade,
Land, Electricity & Energy, Sports & Yourth Affairs, Cultural & Arts
Affairs and  Information Technology of North Western Province
Provincial Council.
