NOTIFICATION BY MR. SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Wednesday, 10th January,
2018 at 10.30 a.m.
I , Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order No. 14 of the Standing
Orders of Parliament, I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Wednesday the 10th of January, 2018 at 10.30 a.m.
having been requested so to do by the Hon. Prime Minister.
KARU  J AYASURIY A,
Speaker.
Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte,
08th January, 2018.
