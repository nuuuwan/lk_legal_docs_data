SERVICE  MINUTE  OF  THE  PUBLIC  MANAGEMENT  ASSISTANTS’  SERVICE   - REVISION  No.  02
IT has been ordered by the Public Service Commission to revise the Minute of Public Management Assistants’ Service
revised by the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2032/26 dated 16.08.2017
and published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1840/34 dated 11th
December 2013, as follows :
01. The Section 7.3.2.1 is revised as follows :
7.3.2.1 Educational Qualifications
Shall have passed the G. C. E. (O/L) Examination in six (06) subjects including Sinhala/Tamil/English and
Mathematics/Arithmetic/Pure Mathematics/Elementary Mathematics/Commercial Artithmetic with at least two
credit passes at not more than two sittings.
02. The term ”Efficiency Bar Examination” shall be included in substitution for the term “Examination” in Sections
02, 04 and 05 under Annex 06.
03. Following shall be included as No. 06 at the end of Annex 06.
06. Officers shall be exempted from the requirement of passing the Efficiency Bar Examination prescribed for
the officers in Grade I on successful completion of the 10 day course including the basic principles of which a
Senior Officer in public service should be aware, within 05 years from the date of promotion to Grade I.
i. Conducting authority of the course : Director General of Combined Services
ii. Duration of the course : The notification for calling applications relevant to the training course prescribed
for Grade I shall be published in the first quarter of every year and the number  of training course conducted
shall be determined in considering the number of applications received.
