1A   G   29154 —17  (12/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   MATARA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of Member of Matara Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which  Mohammed
Fazul Ajwad the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
returned  member if People’s Liberation Front  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Electioins Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a Member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Hewa Kuda Anthonige Somaweera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Thananthri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer of the Matara Municipal Council,
acting in terms of section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Hewa
Kuda Anthonige Somaweera was selected as the Member of the Matara Municipal Council.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Matara,
