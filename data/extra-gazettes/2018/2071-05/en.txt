THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provision of that Act.
An allotments of land with below mentioned names and extents
depicted in Advance Tracing No. MR/MLM/2017/132 and dated
28.04.2017 which is prepared by the Surveyor General and situated
in the Village of Katuwangoda in Weligam Korale Minor Division, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretary’s Division in Matara District in
Southern Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Hettigewaththa – Extent: 0.0847 Hectare
North: Road, Hettigewatta claimed by D. G . Rupika and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Hettigewatta
which is claimed by Indunil Malawiarachchi; South: Hettigewatta
claimed by Indunil Malawiarachchi; West: Road, Hettigewatta which
is claimed by D. G . Rupika Damayanthi.
