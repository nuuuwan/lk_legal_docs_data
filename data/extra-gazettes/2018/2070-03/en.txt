 FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
THE order under Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as amended by Acts, No. 13 of 1966,
No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I,
Maithripala Sirisena, Minister of Mahaweli Development and Environment do by this Order declare that Halgolla,
Udangomuwa, Galgewatta, Alwatta, Karagahalanda, Pallehapuvida, Muwandeniya proposed Reserve Forest of 28.9061
hectares bounded by the limit set forth in the schedule 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06, 07, 08, 09, 10, 11, 12 and 13 hereto as
Halgolla, Udangomuwa, Galgewatta, Alwatta, Karagahalanda, Pallehapuvida, Muwandeniya Reserved Forest from 04th day
of April, 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
Minister of Mahaweli Development and Environment.
Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment,
Battaramulla,
