1A   G   27413 —27  (04/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  TRINCOMALEE   TOWN  &
GRA VETS    PRADESHIYA   SABAH   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Rasathurai
Sivaraja the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Ward 02 Puliyankulam if Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Eliyezer Gnananayagam Gnanagunalan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, returning officer of the Trincomalee Town & Gravets, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Eliyezer
Gnananayagam Gnanagunalan was elected as the member of the Ward 02 Puliyankulam of Trincomalee Town & Gravets
Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA W ELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
04th April, 2018,

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  TRINCOMALEE   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Trincomalee Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Thurairasa
Dhanaraj the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that Ward 01 Anbuvalipuram if Trincomalee Urban Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Nagarasa Rasanayagam to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, returning officer of the Trincomalee Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Nagarasa
Rasanayagam was elected as the member of the Ward 01 Anbuvalipuram of Trincomalee Urban Council.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA W ELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
