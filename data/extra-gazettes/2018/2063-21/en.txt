STATEMENT OF MATTER  IN DISPUTE
 The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
" 1. Whether  Mr. K.K. Jayasena who worked at the V ocational Training Authority of Sri Lanka and had faced the interview
held in year 2014 for recruitment of officers to the post of Director General, V ocational Training Authority of Sri Lanka, had
been caused injustice by not been given that post and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
2. Whether  Mr.  N.S.D. Nawaratne who works at the V ocational Training Authority of Sri Lanka and had faced the interview
held in year 2014 for recruitment of officers to the post of Director (Planing, Research and Development) of the, V ocational
Training Authority of Sri Lanka, had been caused injustice by not been given that post and if so, to  what reliefs he is
entitled.
3. Whether  Mr. U.K. Nanda  who works at the V ocational Training Authority of Sri Lanka and had faced the interview held
in year 2014 for recruitment of officers to the posts of  Director (Training), Director ( Career Guidance, Placement and
Special projects), and Director (Planning, Research and Development) of the V ocational Training  Authority of  Sri  Lanka,
had been caused injustice by not being given either of these posts and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled."
