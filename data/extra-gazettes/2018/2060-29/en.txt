PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Proclamations & C., by the President
PRESIDENTIAL   DIRECTIVE
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
WHEREAS, in pursuance of powers vested in me by Article 33 (2) of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
National  Salaries and Cadre Commission was established by the Presidential Directive on March 03, 2016
Whereas, the time given for the completion of the functions and responsibilities assigned to the said Commission
will expire on March 02, 2018,
And whereas, since the need for the said Commission to be continued further has been identified,
I, Maithripala  Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby enlarge the time
for completion of  the said functions and responsibilities of the said  Commission by further two years from 03.03.2018.
Given at Colombo under the seal of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka,  on this  28th day of
February, 2018.
                                                                                    By  His Excellency's Command,
S
UMITH ABEYSINGHE,
Acting Secretary to the President.
