THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke as the Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declared.
The following land is required for a public Purpose and therefore the
Government intends to acquire the mentioned land under, the Sub
Section (1) of the Section 05 of the land acquisition (amendment) Act
number 20 of 1964.
Details of land acquisition :- land acquiring for the Pallama Divisional
Secretariat office.
Details of the Acquiring land
The intended land to acquire is the Lot  A of the land Surveyed by the
superintendent of Survey Puttlam in Preliminary Plan No. PU/PLL/
2015/452 of the village of Poththukkulama in No. 664/C.
