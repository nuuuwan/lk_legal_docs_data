THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 2 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
 I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka by virtue of  powers vested in me
under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 1980, being  of  the  opinion upon
the recommendation of  the Minister - in - Charge of  the subject of Urban Development that the land specified in the
below-mentioned schedule is urgently required for Bandaragama Twon Development Project do by this Order, declare
that the said allotment of land is required for the relevant purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
28th October, 2018.
