THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Allotments of land in the letters from 'A' to 'B' in the extents given
below depicted in advanced Tracing No.KE/GLG/2018/73 of
24.05.2018 prepared by Surveyor General in extent about 1.7505
Hectares, situated in the village of Arandara in the Grama Niladari
Division of No. 64A, Arandara Grama Niladari Division in Kandupita
Pattuwa South Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Galigamuwa in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province are
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 
1.6942 Hectares
North: Road; East: Road and Lot B;  South: Lot B, Atala Watta
Batupitiya Division and Koswatta Kumbura; West: Koswatta
Kumbura.
02. Lot ‘B’ –  Extent: 0.0563 Hectares
North: Lot A and Road; East: Atala Watta Batupitiya Division;
South: Atala Watta Batupitiya Division;West: Atala Watta Batupitiya
Division and Lot A.
