SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Eran
Wickramaratne, Acting Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this
order impose in respect of the list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy  at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from March 08, 2018 subject to provisons of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
E
RAN W ICKRAMARA TNE,
Acting Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
07th March, 2018.
