THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of Land depicted in Advance Tracing No.
CO/KTT/2017/405 and  dated 12th September, 2017 prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated at Pitakotte (within Ward No.6 -
Pitakotte Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council) in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte in the
Colombo District in Western Province computed to certain in extent
0.1317 hectare and bounded:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Madatiyagahawatta – Extent: 0.1317 Hectare
North: land bearing Assmt. No. 163/3, Thalawathugoda Road,
cemented drainage and name unknown land; East: name unknown
land, Assmt. No. 171/1, Thalawathugoda Road and land bearing No.
171/1/A; South:land bearing Assmt. No. 171/1 and 171/1/A
Thalawathugoda Road and Thalawathugoda Road; West:
Thalawathugoda Road, land bearing Assmt. No. 163/3,
Thalawathugoda Road and cemented drainage.
