THE   WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD   FOR   THE   RETAIL   &  WHOLESALE   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, 1971 that Ms. Geetha Anoma Yasanayake
have been appointed as Employee's Representatives of the Wages Board for the Retail & Wholesale Trade in place of Mrs.
Poornima Maheshi Anandasiri by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 9 of the Wages Boards
Ordinance (Chapter 136). This appointment is valid since 2018.07.01 to 2020.11.30
S. A. N. S
ARANA THISSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
