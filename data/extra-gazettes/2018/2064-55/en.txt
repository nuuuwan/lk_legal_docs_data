1A  - PG  3998—432  (04/2018)
 INLAND  REVENUE  ACT,  No.  24  OF  2017
Notice under Sections 18
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 18(4) of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 24 of 2017 in relation to the
circumstances in which losses on financial instruments may only be set against gains on financial instruments, I, Disanayake
Mudiyanselage Lalith Ivan Disanayake, Commissioner General of Inland Revenue, do by this notice specify any circumstance
in which loss on a financial instrument is occurred as the circumstance in which loss on a financial instrument set against
the gain from same type of financial instrument.
D. M. L. I. D
ISANAYAKE,
Commissioner General of Inland Revenue.
Department of Inland Revenue,
Colombo 02,
01st April 2018.
