THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : 2/3/1/4/4.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 4-3/2015/HA/299.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which
is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land  to be acquired:
Public purpose means the land Acquisition for the land
where Ambanganga Korale Divisional Secretariat is
situated.
The land called Opalgala Estate, containing in extent
0.3744 Hectare, depicted as lot A shown in P .P .Ma. 1729,
prepared by Surveyor General dated on 23.08.2018, situated
at Palletenna village, in Palletenna Grama Niladhari
Division, Ambanganga Korale minor Division of
Ambanganga Korale Divisional Secretary's Division,
Matale District, Central Province.
Lot A
North: Palletenna Estate where Ambanganga Korale
Education Office is situated, Road Development Authority
road, Pradeshiya Sabha road; East: Road Development
Authority road and road; South: Road and Palletenna Estate
claimed by S. Sivakumar and others; West: Road and
Palletenna Estate claimed by S. Sivakumar and others and
Palletenna Estate where Ambanganga Korale Education Office
is situated; Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.3744
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally , or by agents before me at 10.00
a.m. on the 21st day of December 2018 at the Ambanganga
Korale Divisional Secretariat.
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of December
2018, the nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Ambanganga Korale Divisional Secretary's Division,
Matale District.
At Ambanganga Korale Divisional Secretariat,
01st  November, 2018.
