No. 2072/4 – MONDAY, MAY 21, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Four allotments of land depicted as Lots ‘A ’ to ‘D’, depicted in
the Advance Tracing No.: KU/BMK/2014/107 dated  19.09.2014,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village called
Hunukumbura in the Minor Division of Walgampattukorale in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Bamunakotuwa in Kurunegala
District in North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  – Udupitiya Kumbura –  Extent: 0.0136 Hectare
North: Land claimed by Lionel Siriwardhane; East: Lot B; South:
Balance portion of this land; West: Pradeshiya Sabha Road.
02. Lot ‘B’  – Udupitiya Kumbura –  Extent: 0.0186 Hectare
North: Land claimed by Lionel Siriwardhane; East: Lot C; South:
Balance portion of this land; West: Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’  – Udupitiya Kumbura –  Extent: 0.0056 Hectare
North: Land claimed by Sarath Jayaweera; East: Lot D; South:
Balance portion of this land; West: Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’  – Hitinawatta alias Kosgahamulawatta –  Extent:
0.0177 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Land claimed by K. V .
Piyadasa; South: Udupitiya Kumbura claimed by K. V . Thilakaratne;
West: Lot C.
