L. D. B. 21/1978 (x)
UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25A
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me bySection 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Colombo
International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, a  compnay incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies
Act, No, 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 17745, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under
the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2047/26 of November 28, 2017 by the addition immediately after
entry "Bachelor of Management Honours in Supply Chain Management" appearing in column I and it's corresponding entry
in Column II respectively of the Schedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule
II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto:-
Schedule I
CONDITIONS
Colombo International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, shall, when admitting students for the degree
programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the;
(a) Bachelor of Management Honours in Tourism and Hospitality Management, adhere to minimum entry qualifiaction
of three (03) 'S' passes in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advacnced Level) Examination or
equivalent.
(b) Bachelor of Education Honours in English, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of three (03) 'S' passes in any
subjcet stream ('English' shall be one of the main three subjects) in one and the same sitting at GCE (Advanced
Level) Examination or equivalent.
(c) Bachelor of Education Honours in Physical Sciences, adhere to minimum entry qualification of three (03) 'S' passes
from among the subjects Combined Mathematics or Higher Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Information and
Communication Technology and Mathematics in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) Examination
or equivalent.
