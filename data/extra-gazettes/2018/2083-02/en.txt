1A- PG  4178 — 557  (08/2018)
EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance & Mass Media, do by this Order, insert the following items (iii) and (iv)
immediately after item (ii) of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2082/10 dated July 31, 2018.
(iii) item (ii) of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2082/10 dated July 31, 2018 shall be applicable
for any motor vehicle, imported based on the letter of credit (LC), established on or before August 01,
2018, and cleared from Sri Lanka Customs on or before January 31, 2019, subject to no amendment is
(a) vehicle identification number (b) description of vehicle, and (c) technical specifications of the vehicle in
such letter of credit.
(iv) the rate of Excise duty applicable, for motor vehicle imported based on the letter of credit (LC), established
on or before August 01, 2018 with any amendment(s) to (a) vehicle identification number (b) description
of vehicle and/or  (c) technical specifications of the vehicle(s), that have been made after August 01, 2018,
and cleared from Sri Lanka Customs on or before January 31, 2019 shall be as specified in item (i) of the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2082/10 dated July 31, 2018.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
