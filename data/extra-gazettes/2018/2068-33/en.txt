 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathilleka, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of  land in total extent of about 0.0098 hectare
depicted as lot No. A in the Plan No. Gam/Gam/2017/117 dated
08.06.2017 prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Survey's
Gampaha district for the land called "Keenagahalandawatta" situated
in the village called Aluthgama Bogamuwa in Medapattuwa minor
division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Gampaha in
Gampaha District of the Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Keenagahalandawatta – Extent: 0.0098 Hectare
North : Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Road; East : Road; South :
Balance portion of this land and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West :
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha).
