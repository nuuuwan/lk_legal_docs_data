No. 2088/17–  MONDAY, SEPTEMBER,  10,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT. No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
  
Reference No.: CHI/LND.1/AC/5/71. 
4-3/09/2016/ WS/135.  
I,  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:  
The Allotment of land described in Preliminary Plan               
No. P. P. Pu. 5362 dated 20.07.2018 prepared by the 
Surveyor General, situated in the 568 Munneshwaram 
Grama Niladhari Division, Village of Munneshwaram in 
the Munneswaram Pattu Minor Division in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Chilaw in Puttalam District in the 
North Western Province. 
 
 Lot No. 01 
 Name of the Land: Lunupitiyawatta; Description: 
Garden, Contains 42 Coconut Trees 25 -30 years old, 09 
Teak Trees 25 -30 years old and a Toilet; First Claimant: 
Kanagasabe Rajamani, Kanapathi Hotel, Munneshwaram, 
Chilaw; Extent: 0.2240 Hectare.   . 
  
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Land hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Chilaw on the 19th day of November, 2018 
at 10.00 a. m. and are  hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 31st day of October 
2018,  the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, and the  details of  the 
computation of such amount. 
  
 D. M. A. K. DISSANAYAKE 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Chilaw. 
  
05th September  2018, 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Chilaw.
