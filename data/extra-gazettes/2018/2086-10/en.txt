THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called as Nelukosgahamula Watta, in extent
of about 0.0836 Hectare shown in Superintendent of  Surveys
Advance Tracing No. MA/RTT/2017/659 and dated of 29.01.2018
as Lot A   situated in Kuruwawa Village, in the Rattota Divisional
Secetariat  Division, Matale Pallesiyapattuwa Minor Division, in
Matale District in Central Province and bounded as follows.
North: Pallegedara Watta claimed by Kumara Hewapathirana and
Pallegedara Watta claimed by Weerasinghe S.G. I.G.; East: Pallegedara
Watta claimed by Weerasinghe S.G . I.G. and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);
South: NelukosgahamulaWatta claimed by Disanayake W.K.; West:
Punchaya Watta claimed by  Yasarathna  and others, Punchaya Watta
claimed by Karunarathna and Others and Pallegedara Watta claimed
by Kumara Hewapathirana.
