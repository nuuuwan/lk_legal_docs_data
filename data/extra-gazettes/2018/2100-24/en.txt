No. 2100/24 – THURSDAY, DECEMBER 06, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in extent of about 0.0290 Hecatre, depicted
as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing No. MR/MLT/2016/349 dated
21.11.2016, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Ransagoda (F.V .P . 97) in the Minor Division of Kandabadapattuwa
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mulatiyana in Matara
District, Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ransagoda ancient temple premises alias
Muththettu Idama– Extent: 0.0290 Hectare
North and East: Ransagoda ancient temple premises  alias
Muththettu Idama claimed by Ven. Gombaddala Manirathana Thero;
South: Ransagoda ancient temple premises alias Muththettu Idama
claimed by Ven. Gombaddala Manirathana Thero and Way; West:
Way and Ransagoda ancient temple premises alias Muththettu Idama
claimed by Ven. Gombaddala Manirathana Thero.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
