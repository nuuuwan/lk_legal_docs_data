1A – G 27524 – 12 (04/2018)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Boundaries for the allotments of land containing in extent 1.0502
Hectare depicted as from Lot Letter A to E in the Advance Tracing
No. MR/DVN/2016/33 and dated 19.08.2016 made by Surveyor
Superintendent of Matara and situated in the Village of Talalla in
Wellabada Pattu East Minior Division, Divisional Secretary's Division
of Devinuwara, Matara District, Southern Province:
01. Lot  'A' –  Kapuhena –  Extent: 0.3279 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Thanthirigewatta claimed by
S. H. Nalinda Wasantha; East: Thanthirigewatta claimed by S. H.
Nalinda Wasantha and Kombugewatta claimed by H. G . Piyasena;
South: Kombugewatta claimed by H. G . Piyasena and 'Lot B'
Dunkortuwahena Watta which has no claimant and Road; West: Lot
B and Ihala Koratuwa claimed by Nevil Nishantha and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha).
02. Lot  'B' –  Arachchigehena –  Extent: 0.1844 Hectare
North: Ihala Koratuwa claimed by Nevil Nishantha and Lot A;
East: Lot  A and Kombugewatta claimed by H. G . Piyasena; South:
Pattigewatta alias Mahawatta claimed by Magamage Chandradasa;
West: Arachchigehena claimed by R. A. Priyaratha.
03. Lot  'C' –  Kombugewatta –  Extent: 0.4974 Hectare
North: Kombugewatta claimed by H. G . Piyasena; East: Outer
Canal and Lot D; South: Lot D and Pattigewatta alias Mahawatta
claimed by Magamage Chandradasa; West: Pattigewatta alias
Mahawatta claimed by Magamage Chandradasa and Lot B.
04. Lot  'D' –  Kombugewatta –  Extent: 0.0295 Hectare
North: Lot C; East: Outer Canal, Road and Lot E; South: Lot  E
and  Pattigewatta alias  Mahawatta claimed by Magamage
Chandradasa; West: Lot C.
05. Lot  'E' –  Kombugewatta –  Extent: 0.0110 Hectare
North: Lot D; East: Kombugemidellamure claimed by Neel
Ramasundara and others; South: Kombugemidellamure claimed by
Neel Ramasundara and Pattigewatta alias Mahawatta claimed by
Magamage Chandradasa; West: Lot D.
