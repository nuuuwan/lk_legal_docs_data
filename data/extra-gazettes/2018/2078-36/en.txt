MEDIATION   (SPECIAL   CATEGORIES   OF   DISPUTES)   ACT,   No   21   OF   2003
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 02 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act, No. 21 of
2003, I, Thalatha Athukorale, Minister of Justice, do by this Order, determine that the categories of disputes specified in
the Schedule hereto as the categories of disputes to which the provisions of the said Act shall apply regarding the settlement
through mediation.
T
HALA THA ATHUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
19th June, 2018.
SCHEDULE
Any dispute relating to grievances of migrant workers and members of  the family residing in Sri Lanka.
