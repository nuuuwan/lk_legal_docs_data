1A   G   27731 —17  (05/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  NORWOOD   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66B(2) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman in Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ceylon Workers Congress (Political Wings), the recognized political party
to which Kalimuththu Sivasamy, the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party  who shall
be declared elected as  Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thangaraj Kisokumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pulasthi Pathirana, returning officer of the Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Thangaraj Kisokumar elected as Vice Chairman of the Norwood
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P ATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
