No. 2088/24 – MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land containing in extent of 19.1823 Hectares,
depicted in Surveyor-General’s Advance Tracing No. Ma/AMB/
2014/143 and dated 08.10.2015, situated at Village of Gammaduwa
in the Minor Division of Ambanganga Korale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Ambanganga Korale in Matale District in
Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Karagastenna Estate – Extent: 4.5239 Hectares
North: Karagastenna Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission;
East: Karagastenna Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission and
Road; South: Road; West: Karagastenna Estate claimed by Land
Reform Commission.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 0.0734 Hectare
North: Road; East: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission; South: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission and Road; West: Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 5.3949 Hectares
North: Road; East: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission and Ela; South: Ela, Road and Gammaduwa Estate
claimed by Eco Field Plantation Company; West: Gammaduwa Estate
claimed by Eco Field Plantation Company.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 4.4052 Hectares
North: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission
and Road; East: Ela; South: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by W. U.
Premasiri and others; West: Road and Gammaduwa Estate claimed
by Land Reform Commission.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 0.9294 Hectare
North: Akkara Wissa Estate claimed by A. P. Gunawathi and
others and Road; East and South: Road; West: Ela and Akkara Wissa
Estate claimed by A. P. Gunawathi.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 2.0707 Hectares
North: Ela and Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission; East: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission and Ela; South: Ela and Akkara Wissa claimed by
D. A. Jayarthna and others; West: Ela.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Gammaduwa Estate – Extent: 1.7848 Hectares
North: Ela and Lalan Estate claimed by Eco Field Plantation
Company; East: Lalan Estate claimed by Eco Field Plantation
Company; South: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission; West: Gammaduwa Estate claimed by Land Reform
Commission and Ela.
