The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PASBAGE KORALE    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  being Ceased to be
a member of the Party;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Janasetha Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Pemasiri
Bogahawaththa   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  the  member of that 09-  Kadiyanlena if Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha   who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Namadagaswewa Sugathasoma Thero  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the   Pasbage
Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare that Namadagaswewa Sugathasoma Thero  was elected as the member of the 09-  Kadiyanlena of Pasbage
Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
28th  September  2018.
