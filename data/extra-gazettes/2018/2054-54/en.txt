 ANNOUNCEMENT  UNDER  SECTION  76A  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ACT,  (CHAPTER 262)   SUSPENSION   OF   PROPAGANDA
ACTIVITIES
AS it is the responsiblity of the Elections  Commission to conduct free and fair elections in accordance with the Article
104 B (1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, and as it is empowered to issue directives in
order to overcome the obstacles in the conduct of elections to be held under the Local Authorities Elections Act
(Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendments) Act, No. 22 of 2012, it is hereby announced that the
following directives are issued by the Commission.
01. Taking photographs and videoing inside the polling station.
It is hereby announced that taking photographs or videoing stages of marking ballot papers or marked ballot papers
inside postal voting centres or in polling stations or distribution or publishing of photographs of marked ballot papers
through print, electronic, social or any other media is prohibited.
It is also announced that the Elections Commission will issue permission in writing when there is a need to take
photographs or to video any special occasion or procedure inside a polling station, for the purpose of preparing official
reports and collection of photographs on the poll
