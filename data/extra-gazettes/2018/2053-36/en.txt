Appointments  made by  the  Governor
BY virtue of the powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of the Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mrs. R. H. C. Priyanthi, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to Act as Director to the
Social Service of Uva Province, on full time basis with immediate effect (from the date 25.07.2017)
2) Mr. S. B. Udovita, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Municipal Commissioner of
Bandarawela Municipal Council, with immediate effect (from the date 03.08.2017)
3) Mrs. G . W. C. S. Priyabhashini, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties
of the Director to the Industries of Uva Province, in addition to her duties of permanent post, from the date
05.08.2017 till the return to the Island of Mr. Ajith Dissanayake, the Director to the Industries of Uva Province,
who is abroad from the date 05.08.2017 to 15.08.2017.
4) Mrs. C. A. Herath, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Municipal Commissioner of
Badulla Municipal Council, with effect from the date 21.08.2017 until re inform.
5) Mr. D. M. L. Bandaranayake, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Secretary
to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Women’s Affairs and Social Welfare

return to the Island of Mr. L. L. Anil Wijesiri, the Secretary to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine,
Probation and Childcare, Women’s Affairs and Social Welfare of the Uva Provincial Council, who is abroad
from the date 25.08.2017 to 01.09.2017.

the Secretary to the Ministry of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure and Co-
operative of the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his duties of permanent post, from the date 25.08.2017
till the return to the Island of Mr. G . K. S. L. Rajadasa, the Secretary to the Ministry of Road Development,
Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure and Co-operative of the Uva Provincial Council, who is abroad
from the date 25.08.2017 to 01.09.2017.
7) Mr. Dr. Sanath Gamage, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Animal Product and Health Service, as Acting Provincial
Director of the Department of Animal Product and Health of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent
post, from the date 25.08.2017 till the return to the Island of Mr. S. K. Weerasundara, Acting Provincial Director
of the Department of Animal Product and Health of Uva Province, who is abroad from the date 25.08.2017 to
01.09.2017.
8) Mr. S. K. Weerasundara, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Animal Product & Health Service, as Provincial
Director of the Department of Animal Product and Health of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent
post, with  effect from the date 21.08.2017.
9) Mr. W. M. M. G. Abesinghe Banda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, As Acting Secretary
to the Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production and Inland Fisheries Affairs of the Uva Provincial
Council, in addition to his duties of permanent post, from the date 25.08.2017 till the return to the  Island of
Mr. Gunadasa Samarasinghe, the Secretary to the Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production and
Inland Fisheries Affairs of the Uva Provincial Council, who is abroad from the date 25.08.2017 to 01.09.2017.
