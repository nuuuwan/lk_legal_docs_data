NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 07  OF   THE   LAND
ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460) AS  AMENDED
BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT) ACT,
NO. 28 OF 1964
Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/69.
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2015/A/09'
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of  Section (7) of
the  Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land / servitude
which is required for the construction of Distribution, officer's
official residence.
 Description of the land or servitude to be  Acquired:
Allotments of land described in bellow in Plan No. Ma
1739, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of Surveys,
situated in Kaludewala village, Viharagama Gramaniladari
Division, in Sub Division of Matale Pallesiya Pattuwa in the
Rattota Divisional Secretariat Division, in Matale District, in
Central Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Suduganga Watta; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Land Reform Commission, P .O. Box 1526, No.C 82,
Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawata., Colombo.; Extent: 0.0682
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Rattota Divisional Secretariat on 21st
November, 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writting (in duplicate) on or before 13th
November, 2018  the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
H.M.N.DINIPRIYA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rattota.
At Divisional Secretariat, Rattota.
11th October, 2018.
