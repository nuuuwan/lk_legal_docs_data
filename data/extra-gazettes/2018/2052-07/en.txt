THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No. : 3/6/2016/5.
Ministry of Lands Reference No.:4-3/7/2016/HW/82.
I hereby give notice  under sub Section (1) of the Section 7, of
the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964  that the Government
intends to acquire the following land /servitude* which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land  or servitude to be acquired:
Under mentioned Land shown in the Surveyor General's
plan P . P . N. 2357, Minor Division Udapola Othotakorale West,
situated in the Village of Wewala and Koradoluwa in Grama
Niladari Division of No. 912 Koradoluwa in Polgahawela
Divisional Secretary's Division in Kurunagala District of North
Western Province.
01. Lot No. 40
Name of land: -; Description: Road; Claimant: Government;
Original Claimant: claimant not known; Extent: 0.0163 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/Lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at the Polgahawela Divisional Secretariat, on the day of 22nd
February 2018, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 19th February, 2018
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for computation  for the said land/servitude the amount
of compensation  the details of the computation of such
amount.
