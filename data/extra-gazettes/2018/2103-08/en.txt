No. 2103/8–  MONDAY, DECEMBER,  24,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  
ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
  
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land  or servitude to be  acquired: 
  
Allotment of land called in extent 0.9315, extent 0.1909 
of Hectare shown as Lot 589, 590 in FVP 21 dated 
2017.10.23 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in 
with in the Pradeshiya Sabha limits of Trincomalee No. 
229E Kappalthurai Grama Niladhari Division, Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Town & Gravets, Trincomalee 
District and bounded as follows. Details Attached. 
 
Lot No. 589 - Extent: 0.9315 Hectare 
 
North Lots: Lot 590; East Lots: Lots 590 and 71; South 
Lots: Lots 71; West Lots: Lots 71 and 590 
Lot No. 590 - Extent: 0.1909 Hectare 
 
North Lots: Lot 71; East Lots: Lot 71; South Lots: Lots 
589  and 71; West Lots: Lot 71    
   
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which  a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing  
before me at Divisional Secretariat Trincomalee on the 
11th day of February 2019 at 10.00 a. m.  And you are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 28th day of January  2019,  the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount. 
 
 All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
  
 J. S. ARULRAJ, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Town and Gravets 
 Trincomalee. 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Town and Gravets, 
Trincomalee. 
21st December, 2018.
