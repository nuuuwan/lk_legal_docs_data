MINUTE  OF  SRI  LANKA  TECHNOLOGICAL  SERVICE -  AMENDMENT  No.  02
THE following amendments are made to the Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Technological Service published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1930/12 dated 01.09.2015 as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1995/10 dated
29.11.2016 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the other matters and conditions set out therein shall
remain unchanged.
01. Note 08 in Page No. 7A should be amened as follows.
08. Note :-
(I) The officers who have completed five (05) years of service after their promotion to Grade I of the
Sri Lanka Technological Service as at the effective  date of this Service Minute are exempted from
the requirement of passing the third efficiency bar examination ;
(II) In addition to the officers in Grade I, the officers who have pass the second efficiency bar
examination are also able to complete the course prescribed for the third efficiency bar examination.
                                                      By order of the Public Service Commisison.
M. A. B. D
AYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
