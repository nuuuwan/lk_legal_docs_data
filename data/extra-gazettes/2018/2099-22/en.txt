THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: KDS/UDP/4/1/LAQ ENTR.MORAGO.05.
Land Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/201.
THE Land Acquisition for construction of Power House access
road of Hydro Power Project of Moragolla, Gampola.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The land allotment called Devita Meddaliyaddahena in total
extent of about 5.2970 Hectares depicted and prepared by
Surveyor General in cadstral map No. 320229(6)2 in Lot No. 24
situated in Grama Niladhari Division of Galatha in Kandukara
Ihala Korale in Divisional Secretary Division of Udapalatha in
Kandy District, Central Province.
The dates and the time of the inquiries of Compensation
01. Lot No. 24
Amount: 01; Cadastral Plan No.: C.M. 320229(6)2;  Date of
inquiries of compensation: 19.12.2018; Time: 10.30 a.m.; Place:
Divisional Secretariat, Udapalatha, Gampola.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, of Udapalatha on the 19th  day of
December 2018 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of
December, 2018 the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compenstation for the acquisition
of  the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
R. A. D. T. N. THENNAKOON,
Divisional Secretatry,
Udapalatha Division,
Gampola.
Divisional Secretariat of Udapalatha,
 27th November,  2018.
