2A
The qualifications set out under 7.1 of the Service Minute of the Combined Service
of Officers in the field of Sports published in the Gazette No. 1009 dated 02 January
1998 will be made applicable in place of the education and professional
qualifications referred to under 7.2.3.2.1 of the said Service Minute in the
recruitment to the post of Sport Coach in the category of Supervisory Management
Assistant Technical Service of the Combined Service of Officers in the field of
sports up to 26.09.2021, the date on which the said officers complete five (05)
years of service from the date of the said Service Minute takes effect.
Note:
The officers appointed to the post of Sports Coach in terms of interim provisions
referred to under 13.4 without the completion of qualifications of Technical Diploma
relating to the field of Sports at the level of National V ocational Qualification (NVQ
06) recognized by the Tertiary and V ocational Education Commission referred to
under 7.2.3.2.1 (ii) of the said Service Minute,
(a) Should complete the qualification referred to under 7.2.3.2.1(ii) of the
said Service Minute before the completion of a period of five (05)
years following the appointment to the post.
(b) The salary increments of the officers who do not complete the
qualification as per (a) above will be deferred from the date they
complete a period of five (05) years up to the date they complete the
qualification referred to under 7.2.3.2.1(ii).
The officers who have earned five (05) salary increment having completed a period
of five (05) years of active and satisfactory service in Grade II of the Executive
Service Category of Combined Service of Officers in the field of Sports and who
have demonstrated a performance at satisfactory level or above during the entire
period of five (05) years  preceding the date of promotion in accordance with
approved procedure of performance appraisal will be promoted to Grade I of the
Executive Service Category according to number of vacancies existing in the post
in Grade I of Executive Service Category irrespective of the qualification referred
to in 10.6.1.1.(i) and (ii).
