L.D.B. 104/44(v)
TRUST   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “The Arthma Jothi Foundation” namely-
1. Aiyadurai Somaskanda moorthy alias Aiyadurai Thirugnanasampanthan
2. Kandaih Balasubramaniam
have applied to the Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms for their incorporation under Section 114 of the
Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87) ;
Now Therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Thalatha Athukorale, Minister
of Justice and Prison Reforms do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare
that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
said Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing No. 2081 of August 7, 2012 attested by Murugesu and
Neelakandan Notaries Public of Colombo as amended by the Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing
No. 136 of June 30, 2018 attested by Murugesu and Neelakandan Notaries Public of Colombo shall be a body
corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style of the “The Arthma Jothi
Foundation” ;
