Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Civil Aviation (Licensing of Personnel and Training Institutions)
Regulation No. 01 of 2018.
2. The Director-General of Civil Aviation (hereinafter referred to as the “Director -General”) may on being
satisfied with the fulfillment of the requirements as specified in Column IV of the Schedule hereto and also specified in
respective implementing Standards, by any person or institution for a licence or permit, as the case may be, to provide any
training course in respect of any activity specified in Column I, issue the respective licence or permit, or refuse to issue
the licence or permit for reasons assigned therefor. The category of such licence or permit, as the case may be, is
specified in Column II of the Schedule hereto and the guide lines to the Implementing Standards specified in Column IV
of the Schedule shall be in the Manuals as specified in Column III thereof.
3. The procedure for the issuance, amendment, renewal, ground for suspension and revocation of any licence or
permit, as the case may be, issued under regulation 2, the terms and conditions applicable to any such licence or permit
and the privileges attached thereto shall be in compliance with the relevant implementing Standards for each such activity
as specified in Column IV of the Schedule and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1713/7 dated July 05, 2011.

4. The application processing fee, the licence or permit fee and the fee for any renewal thereof shall be as specified
in the Civil Aviation Authority Fees and Charges Regulation No. 1 of 2014 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1869/
32 dated July 02, 2014.
5. Every licence holder shall prior to the date of expiry of such licence or permits, as the case may be, issued
under regulation 2, make an application to the Director-General for the renewal of such licence or permit.
6. The Director-General may on having proof of any contravention of any provision relating to aviation safety
requirements issued under the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 or any regulation made thereunder, by any person or
institution to whom or which a licence or permit has been issued under these regulations, suspend or revoke such licence
or permit, as the case may be.
7. Any person or institution aggrieved by a decision made by the Director-General under these regulations may,
before the expiry of thirty working days from the date of communication of such decision to such person or institution,
prefer an appeal to the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister under Section 112 of the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of
2010.
8. The following regulations of the Air Navigation Regulations of 1955 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 10812 dated 01.07.1955 are hereby repealed without prejudice to anything previously done thereunder
(a) regulations 22 to 30 of Chapter (IV) ;
(b) regulations 48 to 73 of Chapter (V) and ;
(c) regulations 74 to 80 of Chapter (VI) ;
9. For the purpose of these regulations -
“Convention” shall have the same meaning as assignged in the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 ;
“Director-General” means the Director-General of Civil Aviation appointed under Section 11 of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002.
