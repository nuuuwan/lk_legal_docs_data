PART 1: SECTION (1) - GENERAL
PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
IN TERMS  of Section 10 of Regulations framed under the Pilgrimage Ordinance published in the Govern ment Gazette
No. 11959 dated 20th November 1959, it is hereby notified that Annual Esala Festival - 2018 of Devinuwara Sri  Vishnu Maha
Devala in Matara District, Southern Province will commence on Saturday the 21st of July 2018 and continue until Friday the
27th of July 2018.
PRADEEP R ATHNAYAKE,
District Secretary/ Govt. Agent,
Matara.
Distict Secretariat of Matara,
30 th May,  2018.
