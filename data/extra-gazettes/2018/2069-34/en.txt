THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land desribed as Lot ‘A’ in total extent of about
2.5015 Hectares depicted in the Advance Tracing No. R/KLW/2017/
756 and dated 11th November, 2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General situated in the Village of Puhulpelpola (V .P. 946) in the
Minor Division of Kukulu Korale, Uda Pattuwa in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kalawana in Ratnapura District in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:

2.5015 Hectares

1302 and 320; South: Lot Nos. 320, 1303, 1302 and 1321; West: Lot
No. 1321.
