 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of Land called Kappalthuraikadu in extent of
0.9445 Hectare depicted as lot A in advance tracing Plan No. TRI/
TWG/2015/008 dated 31.03.2015 prepared by the Snr. supdt. of
Surveys Situated within the Town and Gravets, Pradeshiya Sabha
limits of Trincomalee situated in the No.229E in the Kappalthurai
Grama Niladhari Division, in Divisional Secretari at Division of
Town and Gravets, Trincomalee District in Eastern Province. The
Land is bounded as be follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Kappalthuraikadu – Extent: 0.9445 Hectare
North: Path (Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Path (Pradeshiya Sabha)
and Kappalthuraikadu; South: Kappalthuraikadu; West:
Kappalthuraikadu and Path (Pradeshiya Sabha).
