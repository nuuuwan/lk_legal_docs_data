FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   DEHIOWITA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Hapu
Arachchillage Somasundhara   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the  member of that 17 Panawala of  Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha   who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Balasooriya Arachchilage Neetha Balasooriya  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the   Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) (a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Balasooriya
Arachchilage Neetha Balasooriya  was elected as the member of the 17 Panawala of  Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Dehiowita Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
02nd   October  2018.
