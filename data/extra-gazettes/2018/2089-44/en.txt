CONSULAR  FUNCTIONS  ACT,  No.  4  OF  1981
Notification under  Section 8
I, Tilak Marapana, Minister of  Foreign  Affairs in terms of the powers vested in me under Section 8 of the Consular Functions
Act, No. 04 of 1981, do by this Notification specify that, with effect from 01st September 2018, the Divisional Secretaries
will be authorized to charge the fees prescribed for the attestation by the Consular Affairs Division of the Ministry of
Foreign Affairs, acting as Consular officers.
T
ILAK M ARAPANA,
Minister of  Foreign Affairs.
Colombo,
01st September, 2018.
