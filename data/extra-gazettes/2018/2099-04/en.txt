Notice  under  Section 7
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section (7) of
the Land Acquisition Act 1964 that Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:-
The following allotments of land in extent of 1.9435
Hectares depicted in the P .P .K. 3619 dated 30.09.2018 prepared
by Surveyor General, within the boundaries shown below
situated in the village of Welihelatenna in the Kithulgala
Palatha Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Yatiyantota in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
01. Lot A – Name of the Land: Not indicated; Extent:
0.0456 Hectare.
North: Lavent Estate; East: Lavent Estate, Road and Lot C
(Road); South: Lot B, Lot C (Road); West: Road and Lavent
Estate.
02. Lot B – Name of the Land: Lavent Estate; Extent:
0.4854 Hectare.
North: Lot  A (Road); East: Lot A (Road) and Lot C (Road);
South: Lot C (Road); West: Lot C (Road) and Lot A (Road).
03. Lot C – Name of the Land: Not indicated; Extent:
0.1279 Hectare.
North: Lavent Estate, Lot F, Lot D (Road), Lavent Estate,
Lot A (Road) and Lot B; East: Road, Lot A (Road) and Lavent
Estate; South: Lavent Estate and Road; West: Road, Lot D
(Road) and Lot F.
04. Lot D – Name of the Land: Not indicated; Extent:
0.1301 Hectare.
North: Lavent Estate; East: Lavent Estate and Lot C (Road);
South: Lot C (Road), Lot F, Lot E (Road); W est: Lavent Estate.
05. Lot E – Name of the Land: Not indicated; Extent:
0.0189 Hectare.
North: Lot D (Road); East: Lot F; South: Lot F; West: Lot F.
06. Lot F – Name of the Land: Lavent Estate; Extent:
1.1356 Hectares.
North: Lot D (Road) and Lot E (Road); East: Lot D (Road)
and Lot C (Road); South: Lot C (Road); West: Lot D (Road).
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Yatiyantota Divisional Secretariat on
27.12.2018 at 11.00 a.m. Further, you are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 21.12.2018
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and details of computation of
such amount.
