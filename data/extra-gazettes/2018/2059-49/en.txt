THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An  allotment of land in total extent of about 0.7669 Hectare, depicted
as Lot ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing No. MR/DVN/2017/255 of 22.09.
2017 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys Matara District

Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Devinuwara in
Matara District of the Southern Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Paragahadeniyewatta, Hettigamagewaththa,
Paragahahenawatta and Paragahadeniyahena – Extent:  0.7669
Hectare
