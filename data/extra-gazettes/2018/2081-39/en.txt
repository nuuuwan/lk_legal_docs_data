FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF    DEHIWALA - MOUNT  LA VINIYA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL    UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Dehiwala - Mount Laviniya Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party  to which
Hapange Ranjan De Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that 25 Rathmalana West, if Dehiwala - Mount Laviniya Municipal Council  who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Rambukkana Maggonage Asanga Padmal Perera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of   Dehiwala - Mount Laviniya
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Rambukkana Maggonage Asanga Padmal Perera was elected as the member of the 25 Rathmalana West
of Dehiwala - Mount Laviniya Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mount Laviniya Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
25th July, 2018.
