CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me by Article 44 of the Constitution
of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2096/18 of 05th November, 2018
and No. 2096/26 of 07 th November, 2018, is amended with effect from 09th November, 2018 in the manner set out in the
Schedule below :
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
November 09, 2018.
SCHEDULE
(1) ( a) In the Notification No. 2096/18, by omitting the Heading "No. (1) State Minister of National Unity,
Co-existence & Muslim Religious Affairs" and the items listed in Columns I, II and III under that Heading;
(b) In that Notification, by omitting the Heading "No. (2) State Minister of Youth, Women & Child Affairs" and the
items listed in Columns I, II and III under that Heading;
(c) In that Notification, by omitting the Heading "No. (3) State Minister of Mass Media & Digital Infrastructure"
and the items listed in Columns I, II and III under that Heading;
(2) ( a) In the Notification No. 2096/26, by omitting the Heading "No. (1) State Minister of Petroleum Resources
Development" and the items listed in Columns I, II and III under that Heading;
