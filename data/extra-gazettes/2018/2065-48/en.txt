THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Cylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts. No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Desputes (Special Provisions)
Act No. 37 of 1968 dated 17/04/2007 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinar y No.
1494/26 dated 27/04/2007 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated
22/03/2007.
Inter Company Employee's Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Talangama of the one Part.
and
(1) Chevron Lubricants Lanka PLC, (formerly Caltex Lubricants Lanka Limited), P.O.Box 06, Kolonnawa. and
(2) Mr. N. H. A. Somapala, No. 114A, Pahala Biyanwila, Kadawatha of the other Part.
Was referred to Mr. B. Manawadu for settlement by arbitration and whereas it is now deemed expendient that the said order be revoked, I,
W.D.J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamuwa Development do hereby revoke the said order and
further make order that no proceeding be taken upon the said Order dated 17/04/2007.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development.
