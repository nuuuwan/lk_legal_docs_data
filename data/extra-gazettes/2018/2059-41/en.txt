THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Nine (09) allotments of land depicted and in  Advance Tracing No.
GA/GFG/2017/821 dated 18.09.2017, prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the Village called Dangedara in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Gall Four Gravets in Galle District of Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' – Appaladoowa – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North: Appaladoowa claimed by S. W. Senanayake and Moragoda
Ela; East: Moragoda Ela and Appaladoowa cross Canal; South:
Appaladoowa cross Canal, Lot B and Road; West: Appaladoowa
claimed by S. W. Senanayake.
02. Lot  'B' – Extent: 0.0002 Hectare
North: Road, Appaladoowa claimed by S. W. Senanayake and Lot
A; East: Appaladoowa cross Canal; South: Appaladoowa cross Canal
and Lot C; West: Mahadoowawatta of which the claimant could not
be traced and Road.
03. Lot  'C' – Mahadoowawatta – Extent: 0.0015 Hectare

Appaladoowa cross Canal and Mahadoowawatta climed by H. K.
