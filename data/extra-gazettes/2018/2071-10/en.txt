THE  REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  ACT,  No.  44 OF  1980
IN pursuance of Section 12(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, it is hereby notified that the revision
of registers of electors for the year 2018 commences on May 15, 2018.

03. Every person qualified to be an elector in any  Electoral District should inspect the reigster of electors to
ascertain whether his/her name is included in the register.
04. From 15.05.2018 the enumerators will distribute enumeration forms to chief occupants of each household
to register the names  of the persons who are qualified to be electors for the year 2018. Collection of enumeration forms
dully filled by the chief occupants will commence from 01st of June 2018. The Election Commission requests every
chief occupant to give the assistance to enumerator by furnishing without delay, the essential and accurate information of
all the persons who are qualified to be registered as electors in his/her household.
