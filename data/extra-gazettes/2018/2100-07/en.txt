THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. CO/
DSO/2015/752 and dated 23.11.2015, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated  in the Village of Narahenpita in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Gothamipura in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Thimbirigasyaya of Colombo District in Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Assmt. No. 151/3, Sri Jayawardanapura Mawatha
– Extent: 0.0347 Hectare
North: Land of which Assmt. No. is not found; East: Land of
which Assmt. No. is not found and land bearing Assmt. No. 155/2;
South: Lands bearing Assmt. Nos. 155/2 and 151/3; West: Land
bearing Assmt. No. 151/3, Road and land of which Assmt. No. is not
found.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
