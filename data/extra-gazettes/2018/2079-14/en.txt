EXTRAORDINARY
HAMBANTOTA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
lmposing  Entertainment  Taxes - 2018
IT is hereby notified that, in  Order to by virtue of  Powers vested by Section 2 Sub Section (2) of  Entertainment Tax Ordinance
read with  Section 2 Sub Section (1) Paragraph (a) of  Provincial Councils (Consequential Provision) Act, No. 12 of  1989, I,
Gusthigngnawadu Shan Wijayalal de Silva, Minster of  Local  Government of Southern Province has resolved the suggestion
as shown below passed under Resolution No. 6.1 in the Meeting of Sabha held by the Hambantota Pradeshiya Sabha in the
Hambantota District on 24.07.2015 in Order to virtue of powers vested by Section 2 Sub Section (1) of  Entertainment  Tax
Ordinance (Chapter 267).

RESOLUTION
Any entertainment show activity by Entertainment  Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267), the same Entertainment show
activity conducted within the  Authorized Area of  Hambantota Pradeshiya Sabha by any person-
(a) In the matter of any entertainment show should be paid 10 percent (10%) of the Amount recovered
from persons entering for it.
Hambantota  Pradeshiya Sabha has resolved that, in Order to by virtue of powers vested in Institutions of
Local Governments by Section 2 aforesaid Ordinance, Entertainment Tax Should be imposed and aforesaid
Entertainment Tax Should be paid to the Hambantota Pradeshiya Sabha before the date to be held aforesaid
entertainment show activity.
