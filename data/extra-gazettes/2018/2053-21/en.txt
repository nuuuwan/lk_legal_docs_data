EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise  Notification  No. 01/2018
BUSINESS   HOURS   FOR   LIQUOR   LICENSED  ESTABLISHMENTS
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section (1) of Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from
time to time, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do by this Order declare that business hours
of the liquor licensed establishments set out in the Schedule hereto shall come into force with effect from 11 January,
2018.
The Excise Notification No. 675 published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 88/7 dated 14 May 1980 is hereby rescinded.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
09 January 2018.
