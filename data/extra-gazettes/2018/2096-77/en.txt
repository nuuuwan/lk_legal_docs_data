No. 2096/77 –  FRIDAY, NOVEMBER,  09,  2018 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
(Notice Under Section 07) 
 
Ref No.: MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/50 
Land Ministry No.: 4-3/6/2017/HW/220. 
 
 
IT is hereby give notice under sub section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition amendment Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intended to acquire the following land or 
servitude which is required for public purpose:- 
 
      Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Below mentioned land depicted as lot No. 1 and 2 in plan 
No. P.P./MR/2744 situated in the village of Malimbada in 
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division in Matara 
District and in Southern Province was acquired for the  
re-establishment of the Southern Expressway Project 
(Southern part extension). 
     
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Nugadugodawaththa, Nugadugoda 
alias Nugaduwa; Description of the Land : Home graden, 
having multi crop plantation; Initial Claimant of the 
Land: Government; Extent : 0.6805 Hectare.   
02. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Nugadugodawaththa, Nugadugoda 
alias Nugaduwa; Description of the Land : Home graden, 
having multi crop plantation; Initial Claimant of the 
Land: Government; Extent : 0.0062 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Malimbada 
on the 10th day of December 2018 at 10.30 am and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 03rd day of December 2018 the nature of 
their interests  in the land, the particulars  of there claim 
for  compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details  of 
the computation of such amount. 
    
 DEEPIKA K. GUNARATNA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Malimbada 
 
 On the 05 day of November, 2018, 
At the Malimbada Divisional Secretariat.
