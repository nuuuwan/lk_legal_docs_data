1A  - PG 4202 — 207 (09/2018)
Direction No. 67.
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS    AUTHORITY    ACT,   No.    09    OF  2003
Direction under Section 12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority directs that no trader, caterer, supplier of processed food and/or any other person/persons shall engage in
the business of catering or other similar or connected activity/ activities unless they obtain a Good Manufacturing Practices
(GMP) certification based on the Sri Lanka Standard Code of Hygenic Practice for Catering Establishments (SLS 956)
published by Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI).
Provided however, that traders, caterers, suppliers of processed food and/or other persons engaged in the business
of catering who possess a HACCP, FSSC 22000 or ISO 22000 certificate from an accredited certification body shall be
exempted from obtaining the above GMP certification from the Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI)
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Direction, rescind Direction,  No. 56 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1918/19 dated 11th June 2015.
This Direction shall become operative with effect from 01st March, 2019.
                                               By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,

NURA B. MEDDEGODA,
