FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(1 a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of Member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And Whereas the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Bombuwala Dewage Sujatha Dharmapriya the
Member elected to Ward No. 01, Mirihana South of Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under Section
66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 01 Mirihana South of Maharagama Urban
Council  who belongs to  such Independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a period of
thirty (30) days ;
And Whereas the Leader of  aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Hewa Gajaman Paththinilage Chithrani Sugathapala to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, returning officer of Maharagama Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Hewa Gajaman Paththinilage Chithrani Sugathapala was elected as the Member of the Ward No. 01 Mirihana South of
Maharagama Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
04th June, 2018.
