THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 2 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of   Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-Charge of the subject of  urban  Development, being of the opinion that the land specified in the Schedule
below is urgently required for the purpose of allocating the land for religious places which were relocated by Mihidusenpura
Housing Project Stage II, do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such  purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
05th April 2018.
