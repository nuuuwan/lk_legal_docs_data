THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land  described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of  land in total extent of about 0.1012 hecatre
depicted in Advance Tracing No. B/HLE/2017/64 and dated
30.06.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
of Mahawela in 56 D Neludanda Grama Niladhari Division of Deivini
Korale in Hali Ela Divisional Secretary's Division in Badulla District
in Uva Province and bounded as  follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Deendland Watte, Ettampitiya Watte – Extent:
0.1012 Hectare
North: Road and Lot No. 303 in F. V . P. 108; East and South: Lot
No. 303 in F. V. P . 108; West: Lot No. 303 in F. V. P . 108 and Road.
