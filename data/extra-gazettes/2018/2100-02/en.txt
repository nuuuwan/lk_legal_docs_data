SPECIAL COMMODITY LEVY ACT, NO. 48 OF 2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda 
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order 
impose in respect of the list of commodities speciﬁ ed in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at the 
rates speciﬁ ed in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
   This Order shall be valid for a period of 03 Months commencing from December 03, 2018 subject to provisions of 
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
 M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,         
 Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
02nd December, 2018.
