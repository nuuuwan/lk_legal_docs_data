EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise  Notification
AMENDMENT   OF   REGULATIONS   ON   NON   POTABLE   SPIRITS   IMPORTED   INTO   SRI LANKA
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of  the subject  of Finance, by virtue of  the
powers vested in him by Section 2, 12, 22 and 25, read together the Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as
amended, and published as the Excise Notification No. 10/ 2018 in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2075/ 23 dated June
13, 2018, in order to amend the Excise Notification No. 07/ 2018, published in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2059/ 32
dated February 21, 2018, with the view to update and simplify the rules applicable on importation of non-potable spirits,
was passed by parliament on September 20, 2018.
S. R. A
TTYGALLE,
Secretary,
Ministry of  Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry  of  Finance and Economic Affairs
Colombo 01,
November  15, 2018.
