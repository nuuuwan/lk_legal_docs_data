THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Order under Section 49 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by Section 49 A of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) read with Article 44 of
the Constitution, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade of  the Democratic
Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka,  do by this order, declare that the land specified in the Schedule hereto is required by the
Board of Investment of Sri Lanka being a public corporation established by the Board of investment of Sri Lanka Law,
No. 4 of 1978.
MALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
05th  September, 2018.
SCHEDULE
The allotment of Land from Lot   A in ‘‘Aluwinnekubura’’ and  ‘‘Kideismaththa’’ depicted in Plan No. 14042A
dated 19.10.2017 made by J. P. N. Jayasundara, Registered Licensed Surveyor, which is 1.6617 ha. (4A. 00R. 17 P.) in
extent and situated at Walgama in Biyagama Divisional Secretariat Division in Adikari Pattu of Siyane Korale in Gampaha
District of Western Province of which the boundaries are as follows :-
Lot No. A
North by : BOI Land (Lots 83, 81 and  80 in PP. GAM 116)  ;
East by : BOI Land (Lots 81, 80 and  79 in PP. GAM 116)  ;
South by : Road, BOI Land (Balance portion of Lots 85 and 84 in PP. GAM 116) and Land of
Dayna Wash (Pvt) Ltd ;
West by : BOI Land (Balance portion of Lot 85, Lot 84 in PP. GAM 116, Lot B in Plan No. 14042
and  Land of Dayna Wash (Pvt) Ltd.
Extent - 1.6617 ha. (4A. 00R. 17.00P.).
