PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made by  the  Governor of the Uva Province
IT is hereby notified that I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor of Uva Province, do hereby announce that, The Chief Minister Hon.
Don Arthur Chamara Sampath Dassanayake of Uva Province, resigned the post of subject Minister of Education of the Uva
Province from the date 21st January 2018, who was appointed by me as a Minster of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace,
Education, Local Government, Power and Energy, Construction, Rural Infrastructure facilities and Land of the Uva Province
from the date 09th December, 2015 by virtue of powers vested to me under Section 154F(5) of the 13th Amendment to
the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
M. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province,
At office of the Uva Governor,
Badulla,
21st January 2018.
