No. 2086/30 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 30, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GA/
BNT/2017/158 and dated 08.07.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Villages of Angagoda and Pahurumulla in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Bentota in Galle District in
Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Baliwala Kumbura – Extent: 1.4907 Hectare
North: Wekanda Addara Ovita claimed by Athula Dayasiri
Amarasekara, Hendrickgewatta claimed by Ranjith Vandabona
and Dhanusha Wijesekara and Jambugahawatta claimed by State;
East: Jambugahawatta claimed by State, Welewatta claimed by
Diddenipotha Matharage Henri and others, Suwarnapaya and
Jandipedigewatta claimed by Bernard and others; South:
Suwarnapaya, Jandipedigewatta and Stream claimed by Bernard and
others; West: Stream.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
