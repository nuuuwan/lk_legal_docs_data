       THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0024 Hectare depicted
as Lot ‘A’ in the Advanced Tracing No. Ya/JFF/2016/215 dated
19.08.2016 prepared by the Surveyor- General, situated in the Village
called Chundikuli, Word No. 04 within the Municipal Council Limited
of Jaffna in the Grama Niladhari Division No. J/74 Jaffna Town
East  in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Jaffna in Jaffna District
of Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Thevareer Kulam - Extent: About 0.0024 Hectare
North, East, South and West: Thevareer Kulam Property of Jaffna
Municipal Council.
