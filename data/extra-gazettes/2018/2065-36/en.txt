EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                            My No.: Bala/2/1/4/11. 
           Land Ministry’s No.: LD/05/2001/PC/384. 
 
I hereby  give  notice under Sub - section (1) of the Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ servitude* 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
   The Land called Maradanawatta extent about 0.0017 
Hectare depicted as Lots 01 and 02 in the Surveyor 
General’s Plan of P.P.G. 4292 of 14.11.2017 situated in 
88 A Kandegoda Grama Niladhari Division in Balapitiya 
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Galle District. 
 
Boundaries as follows:   
01.  Lot No. 01– Maradanawatta - 0.0016 Hectare 
 
North: Maradanawatta claimed by L.A.P.K. De Silva; East: 
Maradanawatta claimed by L.A.P.K. De Silva; South: 
Kandegoda Devata Road; West: Kandegoda Devata Road. 
 
02.  Lot No. 02 - Maradanawatta - 0.0001 Hactare 
 North: Kandegoda Devata Road; East: Kandegoda Devata 
Road; South: Maradanawatta claimed by W. Janak Wasantha; 
West: Maradanawatta  claimed by W. Janak Wasantha. 
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of  
Balapitiya on the 11 day of  July 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 04 day of July 2018 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ 
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation  of such amount. 
 
  
 B.P.S. ANURADHI, 
 Divisional Secretary , 
 Balapitiya. 
 
03th  April,  2018.
