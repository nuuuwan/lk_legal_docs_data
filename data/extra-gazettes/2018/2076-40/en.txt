ANTIQUITIES  ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sub-section 39“a” of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) as amended by the
Act, No. 24 of 1988, I, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, do, by this Order, appoint the members referred
to in the Schedule here to the Advisory Committee on Archaeology as effective for a period the 01st of June 2018 to 30th
of May 2020.
Dr. W
IJEYADASA  R AJAPAKSHA,
President's Counsel,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Ministry of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs,
17th May 2018.
