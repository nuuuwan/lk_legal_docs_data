 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in  Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1603/35 of May 28,
2009  (as last amended, by Order published in  Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2045/17 of  November 15, 2017), which
specifies the Faculties  and  the  “Department of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by
virtue of  the provisions of  Section 139 of  the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said
Act, by the addition immediately after the entry ‘‘ 13. Department of  Philosophy and Psychology” appearing in
Column II,   of the  Schedule which corresponds to the entry ‘‘Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences’’ appearing in
Column I  of the Schedule of  the  following entry :-
“14. Department of English Language Teaching”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
04th January 2018.
