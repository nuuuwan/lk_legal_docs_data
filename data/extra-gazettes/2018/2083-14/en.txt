I hereby give notice, under sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, That the government intends to 
acquire the following lands which  are required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land described below in total extent of 
0.0894 Hectare, shown as 1 in the preliminary plan dated 
13.06.2018 for Gam 4074 prepared by the Surveyor 
General situated in village of Palliyawatte, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wattala in the District 
of Gampaha, in Western Province and bounded as 
follows. 
     
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Palliyawatte; Description : Building 
with Land; Claimant: K.B.L.A. Alwis and K.B.J.P. 
Jayawardane, No.204, Palliyawatte; Original claimant: -; 
Extent : 0.0894 Hectare.  
  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Wattala on the 31st Day of October 2018 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 31st Day of  October 2018 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of  compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
