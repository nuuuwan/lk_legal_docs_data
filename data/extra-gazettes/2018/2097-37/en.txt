EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
BUSINESS   HOURS   FOR   LIQUOR   LICENSED  ESTABLISHMENTS
IT  is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in Charge of the subject of Finance, by virtue of the
powers vested in him by Section (1) of Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, and published as the
Excise Notificaton No. 03/2018 in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2054/41 of January 18, 2018, in order to amend the
Excise Notification No. 686 published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 126/3 dated February 02, 1981, to declare that
business hours of the liquor licensed establishments set out in the Schedule hereto shall come into force with effect from
18 January 2018, was passed by Parliament on June 07, 2018.
S. R. ATTYGALLE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affaris.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
November 15, 2018.
