Regulations
1. The Motor Traffic (Fees) Regulations 2011, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1722/27 of September 9, 2011,
as amended by regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1796/22 of February 8, 2013 are hereby further
amended as follows :
(1) in regulation 3 thereof, by the repeal of items (i), (ii), (iii) and (iv) of paragraph (a) of such regulation and the
substitution therefor of the following :-
“(i) importation of the motor vehicle ;
(ii) purchase of motor vehicle manufactured or assembled or manufactured and assembled locally, as the
case may be, of such motor vehicle ; or
(iii) auction of the unregistered motor vehicle belonging to the State or to a Semi-Government Organization
or to the Sri Lanka Customs,” ;
(2) in regulation 6 thereof, by the repeal of paragraphs (2), (3) and (4) of such regulation and substitution therefor
of the following :-

(3) Where an application for registration is not submitted within one hundred and eighty days of the change of
possession of a motor vehicle upon the death of the registered owner of the vehicle, an additional delay charge for each
foregone day shall be charged along with the fee payable. The payable additional delay charge for each forgone day for the
categories specified in Column I of Schedule IIIA to these regulations shall be specified in corresponding entry of Column
II of that Schedule. However, the maximum additional delay charge shall not exceed the charge specified in the corresponding
entry of Column III of that Schedule.
Provided however, an application for registration is submitted within thirty days of a courts order consequence
to due testimonial proceedings of the deceased registered owner no additional delay charge shall be charged. An application
for registration is submitted after thirty days of such court order, an additional delay charge for  each foregone day shall
be charged along with the fee payable. The payable additional delay charge for each forgone day for the categories specified
in Column I of Schedule IIIA to these regulations shall be as specified in corresponding entry of Column II of that
schedule. However, the maximum additional delay charge shall not exceed the charge specified in the corresponding entry
of Column III of that Schedule.
(4) Upon the sale of a vehicle belonging to the State or to a Semi-Governmental Organization or to the the Sri
Lanka Customs an application for registration shall be submitted within one hundred and eighty days of such sale. An
application for registration is submitted after one hundred and eighty days of such sale, an additional delay charge for each
foregone day shall be charged along with the fee payable. The payable additional delay charge for each forgone day for the
categories specified in Column I of Schedule IIIA to these regulations shall be as specified in corresponding entry of
Column II of that Schedule. However, the maximum additional delay charge shall not exceed the charge specified in the
corresponding entry of Column III of that Schedule.”.
