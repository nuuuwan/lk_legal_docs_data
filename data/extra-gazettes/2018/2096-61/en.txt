1A- G 28917 — 372  (11/2018)
THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Article 43 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2096/17 of
05th November, 2018 is amended with effect from November 09, 2018 in the manner set out in the Schedule below :-
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 09, 2018.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the Heading (1) Minister of Defence of the said notification, as follows :
(a) In Column I thereof, by the insertion immediately after Item 20 thereof, of the following item,
21. Matters relating to the Government printing and publications
(b) In Column I thereof, by re-numbering the items 21 and 22
(c) In Column II thereof, by the insertion immediately after Item 17 thereof, of the following item,
18. Department of Government Printing
PRINTED A T THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
