I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of  Section 07 of
the  Land Acquisition  Act, that and the government intends
to acquire the that following land which is required for public

 Description of the land to be  Acquired:
An allotment of land No. 01 called Ambadeniya watta
Welikanda Part (Part 97), 1.0117 Hectares in entire extent
mentioned in the plan No. M.P .K. 3601 and dated 2018.06.20
prepared by the Senior Suryeyor of Survey Authority
(Kegalle), Situated at Attanagoda village in No. 15B Grama

Secretary Division of Mawanella, in Kegalle District in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Ambadeniyawatta Welikanda part (Part
97); Owners: 1. Management Director, Kegalle Plantation
Company, No. 310, Navinna, Maharagama, 2. Director, Land

Mawatha, Colombo 7.; Extent: 1.0117 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which  a

personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divitional Secretariat on 31st October 2018 at 10.00 a.m.
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writting (In
duplicates)on or before 23rd October 2018  the nature of their
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude,
the amount of compensation and the details of computation
of such amount.
M.M.P. PRIYANGANI PETHANGODA,
