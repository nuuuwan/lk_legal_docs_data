I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act (Chapter 460), as amended by the
Land Acquisition  (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, was given
by me that the Government intends to acquire the following
land for catchment of Hedeniya water supply system.
     Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired:

the preliminary plan No: Maha/6708 prepared by the Kandy
District Surveyor General situated at Bogala village of
Siyapaththuwa Minor division at Harispaththuwa Divisional
Secretary's Division of Kandy District in the Central Province.

All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are required to
appear before me personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, at  Divisional Secretary's Office, Harispaththuwa on
the 26 .03. 2018 at 10 .00 a.m. and  hereby requested notify me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26.03.2018, the nature
of their interests in the land and the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the aforesaid land the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
