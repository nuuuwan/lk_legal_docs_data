 Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005
By virture of the powers vested in me by the Section 70 of the Tourism Act No. 38 of 2005 I, John An tony Emmanual
Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs, do hereby order in line with the Section 49
(a) of the Land Acquisitions Act, that the land mentioned in the schedule below is required to be acquired for the purpose of
development of tourism by Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
Schedule
The land 11.8652 Hectares (29 Acre, 1 Rood, 9.11 perch.) in extent depicted as lots A, B, G , H, of Surveyor General's Tracing

Divisional Secretary's Division in Galle District of Southern Province.
North : Katukurunda Watta of Robolgoda Village, Alawathogoda Village; East :  Alawathogoda Village, Alwis Mawatha;South:
Bandarawatta, Lunukerawa, Lunukerawayaya of Warahena Village; West:  Colombo -Galle Highway
