CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD   ACT,   No.  17   OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Power, Energy & Business Development, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of the land required
by the Ceylon Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto.
