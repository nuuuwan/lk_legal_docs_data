THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 07
My Reference No. : RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/15/(CEB).
Land Ministry Reference No. : 4-3/2/2016/CEB/401.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section (7)
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose:
Description of the Lands to be acquired:
Following allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 377 in
Plan No. F.V .P . 377 made by Survey General, situated in
Nugepola Village in 164 Gallella Grama Niladhari Division
of Uda Paththuwa sub division in Divisional Secretary's
Division Rathnapura in the District of Rathnapura in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot No.  377
Name of the Land: Galaboda; Land Use: Open barren
land; Claimant: Hapugasthenna Plantation, No. 95/A,
Nambapana, Ingiriya; Extent: 1.4489 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which ser-
vitude is to be acquired are here by requested to appear per-
sonally, or by agents duly  authorized in writing, before me at
10.00 a.m. on 28th February 2019 at Divisional Secretariat
Rathnapura.
Moreover, they are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing with two duplicates on or before 21st February 2019
the nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
Computation of such amount.
I.R.M. PRADEEPIKA D. ILLUKKUMBURA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rathnapura.
At Divisional Secretariat,
Rathnapura.
12th December, 2018.
