THE AW ARD
The Honourable Minister of Labour Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamu Development do by virtue of the powers
vested in him by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legistative Enactments of Ceylon (1956
Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 62 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes –
Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 appointed me to be the Arbitrator by his order dated 10th November 2017 and referred
the following disputes to me for settlement by Arbitration.
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties are:–
Whether Mr. Y . M. Punchibanda who has served as a bus driver of the Teldeniya Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport
Board, had been caused injustice by termination of his services with effect from 23.02.2008, the date from which his services
had been temporarily suspended, through letter No. Sri Langama/Masaka/Kam.saba/podu/dated 01.10.2015 of the Sri Lanka
Transport Board and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
Appearance:
Mr. Y . M. Herath Bandara Representative for the Party of the First Part
Ms. Nimashja Vithana Attorney-at-Law for the Party of the Second Part
Both parties appeared on 24.04.2018, Party of the Second Part has filed the 1st Statement under Regulations 21(1) and
21(2) of the Industrial Disputes Regulations of 1958 as amended.
Thereafter the Applicant neither appeared on 22.05.2018, 17.07.2018 and 07.09.2018 nor filed his 1st Statement.
Therefore the case was dismissed.
In the circumstances I make no award.
K
APILA M. SARATHCHANDRA,
19th September, 2018. Arbitrator.
