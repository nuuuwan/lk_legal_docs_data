THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW,  No. 41 OF 1978
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978, I, Patali
Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, being of the opinion that the areas specified in
Columns I excluding the already declared areas mentioned under Schedule I, Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha Area, Schedule
II, Mawathagama Pradeshiya Sabha Area, Schedule III, Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha Area in Column I respectively hereto,
having the metes and bounds specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedules hereto are suitable for
development, do by this order declare such areas to be an urban development area.
P
ATALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (MP),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,
17th & 18th Floor,
“Suhurupaya”
Sri Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
06th April 2018.
