EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT  is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in Charge of the subject of Finance, by virtue of the
powers vested in him by Section  32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, and published as the Excise
Notificaton No. 11/2018 in the Extraordinary  Gazette No. 2075/24 of June 13, 2018, in order to amend the Excise
Notification No. 03/2018, published in the  Extraordinary Gazette  No. 2054/41dated January 18, 2018 to revise the
opening and closing hours of Toddy Taverns, specified in the Item No. 02 of Schedule of that Notification by substituting
the opening and closing hours specified in the Schedule below with effect from June 13, 2018, was passed by Parliament
on September 20, 2018.
S. R. ATTYGALLE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affaris.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
November 15, 2018.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
License Type Opening and closing Hours
(i) Toddy Taverns From 11.00 a. m. to 2.00 p. m. and
From 5.00 p. m. to 8.00 p. m.
(ii) T oddy Taverns (Operated by the Co-operative System) From 8.00 a. m. to 9.00 p. m.
02. Definition : “Under the Co-operative System”  means the Cooperative Societies and Clusters registered by
the Provincial Department of Cooperative Development.
