MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE  IN  CRIMINAL  MATTERS  ACT,  No.  25  of  2002
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters Act, No. 
25 of 2002, I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Order declare that the provisions of the 
aforesaid Act, shall apply in respect of any country that is -
(a) a party to -
 (i) the United Nations Convention against corruption speci ﬁ ed in the Schedule I hereto ; and
 (ii) the Council of Europe Convention on Cybercrime speci ﬁ ed in the Schedule II hereto ;
(b) a member of the Commonwealth speciﬁ ed in the Schedule III hereto.
 The Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1297/1 of July 14, 2003, is hereby rescinded without 
prejudice to anything previously lawfully done thereunder. 
 T
HALATHA ATUKORALE, 
 Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo, 
10th September, 2018.
