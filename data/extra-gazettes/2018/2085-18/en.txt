1A   G   28336 —17  (08/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  POLONNARUWA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending consecutive
meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Meregngna Lionel Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon  returned member if Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not disqualified  under
Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Meregngna Lionel Silva to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Leelanath Wikrama Arachchi, returning officer of the Polonnaruwa Municipal Council , acting
in terms of selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Meregngna
Lionel Silva was selected as the member of the Polonnaruwa Municipal Council.
L
EELANA TH WIKRAMA ARACHCHI,
Returning Officer,
Polonnaruwa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
