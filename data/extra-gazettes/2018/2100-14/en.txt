No. 2100/14 – THURSDAY, DECEMBER 06, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is required
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MR/
ART/2016/263 dated 02.03.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Athuraliya in the Minor Division
of Weligam Korale North in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Athuraliya in Matara District, Southern Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ambalangodella – Extent: 0.0152 Hectare
North: Ambalangodella claimed by Athuraliya Sri Niwasarama
Temple; East: Ambalangodella claimed by Athuraliya Sri Niwasarama
Temple and Road; South: Road, Ambalangodella claimed by
Athuraliya Sri Niwasarama Temple and Road (Provincial R.D.A.);
West: Ambalangodella claimed by Athuraliya Sri Niwasarama Temple
and Road (Provincial R.D.A.).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2016/HA/88.
