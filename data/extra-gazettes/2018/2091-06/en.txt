THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa Rajapaksa,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35  of May 28, 2009    (as last amended
by Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 2079/11 of  July 09, 2018),  which specifies the Faculties and  Departments
of the University of  Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the
aforesaid Act, to be a University  established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after the entry
‘‘10. Department of  Sports Science’’ appearing in Column II of  the Schedule which corresponds to the item ‘‘ Faculty of
Applied Sciences’’ appearing in  Column I of that Schedule, of  the following new  entry :-
‘‘11. Department of  Polymer Science’’.
Dr. W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
28th  September 2018.
