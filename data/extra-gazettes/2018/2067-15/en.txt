BY virtue the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I Kabir Hashim, the Minister of
Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of the Colombo
International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 17745, do by this Order, amend the shedule to the Order made under
the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2053/39 of January 11, 2018 by the addition immediately after the
entry “Bachelor of Education Honours in physical Sciences” appearing in column I and it’s corresponding entry in column
II respectively of the Shedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in schedule I hereto:-
KABIR HASHIM,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo 01,
18th  April , 2018.
SCHEDULE  I
CONDITIONS
Colombo International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, shall, when admitting stud ents for the degree
programmes specified in schedule II hereto in respect of:
(a) Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Mechanical Engineering, Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering
in Automotive Engineering, Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Electronic and Telecommunic ation
Engineering and Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Civil Engineering, adhere to minimum entry qualification
of three (03) ‘S’ passes in Physical Science stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advance Level) Examination
or equivalent.
