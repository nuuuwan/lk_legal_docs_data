SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Eran
Wickramaratne, Acting Minister of Finance and Mass Media the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this
order impose in respect of list of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity
Levy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from September 08,  2018, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
E
RAN WICKRAMARATNE,
Acting Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
07th September, 2018.
