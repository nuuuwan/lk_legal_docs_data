PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Western Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Western Province Provincial Council by reason of Resignation ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the United Progressive Alliance, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Stanley Anthony
Sanmugam to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Stanley Anthony Sanmugam, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the
Administrative District of Colombo in respect of which such vacancy occured.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA N. J. ABEYSEKERE S. RATNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE
Chairman Member Member
