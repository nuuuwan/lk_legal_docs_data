THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of land called ‘‘Kandehena’’ in  extent of about
0.0766 Hectare depicted in Advanced Tracing No. KE/GLG/2016/
253 and dated 25.05.2017, prepared by Surveyor General situated in
the Villages of Kobbewela in the Grama Niladhari Division of
No. 71, Kobbewela in the Kandupita Pattuwa – North Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Galigamuwa in Kegalle
District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as  follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kandehena – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North: Road and Kandehena; East and South: Kandehena; West:
Road.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kandehena – Extent: 0.0759 Hectare
North and East : Kandehena; South: Sirimala Weda’s Land; West:
Kandehena.
