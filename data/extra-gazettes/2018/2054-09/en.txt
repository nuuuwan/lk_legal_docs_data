Regulations
01    These regulations may be cited as the spot fine Regulations, No 01 0f 2018.
2. Where  a police officer makes a detection in respect of an offence set out in Column I pertaining to the
relevant provisions in Column II of the Second Schedule to this Act, and where the person involved admits liability for
such offence, such person may, in lieu of prosecution for such offence, pay the spot fine as set out in the corresponding
Column III of the following Schedule, in the manner provided in the relevant section of the Act.
