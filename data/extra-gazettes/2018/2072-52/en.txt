FINANCE  ACT,  No.  11 OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order vest in the People's Bank established under the
People's Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from, 25th May Two Thousand and Eighteen, the properties described in
the Schedule hereto.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Colombo,
May 25, 2018.
SCHEDULE
All that divided and defined allotment of land marked as Lot 4-1B1 called as “Kanaththewatta and Delgahawatta”
depicted in the Plan No. 887 and dated 13.10.2007 made by Licensed Surveyor B. K. S. Bamunusingha situated in Gramasewa
Division of Kalalgoda No. 493, Divisional Secretary's Division of Maharagama in Palle Pattu in Hewagam Korale in the
District of Colombo in Western Province and bounded on the North by Kanaththewatta, on the East by road, on the South
by Lot No. 4-1B2, on the West by Lot No. 4-1A depicted in the Plan No. 255/303 containing in extent of ten perches
(00 A., 00 R., 10 P.) together with soil, trees, everything standing thereon and the right to use the roadways.
This land is registered in folio G-1657/245, 915/22 in the Land Registry, Homagama.
