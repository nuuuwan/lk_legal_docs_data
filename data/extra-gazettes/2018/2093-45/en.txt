THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My Ref. No. GCF/A TH/NG/08/LR.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. 03/J91/LRB/91.
The General Public is hereby notified that the compensation
payments in respect of allotment of land described in the Schedule
below of which the titles were not proved and depicted under Lot
No. 28 in Preliminary Plan No. 7948 prepared by Surveyor-General
that has been vested in the Government through Proviso 38(a)
No. 144 of 1991 published in the Gazette Notification No. 685/6
dated 23.10.1991 under Greater Colombo Flood Control and
Environment Improvement Project, have been deposited in the
District Court, Nugegoda in terms of Section 33 of the Land
Acquisition Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotment mentioned below could obtain the compensation payable
for the allotment after submitting necessary documents to the District
Court, Nugegoda to prove their ownership.
