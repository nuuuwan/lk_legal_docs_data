The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   IBBAGAMUWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in 16 -  Hunupola of  Ibbagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National  Party, the recognized political party to which Yapa Mudiyanselage
Jagath Manjula Yapa   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon the member of that 16 - Hunupola   of   Ibbagamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within
a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate
Wijesinghe Mudiyanselage Wijekoon Banda   to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore  I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Ibbagamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wijesinghe
Mudiyanselage Wijekoon Banda was elected as the member of the 16 - Hunupola   of   Ibbagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
21st June,  2018.
