EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Amended by Land Acquisition ( Amendment) Act 
No. 28 of 1964  
 
NOTICE UNDER  SECTION 
 
        My Referance No.: 4/1/5/29 
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands and Parlimentery      
     Reforms: 4-3/9/2015/WS/332 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of the Section 
7 of Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land, which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The alloment of Land called “ Kumbalkotuwe watta” in 
total extent of about 0.0354 Hecteres, depicted as Lot No. 
153 in Cadastral Map No. 420371 Bolck No. 01 Sheet 
No. 04, dated on 10.11.2017, Surveyed and Prepared by 
the Surveyor General,  Situated at the Village of 
Gangoda, Ward No. 1 ( Within the Municipal Council 
Limits, Kurunegala), in Gangoda Grama Niladhari 
Division of No. 832, in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Kurunegala in Kurunegala District, North 
Western Province and bounded as Follows. 
 
 
01.  Lot No. 153 
Name of the Land: Kumbalkotuwe Watta; Extent of the 
Land: 0.0354 Hectares Boundaries North: Ananda 
Hamangoda Mawatha and Kumbalkotuwe Watta and Lot 
No. 154, East: Lot No. 154 and Lot No. 148; Southt: No. 
148 and 152; West: Lot No. 152 and  Ananda 
Hamangoda Mawatha; Name and Address of the 
Claimant: Sunree Advertising Services Private Limited, 
No.301, Puttalam Road, Kurunegala; Extent: 0.0354  
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly   
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s office, at Kurunegala on the 26th day of 
February 2018 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby required to 
notify to me in writing ( in duplicate) on or before the  
19 th day of  February 2018,  the nature of their interests 
in the Land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said Land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. . E. M. M. S. EKANAYAKE 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kurunegala.  
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Kurunagala.
