CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Naming of Rikillagaskada Bus Stand as ‘‘Saliya Dissanayake Bus Stand’’ under the Provincial
Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute No. 01 of  year 2000
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and Construction of
the Central Provincial Council hereby announce under the powers vested in me by sub clause ‘‘O’’ of  paragraph 19 of  the
Central Provincial Passenger Transport  Authority Statute No. 01 of  year 2000 of the Central Provincial Council, Name the
Rikillagaskada Bus Stand owned the Central Province Passenger Transport Authority by an Extra Special Gazette Notification
No. 1210/ 2 dated 12.11.2001 of  the Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as ‘‘Saliya Dissanayake Bus Stand’’
with effect from 11 June 2018.
E
DIRIWEERA  WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and
Energy, Housing and Construction,
Central Province.
