 WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
RUBBER  CULTIV ATION  AND  RAW  RUBBER  PROCESSING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated
04.06.1971 that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamuwa Development has  been pleased
to appoint the following persons to be members of the Wages Board for the Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Processing
Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance
(Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 01st  January 2018.
