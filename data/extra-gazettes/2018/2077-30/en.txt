FILLING OF A VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF MEMBER OF MAHARAGAMA URBAN COUNCIL UNDER
SECTION 66A(1) (a) OF THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Widanagamage  Nisantha Kumara the
Member appointed to Ward No. 18 Maharagama South of Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by
me under Section 66 A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No.18,
Maharagama South of Maharagama Urban Council who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02,  has communicated his decision to
nominate Nuwan Madhushanka Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I. Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare that Nuwan Madhushanka Fernando was slected as the member of the Ward No. 18, Maharagama
South of Maharagama Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
26th June, 2018.
