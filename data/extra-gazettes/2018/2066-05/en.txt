BANK  OF  CEYLON  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  397)
Order under Section 7(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of Section 7 of the Bank of Ceylon Ordinance (Chapter 397), I,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order determine that the paid-up capital of the
Bank of Ceylon shall be increased from rupees Fifteen Billion to rupees Twenty Billion.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Colombo,
05th April 2018.
