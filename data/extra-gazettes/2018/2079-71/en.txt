THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Boundaries for the allotment of land containing in extent as below
and  depicted  in the Advance Tracing No. R/KRE/2017/683 dated
24.10.2017 prepared by the Senior Superintendant of Survey for the
village called Matuwagala, in the Uda Pattu North Minor Division of
Kuruwiti Korale in the  Divisional Secretariat Division of Kiriella in
Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Matuwagala Estate – Extent: 4.4515 Hectares
North: Matuwagala Estate belongs to Matuwagala Plantation
Company Limited, Diyahonda ela and Road (P.S); East: Diyahonda
ela and Road (P .S.), Matuwagala Estate situated the Volleyball ground
and Cemetery, Aluketiye watta possessed by K. A. S. Prasanna
Gunasekara, K. G . Indra Kumara, Arthur Thalgahagoda and A. G .
Nandasena and Matuwagala Estate belongs to the State; South:
Matuwagala Estate belongs to the State, Maldenige watta possessed
by Bamunuge Don Dhanawardhana and Diyaneththe Kumbura
possessed by Kandaboda Gamaethiralalage Jayaratne; West:
Diyaneththe Kumbura possessed by Kandaboda Gamaethiralalage
Jayaratne, Diyaneththe dola, Matuwagala watta possessed by

Kahambiliya henyaya, Nawala henyaya, Kokile hena ad Matuwagala
Estate belongs to Balangoda Plantaions.
