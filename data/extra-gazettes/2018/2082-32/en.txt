THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the said Act,  published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 896/2  of November 07, 1995, and   last amended by Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary ,
No. 2056/5 of  January 29, 2018,  establishing the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately
after the item ‘‘Faculty of Technology’’  in Column I of  the Schedule and its corresponding entries in  Column II of the
Schedule of  the following item and entries :-
‘‘Faculty of  Medicine
1. Department of Anatomy
2. Department of Biochemistry
3. Department of  Physiology
4. Department of  Parasitology
5. Department of Microbiology
6. Department of  Forensic Medicine
7. Department of  Pathology
8. Department of Community Medicine
9. Department of  Family Medicine
10. Department of  Pharmacology
11. Department of Surgery
12. Department of Medicine
13. Department of  Pediatrics
14. Department of  Obstetrics and Gynaecology
15. Department of Psychiatry.’’.
Dr. W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
31st  July, 2018.
