THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 1.2141 hectares
depicted as Lot "A" in the Advance Tracing No. BT/KTT/2017/19
dated 31/03/2017, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in
Village called New Kattankudy ward No. 6 within the Urban Council
Limits of Kattankudy, in the Grama Niladhari Division No: 167/B New
Kattankudy East in the Divisional Secretary's division of Kattankudy
in Batticaloa district of Eastern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Alenodey – Extent: about 1.2141 Hectares
North: Alenody Graden claimed by U. L. Suhara Ummah and
others and Mohomed Musthafa Abdul Aleem; East: Alenody Graden
claimed by Mohomed Musthafa Abdul Aleem, Path (From Garden
to Sea Shore) and Premises of Urban Council; South: Alenody Graden
Premises of Urban Council and Garden Calimed by Abdul Raheem
Pazana and Mohomad Ibrahim Mohomed Uvais; W est: Alenody
Graden claimed by Mohomad Ibrahim Mohomed Uvais.
