CONTROL OF PESTICIDES  ACT, No. 33 OF 1980
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by Section 9  of the Control of Pesticides Act, No. 33 of  1980, I, Jayakody Arachchige
Sumith, Registrar  of Pesticides, do by this Order-
(i) declare that every pesticide in such trade Name as specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an approved
pesticide; and
(ii) Classify such pesticides as restricted pesticides.
DR J. A. SUMITH,
Registrar of Pesticides.
Peradeniya,
13th  September, 2018.
