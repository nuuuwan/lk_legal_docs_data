APPOINTMENT  OF   DISTRICT  OPIUM BOARDS  UNDER  THE  OPIUM  ORDINANCE IN  1910,  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE   POISONS, OPIUM  AND  DANGEROUS  DRUGS  ORDINANCE  No. 17  OF  1929
IN terms of the powers vested in me under the Section 42 (2) of  the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous  Drugs Ordinance No.
17 of 1929 (Chapter 549), I, Rajitha Senarathna, Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine do hereby amend
the Opium Board constituted for Puttalam District as follows :-
Accordingly, Dr. Dharmadasa Athugala,  and Dr. M. I. A. Muzajdeen who were appointed as member of the District
Opium Board in the Puttalam District by Gazette Extraordinary No. 1638/6 and dated 25.01.2010 have been replaced by
the following.
01. Dr. (Mr). P. G. Ranaweera,
Gampaha Clinic,
Kottarumulla.
02. Dr. (Mr). R. A. Premadasa Ranasinghe,
Ayurveda Clinic,
Arachchikattuwa.
DR. R AJITHA S ENARATNE,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine.
