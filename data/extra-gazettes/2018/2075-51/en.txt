EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 as amended,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that, the Order made under
Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act No. 13 of 1989 and published by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2073/41 dated May 31, 2018 is hereby rescinded effective from June 14, 2018.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
