FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   MAHARAGAMA  URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(1a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  Independent Group No. 02 to which Bombuwala Dewage Josaphin  the member
elected to Ward No. 13 Rukmale of Maharagama Urban Council  belonged was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 13, Rukmale of Maharagama Urban Council who belongs to
such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of the Local  Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Heenpellage Dona Piyumi Prasadini to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, returning officer of  Maharagama Urban
Council acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Heenpellage Dona Piyumi Prasadini was elected as the member of the Ward No. 13 Rukmale of Maharagama Urban
Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo,
21st November, 2018,

WHEREAS the office of member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02 to which Hansani Ruwanthi the member elected to
Ward No. 15  Kottawa South of Maharagama Urban Council  belonged was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 15, Kottawa South of Maharagama Urban Council who
belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under Section 8 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)

Hewa Pedige Asha Ganga Kumari to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, returning officer of the Maharagama Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Hewa Pedige Asha Ganga Kumari was elected as the member of the Ward No. 15 Kottawa South of Maharagama Urban
Council.
