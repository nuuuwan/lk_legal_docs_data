FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF   THE  MANNAR  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Aboobucker
Tharseen  the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon
returned  member if Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and  who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Aliyar  Sabeer to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Samarasinghe Appuhamilage Darsha Praveena, Returning Officer  of the Mannar Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Aliyar Sabeer was selected as the member of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A. D
ARSHA PRA VEENA,
Returning Officer,
Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mannar.
08th October, 2018.
