TOWN  AND  COUNTRY   PLANNING  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2) (b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development do by this Order :-
(a) declare that the area of land called “Rangiri Raja Maha Vihara Area” specified in the schedule hereto shall be an
urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance ; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the urban development area declared to be “Rangiri
Raja Maha Vihara Area”
PA TALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Colombo,
14th  March, 2018.
SCHEDULE
THE AREA OF LAND COMPRISING THE RANGIRI RAJA MAHA VIHARA AREA
The areas of land containing in an extent of 5.6641 Hectares (13A., 3R., 39.42P.) shown by the Survey General’s
Tracing No. H/AMB/2016/185 dated 16th May 2016 and situated in the village of Walawa, Minor Division of Magam
Paththuwa within the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ambalanthota in the Administrative District of Hambanthota in
the Southern Province and bounded as follows :—
North : The Land claimed by Piyasena Hettihewa, land claimed by K. K. Samararathna, land claimed by
Shamali Anoma de Silva and land claimed by Department of Irrigation ;
East : The remaining portion of the same land and land claimed by Goigoda Gamage Nihal Vimalasiri
South : The land claimed by Premadasa Ranaweera, land claimed by Sunil Jinadasa Munasingha and Road ;
West : Walawe River
