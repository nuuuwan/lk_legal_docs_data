The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)a  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of the Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kelaniyage Rathnapala of the Eheliyagoda
Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the
Socialist Party of Sri Lanka.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATENNE ,
Returning Officer,
Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
17th September, 2018.
