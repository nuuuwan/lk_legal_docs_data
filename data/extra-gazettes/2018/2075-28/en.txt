FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  MAHARAGAMA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Bombuwala Dewage Sarath Dharmapriya the
Member elected to Ward No. 02 Mirihana North of  Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under section
66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 02 Mirihana North of Maharagama Urban
Council who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Chamidu Lakshan Ranasinghe to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Chamidu Lakshan Ranasinghe was elected as the Member of the Ward No. 02 Mirihana North of Maharagama Urban
Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo.
13th June, 2018.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 14.06.2018
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  MAHARAGAMA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in Maharagama Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of Independent Group No. 02, to which Rohan Kumara Widanegamage the Member
elected to Ward No. 19 Maharagama North of Maharagama  Urban Council belonged, was required by me under section 66A
(1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No.19 Maharagama North of Maharagama Urban
Council who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member within an period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Goluwa Marakkalage Tony Shantha to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Goluwa Marakkalage Tony Shantha was elected as the Member of the Ward No. 19 Maharagama North of Maharagama
Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo.
13th June, 2018.
