UNIVERSITIES ACT  No. 16 OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act. No. 16 of 1978, I Kabir Hashim, the Minister of
Higher Education and highways, having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of the Sri Lanka
Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the companies Act No.
07 of  2007 and bearing registration number GL 24, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under the said Act,
and published in Gazette  Extraordinary , No. 2055/31 of January 24, 2018 by the addition immediately after the entry
''Bachelor of Education Honours in Physical Sciences'' appearing in column I and it's corresponding entry in Column II
respectively of the Schedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto:-
KABIR HASHIM,
Minister of  Higher Education and Highways.
At Colombo,
18th April,  2018.
