THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provision of that Act.
An allotments of land extent of about 0.0519 Hectare as Lot ‘A’
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Ka/MTG/17/214 and dated
27.07.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in Village of
Wellipenna in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Matugama in
District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Piburawatta – Extent: 0.0519 Hectare
North: Dikpitawatta claimed by Weerasinhage Punyakanthi and
Don Heshan Asanga Jayanetthi; East: Dikpitawatta claimed by  Don
Heshan Asanga Jayanetthi and Arumapperuma Arachchige Theja
Indrani; South: Dikpitawatta claimed by Road and Arumapperuma
Arachchige Theja Indrani; West: Dikpitawatta claimed by Road and
Weerasighage Punyakanthi.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
