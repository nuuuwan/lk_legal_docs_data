THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of the
Act.
The Allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Ma/SG/2017/146 dated of 27.06.2017 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys - Matale District on behalf of the Surveyor
General, situated in the village of Hewanathenna in the Grama Niladhari
Divisions of No. E430C, Kedangamuwa and Minor Divison of
Udugoda Pallesiya Pattuwa and in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Galewela of Matale District in Central Province and described in
below names and extents as follows.
01. Lot No. 1 – Hewanathenna Watta – Extent: 0.0375
Hectare
North: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by Samarakoon
Mudiyanselage Rathnayake and Canal; East: Lot 2; South: Lot 2,
Canal and Hewanathenna Watta claimed by Samarakoon
Mudiyanselage Rathnayake; West: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Rathnayake.
02. Lot No. 2 – Extent: 0.0111 Hectare
North: Lot 1, Hewanathenna Watta claimed by Samarakoon
Mudiyanselage Rathnayake and Canal; East: Hewanathenna Watta
claimed by State and Lot 3; South: Lot 3, Hewanathenna Watta
claimed by State and Canal; West: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Rathnayake and Lot 1.
03. Lot No. 3 – Hewanathenna Watta – Extent: 0.9949
Hectare
North: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by State; East: Hewanathenna
Watta claimed by State; South: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by
Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage Sunil, Lot 4 and Kurunegala District;
West: Kurunegala District, Hewanathenna Watta claimed by State,
Canal, Lot 2 and Canal.
04. Lot No. 4 – Hewanathenna Watta – Extent: 0.0338
Hectare
North: Lot 3 and Hewanathenna Watta claimed by State; East:
Hewanathenna Watta claimed by Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage Sunil;
South: Hewanathenna Watta claimed by Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage
Sunil and Kurunegala District; West: Kurunegala District.
