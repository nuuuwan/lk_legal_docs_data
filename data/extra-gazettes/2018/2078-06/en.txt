V ALUE ADDED TAX  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2002
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 (15) of the  Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002 as last amended by
the Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 20 of 2016, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of  Finance and Mass Media do
by this regulation prescribe that the cost of the Services set out in the Schedule hereto, shall be excluded in calculating the
value of supply of healthcare services for the purpose of Value Added Tax with effect from July 02, 2018.
Order made under Section 5 (15) of the said Act and published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification,
No. 1991/17 dated 01.11.2016 is hereby rescinded.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
02nd July, 2018.
Schedule
Cost of any healthcare Services other than the hospital Room charges.
