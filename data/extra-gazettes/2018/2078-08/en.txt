PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of  Uva Province
IT is hereby announce that, the following appointments has been appointed by Hon. Governor of Uva Province under the
Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1) Community Health Specialist Dr. Mr. Nimal Shantha Gamagedara, as acting Director of the Health Service of
Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent post, with effect from the date 24.04.2017.
2) Mr. T. Gajendran Kumar, Class III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province, in addition to his duties of permanent post,
from the date 10.12.2017 to 13.12.2017, till the return to the Island of Mr. M. M. Wijenayake, who is the
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province.
3) Mr. G. K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry
of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure and Co-operative and Tamil Education
(Except Muslim Schools) of the Uva Province with effect from the date 24.01.2018.
4) Mr. S. M. Gamage, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Animal Product & Health Service, as Additional Director of
the Department of Animal Product & Health of Uva Province, with effect from the date 09.03.2018.
5) Mrs. A. M. D. Athapattu, Class II officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, as Badulla Deputy Director of
Agriculture, from the date 09.03.2018.
6) Mrs. A. M. D. Athapatthu, Class II officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, as acting Provincial Director
of Agriculture of the Department of Agriculture of Uva Province, from the date 28.03.2018.
