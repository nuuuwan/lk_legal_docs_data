CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, H. P. Jayananda Acting,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from  02nd  April, 2018, all  duties of Customs
as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2064/1 of
26th  March,  2018 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before  08th  April, 2018.
H. P . J
AY ANANDA,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
29th   March,  2018.
