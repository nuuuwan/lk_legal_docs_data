NOTIFICATION   UNDER  SECTION  16  SUB  SECTION  (2 D) OF THE  COAST  CONSERV ATION  AND
COASTAL  RESOURCE  MANAGEMENT  ACT,  No.  57  OF  1981
Proposed 364 Units of Residential Apartment Complex - Stage 3
Randombe North, Balapitiya
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT   ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impacts Assessment study on proposed 364 Units of Residential
Apartment Complex - Stage 3 at Randombe North, Balapitiya, submitted by the Mr. Ajith Wijesinghe, No. 100, Kinsey
Road, Colombo 07 as required by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Act,
No. 57 of 1981 as amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public
at the following institutions from 8.30 a. m. to 4.15 p. m. for a period of 30 days from 08.06.2018. (Except weekends and
public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official web site (www.coastal.gov.lk) of the
Department of Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource  Management.
