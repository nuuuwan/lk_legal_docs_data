Regulations

2. The Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka (hereinafter referred to as the “Authority”) may classify any aerodrome
in Sri Lanka as follows :-
(a) based on the design of such aerodrome :
Code T ype of Design
A1 Land Aerodrome
A2 Water Aerodrome
A3 Heliport
A4 Short Take-Off and Landing (STOL) Port

in paragraphs (b) to (f) of regulation 2 may be used in sequence with the appropriate code for type of Design of such
aerodrome as specified in paragraph (a) of regulation 2 followed by the sub-classification code separated by a back slash.

4. A runway of an aerodrome may be classified in accordance with the Criteria specified in Annex 14 “Aerodromes”
to the Convention, which locally implemented by the Director-General of Civil Aviation in terms of the Implementing Standards
No. 30 issued under Section 120 of Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010.
5. For the purpose of these regulations, unless the context otherwise requires–
“Aerodrome” shall have the same meaning assigned to it by the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 ;
“Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka” means the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka established under the
Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002 ;
“Convention”shall have the same meaning as assigned in the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 ;
“Director-General” means the Director-General of Civil Aviation appointed under Section 11 of the Civil Aviation
Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002.
