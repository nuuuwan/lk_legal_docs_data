STAMP   DUTY  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 12  OF  2006
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under section 3 of the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 2006, I,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order amend the Order made under that Section and
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1465/19 dated October 05, 2006 as last amended by the Order published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1947/45 dated January 1, 2016, with effect from January 1, 2019, as specified in the SCHEDULE
hereto.
The Order made under Section 3 of the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 2006 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2091/3 dated October 01, 2018 is hereby rescinded with effect from January 01,
2019.
M
ANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
December 31, 2018.
SCHEDULE

amended by the substitution therefore, of the following item 04 :–
“04. (a) Any license issued authorising the holder to carry on any trade,
business, profession or vocation, other than any trade or
business for the sale of liquor, for any period (as specified in
such license)
