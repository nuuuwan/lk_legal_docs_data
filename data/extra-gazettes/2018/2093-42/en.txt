THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakaratne Bandara Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Covering Minister), hereby declare, under
Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the
land described below is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land described as Lot ‘A’  in Advance Tracing
No. Gam/Dom/2016/271 and dated 10.08.2016, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Udugama in the Minor
Division of Mada Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Mirigama in Gampaha District of Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kadurugahalanda – Extent: 0.1334 Hectare
North: Land called Kadurugahalanda claimed by K. S. Dharmawathi
Peiris and land called Kadurugahalanda alias Paragahakanda claimed
by Ceylon Electricity Board; East: Land called Kadurugahalanda
alias Paragahakanda claimed by Ceylon Electricity Board and land
called Kadurugahalanda claimed by K. S. Dharmawathi Peiris; South
and West: Land called Kadurugahalanda claimed by K. S.
Dharmawathi Peiris.
T. B. Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Covering).
My No.: 4-3/2/2016/CEB/21.
