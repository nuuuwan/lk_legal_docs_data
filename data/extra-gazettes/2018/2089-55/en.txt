2A
Establishing and Regularizing of Community Based Organizations (Water Supply and Environmental Sanitation) Statute
No.01 of 2013 of the Provincial Council of North Western Province.
The amendment of Section 13, 15 (1) and 15 (2) of Establishing and Regularizing of Community Based Organizations
(Water Supply and Environmental Sanitation) Statute No.01 of 2013  of the Provincial Council of North Western Province.
Be it enacted by the Provincial Council of North Western Province by virtue of the powers vested in the said
Provincial Council, by article No.28 in the 1 st (Provincial Council) List in Schedule 9 in the Constitution of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
The composition of the Provincial Committee is hereby amended by repeal of the paragraph “The directors
of the Department of Roads and Department of Highways of North Western Province” and the paragraph
“The Secretaries of Local Government bodies who operate drinking water supply and sanitation projects”
specified respectively under serial No. 11 and 12 in Section 13 of the statute.
The Section 15 (1) is hereby amended by substitution of the word “once in six (06) months” for  “once
in two (2) months”  and substitution of the word “ meetings and extra persons”  for “ extra meetings”.
The Section 15 (2) is hereby amended by substitution of the word “eight members” for “ten members”.
