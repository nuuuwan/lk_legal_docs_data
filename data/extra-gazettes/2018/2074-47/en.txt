FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  DELFT  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in 03- Delft 03 of  Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Mr. Shanmugam
Logeswaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that 03 - Delft 03 of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualifide under
Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to nominate
Rasalingam Parameswary to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, Returning Officer  of  Delft Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Rasalingam Parameswary was
elected as the member of the 03- Delft 03 of Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
09th June 2018.
