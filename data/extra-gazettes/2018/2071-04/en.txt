No. 2071/4 – MONDAY, MAY 14, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Mulankadukele alias Medamookalana
in total extent of about 0.0066 Hectare depicted as Lot ‘ w’ in the
Advance Tracing No. PU/DNK/2014/424 and dated 29.12.2014
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village called
Kirimetiyana - East in the Minor Division of Othara Palatha in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Dankotuwa in Puttlam District in
North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘w’ – Mulankadukele alias Medamookalana – Extent:
0.0066 Hectare
North: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Mulankadukele  alias
Medamookalana claimed by K. L. M. K. Karunanayake; East and
South: Mulankadukele alias Medamookalana claimed by K. L. M.
K. Karunanayake; West: Ambagahawatta claimed by Sasanka
Jayasundara.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
