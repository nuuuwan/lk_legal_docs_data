THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act:
Allotment of land called Plantain Point in extent of 0.4079 Hectare
depicted as Lot 'A' in Advance Tracing Plan No. TRI/TWG/2002/
487 dated 2002.10.15 prepared by Surveyor General Situated within
the Town and Gravets, Urban Council limits of Trincomalee situated
in the No: 244P in the Orr's Hill Grama Niladhari Division, in
Divisional Secretariat Division of Town and Gavets, Trincomalee
District in Eastern Province. The land is bounded as bellows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pudupadu Alias Plantain Point, Assmt. No. 7/3 –
Extent: 0.4079Hectares
North: Lot 4 in PPA 839, Path and Lot 1 in PP Tri 276; East: Lot
1 in PP 276, Path and Lot 4 in PPA 839; South and West: Lot 4 in
PPA 839.
