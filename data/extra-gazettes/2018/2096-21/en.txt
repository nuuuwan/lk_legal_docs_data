SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affaris of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from November 08, 2018, subject to provisions
of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs.,
Colombo 01,
05th November, 2018.
