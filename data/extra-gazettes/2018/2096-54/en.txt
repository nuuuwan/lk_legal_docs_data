FILLING OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Seinul
Abdeen Halilur Rahman the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon  returned member if Sri Lanka Freedom Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Ahamed Lebbe Rafeek to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  Don Thilna Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  Kalmunai Municipal Council acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare That Ahamed Lebbe
Rafeek was selected as the member of the Kalmuani Municipal Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
05th November 2018.
