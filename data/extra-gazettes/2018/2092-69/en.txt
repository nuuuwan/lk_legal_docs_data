THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following
land/servitude* which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land or servitude to be
acquired:
The land named as Batadombagahawatta in total extent
of 0.0010 Hectare (A: 0 R: 0 P : 0.3955) marked as lot No.1 in
Preliminary Plan bearing No. B.P .G . 4336 made by Surveyor
General situated in the Village of Gonapinuwala, in the
Divisional Secretariat Gonapinuwala, District of Galle. The
boundaries of  the land are as follows:-
North : Road (R.D.A) and Lot No. 57 depicted in
Preliminary Plan No. G . 3857; East : Lot No. 57 depicted in
Preliminary Plan No. G . 3857 and Batadombagahawatta
claimed by P .D.R. Dewasiri and J.A. Wijewickrama; South:
Batadombagahawatta claimed by J.A. Wijewickrama,
Batapolagewatta claimed by Wimal Thilaka Pinnaduwa and
Lot No. 58 depicted in Preliminary Plan No. G . 3857; West: Lot
No. 58 depicted in Preliminary Plan No. G . 3857 (R.D.A.).
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Gonapinuwala on the 26th day of
December 2018 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 26th day of December 2018, the nature of their interests in
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation and
the details of the said land/servitude*, the amount of com-
pensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
L.N. PIUMALI  LIYANAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Gonapinuwala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Gonapinuwala.
28th September 2018.
