The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   WARAKAPOLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United Peoples Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Bope Arachchilage Chandana Kirthisiri Wijethunga   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if   United Peoples Freedom Alliance   who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Karunanayaka Thenannehelage Ananda Karunanayake  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer  of  the  Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Karunanayaka
Thenannehelage Ananda Karunanayake  was selected as the member of the Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH WASANTHA WELGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
03rd   December  2018.
