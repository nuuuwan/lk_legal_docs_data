and
National Gem and Jewellery Authority, No. 25, Galle Face Terrace, Colombo 03  of the other part
STA TEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. K. Piyatilake who had joined the National Gem Corporation as a Junior Clerk and been promoted as a Senior Clerk
and then as the Acting Project Officer, who had made a request to retire after the completion of 55 years at a time when he had
been absorbed into the National Gem and Jewellery Authority on a decision of the Board of Directors had been caused injustice
by the termination of his employment when he had already informed the Authority before the lapse of one month from his request
that he withdraws the application and if such injustice had been caused, to what reliefs he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner  of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner  of Labour,
Colombo this 04th day of July, 2018.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. Nandasiri Lokukaluge who served as the Senior Executive Assistant Manager of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC had been
caused injustice by being retired with effect from 04.01.2017 on the completion of 55 years of age without being permitted a
service extension upto 60 years of age and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
