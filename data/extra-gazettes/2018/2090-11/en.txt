L.D.B. 4/81(VI)
THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL  ACT,  No.  47  OF  1980
Order under  Section 24C and 24D
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 24C of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980, read with Section
51 of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Maithripala Sirisena,
President, do by this Order amend the Order, made under Section 24C and 24D of the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1466/26 of October 13, 2006, declaring an environmental protection area called the “Muthurajawela
Environmental Protection Area.”
(a) in paragraph (c) thereof, by the substitution for the words and figures, “September 15, 2006” of the words and
figures, “September 15, 2004” ; and
(b) by the repeal of the Schedule I thereto and the substiution thereof, of the following Schedule :-
“SCHEDULE  1
LIMITS OF THE MUTHURAJAWELA ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AREA
All that area of State Land in extent 162.10 Hectares in extent, called Kahata Meda Welyaya, Muthurajawela
situated in the District of Gampaha in the Western Province in the Ragam Pattu of Pattiyawela village in the Divisional
Secretary's Division Wattala depicted as Lot 2 in Surveyor General's Preliminary Plan No. GAM 2722 of 19.08.1997 and
bounded as follows :
