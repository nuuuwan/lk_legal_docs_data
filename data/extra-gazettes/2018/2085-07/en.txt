1A   G   28327 —17  (08/2018)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Rasa
Kumarasami the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon  the member of that 12-Chunnakam South if Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Raththinasingam Kenkatharan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, returning officer of the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of selection 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Raththinasingam Kenkatharan was elected as the member of the 12-Chunnakam South of Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
20th August, 2018.
