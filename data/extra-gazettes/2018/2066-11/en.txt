FILLING OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   AKKARAIPATTU   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of returned member in Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the National Congress, the recognized political party to which Muhammadismail
Muhammadu Rafeek the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that ward 07 Nooraniya, Akkaraipattu Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period
of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Muhammadibra Lebbe Muhammadu Shabuddeen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Thilina Wickramarathne, Returning Officer  of the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Muhammadibra
Lebbe Muhammadu Shabuddeen was elected as the member of the ward 07 Nooraniya, Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNE ,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
09th April 2018.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 09.04.2018
FILLING OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MATALE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(A)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of returned member in Matale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Herath Mudiyanselage Lansakara Gedara Kithsiri Banda Herath the member belonged, was required by me under secton
66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that ward 10 Dorakumbura, Matale Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Nilantha Nalaka Bandara Kottegoda to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, S. D. S. A. Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Nilantha Nalaka Bandara
Kottegoda was elected as the member of the ward 10 Dorakumbura, Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
09th April 2018.
