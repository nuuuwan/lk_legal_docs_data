THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
TRI/KNN/2017/114 and dated 20.07.2017 prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village called Upparuwatta in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Upparu in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kinniya in Trincomalee District, Eastern Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Upparuwatta – Extent: 4.8562 Hectares
North: Path and Road (R.D.A.); East and South: Upparuwatta
(Garden); West: Upparuwatta (Garden, paddy field ect.) and Path.
