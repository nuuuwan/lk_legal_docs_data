THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
                                                                                            
My Ref. No.: MUL/LND/02/ACQ/42 
Land Ministry’s Ref No.: 4 /3/15/2014/VE/48. 
  
I hereby give notice under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7, 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 0f 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ servitude 
which is required for a public purpose: 
 
DESCRIPTION OF THE LAND OR SERVITUDE TO 
BE  ACQUIRED: 
 
Under mentioned land shown in the Surveyor Generals 
plan of  F.V.P. 165, supplement No. 27, sheet No.30 
prepared by Surveyor General situated in the village of 
Dewalegama, Dewalegama West Grama Niladhari 
Division, Mulatiyana Divisional Secretarys Division in 
Matara District Sourthern Province. 
 
Lot No. 521 
Name of the land: Honpalaketiya Watta; Description: 
Garden, Five coconut trees (about 11 — 15 years of age)
and a rain tree (about 18 years of age); Claimant: 
Government; Original Claimant: Tharaka 
Wasalamudaliarachchi, Nishantha Witharana; Extent: 
About 0.0341 Hectare.  
                                                                                                                             
Lot No. 522 
Name of the land: Honpalaketiya Watta; Description: 
Garden, Five coconut trees (about 11 — 17 years of age)
and two Attoniya trees  (about 3 years of age); Claimant : 
Government; Original Claimant: Tharaka 
Wasalamudaliarachchi, Nishantha Witharana; Extent: 
About 0.0410 Hectare.  
                                                                                                                             
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before 
me at Divisional Secretariat Mulatiyana on the 12th day of 
September 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 10th day 
of September 2018 the nature of their interest in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for computation for the of the 
said lands, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 SUSANTHA ATHTHANAYAKA 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Mulatiyana. 
Mulatiyana Divisional Secretariat,
