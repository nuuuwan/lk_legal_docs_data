 INLAND  REVENUE  ACT,  No.  24  OF  2017
Notice under Section 31(3)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 31(3) of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 24 of 2017 in relation to the any
period for which interest on a blended loan is compounded, I, Disanayake Mudiyanselage Lalith Ivan Disanayake,
Commissoner General of Inland Revenue, do by this notice specify any other period which is considered by the generally
accepted accounting principles.
D. M. L. I. D
ISANAYAKE,
Commissioner General of Inland Revenue
Department of Inland Renenue,
Colombo 02,
