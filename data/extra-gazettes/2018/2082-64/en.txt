The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,   No.   2   OF   1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial  Council
WHEREAS  the Secretary of the Central Province  Provincial Council  has informed the  Election Commission under Section
65 (1) of  the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the  Membership of  the Central
Province  Provincial Council by reason of  Expulsion from the  Party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the former
member Abeyarathna Chithra Srimathee Manthilaka belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65
(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated  Mr. M. S. M. Shafi  to fill such
vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65 (2) of  the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the  Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. M. S. M. Shafi, elected as a member of  the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District
of Kandy in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
