1A – G 26933 – 12 (2018/01)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose (Knuckcles Conservation Zone) and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Gonawala Watta in total extent of
about 0.3995 Hectare depicted as Lot A in the Advanced Tracing No.
Maha/MDD/2010/316 and dated 28.10.2010, prepared by the
Surveyor General and situated in the Village called Galabodawatta in
Palispattuwa Korale East Minor Division, of the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Medadumbara in Kandy District of the Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' –  Gonawala Watta –  Extent: 0.3995 Hectare
North: Gonawala Watta claimed by Land Reform Commission;
East: Gonawala Watta claimed by U.L. Rajapaksha; South and West:
Gonawala Watta claimed by Land Reform Commission.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
