Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts, No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 dated 20/06/2014 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No 1869/31
dated 02/07/2014 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 16/05/2014.
Mr. K. Navaratne Rajah, No. 27/16, Tangalle Road, Samagipura, Hambantota. of the one part
and
Distilleries Company of Sri Lanka PLC, No. 110, Norris Canal Road, Colombo 10. of the other part,
was reffered to Mr. P. Danansuriya for settlement by arbitration,
 And whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order by revoked, I, W.D.J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development do hereby revoke the said Order and further make order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order
dated 20/06/2014.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations
and Sabaragamuwa Development.
Colombo,
04th January, 2018.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
