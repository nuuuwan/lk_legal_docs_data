THE   WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES    BOARD    FOR  THE   PRAWN  CULTURE  AND  EXPORT   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, 1971 That Mr. Ahamad Risvi Haneefa has
been appointed as Employer Representative and Mr. K. G . Piyadasa has been appointed as Employee Representative of
the  Wages Board for the Prawn Culture and Export Trade by the Hon. Minister of  Labour under Section 9 of the Wages
Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136). This appointment is  valid only up to 1 st July 2020.
S. A. N.   S
ARANATHISSA,
