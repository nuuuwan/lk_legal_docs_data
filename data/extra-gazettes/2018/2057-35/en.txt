CONSULAR   FUNCTIONS   ACT,   No. 4  OF   1981
Notification under Section 8
I, Tilak Marapana, Minister of  Foreign Affairs in terms of  the powers vested in me under Section 8 of  the Consular
Functions Act, No. 04 of 1981, do by this Notification specify that with effect from March 01, 2018 the fees set out in
Column II of  the Schedule to this Notification shall be levied in respect of  Consular Functions specified in Column I of
the Schedule hereto, which are performed  by the Sri Lanka Missions/ Posts abroad.
