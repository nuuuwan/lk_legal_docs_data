THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice  under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. : 4-3/15/2015/VE)157
Divi. Secretary's Ref. : HIK/DS/LND/04/02/50
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude*  which is required for
a public purpose.
    Description of the land/ servitude to be acquired :
A block of land in total extent of 0.4443 Hectare depicted in
basic plan No. 4290 of 16.11.2017 prepared by Surveyor General,
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division, No. 45 Maha Hegoda,
in the village of Maliduwa in Divisional Secretary's Division
of Hikkaduwa, District of Galle, Southern Province. The
boundaries of the aforesaid block of land are as follows :-
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Hadanagodawatta; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: Land Reforms Commission; Extent: 0.4443
Hectare.
North : Lot No. 03 depicted in B.P.G. 2500; East: Lot No. 03
depicted in B.P .G . 2500; South: Lot No. 03 depicted in B.P .G .
2500; West: Lot No. 03 depicted in B.P .G . 2500;
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Hikkaduwa Divisional Secretariat on the 09 .04. 2018 at
11 .00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 02.04.2018, the nature of their
interests in the  land the particulars of their claims for
compensation and the details of  the said land/servitude*,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
W. S. S ATHYANANDA,
Divisional Secretary,
Hikkaduwa.
At the Divisional Secretariat, Hikkaduwa,
On 27th February , 2018.
