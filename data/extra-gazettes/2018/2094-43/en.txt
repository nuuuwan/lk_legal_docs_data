NOTIFICATION
                                              P.S. No. : PS/CSA/00/1/3/1/1
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President in the exercise of powers conferred upon him under the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has removed Hon. Ranil Wickremasinghe, who was appointed by
His Excellency the President under Article 42(4) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
from the office of the Prime Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with immediate effect.
By order of His Excellency,
 U
DAY A R. SENEVIRA TNE,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
26th October, 2018.

PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY   THE  PRESIDENT
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
IN terms of the powers vested in me by the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Maithripala
Sirisena, President do hereby dissolve the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic with immediate effect.
Given at Colombo on this Twenty Sixth day of October, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
                                                                                    By His Excellency's Command,
UDAYA R S ENEVIRATNE,
Secretary to the President.
