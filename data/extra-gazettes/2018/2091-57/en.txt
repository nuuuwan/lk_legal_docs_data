The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning officer of  the Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha  do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  Kirubairajah Naguleshwaran of
the Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of  member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has
ceased to be a member of the Eelam People’s Democratic Party.
J. J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Trincomalee Town & Gravets Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
02nd   October  2018.
