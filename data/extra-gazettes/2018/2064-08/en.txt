WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order under Section 32(2) of  Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Council's
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990 assigning the powers of Western Province Public Service
I, K. C. Logeswaran, the Governor of Western Province by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of Provincial
Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 as amended by Provincial Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990, do hereby delegate
powers vested in me regarding appointments, transfers, promotions, dismissal from service and disciplinary control of
officer of Western Provincial Public Service to the Provincial  Public Service Commission in terms of Section 32(2) of
the said Act, subject to conditions stipulated in Schedules 01 with effect from 16th April 2018. Powers of appointment to
posts in Schedule 02 shall continue to be with the Governor of the Western Province,
Officers aggrieved by the decision of either the disciplinary authority and/or the Provincial Public Service
Commission shall have a right of appeal to the Governor.
Delegation of powers published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 1859/32 dated 24.04.2014 is hereby repealed
without prejudice to actions taken under said order.
K. C. L
OGESWARAN,
Governor of the Western Province.
Governor's Secretariat of Western Province,
No. 109, Level 05,
Rotunda Tower,
Colombo 03,
22nd March 2018.

Schedule No. 01
A. Chief Secretary or any Officer of the Western Provincial Council acting under delegated powers shall before
making appointments notify the Secretary to the Public Service Commission of Western Province.
B. Chief Secretary or any Officer of the Western Provincial Council acting under delegated powers, shall before
transfer of officers on grounds of exigencies of service, obtain the concurrence of the Secretary to the Public
Service Commission of Western Province.
