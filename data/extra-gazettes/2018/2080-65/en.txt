Notices  under the Local  Authorities  Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of Member of Jaffna Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three consecutive
meetings without permission  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Sutharsing Vijayakanth the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that 22 Thirunagar if Jaffna Municipal Council  who belongs to  such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a Member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Jeyatheeswaran Narmatha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, returning officer of the Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jeyatheeswaran
Narmatha was elected as the Member of the 22 Thirunagar 0f Jaffna Municipal Council.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
19th July, 2018.
