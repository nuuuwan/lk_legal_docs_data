THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
 IT is hereby notified by me that whereas the Ministries, subjects and functions thereof to be assigned in terms of Article
44(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to the Ministers who are not members of the
Cabinet of Ministers appointed by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Article 44(1) of the Constitution have
been determined, the Ministries, subjects and functions of the said Ministries, the departments,  state corporations and
statutory institutions under the purview of the said Ministries shall be as set out in the Schedule given below with effect
from 05th November 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 05, 2018.
