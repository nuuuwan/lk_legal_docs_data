No. 2068/43 – FRIDAY, APRIL  27, 2018
 THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The boundary lines for the following mentioned lot numbers and
name of the lands called in depicted the Advance Tracing No. B/A/
80/3 on 13.05.1980 made by the Superintendent of survey's in
Badulla situated at Bandarawela town within the limits of Divisional
Secretariat of Bandarawela in Badulla District and province of Uva
are as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Ulapatharawa (there is no amendment to pay
the tax) – Extent: 0.1705 Hectare
North: Lot No. 9 and Lot No. 139 of Bandarawela town Plan;
East: Tracing Lot No. B and cement ditch; South: Lot No. 9 of
Bandarawela town Plan; West: Lot No. 8 of Bandarawela town
Plan.
02. Lot 'B' – Thubawelwagurewatta (there is no amend-
ment to pay the tax) – Extent: 0.0518 Hectare
North: Lot No. 139 of Bandarawela town Plan; East: cement
ditch; South: cement ditch; West: Tracing Lot No. A.
03. Lot 'F' – Thubawelwagurewatta (there is no amendment
to pay the tax) – Extent: 0.0025 Hectare
North: Lot No. 139 of Bandarawela town Plan; East: Tracing Lot
A; South: Tracing Lot A; West: Tracing Lot No. A.
4. Lot 'C' – Ulpathrawa ratable No.79, Temple Road – Ex-
tent: 0.1776 Hectare
North: Lot No. 11 of Bandarawela town Plan; East: Temple Road;
South: Lot No. 11 of Bandarawela town Plan; West: cement ditch.
5. Lot 'D' – Badalinpalla (there is no amendment to pay the
tax) – Extent: 0.0038 Hectare
North: Lot No. 1 of Bandarawela town Plan; East: Kabellaewela
Kadura; South: Temple Road; West: Temple Road.
6. Lot 'E' – Badalinpalla ratable No.58, Temple Road –
Extent: 0.0708 Hectare
North: Kabellaewela Kadura; East: Kabellaewela Kadura; South:
Lot No. 2 of Bandarawela town Plan; West: Temple Road.
