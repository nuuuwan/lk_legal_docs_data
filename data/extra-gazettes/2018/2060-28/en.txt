EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
My No. : 4-3/15/2015/VE/74. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government indents to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired : 
 
Preliminary plan  : K 161 
Situation Village  : Paranthan 
GN Division  : Paranthan KN/44 
DS Division : Kandawalai 
District  : Kilinochchi 
Province  : Northern 
 
Lot No. 01 - Name of Land: Korakkankaddukkadu; 
Extent : 4.4387 Hectares 
 
North: Lot No. 31 in PPYA 215; East: Lot 34 in 
PPYA 215; South: Lot 35 in PPYA 215; West: Lot 37 and 
31 in PPYA 215. 
 
All person interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Division at Secretariat, Kandawalai on the 16th 
Monday of April 2018 at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 09th of Monday April 2018  the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particular of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land amount 
of compensation and the details of the such amount. 
  
                                               G. NAHESWARAN, 
                Divisional Secretary/Acquiring Officer,                                     
                                       Kandawalai.  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kandawalai, 
26th February, 2018.
