PART 1: SECTION (1) – GENERAL
SPORTS  ACT ,  No.  25  OF  1973
Orders  under  Sections  32  and  33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera, the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act
No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend, with immediate effect, the registration of the Hocky Federation of Sri Lanka, which is the
National Federation for Hockey and as an interim arrangement, appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardene Tennakoon
Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports Development as competent authority
with effect from 04.01.2018 to continue its functions and further order that the election be convened and held to elect new
office-bearers to Hocky Federation of Sri Lanka for 2018.
DAYASIRI JAYASEKERA M.P. (ATTORNEY AT LAW),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
04th January 2018.
