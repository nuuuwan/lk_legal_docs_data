No. 2097/19 – WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 14, 2018
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCATION UNDER SECTION 39(1)
By the Order No. 332 of 2014 dated 17.11.2014 made under the
Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)
and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary  No. 1889/37 dated
21.11.2014, the land specified morefully in the Schedule below was
vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
But the possession of the said land has not yet been taken for and
on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore,
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, by virtue of powers vested in me under section 39 (1) of
the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the Order made in respect
of the land sepcified morefully under the aforesaid Order No. 332 of
2014 dated 17.11.2014 published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 1889/37 dated 21.11.2014.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
