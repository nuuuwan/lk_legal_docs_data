LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
  Reference No.: 17/592/A TH.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of land, total extent to about 0.0225 Hectare
depicted in Plan No. CO 9704 prepared by Superintendent
of survey, Colombo District, situated in the village of
Mirihana in 523, Mirihana North Grama Niladhari Division
in Divisional Secretaries, Division of Maharagama in
Colombo District Western Province and bounded as
follows.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Unidentified; Description: Open bare
land; Claimant of the Land: Unidentified; Extent: 0.0184
Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of the Land: Unidentified; Description: Road;
Claimant of the Land: Unidentified; Extent: 0.0036 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of the Land: Kotte Raja Maha Vihara premises;
Description: Home garden; Claimant of the Land: Kotte Raja
Maha Viharaya; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat of
Maharagama on the 15 of September 2018 at 09.00 a.m.
And are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 01 of September 2018  the nature
of their in interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount. In order to proof the ownership relevant
documents and deeds are submitted on the date of inquiry.
M.M.K. D ILRUKSHI WALPOLA,
Divisional Secretary,
Maharagama.
Divisional Secretariat,
Maharagama.
