THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/LI/3950.
Reference No. of: L.A 4-3/2/2014HA/114.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of the Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 Government
intends to acquire the following lands/servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
The following Land situated in the village of Kada V eediya in
the Kada Veediya East Grama Niladhari Division in Matara
Divisional Secretariat Division, Matara District, Southern
Province and depicted as Lot No. 01, 02, 03 in Primary Plan
Mara 2730 has been acquired to build Land and District
Registrar's Office, Matara.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Kottambagahawatta; Description:
Garden, Permanent two store building, two permanent
buildings, water tank and two toilets; Claimant: H. M. N.
Jayarathna, No. 59, Kumaradasa Mawatha, Matara; Extent:
0.0838 Hectare.
1A - G 27800 - 17 (2018/06)
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Mestrigewatta alias Kandukottam;
Description: Open wasteland; Claimant: H. M. N. Jayarathna,
No. 59, Kumaradasa Mawatha, Matara; Extent: 0.0040 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land: Mestrigewatta alias Kandukottam;
Description: Cement pillar; Claimant: Can't find a claimant;
Extent: 0.0007 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Matara Four Gravets Divisional Secretariat on the 25th
June 2018 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18th June 2018
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
D. D. K. W
ICKRAMARACHCHI,
