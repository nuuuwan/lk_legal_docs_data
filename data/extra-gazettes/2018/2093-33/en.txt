THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Boundaries of an allotment of land with before mentioned extent
and name and extent of about 1.4164 Hectares depicted as Lot A in
the Advanced Tracing No. ML/ MLM/ 2017/ 131 and Land
28.04.2017 which is prepared by Surveyor General, situated in the
Village of Katuwangoda in Weligama Koralaya minor Division, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat, Division in Matara District, in
Southern Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Katuwalagewatta and Punchilamahamilage
Koratuwa – Extent: 
1.4164 Hectares

Mudalige and Elokanda which is claimed by Jinadasa Weragoda;
East: Hompalahena which is claimed by Gunadasa Mudalige and
Elokanda which is claimed by Jinadasa Weragoda, Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha);
 South: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha), Katuwalage Watta and
Punchilamahamilage Watta which is claimed by N. B. Palihewadana
and Ramasiri Palihewadana and Ihalagewatta which is claimed by U.
