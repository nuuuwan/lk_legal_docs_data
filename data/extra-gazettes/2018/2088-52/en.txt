THE   LAND    ACQUISITION   ACT   (Chapter   460)
Notice under Section 07
My Reference No.: BKP/LAND/ACQUI/A3/22.
Land  Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/317'
I, hereby  give notice under Sub section (1) of  the Section 07
of the land Acquisition Act as amended by the  land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964,  that
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for resettlement of persons displaced by landslides
in 2016 in Bulathkohupitiya Divisional Secretary's Division.
 Description of the land to be  Acquired:
Three allotments (1, 2, 3) of land depicted in the schedule
in the preliminary plan No. P.P .K./ 3582 prepared by the
Supirintendent of Survey, Kegalle situated in the village of
Rangalla in the Grama Niladhari Division of Rangalla in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Bulathkohupitiya in the
District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province :
01. Lot No. 01, 02, 03
Name of the land: Yatideriya Estate - Rangalla, Lappana
Division; Names and Addresses of Claimant: 01. Land
Reforms Commission, No. 475, Kaduwela Road, Battaramulla.
02. Managing Director, Kegalle Plantation Company, No. 310,
High Level Road, Maharagama. 03. Ministry of Plantation
Industries, 2
nd Floor, Sethsiripaya Phase 11, Sethsiripaya;
Extent: 4.0516 Hectares.
Date of examination for claims for compensation on
30/10/2018 at 10.00 a.m. for allotment No. 3 P .P .K. 3582.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Bulathkohupitiya Divisional Secretariat
on the aforesaid date and time.
Further, you are  here by requested to notify to (me in writing)
in duplicate on or before 23.10.2018 the nature of their interests
in the land particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
J.M.R
AMYA JAYASUNDARA
Divisional Secretary/
Land Acquisition Officer,
Bulathkohupitiya.
 Divisional Secretariat,
Bulathkohupitiya.
05th September, 2018.
