THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act:
Two (2) allotments of land depicted in following names and extents,
in Advanced Tracing No. GA/GFG/2017/775 of 06.10.2017 prepared
by the Surveyor General, situated in the village called Deddugoda
North, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Galle Four Gravets
in Galle District of Sourthern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mahaovita – Extent: 0.0074 Hectares
North: Mahaowita claimed by G . L. Wimalasiri and E. K.
Kulawathi; East: Lot B, Mahaowita claimed by G. L.Wimalasiri,
E. K. Kulawathi and J. Kariyawasam; South: Mahaowita claimed by
J. Kariyawasam, P. L. Wimalasiri,  E. K. Kulawathi and Lot B; West:
Mahaowita claimed by G . L. Wimalasiri, E. K. Kulawathi.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Mahaowita– Extent: 0.0008 Hectare
North: Mahaowita claimed by G. L. Vimalasiri and E. K. Kulawathi,
J. Kariyawasam and Lot A; East: Mahaowita claimed by J.
Kariyawasam; South: Mahaowita claimed by J. Kariyawasam and
the Road; West: Lot A and Mahaowita claimed by J. Kariyawasam,
G. L. Wimalasiri, E. K. Kulawathi.
