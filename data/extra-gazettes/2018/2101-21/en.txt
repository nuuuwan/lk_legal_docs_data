LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: KDS/PDB/4/3/2/6.
Land Ministry Ref.  No.: 4-3/9/2014/WS/144.
I do hereby give notice under sub section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of  the Land to be Acquired :
The allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0625
Hectares. depicted in the Preliminary Plan Maha/6776
surveyed and prepared by the Senior Surveyor Superintendent
of Kandy on behalf of the Surveyor General and situated in
the village of Paranagama in Pallegampaha Korale minor
division of Pathadumbara Division in Kandy District..
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Bogahamudune watte  alias Naidage
Hena alias Jambugolla Watte; Name and Address of the
claimant: R. M. Praneeth Priyanka, No. 33, Dambagaha-
muduna, Jambugahapitiya; Extent: 0.0625 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North:Lot Nos. S469, E468 and D468 of Preliminary Plan
3176; East: Lot No. D468, E468 and T469 of Preliminary Plan
3176; South: Lot No. T469 and S469 of Preliminary Plan 3176;
W est: Lot No. S469 of Preliminary Plan 3176.
I do hereby give public notice that all persons interested in
the aforesaid lands are required to appear before me
personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing at the
Divisional Secretariat, Pathadumbara, on the 30th day of
January 2019, at 10.00 a.m. and they are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing with duplicates on or before the 23rd
day of January, 2019 the nature of their interests in the
aforesaid land and the amount and the particulars of their
claims for compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
D. G. C. JAYATHUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Pathadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Pathadumbara,
