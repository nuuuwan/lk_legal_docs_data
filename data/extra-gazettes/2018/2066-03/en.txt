THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, that under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the  Land Acquisition Act, that the land, described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that act.
An allotment of land in extent of about  0.0018 Hectares depicted
in Plan No. Pu/MHW/2016 /436 and dated 22nd December 2016
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys of Puttalam, in
the Village called Havana in Yatakalan Pattu in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Mahawewa in the Puttalam District in North
Western Province and bounded as follows.

About 0.0018 Hectare
