THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
GAM/MINU/2017/123 and dated 01.06.2017 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village called Naywala in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. 99/1, Naywala in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Minuwangoda in Gampaha District, Western Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Naywala Watta – Extent: About 0.0379 Hectare
North: Naywala Watta claimed by Wijitha Athukorala; East: Road;
South: Naywala Watta claimed by Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha;
West: Dug drain.
