1. Mr. N. G . Wilson, No. 166, Korala Limit Road, Kalatuwawa - West, Thummodara.
2. Mr. H. P. S. Hewawasam, No. 47, Ratnapura Road, Kaluaggala, Hanwella.                 of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05.                      of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Mr. N. G . Wilson and Mr. H. P. S. Hewawasam had joined the SLTB first of all. At that time they had been given membership in the Sri
Lanka Transport Board approved Employees Provident Fund and in 1991 when the Board had been peoplized they had becomem employees
of peoplized bus companies in which event they had been removed from approved EPF and given membership in the EPF. Once again they had
become employees of the Sri Lanka Transport Board in terms of SLTB Act, No. 27 of 2005 and SLTB Circular No. 11 of 2011. Whether they
had been caused injustice by not being given approved EPF memebership after becoming employees of the SLTB and if so, to what reliefs they
are entitled.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 22nd day of June, 2018.
