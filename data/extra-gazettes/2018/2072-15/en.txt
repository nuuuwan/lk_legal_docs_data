PART IV (B) – LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Filling of a vacancy in the office of member of the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha under Section 66A(1)
of the local authorities Elections ordinance (Chapter 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the People's Liberation Front the recognized political party to which Pahala Walpola
Gamaralalage Chaminda the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned member if People's Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section 9 of the
local authorities elections ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wasantha Roy Munasinghe to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, returning officer of the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya S abha
acting in terms of section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Wasantha
Roy Munasinghe was selected as the member of the Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha.
 RAVINDRA SUBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Angunakolapelessa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election office,
Hambantota,
17th May , 2018.
