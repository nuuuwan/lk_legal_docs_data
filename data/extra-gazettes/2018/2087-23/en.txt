The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER   OF   THE  MIRIGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resigning ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which
Hewasinhage Nalin Pushpakumara Hewasinghe  the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon returned  member of Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and  who shall be declared selected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Hettiarachchige Santha Geethadeva to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of the Mirigama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that  Hettiarachchige Santha Geethadeva was selected as the member of the Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha.
04th September, 2018.
