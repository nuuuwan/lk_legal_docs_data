FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NORWOOD   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of returned member in  Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ceylon Workers  Congress, (P. Wing), the recognized political party to
which Kalimuthu Sivasamy  the member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that ward 16  Wanaraja, Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Kanapathy Kulandevel Ravi  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer  of the Nuwara  Eliya  acting in terms of Section
66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Kanapathy Kulandevel Ravi  was
elected as the  member of the ward 16  Wanaraja, Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P A THIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
24th April, 2018.
