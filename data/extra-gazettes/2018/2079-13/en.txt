THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the said Act,  published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 896/2  of November 07, 1995  (as last amended by Order  published in the Gazette Extraordinary ,
No. 2037/ 15 of  September  21, 2017),  which specifies the   Faculties and Departments of  the Raja Rata University of
Sri Lanka,  as follows :-
(1) by the addition immediately after the  entry  ‘‘2. Department of  Accountancy and Finance’’ appearing  in
Column 1I of  that Schedule corresponding to the item ‘‘Faculty of  Management Studies’’ appearing in
Column 1  of  that Schedule, of  the following entries :-
‘‘3. Department of  Marketing  Management
 4. Department of  Human Resources  Management ”;
(2) by the addition immediately after the  entry  ‘‘4. Department of  Home Sciences’’ appearing  in Column 1I
of  that  Schedule corresponding to the item ‘‘Faculty of  Applied Sciences’’ appearing in Column 1  of
that Schedule, of  the following entries :-
‘‘5. Department of  Health Promotion
  6. Department of Computing ”.
Dr. W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo,
06th  July,  2018.
