1A  - G   27320 — 08  (05/2018)
NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, and Commander-
in-Chief of the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to confer the Vishista Sewa Vibhushanaya at an Investiture to be
held at the President Secretariat on the Nineteenth day of May in the year Two Thousand and Eighteen on the officers
named in the Schedule hereto in terms of Clause Four of the Presidential Warrant instituting the Vishista Sewa Vibhushanaya
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 156/5 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka issued on the First
day of September in the year One Thousand Nine Hundred and Eighty One.
                                                                                              By His Excellency's command
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
18th May, 2018
