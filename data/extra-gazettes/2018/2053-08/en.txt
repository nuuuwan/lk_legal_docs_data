No. 2053/8 – MONDAY, JANUARY 08, 2018
SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment made by the Governor of the Southern Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under sections 154 F (1) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Hemakumara Wickramathilake Nanayakkara, the Governor of the Southern Province, do
hereby announce that I have made the following appointment for the seventh provincial council.
1. Hon. Manoj Prasanga Hewagampalage – Southern Provincial Minister of Sports and Youth affairs, Rural
Development, Cultural and Art affairs, Social Welfare, Probation
and Child Care Services, Women’s Affairs, Home Economic
Enhancement, Housing and Construction and Manpower and
Employment with effect from 04th January 2018.
DR. HEMAKUMARA WICKRAMA THILAKE NANAYAKKARA,
Governor of the Southern Province
Governor’s Office,
Lower Dickson Road,
Galle.
04th January, 2018.
