1A   PG   4077 — 557  (06/2018)
EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  NO.  13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order,
(a). amend Item No. 1(b) and 1(c) of the Schedule II of the Order made under that Section and published by the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017 as amended in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2058/34 dated February 15, 2018, by substitution of the following Schedule A
hereto ; and
(b). amend the Schedule II of the Order made under that Section and published by the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2044/32 dated November 09, 2017 as amended in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No.
2058/34 dated February 15, 2018, as set out in the Schedule B hereto ;
This Order shall come into effect from June 09, 2018.
The Order made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act No. 13 of 1989 and published by the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2058/34 dated February 15, 2018 is hereby rescinded effective from June 09,
2018.
M
ANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
08th June 2018.
