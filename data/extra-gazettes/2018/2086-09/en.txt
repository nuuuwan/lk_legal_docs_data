THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of the
Act.
The Allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KU/
RDG/2016/703 dated of 26.10.2016 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys - Kurunegala District on behalf of the
Surveyor General, situated in the villages of  Wedipola and Pethangalla
in the Grama Niladhari Divisions of No. 551, Rathwita and 552,
Gopallawa  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Rideegama of
Kurunegala District in North Western Province and described in
below names and extents as follows.
01. Lot No. 133 - Kahatagaha Kumbura -  Mahakapuyaya -
Extent: 0.0145 Hectare
North: Kahatagahakumbura claimed by K.M.Sunil Bannaka and
Mailagahakumbura claimed by H.M.P.S.Bandara; East: Lots 132
and 129; South: Lot 129 and Wedipola Canal; West:
Kahatagahakumbura claimed by K.M.S.Bannaka and
Kahatagahakumbura claimed by K.M.Sunil Bannaka.
