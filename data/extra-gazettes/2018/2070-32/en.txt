1A
THE HOLIDAY ACT, No. 29 OF 1971
Order under Section 8
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane, Minister of Home
Affairs, do by this order, declare that, as regards the year 2019, each of the days specified in the Schedule here to shall be Full
Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this act.
WAJIRA ABEYW ARDANE,
Minister of Home Affairs.
Ministry of Home Affairs,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
12th April,  2018.
