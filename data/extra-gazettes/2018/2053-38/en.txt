UNIVERSITIES   ACT,    No.   16   OF   1978
Order under Section 25 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I. Laksman Kiriella, the Minister
of Higher Education and Highways having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the Horizon
College of Business and Technology (Pvt.) Ltd, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of  2007
and bearing registration number PV 62877, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made und er said Act, and
published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2039/27 of October 04, 2017 by the addition immediately after entry “Bachelor of
Information Technology Honours in Networking and Mobile computing” appearing in column I and it’s corresponding entry
in Column II respectively of the Schedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II  respectively of the
Schedule  II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE   1
CONDITIONS
Horizon College of Business and Technology (Pvt.) Ltd, shall, when admitting students for the degree programmes
specified in Schedule 11 hereto in respect of
(a) Bachelor of Education Honours in Physical Sciences. Adhere to minimum entry qualification of three (03) ‘S’ passes
from among the subject Combined Mathematics of Higher Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Information &
Communication Technology and Mathematics in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) Examination
or equivalent.
