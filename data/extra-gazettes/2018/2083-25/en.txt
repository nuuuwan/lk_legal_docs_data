REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT,  No. 32  OF  1968
Notification  under Section 9(2)(bb)(i)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 9(2)(bb)(i) of  Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of  1968,
I, S. B. Navinna, Minister of Internal Affairs and Wayamba Development hereby inform that a fee of  One Hundred Rupees
should be paid in respect of an application forwarded under aforesaid Section 9, for registration and obtain a national
identity card for the first time with effect from 01.09.2018.
I hereby cancel the notification  published in this regard in  the  Gazette No. 1922/25 of  08th July 2015.
S. B. N
AWINNE,
Minister of  Internal Affairs and Wayamba Development.
Battaramulla,
07th August 2018.

REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT,  No. 32  OF  1968
Notification  under Section 16(2)(b)(i)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 16(2)(b)(i) of  Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of  1968,
