BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
