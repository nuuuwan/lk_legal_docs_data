SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy  at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 31 days commencing from November 02, 2018 subject to provisons of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
AHINDA  R AJAPAKSA ,
Minister of  of Finance and Economic Affairs.
Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs,
Colombo 01,
31st  October, 2018.
