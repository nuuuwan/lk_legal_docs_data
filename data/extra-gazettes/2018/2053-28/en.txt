EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT) ACT 
No. 28 of 1964   (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/MDB/4/2/1/79/06. 
 Land Ministry Ref. No.: 13/97/FR/244. 
 
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No: 28 of 1964
(Amendment), that the Government  intends to acquire 
the following land/servitude  which is required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
Purpose of Acquisition Knuckles Range Reservation: 
 
Two  land allotments  of Land called   Angurukolawatta 
in total  extent of about 3.6586 Hectares depicted as Lot 
01, 02 and 03 in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6718 
Dated 20.10.2017 prepared by the Senior Superintendent 
of Surveys Kandy District, situated in the Village called 
Mangoda in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 806 of 
Mangoda in the Udasiyapattuwa South Minor Division in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Medadumbara in 
Kandy District of the Central Province and more fully 
described as follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
 Name of the Land: Angurukolawatta; Description: 
Scrub; Claimants: M. F. A. Aabdeen, No. 11, Minipe 
Road, Medhamahanuwara;  Extent :0.0160 Hectare. 
2. Lot No. 02 
 Name of the Land: Angurukolawatta; Description: 
Scrub; Claimants: State;  Extent :3.4561 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 03 
 Name of the Land: Angurukolawatta; Description: 
Scrub; Claimants: M. F. A. Aabdeen, No. 11” Minipe 
Road, Medhamahanuwara;  Extent :0.1865 Hectare. 
 
  
All  persons interested in the aforesaid  land/servitude 
are  hereby required to appear personally or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Medadumbaa 
Divisional Secretariat on the  14th day of March 2018 at 
10.00 a. m. And are hereby requested to  notify me in 
writing  (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of March 
2018 the nature of their interests in the aforesaid land/
servitude, the particulars of their claims of  compensation 
and the details of  computation of such amount. 
  
 A. M. WICKRAMARACHCHI, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Medadumbara. 
  
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Medadumbara, 
06th December, 2017.
