THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GAM/
MAHA/2015/215 and dated 29.07.2015, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys, situated in the Village called Ihala
Karagahamuna in the Grama Niladhari Division 248A of Ihala
Karagahamuna South in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Mahara in Gampaha District in Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Nadunmandiyawatta – Extent: 0.0374 Hectare
North and East: Kadawatha Bus Stand; South and West: Road
(R.D.A.).
