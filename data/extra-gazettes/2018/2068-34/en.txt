THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed  for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called '' Kebellagahakumbure Kotasa" and
"Delgahakumbure Kotasa'' in total extent of about 0.0152 hectare
depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’ in the Plan No. Gam/Gam/2017/119 dated
28.04 .2017 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village
called Kudu Bollatha in Ragam Pattuwa Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Gampaha in Gampaha District of
the Western province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kebellagahakumbure Kotasa and
Delgahakumbure Kotasa – Extent:  0.0152 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land;East: Balance portion of this
land; South: Balance portion of this land and Road; West: Road and
Balance portion of this land.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
 Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/8/2016/HA/404.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
