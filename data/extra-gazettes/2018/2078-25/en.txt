respect of allotment of land of which the claims were not presented,
as depicted in the Schedules 01 to 36 below and included in
Preliminary Plan No. 9557 prepared by Surveyor General that has
been vested in the Government through Proviso 38 (a) No. 386 of

depicted in the same schedule under Section 33 of the Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotments mentioned above could obtain the compensation payable
to the allotment concerned after submitting necessary documents to
prove their owenership to the District Court Colombo.
