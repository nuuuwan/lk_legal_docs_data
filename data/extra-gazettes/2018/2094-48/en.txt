THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
MY Reference No. : 4/1/5/21.
Reference No. of Ministry of Lands and
Parliamentary Reforms : 4-3/9/2015/WS/29.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the servitude over the
following Land, which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land/Servitude to be acquired:
The allotment of land called 'Paramount Watta' in total
extent of 0.0134 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 1 in
Preliminary Plan No. KU 2383 dated 25.09.2018, surveyed
and prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the town
of Kurunegala (Ward No. 5, within the Municipal Council
Limits, Kurunegala) in Kurunegala North - East Grama
Niladhari Division No. 834, in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kurunegala, in Kurunegala District, North
Western Province and bounded as follows,
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Paramount Watta; Boundariea of the
land: North By: Paramount Watta claimed by N M Gamage,
Road and Lot No. 3 in Preliminary Plan No. KU 1020, East
By: Road and Lot No. 2 in this plan, South by: Lot No. 2 in
this plan and Suratissa Mawatha, West By: Suratissa Mawatha
and Paramount Watta claimed by N M Gamage; Names and
address of the claimants: Mrs. N M Gamage, Hotel Paramount,
Suratissa Mawatha, Kurunegala; Extent: 0.0134 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly  authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary's office at
Kurunegala on the 26th day of November, 2018, at 10.00 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of November, 2018 the
nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
Computation of such amount.
E. M. M. S. E KANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Kurunegala.
Divisional Secretary's Office
Kurunegala
