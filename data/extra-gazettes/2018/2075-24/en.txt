EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 11/2018
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, I, Mangala Samaraweera,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order amend the Excise Notification No. 03/2018, published in the Extraordinary
Gazette No. 2054/41 dated January 18, 2018 in order to revise the opening and closing hours of Toddy Taverns, specified in the
Item No. 02 of Schedule of that Notification by substituting the opening and closing hours specified in the Schedule below
with effect from June 13, 2018.
MANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
June 13, 2018.
SCHEDULE
Column  I Column  II
License Type Opening and Closing Hours
(i) Toddy Taverns From 11.00 a.m. to 2.00 p.m. and From 5.00 p.m. to 8.00 p.m.
(ii) Toddy Taverns (Operated by the Co-operative System) From 8.00 a.m. to 9.00 p.m.
02. Definition : “Under the Co-operative System” means the Cooperative Societies and Clusters registered by the Provincial
Department of Cooperative Development.
