THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.8586 Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. NU/DSO/2017/242 and dated
14.11.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Udapussellawa in the Minor Division of Uda Palatha Korale in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Walapane in Nuwara-Eliya
District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Blairlomond Estate – Extent: About
0.8586 Hectare
North: Blairlomond Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission;
East: Blairlomond Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission and
Blairlomond Estate claimed by K. A. Sumanapala and others; South:
Blairlomond Estate claimed by K. A. Sumanapala and others, Road
and Blairlomond Estate claimed by Gillbat Silva and others; West:
Blairlomond Estate claimed by Gillbat Silva and others and
Blairlomond Estate claimed by Land Reform Commission.
