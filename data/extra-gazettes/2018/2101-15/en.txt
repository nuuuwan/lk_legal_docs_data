THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No.: lvq$bvï$03$160$CEB.
Land Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2018/CEB/163.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendement) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/Servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired :
The land allotment called Puwakgahadeniya Kumbura in
total extent of about 0.510 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in
Plan No. uq' ms' fld' 9798 dated 07.12.2018 prepared by
Surveyor General situated in the village called Dadigamuwa,
in the Grama Niladari Division No. 480 of Welipillawa, in the
Divisional Secretary's division of Kaduwela of Colombo
district in Western province.
Plan No. uq' ms' fld' 9798
Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Puwakgahadeniya Kumbura; Present
use of the land: Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original
Claimant: Waruna Yasas Embuldeniya, No. 58/16, Shalawa
Road, Embuldeniya, Nugegoda; Extent: 0.0510 Hectare.
The date and the time of the Inquiries:
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitutude is to be acquired by are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 09th day of January 2019 at
Kaduwela Divisional Secretariat.
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd day of January
2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of
their claims for the compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
A. D. Y. ANANDANI,
