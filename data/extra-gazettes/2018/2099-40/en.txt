I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of the powers vested in me
by Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) do hereby enlarge the period of time upto 15th February
2019 having enlarged the six month period ending 31st July 2018 by further five months upto 31st December 2018 by
Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2082/3 dated 31st July 2018 in the exercise of powers vested in
me by Section 4 of  the said Act for the rendering of the final report on completion of said tasks of the Commission
established under Section 2 thereof on 31st January 2018 by Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 2056/ 41 dated 02 nd February 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate and inquire into
alleged irregularities in connection with Sri Lankan Airlines Ltd, Sri Lankan Catering Ltd and Mihin Lanka (Pvt) Ltd
during the period from 01st January 2006 to 31st January 2018.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
29th November 2018.
