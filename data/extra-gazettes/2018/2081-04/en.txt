PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE 
 
Sri Sithira Velayutha Swami  Temple Annual Festival - 2018 
 
DIVISIONAL SECRETARIAT OF THIRUKKOVIL DIVISION IN  AMPARA DISTRICT 
 
IT, this hereby notified for the information of the Pilgrims who attend the above festival and other  concerned that the 
above festival commences 25.07.2018 and terminate on 11.08.2018 with water cutting ceremony. 
 
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the festival and others concerned is drown to standing regu lation published 
in the government Gazette No. 10247 dated 11th May 1951 which will be in force during the above festival. 
 
The camp area of the above festival has been enlarged as follows. 
 
North  -  By Maha Vidyalaya Road, Thambiluvil 
East  -  By Fisheries Road, Thirukkovil 
South  -  By Beach Road, Thirukkovil 
West - By Main Road, Thirukkovil 
   
 S. JAGARAJAN, 
 Divisional Secretary. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Thirukkovil.
