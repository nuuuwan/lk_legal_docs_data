The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Valikamam South  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of being Ceased to
be a Member of the Party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the  Ilanki Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Thiyakarasa
Pirakash   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the
member of that ward 01- Erlalai - North East of   Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Annathanam Richchard Thavappiragasam  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, Returning Officer  of  the  Valikamam South  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Annathanam Richchard Thavappiragasam  was  elected as the member of the Ward 01- Erlalai- North East of  Valikamam South
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam  South  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
28th  December  2018.
