THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : KU/PLG/2016/627.
Ministry of Land Reference No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/82.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends  to    acquire the following land/ servitude* which is
required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
The allotments of land,  Depicted in the surveyor General’s
plan of Preliminary Plan No.: KU 2357,  situated in the village
of Wewala and Koradoluwa, No.912, Koradoluwa Grama
Niladari Division, Polgahawela Divisional Secretary’s Division
in Kurunegala District North Western Province.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: —; Description: Road; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Unknown; Extent: 0.0019
Hectare.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: —; Description: Road; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Unknown; Extent: 0.0105
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear per-
sonally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at
Polgahawela Divisional Secretariat, on the day of the 02nd
May, 2018, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th May,  2018
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for  computation for the said land/servitude the amount
of  compensation the details of the computation of such amount.
                D. ANOMA DISSANAYAKE,
    Divisional Secretary,
          Polgahawela.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Polgahawela,
05 th April, 2018.
