LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/47.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2016/LO/541.
IT is hereby give notice under sub section (1) of Section 7 of
land acquisition act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the government
intended to acquire the following land or servitude which is
required for public purpose.
Below mentioned land depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No.
RP/MR/2752 situated in the village of Malimbada, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division, in Matara District,
in Southern Province was acquired for the construction of
Maimbada Police Station.
SUB SCHEDULE
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Dangahadeniya; Description of the land:
Paddy field; Initial claimant of the Land: V en. Malimbada
Yasassi Thero, Viharadhipathi, Idurukawa Viharaya,
Malimbada, Palatuwa; Extent: 0.0650 Hectare.
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agent duly authorized in writing, before me
at Malimbada Divisional Secretariat on the 27th day of
December, 2018 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day
of December 2018 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particular of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of the compensation of such amount.
DEEPIKA K. GUNARATNE,
Divisional Secretary,
Malimbada.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Malimbada,
26th November, 2018.
