THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/44-1.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/519.
IT is hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/Servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Below mentioned land depicted as lot No. 01 in Plan No.
PP/MR/2756 situated in the village of Malimbada in Malimbada
Divisional Secretariat Division, in Matara District, in Southern
Province was acquired for the construction of underground
tank and Official Quarters in Malimbada under Matara ivth
step of water supply scheme.
Sub Schedule :-
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Nakandagodahena; Description of the
land: Garden, Cinnamon bushes which are about 30 years
old; Initial claimant of the land : Mr. V . S. Kumarage, Tankiya
Kade, Malimbada, Palatuwa; Extent of the Land: 0.4957
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitutude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Malimbada Divisional Secretariat on this 27th day of
December 2018 at 10.30 a.m.  and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day
of December the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for the compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D
EEPIKA K. GUNARATNE,
Divisional Secretary,
Malimbada.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Malimbada.
26th November, 2018.
