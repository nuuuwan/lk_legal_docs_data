1A  - PG 3878 — 57  (05/2018)
SPORTS  LAW,  No.  25  OF  1973
REGULATION made by the Minister of Sports under Section 41 of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973.
FAISZER MUSTHAPHA, PC,
Minister of Provincial Councils,
Local Government and Sports.
Colombo,
31st May, 2018.
Regulation
Whereas by virtue of the order made by the Court of Appeal in case bearing No. CA WRIT 186/2018 on the 30th of May
2018 an interim order has  been issued staying the holding of the election of the office bearers of Sri Lanka Cricket which
is due to be held on the 31st of May 2018.
Whereas the term of office of the present office bearers of Sri Lanka Cricket comes to an end on the 31st of May
2018.
Whereas it has now become compelling on me to appoint a Competent Authority in order to ensure the
administration of Sri Lanka Cricket.
I, Faiszer Musthapha, PC, Minister of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports, in terms of Section 41 of
the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973 as amended hereby appoint Mr. Hewa Thondilage Kamal Pathmasiri, Secretary to the Ministry
of Provincial Councils, Local Government and Sports as the Competent Authority of Sri Lanka Cricket with effect from 1st
June 2018 to continue with administration and perform any such other functions necessary for the due administration of Sri
Lanka Cricket.
