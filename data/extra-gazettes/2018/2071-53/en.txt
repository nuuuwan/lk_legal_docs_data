STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is .–
‘‘Whether the below - mentioned 10 employees have been caused injustice by the earlier practice of payment of gratuity based on  one
months salary per year for employees retiring with a service period of more than 10 years at the  Upali Newspapers, being changed to half
a months salary per year and if such injustice has been caused, to what reliefs they are entitled.’’
