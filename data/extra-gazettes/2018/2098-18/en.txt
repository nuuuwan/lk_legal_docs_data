THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUITION 
(AMENDEMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
(Notice Under Section 7) 
 
 My Ref No.: GAL/LAN/AQUIS/A13/35. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/187. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (amendment Act) No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intends to acquire the lands/servitude 
described in the Schedule given below for a public 
purpose Sarvitude:- 
 
      Description of the lands/ servitude to be acquired : 
 
     Allotment of land in extent about 0.0643 Hectare 
depicted in the Plan No. Ke/3643 dated 29.10.2018 
prepared by Surveyor General situated in the villages of 
Pitagaldeniya in 67 Narangaoda Grama Niladhari 
Division sub division of Kandupita Pattuwa North Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Galigamuwa in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuva 
Province. 
     
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Kurukosgahamula Watta and 
Galketiye Watta; Name of the Claimant : E.M.D.R.B. 
Hatnagoda, Pitagaldeniya, Kegalle; Extent : 0.0378 
Hectare.  
02. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Kurukosgahamula Watta and 
Galketiye Watta; Name of the Claimant : E.M.D.R.B. 
Hatnagoda, Pitagaldeniya, Kegalle; Extent : 0.0265 
Hectare.  
 
Dates for Inquires: 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be 
acquires are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Galigamuwa on 05.12.2018 at 10.00 a.m and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing with 
duplicate on or before the 28.11.2018 the nature of their 
interests  in the land, the particulars  of their claims for  
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details  of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
   
 H.M.C.SUSANTHA HERATH, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Galigamuwa. 
 
 
 16th November, 2018, 
Divisional Secretariat,
