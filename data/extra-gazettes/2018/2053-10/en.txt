L.D.B 13/2016
THE  ARMY   ACT  (CHAPTER 357)
REGULATIONS made by the President under Section 155 read with Section 29 of the Army Act (Chapter 357), read with
Article 4 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Section 51 of the Nineteenth
Amendment to the Constitution.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
04th January, 2018.
Regulations
The Army Pensions and Gratuity Code 1981 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 562/11 of June 15, 1989 as
amended from time to time is hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) in regulation 19, by the insertion immediately after sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) thereof, of the following
proviso :-
“Provided however, where such disablement or death, if caused by terrorist activities, is certified by
an Army Medical Board, to be attributable to military service or to the performance of non-military duties
under Section 23 or 23A of the Army Act, the aggregate amount of the pension so awarded to an officer or
in respect of an officer, together with the Army Service Pension, if any, shall not exceed 100% of pay per
annum.”.
(2) in regulation 28, by the insertion immediately after sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) thereof, of the following
proviso :
“Provided however, where such disablement or death, if caused by terrorist activities, is certified by
an Army Medical Board, to be attributable to military service or to the performance of non-military duties
under Section 23 or 23A of the Army Act, the aggregate amount of the pension so awarded to a soldier or in
respect of a soldier, together with the Army Service Pension, if any, shall not exceed 100% of pay per
annum.”.

THE  NA VY   ACT  (CHAPTER  358)
REGULATIONS made by the President under Section 161 of the Navy Act (Chapter 358), read with Article 4 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Section 51 of the Nineteenth Amendment to the
Constitution.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
04th January, 2018.
Regulations
The Navy Pensions and Gratuities Code 1981 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 654/10 of March 20,
1991 as amended from time to time is hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) in regulation 16, by the insertion immediately after sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) thereof, of the following
proviso :-
“Provided however, where such disablement or death, if caused by terrorist activities, is certified by
an Navy Medical Board, to be attributable to Naval service or to the performance of Naval duties under
Section 21 of the Navy Act, the aggregate amount of the pension so awarded to an officer or in respect of
an officer, together with the Navy Service Pension, if any, shall not exceed 100% of pay per annum.”.
(2) in regulation 25, by the insertion immediately after sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) thereof, of the following
proviso :–
“Provided however, where such disablement or death, if caused by terrorist activities, is certified by
an Navy Medical Board, to be attributable to Naval service or to the performance of Naval duties under
Section 21 of the Navy Act, the aggregate amount of the pension so awarded to a sailor or in respect of a
salior, together with the Navy Service Pension, if any, shall not exceed 100% of pay per annum.”.
