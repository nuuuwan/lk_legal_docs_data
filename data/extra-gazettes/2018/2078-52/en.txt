I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following Land which
is required for a public purpose.
Public Purpose Required :
To acquire lands for the activities related to the expansion
of Kesbewawewa.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Following three (03) allotments of land depicted in Sheet
No. 02, Zone No. 05 in Cadastral Map No. 521210 dated
21.11.2017 prepared by the Senior Surveyor Superintendent
in the Grama Niladhari's Division of Kesbewa East
No. 572B of Village Demaladuwa in the Kesbewa Division
of Divisional Secretary of Colombo District, Western Province
Containing in extent about 0.5331 Hectare.
01. Lot No. 351
Name of the Land: Wewalhala Kumbura; Description:
Abandoned paddy field; Claimant of the Land: Henadheera
Pathirage Nihal Dhanusiri Dasanayake of No. 446/7, Meeganga
Place, Thimbirigasyaya, Colombo 05; Extent: 0.1165 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 352
Name of the Land: Wewalhala Kumbura; Description:
Abandoned paddy field; Claimant of the Land: P . R. Dingiri
Mahaththaya of No. 112/A, Horana Road, Kesbewa; Extent:
0.1396 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 353
Name of the Land: Wewalhala Kumbura; Description:
Abandoned paddy field; Claimant of the Land: Henadheera
Pathirage Suranimala Ranjith Dasanayake of No. 165/1,
Gnanendra Mawatha, Nawala, Rajagiriya; Extent: 0.2770
Hectare.
