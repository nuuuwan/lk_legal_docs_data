THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts. No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 dated 04.04.2018 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2066/7
dated 09.04.2018 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 23.03.2018.
Mr. H. K. Nimal, R.G. 4, Soyzapura, Moratuwa. of the one part
and
State Engineering Corporation of Sri Lanka, No. 130, W. A. D. Ramanayaka, Mawatha, Colombo 02.  of the other part
was referred to Mr. Nandasena Bedigama for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union
Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make order that no proceedings be taken upon the said  Order dated 04.04.2018.
RAVINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
30th July, 2018.
Colombo.
