WAGES BOARDS ORDINANCE
NOTIFICATION
Wages Board for the Prawn Culture and Export Trade
IT is hereby notified, under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members
of the Wages Board for the Prawn Culture and Export Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour
under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on
01st July, 2017.
