No. 2104/8 – MONDAY, DECEMBER 31, 2018
UV A   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment made by the Governor of the Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Sections 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act. No. 42 of 1987, I, Ariya B. Rekawa,
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1. Mrs. G . A. M. S. P . Ambanwala, Retired officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, has been re-appointed on
contract basis as Secretary to the Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education (Except Tamil
Schools), Local Government, Power and Energy, Land, Construction and Rural Infrastructure Development of Uva
Province, from the date 09.10.2018 till the appointment of a Permanent officer or up to 08.10.2019.
2. Medical officer (Non-infectious diseases) Dr. Y . Upasena, to perform the  Duties of the Provincial Director to the
Health Service of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 13.10.2018 to 05.11.2018, till the
return of Dr. Nimal Shantha Gamagedara, to the Island who is the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva
Province.
3. Mr. R. M. Dayarathne, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service to perform the Duties of the Deputy Chief
Secretary (Planning) of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 31.10.2018 to 03.11.2018, till
the return of Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara Mapa to the Island, who is the Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning)
of Uva Province.
4. Additional Regional Dental Surgeon Dr. K. K. U. N. Dharmasiri, to perform the Duties of the Regional Diretor to the
Health Service of Badulla, in addition to his permanent post, from  the date 28.10.2018 to 03.11.2018, till the return of
Dr. Mrs. J. C. M. Thennakoon to the Island, who is the Regional Director to the Health Service of Badulla.
