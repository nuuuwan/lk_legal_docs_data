 Mr. T. Nelson A. Fernando, No. 608/1, Galle Road, Panadura. of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05.of the other part,
STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. T. Nelson A. Fernando who had worked as a Grade V Officer (Court Officer) of the Kalutara Regional Office of
the Sri Lanka Transport Board and worked in the same post on contract basis from 02/05/2011 to 28/10/2015 after retirement had
been caused injustice by the non-payment of salary relevant to the post, annual increments and increased allowances for the
services rendered during the period and if such injustice had been caused, to what reliefs he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo this 22nd day of  March  2018.
