CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, P. S. M. Charles,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from  09th  April, 2018, all  duties of Customs as well
as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and received at
the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2065/1 of
02nd  April,  2018 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 15th  April, 2018.
P. S. M.  C
HARLES,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
06th  April,  2018.
