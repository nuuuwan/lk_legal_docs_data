  NOTIFICATION   MADE   UNDER   SUB   SECTION   (4)d   OF   SECTION 66 OF   THE LOCAL

INSTITUTES   ELECTION (AMENDMENT)  ACT, No. 22 OF 2012
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Sub Section (4)d of Section 66 of Local Government Institutes Election Ordinance
amended by Local Government Institutes Election (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012, I, J. M. R. P. Jayasinghe the
Commissioner of Local Government in the North Western Province, hereby notify that the meeting for the purpose of
electing Chairman/Vice Chairman for the Urban Council Chilaw will be convened at the conference hall of Urban Council
Chilaw at 9.30 a. m. on 09.04.2018.
J. M. R. P. J
AYASINGHE,

North Western Province.
