THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.:MUL/LND/2/ACQ/47.
Land Ministry Ref. No. :4-3/15/2014/VE/236.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Under mentioned allotments of land shown in the Surveyor
Generals plan of F.V .P . 83, supplement No. 16, sheet No. 12
Prepared by Matara Surveyor General, situated in the village
of Pandithaporuwa, Deiyandara Grama Niladhari Division,
Mulatiyana Divisional Secretarys Division in Matara District
Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 106
Name of Land: Kongahahenawatta; Description: Garden,
28 Coconut trees (about 20-40 years of age) and Tea plantation
(about 10 years of age) and mixed crops can be seen; Claimant:
Government; (Original Claimant: Prema Weerabaddana,
Kongahahena, Deiyandara); Extent: 0.1576 Hectare.
1A - G 26876 - 87 (2017/09)
02. Lot No. 229
Name of Land: Kongahahenawatta; Description: Garden,
15 Coconut trees, (about 20-40 years of age) and banana and
mixed crops can be seen; Claimant: Government; (Original
Claimant: Prema Weerabaddana, Kongahahena, Deiyandara);
Extent: 0.0882 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Mulatiyana on the 29.03.2018 at
10.00 a.m. o'clock and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing [in duplicate] on or before the 22.03.2018, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
computation for the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
Susantha Attanayaka,
Divisional Secretary,
Mulatiyana.
29th December 2017,
Divisional Secretariat Office,
Mulatiyana.
