THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provision of that Act:–
An allotment of land Advance Tracing No. CO/THI/2016/292 and
Dated 22.09.2016 prepared by the surveyor General situated at
Dematagoda village (Division No. 29 - Limits of  Colombo Municipal
Council) in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Thimbirigasyaya,
Colombo District, Western Province. Name, limits and Extent of the
above Land are as follows :–
01. Lot No. 01 – A portion of Land bearing Assmt. No. 181
Aramaya Place – Extent: 0.0600 Hectares
North: Land bearing Assmt. No. 183, Aramaya Place; East:
Aramaya Place; South: Aramaya Place and Land bearing Assmt. No.
181, Aramaya Place; West: Lands bearing Assmt. No. 181, 183
Aramaya Place.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Ministry of Land and Parliamentary Reforms.
