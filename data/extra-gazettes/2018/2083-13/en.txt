No. 2083/13 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 09, 2018
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
KU/KTV/2017/27 and dated 16.06.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Elagammillawa (F.V .P. 3139) in the
Minor Division of Magul Madagandahaye Korale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kotawehera in Kurunegala District in North
Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Elagammillawakamunagedarayaya – Extent:
About 0.0759 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of the same land claimed by R. M.
Dissanayaka; East: Land claimed by A. H. M. Tikiribanda; South:
Land claimed by A. M. U. Dingiribanda; West: Pradeshiya Sabha
Road.
