Serial No. Paragraph No. Proposed revision 
 
01 18.    18. Interim provisions 
 
 Interim provisions are effective until 22.10.2020. 
 
 18.1  Promotion within the interim period 
 
 18.1.1.  Promotion to Grade II 
                 
 Promotion will be given to the officers passed the efficiency bar examination 
mentioned in Sri Lanka Principals’ Service Minute no. 1086/26 dated 02.07.1999 to 
grade II at the date of completing other qualifications apart from (vii) of 10.1.1, 
releasing from the requirement of passing the efficiency bar examination 1 as well as 
capacity development training course 1 mentioned in this statute / minute when 
promoting officers in Class 3 to grade II during the interim period. 
 
(Published by Authority)

     Note: 
 
 Provisions under 18.1.1 will not be applicable to the officers recruited to grade III 
during the interim period whereas they are to accomplish all qualifications mentioned 
in 10.1.1 including the efficiency bar examination 1 stated in this service minute in 
order to be promoted to Grade II. 
  
      
 18.1.2.   Promotion to Grade I 
  
 Officers who have passed the efficiency bar examination and who meet with other 
qualifications necessary for promotion to Class I under the Service Minute of the Sri 
Lanka Principals’ Service No. 1086/26 dated 02.07.1999 will be released from the 
requirement of passing over the efficiency bar examination 1 and 2 and promoted to 
Grade I as at the date on which they complete other qualifications except for (v), (vi) 
and (vii) of section 10.2.1. 
 
 Competency in the national Language should be obtained as per 9.1 of this Service 
Minute in order to be promoted to Grade I. 
  
  Note: 
 
 i. Provisions stated under 18.1.2 are not applicable for officers promoted to Grade II 
during the interim period and such officers should complete all qualifications stated in 
10.2.1 including the second efficiency bar examination referred to in this Service 
Minute in order to be promoted to Grade I. 
  
 ii. When grade to grade promotions of all officers recruited to the post of principal after 
01.07.2007 are made they should complete the requirement of the national language 
stipulated by the Public Administration Circular No. 01/2014 and the Circulars 
incidental thereto 
  
 The officers absorbed into this Service Minute will be released from the requirement of fulfilling the link language 
requirement referred to under 9.1 of the new Service Minute.
