I, hereby given notice under Sub section (1) of Section 07 of
the land of the land Acquisition Act as amended by the  land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the
Government intends to acquire part of the Land at left side of
Madiwela East Diversion Scheme situated  in the Village
called Udumulla, implemented under a sub project of Metro
Colombo Urban Development Project for the conservation
of embankments.
 Description of the land to be  Acquired:
Two allotments of land in total extent of  about 0.0663
Hectare, depicted as lot No. 01 and 02 in the Preliminary Plan
No. Co. 9763 dated 10.05.2018 situated in the Village called
Udumulla in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 502 of Udumulla
- North in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kolonnawa
in Colombo District of the Western province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Elamulla Watta; Discription: Home
Garden; Claimant: Balasooriya Appuhamilage Padmini Perera,
No. 05, Ambathale, Angoda; Extent: 0.0316 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the land: Elamulla Watta  and Bogahawaththa;
Discription: Home Garden; Claimant: Patthiniyage Don
Karunarathna, No. 52, Ambathale, Mulleriyawa; Extent: 0.0347
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which is
to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or
by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Land
Acquisition Office located in the Head Office of the Sri Lanka
Reclamation and Development Corporation, No. 03, Sri
Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on
05.07. 2018 at 9.30 a.m. and are here by requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before  the 29. 06. 2018 , the
nature of their interests in the land / lands, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land , the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
C.W. ABEYSURIYA.
