PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION   29  OF  THE  LAND   REFORM  LAW  No.  1  OF 1972  AS  AMENDED BY LAND
REFORM  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  39  OF 1981  AND   LAND   REFORM  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT, No. 14 OF  1986
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Section 29 of the Law, I hereby, direct every person who is interested in any
agricutlural land or lands described in the Schedule hereto immediately before the date on which such land or lands have
been vested in the Land Reform Commission to make, within one month from 20.09.2018 a written claim in triplicate to the
whole or any part of the compensation payable under the Law in respect of such land or lands and to specify in the claim.
a) His name and address
b) Nature of his interest in such land/lands
c) Particulars of his claim and
d) How much of such compensation is claimed by him
All claims should be sent by registered post addressed to the Chairman Land Reform Commission.
S
AMPATH S UBASINGHE  ARACHCHI ,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
Office of the Land Reform Commission,
No. 475,
Kaduwela Road,
Battaramulla,
16th August, 2018.
