FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  KALPITIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Kalpitiya   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Abdul
Raidu Muhammadu Razak Ameen   the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned member of  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress  who belongs to such  party  and is not
disqualified  under Section 9 of  the local authorities election ordinance and who shall be declared elected as  a member
within a  period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Abdul Rahuman Mohamad Jeseem  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the Kalpitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Rahuman
Mohamad Jeseem  was selected as the member of the Kalpitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Kalpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
28th  August, 2018.
