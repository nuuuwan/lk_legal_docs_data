NOTICE  UNDER  REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  ACT,  No.  44 OF 1980  AS AMENDED BY THE
REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  10  OF  2017
WHEREAS certain persons have been internally displaced as a result of any actions of terrorist militant or other group
during the recent past ;
02. Whereas in terms of Section 2 of the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 2017, any
citizen of Sri Lanka :
2.1 who is or had been an internally displaced person ;
2.2 whose name appeared in the register of electors for any electoral district for any year, until the end of the
year 2009 ; and
2.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation  subsequent to the year 2009,
shall on  proof of the above matters and on production of a certificate issued by the Grama Niladhari of the area
in which he is presently residing to the effect that he is or had been an internally displaced person, be entitled to make an
application  to the registering officer of the electoral district within which he was premanently resident prior to May 18,
2009, to be registered in the register of electors of such electoral district ;
03. And whereas in terms of Section 2(3) of the aforesaid Special Provisions Act, the children of a citizen of Sri
Lanka referred to in paragraph 2 above :
3.1 who had not attained the age of eighteen years on the date on which such citizen became an internally
displaced person and have attained the age of eighteen years or more on the date on which the revision
commenced in respect of the register in operation, at the time in which the application is made ;
3.2 who are born after such citizen became an internally displaced person and have attained the age of eighteen
years or more on the date on which the revision commenced in respect of the register in operation, at the
time in which the application is made ; and
3.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009,

04. In terms of the powers vested to the Election Commission under the Registration of Electors (Special

November 2018 ; that relevant applications forms could be obtained from the offices of the registering officers of all
electoral districts in Sri Lanka ; and that dully filled applications be submitted to the relevant registering officers.
05. Election Commission  further notify that it is expected to finalize the preparation of the supplementary
register referred to in paragraph 04 above on 02nd day of November 2018 and the notice thereon to be issued on that day

specified above and whose names are not entered in the said supplementary register to make appeals to enter their names
