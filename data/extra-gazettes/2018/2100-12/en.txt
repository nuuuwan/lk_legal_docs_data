L.D.B 21/78(xv)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Wijayadasa
Rajapaksa, Minister of   Education and  Higher  Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South
Eastern University of  Sri Lanka,  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 916/7  of  March 27, 1996 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2038/75  of   September  29, 2017), by the addition  immediately  after the
entry ‘‘ (c) Department of  Mathematical Sciences’’  appearing in Column II under the heading ‘‘Department of   Study’’
which corresponds to the  item ‘‘Faculty of Applied Sciences’’  appearing in  Column I under the heading ‘‘Faculty’’ of  the
Schedule II to such Order, of the  following new entry :-
          “(d) Department of Chemical Sciences’’.
Dr. W
IJAY ADASA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Education and Higher Education.
Colombo,
26th November  2018.
