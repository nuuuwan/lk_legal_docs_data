 LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  NO. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
My  No. : KEG/LAN/AQUIS/A3/1/112.
I  hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the land lots described in the schedule given below
for the development of the water -way of restoration of Bus
Depot Kegalle.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land attached below depicted in
Priliminary Plan No. 3606 prepared by Surveyor General,
Kegalle situated in Waldeniya Grama Niladari Division in
Kegalle Divisional Secretary's Division (within the Kegalle
Pradeshiya Sabha Limits) in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuwa
Province.
Schedule
Plan No. P.P.K. 3606
     01. Lot No. 1
Name of land:  Edandewela alias Weganthale Kumbura;
Claimant by: W.A. Premawathie, No. 172, Piscal Watta,
Paragammana; Extent: 0.0073 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land:  Edandewela alias Weganthale Kumbura;
Claimant by: W.A. Premawathie, No. 172, Piscal Watta,
Paragammana; Extent: 0.0092 Hectare.
Dates of inquiries for compensation :
Lot No. 01-02 of  P .P .K 3606 December 2018, on the date of
27 from 9.30 a.m.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be acquired
are hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly
authorized in written before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kegalle
on the above mentioned date and on the scheduled time.
And  are hereby requested to notify me in writing with
duplicate of on or before 06 day of December 2018 the nature
of their inerest in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
K. G. S. NISHANTHA,
Acquiring/ Divisional Secretary,
Kegalle.
