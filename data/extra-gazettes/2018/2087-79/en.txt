THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathillake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotment of Land indicated in following names and extents depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. KE/BLT/2015/ 264 and dated of
12.08.2016 prepared by Surveyor-General, situated in the village of
Welathuduwa in the Minor Division of Uduwa Palatha of the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Bulathkohupitiya in Kegalle
District of the Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows :
1. Lot No. ‘A’ – Karandawatta (A Section of Urumeewala
Estate) – Extent:  0.5169 Hectare
North and East: Haloluwa Oya; South: Lot No. B and Stream;
West: Stream and Haloluwa Oya.
