EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   YATAWATTA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER    SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha   has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the Independent  Group , the Independent  Group to  which  Ekanayake
Mudiyanselage Pahala  Arampola Malala Bandara Ekanayake   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,   to decide  upon the  member if    Independent  Group who belongs to  such
Independent  Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the leader of  the aforesaid  Independent  Group, has  communicated his decision to nominate
Eramudumade Gedara Buddhika Prasad Weerasekara  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Prathapasinghe Arachchige Kumarinee Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the  Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Eramudumade Gedara Buddhika Prasad Weerasekara  was Selected as the member of the Yatawatta
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Yatawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
17th  December  2018.
