The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING  OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF   THE  KARANDENIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION   (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16 OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of a  Vice Chairman in  Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Ranjith Donald Ranaweera the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under section 66B(2) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be
declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has communicated his decision to
nominate Subasinghe Pathiranage Nimal Bandu to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local
Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Subasinghe Pathiranage Nimal Bandu elected
as Vice Chairman of the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
03rd September 2018.
