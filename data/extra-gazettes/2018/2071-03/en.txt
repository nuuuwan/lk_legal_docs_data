THE  UNITED  NATIONS  ACT,   No. 45  OF   1968
Order under regulation 19(1) of the United Nations (Sanctions in relation to Democratic
People's Republic of Korea) Regulations of 2017
I, Kapila Mudantha Waidyaratne, Competent Authority appointed for the purpose of the United Nations (Sanctions in
relations to Democratic People's Republic of  Korea) Regulations of 2017, published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2039/32 of October 6, 2017 acting in terms of  the powers vested in me by regulation 19(1) of  the said Regulations,
do by this Order, freeze all funds, other financial assets and economic resources in Sri Lanka which are owned or controlled
directly or indirectly by any person and entity designated and listed under regulation 19(3) of  the above regulations and
published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2067/14 of April 19, 2018 and any other person entity who acts on behalf of
or under the direction of such designated persons and entities, to or for the benefit of such designated persons and
entities.
K
APILA W AIDYARATNE,
President's Counsel,
Competent Authority,
Secretary,
Ministry of Defence.
Colombo,
11th May 2018.
