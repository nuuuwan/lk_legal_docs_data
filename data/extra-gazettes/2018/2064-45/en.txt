EXTRAORDINARY
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE  WARD  10 DOW A  OF  ELLA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  66A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of Member in Ward 10 Dowa of Ella Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason dead;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to which Malavige Sanjeewa
Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Ward 10 Dowa of Ella Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a Member within a period of thirty (30)
days;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate  Attanayaka
Mudiyanselage Hemawathie Kumarihamy to fill such vacancy:
NOW THEREFORE I, H. I. R. Hathurusinghe Returning Officer of Ella Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Attanayaka Mudiyanselage Hemawathie
Kumarihamy was elected as the Member of the Ward 10 Dowa of Ella Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Ella Pradeshiya Sabha.
29th March, 2018
District Election Office.
Badulla.
