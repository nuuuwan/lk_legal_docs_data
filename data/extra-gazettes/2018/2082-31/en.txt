SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 02 months commencing from August 01, 2018, subject to provisions of Sub
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2070/2 of 08.05.2018 in respect of Fish and Gazette Notification No. 2075/31 of 14.06.2018 in respect
of Sail Fish, Marlin Fish and Thora  Fish  are rescinded with effect from August 01, 2018.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
July 31, 2018.
