 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing registration number PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the schedule to Order made under the said Act,
and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1975/48 of July 14, 2016 by the addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor
of Management Honours in Accounting and Finance” appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding entry in Column II
respectively in the Schedule II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule
II hereto, subject of the conditions specified I hereto :–
Schedule I
CONDITIONS
National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme specified in
Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer System Engineering, adhere to the minimum
entry qualifications of three (03) ‘S’ passes in Physical Science stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE Advance
Level Examination or equivalent.
Schedule II
Column I Column II
Degree Abbreviation of the Degree
Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer BScHons (Computer System Engineering)
System Engineering
