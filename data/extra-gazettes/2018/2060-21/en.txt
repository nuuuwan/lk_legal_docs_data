My  Ref.: BKP/LAND/ACQUI/A3/19.

I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition  Act as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for resettlement of persons displaced by landslides
in 2016 in Bulathkohupitiya Divisional Secretary's Division.
     Description of the land to be acquired:
Three allotments of  land called Urumeewala Estate, Wagolla
Division depicted in the preliminary plan No. P . P . K/3569
prepared by the Superintendent of Survey, Kegalle situated in
the village of Wegalla in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 128
B Wegalla in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Bulathkohupitiya in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
Date of examination for claims for compensation :- On 13th
March, 2018 at 10.00 a.m for allotments No. 1 to 3 of  P . P . K.
3569.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at  Bulathkohupitiya Divisional Secretariat on the
aforesaid date and time.
Further,  you are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before 10.03.2018, the nature of  their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount
of compensation and details of computation of such amount.
J. M. RAMYA JAYASUNDARA,
