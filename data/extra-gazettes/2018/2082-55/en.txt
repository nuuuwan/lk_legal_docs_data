 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT   (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: KDS/DEL/4/4/10/1.
Land Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2013/HA/687.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of the
land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired:
(Four) Allotments of land in total extend of about 0.7680
Hectare, depicted in advance tracing p.p. maha 6796 and
28.06.2018 of prepared by the Surveyor General Situated in
the Village of Suduwella in the Divisional Secretarys Division
of Deltota in Kandy District and bounded as follows.
01. Lot No. 1 - Littlevally Waththa; Extent  About 0.0112
Hectare.
North: Stream, Road (RDA) and Lot No. 2; East: Lot No. 2;
South: Lot No. 2 and Stream; W est: Stream.
02. Lot No. 2 - Littlevally Waththa; Extent  About 0.3332
Hectare.
North: Lot No. 1, Road (RDA) and Lot No. 3; East: Road
(RDA) and Stream; South:Stream, L.R.C. Claimed by Littlevally
Waththa; West: Stream and Lot No. 1.
1A - G 28216 - 17 (2018/08)
03. Lot No. 3 - Littlevally Waththa; Extent About 0.0026
Hectare.
North: Lot No. 2 and Road (RDA); East: Road (RDA) and
Lot No. 2; South: Lot No. 2 ; West: Lot No. 2.
04. Lot No. 4 - Littlevally Waththa; Extent About 0.4210
Hectare.
North: Stream and Road (RDA); East: Road (RDA) and
Foot path; South: Foot Path L.R.C. Claimed by Littlevally
Waththa and Stream; W est: Stream.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a serviturde is to be Acquired are hereby required to appear
Personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Deltota D.S. Office on the 29th day of August 2018 at
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicated) on or before the 23rd day of August 2018 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claimes for compensation for the acquisition for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude the amount of compensation and
the details of the Computation such amount.
