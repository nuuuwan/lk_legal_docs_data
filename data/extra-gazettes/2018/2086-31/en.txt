Provincial Councils Notifications
 WESTERN    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Amendment rules  formulated  under  the  Cooperative  Employees'  Pensions  Statute  No. 06  of  2010
of   Western  Provincial  Council
AS per powers entrusted to me under Section 23 of the Cooperative Employees' Pensions Statute No. 06 of 2010 of Western
Province, I,  Kejitan Lalith Wanigarathne, the Minister of Roads, Transport, Cooperative Development and Trade, Housing
and Construction, Estate Infrastructure Facilities, Industries and Rural Development do hereby amend the rules formulated
under the said Section of Cooperative Employees' Pensions Statute No.06 of 2010 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette
paper No. 1698/22 dated 24th March 2011 as follows.  These amendment rules shall be enforced from the date of their
publication in the Gazette paper.
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk
1A PG 4192 — 1407 (08/2018)
(1) By substituting the following rule instead of rule 3(2), the said rule is amended as follows:
According to the matters contained in this rule, an employees recruited to the permanent service
subsequent to the date of enforcement of these rules, within 90 days from the date of confirmation of his
service by the Wstern Provincial Cooperative Employees' Commission shall present to the Director  an
application prepared as prescribed by these rules.
(2) Inclusion of a new rule as 3(3) immediately after rule 3(2) as follows.
In case any problematic situation occurs regarding contribution to the Western Provincial Cooperative
Employee's Pension Scheme, the Director shall reach into a decision on the agreement of the Control
Board.
