THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of section  5 of the
land Acqusition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of lands below, depicted in  Advance Tracing No.
KE/RWN/2017/174 and dated 12.09.2017 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in villages of Imbulana and Anguruwella  and Grama
Niladhari  Divisions of No.  100, Imbulana and No.105 B, Doranuwa
in the Minor Division of Atulugama Korale in Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Ruwanwella in the Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  - Gonnagahapelessa Watta alias Baiya Watta -
Extent: 0.0036 Hectare
North: Balance portion of same land; East: Balance portion of
same land and Gonnagahapelessa Watta alias Baiya Watta; South:
Lot E and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘B’  - Ambagahamulahena - Extent: 0.0052 Hectare
North: Balance portion of same land and Road (R.D.A.); East:
Road (R.D.A.); South: Lot C and Ambagahamulahena; West:  Balance
portion of  same land.
03. Lot ‘C’  - Ambagahamulahena - Extent: 0.0048 Hectare
North: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.); South:
Lot D and Gonnagahapelesse Watta alias Baiya Watta; West:  Balance
portion of  same land  and Ambagahamulahena.
04. Lot ‘D’  - Gonnagahapelesse Watta alias  Baiya Watta -
Extent: 0.0127 Hectare
North: Lot C and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.); South:
Gonnagahapelesse Watta alias Baiya Watta; West:  Balance portion
of  same land.
05. Lot ‘E’  - Gonnagahapelesse Watta alias  Baiya Watta -
Extent: 0.0015 Hectare
North: Lot A and Gonnagahapelessa Watta alias Baiya Watta;
East: Balance portion of same land; South: Gonnagahapelesse Watta
alias Baiya Watta and Lot F; West:  Road (R.D.A).
06. Lot ‘F’  - Gonnagahapelesse Watta alias  Baiya Watta -
