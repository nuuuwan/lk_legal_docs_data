CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION
The Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
ORDER   UNDER  ARTICLE  10  OF  SCHEDULE “E” OF  THE  CUSTOMS  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 2  OF  2003
IN pursuance of the powers vested in me by Article 10 of Shedule “E” of the Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2003,
I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do hereby order that the Customs value of motor vehicles imported and
classifiable, within the Headings 87.02, 87.03, 87.04, 87.05 and 87.11 of the Harmonized Commodity Description and
Coding System shall be as set out below and all Customs duties and other levies payable at the time of imporation shall be
claculated on the basis of   such value. This Order shall be in force from 01st March 2016 and continue until further notice.
CUSTOMS VALUATION OF MOTOR VEHICLES
The Customs value of motor vehicles of HS Heading 87.02, 87.03, 87.04, 87.05 and 87.11 shall be the aggregate
of the following items (i), (ii), (iii) and (iv).
(i)    Higher of the two alternative values of (a) and (b) below ;
(a) Value of the vehicle, determined by the Director General of Customs based on the price furnished
by the manufacturer of such vehicle.
(b) Invoiced Free on Board Value of the vehicle.
(ii) Cost of transport to the port of Sri Lanka
(iii) Loading, unloading and handing charges associated with the transport to the port of Sri Lanka.
(iv) Cost of insurance to the port of Sri Lanka
The Gazette Notification No. 1933/16 of 22nd September 2015 and the Gazette Notification No. 1937/3 of
19th October 2015, published under Article 10 of Schedule “E” of the Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2003 are
hereby repealed with effect from 01st March 2016. However for the purpose of determination of Customs value of
motor vehicles, imported based on the Letter(s) of Credit established on or before 18th October 2015 and not amended
subsequently, the Gazette Notification No. 1901/3 dated 10th February 2015 shall be deemed to be in effect and applicable.
