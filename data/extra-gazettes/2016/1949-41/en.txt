THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPULIC  OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that I acting under Paragraph (2) of Article 45 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, determined that withe effect from January 01, 2016, the assignment of Duties and Functions to the
Deputy Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs appointed under Paragraph (1) of Article 44 of
the Constitution, to be as indicated in the Schedule given below :
J
OHN E. A. AMARATUNGA M. P .,
Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
06th January 2016.
SCHEDULE
D
EPUTY MINISTER OF TOURISM DEVELOPMENT AND CHRISTIAN RELIGIOUS AFFAIRS
DUTIES   AND  FUNCTIONS
01. Formulation, Monitoring and Evaluation of Projects/Programmes in regard to tourism promotional activities
in consulation with the Minister.
02. Ensure success of the proposed registration process of “Inform sector” engaged in tourism related activities
illegally (eg. Hotels, Restaurants, Bars, Guides, Travel Agents, etc.) Island wide, so that by June 2016 they
become formal entities and to overlook this process along with Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
03. Review and monitor the progress in the implementation of domestic tourism promotional programmes.
04. Formulation, Monitoring and Evaluation of programmes implemented at divisional level in relation to Christian
Religious Affairs.
05. Matters relating to the development and regulation of tourist hotels and also emphasis on hotel classification
exercise.
06. Close rapport with and review, monitor and evaluate the affaris of the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority
and ensure productivity.
07. Matters relating to the training the human resources required in the tourism field.
08. Any other duty or fuction that may be entrusted by the Minister from time to time.
