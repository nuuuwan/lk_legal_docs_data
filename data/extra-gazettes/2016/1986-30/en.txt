102 (a) A fund that shall be hereinafter referred to as “Fund” in this Section cited as “Revenue
Incentive Fund” shall be established
(b) An amount appropriated annually by the Western Province Provincial for the purpose of
the Fund on behalf of each year shall be paid to the Fund
(c) (i) Where a revenue has been earned on a statement and/or assessment made by an
assessor considering the matters of conformation that the value stated in any
instrument, the turnover stated in a report has been stated falsely when inspecting
as per provisions of the statute ; and/or not producing reports prescribed to be
presented by any statute executed by the Provincial Commissioner of Revenue ;
and/or where as revenue has been collected when any tax/fee specified to be paid
by a person as per provisions of any statute executed by Provincial Commissioner
of Revenue has not been paid within the specified time period.
Twelve point five percent (12.5%) from the revenue collected thus.
(ii) Five percent from the revenue collected through fines imposed under any statute
executed by the Provincial Commissioner of Revenue.
Shall be credited to the Fund.
(d) From the amount earned under the above mentioned percentages, the incentive for the
officers shall be paid by the Fund according to a scheme formulated by the Commissioner
of Revenue and approved by the Minister of Finance.
(e) Provincial Commissioner of Revenue shall administer the Fund in compliance to the
proper procedure of the aforementioned scheme.
(f) The Commissioner, subjected to the approval of the Finance Minister shall formulate
regulations as to which principles and conditions should be incentives be paid for relevant
officers from the money lying at credit in the said Fund.
(g) Subjected to the approval of the Finance Minister, the following money and allowances
shall be paid from the Fund.
(i) Money required for officers' welfare in Provincial Department of Revenue ;
(ii) Collective incentive allowances for all the officers of the Provincial Department
of Revenue to ensure efficiency in execution of certain statute executed by the
Provincial Commissioner of Revenue.
(h) Be there any balance at credit in the said Fund subsequent to the payment of incentives to
officers at the end of financial year, it shall be credited to the Provincial Fund.
03. If there is an inconsistency between Sinhala, Tamil and English texts of this Statute, the Sinhala
text shall prevail.
