FIREARMS   ORDINANCE
Declaring a Period of Time for Firearms without Licence to be Handover
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 30 of the Firearms Ordinance, as amended by Ordinances No. 01 of
1920, No. 12 of 1945, No. 07 of 1947 and Acts No. 18 and 22 of 1955, No. 38 of 1975, No. 08 of 1980, No. 50 of 1981
and No. 22 of 1996 (Chapter 182), I, Maithripala Sirisena, Minister of Defence do hereby declare a period of time as
mentioned in the following Schedule to be enforced islandwide for firearms without licence to be handedover to the
government.
Further, when firearms without licence are handedover within the declared period of time, it is ordered that the
person handing over the firearm should not be fined or punished for an offence committed contrary to Section 22 of the
Firearms Ordinance with regard to keeping in custody, possession or utilization of the firearm before the date of hand
over.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
Minister of Defence.
Colombo,
01st March 2016.
SCHEDULE
01) Places to handover firearms without licence
i. Every District Secretariat ;
ii. Every Divisional Secretariat ;
iii. Every Police Station
02) Duration of time
From 9.00 a. m. of 25.04.2016 to 4.00 p. m. of 06.05.2016
