NOTICE   BY   THE   DIRECTOR   GENERAL   OF   CIVIL    AVIATION   UNDER   THE   AIR   NA VIGATION
REGULATION   196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that I have received on 06th September 2016 an application dated 05th July
2016 from Sakurai Aviation Limited, at No. 121/3, Kirulapone Avenue, Colombo 05, seeking from this office an Air
Operator Certificate/Airline Licence to engage in Domestic Scheduled Operations, Charter Operations and Aerial Work
to engage in Domestic Operations in pursuance of the Air Navigation Regulations of 1955. Business name of the Company
has been registered as Sakurai Aviation Limited and the trade name as “Sakurai Aviation Limited”. The Directors of the
Sakurai Aviation Limited are declared to be as follows.
1. Mr. Malape Gama Ethi Rallalage Asanga Prabath Yasawardana
2. Mr. Hapuarachchige Don Nimal Jayantha
3. Mr. Warnakulasuriya Rushan Malinda Fernando
Sakurai Aviation Limited has nominated Mr. Tharanga Sarathchandra Piyawansa to be the Accountable Manager in
charge of the proposed operations of air services.
A copy of the complete Application of the aforementioned applicant is kept at the Technical Library, Ground
Floor of Head Quarters of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 04, Hunupitiya Road, Colombo 02, for the
perusal of the members of the public, during office hours of any working days.
Members of the public are hereby informed that I have decided to commence after 07th November 2016 the
certification process of this application as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant’s compliance
with the applicable Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended practices specified by ICAO
and associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure,
efficient operation of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Airline Licence,
