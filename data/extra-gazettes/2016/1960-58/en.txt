No. 1960/58 –  THURSDAY, MARCH,  31, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My No.: MO/KTG/LND/03/AQSTN/2012/02.  
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.:4/3/2/2012/AS/316.  
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act,  that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a Privet 
Land Acquisition of Yala National Park. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of Land in TOPO PP. 25, Sheet No. P 
3/31, 32, 39, 40  Prepared by the superintendant  
Surveyor in Monaragala, situated in Katharagama Village 
in the Katharagama Divisional Secretary’s Division of the  
District of Monaragala in Uva Province described as 
follows:- 
 
1. Lot No. 1A 
 
Name of the Land: Kumburukele; Claimant: Ruhunu 
Maha Katharagama Dewalaya, Katharagama;  ;  Extent: 
179.7566 Hectares. 
 
2. Lot No. 1B 
 
Name of the Land: Kumburukele; Claimant: Ruhunu 
Maha Katharagama Dewalaya, Katharagama;    Extent: 
24.0991 Hectares. 
 
All Persons interested in the  aforesaid land hereby 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Katharagama Divisional Secretariat on the 25th Day of 
May 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby Requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or  before the 
18th day of May 2016 the nature of their interests in the 
Land, the particulars or their claims for Compensation for 
the said land the amount of compensation and the details 
of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
        S. C. WEERAKOON,                                       
        Divisional Secretary, 
        Katharagama. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Katharagama. 
28th  March, 2016.
