PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
IN terms of Section 10 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the Government Gazette No. 11959 dated
20th November 1959, it is hereby notified that Annual Esala Festival - 2016 of Sri Vishnu Maha Devala of Devinuwara,
Matara District will be held from 13th Wednesday of July to 27th Wednesday of July 2016.

Matara.
