CEYLON ELECTRICITY BOARD ACT, NO. 17 OF 1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands required by the
Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  SIYAMBALAPITIYA,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
16 th March 2016 .
The lands situated in the village of Kalukohuthenna in Grama Niladari's Division of No. 134 D, Kalukohuthenna in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Yatiyantota at Dehigampala Korale, in the Administrative District of Kegalle in the Province
of Sabaragamuwa of Sri Lanka containing in extents of 0.1265 (50 Perches) Hectares shown in Draft Plan No. BHP 001 dated
26.02.2016, depicted as Block No. 01 and 0.5691 (l Acre, 1 Rood and 25 Perches) Hectares shown in Draft Plan No. BHP 002
dated 26.02.2016, depicted as Block No. 02 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board and bounded as follows :
01. Block No. 01 - Land in extent of 0.1265 Hectares (50 Perches) with permanent building
North : A vissawella - Hatton main road ;
East : Not relevant ;
South : Road to Pokunawala village ;
West : Not relevant.
Claimed by : unknown.
02. Block No. 02 - Land in extent of 0.5691 Hectares (l Acre, I Rood and 25 Perches)

East : Kelani River ;
South : Kelani River ;
West : Gamsabha Road
