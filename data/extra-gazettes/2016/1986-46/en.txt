No. 1986/46 –   THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 29, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  Reference No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/05/05/09. 
  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section (7) 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire 
the Following lands which are required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Following allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 1 in 
extent 0.3729 Hectare in Plan No. R. 313 made by 
Surveor General, situated in Ratnapura (Division No. 3 
Veralupa, within Ratnapura Municipal Council area) 
Village of 152 B Veralupa Grama Niladhari Division of 
Uda Paththuwa sub Division in Kuruwita Koralaya, 
Divisional Secretary’s Division Rathnapura in the District 
of Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01   
Name of Land:Diwulgaha Kumbura, Mulwakkada, 
Godakele; Description: Open bare land; Claimant: 
Government; First Claimant: H. M. Shelton Premathilaka 
and Jayalal Premasinha, Demuwawatha, Rathnapura; 
Extent: 0.3729 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which 
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the 10.00 a.m. on 25th day of November 2016 at 
Rathnapura Divisonal Secretariat. 
 
Moreover, they are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing with two duplicates on or before the 18th day 
of November, 2016 the nature of their interest in land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and details of the computation of such amount. 
 
  
 C. M. KOTTAWATHTHA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rathnapura. 
 
At Rathnapura Divisional Secretariat, 
28th September, 2016.
