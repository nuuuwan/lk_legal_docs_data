The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  2 OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No.  2
of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of
the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development being of the opinion that the lands specified in the Schedules
hereto are urgently required for the improvement of Koswatta Junction and Town Development Programme do by this
order, declare that the said lands are required for such purpose.
    M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
16th November 2016.
SCHEDULE
Boundaries of the land are as follows an allotment of land marked as Lot A and B of land called Koswatta Co-
operative Filling Station and K. I. U. Campus depicted in sketch plan containing in extent of 18P (0.0455 Ha.) situated in
at the village of Thalangama North, within the limits of Kaduwela Municipal Council area, Palle Pattu of Hewagam Korale,
Kaduwela Divisional Secretary’s Division in Colombo District, Western Province :
Land called Koswatta Co-operative Filling Station
(Lot No. A - 13p.)
North : Premises of K. I. U. Campus and road reservation and Lot No. B in Sketch Plan ;
East : Kaduwela road ;
South : Asst. No. 247, Koswatta, Thalangama building claimed by Idamgodage Amara Jayalath Perera ;
West : Building of K. I. U. Campus.
