The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
MINISTRY   OF  WOMEN  AND   CHILD  AFFAIRS
IT is hereby notified that from among the subjects and functions of Women and Child Affairs assigned to me through
Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1933/13 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated 21.09.2015, subjects and
functions mentioned in the following Schedule are assigned to the State Minister of Child Affairs, exercising the powers
vested in me under paragraph 5 of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
     K. D. M. C
HANDRANI BANDARA,
Minister of Women and Child Affairs.
Ministry of Women and Child Affairs,
6th Floor, 11nd Stage,
Sethsiripaya,
Battaramulla,
23rd November 2016.
Schedule
01. Supervision of the Implementation of policies and programmes on early childhood care and development
aimed at bringing up a physically and mentally healthy child, coordination and liaison with provincial level
committees, and monitoring of activities.
02. Supervision of the special programme on providing nutrition food with value of Rs. 20,000 to expecting mothers.
03. Supervision of the programme on glass of Fresh milk for children between 3-5 of age.
