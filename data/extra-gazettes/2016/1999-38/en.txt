PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
UVA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
My No. 01/10/03/04
Appointments   made by  the  Governor  of   Uva  Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mr. W. V . P. Nandapala, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, to perform the duties of the
Director of Finance of Uva Province, from the date 31.08.2016 to 14.09.2016 till the return of Mr. W. M. N.
Wickramasinghe to the Island who is the Director of Finance of Uva Province.
2) Community Physician Consultant Mr. Dr. Nimal Shantha Gamagedera, to perform the Duties of the Provincial
Director Health Service of Uva Province, from the date 12.09.2016 to 27.09.2016 till the return of Mrs. Dr.
M. K. D. Senanayake to the Island who is the Provincial Director Health Service of Uva Province.
3) Mrs. T. B. W. Chandrani de Silva, retired officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, has been re-appointed
on contract basis as Assistant Secretary to the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural
Affairs and Textiles and Small Industries of Uva Province, with immediate effect from the date 07.10.2016
till the appointment of permanent officer or up to 20.04.2017.
4) Miss K. I. Anusha, Class III Officer of the Sri  Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Provincial Land Commissioner of Uva Province, from the date 10.10.2016 to 15.10.2016 till the return of
Mr. W. D. P. C. Samarasekera to the Island, who is the Provincial Land Commissioner of Uva Province.
5) Mr. M. M. J. S. Wijekoon, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the duties of the
Director to road of Uva Province, from the date 15.10.2016 to 23.10.2016 till the return of Mr. K. A. B.
Senevirathna to the Island, who is the Director to road of Uva Province.
