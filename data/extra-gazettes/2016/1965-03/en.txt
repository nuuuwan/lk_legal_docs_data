 APPROV AL   FOR   THE   AMENDED   DEVELOPMENT   PLAN   PREPARED   FOR   THE   KEGALLE
URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   AREA
GENERAL public is hereby noticed that the Amended Development Plan prepared for the Kegalle Urban Council area in
accordance with the powers vested under Section 8“H” of the Urban Development Authority Act, (Amended) bearing
No. 04 of 1982 has been approved on 19.04.2016 by the Hon. Patalee Champika Ranawaka the Minister of Megapolis and
Western Development by virtue of the powers vested with him under Section 8“F” of the Urban Development Authority
Act, (Amended) No. 4 of 1982.
R
ANJIT F ERNANDO ,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
26th April 2016.
Approval for the Amended Development Plan prepared for the Kegalle Urban Development Area
I, Patalee Champika Ranawaka the Minister of Megapolis and Western Development by virtue of the powers
vested with me under Section 8“H” of the Urban Development Authority Act, (Amended) No. 4 of 1982 hereby approved
the Amended Development Plan prepared for the Kegalle Urban Area of the Kegalle Urban Council jurisdiction considering
the recommendation made by the Board of Management of the Urban Development Authority at its meeting held on
05.09.2012.
P
ATALEE C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and
Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,
17th Floor, “Suhurupaya”,
Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
19th April, 2016.
