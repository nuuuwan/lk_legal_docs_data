THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF    SRI LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that I have in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (5) of Article 44 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined that with effect from 15th February 2016, the assignment of
Duties and Functions, Departments, Statutory Institutions and Public Corporations to the State  Minister of International
Trade appointed under paragraph (1) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to be
as indicated in the Schedule given below.
M
ALIK SAMARAWICKRAMA,
Minister of  Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
1st  February 2016.
