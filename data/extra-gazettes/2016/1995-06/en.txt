THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT, (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
My No.: Do/L/1/Ath/Adi/108.
Land Min. Reference No.: 4/3/6/2012/HW/229.
I, hereby notify under Sub-section (7) of the Land Acquisition
Act as amended by the land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.
28 of 1964 that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for public purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :
Allotments of the land situated at Sapugahawatta Village in the
Grama Niladhari’s Division, Sapugahawatta of the Divisional
Secretariat, Dodangoda in Kalutara District in Western Province,
depicted from Lot No. 01 to Lot No. 02 of  P .P .Ka.4527 & dated
2016.10.25 made by the Surveyor General, containing in extent
about 0.0194 Hectare and boundaries of the land given below:
1. Lot No.01—Name of the Land: Katakarallagahawatta; Land
Use: Gardan; Land ownership: State;  First Claimant:U.K.D.
Jinasena, Sapugahawtta, Dodangoda; Extent: about
0.0033Hectare.
North: Katakarallagahawatta claimed by U.K.D. Jinasena and
Mahesh De Silva and Lot No. 02; East: Lot No. 02; South: Road,
(Road Development Authority); West: Road, (Road Development
Authority) and Lot No. 55 of P .P .Ka. 3557
2. Lot No.02—Name of the Land: Katakarallagaha
watta; Land Use: Gardan; Land ownership: State;  First
Claimant: Mahesh De Silva, Sapugahawtta, Dodangoda;
Extent: about 0.0161 Hectare.
North: Katakarallagahawatta claimed by  Mahesh De
Silva and ; East: Road, (Road Development Authority);
South: Road, (Road Development Authority) and Lot
No. 01; West:  Lot No. 01 and Katakarallagahawatta
claimed by U.K.D. Jinasena.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at the
Divisional Secretariat, Dodangoda on 21.12.2016 at 10.00
a.m. Further, I hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 16.12.2016 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation of the said land/servitude, the amount
of compensation and the details of compensation of such
amount.
R.M. DARSHANA RANASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Dodangoda.
Divisional Secretariat at the Dodangoda,
22nd November 2016.
