I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act. No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
servitude which is required for the public purpose of the 
reconstruction of Polwathumodara bridge. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Lands depicted in the survey plan bearing No. 
P.P.MR/2644, situated in the villages of Pelana North, 
Pelana South and Polwathumodara in Grama Niladari 
Divisions of Pelana North, Pelana South and 
Polwathumodara in Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Weligama, Matara District. This acquisition is made for 
the reconstruction of Polwathumodara bridge.
