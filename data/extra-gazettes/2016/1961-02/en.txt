THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF 1971
Order  under Section 8

Home Affairs, do by this order declare that, as regards the year 2017, each of  the days specified in the Schedule hereto
shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
