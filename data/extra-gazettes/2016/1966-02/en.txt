THE  CONSTITUTION  OF   THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) of
Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1933/13 of 21st September 2015 and amended from time to time, is further amended with effect from April 27, 2016
in the manner set out in the Schedule below :
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
04th May 2016.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the Heading, “Minister of Justice (No. 27)” of the said Notification, as follows :-
(a) In Column I thereof, by the insertion immediately after item II thereof, of the following items ;
“12. All matters relating to the Assistance to and Protection of Victims of Crime and Witnesses Act, No.
4 of 2015 ;
13. All other subjects that come under the purview of Organizations listed in Column II ;
14. Supervision of the Organizations listed in Column II”
(b) In Column III thereof, by the insertion immediately after the expression “Public Trustee Ordinance, No. 1
of 1922” of the following item :-
“Assistance to and Protection of Victims of Crime and Witnesses Act, No. 4 of 2015”
