Appointment   made by  the  Governor  of   Uva  Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Section 154F. Sub sections (4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce
that I have made the following appointment :
Hon : Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Kumarasiri Rathnayake.
Minister of Health and Indigenous Medicine, Probation and
Childcare, Women’s Affairs and Social Welfare of the Uva
Provincial Council.
M. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Governor,
Uva Province.
Office of the Governor - Uva,
08th May, 2016.
