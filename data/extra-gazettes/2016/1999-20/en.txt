CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
By-Laws
IT is hereby notified that the By-Laws relating to the Disaster Management, in the Municipal Councils, Urban Councils
and Pradeshiya Sabhas of Central Province, made by the Minister of Local Government in the Central Provincial Council,
by virtue of power vested in him under Sub section I of Section (2) of the Chapter 261, Local Authorities (Consequential
Provisions) Act, No. 06 of 1952, read together with Paragraph (a) of the Sub section (1) of Section 2 of Provincial
Councils (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 12 of 1989, published in the Part IV (A), Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 1960/70, dated 01.04.2016, has been seconded by the Central Provincial Council.
Secretary,
Local Government and Chief Ministry,
Central Provincial Council.
Chief Ministry Office,
Central Provincial Council,
Pallekele,
29th September 2016.
