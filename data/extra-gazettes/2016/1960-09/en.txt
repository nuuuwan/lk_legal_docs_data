THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.   16  OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Educations and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order establishing the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the
provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1819/27 of July 18, 2013) by the addition immediately after
the entry “Faculty of Engineering”  appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column II thereof, of the
following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology 1. Department of Bio Systems Technology
2. Department of Engineering Technology
3. Department of Inter Disciplinary Studies”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th March, 2016.
