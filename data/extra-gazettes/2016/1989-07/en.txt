HAMBANTOTA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Programmed Budget - 2016
IT is hereby notified that under Section 212 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) the programmed budget
for the year 2017 of Hambantota Municipal Council is open for public inspection at this office during working hours from
21st October to 28th October 2016 at the Hambantota Municipal Council Office (except on Sundays and Public Holidays).

ASUN E PA SENEVIRATHNA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Hambantota Municipal Council.
