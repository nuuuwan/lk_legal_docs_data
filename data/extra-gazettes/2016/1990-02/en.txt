No. 1990/2  –  MONDAY, OCTOBER 24, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 13/97/FR/199. 
My Reference No.:Wa/L/2/Ath/17. 
  
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
      Description of the Land to be acquired: 
  
Lot  No. 385 allotment in total extent of about 
11.5688 Hectares, of land in Final Village Plan No. 87, 
Supplement No. 10 and Lot Nos. 114, 117 in total extent 
of about 10.7230, of land in Final Village Plan No. 80, 
Supplement No. 06, prepared by the Surveyor General 
Situated in the Village of Gammana in the Divisonal 
Secretary’s Division of Walallawita in Kaluthara District 
in Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 385 
Name of Land: Dediyaowita, Gammanwelaowita, 
Liyanagedara alias Gamanwelaowita, Gangaaddaradeniya, 
Kadolakerewa alias  Gangaaddaradeniya and                    
Benthara-gangaaddaradeniya; Description: Jungle and 
Wetland; Original Claimant: Cannot be found; Extent: 
11.5688 Hectares. 
 
2. Lot No. 114 
Name of Land: Kadola, Ambagasthuduwekumbura 
and Kiriligahaowita; Description: Jungle and Wetland; 
Original Claimant: Cannot be found; Extent: 8.3570 
Hectares. 
 
3. Lot No. 117 
Name of Land: Kadola, Kiriligahaowita; Description: 
Jungle and Wetland; Original Claimant: Cannot be found; 
Extent: 2.3660 Hectares. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Walallawita 
on 23rd November, 2016 at 10.00 a. m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 16th November, 2016 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said                        
land/servitude the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 D. R. JAYASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Walallawita. 
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Walallawita, 
03rd October, 2016.
