Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE   RUBBER   CULTIV ATION   AND  RAW   RUBBER   PROCESSING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Mr. J. N. Hettiarchchi has been appointed
as the Employers’ Representatives member of the Wages Board for the Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Trade in place
of Mr. N. S. Jayawardhana by the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 9 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136).
This Appointment is valid only up to 01.01.2018.
