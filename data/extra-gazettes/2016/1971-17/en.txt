(10) Sub- paragraph 11.2  is hereby repealed and the following new sub- paragraph 11.2 substituted therefore :-
11.2 Method of Selection :-
Selection for posts of Director, District Director of Agriculture, Additional  Director, Registrar of
Pesticides Control , Principle Agriculture Scientist and  Principle Agriculturist will be made in accordance
with a procedure approved  by the Public Service Commission by calling applications from the officers
who have three(03) years experience in Grade I of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service and afterwards
calling candidates out of them, not more than thrice the  existing vacancies according to the seniority of
Grade I for the interview.
In instances officers with three(03) years experience in Grade I have not applied  for the posts, selection
will be made in accordance with a procedure approved by the Public Service Commission by calling
applications from Officers in Grade I of the Sri Lanka Agricultural  Service and afterwards calling candidates
out of  them , not more than thrice of the existing vacancies according  to the  order of Seniority of Grade
I for the interview.
Selection for posts of Deputy Director, Deputy Registrar of Pesticides Control  will be  made in accordance
with a procedure approved by the Public Service Commission by calling applications from  officers in
Grade I or Officers who have three(03) years experience in Grade II of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service
and afterwards, calling candidates, out of them, not more than thrice of  the existing vacancies according
to the order of Seniority for the interview.
In instances, officers in Grade I or officers who have  three(03) years experience  in Grade II have not
applied for the posts , selection will be made in accordance with a procedure approved by the Public
Service Commission by calling applications from Officers in Grade II of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service
and   afterwards, calling candidates out of them, not more than thrice of the existing  vacancies according
to the order of Seniority in Grade II for the interview.
