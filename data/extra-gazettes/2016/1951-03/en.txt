THE  MONETARY  LAW  ACT,  REGISTERED   STOCK  AND  SECURITIES  ORDINANCE  AND  LOCAL
TREASURY   BILLS  ORDINANCE
It is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Monetary Board under the provisions of the Monetary Law
Act (Chapter 422), Registered Stock and Securities Ordinace (Chapter 420) and Local Treasury Bills Ordinance (Chapter
417), the Monetary Board, in terms of Sections 5 and 86 of the Monetary Law Act, Regulation 11 of the Local Treasury
Bills (Primary Dealers) Regulations No. 01 of 2009 dated 24.06.2009 and Regulation 11 of the Registered Stock and
Securities (Primary Dealers) Regulations No. 01 of 2009 dated 24.06.2009 made by the Minister of Finance under the
aforesaid Local Treasury Bills Ordinance and the Registered Stock and Securities Ordinance, respectively, having
considered the serious regulatory and supervisory concerns on the affairs of the Entrust Securities PLC, its weak financial
position, has decided, with effect from 04 January 2016, to suspend the Board of Directors of the said Entrust Securities
PLC, to vest the management of the said Entrust Securities PLC with the National Savings Bank, and to appoint the Board
of Directors of the National Savings Bank to act as the Board of Directors of the said Entrust Securities PLC until further
notice in the interest of maintaining monetary, banking and financial system stability of the country.

AKSHMAN ARJUNA M AHENDRAN ,
