No. 1981/97 – FRIDAY, AUGUST 26, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of below name lands in total extent of about 0.0589
Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. Ka/DDN/15/593 and dated
23/12/2015, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
of Kethhena in Divisional Secretary's Division of Dodangoda in the
Kaluthara District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Paramulle Owita – Extent: 0.0130 Hectare
North: Paramulle Owita claimed by Don Chandrani; East:
Paramulle owita claimed by Road(R. D. A.); South: Paramulle Owita
claimed by Road(R. D. A.) and Lot B; West: Lot B, Paramulle Owita
claimed by Paramullage Don Pemawathi, Paramullage Don Sriyani
and Paramullage Don Chandrani Malkanthi.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Paramulle Owita – Extent: 0.0312 Hectare
North: Paramulle Owita claimed by Paramullage Don Pemawathi,
Paramullage Don Sriyani and Paramulle Owita claimed by Paramullage
Don Chandrani Malkanthi and Lot A; East: Lot A and Paramulle
Owita claimed by Road(R. D. A.); South: Paramulle Owita claimed
by Road(R. D. A.); West: Iriyangalla Canal, Paramulle Owita claimed
by Paramullage Don Pemawathi and  Paramullage Don Sriyani.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Iriyangalladeniya – Extent: 0.0147 Hectare
North: Klaidewatta, Iriyangalladeniya claimed by Superintendent
and Iriyangalla Canal; East: Iriyangalladeniya claimed by Road
(R. D. A.); South: Iriyangalladeniya claimed by Road (R. D. A.);
West: Klaidewatta, Iriyangalladeniya claimed by Superintendent.
