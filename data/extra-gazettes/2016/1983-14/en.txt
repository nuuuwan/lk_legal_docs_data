PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
PAYMENT  DEVICES  FRAUDS  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2006
Order under Section 8(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Sub-section (1) of Section 8 of the Payment Devices Frauds Act, No. 30 of
2006, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby appoint the following
persons and the Institution to the Panel of Experts for the purposes of Sub-section (1) of Section 8 of the aforesaid Act.
1. Mr. K. G. P. Sirikumara
2. Mrs. C.  C. P. Moragoda
3. Mr. Ashan De Soysa
4. Dr. C. R. De Silva
5. Mr. Jerome C. Henricus
6. Sri Lanka Computer Emergency Response Team (SLCERT)
The Order made under Sub-section (1) of the Payment Devices Frauds Act, No. 30 of 2006, and published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1536/28 of February 14, 2008 is hereby rescinded, without prejudice to anything duly done
thereunder.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
31st August 2016.
