STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  the deprival of allowances such as Interim Allowance 01 Cost of Living Allowance Adjustment, Meal Cost, etc., which had been
enjoyed by Mr. A. Supesan, Mr. E. K. S. Senadeera, Mr. A. P . Gamage, Mr. O. C. N. Jayawardana and Mrs. K. H. G . B. Nalani working at
Sri Lanka Insurance Corporation Ltd. until the promotion to the Post of Branch Sales Manager on 15.10.2010 is just, and if not so, to what
reliefs each of them is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
