Government  Notifications
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2 OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the North Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the North Central Province Provincial Council by reason of Death ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Nongomu Vithanalage
Samantha to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Nongomu Vithanalage Samantha, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the
Administrative District of Polonnaruwa in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA, N. J. A BEYSEKERE, S. R A TNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE,
