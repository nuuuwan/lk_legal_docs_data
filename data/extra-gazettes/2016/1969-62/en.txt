PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
Order No. 38.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09 OF  2003
Order  under Section  20(5)
THE Consumer Affairs Authority under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Authority Act, No. 09 of
2003, do by this Order rescind Order No. 33 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1908/9 dated 31st March 2015, in
respect of the Maximum Retail Price of Cement.
                                        By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority.
HASITHA TILLEKERATNE,
 Chairman.
Colombo,
26th May 2016.
