WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment  made  by  the  Governor of   the  Western  Province
BY virtue  of  powers vested in me under Section 154 F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka,  I, K. C. Logeswaran, Governor of the Western Province, do hereby appoint  Murukkuwadura Sumith
Lal Mendis as the   Minister of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Child Care Services, Women’s
Affairs and Council Affairs  of  the Western Province with effect from 08.02.2016.
K. C. L
OGESWARAN,
Governor of the Western Province.
Secretariat of the Governor, Western Province,
Level 05, Rotunda Tower,
No. 109, Galle Road,
Colombo 03,
23rd  February, 2016.
