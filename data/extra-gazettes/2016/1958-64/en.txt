WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between –

and

as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of
1968) hereby appoint, Mr. P . Danansooriya, No. 114/1, Moratuwatta, Paththalagedara, Weyangoda, to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is.-
Whether Mr. M. I. V . Muneer who is in the service of the Batticaloa Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board as an Internal Audit
Officer is entitled to receive arrears of salary in respect of the period of interdiction of his service from 12.02.2007 to 31.08.2007 taking into
account that he was exempted from all the charges by letter No. YS%,x.u$ke'm%d$wNsu$2007$01 dated 07.11.2007 issued by the Appeal Board
of the said Board and if so entitled to what quantum of payment is due to him as arrears of salary.
