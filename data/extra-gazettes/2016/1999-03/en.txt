The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART  IV (A) — PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Proclamations made by Governors by virtue of powers entrusted under Paragraph (8)(a) of Clause 154B of the
Constitution
To all whom those presents shall come :
I, Kanapathipillai Cathiravelpillai Logeswaran the Governor of the Western Province, in accordance with the powers vested
in me by Paragraph (8)(a) in clause 154B of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby
summon the Western Province Provincial Council to meet on 29th December 2016 at 9.30 a. m. in order to consider the
Bill of Development (Special Provision) issued on 25th November 2016 in the Council Chamber of the Provincial Council
Building Complex situated at No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
K. C. L
OGESWARAN,
Governor of the Western Province.
Secretariat of the Governor,
109, 5th Floor,
Rotunda Tower, Galle Road,
Colombo 03,
26th December 2016.
