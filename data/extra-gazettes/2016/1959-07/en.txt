WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
INVESTIGATION   CONDUCTED   UNDER   SECTION   2   OF   THE   CHAPTER   No.   04   OF   1991   RELATING
TO   POWERS   OF   SUPERVISION   OF   ADMINISTRATION   OF   THE   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT
INSTIUTIONS
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 2 of the Charter No. 04 of 1991 relating to powers of supervision of
administration of the Local Government Institutions I, Isura Devapriya, the Chief Minister of Western Provincial Councils
and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial
Administration, Economic Promotion, Power and Energy, Environment Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism
Affairs, do hereby appoint Mr. Kumara Hewage Sumithrapala, Retired Judge of the High Court, as an Investigation Committee
of one member, to investigate and submit a report within three months as to whether the offences specified under Section
2(1) of the above Charter have been committed by  W. Samanlal Fernando whilst holding office as the Mayor of Moratuwa
Municipal Council.
Matters/Charges which are to be investigated.
1. You have committed, whilst holding office as the Mayor of the Municipal Council Moratuwa, the offences
mentioned in Section/s 2(i)(a) and/or 2(i)(c) of the Charter No. 04 of 1991 by way of granting approval to fell
and remove the teak wood trees  in the cemetery of Rawatawatta under the administration of Municipal Council
Moratuwa during the period from 14.11.2015 to 07.12.2015 or a close period, without obtaining proper approval
from the Municipal Council.
2. You have committed, whilst holding office as the Mayor of the Municipal Council Moratuwa, the offences
mentioned in Section/s 2(i)(a) and/or 2(i)(c) of the Charter No. 04 of 1991 by way of taking action to enter into
an agreement with W. E. Security Services Pvt. Ltd., at No. 26/1/1, Church Road, Rathnapura to maintain security
services within the premises of the Municipal Council Moratuwa without obtaining proper approval from the
Municipal Council and/or getting approval of the Council for the agreement.
3. You have committed, whilst holding office as the Mayor of the Municipal Council Moratuwa, the offences
mentioned in Section/s 2(i)(a) and/or 2(i)(c) of the Charter No. 04 of 1991 by not implementing the Court
Order issued to remove the unathorized construction at No. 163A, Station Road, Moratuwa and/or making an
environment not to implement the said order during the period from 10.07.2009 to 30.08.2010 or a close
period.
