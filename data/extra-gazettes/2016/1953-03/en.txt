No. 1955/3 –   TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 23, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 400)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: BIN/3/9/AC/20. 
 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/8/2012/PC/405. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire Description of the land to 
be acquired. 
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
An allotment of land shown as Lot No.34 in 
Supplement No. 02, sheet No. 02 of the Final Village 
Plan No. 2095 in the village of Moragolla, Karadapattu 
Korale Minor Division, Bingiriya Divisional Secretary’s 
Division, Kurunegala District, North Western Province. 
 
Lot No. 34 —  Name of land: Mylagahamulahena; 
Description: Garden, Contains 15 Coconut trees         
30 - 40 Years Old, One Kumbuk tree 25 - 30 Years 
old, One Kohomba tree 25 - 30 years old; Claimant: 
Mr. L. A. S. Mahinda Lekam, Moragolla, Nagolla -
goda; Extent: 0.1417 Hectare. 
 
Boundaries : 
 
North: Lot No. 22 B; East: Lot No. 22A and 22; 
South: Lot No. 22 and 9; West: Lot No. 9 and 22B. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are here by 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Bingiriya on the 04th day of May, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are here by requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 20th day of April, 2016 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 M. D. SOMARATHNE,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Bingiriya. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Bingiriya, 
17th February, 2016.
