THE   UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act in relation to the establishing of the Wayamba University of
Sri Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1093/8 of August 17, 1999 in the Schedule thereto as follows :-
1. by the addition immediately after the item “Department of Biotechnology” appearing in Column 2 which
corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Agriculture and Plantation Managment” in Column I thereof, of the
following new item :-
“Department of Biosystems Engineering”
2. by the addition immediately after the entry “Faculty of Livestock, Fisheries and Nutrition” appearing in
Column I and its corresponding entries in Column 2 thereof, of the following :-
