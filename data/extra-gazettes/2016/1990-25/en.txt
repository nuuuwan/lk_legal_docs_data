PUBLIC  SERVICE  COMMISSION
Appointment of the Committee in terms of Article 56(1) and 56(2) of the Constitution
01. As directed by the Public Service Commission, the following three members Committee is hereby appointed in terms
of the Article 56(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in respect of the Health Service
Sector.
02. Hereinafter the Committee will be referred as Health Service Committee.
03. The Health Service Committee is consisted with following members.
I. Mr. W. M. Bandusena (Chairman)
II. Ms. S. P.  Wellappili (Member)
III. Dr. C. K. Shanmugarajah (Member)
04. The Health Service Committee shall come into force with effect from the 01st day of November, Two Thousand
and Sixteen (2016).
                                                                                By order of the Public Service Commission.
