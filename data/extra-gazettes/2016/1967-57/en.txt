SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of list
of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from 21st
May 2016, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1960/59 of 01.04.2016  in respect of Red Onions, Seeds of Cumin and Seeds of fennel  (H.S Code
No. 0703.10.10, 0909.31, 0909.32 and 0909.61.20) are rescinded with effect from  21st May 2016.
R
AVI K ARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
20th May, 2016.
