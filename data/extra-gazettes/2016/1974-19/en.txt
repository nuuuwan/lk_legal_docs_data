Government  Notifications
SRI   LANKA  INSTITUTE  OF   ARCHITECTS
NEW Registration and Renewal of Registration of “Chartered Architects”, “Architects” and “Architectural Licentiates” for
the year 2016/2017 under the Architects Registration Board, established as per the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects
(Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 1996.
Reference is made to the notes appearing on pages 1A, 2A, 3A, 4A and 5A of the Gazette (Extraordinary )
No. 1015/19 of Friday, the 20th February, 1998 of the Democratic Socilaist Republic of Sri Lanka with regard to the
Registration of persons who are eligible to practice Architecutre in Sri Lanka under the Categories “Chartered Architects”,
“Architects” and “Architectural Licentiates”. The interpretations of the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects (Amendment) Act,
No. 14 of 1996 in the above Gazette Notification will apply in all respects to the list published below.
The Architects Registration Board of the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects as per the functions and powers vested
upon it by the said Act of Parliament, has now prepared the list of the “Chartered Architects”, “Architects” and “Architectural
Licentiates” who are eligible to be Gazette for the year 2016/2017 (7th July, 2016 to 31st January, 2017).
The Architects Registration Board directed me to publish the Names, Addresses and Registration Numbers of the
“Chartered Architects”, “Architects” and “Architecural Licentiates” who have been registered as given below.
This registration expires on 31st January 2017.
S. R. B
OPAGE,
Registrar,
Architects Registration Board.

Colombo 07,
