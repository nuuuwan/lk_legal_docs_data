THE LAND ACQUISITION  AMENDMENT ACT,  
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/05/2008/D/262. 
My Reference No.: KDS/MDB/4/2/1/209. 
 
I, hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire. 
The following Land which is required for the 5th 
(Volunteer) Sri Lanka Singha Regiment.   
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land called Victoria Watta in total 
extent of 2.1730 hectares depicted as Lot No. 01 and 02 
in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6362 dated 20.02.2015 
prepared by the Survey General, situated in the village 
called Maberiyathenna in the Grama Niladhari Division 
No. 827 of Maberiyathenna of Palispattu West Korale 
minor  Division in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Medadumbara in Kandy District of the Central Province 
and more fully described as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Victoria Watta; Description: 
Garden containing mixed cultivation and nine permanent 
buildings; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Land 
Reform Commission; Plan No.: Maha 6362; Extent: 
2.0832 Hectares. 
2. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of the Land: Victoria Watta; Description: 
Garden containing mixed cultivation; Claimant: State; 
Plan No.: Maha 6362; Extent: 0.0898 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands   are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by Agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Medadumbara 
Divisional Secretariat  on the 23rd March, 2016 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 16th day of March, 2016. 
The nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for  compensation for the acquisition of the 
said lands, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 
                                                  J. M. J. K. JAYASUNDARA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Medadumbara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Medadumbara,
