FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
MORATUWA   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Moratuwa Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to
which the former member, Wedige Nimal Yasasiri Peiris belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as
a member of that Moratuwa Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Mahamarakkalage
Berty Ishan Suranga Perera being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Moratuwa Municipal Council acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Mahamarakkalage Berty Ishan Suranga
Perera  elected as a member of the Moratuwa Municipal Council.
S
AMAN S RI R ATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Moratuwa Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
13th  May, 2016.
