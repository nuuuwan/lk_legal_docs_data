REGULATIONS
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 75 (1) (a) of the Finance Statute, No. 01 of 2014 of the
Northern Provincial Council, read with Section 35 of that Statute. I, Canagasabapathy Viswalingam Wigneswaran
Chief Minister and Minister of Finance Northern Provincial Council, declare that Stamp Duty shall be chargeable with
effect from 08th July 2015 on every instrument specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto at the rates specified in the
corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
