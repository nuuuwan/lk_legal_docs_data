ORDER   UNDER   SECTION   34(2)  OF  THE  REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  08 OF  2016
BY virtue of powers vested with me under Section 34(2) of the Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 08 of
2016, I, S. B. Navinna, the Minister of Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs hereby ordered that,
the Sections 6, 9, 14, 16 and 17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation (except the
registration of persons regulations 1990 published in the Gazette No. 637/6 dated 1990.11.20) made under that Act in
force immediately prior to the commencement of the Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 08 of 2016, shall
continue to be in operation up to 30.06.2017, until the necessary infrastruture arrangements and technological
methodologies are made to give effect to the amendments made to Sections 6, 9, 14, 16 and 17 of the Registration of
Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968, by the Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 08 of 2016.
S. B. N
A VINNA,
The Minister of Internal Affairs,
Wayamba Development and Cultrual Affairs.
at Battaramulla,
01st of August 2016.
