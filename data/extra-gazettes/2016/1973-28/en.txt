THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.   16  OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 916/7
of March 27, 1996  (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1964/11 of April 26, 2016)
which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the South Eastern University of Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately
after the item “Faculty of Engineering”  appearing in Column I of the Schedule and its corresponding entries in Column II
of the Schedule of the following item and entries :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology Department of Bio Systems Technology
Department of  Information and
Communication Technology”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
27th June, 2016.
