EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                                 My Reference No.: අǩ/5/3/11/1/10. 
 
Land Ministry Ref No.: 4/3/9/2012/WS/366. 
  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land for the 
construction of water tank and quarters under the 
Anuradapura North water supply project. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 149 in 
depicted preliminary plan No. FVP 627 prepared on 
31.08.2016 by the Anuradapura District Superintendent 
of surveys in total Extent of 4047 Hectare situated in No. 
55 Ethakada Grama Niadhari Division in the village of 
Ethakada in the Divisional Secretaries Division of 
Madawachchiya in Anuradapura District and bounded as 
following. 
01. Lot No. 149 —  Name of the land: Ethakada -
landa Kele; Description: Garden contain mixed 
cultivation; Claimant of the land: Not Known. 
 
North: Lot Nos. 150, 290 and 148; East: Lot Nos. 148 
and 147; South: Lot Nos. 147 and 150; West: Lot No. 
150. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional  
Secretariat Madawachchiya on the 16th day of 
November, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
16th day of November, 2016 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
   
 D. M. SANTHA DASANAYAKA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Madawachchiya. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Madawachchiya, 
21st October, 2016.
