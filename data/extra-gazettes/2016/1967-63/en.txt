NOTIFICATION  BY  MR.  SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Wednesday, 25th May,
2016 at 1.00 p.m.
I, Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order No. 14 of the Parliament,
I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Wednesday the 25th May, 2016 at 1.00 p. m. having been requested so to do
by the Hon. Prime Minister.
K
ARU JAY ASURIYA,
Speaker.
Sri Jayawardenepura, Kotte,
20th May 2016.
