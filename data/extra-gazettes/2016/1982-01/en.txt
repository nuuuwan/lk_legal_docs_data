CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I,  A. K. Seneviratne,  Acting  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 29th  August, 2016, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance   (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1981/1   of   22nd  August,
2016 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification to be
published on or before 04th  September,  2016.
 A. K. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
26th  August,  2016.
