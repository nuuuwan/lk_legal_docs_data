I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Five Allotments of land called Goluwapokunawatta in 
total extent of 39.8694 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 01 to 
05 in the Priliminary Plan Gam. 3993 prepared by the 
Senior Superintendent of Surveys - Gampala, situated in 
the Village called Kadirana in Dasiya Pattuwa Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Katana 
in Gampaha District of the Western Province and more 
fully described as follows. 
 
Schedule 
 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Goluwapokunawatta;  Description: 
Open waste land, part of a permanent building with 
roofing sheets available; Claimant: Could not be traced; 
Extent:  0.5299 Hectare. 
2. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of the Land: Goluwapokunawatta;  Description: 
Coconut estate, Coconut trees of about 25 -30 years old 
are available, two tile roofed permanent buildings, two 
permanent buildings with roofs of galvanized sheets, one 
temporary building with a roof of galvanized sheets, part 
of a permanent building with a roof of galvanized sheets, 
two permanent buildings with roofs of asbestos sheets 
and four toilets available; Claimant: Could not be traced; 
Extent:  38.5053 Hectares. 
 
3. Lot No. 3 
 
Name of the Land: Goluwapokunawatta;  Description: 
Open waste land; Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent:  
0.2913 Hectare. 
 
4. Lot No. 4 
 
Name of the Land: Goluwapokunawatta;  Description: 
Road; Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent:  0.2043 
Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 5 
 
Name of the Land: Goluwapokunawatta;  Description: 
Home Garden, part of a permanent building with a tile 
roof, a toilet and cement constructed well available; 
Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent:  0.3386 Hectare.

authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional

Following lands mentioned in the preliminary Plan 
No. 2647 of Surveyor General Matara and situated in

Walasgala west in Dickwella Divisional Secretariat of 
Matara District. 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Godekoratuwa, Dumkoratuwa 
alias Dumwatta alias Godallahena;  Description: Open 
bare land ; Claimant: Government; Extent:  0.0360 
Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of the Land: Godekoratuwa, Dumkoratuwa 
alias Dumwatta; Description: Road; Claimant: 
Government; Extent:  0.0015 Hectare.
