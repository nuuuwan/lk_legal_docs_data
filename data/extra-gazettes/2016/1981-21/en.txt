Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 19.11.2010 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

of Labour dated 08.11.2010.

and
University Grants Commission, No. 20,Ward Place, Colombo 07, of the other part

Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies

as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of  1968)

and
University Grants Commission, No. 20,Ward Place, Colombo 07, of the other part.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the demand made by Mr. D. G . Wijeratne who held the post of Staff Assistant (Book - Keeper) of the University of Peradeniya that
the promotion to the post of  Assistant Internal Officer which was granted to him by the University Grants Commission should be  back
dated to 05.02.2008 and the said post be designated as the Assistant Bursar and be paid arrears of salary and other allowances accordingly
is justified and to what relief  he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 25th day of July, 2016.
