and
National Housing Development Authority, No. 34, Sir Chittampalam A. Gardiner Mawatha, Colombo 02, of the other part,
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are –
01. Whether Mr. D. K. Shantha Senaratne who was appointed to the post of Accountant Grade V with effect from 01.01.2001 by the
National Housing Development Authority is entitled to back date his post to 26.01.1996 taking into account that he had acted in the said post as
from  26.01.1996 and
02. If he is so entitled, whether the period of service of Mr. D. K. Shantha Senaratne in Grade V should be calculated by the N ational
Housing Development Authority as from 26.01.1996 at the time of calling for interviews for the purpose of granting promotions to Grade IV in
the year 2006 and if he is so entitled to calculate his period of service. What should be the other relief he should be granted accordingly.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.

Colombo, this 25th day of July, 2016.
