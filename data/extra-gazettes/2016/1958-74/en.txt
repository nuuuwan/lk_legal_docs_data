Name of the Land: — ; Land Use: Road (Road 
Development Authority); Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Required for development of Padeniya, 
Anuradhapura Road;  Extent: 0.0216 Hectare.

Pathirannehelage Punchirala Senarathgama, Mahagal -
kadawala. (Possessed by Pathiranage Lional Kumara); 
Extent: 0.0008 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 717 
 
Name of the Land: Palugahahena; Land Use: Open 
waste Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 
Pathirannehelage Punchirala Senarathgama, Mahagal -
kadawala. (Possessed by Pathiranage Nishantha Pradeep 
Kumara); Extent: 0.0031 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Galgamuwa 
of the 15th May, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 25th April, 2016 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land/servitude*, the amount 
of compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount. 
 
 P. M. S. P. PATHIRAJA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Galgamuwa.
