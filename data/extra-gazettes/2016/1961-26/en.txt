No. 1961/26 –  WEDNESDAY APRIL, 06, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
Servitude* which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land marked as Lot No. 1269 in total 
extent of 7.5496 Hectares depicted in F.V.P. 658 
additional 52 of 13.09.2010 made by Surveyor General 
situated in the village of Ihalagoda, 109 B Ihalagoda 
South Grama Niladhari Division in Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Akmeemana District of Galle Southern 
Province. 
 
 Lot No. 1269 
 
Name of the Land: Lechchamiwatta; Description: 
Home Garden, 145 permanent buildings and a shrine 
room; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Land Reforms 
Commission; Extent: 7.5496 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Akmeemana 
on the 08th day of June, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 01st  day of June, 2016 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the said land /servitude* , 
the amount of Componsation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.   
 
 PUSHPIKA THUSHARI JAGODA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Akmeemana. 
  
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Akmeemana, 
01st Aprial, 2016.
