THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No. : 2/3/1/4/2.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2001/IM/296.
I hereby give notice, under sub section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Public purpose means the Acquisition of land where the
Independence Television Network Service Karagahatenna
Transmission centre is situated.
The allotments  of land called Karagahatenna estate,
containing in total extent 0.3417 hectares, depicted as lots 1
and 2 in Preliminary Plan No. Ma 1655, dated 19.02.2016,
prepared by Surveyor General, situated at Karagahatenna
V illage in Gammaduwa Grama Niladhari Division, Ambanganga
Korale minor Division of Ambanganga Korale Divisional
Secretary's Division, Matale District, Central province.
Lot No. 1 - Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent:
0.3026 Hectares
North: Karagahatenna Estate; East: Karagahatenna Estate;
South: Lot 1 in P . P . Ma. No. 1570 and lot 2 in this plan; West:
Karagahatenna estate.
Lot No. 2 - Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent:
0.0391 Hectares
North: Lot 1; East: Lot 1 and 2 in P . P . Ma No. 1570; South:
Lot 2 in P . P . No. 1570 and lot 1 in P . P . No. 1247; W est: Lot 1 in
P . P . No. 1247 and Lot 1 in this plan.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Ambanganga Korale Divisional Secretariat,  on the 09th
day of  August, 2016 at 10.00 a. m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd day
of August 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
WASANTHA WARNAKULASOORIYA,
Divisional Secretary,
Matale.
 Divisional Secretariat, Ambanganga Korale,
1st April 2016.
