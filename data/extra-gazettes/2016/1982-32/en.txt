THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. :  DS-WD/LND/1/3/14/50 (2011/24).
Land Ministry Ref. No. : LD/05/2005/HW/STDP/ADB/605.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1 allotments in total extent of about 0.0147 Hectare,
of land in Preliminary Plan No. G4066 and dated 05.03.2014
prepared by the Sinior - Superintendent of Survey of Galle
Dictric on behalf  of  Survey General, situated in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. 193E, Nugethota in the village of
Welivitiya in Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Welivitiya -
Divithura in Galle District in the Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Athapaththugewatta; Description: Contains
a tea cultivation, 140 tea bushes about 12 years old; Claimant:
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING, SRI LANKA.
State; Original Claimant: K. M. P . Umawathi, Near the Sawanna
Dam, Nugethota; Extent: 0.0147 Hectare.
         All  persons interested in the afore said lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of Welivitiya
- Divithura on the from 26th September, 2016 at  9.30 a.m  and
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing  (in duplicate)
on or before the 19th September, 2016 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said  lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
A. M. A. U. G. KARIYAWASAM,
Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer,
Welivitiya - Divithura.
Divithura Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Welivitiya,
25th August, 2016.
