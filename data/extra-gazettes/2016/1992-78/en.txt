MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE  IN  CRIMINAL   MATTERS  ACT,  No.  25  OF 2002
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by  sub-section (3) of Section 2 of  the Mutual  Assistance in Criminal Matters Act,
No. 25 of 2002, I,  Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice, do by this Order declare that the provisions of  the aforesaid
Act shall apply in respect of  the Agreement between the Government of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and
the Government of  the Ukraine made at Kew on the Twenty Fifth day of June, Two  Thousand and Sixteen. This Agreement
which was made between the Government of Sri Lanka and the Government of the Ukraine, which is a Non- Commonwealth
Country, deals with mutual legal assistance in criminal  matters and concerns inter alia the improved effectiveness on the
part of both parties to the Agreement through cooperation in relation to combating crimes including terrorism within their
respective jurisdictions.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
09th  November 2016.
