UV A   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  (PAYMENTS  OF  SALARIES  AND   ALLOWANCES)  AMENDED  STATUTE
No.  02 OF 2016
A Statute to provide for amending the Sub Section No. 2(a) and 2(b) of amended Statute, No.
09th of 2010 to make payments of Salaries and Allowances for the Chairman and members of the Public
Service Commission of Uva Provincial Council.
Be it enacted by the Uva Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
as follows :
01. This amended Statute may be cited as the Uva Provincial Council Public Service Commission
(Payments of Salaries and Allowances for the Chairman and the Members) amended Statute
No. 02 of 2016 and shall come into operation on such day as approved by the Governor of Uva
Province.

02. With effect from 1st January 2016
a. A sum of Rupees Thirty Five Thousand (Rs. 35,000/-) has to be paid to the Chairman of
the Public Service Commission as a monthly Allowances,
b. A sum of Rupees Twenty Thousand (Rs. 20,000/-) has to be paid to a Member of the
Public Service Commission of the Uva Provincial Council as a monthly Allowances.
03. The Allowances paid for the Chairman and the Members of the Public Service Commission of
the Uva Provincial Council and be a debit to the Provincial Council fund under this Statute.
