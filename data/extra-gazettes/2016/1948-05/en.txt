PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT, No.  2  OF  1988
FILLING  OF   A  V ACANCY   IN  THE
UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Uva Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Uva Province Provincial Council by reason of being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by the Election Commission uner Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Murugan
Sachchithananthan to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Murugan Sachchithananthan, elected as a menber of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Badulla in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A, N. J. A BEYSEKERE, S. R ATNAJEEVAN, H. H OOLE,
Chairman. Member. Member.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
04th  January, 2016.

FILLING  OF   A  V ACANCY   IN  THE
UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Uva Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under Section
65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Uva
Province Provincial Council by reason of being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party, to which the member

person to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated Mr. Welimahara Sarukkali
Patabendige Pragathi Anushalya to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Welimahara Sarukkali Patabendige Pragathi Anushalya, elected as a menber of the above Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of Badulla in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
