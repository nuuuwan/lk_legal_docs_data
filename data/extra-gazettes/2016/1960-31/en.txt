CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD   ACT,   No.  17   OF   1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 57 of  the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No.17 of  1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of  the land
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of  Power and Renewable Energy.
28th March, 2016.
SCHEDULE
The land situated in the village of Nugepola  in Grama Niladari’s Division of  Gallella (No.164), in the Divisional
Secretary’s  Division of Rathnapura, in the Uda Pattu  of   Nawadun  Korale, in the Administrative District of  Rathnapura,
in the Province of Sabaragamuwa of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 1.4508 Hectares (03 Acres 02 Roods and 13.6  Perches)
shown in sketched plan No. CEB/ REATDP/ TE/ 51 dated 10.02.2016 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board and bounded as
follows :-
North : Part of  the same land;
East : Wewalwatta-Rathnapura Road ;
South : Road (12 feet) and land occupied for a playground ;
West : Part of  the same land.
Claimed by : Janatha Estate Development Board.
