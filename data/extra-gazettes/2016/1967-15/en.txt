THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  2   OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 1980.
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of  the Democratic  Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka   upon the recommendation of the
Minister - in Charge of the subject of Urban Development, being of the  opinion that the land specified in the schedule I and
II hereto is urgently required  for the purpose of  implementing Urban Development Projects including the construction of
Malambe Bus Stand do by this order, declare  that the  said land  is required for the such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presdential Secretariat,
Colombo,
16th May  2016.
SCHEDULE - I
Allotment of  land depicted as   Lot  01 in  Plan No. 3762 prepared in respect  of land  called  Dombagahawatte alias
Tambugahawatte alias Malambe Bus Stand   in extent of   1A - 0R - 38.60P (0.5023Hect.) situated in Division No. 7, in
Talangama North, Pallepattuwa Hewagam Korale within Kaduwela Municipal Council limits in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of  Kaduwela in Colombo District, Western Province.
Boundaries of said allotment are as follows :-
North : Udawatte Road and New Kandy Road  ;
East : New Kandy Road, Road upto Dombagahawatta and  Athurugiriya;
South : Metro Commercial Complex and Malambe Market ;
West : Malambe Market, Kaduwela Divisional Secretariat, Malambe Post  Office   and   Udawatte
Road.
