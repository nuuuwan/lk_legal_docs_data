Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No.: බ/ඉ/1/3/1.

I hereby give Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which is required 
Badalkumbura Divisional Secretariat.  
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of Land in F.V.P 210, Suppliment No. 14, 
Sheet No. 17 Prepared by the Superintendant Surveyor in 
Monaragla, situated in Alupotha Village in the 
Badalkumbura Divisional Secretary’s Division of the 
District of Monaragala in Uva Province described as 
follows. 
  
01. Lot No.  353 
 
Name of the Land: Vikumbura Wattha alias 
Hapugaslandahenyaya; Claimant: State; Prior Claimant: 
Janatha Estate Development Board, No. 320, T. B. Jaya 
Mawatha, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.0812 Hectare. 
 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Badalkumbura Divisional Secretariat on the 16 day of 
February 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th 
day of January 2017 the nature of their interest in the Land, 
the Particulars or their claims for Compensation for the 
said land the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
