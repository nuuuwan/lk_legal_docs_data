Land Acquisition Notices 
අංක 1996/6 - 2016 ෙදසැම්බර්  මස 05 වැනි සඳුදා - 2016.12.05 
 
No. 1996/6 – MONDAY, DECEMBER 05, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: IMDS/LND/05/ACQ/25/2005(13).     
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire following land/servitude* 
which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired:  
 
Lot No. 161 (Lot 1) allotments below name land in 
Final Village Plan No. 563, Supplement No. 07 and dated 
14.02.2011, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Surveys, Rathnapura District, situated in the village of 
Puwakgasarawa in the Grama Nilardhari Division of 
Kadawatha Korale Seelagama in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Imbulpe, in the District of 
Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 161 
 
Name of the Land: Udahawalauwewatta alias Puwak-
gasarawegodawatta; Land use: Open waste land; 
Claimant: State; Original Cl aimant: W. M. Kiribandara, 
Seelagama, Belihuloya; Extent: 0.0002 Hectares. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Imbulpe of the on 
16th December 2016 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 02nd December 2016 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land/servitude*, the amount 
of compensation and the details of the compensation of 
such amount. 
   
 D. P. G. R ENUKA, 
 Divisional Secretary (Covering up)/ 
 Land Acquiring Officer, 
 Imbulpe - Rathn apura District. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Imbulpe, 
30th  November,  2016.
