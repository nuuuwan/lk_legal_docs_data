WELFARE   BENEFITS   ACT
Order under Section 1 of the Welfare Benefits Act, No. 24 of 2002
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 1 of  the  Welfare Benefits Act, No. 24 of 2002, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka do hereby make order that the said Welfare Benefits Act,
No. 24 of 2002 shall come into operation on the 15th February 2016.
RAV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance,
02nd  February 2016.
