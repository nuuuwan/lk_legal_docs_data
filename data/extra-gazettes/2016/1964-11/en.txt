THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order Under Section 27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, establishing the South Eastern University of Sri
Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 1906/6 of March, 16, 2015) by the addition immediately after item (d) appearing in Column II under
the heading “Department of study” which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Arts and Culture” appearing in Column I
under the heading “Faculty” of the Schedule II to such Order, of the following item :-
“(e) Department of Economics and Statistics”.
L
AKSHMAN, K IRIELLA ,
Minister of Higher Education
and Highways.
Colombo,
25th April 2016.
