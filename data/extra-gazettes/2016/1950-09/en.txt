EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
            Reference No.: 4/3/1/128. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be  acquired: 
 
The following allotment of land situated in the village 
of Velapura Kalutara in Grama Niladari division of 725 
Kalutara South, Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kalutara in the District of Kalutara in Western Province 
and marked in Surveyor General’s Plan No. P.P.K. 4545 
and contains in Extent 0.1335 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of Land: Mankattawatta; Description:         
Garden Two Permanent Buildings; Claiments: Chief 
Manager (Property) Head Office,, Bank of Ceylon, 
Colombo 01; Extent: 0.1335 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Kalutara 
on the 29th day of February, 2016 at 10.30 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 23rd day of February, 2016 the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. You should present 
relevant Deeds, Plans, and other Documents to prove 
ownership to the land on the inquiry date.  
 
 WIMAL GUNATUNGE,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kalutara. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office,  
Kalutara, 
13th January, 2016.
