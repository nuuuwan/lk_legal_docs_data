THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amaratunga,Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands below mentioned are required for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that act.
The allotments of land, in extent of about 0.1012 Hectare, depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. MR/KMB/2014/698  and dated
06.05.2015 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Kamburupitiya  in the Gangabada Pattuwa North Minor Division
in the Kamburupitiya Divisional, Secretary Division in Matara
District in the Southern Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ratalankawawatta – Extent: 0.0996 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Ratalankawawatta claimed
by Land Reform Commission; East: Ratalankawawatta  claimed by
Land Reform Commission; South: Ratalankawawatta claimed by
Land Reform Commission, Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot B;
West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot B.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Ratalankawawatta – Extent: 0.0016 Hectare
North: and East: Lot A; South: Lot A and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);
West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot A.
