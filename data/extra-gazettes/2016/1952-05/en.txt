CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
1A
Announcement
BY virtue of power vested in me, under Section 5 of Chapter 563 of Public Cemeteries and Private Cemeteries Ordinance,
read with the Chapter 252 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance, read along with Section 2 of the Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Sarath Ekanayake the Minister in charges of the subject of Local
Government, Central Province, do hereby publish that the Dodamwela Cemetery located within the limits mentioned
herein in the Schedule I, which was accepted by the Kandy Municipal Council through Resolution No. 8(54) on 14.10.2015
as the cemetery belongs to Kandy Municipal Council.
Furthermore, the areas covering cremation and burial formalities in the said Dodamwela Cemetery where
mentioned in the Schedule II, herein.
S
ARATH E KANAYAKE,
The Chief Minister of the Central Province,
and the Minister in charge of the subject
of Local Government.
20th date of January 2016.
SCHEDULE - 1
Location and limits of Dodamwela, in the Dodamwela Kandy Division, surveyed by the Surveyors' Department of
Sri Lanka, Plan No. K 3858, Lot No. 01, on 22.08.1991, Ward No. 05 Dodamwela of the Gangawatta Korale Divsional
Revenue Officer's Division, in Kandy District in the Central Province :
North by : Dodamwela Lane and the rest portion of this land ;
East by : The rest part of this land ;
South by : The rest portion of this land and Peellagawa watta ;
West by : The land portion situated in Dodamwela Lane hectare 0.1633 in extent.
SCHEDULE - II
North by : Mahaweliganga River and Sir Cuda Ratwatta Mawatha ;
East  by : The road belongs to Road Development Authority, Katugastota, Kand Sri Sumangala Mawatha,
Asgiriya, Wattarantenna Road, Anniwatte Road and George E de Silva Mawatha ;
West by : Mahaweliganga River and Getambe - Gannoruwa road belongs to Road Development Authority
South by : Kandy Peradeniya old road.
