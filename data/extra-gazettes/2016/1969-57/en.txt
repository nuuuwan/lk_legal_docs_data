LOCAL  TREASURY  BILLS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 417)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Finance under Section 16 of the Local Treasury Bills Ordinance (Chapter 417).
RAV I  K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Colombo,
03rd June 2016.
Regulations
The Local Treasury Bills (Primary Dealers) Regulations No. 01 of 2009 published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1607/18 of June 24, 2009 is hereby amended in regulation 9 as follows :-
(1) in Paragraph (1) thereof, by the substitution for the words and figures “The Registrar of Public Debt appointed
under the Local Treasury Bills Ordinance (Chapter 417), on behalf of the Central Bank” of the words and
figures “The Director of the Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions, of the Central
Bank, appointed in terms of Monetary Law Act, (Chapter 422)” ; and
(2) in paragraph (2) thereof, by the substitution for the words “the Registrar of Public Debt” of the words “the
Director of the Department of Supervision of Non-Bank Financial Institutions.”
