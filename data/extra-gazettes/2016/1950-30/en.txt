SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of  Finance, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in
the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing
from 21st January 2016, subject to provisions of Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,
No. 48 of 2007.
R
AVI K ARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of  Finance.
 Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
18th January, 2016.
