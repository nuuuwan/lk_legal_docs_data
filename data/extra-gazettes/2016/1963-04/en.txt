THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3942 
 
I  hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964, that  the Government intends to acquire the 
following land/servitude which is required for a public  
purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following Land depicted as Lot No. 01 in 
preliminary Plan No. 2661, located in the village of 
Wewahamanduwa in the Kanaththagoda - North Grama 
Niladhari Division in the  Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Matara, Matara District, Southern Province, will be 
acquired for  distribution among Tsunami displaced 
persons. 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Puwakwaththa; Current land Use: 
Garden, Mixed crops around 10 to 20 Years old 10 
permanent buildings and Well; Claimant: Ven. Hakmana 
Dharmarathna Thero, Chief Inclumbent of Kotikagoda 
Temple Wewahamanduwa, Matara;  Extent: 0.5260 
Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands and 
Which a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in 
writing before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 
5th day of May, 2016 at 10.30 a.m. and  are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 27th of April 2016  the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said  land/
servitude, the amount of  compensation and the details of  
the computation of such amount, 
  
 RANJITH YAPA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
 
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Matara, 
11th  April, 2016
