RE-ESTABLISHMENT   OF   HEALTH   MANAGEMENT   ASSISTANT   SERVICE
THE Health Management Assistant Service which was declared by the Gazette No. bearing 1355/15 dated 25.08.2004 and
amalgamated to Public Management Assistant Service is hereby re-established with effect from 02.12.2015 with the
approval of the Cabinet of Ministers.
All the actions taken in respect of the officers of this service who were governed under the Public Management
Assistant Service during 15.10.2009 to 02.12.2015 shall be valid and intact.
The rest of the clauses of the minute on Health Management Assistant Service dated 25.08.2004 shall be intact
and valid.
A
NURA J AYAWICKRAMA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine.
Colombo,
28th April 2016.
