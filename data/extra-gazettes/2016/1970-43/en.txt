No. 1970/43 – THURSDAY, JUNE,  09,  2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 
NO. 28 OF 1964  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land mini’s. Reference No.: 4/3/4/2010/D305. 
My Reference No.: NCP/HG/L02/2508 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of  1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a Public purpose. 
 
Description of  the land  to be acquired : 
 
Two allotments of land called Patapilikanda Kele in 
total extent of 0.1104 hectare depicted as Lot No. 173 and 
175 in Cadastral Map No. 120134 Block No. 02 Sheet 
No.  10 prepared by the Survey General, situated in the 
village called Minneriya  in the  Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 56 of Minneriya in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Hingurakgoda in Polonnaruwa 
District of the North Central Province and more fully 
described as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE  
01. Lot No. 173 
Name of the Land: Patapilikanda Kele; Description: 
Home Garden , a permanent building available; Claimant: 
Peoples Thrift and Credit Co -operative Society, 
Minneriya; Extent: 0.0866 Hectare. 
 
 
02. Lot No. 175 
Name of the Land: — ; Description: Road; Claimant: 
Peoples Thrift and Credit Co -operative Society, 
Minneriya; Extent: 0.0238 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are  hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Hingurakgoda 
Divisional Secretariat on 04th July 2016 at 10.30 a. m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                       
(in duplicate) on or before 04th July, 2016 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
              R. M. D. P. PUSHPAKUMARI, 
              Divisional Secretary, 
            Hingurakgoda. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Hingurakgoda. 
01st June, 2016
