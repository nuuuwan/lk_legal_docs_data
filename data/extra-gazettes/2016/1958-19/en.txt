No. 1958/19–  WEDNESDAY, MARCH,  16, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My Reference No.: MCUDP/LA/ABT(WH 05)/14/03.  
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4/3/10/2014//LRB/292. 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire part of the land at left side 
of Madiwela East Diversion Scheme situated in the 
village called Udumulla, implemented under a sub project 
of Metro Colombo Urban Development Project for the 
conservation of embankments. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Five allotments of land  in total extent of about 0.5287 
hectare, depicted as No. 01, 02, 03, 04 & 05 in the 
Preliminary Plan No.  Co. 9615 dated 04.03.2016 situated 
in the village called Udumulla in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 502 of Udumulla - North in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Divisions of Kolonnawa in Colombo District 
of the Western Province and bounded as follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Bogahawatta, Elamulla watta; 
Description: Home Garden; Claimant: N. N. Pathberiya, 
Ambathale, Angoda; Extent: 0.1465 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the Land: Ambathalewatta; Description: 
Home Garden, part of a temporary building available; 
Claimant: K. D. Gunasekara, K. D. N. Ariyarathne and  
K. D. S. Siriwardhana, No. 62, Ambathale, Angoda; 
Extent: 0.0385 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 03 
 
Name of the Land: Ambathalewatta; Description: 
Home Garden, part of a permanent building available; 
Claimant: K. V. P. Gunasekara, No. 62, Ambathale, 
Angoda; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare. 
 
4. Lot No. 04 
 
Name of the Land: Ambathalewatta; Description: 
Home Garden; Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent: 
0.0014 Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 05 
 
Name of the Land: Could not be traced; Description: 
Home Garden; Claimant: H. Hemachandra Pinto, No. 05, 
Ambathale, Angoda; Extent: 0.3418 Hectare.
