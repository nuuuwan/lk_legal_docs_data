EXCISE  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  52)
Excise Notification  No. 990
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 22(2) of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as  amended from time
to time, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that the excise duty imposed by the Excise
Notification No. 989 and published in Extraordinary Gazette No. 1981/87 of 26.08.2016 shall not be charged on foreign
liqour imported for the purpose of re-export or to sell at duty free shops for foreign currency, with effect from 04.10.2016.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
03rd October 2016.
