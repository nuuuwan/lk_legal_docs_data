PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS

UVA     PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of  the  Uva  Province
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1947, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
01.  Mr. R. M. Dayarathne, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, to perform the Duties of the Deputy Chief
Secretary (Planning) for the Uva Provincial Council, from the date 10.01.2016 to 25.01.2016, till the return of Mr. M.
M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara to the Island who is the Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning) for the Uva Provincial
Council.
02. Mr. P. S. S. Vithanage, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the Deputy Chief
Secretary (Engineering Serviece) for the Uva Provincial Council, from the date 10.01.2016 to 25.01.2016, till the
return of Mr. A. A. P. S. K Karunaratne to the Island who is the Deputy Chief Secretary (Engineering Service) for the
Uva Provincial Council.
03. Mr. A. M. J. T. Gangananda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the
Director to Building for the Uva Provincial Council, from the date 10.01.2016 to 25.01.2016, till the return of Mr. A.
A. P. S. K. Karunaratne to the Island who is the Acting Director to Building for the Uva Provincial Council.
04. Mrs. D. M. Vineetha Dissanayake, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of
the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance & Planning, Law & Peace, Education, Local Government, Power & Energy,
Construction, Rural Infrastructure facilities and Land of Uva Province, from the date 10.01.2016 to 25.01.2016, till
the return of Mrs. G. A. M. S. P. Ambanwela to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance &
Planning, Law & Peace, Education, Local Government, Power & Energy, Constructioin, Rural Infrastructure facilities
and Land of Uva Province.
05. Mr. R. M. Thilakaratne, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, to perform the duties of
the Director to Education of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 10.01.2016 to 25.01.2016,
till the return of Mr. R. M. P. Rathnayake, to the Island who is the Director to Education of Uva Province.
