THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
AS AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
   Reference No.:  VSW/DS/LB/Acqu/ Pan/ Police.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, As amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to accquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose :-
     Description of the land to be acquired:
Preliminary Plan: Y a 2843; Situation: Village -  Pandath-
tharippu J/146; DS Division: Valikamam South West,
Sandilipay; District: Jaffna; Province: Northern.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Mappapulam; Description: Garden contains
six permanent buildings, Part of one permanent building, Part
of one overhead water tank (under construction) and two
pits; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.3163 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at V alikamam South West, Sandilippay
Divisional Secretariat office on  03rd March 2017 at 10.00 a.m
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing  (in
duplicate) on or before the 20th February 2017 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims fo r
compensation for the acquisition of the said  land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. All deeds and Documents in support of claim should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
