THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order published made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 420/25 of September 26, 1986 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1876/27 of
August 19, 2014), which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, by the addition
immediately after the item  “10. Department of Hindu Civilization” appearing in Column II of the Schedule and which
corresponds to the item “Faculty of Arts and Culture” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following  entry :-
“11. Department of Philosophy and Value Studies”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th November 2016.
