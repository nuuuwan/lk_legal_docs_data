THE  WAGES   BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD   FOR   THE   JANITORIAL    SERVICE   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated
04.06.1971 that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons
to be members of the Wages Board for the Janitorial Service Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of
Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years
commencing on 2nd December 2015.
S. M. G
OTABAYA J AYARATHNE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
20th May 2016.
