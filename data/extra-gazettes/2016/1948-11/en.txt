L.D.B 21/1978(X)
UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 25 A
BY Virtue of powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing Registration Number GA 2232, do by this Order, recognize the Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy,
as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education therein, leading to the award of the degrees
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the abbreviations are specified in the corresponding entries in
Column II of the Schedule hereto.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degrees Abbreviations of Degrees
Bachelor of Arts BA (Gen)
Bachelor of Arts (Special) in Pali BA (Sp) Pali
Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Bsc IT

 Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
