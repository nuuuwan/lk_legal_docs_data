EXTRAORDINARY 
AMENDMENT 
 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini’s Ref. No. 13/2000/E/359. 
My. Ref. No. Ana/L/2/6. 
 
As per 2011.12.29 Government Gazette Instruction 
(Gazette No. 1738/32) The Examination Date will 
Change as below: 
 
I  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964, 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose:-  
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Five (05) allotments of land called Kottukachchiya 
Janapada Viyaparaya in total extent of land 1.7214 
Hectares depicted as Lot Nos. 556, 557, 558, 559 and 561 
in sheet No. 09, supplementary No. 8 of final Village Plan 
No. I.S.P.P. 19 prepared by the Survey General, situated 
in the Village called Kottukachchiya in Perawili Pattu in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Anamaduwa in 
Puttalam  District of North Western Province and 
bounded as follows. 
 
Schedule 
 
1. Lot No.  556 —  Kottukachchiya Janapada 
Viyaparaya —  Extent: 0.5615 Hectare 
 
North: Lot No. 311; East: Lot No. 311; South: Lot No. 
558; West: Lot No. 557 and 423 (Pradesheeya Sabha Road) 
 
2. Lot No.  557 —  Kottukachchiya Janapada 
Viyaparaya —  Extent: 0.0405 Hectare 
 
North: Lot No. 423 (Pradesheeya Sabha Road); East: 
Lot No. 556; South: Lot No. 558; West: Lot No. 423  
(Pradesheeya Sabha Road) 
 
3. Lot No.  558 —  Kottukachchiya Janapada 
Viyaparaya —  Extent: 0.4578 Hectare 
 
North: Lot No. 557 and 556; East: Lot No. 311; South: 
Lot No. 559; West: Lot No. 423 (Pradesheeya Sabha Road) 
 
4. Lot No.  559 —  Kottukachchiya Janapada 
Viyaparaya —  Extent: 0.4300 Hectare 
 
North: Lot No. 558; East: Lot No. 311; South: Lot No. 
561 and 560; West: Lot No. 423 (Pradesheeya Sabha Road) 
 
5. Lot No.  561 —  Kottukachchiya Janapada 
Viyaparaya —  Extent: 0.2316 Hectare 
 
North: Lot No. 559; East: Lot No. 311; South: Lot No. 
563, 562 and 424; West: Lot No. 560  
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at the Anamaduwa Divisional 
Secretary’s Office  at 16th February on 2017. Further your 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 10th February 2017 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount. 
 
  T. M. J. W. THENNAKOON,
