THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakarathne Bandara Ekanayake,
Acting Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
An allotment of land called Arachchiya Kumbura in total extent of
about 0.5828 Hectare, depicted  as Lot No. 02 in Advance Tracing
No.: Co/CoII/ DSO/ 98/80, prepared by the Surveyor general situated
in  the Village of Welikada (within the Municipal Council limit of Sri
Jayawardhanapura Kotte) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division  of
Nugegoda in Colombo District of Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot No. 02 – Arachchiya Kumbura – Extent: About 0.5828
Hectare
North: Kolonnawa canal; East:  Kolonnawa canal and Arachchiya
Kumbura claimed by Edwin Vithana ; South: Arachchiya Kumbura
claimed by P. A. Wilson, Dalsy Botheju and some others and  Lot No.
2 ; West: Arachchiya Kumbura of which the claimant could not be
traced.
