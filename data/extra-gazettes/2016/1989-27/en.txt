PUBLIC  SERVICE   COMMISSION
Appointment of the Committee in terms of Article 56 (1) and 56 (2) of the Constitution
01. As directed by the Public Service Commission, the following three members Committee is hereby appointed in
terms of the Article 56 (1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in respect of the
Education Service Sector.
02. Hereinafter the Committee will be referred to as Education Service Committee.
03. The Education Service Committee is consisted of the following members :-
I. Mr. M.M.N.D. Bandara (Chairman)
II. Mr. E.H.M.P. Elkaduwa (Member)
III. Mr. K.L.M. Thamby (Member)
04. The Education Service Committee shall come in to force with effect from the 20th day of October, Two
Thousand and Sixteen (2016).
