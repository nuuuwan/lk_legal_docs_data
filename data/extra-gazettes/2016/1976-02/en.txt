CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order under Section 50(1) of Pawn Brokers' Statute of the Central Province No. 04 of 2014
BY virtue of power vested in me under Section 50(1) of the Pawn Brokers' Statute No. 04 of 2014 of the Provincial Council
of the Central Province, I, Sarath Ekanayake, the Chief Minister of the Provincial Council of Central Province and the
Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local Government, Manpower, Education and Cultural Affairs, Tourism,
Land, Co-operative Development, Trade and Commerce, Food Supplies and Distribution and Investment Co-ordination do
hereby order to implement the under mentioned amendments related to the Pawn Brokers' Statute, with effect from the
31st day of May, 2016.
ORDER
(b) 10.(2) Sub section :
In case of a licence holder being a Co-operative Rural Bank, a surety certificate obtained from the Commissioner
of Co-operatives shall be forwarded on behalf of a security deposit amount.
S
ARATH E KANAYAKE,
 Chief Minister of Central Provincial Council
