PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS ACT,   No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Northern Province Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Northern Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occured in the membership of
the Northern Province Provincial Council by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recongnized political party, has nominated Mr. Andiyaiya
Puvaneswaran to fill such vacancy ;
THE ELECTION COMMISSION, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No.
2 of 1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Andiyaiya Puvaneswaran, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Mullaitivu in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
Mahinda Deshapriya Chairman Election Commission
N. J. Abeysekere Member Election Commission
S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole Member Election Commission
08th December 2016,
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
