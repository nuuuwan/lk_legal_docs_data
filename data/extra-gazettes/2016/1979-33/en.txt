SERVICE Minute of Sri Lanka Administrative Service published in the  Gazette  Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1842/2  dated  23rd of December 2013 and amended by the Gazette  Extraordinary  of the

Schedule  II
INTERIM  PROVISIONS
1. General Conditions :
2. 1.1 Interim Period :
Interim period shall be effective up to 31st of December 2017.
2. Method of Promotion :
2.1 Promotion of the Grade (II) of Service :
2.1.1 : Promotion of officers who were recruited to Class (III) of service before 01st of January 2008 to the
Grade (II) shall be made under the provisions stipulated in the sub para 2.1.1(a)
2.1.2 : Promotion of officers who were recruited to Class (III) of service from 01st of January 2008 up to the
effective date of this Minute to the Grade (II) shall be made under the provisions stipulated in the sub
para 2.1.2(a)
