And whereas, Degiri Amendra Mendis Seneviratne Esquire, Judge of the High Court, has passed away;
And whereas, the need to fill the vacancy this caused has been identified;
Now therefore,  I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, reposing
great trust and confidence in his prudence, ability and fidelity , do in pursuance of the provisions of section 3 of  the
Commissions of  Inquiry Act, by these presents, appoint Piyasena Ranasinghe Esquire, Judge of the High Court to be a
Commissioner of the Commission of Inquiry constituted in terms of the Warrant referred to above.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this 01st 
 day of June,
Two Thousand and Sixteen.
                         By order of His Excellency,
P. B. ABEYKOON,
