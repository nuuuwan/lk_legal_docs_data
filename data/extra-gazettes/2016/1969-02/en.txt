L.D.B. 21/1978(xiv)
UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order  under Section  20(4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order appoint Professor Jayantha Walihinda to be the Competent
Authority with effect from May 27, 2016, for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of Director of
Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute Sri Lanka, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred
or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurvedic Institute, Sri Lanka under the
said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statue, by Law, Regulation and the Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
27th May 2016.
