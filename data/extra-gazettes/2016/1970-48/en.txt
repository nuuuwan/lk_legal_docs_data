THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of  Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Four (4) allotments of lands  in total extent of about 0.9525
Hectare, shown as Lot Nos. 1 to 4 in Advance Tracing No. CO/
KDW/2015/298 of  10th July 2015 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Hewagama within the Divisional
Secretary's Division of  Kaduwela in  Colombo District, Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 –  Rukattanagaha Owita  –  Extent: 0.0464
Hectare
North:  Avissawella Road; East: Rukattanagaha Owita claimant
not known; South: Ela; West:  Road.
02. Lot No. 2 – Land name not known – Extent: 0.0288
Hectare
North and East:  Claimant and name of the land not known, land
used for public Bus Stand; South: Claimant and name of the land not
known, land used for public Bus Stand and cement drain; West:  Lot
No. 3, Ela and land called Malabadagahawatta claimed by Pilip
Samarathunga and Nisha Perera.
03. Lot No. 3 – Hakuruge Kumbura and Owita – Extent:
0.4614 Hectare
North: Ela and Lot No. 02; East:  Claimant and name of the land
not known, land used for public Bus Stand and cement drain, name
of the land not known claimed by Senarath Perera, Yasawathi and
others; South: Name of the land not known, claimed by Senarath
Perera, Yasawathi and others, Road and name of the land  not known
claimed by Senarath Perera; West:  Road, Name of the land not
known, claimed by Senarath Perera and Lot No. 4.
04. Lot No. 4 – Hakuruge Kumbura and Owita – Extent:
0.4159 Hectare
North: Bataketiya claimant not known  and Ela; East:  Ela and Lot
No. 03; South: Lot No. 03, Name of the land not known, claimed by
Senarath Perera; West:  Name of the land not known, claimed by
Senarath Perera, Bataketiya claimant not known  and Ela.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
