PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
 NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Declaration by the Hon. Governor  of  the  North Central Province  Provincial Council
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under paragraph 8(b) of Section 154(b) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, P. B. Disanayake, Governor of the North Central Province declare hereby that the forth
session of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province came to end on mid-night of December 21st 2015,
and
By virtue of the powers vested in me under paragaraph 8(a) of Section 154(b) of the aforesaid Constitution,
declare hereby that the five session of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province shall meet at 10.00 a. m.
on 18th February 2016, at the conference hall of the North Central Provincial Council.
P. B. D
ISANAYAKE,
Governor, North Central Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Provincial Council,
10th February 2016.
