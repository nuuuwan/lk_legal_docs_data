PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Wages Board for the Marine Transport and Operational Services Trade
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  7
I, W. D. J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations hereby notify under Section 7(1) of the Wages
Boards Ordinance that I intend to make an order regarding the Marine Transport and Operational Services Trade under
Section 6(1) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136).
Objection if any, to the aforesaid order will be received by me until 12.00 noon on 24th October 2016.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain  a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
04th October 2016.
O
RDER
The provisions of Part II of the Wages Board Ordinance, No. 27 of 1941 shall apply to the following trade. Under
mentioned worker categories engaged in marine transport, operational services and other related transport and operational
services in and out of dock, harbour and port ; and other workers engaged in incidental tasks thereto shall come under the
Wages Board for the marine transport and operational services trade.
Unskilled
1. Work Assistant
2. Afloat Labourer
3. Ship Searing
