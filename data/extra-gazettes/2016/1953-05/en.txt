NOTIFICATION
I, the  Minister of  Social Empowerment and Welfare, by virtue of power vested in me in terms of Section 45 (2) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby notify that the following subjects and functions
under the  purview of  the Ministry of Social Empowerment and Welfare here to be vested in the Deputy Minister of Social
Empowerment and Welfare, by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1933/ 13 dated 21.09.2015.
01. All affairs related to the National Institute of Social Development.
02. All affairs related to the Social Security Board.
03. All affairs related to the  Rural  Development, Training and Research Institute (Borella, Pilimatalawa).
S. B. D
