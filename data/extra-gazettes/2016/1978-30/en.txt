NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Local Authority Notifications
IT is hereby notify that the Provincial Council of North Western Province has been approved by laws of “Formalize,
Administrate Crematoriums and Collect Charges, Administrate, Formalize, Inspect and Collect Charges from Florists,
Presenting term reports and information, Prepare Committees and Conduct Meetings” of Pradeshiya Sabha at the Provincial
Council Meeting held on 16.02.2016 that notified by the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1930/06 dated 31st August 2015 in
part IV(A) of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka made by the Minister of Local Government of the North
Western Province under powers vested in the sub schedule (1) of the statement (2) in the Local Authorities Statute No. 6 of
1952 under Authority 261, read with the paragraph (a) of Schedule (1) of the statement 2 in the Provincial Council Statute
No. 12 of 1989.
P. B. M. S
IRISENA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Local Government,
North Western Province Provincial Council.
Kurunegala,
07th July 2016.
