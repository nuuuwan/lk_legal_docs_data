CUSTOMS  NOTIFICATION
Method for Determination of Customs Value under the Schedule ‘E’ of the Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of
2003 for Disposal of Vehicles imported on 100% Duty/Tax Free Basis
WITH the provisions of the Schedule ‘E’ of the Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2003, a method for determination of
Customs value for disposal of motor vehicles imported and registered on or before November 19, 2015 on 100% duty/tax
free basis by the Diplomats and Diplomatic Organizations  is hereby established.
Accordingly, the Customs value for  disposal of motor vehicles imported and registered on or before November 19,
2015 on 100% duty/tax free basis by the Diplomats and Diplomatic Organizations is a value, established on the basis of the
period of usage of such vehicle, from the CIF Value of such vehicle at the time of importation as stipulated in the Schedule (1)
below.
Period between the date of first registration and the date of disposal of the relevant vehicle shall be taken for the
computation of the period of usage of such vehicle. The Fiscal levies payable at the time of disposal of such vehicle to be
claculated at the rates of duties prevailed at the time of importation of such vehicle.
SCHEDULE (1)
Period of Use by the Diplomat/Diplomatic Organization CIF Value
from the date of First Registration
Less than 01 year 85%
More than 01 year and less than or equal 02 years 75%
More than 02 year and less than or equal 03 years 70%
More than 03 year and less than or equal 04 years 65%
More than 04 year and less than or equal 05 years 60%
