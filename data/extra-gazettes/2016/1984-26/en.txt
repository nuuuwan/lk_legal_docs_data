SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
list of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in
the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing
from 16th September 2016, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,
No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1976/4 of 18.07.2016 in respect of Sugar (HS Heading  Nos. 17.01) are rescinded with effect from
16th September 2016.
R
AVI K ARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
15th September, 2016.
