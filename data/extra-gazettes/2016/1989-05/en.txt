Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act,

and

Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 12.09.2011.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the demand made by Mrs. M. G. J. G . G . Nanayakkara to the Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka that her appointment to the post
of Administrative Assistant should be backdated to 01.03.1990, her appointment to the post of Administrative Officer should be backdated to
01.03.1994 and that she should receive the arrears in salary and the increments accordingly can be justified and if so, to what relief she is
entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGA,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 03rd day of September, 2016.
