EXTRAORDINARY
1A
THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the the Schedule  hereunder has been named as “Bala Tampo Lane” by the
Honourable Chief Minister of Western Provincial Council and Honourable Minister  in charge of the subject of Local
Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read with
the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequentional Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
V . K. A. A
NURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
01. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
02. District - Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street - 22nd Lane
04. New Name of the Street - “Bala Tampo Lane”
05. Road Description - This road, commencing from in the between Assessment No. 80 and
Assessment No. 86 near by the Kollupitiya Road belonging to the
Kollupitiya Municipal Ward, ends from near the sea.
