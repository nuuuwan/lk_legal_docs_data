The Uva Provincial Council Co-operative Employees Commission Statute No. 04 of 2010
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Sections 23 and 28 of the Uva Provincial Council Cooperative Employees Commission
Statute No. 04 of 2010, I, Senthil Thondaman, Minister of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure

CHAPTER   IV
34. Retirements
1. The age of retirement of a cooperative employee should be 60 years. If any Cooperative Employee wishes to
continue his services after the completion of 57 years of age, he will be allowed to stay in services until 60
years of age which is the compulsory retirement age without any request for an extension in service.
2. On completion of 55 years of age, if the employee wishes to retire from service, he could do so by submitting
a written request to the Committee.
3. On Completion of 55 years of age and the Employee decides to retire ; he should give the date of retirement
to the committee in written prior 03 months.
