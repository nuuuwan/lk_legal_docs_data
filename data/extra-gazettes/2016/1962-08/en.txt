No. 217/ 3 of 2016
P. S. No. : CSA/1/21/ 8th P.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 44(1) of the Constitution  of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Hon. Lakshman Pinto Jayathilaka Senewiratne, MP , as State Minister
of Science, Technology and Research, with effect from April 06, 2016.
