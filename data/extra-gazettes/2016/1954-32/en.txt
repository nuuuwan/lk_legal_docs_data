THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakarathna Bandara Ekanayake,
Acting Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land with following name and extent containing in
extent 0.0089 Hectare, called Panukaraketiya, depicted as Lot ‘A’ in
the Advance Tracing No.: MR/PSG/2014/233 dated 08.05.2015
prepared by Senior Surveyor-General and situated in the Village of
Urubokka (F.V .P. 23) in Minor Division of Morawak Korale,
Divisional Secretary’s Division  of  Pasgoda in Matara District:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Panukaraketiya – Extent: 0.0089 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 881 and 872; East:  Lot Nos. 872 and 873; South:
Lot Nos. 873 and 872; West: Lot Nos. 872 and 881.
