THE  COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Amendment  for  the  Notice  Published  in  Extra ordinary  Gazette  No. 1956/15  of  01.03.2016
IT  is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “Ven. Pitiduwe Dhammika Himi
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the
subject of Local Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall
read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Councils Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
V . K. A. A
NURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07,
18th May 2016.
SCHEDULE
01. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
02. District - Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street - Sri Negrodharama Mawatha
04. New Name of the Street - “ Ven. Pitiduwe Dhammika Himi Mawatha”
05. Road Description - This road commencing in between from the Assessment Numbers 01 and 14
adjacent to Danister de Silva Mawatha belonging to Wanatamulla Municipal
Ward, terminates adjacent to Ven. Saranapala Himi Mawatha in between from
 Assessment Numbers 65 and 66/ 61.
