 NOTIFICATION
IT  is hereby notified that following subjects and functions out of the subjects and functions assigned under the Ministry
of Skills Development and V ocational Training by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1933/13 of 24.05.2015 are assigned to
the Hon. Palitha Range Bandara, the State Minister of Skills Development and V ocational Training by exercising powers
vested in me as per the sub-article 44(5) of Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1. All functions pertaining to Sri Lanka Institute of Printing,
2. Functions relating to giving vocational and technical education suitable for the job market pertaining to the
Department of Technical Education and Training.
3. Functions relating to offering training opportunities to the youth seeking overseas employments in an extent
sufficient for the international demand, in collaboration with the Bureau of Foreign Employment.
