No. 1981/76 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 25, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below is needed  for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
1. Description of Servitude.– The servitude to be acquired for
the construction of access road to the water treatment plant under
the Ridiyagama water supply scheme.
2. Description of the Land .–  An allotment of land called
Irrigation land alias state land in extent of 0.1670 Hectare, depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. H/AMB/2012/624 dated on 04.12.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at the Village of
Ridiyagama (F.V .P. 564) in Magam Pattu Minor Division, in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Ambalantota-Hambantota District
in Southern Province :
01. Lot ‘B’ – Irrigation land alias State land – Extent: About
0.1670 Hectare
North: Land claimed by the Department of Irrigation and Ridiyagama
- Ambalantota Road; East: Ridiyagama - Ambalantota Road and land
claimed by the Department of Irrigation; South: Land claimed by the
Department of Irrigation and the land  claimed by Ambalantota
Pradeshiya Sabha; West: Land  claimed by Ambalantota Pradeshiya
Sabha and the land claimed by the Department of Irrigation.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
