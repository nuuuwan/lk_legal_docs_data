ASSIGNING  THE   FUNCTIONS  AND  DUTIES  OF  THE  MINISTRY  OF  AGRICULTURE
TO  THE  HON.   STATE   MINISTER  OF   AGRICULTURE
AS  the Minister of Agriculture of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, out of the functions and powers vested
in me, I hereby declare and assign the responsibilities and powers of discharging the following functions prescribed in the
Schedule below, to Hon. Wasantha Laksminath Aluvihara, the State Minister of Agriculture, in terms of the provisions in
article 44(5) of the constitution, and that the State Minister has further scope of looking into, intervention and coordination
of all the matters coming under the purview of this ministry :-
