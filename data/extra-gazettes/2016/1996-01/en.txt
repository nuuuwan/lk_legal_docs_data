Proposed 192 Roomed Hotel Project, Modara, Kosgoda
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT   ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impacts Assessment study on proposed 192 Roomed Hotel Project
- Modara, Kosgoda submitted by the Melwa Hotels and Resorts (Pvt.) Ltd, No. 30-2/7, Abdul Jabbar Mawatha, Colombo
