PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
DEHIWALA - MOUNT   LA VINIA    MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of the Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia  Municipal Council ,  do hereby declare
under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262 ) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Krees Wilhelm Balthasar  of
the Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council   has vacated his office of   member of that  Council as he  has ceased to be
a member of the United National Party.
S. ACHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia  Municipal Council.
City  Elections Office,
Colombo,
26th January, 2016.
