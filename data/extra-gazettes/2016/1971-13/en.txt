REGULATIONS  MADE  UNDER   PARAGRAPHS  (d)  AND  (e)  SUBSECTION  (2) OF SECTION  36  OF  THE

BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 67 read with paragraphs (d) and (e) of Subsection (2) of Section 36 of
the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007 and on the recommendation of the Sri Lanka Sustainable
Energy Authority, I, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya as the Minister of Power and Renewable Energy do by this order make the
under mentioned regulations for Energy Performance Standards of Appliances (Double capped Tubular Fluorescent Lamps
and Fluorescent Lamp Ballasts).
R
ANJITH  S IY AMBALAPITIYA,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.

2. No person shall import into Sri Lanka or manufacture in Sri Lanka, any item specified in Column I of the
Schedule hereto unless it conforms to the Sri Lanka Standard (SLS) specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of
the Schedule hereto.
3. Every importer or a manufacturer of any item specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto shall -
(a) affix to the item or to the cover or to the wrapper or to the box which contains it, an energy label which
conforms to the Sri Lanka Standard specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule
hereto ; and
(b) when required by the Authority, submit a certificate issued by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution, certify-
ing that the item imported or manufactured, conforms to the standard specified in the corresponding
entry in Column II of the Schedule hereto.
4. No person shall store, display, sell either wholesale or retail, offer for sale or distribute any item specified in
Column I of the Schedule hereto, whether imported into Sri Lanka or manufactured in Sri Lanka, to which an energy label is
affixed to such item or to the cover or to the wrapper or to the box in which the item is contained, which does not comply
with the requirement imposed by regulation 3(a) hereto.
5. In these Regulations -
“Act” means the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007 ;
“Authority” means the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority established by the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy
Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007 ; and
“Sri Lanka Standards Institution” means the Sri Lanka Standards Institution established by the Sri Lanka Stan-
dards Institution Act, No. 6 of  1984.
