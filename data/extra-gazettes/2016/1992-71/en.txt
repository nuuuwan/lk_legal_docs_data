No. 1992/71 - FRIDAY,  NOVEMBER  11, 2016
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE   URBAN  DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY   LAW   No. 41 OF  1978
ENACTED   BY   THE   NATIONAL   STATE   ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 enacted by
the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Magapolis and Western Development do by this
Order, vest with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today the land situated is Lunupokuna ward within Limits
of the Municipal Council of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land belonging to the Colombo
Municipal Council.
P
A TALI  CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA M.P.,
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development,
17 th & 18 th Floors,
‘‘Suhurupaya’’,
Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
31st  August, 2016.
Schedule
The land is containing an extent of 2A.- 0R.22.13P. depicted as Lot Nos. 1 to 4 in P Plan No. A 5353 dated 20.06.1964
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at Lunupokuna Ward in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo within the
Municipal Council limits of Colombo, in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
The land is bounded as follows :
North : Aluth Mawatha Road, Canal and Lot No. 9 in P P A 2948 ;
East : Lot No. 9 in P P A 2948 and No. 8 in P P 2948 ;
South : Lot No. 7 in P P A 2948 and Asst. Nos. 266, 262/1 to 262/16 and 262 Aluth Mawatha Path ;
West : Path, Asst. Nos. 266, 262/1 to 262/16 & 262 Aluth Mawatha Road, Asst. No. 254 Aluth
 Mawatha Road, Asst. No. 250/6 Aluth Mawatha Road.

BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 enacted by
the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Magapolis and Western Development do by this
Order, vest with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today the land situated is Kotahena ward within the limits
of the Municipal Council of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land belonging to the Colombo
Municipal Council.
P
ATALI  CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA M.P.,
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development,
17 th & 18 th Floors,
‘‘Suhurupaya’’,
Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla.
20th  September, 2016.
Schedule
The land is containing an extent of 2A-3R-27.77P depicted as Lot Nos. 1 to 8 in  Plan No. 414771 dated 06.05.1931
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at Kotahena Ward in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo within the
Municipal Council limits of Colombo, in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
The land is bounded as follows :
North : Gorakagahakumbura and Gorakagahawatta claimed by A P Casie Chetty ;
East : Wasala Road, premises bearing Assmt. No. 65 claimed by Louisa Fernando &
 others, 66 claimed by Christina Fernando & others & 67 claimed by P Louisa Fernando &
others ;
South : Pansala Road ;
West : The remaining portion of premises bearing Assmt. Nos. 74 & 80 claimed by T M Fernando.
