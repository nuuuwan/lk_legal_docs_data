SERVICE  MINUTE  OF  SRI   LANKA  TECHNOLOGICAL   SERVICE
THE following amendments are made to the Service Minute of Sri Lanka Technological Service published in the Gazette
Extraordinary of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1930/12 dated 01st September 2015 and the other
matters and conditions of the said notification will remain unchanged.
01. Para 7.5(e) of page no. 7A shall be amended as follows :
 7.5 (e) I. Where there should be change of the training period of the applicants who are include in the training grade of
a particular post under the open or limited stream, on case basis according to the need of the relevant Ministry/
Department, the appointing authorities concerned shall take action to include the training period in each scheme
of recruitment after obtaining the approval of the Public Service Commission.
II. However, above Section 7.5(e) (I) shall not be applied with regard to the posts for which it is prescribed that a
training of two years or more should be obtained as per the approved recruitment procedures, except with
regard to the posts for which the training period has been limited to one year at present on special grounds.
On the Order of the Public Service Commission,
H. M. G
AMINI SENEVIRATHNE,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
22nd November 2016.
