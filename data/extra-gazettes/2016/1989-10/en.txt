No. 1989/10  –  TUESDAY, OCTOBER 18, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND ACQUISITION  
(AMENDMENT)  ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
IT is hereby notify that the government intends to acquire 
the private land situated at the Thabbowa Sanctuary 
belonging to the Department of wild Life Conservation 
for the purpose of Wildlife’s Conservation under Sub         
Sec (1), Section 7 of Land Acquisition Act (Cap. 460) as 
amended by the land Acquisition (Amendment) Act. No. 
28 of 1964. 
 
Particulars of the lots of lands are bounded on as 
follows: 
 
North by : Cart road; East by : Lot No. 1821 of FTP 
22; South by : Lot No. 1826, 1827 and 1828 of FTP 22; 
West by : Lot No. 1819 of FTP 22. 
 
The lots of land containing in extent 4.0090 Hectares 
as mentioned in the lot No. 8200 of Preliminary Survey 
Plan No FTP 22 prepared on 29th April 2016 by the 
District Superintendant of Survey and situated in 
Karadipuval Village in the Karadipuval GS Division     
No. 637/3 in Vanathawilluwa DS Division of Puttalam 
District, North Western Province. 
 
Those who claim the ownership, possession or other 
connection or the Authorized persons on their documents 
for the said lots of land shall meet me on 30th day of 
November 2016 at 10.00 a. m.  At Divisional Secretariat, 
Vanathawilluwa. 
 
Moreover, 02 copies with mentioning their nature of 
ownership for the land, details of facts to claim the 
compensation for the land, amount of compensation and 
the method of calculation of such compensation on the 
said acquisition along with the relevant documents should 
be submitted on or before 20.12.2016 
 
 SANJEEWANI HERATH, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Vanathawilluwa. 
 
11th October, 2016, 
At Divisional Secretariat,
