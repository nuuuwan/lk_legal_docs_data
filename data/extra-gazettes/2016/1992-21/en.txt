  AMENDMENT   TO   PARAGRAPH   6(i)   OF   MINUTE ON   THE   AUDIT   EXAMINERS’   SERVICE
THE paragraph 6(i) of  Minute on the Audit Examiners’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 380/61 of
20th December 1985 has been amended by the Public Service Commission and the amended paragraphs are published for
information.
This amendment will be effective from 11th September 2007.

Amendment to Paragraph 6(i) of Minute on the Audit Examiners’ Service
The paragraph 6(i) of Minute on the Audit Examiners’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 380/61 of 20th
December 1985 has been amended by the Public Service Commission and the amended paragraphs are published for
information.
6(i) In case of a request made to the Appointing Authority according to the specimen form by the Officers of
Audit Examiners’ Service - Grade II who has fulfilled the following qualifications, will be promoted to the Grade I after
scrutinizing the qualifications by the Appointing Authority.
