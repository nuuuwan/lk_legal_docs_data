Regulation
The Land Acquisition (Payment of Compensation) Regulations 2013, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1864/54
of May 30, 2014 is hereby amended as follows :
1. In Regulation 2 thereof by the insertion immediately after Sub-paragraph (r) of paragraph (2) of the following
sub-paragraphs :
“(s) Matara - Hambantota Extension of Southern Expressway ;
  (t) Central Expressway.”

following :-
                               “Minister” means the Minister assignged the subject of Lands”
