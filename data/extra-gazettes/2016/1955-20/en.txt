UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Edcuation and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number GL 24, do by this Order, recognize the Sri Lanka Institute
of Information Technology (Guarantee) Limited as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher
Education therein, leading to the award of the Degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the
abbreviations are specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of the Schedule hereto.
