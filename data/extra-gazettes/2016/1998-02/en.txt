PRADESHIYA  SABHA   ACT,   No.   15   OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by Paragraph (d) of Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, I,
Faiszer Musthapha, Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government, do by this Order dissolve Thamankaduwa
Preadeshiya Sabha constituted by an order published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 453/24 dated 11th May 1987 with
effect from 30th June 2017 for the purpose of constitution of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council and Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya
Sabha.

PRADESHIYA  SABHA   ACT,   No.   15   OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by Section (1) of Section 2, Section 4 and Section 6 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987 and  Section 27F of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, I, Faiszer Musthapha, Minister of Provincial
Councils and Local Government, do by this Order–
(1) declare the area specified in Part -1 of the Schedule hereto, to be a Pradeshiya Sabha area for the purpose of
that Act ;
(2) define the administrative limits of the Pradeshiya Sabha area to be the limits as set out in Part - II of that
Schedule ;
(3) constitute a Pradeshiya Sabha for the Pradeshiya Sabha area declared under paragraph (1) of this Order and
assign the following name to such Sabha ;
“Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha”
(4) I, order that Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of -
(i) ten (10) members to be appointed under Paragraph (a) of the subsection (1) of Section (4) of the Pradeshiya
Sabha Act ;
(ii) three (03) members to be appointed under Paragraph (b) of section  (4) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act ;
(iii) four (04) female members to be elected under subsection (3) of Section 27F of  Local Authorities Elections
Ordinance.
(5) I, appoint 01.07.2017 as the date on which the term of office of the Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha shall
commence.
F
AISZER M USTHAPHA ,
Minister of Provincial Councils and
Local Government.
Colombo,
17th December 2016,
