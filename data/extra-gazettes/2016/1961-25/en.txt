No. 1961/25 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 06, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Tilakarathne Bandara Ekanayaka,
Acting Minister of  Lands, here by declare under Sub section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0466 Hectare, depicted
as A and B in the Advance Tracing No. Gam/Biya/2014/100 dated
16.02.2015 prepared by the Surveyor General of Gampaha, situated
in the village called Malvana, in the Division of Adikari Pattuwa in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Biyagama in Gampaha District
of the Western province shall be acquired to establish a Post Office
in Malvana and the boundaries are as follows:.
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Ilukovita – Extent: 0.0423 Hectare
North: Ilukovita claimed by A. C. M. Hussain and M. M. A.
Faizz, Road (R.D.A) and Lot B; East: Lot B and Ilukovita claimed
by K. N. Jayarathne; South: Ilukovita claimed by K. N. Jayarathne
and Ilukovita claimed by A. C. M. Hussain and M. M. A. Faizz;West:
Ilukovita claimed by A. C. M. Hussain and M. M. A. Faizz and
Road (R.D.A).
02. Lot ‘B’  –  Ilukovita – Extent: 0.0043 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A); East: Ilukovita claimed by K. N. Jayarathne;
South: Ilukovita claimed by K. N. Jayarathne and Lot A;West: Lot A
and Road (R.D.A).
T. B. Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Acting).
