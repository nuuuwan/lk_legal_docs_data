THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I,Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakaratna Bandara Ekanayake,
Acting Minister of Lands,  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land in total extent of about 2.8605 Hectares
depicted in Advance Tracing  No. R/ELP/2013/863 dated 12.08.2014,
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the   Village of
Dambuluwana (FVP 40) of Nawadun Korale, Palle Paththuwa Minor
Division in Divisional Secretary's Division of Elapatha in Ratnapura
District, in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dambuluwana Estate – Extent : About  2.8605
Hectares
North: Road and Velamitimulla Estate; East: Velamitimulla Estate
and Dambuluwana Estate; South: Dambuluwana Estate  and Road
(Pradeshiya Saba); West: Road (Pradeshiya Saba) and Dambuluwana
Estate.
T. B. Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Acting).
