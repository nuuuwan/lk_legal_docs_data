L.D.B. 21/78
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order establishing the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which Order is deemed, by
virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1948/58 of January 08, 2016) as follows :
(1) by the addition immediately after entry “9. Department of Sociology” appearing in Column II which
corresponds to the item “Faculty of Arts” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entries :-
“10. Department of Buddhist Studies” ;
(2) by the addition immediately after entry “14. Department of Surgery and Anesthesiology” appearing in
Column II which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Medicine” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of
the following entries :-
“15. Department of Medical Humanities
 16. Department of Medical Education
 17. Department of Allied Health Sciences.”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
03rd August 2016.
