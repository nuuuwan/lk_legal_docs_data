No. 1951/15 –   WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 27, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: DSB/LND/02/F1/14..  
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/16/2013/NRD/26. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for Matara-Kataragama Railway Project. 
 
Description of the Land: 
 
Lot Nos. 191 and 192 depicted in the Plan F. V. P. 
307-5/5 dated 27th November 2015, prepared by the 
Surveyor General and situated in the Village of Ovilana 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Beliatta in 
Hambantota District. 
  
Lot No. 191  
Name of the Land - Lindaarawatta - Claimant: Nilanthi 
Rupika Wagachchi, Ovilana, Beliatta; Extent: 0.0237 
Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 192  
Name of the Land - Lindaarawatta - Claimant: Indra 
Kusumalatha Wagachchi, Ovilana, Beliatta; Extent: 0.0123 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
of Beliatta on the 2nd March, 2016 at 10.00 a. m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 24th February 2016, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
All deeds and other documents in support of the 
claims should be produced at the inquiry. 
 
 J. H. S. P.  JAYAMAHA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Beliatta. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Beliatta, 
20th   January, 2016.
