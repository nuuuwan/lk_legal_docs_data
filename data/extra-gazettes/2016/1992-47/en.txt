I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose .
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called "Veheragawamukalana" in Total
extent of about 0.0212 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1075 in
Village Plan No. 753, Supplement No. 43, Sheet No. 43, dated
on 23.06.2016 surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated at the Village of Panamura in Diyapotagam Pattuwa
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Embilipitiya in Rathnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province
and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 1075
Name of Land: Veheragawamukalana; Description: --;
Claimant: Mr. R. K. Jayalath, We Athura, Panamure;  Extent:
0.0212 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North : Lot No. 402; East : Lot Nos. 402, 1070 and 1066;South
: Lot Nos. 402, 1070, 1066 and 1065; W est : Lot Nos. 1065 and
402.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Divisional   Secretary's Office, at
Embilipitiya on the 28th day of  December, 2016 at 10.00 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing [in
duplicate] on or before the 20th day of December  2016, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
WASANTHA G UNARATHNA,
