No. 1980/38 – FRIDAY, AUGUST 19, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, here
by declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Uduwara Watta’’ in total extent of
about 0.1603 Hectare, shown and described in the Advance Tracing
No. B/ HLE/2015/663 dated 31.12.2015, prepared by the Surveyor
General situated at the village of Wattepanguwa (F.V .P .176) in
Hapuwala kumbura Grama Niladhari Division in Bogoda Korale
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division  of Hali Ela in
Badulla District, Uva Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Uduwara Wattta – Extent: About 0.1603 Hectare
North: Road and Badulu Oya; East: Badula Oya and Uduwara
Watta claimed by Douglus Silva Nanayakkara; South: Uduwara Watta
claimed by Douglus Silva Nanayakkara, Road (R.D.A.) and Uduwara
Watta claimed by Chandrarathna Nanayakkara;West: Uduwara Watta
claimed by Chandrarathna Nanayakkara and Road.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
