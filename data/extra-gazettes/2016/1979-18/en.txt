THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Notification issued by me in the excercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (1) of
Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1933/13 of 21st September, 2015 and amended from time to time, is further amended with effect from August 01,
2016 in the manner set out in the Schedule below :
 M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
August 04, 2016.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the Heading “Minister of Justice (No. 27)” of the said Notification, as follows :-
(a) In Column II thereof, by the insertion immediately after Item 11 thereof, of the following item ;
“12. Commercial Mediation Centre of Sri Lanka”
(b) In Column III thereof, by the insertion immediately after the expression “Assistance to and Protection of
Victims of Crime and Witnesses Act, No. 4 of 2015” of the following Item :-
“Commercial Mediation Centre of Sri Lanka Act, No. 44 of 2000”
