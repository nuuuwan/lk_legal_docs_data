Land Acquisition Notices
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands do hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of the Land Acquisition Act.
An allotment of  land called Watta depicted in the Advance Tracing
No. PO/HKG/2012/346 dated 20.09.2016, prepared by the Surveyor
General in the Village of  Minneriya in the Sinhala pattuwa Minor
Division in the Hingurakgoda Divisional Secretary's Division in the
District of Polonnaruwa in the North Central Province and containing
in extent 0.0637 Hectare and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  A – Watta – Extent: 0.0637 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of the land claimed by B. L. Peter; East:
Minneriya Rathmale Reserve and Lot B; South: Land wherein
Minneriya Water Plant is located; West:Remaining portion of the
land claimed by B. L. Peter.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
My No. : 4-3/9/2012/WS/54.
Ministry of Lands,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
13th October 2016.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of  Lands, hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the servitude over the land,  described  in the following
Chapter 1 is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under
the provisions of that Act. It describes in Chapter 2 from which land
that the servitude will be acquired.
1. Describe of the Servitude .– Acquisition of Servitude over
the land, for the Access Road to Kurunegala Water Treatment Plant
of Greater Kurunegala Water Supply & Sanitation Projecct.
2. Describe of the Land .–  The allotment of land called
‘‘Paramount Watta’’ in total extent of about 0.0134 Hectare, described
in the Advance Tracing No. KU/KRN/2015/281 dated on 22.06.2015,
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at the Village of
Kurunegala in Kurunegala Town - North - East Grama Niladhari
Division, No. 834 in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kurunegala
in Kurunegala District, North Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot  ‘A’ – Paramount Watta – Extent: About
0.0134 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of the said land claimed by N. M. Gamage
and Meagazinkanda; East: Megazinkanda, Road and Open waste
land; South: Open waste land and Suaratissa Mawatha; West:
Suaratissa Mawatha and remaining portion of the said land claimed
by N. M. Gamage.
