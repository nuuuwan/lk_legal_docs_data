THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub – section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The alloments of lands mentioned below in the Advance  Tracing
No. MR/ MTR/2014/ 485 dated 12.11.2014 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the village of Hiththatiya central, in the Matara
Four Gravet Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Matara in Matara District in Southern Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Batuwitagewatta – Extent: 0.0030 Hectare
North: Batuwitagewatta claimed by Kariyawasam Munasinghage
Rupasingha and Road ; East: Road ; South: Road and Batuwitagewatta
claimed by Amarasingha Dissanayaka; West: Batuwitagewatta claimed
by Amarasingha Dissanayaka.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Rathuralagewatta alias Ampitiyawatta alias
Koongahahena – Extent: 0.0039 Hectare
North: Road, Rathuralagewaththa claimed by Hewa Marabhage
Sajeewa Dias ; East: Rathuralagewaththa alias Ampitiyawaththa alias
Koongahahena claimed by Hewa Nala Gamage Nishanthapriya ; South
and West: Road.
