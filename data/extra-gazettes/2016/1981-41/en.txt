Notification
IT is hereby notified that the duties and functions and Departments, Statutory Institutions and Government Corporations
vested in me as the Minister in charge of the subject of lands in terms of the provisions in Paragraph (1) of Article 43 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be delegated to the State Minister of Lands in terms
of the provisions in Paragraph (5) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
effective from 10th August 2016 as indicated in the following Schedule
J

Minister of Lands.
At the Ministry of Lands,
22nd August 2016.
