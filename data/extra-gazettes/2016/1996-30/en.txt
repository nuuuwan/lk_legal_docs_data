THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No. : MCUDP/CMC/MD/CA/P4.
Reference No.: 4-3/8/2015/PC252.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following lands for the widening and
development of Norris Canal in Maradana under the Metro
Colombo Urban Development Project:
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Three (3) allotments of lands described in following name
and Total Extents of 0.0217 Hectare depicted as Lot Nos. 1, 2
and 3 in the Preliminary Plan No. CO 9669 dated 02.12.2016
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village called
Maradana, in the Colombo Divisional Secretariat Division in
Colombo District, Western Province within the Colombo
Municipal Council limit, and morefully described as follows.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Asst. No. 338 (Part) T. B. Jayah Mawatha,
Colombo 10; Land Use: Home Garden; Claimant: Cannot be
traced; Extent: 0.0002 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of the Land: Asst. No. 338 (Part) T. B. Jayah Mawatha,
Colombo 10; Land Use: Home Garden; Claimant: Cannot be
traced; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of the Land: Asst. No. 338 (Part) T. B. Jayah Mawatha,
Colombo 10; Land Use: Home Garden Parts of two permanent
buildings and a guard room; Claimant: Cannot be traced;
Extent: 0.0214 Hectare.
Any person interested in the aforesaid lands, are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Municipal Commissioner's office
Colombo, on the 28th December, 2016 at 9.30 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before 20th December, 2016 the nature of their interest in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
V. K. A. ANURA,
Municipal Commissioner and
