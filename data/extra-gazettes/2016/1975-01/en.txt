CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I,  W. A. Chulananda Perera,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 11th  July, 2016, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance   (Chapter 235), shall
be   paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1974/1  of
04th July, 2016 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notifi-
cation to be published on or before 17th July  2016.
W. A. C
HULANANDA PERERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
08th   July  2016.
