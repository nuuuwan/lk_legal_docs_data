STAMP   DUTY   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  12  OF  2006
Order under Section  5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 2006, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance do by this Order, amend the Order made under that Section and published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1465/20 of October 05, 2006, as last amended by the Order made under the said Section and
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1889/48 of November 21,2014, as specified in the SCHEDULE hereto.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
31st December 2015.
SCHEDULE
by the addition, immediately after item 33 of the SCHEDULE thereto, of the following new item :-
“34. Any claim, demand, or request presented for the payment of any sum of money due in respect of every credit
card transaction entered into during any period commencing on or after January 1, 2016, where the merchant country
thereof is Sri Lanka (in which the purchase of goods or services or the withdrawals of money taken place), irrespective of
whether it was a card present or not present transaction in any currency.
35. Any share certificate issued consequent to the issue, transfer or assignment of any number of shares of any
