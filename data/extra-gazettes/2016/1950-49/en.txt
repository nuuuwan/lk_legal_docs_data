AND WHEREAS, Preethi Padman Surasena Esquire, Judge of the High Court, who was appointed as a Member and
the Chairman of the Said  Commission, has been appointed as a Judge of the Court of Appeal ;
NOW THEREFORE, I,  Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, reposing
great trust and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity, do in pursuance of the provisions of Section 3 of  the
Commissions of Inquiry Act, by  these presents, appoint Preethi Padman Surasena Esquire Judge of the Court of  Appeal, to
be the Chairman of  the said Commission.
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, on this 20th day of January,
Two Thousand and Sixteen.
                                                                                                                           By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
