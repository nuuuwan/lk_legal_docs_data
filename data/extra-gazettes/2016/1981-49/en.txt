CEYLON  ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIYA ,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
23rd August 2016.
SCHEDULE
The lands situated in the Village of Polpitiya in Grama Niladari’s Division of No. 318, Ambagamuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ambagamuwa Korale, in the Administrative District of Nuwara Eliya in the Province
of Central of Sri Lanka containing in extents of 1.48 Hectares depicted as Block No. 01 and 0.17 Hectares depicted as
Block No. 02 shown in Draft Plan No. BHP 003 dated 01.07.2016 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board and bounded as
follows :-
01. Block No. 01 - Land in extent of 1.48 Hectares with permanent buildings and home cultivation
North : Hagarapitiya Road ;
East : Hagarapitiya Road ;
South : Hagarapitiya Road  and few private lands ;
West : Hagarapitiya Road ;
Claimed by : unknown.
02. Block No. 02 - Land in extent of 0.17 Hectares with permanent buildings and home cultivation
North : Yaddhala Road ;
East : Hagarapitiya Road ;
South : Few unknown private lands ;
West : Few unknown private lands ;
Claimed by : unknown.
