THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of the land situated at Grama Niladhari's Division of
Kosmodara  (242 E) of Kotapola (F.V .P.45) village of Morawak Korale
Minor Division in Divisional Secretariat, Kotapola of  Matara District
in Southern Province, depicted in Survey General's Tracing No.MR/
KTP/2015/604 and dated 14-03-2016 and the boundaries of the land
given below.
1. Lot  ‘A’ – Sambhodiwatta   – Extent: 0.0155  Hectare
North: Lot No. 742 1/2 of F.V .P 45 and Sambodhiwatta claimed
by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board; East: Lot No. 742 1/2 of F.V .P 45
and Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board and
Lot B; South and West: Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta
Trustee Board.
2. Lot ‘B’ –  Sambhodiwatta– Extent: 0.0066  Hectare
North: Lot No.742 1/2 of F.V .P 45; East: Lot No.742 1/2 of F.V .P
45 and Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board;
South: Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board;
West: Lot A and Lot No.742 1/2 of 45.
3. Lot ‘C’ –  Sambhodiwatta– Extent: 0.0618  Hectare
North: Lot No. 742 1/2 of F.V .P 45 and Sambodhiwatta claimed
by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board; East: Lot No. 744 1/2 of F.V .P 45
and Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board; South:
Lot No.744 of F.V .P 45 and Sambodhiwatta claimed by
Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board; West: Lot No.742 1/2 of f.v.p 45
Sambodhiwatta claimed by Sambodhiwatta Trustee Board.
