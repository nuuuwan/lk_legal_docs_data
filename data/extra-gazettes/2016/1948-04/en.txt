No. 1948/4 –   MONDAY, JANUARY 04. 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
            My Ref. No.: 8/3/5/25. 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4/3/15/2011/VE/45. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land shown as lot 1 & 2 in 
Preliminary Plan No. Ma/1629, prepared by Surveyor 
General, situated at Udawaradamana Village in 
Gampahasiya pattuwa minor Division of Matale 
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central 
province. And bounded as follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 1 —  Name of the Land: Hapugahalanda-
watta; Original ownership: Land Reform Commission; 
Extent: 0.3591 Hectare. 
 
North : Hapugahalandawatta claimed by  
S. Sotthilingam; East: Hapugahalandawatta climed by T. 
G. Karunarathna and others; South: Road; West: Road 
(Pradeshiya Sabha). 
1. Lot No. 2 —  Name of the Land: Hapugahalanda-
watta; Original ownership: Land Reform Commission; 
Extent: 0.8561 Hectare. 
 
North: Road (Pradeshiya sabha) and Road; East: 
Hapugahalandawatta climed by Land Reform 
Commission and Road; South: Hapugahalandawatta 
claimed by Land Reform Commission; West: Road 
(Pradeshiya Sabha). 
 
All person interested in the aforesaid Land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Matale Divisional Secretariat on the 26th day of 
February, 2016 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
19th day of February, 2016 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
  
 K. P. K. L. P. MADHUWANTHI,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matale. 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Matale, 
29th December, 2015.
