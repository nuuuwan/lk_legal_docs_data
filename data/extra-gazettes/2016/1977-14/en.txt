THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
1. Description of Servitude.–Servitude for the laying of pipe lines
and improvement of access to the water treatment plant under the
Gampaha - Attanagalle - Minuwangoda Integrated Water Supply
Scheme.
2. Description of Land.– The allotments of land extent of about
0.1268 Hectare depicted in the  Advance Tracing No. Gam/Ath/2014/
564 dated 19th  January 2015 prepared by the Surveyor General situated
in the village of Karasnagala in Udugaha Pattu Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Attanagalla, Gampaha District
Western Province.
01. Lot No. 1 – Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and
Gorokkotuwawatte – Extent: About 0.0300 Hectare
North: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte
claimed by Kurunegala Plantation Company; East: Oyabadawatte,
Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte and Gorokkotuwawatte claimed
by National Water Supply and  Drainage Board and Road; South:
Road; West: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte
claimed by Kurunegala Plantation Company, Road and Lot No.02.
02. Lot No. 2 – Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and
Gorokkotuwawatte – Extent: About 0.0217 Hectare
North: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte
claimed by K.A. Padmalal Jayasinghe; East: Oyabadawatte,
Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte claimed by  Kurunegala
Plantation Company, Road, Lot No.1 and Road; South: Road; West:
Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte claimed by
Nadun Abeywickrama and Lot No.3.
03. Lot No. 3 – Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and
Gorokkotuwawatte – Extent: About 0.0294 Hectare
North: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte
claimed by Nadun Abeywickrama; East: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda
and Gorokkotuwawatte claimed by K.A. Padmalal Jayasuriya, Lot
No. 2 and Road; South: Road; West: Road and canal.
04. Lot No. 4 – Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and
Gorokkotuwawatte – Extent: About 0.0103 Hectare
North: Oyabadawatte, Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte
claimed by Sumith Jayantha Harischandra and canal; East: Road;
South: Road and Lot No. 3; West: Lot No. 5 and Oyabadawatte,
Halgahalanda and Gorokkotuwawatte claimed by K.W. Jagath
Anthony.
