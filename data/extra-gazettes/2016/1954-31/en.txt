PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual Higher Ordination of Rohana Sanga Sabhawa
IT is hereby informed that the 31st Higher Ordination Ceremony of the Sri Rohana Sanga Sabhawa is to be held  on 13th,
14th and 15th May 2016 at Sri Rohana Sanga Sabhawa of Siri Rathanajothi Piriwena, Urapola, Thihagoda and Mangala
Seemamalakaya of Randupatha Sri Rohana Upoosathagaraya Maha Vihara, Kotuwegoda, Matara and that the pilgrims should
refrain from carrying with them any peastic or polythene materials and that the standing orders and regulations under
Section 02 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175 - L.D.B/11/60) declared on 14th October 1991 and published in
the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, will be in force during the period in which the ceremony
is held.
R
ANJITH YAPA,
