THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthoni Emmanuvel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,  hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land in total extent of about 0.5239 Hectare
depicted in Advance Tracing  No. Maha/HRS/2013/1641 dated 20th
November 2013,  prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the
Village of Medawela in the Minor Division of Kulugammana Siya
Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary's  Division of Harispaththuwa
in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Gedara Kumbura alias Wagale Kumbura –
Extent :  0.2404 Hectare
North: Talagahapele Kumbura claimed by M. G. Tilakarathna,
Moragahamula Watta claimed by Esmend Ekanayaka and
Godamadiththa Hena claimed by Ananda College of Kandy; East:
Godamadiththa Hena claimed by Ananda College of  Kandy and Lot
B; South: Lot B  and  Hitinagedara Watta claimed by Anil Weerasekara;
West: Hitinagedara Watta claimed by Anil Weerasekara and
Talagahapele Kumbura claimed by M. G . Tilakarathna.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Atalahekumbura – Extent :  0.1900 Hectare
North: Lot A and  Godamadiththa Hena claimed by Ananda College
of Kandy; East: Godamadiththa Hena claimed by Ananda College of
Kandy and Lot C; South: Lot C, Medawalapitiye Watta claimed by
M. N. G . Mahinda Weerasingha, Medawalapitiye Watta claimed by
H. M. Danawathi and Hitinagedara Watta claimed by Anil
Weerasekara; West: Hitinagedara Watta claimed by Anil Weerasekara
and Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Nagahamula Ange Kumbura – Extent :  0.0935
Hectare
North: Lot B,  Godamadiththa Hena claimed by Ananda College
of Kandy and Malwatta Tenna claimed by H. M. Abeysingha; East:
Malwatta Tenna claimed by H. M. Abeysingha and Medawalapitiye
Watta claimed by M. N. G . Mahinda Weerasingha; South: Malwatta
Tenna claimed by Udeni Samarasingha, Medawalapitiye Watta
claimed by M. N. G . Mahinda Weerasingha and  Medawalapitiye
Watta claimed by H. M. Danawathi; West: Medawalapitiye Watta
claimed by M. N. G . Mahinda Weerasingha,  Medawalapitiye Watta
claimed by H. M. Danawathi, Hitinagedara Watta claimed by Anil
Weerasekara and Lot B.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
