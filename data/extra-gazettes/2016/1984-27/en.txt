I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the servitude of the lands 
described below for the construction of a tunnel to create 
a drainage way from the main canal in Aluth Mawatha 
area up to the sea under a sub project of Metro Colombo 
Urban Development Project.
