No. 1964/68 –  FRIDAY, APRIL,  29, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No.  28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  Reference No.: BD/ST/LND/1/7/33. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 13/96/HW/184. 
 
I  hereby give notice, Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire  the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Public Purpose means the Construction for Badulla 
Karametiya Andaulpatha 14/9 Bridge. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Portion of Land described in the schedule hereunder, 
situated  in the Nagollagama Village in the Soranathota 
Korale in the Soranathota Divisional Secretary’s Division 
in Badulla District, in the Province of Uva containing  
about 0.0868 Hectare in extent and depicted in the 
Supplement No. 31 of  the Final Village Plan No. 178, 
Prepared by the Surveyor General. 
 
THE SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No. 721 
 
Name of the Land: Kosgahakumbura; Land Use: 
Paddy Field; Owner: State; Extent: 0.0538 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 722 
 
Name of the Land: Kosgahakumbura; Land Use: 
Road (High Road); Owner: State; Extent: 0.0330 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired  to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Soranathota Divisional Secretary’s Office on the 29th 
Day of June, 2016 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
15th Day of June 2016 the nature of their interests in the 
land the particulars of their claims for compensation of 
such amount. 
 
                        H. M. NANDASENA, 
                         Divisional Secretary, 
                    Soranathota. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Soranathota, 
27th April 2016.
