EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that —
1. ( a) The Excise Duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule I hereto shall be payable at the
rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule I ;
(b) The concessionary Excise Duty on excisable articles specified in Column II of the Schedule II hereto shall
be payable at the rate specified in Column III of the Schedule II wherever applicable ; and
(c) Wherever an ad valorem rate and a unit rate are specified in Column IV of the Schedule I hereto, the
applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the Excise Duty payable shall be the rate which results in a
higher amount being payable as Excise Duty.
2. The orders made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notifications No. 1941/29 of 20.11.2015, No. 1949/29 of 12.01.2016, No. 1964/70
of 29.04.2016, No. 1967/52 of 19.05.2016, No. 1968/19 of 26.05.2016, No. 1980/41 of 19.08.2016,
No. 1980/42 of 19.08.2016 and No. 1987/08 of 03.10.2016 are hereby rescinded.
3. This order shall come into effect from 11.11.2016.
RAV I  K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
10th November 2016.
