L.D.B - 104/44(V)
TRUSTS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “The Revival Christian Ministry Trust” namely :-
1. Rev. Mary Joseph Gread Jebakumar
2. George Manoharan Ariyathas
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87).
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Wijayadasa Rajapaksa, Minister of
Justice do this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that -
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing No. 4107 of October 02, 2014 attested by
D. L. & F. de Seram, Notary public of Colombo shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a
common seal under the name style of the “The Revival Christian Ministry Trust” ;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said names and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust ; and
(c) the trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and
to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and actions
arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me be
necessary.
W
IJAYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
19th December 2016,
