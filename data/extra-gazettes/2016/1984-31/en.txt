THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare under Sub section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of lands in extent about 2.0207 Hectares depicted in a
Advance Tracing No.: KE/GLG/2015/104 of  05.06.2015 prepared
by Surveyor General  situated in the village of Helamada in Eastern
Sub Division in Beligal Korale in Galigamuwa Divisional Secretary's
Division in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.0250 Hectare
North: Dompae Watta and Kotuhena; East: Kotuhena; South: Lot
B; West: Canal and Dompae Watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.0170 Hectare
North: Lot E, Dompae Watta, Canal and Lot A; East: Kotuhena,
Road and Lot C; South: Lot  C, Canal and Lot D; West: Lot E and
Dompae Watta.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.0247 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Road and Kotuhena; South: Kotuhena; West:
Canal and Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.0182 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Lot B and Canal; South: Canal and Kotuhena;
West: Lot B.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.0242 Hectare
North: Lot F and Dompae Watta; East: Dompae Watta and Lot B;
South: Lot B and Kotuhena; West: Canal and Dompae Watta.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
1.4473 Hectare
North: Orange Grove Watta alias Kotuhena, Lot G and Dompae
Watta; East: Dompae Watta, Lot E and Kotuhena; South: Kotuhena
and Epitahena; West: Orange Grove Watta alias Kotuhena.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Orange Grove Estate alias Kotuhena – Extent:
0.4643 Hectare
North: Orange Grove Watta alias Kotuhena and Kongahamula
Watta; East: Kongahamula Watta; South: Dompae Watta and Lot F;
West: Orange Grove Watta alias Kotuhena.
