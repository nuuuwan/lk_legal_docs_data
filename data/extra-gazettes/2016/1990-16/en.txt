THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16 OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1482/26 of February 02, 2007 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1960/8 of March 29, 2016), which specifies the Faculties and
the Departments of the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of
the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the entry “2.
Department of Anesthesiology” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Medicine”
appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry :-
“2. Department of Anaesthesiology and Critical Care”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th September 2016.
