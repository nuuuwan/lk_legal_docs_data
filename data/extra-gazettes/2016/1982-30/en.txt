CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notice
I do hereby announce in terms of Subsection 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Repulic of Sri Lanka
that I delegate the functions of the following Schedule assigned to me as the Minister in charge of Labour and Trade Union
Relations under Subsection 44(1) of the Constitution to the Minister of State of Labour and Trade Union Relations with
effect from 23.08.2016.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
