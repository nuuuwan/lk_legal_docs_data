THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the  Order establishing the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virute
of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made  under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1859/8 of April 22, 2014)  by the addition immediately after the
entry “Faculty of Medical Sciences”  appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column II  thereof, of the
following :-
