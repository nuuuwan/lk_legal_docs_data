THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakarathne Bandara Ekanayaka,
Minister of Lands (Acting) hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed to establish a police barrier in Pothupitiya and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land described as Lot ‘A’ in total extent of about
0.1075 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. R/KLW/2014/778
and dated 7th November, 2014 prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Pothupitiya, (F.V.P. 936) Uda pattuwa
Minor Division in Kukulu Korale, in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kalawana in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Polkoratuwa – Extent: 0.1075 Hectare
North: Road, Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Coconut land claimed
by V. A. Samarasinghe; East: Coconut land claimed by V . A.
Samarasinghe, V. Piyasena, V . J. Kumara and J. G. Kodagoda; South:
Coconut land claimed by V . Piyasena, V . J. Kumara and J. G .
Kodagoda and Road; West: Road.
