REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT  No. 32  OF  1968
Notification under Section 36(2)
BY virtue of powers vested in me under section 36(2) of the Registration of Persons Act No. 32 of 1968 I, S. B. Navinna
Minister of Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs hereby notify that a maximum fee of one hundred
and fifty rupees should be charged by a registered photographer, in respect of supplying three copies of a photograph after
taking a photograph of a person applies for obtaining an identity card or duplicate of an identity card after registration.
I hereby cancel the notification published in Gazette No. 14998 of 18th February 1972 in this regard.
S. B. N
AVINNA,
Minister of Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development
and Cultural Affairs.
Battaramulla,
05th  May  2016.
