WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 02 of the powers of Supervision of the  Administration   of   the   Local   Authorities
Statute,  No. 04  of  1991  of   the  Western  Province  Provincial  Council
I, Isura Devapriya, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Economic Development, Power and Energy, Enviornmental Affairs, Water
Supply and Drainage and Tourism of the Western Province Provincial Council do hereby announce that the inquiry period
will be extended to 2nd August of the Single Person Tribunal comprising retired Judge of High Court Hon. Kumara Hewage
Sumithrapala appointed on 21st March 2016 by the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1959/7 of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as per the powers entrusted to me by the provisions of Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of
the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council to inquire and
submit a report based on the following matters/allegations within 03 months, whether the Mayor of Moratuwa Municipal
Council, W. Samanlal Fernando, while functioning in his office has made the offences stated under Section 2(1) of the
aforementioned statute.
