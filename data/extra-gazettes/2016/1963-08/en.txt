ORDER   UNDER   SECTION  10(1)(a) OF  THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10(1)(a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane,
Minister of Home Affairs, do by this order declare Friday, 15th April, 2016 a Public Holiday on account of the Sinhala and
Tamil New Year 2016.
W
AJIRA ABEYWARDANE,
Minister of Home Affairs.
Ministry of Home Affairs,
Indepedence Square,
Colombo 07,
