THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1454/37 of July 22, 2006 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1947/24 of December 30, 2015) which specified the Faculties and the
Departments of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the
aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after entry “16.
Department of Disability Studies” appearing in Column II which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Medicine” appearing
in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry :-
“17. Department of Medical Education”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
01st November 2016.
