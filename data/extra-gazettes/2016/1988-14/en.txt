THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. D. S. M. Wijesinghe, Opposite Wettawa School, Wettawa, Dambadeniya, of the one part
and
Commercial Credit and Finance PLC, No. 106, Yatinuwara Veediya, Kandy, of the other part.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the claim made by Mr. D. S. M. Wijesinghe who worked in the Commercial Credit and Finance PLC that, he had been caused
injustice by not being paid the annual salary increments, bonuses and the profits relevant to the periods from 01.04.2012 to 31.03.2013 and from
01.04.2013 to 31.03.2014, is just and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGA,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo,
19th day of August, 2016.
