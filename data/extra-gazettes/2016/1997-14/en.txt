THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 02 OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No.
02 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation
of the Minister-in-charge of the subject of Urban Development, being of the opinion that the land specified in the Schedule
hereto is urgently required for the purpose of the construction of a hostel for the University of Sri Jayawardenapura do by
this Order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
08th December 2016.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land depicted in the plan prepared in respect of land called Lansiyadeniya in extent of 0.560 Ha.
situated in Gangodawila South, Grama Niladhari Division of within the Maharagama Urban Council Limits in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Maharagama in Colombo District, Western Province :—
Boundaries of said allotment are as follows :
North : Second Lane, Andiris Mawatha ;
East : Private Road ;
South : Plot of land, Asst. No. 115/45 in Andiris Mawatha claimed by Anushka Subhashini
Gonagalage and Andiris Mawatha ;
West by : Second Lane, Andiris Mawatha.
