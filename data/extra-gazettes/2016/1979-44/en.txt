No. 1979/44 –  FRIDAY,AUGUST,  12, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: 6/3/7/15/07/03. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/MV/118C. 
 
I,  hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Public purpose means: Land Acquisition for 
Moragahakanda and Kaluganga Development Project. 
. 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land shown in Topopp 17 Supplement 
No. 23, Sheet No. 70 approved by Surveyor General on 
22.05.2013, Situated at Medapihilla Village in 
Medapihilla Grama Niladhari Division Gangala Pallesiya 
of Naula Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matale District, 
Central Province, and bounded as follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 3126 
Name of the land: Kendagolla; Description: Light 
Forest; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0835 Hectare. 
2. Lot No. 3167 
Name of the land: Kendagolla; Description: Light 
Forest; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.03551 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 3168 
Name of the land: Kendagolla; Description: Garden; 
Claimant: State; Extent: 1.1989 Hectare. 
 
 
All Person interested in the aforesaid Land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Naula Divisional Secretariat on the 25th day of 
August 2016 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in wiring (in duplicate) on or before the 18th 
day of August, 2016 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 D. R. B. N. B. KARUNATHILAKA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Naula. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, Naula, 
05th August, 2016.
