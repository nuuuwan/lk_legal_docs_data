THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My No. : 4/2/1/33(II).
I, H. G . J. P. Wijayasiriwardana, the Divisional Secretary to the
Divisional Secretariat Dompe of Gampaha District in Western Province,
in terms of the Section 33 of the Land Acquisition Act hereby give
notice that statutory compensation payable in respect of the acquisition
of land described in the Schedule below has been deposited in the
District Court - Pugoda as the owners to the lands acquired to the
government have not been proved/have passed away.
H. G . J. P. Wijayasiriwardana,
Divisional Secretary,
Dompe.
20th October, 2016.
