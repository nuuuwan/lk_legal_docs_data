Lot No. 718 —  Name of the Land: Rambewa kele;

jain Nona, No. 251, Mihintale Road, Rambewa.;

North: Lot No. 226 ; East: Lot No. 225; South: Lot 
No.719; West: Lot   No. 227. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office, at Rambewa on the 8th day of June 
2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day of 
May 2016, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
