THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land called Ketakelagaha Hena alias Kiriwalgoda
Hinna in total extent of about 0.0069 Hectare, depicted in Advance
Tracing No. Maha/HRS/2014/464 of 23rd May, 2015, prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Kondadeniya, in the
Minor Division of Kulugammana Siya Paththuwa in Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Harispaththuwa in Kandy District Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ketakelagaha Hena alias Kiriwalgoda Hinna –
Extent: 0.0069 Hectare
North: Ketakelagaha Hena alias Kiriwalgoda Hinna claimed by
Public Trustee and incumbent priest of Kondadeniya Rajamaha Vihara
and Gamini Palitharathna; East: Dingiriya Deniya claimed by Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha) and National Water Supply and Drainage Board;
South: Dingiriya Deniya claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board; West: Ketakelagaha Hena alias Kiriwalgoda Hinna
claimed by Public Trustee and incumbent priest of Kondadeniya
Rajamaha Vihara and Gamini Palitharathna.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
