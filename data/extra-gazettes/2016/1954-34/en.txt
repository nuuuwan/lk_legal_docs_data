THE   JUDICATURE  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1978
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Justice under Section 61 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 read with Section

WIJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
12th February 2016.
Regulation
1. This Regulation may be cited as the Justices of the Peace Regulation No. 1 of 2016.
2. Every Grama Niladhari who is in the permanent service, shall in his ex-officio capacity, be a Justice of the
Peace for the Divisional Secretary’s Division within which his Grama Niladhari Division is situated.
