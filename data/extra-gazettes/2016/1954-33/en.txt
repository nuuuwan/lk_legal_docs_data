OTICE REGARDING THE GOVERNING BOARD OF THE INSTITUTE "APE GAMA"
The institute “Ape Gama” has been entrusted under the purview of the Minister of Education by Gazette
Extraordinary No 1933/13 dated 21.09.2015 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.The
administration,financial management and the governance of the institute “Ape Gama” is hereby entrusted to a Governing
Board consisted in accordance with the provisions provided hereinafter as an interim provision in effect until a formal
legal framework is complied to perform the aforementioned functions. The composition and the powers entrusted to the
Governing Board is as stipulated below.

The Governing Council shall be comprised of the following persons: -
(i) Chairman appointed by the minister in charge of the subject of Education.
(ii) W orking  Director appointed by the minister in charge of the subject of Education
(iii) 04 other members appointed by the minister in charge of the subject of Education
(iv) The following persons who are ex-officio  members : -
(a) The Secretary to the Ministry under the purview of the minister in charge of the subject of Education or
a senior officer nominated by the Secretary.
(b) Chief Financial Officer of the Ministry under the purview of the minister in charge of the subject of
Education.
(c) An officer nominated by the Secretary of the General Treasury.
The Quorum of board  meetings shall be 05 members.
The powers of the Governing Board shall be as follows: -
(i) Financial management and administration  of the institute “Ape Gama” .
(ii) Recruitment and administration of officers and the other employees deemed necessary for the
implementation of the functions of the institute.
(iii) Preparation, implementation and regulation of plans for the development of the institute “Ape Gama”.
(iv) Establishment and the maintenance of a fund for the execution of powers of the Governing Board.
