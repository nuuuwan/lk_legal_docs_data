THE  WAGES   BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE   ICE,  AERATED  WATERS, FRUIT  JUICE  AND  JAM  MANUFACTURING   TRADE
IT is hereby notifed under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Ms. Nisansala Paranayapa has been appointed
as the Employers’ Representatives member of the Wages Board for the Ice, Aerated Waters, Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing
Trade in place of Mr. Nilantha Jayasinghe by the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 9 of the Wages Boards Ordinance
(Chapter 136). This appointment is valid only upto 30.06.2017.
S. M. G
OTABAY A JAY ARA THNE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05.
07th September 2016.
