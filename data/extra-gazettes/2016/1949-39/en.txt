NORTHWESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order under Section 247 of the Urban Councils Ordinance to be read with Section 2 of  the  Provincial
Councils  (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12  of  1989
I, Dharmasiri Dasanayake, the Chief Minister of Northwestern Province and Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering
Services, Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Economic  Promotion, Power and Energy,
Environmental Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs, do hereby notify the following Order in terms of
Section 247 of the Urban Councils Ordinance (Chapter 255) to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
D
HARMASIRI  D ASANAY AKE,
Northwestern Province Minister of Finance and Planning,
Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local Government
and Provincial Administration, Economic Promotion,
Power and Energy, Enviromental Affairs, Water Supply and
Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
at Kurunegala,
on the 12th of January 2016.
ORDER
1. This Order is cited as “Order of enforcing Budget Document for the year 2016 as Chilaw Urban Council in
Northwestern Province.”
2. In case of Budget Document relating to the year 2016 could not be passed in certain Urban Councils within the
Northwestern Province in terms of provisions stipulated in Section 178 of the Urban Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 255), it is lawful for relevant Urban Council to enforce for the year 2016 the draft Budget formulated
for 2016 under the approval of Commissioner of Local Government in Northwestern Province.
3. This Order shall be in force with effect from 01.01.2016.
