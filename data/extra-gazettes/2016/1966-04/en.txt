Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini’s Ref. No.: 4/3/4/2011/D/187.

I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a Public Purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called Imbulgaswadiyawatta in 
total Extent of 0.8133 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in 
the Preliminary Plan. Gam. 3906 prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys -Gampala, situated in the 
village called Kadirana North in Doonagaha Pattuwa 
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Katana in Gampaha District of the Western province and 
morefully described as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Imbulgaswadiyawatta ; Description: 
Home garden, six permanent buildings, two temporary 
buildings, water tank and a cement constructed well; 
Claimant: Land Reforms Commission; Extent: 0.8133 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Katana Divisional 
Secretariat on 11th July, 2016 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 04th July, 2016 the nature of their interest in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 K. G. H. H. R. KIRIELLA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Katana. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Katana, 
04th May, 2016.
