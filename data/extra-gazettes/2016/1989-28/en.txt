PUBLIC   SERVICE   COMMISSION
Rules on Procedure and Quorum for meetings of Committees appointed by the Public Service Commission
RULES made by the Public Service Commission under Article 56(3) of the Constitution, in respect of the procedure and
quorum for meetings of the Committee appointed by the Public Service Commission under Article 56 (1) of the Constitution.
1. These Rules shall be known as the “Rules for the Procedure and Quorum of Committees made by the Public
Service Commission under Article 56(3) of the Constitution.
2. The member designated by the Public Service Commission as Chairman, shall preside at all meetings.
3. In the absence of the Chairman from a meeting of the Committee, one of the other two members shall chair
such meeting.
4. The quorum for a meeting shall be two members.
5. A decision taken at any meeting by two members shall constitute a valid decision of the Committee.
6. A Committee shall, in consultation with the Public Service Commission, meet as often as is necessary to
ensure the efficient discharge of the functions delegated to the Committee.
7. The Secretary to a Committee appointed by the Public Service Commission, acting under Article    56(4) of the
Constitution, shall maintain Minutes of every meeting of the Committee which shall contain a true record of
the proceedings of such meetings.
8. The Minutes so maintained shall be accepted as a true record of the proceedings of a meeting when confirmed
as such, by the Committee.
