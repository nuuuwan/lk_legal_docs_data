SUPPLEMENTARY   PROVISION   FOR   APPROPRIATION   STATUTE   No.   05  OF  2015
A Statute to provide Supplementary Provisions to previously approved expenditure of the Northern
Provicial Council for the financial year 2015 by the Honourable Governor on the 22nd day of December
2014 in accordance to First Schedule to this statute and to make payments by way of advance out of the
Northern Provincial Council funds or monies to provide for the refund of such monies to the Northern
Provincial Council funds and to make provisions for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council  as follows :
1. This statute may be cited as the  Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statute No.
05 of 2015
2. Without prejudice to any other law which authorizes for any such expenditure, the
expenditure of the Northern Provincial Council during the financial year begining from
1st January 2015 and ending on 31st December 2015 referred to as the “Financial year
2015” in this Statute re-allocation of  Rupees Three Thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety
One Million Five Hundred and Sixty Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Ten only
(Rs. 3,791,562,910) from the expenditure the Honourable Governor approved  on the 22nd
December 2014 and to the limits stated in the First Schedule of this Statute shall be met.
