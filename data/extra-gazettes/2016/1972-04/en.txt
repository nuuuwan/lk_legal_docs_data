THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Two allotments of land called Galpadigawa Waththa in total Extent
of about 0.0123 Hectare depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/
HRS/2015/248 dated 16.07.2015, prepared by the Surveyor General
situated  in the Village of Medawela in the Minor Division of Medesiya
Pattuwa in Divisional Secretary's  Division of Harispattuwa in Kandy
District in Central Province and bound as follows:
01. Lot ‘A ’– Galpadigawa Waththa – Extent: 0.0056 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Galpadigawa Waththa claimed
by D.R.G . Somawathi; East and South:Galpadigawa Waththa claimed
by D.R.G . Somawathi; West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Galpadigawa Waththa – Extent: 0.0067 Hectare
North: Galpadigawa Waththa claimed by D.R.G . Somawathi; East:
Galpadigawa Waththa claimed by D.R.G. Somawathi and Galpadigawa
Waththa claimed by the State; South:Galpadigawa Waththa claimed
by the State; West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Galpadigawa
Waththa claimed by  P. G . S. Tilakasena.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
