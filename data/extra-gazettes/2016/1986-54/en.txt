PART IV (A) — PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
Appointments & C,. by the Governors
 LOCAL  GOVERNMENT  (ADMINISTRATIVE  AREA)  ORDINANCE  OF  265TH  CHAPTER
I, P. B. Dissanayaka as the Governor of North Central Province, in terms of the powers vested in me under Paragraph (B) of
Section 02 of Local Government (Administrative Area) Ordinance of 265th Chapter and Sub section (01) of Section 32 of
Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 that should be read with paragraph (01)(a)  of Section 02 of Provincial Council
(Incidental Provision) Act, No. 12 of 1989 Mrs. M. P. D. P . Jayasinha, Sri Lanka Administrative Service Class III is appointed
as Assistant Commissioner for said duties in Anuradhapura Administrative area with effect from 11.08.2014 until further
notice.
P. B. D
ISSANAY AKA,
Governor of the North Central Province.
Governor’s Office of North Central Province,
Anuradhapura,
25th June 2016.
