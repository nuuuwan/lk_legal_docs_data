PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2 OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Southern Province Provincial Council by a reason of Resignation ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Baraniwala Liyanage
Lal Premanath to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Baraniwala Liyanage Lal Premanath, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the
Administrative District of Matara in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
