Regulations
The United Nations Regulations No. 02 of 2012 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1760/40 of May 31,
2012, are hereby amended as follows :–
(1) by the substitution for regulation 2 thereof, of the following :-
“2. The United Nations Security Council Resolution 1267(1999) and the modifications and
strenghening of the Resolution’s sanctions regime by subsequent resolutions including 1526 (2004), 1617
(2005), 1735 (2006), 1822 (2008),  1904 (2009),  1988 (2011), 1989 (2011) and 2253 (2015) and any
other subsequent resolution, which set out wide-ranging strategies to combat terrorism and in particular
measures to be imposed against the Afghan faction known as the Taliban (which also calls itselt the Islamic
Emirate of Afghanistan), Islamic State of Iraq and Levant ( ISIL, also known as Da’esh) and Al-Qaida,
impose upon member States of the United Nations a series of obligations to apply sanction measures to
any natural or legal person, group or entity associated with Taliban, ISIL (Da’esh) or Al-Qaida as designated
