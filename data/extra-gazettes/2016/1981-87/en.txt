EXCISE  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 52)
Excise Notification No. 989
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 22 read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this declare that there shall be imposed an excise duty per
bulk liter of following imported foreign liquor as per rules set out hereto and shall come into force on 01.09.2016.
(i) Foreign Malt Liquor Rs. 50.00
(ii) Foreign Wine Rs. 100.00
(iii) Any other Foreign Liquor Rs. 200.00
(which are not included in (i) and (ii) above)
R
AV I KARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
25th August 2016.
RULES
1. A person who wishes to import liquor shall submit an application form set out in accordance with the Schedule  I
to the Commissioner General of Excise.
2. If the Commissioner General of Excise is satisfied about the application forwarded by the applicant, the
approval shall be sent to the Controller of Imports and Exports with the form set out in accordance with the Schedule II.
3. If Commissioner General of Excise is not satisfied about the application, such Commissioner shall inform the
applicant, reason for rejection within five days of the receipt of the application.
