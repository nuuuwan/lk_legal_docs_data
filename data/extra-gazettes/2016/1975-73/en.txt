PILGRIMAGES   ORDINANCE
SRI  SITHIRA  VELAYUTHA  SWAMI  TEMPLE   ANNUAL  FESTIV AL -  2016
Divisional  Secretariat  of  Thirukovil  Division  in Ampara  District
IT this hereby notified for the information of the pilgrims who attended the above festival and other concerned that the
above festival commence 16.07.2016 and terminate on 04.08.2016 with execution of duties to the manes.
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the festival and others concerned is drawn to standing regulation published
in the Government Gazette No. 10247 dated 11th May 1951 which will be in force during the duration of the above
festival.
The camp area of the above festival has been enlarged as follows :
North : By Maha Vidyalaya Road, Thambiluvil ;
East : By Fisheries Road, Thirukkovil ;
South : By Beach Road, Thirukkovil ;
West : By Main Road, Thirukkovil.
S. J AGARAJAN ,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat,
Thirukkovil.
