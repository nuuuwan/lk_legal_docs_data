THE   CONSTITUTION   OF  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Article 45(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, I, do hereby declare that the following functions of the subjects under the purview of Ministry of Education
shall be entrusted with the State Minister of Education.
A
KILA VIRAJ KARIYAW ASAM,
Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
Isurupaya,
Pelwatte, Battaramulla.
08th January 2016.
State Minister of Education
Functions and duties
1. Formulation, monitoring and evaluation of policies,
programmes and projects in relation to the
development of the schools in the estate sector
2. Formulation, monitoring and evaluation of policies,
programmes and  projects in relation to the development
of the schools in the Northern and Eastern Provinces
3. Supervision of International Schools in conformity
with the National Education Policy
4. Taking necessary steps to ensure the educational
requirements of the students with special needs
5. Adopt necessary measures to promote the
National Language and the Link Language Education
6. Supervisory activities pertaining to the Programmes
of Teacher Training Institutions which are operated in the Tamil Medium
Performance of the above functions and duties
in coordination with the Ministry of Education
and the relevant institutions
