SERVICE  MINUTE  OF  SRI  LANKA  INFORMATION   AND   COMMUNICATION   TECHNOLOGY
SERVICE
Revision No. 01
THE Interim Provisions of the Service Minute of Sri Lanka Information and Communication Technology Service, which
was published in the Gazette Extraordinary of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1894/26 dated 26.12.2014
is hereby revised by adding the following Section under 15.4
15.4   It is sufficient for the officers, who have been absorbed into Grade III of Class I or Grade II of Class 1 of
the service in the Minute of Sri Lanka Information and Communication Technology Service as at 01.07.2009 and promoted
to Grade II of Class I as at 02.07.2009 or later, to have completed a Post Graduate Diploma in an area mentioned in
Section 10.5.1.1(i) of the Minute of Sri Lanka Information and Communication Technology Service, instead of the Post
Graduate Degree qualification mentioned in the said Section, when promoting to Grade I of Class I up to 31.12.2018.
