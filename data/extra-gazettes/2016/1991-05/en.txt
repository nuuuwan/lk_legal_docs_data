No. 1991/5  –  MONDAY, OCTOBER 31, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following Land situated in the village of 
Kanaththagoda-North in the Matara Division Secretariat 
Division, Matara District, Southern Province and depicted 
as lot 162 in sheet No. 2 of Zone No. 02 in cadastre Map 
No. 820010 has been acquired to be granted as alternative 
lands to those persons who were displaced by 2003 
Flooded. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No. 162 
 
Name of Land: Puwakwaththa alias Thalgaspittaniya; 
Current land use: Home, Garden, around 10 to 20 years 
old Mixed crops and ten (10) permanent buildings; 
Claimant: Ven. Hakmana Dharmarathna Thero, Chief 
Incumbent of Kotikagoda Temple Wewahamanduwa, 
Matara; Extent: 0.3324 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 
10th November, 2016 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 1st of November, 2016 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 RANJITH YAPA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara.
