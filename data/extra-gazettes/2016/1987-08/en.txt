EXCISE  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified in the Columns
II and III of the Schedule hereto, corresponding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Headings mentioned
in the Column I of the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1941/29 dated 20.11.2015 issued under
the Section 3 of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule
hereto, with effect from 04.10.2016.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
03rd October 2016.
