 MINUTE   ON  SECRETARIES
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President is pleased to order  the amendment of the Minute on Secretaries
made as ordered by the President and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 48/2 of August 06, 1979, in the manner as
set out in the Schedule below, with effect from 16.09.2015.
                                                                                   By order of His Excellency the President.
     P. B. ABEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
08th November 2016.
Schedule
(1) by the repeal of paragraph 11 of the aforesaid Minute, and the substitution therefor of the following paragraph :-
“(11) The foregoing provisions of this Minute shall mutatis mutandis apply to the Secretary to the President,
the Secretary to the Cabinet of Ministers, the Secretary to the Prime Minister, the Chief of Staff of the
Presidential Secretariat and the Senior Additional Secretaries to the President ;
