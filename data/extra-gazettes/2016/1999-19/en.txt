PART  IV (A) — PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice  by the  Governor  of  the  Central  Province
KNOW Ye that virtue of powers vested by the Article 154(b):8(b) of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Niluka Ekanayake, Governor of the Central Province, do by this Notice
Prorogue the fourth Session of the Sixth Provincial Council of the Central Province with effect from 09th December Two
Thousand Sixteen.
By virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph 8(a) of the said Article, I, do hereby summon the said Council to
meet at the Chamber of the Central Province Provincial Council Complex at Pallekele, Kundasale on Monday the 06th
February Two Thousand Seventeen at 10.00 a. m.
N
ILUKA E KANAYAKE,
Governor of the Central Province.
Kandy,
09th December 2016.
