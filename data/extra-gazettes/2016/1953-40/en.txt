1A
 NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Hon. Governor  of   the North Central  Province
PUNCHIBANDA Dissanayaka, Governor of North Central Province, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section
32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 do hereby notify that following officers have appointed to the
respective posts.
1. To appoint Mr. R. G. A. Kularathna, Sri Lanka Administrative Service (Special Grade) as the Deputy Chief
Secretary (Administrating) of Chief Secretary office of North Central Province with effect from 30th
September 2015 until 12.01.2016.
2. To appoint Mr. R. G . Ananda Kularathna as the Secretary to the Governor of North Central Province from
13.01.2016 until an order is made in this regard.
3. To appoint Mrs. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa, Sri Lanka Administrative Service Grade 1 as Deputy Chief Secretary
(Training) to Chief Secretary office of North Central Province from 30.10.2015 until 13.01.2016.
4. To appoint Mrs. S. M. S. Tennakoon, Sri Lanka Administrative Service (Special Grade) as the Deputy Chief
Secretary (Training) of Chief Secretary office of North Central Province  from 14.01.2016 upto the Order
is made in the regard.
5. To appoint Mrs. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa, Sri Lanka Administrative Service  Grade I as Deputy Chief Secretary
(Administrating) to Chief Secretary office of North Central Province.
6. To appoint Mr. P. K. Jayalath of Sri Lanka Administrative Service Grade I as Secretary to the Minister of
Provincial Irrigation Rural Development and Women Affair’s  from 01.10.2015 until order is made in this
regard.
7. To appoint Mr. G . D. Kerthi Gamage of Sri Lanka Administrative Service Grade I as Secretary to the Minister
of Provincial Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Child Protection Service,
Environment and Provincial Council Affairs of North Central Province from 28.10.2015 until on order is
made in this regard.
