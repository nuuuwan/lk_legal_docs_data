EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  13  OF  1989
Order  under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by  this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified in the
Column II and III of the schedule hereto, corresponding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Headings
mentioned in the Column I of the Schedule of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1941/29 dated 20.11.2015
issued under the Section 3 of this Act shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the
Schedule hereto, with effect from 20th August 2016.
RAV I K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
19th August 2016.
