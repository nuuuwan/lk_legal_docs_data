No. 1999/45–  FRIDAY DECEMBER 30, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENTED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT NO. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: GAL/LAN/ACQUIS/A13/20. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/688. 
 
I  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964, 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose:-  
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land in extent about 0.0151 Hectare 
depicted in Plan No. Ke/3519 prepared by Surveyor 
General situated in the Village of Bisowela in Beligal 
Korale East Division in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division, Galigamuwa in the District of Kegalle in 
Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Bibile Kumbura Now Watta; 
Name of the Claimant: R. A. Sumanawathi, No.39, 
Bisowela, Galigamuwa Town; Extent: 0.0147 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Bibile Kumbura Now Watta; 
Name of the Claimant: R. A. Sumanawathi, No.39, 
Bisowela, Galigamuwa Town; Extent: 0.0004 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at  Divisional Secretariat, 
Galigamuwa on the day of 25th January 2017 and at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify me in writing with 
duplicate of on or  before 18th January 2017 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for acquisition of the said land the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
  K. G. S. NISHANTHA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Galigamuwa. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Galigamuwa, 
On the Date of December 28th 2016.
