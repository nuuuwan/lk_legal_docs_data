THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land shown as "Bo Gaha Yata Pela, Assmnt.
No.595/1, William Gopallawa Mawatha" in total extent of about 0.0350
Hectares, depicted in  Advance Tracing No.  Maha/MHN/2015/711
dated 19.01.2016 prepared by the Surveyor - General, situated in the
Village of  Peradeniya in Wel Ata Ward No. 1(within the Kandy
Municipal Council Limits) in Minor Division of  Gangawata Korale in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawatakorale in Kandy District in Central Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot "A"  –  Bo Gaha Yata Pela, Assmnt. No.595/1,
William Gopallawa Mawatha –  Extent: 0.0350  Hectare
North: Bo Gaha Yata Pela claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board, Road and Walawwe Watta alias Bo Gaha Yata Pela
claimed by S. K. B. Yatiwella; East: Walawwe Watta alias Bo Gaha
Yata Pela claimed by S. K. B. Yatiwella and Bo Gaha Yata Pela
claimed by Thun Bo Maluwa Temple; South: Bo Gaha Yata Pela
claimed by Thun Bo Maluwa Temple and Bo Gaha Yata Pela claimed
by Lorance Perera; West: Bo Gaha Yata Pela claimed by Thun Bo
Maluwa Temple and Bo Gaha Yata Pela claimed by Lorance Perera,
Bo Gaha Yata Pela claimed by National Water Supply and Drainage
Board.
