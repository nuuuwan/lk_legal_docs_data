All Persons whom interested  in the aforesaid lands, 
There names are in the Gazette notice or not are requested 
to submit their claims in writing, the nature  of their 
claims for compensation, on the day of 25.11.2016 to me 
at my office. 
 
Since all persons who submit their claims may call for 
an inquiry. The dates of inquiry will informed individually 
to each claimant. On the due date claimant or his/her 
agent duly authorized by the claimant in writing should 
presented the inquiry and submit their claims by deeds 
and other documents informed by me. In addition to said 
documents the amount of the compensation and details of 
the compensation should submit in two copies. If an agent 
present at the inquiry, he/she should submit a duly 
authorized letter from the original claimant without such a 
letter any statement or evidence will not be recorded at the 
inquiry.
