WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Spe cial

and

Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 25.10.2012.

( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. B. A. Indradasa, No. 114 C, Padagoda, Beruwala, of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board,  No. 200, Kirula Road, Narahenpita, Colombo 05, of the other part,
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the decrease of salary of Mr. B. A. Indradasa, who was serving as the Director operations of the Sri Lanka Transport Board from
Rs. 12,000/- to Rs. 8,690/- in removing him from his office on 29/06/2004 is just, and if not so, to what relief he is entitled for from 29.06.2004
to 09.12.2006, his date of retirement.
M. D. C. A
MARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 2nd day of April, 2016.
