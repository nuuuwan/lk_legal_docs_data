EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini’s Ref. No.: LD/5/2001/VEP/226. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Five allotments of land in total extent of 2.5000 
Hectares depicted as Lot No. 01, 02, 03, 04 and 05 in the 
Preliminary Plan No. Ka. 4513 dated 30.06.2015 prepared 
by the Superintendent of Surveys - Kalutara situated in 
the villages called Keeranthidiya and Kolahekada in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mathugama in 
Kaluthara District of the Western Province and more fully 
described as follows: 
 
Schedule 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Pallagodawatta; Description: 
Home Garden, three permanent buildings, part of a 
permanent building, Mix cultivation and forest of 
Godapara; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Namunu -
kula Plantation Company; Extent: 2.4427 Hectares. 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the Land: Wallabadakubura; Description: 
Home Garden, part of a permanent building, Mix 
cultivation; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: D. M. 
Wimalasena and Some others; Extent: 0.0151 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
 
Name of the Land: Ramiyawala; Description: Garden, 
Mix cultivation; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:            
D. M. Wimalasena and Some others; Extent: 0.0144 
Hectare. 
 
04. Lot No. 04 
 
Name of the Land: Walmiya owita; Description: 
Garden, Mix cultivation; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: D. M. Wimalasena and Some others; Extent: 
0.0181 Hectare. 
 
05. Lot No. 05 
 
Name of the Land: Walmiya owita; Description: 
Garden, Mix cultivation; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: — ; Extent: 0.0097 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Mathugama 
Divisional Secretariat on 30.08.2016 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 23.08.2016 the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
