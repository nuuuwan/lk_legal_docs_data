PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT, No.  2  OF  1988
Filling  of   a  vacancy   in  the Western  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Western  Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Western Province Provincial Council by reason of Death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's,  Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party to
which the member belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to
nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Sumith Vijaya Muni De
Soyza  to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Sumith Vijaya Muni De Soyza , elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the
Administrative District of Colombo  in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
