REGULATION   OF  INSURANCE   INDUSTRY   ACT,  No.  43 OF  2000
RULES made by the Insurance Board of Sri Lanka under Section 105 read with sub paragraph(a) of subsection (1) of
Section 80 of the Regulation of Insurance Industry Act, No. 43 of 2000.
Chairperson,
Insurance Board of Sri Lanka.
Colombo,
25th  May 2016.
Rules
The Insurance Board of Sri Lanka Rules of 2005 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1412/30 of September
29, 2005, are hereby amended by the substitution for Rule 5 thereof of the following :-
“5. Any person who wishes to be registered as a broker under this Act shall be required to have and maintain a paid
up share capital of the company, of a sum not less than rupees two million five hundred thousand.”
