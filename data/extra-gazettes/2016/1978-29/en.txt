1A
NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Local Authority Notifications
IT is hereby notify that the Provincial Council of North Western Province has been approved by laws of “Solid Waste
Management” of Municipal Councils at the Provincial Council Meeting held on 15.03.2016 that notified by the Extraordinary
Gazette No. 1933/40 dated 25th September 2015 in part IV(A) of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka made by
the Minister of Local Government of the North Western Province under powers vested in the sub schedule (1) of the
statement (2) in the Local Authorities Statute No. 6 of 1952 under Authority 261, read with the paragraph (a) of Schedule
(1) of the statement 2 in the Provincial Council Statute No. 12 of 1989.
P. B. M. S
IRISENA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Local Government,
North Western Province Provincial Council.
Kurunegala,
07th July 2016.
