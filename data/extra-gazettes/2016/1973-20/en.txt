No. 1973/20 – TUESDAY, JUNE 28, 2016
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land in
total extent of about 0.0195 Hectare, Shown as Lot A in Advance
Tracing No. GA/GFG/2015/449 dated 27th August, 2015, prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Kumbalwella in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Four Gravets, Galle in the
District of Galle, Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land - Extent:
about 0.0195 Hectare
North: Galgodahena Poyagalakanda alias Sri Dharmaraja Maha
Vihara land claimed by A. Gunathilaka; East: Galgodahena
Poyagalakanda alias Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land claimed by
A. Gunathilaka and Sri Dharmaraja Mawatha; South: Sri Dharmaraja
Mawatha, Galgodahena Poyagalakanda alias Sri Dharmaraja Maha
Vihara land claimed by A. Gunathilaka and Galgodahena
Poyagalakanda alias Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land claimed by Sri
Dharmaraja Maha Viharaya; West: Galgodahena Poyagalakanda alias
Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land claimed by A. Gunathilaka and
Galgodahena Poyagalakanda alias Sri Dharmaraja Maha Vihara land
claimed by Sri Dharmaraja Maha Viharaya.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
