STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14 OF  2008
Order  under Sub Section  (4) of  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub Section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of
2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister
in Charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub Section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11th July 2011, do by this Order declare that,
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11th July 2011, is hereby
amended to read  as follows is order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct, a luxury hotel as Mixed Use Complex Including High Quality Residencies
and a High - End Shopping Mall, in Fort, Colombo 01, in the Western Province’’ Shall commence commercial operation
on 30.06.2019.’’
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
24th June, 2016.
