FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
MATARA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Matara Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to
which the former member, Hadunge Sosindra Nandana belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member
of that Matara Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Usman Mohomad
Mohomad Safar being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Wewalwala Hewage Ravinda Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Matara Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Usman Mohomad
Mohomad Safar elected as a member of the Matara Municipal Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Matara,
13th  January, 2016.
