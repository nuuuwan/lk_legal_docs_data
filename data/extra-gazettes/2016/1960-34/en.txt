THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuwel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Enasalmada Watte in Extent of 1.6387
Hectares as Lot  A in the  Advance Tracing No. MA/UKW/2013/406
dated 06.02.2014, prepared by Senior Superintendent of Surveys,
Matale and situated at Enasalmada Village in Enasalmada Grama
Niladhari Division in Ukuwela Divisional Secretary’s Division in
Matale District in Central Province:
01. Lot A – Enasalmada Watte – Extent: 1.6387 Hectares
North: Mala Ela and Road; East: Road ; South and West: Remaining
portion of same land.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2013/VE/406.
Ministry of Lands,

Battaramulla.
