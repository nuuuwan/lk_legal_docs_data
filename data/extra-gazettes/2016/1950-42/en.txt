CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES LAW, No. 5 OF 1972 READ WITH THE CO- OPERATIVE
SOCIETIES (AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 32 OF 1983 AND THE CO-OPERATIVE
 SOCIETIES (AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 11 OF 1992
Conferment of Powers - Order
IN pursuance of  the powers vested in me under the Section 2 (2) of the Co- operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read
with  Co- operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the  Co - operative Societies (Amendment) Act,
No. 11 of 1992, I, Rishad Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce do hereby confer on the persons appointed to
assist the Commissioner of Co- operative Development and the Registrar of  Co- operative Societies and  specified in the
Column 1 of  the Schedule hereto, from the date specified in Column II under Co- operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972
read with  Co- operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the  Co- operative Societies (Amendment) Act,
No. 11 of  1992 and under the rules made there under, the powers specified in column III of the said schedule.
R
ISHAD B A THIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Colombo ,
30th December, 2015.
