THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,
hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land , described in the
following paragraph 1 is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in paragraph
2 from which land that the servitude will be acquired.

land, for laying of Distribution Lines, from Pallegattuwana High Lift
