THE   UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 896/2
of November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1903/63 of February 27,
2015) which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, by the substitution
for the item “Department of Cartography, Photogrammetry, Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System” appearing
in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Geomatics” appearing in Column I of the Schedule
of the following item :-
“Department of Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System”
LAKSHMAN  K IRIELLA ,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
16th February 2016.
