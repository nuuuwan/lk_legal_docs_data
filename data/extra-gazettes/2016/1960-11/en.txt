UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.   16  OF 1978
Order under Section 20(4)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 20(4) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Eastern University,
Sri Lanka have ben restored to normal conditions, do by this Order, revoke with effect from 21, January, 2016, the Order
made by me under the aforesaid Act, and published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 1947/25 of 30th
December, 2015 appointing Professor Uma Coomaraswamy as the Competent Authority in lieu of the Vice-Chancellor
of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th March, 2016.
