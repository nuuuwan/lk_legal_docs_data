THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of  Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land Called Talahena in total extent of about
0.0379 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/AKR/2015/
509 dated 17.11.2015, prepared by the Surveyor - General, situated
in the Village of Dippitiya in Udagampaha Korale Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Akurana in Kandy District
in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A '–  Talahena – Extent: 0.0379 Hectare

D. G . Piyasena; West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha).
