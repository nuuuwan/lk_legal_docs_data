THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.   16  OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Educations and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order establishing the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the
provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1885/2 of October 20, 2014) by addition immediately after
item 5 appearing in Column II under the heading “Department” as corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Allied Health
Sciences” appearing in Column I under the heading “Faculty” of the Schedule to such deemed Order, of the following new
item :-
“6. Department of Basic Sciences.”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th March, 2016.
