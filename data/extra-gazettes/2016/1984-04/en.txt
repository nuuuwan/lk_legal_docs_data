A  STATUTE TO  AMEND  THE  APPROPRIATION  STATUTE  No.  04  OF  2015
Appropriation Statute
2016
For the year 2016
A Statute to amend the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of the 2015, of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
1. This Statute may be cited as the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Appropriation (Amendment) Statute No. 02
of the 2016, and shall come into operation of the date of receiving the assent of the Governor of the
Sabaragamuwa Province.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Twenty Six Thousand and
Fourteen Million and Four Hundred and Ninety Eight Thousand Only (Rs. 26,014,498,000.00) for the service
of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2016 and ending 31st of December, 2016 in this Statute referred to as
the “Financial Year 2016” , the first Schedule is hereby amended as the expenditure of the Sabaragramuwa
Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Twenty Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty Million and Two
Hundred and Nineteen Thousand Five Hundred and Seventy One and Cents Thirty only (Rs. 29,650,219,571.30)
for the service of the period beginning on 01st January 2016 and ending 31st of December 2016 in this statute
referred to as the “Financial Year 2016” as the First Schedule hereof.
3. The limits  mentioned in the Second Schedule of the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of 2015 relevant to the limits
of the Advanced Accounts in hereby amended as per the second schedule hereof.
