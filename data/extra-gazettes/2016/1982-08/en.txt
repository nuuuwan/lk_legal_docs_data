HOMOEOPATHY  ACT
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Homoeopathy Act, No. 10 of 2016. I, Dr. Rajitha Senarathne, Minister
of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine, do by this order, appoint the 22nd of August 2016 as the date on which the
aforesaid Act, shall come into operation.
D
R. RAJITHA SENARATHNE,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine,
Colombo,
18th August 2016.
