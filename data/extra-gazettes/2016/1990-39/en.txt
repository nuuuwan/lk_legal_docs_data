No. 1990/39  –  FRIDAY, OCTOBER 28, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My Reference No.: මහ/ඉ/අƮ/UDA/325. 
  
IT is hereby notified under Sub -section (1) of Section 07 
in Land Acquisition Act amended by Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the government 
intends to acquire the following land which is required for 
a public affair. 
 
Details of the land intended to be acquired: 
 
The land described below, situated in Polwatte Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 529 of Maharagama Divisional 
Secretariat area of authority, Colombo district of Western 
Province with an extent of around 0.2941 Hectares and 
shown in main plan No. Co 9636, prepared by the 
Surveyor General. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Atambagahawattha or Kurundugaha -
wattha Old Polwattha; Land Utilization: Home Garden; 
Entitled Persons: Cannot be traced; Extent: 0.2857 
Hectares. 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of Land: Atambagahawattha; Land Utilization: 
Home Garden; Entitled Persons: The State; Extent: 
0.0084 Hectares. 
 
All the persons claiming entitlement, rights or other 
affiliations to the above land shall stand before me in 
person or through their representatives empowered by 
documents issued by them, at Maharagama Divisional 
Secretariat on 16th November, 2016 at 9.00 a.m. 
 
Furthermore, I should be informed by way of a 
document in two copies as to the nature of their 
entitlement regarding the said land, details of matters 
enabling them to request compensation on the acquiring 
of the said land, compensation amount and how it was 
calculated, before 09th day of November, 2016. Deeds 
required for confirming entitlement and their extracts and 
other documents should be presented to me at the inquiry. 
 
 BUDDHI THARANGA KARUNASENA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Maharagama. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Maharagama, 
25th October, 2016.
