EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)

Notice under Section 7  
My Reference No.: KDS/KGK/4/2/2011/02/26. 
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands: 4-3/9/2012/
WS/108. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a Public 
purpose. 
 
Description of  the land  to be acquired : 
 
Two allotments of land called “Wemulla Watta” in 
total extent of about 0.0158 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 
222 and 223 in Cadastral Map No. 320390, Block No. 2 
of Block No. 4, Sheet No. 5 dated on 04.05.2016, 
surveyed and  prepared by the Surveyor General, situated 
at the village of Walagammedda in the Divisional  
Secretary’s Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in 
Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows:- 
 
01. Lot No. 222 
Name of the Land: Wemulla Watta; Extent of the 
Land: 0.0030 Hectare. 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 208 and Lot No. 212;  East: Lot                 
No. 223 and Lot No. 224; South: Lot No. 224; West: Lot 
No. 224 and Lot No. 210. 
Names and Address of the Claimants: 1. Mr. S. 
Dabare, No. 339/6. Wemulla Watta, Ampitiya.  
02. Lot No. 223 
Name of the Land: Wemulla Watta; Extent of the 
Land: 0.0005 Hectare. 
 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 212; East: Lot No. 224; South: Lot 
No. 224; West: Lot No. 222 and Lot No. 208. 
 
Names and Address of the Claimants: 1. Mr. C. K. D. 
Vitanage, No. 339/4, Wemulla Watta, Ampitiya. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are  hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at  Divisional Secretary’s Office, at 
Kandy and Gangawata Korale on the 30th day of 2016 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of August 
2016,  the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
              B. M. P. G. P. V. BANDARA, 
              Divisional Secretary, 
            Kandy and Gangawata Korale. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kandy and Gangawata Korale Division, 
21st June, 2016.
