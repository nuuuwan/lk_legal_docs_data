THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of below name lands depicted in Advance Tracing
No. Maha/MHN/2016/74 and dated 16/06/2016, prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in village of Dodanwala in the Minor
Division of Gangawatakorale in Divisional Secretary's Division of
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawatakorale in the Kandy District in
the Central Province bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Halawewatta alias Hallemulle Watta – Extent:
0.0027 Hectare
North: Halawewatta alias  Hallemulle Watta claimed by
Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha Peiris; East: Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha); South: Lot B; West: Lot B and Halawewatta alias Hallemulle
Watta claimed by Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha Peiris.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Halawewatta alias Hallemulle Watta – Extent:
0.0285 Hectare
North: Halawewatta  alias  Hallemulle Watta claimed by
Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha Peiris and Lot A; East: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); South: Lot C; West: Lot D.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Nagasthenna Watta alias Diggale Watta – Extent:
0.0334 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); South:
Nagasthennawatta alias Diggalewatta claimed by Habaragamuralalage
Nimal Nishantha Peiris; West: Lot D.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Nagasthenna Watta alias Diggale Watta – Extent:
0.0187 Hectare
North: Halawewatta alias  Hallemulle Watta claimed by
Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha Peiris; East: Halawewatta alias
Hallemulle Watta claimed by Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha
Peiris, Lots B and C; South: Nagasthennawatta alias Diggalewatta
claimed by Habaragamuralalage Nimal Nishantha Peiris; West:
Mahaweli River.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
