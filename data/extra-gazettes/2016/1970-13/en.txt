EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land min. Reference No.: LD/05/2005/E/151. 
 
My Reference No.: DSWA/LND/4/ACQUI/F1/24. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following              
land/servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the land  or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The Land called in Final Village Plan No.51(FVP 
No.51) Situated at Saputhanthrikanda Grama Niladhari 
division in Walasmulla Divisional Secretarial area in 
Hambantota District in Southern province. 
 
Scheduled on follows:- 
 
Lot No. 266  
 
Name of the Land: Purana Kumbura; Land use: 
Garden, Two permanent buildings, Three permanent 
toilets, One permanent Shrine; Owner: K. S. Advin,                 
H. A. Nikulas, W. A. Karunasena, K. Charliss,                   
H. Piyasena, H. P. Gunapala, P. P. Gunapala; Extent: 
0.1267 Hec. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitudes is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office 
Walasmulla on the day of 21st July 2016, at 10.30 
a.m.and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 24th day of June, 2016 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude*, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
              N. K. R. PATHIRANA, 
              Divisional Secretary, 
            Walasmulla. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Walasmulla,  
22nd April, 2016.
