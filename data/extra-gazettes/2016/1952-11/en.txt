NORTH  WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
1A
Order under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Ordinance to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Consequentional Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Dharmasiri Dasanayake, The Chief Minister of North Western Province and Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering
Services, Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Economic, Promotion, Power and Energy,
Enviornmental Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs do hereby notify that following order in terms of
Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of year 1987 which reads with the Section 2 of the Consequentional
Provisions of Provincial Councils Act, No. 12 of year 1989.
D
HARMASIRI  D ASSANAY AKE,
The Chief Minister of North Western Province and
Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Economic, Promotion, Power and Energy, Enviornmental Affairs,
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
At Kurunegala,
on the 29th of January 2016.
ORDER
1. This Order is cited as “Order of enforcing Budget Document for the year 2016 as Pradeshiya Sabhas' in North
Western Province”
2. In case of Budget Document relating to the year 2016 could not be passed in certain Pradeshiya Sabhas within
the North Western Province in terms of provisions stipulated in Section 168 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act it is
lawful for relevant to Pradeshiya Sabhas to enforce for the year 2016 the draft Budget formulated for 2016
under the approval of Commissioner of Local Government in North Western Province.
3. This Order shall be in force with effect from 01.01.2016.
