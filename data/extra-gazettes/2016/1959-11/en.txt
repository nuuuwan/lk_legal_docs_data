FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER  OF  THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  a  Member in Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the  Recognized Political Party namely Democratic Peoples Front to which the former
member Aananthan Murugaiah Thirubagara Kumar belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Colombo Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Lowrance Fernando being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Colombo Municipal Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Lowrance Fernando elected as a member of the
Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
23rd of March, 2016.
