THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of  Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land  in total extent of about 6.4070 Hectares depicted
as Lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No. Ki/KND/2015/137 dated 19.08.2015
prepared by the Surveyor General  situated in the Village of
Korakkankaddu, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kandawalai
in Kilinochchi District, in Nothern Province and bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Korakkankaddukadu  –  Extent: 6.4070
Hectares
North: Korakkankaddukadu garden claimant not known; East:
Stream; South: Korakkankaddukadu garden claimant not known; West:
Korakkankaddukadu paddy (abandoned) claimant not known.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
My No. : 4-3/15/2015/VE/79.
Ministry of Lands,
''Mihikatha Medura'',
1200/6, Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
