I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as Land Acquisition 
(Amended) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose :- 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
The Allotment of known as POLKORATUWA and 
shown as lot 1068 sheet number 11 and supplement 
number 11 (01 block) of land in total extend  of about 
0.1075 hectares F.V.P. 936 situated Pahala Pothupitiya 
village and Pothupitiya North Grama Niladhari Division  
in Uda Pattu of Kukulu Korale  in Kalawana Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Ratnapura District Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
 
1. F.V.P. 936 Lot No. 1068 Sheet No. 11 and 
supplement No. 11  
Name of Land: Polkoratuwa; Claimant Name and 
Address: H. M. Susanth Herath, Vidyala Mawatha, 
Pothupitiya; Extent: 0.1075 Hectare. 
 
With public purpose : —  “Land Acquisition For the 
established Police burrier in Pothupititya Polkoratuwa in 
all person interested in below aforesaid lands on which is 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agent dully authorized with document in 
writing meet me at 30.11.2016 at 10.00 a.m. Kalawana 
divisional secretariat. And are hereby required to notify to 
me in written (with 2 duplicates) on or before me the 
23.11.2016 and nature interests in the land the particular 
of their claims for compensation for Acquisition of said  
lands servitude the amounts of compensation and details 
of the calculation of such amount.
