No. 1985/21 – THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 21, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Four (04) allotments of lands in total extent of about 0.0374 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Co/DSO/2015/806 on  dated
12.01.2016,  prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Gangodawila, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte in Colombo District  in Western Province
and bounded as follows:
1. Lot No. 01 –  Part of No. 1/A, Old Kesbewa Road - Extent:
0.0071 Hectare
North: Old Kesbewa Road and Highlevel Road; East: Highlevel
Road and land bearing No. 1/A, Old Kesbewa Road; South: Land
bearing No. 1/A at Old Kesbewa Road, Sri Bodhimalu Viharaya and
Lot No. 2; West: Lot No. 2 and Old Kesbewa Road.
2. Lot No. 02 –  Name of the land could not be traced - Extent:
0.0177 Hectare
North: Old Kesbewa Road and Lot No. 1; East: Lot No. 1, land
bearing No. 1/A, Old Kesbewa Road and Sri Bodhimalu Viharaya;
South: Sri Bodhimalu Viharaya at  Old Kesbewa Road, land at Old
Kesbewa Road of which the assesment number and the claimant could
not be traced and Lot No. 3; West: Lot No. 3, Cement Drain and Old
Kesbewa Road.
3. Lot No. 03 –  Part of No. 526, Old Kesbewa Road - Extent:
0.0069 Hectare
North: Cement Drain and Lot No. 2; East: Lot No. 2, Sri Bodhimalu
Viharaya at Old Kesbewa Road and land bearing No. 526; South:
Land bearing No. 526,  Old Kesbewa Road and at Lot No. 4; West:
Lot No. 4 and Cement Drain.
4. Lot No. 04 –  Name of the land could not be traced - Extent:
0.0057 Hectare
North: Cement Drain and Lot No. 3; East: Lot No. 3, land bearing
No. 526, Old Kesbewa Road and land of which the assesment number
and the claimant could not be traced; South: Land at Old Kesbewa
Road of which the assesment number and the claimant could not be
traced; West: Cement Drain.
