02. Lot No. 287 - Extent 9.1988 Hectare.

64B, 176, 169 and 177; South: Lots 177, 169, 164, 163, 178 2/2,
64  and 175;W est: Lots 169, 164, 163, 178 2/2, 64, 175, 178 2/2
