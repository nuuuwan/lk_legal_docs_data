 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the S L T CAMPUS (PVT) LTD, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and
bearing registration number PV 101137, do by this Order recognize the S L T CAMPUS (PVT) LTD as a Degree Awarding
Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degrees specified in Column
I of the Schedule II hereto of which the abbreviations are set out in the respective corresponding entries in Column II of
the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in the Schedule I hereto.
SCHEDULE   I
CONDITIONS
S L T CAMPUS (PVT) LTD shall adhere to the minimum entry qualification of 3 ‘S’ passes in the Physical Science stream
in one and the same sitting at GCE Advanced Level Examination or equivalent, as per UGC guidelines when admitting
students for the following Degree Programmes.
SCHEDULE   II
Column I Column II
Degrees Abbreviations to the Degrees
Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Electronics B Sc Hons. Eng. (Electronics and Power Systems)
and Power Systems
Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Electronics B Sc Hons. Eng. (Electronics and Telecommunication)
and Telecommunications
Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Electronics B Sc Hons. Eng. (Electronics and  Engineering
and Engineering Management Management)
LAKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
