STAMP   DUTY  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  12  OF  2006
Order under Section  3
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 2006, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Order made under that Section and published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1465/19 dated October 05, 2006 as last amended by the Order published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1933/14 dated September 21,2015, as specified in the SCHEDULE hereto.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
31st December 2015.
SCHEDULE
The item 05 of the SCHEDULE published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1465/19 (as amended) is hereby further
amended by the substitution therefor, of the following item 05:-
“05. Any claim, demand, or request presented for the payment of any
sum of money, not including any finance or service  charge,
due in respect of every credit card transaction entered into during any
period commencing on or after January 1, 2016,  where the merchant
country (the country in which the purchase of goods or service
or the withdrawals of money taken place) thereof is a country
other than Sri Lanka, irrespective of whether it was a card present or
not present transaction in any currency
for every Rs. 1000 or part of thereof of such sum of money”. Rs. 25.00”
