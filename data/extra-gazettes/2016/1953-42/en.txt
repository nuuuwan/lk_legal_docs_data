Notification made by Chief Minister of the Central Province Provincial Council
VESTING   OF   POWERS   UNDER   CO-OPERATIVE   SOCIETIES   STATUTE   No. 10   OF   THE   CENTRAL
PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL   AS   AMENDED   BY  CO-OPERATIVE   SOCIETIES   (AMENDMENT)
STATUTE   No. 04   OF   THE   1993   OF   THE   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
I, Sarath Ekanayake, Chief Minister of Central Province Provincial Council, Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and
Peace, Local Government, Local Administration, Manpower, Education, Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Land, Food Supply and

proclaim that in accordance with powers vested in me under sub clauses 2 and 4 of clauses of the Co-operative Societies
No. 10 of the Central Province Provincial Council as amended by Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Statute No. 04 of
the 1993 of the Central Province Provincial Council empower the officers name under Column I below as Assistant
Commissioner of Co-operative Development (SLAS) in Central Province Provincial Council denoting the duties
empowered on them under Column III to be effective from date under Column II respectively to function as follows.
Column I Column II Column III
Mrs. H. K. M. D. K. Kavisekara 23.11.2015
