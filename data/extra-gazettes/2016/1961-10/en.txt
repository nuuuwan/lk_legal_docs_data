No. 1961/10 – TUESDAY, APRIL 05, 2016
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Developement, here by declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Fourteen (14) allotments of  land, containing in extent of 13.5991
Hectares, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ma/Glw/2009/1/223
dated 21.10.2011 of the Survey General situated at Pottawa Village
in Udugoda Pallesiy Pattuwa Minor Division of Galewela Divisional
Secretary's Division in the District of Matale, Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘‘A’’ –  Ehalagollayaya – Extent: 0.5515 Hectare
North: Wemedilla reservation; East: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by
W. D. Piyadasa and Lot B; South: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by I. G .
Jayaneris;West: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by I. G . Jayaneris and I. G .
Jemis.
02. Lot ‘‘B’’ –  Ehalagollayaya – Extent: 0.2691 Hectare
North: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by W. D. Piyadasa; East: Lot C;
South: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by I. G . Jayaneris;West:
Ehalagolleyaya claimed by I. G . Jayaneris and Lot A.
03. Lot ‘‘C’’ –  Ehalagollayaya – Extent: 0.5607 Hectare
North: Ehalagolleyaya claimed by W. D. Piyadasa and Lot B;
East: Pottawa Forest; South: Pottawa  Forest and Ehalagolleyaya
claimed by I. G . Jayaneris;West: Lot B.
04. Lot ‘‘D’’ –  Katayatawatta – Extent: 0.8493 Hectare
North: Kottuwahena Watta and A. G. Piyasena; East: Pottawa
Forest; South: Lot E;West: Pottawa River.
05. Lot ‘‘E’’ –  Katayatawatta – Extent: 1.8474 Hectares
North: Lot D; East: Pottawa Forest; South: Lot G;West: Pottawa
River.
06. Lot ‘‘F’’ –  Galkaruwahena – Extent: 0.3186 Hectare
North: Galkaruwahena claimed by I. G . Saman Kerthi; East:
Pottawa River; South: Naran Gahalanda Ela;West: Galkaruwahena
claimed by I. G . Anulawathi.
07. Lot ‘‘G’’ –  Katayatawatta – Extent: 2.2137 Hectares
North: Lot E; East: Pottawa Forest; South: Lot K;West: Pottawa
River.
08. Lot ‘‘H’’ –  Narangahalanda – Extent: 0.3038 Hectare
North: Narangahalanda Ela; East: Pottawa River; South: Lot J;West:
Galkaruwahena claimed by I. G . Karunarathne.
09. Lot ‘‘J’’ –  Pelpanguwa – Extent: 0.0843 Hectare
