6.2.3.3 v
Five percent (5%) out of the total number of recruitments will be for males. However the percentage of recruitment
of males may differ from time to time on exigency of service subjects to the maximum of 5%. This provision is not
applicable for the BSc (Nursing) graduates enrolled for the orientation and co-ordination course.
6.2.4.3 Recruitment on the basis fo BSc (Nursing) Degree
Nursing graduates who successfully complete the orientation and co-ordination course, are recruited for the
vacancies exist in the post of Staff Nursing Officer in Grade III of Sri Lanka Nursing Service in the order of a merit list
prepared as follows considering the effective date of the degree and G. P . A.
A. When granting appointments for a group of the same university, the merit list is prepared based on the G . P. A.
given by the university.
B. When granting appointments for two groups of the same university or for these who have graduated from
different universities, the merit list is prepared based on the effective date of the degree.
