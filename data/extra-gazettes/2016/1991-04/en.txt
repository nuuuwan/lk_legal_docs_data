CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES  LAW  No.  5 OF  1972  READ  WITH  THE  CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,   No.  32  OF  1983  AND  THE  CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES  (AMENDMENT)

Conferment of Powers - Order
IN pursuance of the powers vested in me under the Section 2(2) of the Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read
with Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11
of 1992, I, Rishad Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce do hereby confer on the persons appointed to assist
the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies and specified in the Column
I of the Schedule hereto, from the date specified in Column II under Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read with
Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of
1992 and under the rules made thereunder, the powers specified in Column III of the said Schedule.
R
ISHAD B ATHIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Colombo,
07th September 2016.
