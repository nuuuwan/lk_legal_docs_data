PROCLAMATION   BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
WHEREAS, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Repulic of Sri Lanka being of the opinion that by
reason of the existence of a state of disaster in such provisions of Sri Lanka as are specified in the Schedule hereto, it is
expedient so to do in the interests of Public Security, the preservation of Public Order and the maintenance of supplies and
services essential to the life of the community, do, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the Sri Lanka
Disaster Management Act, No. 13 of  2005, by this Proclamation declare that the provisions of the aforesaid Act, shall
come into operation in such Provinces of Sri Lanka as are specified in the Schedule hereto, on the 30th day of May, Two
Thousand and Sixteen.
Given at Colombo, on the 30th day of May, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
                                  By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
Schedule
1. Western Province
2. North Western Province
3. Sabaragamuwa Province
4. North Central Province
5. Central Province
6. Uva Province.
