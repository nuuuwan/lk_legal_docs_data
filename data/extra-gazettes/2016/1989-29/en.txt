PUBLIC   SERVICE   COMMISSION
Delegation of Powers by the Public Service Commission in terms of Article 57 (1) of the Constitution
01. The Public Service Commission in terms of the provisions in Article 57(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka hereby delegates to the Public Officers stated in Column II of the Schedule
“A” hereto, the powers set out in Column I thereof.
02. However, where there exists an uncertainty concerning the scope of any delegation of powers made by the
Public Service Commission or the discharge of any delegation of powers so made by the said Commission, the decision
of the Public Service Commission shall be final and conclusive.
03. Moreover, in the event that a Public Officer referred to in Column II of the Schedule hereto, is either the
complainant or a witness or a potential witness at any disciplinary inquiry in respect of an officer belonging to a category
set out in the corresponding item in Column I of the Schedule hereto, the powers delegated in respect of such officer
shall be deemed to have been rescinded and the Public Service Commission shall exercise the powers so delegated in
respect of such officer, as if no such delegation has been made in respect of such officer.
04. The provisions relating to the subject of Education concerning the delegation of powers set out in Part I of
the Schedule published in Gazette Extraordinary No.1941/41 dated 20.11.2015 as amended by Gazette Extraordinary
No.1955/22 dated 25.02.2016 that delegated powers in terms of Article 57(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka are hereby rescinded, without prejudice to any action taken or purported to be taken under
the Delegation of Powers published in the aforesaid Gazette.

Delegation of Powers by the Public Service Commission in terms of Article 56 (1) of the Constitution
01. The Public Service Commission in terms of the provisions in Article 56 (1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka hereby delegates to the Education Service Committee appointed by the
Public Service Commission as stated in the Schedule “B” hereto, the powers set out in Column II in respect of the
category of officers stated in column I thereof.
02. However, where there exists an uncertainty concerning the scope of any delegation of powers made by the
Public Service Commission or the discharge of any delegation of powers so made by the said Commission, the decision
of the Public Service Commission shall be final and conclusive.
03. All matters pertaining to the powers delegated to the Education Service Committee that are pedning
before the Public Service Commission or the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister in charge of the subject of
Education on the date prior to the effective date of this delegation of powers shall, from the effective date of this
delegation of powers, be vested in the Education Service Committee.
