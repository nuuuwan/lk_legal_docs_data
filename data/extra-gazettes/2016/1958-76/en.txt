2A
Greetings :
WHEREAS the National Salaries and Cadre Commission was established by the Presidential Directive dated March
03, 2016 in the exercise of powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
AND WHEREAS representation of human resources in diverse fields is identified as a vital contributory factor in the
effective outcome of the Commission,
AND  WHEREAS it is apparent that the diversity of membership composition of the Commission needs to be further
widened,
Now, therefore, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka reposing great
trust and confidence in the prudence, ability and  fidelity of Suranga Naullage Esquire do by these presents appoint the said
Suranga Naullage Esquire as a member of the National Salaries and Cadre Commission in terms of the aforesaid Presidential
Directive.
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this 18 th day of March, in the
year Two Thousand and Sixteen.
                                                              By Order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
