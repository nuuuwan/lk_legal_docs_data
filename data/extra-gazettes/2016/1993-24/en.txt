No. 1993/24–  THURSDAY, NOVEMBER,  17, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference  No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/UDA/01. 
Land  Ministry’s Reference  No.: 4-3/10/2014/UDA/23. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government  intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
Public Purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
  
 Following allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 592 
& 593 in extent 13.4764 Hectare in the Preliminary Plan 
No. 342 prepared by Survey General situated in Hidellana 
Village of New Town Grama Niladari Division of 
Divisional Secretary’s Division Rathnapura in the District 
of Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province.  
  
 01. Lot No. 592  
 Name of the Land: Eriyagahadeniye Kumbura, 
Kanuketiye Kumbura, Kanuketiyegodella, Palm Garden 
Estate Details of the Land, Barren Paddy Field; Claimant: 
Government, incipient claimant Balangoda Plantation 
Ltd. P. B. 06, Palmgarden, Ratnapura, Land Reformation 
Commission, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Colombo 
07; Extent: 2.1466 Hectares. 
 
01. Lot No. 593  
 Name of the land: Palm Garden Estate, 
Eriyagahadeniye Kumbura, Kanuketiye Kumbura, Details 
of the  land: Estate, There is 8 -10 years old Rubber 
cultivation; Claimant: Government, incipient claimant 
Balangoda Plantation Ltd. P. B. 06, Palmgarden, 
Ratnapura, Land Reformation Commission, Hector 
Kobbedaduwa Mawatha, Colombo 07; Extent: 11.3298 
Hectares.  
  
All Persons interested  in the aforesaid lands on which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby requested to appear 
personally or by agent duly authorized in writing, before 
me at 10.00 a. m. on 28th December 2016, at Rathnapura 
Divisional Secretariat. 
 
Moreover, they are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing with two duplicates on or before the 21st day of 
December 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their clams for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.  
 
  
 C. M.  KOTTAWATHTHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rathnapura. 
 
At  Divisional Secretariat-Rathnapura 
10th day of November,  2016.
