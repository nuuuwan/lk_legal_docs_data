PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
Statutory Determination No. : 4639
Unique No. : Co/1807
Co/1808
STATUTORY   DETERMINATION   UNDER  SECTION   19  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW  No.  01  OF  1972
WHEREAS Late Dr. Gamini Corea of No. 21, Norton Lodge, Horton Place, Colombo 07 had made a Statutory Declaration
under Section 18 of the Law, by virtue of the powers vested in it under Section 19 of the law, a Statutory Determination
had been made by the Commission under Statutory Determination No. 3244 published in Government Gazette Extraordinary
No. 291/18 dated 16.11.1977 specifying the portion of agricultural land which the statutory lessee should be allowed to
retain.
Moreover late Mrs. Alis Frida Corea who was residing at the same address and the statutory declarant  of Co/
1808 and the late mother of late Dr. Gamini Corea, the statutory declarant  of Co/1807, had also made a Statutory Declaration
and accordingly Statutory Determination had been made under Statutory Determination No. 3533 published in Government
Gazette Extraordinary No. 21/4 dated 29.01.1979 and Mrs. Alis Frida Corea, the statutory declarant  of Co/1808 demised
on or about 19.10.1978, late Dr. Gamini Corea became as owner of land in excess of the ceiling as the sole legal heir of
Mrs. Alis Frida Corea.
Therefore, by virtue of the Section 05 of the land Reform Law No. 01 of 1972, as amended by Land Reform
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 and Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 14 of 1986, the extent of
agricultural lands owned by late Dr. Gamini Corea in excess of the ceiling is vested in the Land Reform Commission.
As such new Statutory Determination specifying the portion of land due to the estate of said late Dr. Gamini
Corea the declarant in Co/1807 is allowed to retain, had to be determined and is specifically described in the following
Schedule.
Hence, the Statutory Determination already published in the name of Dr. Gamini Corea, the statutory declarant of
Co/1807 under the Statutory Determination No. 3244 in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 291/18 dated
16.11.1977 is hereby revoked.
T. A. S
UMANA THISSA THAMBUGALA,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
No. C 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
15th March 2016.
