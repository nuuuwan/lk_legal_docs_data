NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No. 02 OF  1974
Order under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No.02 of
1974, I, Rauff Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning and  Water Supply, do hereby approve the acquisition of the immovable
property, shown in the following schedule as it is required by the National Water Supply and Drainage Board, for the
resettlement of  the affected residents of  the Divisional Secretary Division of  Attanagalle due to construction of  the
Basnagoda Reservoir under the Gampaha, Attanagalle and Minuwangoda Joint   Water  Supply Scheme.
