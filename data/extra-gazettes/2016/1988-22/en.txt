THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
ORDINANCE made by the University Grants Commission under Section 18 read with Sections 24A and 24B of the
Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978.
Secretary,
University Grants Commission.
Colombo,
12th October 2016.
Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute (Amendment) Ordinance,
No. 03 of 2016 and shall come into force upon its publication in the Gazette.
2. The Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute Ordinance, No. 01 of 1995, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 862/8 of March 13, 1995 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 909/13 of February 09, 1996 is hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) in paragraph 4 thereof, by the substitution for the word “degrees,” in sub-paragraph (e) of that paragraph
of the words “postgraduate degree, degrees,” ;
(2) in paragraph 11 of that ordinance, by the repeal of sub-paragraph (2)(f) thereof, and the substitution
therefor of the following :-
“(f) to recommed to the University in consultation with the relevant Departmental Committee or
Committees, the postgraduate degrees, degrees, diplomas, certificates and other academic
distinctions which shall be awarded in the several branches of Ayurveda and the courses of study and
training to be followed, the examinations to be passed, and other conditions to be satisfied by
students who wish to qualify for such postgraduate degrees, degrees, diplomas, certificates and
other academic distinctions ;” ;
(3) in paragraph 15 thereof, by the substitution for the word “degrees,” in sub-paragraph (e) of that paragraph
of the words “postgraduate degrees, degrees,”.
