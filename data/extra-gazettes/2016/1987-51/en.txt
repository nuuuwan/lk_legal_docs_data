Government  Notifications
Direction No. 62
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders of all edible oils and sold in
bottles, packs or containers, retail or in bulk, that on each and every bottle, pack or container of such edible oils the
following information shall be printed legibly.
(a) Batch No.
(b) Date of Manufacture
(c) Date of Expiry
(d) V olume/Weight
(e) Maximum Retail Price
(f) Name and address of manufacturer
(g) If it is imported, name and address of the importer and the country of origin
(h) If it is imported in bulk and repacked, the date of manufacture and the date of repacking
(i) If it is a blended oil, a declaration to that effect with a description of the constituents of the oil blend and its
percentages
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Direction, remove “Edible oil in packs or containers” from Direction
Nos. 2 and 4 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1303/1 dated 25th August 2003 and No. 1407/23 dated 26th August
2005 respectively.
This directions shall come into effect from 01st March 2017.
                                                               By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
H
ASITHA TILLEKERATNE,
Chairman.
Colombo,
07th October 2016.
