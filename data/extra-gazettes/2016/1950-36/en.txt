UV A   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
1A
Departments under each Ministries of the Uva Provincial Council
I, hereby announce that the following Departments of Provincial Council of Uva Province, shall function under the mentioned
Ministries, from the date 10th December 2015.
01. Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Power and Energy, Construction,
Rural Infrastructure Development and Land of Uva Province.
1. Uva Provincial Department of Education
2. Uva Provincial Department of Local Government
3. Uva Provincial Department of Revenue
4. Uva Provincial Department of Commissioner of Land
5. Uva Provincial Department of Rural Development
02. Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production, Inland Fisheries Affairs of Uva Province.
1. Uva Provincial Department of Agriculture
2. Uva Provincial Department of Irrigation
3. Uva Provincial Department of Animal Production.
03. Ministry of Health and Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Women's Affairs and Social Welfare of
Uva Province.
1. Uva Provincial Department of Health Service
2. Uva Provincial Department of Ayurveda
3. Uva Provincial Department of Probation and Childcare
4. Uva Provincial Department of Social Welfare
04. Ministry of Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure and Co-operative Affairs of Uva
Province.
1. Uva Provincial Department of Road Development
2. Uva Provincial Department of Co-operative Development
