THE LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry Reference No. : 13/97/HW/23(3).
My Ref. No. : C/ADS/Ath/RDA/SP/4/8(50).
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land  which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 50 (Lot 1) allotments in total extent of about 0.0758
Hectare of land in Preliminary Plan No. CO 8049 dated 25.07.2016
prepared by the Colombo District Surveyor-Superintendent
on behalf of the Surveyor General situated in the Village of
Bambalapitiya Division No. 47, Bambalapitiya, within Colombo
Municipal Council Limit in the Grama Niladhari Division
Wellawatta North of Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Thimbirigasyaya in Colombo District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 50
Name of land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: Urban
Development Authority , No. 27, D. R. Wijewardhana Mawatha,
Colombo 10; Original Claimant: –(Parts of Lot No. 1 in
Preliminary Plan No. CO 7144); Extent: 0.0758 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Road Development Authority, Land
Division, 4th Floor, "Maganeguma Mahamedura", Denzil
Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Koswatta, Battaramulla of the 09th
September 2016 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd September
2016, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
D. M. DAYARATHNA,
Director (Land) & Land Acquiring Officer,
Road Development Authority.
Land Division, Road Development Authority,
4th Floor, "Maganeguma Mahamedura",
