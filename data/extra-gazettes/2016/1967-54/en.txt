PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government   Notifications
SPORTS  ACT, No. 25 OF 1973
Direction under Section  54 (3) of  National Sports Association Regulations
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under  Section 54 (3), Regulations of  National Sports Associations, published in the
Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 1825/ 38 of 30th of August 2013, made under Section 41 of  the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973,
I, Dayasiri Jayasekara, Minister of Sports, do by  this order direct  Kaludura Shantha Ruwanchandra de Silva  the  Director
General of  the Department of Sports Development, under the Ministry of Sports to convene and   conduct  a  meeting of
Sri Lanka Auto Mobile Sports Association to elect new office bearers.
DAY ASIRI JAYASEKARA (Attorney at Law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
16th of  May, 2016.
