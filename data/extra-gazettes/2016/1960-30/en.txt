CEYLON   ELECTRICITY    BOARD   ACT,   No.  17   OF   2009
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 57 of  the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No.17 of  1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this order, approve the proposed acquisition of  the land
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of  Power and Renewable Energy.
28th March, 2016.
SCHEDULE
The land situated in the village of Udapola in Grama Niladari’s Division of  Udapola (114A), in the Divisional
Secretary’s  Division of Deraniyagala, in the Pahathabulathgama Megodapotha Pattu of Atalugam Korale East, in the
Administrative District of  Kegalle, in the Province of Sabaragamuwa of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 1.2263 Hectares
(3 Acres, 0  Roods and 4.84 Perches) shown in sketched plan No. CEB/ REATDP/ TE/ 41 dated 21.01.2016 prepared by
Ceylon Electricity Board and bounded as follows :-
North : Road (10 feet wide);
East : Part of  the same land (Udapola Estate) ;
South : Part of  the same land (Udapola Estate)  ;
West : Part of  the same land (Udapola Estate)
Claimed by : Janatha Estate Development Board.
