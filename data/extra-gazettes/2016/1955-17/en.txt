EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3958. 
 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 3/J/68/69/L/81/VE. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire  the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
The below mentioned land depicted as lot 02 in 
Preliminary Plan No. MR 729, located in  the village of 
Hittetiya -  East in Matara Divisional Secretariat Division, 
Matara District, Southern Province, has been acquired for 
the Matara Kirala Kele Project. 
 
Schedule 
 
1. Lot No. 2 
Name of land: Modara Kella; Nature of land: Marsh; 
Original Owner of  the land: Gunadasa Kula 
Samaranayake, No. 89, Lighthouse Road, Dondra; 
Extent: 7 acres, 1 Roods, 17 Perches, 
 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands*  on 
which a Servitude is to be acquired are here by required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat,  on 
the 18th day of March, 2016 at 10.30 a.m. and are here by 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 11th day of March, 2016 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for  the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 RANJITH YAPA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Matara, 
20th February, 2016.
