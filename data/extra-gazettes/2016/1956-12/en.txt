THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4 (1) of the  Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968, dated 11.04.2014 and published in the Gazette  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 1862/7 dated 13.05.2014 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the
Commissioner of Labour dated 03.04.2014.
Mr. W. M. Jayasinghe, ‘‘Sampath’’, Eranawela, Bandarawela of the one part
and
The Sri Lanka Transport Board, Head Office, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05 of the other part
was referred to Mr. K. R. M. N. Lawrence for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, W. D. J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour and Trade Un ion
Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 11.04.2014.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
24th February, 2016.

THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 13 1 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies
this order exists between,
Mr. W. M. Jayasinghe, ‘‘Sampath’’, Eranawela, Bandarawela of the one part
                                                                             and
The Sri Lanka Transport Board, Head Office, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05 of the other part
NOW THEREFORE I, Welatantirige Don John Seneviratne, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers
vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Acts Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of  1968
hereby appoint.
Mr. Sarath Liyanage, No. 13/30, Sarvodaya Mawatha, Kesbewa, Piliyandala to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to
him for settlement by arbitration.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
24th February, 2016.
My No.: IR/10/50/2011.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 13 1 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. W. M. Jayasinghe, ‘‘Sampath’’, Eranawela, Bandarawela of the one part
                                                                              and
The Sri Lanka Transport Board, Head Office, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05 of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. W. M. Jayasinghe who worked at the Sri Lanka Transport Board had been caused injustice in receiving salaries and allowances
relevant and in being placed in the respective grades at the time of being appointed to the post of Divisional Running Manager from
15.07.2008 and at the time of being appointed to the permanent post of Chief Divisional Manager with effect from 21. 08.2010 and if so,
to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARA THUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, 10th day of February, 2016.
