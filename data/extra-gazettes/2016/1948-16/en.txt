NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that I have in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (5) of Article 44 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined that with effect from November 30, 2015, the assignment
of Duties and Functions, Department, Statutory Institutions and Public Corporations to the State Minister of Industry and
Commerce appointed under paragraph (1) of Article 44, to be as indicated in the Schedule given below.
R
ISHAD B ATHIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Colombo,
12th December 2015.
