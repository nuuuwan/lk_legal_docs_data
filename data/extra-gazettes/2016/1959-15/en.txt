NOTICE No : 31/0190 Mathale District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 310087 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Yatigalpoththa  
(Block 06) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Nilad-
hari Division of 433A - Yatigalpoththa in the Divisional Secre-
tariat Division of Galewela in the District of Mathale in the Prov-
ince of Central should submit his claim to the undersigned before 
22nd April, 2016 In the event of failure  to submit any claim be-
fore the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an 
exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly pub-
lish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 310087 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals‚Äô office.
