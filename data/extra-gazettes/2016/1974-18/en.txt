THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of  Lands  hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of  land called ‘‘Hingurugala yaya” in total extent
of about 0.0025 Hectare, shown and described in the Advance Tracing
No. R/GDK/2014/621 dated 17.12.2014 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated at the Village of Balavinna in Atakalan Korale
Divisional Secretary's Thambagamu Pattuwa Minor  Division, in
the  Divisional Secretary's Division of Godakawela in Rathnapura
District, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Hingurugala Yaya – Extent: About 0.0025
Hectare
North: Hingurgala Yaya claimed by W. M. Podi Appuhamy and
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and
Hingurgala Yaya claimed by W. M. Podi Appuhamy; South:
Hingurgala Yaya claimed by W. M. Podi Appuhamy and D. K.
Wijesingha; West: Hingurugala Yaya claimed by D. K. Wijesingha
and W. M. Podi Appuhamy.
 John A.E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
