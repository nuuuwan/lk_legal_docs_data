THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: EP/LD/AA/2008-2015/01.
Land Ministry No.:  LD/05/2008/HW/STDP/JBIC/272.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
     Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1-2 (Lots 2) allotments in total extent of about 0.0638
Hectare, of  land  in Preliminary Plan No. Ga 4166 and dated
2016/03/30 prepared by  the  Sinior - Superintendent of  Survey
of  Galle Distric on behalf  of  Survey General in the village of
Nawandagala in Grama Niladhari  Divisions of  25A,
Nawandagala in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Elpitiya in
Galle District in the Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Wattahenakotuwa; Description: Road (Part);
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Thilaka Iddamaldeniya,
Atakohota, Nawandagala (Part of the plot of Land No. 2267 in
P .P .9500); Extent: 0.0229 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Wattahenakotuwa; Description: Open Waste
Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Thilaka
Iddamaldeniya, Atakohota, Nawandagala (Part of the plot of
Land No. 2267 in P .P .9500); Extent: 0.0409 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Elpitiya
on the  from 13th July, 2016 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 05th July, 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
N. A. K. L. VIJENAYAKA,
Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer,
Elpitiya.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Elpitiya.
03rd June, 2016.
