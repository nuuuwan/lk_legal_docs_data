EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT ACT NO. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Land Ministry’s No.: 03/J/79/E/1106. 
My Reference No.: BER/4/5/2/49. 
 
I hereby give Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act,  as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:-  
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Three (3) allotments of land in total extent of 0.5214 
Hectare. Drown as Lot Nos. 1, 2 and 4 in preliminary 
Plan No. Ka. 4618 and dated 04.11.2016 prepared by the 
Surveyor General, Situated at Grama Niladhari’s Division  
of 768 - Padagoda in Divisional Secretariat Divison of 
Beruwala in Kalutara District, Western Province,  
morefully described as follows:  
 
  
1. Lot No.  01 
 
Name of the Land: Madangahakuruduwatta alias 
Madangahadigana; Description: Open wasteland, Use as 
Play ground of Padagoda Maha Vidyalaya - Reserved 
land for  bay; Claimant: Crown; Extent: 0.0108 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No.  02 
 
Name of the Land: Madangahakuruduwatta alias 
Madangahadigana; Description: Open wasteland, Use as 
Play ground of Padagoda Maha Vidyalaya ; Claimant: 
Crown; Extent: 0.5054 Hectare. 
 
 
3. Lot No.  04 
 
Name of the Land: Madangahakuruduwatta alias 
Madangahadigana; Description: Open wasteland, Use as 
Play ground of Padagoda Maha Vidyalaya - Reserved 
land for  bay; Claimant: Crown; Extent: 0.0052 Hectare. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which  a servitude is to be acquired  are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat  of Beruwala on the 
04th day of January, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 
the 28th day of December, 2016 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said  land/servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
   
 
  K. G. D. CHATHURA MALRAJ, 
                                      Divisional Secretary, 
                                                         Buruwala. 
 
 
 
 
At Divisional Secretariat of Buruwala, 
18th November, 2016.
