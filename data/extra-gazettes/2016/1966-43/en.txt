Direction No. 60
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers and traders that all conditions contained in warranty
certificates/documents issued in respect of goods shall be printed in Sinhala, Tamil and English Languages.
This Direction shall come into effect from 01st January 2017.
                                                 By Order of he Consumer Affairs Authority.
