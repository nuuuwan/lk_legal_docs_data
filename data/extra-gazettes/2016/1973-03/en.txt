THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘Kudagodawatta’ in total extent of
about 0.0209 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. R/GDK/
2012/954 and dated 15.12.2012 prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated at the Village of Masimbula in Meda Pattuwa Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Godakawela in Ratnapura
District, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Kudagodawatta  –  Extent: About 0.0209 Hectare
North: Kudagodawatta claimed by N. M. G . Janadasa, Road and
Kudagodawatta claimed by K. Nandasena; East: Kudagodawatta
claimed by K. Nandasena and Kudagodawatta claimed by N. M. G .
Janadasa; South: Kudagodawatta claimed by N. M. G . Janadasa;
West: Kudagodawatta claimed by N. M. G . Janadasa and Road.
John A. E. Amaratunga,
Minister of Lands.
My Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2012/WS/193.
Ministry of Lands,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
