PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  29  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  LA W  No. 1 OF 1972 AS AMENDED BY  LAND  REFORM
(SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 39  OF  1981 AND LAND  REFORM  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  14 OF 1986
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 29 of  the Law, I hereby direct every person who is interested in any
agricultural land or lands described in the Schedule hereto immediately before the date on which such land or lands have
been vested in the Land Reform Commission to make, within one month from 06.05.2016  a written claim in triplicate to
the whole any part of the compensation payable under the law in respect of such land or lands and to specify in the claim.
(a) His name and address
(b) The nature of his interest in such land/lands
(c) Particulars of his claim and
(d) How much of such compensation is claimed by him.
All claims should be sent by registered post, addressed to the Chairman, Land Reform Commission.
T. A. S
UMANA TISSA THAMBUGALA,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
Office of the Land Reform Commission,
No. C 82,
Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
04th April 2016.
