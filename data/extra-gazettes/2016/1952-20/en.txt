RECTIFICATION
Service Minute of Sri Lanka Architectural Service
Following revisions are made to the notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary of Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka No. 1877/28 dated 28th Thursday, August 2014, under the above title and the other matters and conditions of
the Service Minute shall remain unchanged.
01. The Salary step mentioned in the note below the table No. 5.3 in page No. 2A, should be revised as Salary Step
6. Accordingly the note should be as follows :
An officer is placed on 6th salary step of Grade III of Sri Lanka Architectural Service when the officer has
obtained full  professional qualifications (the status of Chartered Architect) at the time of recruitment to
Grade III of Sri Lanka Architectural Service, or obtained at a later occasion. Even such an officer should pass
the First Efficiency Bar Examination within 3 years of joining the service.
02. Educational qualification section under 7.2.2.1 of page 3A, should be replaced with the following.
I. Have obtained a degree in Architecture of five years from a university recognized by the University
Grants Commission.
Or
II. Have obtained a BSc. degree in Built Environment of three years from a university recognized by the
University Grants Commission along with a Post Graduate Diploma in the relevant field of study.
Or
III. Have obtained a BSc. degree in Built Environment of three years from a university recognized by the
University Grants Commission along with experience of 05 years in the relevant field.
03. No. III of 7.3.2.3 in page No. 5A should be removed
