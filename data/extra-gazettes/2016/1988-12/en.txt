THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.3133 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. KU/PLG/2014/279 and dated
16.08.2014, prepared by the Surveyor General of Kurunegala,
situated in the Village called Kahathivala in the Grama Niladari
Division of Udapola Othota in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Polgahawela in Kurunegala District in North Western Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kehel Elle Kumbura – Extent: 0.3133 Hectare
North: Kehel Elle Hena claimed by Ku/Al Aksha Maha Vidyalaya;

Nona  and Kehel Elle Kumbura claimed by S. Balasinghe; South:
Kehel Elle Kumbura claimed by S. Balasinghe and A. Nandasena;
West: Ela.
