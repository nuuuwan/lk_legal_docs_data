THE   WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES    BOARD    FOR  THE   RUBBER   CULTIV ATION   AND   RAW   RUBBER   PROCESSING   TRADE
IN accordance with Section 28 of  the wages Boards Ordinance (chapter 136), the Wages Board of  the Rubber Cultivation
and Raw Rubber Processing Trade  hereby notifies that the board by virtue of powers vested in it by Section 30 of the
aforesaid Ordinance propose to vary in the manner set out in the schedule here to such decisions of  the Board as are
specified in that schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 31st
March 2016.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of  the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
M. D. C.  A
MARA THUNGA,
Chairman,
Wages Board for  the Rubber Cultivation and
Raw Rubber Processing Trade.
Labour  Department,
Colombo 05,
29th  February 2016.

SCHEDULE
The decision made by the Wages Board for the Rubber  Growing Manufacturing Trade and set out in the schedule
to the notification published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 10047 dated 25 th November 1949 re-established as Wages
Board for the Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Processing Trade in the notification published in Gazette  Extraordinary
No. 1913/ 5 dated 05 th May  2015, with reference to the aforesaid decisions as varied from time to time and lastly varied
by the notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1870/3 dated 07 th July 2014, shall be further varied by
the substitution of the following new part instead of the part III (a) and (b).
PART III - OVERTIME RATE
In respect of each hour of work in excess of normal working day, the minimum overtime rate shall be the minimum
hourly rate (ascertained by dividing the daily minimum rate of wages of time rate by 8) increased by 50%.
