THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, CHAPTER No. 460 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My No.: Bi/03//1/1/256. 
  
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
      Description of the Land to be acquired: 
  
The allotment of  in total Extent of about 0.0014 
Hectare, shown as Lot No. 132 on the Preliminary Plan 
No. Gam. 3697 dated of 07.04.2016 prepared by 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Ihala 
Biyanwila, Adikari Paththuwa Minor Division, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Biyagama, in the 
District of Gampaha, in Western Province and bounded as 
follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 132 
Name of Land: Jambugahawatta; Description: Road 
(Siramadana Mawatha); Claimant: State;  Original 
Claimant: K. Malani Katulanda, No. 145/A, Kandy Road, 
Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0014 Hectare. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretary’s Office of Biyagama on the 27th October, 
2016 at 10.00 a. m. and are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27th 
October, 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars or their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
