THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. No.:  4-3/6/2014/HW/361.
                                       My Reference No.: Mil/L4/2/83 - Mil/L4/03.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of theLand
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.

All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally,
or by agents duly authorised in writing, before me at Divisional
Secretariat, Millaniya of the .06th July, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing [in duplicate] on or
before the 29th June, 2016, the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
