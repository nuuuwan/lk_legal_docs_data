PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
 LAND  (RESTRICTIONS   ON   ALIENATION)  ACT,  No.  38  OF  2014
Order under Section 3(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of Section 3(2) of the Land (Restrictions on Alienation), Act, No. 38 of
2014, I, Ravi Karunanayake, being the Minister of Finance in consultation with the Minister of Lands and with the prior
written approval of the Cabinet of Ministers, do by this order exempt Avic International Hotels Lanka Limited which is
engaged in and Infrastructure Development Project which is a mixed development project with an apartment complex at
No. 418, 420 and 422, R. A. De Mel Mawatha, Colombo 03 identified as a Strategic Development Project in terms of the
Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008, from the application of the provisions of Section 2 of the Land
(Restrictions on Alienation) Act, No. 38 of 2014, in relation to the land defined as Lot A as depicted in the Plan No. 4839
dated 3rd July, 2004 prepared by Gamini B. Dodanwela, Licensed Surveyor, bearing Assessment  No. 418, R. A. De Mel
Mawatha, Colombo 03.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
08th November 2016.
