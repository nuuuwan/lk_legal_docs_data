CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Central Province Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute No. 01 of  Year 2000
Dissolving of Appointed Members
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of  Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and Construction of  the
Central Provincial Council hereby announce under the powers vested in me by Paragraph No. 6 (C) (4) of  Central Provincial
Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute No. 01 of 2000 of Central Provincial Council, dissolve the under mentioned
member from the Director Board of the Central Province Passenger Transport Services Authority with effect from 05.08.2016
as the said member has informed me that by his own wish has resigned from post as per the Section 13 of the Authority
Statute.
EDIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power
and Energy and  Housing and Construction
Central Province.
