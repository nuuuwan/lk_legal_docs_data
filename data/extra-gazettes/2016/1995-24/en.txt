THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1954/44 of February 19, 2016), which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of
Section 139 of the aforesaid, Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for
the entry “9. Department of English” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of
Humanities and Social Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry :-
“9. Department of English and Linguistics”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th November 2016.
