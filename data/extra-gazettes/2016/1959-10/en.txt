PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT  No. 2 OF 1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under Section
65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occured in the membership of the Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member
belogned was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mrs. S. S. W. Kongahage to
fill such vacancy;
The Election Commission acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mrs. S. S. W. Kongahage, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Kandy in respect of which such vacancy occured.
Mahinda Deshapriya - Chairman - Elections Commission
N. J. Abeysekere - Member - Elections Commission
S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole - Member - Elections Commission
23rd March, 2016,
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
