LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/PDB/4/3/2/2. 
 
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands: 
 4-3/9/2014/WS/30. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
The allotment of land called “ Woodthoop Watta” in 
total extent of about 0.0506 Hectare, depicted as Lot    
No. 1 in Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6536, dated on 
01.04.2016, surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated at the Village of Aluthgama 
Mahakumbura Gammedda in Pallegampaha Korale Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Pathadumbara in Kandy District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows:- 
 
Lot No. 1 —  Name of the Land: Woodthoop 
Watta; Names and Address of the Claimants: 1. Mr. D. 
G. Pelis, No. 1/92, Aluthgama, Jambugahapitiya; 
Extent of the Land: 0.0506 Hectare. 
  
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 127 in PP A 1742; East: Lot No. 127 
and 107 in PP A 1742; South: Lot No. 127 in PP A 1742; 
West: Lot No. 127 in PP A 1742. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office, at Pathadumbara on the 20th day of 
July, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in Duplicate) on or before the 
13th day of July, 2016 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of computation of such 
amount. 
 
              G. H. M. A. PREMASINGHE, 
              Divisional Secretary, 
            Pathadumbara Division. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Pathadumbara Division,  
31st May, 2016.
