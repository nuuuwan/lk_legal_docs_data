TOWN   AND   COUNTRY  PLANNING   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  269)
Order under Sections 6(2)(b) and 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development do by this Order :
(a) declare that the area of land called the “Naigala Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area” specified in the Schedule
hereto shall be an Urban Development Area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance ; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the Urban Development Area declared to be the
“Naigala Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area”
PA TALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and
Western Development.
Colombo 01,
23rd March 2016.
