I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  No. 28 of  1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
An  allotment of land called Imbulgaswadiyawatta in 
Total Extent of 0.0985 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in 
the Preliminary Plan. Gam. 3961 prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys, Gampaha, situated in the 
Village called Kadirana North in Doonagaha Pattuwa 
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Katana in Gampaha District of the Western Province and 
morefully described as follows. 
 
Schedule 
 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of land: Imbulgaswadiyawatta; Description: 
Home garden, a permanent  building, part of a permanent 
building, toilet and a cement constructed well; Claimant: 
Land Reforms Commission; Extent: 0.0985 Hectare. 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Katana Divisional 
Secretariat on the 24th day of August, 2016 at 9.30 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
Duplicate) on or before the 17th day of August, 2016 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
computation of such amount.
