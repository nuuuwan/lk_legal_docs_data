THE  RENT  ACT,  No. 7  OF  1972
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Housing and Construction under Section 43 of the Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972 read
with Sub-section (5) of Section 2 of that Act.
SAJITH P REMADASA ,
Minister of Housing and Construction.
Colombo,
27th July 2016.
Regulations
1. The Schedule to the Rent Act, No. 7 of 1972 is hereby amended by the addition immediately after item 4
thereof, of the following new items :-
“5. Any premises of which the landlord is a co-operative society registered under the Co-operative
Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 or any statute of a provincial council, the State, a public corporation, a
public authority or a company registered under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 shall be excepted
premises for the purpose of this Act.
6. Any premises of which the tenant is a local authority or a co-operative society registered under the
Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 or any statute of a provincial council, the State, a public
corporation, a public authority or a company registered under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 shall
be excepted premises for the purpose of this Act.”.
2. The Regulations published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1305/17 of September 9, 2003 is hereby rescinded
without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
