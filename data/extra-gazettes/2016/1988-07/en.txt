THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare under Sub section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Werugodella containing in extent 0.8930
Hectare depicted in Surveyor General’s Advance Tracing No. MA/
SG/2013/394 dated 15.02.2013 situated at Thalagoda (a portion) (within
FVP 535) village in Gangala Pallesiya pattuwa minor Division of Naula
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central Province and
bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Werugodella – Extent: 0.8930 Hectare
North: Ela and Werugodella land claimed by R. M. A. G . Jayantha
Kumara Rathnayake; East: Werugodella land claimed by R. M. A. G.
Jayantha Kumara Rathnayake and road (PS); South: Road (PS) and
Werugodela land claimed by H. M. G . Wijerathne; West: Ela.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
     Minister of Lands.
