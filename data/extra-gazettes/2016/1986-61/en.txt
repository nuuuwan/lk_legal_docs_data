The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
SPORTS   ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Direction under Section 54(3) of National Sports Association Regulations
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 54(3), Regulations of National Sports Associations, published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1825/38  of 30th August 2013, made under Section 41 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973.
I, Dayasiri Jayasekara, Minister of Sports do by this Order inform the Sri Lanka Auto Mobile Sports Association
that Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardhana Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of
the Department of Sports Development has been appointed as the Competent Authority, in terms of the Section 39(1) of the
Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 for the smooth functioning  of that Association and direct that Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake
Rajawardhana Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports
Development, to convene the election of the Sri Lanka Auto Mobile Sports Association to elect new office bearers until an
election is held lawfully.
D
AY ASIRI JAY ASEKARA (Attorney-at-law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
28th September 2016.
