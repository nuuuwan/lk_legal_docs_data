Pawn Brokers’ Statute (Amendment) No. 01 of 2016 of the Central Provincial Council
A Statute for amendment of Pawn Brokers' Statute No. 04 of 2014 of the Central Provincial
Council
Be it passed by the Central Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka
1. This Statute shall hereby be called and known as the Pawn Brokers' Statute (Amendment)
No. 01 of 2016 of the Central Provincial Council and shall become effective from the
date of receiving the assent of the Governor of the Central Province.

2. The Pawn Brokers' Statute No. 04 of 2014, hereinafter referred to as the Principal Statute,
the part of sentences and words “24 months from the date of pawning”  under Section 22
of the Principal Statute shall be deleted and the words “12 months from the date of
pawning” shall be substituted and the said Section shall be amended accordingly.
3. the interpretation given for “Registered Bank” under Section 63 of the Principal Statute
shall be substituted by the interpretation mentioned below and the said Section shall be
amended accordingly. “Registered Bank means a licensed bank according to Banking
Act, No. 30th of 1988”.
4. The part of sentence and words “such a pawned article in respect of a pawn receipt may be
sold by auction if that pawned article is not redeemed within 24 months” included in the
2nd Schedule of the Principal Statute shall be deleted, substituting the following part of
sentence and words and the said 2nd Schedule shall be amended accordingly.” such a
pawned article in respect of the pawn receipt may be sold by auction if that pawned
article is not redeemed within 12 months”
5. Any inconsistency in the Sinhala, Tamil and English texts of this Statute the Sinhala content
prevail.
