 UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation   by  the Hon.  Governor  of   Uva  Province
KNOW YE that  by virtue of  the powers vested to me by  Section 154b. Subsection (8) (b) of  the 13th Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Marap Pullige Jayasinghe, the Governor of Uva Province,
do hereby this proclamation prorogue the first session of  the Sixth  Provincial Council of the Uva Province, with effect
from midnight of 29th July, Two Thousand Sixteen.
M. P. J
AYASINGHE,
Governor of the Uva Province.
Office of the  Governor of Uva  Province,
King’s Street,
Badulla,
26th July, 2016.

 UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation   by  the Hon.  Governor  of   Uva  Province
KNOW YE that  by virtue of  the powers vested to me by  Section 154b. Subsection (8) (a) of  the 13th Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Marap Pullige Jayasinghe, the Governor of Uva Province,
do hereby this proclamation summon the Second session of  the Sixth  Provincial Council of the Uva Province to meet at
09.30 a.m. on 25th August, Two Thousand Sixteen, at the Council Chamber of the Uva Provincial Council.
M. P. JAYASINGHE,
Governor of the Uva Province.
Office of the  Governor of Uva  Province,
King’s Street,
Badulla,
26th July, 2016.
