THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1093/8 of August 17, 1999 (at last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1973/76 of July 1, 2016), which specifies the Faculties and the Departments
of the Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after
entry  “Department of Engineering Technology” appearing in Column 2 of the Schedule which corresponds to the item
“Faculty of Applied Sciences” appearing in Column 1 of the Schedule of the following  entry :-
“Department of Nano Science Technology”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th November 2016.
