No. 1980/34–  THURSDAY,AUGUST,  18, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: 3/2/8/9/1. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4/3/15/2010/VE/297. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (i) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, As amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for the area described below is required to 
give out under widening of villages :- 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Following allotment in the extent of 02.9223 hectares, 
mentioned in the Preliminary Plan Mu.Pi.Nu.2900 dated 
13.11.2013 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the village of Maethagama in Medapane Korale in 
Kotmale Divisional Secretary’s Division of Nuwaraeliya 
District.  
 
1. Lot No. 1 
Name of the land: Dombahasthalawa; Division:            
456 H, Dombagasthalawa; Description: required to give 
out under widening of villages; Claimants: Land Reform 
Commission; Extent: 2.9223 Hectare. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquire are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat Office of Kotmale on the 
20th day of September, 2016 at 9.00 a.m. 
 
 
Further, it is hereby requested to notify me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 13th day of September, 
2016, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 I.M. SENANAYAKA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kotmale. 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, Kotmale, 
12th August, 2016
