No. 1976/42 –  FRIDAY, JULY,  22,  2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/7/2014/HW/112. 
 
My Ref. No.: KDS/KUN/04/07/16/04/60(III). 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
Lot No. 1 - 2 (Lots 2) allotments in total Extent of 
about 0.0080 Hectare of land in Preliminary Plan No. 
MAHA 6575 dated 19.05.2016 prepared by the Kandy 
District Surveyor -Superintendent on behalf of the 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Menikhinna 
in the Gramaniladhari Division Menikhinna (705) of 
Minor Division of Udagampaha Korale of Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Kundasale in Kandy District in 
the Central Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of Land: Thannegedarawatta; Description: 
Open Waste land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:         
R. M. I. P. Rajapaksha, No. 47, Pitawala, Menikhinna; 
Extent: 0.0040 Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of Land: Girawange Watta; Description: 
Garden, Contains Steps and Retaining Wall; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: P. A. Weerasekara, No. 25, 
Theldeniya Road, Menikhinna; Extent: 0.0040 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Office, Kundasale of 24th August, 2016 on 9.30 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 22nd August, 2016, the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 SAMANTHI D. NAGAHATHENNA, 
 Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer, 
 Kundasale. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Kundasale, 
14th July, 2016.
