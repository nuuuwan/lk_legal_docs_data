Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe, Secretary to the Cabinet to act in the post
of Secretary to the President with effect from 15.08.2016 until Mr. P. B. Abeykoon, Secretary to the President returns to
the Island.
