AMENDMENT
L.D.B 24/51(X).
THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER  203)
THE first sentence of the notification published under the aforesaid Heading in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1946/10 of
22.12.2015  was  published as,
“REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 of the Motor Traffic Act, (Chapter 203),
read with Sections 112A, 124, 125 and 126 of the aforesaid Act” and it should be corrected to  read as,
“REGULATIONS made by the Minister of  Transport and Civil Aviation under Section 237 of the Motor Traffic Act,
(Chapter 203), read with Section 213D of the aforesaid Act”.
NIMAL SIRIPALA DE SILV A,
Minister of Transport and
Civil   Aviation.
Colombo,
24th March, 2016.
