PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
1A
AMENDMENT
CENTRAL    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Ganga  Ihala  Korale Pradeshiya  Sabha
BY-LAWS
I, do hereby make amendment in the Sinhala text announcement related to the By-Law relating to Parking Hiring Cars and
Motor Vehicles of Ganga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, published in the Section IV(a) of Extraordinary Gazette
No. 1877/32 and dated 28.08.2014 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
I, Sarath Ekanayake, as the Miniser in Charge of the subject of Local Government of the Central Provincial
Council, do hereby notify according to the powers vested in me under Sub-section (1) of Section 123 of the Pradeshiya
Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, to be read with Sub-section (1)(a) of Section 2 of the Provincial Council Act (Consequential
Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, has approved the following By-Laws framed by the Ganaga Ihala Korale Pradeshiya Sabha,
according to powers vested under paragraph (e), Sub-section (VII) of Section 126 of the said Act, to be read with Sub-
section (1) of Section 122 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, and the said By-Laws shall be effective within the
Pradeshiya Sabha authority area of Ganga Ihala Korale from the date of publication of this notification in the Government
Gazette .
S
ARATH E KANAYAKE,
The Chief Minister of Central Province and
the Minister-in-charge of the subject of
Local Government.
