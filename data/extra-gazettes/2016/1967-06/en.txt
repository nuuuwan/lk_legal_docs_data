EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
           My Reference No.: Ambako/3/1/A/143-V 
             Land Ministry Reference No: 4-3/7/2013/HW/577  
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7, 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
Servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or Servitude  to be acquire:- 
 
Lot No. 01 of Kudaoya Watta below name land in 
total extent about 0.0145 Hectare, shown in Preliminary 
Plan No. Nu. 3014 and dated 30.07.2015, prepared by the 
Surveyor Genaral, situated in the village of Kudaoya and 
Minor Division of Ambagamuwa Korale in the Divisional  
Secretary’s Division of Ambagamuwa, in the District of 
Nuwara Eliya. 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Kudaoya Watta; Names and 
Addresses of the Claimant: P. K. C. S. Kumara, No. 06,, 
Nuwaraeliya Road, Kudaoya, Hatton; Extent: 0.0145 
Hectares. 
 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Road ( Road Development Authority) and Lot 
No. 92 of P.P.NU. 2786; East: Road ( Road Development 
Authority), Lot No. 93 of  P.P.Nu. 2786; South: Road 
(Road Development Authority), Lot No. 93 of P.P.Nu. 
2785 and Part of Hi. Pi. 45509; West: Part of Hi. Pi. 
45509 and Road. 
  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on  
witch a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambagamuwa of the 13th day of July, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 29th Day of June, 2016 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/ servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 R. T. P. S UMANASEKARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ambagamuwa Koralaya, 
 Ginigathhena. 
 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Ambagamuwa,
