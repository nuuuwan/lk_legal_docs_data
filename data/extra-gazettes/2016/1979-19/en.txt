THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  2 OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 2 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation
of the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development being of the opinion that the lands
specified in the shcedules hereto are urgently required for the purpose of developing the Horana Industrial Zone and
Urban Development Project do by this order, declare that the said lands are required for such purpose.
 M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
30th July, 2016.
