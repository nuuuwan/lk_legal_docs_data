THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land called Kajugaha Kumbura having following
mentioned extent and limitation in total extent of 0.5040 Hectare
shown as Lots ‘A ’ and ‘B’ in Amended  Advance Tracing No. Gam/
Watt/2013/122 and dated 29th May, 2013, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys  situated in the Village of Nawammahara
in Ragam Pattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Wattala in the District of Gampaha in Western Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Extent: 0.1020 Hectare
North: Kajugaha Kumbura claimed by Gamunu Force Base Sri
Lanka Navy, Canal and Kajugaha Kumbura claimed by Gamunu
Force Base Sri Lanka Navy; East: Kajugaha Kumbura claimed by
Gamunu Force Base Sri Lanka Navy and Lot B; South: Lot B, Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha) and Canal; West: Canal, Kajugaha Kumbura
claimed by Gamunu Force Base Sri Lanka Navy and Canal.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kajugaha Kumbura – Extent: 0.4020 Hectare
North: Lot A (Canal) and Kajugaha Kumbura claimed by Gamunu
Force Base Sri Lanka Navy; East: Kajugaha Kumbura claimed by
Gamunu Force Base Sri Lanka Navy; South: Kajugaha Kumbura
claimed by Gamunu Force Base Sri Lanka Navy, Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha) and Lot A (Canal); West: Canal  and Lot A (Canal).
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
