THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order Under Section 27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways,  on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend  the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act,  published in Gazette Extraordinary ,
No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 establishing the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, (as last amended by Order published in
Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1948/60 of January, 08, 2016) by the addition immediately after the entry “Faculty of Science”
appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column II thereof, of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology 1. Department of Engineering Technology
2. Department of Bio Systems Technology
3. Department of Information and Communication Technology
4. Department of Multidisciplinary Studies”.
L
AKSHMAN, K IRIELLA ,
Minister of Higher Education
and Highways.
Colombo,
25th April 2016.
