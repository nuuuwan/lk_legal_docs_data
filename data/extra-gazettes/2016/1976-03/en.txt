I, Sarath Ekanayake do hereby declare notice on the powers delegated upon to me as Minister in-charge of Local Government
in Central Province, as per Sections 171, 173 and 184 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and read along with Section
2 of  Provincial Council Act (Consequential Provincial Act) No. 12 of 1989, to be included the following order beyond to
the Section 178(3) of code of regulations (Finance and Administration) of Pradeshiya Sabha of 1988 published by the
Special Gazette Notification No. 554/5 dated 17.04.1989 prepared by the Minister in-charge, Local Government subject
in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

of Local Government,
