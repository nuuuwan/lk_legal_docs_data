Order of Delegating Power
BY virute of the powers vested in me under Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009 of North Central Province

the duties and functions of the post of Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development (Sri Lanka Administrative

Commissioner of Co-opeative Development and Registrar from the date mentioned in Column II in terms of the powers
under Co-opeative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009 that shall be read with Co-operative Societies Amendment Statute
No. 01 of 2011 of North Central Province Provincial Council.
