EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: Ambako/3/1/A/118. 
 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/168. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
Servitude Which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the land  or Servitude to be acquired : 
 
Lot No. 241 of Pawalamale Watta below name land in 
total extent about 0.0090 Hectares, shown in FVP No. 
106 Sheet No. 22 Supplement No. 19 and dated 
14.03.2016 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the village of Pawalamale Watta in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Ambagamuwa, in the District of 
Nuwara Eliya. 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 29;  East: Lot No. 29; South: Lot No. 
29; West: Lot No. 29. 
Claimant’s Name and Address: K. K. Chandradasa, 
“Chandana”, Bandaranayaka Mawatha, Ginigathhena. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambagamuwa on the 10th day of August, 2016 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before 27th day of July, 2016, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the perticulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of pensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 R. T. P. S UMANASEKARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ambagamuwa Koralaya, 
 Ginigathhena. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,
