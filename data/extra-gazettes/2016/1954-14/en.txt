EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/01/08.  
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be  acquired: 
 
Lot No. 01 allotments of below name land in total 
extent of about 0.0118 Hectares, shown in Preliminary 
Plan No. Ra. 299, prepared by the Surveyor General, 
situated in the village of Rathnapura Town (within the 
Rathnapura Municipal Council limit) in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of No. 152 B Weralupa and Minor 
Division of Kuruwita Korale Udapattuwa in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Rathnapura in 
Rathnapura District in the Sabaragamuwa Province. 
  
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Owilanawatta; Description: Open 
waste land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Dickson 
Dela Bandara, Nilanga Dela Bandara, Sujeewa 
Wijewardhana and Sudath Wijewardhana, No. 278, 
Colombo Road, Rathnapura; Extent: 0.0118 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Division al Secretariat, Rathnapura  
of the 26th day of February 2016 at 2.00 p.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writi ng                      
(in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of February, 2016 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land/servitude*, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
  
 C. M. . K OTTAWATTA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rathnapura.  
  
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Rathnapura, 
26th   January, 2016.
