EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I do hereby give notice according to the Sub -section (1) 
of the 7 th section of the Land Acquisition Act as 
amended of land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government is supposed to acquire the Land/right of 
possessing of the following land for Public Purpose. 
 
 That the particulars of the Land/right of possessing to be 
acquisition—  
 
The land situated at Pathiniyarmahilankulam in 
Thandikulam Grama Niladhari’s Division (No. 214A) in 
Vavuniya Divisional Secretariat’s Division in the 
Vavuniya District of the Northern Province. The Extent 
of the Land is about 0.0092 Hectares according to the 
Advance Tracing prepared by Surveyor General No. FVP. 
No. 58 and Dated 14.12.2014 and within the following 
boundaries. 
   Lot No. 290 — Name of the Land: “Oyarsinnakula 
Edam”;   
 
 North by : Lot No. 289; East by : Lot No. 289; South 
By: Lot Nos. 289, 29 (Road, Pradeshiyasabha); West by: 
Lot No. 29, (Road Pradeshasabha Lot 289). 
 
 
Claimer’s Name and address: Selliah Vimaleswaran,                    
No. 03, RDD Office’s Quarters, Park Road, Vavuniya. 
 
  
I do hereby requested to appear before me personally or 
the Land owners those who have got written power for 
ownership for the Land and claiming the land/possession 
in acquisition at the Conference Hall of Divisional 
Secretariat, Vavuniya at 9.00 a. m. on 28th of November 
2016 and to submit with three copies that their nature of 
the ownership and the amount of compensation and 
details regarding to calculation of the compensation 
before 21st of November 2016. 
 
 
 K.  UTHYARAJAH, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
    Vavunia 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Vavuniya.
