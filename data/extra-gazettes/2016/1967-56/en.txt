No. 1967/56 –  THURSDAY, MAY,  19,  2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
      LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT       
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: 3/4/71. 
Land Min. Ref No.: LD/05/2006/HA/490. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Two allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 282 and 
283 in Final Village Plan No. 849, Supplimentary No. 06 
Sheet No. 06 prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Surveys Kurunegala, situated in the village called 
Udahorombuwa in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 565 
of Madure Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Rideegama in Kurunegala District of the Northwestern 
Province and more fully described as follows : 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
01. Lot No. 282 
 
Name of the Land: Hendillehena, Hedillehena alias 
Hendillewatta; Claimant: Ms. Pallegedara Premawathie, 
Udahorombuwa; Extent: 0.0042 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 283 
 
Name of the Land: Hendillehena, Hedillehena alias 
Hendillewatta, Pansalewatta; Claimant: Mr. Hendille -
gedara Somadasa, Udahorombuwa; Extent: 0.0271 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Rideegama Divisional 
Secretariat on 20th July, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 13th July, 2016 the nature of their interest in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 M. S. M. NAVAZ, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rideegama.  
Divisional Secretariat,  
Rideegama, 
17th May, 2016.
