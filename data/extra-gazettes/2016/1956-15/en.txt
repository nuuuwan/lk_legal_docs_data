EXTRAORDINARY
1A
THE  COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT  is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “ Ven. Pituduwe Dhammika
Himi Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in
charge of the subject of Local Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947
(Chapter 252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Councils Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No.
12 of 1989.
V . K. A. A
NURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
01. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
02. District - Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street - Sri Negrodharama Mawatha
04. New Name of the Street - “ Ven. Pituduwe Dhammika Himi Mawatha”
05. Road Description - This road commencing in between from the
Assessment Number 01 and 14 adjacent to Danister
de Silva Mawatha belonging to Wanatamulla
Municipal Ward, terminates adjacent to Ven.
Saranapala Himi Mawatha in between from
Assessment Number 60 and 66.
