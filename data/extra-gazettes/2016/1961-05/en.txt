EXTRAORDINARY
1A
THE   COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
It is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “ Mahagama Sekara Mawatha ” by the
Honourable Chief Minister of Western Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government
under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the
Provincial Council’s Act, (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
V . K. A. ANURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
01. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
02. District - Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street - A portion of the Paget Road and Stanmore Crescent
04. New Name of the Street - “ Mahagama Sekara Mawatha ”
05. Road Description - This road belonging to the Tibirigasyaya Municipal Ward
stating from between Assessment No. 204 and 206 of
Baudhdhaloka Mawatha ends from between Assessment
No. 11/1 of Keppetipola Road and Assessment No. 10
of Stanmore Cresent (A portion of Paget Road and
Stanmore Crescent belong to this road).
