PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW No. 41  OF  1978  ENACTED  BY  THE  NATIONAL
STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 enacted
by the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development do by this
Order vest with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from today, the land situated at Dematagoda in Wanathamulla
Ward within the limits of the Municipal Council of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto, being the land
called “Slaughter House for Sheep” belonging to the Colombo Municipal Council
     P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA (M.P.),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,
17th and 18th Floors,
“Suhurupaya”,
Sri Subuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
18th November 2016.
SCHEDULE
Allotment of land in extent of 02A., 01R., 3.77P. depicted as Lot No. 01 in Plan No. 33/2012 dated 30.07.2012
prepared by Registered Licensed Surveyor T. R. de Zoysa situated at Dr. Danister de Silva Mawatha in Dematagoda Ward
No. 30, Wanathamulla within the Municipal Council limits of Colombo in the District of Colombo, Western Province
bounded as follows :
North : Private Land ;
East : Land bearing Asst. Nos. G 247,  243  Dr. Danister De Silva Mawatha ;
South : Land bearing Asst. Nos. 4 to 28 Mount Pleasant Road ;
West : Muslim Burial Ground.
