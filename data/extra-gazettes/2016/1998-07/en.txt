PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 994
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32 read with Section 25 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance do by this Order direct the following with effect from
01.01.2017.
“Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in  any other Notification, there shall be charged a license fee of Rs.
100,000 for arrack licenses AFL 1, AFL 2, AFL 3, AFL 4 which are issued together with Foreign Liquor (FL) license and
Rs. 50,000 for the sale of bottled toddy.”
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
19th December 2016.
