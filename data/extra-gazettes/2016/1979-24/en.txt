UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of   Uva  Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1947, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mr. W. M. M. G . Abeysinghe Banda, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to
the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural Affairs and Textiles and Small Industries
of Uva Province, with immediate effect from the date 17.02.2016 ;
2) Mr. D. M. L. Bandaranayake, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administative Service, as Deputy Chief
Secretary (Personnel and Training) of Uva Province, with immediate effect from the date 17.02.2016 ;
3) Mrs. R. M. L. C. Abesinghe, Class III Officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service to perform the Duties of the
Director of Sports of Uva Province, from the date 04.02.2016 to 19.02.2016, till the return of Mrs. Geetha
Nandani Pathiranawasam to the Island, who is the Director of Sports of Uva Province.
4) Mr. B. M. K. G. Bandara, Class III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of
the Provincial Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province, in addition to his Permanent Post,
from the date 21.03.2016 to 29.03.2016, till the return of Mr. M. M. Wijenayake to the Island, who is the
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province.
5) Mr. H. M. Jayarathna, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Servicce, to perform the Duties of the
Director to Building of Uva Province, in addition to his Permanent Post, from the date 13.03.2016 to
25.03.2016 till the return of Mr. P. S. S. Withanage to the Island, who is the Acting Director to Building of
Uva Province.
6) Mr. H. M. Jayarathna, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the
Deputy Chief Secretary (Engineering Service) of the Uva Province, in addition to his Permanent Post, from
the date 19.03.2016 to 26.03.2016, till the return of Engineer Mr. A. P. S. K. Karunarathne, to the Island,
who is the Deputy Chief Secretary (Engineering Service).
