THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT,No.28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                                 My Reference No.: KDS/TUM/04/01/04. 
Land Ministry’s Ref No. : 4 - 3/9/2014/WS/29.  
  
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition  Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “Paragahamulahena” in 
total extent of about 0.0360 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 
1 in Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6546, dated 18.03.2016, 
surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor General, situated 
at the village of BanAnga in Meda Siyapattuwa Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Thumpane in Kandy District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 1 - Name of the Land: 
Paragahamulahena; Name and Address of the 
claimants: Mr. S. M. M. P. K. Senavirathne, No. 54 A, 
BanAnga Girihagama, Kumburegama. Extent: 0.0360 
Hectare.  
 
Boundaries: 
North: Paragahamulahena claimed by S. M. M. P. K. 
Senavirthne; East: Paragahamulahena claimed by S. M. 
M. P. K. Senavirthne and Road (Pradesiya Sabha); South: 
Road (Pradesiya Sabha); West: Road (Pradesiya Sabha). 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office, at the Thumpane on the 24th 
November day of 2016 at 09.30 a.m and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 03 rd day of November 2016, the nature of 
their interests in the Land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
  THUSITHA N. W. RANASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Thumpane. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Thumpane Division. 
05th October, 2016.
