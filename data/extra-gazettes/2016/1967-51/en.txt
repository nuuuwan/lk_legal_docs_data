THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land in total extent of about 0.7854 Hectare, depicted
in Advance Tracing No. AM/MHO/2014/329 dated 05.11.2014,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Thambadeniya in the Minor Division of Bintennapattu North in
Maha Oya Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ampara District, in
Eastern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thambadeniya – Extent: About 0.4145 Hectare
North: Thambadeniya and Lot No. 829 depicted in the Topo PP
20; East: Lot Nos. 829, 705 depicted in the Topo PP 20 and Lot B;
South: Lot B, Lot No. 198 depicted in the Topo PP 20, Thambadeniya
and Footpath; West: Thambadeniya and Footpath.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Thambadeniya – Extent: About 0.3709 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 829, 705 depicted in the Topo PP 20 and Lot A;
East: Lot Nos. 705 and 706 depicted in the Topo PP 20; South: Lot
B, Lot Nos. 706, 206 and 198 depicted in the Topo PP 20; West: Lot
No. 198 depicted in the Topo PP 20, Thambadeniya and Lot A.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
