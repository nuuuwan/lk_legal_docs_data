THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
Servitude* which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotment of Land called Kent Watta in extent of 
about 8.3120 Hectares, shown as a Lot No. 127 in the 
F.V.P 60 Sheet No. 6 Supplement No. 7, Prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Galahitiyawa 
of Minor Division of Udugoda Udasiya Pattuwa at 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pallepola of Matale 
District of Central Province and bounded as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
Lot No. 127 
 
North: Maningamuwa F.V.P. 48, Lot No. 6 and 
Monaruwila F.V.P. 49; East: Monaruwila F.V.P. 48; 
South: Lot No. 105 and 39; West: Lot No. 105, 39, 126, 
125, 124, 123, 122, 121, 120, 119, 118 and 117. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Pallepola Divisional Secretariat on 
the 08th day of June, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 18th day of May, 2016 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
 
 W. M. ANANDA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pallepola Division. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Pallepola, 
17th March, 2016.
