THE   URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No. 2 of
1980, I Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-Charge of the subject of urban development being of the opinion that the land specified in the schedule I & II
hereto is urgently required for the purpose of setting up a Science and Technology related urban development project
under Mahenawatta Stage II development programme do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such
purpose.
MAITHRIPALA  S IRISENA ,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
29th January 2016.
