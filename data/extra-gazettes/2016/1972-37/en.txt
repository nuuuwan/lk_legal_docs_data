CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha
IMPOSING   ENTERTAINMENT    TAX
IT is hereby notified under Sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) that the under
mentioned Proposal was approved by the Minister in charge of  the subject Local Government, adopted by the Minipe
Pradeshiya Sabha, by virtue of power vested on it under Sub-section (1) of  the said Ordinance.
P. B. W
IJAY ARATNA,
Secretary,
Chief, Education and Ministry of  Local
Government,
Central Province.
Pallekele,
04th June 2016.
PROPOSAL
In term of Entertainment Tax Ordinance, it has been resolved to impose and levy a tax (other than Entertainment
Tax).equal to ten per centum (10%) of the entry payment on certain entertainment activity mentioned in the Entertainment Tax
Ordinance, and it shall be effective from the first day of the following month, after publication of this Proposal notification in
the Gazette.
The tax shall be levied at the rate of seven point five (7.5%) per centum for a period of first two years of the
implementation of this tax, on the amount payable for a film show entry.
