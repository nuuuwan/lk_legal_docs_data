Regulation
The Land Acquisition (Payment of Compensation) Regulations 2013, published in Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 1864/ 54 of  May 30, 2014 is hereby amended as follows :-
(1) in regulation 2 thereof by the insertion immediately after sub- paragraph (r) of paragraph (2) of the following
sub- paragraphs :-
‘‘ (s) Matara - Hambantota - Extension of  Southern Expressway ;
   (t) Central  Expressway.’’ ;
(2) in regulation 7 thereof by the repeal of  the definition of expression ‘‘ Minister” and substitution therefor
of the following :-
‘‘ Minister”  means the Minister assigned the subject of lands;”.
