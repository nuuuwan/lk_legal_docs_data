THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of the
National School of Business Management Limited a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing registration number PB 4833, do by this Order, recognize the National School of Business Management
Limited as a degree awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education therein, leading to the award of the
Degrees and Post Graduate Diplomas specified in Column I of Schedule I (subject to the conditions specified) and Schedule
II respectively, of which abbreviations are specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of Schedule I and Schedule II
respectively.
SCHEDULE  I
APPLICABLE  CONDITIONS
1. National School of Business Management Limited shall adhere to the minimum university entry qualifications
stipulated by the University Grants Commission including a minimum of a simple pass in General English at General
Certificate of Education (GCE) Advance Level.
2. National School of Business Management Limited shall provide sufficient number of computers for the students,
within 3 months from the date of publication of this Order and keep the Specified Authority informed of it.
Column I Column II
Degree Abbreviation to the Degree
Bachelor of Arts in Business Communication BA (Business Communication)
