L.D.B. 21/1978(x)
UNIVERSITIES   ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of
the Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing Registration Number GA 2232, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under the said
Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1948/11 of January 06, 2016 by the addition immediately after the entry
“Bachelor of Science in Information Technology” appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding entries in Column II of the
Schedule thereof, of the following :-
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Abbreviation to the Degree
Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology and B Tech (IT and Communication)
Communication
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
08th August 2016.

Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of

the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 17745, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1818/7 of July 09, 2013 by the addition

corresponding entries in Column II of the Schedule thereof, of the following :-
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degree Programme Abbreviation to the Degree
Bachelor of Arts in English BA (English)
