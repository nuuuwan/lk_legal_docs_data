WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Amended order Pertaining to the Delegation of powers in the Western Provincial Public Service under
Section 32(2a) of  Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987, amended by the Provincial Councils (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1990
IT is hereby notified that without any detriment to actions taken under Schedule 06 of the Order dated 23.12.2015,
published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification dated 21st January 2016 No. 1950/47 of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka with regard to delegation of powers in terms of appointments, transfers, dismissals and disciplinary
control of officers in the Western Provincial Public Service as per Section 32(2a) of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42
of 1987 as amended by the Provincial Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990, the said Order is amended by the
removal of the said Schedule 06 and by the insertion instead thereof the Schedule No. 06 as stated below hereof with
effect from the twentieth of September 2016.
K. S
ARATH G UNATHILAKE,
Chairman,
Provincial Public Service Commission,
Western Province.
Provincial Public Service Commission,
Western Province,
109, Main Street, Battaramulla,
On this 20th day of September 2016.
