FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
DEHIWALA - MOUNT  LA VINIA   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being
ceased to be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United National Party to which the
former member, Krees Wilhelm Balthasar belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that
Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Munkapu Arachchige
Nagasinha Rohitha being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia  Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Munkapu Arachchige
Nagasinha Rohitha  elected as a member of the Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
City  Elections Office,
Colombo,
05th February, 2016.
