PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
A Proclamation made by the Governor of Uva Province
BY , virtue of the powers vested to me by Article 154B, Section (08) Subsection (a) of the 13th Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Marap Pullige Jayasinghe, the Governor of Uva Province,
do hereby summon the Provincial Council of the Uva Province to meet at 9.30 a. m. on the 21st day of November, 2016
in the Council Chamber of the Provincial Council of Uva Province.
M. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Governor of the Uva Province.
At Office of the Governor of Uva Province,
Badulla,
15th November 2016.
