I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the abandon paddy field 
described below and located between Horana - Colombo 
bus route and Ranketha Place at Paropakara Mawatha, 
Boralesgamuwa to create a water reserve, water tank, 
water ways and townscape development under Weras 
Ganga Storm Water Drainage and Environment 
Improvement Project. 
 
Description of  the land to be acquired: 
 
Twenty eight (28) allotments of land in total Extent of 
2.7016 hectares depicted as Lot No. 09 to 15 and Lot No.
