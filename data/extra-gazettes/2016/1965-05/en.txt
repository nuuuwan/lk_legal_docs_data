CEYLON  PETROLEUM  CORPORATION  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1961
Order under Section 40(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 40(1) of the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation Act, No. 28 of 1961 as
amended by the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation (Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 1963, I, Chandima Weerakkody, Minister of
Petroleum Resources Development, do hereby approve the proposed acquisition of the property described in the Schedule
hereto.
C
HANDIMA  W EERAKKODY ,
Minister of Petroleum Resources Development.
Colombo,
02nd May 2016.
SCHEDULE
All that allotment of land marked Lot 1 depicted in Plan No. 545 dated 19.10.1993 made by R. B. Abeykoon,
Licensed Surveyor of the land called “Patapilikanda Pahala Watta” situated in Minneriya Village, Sinhala Pattuwa Minor
Division, Hingurakgoda Divisional Secretary’s area in the District of Polonnaruwa, North-Central Province and bounded
on the North by property claimed by H. Aronsingho, on the East by property claimed by H. Aronsingho, on the South by
Post Office, Minneriya and on the West by Main Road and containing in extent Twenty Seven decimal Eight Nine perches
(0A., 0R., 27.89P.) (0.07054 Hectares) according to the said Plan No. 545.
