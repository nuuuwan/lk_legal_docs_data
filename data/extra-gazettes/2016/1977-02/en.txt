Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref: ඉ/අත්/UDA/351. 
 
I hereby give public notice, under sub-section 1 of 
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land for 
a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
The land depicted in the Surveyor General’s 
Preliminary Plan No. 9634 having an Extent of 0.8207 
Hectare, in the Grama Niladari Division No. 498 A of 
Makumbura North Maharagama Divisional Secretary’s 
Division, Colombo District in the Western Province. 
 
Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; 
Description: Abandoned paddy field and Owita, 13 Nos. 
of Coconut trees within 15 – 20 years old; Ownership: 
Yasapala Elvitigala and others of Makumbura 
Pannipitiya; Extent: 0.4180 Hectare. 
Lot No. 2 
 
Name of the Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; 
Description: Abandoned paddy field; Ownership: 
Hendrik & others of Makumbura Pannipitiya; Extent: 
0.2960 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 3 
 
Name of the Land: Mahakumbura Welyaya; 
Description: Abandoned paddy field, 5 Nos. of Coconut 
trees within 15 - 20 years old and well; Ownership: 
Kaluthanthreege Indrawathi e Peiris of No. 185/A, 
Malapalla Pannipitiya; Extent: 0.0942 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 4 
 
Name of the Land: Not known; Description: Road; 
Ownership: Not known; Extent: 0.0125 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby 
required to appear personally or through their agents, 
duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Maharagama on the 24th August, 2016 at 
9.00 a.m. and all hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before 29th July, 2016 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation, and the details of the 
compensation of such amount.
