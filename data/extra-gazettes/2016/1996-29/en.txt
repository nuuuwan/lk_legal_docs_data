THE   LAND   ACQUISITION    (AMENDMENT)  ACT
(CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. :  NE/3/1/8/Acquisition/160.
Land and Land Ministry Ref. No. :  4/3/10/2013/UDA/639.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government
intended to acquired.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotment of land called in Total Extent about 5.9181 Hectare
described as Lot 01 to 02 in preliminary Plan No. P .P .Nu 1840
of 30.11.1996 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in
Nuwara Eliya (Division No. 7 park Road, Nuwara Eliya
Municipal Council limits) Village in Oya palatha Korale Minor
Division in Nuwara-eliya Divisional Secretary's Division in
Nuwara-eliya District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Oakley Cottage; Details: Garden, Contains 10
Cyprus trees 20-100 years, 132 Akeshiya trees 35-85 years, 9
Terpantine trees 40-80 years , 33 Indian desert trees 30 years
and permanent building; Claimant: Kelsey Property
Developers (Pvt - Ltd, Dunamis Capital PLC and Nextventures
Limited); Extent: 5.2639 Hectare.
01. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Oakley Cottage; Details: Garden, Contains 1
Cyprus trees 20 years, 3 Akeshiya trees 35 years, 33 Indian
desert trees 30 years; Claimant: Kelsey Property Developers
(Pvt - Ltd, Dunamis Capital PLC and Nextventures Limited);
Extent: 0.6542 Hectare.
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be acquired
are hereby required to appear  personally or by agent  duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Nuwara-eliya 08th January 2017 at 11.00 a.m. relevant to each
land lots as stated below and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing  (in duplicate) on or before the 28th day of
December 2016. The nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said  land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
S. P . K. BODHIMANNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Nuwara Eliya.
05th December, 2016,
Nuwara Eliya Divisional  Secretary’s Office.
