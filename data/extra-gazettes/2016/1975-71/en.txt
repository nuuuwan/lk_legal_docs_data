NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 (Amended) 
 
 
THE notice is hereby given under the subsection (01) in 
the section 07 of the land acquisition Act (Amended)  
No. 28 of 1964 as the government indents to acquire the 
below mentioned land/ Servitude for a public purpose. 
 
The description of the land to be acquired -   
 
Acquiring land to be widening the Road drawn from 
Keraminiya to Pathiraja Road. 
 
 
The Lot  No. 11 of the Preliminary Plan bearing           
No. 4093 of the Surveyor General, located at the 
Keraminiya Village, within the Grama Niladhari Division 
86-B Keraminiya, of the Ambalangoda Divisional 
Secretariat Division, in Galle District. 
 
Lot No. 11 
 
Name of the Land: Wiharagoda Badda; Description: 
Open Waste land; First Claimant: Manimelwadu 
Rathnasiri, 17/24, Pathiraja Road, Keraminiya; Extent: 
0.0038 Hectare. 
 
The All Claimants and people who have ownership or 
other relation to the aforementioned land themselves or 
the people who are authorized by their documents should 
be appeared before me at 9.30 a.m., on September 20th in 
2016, at the Divisional Secretariat, Ambalangoda. 
 
Further, the nature of their ownership related to the 
mentioned land, details on the facts for claiming 
compensation regarding the acquiring that land, the 
amount of compensation and the way it is calculated shall 
be received to me by duly completed copies prior to the 
September 1st in 2016. Deeds and tirpes should also have 
to be forwarded for the confirmation of the ownership. 
 
 
 
              H. N. S. T. K. DE SILVA, 
              Divisional Secretary, 
            Ambalangoda. 
On 31st May 2016, 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambalangoda.
