IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under paragraph (2) of Article 43 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Field Marshal Hon. Gardihewa Sarath Chandralal Fonseka,
Member of Parliament, as the Minister of Regional Development , with effect from February 25, 2016.
