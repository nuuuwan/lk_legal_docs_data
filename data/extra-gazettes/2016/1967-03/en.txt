 REGISTRATION  OF   ELECTORS  ACT,  No.  44  OF  1980
IN the exercise of the powers vested  by Section 8(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, the Election
Commission does hereby appoint for the Electoral District  specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, the Registering
Officers and Assistant Registering Officers mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column II and Column III respectively.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA N. J. ABEYSEKERE S. RATNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE
 Chairman Member Member
Election Commission,
Rajagiriya,
16th May 2016.
