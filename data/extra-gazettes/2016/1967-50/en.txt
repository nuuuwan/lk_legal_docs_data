THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Hon, Minister of Lands
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acqui-
sition Act, that the land, described below are needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land in Surveyor General's Tracing No. KU/
KBI/2014/305 situated in the village of Ujekalle, 1324 -
Welpothuwewa Grama Niladhari Division in Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kobeigane in Kurunegala District in North Western Prov-
ince and bounded as follows;
01. Lot A – Modara Hena – Extent: 0.0280 Hectare
North: Daduru Oya; East: Balance portion of this land; South:
Channel; West: Channel and balance portion of this land.
02. Lot B – Halambegahamulahena – Extent: 0.2274 Hect-
are
North: Balance portion of this land and channel; East: Balance
portion of this land; South: Balance portion of this land and Pradeshiya
Sabha Road; West: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and balance portion of
this land.
03. Lot C – Galvatiyewaththe or Rossalin Estate – Extent:
0.2274 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land and Pradeshiya Sabha Road;
East: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and balance portion of this land; South:
Galvatiyawaththe owned by Mr. N. M. L. Marikkar and Lot D;
West: Galvatiyawaththe owned by Mr. N. M. L. Marikkar and
balance portion of this land.
04. Lot D – Galvatiyewaththe – Extent: 0.1369 Hectare
North: Galvatiyawaththe owned by Mr A. L. Gunarathne and Lot
C; East: Balance portion of this land and Pradeshiya Sabha Road;
South: Pradeshiya Sabha Road; West: Balance portion of this land.
05. Lot E – Galvatiyewaththe – Extent: 0.0925 Hectare
North and East: Pradeshiya Sabha Road; South: Pradeshiya Sabha
Road and Balance portion of this land; West: Balance portion of this
land.
