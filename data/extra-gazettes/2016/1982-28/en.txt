ELECTORAL DISTRICT - NO. : 1 - C OLOMBO
NOTICE is hereby given under Section 13(2) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, that–
(1) List “A” containing names of persons in the electoral register  who are dead or have become disqualified ; and
(2) List “B” containing the names of persons whose name do not appear in the current electoral register but now
eligible to be included in the register ;
have been completed in respect of the above Electoral District and that the register in operation and such lists or portions
thereof are open for inspection of the general public during office hours as follows :
Places of Exhibition Register and Lists or Portions thereof for
Exhibition

Colombo District Electoral District and copies of the relevant “A” and “B” Lists.
(ii) Each Divisional Secretariat A copy of portion of the electoral register in force containing all Polling
Districts lying within the relevant Divisional Secretary’s area and copies
of  the related  “A” and “B” Lists.
(iii) Each Local Authority A copy of the electoral register in force containing all Polling Districts lying
within the area of authority of the relevant Local Authority and copies of
the  related  “A” and “B” Lists.
(iv) Each Grama Niladhari’s A copy of the electoral register in force containing all Polling District lying
Office within the relevant Grama Niladhari’s Division and copies of the related
“A” and “B” Lists.
