L.D.B. 104/44(v)
TRUST   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “Maviddapuram Pon Trust” namely –
1. Sangarapillai Nagendran
2. Sangarapillai Manoharan
3. Jeremiah Thiruchchandran
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87) ;
NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Wijayadasa Rajapakshe, Minister
of Justice do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that -
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing No. 2086 of October 6, 2012 attested by
Kandiah Neelakandan, Notary Public of Colombo shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a
common seal under the name and style of the “Maviddapuram Pon Trust”
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, taken on loan or otherwise acquire,
sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable, on behalf
of the said Trust ; and
(c) the Trustee shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and to be sued, to answer and
to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and actions
arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
20th December 2016.
