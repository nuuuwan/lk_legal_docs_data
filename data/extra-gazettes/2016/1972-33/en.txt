CORRECTION
MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT (CHAPTER  203)
PRINTING Mistakes of Sinhala and Tamil texts of Motor Traffic (Driving Schools, Driving Instructors and Assistant Driv-
ing Instructors) Regulations No. 1 of 2015 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1939/4 dated 02nd of November
2015 shall be corrected as follows, and the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1963/36 dated 22.04.2016 published to correct
printing mistakes of such thereof is hereby repealed.
The Section 14(d) of Part II of Page 6A of Sinhala text of Motor Traffic (Driving Schools, Driving Instructors and
Assistant Driving Instructors) Regulations No. 1 of 2015 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1939/4 dated 02nd
November 2015 shall be corrected as follows :
14(d) not be an employee of a Government or Semi Government Organization or other such Organization, those
who are involved in driving instructor under the respective Organization.
Part (d) of Section 14 of Page 8A of Tamil text shall be incorporated as follows.
(14d) not be an employee of a Government or Semi Government Organization or other such Organization, those
who are involved in driving instructor under the respective Organization.
NIMAL SIRIPALA DE SILV A,
  Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
21st June 2016.
