WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointing under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987
UNDER powers vested in me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, K. C. Logeswaran, the
Governor of the Western Province, hereby notify that with effect from 26.01.2016, Mr. Parape Arachchige Asiri Sandaruwan
Weerasekara of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service Class III, has been appointed to assigned the duties as the Western
Province Commissioner of the Co-operative Development and Registrar of the Co-operative Societies.
K. C. L
OGESWARAN,
Governor of the Western Province.
03rd of March, 2016,
Secretariat of  the Governor,
Western Province.
