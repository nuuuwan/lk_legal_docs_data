THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies
this order exists between,
Mr. M. A. Ranasinghe, Padiwatte, Kundasale, Nattarampotha, of the one part
and
Sri Lankan Airlines Limited, Airline Centre, Bandaranaike International Airport, Katunayaka, of the other part.
NOW THEREFORE I, Welatantirige Don John Seneviratne, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers
vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 o f 1968
hereby appoint Mr. S. Virithamulla, No. 128, Piriwena Road, Boralesgamuwa to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for
settlement by arbitration.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo,
03rd August, 2016.

THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Mr. M. A. Ranasinghe, Padiwatte, Kundasale, Nattarampotha, of the one part
and
Sri Lankan Airlines Limited, Airline Centre, Bandaranaike International Airport, Katunayaka, of the other part.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  the salary increase given to the employees of the Sri Lanka Nidahas Sewaka Sangamaya in terms of the Agreement made between
Sri Lankan Airlines Limited and the said union for the period from 01.11.1996 to 31.05.1999 not being given to Mr. M. A. Ranasinghe who
was employed  as an Inflight Service  Agent of the Company is just and if not so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 16th day of June, 2016.
