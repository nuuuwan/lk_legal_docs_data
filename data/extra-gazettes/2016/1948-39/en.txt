THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
            My No.: WGSWD & EIP/ACQ/KB/15. 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4/3/10/2012/LRB –323. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
to create a water reserve in the paddy field located 
between 120 bus route and the sub canal stretches 
towards Piliyandala Gangarama Road from the Maha Ela 
at Bokundara under Weras  Ganga Storm Water Drainage 
& Environment Improvement Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
Seven (07) allotments of land in total extent of 0.1752 
Hectare depicted as lot no. 180 to 186 in the Cadastral 
Map No. 521211 dated 17.12.2015 prepared by the 
Survey General situated in the Village called Bokundara 
in the Grama Niladari Division No. 575 of Bokundara in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kesbewa in 
Colombo District of Western Province and more fully 
described as follows. 
 
     1. Lot No. : 180 
 Name of the land: Could not be traced; Description: 
Home Garden (part of a temporary building available);

Extent: 0.0133 Hectare.
