SOUTHERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Southern Province Industrial Development Authority (Amendment) Statute
Be it, enacted by the Southern Province Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-
1. This Statute may be cited as the Southern Province Industrial Development Authority
(Amendment) Statute No. 01 of 2016 and shall come into operation on 03.06.2016 as the Governor has
assented to this Statute on 03.06.2016.
2. Section 4 of the Southern Province Industrial Development Authority Statute No. 01 of 2002
(hereinafter in this Statute referred to as the “Statute”) is hereby amended by the insertion immediately
after paragraph (a) thereof, the following new paragraph :-
“(aa) to encourage the formation of societies and associations by the industrialists
within the province for the promotion and advancement of the products of their
industries ;”
3. Section 6 of the Statute is hereby amended  by the insertion immediately after paragraph (17),
the following new paragraph :-
“(18) register and issue a certificate or registration to any society or association established
by the industrialists within the province for the promotion and advancement of the
products of their industries.”
