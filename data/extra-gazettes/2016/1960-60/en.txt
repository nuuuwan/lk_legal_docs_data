Appointment to the Director Board under Road Development Authority Statute No. 02 of  2003

CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Central Province Road Development Authority Statute No. 02 of 2003 of the Central Provincial Council
DISSOLVING   OF  APPOINTED  MEMBERS
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy Housing and Construction of
the Central Provincial  Council hereby announce under the powers vested in me by Paragraph No. 3(8) of Section No. 1 of
Central Provincial Road Development Authority Statute No. 02 of 2003 of Central Provincial Council, dissolve the
undermentioned member from the Director Board of the Central Province Road Development Authority with effect from
the date mentioned before his name as the said member by his own wish has resigned from his post as per paragraph
No. 3 (10) of Section 1 of the Authority Statute.
E
DIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy
Housing and Construction of the Central Provincial  Council.
