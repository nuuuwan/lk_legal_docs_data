THE   UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order establishing the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by
virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act,
(as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1948/59 of January 08, 2016) by addition immediately
after item 10 appearing in Column II under the heading “Departments” as corresponds to the item “ Faculty of Humanities
and Social Sciences” appearing in Column I under the heading “Faculty” of the Schedule to such deemed Order of the
following new item :
“11. Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice”.
LAKSHMAN  K IRIELLA ,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
16th February 2016.
