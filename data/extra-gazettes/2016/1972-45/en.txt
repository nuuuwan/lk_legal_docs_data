WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Industrial   Development   Authority   Statute   (Western   Province) No. 2 of  1994
REMOV AL FROM POSITION - MEMBER OF  DIRECTOR BOARD, INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY
(WESTERN PROVINCE)
I. Kajitan Lalith Wanigarathne, being the Western Provincial Minister of Roads, Transport, Co-operative Development and
Trade, Fisheries, Housing and Construction, Urban and Estate Sector Infrastructure Facilities, Industries and Rural Development
do hereby announce that Mr. Lionel Appuhami, member of  the  Director Board of  Western Provincial Industrial Development
Authority was removed with immediate effect from the Director Board membership of the said Western Provicial Industrial
Development Authority as per powers assigned to me under Section 29 (2) of the Industrial Development Authority Statute
(Western Province) No. 02 of 1994.
K
