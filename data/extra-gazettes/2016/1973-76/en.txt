L.D.B 21/78 (XII)
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF 1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act,
published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1093/8 of August 17, 1999 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1954/43 of February 19, 2016) which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the Wayamba
University of Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after entry 4 appearing in Column 2 which corresponds to the item
“Faculty of Applied Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry -
“Department of Engineering Technology”
LAKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
27th June, 2016.
