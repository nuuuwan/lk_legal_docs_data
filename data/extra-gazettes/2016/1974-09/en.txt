PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT, No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a vacancy in  the Northern  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Northern  Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Northern  Province Provincial Council by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated Mr. Senthilnathan
Mayooran to fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Senthilnathan Mayooran, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of   Vavuniya in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
