NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION  16  SUB  SECTION  (2D)  OF  THE  COAST  CONSERV ATION  AND
COASTAL  RESOURCE   MANAGEMENT  ACT, No.  57  OF  1981
Proposed 250 Roomed Hotel Project, Welledewala Road,  Yaddehimulla,  Unawatnua
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT   ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impacts Assessment study on proposed 250 Roomed Hotel Project
- Welledewala Road, Yaddehimulla, Unawatuna, submitted  by the Araliya Unawatuna Beach Hotel (Pvt.) Ltd., Welledewala
Road, Yaddehimulla, Unawatuna, as required by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource
Management Act, No. 57 of 1981 as amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection
by the public at the following institutions from 8.30 a. m. to 4.15 p. m. for a period of 30 days from 26.09.2016. (Except
weekends and public holidays).
1. Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment, No. 500, T. B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10 ;
2. Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor,
Maligawatte, Colombo 10 ;
3. Wildlife Conservation Department, No. 811/A, Jayanthipura Road, Battaramulla ;
4. Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa”, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla ;
5. Urban Development Authority, 6th and 7th Floors, “Sethsiripaya”, Battaramulla ;
6. District Secretariat, Galle ;
7. Divisional Secretariat, Habaraduwa ;
8. Pradeshiya Sabhawa, Habaraduwa ;
9. District Fisheries Office, Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, Galle ;
10. Southern Provincial Irrigation Department, Galle ;
11. Regional Office, Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Department, No. 274, Peellagoda,
Unawatuna.
