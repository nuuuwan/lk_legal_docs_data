THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthoney Emmanuvel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Ganekanda Mukalana’’ in total extent
of about 0.1012 Hectare, shown and described in the Advance Tracing
No. KU/ALW/2014/392 dated 14.02.2015, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Alawwa in Udukaha
Korale South Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Alawwa in Kurunegala District, North Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ganekanda Mukalana – Extent: About 0.1012
Hectare
North: Lot No. 2 
AC in FVP 463; East: Lot No. 2 AF in FVP 463;
South and West: Lot No. 2 AH in FVP 463.
