THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of below mentioned lands, depicted in Advance
Tracing No. Gam/Divu/2015/189 and dated 17.06.2015, prepared
by the Surveyor - General, situated in the Village of Badalgama,
Horakandawila in the Minor Division of Dunagaha Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Divulapitiya in Gampaha District
in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Walowitawatta – Extent: 0.1038 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Balance portion of same land;
South: Balance portion of same land and Kuda Oya; West: Road
(R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Muruthagahaowita alias Walowitawatta– Extent:
0.1251 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and Kuda Oya; East and South: Balance
portion of same land; West: Road (R.D.A.).
