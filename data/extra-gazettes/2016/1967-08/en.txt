L. D-B 8/2015
ASSISTANCE   TO   AND  PROTECTION  OF  VICTIMS  OF  CRIME   AND  WITNESSES   ACT,  No.  04 OF 2015
Order under Sub-section (2) of Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Assistance to and Protection of Victims of Crime
and Witnesses Act, No. 4 of 2015, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice, do by this Order appoint May 18, 2016, to
be the date on which the provisions of Parts I, II, III, IV , V , VI, VII, VIII and IX of the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
The order made under Sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Assistance to and Protection of Victims of Crime and
Witnesses Act, No. 4 of 2015 and published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1926/51 dated 7th August, 2015 is hereby
rescinded.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
13th May, 2016.
