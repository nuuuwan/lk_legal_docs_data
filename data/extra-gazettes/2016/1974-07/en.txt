STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14 OF  2008
Order  under Sub section  (4) of  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of
2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister
in Charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/4 dated 11th July 2011, do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/4 dated 11th July 2011, is hereby
amended to read  as follows is order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct, a luxury hotel and residential units and/or villas in the administrative
district of Hambantota, in the Southern Province” shall commence commercial operations on 31.12.2016 being a
period of seventy months (70) from February 7, 2011 being the date on which the project Agreement between the
Board of Investment of Sri Lanka and the Project Company has been executed.”
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
24th June 2016.

STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14 OF  2008
Order  under Sub section  (4) of  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of
2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister
in Charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/22 dated 19th December 2012, do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/22 dated 19th December 2012, is
hereby amended to read  as follows is order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct and operate a 475 roomed city  hotel with 90 serviced apartment at
No. 116, Galle Road, Colombo 3 in the Western Province” shall commence commercial operations within a
period of sixty five months from the date of the project agreement signed with the Board of Investment of Sri
Lanka on the 19th of July 2012 and ending on the 31st December 2017”
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
24th June 2016.

STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14 OF  2008
Order  under Sub section  (4) of  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of
2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister
in Charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1782/28 dated 31st October 2012, do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1782/28 dated 31st October 2012, is hereby
amended to read  as follows is order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct, build  and operate a Mixed Utility Complex including a luxury hotel at
Fort, Colombo 01 in the Western Province shall commence commercial operations on 31.12.2021 within a time
period from the date of approval of the Mixed Use Project as  a Strategic Development Project under the said
Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008 as amended.”
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
1st July 2016.
