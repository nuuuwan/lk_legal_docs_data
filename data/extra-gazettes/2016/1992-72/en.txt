ORDER   UNDER   SECTION   22(A)   OF   GREATER   COLOMBO   ECONOMIC   COMMISSION   LAW
(BOARD   OF   INVESTMENT   LAW) No. 04   OF   1978   AS   AMENDED   BY   ACT,   No.   43  OF   1980
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 22(a) of Greater Colombo Economic Commission Law (Board of
Investment) No. 04 of 1978 as amended by Act, No. 43 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, upon the recommendation of the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of development strategies and
international trade hereby declare areas described in the Schedule below to be a licensed zones for the purpose of this
Act.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
November 08, 2016.
Schedule I
All that divided and defined allotment of land depicted as  Lot No. 1 of Plan No. 476 dated 27th April 1999 made
by Mr. H. K. Mahinda, Licensed Surveyor and leveller for the land called E-dallawatta - Serapis farm (being a part of land
No. T. P. 118723) (being a resurvey and an amalgamation of Lot No. 1 and Lot No. 2 of pre tracing No. 99/01 dated 12th
April  1999 made by Mr. H. K. Mahinda, Licensed Surveyor and Leveller) situated in the village of Kulipitiya in the
Grama Niladhari Division of Serapis watta No. 933 in Dambadeni Hathpattu west in Udapola Orthota Korale, East, in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Polgahawela in Kurunegala District in North Western Province containing in extent of
fifty acres (50A., 0R., 0P.) or twenty point two  three four three Hectares (20.2343 Hectares) in extent and all buildings
and everything else standing thereon.
The said Land is bounded as follows :
North : Kuda Oya and reservation for railway line ;
East : Balance portion of Serapis farm ;
South : Balance portion of Serapis farm and main road ;
West : Main road, Road, Ihalagama, Jana Udagama  and Kuda Oya.
