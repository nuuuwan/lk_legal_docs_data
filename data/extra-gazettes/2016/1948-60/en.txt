THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1567/23 of September 19, 2008 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1909/21 of April 07, 2015), which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139
of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the item
“Department of Limnology” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Fisheries
and Marine Sciences and Technology” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following item :-
“Department of Limnology and Water Technology”
