THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                                 My. No.: 6/3/7/15/5/8 
Land Ministry’s Ref No. : LD /05/2007/MV/110 
  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose :- 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Public purpose means the land Acquisition for the 
Moragahakanda & Kaluganga Development Project. 
 
The lands described below, shown in ToPoPP No. 17 
of supplement 33 in sheet No. 88 , approved by Surveyor 
General on 08.07.2016 situated at Thalagoda village (a 
portion) in E 407 A Thalagoda Grama Niladhari Division, 
Gangala Pallesiya Pattuwa of  Naula Divisional 
Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central Province, 
 
1. Lot No. 3717  
Name of Land: Kumbukgollayaya; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State, encroached by Kurukohugama 
Gedara Heenbanda of Thalagoda Junction, Kadawatha; 
Extent: 0.0198 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 3718  
Name of Land: Kumbukgollayaya; Description: Hena; 
Claimant: State, encroached by Kurukohugama Gedara 
Heenbanda of Thalagoda Junction, Kadawatha; Extent: 
0.0142 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 3720  
Name of Land: Kumbukgollayaya; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State, encroached by Kurukohugama 
Gedara Heenbanda of Thalagoda Junction, Kadawatha; 
Extent: 0.0416 Hectare. 
 
4. Lot No. 3721  
Name of Land: Kumbukgollayaya; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State, encroached by Kurukohugama 
Gedara Heenbanda of Thalagoda Junction, Kadawatha; 
Extent: 0.2284 Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 3722  
Name of Land: Kumbukgollayaya; Description: Hena; 
Claimant: State, encroached by Kurukohugama Gedara 
Heenbanda of Thalagoda Junction, Kadawatha; Extent: 
0.1017 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents before me at 
09.00 a.m. on  the 19th day of October 2016 at the Naula 
Divisonal Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13 day of 
October 2016, the nature of their interests in the Land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
