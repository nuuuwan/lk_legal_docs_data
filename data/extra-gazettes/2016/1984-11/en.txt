NOTICE No : 81/0088 Galle District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 810027 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Thalapitiya (Block 
03) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Divi-
sion of 99A - Thalapitiya in the Divisional Secretariat Division of 
Galle  in the District of Galle in the Province of Southern should 
submit his claim to the undersigned before 06th October, 2016 In 
the event of failure  to submit any claim before the above-
mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to 
determine title to the land and accordingly publish such determi-
nation in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the 
above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 810027 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals‚Äô office.
