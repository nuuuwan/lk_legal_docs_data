CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
REGULATION imposed under 20th Clause of Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute Number 01 of 2000 and 24(2)
(1) (xii), (xiii) and 24(3) Clauses of Passenger Transportation Statute Number 04 of 1992.
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Development, Transportation, Power and Energy and Housing and Construction
Central Province, as per the powers vested on me under 20th Clause of Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute
Number 01 of 2000 and 24(2) (1) (xii), (xiii)  and clauses of  Passenger Transportation Statute Number 04 of 1992, impose the
following regulation with effect from the date of  publishing of this Gazette.
As per the recommendations made in the interim report issued on 25.10.2016 by the Department of Transport and Logistics
Management, University of  Moratuwa, who regulated the pilot project named "SAHASARA" which was initially implemented
on two routes from 20.07.2016 to 19.10.2016 with the objective of introducing a new transportation system aimed at minimizing
the difficulties faced by the passengers due to unhealthy competition among the bus operators, the so called ‘‘SAHASARA’’
Project is to be implemented on all routes where buses registered under Central Provincial Passenger Transport Service
Authority shall transport passengers.
E
DIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Central Provincial Road
Development, Transport, Power and Energy and
Housing and Construction.
At the Office of the Minister of Central Provincial Road
Development, Transport, Power and Energy and
Housing and Construction,
Kandy.
27th October, 2016.
