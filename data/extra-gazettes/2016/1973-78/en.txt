PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
  CIVIL   AVIATION  AUTHORITY   OF   SRI   LANKA   ACT,  No.  34  OF  2002
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 9 of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002, and
in pursuance of the Cabinet decision taken on 14th June 2016 on the Cabinet Memorandum No. 16/1031/709/035-1 dated
31st May 2016, on “Operation and Maintenance of Batticaloa Airport” submitted by me, I, Nimal Siripala De Silva, Minister
of Transport and Civil Aviation, do by this Order, transfer and vest in the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka the Batticaloa
airport which it listed under Second Schedule Aerodromes to the aforementioned Act with immediate effect.
N
IMAL SIRIPALA DE SILV A (M.P.),
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Ministry of Transport and Civil Aviation,
Sethsiripaya Stage II,
Battaramulla,
30th June, 2016.
