SRI  LANKA   EXPORT   DEVELOPMENT   ACT,   No.  40  OF  1979
Order  under Section 14
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Sujeewa
Senasinghe, Acting Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade with the concurrence of the Minister of
Finance, do by this Order declare that, the Cess shall be charged, levied and paid on import of every article specified in
Column II and III of the Schedule hereto, corresponding to the import of every article specified under the following H. S.
Headings mentioned in the Column I of the Schedule of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1941/33 dated
20.11.2015 issued under the Section 14(1) of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in
Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from 29th June 2016.
S
UJEEWA S ENASINGHE,
Acting Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Ministry of Development Strategies and International Trade,
Colombo 01.
28th June 2016.
