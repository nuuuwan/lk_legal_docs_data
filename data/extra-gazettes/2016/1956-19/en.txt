The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
THE  EXCISE  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  52)
Excise Notification No. 988
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32 read with Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as
amended from time to time, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this declare that the rules set out hereto
shall come into force on 01.04.2016.
The Excise Notification No. 972 published in the Gazette  Extraordinary No. 1885/44 of  24.10.2014 is hereby
rescinded.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
02nd March 2016.
RULES
1. There shall be a  imposed and charged excise duty  on the articles specified in Column I and on the respective
quantities in the Column II at the rates specified in   Column III of the Schedule I.
2. A person who wishes to import Ethyl alcohol shall submit an application form set out in accordance with the
Schedule II to the Commissioner General of Excise.
3. If the Commissioner General of Excise is satisfied about the application forwarded by the applicant, the
approval shall be sent to the Controller of Imports and Exports with the form set out in accordance with the Schedule III.
