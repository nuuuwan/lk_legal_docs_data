THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
ORDINANCE  made by the University Grants Commission under Section 91 read with Section 18 of the Universities Act.
No. 16 of 1978.
Secretary,
University Grants Commission.
Colombo,
24th August 2016.
Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Universities Provident Fund (Amendment) Ordinance No. 1 of 2016 and
shall come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Universities Provident Fund Ordinance No. 1 of 2015 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1906/8 of
March 17, 2015, is hereby amended as follows :-
(1) in paragraph 6, by the repeal of sub-paragraph (3)(a) thereof and the substitution therefor, of the following
sub-paragraph :-
“(a) the Vice-Chairman of the Commission ;” ;
(2) in paragraph 10,-
(a) by the repeal of item (i) of sub-paragraph 2(a) thereof and the substitution therefor of the following
item :-
“(i) the Chairman of the Commission or his nominee who shall be the Chairman of the Board ;” ;
(b) by the repeal of item (ii) of sub-paragraph 2(b) thereof and the subsitution therefore of the following
item:-
“(ii) a contributor to the Fund who has a good exposure in any one of the fields of economics,
management or financial administration, selected by the Commission out of three persons
nominated by the Secretary, in consultation with the Chairman and the Vice-Chairman of the
Commission ;”.
