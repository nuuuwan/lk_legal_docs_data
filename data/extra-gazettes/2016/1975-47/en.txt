UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Horizon College of Business and Technology (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under  the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 62877, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the
Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 1944/ 3 of  December 07, 2015 by the
addition immediately after the entry ‘‘Bachelor of Management Honours in Marketing’’ appearing in Column I and it’s
corresponding entry in Column II  thereof, of the following, subject to the conditions specified below :-
CONDITIONS
Horizon College of  Business and Technology (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the ‘‘Institute”)
Shall -
(1) in respect of  the Bachelor of  Education programme adhere to the minimum University entry qualification
of minimum 3 passes at the General Certificate of Education Examination (Advanced Level) stipulated by
the University Grants Commission or minimum of 3 passes in London (Advanced Level) Examination in
one sitting.
(2) in respect of  the Bachelor of  Science in Information Technology and Bachelor of Science Honours in
Information Technology shall comply with the following requirements :-
The Institute shall, submit the following to the specified Authority :-
(a) within three months from the publication of this Order clearly state the results of the foreign
examinations qualifications which are equivalent to the General Certificate of Education Examination
(Advanced Level) conducted by the Department of Examination, Sri Lanka.
(b) prior to the commencement of the Degree programme strengthen the Academic staff with persons
who have the relevant research postgraduate qualifications (PhD), (MPhil).
