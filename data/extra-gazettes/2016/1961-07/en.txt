(2) Schedule III of that Notification is hereby amended as follows : -
a. By the repeal of Paragraph 5 and the substitution therefor, of the following :-
“5. A non-refundable fee of Rupees Ten Thousand (Rs. 10,000) shall be paid when applying for a new
license or renewal of license” ;
b. By the repeal of Paragraph 6 and the substitution therefor, of the following :-
“6. Every license shall be valid for a period of one year. The License which is issued in the middle of
the year shall be issued for the remaining period of the year” ;
c. By the repeal of Paragraph 7 and the substitution therefor, of the following :-
“7. Every license issued under the Excise Ordinance shall be subject to a license fee set out in Schedule
I and such fee shall be charged on a pro-rate basis in the case of a new license issued for a part of a
year. The payment shall be made at least seven working days prior to the date of commencement of
the relevant financial year. However, if the fee is not received by the relevant Divisional Secretary at
least seven working days after the commencement of a financial year, an additional period of four
weeks shall be allowed to make payment subject to a penalty of 1% of the value of license fee. After
the period of four weeks, Penalty shall be 3% of the value of license fee.” ;

EXCISE   NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that the Excise Notification No. 984 issued by the Honourable Minister of Finance by virtue of the
powers vested in him by Section 25 read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as ammended from time
to time, and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1945/17 dated 15.12.2015 to cancel the Excise Notification
No. 926 of Gazette Extraordinary No. 1680/24 dated 19.11.2010 and Excise Notification No. 974 of Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1901/19 dated 13.02.2015 effective from 15.12.2015, was passed by parliament on 10.03.2016.
