THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub – section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land marked as Lots ‘A’ and ‘B’ containing in
extent about 7.5036 Hectares, depicted in Surveyor General’s Advance
Tracing No. MA/ MLT/2011/346 dated 30.04.2013 situated at
Madawala (FVP 23) Village, in Gampahasiya Pattuwa minor Division,
Matale Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central
Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – German Watta – Extent About : 7.4441 Hectares
North: Remaining portion of this land (road); East: Remaining portion
of this land; South: Road and German Watta claimed by P. M.
Gunasekara ; West: German Watta claimed by P. M. Gunasekara.
02. Lot ‘B’ – German Watta – Extent About : 0.0595 Hectare
North: Road; East and South: German Watta claimed by P . M.
Gunasekara ; West: Road.
