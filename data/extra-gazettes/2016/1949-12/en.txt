WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Announcement made under Section 268 of Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with
Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
IT is hereby announced that the By-laws relating to Regularization, Control, Administration and Levying Charges on
Private Fairs conducted within Negombo Municipal Council Area of Authority ; By laws relating to Control and Levying
Charges on laying water pipes and drawing underground cables by various Government/Semi-government/Corporation/
Boards, Companies and persons in streets controlled by the Municipal Council in Negombo Municipal Council Area of
Authority ; By laws relating to Control and Levying Charges on the tasks of fixing pillars and towers constructed by
various Government/Semi-government/Corporations/Boards, Companies and persons in streets controlled by the said
Council in Negombo Municipal Council Area of Authority ; drafted by the Negombo Municipal Council under Section
272(32) of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252), approved by the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of
the subject of Local Government under Section 268 of Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2
of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and published in the Extraordinary Gazette
Paper No. 1622/21 dated 07.10.2009 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, were adopted  by the Western
Province Provincial Council on 22nd September 2015 as per provisions further stated in Section 268 of Municipal
Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12
of 1989.
