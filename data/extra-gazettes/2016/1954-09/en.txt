THE   WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notice
APPOINTMENT   OF   WAGES   BOARD   SECRETARY
IT is hereby notified that the Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint Labour Officer,
Mr. Hangili Gurunnehelage Wasantha Gunawardana to be the Secretary of Wages Boards for the under-mentioned trades
established under Section 8 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) in place of the Wages Boards Secretary Mrs.
Walpitage Dona Biyanka Sandamali Walpita until further order.
This appointment will be effective from 09.02.2016.
S. M. G
OTABAYA JAYARATHNE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and
Trade Union Relations.
Colombo,
09th February 2016.
