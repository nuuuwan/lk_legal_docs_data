THE  CONSTITUTION   OF  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Article 45(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka I, hereby declare that the following functions of the subjects under the purview of Ministry of Telecommunication
and Digital Infrastructure shall be assigned to Deputy Minister of Telecommunication and Digital Infrastructure.
(1) Monitoring and evaluation of projects with regard to the subjects of Telecommunication and Digital
Infrastructure.
(2) Implementation of programs for promotion of Computer Literacy.
(3) Labour relations of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC.
(4) Introduce new IT Parks and develop IT Parks in Jaffna and Mannar.
H
ARIN FERNANDO (M. P .),
Minister of Telecommunication
and Digital Infrastructure.
