Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: BPN/L/03/2/17. 
Ministry of Lands and Land Development’s Reference 
No.: 4/3/9/2014/IR/116. 
 
I  hereby inform under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the 
Land Acquisition that the Government  has proposed to  
acquire the under mentioned lands for public purpose:- 
 
       Particulars  of lands proposed to be acquired: 
 
A portion of land about 0.4085 Hectare in extent from 
the lands situated in the Village called Thambadeniya in 
Thambadeniya Grama Niladhari Division in Maha Oya 
Divisional Secretariat Division in Ampara District 
depicted as Lot No. 2052 in the Interim Plan No. 125 of 
primary Plan No. Topo P.P. 20 and bounded as follows 
within the following boundaries: 
 
1. Lot No. 2052 —  Thambadeniya —  extent 0.4085 
Hectare. 
 
On the North by: Lot Nos. 2054 and 829; On the East by: 
Lot Nos. 829, 705 and 2051; On the South by: Lots Nos. 
2051, 198, 2054 and 2053; On the West by: Lots Nos. 
2054 and 2053. 
 
 
All the Persons  who claim ownership of the land or 
those who were authorized by the owners of land should be 
present personally with their documents pertaining to the 
land on 11th January 2017 at 10.00 a.m. at the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Maha Oya to be interviewed. 
All those who claim ownership for the land should 
submit their application in duplicate from 04th January 
2017 with the details of the nature of ownership, the 
reasons for requesting compensation, amount of 
compensation and the way of calculation calculated the 
amount for compensation. The deeds of the land claims 
ownership should be submitted for inspection. 
 
 
 
  B. V. SUDARSHANA GUNASENA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Bintennapattu North, 
  Maha Oya.
