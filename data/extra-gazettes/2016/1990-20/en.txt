COMMUNITY   BASED  CORRECTION  ACT,  No.  46 OF 1999
Order under Sub section (3) of Section 1
BY virtue of the power vested in me by Section 1 of the Community Based Correction Act, No. 46 of 1999, I, D. M.
Swaminathan, Minister of Prison Reform, Rehabilitation, Resettlement and Hindu Religious Affairs do by this order that
the provisions in Section II of  this Act, concerning the Judicial Divisions specified in the Schedule hereto should be
implemented with effect from 01st of January 2016.
D. M. S
WAMINA THAN,
Minister of Prison Reforms,
Rehabilitation, Resettlement and Hindu Religious Affairs,
Colombo,
14th October, 2016.
