I,  hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
to create a water tank in the upper area of Bellanwila 
Divulpitiya area under Weras Ganga Storm Water 
Drainage & Environment Improvement Project. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:

1.4032 Hectares depicted as Lot Nos. 01 to 36 in the 
Preliminary Plan No. Co. 9569 dated 01.08.2015 prepared 
by the Surveyor General situated in the Villages called 
Pepiliyana in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 535B of 
Divulpitiya West in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kesbewa in Colombo District of Western Province and 
morefully described as follows.
