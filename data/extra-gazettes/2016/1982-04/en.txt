THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare under Sub section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose  and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Sri Sudharmarama Purana Vihara in
total extent of about 0.0051 Hectare depicted in the Advance Tracing
No.: MR/HKM/2014/620 dated 01.06.2015 prepared by the
Surveyor General situated in the village of Udupeellegoda (FVP 497)
in Kandabada Pattu (East) Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Hakmana, Mathara District Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Sri Sudharmarama Purana Vihara – Extent:
0.0051 Hectare
North: Canal and Road (Pradeshiya Saba); East: Road (Pradeshiya
Saba), Sri Sudharmarama Purana Vihara land claimed by ven.
Malimboda Somaloka Thero; South: Sri Sudharmarama Purana Vihara
land claimed by ven. Malimboda Somaloka Thero; West:
Sri Sudharmarama Purana Vihara land claimed by ven. Malimboda
Somaloka Thero and canal.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
     Minister of Lands.
