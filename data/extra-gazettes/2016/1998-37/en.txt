No. 1998/37 –  FRIDAY DECEMBER, 23, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
AMENDMENT 
 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
     
My Reference No.: PLDS/LND/04/ACQ/71. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub -section (1) of Section         
(7) of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following lands which are required 
for a public purpose. 
 
    I hereby make the following amendment to the Gazette 
Extraordinary  bearing Number 1305/15 dated 
09.09.2003. 
 
    Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired :  
 
    One (01) allotment below name land shown in Final 
Village Plan No. 209, Supplement No. 12, prepared by 
the Superintendent of Surveys, Rathnapura District 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Udapattuwe 
Sannasgama in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Pelmadulla in the District of Rathnapura in 
Sabaragamuwa Province. 
      01. Lot No. 445 
Name of the Land: Manakumburegoda; Description : 
Open waste land; Claimant : State; Original Claimant : Rathna 
Malwatta, Lellopitiya, Sannasgama; Extent : 0.0051 Hectare. 
 
      All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Pelmadulla 
of the 16th, January 2017 at 9.30 a. m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 16th, January 2017 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 SUGATH SISIRA KUMARA, 
 Divisional Secretary and 
 Acquiring Officer, 
 Pelmadulla. 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat,  
Pelmadulla, 
21st December, 2016.
