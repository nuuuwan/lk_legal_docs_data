Notice under Section 7 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition  Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotment of land called in extent 2.5962 of Hectare 
shown as lot 1823 in FVP 29 dated 25.05.2016 prepared 
by the Surveyor General situated in with in the 
Pradeshiya Sabha limits of Trincomalee No. 244N 
Abayapura Grama Niladhari Division, Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Town and Gravets, Trincomalee 
District and bounded as follows. Details Attached. 
 
01. Lot No. 1823 —  Extent: 2.5962 Hectares 
North: Lots 979, 34; East: Lots 34, 31; South: 
Lots 31, 952, 951, 979, 950, 949; West: Lots 952, 
951, 979, 950, 949, 979. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat -
Trincomalee on the 03rd day of November 2016 at        
10.00 a.m. and you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13 day of 
October 2016 the  nature of their interests in the land the 
particulars of their claims for compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.  
 
All deeds and documents in Support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the Inquiry  
  
 J. S. ARULRAJ,
