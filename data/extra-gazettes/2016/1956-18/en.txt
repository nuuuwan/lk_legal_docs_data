FINANCE  ACT,   No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963, I, Ravi

No. 29 of 1961, with effect from, 02nd March Two Thousand and Sixteen, the properties described in the Schedule
hereto.
