THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : MOR/LAN/01/FI/202
Land Ministry Reference No. : 4 - 3/06/2014/HW/310
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 209 - 210 (Lots. 02) allotments in total extent of
about 0.0243 Hectare of  land in Cadastral Map No. 520214,
Block No. 03, Sheet No. 06, dated 25.01.2016 prepared by the
Surveyor-Superintendent on behalf of Surveyor- Colombo,
General situated in the Village of Moratuwella North (Within
the Municipal Council Limits of Moratuwa) in the  Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Moratuwa Divisional Secretary
Division in Colombo District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 209
Name of land: Assmt. No. 02 (A  part) Old Galle Road;
Description: Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: – Not
mention; Extent: 0.0107 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 210
Name of land: Assmt. No. 2 (A  part) Old Galle Road;
Description: Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Hewa
Dewage Saiman Fernando; Extent: 0.0136 Hectare.
        All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of Moratuwa
on 11th day of May , 2016 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th
day of May, 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
                                                                        K. C. NIROSHAN,
Divisional Secretary,
Moratuwa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Moratuwa.
