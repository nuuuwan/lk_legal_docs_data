SOUTHERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the Department hereinafter referred to as “Southern Provincial Department of Sports” is established
from 18.04.2016 to function under the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Rural Development, Cultural and Art Affairs, Social
Welfare, Probation and Child Care Services, Women Affairs and Home Economic Enhancement, Housing and Construction
and Manpower and Employment of the Southern Province in order to exercise powers vested in the Provincial Council under
the 13th amendment to the Constitution.
Dr. H
EMAKUMARA  N ANAY AKKARA,
Hon. Governor of the Southern Province.
18th April 2016.
