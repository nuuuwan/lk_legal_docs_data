SPECIAL   COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,   No.  48  OF  2007
Order  under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Lakshman
Yapa Abeywardana, Acting Minister of Finance, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose
in respect of list of the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 03 months
commencing from 1st July 2016, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of  Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy
Act, No. 48 of 2007.
L
AKSHMAN YAPA ABEYWARDANA,
Acting Minister of Finance
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01.
30th June 2016.
