EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT NO. 28 
OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment Act, No.28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the lands described in the 
Schedule given below for the Restoration of, Bus Depot, 
Kegalle. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired:- 
 
Eight allotments  of land described in the Scheduled 
attached below depicted in Priliminary Plan No. Ke. 3488 
prepared by Surveyor General, Kegalle situated in 
Waldeniya Grama Niladhari Division in the boundaries of 
Kegalle Urban council and Pradeshiya Sabha in the 
Divisional Secretariaat Division in Kegalle District of 
Sabaragamuva Province. 
 
Dates of Inquiries —   
Lot No. 01— 04 20th of June 2016—  from 9.00 a.m. 
Lot No. 05— 08 of 21st June 2016—  from 9.00 a.m.
