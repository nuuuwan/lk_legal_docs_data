No. 1958/63–  FRIDAY, MARCH,  18, 2016 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My No: KDS/HAR/4/2/1/40.  
Land Ministry’s Ref. No: 4/3/9/2014/WS /28. 
 
I  hereby give Public notice under Sub -section (1) of 
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that  
the Government intends to acquire the following Land/
Servitude which is required for construction of 
Kulugammana Pump House of Pathadumbara —  Kandy 
North Combined Service  Water Supply Project. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0250 
Hectare and depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Maha/6534, 
Surveyed and prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Surveyor of Kandy District, situated in village of 
Kulugammana, in Minor Division of Kulugammana 
Siyapathtuwa in Harispathtuwa Divisional Secretariat 
Division in  Kandy District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows. 
1. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Pansal Waththa  alias Pansal 
Aramba; Claimant: Government;  Extent: 0.0250 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land is hereby 
Personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretariat Harispathtuwa, on the 25th 
day of May 2016 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
25th day of May, 2016 the nature of their interests in the 
Land, the Particulars or their claims for Compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land/Servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
                         R. M. P. S. B. RATHNAYAKE, 
                                      Divisional Secretary, 
                 Harispaththuwa. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Harispaththuwa. 
16th  March 2016
