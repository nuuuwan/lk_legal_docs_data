CIVIL  A VIATION  AUTHORITY  OF   SRI   LANKA  ACT,   No.  34  OF  2002
Order under Section 36
ACQUISITION  OF   LAND   FOR  DEVELOPMENT  OF  DOMESTIC  AIRPORTS
MEMBERS of the public are hereby informed that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 36 of the Civil Aviation
Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002, I, Nimal Siripala De Silva, being the Minister in charge of the subject of Civil
Aviation has approved acquisition of lands specified in the following Schedule to the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka
for the purpose of development of domestic airports as proposed in the National Budget - 2016 passed by Parliament.
N
IMAL SIRIPALA DE SIL V A,
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Ministry of Transport and Civil Aviation,
Sethsiripaya Stage II,
Battaramulla,
01st March 2016.
