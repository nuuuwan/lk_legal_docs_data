THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands here
by declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Description of Servitude:
The servitude to be acquired for the construction of access road to
the production well and pump house for the augmentation activities
under  the Nivithigala water supply scheme.
Description of Land:
An allotment of  land called Gangaaddarawatta in extent of 0.0538
hectare depicted in the Advance Tracing No. R/NVT/2014/464 dated
23.09.2014 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
of Nivithigala, Nawadun Korale Mada Pattu Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Nivithigala, in the  District of
Rathnapura in the Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
Lot A - Gangaaddarawatta - Extent : about 0.0538 Hectares
North: Gangaaddarawatta claimed by W. G . Nimal Jayathissa and
S.U. Perera Thennakoon; East: Road and Gangaaddarawatta claimed
by W. G . Nimal Jayathissa; South: Gangaaddarawatta claimed by W.
G . Nimal Jayathissa; West: Road and land claimed by W. G . Nimal
Jayathissa and S.U. Perera Thennakoon.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
       Minister of Lands.
