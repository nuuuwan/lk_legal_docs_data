CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Regulation imposed under the 20th Statement of the Central Provincial Passenger Transport Services
Authority Statute No. 01 of 2000 and the Statements No. 24(2)(1)(xiii) and 24(3) of Passenger Transporting
Statute No. 04 of 1992
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Central Provincial Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy and Housing
and Construction regulate the following with effect from 22.08.2016 as per the powers bestowed on me by the 20th
Statement to be read along with the Statement No. 24(2)(1)(xiii) of the Passenger Transporting Statute No. 04 of 1992 and
the Statements No. 19(N) and (T) of the Central Provincial Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute No. 01 of 2000.
REGULATIONS
A passenger travelling in a bus licensed under the Central Provincial Passenger Transport Services Authority,
should acquire a valid ticket approved by the transport services authority which indicates the amount relevant to one's
destination and the starting point and the end point, and should also keep it with oneself from the begining to the end of the
journey. If not, he/she will be subjected to a local service charge of Rs. 250 plus double the amount of the full fare.
