THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984 (as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1964/10 of April 26, 2016), which specifies the Faculties and the
Departments of the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition
immediately after entry  “14. Department of Surgery” appearing in Column II  which corresponds to the item “Faculty of
Medicine” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following  entry :-
“15. Department of Anaesthesiology”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th November 2016.
