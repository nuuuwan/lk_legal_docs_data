AMENDMENT  TO  THE  MEDICAL  SERVICE  MINUTE  OF   SRI   LANKAN  HEALTH   SERVICE
THE Medical Service Minute of the Sri Lankan Health Service published in the Government Gazette No. 1883/17 dated
11.10.2014 is hereby amended in such a manner as to incorporate the following note immediately after Section 7.2 of the
Medical Service Minute of the Sri Lankan Health Service.
“Note :
The recruitement age set down in Section 7.2 will not be applicable only to the persons mentioned in the Orders
of the Supreme Court in SC. FR. Application Nos. 135/07 and 136/07, who are presently serving as Registered Medical
Officers/Assistant Medical Officers in the Ministry of Health or the Provincial Ministries of Health, provided that they
fulfill the criteria laid down by the Supreme Court in the Order dated 07.03.2014 as amended by the Order dated
16.07.2014.”
Other provisions of the aforesaid Service Minute shall remain in force unchanged.
                                                           By order of the Public Service Commission.
H. M. G
AMINI SENEVIRA THNA,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
