THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land called "Broadland Estate" in depicted as
amended Advance Tracing No. Nu/AMB/2016/49, dated on
06.05.2016, situated in the Village of  Pitawala,  in Ambagamuwa
Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Ambagamuwa Korale in Nuwara Eliya District, Central Province and
bounded as follows.
01. Lot 'A' –  Broadland Estate – Extent: 1.5638 Hectares
North: Delpath Oya and Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas
Jayasinghe; East and South: Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas
Jayasinghe; West: Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas Jayasinghe
and Delpath Oya.
02. Lot 'B' –  Broadland Estate – Extent: 0.0333 Hectare
North: Delpath Oya; East:  Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas
Jayasinghe; South: Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas Jayasinghe
and Lot C of Advance Tracing No. NU/AMB/2016/49; West:
Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas Jayasinghe and Delpath Oya.
03. Lot 'C' –  Broadland Estate – Extent: 0.0437 Hectare
North: Broadland Estate claimed by Thomas Jayasinghe and Lot B
of Advance Tracing  No. NU/AMB/2016/49; East and South: Broadland
Estate claimed by Thomas Jayasinghe; West: Broadland Estate claimed
by Thomas Jayasinghe and Road (R.D.A).
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
