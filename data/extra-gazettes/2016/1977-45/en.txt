PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
1A
SOUTHERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointment  made by  the  Governor  of  the  Southern   Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 154F (1), (4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the constitution of  the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Hemakumara Wickramathilake Nanayakkara, the Governor of  the Southern
Province, do hereby announce that I have made the following appointment for the seventh provincial council.
1. Hon. Hewa Wellalage Gunasena - Southern Provincial Minister of  Fisheries, Animal
Production and Development, Environment, Rural Industries,
Electricity and Rural and Estate Infrastructure facilities
Development with effect from 20th July 2016.
D
R. H EMAKUMARA  W ICKRAMATHILAKE  N ANAYAKKARA,
Governor of the  Southern Province.
Governor’s Office,
Lower Dickson Road,
Galle,
20th  July, 2016.
