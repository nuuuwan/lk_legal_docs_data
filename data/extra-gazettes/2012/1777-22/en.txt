 SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province
I,  W. J. M. Lokubandara, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, by virtue of the powers vested in me in act of article
154(F), (1), (4) & (5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka has made following apointments for
the Sixth Provincial Council of Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council with effect from 24th September, 2012.
01. Hon.  Mahipala Herath - Chief Minister and the Minister of Law and Order, Finance and
Planning, Local Government, Health, Indigenous Medicine and
Women's Affairs, Co-operative, Trade and Food, Transport,
Estate Welfare, Environment, Media and Housing and
Construction.
02. Hon. Athula Kumara Rahubaddha - Minister of Provincial Roads Development, Rural Infrastructure,
Tourism and Sports and Youth Affairs.
03. Hon.  Sri Lal Wickramasinghe - Minister of Lands, Provincial Irrigation, Agriculture, Animal
Production and Animal Health and Fisheries.
04. Hon.  Ranjith Bandara - Minister of Social Service, Probation and Child Care, Rural
Development and Rural Industries.
05. Hon.  A. A. Prabath Bhanu Munipriya - Minister of Education, Information Technology and Cultural
Affairs.
W. J.M. L
OKUBANDARA,
Governor of
Sabaragamuwa Province.
