1A
   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  under  Section 10 A  (1)  (a)
PANADURA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning  Officer of the Pandura  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that  Mr.  H. Jagath Pushpa Kumara Perera of the Panadura
Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that   Pradeshiya  Sabha  as he has ceased  to be a member of the
People’s Liberation Front.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
04th July  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   BERUWALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Beruwala   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending  meeting of
the Council  for a period of three months  without permission  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Wellappuli Arachchige Don Ajantha Chanaka,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Beruwala
Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
