SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation by the Honourable  Governor of the Sabaragamuwa  Province
KNOW ye that by virtue of  the powers vested in me by  Section 154B  Sub- section (10) (a) of  the Thirteenth Amendment to
the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Wijesinghe Jayaweera Mudiyanselage Lokubandara,
Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do by this Proclamation summon the Provincial Council  of the  Sabaragamuwa
Province to  meet at the Provincial Council Chamber in the Provincial Council Secretariat, Ratnapura on the 23rd day of
October Two Thousand Twelve at 10.00 a. m.
 Given at  Ratnapura this 04th day of October Two Thousand Twelve.
W.  J. M.  L
OKUBANDARA,
Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
