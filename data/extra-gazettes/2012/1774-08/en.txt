L. D. B – 21/78 VIII.
UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 20 (4) (a)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph (a) of subsection (4) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, on being satisfied that the
work and the administration of the Higher Educational Institutions and Institutes for Higher Learning as attached to them as
specified in the Schedule to the order made by me under the aforesaid  Act and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1772/ 5 of August 21, 2012 have been restored to normal conditions, do by this Order, rescind the said order with effect
from September 6th, 2012.
DISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA
BANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
04th September, 2012.
