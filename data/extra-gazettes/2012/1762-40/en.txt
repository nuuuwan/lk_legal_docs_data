CUSTOMS  NOTIFICATION
Customs  Ordinance
 SECTION 6A
PAYMENT   OF   A  FEE   FOR   A  SPECIAL   SERVICE  OF   CLEARANCE  OF  GEM  PARCELS   AT  AIRPORT
WHEREAS  Hon. Minister of Finance, by an order made by him, had determined that a service, granted to an incoming
passenger to clear the gem parcels brought by him at Airport through a special Customs procedure designed by the Director
General of Customs, shall be a special service within the meaning of section 6A (a) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
as amended by Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 83 of 1988;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of the powers vested in me by Section 6A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), I,
Neville Goonawardena, Director General of Customs, do by this Notification specify that, with effective from 15.06.2012, a
payment shall be made for such special service at a rate of Sri Lanka Rupees equivalent to Two Hundred United States Dollars
(US $ 200) in respect of each Customs Goods Declaration (Cusdec) made by the passenger.
