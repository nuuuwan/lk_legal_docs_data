THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: LEI-CDP/SD/R/N1-1.
I hereby give notice under Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act,

Three (3) alloments of land in extent of about 0.0039 Hectare,

9105 dated 08.07.2010 prepared by the Surveyor General situated
(within the Municipal Council limits of Dehiwala – Mt. Lavinia) in

Ratmalana Colombo District Western Province and more fully
described below:
01. Lot No. 07
Name of land: Assmt. No. 63/12, Sri Dharmarama Road;
Description: Road; Original Claimant: B. A. I. Perera, 63/12, Sri
Dharmarama Road, Ratmalana; Extent: 0.0036 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 14
Name of land: Assmt. No. 81/3, Sri Dharmarama Road;
Description: Road; Original Claimant: S. S. Jayasinghe, 81/3, Sri
Dharmarama Road, Ratmalana; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 15
Name of land: Assmt. No. 61/25, Sri Dharmarama Road;
Description: Road; Original Claimant: S. S. Silva, 61/25, Sri
Dharmarama Road, Ratmalana; Extent: 0.0002 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me on 20.06.2012 at Lunawa Environment Improvement and
Community Development Project Office, No. 51, Pirivena Road,
Ratmalana at the time intimated to them in due cours and are  hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
15th June 2012, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
S. Weerabahu,
Land Acquiring Officer.

Community Development Project,
51, Pirivena Road,
