EXCISE    ORDINANCE
Excise  Notification No.  952
EXCISE  DUTY  ON  LIQUORS   OTHER  THAN  TODDY  OR  ANY  LIQUOR  MADE  FROM  ANY  CEREAL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from 06th October, 2012—
(1) there shall be levied on Liquors (other than toddy or any liquor made from any cereal) containing more
than 4 per cent (4%) by V olume, of alcohol manufactured by process other than distillation from natural
products of the Palm Tree or any other plant, manufactured in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of Rupees
Eight  Hundred and Ninety Eight  (Rs. 898.00) on every proof litre, provided that such duty shall not be levied
or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid liquor which is exported out of the Island ; and
(2) the Excise Notification, No. 947 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1751/29 of 30.03.2012 is hereby
rescinded.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
05th  October, 2012.

EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 953
 EXCISE  DUTY  ON  MOLASSES,  PALMYRAH,  COCONUT  AND  PROCESSED  ARRACK
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from  06th October, 2012 —
(1) there shall be levied on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed  Arrack manufactured in and issued
from any licensed manufactory established in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of Rupees One Thousand and
Forty three (Rs. 1,043.00) per proof litre on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed Arrack provided
that  such duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid  liquor which is exported out
of the Island ; and
(2) the Excise Notification, No. 948  published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1751/29  of 30.03.2012 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
 05th October, 2012.

EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 954
EXCISE  DUTY  ON  COUNTRY  MADE  FOREIGN  SPIRITS  MANUFACTURED  IN  SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from  06th October, 2012 —
(1) there shall be levied on Country made “Foreign” spirits manufactured in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of
Rupees One Thousand  One Hundred and Eighty Three  (Rs. 1,183.00) on every proof litre provided that such
duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid “Foreign” spirits which is exported out
of the Island ; and
(2) the Excise Notification, No. 949 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1751/29  of 30.03.2012 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.

EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 955
EXCISE  DUTY  ON  MALT  LIQUOR
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from  06th October, 2012 —
(1) there shall be imposed on Malt Liquor of less than Five per centum (5%) of absolute strength as indicated
in the label, manufactured in and issued from any licensed brewery established in Sri Lanka, a duty at
the rate of Rupees One Hundred (Rs. 100.00) only on every litre so issued,
(2) there shall be imposed on Malt Liquor of Five per centum  (5%) and above of absolute strength as
indicated in the label, manufactured in and issued from any licensed brewery established in Sri Lanka,
a duty at the rate of Rupees One Hundred and Sixteen  (Rs.116.00) only on every litre so issued,
(3) the aforesaid duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of such liquor which is issued from
such brewery for the use of any Diplomatic Mission in Sri Lanka or any foreign country, if payment
for such quantity of Liquor is made from the foreign account of such Diplomatic Mission ; and
(4) the Excise Notification, No. 950  published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1751/29 of 30.03.2012  is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
05th October, 2012.
