No. 1774/25 – WEDNESDAY,  SEPTEMBER 05, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: HAK/5/1/1/97.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1)of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land  called Awariyawattahena alias
Thelambugahawattahena alias Puhukosgahahena Awariyagahawatta
in total extent of 0.0723 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 259 in the final
village plan No. 185 sheet No. 17 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys of Matara District situated in the village
of Muruthamuraya - West in Minor Division Kandabadapattu in
Divisional  Secretaries Division of Hakmana in Matara District of
Sourthern  Province and bounded as follows:
North and West: Lot No. 112; South: Pradeshiya Saba Road;
West: Lot No. 112.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land  are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Hakmana on the  17th day of September
2012  at  10.00  a.m .  and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 28th day of  August, 2012
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars  of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land , the
amount of  compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
Kusalaka Nanayakkara.
Divisional Secretary,
Hakmana.
Divisional Secretariat,
Hakmana,
29th  August, 2012.
