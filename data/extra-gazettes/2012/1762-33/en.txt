UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCLAL  COUNCIL
Announcement   made  by  the  Hon.  Governor  of  the  Uva  Province
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Provincial Councils Act No. 42 of 1987, Section 32(1) and 32(3), I, Caluwadewagey
Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce that I have created a separate Uva Provincial Council
Public Service Disciplinary Code for the Uva Provincial Public Servants instead of following the Establishment code
section II of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka with effect from 01 st June 2012. This code is published in all three
languages viz Sinhala, Tamil and English.
C. N
ANDA MAT H Ew,
Governor of Uva Province.
