MINISTRY   OF   CIVIL    AVIATION
Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   BY   THE   DIRECTOR   GENERAL   OF   CIVIL   AVIATION   UNDER   THE   AIR  NAVIGATION
REGULATION   196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that I have received on 11th June, 2012 an application dated 05th June 2012 from
Saffron Aviation (Pvt) Ltd. at No. 11, York Street, Colombo 01, seeking from this office an Air Operator Certificate/Airline
Licence to engage in Domestic scheduled operations, charter Operation and Aerial Work to engage in domestic operations
in pursuance of the Air Navigation Regulations of 1955. Business name of the company has been registered as Saffron
Aviation (Private) Limited and the trade name as “Cinnamon Air”. The Director of the Saffron Aviation (Pvt.) Ltd. is declared
to be as follows:
Mr. Ajit Damon Gunewardene,
Mr. Romesh Michael David,
Mr. Jitendra Romesh Gunaratne,
Mr. Bernard Anton Bandara Goonetilleke,
Mr. Kandasamypillai Balasundaram,
Mr. Feroz Omar,
Mr. Rusiru Tharindra Abeysinghe.
Saffron Aviation (Pvt) Ltd. has nominated Mr. Romesh David to be the Accountable Manager in charge of the proposed
operations of air services.
A copy of the complete Application of the aforementioned applicant is kept at the Technical Library, Ground  Floor,
of Head Quarters of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 04, Hunupitiya Road, Colombo 02. for the perusal of the
members of the public, during office hours of any working days.
Members of the public are hereby informed that I have decided to commence after 25th October 2012 the certification
process of this application as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant’s compliance with the
applicable Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended practices specified by ICAO and associated
local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure, efficient operation of
proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Airline Licence, Charter Licence and Aerial
Work Licence together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
