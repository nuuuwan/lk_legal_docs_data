No. 1777/14 – MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 24, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry's Re. No. : LD/05/2008/HW/329.
My No.: HIK/DS/LND/INV/07.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for the Akurala Bridge in Colombo-Galle-
Hambantota-Wellawaya Road and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Five (05) Allotments of land in extent of about 0.0302 Hectare,
depicted in  Preliminary Plan No. G.3972 dated 05.07.2012 prepared
by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Hikkaduwa
Divisional Secretary's Division of No. 76 B, Akurala  in  Galle District
and bounded as follows :
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Elabodawaththa alias  Lanka Elawaththa ;
Description: Waste Land ;  Extent: 0.0057 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Elabodawaththa alias  Lanka Elawaththa ;
Description: Waste Land ;  Extent: 0.0072 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Elabodawaththa alias  Lanka Elawaththa ;
Description: Waste Land ;  Extent: 0.0031 Hectare.
4. Lot No. 4
Name of land: Thalgahapitiyawaththa alias Lanka Elawaththa ;
Description: Waste Land ;  Extent: 0.0102 Hectare.
5. Lot No. 5
Name of land: Thalgahapitiyawaththa alias Lanka Elawaththa ;
Description: Waste Land ;  Extent: 0.0040 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretary’s Office Hikkaduwa on the 05th November,
2012 at 10.00.a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th October, 2012 the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Anusha Batawala Gamage,
Divisional Secretary,
Hikkaduwa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Hikkaduwa,
