BY - LAW  RELATING  TO  PARKING  HIRING CARS AND MOTOR VEHICLES
01. This by-law is cited as these by-law for parking controlling and regulating hiring cars and motor vehicles within
the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha authority area and for levying licensing fees from such vehicles.
02. No motor vehicle shall be parked at any public place for the purpose of hiring such vehicles except at a place
approved by the Pradeshiya Sabha of Yatinuwara and duly notified by the Chairman according to 1st Schedule shown herein.
Such parking places shall be amended and new parking places shall be named on a resolution passed in the Council.
03. Hiring cars and Motor Vehicles shall be parked at places shown in the 1st  Schedule hiring, on payment of fees,
shown in the 2nd Schedule together with and the relevant crown fees to council annually. Such hiring cars and Motor
Vehicles shall be registered in the Council. Relevant Crown fees shall be  determined by the Council from time to time and such
amended fees shall be valid only after publication of a notification in the Government Gazette.

04. No person shall park a Motor Vehicle in the parking places shown in the 1st Schedule herein situated within the
Pradeshiya Sabha authority area, except on a license issued by the Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha or by an officer
authorized by him. Such parking arrangement shall operate from 6.00 a.m. to 7.00 p.m. daily. Whenever a new parking place is
established according to 1st Schedule the approval of the general Assembly shall be obtained by passing a resolution.
05. The period of validity of every license issued under the by-laws shall expire on the last date of the calender
month for which it is issued unless it is cancelled ealier by the Chairman or by an Officer authorized by him.
06. For the purpose of obtaining a license, as stipulated in the third Schedule herein the owner or driver of the
vehicle shall apply to the Chairman by forwarding an application to be obtained from the Pradeshiya Sabha of Yatinuwara.
07. The license issued in respect of a Motor Vehicle parked in a parking place shall be produced for inspection of a
traffic warden or a person appointed by the Chairman for such purpose whenever a request is made from the owner or driver
of such vehicle.
08. Vehicle parking license shall have to be obtianed every year before the 31st January of the relevant year.
09. No Motor Vehicle shall be parked in a hiring car and Motor Vehicle parking place without a license issued by the
Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
10. Whenever a Motor Vehicle is to be removed from a vehicle parking place permanently or for a period not less than
14 days, such removal shall be immediatly notified to the Council Office and annual fees shall have to be paid even if fail to
notify accordingly.
11. No vehicle shall be washed or cause to be washed or repaired within a hiring car and Motor Vehicle parking place.
12. No person shall behave under influence of liquor, or misbehave or behave in disorderly manner causing any
hindrance inside a hiring car parking place or inside a vehicle therein.
13. Contravening any of the provisions of these by-laws is an offence and when convicted in a Court of Law having
jurisdiction, the maximum fine, and punishment imposable, and when such contravention is committed continuously and
when convicted or in the case of continuous contravention, after delivering a written notice by the Chairman or by any other
authorized Officer, drawing attention for such contravention, to maximum additional fine imposable for each day of continuing
such contravention shall respectively be as defined under Sub-section (02) of Section (112) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.
15 of 1987.
14. It shall be lawful to cancel a license of a person who has convicted in a Court of Law twice or more times on
account of violation of these by-laws and or in addition to any other punishment imposed by a Court of Law having
jurisdiction and such person has no right to claim any compensation for such cancellation.
15. Unless the context otherwise requires in these by-laws
“ Council” means the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
“Chairman” menas the Chairman of the Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
“Traffic Warden” means an Officer appointed by the Pradeshiya Sabha to control traffic.
“Motor vehicle or hiring car” means a three wheeler, lorry, motor van., tractor, truck, motor car and hand
tractor.
“Authorized Officer’ means an Officer authorized by the Chairman of Yatinuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.

the Pradeshiya Sabha authority area of Rattota and for levying fees from such vehicles.

approved by the Pradeshiya Sabha and duly notified by the Chairman according to 1st Schedule shown herein. Such parking
places shall be amended and new  places nominated on  a resolution passed in the Council.

hiring cars and vehicles within the Rattota Pradeshiya Sabha authority area.
04. Annual registration fees and annual licensing fees shall be charged as shown in the 4th schedule for parking in
places, situated within the Pradeshiya Sabha authority area, as shown in the 2nd schedule herein.
05. According to these by-laws, the drivers, lessors and owners shall keep with them the receipt issued for payment
of daily fees for three wheelers lorries, and motor vans used for hiring, parked within the town limits of Rattota.
06. No person shall park motor vehicles in the parking places shown in the 1st schedule herein, situated within the
Pradeshiya Sabha authority area, without a license issued by the Chairman or by an Officer authorized by him, and shall be
effective from 6.00 a.m. to 7.00 p.m.
