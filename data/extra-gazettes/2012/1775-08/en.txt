PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL

THE   LAND  REFORM   LAW,  NO.  1 OF   1972
Order under Subsection (4) of  Section 27A
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (4) of the Section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as amended
by the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby -
(a) amend the Order made under Subsection (1) of Section 27A of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette Extraor-
dinary No. 344/5 of April 09, 1985 relating to the land called and known as Uduwela morefully described in the First
Schedule to this Order and which has vested in the Janatha Estates Development Board established by the State
Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972, by revoking on grounds of non-compliance with such terms and
conditions relating to consideration for the vesting in such Board, such portion of the said Order as relates to the
extent of land called Uduwela specified in the Second Schedule hereto ;
(b) state that upon revocation, such extent of land shall re-vest in the Land Reform Commission.
