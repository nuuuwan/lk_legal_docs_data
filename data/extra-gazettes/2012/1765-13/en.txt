CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION
Customs   Ordinance
Section 6A
PAYMENT OF A FEE FOR A SPECIAL SERVICE OF CLEARANCE OF GEM PARCELS AT AIRPORT
WHEREAS  Hon. Minister of Finance, by an order made  by him, had determined that a service, granted to an incoming
passenger to clear the gem parcels brought by him at Airport through a special customs procedure designed by the Director
General of Customs, shall be special service within the meaning of Section 6A (a) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as
amended by Customs (Amendment) Act, No. 83 of 1988.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of the powers vested in me by Section 6A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), I,
Neville Goonewardena, Director General of Customs, do by this Notification specify that, with effective from 05.07.2012, a
payment shall be made for such special service at a rate of Two Hundred United States Dollars (US$ 200), or the equivalent in
any convertible foreign currency as per the prevailing exchange rates on the date of payment, in respect of each Customs
Goods Declaration (Cusdec) made by the passenger.
The order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1762/40 dated June 15th 2012 is hereby rescinded.
D
R. NEVILLE GOONEW ARDENA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
