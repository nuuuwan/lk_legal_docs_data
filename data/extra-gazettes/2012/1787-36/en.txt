Regulations
1. These Regulations shall be cited as Land Development Regulations 2012.
2. The declaration made by the Land Commissioner/Land Commissioner General in case where the documents
pertaining to the disposition of a land alienated by a grant as stipulated in Section 162(1) of the Land Devel-
opment Ordinance, had been prepared inconsistent with the provisions of this Ordinance, making such
documents null and void, shall be in accordance with the ‘Annex A’.
