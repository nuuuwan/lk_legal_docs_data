EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   AKKARAIPATTU MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the National Congress, the recognized political party to which
Aatham Lebbe Thavam the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Akkaraipattu Municipal Council  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Aathamlebbe Junaideen
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordiance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  S. Achchuthan, Returning Officer of Akkaraipattu Municipal Council, acting in  terms of Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Aathamlebbe Junaideen elected as a
member of the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
Ampara,
24th September  2012.
