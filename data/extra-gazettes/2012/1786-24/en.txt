THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: LD/05/2009/IR/192/193/194.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Four  (04)  allotments of land depicted in the final Village Plan No.
F. V . P. 3229 prepared by the Surveyor General siutated in the
Village called Medagama in Karambe Pattuwa in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Anamaduwa in Puttalam District and more
fully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 73
Name of land: Helambagahayaya; Original Claimant: 1. M. W. A.
Dharamawardhana, Asanka Hotel , Uswewa Road, Anamaduwa.
2. M. W. Sriyani Dharmawardhana, Near Cooprative shop, Puttalam
Road, Anamaduwa. 3. M. W. R. Dharmawardhana. 12/21, Wendesi
watta, Anamaduwa. 4. M. W. Mallika Dharmawardhana, Puttalam
Road, Anamaduwa. 5. M. W. S. Dharmawardhana. 653, 2nd Street,
Uswewa Road, Anamaduwa.; Extent:  2.5365  Hectare.
02. Lot No. 75
Name of land: Helambagahayaya and Medagamawela; Original
Claimant: A. N. M. B. V . Herathhami, H 8 Gammanaya, Maha
Uswewa; Extent:  1.1046  Hectare.
03. Lot No. 76
Name of land: Helambagahayaya and Medagamawela; Original
Claimant: N. P . K. Kosta, 121, Nithil Road, Moodukatuwa, Marawila;
Extent:  1.6616  Hectare.
04. Lot No. 77
Name of land: Helambagahayaya; Original Claimant: H. M.
Herathbanda, ‘‘Chandra wasa’’, Maha Uswewa; Extent:  2.1324
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, on the 15th day of January, 2013 at
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 08th day of January, 2013, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. M. Sanjeewani Herath,
Asst. Divisional Secretary,
Anamaduwa.
