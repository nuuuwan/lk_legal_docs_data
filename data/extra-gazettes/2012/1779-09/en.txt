EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   MUTTUR   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Muttur Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Kathiraveluppillai thiruchelvam  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Muttur  Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Velautham Mohan, being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Nalaka Rathnayake, Returning Officer of Muttur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in  terms of Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Velautham Mohan, elected as  a
member  of   the Muttur Pradeshiya Sabha.
N
ALAKA RA THNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Muttur Pradeshiya Sabha.
Trincomalee,
03rd  August  2012.
