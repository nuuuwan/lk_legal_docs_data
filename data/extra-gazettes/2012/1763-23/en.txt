EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  KADUGANNAWA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Kadugannawa  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United  National Party the recognised political party to which Subhasinghe
Siriwardhane the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Kadugannawa Urban Council  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the aforesaid recognised political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Rathnayake Mudiyanselage
Premarathne  being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning  Officer  of   Kadugannawa  Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Rathnayake Mudiyanselage
Premarathne elected as a member of the  Kadugannawa  Urban Council.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kadugannawa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
12th  June 2012.
