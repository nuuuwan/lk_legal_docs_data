EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   AMBAGAMUWA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Ambagamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Death  ;
 And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Sinnappan Thangaraj   the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Ambagamuwa   Pradeshiya Sabha  who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Parani Periyasamy
Kamalesvaran,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando,  Returning  Officer of  Ambagamuwa  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Parani Periyasamy Kamalesvaran, elected as  a member  of   the Ambagamuwa  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
H. A. S. FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Ambagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara - Eliya,
09th  November, 2012.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   HOROWPOTHANA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Horowpothana   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as a
member of  the North Central Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the  recognized political party to which   Pakeer Saheedu   the
member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under
the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Horowpothana   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be
declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Gunarathnage Sujith
Amarasiri,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning  Officer of  Horowpothana  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Gunarathnage Sujith Amarasiri, elected as  a member  of   the Horowpothana  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Horowpothana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
09th  November, 2012.
