PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Statutory  Determination No. 4621
Unique No. : Maha/105
STATUTORY   DETERMINATION  UNDER   SECTION  19 OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW, No. 1 OF 1972
WHEREAS  a statutory declaration under Section 18 of the law has been made by Mr. Herath Mudiyansela Meegahaele
Appuhamy Herath of Hiyadala, Walpola, Minigamuwa, Galagedera, the Land Reform Commission by virtue of the powers
vested in it under Section 19 of the Law, has made a statutory determination specifying the portion of agricultural land owned
by the statutory lessee which he shall be allowed to retain. The agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the statutory
lessee is described in the Schedule below :
N
IMAL G. PUNCHIHEW A,
Attorney-at-Law,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
C 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
31st July, 2012.
SCHEDULE
1. An allotment of land marked Lot Nos. 644, 645, 646, 647 and 648 of the land called Ritigahatenne Asweddume hena
Angei Kumbura, Tennewala Kumbura, Tannewela watta situated in the village Minigomuwa in the Minor Division
of Udapalatha West Korale of the D. R. O.’s Division of Thumpane in the District of Kandy, Central Province,
containing in extent 02 Acres, 1 Rood, 21 Perches, in the Final Village Plan No. 158, Supplement No. 02 made by the
Surveyor-General together with the buildings and plantations thereon ; and
2. An undivided extent of 36 Acres, 3 Rood, 10 Perches, from and out of allotments of land marked Lot Nos. 157, 158
and 159 of the land called Merian Hill Estate situated in the village Sivurupitiya in the Minor Division of Ganatha
Palatha of the D. R. O.’s Division of Thumpane in the District of Kandy, Central Province depicted in the Final Village
Plan No. 167, Supplement No. 01 made by the Surveyor-General together with the buildings and plantations thereon ;
and
3. An undivided extent of 10 Acres, 0 Rood, 17 Perches, from and out of allotments of land marked Lot Nos. 396 and 397
of the land called Merian Hill Estate situated in the village Eramuduliyadda in the Minor Division of Ganatha Palatha
of the D. R. O.’s Division of Thumpane in the District of Kandy, Central Province depicted in the Final Village Plan
No. 168, Supplement No. 02 made by the Surveyor-General together with the buildings and plantations thereon.
