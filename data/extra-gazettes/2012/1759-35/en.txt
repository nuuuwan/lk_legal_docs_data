HIGH COURT OF  THE PROVINCES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  10 OF  1996
Notification
(PARAGRAPH (1) OF  THE FIRST SCHEDULE)
I, Rauff Hakeem,  the Minister of  Justice, do by this Notification fix, with effect from May 31, 2012 rupees five million, as the
amount for the purpose of paragraph (1) of  the First Schedule to the High Court of  the Provinces (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 10 of 1996, in lieu of the sum of rupees three million.
This increase in the monetary value shall not affect the actions filed in the High Court before the 31st of May, 2012.
Notification made under paragraph (1) of the First Schedule to the High Court of the Provinces (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 10 of 1996 and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 943/ 12 of 01st October, 1996, is hereby rescinded without
prejudice to anything duly done thereunder.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of  Justice.
Colombo,
18th May 2012.
