Reference No.: 3/3/4/26.

I hereby give Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Hapugolle hena  in total extent of
about 0.4050 Hectare,  shown as Lot No. 271 of F.V .P. 639 at
Supplement No. 9 and Sheet  No. 9 situated in the Village of Deliwala
in Deliwala South Grama Niladari Division, in Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Walapane in Nuwara Eliya District in the Central Province
and described as follows:
01. Lot No. 271
Name of land: Hapugolle hena; Description: Garden, contains 12
Satin Woods trees 8-10 years of age, 1 Koon tree, 3 Hulanhik trees,
2 Bulu trees and forty years aged 01 Jak tree; Claimant:
Yapamudiyanseleage Dingiri Banda;  Extent: 0.4050 Hectare.
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby required to
appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary’s Office of Walapane on the 09th day of
January 2013  at 12.00 noon and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd day of January,
2013, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
I. K. G . Muthubanda,
