THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT No. 02 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No. 2 of
1980, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the
subject of Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the
Urban Development Project do by this Order, declare that the said land are required for such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President Secretariat, President.
Colombo 01,
  2012.
