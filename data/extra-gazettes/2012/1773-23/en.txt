MINISTRY  OF  PUBLIC  ADMINISTRATION  AND  HOME  AFFAIRS
Sri Lanka Administrative Service Minute
PUBLIC Service Commission has approved to make the following revisions at the end of the list of posts in Sri Lanka
Administrative Service indicated in the  Gazette notification on Sri Lanka Administrative Service Minute published in the
Gazette of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No 1419/ 3 dated 14.11.2005.
''Notwithstanding anything contrary to the SLAS Minute, posts already approved for the All Island Services and to
be approved in future for the All Island Services would continue to be held by the respective Services''.
