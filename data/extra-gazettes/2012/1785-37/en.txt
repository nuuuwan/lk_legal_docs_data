PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & c., by the President
  PS No.:  E&O/HR/1/101.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1)  of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Sumith Abeysingha, Secretary to the Cabinet with effect from
23.11.2012, until Mr. Lalith Weeratunga, Secretary to the President returns to the Island.
