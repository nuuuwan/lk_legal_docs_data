Statutory Determination No. 4625
Unique No. Nu/44
Statutory Determination under section 19 of the land reform law,
No. 1 of 1972
WHEREAS a statutory declaration under Section 18 of the Law has been made by Directress of Kumarage Estate Company
Limited of Rikillagaskada Estate, Rikillagaskada on behalf of late Agnes Magarita Naniel de Soysa who was the only share-
holder who owns lands over ceiling under  Section 8, the Land Reform Commission by virtue of the powers vested in it under
Section 19 of the Law, has made a statutory determination specifying the portion of agricultural land owned by the statutory
lessee which she shall be allowed to retain. The agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the statutory lessee is described
in the Schedule below.
N
IMAL G. PUNCHIHEAWA,
Chairman,
Attorney-at-Law,
Land Reform Commission.
C, 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 7,
August 17th, 2012.
SCHEDULE
An allotment of land marked Lot Nos. 02, 04, 06, 07, 10, 11, 13, 14, 16 and 24 of land called Rikillagaskada Estate situated
in the Rikillagaskada and Walalawalagama villages in Gramaseva Niladari Division of 492A Rikillagaskada in Minor Division of
Diyatilaka Korale of the Divisional Secretariat Division of Hanguranketha in the District of Nuwaraeliya, Central Province,
containing in extent of Forty Nine Acres, One Rood, Thirty Five Point Seven Four Perches (49A., 1R., 35.74P.) (20.0216
Hectares) in the Preliminary Plan No. Nu/2771 made by the Surveyor-General together with the buildings and plantations there
on.
