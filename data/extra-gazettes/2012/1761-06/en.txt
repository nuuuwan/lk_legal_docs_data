No. 1761/6– WEDNESDAY, JUNE 06, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 3/2/8/1/12.
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/05/2011/CEB/327.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for the construction of 220 kv High - tension
Transmession Line, under Upper Kothmale Hydro Power Project:
Description of the land to be acquired:
One Lot of land in total extent of 0.2648 Hectare, shown in the
Preliminary Plan No. PP Nu 2798 and dated 23rd April, 2012
prepared by Surveyor General, situated in Kalapitiya Village,
Medapane Korale Minor Division, Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kotmale in Nuwara Eliya District :
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Wewaliyadde Hena; Description: Open waste land;
Claimant: A. S. J. Weerasekara, No. 36A 126, Rukmalgama, Kottawa;
Extent: 0.2648 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Kotmale on the 15th of June 2012,
order No. 1 between 9.30 a.m. to 12.30 p.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of
June, 2012, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
H. M. M. U. B. Herath,
Divisional Secretary,
Kotmale.
