Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No : 51/0455 Gampaha District  
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 510407 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Walpitamulla (Block 
1) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Divi-
sion of 100 -Walpitamulla in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Minuwangoda in the District of Gampaha in the Province of 
Western should submit his claim to the undersigned before 19th 
August, 2012.In the event of failure  to submit any claim before 
the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte 
inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such 
determination in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 
of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 510407 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals’ office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
