Notice Under Section 10A(1)(a)
EMBILIPITIYA   PRADESHIY A   SABHA
I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri, Chandrawansa Perera Returning Officer of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Withanage Wijaya Kumara
of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a
member of the United People's Freedom Alliance.
M. A. P. C. PERERA,
