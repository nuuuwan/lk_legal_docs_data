THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02)  allotments of land in total Extent of about 1.2242
Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. of Maha/PTH/2011/386
Dated  31st May 2012 prepared by the Surveyor-General  situated
in the village of Hanthana Watta, Gandahaya Uthura Korale in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Patha Hewaheta in Kandy District
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  ‘‘A’’ – Hanthana Watta Uduwela Middle Division –
Extent:  About 0.5656 Hectare
North: Hanthana Watta Uduwela Middle Division claimed by
Janatha Estate Developmen Board and Estate Road;  East: Estate
Road and Hanthana Watta Uduwela Middle Division claimed by Hill
country sports shooting club; South: Hanthana Watta Uduwela
Middle Division claimed by Hill country sports shooting club; West:
Hanthana Watta claimed by State and Hanthana Watta Uduwela
Middle Division claimed by Janatha Estate Development Board.
02. Lot  ‘‘B’’ – Hanthana Watta Uduwela Middle Division –
Extent:  About 0.6586 Hectare
North: Estate Road;  East: Estate Road and Canal; South: Canal
and Estate Road; West: Estate Road.
