EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   BUTTALA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Buttala   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Kahagala Hawage Ariyasena  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Buttala    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Habaraduwa Peellage
Sanjeewa Predeep,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,Surange Ambagahathenne,  Returning  Officer of  Buttala   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Habaraduwa Peellage
Sanjeewa Predeep, elected as  a member  of   the  Buttala   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
SURANGE AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Moneragala,
20th  August  2012.

Notice under  Section 10 A (1) (a)
  AKURESSA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana, Returning Officer of  the Akuressa  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under

Alliance .
C. A. SUNETH LOCHANA,
