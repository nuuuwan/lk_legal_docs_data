CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATE  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Ranasinghe Semasinghe, Acting  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from  14th,  May,  2012, all  duties
of  Customs as well as other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the  Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1757/01 of
07th May, 2012  is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 20th May, 2012.
R. S
EMASINGHE,
 Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
11th May  2012.
