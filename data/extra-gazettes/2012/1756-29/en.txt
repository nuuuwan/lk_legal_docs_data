Regulations  Under  Section  101 (Chapter 235) of  The  Customs  Ordinance
DETERMINATION OF COST OF TRANSPORT FOR CUSTOMS V ALUATION, WHEN THE
IMPORTER IMPORTS HIS OWN GOODS BY HIS OWN VESSEL
1. BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 101 (1) (h) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning, being satisfied that it is necessary and expedient so to do, hereby fix, when the
importer’s own ship is used for importing of their own goods to any port in Sri Lanka, the cost of such transport for the
purpose of customs valuation referred to in Article 8.1(e)(i) of Schedule E to the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), as fifteen
per cent of the Free On Board (FOB) value of such goods.
2. These regulations may be cited as Customs V aluation Regulation No. 01 of 2012 and shall come into operation on
04, May 2012.
M
