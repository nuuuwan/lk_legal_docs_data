WAGES  BOARD  ORDINANCE
Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Processing trade
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  07
I,  Gamini Lokuge Minister of Labour and Labour Relations here by notify under Section 7(1) of the Wages Board Ordinance
that intended to make an order regarding Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Processing Trade, under Section 6(1) of the
Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136).
Objection if any to the aforesaid order will be received by me, until 12.00 noon on 09th January 2013
Every such objection must be in writting and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection
in taken.
G
AMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
20th November 2012.
ORDER
The Provisions of Part II of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136) shall be relevant to the below mentioned
trade.
Rubber Cultivation and Raw Rubber Processing Trade,
(1) Raising and Maintenance of rubber plantation of 10 acres or over in extant ;
(2) Manufacture of marketable Raw Rubber (This includes all types of lace rubber, scrap rubber, centrifugal latex
and Technically Specified Rubber - TSR).
The works of the follwing workers of the trade mentioned in the Schedule below are also included.

SCHEDULE
1.1 Workers engaged in Raising and Maintenance of Rubber cultivation :
* Harvesting Attendent ;
* Sundry Workers.
Harvesting attendant
Tapping, Collection of scrap and latex, Carrying scrap an latex, Assisting to weighing, Appling panel treatment,
Protecting rain guards, Application of chemicals.
Sundry Workers
Nursery activites including bud grafting, Land clearing. Construtction of roads and bridges, Fencing and boundaries,
Soil conservation work, Construction and repairing of drains, Planting work, Weeding (manual/chemical), Manuaring, Marking
and census work of trees, Appling pesticides and diseases, Maintenance of trees, Fixing of rain guards, Tapping panel
treatment, General cleaning, Watchers, Kangani.
2.1 Workers engaged in Manufacture of marketable Raw Rubber
Transferring latex in to bulking tanks, Fractionation, removal of froth and coagulation of latex, Processing of
coagulum, Operation and maintenance of raw rubber and latex processing machinery, Handling of wet and dry laces and
sheets, Sorting, Grading and packing of all types of raw rubber, Cleaning of all latex collecting utensils, tanks, factory
premises and transportation vehicles, Operating and maintenance of smoke houses and drying towers, boilers, Processing of
firewood, Operation and maintenance of waste water treatment plant installed in rubber estates/Factories, Analysis of latex
quality parameters in centrifuged latex manufacture.
Other workers engaged in the Rubber Cultivation and Taw Rubber Processing Trade and related work, but not
specified in the above schedule are also covered under this trade.
But the following workers are excluded
Rubber maker, Engine driver, Lorry and van driver, Mechanic, Clerk, Conductor, Kanakapulle, Store Keeper, Dispense,
Midwife, Bungalow servant, Dhoby, Barber, Teacher and Ward attendent.
