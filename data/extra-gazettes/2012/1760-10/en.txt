1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  HIKKADUWA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Hikkaduwa   Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of   not attending  more than
three  consecutive meetings  without permission  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Lokuge Don Sudath,
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible
under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Hikkaduwa  Urban Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be
declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  aforesaid  recognised political party has nominated Mr. Mahayalage Pemasiri
Fernando  being  an eligible person under the  aforesaid  Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   the Hikkaduwa   Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mahayalage
Pemasiri Fernando  elected as a member of the  Hikkaduwa   Urban Council .
A. B. I. De  Silva,
Returning Officer,
Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
