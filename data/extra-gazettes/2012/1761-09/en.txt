PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF  1988
Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  Sabaragamuwa  Province   Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Sabaragamuwa Province
Provincial Council by reason of Expulsion from the Party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the  aforesaid  Act,  to  nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. R. P. Manoj Rajapaksha
to fill such vacancy  ;
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in  terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. R. P. Manoj Rajapaksha, elected as a member of the above Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of Kegalle in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
05th June, 2012.
