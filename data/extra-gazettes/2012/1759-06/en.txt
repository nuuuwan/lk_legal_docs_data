FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   UDUBADDAWA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Hendiyagala Pagngnaransi Thero,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordi-
nance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Rathnayake Mudiyanselage
Harischandra Hearath Rathnayake being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Muhammad Nafeel, Returning Officer of Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Harischandra Hearath Rathnayake, elected as  a member  of   the Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
A. O. M. N
AFEE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kurunegala,
14th May, 2012.
