Regulations
The  regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1734/ 12 of November 30, 2011 are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of  regulation  6 thereof,   and the substitution therefor of  the  following :-
''6. No person shall transport any live rock within Sri Lanka without a licence issued by the Director General
of National Aquaculture Development Authority or his authorized officer'';
(2) by the insertion immediately after regulation 6 of the following regulations :-
''6A. An application for a  licence under  regulation 6 shall be substantially in the form set out in schedule I hereto.
6 B. Any licence granted under regulation 6 shall :
(i) not be transferable ;
(ii)  substantially be in the form set out in Schedule II hereto and be subject to the  terms and conditions as are
specified therein'' ;
