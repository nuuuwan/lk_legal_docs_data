UV A  PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation   by  the  Hon.  Governor of  Uva Province
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me under the 13th Amendment and under section 154 b (8) (b) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Caluadewagey Nanda Mathew, the Governor of Uva
Province, do hereby this proclamation prorogue the Second session of the Fifth Uva Province Provincial Council with effect
from midnight of 28 th September, Two Thousand Twelve.
C. N
ANDA MAT H Ew,
Governor of the Uva Province.
Office of the Governor of Uva Province,
King’s Street,
Badulla,
14th September, 2012.
UV A  PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation   by  the  Hon.  Governor of  Uva Province
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me under the 13th Amendment and under section 154 b (8) (a) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Caluadewagey Nanda Mathew, the Governor of Uva
Province, do hereby this proclamation summon the Third session of the Fifth Uva Province Provincial Council to meet at
10.00 a.m. on the 25th October, Two Thousand Twelve, at the Provincial Council meeting Hall, Badulla.
C. N
ANDA MAT H Ew,
Governor of the Uva Province.
Office of the Governor of Uva Province,
King’s Street,
Badulla,
14th September, 2012.
