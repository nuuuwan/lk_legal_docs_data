FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KINNIYA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kinniya Urban  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the group Leader of the Independent group, to which Mohamed Marsook Riswan Mohamed  the
member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under
the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Kinniya Urban Council  who belongs to  such group  who shall be declared
elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group  has  nominated  Mr. Mammathu Aniffa Mohamed Nijam, being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Nalaka Rathnayake, Returning Officer of  Kinniya Urban Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mammathu Aniffa Mohamed Nijam, elected
as  a member  of   the  Kinniya Urban Council.
N
ALAKA RA THNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office,  Kinniya Urban Council.
Trincomalee,
03 rd  August  2012.
