EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  PUTTALAM  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Puttalam  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the group Leader of the Independent Group No. 08 to which M. F. M. Faiz   the member  belonged, was
required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for
Election as a member of  that   Puttalam  Urban Council  who belongs to  such group   who shall be declared  elected as  a
member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of  the aforesaid  group  has   nominated   Mr.  A. R. M. Mahir   being an eligible person under
the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Niruka Dananga Laththuwahandi,  Returning  Officer  of   the Puttalam  Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  A. R. M. Mahir
elected as a member of the  Puttalam  Urban Council .
N
IRUKA DANANGA LA THTHUW AHANDI,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
