No. 1771/5 – TUESDAY, AUGUST 14, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land  which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Kumbutukuliya Watta  alias
Sausiriuyana   in total extent of about 0.2026 Hectare, depicted as
Lot No.1 in Plan No. P.P.PU 5010 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys Puttalam situated in the Village of
Kumbutukuliya, in Anavilundan  Pattu Divisional Secretary's Division
of Arachchikattuwa in Puttalam District in North Western Province
and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 01 – Kumbutukuliya – Extent: 0.2026 Hectare
North: Kumbutukuliya Watta alias Sausiriuyana owned by the
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha; East and South: Pradeshiya
Sabha Road; West: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Kumbutukuliya Watta
alias Sausiriuyana owned by Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat - Arachchikattuwa on the 10th day of
October, 2012 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of September
2012, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
H. S. S. Lusena,
Divisional Secretary,
Arachchikattuwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Arachchikattuwa.
09th August, 2012
