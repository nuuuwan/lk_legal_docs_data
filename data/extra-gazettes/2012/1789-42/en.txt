PART IV (B) — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Notices under  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION 10A (1) (a)
Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10 A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Dewamunige Asanka Prabath Silva of
the Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be
a member of the Up Country People’s Front.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Pasbage Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
14th  December,  2012.
