THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An  allotment of land called in Lot ‘A’  in extent of about 0.0118
Hectare in Plan of Extract No. R/RTN/2011/733 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in Weralupa Village (F.V .P. 347) of
Weralupa Grama Niladhari Division in Minor Division of Kuruwita
Korale Southern Udapaththuwa in  Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Rathnapura in the District of Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
