LOCAL  GOVERNMENT   ACT, No. 6 OF 1952 (APPROVED BY -  LAWS)
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha
THIS is to notify that the following resolution was passed under the powers vested in the Pradeshiya  Sabha under sub-
Section 1 of Section 3 of  Local  Government Act, No. 6 of 1952 (Approved by Laws) (Chapter 261) of the Legislative
Enactments in the meeting of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha held on 21st of June 2011.
K. JAGATH SAMANTHA PERERA,
Chairman,
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha,
12th  December, 2012.
 The  Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha resolved to implement and accept any Proposal with effect from the date
announced in the Gazette under Sub- Section 1 of  Section 3 of  the Local Government (Approved by - Laws) relating to
carrying trades kind of unpleasent hazardous, tourist, parking vehicles, eradicating germs spreding diseases and mosquitos,
decorating streets and roads, establishing tourist lodges and restaurants within the administrative limits of Pradeshiya Sabha
published in Part IV (A) of the  Gazette Extra ordinary  No. 1703/ 18 dated of 28.04.2011 and approved by the North Western
Province Provincial  Council  prepared by the Minister incharge of the portfolio of Local Government of North Western
Province Provincial Council under the Powers  vested in the  Minister  of  Local Government of North Western Province
Provincial Council published in Part IV (A) of Gazette No. 1663 dated 16.07.2010 of the Government of  Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka read together with the Local Government Act, No. 6 of 1952. (Chapter 261 of the Legislative Enactments)
and Local Government (Approved by Laws) and the Provincial Councils (Consequential provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
