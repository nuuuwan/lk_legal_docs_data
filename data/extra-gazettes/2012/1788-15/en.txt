DECLARATION UNDER SUB SECTION (1) OF SECTION 3A OF THE LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTION ORDINANCE (CHAP. 262)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section  3A of the Local Authorities Election Ordinance
(Chap. 262),  I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils do hereby declare that the National
Delimitation Committee on demarcation of wards for local authorities consisting of the following members was appointed with
effect from 07.12.2012 and the office of the Committee was established at No. 51, Keppetipola Mawatha, Colombo 05.
Mr. Jayalath R. V . Dissanayaka (Chairman)
Mr. P. H. G. Premasiri  (Member)
Mr. Wimal Rubasinghe   (Member)
Mr. A. M. Nahiya  (Member)
Mr. Thevaraj Adhavan  (Member)
A. L. M.  A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
07th December, 2012.
