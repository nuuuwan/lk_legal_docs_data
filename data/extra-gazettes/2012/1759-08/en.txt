Memorandum  of  Understanding  No. 01 of 2011
MEMORANDUM  OF  UNDERSTANDING
This Memorandum of Understanding is made and entered
into on this 3rd day of November 2011 between Bartleet
Agencies (Private) Limited, a company duly registered in Sri
Lanka and having its registered office at Level 1, Bartleet
House, No. 65 Braybrooke Place, Colombo 2, hereinafter
referred to as ‘‘the Employer’’ or ‘‘the Company’’ as
appropriate, of the One Part and the Ceylon Mercantile,
Industrial and General Workers’s Union (CMU), a trade union
duly registtered in Sri Lanka and having its registered office
at No. 3, 22nd Lane Colombo 3, hereinafter referred to as ‘‘the
Union’’ of the Other Part.
Whereas the Union representing a Majority of the clerical,
supervisory and allied grade staff of the Company has
requested the Employer to consider Subscribing to the EFC/
CMU Collective Agreement of 2011 and after considering all
matters the Company has agreed to subscribe to the said
Collective Agreement with effect from 1.9.2011 based on the
following conditions, to which the parties to this agreement
have concurred.
01. The union will give credit to the Company for the
following salary revisions already granted :
(a) 20% annual wage revision granted in November
      2009.
(b) 10% revision granted with effedt from 1.5.2011.
