I hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province : -
01. Mr. M. H. J. Herath Class III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Municipal Commissioner of
the Bandarawela Municipal Council, in addition to his permanent post, with effect from the date 01.10.2012 till
Mr. W. Randeniya resumes his duty who is the Municipal Commissioner of the Bandarawela Municipal Council,
after participating in a Training from the date 01.10.2012 to 19.12.2012, for new recruits of Sri Lanka Administrative
Service,
02. Mrs. Kusum Pathiraja, Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, Reappointed on contract basis as
Assistant Secretary to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation, Childcare and Women's Affairs of
Uva Province, from the date 01.10.2012  till the appointment of a Permanent Officer or Up to 31.12.2012,
03. Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Mr. R. M. Jayawardena to perfrom the Duties of the Provincial
Commissioner of Local Government for  Uva Province, from the date 07.10.2012  to 11.10.2012,  till the return of
Mr. M. P. N. M. Wickramasinghe, to the Island who is the Provincial Commissioner of Local Government for Uva
Province,
04. Director (Planning), Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara, to act as Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning) for  Uva
Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 15.10.2012  to 21.10.2012,  till the return of Mr. V . C.
Hettiarachchi, to the Island who is the Deputy Chief Secretary (Planning)  for Uva Province,
05. Mr. M. A. B. Daya Senarath, Special Grade Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as  Secretary to the
Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation, Childcare and Women's Affairs of the Uva Provincial Council,
in addition to his permanent post, from the date 15.10.2012 to 21.10.2012,  till the return of Mrs. G.. A. M. S. P.
Ambanwela, to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation,
Childcare and Women's Affairs of the Uva Provincial Council,
06. Mr. W. M. M. G. Abesinghe Banda, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service to act as Chief Secretary
for Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post,  from the date 15.10.2012 to 21.10.2012, till the return of
Mr. P. B. Amarasekara, to the Island who is the Chief Secretary for Uva Province,
