FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE  NOCHCHIYAGAMA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   Vice Chairman in  Nochchiyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Vice Chairman
has  been appointed as  Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nalin Dissanayaka   the Vice  Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of
the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   decide upon the   member of  that  Nochchiyagama   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as Vice  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Ranaweera Arachchilage Chaminda Pradeep Ranaweera ,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning  Officer of   Nochchiyagama    Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Ranaweera Arachchilage  Chaminda Pradeep Ranaweera, elected as  Vice  Chairman  of   the Nochchiyagama     Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Nochchiyagama    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
13th  December  2012.

WHEREAS the office of   Vice  Chairman in  Addalachenai   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of   Vice Chairman
has  been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Sri Lanka  Muslim Congress, the  recognized political party to which

elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF A  MEMBER  OF  THE  ADDALACHENAI   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a  member  in  Addalachenai   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as  a
member of  the Eastern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Sri Lanka  Muslim Congress , the  recognized political party to which
Ahamed Lebbe Mohamed Nazeer   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible    under the Ordinance  for Election  as a member of   that   Addalachenai  Pradeshiya
Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a  period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated  Mr.  Abdul Samad Mohamed
Uwais , being  an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to    fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer of  Addalachenai   Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare    Mr.  Abdul Samad
Mohamed Uwais  elected  as a member of the    Addalachenai   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. Achchuthan,
Returning Officer,
 Addalachenai   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
12th  December  2012.
