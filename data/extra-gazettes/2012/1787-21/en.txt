THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:Do/La/1/Ath/Adhi/78.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Three(03)  allotments of  land in total  extent of  0.0181 Hectare,
as Lot Nos. 1 -3  depicted in Preliminary plan No. Ka4301  and
dated  30th October, 2012 prepared  by the Surveyor-General,
situated  in the  Village  of  Bombuwala,  in  the Divisional   Secretary’s
Division of  Dodangoda  District of  Kalutara  in Western Province
morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of  land: Mirispelawatta; Description: Garden, Contain
Mix cultivation; Claimant: State; Original claimant: K. A. D.
Nawantha ,  Mirispelawatta, Bombuwala; Extent: 0.0068 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Malwattavilalanda; Description: Garden, Contain
Mix cultivation; Claimant:  State; Original claimant: W. Palayangoda,
Waththegedara, Bombuwala; Extent: 0.0060 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of   land: Mirispelawatta, Udumulla; Description: Garden,
Contain Mix cultivation; Claimant: State; Original claimant: D. M.
A. M. L. D.  Andradi, Mirispelawatta, Bombuwala; Extent: 0.0053
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Dodangoda Divisional Secretariat on the 08 th day of February
2013 at  10.00.a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day of  February 2013 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
                     H.W.T.R. Weththasingha,
                              Divisional Secretary,
                 Dodangoda.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Dodangoda.
