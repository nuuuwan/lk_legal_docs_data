Statement of matters in dispute
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
(1) Whether the termination of the services of the two
hundred and ten (210) factory employees of the
Daintee Foods Limited whose names are referred to
in the attached schedule 01 with effect from
15.01.2001 by the said Company is justified and to
what relief each of them is entitled ; and
(2) Whether the non-employment in service of the four
(04) factory employees of the Daintee Foods Limited
whose names are referred to in the attached schedule
02 with effect from 10.01.2001 by the said Company
is justified and to what relief each of them is entitled.

Statement of/ matters in dispute
The Matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
(1) Whether unjust has been caused to Mr. J. M. M. S.
Jayasundara of  the Peoples' Bank by the degradation of
seniority in the appointment to Class I  caused by the delay
from 1989 to 1996 in promoting him to Class II and by adding
the points wrongfully in amending points for Class I and
thereby depriving the relevant promotion and if so, to what
relief he is entitled.
(2)  Whether the complainant has been caused unjust by
the non- compliance of the disciplinary procedure in the
disciplinary inquiry held and by not conducting a further
inquiry according to the Human Rights recommendations and
if so, what relief he is entitled.
(3) Whether unjust has been caused to the complainant
by terminating him with effect from 18.12.2002 although his
services had been extended upto 27.02.2003 and if so, to
what relief he is entitled.
