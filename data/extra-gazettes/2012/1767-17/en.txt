EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   DAMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognized political party to which
D. D. Meththananda de Silva  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Damana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. D. Gamini Ranjith, being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, S. Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer of  Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. D. Gamini Ranjith   elected as  a member  of
the  Damana Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara.
09th July 2012.
