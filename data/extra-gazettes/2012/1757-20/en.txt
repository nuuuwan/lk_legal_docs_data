THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: R/D/02/197 II.
Land Ministry’s No.: LD/05/2008/HW/439.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
 Two (2) allotments of land in total Extent about 0.0025 Hectare,
depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Gam 3806 and dated 10.03.2012,
prepared by Surveyor General, Situated in the Village of Kirillawala
in the Minor Division of Adikari Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Mahara in Gampaha District in Western Province.
P. Plan No.: Gam 3806
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Millagahawatta; Description: Road; Claimant:
Mangala Wickramasinghe Dalupitiya, Mahara, Kadawatha; Extent:
0.0021 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Millagahawatta; Description: Part of Permanent
Building; Claimant: Mangala Wickramasinghe, Dalupitiya, Mahara,
Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0004 Hectare.
Inquiry Date – Lot No. 01 - 02 (11th July 2012)
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretary's Office of  Mahara on  the above date at
10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 27th June 2012, the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
Thamara D. Perera,
