PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF  1988 FILLING  OF  A V ACANCY  IN  THE  EASTERN
PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the  aforesaid  Act,  to  nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Demuni Daya Meththananda
De Silva to fill such vacancy  ;
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in  terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Demuni Daya Meththananda De Silva, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Ampara in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
29th May, 2012.
