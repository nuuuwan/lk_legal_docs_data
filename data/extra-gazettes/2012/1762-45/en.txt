PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & c., by the President
P.S. No.: E&O/HR/1/101.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1) of the Constitution
of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Gamini S. Senerath, Chief of Staff to the President to act in
the Post of Secretary to the President with effect from 15.06.2012 until Mr. Lalith Weeratunga, Secretary to the President returns
to the Island.
By  Order  of  His  Excellency,
D. S. GUNARATNE,

 (Establishment & Operations),
for Secretary to the President.
