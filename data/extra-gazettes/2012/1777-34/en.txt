NORTH  CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Proclamation made  by  the  Hon.  Governor  of  the  North Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested in  me under paragraph 8 (a) of Section 154 (b) of the Constitution of  the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Herath Mudiyanselage Karunaratna Divulgane, Governor of the North Central Province, hereby
declare that North Central Provincial Council shall meet at 9. 30 a. m. on 02.10.2012 at North Central Provincial Council
Meeting Hall located on Harischandra Mawatha, Anuradhapura.
K
ARUNARATNA DIVULGANE,
Governor ,
North Central Province.
Office of the Governor of North Central Province,
Anuradhapura,
25th  September 2012.
