THE MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER  203)
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 of the Motor Traffic Act, (Chapter 203) read with section
19 of the aforesaid Act.
KUMARA WELGAMA,
Minister of Transport.
Colombo,
12th May, 2012.
Regulations
The Motor Traffic (Construction of Vehicles) Regulations, 1983 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 248 of
June 3, 1983, as amended from time to time and lastly amended by regulations published in Gazette  Extraordinary No. 1726/12
of October 5, 2011 are hereby further amended by the repeal of paragraph (2) of regulation 3 thereof and the substitution
therefore of the following:-
''(2)(a) The overhang of an articulated vehicle shall not exceed the measurement specified for any motor vehicle in
paragraph (1) of this regulation or 2,850 millimeters, whichever is less.
      (b) The engine cubic capacity of a motor cycle shall not exceed 450 cubic centimeters (cc).''
