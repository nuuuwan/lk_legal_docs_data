STATEMENT OF MATTERS IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
1. The qualified employees of the Establishment being deprived of the opportunity of being recruited as Security Officers as a result
of outsiders being recruited to these posts by the Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka.
2. Many Security Guards in Grade I having to retire in their present posts as a result of not being promoted to posts according to
their qualifications.
3. Some of the Security Officers being promoted violating the promotion procedures.
4. Getting the promotion entitlements of Security Guards – Grade I before their retirement.
V. B. P. K. Weerasinghe,
Commissioner of Labour.
