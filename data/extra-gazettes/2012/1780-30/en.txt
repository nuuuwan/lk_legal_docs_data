EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   AKURESSA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a member
of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
the S. L. Sunil  the Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   decide upon
the   member of  that  Akuressa    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Chairman
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  I. D. Indunil Prasanga Jayaweera,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning  Officer of  Akuressa  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. I. D. Indunil
Prasanga Jayaweera , elected as  a Chairman  of   the Akuressa    Pradeshiya   Sabha.
C
ANDAUDA ARACHCHIGE  SUNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
 Akuressa   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
17th  October  2012.
