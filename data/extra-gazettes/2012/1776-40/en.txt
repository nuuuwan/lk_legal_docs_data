MINUTES ON PENSIONS
NOTICE under Minutes on Pensions issued on 5th February 1934 and Section 51 of the amended Minutes on Pensions dated
5th May, 1972.
Notice
The Minutes on Pensions dated 05.02.1934 amended from  time  to time is further amended by insertion of following
proviso immediately after the Section 2: 43 A of Minutes on Pensions.
''If any overpayment of money/ gratuity/ pension/ arrears of pension or arrears of gratuity or any other  overpayment
or otherwise any other overpayment of pension made to any retired government servant exist at the time of drawing
pension, such overpayment should be deducted from the pension.''
The amendments made by this notification to the Minutes on Pensions should be considered effective from
05th February 1934 for all purposes.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,

and Home Affairs.
