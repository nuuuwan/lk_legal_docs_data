TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order  under  Section  114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the ‘‘The Dayalbagh Radhasoami Satsang Association of Sri Lanka ’’ namely, –
1. Velayuthan Thevasenathipathy,
2. Velayuthan Sivagnanasothy,
3. Velayuthan  Sunthareswaran,
4. Kalidasan Vishnudasan,
5. Sabapathipillai Sivanathan,
6. Velupillai Thiruneelakandan, and
7. Vallipuram Kalidasan,
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87),
Now Therefore, by virtue of powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of Justice, do by this
Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that –
(a) the Trustees of the said Turst named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the rules
of the said Trust and the Declaration of Trust bearing No. 836 of September 18, 2011 and attested by Kanapathippillai
Gnanasegaram Notary Public of Colombo and shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common
seal under the name and style of ‘‘The Dayalbagh Radhasoami Satsang Association of Sri Lanka’’;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise acquire,
sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable, on behalf of
the said Trust ; and
(c) the Trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and to
be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all causes and actions arising
in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,
Colombo 01, Minister of Justice.
16th October  2012.
