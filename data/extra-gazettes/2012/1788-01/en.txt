FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   KURUWITA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Kuruwita  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Alwis Prabhath Mahesha de the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of  the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   decide upon the member of  that Kuruwita  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated   his decision to nominate
Mr. Anil Jagath Kumara Liyanage, to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning  Officer of  Kuruwita  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Anil Jagath Kumara Liyanage elected as  Vice Chairman of  the  Kuruwita  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Kuruwita  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura.
30th November, 2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KURUWITA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kuruwita  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Alwis Prabhath Mahesha de member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Kuruwita Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. Yamannalage Karunadasa,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning  Officer of  Kuruwita Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Yamannalage Karunadasa elected as a member  of  the  Kuruwita  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Kuruwita Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura.
