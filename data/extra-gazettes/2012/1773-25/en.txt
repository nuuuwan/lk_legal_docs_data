No. 1773/25 – WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 29, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a Acquisition of lands under forest
conservation.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
The  allotments of land known as Illumbakanda Watta and shown
as Lot Nos. 848 to 851 (4 lots) of land in total extent of about
20.4489 Hectare, depicted in sheet No. 01 and Supplementary
No. 01 of F.V .P. No. 932, situated in Illumbakanda Grama Niladari
Division in Uda Pattu of Kukulu Korale in Kalawana Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
1. Lot No. 848
Name of land: Illumbakanda Watta; Claimant: H. D. Piyadasa,
No. 280, Parakrama Avenue, Peliyagoda; Extent: 5.7801 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 849
Name of land: Illumbakanda Watta; Claimant: H. D. Piyadasa,
No. 280, Parakrama Avenue, Peliyagoda; Extent: 4.1265 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 850
Name of land: Illumbakanda Watta; Claimant: H. D. Piyadasa,
No. 280, Parakrama Avenue, Peliyagoda; Extent: 3.0013 Hectare.
4. Lot No. 851
Name of land: Illumbakanda Watta; Claimant: H. D. Piyadasa,
No. 280, Parakrama Avenue, Peliyagoda; Extent: 7.5410 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Kalawana on the 26th day of November,
2012 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of November, 2012
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
G . D. L. Udaya Kumari,
Divisional Secretary,
Kalawana.
Divisional Secretary’s Division,
Kalawana.
22nd August, 2012.
