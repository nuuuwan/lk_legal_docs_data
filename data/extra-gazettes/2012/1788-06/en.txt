No. 1788/6 – TUESDAY, DECEMBER 11, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Seven (07) allotments of land described below containing in extent
of 1.7437 Hectares, shown in Surveyor-General’s Advance Tracing
No. MA/PLL/93/185, situated in the village of Kirioruwa in the
Minor Division  of  Udugoda Udasiya Pattuwa of Pallepola Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Matale District in the Central Province.
01. Lot No. 916
Name of land: Pihilithuduwa Kumbura; Description: –; Claimants:
K. G . Dingiri Amma, K. G . Ukku Amma, K. G . Leelawathi and
K. G . Karunawathi, Kirioruwa, Akuramboda;  Extent: 0.0123 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 917
Name of land: Pahala Pihillathuduwa; Description: –; Claimants:
R. A. Karunarathne and R. A. Dingiri Amma, Mananwatta, Wahakotte;
Extent: 0.0121 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 918
Name of land: Pahala Pihillathuduwa; Description: –; Claimants:
R. A. Karunarathne and R. A. Dingiri Amma, Mananwatta, Wahakotte;
Extent: 0.0026 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 930
Name of land: Pahala Mahawelakumbura; Description: –;
Claimants: P. M. P. M. Abesinghe, “Abaya Mahal”, Mananwatta,
Wahakotte and M. A. Wijerathne, Kirioruwa, Akuramboda; Extent:
0.0082 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 931 2/1
Name of land: Ihala Mahawela Pillewa; Description: Private Canal;
Claimant: –; Extent: 0.0004 Hectare.
06. Lot No. 931 2/2
Name of land: Pahala Mahawelakumbura, Ihala Mahawela
Kamatha; Description: –; Claimant: P. G . Guruhamy, Kirioruwa,
Akuramboda; Extent: 0.0121 Hectare.
07. Lot No. 932
Name of land: Ehetuwelagawa Pillewa  alias Laime Kanaththa;
Description: –; Claimant: K. K. J. Karunakumara, Kirioruwa,
Akuramboda; Extent: 0.0002 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Pallepola Divisional Secretariat, on the 30th day of January, 2013
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th day of January, 2013, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
W. M. Ananda,
Divisional Secretary,
Pallepola.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Pallepola,
29th November, 2012.
