EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  AMPARA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Ampara   Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of   being elected as a  member of
the Eastern Province Provincial   Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Abeysundara
Wikramasooriya Boosabaduge Nishantha Manjula Fernando,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section
65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Ampara  Urban Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  aforesaid  recognised political party has nominated Mr. H. H. Mahee Prasad De
Silva  being  an eligible person under the  aforesaid  Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer  of   the Ampara   Urban Council,  acting in  terms
of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. H. H. Mahee Prasad De
Silva  elected as a member of the  Ampara  Urban Council .
S.  ACHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Ampara  Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
17th  October 2012.
