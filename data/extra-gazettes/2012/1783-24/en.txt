EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Mawanella  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected  as  a member
of  the Sabaragamuwa  Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
B. P. Indunil Shantha Gunasena  the Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   decide upon the   member of  that  Mawanella    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be
declared  elected as  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Korale Gedara Piyathissa,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Talangama Appuhamilage  Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning  Officer of   Mawanella   Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Korale Gedara Piyathissa , elected as   Chairman  of   the Mawanella     Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C. N. TALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
 Mawanella    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalla,
06th  November  2012.

NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A (1) (a)  OF THE LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Dehiowita  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning  Officer  of  the Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10 A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (chapter 262) that Mr. Priyantha Udaya Kumara

the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
