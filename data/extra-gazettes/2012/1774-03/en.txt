SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  40  OF  1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Rishad
Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this
order declare that,
(I) A Cess shall be charged, levied and paid on import of any article specified in the Column II and Column  III at the
rates specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule  I hereto, with effect from 03
rd September,
2012.  Accordingly, the respective Cess rates  for articles specified under HS Headings 69.07, 69.08, 69.10, 69.11,
69.12, 69.13 and 69.14 mentioned in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 1733/6 dated 21.11.2011, are hereby revised;
 And
(II) Cess charged on import of  selected articles specified under HS Headings 07.12 and 72.13 in the schedule
II hereto, mentioned in the Schedule of the Gazette Extraodinary, No. 1764/5, dated 27.06.2012, is hereby
removed ;
with effect from 03rd September 2012.
RISHAD BA THIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of Industry and Commerce,
Colombo,
03rd September, 2012.
