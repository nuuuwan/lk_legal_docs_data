NOTICE CALLING FOR CLAIMS TO 
LAND PARCELS 
 
Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No : 52/0679 Colombo District  
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 520502 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Dutugemunu (Block 
08) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Divi-
sion of 537A - Dutugemunu in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Dehiwala in the District of Colombo in the Province of West-
ern should submit his claim to the undersigned before 30th Octo-
ber, 2012 In the event of failure  to submit any claim before the 
above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte 
inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such 
determination in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 
of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 520802 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Land Title 
Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement. 
 
 A.H.S.W IJESINGHE, 
 Commissioner of Land Title Settlement. 
   
Land Title Settlement Department, 
1200/6, “Mihikatha Madura”

Battaramulla
