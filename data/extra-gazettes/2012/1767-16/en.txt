THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is  required for a Public Purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in total extent of  0.4360 Hectare, depicted in
Preliminary Plan No. GA./3910 and dated 08.11.2011 prepared by
the  Surveyor General situated in the Village of Hipankanda in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Karandeniya in the Galle  District
in Southern  Province morefully described as follows.
01.  Lot No. 01
Name of land: Galkanda alias Gonagahakele  alias Gonagala Kele ;
Description : Garden has permenant building and a toilet ;  Claimant :
State; Original Claimant : Chief incumbent venerable Hipankande
Saddhasiri Thero, Nawadagala, Hipankanda ; Extent: 0.4360 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Karandeniya on  31st day of October
2012 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before 24 th day of October, 2012 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. N. S. T. K. De Silva,
Divisional Secretary,
Karandeniya.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Karandeniya,
13th July, 2012.
