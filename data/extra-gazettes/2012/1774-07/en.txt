EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   DICKWELLA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Dickwella   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a member
of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the  recognized political party to which
the R. P . Sudusingha  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Dickwella    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  K. P . Somasiri,  being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning  Officer of  Dickwella   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. K. P. Somasiri ,
elected as  a member  of   the  Dickwella   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
C. A.  SUNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Dickwella  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
23rd  August  2012.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   POLGAHAWELA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Polgahawela   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the  recognized political party to which
Wijesundara Wickramasinghe Panditharathne Wasala Mudiyanse Ralahamillage Vimukthi Duminda Bangdara Wijesundara
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible
under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Polgahawela    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall
be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Edirisinghe Hewage
Somarathne Edirisinghe  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Muhammad Nafeel,  Returning  Officer of   Polgahawela   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Edirisinghe Hewage
Somarathne Edirisinghe  elected as  a member  of   the  Polgahawela  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. O. M.   N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Polgahawela  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
27th  August  2012.
