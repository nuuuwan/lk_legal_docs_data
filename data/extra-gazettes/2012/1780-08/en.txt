THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry’s No: 03J/83/IR/28.
My Reference No: Nawa/L/1/1/11/LA4603.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment  of  land described below in total  extent of  about
2.0971 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 408 in Supplement No. 10 of
the Preliminary Plan No. F.V .P. 3218 prepared by the  Surveyor-
General, situated  in the  Village  called Koonkadawala in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 645 Koonkadawala of Kirimetiyapattuwa
Minor Division of the Divisional   Secretary’s  Division of
Nawagattegama in Puttlam District in North Western province and
bounded as follows:
Lot No. 408
Name of the Land: Dangahawatta; Description: Hena; Claimant:
R. H. M. Punchibanda, R. H. M. Dingiriamma and R. H. M. Dingiri
Banda;   Extent: About 2.0971 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Nawagattegama Divisional Secretary’s Office on 19th
December, 2012 at 10.00  a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of December,
2012 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars or their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
produced before me at the inquiry.
                         
R. P. G. Podineris,
Divisional Secretary,
