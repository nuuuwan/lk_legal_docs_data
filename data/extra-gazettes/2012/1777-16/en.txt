No. 1777/16  — MONDAY    SEPTEMBER  24,  2012
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTIONS   ACT,  NO.  1  OF   1981
Filling of a Vacancy under Section 64(5)
WHEREAS   the Secretary General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64(5) of  the Parliamentary Elections Act, No.
1 of 1981, as amended by Section 6 of the Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 35 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the
membership of the Seventh Parliament by reason of the resignation of Mrs. Malani Fonseka being a member nominated by the
United People's Freedom  Alliance, a recognized political party, and declared elected as a Member of Parliament under Article
99A of the Constitution.
And whereas the Secretary of that Political Party was required by me under the aforesaid section to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid Political Party has nominated Mrs. Malani Fonseka to fill such  vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers conferred on me by
Section 64(5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, do hereby declare Mrs. Malani Fonseka elected as a member of
the Seventh Parliament to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of aforesaid Mrs. Malani Fonseka.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
24th September, 2012
