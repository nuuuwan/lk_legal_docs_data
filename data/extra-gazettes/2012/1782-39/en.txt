STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the decrease of salary of Mr. B. A. Indradasa, who was serving as the Director Operations of the Sri Lanka Transport Board from Rs.
12,000/- to Rs. 8690/- in removing him from his office on 29.06.2004 is just and if not so,  to what relief he is entitled for from 29.06.2004
to 09.12.2006 his date of retirement.
V. B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 14th day of September 2012.
