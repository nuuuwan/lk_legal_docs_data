PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
THE REGULATION OF INSURANCE INDUSTRY ACT No. 43 of 2000
Order under Section 31
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1A) of  Section 31 of the Regulation of Insurance Industry Act No. 43 of
2000, as amended by the Regulation of Insurance Industry (Amendment) Act No. 27 of 2007 read with article 44(2) of  the
Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, do by this order declare
that:-
a. Thirty percentum as being the percentage payable by the insurer on the total liability arising out of every
general re-insurance, such insurer is required to re-insure with National Insurance Trust Fund Board estab-
lished by the National Insurance Trust Fund Act No. 28 of 2006 excluding long-term re-insurance contracts
entered into by the insurer.
b. The order made under the Subsection (1A) of Section 31 of the Regulation of Insurance Industry Act No. 43 of
2000, as amended by the Regulation of Insurance Industry (Amendment) Act No. 27 of 2007 and published in
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1528/20 of 19.12.2007 is hereby rescinded.
c. This order shall come into effect from 01.01.2013
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
Minister of Finance & Planning.
Colombo,
31st December, 2012.
