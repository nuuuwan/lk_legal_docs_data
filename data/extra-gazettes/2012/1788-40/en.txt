EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   DEHIOWITA     PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of being   ceased to be a
member of  the Party ;
 And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Priyantha Udaya Kumara  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Dehiowita    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Mahathanthilage
Ravindra Lakmal Samararathna,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning  Officer of  Dehiowita
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  Mahathanthilage Ravindra Lakmal Samararathna, elected as  a member  of   the Dehiowita  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C.  N. THALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle,
11th  December, 2012.
