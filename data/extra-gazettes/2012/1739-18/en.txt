WESTERN  PROVINCE PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, the Chief Minister and the Minister  of Finance   Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local
Government  Provincial Administration, Power and Energy, Manpower and Employment, Economic Development and  Trade
of   Western Province hereby enact the rules on expenses for settling residential and mobile telephone bills for duties of the
members of Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province prepared according to the powers ventured upon me by Section No. 03
of the statute to grant telephone facilities for the duties of the members of Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province
No. 10 of 2010.
These rules will come in to effect from the date of publishing a notification on the obtainment of  the approval of the
Provincial Council of western Province after obtaining aforesaid approval.
P
RASANNA RANATHUNGA,
The Chief Minister and the Minister  of
 Finance and Planning, Law and Order,  Land,
Education, Local Government ,
Provincial Administration, Power and Energy,
Manpower and Employment, Economic Development
and Trade of   Western Province.
Srawasthi Mandiraya,
Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
04th January, 2012.
RULES
These rules will be identified as rules on  expenses for settling bills for residential and mobile phone facilities
for duties of the members of Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province.
The maximum amount that can be bored by the pradeshiya Sabha Fund for settling residential and mobile phone bills
for duties of the members of Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province is Two Thousand Rupees. (Rs. 2000/-).
