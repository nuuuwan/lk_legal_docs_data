Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of Science (Amendment) Ordinance No. 1 of 1996, and
shall come into operation with effect from April 25, 2012.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of Science Ordinance No.1 of 1996 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 909/13 of
February 9, 1996 is hereby further amended, by the repeal of paragraph 9 thereof and the substitution therefor of the following
paragraph : -
“ 9. The Board of  Management, (hereinafter referred to as the “Board”) shall function as the academic and
executive body of the institute and shall consist of the following persons :-
(1) the following ex-officio members, namely -
(a) the Director ;
(b) the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister to whom the subject of Higher Education is assigned, or the
nominee of such Secretary ;
(c) the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister to whom the subject of  Finance is assigned, or the nominee
of such Secretary ;
