EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   NOCHCHIYAGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Nochchiyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as
a member of  the North Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
the K. P. S. Kumarasiri  the Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
decide upon the   member of  that  Nochchiyagama    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nalin Dissanayaka,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning  Officer of   Nochchiyagama  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nalin Dissanayaka, elected as  a Chairman  of   the Nochchiyagama   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
 Nochchiyagama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
18th  October  2012.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   GALENBINDUNUWEWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of being elected as
a member of the North Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Patriotic National Front, the  recognized political party to which
Punchi Bandage Ranjith Kumara   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Galenbindunuwewa   Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Jayalath  Mudiyanselage
Abewickckrama Jayalath,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning  Officer of  Galenbindunuwewa
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr. Jayalath  Mudiyandelage Abewickckrama Jayalath, elected as  a member  of   the  Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
19th  October  2012.
