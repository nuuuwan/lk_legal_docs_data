Appointments   by  the  Hon.  Governor  of  the  Uva  Province
I hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province.
01. Removing Mr. Sumith Suraweera Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, from the Post of Secretary
to the Council of the Uva Provincial Council, with effect from the date 27.02.2012 and attach him to office of the
Chief Secretary of Uva Province.

Council of the  Uva Provincial Council,  with effect from the date 05.03.2012.

of the Senior Assistant  Secretary to the Ministry of Agriculture, Agrarian Development, Animal Products, Inland

post, with effect from the date 05.03.2012.

Province, in addition to her permanent post, from the date 12.03.2012 to 25.04.2012, till the return of Dr. Mrs. N. S.
R. Hewageegana to the Island who is the Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province.
