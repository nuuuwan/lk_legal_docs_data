No. 1775/20 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 14, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Mini’s Ref. No.:–
Ref No.: RA T/ LAN/ 09/FI/77.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An  allotment of land in total extent of about 0.1953 Hectare,
depicted as Lot No. 23 in the Cadastral Map No.521001/06/01
dated 17.01.2012 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the
Village called Kandawala within Municipal Council Limits of
Dehiwala Mount Lavenia in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Rathmalana in Colombo District of the Western Province and more
fully described as follows.
Lot No. 23
Name of land: Kandawala Watta; Description: Assmt. No. 76,
Kandawala Watta, Kandawala Road, Rathmalana; Original claimant:
Da Vinci B.S. D. Engineering Construction and Investment (Pvt)
Ltd., No. 39, St. Michael’s Road, Colombo 03; Extent: 0.1953 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat at Galle Road, Ratmalana on 15th day of
November, 2012 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08th day of November,
2012 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
Pradeep Rathnayake,
Divisional Secretary,
Rathmalana.
Divisional Secretary’s Division,
Ratmalana.
11th September, 2012.
