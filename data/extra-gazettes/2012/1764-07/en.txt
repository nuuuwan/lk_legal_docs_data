PART  IV (A) – PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
ARTICLE  154  (B)  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
BY Virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph (8) of Article 154  (B) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka. I, Herath Mudiyanselage Karunarathna Divulgane, Governor of the North Central Province do hereby dissolve
the North Central Provincial Council with effect from midnight 27th June, 2012.
K
ARUNARATHNA DIVULGANE,
Governor of the North Central Province.
Office of the Governor of the North Central Province,
27th June, 2012.
