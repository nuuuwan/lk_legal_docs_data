THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Lanka Security Service Union, No. 103, P. S. Perera Mawatha, Mampe, Piliyandala.
and
Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 500, T. B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10.
Statement of matters in dispute
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
1. Whether unjust has been caused to qualified employees of the Mahaweli Authority by recruitment of employees from outside the
establishment in the recruitment/filling of vacancies of Security Officers, and if so, to what relief they are entitled.
2. Whether of the Officers in Grade I of the Security Guards service have been caused unjust by not being promoted and having to retire
in their earlier posts as a result, and if so to what relief they are entitled.
3. Whether the qualified Security Officers have been caused unjust, by others being promoted, violating the accepted promotion
procedures, and if so, to what relief the qualified Officers are entitled.
4. Whether the qualified Security Officers of Grade I who have retired from service, have been caused unjust by not being promoted
their retirement and if so, to what relief they are entitled.
V. B. P. K. Weerasinghe,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 03rd day of August 2012.
