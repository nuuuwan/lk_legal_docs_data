THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: KA/LD/1/1/43.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of  land in total extent of  0.0022 Hectare,
depicted as Lot Nos. 1 & 3 in the Preliminary Plan No. Ga 3850  and
dated 03.11.2012 prepared   by the Surveyor-General, situated  in
the Village  of  Kurundugahahethekma,  in  the Divisional   Secretary’s
Division of  Karandeniya in the District of  Galle  in Southern
Province and morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Shantha Lenard on c watta; Description:
Drain; Claimant: State; Encroached by: H.D. Jayanthi,
Kurundugahahethekma;  Extent: 0.0017 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Shantha Lenard on c watta; Description: Drain;
Claimant: State; Encroached by : I.D.Chandana Predeep Kumara,
Kurundugahahethekma;  Extent: 0.0005 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Karandeniya Divisional Secretariat on the 30th January 2013
at  10.00.a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the  23rd January 2013 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
H.N.S.T.K de Silva,
Divisional Secretary,
Karandeniya.

Karandeniya.
