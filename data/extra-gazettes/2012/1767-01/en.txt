THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
THE  METAL  QUARRY AND  METAL  CRUSHING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation  30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that   Mrs. Anjali Wijesingha  has been  appointed
as the Employers' Representatives Member of  the  Wages Board for  the  Metal  Quarry and Metal  Crushing  Trade   in place
of  Mr. D. S. W. Wijerathne by the  Hon. Minister of Labour nd  Labour Relations  under Section 9 of the Wages Boards
Ordinance (Chapter 136) . This Appointment is valid only up to 15.08.2013.
W. J. L. U.WIJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and  Labour Ralations.
