1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   THANAMALWILA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  a vacancy has occurred in the Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha due to a member ceasing to hold office by reason
of being ordered for vigourous imprisonment to five years ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Kamkanam Witharanage Chandrasekara the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Thanamalwila Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has failed to make a nomination  within the
period specified ;
Now therefore, I,  Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in  terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kumarasingha
Wanniarachchige Susantha Ranjith who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared
elected at the election held on 17th March 2011, to Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha elected as  a member  of   the Thanamalwila
Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Tha namalwila Pradeshiya Sabha.
Monaragala,
27th  July  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   PANADURA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
H. Jagath Pushpa Kumara perera  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated Mr. Moratuwage Ajith Kumara
Sirisena being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiries, Returning Officer of Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Moratuwage Ajith
Kumara Sirisena elected as  a member  of   the Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kalutara,
02nd August  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   KAYTS   PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of Vice Chairman in Kayts  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Paramalingam Arulkaran  the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  decide upon the member of that Kayts Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Vice
Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Manuel Fernando Felix Alberd to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Ponnudurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of  Kayts  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in  terms of  Section
65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Manuel Fernando Felix Alberd
elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Kayts  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kayts  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Jaffna,
27th July,  2012.
