FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
B. P. Indunil Shantha Gunasena  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Mawanella Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Mohamad Niyas Mohamad
Aslam being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama  Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of Mawanella Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Mohamad Niyas Mohamad Aslam elected as  a member  of   the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. TALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kegalle,
20th  November  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  NOCHCHIYAGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Nochchiyagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member of the North Central Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
K. P. S. Kumarasiri  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Nochchiyagama Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Ahamad Mohomad Hanifa
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of Nochchiyagama Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Ahamad Mohomad Hanifa elected as  a member  of   the Nochchiyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Nochchiyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Anuradhapura,
15th  November  2012.
