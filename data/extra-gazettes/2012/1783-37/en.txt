THE  EXTRA - ORDINARY GAZETTE  No. 1670/32
THE Minute on the Sri Lanka Planning Service published in Extra–Ordinary Gazette No. 1670/32 dated 10.09.2010 is amended
on the approval of Public Service Commission as follows :–
06 Posts belonging to the category of officers
I
6.2  Number of combined officers : 832 II grades
III
10.3  Promotion from Grade I to Special Grade
10.3.1 Promotion of Grade I officers of the Sri Lanka Planning Service who have fulfilled following qualifications to
Special Grade will be effected by the Public Service Commission or an officer authorized by the Cabinet of Ministers :–
Accordingly an officer –
(i) Should have completed 18 years service in the Sri Lanka Planning Service and 05 years service in Grade I and
has earned all the increments on the due dates within the period of 05 years immediately preceding of gaining
eligibility for promotion ;
(ii) Should  have not been subjected to a disciplinary action for an offence committed within the period of 05 years
immediately preceding for promotion ;  (Excluding warnings)
(iii) Should have gained a performance level above the satisfactory level within the period of 05 years immediately
preceding for promotion in terms of the approved performance evaluation procedure.
The Appointing Authority will promote the officers who fulfilled the above qualifications with effect from the date of
fulfilling all the qualifications, to the Special Grade based on the existing number of vacancies of the Special Grade as per the
recommendation of a Board of Interview appointed by the Appointing Authorithy.
