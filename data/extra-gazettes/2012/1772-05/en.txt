L. D. B – 21/78 VII
UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 20 (4) (a)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by paragraph (a) of subsection (4) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister Higher Education, having being satisfied after
consulting the relevant authorities of Higher Educational Institutions and Institutes for Higher Learning attached to them,
that due to the on going strike the work and the administration of such Higher Educational Institutions and Institutes for
Higher Learning as specified in the Schedule hereto, have been seriously dislocated, do by this Order, close such Institutions
and Institutes with effect from 21st August, 2012 in relation to academic activities, until further notice.
D
ISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA
BANDA DISSANAY AKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
21st August, 2012.
