THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
     My Ref. No.: DS/KGK/3/2/W/2005/23.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
One allotment (01) of  land called Ogastawatta, extent of about
0.0304 Hectare out of the total extent, shown in the P.P.MAHA
6171 and dated 11th January, 2012 prepared by Senior-
Superintendent of  Surveys (Kandy District), situated in the Village
of Bowalawatta, in the Grama Niladari Division No. 247 of
Bowalawatta in the Minor Division of Gangawata Korale in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale
in Kandy District, Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Ogastawatta; Original Claimant: Manager,
Kandy City mission Institute, D. S. Senanayaka Veediya, Kandy;
Extent: 0.0304 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretariat of Kandy and Gangawata Korale,
on the  27th day of June, 2012 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the  13th day
of  June, 2012  the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of  compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
D. M. N. G . G. T. Karunarathne,
 Divisional Secretary,
Kandy and Gangawata Korale.
