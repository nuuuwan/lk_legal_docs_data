THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act , that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of land called Kaluwala Kumbura in extent of about
0.0276 Hectare, as Lot No. 1 depicted in Advance Tracing No.
CO/HMG/2010/214 of 13.07.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-
General situated in the village of  Kirigampamunuwa in the Minor
Division of Udugaha Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Homagama in  Colombo District in Western Province and  bounded
as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Kaluwala Kumbura – Extent: 0.0276 Hectare
North: Kaluwala Kumbura claimed by B. D. Nandawathi and
others; East: Ela; South: Kaluwala Kumbura claimed by B. D.
Nandawathi and others; West: Southern Expressway.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
