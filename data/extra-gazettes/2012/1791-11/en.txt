No. 1791/11 – MONDAY, DECEMBER 31, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land called Pelmadulla Estate in extent of
1.5518 Hectares, depicted as Lots ‘A ’ – ‘D’ in Advance Tracing
No. R/PLM/2011/665 prepared by the Survey-Superintendent of
Ratnapura and situated in Panawenna Uda Pattuwa of Pelmadulla
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ratnapura District and bounded
as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’ – Pelmadulla Estate – Extent: 0.6282 Hectare
North and East: Pelmadulla Estate; South: Pelmadulla Estate and
Road; West: Road and Pelmadulla Estate.
2. Lot ‘B’ – Pelmadulla Estate – Extent: 0.6236 Hectare
North and East: Road; South: Road, Ela, Pelmadulla Estate and
Road; West: Ela, Pelmadulla Estate and Road.
3. Lot ‘C’ – Pelmadulla Estate – Extent: 0.0993 Hectare
North: Pelmadulla Estate and Road; East: Road; South: Pelmadulla
Estate and Road; West: Pelmadulla Estate.
4. Lot ‘D’ – Pelmadulla Estate – Extent: 0.2007 Hectare
North: Ela and Road; East and South: Road and Pelmadulla Estate;
West: Road, Pelmadulla Estate and Ela.
