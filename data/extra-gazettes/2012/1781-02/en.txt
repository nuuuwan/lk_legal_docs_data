EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN OF  THE  MAHAW A  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary  of the United People's Freedom Alliance the recognised  Political Party to which
Medhagedhara Menik Ralalage Rohana Vasantha Kumara Medhagedhara, the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me
under Section 65A(1) of  the aforesaid  Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Mahawa  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised Political Party   has  communicated  his decision to nominate
Mr. R. Nimal Wickramasingha to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Muhammadu  Nafeel, Returning Officer of  Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in  terms
of   Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. R. Nimal Wickramasingha
elected as Vice Chairman of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
17th October, 2012.
