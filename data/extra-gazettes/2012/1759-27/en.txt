THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: 2/1/3/1/9.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment  of  land in total  extent of  0.0116 Hectare, depicted as
Lot No. 1 in preliminary plan No. Ga 3710  and  dated  31.05.2010
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the village of  Kudauragaha,
in  the Divisional   Secretary’s  Division of  Benthota  in the District of
Galle  in Southern Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Niyakolahena; Description: Cinnamon cultivation,
contain 20 Cinnamon bushes about 10 years old; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: S.H. Sunil Shantha, Niyakolahena, Kudauragaha,
Uragaha; Extent: 0.0116 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Benthota Divisional Secretariat on the 26.07.2012 at  10.00.a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on
or before the 19.07.2012 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars or their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
K. K. Gunapala,
Divisional Secretary,
Benthota.
Divisional Secretary’s Office, Benthota.
