WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, Chief  Minister and the Subject  Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education,
Local Government, Provincial Administration, Electricity and Energy Human Resources and Employment and Economic
Development by virtue of powers vested in me under section 184 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 which should
be read along with section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act , No. 12 of 1989 do hereby enact regulations
for the payment of allowances to the Chairman, Vice Chairman, and Members of Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province for
attending to Committee meetings,
This regulation is valid with effect from the date of publication of the notification in respect of approval of the
Provincial Council of Western Province in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic Sri Lanka after having been so
approved.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief  Minister of Western Province and the Minister of Finance,
Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education, Local Government,
 Provincial Administration, Electricity and Energy Human Resources and
Employment and Economic Development,
Western Province.
At the Chief Minister of,
Western Province,
Situated at Srawasthi Mandiraya,
Colombo 07,
On this 20 day of  July 2012.
REGULATIONS
1. These regulations are identified as Regulations for the payment of allowances for Chairman, Vice Chairman and
Members of Pradeshiya Sabha for attending to Committee meetings.
2. When a Chairman, Vice Chairman or a Member of a Pradeshiya Sabha is a member of committee appointed under
Section 12 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, a sum of Rs.500 can be paid to him from the Pradeshiya Sabha General
Fund for one meeting for attending to meeting of every day the said committee meets.

Notification
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, Chief  Minister and the Subject Minister of Finance,  Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education, Local
Government, Provincial Administration, Electricity  and Energy Human Resources and Employment and Economic Development
by virtue of powers vested in me under section 193(1) (GG) of Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255),  read along with
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 do hereby enact regulations for the payment
of allowances to the Chairman, Vice Chairman, and Members of Urban Councils of Western Province for attending to
Standing Committee meetings,
This regulation is valid with effect from the date of publication of the notification in respect of approval of the
Provincial Council of Western Province in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic Sri Lanka after having been so
approved.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief  Minister of Western Province and the Minister of Finance,
Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education, Local Government,
 Provincial Administration, Electricity and Energy Human Resources and
Employment and Economic Development,
 Western Province.

Members of Urban Councils for attending to Committee meetings.
2. When a Chairman, Vice Chairman and Members of Urban Council is a member of committee appointed under
Section 29 of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255), an allowance can be paid to him from the Urban Council General

Chairman of a Urban Council, all the members of the Urban Council  shall have obtained membership at least in one committee.
4. Under these regulations, the maximum number of committees for which allowances can be received shall be four
committees per month.

WESTERN  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, Chief  Minister and the Subject Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education, Local
Government, Provincial Administration, Electricity  and Energy Human Resources and Employment and Economic Development
by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 289 of Municipal Council Ordinance,  read along with Section 2 of Provincial
Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 do hereby enact regulations for the payment of allowances to the
Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Members of Muncipal Councils of Western Province for attending to Standing Committee
meetings.
This regulation is valid with effect from the date of publication of the notification in respect of approval of the
Provincial Council of Western Province in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic Sri Lanka after having been so
approved.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief  Minister of Western Province and the Minister of Finance,
Planning, Law and Peace, Land, Education, Local Government,
 Provincial Administration, Electricity and Energy Human Resources and
Employment and Economic Development,
 Western Province.
At the Chief Minister of,
Western Province,
Situated at Srawasthi Mandiraya,
Colombo 07,
On this 20 day of  July 2012.
REGULATIONS
1. These regulations are identified as Regulations for the payment of allowances for Mayor, Deputy Mayor and
Members of Muncipal Councils for attending to Standing Committee meetings.
2. When a Mayor, Deputy Mayor or a  Member of a Muncipal Councils is a member of Standing committee
appointed under section 26 of the Municipal  Council  Ordinance (Chapter 252), an allowance can be paid to him from the
Municipal  Council General Fund for every day the said standing committee meets at Rs.500 for one meeting for attending.
3. Prior to receiving membership more than once in certain Standing committee that appoints any member other
than the Mayor of a Municipal Council, all the members of the Municipal Council  shall have obtained membership at least in
one standing committee.
4. Under these regulations, the maximum number of Standing committees for which allowances can be received
shall be four  standing committees per month.
