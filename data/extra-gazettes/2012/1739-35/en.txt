Land Acquisition Notices
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: 4/1/5/16.
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/05/2007/D/223.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
The allotments of land in extent of about 0.5819 Hectare, depicted
as Lot Nos. 01 and 02 in the Preliminary Plan No. 2267 dated
03rd November, 2011, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in Hadirawalana Pahala Gama in Minor Division of Mahagalboda
Megoda Korale West in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kurunegala, in the District of Kurunegala.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Pahala Kade Watta; Description: Police Station;
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.5672 Hectare.
North: Bellanwala Paddy Fields claimed by K. P. Wijerathna,
K. P. C. P . Eriyagolla, K. P. Nimal Asiri Jayalath and others; East:
Lot No. 2; South: Lot No. 34 in P.P .Ku. 1422; West: Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha).
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Pahala Kade Watta; Description: Garden; Claimant:
Not known; Extent: 0.0247 Hectare.
North: Bellanwala Paddy Fields claimed by K. P. Wijerathna,
K. P. C. P . Eriyagolla, K. P. Nimal Asiri Jayalath and others; East:
Lot Nos. 1 and 2 in P .P.Ku. 1422; South: Lot No. 34 in P.P.Ku. 1422;
West: Lot No. 1.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Kurunegala on the 27th day of February,
2012 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 20th day of February, 2012, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
R. M. R. Rathnayaka,
Divisional Secretary,
Kurunegala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kurunegala,
04th January, 2012.
