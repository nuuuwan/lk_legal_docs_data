EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE    COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Colombo Municipal  Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Death;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which  Mohomed
Maharoof   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for  Election as a member of that Colombo Municipal Council who belongs to such  party who
shall be declared  elected as a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has nominated Mr. Rathnayake Mudiyanselage
Thushara Hemantha being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hannadige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning  Officer  of  Colombo Municipal Council ,  acting in
terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.  Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Thushara Hemantha elected as a member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council .
Colombo City Elections Office,
Rajagiriya,
13th  December, 2012.
