STAMP  DUTY  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  12 OF  2006
Order  under  Section  5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 2006 read with
article 44 (2) of the Constitution. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this
Order, amend the order made under that section and published in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1465/20 dated
October 05, 2006 as last amended by Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1757/4 of May 09.2012, by the addition of the
following new item immediately after item 28 of the schedule thereto.
SCHEDULE
‘‘29. Share Certificate on the initial issue of any share by :
- a company to another company where both companies are owned by the Government of Sri Lanka ; or
- a company which has entered into an agreement with the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka under the
Strategic Development Project Act, No. 14 of 2008 to a company owned by the Government of Sri Lanka.’’
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
4th October  2012.
