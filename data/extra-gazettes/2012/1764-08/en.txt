PART  IV (A) – PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
ARTICLE  154  (B)  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
BY Virtue of powers vested in me by Chapter XVIIA Paragraph (8) of Article 154  (B) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. I, Wijesinghe Jayaweera Mudiyanselage Lokubandara, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa
Province do hereby dissolve the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council with effect from midnight 27th June, 2012.
W.J.M. L
OKUBANDARA,
Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province.
Office of the Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province,
27th June, 2012.
