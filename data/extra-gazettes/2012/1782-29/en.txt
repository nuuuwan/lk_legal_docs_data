PRADESHIYA   SABHA   ACT,   No.  15  OF  1987
Under Section 134(1)
It is hereby declared that I have approved the area denoted by the resolution in the schedule hereto adopted in the general
meeting held on 30th November 2010 in terms of the powers vested to Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha under Section 134(1) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987 as a developed area in terms of the powers vested to me under section 134(1) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987 to be read with Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act No.12 of 1989.
V
IRAJ MERVYN PERERA,
Asst: COMMISSIONER OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Polonnaruwa District.
On 24th October, 2012
At Polonnaruwa.
SCHEDULE
Resolution :-
32. Namely Thalpotha town described in the following schedule in Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha area in terms of Section 134(1)
of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987.
North .— Bounded to the area starting from 317 Junction of Polonnaruwa Singhapura road towards “Somasiri Stores”
(Covering 100 meters towards west from the center axis) and headed to the by road to Hingurakgoda (Covering 100 meters
towards north) and up to the running across 141 and 151 through Weliela road (Covering 100 meters towards west)
East .— Bounded to the area running through Polonnaruwa Singhapura road passing 316 junction and up to 317 junction
running close to Pradeshiya Sabha name board (Covering 100 meters from the center axis)
South .— Bounded to the area starting from Kaluwaragashandiya and running close to weekly fair up to ‘’Peris Boutique” and
from that point up to 316 junction through Polonnaruwa Singhapura road.
