NOTIFICATION
P.S. No. : CSA/1/16/1.
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Gitanjana Rupasinghe Gunawardena, MP, who was appointed to the post of Deputy Minister
of Finance and Planning by His Excellency the President, in terms of powers vested in him by Article 46(1) of the Constitution
and the Notification of which appointment was published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1681/2 of November 22, 2010 has
resigned from the said post of Deputy Minister by Article 47(b), with effect from November 01, 2012.
By order of His Excellency,
L
ALITH  WEERA TUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
