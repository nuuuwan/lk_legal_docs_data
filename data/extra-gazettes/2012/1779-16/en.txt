CONSTITUTION  OF THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF SRI  LANKA
Direction  under  Article 22
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 22 (1) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby direct that both the Sinhala and Tamil Languages be used as the languages of
administration in the Divisional Secretary’s Divisions set out in the Schedule hereto.

AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
President.
