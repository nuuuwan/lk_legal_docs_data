TRUSTS    ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 87)
 Order under Section 114
WHEREAS  the Trustees of the ''M. H.  Kanji Charitable Trust'' namely,-
1. Mr. Abbasally Akbarally,
2. Mr. Huzefa Akbarally,
3. Mr. Ali Asghar Akbarally,
4. Mrs. Khaleda Bardai, and
5. Mr. Ali Reza Bardai.
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance
(Chapter 87).
Now Therefore, by virtue of powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of Justice,
do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that -
(a) the Trustees of the said named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the rules
of the said Trust and the Declaration of Trust bearing No. 61 of June 8, 2001 and September 18, 2001 attested
by Harold Tyronne Mathews, Notary Public of Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession
and  a common seal under the name and style of the ''M. H. Kanji Charitable Trust'' ;
(b) the  Said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust ; and
(c) the Trustees shall by  the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and
to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all causes and actions
arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be
necessary.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
09th August 2012.
