PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
MINUTE   OF   THE   PROGRAMME    OFFICERS’   SERVICE
Revision No. 01
IT  has been ordered by the Public Service Commission to revise the name of the Minute of the Programme Officers' Service
which was published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1745/11 dated
14.02.2012 as the “Minute  of the Development Officers' Service”, and the service as the “Development Officers' Service” and
further to apply the designation “Development Officer’ ” for wherever the designation programme officer is mentioned.
B. P. P. S. A

Director General of Combined Services,
Ministry of Public Administration and Home Affairs.

Colombo 07,
