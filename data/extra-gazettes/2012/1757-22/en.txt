PART IV (B) — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE MANMUNAI SOUTH WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member   in  Manmunai  South West  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Paramanandam Kapilan, the  member   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to nominate a person  eligible under the  Ordinance  for  Election as a member of  that  Manmunai  South West    Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  failed to make  a   nomination  within  the
period specified ;
Now therefore, I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara  Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer  of  Manmunai  South
West  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting   in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare    Mr.  Gopalapillai Gunasingam who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of  the
member declare delcted at the election held on 10th  March, 2008 to Manmunai  South West,  Pradeshiya   Sabha  elected as a
member of the Manmunai  South West Pradeshiya   Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
A
SANKA RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai  South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
26th  April  2012.
