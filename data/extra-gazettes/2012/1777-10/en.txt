APPOINTMENT  OF  EASTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I, Mohan Wijewickrama, Governor of the Eastern Province by virtue of powers vested in me under the Section 154 F (4) of the
13 Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby announce that I have made
the following appointment.
Hon. Abdul Majeed Mohomed Najeeb
Chief Minister of the Eastern Province Provincial Council with effect from 18th of September, 2012.
MOHAN WIJEWICKRAMA,
Governor of the Eastern Province.
