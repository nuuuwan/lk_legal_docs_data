CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION
Notification made by the Director General of Customs under Section 47 and 57 of the Customs Ordinance
1. By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 47 and 57 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter  235), I, Neville
Goonewardena, Director General of Customs, do hereby notify that with effect from midnight 29th November/ 30th
November, 2012 :
(a) Every person, hereinafter referred to as the declarant, desiring to clear import cargo or forward export cargo, shall
declare to the Customs in a Bill of Entry known as Customs Goods Declaration (CUSDEC) substantially in the form
described as CUSDEC I and CUSDEC II and set out in Schedule I hereto, in white colour with the size of 210mm x
297mm (A4) paper with a thickness of not less than 80 Grams per square Meter (GSM), and shall present such Bill
of Entry along with the necessary supporting documents ;
(b) A Bill of Entry may also be declared electronically to the Sri Lanka Customs through the Customs software namely
Automated Systems for Customs Data (ASyCuDA) and in which case, until a paperless system is introduced, a
duly signed copy of the Bill of Entry, electronically generated through the ASyCuDA, in white colour with the size
of 210mm x 297mm (A4) paper with a thickness of not less than 80 Grams per Square Meter (GSM) shall be
presented along with the necessary supporting documents, to the Customs for the clearance of import cargo or for
the forwarding of export cargo.
(c) Every declarant who is an importer, exporter or Customs House Agent (CHA), whose business is required to be
registered interms of the Companies Act, No. 7 of  2007 and amendments thereto or in terms of any other written
law, shall register with the Customs for the purpose of identification by declaring the Customs Identification
Number (CIN) issued by the Sri Lanka Customs.
(d) Every declarant shall use the official codes approved by the Customs for perfecting a Goods Declaration (CUSDEC).
The official codes are published time to time by the Director General of Customs under the caption e-CUSTOMS
GUIDE ;
Provided however that, a declarant is also permitted to submit a Bill of Entry in the format of Schedule II of the
previous Gazette No. 1058/1 of 14.12.1998, until 31st December, 2012.
2. Subject to the Proviso of Paragraph 1, all previous notifications gazetted under Section 47 and 57 of the Customs Ordinance
are hereby rescinded.
D
R. NEVILLE  GOONEWARDENA,
Director General of Customs.
