THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
LIQUOR  AND  VINEGAR  TRADE
IN accordance with the provisions of Section 28  of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), the  Wages Board for the Liquor
and Vinegar  Trade hereby notifies that the Board, by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance
proposes to vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by me until 12.00 noon on 09th January 2013.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
V . B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Liquor and Vinegar Trade.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
30th October, 2012.
