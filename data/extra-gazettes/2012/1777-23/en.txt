PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
THE   HOLIDAYS   ACT,  NO.  29 OF   1971
Order under Section 11(1)(b)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11(1)(b) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J. Senevirathne, Minister
of Public Administration and Home Affairs, do, by this order declare that the 15th of April 2013 an additional Bank Holiday for
the purpose of the Act, in addition to the days for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to that Act.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRA THNE,
Minister of Public Administration and Home Affairs.
Ministry of Public Administration and Home Affairs,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
20th September 2012.
