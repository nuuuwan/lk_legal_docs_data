PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
NOTIFICATION
IT  is hereby notified that His Excellency the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, and the Commander-
in-Chief of the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to re-award the President's and the Battalion Colours to the 1st
Battalion and the 2nd and 3rd (V olunteer) Battalions of the Gemunu Watch and award the President's and the Regimental
Colours to the Gemunu Watch at a parade held in Kuruwita on the fifteenth day of August, in the year Two Thousand and
Twelve.
