PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF  1988
Filling  of  a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central   Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central  Province Provincial
Council by reason of Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance, the recognized political party to which
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the  aforesaid  Act,  to  nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Ujith Anuradha  to fill
such vacancy  ;
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in  terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Ujith Anuradha , elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from
the Administrative District of  Matale  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09th September, 2012.
