THE  PRADESHIYA SABHA   ACT ,  No.  15  OF 1987
Order
BY virture of the powers vested in me by Section 2, 4 and 6 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, as amended by the
Pradeshiya Sabha (Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 1999, I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government and Provincial
Councils, do by this Order -
(1) declare the area specified in Part  - I of the Schedule hereto, to be a Pradeshiya Sabha area for the purpose of that
Act;
(2) define the administrative limits of that Pradeshiya Sabha area to be the limits as set out in Part - II of that Schedule
(3) constitute a Pradeshiya Sabha for the Pradeshiya Sabha area declared under Paragraph (1) of this Order and assign
the following name to such sabha;
“MILLANIY A  PRADESHIYA  SABHA”
(4) determine that the Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha shall consist of the Chairman, Vice Chairman and Nine other
elected members ;
(5) appoint the First day of April, 2015 as the date on which the term of office of  the Millaniya Pradeshiya Sabha shall
commence.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
26th September 2012.

THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA   ACT , NO. 15 OF 1987
Order
By virtue of the powers vested in me, by Paragraphs (a) and (b) of Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabha  Act, No. 15 of 1987,
as amended by the Pradeshiya Sabha (Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 1999 , I, A.L.M. Athaullah, Minister of Local Government
and Provincial Councils, do hereby amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 453/7 of May 12, 1987 relating
to the constitution of the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha -
(1) (i) by the deletion of the schedule thereto, and substitution of the
schedule hereto
(ii) by the repeal of the word “Sixteen” in the fourth paragraph of the said Order and the substitution
thereof, of the word “eleven” ;
(2) The above Order shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 2015.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
26th September 2012.
