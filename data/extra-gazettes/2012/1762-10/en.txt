NOTICE CALLING FOR CLAIMS TO PROPERTY OF INTESTATE 
 
REGISTRATION OF TITLE ACT NO. OF 1998 
 
(Section 55) 
 
Reference the intestate property of late Mr. Dasanayakage Chandrapala who resides in the village of Kakkele , in the Divisional  Secre-
tary’s Division of Mirigama , in the District of Gampaha. 
 
It is hereby notified that any person/s who have a claim to or interest in the intestate property of the late Mr. Dasanayakage Chandrapala 
described in the Schedule hereto should submit his/their claim to the undersigned in writing before 18th July, 2012 stating his /their right 
or interest to such intestate property. In case of failure to do so, exparte action will be taken to register title under Secti on  55 (2) and (3) 
of the Registration of Title Act. No. 21 of 1998.
