STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether unjust has been caused to Mr. K. Premaratne who has served the Ceylon Electricity Board for an active service period of more than
fifteen (15) years in the capacity of Human Resources Officer-Grade K 4 and K 3  by not being placed in Grade K 2 with effect f rom
01.01.2005, and if so, Whether he is entitled to be placed in Grade K 2 wtih effect from the said date and financially entitled for the allowance
and if not so, to what other relief he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.

Colombo, this 04th day of September 2012.
