NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry conducted under Section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with
Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
IN terms of the powers vested in me under sub section 185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with section
2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Hettiachchi Mudiyanselage Athula Sarath Kumara
Wijesinghe, Chief Minister of the North Western Province and Minister of  Local Government do hereby notify that the
duration of the  one man commission of  Inquiry comprising retired  High Court Judge, O. W. O. B. Weerasena Esquire,
appointed by the extraordinary gazette paper No. 1717/ 18 dated 3rd August  2011 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, to inquire into whether the offences mentioned under section 185 were committed by the Chairman S. M. Bandula
Pushpakumara Senanayaka while holding his office as the Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho and to submit a report within
a period of 3 months, is extended up to 15th March 2012.
