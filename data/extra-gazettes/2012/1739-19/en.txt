PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
IMMIGRANTS  AND  EMIGRANTS  ACT
ORDER made by the Minister of Defence & Urban development under Subsection (2) of Section (2) in Immigrants and
Emigrants Act (Chapter 351), read with article 44(2) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development.
Colombo,
01st January, 2012.
Order
1. Exemption granted by the Government of Sri Lanka through orders made by the Minister on a number of
occasions to citizents of various countries to stay in Sri Lanka as genuine tourists or entrepreneurs for a period
not exceeding one month without visa is hereby rescinded with effect from 01.01.2012.
2. However, the exemption granted to citizens of the countries which have presently granted the facility of
exemption from obtaining visa to genuine tourists or entrepreneurs from Sri Lanka when entering the relevant
country will continue to be effective when they enter Sri Lanka as genuine tourists or entrepreneurs.
3. The provision in regard to visas agreed upon with the Government of Sri Lanka through bilateral agreements
that are presently in force in respect official and diplomatic passport holders will continue to be effective in
accordance with the  terms of such agreements.
4. The citizens of all countries not covered by  Sections  (2) and (3) above when arriving in Sri Lanka for short term
visits shall enter Sri Lanka having obtained approval for visa through the Electronic Travel Authority (ETA)
system with effect from 01.01.2012.
