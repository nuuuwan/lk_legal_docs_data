THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Polongaha Kumbura, Nagoda
Kumbura, Wetakeyapana Kumbura, Attapatthu Kumbura and
Ketakelagaha Kumbura in total extent of about 3.8210 Hectares,
depicted as Lot No. 17 in the amended Advance Tracing No. Co/92/
338 dated  05.01.2009, prepared by the Superintendent of Surveyor's
in village called Pelawatta of the Divisional Secretary’s Division of

follows:
North: Highway and balance portion of this land; East: Balance
portion of this land, Lot Nos. 4, 5, 6 and 7, Kottanuwe Idama,
Kandawatta and Lot Nos. 8, 12 and 2; South: Lot Nos. 13, 14, 15,
16, Karandagaha Kumbura and Highway;West: Highway.
