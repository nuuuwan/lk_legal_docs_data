NOTIFICATION
I, Gustingnawadu Shan Wijayalal De Silva, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine and Local Government of the
Southern Province, do hereby impose the following regulations relating to the travelling expenses of the mayors, deputy
mayors and councillors of municipal councils, made by me under the powers vested in me in terms of Section 289 of the
Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12
of 1989. These regulations shall take effect from 01.01.2012.
G
USTINGNAWADU SHAN WIJAYALAL DE SILV A,
Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance, Planning,
Law and Order,  Transport, Water Supply and Drainage,
Electricity, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine
and Local Government of the Southern Province.
10th October 2012,
Regulations
01. These Regulations may be cited as “Regulations on the Payment of Travelling Expenses of Mayors, Deputy
Mayors and Councillors of the Municipal Councils.”
2. A Mayor, Deputy Mayor or a Councillor of a Municipal Council who uses his own vehicle on any official
functions assigned to him by the Council, may be paid a fee per kilometer at the rate specified below as a
reimbursement of expenses incurred in travelling.
(i) For a motor car/three wheeler — Rs. 10.00 per km
(ii) For a motor cycle — Rs. 4.00 per km
(iii) In the case of public transport the actual amount paid.
3. If the motor car/ three wheeler or the motor cycle used on official functions is not his own, it may be treated as
own vehicle if the following documents relating to the motor car/three wheeler or motor cycle concerned, are
produced.

Mayor or Councillors of a Municipal Council.
