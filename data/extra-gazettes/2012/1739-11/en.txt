THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Hotel and Catering Trade
IT is hereby notified under  Section 29(3) of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136), that the decisions of the Wages Board
for the Hotel and Catering Trade made under Section 30 of that Ordinance and specified in the schedule hereto have been
approved by the Honourable Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
The decision shall come into force on 01st of January 2012.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Minister of  Labour and Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
28th December, 2011.
S
CHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Hotel and Catering Trade and set out in the schedule to the
notification published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 34 of 27th April, 1979 as varied from time to time and last varied by the
Notification of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1660/35 of 30th June, 2010 shall be further varied (besides the minimum wages)
as follows,
"The regulations of employment of the Wages Board for the Hotel and Catering Trade covers fully by the Act to
provide for the regulations of employment in Shop and Office Employees (Regulation of Employment and Remuneration) Act
No. 19 of 1954 and several other amendments to the aforesaid Act."
