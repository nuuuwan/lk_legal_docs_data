FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  PUTTALAM  URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Puttalam Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Group Leader  of the Independent Group No. 08  to which  M. S. M. Nawfi  the member  belonged,
was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for
Election as a member of  that  Puttalam Urban Council who belongs to such group  who shall be declared  elected as a member
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group  has  nominated  Mr. A. K. Yehiya Khan being an eligible person
under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Muhammad Nafeel, Returning Officer of  Puttalam Urban Council, acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. A. K. Yehiya Khan elected
as a member of the Puttalam Urban Council.
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
24th December, 2011.
