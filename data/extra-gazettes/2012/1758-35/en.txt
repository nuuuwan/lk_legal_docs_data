Regulation
The Regulation made under Section 61 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 43/4 of July 2, 1979 as amended from time to time, is hereby further amended in the Schedule to that Regulation as
follows :–
(1) by the insertion, immediately after the entries relating to the “Magistrate’s Court of Kaduwela” appearing in
Column I and Column II of that Schedule, of the following :
“The Magistrate’s Court of Jaffna All actions, proceedings or matters against or in relation to a
“Child” or a “Young Person” as defined in the Children and
Young Persons Ordinance (Chapter 23)
(a) arising under the Children and Young Persons Ordinance
(Chapter 23) ; and
(b) arising out of, or in relation to, an offence under the Penal
Code or any other Law for the time being in force,
within the Judicial Divisions of Jaffna, Point Pedro,
Chavakachcheri, Mallakam and Kayts.” ; and
(2) by the omission of any entry relating to the “Magistrate’s Court of Jaffna, Point Pedro, Chavakachcheri, Mallakam
and Kayts” appearing in Column I and Column II of that Schedule.
