One (01) allotment of land in extent of about 2.029 Hectare, depicted

situated in the Village of  Thelehare in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Kobeigane in the district of Kurunegala,  in the  North
Western Province.

Name of land: Ambegahamule Hena or Ambegahamule Waththe;
Description: Where the School Play Ground is currently situated;

Hectare.
