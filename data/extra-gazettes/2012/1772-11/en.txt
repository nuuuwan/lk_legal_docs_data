THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My Ref. No.: GCF/ATH/KL/15B/LR.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: L/O3J/92/LRB/132.
The Public are hereby notified that the compensation payment in
respect of allotments of land of which the claims were not presented,
as depicted in Schedules 01 to 13 below and included in Preliminary
Plan No. 8183 prepared on 13th January 2003  by Surveyor General
that have been vested in the Government through Proviso (38a)
No. 78 of 1993 published in the Gazette Notification No. 769/6
dated 01st June 1993  under Greater Colombo Flood Control and
Environment Improvement Project have been deposited in the Dis-
trict Court of  Colombo, under case numbers depicted in the same
schedule under Section 33 of the Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotments mentioned below for which the said money has been
deposited on nominal basis with a view to completing this acquisi-
tion, could obtain the compensation payable to the allotment con-
cerned by appearing before me, after submitting necessary docu-
ments to prove their ownership to the Court of Justice.
C. W. Abesuriya,
Land Acquiring Officer,
Greater Colombo Flood Control Project,
Land Acquiring Office,

Welikada, Rajagiriya.
