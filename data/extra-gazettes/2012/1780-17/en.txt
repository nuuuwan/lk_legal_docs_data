PART IV (B) — LOCAL GOVERNMENT
Notices under  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION 10A (1) (A)
Tangalle Urban Council
I, Wewalwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Tangalle Urban Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10 A(1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Anjana Kusum Ruwan Balapitiya Liyanage of the
Tangalle Urban Council has vacated his office of the member of that Council as he has ceased to be a member of the United
People’s Freedom Alliance.
W. H. R.W
IJAY A KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
09th  October,  2012.
