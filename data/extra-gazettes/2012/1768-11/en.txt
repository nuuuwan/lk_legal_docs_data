Appointments Granted under the Statute of Co - operative Employee
No. 14 of 1990 the Central Province
THE  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL   CO - OPERATIVE   EMPLOYEES  COMMISSION
BY virtue of powers vested on me under the section of 07 (1) and 04 (2) of the statute of Co- operative Employees Commission
No. 14 of 1990 in the Central Province, I,  Sarath Ekanayake,
Central province, hereby notified that Mr. Mahendra Karaputugala will be appointed as a Member and the  Chairman
of  the Central Provincial Co-operative Employees Commission with the effective date of 01.06.2012 and under the
Section 04 (2) of such statute, following members will be appointed as other members of the aforesaid commission with the
effective date of 01.06.2012.
1. Lawyer Mr. Edirisinghe Gamini Samaratunge,
2. Mr.  Wathuwala Gedara Chaminda Thushara Kamalarathne.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
