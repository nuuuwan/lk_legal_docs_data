1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  KATTANKUDI  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kattankudi    Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of   Resignation ;
And  whereas the group Leader of  the independent group No. 1 to  which Muhammathu Musthafa Abdul Rahuman,
the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible
under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kattankudi Urban Council  who belongs to  such group   who shall be
declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of  the  aforesaid  group  has nominated Mr. Muhammathanifa Abdul Naseer   being  an
eligible person under the  aforesaid  Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer  of   the  Kattankudi
Urban Council,  acting in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare Mr. Muhammathanifa Abdul Naseer  elected as a member of the  Kattankudi  Urban Council .
A
SANKA  RA THNAY AKA,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudi  Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Batticoloa,
26
th  October 2012.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   NAVITHANVELI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as a
member of  the Eastern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  ILankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the  recognized political party to which
the Thawaraja Kalaiyarasan the Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the   member of  that  Navithanveli   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Sivalingam Gunareththinam,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer of  Navithanveli   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sivalingam
Gunareththinam , elected as  a Chairman  of   the Navithanveli   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Navithanveli    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
25th  October  2012.
