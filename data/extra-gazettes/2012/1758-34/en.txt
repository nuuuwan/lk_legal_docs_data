THE FINANCE ACT, No. 11 OF 1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 72 read with section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963 as amended from time
to time, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order, vest in the
People's Bank established under the People's Bank  Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 18th, May two thousand twelve, the
property described in the Schedule hereto.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
The Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
18th May 2012.
SCHEDULE
All that undivided portion of the amalgamation of the lands called ''Visinge Watte'' and the adjoining South portion of
''Visinge Hena",''Visinge Gedaragawa Watte'', Visinge Watte alias the adjoining land, Visingedaragawa Watta, Puligu Ralalage
Watte, Pallege Watte together with everything situated thereon situated at Moderathanna in Galabada Pattuwa of Galabada
Korale in the District of  Kegalle, Sabaragamuwa Province bounded on the  North by the Edaru fence separating the land
belonging to Ukkubanda and the said land, and the Pallehena Thotille Tree, on the East by Galhiriya which separates the Lot
No. 1 marked in the survey plan dated 03.07.1917 made by M. Keyt, Licensed surveyor, on the South by Mahaoya stream, on
the West Moderathanna paddy field  and the ditch containining in extent 9 Acres, 3 Rood, 14  Perches (9A, 3R, 14 P.)
The said undivided portion containing 1 Acre in extent is registered under title C581.279 in the Kegalle District Land
Registry.
