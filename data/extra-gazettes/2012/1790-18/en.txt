LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS (AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 22 OF 2012
Order under  Subsection (3) of Section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Subsection (3)  of  Section 1 of  the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act,
No. 22 of  2012, I, A. L. M. Athaullah, Minister of Local  Government and Provincial Councils, do hereby appoint,  January 01,
2013 as the date on which the provisions of the above Amendment, other than the provisions of Sections 3A, 3B, 3C, 3D and
3 E, shall come into  operation.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Ministry of Local Government and Provincial Councils,
Colombo,
21st  December, 2012.
