CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATE  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Ranasinghege Semasinghe, Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 20th  August, 2012, all
duties of  Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235),
shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1771/1  of
13th  August, 2012 is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 26th  August,  2012.
R. S
EMASINGHE,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
17th  August  2012.
