WAGES BOARD FOR THE BAKERY TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of  the Wages Board Regulations, published in Gazette No. 14961 dated  04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations has been pleased  to appoint the following persons to be members of the
Wages Board for the Bakery Trade, by virtue of  the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 09 of theWages
Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 01st of  November, 2012.
W. J. L. U. W
