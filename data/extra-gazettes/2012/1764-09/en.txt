Regulations
The Minimum F.O.B. price of a Metric Ton of paddy and rice (H.S. Codes 10.06) exported from Sri Lanka should
be Rs. 300,000/-.
It is allowed to export only specific varieties of rice exported to the targeted export market subject to export licenses.
This regulation will be effective from 28th June, 2012.
