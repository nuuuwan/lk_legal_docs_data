PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & c., by the President
  PS No.:  E&O/HR/1/101.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1)  of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Gotabaya Rajapaksa, Secretary to the Ministry of Defence and
Urban Development to act  in the Post of Secretary to the President with effect from 22.10.2012, until Mr. Lalith Weeratunga,
Secretary to the President returns to the Island.
      By  order of  His  Excellency,
D. S. G
UNARATNE,

(Establishment & Operations)
for Secretary to the President.
