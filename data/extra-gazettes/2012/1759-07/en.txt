FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Colombo Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Asoka Milinda Moragoda,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Colombo Municipal Council  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Kapu Geekiyanage
Kulathissa being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, A. Senanayake, Returning Officer of Colombo Municipal Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kapu Geekiyanage Kulathissa, elected as  a
member  of   the Colombo Municipal Council.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo City Elections Office, Colombo Municipal Council.
Rajagiriya,
21st May, 2012.
