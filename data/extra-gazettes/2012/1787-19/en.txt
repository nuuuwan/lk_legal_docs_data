FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  TANGALLE  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Tangalle   Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to  be a member
of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s  Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to  which Anjana
Kusum Ruwan Balapitiya  Liyanage,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid
Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Tangalle Urban Council  who
belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  aforesaid  recognized   political party has  nominated Mr. Edirisooriya Patabendige
Sunil    being  an eligible person under the  aforesaid  Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra  Wijaya  Kumara,  Returning  Officer  of   the  Tangalle   Urban Council,
acting in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Edirisooriya Patabendige Sunil elected as a member of the  Tangalle   Urban Council .
W. H. R. WIJAY A  KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle   Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
23rd  November 2012.
