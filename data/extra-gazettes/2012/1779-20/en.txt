No. 1779/20 – THURSDAY, OCTOBER 11, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.:3/3/2/75.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Seven(07) allotments of  land in total extent of  0.1117 Hectare,  as
Lot Nos. 1-7 depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Co.9254 and  dated
07.09.2012 prepared  by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Weniwelkola,  in  the Minor Division of Meda Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Homagama in the District of
Colombo  in Western Province and morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Halgolla Wagura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: K. A. Ananda and Others,
No.87/B, Kahathuduwa, Old Police Road, Polgasovita, Extent: 0.0026
Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Aswedduma Wagura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: K.T.Nandawathi Alwis,
No.14/B, Horathuduwa, Polgasovita and K.T.M.Alwis, No. 71,
School Road, Polgasowita; Extent: 0.0123 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of   land: Aswedduma Wagura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: A. Gunawathi and Others,
No. 181/2, Kahathuduwa, School Road, Polgasowita; Extent:
0.0152 Hectare.
4. Lot No. 4
Name of   land: Aswedduma Kumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: M.D.J.Deshapriya, No. 261,
Shanthipura, Thalawathugoda, Extent: 0.0204  Hectare.
5. Lot No. 5
Name of   land: Welegedara Watta; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: P. Piyadasa and Others, No. 24/1,
Weniwelkola, Polgasovita, Extent: 0.0127  Hectare.
6. Lot No. 6
Name of   land: Welegedara Watta; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: P.J.Fernando and Others,
No. 23/1, Weniwelkola, Polgasovita, Extent: 0.0151 Hectare.
7. Lot No. 7
Name of   land: Aswedduma Kumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: A.Gunawathi, No. 23/1,
Weniwelkola, Polgasovita and  P.Piyadasa  of No. 39/13, Idalgaswatta,

All perosons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Homagama Divisional Secretariat on the 17th December, 2012,

(in duplicate) on or before the 10th December, 2012 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Predeep Yasarathne,
Divisional Secretary,
Homagama.

Homagama,
