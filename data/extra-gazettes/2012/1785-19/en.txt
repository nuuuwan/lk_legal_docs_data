PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & c., by the President
PS No.:  E&O/HR/1/101.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint  Mr. Gamini S. Senerath, Chief of Staff to the President with effect from
19.11.2012 until Mr. Lalith Weeratunga, Secretary to the President returns to the Island.
     By  order of  His  Excellency,
D. S. GUNARATNE,

(Establishment and Operations),
for Secretary to the President.
