PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
ESALA  FESTIV AL  OF  MAHIYANGANA  -  2012
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  the Transfer of Powers (Divisional Secretaries) Act No. 58 of 1992, and according to
the regulations in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 362) published in Government Gazette
No.9902 of 10th September 1948, Gazette No. 10283 of 17th August 1951 Gazette No. 14778 of 25th March 1995 and Gazette
No.14752 of 09th June 1967, I, Kalaniya Bandara Abeykoon Mudiyanselage Sunil Abeykoon, the Divisional Secretariate of
Mahiyangana, do hereby inform the following orders shall be in force with regard to the camp of Mahiyangana Esala Festival
2012.
1. It is announced, the Mahiyangana Esala festival will be commenced on the 17th August 2012 and the closure of the
event will be followed by the "Diya Kapeema" ceremony on the 01st  September 2012.
2. The Camp area for abode of pilgrims shall be the land demarcated by the 37th Mile post of Mahiyangana - Bibila
main road up to the 1/39 culvert in front of Mahiyangana National School, and the land situated between the
Mahiyangana - Meegahakiula road the leading to the clock tower in front of Mahiyangana Hospital and the new
highway.
3. No persons shall cook meals in "Vihara Maluwa", "Dewala Boomiya", "Palle Veediya" (The court yards of the
Vihara, Bodi and Devala or within those premises.
4. On a permit issued by me or by the Officer incharge of the camp, Selling of flowers, Joss-sticks, candles, camphor,
offering items and statues for fulfillment of vows, is allowed in the strip of land opposite the "Sthupa" at "Mada
Veediya" and in the strip of Land issued to the left of the"Makara Thorana".
5. Within the camp area specified in above section 2, selling and distribution of all food to pilgrims and selling of
other commodities and allowed in the 24 permanent buildings or the Officer in-charge of the camp. The area
beyond 20ft, from the drainage system of both sides of Palle Veediya, Mada Veediya, Dutugamunu Vishrama Shala
Mawatha (Pilgrims rest road) Ammodilla road, Badulla Mahiyangana main road, Bodimaluwa Mawatha and the
road in front of the"Sangawasa" (Bikku abode), prescribed as the way of Perehara procession by the "Basnayaka
Nilame", is reserved for business purposes.

6. No person shall expose any commodity of description for sale within the camp area in any place not appointed for
the purpose as specified in above section 4 & 5.
7. Parking of private vehicles in the main street is hereby prohibited and no person shall enter any type of vehicle
in to the camp area including bicycles and bullock carts without a permit issued by me or by the officer in charge
of the camp.
8. No person shall enter any moter vehicle into the camp via the road reaching the sacred area by the side of the
"Vahalkada" (Vestibule) which faces Mahiyangana Bibila main road, Mahiyangana Bibila old road, the main
access to the camp area, the road by the side of the clock tower located in front of the hospital, and the road by the
side of 1/39 culvert in front of Mahiyangana National School, unless written permission is obtained.
9. Public notice will be made by me on the procedure of leasing the parcels of land for business purposes within the
camp area.
10. All the temporary constructions in the leased out commercial lands shall comply with the specifications made by
the Department of Health. 01 or 02 sanitary toilets adjacent to each boutique will have to be made available for the
use of pilgrims, if ordered. After the removel of the temporary buildings/sheds by the settlers of the leased out
lands at the end of the festival, all refuse and garbage including pollythene shall be collected and disposed under
the supervision of the Public Health Instructors.
11. No person shall construct any building within the camp area unless a license has been obtained in that behalf from
me.
12. No person shall, without the previous sanction of the Divisional Secretary, construct any booth or any sort of
building or extend any building, so as to encroach on any land used or set apart for use as a street within the camp
area or on any land forming the banks of the river within the camp area.
15th Regulation (Pilgrimages Ordinance)
(a) No person shall, without the previous of the Divisional Secretary, construct any booth or any sort of building or
extend any building, so as to encroach on any land used or set apart for use as a street within the camp area or on
any land forming the banks of the river within the camp area.
(b) Firing of any sort of crackers is strictly prohibited within the camp area.
(c) No person shall make any collection of money within the camp area on selling tickets, flags, or on any other
purpose unless permission is granted by the Divisional Secretary or other authorized officer.
K
ALANIY A BANDARA ABEYKOON MUDIY ANSELAGE SUNIL ABEYKOON,
Divisional Secretary - Mahiyangana,
Badulla District.

MAHIYANGANA  ESALA  FESTIV AL  -  2012
ACCORDING to the regulations interms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 362), published in Govern-
ment Gazette No. 9902 of 10th September, 1948, Gazette No.10283 of 17th August. 1951, Gazette No. 14778 of 25th March 1955,
and Gazette No. 14752 of 09th June 1967 and the Transfer of Powers Act No.58 of 1992, I, Kalaniya Bandara Abeykoon
Mudiyanselage Sunil Abeykoon, The Divisional Secretary of Mahiyangana, do hereby notify that the following places are
allocated for the purpose of distributing and selling of food items among the pilgrims.
(a) All the permanent and temporary buildings in between the 37th K.m.  1/39 culvert opposite the National School,
along Mahiyangana - Bibila road.
(b) Permanent and temporary buildings on both sides of Mahiyangana - Meegahakiula old road, up the clock tower
in front of the hospital.
(c) Temporary buildings constructed on a permit issued by me, within the allocated ground for abode of pilgrims
inside the camp area.
No person shall distribute any sort of food item in any place not appointed for the purpose, except specified in above.
KALANIY A BANDARA ABEYKOON MUDIY ANSELAGE SUNIL ABEYKOON,
Divisional Secretary - Mahiyangana,
Badulla District.
03rd  August  2012,
Divisional Secretariat of Mahiyangana.
