EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  ANGUNAKOLAPELLESSA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Angunakolapellessa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Senarath Arachchige Priyantha  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Angunakolapellessa Pradeshiya  Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Pahala Walpola Gamaralalage
Chaminda  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Wewalwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of  Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Pahala Walpola Gamaralalage Chaminda elected as  a member  of   the  Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Angunakolapellessa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Hambantota,
29th  November  2012.
