No. 1770/14 – WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 08, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref No.: wa/18/82/2008.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Name of land: Weralabadathalgahawatta; Description: Garden, 03
permanent buildings and foundation; Claimant: State; Original
claimant: National Savings Bank, No. 255, Galle Road, Colombo 3;
Extent: 0.5217 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary’s Office, Wattala on the 30th day of August,
2012 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 30th day of August, 2012 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Chandima Disanayake,
Divisional Secretary,
Wattala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Wattala
03rd August,  2012.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No. Div. sec/Acq3/1/69.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for Acquisition of a land with a house for the
hostel of the officers of the State Intelligence Service.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1 as depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Co. 9214 situated in
the Village of Pepiliyana in the Grama Niladhari’s Division No. 535
Pepiliyana west in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kesbewa
in the Colombo District in Western province.
Name of land: Kongahawatta; Description: Home garden with a
permanent building; Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0359 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Kesbewa on the 10th day of September,
2012 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 03rd day of September, 2012 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
L. A. Kalukapuarachchi,
