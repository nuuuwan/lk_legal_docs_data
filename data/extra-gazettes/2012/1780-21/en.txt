STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter  in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
 Whether it is unjust has been caused to Mr. Niranjan Udumalagala serving as a Special Grade Pharmacist of the University of Colombo, by
the deferment of his earned salary increment  for year 2007 through letter of the Registrar dated 15th March, 2007 and by the deferment of his
earned salary increments for year 2008 and 2009 without prior notice and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
