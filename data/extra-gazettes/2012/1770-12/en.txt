AMENDMENT
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
In the Notice under Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act,
(Chapter 460) which was published by me in the Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 1718/26 dated 12.08.2011 for the plots of
land depicted in the Surveyor General's Final Topographical Plan
No. K 4317 dated 03.07.2011 in the Village of  Nagoda in
Kajuduwawatta Minor Division in Kalutara District in Western
Province, the extent of land to be acquired of the Lot No. 119 is
mentioned as 0.0420 Hectare.
I hereby inform that the above should be corrected as
0.0042 Hectare as per the amended plot details registered by the
Survey Department on 17.05.2012.
Sirisoma Lokuwithana,
Divisional Secretary,
Kalutara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kalutara,
