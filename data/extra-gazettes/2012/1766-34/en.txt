FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   SRI   JAYAWARDENAPURA   KOTTE
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Sri  Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Welatantrige Nandasiri Upali Bateju,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte
Municipal Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Don Ranjan Ponnamperuma
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, A. Senanayake, Returning Officer of Sri  Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council, acting in  terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Don Ranjan Ponnamperuma
elected as  a member  of   the Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council.
Colombo,
10th July 2012.
