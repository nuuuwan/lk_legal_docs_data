CORRECTION
THE  NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  LAW No. 2 OF 1974
Notice under Section 84
Following section of the notice given in terms of section 84 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Law No. 2 of 1974
in the Gazette No. 1776/13 dated 18.09.2012 to change the Water Tariff with effect from 01st day of October 2012 for all the
consumers supplied with water from the water supply schemes of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board shall be
corrected as Follows
Tariff 13
Community based Organizations
The monthly charges for supply of water under this tariff given in the Gazette notice as Rs. 17 shall be corrected
as Rs. 13 per unit :
This correction apply to all three languages Gazettes
Tariff 14
Bowser Supply
The tariff for supply of water through Bowsers given in the Gazette notice as Rs. 65 shall be corrected as Rs. 72
per unit :
This correction apply only to Tamil language Gazette

ARUNASENA HETTIARACHCHI,
Chairman.

Ratmalana,
