EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   MAHARA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Mahara   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
the Manchanayake Arachchige Don Rathnasiri the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Mahara    Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  Arachchige Gayan
Sewwanda Samarathunga,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning  Officer of  Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Arachchige Gayan
Sewwanda Samarathunga , elected as  a member  of   the  Mahara   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
24th  August  2012.
