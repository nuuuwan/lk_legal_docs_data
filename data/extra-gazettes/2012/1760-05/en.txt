PRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   PELMADULLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Upali Waduarachchi,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Kithulpe Gamaethi
Ralalage Deepal Kithulpegama being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera, Returning Officer of Pelmadulla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Kithulpe Gamaethi Ralalage Deepal Kithulpegama, elected as  a member  of   the Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
M.A.P.C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Pelmadulla Pradeshiya Sabha.
Ratnapura,
21st May 2012.
