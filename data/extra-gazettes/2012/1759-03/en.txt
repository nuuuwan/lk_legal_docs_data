CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATE  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Neville Goonewardana, Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 21st  May, 2012, all  duties of  Customs
as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be paid and
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1758/01  of
14th  May, 2012 is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 27th  May,  2012.
D
R. NEVILLE GOONEWARDANA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
18th  May  2012.
