Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of the North Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under sections 154(E) (1) (4) (5) of the 13th Amendment to the constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Karunaratna Divulgane, Governor of the North  Central Province hereby notify that the following
appointments are made.
1.  Hon. S. M. Ranjith Samarakone
As the Chief Minister of the North Central Province and as the Provincial Minister of Law and order, Finance and Plan
Implementation, Local Government, Provincial Irrigation, Land, Provincial Road Development, Rural Infrastructure
Facilities, Sports and Youth Affairs, with effect from 24.09.2012.
Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. R. G . Ananda Kularatna, SLAS - Class 1
2.  Hon. H. B. Semasinghe
As Minister of North Central Provincial Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare,
Women’s Affairs, Transport and Housing, with effect from 24.09.2012. Housing subject was assigned with effect from
24.10.2012.
Secretary to the Ministry - Ms. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa, SLAS - Class 1
3.  Hon. K. H. Nandasena
As Minister of North Central Provincial Cooperative, Trade and Food, Small Industries, Textile Industries, Rural
Development and Tourism, with effect from 24.09.2012.
Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. A. M.W.A. Amunugama, SLAS - Special Grade.
4.  Hon. R. M. P. B. Ratnayaka
As Minister of  North Central Provincial Agriculture, Animal Production and Animal Health, Fisheries, Environment
and Provincial Council Affairs, with effect from 24.09.2012.
Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. P. K. Jayalath, SLAS - Class 1

NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of the North Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under section 32 (1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Karunaratne Divulgane,
Governor of the North Central Province hereby notify that the officers named below were appointed to the respective posts
indicated, in the Provincial Public Service.
1.  Mr. A. M. W. A. Amunugama-SLAS Special Grade, as the Secretary to the Ministry of North Central Provincial
Cooperative, Trade and Food, Small Industries, Textile Industries, Rural Development and Tourism, with effect from 03.10.2012
until further notice.
2.  Mr. R. P. Ananda Kularatna - SLAS - Class I, as the Secretary to the North Central Provincial Chief Ministry and North
Central Provincial Ministry of Law and Order, Finance, Plan Implementation, Local Government, Provincial Irrigation, Land,
Provincial Road Development, Rural Infrastructure Facilities, Sports and Youth Affairs, with effect from 01.10.2012, until
further notice.
3. Ms. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa, SLAS-Class I, as the Secretary to the Ministry of North Central Provincial Health,
Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare, Women’s Affairs, Transport and Housing, with effect from  03.10.2012
until further notice.
4. Mr. P. K. Jayalath - SLAS -Class I, as  the Secretary to the Ministry of North Central Provincial Agriculture, Animal
Production and Animal Health, Fisheries, Environment and Provincial Council Affairs, with effect from 04.10.2012 until further notice.
5. Mr. N. G . Panditharatna  - SLAS -Class I, as  the Secretary to the Ministry of  North Central Provincial Education,
Information and Technology and Cultural  Affairs, with effect from 03.10.2012 until further notice.
6. Mr. R. K. Premasiri - SLAS -Class I, as  the Provincial Council Secretary of  the North Central  Province Provincial  Council
with effect from 03.10.2012 until further notice.
7. Mr. G. D. Keerthi Gamage - SLAS -Class I, as  the North Central Provincial  Deputy Chief  Secretary  (Administration and
the Training) with effect from 02.10.2012 until further notice.
8. Ms. D. H. M. N. P. Dasanayaka, SLAS-Class I, to discharge the functions of  the Secretary  to  the Ministry of  North
Central Provincial Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare, Sports and Youth Affairs, for the period
from  02.01.2012 to 02.10.2012.
