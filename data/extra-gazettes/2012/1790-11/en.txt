FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   DEHIATTAKANDIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS  the office of Vice Chairman in Dehiattakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Konagedara Wijerathna  the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  decide upon the member of that Dehiattakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Ranaweera Mudiyanselage Prabhath Duminda Ranaweera to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I,  Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of  Dehiattakandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ranaweera
Mudiyanselage Prabhath Duminda Ranaweera elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Dehiattakandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Dehiattakandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
20th December,  2012.
