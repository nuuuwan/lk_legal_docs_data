EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE  DEHIOWITA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice  Chairman in  Dehiowita  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be
a member of  the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Priyantha Udaya Kumara  the Vice Chairman   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the   member of  that  Dehiowita   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected
as Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  A. Damidu Chaminda,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Talangama Appuhamilage  Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning  Officer of   Dehiowita  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. A. Damidu Chaminda, elected as   Vice Chairman  of   the Dehiowita   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C. N. TALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
 Dehiowita   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle,
04th  December  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   DEHIATTAKANDIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Dehiattakandiya   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of   being   elected as
a member of  the Eastern Province Provincial Council ;
 And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
Thennakoon Mudiyanselage Jayasena  the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Dehiattakandiya   Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. Weerashinghe
Pandithagedara Sumanathilaka,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer of  Dehiattakandiya   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Weerashinghe
Pandithagedara Sumanathilaka, elected as  a member  of   the Dehiattakandiya  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Dehiattakandiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
30th  November, 2012.
