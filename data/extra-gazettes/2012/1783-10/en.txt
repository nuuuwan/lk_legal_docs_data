SPORTS ACT, No. 25 OF 1973
Orders under Sections 40 and 41
 By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 40 A, Regulations of National Sports Associations, published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 252/8 of 17th February 1977, made under Section 41of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973,
I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports do by this order direct that Mrs. Maharage Dugganna Ralalage
Ranjani Jayakody, the Director General of the Department of  Sports Development, Ministry of  Sports to convene and
conduct a meeting of the Sri Lanka Netball Association to elect the new office bearers.
