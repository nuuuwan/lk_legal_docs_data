THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: Bagama/DS/2/2/60.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two (02)  allotments of  land in total extent of  0.0254 Hectare,  as
Lot Nos. 1 and 2 depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Ga 3996 and
dated 11.12.2012 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the
village  of Baddegama (East) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Baddegama  in the District of  Galle  in Southern Province morefully
described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of   land: Mahahengodakanda; Description: Garden, contain
Mix Cultivation with Cinnamon about 2-3 years old; Claimant: State;
Original claimant: R.Abeywardhan of No.03, Fort, Galle; Extent:
0.0250 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of   land: Mahahengodakanda; Description: Open Wasteland;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: R. Abeywardhan of No. 03, Fort,
Galle; Extent: 0.0004 Hectare.
All perosns interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Baddegama Divisional Secretariat on the 05.03.2013 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 26.02.2013 the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
D. K. S. Jayalakshmi,
Divisional Secretary,
Baddegama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Baddegama.
18th December, 2012.
