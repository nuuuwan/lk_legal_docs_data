UNDER  SECTION  44 (SUBSECTION 2)  OF  THE  PROVINCIAL  ENVIRONMENTAL  STATUTE
No. 12  OF 1990
Proposed 600 MW (300 MW x 2) Coal  Fired Thermal Power Plant Project  Phase II at Norochcholei
THE Environmental Impact Assessment report prepared for the proposed 600 MW (300 MW X 2) Coal Fired Thermal Power
Plant project phase II at Norochcholei has submitted by the Ceylon Electricity Board, No. 50, Sir Chiththampalam A Gardener
Mawatha, Colombo 02, to this authority for approval.
The Environmental Impact Assessment reports are made available for inspection by the public in following places
as defined in the section 44 (2) of the North Western Provincial Environmental Statute No. 12 of  1990. The inspection on the
Environmental Impact Assessment report can be done between 8.30 a.m.  to 4.00 p. m. for a period of 30  working days, from
the date of this notification.
Any member of the public may submit their comments in writing to me within 30 working days, as mentioned above.
