SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation by the Honourable Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province
KNOW Ye that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 154 B Sub-section (8) (a) of the Thirteenth Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Wijesinghe Jayaweera Mudiyanselage Lokubandara,
Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do by this Proclamation summon the Provincial Council of the Sabaragamuwa
Province to meet at the Provincial Council Assembly Hall of the Provincial Council Secretariat, Ratnapura on the 27th of
September, Two Thousand Twelve at 10.00 a.m. for the conduct of Business specified in Part II Section 7 (1) of the Provincial
Council Act, No. 42 of 1987.
Given at Rathnapura this 21st day of September, Two Thousand Twelve.
W. J. M. L
OKUBANDARA,
Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
