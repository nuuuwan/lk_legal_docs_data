PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
STATE  AGRICULTURAL  CORPORATION  ACT,  No. 11  OF  1972
Order Under Section 2 (i)
I, order in terms of Gazette Extraordinary Notification No. 302/12  dated 01.02.1978 issued under the provision of Section 2(i)
of the State Agricultural Corporation Act, that the implementing areas of the Hadabima Authority of Sri Lanka established
under the said Gazette Notification should be the Divisional Secretariat areas referred to under Column two of Administrative
Districts referred to under Column one of the following Schedule in the manner as referred to in paragraph 1 of the first
Schedule of the said Gazette Notification .
Accordingly Gazette Extraordinary No. 1708/30 dated 03.06.2011 issued by me is hereby cancelled.
M
AHINDA YAPA ABEW ARDENA,
Minister of Agriculture.
16th  July  2012.
