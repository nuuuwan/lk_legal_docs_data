THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No:HP/D/2/LA/82.
Land Mini. Ref. No:4/3/3/2011/WS/36.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for the Improvements to the Water Treatment
Plant Complex, Katugastota under the Greater Kandy Water Supply
Project of National Water Supply and Drainage Board.
Description of the land to be acquired:
One (01) allotment  of  land in total  extent of  about
0.0420 Hectare depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha/5398
surveyed  and   prepared  by Superintendent of  Surveys, Kandy
District, situated  in the  Village  of  Kondadeniya, within
Kulugammana Siyapattuwa Minor Division in Harispattuwa
Divisional Secretary’s Division, Kandy District,  Central Province:
01. Lot No. 31
Name of   land: Dingiriyadeniya;  Claimant: Valala Rajamaha
Viharaya;  Extent: 0.0420 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Office, Harispattuwa Divisional Secretary’s on the 13th
day of February 2013 at  10.00. a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 06th day of
February, 2013 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
or their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
  R. M. P. S. B. Rathnayaka,
Divisional Secretary,
Harispattuwa.
