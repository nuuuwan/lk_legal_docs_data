SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  40  OF  1979
Order under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Rishad
Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce, with the concurrence of the Minister of Finance and Planning, do by this
order declare that, a Cess shall be charged, levied and paid on import of any article specified in the Column II and III of the
Schedule hereto at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule hereto, with effect from 28th
June, 2012. Accordingly, for every article specified under the H S Headings 0710.80.10, 0712.20, 0712.90.10, 0712.90.90,
0805.20.10, 11.01, 1901.90.10, 1901.90.90 and 72.04 mentioned in the Column I of the Schedule of the Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 1733/6 dated 21.11.2011, the respective Cess rates mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule
are hereby repealed with effect from 28th June, 2012.
R
ISHAD BA THIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of Industry and Commerce,
Colombo,
27th June, 2012.
