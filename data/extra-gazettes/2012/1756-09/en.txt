PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
1A
Order No. : 19
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader, importer or distributor shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display for sale
any Full Cream Milk Powder above the maximum retail prices given below.
Maximum Retail Price
400 g pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 319.00
1 kg pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 785.00
All other pack sizes of Full Cream Milk Powder below 400 g shall not be sold for more than the proportionate price
of Rs. 80.00 per 100 g and pack sizes above 400 g shall not be sold for more than the proportionate price of Rs. 78.50 per 100g.
The Consumer Affairs Authority hereby rescind the Order No. 16 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1704/8
dated 02nd May 2011 issued in respect of the Maximum Retail Price of Full Cream Milk Powder.
By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY  MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
Consumer Affairs Authority,
03rd May, 2012.
