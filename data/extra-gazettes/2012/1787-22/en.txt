Direction No. - 42.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs the Manufacturers and Importers of Pharmaceuticals and Vitamins.
(a) to mark the Maximum Retail Price ;
(b) to specify the Batch Number ;
(c) to specify the date of Manufacture ;
(d) to specify the date of Expiry ;
(e) to specify the strength ;
(f) to specify name and address of the manufacturer and/or importer or the trade mark registered in Sri Lanka.
on the packs or containers or units of all pharmaceuticals and vitamins. The above details have to be legible and should be
marked in indelible ink.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Direction remove "Pharmaceuticals and Vitamins sold in packs or
containers" from the Direction Nos. 2 and 4 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1303/1 dated 25th August 2003 and
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1407/23 dated 26th August 2005 respectively.
The Direction shall come into effect from 01st January 2013.
By the Order of the Consumer Affairs Authoirty,
Rumy Marzook,
Chairman.
Colombo,
04th December 2012

Direction No. - 43.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs the Manufacturers and Importers of Pharmaceuticals and Vitamins that they should
furnish the Consumer Affairs Authority with,
(a) the current retail selling price of every pharmaceutical or vitamin ;
(b) any revision of current retail selling price of any pharmaceutical or vitamin, specifying such revised prices,
batch number and effiective date prior to making such revision,
(c) where any brand of such pharmaceutical or vitamin is manufactured and/or imported subsequent to the date
of this Direction, the information required to be furnished in terms of (a) and (b) above should be furnished
before such pharmaceutical or vitamin is made available for sale.
The Direction shall come into effect from 01st January 2013.
By the Order of the Consumer Affairs Authoirty,
Rumy Marzook,
Chairman.
Colombo,
04th December 2012
