Special Notice
Colombo Municipal Council
MUNICIPAL  TREASURE’S DEPARTMENT
PROVISION  OF  ALTERNATIVE   PREMISES  TO  PAVEMENT  HAWKERS IN  PETTAH AND  FORT AREAS AND
TO   THOSE WHO  LOST THEIR  TRADE STALLS  DUE TO TOWN  DEVELOPMENT   PROGRAMS
COLOMBO Municipal Council has initiated action to provide alternative premises to pavement hawkers in Pettah Fort and
other areas and to those who lost their trade stalls due to Town Development Programs.
The affected tenders should obtain applications from the Revenue Division of the Municipal Treasurer’s Department
and submit to the Deputy Municipal Treasurer (Revenue) together with the documentary evidence in their possession.
Applications will be issued from 27.09.2012 and the closing date of applications will be 15.10.2012. You are hereby kindly
informed that applications will not be accepted under any circumstances after 15.10.2012 and the interested traders should
submit their applications before that date.
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
