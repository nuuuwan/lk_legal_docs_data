No. 1789/16 – TUESDAY, DECEMBER 18, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:- 3/1/2/28.
I hereby give notice under  sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired  the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land has acquired:
One (01) allotment of land in total extent of  0.0003 Hectare,
depicted as Lot No. 187 in the F.V .P. 1105 Sheet (10) Supplement
(10)  prepared by Superintendent of Surveys Kurunagala District,
situated in the Village of Delpaddana in Grama Niladhari Division of
No. 648 Delpaddana in the Weuda Korale Minor Division in
Mawathagama Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kurunagala District
in North Western Province morefully describe as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Heenkenda Kumbura; Description: Road; Claimant:
State; Original claimants:  M.H.M. Haleel and M.H.M. Najar, No.
97, Kandy Road, Paragahadeniya, Weuda; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
All the persons interested  in the aforesaid land acquired are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Mawathagama Divisional  Secretary’s office on the
16th day of January, 2013 at 10.00 A.M. and are hereby requested to
notify  to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 09 th day of
January, 2013 the nature of their interests in the land,the particulars
of their claims for compensation  for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation  and details of the computation of such
amount.
E.M.M.S. Ekanayaka,
Divisional Secretary,
Mawathagama.
Divisional Seretariat,
Mawathagama.
