THE   WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
GLASSWARE  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IN pursuance of the authority conferred on me by the Minister of Labour by an Order made by him under Section 33(1) (a) of
the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 58 of 12th October 1979, I, V . B.
P. K. Weerasinghe, Commissioner of Labour, hereby notify under Section 28 of the aforesaid Ordinance, that I propose to
determine under Section 33(2) of that Ordinance the minimum rate of wages for time work set out in the Schedule hereto in
respect of the workers employed in the Glassware Manufacturing Trade.
Objections to the proposed determination will be received by me until 12.00 noon on 21st November, 2012.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
V . B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
16th October, 2012.

CHEDULE
The determination made by the Commissioner of Labour relating to the minimum rates of wages for  time work in
respect of workers in the Glassware Manufacturing Trade  set out in  the Schedule to the Notification published in
Gazette Extraordinary No.  94/6 of 26th  June, 1980,  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, as varied from time
to time, and last varied by the Notification  in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1,656/10 of 31.05.2010, shall be further varied by the
substitution of the following new schedule.
The minimum rates of wages for time work applicable to the workers in the Glassware Manufacturing Trade Specified
in Column I, shall be the rate set out in the corresponding entry in  Column II.
