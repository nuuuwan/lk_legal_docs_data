THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L1/3902 Ex.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
The following mentioned land depicted in Preliminary Plan No.
MR/2417, lot 117  situated  in the  Village  of  Isadin Town,  in
Matara Divisional   Secretary's  Division, Matara  District,  Southern
Province, has been proposed for acquisition to expand the Nupe-
Godagama highway in Matara:
1. Lot No. 117
Name of   land: Bogahamandiya Alias Bogahakella; Description:
Road; Extent: 0.0037 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 15.08.2012 at  10.30 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on
or before the 10.08.2012 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
B. Ranaweera,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Matara.
18th Julyl, 2012.
