THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131
OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
(1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Order under Section 4 (1)
WHEREAS an Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter
specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between :
Mr. K. W. Perera,
No. 422/5, Kapuwatte,
Ja - Ela.
and
State Timber Corporation,
No. 82, Rajamalwatte Road,
Battaramulla.
Now therefore, I, Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and
Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by
Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of
the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of
1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions)
Act No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. R. S. Ariyapperuma,
No. 03, Araliya Mawatha, Sirimal Uyana, Ratmalana to be the
arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement
by arbitration.
G
AMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and
Labour Relations.
23rd December, 2011,
Colombo.
My No.  : IR/10/52/2010.
THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF
THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
(1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. K. W. Perera,
No. 422/5, Kapuwatte,
Ja - Ela.
and
State Timber Corporation,
No. 82, Rajamalwatte Road,
Battaramulla.
Statement of matter in dispute
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is :
Whether unjust has been caused to Mr. K. W. Perera, the
complainant, who served the State Timber Corporation as
the Deputy Manager (Administration) from year 2003 by not
being placed in the relevant salary / whether the demand
made by him to the State Timber Corporation to place him in
the respective salary is just and if so, to relief he is entitled.
V . B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo,
15th November, 2011.
