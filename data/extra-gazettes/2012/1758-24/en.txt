No. 1758/24 – THURSDAY, MAY 17, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:- PASKO/L/3/1/ACQ/16.
I hereby give notice under  Sub -section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land has acquired:
An allotment of  land in total extent of 0.0395 Hectare, depicted
in the P .P. No. Maha/6169 dated 19.10.2011 prepared by
Superintendent of Surveys, Kandy District, situated in the Village
of Meepitiya in Pasbage Korale Minor Division in Pasbage Korale
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kandy District in Central Province
morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Bodadeniyawatta; Description:Garden; Claimant:
State; Original claimant U.H. Premawathi, Meepitiya, Nawalapitiya ;
Extent: 0.0395 Hectare.
All persons interested  in the aforesaid land  hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Pasbage Korale Divisional  Secretary’s Office on the 26th of
September, 2012 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of September,
2012, the nature of their interests in the land,the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and details of the computation of such amount.
Madhupani Piyasena,
Divisional Secretary,
Nawalapitiya.
Divisional Seretariat,
Nawalapitiya.
22nd February, 2012.
