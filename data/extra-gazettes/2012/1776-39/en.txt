THE WAGES BOARDS ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES BOARD FOR THE PLUMBAGO TRADE
IN accordance with the Provisions of Section 28 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), the Wages Board for the
Plumbago Trade hereby notifies that the Board, by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance,
proposes to vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 12th October,
2012.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
V . B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Plumbago Trade.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
21st August, 2012.

Schedule.
PART  II
The minimum daily rates of wages for time work for a normal working day applicable to workers specified in Column
I shall be the rates set out in Column II.

CHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Rubber Export Trade, and set out in the Schedule to the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 9436 of 21st July 1945 as varied from time to time and last varied by the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1660/35 of 30th June, 2010 shall be further varied by the substitution of the following
new part for Part II of that Schedule.
PART  II
MINIMUM RATE OF WAGES FOR A MONTH
Class of Workers
Grade II
Workers employed in stripping, clipping and bark cutting, Washing and re-smoking mouldy rubber, General labour
including moving to presses, weighing machines and other parts of the store; Assembling, wrapping and pricking rubber for
bailing, pressing, hooping and wire tying, cutting, jute hessian covers, painting, drying, assembling and marking, Treating
jute hessian for special packing, Covering bales with jute hessian and stitching, Laying out bales, cleaning and sweeping of
stores and drains.
Intermediate Grade
Workers employed in weighing, grading and sorting, the work of press operators.
Grade I
Workers employed in loading, unloading and carrying bales, stacking, and sorting.
