LOCAL  LOANS  AND  DEVELOPMENTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 404)
REGULATIONS  made by the Local Loans and Development Commissioners under Section 11 of the Local Loans and

Colombo,
29th June, 2012.
Regulations
Local Loans and Development (Miscellaneous) Regulations No. 1 of 1995 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 905/8 of January 10, 1996 as amended by Regulation published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 922/15 of May, 10, 1996 is
hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) in regulations 11, by the repeal of paragraph (2) thereof, and the substitution therefor, of the following
paragraph :-
“(2) The Commissioners may grant loans upto  a maximum of eighty per cent (80%) of the estimated cost
of any project referred to in paragraph (2) of regulation 10 :
Provided however, any such loan -
(a) not exceeding two million rupees, may be granted by the Commissioners on the recom-
mendation of the Chairman ;
(b) exceeding two million rupees but not exceeding ten million rupees may be granted by the
Commissioners; and
(c) exceeding ten million rupees may be granted by the Commissioners, on the recommenda-
tion of a Deputy Secretary to the Treasury, authorized in that behalf, by the Minister.” ;
(2) in regulation 12, by the insertion immediately after the definition of the word “Commissioners” of the following
definition :-
“Chairman” means the Chairman of the Board of Commissioners appointed under Section 2 of the
Ordinance.”
