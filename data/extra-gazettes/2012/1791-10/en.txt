WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Announcement
“UNDER the provision of Section 289 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance which shall be read along with the Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I hereby announce that as the Chief Minister and
Minister purviewing the subject of Local Government, regulations for the payments of allowances to the Mayor, Deputy
Mayor and  Members if Municipal Councils of Western Province  for attending to Standing Committee meetings, which have
been compiled as per  powers assigned under  Section 289 of Municipal Councils Ordinance which should be read along with
Section 02 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989  has been published in the Extraordinary
Gazette No. 1769/4, dated 30th July, 2012 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, were adopted by the Western
Province Provincial Council at the Council meeting held on 06.11.2012.”.
P
RASANNA RANA THUNGA,
 Chief Minister and the Minister in charge of  Finance and
Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land,  Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Electricity and Power, Man Power and
 Employment and Economic Promotion of the
Western Province Provincial Council.
“Sravasthi Mandiraya”,
Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
30th November, 2012.
