No. 1768/26 – FRIDAY,  JULY 27, 2012
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1)of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Two (2) allotments of land in total extent of 0.810 Hectare as Lot
Nos. 1 and 2 depicted in Preliminary Plan No. GA. 2539 and dated
14th August 1993 prepared by Surveyor General   situated in the
village of Borakanda in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Karandeniya in the, District of Galle in Sourthern  Province morefully
described as follows:
01. Lot No.01
Name of Land: Portion of Mulketiya Ela Addara Kumbura; Extent:
01 Acre.
02. Lot No.02
Name of Land: Narawela alias Halwathura Ela Yaya (Portion);
Extent: 01 Acre.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Lands  are hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Karandeniya Divisional Secretariat on the  2nd October 2012
at  2.00  p.m .  and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 26th September, 2012  the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars  of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land , the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. N. S. T. K de Silva.
Divisional Secretary,
Karandeniya.
Divisional Secretariat,
Karandeniya,
23rd July, 2012.
