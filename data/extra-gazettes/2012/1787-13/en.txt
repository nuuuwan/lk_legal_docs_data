1A
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
Notice under Section 10A(1)(b)
I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana, Returning Officer of the Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting under paragraph
(b) of Subsection (1) of Section 10A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), do hereby declare that Mr.
Tharmalingam Yokarajah who contested at the election for Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha held on 23rd July 2011 as a
candidate on the nomination paper of the Tamil United Liberation Front, has forfeited his rights to have his name retained in
the nomination paper of that recognised political party, for filling any casual vacancy, by reason of him ceasing to be member
of that recognised political party.
K. A. S
UMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
22nd November, 2012.
