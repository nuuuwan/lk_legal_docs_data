FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE   MANMUNAI   SOUTH   WEST
PRADESHIYA   SABHA    UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
meetings of the Council for a period of three months without permission  ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group to which Sinnaththurai Yogendralingam the member
belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the
Ordinance for Election  as a  member of that Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such Group who shall
be declared  elected as  a  member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  Group  has failed to make a nomination within the period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning  Officer  of  Manmunai South
West Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of    Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare   Mr.  Subramaniam Sivalingam who has secured the highest number of preferences next to the last member
declared elected at the election held on 10th March, 2008 to Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha  elected as  a member of
the  Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occured.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Manmunai South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
19th  May, 2012.
