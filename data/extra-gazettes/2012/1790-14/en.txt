THE BOARD OF INVESTMENT OF SRI LANKA LAW, No. 4 OF 1978
Order under Section 22A
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 22A of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law No. 4 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do by this  order declare the area as specified in the
Schedule hereto, to be a Licensed Zone for the purpose of this Law.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
24th December, 2012.
SCHEDULE
All that defined and contiguous allotments of Land depicted in Tracing No. H/ SG/ SRY/ 2012/434 dated
11th  October 2012 made and  certified by K. W. A. Wijewardena, Superintendent of Surveyor in District of Hambantota situated
in Grama  Niladari Divisions of Weliwewa, Meegahajabura and Ihalakubukwewa within the Divisional Secretariat Division of
Suriyawewa in the District of  Hambantota Southern Province and Grama Niladari Division of  Nugegalayaya within the
Divisional  Secretariat Division of Sevenagala in the District of Monaragala, Uva Province and  which said lands are bounded
as follows:-
North  : Mailagahawatta Low Jungle and Road (RDA) ;
East  :  Road (RDA) Irrigation Channel and Road ;
South : Irrigation Channel, Road , Current Fence (Elephant Fence) and Road (newly constructed) and ;
