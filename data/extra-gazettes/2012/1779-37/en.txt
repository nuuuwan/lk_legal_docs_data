The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
SPORTS   ACT,   No.   25,  1973
Order under Sections 32, 33, 34
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 32, 33 and 34 of the Sports Act, No. 25, 1973, I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage,
Minister of Sports do by this order direct that, with effect from 1st of October 2012-
The appointments, made as a temporary measure until election of office bearers to the Sri Lanka Netball Association,
the national federation for netball in Sri Lanka with effect from 1st of June, 2011, published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1719/38 of 19.08.2011 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka are hereby rescinded.
The list of office bearers appointed as a temporary measure until further notice to continue the activities of the Sri
Lanka Netball Association till its next election is as follows. These appointments are effective from 2nd of October,  2012.
Mr. N. K. Abeysinghe - Chairman,
Mrs. R. Y . A. Seetha Ramani - Secretary,
Mrs. Lakshmi Victoria - Member,
Mrs. Lakshmi Samarasinghe - Member.
M
AHINDANANDA ALUTHGAMAGE,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
01st  October, 2012.
