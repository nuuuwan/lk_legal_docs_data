Regulations
1. The Forest Regulations No. 1 of 2008, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1548/ 29 dated May 9, 2008 are
hereby amended as follows :-
(a) by the repeal of regulation 3 thereof and the substitution therefor of the following regulation :-
''3. (1)  The transportations of all species of timber specified in Column III of the Second Schedule or any item
made out of such species of timber within the Divisional Secretary' s Divisions specified in Column
II of that Schedule and located within the Administrative  Districts  referred in Column I of  the said
Second Schedule, shall be exempted from the requirement of obtaining permits.
(2) The transportation of sawn timber specified in Column III of the Third Schedule and any item made
out of  such species of timber within the Municipal Council and Urban Council limits specified in
Column II of that Schedule and located within the Administrative Districts referred to in Column I of
the Third Schedule, shall be exempted from the requirement of obtaining permits .''.
(b) by the repeal of the Second Schedule thereof and the substitution therefor of the following Schedules :-
N.B.—Gazette Extraordinary series No. 1761/1 ends with 1761/22.
