FISHERIES  AND  AQUATIC  RESOURCES  ACT, No. 2 OF 1996
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development under Section 61 read with Sub-
Section 59 of the Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Act, No. 2 of 1996.
Dr. RAJITHA SENARATNA,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development.
Colombo,
03rd December 2012.
Regulations
The payment of Reward Regulations, 2006 published Gazette Extraordinary No. 1430/5 of January 30, 2006 are
hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 2 thereof and the substitution therefore of the following regulation :
"2. The maximum sum of money that may be paid out of the Fisheries Reward Fund for each case, as reward to each
person referred to in paragraph (a), (b) or (c) of Sub-Section (4) of Section 59 of the Act, shall be Rupees Five Hundred
Thousand (Rs. 5,00,000).
