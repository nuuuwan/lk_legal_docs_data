1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE   CHA V AKACHCHERI   PRADESHIYA
SABHA    UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil  Arasu Kadchi  the recognized political party to which  Rasaiah
Matheeswaran   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for  Election as a member of that Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party who shall be declared  elected as a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Kahandawa Arachchige Sumith Chandana,  Returning  Officer  of  Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of    Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Siriskantharasa Siriranjan who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared
elected at the election held on 23rd July, 2011 to Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha elected  as a member of the Chavakachcheri
Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occured.
K. A. S
UMITH CHANDANA,
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
16th  November, 2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  DAMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a  member
of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
R. M. Vipula Rathnayake,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Ajith Kumarasiri
Rajapaksha Dewage,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer  of  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha,   acting in  terms
of    Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr.  Ajith Kumarasiri
Rajapaksha Dewage,   elected as  a member  of   the  Damana  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
23rd  November, 2012.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE  EMBILIPITIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman in  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be   a
member of  the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Withanage Wijaya Kumara,  the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
decide upon the member of that Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as
Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Amil Meegasdeniya Kankanamge to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning  Officer  of  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha,   acting in  terms of    Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Amil Meegasdeniya Kankanamge,   elected as  Chairman of   the  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Embiliptiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
22nd  November, 2012.
