EASTERN    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments made  by  the Governor   of  the Eastern  Province
I,  Mohan Wijewickrama, Governor of the Eastern Province by virtue of power vested in me under the Section 154F(1), (4) and
(5) of the 13th Amendment  to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby announce that
I have made the following appointments.
Hon. Abdul Majeed Mohamed Najeeb - Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local
Government, Regional Administration, Man Power, Rehabilitation
and Resettlement, Rural Development and Environment of the
Eastern Provincial Council with effect from 24th of September, 2012.
At the Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. U. L. A. Azees - S. L A. S. - Spl.
Hon. Ahamed Nazeer Zainulabdeen - Minister of Agriculture, Animal Production and Development, Rural
Industries Development, Fisheries and Tourism of the Eastern
Provincial Council with effect from 24th of September, 2012.
At the Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. K. Pathmanathan - S. L. Ag. S. - Class I
Hon. Dissanayaka Wimalaweera - Minister of Education, Cultural Affairs, Lands and Land
Development and Transport with effect from 24th of September,
2012.
At the Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. N. A. A. Pushpakumara - S. L. A. S. - Class I
Hon. Meera Sahibu Udumalebbe - Minister of Road Development, Irrigation, Housing and
Construction, Rural Electrification and Water Supply of the Eastern
Provincial Council with effect from 24th of September, 2012.
At the Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. A. H. M. Ansar - S. L. A. S. - Spl.
Hon. Ibrahim Mohamed Mohamed Monsoor - Minister of Health and Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare,
Probation and Childcare Services, Women's Affairs, Youth Affairs,
Sports, Information Technology Education, Co-operative
Development, Food Supply and Distribution of the Eastern
Provincial Council with effect from 24th of September, 2012.
At the Secretary to the Ministry - Dr. S. Amalanathan - S. L. A. S. - Spl.
M
OHAN WIJEWICKRAMA,
Governor,
Eastern Province.
