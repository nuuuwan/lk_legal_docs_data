1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER   OF  THE   YATIYANTOTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in  Yatiyantota  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of being   elected as a member
of  the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council ;
 And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party the recognized political party to which Annamalei Paskaran
the  member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible
under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Yatiyantota   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall
be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.  K. A. Premasinghe,  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning  Officer of  Yatiyantota
Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare  Mr.  K. A. Premasinghe, elected as  a member  of   the Yatiyantota  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C.  N. THALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Yatiyantota  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle,
30th  November, 2012.
