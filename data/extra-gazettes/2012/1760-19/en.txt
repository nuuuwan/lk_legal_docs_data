It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 310039 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Thumbakarawila-
gama (Block 02) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama 
Niladhari Division of E 455 E - Haluapullanawewa in the Divi-
sional Secretariat Division of Dambulla in the District of Matale 
in the Province of Central should submit his claim to the under-
signed before 22nd June, 2012 In the event of failure  to submit 
any claim before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken 
to hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and ac-
cordingly publish such determination in the Government Gazette 
in terms of Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 310039 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals’ office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
