PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATIONS
Order  under Section 111 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 111 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) read with Article 44(2) of the
Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, do by this order declare and appoint Hambantota in the Southern Province to be a part of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka and its respective limits set out in Schedule “A”, I do further declare and appoint the places set out in the
Schedule “B” to be legal quays within such limits for the loading and unloading of goods and declare the bounds and extent
of such quays to be set out in the Schedule “B” within the Port of Hambantota.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
(Minister of Finance and Planning)
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
01st June 2012.
