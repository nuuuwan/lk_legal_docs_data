1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN   OF  THE   DAMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER   SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Damana   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected  as  a member
of  the Eastern   Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the  recognized political party to which
W. D.  Weerasinghe  the Chairman   belonged, was  required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   decide
upon the   member of  that   Damana   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Chairman
within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Jagoda Arachchige  Mangala Mahinda Gunawardhana,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sivasubramaniam  Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer of   Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr. Jagoda Arachchige
Mangala Mahinda Gunawardhana , elected as   Chairman  of   the Damana   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
 Damana   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
02nd  November  2012.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE DAMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member   in  Damana   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of  the Eastern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
W . D. Weerasinghe, the  member   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,   to nominate
a person  eligible under the  Ordinance  for  Election as a member of  that  Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated  Mr. D. M. Sisil Wijerathne
being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I,  Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning  Officer  of  Damana Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting   in  terms
of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare    Mr.   D. M. Sisil Wijerathne
elected as a member of the Damana  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Damana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
02nd   November  2012.
