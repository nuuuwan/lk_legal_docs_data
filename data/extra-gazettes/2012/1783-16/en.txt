THE  NATIONAL   AUTHORITY  ON  TOBACCO  AND  ALCOHOL  ACT
Regulation  made by the Minister of Health under Section 30 read with Section 34 of the National Authority on Tobacco and
Alcohol Authority Act, No. 27 of 2006.
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
Minister of Health.
Colombo,
26th  October, 2012.
Regulation
The Tobacco products (Labelling and Packaging) Regulations No. 1 of 2012 published in Gazette Extraordinary No.
1770/15 of August 8, 2012 are hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 1 thereof and the substitution therefor, of the
following regulation :
“1. These regulations may be cited as the Tobacco Products (Labelling and Packaging) Regulations No. 1 of 2012.
The provisions of these regulations other than this regulation shall come into operation on March 1, 2013”.
