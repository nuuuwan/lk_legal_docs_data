THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
W AGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  MATCH  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IN accordance with the Provisions of Section 28 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) the Wages Boards for the
Match Manufacturing Trade hereby notifies that the Board, by virtue of the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid
Ordinance, proposes to vary in the manner set out in the Schedule hereto, such decisions of the Board as are specified in that
Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on 04th December,
2012.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is made.
V . B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Match Manufacturing Trade.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
06th November, 2012.

SCHEDULE
The decisions made by the Wages Board for the Cigar Manufacturing Trade and set out in the Schedule
to the Notification published in Gazette  No. 9,585 of July 26, 1946,  as last varied in the manner set out in the  Notification

PART I
Minimum Rates of Wages for Piece Work
The (consolidated) minimum rates of wages for piece work for Cigar rolling (inclusive of the cleaning up of fillers, the
preparation of wrappers for rolling, the preparation of fillers for rolling including filling up with cuttings, the tying up of rolled
cigars with thread, and the bundling of cigars into tens) shall be —
Rs. 595.00 per 1000 Cigars.
