EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   MAHAWA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  removal of Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
S. M. Bandula Pushpa Kumara  the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to decide upon the  member of that Mahawa  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to  such party who shall be declared  elected as
Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Medagedara Menik Ralalage Rohana Wasantha Kumara Medagedara to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Muhammadu Nafeel,  Returning  Officer  of  Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of    Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr.  Medagedara
Menik Ralalage Rohana Wasantha Kumara Medagedara elected as Chairman of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
14th  August, 2012.
