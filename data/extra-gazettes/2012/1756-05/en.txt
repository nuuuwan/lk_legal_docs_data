1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  WELIGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
 WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Weligama  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  death ;
And  whereas the the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Oliver Perera Wickramasingha the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of  the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Weligama Urban Council  who belongs to
such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the aforesaid  recognised political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Justin Migel Perera
being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning  Officer  of   the Weligama  Urban Council,  acting
in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Justin Migel
Perera  elected as a member of the  Weligama  Urban Council .
C. A. Suneth Lochana,
Returning Officer,
Weligama Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Matara,

th  April, 2012.
