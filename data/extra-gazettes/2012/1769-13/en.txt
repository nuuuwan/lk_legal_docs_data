PRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Jaffna  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Ajmaeen Asfar,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Jaffna Municipal Council  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr. Bava Sahib Mohideen
Sharapul Annam being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Ponnudurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in  terms of Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Bava Sahib Mohideen Sharapul
Annam, elected as  a member  of   the Jaffna Municipal Council.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Jaffna Municipal Council.
Jaffna,
23 rd  July  2012.
