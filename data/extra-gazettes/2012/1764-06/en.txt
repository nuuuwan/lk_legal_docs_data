PART  IV (A) – PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
ARTICLE  154  (B)  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
BY Virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph 8 of Article 154  (B) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka. I, Don Sarath Mohan Wijewickrama, Governor of the Eastern Province do hereby dissolve the Eastern Provincial
Council with effect from midnight 27th June, 2012.
D.S. M
OHAN WIJEWICKRAMA,
Governor of the Eastern Province.
Office of the Governor of the Eastern Province,
