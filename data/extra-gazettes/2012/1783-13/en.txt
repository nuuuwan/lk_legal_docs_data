EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  NUWARA-ELIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ceylon Democratic Unity Alliance the recognised political party to which Rannathan
Raviran the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such  party
who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the aforesaid  recognised political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Kuppan Ramalingam
being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando,  Returning Officer of Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of   Section 65A(2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Kuppan Ramalingam elected as a member of the  Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara-Eliya.
08th  October 2012.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  AKURESSA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance the recognised political party to which S. L.
Sunil the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such  party  who
shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the aforesaid  recognised political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Hewa Kankanamge
Chaminda Pradeep Kumara  being an eligible person under the  aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning Officer of  Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha , acting
in terms of   Section 65A(2) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Hewa Kankanamge
Chaminda Pradeep Kumara elected as a member of the  Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha.
C
ANDAUDA ARACHCHIGE SUNETH LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Akuressa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara.
