PART I : SECTION (I) — GENxERAL
THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW,  No. 41 OF 1978
Order under Section  03
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 03 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development being of the opinion that the area specified in  Column I of the
Schedule II hereto and having the metes and bounds specified in corresponding entry in Column II of Schedule II, except the
areas already declared as Urban Development Areas and set out in Schedule I hereto are suitable for development and do
hereby this order declare such areas to be Urban Development Areas for the purpose of the said Section.
I do also hereby declare that the already declared areas set out in Schedule I together with the other areas declared as
aforesaid, shall be included within the limits of Hikkaduwa Urban Development Area set out in Column II under Schedule II
hereto.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development.
Ministry of Defence & Urban Development,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo 03,
25th June  2012.
