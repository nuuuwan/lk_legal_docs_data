WAGES BOARDS  FOR THE RUBBER (INCLUDING TYRE MANUFACTURING AND REBUILDING)
PLASTIC AND PETROLEUM RESIN PRODUCTS MANUFACTURING TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards  Regulations that Mr. Sanjeewa Serasingha has been
appointed as Employers' Representative in place of Mr. P. A. D. Leo Samson and Mr. U. C. Perera, Mr. P. J. A. Perera and
Mrs. R. M. Shanthi Sujeewa Rathnayaka have been appointed as Employees' Representatives of the Wages Board for the
Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing and Rebuilding) Plastic and Petroleum Resin Products Manufacturing Trade in place
of Mr. T. P. Upali Perera, Mr. L. H. A. Priyantha and Mr. M. Hemapala, respectively by the Hon, Minister of Labour and Labour
Relations under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance No. 27 of 1941 (chapter 136). This Appointment is valid only up
to 15.02.2013.
HERATH YAPA,
Actg, Secretary,
