NOTICE UNDER  SECTION 10 A (1) (a)
  Nuwara- Eliya   Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of  the Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10 A (1) (a) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Murugiah Yogeswary of the
Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as her has ceased to be a member
of the Ceylon  Democratic Unity Alliance.
