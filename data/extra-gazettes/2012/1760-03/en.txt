KADUWELA   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the  Chief  Minister  and  Minister  in charge  of  the subject of  Local  Government in the  Western
Province Provincial Council, under Section 71 (1) of the  Chapter 252 of  the Municipal Council  Ordinance read with  Section
2 of the Provincial Councils  (Consequential Provisions)  Act No. 12 of  1989, has named the road as ‘’Major Ravi Alagiyawanna
Mawatha” as  per details given in the  following Schedule.
G
AMINI GUNASEKARA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Kaduwela Municipal Council.
Kaduwela Municipal Council,
24th May 2012.

2. District - Colombo
3. Present Name of the Road - Henawatta Road
4. New Name of the Road - Major Ravi Alagiyawanna  Mawatha
5. Details of the Road
(i) Road Commencement -
Left   :   Property of Assessment No . 7 on the same road
Right :  Property of Assessment No . 4 on the same road
(ii) Road ending -
Left   :   Property of Assessment No . 7 at 6th Lane of Ramahera Circular road,
Right :  Property of Assessment No . 8 at 6th Lane of Ramahera Circular road.
