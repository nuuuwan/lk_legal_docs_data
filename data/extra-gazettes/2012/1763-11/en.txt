SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48 OF 2007
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of  2007,  I, Sarath Amunugama,
Acting Minister  of   Finance and Planning of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, do by this Order impose in
respect of the commodity items specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates  specified
in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of  01 months commencing
from 20th  June  2012, subject to provisions of  Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of  2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette Extraordi-
nary No. 1757/23 of  11th May 2012,  in  respect  of  Sugar  ( H. S. No. 17.01)  is rescinded with   effect from 20th June 2012.
S
ARATH AMUNUGAMA,
Acting Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
19th June  2012.
