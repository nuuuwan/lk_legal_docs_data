Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS  by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act
No. 37 of 1968 dated 13/1/2010 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 1637/18
dated 21.01.2010 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter/ specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 06.01.2010.

and
Packserve (Pvt) Ltd, No. 130, Wevelduwa, Kelaniya.
was referred to Mr. V. I. Jayasuriya for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order by revoked, I Jagath Balasooriya, Acting Minister of Labour and Labo ur
Relations do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said Order dated 13.01.2010.
Dr. Jagath Balasooriya,
Acting Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
