Mr. Senanayake Mudiyanselage Bandula Pushpakumara, Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho is not guilty of the allegations
ii, iii  included in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1717/18 and dated on 03.08.2011 of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, further it has been decided that Mr.  Senanayake Mudiyanselage Bandula Pushpakumara, Chairman of Pradeshiya
Sabha Maho is guilty of the allegations of i, ii, iii, iv, v, vi  included in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1720/7 and dated
on 24.08.2011 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
I am satisfied with the findings after deeply studying the evidences submitted in respect of all the allegations and
findings forwarded by Single Person Commission of Inquiry.
Accordingly, I am satisfied that Mr. Senanayake Mudiyanselage Bandula Pushpakumara, Chairman of Pradeshiya
Sabha Maho has committed offences set out in Sections 185(1) (a), 185(1) (b), 185(1) (c), 185(1) (d), 185(1) (e) of  Sub-section
185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, due to the commitment of offences pertaining to the allegations ii, iv, v, vi, vii,
viii  included in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1717/18 and dated on 03.08.2011 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka and allegations i, ii, iii, iv, v, vi included in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1720/7 and dated on 24.08.2011 of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
On satisfying so, I hereby notify that Mr. Senanayaka Mudiyanselage Bandula Pushpakumara is removed from the
post of “Chairman” the Executive Post of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho with effect from 10 July 2012 by virtue of powers vested
in me under Sub-section (1) of 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section Two of Provincial
Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
