THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARDS   FOR  THE  TANNING  FOOTWEAR AND   LEATHER  GOODS  MANUFACTURING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation  30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that  Mr.  D. S. Dissanayaka   has been  appointed
as the Employer   Representatives Member  of  the  Wages Board for   the  Tanning Footwear and  Leather Goods Manufacturing
Trade  in place of  Mr. D. S. W. Wijerathne by the  Hon. Minister of    Labour and  Labour Relations  under Section 9 of the
Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) . This Appointment is valid only up to 16.02.2013.
W. J. L. U.W
IJAYAWEERA,
