PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
Filling of a vacancy in the North Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988  that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Central Province
Provincial Council by reason of failing to make and subscribe an oath or affirmation in terms of the Constitution of the
Democratic  Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated Mr. Godayale Gedara Chandrasena
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Godayale Gedara Chandrasena, elected as a member of
the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Polonnaruwa in respect of which such vacancy occured.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
26th December, 2012.
