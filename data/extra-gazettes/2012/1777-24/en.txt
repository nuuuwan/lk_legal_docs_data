PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Proclamations & c., by the Governors
1A
EASTERN    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice
KNOW  ye that by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 154(B), 8(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mohan Wijewickrama,  the Governor of the Eastern Province, do by this Proclamation, summon the
First Session of the Second Eastern Provincial Council, to meet at 11.00 a. m. on the 01st of October, Two Thousand Twelve
at the Provincial Council Assembly Hall at Assembly Secretariat of Eastern Provincial Council, Trincomalee.
M
OHAN WIJEWICKRAMA,
Governor,
Eastern Province.
Governor's Office of the Eastern Province,
Trincomalee,
25th September  2012.
