 STATUTORY DETERMINATION UNDER SECTION 19 OF THE LAND REFORM LAW, No. 1 OF 1972
WHEREAS a statutory declaration under section 18 of the Law has been made by Mr. Don Solomon Kotalawela of No. 8/7,
Bagatale Terrace, Colombo 3, and  the Land Reform Commission by virtue of the powers vested in it under section 19 of the
Law, has made a statutory determination to the estate of the deceased declarant specifying the portion of agricultural land
owned by the statutory lessee which he shall be allowed to retain. The agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the
statutory lessee is described in the schedule below.
