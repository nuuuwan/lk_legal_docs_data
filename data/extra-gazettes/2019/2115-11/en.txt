Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that Government intends to
acquire the following *land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the *land/Servitude to be acquired :
The allotments of land from A to D in extent of 1.2343
Hectares depicted in the Final Village Plan No. F.V .P . 783 Add
13 dated 29.12.2018 prepared by the Surveyor General situated
in the village of Parawalatenna in the Grama Niladhari Division
of No. 134 B, Kithulgala South in the Kithulgala Palatha minor
division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Yatiyantota
in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot No. A – Ahaslena Estate – Extent: 0.5385 Hectare.
North: Kelani River; East: Ahaslena Estate; South: Lot No. B
(Road); West: Ahaslena Estate.
02. Lot No. B – Ahaslena Estate – Extent: 0.0387 Hectare.
North: Lot No. A and Ahaslena Estate; East: Road and
Ahaslena Estate; South: Lot No. D (Road) and Lot No. C;
West: Ahaslena Estate and Road.
03. Lot No. C – Ahaslena Estate – Extent: 0.6263 Hectare.
North: Lot No. B (Road); East: Lot No. D (Road); South:
