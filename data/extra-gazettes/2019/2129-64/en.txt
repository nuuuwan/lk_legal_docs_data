FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   ARANAYAKA    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Aranayaka pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Amarasingha Arachchilage Ishara Madushani Amarasingha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A
(1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that 7 - Debathgama of Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ethugal Pedige Wimalawathi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Aranayaka pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ethugal
Pedige Wimalawathi was elected as the member of the  7 - Debathgama of Aranayaka pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Aranayaka pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
25th June, 2019.
