Regulations
01. These Regulations may be cited as the Imports and Exports Control Regulation No. 02 of 2019.
02. The schedule I and IV of the special Import License Regulations published in the Gazette (Extraordinary )
No. 2044/40 dated 09
th November 2017 and Schedule I of the published in the Gazette (Extraordinary )
No.2076/4 dated18 th June, 2018 are hereby amended.
03. The Regulation No. 03 of the Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 2044/40 dated 09 th November, 2017 is amended as
follow
These regulations shall also apply to the business entities established under the Board of Investment Law No. 4 of
1978 subject to the Commercial Hub Regulation No. 01 of 2013 published in the 
Gazette (Extraordinary )
No.1818/30 dated 11 th July 2013 issued under the Financial Act No.12 of 2012 and No.12 of 2013 as amended
from time to time.
04. The following items specified under H.S. Headings and H.S. Codes appearing in Column I and II and its corresponding
entries appearing in Column III of the Schedule I of the Special Import License Regulations published in the
Gazette (Extraordinary) No.2044/40 dated 09
th November 2017 shall be deleted by  this Regulation.
