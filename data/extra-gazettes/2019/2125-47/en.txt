  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,
the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the
aforesaid Act, in respect of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka
under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the Order
made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2067/17 of April 19, 2018, by the repeal of the
Schedule I and II to that Order and the substitution therefor of the following new Schedule I and II as follows :-
“SCHEDULE   I
Condititons
National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admiting students for the degree programme,
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of –
(i) the Bachelor of Interior Design, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of three (3) ‘simple passes (S)’ in
any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advanced Level) Examination or equivalent.
(ii) the Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer Science and the Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer
Networks, adhere to the minimum entry qualification of -
