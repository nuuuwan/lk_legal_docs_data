No. 2130/48 –  FRIDAY, JULY 05, 2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 
 
My Ref. No.:  Sri Japura/LA/68/NWSD 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for public 
purpose.  
 
Description of the land to be acquired:  
Allotments of land, total extent of about 0.0316 Hectare 
described below, depicted as lot No. 1 in Plan                           
No. CO 9827 and dated 16.05.2019 prepared by 
Superintendent of survey, situated in the village of 
Welikada in 514 A, Welikada West Grama Niladari 
Division in Divisional Secretaries Division of Sri 
Jayawardhanapura Kotte in Colombo District Western 
Province. 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: MillagahawaththaAssessment No. 
219/1, Rajagiriya Road: Description: Garden, part of a 
building; Claimant of the land: Government, Original 
Claimants: M.A.U. Fernando, Katudeniyawaththa, 
Bopitiya; Extent: 0.0316 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
on 10th July 2019. 
 
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
with two copies on or  before the 08th July 2019 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount and proof the ownership 
relevant documents and deeds. 
 
 Y.K.SHIROMI JEEWAMALA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Sri Jayawardhanapura, Kotte. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Sri Jayawardhanapura, Kotte, 
04th July, 2019.
