SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sec. 32 and 33
BY virtue of powers vested in me, under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Harin Fernando,
Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports suspend the registration, with immediate effect, of the
Sri Lanka Table Tennis Association, the National Association for the Sport of Table Tennis and appoint the following
interim committee to continue the activities thereof as a Provisional  arrangement.
1. Mr. Galla Liyanage Sajith Jayalal - President
2. Dr. Dhammika Senanayake - Secretary
3. Mrs. Kariyawasam Devarakottage A yanthi Shalika Kariyawasam - Treasurer
And, I do hereby authorize the Director  General of the Department of Sports Development to convene a Special
General Meeting of the Table Tennis Association and appoint an Election Committee in terms of the Sports Act, No. 25 of
1973 and Subsequent amendments made thereto and convene and conduct the election under supervision of the Director
General.
H
ARIN F ERNANDO ,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
01st  March, 2019.

SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sec. 32 and 33
BY virtue of powers vested in me, under Section 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Harin Fernando,
Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports do hereby suspend the registration of with immediate
effect, of the Sri Lanka Body Building and Physical Fitness Federation and the Sri Lanka Powerlifting Federation and
appoint the following interim committees to continue the activities thereof as a Provisional  arrangement.
The Interim Committee to be appointed to the Sri Lanka Body Building and Physical Fitness Federation as follows :
1. Mr. Mahesh Gammanpila - President
2. Mrs. Shiromi Ranawaka - Secretary
3. Mr. P. B. Jayathilake - Treasurer
The Interim Committee to be appointed to the Sri Lanka Powerlifting Federation as follows :
1. Dr. Lal Ekanayake - President
2. Mrs. Lakmini Samarasinghe - Secretary
3. Mr. Ravindra Kumara - Treasurer
And, I do hereby authorize the Director  General of the Department of Sports Development to convene a Special
General Meeting of the Sri Lanka Body Building and Physical Fitness Federation and Sri Lanka Powerlifting Federation
in terms of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 and Subsequent amendments made thereto and appoint election committee to
convene and conduct the election under supervision of the Director General.
H
ARIN F ERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
01st  March, 2019.
