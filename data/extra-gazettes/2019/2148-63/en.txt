NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   66G   OF   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION   ORDINANCE   AS
AMENDED   BY   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   (AMENDMENT)   ACT,   No.   22   OF   2012
I, M. Kodippili Arachchi, Commissioner of Local Government of North Central Province do hereby notified that the
special meeting of Municipal Council will be held at Conference Hall of Municipal Council, Polonnaruwa on 10.00 a. m.
of 20.11.2019, for the purpose of appointing a member for the vacant post of Deputy Mayor in Municipal Council
Polonnaruwa within North Central Province, in terms of the powers vested in me under Section 66G of Local Authorities
Election Ordinance as amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012.
M. K
ODIPPILI ARACHCHI,
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government,
North Central Province.
Department of Local Government,
North Central Provincial Council Complex,
Anuradhapura,
08th November, 2019.
