FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KOLONNAWA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Kolonnawa Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending
three consecutive meetings of the Council without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Batagoda Kankanamage Dona Champika the member belonged, was required by me under Section   66 A (1)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as
a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Podi Menike to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the Kolonnawa
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Podi Menike was selected as the member of the Kolonnawa Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Kolonnawa Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
29th March, 2019.
