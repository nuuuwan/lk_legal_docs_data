APPOINTMENT  OF  DISTRICT  OPIUM  BOARDS  UNDER  THE  OPIUM  ORDINANCE IN 1910,  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  POISONS,   OPIUM   AND  DANGEROUS  DRUGS  ORDINANCE  No.  17  OF  1929
IN terms of the powers vested in me under the Section 42(2) of the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs Ordinance
No. 17 of 1929 (Chapter 549), I, Rajitha Senarathna, Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine do hereby
amend the Opium Board constituted for Gampaha District as follows :
Accordingly, Dr. (Mr) K. Sayakkara and Dr. (Mrs.) C. H. G. Kasthuriratne who were appointed as member of the
District Opium Board in the Gampaha District by Gazette Extraordinary No. 1638/6 and dated 25.01.2010 have been
replaced by the following
01. Dr. (Mr.) M. A. A. K. Amarasinha,
232/C/1, Perera Road,
Ganemulla.
02. Dr. (Mr.) Sunil Shantha Wijethunga,
Kaluwalgoda,
Makowitha.

R. R AJITHA S ENARA THNA,
