THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  No. 2129/96  DATED  28TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/96 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4555/14).
In the caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “GREATER” Shall be substituted
with “KANDY  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AREA  COMPRISING  OF  THE”.
In the content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification the word “had” in the fourth line shall be
substituted with “have”
The word “Greater” in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be substituted with “Kandy
Urban Development Area comprising of the”.
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be read as follows;

PART I  :  SEC. (I) - GAZETTE    EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRA TIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 08.11.2019
“NOTICE  OF APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE KANDY URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA
COMPRISING  OF  THE  KANDY  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  AREA.”
“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section
8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the Kandy Urban Development
Area comprising of the Kandy Municipal Council area, prepared under Section 8A of the said Law. ”
P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
In the caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “Greater” shall be substituted with
“Kandy Urban Development Area Comprising of the”.
In the content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Amendment)” in the second line
shall be substituted with “Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the”  and the word “Greater” in the second line shall be
substituted with “Kandy Urban Development Area comprising of the”
The word “Amendment Act” given in the last line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be subsitituted with
“amended Law”.
The rectifications introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Kandy Urban Development Area comprising of the Kandy Municipal Council
Area.”.
“Public are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority
Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982, for the Kandy Urban Development Area comprising of the
Kandy Municipal Council area has been approved on 28th June, 2019, by Hon. Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of
Megapolis & Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 8F of the said amended Law.”
D
R. JAGATH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
07th November, 2019,
Notwithstanding the provisions contained in the antecedent  Gazette Notification bearing No. 2129/96 dated 28th June,
2019 (07-4555/14), the rectification hereinbefore mentioned shall have retrospective effect and shall be deemed to have
come into operation on the 28th day of June, 2019.
P
ATALI  CHAMPIKA  RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
