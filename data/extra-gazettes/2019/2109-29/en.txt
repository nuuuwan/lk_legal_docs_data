LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry's Reference  No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/233.
My Reference No.: W AR/LAN/ACQUS/A2/5-67.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the Land in be acquired :
The following allotments of land from Lot No. 01 to 02
depicted in the preliminary Plan No. K 3632 dated 29.12.2018
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Survey, Kegalle
District for Surveyor General within the boundaries shown
below situated in the Village of Bopitiya in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 94, Bopitiya in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Warakapola in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land : Pallegama Estate (Batuwana Division);
Description of the Land: Estate; Claimant: Government; First
Claimant: Unknown; Extent : 3.1986 Hectares.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land : Pallegama Estate (Batuwana Division);
Description of the Land: Thicket; Claimant: Government;
Extent : 0.0301 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at Warakapola Divisional Secretariat on
21.03.2019 at 10.00 a.m..
Further, you are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before 07.03.2019 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount
of compensation and details of computation of such amount.
L
AXMENDRA DAMAYANTHKUMARA THENNAKOON,
Divisional Secretary,
Warakapola.
At the Warakapola Divisional Secretariat,
06th Febuary, 2019.
