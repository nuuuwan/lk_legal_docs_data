FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   DEHIWALA - MOUNT
LA VINIA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which S. W.
N. Danuska Silva the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate K. Premasiri to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the  Dehiwala - Mount
Lavinia Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that K. Premasiri was selected as the member of the Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
16th September, 2019.
