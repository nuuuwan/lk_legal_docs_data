1A  G  30373— 57  (06/2019)
NOTICE  OF  APPROV AL  FOR  RECTIFICATION   OF  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE
NOTIFICATION
NOTICE  is given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I, Patali Champika
Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8H(1) of
the Urban Development Authority Law No. 4 of 1982 as amended, hereby approve the rectification in the English version
of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification bearing No. 2054/45 dated 18th January, 2018.
In the last para of the first page in the aforementioned Gazette Notification has been erroneously printed as
“said” where it has to be rectified as “side”.
This rectification shall be deemed to have come into operation on 18th January, 2018 and accordingly, every act
done or omitted to done and every order made in pursuance of any such provision of regulation shall be deemed to have
been valid and effectual as if the provisions of the said regulation had been in operation from the dates hereinbefore
mentioned.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
10th June 2019.

NOTICE  OF  APPROV AL  FOR  RECTIFICATION   OF  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE
NOTIFICATION
NOTICE  is given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I, Patali Champika
Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8H(1) of
the Urban Development Authority Law No. 4 of 1982 as amended, hereby approve the rectification in the English version
of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification bearing No. 2054/45 dated 18th January, 2018.
In the Third Column of the substituted revised form C2 “m” was erroneously printed in Page 3A of the English
version of the aforementioned Gazette Notification as “(%)” where it has to be rectified as “m”.
In the Fourth Column of the same substituted revised form C2 “%” was erroneously printed in Page 3A of the
English version of the aforementioned Gazette Notification as “(m)” where it has to be rectified as “(%)”.
This rectification shall be deemed to have come into operation on 18th January, 2018 and accordingly, every act
done or omitted to done and every order made in pursuance of any such provision of regulation shall be deemed to have
been valid and effectual as if the provisions of the said regulation had been in operation from the dates hereinbefore
mentioned.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
10th June 2019.
