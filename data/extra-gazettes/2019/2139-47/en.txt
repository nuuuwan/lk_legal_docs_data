UV A  PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
APPOINTMENTS   MADE  BY  THE  HON. GOVERNOR  OF  UV A  PROVINCE
IT is hereby announce that, the following Appointments have been made by Hon. President's Counsel Marshall Perera, the
Governor of the Uva Province under the Section 32 (1) of the Provincil Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1. District Sports officer Mr. R. M. S. L. B. Rathnayake, to cover up duties of the Director of Sports of Uva
Province, with effect from the date 13.05.2019.
2. Mrs. A. A. L. Amarasekara, Class III officer of  the Sri Lanka Administrative  Service, to perform the duties of
the Commissioner of Badulla Municipal Council, with immediate effect (from the date 15.05.2019) on full time
basis.
3. Dr. Mrs. J. C. M. Thennakoon, as Provincial Director of Health Service of Uva Province, with effect from
the date 06.06.2019.
4. Mrs R. A. H. A. Samarasinghe, Retired special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, has been
re- appointed on contract basis as the Secretary to the Council of the Uva Provincial Council, from the date
23.07.2019 up to 30.11.2019.
