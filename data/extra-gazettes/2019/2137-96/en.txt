Government  Notifications
L.D.B 21/78(xiv)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem,  Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the
said Institute have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Major General (retired) Herath
Mudiyanselage Udaya Bandara Herath to be the Competent Authority with effect from August 1, 2019 for the purpose of
exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of the Director of the Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any
power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director under the
said Act, or  any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
23rd August, 2019.
