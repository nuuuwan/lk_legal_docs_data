FILLING OF A V ACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13(b) OF ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF
THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary General of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under Section 64(1) of the
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by the Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a Vacancy has occured in the
Membership of the Eighth Parliament by reason of death of Mr. Salinda Dissanayake, who was declared elected as a
Member of Parliament for the Electoral District No. 15 - Kurunegala under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act ;
And Whereas the Returning Officer, for the Electoral District which returned the said member was directed
by the Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No.1 of 1981 to fill this vacancy
as provided for under Paragraph 13(b) of Article 99 of the Constitution;
And Whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2)of the aforesaid Act has made a return in
Form “M” setout in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. H. M. D. B. Herath was declared
elected as a Member of Parliament for the said Electoral District;
Now therefore, we, the Election Commission, in the exercise of the powers vested in the Election
Commission by Section 64(2) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 which shall be read with the
Provisions of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby notify the General Public that Mr. H. M. D. B. Herath has been declared elected as a Member of the Eighth
Parliament for the Electoral District No. 15 - Kurunegala to fill the vacancy caused by reason of death of
Mr. Salinda  Dissanayake.
