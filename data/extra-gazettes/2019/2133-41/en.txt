SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Granting  Permission  by  Virtue  of  Powers  Vested  in  me  Under  Section  27(A)  of  Provincial
Council  Act, No. 42 of 1987 as the  Term  of  the  Sixth  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial Council of the
Democratic  Socialist  Republic  of  Sri Lanka  Ended on 26 th  September, 2017
AN order to provide for the services during the year 2019, to authorize the raising of loans within the Province of  Sabaragamuwa
for the purpose of such service, to make financial provisions in respect of certain activities of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council during that more months, to enable the payment, by way of advances out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
fund or any other fund or  monies of, or  at the disposal of,  the Sabaragamuwa  Provincial Council  Fund, and to make Provision
for matters connected with or incidental to the aforesaid matter.
BE it enacted by me, the Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows.
1. I, do hereby grant Permission unto the Chief  Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the province
to spend Rupees Thirty One Billion and One Hundred Million only, (Rs. 31,100,000,000/-) and which
estimated for the services of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the year  beginning on 01st
January, 2019 and ending on 31st December, 2019, by vitue of powers vested  in me  under Section
27 (A) of  the  Provincial Council  Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the Sixth  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial
Council ended  on 26th September, 2017.
2. (1). W ithout prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which estimate will be Rupees Thirty One Billion and One Hundred  Million
(Rs. 31,100,000,000) for  the Service of the period beginning on 01st January 2019 and ending on
31st December. 2019 in this order referred to as the ‘‘financial year 2019’’  shall be met.

specified in the corresponding entry column (viii) of that schedule.

Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council from any activity specified in column (iv) of the second schedule

Council on such activity, the  minister may, from time to time, by order direct that such sums may
deem necessary to meet such expenditure  shall be payable by way of advances out of
Sabaragamuwa Provincial councial fund or any other  fund or monies of or at the disposal of, the
Sabaragamuwa Provincil Council,so however that the aggregate of the sums so advances  does
not exceed the maximum  limit of entry in column (v) of that schedule. Any sums so advanced in
respect of such  activity shall be refunded to the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council fund in such
manner as the Governor may by order direct.
7. (1) Any money which, by virtue of the provisions of  the first schedule to this order, have been
allocated to recurrent expenditure under any programme appearing under any head specified in
that schedule  but have not been  expended  or may likely to be unexpended, may be transferred
to the allocation of recurrent expenditure or capital expenditure under any other programme
within that head by order of the Chief  Secretary to the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or any
other officer authorized by him.

I do hereby grant Permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
spend a sum of Rupees Thirty One Billion One Hundred Million (Rs. 31,100,000,000/-) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council  fund, which is estimated for the expenses of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the period that  start on 01st of
January, 2019 and end  on 31st  of  December 2019, by viture of powers  vested in me under Section  27 (a) of the Provincial
Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial  Council  of  the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka ended  on 26th September, 2017.
This permission will be valid till 31st of  December, 2019 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of summoning
the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-starts  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
which occurs first.
Further, the  expenditure incurred on the revenue and expenditure estimates which I gave permission on 30th November ,
2019 to disburse the expenses of the first four months of the year 2019, should be covered from the allocations given by  this
Permission.
D
HAMMA DISSANAYAKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
