URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY   LAW  No.  41  OF  1978
Order  under Section  3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Patali
Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Magapolis & Western Development being a opinion that the areas specified in Column
I excluding the already declared areas mentioned under Column I of Schedule II hereto and having the metes and bounds
specified in corresponding entry in Column II of Schedule II hereto are suitable for Development and  do hereby this Order
declare such areas for the purpose of the said Section.
I do hereby declare that, the already declared areas set out in Schedule I together with the other areas declared as
aforesaid, shall be included within the limits of the expanded Rideegama  Urban Development Area set out in Column II under
Schedule II hereto.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M. P.),
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development,
17th & 18th Floors, “Suhurupaya”,
Sri Subhuthipura Road,
Battaramulla,
21st April, 2019.
