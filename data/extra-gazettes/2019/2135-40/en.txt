PROVINCIAL COUNCIL NOTIFICATIONS
PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF CENTRAL  PROVINCE
I, Maithree Gunaratne, the Governor of Central Province, hereby make following regulations by virtue  of the powers vested in me by
Section 184 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, which should be read with Section 2 (1) (a)  of the Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I hereby impose regulations in respect of following allowances for Members of
Pradeshiya Sabha. These regulations shall be effective in respect of fuel and telephone allowances f rom 08th of May, 2019.
MAITHREE GUNARATNe,
Governor,
Central Province.
At the Governor’s Office.
On this day of 08th of May, 2019.
REGULATIONS
01. These regulations may be cited as Regulations for payment of Fuel and Telephone Allowances for Membe rs of Pradeshiya
Sabhas.
02. These allowances shall be in addition to the regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1914/40 of 15.05.2015 of
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Additionally, the other conditions imposed on furel allow ance and telephone allow-
ance under L. G . C. circular 01/2013 dated 05.04.2013 issued by me and Extraordinary Gazette No. 1900/08 dated 02.02.2015 will
remain effective. Accordingly members of  Pradeshiya Sabhas can be paid a fuel allowance and a telep hone allowance as shown
below from the Pradeshiya Sabha Fund of the respective Pradeshiya Sabha according to the given class ification of Pradeshiya
Sabhas.
