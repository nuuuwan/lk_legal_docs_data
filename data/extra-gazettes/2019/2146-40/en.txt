Paragraphs issued by the Election Commission in addition to the Code of Conduct published in the Gazette
No. 2142/47 of 25.09.2019
1. Photographs should not be taken inside the polling stations or postal voting centres and these stations should
not be  videoed. If such photographs or videos have been taken by somebody else, they should not be induced to
publish or distribute such photographs or videos.
2. On the part of the public officers who do not have political rights and members of the Armed Forces and police
officers in active service, it is an offence to publish their photographs or declarations through electronic, print
or any other media, in order to promote or prejudice a political party or a candidate.
3. Government living quarters, allocated to politicians should not be used as election offices to promote any
political party or a candidate. Such official quarters should not be used for any corrupt or illegal practice,
contravening the election laws or directions published in the Gazette by the Election Commission.
