AMENDMENT No. 03
Service Minute of the Public Management Assistants Service
THE  Public Service Commission has ordered to amend the Service Minute of the Public Management Assistants Service
Published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1840/34 dated 11.12.2013
as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2032/26 dated 16.08.2017 and No. 2052/26 dated 04.01.2018 as follows :
1. The title of the Service Minute referred to as the “Minute of the Public Management Assistants’ Service”
will be substituted by the following title.
“Service Minute of the Management Service Officers Service”
2. Every term referred to as “Public Management Assistants’ Service” and every term referred to as “Public
Management Assistant” in the relevant Service Minute shall be amended as “Management Service Officers
Service” and “Management Service Officers” respectively and the term referred to as “Officer” in the said
Service Minute shall be reckoned as Management Service Officers.
K. V . P. M. J. GAMAGE,
Director General of Combined Services.
