Order pertaining to the delegation of powers of the Western Provincial Public Service under the Section
32(2a) of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 as amended by the Provincial Councils (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1990
IT is hereby announced that the without prejudice to the actions taken under the Schedule number 01 of order dated
26.06.2018 published on the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2082/7 dated 31st July 2018 regarding the course of implementing
the powers delegated with relevance to the appointment, transfers, promotion, dismissal and disciplinary control of the
officers of the Western Provincial Public Service subject to the provisions in clause 32(2a)  of the Provincial Councils Act,
No. 42 of 1987 as (Amended) Act, No. 28 of 1990, the said Order is amended by removing Schedule Number  01 and
substituting it by the Schedule mentioned below as Number 01 to be effective from 16.05.2019.
S
UNIL ABEWARDHANA,
 Chairman,
Provincial Public Service Commission,
Western Province.
Western Provincial Public Service Commission,
No. 109, Main Street,
Battaramulla,
On this 16th day of May 2019.
