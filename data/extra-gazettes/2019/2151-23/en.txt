PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT,  No. 15  OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 18
of the Interpretation Ordinance (Chapter 2), I, Wajira Abeywardana, Minister of Internal & Home Affa irs and Provincial
Councils & Local Government do by this order, further Amend the Order, published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2043/56 of 02nd November 2017 amended by the order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2068/45 of 27th April
2018 relating to the Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha by adding of the following paragraph immediately after paragraph 4 thereof;
"(5) The term of Office of the Members of this Pradeshiya Sabha shall Commence with effect from 05.11.2019."
WAJIRA ABEYWARDANA,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and
Provincial Councils & Local Government.
Colombo,
05th November, 2019.
