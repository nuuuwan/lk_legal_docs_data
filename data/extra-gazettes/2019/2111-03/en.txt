THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER SECTION 39A(1)
The allotment of land specified in the Order No. 308 of 1985 made
under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act
(Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.  371/
12 dated 15.10.1985 is vested absolutely in the State and the
possession of which has been taken for or on behalf of the State
under Para (a) of Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by the virtue of
powers vested in me by Sub section (1) of Section 39 (a) (inserted by
Act, No. 8 of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act. I, Gayantha
Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms do
hereby divest only the allotment described in the Schedule I below.
