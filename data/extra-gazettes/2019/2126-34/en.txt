Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the land Acquisition Act, as amended by land  
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands/
servitude which is required for a public purpose.. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Situated in the village Palawela in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of Batakada, in Nawadun Korale of the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Elapatha, in Ratnapura 
District, Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
Plan No. F. V. P. 60, Supplement No. 15 
 
Lot No. 123 
 
Name of the land: Diyakadagawa, Aluthwaththa; 
Claimed by: The Government acquired under  Provision 
38 (a) of the land Acquisition Act; The Original Claimer 
is Asanka Weerasuriya of Uda Niriella, Palawela; Extent: 
0.0075 Hectare. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Elapatha at 10.00 a. m. on the 17th day of July 2019. 
 
 
  Further, they should notify me in writting and in 
duplicate before the 03rd of July 2019, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the  amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. Documents should be 
submitted to me on the same date to prove the interests.
