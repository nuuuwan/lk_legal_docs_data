The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Nishshanka Dhanushka Rathnajeewa Pushpa
Kumara of the Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of  member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased
to be a member of the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
GAMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
25th November, 2019.
