FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   MAYOR  OF   THE   BANDARAWELA  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262) AS  AMENDED   BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Mayor in Bandarawela Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group No. 1 to  which Naleen Priyantha Suriyage the former
member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 B (2)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member of
that Bandarawela Municipal Council  who belongs to such Independent Group and who shall be declared elected  as
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the  Indepdendent Group has communicated his decision to nominate Janaka
Nishantha Rathnayaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  Bandarawela Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare Janaka Nishantha Rathnayaka elected as  Mayor of the
Bandarawela Municipal Council.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Bandarawela Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Badulla,
09th January  2019.
