LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 Ref. No.: MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/44-3.
Land Ministry's Ref. No .: 4-3/9/2017/WS/11.
IT  is hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land acquisition
amendment act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government
intended to acquire the following land or servitude which is
required for public purpose:
Below mentioned land depicted as lot No. 01 in plan no.
PP/MR/2774 situated in the Village of Katuwangoda, in
Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division, in Matara
District in Southern province was acquired for the
construction of Uninduwela water purifier, storage that,
Administration building, Factory and office quarters under
Matara 4th step of water supply scheme.
Sub  schedule.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Katuwalage watta and
Punchilamahamilage Koratuwa; Description of the land:
Jungle with scrub; Initial claimant of the land: N. B.
Palihawadana and Ramasiri Palihawadana; Extent: 1.4164
Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a land / servitude is to be acquired are hereby to appear,
personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat Malimbada on the 27th day of
June 2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day of
June the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude,  the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
D
EEPIKA K. GUNARATNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Malimbada.
