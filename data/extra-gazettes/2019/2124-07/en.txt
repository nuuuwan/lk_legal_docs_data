LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub Section (1) of section 7 of the
land acquisition Act, as amended by land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
All that divided and defined allotment of land marked Lot
No. 3515 depicted in plan No. Padiyathalawa/Suppliment 53
of TOPO PP 49 made by the surveyor General, of the land
called Mahabeddawatta situated in the village of
Padiyathalawa in the minor Division of Binthenna pattu
southin the Divisional secretary's Division of Padiyathalawa,
in Ampara District, Eastern which is bounded on the North
by Lot No. 3495, 242 and 243 on the East by Lot No. 242 and
243 on the South by Lot No. 242, on the West by Lot No. 242
and 3495 containing in extent of One Decimal Zero one two
zero Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude to
be acquired are hereby required to appear personally or by
agent duly authorized in writing, before me at 10.30 a.m. on
Divisional Secretariat Padiyathalawa, 13th day of June 2019
and are hereby further requested to notify to me in writing in
duplicate on or before the 13th day of June 2019 the nature of
their interests in the land, the Particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude,
the Amount of compensation and the details of the
compensation of such amount.
R. M. N. WIJAYATHUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Padiyathalawa.
At Divisional Secretariat, Padiyathalawa,
