THE   INLAND   REVENUE   ACT,  No.  24  OF  2017
Notice under Section 1(a) (ii) of the Fifth Schedule

Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do hereby declare that the “Cancer Care Association - Sri Lanka”
organization referred to in the Schedule given below be an approved charity for the purpose of that Section.
MANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,

27th September, 2019.
