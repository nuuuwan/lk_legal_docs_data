THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
Pre School Service Trade
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) read with Section 33(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Cha pter 136), that the
determination relating to the minimum rate of wages in respect of teachers and assistants engaged in the Pre School Service
Trade specified in the Schedule here to made by the Commissioner of Labour under Section 33(2) of the aforesaid ordinance,
in pursuance of the authority conferred on him by an order made by Hon . Minister of Labour and Trad e Union Relations
under Section 33(1)(a) of the Ordinance and Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1120/3 of 21st February 2000 has
been approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
The determination shall come into force on the 01st of September, 2019.
R. P. R. RAJAPAKSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations,

PART 1: SEC. (1) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA  -  26.08.2019
SCHEDULE
The decisions on minimum wages made by the Wages Board for Beedi Manufacturing and set out in the Schedule to the
Notification Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 11208 of  29th November 1957 and varied from time to time were last
published by the notification in the  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/19 of  18th December, 2012. The decisions on minimum
wages relevant to part II made by Commissioner of Labour after conferring on him the powers by the Gazette Notification
No. 1916/13 of 27th May, 2015 to determine the minimum wages of this trade shall be notified as follows.
Part II
The minimum rates of wages for piece work
The minimum rates of the wages for piece work for Beedi Rolling (Inclusive of the preparation of wrappers for rolling,
cutting, wrapping, leaves, filling wrappers with "Beedi Tobacco", Beedi Rolling and Tying rolled Beedies) shall be:
(a) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long Rs. 400.00
(b) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long but less than 3 inches Rs. 450.00
(e) For 1,000 beedies not less than 3 inches long Rs. 490.00
The minimum rates of wages for piece work for beedi Rolling (inclusive of filling wrappers with "Beedi Tobacco" Beedi
Rolling and tying rolled Beedies but exclusive of the preparation of wrappers for rolling and cutting wrapping, leaves) shall
be:
(a) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long Rs. 300.00
(b) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long but less than 3 inches Rs. 340.00
(e) For 1,000 beedies not less than 3 inches long Rs. 375.00
The minimum rates of wages for piece work for cutting, wrapping, leaves (inclusive of the preparatio n of wrappers for
rolling) shall be:
(a) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long Rs. 200.00
(b) For 1,000 beedies not more than 2 inches long but less than 3 inches Rs. 220.00
(e) For 1,000 beedies not less than 3 inches long Rs. 230.00
The minimum rates of the wages for piece work for fixing ring labels round rolled Beedies shall be Rs. 50.00 per 1,000
Beedies.
