THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
TEA  GROWING  AND  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members
the Wages Board for the Tea Growing And Manufacturing Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of
Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, This Appointment has been valid up to
01st January, 2021.
