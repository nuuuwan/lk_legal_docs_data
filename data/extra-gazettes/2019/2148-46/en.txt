EXTRAORDINARY
THE NATIONAL THOROUGHFARES ACT, No. 40 OF 2008
Order under Section 11
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir Hashim, 
Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum Resources Development, do by this Order designate the Nation-
al Highway which was designated as an "Expressway" by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2148/ 43 of 
08.11.2019 and speciﬁ ed in the Schedule to this Order to be an "User Fee National Highway".
 The Order made under section 11 of the aforesaid Act designating "Kottawa - Kadawatha Section of Outer Circular 
National Highway (from 0.000 km to 19.245 km)" as an User Fee National Highway, and published in the Gazette Extraor-
dinary No. 1922/26 of July 08, 2015 is hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything done thereunder.   
  
 K
ABEER HASHIM,
 Minister of Highways & Road Development and    
 Petroleum Resources Development 
Battaramulla,
18th November, 2019        
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHWAY
 The stretch of road being 28.867 kilometers long and 40.000 - 75.000 metres broad, situated between the Kottawa 
interchange (0.000km) and Kerawalapitiya interchange (28.867km) of the Expressway, designated as the Outer Circular 
National Highway.
