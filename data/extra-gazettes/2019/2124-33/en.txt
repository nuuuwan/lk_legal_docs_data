THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Kadu /Land /01 /134 /CEB.
Land Ministry  Reference No: 4-3/2/2016/CEB/23.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Ammendment) Act No.28 of 1964, that the
Government has intends to acquire the undermentioned land/
Servitude  for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
Two allotments of  Lands as depicted in the names and extents
in the Preliminary Plan No. fld 9811 prepared by the
Surveyor General, and situated in the village of Malabe in
the Grama Niladhari Division  No. 476/A, Malabe West in the
Division of the Divisional Secretary of Kaduwela in Colombo
District of the Western Province in extent of 0.4199 hectares
as describe  in below.
Preliminary Plan No. fld 9811 - Lot description
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Ataheraliyagahakumbura; Description of
Land: Paddy field; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:
Mrs. H. Lilawathi Silva and others, No. 148/4, '' Sethsiri",
Thalahena, Malabe.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Ataheraliyagahakumbura; Description of
Land: Paddy field ( Fixed in two high voltage towers); Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Mr. S.L. Suriyaarachchi,
Mrs. A.A.R.Swarna Perera, Mrs. A.A.R. Indra Kanthi, ''
Ramya'', Pore, Athurugiriya.
All persons interested, possessed the right or with any
other relation in the aforesaid lands/servitude are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Kaduwela Divisional Secretariat, on
the day of the 26th June 2019   at 10.00 a.m. Further, they  are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing with two copies
on or before  19.06.2019 the nature of their interests in the
land /servitude,  the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
                                                               A.D.Y.ANANDANI,
                                                                Divisional Secretary,
                                                               Kaduwela.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Kaduwela.
