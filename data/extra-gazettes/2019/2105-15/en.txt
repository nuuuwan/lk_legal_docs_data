THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref.No.:KDS/AKU/4/2/1/65.
Land and Parliament Reforms Ministry's Ref.No.: 4/3/4/
2016/LO/327.
I hereby give notice,  under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of
the land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) as amended by the
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964,  that the
Government intends to acquire the following Land for a public
purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
The Lots 01 and 02 of the land called Ambalampitiyawatte
& Hapugodadeniya  Kumbura in total extent of Hectare 0.0592
depicted in the preliminary plan No. Maha/6868 and dated on
06th December 2018, prepared by the Surveyor General
situated at Alwathugoda Village of Alawathugoda Grama
Niladhari Division at Udagampaha Korale Minor division in
the Akurana Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy District
in the Central Province.
01. Lot No. 1 - Name of  the land: Ambalampitiyawatte,
Hapugodadeniya Kumbura; Extent: 0.0084 Hectare.
North:  Lot No. 05 of B.P .A 2222 & Ambalampitiyawatte
Hapugodadeniya Kumbura claimed by M.O.A.Kareem and
Others; East: Lot No. 2; South: Lot No. 2 and Lot No. 01 of
B.P . Maha-1527; W est: Lot No. 01 and B.P .Maha - 1527 and Lot
No. 05 of B.P .A 2222.

Maha -1527. Owner of the land: Not Known.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are  required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing
at Divisional Secretary's Office, Akurana, on the 01.02.2019 at
10.00 am, and hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the day of 21.02.2019 the nature of
their interests in the land and the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the aforesaid land the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
