THE   HOLIDAY  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
Order  under  Section 11(1)b
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11(1)b of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abewardana,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government do, by this Order declare that the days
specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an additional Bank Holidays for the purpose of the Act, in addition to the days
for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to that Act.
