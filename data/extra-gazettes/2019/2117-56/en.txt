I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of Land called Uvarinilam in total extent 
of about 2.7572 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 and 2 in 
Plan No. MN 810 and prepared by Surveyor General 
situated in the village called  Uvarai, in the Grama Nildari 
Division No. 60 of Olaithoduvai in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Mannar Town of Mannar District 
in Northern Province. 
 
Plan No. MN 810 
 
Lot No. 1 &  2 
Name of the Land: Uvarinilam; Present use of the 
land: Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: 
Seylan Business (PVT) Ltd, of No. 10/18 Lake Drive, 
Colombo 08, Not known; Extent:  2.7572 Hectares. 
   
The dates and the time of the inquiries: 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 a. m. on the 09th day of May 
2019 at Mannar Town Divisional Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd day of May 
2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars 
of their claims for the compensation for the acquisition of 
the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and 
the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
  
 K. SIVASAMPU, 
                                           Divisional  Secretary,  
                                         Mannar Town Division 
 Mannar.
