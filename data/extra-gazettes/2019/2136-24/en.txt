SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Granting  Permission  by  Virtue  of  Powers  Vested  in  me  Under  Section  27(A)  of  Provincial
Council  Act, No. 42 of 1987 as the  Term  of  the  Sixth  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial Council of the
Democratic  Socialist  Republic  of  Sri Lanka  Ended on 26 th  September, 2017
2019
1. This permission may be cited as ‘‘Permission to disburse under first financial statement - 2019 of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council’’ and come in to operation from 19.07.2019.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty One  Billion One Hundred
Million only, (Rs. 31,100,000,000.00) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2019 and ending 31st
December, 2019 is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Four Billion twenty Three Million Four Hundred and Six Thousand
one Hundred and Ninety  Seven and Cents Twenty  Four only (Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) and grant permission to Chief
Secretary  of this province and other Accounting officers  to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
Fund.
3. Accordingly the first schedule of the said gazette notification is amended as the first schedule hereof and grant permission
to disburse the same.
4. This permission will be valid till 31st of  December, 2019 or upto the date that expires three months (03) from the date of
summoning  the Seventh (07th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council which occurs first.

Authorization
I do hereby grant Permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
disburse as per the first supplementary financial statement for the year 2019, by viture of powers  vested in me under Section
27 (a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial  Council  of  the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended  on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty One Billion  One Hundred  Million
only (Rs. 31,100,000,000.00) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2018 and ending 31st December  2018
which is specified in annual financial statement - 2019 is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Four Billion Twenty Three Million,
Four Hundred and six Thousand one Hundred and Ninety Seven and cents Twenty Four only (Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) and grant
permission to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st of  December, 2019 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of summoning
the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
which occurs first.
D
HAMMA DISSANAYAKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
