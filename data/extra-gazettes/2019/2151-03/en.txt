CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES  ACT
Conferment of Powers - Order
IN pursuance of the powers vested in me under the Section 2(2) of the Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read with
Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992,
I Rishad Bathiudeen, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Resettlement of Protracted Displaced Persons, Co-operative
Development and V ocational Training and Skills Development do hereby confer on the persons appointed to assist the
Commissioner of Co-operative Development and the Registrar of Co-operative Societies and specified in the Column I of the
Schedule hereto, from the date specified in Column II under Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read with Co-operative
Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and under the
rules made there under, the powers specified in coloum III of the said schedule.
R
ISHAD BA THIUDEEN,
Minister of Industry and Commerce,
Resettlement of Protracted Displaced
Persons, Co-operative Development and V ocational
Training and Skills Development.
13th November, 2019.
Colombo.
