My No.: KEG/LAN/AQUIS/A3/1/110.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/322.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the land of Undugoda, Yatideriyawaththa described
in the Schedule given below for re-settlement the people who
were displaced due to the earth slides occurred in 2016 in the
Divisional Secretariat Division of Kegalle.
Description of the Lands to be acquired:
Five (05) allotments of land attached below depicted in
Preliminary Plan No. 3699 prepared by Surveyor General,
Kegalle situated in the Yatideriya village of 56B Undugoda
Grama Niladhari Division in Kegalle Divisional Secretary's
Division in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuwa Province.
Dates of inquiries for compensation-
Lot No. 01 – 05 of  P .P .K. 3699. On the date of 5th December
2019  from 9.30 a.m.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be required
are hereby to appear personally or by agents duly authorized
in written before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kegalle on the
above mentioned date and on the scheduled time.
And are hereby requested to notify me in writing with
duplicate of on or before 22.11.2019 the nature of their interest
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
