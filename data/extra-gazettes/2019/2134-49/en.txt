The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   POONAKARY   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Selvarasa
Niroshan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Krishnasamy Thivakaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer  of  the  Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Krishnasamy Thivakaran was selected as the member of the Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
31st July, 2019.
