FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   ANURADHAPURA
MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in  Anuradhapura Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Ananda Dahanayaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  the member of that 11- Samanpura of Anuradhapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid Recognized  Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Henda Weetharanage Indika Upul Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer  of  the  Anuradhapura Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that  Henda Weetharanage Indika Upul Kumara  was elected as the member of the 11- Samanpura of  Anuradhapura
Municipal Council.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Anuradhapura Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
15th July, 2019.
