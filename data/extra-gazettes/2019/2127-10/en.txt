The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAHIYANGANA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Mahiyangana pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group to which  Wanninayaka Mudiyanselage Saman
Pushpakumara Wanninayaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of  that Ward No. 12, Dambarawa of Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Aththanayaka Mudiyanselage Sumanarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, H. I. R. Hathurusinghe, Returning Officer of the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aththanayaka
Mudiyanselage Sumanarathna  was elected as the member of the Ward No. 12 Dambarawa of Mahiyangana  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Mahiyangana pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Badulla,
10th June, 2019.
