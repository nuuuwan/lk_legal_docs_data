Proclamations  & c.,  by  the  Governors
NOTIFICATION  MADE  UNDER  CLAUSE  154 E  OF  THE  13TH  AMENDMENT  TO  THE
CONSTITUTION  OF  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
I, Mohamed Azath Sanoon Salley, the Governor of Western Province do hereby announce that the Western Province
Provincial Council will be considered as come to dissolution from the midnight of 21st April 2019 according to the provisions
stated in Clause 154 E of the 13
th Amendment to the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
MOHAMED AZATH SANOON SALLEY,
Governor of Western Province.
At the Office of the Governor of Western Province,
10th Floor, No. 628, Janajaya Building,
Nawala Road,
Rajagiriya,
On 21st April 2019.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING,  SRI LANKA.
