THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of lands depicted in the  Advance Tracing No. KU/
MLL/2018/586 dated 25.02.2019 prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the village called Mehiella in 713, Mehiella Grama Niladhari
Division, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of  Mallawapitiya in
Kurunegala District in North Western Province and boundaries as
follows:
1. Lot No. 1 – Dangollawatta – Extent: 0.7869 Hectare
North: Pradeshiya Saba Road, Dangollawatta claimed by M. F.
Kaleel and Lot No. 37 in Advance Tracing No. KU/MLL/2018/130;
East: Lot Nos. 36 and 35 in Advance Tracing No. KU/MLL/2018/
130 and Dangollawatta cliamed by M. F. Kaleel; South:
Dangollawatta cliamed by M. F. Kaleel and Cannel; West: Lot Nos.
2, 3, Pradeshiya Saba Road and Dangollawatta claimed by M. F. Kaleel.
2. Lot No. 2 – Extent: 0.0020 Hectare
North: Lot No. 37; East: Lot No. 1 and Dangollawatta claimed by
M. F . Kaleel; South: Cannel and Pradeshiya Saba Road; West: Lot
No. 3.
3. Lot No. 3 – Extent: 0.0135 Hectare
North: Belikotuwa claimed by M. D. Chandrasena, Pradeshiya
Saba Road and Dangollawatta claimed by M. F. Kaleel; East: Lot

Kohombagahamula claimed by R. D. Disanayake; West:
Kohombagahamula claimed by R. D. Disanayake and Belikotuwa
claimed by M. D. Chandrasena.
