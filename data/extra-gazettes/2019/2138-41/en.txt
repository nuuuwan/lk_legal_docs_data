THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: 6/3/7/15/5/7.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/MV/110H.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Public purpose means: Land Acquisition for
Moragahakanda and Kaluganga Development Project.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
The allotment of land shown in Surveyor General's Plan
No. Topopp 17 Supplement No. 36, Sheet No. 94, situated at
Thalagoda Village in Thalagoda Grama Niladhari Division
Gangala Pallesiya Pattuwa of Naula Divisional Secretary's
Division, Matale District, Central province,  and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot No. 3770
Name of Land: Kumbukgolla; Description: Light forest;
Claimant: Mr. Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Weerakoon
Banda (Deceased) of Thalagoda, Kumbukgolla Y aya; Extent:
0.3604 Hectare.
All person interested in the aforesaid Land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Naula Divisional Secretariat on the 25th September, 2019 at
9.30 a.m.
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the day of 18th September, 2019 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amoun of compensation and the details of the computaion
of such amount.
ATHMA DILUKSHI JAYARATHNE,
Divisional Secretary,
Naula.
Divisional Secretariat, Naula,
28th August, 2019.
