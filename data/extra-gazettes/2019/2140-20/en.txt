SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance  of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of the  list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rate  specified
in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from September11th, 2019, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2130/20 of 03.07.2019 in respect of Black gram (HS Codes 0713.31.29) is
rescinded with effect from September 11, 2019.
MANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
September 10,  2019.1A-  PG   4644 — 567   (09/2019)
