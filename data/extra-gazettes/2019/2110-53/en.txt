DEPARTMENT OF POSTS
Post office Ordinance
ORDERS  ISSUED  BY  THE  MINISTER OF  POSTAL SERVICES AND  MUSLIM RELIGIOUS  AFFAIRS  UNDER  THE
SECTION 8 (1) (D) OF  THE POST  OFFICE ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 190)
INCREASING of stamps value in relation to the free postal facilities given for Hon.  Members of Parliament and increasing of
free postal facilities provided for  Hon. Members of Provincial Councils were approved by the Cabinet paper No. 18/ 0017/
743/001 at the Cabinet Meeting held on 16.01.2018 Accordingly.
1. It  was amended the Limit of Rs 175,000/= given for one year the free postal facilities of the Hon. Members
of  Parliament up to Rs. 350,000/=
2. It was amended the limit of Rs. 24,000/= given for one year the free postal facilities of the  Hon. Members
of Provincial Council up to Rs. 48,000/=
It is hereby published that these orders will be effective from 16.01.2018.
M. H. A. HALEEM,
Minister of  Postal Services and Muslim Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
09th  January, 2019.
