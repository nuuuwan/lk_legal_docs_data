THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Mini's. Reference  No.:4-3/15/2013/VE/32.
Sec.Re.No.: 9/3/5/19.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964(Amendment), that
the Government intends to acquire the following land which
is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land  to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Yatawaththa Waththa in total
extent of 1.0136 hectare depicted in the Preliminary Plan No.
MA 1712 situated in the village called Yatawaththa Waththa
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. of  Maligathenna in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Yatawaththa in Mathale
District in the  Central Province and morefully described as
follows.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the land: Yatawaththa Waththa; Description:
Destribution under  village expansion among the people who
    G 29770 - 17 (2019/04)
are residing at present; Claimant: Udupussellawa Plantation
Company , Y atawaththa Waththa, Yatawaththa; Extent: 1.0136
Hectare.
   All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Yatawaththa  Divisional Secretariat
on the 19th day of June 2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 03rd day of  June, 2019 .The nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said lands the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.All  deeds
and documents in support of the claims should be produced
before me at the inquiry.
D. R. K. HENNAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Yatawaththa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Yatawaththa,
