I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose (the conservation of Knukles 
range).  
 
Description of the land/servitude  to be acquired: 
 
An allotment  of Land called ‘Winchfield Park watta” 
bearing an extent of about 5.6772 Hectares mentioned in 
P. P.  Maha 6866, supplement of the Surveyor General, 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Karambakatiya, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province. 
Name and address of Claimants: 
 
 
A. V. B.  Abeygunasekara - 3/B/3, Ihalawela, Kengalle. 
A. W. M. M.  Abeygunasekara - 211/A,  Akaravita, 
                                                 Gampaha 
A.V. S. Abeygunasekara - 21, Parakrama Samuddraya,                
                                             Polonnaruwa 
A. V. H. Abeygunasekara - 169/6/A, Gamamada para, 
                                           Raddoluwa, Seeduwa 
A. W. W. L. Abeygunasekara - 10G, Wagolla Road,                                                                                 
                                                                            Lewalla    
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
on which is to be acquired are herby required to appear 
Personally or by agents duty Outhorised in writing, 
before me at the  Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara on 
the 15th May 2019 at 11.00 a. m. And are hereby 
requested to  notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 08th May 2019   nature of their interests in the 
land,  the particulars of their claims of  compensation, for 
the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
