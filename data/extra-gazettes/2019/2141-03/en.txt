PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 28.03.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude described below in Paragraph
1 is required for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act. The land over which the said servitude is to
be acquired is given in Paragraph 2.
1. Description of the Servitude.– Servitude to be obtained under
the Asia Development Bank 5 project, acquiring a block of land for
the pipe line for waste water in the Bangadeniya water storage tank.
2. Description of the Land.– Two (02) allotments of land called
Kumbutukuliya depicted as Lots ‘A ’ and ‘B’ in the Advance Tracing
No. PU/ARC/2017/161 dated 31.05.2017, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys in charge of the Division of Puttlam and
situated in the Village of Kumbutukuliya in the Minor Division of
Munnessaram in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Arachchikattuwa, Puttlam District of North Western Province and
containing to total extent of 0.0120 Hectare and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kumbutukuliya  Watta – Extent:
0.0042 Hectare
North: Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by M. M. G . Hayasintha
Costa and Kumbutukuliya Road; East: Kumbutukuliya Watta
claimed by M. M. G . Hayasintha Costa and Lot B; South: Lot B and
Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by Chandrasiri Mayakaduwa; West:
Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by Chandrasiri Mayakaduwa and
Kumbutukuliya Watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kumbutukuliya  Watta – Extent:
0.0078 Hectare
North: Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by Chandrasiri
Mayakaduwa, Lot A and Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by
M. M. S. Hayasintha Costa; East: Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed by
J. A. D. Shelton Appuhami; South: Kumbutukuliya Watta claimed
by J. A. D. Shelton Appuhami and Road; West: Road, Kumbutukuliya
Watta claimed by J. A. D. Shelton Appuhami and Kumbutukuliya
Watta claimed by Chandrasiri Mayakaduwa.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
