THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Kadu/Land/03/146/UDA.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No.28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land/servitude to be Acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land in extent of 0.3221 Hectares
described below and depicted as Lot No.1 and 2 in Preliminary
Plan Co.9830 dated 03.10.2019 prepared by Surveyor General
situated in the village of Battaramulla (within  the Municipal
Council limits of Kaduwela) in 492, Subuthipura Grama
Niladhari Division, Divisional Secretariat Division of
Kaduwela, Colombo District, Western Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Godellawatta alias Millagahawatta and
Kottanuwe Kumbura; Description: Open waste land;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Urban Development
Authority;Extent: 0.0060 Hec.
Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Godellawatta alias Millagahawatta and
Kottanuwe Kumbura; Description: Open waste land;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: N.S. Senewirathne, St.Dora
Private Hospital, Kottawa, Battaramulla;Extent: 0.3161 Hec.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat,
Kaduwela on 27th November, 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 20th November, 2019 the nature of their interests
in the land/servitude, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount. All deeds and other documents
in suport of the claims should be produced at the inquiry.
                                                             A.D.Y.ANANDANI,
                                                                Divisional Secretary,
                                                               Kaduwela.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Kaduwela,
08th November, 2019.
