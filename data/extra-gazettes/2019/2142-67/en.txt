THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES BOARD  FOR THE LIQUOR AND VINEGAR TRADE
It is hereby notified under section 29(3) of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136), that the decisions of  the  Wages Board
for the Liquor and Vinegar Trade made Section 30 of that Ordinance  and specified in the Schedule here to have been
approved by the Hon.Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
The decision shall came into force  on the 01st of October 2019.
R.P.R. Rajapaksa,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05.

3A
Whenever a worker is employed to tap toddy for the Arrack Trade or Vinegar Trade, the aforesaid rate should be paid to him
for tapping Coconut, Kithul a Palmyrah palms subject to the condition that the alcohol content of the volume of toddy
delivered by him should be not less than 7.5%. However if the alcohol content of the volume of toddy is less than 7.5% he
should be paid in proportion to the aforesaid rate.
