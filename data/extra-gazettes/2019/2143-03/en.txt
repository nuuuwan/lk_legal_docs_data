SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rates specified
in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from October 01, 2019 subject to provsions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
September 30, 2019.
