THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and the land will be acquired under provision that
Act.
An Allotment of land which Names, extent and boundaries are
mentioned below is depicted in Advance Tracing No. KE/ARN/
2017/325 and dated 29.12.2017, prepared by the Surveyor General,
and situated in the Village of Thalgamuwa, in the Grama Niladhari
Division of No. 33 Thalgamuwa in the Minor Division of Meda
Paththuwa, in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Aranayaka, in
the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Gammale watta, Egodakapuwellehena,
Gurubevla and Ambagatehena – Extent: 3.3986 Hectares
North: Gammale watta, Gurubevela, Ambagatehea and Kuda Oya;
East: Kuda Oya,  Gurubevla and Walkotuwa; South: Arakotawalla
Gama and Ambadeniya Gama; West: Lot B and Mala (dried) Stream
and Gammale watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Extent: 0.0216 Hectare
North: Lot C, Gammale watta and Mala (dried) Stream; East:
Gammale watta and Lot A; South: Lot A and Ambadeniya Gama;
West: Ambadeniya Gama and Lot C.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Gammale watta, Egodakapuwellehena,
Gurubevla and Ambagatehena – Extent: 0.0665 Hectare
North: Gammale watta and Mala (dried) Stream; East: Lot B;
South: Lot B and Ambadeniya Gama; West: Gammalewatta.
