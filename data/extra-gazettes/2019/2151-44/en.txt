PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
 SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.   48   OF   2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by the Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economy and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby make an order to waive off;
(i) the balance amount after collecting Rs. 25 per kg from the applied Special Commodity Levy of 10% or Rs. 100 per kg
on the importation of fish, classifiable under the HS Heading of 03.02 and 03.03 (Other than the Mackerel fish
coming under HS Code 0303.54), listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2143/3 of 30th September, 2019.
And,
(ii) Rs. 20 per kg from the applied Special Commodity Levey of Rs. 25per kg on the importation of Chillies (Dried, neither
crushed nor ground) classifiable under the HS Code of 0904.21.10 listed out in the Schedule of the Order published
in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2132/13 of 18th July, 2019.
In terms of this Order, a Special Commodity Levey of Rs. 25 per kg on importation of fish (HS Heading of 03.02 and 03.03, other
than the Mackerel fish coming under HS Code 0303.54)  and Rs 5 per kg on importation of Chillies (Dried  neither crushed nor
ground/HS Code 0904.21.10) shall be collected for a period of two months commencing from November, 29th 2019.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economy and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economy and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
28th November, 2019.
