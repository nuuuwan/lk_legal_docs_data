The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PITABEDDARA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Anil Chandranath Wijesiri Jayawardana Weeraman the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A
(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Gigummaduwa Kankanamge Ajith Wasantha Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Tananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the  Pitabeddara  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Gigummaduwa Kankanamge Ajith Wasantha Kumara was selected as the member of the Pitabeddara  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Pitabeddara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara District,
02nd September, 2019.
