THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
AS  AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose:
      Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Allotment of land called in extent 0.4079 of Hectare shown
as Lot 1 in P .P .Tri 560 dated 23.10.2018 prepared by the
Surveyor General Situated in within the Urban Council Limits
of Trincomalee No. 244P Orr's Hill Grama Niladhari Division ,
Divisional Secretariat Division of Town & Gravets, Trincomalee
District and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 1
North Lots: Lot 4 in PPA 893, Lots 2 and 1 in PP tri 276; East
Lots: Lot 2, 1 in PP Tri 276 and Lots 4 in PP A 839; South: Lot 4
in PP A 839; W est Lots: Lot 4 in PP A 839; Extent: 0.4079 Hectare.
      All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally , or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at
Divisional Secretariat Trincomalee on the 11th day of March
2019, at 10.00 a.m. and you are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th day of  February,
2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for  compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount. All deeds and documents in support of the
claims should be produced before me at the inquiry.
 J. S. ARULRAJ,
Divisional Secretary,
Town and Gravets,
  Trincomalee.
31st January , 2019.
