COMMUNITY  BASED  CORRECTIONS  ACT,  No.  46  OF  1999
Order under Subsection (3) of Section 1
I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, by virtue of powers vested in me by Subsection (3) of
Section 1 of the Community Based Corrections Act, No. 46 of 1999, do, by this Order, hereby order that the date appointed
in terms of the Provisions contained in Part II of the Act in respect of judicial division specified in the Schedule below
shall be 22.05.2017.
T
HALA THA ATUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
At Colombo,
23rd of July, 2019.
