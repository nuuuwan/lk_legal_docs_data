1A   G   29358—27  (01/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group No. 2  to which  Krishnapillei Kirubanandan the member
belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  returned  member if
Independent Group No. 2 who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Rasaiya Ganeshan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
that Rasaiya Ganeshan was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
