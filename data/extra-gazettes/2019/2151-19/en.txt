No. 2151/19 – TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 26, 2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GA/
GFG/2019/391 of 08.07.2019 prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Villages of Deddugoda South and Dangedara in the
Grama Niladhari Divisions of  Dangedara East, 97D and Deddugoda
South, 101A in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Galle - Four
Gravets in the District of Galle in Southern Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mahamoragodawatta – Extent: About 0.0015
Hectare
North: Mahamoragodawatta claimed by D. P . Y . Gamage; East
and South: Road;  West: Mahamoragodawatta claimed by D. P . Y .
Gamage.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Mahamoragodawatta – Extent: About 0.0014
Hectare
North: Road; East: Mahamoragodawatta claimed by B. P . Udaya
Kasun; South and West: Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Mahamoragodawatta – Extent: About 0.0013
Hectare
North: Road and the land acquired for Moragoda canal; East:
Wellabagala Eladdara Ovita claimed by H. M. Dharmasena; South:
Road and Lot D; West: Road.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Extent: About 0.0010 Hectare
North: Road and Lot C; East: Lot C, Wellabagala Eladdara Ovita

Kurunduwatta, Pashaul Ovita claimed by Sajith Dayarathna.
