PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 04.06.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of land called following name and extent, depicted
and described in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/KND/2018/144 and
dated 01.07.2018, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Lunuketiya Madiththa in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Lunuketiya Madiththa (709) in the Minor Division of Udugampaha
Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kundasale in Kandy
District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Lunuketiya Madiththa – Extent: About
0.0124 Hectare
North: Road and Lunuketiya Madiththa Hena, claimant is
unknown; East: Lunuketiya Madiththa Hena claimed by Malani
Ariyarathne; South: Lunuketiya Madiththa Hena claimed by Malani
Ariyarathne and Lunuketiya Madiththa Hena, claimant is unknown;
West: Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/528.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
