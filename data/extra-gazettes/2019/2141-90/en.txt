THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                               My Ref. No. :10/3/1/3/75.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2018/WS/48.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act,as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for the
construction of Rattota Water Purification Project of Greater
Matale Water Supply Project.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
The allotments of land described below , shown in
Preliminary Plan No. Ma 1789 of 30.07.2019, certified by Senior
Superintendent of Survey Matale, situated at Maussagolla
village in Maussagolla Grama Niladari Division, Matale
Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor Division of Rattota Divisional
Secretary's Division Matale District, Central Province.
G 31038 - 27 (2019/09)
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Asweddume watta; Description: Garden;
Claimant: 1. Mr.V . Palitha Gunarathna Dikkumbura, Rattota. 2.
D.G .P . Samaranayaka and others, No. 71, Maussagolla,
Rattota;  Extent: 0.6015 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be  acquire are hereby required to appear
personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Rattota Divisional Secretariat on the 30th of October 2019
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the  22nd October 2019 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
H.M.N. DINIPRIYA
                                                                 Divisional Secretary,
                                                                          Rattota.
Divisional Secretariat Rattota,
20th September 2019.
