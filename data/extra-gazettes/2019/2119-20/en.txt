NOTIFICATION  BY  MR.  SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
on Tuesday, 23rd April, 2019 at 1.00 p.m.
I, Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order 16 of the Standing Orders of
Parliament, I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Tuesday the 23rd day of April 2019 at 1.00 p.m. having been
requested so to do by the Hon. Prime Minister.
K
ARU  JAY ASURIYA,
Speaker.
Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte,
21st April, 2019.
