THE   INLAND   REVENUE  ACT,  NO.  24  OF  2017
Notice under Section 1(a)(ii) of the Fifth Schedule
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 1(a)(ii) of the Fifth Schedule of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 24 of 2017, I,
Mangala Samaraweera Minister of Finance, do hereby declare that the  “Centre for Handicapped” organization referred
to in the Schedule given below be an approved charity for the purpose of that section.
MANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
09th October, 2019.
