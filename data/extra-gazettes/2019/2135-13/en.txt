ADMINISTRATIVE  DISTRICTS  ACT,  No.  22  OF  1955
Notice of inclusion of Colombo Port City Land as a part of Colombo Administrative District under the
Section 3 of Administrative Districts Act, No. 22 of 1955
I, Wajira Abeywardena, the Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils and Local Government, do
hereby give notice to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that the area of authority and
the limits of the Administrative District of Colombo specified in the First Schedule to the Administrative District Act,
No. 22 of 1955 shall be altered by inclusion of the reclaimed area of land known as ‘Port City Colombo’ described in the
Schedule hereto, within the area of authority and the limits of the Administrative District of Colombo based on the
resolution passed in the Parliament on 23rd of July 2019.
This reclaimed area of land shall be annexed to Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo in the Administrative
District of Colombo.
W
AJIRA ABEYWARDENA,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and
Provincial Councils and Local Government.
02nd of August, 2019.
