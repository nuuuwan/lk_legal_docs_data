WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification made under Section 2(2) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
I, Isura Devapriya, the Chief Minister of Western Province and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and  Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Economic Promotions, Power and Energy, Environmental
Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism of Western Province do hereby notify that permission is granted under
and by virtue of powers vested in me by provision of Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 that
has to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, to impose and
recover the entertainment tax of 15% out of the entrance fee that is recovered for the entertainment activities as per terms
of expression in Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, that is amended by (Amendment) of Entertainment Tax
Act, No. 37 of 1984, however as per provisions of Section 2(1) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, the
resolution that has been passed in the meeting held on 19.07.2018 in the Pradeshiya Sabha of Homagama, to impose and
recover 10% of the entertainment tax, for the displays those are displayed in the cinema halls, which fall within the
administrative limit of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabhawa.
I
SURA DEV APRIY A,
 Chief Minister of Western Provincial Council,
 Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and  Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Economic Promotions, Power and Energy, Environmental Affairs,
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism of Western Province.
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla,
on this 18th day of April, 2019.

WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice published under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
I, Isura Devapriya, the Chief Minister of Western Province and the Provincial Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering
Services, Law and  Order, Local Government and Provincial Administrations, Economic Promotion, Electricity and Power,
Environmental Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs, hereby give notice that by virtue of the powers
vested in me under the provisions set out in Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12
of 1989 which should be  read with Section 2(2) of the  Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, approval is hereby
granted to the proposal passed by the Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha on 10.10.2018 in terms of the provisions laid down in
Section 2(1) of the Entertaiment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, to impose and charge 7.5% of the entrance fee to be
charged from entertainment activities set out in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 as amended by
Entertainment Tax (Amendment) Act  No. 37 of 1984.
I
SURA DEV APRIY A,
 Chief Minister of Western Province,
 Provincial Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and  Order, Local Government and Provincial Administrations,
Economic Promotion, Electricity and Power, Environmental Affairs,
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
At the Provincial Council Building,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla.
on this 18th day of April, 2019.

WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
This Notification is made under Section 2(2) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
IT is hereby notified that, I, Isura Devapriya, the Chief Minister of Western Province and the Minister of Finance and
Planning,  Law and  Order, Lands, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Trade, Power and Energy,
Man Power and Economic Promotion, by virtue of the power vested with me in terms of Section 2(2) of Entertainment
Tax Ordinance to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Councils  (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 have
approved the resolution which was passed on 11.12.2018 by the Negombo Municipal Council as per the powers vested in
terms of Section 2(1) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1989 to impose and levy a tax equivalent to 10% precent
of the payment made for admission to entertainments defined in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended
by the Entertainment Tax (Amendment) Ordiance No. 37 of 1984.
I
SURA DEV APRIY A,
 Honourable Chief Minister of Provincial Council of Western Province,
 Minister in charge of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and  Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Economic Promotion, Power and Energy, Environmental Affairs,
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
At the Provincial Council Building,
No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Battaramulla,
on this 18th day of April, 2019.
