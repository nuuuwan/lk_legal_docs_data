No. 2116/1  -  MONDAY  MARCH  25,  2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, P. S. M. Charles, Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 25th  March, 2019, all  duties of Customs as well
as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and received at
the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2115/1 of   18th  March,
2019 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification to be
published on or before 31st   March, 2019.
P. S. M. CHARLES,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
22nd  March, 2019.
