THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I , Gayantha Karunatilleke Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Following allotments of land depicted  in the Advance Tracing
No. MR/DKW/2015/517 dated 16.02.2016 made by Surveyor
General  and situated in the Village of Pathegama Central in Minor
Division of Wellabada Pattu in Divisional Secretary's Division  of
Dickwella, Matara District, Southern  Province.
01. Lot  ‘A’ -  Rubberwatta - Extent: 0.0340  Hectare
North: Land which has been acquired for Matara - Kataragama
Proposed railway line; East: Lot  B, Koongahakoratuwa alias
Paluwatta of which claimant cannot be found  and Rubberwatta
claimed by  Sugathapala Palihakkara; South: Rubberwatta  claimed
by  Sugathapala Palihakkara; West: Karandagoda claimed by Dalugama
Gamage Piyasena and Land which has been acquired  for Matara -
Katharagama  proposed Railway line.
02. Lot  ‘B’ -  Kongahakoratuwa alias Paluwatta - Extent:
0.0222  Hectare
North: Land which has been acquired for Matara - Kataragama
proposed railway line; East: Rubberwatta  claimed by  D. H.
Palihakkara and  Kongahakoratuwa alias Paluwatta of which  claimant
cannot be found; South: Koongahakoratuwa alias Paluwatta of which
claimant  cannot be found; West: Lot A and Land which has been
acquired for Matara - Kataragama proposed railway line.
