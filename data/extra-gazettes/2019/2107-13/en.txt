No. 2107/13 – TUESDAY, JANUARY 22, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted as Lot ‘A’ in total extent of about
2.0234 Hectares in the Advance Tracing No. R/KLW/ 2017/786 and
dated 15.11.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Kukulegama South (F.V .P. 250) in the Minor Division of
Meda Pattuwa of Kukulu Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kalawana in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Marawegulana (Rukmalwatta) – Extent: About
2.0234 Hectares
North: Watta Berawakanda Watta Kukulegama South Kalawana
and Road; East: Road and Balance portion of the same land;  South:
Balance portion of the same land; West: Balance portion of the same
land and Watta Berawakanda Watta Kukulegama South Kalawana.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/424.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
