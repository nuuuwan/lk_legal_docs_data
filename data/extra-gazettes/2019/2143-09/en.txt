I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby, in the exercise of powers
vested in me by Section 4 of the Commission Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) once again enlarge the period of time up to 31st
October 2019 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission established under
Section 2 of the said Commission of Inquiry Act on 14th January, 2019 by Notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2106/1 dated 16th January 2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate and
inquire into acts of corruption, fraud, criminal breach of trust, criminal misappropriation of property, cheating an abuse
or misuse of power or authority, state resources and privilages which offences and acts of wrong doing had allegedly
occurred during the period of 15th January 2015 to 31st December 2018 having enlarged the period of six months ending
on 14th July 2019 by two and a half months from 14th July 2019 up to 30th September 2019 by Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2132/4 dated 15th July 2019 in the exercise of the said powers vested in me.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
26th September, 2019.
