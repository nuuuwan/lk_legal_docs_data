EXTRAORDINARY
THE NATIONAL THOROUGHFARES ACT, No. 40 OF 2008
Order under Section 11
         
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir Hashim, 
Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum Resources Development, do by this Order designate the National 
Highway which was designated as an "Expressway" by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2148/ 41 of 
08.11.2019 and speciﬁ ed in the Schedule to this Order to be an "User Fee National Highway".
 K
ABIR HASHIM,
 Minister of Highways & Road Development and    
 Petroleum Resources Development. 
Battaramulla,
08th November, 2019.      
SCHEDULE
AREA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHWAY
 The stretch of road being 21.795 kilometers long and 80.000 - 90.000 metres broad, situated between the 
Barawakumbuka (178.656 km) and Mattala (200.451 km) of the Expressway, designated as the Barawakumbuka - Mattala 
National Highway (Section of Southern Expressway).
