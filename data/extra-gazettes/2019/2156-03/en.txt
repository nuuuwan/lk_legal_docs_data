THE  CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
I, hereby declare that out of the powers and functions vested in me in my capacity as the Minister of Finance, Economic
and Policy Development of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, powers and functions specified in the following
Schedule I are delegated to the State Minister of Economic and Policy Development, Hon. John Senevirathna and powers
and functions specified in the following Schedule II are delegated to the State Minister of Development Banks and Loans,
Hon. Shehan Semasinghe in terms of the provisions of the Article 44(5) of the constitution and the both State Ministers
have the opportunity of inquiring, mediating and coordinating all functions coming within the scope of work of the Ministry.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSE,
 Minister of Finance, Economic
and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
The Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
30th December 2019.
