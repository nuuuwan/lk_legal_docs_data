Order No. 54
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY  ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, distributor or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale or display for
sale the following varieties of rice above the Maximum Retail Prices given hereunder :-
V ariety Maximum Retail Price
White Samba - steamed/boiled - Local Rs. 85/- per kg
(Excluding Keeri Samba and Suduru Samba)
White Nadu - boiled/steamed - Local Rs. 80/- per kg
(Excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari)
Red Nadu - boiled/steamed - Local Rs. 74/- per kg
(Excluding Mottaikarupan and Attakari)
This Order shall come into effect from 31st May, 2019.
