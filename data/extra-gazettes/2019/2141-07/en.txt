DRUG  DEPENDANT  PERSONS  (TREATMENT  AND  REHABILITATION)   ACT,  No.  54  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Drug Dependant Persons (Treatment and Rehabilitation) Act,
No. 54 of 2007, read with the provisions of the Constitution, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, on the recommendation of the National Dangerous Drug Control Board, do by this Order, designate
the place specified in the Schedule hereto be a Treatment and Rehabilitation Centre for the purpose of providing care and
rehabilitation to drug dependant persons.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
13th August, 2019.
SCHEDULE
Treatment and Rehabilitation Centre
² Weerawila Rehabilitation Centre
