No. 2141/10  - TUESDAY  SEPTEMBER  17, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PANVILA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of  Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United  Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front at Present), the
recognized political party to which Suppaiya Uma Saraswathi the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United  Progressive Alliance (Democratic
People’s Front at Present), who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Soosai Vijayamala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the  Panvila
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Soosai Vijayamala was selected as the member of the Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
12th September, 2019.
