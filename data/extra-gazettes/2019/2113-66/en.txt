PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 08.03.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Co/DSO/2016/876 and dated 11.01.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Thalangama North (within the
Municipal Council limits of Kaduwela) in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 477-A in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kaduwela in Colombo District, Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘B’ – Koswatta Filling Station – Extent: 0.0279 Hectare
North: Assmt. No. not known land at Koswatta Thalangama Road
and Lot A; East: Lot A and Koswatta Junction; South: Koswatta
Junction and Private Road; West: Private Road and Assmt. No. not
known land at Koswatta Thalangama Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
