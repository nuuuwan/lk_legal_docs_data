Act, in respect of the S L T Campus (Pvt) Ltd., a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of
2007 and bearing Registration Number PV 101137, do by this Order, amend the Schedule II to the Order made under the
said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1995/39 of December 02, 2016 (as last amended by Order published in
Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2083/35 of August 10, 2018) by the addition immediately after  entry “Bachelor of Technology
Honours in Electronics” appearing in Column  I and its corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified
under Column I and II  respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule  I hereto:-
SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS

respect of the Bachelor of Science Honours in Engineering in Information and Communication Engineering, adhere to the
minimum entry qualifications of three (03) ‘S’ passes  in Combined Mathematics and Physics and in any other subject from
among the subjects of Chemistry, Information and Communication Technology and Higher Mathematics in one and the same
sitting at the GCE Advanced Level Examination or equivalent
