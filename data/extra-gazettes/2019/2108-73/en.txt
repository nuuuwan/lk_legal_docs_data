PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 02.02.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted as Lot ‘A’ in extent of
about 0.4117 Hectare in the Advance Tracing No. R/ELP/2018/166
dated 11.05.2018, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Hangamuwa (F.V .P. 84) in the Minor Division of Palle
Pattuwa in Navadun Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Elapatha in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rambukkanda Estate – Extent: 0.4117 Hectare
North: No. 182E Ethoya Division and Rambukkanda Estate
possessed by Balangoda Plantation Company; East and South:
Rambukkanda Estate possessed by Balangoda Plantation Company;
West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and No. 182E Ethoya Division.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/583.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
02nd October, 2018.
