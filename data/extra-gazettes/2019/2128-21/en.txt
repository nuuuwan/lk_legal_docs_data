THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration Under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of  Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land called as Walgamage Kumbura situated in
the village of Kongala (F.V .P. 187)  in the Kandabada Paththu East
minor Division within  the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Hakmana, in Matara District, Southern Province depicted in the
Advance Tracing No. MR/HKM/2017/10 and dated 11.08.2017
prepared by the Surveyar-General and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘‘A’’ –  Walgamage Kumbura  – Extent: 0.0071  Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.)
and Walgamage Kumbura land  claimed by S. M. Wakishta; West:
Walgamage Kumbura land  claimed by S. M. Wakishta and Road
(R.D.A.).
