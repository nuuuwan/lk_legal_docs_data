THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister  Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, do by this Order -
(a) on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha Wickrmarachchi Ayurveda Institute have
been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said institute have failed to restore normal conditions,
appoint Senior Professor Janitha Abeywickrama Liyanage to be the Competent Authority with effect from December
13, 2019 for the purpose of exercising, peforming and discharging in  lieu  of the Director of the Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed
on, or assigned to the said Director under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation
or Rule made thereunder ; and
(b) rescind the Order made under Section 20(4)(b) and published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2148/34 of November 7, 2019.
B
ANDULA  G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation.
Colombo,
13th December, 2019.
