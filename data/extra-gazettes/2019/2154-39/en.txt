Appointments/Dismissals  made  by  the  Governor of  the Uva Province
I, Raja Collure, the Governor of Uva Province do hereby proclaim the following dismissals of respective Public
Commissions, Authorities and Boards that come under the purview of Uva Provincial Council.
FIRST   PROCLAMATION
By this proclamation made under and by virtue of the powers vested in Article 154(C) and 154 (E) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Section 13.2 of the Statute of the Uva Province
Library Service Board, No. 05 of 2000, it is decreed upon that the following persons are dismissed from the Chairman/
Membership of the Director Board of Uva Province Library Service Board with effect from 19.12.2019.
1. Mr. Manel Rathnayaka (Chairman)
2. Mrs. G. A. M. S. P. Ambanwala (Member)
3. Mr. R. M. Dayananda (Member)
4. Mrs. M. E. Sumithra Silva (Member)
5. Mr. R. M. P. Rathnayaka (Member)
6. Mr. M. Mangala Wijenayake (Member)
7. Mr. W. Sunil (Member)
8. Dr. K. M. R. K. Kulathunga (Member)
9. Mr. Wijaya Premadarshana (Member)
10. Mr. K. P. Nimalasena (Member)

FOURTH   PROCLAMA TION
As the Uva Provincial Council has been dissolved with effect from 08th October 2019, it is considered appropriate
to appoint a new Provincial Public Service Commission for the Uva Provincial Council, by virtue of the powers vested
under Section 33(3) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987. This proclamation is made and it is decreed upon that
the following persons are dismissed from the Chairman/Membership of the Uva Provincial Public Service Commission
with effect from 19.12.2019.
1. Mr. Mohan R. A. Rathwaththa - Attoreney-at-Law (Chairman)
2. Mr. Rajarathnam Gnanasekeram (Member)
3. Mr. Dhanushka Weligama Attoreney-at-Law (Member)
4. Mr. M. M. Vijitha Pieris Attoreney-at-Law (Member)
5. Mr. R. M. Gunawardena (Member)
6. Mr. M. S. M. Farook (Member)
7. Mr. W. M. Senevirathna Weerasinghe Attoreney-at-Law (Member)
8. Mr. D. Sujeewa Jayasinghe Attoreney-at-Law (Member)
