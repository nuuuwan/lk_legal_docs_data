PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
GENERAL Public is hereby notified that annual Lenten season held in Calvary Shrine (St. Anne’s Church) being functioned
ceremonially in Grama Nildhari Divisions Hinuduma South and Malhathawa in the Divisional Secretariat Division Thawalama
Galle District, Southern Province, scheduled to be held during the period from Wednesday the 6th March to Monday the
22nd of April 2019 as per the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175) to terms of the regulations laid down
by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2057/33 dated 09.02.2018 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka.
