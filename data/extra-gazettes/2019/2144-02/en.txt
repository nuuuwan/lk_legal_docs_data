THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
             My Reference No.: DIVBA/LND/ACQSTN/01/01/02.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of
Land Acquisition Act No.28 of 1964 (Amended) that the
Government intends to acquire the following lands which are
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Four allotment of land depicted in the Preliminary Plan
No. KU.2419 situated in the village called Hunukumbura in
Dewamadi Hathpaththuwe Walgampaththu Korale in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. 1211 of Mirihampitiya in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Bamunakotuwa in
Kurunegala District of the North Western Province and more
fully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 01, P.P.KU. 2419
Name of land: Udupitiya Kumbura;  Claimant: K.V. Piyadasa;
Extent: 0.0136 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02, P.P.KU. 2419
Name of land: Udupitiya Kumbura; Claimant: K.V. Pema
Jayawardhana; Extent: 0.0186 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03, P.P.KU. 2419
Name of land: Udupitiya Kumbura; Claimant: K.V .
Jayathunga; Extent: 0.0056 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 04, P.P.KU. 2419
Name of land: Udupitiya Kumbura; Claimant: W.H.Sarath
Jayaweera; Extent: 0.0177 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Bamunakotuwa on the 04.12.2019
at  10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04.12.2019, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
B.M.R.D. BASNAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Bamunakotuwa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Bamunukotuwa.
02nd October, 2019.
