THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                     My No.: MO/ED/03/ACQ/20.
Land Min. No. : 4/3/4/2014/L O/431.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a Police Superintend
Office of Moneragala.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Allotments of Land in F.V .P . 172, Supplement No. 409, Sheet
No. 450 prepared by the superintend surveyor in Monaragala,
situated in Muppane Village in the Moneragala Divisional
Secretary's Division of the District of Monaragala in Uva
Province described as follows :
01. Lot No. 4035
Name of the land: Kumarawattha; Claimant: Land reform
commission; Extent: 0.2860 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 4036
Name of the land: Kumarawattha; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Extent: 0.1290 Hectare.
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Moneragala
Divisional Secretariat on the 29th day of October, 2019 at 10.00
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 15th day of October, 2019 the nature
of their interests in the Land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the said land the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
K. P
RAGEETH KULATHILAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Moneragala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Moneragala,
12th September, 2019.
