PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI  LANKA - 08.11.2019
1A
MINUTE   ON   SECRETARIES
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has directed that the Minute on Secretaries published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 48/2 dated 06th August, 1979 and amended from time to time on the Orders of the President
be further amended with effect from 14th November, 2019 in the manner set out in the Schedule given below.
                           By Order of His Excellency,
UDAYA R. S ENEVIRATNE,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 14, 2019.
Schedule
The following new paragraph is inserted immediately after the paragraph 9a thereof and it shall be in force as
paragraph 9b of the aforesaid Minute.
9b Provisions of paragraph 9a of this Minute shall be equally applicable to the officers re-employed under
05 of the Schedule A inserted under paragraph 1A of the aforesaid Minute.
