EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of  1989, as amended, I,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of  Finance, do by this Order to amend the Order published in the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2117/48 dated April 04, 2019.
(i) by the substitution for the words and figures “August 05, 2019” of the words and figures “October 31, 2019” ; and
(ii) in the item 2 of the Column I of the Schedule I of that Gazette Notification, by the substitution for the words and
figures “within four (04) months period from the date of publication of this Gazette Notification”, of the words and
figures “on or before October 31, 2019”.
MANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
08th of August, 2019.
