No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, t hat have to be 
read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of S ri Lanka,                           
I Maithripala Sirisena, President do by this order declare the Mankikaddu Mangrove Forest of 21.19 Hectares  
bounded by the limit set forth in the schedule hereto as Mankikaddu Mangrove Reserved Forest from 12th day of 
October, 2019. 
  
  
 MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA, 
 President. 
 
 
Polonnarawa, 
12th day of October,  2019. 
.
