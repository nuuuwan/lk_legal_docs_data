 LAND  REFORMS  ACT,  No.  01  OF  1972  OF   THE  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Sub section (4) of Section 27a
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by the Sub Section (4) of Section 27a of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 of
the National State Assembly amended by Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, which should be read
with Sub Article (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Gayantha
Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms do hereby :-
(a) Amend the Order made under Sub Section (2) of Section 27a of the aforesaid Act, and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 815/10 dated 21st April, 1994 relating to the land called and known as Newchattlewatta depicted
in detail in the First Schedule of this order and alienated to Janatha Estate Development Board established under
State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972 and alienated under the Sub Section (1) of Section 27(a) of
the aforesaid Land Reform Act, a part of land called and known as Newchattelwatta and the aforesaid Orders
pertaining to the extent of land depicted morefully in detail in the Second Schedule hereto as Newchattlewatta due
to the revocation for the establishment of an Industrial and Technological Development Supper Zone, such portion
of the said order as relates to the extent of lands depicted in Second Schedule hereto which is part of the land
called and known as Newchattlewatta.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
