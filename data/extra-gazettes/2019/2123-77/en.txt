PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 17.05.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called “Winchfieldpark Watta” in extent of
about 12.9745 Hectares, depicted as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing
No. Maha/UDD/2016/384 dated 26.12.2016, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Karambakatiya in the
Minor Division of Gandeka Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Udadumbara in Kandy District of Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Winchfieldpark Watta – Extent:  10.9859 Hectares
North and East: Winchfieldpark Watta, claimant not known; South:
Stream; West: Stream and Winchfieldpark Watta, claimant not
known.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2010/FR/207.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,

Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
