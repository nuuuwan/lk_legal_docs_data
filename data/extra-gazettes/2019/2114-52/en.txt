EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE CHAIRMAN   OF   THE   BORALESGAMUWA  URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66B (2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER
262)  AS  AMENDED  BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT, No. 16 OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in Boralesgamuwa  Urban Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary  of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna the recognized political party to, which
Subaweerage Swarnalatha Dias   the former Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 66 B (2) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that Boralesgamuwa  Urban Council  who belongs to such party who
shall be declared  elected as Vice Chairman  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Liyanage Udaya Srilal Sigera  to fill such vacancy ;

NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  Boralesgamuwa
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended
by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of  2017 do hereby declare Liyanage Udaya Srilal Sigera elected
as Vice Chairman of the Boralesgamuwa  Urban Council.
