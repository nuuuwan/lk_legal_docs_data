ADMINISTRATION   OF   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   STATUTE   No. .... OF   2019   OF   WESTERN
PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
A Statute to provide provisions for the administration and supervision of affairs relevant for the
duties and functions of the Local Authorities of Western Province for obtaining information and
documents relevant for it including the incidentals thereto.

PART  IV(A) — GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA -  17.05.2019
2A
This Statute is inconsistent to the Section 278 of Municipal Council Ordinance, Section 197
of the Urban Council Ordinance and Section 196 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
Be it enacted by the Western Province Provincial Council of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
1. This Statute is referred to as the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. ..........
of 2019 of Western Province Provincial Council and shall come into operation on the
date of receiving the assent granted by the Governor of Western Province.
2. An Investigation Officer who engages in an investigation task relevant thereto or any
officer for whom the power has been specifically delegated for the same by the Minister
or the Commissioner, may request the Administrative Officer of the Local Authority
or any other officer for whom the power has been delegated by him for that task ; for
a document or a book which is under the custody of Local Authorities and with regard
to the execution of powers or to perform the duties thereof or its administration, a
certain task that is being carried out or was being carried out under  the order of the
Council or a document, file relevant thereto, Minutes of a committee of the Council
or a committee appointed by the Council, a piece of information regarding and income,
expense relevant to the fund of the Local Authority or reports on accounts or copies
of such documents or a piece of information relevant to a property which is under the
control of the Local Authority and may take them into custody for the function of
inspecting.
3. The Commissioner or the Investigation Officer for whom the power is delegated for
the ad hoc, shall have the power and authority to enter a Local Authority or any premises
which is under the control of the Local Authority, to perform the function of inspecting
which is mentioned under the above Section 2.
4. Where a document, file, book or any other information is requested, deliberately
neglecting or obstructing to provide such information shall be an offence and shall be
liable to a fine not exceeding Rs. 5,000 after having been convicted by a Magistrate
Court.
5. In this Statute, unless the context otherwise requires :
“Minister” means the Minister of Western Province, who is in-charge of the
Subject.
“Local Authorities” means Municipal Councils, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya
Sabha of Western Province.
“Commissioner of Local Government” means the Western Provincial
Commissioner of Local Government.
“Investigating Officer” means a Senior Investigating Officers that bears a post in
Provincial Council Public Service having been attached to the Department of
Local Government of Western Province or an Investigation Officer.
6. In the event of any inconsistency between Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Statute, the
Sinhala text shall prevail.
