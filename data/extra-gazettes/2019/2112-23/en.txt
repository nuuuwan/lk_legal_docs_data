The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MATARA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the group leader of  the Independent group, the recognized political party to which Hewamirissage
Jagath Prasantha  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned  member if  Independent group who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the group leader  of the Independent group  has communicated his decision to nominate  Makawita
Liyanage Pethum Priyantha  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the Matara  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that  Makawita Liyanage Pethum Priyantha  was selected as the member of the Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara District,
26th February, 2019.
