I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required 
for a public purpose :- 
 
      Description of the land or  servitude to be acquired : 
 
     An allotment of land is called Panapura watta about 
2.0738 hectares in extent and depicted as Lot 01,02,03,04 
and 05 in P.P.K. Plan Number 3595 and dated 05.07.2018 
made by the Survey General and situated in the village of 
Thalgaspitiya Marakalagama, in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of No. 44C, Thalgaspitiya Marakkalagama, in 
the Minor Division of Mahapalatha Pathuwa, in the 
Divisional Secretariat Division of Aranayaka, in the 
District of Kegalle, in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
Boundaries are as follows . 
     
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Panapurawatta; Extent : 0.7182 
Hectare.  
 
Boundaries 
North : Panapura Ma Kumbura and Panapurawatta; 
East : Panapura watta and Road (Road Development 
Authority); South: Road (Road Development Authority), 
Lot No. 2 and Panapurawatta; West: Panapura watta and 
Panapura Ma Kumbura. 
 
Other particulars : Lot No. 24 (T.P. 246218) is a part of 
preliminary plan No. 8108 and the Land has been acquired 
under Section 38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act. Mr. 
Mothilal Neru and Mr. M. Weerapandian who are 
preliminary claimants and resident of Thalgaspitiya 
Muslimgama, for the completion of acquisition activities. 
 
02. Lot No. 2

(Road Development Authority) and Panapurawatta; 
South: Panapurawatta and Lots No. 3 and 4; West: Lot 
No. 5 and Panapurawatta. 
 
Other particulars : Lot No. 24 (T.P. 246218) of
