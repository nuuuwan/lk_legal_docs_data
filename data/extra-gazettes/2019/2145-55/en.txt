 TOWN   AND   COUNTRY   PLANNING   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virute of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, do by this Order :-
(a) declare that the area of land called “Shrine of Our Lady of Madhu” specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an
Urban Development Area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance ; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the Shrine of Our Lady of Madhu which is declared as
above to be an Urban Development Area.
PA TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M. P.),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Colombo,
11th Day of October, 2019.
