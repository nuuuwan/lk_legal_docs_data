CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY   ACT,  No.  09,  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
THE Consumer Affairs Authority under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act,
No. 09 of 2003 do by this Order remove the item “Coconut” in Order No. 48 published in Government Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2048/30 dated 06th December, 2017.
