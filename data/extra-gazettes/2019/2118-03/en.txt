PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL

CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is h ereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) 
I, H. P.  Jayananda, Additional Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 08th  April,  2019, all  duties 
of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be 
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2117/1 of  01st  April,  2019 
is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification to be published 
on or before 14th   April, 2019.
H. P. J AY ANANDA,
Additional Director General of Customs,
For Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
05th  April, 2019.
