TOURISM  ACT,  No. 38  OF  2005
I, John Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, by virtue of power vested
in me by Section 70 of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005 do hereby order in line with the Section 49(a) of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land mentioned in the Schedule below is required to be acquired for the purpose of development of tourism by
Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
JOHN ANTONY EMMANUAL AMARATHUNGA,
Minister of Tourism Development,
Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
18th September, 2019.
