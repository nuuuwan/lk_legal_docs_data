No. 2107/12 – TUESDAY, JANUARY 22, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. R/NVT/
2013/520 dated 17.12.2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Dombagammana (F.V .P. 23) in the Minor
Division of Meda Pattuwa in Nawadun Korale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Nivithigala in Ratnapura District,
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kiribathgala Estate - Dombagammana Division
– Extent: 8.0000 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);
and Kiribathgala Estate - Dombagammana Division claimed by
Agalawatta Plantation;  South: Kiribathgala Estate - Dombagammana
Division claimed by Agalawatta Plantation, Kebellawatta
claimed by Inoka Kumari Jayasundara, Ranjith Wijewardana and
others; West: Kiribathgala Estate - Dombagammana Division
claimed by Agalawatta Plantation, Kebellawatta claimed by
 Inoka Kumari Jayasundara, Ranjith Wijewardana and others and
Road (R.D.A.).
