No. 2145/28  - TUESDAY  OCTOBER  15, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARANDENIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return member of  Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Gurusinghe
Dapanadurage Ranjanie Mangalika the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kaluwa Dewa Chandrika Kanthi Weerasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the  Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kaluwa
Dewa Chandrika Kanthi Weerasinghe was selected as the member of the Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
14th October, 2019.
