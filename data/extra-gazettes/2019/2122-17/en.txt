THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No. : Ma/San/03/231.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose:
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Lot Nos. 1-5 of land allotments  in total exetent of about 0.0333
Hectare, in the Preliminary Plan No.4152, dated of 30.04.2019 was
prepared by Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Ihala
Karagahamuna, in the Grama Niladari Division of  No. 248, Ihala
Karagahamuna North in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Mahara, in the Gampaha District in Western Province and bouned as
follows:
 1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden, A part of
permanent building ; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K.A.I.
Nanayakkara and U. L. D. Jayarathna, No. 72, Ganemulla Road,
Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0129 Hectare.
 2. Lot No. 2
Name of land:Millagahawatta; Description: Garden and a
permanent building ; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: J. A. D.
Sripala, No. 116/4/1, Mangala Mawatha, 6th Croess Road, Ihala
Karagahamuna , Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0018 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant:

Ihala Karagahamuna,  Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0065 Hectare.
 4. Lot No. 4
Name of land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant:

5. Lot No. 5

No. 84/37, Koppiwatta, Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0059 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretary’s Office of  Mahara on the 21 May 2019
at 9.00. a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the13th May 2019 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
