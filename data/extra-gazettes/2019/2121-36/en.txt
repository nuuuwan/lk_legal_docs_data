AMENDMENT   OF   CHAPTER 7.3   OF   THE   SERVICE   MINUTE   OF   THE
SRI   LANKA    SURVEYORS’    SERVICE
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1434/5 dated 27.02.2006 as amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1482/17 dated 31.01.2007, No. 1509/15 dated 10.08.2007, No. 1623/13 dated
15.10.2009, No. 1654/13 dated 20.05.2010, No. 1675/20 dated 12.10.2010 and No. 2042/12 dated 24.10.2017 should
be amended as follows.
                               By order of the Public Service Commission,
M.A.B. D
AYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
Public Service Commission,

7.3 “The officers serving as Assistant Superintendents of Survey/Surveyors in Class II/II will be promoted to
Class II/I without any prejudice to the seniority of other Assistant Superintendents of Survey and with no further right to
appointment in Class I if they have not passed the 2
nd Efficiency Bar and have completed a satisfactory service during a
period of immediately preceding 5 years at the time such officers complete a service of 10 years. This concession is
applicable only for the officers serving as Assistant Superintendents of Survey/Surveyors as at 01.08.2005, the date on
which this Service Minute takes effect.”
The amendment will come into force only up to 24.12.2018, the date on which the Service Minute of the Sri
Lanka Surveyors’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
No.2103/2 dated 24.12.2018 takes effect.
