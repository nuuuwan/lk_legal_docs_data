THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)
ACT, NO. 28 OF  1964  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                 My No.: LAN/ACQU/NA VPE/D31/02.
Land Ministry Ref No.: 4-3/4/2017/D/520.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section 07 of Land Acquisition
Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose:
Description of the land to be Acquired:
The allotment of land in total Extent of about 1.0117 Hectare
depicted in PP/MADA/2793 Prepared by the Surveyor General
Situated in the Village of Karungalicholai in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Koralaipattu in Batticaloa District,
Eastern Province more fully described below:
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Vellaiyadipooval; Description: The land
contain Permanent Buildings, 18 Palmyra trees, 10 Coconut
trees, 01 Mango tree and two Masonry wells; Claimant: N. V .
Bede Johnpillai; Extent: 1.0117 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North: V alaichenai Aru and V ellaiyadipooval Garden Claimed
by N. V . B. Johnpillai; East: V ellaiyadipooval Garden Claimed
by N. V . B. Johnpillai; South: V ellaiyadipooval Garden Claimed
by N. V . B. Johnpillai Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha), V ellaiyadipooval Garden claimed by Not
known and V alaichcheni River.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Koralaipattu Secretary's Office on
27th day of September, 2019 at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 20th day of September, 2019 the nature interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for the compensation and
the computation of such compensation and the details of the
compilation of such amount. All deeds and documents in
support of the claims should be produced before me at the
inquiry .
Divisional Secretary,
Koralaipattu,
V alaichenai.
Divisional Secretariat,
Koralaipattu.
