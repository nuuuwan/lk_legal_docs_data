I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, No.28 of 1964(Amendment), that
the Government intends to acquire the following land which
is required for a Public Purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Two allotments of  land in Total Extent of about 0.1166
Hectare depicted as lots 1 to 2 in the Preliminary Plan No. Co.
9823 dated 20.06.2019 situated in the Village called
Panchikawatta (Ward No. 25 within Colombo Municipal
Council limit) in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Colombo in Colombo District of the Western Province and
morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Assessment No. 175/40, 191/20 and
other Sahir Cassim and Hajiyar Place; Description: Home
garden, Two permanent two storied buildings are available;
Original Claimant: State;Claimant: W.T.Hemarathna, Sitti
Suleina and some others; Extent: 0.0905 Hec.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Assessment number not available;
Description: Road; Original Claimant: State;Claimant: could
not be traced; Extent: 0.0261 Hec.
All persons interested, in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Colombo Divisional Secretariat
on 06th  December, 2019  at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing in duplicate on or before  27th
November, 2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
