EXTRAORDINARY
NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a) OF   THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Batticaloa Municipal Council
I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of the Batticaloa Municipal Council, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Sivalingam Somasuntharam of the Batticaloa Municipal
Council has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a member of the  Tamil United
Liberation Front.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
05th April, 2019.
