THE  PREVENTION  OF  TERRORISM   (TEMPORARY  PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.   48   OF   1979
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of Section 9 of the Prevention of Terrorism (Temporary Provisions)
Act, No. 48 of 1979, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President do, by  this Order determine that the premises specified in the
Schedule hereto, shall be a place of detention for the purposes of Subsection (1) of Section 9 of the Prevention of
Terrorism (Temporary Provisions) Act, No. 48 of 1979.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
24th May, 2019.
SCHEDULE
Sri Lanka Naval Ship-Gemenu, situated in Welisara, Ragama.
