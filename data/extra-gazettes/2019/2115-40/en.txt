THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is required  for
a public purpose and the land will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
Allotments of land described below in the Plan No. KU/KPW/
2018/124 and dated 31.05.2018 shown by the Surveyor General
situated at the Village of Assadduma in F.V .P. 976 in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Assadduma 1158 in Minor Division of Yatikaha
Korale South in Divisional Secretary's Division of Kuliyapitiya West
in the  District of Kurunegala in the North Western province and
bounded as following:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thalgahamulawatta – Extent: 0.1732 Hectare
North: Thalgahamulawatta claimed by T. M. K. D. Thennakoon
and Others and Chanel; East: Chanel; South: Thalgahamulawatta
claimed by J. A. D. R. Kumara, Road and Thalgahamulawatta claimed
by A. N. Ranmenike; West: Thalgahamulawatta claimed by A. M.
Sunil Adhikari.
