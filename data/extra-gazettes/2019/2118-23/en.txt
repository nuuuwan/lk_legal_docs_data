THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF  1971
Order  under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane, Minister of
Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government, do by this order,declare that, as regards the year
2020, each of  the days specified in the Schedule hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
W
AJIRA ABEYWARDANE,

and Provincial Councils & Local Government.

and Provincial Councils & Local Government.
