The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   GIRIBAWA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Giribawa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  death;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Vishnaka Mohotti Mudiyanselage Nayanananda Vishnaka the Member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that 2- Perakumpura of Giribawa   Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who
shall be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate  Adhikari Mudiyanselage  Ariyarathne  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Giribawa   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Selection 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Adhikari Mudiyanselage  Ariyarathne  was elected as the member of the 2- Perakumpura of Giribawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Giribawa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
05th March, 2019.
