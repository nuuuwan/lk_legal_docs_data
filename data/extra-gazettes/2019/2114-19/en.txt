THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that I have in the exercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (5) of Article 44 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined that with effect from March 13 th, 2019, the assignment of
Duties and Functions, Departments, Statutory Institutions and Public Corporations to the State Minister of Finance appointed
under Paragraph (1) of Article 44, to be as indicated in the Schedule given below.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Colombo 01,
13th March, 2019.
Schedule
(1) State Minister of Finance
Sri Lankan Airline Ltd. and its subsidiaries.
