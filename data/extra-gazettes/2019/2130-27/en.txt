THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0282 hectare depited
in the Advance Tracing No. CO/DSO/2016/733 dated  26.05.2017
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village called
Egoda Kolonnawa (within Kolonnawa Urban Council limit), in the
Grama Niladhari Division of Sinhapura, in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kolonnawa of Colombo District in Western Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Awalakumbura and Ovita and
Kahatagahawatte wanatha land – Extent: 0.0282 Hectare
North: Road and Awalakumbura and Ovita and Kahatagahawatte
Wanatha land claimed by K. Suneetha Damayanthi; East:
Awalakumbura and Ovita and Kahatagahawatte Wanatha land claimed
by K. Suneetha Damayanthi and Sumanarama Temple land in which
ownership cannot be found; South: Sumanarama Temple land in
which ownership cannot be found and Road; West: Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2016/CEB/93.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
25th June, 2019.
