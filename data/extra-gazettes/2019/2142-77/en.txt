EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 05/2019
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4, 13 and 17 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), I, Mangala Samaraweera,
Minister of Finance, do hereby declare that, with effect from September 27, 2019.
(1) The limit of sale of foreign liquor by retail, with respect to the whole Island shall be 80 (Eighty) Bulk Litres; and
(2) The maximum quantity for transport and possession of foreign liquor for the bona fide private consumption or use
without a pass issued under the Ordiance with respect to the whole Island shall be 80 (Eighty) Bulk Litres.
The Excise Notification, No. 985 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1947/41 of  December 31, 2015 is hereby
rescinded.
MANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
27th September, 2019.
