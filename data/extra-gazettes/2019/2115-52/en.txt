EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
three consecutive meeting without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ahila Ilkankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Somalingam Jeevananthan the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that ward 04-Kuppilan if Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and
is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Rasaraththinam Ushanth to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of  the Valikamam South Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Rasaraththinam Ushanth was elected as the member of the Ward 04 - Kuppilan of Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
22nd March, 2019.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 23.03.2019
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY  IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ahila Ilkankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Arulnathan Kirusnaveni the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide upon returned member if Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Kanthasami Sothimalar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of  the Valikamam South Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Kanthasami Sothimalar was selected as the member of the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna
22nd March, 2019.
