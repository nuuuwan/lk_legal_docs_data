PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
 THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE   DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified by me, as the Cabinet Minister in Charge of  the Subject of Transport and Civil Aviation, in
terms of the Article 44 (5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that assignment of
subjects and  functions for State Minister of  Transport and Civil Aviation, appointed under the Article 44(1) of the
Constitution, shall come into effect immediately under my supervision, control and guidance as listed in the following
Schedule.
A
RJUNA  R ANATHUNGA ,
Minister of  Transport and Civil Aviation.
16th January, 2019.
