THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, do by this Order declare that the term : Municipal Council where the allotment of land described in the Schedule
II of the Notification No. 2046/11 dated 21st November 2017 which is urgently required for construction of flats for
resettlement of underserved settlers on the recommendation of the Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis and
Western Development is situated, should be amended to read “within the Municipal Council Limits of Moratuwa ” in
place of “within the Municipal Council Limits of Dehiwela - Mount Lavinia”.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
August 24, 2019.
