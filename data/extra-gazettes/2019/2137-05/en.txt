6TH   AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1842/2 dated
23rd of December 2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary
No. 1923/47 dated 16th of July 2015, No. 1979/33 dated 11th August 2016, No. 2086/22 dated 30th of August 2018, No. 2095/9
dated 30th of October 2018 and No. 2095/48 dated 2nd of November 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is
amended as follows and these amended provisions will be made effective from 26th of February 2019, the date on which the
Cabinet of Ministers made the policy decision.
                                                                     By Order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
