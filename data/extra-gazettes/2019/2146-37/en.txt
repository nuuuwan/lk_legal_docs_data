Act, No. 36 of 2003.
SUJEEWA SENASINGHE (M. P.),
Minister of Science, Technology and Research.
Ministry of Science, Technology and Research,
Battaramulla,
23rd October, 2019.
Fees Regulations of National Intellectual  Property Office of Sri Lanka
The notification relating to the fees published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1445/10 of 17th May 2006 is
hereby rescinded and the fees specified in the Second Schedule of the said notification are hereby amended as follows in
terms of the proposal 261 of Budget Proposals 2018 to increase fees charged for services provided by the government
agencies which have not been revised during last 03 years, by 15%.
These regulations may be cited as the Intellectual Property Regulations No. 02 of 2019 and shall come into
operation on 30.10.2019.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
