THE  PUBLIC   SECURITY   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   40)
Notice under Section 2(7)
IT is hereby notified that the proclamation made by His Excellency the President under Section 2 of the Public Security
Ordinance (Chapter 40), bringing into operation the Provisions of Part II of the aforesaid Ordinance throughout Sri Lanka
with effect from 22nd April, 2019 was approved by a resolution of Parliament on 24th April, 2019.
