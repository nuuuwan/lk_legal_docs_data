THE  AW ARD
The Honourable Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations by virtue of the powers vasted in him by Section 4(1) of the
Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition) as amended by Acts
Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 appointed me
as Arbitrator by his order dated 03rd May 2019 and referred the following disputes to me for settlement by arbitration.
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties are :-
Whether the cancellation of  salary revision given to Mr. A. M. Nishanthapriya, Supra Grade Clerk of the Ceylon
Fishery Harbours Corporation on two occasions with effect from 01.08.2014 and 01.01.2016 is just and if not so, to what relief
he is entitled.
Appearance
Mr . Gratian Silva, Representative appeared for the party of the First Part.
Mr .D. M. S. K. Wijetunga, Assistant Human Resources Manager appeared for the  Party of the Second Part.
Party of the First Part filled the 1st statement under regulations 21(1) and 21(2) of the Industrial Disputes Regulations
of 1958 as amended.
On behalf of Party of the Second Part Mr. D. M. S. K. Wijetunga Assistant Human Resources Manager informed the Court
salary revision was already granted to the Party of the First part. Futher the Party of the First Part also informed the Court that
he was granted the relief he sought for. Therefore the matter was settled. in the circumstances I make no award.
KAPILA M. SARATHCHANDRA,
Arbitrator.
