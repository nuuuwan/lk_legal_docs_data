THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER  460)
Order under Section 49A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 49A of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) read with Article 44 of the
Constitution, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Order, declare that the land specified in the Schedule hereto is required by the
Board of Investment of Sri Lanka being a public corporation established by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law,
No. 4 of 1978.
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies & International Trade.
Colombo,
07th May, 2019.

North Western Province, in Tracing No. KU/DSO/2017/128 dated 19.04.2017 certified by T. M. P. U. K. Thennakoon,
Senior Superintendent of Surveys, are as follows :
