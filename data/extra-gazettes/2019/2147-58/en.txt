L.D.B. 5/2007
ELECTRONIC  TRANSACTIONS  ACT,  NO.  19  OF  2006
Order  under  Section  18
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Secton 18 of the Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006, I , Ajith P. Perera,
Minister of Digital Infrastructure and Information Technology, do by this order, designate the Sri Lanka Compurter
Emergency Readiness Team (Sri Lanka CERT) registered as Sri Lanka CERT (Private) Limited under the Companies Act,
No. 7 of 2007, as the Certification Authority for the purposes of the said Act.
The Order made under Section 18 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1829/29 of September, 24,
2013 is hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
A
JITH P. PERERA,
Minister of Digital Infrastructure and
 Information Technology.
at Colombo,
28th October, 2019.
