THE  CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ravi
Karunanayaka, Minister of Power, Energy and Business Development, do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition
of the land required by the Ceylon Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto :
R
AV I K ARUNANAY AKA,
Minister of Power, Energy and Business Development.
14th November, 2019.
