FOREST  CONSERVATION  ORDINANCE
THE order under section 3 that has to be read with Section 64(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordiance (Chapter 451), as
No. 13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009 that have
to be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by Acts,
No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, that have to be read with
Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I Maithripala Sirisena,
Precedent do by this order declare the Uggalkanda Mukalana Alias Uggaldeththa Forest of 13.9113 Hectares bounde by
the limit set forth in the schedule hereto as Uggalkanda Mukalana Alias Uggaldeththa Reserved Forest from 18th day of
March 2019.
M
AITHRIPALA  SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
18th March, 2019.
