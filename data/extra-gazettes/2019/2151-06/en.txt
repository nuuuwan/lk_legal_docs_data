NOTIFICATION
P. S. No. PS/CSA/00/1/3/1/1
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Ranil Wickremasinghe, M. P., Prime Minister has resigned the Office of Prime Minister
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Article 46(2)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, with effect from November 21, 2019.

P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
November 21, 2019.
