FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   WANATHAWILLUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  W anathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group 1,  to which Katuwala Kande Gamladdalage Pradeep
Aruna Kantha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member if Independent Group No. 1 who belongs to such Independent Group  and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  elected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Kaleefa Marikkar Abdul Asees to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the  Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kaleefa
Marikkar Abdul Asees  was selected as the member of the Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
20th September, 2019.
