THE   FINANCE   ACT,  No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order, vest in the People’s Bank established under the People’s Bank Act,
No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 20th September, two thousand  nineteen, the properties described in the Schedule hereto.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Colombo,
20th September, 2019.
SCHEDULE
All that land called and known as “Periya Puhailai Totam” situated at Karunkaliyadi in Daluwa Village in Mel
Akkaraipattu South in Kalpitiya Division in Puttalam District, North Western Province in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Daluwa in the Division of the Divisional Secretary Division of Kalpitiya in the District of Puttalam North Western Province,
depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No. 117 dated 04.04.1985 and made by N. Sankaralingam, Licensed Surveyor of Puttalam
containing in extent one rood and six perches (0A., 1R., 6P.) and bounded on the North by : land belonging to Jasintha, East
by : land belonging to George Gamini, South by : land belonging to M. Amirtham and on the West by  : V . C. road (Church
Road).
This land is registered in folio D 10/85 in the Land Registry, Puttalam.
