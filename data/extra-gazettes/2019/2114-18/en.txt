KADUWELA   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
(Chapter 267) Entertainment Tax Ordinance
IMPOSING  ENTERTAINMENT  TAX
IT is hereby notified the public that I, Isuru Devapriya, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance & Planning, Local
Governments, Economic Development, Electricity & Power, Environment Affairs, Water Supply & Drainage and Tourism
Affairs of Western Province Provincial Council has approved the following resolution passed under Resolution
No. 05(1)(i)(w)/02/uq/0288 at the General Meeting held on 03rd August, 2018 by the Kaduwela Municipal Council under
virtue of the powers in me by Sub Section (2) Section 2 of (Chapter 267) Entertainment Tax Ordinance read with Paragraph
(a) Sub Section (1) Section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequentional Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I
SURU DEVAPRIY A,
Chief Minister and Minister of Finance & Planning,
Local Governments, Economic Development, Electricity & Power,
Environment Affairs, Water Supply & Drainage and Tourism Affairs,
Western Province Provincial Council.
