No. 2121/25  - TUESDAY,  APRIL  30, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VALIKAMAM   EAST
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return  member of  Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group to  which Thevan Sasinanthini the member belonged, was
required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  Independent
Group  who belongs to such Independent Group   and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Selvakumary Selvaruban to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Selvakumary Selvaruban was selected as the member of the Valikamam East Pradeshisya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
29th April, 2019.
