The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALIKAMAM  WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which
Thiruganasampanthan Subaruban the Member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon returned member if Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Thavarasa Satheswaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Thavarasa Satheswaran was selected as the member of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
25th March, 2019.
