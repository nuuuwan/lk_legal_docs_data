Statutory Determination No : 4641
Unique No : fld/1181
STATUTORY  DETERMINATION   UNDER   SECTION  19  OF  LAND   REFORM  ACT,  No.  1  OF 1972
THE Statutory Determination No. 2319 published in respect of the declaration fld/1181 in Extraordinary Gazette
No. 220/9 and dated 7th July 1976 of the Republic of Sri Lanka is hereby cancelled and in terms of the Statutory Declaration
made under Section 18 of the Act, by Mr. Lloyd Joseph Ivor Fernando, C/O Mr. Cecil Arsecularatne of Hemas Building,
Colombo 1 and by virtue of the powers vested in the Land Reform Commission a new Statutory Determination has been
made following the Statutory Lessee to specify the portion of agricultural land out of the lands owned by him. The said
portion of the land that is allowed to the said Lessee is described in the Schedule hereto.
