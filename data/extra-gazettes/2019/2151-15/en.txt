No. 2151/15  - TUESDAY  NOVEMBER  26, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Muhandiram Ralalage Dammika Udayangani
Pushpakumari of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of  member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as she
ceased to be a member of the United People’s Freedom Alliance.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
22nd November, 2019.
