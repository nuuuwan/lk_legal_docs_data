THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I , Gayantha Karunatilleke , Minister of  Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Boundaries of the portion of land in extent about 2.0200 Hectares
depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’ in the Advance Tracing No. R/ELP/2018/195
dated 10.05.2018 prepared by the Surveyor-General  and situated in
the  Village Damme (F.V .P .71) in the Minor Division of Palle
Paththuwa in Navadun Korale, in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Elapatha in Rathnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ - Kumbalgoda Waththa, Paradeniyahenyaya,
Paradeniyahena - Extent: About 2.0200 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 235, 9Z, 9T, 9P of F.V .P. 70 and Lot No.3C of
F.V .P.71; East: Lot Nos.3C and 3B of  F.V .P.71; South: Lot Nos. 3B,
3C and 2; West: Lot Nos.3C, 50, 49 of  F.V .P.71 and Lot Nos. 235
and 9Z of F.V .P. 70
