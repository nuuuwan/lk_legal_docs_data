THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCA TION UNDER SECTION 39(1)
THe allotment of land described in detail in the Order No. 189 of
2002 dated 11.11.2002 made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the
Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra
Ordinary No. 1262/26 dated 15.11.2002 is vested in the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
But the possession of the land described below has not yet been
taken for and on behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka. Therefore, I Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and
Parliamentary Reforms, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 39 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke
allotments described below, which are included in the aforesaid Order
No. 189 of 2002 dated 11.11.2002 published in the Gazette Extra
Ordinary No. 1262/26 dated 15.11.2002.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
