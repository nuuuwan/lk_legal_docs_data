REGULATIONS  enacted by the Uva Provincial Minister of Sports, Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural Affairs,
Textiles and Small Industries in respect of  the composition of  the Uva Provincial Road Transport Service Authority under
Sections 5 and 6 of  the Statute for Uva Provincial Passenger Transport  Service Authority of  No. 01 of 2001 and the

REGULATIONS
1. The Regulations published in the Gazette (Extraordinary) of  No. 2108/60 of Thursday, 31 st January 2019 of  the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka related to the appointment of  the Board of Directors of  the Uva
Provincial Passenger Transport Service Authority are hereby amended as follows.
2. The appointments of the  below mentioned members of the Director Board of the Uva Provincial Road Transport
Service Authority are hereby cancelled and instead, new members are appointed as follows.
