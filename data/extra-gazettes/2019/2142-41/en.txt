FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KATANA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Katana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group No. 1,  to which Warnakulasooriya Sanath Rohan Fernando
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned
member of Independent Group No. 1 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group No. 1 has communicated his decision to nominate
Melani Rose Ferreira to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Katana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Melani Rose Ferreira was selected as the member of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
24th September, 2019.
