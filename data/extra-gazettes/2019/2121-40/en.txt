PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No. : 8/3/5/3/9. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2017/WS/523. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the lands shown below 
which are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Lands to be Acquired: 
 
The allotmentof land described below containing in 
extent 0.2799 Hectare, depicted as lot No. 357 in Final 
Village Plan No. 25, prepared by Surveyor General, 
situated at Rajjammana Village in Gampahasiya Pattuwa 
Minor Division of Matale Divisional Secretary’s 
Division, Matale District, Central province: 
 
01. Lot No. 357 
Name of land: Rajjammana Plantation alias 
Rajjammana Special Project Colony; Original Claimant: 
K.A. Premawathi. 
On the North by: Lot Nos. 148 and 147; On the East 
by: Lot Nos. 147 and 349; On the South by: Lot Nos. 351 
and 132; On the West by: Lot No. 132.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Matale Divisional Secretariat on the 30th day of 
May 2019 at 11.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 23rd of May, 
2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars 
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.   
   
   
 K.P.K.L.P. MADHUWANTHI,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matale . 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Matale. 
29th April, 2019.
