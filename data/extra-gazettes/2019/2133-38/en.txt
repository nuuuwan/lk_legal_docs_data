AMENDMENT
UTHTHAMA   SEV A   PADAKKAMA
Regulations
THE  regulation for the Uththama Seva Padakkama published in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 156/5 dated 01st September
1981 and Extraordinary Gazette No. 1692/29 dated 11th February, 2011 are hereby amended by the substitution of the
following new paragraph 5 to be effective only for the year 2018, 2019 and year 2020. Therefore in place of Paragraph 5
thereof.
Service Regulation 5 : “The grant of the award shall be restricted to 3% of the officers and 1% of the other ranks
per annum from within the Tri Services as at the date of 01st January of the relevant year
shall be separately in numerical value”.
                   By His Excellency’s Command,
U
DAYA R. S ENA VIRATHNE,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
12th July 2019.
