The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Colombo Municipal Council
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Nobert Neriyas Fernando of the
Colombo Municipal Council has vacated his office of  member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a member
of the United National Party.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Colombo City,
20th December, 2019.
