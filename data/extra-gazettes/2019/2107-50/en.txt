Regulations
Minimum standards for the citizens of Sri Lanka engaged in fishing operations in foreign flagged fishing boats
Regulations published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1945/7 of December 14, 2015 are hereby amended by the
addition immediately after regulation 6 thereof of  the following regulation :-
“7. No citizen of Sri Lanka serving in a foreign flagged fishing vessel within the Exclusive Economics Zone of
Sri Lanka, High seas or within the Exclusive Economic Zone of any other state shall engage in any
illegal, unreported and unregulated fishing activity.”.
