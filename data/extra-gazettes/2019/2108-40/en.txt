Regulations
The Sea Mammals (Observation, Regulation and Control) Regulations No. 01 of 2012, published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1774/27 of September 06, 2012 are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of regulation 13 and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“(13) (1) Every person or group of persons who intends to observe sea mammals shall obtain a
permit in that behalf from the Director-General or an officer authorized in that behalf by the Director-
General who shall be present at the departure point for such purpose.
(2) The person seeking to obtain a permit shall furnish information required in the form set out
in Schedule IV .
(3)  The permit so issued shall be in the form set out in Schedule V . The permit shall be issued
on payment of a specified fee as set out in Schedule VI.” ;
(2) by the repeal of Schedule V thereof and the substitution therefor of the following :-
