NOTICE  OF  POLL
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION 38(1)
ELECTION   OF   CHAIRMAN,   VICE   CHAIRMAN   AND   MEMBERS   OF   ELPITIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
NOTICE is hereby given under Section 38(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that the above election  is
contested and that a poll will be taken between 7.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m. on 11.10.2019 and that,
(a) the names of the candidates in the first nomination paper as indicated by the candidates of each Recognized
Political Party for Election as Chairman, Vice Chairman and Members of the above mentioned Local
Authority, the numbers and names of the respective wards and the names of candidates in the second
nomination paper to be returned ; and the approved election symbol allotted to each party ; and
(b) the situation of the polling station or stations and the particular polling stations reserved for female
voters in the polling districts of the above mentioned electoral area.
are as specified in the schedule below.
2.  The order in which the name of each Recognized Political Party  appearing in the Schedule below is the same
order in which these particulars will be printed on the ballot paper.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha,
Assistant Returning Officer,

for Returning Officer.
