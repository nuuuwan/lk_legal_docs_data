THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotments of  land called ‘‘Liyankobulla Watta ’’ in extent
about 16.242 Hectares, depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’ in the Advance Tracing
No.  Maha/UDD/2017/176 prepared by the Superintendent of Survey
- Kandy on 09.11.2017, situated in Karambaketiya Village in the
Gandeka  Korale in Udadumbara Divisional Secretariat  in Kandy
District in bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Liyankobulla Watta – Extent: 16.242 Hectare
North: Stream, Land called ‘‘Liyankobulla Watta’’ claimed by

Land called ‘‘Liyankobulla Watta claimed by M. A. Siddik; South:
Land called ‘‘Liyankobulla Watta claimed by M. A. Siddik and Crown
Rock; West: Crown Rock and Stream.
