The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me under section 3A. that has to be read with section 64(1) of the For est Conservation
Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 13 of  1966 as amended by Acts No. 56 of  1979, No. 13 of  1982, No. 84 of  1988, No. 23 of  1995
and No. 65 of 2009, that have to be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of  Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I Maithripala Sirisena, the President do by this order declare the Agrabopath  Forest of  2926.72
Hectares bounded by the limits set forth in the Schedules 01 and 02 hereto as Agrabopath conservation Forest from 18th day
of March, 2019.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
18th March, 2019.
