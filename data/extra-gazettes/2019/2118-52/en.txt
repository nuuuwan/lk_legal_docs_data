FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VA VUNIYA   SOUTH  (TAMIL)
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Vavuniya  South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group, to which Sivakulanthai Nandaraj the member belonged,
was required by  me under Section   66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  Independent
Group who belongs to such Independent Group, and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Maniyam Nagendhirarasa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer  of  the V avuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Maniyam Nagendhirarasa was selected as the member of the Vauniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
09th April, 2019.
