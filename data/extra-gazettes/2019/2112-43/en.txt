THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. :  2018/18.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/6/2018/HW/20.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot No. 1 allotments in total extent of about 0.0962 Hectare,
of land in Preliminary Plan No. Mara 2765 and dated 09.01.2019
prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of Surveys of  Matara
District on behalf of  Surveyor General, situated in the Grama
Niladhari Division of  No. 327 B, Walakanda East in the village
of Bajjankale in Minor Division of Gangabada Paththuwa in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kirinda Puhulwella in
Matara District in the Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Gahalakolamadiththa; Description: Contains
a garden, mixed cultivation; Claimant: Kodithuwakkuge
Piyadasa, "Wijaya", Gahalakolamadiththa, Walakanda;
Original Claimant: - (Parts of the Hi.P. 230525 and Hi.P. 230839);
Extent: 0.0962 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the afore said lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Office of Kirinda
Puhulwella on the from 21.03.2019 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing in duplicate) on or before
the 14.03.2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of  their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said  lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
C
HINTHAKA ABEWICKRAMA,
Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer,
Kirinda Puhulwella.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kirinda Puhulwella,
26th February, 2019.
