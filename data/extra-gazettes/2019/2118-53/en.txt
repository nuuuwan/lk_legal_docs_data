PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
ARTICLE   154 B   OF   THE   CONSTITUTION   OF  THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF
SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph 8 of Article 154B of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, I, Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Rajith Keerthi Tennakoon, the Governor of Southern Province, do hereby
dissolve the Southern Provincial  Council with effect from the midnight 10th April, 2019.
T
ENNAKOON M UDIYANSELAGE R AJITH K EERTHI TENNAKOON,
Governor of the Southern Province.
Office of the Governor of the Southern Province,
10th April,  2019.
