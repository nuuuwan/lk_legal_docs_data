PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 17.07.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ya/
TLL/2018/40 and dated 30.06.2018, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village called Thellippalai South West in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. J/223 of Viththakapuram in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Valikamam North in Jaffna District
in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Neelawattai and Kaddukkumpalai – Extent:
0.0312 Hectare
North: Neelawattai and Kaddukkumpalai Garden claimed by
Mylvakanam Thevambikai; East: Neelawattai and Kaddukkumpalai
Garden claimed by Vaitheeswaran Kunalan; South and West: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
