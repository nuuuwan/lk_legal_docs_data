Government  Notifications
L.D.B 21/78(XVI)(A)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 24A(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of Section 24A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,
A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,  Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, in consultation with the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order -
(1) establish with effect from 09.09.2019 the Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences as an Institute for Higher
Learning, for the purpose of providing, promoting and developing higher educaion ; and
(2) assign to such Institute the name and style of “the Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences, University of
Peradeniya”.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
09th September, 2019.
