L.D.B. 2/1995
ARBITRATION  ACT,  No.  11 OF  1995
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of the Arbitration Act, No. 11 of 1995, I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of
Justice and Prison Reforms do by this Order, with the concurrence of the Chief Justice, designate the High Court of Sri
Lanka holden in the Judicial Zone of Jaffna and in the Judicial Zone of Kandy in addition to the High Court of Sri Lanka
holden in the Judicial Zone of Colombo for the purpose of the implementation of the provisions of the Arbitration Act,
No. 11 of 1995.
T
HALA THA ATHUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
September 12, 2019.
