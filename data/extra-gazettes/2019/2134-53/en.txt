THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advanced Tracing
No. MR/DKW/2018/362 dated 21.09.2018 prepared by the
Surveyor-General and situated in the Village called Dambagasara in
the Minor Division of Wellabada Pattuwa East in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Dikwella in Matara District in Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Iththegodella – Extent: 0.3117 Hectare
North: Ambagahakoratuwa claimed by K. H. Gunawathie,
Iththegodella claimed by Vijitha Andarage, Iththegodella claimed by
K. G . Amarapala and Baduwatta claimed by Wilbert Wickramasinghe;
East: Iththegodella claimed by K. G. Amarapala, Baduwatta claimed
by Wilbert Wickramasinghe, Iththegodella of which the claimant
could not be traced and Iththegodella claimed by A. P. Ariyawathie
and others; South: Iththegodella claimed by A. P. Ariyawathie and
others and Lot B; West: Lot B and Ambagahakoratuwa claimed by
K. H. Gunawathi.
