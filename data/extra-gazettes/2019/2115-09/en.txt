IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Paragraph (2) of Article 43 of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Hon. Ranil Wickremasinghe, Member of Parliament, as the
Minister of National Policies, Economic Affairs, Resettlement & Rehabilitation, Northern Province Development and
Youth Affairs, with effect from March 18, 2019.
                   By order of His Excellency,
