PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GA/
BIY/2018/539 and dated 16.10.2018 prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Mabima in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 276A of Mabima West in the Minor Division of Adikari
Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Biyagama in
Gampaha District of Western Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galabodawatta – Extent: About 0.0200 Hectare
North: Galabodawatta claimed by the state and land claimed by
the state; East: Land claimed by the state and Galabodawatta claimed
by K. A. D. A. Calistus; South: Galabodawatta claimed by K. A. D.
A. Calistus, Land claimed by K. D. S. Kumara and others and Lot B;
West: Lot B and Land claimed by the state.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Name of the land unknown – Extent: About
0.0140 Hectare
North: Land claimed by the state, Galabodawatta claimed by
state and Lot A; East: Lot A, Galabodawatta claimed by K. A. D. A.
Calistus and Land claimed by K. D. S. Kumara and others; South:
Land claimed by K. D. S. Kumara and others and Pradeshiya
Sabha Road; West: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Land claimed by the
state.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Gonnagahawatta – Extent: About 0.0345 Hectare
North: Land claimed by the state and Pradeshiya Sabha Road;
East: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Gonnagahawatta claimed by
M. K. B. M. Alwis; South: Gonnagahawatta claimed by M. K. B.
M. Alwis and Pradeshiya Sabha Road; West: Gonnagahawatta
claimed by the state.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Gonnagahawatta – Extent: About 0.0387 Hectare
North: Road and Pradeshiya Sabha Road; East: Gonnagahawatta
claimed by M. K. B. M. Alwis; South: Kongahawatta alias
Galabodawatta claimed by Ven. Elakake Sangarakkitha Thero and
Lot E; West: Lot E, Kongahawatta alias Galabodawatta claimed by
state and Road.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Kongahawatta or Galabodawatta – Extent: About
0.0353 Hectare
North: Kongahawatta or Galabodawatta claimed by the state,
Road and Lot D; East: Lot D, Gonnagahawatta claimed by M. K. B.
M. Alwis and Kongahawatta or Galabodawatta claimed by
Ven. Elakake Sangarakkitha Thero; South: Kongahawatta or
Galabodawatta claimed by Ven. Elakake Sangarakkitha Thero,
Thalgahawatta claimed by D. M. Kahanda and Lot F; West: Lot F,
Thalgahawatta claimed by the state and Kongahawatta or
Galabodawatta claimed by state.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Thalgahawatta – Extent: About 0.0719 Hectare
North: Thalgahawatta claimed by the state, Kongahawatta or
Galabodawatta claimed by state and Lot E; East: Lot E, Kongahawatta
or Galabodawatta claimed by Ven. Elakake Sangarakkitha Thero,
Thalgahawatta claimed by D. M. Kahanda and Paddy field claimed
by D. M. Kahanda; South: Thalgahawatta claimed by D. M. Kahanda
and Paddy field claimed by D. M. Kahanda; West: Thalgahawatta
claimed by the state.
