THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT) ACT No.
28 OF 1964  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                My Ref. No. : KDS/MDB/4/2/1/71/24.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms
Reference No.:13/96/FR/505.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition  Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment)  that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
      Purpose of acquisition: Knuckles Range Reservation
Two allotments of land called Angurupolawatta in total
extent of about 4.0168 hectares depicted as Lot 01 in the
Preliminary Plan No. P .P . Maha 6735 Dated 19.01.2018 prepared
by the Senior superintendent of Surveys - Kandy District,
situated in the village called Mangoda in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 806 of Mangoda in the Udasiyapattuwa Korale
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
    G 29324 - 17 (2019/01)
Medadumbara in Kandy District  of the Central Province and
more fully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of the land: Angurupolawatta; Description of the
Land: Jungle; Claimant: H. M. Muthubanda, Mangoda,
Medamahanuwara; Extent: 4.0168Hectares.
   All Persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Medadumbara Divisional
Secretariat on the 24th day of April, 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on
or before the 17th day of  April, 2019 .The nature of their
interest in the aforesaid land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
