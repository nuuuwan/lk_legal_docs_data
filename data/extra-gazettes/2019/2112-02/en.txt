FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER  OF   THE   COLOMBO MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A (1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of  Colombo  Municipal Council  has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the  recognized political party to which
Mohomed Asath  Sanoon Sali the member belonged, was required by me  under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned member if United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to  such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Wathupiti Kandalage Shantha Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Colombo  Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Wathupiti Kandalage Shantha Kumara  was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
 P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo  Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
25th  February  2019.
