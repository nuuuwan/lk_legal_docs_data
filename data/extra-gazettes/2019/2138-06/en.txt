NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE
Amendment of Rules published in the Government Extraordinary Gazette No. 2086/15 of dated 29.08.2018
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in the Minister in charge of the subject, under Section 289(1) of Municipal Council
Ordinance (Chapter 252) that should be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of
1989 and the power vested in me in the Minister in charge of the subject under Subsection 184(1)(c)(e)(f) of Pradeshiya
Sahba Act, No. 15 of 1987 and in accordance with powers vested in me as the Governor of North Central Province, I do
hereby order to amend the rules published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2086/15 and dated 29.08.2018 and to implement
as following :
This Rules shall be effected from 1st of April, 2019.
S

02. Refreshment allowances and refereshment expenses for honourable Chairman and members of Pradeshiya
Sabha
Present System
3.I Monthly allowance of Rs. 2000.00 can be paid to the Chairman of  Pradeshiya Sabha as refreshment
allowance. The expenditure Head should be submitted for the purpose.
3. II For the Members of Pradeshiya Sabha participation for meeting of Pradeshiya Sabha each member
for each meeting could be spent Rs. 100.00 under Pradeshiya Sabha Fund.
System after the revision
3.I Monthly allowances of Rs. 4000.00 can be paid to the Chairman of  Pradeshiya Sabha as refreshment
allowance. The expenditure Head should be submitted for the purpose.
3. II For the Members of Pradeshiya Sabha participation for meeting of Pradeshiya Sabha each member
for each meeting could be spent Rs. 200.00 under Pradeshiya Sabha Fund.
03. Allowances for Personal Staff of the Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha
Present System
6.I The Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha could appoint a personal assistant and a maximum amount of
Rs. 7500.00 could be paid for him/her under Pradeshiya Sabha Fund.
System after the revision
6.I The Chairman of the Pradeshiya Sabha could appoint a personal assistant and a maximum amount of
Rs. 12,500.00 could be paid for personal assistant under Pradeshiya Sabha Fund.
