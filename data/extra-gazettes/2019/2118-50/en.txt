THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of land described in below names and extents
depicted in  the Advance Tracing No.: GA/MAH/2018/601 dated of
27.12.2018 was prepared by Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of Ihala Karagahamuna, in the Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 248,
Ihala Karagahamuna North, Ciyane Koralaye  in Minor Division of
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Mahara, in the Gampaha
District in Western Province and 
bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A1’ – Millagahawatta – Extent: 0.0129 Hectare
North: Mangala Mawatha and Lot Nos. 10 and 11 in Gam 4115;
East: Lot No. 11 in advance Tracing No. Gam 4115; South: Lot Nos.
170 and 171 in advance Tracing No. Gam 3810 and Lot A2 is in this
Advance tracing; West: Lot A2 and Mangala Mawatha.
02. Lot ‘A2’ – Millagahawatta – Extent: 0.0018 Hectare
North: Mangala Mawatha and Lot A1; East: Lot A1; South: Lot
Nos. 170, 171 and 172 in advance Tracing No. Gam 3810 and Lot
A3; West: Lot A3 and Mangala Mawatha.
03. Lot ‘A3’ – Millagahawatta – Extent: 0.0065 Hectare
North: Mangala Mawatha and Lot A2; East: Lot A2; South: Lot
Nos. 172 and 173 in advance Tracing No. Gam 3810 and Lot A4;
West: Lot A4 and Mangala Mawatha.
04. Lot ‘A4’ – Millagahawatta – Extent: 0.0062 Hectare
North: Mangala Mawatha and Lot A3; East: Lot A3; South: Lot
Nos. 172, 173 and 176 in advance Tracing No. Gam 3810 and Lot
A5; West: Lot A5 and Mangala Mawatha.
05. Lot ‘A5’ – Millagahawatta – Extent: 0.0059 Hectare
North: Mangala Mawatha and Lot A3; East: Lot A4; South: Lot
Nos. 172, 176 and 179 in advance Tracing No. Gam 3810; West:
Mangala Mawatha.
