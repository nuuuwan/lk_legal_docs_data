THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: GCF/A TH/KD/23/LR.
Land Min's Ref. No. : 13/97/LRB/360.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the land described below to develop water retaining
area in upper part of the Parliament Lake under Greater
Colombo Flood Control and Environment Promotion Project.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.1892
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 and 02 in the Plan No. Co. 9790
dated 31.01.2019 prepared by the Survey General situated in
the village called Pelawaththa in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 479 F of Aruppitiya within Kaduwela Municipal Council
minor division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Kaduwela in Colombo District of Western Province and
described as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Could not be traced; Present land use:
Home garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: W . K. G . N.
Perera, No. 47/50, Perera Mawatha, Thalangama South,
Battaramulla; Extent: 0.1629 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the land: Could not be traced; Present land use:
Home garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Welikadage
Badra Rohini Perera, No. 41/1, Pagoda Road, Nugegoda; Extent:
0.0263 Hectare.
