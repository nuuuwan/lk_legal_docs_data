THE  SPORTS  LAW   No. 25  OF  1973
ORDER made by the Minister of Sports under Sections 32 and 33 of  the Sports Law,  No. 25 of 1973.
HARIN FERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
31st  December, 2018.
Order under Sections 32 and 33
I, Harin Fernando, the Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports by virtue of powers
vested in me under Sections 32 (b) and 33 of  the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 and it’s Amendments do hereby temporary
suspend the registration of  the Sri Lanka  Cricket which is the National Association for Cricket and as an interim
arrangement, appoint  Mr. Wedikkara Arachchige Chulananda Perera, Secretary to the Ministry of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports as Competent Authority with effect from 31st of  December 2018 to continue with
administration and perform any such other functions necessary for the due administration of Sri Lanka Cricket until the
office bearers are elected from the election.
Gazette Notification bearing No. 2094/ 37 dated 26.10.2018 is hereby rescinded with effect from 31st of  December
2018.
