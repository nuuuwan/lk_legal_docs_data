1A – G 29456– 16(02/2019)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land  in total extent of about 0.1595 Hectare
depicted  in the advance tracing No. MA/YTT/2017//250 dated
12.12.2017 prepared by Surveyor General situated in the village
called Dambarawa in Grama Niladhari Division of Dangan Place
(E 337B) of Asgiri Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor Division in the  Divisional
Secretary's  Division of  Yatawatta in Matale District of  Central
Province and bounded as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’ – Dangan watta – Extent: 0.1595  Hectare
North: Road (RDA) and Dangan watta in which the Bungalow  of
Divisional Secretary Yatawatta; East: Dangan watta in which the
Bungalow  of Divisional Secretary Yatawatta and Dangan watta of
which the Public well is Situated;  South: Dangan watta of  which the
Public well is situated and Road; West: Road and Road (RDA).
                                                Gayantha Karunatilleke,
