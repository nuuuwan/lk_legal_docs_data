THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of below mentioned lands depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. CO/MHR/2018/28 and dated 22.02.2018, prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Arawwala (within
the Urban Council of Maharagama in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Maharagama in Colombo District in Western Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Extent: 0.0100 Hectare
North: Lot No. 3 and Road; East: Kekunagahawatta and
Delgahawatta claimed by P. A. D. C. J. Periyapperuma and others;

P. A. D. C. J. Periyapperuma and others, Lot Nos. 3 and 2; West: Lot
Nos. 3, 2 and Road.
02. Lot No. 2 – Kekunagahawatta and Delgahawatta – Extent:
0.0018 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 3 and 1; East: Lot Nos. 1 and 2; South and West:
Lot No. 3.
