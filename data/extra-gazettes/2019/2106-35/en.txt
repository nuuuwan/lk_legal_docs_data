THE  LAND  ACQUISITION   (AMENDMENT)  ACT,
No.  28  OF  1964  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: KDS/MDB/4/2/1/64/06.
Ministry of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms Ref. No.: L13J/96/FR/178.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Purpose of acquisition: Knuckles Range Reservation :
Two allotments of land called Dothulukanda in total extent
of about 2.6135 Hectares depicted as lots 02 and 03 in the
Preliminary Plan No. P .P . Maha 6733 Dated 22.02.2018 prepared
by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys - Kandy District,
situated in the Village called Rambukpotha in the Grama
Niladhari No. 807 of  Rambukpotha in the Udasiyapattuwa
South Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Medadumbara in Kandy District of the Central
Province and more fully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 02
Name of the land: Dothulukanda; Description: Jungle;
Claimant: H. M. W . G . T. B. Herath Ekanayake, Rambukpotha,
Medamahanuwara; Extent: 0.2835 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 03
Name of the land: Dothulukanda; Description: Jungle;
Claimant: H. M. W . G . T. B. Herath Ekanayake, Rambukpotha,
Medamahanuwara; Extent: 2.3300 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agent duly authorized in
writing before me at the Medadumbara Divisional Secretariat
on the 03rd April, 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th day
of March, 2019. The nature of their interest in the aforesaid
land, the particulars of their claims of compensation and the
details of computation of such amount.
A. M. WICKRAMARACHCHI,
Divisional Secretary,
Medadumbara.
At Divisional Secretariat,
Medadumbara,
14th January, 2019.
