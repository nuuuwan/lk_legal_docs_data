SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rates specified
in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of thirty one days commencing from July 11, 2019, subject to provisions of
Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2111/58 of 24.02.2019 and the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2124/2 of 21.05.2019 in respect of Dates (HS Codes 0804.10.10, 0804.10.20) and the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2090/47 of 29.09.2018 in respect of Oranges (HS Codes 0805.10.10) an Apples (HS Codes 0808.10)
are rescinded with effect from July 11, 2019.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
July 10, 2019.
