No. 2141/9 –  TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER  17,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 
D. S.’s Reference . No.: ELDS/LND/04/ACQ/2014-06. 
Land  Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/8/2016/HA/284. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the land Acquisition Act, as amended by Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public  purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired : 
 
Situated in the Village of Kahawatta in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of Kahawatta (189A), in Pallepaththu 
of the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Elapatha, in 
Ratnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
Plan No. F. V. P. 58, Supplement No. 11, Sheet No. 
12. 
 
Lot No. 232 - Name of the land: Gangagawawatta; 
Extent: 0.0126 Hectare. 
Bounded by 
North : Lot No. 9; East : Lot No. 9; South:  Lot No. 9;  
West: Lot No. 9. 
 
Claimed by: Konara Mudiyanselage Subasinghe, 
Kahawatta, Dambuluwana. 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
are hereby  required to appear personally, or by agent 
duly authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Elapatha at 10.30 a. m. On the 16th day of 
December 2019. 
 
Further, they should notify me in writing and in 
duplicate before the 09th December 2019, the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the  amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. Documents should be 
submitted to me on the same date to prove the interests.
