The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   JAFFNA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Jaffna Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Ajantha Thanabalasingam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned member of  Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Deisy Philip Jeyaranjan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Jaffna Municipal Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Deisy
Philip Jeyaranjan was selected as the member of the Jaffna Municipal Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
04th June, 2019.
