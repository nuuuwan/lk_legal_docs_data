THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of land called as Galgahaowita in extent of about
0.0102 Hectare, depicted as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing No. GA/
NYG/2017/903 and dated 11.10.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Pitigala (F.V .P. 592) in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Niyagama in Galle District in Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galgahaovita – Extent: About 0.0102 Hectare
North: Galgahaovita claimed by W. D. M. Pradeep; East:
Galgahaovita claimed by W. D. M. Pradeep; South: Road and
Galgahaovita claimed by W. D. M. Pradeep; West: Galgahaovita
claimed by W. D. M. Pradeep.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
