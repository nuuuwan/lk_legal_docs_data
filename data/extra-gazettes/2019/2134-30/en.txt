CORRECTION
ORDER   UNDER   PRADESHIYA   SABHAS   ACT
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section
18 of the Interpretation Ordinance, Chapter 12, I, Wajira Abeywardana, Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial
Councils & Local Government, do by this Order amend the English translation of the Order published in the page
No. 107A of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2043/56 dated 02nd November 2017 by substitution of the
words, “Maritimepattu Pradeshiya Sabha ” for the words “Muhudubadapattu Pradeshiya Sabha”.
W
AJIRA ABEYWARDANA,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and
Provincial Councils & Local Government.
Colombo,
15th July 2019.
