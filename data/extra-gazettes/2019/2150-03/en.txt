THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
 AS  AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION

Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: DIVKE /LND /LND2 /ACQUT/5/7.
: 4-3/8/2016/PC/509.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No.28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following Land/Servitude
which is required  for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
The allotment of land referred in the sheet No. 5 of additional
No.5 of F.V .P . 2563, in Hodiwewa village in 1128 Madalpola
Grama Niladhari Division in Kuliyapitiya - East Divisional
Secretary Division in Kurunegala District of North Western
Province.
1. Lot No. 88
Name of the Land: Attikkagahamula, Katakalagahamula
waththa, Kongahamulawaththa; Description of Land:
Waththa, A permanent building and a well, constructed using
cement; Claimed by: Punchibanda H.M. and Hereth H.M.S.K,
reside at Alahitiyawa, Hodiwewa; Extent: 0.0149 hec.
North by: Lots No. 37 and 36;  East by: Lot 36; South by:
Lots No.36 and 37; West by:  Lot 37.
All persons interested, in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be  acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Kuliyapitiya -East Divisional Secretariat, on the day of
the 22nd January 2020   at 10.30 a.m.
At 15th January 2020    hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the day of the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ servitude,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
