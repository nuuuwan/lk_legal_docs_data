STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mrs. N. Shilia Shanthini is entitled from Sri Lankan Airlines Limited, the salaries and other entitlements relevant to the period from
17.01.2011, the last day on which she should have been reinstated in service in accordance with the Labour Tribunal order, up to 02.11.2015,
the date on which she was actually reinstated in service, and if so the way in which the calculation should be done.
