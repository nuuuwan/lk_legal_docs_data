 SAMURDHI  ACT,  No.  1,  2013  AMENDED  BY  DIVINEGUMA  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  2,  2017
Regulation under Section No. 8
AS per the power vested by me by Section No. 8 of Samurdhi Act, No. 1, 2013 amended by Divineguma (Amendment)
Act, No. 2, 2017, I, Daya Gamage, Minister of Primary Industries and Social Empowerment establish the administration
zones specified in Schedule II instead of the administration zones given below in Schedule I published in the Schedule of
the Gazette Extraordinary of Democratic Socialist Republic  of Sri Lanka No. 1844/69 and 10th Friday of January
2014, to coform that the provisions of above Act are being implementing in effectively and efficiently, by this Order.
D
AYA G AMAGE,
Minister of Primary Industries and
Social Empowerment.
at Colombo,
On the date of 16th October, 2019.
