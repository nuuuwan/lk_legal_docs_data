Order under Section 4 (1)
WHEREAS an industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between,
G. Daya Hemantha Silva, ‘‘Hemantha’’, Galle Road, Kuda Waskaduwa, Waskaduwa of the one part
and
Lanka Salu Sala Limited, No. 93, Jawatta Road, Colombo 05of the other part
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers
vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised
Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes-Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. R. S. Ariyapperuma, No. 03, Araliya Mawatha, Sirimal Uyana, Ratmalana to be the arbitrator
and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo,
23rd May, 2019.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. G. Daya Hemantha who had retired on the V oluntary Retirement Scheme after working at Lanka Salu Sala Limited
as the Senior Administration Manager had been caused injustice by the deduction of a sum of Rupees two hundred nineteen
thousand one hundred and thirty seven cents ninety (Rs. 219,137.90) from the Compensation and Gratuity paid to him at his
retirement and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo, this 10th day of May, 2019.
