PART I: S EC. (I) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 19.07.2019
1A
Land Reforms Act, No. 01 of 1972 of the National State Assembly
By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27 A (4) of the Land Reform Law No. 01 of 1972 of the National State
Assembly as amended by the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 and to be read together with Sub Article (2)
of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands
and Parliamentary Reforms of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby:
(a) The allotment of land published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 403/18 dated 30th May 1986 in total extent of
282 acres 02 roods 28 perches depicted in the Plan No. Gam 148 prepared by the Licensed Surveyor, Mr. Ranasinghe
Thambugala on 30.09.1983, more fully described in the First Schedule hereto and alienated to the National Housing
Development Authority, established by the Act, No. 17 of 1979 as amended by the Acts, No. 5 of 1982, No. 20 of 1988,
No. 30 of 1999, No. 23 of 2002, No. 32 of 2003, is the land called Pokunawala Watta alias Ran Pokuna Watta, which has
been alienated under Sub Section (1) of Section 27a of the Land Reform Law.
(b) Amend that order by revoking the aforesaid orders made for divesting the allotment of private land in extent of 01 acre
01 rood 11 perches claimed by Kosala Samith Liyanage relatitng to the land called and known as Pokunawala Watta
alias Ran Pokuna Watta more fully described in the 2nd Schedule, identified as included in the aforesaid land
described more fully in the First Schedule hereto.
