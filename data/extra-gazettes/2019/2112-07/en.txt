PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 25.02.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Ma/GLW/2016/395 and dated 12.04.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Nabadagahawatta in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. E 431 E Nabadagahawatta in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Galewela in Matale District in Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Indigolla Jungle – Extent: 0.0130 Hectare
North: Canal and Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown; East:
Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown, Indigolla claimed by
M. H. M. Nausar and Lot B; South: Lot B, Indigolla claimed by
M. H. M. Nausar and Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown; West:
Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown and Canal.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Indigolla Paddyfield – Extent: 0.0746 Hectare
North: Lot A, Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown and Indigolla
claimed by M. H. M. Nausar; East and South: Indigolla claimed by
M. H. M. Nausar; West: Indigolla claimed by M. H. M. Nausar,
Indigolla claimed by claimant unknown and Canal.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
