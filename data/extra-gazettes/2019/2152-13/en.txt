EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 09/2019
EXCISE   DUTY ON   LOCALLY   PRODUCED   WINE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to time,
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development do by this Order direct that, with effect
from December 03, 2019.
(1) There shall be levied on liquors produced only from local plant materials or plant products or potable local
animal milk, containing not more than 18% by volume of alcohol manufactured by process other than distillation
in Sri Lanka shall be levied a duty rate of Rupees Nine Hundred (Rs. 900.00) per litre of alcohol, that such duty
shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid liquor, which is exported out of the Island ;
(2) The Excise Notification No. 03/2019 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2113/8 of 05 March 2019 is
hereby rescinded.
M
AHINDA R AJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance, Economic and Policy Development.
Ministry of Finance, Economic and Policy Development,
Colombo 01,
December 03, 2019.
