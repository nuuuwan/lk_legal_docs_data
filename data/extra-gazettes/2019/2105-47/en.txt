THE  FINANCE   ACT,   No.   35   OF   2018
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 41 (3) read with Section 51 of the  Finance Act, No. 35 of  2018,  I, Mangala
Samaraweera,   Minister of Finance and Mass Media,  do by this Order  prescribe the following regulations.
MANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of  Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
11th  January, 2019.
REGULATIONS
1.  These regulations may be cited as the Finance (Carbon Tax) Regulations, No. 1 of 2019.
2. The Carbon Tax (hereinafter referret  to as ‘‘ the tax’’), shall be paid by the registered owner of any relevant Motor
vehicle and collected by the Divisional Secretary in accordance with the Part XI of the Finance Act, No. 35 of 2018. If the
renewal of annual registration of the motor vehicle is done by Provincial Commisioner of Motor Traffic, the tax shall be
collected by such Commissioners at the time of  renewal of  annual registration of the motor vehicle.
In the case of online renewal of annual registration of moter vehicle, the tax shall be paid by the registered
owner of the  relevant moter vehicle separately to the Divisional Secretary or Provincial Commissioner of Motor Traffic
as the case may be.
3. Any Divisional Secretary or Provincial Commissioner of Motor Traffic who collects the tax, as the case may be,
shall remit the amounts collected in any month as per the paragraph 2 above, to the credit of the Consolidated Fund, on or
before 10th day of  the following month.
4. Any Divisional Secretary or Provincial Commissioner of Motor Traffic shall submit a monthly statement of
revenue on the tax collected and remitted to the Consolidated Fund to the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic.
