FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   UDUBADDAWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of   Not Attending
for council meetings period of three months without  permisson ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Alpenerisge
Hasini Sandathaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned  member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thamma Hetti Mudalige Agnas Jasintha Peiris to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the  Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Thamma Hetti Mudalige Agnas Jasintha Peiris  was selected as the member of the Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE D ILIP N ISANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Udubaddawa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
23rd July, 2019.
