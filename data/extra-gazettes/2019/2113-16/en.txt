Regulations
1.  These Regulations may be cited as the Food (Preservatives) Regulation, 2019, and shall come into operation
from 01.01.2020.
2.  No person shall manufacture, import, store, transport, sell, offer for sale, distribute, use or advertise -
(a) any food preservative other than the preservatives specified in Schedule I hereto ;
(b) any food which has in or upon it any preservative, other than those specified in Schedule II hereto.

3. Any food specified in Column I of Schedule II (hereinafter referred to as a “specified food”) may have in or
upon it, a preservative of the description specified in the corresponding entry of Column II therof (hereinafter referred to
as a “permitted preservative”) and the use of such preservative shall be in compliance with the proportions set out in the
corresponding entry of Column III thereof.
4. Any specified food may have in or upon it, a mixture of two or more preservatives specified in Schedule II
hereto, only if the sum total of the preservatives does not exceed one hundred percentage when the quantity of each of such
preservative present in such food is expressed as a percentage of the maximum quantity of that preservative appropriate  to
the food as specified in that Schedule.
5. Any compounded food may contain any permitted preservative -
(a) introduced in the process of preparing such food using one or more specified foods, if the permitted
preservative :-
(i) is present in any specified food used in the compounded food ; and
(ii) is present in the specified food used, in no greater proportion than the proportion specified in relation
to the proportion specified in Column III of Schedule II.
(b) if the compounded food is a specified food and does not contain any permitted preservatives in any greater
proportion than is specified in Column III thereto.
6. No person shall manufacture, import, store, transport, sell, offer for sale, distribute, use or advertise any permitted
preservative (including any permitted preservative mixed with any other substance) for use as an ingredient in the preparation
of food-
(a) unless such preservative conforms to the standards of purity prescribed by the standards prescribed by the
Combine Compendium of Food Additives Monographs by JECFA of the Food an Agricultural Organization
- (FAO)
(b) except in a container bearing a label in accordance with the requirements contained in paragraphs (1) and
(2) of Schdeule III hereto.
7. All proporations mentioned in these regulations are proportions calculated by the weight of the product unless
a contrary intention is expressed.
8. The label on  every container or package of food, to which a permitted preservative as specified in these regulations
has been added, shall have a declaration as set out in the Food (Labelling and Advertising) Regulation 2005 published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1376/9 of January 19, 2005 as amended from time to time or replaced.
9. The Food (Preservatives) Regulations 1989 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 615/11 of 19th June
1990 are hereby rescinded.
10. In these Regulations -
“Preservative” means a food additive, which prolongs the shelf-life of a food by protecting against
deterioration caused by microorganisms.
“Compounded food” means food containing two or more ingredients.

SCHEDULE   III
1. Each container of package to which regulaiton 3 applies shall bear a label indicating a statement -
(a) of each permitted preservative, the common name and INS number ;
(b) where more than one substance is present, the common Games and INS numbers ;
(c) where a permitted preservative is admixed with a diluent the proportions of the diluent and the preservative
present, in the diluted preparation.
2. Any statement required to be printed/attached in terms of paragraph 1-
(a) shall be clear and legible ;
(b) shall be in a conspicuous position on the label which shall be securely attached/printed to the container or
package in such a manner that the statement shall be easily read and understood by a prospective buyer ;
(c) shall not be in any way hidden or obscured by any other matter.
