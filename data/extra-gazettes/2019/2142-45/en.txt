The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   UHANA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Henakaralalage
Senarath Bandara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the
Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Horathal Pedige Priyantha Sisira Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of  the  Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Horathal Pedige Priyantha Sisira Kumara was selected as the member of the Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
ILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Uhana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
24th September, 2019.
