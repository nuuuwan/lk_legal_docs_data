SAMURDHI   ACT,  No. 1  OF  2013
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Primary Industries and Social Empowerment under the Section 42 of Samurdhi
Act, No. 1, 2013.
DAYA G AMAGE,
Minister of Primary Industries and Social Empowerment.
10th September, 2019,
Colombo.
1. These Regulations are called Samurdhi Regulations No. 1 of 2019
2. Director General of Department of Samurdhi should take necessary actions to recruit Computer Assistants and
pay appropriate remuneration to them by using fund of relevant Samurdhi Community Based Bank and Samurdhi
Community Based Bank Society to implement the Samurdhi Banks and Bank Societies computerizing programme
for Samurdhi Community Based Banks established under Section 25(1) and Samurdhi Community Based Bank
Societies established under Section No. 29(1) of Samurdhi Act, No. 1, 2013 and these actions should be taken
under the approval of the Board of Management of the Samurdhi Community Based Banks and Samurdhi Community
Based Bank Society established under Section 33(1) of Samurdhi Act.
