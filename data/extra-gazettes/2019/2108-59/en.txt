No. 2108/59 – THURSDAY, JANUARY 31, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Below mentioned allotment of land depicted as Lot ‘A ’ in extent
of 3.5525 Hectares in the Progression Tracing No. R/RTN/2017/372
and dated 14.05.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the Village of Medagama in the Minor Division of Kuruwita Korale
Uda Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ratnapura in
Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast Estate –
Extent: 3.5525 Hectares
North: Beri-Feras Estate  alias Beri Farast Estate claimed by
Embilipitiya Estate Cooperation, Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast
Estate claimed by Sirisena Herath and New Malwala Estate claimed
by D. W. Kusumawathi; East: Beri-Feras Estate  alias Beri Farast
Estate claimed by Sirisena Herath, New Malwala Estate claimed by
I. M. Thilakasiri and D. W. Kusumawathi and Beri-Feras Estate
alias Beri Farast Estate that claimant not found; South: New Malwala
Estate claimed by I. M. Thilakasiri and D. W. Kusumawathi,
Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast Estate that claimant not found
and Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast Estate claimed by Embilipitiya
Estate Cooperation; West: Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast Estate
and Beri-Feras Estate alias Beri Farast Estate claimed by Embilipitiya
Estate Cooperation.
