THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35  of
May 28, 2009 as last amended by Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 2091/6 of  October  01, 2018,  which
specifies the Faculties and  Departments of the University of   Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue
of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University  established under Section 21 of the said Act, as
follows :-
(1) by the repeal of  entry ‘‘17’’ appearing in Column II  which corresponds to  item ‘‘ Faculty of Medical
Sciences”  in  Column I  thereof; and
