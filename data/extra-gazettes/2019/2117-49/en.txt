SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sections 32 and 33
I, Harin Fernando, the Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports in pursuance of powers vested in
me  under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973,  do hereby suspend the registration  of the Sri Lanka
Netball Federation, the National Association for netball with immediate effect and appoint the following interim committee
to continue the activities of the above Netball Federation, as a provisional arrangement.
1. Mitimanage Yamuna Perera - President
2. Ranjith Wimalasooriya - Secretary
3. Edirisinghe Dewage Asha Lakmali Edirisinghe - Treasurer
Moreover, the Director General of the Department of Sports Development is delegated with power, in terms of
the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 and amended regulations thereto, for convening a special general meeting and thereafter
appoint an Election Committee and conduct the election of Sri Lanka Netball Federation, under the supervision of the
Director General.
H
ARIN FERNANDO (M. P.),
Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign
Employment and Sports.
03rd April, 2019.
