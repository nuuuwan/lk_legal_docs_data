THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
I, hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude* which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired :
An allotment of land in total Extent of 0.0023 Hectares
depicted as Lot No. 118 in Sheet No. 02, Zone No. 10 of
Cadastral Map. No. 810039 made by the Surveyor General
situated in the Village of Madawalamulla in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 98A, Madawalamulla North, within Municipal
Council Limits, in the Divisional Secretariat, Galle - Four
Gravets, District of Galle, Southern Province. The details of
the land are as follows:
Lot No. 118 – Name of the Land: Dinginaidegewatta; Land
Use: Home Garden, Permanent Building (Motor Room);
Claimant:  State; Original Claimant: Couldn't find; Extent :
0.0023 Hectare.
North: Lot No. 119; East: Lot No. 119 and Lot No. 62; South:
Lot No. 62 and Lot No. 60; West: Lot No. 60.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Galle Four Gravets on the 20th day of
November, 2019 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13th November,
2019 the nature of their interests in the land the particulars of
their claims for compensation and the details of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
I. HIMALI RATHNAWEERA,
Divisional Secretary,
Galle - Four Gravets.
Galle-Four Gravets Divisional Secretariat,
30th September, 2019.
