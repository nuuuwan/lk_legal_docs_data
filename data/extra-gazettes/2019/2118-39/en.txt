FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KURUNEGALA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return  member of  Kurunegala Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of loss of seat
or office ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Krishnapalan Thiyagarajah the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Brahakmana Arachchige Susantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Brahakmana Arachchige Susantha was selected as the member of the Kurunegala Municipal Council.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
08th April, 2019.
