THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotments of  land depicted in the Advance Tracing Plan
No.  KE/MWN/2018/119  and dated 28.09.2018 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated at the Villages of Wadiyathenna and
Madulbowa in No. 39A Wadiyathenna Grama Niladhari Division
and No. 39B, Madulbowa Grama Niladhari Division respectively, in
Mahapalatha  Paththu Minor Division of the Divisional Secretariat
Division of Mawanella in Kegalle District, Sabaragamuwa Province
and bounded by
:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kalotuwanehena – Extent: 0.0023 Hectare
North: Kalotuwanehena; East: Kehelpannala Oya and Lot B; South:
Lot B; West: Road (R.D.A.).

North: Lot A and Kalotuwanehena; East: Kehelpannala Oya; South:
Lot C and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.).

North: Lot C and Kehelpannala Oya; East: Kehelpannala Oya and

and Lot D.
