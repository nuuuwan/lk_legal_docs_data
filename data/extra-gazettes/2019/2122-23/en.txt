CONSULAR  FUNCTIONS  ACT,   No.  4  OF  1981
Notification  under Section  8
I, Tilak Marapana, Minister of Foreign Affairs in terms of the powers vested in me under Section 8 of the Consular
Functions Act, No. 04 of 1981, do by this notification specify that, with effect from 02.05.2019, the Ceylon Chamber of
Commerce will be authorized to charge the fees prescribed for the attestation by the Consular Affairs Division of the
Ministry of Foreign Affairs, acting as consular officers.
T
ILAK M ARAPANA,
Minister of Foreign Affairs.
Colombo,
05th May, 2019.
