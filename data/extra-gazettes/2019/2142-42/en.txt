The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   TANGALLE  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’ s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Loku
Yaddehige Pemadasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Yakdehige Ajith Nishantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the  Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Yakdehige Ajith Nishantha was selected as the member of the Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
A VINDRA S UBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambantota,
23rd September, 2019.
