NOTIFICATION   BY   MR.  SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Monday, the 11th of
November, 2019 at 11.30 a. m.
I, Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order 16 of the Standing Orders
of Parliament, I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Monday the 11th of November 2019 at 11.30 a. m. having
been requested so to do by the Hon. Prime Minister.
K
ARU J AYASURIYA,
Speaker.
Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte,
07th November, 2019.
