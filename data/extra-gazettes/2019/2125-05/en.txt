FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BATTICALOA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front,  the recognized political party to which
Sivalingam Somasuntharam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member if Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party , and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Vadivel Kuberan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Vadivel
Kuberan  was selected as the member of the Batticaloa Municipal Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
24th May, 2019.
