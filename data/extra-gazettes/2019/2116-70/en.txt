THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER 460)  AS
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No.: 3/3/6/2/16.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land which are required for a
public purpose.
Land Acquisition for Katudeniya Clinical Center .
Lot No. 01 and 02 in total extent of above 0.0441 Hectare of
lands in preminary plan mu/p/ma/1763 prepared by senior
superintendent of Matale, situated at Katudeniya Village in
Katudeniya Grama Niladhari Division, of Minor Division in
Matale Udasiyapaththu of Divisional Secretary Division
Ukuwela in the District of Matale Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Claimant: Land Reforems Commission; Extent: 0.0017
Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Claimant: Land Reforems Commission; Extent: 0.0424
Hectare.
All person interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
waiting before me 9.30 a.m. on 24.07.2019 at Divisional
Secretariat Ukuwela.
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) before the 03rd day of July 2019, the nature of their
interest in the lands, the particulars of their claims of
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of the compensation
and the details of the compensation of such amount.
E. W. N. A. EGODAWELA,
Divisional Secretariet,
Ukuwela.
At Ukuwela Divisional Secretariat,
