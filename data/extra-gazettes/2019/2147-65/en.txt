PRESIDENTIAL     SECRETARIAT
CALLING   APPLICATIONS   FOR  THE  APPOINTMENT   OF   PRESIDENT’S   COUNSEL
APPLICATIONS are called for the appointment of President’s Counsel in terms of the Article 33(2)(e) of the Constitution.
The Attorneys-at-Law who have reached eminence in the legal profession and maintained high standards of conduct and
professional rectitude are eligible to apply for the above appointment.
Accordingly, the Attorneys-at-Law who satisfy the eligibility criteria specified in the guidelines published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2147/37 dated 29.10.2019 should send the duly completed applications on the from given
below under  registered cover to “Secretary to the President, Presidential Secretariat, Colombo 01” on or before
07.11.2019.
The legend “Appointment of President’s Counsel” should be clearly written on the top left corner of the envelope
containing the applications
Any applications received after the said date will not be entertained and the Presidential Secretariat will not be
held responsible for any misplaced or delayed applications.
Applications can be downloaded via www.presidentsoffice.gov.lk.
                              By Order of His Excellency the President,
U
DAYA R. S ENEVIRATHNE,
Secretary to the President.
Proclamations & c., by the President
