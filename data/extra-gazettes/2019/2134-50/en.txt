PART I:S EC.(I) - GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY  OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 02.08.2019
1. These regulations are called as Botanic Gardens (Admission Fees) of 2019 and shall be implemented with effect
from 05th August, 2019.
2. The amount of the fees levied to enter person and vehicles to any Botanic Gardens shall be as mentioned in the
schedule here.
Even though the below mentioned persons will be exempted from paying this entrance fee :
A. Visitors of Sri Lankan Republic
B. Dwellers of the residences within the gardens.
C. Government servants who enter into the gardens for official duties.
D. Person who are granted free admission to the Director General.
Provided further in relation to the Botanic Gardens where the vehicles are not permitted, no vehicle other
than vehicles permitted by curator of Botanic Gardens shall be permitted to enter such Botanic Gardens.
3. The admission to Botanic Gardens Regulation published to Gazette Extraordinary  No.1685/17 of 22.12.2010
and amended by the GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY No.1700/10 of 06.04.2011  & by the Gazette Extraordinary  No.1969  of
27.05.2016 is hereby rescinded.
Schedule
01. Persons ( for one person)
(A) Sri Lankans:-
1. Adults and children over twelve (12) years of Age Rs.100.00
2. Children below twelve (12) years of Age    and school children Rs.  20.00
3. Adults over the age of sixty (60) on producing National Identity Card Rs.  20.00
(B) Foreigners :-
1. Adults and children over twelve (12) years of Age Rs. 2,000.00
2. Children below twelve (12) years of Age Rs. 1,000.00
3. School Children & University students  ( Upon producing of  Satisfactory
proof of the same to the curator of the Botanic Gardens) Rs.1,200.00
02. Vehicles  ( each vehicle)
1. Cars, Jeeps ,Station Wagons  and Three wheelers Rs. 250.00
2. Motor Bicycles and Motor Scooters Rs. 100.00
3. Foot Cycles  Rs.  50.00
03. For ride boats at the boat yards of Gampaha (Henerathgoda) Botanic Gardens and
Seethawaka Wet Zone Botanic Gardens
Limited to half an hour for two persons Rs. 300.00
04. Charges for Battery cars
1.  08 Seater Battery  car Rs. 2,000.00
2.  03 Seater Battery  car Rs. 1,000.00
