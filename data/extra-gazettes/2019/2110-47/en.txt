LIVESTOCK DEVELOPMENT, IRRIGATION AND FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT
TO STATE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE,  IRRIGATION AND RURAL ECONOMIC AFFAIRS
I hereby declare that out of the powers and functions vested in me in my capacity as the Minister of Agriculture,Rural
Economic Affairs, Livestock Development, Irrigation and Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, powers and functions specified in the following schedule are delegated to the State Minister
of Agriculture, Irrigation and Rural Economic AffairsWasantha  Lakshminath  Aluwihare in terms of provisions of the
Article 44(5) of the Constitution and he has the opportunity of inquiring, mediating and co-ordinating all functions com-
