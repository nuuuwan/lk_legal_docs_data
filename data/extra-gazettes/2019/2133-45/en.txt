EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under  Section 07 
 
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms Reference 
No. : 13/98/PC/200. 
Divisional Secretary Reference No.: BEN/2/7/3/15/II. 
 
THE notice is hereby given under the Sub section (01) in 
the Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act (Amended)  
No. 28 of 1964 as the Government intends to acquire the 
below mentioned land/Servitude for a public purpose. 
 
The description of the land to be Acquired : 
 
The lots No. 01 & 02 called as Kuruntuwatta in two 
lands of the Preliminary Plan bearing No.: ගා 4322 of the 
Surveyor General, located at the Induruwa village, within 
the Grama Niladhari Division 8 C - Athuruwella, of the 
Bentota Divisional Secretariat Division, in Galle District, 
Southern Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Kuruntuwatta; Details: Cemetery; 
Extent: 0.0791 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of the land: Kuruntuwatta; Details: Cemetery; 
Extent: 0.1241 Hectare. 
 
The all claimants and people who have ownership or 
other relation to the aforementioned land themselves or 
the people who are authorized by their documents should 
be appeared before me at 10.00 a.m. on 25th, September, 
2019, at the Divisional Secretariat, Bentota. 
 
Further, the nature of their ownership related to the 
mentioned land, details on the facts for claiming 
compensation regarding the acquiring that land, the 
amount of compensation and the way it is calculated shall 
be received to me by duly completed copies prior to the 
18.09.2019. 
 
 W. W. K. VITHANAGE, 
  Divisional  Secretary,  
 Bentota.  
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, Bentota. 
22nd of July, 2019.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice Under  Section 07 
 
 
Land Ministry's No. : 4-3/8/2017/PC/515. 
My Reference No.: BER 4/5/2/58. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land  
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land or Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The lot No. 01 in Surveyor General’s Plan No. ක 4757 
is situated at village of Aluthgama in Grama Niladhari’s 
Division of 765-Aluthgama East in Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Beruwala in Kaluthara District, Western 
Province.  
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Dehigahawatta; Description: 
Acquisition for K/Sanghamiththa Balika Vidyalaya; 
Claimant: Sembakutti Kankanamge Samson Fernando; 
Extent: 0.0885 Hectare. 
 
Description of the Land Marks : 
North : Road and Dehigahawatta claimed by B. 
Indunil Fernando and Road; East : Dehigahawatta claimed 
by B. Indunil Fernando, Road and Walipanna Road; 
South : Walipanna Road and Sellagewatta claimed by 
Sangamiththa Balika Vidyalaya; West : Sellagewaththa 
Possess by Sangamiththa  Balika Vidyalaya and 
Lindamulagewatta (Lot No. 1 of Plan No. P.P.K. 2386). 
 
 
 
  
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby  required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretariat of Beruwala on the 
26th day of August 2019 at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or  
before the 19th day of August 2019, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the  amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
 
 
 
 K. G. D. CHATHURA MALRAJ , 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Beruwala. 
 
 
 
 
 
At Divisional Secretariat of Beruwala, 
10th July 2019.
