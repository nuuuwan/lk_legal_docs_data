FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (1a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  in Maharagama  Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  Independent Group No. 02, to which Lakmini Thushari Gunawardhana the member
elected to Ward No. 11 Kottawa West of  Maharagama Urban Council belonged, was required by me under Section
66A (1) (a) of  the aforesaid Ordinance, to  decide upon the member of  that Ward No. 11 Kottawa West of  Maharagama
Urban Council who belongs to such Independent Group  and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under
Section 8 of the Local authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of aforesaid  Independent Group No. 02 has Communicated his decision to nominate
Ganegoda Haputhanthrige Sarojini  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the Maharagama
Urban Council,  acting in terms of Section 66A(1) (1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Ganegoda Haputhanthrige Sarojini   was elected as the member of the Ward No. 11 Kottawa West of
Maharagama  Urban Council.
S.D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama  Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
25th February 2019.
