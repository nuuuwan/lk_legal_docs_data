I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section 7 of  Land Acquisition
Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act,
No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends to acquire the
following land/servitude which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired :
The allotment of Land described below depicted in
supplementary plan No. 44 of  F.V .P . No. 87 prepared by

secretary's division of Athuraliya in Gangbada Paththu in the
Matara District.
Lot No. 669
Name of the land: Kampatamulla; Particulars of the land:
Garden, Mixed Cultivation aged 20-25 years & 7 permanent
Building situated; Claimant: Land Reform Commission P .O. Box
1526, No. G .82 Hector Kobbekaduwa MW , Colombo 07; Extent:
1.5455 Hectares.
Lot No. 700
Name of the land: Kampatamulla; Particulars of the land:
Road; Claimant: Land Reform Commission P .O. Box 1526, No.
G .82 Hector Kobbekaduwa MW, Colombo 07; Extent: 0.0750
Hectare.
Lot No. 701
Name of the land: Kampatamulla; Particulars of the land:
Garden, Mixed Cultivation aged 10-15; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission P .O. Box 1526, No. G .82 Hector Kobbekaduwa
MW, Colombo 07; Extent: 0.2631 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing to appear
before me at Athuraliya Divisional secretary, on the 25th day
of March, 2019, 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18.03.2019 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
