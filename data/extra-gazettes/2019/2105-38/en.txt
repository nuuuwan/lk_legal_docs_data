 THE  SPORTS  LAW,  No.  25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS  made by Minister of Sports under Section 41 read with Section 31 of Sport Law 25 of 1973 and its amendments.
HARIN F ERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
09th January, 2019.
Regulations
The National Associations of Sports Regulations, No. 01 of 2016 published in the Gazette Extroardinary No. 1990/
23 dated 27.10.2016, No. 2006/13 dated 13.02.2017, No. 2017/41 dated 05.05.2017, No. 2089/56 dated 22.09.2018 and
No. 2093/39 dated 18.10.2018 are hereby amended as follows :
1. By deletion of Regulation 3(d) of  Gazette No. 2089/56 dated 22.09.2018 thereof and should be substituted as
follows :
“3(d) On appointment of the Election Committee as stated above, nominations shall be called after a period of
two months from the said date of appointment of the Election Committee”
2. By deletion of regulation 8(2) of Gazette No. 2089/56 dated 22.09.2018 thereof and should be subtituted as follows :
‘‘8(2) Minister in charge of the subject of Sports, after considering the appeal shall submit the said appeal to an
Advisory Board comprising of 03 members headed by a retired Judge of the Supreme Court or retired Judge of
the Appeal Court along with two retired senior public officials to Grade ‘A’ and ‘B’ Category Association, and
three retired sSenior Public Officials relating to Grade ‘C’ Category Association and the Committee shall convey its
decision on such appeal in writing to the Minister in charge of the subject of Sports, within one week (07 days)
of the referring of the same for its review.”
