THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and the land will be acquired under provisions of that
Act.
An Allotment of the land described as the following names and
extents, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/KND/2018/143
and dated 31.07.2018, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in
the Village of Lunuketiya Madiththa in Udugampaha Korale Minor
Division, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kundasale, in
Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena – Extent: 0.1391
Hectares
North: Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by N. M. Ariyarathne
and Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by D. G . M. D. Wijesiri;
East: Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by D. G . M. D. Wijesiri
and Road; South: Road and Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by
M. Ariyarathne; West: Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by
M. Ariyarathne and Lunuketiya Madiththe Hena claimed by N. M.
Ariyarathne
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
