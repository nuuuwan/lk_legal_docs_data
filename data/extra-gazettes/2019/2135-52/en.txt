FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V A VUNIYA SOUTH (TAMIL)
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) (a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Vavuniya  South (Tamil)  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Nahur Pichchai
Jawahir  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the
member of  that ward 06 Salambeikulam if Vavuniya South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Subramaniyam Ganesan  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Jesuthasan Jeniton, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya  South (Tamil) Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Subramaniyam Ganesan was elected as the member of the Ward 06 Salambeikulam of  Vavuniya  South (Tamil)  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
J.  J
ENITON,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya  South (Tamil)  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
07th August, 2019.
