No. 2126/38  - THURSDAY  JUNE  06, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   THUMPANE  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council meetings for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Kandanhena Yamanage Manjula Dinesh Yamanage the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon   member of that Number 05 Bananga if Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Dayarathna Banda to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the  Thumpane
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that  Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Dayarathna Banda was elected as the member of the  No. 5 Bananga of
Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Thumpane Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
4th June, 2019.
