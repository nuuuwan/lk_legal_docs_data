ESALA  FESTIVAL  OF  MAHIYANGANA  2019
BY virtue or the powers vested in me by the Transfer of Powers (Divisional Secretaries)Act No.58 of 1992,and according
to the regulations in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (chapter 362)publish in Government Gazette
No.9902 of 10th September 1948,Gazette No.10283 of 17th August 1951 Gazette No.14778 of 25lh March 1995 and Gazette
No.14752 of 09th June 1967, I, Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nimal Dissanayaka the Divisional Secretaries of Mahiyangana,
do hereby inform the following orders shall be in force with regard to the camp of Mahiyangana Esala Festival 2019.
1. It is announced, the Mahiyangana Esala Festival will be commenced on the 30th August 2019 in the closure of the
event will be followed by the “Diya Kepeema” ceremony on the 14th September 2019.
2. The camp are for abode of pilgrims shall be the land demarcated by the 37 th Mile post of Mahiyangana-Bibila
main road up to the 1/39 culvert in front of Mahiyangana National School,and the land situated between the
Mahiyangana-Meegahakiula road the leading to the clock tower in front of Mahiyangana hospital and new highway.
3. No person shall cook meals in “Vihara Maluwa’,'Dewala Boomiya”, “Palle Veediya” (The court yards of the Vihara,Bodi
and Dewala or within those premises.
4. On a permit issues by me or by the officer incharge of the camp, Seelling of flowers, Joss sticks, candles,
camphor, offering itemas and statues for fulfillment of vows,is allowed in the strip of land opposite the “Sthupa”at
“Mada Veediya” and in the strip of Land issued to the left of the “Makara Thorana”.
5. Within the camp area specified in above section 2,selling and distribution of all food to pilgrims and selling of other
commodities and allowed in the 24 permanent building or the Officer in-charge of the camp. The area beyond 20ft,
from the drainage system of both sides of Palle V eediya,Mada V eediya.Dutugamunu Vishrama Shala Mawatha

According to the regulations in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (chapter 362)publish in Government

Gazette No.14752 of 09th June 1967 and the transfer of powers Act No.58 of 1992, I, Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nimal
Dissanayaka the Divisional Secretaries of Mahiyangana,do hereby notify that the following places are allocated for the
purpose of distributing and selling of food items among the pilgrims.
(a) All the permanent and temporary building in between the 37th K.m 39/1 culvert opposite the National
School, along Mahiyangana Bibila road.
(b) Permanent and temporary building on both side Mahiyangana/ Meegahakiula old road up the clock tower in
front of the hospital.
(c) Temporary building construction on a permit issued by me, within the allocated ground for abode of pilgrims
in side the camp area.
No person shall distribute any sort of food item any place not appointed for the purpose, except specified in above .
