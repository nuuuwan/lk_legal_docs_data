THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby declare, under the Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of lands depicted as amended Advanced Tracing No.
NU/AMB/2013/154, dated on 29.11.2016, situated in the Village of
Division No. 1 of Hatton Dikoya Urban Council Limit, in
Ambagamuwa Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Ambagamuwa Korale in Nuwara Eliya District, Central
Province and bounded as follows :
1. Lot ‘A’ – Hatton Poolbank Estate – Extent:  0.0094 Hectare
North: Road (Urban Council); East: Part (B) and Canal; South:
Canal and Hatton Poolbank Estate of claimed by Land Reform
Commission; West: Hatton Poolbank Estate of claimed by Land
Reform Commission and Road (Urban Council).
