THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of below mentioned lands depicted as Lots ‘A’  to
‘M’ (Lots 11) in extent of about 2.1920 Hectares, in the Advance
Tracing No. H/BLA/RDA/2018/258 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys, Hambanthota District on behalf of the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Villages of Nayakawatta,
Gatamanna South in the Minor Division of Giruwa Pattuwa South
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Beliatta in Hambantota
District in Southern Province and bounded as follows:

0.9189 Hectare

Sumanarathana,  Lots K and H; South: Lot H, Proposed Southern
Expressway and Lot D; West: Lot D and Rathuheraliyagahawatta ,
Galpalehena claimed by claimant cannot be found.
