The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
 (CHAPTER 262)  LOCAL  GOVERNMENT  AUTHORITIES ELECTION  ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 66E
I, as per the powers vested in me under the amended Section 66E of the Local Government Authorities Elections Ordinance
of  Number 22 of 2012 Local Government Authorities Elections (amendment) Act, hereby announce that the first meeting
for electing the new Chairman due to the vacancy of the Chairman post in Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya Sabha of the Kandy
District in Central Province, is Scheduled to be held at the exact time, day and the place mentioned in the below Schedule
of this announcement.
H. M. M. U. B. H
ERATH,
Commissioner of Local Governments,
Department of Local Governments,
Central Province.
At Provincial Council Complex,
Pallekele,
Kundasale,
15th March, 2019.
