FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   WELIVITIYA - DIVITHURA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Bandarigodage
Gotabhaya the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Yakgaha Hewage Ajith Induka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the  Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that  Yakgaha Hewage Ajith Induka was selected as the member of the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
28th June, 2019.
