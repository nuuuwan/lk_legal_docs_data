I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of City Planning and Water Supply & Higher Education hereby order under Section 49(A) of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described in below Schedule is needed to be acquired for common amenities of the
Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka.

AUFF H AKEEM,
