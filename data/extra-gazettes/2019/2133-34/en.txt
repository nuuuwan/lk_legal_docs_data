The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VADAMARADCHI  SOUTH
WEST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  V adamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which
Yothiswarasarma Nagaraja Sarma the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that Ward 17 - Thunnalai if Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall
be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mahendiran Pireman to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the  V adamaradchi South West
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that  Mahendiran Pireman  was elected as the member of the Ward 17 - Thunnalai of Vadamaradchi South
West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
23rd July, 2019.
