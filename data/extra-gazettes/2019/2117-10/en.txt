MINISTRY  OF  HOUSING,  CONSTRUCTIONS  AND  CULTURAL  AFFAIRS
Declaring  National  Cinema  Day
BY virtue of the powers vested in me as the Minister in charge of the subject under Section 61.(2) O of National Films
Corporation Act, No. 47 of 1971 amended by Act No. 45 of 1980, I do hereby declare 05th of April, on which the birth
anniversary of Dr. Lester James Peries, veteran film maker, who marked the turning point of the cinema by his work “Rekava”
creating an authentic expression of local cinema and releasing Sri Lankan cinema from the South Indian influence, falls as the
‘National Cinema Day’ in felicitation of the exemplary service rendered by Dr. Lester James Peries.
S
AJITH  PREMADASA,
Minister of Housing, Constructions and Cultural Affairs.
02nd April, 2019.
