1A – G 29396 – 21(02/2019)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of  land called  Thenahandiwatta alias Gankandewatta
alias Mawatawatta in extent of about 0.0374 Hectare, depicted as
Lot  ‘A’  in Advance Tracing   No. GA/HKK/2018/283  dated
05.07.2018  prepared by the Senior Superintend of  Surveyor Galle
District, situated in the Village of Akurala in the Divisional Secretariat
Division  of Hikkaduwa, Galle District  Southern Province are
bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Thenahandiwatta alias Gankandewatta alias
Mawatawatta – Extent: 0.0374  Hectare
North: Mudiyansegewatta claimed by M.N.C.Imalika De Silva
and others and roads (RDA); East: Roads (RDA) Thennahandiwatta
alias Gankandewatta alias Mawathawatta claimed by Rannulu
Kumara de soiza and others; South: Thenahandiwatta alias
Gankandewatta alias Mawathawatta claimed by Rannula Kumara
de soiza and others and the beach; West: The beach and
Mudiyansegewatta claimed by M. N. C. Imalika de Silva and others.
                                                Gayantha Karunatilleke,
