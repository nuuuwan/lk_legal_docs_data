No. 2107/10 –  TUESDAY, JANUARY,  22,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
(Notice Under Section 7) 
 
 My Ref No.: GM/DS/2016/2/2/1/138. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/8/2016/HA/405. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose :- 
 
      Description of the land or  servitude to be acquired : 
 
     An allotment of Land in total extent of about 0.0101 
hectare depicted as lot No. 1 in the P.P.GAM. No. 4108 
dated 02.11.2018 prepared by the Senior Superintendent 
of Surveys - Gampaha Districts situated in the village 
called Katuwalamulla, in the Grama Niladhari Division 
No. 244D or Katuwalamulla North, in the Divisional 
Secretary’s division of Gampaha of Gampaha district in 
Western Province and morefully described as follows . 
     
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Gonnagahawatta and 
Ambagahawatta; Description of the Land: Home Garden, 
a permanent building available; Claimants: 
Yathramullage Dharmasiri, No. 52, Katuwalamulla, 
Ganemulla; Extent : 0.0101 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Gampaha Divisional Secretariat on 
the 08th day of May 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 29th day of April 2019 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
   
 K.A.S. Lakshreeni Gunathilake, 
 Divisional Secretary/ Acquiring Officer, 
 Gampaha Division. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Gampaha. 
18th January, 2019.
