FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   CHA V AKACHCHERI   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Sivakunasothy Vallipuram the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Kanthaiya Thurkka  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the  Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Kanthaiya
Thurkka  was selected as the member of the Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
31st  May, 2019.
