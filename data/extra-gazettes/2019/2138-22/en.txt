FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  V ALIKAMAM   WEST     PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group to which Selvarasa Krishnarasa  the member  belonged, was
required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  Independent Group
who belongs to such Independent Group and is not  disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid  Independent Group has communicated his  decision to nominate
Kunabalakrishnan Kirubakaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam West Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Kunabalakrishnan Kirubakaran was selected as the member of the Valikamam West Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
26th August, 2019.
