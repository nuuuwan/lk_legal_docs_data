TRANSFER   OF   POWERS   (DIVISIONAL   SECRETARIATS)   ACT,   No.   58   OF   1992
Order under Section 2
ESTABLISHMENT   OF   DIVISIONAL   SECRETARY’S   DIVISIONS   IN   NUWARAELIYA,   GALLE   AND
RATHNAPURA   DISTRICTS
I, Wajira Abeywardana, Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government hereby establish
10 new Divisional Secretary’s Divisions in Nuwaraeliya Administrative District, revising the 05 existing Divisional
Secretary’s Divisions, 02 new Divisional Secretary’s Divisions revising Divisional Secretary’s Division Baddegama, and
03 new Divisional Secretary’s Division revising Divisional Secretary Division Hikkaduwa in Galle District, 02 new Divisional
Secretary’s Divisions in Rathnapura District revising Divisional Secretary’s Division Balangoda, on the power vested on
me by Section 2 of the Transfer of Powers (Divisional Secretaries) Act, No. 58 of 1992. Further the names of Divisional
Secretary’s Division are mentioned in Column I of the Schedule and each Divisionial Secretary’s Division is comprised of
Grama Niladhari Divisions indicated in Column II. The boundaries of the Divisional Secretary’s Divisions are mentioned
against each name in Column III and the date of implementing the provisions of Section 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th and 7th of the Act
for each Divisional Secretary’s Division are indicated in Column IV .
W
AJIRA ABEYW ARDANA (M. P.),
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and
Provincial Councils & Local Government.
Ministry of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils,
& Local Government,
“Nila Medura”,
Narahenpita,
Colombo 05,
23rd Ocrober, 2019.
