THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified by me in terms of Article 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka that subjects, functions and Departments, Statutory Bodies and State Corporations assigned to me as the
Minister of Defence under Article 43(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka have been
assigned to the State Minister of Defence with effect from 21.02.2019 as set out in the following Schedule.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President,
Minister of Defence.
Ministry of Defence,
Colombo 03,
21st February, 2019.
