PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 05.02.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of land described as following names and extents
in the Advance Tracing No. KU/PLG/2018/01 dated on 29.01.2018,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village  of
Serapiswatta in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 993,
Serapiswatta in the Minor Division of Dambadenihatpattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Polgahawela in Kurunegala District
in North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Serapiswatta – Extent: About 2.5721 Hectares
North: Railway Reservation; East: Railway Reservation and Assmt.
No. 132 of Edella Janapadaya; South: Lot B, Serapiswatta claimed
by the Land Reform Commission and the Ministry of Plantation
Industries and land claimed by the Board of Investment; West: Land
claimed by the Board of Investment.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Serapiswatta – Extent: About 0.0448 Hectare
North: Lot A and Assmt. No. 132 of Edella Janapadaya; East:
Assmt. No. 132 of Edella Janapadaya; South: Road and Serapiswatta
claimed by the Land Reform Commission and the Ministry of
Plantation Industries; West: Serapiswatta claimed by the Land Reform
Commission and the Ministry of Plantation Industries.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
