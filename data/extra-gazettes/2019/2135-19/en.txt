THE   CIVIL  A VIATION  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2010
Order under Section 6
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6 of the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010, I, Arjuna Ranatunga, Minister
of Transport and Civil Aviation, in consultation with the Civil Aviation Authority, do by this Order amend the Schedule II
of the Order made by the Minister of Civil Aviation and published in Gazette Extrarodinary No. 1797/27 of February 15,
2013 as amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1973/77 of July 01, 2016 by repealing paragraph 1
thereof and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“1. The Development, operation and maintenance of-
(i) Bandaranaike International Airport, Colombo in the Western Province ;
(ii) Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport in the Southern Province ;
(iii) Colombo Airport, Ratmalana in the Western Province ;
(iv) Batticaloa Airport in the Eastern Province ; and
(v) Palaly Airport in the Northern Province.”.
ARJUNA R ANATUNGA,
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Battaramulla,
05th August, 2019.
