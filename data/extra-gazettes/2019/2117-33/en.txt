THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
 Order under Section 4(1)
WHERE as an industrial dispute in respect of  the matter/specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which
accompanies this order exists between,
Trade Union of Mihin Lanka, (On behalf of Chief Organizer - Mr. R. A. S. Lionel Premasiri and 49 employees) No. 497/1A,
Heenkenda, Ragama)  of the one part
and
Mihin Lanka (Pvt) Ltd, EML Building, No. 61, W. A. D. Ramanayaka Mawatha, Colombo 02  of the other part
Now Therefore, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of  Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in
me by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957,  4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37
of 1968 hereby appoint.
Mr. L. R. Sumathipala, Principal Quarters, Nurses'Training School, Kandy to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid
dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
26th March, 2019.

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the 49 employees inclusive of R. A. S. Lionel Premasiri, mentioned in the schedule had been caused injustice by the
V oluntary Retirement Compensation Scheme not being followed in the termination of employees from Mihin Lanka (Pvt) Ltd in
accordance with Public Enterprise Circular No. 02/2016 dated 08.03.2016 introduced by the Ministry of Finance for surplus
workers of public enterprises and a less favourable method being followed and if such injustice had been caused, to what relief
each of them is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner  of Labour.
