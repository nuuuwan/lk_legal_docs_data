1A  PG  4576 — 87  (07/2019)
EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
EXCISE   DUTY   ON   IMPORT   OF   NON-POTABLE   SPRITS
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance, by virtue of the
power vested in him by Sections 2, 12, 22 and 25 read together with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as
amended, and published as the Excise Notification No. 12/2018 in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2091/50 dated October
03, 2018, in order to amend the Paragraph (b) of the Excise Notification No. 07/2018 published in the Extraordinary
Gazette No. 2059/32 dated February 21, 2018 by the substitution of the following new Paragraph was passed by Parliament
on April 05, 2019.
“(b) any export oriented manufacturing company, which has entered into an agreement with the Board of Investment of
Sri Lanka under Section 17 of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law, No.04 of 1978 (Hereafter called “BOI
approved manufacturing company”), and export 100% of its products, manufactured by using the items in the
Schedule 1 of Excise Notification No. 07/2018 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2059/32 dated February 21,
2018 as its raw materials and such export is certified by the Director General of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka,
Excise duty on importation of such raw materials shall not be levied with effect from October 04, 2018.
