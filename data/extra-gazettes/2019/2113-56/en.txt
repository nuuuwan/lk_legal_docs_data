My No.: IR/COM/04/V/2015/154.
THE INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between,
Mrs. M. I. P. Kulathilaka, “Upamali”, Beragala, Kegalle.
and
Employees Trust Fund Board, First Floor, Labour Secretariat, Colombo 05.
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers
vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial disputes-Special
Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. S. G. Sooriyaarachchi, No. 22/1, Sri Pemarathana Mawatha, Menikdiwela to
be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo,
28th February, 2019.

and
Employees Trust Fund Board, First Floor, Labour Secretariat, Colombo 05.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mrs. M. I. P. Kulathilaka who was working in a Grade III Executive post of the Employees Trust Fund had been caused
injustice by being placed in a Junioir Management post which is a Grade lower than the post held earlier and thereby being
deprived of salaries, annual salary increments, other Privileges and promotions and if such injustice had been caused, to what
reliefs she is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, this 21st day of February 2019.
