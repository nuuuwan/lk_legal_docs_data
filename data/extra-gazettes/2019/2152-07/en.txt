KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, I, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this Proclamation,
prorogue Parliament with effect from midnight of the Second day of December, Two Thousand and Nineteen and hereby
fix the Third day of January, Two Thousand and Twenty, at 10.00 a.m. for the commencement of the next session and
summon Parliament to meet in the Chamber of Parliament, Sri Jayewardenapura.
Given at Colombo on this Second day of December, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
                  By His Excellency’s command,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
 Secretary to the President.
