PROVINCIAL COUNCIL NOTIFICATIONS
CENTRAL PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
I, Maithree Gunaratne, Governor of  the Central Province, hereby make following regulations by virtu e of the powers vested in me by
Section 188 (i) m. m. and Section 289 of  the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) which shoul d be read with Section 2 of  the
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989. This Order shall be effective fro m 08th of May, 2019.
REGULATIONS
The special allowances payable to the Mayor Deputy Mayor and a Member of the Dambulla Municipal Coun cil of the Central Province
for participating in monthly standing committees, sub committees, advisory committees and special ge neral meetings from the Dambulla
Municipal Council fund. These allowances shall be in addition to the regulations published in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1914/40 of
15.05.2015 of  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

For Standing Committee - Rs. 750.00
