No. 2111/53 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 22, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land Nos. 1 and 3 in extent of 0.9007 Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Gam/Mi/2009/369 dated
10.05.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Zone 1 of Heediyawala (Cadastral Plan 510242) in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. 31B Pelapitigama in the Minor Division
of Yatigaha Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Meerigama of Gampaha District in Western Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot No. 01 – Rukmal Agarawatta – Extent: 0.1108 Hectare
North: Lot No. 159 of Zone No. 1 of Cadastral Plan No. 510242;
East: Lot No. 02; South: Lot Nos. 02, 03 and Gaspe Village; West:
Gaspe Village.
02. Lot No. 03 – Rukmal Agarawatta – Extent: 0.7899 Hectare
North: Lot No. 2; East: Lot No. 02 and Lot No. 30 of Zone
No. 1 in Cadastral Plan No. 510242; South: Lot Nos. 30 and
36 of Zone No. 1 in Cadastral Plan No. 510242; West: Lot
Nos. 36, 32, Gaspe Village and Lot No. 01 of Zone No. 1 in Cadastral
Plan No. 510242.
