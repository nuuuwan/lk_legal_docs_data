THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and  Higher  Education, on the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15
of  October 09, 2007 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2023/32 of June 14, 2017),
which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka which is deemed, by virtue of
the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, as
follows :-
(1) by the substitution for the item “Faculty of Arts” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column
II thereof, of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Arts 1. Department of Economics
2. Department of Geography
3. Department of Philosophy
4. Department of Linguistics and English
5. Department of Christian and Islamic Civilization
6. Department of Tamil
