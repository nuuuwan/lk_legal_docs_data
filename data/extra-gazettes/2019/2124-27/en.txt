THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCATION ORDER UNDER SECTION 39 (1)
BY the Order made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land
Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No.  2055/58 dated 26.01.2018 and the land specified
more fully in the Order No. 21 of 2018 dated 25.01.2018 was vested
in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. But the possession
of the said land has not yet been taken for and on behalf of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore, I  Gayantha
Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms, by
virtue of powers vested in me under Section 39 (1) of the land
Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the aforesaid Order No. 21 of
2018 dated 25.01.2018 published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 2055/58 dated 26.01.2018.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
