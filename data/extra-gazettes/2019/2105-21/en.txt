NOTICE   OF   APPROV AL   OF   THE   AMENDMENT   OF   PLANNING   &   BUILDING   REGULATIONS
2008-2020   FOR   SRI   JAYEWARDENEPURA   KOTTE   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL   PREPARED   UNDER   THE
URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY   LAW   No.   41  OF  1978
NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8F of the Urban
Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, have approved the proposed amendment to be included to the Planning &
Building Regulations 2008 -2020 for Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council Area.
I, hereby approve the amendment applicable to Section 37 (1) of the Planning & Building Regulations  for Sri
Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8H of the Urban Development
Authority Law No. 41 of 1978.
01 Section 37 (1) of the Planning & Building Regulations for Sri JayawardenapuraKotte
Municipal Council Area is hereby further amended by the insertion of clause “f” as follows;
“ The number of floors for a given development is decided on the width of the road
given under the guidelines in Schedule 6 - Form C in Planning & Building Regulation
for Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte  Municipal Council Area therein the corresponding access
road can have a minimum road width of 12 meters (40 feet),or two or more alternative
and separate access roads, each of which can have a minimum width of 6 meters, subject
to such discretion vested in the Authority to grant necessary approval.”
