SRI   LANKA   SUSTAINABLE   ENERGY   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.   35   OF   2007
Order under Sub section (1) of Section 12
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub Section (1) of Section 12 of Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No.
35 of 2007, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Power, Energy and Business Development, in consultation with the Ministers
in Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment, Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock Development, Irrigation and
Fisheries & Aquatic Resources Development, Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms, Ministry of Public
Administraion, Disaster Management & Rural Economic Affairs, Ministry of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial
Councils & Local Government, Ministry of City Planning, Water Supply & Higher Education, Ministry of Tourism
Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development and the relevant
Ministries of Eastern Provincial Council to whom the subject of Environment is assigned, do by this Order declare the
areas of lands described in the Schedule hereto as Energy Development Areas in the Batticaloa District for the purpose of
the aforesaid Act.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Power, Energy and Business Development.
Ministry of Power, Energy and Business Development,
No. 72, Ananda Kumaraswami Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
07th August, 2019.
