GALLANTRY  AWARDS
It is hereby notifies that HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and
Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Force has been graciously pleased to confer the Gallantry Awards of Weerodara
Vibhushanaya, Weera Wickrama Vibhushanaya, Rana Wickrama Padakkama and Rana Sura Padakkama on the 11
thday of
November in the Year Two Thousand Nineteen , to the persons named in the schedule hereto.
      By His Excellency’s Command,
UDAYA R. SENEVIRATHNE,
Secretary to the President.
At the Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
8
th  November 2019.
