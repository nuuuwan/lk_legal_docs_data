THE  CODE  OF  CRIMINAL  PROCEDURE  ACT,  No.  15  OF  1979
Notification under Section 108
I, R. M. D. B. Meegasmulla, Secretary, Ministry of Justice and Prison Reforms do hereby inform that by virtue of the
powers vested in her under Section 108 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15 of 1979, Hon. Thalatha Atukorale,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms has appointed the Director General of the Sri Lanka Tourism Development
Authority as an Inquirer for the Whole Island only for the purposes of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005.
R. M. D. B. M EEGASMULLA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
12th February, 2019.
