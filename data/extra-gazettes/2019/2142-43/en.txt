FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BATTICALOA  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Batticaloa Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the T amil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Veluppillai Gangeswary the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned  member of Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Varneswary Ravichandran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the  Batticaloa Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Varneswary
Ravichandran was selected as the member of the Batticaloa Municipal Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Batticaloa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
24th September, 2019.
