PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 27.05.2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCATION ORDER UNDER SECTION 39 (1)
The servitude of the allotment of land described in detail in the Order
No. 145 of 2014 dated 23.06.2014 made under the Proviso (a) to
Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and published
in the Gazette Extra Ordinary  No. 1868/51 dated 25.06.2014 is
vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore,
I Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 39(1) of
the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the aforesaid Order 145
of 2014 dated 23.06.2014 published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 1868/51 dated 25.06.2014.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
