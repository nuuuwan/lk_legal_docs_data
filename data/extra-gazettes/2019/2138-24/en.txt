FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KINNIYA   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kinniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Front For Good Governance, the recognized political party to
which Abdul Majeedu Anees the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member of  National Front For Good Governance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Umer Ali Ranees to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the  Kinniya Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Umer Ali
Ranees was selected as the member of the Kinniya Urban Council.
S. S
UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Kinniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
26th August, 2019.
