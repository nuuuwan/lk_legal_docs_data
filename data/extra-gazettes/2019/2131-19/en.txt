1A   G  30482— 22  (07/2019)
NATIONAL   COLLEGE  OF  EDUCATION   ESTABLISHED  FOR  THE  STUDENTS  OF  TECHNOLOGY
STREAM
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Section 2(1)(a) of the Colleges of Education Act, No. 30 of 1986 and pursuant
to the cabinet approval granted on 18.01.2017 for the Cabinet Memorandum No. 2016/ED/E/33 dated 26.09.2016 and in
accordance with the Plan of the Surveyor General bearing the Advance Tracing No. KU/KPW/2016/635 dated 23.08.2016
and in terms of the notice on the Acceptance of Possession No. DIVKW/LND/ACQLA/4-D/38, I, declare that the new
National College of Education established in the land consisting of three plots to the extent of 15 Acres, 0 Rood, and
15.44 Perches belonging to the Ministry of Education situated in Poogalla and Narangalla villages of the  Narangalla
Grama Niladhari Division to be named as The National College of Education on Technology - Kuliyapitiya .
A
KILA VIRAJ KARIY AWASAM,
Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
Isurupaya,
Battaramulla,
