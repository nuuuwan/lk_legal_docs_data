FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   WARAKAPOLA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of return  member of  W arakapola Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group,  to which Kotuwegoda Palliyaguruge Danusha Nawodhana
Kumara Kotuwegoda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  Independent Group who belongs to such Independent Group  and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid  Independent Group  has communicated his decision to nominate
Gamlath Arachchillage Wasantha Sisira Kumara Samaratunga to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Gamlath
Arachchillage Wasantha Sisira Kumara  Samaratunga was selected as the member of the Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
27th May, 2019.
