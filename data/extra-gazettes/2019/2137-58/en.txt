Notice under Section 7
My No.: 11/3/3/1/14 I.

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land

required for a public purpose:

Moragahakanda and Kaluganga Development Project.

Matale District, Central province.

the Divisional Secretary's Division of Medadumbara in Kandy
District of the Central Province and more fully described as
follows:
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Gonawalawatta; Description: Watta with
abandoned tea Cultivation; Claimants: Jinadasa Rajapakshe,
No. 190, Makuldeniya, Teldeniya; Extent: 0.3995 Hectare.

required to appear personally, or by agent duly authorized
in writing before me at the Medadumbara Divisional
Secretariat on the 11th day of November, 2019 at 2.00 p.m.
and are here by requested notify me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the 04th day of November, 2019. The nature
of their interest in the aforesaid land, the particulars of their
claims of compensation and the details of computation of
such amount.
