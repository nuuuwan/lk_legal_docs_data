THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1454/37 of
July 22, 2006 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2069/16 of May 01, 2018) which
specifies the Faculties and  Departments of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the
provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the
substitution for the entries “2.  Department of Physics” and “4. Department of Botany” appearing in Column II thereof
which corresponds to the item  “Faculty of Science” appearing in Column I thereof of the entries “2. Department of
Physics and Electronics” and “4. Department of Plant and Molecular Biology” respectively.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
25th  March, 2019.
