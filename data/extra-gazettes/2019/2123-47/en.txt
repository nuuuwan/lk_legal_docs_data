ORDER   UNDER   SECTION   10(1)(A)   OF   THE   HOLIDAYS   ACT,    No.   29   OF   1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10(1)(a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government, do by this Order declare Monday 20th
of May, 2019 a Public Holiday on account of the Celebrating the Vesak Full Moon Poya Day, 2019 and the day following
vesak full moon poya day on Saturday and Sunday.
