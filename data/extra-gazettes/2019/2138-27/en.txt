5TH AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1836/6 dated
11th of November, 2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary
No. 1887/21 dated 05th of November 2014, No. 1905/07 dated 09th of March 2015, No. 2006/53 dated 17th of February
2017 and No. 2049/14 dated 12th of December 2017 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended as
follows and these amended provisions will be made effective from 26
th of February 2019, the date on which the Cabinet
of Ministers made the policy decision.
                                                                     By Order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
