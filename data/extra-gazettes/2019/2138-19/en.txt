THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land described in below name and extent, depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. B/ACQ/BDL/2017/117 dated 30.09.2017
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village  of Badulla
East (Division No. 06) (within Badulla Municipality) in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Badulla East (78B) in the Minor Division of
Rilpola Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Badulla in
Badulla District in Uva Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Oyawelona – Extent: 0.0126 Hectare
North: River side Road and Oyawelona of which the claimant
cannot be found; East and South: Badulu Oya and Oyawelona of
which the claimant cannot be found; West: River side Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
