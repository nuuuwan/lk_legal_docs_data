UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments by  the  Governor of  Uva Province
IT is hereby announce that, the following Appointments has been appointed by Hon. Maithri Gunaratne, the Governor of
the Uva Province under the Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987.
01. Mrs. R. H. C. Priyanthi, Grade III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Director of Social
Service of Uva Province, in addition to the duties of her permanent post with effect from the date 01.08.2019.
02. Engineer Mr. Chandrathilake, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, as Director to the Road of
Uva Province, with immeditate effect (from the date 29.10.2019).
03. Mr. P. W. W. Dissanayake, Retired Grade II officer of the Sri Lanka Ayurvedic Medical Service, has been re-
appointed on contract basis as the Provincial Commissioner of Ayurvedic of Uva Province, from the date
24.10.2019 till the appointment of a permanent officer up to 23.02.2020.
04. Engineer Mr. K. A. B. Senevirathne, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, as the acting Deputy
Chief Secretary (Engineering Services) from the date 26.10.2019 on full time basis.
