The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Colombo Municipal Council
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Mohomad Masahir Mohomad
Binkas of the Colombo Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that Council  as he has ceased to be a
member of the United Peace Alliance.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
09th July, 2019.
