LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7(1)
My Ref. No.: KDS/PDB/4/3/10(iv).
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/11/2017/UD/172.
I, do hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land  to be acquired:
The allotment of land Lot No. 01 in total extent of about
0.0008 hectares, depicted in the Preliminary Plan Maha/6911,
surveyed and prepared by the senior surveyor superintendent
of Kandy for Surveyor General and situated in the village of
Walala in Pallegampaha Korale minor division of Pathadumbara
Division in Kandy District, Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Kankaniyage Kumbura Wanatha; Extent:
0.0008 Hectares.
North: Road (Road Development Authority) and Lot No. 61 in
PP/Maha/6394; East: Lot No. 60 and 61 in PP/Maha/6394 and
Kankaniyage Kumbura claimed by W.M.P .N. Wahindara;
South: Kankaniyage Kumbura claimed by W .M.P .N. Wahindara
and forest adjacent to Kankaniyage Kumbura claimed by
K.A.N.W. Nanayakkara; West: forest adjacent to Kankaniyage
Kumbura claimed by K.A.N.W. Nanayakkara and channel.
Names and Addresses of the claimants: K.A.N.W.
Nanayakkara, No. 174, 8th Mile Post, Walala, Menikhinna.
    I do hereby give public notice that all persons interested in
the aforesaid lands are required to appear before me
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing  at
Divisional Secretariat Pathadumbara on 29 day of May 2019,
at 10.00 a.m.  in the morning and they are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing  with duplicates on or before 22 day of
May 2019, the nature of their interests in the aforesaid land,
and the amount and the particulars of their claims for
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D. G. C. JAYATHUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Pathadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat - Pathadumbara,
10th April, 2019.
