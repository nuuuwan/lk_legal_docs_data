SECOND   SUPPLEMENTARY   FINANCIAL   STATEMENT
GRANTING PERMISSION BY VIRTUE OF POWERS VESTED IN ME UNDER SECTION 27(A) OF PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL ACT, NO. 42
OF 1987 AS THE TERM OF THE SIXTH SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF

TH
 SEPTEMBER,  2017.
2019
1. This permission may be cited as “Permission to disburse under second financial statement - 2019 of  the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial  Council” and come  in to operation from 16th, September 2018.
2. I do hereby grant premission to the Chief Secretary of this province and other accounting officers of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to disburse the province and other accounting officers of the Sabargamuwa
Provincial Council to expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty
Four Billion Twenty Three Million, Four Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Seven and
Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) for the service of the period beginning on 01 st of January
2019 and ending  31 st December, 2019, which is specified as “Financial Year 2019” in the Budget Estimate -
2019, that was approved on 10.04.2019 and amended by First Supplementary Statement - 2019.
3. Accordingly the first Schedule of the said amended Gazette Notification is amended as the first schedule
hereof and grant permission to disburse the same.
4. Advanced accounts limits which is specified in second schedule of Gazette Notification  No. 2133/41 dated
24.07.2019, is hereby amended as second  schedule of this notification and grant permission to Chief Secretary
of this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council Fund.
5. This permission will be valid till 31 st of December, 2019 or upto the date that expires three months (03) from
the date of  summoning the Seventh (07 th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the
functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which occurs first.

AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
to disburse as per the second supplementary financial statement for the year 2019, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27 (a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamua Provincial Council which is estimated for the service of the period beginning
on 01st January, 2019 and ending 31st December, 2019, and specified in the annual financial statement - 2019 amended by
First Supplementary Estaimate - 2019, Rupees Thirty Four Billion Twenty Three Million Four Hundred and Six Thousand
One Hundred and Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four only (Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) and grant permission to disburse the
same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st December, 2019 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which occurs first.
D
HAMMA D ISSANAY AKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
16th September, 2019.
AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby approve to amend the advance account limits stated in the Sixth Schedule of the Second Supplementary Financial
Statement for the year 2019, by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 27 (a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of
1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended
on 26th September, 2017.
Accordingly, I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council to bear the expenses subject to those limits.
This permission will be valid till 31st December, 2019 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which occurs first.
D
HAMMA D ISSANAY AKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
16th September, 2019.
