FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KUCHCHA VELI
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kuchchaveli  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Subramaniyam Paramesvaran  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member of  Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Soosaipillai Mohanadas to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai  Suthakaran, Returning Officer  of  the  Kuchchaveli  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Soosaipillai
Mohanadas was selected as the member of the Kuchchaveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. S
UTHAKARAN,
Returning Officer,
Kuchchaveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
26th August, 2019.
