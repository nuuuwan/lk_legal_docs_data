The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   AKURANA    PRADESHIYA    SABHA
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party the recognized political party to which Narayanasamy
Jayaseelan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of  that Number 03 - Alawathugoda of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Giddawa Gedara Niranjan Karunaratne to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Giddawa Gedara Niranjan Karunaratne was elected as the member of the Number 03 - Alawathugoda of Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
28th August, 2019.
