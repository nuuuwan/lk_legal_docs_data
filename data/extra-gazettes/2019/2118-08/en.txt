FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
The Order under Section 3 that has to be read with Section 64(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as
No. 13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of
2009 that have to be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, that have
to be read with Section 51 of the 19th Amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I
Maithripala Sirisena, President do by this Order declare the Katagamuwa Demeda Mukalana Forest of 62.583 hectares
bounded by the limit set forth in the Schedule hereto as Katagamuwa Demeda Mukalana Reserved Forest from 18th day of
March 2019.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
18th March, 2019.
