THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                               My No. :  KDS/PBK/4/4/17.
                                      Land Ministry Ref. No. :  LD/05/2004/HW.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends  to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
      Description of the land to be acquired :
An allotment in extent of about 0.0229 Hectare, of land in
Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6426 and dated 29.05.2015 prepared
by the Senior-Superintendent of Surveys of Kandy District
on behalf of Surveyor General, situated in the Village of
Pallegama in the Grama Niladhari Division of Ward No. 1104,
Pallegama, in the Minor Division of Pasbage Korale in
Divisional Secretary's Division of Pasbage Korale in Kandy
District in the Central Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Gallambudeniya; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Sunethra Kumari Uthuwana,
No. 236/9, Dolosbaga Road, Nawalapitiya (Acquired under
Section 38 (A) of Land Acquisition Act);  Extent: 0.0229 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretary Office of Pasbage
Korale on  25.02.2019 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18.02.2019
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of  their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Pasbage Korale.
