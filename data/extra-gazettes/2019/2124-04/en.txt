SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose, in respect
of the list of commodity specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rate specified in
the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from May 22, 2019, subject to provisions of
Sub-Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2112/48 of 02.03.2019 in respect of B’ Onions is rescinded with effect from
May 22, 2019.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
21st  May, 2019.
