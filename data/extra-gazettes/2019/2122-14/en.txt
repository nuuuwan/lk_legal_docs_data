THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: Y AK/LAN/01/03/16.
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/364.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
An allotment of land in extent of 4.9954 Hectares shown as
Lot No. 858 depicted in Sheet 25, supplementary 24 of Final
Village Plan No. 638 described below prepared by Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Nakiyadeniya in the Grama
Niladhari Division of 179A Nakiyadeniya in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Thalpe Pattuwa North, Yakkalamulla
in Galle District, Southern Province.
Lot No. 858
Name of Land: Thalangahawatte; Usage of Land: Garden
with mixed cultivation; Claimants: State; Original claimant:
Watawala Plantation Company; Extent: 4.9954 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land/servitude are
hereby requested to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Yakkalamulla Divisional
Secretariat on the 29th day of  May 2019  at 9.00 a.m. and are
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 22nd day of  May 2019 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of he said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount in duplicate.
CHANDIMA C. M UHANDIRAMGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Y akkalamulla.
Divisional Secretariat, Y akkalamulla,
29th April, 2019.
