REGULATIONS
         
(1)   These Regulations may be cited as the National Thoroughfares (User Fee National Highway) Regulations  
        
(2)    These regulations shall apply to -
The Barawakumbuka – Mattala User fee National Highway (Section of Extension of Southern Expressway) and 
Andarawewa – Hambantota User Fee National Highway declared by Order made under Section 11 of the National 
Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. Gazette No. to be inserted once 
this has been declared as a National Highway)
(3)  (1)  There shall be levied from a driver of every vehicle being used on the aforesaid User Fee National Highways, a 
fee in respect of the use of the User Fee National Highway by such driver, at such rates speciﬁ ed in Part A of the 
Schedule to these regulations.

 (2)  The fee shall be charged in respect of the whole distance, or part thereof, of the User Fee National Highway.
 (3)  A single rate shall be applied for all categories of vehicles speci ﬁ ed in Part A of the schedule to these regulations.
(4)  (1)  A driver of every vehicle who enters a User Fee National Highway from an interchange shall, prior to being 
permitted to proceed along such highway, obtain a ticket from the Teller on duty, at such interchange.
 (2)  The driver of a vehicle which has been proceeding along the User Fee National Highway, when arriving at the 
gate of an interchange for the purpose of exiting such User Fee National Highway shall surrender to the Teller 
the ticket obtained at the interchange through which he entered.
 (3)   The Teller shall inform the driver of the amount of the fee payable and accordingly the driver shall pay such 
amount, prior to being permitted to pass through such interchange, and the teller shall issue a receipt in respect 
of such payment.
(5)  (1) A driver of every vehicle shall keep the ticket referred to in regulation 4 (1), securely in his possession until he 
reaches the toll gate of the interchange through which he intends to exit such User Fee National Highway.
 (2)  Where the driver is unable to surrender the ticket to the Teller, and the Teller fails to identify the entry location, th e 
driver shall be deemed to have travelled the entire distance of the User Fee National Highway from the furthest 
possible point of entry to the actual point of exit and shall accordingly be charged a fee at the rates speci ﬁ ed in 
Part A of the schedule to these regulations.
(6)  (1)  The Road Development Authority, established under the Road Development Authority Act, No. 73 of 1981, 
(hereinafter referred to as “the Authority”) shall ensure that, for the purpose of implementation of the provisions 
of these regulations, suf ﬁ cient number of gates by taking into account the average Traf ﬁ c volume at exit and 
entry points are positioned at the required places along the User Fee National Highways. 
 (2)  The gates so positioned along the User Fee National Highway shall be owned, operated and maintained by the 
Authority, and each gate shall be manned by a Teller. 
 (3)  The Teller shall be responsible for the collection of the user fee at every gate referred to in  paragraph (2), in such 
sum as is speciﬁ ed in Part A of the Schedule to these regulations from every driver using such User Fee National 
Highways :
  Provided that the provisions of these regulations shall not apply to a driver who is driving a vehicle of a category 
speciﬁ ed in Part B of the Schedule to these regulations, which is exempt from such payment. 
(7)  The Teller shall at such times as are speci ﬁ ed, hand over the day’s collection of the fees to the ofﬁ cer authorized in that 
behalf by the Authority and obtain a receipt in respect of such amount.
(8)   The money so collected as user fee shall be credited to the Fund of the Authority and be used for the purpose of 
developing and maintaining the User Fee National Highways to which these regulations apply.
(9)  For the purpose of these regulations-
“Teller” means an ofﬁ cer who is manning the gates at an interchange;
“User Fee National Highways” includes Barawakumbuka – Mattala User Fee National Highway (Section of Extension 
of Southern Expressway) from Barawakumbuka (178.656 km) to Mattala (200.451 km) and Andarawewa – 
Hambantota User Fee National Highway from Andarawewa (0.000km) to Hambantota (16.558km). 
(10)  The driver of a vehicle who contravenes the provisions of these regulations shall commit an offence and shall on conviction 
after summary trial by a Magistrate be liable in the case of-       
  
(a)  a ﬁ rst offence; to a ﬁ ne not exceeding ﬁ ve thousand rupees; and 
(b)  a second or subsequent offence; to a ﬁ ne not exceeding ﬁ fty thousand rupees.
