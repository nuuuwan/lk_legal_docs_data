PUBLIC   SERVICE   COMMISSION
Appointment of Committees in terms of Articles 56(1) and 56(2) of the Constitution
01. On the direction of the Public Service Commission, following members are hereby appointed to the Committee
for the Education Service Sector constituting three members for a period of one year in terms of Article
56(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka with effect from 1st of March
Two Thousand Nineteen (2019).
I. Mr. M. M. N. D. Bandara (Chairman)
II. Mr. E. H. M. P. Elkaduwa (Member)
III. Mr. K. L. Mohomed Thamby (Member)
02. Accordingly, the contents stated in the Gazette Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 1989/27 dated 19th October 2016 are amended with effect
from 1st of March Two Thousand Nineteen (2019).
