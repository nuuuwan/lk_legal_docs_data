 UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,
the Minister of  City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, having obtained a report under section 70C of the
aforesaid Act, in respect of the Esoft Metro Campus (Private) Limited, a Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and  bearing Registration Number PV 94112 and first registered on 23.08.2013 and
amalgamated on 02.02.2018, located at No. 03, De Fonseka Place, Colombo 04, do by this Order  and subject to the
conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the Esoft Metro Campus (Pvt) Limited as a Degree Awarding Institute
for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of the
Schedule II hereto, of  which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule II hereto
:-
SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS
1. Esoft Metro Campus (Private) Limited shall conduct the degree programme relevant to the Degree
specified in Schedule II hereto, only at No. 03, De Fonseka Place, Colombo 04.
2. Esoft Metro Campus (Private) Limited shall, within five (5) years from the date of publication of this Order,
submit the evidence of the establishment of  the institute sought to be established under this Order, in a
location registered under the name of the institute to the specified Authority.
