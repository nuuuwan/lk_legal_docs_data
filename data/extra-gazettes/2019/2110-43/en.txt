LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.:  RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/21(VE).
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/8/2016//VE/256.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section (7) of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by Land Acquisition
Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands to be acquired :
Following allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 113 and
114 in extent of 4.8565 Hectares in F.V .P . No. R.423 made by
Survey General, situated in Pelendagama Village of 164A
Bambarabotuwa Grama Niladari Division of Divisional
Secretary's Division Rathnapura in the District of Rathnapura
in Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot No. 113
Name of the Land: Pothuetathenna Mookalana; Land Use:
Estate; Claimant: Hapugasthenna Plantation, No. 95/A,
Nambapana, Ingiriya; Extent: 4.3285 Hectares.
01. Lot No. 114
Name of the Land: Pothuetathenna Mookalana; Land Use:
Estate; Claimant: Hapugasthenna Plantation, No. 95/A,
Nambapana, Ingiriya; Extent: 0.5280 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby requested to appear
personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.00 a.m. on 28th day of March, 2019 at Rathnapura
Divisional Secretariat.
Moreover, they are here by requested to notify to me in
writing with two duplicates on or before the 21.03.2019 the
nature of their interest in land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisitiion of the said lands, the
amount of compensation and details of the computation such
amount.
I. R. M. P
RADEEPIKA D. ILLUKKUMBURA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rathnapura.
At Rathnapura Divisional Secretariat,
30th January, 2019.
