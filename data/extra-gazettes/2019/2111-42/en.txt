LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
 My Reference No.: 3/5/DO/ms/ke/m/01.
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/9/2012/IR/210.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude* which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
One (01) Allotments of land in Total Extent of about 8.0937
Hectares, depicted in F.V .P . 1268, Sheet No. 10, Inset No. 10
and Lots No. 1323 situated in Rambawewa in 441 - Neerawiya
Grama Niladhari Division of, Thiththaweligandahee Korale, in
Divisional Secretariat of Ganewatta, Kurunegala District in
North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1323 – Garden, Rambawewaheena, Claimant:
State; Extent: 8.0937 Hectares.
North: Lot No. 1042; East: Lot No. K 1040N; South: Lot No.
K 1040 D; W est: Lot No. K 1040 D.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary's Office Ganewaththa on the 30th day
of May , 2019 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicated) on or before the 16th day of May,
2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation of the land/servitude*, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
R. N. N. L. KARUNARATHNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ganewaththa Division.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Ganewaththa,
18th February , 2019.
