THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 Reference No.: 3/3/6/2/30. 
 
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964, that the Government  intends to acquire the 
following land which are required for a public purpose. 
 
Land acquisition for building the office OIC (officer-in-
charge) of Visal Matale Water Project at Ukuwela. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment No. 01 in extent 0.1522 Hectares, of the 
land in Preliminary Plan No. Mu.P.Ma. 1780 prepared by 
the surveyor superintendent , of, Matale situated at 
Ukuwela village in Ukuwela Grama Niladhari Division, 
Matale Udasiyapathtuwe in Divisional Secretary Division 
of Ukuwela in the District of Matale Central Province. 
Lot No. 01 
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Shanthi 
Munasinghe Samagi Mawatha, Ukuwela; Land Extent: 
0.1522 Hectare 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid Lands on are  
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
of Ukuwela at 9.30 a. m. on 04.12.2019. 
 
And hereby requested to notify me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 20.11.2019 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands amount 
of compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount. 
  
 S. N. K. S ERASINGHE, 
                                               Divisional  Secretary,
