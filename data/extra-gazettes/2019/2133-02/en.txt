MAHA WELI AUTHORITY OF SRI LANKA ACT, NO. 23 OF 1979
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 23 of 1979, read with
Section 51 of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka I, Maithripala
Sirisena, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby declare the areas set out in the Schedule
hereto as “Special Area” for the purposes of the Act.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
15th July, 2019.
SCHEDULE
Area depicted by the following boundaries of the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Minipe in the Administrative District of
Kandy and the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Vilgamuwa in the Administrative District of Matale.
To North:
From the place where Dunuwila Lake dam and Dunuwila Oya joins to the place where Jeep or cart track meet the Thoppalapitiya
Government Forest along the Dunuwila Oya and thence to the place where the center meets Muthuwangana Oya along the
Jeep or cart track and thence to the center point of the Mahaweli River through the Muthuwangana Oya.
