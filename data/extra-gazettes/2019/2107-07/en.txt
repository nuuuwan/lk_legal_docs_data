MINUTE   OF   THE   CENTRAL   PROVINCIAL   PUBLIC   MANAGEMENT   ASSISTANT’S   SERVICE
AMENDMENT   No.   02
THE Hon. Governor of Central Province has approved to amend the Minute of the Central Provincial Public Management
Assistant’s Service revised by the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 2047/43
dated 30.11.2017 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.
1869/4 dated 30.06.2014, as follows:
1. The Section 7.3.2.1 of Para. 07 is revised as follows  :
7.3.2.1 Educational Qualifications
Shall have passed the G . C. E. (O/L) Examination in six (06) subjects including Sinhala/Tamil/English and
Mathematics/ Arithmetic/ Pure Mathematics/ Elementary Mathematics/ Commercial Arithmetic with at least two
credit passes at not more than two sittings.
The Section 7.3.3.1 of Para. 07 is revised as follows :
7.3.3.1 Written Examination
Subjects Maximum Marks Pass Marks
Language ability and Eligibility Test of 100 40
Management Assistants’ Service
Aptitude 100 40

2A
Candidates who have scored minimum of 40% marks for each subject and obtained an aggregate of at least 50% of
the total marks of the examination that’s mean total marks of 100 or more and have fulfilled the basic educational
qualifications required for recruitment shall be appointed in the order of the merit and the number of vacancies.
(Annex 03)
2. The term “Efficiency Bar Examination” shall be included in substitution for the term “Examination” in sections
02, 04 and 05 under Annex 06.
3. Following shall be included as No. 06 at the end of Annex 06.
06. Officers shall be exempted from the requirement of passing the Efficiency Bar Examination prescribed for the
officers in Grade I of Central Provincial Public management Assistant Service on successful completion of the 10
day course including the basic principles of which a Senior Officer in public service should be aware, within 05
years from the date of promotion to Grade I.
i. Conducting authority of the course : Central Provincial Chief Secretary and
Director General of Combined Services
ii. Duration of the course : The notification for calling applications relevant to the training
course prescribed for Grade I shall be published in the first
quarter of every year and the number of training course
conducted shall be determined in considering the number of
applications received.
iii. Syllabus of the course
Module Duration
Disciplinary Procedure of the Public  Service 02 days
Public Finance and Assets Management 03 days
Organizational Productivity 02 days
Procurement Procedure 02 days
Right to Information Act and Customer Care 01 day
Note :
01. Officers shall be exempted from the Efficiency Bar examination prescribed for Grade I of Central Provincial
Public Management Assistants’ Service strictly on successful completion of the relevant five modules Course,
which shall be conducted by Public Service Training Institute on the approval of the Central Provincial Chief
Secretary or the Director General of Combined Services as determined by the Central Provincial Public Service
Commission.
