THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An Allotment of land depicted in the Advanced Tracing No. NU/
AMB/2014/113 dated 14.06.2017 situated in the Village of
Wewalthalawa in Ambagamuwa Korale Minor Division in the

Eliya District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Wewalthalawa – Extent : 3.8750 Hectares
North, East, South and W est: Wewalthalawa claimed by Land
Reform Commission.
