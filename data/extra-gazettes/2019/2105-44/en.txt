THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                               My Ref. No. : THI/LND/L2/ACQVI/142.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
 Allotments of  Lot No. 01 and 02 in  Total Extent of about
0.0182 Hectare, depicted in Preliminary  Plan No. Mara 2750,
prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of Survey of  Matara
District on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the
Village of  Kapuduwa in the Grama Niladhari Division of  335 A
Kapuduwa in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Thihagoda in Matara District.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: 5/6 th Lot of the land called Paluwatta;
Description: Garden, contains a mixed cultivation; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: –; Extent: 0.0079 Hectare.
    G 29226 - 17 (2019/01) PRINTED A T THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: 5/6 th Lot of the land called Paluwatta;
Description: Garden, contains a mixed cultivation; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: –; Extent: 0.0103 Hectare.
   All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretary's office of  Thihagoda
on the 30th  January , 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 23rd
January, 2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
