Mr. N. K. Upali Rupasinghe, No. 24/2, Liyanage Mawatha, Nawala Road, Rajagiriya.
and
Sri Lankan Airlines Limited, Bandaranaike International Airport, Katunayake.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  Mr. N. K. Upali Rupasinghe working at Sri Lankan Airlines Limited had been caused injustice by,
1. Not being promoted up to now, after joining as a Grade E-V Executive Engineer from 20.02.1998 with a long foreign experience
and been promoted as a Grade 9 Manager from 01.04.2001,
2. Suspension of the previdge given when he joined employment to reimburse a sum equal to the value of 30 gallons of petrol with
effect from 01.10.1999 without prior notice,
3. Being given only the first 20% salary increase out of the 40% salary increase allowed by Circular dated 23.11.1999 which had
been given to all other employees in two 20% installments and
4. Deprival of the on-call allowance of  Rs.5000/- which was given from his appointment date with effect from 01.01.2011, And
if such injustice had been caused to what relief he is entitled.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 26th day of January 2019.
