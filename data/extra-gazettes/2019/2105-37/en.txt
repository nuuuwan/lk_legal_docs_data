The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   HAKMANA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of  Hakmana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Loku Hakuruge Shantha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  the member of   that 4 - Narawelpita if Hakmana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Hewa Walimunige Ranil Gunathilaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the  Hakmana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Selection 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Hewa Walimunige Ranil Gunathilaka  was elected as the member of the 4 - Narawelpita of Hakmana Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Hakmana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
04th January  2019.
