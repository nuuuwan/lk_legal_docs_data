NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1974
Order under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 02 of 1974,
I, Rauff Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education do hereby approve the acquisition of the
immobile properties of allotments of land described in the following schedule as the land is required by the said Board, for
resettlement of families in Attanagalla Divisional Secretariat area that will be relocated due to the construction of proposed
Basnagoda Reservoir under Gampaha, Minuwangoda and Attanagalla integrated water supply project.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,

and Higher Education.
