PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 08.02.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Co/KSB/2016/262 dated 25.08.2016, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Pepiliyana in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Pepiliyana West No. 535 in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kesbewa in Colombo District of Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – A block of land of Assmt. No. 80 of Kajugahawatta,
Dehiwala Road – Extent: 0.0304 Hectare
North: Salmal Mawatha, the land bearing Assmt. No. 12/3; East
and South: The premises of Sunethradevi Balika Vidyalaya; West:
Dehiwala Road, the land bearing Assmt. No. 80.

Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
