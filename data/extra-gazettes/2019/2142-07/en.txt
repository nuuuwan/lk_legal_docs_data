Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance do hereby this Order  amend the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2049/6 dated December 13, 2017 as follows :
1. By the addition immediately after subparagraph (g) of paragraph 2 as introduce by the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2113/10 dated March 05, 2019 of the addition of the following subparagaphs
h) “Any goods imported by any enterprise, which invests US $50 million or above on a project on or after
March 06, 2019, during the project implementation or construction period of the said project but prior
to commencement of commercial operations.
i) Any goods imported by any enterprise which invests on a project to establish climate controlled warehouse
facility/cold chain logistics infarstructure facility, subject to approval of list of goods, as classified
under the Harmonized System of Commodity classification (08 Digit basis) by  the secretary to the
Ministry of Finance on the recommendation of the Secretary in charge of the subject of the Food
Commissioner’s Department during the project implementation or construction period of the said project
but prior to commencement of commercial operations. Provisions of this paragraph shall be valid for 3
years from the date of publication of this Order”
