FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARANDENIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Ilandari
Dewa Samantha Kumar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  United National Party  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mohomad Kiyas Mohomad Siras  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Karandeniya  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mohomad
Kiyas Mohomad  Siras was selected as the member of the Karandeniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
17th June, 2019.
