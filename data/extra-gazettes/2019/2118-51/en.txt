PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 10.04.2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER PROVISO (a) TO SECTION 38
Order No. 75 of 2019
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2018/CEB/162.
Sec. Ref. No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/43.
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Proviso (a) to Section 38 of
the Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) I, Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms do hereby direct the
Acquiring Officer or any other officer authorized by him referred to
in Paragraph 1 of the Schedule to take immediate possession of the
land specified in the corresponding entry in Paragraph 2 of that
Schedule.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.

SCHEDULE
01. Acquiring Officer.– Divisional Secretary, Mannar Town.
02. Description of the land.– Four allotments of land depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. Ma/MNN/2018/159 dated 13.02.2019
prepared by the Surveyor-General situated in the Village called
Thoddaveli in the Grama Niladhari Division No. MN/62 of Thoddaveli
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mannar Town of Mannar
District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thoddakani – Extent: 0.1850 Hectare
North: Thoddakani claimant not known; East: Thoddakani,
claimant not known and Thoddakani claimed by Four Teess Rest Inn
and Lots B and C; South: Lots B, C and D; West: Lot D and
Thoddakani, claimant not known.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Thoddakani – Extent: 0.4560 Hectare
North: Lot A, Thoddakani claimant not known and Thoddakani
claimed by Four Teess Rest Inn; East: Thoddakani claimed by Four
Teess Rest Inn and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot C;
West: Lots C and A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Thoddakani – Extent: 0.4560 Hectare
North: Lots A and B; East: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road
(R.D.A.) and Lot D; West: Lots D and A.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Thoddakani – Extent: 1.1030 Hectare
North: Thoddakani claimant not known and Lots A, C; East: Lots
A and C and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Thoddakani,
claimant not known.
