 NOTIFICATION   UNDER  SECTION  16  SUB-SECTIONS   (2D)   OF   THE   COAST  CONSERV ATION  AND
COASTAL  RESOURCE   MANAGEMENT  ACT,   No.  57  OF   1981
Proposed Liquefied Natural Gas (LNA) Infrastructure Development Project
Colombo Port, Kalanitissa, Kerawalapitiya
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT  ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Liquefied Natural Gas
(LNG) Infrastructure Development Project at Colombo Port, Kalanitissa, Kerawalapitiya submitted by the Sri Lanka
Ports Authority, No. 19, Chaithya Road, Colombo 01 on behalf of the Sri Lanka Gas Terminals Company Ltd as required
by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conversation and Coastal Resource Managment Act, No. 57 of 1981 as amended by the
Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following institutions from
8.30 a. m. to 4.15 p. m. for a period of 30 days from 25.11.2019 (Except weekends and public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official website (www.coastal.gov.lk) of the
Department of Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management.
(1) Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor,
Maligawatte, Colombo 10.
(2) Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources New Secretariat Building, 3rd Floor, Maligawatta, Colombo
10
(3) Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa” Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
(4) Ceylon Petroleum Corporation, No. 609, Dr. Danister De Silva Mawatha, Colombo 09
