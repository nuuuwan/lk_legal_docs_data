FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KAHAWATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member in Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Gasperalalage Susil Sisira Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Manathunga Arachchige Jagath Shantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Manathunga
Arachchige Jagath Shantha  was selected as the member of the Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Kahawatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
04th April, 2019.
