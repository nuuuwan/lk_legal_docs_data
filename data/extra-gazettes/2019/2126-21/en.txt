L.D.B 21/78(XV)(A)
 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, the Minister of   City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on   the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the  said Act,  published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7  of  January 24, 1984 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2111/12  of   February 18, 2019), establishing the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after
the entry “3. Department of Marketing” in Column II  of the Schedule  which corresponds to the item ‘‘Faculty of
Management and Finance’’  in  Column I of that Schedule, of the  following  entry :-
          “4. Department of Human Resource Management’’.
A. I
BA THUL RAUFF  HAKEEM,
Minister of   City Planning, Water Supply and
Higher Education.
Colombo,
03rd  June  2019.
