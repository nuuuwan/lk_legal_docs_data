L.D.B 9/73 (xii)
THE   FAUNA  AND   FLORA  PROTECTION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  469)
Order under Subsection (2) of Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me subsection (2) of section 2 of the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance (Chapter 469),
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amaratunga, Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, do by
this Order declare the area of land described in the Schedule hereto to be a Sanctuary for the purposes of the aforesaid
Ordinance and further declare that such sanctuary shall be known as the “Great Basses and Little Basses Marine Sanctuary”.
J
OHN ANTHONY EMMANUEL AMARA TUNGA,
Minister of Tourism Development,
Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
