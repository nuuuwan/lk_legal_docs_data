EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDEMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964   
 
Notice under Section 07  
 
My  Reference No.: DIK/LAN/02/60. 
  
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 07, 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendement) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:-  
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary plan                 
No. 2797 of Surveyor General Matara and situated in 
village of Belideniya, Grama Niladhari Division of 
Belideniya in Dickwella Divisional Secretariat of Matara 
District. 
 
1. Lot No.  01 
Name of the land: Mataraaththannehena Alias 
Mataraattannewatta; Description: Home Garden, there is a 
mix cultivation; Claimed by: Gamini Wijendra, Belideniya, 
Kottagoda; Extent: 0.3103 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby  
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Dickwella 
Divisional Secretary’s Office on the 10th day of July 
2019 at 10.30 a.m. are hereby requested to Notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 03rd of July, 
2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars 
of their claims for compensation, for the Acquisition of 
the said lands the amount of compensation and the details 
of the computation of such amount. All deeds and 
documents in support of the claims should be produced 
before me at the inquiry. 
 
 B. S. R
ANJITHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
  Dickwella 
 
Divisional Secretariat Dickwella, 
03rd June, 2019.
