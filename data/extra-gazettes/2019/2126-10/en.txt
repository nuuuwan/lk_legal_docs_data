 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of   City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on   the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act,
establishing the South  Eastern University of  Sri Lanka,  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 916/7  of  March 27,
1996 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2100/12  of   December  05, 2018), by the
addition  immediately  after the entry ‘‘ (c). Department of  Management and Information Technology’’  appearing in
Column II  of the Schedule II to such Order, which corresponds to the item ‘‘Faculty of Management and Commerce’’
appearing in  Column I of that Schedule  of the  following new entry :-
          “(d). Department of Marketing Management’’.
A. I
BA THUL RAUFF  HAKEEM,
Minister of   City Planning, Water Supply and
Higher Education.
Colombo,
03rd  June  2019.
