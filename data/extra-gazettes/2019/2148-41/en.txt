THE NATIONAL THOROUGHFARES ACT, No. 40 OF 2008
Order under Section 9
         
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 9 (1) (a) of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir 
Hashim, Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum Resources Development, do by this Order designate the 
National Highways which were declared as a National Highways by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2148/5 
of 04.11.2019 and speciﬁ ed in Schedule to this Order to be an Expressways.
K
ABIR HASHIM,
Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum 
Resources Development.
Battaramulla,
08th November, 2019.
