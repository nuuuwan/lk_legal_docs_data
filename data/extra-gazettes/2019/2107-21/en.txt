No. 2107/21 – TUESDAY, JANUARY 22, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Co/
KDW/2017/34 dated 13.11.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Thalangama North (within the Municipal
Council limits of Kaduwela) in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 477-A of Thalangama North B in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kaduwela in Colombo District in Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Dombagahawatta – Extent: 0.1374 Hectare
North: Dombagahawatta claimed by Road Passenger Transport
Authority and New Kandy Road; East: New Kandy Road,
Road (R.D.A.) and Lot No. 2; South: Lot No. 2 and Dombagahawatta
claimed by Road Passenger Transport Authority; West:
Dombagahawatta claimed by Road Passenger Transport Authority.
02. Lot No. 2 – Dombagahawatta – Extent: 0.0861 Hectare
North: Lot No. 1 and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.) and
Dombagahawatta claimed by Road Passenger Transport Authority;
South: Dombagahawatta claimed by Road Passenger Transport
Authority; West: Dombagahawatta claimed by Road Passenger
Transport Authority and Lot No. 1.
