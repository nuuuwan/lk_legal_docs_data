FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member of  Kalmunai  Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Front of Good Governance, the recognized political party to which
Abdul Gafoor Mohammathu Natheer  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  National Front of Good Governance  who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Thajutheen Mohammathu Mufaris to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer  of   Kalmunai  Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thajutheen
Mohammathu Mufaris was selected as the member of the Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
T
HILINA   W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
13th June, 2019.
