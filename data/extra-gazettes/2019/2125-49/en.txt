Land Acquisition Act amended by Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 (Chapter 460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
My Ref No.: NIDS/LND05/ACQ70. 
 Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2014/HA/200. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of lands in total extent of 0.0145 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 1609, 1610, 1611 and 1612 in the 
sheet No. 90 supplement No. 97 of F. V. P. 36 dated 
28.11.2018 situated in the village called Nivithigala in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Nivithigala in 
Nawadunkorale Minor Division, in the Divisional 
Secretary’s division of Nivithigala in Rathnapura district 
of Sabaragamuwa Province and morefully described as 
follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 1609 
Name of lan d: Vah alboda Kan ath tha 
(Vahalugodakanaththehena) Radaliyadda Kumbura; 
Original Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent: 0.0078 
Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 1610 
Name of lan d: Vah alboda Kan ath tha 
(Vahalugodakanaththehena); Original Claimant: Could 
not be traced; Extent: 0.0012 Hectare. 
03. Lot No. 1611 
Name of lan d: Vah alboda Kan ath th a 
(Vahalugodakanaththehena); Original Claimant: Could 
not be traced; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare. 
 
01. Lot No. 1612 
Name of lan d: Vah alboda Kan ath th a 
(Vahalugodakanaththehena) ;Original Claimant: Mr. D. 
P. Senevirathne; Extent: 0.0050 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/Lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are herby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Gampaha Divisional Secretariat on 
the 28th August day of 2019 at 2.00 p. m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 21st   August 2019 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
