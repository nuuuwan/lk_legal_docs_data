EASTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Provincial Councils Notification of Eastern province
CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES  LAW  No. 5  OF 1972  AS  AMENDED  BY  CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 32 OF 1983 AND  THE  CO-OPERATIVE  SOCIETIES  (AMENDMENT)  ACT, No. 11 OF
1992 READ  IN  CONJUNCTION  WITH  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  (CONSEQUENTIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT No. 12 OF 1989
CONFERMENT  OF POWERS
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by section 2(2) of the Co-operative societies Law No.5 of 1972 as amended by
Co-operative societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of
1992 read in conjunction with provincial council (Consequential Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989, I, Shan Wijayalal De
Silva Governor of Eastern Province do hereby confer on each of the persons named in column I in the schedule hereto,
who have been appointed to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar of Co-operative societies
of the Eastern province, such powers of the Registrar under the Co-operative societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 as amended by
Co-operative societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of
1992 and the rules there under and which are more fully described in Column III of the said schedule in respect of their
area with effect from the dates shown in Column II of the said schedule, to be exercised by the aforesaid during their
tenure of office.
S
HAN WIJAYALAL DE SIL V A,
Governor,
Eastern Province.
Trincomalee,
25th October 2019.
