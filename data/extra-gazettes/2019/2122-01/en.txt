THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
              Reference No.: DSB/LND/03/FI/35/XVI-02/2019.
Land Ministry's Ref. No. : 4/3/6/2018/HW/38.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 2090 and 2100 (Lots 11) allotments in total extent of
about 2.1920 Hectares, of land in Paln No. F.V .P . 296 and
Suppliment No. 60 and Sheet No. 54 and dated 03.04.2019
prepared by the Senior - Superintendent of Surveyor of
Hambanthota District on behalf of the Surveyor General,
situated in the village of  Gatamanna, in the Grama Niladhari
Divisions of  No. 338, Nayakawatta in  the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Beliatta in  Hambanthota District in
the Southern Province.
