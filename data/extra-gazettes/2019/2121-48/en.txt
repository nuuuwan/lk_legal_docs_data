FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignations ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Iyampillai
Ashokkumar the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned  member of  Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Velayutham Gajan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer of the Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Velayutham
Gajan  was selected as the member of the Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
30th April, 2019.
