THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I , Gayantha Karunatilleke , Minister of  Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0885 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Ka/Ber/2018/59 and 11.05.2018
prepared by the Surveyor- General situated in the Village of Aluthgama
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Beruwala in Kalutara District
Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  A - Dehigahawatta - Extent: 0.0885 Hectare
North: Road, Dehigahawatta claimed  by Indunil and Road; East:
Dehigahawatta claimed  by Indunil, Road and Welipanna Road; South:
Welipanna Road - Lindamulagewatta claimed by Sangamiththa
Vidyalaya in Aluthgama and Sellagewatta; West: Lindamulagewatta
claimed by Sangamiththa Vidyalaya in Aluthgama and Sellagewatta
and Road.
                                                  Gayantha Karunatilleke,
                                Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
