No. 2116/10  - TUESDAY,  MARCH  26, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PATHAHEWAHETA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasinghe Mudiyanselage Upual Bandara Weerasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that 16-Hewawissa of Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya Sabha
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall
be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Wagaha Mulagedara Majee Sulbika Marikkar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of the Pathahewaheta
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Wagaha Mulagedara Majee Sulbika Marikkar was elected as the member of the 16-Hewawissa of  Pathahewaheta
Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
26th March, 2019.
