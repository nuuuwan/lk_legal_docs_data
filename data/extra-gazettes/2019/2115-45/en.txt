(2) by the repeal of paragraph 10 thereof and the substitution therefor of the following :
“10. (1) The Board of Management (hereinafter referred to as the “Board”) shall be the
academic and executive body of the Institute, and shall consist of :-
(a) the following ex-officio members :-
(i) the Director ;
(ii) the Deputy Director ;
(iii) the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister assigned the subject of Higher Education
or the nominee of such Secretary ;
(iv) the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister assigned the subject of Health or the
nominee of such Secretary ;
(v) the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister assigned the subject of Finance or the
nominee of such Secretary ;
(vi) the Director General of Health Service (hereinafter referred to as the “Director
General”) ;
(b) the following other members :-
(i) one member each nominated by the Faculty Board of the Faculties of Medicine,
Medical Sciences, Medical and Allied Sciences, Health Care Sciences of the
Universities established under the Act, as elected by the Faculty Board of each such
Faculty, from among the Senior Professors, Professors, and Associate Professors
in the permanent cadre of each Faculty ;
(ii) one member each nominated by the Faculty Board of the Faculties of Dental Sciences
of the Universities established under the Act, as elected by the Faculty Board of
each Faculty, from among the Senior Professors, Professors, and Associate
Professors in the permanent cadre of each such Faculty ;
(iii) two members nominated by the University Council from among the members
appointed by the Commission to the Council ; and
(iv) seven members appointed by the Commission from among persons who have
rendered distinguished service in educational, professional, commercial, industrial,
legal, scientific or administrative spheres.
(2) The Board may co-opt any person to the Board for a specified period having experience and knowledge in a
special area relating to a specific purpose that may be dealt with by the Board. Any person so co-opted shall not be a
member of the Board and shall not have any right to vote on any matter being decided at any meeting of the Board, but shall
attend meetings thereof when requested to do so by the Board.”
