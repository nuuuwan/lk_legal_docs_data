No. 2152/9  - TUESDAY  DECEMBER  03, 2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VA VUNIYA  (TAMIL)  SOUTH
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya  (Tamil) South Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Eelam People’s Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which
Selvarasa Reegan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of Eelam People’s Democratic Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Tharmakunasingam Sujivan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya  (Tamil) South
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Tharmakunasingam Sujivan was selected as the member of the Vavuniya  (Tamil) South Pradeshiya
Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya  (Tamil)  South Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd December, 2019.
