1A   G   29362—27  (01/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   SRI  JAYAWARDANAPURA   KOTTE
MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Ranawaka Aarachchige Don Janaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon  returned  member if United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Vidyapathi Tharakaratne Gorakgoda Namage Prabath to fill such vacancy ;
NOW  THEREFORE I, Pathiraja Arachchilage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of the Sri Jayawardanapura
Kotte Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare that Vidyapathi Tharakaratne Gorakgoda Namage Prabath  was selected as the member of the Sri
Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARA TNE,
Returning Officer,
Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
30th January, 2019.
