THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of
June 09, 2006 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2025/39 of June 30, 2017) which
specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the
provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the
addition immediately after the entry “17. Department of Allied Health Sciences” appearing in Column II of the Schedule
which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Medicine” appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the following entry :-
 “18. Department of Family Medicine”.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
03rd  March, 2019.
