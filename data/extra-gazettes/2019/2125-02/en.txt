FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PANNALA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of  Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group, to which Adhikari Mudiyanselage Nalini Gunasena the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned
member of Independent Group who belongs to such Independent Group, and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Liyana Pathirannehelage Chandrapala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Pannala  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Liyana
Pathirannehelage Chandrapala  was selected as the member of the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
24th May, 2019.
