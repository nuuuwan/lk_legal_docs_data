Land Reform Commission Notices
LAND   REFORM   COMMISSION
Notice under Section 29 of the Land Reform Law No. 1 of 1972 as amended by Land Reform (Special
Provision) Act, No. 39 of 1981 and Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 14 of 1986
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 29 of the law, I, hereby direct every person who is interested in any
agricultural land or lands described in the Schedule hereto immediately before the date on which such land or lands have
been vested in the Land Reform Commission to make, within one month from 25.04.2019 a written claim in triplicate to
the whole or any part of the compensation payable under the law in respect of such land or lands and to specify in the
claim.
a) His name and address
b) Nature of his interest in such land/lands
c) Particulars of his claim and
d) How much of such compensation is claimed by him
All claims should be sent by registered post, addressed to the Chairman, Land Reform Commission.
S
IRIMEW AN D IAS,
Attorney-at-Law,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
Office of the Land Reform Commission,
No. 475, Kaduwela Road,
Battaramulla,
22nd March, 2019.
