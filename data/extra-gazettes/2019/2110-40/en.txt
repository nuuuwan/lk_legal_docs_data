SPORTS   ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under  32 and 33
I, Harin Fernando, the Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports in pursuance of powers vested in me
in terms of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby appoint the following three officers with effect from 14.02.2019.
1. Mr. Liyanaarachchi Appuhamilage Deepal Liyanaarchchi - V ice President
2. Mr. Wanninayaka Mudiyanselage W asantha Sisira Wanninayaka - T reasurer
3. Mr. Mitimanaye Judith Priyani Perera - Secretary
as a provisional arrangement until such time a lawful election is held of the Sri Lanka Badminton, the National
Association for the Sports of Badminton, of which registration has at present been suspended and consequent 03 positions
have fallen vacant whereas,
1. G . G. Mangala - Treasurer
who was a member of  the first Interim Committee appointed by Gazette Notification  No. 2053/06 published on
08.01.2018 and whereas.
1. Kariyawasam Gonapinuwala Gamage Anjan Mohan Wijesinghe - Secretary
2. Wasantha Kumara - Vice President
Who were appointed as members of that Committee by Gazette Notification No 2066/41 dated 12.04.2018 have
resigned.
Moreover, I do hereby authorize to Interim Committee to convene a special General Meeting of the Sri Lanka
Badminton in terms of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 and the amended Regulations thereof and appoint an Election
Committee under the Supervision of the Director General of the Department of Sports Development.
HARIN F ERNANDO (MP),
Minister of  Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
14th February 2019.
