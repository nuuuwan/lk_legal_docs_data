5th AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Architects Service
THE  Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Architects Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1877/28 dated 28th of
August, 2014 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1882/18 dated
01st of October 2014, No. 1952/20 dated 02nd of February 2016, No.1994/68 dated 24th of November 2016 and No. 2085/46
dated 24th of August 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended as follows and these amended
provisions will be made effective from 26th of February 2019, the date on which the Cabinet of Ministers made the policy
decision.
                                                                    By order of the Public Service Commission,
M. A. B. D
AYA SENARA TH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
07th August, 2019.
