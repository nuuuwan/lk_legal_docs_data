THE  PILGRIMAGE   ORDINANCE
The Annual Feast Pilgrimage of our Lady of Holy Rosary Shrine, Periyapullumalai - 2019
THE Annual feast and the  Pilgrimage of our Lady of Holy Rosary Shrine, Periyapullumalai - 2019. It is hereby for the
information of the Pilgrims who attend the above feast and the others concerned that the annual feast for the year 2019
begins on the 26th of April (26.04.2019) with hoisting of flags, and ends on the 05th of  May with the concelebrated
Eucharistic  Celebration.
The special event of  the feast would be a Pilgrimage which begins on 03rd of May at 3.30 p. m. from St. Mary’s
Cathedral, Puliyanthivu, Batticaloa and proceeds through Trinco Road, Chenkalady, Baddulla Road.
The attention of  the Pilgrims who attend the above feast and others concerned is drawn to the regulation published
in the Government Gazette No. 9886 of 16.07.1984 which will be inforce during the period of the feast.
N. VILV ARETNAM,
Divisional Secretary,
Eravur Pattu.
