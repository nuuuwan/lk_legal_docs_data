PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  NOTIFICATIONS
CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Imposing of  Entertainment Taxes by the Kandy Municipal Council
The following resolution passed by the Kandy Municipal Council under decision No. 8 (23) at the Gene ral Meeting held on 20.12.2018
in terms of Sub - section (I) of Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267) has been  approved by the Hon. Governor of
the Central Province in terms of Sub - section (2) of that Section and is hereby published in terms of  that Sub - Section.
MAITHREE GUNARATNE,
Hon. Governor,
Central Province.
At Governor’s Office,
On this 22nd day of April, 2019.
RESOLUTION
In accordance with Section 2 (1) of the Entertainment Tax ordinance and Entertainment Tax (Amended) Act, No. 37 of 1984, the Kandy
Municipal Council hereby resolves to impose and levy an entertainment Tax equivalent to 25% of the f ace value of entry tickets for every
magic show, circus, horse show, musical show, exhibition match, carnival and any other event where a fee is charged and an entertainment
tax equivalent to 7.5% of the face value of entry tickets to films in cinemas hed in the area within  the administrative limits of the Kandy
Municipal Council.
