( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. R. P. Ananda Sagara, No. 8/4 A, Annasiwatta, Halgampitiya, Kalagedihena.
AND
Sri  Lanka Ports Authority, No. 19, Chaithya Road, Colombo 01.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether Mr. R. P. Ananda Sagara, who had worked as a Security Guard of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority from 10.02.1987 and
found with unauthorized goods in the van owned by him in which he was travelling with 03 others after the days work on
05.05.2008, following which 01 person had been released and an internal disciplinary inquiry had been conducted against
03 after which 02 of them were reinstated in service and only the complainant was terminated from employment, had been
caused injustice by being the only person not to be reinstated in employment even after all the 03 accused persons were
acquitted and discharged in a case filed at Magistrate Court, Colombo regarding the same incident and if so, to what relief he
is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 23rd day of January, 2019.
