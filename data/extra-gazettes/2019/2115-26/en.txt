SPORTS  ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulation under Sections 32 and 33
I, Harin Fernando, the Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports in pursuance of powers vested in
me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby appoint Dr. (Mrs.) Daminda Aththanayake
with effect from 18.03.2019 in place of Ashen Fernando, a member of the former Interim Committee appointed by
Gazette Notification No. 2097/49 dated 16.11.2018, until such time a lawful election of the Sri Lanka Aquatic Association,
the National Sports Union for Aquatic Sports, is held as a provisional arrangement.
H
ARIN F ERNANDO ,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
18th March, 2019.
