Notice under Section 7
My No.: KEG/LAN/AQUIS/A3/1/84.
Land Ministry's Ref. No .: 4-3/15/2016/HW/580.
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the land called Radagolahela
Kumbura described in the Schedule given below for
construction of a retaining wall in the boundary place of
0+420 km of Kegalle By Pass (Phase 11) of Kegalle
Divisional Secretariat Division.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land attached below depicted in
Preliminary Plan No. 3567 prepared by the Surveyor
General, Kegalle situated in the Golahela village (Within
Kegalle Town limits) of 15E, Golahela Grama Niladhari
Division in Kegalle Divisional Secretary's Division of
Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
SCHEDULE
Plan No. P.P. K. 3567
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Radagolahela Kumbura; Description
of the Boundaries: Name and Address of claimant: First
claimants are Gamaralalage Nimal Jayantha Abeywardana of
No. 171, Main Street, Kegalle, Disanayake Halawath
Mudiyanselage Anuruddhika Manori Wijesinghe of No. B92,
Gepala, Ekiriyagala, Kegalle and H.M.P.T. Tishan Wijesinghe
of No. 292, Bandaranayaka Mawatha, Hospital Junction,
Kegalle; Extent: 0.0248 Hectare.
