Direction No. 71
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders that the following information
shall be legibly  printed in Sinhala, Tamil and English lanaguages on all packs, containers or on the wrappers of all articles/
goods  specified in the Schedule hereto :
Maximum Retail Price
Batch Number
Date of Expiry
Date of Manufacturer
Net Weight/V olume
Country of Origin/Country of manufacturer
Date of Repacking (Products which are imported in bulk and repacked or products manufactured and packed in bulk and
repacked)
