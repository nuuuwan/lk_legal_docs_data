THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and  Higher  Education, on the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the  said Act and
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 896/2  of  November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2079/13  of   July 09, 2018), which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the Raja Rata University
of Sri Lanka, by the addition  immediately  after the entry ‘‘6. Department of  English Language Teaching’’  appearing in
Column II   of  the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities” appearing in
Column I of that Schedule of the  following  entry :-
          “7. Department of Economics’’.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of  City Planning, Water Supply
and  Higher Education.
Colombo,
06th March, 2019.
