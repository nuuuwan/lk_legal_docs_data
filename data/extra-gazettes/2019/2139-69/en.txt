THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, the Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of land called ‘‘Winchfieldpark watta’’ in extent
about 0 Acres, 03 Roods and 28 Perches, depicted as Lot ‘‘23’’ in the
P. Plan No. 1930 prepared by the Superintendent of survey – Kandy
on 31.10.1979 situated in Pusse-ela village in the Gandeka Korale in
Udadumbara Divisional Secretariat in Kandy District in bounded as
follows:
01. Lot No. 23 – Winchfieldpark watta – Extent: 03 Roods
and 28 Perches
North: Lot Nos. 27 and 22; East: Lot No. 21; South: Lot Nos. 21
and 24; West: Lot Nos. 24, 26 and 27.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
