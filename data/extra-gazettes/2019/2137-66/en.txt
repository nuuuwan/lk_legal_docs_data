5TH AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Accountants Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Accountants Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1670/33 dated
10th of September, 2010 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary
No. 1695/15 dated 01st of March 2011, No. 1865/36 dated 06th of June, 2014, No. 1981/99 dated 27th of August 2016
and, No. 2070/7 dated 09th of May 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended as follows and
these amended provisions will be made effective from 26
th of February 2019, the date on which the Cabinet of Ministers
made the policy decision.

M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
