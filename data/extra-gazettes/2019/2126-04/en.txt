FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   CHILAW  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Chilaw pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Balapuwaduge Nuwan Sanjeeva Mendis the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  member of  that 13 Pothuvila of Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ranasinghe Arachchige Amila Rangana Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer of the Chilaw pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ranasinghe
Arachchige Amila Rangana Kumara  was elected as the member of the 13 Pothuvila of Chilaw pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Chilaw pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
31st May, 2019.
