PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
34th Higher Ordination Ceremony of Sri Rohana Maha Sangasabha of
Syamopali Wanshika Maha Nikaya
IT is hereby informed that the 34th Higher Ordination Ceremony of Sri Rohana Sanga Sabha in Divisional Secretary's Division
of Kamburupitiya, Matara District will be held on 11th, 12th and 13th of May 2019 at Rohana Sanga Sabha Headquarters of
Siri Rathanajothi Temple of Urapola and pilgrims should refrain from carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials
and standing orders and regulations under Section 2 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175-L.D.B. 11/60) declared on 14th
October 1991 and published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka will be in force during the period in
which the ceremony is held.
NILMINI  HERATH,
Divisional Secretary,
Kamburupitiya.
