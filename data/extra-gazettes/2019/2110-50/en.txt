EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
AS AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
My Reference No.: KEG /LAN/AQUIS/A3/1/96. 
 Land  Min. No. : 4/3/5/2014/HW/64. 
 
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government  intends to acquire the additional land lots 
described in the schedule given below for construction of 
phase 111 of Alternative Highway, Kegalle 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
One (01) allotment of  land attached below depicted in 
Preliminary Plan No. 3666 prepared by Surveyor General, 
Kegalle situated in 51E Golahela Town limits Grama 
Niladhari Division of Kegalle Divisional Secretary’s 
Division in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
Dates of Inquries in P.P. K. 3666 
 
Lot No. 01; 21.03.2019 from 10.00 a. m. 
 
 
 
 Acquisition of  Land lots for  construction of  Kegalle 
Alternative Highway 
Schedule Plan No. P. P. K. 3666 
 
Lot No. 01 — Name of the Land: No. 252, 
Bandaranayake Mawatha, Kegalle; Name and Address of 
Claimant: C. N. C. Manathunga, No. 252, Bandaranayake 
Mawatha, Kegalla; Extent: 0.0140 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid  lands to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat, Kegalle on the above mentioned date and on 
the scheduled time. 
 
 And  are hereby requested to  notify to me in writing  
with duplicate of on or before 07th of March 2019 the 
nature of their interest in the land the particulars of their 
claims for compensation, for acquisition of the said land, 
and the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
  
 K. G. S. N ISHANTHA, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Land Acquisition Officer, 
 Kegalle. 
  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kegalle.
