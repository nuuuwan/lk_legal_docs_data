THE   LAND    ACQUISITION    ACT   (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 My Reference No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/12'
                            Land Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/249.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that
the Government intends to acquire the following land which
is  required for a public purpose.
     Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired:
The land called Doctorthoddam, Elanthalirodai in total
extent of about 3.4560 Hectares depicted as Lot  Nos. 1, 2 and
3 in Plan No. MN779 and prepared by the Surveyor General
situated in the Village called Nadukkuda, in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 54 of Tullukkudiyiruppu, in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Mannar Town of Mannar District in
Northern Province.
Plan No. MN 779.
01. Lot Nos. 01, 02 and  03
Name of Land: Doctorthoddam  Elanthalirodai; Present use
of the land: Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original
  G 29223 - 17 (2019/01)
Claimant: Seylan Business (Pvt) Ltd of No. 10/18 Lake Drive,
Colombo 08; Extent: 3.4560 Hectares.
    All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a  servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before
me at 10.00 a.m. on the 15th February, 2019 at Mannar Town
Divisional Secretariat.
  And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08th February, 2019 the
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for the compensation for the acquisition of the said
land/servitude, the mount of compensation and the details of
the computation of such amount.
       K. SIVASAMBU,
Divisional Secretary,
             Mannar Town Division.
Divisional Secretariat,
Mannar Town,
07th January, 2019.
