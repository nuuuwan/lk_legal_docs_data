Notice under Section 7
My No.: BI/E/3/Road-3/Acquisitions.
Land Ministry No.: 4/3/7/2011/HW/154.
IT is hereby notified under Section 7(1) of the Land
Acquisition Act since the Government intends to acquire the
following lands required for the expansion of the Peradeniya
- Badulla - Chenkaladi Road.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
In the last map of the village map prepared by the Survey
Superintendent of Monaragala in Bibila in the Monaragala
District of the Uva Province, the Survey Plan of the F.V .P . 573
Supplement No. 87 of the Sheet No. 88 is as follows:
Total Extent of the Hectares 0.0003.
01. Lot No. 1392
Name of the Land: –; Claimant: Government; Encroacher:
W . L. Nandasiri; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
All persons who claim rights, rights or any other kind of
grounds of origin are the authorized persons or authorized
persons of their respective places or may contact the Bibila
Divisional Secretariat on the 28th October, 2019.
Further the nature of their rights and the nature of their
rights before the date of 21st October, 2019 should be notified
to me by a document with two copies of the particulars of the
request for compensation and the manner of calculating
compensation and the amount of compensation.
UPEKSHA N. WIJESINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Bibile.
