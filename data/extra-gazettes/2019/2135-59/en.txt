No. 2135/59 –  THURSDAY  AUGUST,  08, 2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  ANNUAL  FEAST  OF  THE  OUR  LADY’S   RAGAMA  LANKA  BASILLICA  CHURCH  2019 
 
Gampaha District 
 
 
IT is announced for the general  public to inform that , The Annual Feast of the Ragama Thewatta Lanka Basillika 
Church 2019 (Feast of the Patients) will  be held on 25th  of August in 2019 and from 18.08.2019 to 27.08.2019 as the 
duration of the Annual Feast. 
 
 1.  This will be brought to the attention of the general public on the regulations published in Gazette No. 1953/43 
dated 12th February 2016 under the Pilgrimages Ordinance. 
 2.  These publicity –issued laws will be valid during the period of this ceremony. 
 
  
 S UNIL JAYALATH 
 District Secretary / Government Agent, 
 Gampaha. 
 
07th of August 2019, 
At District Secretariat Gampaha.
