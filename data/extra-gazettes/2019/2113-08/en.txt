EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notificatioin No. 03/2019
EXCISE  DUTY   ON  LOCALLY   PRODUCTED   WINE
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to time,
I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance do by this Order direct that with effect from March 06, 2019 :
(1) There shall be levied on liquors produced from local plant materials or plant products or potable local animal
milk, containing not more than 18% by volume of alcohol manufactured by process other than distillation in Sri
Lanka, shall be levied a duty at the rate of Rupees Six Hundred (Rs. 600.00) per litre of alcohol, that such duty
shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid liquor, which is exported out of the island.
(2) The Excise Notification, No. 946 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1733/4 of 21 November 2011 is
hereby rescinded.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
March 05, 2019.
