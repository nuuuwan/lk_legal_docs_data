No. 2135/53 – WEDNESDAY , AUGUST 07, 2019
Direction No. 68
Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the Powers vested in it by Section 10 (1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders that the following information shall
be legibly printed in Sinhala, Tamil and English languages on all packs, containers or on the wrappers of all articles/goods
specified in the Schedule hereto:
Maximum Retail Price
Batch Number
Date of Expiry
Date of Manufacture
Net weight/V olume
Country of Origin/Country of manufacture
Date of Repacking (Products which are imported in bulk and repacked or products manufactured and packed in bulk and
repacked)
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
