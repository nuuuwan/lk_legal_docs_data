No. 2122$46 – THURSDAY, MAY 09, 2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, the Minister of Lands and  Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby declare under  the subsection (1) of the section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act that land described below is  required for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of  land called ‘‘Winchfieldpark Watta’’ in extent of
about 12.9745 Hectares, depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’ in the Advance Tracing
No. Maha/UDD/2013/1278 prepared by the Superintendent of
Survey’s Kandy on 25.09.2013, situated in Karambakatiya Village
in  Gandeka Korale in Udadumbara Divisional Secretary’s Division
in Kandy District is bounded as follows;
01. Lot ‘A’- Winchfieldpark Watta- Extent:12.9745 Hectares
North: Winchfieldpark Watta claimed by Bank of Ceylon (Kandy)
and stream; East: Stream and Winchfieldpark Watta claimed by R.M.
Rathnayake; South: Winchfieldpark Watta claimed by R.M.
Rathnayake and ‘‘Winchfieldpark Watta’’ claimed by Bank of Ceylon
(Kandy); West: Winchfieldpark Watta claimed by Bank of Ceylon
(Kandy).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
Ref: No. : 13/97/FR/124.
"Mihikatha Medura",

Battaramulla.
