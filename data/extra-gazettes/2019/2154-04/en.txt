I hereby give notice, under Sub-section(1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land in total extent of 0.0082 hectare 
depicted in sheet No. 16 of Block No. 05 of Cadastral 
Map No. 810022 as lot no 263,264 prepared by Survey 
General situated in village of Deddugoda in Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 101C, Deddugoda North, within 
Municipal Council limit of Galle in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Four Gravets Galle in District of 
Galle, Southern Province.  The names and extents of 
about named lots are as follows: 
 
Lot No. 263 
 
Name of the land: Mahaovita, assessment number 498, 
Hirimbura Road; Description: Home garden and Part of a 
permanent building; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 
Batuwanthudawa Kankanamge Kulavathi; Extent: about 
0.0074 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 264 
 
Name of the land:  Mahaovita, assessment number 500 A, 
Hirimbura Road; Description: Home garden;  Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: Jayantha Kariyawasam; Extent: 
about 0.0008 Hectare.
