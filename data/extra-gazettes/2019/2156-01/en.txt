No. 2156/1  -  MONDAY  DECEMBER  30,  2019
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, A. V . Janadara, Director General of Customs (Covering up duties), determine that with effect  from 30th  December,  2019, all
duties of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2155/1 of
23rd  December,  2019 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 05th January, 2020.
A. V . JANADARA,
Director General of Customs
(Covering up duties).
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
27th December, 2019.
