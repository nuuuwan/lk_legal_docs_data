STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. S. B. Udaya Pushpakumara working as a Unit Manager of the Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka who was
suspended on 19.09.2011 had been caused injustice by the Charge Sheet being served after two years from that date on
25.09.2013 and conduct of disciplinary inquiry or the disciplinary order being given on 15.02.2014, or both of these matters and
if so, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General  of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner  General of Labour,
Colombo this 09th day of March, 2019.
