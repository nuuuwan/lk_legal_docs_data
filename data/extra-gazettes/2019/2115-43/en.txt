INLAND   REVENUE  ACT,  No.  24  OF  2017
Notice under Section 86(2)(d)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 114 of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 24 of 2017 read with Paragraph
(d) of Subsection (2) of Section 86,  I, Asoka Nadun Guruge, Commissioner General of Inland Revenue, do by this Order
specify the Annual Statement of the Employer, Statement of the Withholding Tax and the Schedules thereof to be furnished
by any person in respect of any payment withheld under Section 83, Section 84 and Section 85 of the Inland Revenue Act,
No. 24 of 2017, as set out in the Schedule hereto.
A. N. G
URUGE,
Commissioner General of Inland Revenue.
Department of Inland Revenue,
Colombo 02,
22nd March, 2019.
SCHEDULE
1. Every withholding agent who withheld tax from payments under Section 83 of the Act shall file with the
Commissioner-General within thirty days after the end of each year ending on the  thirty first day of March, the
Annual Statement of the Employer and the Schedules in the format specified in Annexure I.
2. Every withholding agent who withheld tax from payments under Section 84 and 85 of the Act shall file with the
Commissioner-General within thirty days after the end of each year ending on the thirty first day of March the
Statement of the Withholding Tax and Schedules in the format specified in Annexure II.
3. The Commissioner General may amend the format of the Annual Statement of the Employer, the format of the
Statement of the Withholding Tax and the Schedules thereof time to time as he thinks necessary.
