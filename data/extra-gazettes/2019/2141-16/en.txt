 WELFARE   BENEFIT   ACT,  No.  24  OF  2002
BY virtue of the power vested in me under Section 09 of the Welfare Benefit Act, No. 24 of 2002, I, Mangala Samaraweera,
Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby amend the effective date of
implementation of methodology to identify low-income families for welfare benefit payments schemes published in the
Extraordinary Gazette No. 2128/24 dated 20th June 2019 as 01st January 2020.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
18th September, 2019.
