 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15 of
October 09, 2007 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2116/43 of March 27, 2019),
which specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the
provisions of section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the
addition immediately after the item “Faculty of Hindu Studies” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in
Column II thereof, of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Allied Health Sciences 1. Department of Nursing
2. Department of Pharmacy
3. Department of Medical Laboratory Sciences.”.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
29th August, 2019.
