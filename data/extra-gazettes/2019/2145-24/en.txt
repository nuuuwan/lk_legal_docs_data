(CHAPTER 351)
Order  under Section   03
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by section 03 of the Immigration and Emigration Act (Chapter 351) read with
paragraph (2) of article 44 of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I Vajira Abeywardena,
Minister of Internal and Home Affairs and Provincial Councils and Local Government, do this by order declare that the
Airports specified in the Schedule to this, shall be on “Approved port of Entry and Departure for the purposes of this Act.
