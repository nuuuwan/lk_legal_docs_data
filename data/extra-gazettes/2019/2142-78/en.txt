PUBLIC  UTILITIES  COMMISSION  OF  SRI   LANKA
Estimated Expenditure for the year 2020
1. Electricity Section
Outcome 01  : Improved productivity & convenience for electricity consumers Rs. 163,005,351
Major goals for outputs of activities  :
Goal 1 : All electicity consumers receive the statutory quality levels, 230 V + 6% for voltage an 50 Hz +
0.5% for frequency by the year 2025
Goal 2 : The total electricity outage time experienced by a consumer whithin year is below 24 hours (on
average basis) by the year 2025
Goal 3 : The total number of electricity interruptions experienced by a consumer within a year is below 30
(on average basis) by the year 2025
Goal 4 : The electricity breakdown restoration time for consumer service line faults is below 2 hours (on
average basis) by the year 2025
Goal 5 : Average time spent by a  consusmer to know his/her Rights and obligations in connection with the
electricity or supply of electricity is below 1 day by year the 2025
Goal 6 : The average time taken by an electricity service provider to serve consmuer inquiry/request/
complaint is below 14 days by the year 2025
Goal 7 : The average time taken by PUSCL to serve consumer is below 14 days by the year 2025
Goal 11 : Electricity generation capacity is installed to ensure that the electricity demands in the country
are met all the time and under any circumstances by the year 2030 and thereafter
