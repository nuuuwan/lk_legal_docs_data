FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  BERUWALA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Beruwala Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending for
council meetings period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Mohamed
Nalim Fathima Shiyama the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mohamed Hasan Fathima Rinusa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer of the Beruwala
Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Mohamed Hasan Fathima Rinusa was selected as the member of the Beruwala Urban Council.
K
ALINDRA J AYAWEERA F ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Beruwala Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
11th June, 2019.
