LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under  Section 7(1)
        Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/235.
My Reference No.: W AR/LAN/ACQUS/A2/5-70.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of section (7) of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amendeded by the land
acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that Government
intends to acquire the following land which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the land  in be acquired:
The following allotment of land depicted in the preliminary
Plan No. K 3625 dated 27.08.2018 prepared by the Sunior
Superintendent of Survey, Kegalle District for Surveyor
General, within the boundaries shown below situated in the
village of Ambakumbura in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 77, Waddeniya in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Warakapola in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Orengegrow Estate (Ambakumbura
Division); Description of the land: Estate; Claimant: Government;
First Claimant: Unknown; Extent: 0.0073 Hectare.
    All person interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing before me at Warakapola Divisional Secretariat on
26.06.2019 at 10.00 a.m.  Further you are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 12.06.2019
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and details of computation of
such amount.
LAXMENDRA D AMAYANTHKUMARA THENNAKOON,
Divisional Secretary,
Warakapola.
At the Warakapola Divisional Secretariat,
16th April, 2019.
