URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY   LAW  No.  41  OF  1978
Order  under Section  3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Patali
Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Magapolis & Western Development being of opinion that the area specified in Column
I of Schedule II hereto and having the meet’s and bounds specified in corresponding entry in Column II of Schedule II
except the area already declared as Urban Development Area and set out in Schedule I hereto are suitable for development
and do hereby this Order declare such areas for the purpose of the said Section.
I do hereby declare that, the already declared area set out in Schedule I together with the other areas declared as
aforesaid, shall be included within the limits of the expanded Ella Urban Development Area set out in Column II under
Schedule II hereto.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M. P.),
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis & Western Development,

Battaramulla,
21st April, 2019.
