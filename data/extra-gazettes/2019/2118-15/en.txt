FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   WALAPANE  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member of Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending for
council meetings for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Jagath Kumara Samarahewa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate  Jagath Kumara Samarahewa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Walapane  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Jagath Kumara
Samarahewa was selected as the member of the Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P ATHIRANA ,
Returning Officer,
Walapane Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
08th April, 2019.
