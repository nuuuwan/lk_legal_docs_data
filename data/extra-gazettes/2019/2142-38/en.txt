Declaration  of  Determination  of  the  Commissioner  of  Title  Settlement  under  Section  14

clare my determination as set out in the Schedule appended hereto in regard to the  title to parcel of Land Nos. 47, 48, 49, 50, 51, 52, 53,

gala within the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 174 A - Yakdehiwaththa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Nivithigala, in the 
District of Ratnapura, in the Province of Sabaragamuwa, referred to in Notice No. 62/0429 calling for claims to land parcels which was 
duly published in the Gazette No. 1973/9 of 28th June, 2016 in terms of Section 12 of the Registration of Title Act, No.21 of 1998.
