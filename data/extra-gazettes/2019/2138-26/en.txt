No. 2138/26 –  WEDNESDAY, AUGUST  28,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/PDB/4/3/2/4. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2015/WS /127. 
 
I, do hereby give notice that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land for a public purpose, under sub
-section (1) of  Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act 
(Chapter 460), as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964. 
 
Description of the acquired Area: 
 
Two allotment of land, stated as Lot No. 1 and 2 with 
extent of about 0.0078 Hectare, depicted in the 
preliminary Plan Maha/6918, surveyed and prepared by 
the superintendent of surveyor of Kandy District and 
situated in Aluthgama Mahakumbure Gammedda Village 
in Pallegampaha Korale minor Division of Pathadumbara 
division in Kandy District Central Province. 
 
Lot No. 1 - Name of the Land: -; Extent: 0.0074 
Hectare 
 
Boundaries by :  
 
North: Allotment 127 of PP/A/1742, Allotments 1 and 
2 of PP/Maha 6917; East: Allotment 2; South: Allotment 
127 of PP/A/1742; West: Road 
Name and Addressess of Claimants: The Government 
 
Lot No. 2 - Name of the Land: -; Extent: 0.0004 
Hectare  
Boundaries by :  
 
North: Allotments 1 and 2 of  PP/Maha/6917, East: 
Allotment 107 PP/A/1742; South: Allotments 107 and 
127 of PP/A/1742; West: Allotment 01  
Name and Addresses of Claimants : The Government 
 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land  are 
required to appear before me personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing at the Divisional Secretariat 
Pathadumbara on the 23rd day of October 2019 at 10.00   
o’ Clock in the morning and notify to me in writing with 
duplicate before the 16th day of October 2019 the nature  
of their interests in the aforesaid land  and the amount and 
the particulars of their claims for compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.
