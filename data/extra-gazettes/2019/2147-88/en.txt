FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER  OF   THE   WANATHAWILLUWA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group 1,  to which Pathirannehelage Rohini Mallika the  member
belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned member if
Independent Group No. 1 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  has communicated his decision to nominate
Kombala Thambiliya Godage Ruwani Nisansala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer  of  the  Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kombala
Thambiliya Godage Ruwani Nisansala was selected as the member of the Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Wanathawilluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
02nd November, 2019.
