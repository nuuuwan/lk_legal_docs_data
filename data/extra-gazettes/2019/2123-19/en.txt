THE   FINANCE   ACT,  No.  11  OF  2004
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 22 read with Section 26 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2004, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, the Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Schedule I of the International Telecommunications
Operators Levy (Imposition) Regulations No. 1 of 2010, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1662/1 dated July
12, 2010 as last amended by the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1945/2 dated December 14, 2015, by
repeal of Tables A, A(i), B, C and D and the substitution therefor of the Tables A, A(i), B, C and D of the Schedule hereto.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
May 14,  2019.
