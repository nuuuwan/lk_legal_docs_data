PILGRIMAGES   ORDINANCE
GENERAL public is hereby notified that Annual Sri Maha Bodhiraja Procession of Sri Shasanalankararama Ancient Temple
in Grama Niladhari Division, Udugama, Divisional Secretariat Division, Nagoda, Galle District of the Southern Province
scheduled to be held from 01st of January to 28th of January in 2020 as per the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance
(Chapter 175) in terms of the regulations laid down by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 1956/11 dated
01.03.2016 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 S
OMARATHNE  V IDANAPATHIRANA ,
District Secretry/Government Agent,
Galle.
At the District Secretariat,
