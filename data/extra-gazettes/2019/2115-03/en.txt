The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1) (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10 A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Darshana Dilip Kumara Balasooriya of
the Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of  member of  that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased
to be a member of  the Janasetha Peramuna.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
 Welivitiya - Divithura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
15th March, 2019.
