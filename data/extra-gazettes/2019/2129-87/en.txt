LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT  No. 28  OF
1964  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
DIK/LAN/02/31-ii.
I here by give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (amendment) that the
Government intends  to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose:
Details of the land to be obtained:
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary plan No. 2792
of surveyor General Matara and situated in Village of
Suduwalla, Grama Niladhari Division of Suduwalla in Dickwella
Divisional Secretariat Matara District.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Gedarawatta; Description: Open bare land;
Claimed by: Saunda Hannadige Ghanasiri, Suduwalla,
Kottagoda; Extent: 0.0031 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of Land: Gedarawatta; Description: Open bare land;
Claimed by: Saunda Hannadige Ghanasiri, Suduwalla,
Kottagoda; Extent: 0.0033 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Dickwella Divisional Secretary's Office
on 02.08.2019 at10.30 a.m. are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in Duplicate) on or before 26.07.2019 the nature
of there interest in the land, the particulars of there claims for
comepnesation for the acquisition of the said lands the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount All deeds and documents in support of the claims
should be produced before me at the inquiry.
B. S. RANJITHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat, Dickwella.
Divisional Secretariat, Dickwella,
26th June, 2019.
