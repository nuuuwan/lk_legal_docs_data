Guidelines issued by the Election Commission in addition to the Media Guidelines published in the Gazette No.
2141/87 of 21.09.2019.
1. Photographs or declarations of public officers who do not have political rights or of members of Armed forces
and police officers who have are in active service or photographs taken and declarations made by retired officers
while they were in active service and in uniform, should not be published through electronic, print, social or any
other media to promote or prejudice a party or a candidate.
2. Photographs should not be taken and videoing inside polling stations should not be done without the written
approval of the Election Commission. Photographs or videos taken inside a polling station or a postal voting
centre taken without authority should not be published through electronic, print or social media.
3. Unofficial results of the poll should not be published untill they are officially declared by the Election
Commission.
