The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PORATIVU   PATTU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Porativu  Pattu  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Krishnapillai Sivanesan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member if Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Gopalapillai Rasu to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the  Porativu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Gopalapillai
Rasu was selected as the member of the Porativu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Porativu Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
29th August, 2019.
