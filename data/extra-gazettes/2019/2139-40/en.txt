TOWN   AND   COUNTRY   PLANNING   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 269)
Order under Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2)(b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning
Ordinance (Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, do by this
Order :-
(a) declare that the area of land called “Weragala Lenagala Raja Maha Vihara Area” specified in the Schedule hereto
shall be an urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance ; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Weragala Lenagala Raja Maha Vihara Area” which is
declared as above to be an urban development area.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Colombo,
28th August, 2019.This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
