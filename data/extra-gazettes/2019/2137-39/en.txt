THE  STATE  INDUSTRIAL  CORPORATION  ACT,   No.  49  OF  1957
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the State Industrial Corporation Act, No. 49 of 1957, I, Rajitha
Senaratne, Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine with the approval of the Government by the Cabinet
Decision of 18/0769/718/043 of dated 10th April 2018, do, by this Order repeal the First Schedule to the Order made
under Subsection (1) of the Section 2 of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 456/21 dated 4th
June 1987 and substitute the following :
“First Schedule
The objects of the Corporation shall be :
(1) either by itselft or by entering into such Joint Venture, Public-Private Partnership or other such agreement to
manufacture, process, stock, pack or repack medicine ;
(2) to create subsidiary companies ;
(3) to enter into International Agreements with potential investors ;
(4) to provide technical assistance for the manufacturing and processing of medicine ;
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
