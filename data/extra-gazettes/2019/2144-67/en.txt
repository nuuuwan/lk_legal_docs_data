No. 2144/67 – SATURDAY, OCTOBER 12, 2019
UV A PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Appointments by the Governor of the Uva Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me uder Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987, I, Maithri Gunaratne, Governor
of the Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been made by me.
01. Mrs. D. M. Vineetha Dissanayake, Retired Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, has been re-appointed
on contract basis as the Director of Rural Development of Uva Province, from the date 20.08.2019 till the appointment
of a Permanent officer or up to 19.08.2020.
02. Mrs. S. B. A. M. A. M. Gunawardana, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, as the Provincial Director to
Agriculture of Uva Province, with immediate effect (from the date 09.09.2019).
03. Mr. W. M. Ananda, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of the Secretary
to the Governor of Uva Province, from the date 15.09.2019 till the return to the Island of Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya,
who is the Secretary to the Governor of Uva Province.
04. Mr. W. M. Ananda, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of the Director
to the Institute of Uva Management Development, from the date 15.09.2019 till the return to the Island of Mr. R. M. T.
B. Hathiyaldeniya, who is the Acting Director to the Institute of Uva Management Development.
