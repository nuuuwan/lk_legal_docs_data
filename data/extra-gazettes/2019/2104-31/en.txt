THE   DEBT   CONCILIATION   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   81)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms under Sections 6 and 10 read with section 63 of the
Debt Conciliation Ordinance (Chapter 81).
Minister of Justice and
Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
20th July, 2018.
Regulations
1. The Debt Conciliation Regulations, 1942, published in Gazette No. 8979 of July, 31st 1942 as amended from
time to time are further amended as follows:-
(1) by the substitution for regulation 3 thereof of the following regulation:-
“3. (1) Every member of the Board or of a Branch Board who attends a meeting of the Board or of a
Branch Board shall be paid a fee of Rupees Three Thousand in respect of each sitting.
(2) The Chairman of a Board who attends a meeting of the Board shall be paid a fee of Rupees Five
Thousand in respect of each sitting.” ;
(2) in regulation 4 thereof -
(a) in paragraph (1) of that regulation by the substitution for sub-paragraph (ii), of the following sub-
paragraph:-
“(ii) where a member uses a motor vehicle to attend a meeting, Rupees One Thousand per meeting
and in the case of the Chairman, Two Thousand per meeting.” ;
2. These regulations shall be deemed to have come into force on November, 1, 2017.
