THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare that under the Sub-section (1) of Section
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
Boundaries of the block of land described in the Advance tracing
No. CO/DSO/2015/197 and dated 02.10.2015 of the Surveyor
General, located in Kirulapana, Thimbirigasyaya Divisional
Secretariat Division, Colombo District, Western Province.
1. Lot 'A' – Assessment No. 200 (a portion) High-level road –
Extent: 0.0202 Hectare
North: The lands bearing Assessment No. 200/3 in High level road
and the Kavinda place; East: The lands bearing Assessment No. 200
in the High level road; South: Lot B of this plan, the lands bearing
Assessment No. 05 in  Kavinda place and Assessment No. 200 in
High level road; West: Kavinda place.
2. Lot 'B' – Assessment No. 05 (a portion) Kavinda place –
Extent: 0.0177 Hectare
North: The lands bearing Assessment No. 200 in High level road,
Lot A of this plan and the Kavinda place; East: The lands bearing
Assessment No. 05 in the Kavinda place; South: Kavinda place and
the lands bearing Assessment No. 5, 7/1 in Kavinda place; West:
Kavinda place.
