THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference  No.:4/3/1/140.
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of  section 7 of the
Land Acquisition act as amended by the land acquisition
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/ servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land  to be acquired :
The following allotment of land situated in the Grama Niladhari
division of village of 725 Kalutara South in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Kalutara in the district of Kalutara in
Western province and marked in Surveyor General's plan No.
P.P.K. 4708.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the land: 
Lansiya watta, else Duch sematory. Assmt
No. 48, 48A, 48B, 48/1, 48/2,  48/3, 48/4, 48/5, 48/6/48/1/1A,
48/1/2, 48/1/3, 48/1/4, 48/1/5, 48/1/6, 48/1/7; Description:
Garden, Part of the permanent Building; Claimant:
Government; Previous claiment: Doratuwa privet Institute,
No.132, Daham Mawatha, Kaldemulla, Moratuwa; Extent:
0.0242 Hectare.
    G 29606 - 17 (2019/03)
2. Lot No. 2
Name of the land: Lansiya watta, else Duch semetarry.
Assmt No. not known, Galle Road;Description: Garden, Two
Temporary Building; Claimant:Government;Previous
claiment: Doratuwa privet Institute, No.132, Daham Mawatha,
Kaldemulla, Moratuwa; Extent: 0.0098 Hectare.
   All Persons interested in the aforesaid land /lands on which
a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Kalutara  Divisional Secretariat
on the 03rd day of April 2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 27th day of  March, 2019 .The nature of their interests in
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for
the acquisition of the said land servitude the amount of
Compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.You Should present relevant Deeds, Plans, and other
documents to prove ownership to the land on the inquiry
date.
