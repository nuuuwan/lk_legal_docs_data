Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Control of Bill Boards and Hoardings (Visual Pollution) Regulations
No. 01 of 2019
2. Any bill board, hoarding, structure or device used for the purpose of displaying an advertisement shall be in
conformity with these regulations and shall not be erected obstructing the view of natural environment, landscape, visual
amenity and safety of persons.
3. A person shall not erect any bill board, hoarding, structure or device for the purpose of displaying and
advertisement -
(a) without a permit issued for such purpose by the relevant Authority specified in the Schedule hereto,
subject to the payment of a fee as may be charged by such Authority ; and

(b) except in accordance with the guidelines relating to the prevention of obstruction of the view of natural
environment, landscape, visual amenity and safety of persons as may be issued from time to time by
such relevant Authority.
4. The relevant Authority shall issue the permit for a period not more than twelve (12) months and may be
renewed annually subject to the payment of a renewal fee as may be charged by such Authority.
5. Where any bill board, hoarding, structure or device used for the purpose of displaying an advertisement is
displayed for more than twelve (12) months without the permit being renewed under regulation 4, the relevant Authority
shall inform the holder of permit to remove such bill board, hoarding, structure or device used for the purpose of displaying
an advertisement from such place.
6. Where a bill board, hoarding, structure or device used for the purpose of displaying an advertisement is not
removed as informed by the relevant Authority under regulation 5, such Authority shall remove it and recover the cost of
such removal from the holder of the permit.
7. The relevant authority shall have the power to remove any bill board, hoarding, structure or device used for the
purpose of displaying an advertisement -
(a) to ensure safety and security of persons ;
(b) to prevent the obstruction of the view of natural environment, landscape, visual amenity ; and
(c) to comply with any statutory requirement.
8. In these Regulations -
“advertisement” includes any word, letter, sign, signboard, banner, balloon and poster illuminated or non-
illuminated or digitally displayed (static or moving) and posted on land, buildings, walls, hoardings,
frames, kiosks, posts, tree guard, gantries, mobile vehicles, passage, shelters or temporary arches.
