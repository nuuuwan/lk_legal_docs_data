1A   G   29593 —27  (03/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF   THE  SIYAMBALANDUW A
PRADESHSIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of member of Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Loku Banda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon  the member of that No. 01 Buddama of Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Adikari Mudiyanselage Appuhami to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, returning officer of the Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Adikari
Mudiyanselage Appuhami was elected as the member of the No. 01 Buddama of Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA G AYAN P ATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Monaragala,
06th March, 2019.
