THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of the said Act establishing the
Uva Wellassa University and  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1395/15 of June 01, 2005 as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2079/12 of July 09, 2018 as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of item “1. Faculty of Science and Technology” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries
“1” and “2” appearing in Column II thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“1. Faculty of Applied Sciences 1. Department of Science and Technology
