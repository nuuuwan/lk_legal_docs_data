THE  REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  ACT,  No. 44  OF  1980
IN pursuance of Section 12(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, it is hereby notified that the revision
of registers of electors for the year 2019 has commenced, on 15th May 2019.
02. Copies of registers of electors now in force or portions thereof will be exhibited by Registering Officers in

03. Every person qualified to be an elector in a certain Electoral District shall take action to peruse the valid
Electoral Register and ascertain whether his/her name is in the register or not.
04. From 15.05.2019 enumerators will distribute enumeration forms to chief occupants of each household to
obtain the particulars of persons, who are qualified to be registered as electors for the year 2019. Completed forms of the
chief occupants will be collected, starting from 01st June 2019. Chief occupants are required to assist the enumerators
by promptly submitting correct information of all persons, who are qualified to be registered as electors under their
household.
