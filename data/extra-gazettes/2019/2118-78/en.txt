THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 7
        My Reference No.: DIVIB/LND/LADAQ/
CEP/05/2015/16.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/538.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7  as
amendment by the land acquisition Act. No. 28 of 1964, that
the Government intends to acquire the following lands which
are required for a public purpose.
Description of the lands  to be acquired:
The allotment of land, containing in extent of 0.0299 Hectare,
Lot. 14902 (1 lot) under mentioned land shown in the Surveyor
General's plan of  F.V .P . 1187 Supplement No. 04 and Sheet
No.06 dated 22.02.2019, situated in the village of
Paranagampitiya in Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 522,
Bakmeegolla in the Minor Division of Ihala Wisideke West
Korale in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Ibbagamuwa
in the Kurunegala District in North Western Province.
01. Lot No. 14902
Name of Land: Wathumullekumbura; Description: Paddy
Field; Claimant: Government ; Original Claimant: A Part of  Lot
No. 13454. Taken over under section 38 (A) of Land
Acquisition Act. Original Claimant Seiyadu Ahamadu
Nabishan. To finalize the land acquisition process; Extent:
0.0299 Hectare.  (299 sqm)
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired by are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Ibbagamuwa Divisional Secretariat, on the day of the 10th
June 2019 at 09.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27th May 2019
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for computation for the said land/servitude the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
M. A. S. MARASINGHE,
Divisional Secretary,
Ibbagamuwa.
