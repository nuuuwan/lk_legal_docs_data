LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref No.: W A/18/04/2013.
Land Ministry Ref.No.: 4-3/4/2013/D/535.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which are required for a public purpose.
The land allotments of land described below in total extent
of 0.5495 Hectares, shown as 01 and 02 in the preliminary
plan dated 2018-12-31 for Gam 4123 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in Village of Nawammahara, the minor
division of Ragam Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Wattala in the District of Gampaha, in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
1. Lot No. 01
Name of land : Kajugahakumbura ; Description: Open
marshy land; Claimant: Janaka Ambagahawatte, No. 18/3, Sun
City Apartment, St. Anthony's lane, Colombo 03; Original
Claiment: - ; Extent : 0.0610 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of land : Kajugahakumbura ; Description: Canal;
Claimant: Janaka Ambagahawatte, No. 18/3, Sun City
Apartment, St. Anthony's lane, Colombo 03; Original Claiment:
- ; Extent : 0.4885 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Wattala on the
24th Day of April, 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 24th
day of April, 2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
P. D. T. C. RAJIKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Wattala.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Wattala,
26th January, 2019.
