 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PB 4833, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the
Order made under the Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 2067/17 of April 19, 2018 by the addition
immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Science in Computer Networks” appearing in Column I and its corresponding
entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
CONDITIONS
National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Management Honours in International Business, adhere to
the minimum entry qualification of three (03) ‘S’ passes in any subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE
(Advance Level) Examination or equivalent.
