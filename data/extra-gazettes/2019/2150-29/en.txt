FOREST CONSERVATION ORDINANCE 
  
THE order under Section 3. that has to be read with Section 64. (1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), 
as No. 13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 199 5 and No. 65 of 
2009 that have to be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka. 
  
THE ORDER 
  
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3.of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by 
Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2 009, that have to 
be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic o f Sri Lanka,                  
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President do by this order declare Kuttithivu  Mangrove Forest of 71.94 hectares bounded by the 
limit set forth in the schedule 01, schedule 02 and schedule 03 hereto as Kuttithivu Mangrove Reserved Forest from 04th 
day of November, 2019. 
  
  
 MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA 
 President. 
  
  
04th day of November, 2019. 
Colombo.
