No. 2148/7 – MONDAY, NOVEMBER 04, 2019
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
MINUTE  OF  THE  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC  MANAGEMENT  ASSISTANTS’
SERVICE  -  AMENDMENT  No.  03
Hon. Governor of Central Province has approved to amend the Minute of the Central Provincial Public Management Assistants’
Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1869/4 dated 30.06.2014 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
as amended by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2047/43 dated 30.11.2017 and No. 2107/7 and 22.01.2019 as follows.
1. The title of the Service Minute referred to as the ‘‘Minute of the Central Provincial public Management Assistants’
Service’’ will be substituted by the following title.
‘‘Minute of the Central Provincial Management Service Officers Service’’
2. Every term referred to as ‘‘Central Provincial Public Management Assistants’ Service’’ and every term referred to as
‘‘Central Provincial Public Management Assistant’’ in the relevant Service Minute shall be amended as ‘‘Central
Provincial Management Service Officers Service’’ and ‘‘Central Provincial Management Service Officers’’ respectively
and the term referred to as ‘‘Officer’’ in the said Service Minute shall be reckoned as Central Provincial Management
Service Officers.
M. G. A. THILAKARATNE,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission,
Central Provincial Council.
29th October, 2019
Central Provincial Public Service Commission,
No. 244, Katugastota Road,
Kandy.
