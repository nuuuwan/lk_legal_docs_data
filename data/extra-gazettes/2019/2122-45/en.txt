STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. K. P. Pemasiri (Chief Cashier) and Mr. B. A. Dharmasena Jayantha (Office Aid) who had worked at the Royal
Colombo Golf Club had been caused injustice by the non-payment of the Exgratia Allowance paid to some employees who had
worked at the Club and retired with more than 10 years of service in addition to the general gratuity payment and if such
injustice had been caused to what reliefs they are entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner  General of Labour.

Colombo this 11th day of  April, 2019.
