FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MIRIGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Wijalathpedige Nimal Thilakarathna the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kamburadeni Dewage Chaminda Lalith Kumara to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Mirigama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Kamburadeni Dewage Chaminda Lalith Kumara was selected as the member of the Mirigama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
22nd August, 2019.
