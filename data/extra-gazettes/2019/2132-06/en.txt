My Ref. No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/03.

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/Servitude which is required for
a public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
The lands allotment called Karupankudiyiruppukani in total
extent of about 2.2557 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan
No. MN 822 and prepared by Surveyor General situated in
the Village called Olaittoduvai, in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 60 of Olaittoduvai, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Mannar Town of Mannar District in Northern Province.
Plan No.: MN 822
Lot No. 1
Name of  the Land: Karupankudiyiruppukani; Present use
of the land: Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original
Claimant: KDG . Global, No. 124, Maya Avenue, Colombo 06;
Extent: 2.2557 Hectares.
The dates and the time of the inquiries;
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
servitutde is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.00 a.m. on the 22nd day of August, 2019 at Mannar
Town Divisional Secretariat.
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th day of August,
2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for the compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
K. SIVASAMBU,
Divisional Secretary,
