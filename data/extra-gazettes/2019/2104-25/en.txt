 PUBLIC   UTILITIES  COMMISSION  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notice under Section 20(2) of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act,  No. 20 of 2009
INTENTION   OF   REVOKE   LICENSES
THE Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka (“the Commission”) hereby gives notice pursuant to Section 20(2) of the
Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009 that the Commission intends to revoke the electricity generation licenses
granted to below identified entities in order to generate electricity, due to the amalgamation of those entities with WKV
Hydro Technics (Pvt) Ltd with effect from 30th March 2018.
The Commission has already issued an electricity generation license for WKV Hydro Technics (Pvt)
Ltd to operate Pathaha Oya Mini Hydro Power Plant (1.5 MW) under the Electricity Generation License Number EL/GS/
10-056.
