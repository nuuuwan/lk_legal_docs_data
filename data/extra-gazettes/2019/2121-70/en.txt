REGISTRATION OF PERSONS ACT, N o. 08 OF  1968
Order Under Section 34
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of Registration of persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016, I, Wajira
Abeywardena, Minister of internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government do by this order, alter the
date of expiration published in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of Section 6, 9,
14, 16 and 17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the aforesaid Act (other than
the Registration of Persons Regulation 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of November, 20, 1990) to be 30.06.2020, until
the necessary infrastructure, arrangements and technological methodologies are made to.
WAJIRA ABEYWARDENA,
Minister of Internal & Home
Affairs and Provincial Councils
& Local Government.
12th  April, 2019,
at Battaramulla.
