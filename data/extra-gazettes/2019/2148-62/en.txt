THE NATIONAL THOROUGHFARES ACT, No. 40 OF 2008
Order under Section 11
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir
Hashim, Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum Resources Development, do by this Order designate
the National Highway which was designated as an “Expressway” by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2148/ 61 of 09.11.2019 and specified in the Schedule to this Order to be an “User Fee National Highway”.
K
ABIR HASHIM,
Minister of Highways & Road Development and
Petroleum Resources Development.
Battaramulla,
08th November, 2019.
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NA TIONAL HIGHW AY
The stretch of road being 41.067 kilometers long and 80.000 - 100.000 metres broad, situated between the Godagama
(122.389km) and Kasagala (163.456km) of the Expressway, designated as the Godagama- Kasagala National Highway (Section
of  Southern Expressway).
