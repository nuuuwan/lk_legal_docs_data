Notice under Section 7
             My No.: UDU/2/2/10/38.

I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7  of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude for public purpose
(the conservation of Knukles range.)
Description of the land/Servitude to be acquired :
An allotment of land called "Winchfield Park watta" bearing
an extent of about 14.2860 Hectares mentioned in P .P . Maha
6865, Supplement of the Surveyor General, situated in the
Grama Niladhari Division of Karambakatiya in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central
Province.
