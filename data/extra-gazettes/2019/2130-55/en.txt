FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   DEPUTY  MAYOR  OF   THE   AKKARAIPATTU
MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66B(2)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED   BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No. 16  OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Deputy Mayor in  Akkaraipattu Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Congress, the recognized political party to which Abdul Gafoor
Azmy the former Deputy Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 66 B (2)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  me member of that Akkaraipattu Municipal Council who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as a Deputy Mayor  within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid Recognized  Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate  Muhammed Caseem Muhammed Yaseer to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of  the  Akkaraipattu Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare that Muhammed Caseem Muhammed
Yaseer elected as the Deputy Mayor of the Akkaraipattu Municipal Council .
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
04th July, 2019.
