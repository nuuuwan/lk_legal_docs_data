THE  PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Godapitiya Muslim Festival - 2019
IN  terms of regulations "3" of the regulations formed under the pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) & Public in the Government
Gazette No. 10630 of 8th January 1954. It is hereby notified that annual pilgrimage of Godapitiya Poruwai Mosque "Muhiyadeen
Muslim Mosque" at Godapitiya, Akuressa in Athuraliya Division in Matara District. Will Commence on 11th December and
terminate 22nd December 2019.
The regulations relating this pilgrimage Ordinance in Government Gazette No. 10630 of 8th January 1954. Shall in force
within the camp above referred to in the regulations & described in the scheduled there during the period of the Pilgrimage
referred to above.
N
IROSHA S. GAMAGE,
Divisional Secretary,
Athuraliya.
Divisional Secretariat,
