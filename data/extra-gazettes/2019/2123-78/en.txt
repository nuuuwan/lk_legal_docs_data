The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAHIYANGANA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member of  Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of the Independent Group to which  Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Shayamalee Kumari
Dissanayake the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  the member of  that Ward No. 06 Dambana of Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such Independent
Group  and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Akurugoda Kalu Arachchilage Manel Asokamala Kaluarachchi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer of the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Akurugoda
Kalu Arachchilage Manel Asokamala Kaluarachchi  was elected as the member of the Ward No. 06, Dambana of Mahiyangana
Pradeshiya Sabha .
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha .
District Election Office,
Badulla,
16th May, 2019.
