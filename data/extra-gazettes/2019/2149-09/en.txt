Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Special Mediation (Qualifications of Mediators) Regulations No. 1 of
2019.
2. A person eligible to be appointed as a Member of a Panel of Mediators, in the Special Mediation Board area specified
in the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2125/8 of May 27, 2019 relating to a dispute of the category of
disputes determined by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2078/36 dated July 04, 2018, shall :-
(a) be between 35 to 70 years of age;
(b) be a resident of the Special Mediation Board area;
(c) have a professional or academic qualification obtained from a recognized professional or
higher education body established by or under any written law; and
(d) have been or be a public officer, or a person who holds knowledge and skills in an area
relevant to settlements of disputes and grievances of migrant workers.
