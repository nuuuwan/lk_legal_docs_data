I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of the powers vested in me
by Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act, (Chapter 393) do hereby yet again enlarge the period of time of the
Presidential Commission of Inquiry established by me on 31st January 2018 in terms of the provisions of Section 2 of the
aforesaid Act by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2056/41 dated 02nd February, 2018 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate and inquire into alleged irregularities in connection with Sri
Lankan Airlines Ltd, Sri Lankan Catering Ltd and Mihin Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd. during the period 01st January 2006 to 31st
January, 2018, up to 30th May, 2019 further considering the current role of the commission, for rendition of the final
report on completion of the task, having enlarged the six month period ending 31st July 2018 granted for the said purpose
by five months i. e., up to 31st December 2018 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2082/3 dated
31st July 2018 by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), and
again up to 15th February 2019 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2099/40 dated 30th November,
2018 by virtue of the powers so vested in me.
Maithripala Sirisena,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
February 06, 2019.
