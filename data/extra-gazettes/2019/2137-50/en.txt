FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KALUTARA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Gardi
Hewage Chamila Kumari the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Govinnage Rammyamala Ranjani Perera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Kalutara
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Govinnage Rammyamala Ranjani Perera was selected as the member of the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K
ALINDRA J AY AWEERA F ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
20th August, 2019.
