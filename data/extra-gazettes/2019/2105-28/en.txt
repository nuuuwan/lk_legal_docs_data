THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
My No.: YAK/LAN/01/03/05. 
 Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/8/2014/PC/274. 
 
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude*  which is required for a public purpose:-

The  allotments  of Land in total extent  of 0.0038

East, in the Divisional Secretariat Yakkalamulla Thalpe 
Paththuwa North, District of Galle, Southern Province. 
The names and extents of the allotments of land are as 
follows: 
 
  
 Lot No. 1310  
 Name of the Land: Kajjugaha  Liyadda, Jambugaha

Ranjith Dias, Indigahagodalla, Yakkalamulla; Extent : 0.0033 
Hectare.  
 Lot No. 1311  
 Name of the Land: Kajjugaha  Liyadda, Jambugaha

 Lot No. 1312  
 Name of the Land: Kajjugaha  Liyadda, Jambugaha

All  persons interested in the aforesaid  land/Lands* 
on which  servitude is to be acquired are  hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Yakkalamulla 
the 13th day of February 2019  at 10.00 o clock  and are 
hereby requested to  notify to me in writing  (in duplicate) 
on or before the 06th day of February  2019 the nature of 
their interests in the land the particulars of their claims of  
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
