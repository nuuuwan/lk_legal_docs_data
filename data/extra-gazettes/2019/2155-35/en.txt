The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   POLGAHAWELA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased
to be member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party
to which Nishshanka Dhanushka Rathnajeewa Pushpa Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member if  United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Balasooriya Mudiyanselage Anura Kumara Balasooriya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Balasooriya Mudiyanselage Anura Kumara Balasooriya was selected as the member of  the Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Polgahawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
27th December, 2019.
