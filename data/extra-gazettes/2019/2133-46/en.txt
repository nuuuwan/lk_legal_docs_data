No. 2133/46–  THURSDAY, JULY,  25,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
ANNUAL  PERAHARA  OF  BADULL  KATARAGAMA  DEVALAYA 
 
IN accordance with the powers vested in me by Gazette Notifications No. 9902 of 10.09.1948, No. 10283 of 17.08.1951 
and No. 14778 of 25.03.1955 published under Chapter 362 of the Pilgrimage Act, and under the powers conferred on 
Divisional Secretaries by Act No. 58 of 1992, and according to the constitution published in No. 162 2 Gazette 
Notification of 02.10.2009 on Annual Perehara of Badulla Kataragama Devalaya, I Nihal Gunarathne, the Divisional 
Secretary of Badulla do hereby make the following orders in connection with the Annual Perehara of B adulla 
Katharagama Devalaya, 2018. 
 
1. I, do hereby notice that, the Annual Perehara of Badulla Katharagana Devalaya shall begin with “Kap Situvima” 
on 31st of  August 2019 and by having inner Perehara from 31 st August 2019 to 04th September 2019 (5 days), 
by having Kumbal Perehara from 05th September 2019 to 09 th September 2019 (05 days), by having Rand oli 
Perahera  from 10 th September to 14 th September 2019 (5 days) by having water cutting ceremony in the 
morning on 15th September 2019 and end in the afternoon on 15 th September 2019 after having returned to the 
Devalaya. 
 
2. Route of the Perehara 
 
 The Perahera shall start from Badull Katharagama Devalaya premises and proceed along Badulla lower  Street, 
 Passara Road (Muthiyangana Roundabout), Longden Road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Cocowatte Road, 
 Udayaraja Mawatha, King’s Street, Bazzar Street lower Street and back to Badulla Katharagama Devalaya. 
 
3. Cooking and selling article of  trade are prohibited in and around Badulla Katharagama Devalaya comp ound 
where the throne of the Devalaya located. 
(Published by Authority)
