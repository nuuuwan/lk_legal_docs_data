The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NALLUR  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the the Leader of the Independent Group, to which Nagan Sivaloganathan the member belonged,
was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if Independent
Group who belongs to such Independent Group, and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Mervin Roshan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Mervin
Roshan was selected as the member of the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
03rd May, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NALLUR  PRADESHIYA

terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Nagarasa
Elilmohan  was selected as the member of the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
