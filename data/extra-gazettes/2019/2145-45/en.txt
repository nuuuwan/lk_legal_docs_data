I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following Land/Servitude
which is required for a public purpose mentioned below.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Following portion of land depicted in Preliminary Survey
Plan No. MR 2807 prepared by Surveyor General, situated in
the village of Babarende Central in Grama Niladari's Division
of Babarenda Central 450 D in Divisional Secretary's Division
of Dickwella in Matara District, Southern Province.
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Galketiyewatta; Description: Open;
