TOURISM  ACT,  No.  38  OF  2005
Order  under Section 26(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Sub section (1) of Section 26 of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005, I, John
Anthony Emmanual Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, do by this
Order declare the area specified in the Schedule hereto to be a Tourist Development Area for the purpose of the aforesaid
Act.
J
OHN ANTHONY  E MMANUAL  AMARATHUNGA ,
Minister of Tourism Development,
Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
22nd October, 2019.
SCHEDULE
B
OUNDARIES  OF THE  I RANA VILA TOURISM  DEVELOPMENT  Z ONE
All that area of land depicted in the Preliminary Plan. mQ' 2937 and mQ' 3021 of the Survey General containing
138.125 ha. and 27.913 ha. respectively in the villages of Iranavila, Galahitiyawa and Thuduwawa North of the Iranavila
Western and East Grama Niladhari divisions in the Mahawewa Divisional Secretariat Division of the Puttalam District in
the North Western Province.
North : Iranavila Estate belongs to the Land Reform Commission ;
East : Galahitiyawa village and Lunu Oya ;
South : Thoduwawa North ;
West : This land belongs to Private Parties distributed by Land Reform Commission.
