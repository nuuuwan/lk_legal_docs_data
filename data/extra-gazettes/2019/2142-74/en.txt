CIVIL  AVIATION  AUTHORITY  OF  SRI  LANKA   ACT,  No.  34  OF  2002
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me as the Minister in charge of the subject of civil aviation under Section 9 of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri  Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002 and, in conformity with the Cabinet decision taken on 06th August,
2019 after considering the Cabinet Memorandum No. 19/1799/105/009-11 dated 24th June 2019, on “Development of
Palaly Airport of Operation of Regional Commercial Flights” submitted by Hon. Prime Minister and Minister of National
Policies, Economic Affairs, Resettlement and Rehabilitation, Northern Province Development and Youth Affairs, I, Arjuna
Ranatunga, Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation, do by this Order, transfer and vest in the Civil Aviation Authority of
Sri Lanka the Jaffna International Airport which is listed under the Second Schedule to the aforementioned Act with
immediate effect as specified in the Schedule below.
SCHEDULE
The runway and the runway Strip of Jaffna International Airport and the whole portion of the airport land located
northwards abutting the runway.
A
RJUNA R ANATUNGA (MP),
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Ministry of Transport and Civil Aviation,
Sethsiripaya Stage II,
Battaramulla,
26th September, 2019.
