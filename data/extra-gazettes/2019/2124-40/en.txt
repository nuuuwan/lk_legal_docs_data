LAND   REFORM   COMMISSIOIN   ACT,   No.   1  OF   1972   OF   THE   NATIONAL   STATE   ASSEMBLY
Order under Sub-Section (4) of Section 27a
I, Gayantha Karunathilaka, the Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms, by this Order made in terms of powers
vested in me by Sub-section (4) of Section 27A of Land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972 of the National State Assembly as
amended by Land Reforms (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981.
(a) Do hereby amend that rule by revoking them on the basis of not adhering to the conditions and orders when
vesting the land called Walahanduwa Watta specified in Schedule two here to in Sri Lanka State Plantation
Corporation, which had been vested in Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation established under Sri Lanka
State Plantation Corporation Act, No. 4 of 1958 and transferred under Sub-Section (1) of Section 27A of the
Land Reform Commission Act, No. 1 of 1972 as published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 815/10 on 21st
April, 1994 relevant to the land called and mentioned as Walahanduwa Watta as detailed in the First Schedule
hereto.
(b) Do hereby state that, following this revocation, the said extent of land explained in detail in the Second
Schedule hereto is conveyed back to Land Reform Commission.
G
AY ANTHA K ARUNA THILAKA,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
Mihikatha Medura,
Land Secretariat,
1200/6, Rajamalwatta Lane,
Battaramulla,
23rd May, 2019
