1A   G   29355—27  (01/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Jaffna Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of the
council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eelam People’s Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which
Karthigesu Velum Mayilum Kukenthiran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide upon  returned  member if Eelam People’s Democratic Party who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Sebasthiyampillei Mariyadasan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW  THEREFORE I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, Returning Of ficer of the Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Sebasthiyampillei
Mariyadasan  was selected as the member of the Jaffna Municipal Council.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
28th January, 2019.
