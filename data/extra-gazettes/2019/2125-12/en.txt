PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 28.05.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Four allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Ma/MNN/2018/159 dated 13.02.2019, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village called Thoddaveli in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. MN/62 of Thoddaveli in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Mannar Town in Mannar District in Northern Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thoddakani – Extent:  0.1850 Hectare
North: Thoddakani, claimant not known; East: Thoddakani,
claimant not known, Thoddakani claimed by Four Teess Rest Inn
and Lots B and C; South: Lots B, C and D; West: Lot D and
Thoddakani, claimant not known.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Thoddakani – Extent:  0.4560 Hectare
North: Lot A, Thoddakani, claimant not known and Thoddakani
claimed by Four Teess Rest Inn; East: Thoddakani claimed by
Four Teess Rest Inn and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and
Lot  C; West: Lots C and A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Thoddakani – Extent:  0.4560 Hectare
North: Lots A and B; East: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road
(R.D.A.) and Lot D; West: Lots D and A.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Thoddakani – Extent:  1.1030 Hectare
North: Thoddakani, claimant not known and Lots A and C; East:
Lots A and C and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Thoddakani,
claimant not known.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2018/CEB/162.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Avenue,
Battaramulla.
23rd May, 2019.
