The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER  OF   THE   VA VUNIYA   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Vavuniya Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Eelam People’s Democratic Party, the recognized political party to which
Balasingam Prasanna the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of  Eelam People’s Democratic Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Wijayakumar Joel Niroshan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Wijayakumar Joel Niroshan was selected as the member of the Vavuniya Municipal Council.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
02nd December, 2019.
