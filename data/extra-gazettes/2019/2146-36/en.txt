IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to appoint the following persons as Commissioners
of the Mediation Boards Commission, with immediate effect, under the Provisions of Section 2(1) of the Mediation
Boards Act, No. 72 of 1988.
1. Justice Hector Yapa - Retired Judge of the Supreme Court
2. Justice Nimal Dissanayake - Retired Judge of the Supreme Court
3. Justice Chandradasa Nanayakkara - Retired Judge of the  Court of Appeal
4. Mr. M. Somasundaram
5. Mrs. Faleela Be Jurangpathy
His Excellency the President has nominated Justice Hector Yapa, retired Supreme Court Judge under the above-
mentioned provisions, to be the Chairman of the said Commission.
                   By  His Excellency’s Command,
