 STRATEGIC   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS   ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Subsection (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub Section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14
of 2008 as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the
Minister in charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub Section (4) of
Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary Notification No. 1789/22 dated 19th December 2012, as amended by the
subsequent Order published under Sub Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/7 dated 04th July
2016, No. 2049/59 dated 15th December 2017 and No. 2062/47 dated 16th March 2018 do by this Order declare that, -
Clause (4) of the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1789/22 dated 19th December 2012,
as amended by the subsequent Orders published under Sub Section (4) of Section 3 in the Gazette Extraordinay
No. 1974/7 dated 04th July 2016, No 2049/59 dated 15th December 2017 and No. 2062/47 dated 16th March 2018 is
hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid “project to construct and operate a 475 roomed city hotel with 90 serviced Apartments at
No. 116, Galle Road, Colombo 3 in the Western Province” shall commence commercial operations within a period of
one hundred and twenty five (125) months from the date of the Project Agrement signed with the Board of Investment of
Sri Lanka on the 19th of July 2012 and ending on the 31st December, 2022 and the minimum investment of United States
Dollars One Hundred Fifty Eight Million and Eight Hundred Ninety Thousand (US$ 158,890,000) indicated in the
Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1771/18 dated 15th August 2012 shall be increased to United
States Dollars Two Hundred and Fifty Million (US$ 250,000,000) to reflect the additional investment required to complete
construction.”
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies,
and International Trade.
