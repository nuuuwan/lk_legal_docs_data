FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NEGOMBO   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Negombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which
Ponnamperumage Fred Dexter the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member if United National Freedom Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Balasuriyage Nanda Sulochana Perera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Negombo
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Balasuriyage Nanda Sulochana Perera  was selected as the member of the Negombo Municipal Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
01st July, 2019.
