PART IV (A) -- PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Notification of the Provincial Councils
WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance, No. 12 of 1946, read with Section
2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I  Isura Dewapriya, the Chief Minister of Western
Province and Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local Government  and Provincial
Administration, Economic Promotion, Electricity and Power, Environment Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism
Affairs of Western Province do by this order declare that I have approved to impose and charge, a 20% Entertainment Tax on
entrance fee for entertainment activities stated in the Entertainment Tax  Ordinance, No. 12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment
Tax (Amendment) Act,  No. 37 of 1984, however only 7.5% to be charged for the entrance fee for films screened in Cinema halls
within the limits of the Urban Council, the proposition No. 2583 of which has been made by the secretary of Urban Council
Kalutara on 02nd November 2017 as per provisions stated in section 2 (1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
to be read with Section 184 (A) of Urban Council Ordinance.
ISURA DEWAPRIYA,
Chief Minister of Western Province and Provincial
Minister of Finance and Planing, Engineering Services,
Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Economic Promotion,  Electricity and Power, Environment Affairs,
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
At Provincial Council building,
No. 204,
Densil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Baththaramulla.
On 12th of April 2019
