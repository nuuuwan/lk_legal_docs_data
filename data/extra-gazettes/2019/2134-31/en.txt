FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PUTHUKKUDIYIRUPPU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member  of  Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group,  to which Anton Radnaraja Lavanja the member belonged,
was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned member if Independent
Group who belongs to such Independent Group,  and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  has communicated his decision to nominate
Selvarani Arulanantham to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the  Puthukkudiyiruppu  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Selvarani Arulanantham was selected as the member of the Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Puthukkudiyiruppu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mullaitivu,
29th July 2019.
