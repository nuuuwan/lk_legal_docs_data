L.D.B. 6/2017
CIVIL  A VIATION  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2010
Order under Section 6
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of section 6 of the Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010, I, Arjuna
Ranatunga, Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation, do by this Order, amend the Order made under section 6 of the
aforesaid Act, and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1922/4 of July 7th, 2015, by the substitution for the
words, “Mattala Rajapakse International Airport, Mattala, Hambantota” of the words “the Mattala Rajapakse International
Airport, Mattala, Hambantota, the Colombo International Airport, Ratmalana, the Jaffna International Airport and the
Batticaloa International Airport”.
A
RJUNA R ANATUNGA,
Minister of Transport and Civil Aviation.
Colombo,
11th October, 2019.
