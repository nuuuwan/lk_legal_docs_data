FOREST    CONSERV ATION   ORDINANCE
THE order under section 3A. that has to be read with Section 64 (1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451),
as No. 13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of
2009 that have to be read with Section 51 of the 19
th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3A.of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, that have
to be read with Section 51 of the 19
th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President  do by this order declare the Pol Ela Mangrove Forest of 29 hectares bounded by the
limit set forth in the schedule hereto as Pol Ela Mangrove Conservation Forest from 26 day of July, 2019.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
25th day of  July 2019.
