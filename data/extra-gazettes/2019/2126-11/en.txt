 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1400/24 of July 08, 2005 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1686/22 of December 29, 2010), establishing the University of the Visual and Performing Arts, by the additon
immediately after the entry “6. Department of Musicology” in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item
“Faculty of Music” in Column I of that Schedule, of the following entry :-
“7. Department of English Language Teaching”.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
03rd June, 2019.
