ORDER   UNDER   SECTION   68(A)(1)   OF   THE   SRI   LANKA   TELECOMMUNICATION
(AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No. 27  OF  1996
I, Minister of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Minister in charge of the subjects of Sri
Lanka Telecommunication Regulatory Commission in terms of  powers vested in me by Section 68(A)(1) of the Sri
Lanka Telecommunication (Amendment) Act, No. 27 of 1996, I, do hereby approve the acquisition of immovable property
described below depicting the letter “A” in Clarification Advanced Tracing bearing No. CO/COL/2018/703 of Surveyor
General required for the development activites in second phase of the “Lotus Tower” project.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
Minister of Defence.
Colombo,
19th August, 2019.
Clarification Advanced Tracing
Allotment of Land approximately in an extent of 1.2141 hectares depicted as letter “A” of the Clarification Advanced
Tracing bearing No. CO/LOL/2018/703 of Surveyor General situated in the Colombo Municipal Council limit in the
Village of Suduwella, No. 24 in Suduwella Grama Niladhari Division, Colombo Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo
District in Western Province.
1. Lot No. : Lot No. A
Name of the Land : Assessment No. 300 (a lot) D. R. Wijewardhana Mawatha
Extent : 1.2141 hectares
