FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MAHAWA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group, to which Andarawad Patabedige Abeyrathna Gunawardhana
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned
member if Independent Group who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Athapaththu Konara Mudiyanselage Sujeewa Sampath Athapaththu to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the  Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha , acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Athapaththu
Konara Mudiyanselage Sujeewa Sampath Athapaththu  was selected as the member of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha .
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
12th July, 2019.
