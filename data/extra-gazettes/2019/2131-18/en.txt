1A   G  30481— 20 (07/2019)
THE  SPORTS  LAW   No.  25  OF  1973
Cancellation
I, Harin Fernando, Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports in terms of Powers vested in me
under Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby lift the Registration suspended by the Gazette Notification (Extraordinary)
No. 2110/40 dated 14.02.2019, of the Sri Lanka Badminton Association with effect from 21.06.2019 and entrust
administration and the functions thereof, with the office bearers of the Sri Lanka Badminton Association which has been
lawfully elected, with effect from the above date.
HARIN F ERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
24th June 2019.
