The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KATTANKUDY   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Front For Good Governance, the recognized political party to
which Haneefa Rakeefa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  National Front For Good Governance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abdul Hameedu Jemhooth Niza to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the  Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Abdul Hameedu
Jemhooth Niza was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
30th August, 2019.
