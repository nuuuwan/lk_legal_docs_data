CODE  OF  CRIMINAL  PROCEDURE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.   2  OF  2013
Order under Section 7(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 7(2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2
