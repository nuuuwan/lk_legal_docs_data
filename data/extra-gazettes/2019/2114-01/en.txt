SCHEDULE
Form and Manner of Maintaining the Register of  Adjudicators
(rule 2)
1. The Register of Adjudicators shall be maintained by the Authority as a  Directory which shall be available for
inspection free of charge at the office of the Authority and also as a web directory in the website of the Authority.
2. The Register of Adjudicators shall include the following details which shall be included in such Directory and
also uploaded to web directory after completion of registration :-
(a) photograph of the adjudicator ;
(b) registration number ;
(c) name of the adjudicator ;
(d) qualifications ;
(i) educational
(ii) professional
(e) field of specialty ;
(f) address ;
(g) telephone numbers and email address ;
(h) date of first registration and current registration period.
3. The Register of Adjudicators shall be updated in the following events :-
(a) registration of a new adjudicator ;
(b) renewal of registration of a registered adjudicator ;
(c) non-renewal of registration of a registered adjudicator.
