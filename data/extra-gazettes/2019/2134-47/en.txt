1A   PG  4604— 92 (08/2019)
FOREST    CONSERV ATION   ORDINANCE
THE order under section 3. that has to be read with Section 64 (1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451),
as No. 13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of
2009 that have to be read with Section 51 of the 19
th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3. of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended
by Acts, No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, that have
to be read with Section 51 of the 19
th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President  do by this order declare the Paspolakanda Mukalana Alias Wedikanda Mukalana Forest
of 38.653 hectares bounded by the limit set forth in the schedule hereto as Paspolakanda Mukalana Alias Wedikanda
Mukalana Reserved Forest from 15th day of July, 2019.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
15th day of  July 2019.
