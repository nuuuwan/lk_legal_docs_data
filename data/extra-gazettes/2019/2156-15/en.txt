CONSTITUTION   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST    REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
Notice
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me by Section 45(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I hereby delegate following duties and functions of the Ministry of Education to the State
Minister of Education Services.
D
ULLAS ALAHAPPERUMA ,
 Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
Isurupaya,
Pelawatta,
Battaramulla,
31st December 2019.
