THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: KDS/P AN/04/04/02/37.
Ministry of Lands & Land Development
Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/84.
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired:
Lot Nos. 01 - 08 (Lots 08) allotments of land in Total Extent
of about 0.1641 Hectare in Preliminari Plan No. Maha 6900
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of
Kosgama in the Grama Niladhari Division of Kosgama (739)
in the Minor Divison of Palispattu Korale (West) in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Panwila in Kandy District
in the Central Province.
