L.D.B 21/1978(X)(A)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
the Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the Order made under
the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2053/40 of January 11, 2018, by the repeal of the Schedule I
and II to that Order and Substitution therefor of the following new Schedule I and II as follows :–
“SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS
National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programme
relating to the degree specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science Engineering Honours in
Computer System Engineering, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of three (3) simple passes (‘S’)  in Physical
Science subject stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (Advance Level) Examination or equivalent.

L.D.B 21/1978(X)(A)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Bandula Gunawardana,
the Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid
Act, in respect of the National School of Business Management Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PB 4833, do by this Order, amend the schedule II to the
Order made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2121/43 of May 2, 2019, by the addition
immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Management Honours in International Business” appearing in Column I and it’s
corresponding entry in Column II thereto, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the Schedule II
hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :–
“SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
National School of Business Management Limited shall, when admitting students for the degree programmes
relating to the degrees specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science Engineering Honours in
Electrical and Electronic Engineering  and Bachelor of Science Engineering Honours in Mechatronic Engineering, adhere
to the minimum entry qualifications of three (3) simple passes (‘S’)  in Physical Science subject stream in one and the
same sitting at the GCE (Advance Level) Examination or equivalent.
