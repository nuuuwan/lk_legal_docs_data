FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   GALLE  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member of  Galle Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Group Leader of  the Independent Group 02 to which Undugoda Pathirannehelage Kulathilaka
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned
member if Independent Group 02 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Group Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group 02 has communicated his decision to
nominate Loku Yaddehige Anuradha Gayan Sanjeewa to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer  of  the Galle Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Loku Yaddehige
Anuradha Gayan Sanjeewa was selected as the member of the Galle Municipal Council.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Galle Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Galle,
23rd May, 2019.
