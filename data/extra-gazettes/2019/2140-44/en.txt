Notice under Section 07
My Ref. No.: BKP/LAND/ACQUI/D2/1/3.
Land Ministry's No. : 4-3/15/2016/VE/426.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 07 of
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act of, No. 28 of 1964, that Government intends
to acquire the allotments of lands which have been enclosed
in the land called Kiriporuwa Part II mentioned in the schedule
for acquisition of land for resettlement of displaced persons
by landslides in 2016 in Divisional Secretary's Division of
Bulathkohupitiya.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Allotment of lands No. 6, 7 (Lots 02) mentioned in the
Schedule and enclosed in the P .P .K. No. 3679 of Superintendent
of Surveys, Kegalle situated in the Village of Kiriporuwa in
Grama Niladhari Division of Kiriporuwa in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Bulathkohupitiya in Kegalle District in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
Lot No. 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06
Name of Land: Kiriporuwa Part II; Claimants Name and
Address: 01. Lands Reform Commission No. 475, Kaduwela
Road, Battaramulla. 02. Managing Director Kegalle Plantations
PLC No. 310, High-level Road, Maharagama. 03. Ministry of
Plantation Industries Second Floor, Sethsiripaya, Stage II,
Battaramulla; Extent: 1.8729 Hectares.
Date of examinations for claims of compensation:
For Lots No. 06, 07 in P .P .L. No. 3679 on 15th October, 2019
at 10.00 a.m.
