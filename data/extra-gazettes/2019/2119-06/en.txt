PUBLIC  SERVICE  COMMISSION
Open  Competitive  Examination  for  Recruitment  to  Grade  III  of   Sri  Lanka Agricultural
Service  -  2013 (2014)
IT is hereby notified that action will be taken to hold Open Competitive Examination for filling of vacancies under Serial
No. 2. 3 of  the Gazette Notification No. 1929 dated 21.08.2015 titled ‘‘Open/ Limited Competitive Examination for recruitment
to Grade III Sri Lanka Agricultural Service’’ amended by Gazette Notification No. 1933 dated 18.09.2015 and temporarily
suspended by Gazette Notification No. 1940 dated 06.11.2015.
1. To fill 51 out of  the vacancies on Open basis under Agri  development  stream until a Court  Ruling is given
for case No. SC (FR) 283/ 2014 filed at the Supreme Court without any impact on that case ;
2. To fill the remaining 18 vacancies on Open basis under Agri Development stream after Court Ruling is given
for case No. SC (FR) 283/ 2014 filed at the Supreme Court.
1A - G  29894 — 20  (04/2019)
