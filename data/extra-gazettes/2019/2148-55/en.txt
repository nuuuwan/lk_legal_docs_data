THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  NO. 2129/94  DATED  28TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/94 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4553/2).
In the caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification “NOTICE OF” Shall be inserted at the
beginning of the caption and the word “(PART)” shall be included immediately after “WATTALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA”
and “KELANIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA” and “URBAN DEVELOPMENT” shall be included after “COLOMBO
COMMERCIAL  CITY.”
The content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be rectified by inserting the word “(Part)”
immediately after “Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha” and “Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha” and “Urban Development” shall be included
after “Colombo Commercial City.”
The word “had” in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with “have”.
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;

“NOTICE OF APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE COLOMBO COMMERCIAL CITY URBAN
DEVELOPMENT AREA COMPRISING OF COLOMBO MUNICIPAL COUNCIL, DEHIWALA - MOUNT LAVINIA MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL, KOLONNAWA URBAN COUNCIL, BORALESGAMUWA URBAN COUNCIL, PELIYAGODA URBAN COUNCIL,
WATTALA - MABOLA URBAN COUNCIL, WATTALA PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART) and KELANIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA
(PART) AREAS.”
“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
Section 8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the Colombo
Commercial City Urban Development Area comprising of Colombo Municipal Council, Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia
Municipal Council, Kolonnawa Urban Council, Boralesgamuwa Urban Council, Peliyagoda Urban Council, Wattala -
Mabola Urban Council, Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas, prepared under
Section 8A of the said Law.”
P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
In the caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Part)” shall be included immediately
after “Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha” and “Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha” and “Urban Development” shall be included after
“Colombo Commercial City”.
In the content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Amendment)” given in the second
line thereof shall be substituted with “Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the”.
The content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be rectified by inserting the word “(Part)”
immediately after “Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha” and “Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha”, and “Urban Development” shall be
included after “Colombo Commercial City”.
The word “have” in the fifth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be subsitituted with “has”.
The word “Amendment Act” given in the last line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with
“Amended Law.”
The rectifications introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Colombo Commercial City Urban Development Area comprising of Colombo
Municipal Council, Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council, Kolonnawa Urban Council, Boralesgamuwa Urban
Council, Peliyagoda Urban Council, Wattala - Mabola Urban Council, Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Kelaniya
Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) Areas”.
Public are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority
Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982, for the Colombo Commercial City Urban Development Area
comprising of Colombo Municipal Council, Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council, Kolonnawa Urban Council,
Boralesgamuwa Urban Council, Peliyagoda Urban Council, Wattala - Mabola Urban Council, Wattala Pradeshiya
Sabha (Part) and Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas has been approved on 28th June, 2019, by Hon. Patali
Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in him under
Section 8F of the said amended Law.”
D
R. JAGATH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
07th November, 2019,
