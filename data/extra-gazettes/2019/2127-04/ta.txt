FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KORALAI  PATTU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a elected member of  Koralai Pattu pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Thamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Nagalingam Thiraviyam the member elected belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon the  member of  that  ward 06-Kalkudha of Koralai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ponnaiyah Pushpenthiran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of the Koralai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ponnaiyah
Pushpenthiran  was elected as the member of the Ward 06-Kalkudha  of Koralai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. Saseelan,
Returning Officer,
Koralai Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
07th June, 2019.
