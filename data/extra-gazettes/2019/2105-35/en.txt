FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BANDARAWELA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member  of   Bandarawela Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the Independent Group No. 1 to  which Naleen Priyantha Suriyage the member
belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of   that
ward No. 05, Bandarawela West of Bandarawela Municipal Council  who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Indepdendent Group No. 1 has communicated his decision to
nominate Heenkenda Mudiyanselage Mithrapala Heenkenda to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, H. I. R. Hathurusingha, Returning Officer  of  the  Bandarawela Municipal Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Heenkenda
Mudiyanselage Mithrapala Heenkanda was elected as the member of the Ward No. 05 Bandarawela West of Bandarawela
Municipal Council.
H. I. R. H
ATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Bandarawela Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Badulla,
09th January  2019.
