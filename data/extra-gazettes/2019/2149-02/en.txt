 NOTIFICATION   UNDER  SECTION  16  SUB-SECTIONS   (2D)   OF   THE   COAST  CONSERV ATION  AND
COASTAL  RESOURCE   MANAGEMENT  ACT,   No.  57  OF   1981
Proposed Rehabilitation of Port of Kankasanthurai
Jaffna District
ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT  ASSESSMENT   REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Rehabilitation of Port of
Kankasanthurai in Jaffan District submitted by the Sri Lanka Ports Authority, No. 19, Chaithya Road, Colombo 01 as
required by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conversation and Coastal Resource Managment Act, No. 57 of 1981 as
amended by the Act, No. 64 of 1988 and No. 49 of 2011 will be available for inspection by the public at the following
institutions from 8.30 a. m. to 4.15 p. m. for a period of 30 days from 11.11.2019 (Except weekends and public holidays)
In addition, this report with 03 languages can be used in the official wedsite (www.coastal.gov.lk) of the
Department of Coast Conservation & Coastal Resource Management.
(1) Coast Conservation & Coastal Resources Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor,
Maligawatte, Colombo 10.
(2) Pradeshiya Sabha, Valikamam North, Jaffna
(3) District Secretariat, Jaffna
(4) Divisional Secretariat, Tellippalai
(5) District Fisheries Office, Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, Jaffna
(6) Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa”, Densil Kobbakaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
