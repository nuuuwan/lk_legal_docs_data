The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Amarasinghe Arachchilage Ishara Madushani
Amarasingha of the Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as she ceased
to be a member of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna.
SAJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Aranayaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
03rd May, 2019.
