THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry's No.:  LD/5/2008/HW/442.
My Ref. No.:  DST/LND/1/ACQ/57.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired:
Land Acqusition of the Bridge No. 210/3 of Colombo, Galle,
Hambanthota, Wellawaya Road.
Land called in Ketangahakoratuwa, Pallemeathmaga, Pallama
and Ambagahawela depicted as Lot Nos. 732 and 733 (Lots
02) in total extent of about 0.0058 Hectare, of land in Final
Village Plan No. 328 dated 30.09.2016 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the village Ranna and Minor Division of
Negenahira Giruwa Pattuwa in the Grama Niladari Division of
Ranna West Divisional Secretary's Division of Tangalle in
Hambanthota District in Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 732
Name of land: Ketagahakoratuwa, Pallemeathmaga and
Pallama; Extent: 0.0052 Hectare.
G 30023 - 17 (2019/05 )
North, East and South by: Lot No. 79; West by: Lot Nos.
79, 81A  and 733.
02. Lot No. 733
Name of land: Ambagahawela; Extent: 0.0006 Hectare.
North: Lot No. 81A; East by: Lot Nos. 79 and 732; South
and West by: Lot No.81A.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands are
hereby required to appear personally or by agents  duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat
Tangalle on the 10th July 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the  03rd July 2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/Servitude the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
