The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   POLONNARUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
D. Lahiru Ashen the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate H. M. Sunil Kulathilaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Leelanath Wickrama Arachchi, Returning Officer  of  the  Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
H. M. Sunil Kulathilaka was selected as the member of the Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
L
EELANA TH WICKRAMA ARACHCHI,
Returning Officer,
Polonnaruwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
27th of November, 2019.
