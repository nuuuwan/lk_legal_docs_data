KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Notice on repealing the Notifications of the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1725/22 dated 30th of September
2011
I, the Mayor of Kalmunai Municipal Council, by the virtue of powers vested in me by the provision of Section 14(5) of
Chapter 252 of Municipal Council Ordinance, repeal the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1725/22 dated 30th of September,
2011 published by the Special Commissioner of Kalmunai Municipal Council, M. M. Nawfal who functioned until the
members elected by the Local Government Election.

KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Notice on levy for Solid Waste Disposal Management Service provided within the Kalmunai Municipal
Council Limits
BY the Extraordinary Gazette No. 51/7 dated 28.01.1989 under the provision of Local Authority Council Act, No. 6 of
1952 (Statutes by-laws) and by the order recognized in the Local Government Gazette No. 1093 dated 13.08.1999 published
by the Governor of Eastern Province and by the order published in the Local Government Gazette No. 1240 under the
Section 3 of the above Local Authority Councils Act (Statutes by-laws) accepted by Kalmunai Municipal Council and by the
virtue of powers vested in Kalmunai Municipal Council by the 4th sub law under the Chapter XI (by laws as to waste
disposal), and by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1808 dated 26.07.2013 recognized and accepted by decision taken in the
council meeting held on 27th of March, 2013, it is hereby informed that the implemented levy on solid waste disposal
management service indicated in the budget proposal for the year 2019 recognized by Kalmunai Municipal Council on
14.01.2019 is amended as follows :
S. No. Category of the service beneficiary Levy for the Solid Waste
Disposal Service
Rs.
01 Per a Residence for a day 10.00
02 Per Commercial Institution for a day 15.00
(except canteens, hotels, plantain shops)
03 Per a Factory for a day 20.00
04 Per Canteens, hotels, plantain shop and beef stalls, 5.00
mutton stalls, chicken stalls and fish stalls for
a kilogram (IKG)
05 Per a used coconut and a king coconut (Must be cut 1.00
equally halves)
The above levy for Waste Disposal Management service is come in force with effect from 15th of January, 2019.
A. M. R
AKEEB, LLB
Mayor,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
Municipal Council Office,
Kalmunai,
25th February, 2019.
