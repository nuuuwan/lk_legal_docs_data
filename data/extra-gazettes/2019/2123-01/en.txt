The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, C. Jayasuriya, Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 13th  May,  2019, all  duties of Customs
as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2122/2 of  06th  May,  2019
is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification to be published
on or before 19th   May, 2019.
C. J
AY ASURIY A,
Acting  Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
10th  May, 2019.
