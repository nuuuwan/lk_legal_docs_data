I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of  the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the land described below for the construction of
the canal connecting Kiththampahuwa Canal and Kollonnawa
canal through Harvard Dam, implemented under the Metro
Colombo Urban Development Project.
