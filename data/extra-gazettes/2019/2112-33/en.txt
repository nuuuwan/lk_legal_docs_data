HIGH  COURT  OF   THE  PROVINCES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 10  OF 1996
Notification
(PARAGRAPH  (1)  OF  THE  FIRST  SCHEDULE)
I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Notification, fix with effect from 15th of  March 2019
Rupees Twenty Million, as the amount for the purpose of paragraph (1) of the First Schedule to the High Court of the
Provinces (Special Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 1996, in lieu of the sum of  Rupees Five Million.
This increase in the monetary value shall not affect the actions filed in the High Court before the 15 th of March
2019.
Notification made under paragraph (1) of the First Schedule to the High Court of the Provinces (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 10 of 1996 and published in Gazette Extraordinary  No.1759/ 35 of  25th May, 2012 is hereby rescinded without
prejudice to anything duly done thereunder.
THALATHA ATUKORALE,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
22nd  February 2019.
