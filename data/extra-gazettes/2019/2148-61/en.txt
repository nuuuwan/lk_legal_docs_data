THE NATIONAL THOROUGHFARES ACT, No. 40 OF 2008
Order under Section  9
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 9 (1)(a) of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir
Hashim, Minister of Highways & Road Development and Petroleum Resources Development, do by this Order designate
the National Highways which were declared as a  National Highways by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2148/ 60 of 09.11.2019 and specified in Schedule to this Order to be an  Expressway.
K
ABIR HASHIM,
Minister of Highways & Road Development and
Petroleum Resources Development.
Battaramulla,
08th November, 2019.
