FILLING  OF  A   V ACANCY   IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER   OF  THE  DEHIWALA - MOUNT   LA VINIA
MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION  66A(1)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPER  262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason
of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Dilini Nelunika Antonet Weerasinghe the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Lathpandurage Diana Gayani Perera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the Dehiwala - Mount
Lavinia Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that Lathpandurage Diana Gayani Perera was selected as the member of the Dehiwala - Mount
Lavinia Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala - Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
08th May, 2019.
