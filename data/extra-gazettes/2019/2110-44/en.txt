THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land  morefully described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of  the land Acquisition Act.
An allotment of land  in total extent 0.0736  Hectare depicted  as
Lot  A  in the advance tracing No. PO/HKG/2017/326 dated 16.10.2017
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village of Minneriya
in the Sinhala Pattuwa Minor Division  in the Hingurakgoda Divisional
Secretary’s Division in the  District of Polonnaruwa in the North
Central  Province and as bounded.
1. Lot ‘A’ – Patapilikanda Pahala Watta, Patapilikanda Kele –
Extent: 0.0736  Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 4 and 7 in C.P. 120134 zone 2; East: Lot No.  7 in
C.P. 120134 zone 02;  South: A part of Lot Nos. 7 and 5 and Lot 161
in C.P. 120134 zone 2; West: Lot  4  in C.P . 120134 zone 2.
