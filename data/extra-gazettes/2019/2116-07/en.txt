NOTIFICATION  UNDER   THE  CUSTOMS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  235)
NOTIFICATION under Sub-section (1) and (2) of the Section 22A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended
by Act, No. 83 of 1988 made by the Minister of Finance is hereby published for general information.

Secretary,
Ministry of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
26th March, 2019.
Notification under Sub-sections (1) and (2) of the Section 22A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Sub-sections (1) and (2) of the Section 22A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter
235) as amended by Act, No. 83 of 1988, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance of Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, do by this Notification amend the Rule No. 4 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1053/11 of 11.11.1998
by insertion of the following new section immediately after the Section 4 of the Gazette.
4.1 The importation of Black Pepper, Areca-nut, Nutmeg/Mace, Tamarind and Cinnamon shall not be permitted under
this Scheme.
M
ANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
26th March, 2019.
