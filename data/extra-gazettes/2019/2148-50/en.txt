THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  NO. 2129/95  DATED  28TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/95 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4554/6).
The caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification “AND  MAGALLE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA”
Shall be deleted.
The word “AND” shall be inserted immediately after “BOPE  PODDALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  (PART)”.
The content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by repealing “Magalle
Pradeshiya Sabha” as being part of the Greater Galle Urban Development Area.
The word “had” in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be substituted with “have.”
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be read as follows;
“NOTICE OF APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE GREATER GALLE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA
COMPRISING OF GALLE MUNICIPAL COUNCIL, AKMEEMANA PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART), BOPE PODDALA
PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART) AND HABARADUWA PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART) AREAS.”

PART I : S EC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA - 08.11.2019
“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
Section 8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the Greater Galle
Urban Development Area comprising of the Galle Municipal Council, Akmeemana Pradeshiya Sabha (Part), Bope
Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas, prepared under Section 8A of the
said Law.”
P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
The caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification “Magalle Pradeshiya Sabha” shall be deleted.
The word “and” shall be inserted immediately after “Bope Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part)”.
In the content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Amendment)” in the second line
shall be substituted with “Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the”.
The content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by repealing “Magalle
Pradeshiya Sabha” as being part of the Greater Galle Urban Development Area.
The word “have”in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be substituted with “has”.
The word “Amendment Act” given in the last line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with
“amended Law”.
The rectifications introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;.
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Greater Galle Urban Development Area comprising of Galle Municipal
Council, Akmeemana Pradeshiya Sabha (Part), Bope Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Habaraduwa Pradeshiya
Sabha (Part) Areas.”
“Public are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority
Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982, for the Greater Galle Urban Development Area comprising of
Galle Municipal Council, Akmeemana Pradeshiya Sabha (Part), Bope Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Habaraduwa
Pradeshiya Sabha (part) areas has been approved on 28th June, 2019, by Hon. Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of
Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 8F of the said amended Law.”
D
R. JAGATH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
07th November, 2019,
Notwithstanding the provisions contained in the antecedent Gazette Notification  bearing No. 2129/95 dated 28th June,
2019 (07-4554/6), the rectification hereinbefore mentioned shall have retrospective effect and shall be deemed to have
come into operation on the 28th day of June, 2019.
P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
