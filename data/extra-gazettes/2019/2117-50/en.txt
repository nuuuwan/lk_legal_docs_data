THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 2 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the
recommendation of the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development being of the opinion that the allotments
land specified in the Schedule hereto is urgently required for the purpose of Kalido Beach Development activities under
Kalutara Urban Development programme do by this Order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
April 02, 2019.
