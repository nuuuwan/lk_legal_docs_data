PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 15.05.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Unapadhure Ovita alias Attikkagaha
Ovita alias Unapadhura Ovita and Attikkagaha Ovita in total extent
of about 0.1660 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in the Advance
Tracing No. CO/HNW/2017/149 of 03.08.2018, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Kaluaggala in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 436/A of Suduwella in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Seethawaka in Colombo District  of Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Unapadhure Ovita  alias Attikkagaha Ovita
alias Unapadhura Ovita and Attikkagaha Ovita – Extent: About
0.1660 Hectare
North: Thelambugahawatte Kebella of which claimant could not
be traced and balance portion of this land; East: Muanamalewatta of
which the claimant could not be traced and in which the 5th Military
Intelligence Corps is located, Egalage Ovita alias Peringaha Ovita
claimed by K. A. Anil, Egalage Ovita alias Peringaha Ovita claimed
by Nazwan and Egalage Ovita and Madawala Ovita claimed by
D. G . Mary Justin; South: Egalage Ovita alias Peringaha Ovita claimed
by K. A. Anil, Egalage Ovita  alias Peringaha Ovita claimed by
Nazwan and Egalage Ovita and Madawala Ovita claimed by D. G .
Mary Justin and Road; West: Road, Thelambugahawatte Kebella
of which claimant could not be traced and balance portion of
this land.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
