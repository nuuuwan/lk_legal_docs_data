THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  No. 2129/95  DATED  28TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/95 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4554/9).
In the caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the words “MUNICIPAL COUNCIL  AREA”
Shall be substituted with  “URBAN DEVELOPMENT  AREA  COMPRISING  OF  ANURADHAPURA  MUCICIPAL  COUNCIL,
NUWARAGAM  PALATHA  CENTRAL  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  (PART)  AND  NUWARAGAM  PALATHA  EAST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  (PART)  AREAS.”
The content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by establishing the
relevance of the said Development Plan to, Anuradhapura Urban Development Area comprising of Anuradhapura Municipal
Council, Nuwaragama Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Nuwaragama Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas.
The word “had” in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with “have”.
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;

“NOTICE OF APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE ANURADHAPURA URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA
COMPRISING OF ANURADHAPURA  MUNICIPAL COUNCIL, NUWARAGAMA  PALATHA  CENTRAL  PRADESHIYA
SABHA (PART)  AND  NUWARAGAMA  PALATHA  EAST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  (PART) AREAS.”
“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis & Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section
8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the Anuradhapura Urban
Development Area comprising of Anuradhapura Municipal Council, Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Shaba
(Part) and Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas prepared under Section 8A of the said Law.”
P
ATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
In the caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the words “Municipal Council Areas” shall
be Subsitituted with “Urban Development Area comprising of Aunradhapura Municipal Council, Nuwaragam Palatha
Central Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Nuwaragama Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) Areas.”
The content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by establishing the
relevance of the said Development Plan to, Anuradhapura Urban Development Area comprising of Anuradhapura Muncipal
Council, Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradehiya Sabha (Part) and Nuwaragama Palatha East Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) Areas.
In the content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Amendement)” in the second
line shall be subsititued with “Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the”.
The word “Amendment Act” given in the last line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with
“Amended Law.”
The rectifications introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows:
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Anuradhapura Urban Development Area comprising of Anuradhapura
Municipal Cooucil, Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) and Nuwaragam Palatha East Pradeshiya
Sabha (Part) Areas.”
Public are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority
Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982, for the Anuradhapura Urban Development Area comprising of
Anuradhapura Municipal Council, Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Shaba (Part) and Nuwaragam Palatha
East Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas has been approved on 28th June, 2019, by Hon. Patali Champika Ranawaka,
Minister of Megapolis & Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 8F of the said amended
Law.”
D
R. JAGATH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
07th November, 2019,
