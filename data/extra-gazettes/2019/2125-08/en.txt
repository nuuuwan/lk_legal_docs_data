THE  MEDIATION   (SPECIAL   CATEGORIES   OF   DISPUTES)  ACT,   No.   21   OF   2003
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act, No. 21 of 2003,
I, Thalatha Athukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by  this Order, specify the area set out in the Schedule
hereto, as a Special Mediation Board Area within which the settlement of disputes relating to grievances of migrant
workers and members of their families residing in Sri Lanka as determined by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2078/36 dated July 04, 2018 shall be carried out, under the provisions of the said Act.
T
HALATHA ATHUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
16th May, 2019.
