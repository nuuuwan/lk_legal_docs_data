Bata Shoe Company of Ceylon Limited, 100, General Sir John Kotalawala Road, Ratmalana.of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. H. S. L. Perera working at Bata Shoe Company of Ceylon Limited who was confirmed in the post of Manipulant
with effect from 02.02.2004 had been caused injustice by
1. The payment of a lower salary
2. Reduction of the monthly salary and
3. Not increasing the salary scale
and if so, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, this 07th day of September, 2019.
