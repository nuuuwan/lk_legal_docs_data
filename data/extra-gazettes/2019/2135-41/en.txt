PROVINCIAL COUNCIL NOTIFICATIONS
CENTRAL PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
I, Maithree Gunarathna, The Governor of Central Province impose the following regulations on the payment of fuel allowance
for Members of the Urban Council, made as per the powers vested on me by Section 193 (g) of the Muni cipal Council
Ordinance which is the 255th rule to be read with clause 2 (1) (a) of Provincial Councils Act, (Cons equential Provisions)
No. 12 of 1989. These regulations shall be effective  from 15.03.2019.
MAITHREE GUNARATHNE,
Governor,
Central Province.
On 15th, March, 2019,
At Governor’s Secretariat,
Central Province.
Regulations
01. These regulations may be cited as the regulations on payment of fuel allowance for Members of Urban Council.
02. These allowances are in addition to the allowances stated in the extraordinary Gazette No. 1914/40 of 15.05.2015 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. A fuel allowance may be paid to the Members of the Urban Council by their
relevant Urban Council funds as follows.
