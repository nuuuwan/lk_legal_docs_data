1A  G  30464— 27  (07/ 2019)
TOURISM   ACT,   No.  38  OF  2005
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 70 of the Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005, I, John Antony Emmanual
Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, do hereby order in line with the
Section 49(a) of the Land  Acquisitions Act, that the land mentioned in the Schedule below is required to be acquired for the
purpose of development of tourism by Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority.
J
OHN ANTONY E MMANUAL  AMARATHUNGA ,
Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and
Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
27th June 2019.
Schedule
The land 199.6 hectare approximately (493 Acre) in extent depicted by Geodetic coordinate and plan given below prepared by
Divisional Secretary (Hikkaduwa) situated in the villages of Akurala, Galdoowa, Dimbuladoowa, Uduwaragoda, Kahawa,
Weragoda in the Grama Niladhari Divisions Akurala, Galdoowa, Dimbuladoowa, Uduwaragoda North, Kahawa, Weragoda
in Hikkaduwa, Divisional Secretary’s Division in Galle District of Southern Province.
North : Northern boundaries of Grama Niladhari Divisions of Dimbuldoowa, Galdoowa and Weragoda.
East : Eastern boundaries of Grama Niladhari Divisions of Dimbuldoowa, Galdoowa and Weragoda.
South : Southern boundaries of Weragoda, Kahawa, Uduwaragoda North and Akurala ;
West : Western boundaries of Kahawa, Uduwaragoda North, Akurala and Dimbuldoowa
