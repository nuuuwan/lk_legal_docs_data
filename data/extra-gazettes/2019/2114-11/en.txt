AGRARIAN  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  46  OF  2000
Determination under Section 9
IN accordance to the powers vested in me under Section 9(1) of the Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000 as
amended by the Agrarian Development (Amendment) Act, No. 46 of 2011, I, W. M. M. B. Weerasekara, Commissioner
General of Agrarian Development hereby determined that the specified rent in the Column II for any extent of paddy land
located completely or mainly in the area specified in the Column I of the Schedule of this Notification is the rent to be
paid for paddy cultivation season by the tenant cultivator to the landlord.
02. The notice published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2086/26 and 30.08.2018 dated, under
Section 35(1) of the Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000 is hereby repealed.

AGRARIAN  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,  No.  46  OF  2000
Determination under Section 35(1)
IN accordance to the powers vested in me under Section 35(1) of the Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000 as
amended by the Agrarian Development (Amendment) Act, No. 46 of 2011, I, W. M. M. B. Weerasekara, Commissioner
General of Agrarian Development hereby determined that the specified rent in the Column II for one acre cultivated in
each cultivation season in any paddy land extent, for agricultural crops specified in Column I of the Schedule of this
Notification is the rent to be paid by the tenant cultivator to the landlord, where any extent of paddy land is cultivated with
any other agricultural crop other than paddy under said Act.
02. The notice published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2086/26 and 30.08.2018 dated, under
Section 35(1) of the Agrarian Development Act, No. 46 of 2000 is hereby repealed.
W. M. M. B. W
