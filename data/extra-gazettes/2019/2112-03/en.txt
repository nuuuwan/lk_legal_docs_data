Government intends to acquire the following land /
servitude which is required for a public purpose.

extent of about 2.4522 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 in 
Plan No. MN 798 and prepared by Surveyor General 
situated in the village called Konnayankudiyiruppu, in the 
Grama Niladari Division No. 62 of Thoddaveli, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s division of Mannar Town of 
Mannar District in Northern Province.  
 
Plan No.: MN 798  
Lot No.01 
Name of the land: Perukadipannaikani; Present Use of 
the Land: Bare Land; Claimant: Government; Original 
Claimant: Not Known; Extent: 2.4522 Hectares.   
The dates and the time of the inquiries: 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby  required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly  authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 22nd day of 
March 2019 at Mannar Town Divisional Secretariat. 
 
and Further, you are hereby requested  to notify to me in

the details of the computation of such amount.
