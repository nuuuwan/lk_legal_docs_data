FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KATTANKUDY   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the All Ceylon Makkal Congress, the recognized political party to which
Ahamed Mohideen Mohamed Mahir the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  All Ceylon Makkal Congress who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thamby Lebbe Theenul Jawfer to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thamby Lebbe
Theenul Jawfer was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
30th April, 2019.
