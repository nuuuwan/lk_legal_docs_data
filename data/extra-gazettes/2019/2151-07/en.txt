Direction No. 69
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders of float operated valves and
spare parts, thereof that on each and every pack, following information shall be printed legibly and indelibly in Sinhala,
Tamil & English languages.
1. Brand Name
2. Name of the Product/spare part
3. Name and address of the Manufacturer, Contact details of the Manufacturer, if the product is manufactured/
assembled in Sri Lanka.
4. Name, Address and contact details of the importer
5. Country of Origin (Factory Location)
6. Registration Number issued by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution
7. Batch Identification Number/Code
8. Maximum Retail Price
9. Nominal size of the valve
10. Pressure Category (Low/Medium/High)
11. Body Pattern Number
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
