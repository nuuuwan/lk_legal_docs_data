NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made  by  the  Hon. Governor  of  North  Central  Provincial Council
BY virtue of the power vested under Section 32(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, Hon. Sarath Ekanayake,
the Governor of the North Central Province, do hereby notify that the officers, names mentioned below have been appointned
for the posts North Central Provincial Council Services.
01. Mrs. Thejani Thilakerathne, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka Administrative Service as the Deputy
Secretary (Admin.) of the Chief Ministry of North Central Province with effect from 28.01.2019.
02. Mr. W. A. R. Wimalasiri, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka Administrative Service (retired) as the  Secretary
of the Proivincial Public Service Commission of North Central Province for 03 months with effect from
11.03.2019 to 10.06.2019 on contract basis.
03. Mr. Ananda Kularathne, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka Administrative Service (retired) as the  Secretary
of the Chief Ministry of North Central Province for 02 months with effect from 01.07.2019 untill 31.08.2019
on contract basis.
04. Mr. P. K. Jayalath, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka Administrative Service as the  Secretary of the
Ministry of Provincial Lands, Irrigation, Highways, Rural Development, House and Women Affairs of  North
Central Provincial Council with effect from 17.05.2019 untill 31.08.2019 on contract basis.
