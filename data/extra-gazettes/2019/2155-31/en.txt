The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   HOMAGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of member of  Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Jayamuni Thilan Randima Gunarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that Ward No. 05 Nawalamulla of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kuruppu Arachchige Chaminda Priyadarshana Kuruppu Arachchi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  the of Homagama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Kuruppu Arachchige Chaminda Priyadarshana  Kuruppu Arachchi was elected as the member of the
Ward No. 05 Nawalamulla of Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
27th December, 2019.
