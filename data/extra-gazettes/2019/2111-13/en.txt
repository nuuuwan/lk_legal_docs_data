L.D.B. 21/1978(XIV)
THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the South Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been restored to normal conditions, do by this Order, revoke with
effect from August 9, 2018, the Order made under the aforesaid Act, and published in the Government Gazette
Extrarodinay No. 2076/13 of June 19, 2018 appointing Professor Uma Coomaraswamy as the Competent Authority in
lieu of the Vice-Chancellor of the South Eastern University, Sri Lanka.
A. I
BATHUL R AUFF H AMEEM,
Minister of City Planning,
Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
06th February, 2019.
