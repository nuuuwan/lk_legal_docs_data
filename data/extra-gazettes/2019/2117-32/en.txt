STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
01. In view of letter dated 25.09.2002 issued by the Personnel Manager, Sri Lanka Transport Board to Mr. P . A. A. Gunatilake
who was recruited to the service of the said Board with effect from 2nd July 1970. What date should be cosidered as the
date of first appointment of him?
02. What should be the retiring age of Mr. P. A. A. Gunatilake according to the said date of first appointment?
03. Whether any unjust was caused to Mr. P . A. A. Gunatilake due to retiring him at the age of 55 years effective from
28.12. 2005? if any unjust was caused, to what relief is entitled, and
04. Whether Mr. P. A. A. Gunatilake is entitled to receive arrears of salary in terms of the Circular No. 02 of 2004 issued by
the Finance Division of the Sri Lanka Transport Board. If so, what should be the quantum in arrears payable to him.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
