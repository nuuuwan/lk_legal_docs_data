APPOINTMENT   OF   DISTRICT   OPIUM   BOARDS   UNDER   THE   OPIUM   ORDINANCE   IN 1910,
AS AMENDED   BY   THE   POISONS,   OPIUM   AND   DANGEROUS   DRUGS   ORDINANCE
No. 17 OF 1929
IN terms of the powers vested in me under the Section 42 (2) of the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs Ordinance
No. 17 of 1929 (Chapter 549), I, Rajitha Senarathna, Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine do hereby
amend the Opium Board constituted for Badulla District as follows.
Accordingly, Dr. (Mrs.) R. M. C. Janaki Ekanayake who was appointed as member of  the District Opium Board
in the Badulla District by Gazette Extraordinary No. 1638/6 and dated 25.01.2010 has been replaced by Dr. (Mrs.)
Nirupa Sanjeewani Ratnayake.
D
R. R AJITHA S ENARA THNA,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine.
23rd March, 2019.
