THE  FINANCE  ACT,  No.  35  OF  2018
Order under Section 19 read together with Section 51
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section  19 read together with Section 51 of the Finance Act, No. 35 of 2018, I,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Finance (Luxury Tax on Motor Vehicle) Regulations
No. 1 of 2019, published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2113/11 dated March 05, 2019, by deleting
Regulation No. 3 including its sub-paragraphs (a) and (b) and replacing by the following new Regulation No. 3.
“3. The luxury tax on motor vehicle payable in terms of Section 19 of the Act, on any specified motor vehicle,
imported into Sri Lanka based on any Letters of Credit (LC) established or assembled in Sri Lanka for which, the bill of
entry delivered to the Director General of Customs on or after March 06, 2019, shall be paid to the Director General of
Customs, by the person importing such motor vehicle, or delivering such bill of entry, as the case may be, at the time of
releasing the motor vehicle from the Sri Lanka Customs”.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance
