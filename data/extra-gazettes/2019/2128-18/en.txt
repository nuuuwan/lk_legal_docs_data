THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                        My No.: AO/MN/02/07/2013.
                    Land Ministry No.: 4/3/4/2012/D/300.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which are
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The land allotments called vadalikoodalin in total extent
of 1.5064 Hectares depicted as Lot Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4 in Plan
No. MN 744 and prepared by Surveyor General situated in
the Village called Ward No. 04, Pesalai, in the Grama Niladari
Division of No. 57 of Pesalai North, in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Mannar Town of Mannar District in
Northern Province.
Plan No. MN 744
01. Lot No. 1, 2, 3, 4
Name of the Land: Vadali koodal; Present use of the land:
Garden contains; Claimant: Sebamalai Thomas Selvam Croos
and Savarian Pathinathan Culas; Original Claimant: Sebamalai
Thomas Selvam Croos and Savarian Pathinathan Culas;
Extent: 1.5064 Hectares.
The dates and the time of the inquiries:
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.00 a.m. on the 23rd day of July, 2019 at Mannar Town
Divisional Secretariat.
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 30th day of July 2019
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of
their claims for the compensation for the acquisition of the
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
K. Sivasampu,
Divisional Secretary,
Mannar Town Division,
Mannar.
Divisional Secretariat of Mannar Town,
14th June 2019
