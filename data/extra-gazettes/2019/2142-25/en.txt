PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
L.D.B 21/78(xiv)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities
of the said University have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint B. Asoka Keerthi De Silva to be
the Competent Authority with effect from September 24, 2019 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging
in lieu of the Vice-Chancellor, University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri Lanka, any power, duty or function
respectively which  has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor under the said Act, or
under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
24th September, 2019.
