THE   INDUSTRIAL   DISPUTES   ACT,   CHAPTER   131   OF   THE   LEGISLATIVE   ENACTMENTS   OF   CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Revocation of order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by order made under Section 4(1) of the  Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956 ), as amended by Acts Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968  dated 16.03.2018 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2063/22 dated 20.03.2018 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the
statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 27.02.2018.
Mr. J. Jagatheeswaran, No. 3/1, 3rd Cross Street, Ramakrishnapuram, Kallady.
and
Ceylon Petroleum Corporation, No. 609, Dr. Danister de Silva Mawatha, Colombo 09.
was referred to Mr. P. Danansooriya for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said order by revoked, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and
Trade Union Relations do hereby revoke the said order and further make order that no proceedings be taken upon the said
order dated 16.03.2018.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
01st August, 2019.
Colombo.

THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS an industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between,
Mr. J. Jagatheeswaran, No. 3/1, 3rd Cross Street, Ramakrishnapuram, Kallady.
and
Ceylon Petroleum Corporation, No. 609, Dr. Danister de Silva Mawatha, Colombo 09.
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested
in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised
Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial disputes-Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. P. Nawaratne, No. 570/B/1, Ekamuthu Mawatha, Off Nugegoda Road, Thalawathugoda to be
the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
01st August, 2019.
Colombo.

and

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. J. Jegatheeswaran who worked as the supervisor of the Eastern office of the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation,
interdicted from 19.10.2010 and released from all charges following the disciplinary inquiry against him had been caused
injustice by not been paid the salary and allowances forms 19.10.2010 to 02.04.2012, the period during which he had been
interdicted and if such injustice had been caused, to what relief he is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 29th day of July 2019.
