SRI  LANKA  INSTITUTE  OF  ADV ANCED  TECHNOLOGICAL  EDUCATION  ACT,  No. 29 OF  1995
Order under Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20 of the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education Act,
No. 29 of 1995,  I, A. Ibathul Rauff  Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, in
consultation with the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education do by this Order -
(a) establish with effect from September 1, 2019 an Advanced Technological Institute for the purpose of providing,
promoting and developing the education of technological personnel and associate professionals ;
(b) assign the name and style of the “Batticaloa Advanced Technological Institute” to such institute ;
(c) specify that the location of such institute shall be in the “District of Batticaloa” ;
(d) assign departments as “Management and Accountancy”, “Languages and Information Technology” and “Natural
Science and Tourism Hospitality Management” to such institute ;
(e) specify divisions of study as “Management”, “Accountancy”, “Languages”, “Tourism Hospitality Management”
and “Information Technology”.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF  H AKEEM,
Minister of City Planning,
Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
02nd October, 2019.
