FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PUTHUKKUDIYIRUPPU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Sabareththinam Thiruchchelvam the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that 03 Udayarkaddu of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiy Sabha who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Valarmathi Subakaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanthirasa Kantheepan, Returning Officer  of  the  Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Valarmathi Subakaran was selected as the member of the 03 Udayarkaddu of Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. K
ANTHEEPAN,
Returning Officer,
Puthukkudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Mullaitivu,
24th September, 2019.
