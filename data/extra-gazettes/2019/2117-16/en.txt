1A

THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour
which accompanies this order exists between,
Inter Company Employees Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Thalangama.  of the one part
and
Noratel International (Pvt) Ltd, P.O. Box. 15, Phase 11, KEPZ, Katunayake.  of the other part
NOW THEREFORE I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested
in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised
Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes-Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mr. L. R. Sumathipala, Principal Quarters, Nurses’ Training School, Kandy to be the arbitrator
and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
26th March, 2019.

Inter Company Employees Union, No. 259/9, Sethsiri Mawatha, Koswatta, Thalangama.  of the one part
and
Noratel International (Pvt) Ltd, P.O. Box. 15, Phase 11, KEPZ, Katunayake.  of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the claim made by the Inter Company Employees’ Union for a salary increase of Rs. 500/- and a 16.5% increase of
basic salary for year 2019, which is the cause of the strike pending in Noratel International (Private) Limited without agreement
from 11.02.2019 is just, and if not so, to what reliefs the employees are entitled.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 21st day of March, 2019.
