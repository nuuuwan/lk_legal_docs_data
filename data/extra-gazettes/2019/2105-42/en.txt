THE   LAND    ACQUISITION    ACT   (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
My Reference No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/13'
                   Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/250.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands/Servitude which is required for a public
purpose.
      Description of the land or Servitude  to be acquired:
The land allotment  called Kulaielanthai in total extent of
about 2.8518 Hectares depicted as Lot  No. 1 & 2 in Plan No;
MN778 and prepared by Surveyor General situated in the
Village called Nadukkuda, in the Grama Niladhari Division
No. 54 of Tullukkudiyiruppu, in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Mannar Town of Mannar District in Northern
Province.
  G 29224 - 17 (2019/01)
 Plan No. MN 778.
01. Lot No. 01 & 02
Name of Land: Kulaielanthai; Present use of the land: Bare
land; Claimant: Government; OriginalClaimant: Mr. M. S. M.
Salihu, Tharapuram, Mannar; Extent: 2.8518Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on
which a  servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing,
before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 15th day of February, 2019 at
Mannar town Divisional Secretariat, and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing [in duplicate] on or before the
08.02.2019, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
          K.SIVASAMBU,
   Divisional Secretary,
