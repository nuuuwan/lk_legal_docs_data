The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   AKURANA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which Abdul Majeed Mohomed Simsan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United People’s Freedom Alliance who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Pahala Walauwe Abdul Azeez Hajju Mohideen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the  Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Pahala Walauwe Abdul Azeez Hajju Mohideen was selected as the member of the Akurana Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
30th September, 2019.
