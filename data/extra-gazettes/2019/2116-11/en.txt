L.D.B 21/1978(X)(A)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,
the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the
aforesaid Act, in respect of the Aquinas College of Higher Studies, do by this Order amend the Schedule II to the Order
made under the said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2005/11 of February 07, 2017 by the addition
immediately after the entry “(5) Bachelor or Science Honours in Agro Industry Management” appearing in Column I
and it’s corresponding entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the
Schedule II hereto, subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
CONDITIONS
Aquinas College of Higher Studies shall, when admitting students for the degree programme specified in
Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Education Honours in English, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications
of three (03) ‘S’ passes in any subject stream (‘English’ shall be one of main three subjects) in one and the same sitting at
the GCE Advanced Level Examination or equivalent.
