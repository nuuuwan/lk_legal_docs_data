THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No. : AO/MN/01/07/2014.
Land Ministry  No. : 4-3/2014/D/195.
I  hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment ) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
a security purpose.
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired:
The land allotment called selluvari in total extent of about
4.1359 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No. MN 741
land prepared by Surveyor General situated in the village
called Pavillupaddan Kudiyiruppu, in the Grama Niladari
Division No. MN/53 of Kaddukarankudiyiruppu, in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Mannar town of Mannar
District in North Province.
Plan No. MN 741
     01. Lot No. 1
Name of land:  Selluvari;  Present use of the land:  Garden
contains Palmyra Trees; Claimant by: Land Reform
Commission; Original Claimant:  Land Reform Commission;
Extent: 4.1359 Hectares.
The dates and the time of the inquiries:
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writting before me
at 10.00 a.m. on 28th February 2019 at Mannar Divisional
Secretariat.
And  further you are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 23rd February 2019 the
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for the compensation for the acquision of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
K. SIVASAMPU,
Divisional Secretary,
Mannar town.
