THE   LAND    ACQUISITION    ACT   (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                       My No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/11'
                               Land Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/248.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
      Description of the land or servitude  to be acquired:
The land called Doctorthoddam and Doctorodai in total
extent of about 3.2657 Hectares depicted as Lot  Nos. 1, 2 and 3
in Plan No. MN781 and prepared by the Surveyor General
situated in the Village called Nadukkuda, in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 54 of Tullukkudiyiruppu, in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Mannar Town of Mannar District in
Northern Province.
01. Lot Nos. 01, 02 and 03
Name of Land: Doctorthoddam Doctorodai; Present use of
the land: Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:
   G 29225 - 17 (2019/01)
Seylan Business (Pvt) Ltd of No. 10/18 Lake Drive, Colombo
08; Extent: 3.2657 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Mannar Town on the 15.02.2019
at 10.00 a.m. and further you are hereby requested to notify
to me in. writing [in duplicate] on or before the 08.02.2019, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
       K. SIVASAMBU,
Divisional Secretary,
        Mannar.
Divisional Secretariat,
Mannar Town,
07th January, 2019.
