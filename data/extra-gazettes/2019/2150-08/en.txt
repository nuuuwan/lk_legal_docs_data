No. 2150/8 – TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 19, 2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
REVOCATION UNDER SECTION 39(1)
The land specified morefully in the Order No. 172 of 2018 dated
02.08.2018 made under the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land
Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette
Extra Ordinary No. 2082/61 dated 03.08.2018 was vested in the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. But the possession of
the land described below has not yet been taken for and on behalf of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore, I, Gayantha
Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms, by
virtue of powers vested in me under Section 39 (1) of the Land
Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the Order made in respect of the
lands sepcified morefully in the Schedule below, under the aforesaid
vesting Order No. 172 of 2018 dated 02.08.2018 published in the
Gazette Extra Ordinary No. 2082/61 dated 03.08.2018.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
