The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIEIS  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Nagalingam Regan, Returning Officer of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a)
of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Adhikari Mudiyanselage Iranganie Pushpalatha of
the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as she has ceased to be
a member of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
N
AGALINGAM  R EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
25th November, 2019.
