Colombo 01,

PART 1: SEC. (1) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA  -  09.07.2019
Schedule
The following new paragraph is inserted immediately after paragraph 9 thereof and shall be effective as paragraph 9A of
the said Minute.
9A. However, when the services of a Secretary who is re-employed under para. 03of the schedule A. inserted under
paragraph 1A of this Minute cease, his/her pension shall be revised based on the salary drawn by him at the time
of the cessation* of his service provided such cessation of service is due to a non-disciplinary reason.
* Here the salary drawn by him at the time of cessation of his services is considered as the salary drawn by that
officer including the salary increments earned by him, salary increases granted by the Government from time to
time, pensionable allowances and any other allowances which is pensionable.
