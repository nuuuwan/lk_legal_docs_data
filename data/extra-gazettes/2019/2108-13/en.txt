THE LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref.No. : 1782.
Ministry of Lands & Parliamentary Reforms Reference No. :  4-3/
7/2016/HW/392.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/Servitude which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired:
The allotments of Land, containing in extent of 0.1060 Hectare,
Lots 1 - 3 (3 lots) under mentioned land shown in the Surveyor
General’s Preliminery Plan No. 1745 dated on 14.09.2018
situated in the Minor Division of Wagapanaha Pallesiya
Paththuwa in the Village of Welamitiyawa in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Welamitiyawa (E 440 D),  in Dambulla
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Matale District of Central
Province.
1. Lot No.1
Name of the Land: -; Description of Land: Road; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Dambulu Raja Maha
Viharaya; Extent: 0.0124 Hectare.
2. Lot No.2
Name of the Land: Weththiyaya; Description of Land: Paddy
field; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: Dambulu Raja
Maha Viharaya, Cultivated by Ekanayake Mudiyanselage
Gnanathilake; Extent: 0.0833 Hectare.
3. Lot No.3
Name of the Land: Weththiyaya; Description of Land: Paddy
field; Claimant: Government; Dambulu Raja Maha Viharaya,
Cultivated by V elvidanayalage Sisira; Extent: 0.0103 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Dambulla Divisional Secretariat, on 25 th  day of February
2019  at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th day of February
2019 the nature of their interests in the particulars of their
claims for computation for the said land/servitude the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
