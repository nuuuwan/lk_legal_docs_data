My No.: IR/COM/03/2017/08.
THE   INDUSTRIAL   DISPUTES   ACT,  CHAPTER   131   OF   THE   LEGISLATIVE   ENACTMENTS   OF   CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
 Order under Section 4(1)
WHERE as an industrial dispute in respect of  the matter/specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which
accompanies this order exists between,
Mr. B. M. Kumarasinghe, Gaduguduwawa, 15th Mile Post, Uraniya  of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05  of the other part
Now Therefore, I, Ravindra Samaraweera, Minister of  Labour and Trade Union Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in
me by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957,  4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37
of 1968 hereby appoint.
Mr. S. G . Sooriyaarachchi, No. 22/1, Sri Pemarathana Mawatha, Menikdiwela to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid
dispute to him for settlement by arbitration.
R
A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Colombo.
26th March, 2019.

STA TEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the procedures followed in deciding the compensation paid in the retirement of Mr. B. M. Kumarasinghe, who had
worked as a Driver attached to Mahiyangana Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board are just and if not so, to what reliefs he
is entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General  of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner  General of Labour,
Colombo this 18th day of March, 2019.
