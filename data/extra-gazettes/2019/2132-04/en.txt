1A   G  30521— 387  (07/2019)
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby, in the exercise of powers
vested in me by Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), enlarge the time by two and a half months
(2 1/2) from 14th July, 2019 for rendition of the final report on completion of the tasks assigned to the Commission
established under Section 2 of the said Commission of Inquiry Act on 14th January, 2019 by Notification published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2106/11 dated 16th January, 2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to
investigate and inquire into acts of corruption, fraud, criminal breach of trust, criminal misappropriation of property,
cheating and abuse or misuse of power or authority, state resources and privilegs which offences and acts of wrong doing
had allegedly occurred during the period 15th January, 2015 to 31st December, 2018.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
14th July, 2019.
