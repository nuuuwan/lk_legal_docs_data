Notice  Under Section 7
Land Mini’s Ref No. : 4-3/2/2016/PT/08.
My Ref. No. : DIVPP/LND/ACQSA/3/10-1.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the the following land which
is required for a public purpose.Description of the land to be
acquired:-
Public Purpiose: Acquisition of land for:
An allotment of land in total extent of 0.0250 depicted as
lot No. 1183 in sheet No.35 of  Suppliment No.27 of F.V .P 1961
dated 15.11.2018 situated in the village called Ma Eliya in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. 372 of Ma Eliya South
Divugandahaye Korale minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Polpithigama in Kurunegala District
in the North-Western province and more fully described as
follows:
Lot No. 1183
Name of the Land: Bulugahamulakotuwa; Description:
Home Garden; Claimant : Meragalpedi Durayalage Sunil
Sirisena, Total Extent: 0.0250 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Polpithigama Divisional Secretariat
on 27th may 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 26.04.2019
the nature of their interest in the land,the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
lands,the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
