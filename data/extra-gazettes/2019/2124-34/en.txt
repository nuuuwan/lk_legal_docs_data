PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 24.05.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MR/
DVN/2017/560 dated 31.07.2018, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Kapugama in the Minor Division of Wellabada
Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara in
Matara District of Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Gedarawatta  alias Gallegedarawatta,
Julgahakoratuwa  alias Julgahawatta, Ihalawatta – Extent:
0.8855 Hectare
North: Road; East: Ihalahenawatta of which claimant is not known,
Nugagahahena of which claimant is not known and Wathupitawatta
claimed by Wimalasena Alahakoon; South: Ihalahenawatta of which
claimant is not known, Nugagahahena of which claimant is not
known, Wathupitawatta claimed by Wimalasena Alahakoon and
Road; West: Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/118.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,

Battaramulla.
