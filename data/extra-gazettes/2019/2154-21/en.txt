Conditions and orders:-
 ( a) Unless there is recommendation in writing from the Director o f irrigation of the Southern Province Irrigation 
Department, no construction of buildings, structural work or re clamation work should take place within the 
stream reservation, declared as a reservation area.
 ( b) The minimum area of reservation relevant as per the attached list, should be maintained in the ﬁ eld.
 ( c) It is necessary obtain the permission of the Provincial Direc tor of Irrigation to build a boundary wall or a 
fence within thereservation, and such approval for a particular  place would be granted subject to signing a 
‘no - compensation’ agreement. In this, the approval will be gr anted based on the observations made by the 
District irrigation Engineers on the ﬁ eld reports (A specimen of the ‘no-compensation’ agreement is attached as 
attachement 1). 
Reservation areas for Reservoirs and Lagoons:-
The reservation area of a reservoir or lagoon, is generally determined as the wetting area of purview covered by a point 0.5 
meters high on the perpendicular line of High Flood Level-HFL. This area however, should not exceed a length of 6.5 meters 
from the reservoir / lagoon bund. Despite that position however , any construction taking place beyond the said area could 
be subject to the following conditions.
 (i) Since the water table in the area, in proximity to a reser voir or lagoon, is at a higher (upper) level, a proposal for 
the disposal of wasterwater resulting from any commercial construction or operation should have been submitted 
to the Central Environmental Authority and approval obtained prior to such construction. Under no circumstance 
should such wastewater be permitted to enter, directly or indirectly, into the reservoir or lagoon.
