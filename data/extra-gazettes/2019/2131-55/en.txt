THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
One (1) allotment of land in total extent of about 0.4925 Hectare
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. BA/ACQ/LNG/2016/256 of
26.12.2012 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
Madawela East in Millabedde Grama Niladhari Division in the
Division of Lunugala Divisional Secretariat in the Badulla District
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Hoppton Watta – Extent: 0.4925 Hectare
North: Hopton Watta claimed by State and Hopton Watta claimed
by M. Yoganadan; East: Hopton Watta claimed by M. Yoganadan
and D. M. Karunarathna; South: Hopton Watta claimed by D. M.
Karunarathna, M. Yoganadan and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road
(R.D.A.), Hopton Watta claimed by Estate and Road.
