RULES   AND   REGULATIONS   OF   THE   SOUTHERN   PROVINCE   AGRICULTURAL   PRECEPT
Directives
REGISTRATION OF V OLUNTARY AGRICULTURAL D EVELOPMENT  O RGANIZATION UNDER THE S OUTHERN P ROVINCIAL  D EPARTMENT
OF AGRICULTURE
Conditions  :–
1. It should be an organization formed within the Southern Province for a specific agricultural purpose (agricultural
productions that are acceptable within the Southern Province Agricultural Department such as organic
productions, Value added products, produce made under Good Agricultural Practices (GAP), Production of
Agricultural Seeds and Planting, Bee Keeping, Mushroom Cultivation or Flowericulture).
2. Such Agricultural organizations should have been permanently formed and have been active within the six months
preceding.
3. The voluntary agricultural organization should agree to work under the instructions of the Agricultural Instructor
of the area.
4. Should bide by the conditions imposed currently and in the future on V oluntary Agricultural Organization  by the
Southern Provincial Council.
5. Should conduct its activities within an annual plan of the organization.
6. All activities of the V oluntary Agricultural Organization should be conducted under the supervision of the
Agricultural Instructor of the area.
7. The voluntary agricultural organization should hold its meetings of the membership within a specific time
interval and should maintain its records.
8. While the time interval above should be a minimum of 2 months the members should meet at least once every
month.
9. To register, the V oluntary Agricultural Organization should submit six reports of such meetings.
10. The V oluntary Agricultural Organization should adopt the constitutions made available by the Southern Provincial
Council.
11. While the minimum number of members of the V oluntary Agricultural Organization should be 10 members the
maximum number of members should not exceed 25.
12. Such V oluntary Agricultural Organization are permitted to work, either within, the Grama Niladhari Division,
Agricultural Department’s area of purview, Divisional Secretariat area or the area of purview of the office of
Assistant Director of Agriculture.
Registration
1. Should apply for registration with a formal application form.
2. The registration applications can be obtained on the payment of a specific fee from, the extension offices
located in the Agrarian Service Centres, offices of the Assistant Directors of Agriculture and from the offices
of the Deputy Director of Agriculture.

REGISTRATION   OF  ORGANIC  FERTILIZER   MANUFACTURERS   UNDER   THE  SOUTHERN
PROVINCE  AGRICULTURE  DEPARTMENT
Conditions  :
1. Should be a permanent resident of the Southern Province.
2. The organic manufacturing plant should be located within the Southern Province.
3. The organic fertilzer manufacturing process should be subject to the supervision of the Provincial Director of
Agriculture or an officer of the Southern Province Department of Agriculture empowered by the Provincial
Director of Agriculture, in periodic intervals.
4. The manufacturer should provide samples of the organic fertilizer produced to the relevant authorized officer
appointed to inspect the quality of the fertilizer whenever such requirement to inspect arises.
5. The manufacturer should ensure that the quality standards mandatory for the fertilizer produced should be
maintained at all times.
6. In the event where different organic fertilizer manufacturers have been subject to a categorization by the Southern
Province Department of Agriculutre, the manufacturer should submit to such categorization.
7. If the organic fertilizer manufactured is being sold in the market, the fertilizer should be in a regularised contianer
(bag) approved by the Southern Province Agricultural Department.
8. An organic fertilizer manufacturer who manufacturers fertilizer as above will be entitled to sell his fertilizer at
the Ruhunu Rasara Trade Organization after having obtained the necessary registration.
Registration  :
a. It is possible to register or to renew the existing registration by duly completing an application form provided
by the Department of Agriculture of the Southern Province and handing over such application to the Extention
office of the area.
b. The manufacturer should agree to  pay the registration fee at the time of registration.
c. The manufacturer should be in agreement with the full registration process of organic fertilizer manufacturers
of the Department of Agriculture of the Southern Provincial Council.
d. The Southern Province Department of Agriculture will issue a registration number at the point of registration to
every manufacturer and that number, thereafter, should be mentioned by every manufacturer in their
correspondence with the department.
e. The registration, so obtained, is valid only for one calendar year and at the end of that year the registration has
to be renewed by paying the required fee.
f. The year, as described above, should mean an year commencing on the 1st January to the 31st of December
every year. In each year the registration of organic fertilizer manufacturers or the renewal of their agreement
will take place betweeen the  1st January to 31st March of the relevant year.
g. A manufacturer seeking a new registration should have been engaged in the manufaturing process for a period of
at least 6 months and accordingly, he should be prepared to provide proof of such engagement when required.
