The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PANDUWASNUWARA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Yapa
Mudiyanselage Somarathne the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Panambara Mudiyanselage Kumarasena Jayalath to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the  Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Panambara Mudiyanselage Kumarasena Jayalath was selected as the member of the Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya
Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Panduwasnuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
18th September, 2019.
