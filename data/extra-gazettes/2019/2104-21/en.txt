Notice under Section 7
My No.: KDS/KGK/4/9/2017/01/LA.
Land Min's Ref. No.: 4-3/11/2016/UD/521.
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the land described below for a public purpose.
Description of  the land to be acquired :
Allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0155 Hectare
depicted as Lot No. 01, 02 and 04 in below names and extents
in the Preliminary Plan No. uy 6891 dated 10.12.2018 prepared
by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys - Kandy District on
behalf of the Surveyor General situated in the village called
Bahirawakanda Division No. 18, Katukele in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. 255 of Katukele of Gangawata Korale
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Four
Gravets Kandy and Gangawata Korale in Kandy District of
the Central Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Assessment No. 187, Sirimavo
Bandaranaike Mawatha; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
P . H. R. G . Jinasena, No. 47, Sri Dhammasidhdhi Mawatha,
Asgiriya, Kandy; Extent: 0.0115 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the land: Assessment No. 247/4, S.W.R.D.
Bandaranaike Mawatha; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
C. K. J. S. Karunarathne and others, No. 274/4, S. W. R. D.
Bandaranaike Mawatha, Kandy; Extent: 0.0037 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 04
Name of the land: –; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not
Known; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing
before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Four Gravets, Kandy
and Gangawata Korale on 20.02.2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on
or before the 13.02.2019 the nature of their interests in the
land/lands, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
B. M. P. G. P. V. BANDARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Four Gravets Kandy and Gangawata Korale.
At the four Gravets, Kandy and Gangawata Korale,
27th December, 2018.
