PART I : SECTION (I) -- GENERAL
Government  Notifications
FOREST  CONSERV ATION  ORDINANCE
THE order under section 3. that has to be read with Section 64 (1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), as No
13 of 1966, as amended by Acts, No 56 of 1979, No 13 of 1982, No 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009 that have to
be read with Section 51 of the 19th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
The Order
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 3. of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) as amended by Acts,
No. 13 of 1966, No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, that have to be read with
Section 51 of the 19
th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I Maithripala Sirisena,
Precedent do by this order declare the Buruthagoda Forest of 392.3719 hectares bounded by the limit set forth in the
schedule hereto as Buruthagoda Reserved Forest from 19th day of March 2019.
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
19th March,  2019.
