NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Authorization
I, do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the North Western Provincial Council
to spend a sum of Rupees Fourteen Billion Two Hundred Fourteen Million and Four Hundred  Ninety Thousand only
(Rs. 14,214,490,000) out of the North Western Provincial Council Fund, which is estimated for the expense of the North
Western Provincial Council for the period that starts on 01st of January, 2019 and end on 30th of April, 2019, by virtue of
power vested in me under Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987 which was amended by Act No. 28
of 1990 as the term of the 06th North Western Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
ended on 10th October 2018.
This Permission will be valid till 30th April, 2019 or up to the date that expires three months (3) from the date
summoning the 07th North Western Provincial Council or from the re-start of the functions of North Western Provincial
Council which occurs first.
As the details regarding treasury grants and revenue targets for the year 2019, which are necessary to prepare
annual financial statement were not received to the Provincial Council, this estimate has been prepared by nature of a vote
on account. This permission is limited to spend up to the maximum revenue collected and imprests for treasury grants to
be received during first four months in the year 2019 expecting that the grants and revenue targets will be given by the
treasury in due course.
K. C. L
OGESWARAN,
Governor,
North Western Province.

VOTE   ON   ACCOUNT   No.   01   OF   2018
A V ote on Account to provide for the service of the first 04 months of the financial year 2019 ; to authorize the raising of
loans in or outside Sri Lanka, for the purpose of such service ; to make financial provision in respect of certain activities
of the North Western Provincial Council during that period ; to enable the payment by way of advances out of the Provincial
Council fund or any other fund or moneys, of or at the disposal of the North Western Provincial Council, of moneys
required during the first 04 months of that financial year for expenditure on such activities ; to provide for the refund of
such moneys to Provincial Council fund and to make provision for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Be it enacted by the Governor of the North Western Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka as follows :-
1. This V ote on account may be cited as the V ote on Account, No. 01 of 2018 of the North
Western Provincial Council
2. (1) Without prejudice to any other law authorizing any expenditure and subject to the
provisions of this section, the expenditure of the North Western Provincial Council which
is estimated will be rupees fourteen thousand two hundered and fourteen million four
hundred and ninety thousand for the service of the period beginning on January 01, 2019
and ending on April 30, 2019 shall be met -
(a) from payments which are hereby authorized to be made out of the North Western
Provincial Council fund or any other fund or moneys of or at the disposal of the
Provincial Council ; and
(b) from borrowings made in the financial year 2019 which are hereby authorized in
terms of relevant laws for moneys to be raised whether in or outside Sri Lanka, for
and on behalf of the North Western Provincial Council.
