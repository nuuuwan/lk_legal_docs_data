I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose: Acquisition of land for an access
road to 5th Military Intelligence Corps of Sri Lanka Army.
An allotment of  Land in total extent of 0.1685 Hec. depicted
as Lots 01 and 02 in the Preliminary Plan No. fld 9849 prepared
by the Surveyor General, situated in the village called
Kaluaggala in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 436A of
Suduwella of Udugahapattuwa Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Seethawaka (Hanwella) in
Colombo District of the Western Province and morefully
described as follows.
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Unapandure Owita alias Attikkagha
Owita alias Unapandure Owita and Attikkagaha Owita at
present Unapandure Waththa: Description: Home garden;
Claimant: Kathriarachchige Don Fransis Lambert No. 120,
Pahala Kaluaggala, Hanwella; Total Extent: 0.1663 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Could not be traced: Description: Home
garden; Claimant: Could not be traced; Total Extent: 0.0022
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Seethawaka Divisional Secretariat
on 19.02.2020 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 12.02.2020 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
