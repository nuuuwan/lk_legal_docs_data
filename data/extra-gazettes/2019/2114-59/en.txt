The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(1a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in  Maharagama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  Independent Group No. 02, to which Chamidu Lakshan Ranasinghe, the member
appointed to Ward No. 02, Mirihana North of Maharagama Urban Council  belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) (a)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 02, Mirihana North of Maharagama
Urban Council who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under
section 8 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected  as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Wanni Arachchige Don Amarapala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of Maharagama Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Wanni Arachchige Don Amarapala was elected as the member of the Ward No. 02 Mirihana North of Maharagama
Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
15th March, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(1a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member in  Maharagama Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  Independent Group No. 02, to which Lalith Gunawardhana, the member elected
to Ward No. 16, Kottawa Town of Maharagama Urban Council  belonged, was required by me under Section 66A (1) (a)  of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ward No. 16, Kottawa Town of Maharagama Urban Council
who belongs to such independent group and is not disqualified under Section 9 and is qualified under section 8 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be declared elected  as a member within a period of
thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of  aforesaid Independent Group No. 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Ranpathiranage Gnanasiri Dayananda Weerakoon to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer  of  Maharagama Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(1a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby
declare that Ranpathiranage Gnanasiri Dayananda Weerakoon was elected as the member of the Ward No. 16 Kottawa
Town of Maharagama Urban Council.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo,
15th March, 2019.
