FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VALIKAMAM  EAST
PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Arumugam Nadesu Rajenthiran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that Ward 01 - Valalai if Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Rasu Kugatheesan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of  the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Selection 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Rasu Kugatheesan was elected as the member of the Ward 01 - Valalai of Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
11th April, 2019.
