FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   ADDALACHCHENAI
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Addalachchenai pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the National Congress, the recognized political party to which Meerasaivu
Muhammadu Jahfer the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  National Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9
of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Meerasaivu Muhammadu Niyaal to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of  Addalachchenai  pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Meerasaivu Muhammadu  Niyaal was selected as the member of the Addalachchenai  pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Addalachchenai  pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
23rd May, 2019.
