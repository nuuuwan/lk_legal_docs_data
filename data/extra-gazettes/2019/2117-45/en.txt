ORDER   UNDER   SECTIOIN  10(1)(a)  OF  THE  HOLIDAYS   ACT,   No.  29  OF  1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10(1)(a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and Provincial Councils & Local Government, do by this Order declare Monday,
15th of April, 2019 a Public Holiday on account of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year 2019.
WAJIRA ABEYWARDANE,
Minister of Internal & Home Affairs and
Provincial Councils & Local Government.
