SPORTS   ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sports under Section 41 read with Section 31 of the Sports Act No. 25 of 1973 :-
HARIN F ERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports
Colombo,
31st July, 2019.
Regulations
The Regulations No. 01 of 2016 pertaining to National Sports Associations published in  Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1990/23 dated 27.10.2016, Regulations, No. 2006/13 dated 13.02.2017, Regulations, No. 2017/41 dated 05.05.2017,
Regulations, No. 2089/56 dated 22.09.2018, Regulations, No. 2093/39 dated 18.10.2018 and the Regulations, No. 2105/38
dated 10.01.2019 are again  amended as follows :
1. By repealing the Regulation 8(2) in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2089/56 dated 22.09.2018 and substituting
the following Regulation :-
‘8(2) After having considered an appeal in regard to a decision given or action taken by a National Sports
Association Registered by the Minister in charge of the subject of Sports or having considered a decision
given by the National Sports Association Election Committee such an appeal shall be submitted to a three-
member Appeals Advisory Committee comprising of retired judges of the Supreme Court or retired Senior
Public officers and the Appeals Advisory Committee shall notify the Minister in charge of the subject of
Sports of its decision in writing within 07 days of the receipt of the said appeal.
