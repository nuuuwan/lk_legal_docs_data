THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare under the sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, the land described below is required for
public purpose and it shall be acquired under the provisions of the
Land Acquisition Act.
The boundaries of the block of land of with following names and
extents, indicated in detail in the progression tracing as bearing number
B/ACQ/BDL/2016/159 dated 27.09.2016 prepared by the Surveyor
General which is situated in the Village of Hanwella (F.V .P. 80) coming
under Hindagoda (78 K) Grama Niladhari Division in Rilpola Korale
Minor Division of Badulla Divisional Secretary Division in Badulla
District in the Province of Uva:
01. Lot "A"  – Othalawa Middle Othalawa Pattiyakumbura –
about 0.6801 Hectares in extent
North : Othalawa Lower Othalawa claimed by N.G . Sumanasena
and N.G . Upasena, G .P.I. Weerasooriya and G .P.K. Weerasooriya
and Rambukpotha oya; East and South : Rambukpotha Oya and
Pattiyakubumbura; West : Pattiyakumbura, Palleothalawa claimed
by N.G . Sumanasena and N.G . Upasena and Othalawa Lower
Othalawa claimed by G .P.I. Weerasooriya and G .P.K. Weerasooriya.
