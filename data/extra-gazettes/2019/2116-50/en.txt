PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 28.03.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude described below in Paragraph
1 is required for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act. The land over which the said servitude is to
be acquired is given in Paragraph 2.
1. Description of the Servitude.– Servitude to be obtained for
construction of access road to Booster Pump House.
2. Description of the Land.– An allotment of land depicted in
the Advance Tracing No. CO/KTT/2017/315 dated 30.08.2017,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of  Welikada
in the Grama NIladhari Division of 514A, Welikada West in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte in
Colombo District, Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Extent: About 0.0198 Hectare
North: Land bearing Assmt. Nos. 219/1, 225/2, 225/3, 225 and
223 of Rajagiriya Road; East: Land bearing Assmt. Nos. 225/2,
225/3, 225 and 223 of Rajagiriya Road and Rajagiriya Road; South:
Land bearing Assmt. No. 215 of Rajagiriya Road; West: Land bearing
Assmt. Nos. 215 and 219/1 of Rajagiriya Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
