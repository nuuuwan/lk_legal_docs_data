THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
 My Reference No. : POL/HGD/LND/13/56/005.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: 4-3/9/216/PRD/453.
HA VING been duly directed by the Hon. Minister of  Lands
and Parliamentary Reforms under the SubSection One of the
Section Two of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, I hereby
give notice to you as required by the SubSection Two of the
said Section that the land morefully describe below is required
for a public purpose :
Public  purpose means : To acquire portions of land to provide
a filling station to the Minneriya area.
Description of the area above referred to :- The allotment
of  land called Patapilikanda Kele to contain 00736 Hect.,
depicted as Lot Alphabet A in the Advanced Tracing No.
PO/ HKD/2019/127 dated 21/8/2019 prepared by the Surveyor
General, located in the village of Minneriya in the Sinhala
Pattuwa Minor Division in the Hingurakgoda Divisional
Secretary's Division in the District of Polonnaruwa in the North
Central Province.
G31632 - 17 (2019/12)
Boundaries :
On the North by: Lot No. 4 and 7; On the East by: Lot No. 7;
On the South by: Lot 7 and 194; On the West by: Lot No.
194,161 and 4.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are required  to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing
before me at the Hingurakgoda Divisional Secretariat on 30th
November 2019.
It should by notified to me in document in duplicate the
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for computation for the land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount, on or before 20th November, 2019.
R.M.D.P.PUSHPAKUMARI,
Divisional Secretary,
Hingurakgoda.
