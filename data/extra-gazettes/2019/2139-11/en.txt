 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2113/62 of March 08, 2019), which specifies the Faculties and
Departments of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of section 139 of aforesaid
Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substituion for the item “Faculty of Medicine”
appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column II thereof, of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Medicine 1. Department of Anatomy
2. Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology
3. Department of Community Medicine
4. Department of Forensic Medicine and Toxicology
5. Department of Clinical Medicine
6. Department of Microbiology
7. Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology
8. Department of Paediatrics
