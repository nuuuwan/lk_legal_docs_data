FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KATTANKUDY   URBAN
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kattankudy Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Muslim National Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mukammathu Farook Mukammathu Sibli the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of Muslim National Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ahamathulebbe Ali Akbar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of  the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Ahamathulebbe Ali
Akbar was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
04th October, 2019.
