FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER   PARAGRAPH  (13)(b)  OF ARTILCE  99  OF  THE
CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST  REPULIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary General of Parliament has informed the Election Commission under Section 64 (1) of the
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1  of  1981, as amended by the Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the
membership of  the Eighth Parliament by reason of   death of  Mr. Thirimadura Ranjith De Zoysa,  who was declared
elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District No. 21 - Rathnapura under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act ;
AND WHEREAS the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by
the Electioin Commission under Section 64(1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under Pararaph
(13)(b) of Article 99 of the Constitution ;
AND WHEREAS the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return in
Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Udagama Liyanage Waruna Priyantha
Liyanage was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the said electoral distict ;
NOW THEREFORE , we the Election Commission, in the exercise of the powers vested in the Election Commission
by Section 64 (2) of  the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1  of  1981, which shall be read with the provisions of the
Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby notify  the
general public that Mr. Udagama Liyanage Waruna Priyantha Liyanage, has been declared elected as a Member of the
Eighth Parliament for the Electoral District No. 21 - Rathnapura to fill  the vacancy caused by reason of death of
Mr. Thirimadura Ranjith De Zoysa.
 
MAHINDA D ESHAPRIYA N. J. ABEYSEKERE S. RATNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE
