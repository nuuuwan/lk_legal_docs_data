PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 06.06.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that servitude of the land described below in
Paragraph 1 is required for a public purpose and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act. The land over which the said
servitude is to be acquired is given in Paragraph 2.
1. Description of the Servitude .– For Access Road to Tenne-
kumbura Service Reservoir of the Kundasale - Haragama Water Supply
Project.
2. Description of the Land .– The allotments of land called
Semaneriya Estate and Tennekumbura Estate depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. Maha/MHN/2017/262 and dated 14.09.2017, prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Villages of Semaneriya Watta
and Thannekumbura in the Grama Niladhari Divisions No. 269
Ampitiya North and No. 275 Thennekumbura in the Minor Division
of Gangawata Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Divisions of
Kadawathsathara and Gangawata Korale in Kandy District, Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Semaneriya Estate – Extent: 0.0116 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Semeneriya Estate claimed
by D. M. Jayathilaka; East: Semeneriya Estate claimed by D. M.
Jayathilaka, Tennekumbura Estate claimed by Creditline Financial
Services (Pvt.) Ltd. and Lot B; South: Semeneriya Estate claimed by
A. M. Wijitha Bandara; West: Semeneriya Estate claimed by A. M.
Wijitha Bandara and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Semaneriya Estate – Extent: 0.0192 Hectare
North: Lot A, Semeneriya Estate claimed by D. M. Jayathilaka
and Thennekumbura Estate claimed by Creditline Financial
Services (Pvt.) Ltd.; East and South: Thennekumbura Estate
claimed by Creditline Financial Services (Pvt.) Ltd.; West:
Thennekumbura Estate claimed by Creditline Financial
Services (Pvt.) Ltd. and Semeneriya Estate claimed by A. M. Wijitha
Bandara.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
