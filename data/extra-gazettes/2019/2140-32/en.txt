The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V A VUNIYA   NORTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Thirunavukkarasu Vijikaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon returned  member if Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as  member a within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kanagar Kanapathippillai to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Kanagar Kanapathippillai was selected as the member of the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G. L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
09th September, 2019.
