THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16 OF  1978
Order under Section 20 (4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 20(4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 6 of 1978, I , Bandula Gunawardana,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation, do by this Order -
(a) On being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri
Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said University have failed to restore
normal conditions, appoint Emeritus Professor Weerakoon Mudiyanselage Abeyrathna Bandara to be the
Competent Authority with effect from December, 13, 2019 for the purpose of exercising, performing and
discharging in lieu of the Vice-Chancellor, University of the Visual and Performing Arts, Sri Lanka, any power,
duty or function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Vice-Chancellor
under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder ; and
(b) rescind the Order made under Section 20(4) (b) and published in the Governmet Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2142/25 of September 24, 2019.
 B
ANDULA G UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Higher Education, Technology and Innovation
Colombo,
13th December, 2019.
