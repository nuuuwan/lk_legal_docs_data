1A   G   29435—27  (02/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   KATTANKUDY  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Kattankudy Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the National Front For Good Governance, the recognized political party to
which  Muhammathu Buhari Muhammathu Firdawsi the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon  returned  member if National Front For Good Governance who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as
a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Abdullatheef Mohammadu Sabeel to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Rajendra Saseelan, Returning Officer of the Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in terms of
Selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Abdullatheef
Mohammadu Sabeel was selected as the member of the Kattankudy Urban Council.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
11th February, 2019,
