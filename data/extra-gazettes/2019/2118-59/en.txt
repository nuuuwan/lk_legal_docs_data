THE  FAUNA  AND   FLORA  PROTECTION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  469)
Order under Subsection (2) of Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of Section 2 of the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance (Chapter
469), I, John Amarathunga, Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife and Christian Religious Affairs, do by this Order
declare the area of land described in the Schedule hereto to be a Sanctuary for the purposes of the aforesaid Ordinance
and further declare that such Sanctuary shall be called and known as the “Kayankerni Marine Sanctuary”.
J
OHN AMARATHUNGA ,
Minister of Tourism Development, Wildlife
and Christian Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
11th April, 2019.
