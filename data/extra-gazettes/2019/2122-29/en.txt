Regulation No. 01 - Determination of fuel limits for official visits
1. Determination of fuel limits for the Mayors of the Municipal Councils with regard to the official visits of the Municipal
Councils.
I. If the Municipal Council vehicles are used an amount of diesel not exceeding 400 Litres per month or
II. In case of Municipal Council vehicles is not available, under the expenditure limit for the above fuel expenditure
can be incurred with the council fund for the private vehicle with the approval of the Minister in charge
Regulation No. 02 - Combined allowances
1. A combined allowance not exceeding Rupees 1000/= for the full day can be paid for the Mayors of the Municipal Councils
subject to the provisions of the establishment code, this allowance can be obtained subject to maximum of only 10 days per
a month.
2. The combined allowance will be paid only for official visits outside the Municipal Council area. This allowance should be
made in accordance with the provisions of the establishment code.
3. This allowance will not be paid for the visits made within the Municipal Council limit.
4. The above mentioned travelling and combined allowances can be obtained only for the official visits regard to the relevant
Municipal Council. Except in case of receiving an official invitation for a ceremony , conference or a meeting pertaining to the
functions of the Municipal Council, not entitled to obtain travelling or combined allowance from the Municipal Council
fund for travelling to participate in a conference or a meeting.
Regulation No. 03 - Telephone allowances
1. Permission is granted for Mayors and Deputy Mayors to obtain an allowance from the council fund as mentioned below for
telephones.
I. For the Mayor monthly - Rs. 7,500/=
II. For the Deputy Mayor monthly - Rs. 5,000/=
III For the Municipal Counselor monthly - Rs. 3,500/=
Regulation No. 04 - Allowance for participating in committees
1. This regulation is cited as the regulation to pay the participation allowances for the committee members appointed as per
the section 26(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance (Article 252).
2. An expenditure can be incurred for an allowance of Rs. 500/= from the council fund for the committee members to participate
in one committee.
3. The following points and conditions should be cosidered in paying the participation allowance for the committee members.
I. The committee referred herein is a formal committee appointed under section 26(1) of the Municipal Council
Ordinance (Article 252).
II. The participation should be confirmed through an attendance register of the committee members to obtain the
participation allowance for each committee.
When making the above payments, by considering the financial status of the Municipal Council Fund and the allocation
of the financial statement a specific percentage should be allocated based on the approval of the general council.

Council Members in terms of the powers vested in me under section 289 of the chapter 252 of Municipal Council Ordinance,

Regulation No. 01 - Monthly allowance paid to people's representatives
1. This regulations is cited as the regulations to pay allowance for the Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Members of the Municipal
Councils of Uva Province.
2. A monthly allowance should be paid according to the following rates from the 01st of November 2014 f or the Mayors,
Deputy Mayors and the Members elected for the Municipal Councils of Uva Province.
Mayor - Rs. 30000.00
Deputy Mayor - Rs. 25000.00
Municipal Council Member - Rs. 20000.00
3. (1) In the following instances a Mayor, Deputy Mayor or a Municipal Council Member shall not be entitled for the above
allowances
(i) Not attending the Council meetings and standing committees meeting held on the months relevant to payment of
allowances.
(ii) In detention or in imprisonment in connection with any criminal charge.
(iii)Except on medical grounds, on leave without the proper consent of the Council.
(iv)Service of a Mayor, Deputy Mayor or Municipal Council Member has been suspended in accordance with the
statue No. 2 of 2011 relevant to the authority to supervise the administration of Uva Provincial Local Government
institutions of the Provincial Council of Uva Province.
However, in case if the time period in detention, imprisonment or on leave or the period of suspended service
is part of a particular month, payments for remaining period of that month can be paid by calculating the proportional
days.
(2) Notwithstanding anything in paragraph 3 (I),
A Mayor or Deputy Mayor or a Municipal Council Member should not be deprived of his/her allowances on the
grounds of not attending the council meeting or standing committee meeting in instances he/she in official duties
with the proper consent of the council.
(3) According to clause 13 of article 252 of the Municipal Council Act, in case of rejection of the post of any Member,
resign from the post, vacant of post and when the Council is dissolved, if it is the day before the last day of the month,
the allowances for the Members should be paid by adjusting the proportionate days of that month.
