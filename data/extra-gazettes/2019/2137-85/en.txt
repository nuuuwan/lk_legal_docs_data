SPORTS   ACT,   25  OF  1973
REGULATIONS made by Minister of Sports under Section 41 read with Section 31 of Sport Law 25 of 1973 and its
amendments.
HARIN F ERNANDO,
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
21st August, 2019.
Regulations
The National Associations of Sports Regulations, No. 01 of 2016 published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1990/23 dated 27.10.2016, No. 2006/13 dated 13.02.2017, No. 2017/41 dated 05.05.2017, No. 2089/56 dated
22.09.2018, No. 2093/39 dated 18.10.2018 and No. 2105/38 dated 10.01.2019 are hereby amended as follows :
1. In regulation 5(4) of Sports Regulation published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1990/23 dated 27.10.2016 :-
by the deletion of Sub section 5(4)(n) therefore and the substitution of the following Sub section :-
“(n) he or any member of his immediate family (being a spouse, parent, sibling, son or daughter or son-
in-law or daugther-in-law) has an interest in betting, gaming or wagering, or in a betting, gaming or
wagering organization or is employed in the day to day operations of such organization or organizations.”
