EXCISE   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13  OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, as amended, I,
Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that, the  Excise Duty charged, levied and collected on
motor vehicles imported during the period of January 2015 to November 2017 specified in Column I of the Schedule I hereto
shall be payable at the rate specified in Column II of the Schedule I wherever applicable with effect from April 05, 2019.
Any importer/individual who is fulfilling the criteria specified in the Schedule I to this Order and willing to
release his/her motor vehicles, on payment of such special duty rate should submit his/her application as specified in the
Schedule II to this Order to the Director General, Department of Fiscal Policy of the General Treasury on or before
August 05, 2019.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01.
March 29, 2019.

SCHEDULE   I
 Description Excise Duty
II I
1. Motor vehicles, which were imported during the period of January 2015 to
November 2017 and pending for Customs clearance at the Port of Sri Lanka
(Hambantota) shall be released to the importer(s) subject to fulfilling the
following criteria :
(i) Said motor vehicles imported by an importer / individual based on the
Letter(s) of Credit established on his/her name for personal usage or
(ii) Said motor vehicles imported by any authorized agent / motor vehicles
importer on the request of identified customer(s), who have paid
required money to the said authorized agent / importer through any
official channel(s) (financial institutions approved by the Central Bank
of Sri Lanka) for importation of such vehicle(s) for personal usage.
2. Any importer or individual, fulfilling criteria listed above, shall submit his /
her application as specified in the Schedule II of this Gazette Notification, to
the Director General, Department of Fiscal Policy of the General Treasury
along with all official / duly certified copies of evidence for authorizing
clearance of such motor vehicle(s) from the Sri Lanka Customs on payment
of Excise duty specified in Column II of Schedule I within four (04) months
period from the date of publication of this Gazette Notification.
3. All motor vehicles, cleared from the Sri Lanka Customs under the proposed
concession shall be registered in the name of the individuals referred in I
above within 30 days from the date of release of such motor vehicles from
the Sri Lanka Customs. Any motor vehicle, not be registered within 30 days
from the date of release of Sri Lanka Customs, shall be liable for payment of
applicable excise duty at the time of release of such motor vehicle from the
Sri Lanka Customs
