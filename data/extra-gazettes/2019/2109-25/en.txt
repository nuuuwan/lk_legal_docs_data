Notice under Section 7
My Reference  No.: EDS/05/05/14/2012/1.
L.M. Reference No.: 4/3/15/2012/VE/173.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired :
Land containing in extent of about 9.1057 Hectares in
Rambukkandawatta, Gangulwitiyawatta, Dawatagahadeniyahena
situated in the Village of Gangulwitiyagama in the (183 C)
Grama Niladhari Division of Gangulwitiya in Palle Paththuwa
in Nawadun Korale, Divisional Secretary's Division of
Elapatha of Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
Lot No. 108 – Name of the Land : Rambukkandawatta,
Gangulwitiyawatta, Dawatagahadeniyahena.
Boundaries:
North: Lot No. 19, Road (Pradeshiya Sabha), Lot No. 73
and Lot No. 18; East: Lot No. 19 and Lot No. Gangulwitiya
Dola; South: Lot No. 19 and Lot No. Gangulwitiya Dola; W est:
Lot No. 19 and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) Extent: 6.6163
Hectares.
Lot No. 109 – Name of the Land: Rambukkandawatta,
Gangulwitiyawatta, Dawatagahadeniyahena.
Boundaries:
North: Dola, Road (Pradeshiya Sabha), Lot No. 56 and Lot
No. 74; East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot No. 56; South:
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot No. 50; West: Lot No. 50
and Dola; Extent : 2.4894 Hectares.
Claimant : Government (has been acquired under the 38(A)
interim Order of Land Acquisition Act); Previous Claimant:
Land Reforms Commission.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Office of Elapatha on the 02nd
day of May in 2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 25day of
April in 2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
the details of computation of such amount.
S. A. DILRUK,
Divisional Secretary,
Elapatha.
Divisional Secretariat Elapatha,
31day of January in 2019.
