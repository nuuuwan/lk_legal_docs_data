APPOINTMENT   OF   RELEV ANT   OFFICERS   FOR   THE   ADVISORY   COMMITTEE   UNDER   URBAN
DEVELOPMENT    AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.  41  OF   1978
I, do hereby appoint following officers for the Advisory Committee under Section 7(1), 7(2), 7(3) of the Urban
Development Authority Act, No. 41 of 1978, to carry out a comprehensive inquiry on the demands for housing facilities
with special needs and to provide appropriate recommendations, focusing special attention on the requisitions made by
the applicants who are resident within the areas of Urban Regeneration Project implemented by Urban Development
Authority under Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development.
