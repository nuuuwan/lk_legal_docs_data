THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : 2019/16.
  Land Ministry Ref. No.:4-3/6/2018/HW/70.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of  Lot No. 01 and 02 in  Total Extent of about
0.0098 Hectare, depicted in Preliminary  Plan No. Mara 2820
and dated 18.09.2019 prepared by the Senior - Superintendent
of Surveyor of  Matara District on behalf of the Surveyor
General, situated in the village of  Uda Aparekka in the Grama
Niladhari Divisions of  No. 448, Uda Aparekka in the  Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Devinuwara in Matara District in
Sourthern Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Magahena; Description: Garden; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant:  Prabath Senevirathna, Batadolawatta,
Uda Uparekka, Uparekka (Parts of T. P . 158227 and 158228.
Acquired under Section 38 (A) of  Land Acquisition Act, To
finalise the land acquisition); Extent: 0.0018 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Kirigodallahena; Description: Garden,
Contains a cultivation of  Cinnamon about 5-6 years old;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant:  Late of H. K. S. C.
Siriwardhana, Iskolahena, Uda Uparekka, Uparekka (Parts of
T. P . 158227 and 158228. Acquired under Section 38 (A) of
Land Acquisition Act., To finalise the land acquisition); Extent:
0.0080 Hectare.
