THE   NOTIFICATION  MADE   BY   THE   HON.   GOVERNOR   OF   NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE
Rules and Regulations issued in terms of Section 32(3) of Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987
I do hereby notify that the Rules and Regulations hereunder are issued in terms of Section 32(3) of Provincial Council Act
No. 42 of 1987 and will be take effect from 01.09.2019.
PESHALA J AYARA THNA B ANDARA,
Governor of North Western Province
26th August, 2019.
RULES  AND   REGULATIONS  FOR  SCHOOL   WORK   ASSISTANTS   OF  THE   SCHOOLS   IN   NORTH
WESTERN   PROVINCE
01. The working hours for School Work Assistants shall be from 7.00 a.m. to 3.45 p.m. Provided that they may leave
after closing of the school unless engaged in a special duty.
02. The short leave shall be 1 hour and 30 minutes from 7.00 a.m. to 8.30 a.m. or 2.15 p.m. to 3.45p.m., before lunch
or after lunch, in a manner which does not affect duty.
03. Half day leave shall be 04 hours up to 11.00 a.m. or from 11.45.a.m.
04. Establishment Code and the circular concerned shall be applicable for other leave.
05. The principal must call for another female employee or a lady teacher in case of employing a female School Work
