SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order  under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby make an order to waive
off :
(i) the balance amount after collecting Rs. 25 per kg. from the applied Special Commodity Levy of 10% or Rs. 100
per kg on the importation of fish, classified under the HS Heading of 03.02 and 03.03 (Other than the Mackerel
fish coming under HS Code 0303.54) listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinary
Gazette Notification No. 2143/3 of 30th September 2019.
and
(ii) Rs. 20 per kg from the applied Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 25 per kg on the imporation of Chillies (Dried,
neither crushed nor ground) classifiable under the HS Code of 0904.21.10 listed out in the Schedule of the Order
published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2132/13 of 18th July 2019.
In terms of this Order, a Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 25 per kg on imporation of fish (HS Heading of 03.02 and 03.03,
other than the Mackerel fish coming under HS Code 0303.54) and Rs. 5 per kg on importation of Chillies (Dried, neither
crushed nor ground/HS Code 0904.21.10) shall be collected for a period of one month commencing from October 29,
2019.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
28th October, 2019.
