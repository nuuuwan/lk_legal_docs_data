PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
NORTH   CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Hon.  Governor of North Central  Provincial Council
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Marappulige
Jayasinghe, the Governor of the North Central  Province do hereby notify that the officers, names mentioned below have
been appointed for the posts North Central Provincial Council Services.
1. Mr. H. M. Thilaka Bandara, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service  as the Secretary of
the Chief Ministry of North  Central Province  with effect  from 2018.07.11 until further an order is made.
2. Mr. W. A. R. Wimalasiri, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service (retired)  as the
Secretary of the Provincial Public Service Commission  of  North  Central Province  for 03 months with effect
from 2018.12.11 to 2019.03.10 on contract basic.
3. Mr. R. A. S. Jayathilake, Officer in Special Grade of Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service for acting as the
Secretary (Personnel and Training) of the Chief Ministry of  North  Central Province in addition to the duties of
the permanent post with   effect  from 2018.07.06 to 2018.10.22 .
4. Mrs. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa, Officer in  Grade I of Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service (Retired)  as the  Deputy
Secretary (Admin.) of  the Chief  Ministry of   North  Central Province for three months  with effect  from
2018.10.12 untill 2019.01.11 on contract basic.
5. Mrs. G . K. S. Chandralatha, Officer in Special  Grade of  Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service  as the  Deputy Secretary
(Personnel and Training) of  the Chief  Ministry of   North  Central Province with  effect  from 2018.10.23 untill
further order is made.
6. Mr. P. K. Jayalath, Officer in Grade  I of Sri  Lanka  Administrative  Service for acting as the  the Secretary of the
Ministry of  Co-operative of  North Central  Provincial Council  with effect  from 2018.11.21 to 2018.12.27 in
addition to the duties of his permanent post.
