NATIONAL WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD, ACT, No. 2 OF 1974
Order under Section 92
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, under Section 92 of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, No. 2 of 1974,
I, Rauff Hakeem, the Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education,  hereby approve the  acquisition of the
immovable property, described in the following schedule as it is required by the National Water Supply and Drainage Board,
for the construction of Training Centre, Hostel and Circuit Bungalow under Kandy North - Pathadumbara Integrated Water
Supply Project.
