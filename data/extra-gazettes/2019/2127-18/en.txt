The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   GALIGAMUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
for council meeting for a period of three months  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Senadinennehelage Champa Kumari Senadheera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not
disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member
within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Danansooriya Arachchillage Mallika Danansooriya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sajith Wasantha Welgama, Returning Officer of the Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Danansooriya
Arachchillage Mallika Danansooriya was selected as the member of the Galigamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AJITH W ASANTHA  W ELGAMA ,
Returning Officer,
Galigamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kegalle,
11th June, 2019.
