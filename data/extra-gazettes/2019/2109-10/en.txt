PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 05.02.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ka/
MTG/17/813 and dated 07.02.2018 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveyor General, situated in the Village of
Walipanna in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Matugama of
Kalutara  District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mountpearl Watta – Extent: 3.6209  Hectares
North: Mountpearl Watta claimed by A. Jayanetthi, B. Jayanetthi,
Nihal Gunathilake and Susantha Sendanayaka, Mountpearl Watta
claimed by the Land Reform Commission and Mountpearl Watta of
which the claimant could not be traced; East: Mountpearl Watta
claimed by Susantha Sendanayaka, Mountpearl Watta claimed by
the Land Reform Commission and Mountpearl Watta of which the
claimant could not be traced; South: Mountpearl Watta claimed by
the Land Reform Commission, Lot C of this tracing ( Road); West:
Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land Reform Commission, Lot C
of this tracing ( Road) and Mountpearl Watta claimed by A. Jayanetthi,
B. Jayanetthi and Nihal Gunathilake.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Mountpearl Watta – Extent: 0.3884  Hectare
North: Mountpearl Watta claimed by R. A.Indika and Mountpearl
Watta claimed by the Land Reform Commission; East: Mountpearl
Watta claimed by R.A. Indika, Mountpearl Watta claimed by the
Land Reform Commission and Lot C of this tracing ( Road); South:
Lot C of this tracing ( Road), Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land
Reform Commission and Road; West: Mountpearl Watta claimed by
the Land Reform Commission and Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Mountpearl Watta – Extent: 0.0376 Hectare
North: Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land Reform Commission
and Lots B and A of this tracing; East: Lot A of this tracing and
Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land Reform Commission; South:
Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land Reform Commission and
Road; West: Road, Mountpearl Watta claimed by the Land Reform
Commission and Lot B of this tracing.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
