EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   ATTANAGALLA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Wepola Pathirannehelage Gihan Chinthaka the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a)  of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that No. - 07 Magalegoda of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall
be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Rupasinghe Arachchillage Neel Pradeep Rupasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Attanagalla
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Rupasinghe Arachchillage Neel Pradeep Rupasinghe was elected as the member of the No. - 07
Magalegoda of Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
26th August, 2019.
