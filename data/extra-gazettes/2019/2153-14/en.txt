EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Sathasivam Ponnuththurai the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon the member of that No. 8 Wattakkachchi of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party and is
not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a
member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Anthonippillai Sivakumaran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer  of  the  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Anthonippillai Sivakumaran was elected as the member of the No. 8 Wattakkachchi of Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
10th December, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Pirabamany Kanesamoorthy the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon returned  member of that Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Lalithakumari Saththiyamoorthi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer of  the  Karachchi Pradeshsiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Lalithakumari Saththiyamoorthi was selected as the member of the  Karachchi Pradeshsiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
