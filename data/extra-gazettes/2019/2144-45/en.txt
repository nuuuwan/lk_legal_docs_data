FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MATALE   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Matale Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meeting of the council without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Konara
Mudiyanselage Gewaththe Gedara Chaminda Kumara Konara the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Palle Kankanamage Don Chandana to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Prathapasingha Arachchige Kumarini Dasanthi, Returning Officer  of  the  Matale Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Palle Kankanamage Don Chandana was selected as the member of the Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. K. D
ASANTHI,
Returning Officer,
Matale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matale,
08th October, 2019.
