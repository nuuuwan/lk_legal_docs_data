FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER  OF   THE   NEGOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Negombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group 03, the Independent Group to which Rodriguge Sunil
Wilfred Silva the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned member of  Independent Group 03 who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid  Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
Warnakulasuriya Shamal Harshana Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Negombo
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Warnakulasuriya Shamal Harshana Fernando was selected as the member of the Negombo Municipal
Council.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
29th of November, 2019.
