URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No.
2 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation
of the Minister-in-charge of the subject of Urban Development being of the opinion that the lands specified in the
Schedule hereto are urgently required for the Kottawa Town Centre Development Project do by this Order declare that
the said land is required for the aforesaid purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
October 8th, 2019.
