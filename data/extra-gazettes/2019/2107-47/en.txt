No. 2107/47 – FRIDAY, JANUARY 25, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of lands in a total extent of about 0.1849 Hectare,
depicted in the Advanced Tracing No. Maha/PJP/2016/346 and dated
30.12.2016, prepared by the Surveyor - General and situated in the
Villages of Diddeniya and Medagoda in the Minor Division of
Pallegampaha Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Poojapitiya in the District of Kandy in Central Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ginigewatta Hena alias Ekkassa – Extent: About
0.0038 Hectare
North: Road and Road (Provincial R.D.A.); East: Ginigewatta
alias Ekkassa claimed by Eranda Wijerathna; South: Ginigewatta
alias Ekkassa claimed by Eranda Wijerathna and Road; West: Road
and Road (Provincial R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘C’ – Ginigewatta Hena alias Ekkassa – Extent: About
0.1281 Hectare
North: Ekkassa Watta claimed by Sankha Muthubanda and others
and Road; East: Road, Ekkassa Watta claimed by Mallika
Kumarihamy and Lot D; South: Ekkassa Watta claimed by Mallika
Kumarihamy and Lots D and E; West: Lot E, Ekkassa Watta and
Road claimed by Sankha Muthubanda and others and Road.
03. Lot ‘D’ – Ginigewatta Hena alias Ekkassa – Extent: About
0.0073 Hectare
North: Lot C and Ekkassa Watta claimed by Mallika Kumarihamy;
East: Ekkassa Watta claimed by Mallika Kumarihamy; South: Ekkassa
Watta claimed by Mallika Kumarihamy, Pihibiyagahamula Hena
claimed by Sunil Keerthirathne and Lot E; West: Lot C.
04. Lot ‘E’ – Pihibiyagahamula Hena – Extent: About
0.0457 Hectare
North: Ekkassa Watta claimed by Sankha Muthubanda and others
and Lots C and D; East: Lots C and D, Ekkassa Watta claimed by
Mallika Kumarihamy, Pihibiyagahamula Hena claimed by Sunil
Keerthirathne and Pihibiyagahamula Hena claimed by D. G . M.
Abeyrathne; South: Pihibiyagahamula Hena claimed by Sunil
Keerthirathne, Pihibiyagahamula Hena claimed by D. G . M.
Abeyrathne and Road (Provincial R.D.A.) claimed by D. G . M.
Abeyrathne; West: Road and Ekkassa Watta claimed by Sankha
Muthubanda and others.
