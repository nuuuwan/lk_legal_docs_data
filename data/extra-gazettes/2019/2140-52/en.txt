FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of  Colombo Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United Peace Alliance, the recognized political party to which Mohomad
Masahir Mohomad Binkas the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member of  United Peace Alliance who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Cadar Mohideen Mohamed Nizamdeen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the  Colombo Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Cadar Mohideen Mohamed Nizamdeen was selected as the member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
10th September, 2019.
