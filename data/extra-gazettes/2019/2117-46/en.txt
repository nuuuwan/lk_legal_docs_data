SRI  LANKA  EXPORT  DEVELOPMENT  ACT,   No. 40  OF  1979
 Order  under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Malik
Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, with the concurrence of the Minister of
Finance, do hereby this Order amend the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2113/4 dated March 05, 2019, by the
addition of the following sub paragraph immediately after sub paragraph (3) of paragraph A.
“(4) Any goods imported on the basis of Letter of Credit (LC), established on or before March 05, 2019 and for which, the
Bill of Entry to be delivered to the Sri Lanka Customs on or before June 30, 2019, shall be paid at the rate specified in the
Column IV of the Schedule in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2047/2 dated November 27, 2017.”
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies
and International Trade.
Ministry of Development Strategies and International Trade,
Colombo 01,
April 04, 2019.
