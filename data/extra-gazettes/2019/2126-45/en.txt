PROMOTION   APPROVED   BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT

PROMOTION   AND   APPOINTMENT   APPROVED   BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Air Vice Marshal Dabare Liyanage
Sumangala Dias, RSP and three Bars, VSV , USP, MSc, ndc, psc, to the rank of Air Marshal and to appoint him under Article
61E(1)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka Air Force,
with effect from May 29, 2019.
                                               By  His Excellency’s Command,
U
