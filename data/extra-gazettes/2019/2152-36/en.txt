SOUTHERN  PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments / Dismissals  made  by  the  Southern Province Governor
I, Dr. W. W. Gamage, the Governor of Southern Provincial Council do hereby proclaim the following dismissals of respective
public authorities that come under the purview of Southern Provincial Council.
First Proclamation
By virtue of powers vested under Sections 154(c) and 154(e)(2) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka and Section 3(7) of the Statute of Southern Province Road Passenger Transport Authority No. 2 of
1996, by this proclamation it is decreed upon that the following persons are dismissed from the membership of the
Director Board of Southern Province Road Passenger Transport Authority with effect from 02.12.2019.
(a) K. H. Kapila Senarathna
(b) Piyasena Guruge
(c) W. T. Punyasiri
(d) A. H. Herman Sarath De Silva
