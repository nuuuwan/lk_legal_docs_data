STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Sub section (4) of Section 3
BY  Virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects Act, No. 14 of 2008
as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister in
charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11th July, 2011, as amended by the subsequent Order published under Sub-section
(4) of Section 3 under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/15 dated 05th July, 2016, do by this Order declare that,-
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1714/3 dated 11th July, 2011, as amended by the
subsequent Order published under sub-Section (4) of Section 3 under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1974/15 dated 05th July,
2016, is hereby further amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
"(4) The aforesaid "project to construct a luxury hotel as Mixed Use Complex Including High Quality Residencies and
a High - End Shopping Mall, in Fort, Colombo 01, in the Western Province" shall commence commercial operation on
30th June, 2020".
MALIK S AMARAWICKRAMA,
Minister of Development strategies and International Trade.
Colombo, 23rd September, 2019.
