1A   G   29178—17  (01/2019)
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  MEMBER  OF  THE   BADDEGAMA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION  66A(1) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Freedom Front, the recognized political party to which
Punyarathna Wickramasingha the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide upon  returned  member if United National Freedom Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Upasaka Dewayalage Ajith Prema Kumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare that Upasaka
Dewayalage Ajith Prema Kumar was selected as the member of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
