THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  No. 2129/96  DATED  28 TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/96 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4555/12).
The caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by inserting “THE
MAHO  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AREA  COMPRISING  OF” Immediately after “FOR” and by inserting the word
“(PART)” immediately after “MAHO  PRADESHIYA  SABHA”.
The content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by inserting “the Maho
Urban Development Area comprising of” immediately after “for” given in the fourth line thereof and by inserting the word
“(Part)” immediately after “Maho Pradeshiya Sabha” given in the fifth line thereof.
In the content of the upper of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification, the word “had” in the fourth line shall be
substituted with “have”.
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be read as follows:

“NOTICE  OF  APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE MAHO  URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA
COMPRISING  OF THE  MAHO  PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART) AREAS.”
“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
Section 8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the Maho Urban
Development Area comprising of the Maho Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) area, prepared under Section 8A of the said
Law.”
P
ATALI  CHAMPIKA  RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
The caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by inserting “the Maho
Urban Development Area Comprising of” immediately after “for” and by inserting the word “(Part)” immediately after
“Maho Pradeshiya Sabha”.
The content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be rectified by inserting “the
Maho Urban Development Area comprising of” Immediately after “for” given in the second line thereof and by
inserting the word “(Part)” immediately after “Maho Pradeshiya Sabha” given in the second line thereof.
The word “(Amendment)” in the second line of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be substituted
with “Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the”.
The word “Amendment Act” given in the last line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with
“Amended Law.”
The rectification introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Maho Urban Development Area comprising of Maho Pradeshiya Sabha
(Part) Area”.
“Public are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority
Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the Act, No. 4 of 1982, for the Maho Urban Development Area comprising of the Maho
Pradeshiya Sabha (Part) areas has been approved on 28th June, 2019, by Hon. Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of
Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 8F of the said amended Law.”
D
R. JAGA TH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.
07th November, 2019.
Notwithstanding the provisions contained in the antecedent Gazette Notification  bearing No. 2129/96 dated 28th June,
2019 (07-4555/12), the rectification hereinbefore mentioned shall have retrospective effect and shall be deemed to have
come into operation on the 28th day of June, 2019.
P
ATALI  CHAMPIKA  RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
