PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 27.05.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land described
below in Paragraph 1 is required for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act. The land over which the
said servitude is to be acquired is given in Paragraph 2.
1. Description of the Servitude.– Acquisition of servitude over
the land, for the construction of Access Road to Pujapitiya Service
Reservoir and Pump House of Kandy North - Pathadumbara
Intergrated Water Supply Project.
2. Description of the Land.– The allotments of land depicted in
the Advance Tracing No. Maha/PJP/2015/602 and dated 08.02.2016,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Villages of
Diddeniya and Medagoda in the Minor Division of Pallegampaha
Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pujapitiya in Kandy
District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ekkassawatta – Extent: About 0.0066 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and Ekkassawatta claimed by Eranda
Wijerathne; East: Ekkassawatta claimed by Eranda Wijerathne;
South: Ekkassawatta claimed by Eranda Wijerathne, Lot C,
Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta claimed by B. M. S. Bandaranayaka
and Lot B; West: Ekkassawatta claimed by K. M. W. Sankha Muthu
Bandara and Road (R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Ekkassawatta – Extent: About 0.0043 Hectare
North: Ekkassawatta claimed by K. M. W. Sankha Muthu Bandara
and Lot A; East: Lot C and Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta claimed
by B. M. S. Bandaranayaka; South: Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta
claimed by B. M. S. Bandaranayaka and Road; West: Road and
Ekkassawatta claimed by K. M. W. Sankha Muthu Bandara.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta – Extent: About
0.0136 Hectare
North: Lot A and Ekkassawatta claimed by Eranda Wijerathne;
East: Ekkassawatta claimed by Eranda Wijerathne; South: Road and
Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta claimed by B. M. S. Bandaranayaka;
West: Ekkassawatta alias Ginigewatta claimed by B. M. S.
Bandaranayaka and Lot B.
