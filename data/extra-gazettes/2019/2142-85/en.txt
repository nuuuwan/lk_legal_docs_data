FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PANADURA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which Pethahandi
Nevil Chandrasoma the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned  member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kumara Unnehelage Pradeep Nishshanka Dayas to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wannakuwatta Waduge Kalindra Jayaweera Fernando, Returning Officer  of  the  Panadura
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Kumara Unnehelage Pradeep Nishshanka Dayas was selected as the member of the Panadura Pradeshiya
Sabha.
K
ALINDRA J AYAWEERA F ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kalutara,
26th September, 2019.
