THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/05(RDA).
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/8/2013/HW/150.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by Land Acquisition Act,
that the Government intends to acquire the following lands
which are required for a public purpose.
      Description of the lands to be acquired:
Following allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 81 and 84
in Extent of 0.0005 Hectare in cadaster map No. 620296 made
by Surveyor General, situated in Ratnapura (Within Ratnapura
Municipal Council area) Village of 152F Ratnapura Town West
Grama Niladari Division of   Divisional Secretary's Division
Rathnapura in the District of Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa
Province.
01. Lot No. 81
Name of the land: Badalowita; Land Use: Road; Claimant:
Government; First Claimant: Weerakoon Arachchige Jinadasa
and Mahabaththana Ralalage Ranjani Menike Weerakoon of
No. 255, Bandaranayaka Mawatha; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 84
Name of the land: Badalowita; Land Use: Road; Claimant:
Government; First Claimant: Weerakoon Arachchige Jinadasa
and Mahabaththana Ralalage Ranjani Menike Weerakoon of
No. 255, Bandaranayaka Mawatha; Extent: 0.0004 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid  lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are here by requested to appear
personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.00 a.m. on 27th day of March, 2019 at Rathnapura
Divisional Secretariat.
Moreover, they are here by requested to notify to me in
writing with two duplicates on or before the 19th of March,
2019 the nature of their interest in land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and details of the computation
of such amount.
 I. R. M. PRADEEPIKA D. ILLUKKUMBURA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rathnapura.
At Rathnapura Divisional Secretariat,
08th February , 2019.
