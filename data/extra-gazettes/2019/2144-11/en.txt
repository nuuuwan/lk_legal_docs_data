PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Article  154E of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
I, Maithri Gunaratne, the Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce that, the Uva Province Provincial Council
having completed its five year Term, shall stand dissolved from the midnight of 08th October 2019, according to the
Article 154E of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
M
AITHRI  G UNARATNE,
Governor of the Uva Province.
At office of the Governor of the Uva Province,
07th October, 2019.
