FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MORATUWA  MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of  Moratuwa Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Babara
Guwendaleen Pieris the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member if United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid Recognized  Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Ranhaluge Krishani Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer  of  the  Moratuwa Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that  Ranhaluge Krishani Fernando  was selected as the member of the Moratuwa Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
Moratuwa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Colombo City,
23rd July, 2019.
