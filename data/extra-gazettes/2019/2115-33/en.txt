The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   HARISPATTUWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of the return member of  Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which K. A. Janaka
Nayanananda the Member belonged, was required by me under Section  66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned member of People’s Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of
the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate D. A. Bandulasiri Nimal Santha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer  of  the
Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter
262) do hereby declare that D. A. Bandulasiri Nimal Santha was elected as the member of the Harispattuwa Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Harispattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kandy,
19th March, 2019.
