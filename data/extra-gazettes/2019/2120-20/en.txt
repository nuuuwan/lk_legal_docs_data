WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARDS   FOR  THE PADDY  HULLING  TRADE
IT is hereby notifies under Regulation 30 of the Wages Board Regulations publilshed in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members
of the wages board for the Paddy Hulling Trade, by virture of  the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade
Union Relations under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years
commencing on 01st April 2018.
R. P. R
AJAPAKSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05.
