SPORTS  ACT,   No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sections 32 and 33
I, Harin Fernando, Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports in pursuance of powers vested in me
under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby further extend the period of suspension of the
Sri Lanka Hokey Federation, the National Federation for Hokey and the following Interim Committee is appointed as a
provisional arrangement to continue the activities of the above Hokey Federation to finalize the election activities under
the supervision of Director General of the Department of the Sports Development.
Mr. Nishantha Anuruddha Weerasinghe - President
Mr. H. A. W. Abayakumara - Secretary
Mrs. K. K. S. K. Wijesinghe - Treasurer
Further, I do hereby cancel the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2053/7 dated 08.01.2018 with effect from 18th
March, 2019.
H
ARIN F ERNANDO (M.P.),
Minister of Telecommunication,
Foreign Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
18th March, 2019.
