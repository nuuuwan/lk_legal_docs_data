No. 2137/93–  FRIDAY, AUGUST, 2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
Ref No.: HAK/LND/1/16.  
Land Ministry Ref No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/200. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 07, 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land /
servitude which is required for a public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired:-  
Following allotments  of lands depicted in Sheet No. 09 
in Additional No. 10 of Final Village Survey Plan No. 
176, prepared by Senior Survey Superintendent of Matara 
District,  situated in the village of Kebiliyapola South, in 
Grama Niladhari’s Division No. 284A, Minor Division of 
Kandabada Pattu East in Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Hakmana, Matara District, Southern Province. 
 
1. Lot No.  273 
Name of the land: Godamabewela Bogahahena; 
Original Claimant: K. M. Siyadoris,Welegedara, 
Kebiliyapola, South Hakmana; Extent: 0.0114 Hectare.   
Boundaries : 
North : Lot Nos. 113 and 89; East: Lot Nos. 89 and 
113; South: Lot No. 113; West: Lot Nos. 113 and 114. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or agents duly  authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat of Hakmana on the 
23rd day of October 2019, at 10.00 a.m and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 16th day of October 2019, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation, for the Acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 KUSALAKA NANAYAKKARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Hakmana.  
21st day of August, 2019.
