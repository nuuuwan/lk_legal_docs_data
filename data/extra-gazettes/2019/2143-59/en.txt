IT is hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intended to acquire the following land or servitude
which is required for a public purpose.
Below mentioned additional part of a land depicted as Lot
No. 01 in Plan No. PP/MR/2809, situated in the Village of
Katuwangoda, in Malimbada Divisional Secretariat Division,
in Matara District, in Southern Province was acquired for the
construction of the "Thuluman Linda" and water pump house
under the Matara 4th step of water supply scheme.
