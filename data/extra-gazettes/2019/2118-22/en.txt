THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the above provisions of
the said Act. The particulars of the acquired land are described in
paragraph II.
I – Particulars of servient right
Aquiring an allotment of land to build the entrance road of the
Uduwawala Service reservoir under the Kandy North and
Pathadumbara combined water supply project.
