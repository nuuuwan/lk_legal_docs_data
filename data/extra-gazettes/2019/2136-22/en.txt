FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VELANAI    PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Pulenthiran Kajan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned  member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Aarumugam Kobalakirshnan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendran Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the  Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Aarumugam
Kobalakirshnan was selected as the member of the Velani Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Velanai Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
15th August, 2019.
