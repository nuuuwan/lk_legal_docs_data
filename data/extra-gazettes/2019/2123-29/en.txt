CEYLON  ELECTRICITY  BOARD   ACT,  No. 17  OF  1969
Order under Section  57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Power, Energy and Business Development do by this Order approve the proposed acquisition
of lands required by the Ceylon Electricity Board described in the Schedule.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Power, Energy & Business Development.
09th May, 2019.
