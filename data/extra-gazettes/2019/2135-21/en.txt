EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of the Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mrs. Suppaiya Uma
Saraswathi of the Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated her office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as she has ceased
to be a member of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at Present).
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Panvila Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
01st August, 2019.
