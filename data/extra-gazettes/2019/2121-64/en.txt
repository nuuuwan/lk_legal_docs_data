FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BELIATTA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Amarakon
Manamperige Kapila Priyadarshana the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Kotta Hewage Janaka Krishantha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Ravindra Subhashana Wedage, Returning Officer  of  the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Kotta
Hewage Janaka Krishantha was selected as the member of the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
A VINDRA S UBHASHANA WEDAGE,
Returning Officer,
Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Hambanthota,
03rd May, 2019.
