No. 2143/53 – FRIDAY, OCTOBER 04, 2019
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Order under Section 39 A (1)
The allotments specified in the Order No. 39 of 2013 made under the
Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)
and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1796/17 dated 07th
February 2013 is vested absolutely in the State and the possession
of which has been taken for or on behalf of the State under Para (a) of
Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by the virtue of powers vested
in me by Sub section (1) of Section 39 (a) (inserted by Act, No. 8 of
1979) of the Land Acquisition Act, I, Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms do hereby divest only
the allotments described in the Schedule I below.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
