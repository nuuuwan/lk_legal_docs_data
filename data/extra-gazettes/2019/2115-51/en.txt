THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref No.:  MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/44-2.
Land Ministry Ref No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/518.
IT is hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the government intended
to acquire the following land or servitude which is required
for public purpose.
Below mentioned land depicted as Lot No. 1 in plan No. PP/
MR/2761 situated in the village of Malimbada, in Malimbada
Divisional Secretariat Division, in Matara District, in Southern
Province was acquired for the construction of "Thuluman
Linda" and pumping station under water supply scheme of
IV th Step, Matara.
Sub Schedule
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the land: Hettigewatta; Description of the Land:
Scrub; Initial claimant of the Land: K. K. Nilantha Lakmal,
Kithalagama East (iii), Thihagoda; Extent: 0.0846 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude is to
be acquired are hereby required to appear  personally or by
agent  duly authorized in writing, before me at Malimbada
Divisional  Secretariat  on this 05th April 2019 at 10.30 a.m. and
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the 28.03.2019 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of  their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said  land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
DEEPIKA GUNARATNE,
Divisional Secretary,
Malimbada.
On the 21 day of March 2019,
At the Malimbada Divisional Secretariat.
