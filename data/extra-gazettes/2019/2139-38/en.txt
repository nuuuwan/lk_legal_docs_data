The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   GODAKAWELA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member in Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic People’s Front at Present), the
recognized political party to which Rajamanikkam Asok Kumar the member belonged, was required by me under Section
66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of United Progressive Alliance (Democratic
People’s Front at Present) who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Annamalai Bothimathavan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer  of  the  Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Annamalai Bothimathavan was selected as the member of the Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
02nd September, 2019.
