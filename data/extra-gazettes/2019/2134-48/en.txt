THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of Land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. GA/
GFG/2016/313 and dated 14.09.2016 situated in the Village of Dadalla
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kadawathsathara in Galle
District in  Southern Province and bounded by the lands with following
names and extent:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kanaththa or Kanaththa Waththa and Deniya
Kanaththa – Extent: About 0.2962 Hectare
North: Generalge Waththa claimed by Ginthota Depot, Ceylon
Transport Board; East: Lot B and Atambagaha Waththa; South: Lot
B; West: Lot B and Generalge Waththa claimed by Ginthota Depot,
Ceylon Transport Board.
