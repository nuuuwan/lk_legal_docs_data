WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  BAKERY  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Board regulations publilshed in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971 that
the Hon Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of
the Wages Board for the Bakery Trade, by the virture of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union
Relations under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing
on 1st of November 2018.
