SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I Rajith Keerthi Tennakoon, the Governor of the Southern Province herewith notify that the following  position of the
Provincial Road Development Authority of  Southern Province is removed under Sections, 154 C and 154  F of 13th
amendment of the constitution of  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1. Removal of Mr. Amila Sampath Wijesinghe, campaigned as a Member of Director Board and the Director o f
Provincial Road Development  Authority of Southern Province after the date of 04.06.2019 from the positions held
as a Member of Director Board and the Director at the aforesaid authority.
RAJITH KEERTHI TENNAKOON,
Governor of Southern Province.

Lower Dickson Road,
Galle.
