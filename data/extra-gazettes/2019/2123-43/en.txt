Urban Development Authority  Ref. No.: 10/3/590. 
 
It is hereby  given  notice, that the Gazette Notification 
published in Mr. Mahinda Rajapaksha, Former Minister 
of Defence &  Urban  Development regarding the Urban 
Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 of the 
National State Assembly Order under Section 15(1) 
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1802/10 
dated 19.03.2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka should be amended as follows. 
 
 PATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA (M. P.), 
                Minister of Megapolis & Western Development 
 
The Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 
1978 of the National State Assembly Correction Notice 
under Section 15 (1) 
 
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) 
of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 
1978, I Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of 
Megapolis & Western Development do by this Order vest 
with the Urban Development Authority, with effect from 
today the land described in the schedule hereto belongs to 
the Jaffna Municipal Council. 
 
Description of the land to be vested: 
 
The land called Rest House premises, Kandy Road 
containing in extent of 31.46P depicted as lot No. 1 in 
Plan No. 4013/10 dated 24.08.2010 made by  
K. Kanagasabai, Registerd Licensed Surveyor and 
Leveller situated at Village of Koddai in Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Jaffna within the Municipal 
Council limits of Jaffna in District of Jaffna Nothern 
Province. 
 
The land is bounded as follows: 
 
North: Kandy main Road; East: Trace of an old 
foundation; South: Reservation for Road; West: Beach 
Road.
