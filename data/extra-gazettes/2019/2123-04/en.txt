Regulations
The Emergency (Miscellaneous Provisions and Powers) Regulations No. 1 of 2019 published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2120/5 of April 22, 2019 as amended by regulations published in the Gazette  Extraordinary
No. 2121/1 of April 29, 2019 are hereby further amended as follows:-
(1) in regulation 26,  by the repeal of all the words from “(c) any other activity” to the end of that regulation,
and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“(c) any other activity in furtherance of any act of terrorism or specified terrorist activity committed by
any person, group or groups of persons,

(3) in paragraph (1) of regulation 32A-
(a)  by the repeal of subparagraph (a) thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“ (a)  No person shall wear in any public place, any garment, clothing or other material
concealing the full face which will in any manner cause any hindrance to the identification of
such person:
Provided, however, a member of the Sri Lanka Army, Sri Lanka Navy, Sri Lanka Air Force,
a police officer or a member of the Department of Civil Security may require such person to
remove such garment, clothing or other material which conceals such person’s full face including
the ears, worn by that person so as to enable such member of the Sri Lanka Army, Sri Lanka
Navy, Sri Lanka Air Force,police officer or member of the Department of Civil Security, to
identify such person.”;
(b) in subparagraph (b), by the repeal of the definition of the expression “full face”, and the substitution
therefor of the following :-
 “ “full face” means a person’s face from the top of the forehead to the bottom of the chin and
between (but not including) the ears;”.
