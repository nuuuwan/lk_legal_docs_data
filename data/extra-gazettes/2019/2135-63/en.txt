THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My No.: GCF/ATH/CB/22/LR.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 03/J 91/LRB/133.
The General Public is hereby notified that the compensation
payments  in respect of allotment of land described in the Schedule
below of which the titles were not proved and depicted under Lot
Nos. 01, 04 and 05 in Preliminary Plan No. 7775 prepared by the
Surveyor-General that has been vested in the Government through
Proviso 38(a) No. 100 of 1993 published in the Gazette Extra
Ordinary Notification No. 775/3 dated 12.07.1993 under Greater
Colombo Flood Control and Environment Improvement Project,
have been deposited in the District Court, Nugegoda in terms of
Section 33 of the Land Acquisition Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotment mentioned below could obtain the compensation payable
for the allotment after submitting necessary documents to the District
Court, Colombo to prove their ownership.
C. W. Abeysuriya,
Acquiring Officer.
