No. 2143/22–  WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER,  02,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 Ref. No.: THI/LND/L2/ACQVI/140. 
 
I  hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude  which is required for a public purpose 
mentioned below: 
 
Description of the Land to be Acquired: 
 
Following allotments of Lands containing in extent 
0.4821 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in Preliminary 
Survey Plan No. MR 2814 dated 03.09.2019 prepared by 
Senior Survey Superintendent of Matara District, situated 
in the Village of Andaledduwa, in Grama Niladari’s 
Division  of Uduwa East 333 in Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Thihagoda Matara District, Southern 
Province. 
 
  Lot No. 1  
  Name of the Land: Manigewatta alias Managewatta; 
Description: Home Garden, Permanent building a part of a 
toilet, a well build with cement, mixed cultivation with jak, 
coconut trees aged about 50-60 years; Extent: 0.4821 Hectare. 
 
  
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/Lands on 
which  a servitude is to be acquired are  hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of Thihagoda 
on the 28 day of November 2019  at 10.00 a. m.  and are 
hereby requested to  notify to me in writing  (in duplicate) 
on or before the 20th day of November 2019 the nature of 
their interests in the land,  the particulars of their claims 
of  compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
  
 UMESHA SURANGI MATHARAGE, 
                                               Divisional  Secretary,  
                                                     Thihagoda.  
30th day of September 2019, 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Thihagoda.
