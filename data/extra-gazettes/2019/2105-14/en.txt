Minister of lands and Parliamentary Reforms
Reference No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/414.
I hereby give notice,  under Sub section (1) of section 7 of the
land Acquisition Act,  that the Government intends to acquire
the following Land/Servitude which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land is called Duldeniya watta about 0.8159
hectares in extent depicted as Lots 01, and 02, in Preliminary
plan Number P .P .K 3603 and dated 22/06/2018 made by the
Survey General and situated in the Village of Duldeniya, in the
Grama Niladari Division of No-42 A Duldeniya, in the Minor
Division of Thunpalatha Paththuwa, in the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Aranayaka, in the District of Kegalle, in
Sabaragamuwa Province. Boundaries are as follows:
01. Lot No. 1; Name of the Land: Duldeniya watta;
Extent:0.3698 Hectare;
     Boundaries:
North: Road (Pradeshiya sabha) and Duldeniyawatta; East
and South: Duldeniyawatta; West: Road (Pradeshiya sabha).
   Other particulars : The Land has been acquired under section
38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act. Preliminary claiments are
Mr. S.O.N.N. Moorthi resident of Thambarawa, Thalgaspitiya
and Mr. P.D. Wimalasena resident of Ahalgastenna,
Gavilipitiya, Aranayaka for the completion of acquisition
activities.
02. Lot No. 2; Name of the Land: Duldeniya watta;
Extent:0.4461 Hectare;
     Boundaries:
North: Dry Stream (Malaela) and Duldeniyawatta; East:
Duldeniyawatta and Road (Pradeshiya sabha); South: Road
(Pradeshiya sabha) Road and Duldeniyawatta; West: Dry
Stream (Malaela).
   Other particulars : The Land has been acquired under section
38 (A) of the Land Acquisition Act. Preliminary claiments are
Mr. S.O.N.N. Moorthi resident of Thambarawa, Thalgaspitiya
