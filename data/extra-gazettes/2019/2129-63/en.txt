FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V ALVETTITHURAI   URBAN
COUNCIL  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Valvettithurai Urban Council has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Kandaiya Perananthan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member of  Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thurairasa Sritharan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer of the Valvettithurai Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thurairasa
Sritharan was selected as the member of the Valvettithurai Urban Council.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valvettithurai Urban Council.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
25th June, 2019.
