The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN OF   THE   EMBILIPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS   AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION (AMENDED)
ACT,  NO. 16  OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of loss of seat or office
;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Meegasdeniya Kankanamge Amil the Former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  Embilitpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Weerasinghage Surantha Jayalath to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Election (amended) Act,  No. 16  of 2017 do hereby declare  Weerasinghage Surantha Jayalath elected as chairman of the
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
20th June, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN OF   THE   EMBILIPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262) AS   AMENDED BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTION (AMENDED)
ACT,  NO. 16  OF 2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman
has been appointed as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasinghage Surantha Jayalath  the former vice chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66B(2) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of that  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as vice chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mathara Arachchige Jayaweera to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
Election (amended) Act,  No. 16  of 2017  do hereby declare  Mathara Arachchige Jayaweera elected as vice chairman of
the  Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATHENNE ,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
20th June, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER OF   THE   EMBILIPITIYA  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Kekanadura Vidana Arachchige Chandrasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the
aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that 5 Gangeyaya of Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared
elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Paththini Wickramarachchige Nimal Kumarasingha to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Paththini
Wickramarachchige Nimal Kumarasingha  was elected as the member of the 5 Gangeyaya of Embilitpitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATHENNE ,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura,
20th June, 2019.
