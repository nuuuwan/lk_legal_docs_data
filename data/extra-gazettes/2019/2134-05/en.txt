THE   FINANCE  ACT,  No.  25  OF  2003
Order under Section 2(1)

Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2116/62 dated March 29, 2019 by repeal of Schedule I and Substitution therefor of the following Schedule with
effect from August 01, 2019.
