SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Eran
Wickramaratne, Acting Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose, in
respect of the list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of one year commencing from July 19, 2019, subject to provisions of Sub-
Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
E
RAN W ICKRAMARA TNE,
Acting Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
July 18, 2019.
