L.D.B. 104/44(v)
TRUSTS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order  under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of  “the Trustees of the Society of Jesus in Sri Lanka” namely -
1. Reverend Father Dexter Fredric Gray,
2. Reverend Father Noel Felix Anton Pieris,
3. Reverend Father George Anthony Fernando,
4. Reverend Father Clement Ranjan Jayamanne.
have applied to the Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms for their incorporation under Section 114 of the
Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87) ;
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Thalatha Athukorala, Minister
of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Order authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees, and direct and declare
that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under
the Deed of Trust bearing No. 486 of June 25, 2019, attested by Tharini Prakarshi Dias Senerath
Gunasekera, Notary Public of Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a
common seal under the name and style of “the Trustees of the Society of Jesus in Sri Lanka” ;
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk

(b) the said Trustees may, under the said name and style, purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise,
