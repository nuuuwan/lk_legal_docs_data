SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sections 32 and 33
BY  virtue of powers vested in me, under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Harin Fernando,
Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign Employment and Sports, suspend the registration, with immediate effect, of the
Sri Lanka Judo Association, the National Association for the Sport of Judo and appoint the following Interim Committee
to continue the activities thereof as a provisional arrangement.
1. Mr. W. S. E. Jayasundara - President
2. Dr. (Mr.) K. A. P. Kiriella - Member
3. Mrs. D. M. S. S. Basnayake - Member
And, I do hereby authorize the Director General of the Department of Sports Development to convene a Special
General Meeting of the Judo Association and appoint an Election Committee in terms of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973
and subsequent amendments made thereto and convene and conduct the election under supervision of the Director General.
H
ARIN F ERNANDO ,
Minister of Telecommunication, Foreign
Employment and Sports.
Colombo,
29th March, 2019.
