THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
My Ref. No.: KHDS/LND/03/ACQ/13-2.
Ministry of Land and Parliamentary Reforms
Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2017/VE/425.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act (Amendment),  No. 28 of 1964 that the
Government intends to acquire the following lands which are
required for public purpose.
Description of the Land to be Acquired:
Allotment of Land in total extent about 0.8488 Hectare
depicted in Lot No. 340 in the Preliminary Plan F. V . P . No. 8
Supplement No.13 Sheet No.14 and dated 17.03.2019 Prepared
by the Surveyor General situated in the village of Nambuluwa
of Pannil Pattu Minor Division of Atakalan Korale, Divisional
Secretariat of Kahawatta in Rathnapura District
Sabaragamuwa Province.
1. Lot No. 340
Name of Land: Welladurawatta; Description: Open Waste
land; Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation, Corporation, No. 52,
Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.8488 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Kahawatta on the 04th December,
2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27th November, 2019,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
B. T. G
AY ANI I. KARUNARA THNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kahawatta.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kahawatta,
01st October, 2019.
