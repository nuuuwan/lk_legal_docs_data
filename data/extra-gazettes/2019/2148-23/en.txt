MEDIATION  (SPECIAL  CATEGORIES  OF  DISPUTES)  ACT,  No. 21  OF  2003
Order under section 8(1)
BY  VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of section 8 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act,
No. 21 of  2003, I, Thalatha Athukorale, Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms, do by this Order, specify for the purpose of
the aforesaid section the monetary value of the subject matter of a dispute belonging to the category of disputes set out in
Column I of the Schedule arising wholly or partly within the Special Mediation Board Area specified in Column II of the
Scheudle shall be the amount specified in the corresponding entry in Column III thereof.
T
HALA THA ATHUKORALE,
Minister of  Justice and Prison Reforms.
Colombo,
29th October, 2019.
