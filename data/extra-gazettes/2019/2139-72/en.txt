PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 06.09.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 33
My No.: MCUDP/LA/MUT(W-14)/14/02/A.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/10/2015/LRB/285.
The Public is hereby notified that the compensation payments  in
respect of allotment of land of which the claims were not presented
as depicted in the Schedules 01 to 14 below and included in
Preliminary Plan No. 9654 dated 07.10.2016 prepared by the
Surveyor General which are to be acquired to the Government through
Section 7 Notice under the Land Acquisition Act( Chapter 460)
published in the Gazette Notification No. 2095/ 38 dated 01.11.2016
under Metro Colombo Urban Development Project, have been
deposited in the District Court, Colombo under case numbers
depicted in the same schedule under Section 33 of the Act.
The General Public is hereby informed that any claimant to the
allotments mentioned below could obtain the compensation payable
for the allotment concerned after submitting necessary documents to
the District Court, Colombo to prove their ownership.
C. W. Abeysuriya,
Land  Acquiring Officer.
Rajagiriya.
Metro Colombo Urban Development Project,
Land Acquisition Office, No. 03,
Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya.
06th September 2019.
