PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL

URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of
the Miniser-in-Charge of the subject of Megapolis & Western Development  being of the opinion that the land specified
in the Schedule hereto is urgently required for Tec City Development Project, do by this Order declare that the said land
is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
June 03, 2019.
