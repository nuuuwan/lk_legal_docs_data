The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN  OF   THE   KAMBURUPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUHORITIES  ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasuriya Mudiyanselage Sanjeewa Priyanath the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 66 B
(2) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such
party who shall be declared  selected  as Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  Political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Weerasinghe Kankanamage Ananda Weerasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the  Kamburupitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as
amended by Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare  Weerasinghe Kankanamage
Ananda Weerasinghe elected as Chairman of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
02nd October, 2019.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 03.10.2019
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE CHAIRMAN  OF   THE   KAMBURUPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66B(2) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUHORITIES  ELECTIONS  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No.  16  OF  2017
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Appointment
as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasinghe Kankanamge Ananda Weerasinghe the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section
66 B (2) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to
such party who shall be declared  elected  as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Lalith Alwis Samarasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66B(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2017, do hereby declare Lalith Alwis Samarasinghe elected as
Vice Chairman of the Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
02nd October, 2019.

FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KAMBURUPITIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Weerasuriya Mudiyanselage Sanjeewa Priyanath the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  the member of  that 1 - Gathara of Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared  elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Rejee Namel Wellappili to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Thananthiri Withanage Poshitha Kaumadi, Returning Officer  of  the  Kamburupitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Rejee Namel Wellappili was elected as the member of the 1- Gathara Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha.
T. W. P
OSHITHA KAUMADI,
Returning Officer,
Kamburupitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Matara,
02nd October, 2019.
