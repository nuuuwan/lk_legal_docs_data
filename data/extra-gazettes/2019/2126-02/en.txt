The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARANDENIYA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Karandeniya pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Bodiya Baduge Lalith Rathnakumara Gamini Perera the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna who belongs to such party
and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thommaya Hakuru Nirosh Sanjeewa Ranasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Kanda Uda Chandralal, Returning Officer of the Karandeniya  pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Thommaya
Hakuru Nirosh Sanjeewa Ranasinghe was selected as the member of the Karandeniya  pradeshiya Sabha.
K. U. C
HANDRALAL,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya  pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Galle,
30th May, 2019.
