HOSPITAL  UPGRADING  OF  MINISTRY  OF  HEALTH,  NUTRITION  AND   INDIGENOUS  MEDICINE
AS  proposed under the Budget Speech 2017, two new Medical Faculties were established in the Sabaragamuwa University
of Sri Lanka and Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, with the aim of expanding medical education in Sri Lanka.
The Provincial General Hospital (PGH), Ratnapura and Base Hospital-Kuliyapitiya have been selected in order
to facilitate the clinical training for students of the new faculties. Accordingly, these two hospitals were upgraded as
Teaching Hospitals with effect from the dates given below to develop the required facilities for under-graduate and post-
graduate training.
No. Name of the University Name of the Hospital Effective Date
1. Sabaragamuwa University of Teaching Hospital, Ratnapura 31.10.2018
Sri Lanka
2. Wayamba University of Teaching Hospital, 25.01.2019
Sri Lanka Kuliyapitiya
