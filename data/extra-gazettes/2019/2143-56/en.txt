Edition), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial disputes - Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint Mrs. K. K. Kumari Dhanapala, 149/102, Madhra Uyana, Maththegoda to be the arbitrator and
refer the aforesaid dispute to her for settlement by arbitration.

A VINDRA SAMARAWEERA,
