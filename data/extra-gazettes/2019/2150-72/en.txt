Third Supplementary Financial Statement
GRANTING  PERMISSION   BY   VIRTUE   OF   POWERS   VESTED   IN   ME   UNDER   SECTION   27(A)   OF
PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL ACT, No. 42 OF 1987 AS  THE  TERM  OF  THE  SIXTH   SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCIAL
COUNCIL  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  ENDED  ON  26 TH SEPTEMBER,  2017
2019
1. This permission may be cited as ''Permission to disburse under third financial statement - 2019 of  the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial  Council'' and come  in to operation from 09.10.2019
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Four Billion
Twenty Three Million, Four Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty
Four only (Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) for the service of the period beginning on 01 st of January 2019 and
ending on 31st December, 2019, which is specified as  ”Financial Year 2019” in the Budget Estimate - 2019,
that was approved on 10.04.2019 and amended by First and Second Supplementary Financial Statement -
2019, is hereby amended  as, Rupees Thirty Four Billion Fifty Seven Million Eight Hundred and Six Thousand
One Hundred Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,057,806,197.24) and grant permission to
the Chief Secretary of this Province and other Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
3. Accordingly the first Schedule of the said Gazette Notification is amended as the first schedule hereof and
grant permission to disburse the same.

PART IV(A) — GAZETTE   EXTRA  ORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA - 22.11.2019
AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
to disburse as per the Third Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2019, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as term of the 6th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Four Billion Twenty
Three Million, Four Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four only
(Rs. 34,023,406,197.24) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2019 and ending 31st December,
2019, which is specified in annual financial statement - 2019 and amended by first supplementary estaimate - 2019 is
hereby amended as, Rupees  Thirty Four Billion Fifty Seven Million Eight Hundred and Six Thousand One Hundred and
Ninety Seven and Cents Twenty Four Only (Rs. 34,057,806,197.24) and grant permission to disburse the same from the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st of December, 2019 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of
summoning the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial
Council which occurs first.
D
HAMMA D ISSANAY AKE,
Hon. Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
