FIFTH SUPPLEMENTARY FINANCIAL STATEMENT
GRANTING  PERMISSION  BY  VIRTUE  OF  POWERS  VESTED  IN  ME  UNDER  SECTION  27(A)  OF
PROVINCIAL COUNCIL  ACT, No. 42 OF 1987 AS THE  TERM  OF  THE  SIXTH  SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCIAL
COUNCIL OF THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI LANKA  ENDED  ON 26TH  SEPTEMBER, 2017.
2018
1. This permission may be cited as ‘‘ Permission to disburse under Fifth financial statement - 2018 of the Sabaragamuwa
Provincial Council’’ and come into operation from 26.12.2018.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Two Billion Eight Hundred
and Thirty Four Million, Four Hundred and Eight Thousand One Hundred and Twelve and Cents Thirty Five only
(Rs. 32,834,408,112.35) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2018 and ending 31st December, 2018,
which is specified in 2nd paragraph of Gazette Notification No. 2052/15 dated 03.01.2018, which is amended by fourth
supplementary financial statement for the year 2018 and assented on 13.11.2018, is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty
Two Billion Nine Hundred and Thirty Four Million, Four Hundred and Eight Thousand One Hundred and Twelve and
Cents Thirty Five Only, (Rs. 32,934,408,112.35) and grant permission  to Chief Secretary of this Province  and other
Accounting Officers to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund.

NILUKA EKANAYAKE,
Honourable Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
26th day of December, 2018.
AUTHORIZATION
I, do hereby grant Permission to the Chief Secretary and the Accounting Officers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council to
disburse as per the Fourth supplementary financial statement for the year 2018, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 27 (a) of the provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th  Sabaragamuwa  Provincial  Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended  on 26th September, 2017.
The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Two Billion Eight Hundred and
Thirty Four Million, Four Hundred and Eight Thousand One Hundred and Twelve and Cents Thirty Five Only
(Rs. 32,834,408,112.35) for the service of the period beginning on 01st of January, 2018 and ending 31st December, 2018, which
is specified in annual financial statement - 2018 (amended) is hereby amended as, Rupees Thirty Two Billion Nine Hundred and
Thirty Four Million, Four Hundred and Eight Thousand One Hundred and Twelve and Cents Thirty Five only
(Rs. 32,934,408,112.35) and grant permission to disburse the same from the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  Fund.
This permission will be valid till 31st of  December, 2018 or upto the date that expires 03 months from the date of summoning
the 07th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start  the functions of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
which occurs first.
