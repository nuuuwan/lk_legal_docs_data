PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT)ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964  
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section (7) 
of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The allotments of land indicated in following letters in 
extent of 1.7472 Hectares depicted in the Preliminary 
Plan No. P.P.K. 3620 dated 29.12.2018 prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the village of Panawatta 
West in the Atalugam Koralaya in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Yatiyantota in the District of 
Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province: 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of land: Not indicated; Extent: 0.0635 Hectare. 
On the North by: Panawatta; On the East by: 
Panawatta Estate, Road and Lot No. 2; On the South by: 
Lot No. 2, Panawatta Estate, Stream and Lot No. 4; On 
the West by: Stream and Panawatta Estate.  
 
02. Lot No. 02 
Name of land: Not indicated; Extent: 0.4047 Hectare. 
 
On the North by: Lot No. 1 and Road; On the East by: 
Panawatta Estate; On the South by: Panawatta Estate; On 
the West by: Panawatta Estate, Stream, Lot No. 4 and 1. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
Name of land: Not indicated ; Extent: 0.4352 Hectare. 
 
On the North by:  Lot No. 4; On the East by:  Stream; 
On the South by:  Stream and Panawatta Estate; On the 
West by: Pradeshiya Sabha Road. 
 
04. Lot No. 04 
Name of land: Not indicated ; Extent: 0.0973 Hectare. 
 
On the North by: Lot No. 5, Athukoralaya Watta, 
Stream and Lot No. 1; On the East by: Stream and Lot 
No. 3; On the South by: Lot No. 3; On the West by: Lot 
No. 3, Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Lot No. 5.
