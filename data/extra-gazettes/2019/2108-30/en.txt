No. 2108/30 – TUESDAY, JANUARY 29, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The Allotments of below mentioned lands depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. KE/GLG/2017/364 of 04.05.2018, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Arandara in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No. 64A, Arandara in the Minor Division of
Kandupita Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Galigamuwa in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mailands Watta – Extent: 3.3025 Hectares
North: Lots C and B; East: Lots C, D and E; South: Lot H; West:
Koswatta Kumbura.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Extent: 0.0133 Hectare
North: Lot C; East, South and West: Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Extent: 0.1658 Hectare
North: Weralugolla Watta and Lot G; East: Weralugolla Watta and
Lot G; South: Lots H, A. E, F and D; West: Lots A. E, F and D.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Extent: 0.0679 Hectare
North: Lot A; East: Lots C and F; South: Lots F, E and A; West:
Lot A.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Extent: 0.0368 Hectare
North: Lots D and F; East: Lots F and C; South: Lot A; West:
Lots A and D.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Mailands Watta – Extent: 0.4022 Hectare
North: Lots D and C; East: Lot C; South: Lot E; West:
Lots E and D.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Mailands Watta – Extent: 0.4063 Hectare
North: Werallugolla Watta and Milands Watta; East and South:
Lot H; West: Lot C.
08. Lot ‘H’ – Extent: 0.1721 Hectare
North: Koswatta Kumbura, Lots A, C and G and Milands Watta;
East: Lot J; South: Lots J, K and Atala Watta Batupitiya Division;
West: Atala Watta Batupitiya Division, Road and Koswatta
Kumbura.
09. Lot ‘J’ – Milands Watta – Extent: 0.9940 Hectare
North: Lot H and Mailands Watta; East: Mailands Watta; South:
Mailands Watta and Atala Watta Batupitiya Division; West: Atala
Watta Batupitiya Division, Road and Lots N, K and H.
10. Lot ‘K’ – Extent: 0.0055 Hectare
North: Atala Watta Batupitiya Division, Lots H and J; East: Lot J
and Atala Watta Batupitiya Division; South: Atala Watta Batupitiya
Division and Road; West: Atala Watta Batupitiya Division.
