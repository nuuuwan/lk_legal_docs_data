PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 11.03.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardene, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under
Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the
land described below is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0029 Hectare depicted
as Lot No. 230 in the Cadastral Plan No. 520210 dated 12.06.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Laxapathiya (Ward No. 01, Laxapathiya within the Municipal Council
limit of Moratuwa) in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 550A of
Laxapathiya South in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Moratuwa
in Colombo District of Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 230 – Extent: 0.0029 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 229 and 91; East: Lot Nos. 99 and 234; South:
Lot Nos. 234 and 231; West: Lot Nos. 231 and 229.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardene,
Minister of Lands.
