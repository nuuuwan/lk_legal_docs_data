FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  KANTHALE  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Kanthale pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent Group 02 which Dehiwala Gurunnanselage George Camilus
Costa,  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
return member  of Independent Group 02 who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group 02 has communicated his decision to nominate
Hitihamu Mudiyanselage Muthubanda to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sangarapillai Suthakaran, Returning Officer of the Kanthale pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Hitihamu
Mudiyanselage Muthubanda was selected as the member of the Kanthale pradeshiya Sabha.
S. S
UTHAKARAN ,
Returning Officer,
Kanthale pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Trincomalee,
10th June, 2019.
