THE  UTTAMA  SEV A  PADAKKAMA - 2017
IT is hereby notified that HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka and the Commander
in Chief of the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to award the Uttama Seva Padakkama for the years Two Thousand
Seventeen in terms of regulation four of the Presidential Warrant instituting, constituting and creating the Uttama Seva
Padakkama published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 156/5 of 01st September, 1981 to the persons named in
the schedule hereto.

H
EMASIRI FERNANDO,
Secretary,
Ministry of Defence.
Colombo,
March 2019.
