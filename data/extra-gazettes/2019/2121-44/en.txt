BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff Hakeem, the

Act, in respect of the S.L.T. Campus (Pvt) Ltd., a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007
and bearing registration number PV 101137, do by this Order, amend the Schedule II to the Order made under the said Act, and
published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2105/31 of January 09, 2019 by the addition immediately after the entry "Bachelor of
Science Honours in Engineering in Information and Communication Engineering" appearing in Column I and it's corresponding
entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of th e Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto.
