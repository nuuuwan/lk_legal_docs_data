No. 2105/40 –  THURSDAY, JANUARY,  10,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
My No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/14. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/251. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, That the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
Servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The land allotment called Kulaielanthai in total extent 
of about 3.0266 Hectares depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan 
No. MN 780 and prepared by Surveyor General situated 
in the village called Nadukkuda, in the Grama Niladari 
Division No. 54 of Tullukkudiyiruppu, in the Divisional 
Secretary's division of Mannar Town of Mannar district 
in Northern Province: 
     
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Kulaielanthai; Present use of land : 
Bare land; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: -; 
Extent : 3.0266  Hectares.  
 
 
The dates and the time of the inquiries; 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 a.m. on the 15th day of 
February 2019 at Mannar Town Divisional Secretariat. 
 
And further you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 08th day of 
February 2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for the compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 K. SIVASAMBU, 
 Divisional Secretary , 
 Mannar Town Division, 
 Mannar. 
 
 
Divisional  Secretariat of Mannar Town, 
On 07th January, 2019.
