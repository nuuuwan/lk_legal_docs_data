The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KURUNEGALA
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   return  member of  Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Jipri
Thikrulla the member belonged, was required by me under Section   66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon
returned  member if  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the
Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)
days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mohamad Munas Mohomad Museen to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer  of  the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Mohamad Munas Mohomad Museen was selected as the member of the Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE D ILIP N ISHANTHA ,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kurunegala,
02nd April, 2019.
