THE   AMENDMENT   TO   THE   GAZETTE   NOTIFICATION
THE following Section (D) shall be included as an amendment to the 03 : 07 definition for the Trained Pirivena Teacher in
Part I of the Paragraph 3, under the regulations in the Gazette Notification No. 1113/12 and Tuesday the 4th of January,
2000, issued by the subject Minister under the Sections 26, 27 and 33 in the Pirivena Education Act, No. 64, 1979.
AKILA VIRAJ KARIY AWASAM,
Minister of Education.
At the Ministry of Education,
Located at Isurupaya, Pelawatta,
Battaramulla,
14th October, 2018.
D) The pass obtained at the final examination held by the Commissioner General of Examination after not less
than 3 years of training undergone at Wenamulla, Pirivena Institute established with the approval of the Ministry of Education
under 13(D) Section of the Pirivena Education Act, No. 64, 1979 will be a qualification to be appointed as a Pirivena
Teacher of Grade I in Class 3 of Pirivena Teacher Service.
