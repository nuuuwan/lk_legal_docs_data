PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of
the Southern Province Provincial Council by reason of Resignation of Mr. Arachchige Krishantha Pushpakumara
Weerasinghe ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
former member Arachchige Krishantha Pushpakumara Weerasinghe belonged was required by the Election Commission
under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Gardiyes Deneththi to
fill such vacancy ;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Gardiyes Deneththi, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Galle in respect of which such vacancy occured.
