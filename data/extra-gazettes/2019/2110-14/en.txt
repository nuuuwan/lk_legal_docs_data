The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   HANGURANKETHA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the return member of Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of
Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognized political party to which Koswaththe
Mankada Gedara Muthubanda the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the local authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of
thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Alawathupitiye Gedara Tikiri Banda to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pulasthi Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Alawathupitiye
Gedara Tikiri Banda was selected as the member of the Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
ULASTHI  P ATHIRANA ,
Returning Officer,
Hanguranketha Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
11th February, 2019.
