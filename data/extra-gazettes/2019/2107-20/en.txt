THE   LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                         My Ref. No. : GM/DS/HW/2015/2/2/1/133.
Land Ministry Reference No.:4-3/8/2016/HA/218.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964  that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/ servitude which is required for
a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in Total Extent of about 0.0152 Hectare
depicted as Lot No.1 in the P .P .GAM.No.4109 dated 14.11.2018
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys - Gampaha
District situated in the Village called Parakandeniya, in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. 244B of Parakandeniya North,
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Gampaha of  Gampaha
District in Western Province and morefully described as
follows.
    G 29298 - 17 (2019/01)
1. Lot No. 1
Name of the land: Millagahalanda; Discription of the Land:
Garden, A permanent building; Claimant: A.D.K.Dharmasiri,
No.262, Parakandeniya, Imbulgoda;; Extent: 0.0152 Hectare.
   All Persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Gampaha Divisional Secretariat on the 08th day of May
2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 29th day of  April, 2019
the nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land/servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
 K.A.S.LAKSHREENI GUNATHILAKE,
