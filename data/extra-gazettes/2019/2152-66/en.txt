THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby give declare that under Sub-Section (1) of section
5 of the Land Acquisition Act that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the Provision
of the Land Acquisition Act.
Boundaries of the Following name Lot of land Containing in extent
about 3.0122 Hectares marked as lot "A" depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. MR/AKR/2018/389 dated 04.04.2019 prepared by the
Surveyor - General, situated in the village of  Diganahena in the
Weligam Korale North Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Akuressa in the District of  Matara in the Southern
Province and bounded  as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  - Aluthhena - Extent:  3.0122 Hectares
North:Aluthhena Claimed by Namunukula plantation and Road;
East: Aluthhena Claimed by Namunukula plantation and Road
(Pradeshiya Saba); South: Aluthhena Claimed by Namunukula
plantation and Road; West: Road and Aluthhena Claimed by
Namunukula plantation and Road.

Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
