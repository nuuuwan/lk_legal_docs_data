PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
G 31635  - 17  (2019/12) 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
My Ref No: DS/GFG/L&L/05/133                                                                   
Ministry of Lands & Land Development Ref No:                       
4-3/16/2018/UD/457  
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section(1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land in total extent of 0.0029 
hectare  depicted in sheet No. 30 of Block No. 02 of 
Cadastral Map No. 810024 as lot Nos. 1077,1078 
prepared by Survey General situated in village of 
Deddugoda in Grama Niladhari Division No. 101 A, 
Deddugoda South, within  Municipal Council limit of 
Galle in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Four Gravets 
Galle in District of Galle , Southern Province.  The names 
and extents of above named lots are as follows :  
 
1. Lot No. : 1077 
Name of the land : Mahamoragodawaththa,; 
assessment number - not known; Description: Home 
garden,  a part of a boundary wall ; Claimant : State; 
Original Claimant : D.P.Y. Gamage; Extent : about 
0.0015 hectare. 
 
2. Lot No.: 1078 
Name of the land :  Mahamoragodawaththa, assessment 
number – 118/4, Hirimbura cross road; Description: 
Home garden, a part of a boundary wall; Claimant : 
State; Original Claimant : Batapola Gamage Udaya 
Kasun; Extent : about 0.0014 hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ servitude 
on which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat – Galle Four 
Gravets on 23.12.2019 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before  16.12.2019,  nature of their interests in the land/ 
lands, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.   
 
 I. HIMALI RATHNAWEERA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Galle Four Gravets. 
 
At Divisional Secretary’s Office,  
Galle - Four Gravets. 
06th December 2019
