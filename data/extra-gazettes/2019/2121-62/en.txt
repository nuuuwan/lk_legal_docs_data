The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VADAMARADCHI  SOUTH
WEST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Rasaiya Sivarani the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide
upon  returned  member if Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Selvarathinam Mallikathevi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendram Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Vadamaradchi South Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Selvarathinam Mallikathevi  was selected as the member of the Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
03rd May, 2019.
