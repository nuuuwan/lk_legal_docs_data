1A – G 29407– 16(02/2019)
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The following allotments of lands is situated  at Village of
Pitabaddara in Divisional Secretary's Division of Pitabaddara in the
District of Matara Southern Province and marked in Surveyor  General
Plan No. F.V .P.6,  Survey Requisition No. MR/PTB/2017 /668 and
date of 26/04/2017 containing in Extent 2.1013 Hectares.
01. Lot 'A' – Tannahena Waththa Land – Extent: 0.6858
Hectare
North: Canal; East and South: Road; West: Tannahena Waththa.
Lot 'B' – Tannahena Waththa Land – Extent: 1.4155  Hectares
North: Road, M. Rajendra claimed, Tannahena Waththa and
Namunukula Estate; East: Korala Hewage Darmadasa and others
claimed  Tannahena Waththa; South: Korala Hewage Darmadasa and
others claimed, Namunukula Estate and Tannahena Waththa; West:
Namunukula Estate and Tannahena Waththa.
