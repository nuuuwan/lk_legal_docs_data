 EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
STICKER   AND   STICKER   MANAGEMENT   SYSTEM
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance, by virtue of the
power vested in him under Section 32 read  with Section 8 and Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), do by
this Order prescribe that the rules cited as the Excise (security features) Rules 2019 published in the Excise Notification
No. 04/2019 of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2128/30 dated June 20, 2019, applied for an importer or a licensed
manufacturer of an excisable artricle referred to in Schedule II of the aforesaid Gazette, was passed by Parliament on
October 23, 2019.
A. R. D
ESAPRIYA,
Secretary (Acting),
Ministry of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
30th October, 2019.
