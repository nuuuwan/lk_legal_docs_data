No. 2110/15 – WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 13, 2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in extent of about 0.1074 Hectare, depicted
in the Survey Plan No. MR/THG/2017/26 and dated 22.02.2017,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Horonduwa in the Minor Division of Gangabada Pattuwa South in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Thihagoda in Matara District
in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thennahana alias Pathinagoda Kandahena and
Ilukhene Godella alias Alhena – Extent: 0.1074 Hectare
North: Road; East: Road, claimant unidentified Thennahena
alias Pathinagoda Kandahena and Ilukhene Godella alias Alhena and
G . A. Gunasekara and Thennahena alias Pathinagoda Kandahena and
Ilukhene Godella alias Alhena claimed by R. T. Kani Elisa Gunasekara;
South: Claimant unidentified Thennahena alias Pathinagoda
Kandahena and Ilukhene Godella alias Alhena and G . A. Gunasekara
and Thennahena alias Pathinagoda Kandahena and Ilukhene Godella
alias Alhena claimed by R. T. Kani Elisa Gunasekara; West:
Thennahena alias Pathinagoda Kandahena and Ilukhene Godella alias
Alhena claimed by R. T. Kani Elisa Gunasekara and Road.
