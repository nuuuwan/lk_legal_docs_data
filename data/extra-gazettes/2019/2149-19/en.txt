Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Institute of Valuers of Sri Lanka (Membership Fee) Regulations No. 01
of 2019.
2. The application fee, entrance fee, annual membership fee and the renewal fee payable in respect of the types of
membership of the Institute of Valuers of Sri Lanka specified in Column I, shall be as specified in the corresponding
entries in Colum II, Column III, Column IV and Column V respectively, in the Schedule hereto.
