FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NATTANDIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  not attending for
Council meetings period of three months without permission  ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to  which Warnakula
Adiththa Arshaneela Iththa Thimasha Subhodini Loach  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)
of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
and is not  disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected
as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political Party has communicated his decision to nominate
Poruthotage Elanrose Fernando  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Nagalingam Regan , Returning Officer  of  the  Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in
terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that  Poruthotage
Elanrose Fernando  was selected as the member of the Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
N. R
EGAN,
Returning Officer,
Nattandiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Puttalam,
25th July, 2019.
