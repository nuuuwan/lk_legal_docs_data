THE ACT ON ACQUISITION OF LAND 
(CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under  Section 7 
 
My Reference Number : HANGU/3/2/1/108.  
Reference Number of the Land and Parliamentary 
Reforms  : 4-3/9/2013/WS/479. 
 
I, hereby inform that the Government intends to acquire the 
dependence of the following land for the purpose of 
building the Brake Pressure Tank No. 1A of the 
Rikillagaskada Water Supply Scheme of the Rikillagaskada 
Water Supply Project in the Nuwaraeliya District Cluster 
Water Supply Project, under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act.   
 
Description of the land dependence to be acquired : 
 
Lot No. 01 
 Lot Number 01 of Plan No: N 3291 of Surveyor General. 
 
Name of the Land: Rahathungoda Watta (Rutland Division); 
Description: Open barren land. There is a brake pressure 
tank; Claimant: Janatha Estate Developments Board,                   
No. 55/75, Vauxhall Lane, Colombo 2; Extent: 0.0173 
Hectare. 
 
 All those who make claims or other connections to the 
above-named land, must be present before me by themselves 
or by the persons who have been authorized in writing at the 
Hanguranketha Divisional Secretariat Office  at 1.00 p.m. on 
the day of 21.08. 2019. 
 
 Further, they must communicate to me in duplicate  
the nature of their title / ownership to this land, the details 
of claiming for compensation for this land,  the amount 
claimed,  and the method of calculating such amount , 
before 14.08.2019. The deeds for claims have to be 
forwarded to at the interview.      
  
 I.M. S
ENANAYAKA, 
        Divisional Secretary, 
    Hanguranketha . 
 
At the office of the Divisional Secretary, 
Hanguranketha.
