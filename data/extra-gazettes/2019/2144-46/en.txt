ACT  OF  CEYLON   ELECTRICITY   BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
The Order under Section 57
I,  Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Power, Energy & Business Development do hereby approve the proposal of Land
Acquisition described in sub schedule here which is necessary to Ceylon Electricity Board by virture of  power vested to
me in Section 57 of Act of Ceylon Electricity Board bearing No. 17 of 1969.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Power, Energy & Business Development.
07th October, 2019.
SUB SCHEDULE
The whole land extent of about 2.5 hectares bounded as below shown in layout No. CEB/MOGL/LA/RAR/01
and dated 20.08.2019 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board situated in Grama Niladhari Division of Galatha bearing
No 1185 in Divisional Secretariat of Udapalatha in Kandy District, Central Province in Sri Lanka.
North : Main Road & Ela ;
East : Ela & Atabagewatta ;
West : Atabagewatta  & Road ;
South : Atabagewatta.
