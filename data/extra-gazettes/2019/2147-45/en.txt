PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
PRESIDENTIAL   ELECTIONS  ACT,   No.  15  OF  1981
Voting by Post
01. The following are the days of postal voting in terms of Section 23 of the Presidential Elections Act, No. 15 of 1981
and the Second Schedule to that Act.
31 October 2019
01 November 2019
04 November 2019
05 November 2019
07 November 2019
02. In the case of the office, unit or institution of each Certifying Officer listed on the list of postal voters
certified by the Returning Officers of each electoral district on 15.10.2019 ;
The date or dates mentioned in the first paragraph above shall mean the poll date or dates of the officers and
employees who are deemed to be postal voters and ;
The place where the poll is taken place is a polling station.
