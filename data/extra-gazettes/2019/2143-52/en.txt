1ST AMENDMENT
Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Surveyors’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2103/2 dated 24th of
December 2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended as follows and these amended provisions will be
made effective from 26th of February 2019, the date on which the Cabinet of Ministers made the policy decision.
