1A  G  30386— 27  (06/2019)
NOTICE  OF  APPROV AL  FOR  RECTIFICATION   OF  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE
NOTIFICATION
NOTICE  is given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I, Patali Champika
Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8H(1) of
the Urban Development Authority Law No. 4 of 1982 as amended, hereby approve the rectification in the Sinhala version
of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification bearing No. 1396/7 dated 7th June, 2005.
Primary Residential zone under the specified Land uses zones category set forth in Section 10.4.1.1(xv) of the
Development Plan Regulation for Kalutara shall be rectified to provide for a floor area not exceeding 250 sq. Meters.
Mixed Residential zone under the specified Land uses zones category set forth in Section 10.4.2.1(ix) of the
Development Plan Regulation for Kalutara shall be rectified to provide for a floor area not exceeding 500 Sq. Meters.
This rectification shall be deemed to have come into operation on 7th June, 2005 and accordingly, every act
done or omitted to done and every order made in pursuance of any such provision of regulation shall be deemed to have
been valid and effectual as if the provisions of the said regulation had been in operation from the dates hereinbefore
mentioned.
