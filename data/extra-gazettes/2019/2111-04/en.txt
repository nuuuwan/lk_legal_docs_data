THE  PRINTING  SERVICE MINUTE  OF  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING  -
AMENDMENT  No.  04
THE Public Service Commission has ordered to amend the Code of Printing Service Minutes of Department of Government
Printing published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1816/12 dated 26th June 2013 amended by the
Extraordinary Gazette  Notification No. 1892/40 dated 12th December 2014, No. 1893/39 dated 19th December 2014
and No. 1981/43 dated 23rd August 2014 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, as follows.
By order of the Public Service Commission
M. A. B. DAYA SENARATH,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
On this 05th day of June, 2018.
(i) Paragraph 15.4 will be omitted and substituted the new paragraph as mentioned below :
15.4 - Up to a period of 07 years, since the approval of this service minute, out of the applicants who are
selected for the Post of Letter Press Operator, Print Finisher, Pre Print Planner, Printing Editor and Computer
Type Setter and Printing Designer, for the Print Finishers that mentioned under the 9th paragraph of the
constitution, selected from the internal applicants, getting through a Book Binding Course with a duration not
less than 06 months conducted by the Sri Lanka Institute of Printing, and for the selected Letter Press Machine
Operators, Pre Print Planner, Printing Editor and Computer Type Setter and Printing Designer getting through
a practical test conducted by the Department of Government Printing after one year from the date they are
selected for the posts, are adequate requirements for them to be appointed for the applicant who are selected
for the Offset Litho Printing Machine Operators, from the internal applicants, it is adequate to get through a
course on Lithography with a duration not less than 06 months, conducted by the Sri Lanka Institute of Printing
for them to be appointed for permanent posts. After 07 years from the date of approval of this constitution, in
case of the NVQ Level 5 will be existed, the provisons contained under Section 10.3 of this constitution must
be applicable.
