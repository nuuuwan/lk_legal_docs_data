EXCISE (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that :-
1. (a) The Excise Duty on every article specified in Column III of the Schedule I hereto shall be payable at the rate
specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule I;
(b) The concessionary Excise Duty on excisable articles specified in Column II of the Schedule II hereto shall be
payable at the rate specified in Column III of the Schedule II wherever applicable; and
(c) Wherever two rates are specified in Column IV of the Schedule I and Column III of the schedule II hereto, the
applicable rate for the purpose of calculating the Excise Duty payable shall be the rate which yields the higher
amount payable as Excise Duty.
2. The orders made under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No.13 of 1989 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette  Notifications  No.2044/32 of 09.11.2017, No.2045/33 of 15.11.2017, No.2046/14 of
22.11.2017, No.2047/25 of 28.11.2017,  No.2058/33 of 15.02.2018, No.2066/40 of 12.04.2018, No.2068/9 of
24.04.2018, No.2074/39 of 08.06.2018, No. 2080/31 of 17.07.2018, No. 2082/10 of 31.07.2018, No. 2082/11
of 31.07.2018, No. 2083/2 of 06.08.2018, No. 2089/18 of 17.09.2018, No. 2092/5 of 08.10.2018, No. 2095/18
of 01.11.2018, No. 2100/3 of 03.12.2018 and No. 2105/48 of 11.01.2019 are hereby rescinded.
3.  This Order shall come into effect from March 06, 2019.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01.
March 05, 2019.
