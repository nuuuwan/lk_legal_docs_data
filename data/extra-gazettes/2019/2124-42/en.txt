I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, by virtue of the powers vested in me
by Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act, (Chapter 393) do hereby once again enlarge the period of time up to 30th
June 2019 further considering the current role of the commission, for  rendition of the final report on completion of the
tasks of the  “Presidential Commission of Inquiry established by me on 31st January 2018 in terms of the provisions of
Section 2 of the aforesaid Act by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2056/41 dated 02nd February,
2018 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate and inquire into alleged irregularities in connection
with Sri  Lankan Airlines Ltd., Sri Lankan Catering Ltd. and Mihin Lanka (Pvt.) Ltd. during the period 01st January 2006 to
31st January, 2018”,  having enlarged the six month period ending 31st July 2018 granted for the said purpose by five
months i. e., up to 31st December 2018 by virtue of the powers vested in me by notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2082/3 dated 31st July 2018  and again up to 15th February 2019 by virtue of the powers vested in me
by notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2099/40 dated 30th November, 2018 and yet again up to 30th
May 2019 by virtue of powers vested in me by notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2109/18 dated
07th February, 2019.
Maithripala Sirisena,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
May 22, 2019.
