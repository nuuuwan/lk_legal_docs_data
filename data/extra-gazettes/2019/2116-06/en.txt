BANK  OF   CEYLON   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 397)
Order under Section 7 (2)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of Section 7 of the Bank of Ceylon Ordinance (Chapter 397),
I,  Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order determine that the paid- up capital of the Bank of Ceylon shall
be increased from Rupees Twenty Billion to Rupees Twenty  Five Billion.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance.
Colombo,
15th  March, 2019.
