PORTS   AND   AIRPORTS   DEVELOPMENT   LEVY   ACT,   No.  18  OF  2011
Order  under  Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act, No.
18 of 2011, I, Eran Wickramaratne, Acting Minister of Finance do hereby this Order amend the Schedule II of the Order
made in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2049/16 of December 13, 2017, by addition to the said  Schedule of
the articles specified in the Column III corresponding to the HS Headings and HS Codes specified in the Column I and II
of the Schedule hereto, effective from May 07, 2019.
E
RAN W ICKRAMARA TNE,
Acting Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
May 07, 2019
