Notice under Section 7
 Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/55.
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/9/2017/WS/189.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for
The Construction of Warapitiya Tambalapola Water Treatment
Plant and Intake of Greater Matale Water Supply Project of
National Water Supply and Drainage Board.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
The Allotments of land described in below in Plan No. Ma
1748, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of Surveys,
situated in Warapitiya village, Warapitiya Gramaniladhari
Division, in Sub Division of Matale Pallesiya Pattuwa in the
Rattota Divisional Secretariat Division in Matale District in
Central Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Tambalapola Hena/Land; Description: Open
Waste; Claimant: Claimant not Known; Extent: 0.0183  Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Tambalapola Hena/Land; Description: Open
Waste; Claimant: Mr. Sarath Ekanayake, Katudeniya,
Warapitiya Road, Thembilipola; Extent: 0.2002  Hectare.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of land: Tambalapola Hena/Land; Description: Open
Waste; Claimant: Claimant not  Known; Extent: 0.0216  Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Rattota on the
27.03.2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19.03.2019  the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
H. M. N. DINIPRIYA,
Divisional Secretariat,
Rattota.
Divisional Secretariat,
Rattota,
