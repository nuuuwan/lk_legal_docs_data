No. 2107/9 –  TUESDAY, JANUARY,  22,  2019 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
(Notice Under Section 7) 
 
Reference No.: 3/7/16/6. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2015/LO/183. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose :- 
 
      Description of the land or  servitude to be acquired : 
 
     Two (02) allotments of land called Wewathenna in 
total extent about 0.1099 Hectare shown as Lot Numbers 
561,562 in the Sheet No. 8 of Supplement No. 8 of the 
Final Village Plan No. 266 dated 28.11.2018 prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated at the Village of Amban 
Ella in Grama Niladhari Division No. 523 B 
Wewathenna, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Walapane in Nuwara Eliya District of the Central 
province and described as follows:- 
     
01. Lot No. 561 
Name of the Land: Wewathenna; Claimants: State; 
Original Claimants: National Housing Development 
Authority;  Land Use: Home Garden, Part of a Permanent 
Building; Extent : 0.0025 Hectare.  
 
02. Lot No. 562 
Name of the Land: Wewathenna; Claimants: State; 
Original Claimant: National Housing Development 
Authority;  Land Use: Home Garden, Water Tank & Two 
Permanent Buildings; Extent : 0.1074 Hectare.  
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Walapane Divisional Secretariat on 
the 28 day of February 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 14 day of February 2019 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
  
  
 I.M. Senanayaka, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Walapane. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Walapane. 
15th January, 2019.
