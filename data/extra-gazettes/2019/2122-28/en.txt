THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 4.5264 Hectares
depicted as Lots ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing No. R/EHL/2014/110
and dated 02.04.2014 prepared by the Surveyor  General, situated in
the Village of Beruwala (F.V .P . 702) in Kuruvita Korale Palle
Paththuwa Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Eheliyagoda in Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Sandarland Estate – Extent : 4.5264 Hectares
North: Sandarland Estate claimed by Pussellawa Plantation
Company; East: Sandarland Estate claimed by Pussellawa Plantation
Company and Jinadasa Hiripitiya and Road; South: Sandarland Estate
