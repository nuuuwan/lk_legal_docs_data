FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF  MEMBER  OF   THE   POLONNARUWA
MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of Polonnaruwa Municipal Council has  fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Mohamadu
Ibraheem Mohamadu Arfa the  member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned member of  United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under section
9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Jayasundara Korale Mudiyanse Ralahamilage Sujeewa Nishantha Gunathilaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Leelanath Wickrama Arachchi, Returning Officer  of  the  Polonnaruwa Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Jayasundara Korale Mudiyanse Ralahamilage Sujeewa Nishantha Gunathilaka was selected as the member of the Polonnaruwa
Municipal Council.
L
EELANA TH W ICKRAMA ARACHCHI ,
Returning Officer,
Polonnaruwa Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Polonnaruwa,
28th October, 2019.
