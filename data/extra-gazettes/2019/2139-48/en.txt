PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 04.09.2019
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. CO/
DSO/2016/587 dated 21.08.2017, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village called Gangodawila (within the Urban Council
Limits of Maharagama) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Maharagama in Colombo District in Western Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kajugahawatta – Extent: 0.2007 Hectare
North: Walauwatta Road; East: Canal; South: Lands bearing Assmt.
Nos. 62/14, 62/15, 62/7 and 62/4, Old Kesbewa Road; West: Lands
bearing the Assmt. Nos. 62/14, 62/15, 62/7, 62/4 and 52, Old Kesbewa
Road.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
