PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
FILLING  OF   A   V ACANCY   UNDER   PARAGRAPH   13(b)  OF   ARTICLE   99   OF   THE   CONSTITUTION
OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary General of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under Section 64(1) of the
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by the Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the
membership of the Eighth Parliament by reason of death of Mr. Gajadeera Vidhanearachchige Bans Chandrasiri, who was
declared elected as  a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District No. 8 - Matara under Section 62 of the aforesaid
Act.
AND WHEREAS the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed
by the Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph
13(b) of Article 99 of the Constitution.
AND WHEREAS the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return in
Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Manoj Prasanga Hewagampalage was
declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the said electoral district.
