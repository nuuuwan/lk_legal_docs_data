Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of  Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands are described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
Land Acquisition that Act.

demarcation given  as follows:
