FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA   UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which
Kumarasamy Tharumarasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon  returned  member if  Sri Lanka Freedom Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Visvanathan Vithiyananthan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer  of  the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that
Visvanathan Vithiyananathan was selected as the member of the Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
11th April, 2019.
