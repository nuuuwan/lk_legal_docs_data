THE   VISHISTA   SEWA   VIBHUSHANAYA
 
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, and Commander
- in - Chief of the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to confer the Vishista Sewa Vibhushanaya at an Investiture to
be held at the Presidential Secretariat on the Twenty Second day of May  in the year Two Thousand and Nineteen on the
officers named in the Schedule hereto in terms of Clause Four of the Presidential Warrant instituting the Vishista Sewa
Vibhushanaya published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.156/5 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka issued on
the First day of September in the year One Thousand Nine Hundred and Eighty one.
                                                                                           By His Excellency’s command,
U
DAYA R. SENEVIRA TNE,
Secretary to the President.
 
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
16th May 2019.
