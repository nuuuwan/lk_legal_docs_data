FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Thangavel Jegatheeshwaran the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance,
to decide  upon return member of  Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period
of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Joseph Jenson to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer of the Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Joseph
Jenson was selected as the member of the Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Kilinochchi,
10th June, 2019.
