1A – G 29560 – 21(03/2019)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands & Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The  allotment of  land depicted in the Advance Tracing
No.  KU/ALW 2015/212 dated 31.12.2015 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village called Wennoruva in the Grama
Niladhari Division, No. 976 of Wennoruwa; West of Dambadeni
Udukaha Korale East Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Alawwa in Kurunegala District of  North Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –Wennoruwa Muthugala Watta – Extent: About
1.2463 Hectare
North: Road (R. D. A.); East: Road; South: Road, Wennoruwa
Watta claimed by E. P . D. Ranasinghe and some others; West:
Wilgamuwa watta claimed by M. S. Wijethilake  and some others
and road (R. D. A.).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands & Parliamentary Reforms.
