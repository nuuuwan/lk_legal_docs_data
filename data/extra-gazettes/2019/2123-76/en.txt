THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of Land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Ma/YTT/2018/179 dated 24.08.2018 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the Village called Deewilla in the Grama Niladhari
Division Deewilla North (E 337 C), in the Minor Division Asgiriya
Pallesiya pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Yatawatta
of Matale District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘B’ – Deewilla Watta – Extent:  0.1144 Hectare
North: Road (P.S.) and Lot A; East: Lot A and Lot No. 60 in the
P.P.Ma. 670; South: Lot No. 60 and 56 in the P.P.Ma. 670; West: Lot
No. 56 in the P.P.Ma. 670 and Road (P .S.).
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
