Notification
IT is hereby notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1)
of Article 43 and paragraph (1)(a) of Article 46 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2103/33 of December 28, 2018 and amended by Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2105/39 of January 10, 2019, is further amended with effect from January 11, 2019 in the
manner set out in the Schedule below:
