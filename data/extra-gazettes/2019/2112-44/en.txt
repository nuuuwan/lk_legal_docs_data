(CHAPTER  460)
Notice Under Section 7
Land Mini's Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2013/WS/461.
My Ref. No.: 9/3/5/7.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section (7) of
Land Acquisition  Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
An allotment of land called Lewakandawaththa in total
extent of 0.1012 Hectare depicted in the Preliminary Plan No.
Ma. 1716 prepared by Surveyor General situated in the village
called Punchilewakandawaththa in the Grama Niladhari
Division in Northern Matale Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Divisiion of Yatawatta in Matale District in the
Central province and morefully described as follows.
Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Lewakandawaththa; Description: For
the construction of Mahalewakanda water tank under the
project of Greater Matale Water Supply; Claimant:
Hapukotuwe Gedara Gisban Hapukotuwa; Total Extent: 0.1012
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Yatawatta Divisional Secretariat on
03.04.2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 21.03.2019 the nature
of their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for
the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should
be produced before me at the inquiry.
D. R. K. HENNAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Yatawatta.
