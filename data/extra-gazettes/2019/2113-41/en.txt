The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   PORATIVPATTU
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return  member of  Porativpattu Pradeshiya Sabha  has  fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Leader of  the Independent group 2, to which V . Ayushman the Member belonged, was
required by me under Section   66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member if  Independent
group 2  who belongs to such Independent Group and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections
Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the leader  of the aforesaid Independent Group has communicated his decision to nominate
N. Indranathan   to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendra  Saseelan, Returning Officer  of  the Porativpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Selection 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that N. Indranathan
was selected as the member of the Porativpattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Porativpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Batticaloa,
06th March, 2019.
