The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of the Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rajamanikkam Asok Kumar of the
Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member
of the United Progressive Alliance (Democratic Peoples Front at present).
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATENNE ,
Returning Officer,
Godakawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Ratnapura
29th July, 2019.
