Mr. L. P. Jayasundara,
No. 14/3, Isurupura,
Kesbewa,
Piliyandala.
and
Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation,
No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha,
Welikada,
Rajagiriya.
Statement of matter in dispute
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. L. P. Jayasundara who had worked in the Sri
Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation had
been caused injustice by,
1. Not been paid arrears of salary from 07.10.1993 to
06.05.1996 relevant to the post of special Grade
Clerks, to which he was appointed with effect from
07.10.1993.
2. Not been paid an allowance for the loan collection
duties carried out in addition to the duties assigned
to him.
3. The salary revision not been carried out property
when he was promoted to the post of Senior
Administration Officer from 01.01.2016.
4. Not been appointed to the post of Manager
(Admminstration) of the Equipment Division even
though he had carried out duties of the post from
20.10.2014.
and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner General of Labour
Colombo this 24th day of June, 2019.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
