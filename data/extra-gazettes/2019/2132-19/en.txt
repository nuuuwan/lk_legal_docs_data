IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has, appointed Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe, Secretary to the  Cabinet of
Ministers to act in the post of  Secretary to the  President with effect from 16.07.2019 until Mr. Udaya R. Seneviratne, Secretary
to the President returns to the Island in terms of the  Provisions of Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka.
By order of His Excellency,
ESALA WEERAKOON,
 Senior Additional Secretary to the President,
for Secretary to the President.
