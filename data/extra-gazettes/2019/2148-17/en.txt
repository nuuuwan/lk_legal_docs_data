STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether  the below-mentioned 07 employees including Mr. Thilak Suminda had been caused injustice by the termination of their employment
in the acquisition of Central Investment and Finance PLC by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka and action taken to close down the company after
settlement of dues and if so, to what reliefs they are entitled:
01. Mr. Thilak Suminda,
02. Mrs. D. M. Dhammika Dissanayaka,
03. Miss. Udani Pabasara Mathagaweera,
04. Mr. M. Nishantha,
05. Mrs. K. A. Nilukshi Chamari,
06. Mrs. Renuka Dahanayaka,
07. Mr. Samantha Nandiratne.
