EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kalmunai Municipal Council
I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of the Kalmunai Municipal Council, do hereby declare under
Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kaththamuththu Ganesh of the
Kalmunai Municipal Council has vacated his office  of that  Municipal Council as he has ceased to be a member of the
Tamil United Liberation Front.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Election Office,
Ampara,
28th November, 2019.
