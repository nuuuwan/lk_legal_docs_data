Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  JANITORIAL  SERVICE  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations publilshed in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members
of the Wages Board for the Janitorial Service Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under

December 2018.
