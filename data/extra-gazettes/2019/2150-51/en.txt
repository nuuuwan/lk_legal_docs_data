MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
AMENDMENT
KARIYAKSHAMA   SEV A    VIBHUSHANAYA
Orders
REGULATIONS mentioned in the following paragraphs, numbered as Fourthly, Seventhly, Eighthly, Ninthly, Eleventhly,
Twelfthly, Thirteenthly, Fourteenthly and Fifteenthly : published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary Number
412/20 dated 01.08.1986, of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka are hereby amended by His Excellency the
President :
Fourth Paragraph : It is ordained that the period of service requisite to qualify for the Decoration shall be an
aggregate of twenty five years Efficient Commissioned Service on the Active Strength of the
Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force, and the ranks shall be Brigadier and above, subject to such
conditions as may be laid down in the regulations hereinafter mentioned, provided that the
period of service so tendered previously to reward any other medal for Efficient Service in the
Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force, may be reckoned to reward the Kariyakshama Seva
Vibhushanaya.

Seventh Paragraph : It is ordained that service shall be reckoned as qualifying service for the Decoration if such
service has already been reckoned for the Efficiency Medal, or, the Kariyakshama Seva
Padakkama.
Eigth Paragraph : It is ordained that Officers who have the necessary qualifying service shall not be awarded the
Decoration unless they are recommended by the Commandant of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer
Force in which the period of service requisite to qualify for the Decoration is completed ; and,
finally approved by the Commander of the Sri Lanka Army, as efficient and thoroughly capable
Officers deserving in every way for the Decoration.
Ninth Paragraph : It is ordained that a recipient of this medal shall not be eligible for a second conferment or
conferment of clasp during his lifetime.
Eleventh Paragraph : It is ordained that the reproductions of the Decoration in miniature, which may be worn on
certain occasions by those upon whom the Decoration is conferred, shall be half the size of the
Kariyakshama Seva Vibhushanaya.
Twelfth Paragraph : It is ordained that sealed patterns of the Kariyakshama Seva Vibhushana, and its miniatures, are
held by the appropriate authority at the Sri Lanka Army Headquarters.
Thirteenth Paragraph : It is ordained that it shall be competent for me, my elected successors, or by and order under
my Seal and Signature, or on a recommendation to that effect by, or, through the Secretary
Defence to cancel, and annul the award of the Decoration in respect of any person ; and that
thereupon his name is struck off the register where such awards are recorded ; but it shall be
competent for me or one of my elected successors to restore the Decoration when such
recommendation has been withdrawn.
Fourteenth Paragraph : It is ordained that the Decoration shall be conferred under such regulation as to grant forfeiture,
restoration, and other matters in amplification of this my Proclamation as may with my approval
signified through the Secretary Defence be issued from time to time by the Commander of the
Sri Lanka Army.
Fifteenth Paragraph : I reserve to myself and my elected Successors the full power of annulling, abrogating,
augmenting, interpreting or dispensing with these rules and regulations, or any part thereof by
a Notification under my Seal and Signature, or under my Command.
                                                                                              By His Excellency’s Command,
U
DAY A R. S ENEVIRATNE,
Secretary to the President.
Office of Secretary to the President,
Colombo,
11th November, 2019.

PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRA TIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA   -   21.11.2019
AMENDMENT
KARIYAKSHAMA   SEV A    PADAKKAMA
Orders
REGULATIONS mentioned in the following paragraphs, numbered as Fourthly, Fifthly, Sixthly, Seventhly, Eighthly, Ninthly,
Tenthly, Eleventhly, Twelfthly and Thirteenthly published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary Number 412/20
dated 01.08.1986, of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka pertaining to Kariyakshama Seva Padakkama are
hereby amended.
Whereas I am desirous of rewarding long, efficient loyal service, and exemplary conduct of proven capacity by
Commissioned Officers, Warrant Officers, Non-Commissioned Officers and Men of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force.
Fourth Paragraph : It is ordained that the Medal shall be awarded to Commissioned Officers, Warrant Officers,
Non-Commissioned Officers and Men of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force who have
completed an aggregate of eighteen years of Efficient Service on the Active list of the Sri
Lanka Army V olunteer Force subject to such conditions as may be laid down in the regulations
hereinafter mentioned and the medal shall be awarded to those who do not receive any other
medal for the efficient service on active list of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force for the
period of service concerned and such service is not counted to award any other medal.
Fifth Paragraph : It is ordained that the award of the Medal to such Commissioned Officer, Warrant Officers,
Non-Commissioned Officers and Men of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force should be
recommended by the Commanding Officer of the Unit in which the period of service requisite
to qualify for the Medal is completed ; and such recommendation is certified by the Colonel of
the Regiment and the Commandant of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force and finally approved
by Commander of Sri Lanka Army as efficient and thoroughly capable Commissioned Officers,
Warrant Officers, Non-Commissioned Officers or Men deserving in every way to award the
Medal.
Sixth Paragraph : It is ordained that Commissioned Officers, Warrant Officers, Non-Commissioned Officers
and Men on the Reserve of the Sri Lanka Army V olunteer Force are entitled to claim for this
Medal, if at, the time they were placed on the Reserve, they had such qualifying service requisite
for the Medal.
Seventh Paragraph : It is ordained that a recipient of this medal shall not be eligible for a second conferment or
conferment of clasp during his lifetime.
Eighth Paragraph : It is ordained that the Eighth regulation ordained previously to be null.
Ninthly Paragraph : It is ordained that sealed patterns of the Kariyakshama Seva Padakkama in miniature, which may
be worn on certain occasions by Commissioned Officers and Warrant Officers on whom the
Medal is conferred, shall be half the size of the Kariyakshama Seva Padakkama.
BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   MAITHRIPALA   SIRISENA,   PRESIDENT   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA
 Seal MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
