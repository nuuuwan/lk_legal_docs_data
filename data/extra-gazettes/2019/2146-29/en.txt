THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
ORDER UNDER SECTION 39A(1)
THE allotment specified in the Order No. 308 of 2014 made under
the Proviso (a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act
(Chapter 460) and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary
No. 1887/41 dated 07.11.2014 is vested absolutely in the State and
the possession of which has been taken for or on behalf of the State
under Para (a) of Section 40 of the aforesaid Act. But by the virtue of
powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 39(a) (inserted by
Act, No. 8 of 1979) of the Land Acquisition Act. I, Gayantha
Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
do hereby divest only the alltoment described in the Schedule I
below.
