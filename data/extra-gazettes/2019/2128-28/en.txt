THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotment of below mentioned land (Lot No. 1) in total extent
of about 0.3502 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KA/
MIL/2018/287 and dated 28.09.2018, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Kennanthudawa in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Millaniya in Kalutara District in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Jambugahawatta – Extent: 0.3502 Hectare
North: Road and Jambugahawatta claimed by Wijesinghe
Withanage Yasawathi; East: Canal; South: Muththettuwekumbura,
the claimant cannot be found and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West:
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha), Road, the rest of the land and Road.
