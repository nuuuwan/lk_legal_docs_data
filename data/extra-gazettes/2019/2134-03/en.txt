DEREQUISITION   ORDER   No. 2019/04/25/101/001
The  Co-operative  Societies (Special  Provisions)  Act,  No.  35  of  1970
ORDER UNDER SECTION 10 (4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me, by the Section 10 (4) of the Co-operative Societies (Special Provisions) Act, No.35
of 1970, I, Buddhika Pathirana, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Resettlement of Protracted Displaced Persons,
Co-operative Development and  V ocational  Training and Skills Development (Acting) do hereby derequisition the property
that had been requisitioned by the Gazette Notification No. 108/09 and dated on 26.04.1974 to conduct any business
activity of  the Yatiyanthota Multi- Purpose Co-operative Society Limited and Specified in the schedule below to the
owners with effect from 17 th day of July 2019.
BUDDHIKA  P ATHIRANA ,
Hon. Minister of Industry and Commerce,
Resettlement of Protracted Displaced Persons,
Co-operative Development and V ocational
Training and Skills Development ( Acting).

17th July, 2019.
