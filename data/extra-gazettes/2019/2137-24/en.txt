PROMOTION   AND   APPOINTMENT   APPROVED   BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Major General Lokugan Hewage
Shavendra Chandana Silva, WWV , RWP, RSP, VSV , USP, ndc, psc to the rank of Lieutenant General with effect from
August 18, 2019 and to appoint him under Article 61E.(1)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka Army, with effect from that date.
By His Excellency’s Command,
U
DAY A R. S ENEVIRATNE,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
August  19, 2019.
