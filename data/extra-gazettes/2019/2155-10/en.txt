The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   BUTTALA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1)(a)  OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  member of  Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of  Not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which
Herathmudiyanselage Chinthaka  Sampath Bandara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)(a) of
the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that No. 01 Pelwaththa of Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be
declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Indrasena Liyana Arachchi to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Indika Gayan Pathirana, Returning Officer  of  the  Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 66A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that Indrasena
Liyana Arachchi was elected as the member of the No. 01 Pelwaththa  of Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA G AYAN P ATHIRANA,
Returning Officer,
Buttala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Moneragala,
23rd December, 2019.
