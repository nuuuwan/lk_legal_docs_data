AND WHEREAS, Pasdunkorale Arachchige Pematilaka Esquire, Retired Auditor General and Lalith R. de Silva
Esquire, retired Secretary to Ministry, who are the members of the said Commission of Inquiry, have resigned their
offices;
AND WHEREAS, the need to fill the vacancies thus caused has been identified;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, do in
pursuance of the provisions of Section 3 of the Commissions of  Inquiry Act, by these presents, appoint W.K.K. Kumarasiri
Esquire, retired Secretary to Ministry and M.P. Dharmawardana Esquire, retired Deputy Auditor General to be
Commissioners of the Commission of  Inquiry constituted in terms of the Warrant above referred to reposing great trust
and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity.
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this 12th day of March,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
