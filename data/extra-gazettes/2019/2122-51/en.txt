ANTIQUITIES  ORDINANCE
Order
IN terms of power vested upon me as the Minister of Housing, Construction and Cultural Affairs, under the purview of
Section 39A of Subsection (1) in Antiquities (Amendment) Act, No. 24 of 1998 (188th Chapter), the members cited in
Schedule are appointed for Archaeological Advisory Committee with effect from 25.04.2019 to 24.04.2021 through the
Order.
With below mentioned appointments, the Archaeological Advisory Committees appointed through Special Gazettes
No. 2049/56 - 2017 and No. 2076/40 - 2018 issued on 14th December, 2017 and 22nd June, 2018 respectively is hereby
nullified.
S
AJITH  P REMADASA ,
Minister of Housing, Construction and Cultural Affairs.
At Ministry of Housing, Construction and Cultural Affairs,
23rd April, 2019.
