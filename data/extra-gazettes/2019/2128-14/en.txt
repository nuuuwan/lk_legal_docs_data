The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE  MAHARA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of   member of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not attending consecutive
meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, the recognized political party to which Wanni
Arachchige Susanthasiri the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  return member if Sri Lanka Freedom Party, who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section
9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty
(30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Wanni Arachchige Susanthasiri to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Mahara  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Wanni Arachchige Susanthasiri was selected as the member of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
18th June, 2019.
