THE  NOTICE  TO  RECTIFY  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST
REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA  BEARING  No. 2129/95  DATED  28TH  JUNE,  2019
I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vested in me under
the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended, do hereby rectify the Extraordinary Gazette Notification
bearing No. 2129/95 dated 28th June, 2019 (07-4554/8).
In the caption of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(PART)” Shall be included
immediately after “EMBILIPITIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA” and the word “AUTHORITY” (given in the second line)
shall be deleted.
The content of the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be rectified by inserting the word “(Part)”
immediately after “Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha” given in the fifth line thereof.
The word “had” in the fourth line of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be substituted with “have”.
The rectifications introduced to the upper part of the said Extraordinary Gazette  Notification shall be read as follows;
“NOTICE OF APPROVAL OF THE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR THE EMBILIPITIYA URBAN DEVELOPMENT AREA
COMPRISING OF EMBILIPITIYA  URBAN COUNCIL AND EMBILIPITIYA PRADESHIYA SABHA (PART) AREAS.”

“NOTICE is hereby given to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 8G of the
Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended from time to time that I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, the
Minister in charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development, by virtue of the powers vest ed in me under
Section 8F of the said Law, have approved the Development Plan on the 28th day of June, 2019 for the  embilipitiya
Urban Development Area comprising of the Embilipitiya Urban Council an Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  (Part) areas,
prepared under Section 8A of the said Law.”
PATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
07th November, 2019.
In the caption of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Part)” shall be included immediately
after “Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha” and the word “Authority” (given in the first line) shall be de leted.
In the content of the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification the word “(Amendment)”  in the second line

The rectification introduced to the latter part of the said Extraordinary Gazette Notification shall be read as follows;
“Approval of the Development Plan for the Embilipitiya Urban Development Area comprising of Embilipi tiya Urban

DR. JAGATH MUNASINGHE,
Chairman,
Urban Development Authority.

Notwithstanding the provisions contained in the antecedent Gazette Notification  bearing No. 2129/95 dated 28th June,

PATALI  CHAMPIKA  RANAWAKA,
