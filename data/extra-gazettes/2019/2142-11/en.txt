The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   MINUWANGODA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognized political party to which Abeysinghe
Liyanage Udara Rukmal Wijerathna the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid
ordinance, to decide  upon returned  member of United National Party who belongs to such party and is not disqualified
under Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a
period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Wijesuriya Arachchige Vishwantha Sadun Wijesuriya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer  of  the  Minuwangoda
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Wijesuriya Arachchige Vishwantha Sadun Wijesuriya  was selected as the member of the Minuwangoda
Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Gampaha,
23rd September, 2019.

Part IV (B) – GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA – 24.09.2019
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   V AVUNIYA  NORTH
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  return member of  V avuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress, the recognized political party to which
Sivaranshini Ragunesan the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1) of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon returned  member of Ahila Ilankai Thamil Congress who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared  selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has communicated his decision to
nominate Thavarani Muththaiya to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Malawenna Godage Lalith Ananda, Returning Officer  of  the  Vavuniya North Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Thavarani Muththaiya was selected as the member of the Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. G . L
ALITH ANANDA,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Vavuniya,
23rd September, 2019.
