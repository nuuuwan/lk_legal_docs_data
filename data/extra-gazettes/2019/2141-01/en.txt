THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
 
My No.: MN/DS/L/D1/AQ/WPP/02. 
Land  Ministry No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/239. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude  to be Acquired:: 
 
The Lands allotment called Olaittoduvaikani, 
Olaittoduvaiodai in total extent of about 3.3070 Hectares 
depicted as Lot No. 1, 2 and 3 in Plan No. MN 830 and 
prepared by Surveyor General situated in the village 
called Olaittoduvai in the Grama Niladari Division No. 60 
of Olaittoduvai, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Mannar Town of Mannar District in Nothern Province. 
 
 
Plan No. MN  830 
Lot No. 1, 2 and 3 
 
Name of the land: Olaitthoduvaikani, Olaitthoduvaiodai; 
Present use of the land: Garden  contain masonry well, 
channel, Palmyra trees; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Kalutota Investment and Leasing Limited, 
562/16, Nawala Road, Rajagiriya; Extent: 3.3070 
Hectares. 
 
 
The dates and the time of the inquiris: 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which servitude is to be acquired  are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.00 A. M. on the 10th day of   
October 2019 at Mannar Town Divisional Secretariat. 
 
 And further you are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or  before the 3rd day of 
October 2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for the compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the  amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.   gfg  
 K. SIVASAMBU,

                                                      Mannar Town Division, 
 Mannar.
