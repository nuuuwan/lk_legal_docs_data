Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends  to    acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose:
     Description of the Land to be acquired:
Lot Nos. 337 and 338 (Lots 02) allotments of land in Total
Extent of about 0.0137 Hectare, in F.V .P. No. 611, Supplement

on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of
Madampe in the Grama Niladhari Division of Madampe South

01. Lot No. 337
Name of land: Yagalagahadeniya alias Yagalagawadeniya;
Description: Road; Claimant: M.K.Ghnanawathi; Extent:

02. Lot No. 338

Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
Encroached by M.K.Ghnanawathi without leisure; Extent:

the 14.05.2019 the nature of their interests
 in the land, the

of the computation of such amount.
