LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference  No.: Miri/Dev. Sec. Land/3/2016/05.
Land Ministry Ref.No.: 4-3/2/2016/CEB/24.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government has intended to
acquire the undermentioned land/Servitude for a public
purpose.
Description of the Land/servitude to be acquired :
Two allotments of lands describe below as depicted in Lot
No. 928 to Lot 929 and total extents of 0.9291 Hectare in
the Plan in Zone No. 1 in the page No. 17 in the Cadaster Map
No. 510243 dated 05.10.2018 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Gampaha District instead of the Surveyor
General, and situated in the village of Udugama in the
Madapattuwa minor Division in the Division of the Mirigama
Divisional Secretariat in Gampaha District of the Western
Province.
01. Lot No. 928
Name of the Land : Kadurugahalanda; Description: Garden
with mixed crop; Claimed by: Government; Original Claimed
by: Dharmawathi Piris, No. 25, Udugama, Veyangoda (Portion
of the land Lot No. 330); Extent : 0.1334 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 929
Name of the Land : Kadurugahalanda; Description: Garden
with mixed crop; Claimed by: Government; Original Claimed
by: Dharmawathi Piris, No. 25, Udugama, Veyangoda (Portion
of the land Lot No. 330); Extent : 0.7957 Hectare.
All persons interested possessed the right or with any
other relation in the aforesaid lands/servitude are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Dompe Divisional Secretariat on 6th
March, 2019 at 9.30 a.m.
Further, they are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
with two copies on or before 25th February, 2019 the nature
of their interest in the Land/servitude, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.

ILV A,

Mirigama.
