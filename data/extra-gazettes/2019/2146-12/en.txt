AMENDMENT
MERITORIOUS   SERVICE   MEDAL
Orders
REGULATIONS mentioned in the following paragraphs, numbered as First, Second, Fourth, and Fifth published in the
Government Gazette Extraordinary Number 2112/32 dated 28.02.2019, of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka for the Meritorious Service Medal are hereby amended:
First Paragraph : Style and Designation  - It is ordained that the medal shall be styled and designated the
"SEV ABHIMANI PADAKKAMA".
Second Paragraph : Description - It is ordained  that the medal shall be of Gold colour  metal and the flower of eight
petals in shape, with a diameter of 3.7 cm  bearing in the centre of the surface a design comprising
an Eagle in flight head to the sinister superimposed on an Anchor with a chain  twined around it, the
whole superimposed upon two crossed swords points uppermost surrounded by the legend
"SEV ABHIMANI  PADAKKAMA" embossed in Sinhala, Tamil and English and in the relief on the
reverse the Armorial Ensign of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka surrounded by a lotus
petal border within aears  of paddy surrounded by the wheel of Doctrine with the filled  Vessel at
the base and the depiction of Sun in splendour and the Moon on the right and left respectively and
the year of institution "2009" embossed below it.

 Fourth Paragraph: Eligibility - It is ordained that the medal shall be granted to the Minister  of Defence, the State
Minister of Defence, the Secretary of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
and all Ranks of the Regular and V olunteer Forces of the Army, Navy and Air Force of the Democratic

service excluding the  basic training period with a good conduct and the permanent civil staff
members with Six Years of aggregate service in Armed Services and  Affiliates of the Sri Lanka

Fifth Paragraph : Registration - It is ordained that the names of all those to whom the  Medal is awarded shall be

the Minister of Defence, the State Minister of  Defence, the Secretary of Defence of the Democratic
Socialist Republic  of Sri Lanka shall be maintained under the Ministry of Defence.
Sixth Paragraph : Order of Wear - It is ordained that in the official list showing the order in which Orders, Decorations
and Medals are to be worn, the Sevabhimani Padakkama shall be worn immediately after the Sri
Lanka Armed Service Long Service Medal.

size of the medal and a sealed pattern of the miniature medal shall be kept in the Headquarters of the

SEV ABHIMANI  PADAKKAMA
1. The Medal shall be awarded to the Minister of Defence, the State Minister of Defence, the Secretary of De-
fence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka for the valuable contribution made since 19.05.2009 and all Ranks
of the Regular and V olunteer Forces of the Army, Navy and Air Force and members of the Sri Lanka Police who have been
in active service on Nineteen day of May Two Thousand Nine or thereafter, and who have completed an aggregate period of
Six Years excluding the basic training period with a good conduct and the permanent civil staff members with Six years of
aggregate service in Armed Services and Affiliates of the Sri Lank Police.
4.  A Board of not less than three Senior Officers nominated by the respective Service Commanders will examine
the recommendations made for the award of the SEV ABHIMANI PADAKKAMA. The recommendations of the Board along
with the recommendations of the respective Service Commanders will be forwarded to His Excellency the President
through the Secretary, Defence for His Excellency’s approval. The recommendations of the Minister of Defence, the State
Minister of Defence and the Secretary of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka should be sent
directly by the Secretary of Defence for His Excellency’s approval.
6. The names of persons whom the Medal is conferred upon, shall be published in the respective Service Orders
and a register of all such recipients shall be maintained under the direction of the respective Service Commander.
8.  Any Officer of the Sri Lanka Army, Navy, Air Force and Police who is cashiered, dismissed or has been called
upon to resign or retire his Commission, shall forfeit the Medal conferred on him unless His Excellency the President
shall direct otherwise.
9.  Every Soldier, Seaman, Airman and Policemen who has been discharged from the Army, Navy and Air Force
with disgrace or with ignominy, shall forfeit the Medal unless His Excellency the President shall direct otherwise.
13. In an event where a Medal is lost, and if it is desirous to replace it, such a loss should be reported to the Police.
A declaration testified by a Justice of the Peace stating the circumstances under which the loss occurred should be submit-
ted along with a copy of the complaint made to the Police. This declaration shall be forwarded to the Commanding Officer
who will forward it to the respective Service Commander through the usual channel of communication. The Medal lost
therewith will be replaced on payment if the explanation as to the loss is considered satisfactory.
14. In those Regulations -
(a) "Services" means - The Sri Lanka Army,
The Sri Lanka Navy,
The Sri Lanka Air Force,
(b) "Police" means The Sri Lanka Police.
(c) "Service Commanders" means - The Commander of the Army,
The Commander of the Navy,
The Commander of the Air Force,
(d) "Inspector General" means - The Inspector General of Police.
(e) "Military Personnel" means - Army, Navy and Air Force personnel
(f) "Member of Police" means - Police Personnel
(g) "Member of the Civil Staff" means - Civilians working in the Army,
Navy, Air Force and Police.

AMENDMENT
SERVICE  MEDAL
Orders
REGULATIONS mentioned in the following paragraphs, numbered as Second, Fourth, and Fifth published in the Govern-
ment Gazette Extraordinary Number 2112/32 dated 28.02.2019, of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka for
the Service Medal are hereby amended:
Second Paragraph : Description - It is ordained  that the medal shall be of Rose Gold  colour metal and square in shape
curved at four corners, with four straight sides in equal length of 2.2 cm bearing in the centre of the
surface a design comprising an Eagle in flight head to the sinister superimposed on an Anchor with
a chain twined around it, the whole  superimposed upon two crossed swords points uppermost
surrounded by the legend ‘SERVICE MEDAL’ embossed in Sinhala, Tamil and English and in the
relief on the reverse the Armorial Ensign of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka surrounded
by a lotus petal border within a ears of paddy surrounded by the wheel of Doctrine  with the filled
Vessel at the base and the depiction of Sun in splendour and the Moon on the right and left respec-
tively and the year of  institution "2009" embossed below it.
Fourth Paragraph : Eligibility - It is ordained that the medal shall be granted to the Minister of  Defence, the State
Minister of Defence, the Secretary of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
and all Ranks of the Regular and V olunteer Forces of the Army, Navy and Air Force of the Demo-
cratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and members of the Sri Lanka Police, for the valuable contri-
bution made since 19.05.2009, for the post-war activities, count not less than Three years of aggre-
gate service excluding the basic training period with a good conduct and the permanent civil staff
members with three years of aggregate service in Armed Services and Affiliates of the Sri Lanka
Police subject to the regulations hereinafter made.
Fifth Paragraph : Registration - It is ordained that the names of all those to whom the Medal is awarded shall be
published in the respective Service Orders and the register of such names shall be maintained under
the direction of the respective Service Commander and Inspector General of Police. It is ordained
that a  register of the names of the Minister of Defence, the State Minister of  Defence, the Secre-
tary of Defence of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be maintained under the
Ministry of Defence.
Sixth  Paragraph : Order of Wear - It is ordained that in the official list showing the order in which Orders, Decora-
tions and Medals are to be worn, the Service Medal shall be worn immediately after the
SEVABHIMANE PADAKKAMA.
