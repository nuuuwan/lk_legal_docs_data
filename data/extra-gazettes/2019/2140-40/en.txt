REGISTRATION  OF   DEATHS  (TEMPORARY  PROVISION)   ACT,  NO. 19  OF  2010
THE regulation made by the Minister under Sub section (02) of Section 01 of Registration of Deaths (Temporary Provision)
Act, No. 19 of 2010.
VAJIRA ABEYW ARDANA,
Minister of Internal and Home Affairs
and Provincial Councils and Local Government.
Colombo,
05th Day of August, 2019.
Regulation
The given effective period of operation of the provisions of Registration of Deaths (Temporary Provision) Act,
No. 19 of 2010, will be extended by another two years from 09th of December 2019 to 09th December 2021.
