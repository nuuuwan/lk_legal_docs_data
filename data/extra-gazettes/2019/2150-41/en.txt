Proclamation
P.S. No:  PS/CSA/1/1/10.
WHEREAS HIS EXCELLENCY GOTABAY A RAJAPAKSA was declared elected President of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka on the Seventeenth day of November, Two Thousand and Nineteen at an Election held under Article 31(3) of
the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
And Whereas, the Term of Office  of the said President commenced, on the Seventeenth day of November Two
Thousand  and Nineteen in terms of Article 31(4) of the said Constitution,
It is hereby proclaimed that H
IS EXCELLENCY GOTABAYA RAJAPAKSA has, by making and subscribing the affirmations
set out in the Fourth and Seventh Schedules to the said Constitution in the manner provided for in Article 32 read with
Article 157 A(7) thereof,  on this Eighteenth day of November Two  Thousand and Nineteen, assumed the Office of
President of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
Whereof all persons are hereby required to take due notice.
Given at Anuradhapura on the Eighteenth day of November, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
       By Order of His Excellency,
P. B. J
AYASUNDERA,
     Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo-01,
November 19, 2019.
