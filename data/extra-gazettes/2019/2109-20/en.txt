SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose
in respect of the  commodity  specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rate
specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months  commencing from February 09, 2019, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
February 08,  2019.
