DECLARING   THE   HOLY   LAND   OWNED   BY   THE   THELWATTA   PURANA  THOTAGAMU
RATHPATH  RAJAMAHA   VIHARAYA AS  A  SACRED    LAND   PARALLEL   TO THE   NATIONAL  VESAK
FESTIV AL 2019
ACCORDING to the communication made by His Excellency the President of  the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2103/33 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, dated  Friday  December
28th 2018, and according to the subject of  planning and developing the sacred places empowered, I, Gamini  Jayawickrama
Perera as the Minister of Buddha Sasana  and Wayamba Development declare that the Land area definitely described in the
schedule herein is a sacred area forever and declare that as the “Thelwatta  Purana  Totagamu  Rathpath  Rajamahavihara
Pooja Holy Land’’.
G
AMINI J AYAWICKRAMA  P ERERA,
Minister of Buddha Sasana,
and Wayamba Development.
Ministry of Buddha Sasana
and Wayamba Development,
No.135,  Anagarika Dharmapala  Mawatha,
Colombo  7,
14th May 2019.
