STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  Mr.  S. Ranasinghe, Civil Engineer - Grade II of the Sri Lanka Transport Board who's service had been suspended on two instances
from 25.10.2010 to February 2011 and from 23.08.2011 to 25.04.2012 on half being  pay had been caused injustice by not receiving the half
pay unpaid during the period of suspension, promotions and other reliefs even after being reinstated in service and if so, to what reliefs he
is entitled.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour.
Colombo this 06th day of March 2019.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
