EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   VALIKAMAM   NORTH
PRADESHIYA   SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member of  Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of not
attending three consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the T amil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
Pakkiyaraja Gobinath the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to
decide  upon  returned  member if  Tamil United Liberation Front who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under
Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who shall be declared selected  as a member within a period
of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Selvarathinarasa Ajeethkumar to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajendran Christy Amalraaj, Returning Officer  of  the Valikamam North Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
that Selvarathinarasa Ajeethkumar was selected as the member of the Valikamam North Pradeshisya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam North Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Jaffna,
29th April, 2019.
