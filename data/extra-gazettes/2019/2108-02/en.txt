THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Two Allotments of land in total extent of about 2.7572 Hectares
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ma/MNN/2017/097 and dated
28.03.2018, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
called Uvari, in the Grama Niladhari Division No. MN/60 of
Olaittoduvai in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mannar Town
of Mannar District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Uvarinilam – Extent: 0.3924 Hectare
North: Uvarinilam, claimant not known, claimed by Seylan Business
(Pvt) Ltd and Lot B; East: Lot B, Uvarinilam, claimed by Shell Coast
Resort and Lanka CZ (Pvt) Ltd., and  claimant not known; South:
Uvarinilam claimant not known and Beach; West: Beach and
Uvarinilam claimant not known.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Uvarinilam – Extent: 2.3648 Hectare
North: Uvarinilam, claimed by Seylan Business (Pvt) Ltd; East:
Uvarinilam, claimed by Seylan Business (Pvt) Ltd and claimed by
Shell Coast Resort and Lanka CZ (Pvt) Ltd; South: Uvarinilam claimed
by Shell Coast Resort and Lanka CZ (Pvt) Ltd, claimant not known
and Lot A; West: Lot A, Uvarinilam claimant not known and claimed
by Seylan Business (Pvt) Ltd.
Gayantha Karunatilleke,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2017/CEB/242.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
