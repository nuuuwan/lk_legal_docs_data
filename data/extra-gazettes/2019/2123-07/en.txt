THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No.:- KDS/DOL/4/4/5/2/4.
Lands Ministry No:- 13/96/ AS/4/461.
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended be the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land for a public purpose.
     Description of the Land to be acquired:
An allotments of lands Called Estate.shown as Lots 01 in
preliminary plan No Maha 4848 prepared by the Surveyor
General in Extent of 0.5330 Hectares, situated in the in the
Village of Ududeniya.Ududeniya Grama Niladhari Division in
Kanduara Pahala Korale Minor division in Divisional
Secretariat Division of Doluwa in Kandy District Central
province.
Lot No. 01
    Name of the land : New forest watta,
    Extent of the land : 0.5330Hectares
    Original Claimant : Janatha Estate Development Board
Colombo.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be acquired
are hereby required to appear personally, or by Agents duly
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, on
the 11th June 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and are here by requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th
June 2019. The nature of their interests in the land the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
T. A. D. W. DAYANANDA,
Divisional Secretary/
Land Acquisition Officer,
Doluwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Doluwa,
07th May , 2019.
