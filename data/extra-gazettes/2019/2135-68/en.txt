Order under Section 3

Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance, do by this Order that, the description specified in the Column II applicable on
item 7 in Column I of the Schedule hereto, shall replace the description specified in Column II applicable on item 7 in
Column I of the Schedule of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2114/25 dated March 13, 2019 issued under
