NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Negombo Municipal  Council
I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Negombo Municipal Council do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Ponnamperumage Fred
Dexter of the Negombo Municipal Council has vacated his office of member of that Municipal Council as he has ceased
to be a member of the United National Freedom Front.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
29th May, 2019.
