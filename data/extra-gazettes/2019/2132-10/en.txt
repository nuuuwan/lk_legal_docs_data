The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
FILLING   OF   A   VACANCY   IN    THE   OFFICE   OF   MEMBER   OF   THE   NUWARAGAMPALATHA
CENTRAL  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER   SECTION   66A(1) OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a return  member in  Nuwaragampalatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha has  fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the Nuwaragampalatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha, the recognized political
party to which Rathnamalala Bandara Wanninayakage Chandraguptha Wanninayaka the member belonged, was required by
me under Section 66 A (1)  of the aforesaid ordinance, to decide  upon  returned  member of  People’s Liberation Front
who belongs to such party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance and who
shall be declared selected as a member within a period of thirty (30)  days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Weliwaththa Gamlathlage Sumith Kulathunga to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Mohideen Bawa Mohamed Sufyan, Returning Officer of the Nuwaragampalatha Central
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 66A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do
hereby declare that Weliwaththa Gamlathlage Sumith Kulathunga  was selected as the member of the Nuwaragampalatha
Central  Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. B. M. S
UFYAN,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragampalatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Election Office,
Anuradhapura,
15th July, 2019.
