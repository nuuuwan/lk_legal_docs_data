                  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms
                  Reference No.:4-3/15/2016/VE/249.
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land/ servitude which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land is called Ambadeniya watta ( Part of
Gavilipitiya) or Boyagoda Henyaya about 4.2271 hectares in
extent and depicted as Lot 01, in Preliminary Plan P .P .K Number
3600 and dated 11.06.2018 made by the Survey General and
situated in the village of Narangammana, in the Grama
Niladhari Division of No-45.D Narangammana, in the Minor
Division of Ganhatha Palatha , in the Divisional Secretariat
Division of Aranayaka, in the District of Kegalle, in
Sabaragamuwa Province. Boundaries are as follow.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Ambadeniyawatta (Gavilipitiya Part) or
Boyagoda Henyaya; Extent: 4.2271 Hectares.
    G 29254 - 21 (2019/01)
Boundaries:
North: Road and Ambadeniyawatta; East: Road; South:
Wasasanthagama and Road; West: Road.
Other particulars : Lot No.1078 of part of Preliminary Plan
No.421 and the Land has been acquired under Section 38 (A)
of the Land Acquisition Act. Managing Director Kegalle
Plantation Company, No.10, Navinna, Maharagama and
Director, Land Reform Commission, No. C 82 Hector
Kobbekaduwa Mawatha Colombo 07 are preliminary claimants
and for the completion of acquisition activities.
   All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat Aranayaka on the 11th day of
February 2019 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of
February 2019 the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and
details of the computation such amount.
Z.A.M.FAIZAL,
