THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under section 20(4)(b)
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by section 20 (4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said University
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Emeritus Prof. Kandasamy Kathirgamanathan to be the
Competent Authority with effect from May 7, 2019 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of
the Vice - Chancellor, University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka any power, duty or function respectively which has been conferred
or imposed on, or assigned to the said Vice - Chancellor under the said Act, or under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-
law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
7th May 2019.
