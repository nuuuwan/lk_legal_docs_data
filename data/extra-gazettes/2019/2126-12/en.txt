LAND   REFORMS ACT, No. 01 OF 1972   OF  THE  NATIONAL   STATE   ASSEMBLY
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 27 A (1) of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 as amended and to
be read together with Sub Article (2) of Article 44 of  the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
and as it is requested by the Land Reform Commission, I, Gayantha Karunathilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby :-
1. Alienate the agricultural Lands specified in the Schedules hereto which had been vested in the Land
Reform Commission established under the Land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972 of the National State
Assembly, in terms of the Section 3 of the said Act by way of  sale, to the Board of Investment of
Sri Lanka established by the Act, No. 4 of 1978 for the below mentioned purposes as per the Section
22 (1) b of said Land  Reform Act.
(a) To construct infrastucture and housing for employees required to run an export processing zone/
industrial zone ;
(b) To sub lease the Land for investment projects approved by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka
and to divest upon the requirement of Board of Investment.
It has been agreed by the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka to pay a sum of Rs 112,000,000.00 (Hundred and Twelve
Million Rupees) as the total consideration for the said sale in respect of the Land scheduled hereto to the Land Reform
Commission.
