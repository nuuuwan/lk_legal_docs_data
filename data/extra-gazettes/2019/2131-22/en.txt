THE  JUDICATURE  ACT,   No.  2  OF  1978
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I, Thalatha Atukorale, Minister of
Justice and Prison Reforms in consultation with the Chief Justice and the President of the Court of Appeal, do by
this Order, amend the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1679/40 of November 10, 2010 by the
substitution for the words and figures “No. 43/4 of July 2, 1979” appearing in item (g) of that Order of the words and
figures  “No. 43/3 of July 2, 1979”.
THALA THA ATUKORALE ,
Minister of Justice and Prison Reforms.
03rd July, 2019,
Colombo.
