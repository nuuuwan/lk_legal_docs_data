PORTS  AND  AIRPORTS  DEVELOPMENT  LEVY  ACT,   No.  18  OF  2011
 Order  under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of  Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development Levy Act,
No. 18 of 2011, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance do hereby this Order declare to :
1. Amend the order made in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2049/16 of December 13, 2007 by
(i) Addition of the following new sub paragraph immediately after sub paragraph (f) of the paragraph 2
“(g) sale of yachts and other vessels for pleasure or sports ; rowing boats and canoes : sailboats, with or
without auxiliary motor classified under the HS Code of 8903.91.00, by any enterprise, which has
entered in to an agreement with the Board of Investement of Sri Lanka established under the Board of
Investement of Sri Lanka Law, No. 4 of 1978 and engaged in the manufacturing of boats, to any enterprise
which has entered into an agreement with the aforesaid Board of Investment of Sri Lanka and chartering
such vessels and yachts for supply of service.”
(ii) Addition at the end of the Schedule II of the article specified in Column III corresponding to the HS headings
and HS codes specified in the Column I and II of the Schedule I hereto,
2. Amend the Schedule to the order made in the Gazette Extraordinary Notification No. 2079/34 of July 11, 2018
by addition at the end of such Schedule of the articles specified in Column III corresponding to the HS heading and
HS codes specified in the Column I and II of the Schedule II hereto,
