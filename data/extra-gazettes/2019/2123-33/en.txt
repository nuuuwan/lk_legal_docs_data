THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, NO. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 20 (4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I , A. Ibathul Rauff
Hakeem, Minister  of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the
affairs of the Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of
the said Institute have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Major General (retired) Shantha
Kumar Thirunavukarasu to be the Competent Authority with effect from May 15, 2019 for the purpose of exercising,
performing and discharging in lieu of the Director, Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa, any power, duty or
function respectively which has been conferred or imposed on , or assigned to the said Director under the said Act, or
under any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
A. I
BATHUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education.
Colombo,
13th May, 2019.
