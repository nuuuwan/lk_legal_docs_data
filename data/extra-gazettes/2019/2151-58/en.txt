Directon No. 70
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.   09   OF   2003
Direction under Section 10(1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10(1)(a) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Manufacturers and Traders of food wrappers (Lunch Sheets) that on each and every
packet or bundle of such food wrappers, a lable containing the following information in all three languages shall be
included.
i. Brand Name
ii. Maximum Retail Price
iii. Name and Address of the Manufacturer
iv. Date of manufacture and shelf life
v . Material used (Virgin PLA, Starch, PABT etc.)
vi. Material Declaration (Food Grade and Biodegradable/Compostable)
vii. Central Environment Authority Registration Number
viii. Size of a wrapper (in cm/inches)
ix. Number of wrappers in a packet/bundle
