THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                         Reference No.: 3/3/6/2/24.
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of section 7 of
Land acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following land/servitude* which is
required for public purpose.
Acquisition for alternative land for the people displaced
due to heavy rain and landslides (Under village Expansion
Programme).
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired:
An plot of land called Enasalmada Watta in extent of about
1.6387 Hectares depicted as Lot 1 in the P .P .M. 1698 prepared
by Senior Superintendent of Survey, Matale and situated at
Enasalmada village in Enasalmada Grama Niladhari in
Kohonsiyapatthuwa minor division in Ukuwela Divisional
Secretary's division in Matale District in Central Province.
Name of the land: Enasalmada Watta.
Claimant: State Plantation Cooperation, No. 11, Duke Street,
Colombo 01.
All persons interested in the aforesaid/lands* on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Ukuwela Divisional Secretariat on the 21st day of August
2019 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of August
2019 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensationfor the acquisition of the said
land/servitude* the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
E. W. N. A. EGODAWELA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ukuwela.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Ukuwela,
14th May , 2019.
