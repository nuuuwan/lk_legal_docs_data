PRESIDENTIAL   ELECTIONS  ACT,   No.  15  OF  1981
Notice under Section 30
IT is hereby notified under Section 30 of the Presidential Elections Act, No. 15 of 1981 that the Notice under Section 21
of the Presidential Elections Act, No. 15 of 1981 issued by the Election Commission in the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 2144/6 dated 07.10.2019 is amended by the substitution in paragraph II thereof for the words and figures
“till 4.00 pm”, of the words and figures “till 5.00 pm”.
