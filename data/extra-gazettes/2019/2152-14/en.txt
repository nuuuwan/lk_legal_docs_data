WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Inquiry conducted under Section 184(1a) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
I, Wijayapala Sri Narayana Wasala Bandaranayake Mohottalalage Seetha Kumari Arambepola, the Governor of  Western
Province do hereby appoint the retired Court of Appeal Judge Mr. Chandradasa Nanayakkara according to the powers
vested in me as per provisions of Section 184(1a) of the  Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section two of the Provincial
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 to inquire into the following matters/allegations and reveal
whether the offences stipulated under Subsection 184(1) of the above said ordinance have been committed by Wattala-
Mabola Urban Council and by Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council while functioning in his office, and submit me
a report within a period of 03 months.
Matters/allegations to be inquired in relation to Wattala-Mabola Urban Council :-
I. Wattala-Mabola Urban Council in its functioning during the period of 21.05.2018 to 05.03.2019 or a
period adjacent to it, committing an offence chargeable under Section 184(1)(b) of Urban Councils
Ordinance by causing to sell, contrary to provisions of Section 36 of the Urban Councils Ordinance, the
land in possession of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council containing 79 Perches in extent or an extent equal to
that marked as Lot No. 1A
2 in the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January 2018 made by Licensed Surveyor
D. A. Weerakkody.
II. Wattala-Mabola Urban Council in its functioning  on 05.03.2019 or on a date adjacent to it, committing an
offence chargeable under Section 184(1)(d) of Urban Councils Ordinance by causing to sell, under an
undervalued consideration and external to the prescribed tender procedure, the land in possession of Wattala-
Mabola Urban Council containing 79 Perches in extent or an extent equal to that marked as Lot No. 1A
2 in
the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January 2018 made by Licensed Surveyor D. A. Weerakkody, and thereby
causing a loss to the Urban Councils Fund.

III. W attala-Mabola Urban Council in its functioning on 21.05.2018  or on a date adjacent to it, committing an

Matters/allegations to be inquired in relation to the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council-
I. While functioning as the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council, committing an offence chargeable
under Section 184(1)(e) of the Urban Councils Ordinance during the period of 21.05.2018 to 13.07.2018
or during a period adjacent to it by taking action to obtain an undervalued consideration to the land  in
possession of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council containing 79 Perches in extent or an extent equivalent to
that marked as Lot No. 1A
2 of the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January 2018 made by Licensed Surveyor
D. A. Weerakkody, stating that the said land will be sold on deed of transfer to Melco Oxygen Company Ltd.
II. While functioning as the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council, committing an offence chargeable
under Section 184(1)(b) of the Urban Councils Ordinance on 01.03.2019 or on a date adjacemt to it by
taking action to sell  the land  in possession of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council containing 79 Perches in
extent or an extent equivalent to that marked as Lot No. 1A
2 of the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January
2018 made by Licensed Surveyor  D. A. Weerakkody, to Mohamed Subeir Fauzal Haq residing at No. 28,
Bagathale Road, Colombo 03 external to proper tender procedure.
III. While functioning as the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council, committing an offence chargeable
under Section 184(1)(b) of Urban Councils Ordinance on 01.03.2019 or on a date adjacent to it by taking
action to issue the letter of acceptance expressing consent to sell  the land  in possession of Wattala-
Mabola Urban Council containing an extent of 79 Perches  or of an extent equivalent to such marked as Lot
No. 1A
2 of  the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January 2018 made by Licensed Surveyor  D. A. Weerakkody, to
Mohamed Subeir Fauzal Haq residing at No. 28, Bagathale Road, Colombo 03 without a resolution passed
by Wattala-Mabola Urban Council.
IV . While functioning as the Chairman of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council, committing an offence chargeable
under Section 184(1)(c) of the Urban Councils Ordinance during the period of 17.02.2019 to 01.03.2019
or during a period  adjacent to it by taking action without considering the instructions and/or directives
dated 07.02.2019 of the Minister in Charge of the subject of Local Government of Western Province
relating to the sale of land  in possession of Wattala-Mabola Urban Council containing 79 Perches in
extent or an extent equivalent to that marked as Lot No. 1A
2 of the Plan No. 1377C dated 17th January
2018 made by Licensed Surveyor  D. A. Weerakkody.
Furthermore, according to the provisions of Section 184(1a) of the Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section two of
the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, powers entrusted to a Commissioner appointed
under the Commissions of Inquiry Act shall be delegated to this Single Person Board of Inquiry, and I, hereby inform
further that written and oral evidences identified as applicable in the process of inquiring into the above matters, have
been included in the Schedule below.
