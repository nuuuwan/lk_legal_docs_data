Regulations
FOOD  (MELAMINE  IN  MILK  AND  MILK  PRODUCTS) REGULATIONS  2010
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Food (Melamine in Milk and Milk Products) Regulations 2010 and shall
come into effect from  1st January, 2011.
2. No person shall import, manufacture, transport, distribute, sell, offer or keep  for sale any milk or milk products  in
Sri Lanka, containing melamine in  levels exceeding 1.0 mg/kg (Part Per Million.)
3. Every person importing  milk or milk products  into the country,  shall produce at the point of entry of such
product, a Health Certificate from the National Food Safety  Authority of the country of origin or the exporting country as the
case may be, which states that the products conforms to the levels specified in these Regulations.
