EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   SAMMANTHURAI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Sammanthurai  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Seenithamby Neinther Lebbe,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Sammanthurai    Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr. Ahamed Abdul
Carder Mohamed Nizam being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,   Returning Officer of  Sammanthurai    Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ahamed Abdul
Carder Mohamed Nizam,  elected as a member of the Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
21st January, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   RAJGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of a member in Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallend vacant by reason of being elected as a  member
of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
G . Deneththi,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Rajgama   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Thirimadura Sujith
Chamin Kumara,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,   Returning Officer of  Rajgama    Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Thrimadura
Sujith Chamin Kumara, elected as  a  member  of   the  Rajgama Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
