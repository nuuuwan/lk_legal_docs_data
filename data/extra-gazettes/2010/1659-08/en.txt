WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS
Inquiry held under Section 2 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute, No. 04
1991 of Western Province Provincial Council
THE one man Commission of inquiry comprising Dr. Chandradasa Nanayakkara Esqr. Retired Judge of the Court of Appeal,
appointed by the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of the Provincial Council,
Western Province in terms of the provisions mentioned in section 2(2) of  the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Authorities Statute, No. 04 of 1991 of the Provincial Council, Western Province, has examined as to whether the
offences mentioned in section 2(1) of the said Statute have been committed in performing duties by the Colombo Municipal
Council due to the following offences.
The charges so examined:—
(a) Commission of an offence/offences stated in section/sections 2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(c) of the Statute of the
Authority of Supervision of Administration of Local Authorities No. 04 of 1991 of the Provincial Council of
Western Province by acting to the contrary of section/sections 229(1)(b) and/or 229(1)(d) of the Municipal
Council Ordinance by adopting a resolution by the Council and acting in pursuance of such resolution to the
effect that the contract for management of solid waste in the Municipal Division of Fort and in administrative
areas of divisional offices of No. 5 be given to the Carekleen Private Limited of No. 96/A, Dutugemunu Mawatha,
Kohuwala, Dehiwala for a period of 2 years onwards from 01.04.2007 on or thereabout in the act of allowing the
Council to operate without calling for tenders and or approval of the council in the act of performing functions
by the Colombo Municipal Council.
(b) Commission of an offence/offences stated in section/sections 2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(c) of the Statute of the
Authority of Supervision of Administration of Local Authorities  No. 04 of 1991 of the Provincial Council,
Western Province by acting to the contrary of the section/sections 229(1)(b) and/or 229(1)(d) of the Municipal
Council Ordinance by adopting a resolution by the Council and acting in pursuance of such resolution to the
effect that the contract for management of solid waste in the  administrative area a divisional office of No. 01 of
the Municipal Division Colombo be given to “Carekleen Private Company” of No. 96/A, Dutugemunu Mawatha,
Kohuwala, Dehiwala for the period of 2 years onwards from 1st of July, 2007 or thereabout, without calling for
tenders and/or approval by the council in the act of performing functions by the Colombo Municipal
Council.

PART IV (A) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 22.06.2010
According to the inquiry report of the One Man Inquiry tribunal comprises of Dr. Chandradasa Nanayakkara Esqr.
Retired Judge of the Court of Appeal investigated the charges against the Colombo Municipal Council it has been concluded
that the Colombo Municipal Council is guilty of the charges a,b,c,d,f and g mentioned above that the said  Municipal
Council is not guilty in respect of the charges e,h and i.
Further the One Man Commission of inquiry comprising B.L.K.Wimalachandra Esqr. Retired Judge of the Court of
Appeal appointed by the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of the Western Province
in terms of the provisions of the Statute of the Authority of Supervision of Administration of Local Authorities No.04 of 1991
has inquired in to the individual and the collective responsibilities of the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Members of the
Colombo Municipal Council in respect of the above mentioned charges a,b,c,d,f and g and the following additional charge.
The additional charge referred to above;
“ Commission of an offence/offences stated in section/sections 2(1)(a) and/or 2(1)(c) of the Statute of the
Authority of Supervision of Administration of Local Authorities No. 04 of 1991 of Provincial Council, Western Province by
not taking action to credit the general fund of the Municipal Council with a sum of Rs.15,555,440.81(Fifteen Million five
hundred and fifty five thousand four hundred and forty and eighty one cent) or an approximate amount accrued by maintaining
“Satutu Uyana” within the Vihara Maha Devi Park belonging to the Colombo Municipal Council during the period from
01.05.2006 to 01.05.2009 in violation of Section 185(2)(e) of the Municipal Council Ordinance.”
I am attending to independent conclusions regarding relevant charges, after a keen study of evidence which have
submitted and the observations submitted by the one person inquiry commission. Accordingly I conclude that the Colombo
Municipal Council is guilty for the above mentioned a,b,c,d,f,g and the additional charge.
Accordingly I am satisfied that due to offences mentioned in the above charges a,b,c,d,f,g and the additional charge
having been committed by the Colombo Municipal Council, offences mentioned in sections 2(1)(a) and 2(1)(c) of the Statute
of the Authority of Supervision of Administration of Local   Authorities No. 04 of 1991 of the Provincial Council of Western
Province, have being committed.
So, after satisfying it is informed that the Colombo Municipal Council has been dissolved with effect from 23rd
June, 2010, according to power vested in me under the section 2(1)(111) of Statute of the Authority of Supervision of
Administration of Local Authorities No. 04 of 1991 of the Provincial Council of Western Province.
