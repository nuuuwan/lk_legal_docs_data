PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE  PRESIDENT
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA
KNOW YE, that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of  Sri Lanka, I,  Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Proclamation summon Parliament to meet at the
Parliament Hall, Sri Jayawardenepura, on the Twenty Second day of April, Two Thousand and Ten at 11.00 a.m.
Given at Colombo, this 31st day of March,  Two Thousand and  Ten.
By order of  His Excellency,
LALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
