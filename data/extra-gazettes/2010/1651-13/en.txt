EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   RIDIGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the  member  in  Ridigama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  not attending meetings
of the Council for the period of three months without permission  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Indika Kavisigamuwa,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Ridigama  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.    Karunathilake W. M.
W. B. D.  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  R. M. A. L. Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of the  Ridigama  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr.  Karunathilake W. M. W.
B. D. elected as  a  member  of   the  Ridigama  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKE,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Ridigama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kurunegala,
22nd  April, 2010.
