EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  DEHIWALA - MOUNT LA VINIA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of   being
ceased to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Achchi Maddumage Don Hasala Dhanasiri Amaratunga,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2)
of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that
Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal Council   who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within
a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.    Pattiyage Amarasena
Peiris   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  A. Senanayake,  Returning Officer of  Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal Council ,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Pattiyage
Amarasena Peiris   elected as a member  of   the Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal Council.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
02nd February,  2010.
