NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  S. M. Saman Bandulasena,  Returning  Officer  of  the Mihintale   Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare  under  Section
10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rangegama Gamladdalage Sumith Nihal of the Mihintale
Pradeshiya  Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the  United
National Party.
S. M. S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
Returning Officer,
Mihintale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
27th December,  2009.
