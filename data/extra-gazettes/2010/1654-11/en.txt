SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice  under Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 2(I) and Section 4 of the
Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern
Provincial Council  and Section  2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I, Gusthinnawadu  Shan Wijayalal De Silva, the Minister  in-charge of the Subjects of  Finance and Planning, Law and
Order, Transport, Water Supply and  Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local
Government of the Southern Province Provincial Council, being satisfied myself of the findings of the retired Judicial
Officer who was appointed by me in terms of Section 185(2) of the Pradeshiya Sabha  Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with
Section 2(2) of the Powers  of  Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990
of the Southern Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, to
inquire into and report if Mr. Hewa Lokuge  Jayasiri, the Chairman of Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, has while
functioning  as the Chairman of the said Pradeshiya Sabha, committed any offence falling under Section 185(I) of the
Pradeshiya  Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and Section 2(I) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government
Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Provincial Council, that the Chairman of  Tissamaharama Pradeshiya
Sabha has committed such offences as referred to under Section 2(I) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration
of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Provincial Council and Section 185(I) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, do  in terms of Section 185(3)(A) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read with
Section 2(I) and 4(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of
1990 of the Southern Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989,
hereby remove Mr. Hewa Lokuge Jayasiri, the Chairman of  Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha from the Office of Chairman
and Membership of Tissamaharama Pradeshiya  Sabha.
G.   W.   S
HAN WIJAYALAL DE SIL V A,
Minister of Finance  and Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services,
Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local Government,
Southern Province.
19th May, 2010.
