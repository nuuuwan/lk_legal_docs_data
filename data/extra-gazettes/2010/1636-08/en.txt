No. 1636 / 8 – WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 13, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Wen/DS/La/90/1.
Ministry Reference No.: LD/05/2004/HA/152.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Three (3) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.301 Hectare
depicted in Surveyor General’s Preliminary Plan  No. PU 4409, situated
in the village of Ulhitiyawa North Minor Division of
Kammalpaththuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Wennappuwa in Puttalam District,  North Western Province:-
Lot No. 1
Name of land: Belmondwatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 0.080 Hectare.
Lot No. 2
Name of land: Belmondwatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 0.087 Hectare.
Lot No. 3
Name of land: Belmondwatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: Land
Reform Commission; Extent: 0.134 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Wennapuwa Divisional Secretariat on 24th day of February, 2010
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 17th day of   February, 2010  the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation  and
the details of the computation of such amount.
S. N. B. A. K. M. N. D. Namarathna,
Divisional Secretary,
Wennappuwa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Wennappuwa,
1st January, 2010.
