EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF THE GALLE  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a Member in Galle  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a member
of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Aeka  Sarath Chandra De Sarath Gunaratna,   the member  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Oridnance for Election as a member of that  Galle  Municipal
Council  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.    Nimal Chandra
Ranasingha,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva, Returning  Officer of   Galle Municipal Council,  acting in terms
of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Nimal  Chandra Ranasingha,
elected as a member of the  Galle  Municipal Council.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Galle Municipal Council.
Galle,
15th June, 2010.
