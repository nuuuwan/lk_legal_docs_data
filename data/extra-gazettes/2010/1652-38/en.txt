  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Central  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mrs. Atampan Anushiya
Sivarajah to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mrs. Atampan Anushiya Sivarajah, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Nuwara Eliya in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
04th May, 2010.

  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the North  Western  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1)
of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North  Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a Member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Dina Eduruge
Senarathna De Silva to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Dina Eduruge Senarathna De Silva, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Kurunegala in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
