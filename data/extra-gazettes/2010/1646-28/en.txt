  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Resignation,
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Murali Ragunathan  to fill
such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Murali Ragunathan, elected as a member of the above Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of  Nuwara-Eliya  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AY ANANDA DISSANAY AKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
24th March, 2010.
