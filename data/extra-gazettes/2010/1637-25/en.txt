THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: 04/03/01/05.
Land Ministry No.: LD/05/2007/HW/310.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotments of land shown as Lot Nos. 01 to 05 in total extent of
0.2495 Hectare, depicted in Preliminary Plan No. K. 4164, prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Ihala Karannagoda

bounded as follows:
Lot No. 01

T. L. D. Gihan Dammika, Weralugas Totupala; Extent: 0.0055 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Weralugas Totupala; Claimant: State; Original claimant:
T. L. A. Ajith, Weralugas Totupala, Ihala Karannagoda; Extent:
0.0080 Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Weralugas Totupala; Claimant: State; Original claimant:
Not known; Extent: 0.1067 Hectare.
Lot No. 04
Name of land: Weralugas Totupala; Claimant: State; Original claimant:
Not known; Extent: 0.0719 Hectare.
Lot No. 05
Name of land: Weralugas Totupala; Claimant: State; Original claimant:
Not known; Extent: 0.0574 Hectare.

at Divisional Secretary’s Office of the Madurawala, on 22nd day of

nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation  and the details of the computation of such amount.
M. A. T. Senarath,
