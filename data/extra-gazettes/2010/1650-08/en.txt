THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Puth/Land/09/15.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition  Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for the purpose of construction of Viluka
Veppamadu Pump House under Puttalam Water Supply Scheme.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two allotments of land called Periyasalan Kulama in extent of
about 0.0021 Hectare, depicted as Lot Nos. 652 and 653 in the Final
Village Plan No. 3355, dated 27th June, 2007, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys in Puttalam, situated in the village of
Vilukkai in Grama Niladhari Division of 606 – Manalthivu in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Puttalam in the Puttalam District
and bounded as below:
Lot No. 652
North: Lot No. 149 and 654; East: Lot No. 653; South: Lot
Nos. 654 and 149 (Pradeshiya Sabha Road); West: Lot No. 149.
Lot No. 653
North: Lot No. 149 and 654; East: Lot No. 654; South: Lot Nos.
654 and 149 (Pradeshiya Sabha Road); West: Lot No. 652.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Puttalam on 14th June, 2010 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before 01st day of June, 2010, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
A. C. M. Nafeel,
Divisional Secretary,
Puttalam.
Divisional Secretariat,
Puttalam,
01st April, 2010.
