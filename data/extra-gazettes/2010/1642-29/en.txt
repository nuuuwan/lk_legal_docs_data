No. 1642 /29 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: Mathu/LA/4/Adhi/Podu.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition  Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
One (01) allotment of  land in total  extent of 0.0505 Hectares, as
Lot No. 1, depicted  in Preliminary Plan No. Ka.4202 and dated
20.01.2010 prepared by the Surveyor-General situated in  Kolahekada
Village in the Mathugama Divisional Secretary’s Division in the District
of Kalutara  in Western Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Pallegodawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: Namunukula Plantation Ltd;  Extent: 0.0505
Hectare.
All perosns interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at
Mathugama Divisional  Secretariat on the 23rd April, 2010 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 16th April, 2010 the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
                  W.P. Sandanayake,
                          Divisional Secretary,
                    Mathugama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Mathugama.
10th February, 2010.
