NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Eravur Pattu  Pradeshiya   Sabha
I, Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam, Returning Officer of  Eravur Pattu  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10 A (1) ( a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Subramaniam Sukumar of the Eravur Pattu
Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased  to be a member of the  Tamil Makkal
Viduthalai Pulikal Party.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Pattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
