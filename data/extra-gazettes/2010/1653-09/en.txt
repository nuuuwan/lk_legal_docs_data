PART III  — LANDS
Land Reform Commission  Notices
Statutory Determination No. : 4610.
Unique No. : Ra/ 48.
STATUTORY  DETERMINATION  UNDER  SECTION  19  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  ACT,  No. 01 OF 1972
SINCE Mr. Hendirige Parakrama Gunasekara residing at Pahala Karandana Watta,  Kalatuwawa  has  made  a statutory
declaration under Section 18 of Land Reform Act, by virtue of the powers vested in the Land   Reform Commission by
Section 19 of the Act, the Commission has made a Statutory Determination determining the   portion of land allowed to be
kept by the tenant out of Agricultural lands which were belonging to him.  Agricultural land which the statutory tenant was
allowed to keep is indicated  in the following Schedule.
N
IMAL G.  PUNCHIHEWA,
Attorney-at-Law,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
Land Reform Commission,
No.  C82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
 11th  May 2010.
Schedule
01. The undivided portion of land in extent 34A. 3R. 11P bearing Lot No. 890, 891 of the land called Karandana
Watta depicted under additional No. 22 of FVP No. 219, prepared by Surveyor General and situated in Karandana Village
in Minor Division Meda Pattuwa in Divisional Revenue Officer’s Division, Kuruwita Korale in Ratnapura District in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
02. The portions of land in extent 5.0966 Hectares bearing Lot No. 2271, 2273, 2274 of the land called Wewala Watta
depicted under additional No. 52 of FVP No. 219 prepared by Surveyor General and situated in Karandana Village in Minor
Division Meda Pattuwa in Divisional Secretary’s Division, Eheliyagoda in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
trees, plantations and buildings standing thereon.
