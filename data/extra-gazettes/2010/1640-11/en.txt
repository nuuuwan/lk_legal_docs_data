No. 1640/11 – TUESDAY,  FEBRUARY 09, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:Bagama/DS/02/02/38,
I hereby give notice, under sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 One allotment of  land  in total extent of  0.0253 Hectares,
depicted   as  Lot No. 1,   in Piliminary  Plan  No. GA3695 and dated
10.12.2009, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated  in the village
of  Baddegama in  the Minor Division  of Baddegama in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Baddegama in the Galle District in Southern
Province and described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of  land: Wewelduwa; Description: Tea cultivation, about
50 Tea  shrubs 20 years old; Claimant: State; Original Claiment:
Maxim Panditharatne, Galgoda Road, Boralukada, Baddegama ;
Extent: 0.0253 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or  by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Baddegama  Divisional Secretariat on the 07 th April, 2010  at
2.30 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 31 st March, 2010 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
 P. S. Gunawardhane,
 Divisional Secretary,
Baddegama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Baddegama.
01st February, 2010.
