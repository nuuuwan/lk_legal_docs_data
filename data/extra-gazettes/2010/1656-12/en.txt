SRI  LANKA  TELECOMMUNICATIONS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1991
Interconnection  Rules  of  2003
RULES  made by the Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of Sri Lanka, under Section 68 of the Sri Lanka
Telecommunication Act, No. 25 of 1991 as amended by Act, No. 27 of 1996, and approved by the Minister.
Telecommunications Regulatory
Commission of  Sri Lanka.
Colombo,
31st May, 2010.
The Interconnection Rules of 2003, made under Section 68 of the Sri Lanka Telecommunications Act, No. 25 of 1991
as amended by Act, No. 27 of 1996 and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1278/18 of March 07, 2003, are hereby amended
as follows :
The repeal of the provisions in Rule 10(3)(b) and replacement of the repealed provisions with the following
provisions :
“The Commission shall reserve the right to introduce cost - oriented interconnection charging
methodology in addition to the criteria specified in sub paragraph (a) and shall be based on volume
and on per second basis”.
