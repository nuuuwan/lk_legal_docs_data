EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   CHILAW  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Ishan Uditha Ranasinghe Rajapaksha,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Chilaw  Pradeshiya   Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Janitha Dewapriya,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Abdul Offoor Mohamed Nafeel, Returning Officer of   Chilaw   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Janitha Dewapriya,
elected as  a  member  of   the Chilaw   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
05th February, 2010.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN OF  THE  ELLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  vice Chairman  in  Ella  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Weerasinghe Devage Ashoka Jayalal Weerasinghe,  the  Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1)
of the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that   Ella   Pradeshiya Sabha,  who belongs to  such  party  who
shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman   within a period of  Thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumathipala,  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of Ella  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumathipala, elected as  Vice Chairman of   the Ella  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ella  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Badulla,
03rd February, 2010.
