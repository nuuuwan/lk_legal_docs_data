EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  NELUWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Neluwa  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United Peopls’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Upul Priyantha Kumara Wijenayaka,   the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Neluwa   Pradeshiya  Sabha  who
belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.    Baraniwala Liyanage
Ananda being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of  Neluwa   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Baraniwala Liyanage Ananda,  elected as a member  of   the Neluwa  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Neluwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
22nd May, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  KARANDENIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Karandeniya  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognised political party to which
Thommaya Hewage Weerasena,   the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Karandeniya   Pradeshiya  Sabha  who
belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.    Ilandari Deva Samantha
Kumara, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of  Karandeniya  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Ilandari Deva Samantha Kumara,  elected as a member  of   the Karandeniya  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Karandeniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
