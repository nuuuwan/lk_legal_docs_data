THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: LD/05/2008/FH/134.
My Reference No.: 4/6/1-1/734A/03.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The Lot No. 01 in Surveyor General's Plan No. Mu.Pi.Ka. 4183
is situated at Grama Niladhari's Division of 734A Kuda Payagala
South in Divisional Secretary's Division of Beruwala in Kalutara
District, Western Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of Land: Samarappuwatta; Description: Land, Acquisition
for construction of fisheries Service centre; Claimants: Mrs Anne
Boys Frankleen, Pearl Bank Estate, Ambadande Gama, Bandarawela;
Extent: 0.0253 Hectare
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat of Beruwala on the 28th day of August,
2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18th day of August, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Wimal Gunathunga,
Divisional Secretary,
Beruwala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Beruwala.
