MUNICIPAL  COUNCILS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 576)
Notification Under Section 277(2)(a)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by section 277(2) (a) of Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 576), I,
Mahinda Rajapaksa, the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby appoint
Mr. Omar Zuraik Kamil of No. 93/1, Horton Place, Colombo 07, as a Special Commissioner with effect from 23rd June, 2010
to 31st December, 2010 to exercise the powers, perform the duties and discharge the functions of a Municipal Council
under the Municipal Council  Ordinance for the Colombo Municipal Council which has been dissolved.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo 01.
22nd June, 2010.
