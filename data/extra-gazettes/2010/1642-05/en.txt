THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 4/133/18/16.
I hereby give notice under  Sub section (1) of section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired  the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land acquired:
Three (03) allotments of  land in total extent of 0.0476 Hectare,
depicted in the F.V .P.133, Sheet 16, Supplement 18  dated 28.01.2008
prepared by Superintendent of Surveys, Kandy District, situated in
the village of Madawala in Udapalatha East Minor Division in
Thumpane Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kandy District in
Central Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 625
Name of land:Angamulla alias Galketiyawatta; Description:
Pathana; Claimant: State (Galagedara Multy Purpose Co-Operative
Socity); Extent: 0.0254 Hectare.
    2.Lot No. 627
Name of land:Angamulla alias Galketiyawatta; Description:
Pathana; Claimant: State (Galagedara Multy Purpose Co-Operative
Socity); Extent: 0.0061 Hectare.
   3.Lot No. 628
Name of land:Angamulla alias Galketiyawatta; Description :
Pathana; Claimant: State (Udaya Wijesundara and Robert Silva,
“Suragiri”, Galagedara); Extent: 0.0161 Hectare.
All the persons interested  in the aforesaid land are hereby required
to appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing ,
before me at Thumpane Divisional  Secretary’s  office  on the 08th
day of April, 2010 at 10.30 a. m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 1st day of April, 2010
the nature of their interests in the land,the particulars of their claims
for compensation  for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation  and details of the computation of such amount.
M.D.K.S. Suriyampola,
Divisional Secretary,
Thumpane.
Divisional Secretariat,
Thumpane - Galagedara,
27th August 2009.
