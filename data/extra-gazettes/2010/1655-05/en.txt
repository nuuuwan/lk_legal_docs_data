EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF DEPUTY  MAYOR OF  THE   KURUNEGALA   MUNICIPAL
COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  the  Deputy  Mayor in Kurunegala Municipal Council   has fallen vacant by reason  of  being
elected as a member of the Parliament  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Abayasinghe  Ashok ,  the  Deputy Mayor  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the member of that   Kurunegala Municipal Council  who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as   Deputy Mayor  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Anuradha Gamini Peramunage to fill such  vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  R. M. A. L. Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of the  Kurunegala Municipal Council ,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr.  Anuradha Gamini
Peramunage, elected as  Deputy Mayor of   the  Kurunegala Municipal Council .
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKE,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kurunegala Municipal Council .
Kurunegala,
18th May,  2010.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
 ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of a member in Kalmunai Municipal Council   has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased  to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which
Athambawa Sakeer Hussain,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) amended by the
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2007, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance  to appoint
as a member of that  Kalmunai Municipal Council  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Athambawa Jalaldeen
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,   Returning Officer of  Kalmunai Municipal Council,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   Athambawa Jalaldeen,
elected as  a  member  of   the  Kalmunai Municipal Council.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kalmunai Municipal Council.
Ampara,
13th May, 2010.
