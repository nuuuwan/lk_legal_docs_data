  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the  Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern  Province Provincial
Council  by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member  belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr.   Gunasoma Wickramasinha
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Gunasoma Wickramasinha, elected as a member of the   above  Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of  Hambantota  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
24th May, 2010.
