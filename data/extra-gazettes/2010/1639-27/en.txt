WIDOWERS’ AND  ORPHANS’  PENSION   ACT,  No.  24  OF  1983
Order under Section 3 (6) (a) and (b)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3(6)(a) and (b) of Widowers’ and Orphans’ Pension Act,No. 24 of 1983
as amended by Widowers’ and Orphans’ Pension (Amendment) Act, No. 02 of 2001, I, Dr. Sarath L. B. Amunugama, Minister
of Public Administration and Home Affairs do by this order determine in order to extend the duration to declare consent
as the last opportunity.
(i) If a female public officer who was employed as at 01.08.1983 decides to exercise the option to be a contributor
to the pension scheme before a given certain date, such date is to be cited as 30.06.2010,
(ii) If the female officer was retired, the arrears of contribution together with compound interest thereon to be
recovered in 180 installments in compliance with the provisions of  Pension Circular 3/2008,
(iii) Declare that if the female officer was dead, the relevant contribution and interest thereon be deducted
at once from the award and if the officer is yet in service, the total contribution and interest thereon to be
recovered before her retirement.
D
R. SARATH  L. B.  AMUNUGAMA,
