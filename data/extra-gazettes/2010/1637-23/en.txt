NOTICE  BY  THE  DIRECTOR  GENERAL  OF  CIVIL  AVIATION  UNDER  THE  AIR  NA VIGATION REGULATION 196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that I have received on 07th October, 2009 an application dated
07th October, 2009 from Cosmos Aviation Services (Pvt.) Ltd., at No. 29, Grenier Road, Cotta Road, Colombo 08, seeking
from this office an Air Operator Certificate/Charter License to engage in Domestic Charter  Operations and Aerial work
operations in pursuance of the Air Navigation Regulations of 1955. The members of the Board of Directors of
Cosmos Aviation Services (Pvt.) Ltd., are declared to be as follows :-
Mr. Sajin De V ass Gunawardena,
Mr. Vajira Kalinga Wijeyawardena,
Ms. Tiyana Deyanthi Anthonisz.
Cosmos Aviation Services (Pvt.) Ltd., has nominated Mr. Vajira Kalinga Wijeyawardena to be the Accountable Manager in
charge of the proposed operations of air Services.
A copy of the complete application of the aforementioned applicant is kept at the  Technical Library, Ground
Floor of Headquarters of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 64, Galle Road, Colombo 03, for the perusal of the
members of the public, during office hours of any working day.
Members of the Public are hereby informed that I have decided to commence after 12th February, 2010, the certification
process of this application as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant’s compliance with the
applicable Air Navigation Regulations. International Standards and Recommended Practices specified by ICAO and
associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure, efficient
operation  of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Charter License, and
Aerial Work License together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.

make representations to  me in writing, setting out the specific grounds on which such representations are made in respect
