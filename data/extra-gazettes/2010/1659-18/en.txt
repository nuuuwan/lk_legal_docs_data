EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise  Notification No.  916
EXCISE  DUTY  ON  LIQUORS   OTHER  THAN  TODDY  OR  ANY  LIQUOR  MADE  FROM  ANY  CEREAL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from 24th June, 2010—
(1) There shall be levied on Liquors (other than toddy or any liquor made from any cereal) containing more
than 4 per cent by V olume, of alcohol manufactured by process other than distillation from natural
products of the Palm Tree or any other plant, manufactured in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of Rupees
Five Hundred and Fifteen (Rs. 515.00) on every proof litre, provided that such duty shall not be levied
or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid liquor which is exported out of the Island ; and
(2) The Excise Notification No. 907 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1594/24 of 26.03.2009 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
23rd June, 2010.

EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 917
 DUTY  ON  MOLASSES,  PALMYRAH,  COCONUT  AND  PROCESSED  ARRACK
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from 24th June, 2010—
(1) There shall be levied on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed  Arrack manufactured in and issued
from any licensed manufactory established in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of Rupees Six Hundred and
Sixty (Rs. 660.00) per proof litre on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed Arrack provided that
such duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid  liquor which is exported out
of the Island ; and
(2) The Excise Notification No. 908 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1594/24 of 26.03.2009 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
23rd June, 2010.

EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 918
DUTY  ON  COUNTRY  MADE  FOREIGN  SPIRITS  MANUFACTURED  IN  SRI  LANKA
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from 24th June, 2010—
(1) There shall be levied on Country made “Foreign” spirits manufactured in Sri Lanka, a duty at the rate of
Rupees Seven Hundred and Ninety (Rs. 790.00) on every proof litre provided that such duty shall not be
levied or recovered on any quantity of the aforesaid “Foreign” spirits which is exported out of the Island ; and
(2) The Excise Notification No. 909 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1594/24 of 26.03.2009 is hereby
rescinded.

AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.

EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 919
EXCISE  ON MALT  LIQUOR
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended
from time to time, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance and Planning do by this Order direct that, with effect
from 24th June, 2010—
(a) There shall be imposed on Malt Liquor of less than five per centum (5%) of absolute strenth as indicated
in the labal, manufactured in and issued from any licensed brewery established in Sri Lanka, a duty at
the rate of Rupees Fifty Eight (Rs. 58/-) only on every litre so issued,
(b) There shall be imposed on Malt Liquor of Five per centum (5%) and above of absolute strength as
indicated in the label, manufactured in and issued from any licensed brewery established in Sri Lanka,
a duty at the rate of Rupees Seventy Four (Rs. 74/-) only on every litre so issued,
(c) The aforesaid duty shall not be levied or recovered on any quantity of such liquor which is issued from
such brewery for the use of any Diplomatic Mission in Sri Lanka or any foreign country, if payment
for such quantity of Liquor is made from the foreign account of such Diplomatic Mission ; and
(d) The Excise Notification No. 906 published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1570/8 of 07.10.2008 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
23rd June, 2010.
