THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land called "Hapugaspitiyawatta" in total extent
of about 0.0037 Hectares depicted as Lot "A" in the Advance Tracing
No.: Maha/PTD/2008/371, dated on 08.10.2008, surveyed and
prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys, Kandy District, situated
in the village of Girakaduwa in Pallegampaha Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Pathadumbara in Kandy District
and bounded as follows:
Lot "A" – Hapugaspitiyawatta – Extent: 0.0037 Hectare
North and East: Hapugaspitiyawatta claimed by U. M. Weerakoon;
South and West: Hapugaspitiyawatta claimed by U. M. Weerakoon
and Ela.
Jeewan Kumaranatunga,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Reference No.: LD/05/2008/WS/61.
"Govijana Mandiraya",
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla,
10th June, 2009.
