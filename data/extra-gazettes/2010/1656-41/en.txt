NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Thanamalwila  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Karunasingha Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning  Officer of the Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that  Mr.  Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Jagath
Suranimala,  member  of  the Thanamalwila Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased
to be a member of the  United  National Party.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Thanamalwila  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Monaragala,
20th May, 2010.
