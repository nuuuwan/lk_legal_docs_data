Local  Authorities  (Standard  By-Laws)  Act,  No.  6  of  1952
I,  Bertie Premalal Dissanayaka, the Provincial Minister  in-charge of Local Government of the North Central Provincial
Council, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Local Government Institutions (V oted By-Laws)
Act, No.  6 of 1952 to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989,
do hereby notify that the Rural Water Supply Schemes Managed by Community Based Organizations By-Laws made in terms
of Section 2 of the Local Government Institutions (V oted By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952 and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 1516/15, dated 25th September, 2007 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under
L.D.B. 6/87 II, be rescinded with effect from the date of publication of this notification in the Gazette.
