NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Pathahewaheta  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  U. Amaradasa,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare   under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  D. M. D. Aruna Shantha Gunatilaka, member   of  the
Pathahewaheta  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased   to be a member of the
Jathika Hela Urumaya.
U. A
MARADASA,
Returning Officer,
Pathahewaheta Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
