Government  Notifications
1A
REGULATION  7(1)  OF  THE  EMERGENCY  REGULATIONS  No. 1 OF  2009
IT  is hereby notified that in terms of the Proclamation made by me under  Section 5 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter
40) and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1583/12 of  January 7, 2009 and pursuant to the powers vested in me by the
Emergency (Proscription of the liberation Tiger of Tamil Ealam) Regulation, No. 1 of 2009, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1583/12 of  January 7, 2009, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Notification state that the immovable property
specified in the Schedule to this Notification has been forfeited to the State.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
28th January, 2010.
