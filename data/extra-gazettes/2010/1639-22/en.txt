1A
THE COCONUT DEVELOPMENT ACT, No. 46 OF 1971
L.D.B 22/71(ii).
Order under Section 30 and 31
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 30 and Sub-section (4) of Section 31 of the Coconut
Development Act, No. 46 of 1971, I, D. M. Jayaratne, Minister of Plantation Industries, do by this Order amend the Order
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 69/4 of December 31, 1979 as follows—
(1) in the First Schedule thereof by the addition immediately after item 8 of such Schedule of the following
functions —
“(9) The cultivation and assistance in and promotion and regulation of the cultivation of land with coconut ;
(10) The specification, popularization, promotion and direction of proper cultivation practices in respect
of the growing of coconut and other crops in coconut plantations.”
(2) in the Second Schedule thereof by the addition immediately after item 6 of such Schedule of the
following  —
“(7) The engaging  in, regulation, control, supervision direction, management and inspection of the
cultivation and utilization of land in coconut plantation and the cultivation  of land with coconut. ;”.
D. M. J
AYARATNE,
Minister of Plantation Industries.
Ministry of Plantation Industries.
Colombo,
03rd February, 2010.
