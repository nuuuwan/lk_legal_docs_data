MINISTRY  OF  PORTS  AND  AVIATION
Civil  Aviation Authority  of  Sri Lanka
NOTICE  BY  THE  DIRECTOR  GENERAL  OF  CIVIL  AVIATION  UNDER  THE  AIR  NAVIGATION  REGULATION  196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that I have received on 01st February, 2010 an application dated
25th January, 2010 from Senok Aviation (Pvt.) Ltd., at No. 3, R. A. De Mel Mawatha, Colombo 05. seeking from this office
an Air Operator  Certificate/Airline License to engage in Domestic Scheduled Operations and Charter License to engage
in Domestic Charter Operations in pursuance of the Air Navigation Regulations of 1955. The Director of the Board
of Senok Aviation (Pvt.) Ltd.,  is declared to be as follows :-
Mr. C. N. V . Selvanayagam
Senok Aviation (Pvt.) Ltd., has nominated Mr. Keerthi Dissanayake to be the Accountable Manager in charge of the
proposed operations of air services.
A copy of the complete application of the aforementioned applicant is kept at the Technical Library, Ground
Floor of Headquarters of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 64, Galle Road, Colombo 03, for the perusal
of the members of the public, during office hours of any working day.
Members of the Public are hereby informed that I have decided to commence after 20th April, 2010, the certification
process of this application as per the established procedures to examine the degree of applicant’s compliance with
the applicable Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended Practices specified by ICAO
and associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe, secure,
efficient operation of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate, Airline License
and Charter License together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
Any person may, not less than ten (10) days before the aforementioned date in the penultimate paragraph,
make representations to me in writing, setting out the specific grounds on which such representations are made in respect
of this application, in accordance with the Air Navigation Regulations. Such representation shall accompany written
