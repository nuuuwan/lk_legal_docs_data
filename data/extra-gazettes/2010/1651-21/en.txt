Appointments & c., by the President

IT is hereby notified that  the following Provincial Governors were appointed by His Excellency the President under
Article 154 B of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka :-
1. Hon.  C ALUW ADEV AGE  NANDA  MA THEW, as Governor of the Uva Province, with effect from 21.04.2010,

with effect from 21.04.2010.
