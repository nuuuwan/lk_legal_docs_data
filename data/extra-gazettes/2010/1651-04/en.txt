EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KEKIRAWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kekirawa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of   being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Waruna Kumara Thoradeniya,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kekirawa Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.    A. A. Le. Kabeer,  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  S. M. Saman Bandulasena,  Returning Officer of the  Kekirawa  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr.   A. A. Le Kabeer,
elected as  a  member  of   the  Kekirawa  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
S. M. S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kekirawa   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Anuradhapura,
17th April, 2010.
