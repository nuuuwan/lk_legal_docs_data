EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   MIHINTALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Mihintale  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Rangegama Gamladdalage Sumith Nihal,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid
Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Mihintale   Pradeshiya   Sabha
who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.  Kiribanda Samarakoon,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  S. M. Saman Bandulasena,   Returning Officer of  Mihintale   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   Kiribanda Samarakoon,
elected as a member of the Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. M. S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
Returning Officer,
Mihintale Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
18th March, 2010.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ADDALACHCHENAI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of a member in Addalachchenai Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased
to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which
Ahamed Lebbe Mohamed Farook,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) amended by the
Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2007, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance  to appoint
as a member of that  Addalachchenai   Pradeshiya   Sabha  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Abubacker Lebbe Abdul
Latheef,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,   Returning Officer of  Addalachchenai   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)(b)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Abubacker
Lebbe Abdul  Latheef, elected as  a  member  of   the  Addalachchenai  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Addalachchenai  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Ampara,
22nd March, 2010.
04-465
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   RAJGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of a member in Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Kombu Sampath De Silva,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Rajgama   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Thommadura Nuwan
Harindra Mendis,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,   Returning Officer of  Rajgama    Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Thommadura
Nuwan Harindra Mendis, elected as  a  member  of   the  Rajgama Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Galle,
24th March, 2010.
