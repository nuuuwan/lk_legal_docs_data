No. 1643/17 – TUESDAY,  MARCH 02, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
3/1/05/G
Reference No.:–––––––––––––––––––––
LD/05/2005/IR/240–E
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Seven (07) allotments of land in total extent of about 1.1016
Hectares, depicted in F.V .P. 2486, Paper 3, Supplementary Plan
No. 2, situated in the village of Walpaluwa in Walpaluwa Grama
Niladhari Division, No. 1254 in Dewamedda Korale Sub Division in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Wariyapola in Kurunegala
District in North Western Province and described as follows:
1. Lot No. 12
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Paddy field,
contain a well and 5 coconut trees between 10-15 years old; Claimant:
Mohottala Don Wickramarathna, Weherayagama, Minuwangate;
Extent: 0.1452 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 13
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden, contain
17 coconut trees between 10-15 years old; Claimant: Gaspe
Mudiyanselage Karunathilaka, Weherayagama, Minuwangate; Extent:
0.1677 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 14
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0497 Hectare.
4. Lot No. 15
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0744 Hectare.
5. Lot No. 16
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden, contain
17 coconut trees between 10-15 years old and one mee tree; Claimant:
Rathnayaka Arachchilage Ananda Rathnayaka, Karambahedigama,
Malagane; Extent: 0.5328 Hectare.
6. Lot No. 17
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Rathnayaka Arachchilage Ananda Rathnayaka,
Karambahedigama, Malagane; Extent: 0.0100 Hectare.
7. Lot No. 18
Name of land: Walpaluwe Mukalana; Description: Garden, contain
3 coconut trees between 10-15 years and 4 peak trees; Claimant:
Hitihamy Mudiyanselage Wimala Herath, Karambahedigama,
Malagane; Extent: 0.1218 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary's Office, Wariyapola, on the 26th day of
March, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of March, 2010,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
A. M. P. Arampath,
Divisional Secretary,
Wariyapola.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Wariyapola.
