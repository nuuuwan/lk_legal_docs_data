EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE  TANAMALWILA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a  member   in Tanamalwila  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which  Kumarasinghe
Wanni Arachchige Susantha Ranjith,  the  member  belonged, was required by me under section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Tanamalwila   Pradeshiya
Sabha,  who belongs to  such  party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  Thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Jagodige Jagath
Sunanda being an eligible person  under the aforesaid Ordinance, to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of Tanamalwila
Pradeshiya   Sabha,  acting in  terms of   section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare Mr.  Jagodige Jagath Sunanda, elected as  a member  of   the Tanamalwila   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Tanamalwila   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Monaragala,
07th June, 2010.

NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Minipe  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  U. Amaradasa,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Minipe  Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare  under  Section 10 A (1) (a) of the
