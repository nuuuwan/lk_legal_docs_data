No. 1642 /30 – FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No: DO/L1/Ath/Adi/81.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land in total  extent of 0.0087 Hectare, as Lot
No. 1, depicted  in Preliminary Plan No.Ka. 4228 and dated
30.12.2009 prepared by the Surveyor-General situated in Ukwatta
Village in the Dodangoda Divisional Secretary’s Division in the District
of Kalutara  in Western Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Batadombagahawatta; Description: Shrub Jungle;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M.D. Hemalatha, Rusiru Sevana,
Pembrookwatta, Kivulawatta, Bombuwala;   Extent: 0.0087 Hectare.
All peorsns interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Dodangoda Divisional  Secretariat on the 03rd May, 2010 at 10.00
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the 26th April, 2010, the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars or their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
H. W. T. R. Wettasingha,
Divisional Secretary,
Dodangoda.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Dodangoda.
10th February, 2010.
