SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Appointments  made  by  the Governor of  the Southern Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 154(F)(5)  of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasuriya, Governor of  the Southern Province, do hereby
announce that, in addition to his currently holding the office of Chief Minister and the subjects of the Ministry of
Finance Planning, Law and Order, Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health,
Indigenous Medicine and Local Government, the subjects of the Ministry of Education and Cultural, Food Supply
and Distribution, Trade and Co-operative Development and the subjects of the Ministry of Land and Land Development,
Irrigation, Provincial Roads, Information and Rural and Estate Infrastructure Facilities have been assigned to the
Chief Minster, Hon. Shan Wijayalal Silva with effect from 04th March, 2010.
N
ERANJALA PUSHPA KUMARI BALASURIYA,
 Governor of  Southern Province.
Office of the Governor,
Dickson Road,
Galle,
04th March, 2010.
