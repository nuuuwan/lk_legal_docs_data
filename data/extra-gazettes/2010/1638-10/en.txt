PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS ACT, NO. 15 OF 1981
Notice under Section 64(2)
I , Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, do hereby notify under Section 64(2) of the Presidential Elections Act,
No. 15 of 1981, that, Mr. Mahinda Rajapaksha has been declared elected as the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, under Section 56 of the aforesaid Act.
