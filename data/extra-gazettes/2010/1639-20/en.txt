THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No. pahe/prale/3/1/3/6.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2008/D/117.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land bearing an extent of about 0.2151 Hectare,
depicted as Lot No. 1 in Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6024, prepared
by the Surveyor-General situated in the Mudunakade Village in the
Grama Niladhari Division of Bootawatta North in Gandahaya Korale
of Pathahewaheta Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kandy District
in Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Gallenahena alias Kollanwalapitiyehena; Claimant:
H. W. Swarnatilake, No. 200/A, Kollanwalapitiyehena, Bootawatta,
Talatuoya; Extent: 0.2151 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Pathahewaheta, on the 18th day of March,
2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 04th day of March, 2010, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation  and
the details of the computation of such amount.
G. H. Jayaweera,
Divisional Secretary,
Pathahewaheta.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Pathahewaheta.
