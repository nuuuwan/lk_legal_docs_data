1A
THE  EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 912
BY  virtue of   powers vested in me by Section 32(1) of the  Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), read with Article 44(2)
of the  Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President  of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri  Lanka, do by this Notification declare that the rules made me under aforesaid Section
and set  out in the Schedule hereto shall come into force from 01st March, 2010.
Excise Notification, No. 744 published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 362/6A, of August, 14, 1985 is hereby
rescinded.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President
Ministry of Finance and Planning,  Minister of Finance and Planning.
Colombo 01,
09th February,  2010.
SCHEDULE
R
ULES
(1) A duty at such rates determined from time to time under Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52)
and published in the Gazette  shall be payable by the Licensee on every litre of Beer, Arrack or
Spirits manufactured by such licensee on or before the 15th day of the succeeding month in respect
of the production in a particular month.
(2) A licensee who fails to pay the duty on the due  date shall be liable to pay in addition to the duty, a surcharge
on the same at the rate of three per centum per month, as a fee.
(3) The concession given to a licensee to pay the duty as specified in above condition (2) shall be limited
to maximum six months from the last date which the licensee has to pay the duty.
