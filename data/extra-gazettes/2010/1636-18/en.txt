Proclamation  made  by  the  Governor  of  North  Central  Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 8(b) of Section 154(b) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Karunarathne Divulgane,  Governor of North Central Province declare that the
Second Session of the Fifth Provincial Council of the North Central Province shall terminate from mid-night of
24th  November, 2009 and
Further by powers vested in me under paragraph 8(a) of Section 154(b) of the aforesaid Constitution the
third Session of the Fifth Provincial Council shall meet at 9.20 a.m. on 20th January 2010 at the Meeting Hall  of the
North Central Provincial Council.
K
ARUNARATHNE DIVULGANE,
Governor,
North Central  Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Province,
13th January, 2010.
