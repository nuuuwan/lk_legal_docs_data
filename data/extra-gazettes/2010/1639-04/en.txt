SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
NOTICE  under Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 15 of 1989 and Sections 3 of the Southern Province Provincial Council’s Local
Authorities Administraive Supervision Powers Statute, No. 4 of 1990.
I, Shan Wijayalal De Silva, Subject Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport, Water Supply and
Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine and Local Government of the Southern Province
Provincial Council do in Pursuance of the Provisions of Section 185(2) of the Pradeshiya Sabaha Act, No. 15 of 1987 read
with Section 2 of the Provincial Council’s (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 2 (2) of the
Southern Province Provincial Council’s Local Authorities Administrative Supervision Powers Statute, No. 4 of 1990, appoint,
Mr. Edirimuni Samith De Silva, retired Judicial Officer for the purpose of inquiry into the matters related to Section 185(1)
of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act and Section 2(1) of the Southern Province Provincial Council’s Local Authorities
Administrative Supervision Powers Statute, No. 4 of 1990, and to report the conduct Mr. Hewa Lokuge Jayasiri when
functioning as Chairman of the Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha of Hambantota District.
02. Furthermore by virtue of Powers vested in me under Section 185(3) (a)(i) Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987,
read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 3 of the
Southern Province Provincial Council’s Local Authorities Administrative Powers Statute, No. 4 of 1990, I, Shan Wijayalal
De Silva, Subject Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering
Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine and Local Government of the Southern Province Provincial Council, do
hereby suspend with immediate effect, the functions and duties of Mr. Hewa Lokuge Jayasiri as Chairman of the
Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha of Hambantota District.
03. Furthermore as I have suspended Chairman of the Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, I direct
Mr. Sirisena Rathnayaka Weerakoon the Vice Chairman of the Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, to exercise the powers and
perform the duties of the Chairman of the Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha with immediate effect according to the
Section 185(3) (a)(i) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 3(2) of the Southern Province Provincial Council’s Local Authorities
Administrative Supervision Powers Statute, No. 4 of 1990.
S
HAN WIJAYALAL DE SILV A,
Subject Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health,
01st February, 2010. Indigenous Medicine and Local Government, Southern Province.
