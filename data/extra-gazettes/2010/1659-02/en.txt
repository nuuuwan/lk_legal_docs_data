PROCLAMATION  BY  THE  HONOURABLE  GOVERNOR  OF  SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 154B Sub-sections (8) (a) and (8) (b) of the Thirteenth
Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic   Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I,   W. J. M. Lokubandara,   the  Governor
of the Sabaragamuwa  Province, do by this proclamation prorogue the Provincial Council of the Sabaragamuwa Province
with effect from midnight of the Twenty-second day of June Two Thousand Ten and fix the Twentieth day of July
Two Thousand Ten as the date for the commencement of the next session of the Provincial Council of the
Sabaragamuwa Province.
Given at Rathnapura this Fourteenth day of June Two Thousand Ten.
W. J. M. L
OKUBANDARA,
 Governor,
Sabaragamuwa  Province.

PROCLAMATION  BY  THE  HONOURABLE  GOVERNOR  OF  SABARAGAMUWA  PROVINCE
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 154B Sub-section (8) (a)  of the Thirteenth  Amendment
to the Constitution of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I,   W. J. M. Lokubandara,  the  Governor of the

Given at Rathnapura this Fourteenth day of June Two Thousand Ten.
W. J. M. L
OKUBANDARA,
 Governor,
Sabaragamuwa  Province.
