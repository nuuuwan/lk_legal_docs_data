EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   PELIYAGODA  URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Peliyagoda  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Death   ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Ranepura Devage Piyasiri Senevirathna,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Peliyagoda  Urban  Council
who  belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.    Maakawitage Lakshman
Rohana Perera,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mohamed Mahdoom Mohamed,  Returning  Officer of Peliyagoda Urban Council,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Maakawitage Lakshman Rohana Perera,  elected as  a member  of   the  Peliyagoda Urban Council.
M. M. M
OHAMED,
Returning Officer,
Peliyagoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
16th June, 2010.
