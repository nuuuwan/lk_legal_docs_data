NATIONAL WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD ACT, No. 02 OF 1974
Order under Section 64(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 64(1) of the National Water  Supply  and Drainage Board
Act,  No. 02  of 1974, I, Ahamed Lebbe Marikkar Athaullah, the Minister of Water Supply and Drainage, do by this order,
embody in the Schedule hereto the provisions of the voluntary transfer scheme approved by me under Section 63(1)
of the aforesaid Law, in respect of the water supply undertakings on by the Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha.
A
HAMED LEBBE MARIKKAR ATHAULLAH,
Minister of Water Supply and Drainage.
Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage,
No. 34,  Narahenpita Road,
Nawala.
25th February, 2010.
SCHEDULE
Contents of the V oluntary Transfer Scheme Section 61(1) :
(a) The follwoing movable and immovable property to be transferred to and vested in the National Water
Supply and Drainage Board to wit,
(i) All water sources, service reservoirs, water treatment plants and balancing tanks which belong
to the Galewela Water Supply Scheme of the Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha and all lands, structures,
water mains and installations of all the mechanical, electrical plant and machinery to operate and
maintain the service reservoirs, pumping stations together with all spares, stand by equipment and
stores required for the maintenance of such machinery.
(ii) All trunks and distribution mains together with servitude for water mains, spares standby equipment and
stores necessary to operate and maintain the water supply scheme of Galewela.

(b) The rights, obligations and liabilities of the Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha together with any contract the
council has entered into in connection with design, supply, installation, construction, operation and maintenance
with respect of the aforesaid property will also be transferred to the Board.
(c) All agreements entered into with water consumers with regard to conditions of the use and supply of
water services and payment to obtain such services will also be transferred to the Board.
(d) The assets of these facilities valued at Rs. 1,225,000.00 (Rupees One Million Two Hundred and Twenty

(e) All agreements entered into by the Pradeshiya Sabha in connection with water supply will also continued
and honored by the Board.
The above transfer will come into effect from the date of publication of this notice.
