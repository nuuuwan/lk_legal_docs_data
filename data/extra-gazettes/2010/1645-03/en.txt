EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & c., by the Governors
1A
 CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 154E(5) of the 13th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of Central Province hereby notify that Hon. Sarath
Ekanayake Chief Minister of Central Province, Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local Government
and Provincial Administration, Manpower, Education and Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Lands, Co-operative
Development Trade and Commerce, Food Supplies and Distribution Affiars and Investment Co-ordinating and the
Subjects which are not taken under other Ministeries, I appoint Hon. Sarath Ekanayake for the appointments indicated
in the Schedule below, in addition to the above subjects, with effect from 5th March, 2010.
* Minister of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare and Probation and Child Care Services in
Central Province,
* Minister of Agriculture, Minor Irrigations, Animal Production and Development, Agrarian
Development, Fisheries and Environmental Affairs in Central Province.
* Minister of Industries, Sports, Women Affairs, Rural Development, Estate Infrastructure
Facilities Development, Hindu Cultural Affairs, Education (Tamil) and Youth Affiars in Central  Province.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUWA,
Governor,
Central Province.
