ANIMAL DISEASES ACT,  No. 59 OF 1992
Proclamation
WHEREAS foot and mouth Disease has broken out among cattles in Kundasale and Medamahanuwara Divisional
Secretary Divisions in Kandy District of the Central Province, I, Alawaththage Don Nimal Chandrasiri, the Director
General of the Animal production and Health by virtue of the powers vested in me under the Animal disease Act, No. 59
of 1992, do hereby declare the area within the following boundaries as an “infected area”.
East : Western border of Ududhumbara Divisional Secretary Division ;
West : South-East border of Pahathadhumbara Divisional Secretary Division and North-East border
of Gangawatakorale Divisional Secretary Division ;
North : Southern border of Panwila  Divisional Secretary Division ;
South : North-East border of Pahathahewahatta Divisional Secretary Division and Northern border of
Hanguranketha Divisional Secretary Division.
Under regulation 5(3), I, Alawaththage Don Nimal Chandrasiri the Director General of the Animal Production
and Health do hereby proclaim that no movement of cattle or cart traffic from and to this area shall be allowed, until this
proclamation is revoked.
The attention of cattle owners and carters in this area is drawn to the Animal Diseases Act, No. 59 of 1992
which lays down the action which persons are by law required to take in an “Infected Area” Details of these regulations can
be obtained from the government Veterinary Surgeons at Kundasale and Teldeniya and the Divisional Secretaries.
This proclamation shall take effect from the date hereof.
A
LAW A THTHAGE DON NIMAL CHANDRASIRI,
  Director General,
Animal Production and Health.
Office of the Director General,
Department of Animal Production and Health,
Peradeniya.
22nd April  2010.
