THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for laying
the Sewerage Pipe Line of the Sewerage Project of Hikkaduwa and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land called Dombagahawatta in extent of
about 0.0142 Hectare (the Assmt. No. can't be found), depicted
as Lot 'A' in Advance Tracing No. GA/HKK/08/347, dated 13th
September, 2008, prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys of
Galle, situated in the village of Pannamgoda in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Pannamgoda, No. 57A in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Hikkaduwa in Galle District in Southern Province and
bounded as follows:
North: Eramudugahawatta claimed by G. Shirani de Silva,
Dombagahawatta claimed by K. P . Silva and the Railway Reserve;
East: Railway Reserve and Dombagahawatta claimed by S. A.
Senanayaka; South: Dombagahawatta claimed by S. A. Senanayaka
and Lot C in the Tracing No. GA/HKK/05/583;West: Lot C in the
Tracing No. GA/HKK/05/583 and Eramududgahawatta claimed by
G. Shirani de Silva.
