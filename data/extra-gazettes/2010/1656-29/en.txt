1A
ANIMAL  DISEASES ACT, No.  59 OF 1992
Revocation
NOTICE is hereby given that the areas declared by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1650/24 of 22.04.2010
as infected  area  in Divisional Secretary Divisions of Kundasale and Medadumbara in Kandy District under the
provisions of  the Animal Diseases Act, No. 59 of 1992 are free of foot and mouth disease and are no longer infected areas.
This declaration shall take effect from the date hereof.
