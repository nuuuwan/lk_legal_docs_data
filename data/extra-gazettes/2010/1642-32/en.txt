1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF A MEMBER OF THE KALMUNAI  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member in Kalmunai  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of   death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which
Kumarasamy Thurairasa,   the member  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that   Kalmunai  Municipal Council    who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.   Theiwasingamani
Chandrakumar  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning Officer of  Kalmunai  Municipal Council ,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Theiwasingamani
Chandrakumar,  elected as a member of   the Kalmunai  Municipal Council .
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
17th  February,  2010.
