No. 1635/29 – FRIDAY, JANUARY 08,  2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No. KA/LD/1/2/17.
I hereby give notice under  sub section (1) of section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired the following land
which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land acquired:
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of 0.0016Hectare,
depicted in the P .P. No. GA. 3702 and dated 13.11.2009  prepared by
the Superintendent of Surveys Galle District, situated in the village of
Mahagoda in Wellabodapattuwa Minor Division in Mahagoda
Gramaniladhari Division in Karandeniya Divisional Secretary’s
Division in Galle District in Southern Province morefully described
as follows:
1. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Bogahawatta; Description: Cement rendered drain;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: N. Nanasena and H.D.
Chamindasiri; Extent: 0.0002 Hectare.
    2.Lot No. 02
Name of land: Bogahawatta; Description: Cement rendered drain;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: H.D. Chamindasiri and Champa
Seneviratne; Extent: 0.0007 Hectare.
    3.Lot No. 03
Name of land: Bolgahawatta; Description: Cement rendered drain;
Claimant: State; Original claimant: K.D. Susila; Extent: 0.0007
Hectare.
All the persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Karandeniya Divisional  Secretary’s  office  on the 17th
day of February, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of February,
2010 the nature of their interests in the land,the particulars of their
claims for compensation  for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation  and details of the computation of such amount.
R.H. Kamal Ranasinghe,
Actg. Divisional Secretary,
Karandeniya.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Karandeniya.
26th November, 2009.
