SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
NOTICE  under Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 3 of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Provincial Council
and Section  2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I, Shan Wijayalal De Silva, the Minister  in-charge of the Subjects of  Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and  Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine and Local Government
of the Southern Province Provincial Council, acting under the powers vested in me under Section 185(2) of the
Pradeshiya Sabaha Act, No. 15 of  1987, read with Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Government Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, have by Gazette Extraorindary No. 1639/4 of 01.02.2010
suspended  with immediate effect Mr. Hewa Lokuge Jayasiri, the Chairman of Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha from
functioning  as the Chairman of the said Pradeshiya Sabha and, in terms of Section 185(3)(a)(i) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 3(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government
Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern  Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Ancillary Provisions)  Act, No. 12 of 1989, ordered Mr. Sirisena Rathnayaka Weerakone, the Vice Chairman of
Tissamaharama Pradeshiya   Sabha to exercise and perform the powers and functions of the office of the Chairman of
the said Pradeshiya Sabha.
02. Since Mr. Sirisena Rathnayaka Weerakone has by his letter dated 03.02.2010, declined to exercise and
perform the powers and functions of the office of the Chairman of Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha, I make
order under Section 185 (3)(a) (i) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, read with Section 3(2) of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial
Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, that Mr. Bogahapitiya
Serasinghe Gamage Ruwansiri, the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Hambantota District, shall exercise
the powers and perform the functions of the office of the chairman of Tissamaharama Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
HAN WIJAYALAL DE SILV A,
Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services,
Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local Government,
Southern Province.
05th February, 2010.
