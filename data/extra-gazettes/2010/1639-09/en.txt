No. 1639 /9 – TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 02, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Lot ‘A’ Part of Assmt. No. 982 of Kotte Road, Allotments of land
in total extent of about 0.0059 Hectare, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, depicted in Advance Tracing No. CO/KTT/2008/579 and
29th May 2009, within the Urban Council Limits of Sri
Jayawardanapura Kotte Atulkotte, Division No. 5 of situated the
village of Atulkotte in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Sri-
Jayawardanapura Kotte, in Colombo District in Western Province
bounded as follows:
North: Atulkotte, Kotte Road, Assmt. No. 982/A and Sri Buddha
Jayanthi Road; East: Atulkotte, Kotte Road, Assmt. No. 982; South:
Wickramasinhapura Road and Atulkotte, Kotte Road, Assmt. No.
982; West: Sri Buddha Jayanthi Road.
Jeewan Kumaranatunga,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: LD/05/2007/HW/121.
Divisional Secretary’s Reference No.: Srijapura/EA/6/54.
