Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Wedi/03/Adhi/Ma/2008/15.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 One (01) allotment of land in total extent of 0.0307  Hectares, as
Lot Nos. 1, depicted  in P .P.Ga. 3739 and dated 25.02.2010 prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in Nugethota Village in the Welivitiya-
Divithura Divisional  Secretary’s Division in the District of Galle   in
Southern Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Mottakanda, Baddegama Wathuyaya; Description:
Open Wasteland; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Land Reform

writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th day of June,  2010 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
                 D. Kusalani de Silva,
