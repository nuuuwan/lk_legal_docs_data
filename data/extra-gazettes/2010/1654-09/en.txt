NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,  No.  02  OF  1974
Order under Section 64(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 64(1) of the National Water  Supply  and Drainage Board
Act,  No. 2  of 1974, I, Dinesh Gunawardena, the Minister of Water Supply and Drainage, do by  this order embody
in the Schedule hereto, the provisions of the V oluntary Transfer Scheme approved by me under   Section 63(1) of the
aforesaid Law, in respect of the water supply undertakings on Dambawela, Elhena, Maligawatta, Sarasavigama,
Ogastawatta, Udabowala, Mahakanda, Hindagala and Udaperadeniya maintained by the Kandy Four Gravets  Pradeshiya
Sabha.
D
INESH GUNAWARDENA,
Minister of Water Supply and Drainage.
Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage,
No. 34,  Narahenpita Road,
Nawala,
14th day of May,  2010.
SCHEDULE
Contents of the V oluntary Transfer Scheme Section 61(1) :
(a) The following movable and immovable property to be transferred to and vested in the  National Water Supply
and Drainage Board to wit,
(i) All water sources, service reservoirs, water treatment plants and balancing tanks which belong to the
Dambewela, Elhena, Mailgawatta, Sarasavigama, Ogastawatta, Udabowala, Mahakanda, Hindagala and
Udaperadeniya Water Supply Scheme of the Kandy Four Gravets and all lands, structures, water
mains and installments of all the mechanical, electrical plant and machinery to operate and maintain the
service reservoirs, pumping stations together with all spares, standby equipment and stores required for
the maintenance of such machinery.

(ii) All trunks and distribution main together with servitude for water mains, spares standby equipment and
