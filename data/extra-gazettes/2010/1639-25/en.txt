1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN OF  THE  HIKKADUW A  URBAN
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Hikkaduwa Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary  of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to
which  Waduge Priyantha the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Hikkaduwa Urban Council who  belongs to such party who shall be declared  elected as Vice
Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party   has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Minal Sembakutti to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Anthoney Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Hikkaduwa Urban Council, acting in  terms
of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare   Mr.  Minal Sembakutti, elected
as Vice Chairman of the Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Hikkaduwa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Galle.
18th  January, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  CHILAW URBAN COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Chilaw Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending more than three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary  of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Antony Rodrigo, the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to nominate a
person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Chilaw Urban Council  who belongs to such party  who
shall be declared  elected as a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party   has nominated Mr.  Lakshman Fernando being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Offoor Mohamed Nafeel,  Returning Officer of the  Chilaw Urban Council, acting
in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Lakshman Fernando, elected as a member of the Chilaw Urban Council .
A. O. M. N
AFEEL,
Returning Officer,
Chilaw Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam.
21st January, 2010.
