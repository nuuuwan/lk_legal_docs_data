PRADESHIYA  SANWARDHANA  BANK  ACT, No. 41 OF  2008
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (1) of Section 42 of the Pradeshiya Sanwardhana Bank Act, No. 41
of 2008 read with article 44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, the President of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka do by this order vest the business presently carried on by the Development Banks established
under the Regional Development Bank Act, No. 06 of 1997 specified in the Column I of the Schedule hereto, in the
acquiring bank specified in the Column II of the such Schedule.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
  President.
Colombo,
30th  April, 2010.
