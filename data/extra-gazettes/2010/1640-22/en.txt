THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Anu/4/5/1/2/1.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: LD/05/2009/D/57.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of  land called Kanugahawewa Kele in total extent of
0.244 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 480 in Final Village Plan No. 122,
situated in the village of Kanugahawewa in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Rambewa, Anuradhapura District in North Central
Province and bounded as follows:
North and East: Lot No. 481; South: Boundary of
Rathmalgahawewa village; West: Boundary of Rathmalgahawewa
village and Lot No. 481.
An allotment of  land called Hikgahahena in total extent of
1.789 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 355 in Final Village Plan
No. 124, situated in the village of Rathmalgahawewa in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Rambewa, Anuradhapura District in North
Central Province and bounded as follows:
North: Boundary of Kanugahawewa village; East: Boundary of
Kanugahawewa village and Lot No. 354; South:  Lot No. 117; West:
Lot No. 356.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Rambewa, on the 05th day of March,
2010 at 11.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25th day of February, 2010,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
S. N. G. Abeysekera,
Asst. Divisional Secretary,
Rambewa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Rambewa.
02nd February, 2010,
