EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   PASSARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Passara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Anawalangu Mudiyanselage Nimal Rupasena Rajakaruna, the member  belonged, was required by me under
Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member
of  that   Passara Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a  period
of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Dissanayake
Mudiyanselage Kalubanda,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of Passara
Pradeshiya  Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
do hereby declare  Mr. Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Kalubanda, elected as  a member of   the Passara Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Passara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Badulla,
10th March, 2010.
