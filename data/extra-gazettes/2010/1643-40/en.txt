No. 1643/40 – THURSDAY, MARCH 04, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Ara/L/1/122.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Land called Veppe Maranthadi Vayalkani depicted as Lot No. 01
in Plan No. P .P.PU 4596 made by the superintendent of surveys of
Puttalam District were the boreholes of the Water Supply Scheme
of Karukapane are situated and continuing in extent 0.0007 hectare
and bounded as follows:
North: Veppe Maranthadi V ayalkani claimed  by K. Kanapathipille
and Veppe Maranthadi Vayalkani claimed by the Government; East:
V eppe Maranthadi Vayalkani claimed by the Government and Lot
No. 01 in P.P.PU 4417; South: Veppe Maranthadi Vayalkani claimed
by the Government and Veppe Maranthadi Vayalkani claimed by
K. Kanapathipille; West: Veppe Maranthadi V ayalkani claimed by
K. Kanapathipille.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat, Arachchikattuwa on the 21st day of
April, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day of April, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
H. S. S. Lusena,
Divisional Secretary,
Arachchikattuwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Arachchikattuwa.
01st March, 2010.
