No. 1636 / 20 – FRIDAY,  JANUARY 15, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:Bagama/DS/L2/15.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 Six allotment of  land  in total extent of  0.3246Hectares,dipicted
as  Lot No 1, 2, 3,4,5 and 6  in Piliminary  Plan  No Ga3694  and dated
12th November, 2009 ,prepared   by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the village of  Kohombanadeniy in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Baddegama in the Galle District in Southern Province and
described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Baduwatta Addara Ovita; Description: mixed
cultivation of 14 Coconut trees of 30 years age, 5 King Coconut
trees,2 Jack trees,30 arecanut trees, 2 Ambarella trees, One belli tree
and one kohomba tree ; Claimant: State; Original Claiment: U.J.
Amaradasa,Kohombanadeniya, Narawala,Poddala; Extent: 0.1003
Hectare.
    2.Lot No. 2
Name of  land: Baduwatta Addara Ovita; Description: mixed
cultivation of  03 ,Jack trees of  20 years age, 2 King Coconut trees of
10 and 10 arecanut trees; Claimant: State; Original Claiment: U.J.
Fransis,Kohombanadeniya, Narawala,Poddala; Extent: 0.0151 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of  land: Baduwatta Addara Ovita; Description: mixed
cultivation,  03  Jack trees of  20 years age, 2 King Coconut trees of  8
years age and 20  arecanut  trees; Claimant: State; Original Claiment:
U.J. Buddadasa,Kohombanadeniya, Narawala,Poddala; Extent: 0.0415
Hectare.
4. Lot No. 4
Name of  land: Baduwatta Addara Ovita; Description: mixed
cultivation,  03  Jack trees of  20 years age, 10  Coconut trees of  30
years age , 15 arecanut trees and one Mahogani Tree; Claimant: State;
Original Claiment: U.J. Sirisena,Kohombanadeniya, Narawala,
Poddala; Extent: 0.0710 Hectare.
5. Lot No. 5
Name of  land: Aluthgedara watta; Description: vacant Land;
Claimant: State; Original Claiment: K.H.Jayasena,Weliwatta, Galle;
Extent: 0.0748 Hectare.
6. Lot No. 6
Name of  land: Aluthgedara watta; Description: Vacant Land;
Claimant: State; Original Claiment: J.W. Pathirana,Weliwatta, Galle;
Extent: 0.0219 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or  by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at  Baddegama  Divisional Secretariat on the 24th February, 2010, at
2.30 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before 17th February, 2010, the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
P.S. Gunawardhane,
Divisional Secretary,
Baddegama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Baddegama,
04th December, 2009.
