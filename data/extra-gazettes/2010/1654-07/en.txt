PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & c., by the Governors
1A
 CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 154E(5) of the 13th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of Central Province hereby notify that the
appointments indicated in the Schedule below were done by me with effect of 12th May, 2010 :
(i) Hon. Amaratunga  Sunil  Kithsiri — Minister of Health, Indigeneous Medicine, Social Welfare and
Probation and Child Care Services in  Central Province,
(ii) Hon. Mrs. Atampan Anushiya Sivarajah — Minister of Industries, Sports, Women Affairs, Rural
Development, Estate Infrastructure Facilities Development,
Hindu Cultural Affairs, Education (Tamil) and Youth Affairs in
Central  Province.
(iii) Hon. Apanayake Nimal Piyatissa — Minister of Agriculture, Minor Irrigation, Animal Production and
Development, Agrarian Development, Fisheries and
Environmental Affairs in Central Province.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUWA,
Governor,
Central Province.
