Amendment to the Survey Regulations – 2005
made under Section 39 of the Survey Act
It is hereby informed to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka that the paragraph 2(2) of the Land Surveys –
2005 (Registration) Regulation approved by me under Section 65 of
the Survey Act,  No. 17 of 2002 to be read with Section 39 of  the
Act, in terms of the Gazette No.: 1420/20 of 23rd November, 2005 is
amended with inclusion of the under mentioned sub paragraphs.

Development.
