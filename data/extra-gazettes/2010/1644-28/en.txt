   PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 1 OF 1981
Filling of a Vacancy under Section 64(5)
WHEREAS the Acting Secretary-General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64(5) of the Parliamentary
Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Section  6  of the Elections  (Special Provisions) Act, No. 35 of 1988, that a
vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Sixth Parliament  by reason of the expulsion from the party of
Mr. Meera Mohideen Mohomed Mustafa who was nominated by the United National Party, a recognized political party,
and declared elected  as a  Member of Parliament  under Article 99 A of the Constitution ;
And whereas  the Secretary of that Political Party was required by me under the aofresaid section to nominate
an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforedsaid Political Party has nominated Mr. Meera Mohideen Mohomed
Mustafa to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers conferred
on me by Section 64(5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, do hereby declare Mr. Meera Mohideen
Mohomed Mustafa elected as a member of the Sixth  Parliament to fill the vacancy caused by the expulsion of aforesaid
Mr. Meera Mohideen Mohomed Mustafa.
D
AYANANDA DISSANAY AKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
11th March, 2010.
