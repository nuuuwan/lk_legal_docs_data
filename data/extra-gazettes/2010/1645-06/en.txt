The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Order No. 12.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09 OF 2003
Order  under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003,
the   Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no manufacturer, importer, packer, distributor or trader  shall sell, expose or
offer for sale, display for sale any 50 kg bag of  Cement bearing the following generic names and sold under any Brand
name, above the maximum retail prices given  below.
Generic Name Maximum Retail Price
(per 50  Kg bag)
Ordinary Portland Cement Rs. 750.00
Portland Limestone Cement Rs. 750.00
Masonry Cement Rs. 700.00
This Order shall come into effect from 16th March, 2010.
               By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
 Chairman.
Colombo,
15th March, 2010.
