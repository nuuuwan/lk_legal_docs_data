SECTION 4 OF THE PRESIDENTIAL  ELECTIONS ACT, No. 15 OF 1981
Change of Situation of Polling Stations
NOTICE  is hereby given that in terms of Section 4 (4) of the Presidential Elections Act, No. 15 of 1981 locations of
the Polling Stations in the following Polling  Districts which were indicated in the relevant notifications in Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 1632/25 of 18.12.2009 in terms of  Section 4 of the Act, under reference, have been altered to read as
recorded   in the Schedule below. The Polling Districts referred to herein are : Polling District No. 81 of Vavuniya “B” of
No. 11  V anni Electoral District and Polling District No. 6 of Seruvila “A” of  No. 14 Trincomalee Electoral District.
