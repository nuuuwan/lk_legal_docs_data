 CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
The Order made under Section 277(1A)  of the Municipal Councils Ordinance Chapter 252 to be  read with
Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)  of  Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 2(3) (a) (i) of
Enactment of Supervision of Local Authorities Administration, No. 7 of 1990
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 277 (1A)  of the Municipal Councils Ordinance Chapter 252 to  be read with
Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) of  Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 2(3) (a) (i) of Enactment
of Supervision of Local Authorities Administration No. 7 of 1990 of the Central Province Provincial Council, I,  Sarath
Ekanayake,  Chief Minister of Central Province and Minister-in-charge of Local Government, do hereby suspend temporarily
with immediate effect of Mr. L. B. Aluvihare Mayor, Kandy Municipal Council from holding his post.
Further, by virtue of powers vested in me by Section 277 (1B)(a)(i) of the Municipal Council Ordinance, to be read
with Section 2 of Provincial Council Act, (Consequential Provisions) of Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 2(3)(a)(i) of
Enactment of supervision and Administration of Local Authorities No. 07 of 1990, I hereby direct Mr. K. L. Suminda
Wickramasinghe, the Deputy Mayor of Kandy Municipal Council, Kandy District, to be perform the duties of the
Mayor and use the powers of the Mayor of Kandy Municipal Council Kandy District of Central Province.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
Chief Minister of Central Province and
Minister-in-charge of Local Government.
Chief Minister’s Office,
Kandy,
08th February, 2010.

PART IV (A)  - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 10.02.2010
2A
CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 277 (1A)  of the Municipal Councils Ordinance Chapter 252 to  be read
with  Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) of  Act, No. 12 of 1989 and Section 2(2)  of
Enactment  of Supervision of Local Authorities Administration No. 7 of 1990 of the Central Province Provincial Council,
I,  Sarath Ekanayake,  Chief Minister of Central Province and Minister-in-charge of Local Government, do hereby appoint
Mr. N. Mahendrarajah, to inquire and report within  3 months time from the dated, Whether the Kandy Municipal Council
Mayor Mr. L. B. Aluvihare has committed may misdeeds described in Section 277(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance
and Section 2(1) of Enactments of the Local Authorities of Central Province.
This officer shall have the powers of commission appointed under the inquiry of Commission Act, pertaining
to this inquiry.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
Chief Minister of Central Province and
Minister-in-charge of Local Government.
Chief Minister’s Office,
Kandy,
08th February, 2010.
