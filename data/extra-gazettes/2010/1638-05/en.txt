No. 1638 / 5 –MONDAY, JANUARY 25, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Ta/5/3/73.
Land Min. Ref. No.: LD/05/2008/NRD/440.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired the following land
which is required for a public purpose. For the distribution of alternative
lands in place of lands acquired for the construction of new railway
line from Matara to Kataragama.
Description of the land acquired :
Land depicted in the Plan No. FVP 302 prepared by the Surveyor-
General and situated in the Village of Nakulugamuwa in Minor
Division of Giruwapattu South in Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Tangalle in Hambantota District in Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Mahagedarawatte; Description: Mixed cultivation,
temporary building and well; Original claimant : Group of persons
including Kananke Liyanage Don Dulal Premajith and Dulal Richard
Gunawardana; Extent: 0.2530 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Tangalle on 25th day of February, 2010 at
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 18th day of February, 2010 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation  and
the details of the computation of such amount.
Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Rathnayake,
Divisional Secretary,
Tangalle.
Divisional Secretariat,
Tangalle.
11th January, 2010.
