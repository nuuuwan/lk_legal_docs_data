THE   ORDER

Construction, Fisheries and Tourism of the Western Province hereby declare that the undermentioned officer has been
appointed as the authorized  officer  in the implementation of the Rent  Act in the Western Province  with effect from
10.07.2009 for the purposes of the Rent Act, No. 07 of 1972. The areas in which the powers and   functions vested in him
to be exercised under the act have been specifically indicated against the name of the officer.
