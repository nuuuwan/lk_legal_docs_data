PART IV (A)  - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 03.06.2010
2A
Be it enacted as follows by the Western Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka :
1. The subsection (3) 10 of the Waste Management Statute No. 01 of 2007 is hereby amended by including the
following subsection as (4) 10 subsequent to the said subsection :-
10  (4) The Local Government Authorities can utilize 50% of the amount credited to this Fund as spot fines,
service charges by those Local Government Authorities for a decided Waste Management Project
within its  administrative limits and 40% out of the rest can be utilized for an indentified common project
that is being implemented within the respective Waste Management Zone.
2. The subsection 1(15) of the Waste Management Statute, No. 01 of 2007 is hereby amended as follows :-
15 (1) Dumping Waste on a public or private road, reservoir, canal, water flow, tributary, river, lake, lagoon, sea
or any public/private place is an offence and when a person is made a conviction in the presence of a
Magistrate  Court with regard to such offence, as per the  nature of the source of waste generation
mentioned in the 2nd column  of the 1st Schedule, in accordance any such person is subjected to a fine
not less than the amounts mentioned  in the 3rd Column and not more than the amounts mentioned in
the 4th Column or an imprisonment for a term not  less than three months and not more than one year or
both such penalty.
