  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Western  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary (Acting) of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. S. Mathugamage Prema
Sugath  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. S. Mathugamage Prema Sugath, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Kalutara in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Office,
Rajagiriya,
03rd May, 2010.
