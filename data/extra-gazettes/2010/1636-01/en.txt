FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  AKMEEMANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Akmeemana Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member  of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognised political party to which
Weerasinghe Arachchige Krishantha Pushpa Kumara,   the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of
the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Akmeemana
Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.   Eriwara Hewage
Lakshman, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Akmeemana     Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Eriwara Hewage Lakshman elected as a member  of   the Akmeemana  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Akmeemana    Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
