Proclamations &c., by the President
 A   PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE  PRESIDENT
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA
Proclamation
WHEREAS a Proclamation (hereinafter referred to as “the emergency Proclamation”) declaring that the provisions of
Part II of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), came into operation on the Second of March, 2010, was made
under  section 2 of the aforesaid Ordinance :
And Whereas paragraph (4) of Article 155 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka,
requires the occasion of the making of the emergency Proclamation to be communicated to Parliament :
And Whereas Parliament stands dissolved at the date of making of such Proclamation and consequently, as
required by sub-paragraph (i) of paragraph (4) of Article 155 of the Constitution, a Proclamation has to be issued for the
meeting of  Parliament within ten days of the making of the emergency Proclamation :
Know ye, Therefore,  that by virtue of the powers vested in me by sub-paragraph (i) of paragraph (4) of Article 155
of the Consitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, P
RESIDENT, do by this Proclamation summon Parliament to meet on the Nineth
day of March, 2010 at 9.30 a.m. in order that the fact of the making the emergency Proclamation be communicated to Parliament
and seeking the approval of Parliament therefor.
Given at Colombo  this Second day of  March,  Two Thousand  and Ten.
By His Excellency's Command,
LALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
