EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   DAMBULLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Dambulla  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased  to be a
member  of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognized political party to which
A. V . Rupasinghe  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to   nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that Dambulla    Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.   Moremada Mudiyanselage
Saman Priyantha Bandara,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, G. Dilip Nishantha de Silva,  Returning  Officer of  Dambulla   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.   Moremada Mudiyanselage
Saman Priyantha Bandara,   elected as  a member  of   the  Dambulla    Pradeshiya   Sabha.
G . D. N. 
DE SIL V A,
Returning Officer,
Dambulla  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
16th January, 2010.
