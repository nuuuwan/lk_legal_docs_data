1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   AKKARAIPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Akkaraipattu  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of   death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the National congress, the recognized political party to which
Mohamed Aboobackar Achchimohamed,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Akkaraipattu   Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Meera Shahib Ripkan,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning Officer of  Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Meera Shahib Ripkan
elected as a member of   the Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha .
M. M. S. K. BANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
07th June,  2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  IMADUW A  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in  Imaduwa  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Jayawickrama Kankanamge Darmarathna Jayawickrama,   the Chairman  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(1)
of the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that Imaduwa    Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs to  such party   who
shall be declared  elected as  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Ahangama Vithanage Sarath Kumara,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Imaduwa   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Ahangama Vithanage Sarath Kumara  elected as Chairman  of   the Imaduwa  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. DE  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Imaduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
09th June, 2010.

Kithulampitiya  Koralege,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy  ;

terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Rathnasiri  Kithulampitiya  Koralege  elected as a member  of   the Imaduwa  Pradeshiya   Sabha.

NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kurunegala  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Ananda Lokubandara Rathnayake, Returning  Officer  of  the  Kurunegala  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  do hereby declare   under Section 10 A (1) ( a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  Dumidhu
Gardiye Punchihewa, member   of  the    Kurunegala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha
as he has ceased   to be a member of the  United  National Party.
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
