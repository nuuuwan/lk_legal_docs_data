2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   WELIGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Weligama  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognised political party to which
Hewa Wellalage Gayan Sanjeewa,  the member  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member   of  that  Weligama   Pradeshiya  Sabha
who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Jayasekara Withanage
Tudar,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,  Returning  Officer  of   Weligama   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Jayasekara Withanage Tudar   elected as  a member  of   the Weligama Pradeshiya   Sabha.
C. A. S
UNETH  LOCHANA,
Returning Officer,
Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
12th February, 2010.
