APPOINTMENTS  OF  THE  UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, the Governor of
the  Uva Province :
01. Mr.  G. K. S. L. RAJADASA,  Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Secretary to the Ministry of
Youth Affairs, Sports, Social Welfare, Estate Infrastructure Development, Power and Energy, Textile and
Small Industries of Uva Province, with effect   from the date 28.01.2010 to  13.02.2010, in addition to his permanent
post,
02. District Sports Officer,  Mr.  K. H. M. J. C. G UNATHILAKA, to perform the duties of the Director of Sports
of Uva Province, with effect from the date 28.01.2010 to 13.02.2010, in addition to his permanent post,
03. Mr.   R. M. T. B. H ATHIYALDENIYA,  Class II of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Commissioner
of Co-operative Development and Registrar of Socties of Uva Province, with effect from the date 28.01.2010
to 13.02.2010. in addition to his permanent post,
04. Mrs.  N. C. H
ENNAYAKE,  Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Commissioner of
Probation and Childcare of Uva Province, from the date 10.02.2010, in addition to her permanent post,
05. Mr.  N. A. S AMARAJEEWA, Class I of the Provincial Revenue Service, as Acting Deputy Commissoiner of
Revenue of Uva Province, from the date 10.02.2010.
06. Miss.  A. M. A. K UMARI ,  Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Municipal
Commissioner of Badulla Municipal Council, from the date 22.02.2010 to 05.03.2010,   in addition to her
permanent post,
07. Mr.  K. D. J AY AWEERA, Retired Officer of the  Sri Lanka Administrative Service, Reappointed as Commissoiner
of Land of  Uva Province, on contract basis from the date 18.03.2010 till appointing a Permanent Officer or up to
31.12.2010.
