APPOINTMENTS  OF  THE  UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, the Governor of
the  Uva Province :
01. Mrs.  A. E. N. DEEPANI, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the Secretary to
the Public Service Commission of the Uva Province,   from the date 24.08.2009 to  28.08.2009, in addition to her
permanent post,
02. Mrs.  G . W. C. S. PRIYABHASHINI, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as acting Municipal Commissioner
of Badulla Municipal Council on full time basis,  from the date 07.10.2009, in addition to her permanent post,
03. Dr.   P. D. K. ADHIKARI,  to perform the duties of the Provincial Deputy Director of Health Service (Monaragala)
of the Uva Provincial Council,  from the date 28.11.2009 to 07.12.2009, in addition to his permanent post,
04. Medical Officer  Dr.  R. M. D. R A THNAYAKE, to perform the duties of the Deputy Director of Health Service
(Badulla) of Uva Province, from the date 28.11.2009 to 07.12.2009, in addition to his permanent post,
05. Assistant Director (Planning)  Mr.  A HJITH DISSANAY AKE, to perform the duties of the Deputy Chief Secretary
(Palanning) of Uva Province, from the date 28.11.2009 to 07.12.2009, in addition to his permanent post,
06. Deputy Director of Provincial Health Services Dr. A. L. M. N AZEER, to perform the duties of the Director of
Provincial Health Services of the Uva Provincial Council with effect from the date 28.11.2009 to 07.12.2009 in
addition to his permanent post,
07. Mrs. N. C. HENNAYAKA, Class III of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties   of the Secretary
to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare and Women’s Affairs with
effect from the date 28.11.2009, to 07.12.2009, in addition to her permanent post,
