THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
THE  ICE,  AERATED  WATERS,  FRUIT  JUICE  AND  JAM  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IN accordance with the provisions of Sections 28 of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter  136) the Wages Board
for the Ice, Aerated Waters, Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing Trade, hereby notifies that the Board by virtue of
the powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance, proposes to vary in the manner set out in the
Schedule hereto,  such decisions of the Board as are specified  in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Board until 12.00 noon on
05th July, 2010.
Every such objection must be made in writing and should contain a statement of the grounds upon which such
objection is taken.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Chairman,
Wages Board for the Ice, Aerated Waters,
Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing Trade.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05,
12th May, 2010.

The decisions of the Wages Board for the Ice, Aerated Waters, Fruit Juice and Jam Manufacturing Trade, and
set    out in, the schedule  to the Notification published in Gazette  No. 13535 of 27th February, 1963 as   varied from time to
time, and last varied by the notification in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1556/4 of 30th June, 2008,   shall be further varied in Part
II of that Schedule by the substitution for the existing part of the following new part.
