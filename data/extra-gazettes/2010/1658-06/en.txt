EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KALUTARA  URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kalutara Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  not attending more than  three
consecutive meetings without permission   ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Kahatapitiyage Nalin Mahesh Perera,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that  Kalutara  Urban  Council   who
belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mrs.    Jayanthi Weerasinghe,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning  Officer of Kalutara Urban Council,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mrs.  Jayanthi Weerasinghe,  elected as  a member  of   the  Kalutara Urban Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
09th June, 2010.
