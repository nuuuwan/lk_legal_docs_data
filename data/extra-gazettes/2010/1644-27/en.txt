WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Minute of the Western Province Public Health Management Assistants’ Service - Amendment No. 01
APPROV AL is granted as follows to amend the Paragraph 8.2 of the Minute of the Western Province Public Health
Management Assistants’ Service, published in the Extraordinary Gazette  Paper No. 1440/24 of 10.04.2006 of the
Democratic Socialist Repubic of Sri Lanka.
8.2 Further,  in order to make the Health Management Assistants’ Service of the Western Provincial Public
Service a multipurpose service, arrangements will be made to absorb the clerks presently serving in the
Provincial Department of the Health Services and Provincial Department of Ayurveda into the Health
Management Assistants’   Service of the Western Provincial Public Service and provisions would be made
to absorb the Typists, Book Keepers,   Shroffs and the Store Keepers who were engaged in the permanent
service of the above mentioned institutions as at    the date of approval of the Service Minute by the Hon.
Governor into the Health Management Assistants’  Service of  the Western Provincial Public Service provided
that they opt to be admitted to the said Service.
