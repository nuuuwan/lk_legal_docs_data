CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATE  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Subasinghe Arachchige Charls Sarath Wijewardena Jayathilake,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect
from 01st February  2010,  all  duties of Customs as well as  other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the
Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1638/03 of
25th January, 2010   is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 08th February,  2010.
S. A. C. S. W. J
AY ATHILAKE,
 Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 01,
28th January,  2010.
