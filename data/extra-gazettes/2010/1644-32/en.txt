STATEMENT OF MATTERS IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the Ceylon Tobacco Company, PLC, had accorded the following entitlements fully to Mr. P . Sunil Silva at the time of
terminating his services of the Company as was agreed to grant them by treating him as an employee who resigned from its service and if not
to what relief he is entitled from the Company.
(1) V oluntary Separation Allowance,
(2) Seniority award for the service over twenty five (25) years,
(3) Profit share allowance for the year 2008,
(4) Salary payable as arrears for five months i.e.– February, March, April, May and June, 2009,
(5) Benefits under ‘Scholarship Scheme for Children’,
(6) Medical benefits for two (02) years.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA ,
Commissioner of Labour.

Colombo, this 24th day of February, 2010.
