EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ERA VURPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of   being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which
Abdul Wahid Iraima Lebbe,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that  Eravurpattu    Pradeshiya   Sabha  who   belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as member  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr. Mohamed Ibrahim
Siddeek, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Eravurpattu   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Mohamed Ibrahim Siddeek,  elected as a member of the Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
05th May, 2010.
