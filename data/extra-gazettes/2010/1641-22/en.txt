No. 1641/22 – TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 16, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Hik/EA/39.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for Colombo-Galle-Hambantota Road (1/104)
(65/1) bridge.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Seven (7) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0098 Hectare,
depicted in P .P. G. 3242 prepared  by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the village of Modara Patuwatha in Grama Niladhari Division of
Dodanduwa No. 51, Divisional Secretary's Division of  Hikkaduwa
in Galle District and bounded as follows.
SCHEDULE
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Iskagarawatta; Extent: 0.0008 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Iskagarawatta; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Iskagarawatta; Extent: 0.0018 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 4
Name of land: Iskagarawatta; Extent: 0.0035 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 5
Name of land: Abandend to Colombo-Galle Road; Extent: 0.0023
Hectare.
06. Lot No. 6
Name of land: Abandend to Colombo-Galle Road; Extent: 0.0008
Hectare.
07. Lot No. 7
Name of land: Abandend to Colombo-Galle Road; Extent: 0.0003
Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Hikkaduwa Divisional Secretary's, Office on the 19th day of
April, 2010 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of April, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be produced
before me at the inquiry.
U. G. Vidura Kariyawasam,
