NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kalmunai Municipal Council
I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Kalmunai Municipal Council,  do hereby declare   under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  Athambawa Zakir  Hussian ,   of the  Kalmunai
Municipal Council  has vacated his office of member of  that  Council  as he has ceased  to be a member  of the  Sri Lanka
Muslim Congress.
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
