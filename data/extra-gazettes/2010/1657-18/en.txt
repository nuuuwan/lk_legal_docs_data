No. 1657 / 18 – WEDNESDAY, JUNE  09, 2010
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose :
Description of the land to be acquired :
Five (05) allotments of land in total extent of about 1.3181Hectare,
shown as Lot Nos.01 to5, depicted in the Preliminary Plan No. Gam
3720, of deted 30.09.2009 prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Gongithota and Pinnameda West  in Grama
Niladhari’s Division of Aluthkuru Korale Minor Division in Mahara
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Gampaha District in Western
Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Uluporane Kumbura; Discription: Marshland
(abandoned paddy field); Claimants: State;  Original claimant: S. D.
Elisabath, A. S. M. Gunasekara, No. 14, Shantha Jude Road,
Pinnameda, Enderamulla, Wattala;  Extent: 0.1329 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Uluporane Kumbura; Discription: Marshland
(abandoned paddy field); Claimants: State;  Original claimants: D.
Pedrick, No. 687, Thuduwegedara, Ragama;  Extent: 0.3808 Hectare.
Lot No. 03
Name of land: Uluporane Kumbura; Discription: Marshland
(abandoned paddy field); Claimant: State; Original claimant: H.
Jayasena Saram, H. Wasantha Saram, H. Sarath Chandra Saram,  No.
610/2, Thuduwegedara, Ragama;  Extent: 0.2157 Hectare.
Lot No. 04
Name of land: Uluporane Kumbura; Discription: Marshland
(abandoned paddy field); Claimant: State;  Original claimant: Nimal
Ralahamy ( Address not known);  Extent: 0.3174 Hectare.
Lot No. 05
Name of land: Uluporane Kumbura; Discription: Marshland
(abandoned paddy field); Claimant: State;  Original claimant: S. M.
G. Perera, No. 685B, Thuduwegedara, Ragama;  Extent: 0.2713
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretariat,  Mahara on the  07th day of  July,
2010, at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the   30th day of  June, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Deeds and extracts in support of claims should be produced at the
inquiry.
Thamara D. Perera,
Divisional Secretary,
Mahara.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Mahara.
10th May, 2010.
