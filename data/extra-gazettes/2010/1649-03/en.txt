AMENDMENT TO THE SURVEY COUNCIL (REGISTRATION) REGULATION OF  2005
IT is hereby informed to the General Public of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that the paragraph 2(2) of the
Land Survey Council (Registration) Regulation of 2005 approved  by me under section 65 of the Survey Act,  No. 17 of 2002
to be read with section 39 of the Act, in terms of the  Gazette, No. 1420/20 of 23rd November, 2005 is amended with the
inclusion of the under mentioned subparagraphs.
JOHNSTON XHA VIER FERNANDO,
Minister of Land and Land Development, Settlement and Ranaviru Welfare.
Ministry of Land and Land Development, Settlement and Ranaviru Welfare,
Colombo,
05th March, 2010.
