STA TEMENT OF MATTERS IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are –
(1) Whether Mr. R. Godellawatta has been placed on the due salary point at the time of reinstating him in service by the Ceylon
Petroleum Corporation with effect from 18th July, 2008 in terms of the decision of the Supreme Court Case No. 35/2007, dated
23rd June, 2008 and if not, to what relief he is entitled.
(2) Whether Mr. R. Godellawatta is entitled to get promotions in the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation considering him as an employee
stagnated in the same Grade for  a period of over ten (10) years in terms of the Personnel Functions Circular, No. 1686 of 06th
November, 2007 on his reinstatement in service with effect from 18th July, 2008 and if not, what relief he is entitled and
(3) Whether Mr. R. Godellawatta is entitled to receive ‘Twenty Years Service Award’ treating him as an employee who had completed
uninterrupted period of twenty years service in the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation since 03rd January, 1986.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA ,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 12th day of May, 2010.
