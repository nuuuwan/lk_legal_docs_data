NOTIFICATION
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Article 46(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, I hereby delegate with effect from 26th April, 2010 to Hon. Nirmala Kothalawala, Deputy Minister of
Highways, the powers and duties conferred or imposed on me as Minister-in-charge of the subject, pertaining
to the acquisition of land for the development work of state agencies and various projects in operation under the
purview of the Ministry of Highways.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
