No. 1654/22 – THURSDAY, MAY 20, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: 4/4/1-1/754/9.
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.:LD/05/2008/FH/338.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The Lot No. 01 in Surveyor General’s Plan No. Ka 4239 is situated
at Grama Niladhari’s  Division of 754 Maradana in Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Beruwala in Kalutara District, Western
Province, and containing in extent 0.0307 Hectare and bounded as
follows.
Lot No. 01
Name of  land: Godaparagahawatta alias Atambagahawatta;
Description: Land Acquisition to Government for Fish market at
Maradana; Claimants: (1) A. S. M. Mansoor, Weththima Rajapura,
Maradana,  Beruwala  (2) S. A. M. Kaleel, No. 155/9C, Abubukar-
Hajjiyar Mawatha, Maradana, Beruwala; Extent: 0.0307 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at Divisional Secretariat of Beruwala, on the 26th day of August,
2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of August, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Wimal Gunatunga,
Divisional Secretary,
Beruwala.
Divisional Secretariat,
Beruwala.
13th May, 2010.
