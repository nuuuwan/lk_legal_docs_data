THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under section 7
Reference No: LEI-CDP/MC/M/08A.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the state has acquired the following land which
is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land acquired:
The allotment of land in extent 0.0002 Hectare depicted as Lot
No. 13 in the Preliminary Plan No. Co.9002 dated 06.03.2009
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the village of
Laxapathiya in the Divisional Secretary Division of Moratuwa, in
the District of Colombo and morefully described as follows;
1. Lot No. 13
Name of land: Assmt. No. 119, Laxapathiya Road; Description:
Hemingalana Ela; Original Claimant: M. Ramanee Fernando,
No. 119, Laxapathiya Road, Laxapathiya, Moratuwa; Extent:
0.0002 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
on 04th May, 2010 at Lunawa Environment Improvement and Com-
munity Development Project Office at No. 51, Pirivena Road,
Ratmalana on the time intimated to them in due course and are
hereby requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
20th April, 2010 the nature of their interests in the lands, particulars
of their claims for compensation of the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation of
such amount.
S. Weerabahu,
Land Acquiring Officer.
Land Acquisition Office,
Lunawa Environment Improvement and
Community Development Project,
51, Pirivena Road,
Ratmalana,
