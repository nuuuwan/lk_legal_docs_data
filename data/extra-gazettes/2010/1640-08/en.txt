EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  DEPUTY  MAYOR OF  THE  DEHIWALA - MOUNT LA VINIA
MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Deputy Mayor  in  Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason
of   being ceased to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Achchi Maddumage Don Hasala Dhanasiri Amaratunga,   the Deputy Mayor  belonged, was required by me under
Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of  that  Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal
Council   who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Deputy Mayor  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to   nominate
Mr.  Ruwan Kithsiri Liyanagunawardana  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  A. Senanayake,  Returning Officer of  Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal Council ,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ruwan Kithsiri
Liyanagunawardana,  elected as Deputy Mayor  of   the Dehiwala - Mount Lavini a  Municipal Council.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
05th  February,  2010.
