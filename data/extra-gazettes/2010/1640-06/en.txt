THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: ID/20/LLA.7585.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land shown as Lot No. 114 in Supplement No. 1,
F.V .P. 515, situated in the village of Menerikumbura in Alakolaella
Grama Niladari’s Divisions of Uduwaggam Pattu Kadawatha Korale
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Imbulpe in Ratnapura District
and described as follows:
Name of land: Nadattewatta alias Vimalehena; Claimant: W. J. A.
iyasiri, Udagama, Pinnawala; Extent: 0.1873 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land, are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretary’s Office, Imbulpe on the 03rd day of May,
2010, at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27th day of  April, 2010,  the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation  and the details of the computation of such amount.
Deeds to any other documents pertaining to this land should be
produced at the inquiry.
M. M. Gunasingha,
Divisional Secretary,
Imbulpe.
01st February, 2010,
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Imbulpe.
