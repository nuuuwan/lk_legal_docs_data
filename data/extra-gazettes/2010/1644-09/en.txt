THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW,  No.  41  OF  1978
Order under Section  03
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 03 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978, I, Dinesh
Chandra Rupasinghe Gunawardena, Minister of Urban Development and Sacred Area Development of the opinion that the
area specified in Column I of the Schedule I appended hereto, having specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of
Schedule I are suitable for development do by this order declare such areas to be an Urban Development Areas.
