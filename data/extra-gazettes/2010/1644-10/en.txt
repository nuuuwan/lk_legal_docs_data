No. 1644/10 – TUESDAY, MARCH 09, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Maha/1/286/Ath.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-Section (1) of  Section 7 of the land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of lands in total extent of 0.0265 Hectare,
shown in the Preliminary Plan No. CO 9091 and 11.12.2009 Dated
prepared by the Survey Superintendent of Colombo, situated at
Makumbura village in the Sub Division of Hewagamkorale in
Maharagama Divisional Secretary's Division in Colombo District of
Western Province:
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Batadombagahadeniya Kumbura; Description:
Kumbura; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Thissa Premasiri,
Makumbura, Hiripitiya, Pannipitiya; Extent: 0.0184 Hectare.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING, SRI LANKA
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Batadombagahadeniya; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M. A. Gnanawathi and other
owners, No. 381/2, High-level Road, Makumbura, Pannipitiya;
Extent: 0.0081 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Maharagama Divisional Secretary's Office on the 22nd day of
March, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 31st day of March, 2010 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
Chandraratna Pallegama,
Divisional Secretary,
Maharagama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Maharagama,
03rd March, 2010.
