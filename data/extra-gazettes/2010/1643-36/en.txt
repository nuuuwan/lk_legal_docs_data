EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KORALEPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Thamodaram Udayajeewadas,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya   Sabha
who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.  Thamodaram
Udayajeewadas  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Thamodaram Udayajeewadas,  elected as a member of the Koralepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
19th February, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ERA VURPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  not attending
meetings of the Council for a period of  three months without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Pathmanadan Vijetharan,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Eravurpattu    Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr. Kandiah Kanthan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  THuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Eravurpattu   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Kandiah Kanthan,  elected as a member of the Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Eravurpattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
25th February, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   KORALEPATTU   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Tamil Makkal Viduthalai Pulikal, the recognized political party to which
Thamodaram Udayajeewadas,   the  Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  decide upon the member of that  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya   Sabha    who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as Chairman within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Thamodaram  Udayajeewadas   to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Thamodaram Udayajeewadas,  elected as Chairman  of the Koralepattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
