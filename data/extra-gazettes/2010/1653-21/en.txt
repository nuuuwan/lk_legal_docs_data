EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KELANIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Janaka Susantha Perera Kolonnage,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid
Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kelaniya Pradeshiya   Sabha
who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.  Nanayakkarawasam
Kudahettige Nihal Bernard Chandrasekera,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Mohamed Mahdoom Mohamed,   Returning Officer of  Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.  Nanayakkarawasam
Kudahettige Nihal Bernard Chandrasekera,  elected as a member of the Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. M. M
OHAMED,
Returning Officer,
Kelaniya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
10thMay, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  KORALEPATTU   WEST  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Koralepattu West  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mohamed Kani Mohamed Mohideen,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid
Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Koralepattu West
Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr. Noor Mohamed
Mohamed Ismail, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Koralepattu West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Noor Mohamed Mohamed Ismail,  elected as a member of the Koralepattu West Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. KRISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu West Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
30th April, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   KORALEPATTU   WEST
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in  Koralepattu  West Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to
be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mohamed Kani Mohamed Mohideen,   the  Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid
Ordinance, to  decide upon the member of that  Koralepattu  West Pradeshiya   Sabha    who  belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Chairman within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Casim Bawa Sahul Hameed  to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,   Returning Officer of  Koralepattu West  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Casim Bawa Sahul Hameed,  elected as Chairman  of the Koralepattu West Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
