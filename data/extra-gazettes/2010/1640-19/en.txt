THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: NCP/MT/6 - III/577/333.
Lands & Land  Development Ministry Reference
No.: LD/05/2006/WS/275.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the follow-
ing land which required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called  "Mihintalekele" in total extent of
about 0.0070 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 340 in the preliminary
Plan  No. F. V . P. 564 sheet 52 supplement 50 dated on 20.08.2009
prepared by the surveyor General, situated in the village of Mihintale,
Kanadara Korale  Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Divi-
sion of Mihintale in Anuradhapura District, North - Central Prov-
ince and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 340
Name of land: Mihintalekele; Claimant: Chief incumbent, Mihintale
Raja Maha Viharaya, Mihintale,  The Public Trustee, Department of
Public Trustee, Colombo 07; Extent: 0.0070 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North: Lot No. 1
A ; East: Lot No. 1C  & Lot No. 1A ; South: Lot No.
1A ; West: Lot No. 1A .
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretary's Office, at Mihintale on the day 25th of
March, 2010 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby  requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18th day of March, 2010,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation of the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
M. W. M. M. Madahapola,
Divisional Secretary,
Mihintale.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
