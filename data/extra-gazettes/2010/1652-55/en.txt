  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the  Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern  Province Provincial
Council  by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr.   Chandana  Priyantha
Bamunawita Gamage  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Chandana  Priyantha Bamunawita Gamage, elected as a member of the
above  Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Matara  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
05th May, 2010.

  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Southern  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary  of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1)   of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern  Province Provincial
Council by reason of  being elected as a Member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Jayantha Jayaweera
Bandula Hewa  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Jayantha Jayaweera Bandula Hewa, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council  from the Administrative District of  Galle  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
