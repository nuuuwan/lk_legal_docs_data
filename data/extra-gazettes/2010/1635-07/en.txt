No. 1635 /7 – MONDAY, JANUARY 04, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Ministry No.: LD/05/2007/UDA/174.
Reference No.: Be/E/3/Acq/39.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act,  that the Government intends to acquire the following
land, which is required for construct public ground at Joolahena that
Kotugodella, Pansalwatta.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called Joolahena, alias Kotugodella, Pansal
watta, depicted as Lot No. 570,  in Survey plan No.  299, situated in
Kudaheella village in Kudaheella East Grama Niladhari’s Division in
Beliatta Divisional Secretary’s Division in South Giruwa Pattuwa in
Hambantota District in Southern  Province:
Lot No. 570, in Final Survey plan 299 – Joolahena alias
Kotugodella, Pansalwatta – Extent: 0.6014 Hectare
North: Lot No. 39; East:  Lot Nos. 459, 550 and 458; South: Lot
Nos. 458, 457 and 38B; West: Lot No. 39.
Original Claimants: W. D. S. G. Praneeth Indrasiri and others,
No. 90/15, Dickwella Road, Beliatta and V en. Witharandeniye
Hemaloka Thero, Sumanananda Chaithyaramaya, Kotugodella,
Dickwella Road, Beliatta.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary’s Office, Beliatta on the17th day of  March,
2010, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of  March, 2010,  the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
Dileka W. Sahabandu,
Divisional Secretary,
Beliatta.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Beliatta.
17th December, 2009.
