PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
Order No. 10.
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority  Act,  No.  09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader or distributor   shall  sell, expose or offer for sale,
display for Frozen Broiler Chicken Meat with Skin (Whole Chicken or Half of a Chicken-Half of carcass, obtained
by a longitudinal cut in a plane along the sternum and the back bone or Quarter of a Chicken-leg and breast quarters
obtained by dividing a half by a transversal cut) above the maximum retail price of Rs. 35.00 per 100 g (Rs. 350.00 per 1 kg).
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
18th February, 2010.
