APPOINTMENT  OF  UV A   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Section 154F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Caluwadewagey Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce
that I have made the following appointment.
Hon. Chief Minister Shashindra Kumara Rajapaksha — As Acting Minister of Youth Affairs, Sports, Social
Welfare, Estate Infrastructure Development, Power
and Energy, Textiles and Small Industries of the Uva
Province, from the date 16th October, 2009, till
the return to Sri Lanka of Hon. Minister A. Akalan
Sendil Thondaman.
