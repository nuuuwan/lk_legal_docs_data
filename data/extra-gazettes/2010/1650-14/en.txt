PART III – LANDS
Land Acquisition Notices
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No:Bagama/DS/2/2/44.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 One (01) allotment of land in total extent of 0.0548 Hectare, as
Lot No. 1, depicted  in P.P.Ga. 3723 and dated 16.03.2010 prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated  in Boralukada Village  in the
Baddegama Divisional   Secretary’s Division in the District of Galle
in Southern Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Boraluduwawatta alias Pilagodawatta; Description:
Open Waste Land and Temporary Cultivation;  Claimant: State;  Original
Claimant: E.G. Mahinda of Thilaka Udagama, Baddegama; Extent:
0.0548 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Baddegama  Divisional Secretariat on the 09th day of June,  2010
at 02.30 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the   02nd day of June,  2010 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
P.S. Gunawardena,
Divisional Secretary,
Baddegama.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Baddegama.
01st April, 2010.
