I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called “Dehigahayatatenne Hena” in total
extent of about 0.0080 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary
Plan  No. Maha 6057 dated on 17.12.2009 prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the village of Kurukuththala Ihala (117) in
Kandupalatha Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Udunuwara in Kandy District, Central Province and
bounded as follows:
Lot No. 01 – Dehigahayatatenne Hena – Extent: 0.0080 Hectare
North: Lot No. 1 in PP Maha 5964 and Road; East: Road;
South: Road and Dehigahayatatenne Hena claimed by M. S.
A. Kareem; West: Lot No. 1 in PP Maha 5964.
Claimant: S. M. S. Razik, No. 59, Kandy Road, Welamboda.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Udunuwara on 03rd day of March, 2010
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 24th day of February, 2010 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation  and
the details of the computation of such amount.
Pasan Rathnayake,
Divisional Secretary,
Udunuwara.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
