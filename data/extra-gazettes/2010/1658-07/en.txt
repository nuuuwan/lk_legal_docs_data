1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  BALAPITIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Balapitiya  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party, the recognised political party to which
Mohamed Mildas Mohomed Masri,  the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Balapitiya    Pradeshiya  Sabha
who belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.    Deepal Priyantha
Ihalage,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Balapitiya      Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Deepal Priyantha Ihalage,  elected as a member  of   the Balapitiya   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
