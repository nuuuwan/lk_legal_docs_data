EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ANGUNAKOLAPELLESSA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Albert Kuda Antony Hewa Gamage,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Angunakolapellessa
Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Jalath Kadupitiya
Arachchige Sirisena,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe,  Returning Officer of the
Angunakolapellessa Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr.  Jalath Kadupitiya Arachchige Sirisena, elected as  a  member  of   the  Angunakolapellessa
Pradeshiya   Sabha.
J. A. S,. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Hambantota,
02nd February, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   BALAPITIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Balapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Jagamuni Mahinda Premaraja De Zoysa,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Balapitiya
Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  G . Pavarasiri De Zoysa,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Balapitiya   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   G. Pavarasiri De
Zoysa, elected as  a  member  of   the  Balapitiya Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Balapitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
