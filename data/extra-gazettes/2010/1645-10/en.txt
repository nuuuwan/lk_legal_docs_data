No. 1645/10 – TUESDAY, MARCH 16, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: LEI-CDP/S2/CW/18(RB).
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government has acquired the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land acquired:
The two  (2) allotments of  land in extent 0.0070 Hectare, depicted
as Lot Nos. 01 and 06 in the Preliminary Plan No. Co. 9046 dated
18th November, 2009, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the Village of Laxapathiya, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Moratuwa, in the District of Colombo and morefully described as
follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Assmt. No. 62/5, Jubilee Road; Description:
Road and Masonary Drain; Original claimant: T. M. N. Fernando,
No. 62/5, Jubilee Road, Laxapathiya, Moratuwa; Extent:
0.0047 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 06
Name of land: Assmt. No. 62/4A, Jubilee Road; Description:
Road and Masonary Drain; Original claimant: M. J. P. Fernando,
No. 62/4A, Jubilee Road, Laxapathiya, Moratuwa; Extent:
0.0023 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
on the 20th day of April, 2010 at Lunawa Environment Improvement
and Community Development Project Office at No. 51, Pirivena
Road, Ratmalana on the time intimated to them in due course and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 07th day of April, 2010, the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
S. Weerabahu,
Land Acquiring Officer.
Land Acquisition Office,
Lunawa Environment Improvement and
Community Development Project,
No. 51, Pirivena Road,
Ratmalana.
