TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “Dr. Reijntjes School for the Deaf,  Trust ” namely —
1. Judge  Christopher  Gregory  Weeramantry,
2. Dr. Justice, Anthony Ranjith Bevis Amarasinghe,
3. Ambalangoda Gurunnanselage Lakshman Perera,
4. Mr. Chandrasoma Wickramapala Gunasekera,
5. Mr. Upali Indrajith Perera Wickramaratne,
6. Mr. Mapalagama Kottege Susiripala De Silva,
7. Ms.  Christina Josephina Maria De  Silva,
8. Mr. Willi Hendrik Maria Engbers, and
9. Ms. Francisca  Amelia Maria Melief
have applied to the Minister of Justice and Law Reforms for their incorporation under Section 114 of the  Trusts
Ordinance (Chapter 87).
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I,  Milinda Moragoda,
Minister of Justice and Law Reforms, do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct
and  declare that —
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being  duly appointed under
the rules of the said Corporation and the Declaration of Trust bearing No. 6921 of  February 13, 2009
attested by Hiranthi Amarasekera, Notary Public of Colombo,  and shall be a body corporate  with
perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style of the “Dr. Reijntjes School for
the Deaf,  Trust ”  ;
