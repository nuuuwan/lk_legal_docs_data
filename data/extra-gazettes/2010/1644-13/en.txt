EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE  MINUWANGODA URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Minuwangoda Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United National Party , the recognized political party to  which    Kotagedara
Liyanage Jastine Perera  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section  65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of  that  Minuwangoda Urban Council, who belongs
to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as a  member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated  Mr.   Mohomed Sawahir
Naushad Mohideen, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Mohamed Mahdoom Mohamed,  Returning  Officer  of   Minuwangoda Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Mohomed Sawahir
Naushad Mohideen,  elected as  a member  of   the Minuwangoda Urban Council.
M. M. M
OHAMED,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
01st March,  2010.
