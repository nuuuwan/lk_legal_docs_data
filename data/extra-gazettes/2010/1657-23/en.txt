EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF THE KALMUNAI  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a Member in Kalmunai  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as
a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which
Mr.  H. M. M. Harees,   the member  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2) amended by the Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2007, to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance appoint as a member of that
Kalmunai  Municipal Council   within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr.   Meera Sahib Mohamed
Nizar,  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning Officer of  Kalmunai  Municipal Council ,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Meera Sahib Mohamed
Nizar,  elected as a member of   the Kalmunai  Municipal Council .
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
31st May,  2010.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF MAYOR OF THE KALMUNAI  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the Office of  Mayor  in Kalmunai  Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being elected as
a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which
Mr. H. M. M. Harees,   the Mayor  belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance to decide
upon the member of that Kalmunai  Municipal Council  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as the Mayor
within a period of thirty (30) days ;.
And  whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Seyed Zain Mohamed Mashoor Moulana,  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning Officer of  Kalmunai  Municipal Council ,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Seyed Zain Mohamed
Mashoor Moulana,  elected as  the Mayor of   the Kalmunai  Municipal Council .
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
31st May,  2010.
