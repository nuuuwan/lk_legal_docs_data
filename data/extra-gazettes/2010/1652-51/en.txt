SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice  under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance Chapter 252 read with  Section 3 of the Powers of
Supervision of the Administration  of  Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 and Section 2 of the
Provincial  Councils (Ancillary Provisions)  Act, No. 12 of 1989.
I, Shan Wijayalal De Silva, the Minister-in-charge of the Subjects of  Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Transport,
Water Supply and  Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and Local Government
of the Southern Province Provincial Council, acting under the powers vested in me under Section 277 of the
Municipal Councils Ordinance Chapter 252  read with Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of
Local Government Authorities Statute No. 4 of 1990 of the Southern Province Provincial Council and Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, have appointed Edirimuni Samith De Silva retired Judicial
Officer, to  inquire into and report with regard to matters referred to under Section 277 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
and Section 2(1) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990
of the Southern Provincial Council, on the part of Mr. Subhawickrema Mallika Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha while
functioning as mayor of Matara Municipal  Council of the Matara District.
02. Further, under the powers vested in me under Section 277(1B)(i) of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
Chapter 252  read with Section 3 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Authorities
Statute No. 04  of 1990 of the Southern Provincial Council and Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions)
Act, No. 12  of 1989, I, Shan Wijayalal de Silva, Minister-in-charge of the subjects of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Transport, Water Supply and Drainage, Electricity, Engineering Services, Health and Indigenous Medicine and
Local Government of the Southern Provincial Council Suspend with immediate effect Mr. Subhawickrema Mallika
Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha, the mayor of Matara Municipal Council of the Matara District, from functioning  as
mayor of the said Municipal Council.
03.  Further, since Mr. Subhawickrema Mallika Vidanachchige Upul Nishantha who held office as mayor of the
Matara Municipal Council has been suspended from functioning in the said office with immediate effect, under
Section 277 (1B)(i) of the Municipal Councils  Ordinance Chapter 252 read with Section 3 of the Powers of Supervision
of the Administration of Local Government Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1990 of the Southern Provincial Council and
Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Ancillary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I order under Section 277 (1B)(i)
