1A
L.D.-B. 13/78
THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of sub-paragraph ( a) of paragraph (1) of Article 44 of the Constitution, I,
Mahinda Rajapaksa having determined the number of Ministers of the Cabinet and Ministers, do by this Notification, assign
to such Ministers the Subject and Functions as are set out below.
The Ministers who are in charge of the Ministries specified in the schedule below shall exercise, perform and
discharge the powers, duties and functions as are specified in relation to each such Ministry and each such Ministry shall
under its purview or be responsible for, the following :-
1. The Powers, Duties and Functions as are specified in Column I in relation to each Ministry ;
2. The Departments, Public Corporations, Statutory Institutions specified in Column II in relation to each such
Ministry ;
3. The implementation of the Laws for the time being in force, as are specified in Column III of each such
Ministry.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
30th April, 2010.
