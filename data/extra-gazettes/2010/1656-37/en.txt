PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
SPORTS LAW No. 25 OF 1973
Order under Sections 32 and 33
I, Ratnayake Mudiyanselage Chandrasiri Bandara Ratnayake, Minister of Sports, do hereby declare that in pursuant to
powers vested in me under Sections 32 and 33  of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973, I, hereby amend with effect from
01.06.2010 the appointments effected through Gazette Extraordinary No. 1595/8 of 30th March, 2009 of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, for the  Sri Lanka Cricket, formerly known as the Board of Control for Cricket, being the
National Association for  Cricket in Sri Lanka  as an interim measure,  for the smooth functioning of the activities of the
Association, until an  election is held for the selection of office bearers or until further notice.
Mr.  D. S. de Silva - Chairman
Mr.  Nishantha Ranatunga - Secretary
Mr.  Sujeeva Rajapaksha - Treasurer
Mr.  Kalinga N. Indatissa - Member
Mr.  Prabhath Fonseka - Member
Mr.   Asanga Chandana Seneviratna - Member
R
ATNAYAKE  MUDIYANSELAGE  CHANDRASIRI  BANDARA RATNAYAKE,
Minister of Sports .

SPORTS LAW No. 25 OF 1973
Order under Sections 32 and 33
I, Ratnayake Mudiyanselage Chandrasiri Bandara Ratnayake, Minister of Sports, do hereby declare that in pursuant
to   powers vested in me under Sections 32(c) and 33  of the Sports Law No. 25 of 1973, I, cancel with effect from 21.05.2010,
the list of names of the persons appointed by Gazette Notifications  No. 1615/35 dated 21.08.2009, for the smooth
functioning of the activities of the Sri Lanka Rugby Football Federation which is the National Association for Rugby
Football in Sri Lanka.
