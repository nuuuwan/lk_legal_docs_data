No. 1644/5 – TUESDAY,  MARCH 09, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Two (02) allotments of land shown as Lots 'A' and 'B' in total
extent of about 20.3042 Hectares, depicted in Advance Tracing
No. Mada/MPN/2007/372, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of Manmunai North, Kokuvil in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Manmunai North in Batticaloa District and
bounded as follows:
Lot 'A' – Ringwood Estate – Extent: 1.6990 Hectares
North: Batticaloa Lagoon and path from Cemetery to Trincomalee
Road; East: Path to Trincomalee Road; South: Cemetery; West:
Batticaloa Lagoon.
Lot 'B' – Ringwood Estate – Extent: 18.6052 Hectares
North: Cemetery, path from Cemetery to Trincomalee Road,
Ringwood Estate and path to Manser Road; East: Ringwood Estate,
path to Manser  Road and Ringwood Estate; South: Ringwood Estate,
Manser Road and Vepadithuraikadu; West: Vepadithuraikadu,
Batticaloa Lagoon, Cemetery and path from Cemetery to Trincomalee
Road.
Jeewan Kumaranatunga,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: LD/05/2005/E/515.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: DS/MN/LB/C/319.
"Govijana Mandiraya",
Battaramulla.
