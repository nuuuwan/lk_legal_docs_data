THE  AW ARD
CORRECTION OF AWARD IN CASE NO. A/3004 UNDER
REGULATION  29 OF THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES
REGULATIONS 1958
I Notice a few clerical erros due to an over-sight
appearing in the Award published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1397/26 dated 15th June, 2005, I do hereby
correct the paragraph commencing from the words “I hold . .
. . . . . . . . . .and ending on . . . . . . . .the date hereof”.
To read as follows :—
“I hold that on the documents marked by the applicant
workman namely A1, A2, A3, A4, A5 and A6 and  the  evidence
of the applicant as follows :-
1. That the 2% commission on achievement of  target
for January 2000 is Rs, 26,464/- and that the applicant
workman is entitled to it.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA
2. That the car rental was Rs. 20,000 per mensum and
that he is entitled to the car rental for the  month of
April and May 2000 amounting to Rs. 40,000.
I make award that Mr. D. Nugawela and S. Nugawela pay
the sum of Rs. 66,464 to the applicant workman Thilan
Senaratne.
The employers to deposit the money awarded hereby with
the Commissioner General of Labour within one month from
the date of publication of this award in the Gazette.
This award shall take effect from the date hereof.”
A. S
RI NAMMUNI,
Arbitrator.
Dated at Colombo,
This 26th Day of October, 2009.
