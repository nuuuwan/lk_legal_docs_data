EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   GALENBINDUNUWEWA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased
to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Hinnegedara Piyarathne,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of   that   Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya   Sabha
who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.  Wasantha Susil Bandara
Rathnayake  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy   ;
Now therefore,  I,  S. M. Saman Bandulasena,   Returning Officer of   Galenbindunuwewa   Pradeshiya Sabha,acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that   Mr.  Wasantha
Susil Bandara Rathnayake ,  elected as a member of the  Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. M.  S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
Returning Officer,
 Galenbindunuwewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
1st March, 2010.
