REGULATIONS
Food (Formaldehyde in fish) Regulations 2010
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Food (Formaldehyde in fish) Regulations 2010 and shall  come into
operation with effect from  1st July, 2010.
2. No person shall import, transport, distribute, store, sell, offer or expose for sale any fish in Sri Lanka, which
contains Formaldehyde in levels exceeding 5 mg/kg (ppm.)
3. Every person importing fish into the country shall produce at the point of entry of such fish into the country, a
certificate of conformity from the relevant National Authority of the country of origin or exporting country as the case may
be, which states that the fish imported, conform to the levels specified in these Regulations.
