PART III – LANDS
Land Acquisition Notices
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for purpose
of housing scheme of Fisheries of Urawatta, Walauwatta and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Two (02) allotments of land called Walauwatta in total extent of
about 0.0506 Hectare, shown as Lots 'A' and 'B' in Advance Tracing
No. GA/HKK/07/1310 dated 13th July, 2008, prepared by the
District Superintendent of Surveys in Galle, situated in the village of
Urawatta in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Hikkaduwa in Galle
District and bounded as follows:
Lot 'A'  – Extent: 0.0048 Hectare
North: Road, Lot No. 04 in P.P. G. 2810 and Lot B of this tracing;
East: Lot B of this tracing, Lot No. 04 in P.P. G. 2810 and Road;
South and West: Road.
Lot 'B' – Walauwatta – Extent: 0.0458 Hectare
North: Lot No. 04 in P.P. G. 2810; East: Lot No. 04 in P .P. G.
2810; South: Lot No. 04 in  P.P. G. 2810 and Lot A of this tracing;
West: Lot A of this tracing and Lot No. 04 in P.P. G. 2810.
