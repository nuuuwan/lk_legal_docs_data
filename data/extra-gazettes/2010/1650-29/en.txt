EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  ANGUNAKOLAPELLESSA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of
Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  People’s Liberation Front, the recognised political party to which
Nilantha Senarath Pathiranage,   the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Angunakolapellessa   Pradeshiya
Sabha  who belongs to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.    Gamini Wickramasinghe,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe, Returning  Officer of the
Angunakolapellessa  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
(Chapter 262) do  hereby declare  Mr.  Gamini Wickramasinghe,  elected as a member  of   the Angunakolapellessa   Pradeshiya
Sabha.
J. A. S. P. J
AYASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Angunakolapellessa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
