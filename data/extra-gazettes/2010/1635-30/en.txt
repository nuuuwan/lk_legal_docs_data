THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development,
do hereby declare that the land described below will be acquired under
provisions of the said Act as it is required for a public purpose.
The allotments of  land depicted as Lots ‘A’ and ‘B’ approximately
in total  extent of 0.0084 Hectare in the Advance Tracing
No. KA/DDN/03/89 and dated 17th April, 2003, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Dodangoda in the
Dodnagoda Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kalutara District and
bounded as follows:
1. Lot No. ‘A’ – Horagasmulla Kumbura – Extent: 0.0062
Hectare
North: Canal, Horagasmulla Kumbura claimed by L.D.A.
Premadasa; East: Horagasmulla Kumbura claimed by L.D.A.
Premadasa, Horagasmulla Deniya and Lot No. B;  South : Lot No. B
and Highway; West: Highway and Canal.
2. Lot No. ‘B’ – Horagasmulla Deniya – Extent : 0.0022 Hectare
North: Lot No. A, Horagasmulla Kumbura claimed by L.D.A.
Premadasa and Horagasmulla Deniya claimed by L.D.A. Premadasa
and Other’s; East: Horagasmulla Deniya claimed by L.D.A. Premadasa
and others; South: Highway; West: Highway and Lot No. A.
