NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  Thuraisingam Krishnananthalingam,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Koralepattu North Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare
under   Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  Selvam Baskaran, of  the  Koralepattu  North
Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased   to be a member of the   Thamil Makkal
Viduthalai Pulikal.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
Returning Officer,
Koralepattu North  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
23rd April,  2010.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   ALAWW A  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Alawwa   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
K. A. Sumathipala,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate
a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Alawwa   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   R. A. D. H. Ranasinghe,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  R. M. A. L. Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Alawwa   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms of
Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   R. A. D. H. Ranasinghe,
elected as  a  member  of   the  Alawwa Pradeshiya   Sabha.
R. M. A. L. R
ATHNAYAKE,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Alawwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kurunegala,
22nd April, 2010.

(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Alawwa   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
H. M. Dharmarathna Herath ,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Alawwa   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who  belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Alagiyawanna

such vacancy  ;

Don Samantha Upul Bandara Alagiyawanna, elected as  a  member  of   the  Alawwa Pradeshiya   Sabha.
