REGULATION  7(1)  OF  THE  EMERGENCY  REGULATIONS,  NO. 1 OF  2009
IT is hereby notified that in terms of the Proclamation made by me under section 5 of the Public Security Ordinance
(Chapter 40) and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1583/12 of January 7, 2009 and pursuant to the powers vested in me
by the Emergency (Proscription of the Liberation Tiger of Tamil Ealam) Regulation, No.1 of 2009, published in Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 1583/12 of January 7, 2009, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Notification state that the
immovable property specified in the Schedule to this Notification has been forfeited to the State.
MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
27th January, 2010.
