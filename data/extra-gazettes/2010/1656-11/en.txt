PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & c., by the Governors
1A
 SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I, W. J. M. Lokubandara, Governor of  Sabaragamuwa Province, by virtue of the powers vested in me in act of Article 154 (E),
(1), (4) and (5) of the Consitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka has made following appointments
for the 5th Provincial Council of  Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council.
01. Hon. Minister  Mahipala  Herath — Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and
Planning, Local Government, Education and Information
Technology, Media, Cultural, Power, Housing and Construction,
Trade and Food, Public Transport, Estate Welfare, Environment
Affairs and Fisheries with effect from 19th May, 2010.
02. Hon.  Minister  Srilal  Wickramasinghe — Minister of Lands, Provincial Irrigation, Agriculture, Animal
Production and Animal Health, Sports and Youth Affairs with
effect from 19th May, 2010.
03. Hon. Minister  Athula  Kumara Rahubaddha — Minister of  Provincial Roads Development, Rural Infrustructure
and Tourism with effect from 19th May, 2010.
04. Hon. Minister Bhanu Munipriya — Minister of Health, Indigenous Medicine and Women’s Affairs
with effect from 12th May, 2010.
W. J. M. L
OKUBANDARA,
Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
Governor’s Secretariat,
Sabaragamuwa Province,
26th May, 2010.
