EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   SAMMANTHURAI  PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of   being ceased to be
a member of the party  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Athamlebbe Thambikandu Mohamed Thamby,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that   Sammanthurai
Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Aswar Uthumalebbe,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa,  Returning Officer of  Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in  terms
of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Aswar Uthumalebbe
elected as a member of   the Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha .
M. M. S. K. B
ANDARA MAPA,
Returning Officer,
Sammanthurai Pradeshiya Sabha
Elections Office,
Ampara,
18th  February,  2010.
