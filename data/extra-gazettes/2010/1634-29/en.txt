Elections Office, No. 395, Old Kotte Road, Rajagiriya within four weeks from the date of publication of this notice in the
Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Every objection in the insertion or retention in the register of the

the names of   claimants is exhibited at the Elections Office, No. 395, Old Kotte Road, Rajagiriya. Such notice will be exhibited

4. Forms for the purpose of making any claim or objection may be obtained from the Elections Office, No. 395, Old
Kotte Road, Rajagiriya,  each Divisional Secretariat, Office of each Local Government Authority, each Grama Niladhari’s
Office and each Post  Office in the Administrative District of Colombo.
