NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kekirawa  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  S. M. Saman  Bandulasena,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Kekirawa Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare   under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr.  Waruna Kumara Thoradeniya, member   of  the
Kekirawa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased   to be a member of the
United  National Party.
S. M. S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
Returning Officer,
Kekirawa Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
02nd March,  2010.
