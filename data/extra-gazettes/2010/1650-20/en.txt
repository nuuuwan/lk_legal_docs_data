EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KATTANKUDI  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kattankudi  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 3  to which  Mohamed Cassim Mohamed Mansoor,
the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate a person eligible
under the Ordinance for Election as a  member of that  Kattankudi Urban Council   who belongs to such group who shall be
declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group has nominated Mr. Mohamed Musthaffa Abdul Rahuman
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam  Krishnananthalingam,    Returning Officer of  Kattankudi Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Mohamed
Musthaffa Abdul Rahuman, elected as a member of   the  Kattankudi Urban Council.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
  Returning Officer,
Kattankudi Urban Council .
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
15th April,  2010.
