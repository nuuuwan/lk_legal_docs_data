PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the Acting Secretary-General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (1) of the Parliamentary
Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the
Sixth Parliament by reason of the expulsion of  Mr.  Indika Bandaranayaka from United National Party, who was declared
elected as a member of Parliament for the Electoral District No. 15 - Kurunegala  under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act ;
And whereas the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed
by me under Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of Article 99
of the Constitution ;
And whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return
in Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr.  Disanayaka Mudiyanselage
Bandaranayaka  was declared elected as a  Member of Parliament for the said electoral district ;
Now therefore, I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested
in me by Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr.  Disanayaka Mudiyanselage
Bandaranayaka   has been  declared elected as a member of the Sixth Parliament for the Electoral District No. 15 - Kurunegala
to fill the vacancy caused by reason of  the expulsion of  Mr.  Indika Bandaranayaka from United National Party.
D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
22nd March,  2010.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE

Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the
Sixth Parliament by reason of the expulsion of  Mr.  Jonston  Xavier Fernando  from United National Party, who was declared

by me under Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of Article 99
