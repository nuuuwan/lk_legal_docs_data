EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN OF  THE  WELIVITIYA-DIVITURA
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Welivitiya-Divitura  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Vice
Chairman being appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Lokuge Gunasiri,   the  Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the   aforesaid  Ordinance, to
decide upon the member of that  Welivitiya-Divitura  Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Vice Chairman  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Samantha Kumara Malawiarachchi to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Welivitiya-Divitura   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr. Samantha Kumara Malawiarachchi  elected as Vice Chairman of   the Welivitiya-Divitura  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Welivitiya-Divitura
  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
21st January, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   RATNAPURA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Ratnapura  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
D. Janaka Kaluarachchi,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Ratnapura   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.   Ukkuwalage
Somawardena,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera,  Returning Officer of  Ratnapura    Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Ukkuwalage Somawardena, elected as  a  member  of   the  Ratnapura Pradeshiya   Sabha.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
  Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Ratnapura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Ratnapura,
28th January, 2010.
