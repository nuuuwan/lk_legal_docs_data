  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Sabaragamuwa  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1)
of the  Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Sabaragamuwa
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Panawalage Nimal
Padmasiri Wijenayake to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Panawalage Nimal  Padmasiri Wijenayake, elected as a member of the
above  Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Ratnapura  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariats,
Rajagiriya,
27th April, 2010.
