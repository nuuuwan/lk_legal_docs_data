EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE   HAPUTALE URBAN COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Haputale Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of   Vice Chairman  has been
appointed as Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United  People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Rashidkhan Mohomed Ashver Khan,   the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that  Haputale Urban Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  Vice  Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   communicated his decision to
nominate   Mr.   Lamahewage Sampath Kumara Lamahewa,  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of  Haputale Urban
Council ,  acting in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Lamahewage Sampath Kumara Lamahewa, elected as  Vice Chairman  of   the Haputale Urban Council.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
 Haputale Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Badulla,
22nd  April, 2010.
