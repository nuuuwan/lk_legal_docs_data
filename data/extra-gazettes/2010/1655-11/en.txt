NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Baddegama  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Anthoni Bimal Indrajith de Silva, Returning  Officer of the Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that  Mr.   Gemunu Deshapriya Ekanayaka,  member  of  the
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased  to be a member of the  United
National Party.
