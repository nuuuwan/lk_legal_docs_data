THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 6/3/1/489.
Lands and Land Development Ministry's
 Reference No.: LD/05/2006/WS/399.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Ellakanda, in extent of about 0.1519
Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary Plan No. PPKA.
4174, dated 22nd May, 2009, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the village of  Horana in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Horana in Kalutara District in Kumbuka Pattuwa in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
Lot No. 01 – Ellakanda – 0.1519 Hectare
North: Road and Ellakanda Watta claimed by the Kotagala
Plantations Company (Lot No. 4500 in PP. 1355 (HP 64057); East:
Road and Ellakanda Watta claimed by the Kotagala Plantations
Company (Lot No. 4500 in PP. 1355 (HP 64057); South: Road and
Ellakanda Watta claimed by the Kotagala Plantations Company (Lot
No. 4500 in PP . 1355 (HP 64057); West: Road and Ellakanda Watta
claimed by the Kotagala Plantations Company (Lot No. 4500 in PP.
1355 (HP 64057).
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretary's Office, Horana on the 14th day of
September, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 01st day of September,
2010, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount. All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be
produced before me at the inquiry.
K. A. D. R. Nishanthi Jayasingha,
Divisional Secretary,
Horana.
Divisional Secretariat,
Horana.
03rd June, 2010.
