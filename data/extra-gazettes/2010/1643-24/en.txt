No. 1643/24 – WEDNESDAY ,  MARCH  03,  2010
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460 )
Notice under Section 07
My Refernce No.: Maprale/3/2/2000/194/LA.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act that the Government intends to acquire the
following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two (02) allotments of land called as, Galkaduwagedarawatte
Kumbura alias Pitakandawatta, Assmt. No. 244, Katugasthota Road
and bearing an extent of about 0.1036 Hectare from total extent
shown in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6068 and dated 06.01.2010
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys (Kandy
District) situtated in the village of Pallemahaiyawa in the Grama
Niladhari Division of Pitakanda, No. 250 in the Minor Division of
Mapanawathura No. 07 (within Urban Council limits) of Kandy and
Gangawata Korale Divisional Secretary's Division in Kandy District,
Central Province.
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Galkaduwagedarawatte Kumbura alias
Pitakandawatta, Assmt. No. 244, Katugasthota Road; Original
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.0421 Hectare.
Lot No. 02
Name of land: Galkaduwagedarawatte Kumbura alias
Pitakandawatta, Assmt. No. 244, Katugasthota Road; Original
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.0615 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land to be acquired are
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized
in writing before me at Gangawata Korale Divisional Secretariat on
the 17th March, 2010 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 10th March, 2010,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
T. M. S. Bandara Thennakoon,
Divisional Secretary,
Kandy and Gangawata Koralaya.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kandy and Gangawata Koralaya.
