The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is

STATEMENT OF MATTERS IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
01. Whether it is justified to state the fact that a charge sheet dated 21.02.2005 was issued on Mr. C. Wickramsinghe who was in the
service at Monaragala Depot which managed under the then Uva Bus Company Limited as a Grade III – A Officer by the Manager of the
Badulla Depot of the same Company being an unauthorized Officer in contrary to the existing disciplinary procedure of the aforesaid Bus
Company and if justified, to what relief he is entitled.
