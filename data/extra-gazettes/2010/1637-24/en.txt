 CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
The Order made under Section 185(1) (iii) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 2 of
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions)  Act, No. 12 of  Section 2(1) (iii) of Enactment of Supervisions of
Local Authorities Administration, No. 7 of 1990
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 185 (1)(iii)  of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with
Section 2 of  Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of    Section 2(1) (iii) of  Enactment   of   Supervisions
of Local Authorities Administration No. 7 of 1990 of the Central Province Provincial Council, I,  Sarath Ekanayake,  Chief
Minister of Central Province and Minister-in-charge of Local Government, do hereby dissolve  with  effect from the date of
suspension of  Udapalatha  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Further, by  virtue of the  powers vested in me by Section 185 (5) a of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be
read with Section 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, I hereby appoint Mr. Udage Mudiyanselage
Abeyratne Banda, as a Special Commissioner to be performed  the duties and use the powers of the Pradeshiya Sabha of
Udapalatha Kandy District of Central Province.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
Chief Minister of Central Province and
Minister-in-charge of Local Government.
Chief Minister’s Office,
Kandy,
20th January, 2010.
