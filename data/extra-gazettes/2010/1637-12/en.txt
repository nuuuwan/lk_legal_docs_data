THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW,  No.  41  OF  1978
Order under Section  3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law, No. 41 of 1978, I, Dinesh
Chandra Rupasinghe Gunawardena, Minister of Urban Development and Sacred Area Development being  of the opinion that
the area specified  in  Column I of the Schedule  I, appended hereto, having  specified the metes and bounds in  corresponding
entry in Column II of the Schedule, is suitable for development, do by  this order declare such area to be an  Urban
Development Area.
D
INESH CHANDRA RUPASINGHE GUNAW ARDENA,
Minister of Urban Development and  Sacred Area Development.
“Sethsiripaya”,
Battaramulla.
12th January, 2010.
