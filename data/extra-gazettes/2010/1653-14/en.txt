No. 1653/14 – WEDNESDAY,  MAY  12, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: Maha/3/316/Ath.
Lands and Land Development Ministry
 Reference No.: LD/05/2008/UDA/05.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Plan: P. P. Co. 9104
One allotment of  land called Millagahadeniya/Nugagahalanda/
Gingerhena situated at Depanama village, Maharagama Divisional
Secretariat Division, Colombo District in Western Province:
Lot No. 01
Name of land: Millagahadeniya/Nugagahalanda/Gingerhena;
Description: Rubber land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: L. D.
S. Samarasinghe, No. 121, Depanama, Pannipitiya; Extent:
3.2047 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretary's Office at Maharagama, on the 03rd day
of June, 2010 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of May, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
All deeds and documents in support of the claims should be produced
before me at the inquiry.
Chandraratne Pallegama,
Divisional Secretary,
Maharagama.
Divisional Secretariat,
Maharagama,
12th May, 2010.
