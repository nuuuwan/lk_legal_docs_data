No. 1638 / 4 – MONDAY, JANUARY 25, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jonston Kshevier Fernando, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, Shelter and War hero Welfare hereby declare under
Sub-section (1) of Section  5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land
described below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land with following names and extents containing
in total extent 0.2883 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. MR/
DKW/2008/458 dated 03.12.2008 prepared by the  Surveyor-General
situated  in the Village of  Dambagasara in,  Minor Division of
Wellabadapattu in theDivisional Secretary’s Division of Dickwella in
Matara District in Southern Province are bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Bogahawatte – Extent: 0.0220 Hectare
North: Bogahawatte alias Hambantota district; East: Hambantota
district, Lots F and E; South: Lots E and D; West: Lots D, B and
Paththinigewatte.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Paththinigewatte – Extent: 0.0318 Hectare
North: Paththinigewatte; East: Bogahawatte claimed by K. B.
Saminona, Lots A and D; South: Lots D, C and Bogahawatte claimed
by Darlis Loku Hennadige; West: Bogahawatte and Paththinigewatte
claimed by Darlis Loku Hennadige.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0656 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Lots B, D and Road; South: Road, Lot J and
Bogahawatte; West: Bogahawatte and Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0313 Hectare
North: Lots B and A; East: Lots  A,  E and Road; South: Road and
Lot C; West:Lots C and B.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0394 Hectare
North: Lots A and F; East: Lot F and Road; South: Road and Lot D;
West:Lots D and A.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0390 Hectare
North: Lot A, Hambantota district  and Lot G; East: Lot G and
Kamarankagahawatte and Sandathenna; South: Sandathenna, Road
and Lot E; West:Lots E , A and Hambantota district.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Kamarankagahawatte – Extent: 0.0102 Hectare
North: Hambantota district; East: Hambantota district and
Kamarankagaha watte; South: Kamarankagaha watte, Sandathenna
and Lot F; West: Lot F and Hambantota district.
08. Lot ‘H’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0153 Hectare
North: Road; East: Road and Sandathenna; South: Sandathenna;
West: Road.
09. Lot ‘J’ – Sandathenna – Extent: 0.0337 Hectare
North: Lot C and Road; East: Road and Sandathenna; South:
Sandathenna and Bogahawatte; West: Bogahawatte and Lot C.
