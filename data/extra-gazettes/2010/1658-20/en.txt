THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Wedi/3/Adhi/Ma/2008(9).
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of  Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the follow-
ing lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two (2) allotments of land in extent of about 0.0618 Hectare,
depicted as Lot No.1-2 in the Preliminary Plan No. GA3741  dated
19th  May, 2010, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
village of Nugethota, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Welivitiya-Divitura, in the District of Galle Southern Province
morefully discribed as follows.
Lot No. 1
Name of  Land: Mottakanda addara watta; Description : waste
Land; Claimant: state; Original  Claimant:¨ Land Reform Commission;
Extent: 0.0202 Hectare.
Lot No. 2
Name  of  Land :  Mottakanda addara watta;  Description: wast
land; Claimant: state; Original Claimant: L.K.Harischandra,
Weraniyagewatta,Baddegam;  Extent: 0.0416 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Welivitiya - Divitura Divisional Secretariat, on the 18th day of
August, 2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of August, 2010,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
D.Kusalani De Silva,
Divisional Secretary,
Welivitiya-Divitura.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Welivitiya-Divitura,
3rd of June, 2010.
