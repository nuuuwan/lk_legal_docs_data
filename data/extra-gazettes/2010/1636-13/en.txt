EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE  BADDEGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Baddegama  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member  of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Baddegama Samitha Thero,   the member belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(2)  of the aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate a person eligible under the ordinance for Election as a member of that  Baddegama    Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs
to  such party   who shall be declared  elected as  a  member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  nominated  Mr.    Withanage Sisira Nihal
Waidyasekara Weerasinghe, being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Baddegama      Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Withanage Sisira Nihal Waidyasekara Weerasinghe,  elected as a member  of   the Baddegama   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Baddegama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Galle,
