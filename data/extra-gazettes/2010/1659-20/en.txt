AIR NAVIGATION (AIR DEFENCE) REGULATIONS  No. 1 OF 2007
NOTIFICATION published by the Director General of Civil Aviation in terms of Regulation 2 of the Air Navigation
(Air Defence) Regulations, No. 1 of 2007.
H. M. C. NIMALSIRI,
Director General of Civil Aviation.
Civil Aviation Authority,
Colombo 03,
19th May, 2010.
The Notification published in Extraordinary Gazette,  No. 1524/16 of 23rd November, 2007 by the Acting
Director General of Civil Aviation in terms of Regulation 2 of the Air Navigation (Air Defence) Regulations, No. 1 of 2007
is hereby amended by the substitution for item 2.3 (e) of Section 2 as follows :—
2.3 (e) : An ADC number is valid for the entire flight until it reaches its destination. Once the ADC number is
issued the flight can depart 30 minites prior to the estimated off block time. If the flight is delayed more
than  one hour (01) from the Flight Planned Off Block Time, a fresh ADC Number should be obtained.
