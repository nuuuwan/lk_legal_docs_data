THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT,  No. 15 OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Paragraphs  ( a) and  ( b) of Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15
of 1987,   I, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do hereby amend the
Order published in  Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 453/8 of May 12, 1987 relating to the  Constitution  of the  Dambulla
Pradeshiya Sabha, As follows :—
(1) By the deletion of Schedule thereto and substitution of Schedule hereto ;
(2) ( a) By the repeal of the word “nine” in the fourth paragraph of the said order and the substitution of
the word “thirteen” ; and
(b) The above Order shall come into force with effect from the first day of April, 2011.
J
ANAKA BANDARA TENNAKOON,
Minister of Local Government and
Provincial Councils.
Ministry of  Local Government and Provincial Councils.
Colombo.
02nd February, 2010.

MUNICIPAL COUNCILS ORDINANCE
Order
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 2, 3, 5 and 9 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Cahpter 252), I, Janaka
Bandara Tennakoon, Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, do by this Order —
(1) declare the area specified in Part - I of the Schedule hereto, to be a Municipality for the purposes of that
Ordinance ;
(2) define the limits of the Municipality to be the limits set out in Part - II of that Schedule ;
(3) Constitute a Municipal Council for the Municipality declared under paragraph (1) of the Order and assign the
following name and designation to such Council ;
“DAMBULLA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL”
(4) prescribe that the Dambulla  Municipal Council shall consist of the Mayor, the Deputy Mayor and thirteen
other Councilors ;
(5) appoint the first day of April 2011, as the date on which the term of office of the Dambulla  Municipal Council
shall commence.
J
ANAKA BANDARA TENNAKOON,
Minister of Local Government and
Ministry of  Local Government and Provincial Councils, Provincial Councils.
Colombo,
02nd February, 2010.
