Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as  the Open University of  Sri Lanka (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 01 of 2010,
and shall come into operation from 21st April, 2010.
2. The Open University of Sri Lanka Ordinance 1990, published in Gazette Extraordinary  No.591/18 of
January 5, 1990, as amended from time to time and last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1563/2 of August 18, 2008, is hereby further amended in paragraph 16 of that Ordinance, as follows :-
(a) in sub-paragraph (3) thereof, by the repeal of item (iv) and the substitution therefor of the following
item :-
“(iv)  Mathematics and Computer Science ;” ; and
(b) in Sub-paragraph (4) thereof, by the repeal of item (vi) and the substitution therefor of the following
item :-
“(vi)  Textile and Apparel Technology ;” .
