EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   MATARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Matara  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People’s Liberation Front, the recognized political party to which
S. A. Charles Nandana,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Matara   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to
such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Ayeshan Dumindu
Gallage,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,    Returning Officer of  Matara    Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ayeshan
Dumindu Gallage, elected as  a  member  of   the  Matara Pradeshiya   Sabha.
C. A. S
UNETH LOCHANA,
  Returning Officer,
Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
15th February, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   NIYAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Niyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of Death  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Gonapinuwalage Sunil Fernando,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance,
to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Niyagama   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Hengedarage
Premakumar   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Anthony Bimal  Indrajith De Silva,   Returning Officer of  Niyagama   Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   Hengedarage
Premakumar, elected as  a  member  of   the  Niyagama  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
 Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Niyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Galle,
