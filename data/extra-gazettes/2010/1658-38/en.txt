EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  BALAPITIYA   PRADESHIYA
SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
 ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Balapitiya  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the Southern Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which
Thrimadura Channa Sri Saliya Mendis  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under   Section 65A(1)  of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that Balapitiya   Pradeshiya  Sabha  who belongs to  such party
who shall be declared  elected as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr.  Waradana Sarath Samarajeewa De Silva,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I,  Anthony  Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer  of   Balapitiya      Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting
in  terms of   Section  65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Waradana Sarath Samarajeewa De Silva,  elected as Vice Chairman  of   the Balapitiya   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E  SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
