I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the

following land which is required for a public purpose :
Description of the land to be acquired :
One allotments of land in total extent of about Hectare, depicted in
the Preliminary Plan No. FVP  706,   prepared by the Surveyor-
General,  situated in the village of Kanduwewa in Wilachchiya Korale
in Nochchiyagama Divisional Secretary’s Division in Anuradapura
District in North Central Province.
Lot No. 240
Name of Land: Kanduwewalanda; Description: Garden; Claimants:

nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
