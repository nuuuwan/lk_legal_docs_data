WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 2 of the Powers of  Supervision of the Administration
 of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranathunga, the  Chief  Minister and the Minister of  Finance and  Planning,  Law and Order, Land, Education,
Local Government and Provincial  Administration,  Power and Energy,  Manpower and Employment and Economic
Promotion of the  Western Province Provincial Council, by virtue of the powers vested in me in accordance
with the Provisions in Section 2(2) of the powers  of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities statute
No. 4 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council, do hereby announce that the team of inquiry of the One-Man
Commission of inquiry consisting of Hon. B. L. K. Wimalachandra, Retired Judge of the Court of  Appeal, appointed
by the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1575/05 dated 10th November, 2008 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka to inquire into and report as to whether   the matters set out in Section 2(1) of the above Statute have been
committed by the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Any Council Member of the Colombo Municipal Council and the individual
and collective responsibility of the said  Mayor, Deputy Mayor  and Council Members, shall be extended from
1st May, 2010 to 31st  May, 2010.
P
