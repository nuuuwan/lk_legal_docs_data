No. 1645/21 – WEDNESDAY, MARCH 17, 2010
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Jeewan Kumaranatunga, Minister of Lands and Land Development
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Boundry to Bogahawatta (A, C) allotments of land in total extent
of about 0.1268 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. H/BLA/
2008/278 dated 02nd July, 2008, of prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the village of Puwakdandawa in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Beliatta in Hambantota District in Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Bogahawatta – Extent: About 0.0674 Hectare
North: Vincent Road and the land situated Beliatta Police Station;
East: The land situated Beliatta Police Station; South: Lot B;
West: Lot B and Vincent Road.
02. Lot 'C' – Bogahawatta – Extent: About 0.0594 Hectare
North: Vincent Road and Lot B; East: Lot B and the land situated
Beliatta Police Station; South: The land situated Beliatta Police Station
and the road; West: Road and Vincent road.
Jeewan Kumaranatunga,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: LD/05/2008/D/192.
