LD/05/2007/E/195.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a playground of NK/Srisumana Siri Maha
Vidyalaya.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land situated in the village of Siyambala Gaswetiya
in Amunukole Grama Niladhari Division – 1307 in Kobeigane
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kurunegala District in North
Western Province as follows:
Plan No. F.V .P. 1624, Supplement No. 04.
Lot No. 168
Name of land: Meda Mookalana, Description: School Playground;
Claimant: D. M. Chandrawathie; Extent proposed to acquire
0.6474 Hectares; Full Extent: 18.4542 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Kobeigane, on the 05th day of May,
2010 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 05th day of May, 2010, the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
R. M. W. S. Samaradiwakara,
Divisional Secretary,
Kobeigane.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kobeigane.
