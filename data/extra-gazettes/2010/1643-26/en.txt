 UV A   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of powers vested to me under Section 154F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Caluwadewagey Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce
that I have made the following appointment.
Hon. Chief Minister Shashindra Kumara Rajapaksha — As Acting Minister of  Road Development, Housing,
Water Supply, Consumer Affairs and Co-operative and
Food Supply and Distribution, of the Uva Province, from
the date 28th January, 2010 till the return to Sri Lanka of
Hon. Minister R. M. Kumarasiri Rathnayake.
C. N
ANDA MATHEW,
Governor of  Uva Province.
