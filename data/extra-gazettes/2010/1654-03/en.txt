  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Eastern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern  Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr.   Nimal Gamini Hewawitharana
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Nimal Gamini Hewawitharana, elected as a member of the   above  Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of  Trincomalee  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
15th May, 2010.

  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Sabaragamuwa  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary  of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1)
of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Sabaragamuwa
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a Member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  U. K. R. Abeyrathna to
fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  U. K. R. Abeyrathna, elected as a member of the above  Provincial Council
from the Administrative District of  Kegalle  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
17th May, 2010.
