NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION 16 SUB-SECTION 2(b) OF THE COAST CONSERV ATION ACT,  No. 57 OF 1981
Proposed Holiday Resort at Passikudah - Batticaloa
ENVIRONMENTAL  IMPACT  ASSESSMENT  REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Holiday Resort Project
at Passikudah, submitted by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority, No. 80, Galle Road, Colombo 03 as required
by the Section 16(1) of the Coast Conservation Act, No. 57 of 1981 will be available for inspection by the public at the
following institutions between 8.30 a.m. and 4.15 p.m. for a period of 30 days from 17.03.2010 (Please note that these
offices are closed on weekends and public holidays).
(1) Ministry of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, New Secretariat Building, Maligawatta, Colombo 10,
(2) Coast Conservation Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor, Maligawatta, Colombo 10.
(3) Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa” Densil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
(4) Urban Development Authority, Sethsiripaya, Battaramulla.
(5) District Secretariat, Batticaloa.
(6) Divisional Secretariat, V alachchenai.
(7) Pradeshiya Sabha, Valachchenai.
(8) Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, District Fisheries Office, Batticaloa.
Any member of the Public may within 30 days from 17th March, 2010 submit their comments in writing on the
above Environmental  Impact Assessment to the Director, Coast Conservation Department.
