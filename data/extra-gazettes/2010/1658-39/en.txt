PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Order No. 13 .
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09 OF 2003
Order  under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003,
the  Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader, importer  or distributor shall sell,  expose or offer
for  sale, display for  sale  any  Full Cream  Milk Powder above  the  maximum  retail prices given below.
Maximum Retail Price
400 g pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 244.00
1 kg pack Full Cream Milk Powder Rs. 598.00
All other pack sizes of Full Cream Milk Powder except the pack sizes stated above, shall not be sold above
Rs. 61.00 per 100 g.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 09 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1627/6 dated 09th November, 2009 issued in respect of the Maximum Retail Price of  Full Cream Milk  Powder.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
 Chairman.
Colombo,
20th June, 2010.
