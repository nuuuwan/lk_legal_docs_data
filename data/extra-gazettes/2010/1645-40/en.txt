THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Ima/L /3/STDP/582.
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
land which is required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
 Two allotmentè of  land  in total extent of  0.0320Hectare,depicted
as  Lot No. 472 and 473, in  FVP No  650 supplement 13  and dated
29.08.2009, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in Wathawana
village  in  the Imaduwa Divisional  Secretary’s Division in the District
of Galle in Southern Province morefully  described as follows:
1. Lot No. 472
Name of  land: Koongahakoratuwa; Description: Garden,  20-25
years old 3 King Coconut trees; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:

0.0059 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 473
Name of  land: Koongahawatta; Description: Tea Cultivation, about
12 years old tea cultivation and Memorial tomb; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant: Piyadasa Liyanage,  “Isuru”, Wathawana, Imaduwa;
Extent: 0.0261 Hectare.

at Imaduwa  Divisional Secretariat on the 18th  of  May, 2010  at 10.00

on or before the 11th of  May, 2010 the nature of their interests in the
land, the particulars or their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
