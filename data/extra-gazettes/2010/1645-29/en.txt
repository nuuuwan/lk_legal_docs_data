1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   KATTANKUDI  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in  Kattankudi  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being  elected as a member
of the Eastern Province Provincial Council   ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 6  to which  Umar  Lebbe Mohamed Noorul
Mubeen,   the Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  decide upon the
member of that  Kattankudi Urban Council   who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman
within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group has communicated his decision to nominate Mr. Ahamed
Lebbe Mohamed Marzook to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam  Krishnananthalingam,    Returning Officer of  Kattankudi Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Ahamed
Lebbe Mohamed Marzook, elected as  Chriaman of   the  Kattankudi Urban Council.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
  Returning Officer,
Kattankudi Urban Council .
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
05th March,  2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE   CHAIRMAN OF  THE   KATTANKUDI  URBAN
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  Vice  Chairman  in  Kattankudi  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of   Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 6  to which  Ahamed  Lebbe Mohamed Marzook,  the
Vice  Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  decide upon the
member of that  Kattankudi Urban Council   who belongs to such group who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman
within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group has communicated his decision to nominate Mr.  Sahul Hameed
Mohamed Asfer  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam  Krishnananthalingam,    Returning Officer of  Kattankudi Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(1)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Sahul Hameed
Mohamed Asfer, elected as  Vice Chriaman of   the  Kattankudi Urban Council.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
  Returning Officer,
Kattankudi Urban Council .
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
05th March,  2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   KATTANKUDI  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Kattankudi  Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason  of   being  elected as a member
of the Eastern Province Provincial Council  ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 6  to which  Umar  Lebbe Mohamed Noorul
Mubeen,  the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid  Ordinance, to  nominate a person
eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kattankudi Urban Council who belongs to such group
who shall be declared elected as a member  within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group has  nominated Mr. Thambi Lebbe Abdul Wahab, being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Thuraisingam  Krishnananthalingam,    Returning Officer of  Kattankudi Urban Council,  acting in
terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Thambi  Lebbe
Abdul Wahab elected as  a member of   the  Kattankudi Urban Council.
T. K
RISHNANANTHALINGAM,
  Returning Officer,
Kattankudi Urban Council .
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
