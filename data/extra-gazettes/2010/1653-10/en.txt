NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Beliatte  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Jayasinghe Arachchillage Samantha Priyaleel Jayasinghe, Returning  Officer of the  Beliatte Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby
declare under  Section 10 A (1) ( a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that  Mr.   Geegana Arachchige Danee,
member   of  the   Beliatte   Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of  that  Sabha  as he has ceased  to be a member
of the  United  National Party.
J. A. S. P. JAY ASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Beliatte Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
02nd May, 2010.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   NUWARAGAMPALATHA  CENTRAL
PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Nuwaragampalatha Central  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  being
ceased to be a member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Wijesooriya Arachchilage Raja Abewickrama,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance, to   nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Nuwaragampalatha
Central   Pradeshiya   Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty
(30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated Mr.   P . G . G . Gunawardena,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore,  I,  S. M. Saman Bandulasena,   Returning Officer of  Nuwaragampalatha Central   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.   P. G. G. Gunawardena,  elected as a member of the Nuwaragampalatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. M. S
AMAN BANDULASENA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragampalatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
03rd May, 2010.
