PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTION  —  2010
Fresh Poll at Polling Stations where the poll has been annulled
WHEREAS the poll in the Parliamentary Election held on April 08th, 2010 at Polling stations mentioned in the Columns III of
the Schedule below, has been annulled, I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Election do hereby give notice that the
fresh poll will be held from 7.00 a.m. to 4.00 p.m. on April 20th, 2010 at such polling stations in terms of Section 48 A(7) (b) of
Parliamentary Elections Act No. 01 of 1981 as amended by Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 35 of 1988.
Names of candidates of each Recognised Political Party or Independent Group, the Serial Number assigned to each
candidate of such parties and groups, and the approved symbol allotted to each political party and independent group and
the distinguishing number allotted to each Independent Group are as specified in the Notice under the Section 24(1) of
Parliamentary Election Act 1 of 1980 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1943/87 on 02nd of March, 2010.
The order in which the names of each recognized political party and the distinguishing number of each independent
group as appearing in the Gazette Notifications referred to, shall be the same order in which these particulars will be printed
on the ballot paper.
The Returning Officers of Mahanuwara and Trincomalee Electoral Districts will conduct the poll at the Polling
Stations in each respective Polling Division.
D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
10th  April, 2010.
