EMERGENCY   (MISCELLANEOUS  PROVISIONS  AND  POWERS)  REGULATIONS,  No. 1 OF  2005
Appointment  of Commissioner - General of Rehabilitation
C.F.  1/64.
BY virtue of the powers vested in  me by Paragraph (1)  of  Regulation 22 of  the Emergency (Miscellaneous Provisions and
Powers) Regulations, No. 1 of 2005, Published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1405/14 of August 13, 2005 and deemed to be in
force by virtue of Section 2A of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, MAHINDA RAJAPAKSA,  PRESIDENT, do  hereby
appoint with effect from January 31, 2010, for the purposes of the aforesaid regulations, Brigadier Sudantha Ranasinghe,
RWP ,  RSP, psc to be  the Commissioner-General of Rehabilitation for  the whole of  Sri Lanka.
The Regulation  published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 1611/ 29 of July 24, 2009, is hereby   rescinded.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
  President.
Colombo,
06th May, 2010.
