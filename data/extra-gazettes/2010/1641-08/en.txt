BADULLA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Entertainment Tax Ordinance
THE  following resolution passed by the Badulla Municipal Council in terms of sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the
Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267), has been approved by the Minister of Local Government in the Uva Province
in terms of Sub-section (2) of that Section and is hereby published in  terms of that Sub-section.
RESOLUTION
By virtue of the powers vested by sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267),
the Badulla Municipal Council, hereby resolves to impose and levy, with effect from the 1st day of the following month
of the month in which this resolution is published in the Gazette,  a tax equivalent to 7.5 percent of the payment
made for admission to Entertainments defined in the Ordinance (Other than entertainment Tax) held in the area within
administrative limits of the Council :
Provided that the tax levied upon a payment made to watch a film shall be 7.5 percent of that payment within
the first two years in which this resolution is in force.
S
UMITH SURAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Local Government,
Uva Province.
Ministry of Local Government,
10th February, 2010.
