  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Eastern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern  Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament.
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member belonged
was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr.  Mohamed Hanifa Sehu
Ismail to fill such vacancy ;
I, Dayananda Dissanayake, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils
Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Mohamed Hanifa Sehu Ismail, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Batticaloa  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
 D
AYANANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
25th May, 2010.
