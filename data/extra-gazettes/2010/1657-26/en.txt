EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER OF  THE   WELIGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Weligama  Pradeshiya  Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason  of  Resignation  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which
Hewa Walgamage Mahinda Kulathunga,   the member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to  nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Weligama   Pradeshiya   Sabha
who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of   thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has   nominated   Mr.  Navimana Liyanage
Kularatna,   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore,  I,  Candauda Arachchige Suneth Lochana,    Returning Officer of  Weligama    Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of   Section  65A(2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare
Mr.  Navimana Liyanage Kularatna, elected as  a  member  of   the  Weligama Pradeshiya   Sabha.
C. A. S
UNETH LOCHANA,
  Returning Officer,
Weligama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
07th June,  2010.

NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10 A  (1)  (a)  OF  THE   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Thirappane Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  S. M. Saman  Bandulasena,  Returning  Officer  of  the  Thirappane Pradeshiya Sabha,  do hereby declare   under

United  National Party.
