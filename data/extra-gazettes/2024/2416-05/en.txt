Central Bank of Sri Lanka Notices
CIVIL PROCEDURE CODE
Notification under Section 192
IT is hereby notified that  by virtue of the powers vested in the Central Bank of Sri Lanka established under the Central Bank of 
Sri Lanka Act, No. 16 of 2023, in terms of Section 192 of the Civil Procedure Code, as amended by the Civil Procedure Code 
(Amendment) Act, No. 06 of 1990, the Central Bank has, having regard to the current rates of bank interest, determined that 
with effect from 01st January 2025, the Legal Rate of Interest shall be twelve point one two per centum (12.12%) per annum.
 Dr. P. Nandalal Weerasinghe, 
 Chairman of the Governing Board and Governor,  
 Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
Colombo,
20th december, 2024
