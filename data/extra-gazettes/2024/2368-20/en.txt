THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY LAW No. 41 OF 1978 OF THE NATIONAL STATE ASSEMBLY
Cancellation of the Vesting Order under Section 15 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in the Minister of Western Region Development under Section 15 (1) of the Urban Development 
Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, the land described in the schedule hereto was vested with the Urban Development Authority, by 
the Order under Section 15(1) published in Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 1273/5 dated 27.01.2003 and Extraordinary 
(Amended) Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 1316/9 dated 27.11.2003.
   I, Prasanna Ranathunga, Minister of Urban Development and Housing do hereby cancel the aforesaid Vesting Order under 
Section 15 (1) published in Extraordinary Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 1273/5 dated 27.01.2003 and Extraordinary (Amended) 
Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 1316/9 dated 27.11.2003 since the subject property is no required for the purpose of the Urban
Development Authority.
   
         P
