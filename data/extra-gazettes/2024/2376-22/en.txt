THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of land described below, depicted in 
Surveyor General’s Advance Tracing No.  Ma/LGG/2018/441 
of 14.06.2019 situated at Dewaladeniya (Topo P.P.20) Village 
in, Gangala Udasiya Pattuwa Minor Division of Laggala 
Pallegama Divisional Secretary’s Division, Matale District, 
Central Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot ‘A’ – Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya – Extent: 
3.4231 Hectares
 North: Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya claimed by 
Konara Mudiyanselage Watte Gedara Wajira Chanaka 
and Ma Oya; East: Mathuwagala claimant not known and 
Mathuwagala claimed by W. G. Nimal Karunarathna; South: 
Mathuwagala claimed by W. G. Nimal Karunarathna, Lot 
B and Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya claimed by Pahala 
Gedara Sirisoma; West: Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya 
claimed by Konara Mudiyanselage Lalith Wasantha Konara 
and Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya claimed by Konara 
Mudiyanselage Watte Gedara Wajira Chanaka.
 02. Lot ‘B’ – Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya – Extent: 
2.2983 Hectares
 North: Wattakkayaya and Bathalayaya claimed by Konara 
Mudiyanselage Lalith Wasantha Konara and Lot A; East: 
Mathuwagala claimed by W. G. Nimal Karunarathna; South: 
Mathuwagala claimed by W. G. Nimal Karunarathna and
