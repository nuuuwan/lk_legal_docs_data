PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTION- 2024 
Section 127 B of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 
FACILITIES FOR VOTING AT ANOTHER POLLING STATION FOR THOSE WHO HA VE A REASONABLE 
FEAR OF NOT BEING ABLE TO CAST VOTE AT A GIVEN POLLING STATION DUE TO THE CONDITIONS 
PREV AILING IN THE AREA WITHIN WHICH HIS POLLING STATION IS SITUATED 
DUE to the prevailing situation in the area where his polling station is situated, an elector who is reasonably fearful that he 
will not be able to go to and cast the vote at the given polling station, may request to be facilitated to vote at another polling 
station. The provisions of Section 127 B of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by the Parliamentary 
Elections Act, No. 15 of 1988, have provided in this regard. 
02. The relevant applications can be obtained from Of ﬁ ces of all District Returning Of ﬁ cers / District Election 
Ofﬁ ces as well as from Divisional Secretariats and Grama Niladhari Of ﬁ ces. The applications must be forwarded to reach 
the Commissioner General of Elections, Election Commission, Sarana Mawatha, Rajagiriya within a week from the date of 
the nomination notice, i. e from 24.09.2024. 
03. The application forms must be certiﬁ ed by the Grama Niladhari of the applicant's residence and the applicant 
must include the National Identity Card number in the form. Applications submitted without the Grama Niladhari Certiﬁ cate 
will be rejected.
