WHEREAS, the office of Member of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation;
And whereas the secretary of the Jathika Jana Balawegaya, the recognized political party to which Kolamba 
Thanthreege Nishantha Perera, the Member belonged, was required by me under section 66A(1) (a) of the aforesaid ordinance, 
to decide upon the member of that Ward Number 15 Ambana Kahadoowa of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such 
party and is not disqualified under Section 9 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and who shall be 
declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Nagaraja Saara Vinodani to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Nagaraja Saara Vinodani was elected as the member of the Ward Number 15 Ambana Kahadoowa of Elpitiya Pradeshiya 
Sabha.
   
 W. H. R. Wijaya KumaRa,
 Returning Officer,
 Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
