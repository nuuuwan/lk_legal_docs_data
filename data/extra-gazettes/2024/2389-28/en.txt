THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below 
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 An  allotment of land, in total Extent of about 1.464 Hectare, 
depicted in the Advanced Tracing No. NU/WLP/93/261 dated 
22.03.1999, prepard by the Surveyor-General, situated in the 
village of Pannala in Oya Palatha Korale Minor Division in 
Divisional Secretary's Division of Walapane in Nuwara Eliya 
District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
 01. Lot 'A' – Hapugolle Hena  – Extent:  about 1.464 
Hectare
 North: Agala and Hapugolle Hena owned by Government; 
East: Adipara and Hapugolle Hena owned by Government; 
South: Adipara and Hapugolle Watta claimed  by Y . M. Dingiri 
Banda; West: Hapugolle Watta, Hapugolle Wewa claimed by 
Y . M. Dingiri Banda and Agala.
 Harin Fernando,
