LOCAL  AuThORITIES   ELECTION  - 2023
Enforcing the Gazette Notifications on Media Guidelines for a further period of Three Months
it is hereby, notified that the Gazette Notification No. 2313/33, dated 04.01.2023 on Media Guidelines and the Gazette 
Notification No. 2318/78, dated 11.02.2023 on Media Guidelines issued as a part and parcel of the said Gazette Notification 
both issued in respect of the Local Authorities Election - 2023 the validity of which was extended for a period of three (03) 
months by the Gazette Notification No. 2352/50, dated 07.10.2023 will be in force for a further period of three (03) months 
from 08.01.2024.
