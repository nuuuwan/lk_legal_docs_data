PROPOSAL
By virtue of powers vested by sub section 2(1) of Entertainment Ordinance No.12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment 
tax (amendment) act No. 37 of 1984 and by virtue of powers vested in me Secretary of tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha by section 
9 (3) of Pradeshiya Sabha act No. 15 of 1987, it is hereby proposed to impose and recover an entertainment tax of 10% of 
the entrance fee charged for entertainment purposes described in the ordinance aforesaid within the administrative limits of 
tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha and this proposal has to be implemented with effect from 01.09.2023. i have further decided that 
entertainment tax so imposed has to be paid to tangalle Pradeshiya Sabha by the organizer or organizers of that event before 
the date of holding such event of entertainment.
