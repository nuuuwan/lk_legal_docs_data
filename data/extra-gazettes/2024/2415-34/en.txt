Provincial Councils Notifications
CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL           
Notification  issued  by  the  Hon. Governor
I, sarath b. s. Abayakoon, Governor of central Province, by virtue of powers vested in me under Article 154(c) of the 13th 
amendment to the constitution of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, do hereby announce that the department 
of Provincial engineering services - central Province, which is currently under the purview of chief ministry & the ministry 
of education under subjects not covered by by any other ministry, has been brought under the purview of the chief secretariat 
- central Province and accordingly, it shall be operated under deputy chief secretary (engineering services) under the 
administration of the chief secretary - central Province.
