Part I : Sec. (I) - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 01.02.2024
7a
1. The difference between the already paid amount of the annual license fee specified in sub-column II of Schedule I above 
and the license fee revised by this notification, shall be calculated as determined by the Commissioner General of Excise 
in relation to the remaining period of the current year and it has to be paid within a month from the effective date of this 
Excise Notification. 
2. The difference between the already paid amount of the security deposit amount specified in sub-column III of Schedule 
I above and the security deposit amount as amended by this notification shall be paid to the Commissioner General of 
Excise within a period of one month from the effective date of this notification. 
3. The one-time fee for industry entry specified under sub-column IV of the above schedule I shall be a non-refundable fee 
to be paid by the licensee only once at the first issuing of the relevant license and it should be paid to the Commissioner 
General of Excise from the effective date of this Excise Notification.
