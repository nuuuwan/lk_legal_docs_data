PRESIDENTIAL  ELECTION  - 2024
Presidential  Elections  Act,  No.  15  of  1981
OrDer uNDer SectiON  2
WhereAS  the term of the President expires on November 17, 2024 and,
Whereas the poll for the election of the President shall be taken not less than one month and not more than two 
months before the expiration of the term of office of the President in office, as stipulated in Article 31 (3) of the Constitution,
in terms of the powers vested in the election commission by Section 2 of the Presidential elections  Act, No. 15 
of 1981 read with Article 104 B of the Constitution it is hereby ordered.
 (I)  15th August 2024 as the date of nomination of candidates for the Election of the President ;
 (II) office of the Election Commission, Sarana Mawatha, Rajagiriya, as the place of nomination of candidates for 
the said election ; and
 (III) 21st September, 2024, as the date for holding the poll for that election.
