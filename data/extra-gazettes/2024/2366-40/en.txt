LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS

Notice Under  Section 7 
 
 
Reference No.: KHDS/LND/03/ACQ/OTHER/18. 
Land Ministry’s  No. : 4-3/9/2019/S/223. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act No.  28 of 
1964 that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land / servitude which is required for a public 
purpose: 
 
The allotment of land in extent of 0.0405 hectare 
depicted as the Lot No. 1 in sheet 1 Block 1 of the 
Cadastral Map No. 620568 of the preliminary plan dated 
20.10.2023, prepared by the surveyor General, situated in 
the Village called Endana in Atakalan Korale Pannil Pattu 
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Kahawatta in Ratnapura District of the Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Endanawatta; Description: Garden, 
a permanent building a mixed crop cultivation with a teak 
tree of about 15 -20 years old are available; Claimant : 
Kahawatta Plantations PLC, Land Reform Commission; 
Extent: 0.0405 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents 
duly authorized in writing, before me at Kahawatta 
Divisional Secretariat, on 25th March, 2024 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 25th March 2024 the nature of 
their interests in the land/servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
