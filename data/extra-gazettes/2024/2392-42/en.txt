ThE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
By virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 3 & 4 of the societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, dr. ramesh Pathirana, 
Minister of Ministry of Industries, do  by this notification ;
 01. authorize the purpose for which the society known as “Kolamba waraye regu welanda Balapathralabeenge 
sangamaya” situated No.128/13/a, delgahawatta, Madapatha Piliyandala as a purpose to which the powers and 
facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended ; and
 02. limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of section 5(4) of 
that Ordinance.
