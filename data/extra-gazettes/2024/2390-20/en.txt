My Reference No.: DIVKW/LND/LND2/ACQUT/5/11

I hereby give notice, under Sub Section (1) of Section (7) 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land / 
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
All that divided and defined allotment of land referred in 
the Sheet No. 01, in the Zene No.04, in the Cadaster Plan 
No. 421124 situated at the village of Horombawa in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Horombawa 1134 in 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kuliyapitiya East in 
the District of Kurunegala in the North Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 - Name of the land: Bogahalanda; 
Land Use: There are Home Garden, Permanent 03 
Buildings and Temporary Crop Cultivation; Claimant: 
Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.0584 Hec.  
Boundaries 
North: Land lot 26 in F.V.P 210; East: Land lot 26 in 
F.V.P 210; South: Land lot 26 in F.V.P 210; West: Land 
lot 26 in F.V.P 210. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land / lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are  hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kuliyapitiya
– East at 10.30 A.M. on 28 th day of August 2024, and 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 21 st day  of August 2024 the nature of 
their interests in the land , the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land / 
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the compensation of such  amount. 
                                                
 D. P. S. Kumara, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kuliyapitiya - East. 
  
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Kuliyapitiya - East.
