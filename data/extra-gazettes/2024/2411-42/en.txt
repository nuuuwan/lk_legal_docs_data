STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. S. D. Ravindra Sukumal who  worked in Hambantota Lanka Salt Limited who had been considered to have 
vacated his post from 18.02.2016 and reinstated in employment with effect from 19.02.2016 by letter dated 08.01.2020 stating 
that arrears of salary and other allowances will be paid and Mr. S. H. N. Chandana de Silva, who had been considered to 
have vacated his post from 09.07.2016 and reinstated in employment with effect from  10.07.2016 by letter dated 08.01.2020 
stating that arrears of salary and other allowances will be paid. have been caused injustice by the non-payment of arrears of 
salary and allowances relevent to the said periods and if so, to what relief they are entitled
  
 H. K. K. A. Jayasundara,
 Commissioner General of Labour.
