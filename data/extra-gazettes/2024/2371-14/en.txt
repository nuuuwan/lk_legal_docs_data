Regulations
 1. these regulations may be cited as the Civil Procedure (Costs) regulations, 2024 and shall come into 
operation on 01st of March, 2024
 2. all bills of costs, whether between a party and parties, or between a registered attorney and a client, 
shall be taxed by the registrar of the court at the rates as set out in the schedule hereto.
