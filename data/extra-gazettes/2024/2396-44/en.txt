FOREST  CONSERvATION  ORDINANCE 
the order under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act 
No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009. 
The Order 
By virtue of the powers vested in me under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451)  
No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 
and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation do by this Order release the block of forest 
lands of the extent of 107.0 hectares and bounded by the limits set forth in the schedules 1, 2, 3, 4,5 & 6 given below, of 
the Irampaikkulam Reserved Forest which was declared as a reserved Forest by Gazette No. 7182 dated 10th June, 1921 
from the reserved status from 07th day of  august 2024.
 
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi, 
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation. 
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation, 
No. 07, hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Colombo 07,
07th day of august, 2024.
