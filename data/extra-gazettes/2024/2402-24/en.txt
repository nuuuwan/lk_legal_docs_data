ThE  PubLIC  SECuRITY  ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER 40)
Curfew Order under Section 16
whereas I, consider that it is necessary to maintain public order in the areas specified in the Schedule hereto, I, Ranil 
wickremesinghe, president, by virtue of the powers vested in me under section 16 of the public security Ordinance (Chapter 
40) do by this Order direct that no person shall be on any public road, railway, public park, public recreation ground or other 
public ground or the seashore in such areas, from 06.00 hour of september 22nd 2024 to 12.00 hour of september 22nd 2024, 
except under the authority of a written permit granted by secretary, ministry of defence or secretary, ministry of public 
Security or any officer authorized by them.
 
Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 president.
Colombo,
september 22, 2024.
