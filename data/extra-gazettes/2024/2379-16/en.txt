REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS - 2024  
Notice of the Election Commission on the display of ‘A’ and ‘B’ Lists, Claims and Objections
Definition
 01. “act”  -  registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of 
electors (amendment) act, No. 22 of 2021.
 02. Valid electoral register  -  the main register of the year 2023 and supplementary electoral registers 
2023(1), 2023(2) and 2023(3).
 03. “a’list” -  list of names of persons whose names appear in the operative electoral 
register but are ineligible due to death or leaving the residence or for any other 
reason namely, persons who are not eligible to be in the valid electoral register 
and to be removed from the address.
 04. “B’list” -  list of persons whose names are not included in the eligible address in the 
operative electoral register but are eligible for inclusion in the electoral register 
for the year 2024 either on completion of 18 years of age or residence or for 
any other reason.

Instructions
 01. On behalf of the Registering Officer of each district, it is hereby notified that the Registering Officers mentioned 
in the Column ii of the schedule below for the electoral districts mentioned in the corresponding Column i of the 
said Schedule have notified on 09.04.2024 that the preparation of Lists “A” and “B” has been completed and that 
the said lists or parts thereof are placed at the aforementioned exhibition centers for inspection by the public during 
office hours, as per Section 13 of the Registration of Electors Act.
 02. Every person who is qualified in terms of Section 14 of the Registration of Electors Act to have his own name or 
someone else’s name entered in the said electoral register appiicable to each electoral district and whose name 
has been omitted from the electoral register and from list “B” or whose name has been included in list “a” may 
claim by himself or by someone else on his behalf, as the case may be. substantially in form “a” and “B” set out 
in the schedule to above registration of electors act to have his or someone else’s name entered or retained in the 
register, and submit it to reach the registering officer of each electoral district on or before May 06, 2024, within 
4 weeks from the date of the publication of this notice in the Gazette of the democratic socialist republic of sri 
lanka i.e. from april 09. 2024.
 03. any person whose name appears in the electoral register or in list “B” for any electoral district may object to the 
entry of his own name or to the entry or retention of the name of any other person appearing in such register or list 
“B”. every such objection shall be made in duplicate and shall be substantially in form “C” set out in the schedule 
to the above Act and shall be made so as to reach the registering officer of the Election Office. All objections to 
the inclusion of the name in the register should be sent to the registering officer of the District Elections Office 
on or before May 06. 2024, within 4 weeks from the date of the publication of this notice in the Gazette of the 
democratic socialist republic of sri lanka i.e. from april 09, 2024.
 04. every objection to the entry of the name of any claimant in the electoral register shall be made in duplicate and 
shall be substantially in form “d” set out in the schedule to the above act and shall be made so as to reach the 
registering officer within 14 days from the date on which the notice containing the name of claimants is exhibited 
at the District Election Office. This notice will be published in the District Election Offices on April 25, 2024 and 
May 09, 2024.
