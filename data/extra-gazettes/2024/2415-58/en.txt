PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
It is hereby notified as per the schedule 1 of Industrial Promotion Act, No. 46 of 1990 which was published as supplement 
under Section II of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated 07th of December 1990, I appoint 
Mr. Yakupity Indika Manjula Silva who is carrying out the duties as the Additional Secretary (Industry & Industrial Estate 
Development) of the Ministry of Industry and Entrepreneurship Development as the Registrar of Industries for the relevant 
activities of Industrial Registration in terms of Section 27 under Sub-section (I) of the above Act.
