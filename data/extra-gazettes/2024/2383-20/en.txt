By virtue of the powers vested in me section 6(2)(b) and section 21(2)(b) of the town and Country Planning Ordinance 
(Chapter 269), I, Prasanna Ranathunga, minister of urban development and Housing do by this Order -
	 (a)	 declare	that	the	area	of	the	land	called	“Battaramulla	Batapotha	Sri	Sudarshanarama	Purana	Viharaya”	specified	in

Extraordinary No. 1929/9 dated 24th of August, 2015.
