THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of 
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial 
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 20.04.2021 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2224/51 dated 24.04.2021 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified 
in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour dated 09.04.2021.
     Mrs Dodampahala Hewage Milanka Upulani, "Tharindi",  Habaragoda Watta, Karagas Wagura, Kappetipola. 
 
 and
      State Timber Corporation, Rajamalwatta Road, Battaramulla
was referred to Mr. Janajitha De Silva Attorney - at - Law for settlement by arbitration. 
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, due to Mr. Janajith De Silva,  Attorney - at - Law, 
the Arbitrator informing that he is resigning from the post of Arbitrator, I, Manusha Nanayakkara, Minister of Labour and 
Foreign Employment do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said 
Order dated 20.04.2021.
 Manusha Nanayakkara,
 Minister of Labour and Foreign Employment.
Colombo.
08th August, 2024.

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mrs. Dodampahala Hewage Milanka Upulani working as a Store Keeper at the State Timber Corporation had been 
caused injustice by the value of shortage and surplus of timber, discoverd at the survey conducted by the Stock Survey Board 
being, deducted from her salary and if injustice had been caused, to what relief she is entitled.
 H. K. K. A. Jayasundara,
 Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo,
