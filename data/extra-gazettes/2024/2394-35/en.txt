PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE 
It is hereby notified that the scheduled dates for the annual Esala Festival and procession of Seenigama Sri Maha Devol 
Devalaya situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Seenigama, Divisional Secretary's Division of Madampagama of the 
District of Galle, Southern Province, published in the Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 2390/15 dated Thursday the 
27th of June, 2024, as per the Provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance, is hereby amended and will be held from Thursday 
the 29th of August, 2024 to Thursday the 05th of September, 2024.
   
 
W. A. DhArmAsiri,

 Administrative District, Galle.
 
at the District Secretariat, Galle,
