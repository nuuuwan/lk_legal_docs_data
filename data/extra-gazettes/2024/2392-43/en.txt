ThE  SOCIETIES  ORDINANCE
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 & 4 of the societies Ordinance (Chapter 123), I, dr. ramesh Pathirana, 
Minister of Ministry of Industries, do  by this notification ;
 01. authorize the purpose for which the society known as “dIwIyata sa vIyak PadaNama ” situated
