PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & c., by the President
No. 910/3 of 2024
 p.s. No. : ps/CsA/00/1/1
IT is hereby notified that the Honourable President, in terms of Article 43(4) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka, has appointed Honourable Harini Amarasuriya, Member of Parliament, to the Office of Prime 
Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with effect from September 24, 2024.
          By Order of the Honourable President,
 dr. N. S. Kumanayake,
 Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
September 25, 2024.
