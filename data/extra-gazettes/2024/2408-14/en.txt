PRADEShIYA  SAbhAS  AcT,  No. 15  Of  1987
Order
i, Harini amarasuriya, Prime minister and minister of Justice, Public administration, Home affairs, Provincial Councils, 
Local Government and Labour; in view of the elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha considered as a Pradeshiya Sabha, dissolved by 
order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2373/51 dated 01st of march 2024 in terms of Sub-section (4) of Section 
66b of the Local authorities elections ordinance (Chapter 262);
 (1) declare that the area indicated in Part i of Schedule mentioned in the order published in the Gazette Extraordinary  
No. 453/11 dated 12th of may 1987 shall be a Pradeshiya Sabha area for the purpose of the Pradeshiya Sabhas 
act, No. 15 of 1987.
 (2) order the limits indicated in Part ii of the said Schedule, as the administrative limits of the said Pradeshiya Sabha.
 (3) assign the following name to the said Pradeshiya Sabha after constituting a Pradeshiya Sabha for the area of 
Pradeshiya Sabha stated in paragraph (1) of this order.
“Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha”
 in terms of powers vested in me by Sub-section 2(1) and Sections 4 and 6 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas act, No. 15 of 1987.
