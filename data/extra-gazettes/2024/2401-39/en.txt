PRESIDENTIAL   ELECTIONS  ACT ,  No.  15  Of  1981
Notice of proceeding under Sub Section 22 (1) (b)
iN accordance with sub section 22(1)(b) of the presidential elections Act, No. 15 of 1981, we inform that mr. idroos mohamed 
illias, who was nominated on August 15, 2024, for the presidential election scheduled for september 21, 2024, and announced 
as a candidate in Gazette No. 2397/51 dated August 15, 2024, passed away on August 22, 2024; upon confirmation of his 
death by the election Commission, mr. K.m.N. sanjaya sampath reman, the nominee of the deceased candidate, was ordered 
under sub section 22(1) of the presidential elections Act, No. 15 of 1981 to nominate a replacement candidate within three 
days of the Order but failed to do so, resulting in the removal of the deceased candidate’s name from the ballot paper and all 
related election documents, and accordingly, measures will be taken by the election Commission under sections 107 and 
118 of the Act, with voting to proceed as specified in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2394/51 dated July 26, 2024.
