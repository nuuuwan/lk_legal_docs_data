NOTICE N o.  : 31/0426 MATALE DISTRICT      
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner 
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 310372 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Kotuwegedara South 
(Block 02) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama 
Niladhari Division of No.E 348 B - Kotuwegedara South in the 
Divisional Secretariat Division of Mathale in the District of 
Matale in the Province of Central Province should submit his 
claim to the undersigned before 20th of December 2024 In the 
event of failure  to submit any claim before the above -mentioned 
date, action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to determine 
title to the land and accordingly publish such determination in the 
Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 310372 referred to above may be 
perused at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional 
Secretariat, District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of 
Title Settlement, or the Surveyor - General’s office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama 
Niladhari, the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title 
Settlement.
