THE  COLOMBO  PORT  CITY   ECONOMIC  COMMISSION   ACT,  No.  11  OF  2021
Order  under  Section   53
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 53 of the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 
of 2021, i, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister of investment Promotion, having considered the recommendations of the 
Colombo Port City economic  Commission and upon the approval of the Cabinet of ministers, do by this order specify that-
 (a) the Cabinet of ministers, by its decision No. 24/1304/631/032 dated, July 09, 2024 has approved the designation 
of the business of the authorised person specified in  Schedule i hereto as a Secondary Business of Strategic 
importance;
 (b) the rationale for considering such business as a Secondary Business of Strategic  importance is as specified in 
Schedule ii hereto;
 (c) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments shall be as specified in Schedule iii hereto; and
 (d) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments specified shall commence and cease to be operative 
on the dates specified in Schedule iv hereto.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe, 
 minister of investment Promotion. 
Colombo, 
July 19, 2024.

SCHedule  ii
Rationale for the consideration of the Secondary Business of Strategic Importance  
the objectives of the Colombo Port City economic Commission, as stipulated in Section 5 of the Colombo Port City 
economic Commission act, No. 1 1 of 2021, have been considered in designating the business as a Secondary Business 
of Strategic importance, in order to ensure the success of establishing the Colombo Port City, having regard to the 
national interest or the advancement of the national economy as provided for in Section 53(5) of the said  act.
PaGero laNka SoftW are eNGiNeeriNG (Pvt) ltd is a company incorporated  in Sri lanka, and is a wholly 
owned subsidiary of Pagero aB, a company  incorporated  in Sweden. With an annual turnover of over uSd 50 million, 
Pagero aB operates as one of the largest B2B networks globally, connecting buyers and sellers across international 
markets. Pagero aB's platform enables the swift and secure digital exchange of critical documents such as orders, 
invoices and payments, empowering businesses to streamline their operations with ease. moreover, Pagero is dedicated 
to promoting environmental responsibility by reducing paper consumption and carbon emissions associated with 
traditional document exchange methods.
PaGero laNka SoftW are eNGiNeeriNG (Pvt) ltd registered as an offshore company in the Colombo Port 
City, is positioned to  be  an  extended  research and development centre for Pagero aB, poised to drive innovation 
in edocument/elnvoicing solutions for global partners. P aGero laNka SoftW are eNGiNeeriNG (Pvt)  ltd 
will recruit approximately 100 skilled software engineers within two years of commencing operations in the Colombo 
Port City and further employ 50  individuals  in  the second  year. With a proposed investment of uSd 100,000, further 
expansion plans forecasts an injection of approximately uSd 2 million through employee remunerations over the 
subsequent years, demonstrating  its commitment to fostering economic growth in Sri  lanka.
