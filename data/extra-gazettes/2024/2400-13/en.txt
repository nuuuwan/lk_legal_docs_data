Notice under  Section  7 
 
My Reference No.: MOR/LAN/01/F1/201. 
Tourism & Land Ministry No.: 4-3/4/2013/D/392.                            
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub–section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for location of Thelawala, Salusala Land for 
the Police Special Task Force. 
 
Description of the Lands to be acquired:  
 
Allotment of Land in extent of 0.7937 Hectare depicted in 
Sheet No. 09 of the block 01 of the Cadastral Map No. 
520211 dated 08.02.2024 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the village called Thelawala in the 
Grama Niladhari Divisions of Borupana in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Moratu wa in Colombo District of 
the Western Province described as follows.    
 
01. Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land : Lady Catherine Group, Borupana 
Road; Present Land Use : Garden, Bodhiya, three 
permanent buildings, five toilets and a water tank; 
Ownership : Cannot be found; Extent : 0.7937 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested  in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Moratuwa Divisional 
Secretariat, on 27th November 2024 at 10.00 a.m and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing ( in duplicate) 
on or before the 06th November 2024,  the nature of their 
interests  in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. All deeds and relevant 
documents is support of claim should be provided at the 
inquiry.   
 
 
 S. P. K
IRIWATHTHUDUWA,
