Regulations
the Foreign exchange (Investment in the International sovereign bonds issued by the Government of sri lanka)                
Regulations No. 6 of 2021 issued under the Foreign exchange act, No. 12 of 2017 published in the Gazette Extraordinary 
Notification  No. 2234/20 of 30 June 2021 is hereby repealed and replaced with the following; without prejudice to anything 
done thereunder:
“Where any borrower under Regulations No. 6 of 2021 has no sufficient foreign exchange balances/cash flows, 
repayment of the loans obtained from overseas for the purpose of investing in International sovereign bonds and  
sri lanka development bonds is permitted by way of conversion of sri lanka Rupees”.
