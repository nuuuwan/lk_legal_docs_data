WHEREAS  A N  industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour

     Free Trade Zone and General Services Employees Union, No. 141, Ananda Rajakaruna Mawatha, Colombo 10.

NOW THEREFORE  I, Dr. Harini Amarasuriya, Miniser of Justice, Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Council, 
Local Government and Labour do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 
131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts Nos. 14 of 1957 4 of 1962 and 39 
of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint.

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the employees of Alumex PLC had been caused injustice by the non-agreement of Alumex PLC to increase the 
salaries of employees of the below - mentioned worker categories by Rs. 7500/- from April, 2023 as requested by the free 
Trade Zone and General Services Employees' Union and if so, to what relief they are entitle .
1. Operations Assistant 4. Quality Controller
2. Production Supervisor 5. Store Keeper
3. Senior Production Supervisor 6. Office Assistant
 H. K. K. A. Jayasundara,
 Commissioner General of Labour.

Colombo,
