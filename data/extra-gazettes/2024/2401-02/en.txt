ThE  CuSTOm  ORDINANCE  
Order under Section 19A
by Virtue of the power vested in me under section 19(a) of the Customs ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended by the 
Customs (amendment) Law No. 35 of 1974 and the Customs (amendment) act, No. 83 of 1988, I, ranil Wickremesinghe, 
Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies do by this Order exempt the goods specified in  
Column V of the Schedule hereto, imported by or consigned to, person or institutions specified in the corresponding entry 
in Column II of that Schedule from payment of the Customs Import Duty, leviable thereon as specified in the corresponding 
entry in Column VIII of that schedule subject to such conditions.
However, any importer or person by whom or on whose behalf any goods, specified and imported under this Order 
are subsequently to be sold or disposed of contrary to the conditions specified, such importer or person shall obtain prior 
approval of the director General of Customs and pay the Customs Import duty payable on such goods at the time of sale or 
disposal to the director General of Customs in terms of section 19 (a) (3). any goods sold or disposed of in contravention 
of such section shall be liable to be forfeited.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe,
 minister of Finance, economic 
 stabilization and National policies.
ministry of Finance, economic 
stabilization and National policies,

august 06, 2024.
