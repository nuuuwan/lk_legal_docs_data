Local Government Notifications
Pradeshiya  sabha  Nawagaththegama 
IT is hereby notified for public information that it has been decided under the Decision No. 398 dated 07.12.2023 that the 
roads and thoroughfares for which legal actions have not been taken in a proper court before the date specified in the said 
notification, and all roads and thoroughfares which have been confirmed to be deemed to be owned by the Pradeshiya 
Sabha Nawagaththegama in accordance with the decree announced by that Court, from among the roads and thoroughfares 
considered to be owned by the Pradeshia Sabha Nawagaththegama in the Schedule set out in the Notification published 
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2342/43 dated 28.07.2023, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of 
“Orders with regard to the Preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabhas in the 
North Western Province and publication of advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North Western 
Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 dated 30th July 2021 and the orders made 
under Section 24 (1) of  Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
   It is further notified that a list of such roads and thoroughfares has been included in the Schedule hereto and every  
road and thoroughfares included therein shall be deemed as roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Nawagaththegama under sub-section (5) of Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act.
                             M. W. L. S
oMachandra ,
 Secretary and Officer who Executes Powers, 
 Duties and Functions
 Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama.

07th December, 2023.

Decision
   In accordance with the decision No. 398 dated  07.12.2023, I, W. M. L. somachandra, the secretary of the Pradeshiya sabha Nawagaththegama do 
hereby decide that the roads and thoroughfares among the roads and thoroughfares mentioned in the list set out in the Schedule of the Notification published in 
the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2342/43 dated 28.07.2023, for which a case has not be filed in the proper court and are included in the schedule below should 
be confirmed as the roads and thoroughfares owned by the Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama, by virtue of powers vested under the Orders in the series of 
“Orders with regard to the preparation of plans for roads and thoroughfares maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha in the North Western Province and publication of 
advertisements specifying the name list” by Hon. Governor in the North Western Province which has been published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2238/35 
dated 30th July 2021, and in terms of the order made under section 24 (1) of the Pradeshiya sabha act, No. 15 of 1987.
