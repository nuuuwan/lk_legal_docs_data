FILLING OF A V ACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13(B) OF ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE 
DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
WHEREAS, the Secretary General of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under Section 64(1) of the 
Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Ninth Parliament by reason 
of the dismissal by the Supreme Court of the petition No. SC/(EXPULSION) No. 02/2023 ﬁ led in the Supreme Court against 
the expulsion of Mr. Maligaspe Koralege Nalin Manusha Nanayakkara from the Samagi Jana Balawegaya party, who 
was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral District No. 07 – Galle under Section 62 of the aforesaid 
Act; 
 WHEREAS, the Returning Of ﬁ cer for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by the 
Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, to ﬁ ll this vacancy as provided 
for under Sub Article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution; 
 AND WHEREAS, the said Returning Of ﬁ cer acting under Section 64(2) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 
1981, and in terms of Sub Article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution has made a return in Form “M” set out in the First Schedule 
to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Bandula Lal Bandarigoda was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for 
the said electoral district;

reason of the dismissal by the Supreme Court of the petition No. SC/(EXPULSION) No. 02/2023 ﬁ led in the Supreme Court 
against the expulsion of Mr. Maligaspe Koralege Nalin Manusha Nanayakkara from the Samagi Jana Balawegaya party.
