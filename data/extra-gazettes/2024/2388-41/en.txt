THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I , Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Lands, described below 
are needed for the Construction of Kolabore water treatment 
plant under wellayaya water project phase II and will be 
acquired under the provisions of that Act.
 The allotments of Land depicted in below name land 
extent in the Advance Tracing No. MO/WLL/2021/605 dated 
31.03.2022 prepared by the Surveyor General Situated in 
the village called Heenapahuwa (F. V . P. 524) in the Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 152 of Kotikambokka, in Wellawaya 
Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division 
of Wellawaya in Monaragala District of the Uva province, 
bounded as Follows. 
 01. Lot 'A' – Mahayaya  – Extent:  0.7843 Hectare
 North: Mahayaya - H. G. Liyanage; East: Mahayaya - H. 
G. Liyanage and canal (dry); South: canal (dry) and Road 
(Pradeshiya Saba); West: Road (Pradeshiya Saba) and 
Mahayaya - H. G. Liyanage.
 02. Lot 'B' – Extent: 0.0315 Hectare
 North: Mahayaya - K. M. Upul Bandara; East: Mahayaya - 
K. M. Upul Bandara and Mahayaya - Rohana Wanniarachchi; 
South: Mahayaya - K. M. Upul Bandara and Mahayaya 
- Rohana Wanniarachchi; West: Mahayayya, K. M. Upul 
Bandara.
