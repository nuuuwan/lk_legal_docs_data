No. 2408/12 – WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER  30, 2024
HAPUTALE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Provide an opportunity for public consideration of the draft budget statement for the year 2025
PURSUANT to the powers available in Section 184 of the Provincial Council Act No. 15 of 1987 read with Section 02 of the 
Provincial Council (Subsidiary Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989 and Section 223 of the Regional Council Act No. 15 of 1987 on 
29.10.2020 through the Governor of Uva Province, Extraordinary Gazette No. 2199/25 of the “2020 Pradeshiya Sabha Budget 
Formulation and Implementation Act” promulgated by 10 (2)(b) the draft budget statement proposed to be presented by the 
Haputale Pradeshiya Sabha for the year 2025 will be submitted to the public consideration from 01.11.2024 to 05.11.2024. This 
paper hereby announces that an opportunity has been set up in the appropriate at Public Library Diyatalawa. 
 K. M. P. C. Kඎඅൺඌൾ඄ൺඋൺ,

 Haputale Pradeshiya Sabha.
