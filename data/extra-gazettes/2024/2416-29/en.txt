CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  N o. 09  OF  2003  (AMENDED ) 
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the 
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no Local Manufacturer, Packer, Distributor or Trader shall Sell, Expose or Offer for 
sale, Display for sale the types of canned fish listed below, above the Maximum Retail Price given here under.
