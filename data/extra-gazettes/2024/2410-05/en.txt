NOW THEREFORE I, Dr. Harini Amarasuriya, Minister of Justice, Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils, 
Local Government and Labour do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 
131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Act Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 
of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint of  Mr. Obadage Leelarathne, 
Attorney-at-Law of No. 242, Sri Sobitha Mawatha, Nagoda, Kalutara to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to 
him for settlement by arbitration.
  
 D
R. HARINI AMARASURIYA,
