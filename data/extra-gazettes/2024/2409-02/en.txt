ANIMAL  DISEASES  ACT,  N o.  59  OF  1992
ORDER  OF  DECLARATION  OF  INFECTED  AREA  UNDER  SECTION   5  AND 
CONSEQUENTIAL  ARRANGEMENTS
WHEREAS, African Swine Fever (AFS) and Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS) are Speciﬁ ed Diseases 
within the meaning of the Animal Diseases Act, No. 59 of 1992, 1st Schedule of the Act and the Order made thereunder and 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1867/8 dated 17.06.2014 has been reported to have occurred and susceptible to be 
occurred in every Divisional Secretary’s Division of the Island, I, Kasthuri Arachchige Chandrika Hemali Abeyrathna Kothalawala, 
the Director General of Animal Production and Health, do by virtue and in the exercise of powers vested in me under that Act 
hereby declare by this Order that –
 (1) Every Divisional Secretary’s Divisions of Sri Lanka to be an infected area or susceptible area for the purposes and 
within the meaning of that Act.
 (2) “Pig” ( sus scrofa) is the susceptible species of animal to this Disease; and 
 (3) This Order shall come into force on the date on which it i s published in the Gazette and shall unless earlier revoked or 
further extended in accordance with the Section 5(3) of that Act, cease to have eﬀ ect at the expiration of a period of 
three months from the date on which it is published in the Gazette.

 02. So long as this Order remains in force, I, the Director General of Animal Production and Health do -
 (1) Hereby prohibit the following activities within or in rela tion to the infected and susceptible area for the purpose of 
preventing the spread from and controlling and containing within, the infected area, of the said African Swine Fever 
(AFS) and Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS).
 I. Under Section 5 (4) of the Animal Diseases Act removal, tak ing or driving or transport pigs, pork, pork products 
or the risk material within, to or from the infected or susceptible area without a permit issued by the Authorized 
veterinary surgeon of respective division as per the format VII of the regulation published in the Gazette No.1108/23 
dated December 03, 1999,
 II. the slaughter of infected species of Animal for meat purpo se,
 III. the performance of artiﬁ  cial breeding procedures in the susceptible species of animals, 
 IV . holding or conducting Live Animal Market using susceptible  species of Animals,
 V . Sale, exposure for sale, oﬀ  ering for sale, store, supply or distribution of pigs, pork, pork products and infected 
materials and processing and further processing of pork, without a permit issued by an Authorized oﬃ  cer who 
is being nominated in this order,
 VI. throwing or otherwise placing in any water body, any carca ss or any part thereof of an animal that at the time 
of its death was suspected of being, aﬀ ected or contaminated with the African Swine Fever (AFS) and Porcine 
Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS) or any other risk material that is suspected or contaminated 
with the African Swine Fever (AFS); or Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS),
 VII. Placing any carcass or any part thereof, of an animal tha t at the time of its death was suspected of being, aﬀ ected 
or contaminated with the African Swine Fever (AFS), Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS), 
in such manner as to facilitate other animal to consume or taking away of those carcasses or any part thereof to 
other places.
 VIII. Slaughter of the pigs in slaughter houses which are not approved by an Authorized Oﬃ  cer who is being nominated 
in this order.     
 03. By virtue, and in the exercise of powers vested in me under Provisions of the Animal Diseases Act, nominate –
 (1) The Following Oﬃ   cers to be Authorized Oﬃ  cers for the purposes of Section 3 (3) of the Act to take action to prevent 
the commission of the prohibited activities set out under Paragraph 02 (1) of this Order and to exercise the powers of 
an Authorized Oﬃ  cer under Sections 13 and 14 the Animal Diseases Act for that purpose.
 (i) Director-Animal Health, Department of Animal Production an d Health.
 (ii) Director-Wild life Health, Department of Wild life and Co nservation.
 (iii) Chief   Municipality Veterinary Surgeons -Municipal Coun cils.
 (iv) Deputy Director - Animal Health, of the Department of Ani mal Production and Health.
 (v) The Provincial Director of Animal Production and Health of  each Province.
 (vi) Subject Matter Specialist of each Province.
 (vii) The Deputy Director of Animal Production and Health in charge of each District and District Veterinary Surgeons.
