No. 2365/76 –  SATURDAY, JANUARY 06,  2024 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) NO. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under  Section 7 
 
 
My No.: ATU/LND/04/DO43. 
Land Ministry’s No. : 4-3/24/2020/HA/102. 
 
I hereby give notice,  under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment)  Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends  to acquire following land which is required for a 
public purpose: 
 
Particulars of the Land/Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of Land described below depicted in 
Cadastral Plan No. 820483 prepared by Surveyor General 
situated at Athuraliya village in 314 D, Athuraliya West 
Grama Niladhari Division in Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Athuraliya in Matara District. 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Nugagaha Pittaniya; Particulars of 
the Land: Home Garden with Permanent Building & 
Lavatory ; Extent: 0.0342 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing  to 
appear before me at Athuraliya Divisional Secretariat on 
13th day of March 2024 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 06th day of March 2024 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 Nංඋඈඌඁൺ S. Gൺආൺ඀ൾ, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Athuraliya.
