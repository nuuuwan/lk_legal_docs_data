SPORTS  LAW  No. 25  OF  1973
Regulations made by the Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs under the Section 32 and 33 of the
Sports Law No. 25 of 1973
I, Harin Fernando, the minister of sports and youth affairs, temporarily suspend the registration of the National association/ 
Federation of sports for the below-mentioned sports by virtue of the power vested in me under the section 32 and 33 of 
the sports Law, No. 25 of 1973 and its amendments, and hereby appoint Professor (Rear admiral) shemal Fernando, the 
director General of the department of sports development, as the competent authority with effect from 29th may, 2024 
as a temporary mechanism to proceed with administrative and other required activities of the said National association/ 
Federation of sports and to call and conduct relevant elections as well as to perform the duties appropriately until then.
1. sri Lanka Cycling Federation
2. sri Lanka Rugby Federation
3. sri Lanka automobile Federation
4. sri Lanka Netball Federation
 
Harin Fernando (M.P.)
 minister of sports and youth affairs.
29th of may, 2024.
