WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL 
Criteria to be adopted when prescribing Service Charges for Sewerage Services maintained 
by the Colombo Municipal Council within its authority area
I hereby announce that as per the provisions prescribed to me under section 4 of the "Order relevant to prescribing charges 
on maintenance of sewerage service by colombo municipal council in the colombo municipal council authority area", 
which was prescribed under section 326 of the municipal councils Ordinance (chapter 252) read with section 2 of the 
provincial councils (consequential provisions) act, No. 12 of 1989 and which was published by the Extraordinary Gazette 
No. 2310/22 dated 14.12.2022 of the democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka, the criteria decided by me as applicable 
when prescribing charges for sewerage services maintained within the colombo municipal council authority area will be 
in included into the below mentioned schedule and published hereby.
 S. K. JayaSundara,

 03. service charge for sewerage shall be determined on the basis of water consumption of each premises and for that 
purpose, only the method of charging declared in the notification issued by the Minister in Charge of the Water Supply 
subject from time to time with amendments under section 84 of the National Water supply and drainage Board act 
No. 02 of 1974 shall be applied.
 04. the water consumption of each premises which will be required in charging service charges for sewerage shall be 
obtained from the National Water supply and drainage Board.
 05.  In the event several other alternative water resources are used other than the water supply of the National Water s upply 
and drainage Board an additional fee appropriately determined by the colombo municipal council shall be charged 
as a usage fee and such fee shall not exceed the usage fee charged by the National Water supply and drainage Board.

additional fee appropriately determined by the colombo municipal council shall be charged according to the cost that 
will be spent to bring the said connection to re-operative level and such charges shall not exceed the charges levied 
by the National Water supply and drainage Board for this task.
