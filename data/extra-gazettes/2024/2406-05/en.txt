AUTHORIZATION
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Acc ounting Oﬃ  cers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council 
to disburse as per the Fifth Supplementary Financial Statement for the year 2024, by virtue of powers vested in me under 
Section 27(a) of the Provincial Council Act No. 42 of 1987, as the term of the 06th Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ended on 26th September, 2017.
I do hereby grant permission to the Chief Secretary and the Acc ounting Oﬃ  cers of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council 
to spend a sum of Rupees Fifty Three Billion Three Hundred Million Nine Hundred and Forty Three Thousand Sixty Eight 
and Cents Eighty Two only (Rs. 53,300,943,068.82) out of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Fund, which is estimated 
for the expenses of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council for the  one year period that starts on 01st of January, 2024 and 
end on 31st of December, 2024.
This permission will be valid till 31st December, 2024 or upto the date that expires three months (03) from the date of 
summoning the Seventh (07th) Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council or from the date re-start the functions of the Sabaragamuwa 
Provincial Council which occurs ﬁ rst.
