IN terms of Section 18 of the Registration of Electors Act No. 44 of 1980, the main electoral register for the year 2024 
related to the revision of the electoral registers for the year 2024 was certified on 01 July 2024.
   02. Whereas the main electoral register 2024 remained in effect as on July 01, 2024, in pursuance of the powers 
vested in the Election Commission under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri 
Lanka, the Election Commission by this Order certify that the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the 
number of members which each electoral district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is 
entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation under Article 97 and Article 98.
