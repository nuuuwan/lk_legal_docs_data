FILLING OF A V ACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13(b) OF ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE 
DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
wHeReas, the secretary general of Parliament has informed the election Commission, under section 64(1) of the 
Parliamentary elections act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Ninth Parliament by 
reason of the resignation of Mrs. thalatha athukorala, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the electoral 
district No. 21 – Ratnapura under section 62 of the aforesaid act; 
 and Whereas, the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by the 
Election Commission under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, to fill this vacancy as provided 
for under sub article 99(13)(B)  of the Constitution; 
 And whereas, the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,                                    
No. 1 of 1981, and in terms of sub article 99(13)(B) of the Constitution has made a return in Form “M” set out in the First 
schedule to the aforesaid act, to the effect that Mr. Karunarathna Paranawithanage was declared elected as a Member 
of Parliament for the said electoral district;
