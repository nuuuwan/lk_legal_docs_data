FOREST  CONSERvATION  ORDINANCE 
the order under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act 
No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009. 
The Order 
By virtue of powers vested in me under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 
of 1907, as amended by act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and 
No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation do by this Order release the block of forest land 
of the extent 38.0 hectares and bounded by the limits set forth in the schedules 1, 2 & 3 given below, of the Kankamkulam 
reserved Forest which is of the extent of 957.17 hectares and was declared as a reserved Forest by Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 2257/59 dated 10th december 2021, from the reserved status from. 07th day of august, 2024. 
 
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi, 
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation. 
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation, 
No. 07, hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Colombo 07,
07th day of august. 2024.
