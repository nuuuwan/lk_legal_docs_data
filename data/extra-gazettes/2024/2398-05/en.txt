AYAGAMA   PRADESHIYA   SHBHA
AS per the function assigned to the Local Councils in Sabaragamuwa Province from “the order of the preparation of plans of 
roads  and narrow lanes maintained by Pradeshiya Sabha in Sabaragamuwa Province and publication of notices showing the list 
of names” in Extraordinary Gazette No. 2316/20 and dated 24th January 2022 published by the Governor of Sabaragamuwa 
Province which should be read with Section 24 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, it is hereby notice to the public that the 
roads and narrow lanes included in the schedule below should be declared as the roads and narrow lanes owned by the Ayagama 
Pradeshiya Sabha at its meeting under the decision No. 03 and dated 20.06.2024. 
 And it is further notice that if it is the opinion of the party who is entitled to the said land, that any road or narrow lane included 
in the said schedule go through, a case should be ﬁ  led in the Court before the expiry of a period of three months from the date 
of this notice to prove the entitlement.
 And it is further noticed that every road and narrow lane included in the said Schedule that cases are not assigned before the 
expiry of the said period of three months, are considered as a road or norrow lane that owned by Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
