NOW THEREFORE  I, Manusha Nanayakkara, Minister of Labour and Foreign Employment do by virtue of the powers vested 
in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised 
Edition), as amended by Act, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes-Special Provisions)
