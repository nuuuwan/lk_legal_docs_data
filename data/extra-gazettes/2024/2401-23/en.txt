Proclamations & C., by the President 
Proclamation  by  the  President
 
Ranil Wickremesinghe,
  
I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, in terms of the powers vested in me 
under Article 33(h) read with Article 34 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby grant 
a Presidential Pardon posthumously to Mr. Diyunuge Edward Henry Pedris, who was unlawfully executed on 7th July 1915 
after being unjustly tried by a Court-Martial appointed by the Proclamation issued by Robert Chalmers, the Governor of 
Ceylon on 25th June 1915.
 Given at Colombo on the 12th day of September, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
 Ranil Wickremesinghe,  
 president.
