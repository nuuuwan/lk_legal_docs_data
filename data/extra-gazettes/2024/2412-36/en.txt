THE UnivERSiTiES acT, no. 16 of  1978
order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Harini Amarasuriya, 
Minister of Education, Science and Technology, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this 
Order, amend the schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1469/38 of November 03, 2006 (as amended 
from time to time) which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which is 
deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of 
the said Act, by the substitution for the item "6. Department of Community Medicine and Family Medicine" appearing in 
Column II to the schedule which corresponds to the item "Faculty of Medicine" (specified in the Order published in Gazette 
Extraordinary No.2163/22 of February 19, 2020) appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the following entry:-
  "6. Department of Public Health and Family Medicine"
 
Harini Amarasuriya,
 Minister of Education, Science and Technology.
