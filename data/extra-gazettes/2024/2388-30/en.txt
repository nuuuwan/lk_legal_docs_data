NOTICE  
Amalgamation of Colombo Commercial Fertilizers Limited (PB  1631) with Ceylon Fetilizer Company 
Limited (PB  499)
the Board of directors of the above companies have made a proposal for the amalgamation of the companies pursuant 
to section 242(1) of the Companies act, No. 07 of 2007, and the said companies are amalgamated into single entity. 
the amalgamated company will operate as “Ceylon Fertilizer Company Limited ”. the respective amalgamation will be 
effective from 01st July, 2024.
By Order of the Board of directors of Ceylon Fertilizer Company Limited and Colombo Commercial Fertilizers 
Limited,
 A. M. C. S. Thilakarathne, 
 Company secretary.
