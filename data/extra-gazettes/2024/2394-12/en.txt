THE  COLOMBO  PORT  CITY   ECONOMIC  COMMISSION   ACT,  No.  11  OF  2021
Order  under  Section   53
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 53 of the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 
of 2021, i, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister of investment Promotion, having considered the recommendations of the 
Colombo Port City economic  Commission and upon the approval of the Cabinet of ministers, do by this order specify that-
 (a) the Cabinet of ministers, by its decision No. 24/1304/631/032 dated, July 09, 2024 has approved the designation 
of the business of the authorised person specified in  Schedule i hereto as a Secondary Business of Strategic 
importance;
 (b) the rationale for considering such business as a Secondary Business of Strategic  importance is as specified in 
Schedule ii hereto;
 (c) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments shall be as specified in Schedule iii hereto; and
 (d) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments specified shall commence and cease to be operative 
on the dates specified in Schedule iv hereto.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe, 
 minister of investment Promotion. 
Colombo, 
July 19, 2024.

SCHedule  ii
Rationale for the consideration of the Secondary Business of Strategic Importance 
the objectives  of the  Colombo  Port City economic  Commission,  as stipulated  in  Section 5 of the Colombo  
Port  City  economic  Commission  act, No.  11 of 2021, have  been  considered  in designating the business as a 
Secondary Business of Strategic importance , in order to ensure the success  of  establishing  the  Colombo  Port  City,  
having  regard  to  the  national  intere st  or  the advancement of the national economy as provided  for in Section 
53(5) of the said act.
HexaW are teCHNoloGieS Sl (Priv ate) limited, a company incorporated in Sri lanka, is a wholly owned 
subsidiary of Hexaware technologies limited, a global it and BPo services company with an annual global revenue 
of over uSd 50 million, incorporated in india. leveraging its global presence with offices in the United Staes, 
Canada, europe, Singapore and australia , Hexaware serves a diverse clientele across industries such as banking and 
financial services, healthcare, insurance, travel and manufacturing.
HexaW are teCHNoloGieS Sl (Priv ate) limited's strategic focus within the Colombo Port City centers 
on exporting it services and solutions to the united States of america and europe, targeting sectors including travel 
and transportation, hospitality , logistics, and banking and financial services. the company will offer a comprehensive 
suite of services includin g application development and management, enterprise resource planning solutions, 
infrastructure management, business intelligence and analytics, and digital assurance and testing. With a planned 
investment of uSd 50,000, Hexaware Sl  aligns with Sri lanka's national goals by fostering talent development and 
innovation through employee it training programs, thereby contributing to human capital development and social 
inclusion.
Hexaware Technologies SL projects the hiring of 90 individuals in the first year, followed  by 50 in the second 
year. Hiring is expected to increase to 90 in year three, with a projected 145 individuals being employed in year 
four. With an additional 225 in the fifth year,  the cumulative total at the end of year five will be 600 employees. 
Hexaware technologies Sl is projecting a business volume of uSd 75-100 million within five years, Hexaware 
technol ogies Sl also incorporates green it initiativ es such as energy-efficient techno logies and renewable energy 
solutions, adhering to the Colombo Port City's sustainable principles.
