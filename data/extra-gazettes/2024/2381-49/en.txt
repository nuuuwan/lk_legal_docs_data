REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  ACT,  No.  44  OF  1980
iN the exercise of the powers vested by Section 8 of the registration of Electors act, No. 44 of 1980, the Election Commission 
does hereby cancel the Gazette No. 1967/3 dated 16.05.2016 and the Gazette No. 2035/20 dated 06.09.2017 issued by the 
Election Commission and appoint for the Electoral Districts specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, the Registering 
Officers and Assistant Registering Officers mentioned by their designations in the corresponding entry in Column II and III 
respectively.
