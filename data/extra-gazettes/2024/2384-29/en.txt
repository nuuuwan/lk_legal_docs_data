ThE  uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section  27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 27(1)(b) read with section 25a of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, 
1, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the minister of Education having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid 
act, in respect of sri Lanka International Buddhist academy (sIBa) a company duly incorporated in sri Lanka under 
the Companies act. No.7 of 2007 and bearing registration Number Ga  2232, and located at Pallekele, Kundasale being 
recognized as a degree awarding institute, do by this Order, amend the Order made under the aforesaid act  and published 
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1979/21 of august 09, 2016, by the repeal of the contents under the heading schedule and the 
substitution there for of the following:-
sChEduLE I
Conditions
1.	 Sri	Lanka	International	Buddhist	Academy	(SIBA)	shall,	when	admitting	students	for	the	degree	program	specified	
in schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of technology Information technology and Communication with a 
minimum	of	90	credits	(SLQF	5)	adhere	to	the	minimum	entry	qualification	of	at	least	three	(3)	simple	passes	(S)	in	
any stream in one and the same sitting at the GCE (advanced Level) Examination conducted by the department of 
Examinations	of	Sri	Lanka	or	an	equivalent	qualification.
