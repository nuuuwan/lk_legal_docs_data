THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
My No. : WGSWD&EIP/ACQ/KB/33. 
Tourism & Lands Min's Ref No.: 4/3/10/2016/LRB/67.                                
 
I hereby  give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28  of 1964, that the 
State has acquired the land located adjacent to 
Karadiyana Waste Recycling Centre and Weras Ganga, to 
maintain a water retention area and resettle the persons 
those who were displaced due to this project under Weras 
Ganga Storm Water Drainage and Environment 
Improvement project. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired : 
 
The below mentioned allotment of land,  depicted in 
below lot numbers in Sheet No. 05, Block No. 16 of the 
Cadastral Map No. 521203 dated 07.04.2021 prepared by 
the Surveyor General situated in the villages called 
Thumbowila in the Grama Niladhari Divisions No. 576 A 
of Tumbowila North in th e Divisional Secretary's 
Division of Kesbewa in Colo mbo District of the Western 
Province and bounded as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 196 
Name of the land: Dunuhinga Kumbura, Karadiyana 
Kumbura and Maaweekotuwa; Description : Home 
garden; Claimant: State; Orig inal Claimant: Mrs. Sarika 
Alwis Weerakkodi, Mrs. Ruwani Dilhari Alwis 
Weerakkodi, Mrs. Chamari Udayanginee Alwis 
Weerakkodi, No. 82,Elhena Road, Maharagama; Extent : 
0.3160 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ servitude on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Land Acquisition Office located in 
the Head Office of the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and 
Development Corporation, No. 03,  Sri Jayawardenapura 
Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on 23rd October 2024  
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th October 2024, 
the nature, of their interests in the land/lands, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
