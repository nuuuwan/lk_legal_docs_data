STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. R. P. P. Jayakody who is working in the Employees’ Trust Fund Board and has been reinstated in employment 
with pay for his Period of suspension (from 07.06.2006 to 04.01.2017) has been caused injustice by,
- not receiving bonuses and incentives if entitled,
- not being promoted if entitled , and
- not getting other entitlements and privileges, if any
for the respective period and if such injustice has been caused, to what relief he is entitled.
