PROCLAmATION
Anura Kumara Dissanayake
WHereAs, pursuant to an election held for the election of a president in terms of paragraph (3) of Article 31 of the 
Constitution of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, Honourable Anura Kumara dissanayake was declared 
elected as the president in terms of section 61 of the presidential elections Act, No. 15 of 1981 by the election Commission, 
on the t wenty second day of september, t wo thousand and t wenty Four.
And whereas, in terms of Paragraph (4) of Article 31 of the said Constitution, the term of Office of the President 
commenced on the t wenty second day of september, t wo thousand and t wenty Four; 
Now therefore, this proclamation gives notice of the fact that, in terms of Article 32 and paragraph (7) of Article 157A 
of the said Constitution,  Honourable Anura Kumara Dissanayake has, by making and subscribing the affirmation set out 
in the Fourth Schedule and the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, assumed the Office of President of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka for a term of five years, on the Twenty Third Day of September, Two Thousand and Twenty 
Four.
Given at Colombo on this t wenty third day of september, t wo thousand and t wenty Four.
by order of the Honourable president,
 dr. N. s. Kumanayake,
     secretary to the president.
