Pൺඋඍ IV (A) – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF S RI LANKA  – 17.05.2024
RESOLUTION
I hereby propose to levy an entertainment tax of 7.5% from 01st  January 2024 on the face value of admission tickets for 
entertainment activities and for every chargeable activity ment ioned in the following schedule. According to section 2 (1) 
of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No, 12 of 1946 and the Entertainment Tax Amendment Act No. 37 of 1984, for levy of 
entertainment tax in Kandy Municipal Corporation area, as per the powers vested in me by the Municipal Council Ordinance 
No. 16 of 1947.
   Furthermore, I hereby announce that this percentage shall be  considered as the entertainment tax percentage for all 
activities mentioned in this schedule and for every chargeable activity, until a new order is made in this regard.
