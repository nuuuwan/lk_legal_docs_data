sCHedule II
Rationale for the consideration of the business of Strategic Importance
the objectives of the Colombo Port City economic Commission, as stipulated in section 5 of the Colombo Port City 
economic Commission act, No. 11 of 2021, have been considered in designating the business as a business of strategic 
Importance, in order to ensure the success of establishing the Colombo Port City, having regard to the national interest or 
the advancement of the national economy as provided for in section 53(5) of the said act.
asiri Hospital Holdings PlC, a leading healthcare service provider, presently has 6 hospitals in sri lanka with a capacity 
of over 780 beds. asiri Hospital Holdings PlC will partner with Mayo Foundation for Medical education and research, 
usa  for the development of the Colombo Port City Hospital project with a foreign direct investment of over usd 100 
million.
Construction of the asiri Port City Hospital is scheduled to be completed within three years, with operations expected to 
commence from year four. the generation of employment at asiri Port City Hospital for approximately 700 personnel will 
result in income earning opportunities in designated foreign currencies.
asiri Port City Hospital will create a state-of-the art medical facility, consisting of 500 beds and new treatments and 
surgical procedures such as organ transplant surgery, cosmetic surgery, sports medicine and an advanced cancer treatment 
center. asiri Port City Hospital intends to transform Colombo to a medical tourism hub in south-east asia, and create the 
platform for local doctors to be trained and work with foreign medical specialists adopting global best medical practices.
