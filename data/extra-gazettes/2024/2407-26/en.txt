No. 2407/26 – THURSDAY, OCTOBER  24, 2024
RULE MADE UNDER SECTION  45 (CHAPTER  190) OF THE POST OFFICE ORDINANCE
Money Orders issued with eﬀ ect from 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2021
 01. A Money Order is valid for six months from the last day of the month of its issue. Under exceptional circumstances, the 
payment of a lapsed Money Order is considered up to two years from the last day of the month of its issue. However, a 
Money Order cannot be revalidated or refunded after lapsing of two years from the last day of the month of its issue and 
the value of such Money Order is credited to Government Revenue.
 02. However, under the following exceptional circumstance, the value of a Money Order lapsed two years could be considered 
for refund from Government Revenue subject to the recovery of relevant charges.
 (I) Money Order being produced in a court of law.
 (II) The Money Order being retained in a Rent Control Board on  legal grounds.
 (III) Payee of the Money Order being out of the country. (The passport should be produced.)
 (IV) Payee being in a very bad state of health for a long peri od. (Should be supported by a Medical Certiﬁ cate from a 
Government Medical Oﬃ  cer.)
