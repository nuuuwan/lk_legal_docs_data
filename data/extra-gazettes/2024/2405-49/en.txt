PARLIAmENTARY   ELECTIONS   ACT,  No.  1  Of  1981
Notice under Section 24 (1) (a), (c) and 33
Notice   is hereby given under Sections 24 (i) (a) and (c) and Section 33 of the Parliamentary elections act, No. 01 of 
1981 that - 
i. a contest will be held for the election of Members of Parliament for each electoral district mentioned in the Schedule hereto;and
