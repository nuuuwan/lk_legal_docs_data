Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance

WHEREAS the office of member of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which Widisingha 
Garudasa the member belonged, was required by me under Section 66A(1)(a) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the  
member of that No. 12 Wallambagala of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party  who shall be declared elected 
as a member within a period of thirty (30) days;
 And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Malwaththage Dilan Kapila  to fill such vacancy; 
Therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, 
acting in terms of Section 66A(1)(a)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) do hereby declare that 
Malwaththage Dilan Kapila was elected as  member of the No. 12 Wallambagala of Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
