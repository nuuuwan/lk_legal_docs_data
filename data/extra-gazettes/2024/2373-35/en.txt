EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of Land in Extent  of about 0.0196 Hectare, 
depicted in Cadastral Plan No. 420206, Zonal No. 07 and 
Sheet No. 02 dated 24.10.2023 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the village of Palle Gattuwana in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of No. 717, Palle Gattuwana in 
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Mallawapitiya in 
Kurunegala District in North - Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Weli Kumbura; Description: Open 
Waste Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: 
Ranathunga Arachchilage Irangani. (Acquired under 
Section 38(A) of Land Acquisition Act., To Finalise the 
land acquisition); Extent: 0.0196 Hectare. 
North: Palle Gattuwana (A part); East: Palle 
Gattuwana (A part); South: Palle Gattuwana (A part); 
West: Lot No. 38 in Preliminary Plan No. Ku. 2478. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear  personally or by agent duly authorized 
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of 
Mallawapitiya on the 29.04.2024 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notified to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 22.04.2024, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
