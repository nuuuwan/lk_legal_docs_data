Proclamation by the Hon. President
 
Ranil Wickremesinghe,
  
KNOW YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, President, do by this Proclamation, prorogue Parliament with effect from midnight 
of the Twenty Sixth day of January, Two Thousand and Twenty Four and hereby fix the Seventh day of February, Two 
Thousand and Twenty Four, at 10.30 a.m. for the commencement of the next session and summon Parliament to meet in the 
Chamber of Parliament, Sri Jayewardenepura.
 Given at Colombo on this 26
th day of January, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
      By Command of the Hon. President,
 E. M. S. B. Ekanayake,  
 Secretary to the President.
