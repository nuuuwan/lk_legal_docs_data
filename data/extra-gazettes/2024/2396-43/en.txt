FOREST CONSERV ATION ORDINANCE 
THE order under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by Act 
No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009. 
The Order 
 By virtue of the powers vested in me under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) 
No. 16 of 1907, as amended by Act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 
1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation do by this Order release the block of 
forest lands of the extent of 155.3 hectares and bounded by the limits set forth in the Schedules 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 & 1 0 
given below, of the Niliyamote 01 Reserved Forest of the extent of 1,199.1 hectares and was declared as a Reserved Forest 
by Gazette Extraordinary No. 2257/61 dated 10th December 2021, from the reserved status from 07th day of August, 2024.
 
 P
A VITHRA WANNIARACHCHI, 
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation. 
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation, 
No. 07, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Colombo 07,
07th day of August. 2024.
