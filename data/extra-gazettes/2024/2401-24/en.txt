Schedule
The Part II of the Wages Board for the Tea Growing and Manufacturing Trade set out in the schedule to the notification 
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 9441 of 31st July 1945 as varied from time to time and last varied by the notification

                The Minimum rate of daily wage for time work
i. Minimum Daily Wage 
(This amount includes the daily budgetary relief allowance and 
should be applied for payment of Employees' Provident Fund and 
Employees' Trust Fund)
- Rs. 1350.00
ii. For each kilogram plucked above the Norm - rs. 50.00
Wages Board decided to implement this decision for three years commencing from 10th September, 2024.
