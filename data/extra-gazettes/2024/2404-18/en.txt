REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS
Notice  that  the  Supplementary  Electoral  Register  2024(2)  has  been certified  under  the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
IN terms of section 20 a (10) of the registration of electors act, notice is given on behalf of the registering Officer of each 
electoral district that the supplementary electoral register 2024(2) including the names of the registered electors, who has 
attained the age of 18 years on a specified date within the period from 01st June 2024 eptember 2024 has been certified on 
01st October 2024 in accordance with the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of 
electors (amendment) act, No. 22 of 2021; that the electoral register for the  electoral districts mentioned in column I of the 
schedule below has been certified by the assistant registering Officer in the corresponding entry in column II thereof; that 
inspection may be made during normal office hours at the district election Office/offices mentioned in the corresponding 
entry in column III thereof.
 Saman Sri Ratnayake,
 commissioner General of 
 elections,
 election commission.
On 01st October, 2024,
