                         My Ref. No.: 3/2/3/10/269 - A (II). 
      Ministry of Lands and Tourism Ref. No. :  
4-3/9/2016/WS/417.  
                                            
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land, which 
is required for a public purpose : 
 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired : 
 
An allotment of land extended 3.9975 Hectares, 
mentioned in the Preliminary Survey plan bearing No. 
GAM. 4229 dated 26.01.2021, prepared by Senior 
Superintendent of Surveys, Gampaha instead of Surveyor 
General, situated in the Village of Welikadamulla in 
Walikadamulla Grama Niladhari Division, No. 363, in 
Attanagalla Divisional Secretary’s Division in Gampaha 
District, Western Province. 
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk 
(Published by Authority) 
 
PART III  — LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
1A - G 041449 - 49 (02/2024)  
N.B.— Since, it has been decided to redemption the plot 
of land called Lot No. 14 in the Preliminary Survey plan 
bearing No. GAM. 4229, under the Section No. 39 (a), 
0.9314 Hectares has been deducted out of the whole 
extend of 4.9289 Hectares of the plan. 
 
All persons who entitled or claim ownership for the 
aforesaid land/servitude are required to appear personally or 
by agent duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat  Attanagalla on 22.03.2024 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Further hereby requested to notify in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 15.03.2024, that the nature of the 
ownership of the said land/lands, reason of the claim, 
compensation for the acquisition of said land/servitude, the 
amount of compensation and the method of the 
computation of such amount. 
                                 
 
 S. P. Gunawardhana, 
 Divisional Secretary/Land  
 Acquisition Officer,         
 Attanagalla.
