PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE 
IN terms of the regulations published in the Gazette of democratic socialist Republic of sri Lanka No. 1620 dated 18th of 
September 2009 under the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance, it is hereby notified that Annual Religious Festival 2024 
of Rotumba Pethmaga ancient temple (Rotumba Purana Rajamaha Viharaya) of Pasgoda divisional secretariat, matara 
district, southern Province will be held from 27th day of august to 02nd day of september 2024.
