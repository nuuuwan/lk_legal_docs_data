No. 2396/35 –  THURSDAY, AUGUST 08,  2024 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, AS AMENDED 
BY THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) 
ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: ARA/LND/ACQVI/D3/52 
Ministry of Tourism and Lands No.: 4-3/15/2018/VE/08.  
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land / servitude which is required 
for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land is called Nikatenna waththa about 
0.0202 hectares in extent is depicted as Lot 01, in 
preliminary plan Number P.P.K 3742 and dated 
17/12/2019 made by the Senior Survey Superintendent 
and situated in the Village of Walimanna, in the Grama 
Niladari Division of No. 43 B, Gataberikantha, in the 
Minor Division of Galbadakorale (West), in the 
Divisional Secretariat Division of Aranayaka, in the 
District of Kegalle, in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
Boundaries are as follows. 
 
Lot No. 01 - Name of the land: Nikathanna 
waththa; Other Details: Land has been acquired 
under Section 38 (A) under provision of Sub section. 
Preliminary claimant is not identified and for the 
completion of land acquisition activities; Extent: 
0.0202 Hectare. 
 
North: Road (Pradeshiya Shaba) and Nikatenna 
waththa; East: Nikathanna waththa and Buwalikada 
waththa; South: Road and Buwalikada waththa and 
Nikathanna waththa; West: Nikathenna waththa and 
Road. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land / lands on 
which a servitude or any other relevance are to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at the 
Divisional Secretariat Aranayaka, 11th day of September 
2024 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Further they should notify me in writing (in duplicate) on 
or before the 04th day of September 2024 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and details of the computation 
such amount.  
 Shamali Piyathissa, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Aranayake.  
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Aranayake.  
02nd of  August 2024.
