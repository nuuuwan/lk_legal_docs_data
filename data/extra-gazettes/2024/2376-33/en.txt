PILGRImAGES   ORDINANCE
39th Ordination Ceremony (Upasampada Ceremony) of Sri Rohana maha Sanga Sabha of  
 maha Vihara Wansika Syamopali  maha Nikaya 
iN terms of regulations mentioned in the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (chapter 175),  L. d. B 11/60 and dated 
14th October 1991, it has been published that the 39th Ordination ceremony (Upasampada ceremony) of Sri rohana maha 
Sanga Sabha of Kamburupitiya divisional Secretary’s division in matara district will be held from 31st of may and 1st, 2nd  
of June, 2024 at the “Seema malaka” of rohana Sanga Sabha headquarters of Urapola Siri rathanajothi Piriven Viharaya 
and the devotees are requested not to bring plastic and Polythene based products. and also it is hereby informed that the 
standing orders and regulations published by the Government Gazette of democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka shall 
be valid within this time period of the Ordination ceremony (Upasampada ceremony).
 Chintha P. W.  Wanniarachchi,
 divisional Secretary,
