PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE  
It is hereby notified that the annual Esala Festival of Historic Welle Dewalaya in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Yaddehimulla, Unawatuna, Divisional Secretary's Division of Habaraduwa, District of Galle, Southern Province, will be 
held during the period from Saturday the 20th of July 2024 to Friday the 26th of July 2024 as per the order made under the 
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No.2125/24 dated 28.05.2019 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms 
of the regulations of the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Cap.175).
   
 W. A. Dharmasiri,
 District Secretary/Government Agent,
 Administrative District, Galle.
 
at the District Secretariat, Galle,
18th June, 2024.
