THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: MUL/LND/02/ACQ/24. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2013/D/520.  
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, As Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands/
Servitudes which are required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land/servitude sought to be 
acquired:- 
 
1.8820 hectares from the total land depicted as Lot No. 1, 
2 and 3 in Paper No. 1, Zone No. 04 of 27.12.2023 dated 
Plan No. C.P. 820414 prepared by Surveyor General and 
situated in the village of Mulatiyana and Neralampitiya in 
the Neralampitiya Grama Niladhari Division in 
Mulatiyana Divisional Secretar y's Division in Matara 
District, Southern Province. 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Diddenipotha Watta Galkaduwa 
Part; Description of the Land: A garden, permanent 
building, toilet and scrub with tea and cinnamon; 
Claimant: Maturata Plantation Ltd; Extent: 1.7235 
Hectare. 
Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Dangalagewatta; Description of the 
Land: Chena Mixed cultivation with tea and cinnamon; 
Claimant: J. J. Abeygunawardhana; Extent: 0.1376 
Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 03 
Name of the Land: Dangalagewatta; Description of the 
Land: Scrub; Claimant: J. J. Abeygunawardhana; Extent: 
0.0209 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Mulatiyana 
on the 05th day of June, 2024 at 10.00 a.m., and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 27th day of May, 2024 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
Servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
