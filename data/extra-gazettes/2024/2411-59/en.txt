uNIvERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  Of  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)  
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 20(4)(b)  of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Harini amarasuriya, 
Minister of Education, Higher Education and V ocational Education on being satisfied that the administration of  the 
university of ruhuna have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said university have failed to restore 
normal conditions, do by this order, appoint senior Prof. r. m. u. s. K. rathnayaka to be the Competent authority with 
effect from 25th November, 2024 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu  of  vice-Chancellor of 
the university of  ruhuna, any power, duty or function which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said 
vice-Chancellor under the said act, or under any order, ordinance, statute, by-law, regulation or rule made thereunder.
 H
arini amarasuriya,

 vocational education.
