 9.3.5 Disciplinary Committee Report
 The disciplinary Committee Report shall have the following:
 (a) The schedule of dates and times appointed for the hearing of the charges by the disciplinary Committee
 (b) summary of evidence of each witness including the evidence of the complainant.
 (c) Final determination of the disciplinary Committee and recommendation, if any.
 9.4 Determination by the Council
	 The	Council	upon	receipt	of	the	Report	of	the	Disciplinary	Committee	shall	act	as	specified	below:
 (1) if the disciplinary Committee has found the Respondent guilty of all or any of the charges made against 
him, shall make order as provided for in sub-section (3) of section 8 of the law No. 1 of 1976 and as 
amended.
 (2) if the disciplinary Committee has found the Respondent not guilty of all charges made against him, an 
order shall be made that the Respondent is exonerated from such charge or charges.
 (3) The decision of the Council shall be communicated to the Respondent with a copy to the Complainant.
