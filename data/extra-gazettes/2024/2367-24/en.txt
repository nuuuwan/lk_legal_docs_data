EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
My Reference No.: GCF/ACQ/DL/40/LR. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: LD/05/2005/LRB/263.  
 
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of  1964, 
that the Government has acquired the below mentioned 
land to develop the drainage system at Aththidiya Wewa 
road under Greater Colombo Flood Control and 
Environment Improvement Project: 
 
  Desription of  the land to be acquired:   
 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of 0.0147 hectare 
depicted as lot number 01 in the preliminary plan  
no. 9366 dated 04.07.2013 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in Village called Aththidiya in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Aththidiya in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Dehiwala in Colombo District of 
the Western province and bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Aththidiya Paddy field; 
Description: Cement drain; Claimant: State; Extent: 
0.0147 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Land Acquisition Office located in 
the Head Office of the Sr i Lanka Land Development 
Corporation, No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha, 
Welikada, Rajagiriya on 13.03.2024 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 06.03.2024 the nature of their interests in 
the land/lands, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
