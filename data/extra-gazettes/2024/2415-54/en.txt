GOVERNMENT NOTIFICATIONS
PUBLIC  DEBT  MANAGEMENT  ACT No. 33 OF 2024
Order under Section 32
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32 of the Public Debt Management Act, No. 33 of 2024, read with paragraph 
(3) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, President Anura Kumara Dissanayake, 
do by this Order, delegate to the Secretary to the Treasury the powers speciﬁ ed in Column I of the Schedule hereto, as provided 
for in the Sections listed in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
ANURA KUMARA DISSANAYAKE,
        President.
Colombo, 
19th December, 2024.
