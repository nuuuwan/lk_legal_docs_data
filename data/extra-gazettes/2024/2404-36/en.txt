ThE  ANTIquITIES   ORDINANCE   (ChAPTER  188)
Order under Section 33
by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 33 of the antiquities ordinance (chapter 188) I, delath Thusitha Mendis, 
the Director-General of Archaeology, with the approval of the Land Commissioner General, do by this Notification declare 
that the area of State land specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an “archaeological reserve” for the purpose of the 
aforesaid ordinance.
 Delath Thusitha Mendis, 
 director - General of archaeology.
colombo, 
17th september, 2024.
