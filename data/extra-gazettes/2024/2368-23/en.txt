No. 2368/23 –  WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 24,  2024 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No. BD/LG/LND/2/9/4. 
Land Ministry No. 4-3/8/2016/PC/205.  
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for BD/Millabadda Vidyalaya 
and will be acquired under the Provisions of the Act. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Lot No. 1 - Hopton Estate - Extent: 0.4535 Hectares. 
 
  North: Part of Millabadda (Hopton Estate Claimed by 
Land Reform Commission Road Hopton Estate Claimed 
by M. Yoganathan); East: Part of Millabadda (M. 
Yoganathan and D. M. Karunarathna Claimed Hopton 
Estate; South: Part of Millabadda (Hopton Estate Claimed 
by M. Yoganathan and D. M. Karunarathna Lot No. 11.1 
and Lot No. 2 of M.P.B. 686); West: Lot No. 11.1 of 
0.686 Council 2 Lot No. 2 of M.P. 2031 and part 2 of 
Millabadda (Hopton Estate Claimed by Land Reforms 
Commission). 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear  personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Lunugala 
Divisional Secretariat on the 25th day of March 2024 at 
9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of March 2024, 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.                                                  
  
 Nirosha Chandhani Hennayake,        
 Divisional Secretary,  
 Lunugala.  
 
Divisional Secretariat Lunugala. 
22nd January, 2024.
