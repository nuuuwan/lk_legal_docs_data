Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the “Imports and Exports (Control) Regulations No. 07 of 2024”.
 
2. These regulations are issued as per the decision of the Cabinet of ministers dated april 01, 2024, on the memorandum 
No. 24/0644/615/003-i dated april 01, 2024, of the minister of Tourism and Lands.

8. director General of Sri Lanka Customs shall report to the Secretary to the Treasury, containing details regarding the 
re-exported goods as stipulated in regulation No. 7 above.
9.  director General of Sri Lanka Customs shall submit a monthly summary report including information on the name and 
address of the import permit holder, vehicle import permit number, type of motor vehicle, cost-insurance-freight value 
and date of importation of such motor vehicle (s) under these regulations to the Secretary to the ministry of Tourism 
and Land with a copy to the Controller General of imports and Exports Control department.
10.  all motor vehicles imported as per the provisions of these regulations shall be registered in the name of the Vehicle 
import Permit holder referred in regulation No. 4 above, within 30 days from the date of Customs clearance.
11.  To achieve the objectives of these regulations, the Controller General of imports and Exports shall issue “Operational 
instructions” to the director General of Customs, licensed banks or any other relevant authorities.
12.  These regulations will only be in force until June 30, 2025.
