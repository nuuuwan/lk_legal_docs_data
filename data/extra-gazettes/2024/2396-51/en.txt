FOREST CONSERvATION  ORDINANCE
the order under sub section 3 (1) that has to be read with sub section 4 (1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 
451), No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act, No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 
of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009.
The Order 
By virtute of the powers vested in me under sub section 3 (1) that has to be read with sub section 4 (1) of the Forest 
Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act, No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 
13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation 
do by this Order declare the state lands of the extent 445.0 hectares bounded by the limits set forth in the schedules 1, 2, 3, 
4 & 5 hereto as Irampaikkulam 2 reserved Forest from 07th day of august, 2024.
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi, 
 Minister of Wildlife and 
 Forest resources Conservation.
Ministry of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation,
No. 07, hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Colombo 07,
07th day of august 2024.
