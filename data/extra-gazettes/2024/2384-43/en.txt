RESOLUTION
   This Parliament resolves under Section 10 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), that with effect from the date on 
which this resolution is notiﬁ ed in the Gazette ;
   Import duties under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) shall be levied and paid on the articles speci ﬁ ed in 
Column 3 of the Schedule hereto at the preferential import duty rates, which is leviable and payable under the Sri Lanka - 
Singapore Free Trade Agreement, set out in the corresponding entries in Column 4 of that schedule as an integral part of the 
Schedule “A” of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
   Customs import duty at the general rate, set out in the corresponding entry in Column 5 and preferential rates, except 
the preferential rate of customs import duty under the Sri Lanka - Singapore Free Trade Agreement which have been revised 
above, set out in the corresponding entry in Column 4, which are leviable and payable on each article speciﬁ ed in Column 3 
of Schedule “A” of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2324/49 dated March 
24, 2023 as amended, including its Schedules are continued to remain in force without change,
   The Schedule “A” of the Customs Ordinance (Revenue Protection Order No. 03/2023), published in the Extraordinary 
Gazette No. 2354/04 dated October 16, 2023 is hereby rescinded effective from January 06, 2024 without prejudice  to any 
action performed under the same during its validity period.
