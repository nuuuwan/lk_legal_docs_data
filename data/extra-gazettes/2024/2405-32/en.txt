As per the powers vested in me under Article 154 c read with Article 154 f of the constitution of the democratic socialist 
republic of sri Lanka and sub-section 19(5) of Provincial councils Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, hanif yusoof, the Governor of 
Western Province do hereby announce that the "rules (Amendment) on Procurement Affairs relevant to the Western Provincial

1. these rules will be enforced to amend the rules on Procurement Affairs relevant to the Provincial council Fund of 
the Western Province published in the extraordinary  Gazette paper No. 2186/25 dated 30th July 2020 of the democratic 
socialist republic of sri Lanka amended by rules (Amended) on Procurement Affairs relevant to the Provincial 
council Fund of the Western Province No. 1 of 2024 published by the extraordinary  Gazette paper No.; 2394/70 dated 
26.07.2024 of the democratic socialist repubic of sri Lanka.
2. these rules shall be cited as rules (Amendment) on Procurement Affairs relevant to the Provincial council Fund of 
the Western Province, No. 02 of 2024.
3. The words "Fifty Million" stated under first subsection in Section 7 of Rules on Procurement Affairs Relevant to the 
Provincial council Fund of the Western Province published by the extraordinary Gazette paper No.; 2186/25 dated 
30th July 2020 of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka amended by rules (Amendment) on Procurement 
Affairs relevant to the Provincial council Fund of the Western Province No. 1 of 2024 published by the extraordinary 
Gazette paper No.; 2394/70 dated 26.07.2024 of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka will be repealed 
hereby and amended by substituting the words "Fifteen Million".
4. should any inconsistency arise among the sinhala, english and tamil texts of these rules, sinhala text shall prevail.
