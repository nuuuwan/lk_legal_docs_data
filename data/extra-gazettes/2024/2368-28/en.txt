Notices Made by the Governor of the Uva Province
I, A.J.M. Muzammil ,the Governor of the Uva Province, hereby notify by virtue of powers vested on me  by the article 154(c) 
of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka through this order that the speci ﬁ ed roads mentioned 
in the following schedule must be the roads belongs to Uva Provincial Road Development Department in accordance with 
subsection 6 of section 6 in part II and section 3 in part I of Uva Provincial Road statute No. 02 of 2010. Accordingly, I declare

    A.J.M.M
UZAMMIL,

At Badulla,
On 27th of December 2023.
AS PER THE STATUTE OF UVA PROVINCIAL ROAD OF NO. 02 OF 2010 OF THE
PROVINCIAL COUNCIL OF UVA PROVINCE, WEIGHT LIMIT OF ROADS UNDER SECTION 26.1
 As per the road statute of Uva Province 26.1 in a ‘C’ class road mentioned in the following schedule the weight of the 
vehicle including the total weight of materials or people, animals and materials loaded should not exceed 12 metric tons, In 
a ‘D’ class road the weight of the vehicle with full weight loaded should not exceed 10 metric tons.
