APPOINTMENT  OF  AUTHORIZED  OFFICERS  UNDER  THE  PRIVATE
MEDICAL  INSTITUTIONS  (REGISTRATION)  ACT,  No. 21  OF  2006
By virtue of the powers vested in me under Sub section 4 of the Section 14 of the Private Medical Institutions (registration) 
Act, No. 21 of 2006, I, Dr. Ramesh Pathirana, Minister of Health do make order by this Notification, by appointing the 
following officers mentioned in the Schedule hereto shall be the Authorized Officers for the purpose of the said Act in 
respect of the area assigned to them in the Schedule mentioned below and in any other area in respect to which they may be 
appointed to act on the exigencies of services. They are appointed by virtue of their ex officio status and this will cover the 
existing officers in the Schedule hereto who are holding such posts as well as officers who will be appointed to such posts 
from time to time. All these appointments shall be valid until the officer exists in the service.
   all such appointments already made under the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2011/33 dated 24.03.2017 shall be valid 
and intact.
    dr
. Ramesh PathiRana,
   Minister of Health.
     
Colombo,
20th december, 2023.
