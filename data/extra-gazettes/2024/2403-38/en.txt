PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
NOTIfICATION
IT is hereby notified that the Hon. President, having consulted with the Hon. Prime Minister under Article 44(3) of the 
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has determined that the ministerial portfolios indicated 
below should be in his charge.
 1. Minister of Finance, Economic Development, Policy Formulation, Planning and Tourism 
 2. Minister of Energy 
 3. Minister of Agriculture, Lands, Livestock, Irrigation, Fisheries and Aquatic Resources
      By Order of the Honourable President,
                    dr. N. S. Kumanayake,
                              Secretary to the President.
