WHEREAS, the office of chairman in Elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of office of the Chairman shall 
be deemed to have resigned under section 169 of Pradeshiya sabha act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended by No. 21 of 2012 
(special Provisions) Local authority act;
and whereas the s ecretary of the sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna, the recognized political party to which 
Ponnamperuma arachchige Karunasena, the former chairman belonged, was required by me under section 66B(2) of the 
aforesaid ordinance, to decide upon the chairman of Elpitiya Pradeshiya sabha who belongs to such party who shall be 
declared elected as chairman within a period of (30) days;
 and whereas the secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate 
Malliyawadu Nalin Chaminda Priyadarshana to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore I, Wewelwala Hewage ravindra Wijaya Kumara, returning officer of the Elpitiya Pradeshiya 
sabha, acting in terms of section 66B (2) of the Local authorities Elections ordinance (chapter 262) as amended by Local 
authorities Election (amendment) act, No. 16 of 2017 do hereby declare malliyawadu Nalin chaminda Priyadarshana 
elected as chairman of the Elpitiya Pradeshiya sabha.
