“ (10) (a) a  candidate for the General entrance examination referred to in sub rule (2) shall be required to 
sit for the following written question papers in-
 (i) General Knowledge;
 (ii) General Intelligence;
 (iii) english Language Competency; and
 (iv) sinhala or Tamil Language Competency.
(b) a  candidate may opt to answer the General Knowledge and General Intelligence question papers 
referred to in sub paragraphs (i) and (ii) of paragraph (a) of this sub rule in the sinhala, Tamil or english medium.
(c) a  candidate who has scored a mark which is less than forty per centum for any one or more of the 
question papers referred to in paragraph (a) shall not be selected for admission.
(d) The marks obtained in the General Knowledge and General Intelligence question papers shall be taken 
into consideration when selecting candidates  eligible for admission, in the order of merit.
(e) The time allocated to answer each question paper at the said examination shall be as follows:-
 (i) One hour for the General Knowledge question paper;
 (ii) One hour for the General Intelligence question paper;
 (iii) One and a half hours for the english Language Competency question paper; and
 (iv) One and a half hours for sinhala or Tamil Language Competency question paper.”.
