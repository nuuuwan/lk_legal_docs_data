IT is hereby notified that the Honourable President, in terms of Section 19 (1) and (2) of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 09 
of 1950 as amended by the Act, No. 28 of 1964, has appointed the following Lawyer Members and Valuer Members of the 
Board of Review for the purpose of hearing appeals as per the provisions of the said Act, for a period of three years with effect 
from February 02, 2024 and  in terms of Section 19(3) of the said Act, has appointed Mr. R.M.S.K. Ratnayaka as Chairman 
with effect from  February 02, 2024 and  Mr. R.W.M.S.B. Rajapakse as Vice Chairman of the said Board of Review, with 
effect from the February 28, 2024.
Lawyer Members:
1. Mr. R. M. S. K. Ratnayaka
