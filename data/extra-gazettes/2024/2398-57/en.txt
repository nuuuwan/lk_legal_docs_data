I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:  
 
An allotment of land-depicted in sheet 01, zone 02 of 
C. M. No. 810462 of 10.05.2024 made by the Senior 
Survey Superintendent, Galle situated in the Village of 
Walpita in the Grama Niladhari Division of 206, Walpita 
North, in the Divisional Secretary's division of 
Baddegama, District of Galle, Southern Province and the 
description of the allotment of land are as follows :- 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of the land : Olimpaswatta; Description : Home 
garden, 02 permanent buildings, a water tank; Claimant:  
Government; Original Claimant: Land Reforms 
Commission; Extent: 0. 0400 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Baddegama on the  
14 th day of October 2024 at 10.00 a.m , and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 07th day of October 2024  the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of compensation and the details or the 
computation of such amount.
