ANNOuNCEmENT  Of  PROfESSIONAL  PLANTERS  IN  SRI LANkA
this is further to the Gazette Notification  No. 2341/19 issued on 18th July (t uesday) 2023 by the National institute 
of Plantation management under the purview of the ministry of Plantation industries on  the title of “announcement of 
Professional Planters in sri Lanka.” the following twenty six (26) professional members of NiPm is included in the list of 
members. these individuals were formally awarded and conferred on 17th august 2024. the Professional planters in sri 
Lanka those who have met the necessary criteria, qualifications or contribution required for the membership is herewith 
listed for public notice. honorary members of National institute of Plantation management are entitled to use designatory 
letters as a qualification “FIPM-Sri Lanka”, Fellow Members are entitled to use designatory letters as a qualification “FIPM-
Sri Lanka” and Associate Members are entitled use as a qualification “AIPM-Sri Lanka” after their names. As per the sub 
section 15F of section 05 of Act No. 05 of 1987 “No person shall take or use the above title unless he has been enrolled as 
such by the institute” and as per the sub section of 15G of section 05 of act No. 5 of 1987 “every person who neglects or fails 
to comply with the provisions of 15F of this act shall be guilty of and offence an on conviction after trial before a Magistrate 
be liable to imprisonment of either description for a period not exceeding six months or to a fine not exceeding one thousand 
five hundred rupees or to both such imprisonment and fine”
 dr. Prasad dharmasena,
 director/Ceo,
 National institute of Plantation management.
