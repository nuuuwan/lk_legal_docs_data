FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13(b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION 
OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF SRI   LANKA
WHereAs, the secretary General of parliament has informed the election Commission, under section 64(1) of the 
parliamentary elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the parliament by reason of 
the resignation of mr. Anura Kumara dissanayake, member of parliament of the electoral district No. 01- Colombo  who 
assumed duties as the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with effect from 23rd of September, 
2024 ;
And Whereas, the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by the 
election Commission under section 64(1) of the parliamentary elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, to 
fill this vacancy as provided 
for under sub Article 99(13)(b) of the Constitution;
ANd Whereas, the said returning of ficer acting under section 64(2) of the parliamentary elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, 
and in terms of sub Article 99(13)(b) of the Constitution has made a return in Form “m” set out in the First schedule to the 
aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Lakshman Nipuna Arachchi was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the said 
electoral district;
