ProPosal
by virtue of powers vested by sub section 2(1) of entertainment ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended by entertainment Tax  
(amendment) ordinance No. 17 of 1984, Pitabeddara Pradeshiya sabha hereby proposes to impose and recover an amount 
similar to Ten percent (10%) of payment as a Tax for an entertainment event described in the ordinance aforesaid which 
are held in the administrative area of Pitabeddara Pradeshiya sabha and this proposal shall be effective from 01.01.2023. It 
is futher proposed that the entertainment Tax aforesaid should be paid to Pitabeddara Pradeshiya sabha by the organizer or 
organizers concerned before the day of holding that event of entertainment.
