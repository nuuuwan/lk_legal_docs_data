Notice  is hereby given to the General Public of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri lanka under Section 8(G) of the 
Urban development authority law, No. 41 of 1978 as amended by the act, No. 4 of 1982 that I, Prasanna Ranathunga , the 
minister in charge of the subject of Urban development & Housing, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 
8(F) of the said act, No. 4 of 1982 have approved the development Plan for the limit of Gampaha municipal Council and 
Pradeshiya Sabha prepared under Section 8 (a) of the said act on the day of 19th July, 2024. 
 Prasanna ranathunga (M.P),
