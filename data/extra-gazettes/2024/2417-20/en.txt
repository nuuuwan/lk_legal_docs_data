2. by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy act No. 48 of 2007, as listed out 
in the Schedule above I, do hereby order to waive - off;
(i) balance amount after collecting rs. 6 per kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of 10% or rs. 400 per kg 
on importation of Mackerel (Scomber scombrus, scomber australasicus, scombe japanicus) classified under the HS 
Code of 0303.54 and Jack and horse mackerel (Trachurus Spp.) classified under the HS Code of 0303.55 classified 
under HS Heading of 03.03 for production of canned fish by local canned fish manufactures on the recommendation 
of the Secretary to the ministry in charge of the subject of Fisheries.
(ii) balance amount after collecting rs. 10 per Kg from the applicable Special Commodity Levy of rs. 40 per Kg 
on importation of Salt (Sodium Chloride BP), classified under the HS Code of 2501.00, by any pharmaceuticals 
manufacturing enterprise, registered with the National medicines regulatory authority (Nmra) for the purpose 
of manufacturing pharmaceuticals, not for commercial purpose, on the recommendation of the Secretary to the 
ministry in charge of the subject of health.
3. Importation of maize, black gram, Green gram, Cowpeas and Kurakkan as listed out above shall be allowed on the 
recommendation of the ministry in charge of the subject of agriculture.
4. This Order shall be valid for a period commencing from January 01, 2025 to december 31, 2025 subject to provisions 
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy act, No. 48 of 2007.
