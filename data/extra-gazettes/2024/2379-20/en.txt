THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  Section 
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below 
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
    Allotments of land depicted in below names and extents 
in the Advance Tracing No. MN/MNN/2023/508 dated 
27.10.2023 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the 
Village called Kaddukarankudiyiruppu in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. MN/53, of Kaddukaran Kudiyiruppu in the 
Divisional Secretary's Division of Mannar Town of Mannar 
District in Northern Province and bounded as follow:
 01.  Lot 'A' – Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani – Extent: 
2.3365 Hectares 
 North: Lot B and Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani (claimant 
not known);  East: Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani (claimant 
not known); South: Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani (claimant 
not known), Nachialkaani (Claimed by A.Amirthanathan 
Thatcroos and shareholders), and Lot C; West: Lot C, 
Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani (claimant not known) and 
Lot B.
 02.  Lot 'B' – Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani – Extent: 
0.0071 Hectare 
 North: Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani (claimant not known);  
East: Lot A; South: Lot A and Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani 
(claimant not known); West: Kaddukarankudiyiruppukaani 
(claimant not known).
