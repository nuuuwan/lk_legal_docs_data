SCHEDULE II
Part I
Activities permitted within the Protection Area
The physical planning and development within the Protection Area shall be only for the following activitics:-
 1. Maintenance of existing infrastructure and buildings (two-r eligious places, two - Ambalamas, Erathna - Sripada Road 
and Pilgrims Support Facilitating Centres).
 2. Operation and maintenance of existing mini hydro power proj ect.
 3. Management of invasive alien species.
 4. Educational and Research activities.
 5. Excavation, conservation and maintenance of Archeological s ites.
 6. Construction and maintenance of vehicle park proposed by th e Ministry of Provincial Roads Development, Rural 
Infrastructure, Tourism, Sports and Youth AÔ¨Ä airs in the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
 7. Maintenance activities of existing road from Erathna to min i hydro power project at Warnagala.
 8. Activities related to the sustainability of the Protection Area.
Part II
Conditions to be applied in carrying out the activities set out in Part I of this Schedule within the Protection Area
1. Any physical planning and development set out in Part I of this Schedule shall be carried out subject to the following 
conditions:-
(a) such physical planning and development shall be carried out subject to the provisions of the Act;
(b) results of such educational and research activities shall be submitted to the Authority along with a copy of the 
study or research report;
(c) such physical planning and development shall be carried out in such manner as to-
 (i) not hinder the sustainability of the Protection Area; and 
 (ii) not disturb fauna and Ô¨Ç  ora habitats;
(d) invasive alien species shall not be introduced to the Protection Area;
(e) any kind of waste or wastewater shall not be disposed to the Protection Area;
(f) reclamation of land, activities relevant to land degradation and soil erosion shall not be undertaken in the 
Protection Area; and
(g) suÔ¨É  cient environmental Ô¨Ç ow shall be maintained in downstream of the Kuru Ganga from weir of the existing 
mini hydro power project.
 
2. Where any Government Department, corporation, statutory boar d, local authority or any public oÔ¨É  cer carries out any 
physical planning and development within the Protection Area under the provisions of section 26, the Authority shall 
prepare and adopt an administrative levy scheme in order to process and evaluate project proposals relating to such 
physical planning and development and shall monitor such activities.
