THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Declaration under Section 5     
I, Nalaka Jude Harin Fernando, Minister of Tourism and 
Lands, do hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of  the Section 
5 of the Land Acquisition Act, the land described below is 
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the 
provisions of that Act.
 The land described as Lot No. A with the extent of 0.0126  
Hect. mentioned in the progression tracing bearing No. B/
ACQ/BDL/2017/116  and dated 30.09.2017 prepared by the  
Surveyor General situated in Sumanathissagama (Division 
Number 11) (within Badulla Municipality) coming under 78 B 
Badulla East Grama Niladhari Division in the minor division 
of Rilpola Korale of Badulla Divisional Secretary Division 
in Badulla District in the Province of Uva. 
 1. Lot  ‘A ’ –  Sumanathissagama  - Extent : 0.0126 
Hectares
 North: Sumanathissagama land possessed by Shriyani 
Dewapura and cement drain; East: Cement drain and 
Sumanathissagama land claimed by Muthiyangana

West: Sumanathissagama land claimed by Muthiyangana 
Rajamaha Viharaya, road and Sumanathissagama land claimed 
by Shriyani Dewapura.
