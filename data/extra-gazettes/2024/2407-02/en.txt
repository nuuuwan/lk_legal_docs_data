 7.2.2.1 The following Note will be inserted immediately after Section (c).
  Note :
  The degree programmes considered under the educational qualifications for recruitment to the approved 
posts of the service should be included in the approved schemes of recruitment of those posts. Furthermore, 
educational qualifications and degree programmes prescribed for recruitment to each post shall be mentioned 
in the notification for calling applications for recruitment to those posts. Where the educational qualifications 
included in the approved scheme of recruitment of any post have not been updated so as to tally with the 
educational qualifications mentioned in the notification for calling applications for recruitment to the aforesaid 
post, the educational qualifications mentioned in the notification for calling applications should be considered 
as the educational qualifications for recruitment to the relevant post.

Syllabus for the open competitive examination
 01. Intelligence Test – Duration: 1 hour – 100 Marks.
  This question paper which is designed to test the candidate’s ability of critical reasoning, general intelligence and 
ability of decision-making, shall consist of 50 questions of multiple choice.
 02. General Paper – Duration: 3 hours – 100 Marks.
  This is a question paper that aims at testing general knowledge, and it shall be designed to test the understanding 
of the candidate on the political, social, cultural and economic environment of Sri Lanka, matters that are currently 
important in national and international aspects, and national and international scientific and technical development. 
This question paper shall consist of two parts. The first part shall consist of questions for short answers, and 25 marks 
shall be allocated. The second part shall consist of questions in semi- structured nature. For this part, the candidate 
shall answer 5 out of 8 questions. 75 marks shall be allocated for this part.
