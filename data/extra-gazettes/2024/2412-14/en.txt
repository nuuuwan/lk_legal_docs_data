TRUSTS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 87) 
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS  the Trustees of the "Jayaratnam Trust Fund in Yogapuram" namely-
1. Dr. Wimalraj Jayaratnam;
2. Thurairaja Joseph Jesuthanandar;
3. Arumugam Sundaramoorthy;
4. Nadesapillai Vijayakumar;
5. Selvaratnam Aravinthan; and
6. Vettivel Vijayakumar.
have applied to the Minister of Justice, Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils, Local Government and 
Labour for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87);
NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Harini Amarasuriya, Minister of 
Justice, Public Administration, Home Affairs, Provincial Councils, Local Government and Labour do by this Order authorize 
the incorporation of the said Trustees, and direct and declare that-

 (a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the Deed 
of Trust bearing No. 2460 of October 28, 2023 attested by Suraweera Arachchige Telari Amanthika Suraweera, 
Notary Public of Colombo, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name 
and style of the "Jayaratnam Trust Fund in Yogapuram";
 (b) the said Trustees may, under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise acquire, 
sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable, on behalf of 
the said Trust; and
 (c) the said Trustees shall, by the said name and style, be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and 
to be answered, to defend and be defended, in any court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and actions arising 
in relation to the said Trust;
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
 Harini Amarasuriya,
 Minister of Justice, Public Administration, Home Affairs,
 Provincial Councils, Local Government and Labour.
Colombo.
