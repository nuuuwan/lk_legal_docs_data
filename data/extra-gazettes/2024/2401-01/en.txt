CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
  
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby noti ﬁ ed that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, P. B. S. C. Nonis, Director General of Customs, determine that with effect from 09th September, 2024, all duties of Customs 
as well as other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be paid and 
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notiﬁ cation relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette No. 2400/01 of 02nd September, 2024 is
hereby rescinded.
