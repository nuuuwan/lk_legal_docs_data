NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL  ACT,  No.  47  OF  1980
Order under section 24C and section 24D
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by section 24C and section 24D of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 
I, Keheliya Rambukwella, Minister of Environment, do by this Order, declare-
 ( a) that the area of land in which the metes and bounds are set out in Part I, Part II and Part III of the Schedule I hereto 
shall be an environmental protection area for the purposes of the Act and shall be called the “Ethaleikulam Tank 
Environmental Protection Area” (hereinafter referred to as the “Protection Area”);
 ( b) that for so long as this Order is in force –
 (i) any planning scheme or project within the Protection Area which is in conﬂ  ict with the provisions of the aforesaid 
Act, shall cease to operate in the Protection Area from the date of operation of this Order;
 (ii) no person other than the Authority shall exercise, perform, and discharge any powers, duties or functions relating 
to the planning and development within the Protection Area:

SCHEDULE II
Part I
Activities permitted within the Protection Area
   The physical planning and development within the Protection Area shall be only for the following activities:-
 1. Management and maintenance of existing irrigation scheme.
 2. Management of alien invasive fauna and ﬂ  ora species.
 3. Fishing activities.
 4. Plucking of ﬂ  owers from aquatic plants.
 5. Educational and research activities.
 6. Eco-tourism activities (without permanent or semi permanent structures).
Part II
Conditions to be applied in carrying out the activities set out in Part I of this Schedule
within the Protection Area
   1. Any physical planning and development set out in Part I of this Schedule shall be carried out subject to the 
following conditions:-
 ( a) motor boats shall not use in ﬁ  shing activities within the Protection Area; 
 ( b) such activities shall be carried out in such a manner as not to disturb the general life pattern of life, the 
livelihood of the villages and fauna and ﬂ  ora habitats;
 ( c) alien invasive fauna and ﬂ  ora species shall not be introduced to the Protection Area;
 ( d) any waste or wastewater of whatever condition shall not be disposed to the Protection Area;
 ( e) reclamation of land, activities relevant to land degradation and soil erosion shall not be undertaken in the 
Protection Area.
   2. The Authority may, subject to paragraph (j) of section 10 of the Act, appoint a committee to assist the Authority 
in the performance of its functions relating to the planning and development within the Protection Area.
   3. Such committee shall consist of-
 ( a) the Director of the Eastern Province of the Authority, and the Deputy Commissioner of Batticaloa District 
of the Department of Agrarian Development, who shall co-chair, the Committee;
 ( b) the Divisional Secretary of Manmunai South Eruvilpattu Divisional Secretariat;
 ( c) the Secretary of Manmunai South Eruvilpattu Pradeshiya Sabha;
