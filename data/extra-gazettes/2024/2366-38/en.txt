aNNual  EXCiSE   liCENSE   FEES 
Fees  Charged  For  LiCenses
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25 read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended 
from time to time, i, ranil Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic Stabilization and National Policies do hereby 
notify that the annual excise license fees specified in Schedule I hereto shall come in to force with effect from January 12, 2024.
The Excise Notification No. 1004 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2049/4 of december 11, 2017 and 
the Excise Notification No. 09/2018 published in Gazette Extraortinary No. 2065/57 of april 06, 2018 are hereby rescinded.
