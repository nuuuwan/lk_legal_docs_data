ThE  FOREST  CONSERvATION  ORDINANCE
the order under sub section 4 (2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451), No. 16 of 1907, as amended by  
act, No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009.
The Order 
By virtute of powers vested in me under sub section 4 (2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451),  
No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act, No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 
1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation do by ths Order release the block of 
forest land of the extent 98.0 hectares and bounded by the limits set forth in the schedules 1, 2 & 3 given below, of the 
elamaradankulam reserved Forest which is of the extent of 429.17 hectares and was declared as a reserved Forest by 
Gazette Extraorinary No. 2257/61 dated 10th december 2021, from the reserved status from 07th day of august, 2024.
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi, 
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation. 
Ministy of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation,
