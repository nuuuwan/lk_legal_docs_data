PART III  —  LANDS 
 
Land Acquisition Notices 
1A -  G 041184  – 19 (01/2024) 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT AS AMENDED BY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT 
No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7   
My  Ref.: 3/2/1/143. 
Ministry of Lands Ref. No.: 4-3/18/2017/WS/525. 
 
I hereby give notice, under section 7 sub section (1) of the 
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition Amendment Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
government intends to acquire the following lands, which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired : 
 
An allotment of land extended 0.0254 hectares, 
mentioned in the Cadastral Map No. 510511 dated 
18.08.2023, appeared as the Lot No. 01, prepared by 
Senior Superintendent of Surveyor Gampaha instead of 
Surveyor General, situated in the village of Medemulla, 
Minuwangoda - North Grama Niladhari Division, No, 
125/2, in Minuwangoda Divisional Secretariat area in 
Gampaha District, Western Province bounded as below: 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Kongahawaththa alias 
Kongahawaththa Kebella; Description: Open waste land; 
Owner: Kaluarachchilage Mala Mangalika 
Kaluarachchi; Extent : 0.0254 Hectares. 
 
All persons who entitled or claim ownership for the 
aforesaid land are required to appear personally or by 
agent duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Minuwangoda on 29th January 2024 at 10.00 
a.m.  
 
Further hereby requested to notify in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 24.01.2024 that the nature of the 
ownership, reason of the claim compensation for the 
acquisition of said land, the amount of compensation and 
the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 U. W. T. U. Rൺඃൺ඄ൺඋඎඇൺ, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Minuwangoda. 
 
On the day of 29th November 2023, 
At Divisional Secretariat Minuwangoda.
