Notice under Section 7

I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, As Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands/
Servitudes which are required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the lands or servitudes to be acquired:- 
 
Three allotments of land, refe rred in the sub schedule in 
Sheet No. 01, Block 02, Cadastral map 421361, prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated in Ihalagama Village in 
No. 1504 Ihalagama South Grama Niladhari Division in 
Udubaddawa Divisional Secretary's Division in 
Kurunegala District of North Western Province. 
 
Sub Schedule 
 
Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Meegahamulawaththa alias

Claimed by: R. P. Indra Padmalatha, Naththandiya Road, 
Udubaddawa; Extent: 0.0502 Hectare. 
Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: Ela; Claimed by: The Government; 
Extent: 0.0042 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 3 
Name of the Land: Koongah amulahena; Claimed by: 
R. P. Indra Padmalatha, Naththandiya Road, 
Udubaddawa; Extent: 0.0215 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear  personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, on the 
13.03.2024 at 10.00 a.m., and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 
06.03.2024 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/Servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
