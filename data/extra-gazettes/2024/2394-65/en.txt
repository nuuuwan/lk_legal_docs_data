Order under Section 27(1) read with Section 21 and Section 139
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978, i, a. d. Susil Premajayanth, 
Minister of Education, on the recommendation of the university Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule 
to the Order Published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15 of October 9, 2007 (as amended from time to time) which 
specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions 
of Section 139 of the aforesaid act, to be a university established under Section 21 of the said act, as follows:- 
(1) by the repeal of the item ‘‘Sri Ponnambalam ramanathan faculty of Performing and Visual arts’’ appearing in 
Column I of the Schedule (specified in the order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2293/22 of august 16, 
2022) and the substitution therefore of the following - 
 ‘‘Sir Ponnambalam ramanathan faculty of Visual and Peforming arts’’
