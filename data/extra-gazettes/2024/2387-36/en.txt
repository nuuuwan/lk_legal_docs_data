 03. Providing of the housing Aids under these regulations shall be done while maintaining a minimum balance of 
Rs. Sixty (60) Million in the Housing Development Fund all the time.
 04. the regulations related to providing housing Aids for Low Income recipients of Western Province published in 
the Extraordinary 
Gazette  of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, No. 2318/51 dated 10th February, 
2023 shall be repealed from the date on which these regulations come into effect.
 05.  Under these Regulations, "a low income recipient" means a person who earns a monthly income not exceeding a 
maximum of Rs. Fifteen Thousand.
 06.  In the event of any inconsistency between Sinhala, Tamil and English texts of these Regulations the Sinhala text 
shall prevail.
