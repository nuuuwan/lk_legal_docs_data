THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
All that allotment of land in total extent of about 0.1536 
Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 387 in sheet No. 28 in 
supplement No. 30 in FVP 564 of the Surveyor General, 
situated at the Village of  Godakoggala, in Ridiyagama 
Grama Niladhari Division, in Magam Pattuwa Minor 
Division, in the Divisional  Secretary’s Division of 
Ambalantota, in Hambantota District, and bounded as 
follows. 
 
 01. Lot No. 387  
 Name of the land: Bangala Godella, Koggala Bedda, 
Koggalla Kele; Description of the Land: Road; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha; 
Extent: 0.1536 Hectare. 
All persons who claim ownership, interest or other 
connection to the aforesaid land are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at 10.30 a.m. on 29 th July 2024 at the 
Divisional Secretariat, Ambalantota. 
 
Further, you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing in duplicate on or before 15 th July 2024 the nature 
of their interests in the land , the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land / servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such  amount. 
 
 N.D.P. Ranasinha, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ambalantota.  
 
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Ambalantota. 
1st July 2024
