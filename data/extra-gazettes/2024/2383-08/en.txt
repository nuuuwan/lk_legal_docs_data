No. 2383/08 –  MONDAY, MAY  06, 2024 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)   
,, 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
  My No.: WAB/LND/09/03. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends  to acquire the following lands 
Servitude which is required for a public purpose to 
conduct Samurdhi Community Based Bank, 
Wanduramba. 
 
 
 
 
Description of  the land  to be acquired: 
 
 
 
An allotment of Land depicted in the preliminary plan 
bearing Cadastral Plan No. 810457 prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of No. 190, Panvila, in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Wanduramba, District of Galle 
Southern Province. The details of the allotment of land is 
as follows:- 
 
 
Name of the Land: Panvila Hena alias Henakanda 
Waththa; Description: Hom e Garden, Two permanent 
buildings and a water tank; Extent: 0.0479 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on  which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing before 
me at Divisional Secretariat, Wanduramba on the 29th 
day of July 2024 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
22nd day of July 2024 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation and 
the details of the said land/ servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
