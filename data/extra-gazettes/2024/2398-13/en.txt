2A
Regulations
   The Fertilizer (Import, Manufacture and Formulation) Regulations, No. 1 of 2010, published in gazette extraordinary  
No. 1,683/5 of december 06, 2010 is hereby amended as follows :-
 1. by the repeal of regulation 3 thereof and the substitution therefor of the following regulation :-
   ''3. The fee payable under section 6 of the Act, in respect of the application for a licence shall be as follows :-
(a) a licence to import fertilizer - Rs. 150,000.00
(b) a licence to manufacture inorganic 
fertilizer - Rs. 150,000.00
(c) a licence to manufacture organic 
or eco-friendly fertilizer - Rs. 20,000.00
(d) a licence to formulate fertilizer - Rs. 150,000.00
   Provided that, a person who manufactures organic or eco-friendly fertilizer for his own consumption shall not be 
required to obtain a licence under the provisions of section 6 of the Act.'' and
 
 2. by the repeal of regulation 4 thereof and the substitution therefor of the following regulation :-
   ''4. The fee payable under section 8 of the Act, in respect of the application for the renewal of a licence shall 
be as follows :-
(a) a licence to import fertilizer - Rs. 100,000.00
(b) a licence to manufacture inorganic 
fertilizer - Rs. 100,000.00
(c) a licence to manufacture organic 
or eco-friendly fertilizer - Rs. 15,000.00
(d) a licence to formulate fertilizer - Rs. 100,000.00
