STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the University Sub-Warden have been caused injustice by the following issues related to leave and shifts and if so, 
to what relief they are entitled.
 1. Having to obtain the approval of the Head of the establishment to obtain leave on week end and public holidays.
 
 2. No solution been given to difficulties faced in engaging an officer for cover-up duties in obtaining leave.
 3. Not been given any payment for working on public holidays and in the weekend.
  
 H. K. K. A. Jayasundara,
 Commissioner General of Labour.
