NORTh  CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order of Delegation of Powers
As per the power vested on me by the Provincial Council Act No. 12 of 1989 (Incidental Provisions) and section No. 
2.2.(5) of the Co-operative society statute No. 05 of 2009 of the North Central Provincial Council ; I, Maheepala herath , 
Governor of the North Central Provincial Council do hereby delegates all power set out in column III of the schedule hereto 
out of the powers of the Co-operative society statute of North Central Provincial Council bearing No. 05 of 2005 read with  
Co-operative society Amendment statute bearing No. 01 of 2011 of the North Central Province with effect from the date set 
out in column II to the officers appointed to Assistant Commissioner (Departmental) to assist the Co-operative Development 
Commissioner of North Central Province and the registrar. 
 
  
Maheepala herath ,
 Governor,   
 North Central Province.
Governor’s Office, 
North Central Province,
On 30th November 2023.
