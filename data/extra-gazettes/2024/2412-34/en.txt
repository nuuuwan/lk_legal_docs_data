By  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) read with Section 21 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,  
I, Harini Amarasuriya, Minister of Education, Science and Technology, on the recommendation of the University Grants 
Commission, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, published in Gazette 
Extraordinary, No. 420/25, of September 26, 1986 (as amended from time to time) establishing the Eastern University,  
Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after the entry "3. Department of Management" appearing in Column II of the 
Schedule which corresponds to the item "Faculty of Commerce and Management" appearing in Column I of the Schedule 
(specified in the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1523/16 of November 14, 2007), of the following entry: -
  "4. Department of Accounting and Finance".
 
Harini Amarasuriya,
 Minister of Education, Science and Technology.
