PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE 
It is hereby notified that the annual Lenten season of Hiniduma Calvary shrine (St. Ann's Church) located within the two 
Grama Niladhari Divisions of Hiniduma South and Malhathawa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Thawalama in the 
District of Galle, Southern Province will be held during the period from 11th February 2024 to 7th April 2024 as per the 
regulations laid down under the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No.2057/33 dated 09.02.2018 in terms of the Section 2 
of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Cap.175).
   
 
Shantha WeeraSinghe,
 Divisional Secretary/Government Agent,
 Administrative District, Galle.
 
17th day of January, 2024.
