SCHEDULE  II
Rationale for the consideration of the Secondary Business of Strategic Importance 
The objectives of the Colombo Port City Economic Commission, as stipulated in Section 5 of the Colombo Port 
City Economic Commission Act, No. 11 of 2021, have been considered in designating the business as a second 
Business of Strategic Importance, in order to ensure the success of establishing the Colombo Port City, having 
regard to the national interest or the advancement of the national economy as provided for in section  53(5) of the 
said  Act.
HORIZON GROUP USA GLOBAL LLC , incorporated in the USA, is a wholly owned subsidiary of Horizon 
Group USA Inc. founded in 1912, Horizon Group USA Inc is a leading creator, developer and distributed of 
Fashion driven products for the Craft and specialty markets. It's diverse product lines include Kids' Arts and 
Craft Components, Activity Kits, Creativity Toys, Fashion Stationery, Back-to-School Supplies, Pet Accessories, 
Celebration and Party Supplies, and innovative educational tools such as S.T.E.A.M, an educational approach that 
uses Science, Technology, Engineering, the Arts and Mathematics as access points for guiding student inquiry, 
dialogue and critical thinking
with an annual turnover exceeding USD 200 Million, Horizon Group USA Inc, serves a broad customer base, 
including prominent retail chains such as Target, Walmart, Tesco, and Costco Wholesale, While employing 
approximately 400 professionals globally.
HORIZON GROUP USA GLOBAL LLC will operate as a Global Shared Service center within the Colombo Port 
city, providing Finance, planning, marketing backend operations and information technology services to Horizon 
Group USA Inc's global sourcing activities, employing approximately 31 individuals within the initial 3 years 
of operation, with an investment commitment of approximately USD 1,200,000 towards operational and capital 
expenditures during this period.
Further, in the long-term HORIZON GROUP USA GLOBAL LLC may expand its Colombo Port City operations 
to facilitate possible expansions to the European and Asian Markets developing the Colombo Port City as a central 
hub for global sourcing activities.
