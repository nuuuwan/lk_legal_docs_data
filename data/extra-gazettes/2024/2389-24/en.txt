uNIvERSITIES   ACT,  No. 16  Of  1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 25a of the universities act, No. 16 of 1978,1, dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, 
the Minister of education having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid act, in respect of the “British school 
of Commerce (Private) Limited”, a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing 
registration Number PV75150 and located at No. 19, Mcleod road, Colombo 4 do by this Order subject to such conditions 
as specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the “British School of Commerce (Private) Limited” as a Degree Awarding 
Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degree specified in Column I of 
the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviation is set out in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule II hereto:-
SCHEDuLE  I
Conditions
1. the British school of Commerce (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “institute”) shall, when admitting 
students for the degree programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Business Management 
Honours with a minimum of 120 Credits (SLQF Level 6), adhere to the minimum entry qualification of;
 (a) at least three (3) simple passes (s) in any stream in one and the same sitting at G.C.e. (advance Level) 
Examination conducted by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka or equivalent qualification.
