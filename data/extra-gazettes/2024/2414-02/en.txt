Order No.: 90
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003  (AMENDED) 
Order under Section 20(5)
aCTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20 (5) of the Consumer affairs authority act, No. 09 of 2003, the 
Consumer affairs authority orders that no Manufacturer, Producer, Supplier, distributor or Trader shall Sell, Supply, Expose 
or Offer for sale, display for sale the Local varieties of Rice listed below, exceeding the Maximum Prices given hereunder. 
Variety Maximum Wholesale Price Maximum Retail Price
White/Red Raw Rice - Local Rs. 215/- per kg Rs. 220/- per kg
White/Red Nadu Rice - Steamed /Boiled - Local
(excluding Mottaikarupan and attakari)
Rs. 225/- per kg Rs. 230/- per kg
White/Red Samba Rice - Raw and Steamed /Boiled - Local 
(excluding Suduru Samba)
Rs. 235/- per kg Rs. 240/- per kg
Keeri Samba Rice- Raw and Steamed/ Boiled - Local Rs. 255/- per kg Rs. 260/- per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 9th december, 2024.
