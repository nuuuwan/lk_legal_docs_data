WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL 
Notification made under Section 3 (8) of the Provincial Road Development Authority
Statute No. 04 of 1989 of the Western Provincial  Council
i, roshan goonetileke, the governor of the Western Province, in terms of the powers vested in me under article 154 (C) of 
the 13th amendment to the Constitution and section 3 (8) of the Provincial road development authority statute No. 04 of 
1989 of the Western Provincial Council do hereby notify the removal of upali Kodikara who functions as a member and the 
Chairman of the Provincial road development authority of the Western Province from the membership of the authority 
as it is deemed satisfied that the said Upali Kodikara has failed to execute the powers and duties of the Authority properly 
when functioning in his office.
