THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)   
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
 Ref No.: WEG/LAN/L8/ACQ/54. 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for the public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
 
 
Description of the land/ Servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
 
 
The following land depicted in C.M. No. 820108 
situated in Gurubebila (Midigama) in Grama Niladhari’s 
Division of Gurubebila—380 A in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Weli gama in Matara District in 
the Southern Province for the purpose of expansion of 
Midigama Market. 
 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Usbandarawatta; Claimant: D.L. 
Mervin Janaka Gunasekara; Extent: 0.0343  Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ Servitude 
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing before me at 
Divisional Seretariat,Weligama on the 30th day of 
October 2024 at  10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
23rd day of October, 2024 mentioning the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 
 
   T.L.N.S. Mendis, 
       Divisional Secretary,  
   W e l i g a m a .  
 
 
 
9th day of September, 2024. 
At Divisional Secretariat, Weligama.
