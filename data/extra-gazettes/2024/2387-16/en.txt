THE ANTIQUITIES ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 188)
Order under Section 16
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by section 16 of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) I, Vidura Wikramanayaka, 
Minister of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs, do by this Order declare that the monuments specified in the 
Schedule hereto shall be deemed to be "Ancient monuments" for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance.
 Vidura Wikramanayaka,
 Minister of Buddhasasana,
 Religious and Cultural Affairs.
Colombo.
29th April, 2024.
