THE  COLOMBO  PORT  CITY   ECONOMIC  COMMISSION   ACT,  No.  11  OF  2021
Order  under  Section   53
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 53 of the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 
of 2021, i, ranil Wickremesinghe, minister of investment Promotion, having considered the recommendations of the 
Colombo Port City economic  Commission and upon the approval of the Cabinet of ministers, do by this order specify that-
 (a) the Cabinet of ministers, by its decision No. 24/1315/631/034 dated, July 09, 2024 has approved the designation 
of the business of the authorised person specified in Schedule i hereto as a Business Strategic importance - duty 
Free retail operations;
 (b) the rationale for considering such business as a Business of Strategic importance duty Free retail operations is 
as specified in Schedule ii hereto;
 (c) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments shall be as specified in Schedule iii hereto; and
 (d) the exemptions or incentives granted under the enactments specified shall commence and cease to be operative 
on the dates specified in Schedule iv hereto.
 Ranil WickRemesinghe, 
 minister of investment Promotion. 
Colombo, 
July 19, 2024.

SCHedule  ii
Rationale for the consideration of the Business of Strategic Importance - Duty Free Retail 
Operations 
the objectives of the Colombo Port City economic Commission, as stated in Section 5 of the Colombo Port City 
economic Commission act, No. 11 of 2021 have been considered in designating the business as Business of Strategic 
importance - duty Free mall operations in order to ensure the success of establishing the Colombo Port City, having 
regard to the national interest or in the advancement of the national economy as stated in Section 53(5) of the said 
act.
ODF PTE LTD is a company incorporated in Singapore which specialises in Duty Free retail and affiliated to FY 
Group. odF Pte ltd is scheduled to manage the duty-Free mall at the Singapore Flyer, a prominent tourist 
destination, covering an expansive 8700 square meters of retail space. 
odF Pte ltd with a foreign direct investment of uSd 5.3 million and the recruitment of over 50 employees is to 
start operations in Port City downtown. With access to multiple suppliers and branded agencies, odF Pte ltd will 
infuse a range of premium luxary brands, and adopt the use of pioneering digital retail technology, such as augmented 
reality promotions for its duty-free retail operations in the Colombo Port City, which will be the first of its kind in Sri 
lanka. augmented reality in the retail industry are interactive experiences that combine the real world and computer-
generated content. the content can span multiple sensory modalities such as visuals, auditory, olfactory.
