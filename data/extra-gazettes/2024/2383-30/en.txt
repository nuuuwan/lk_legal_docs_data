                              P.s. No.: Ps/CsA/00/2/101
IT is hereby notified that the Honourable President, in terms of Section 2 of the Conferring the Honour of Senior Instructing 
Attorneys-at-Law Act, No. 26 of 2023 and the Guidelines on Conferring the Honour of Senior Instructing  Attorneys-at-Law 
made by the Honourable Minister of Justice, Prison Affairs and Constitutional Reforms under Section 3 of the said Act and 
published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2363/73 dated December 22, 2023, has conferred the Honour of Senior Instructing 
Attorney-at-Law to the following Attorneys-at-Law on the 02nd day of May, 2024. 
 01. Dr. Jayanta Mootatamby Swaminathan
 02. Mr. Deva Manoharan Swaminathan
 03. Mr. Gerard Gnanavarothayam Arulpragasam
 04. Ms. Habaragamuwa Ralalage Anoma Dilanie Peiris Goonetilleke
 05. Ms. Shiranthi Nelum Manik Gunawardena
 
By Order of the Honourable President,
