ANNUAL  PERAHARA  OF  BADULLA  KATHARAGAMA  DEV ALAYA
IN accordance with the powers vested in me by Gazette notiﬁ cations No. 9902 of 10.09.1948, No. 10283 of 17.08.1951 and  
No. 14778 of 25.03.1955 published under Chapter 362 of the Pilgrimage Act, and under the powers conferred on Divisional 
Secretaries by Act No. 58 of 1992, and according to the constitution published in No. 1622 Gazette Notiﬁ cation of 02.10.2009 
on Annual Perahara of Badulla Katharagama Devalaya, I, Thushari Anuradha Nanayakkara, the Divisional Secretary of Badulla 
do hereby make the following orders in connection with the Anuual Perahara of Baddulla Katharagama Devalaya, 2024.
 01.  I do hereby notice that, the Anuual Perahara of Badulla K atharagama Devalaya shall begin with "Kap Situvima" 
on 04th September 2024 and by having inner Perahara from 04th t o 08th September 2024 (5 days), by having 
Kumbal Perahara from 09th September to 13th September 2024 (5 days), by having Randoli Perahara from 14th 
September to 18th September 2024 (5 days), by having Water cutting ceremony in the morning on 19th September 
and end in the afternoon on 19th September 2024 after having returned to the Devalaya.
 02. Route of the Perahara
  The Perahara shall start from Badulla Katharagama Devalaya premises and proceed along Badulla Lower Street 
and reach to Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya only 18th of September, and after preceding the Perahara 
of Muthiyangana Raja Maha Viharaya it shall proceed along Longd on road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, 
Cocowatte Road, Udayaraja Mawatha, Vel Bodiya Junction, King's Street, Bazzar street and along the lower street 
and back to the throne.

 03. Cooking and selling articles of trade are prohibited in an d around Badulla Katharagama Devalaya compound 
where the throne of the Devalaya located.
 04. Private vehicles are prohibited to park in the main roads during the Perahara Season. All types of vehicles, including

 05. The permanent buildings and the buildings currently being constructed along the Perahara route under a formal

other garbage lying along the route of Perahara and Devala premises as instructed by the Public Health Inspectors.

obtained from me thereto.
