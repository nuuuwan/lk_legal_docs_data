Regulations under the Imports and Exports Control Act, No.1 of 1969
IN terms of the powers vested in me by section 20 read together with sub-section 4(1) and section 14 of the Imports and 
exports (Control) act, No. 1 of 1969 as amended by act, No.48 of 1985 and act, No. 28 of 1987, I, Ranil wickremesinghe, 
the Minister of Finance, economic stabilization and National Policies, promulgate following Regulations.

3. Importation of goods including capital / project related goods, by an authorized Person, who is also designated as a 
“Business of strategic Importance” within the meaning of the provisions of the Colombo Port City economic 
Commission act, No.11 of 2021 to carry out any approved activities of such authorized Person in and from the area 
of authority of the Colombo Port City, under the Colombo Port City economic Commission act, No. 11 of 2021, 
provided that the goods are consigned in the name of the said authorized Person, shall be allowed for the Customs 
clearance under the Open account Payment term by the director general of sri Lanka Customs on the recommendation 
of the Colombo Port City economic Commission, obtained prior to arrival of such goods at any Ports / airports in sri 
Lanka. Payments for importation of such goods under the Open account Payment term shall be settled from the 
foreign exchange generated by the authorized Person within the provisions of the Colombo Port City economic 
Commission act, No. 11 of 2021. 
4. the Controller general, the department of Imports and exports Control shall issue required “Operating Instructions” 
to the director general of sri Lanka Customs and licensed banks for effective implementation of these Regulations.
5. the director general of sri Lanka Customs shall consult the Controller general, department of Imports and exports 
Control on any importation of goods made by any approved Persons in violation of the Regulation No. 3 above.   
6. these Regulations will only be in force until august 20, 2025.
