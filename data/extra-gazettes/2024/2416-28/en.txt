Local Government Notiﬁ cations
PRADESHIYA  SABHA - BIBILE.
UNDER Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, and in accordance with the directions issued by the Honourable 
Governor of Uva Province, published through Extraordinary Gazette No. 2318/41 dated February 10, 2023, and Gazette No. 
2414/13 dated December 11, 2024, regarding the preparation of plans and name list of streets and narrow roads managed by 
Pradeshiya Sabhas in Uva Province, it has been decide under Decision No. 358 dated September 30, 2024, that the streets and 
narrow roads listed in the schedule below are to be declared as streets and narrow roads belonging to the Bibile Pradeshiya 
Sabha. This declaration is made public for the information of the general public. 
If any street or narrow road included in the said schedule passes through or is part of a private land, and if the concerned 
landowner claims ownership of such land, they are required to ﬁ le a case in the appropriate court asserting their ownership 
rights within three months from the date of this notiﬁ cation. 
Furthermore, it is hereby announced that if no such legal action is initiated within the stipulated three-month period, all streets 
and narrow roads included in the schedule shall be deemed as belonging to the Bibile Pradeshiya Sabha.
D.M. A
NUSHA S.DISSANAYAKE
Council Secretary,
Bibile Pradeshiya Sabha.
 at Bibile Pradeshiya Sabha
 On 13th December 2024 
Decision 
Under the directions of the Honourable Governor of Uva Province, as published in Extraor
dinary Gazette No. 2318/41 dated 
February 10, 2023, and the orders outlined in the regulations pertaining to the noti ﬁ cation of the preparation of plans and 
name list of streets and narrow roads managed by the Pradeshiya Sabhas of Uva Province, issued under Section 24(1) of the 
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, it is hereby decided that the streets and narrow roads listed in the annexed schedule 
are to be considered as streets and narrow roads managed by the Bibile Pradeshiya Sabha.
