 (1) by the substitution for regulation 3 thereof, of the following regulation: -
“3. Every application for a license to carry out pest control activities shall be in the prescribed form and be accompanied 
by a fee of Rupees Twenty  Five Thousand.”.
 (2) by the substitution for paragraph (a) of the regulation 5 thereof, of the following paragraph: -
“(a) be valid for a period of one year and may be renewed upon an application being submitted on that behalf in 
the prescribed form of request, three months prior to the lapse of the current licence together with a renewal fee of 
Rupees Ten Thousand.”.
 (3) by the addition, immediately after regulation 5 thereof, of the following new regulation:-
“5A. Every application for renewal of the licence made after twelve months from the date of its issue shall be 
accompanied by a fee of Rupees Ten Thousand for each late year.”.
