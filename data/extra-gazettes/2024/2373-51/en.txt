UNder the circumstances that; Malliyawadu Nalin chaminda Priyadarshana, having being pronounced to have been appointed 
as chairman of elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha vide extra Ordinary Gazette Notification bearing number 2369/02 of 29.01.2024

(2) of Local Authorities Election Ordinance and the respective Budget for year 2024 submitted by the said chairman to the

Act, No. 15 of 1987 as amended vide Section 17 of Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act 21 of 2012, having being 
defeated with 12 votes in favour and 14 against;
   The said chairman having submitted his resignation from his respective office to the Returning Officer of Elpitiya 
Pradeshiya Sabha on 28.02.2024;
   And elpitiya Pradeshiya Sabha shall be considered as a dissolved Local Government institution by virtue of interim 
Order of Section 66B (4) of Local Authorities Election Ordinance which is the Authority 262;
