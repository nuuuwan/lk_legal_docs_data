No. 2371/40 –  THURSDAY, FEBRUARY  15, 2024 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)   
 
 
 
Notice under  Section  7 
 
 
My Ref. No.: DIVKB/LND/LA/DSKAQ/10. 
Land Min. Ref. No.:-  4-3/5/2019/HA/262. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for Divisional Secretariat Kobeigane: 
 
 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
 
 
The Allotment of land shown in the Cadastral Map 
No. 420722, Block No. 03 and Sheet No. 01 Dated 
30.10.2023 Prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys of 
Kurunegala, in Extent of 0.4846 Hectare, situated in Ihala 
Aralugaswewa village in 13 08 Aralugaswewa Grama 
Niladari Division in Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Kobeigane in Kurunegala District in North Western 
Province. 
 
Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Kahatagahamulahena and 
Kongahamulahena; Descriptio n of Land: Home Garden; 
Claimant : Land Reform Commission, Required for 
Divisional Secretariat Kobeigane; Extent: 0.4846  
Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land to be  
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Kobeigane on the 22nd day of April 2024 at 
10.00 am and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicates) on or before the 15th day of April 
2024 the nature of their interests in the land the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.   
 
 
 
 W. Thilini Nisansala Silva, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kobeigane. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, Kobeigane, 
09th of February 2024.
