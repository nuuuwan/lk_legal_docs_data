PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE 
It is hereby notified that the annual Esala Festival of Sri Devol Maha Devalaya situated in the Grama Niladhari Division 
of Seenigama, Divisional Secretary's Division of Madampagama of the District of Galle, Southern Province, will be held 
during the period from Thursday the 1st of August, 2024 to Thursday the 08th of August, 2024 as per the order made under 
the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1965/39 dated 06.05.2016 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in 
terms of Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Cap.175).
