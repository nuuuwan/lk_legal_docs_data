NOTICE  uNDER  SECTION  76A  Of  ThE  LOCAL   AuThORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE 
AND  SECTION  24A  Of  ThE  REGISTRATION  Of  ELECTORS  ACT ,  No. 44  Of  1980
BY virtue of the powers vested in the Election Commission in terms of sections 76A and 89 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance as amended by the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 25 of 1990 and in terms of section 24A 
of the registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, as amended by the registration of Electors (Amendment) Act, No. 22 
of  2021, we do hereby notify that the interpretation of the term “youth” in section 89 of the Local Authorities Elections 
Ordinance, should be amended by the substitution for the words “means a person not less than eighteen years of age as at first 
June of the year in which the revision of the operative electoral register commenced under the registration of Electors Act, 
No. 44 of 1980” of the words “means a person not less than eighteen years of age as at first June 2024 (i. e. born on or before 
thirty-first September 2006)” to be commensurate with Section 20A(1)(a) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980.
