CONSTITuTION Of ThE DEmOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPubLIC Of SRI LANkA
Order under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution
IN  terms of section 18 of the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980, the main electoral register for the year 2023 
related to the revision of the electoral registers for the year 2023 was certified on 31st October 2023. 
   02. wHereas main electoral register 2023, supplementary electoral register 2023(1) and supplementary electoral 
register 2023(2) remained in effect as on October 31, 2023, in pursuance of the powers vested in the Election Commission 
under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election Commission by 
this Order certify that the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members which each 
electoral district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the 
Proclamation under article 97 and article 98.
