L.D.-B. 3/2024
THE  RECOGNITION  AND  ENFORCEMENT  OF  INTERNATIONAL  SETTLEMENT  
AGREEMENTS  RESULTING  FROM  MEDIATION  ACT,  No.  5  OF  2024
Order under  subsection (2) of section 1
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (2) of section 1 of the Recognition and Enforcement of International 
Settlement Agreements Resulting from Mediation Act, No. 5 of 2024, I, Dr. Wijayadasa Rajapaksha, Minister of Justice, 
Prison Affairs and Constitutional Reforms, do by this Order certify August 28, 2024 as the date on which the United Nations 
Convention on International Settlement Agreements Resulting from Mediation enters into force in respect of Sri Lanka.
 Dr. W
IJAYADASA RAJAPAKSHA,
 Minister of Justice, Prison Affairs and
 Constitutional Reforms.
   
Colombo,
15th July, 2024.
