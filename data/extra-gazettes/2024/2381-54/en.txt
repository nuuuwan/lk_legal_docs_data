PART IV (B) — LOcAL  GOVERNmENT
Local Government Notifications
Kolonna  PradeshIya  sabha
The roads and lanes which are deem to be owned by kolonna pradeshiya sabha in which are relevant notification is included 
published in the Gazette No. 2364/39 dated. 31.12.2023 under the terms made by section 24 of pradeshiya sabha act no. 15 
of 1987.as the "orders on the preparation of plans and posting of notices showing the list of names of the roads and lanes" 
maintained by the Pradeshiya sabha of Sabaragamuwa Province as the Honourable Governor of sabaragamuwa Province has 
publish in the special Gazette No. 2316/20 dated 24 January 2023 and it is hereby published for the public that it has been 
decided under decision No. 18 of 03rd April 2024 that all said road and lanes shall be owned by kolonna pradeshiya sabha 
as because of any case has not been assigned in a competent court on the date fixed by the said notification 
   it is further announced that all roads and lanes included therein shall be deemed to be roads and lanes established 
by the kolonna pradeshiya sabha under sub section (5) of section 24 of pradeshiya sabha act list of those road and lanes is 
included in the schedule here
   
 P. A. Malika,
 Secretary and the implementation officer,
 carrying out the functions,
 Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha.
