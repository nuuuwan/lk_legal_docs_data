SRI LANKA TELECOMMUNICATIONS ACT, No. 25 OF 1991
REGuLATIONS made by the Minister of Technology under section 71(1) read with Section 22 of the Sri Lanka 
Telecommunications Act, No. 25 of 1991 and as amended by the Sri Lanka Telecommunications Act, No. 27 of 1996 and 
Act, No. 39 of 2024.
  Ranil Wickremesinghe,
 President, 
  Minister of Technology.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Regulations for Satellite Broadband Service and shall come into effect from 
Tuesday, August 13, 2024.
2. Licence fees for the use of radio frequency or radio frequency emitting  apparatus by a Satellite Broadband Service 
including user terminals are as setout in the Schedule hereto.
3. In these Regulations, unless the context otherwise requires - “Licence” shall mean a licence issued under Section 22 
of the Act.
