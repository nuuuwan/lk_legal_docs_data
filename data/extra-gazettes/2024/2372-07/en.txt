RULES
the rules published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 305 of July 6th 1984 as amended from time to time and last amended by 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 2117/17of april 3, 2019 are hereby further amended as follows :-
1. In rule 19 -
 (a) by the repeal of paragraph (1) thereof and the substitution therefor, of the following paragraph : -
“ (1) Where a member loses his membership in the Corporation under paragraph 1 of rule 8, such member 
shall be paid a sum of rupees fifteen thousand rupees (rs.15000/-) (hereinafter referred to as the Honorary 
Gratuity) for each and every month for a period of six months from the date of retirement totally amounting to 
ninety thousand rupees (rs.90,000/-)”;
 (b) by the repeal of sub paragraph (4) thereof and the substitution therefor, of the following sub paragraph -
 “(4) every member who wishes to become entitled for the Honorary Gratuity shall make a contribution of 
rupees four thousand five Hundred (rs.4500/-) per annum at the rate of rupees three Hundred and seventy 
five (Rs 375/-) per month for a period of ten years. All recoveries in respect of honorary gratuity shall be 
deposited in a fixed deposit in a State Bank. The interest accrued from the fixed deposit shall be credited to the 
honorary gratuity savings account and shall be used for the payment of honorary gratuity;”
2. In rule 21 -
 (a) by the repeal of paragraph (1) thereof and substitution therefor, of the following paragraph -
“ festival Loans  (l)  (a)  any member who has completed a period of one year as a member and wishes to 
obtain festival loan shall be entitled to such loan of rupees twenty-five thousand 
(rs. 25000/-) once a year. any member who has obtained the said festival loan shall 
settle such amount together with an interest at the rate of twelve per centum (12%) 
in ten monthly installments of Rupees Two Thousand and five Hundred (Rs 2500/-).
  (b)  No member shall be entitled to obtain a festival loan, until any previous festival loan 
obtained by him if any , is fully settled by such member .
