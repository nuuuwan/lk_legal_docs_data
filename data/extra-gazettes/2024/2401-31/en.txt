iN exercise of the powers conferred to me, M. Senthil Thondaman as the Governor of the Eastern Province, I hereby notified 
that under section 154C 13 of the thirteenth amendment of the Constitution of the democratic socialist republic of  
Sri Lanka, with effect from 11.09.2024, the 1128 meters long coastal walkway of the Inner Harbour Road under the Jurisdiction 
of trincomalee municipal Council as shown in schedule 1 below will be changed as shown in schedule 2.
Schedule 1 Schedule 2
this particular walkway was being used without any name 
being mentioned.

breeze walkway" in the future.
This walkway starts at 60 meters from the Port Police 
Station (Location Point 8.5679427, 81.2311127) and ends at 
a distance of 51 meters from The Sinhala Maha Vidyalaya 
(Location Point 8.5757138, 81.2252488).
This walkway is located 60 meters away from the Port Police 
Station (Location point 8.5679427, 81.2311127) starts 
from stupa, naming inscription and historical inscription 
and ends at stupi and naming inscription constructed 
51 meters away from the Sinhala Maha Vidyalaya (Location
