Excise  Notification No. 05/2024
REVIsION  OF  THE  CONdITIONs  sET  OUT  IN  THE  EXCIsE  NOTIFICaTION  No.  666
By virtue of the powers vested in me under section 25 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to 
time, I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, Minister of Finance, Economic stabilization and National Policies do hereby amend the 
Schedule in the Excise Notification No. 666 of the Extraordinary Gazette dated december 31, 1979 as amended by Excise 
Notification No. 04/2018 of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2054/42, dated January 18, 2018, with effect from  
august 14, 2024 as follows;
(1) substituting sub-paragraph (v) of paragraph 5 of the following sub-paragraph,
  “(v) any persons under the age of eighteen years.”, and
(2) Repealing sub-paragraph (d) of paragraph 11.
