INTERPRETATION  ORDINANCE
Notice of cancellation of the Gazette Notification No. 2351/02 dated 25.09.2023 cancelling the name of "Sri Lanka 
Swadeshiya Vaidya Sabawa" under Section 18 of the Interpretation Ordinance.
i, dr. ramesh Pathirana, the Minister of industries, hereby  notify that Gazette Notification bearing No. 2351/02 dated 
25.09.2023 striking off the society named " sri Lanka swadeshiya Vaidya sabawa" which is registered under section 3 
and section 4 of the societies Ordinance (123 chapter) and published under Gazette dated 2317/70-2023 and cancelling the 
name thereof, is hereby cancelled.
