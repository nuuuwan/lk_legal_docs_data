CONSTITuTION  Of ThE DEmOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPubLIC  Of SRI LANkA
Order under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution
IN  terms of section 18 of the registration of electors act, No. 44 of 1980, the main electoral register for the year 2023 
related to the revision of the electoral registers for the year 2023 was certified on 31st October 2023. 
   02. whereas main electoral register 2023, supplementary electoral register 2023(1) supplementary electoral register 
2023(2) and supplementary electoral register 2023(3)  remained in effect as on February 01, 2024, in pursuance of the 
powers vested in the Election Commission under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of 
Sri Lanka, the Election Commission by this Order certify that the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the 
number of members which each electoral district specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is 
entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation under Article 97 and Article 98.
