Regulations

No. 24/1706/601/102 dated august 26, 2024, of H.e. the president; minister of Finance, economic stabilization and 
National policies and minister of Investment promotion.

3.	 these regulations are issued as an integral part of the regulations of the Imports and exports (Control) regulations 
No. 02 of 2023, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2312/78 dated January 01, 2023, as subsequently amended.
4.	 Notwithstanding the regulation No. 4 of the Imports and exports (Control) regulations No. 02 of 2023 referred 
above, an enterprise, established and operating under the board of Investment (bOI) of sri Lanka, shall be allowed to 
Import and Customs clearance of unregistered (Brand-new) Semi Knocked Down (SKD) kits, classified under the HS 
Headings of 87.02, 87.03 and 87.04 to assemble motor vehicles by such enterprise within its motor vehicle assembly 
facility as per the provisions of the agreement, entered into between such enterprise and the board of Investment of 
sri Lanka and duly approved by the Cabinet of ministers, by the director General of sri Lanka Customs on 
recommendation of the secretary, the ministry in charge of  the board of Investment of sri Lanka. 
as per provisions of the said agreement, no unregistered (brand-new) semi-Knocked down kits of motor vehicles 
with the engine cylinder capacity exceeding 3000cc shall be allowed.
5.	 t o achieve the objectives of these regulations, the Controller General of Imports and exports shall issue “Operational 
Instructions” to the director General of Customs, licensed banks or any other relevant authorities.
6.	 The final decision shall be made by the Controller General of Imports and Exports on any issues arising in relation to 
these regulations.
