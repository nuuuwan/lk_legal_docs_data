RAILWAY DEPARTMENT
GENERAL MANAGER'S OFFICE
Revocation of the Steering Committee Pursuant to 30 a (1) of the 
Railway Daily-Paid Benevolent Association act No. 3 of 1965
DUE  to financial misappropriation and anti-constitutional affairs of the Steering Committee, including  the President, Vice 
President, Secretary and the Treasurer of Railway Daily - paid Benevolent Association, elected at the 46th Anniversary, held 
on 24.07.2022, the said steering committee is dispersed by the order of the letter of the Secretary to the Ministry of Transport 
and Highways, bearing the number MT/03/05/21/17-1 and dated of 15.02.2024 as per the authority of the Secretary to  the 
Ministry of Transport and Highways pursuant to the clause 30A(1) in the aforesaid Act. 
A special committee with the following members will be appointed pursuant to the clause 30A(1) in the said Act.
1. Mr. G. A. Krisantha - Superintendent Engineer - President
2. Mr. W. A. P. Dharmasena - Superintendent Engineer (Lower) - Member
3. Mr. H. M. V . B. Siriwardhana - Motive Power Engineer (E. L. S) - Member
 H. M. K. W. Bandara,
 General Manager - Railways,
General Manager Railways Office,
Colombo 10.
28th February, 2024.
