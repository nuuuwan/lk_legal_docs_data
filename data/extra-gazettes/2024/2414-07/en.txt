Order under Section 114
WHereAs the trustees of  “ivan Dissanayake charitable trust” namely-
 1. mr. Lalith ivan Dissanayake ;
 2. ms. srivanthi Arosha Perera; 
 3. ms. meyasi Prathiba Dissanayake; and
 4. ms. Nipuni bhagya Dissanayake,
   have applied to the minister of Justice and National integration for their incorporation under section 114 of the 
trusts Ordinance (chapter 87);
   Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in by section 114 of the aforesaid Ordinance, i, Harshana Nanayakkara, 
minister of Justice and National integration, do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said trustees and direct 
and declare that –
 (a) the trustees of the said trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the Deed 
of trust bearing No. 1365 of march 01, 2019 attested by madawala Liyanage tissa Liyanage Notary Public of 
Gampaha, shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal under the name and style of 
the “ivan Dissanayake chartitable trust”;
