SERvICE   MINuTE  Of  ThE  MANAGEMENT  SERvICE  OffICERS  SERvICE  -  AMENDMENT  No. 04
the Public Service Commission has ordered to amend the Service Minute of the Management Service Officers Service 
Published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1840/34 dated 11 december 2013 as amended by the below mentioned Gazettes 
Extraordinary of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows.
amendment No. 01 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2032/26 dated 16 august 2017
amendment No. 02 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2052/26 dated 04 January 2018
amendment No. 03 - Gazette Extraordinary No. 2140/4 dated 09 September 2019
 1. Section 7.2.2.1 (a) of the Service minute should be amended as follows.
 7.2.2.1 Educational Qualifications
 (a) Should have passed six (06) subject with credit passes for four subjects including Language (Sinhala/tamil/
english) and mathematics at the G.C.e. (ordinary Level) examination at one sitting.
 (the medium of language in which the applicant sits the competitive examination for the recruitment to the 
Management Service Officers Service shall be considered as the Language for which a credit pass should have 
been obtained at the G.C.e (ordinary Level) examination.)
 2. Following Note should be inserted below the Section 7.2.2.1 (b) of the Service minute
Note: The final decision on making applicable a qualification obtained at an examination equivalent to G.C.E.  (Ordinary 
Level) or G.C.e. (advanced Level) examinations conducted by other local or foreign educational institutions for granting 
the appointment shall be made by the Director General of Combined Services upon the verification from the relevant 
educational institutions and Commissioner General of examinations.
