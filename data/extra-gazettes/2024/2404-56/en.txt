EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Orders issued by the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance by virtue of the 
powers vested in him by the Section 25 read with Section 32 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended, and published 
as Excise Notification, were passed by Parliament.
(i) Excise Notification No. 01/2024, Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2366/38 dated January 12, 2024 was 
passed by Parliament on July 10, 2024.
(ii) Excise Notification No. 02/2024, Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2366/39 dated January 12, 2024 was 
passed by Parliament on August 21, 2024. 
(iii) Excise Notification No. 03/2024, Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2369/32 dated February 01, 2024 
was passed by Parliament on July 10, 2024.
 K. M. Mahinda Siriwardana,
 Secretary,
 Ministry of Finance, Economic Development, 
 Policy Formulation, Planning and Tourism.
