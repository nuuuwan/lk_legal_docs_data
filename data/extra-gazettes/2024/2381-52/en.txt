01. Lot No. 1

02. Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: Horahenkele, Shantha Jorge state; 
Description: Garden; Water tank and permanent building; 
Claimant: State; Origina l Claimant: Land Reform 
Commission; Extent: 0.1195  Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of  
Mathugama on the 02nd day of September 2024 at                 
2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of August 
2024 the nature of their interests in the land. The 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/ servitude , the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
