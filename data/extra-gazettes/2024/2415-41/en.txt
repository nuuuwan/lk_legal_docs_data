KURUWITA   PRADESHIYA    SABHA
ACCORDING to the task assigned to the Pradeshiya Sabha in Sabaragamuwa Province, from the series ''Orders on publication 
of notices showing the list of names and planning of roads and narrow lanes maintained by Pradeshiya Sabha’’ published in the 
special gazette No. 2316/20 and dated 24th of January 2022 published by Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province to be read 
with Section 24 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, I do hereby publish to the notice of public that it has been decided 
under the decision No 39 and dated 05.12.2024 of the Secretary under Section 9(3) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act. No. 15 of 1987, 
that the roads and narrow lanes included in the following schedule are owned by the Kuruwita Pradeshiya Sabha.
It is hereby inform that if any person is in a opinion that a certain road or narrow lane included in that schedule go through any 
land or included in any land, before the expiration of the three-months period from the date of this announcement, a case must 
be ﬁ  led in the appropriate court to verify the entitlement  for the said land by the person who claims for that land.
Furthermore, such a case has not been assigned before the end of that three-month period, it is further announce that every road 
and narrow lane included in that schedule are acquired by Kuruvita Pradeshiya Sabha.
