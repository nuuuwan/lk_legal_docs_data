NA VY ACT (CHAPTER 358)
Order under Section 21
By  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 21 of the Navy Act (Chapter 358), I, Ranil Wickremesinghe, President 
of the democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, having considered necessary in the national interest, do hereby order 
the Commander of the Sri Lanka Navy to exercise, perform and discharge the powers, duties and functions of the National 
Hydrographic Office as an ex-officio member of the National Hydrographic Council under the provisions of Section 6. (1) 
(a) (v) of the National Hydrographic Act, No. 7 of 2024, as non-naval duties.
 Ranil Wickremesinghe,
 President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
