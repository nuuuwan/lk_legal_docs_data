Order under Section  27(1)(b) read with Section 25A
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section  27(1)(b) read with section 25a of the Universities act, No. 16 of 1978, I,  
dr. a. d. susil Premajayantha, the minister of education having obtained a report under section 70C of the aforesaid  act, 
in respect of the National school of Business management Limited (NsBm), a company incorporated in sri Lanka under the 
Companies act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing Registration Number PB 4833 and located at mahenwatte, Pitipana, Homagama 
and, being recognized as a degree awarding Institute, do by this Order amend the schedule II to the Order made under the 
said act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2356/32 of November 03, 2023, by the addition immediately after 
entry “Bachelor of management Honours in Law and Business studies” appearing in Column I and its corresponding entry 
in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and II respectively of the schedule II hereto, subject to the 
conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
