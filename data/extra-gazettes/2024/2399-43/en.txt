By virtue of powers vested in me as the governor of uva Province in accordance with sections 32 (1) and 32 (3) of Provincial 
Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987, 1, A. J. M. Muzammil, hereby announce that this uva Provincial Council Public service 
Disciplinary Code will come into effect from 01st of September 2024 to make provisions for disciplinary control of officers 
of the uva Provincial Council Public service and to provide for matters related therewith and incidental thereto.
   the disciplinary Code of uva Provincial Council Public service published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1762/33 
of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka dated 14.06.2012 and in effect since 01.06.2012, will be cancelled as of 
01st september 2024, in a manner that does not prejudice any decisions or actions taken or deemed to have been taken under 
it. the provisions of this new disciplinary Code. will not be an obstruction with any disciplinary actions that have been 
initiated or concluded in accordance with the provisions of that disciplinary Code.
