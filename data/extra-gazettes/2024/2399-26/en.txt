THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT,  CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956  REVISED  EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of 
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial 
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 02.03.2021 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2218/3 dated 08.03.2021 of the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified 
in the statement of the Commissioner General of Labour dated 01.03.2021.
     Mrs. P. Padmini Fernando, No. 02/02, Daham Pasal Mawatha, Wewala, Piliyandala  
 and
      Buddhist and Pali University of Sri Lanka, No. 37, Moragahahena Road, Pitipana, Homagama
was referred to Mr. Janajitha De Silva Attorney-at-law for settlement by arbitration. 
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, due to Mr. Janajith De Silva Attorney - at - Law, 
the Arbitrator informing that he is resigning from the post of Arbitratior, I, Manusha Nanayakkara, Minister of L abour and 
Forign Employment do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said 
Order dated 02.03.2021.
 Manusha Nanayakkara,
 Minister of L abour and Foreign Employment.
Colombo.
01st August 2024,

STATEMENT  OF  MATTER  IN  DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. D. M. Disanayaka who works as a Driver in the Sri Lanka Transport Board, whose service had been temporarily
