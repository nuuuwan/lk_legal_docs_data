The Order
by virtue of powers vested in me under sub section 4(2) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 
16 of 1907, as amended by act, No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of
