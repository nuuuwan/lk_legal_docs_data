CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI LANKA
Order under Article 98 (8) of the Constitution
IN terms of the provisions of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amendment by the Registration of Electors 
(Amendments) Act, No. 22 of 2021, it was notified in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2404/18 dated 01.10.2024 that the 
supplementary electoral register 2024 (2) pertaining to the period commencing from the first day of June 2024 to the thirtieth 
day of September 2024 was certified on the first day of October 2024.
    
   02.  Main register 2024, supplementary electoral register 2024(1) & supplementary electoral register 2024(2)  remained 
in force as of 01st July  2024 and accordingly in pursuance of the powers vested in the Election Commission under Article 98 
(8) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Election Commission by this Order certify that 
the number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members which each electoral district specified in 
the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the Proclamation under Article 
97 and Article 98.
