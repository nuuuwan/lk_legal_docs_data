 FOREST  CONSERvATION  ORDINANCE
THE order under sub section 3(1) that has to be read with sub section 4(1) of the Forest Conservation Ordinance  
(Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by Act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, 
No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009.
The Order 
By virtue of the powers vested in me under sub section 3(1) that has to be read with sub section 4(1) of the Forest Conservation 
Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by Act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 of 1982,  
No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation do by 
this Order declare the state lands of the extent 822.3 hectares bounded by the limits set forth in the Schedule 1 hereto as 
Velankulama Reserved Forest from 29th day of August 2024, excluding the lands of the extent 0.7 hectares bounded by the 
limits set forth in the Schedule 2, located within the limits given in schedule 1.
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi,
 Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation. 
Ministy of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation,
No. 07, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Colombo 07,
29th day of August 2024.
