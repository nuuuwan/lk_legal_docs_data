ThE  CENSuS   ORDINANCE  (ChAPTER  143)
Hon. Mahinda Rajapaksa who was then the Minister of economic Policies and Plan Implementation, under sections 6 and 7 
of the Census Ordinance (Chapter 143), appointed district secretary of each administrative district hereto, to be in addition 
to own duties as the Commissioner of Census for the District, and two other Senior Officers in each District Secretariat were 
appointed as deputy Commissioner of Census and assistant Commissioner of Census respectively, and those appointments 
were published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2264/8 dated on 25th of January 2022.
   Out of these appointments, appointments for the districts mentioned in column 1 of this schedule has been amended 
as mentioned in columns 2 and 3 of this schedule by Hon. Ranil Wickremesinghe, the Minister of Finance, economic 
stabilization and National Policies,
 K. M. Mahinda Siriwardana,
  secretary

 and National Policies.
