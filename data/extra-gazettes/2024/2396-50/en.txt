The Order 
By virtue of the powers vested in me under sub section 3(1) that has to be read with sub section 4(1) of the Forest 
Conservation Ordinance (Chapter 451) No. 16 of 1907, as amended by act No. 13 of 1966 (reprint), No. 56 of 1979, No. 13 
of 1982, No. 84 of 1988, No. 23 of 1995 and No. 65 of 2009, I, Minister of Wildlife and Forest resources Conservation do 
by this Order declare the state lands of the extent 38.0 hectares bounded by the limits set forth in the schedules No. 1 & 2 
hereto as Niliyamote 03 reserved Forest from 07th day of august,2024. 
 
 
Pa vithra Wanniarachchi,
