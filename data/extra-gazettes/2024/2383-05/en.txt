ThE  FAuNA  AND  FLORA  PROTECTION   ORDINANCE   (ChAPTER   469)
Order under Section 2
By virtue of the powers vested in me by subsections (1) and (4) of section 2 of the Fauna and Flora Protection ordinance 
(Chapter 469), I, Pavithra Vanniarachchi, Minister of Wildlife and Forest Resources Conservation, do by this order declare 
that the area of land described in the Schedule hereto shall, with effect from the date of publication of this Order, cease to 
be a part of the “Wedithalathive Nature Reserve” described in the Schedule to the order published in 
Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 1956/13 of March 01, 2016 and that the remaining area of land after exclusion of the area described in the Schedule 
hereto shall be called and known as the “Wedithalathive Nature Reserve”.
                                                                         Minister of Wildlife and Forest  Resources Conservation.
Colombo,
03rd May 2024.
