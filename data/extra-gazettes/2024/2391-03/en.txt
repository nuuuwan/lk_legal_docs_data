REVISION  OF  ELECTORAL  REGISTERS   
Notice that the main Electoral Register for the year 2024 has been certified under the 
Registration  of  Electors  Act
IN terms of Section 20(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, notice is given on behalf of the Registering Officer of each 
electoral district that the Main Electoral Register 2024 has been certified on 01 July 2024 in accordance with the Registration 
of Electors act No. 44 of 1980 ; that the electoral register for the electoral districts mentioned in column I of the schedule 
below has been certified by the Assistant Registering Officer in the corresponding entry in column II thereof; that inspection 
may be made during normal office hours at the District Election Office/offices mentioned in the corresponding entry in 
Column III thereof.
 Saman Sri Ratnayake,
 Commissioner General of 
 Elections,
 Election Commission.
On 01st July, 2024,
At the Elections Secretariat, Rajagiriya.
