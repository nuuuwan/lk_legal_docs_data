fILLING  A vACANCY  uNDER  SECTION  64(5) Of ThE PARLIAmENTARY  ELECTIONS  
ACT,  No.  01 Of 1981
WhErEas, the General secretary of Parliament has informed the Election Commission, under section 64(5) of the 
Parliamentary Elections act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by the Elections (special Provisions) act, No. 35 of 1988, that a 
vacancy in the membership of Parliament has occurred due to the determination of the supreme Court in the application No. 
s. C. appeal No. 11/ 2024, that Mrs. diana Gamage, who was nominated by the samagi Jana Balawegaya, a recognized 
political party, and was declared elected as a Member of Parliament under article 99a of the Constitution of the democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is disqualified to be a Member of Parliament in terms of Article 91 (1) (d) (xiii) read with 
Article 66 (d) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and is thus not entitled to hold office as 
a Member of Parliament of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka ;
 Whereas, the Election Commission has directed the secretary of the aforesaid political party to nominate a member 
of that party who is qualified under the said Section to fill the said vacancy;
    And whereas, the secretary of the said political party has nominated Mr. Mohamad Mujibur Rahman to fill the 
vacancy;

 The Election Commission, acting under section 64(5) of the Parliamentary Elections act, No. 1 of 1981 read with 
provisions of the Constitution of the democratic socialist republic of sri Lanka, announces that Mr. Mohamad Mujibur 
Rahman was declared elected as a Member of Parliament to fill the vacancy created due to the determination of the Supreme
