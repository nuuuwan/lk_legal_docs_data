CONSTITuTION   Of  ThE  DEmOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPubLIC   Of  SRI  LANkA 
Notification 
It  is hereby notified that the Hon. President, having consulted with the Hon. Prime Minister under Article 44(3) of the 
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has determined that the ministerial portfolios indicated 
below should be in his charge.
 1. Minister of Tourism and Lands
 2. Minister of Sports and Youth Affairs
 3. Minister of Labour and Foreign Employment
