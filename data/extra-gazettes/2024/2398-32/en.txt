ELPITIYA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  ELECTION - 2024
Display of Electoral Register
IN terms of Section 12D of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), it is hereby notified that the Electoral 
Register prepared in relation to the said Local Authority and certified on 22.08.2024, in terms of Section 12B of the said 
Ordinance from and out of the Consolidated Electoral Register prepared for the year 2024 and certified on 08th July 2024 as 
per the provisions of the registration of Persons Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the registration of Persons (Amendment) 
Act, No. 22 of 2021 and valid for the purpose of Parliamentary elections, are put on display at elpitiya Pradeshiya sabha, 
for inspection free of charge during office hours.
