01. This Statute may be cited as “the Eastern Provincial Road Passenger Transport Authority
(Amendment) Statute No. 01 of 2014” and shall come into effect from the date on which the
assent of the Governor of the Eastern Province is sought.
02. Section 36 Sub section (2) and paragraph (a) of the Eastern Provincial Road Passenger
Transport Authority Statute No. 02 of 2009 (hereinafter referred to as the Principle Statute)
are hereby amended as follows ;
“(2) The following receipts of the Authority shall first and foremost be credited to the
Provincial Fund and thereafter paid to the Account based on the necessity of the
Authority.
(a) Issuing or renewing the permits by the Authority in the event of exercising and
performing its power and duties and the receipts thereof”
03. In the event of any inconsistency between the Tamil and Sinhala texts of this Statute, the
Tamil text shall prevail
