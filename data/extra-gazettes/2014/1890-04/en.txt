FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
HORANA   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Horana Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending more than three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the former member,  Ashok Krishanthan Nikalas belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that
Horana Urban Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Donmanuwelge Nalin
Chinthaka being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of  Horana Urban Council  acting in terms
of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of V acancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Donmanuwelge Nalin Chinthaka elected as a
member of the  Horana Urban Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
 Horana Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
21st  November,  2014.
