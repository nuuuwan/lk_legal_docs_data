PUBLIC are hereby informed that the Development Plan prepared under Section 8A of the Urban Development Authority

Dambulla Development Area, having considered the recommendations made by the Board of Management of the Urban
Development Authority on 13.11.2008

AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
