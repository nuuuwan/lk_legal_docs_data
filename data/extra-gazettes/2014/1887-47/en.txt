THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 Notice under Section 7 
  
I, hereby give notice, under Subsection (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of 0.0017 
Hectare, shown in Prelimin ary Plan No. GAM. 3937 
prepared by Survey Superintendent of Gampaha District, 
situated at Wanawasala village in Minor Division Ram 
Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division in 
Kelaniya in Gampaha District in the Western Province. 
 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Assessment, No. not known; 
Description: Waste Land; Cl aimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Encroached by W. A. Vialot, No. 29, 
Wanawasala Road, Thorana Junction, Kelaniya; Extent: 
0.0003 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of the Land: Assessment, No. not known; 
Description: Waste Land; Cl aimant: State; Original 
Claimant: W. A. Vialot, No. 29, Wanawasala Road, 
Thorana Junction, Kelaniya; Extent: 0.0010 Hectare. 
3. Lot No. 03 
Name of the Land: Assessment, No. not known; 
Description: Waste Land; Cl aimant: State; Original 
Claimant: K. W. Sureka Chandani, No. 29, Wanawasala 
Road, Thorana Junction, Kelaniya; Extent: 0.0004 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat,  
Kelaniya on the 20th day of November, 2014 at 10.00           
a. m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 13th day of November, 
2014 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
