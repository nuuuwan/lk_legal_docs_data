THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Minarakkadu in total extent of about
2.7235 Hectares depicted in the Advanced Tracing No. Ma/MNN/
2013/091 dated 30.12.2013 prepared by the Superintendent of
Surveys-V avuniya District, situated in the Village called Sampethurai
in the Grama Niladhari Division No. MN/64 of Erukkalampiddy
West in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Mannar Town of
Mannar District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Minarakkadu – Extent: About 2.7235 Hectares
North: Land claimed by Bishop of Mannar and Puthukudiyiruppu
Fishermen Society and Sea shore; East: Sea shore and land claimed
by Bishop of Mannar, Abdul Hameem, Mohamed Ali Jahufer and
Mohomed Abdul Carder; South and West: Land claimed by Bishop
of Mannar, Abdul Hameem, Mohamed Ali Jahufer and Mohomed
Abdul Carder.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: 4/3/4/2013/D/299.
D.S Reference No.: AO/MN/02/06/2013.
