NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A (1) (a) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal  Thalangama, Returning Officer of the Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha do
hereby declare under Section 10A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. M. P. S.
Samaraweera Pathirana of the Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as
he has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kuliyapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
21st April, 2014.
