SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification under the Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987 read with Section 02 of the Provincial
Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989 and Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the
Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989
I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Health and
Indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment, News,
Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do hereby appoint the retired Judicial Officer , retired High Court
Judge Hon. Saman Wickrama Wickramarachchi, as a single person tribunal of inquiry  as per the powers vested in me by the
provisions of Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989 of
the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  to inquire and submit a report based on the matters under Section 2(1) of the
aforementioned statute,  regarding functions of the Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha of the Kegalle District,
Mr. P.P. Wickramasinghe.
02.  I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government,
Health and Indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment,
News, Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do hereby suspend with immediate effect, Mr. P.P.
Wickramasinghe, Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha of the Kegalle District, functioning as the Chairman of the
aforesaid Pradeshiya Sabha, as per the powers vested in me by the provisions of Section 03 of the Powers of Supervision of
the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989 of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
03. As the functions of Mr.P.P. Wickramasinghe as the Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha has been
suspended with immediate effect under the provisions of Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No.15 of 1987 read with
the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989 and Section 03 of the Powers of Supervision of the
Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989, I do hereby order Mr. Mohomed Sally Mohomed Kamel, Deputy
Chairman of the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha to execute the powers and carry out the tasks thereof, under the Section 3 (a)
(i) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989, Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989 and Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987.
M
AHEEPALA HERATH,
Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and
Planning, Local Government, Health and indigenous Medicine,
Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and
Estate Welfare, Environment, News, Housing and
Construction  of the Sabaragamuwa Province.
On this 03rd of June, 2014,
Chief Ministry of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
New Town, Ratnapura.
