THE   LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No. 1  OF   1972
Order  under Subsection  (4) of Section 27A
BY  VIRTUE  of the powers vested in me by subsection (4) of Section 27A of the land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as amended
by the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby -
(a) amend the Order, made under subsection (1) of Section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 and published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 183/10 of March 12, 1982 relating to the land called and known as Hantane Estate
morefully described in the First Schedule to this Order and which had vested in the Janatha Estate Development
Board established by the State Agricultural Corporation Act, No. 11 of 1972, by revoking, on grounds of
non-compliance with such terms and conditions relating to consideration for the vesting in such Board, such
portion of the said Order as relates to the extent of land called Hantane Estate specified in the Second Schedule
; and
(b) State that upon revocation, such extent of land as specified in the Second Schedule to this order, shall re-vest in
the Land Reform Commission.
