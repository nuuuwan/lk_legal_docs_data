THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (2)  allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0013 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. KA/KLT/2013/25 dated  28.02.2013,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of  Nagoda

Kalutara and  bounded as follows.
1. Lot ‘A’ –  Pitagoneliya – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North: Pitagoneliya claimed by E. P. Weerasekara; East:
Pitagoneliya claimed by U. Munasinghe, Lot B and Road; South:
Lot No. 18 of pp No. 4317; West: Road.

North: Pitagoneliya claimed by U. Munasinghe; East: Pitagoneliya
claimed by U. A. Amarasinghe and Road; South: Road; West: Lot
No. 18 of pp No. 4317, Lot A and Pitagoneliya claimed by E. P.
Weerasekara .
