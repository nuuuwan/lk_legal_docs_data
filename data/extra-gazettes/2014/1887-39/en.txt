EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
THE ANTIQUITIES ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 188)
Notification Under Section 33
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 33 of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188), I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Senerath Bandara Dissanayake, Director General of Archaeology, being satisfied that the provisions of the aforesaid section
have been complied with, do by this notification declare the area of state land specified in the Schedule to this Order, shall
deemed to be an “archaeological reserve” for the purposes of the aforesaid Odinance.
S
ENERATH DISSANAYAKE,
Director General of Archaeology.
Colombo,
05th Novemebr, 2014.
SCHEDULE
A
REA COMPRISING THE VILGAM VEHERA SOMAWATHI RAJA MAHA VIHARAYA ARCHAEOLOGICAL RESERVE
All that divided and defined allotment of land called the Vilgam Vehera Somawathi Raja Maha Viharaya Archaeological site
depicted as lot Number 415 (Inset No. 29) in the Final Village Plan depicted in the Topography Priliminary Plan No. 22
containing in extent 49 Acres, 3 Roods and 21 Perches situated in the Seruvila Grama Niladhari Division in the Seruvilla
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Trincomalee District in Eastern Province.
