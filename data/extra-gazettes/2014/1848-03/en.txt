EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBERSHIP  OF  THE  AMBALANGODA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Ambalangoda Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom  Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Mr. Millawithanachchi Rahula Lalithawickrama belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Ambalangoda Urban Council who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mrs. Juwanwadu Bhashini
Menuka Wijesooriya being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Ambalangoda Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs. Juwanwadu Bhashini Menuka Wijesooriya
elected as a member of the  Ambalangoda Urban Council.
A. B. I. De S
ILV A
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Galle,
23rd January, 2014.
