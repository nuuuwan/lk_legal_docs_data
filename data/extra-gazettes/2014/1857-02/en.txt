NOTIFICATION
Proclamation by the Governor of the  Southern Province
BY virtue of the  powers vested in me by Article 154  B,  Section 08 Sub-Section (a) of the Thirteenth   Amendment to the
Constitution of  the Democratic Socialist  Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasuriya,   Governor of the
Southern  Province, do hereby Summon the Seventh Provincial Council of  the Southern Province to meet at 10.00 a.m. on the
11th day of April 2014, in the Council Chamber of  the Southern Provincial Council, Kalegana, Galle.
NERANJALA PUSHPA KUMARI BALASURIYA,
Governor of the Southern  Province.
Given at Galle this 3rd day of April Two Thousand and Fourteen.
