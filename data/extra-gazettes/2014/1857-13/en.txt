  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Death;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party to which the member belonged
was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Abubakker Hasheem
Mohomad Ibraheem  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare  Mr. Abubakker Hasheem Mohomad Ibraheem, elected as
a member of the   above  Provincial Council from the Administrative District of  Matale  in respect of which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
08th April, 2014.

  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Northern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Northern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Northern
Province Provincial Council by reason of Expulsion from the Party;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Arupillai Sinnadurai
Thavarajah  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare  Mr. Arupillai Sinnadurai Thavarajah, elected as a member
of the  above  Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Jaffna  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09th April, 2014.
