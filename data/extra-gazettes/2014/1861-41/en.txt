NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a) OF  THE    LOCAL   AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha do
hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Anura Udaya Munasinghe
Munasinghe Arachchige of the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as
he has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
V ALIKAMAM  SOUTH   WEST   PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL
AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Chairman in V alikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of
resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognised political party  to which Jebanesan
Antonypillai the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the
member of that Valikamam South West  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  a
Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Shanmugam Sivakumaaran to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Valikamam South West  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Shanmugam Sivakumaaran  elected as a   Chairman
of the Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
21st  April, 2014.
