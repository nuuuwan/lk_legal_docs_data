UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment  by the Governor
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon :  C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province.
01. Mr. D. Chandrathilaka, Officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the Duties of the Director to Road
of Uva Province, from the date 13.12.2013 to 16.12.2013, till the return Mr. A. A. P. S. K. Karunarathne, to the Island who is the
Director to Road of Uva Province.
02. Specialist Dr. Mr. I. N. W. Anandagoda, to act as Medical Officer of the Mahiyangana Base Hispital in addition
to his permanent post, from the date 09.11.2013 to 04.12.2013.
03. Mr. S. Nadaraja Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, Reappointed on contract
basis as Deputy Director to the Education of the Bandarawela Zonal Education, from the date 01.11.2013 till the appointment
of a Permanent officer or Up to 30.04.2014.
04. Mrs. R. A. H. A. Samarasinghe, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, appointed as Secretary to
the Council of the Uva Provincial Council, with effect from the date 01.01.2014.
05. Mr. W. M. M. G. Abeysinghe Banda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Deputy Chief
Secretary (Personnel & Training) for the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 01.01.2014.
06. Mr. G . A. Ariyasena, Class II officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, to perform the Duties of the Deputy
Director to Agriculture (Training) of Bindunuwewa in-service Training Institute, on full time basis, from the date 21.11.2013.
