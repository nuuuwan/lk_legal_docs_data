FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
V ALVETITHURAI   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Valvetithurai Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being Ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the former Member,  Mr. Ponnuthurai Theiventhiran   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that
Valvetithurai Urban Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Sivananthaiyar
Selvalingam  being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Ponnuthurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of  V alvetithurai Urban Council  acting in terms
of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of V acancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Sivananthaiyar Selvalingam  elected as a member
of the V alvetithurai Urban Council.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valvetithurai Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna District,
07th November,  2014.
