Provincial  Councils  Notifications
 WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Inquiry held under Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local
Authorities Statute, No. 04 of 1991 of the Western Province Provincial Council
I, Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment and Information of the Western Province Provincial
Council do hereby announce that the inquiry period will be extended to 30th September of the single person tribunal
comprising retired Judge of Court of Appeal Hon. B. L. K. Wimalachandra appointed on 30th January 2013 by the Extraordinary
Gazette Paper No. 1795/29 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as per the powers entrusted to me by the
provisions of Section 2(2) of the Powers of Supervision of the Administration of Local Authorities Statute No. 04 of 1991 of
the Western Province Provincial Council to inquire and submit a report whether the Chairman of the Meerigama Pradeshiya
Sabha, Ananda Premachandra Ranaweera, while functioning in the said office, has made the offences stated under Section
2(1) of the aforementioned statute.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
  Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance
and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Land, Local Government,
and Provincial Administration, Manpower and  Employment,
