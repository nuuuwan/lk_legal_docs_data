No. 1893/40  —  FRIDAY,    DECEMBER   19,  2014
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
DEBT   RECOVERY   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT
Notification under Section 30
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Monetary Board established under the Monetary Law Act
(Chapter 422), in terms of Section 30 of the Debt Recovery (Special Provisions) Act, No. 02 of 1990, the Monetary Board has,
having regard to the current rates of bank interest, determined that with effect from 01st January 2015, the Market Rate of
Interest for the purpose of this Act, shall be seven point seven six per cent (7.76%) per annum.
N
IV ARD  AJITH  LESLIE  CABRAAL,
Chairman of the Monetay Board and
Governor
Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
Colombo,
17th December, 2014.
