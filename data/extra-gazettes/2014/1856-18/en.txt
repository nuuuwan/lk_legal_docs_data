UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Co - operative Employees’ Commission Statute No. 04 of 2010 of Uva Provincial Council
I, Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Kumarasiri Rathnayaka as the Minister incharge of Road Development, Housing, Water
Supply, Consumer Affairs and Co - operative and Food Supply and Distribution, of Uva Province Provincial Council hereby
notice as per section 27 of aforesaid statute that, establishment code published on 2011 July 02 nd, Gazette No. 1712/36
cancelling the extraordinary Gazette No 169/8 of 1981 December 01 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Co-
operative Employees’ regulations read along with amended regulations made under Co-operative Employees’ Commission
Act No 12 of 1972, read along with co-operative Employees’ Commission (Amended) Act No 51 of 1992 as being enacted until
the regulations are published on Co-operative Societies and Employees of Uva Province as per the powers vested on me by
section 23 and 28 of the aforesaid statute.
R. M. K
UMARASIRI RATHNAYAKA,
Minister incharge of Road Development,
 Housing, Water Supply, Consumer Affairs
and Co-operative and Food Supply and
Distribution of Uva  Province Provincial Council.
At Office of the Ministry,
R. H. Gunawardena Mawatha,
Badulla.
