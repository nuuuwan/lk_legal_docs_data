FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
MADURAWALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Madurawala   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party:
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  United National Party, to which  the  Former
member  Amarasingha Kahatapitiya    belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Madurawala
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Gurusinghedapanadurage
Amal Nishantha Samarasinghe  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Madurawala   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Gurusinghedapanadurage Amal
Nishantha Samarasinghe elected as  a member of the  Madurawala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Madurawala   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kalutara,
21st  November, 2014.
