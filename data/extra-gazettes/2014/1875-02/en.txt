FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
WATTALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings of
the Council for a Period of three months without Permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the Former member Pulukkutti Arachchige Don Stanley Norbert, belonged, was required by me under Section 65 A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a
member of that  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Pulukkutti Arachchige
Don Stanley Norbert being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of   Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Pulukkutti Arachchige Don Stanley
Norbert  elected as a member of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S.  S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
07th  August,  2014.
