STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matters in dispute between the aforesaid parties are
Whether Mr. H. A. Cyril, who had served as a Technician of the Ratmalana Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board had been caused injustice
by being interdicted from service without pay from 29.07.2004 and not being paid salaries form the said date to 13.07.2010, by being
transferred and being interdicted again from 13.11.2010 to 12.05.2011, and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
Acting Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 25th day of July, 2014.
