1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
ADDALACHENAI   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Addalachenai  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation:
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, to which  the
former member  Ahamed Lebbe Zubaideen    belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Addalachenai   Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Abdul Careem
Ibralebbe  Mohamed Nazeer  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE   I, Don Thilina Ishan Wikramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Addalachenai    Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Abdul Careem Ibralebbe  Mohamed
Nazeer elected as  a member of the  Addalachenai  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WIKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Addalachenai  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
18th  November, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
KATUWANA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Katuwana  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of Three Months without permisson :
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  United National Party, to which  the
Former member  Jasinge Roshan Karunarathne  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days. a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Katuwana  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Omalpage Chandana
Thilak  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE  I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal  Darshana Chandrarathna,  Returning Officer of  Katuwana  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of  V acancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Omalpage Chandana Thilak
elected as  a member of the  Katuwana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
D
EEPAL CHANDRARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katuwana   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Hambantota,
19th  November, 2014.

Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
NA VITHANVELI   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under

Tamil Arasu Kadchi.

 Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
