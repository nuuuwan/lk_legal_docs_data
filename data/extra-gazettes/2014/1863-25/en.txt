UNITED  NATIONS  ACT,  No. 45  of  1968
Order  under  regulation  5 of the United  Nations  Regulations  No.  1  of  2012
I, Nandasena Gotabaya Rajapaksa, Competent Authority appointed for the purpose of the United Nations Regulations
No. 1 of 2012, published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1758/19 of May 15, 2012, acting in terms of the powers vested in
me by regulation 5 of the said Regulations, do by this Order, freeze all funds, other financial assets and economic resources
belonging to or owned or held by any natural or legal person, group or entity designated and listed under regulation 4 of the
above Regulations and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1854/41 of March 21, 2014.
GOTABAY A RAJAPAKSA RWP, RSP, Psc,
Secretary,
Competant Authority,
Ministry of Defence and Urban Development.
Colombo,
06th May 2014.
