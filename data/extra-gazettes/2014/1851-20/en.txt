No. 1851/20 –  WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: VSW/DS/LAND/PS-ILAVA/D. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under sub-section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the  land be acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan  :- PP 1326 
Situation :- Village:  Mathakal 
DS Division  :- Valikamam South West 
District :- Jaffna 
Province :- Northern  
 
 
01. Lot No. 02 
 
 
Name of Land: Pothipulam; Description: Garden 
contain 142 coconut trees 30-40 years, 8 mango trees                  
10-40 years, 201 Palmyra trees 10-80 years, 5 margosa 
trees 10-40 years, 15 Savuku trees 10-40 years, 4 
permanent buildings, 1 masonry well and Water tank; 
Name of the Claimant: C. R. Thissanayakam, 14, queen's 
Road, Colombo 03; Extent: 4A, 3R, 24.8P. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are  hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at  Valikamam South 
West  DS Office  on the day 04th day of April, 2014 at 
10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 26th day of  March, 
2014, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for Compensation for the 
Acquisition of the said land,  the amount of 
Compensation and the details of the Computation of such 
amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of claim should 
be produced before me at the inquiry.   
 
 S. MURALITHARAN , 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Sandilipay. 
 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Sandilipay, 
25th February, 2014.
