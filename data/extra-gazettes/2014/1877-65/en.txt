DECLARATION OF PUNCHI SIGIRIYA POOJA BHUMI SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Punchi Sigiriya sacred area", for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF PUNCHI SIGIRIYA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 7.9504 Hectares
depicted in below mentioned Part I, Part II, Part III, Part IV , Part V , Part VI, Part VII and Part VIII situated in Grama Niladari
Division named Kothmale Kolaniya in Kothmale Gramaseva Division of Wavgampaththuwa-North Division in the
Divisional Secretariat Division of Uhana of the Administrative District of Ampara in Eastern Province depicted in plan
No.M/UHN/2010/26 dated 30
th June 2010 of Surveyor General.
