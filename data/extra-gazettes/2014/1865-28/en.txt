THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the Notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (1) (a) of
Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1681/3 of  November 22, 2010 and amended by time to time is further amended with effect from June 01, 2014 in the manner
set out in the Schedule below :–
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
02nd June  2014.
SCHEDULE
1. With reference to the heading, Minister of Education (No. 37) of the said Notification, as follows :–
(a) by addition of the following items immediately after item 6 in Column I thereof,
‘‘7. Implementation and direction of the Programme for provision of Free School Text Books
 8. Implementation and direction of the Programme for the provision of Free Uniforms for Lay Students and
     Buddhist Priests in Pirivenas’’;
2. With reference to the heading, Minister of Education Services (No. 68) of the said Notification, as follows :–
(a) in Column I thereof, by the substitution for item 5 thereof of the following item :–
‘‘5. Implementation and direction of Programmes and Projects for the provision of Free Uniforms (except
Pirivenas), Footwear, Desks, Chairs and Equipment, Mid-day Meals, etc.’’;
