No. 1861/28 –  WEDNESDAY, MAY 07, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Reference No.: 03/Kata/LA/75. 
Land Ministry. Reference No.: LD/05/2009/WS/286. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a Public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired :-  
 
An allotment of Land called Pamburugaha Ovita in 
total extent of 0.0470 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in 
Preliminary Plan No. P. P. GAM 3930 prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Raddoluwa in 
Raigam Patthu Minar Division in the Divisional Secretary 
Division of Katana in Gampaha District, Western 
Province. 
 Lot No. 1  
 
Name of the land: Pamburugahaovita; Description:   
Garden with Three Coconut Tr ees (about 35 years aged); 
Claimed: Y. Ajith Silva, No. 286A, Muthuwadiya Road, 
Raddoluwa, Seeduwa; Extent:  0.0470 Hectare. 
 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat, Katana on the 04th day of June 2014, at 9.30 
a. m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 28th day of May, 2014, the 
nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 K. G. H. H. R. K IRIELLA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Katana. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Katana 
02nd April, 2014.
