LD/05/2008/PC/470  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
Kekunagolla Village, situated at 1115 Kekunagolla 
Grama Niladari Division in Maddeketiya Korale in minor 
division in Kuliyapitiya-East Divisional Secretariat in 
Kurunegala district in North Western Province.  
 
Plan No.: P.W.P. 2560; Paper: 04; Additional No.: 04. 
 
Lot No. 136  
Name of the Land: Palliyahena nor 
Paragahamualahena; Description: Garden, 04 permanent 
Buildings, 23 Coconut trees, aged about 40-50 Years, 06 
Kohomba trees, 01 Kaduru tree, 02 Karawu trees, 01 Mee 
tree, aged about 10-15 years, 01 Kottan tree and 01 Kolon 
tree; Claimed by: Trustee board, of the AL-Masjidul 
Salam Jumma mosque in Kekunagolla. 
 
Boundaries Description:  
 
North: Lot No. 06; East: Lot No. 129; South: Lots No. 
132, 131 & 130; West: Lots No. 6, 1 and 2. 
All person interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kuliyapitiya 
East on the 04th day of September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 02nd day of September, 2014 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land servitude* the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 H. L. S UNIL WIJESINGHE,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kuliyapitiya-East. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kuliyapitiya-East. 
30th July, 2014.
