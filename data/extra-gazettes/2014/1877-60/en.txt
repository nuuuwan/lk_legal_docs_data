DECLARATION OF HISTORICAL RIDHEE VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that,  the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as " Historical Ridhee Vihara sacred area", for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
  Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF HISTORICAL RIDHEE VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 12.0721 Hectares
from Ridheegama Watta and Pansal Watta at Ridheegama village in Ridheegama Grama Niladari Division No.557 of Kurunegala
District in North-Western Province marked as lot 35 and lot 39 depicted in plan No.420098, Zone No. 01, Form No. 01 dated
18
th January 2010 of Surveyor General.
