FINANCE  ACT,  No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963 as amended from
time to time, read with Article 44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order, V est in the People's
Bank established under the People's Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 05th March Two Thousand Fourteen, the
properties described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
The Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
05th March, 2014.
