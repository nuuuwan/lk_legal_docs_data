FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
PANADURA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the member,  Mihindukulasuriya Kahapala Arachchige Ariyapala Silva belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as
a member of that Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Muhandiramge
Bandulaksiri Rudrigo being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Muhandiramge Bandulaksiri Rudrigo  elected as
a member of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
16th  July, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
PANADURA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the member,  Wannakuwattha Waduge Neel Sunethra Lal Fernando belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as
a member of that Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Mohan Lal Gunaransi
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mohan Lal Gunaransi  elected as a member of the
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
16th  July, 2014.
