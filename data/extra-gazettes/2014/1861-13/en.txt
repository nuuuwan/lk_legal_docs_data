SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamation  by  the Governor  of  Southern  Province
KNOW YE that by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 154  (b) (8) (a)  of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasuriya, the Governor of the Southern Province, do by this proclamation
summon the first Session of the Seventh Southern Province Provincial Council to meet at 10.00 a.m. on the 20th day of May,
2014, in the Council Chamber of the Southern Province Provincial Council.
N
ERANJALA PUSHPA KUMARI BALASURIYA,
 Governor of the Southern Province.
Governor’s Office,
Dickson Road, Galle,
24th day of April, 2014.

Appointments and C., by the Governor
SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the Governor of the Suthern Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 154F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasuriya, Governor of the Southern Province, do hereby
announce that Hon. Haththotuwa Gamage Weerasumana Weerasingha has, in addition to his present Southern Provincial
Ministerial subjects of Sports and Youth Affairs, Cultural and Art Affairs, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare Services,
Women Affairs, and Home Economic promotion, Housing and Construction and Man Power and Employment been assigned
the subject of Rural Development too with effect from 24th April 2014.
N
ERANJALA PUSHPA KUMARI BALASURIYA,
 Governor of the Southern Province.
Office of the Governor,
Dickson Road,
Galle,
24th April, 2014.

SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by the Governor of the Southern Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 154F (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasuriya, Governor of the Southern Province, do hereby
announce that Hon. Udalamaththa Gamage Dayawansha Ariyathilake has, in addition to his present Southern Provincial
Province Ministerial subjects of Agriculture, Agrarian Development, Irrigation, Water Supply and Drainage, Food Supply
and Distribution and Co-operative Development been assigned the subject of Trade, too with effect from 24th April 2014.
N
ERANJALA PUSHPA KUMARI BALASURIY A,
 Governor of the Southern Province.
Office of the Governor,
Dickson Road,
Galle,
24th April, 2014.

SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of the Southern Province
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasooriya the
Governor of the Southern Province.
1. Mr. Chinthaka S. Lokuhetti   in Special Grade of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service,  in addition to  Secretary to
the Subjects of the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Cultural and Art Affairs, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare
Services, Women Affairs, and Home Economic promotion, Housing and Construction and Man Power and Employment  of
the Southern Province Provincial Council  as Secretary to the subject of Rural Development with effect from 24th April 2014.
2. Mr. Sumith Alahakoon  in Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service in addition to Acting Secretary to the
Subjects of the Ministry of Agriculture, Agrarain Development, Irrigation, Water Supply and Drainage, Food Supply and
Distribution and Co-operative Development of  the Southern Province Provincial Council as Acting Secretary to the subject
of Trade with effect from 24th April 2014.
L
