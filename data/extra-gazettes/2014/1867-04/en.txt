THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a Complex of the Police Check point in Mahakalugolla and will
be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land in total extent of about 1.6378 Hectare, depicted
in Advance Tracing No. MO/SYB/2012/320 dated 12.12.2012,
prepared by Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Pallegama
(C. P. 720002, Zonal No. 4) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Siyambalanduwa in Monaragala District in Uva Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Buruthagahawatta – Extent: 0.0461 Hectare
North: Road and Lot B; East: Lot B; South: Road (R.D.A.); West:
Road.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Buruthagahawatta – Extent: 0.5225 Hectare
North: Lot C; East: Lot C and Lot No. 328 of Zonal No. 4 of C. P .
720002; South: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot A; West: Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Buruthagahawatta – Extent: 1.0692 Hectare
North and East: Lot No. 328 of Zonal No. 4 of C. P . 720002;
South and West: Lots B, A Road, Lot Nos. 336 and 328 of Zonal No.
4 of C. P . 720002.
