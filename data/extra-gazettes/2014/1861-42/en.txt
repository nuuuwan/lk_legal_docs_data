THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for Broaden the part of road from 195+090 KM to 201+820 KM of
Peradeniya - Badulla Chenkaladi Road  and will be acquired under
the provisions of that Act.
The Allotments  of land in total Extent of About 0.0395  Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Mo/RDA/2009/616 dated 05th
March 2010, prepared by the Sureveyor-General situated in the
Village of Kanawegalla (F.V .P. 591) and Ilukepudana (F.V .P . 592) in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Bibile in Monaragala District,
Uva Province and bounded as  follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ilukkepudana – Extent:  0.0019 Hectare
North: Land claimed by W. A. Samantha; East: Road (R.D.A.);
South: Kandura; West: Land claimed by W. A. Samantha.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Ilukkepudana – Extent:  0.0029 Hectare
North: Land claimed by R. M. Esilin Nona and Kandura; East:
Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.); West: Land claimed by R. M.
Esilin Nona.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Ilukkepudana – Extent:  0.0069 Hectare
North: Land claimed by R. W. Premathilaka; East: Road (R.D.A.);
South: Road (R.D.A.) and Kandura; West: Kandura Land claimed by
R. M. Premathilaka.
04. Lot ‘I’ – Not in Use
05. Lot ‘O’ – Not in Use
06. Lot ‘X’ – Ilukkepudana – Extent:  0.0005 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot W; East: Lot W and Land claimed
by Sri Pushparama Viharaya; South: Land claimed by Sri Pushparama
Viharaya; West: Road (R.D.A.).
07. Lot ‘Y’ – Ilukkepudana – Extent:  0.0063 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Land claimed by Sri Pushparama
Viharaya; South: Kandura; West: Road (R.D.A.).
