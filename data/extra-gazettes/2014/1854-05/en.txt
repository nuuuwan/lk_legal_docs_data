EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT NO. 28 OF 1964  
Notice Under Section 7 
 
Reference No.:- VSW/DS/LAND/WS/2011. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition Amendment Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan  : Ya 2715 
Situation  : Village, Manipay 
DS Division  : Valikamam South West, Sandilipay 
District  : Jaffna 
Province  : Northern 
 
Lot No. 01  
 
Name of Land: Sooduventhapulam; Description: 
Garden contains 28 coconu t Trees, 25-35 years, 23 
Palmyrah trees, 50-60 year s, One Permanent building, 
and masonry well; Claimant: Sivasampunathan 
Sivagurunathan & Sivasampunathan Sivananthan, 
Kaddudai, Manipay, Jaffna; Extent: 0.2988 Hectare.
   
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Valikamam South 
West Sandilippay Divisional S ecretary office on the 25th 
April, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 16th 
April, 2014 the nature of their interests in the land. The 
particulars or their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land. The amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
All deeds and documents in support of claim should 
be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
  
 S. M URALITHARAN, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Valikamam South West, Sandilipay. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Valikamam South West, Sandilipay, 
14th March, 2014.
