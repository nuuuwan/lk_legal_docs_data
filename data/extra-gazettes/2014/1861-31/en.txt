WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Policy Statement of Urban Agriculture (Western Province), 2014
I, Udaya Prabath Gammanpila, Minister of Agriculture, Agrarian Development, Minor Irrigation, Industries, Environment,
Arts and Cultural Affairs of Western Province, do hereby notify that the Policy Statement of Urban Agriculture of the
Western Province, which has been approved by the Western Provincial Council on the 17th of August 2011 and by the Hon.
Governor of Western Province on the 21st of September 2011, shall be implemented from the 23rd of May 2014.
U
DAYA PRABATH GAMMANPILA (Attorney-at-Law),

POLICY  STA TEMENT OF  URBAN  AGRICULTURE  - WESTERN  PROVINCE
1. Development of ecofriendly urban agriculture activities through the appropriate use of new and traditional
technologies, to enhance the income and improve living standards of urban people.
2. Strengthening urban agricultural activities by developing strong links with the government, private non-
government organizations, international government and  non-government organizations as well as civil organizations.
3. In the practice of urban agriculture, engage in ecofriendly activities like recycling of water and waste, rainwater
harvesting and efficient use of urban resources.
