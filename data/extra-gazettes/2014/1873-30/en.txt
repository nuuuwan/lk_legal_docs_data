Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987
BY virtue of power vested in me under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, I, Abdul Majeed Mohamed
Najeeb, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Local Government, Rural Development, Rehabilitation,
Re-settlement, Man Power, Law and Order, Regional Administration and Environment of the Eastern Province do hereby
order publish as described in the Schedule hereto.
A
BDUL MAJEED MOHAMED NAJEEB,

and Planning,  Local Government, Rural Development,

Schedule
Until the Budget of Damana Pradeshiya Sabha within the limits of Ampara District for the year 2014 is passed, I
nominate and delegate the power to Regional Assistant Commissioner of Local Government, Ampara to take steps and to
incur expenditure arising out of the subjects described below and incidental thereto effective from 1st January 2014 by virtue
of power vested in me under Section 223 of the said Act with the view to proceed the Pradeshiya Sabha with the functions
charged with as provided therein under Section 3 of the said Act without having interrupted as the Budget of the Pradeshiya
Sabha for the year 2014 has not been passed in terms of Section 168 and 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987.
Subjects :
