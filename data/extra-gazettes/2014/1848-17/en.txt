FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER OF THE  NUWARA-ELIYA  PRADESHIYA SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of a member  in Nuwara-Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of three months without permission :
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognised political party to which Rathnayake Herath
Mudiyanselage Kasun Eranga Rathnayake belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha who
belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Bodhikumara Maldeniya
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumayasiri Fernando,  Returning Officer of  Nuwara-Eliya  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Bodhikumara Maldeniya
elected as a Member of the Nuwara-Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. Fernando,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara-Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Nuwara-Eliya,
30th  January, 2014.
