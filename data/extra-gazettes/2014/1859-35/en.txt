CORRECTION
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY ACT, No. 9 OF 2003
Order No. 26 issued under Section 20(5)
THE last paragraph of Order No. 26 dated 9th April 2014 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1857/11, ie,
“ The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order  rescind Order No. 05 published in gazette extraordinary No.
1593/28 dated 18th March, 2009 with regard to prices of rice” shall be replaced by the following paragraph, ie,
“ The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order  rescind Order No. 15 published in gazette extraordinary No.
1683/29 dated 09th December, 2010 with regard to prices of rice”
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
21st April, 2014.
