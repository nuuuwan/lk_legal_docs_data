THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands  described  below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three allotments of land called 'Veheragawa Mukalana and
Koongahahena' in total  extent of about 0.0581 Hectare, in the
Advance Tracing No. R/EMB/2013/354 dated 21st October, 2013
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Panamura
in Diyapotagam Pattuwa Korale Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Embilipitiya in Rathnapura District,
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Veheragawa Mukalana – Extent:  About 0.0496
Hectare
North and East: Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath;
South: Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath, Koongahahena
claimed by K. A. Heenhamy, Lots C and B; West: Veheragawa
Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Veheragawa Mukalana – Extent:  About 0.0059
Hectare
North: Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath; East:
Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath and Lot A; South: Lot
C; West: Lot C, Koongahahena claimed by K. A. Heenhamy and
Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Koongahahena – Extent:  About 0.0026 Hectare
North: V eheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath and Lot B;
East: Lot B, Veheragawa Mukalana claimed by K. Jayalath and Lot
A; South and West: Koongahahena claimed by K. A. Heenhamy.
