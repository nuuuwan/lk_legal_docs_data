FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
MANNAR  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a  member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Sri Lanka Muslim Congress,  to which the
former member,  Abdul Muthalif Mohamed Risafi  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Mohamed Ismail
Mohamed Issadeen being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Denicius Canute Aravintharaj,  Returning Officer of  Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mohamed Ismail Mohamed Issadeen  elected as
a member of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Mannar,
07th  August, 2014.
