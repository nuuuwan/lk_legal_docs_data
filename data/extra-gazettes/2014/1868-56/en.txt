FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  DEPUTY  MAYOR  OF  THE
KALMUNAI  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Deputy Mayor  in  Kalmunai Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Deputy Mayor has
been appointed as the Mayor ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party to which Mohamed
Nizam Kariapper the Deputy Mayor belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Kalmunai Municipal Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Deputy
Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to  nominate
Mr. Mohamed Ibralebbe Mohamed Firdhouse to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan  Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of  Kalmunai Municipal Council, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mohamed Ibralebbe Mohamed Firdhouse
elected as a Deputy Mayor of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
T
HILHNA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
23rd June,  2014.
