FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF THE  HOMAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Chairman  in Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party  to which Andarage
Don Kumarasiri the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman
within a period of thirty (30) days :
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to communicate his decision with
the period specified  :
Now therefore I, Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardena,  Returning Officer of  Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 do hereby declare Mr. Indika Prashanna Kumara
Koralage who had secured the next highest number preferences at the election held on 23rd July of 2011 to Homagama
Pradeshiya Sabha elected as Chairman of the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDENA,
Returning Officer,
Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
08th  of April, 2014.
