WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made  under Section  2(2) of the Entertainment  Tax Ordinance  No. 12 of 1946
I,  Prasanna Ranatunga, the Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment, Engineering Services and Information of the Western
Province do hereby announce that the motion adopted by the Colombo Municipal Council on 31.07.2014 as per provisions
stated in Section 2(1) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 to enforce an entertainment tax of 10% from the entrance
fee charged for entertainment affairs stated in Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment Tax
(Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 1984 and to charge such fee for year 2015 and 2016, was approved as per powers vested in me
by provisions of Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 which shall be read with Section 2 of the
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
P
RASANNA RANA TUNGA,
Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning,
Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government
and Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment,
Engineering Services and Information of the Western Province
Provincial Council.
