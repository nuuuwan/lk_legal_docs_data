No. 1856/39 – THURSDAY, APRIL 03, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Viharawatte in total extent of about
0.1125 Hectare depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/PTD/
2012/135P of 18th January, 2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Doragamuwa in Pallegampaha Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pathadumbara in Kandy
District  and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01 – Viharawatte – Extent: About 0.1125 Hectare
North: Viharawatte claimed by Chief Incumbent of Sri
Sailapabbatharamaya and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); South and West: Viharawatte claimed by Chief
Incumbent of Sri Sailapabbatharamaya.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Reference No.: 4-3/3/2011/WS/11.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: KDS/PDB/4/3/2/5.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
