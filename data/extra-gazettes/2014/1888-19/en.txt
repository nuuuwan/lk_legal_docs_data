EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
GAMPAHA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Member  in Gampaha  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance,  to which
the  former  member, Appu Acharige Don Nilantha Dilruk Priyadarshana  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2)
of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of  of  thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for
election as a member of  that Gampaha  Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. ILangakoon Pathiranalage
Chathura Mahesh Sugath Kumara  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of  Gampaha  Municipal  Council,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local.
authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. ILangakoon Pathiranalage Chathura
Mahesh Sugath Kumara elected as a member of the  Gampaha  Municipal Council.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha  Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
10th  November,  2014.
