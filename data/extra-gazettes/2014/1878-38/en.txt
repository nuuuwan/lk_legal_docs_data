PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS   ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a Vacancy   in  the Eastern  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress , the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Jainudeen Mohamed
Laheer  to fill such vacancy ;
 I,  Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshpriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Jainudeen Mohamed Laheer, elected as a member of
the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Trincomalee in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
02nd  September, 2014.
