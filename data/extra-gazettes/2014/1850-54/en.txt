SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Regulations under Sections 32, 33 and 34
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32, 33 and 34 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, I, Mahindananda
Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports, shall hereby inform that the appointments of the office bearers of the Karate Do Federation
of Sri Lanka are cancelled from 28th February 2014 and the names of persons given below are appointed from 01st March 2014
to carry out the functions of Karate Do Federation of Sri Lanka until an election is held or until further notice.
Mr. H. M. Gamini Vijaya Bandara - President
Mr. Anura A. Kathriarachchi - Secretary
Mr. Gayan Saminda - Treasurer
Mr. K. Ramachandran - Member
Mr. Klemant De Soysa - Member
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
20th February 2014.
