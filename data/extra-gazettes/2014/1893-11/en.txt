THE   HOLIDAYS   ACT, No.  29  OF  1971
 Order  under Section  11(1)(b)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11(1)(b) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J. Senevirathne, Minister
of Public Administration and Home Affairs, do by this order declare that the Monday 05th of  January 2015 an additional Bank
Holiday for the purpose of the Act, in addition to the day for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to that Act.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRA THNE,
Minister of Public Administration and Home Affairs.
