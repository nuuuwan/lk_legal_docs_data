FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
RAJGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of
Southern Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the member,  Mr. Ariyawansha Sambu  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Rajgama
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Thirimadura Sujith
Chamin Kumara being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Thirimadura Sujith Chamin Kumara elected as a
member of the Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. 
DE SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
25th  July, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
DIVULAPITIYA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the member,  Kirielle Yakdehi Arachchige Jayawardena  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Kadapolagedara
Ranjith Ananda being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Divulapitiya Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kadapolagedara Ranjith
Ananda elected as a member of the Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Divulapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
28th  July, 2014.
