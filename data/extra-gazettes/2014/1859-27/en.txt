EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    OFFICE OF CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
JA-ELA   URBAN  COUNCIL   UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   Chairman in Ja-Ela Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Arambawaththa Upali the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Ja-Ela Urban Council  who  belongs to such party who shall  be declared  elected as  Chairman within
a period of thirty (30) days   ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated  his decision to nominate
Mr. Anju Sri Sham Perera  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Ja-Ela Urban Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr. Anju Sri Sham Perera elected as  Chairman of the Ja-Ela
Urban Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Ja-Ela Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
21st  April, 2014.
