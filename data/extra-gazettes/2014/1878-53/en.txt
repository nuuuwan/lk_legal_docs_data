IMPORTS AND EXPORTS (CONTROL) ACT, No. 01 OF 1969
REGULA TIONS made by the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka under Section 20 read with
sub-Section (3) of  Section  4 and Section 14 of the Imports and Exports (Control) Act, No. 01 of 1969, as amended by Act,
No.48 1985 and No. 28 of  1987 and paragraph (2) of article 44 of  the Constitution.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo,
06 th  September, 2014.
 Regulations
1. This regulation may be cited as the Import and Export Control  regulation No. 05 of 2014.
2. Importation of potatoes- fresh or chilled (+) under HS Code 0701.90 is hereby suspended with effect from the
midnight of 06th September 2014.
