MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE  IN  CRIMINAL  MATTERS  ACT,
No. 25 OF 2002
Order  under Section  2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (3) of section 2 of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters Act, No.
25 of 2002, I, Abdul Rauff Hakeem, Minister of Justice, do by this Order declare that the provisions of the aforesaid Act shall
apply in respect of the Agreement between the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the
Government of the Republic of Belarus made at Minsk on the Twenty Sixth day of August Two Thousand and Thirteen.  This
Agreement which was made between the Government of Sri Lanka and the Government of Belarus, which is a Non-
Commonwealth Country, deals with mutual legal assistance in criminal matters and concerns  inter alia  the improved
effectiveness on the part of both parties to the Agreement through cooperation in relation to the prevention, investigation,
prosecution and suppression of crimes, including crimes relating to terrorism within their respective jurisdictions.
A
BDUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
11th August, 2014.
