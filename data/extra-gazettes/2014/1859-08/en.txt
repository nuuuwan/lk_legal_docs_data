THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Sub-section (1) of Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dissanayake
Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, on the rcommendation of the University
Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the Order establishing the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which
Order is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the
said Act, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1711/24 of June 24, 2011) in the Schedule thereto
by the substitution for all the entries appearing in Column II under the heading “Department” as corresponds to the entry
“Faculty of Applied Sciences” appearing in Column I under the heading “Faculty” of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty Department
Faculty of Applied Sciences 1. Department of Mathematics
2. Department of Physics
3. Department of Chemistry
4. Department of Botany
5. Department of Forestry and Environmental Science
6. Department of Zoology
7. Department of Computer Science
8. Department of  Food Science and Tecnology
9. Department of Statistics.”.
D
ISSANAY AKE MUDIYANSELAGE S UMANAWEERA
BANDA DISSANAY AKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
