No. 1889/27 –  WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER  19, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/63. 
 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/7/2013/HW/445. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/ 
servitude which is required for Re-Construction of bridge 
No. 2/8 (Rattota End) Matale -Illukkumbura-Pallegama 
Road. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land described in bellow in Plan No. 
Ma 1632, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of 
Surveys, situated in Kumburegammedda village, 
Viharagama Gramaniladari Division in Sub Division of 
Matale Pallesiya Pattuwa in the Rattota Divisional 
Secretariat Division in Matale District in Central 
Province. 
 
Lot No. 2 
 
Name of Land: Suduganga Watta; Description: Open 
waste Land; Claimant: Mr. S. P. Da Silva, Nadee Uyana, 
Kaludewala, Matale;  Extent: 0.0020 Hectare. 
Lot No. 4 
 
Name of Land: Suduganga Watta; Description: Open 
waste Land; Claimant: State;  Extent: 0.0168 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Rattota on the 21st January, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 13th January, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the requisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 B. M. G. B. WIJEBANDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rattota. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Rattota, 
15th November, 2014.
