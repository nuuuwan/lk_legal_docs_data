THE  W AGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES BOARD FOR THE FLOWERS, ORNAMENTAL PLANTS, VEGETABLES AND FRUITS GROWING AND
EXPORT  TRADE
IT is hereby notified, under regulation 30 of the  Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), that Mr. Ranjith Galwaduge has been  appointed
in place of Mr. J. M.M.D. Fernando employers’ representatives of the  Wages Board for the Flowers, Ornamental Plants, Vegetables and
Fruits Growing and Export  Trade by the  Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under section 9 of the aforesaid ordinance. This
Appointment is valid only up to 29th February, 2016.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations
Labour, Secretariat,
Colombo 05.
15th Aug, 2014.
