NOTICE No : 82/0069 Matara District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 820030 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Walgama South 
(Block 03) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Nilad-
hari Division of 411C - Walgama South in the Divisional Secre-
tariat Division of Matara in the District of Matara in the Province 
of Southern should submit his claim to the undersigned before 
03rd November, 2014 In the event of failure  to submit any claim 
before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an 
exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly pub-
lish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 820030 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Settle-
