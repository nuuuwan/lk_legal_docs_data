No. 1875/27 –  WEDNESDAY, AUGUST  13, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref.  No.: KDS/KUN/04/07/16/04/53. 
 
Land & Land Dev.Mini. Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/215. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required 
for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “Gallenagodahena” in 
total extent of about 0.0128 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 
1 in the Preliminary Plan, No. Maha 6231 dated 27.06. 
2014, surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor General, 
situated at the village of Gunnepana in Udagampaha 
Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Kundasale in Kandy District, Central 
Province and bounded as follows. 
 
Lot No. 01 - Name of the Land : Gallenagodahena; 
Name and Address of the claimants : Mr. R. W. M. 
Amarapala, No. 156, Uduwela Pallegama, Athulgama; 
Extent of the Land : 0.0128 Hectares. 
 
      Boundaries 
 
North: Gallenagodahena claimed by R. W. M. 
Amarapala and Lot No. 1 in PP Maha 6076; East: Lot 
No. 1 in PP Maha 6076 and Gallenagodahena claimed by 
R. W. M. Amarapala; South: Gallenagodahena claimed 
by R. W. M. Amarapala and Road; West: Road and 
Gallenagodahena claimed by R. W. M. Amarapala.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at  Divisional Secretary’s office at 
Kundasale on the 24th day of September, 2014 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requeste d to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 17th day of September, 
2014 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 L. A. D. G EETHA INDRANI,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kundasale.  
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, Kundasale, 
Menikhinna.
