FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF THE  PALLEPOLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Pallepola  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party,  to which  the member
Channa Indika Herath  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha
who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. R. M Priyantha Rukman
Ratnayaka being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. R. M Priyantha Rukman Ratnayaka
elected as a member of the Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UAPSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Pallepola Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
06th May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF THE  KALUTARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Kalutara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which  the member
Sembapperuma Arachchige Don Nilantha,  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kalutara
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Susantha Gamini Gunasinghe
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Susantha Gamini Gunasinghe elected as a
member of the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
08th May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE    BERUWALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Beruwala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of  United Perople's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which  Opatha
Withanage Prasanna Sanjeewa the Chairman  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Seema Hewage Kusman De Silva  to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Beruwala  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Seema Hewage Kusman De Silva elected as
Chairman of the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
08th May, 2014.
