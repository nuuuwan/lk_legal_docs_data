SRI  LANKA  ELECTRICITY  ACT,  No.  20 OF 2009
Order under Sub - Section (1) of the Section 46
BY VIRTUE of  the powers  vested under Sub - Section (1) of  the Section 46 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009 (as
amend), the Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka does hereby impose an annual levy in such amounts as are set out in
Column II of the Schedule hereto, to be recovered from those licensees holding such licenses as are specified in the
corresponding entry in Column I of  that schedule. The previous Gazette Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 1700/ 28 on Saturday 09th April, 2011 is hereby revoked.
