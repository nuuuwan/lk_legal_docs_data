EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE  KINNIYA   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Kinniya Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation :
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 1  to which Mammathu Anifa Mohamed Nijam
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under
the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kinniya Urban Council who belongs to such Independent  Group who shall
be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Independent Group  has nominated  Mr. Pichchai Thampi Junaideen  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Nalaka Rathnayake  ,  Returning Officer of  Kinniya Urban Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2)
of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Pichchai Thampi Junaideen  elected as a member of the  Kinniya Urban
Council.
N
ALAKA RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Kinniya Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
30th January, 2014.
