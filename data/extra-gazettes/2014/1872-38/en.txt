 L.D.B. 51/53(III).
THE  RUBBER  REPLANTING  SUBSIDY ACT (CHAPTER 437)
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Plantation Industries under section 8 of the Rubber Replanting Subsidy Act
(Chapter 437).
MAHINDA SAMARASINGHE,
Colombo, Minister of Plantation Industries.
01st July, 2014.
Regulations
1. The Rubber Replanting Subsidy Regulations No. 917/1 of 2012 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1776/36 of
September 20, 2012, are hereby amended by the repeal of sub-paragraph (h) of paragraph (1) of regulation 4 thereof,
and the substitutions therefor of the following :–
‘‘(h) The eighth installment of Rupees Twenty Four Thousand (Rs. 24,000) for each hectare shall be paid after
a period not less than six years upon the completion of the replanting.’’.
2. These regulations shall be deemed to have been effective from August 26, 2013.
