CENTRAL    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice by the  Governor  of  the  Central  Province
This Gazette Extraordinary can be downloaded from www.documents.gov.lk
KNOW ye that  by virtue of powers vested by the article 154(b) : (8):(b) of the thirteenth amendment to the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Tikiri Banda Kobbekaduwa, Governor of the Central Province, do hereby this
notice prorogue the Second Session of the Sixth Council of the Central Province with effect from 17th December, Two
Thousand Fourteen.
By virtue of powers vested in me by paragraph 8(a) of the said Article, I do hereby summon the said Council to meet
at the Central Province Provincial Council Meeting Hall at Pallekelle, Kundasale on Thursday the 15th January, Two Thousand
Fifteen at 10.00 a. m.
T
IKIRI BANDA KOBBEKADUWA,
Governor of the Central  Province.
Given at Kandy,
this 14th day of November, Two Thousand Fourteen.
