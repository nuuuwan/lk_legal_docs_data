Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My  No.: Thiprale/1/10/3/10Ath. 
 Ministry of Land  Reference No.: 13/2000/NH/14. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for the purpose of 
establishment of Community Centre of Antharawatha 
Housing Project. 
 
Description of the Land  or servitude to be acquired :-  
 
Two allotment of Land having the following names 
and extents, in total extent about 0.0265 hectares show as 
Lot Nos: 11678, 11678A, in the Advanced Tracing No. 
Co. 9411 and dated 03.02.2014 prepared by the surveyor 
General, situated in Kirulapone Village at Kirula Grama 
Niladhary’s Division within the Limits of Municipal 
council of Colombo in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Thimbirigasyaya, Colombo District, Western Province, 
Description of the above Land as follows: 
 Lot No. 11678  
 
Name of the land: Millagahawatte and Ovita, 
Polhengoda Road Assmt No. 92/14B; Description:   
Garden; Name and Address of the Claiment: Sumathi 
Dharmawardana No. 101, Elvitigala Mawatha, Colombo; 
Extent:  0.0248 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 11678A  
 
Name of the land: Millagahawatte and Ovita; 
Description:   Garden; Name and Address of the 
Claiment: Not Kown; Extent:  0.0017 Hectare. 
 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land/Lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby acquired to 
appear personally or by agent duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Thimbirigasyaya Divisional Secretarial on 
the 12th May, 2014 day  of 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 09th May, 2014 the nature of their interests in the 
Land, the particulars of thei r claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation such 
amount.
