No. 1891/40 –  FRIDAY, DECEMBER  05, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: Aku/4/1/1/3/3. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) as amended 
by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964, was given by me that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following land lot in total extend of Hectares 
0.0394 depicted in the Prel iminary Plan No. Maha/6352 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at 
Pangollamada Grama Niladhari Division at Udagampaha 
Korale Minor Division in  the Akurana Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Kandy District. 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Tannewatte; Claimant: 
Pangollamada Welfare Association, Pangollamada, 
Akurana; Extent: 0.0394 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in  the aforesaid land are 
required to appear before me personally, or by agent duly 
authorized in writing at Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Akurana on the 10th February, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 10th day of February, 2015 the nature of 
their interests in the land and the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
aforesaid land the amount of compensation and the details 
of the compensation of such amount.  
 
 M ADAWA WARNAKULASOORIYA, 
                                                   Divisional Secretary, 
                                                           Akurana. 
 
Divisional Secretariy’s Office, 
Akurana, 
24th November, 2014.
