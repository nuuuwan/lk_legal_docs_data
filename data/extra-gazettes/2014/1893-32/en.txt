NORTHERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Supplementary  Provision  for Appropriation Statute   No. 03 of 2014
A  statute to provide Supplementary Provisions to previously approved expenditure of the Northern Provincial Council for
the financial year 2014 by the Honourable Governor on the 21st day of December 2013 in accordance to First Schedule to this
statute and to make payments by way of advance out of the Northern Provincial Council funds or monies to provide for the
refund of such monies to the Northern Provincial Council funds and to make provisions for matters connected therewith or
incidental thereto :
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council as follows
1. This Statute may be cited as the Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statute
No. 03 of 2014.
2. Without prejudice to any other law which authorizes for any such expenditure, the expenditure
of the Northern Provincial Council during the financial year beginning from 1st January
2014 and ending on 31st December 2014 referred to as the “Financial year 2014” in this
Statute the allocation of Rupees One thousand seven hundred and twenty five million six
hundred and three thousand five hundred and twenty two only (Rs. 1,725,603,522) from
the expenditure the Honourable Governor approved on the 21st December 2013 and to the
limits stated in the First Schedule of this Statute shall be met ;
(A) From the Northern Provincial Council funds or monies, or
(B) From the allocation of funds allocated in accordance to Article 154(R) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and any other funds
allotted or granted by the Government of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
3. The debit balance outstanding at the end of the financial year 2014 of any activities specified
in the Second Schedule of this statute is revised from the maximum and minimum limits the
Honourable Governor approved on 21st December 2013 in the Second Schedule of the
Statute.
C. V . K
ANDIYA SIV AGNANAM, A. S IV ABALASUNDRAN,
Chairman, Secretary,
Northern Provincial Council. Council Secretariat,
Northern Provincial Council.
