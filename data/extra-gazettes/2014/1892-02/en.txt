NOTIFICATION
P. S. No. CSA/1/3/1/1.
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Eranjan Naveen Dissanayaka, MP , who was appointed to the office of Minister of Public
Management Reforms by His Excellency the President, in the exercise of powers vested in him, by Article 44(1)(b) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in terms of Article 47(b) of the Constitution, resigned such
office of Minister, with effect from November 29, 2014.
                                                                                                     By Order of His Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
