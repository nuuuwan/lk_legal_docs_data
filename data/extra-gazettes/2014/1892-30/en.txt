No. 1892/30 –  WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER  10, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
 
 Notice under Section 7 
  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or Servitude to be acquired: 
  
Preliminary Plan No: V.P.78 SupplementNo.1, Sheet No,1  
Description              :  Vettilaikerni, Tsunami Kudiiruppu 
Divisional                :  Vadamaradchi East 
Secretariat Division 
District                     :  Jaffna 
Province                   : North 
 
 
Lot No. 211 
 
Name of the Land: Mudavanpanaiyiyatkadu and 
Kardikunthavitkadu; Description: Permanent House; 
Claimant: K.T. Muralitharan; Extent: 2.2876 Hectare. 
Lot No. 212 
 
Name of the Land: Mudavanpanaiyiyatkadu and 
Kardikunthavitkadu; Description: Permanent House; 
Claimant: claimant not known; Extent: 7.7826 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired Lands are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretary’s Maruthankerny 12th 
January 2015 day of Monday  at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 19th December 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land and the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said/ servitude the 
amount of compensation and the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 N. Thirulinganathan,
