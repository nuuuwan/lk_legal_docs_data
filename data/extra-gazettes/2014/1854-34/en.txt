FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  MIRIGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Mirigama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member
Marasingha  Pedige Dissanayaka belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Mirigama Pradeshiya
Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Horanekarage Leelananda
Jayalath being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne,  Returning Officer of  Mirigama  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Horanekarage Leelananda Jayalath elected as a
Member of the Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Mirigama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
09h  March, 2014.
