 PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  the  Southern  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Death;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognised political party to which the member belonged
was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy;
And  whereas the  Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Kankanam Gamage Kapila
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby Declare Mr. Kankanam Gamage Kapila, elected as a member of the
above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Matara in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections  Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
15th August, 2014.
