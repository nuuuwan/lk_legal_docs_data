NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of North Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Karunaratna Divulgane,
Governor of the North Central Province hereby notify that the officers named below were appointed to the respective posts
indicated, in the North Central Provincial Public Service.
01. Mrs. Chandra Karunaratne, SLAS, Special Grade (retired) - as the Secretary to the Governor of the North Centrals
Province for a period of one year from 10.05.2013 to 09.05.2014 (on contract basis).
02. Mr. R. K. Premasiri - SLAS, Class I - in addition to his functions in the post of Secretary to the North Central
Provincial Council, to act in the post of Secretary to the North Central Provincial Ministry of Education Information Technology
and Cultural Affairs for the period from  27.06.2013 to 13.11.2013.
03. Mr. G . D. Keerthi Gamage - SLAS, Class I - as the Deputy Chief Secretary (Training) of the Chief Secretary’s office
from 27.06.2013, until further notice.
04. Mr. N. G. Panditharatna - SLAS, Class I - as the Deputy Chief Secretary (Administration) of the Chief Secretary’s
office From 27.06.2013 to 04.01.2014.
05. Mr. S. H. Mahavithana - SL Accountants’ Service, Class I - as the Deputy Chief Secretary (Finance) of the Chief
Secretary’s office from 03.06.2013, until further notice.
06. Mr. R. G. Ananda Kularathna - SLAS, Class I - in addition to his functions in the post of Secretary to the Chief
Ministry, to act in post of Secretary to the North Central Provincial Ministry of Education, Information Technology and
Cultrual Affairs from 14.11.2013 to 04.01.2014.
07. Mr.I. G . A. R. S. I. Bandara - SLAS Class III to discharge functions of the post of the North Central Provincial
Commissioner of Motor Traffic, from 12.09.2013, until further notice.
