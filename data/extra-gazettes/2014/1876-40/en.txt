 BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  MAHINDA  RAJAPAKSA

GREETINGS  !
WHEREAS, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in pursuance of the
provisions of  Section 2 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) appointed a Commission of Inquiry by the Directive
dated August 12, 2013 ;
Whereas, amendments and additions have been made to the said original Directive by Directives dated March 21,
2014 and July 14, 2014 ;
And whereas, three international experts have been appointed by me to serve on an Advisory Council to the
Commission of Inquiry by the aforesaid Directive dated July 14, 2014 and it was stated in the said Directive that experts would
be appointed as may be further required ;
And, accordingly, the need for more experts to be appointed has been identified ;
I, hereby appoint the following international experts to serve on the said Advisory Council ;
1. Avdhash Kaushal Esquire
2. Ahmer Bilal Soofi Esquire
Given at Colombo under the Seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this 19th day of August, Two
Thousand and Fourteen.
                                                                                         By Order of His Excellency,
LALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
