THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Sudugangawattha in total extent of
about 0.0264 Hectare depicted as Lot A in the Advance Tracing No.
MA/RTT/2013/305 dated 08.01.2014 prepared by the Surveyor
General  situated in the Kumburegammedda Village, in Pallesiya
Pattuwa Minor Division, Divisional Secretary's  Division of Rattota,
Matale District, of Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Sudugangawattha – Extent: 0.0264 Hectare
North,  East and  South: Sudugangawattha claimed by S. P. Da
Silva; West: Suduganga.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
