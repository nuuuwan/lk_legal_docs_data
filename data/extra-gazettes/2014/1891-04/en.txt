FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
YATINUWARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Yatinuwara   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  United People's  Freedom  Alliance, to
which  the  Former member  Gunarathna Adhikaram Mahathmayalage Kolin Udapitiya  belonged  was required by me under
Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the
Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Horathal Pelige
Wickramasinghe  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayake,  Returning Officer of  Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Horathal Pelige Wickramasinghe
elected as  a member of the  Yatinuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
Yatinuwara   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kandy
28th November, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
KALUTARA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Kalutara   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  United National Party, to which  the  Former
member  Agampodi Weerasiri De Silva  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Kalutara
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Udugahapaththuwage
Don Thusitha Indrathilaka  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga  Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Kalutara   Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.   Udugahapaththuwage  Don Thusitha
Indrathilaka  elected as  a member of the  Kalutara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kalutara,
27th November, 2014.
