EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER  OF  THE
KATARAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the former
member Ashoka Wickramasinghe Withanage, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Dandeniya Gamage
Chandana Sugath being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of  Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Dandeniya Gamage Chandana Sugath elected as  a member
of the Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
SURANGA  AMBAGAHA TENNE,
Returning Officer,
Kataragama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Moneragala,
16th December,  2014.
