THE   HOLIDAY   ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
Order under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J. Senevirathne, Minister of
Public Administration and Home Affairs, do by this order, declare that as regards the year 2015, each of the days specified in
the Schedule hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRA THNE,
Minister of Public Administration  and Home Affairs.
Ministry of Public Administration and Home Affairs,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
27th March 2014.
