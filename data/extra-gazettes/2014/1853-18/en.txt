No. 1853/18 – WEDNESDAY, MARCH 12, 2014
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES BOARD FOR THE TEA GROWING AND MANUFACTURING TRADE
In accordance with the provisions of Section 28 of Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), the Wages Board for the Tea Growing an d
Manufacturing Trade hereby notifies that the board, by virtue of powers vested in it by Section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance propose to
vary the decisions mentioned in the Schedule hereto, as specified in that Schedule.
Objections to the aforesaid proposal will be received by the Chairman of the Wages Board until 12 noon on 2nd April, 2014.
Every Objection should be submitted in writing with a statement indicating the reasons for such objection.
V . B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Chairman,
