No. 1853/2 – MONDAY, MARCH 10, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for the Branch Canal of the Deduru Oya left Canal Project and will
be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments of land in total extent of about 0.9139 Hectare depicted
in the Surveyor-General’s Tracing No. KU/KBI/2013/368 dated
05th August, 2013, situated in the Village of Pahala Halmillekotuwe
in Baladora Korale Minor Division in Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kobeigane in Kurunegala District in North Western Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kithulgahamulahena – Extent: 0.6307 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Balance portion of this
land, Minipitiyehena (Kithulgahamulahena) claimed by H. M.
Bandarathilaka and Lot B; South: Minipitiyehena (Kithulgaha-
mulahena) claimed by H. M. Bandarathilaka and balance portion of
this land; West: Wallpaluwa (F.V .P. 1736).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Minipitiyehena and Kithulgahamulahena –
Extent: 0.2625 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Lot C and Halembewawe;
South: Lot C, Minipitiyehena and balance portion of this land; West:
Kithulgahamulahena claimed by G. H. M. de Silva and Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Minipitiyehena – Extent: 0.0207 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Halembewawe; South: Balance portion of this
land; West: Balance portion of this land, Minipitiyehena claimed by
H. M. Bandarathilaka and Lot B.

Minister of Lands and Land Development.
