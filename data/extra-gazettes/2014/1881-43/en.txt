No. 1881 / 43 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 26, – 2014
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
 Notification
Wages Board for the Rubber Growing and Manufacturing Trade
In Accordance with the provisions of section 29(3) of Wages Boards Ordinance (chapter 136), the Wages Board for the Rubber Growing and
Manufacturing Trade hereby notifies that the board, by virtue of powers vested in it by section 30 of the aforesaid Ordinance propose to vary
the decisions mentioned in the schedule hereto, as specified in that schedule.
The decisions shall come in to force on the 01st October 2014.
W. J. L. U. WIJAYAWEERA,
SECRETARY,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Labour Secetariat,
Colombo 05,
23rd September, 2014.
SCHEDULE
The Part II of the Wages Board for the Rubber Growing and Manufacturing Trade set out in the schedule to the Notification Published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 9441 of 31st July 1945 as varied from time to time and last varied by the notification published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1469/8 of 31st October, 2006, shall be further varied by the substitution of the following new part to the schedule II under
the minimum rate of daily wage.
Part II
The minimum rate of daily wage for time work
