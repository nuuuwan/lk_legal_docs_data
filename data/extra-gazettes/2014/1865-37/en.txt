THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No. 1 OF  1972
Order  under Subsection  (4) of Section 27 A
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 27 A (4) of  the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as amended by the Land
Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with Article 44 (2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby –
(a) amend the Order made under section 27A (1) of the Land reform Law, No. 1 of 1972, and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 181/12 of February 27, 1982 relating to the land called and known as Lelwala Watta morefully described in
the First Schedule to this Orderr and which has been vested in the Sri Lanka State Plantations Corporation established under
the Sri Lanka State Plantations Corporation Act, No. 4 of 1958, by revoking on grounds of non-compliance with such terms and
conditions relating to consideration for the vesting in such Corporation, such portion of the said Order as relates to the extent
of land called Lelwala Watta specified in the Second Schedule hereto ; and
(b) state that upon revocation, such extent of land shall re-vest in the Land Reform Commission.
