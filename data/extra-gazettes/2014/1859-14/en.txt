FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  MATARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Death :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member Mr.
Wasantha Weerasingha Chandrasiri  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Matara
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Ranuluge Yogananda
Soysa being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Mr. Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ranuluge Yogananda Soysa elected
as a Member of the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. Wijaya Kumara,
Returning Officer,
Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
22nd March, 2014.
