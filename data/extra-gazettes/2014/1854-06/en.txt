THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
W AGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  FLOWERS,  ORNAMENTAL  PLANTS,  VEGETABLES  AND
FRUITS  GROWING  AND  EXPORT  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) that Mr. G . J. W. De Silva, Mr. Kapila
Dissanayaka and Mrs. Anusha Weerasekara have been appointed in places of Mr. S. M. D. S. B. Samarakoon, Mr. A. H. D. S.
Preemas and Mrs. N. N. Wadivel as employers’ representatives and Mr. D. Chamara Nanayakkara has  been appointed in
place of Mrs. Sujatha Silva as employees’ representative of the Wages Board for the Flowers, Ornamental Plants, Vegetables
and Fruits Growing and Export Trade by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 9 of the aforesaid
ordinance. This Appointment is valid only up to 29th February, 2016.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAYAWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
10th March, 2014.
