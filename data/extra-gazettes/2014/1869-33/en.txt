CIVIL  A VIATION  ACT,  No. 14 OF  2010
REGULATIONS made by me, the Minister of Civil Aviation by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 117 (q) of the Civil
Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010.
PIYANKARA JAY ARATNE (M. P.),
Minister of Civil Aviation.
Colombo 01,
26th June 2014.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the charges for the use of Aerodromes at the Bandaranaike International Airport
(BIA), Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport (MRIA) and Ratmalana Airport (RMA). These charges shall come
into effect on  01.07.2014.
2. By these Regulations, the charges for the use of Aerodromes and any discounted rates published in other relevant
regulations shall stand cancelled.
3. Every aircraft landing at BIA, MRIA and RMA shall pay the following Landing and Parking Charges to the Statu-
tory Service Provider.
(a)
Landing and Parking rates Landing and Parking rates for all Domestic
for all International Flights Flights including Training Flights
Landing - US$ 4/- per Mt of MTOW of
Aircraft or part thereof subject to a
minimum of US$ 250/- per landing
Parking - First 03 hrs free. 10% of Landing
Charge for 06 hrs or part thereof after
first 3 hrs.
(b) Training Flights are charges only in respect of the Last Landing of a Training sortie.
Landing - Rs. 300/- per Mt of MTOW of Aircraft or part
thereof subejct to a minimum of Rs. 3000/- per landing
Parking - First 03 hrs free. 10% of Landing charge for 03
hrs or part thereof after first 3 hrs.

(c) Any Training Flight breaking-off on Final Approach of a Visual Approach or of and Instrument Approach
Procedure, or Carries-out ‘Circuit and Landing’ (Touch - & - Go’s) without a landing at the Aerodrome will be
considered as a Single Landing for the purpose of charging fees.
4. Notwithstanding the Landing and Parking Charges specified in paragraph ‘3’ above, the following limited catego-
ries of operators to/from MRIA will be afforded with a discount rate of 25% with regard to the applicable Landing
and Parking Charges commencing from the effective date of this Gazette Notification up to 17th March 2016.
The Limited Categories of Operators are
(a) New Charter Flight Operators
(b) New Budget Carriers
(c) New Frequency Increases of Existing Budget Carriers
(d) New Frequency Increases of Legacy Carriers
(e) New Legacy Carriers
* The above discounts on Landing and Parking Charges shall be reviewed by March 2016.
5. Notwithstanding the Landing and Parking Charges specified in paragraph ‘3’ above, the following concessions will
be afforded at MRIA and RMA with regard to the applicable Landing and Parking Charges.
Airport Concessions
MRIA International Landing and Parking
Waive off minimum charge commencing from the effective date of this Gazette Notification
up to 17.03.2016
RMA International Landing and Parking
Waive off minimum charge for 03 years commencing from the effective date of this Gazette
Notification
Domestic Landing and Parking
Landing - Rs. 250/- per Mt of MTOW of Aircraft or part thereof subject to a minimum of
Rs  1000/- per landing
Concession to be applicable for 03 years commencing from the effective date of this Gazette
Notification
6. Notwithstanding the Landing and Parking Charges specified in paragraph ‘3’ above, the following Exemptions will
be afforded at BIA, MRIA and RMA with regard to the applicable Landing and Parking Charges.
Exemptions
All Training Flights are exempted from Minimum Landing Charge.
Any existing Regular Scheduled Carrier which may be affected by the minimum charge
specified in paragraph ‘3’ above will be exempted from same for 01 year commencing from
the effective date of this Gazette Notification.
