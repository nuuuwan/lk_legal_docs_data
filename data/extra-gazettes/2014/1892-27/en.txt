THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in total extent  of about 3.1363 Hectares,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MU/PTK/2014/191 dated of
24.07.2014  prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys  Kilinochchi
District, situated in the Village called Putukkudiyiruppu in the Grama
Niladhari Division, No. Mul/36 Putukkudiyiruppu East, in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Putukkudiyiruppu of  Mullaittivu
District in Northern Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’  – Putukkudiyiruppu Kani  – Extent: 3.1363
Hectares
North: Road (R.D.A) and Putukkudiyiruppu Kani, claimed Not
known; East:  Road (P.S);  South: Parth; West:  Parth and
Putukkudiyiruppu Kani, claimant not known.
 Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My. Reference No.: 4/3/4/2013/D/250.
D/S. Ref. No.: LLD/DO/(J)LA/CH/06.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
No. 1200/6,  “Mihikatha Medura”,
