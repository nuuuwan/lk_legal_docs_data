DECLARATION OF BUDUGEKANDA WALAGAMBA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED
AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND
NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Budugekanda Walagamba Rajamaha Vihara sacred area", for its long-
term security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF BUDUGEKANDA W ALAGAMBA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 7.9190 Hectares in
Pallekanda village in Athulkota Palatha Viyaluwa Korale in the Divisional secretariat Division of Soranathota in Badulla
District in Uva Province depicted as Lot 01 to 07 in  plan No.74172  dated 18.02.1869 of Surveyor General.
Lot No.01 (Wetahiragalge Chena)
North by : Chena belonging to Guruwa;
East by  : Chena belonging to Tikira;
South by   : Chena belonging to Sudha;
West by : Chena belonging to Sudha.
