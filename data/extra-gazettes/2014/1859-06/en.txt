EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader or distributor shall sell, expose or offer for sale or display for sale frozen or
chilled broiler chicken meat (whole chicken) with skin above the maximum retail price of Rs. 380/- per 1 kg.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 24 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1812/24 dated 30th May 2013 in respect of price or chicken meat.
