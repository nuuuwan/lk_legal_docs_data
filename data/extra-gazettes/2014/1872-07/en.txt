THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Chundikulamvayal in total extent
of about 13.8844 Hectares, depicted in the Advance Tracing
No. Ki/KND/2013/061 dated 27th June, 2013, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys-Kilinochchi District, situated in the Village
called Ulavanoor in Grama Niladhari Division No. KN/57 of
Punnaineeravi in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kandawalai
of Kilinochchi District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Chundikulamvayal – Extent: 13.8844 Hectares
North: Lot Nos. 585, 584 and Road (P.S.); East: Road (P.S.), Lot
No. 588 and Path; South: Lot No. 588, Path and Road (R.D.A.);
West: Road (R.D.A.), Lot Nos. 585 and 584.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Ref. No.: 4/3/4/2013/D/130.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: KN/LLRC/KD/03.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
‘‘Mihikatha Medura’’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
