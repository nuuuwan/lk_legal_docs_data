THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.1585 Hectare
depicted as Lots "A", "B" and "C" in Advance Tracing No. H/OKW/
2012/756 and dated 1st March 2013 prepared by the Surveyor -
General, situated in the Village of Kanumuldeniya (F.V .P. 171) in
Uthurugiruwapaththuwa Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Okewela in Hambantota District in Southern Province
and bounded as  follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Bibulahena Watta  – Extent: 0.0527 Hectare
North: Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapurage Kawanihami,
Hewa Alankarage Chandralatha and Road (R.D.A.); East: Bibulehena
Watta claimed by Hewa Alankarage Chandralatha; South: Road; West:
Road, Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapurage Kawanihami and
Road (R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘B’ – Bibulahena Watta  – Extent: 0.0599 Hectare
North: Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapurage Kawanihami and
Road; East: Road; South: School garden claimed by primary school
Kandebedda; West: Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapurage
Kawanihami and school garden claimed by primary school
Kandebedda.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Bibulahena Watta  – Extent: 0.0459 Hectare
North: Road; East: Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapaksage
Amarasooriya; South: Bibulehena Watta claimed by Rajapakshage
Amarasooriya, School garden claimed by primary school Kandebedda
and Road; West: School garden claimed by primary school
Kandebedda and Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
