CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Cancellation of Registration of Co-operative Societies
I, Wadugedara Chandraratne the Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development in Central Province that in accordance
with powers vested in me under clauses of 50(A), (B) of Co-operative Societies Statue No. 10 of 1990 of the Central Province
Provincial Council as amended by Co-operative Societies Statute (Amended) No. 04 of 1993 have decided to cancel the
registration of under named Co-operative Societies of Central Province.
Therefore the members and employees of the said societies and institutions and persons who have connections to
the assets of said societies are hereby informed to submit their requests and claims for deposits, loans and arrears due from the
said societies to them.
