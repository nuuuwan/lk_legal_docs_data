Reference No.: 6/3/1/503. 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a  
public purpose. 
 
 
Lot No. 01 of Galgodakale in extent of 0.1004 Hectare 
decipted in preliminary plan No. K4408 and dated 
28.06.2013 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in 
the village of Kumbuka Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Horana in the District of Kalutara in Western Province 
morefully described as follows: 
 
 
North: Lot No. 01 of ppk 1410 and 
Jambusinghagewattha claimed by Rasangika Sudarshani 
Dasanayake and others; East: Jambusinghagewattha 
claimed by Rasangika Sudarshani Dasanayake and others, 
Lot No. 01 of ppk 1410 and Road; South: Road and Lot 
No. 01 of ppk 1410; West: Lot No. 01 of ppk 1410. 
 
Land Originally claimed by State. 
 
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Horana Divisional 
secretariat on the 19th September 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 12th September 2014 the 
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars or their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land the amount of compensation of such amount. 
 
 K. A. D. R. N
ISHANTHI JAYASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Horana.
