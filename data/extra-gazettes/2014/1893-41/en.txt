No. 1893/41  —  FRIDAY,    DECEMBER   19,  2014
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
CIVIL   PROCEDURE   CODE
Notification under Section 192
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Monetary Board established under the Monetary Law Act
(Chapter 422), in terms of Section 192 of the Civil Procedure Code, as amended by the Civil Procedure Code (Amendment)
Act, No. 06 of 1990, the Monetary Board has, having regard to the current rates of bank interest, determined that with effect
from 01st January 2015, the Legal Rate of Interest shall be seven point seven six per cent (7.76%) per annum.
N
IV ARD  AJITH  LESLIE  CABRAAL,
Chairman of the Monetay Board and
Governor
Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
Central Bank of Sri Lanka,
Colombo,
17th December, 2014.
