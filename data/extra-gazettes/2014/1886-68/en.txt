NOTICE  UNDER  THE  REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  ACT,  No. 44 OF 1980  AS  AMENDED  BY  THE
REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 27  OF  2013
CERTAIN persons have been internally displaced as a result of any actions of a terrorist militant or other group during the
recent past :
02. According to the Section 2 of the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 27 of 2013. any
persons,
2.1 who is or had been an internally displaced person ;
2.2 whose name appeared in the register of electors for any electoral district in the Northern Province or
Eastern Province for any year, until the end of the year 2009 and ;
2.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009 ;
shall on proof of the matters specified in above paragraphs and on production of a certificate issued by the Grama
Niladhari of the area in which the applicant is presently residing, to the effect that the applicant is or the applicant had been
an internally displaced person, be entitled to apply to the  registering officer of the electoral district within which the
applicant was permanently resident prior to May 18, 2009, to be registered in the register of electors of such electoral district,
03. According to the Section 3 of the Special Provisions Act, a child of Sri Lankan citizen mentioned in the 2nd
paragraph,
3.1 who had not attained the age of eighteen years on the date on which such citizen became an internally
displaced person and have attained the age of eighteen years or more on the date on which the revision
commenced in respect of the register in operation, at the time in which the application is made,
3.2 who were born after such citizen became an internally displaced person and have attained the age of
eighteen years or more on the date on which the revision commenced in respect of the register in operation,
at the time in which the application is made,
3.3 whose name has not been entered in any register in operation subsequent to the year 2009,
