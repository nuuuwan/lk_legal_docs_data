NOTICE CALLING FOR CLAIMS TO 
LAND PARCELS 
 
Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No : 51/0678 Gampaha District  
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 512000 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Wattala (Block 02) 
or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Division 
of 176 - Wattala in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Wattala 
in the District of Gampaha in the Province of Western should 
submit his claim to the undersigned before 01st October, 2014 In 
the event of failure  to submit any claim before the above-
mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to 
determine title to the land and accordingly publish such determi-
nation in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the 
above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 512000 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals‚Äô office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Land Title Set-
tlement.
