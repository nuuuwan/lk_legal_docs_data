THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: BE/LAND/2/AQUI/35-14. 
 
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4/3/2/2011/NRD/330.    
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under subsection (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, which the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for construction of high voltage electricity 
post at Dedduwawala. 
 
Description of the Land :  
 
Lot No. 592 land called Meegahakumbura, depicted in 
the Plan H/CGR/BLA/2013/688 (F.V.P 306) dated 11th 
April 2014, prepared by the Surveyor General and 
Situated in the Village of Dedduwawala in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Beliatta in Hambantota District. 
 
Lot No. 592 - Name of the Land: Meegaha-
kumbura; Claimant: 01. Unana Purana Wiharaya; 
Extent: 0.0253 Hectare. 
 
North: Lot No. 41; East: Lot No. 41; South: Lot No. 
41; West: Lot No. 41. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
of Beliatta on the 20th day of August, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 13th day of August, 2014, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 
All deeds and other documents in support of the 
claims should be produced at the inquiry. 
 
 J. H. S. P. J AYAMAHA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Beliatta.  
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Beliatta. 
01st June, 2014.
