DECLARATION OF THIBBOTUGODA SHRI SUBHADRARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Thibbotugoda Shri Subhadrarama Vihara sacred area land", for its long-
term security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014 ,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF THIBBOTUGODA SHRI SUBHADRARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.4587 Hectares
situated in Thibbotugoda Grama Niladari Division in Minor Division of Oyapalatha Korale in the Divisional Secretariat
Division of Walapane of the Administrative District of Nuwara Eliya in Central   Province depicted as Lot A and B in plan
No.NU/WLP/2012/028 dated 9
th February 2012 of Surveyor General.
