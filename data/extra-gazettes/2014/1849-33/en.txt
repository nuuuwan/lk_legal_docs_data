DRUG  DEPENDANT  PERSONS (TREATMENT  AND   REHABILITATION) ACT,  No. 54 OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the Drug Dependant Persons (Treatment and Rehabilitation) Act, No. 54
of 2007, read with paragraph (2) of Article 44 of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, on the recommendation of the National Dangerous Drug Control Board, do by this order, designate the
place specified in the Schedule hereto be a Treatment and Rehabilitation Centre for the purpose of providing care and
rehabilitation to drug dependant persons.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo.
SCHEDULE
Treatment and Rehabilitation Centre
