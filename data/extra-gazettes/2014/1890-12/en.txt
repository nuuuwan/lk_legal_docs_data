UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, the Minister of Higher Education, having obtained a report under section 70 C of the
aforesaid Act, in respect of the Horizon College of Business and Technology (Pvt) Limited, a Company incorporated in
Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No.7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 62877, do by this Order and subject to
the conditions specified in the Schedule hereto, recognize the Horizon College of Business and Technology (Pvt) Limited as
a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degrees
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the abbreviations are set out in the corresponding entries respectively
in Column II of the Schedule hereto.
SCHEDULE
1. Horizon College of Business and Technology (Pvt) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “Institute”) shall before the
expiry of two months from the date of granting the degree awarding status, submit its Articles of   Association duly
amended as set out below to the Specified Authority:-
(a) to specify higher education as an object in the Articles of Association;
(b) to undertake to inform the specified Authority of a voluntary or involuntary winding up of the company prior to
such winding up.
2. The Institute shall inform the Specified Authority of any changes made in any document submitted to the Specified
Authority for recognition of degree awarding status.
3. The Institute shall before the expiry of three months from the date of granting the degree awarding status, submit the
following details to the Specified Authority:-
(i) proof of appointment of qualified academic and non academic staff;
(ii) proof of the availability of the students prospectus, text books and learning material.
