Thoddam, Garden, Satheeskumar Rathnabama and others, Nerunchi
Thoddam, Garden,  Sellathamby Leelawathy and Nerunchi Thoddam,
Garden, Sellappa Selvaruban; South: Nerunchi Thoddam, Garden,
