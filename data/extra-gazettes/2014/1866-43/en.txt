FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
MIRIGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in  Mirigama  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance recognized political party to which Ranathunga
Arachchige Sarath Nandasiri Ranathunga the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to  Communicate his decision
within the Period Specified ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senaratne, Returning Officer of  Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of  Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mudiyanselage Samaradivakara
Karunanayake Chandrasekera Wasala Kumarasingha Manoj Bandara who had secured the next highest number of preferences
at the election held on 17th March 2011 to  Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha elected as Vice Chairman of the  Mirigama Pradeshiya
Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
P. A. S. S
ENARATNE,
Returning Officer,
 Mirigama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
13th June,  2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KALUTARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial  Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Group Leader of the independent group No. 1 to which the member Gamage Nihal Premashantha
Perera, belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty
(30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that  Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be
declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid group has nominated Mr. Jayalathge Don Saman Priyantha being an
eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of   Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Jayalathge Don Saman Priyantha elected as a
member of the Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Kalutara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kalutara,
13th June,  2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
BELIATTA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of
the Southern Province Provincial  Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized Political Party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the member Meegasdeniya Kankanamge Kasun, belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that  Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party has nominated Mr. Kottawaththa Tharanga
Ranjeewa being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathna, Returning Officer of   Beliatta Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, as amended by Filling of V acancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kottawaththa Tharanga
Ranjeewa elected as a member of the Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha.
D
EEPAL CHANDRARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office, Beliatta  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Hambantota,
11th June,  2014.
