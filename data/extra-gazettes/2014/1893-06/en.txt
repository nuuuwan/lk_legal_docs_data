I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose: 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “Viharawatta” in total 
extent of about 0.1125 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 1 in 
the Preliminary  Plan No. Maha 6386 dated 29.10.2014, 
surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor General, situated 
in the village of Doragamuwa in Pallegampaha Korale 
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Pathadumbara in Kandy District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows: 
 
Lot No. 1— Name of Land: Viharawatta; Extent: 
0.1125 Hectare 
 
Boundaries:  
 
 
North: Viharawatta claimed by Sri Shailapabbatharama 
Viharaya, Doragamuwa and Road; East: Road and 
Viharawatta claimed by Sri Shailapabbatharama 
Viharaya, Doragamuwa; South: Viharawatta claimed by 
Sri Shailapabbatharama Viharaya, Doragamuwa; West: 
Viharawatta claimed by Sri Shailapabbatharama 
Viharaya, doragamuwa;  
 
Names and Address of the Claimants : 1. The Chief 
Incumbent” Sri Shailapabbatharama Viharaya, 
Daragamuwa. 
 
2. Public Trustee, Department of Public Trustee, 
Colombo 07. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretary’s,  office at Pathadumbara on 
the 28th day of January 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 21st day of January 2015, the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
