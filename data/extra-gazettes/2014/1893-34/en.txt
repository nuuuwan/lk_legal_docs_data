THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three (03) allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
MAHA/HRS/2014/25P-4 and dated  23.03.2014 prepared by Senior
Superintendent of  Surveys in Kandy  District, situated in the Village
of Katugastota, in the Minor Division  of  Gangawata Korale in  the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale, in
Kandy  District in Central Province  and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01  –  Extent: 0.0027 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Remaining portion of this land; South:
Remaining portion of this land and Road (R.D.A.); West:  Road
(R.D.A.).
02. Lot No. 02  – Kukulalawatta  – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and remaining portion of this land; East:
Remaining portion of this land; South: Remaining portion of this
land and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.).
03. Lot No. 03  – Kukulalawatta  – Extent: 0.0001 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of this land and Road (R.D.A.); East:
Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and remaining portion of this
land; West: Remaining portion of this land.
 Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
