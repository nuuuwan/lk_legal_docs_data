I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired:  
 
Portions of land (Extent—1. 0623 Hectare) depicted as 
Lot No. 04 in the preliminary survey  plan bearing            
No. MR.2583 situated in the village of Talalla, in Grama  
Niladari Division of Talalla South, in Divisional 
Secretary’s division of De vinuwara, Matara District, 
Southern Province. 
 
Lot No. 4  
Name of Land: Maragahawatta; Description: Home 
garden, 01 Jak tree aged about 15 years and 04 Nedun 
Mara trees, 01 Mara Trees, 04 Jak trees, 19 Coconut 
Trees and 15 Teak Trees aged about 40-60 Years; Extent: 
1.0623 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat of Devinuwara 
on the 13th day of October, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 29th day of September, 2014, the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said Land/
servitude, the amount of Compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
 
                                   D. D. K. WICKRAMARACHCHI,  
 Acquisition Officer/Divisional Secretary, 
                                               Devinuwara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,
