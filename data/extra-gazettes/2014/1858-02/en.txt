SRI  LANKA  SUSTAINABLE  ENERGY  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 35  OF  2007
Order under Section 12(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 12(1) of the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007, I,
Achchige Don Susil Premajayantha, Minister of Environment and Renewable Energy, in consultation with the Ministers in
charge of the subjects of Technology, Research and Atomic Energy, Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development, Land
and Land Development, Public Administration and Home Affairs, Irrigation and Water Resource Management, Agricultural
Development and Agrarian Services and the relevant Minister of Eastern Provincial Council to whom the subject of Environment
is assigned, do by this Order declare that the areas of lands, described in the Schedule as Energy Development Areas in the
Ampara District for the purposes of the aforesaid Act.
The Orders declaring Energy Development Areas in the Ampara District, made under section 12(1) of the Sri Lanka
Sustainable Energy Authority Act, No. 35 of 2007, published in Gazettes Extraordinary No.1538/22 dated 26
th February 2008
and No.1632/10 dated 15th December 2009 are hereby rescinded, but without prejudice to anything lawfully done thereunder.
ACHCHIGE DON SUSIL PREMAJAYANTHA.
Minister of Environment and Renewable Energy.
Colombo,
10th April, 2014.
