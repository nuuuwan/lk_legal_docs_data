Regulations
The High Seas Fishing Operations Regulations No. 1 of 2014 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1878/12 dated
September 01, 2014 are hereby amended by the repeal of item 3 of Schedule III thereof and the substitution therefor of the
following item.
                   “3. Boats between Ten Decimal Three (10.3) to Fifteen (15) meters in length - Rupees Five Thousand (Rs. 5000).”
