THE  MEDICAL  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 105)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Health with the consultation of the Sri Lanka Medical Council under Section 72 read
with Section 19(c) of the Medical Ordinance (Chapter 105) and approved by the Parliament.
MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
Minister of Health
Colombo,
05th June, 2014.
Regulations
The Sri Lanka Medical Council (Appointment of Registrar) Regulations, No. 01 of 2014 published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1851/31 of February 28, 2014 are hereby amended by the repeal of regulation 3(1)(b) thereof and the
substitution therefor of the following :-
(1) by the repeal of regulation 3(1) (b) thereof and the substitution therefor of the following :-
"(b) have at least twenty years of experience as a Medical Practitioner in the public sector or in any
university ; preference may be given to applicants who have experience in the field of administration ;
and ".
(2) In regulation 9 (a) thereof, by the substitution for the words "age of sixty" of the words "age of sixty five".
