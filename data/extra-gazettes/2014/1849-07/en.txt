NATIONAL  FILM  CORPORATION  OF   SRI   LANKA  ACT,   No. 47  OF  1971
REGULATIONS  made by the Minister of Mass Media and Information under section 61 of the National Film Corporation of
Sri Lanka Act  No. 47 of 1971.
DR. KEHELIYA RAMBUKWELLA,
Minister of
Mass Media and Information.
Colombo,
10 th February 2014.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the National Film Corporation (Rescission) Regulations No. 1 of 2014.
2. The National Film Corporation (Licensing of Cinemas) Regulations No. 1 of 2007, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1499/17 of 30th May, 2007 are hereby rescinded.
3. The National Film Corporation (Distribution of Films) Regulations No. 2 of 2007, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1499/17 of 30th May, 2007 are hereby rescinded.
4. The National Film Corporation (Importation of Cinematic Film) Regulations No. 3 of 2007, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1499/17 of 30th May, 2007 are hereby rescinded.
5. The National Film Corporation (Miscellaneous Fee) Regulations No. 4 of 2007, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1502/11, of 20th June, 2007 are hereby rescinded.
6. Notwithstanding the repeal of the above Regulations every act or deed previously done thereunder shall be
deemed to be valid and in force.
