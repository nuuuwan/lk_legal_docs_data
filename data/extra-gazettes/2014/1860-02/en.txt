THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for the purpose of constructing
Magalle right hand canal of Deduru Oya Project.
The allotments of  land in total extent of about 5.5913 Hectares
in Surveyor-General’s Tracing No. KU/RSN/2012/114 and dated
03rd April, 2012, situated in the Villages of Nammuwawa
(FVP 2397), Landawatta (FVP 2396A), Polontalawa (FVP 2369),
Kadigawa (FVP 2395) in Magul Medagan Dahe Korale (West) Minor
Division in Divisional Secretariat of Rasnayakapura in Kurunegala
District in North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘B’ – Koongahamulahena Palugamehena Pathalagawa
yaya – Extent: About 0.3420 Hectare
North: Koongahamulahena Palugamehena Pathalagawa yaya
claimed by K. D. A. Kandage, Balance portion of this land and
Nammuwawa tank; East: Nammuwawa tank and Lot A; South:
Lot A and Balance portion of this land; West: Koongahamulahena
Palugamehena Pathalagawa yaya claimed by K. D. A. Kandage and
Lot C.
02. Lot ‘C’ – Koongahamulahena Palugamehena
Pathalagawayaya – Extent: About 0.5257 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Koongahamulahena
Palugamehena Pathalagawa yaya claimed by P. D. Kalyani and
Lot B; South: Koongahamulahena Palugamehena Pathalagawa yaya
claimed by P . D. Kalyani, Balance portion of this land and Road;
West: Lots D, E and Koongahamulahena claimed by P . D. Kalyani.
03. Lot ‘E’ – Koongahamulahena – Extent: About
0.0148 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land; East: Koongahamulahena
Palugamehena Pathalagawa yaya claimed by K. D. A. Kandage and
Lots C, D; South: Lots D and F; West: Lot F and Pathalagawa yaya
claimed by W. M. Pathirathna.
04. Lot ‘F’ – Pathalagawayaya – Extent: About 0.0659 Hectare
North: Aluthwewa, Balance portion of this land,
Koongahamulahena claimed by P. D. Kalyani and Lot E; East:
Lots E and D; South: Road and Balance portion of this land; West:
Aluthwewa.
05. Lot ‘G’ – Thukkiniyadaluwa Mukalana – Extent: About
0.1910 Hectare
North: Lot L, Koongahamulahena claimed by P. D. Kalyani and
Balance portion of this land; East: Balance portion of this land and
Aluthwewa; South: Aluthwewa, Balance portion of this land and

0.2206 Hectare
