FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  MAHARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Vice Chairman  in Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Vanni  Arachchige Susantha Siri the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Kulathunga Arachchige Dhammika Roshan, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kulathunga Arachchige Dhammika
Roshan elected as Vice Chairman of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
24th  June, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER
OF THE  NALLUR    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  to which the
member  Periyathampy Kanagasabapathipillai  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Nallur
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Kumarasamy
Kathirkamannathan  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kumarasamy Kathirkamannathan elected as a member of
the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
24th  June, 2014.
