No. 1856/22 – WEDNESDAY, APRIL 02, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two Allotments of the Land called Wemullawatta bearing an Extent
of about 0.0035 Hectare depicted as Lot "A" and "B" in the Advance
Tracing Plan No. MAHA/MHN/2012/657 dated 26th February,
2013 prepared by Surveyor-General situated in the Village of
Ampitiya Udagama, in the Minor Division of Gangawata Korale, in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale
in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A'  – Wemullawatta – Extent: 0.0030 Hectare
North: Wemullawatta claimed by S. Dabare and C. K. D. Withanage
and Lot B; East: Lot B and Lot No. 13 in P.P.Maha 6041 (Pradeshiya
Sabha Road); South: Lot No. 13 in P.P .Maha 6041 (Pradeshiya
Sabha Road); West: Wemullawatta claimed by S. Dabare.
02. Lot 'B'  – Wemullawatta – Extent: 0.0005 Hectare
North: Wemullawatta claimed by C. K. D. Withanage; East:
Lot No. 13 in P.P.Maha 6041 (Pradeshiya Sabha Road); South:
Lot No. 13 in P .P.Maha 6041 (Pradeshiya Sabha Road) and Lot A;
West: Lot A, Wemullawatta claimed by S. Dabare and C. K. D.
Withanage.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
