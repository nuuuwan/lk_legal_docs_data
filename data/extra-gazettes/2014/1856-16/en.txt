LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
My. No.: 4/3/1/ (Appointment - Acquisition)
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Land and Land Development shall order the below mention regulation under the
provisions of Section 63(2) of the Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) :
Regulation No. 2014/1 - Appointment
The officers mentioned in Schedule II, are hereby appointed for land acquisition duties under the Land Acquisition
Act for the period described in the Schedule III in the areas referred in Schedule I to expedite the land acquisition for Highway
Projects of the Road Development Authority in Mathara, Hambantota and Rathnapura Districts.
