DECLARATION OF OW AGIRI RAJAMAHA VIHARA IN HAKMANA, UMANGALA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Owagiri Rajamaha Vihara in Hakmana, Umangala sacred area", for its
long-term security.
 D
R. D. M. JAY ARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF HAKMANA UMANGALA OW AGIRI RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 4.9704 Hectares
depicted in lot No.A situated in Gangodagama Grama Niladari Division in the Minor Division of Kandabada Pattuwa-East in
the Divisional Secretariat Division of Hakmana of the Administrative Division of Matara in Southern Province depicted in
Tracing No.MR/HKM/2012/295 dated 14
th August 2012 of Surveyor General.
