1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE  NIKAWERATIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262) AS
AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Nikaweratiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation:
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
J. H. M. Wanninayake Jayapathma  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Nikaweratiya  Pradeshiya
Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a member  within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. R. Gunarathna being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
Now therefore I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Nikaweratiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. R. Gunarathna
elected as  a member of the  Nikaweratiya   Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Nikaweratiya   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala District,
29th  January, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE  IRAKKAMAM  PRADESHIYA
SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)
ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of    Vice  Chairman in Irakkamam   Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason  of Vice Chairman  has
been appointed as Chairman  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the  Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the  recognized political party to which
Aliyar Umarkatha Jabeer  the  Vice Chairman   belonged, was required by me  under Section 65 A  (1) of  the  aforesaid Ordinance,
to  decide upon the   member of  that  Irakkamam  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected
as  Vice Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Mohamed Ismail Naizer,   to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna ,  Returning  Officer of   Irakkamam  Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting  in
terms  of  Section  65 A (1) of  the Local Authorities  Elections  Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities  (Special Provisions)  Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr.  Mohamed Ismail Naizer, elected as
Vice Chairman  of   the Irakkamam   Pradeshiya   Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
 Irakkamam Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
29th  January, 2014.
