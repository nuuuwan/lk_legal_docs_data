FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  ATTANAGALLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Vice Chairman  in Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Athukorala Arachchige Priyantha Pushpakumara the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Attanagalla  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Gonadoowage Dimuthu Sanjeewa Jayathilaka, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Attanagalla Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Gonadoowage Dimuthu
Sanjeewa Jayathilaka elected as Vice Chairman of the  Attanagalla  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
22nd  July, 2014.
