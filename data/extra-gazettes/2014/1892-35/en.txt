ORDER
This order shall be cited as orders to grant relieves for fines with regard to arrears for motor vehicles and will be
operated at Office of the Commissioner of Motor Traffic of Western Province at Maligawatta and at Divisional Secretariat
level from 01.01.2015 to 31.03.2015.
Following relieves will be entitled for the license holders who have kept arrears for the license fees, if these owners
of the vehicles pay that arrears during the period from 01st of January 2015 to 01st of March, 2015.
1. Writing off the arrears and fines for the period before establishing Provincial Councils (1991).
2. Exempting arrears and fines for the specific period during which the vehicle was in custody of customs,
police, courts which are out of control of the owner (only on the occasions where the owners is guiltless).
