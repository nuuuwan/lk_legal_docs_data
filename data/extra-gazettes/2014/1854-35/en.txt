THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Silverdail Watta in total extent of about
0.0768 Hectare depicted in the Surveyor-General’s Advance Tracing
No. KA/DDN/2013/105 and dated 06.06.2013 situated in
Dodangoda Village in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Dodangoda in the District of Kalutara in Western Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Silverdail Watta – Extent: 0.0768 Hectare
North: Silverdail Watta claimant not known and Silverdail Watta
claimed by Niluka Nilmini; East: Silverdail Watta claimant not known;
South: Silverdail Watta claimant not known and Road; West: Silverdail
Watta claimant not known.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
