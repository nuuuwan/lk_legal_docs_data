FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
DOMPE    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United National Party  to which the former
member, Liyana Arachchige Thushara Hemachandra Liyanaarachchi  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as
a member of that Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Ranasinghe Arachchige
Sarath Kumara Ranasinghe being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Ranasinghe Arachchige Sarath
Kumara Ranasinghe elected as a member of the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Dompe  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
19th  November, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
NUWARAGAM  PALATHA CENTRAL   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Dead ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United Peoples Freedom Alliance  to which
the former member, Mr. Ekanayaka Keerthisinghe Patabendi Mudiyanse Ralahamilage Rasika Gayan Ekanayake belonged
was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person
eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be
declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. J. S. Jayasingha being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Mr. Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning Officer of  Nuwaragam
Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended
by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare
Mr. J. S. Jayasingha elected as a member of the Nuwaragam Palatha Central Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Nuwaragam Palatha Central  Pradeshiya Sabha.
