THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is  needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of  land  in total extent of  about 17.6938 Hectares,
depicted in  Advance Tracing No.MU/MLL/2012/043 dated 13th

Mullaitivu District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kampiththarai – Extent:  1.2752 Hectare
North: Path; East: Remaining portion of Kaddayan Nagamani and
Lot B; South: Lots B and C (Path); West: Lot C (Path) and Remaining
portion of Kaddayan Nagamani.

North: Lot A and Remaining portion of Mylvakanam Nagaratnam;
East: Remaining portion of Mylvakanam Nagaratnam and Lot F;
South: Lots F and C (Path); West: Lots C (Path) and A.

North: Remaining portion of Veeravagu Kanapathippillai and Lot
C (Path); East:  Lots C (Path) and E; South: Lot E, reservation along
Road and Remaining portion of Veeravagu Kanapathippillai; West:
Remaining portion of Veeravagu Kanapathippillai.
04. Lot ‘E’ – Kampiththarai – Extent:  0.7230 Hectare
North: Lots D and C (Path); East: Lot C (Path) and G; South: Lot

South: Lots J and C (Path); West: Lot C (Path) and B.
