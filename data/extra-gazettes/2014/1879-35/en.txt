LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
GALIGAMUWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of the  Galigamuwa    Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under     Section
10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262)  that Mr. Lekam Ralalage Weerasena of the Galigamuwa

National Party.
