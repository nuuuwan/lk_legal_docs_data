NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a) OF THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of the Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Prasanna Sugath Herath of the
Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member
of the United National Party.
K. J. S. MADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Elections Office, Ipalogama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Anuradhapura,
27th June, 2014.
