EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER  OF  THE
HOROWPOTHANA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL
AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Horowpothana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognised political party   namely United National Party, to which the member
Piyasenage Santha Piyasiri belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within
a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election  as a  member of that Horowpothana Pradeshiya
Sabha  who  shall be declared  elected as  a member  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Wijerathna Mudiyanselage
Charika Udayanga Wijerathna being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning Officer of  Horowpothana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr. Wijerathna Mudiyanselage
Charika Udayanga Wijerathna elected as a member of the Horowpothana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Horowpothana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Anuradhapura
4th  April, 2014.
