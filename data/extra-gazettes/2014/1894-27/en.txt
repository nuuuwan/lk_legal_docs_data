PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS

UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the Governor
BY , virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor
of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me .
01. Mr. K. A. B. Senevirathne, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the duties of  the
Deputy    Chief Secretary (Engineering Service) for  Uva Province   in addition to his permanent post, with effect
from the date 09.09.2014.
02. Mr. K. M. Dissanayake, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Educational  Administrative Service, As Deputy Director
(Education ) to the  Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Land,
Cultural Affairs, Social Welfare and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction for   Uva Province.
03. Mr. G. K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative  Service, to act  As Secretary to the
Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production, Inland Fisheries Industry, Transport  and  Tourism Affairs
for  Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 10.11.2014 till Mr. Gunadasa Samarasinghe
resumes his duty after sick leave, who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production,
Inland Fisheries Industry, Transport and Tourism Affairs for Uva Province.
04. Mr. W. M. Nimal Weerasinghe, Retired officer of the   Sri Lanka Educational  Administrative Service, has been re-
appointed on contract basis as Deputy Director  to the Education of the Viyaluwa Zonal Education, from the date
03.11.2014 till the appointment of  a Permanent officer or  Up to 02.11.2015.
05. Mr. G . K. S. L Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act As Deputy Chief Secretary
(Administration) for Uva Province, from the date 18.10.2014 to 01.11.2014, till the return of  Mrs. R. M. S. D.
Rathnayake, to the Island who is the Deputy Chief  Secretary (Administration)  for  Uva Province.
