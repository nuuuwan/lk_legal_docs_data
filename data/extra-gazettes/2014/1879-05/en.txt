THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land,  described
below in the 1st Paragraph, is needed for a public purpose and will
be acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in the 2nd
Paragraph, from which land that the servitude will be acquired.
1. Description of the Servitude.– Acquisition of Servitude over
the land  used for laying of pumping main and  Distribution lines of
Balagolla Water Tank of Kundasale Water Supply Scheme.
2. Description of the Land .– The allotments of land called
‘‘Balagollawatta’’ in total extent of about 0.0452 Hectare, in the
Advance Tracing No. Maha/KND/2012/80
p dated 17.05.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Kengalla
in Udagampaha Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kundasale in Kandy District, Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Balagollawatta – Extent: About 0.0105 Hectare
North: Balagollawatta claimed by V en. Thalpotha Dhammajothi
Thero; East: Road and Balagollawatta claimed by V en. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero; South:Balagollawatta claimed by Ven. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero; West: Balagollawatta claimed by V en. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero and Mosque Road.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Balagollawatta – Extent: About 0.0221 Hectare
North and East: Balagollawatta claimed by Ven. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero; South: Road and Balagollawatta claimed by
Ven. Thalpotha Dhammajothi Thero; West: Road and Balagollawatta
claimed by Ven. Thalpotha Dhammajothi Thero and Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Balagollawatta – Extent: About 0.0126 Hectare
North: Balagollawatta claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board  Balagollawatta claimed by Ven. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi  Thero; East: Balagollawatta claimed by Ven. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero and Sudharshana Mawatha; South: Sudharshana
Mawatha and Balagollawatta claimed by Ven. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero; West: Balagollawatta claimed by V en. Thalpotha
Dhammajothi Thero.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/133.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
17th June, 2014.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5

Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land,  described

be acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in the 2nd
Paragraph from which land that the servitude will be acquired.

the Additional land for Access Road to Balagolla Water Tank of
Kundasale Water Supply Scheme.
2. Description of the Land .–  An allotment of land called
‘‘Balagollawatta’’ in total extent of about 0.0250 Hectare, in the
Advance Tracing No. Maha/KND/2012/79
p dated 17.05.2012,
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village of  Kengalla
in Udagampaha Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kundasale in Kandy District, Central Province and
