FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF   CHAIRMAN OF  THE  KATUNAYAKE  SEEDUWA  URBAN
COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Katunayake Seeduwa Urban  Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as
a member of Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which Hamudu
Dhammika Lalantha Gunasekera the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Katunayake Seeduwa Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Chairman  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to nominate
Mr. M. Dinal Perera, to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Katunayake Seeduwa Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. Dinal Perera, elected as
Chairman of the Katunayake Seeduwa Urban Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katunayake Seeduwa Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
26th  May, 2014.
