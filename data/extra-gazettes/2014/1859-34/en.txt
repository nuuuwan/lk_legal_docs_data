EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER  OF  THE
KURUNEGALA  MUNICIPAL COUNCIL  UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Kurunegala Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognised political party   namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which the
member Abdul Saththar belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election  as a  member of that Kurunegala Municipal Council
who  shall be declared  elected as  a member  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Abdul Saththar  being an
eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Kurunegala Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr. Abdul Saththar  elected as a
member of the Kurunegala Municipal Council.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kurunegala Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala
07th  April, 2014.
