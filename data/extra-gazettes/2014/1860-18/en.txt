EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE   A  MEMBER  OF  THE   JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL  UNDER
SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member in Jaffna Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member of
the party ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the member Sutharsingh Vijiyakanth belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  the Jaffna
Municipal Council who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated  Mr. Thirunavukkarasu
Sivadasan being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnudurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in  terms of Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provi-
sions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Thirunavukkarasu Sivadasan elected as a member of the  Jaffna
Municipal Council.
P. KUGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
17th of  April,  2014.
