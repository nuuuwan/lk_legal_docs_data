DECLARATION OF SEETHAELIYA SHRI GANGARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as " Seethaeliya Shri Gangarama Vihara sacred area", for its long-term
security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF SEETHAELIYA SRI GANGARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 1.0774 Hectares
covered by Lot No. 1 to 9 situated at Seethaeliya village in Goradihela Grama Niladari Division, 478, Minor Division of Oya
Palatha in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Nuwara Eliya of the Administrative District of Nuwara Eliya in Central
Province depicted in preliminary plan No.NE2756  dated 13
th October 2011 of Surveyor General.
