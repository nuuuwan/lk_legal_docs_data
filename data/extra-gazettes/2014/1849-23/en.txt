THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  2   OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic  Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka and Minister-in-charge of the subject
of urban development, being of opinion that the lands specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the purpose
of expand the access of Pola Road and Pola Development at Kegalle under Kegalle Town Development Project do by this
order declare that the said lands is required for the such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
07th February 2014,
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo.
