PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & C., by the Governors
My No. : 01/10/03/04.
1A
UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the Governor  of  the  Uva   Province
I, hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva Province
under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987.
01. Dr. P. W. W. Dissanayake, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Ayurvedic Medical Service, to act as Medical Officer of
the Bibila Medagama Ayurveda Hospital, in addition to his permanent post, with effect from the date 07.07.2014.
02. Mr. W. M. Nimal Weerasinghe, Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, reappointed
on contract basis as Deputy Director (Education) to the Ministry of Education and Local Government of Uva
Province, from the date 14.08.2014 till the appointment of a Permanent Officer or up to 13.08.2015.
03. Mrs. Gnana Rathnayake, Retired Officer of the Sri Lanka Administative Service, reappointed on contract basis as
Municipal Commissioner of the Badulla Municipal Council, from the date 29.08.2014 till the appointment of a
Permanent  Officer or up to 31.12.2014.
04. Mr. R. M. Ananda Rathnayake, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, to perform the
duties of the Director to Education of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 20.09.2014
to 24.09.2014, till the return of Mr. R. M. P. Rathnayake, to the Island who is the Director to Education of Uva
Province.
H. M. S
OMATHILAKA,
Secretary to the Governor,
Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
King's Street,
Badulla.
