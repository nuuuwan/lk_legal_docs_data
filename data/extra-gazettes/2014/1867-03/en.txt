FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBERSHIP  OF  THE
IBBAGAMUW A  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the member
Mr. Anura Udaya Munasinghe Munasinghe Arachchige  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. D. M. Piyadasa
Disanayake being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama Returning Officer of   Ibbagamuwa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. D. M. Piyadasa
Disanayake elected as a member of the Ibbagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ibbagamuwa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
02nd June,  2014.
