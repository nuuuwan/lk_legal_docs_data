 STATUTORY  DETERMINATION  UNDER  SECTION  19  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No. 1 OF 1972
WHEREAS a statutory declaration under Section 18 of the Law has been made by Mr. Harry Halangoda of Halangoda
Walawuva, Matale on behalf of his deceased wife Mrs. Brenda Seelawathie Halangoda Delvita, the Land Reform Commission
by virtue of the powers vested in it under Section 19 of the Law, has made a statutory determination to the estate of the
deceased declarant specifying the portion of agricultural land owned by the statutory lessee which she shall be allowed to
retain. The agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the statutory lessee is described in the schedule below. The
Statutory Determination No. 4614 Published in the Gazette Extrarordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
bearing No. 1780/13 and dated 16th October 2012 is hereby cancelled.
N
IMAL G. PUNCHIHEWA, Attorney-at-Law,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
