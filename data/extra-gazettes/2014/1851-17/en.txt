EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE  MATARA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262) AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Matara Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party namely United People’s Freedom  Alliance,  to which the
member Mr. Galappaththi Chathura Harsha Kumara belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thrity (30)  days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that Matara  Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member,
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Jayawardana Widana
Pathiranage Janaka being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Mr. Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Matara Municipal Council,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Jayawardana Widana Pathiranage
Janaka elected as a member of the  Matara Municipal Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Matara,
11th February, 2014.
