THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land, which names and extent given below,
depicted in Surveyor General's Advance Tracing No. MA/MTL/
2013/353 dated 08.01.2014, situated at Kaludewala (Ward No. 02,
Mandandawala) (within Municipal Council Limit of Matale) Village
in Matale Divisional Secretary's Division, Matale District, Central
Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot  ‘A’  – Polwatta Kumbura, Assmt. No. 260/1, Rattota
Road – Extent: 0.0461 Hectare
North: Polwatta Kumbura claimed by K. S. Perera; East:
Suduganga;  South: Polwatta Kumbura claimed K. S. Perera and I. K.
Perera;West: Lot B and Polwatta Kumbura claimed by K. S. Perera.
02. Lot  ‘B’  – Polwatta Kumbura, Assmt. No. 260, Rattota
Road – Extent: 0.0043 Hectare
North: Polwatta Kumbura claimed by K. S. Perera and I. K. Perera;
East: Lot A and Polwatta Kumbura claimed by K. S. Perera;  South
and West: Polwatta Kumbura claimed by I. K. Perera.
