FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  ATTANAGALLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (12)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Attanagalla  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being Elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which the
member Rajapaksa Patirannehelage Upul Mahendra Rajapaksa  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a
member of that Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha  shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Akureti Gamage Sampath
Janaka Kumaara being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Akureti Gamage Sampath Janaka
Kumaara elected as a Member of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
28th April, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  CHAIRMAN OF THE  ATTANAGALLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Attanagalla  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being Elected as a
member of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Rajapaksa
Patirannehelage Upul Mahendra Rajapaksa the  Chairman belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha who belong s to such party who shall be
declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty 30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Athukorala Arachchige Priyantha Pushpakumara to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Attanagalla  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Athukorala Arachchige Priyantha
Pushpakumara elected as Chairman of the Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Attanagalla Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
28 April, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  KATANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Katana  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member
Mutuwadige Godwin Chandrasiri Gunawardana  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Katana
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Stephen Malcom Fernando
Pulle being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Katana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Stephen Malcom Fernando Pulle elected as a
Member of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
