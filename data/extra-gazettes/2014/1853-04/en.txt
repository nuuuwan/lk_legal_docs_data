LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 07 
 
My Ref. No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/11(LA/3515). 
 
Land Min. Ref. No.: LD/05/2006/HW/304 
 
I hereby Notice under Sub-section 7, of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acquisition 
(Amendments) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is 
required for public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of the land called Anninkandawatta 
situated at Mathugobe village in Grama Niladhari's 
Division Kalugalahena (244B) of the Divisional 
Secretariat, Kotapola of Matara District in the Southern 
Province, depicted as Lot Nos. from 1435 to No. 1438 of 
Supplementary Sheet No. 123 of Sheet No. 105 in the 
FVP. 169 made by survey General, containing in extent 
about 0.0826 Hectare and boundaries of the land given 
below. Acquistion land for constructing bridge No. 85/7 
of Galle, Deniyaya Rathnapura road. 
 
1. Lot No. 1435 - Anninkandawatta containing in 
extent about 0.0211 Hectare.  
 
North: Lot No. 87; East: Lot No. 87 and Anninkanda 
Dola; South: Anninkand Dola and the Road; West: Road 
and Lot No. 87. 
 
1. Lot No. 1436 - Anninkandawatta containing in 
extent about 0.0382 Hectare.  
 
North: Lot No. 87; East: Lot Nos. 87, 1437 and the 
Road; South: Road; West: Road, Anninkand Dola and Lot 
No. 87. 
3. Lot No. 1437 - Anninkanda watta containing in 
extent about 0.0095 Hectare.  
 
North: Lot Nos. 87, 1438; East: Lot No. 1438 and the 
Road; South: The Road Lot No. 1436; West: Lot No. 
1436 and 87. 
 
4. Lot No. 1438 - Anninkanda watta containing in 
extent about 0.0138 Hectare.  
 
North: Lot No. 87; East: Lot No. 87, the Road and Lot 
No. 1437; South: Lot No. 1437; West: Lot No. 1437 and 
87. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude 
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally 
or by agents duly authorized in writing before me at the 
Divisional Seccretariat, Kotapola on 05th May, 2014 at 
10.00 O’ Clock. 
 
Further, I hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 28th April, 2014 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation of the said land/servitude, the amount 
of compensation and the details of computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 G ANESHA AMARASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kotapola. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kotapola, 
07th March, 2014.
