THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Kimbula Ela Ground depicted as Lot
A in Advance Tracing No.CO/COL/2013/153 dated 26.04.2013
executed by the  Surveyor General in extent of 0.6716 Hectare situated
in the Village of Modera in Grama Niladari Division of No.  6,
Modera within the  Divisional Secretariat of Colombo  in the District
of Colombo, Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Kimbula Ela Ground land at Kimbula Ela Road
– Extent: 0.6716 Hectare
North : Kimbula Ela Road and Kimbula Ela; East: Kimbula Ela;
South: Kimbula Ela Road and Sri Nandanarama Temple bearing Assmt.
No. 594/1 of Kimbula Ela Road; West: Sri Nandanarama Temple
bearing Assmt. No. 594/1 of Kimbula Ela Road and Kimbula Ela
Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: 4/3/10/2012/UDA/341.
