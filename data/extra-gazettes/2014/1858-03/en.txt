THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON

Order under Section 4(1)

THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. J. Piyasena, No. 271, Hagala, Thunkama of the one  part,
and
National Paper Company Limited, No. 93, Jawatta Road, Colombo 05 of the other  part.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. J. Piyasena who served the Embilipitiya Paper Factory of the National Paper Company Limited as the Assistant Security Officer
has been caused injustice by the non-payment of overtime from April 2003 to October 2007, and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
V. B. P. K. W
EERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 17th day of March 2014.

The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether Mr. K. S. Pushpakumara who was promoted to the post of Accounts Clerk – Grade 9 of the Sri Lanka State Engineering Corporation
