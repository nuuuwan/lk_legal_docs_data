FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER
OF  THE  KARACHCHI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
 LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Karachchi  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  to which   the
former member,  Sivarasa Sivachelvan  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Karachchi
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Manikam Sivapalan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Manikam Sivapalan elected as a Member of the Karachchi
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Karachchi Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
05th August, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  KATANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in Katana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Arachchige Nanoby Antony Upul Rajitha the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Katana Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. M. Nanda Wijerathne, to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Katana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. Nanda Wijerathne, elected as Vice
Chiarman of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
04th August, 2014.
