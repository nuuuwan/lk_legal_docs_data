THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An  allotment of  land called  Galkaduwahena  in total extent of
about 0.2023 Hectare, depicted in   Advance Tracing No. MR/WLP/
2013/382 and dated 20.07.2013 made by Surveyor General and
situated in the Village of  Palalla in Minor Division of Weligama
Korale,  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Welipitiya,Matara District
in Southern Province.
1. Lot ‘A’ – Galkaduwahena – Extent: About 0.2023 Hectare
North: Lot  IR, P .P. 14069 and Road; East: Road; South:  Road
and Lot IR, P .P. 14069; West: Lot IR, P.P. 14069.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Reference No.: 4-3/9/2012/WS/385.
