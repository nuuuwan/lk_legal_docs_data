LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  under  Section  10A  (1)  (a)
KELANIYA  PRADESHIY A  SABHA
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne, Returning Officer of the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sahba do hereby declare Under
Section 10 (A) (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Abeynayake Don Sanjaya Sujeewa of the Kelaniya
Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Council as he has ceased to be a member of  the People's Liberation
Front.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
 Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
