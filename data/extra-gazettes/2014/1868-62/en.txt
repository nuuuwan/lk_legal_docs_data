FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
AMBALANTOTA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha   has fallen vacant by reason of being elected for
Southern Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Peoples Liberation Front to which the
member  Athula Welandagoda Hewage   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Ambalantota
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Wanduramba
Mudiyanselage Shantha  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathne,  Returning Officer of  Ambalantota Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Wanduramba Mudiyanselage
Shantha elected as a member of the Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARA THNE,
Returning Officer,
Ambalantota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota
26th  June, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
 SOORIYAWEWA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in Sooriyawewa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as  a
member of the Southern Province Provincial Council  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United Peoples Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Peduru Arachchige Ajith Muthukumarana the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Sooriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as  Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate  Mr. Jathunge Eranda Janaka, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathne,  Returning Officer of  Sooriyawewa Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Jathunge Eranda Janaka
elected as Chairman of the Sooriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Sooriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
26th  June, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF   THE
MINUWANGODA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Minuwangdoa Pradeshiya Sabha   has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
memeber of the Western Province  Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member
Edward Gunasekara belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Minuwangoda Pradeshiya
Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Eranda Indika Subasingha
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Minuwangoda Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Eranda Indika Subasingha
elected as a Member of the Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
26th  June, 2014.
