To :
1. Maxwell Parakrama Paranagama Esquire
2. Madam Dimingu Badathuruge Priyanthi Suranjana Vidyaratne
3. Madam Mano Ramanathan
Greetings :
Whereas, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in pursuance of the
provisions of Section 2 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) appointed a Commission to inquiry by the Directive
dated August 12, 2013 ;
whereas, amendments and additions have been made to the said original Directive from time to time ;
And  whereas, five international experts have been appointed by me to serve on an Advisory Council to the
Commission of Inquiry by the aforesaid amendments and additions ;
And, the need for one more expert to be appointed has been identified by me ;
I hereby appoint Motoo Noguchi Esquire, the international expert to serve on the said Advisory Council :
Given at Colombo under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this Twenty Forth day of
November, Two Thousand and Fourteen.
           By Order of  His  Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
