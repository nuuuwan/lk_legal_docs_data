FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
WATTALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings of
the Council for a Period of three months without Permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the Former member Antony Coorey, belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Wattala
Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Porage Don Felix Palith
Thissa Mohan Bandara being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of   Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Porage Don Felix Palith Thissa
Mohan Bandara  elected as a member of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S.  S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
15th  August,  2014.
