EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
VALIKAMAM   SOUTH  PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL
AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending
meetings of the Council for a period of three months without premission ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, to which the member
Vasanthanathan Sivanathan belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Valikamam South
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Valikamam South  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr.Aarumugam Theiventhiram  who had secured the
highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election held on 23rd of July of 2011 to
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha elected as  a member of the Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such
vacancy occurred.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
18th  March, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBERSHIP  OF  THE
MULATIYANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Member in Mulatiyana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognised political party namely  United People’s Freedom Alliance,  to which the
member Meegahalanda Durage Warunadewa  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate with a period of thirty (30) days  person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Mulatiyana
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be  declared  elected as  a Member  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Ganhewage Martenis
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Mr. Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Mulatiyana  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ganhewage Martenis  elected
as a member of the Mulatiyana Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAY A KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Mulatiyana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
23rd  April, 2014.
