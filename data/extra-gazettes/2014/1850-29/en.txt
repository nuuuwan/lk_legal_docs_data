 NOTICE UNDER  SECTION  10 A( 1) (A) OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kobeigane  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of the Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities  Elections Ordinance that Mr. Samara Arachchige Don Vijitha Pemalal
of the Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA.
Returning Officer,
Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala.
