FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
NARAMMALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United National Party the recognized political party to which Mr. R. S.
Dinapoorna the former member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declard elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Dangolle Lekamalage Thilak  Ajantha Lal Kumara  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of   Narammala
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Dangolle
Lekamalage Thilak Ajantha Lal Kumara  elected as a member of the Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
03rd  September,  2014.
