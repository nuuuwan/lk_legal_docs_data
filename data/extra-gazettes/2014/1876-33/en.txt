FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN OF THE
BOPE - PODDALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in Bope- Poddala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman
has been appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,   to which
Ukwaththe  Chandima Thero  the  Former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Bope - Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of   thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Padmasena Mawella Vithanage  to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of  Bope - Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.Padmasena Mawella Vithanage
elected as Vice Chairman of the  Bope - Poddala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Bope - Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
19th  August, 2014.
