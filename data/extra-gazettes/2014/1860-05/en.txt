THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT  (CHAPTER  131)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 22 of the Industrial Dispute Act, Chapter 131, Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956) as amended by the Industrial Disputes (Amendments) Act,  Nos. 14 & 62 of 1957 and 4 of 1962
[read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1968] I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby -
a. appoint the persons specified in the schedule hereto be members of the panel of which Industrial Courts
shall be constituted ; and
b. specify the period of three yeras from 24th April, 2014 as the period of office of such members.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
25th April, 2014.
