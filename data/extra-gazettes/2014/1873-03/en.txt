Provincial  Councils  Notifications
 NORTHERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
THE  Notification made under Section 255 of the Urban Council Ordinance, No. 67 of 1939 read with Section 2 of the Provincial
Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
2.  I, C. V . Wigneswaran, the Chief Minister  and the Minister of Finance and  Planning, Law and Order, Lands,  Road
Development, Electricity, Housing and Construction, Water Supply, Co-operative Development, Social Service and
Rehabilitation, Women's Affairs, Industries and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local Government and Provincial
Administration and Food Supply and Distribution of Northern Province, by virtue of powers vested in me under sub-
section (1) of the Section 184 (Chapter 255) of the Urban Council Ordinance, No. 67 of 1939 read with Section 2 of the
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, have appointed Mr. Kandiah Ariyanayakam, the retired
District Court Judge, to an individual commission to submit the report by investigating activities of Valvettiturai Urban
Council, regarding the following matters/ charges under the sub- section 184 (1B) (a) (Chapter 255) of the Urban Council
Ordinance No. 67 of 1939.
Matters/ Charges to be investigated :-
(i) Council was made stagnant by the decision of council meeting ;
(ii) Persistent refusal to hold or attend meetings or to transact business at any meetings that may be held ;
(iii) W ilful neglect or misconduct in the performance of the duties imposed by this Ordinance ;
(iv) Incompetence and mismanagement ;
(v) Abuse of the power conferred by this Ordinance.

2A
03.  I, C. V . Wigneswaran, the Chief Minister  and the Minister of Finance and  Planning, Law and Order, Lands,  Road
Development, Electricity, Housing and Construction, Water Supply, Co-operative Development, Social Service and
Rehabilitation, Women's Affairs, Industries and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local Government and Provincial
Administration and Food Supply and Distribution of Northern Province, by virtue of powers vested in me under sub-
section (1)  (1B)  (a) (iii) of  Section 184 (Chapter 255) of the Urban Council Ordinance, No. 67 of 1939 read with Section 2 of
the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions)Act, No. 12 of 1989, do hereby suspend the functions of Valvettiturai
Urban Council in Jaffna District with immediate effect. I do hereby order Mr. Constantine Jackseel, the Regional Assistant
Commissioner of Local Government of Jaffna District to execute the power and carry out the tasks thereof.
