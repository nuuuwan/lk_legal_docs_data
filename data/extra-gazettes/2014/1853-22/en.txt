FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER  OF THE  KOTIKAWATTE - MULLERIYAWA  PRADESHIYA S ABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of a member  in Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of
resignation :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which the
member, Gardi Hewage Chaminda Sujeewa Dharmawikrama belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thrity (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a
member of that Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Hewa Sahabanduge
Randika being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardena,  Returning Officer of  Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Hewa Sahabanduge
Randika elected as a Member of the Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDENA,
Returning Officer,
 Kotikawatte - Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
07th  March, 2014.
