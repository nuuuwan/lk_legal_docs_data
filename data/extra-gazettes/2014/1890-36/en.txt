FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Mawanella   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  removal  of  member;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  United People's Freedom Alliance, to which
the  Former member  P . P. Wickramasinha   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Mawanella
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Haputhenne Viyannalage
Vasantha Sumith Priyalal Weragoda  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha,  Returning Officer of  Mawanella  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Haputhenne Viyannalage V asantha Sumith
Priyalal Weragoda elected as  a member of the  Mawanella   Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kegalle,
27th  November, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
MAWANELLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of   Chairman  in  Mawanella   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  removal of  Chairman;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,   to which   P.
P. Wickramasinha   the  Former  Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
decide upon the Chairman   of that Mawanella  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as  Chairman within a period of   thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. D. M. Kirthi  Bandara Thilaka   to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha,  Returning Officer of  Mawanella  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act,  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.D. M. Kirthi  Bandara Thilaka  elected as
Chairman of the  Mawanella  Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Mawanella   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kegalle,
27th  November, 2014.
