No. 1856/45 –  FRIDAY,  APRIL  04, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
My Ref. No.: DSB/LND/3/AQUIC/19-ii.     
Land Ministry's Reference No.: LD/05/2008/PC/388. 
 
 
I hereby give Notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendments) Act , No. 28 of 1964, which 
the Government intends to acquire the following land  
which is required for construction a playground for 
Angulmaduwa Village. 
 
Description of the Land : 
 
Lot No. 25 of land called Madiwila alias 
Mullepanguwa, depicted in the plan H/BLA/2011/424 (F. 
V. P. 283) dated 28th March 2013, prepared by the 
Surveyor General and Situated in the Village of 
Angulmaduwa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of 
Beliatta in Hambantota District. 
 
 
Lot No. 25 - Name of the Land: Madiwila alias 
Mullepanguwa; Extent: 1.0112  Hectare. 
 
North: Road (to temple), Lot No. 3 and Lot No. 1; 
East: Lot No. 03, Road and Lot No. 1; South: Lot No. 3; 
West: Lot No. 3 and road (to temple) 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Beliatta on the 28th April, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing          
(in duplicate) on or before the 16th April, 2014, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and other documents in support of the 
claims should be produced at the inquiry. 
  
 
 J. H. S. P. J AYAMAHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Beliatta. 
  
Divisional Secretariat,  
Beliatta. 
02nd April, 2014.
