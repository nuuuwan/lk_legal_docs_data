Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN Industrial Dispute in respect of the matters specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies
this order exists between,
1. Lake House Employees' Union, No. 35, D. R. Wijewardana Mawatha, Lake House, Colombo 10 (On behalf of 144 Employees),
2. Sri Lanka Nidahas Sewaka Sangamaya, Lake House Branch, No. 35, D. R. Wijewardana Mawatha, Lake House, Colombo 10
(On behalf of 1030 Employees), of the one part
and
The Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited, No. 35, D. R. Wijewardana Mawatha, Lake House, Colombo 10, of the other
part.
NOW THEREFORE I Gamini Lokuge, Minister of Labour and Labour Relations do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4(1)
of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Act Nos. 14
of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968),  hereby appoint Mr.  T. E.
Santharajan, No. 181/1, A.P .T. 503, W. A. Silva Mawatha, Colombo 06 to be the arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him  for
settlement by arbitration.
GAMINI LOKUGE,
Minister of Labour and Labour Relations.
Colombo.
26th August, 2014.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the 1174 employees mentioned in the Schedule, working at the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited, who were earlier
paid the non-recurrent cost of living gratuity (NRCLG) based on the Colombo Consumer Price Index (CCPI), have been caused injustice by
the ex-parte ceasation of the said allowance from 01.10.2009 and the introduction of an Annual Lump Sum and if so, to what reliefs they are
entitled.
