No. 1859/29 – THURSDAY, APRIL 24, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The under-mentioned allotment of land in extent of 0.3288 Hectare
depicted as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing No. H/WRK/2013/380,
dated 26th June, 2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the Village of Mandaduwa in North Giruwapattuwa Minor Division
in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Weeraketiya in Hambantota
District and bounded as follows:
North: George Rajapaksha Play Ground and Land situated in
Irrigation Office; East and South: Bogahahena claimed by Deepal
Weerasinghe; West: George Rajapaksha Play Ground.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No.: 4-3/10/2013/UDA/274.
