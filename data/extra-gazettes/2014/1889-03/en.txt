THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT,  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. :  2/2/1/125.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 - 04 (Lots 04) allotments in total extent of about
0.1848 Hectare  of  land in Primarary Plan No. Gam: 3942 and
dated 12.11.2014 Prepared by the Surveyor-Superintendent
on behalf of Surveyor - Gampaha, General situated in the
Village of Ihala Ibulgoda in the Gramaniladhari Division 240 -
Ibulgoda North, Minor Division of Meda Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Gampaha in Gampaha
District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Assmt. No. 440 - Kandy Road; Description:
Paddy field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M. Peter and
M. T. Priyadarshana, No. 44, Ihala Ibulgoda, Ibulgoda;  Extent:
0.0135 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Millagahakumbura; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M. P . P . A. Kumara and
Jayalal Mallawaarachchi, No. 319/1, Belummahara, Ibulgoda;
Extent: 0.0774 Hectare.
1A
03. Lot No. 03
Name of land: Millagahakumbura; Description: Paddy field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Sarath Padmakumara,
No. 316/A, Belummahara, Ibulgoda;  Extent: 0.0887 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 04
Name of land: –; Description: Canal; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Not be found;  Extent: 0.0052 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Office of  Gampaha
on 08th December, 2014  at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
01st  December, 2014 the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
C. P . W. GUNATHILAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
