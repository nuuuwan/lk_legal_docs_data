 CONTROL   OF  PESTICIDE  ACT,  No.  33  OF  1980
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the Control of Pesticides Act, No. 33 of 1980, I, Gamage Anura
Wickramasiri Wijesekara Registrar of Pesticides, do by this Order declare that every licence issued in respect of pesticides
containing the active ingredients bearing the Abstract Service Registry Number specified in Schedule hereto shall be cancelled.

R. G. A. W.  WIJESEKARA,
Registrar of Pesticides.
