EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
PANADURA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Chairman in Panadura Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party, to which
M. D. Nandana Gunathilaka the former Chairman, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to  decide upon the member of that Panadura Urban Council who belongs to such party, who shall be declared elected as
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Chakrawarthige Jeradi Riyensi Fernando fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of  Panadura Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Chakrawarthige Jeradi Riyensi Fernando elected
as  a Chairman of the Panadura Urban Council.
R
ASIKA  PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
26th December,  2014.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER  OF  THE
PANADURA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of  a member in Panadura Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the former member, M. D. Nandana Gunathilaka, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Panadura
Urban Council who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Waduge Vijith Vishwanath
Fernando being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of Panadura Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Waduge Vijith Vishwanath Fernando elected as  a
member of the Panadura Urban Council.
R
ASIKA  PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
26th December,  2014.
