EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini.’s Ref. No.: KDS/YNU/4/1/LD/F/565. 
 
My Ref. No. : 4/3/15/2011/VE/16. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964(Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land depicted as Lot Nos. 01 and 
02 in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6350 situated in the 
Village called Yatigammana in the Grama Niladhari 
Division No. 186 of Kirimetiyawatta in Kandupalatha 
Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Yatinuwara in Kandy District of the Central 
province and morefully described as follows:- 
 
 
 The Schedule 
 
Province    : Central Province 
District   : Kandy 
Divisional Secretary’s Div. : Yatinuwara 
Village    : Yatigammana 
Preliminary Plan No.  : Maha 6350 
Name of the Land  : Kirimetiyawatta 
Lot No.    : 01 
Total Extent  : 2.6002 Hectares 
Claimant   : Land Reform Commission 
The Schedule 
 
Province    : Central Province 
District   : Kandy 
Divisional Secretary’s Div.: Yatinuwara 
Village   : Yatigammana 
Preliminary Plan No.  : Maha 6350 
Name of the Land  : Kirimetiyawatta 
Lot No.    : 02 
Total Extent  : 3.9167 Hectares 
Claimant   : Land Reform Commission 
 
. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Yatinuwara 
Divisional Secretariat on 25th day of August, 2014 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of 
August, 2014 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 C HANDRA  JAYATHUNGA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Yatinuwara.
