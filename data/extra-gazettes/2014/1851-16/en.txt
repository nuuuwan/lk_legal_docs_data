1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE  KARUWALAGASWEWA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262) AS
AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Karuwalagaswewa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
meetings:
And whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party  namely  United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the member   Wanninayaka Mudiyanselage Gamini Sarath Kumara    belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days. a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as
a member of that  Karuwalagaswewa  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Jayalath Arachchilage
Indika  Kumara Jayalath  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan,  Returning Officer of  Karuwalagaswewa   Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Jayalath Arachchilage Indika  Kumara Jayalath
elected as  a member of the  Karuwalagaswewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P
AKKIY ARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Karuwalagaswewa   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
11th  February, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER  OF  THE  UDUNUWARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS AMENDED BY LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No.  30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Udunuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party  namely  People’s Liberation Front, to which  the member
Arabegedara Sunil Wickramasinghe   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days. a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that   Udunuwara
Pradeshiya Sabha   who shall be declared elected as a member ,
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has  nominated  Mr. Gangoda Asitha Ranaweera
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
Now therefore I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka,  Returning Officer of  Udunuwara   Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Gangoda Asitha Ranaweera
elected as  a member of the  Udunuwara   Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
 Udunuwara   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kandy,
10th  February, 2014.
