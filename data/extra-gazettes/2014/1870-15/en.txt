Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini’s Ref No.: LD/05/2012/PC/303. 
 
My Ref. No.: KDS/YNU/411/LD/A/707.   
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the Land to be acquired:  
 
Two allotments of land depicted as lot No. 01 and 02 
in the Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6347 situated in the 
village called Uda Eeriyagama in the Grama Niladhari 
division of Kendakaduwa in the Gangapalatha Korale 
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Yatinuwara in Kandy District of the Central province and 
more fully described as follows. 
 
Lot No. 01  
 
Province: Central Province; District: Kandy; 
Divisional Secretary’s Div.: Yatinuwara; Village: Uda 
Eeriyagama; Preliminary Plan No.: Maha 6347; Name of 
the Land: Kotakambewatta; Original Claimant: Chandra 
Kumari Herath, Sirigemunu Niwasa, Uda Eeriyagama, 
Peradeniya; Claimant: State; Total Extent: 0.0525 
Hectare. 
Lot No. 02  
 
Province: Central Provin ce; District: Kandy; 
Divisional Secretary’s Div.: Yatinuwara; Village: Uda 
Eeriyagama; Preliminary Plan No.: Maha 6347; Name of 
the Land: Iskolewatta; Original Claimant: Chandra 
Kumari Herath, Sirigemunu Niwasa, Uda Eeriyagama, 
Peradeniya; Claimant: State; Total Extent: 0.0021 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Yatinuwara 
Divisional Secretariat on 20th August, 2014 at 10.30 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 12th August, 2014 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 C HANDRA JAYATHUNGA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Yatinuwara.
