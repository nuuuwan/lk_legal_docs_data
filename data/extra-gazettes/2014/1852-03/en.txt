FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  A  MEMBER OF THE  BERUWALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of a member  in Beruwala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of vacant :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which the
member Kaluperuma Rathmasiri de Silva belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Beruwala
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Ananda Danush Wettimuni
Silva being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Beruwala  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ananda Danush Wettimuni Silva elected as a
Member of the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Beruwala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
24th  February, 2014.
