FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
COLOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Colombo Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Democratic People’s Front to which the
former member Lowrance Fernando belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Colombo
Municipal Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Thiagaraja Nirosh
being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of   Colombo Municipal Council  acting in
terms of Section  65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Thiagaraja Nirosh   elected as a member of the
Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
24th September,  2014.
