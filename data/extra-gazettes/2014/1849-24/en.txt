Notice under Section 7
My Ref No. : B/ RDA/HLD/2013/ 794. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.:  LD/ 05/ 2005/ HW/ 438. 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which is required for a public purpose.
    Description of the land to be acquired:
    The allotments of land depicted in FVP No. 294 and dated
21.11.2012  prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Garama Niladari Division of Walhaputhenna (157H), Village
of Naranmankada, Kandapalla Korale Minor Division of
Haldummulla Divisional Secretary’s Division, Badulla District
in Uva Province depicted as Lot No. 83 of Sheet No. 2 of the
Supplementary  No. 4   prepared by the Surveyor-General,
containing in extent 0.0046Hectare and described as follows:
  01. Lot No. 83
  Name of land: Walawwekadurawatta ; Description: Patana ;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not known; Extent:0.0046
Hectare.
    All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Haldummulla
on the 21 March 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested
to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 21 day
of March, 2014 and the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for  compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
R. M. V . S. J
