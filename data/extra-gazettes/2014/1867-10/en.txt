FILLING  OF  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KOLONNA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognised political party namely United National Party to which the member
Pechchimuththu Rajendran  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kolonna  Pradeshiya
Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Namminge Gunarathne
being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Matara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera, Returning Officer of  Kolonna  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Namminge Gunarathne  elected
as a member of the Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha.
M. A. P. C. Perera,
Returning Officer,
Kolonna  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
17th June,  2014.
