CONFERMENT OF POWERS
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 (4) of the Co-operative Societies Law No. 5 of 1972 as amended by the
Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992,
read in conjuntion with Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Mohamed Ibrahim Mohamed
Mansoor, Minister of Health, Indigenous medicine. Social services I.T Education and Co-operative of Eastern Province do
hereby confer on each of the person named in column I in the Schedule hereto, who have been appointed to assist the
Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar of  Co-operative Societies of the Eastern Province, such powers
of the Registrar under the Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 as amended by the Co-operative Societies (Amendment)
Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and the rules their under and which are
more fully described in column III of the said Schedule in respect of their area with effect from the dates shown in Column II
of the said schedule, to be exercised by the aforesaid during their tenure of office.
M. I. M. M
ANSOOR,
Minister of Health, Indigenous medicine, Social Services,
10th of January 2014, I.T Education and Co-operative
Trincomalee. Eastern Province, Trincomalee.
