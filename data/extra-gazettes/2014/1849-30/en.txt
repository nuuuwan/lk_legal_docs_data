THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Do/L/1/Ath/Adhi/103.
I hereby give notice, under sub - section (1) of  Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired:
Two (02)  allotments of  land in total  extent of  0.0033
Hectares,  as Lot No. 1 & 2 depicted in preliminary plan
No. Ka4442 and  dated  28.11.2013 prepared  by the Surveyor-
General, situated  in the  Village  of  Gamagoda,   in  the
Divisional   Secretary’s  Division of  Dodangoda,  District of
Kalutara  in Western Province morefully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Ampitiyakele; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: A.A, Wimalasiri of
Gamagoda,Udupitigoda; Extent: 0.0005 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Ampitiyakele; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M.L.D. Somasiri of
Gamagoda,Udupitigoda ; Extent: 0.0028 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Dodangoda Divisional Secretariat on the
27.03.2014 at  10.00.a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20.03. 2014 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars or their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
                     H.W.T.R. W
ETHTHASINGHA,
                              Divisional Secretary,
                 Dodangoda.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Dodangoda.
30th January, 2014.
