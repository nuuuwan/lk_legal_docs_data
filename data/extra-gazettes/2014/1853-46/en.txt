THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  2   OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic  Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the
subject of  Defence and Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently
required  for the purpose of carrying out and Urban  Development Project do by this order declare that the said land is
required for the such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo.,
05th March 2014,
SCHEDULE
One (01) allotment of land depicted as Lot No. A in Plan No. 748 dated 31.08.2003 prepared by A J B Wijayakoon,
Licensed Surveyor and Leveller containing in extent of 0A – 3R – 5.25P situated at Aluthkade in Ward No. 18 Keselwatta
within the Municipal Council limits of Colombo in  the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo, in the District of Colombo,
Western Province.
Boundaries of the said allotment of land is as follows :
North : Premises bearing Asst. No. 331, Olcott Mawatha (Police Quarters) and premises bearing Asst.
 Nos.  8 and 10 Trinity Place ;
East : Premises bearing Asst. Nos. 8 and 10 Trinity Place and Road ;

West : Olcott Mawatha.
