PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & C., by the Governors

My No. : 01/10/03/04
UV A    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  by  the  Governor   of   Uva   Province
I,  hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of Uva
Province.
01. Mr. W. M. M. G. Abeysinghe Banda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Deputy Chief
Secretary (Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, with effect
from the date 01.01.2014.
02. Mr. W. V . P. Nandapala, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, to perform the duties of the Director
(Budget) of Department of Finance Management of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the
date 26.03.2014 to 06.04.2014, till the return of Mr. R. T. S. L. Jayasinghe, to the Island who is the Director (Budget)
of Department of Finance Management of Uva Province.
03. Mr. G . K. S. L Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administratie Service, to perform the duties of the Secretary
to the Ministry of Youth Affairs, Sports, Social Welfare, Estate Infrastructure Development, Power and Energy,
Textile Industries and Small Industries of Uva Province, from the date 26.03.2014 to 06.04.2014, till the return of
Mr. W. M. M. G. Abeysinghe Banda, to the Island who is the Secretary to the Ministry of Youth Affairs, Sports,
Social Welfare, Estate Infrastructure Development, Power and Energy, Textile Industries and Small Industries of
Uva Province.
04. Mr. G . K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the Deputy
Chief Secretary (Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council, from the date 26.03.2014 to 06.04.2014,
till the return of Mr. W. M. M. G . Abeysinghe Banda, to the Island who is the Acting Deputy Chief Secretary
(Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council.
05. Ayurvedic Physician Mr. M .D G . L. Basil Karunaratna, retired officer of the Sri Lanka Ayurvedic Medical Service,
reappointed on contract basis as Medical Officer of the Bibila Medagama District  Ayurvedic Hospital, on
contract basis from the date 27.02.2014 till the appointment of a permanent officer for above post or up to
28.02.2015.
