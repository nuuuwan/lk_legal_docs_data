THE  LAND  REFORM  LAW,  No.  1  OF  1972
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 27A
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 27A (4) of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as amended by the Land Reform
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, read with Article 44(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do hereby -
(a) amend the Order, made under Section 27A (1) of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 517/20 of August, 05, 1988 relating to the estate land called and known as Galaha morefully
described in the First Schedule to this Order and which has been vested in the Janatha Estates Development Board
established under the State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972, by revoking on grounds of non-
compliance with the terms and conditions relating the consideration for the vesting in such Board, such portion of
the said Order as relates to the extent of Land called Galaha (Malberry Division) specified in the Second Schedule
hereto ; and
(b) state that upon revocation, such extent of land as specified in the Second Schedule to this Order, shall re-vest in
the Land Reform Commission.
