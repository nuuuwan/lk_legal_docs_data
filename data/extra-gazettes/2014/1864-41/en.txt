FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
AMPARA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Ampara Urban  Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meeting of the
Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member,
Manjula Sri Ilangamge belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that  Ampara Urban Council who
shall be declared elected as a member ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. H. G . Senavirathnebanda
being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Ampara Urban  Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. H. G . Senavirathnebanda elected as a member of
the Ampara Urban Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Ampara Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
27th May 2014.
