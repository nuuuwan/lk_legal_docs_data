PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
My No. : 01/10/03/04.
UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the Governor
BY , virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor
of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me with immediate effect.
01. Mrs. R. M. S. D. Rathnayake, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, As Deputy Chief Secretary
(Administration) for the Uva Provincial Council.
02. Mrs. A. A. U. Sakunthala Adhikari, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, As Commissioner of
Probation and Childcare service of Uva Province.
03. Mr. A. A. P. S. K. Karunarathne, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, As Director to the Building
of Uva Province.
04. Mr. K. A. B. Senevirathne, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, As Director to the Road of Uva
Province.
05. Mrs. J. W. C. S. Priyabashini, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council in full time basis.
06. Mr. W. M. N. Wickramasinghe, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, As Director (Finance) of the
Department of Finance Management of Uva Province.
07. Mr. V . C. Hettiaratchi, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, As Director (Planning) to the Ministry of
Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Land, Cultural Affairs, Social Welfare and
Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction of Uva Province.
C. N
ANDA MATHEW,
 Governor of Uva Province.
10th October 2014,
Office of the Uva Governor.

Appointments  made by  the Governor
BY, virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor
of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me with immediate effect.
01. Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, As Deputy Chief Secretary
(Planning) for the Uva Provincial Council.
02. Mr. J. A. S. P . Jayasinghe, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, As Secretary to the Co-
operative Service Commission for the  of Uva Province Council.
03. Mr. W. D. P. C. Samarasekara, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Land Commissioner of Uva Province in Full time basis.
04. Mrs. C. A. Herath, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, As Director to the Social Service of
Uva Province.
05. Mr. J. A. S. P . Jayasinghe, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to the
Haputale Town Council in addition to his permanent post.

Land, Cultural Affairs, Social Welfare and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction for the Uva
Provincial Council in Full time basis.
