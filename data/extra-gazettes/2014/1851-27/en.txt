ORDER   MADE  UNDER  THE  SECTION 20(1)  OF  THE  SUGARCANE  RESEARCH  INSTITUTE  ACT,
No. 75 OF 1981
I,  Lakshman Pinto Jayatillake Senaviratne, Minister of Sugarcane Industry Development, under the powers vested with me
by section 20(1) of Sugarcane Research Institute Act, No. 75 of 1981, hereby approved the acquisition of immovable property
described in the following property in view of a portion land required for the purpose of expanding the activities of the
Ensalwatta Sugarcane Breeding substation at Viharadeniya, Deniyaya, which belongs to the Sugarcane Research Institute.
L
AKSHMAN SENA VIRATNE,
Minister of  Sugar Industry Development.
C Wing, 11th Floor,
Sethsiripaya Second Stage,
Battaramulla,
11th February 2014.
Schedule
The allotment of land about 25 acres in extent, Kurulugala Section of the Ensalwatte situated within the Ensalwatte
Village of Viharahena Grama Niladhari Division within Kotapola Divisional Secretariat Division in the District of Matara.
Lot No. 01 : 25 acres
It is bounded on
North by : Sinharaja Forest Reserve ;
East by : Kola Dola Ela ;
South by : Cart Track ;
West by : Estate housed and remaining portion of the land.
