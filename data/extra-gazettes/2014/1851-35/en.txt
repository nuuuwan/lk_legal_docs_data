EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
DELFT  PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   Chairman in Delft Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which Daniel
Rechchiyan  the   Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide  upon
the member of that Delft Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as  Chairman within a
period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Sebasthi Pethuru Arulappu to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnudurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Delft Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section
65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sebasthi Pethuru Arulappu  elected as Chairman of the Delft
Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
21st  February, 2013.
