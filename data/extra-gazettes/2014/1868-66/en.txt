DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA   UV A   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Uva Provincial “Governor's Fund” (Amendment) Statute bearing No. 01 of 2014 of the Provincial
Council, Uva Province
Be it  enacted by the Uva Province, Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
This Statute makes provisions to cancel the amended Statute of Uva Province, Provincial Council
Governor's Fund Statute bearing No. 01 of 2010 and to amend sub sections 3(2), 3(3) and 3(4) of the Statute
of the Uva Provincial Council Governor's Fund bearing No. 03 of 2003 of Uva Provincial Council is being
amended by reinstating subsections as Below :
01. This Statute may be cited as the Uva Provincial Governor's Fund (Amendment), Statute
bearing No. 1 of 2014 of the Provincial Council, Uva Province and shall be in operation from
the date that the Statute got the approval of the Governor of Uva Province.

02.  3.2 The Executive Board of the Fund, hereinafter referred as “The Board” shall consists
of :
(a) The Governor of Uva Province.
(b) The Chief Minister of Uva Province or a representative named by him.
(c) The Chairman of Provincial Council of Uva Province.
(d) Opposition Leader of the Provincial Council of Uva Province or a representative
named by him
(e) The Chief Secretary of Uva Province.
(f) The Secretary to the Governor of Uva Province.
(g) Provincial Director to the Health Service of Uva Province.
(h) Provincial Director to the Education of Uva Province.
(i) Three members should be appointed in addition, according to the wish of the Governor
of Uva Province, appointed which will include an Accountant who is in the Uva
Provincial Council Government Service, An Attorney-at-Law who is a permanent
resident of Uva Province and one other Member. Members should hold their posts for
a period mentioned in the letter of appointment issued by the Governor of Uva Province
subject to conditions appearing in sub section (3) and (4) of this amended Statute.
03. 3.3   Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (i) of sub section (2) of the Governor may
revoke the appointment of any member appointed by him under paragraph (i) of this amended
Statute.
04. 3.4   Any member of the Board appointed by the Governor, under paragraph (i) of subsections
(2) of this amended Statute could retire at any time, from the Board on submission, of a letter
to that effect.
05. “In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Statute, the
Sinhala text shall prevail”.
06. In this Statute unless the context otherwise requires :
“Provincial Council” means the Provincial Council of the Uva Province.
“Governor” means the Governor of Uva Province.
