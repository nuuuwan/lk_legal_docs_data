SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification under the Section 185 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No.15 of 1987 read with Section 02 of the Provincial
Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989 and Section 02 of the Powers of Supervision of the
Administration of Local Authorities Statute No.06 of 1989
I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Health and
Indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment, News,
Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, in terms of the powers vested in me under Section 2(2) of the
Supervision of the Administration of Local Government Institutions Statute No. 06 of 1989, do hereby notify that the duration
of the Commission of Inquiry comprising retired High Court Judge, Saman Wickrama Wickramaarachchi Esquire, appointed
by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1865/25  dated the Tuesday, third June, 2014 in the Part IV (A) - Provincial Council
Notifications of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to inquire whether the matters mentioned under Section 2(1)
of the said Statute were committed by the Chairman, Mr. P. P. Wickramasinghe, while holding his office as the Chairman of the
Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha and to submit a report, is extended further 03 months.
I also notify that the provisions mentioned in the Section 2 and 3 of the said Gazette will be operated further.
M
AHEEPALA HERATH,
Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and
Planning, Local Government, Health and indigenous Medicine,
Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and
Estate Welfare, Environment, News, Housing and
Construction  of the Sabaragamuwa Province.
On this 01st of September, 2014,
Chief Ministry of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council,
New Town, Ratnapura.
