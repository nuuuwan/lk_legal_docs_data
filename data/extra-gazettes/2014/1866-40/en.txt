No. 1866/40  - FRIDAY,   JUNE  13,  2014
EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments & C., by the President
P.S. No.: E&O/HR/1/4/20.
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Gamini S. Senerath, Chief of Staff to the President as Acting
Secretary to the President with effect from 13.06.2014 until Mr. Lalith Weeratunga, Secretary to the President returns to the
Island.
By order of  His Excellency,
D. S. G
UNARATNE,
 Additional Secretary to the President
(Establishment & Operations),
For Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
13th  June, 2014.
