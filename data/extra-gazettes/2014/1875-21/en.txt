THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Lot ‘A ’ in total extent of about 0.3170
Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. KA/DDN/12/177 and
dated 12.09.2012, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of V ogan Watta Deegalla Portion in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Dodangoda, in Kalutara District in Western Province
and bounded as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’ –  Vogan Watta Deegalla Portion – Extent: About
0.3170 Hectare
North: Kotagala Plantation claimed by V ogan Watta Deegalla
Portion and Neboda Matugama Road; East: Neboda Matugama Road
and Kotagala Plantation claimed by V ogan Watta Deegalla Portion;
South: Kotagala Plantation claimed by V ogan Watta Deegalla Portion
and Plantation Road; West: Plantation  Road  and Kotagala Plantation
claimed by V ogan Watta Deegalla Portion.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
