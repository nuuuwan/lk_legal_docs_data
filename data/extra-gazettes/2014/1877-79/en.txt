DECLARATION OF BOLTHUBE SAMAN DEV ALA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Bolthube Saman Devala sacred area", for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.

All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.9571 Hectares in
Bolthube alias Devalegama village in Bolthube Grama Niladari Division, Divisional No.267, in Minor Division of Uduwaggam
Paththuwa in Kadawatha Korale in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Imbulpe in Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa
Province depicted as Lot No.22 in plan No.R/IMB/2010/159  dated 12
th March 2010 of Surveyor General.
(Lot No.22)
