Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of English (Amendment) Ordinance No. 3 of 2014 and
shall come into force on 05.08.2014.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of English Ordinance, No. 4 of 2005, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1417/12
of 31st October, 2005 is hereby amended as follows :–
(1) in paragraph 4 thereof –
(i) by the substitution for the word ‘‘Engish’’ in sub – paragraph (c) of that paragraph, of the word
‘‘English’’ ;
(ii) by the substitution for the word ‘‘distintions’’ in sub – paragraph (e) of that paragraph, of the word
‘‘distinctions’’ ;
(iii) by the substitution for the word ‘‘intitutions’’ in sub – paragraph (f) of that paragraph, of the word
‘‘institutions’’ ; and
(iv) by the substitution for the word ‘‘funtions’’ in sub – paragraph (n) of that paragraph, of the word
‘‘functions’’ ;
