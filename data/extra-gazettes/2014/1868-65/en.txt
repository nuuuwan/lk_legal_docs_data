THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for Re-construction of  Ingiriya - Halwathura - Egaloya Road
No.  5/4 Bridge and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The Allotments  of land in total Extent of About 0.1710  Hectare,
prepared by the  Surveyor-General’s Tracing No. KA/RDA/2013/
522P and dated 27th December, 2013,  situated in the Villages of
Halwathura and Bothale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Bulathsinhala in Kaluthara District in Western  Province and bounded
as  follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kosgahaovita – Extent:  About 0.0009 Hectare
North: Kosgahaovita claimed by R. A. Suhas and Road (R.D.A.);
East and South: Road (R.D.A.);  West: Kosgahaovita claimed by
R. A. Suhas and Lot A.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kosgahaovita – Extent:  About 0.0665 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and  Kosgahaovita claimed by
R. D. Rathnasiri; East: Kosgahaovita claimed by R. D. Rathnasiri;
South: Kosgahaovita claimed by R. D. Rathnasiri and Bothale Canal;
West: Lot A and Road (R.D.A.).
03. Lot ‘C’ – Puskolaovita – Extent:  About 0.0435 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and Bothale Canal; East: Puskolaovita
claimed by R. A. Sunil;  South: Puskolaovita claimed by R. A. Sunil
Leelarathna and Lot G;  West:  Road (R.D.A.).
04. Lot ‘D’ – Puskolaovita – Extent:  About 0.0160 Hectare
North: Puskolaovita claimed by W. U. K. G . Punchihewa; East:
Road (R.D.A.);  South: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot E;  West:  Lot E and
Puskolaovita claimed by R.  Renuka and W. U. K. G . Punchihewa.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Puskolaovita – Extent:  About 0.0113 Hectare
North: Puskolaovita claimed by R. Renuka and W. U. K. G .
Punchihewa and Lot E; East: Lot D and Road (R.D.A.);  South: Road
(R.D.A.) and Lot F;  West:  Lot F and Puskolaovita claimed by R. A.
Renuka and Leelarathna.
