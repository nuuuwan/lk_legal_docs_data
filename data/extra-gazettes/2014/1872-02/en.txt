FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE
KATANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)  OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Katana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of
the Western Province Provincial Council;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the member Arachchige Nanoby Antony Upul Rajitha  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that Katana Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Palakuttige Kamal
Rohitha Gunathunga Perera being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Katana Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Palakuttige Kamal Rohitha Gunathunga
Perera elected as a Member of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katana  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
17th July, 2014.
