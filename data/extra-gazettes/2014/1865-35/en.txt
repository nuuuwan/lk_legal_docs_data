FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
AMBALANGODA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Ambalangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the member,
Kalumarakkala Chandrasiri belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Ambalangoda Urban
Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Kachchilage Pushpika
Indrajith being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith de Silva, Returning Officer of  Ambalangoda Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kachchilage Pushpika Indrajith  elected as a
member of the Ambalangoda Urban Council.
A. B. I. 
DE SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
03rd June,  2014.
