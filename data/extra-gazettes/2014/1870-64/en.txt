ARTICLE  154B  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE DEMOCRATIC
 SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI LANKA
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph (8) of  Article 154 B of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka I, Caluwadevage Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province do hereby, dissolve the Uva Province  Provincial
Council with effect from midnight July 11, 2014.
CALUWADEV AGE NANDA MATHEW,
Governor of the Uva Province.
Office of the Governor of the Uva Province,
11th July, 2014.
