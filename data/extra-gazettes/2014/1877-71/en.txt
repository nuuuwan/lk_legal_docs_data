DECLARATION OF BUSSA SHRI SUDHARSHANARAMA PURANA MAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, as a sacred area and it shall be
cited as " Bussa Shri Sudharshanaramaya Purana Maha Vihara sacred area", for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust, 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF BUSSA SHRI SUDHARSHANARAMA PURANA MAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 2.6920 Hectares
situated at Bussa village in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Hikkaduwa of the administrative District of Galle in Southern
Province marked as lot A, lot B, lot C, lot D, lot E, lot F, lot G, lot H depicted in plan No.GA/HKK/H/294 dated 25
th September
2011 of Surveyor General.
