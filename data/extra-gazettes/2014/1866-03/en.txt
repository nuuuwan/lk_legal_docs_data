THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Allotments of lands depicted in Advance Tracing No. MA/RTT/
2013/306 dated  08.01.2014, prepared by Surveyor-General,  situated
in the Village of Kuruwawa in the Minor Division of Udasiyapattuwa,
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Raththota, in the District of
Matale, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ruckathtanagahamulawatta – Extent: About
0.0003 Hectare
North and East: Balance portion of the land; South: Balance portion
of the land, Banagewatta and Lot B; West: Lot B and Banagewatta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Banagewatta – Extent: About 0.0037 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land and Ruckaththanagahamulawatta
claimed by S. Begam; East: Lot A and Ruckaththanagahamulawatta
claimed by S. Begam; South: Balance portion of the land; West: Road
and balance portion of the land.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Banagewatta – Extent: About 0.0212 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land and Road; East: Balance portion
of the land; South: Balance portion of the land and Oya Reservation;
West: Oya Reservation.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Banagewatta – Extent: About 0.0129 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land; East: Lot C and Banagewatta
claimed by A. M. P. Dayasena Abeweera; South: Balance portion of
the land; West: Kuruwawa Oya.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Pepolyayewatta – Extent: About 0.0913 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land; East: Kuruwawa Oya; South:
Balance portion of the land; West: Canal and balance portion of the
land.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Pepolyayewatta – Extent: About 0.0012 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land and Canal; East: Canal; South:
Road; West: Road and balance portion of the land.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Pepolyayewatta – Extent: About 0.0004 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the land and Road; East: Road; South
and West: Balance portion of the land.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
