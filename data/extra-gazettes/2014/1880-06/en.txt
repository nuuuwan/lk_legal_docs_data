1A
PROMOTING  THE  TEACHERS’  TRAINING  COLLEGE  AT  MAHARAGAMA  AS  A  NATIONAL
COLLEGE  OF  EDUCATION  FOR  PRE-SERVICE  TEACHER  TRAINING  IN  RELATION  TO
ENTERPRENEURSHIP  AND  MANAGEMENT  STUDIES
As per the Decision of the Cabinet granted on the 9th May 2014 to the  Cabinet Memorandum bearing No. 13/0525/330/019
dated 19th April 2013 in terms of the Powers vested upon me under the Section 2 (1) (a) of the National Colleges of Education
Act No. 30 of 1986, it is hereby declared that the Maharagama Teachers Training College which has been operated since
01.07.2014 will be promoted as “Maharagama National College of Education on Enterpreneurship and Management” for
Pre-service Teacher Training in relation to Enterpreneurship and Management Studies.
This Institution is being conducted as non residential institution based on subject oriented requirements and the
relevant pedagogic trainees will be directed to the Government Schools as well as to Local Enterpreneurship Institutions for
residential training.
B
ANDULA GUNAW ARDHANE,
Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
Isurupaya,
Battaramulla,
