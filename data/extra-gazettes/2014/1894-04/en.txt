CONTROL    OF   PESTICIDES    ACT,  No. 33  OF  1980
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section II of the Control of Pesticides Act, No. 33 of 1980, I, Gamage Anura
Wickramasiri Wijesekara, Registrar of Pesticides, do  by this Order, in the interest of the public and on the advice of the
Technical and Advisory Committee, modify every licence issued in respect of pesticides containing the active ingredients
bearing the Abstract Service Registry number specified in Column I of the schedule hereto by prohibiting the use, offer for
sale or sale in the areas specified in Column II.
D
R. G. A. W. WIJESEKARA,
Registrar of Pesticides.
Peradeniya,
22nd  December 2014.
