PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTIONS  ACT, N o. 1 OF 1981
 Filling of a Vacancy under Section 64 (5)
WHEREAS the Secretary General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Section 6 of the Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 35 of 1988, that a vancancy has occurred
in the Membership of the Seventh Parliament by reason of the resignation of Mr. A. H. M. Aswar being a member nominated
by the United People's Freedom Alliance, a recognized political party, and declared elected as a Member of Parliament under
Article 99A of the Constitution ;
And whereas the Secretary of that Political Party was required by me under the aforesaid section to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid Political Party has nominated Mr. Ameer Ali Shihabdeen to fill such
vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers conferred on me by
Section 64 (5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981, do hereby declare Mr. Ameer Ali Shihabdeen elected as a
member of the Seventh Parliament to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of aforesaid Mr. A. H. M. Aswar.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09
th December 2014.
