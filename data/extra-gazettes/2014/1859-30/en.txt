PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
1A
THE URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT  No.  02  OF  1980
Order  under  Section  2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No. 2 of
1980, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister - in - charge of the
subject Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for secure
the Historical Buddhist Heritage Project do by this Order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
17 th April  2014.
Schedule
Part of land depicted in Plan No. 1030 dated 18th September 1934 prepared by Mr. P. F. Goonesekere, permanent
Licensed Surveyor containing in extent Approx. 20.10 (perches) situated in the Pettah within Colombo Municipal Council,
Colombo Divisional Secretariat in District of Colombo, Western Province.
 Boundaries are as follows :
North – Asst. No. 138 in same plan ;
East – Asst. No. 207 Norris Road ;
South – Norris Road ;
West – Asst. No. 195 and 197 Norris Road.
