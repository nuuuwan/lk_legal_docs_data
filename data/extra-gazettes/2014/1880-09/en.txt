No. 1880/9  –  TUESDAY,  SEPTEMBER 16, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDENT BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT)  
 
ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964    
Notice Under Section 7 
 
 My Ref. : Co/Prala/LA/3/79/UDA. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the  Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following       
land / Servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land /Servitude  to be acquired: 
 
One allotment of land depict ed as Lot No. 1 an extent 
of 0.2397 (Hectares) in Preliminary Plan No. Co. 9480 
dated 28.08.2014 prepared by Surveyor General situated 
at Fort (Ward No. 20) Fort Colombo Municipal Council 
limits within the limits of Colombo Divisional Secretariat 
area in the Colombo District, Western Province. 
   
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Colombo 
Divisional Secretary’s office on 17th November, 2014 at 
10.00 a.m. are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(duplicate) on or before the 10th November, 2014 the 
nature of their interests in the land
 / Servitude the 
particulars or their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land / Servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
 
  
 D. P. W ICKRAMASINGHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Colombo. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Colombo, 
15th September, 2014.
