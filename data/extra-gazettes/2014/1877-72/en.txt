DECLARATION OF KOTHMALE NIYAMGAMDORA PURANA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as " Kothmale Niyamgamdora Purana Rajamaha Vihara sacred area", for its
long-term security.
 DR. D. M. JAY ARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF KOTHMALE NIY AMGAMDORA PURANA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.7672 Hectares
depicted in the below mentioned Part I and Part II situated at Niyamgamdora village in Minor Division of Udapane Korale in
the Divisional Secretariat Division of Kothmale of the Administrative District of Nuwara Eliya in Central Province depicted in
plan No.NU/KTM/2010/202, Zone No. 01,  dated 18
th November 2010 of Surveyor General.
