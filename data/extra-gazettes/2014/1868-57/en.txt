EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 Ref. No.: ATH/KD/129/HW. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section  (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following supplemental lands which are 
required for Colombo outer Circular Highway Project. 
 
Description of the Lands to be Acquired :  
 
Allotments of land in extent of about 0.4102 Hectare, 
depicted as Lot Nos. 01 to 6 in the Preliminary Plan             
No. Co. 9309 and 11.06.2014 dated prepared by the 
Surveyor-General, situated in the village of Hokandara, in 
the Minor Division of Palle Paththuwa, Grama Niladhari 
Division of 494/A, Hokandara East in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Kaduwela, in the  Colombo 
District in Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: Dipanie Mudannay aka, No. 43, Noris Cenal 
Mawatha, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.1613 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.0175 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 3 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: Chals Perera, Suhada Mawatha, Horahena; 
Extent: 0.0045 Hectare. 
4. Lot No. 4 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: W. P. Somasiri Jayalath Perera, No. 30, 
Horahna Road, Hokandara East; Extent: 0.0816 Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 5 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: W. P. Pushpa Ranjanie, No. 311/4, Kanaththa 
Road, Pore, Athurugiriya; Extent: 0.1245 Hectare. 
 
6. Lot No. 6 
 
Name of the Land: Idigahawela Kumbura; 
Description: Paddy Field; Claiment: State; Original 
Claimant: W. P. Baba nona, Suhada Mawatha, Horahena; 
Extent: 0.0208 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Kaduwela Divisional 
Secretariat on the 23rd July 2 014 at 10.00 a. m. and are 
hereby  requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 16th July, 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
Compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 
 W. M. D AYAWATHI,  
 Divisional Secretary,
