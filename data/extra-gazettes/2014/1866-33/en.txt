No. 1866/33 –  WEDNESDAY, JUNE  11, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
     Reference No.— Hangu/3/2/1/79.  
 
  
 
I, hereby give notice, under Section 7 of the land 
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire 
the following lands which are required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the Lands to be acquired : 
 
 
The allotments of land  in extent 0.0048 Hectare 
depicted as lot No. 01 in  the preliminary plan No. 
NU/2945 dated 31.03.2014 prepared by the Surveyor 
General, situated in the 492 C Walalawela  
Grama niladhari Division in Divisional Secretary Divition 
of Hanguranketha in the District of Nuwara-Eliya and 
more fully described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Katugashinnawatta;  Land use : 
Garden, Break Pressure Tank Claimant: N. A. N. 
Chandana Pradeep Kumara, “Sarasavi”, Walugama Road, 
Katugashinna, Rikillagaskada; Extent: 0.0048 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are  hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Hanguranketha on the 18th day of August 2014 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requeste d to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of August 2014 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claim for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
  
                                       R. T. P. SUMANASEKARA, 
                     Acquiring Officer/Divisional Secretary, 
 Hanguranketha. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Hanguranketha, 
06th June, 2014.
