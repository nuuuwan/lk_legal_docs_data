PROCLAMATIONS  MADE  BY  GOVERNORS  BY  VIRTUE  OF  POWERS  ENTRUSTED  UNDER
PARAGRAPH  8(a)  OF  CLAUSE  154 (b) OF THE CONSTITUTION
Proclamation
To all whom these presents shall come:
 I,  Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province, in accordance with the powers vested in me by Paragraph
8(a) in Clause 154 “b” of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby summon the Western
Province Provincial Council to meet on 22nd April 2014 at 10.45 a.m. in the Council Chamber of the Provincial Council Building
Complex situated at No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
S. A
LA VI MOWLANA,
Governor of the Western Province.
Secretariat of the Governor,
98/4, Havelock Road,
Colombo 05.
