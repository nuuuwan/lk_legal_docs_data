THE  PUBLIC  SECURITY  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER   40)
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 12 of the Public Security Ordinance (Chapter 40), I, Mahinda Rajapaksa,
do by this order call out all the members of the Armed Forces specified in the First Schedule hereto, for the maintenance
of public  order in the areas specified in the Second Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
