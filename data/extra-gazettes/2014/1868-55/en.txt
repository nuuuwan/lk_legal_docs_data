FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KATUNAYAKE  –  SEEDUWA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Katunayake - Seeduwa Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as
a member of the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the member, Hamudu Dhammika Lalantha Gunasekera belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Katunayake - Seeduwa Urban Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Nimal Silva Handun
being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of  Katunayake - Seeduwa Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Nimal Silva Handun  elected
as a member of the Katunayake - Seeduwa Urban Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Katunayake - Seeduwa Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
23rd June,  2014.
