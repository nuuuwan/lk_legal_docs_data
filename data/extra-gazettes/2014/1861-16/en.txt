LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 7
My No.: KIDS/LND/01/ACQ/03.
Land Ministry No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/291.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land  Acquisition Act as amended by Land Acquisition
(Amended) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Pansalewatta, in extent about
0.0037 Hectares, depicted as Lot 735 in Sheet Number 33 of
the Supplementary Number 36 of Final Village Plan Number
182 prepared by Survey-general and situated in the 145B-
Idangoda Grama Niladhari Division in the Division Secretary
Division of Kiriella in the Meda Pattu of Kuruwiti Korale in the
Ratnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province, is required to
build up Kiriella Watera Pumping Station.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kiriella on 28th
May, 2014 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby required to notify me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 21st May, 2014 the
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims of compensation for the acquisition of the said land the
amount of compensation, the details of the computation of
such amount.
I. R. M. P . D. ILLUKKUMBURA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kiriella.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kiriella.
30th April, 2014.
