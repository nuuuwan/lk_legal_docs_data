FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF   CHAIRMAN OF  THE  NAVITHANVELI  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISION)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Chairman  in Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Sivalingam
Gunareththinam the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
Chairman  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate Mr. Sivalingam Gunareththinam to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Silvalingam Gunareththinam elected
as Chairman of the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
29th  May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF   CHAIRMAN OF  THE  KARAITIVU  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISION)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Chairman  in Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Irasaiya
Sellaiya the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman  within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate Mr. Yogareththinam Gopikanth to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Yogareththinam Gopikanth elected as Chairman
of the Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Karaitivu Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara,
29th  May, 2014.
