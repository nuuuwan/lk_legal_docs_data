FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KESBEWA  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kesbewa Urban  Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of
the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member,
Phalawaththage Don Loshan Leonard Karunarathne belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that  Kesbewa Urban Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Pathirage Don Mahendra
Mohanlal Nanayakkara being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rajapakshage V ajira Nishantha Gunawardhana   Returning Officer of  Kesbewa Urban  Council,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Pathirage Don Mahendra Mohanlal
Nanayakkara  elected as a member of the Kesbewa Urban Council.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDENA,
Returning Officer,
Kesbewa Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
28th May 2014.
