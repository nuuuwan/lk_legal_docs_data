NOTICE   UNDER  THE  REGISTRATION  OF  ELECTORS ACT,  No. 44 OF  1980   AS   AMENDED
BY  THE REGISTRATION   OF
ELECTORS   (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No.  27 OF 2013
Electoral District No. 10 - Jaffna
NOTICE is hereby given under Section 4 of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980 as amended by the Registration of
Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 27 of 2013 that the supplementary register of internally displaced electors in relation to
the aforementioned Electoral District was certified and is being exhibited during office hours for perusal at the District
Elections Office, Jaffna.
 P.  K

District Elections Office,
Jaffna.
