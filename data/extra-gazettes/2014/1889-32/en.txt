 WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Notification
I,  Prasanna Ranatunga, as the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of the Western
Province shall hereby enforce the regulations relevant to Allowance Payments for Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Councilors
of Municipal Councils as per power assigned to me under Section 289 of Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) read
with clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, These regulations shall come into effect
from 01.11.2014 and the regulations put into force from 01.12.2008 in relation to this matter as published in the Gazette Paper
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1578/4 dated 01st December 2008 will be repealed with effect from the
date immediately previous to 01.11.2014.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning,  Law and Order,
Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Manpower and Employment, Engineering Services and Information
of the Western Province Provincial Council.
At Shrawasthi Mandiraya,
Sir Marcus Fernando Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
19th  November 2014.
REGULA TIONS
1. These regulations shall be named as Regulations relating to payment of allowances to Mayors, Deputy Mayors
and Councilors of Municipal Councils of the Western Province.
2. For the Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Councilors appointed to Municipal Councils of the Western Province  an
allowance as stated below shall be paid from 01st of November 2014.
