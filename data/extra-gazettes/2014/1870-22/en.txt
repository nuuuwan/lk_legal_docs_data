THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/20. 
 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: LD/05/2006/WS/364.    
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the Land to be acquired:  
 
An allotment of land called Polgaspamulawela Watta 
in total extent of 0.0650 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1443 
Final Village Plan No. 169 sheet No. 109 supplement No. 
127 prepared by the Surveyor -General in the village of 
Mathugobe in Grama Niladhari Division of Ihalagama in 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kotapala in Matara 
District Southern Province and bounded as follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 1443 - Claimant: Chief incumbent Sri 
Pushparama Viharaya, Temple Road, Deniyaya; 
Extent: 0.0650 Hectare. 
 
North: Gin Ganga and Lot No. 83; East: Lot No. 83 
and 445: South: Lot No. 445 and 83; West: Lot No. 83 
and Gin Ganga. 
 
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Kotapola on the 1st day of September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 25th day of August, 2014 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 G
ANESHA AMARASINGHE,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kotapola.  
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Kotapola, 
03rd  July, 2014.
