THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land  depicted in  Advance Tracing No. GA/IMD/
2013/627 dated 10th October, 2013 prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Angulugaha in the  Divisional
Secretary’s Division  of Imaduwa in Galle District in Southern Province
in extent of about 0.5972 Hectare and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thoragodawila – Extent: 0.5972 Hectare
North: Pansalawaththa (Owned by pantharamaviharaya); East:
Pansalawaththa (Owned by pantharamaviharaya),
Heenatigalawaththa (Owned by Angulugaha M. V . and Don Piyal
Vithanawasam and W. G. Siripala); South: Heenatigalawaththa
(Owned by Angulugaha M. V. Heenatigalawaththa and Road Owned
by  Don Piyal Vithanawasam and W. G. Siripala); West:
Thoragodawila  (Ownered by Rathnasiri Borelassa and Others).

Minister of Lands and Land Development.
