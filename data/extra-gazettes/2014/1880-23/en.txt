THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the servitude over the land described
below in the 1st paragraph is needed for a public purpose and will be
acquired under the provisions of that Act. It describes in the 2nd
paragraph, from whch  land that the servitude will be acquired.
01. Description of the Servitude – Acquisition of servitude over
an additional land for the construction of Access Road to Panamura
Ground Reservoir of Kolonna Balangoda Water supply Project.
02. Description of the Land-  An allotment of land called
Koongahahena in total extent of about 0.1205 Hectare,  in  the Advance
Tracing No. R/EMB/2013/558  dated, 11.12.2013,  prepared by the
Surveyor-General and situated in the Village of  Panamura in
Diyapotagampattuwa Korale Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division in Embilipitiya in Rathnapura District
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows.
1. Lot ‘A’ –  Koongahahena – Extent : About 0.1205
Hectare
North: Road (RDA) Koongahahena claimed by K. A. Heenhamy;
East and  South:  Koongahahena claimed by K. A. Heenhamy;
West: Koongahahena claimed by K. A. Heenhamy, Road (RDA).
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development
.
My Reference No.: 4/3/9/2013/WS/05.
