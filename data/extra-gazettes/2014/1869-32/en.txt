Regulations
Pursuant to Section 117 (2) (s) of Civil Aviation Act, No. 14 of 2010 the Minister of Civil Aviation has made the
following regulations relating to fees to be levied for the issue, validation, renewal, extension, revalidation, or amendment of
any licence, certificate, permit, approval or any other form of legal authority or document to be granted or any other service to
be rendered either by the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka or Director General of Civil Aviation.
1. These Regulations may be cited as “Civil Aviation Authority Fees and Charges Regulation No. 01 of 2014” and shall
come into force with effect from 01st August, 2014.
2. The fees to be levied for the issue, validation, renewal, extension or amendment of any licence, certificate, permit,
approval or any other form of legal authority or document to be granted or any other service to be rendered either by Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka or Director General or Civil Aviation shall be as specified in the Schedule hereto.
3. The money to be collected either by the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka or Director General of Civil Aviation
in the form of fees and charges under this Regulation, shall be credited to the Civil Aviaion Authority Fund as provided under
Section 12 of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka Act, No. 34 of 2002.
4. The following Regulations are hereby repealed with immediate effect.
(a) Air Navigation Regulations published in the Government Gazette (Extra Ordinary) No. 1025/6 dated 28th April,
1998 ;
(b) Air Transport Providers (Passengers) Licensing Regulations published in the Government Gazette
No. 1298/21 dated 22nd July, 2003 ;
5. Any Rule issued by the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka or any implementing Standard issued by the Director
General of Civil Aviation in respect of fees or charges to be levied by the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka for any service
rendered is superseded by these Regulations.

SCHEDULE
1. Any applicant seeking facilities or services mentioned below shall furnish to the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri
Lanka all applicable information as required by the Civil Aviation Authority or Director - General of Civil Aviation together with
the fees mentioned below, at the time of submitting the application.
2. An applicant shall, in addition to the fees mentioned below, bear the full cost of air passage, hotel accommodation
and daily subsistence allowance at the approved rate for the officials whom the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka may have
to engage to perform any work overseas in relating to the application. The cost of air passage is calculated on the basis of full
fare economy class seat round trip if the flight time is less than six hours. For trip involving flight time of six hours or more the
cost of passage will be calculated in the basis of full fare business class round trip.
3. Fees chargeable under these Regulations are not refundable, unless otherwise decided by the Director General of
Civil Aviation and Chief Executive Officer.
