THE  LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land depicted   in the  Advance Tracing No. Maha/
PJP/2013/1583 dated 30th November, 2013 prepared by the
Surveyor-General situated in  the Village of Uggahakumbura in
Galasiyapattu Minor  Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Poojapitiya in Kandy  District, Central Province  and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kuruduwatte Hena – Extent:   0.0174 Hectare
North: Canal and Kuruduwatte Hena owned by
D. T. Kamalawathie; East: Kuruduwatte Hena owned by
D. T. Kamalawathie and the land with unknown name owned by
K/Marathugoda Central College;  South:  Land with unknown name
owned by K/Marathugoda Central College and Canal;  West:  Canal.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
