DECLARATION OF HISTORICAL WELLE DEV ALA IN UNAWATUNA, GALLE SACRED AREA AS SACRED
AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND
NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Historical Welle Devala in Unawatuna sacred area, Galle sacred land",
for its long-term security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF HISTORICAL WELLE DEW ALA IN UNAWA TUNA, GALLE SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.7070 Hectares
depicted in lot No.A,B,C,D,E, and F, situated in Unawatuna village-West in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Habaraduwa
of the Administrative Division of Galle in Southern Province depicted in Tracing No.GA/HBR/2011/385 dated 24
th December
2011 of Surveyor General.
