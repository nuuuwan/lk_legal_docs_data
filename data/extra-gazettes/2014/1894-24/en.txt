NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION 23 BB (4)  OF  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL  ACT
400 kV Transmission Line Project from Sampoor to Habarana
This is to inform the public that after studying the IEE report of the above project, the Ministry of Power & Energy, in terms
of regulation 13 (i) of National Environmental (Procedure for approval of Project) Regulations No. 1 of 1993 published in the
extraordinary gazette No. 1159/22 dated 22 November 2000 has granted approval for implementation of the above project
subject to specified terms and conditions.
M. M. C. Ferdinando,
Secretary,
Ministry of Power & Energy.
