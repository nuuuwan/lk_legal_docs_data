THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)

I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act  that the land described below is intended
to be acquired for the construction of bore-holes at the Chilaw Water
Supply Project under Asian Development Bank's donation.under

  Pansal land in extent of about 0.0127 Hectare depicted

Division of Chilaw in Puttalam District in Northwestern Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Sri Pushparama Vihara land alias Munneswaram
