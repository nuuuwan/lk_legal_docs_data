 EASTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987
I, Abdul Majeed Mohamed Najeeb, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Local Government, Rural
Development, Rehabilitation, Re-settlement, Man power, Law and Order, Regional Administration and Environment of the
Eastern Province do under Section 223 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 and hereby revoke the order published
in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka (Extraordinary) No. 1846/45 dated 2014.01.23 regarding the
Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha and that the order therein remain in operative effective from the date of the notice published in the
Gazette of the said Republic (Extraordinary) No. 1868/6 dated 2014.06.23 under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance.
A
BDUL MAJEED MOHAMED NAJEEB,
 Chief Minister and   Minister of Finance
and Planning,  Local Government, Rural Development,

Environment of the Eastern Province.
