FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  KOBEIGANE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Kobeigane  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party Samara Arachchige Don
Vijitha Pemalal to which belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within
a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kobeigane Pradeshiya
Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage
Gunawardhana being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal ,  Returning Officer of  Kobeigane  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage
Gunawardhana elected as a Member of the Kobeigane Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. TALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kobeigane  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
22nd  March, 2014.
