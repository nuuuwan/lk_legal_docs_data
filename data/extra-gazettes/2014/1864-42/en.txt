 LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  under  Section  10A(1)(a)
MATHUGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of the Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Owitigala Hewage Rohana Chithral Priyantha of the Mathugama
Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the People's
Liberation Front.
 R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office
Kalutara,
27th May 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF THE
YAKKALAMULLA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
 UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)  OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
 (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Yakkalamulla  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of resignations ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party,  to which  the member
Mr. Kananke Liyanage Sumanasiri  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for elections as a member of that  Yakkalamulla
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
