FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER
OF THE  POINT  PEDRO  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Point Pedro Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the member  Sivakolunthu Mahenthiran  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Point Pedro
Urban Council who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Marku Manuvel  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Ponnudurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Point Pedro Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Marku Manuvel elected as a Member of the Point Pedro
Urban Council.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Point Pedro Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
06th  June, 2014.
