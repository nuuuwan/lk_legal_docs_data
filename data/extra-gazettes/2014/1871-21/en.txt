No. 1871/21 – TUESDAY, JULY 15, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, do hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
required for the University of Uva Wellassa and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act.
The land called Hingurugamuwa watte in extent of about 10.1171
Hectares depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’, ‘‘B’’ and ‘‘C’’ in the Progression
Tracing bearing number B/ACQ/BDL/2014/28 dated 26.02.2014
prepared by the Superintendant of Surveys of Badulla Division in
Badulla District, which is situated in the Village of Hanwella coming
under 80 H Glen Alpin Grama Niladhari Division in the Minor
Division of Rilpola Korale of Badulla Divisional Secretary's Division
in Badulla District in the Province of Uva and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘‘A’’ –  Hingurugamuwa Watte – Extent: About 0.6765
Hectare
North: Road; East: Road, Glen Alpin Estate claimed by Balangoda
Plantation Limited and Lot B; South: Glen Alpin Estate claimed by
Balangoda Plantation Limited and Lot B;West: Lot B and Road.
02. Lot ‘‘B’’ –  Road – Extent: About 0.0735 Hectare
03. Lot ‘‘C’’ –  Hingurugamuwa Watte – Extent: About 9.3671
Hectares
North: Glen Alpin Estate claimed by Balangoda Plantation Limited,
Road and Lot B; East and South: Glen Alpin Estate claimed by
Balangoda Plantation Limited, Road and Glen Alpin Estate claimed
by State;West: Glen Alpin Estate claimed by Balangoda Plantation
Limited and Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
