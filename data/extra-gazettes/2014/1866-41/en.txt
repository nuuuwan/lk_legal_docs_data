THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  02  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the
subject of Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the
Thalawathugoda Town Development  Project do by this order, declare that the said land is required for the aforesaid purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
30th May 2014.
SCHEDULE
The land shown as Lot Nos. 1 & 2 in Plan No. 2690 dated 27.04.2014 prepared by Mr. H. Nilupul R. Karunathilaka,
Registered Licensed Surveyor containing in extent of (0.4838 Ha.) 1A.-0R.-31.30P. situated at the village of Thalawathugoda
in the Grama Niladhari Division of Thalawathugoda within the limits of Maharagama Divisional Secretariat in the District of
Colombo,  Western Province.
Details of the said lands are as follows :
Lot No.  1 Extent 0.0490 Ha.
North : Lot No. 14 in PP Co. 5516 and Battaramulla - Pannipitiya Road ;
East : Battaramulla - Pannipitiya Road ;
South : Premises of Asst. No. 708 ;
West : Lot No. 14 in PP Co. 5516.
Lot No. 2 Extent 0.4348 Ha.
North : Drain separating the Asst. No. 708 ;
East : Battaramulla - Pannipitiya Road ;
South : Madiwela Road and premises of Asst. No. 755, 755/1/1 & 755/2/1 ;
West : Lot No. 14 in PP Co. 5516.
