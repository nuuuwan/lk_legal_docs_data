STAMP  DUTY (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 12 OF 2006
Order under Section 5
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 5 of  the Stamp Duty (Special Provisions) Act, No. 12  of 2006, read with
article 44 (2) of  the Constitution  I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, do by
this order, amend the order  made under that Section and published in Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1465/ 20 of
October 05, 2006, as last amended by the order made under the said Section and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1778/ 32 of October 4, 2012 as specified in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President,
Minister of  Finance and Planning.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 1,
 01st September, 2014.

(i) item 27 published in the  Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1752/ 17 dated April 04, 2012 as item 29;

(iii) item 29  published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1778/ 32 dated October  04, 2012 as item
