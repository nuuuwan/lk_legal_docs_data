THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Maha/TMP/2013/700 dated  10th September, 2013, prepared by
the Superintendent of Surveys, and situated within the Madawala
Village, Minor Division of Udapalatha Korale-East in Thumpane
Divisional Secretary’s Division, in Kandy District, Central Province
and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galketiyawaththa – Extent: About 0.0101 Hectare
North: Galketiyawaththa claimed by D. M. D. B. Dasanayaka
and Canal; East: Canal, Lot B and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road
(R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and Galketiyawaththa claimed by
D. M. D. B. Dasanayaka.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Pansalwaththa – Extent: About 0.0101 Hectare
North: Canal and Pansalwaththa claimed by Reverent Andarawewe
Piyaratana; East: Pansalwaththa claimed by Reverent Andarawewe
Piyaratana and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot A;
West: Lot A and Canal.
