EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the Land to be acquired:  
 
An allotment of land about 0.0405 hectares in extent 
from full extent depicted as Lot 1, 2, 3 and 4 surveyed 
and prepared by the Surveyor General in PPK Plan No. 
3464 and situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
Imbulgasdeniya, in the East Beligal Korale Minor 
Division, in the Divisional Secretariat Division of 
Rambukkana, in Kegalle District. 
 
Lot No. 1 - Name of the Land: Yataththawala-
watta; Extent: 0.0358 Hectare.  
North: Mahaoya; East: Road (Road Development 
Authority); South: Stream and Yataththawalawatta; West: 
Yataththawalawatta. 
 
Lot No. 2 - Name of the Land: Yataththawala-
watta; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.  
North: Road (Road Development Authority); East: 
Yataththawalawatta; South: Road (Road Development 
Authority); West: Road (Road Development Authority). 
Lot No. 3 - Name of the Land: Wala aswadduma; 
Extent: 0.0024 Hectare.  
North: Stream and Road (Road Development 
Authority); East: Road (Road Development Authority): 
South: Lot No. 4 and Wala aswadduma; West: Lot No. 1 
in PPK 2084.  
Lot No. 4 - Name of the Land: Wala aswadduma; 
Extent: 0.0020 Hectare. 
 
North: Lot No. 1 in PPK 2084, Lot No. 3 in this plan 
and Road (Road Development Authority); East: Road 
(Road Development Authority): South: Wala 
aswadduma; West: Wala aswadduma.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land which is to 
be acquired are hereby required to appear personally or 
by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Rambukkana Divisional Secretariat on the 11th day of 
August, 2014 at 10.00 o’clock, and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
08th day of August, 2014 the nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 R. P. M
OHAN WIJERATHNA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rambukkana.  
Divisional Secretariat,  
Rambukkana, 
14th July, 2014.
