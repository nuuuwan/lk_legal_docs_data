THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                                  My Ref.  No.: AO/MN/02/02/2013. 
 
Land & Land Dev. Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2012/D/294. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude* which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
Name of the Land: Achchankulam; District: 
Mannar; Divisional  Secretariat Divi sion: Nanattan; 
Grama Niladhari: Achchankulam; Village: 
Achchankulam; Extent: 1 (One) Acre. 
 
      Boundaries : 
 
North: Road; East: Road; West: Lagoon; South: Permit 
Land Yoganathan.   
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Nanattan Divisional Secretariat, on 
the 05th day of September, 2014 at 10.30 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 25th day of August, 2014 the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ 
servitude* the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 N. T HIRUGNAANASAMPANTHER,  
 Special Acquiring Officer, 
 Mannar/Vavuniya District. 
 
Dist. Office of the Ministry of  
Land and Land Development, 
District Secretariat/Kachcheri, 
Vavuniya. 
12th August, 2014.
