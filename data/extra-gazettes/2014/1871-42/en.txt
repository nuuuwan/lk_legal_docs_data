THE PARTITION LAW, No. 21 OF 1977
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of  Justice under section 82A read with sections 8 and 29 of the Partition Law, No. 21 of

ABDUL RAUFF HAKEEM,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
11th  July,  2014.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as the Partition (Rates payable for surveying) Regulations, No.    of 2014.
2. The rates set out in Schedule I to this regulation, shall be the rates chargeable in respect of a Preliminary Survey
carried out in relation to a partition action in terms of the Partition Act, No. 21 of 1977.
3. The rates set out in Schedule II hereto shall be the rates chargeable in respect of a Final Survey carried out in
relation to a partition action in terms of the Partition Act, No. 21 of 1977.
4. The Cost  of  Survey Regulations, No. 1 of 1995 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 899/19 of December 1, 1995
as amended by regulation published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 936/11 of August 15, 1996 and regulation published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1406/1 of August 15, 2005, are hereby rescined.
