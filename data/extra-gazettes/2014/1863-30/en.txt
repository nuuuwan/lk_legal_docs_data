 LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  under  Section  10A (1) (a)
VALIKAMAM  EAST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan Returning Officer of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A (1) (a) of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Asaippillai Vaseenthiran of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the United People's
Freedom Alliance.
 P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
20th May, 2014.
