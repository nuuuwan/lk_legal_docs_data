WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
I, Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province as per powers assigned to me under Clause 154e (5) of the
13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby announce that the Hon.
Chief Minister of the Western Province Prasanna Ranathunga will be assigned with the subject of Trade with effect from
14.07.2014 in addition to the subjects currently assigned to him under the ministries of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Manpower and Employment, Engineering Services and
Information.
S. ALAVI MOWLANA,
Governor of the Western Province.
Governor’s Secretariat (W.P),
98/4, Havelock Road,
Colombo 05,
14th  day of July, 2014.
