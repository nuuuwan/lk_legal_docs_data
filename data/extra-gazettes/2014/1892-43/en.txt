EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PUTTALAM   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Puttalam  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS  the Group of  the Independent Group No. 08  to  which the   Member, Mr. M. A. R. Alisabry
belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) as amended by the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16
of 2007,  to nominate  a person  eligible under  the   Ordinance to appoint as a  member of that Puttalam  Urban Council within
a period of  thirty (30) days ;
AND  WHEREAS the Group leader of the Independent Group has    nominated  Mr. Wasala Mudiyanselage Senaviratna
Wasala Bandara being an eligible person under the aforesiad Ordinance of fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan  Returning Officer  of   Puttalam   Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) (b) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Wasala Mudiyanselage Senaviratna Wasala
Bandara  elected as a member of the Puttalam Urban Council.
P
AKKIY ARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
