THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Article 44(2)
of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order designate the Section of the “Kottawa - Kothalawala
Section  of Outer Circular National Highway” described in the Schedule to this order, which was designated as an “Expressway”
by  Order made under Section 9 of the aforesaid Act and published in the Gazette Extraordinay No. 1848/6 of February 03,
2014  to be an “User Fee National Highway”.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo.
11th February, 2014.
SCHEDULE
AREA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHWAY
The Stretch of road being 9.900 kilometers long and 40.00 - 60.00 metres broad, situated between Kottawa (0.000km.)
and Kothalawala (9.900 km.) of the Expressway, designated as the Kottawa - Kothalawala Section of Outer Circular National
Highway.
