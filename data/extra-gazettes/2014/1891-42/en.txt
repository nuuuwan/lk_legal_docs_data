NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification interms of the Section 3.8 of Establishment of Road Passenger Transport Service Authority Statute No. 08
of 1994 as amended by the North Central Provincial Council Road Transport Service Authority Statute Number
 03 of  2013
INTERMS  of the North Central Provincial Council Road Transport Service Authority Statute Number 03 of 2013 as amended,
I have appointed Mr. M. M. Upalipriya Dayananda of No. 850, Parakum Pedesa, Kaduruwela, as a member of the Authority
with effect from 15.02.2014.
It is hereby notify that I, by virtue of the power vested in me under Section 3.8 of the Road Passenger Transport
Service Authority Statute No. 08 of 1994 as amended by the Statute No. 03 of 2013, removed him from the said post with effect
from 25.11.2014.
H. B. S
EMASINGHE,
