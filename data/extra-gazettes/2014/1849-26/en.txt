And Whereas, Dr. Nihal Jinasena Esquire, the Member of the said Council has resigned from the said membership,
And whereas, the urgent need for the agriculture sector to be represented on the said Council has been identified.
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka reposing great trust
and confidence in the prudence, ability and fidelity of Prof. Weeratunge Arachchige Janendra Mathalie De Costa Esquire,
Department of crop Science, Faculty of Agriculture, Universiry of Peradeniya do hereby appoint the said Prof. Weeratunge
Arachchige Janendra Mathalie De Costa Esquire, as a member of the National Research Council established by the said
Presidential Directive.
Given at Colombo under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on this 12th day of February, Two
Thousand and Fourteen.
By Order of His Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
