Order
01. These orders are cited as Orders of “Widowers” and “Orphans” Pension Fund (allowed for preference).
02. These Orders are applicable for the female members of the service who have obtained a permanent and pensionable
post prior to the date the orders of Local Government Services ‘‘Widowers’ and  Orphans’’ Pension Fund
(Hereinafter cited as “Scheduled date”) of 1986 published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 25th October 2005
come into effect.
03. Any female member who is in service even up to the Scheduled date and could not give preference to subscribe
for the scheme.
a. Could give their preference to subscribe for the scheme after the date that this order is pulished in the
Gazette
b. The final date of giving preference for subscription is 31.12.2014.
04. (1) In case of a female member who is relevant to the 2nd order fails to subscribe for the scheme in between the
term from the scheduled date and 31st December 2014 her husband should give preference within the valid
period.
