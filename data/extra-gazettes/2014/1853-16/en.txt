 THE   WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARDS   FOR   THE   TEA   GROWING   AND   MANUFACTURING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Mr. Prasad de Silva has been appointed as the
Employers' Representatives member of the Wages Board for the Tea Growing and Manufacturing Trade in place of
Mrs. M. Aluvihare by the Hon. Minister of Labour and Labour Relations under Section 9 of the Wages Boards Ordinance
(Chapter 136). This appointment is valid only upto 01.01.2015.
W. J. L. U. W
IJAY AWEERA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Labour Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
06th March 2014.
