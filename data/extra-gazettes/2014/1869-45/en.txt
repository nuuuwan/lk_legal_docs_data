THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three (03) allotments of land, depicted in Advance Tracing
No. P .P.MAHA 5504 and dated 12th October, 2003, prepared by
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Asgiriya (Ward No. 06,
Asgiriya within the limits of Kandy Municipal Council) in the Minor
Division of Gangawata Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in Kandy District, Central Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 03 – Welikandahena – Extent: 0.0022 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 01 and02 in P .P.MAHA 5292; East: Lot No. 02;
South: Lot Nos. 02, 04, 05 and Welikandahena claimed by Nimali
Perera; West: Lot No. 01 in P.P.MAHA 5292.
02. Lot No. 06 – Welikandahena – Extent: 0.0015 Hectare
North: Lot No. 05; East: Lot No. 05 and Welikandahena claimed
by Nimali Perera; South: Welikandahena claimed by Nimali Perera;
West: Welikandahena claimed by Nimali Perera and Lot No. 05.
03. Lot No. 07 – Welikandahena – Extent: 0.0004 Hectare
North and East: Lot No. 05; South: Welikandahena claimed by
Duminda Perera; West: Welikandahena claimed by Duminda Perera
and Lot No. 05.
