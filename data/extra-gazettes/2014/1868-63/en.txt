FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBERSHIP  OF  THE
TANGALLE   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Tangalle Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of the
Southern Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member,
Nihal Wedaarachchi belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Tangalle  Urban Council who
shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Tikirihennadige Sidny
Ranjan being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathna Returning Officer of  Tangalle Urban
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Tikirihennadige Sidny Ranjan
elected as a member of the Tangalle  Urban Council.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle  Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
26th June,  2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
TANGALLE   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Tangalle Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member,
Mohomed Saleeth Insaaf Ahamed belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Tangalle
Urban Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Denagama Vitharanage
Ganesh Priyankara being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathne, Returning Officer of  Tangalle Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Denagama Vitharanage Ganesh
Priyankara  elected as a member of the Tangalle  Urban Council.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Tangalle  Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
26th June,  2014.
