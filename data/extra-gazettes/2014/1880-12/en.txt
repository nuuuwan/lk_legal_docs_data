NOTICE No : 72/0120 Monaragala District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-

lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 720027 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 12 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Kimbulawela (Block 
04) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Niladhari Divi-
sion of 128 - Kimbulawela in the Divisional Secretariat Division 
of Siyambalanduwa in the District of Monaragala in the Province

October, 2014 In the event of failure  to submit any claim before 
the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an exparte 
inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly publish such 
determination in the Government Gazette in terms of Section 14 
of the above Act.

at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat,
