FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE
PELIYAGODA  URBAN  COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in Peliyagoda Urban  Council has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which Amila
Nishantha Kumarasinghe the Vice Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the   member of that Peliyagoda Urban Council who belongs to such Party who shall be declared elected as
Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has Communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Ananda Pushpa Kumara Kalanchi Devage, to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Peliyagoda Urban  Council, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ananda Pushpa Kumara Kalanchi Devage
elected as a Vice Chairman of the of the Peliyagoda Urban Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Peliyagoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
19th May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE   PUTTALAM  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Puttalam Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation :
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 08  to which  the member, M. A. R. Inamul Hasan
belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) as amended by the Local Authorities  Elelctions (Amendment) Act, No.
16 of 2007, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance to appoint as a member of that Puttalam Urban Council within
a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group has nominated Mr. M. A. R. Ali Sabry being an eligible
person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan Returning Officer of  Puttalam Urban  Council acting in terms of Section
65A(2)(b) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. A. R. Ali Sabry elected as a member of the of the
Puttalam  Urban Council.
P
AKKIY ARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
07th May, 2014.
