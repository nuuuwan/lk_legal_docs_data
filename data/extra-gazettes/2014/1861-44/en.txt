MINUTE OF SRI LANKA INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY
SERVICE AMENDMENT No.  01
IT has been ordered by the Public Services Commission to amend the Minute of Sri Lanka Information and Communication
Technology Service, which has been published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka No. 1631/ 20 dated 09th December 2009 and came into effect from 01st July 2009, in the following manner.
(i) 8.1.1 Sub - Section (b) is hereby revised in the following manner.
8.1.1. (b) The age shall not be less than 18 years and not more than 35 years as at the closing date of the
applications.
(ii) 8.1.1. Sub - Section (f) is hereby removed and following Sub - Section is hereby included in substitution to the
above  Sub - Section.
8.1.1. (f) Professional Qualification :-
Shall have followed a course on Computer/ Information Technology at least either at or above the level five
(05) of National V ocational Qualification (NVQ).
K. V . P. M. J. G
AMAGE,

Ministry of Public Administration and Home Affairs,
Colombo 07,
