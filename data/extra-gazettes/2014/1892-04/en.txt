THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three (03) allotments of land containing   in extent of about 0.8093
Hectare (A:02, R: 0, P:0), depicted as Lots.  A, B and C in
Advance Tracing No. MR/HKM/2013/661  prepared by Surveyor
General and situated in the Village of  Vepathaira in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Hakmana, in  Matara  District and bounded
as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’  – Bibulekumbura  – Extent: 0.1515 Hectare
North: Bibule kumbura claimed by G. K. M. Rathnasiri and Road
(R.D.A); East:  Road (R.D.A) and Irikonda claimed by Ranjith
Senanayake; South: Irikonda claimed by H.M.A. Sirinimal and others
and Lot B and C ; West:  Lot B,  C and Bibule kumbura claimed
G . K. M. Rathnasiri.
02. Lot ‘B’  – Bibulekumbura  – Extent: 0.5798 Hectare
North: Lot C and A; East: Irikonda claimed by Ranjith Senanayake,
Irikonda claimed by H.M.A. Sirinimal and others; South: Irikonda
claimed by  H.M.A. Sirinimal and others and Bibule kumbura claimed
by Siripala Samararathna and Ranjan Samararathna; West: Bibule
kumbura claimed by Siripala Samararathna and Ranjan Samararathna
and Lot C.
03. Lot ‘C’  – Bibulekumbura  – Extent: 0.0780 Hectare
North: Bibule kumbura claimed by G . K. M. Rathnasiri and Lot A;
East: Lot B; South: Lot B and  Bibule kumbura claimed by V . G .
Nandasiri; West: Bibule kumbura claimed by G . K. M. Rathnasiri.
