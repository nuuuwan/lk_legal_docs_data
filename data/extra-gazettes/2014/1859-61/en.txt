NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION 10A(1)(a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Ambalangoda  Urban  Council
I, Anthoni Bimal Indrajith De Silva, Returning Officer of the Ambalangoda Urban Council, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Kalumarakkala Chandrasiri of the Ambalangoda
Urban Council has vacated his office of member of that Council as he ceased to be member of the United National Party.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Ambalangoda Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Galle.
