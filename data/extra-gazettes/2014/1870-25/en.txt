THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called Gorakagahawatta alias Passionwatta
in total extent of about 0.552 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in the
Plan No. Gam/A/88/35 dated 31.10.1988 prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys - Gampaha District situated in the Village
called Ihala Biyanwila in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 269B, of
Ihala Biyanwila of  Adikaripattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Biyagama in Gampaha District of theWestern
Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot  No. 01  – Gorakagahawatta alias Passionwatta –
Extent: About 0.552 Hectare
North: Depa Ela; East: Bulugahawatta claimed  by P. Jemis Perera
and Cemetry of Ihala Biyanwila;  South: Depa Ela and Bulugahawatta
claimed by P. Jemis Perera;West: Cemetery of Ihala Biyanwila and
Depa Ela.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
