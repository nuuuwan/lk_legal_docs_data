SPORTS   ACT,  No. 25  OF   1973
Orders under Sections 40 and 41
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 54(3), Regulations of National Sports Associations, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1825/38 of 30th August 2013, made under Section 41 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973.
I, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, Minister of Sports do by this order direct that Mrs. Maharage Duggannaralalage
Ranjani Jayakody, the Director General of the Department of Sports Development, Ministry of Sports to convene and
conduct a meeting of the Sri Lanka Netball Association to elect the new office bearers.
M
AHINDANANDA  ALUTHGAMAGE ,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
25th  November 2014.
