DECLARATION OF WARAPITIYA SITHTHAM GALLENA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Warapitiya Siththam Gallena Rajamaha Vihara sacred area", for its long-
term security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF WARAPITIY A SITHTHAM GALLENA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 7.1157 Hectares
depicted in Lot No. A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H and J situated at Thalapathkanda village in Warapitiya Grama Niladari Division in Minor
Division of Giruwa Paththuwa-North in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Walasmulla of the Administrative District of
Hambanthota in Southern  Province depicted in  plan No.H/WLS/2011/397  dated 29
th July  2011 of Surveyor General.
