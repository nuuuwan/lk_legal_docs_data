DECLARATION OF LANKA PATUNA SAMUDRAGIRI RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED
AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND
NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as “Samudragiri Rajamaha Vihara sacred area”, for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF LANKA PATUNA SAMUDRAGIRI RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 9.3214 Hectares depicted in
below mentioned Part I, Part II situated in Seenanweli Grama Niladari Division in Koddiyar Paththu Division in the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Werugal of the Administrative District of Trincomalee in Eastern Province depicted in plan
No.Tri/ICH/2009/249A dated 25
th March 2011 of Surveyor General.
