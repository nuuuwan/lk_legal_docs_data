EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN OF  THE   HOMAGAMA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   (SPECIAL  PROVISION)   ACT,  No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Chairman in Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Andarage Don Kumarasiri the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
decide upon the member of  that Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected as
Chairman  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has communicated his decision  to nominate
Mr. Handhapangodage Ruwan Prabhoda Peiris to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardena, Returning Officer of  Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local
authorites (Special Porvisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Handhapangodage Ruwan Prabhoda
Peiris elected as Chairman of the  Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDENA,
Returning Officer,
Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Election Office,
Colombo  District,
24th of  April,  2014.
