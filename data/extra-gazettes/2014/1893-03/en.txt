EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)   
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref.  No.: ACQ/O /J/D/51. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired: 
 
Preliminary plan : Ya 2770 
Situation Village : Allarai 
GN Division : Kovilkudiyiruppu, J/301 
DS Division : Chavakachcheri 
District : Jaffna 
Province : Northern 
 
01. Lot No. 1 — Name of the Land: 
Uluthampulakadu; Description: Garden contains 
forty three permanent buildings, Five masonry wells 
and a water tank, Name of the Claimant: Jhon 
Devekumar willson, 1713/B, Model farm Road, 
Borella, Colombo 08;  Extent: 9.9520 Hectares.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Chavakachcheri on the 28th Wednesday of January 2015 
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 14th Wednesday of 
January, 2015 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, amount of compensation and 
the details of the computation of such amount.  
 
 
 
 
 W ICKRAMA WARAGODA, 
 Acquiring Officer (LLRC) 
 District Secretariat, 
 Jaffna.
