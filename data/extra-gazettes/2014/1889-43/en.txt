I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Invented by the Land 
Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intents to acquire the following land. Which is required 
for public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land: 
 
A Portion of land called Kalugalawaththa bearing in 
total extent of 53 acres, S hown as from Lot Numbers 89, 
35, 195 of the plan No. LRC/ බ/64/බ/2 and 19th April, 
1974 Dated, Surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor 
General of Badulla District, Situated in Grama 
Niladhari’s division of Dodamgolla and Villages of 
Stripuregama (FVP 275), Pitahagedara (FVP 276), 
Udawadiya (FVP 277) in the minor Division of Kumbal-
wela Koralage in the Divisional Secretary’s division of 
Ella in Badulla District, Uva-province and described as 
Follows. 
 
FVP No.: 275 Supplement No. 1 
01. Lot No. 89 
 
Name of the Land: Kalugalawaththa; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: A. Karuppan Chettiyar, Kalugala-
waththa, Kalugala; Extent: 15 Acres, 07 Perches. 
FVP No.: 276 Supplement No. 1 
02. Lot No. 35 
 
Name of the Land: Kalugalawaththa; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: A. Karuppan Chettiyar, Kalugala-
waththa, Kalugala; Extent: 07 Acres, 15 Perches. 
 
FVP No.: 277 Supplement No. 1 
02. Lot No. 195 
 
Name of the Land: Kalugalawaththa; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: A. Karuppan Chettiyar, Kalugala-
waththa, Kalugala; Extent: 30 Acres, 03 Roods, 18 
Perches. 
 
 
All persons interested in above land on which a 
Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Ella Divisional Secretary’s Office on the 17th of 
December, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Also hereby requested to notify to me in writing on or 
before the 17th of December,  2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land. The 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amounts.
