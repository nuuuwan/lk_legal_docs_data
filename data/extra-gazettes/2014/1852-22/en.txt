Order No. 10
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Order under Section 18
I. Johnston Xaviour Fernando, Minister of Co-operatives and Internal Trade being of opinion that the following  goods are
essential to the life of the community and having consulted the Consumer Affairs Authority, do by this order prescribe them
as “Specified goods ” under Section 18(1) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act No. 09 of 2003, with immediate effect.
LIST  OF   SPECIFIED   GOODS
All drugs registered with the Cosmetics, Devices & Drugs Authority (CDDA)
J
OHNSTON XA VIOUR FERNANDO,
Minister of Co-operatives and Internal Trade.
Colombo,
04th March 2014.
