EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER OF THE  WILGAMUWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA UNDER SECTION
65A(2) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES ELECTIONS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY LOCAL
AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of a member in Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which the
member Yapa Mudiyanselage Sooriya Bandara belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that
Wilgamuwa  Pradeshiya  Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified;
Now  therefore, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning  Officer of Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. M. A. G . Rupasinghe
Bandara who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election held
on 23rd July 2011 to Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a Member of the Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha  in respect of
which such vacancy occurred.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale.
10th April, 2014.
