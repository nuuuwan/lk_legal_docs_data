THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land called Thippolewatta / Wijehingewatta /
Dombagahawatta depicted in the Advance Tracing No. R/GDK/2011/
0806 of 29.02.2012 prepared by Rathnapura Surveyor General in
the Village of Kotakethana (F.V .P. No. 187) in Medapattuwa Minor
Division in Godakawela Divisional Secretary's Division in Rathnapura
District. Lot ‘‘A’’ containing  in extent of  0.0343 Hectare and bounded
for the same as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thippolewatta, Wijehingewatta, Dombagahwatta
– Extent: 0.0343 Hectare
North: Wea Ganga and Lot No. 2; East: Lot No. 2 and Road;
South: Road and Lot No. 2; West: Lot No. 2 and Wea Ganga.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
