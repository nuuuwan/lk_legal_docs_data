EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 07 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.:-10/3/1/3/39. 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/HW/354. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, 
of land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a Construction of bridge 
No. 25/9, Wattegama-Kandenuwara - Wariyapola Road.  
 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired :-  
 
Allotments of land, described in below in Plan              
No. Ma 1609, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents 
of Surveys, situated in Godapola village, Godapola 
Gramaniladari Division, in Sub Division of Matale 
Udasiya Pattuwa, in the Ratto ta Divisional Secretariat 
Division, in Matale District, in Central Province. 
 01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the land: Godapola Watta; Discription: 
Garden and scrub; Claimant: Mr. Saman Aluvihare,            
No. 27, Main Street, Matale; Extent: 0.0147 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Rattota Divisional 
Secretariat on  the 20th day of August, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing              
(in duplicate) on or before the 12th day of  August, 2014 
the nature of their interests in the Land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 B. M. G. B. W
IJEBANDARA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rattota.
