THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (2)  allotments of land in total extent of about 2.8425 Hectares,
shown as Lots A and B  in Advance Tracing No. MR/WLP/2013/670
and dated 27.12.2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the Village of  Sahabandu Kokmaduwa Minor Division of Weligama
Korale,   Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Welipitiya, in Matara
District in Sourthern Province.
1. Lot ‘A’ –  Delgahawela Kumbura – Extent: 2.8328 Hectare
North: Road (Pra. Sabha), Kutugoda Kumbura claimed by
Mahendra Priyankara and others and Muruthagaha Kumbura claimed
by S. J. K. Richerd and others ; East: Muruthagaha Kumbura claimed
by S. J. K. Richerd and others, Muruthagaha Waththa claimed by
S. J. K. Richerd and others, Egodaradage Waththa claimed by S. J. K.
Edvin Marabe Koratuwa claimed by S. J. K. Shantha and others,
Waguru Kumbura claimed by  M. Somasiri and others, Ketapitiya
Kumbura claimed by S.J.K. Seethawathi and others, Lots C and B;
South: Ketapitiya Kumbura claimed by S.J.K. Seethawathi and
others, Lot C, B and Road (Pra. Sabaha); West: Road (Pra. Sabaha)
and Mohattiwila Kumbura claimed by Poddala Priyankarage Saranelis
and others.
2. Lot ‘B’ –  Ketapitiya Kumbura – Extent: 0.0097 Hectare
North: Lot A; East: Lot C; South: Lot C and Road (Pra. Sabaha);
West: Road (Pra. Sabaha) and Lot A.
