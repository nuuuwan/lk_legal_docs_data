Notice Under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: Co/DS/LA/3/52/UDA. 
 
I hereby Notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the 
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for the public purpose. 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
 
Five allotments of lands depicted as Lot Nos. 1 to 5 an 
extent of 1.2660 Hectares in  preliminary Plan No. Co. 
9357 prepared by Surveyor General situated at Modara 
village, Grama Niladari Division of Mattakkuliya 
(Division No. 1 - Colombo Municipal Council) within the 
limits of Colombo Divisional Secretariat area in the 
Colombo District, Western Province :- 
 
1. Lot No. 01  
 
Name of Land: The land bearing Asst. No. 273 (part), 
Fergusion Road; Description: Garden; Claimant: 
Unknown; Total land Extent: 0.0121 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02  
 
Name of Land: The land bearing Asst. No. 19 (part), 
Henamulla Lane; Description: Garden, A part of 
permanent building; Claimant: State; Total land Extent: 
1.1540 Hectare. 
3. Lot No. 03  
 
Name of Land: The land bearing Asst. No. 19 (part), 
Henamulla Lane; Description: Road; Claimant: State; 
Total land Extent: 0.0330 Hectare. 
 
4. Lot No. 04  
 
Name of Land: The land bearing Asst. No. 19 (part), 
Henamulla Lane; Description: Garden, A part of 
permanent building; Claimant: State; Total land Extent: 
0.0485 Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 05  
 
Name of Land: The land bearing Asst. No. 19 (part), 
Henamulla Lane; Description: Garden; Claimant: State; 
Total land Extent: 0.0183 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Colombo, on the 29th April, 2014 at  10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 22nd April, 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
compensation of such amount.  
 
 D. P. W ICKRAMASINGHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Colombo. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,
