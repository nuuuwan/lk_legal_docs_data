LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Notice  under  Section 10A(1)(a)
MANNAR  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)
(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Abdul Muthalif Mohamed Risafi of the Mannar Pradeshiya Sabha has
vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Mannar  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Mannar,
29th May, 2014.
