UV A   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Proclamation
BY , virtue of the powers vested to me by Article 154 B, Section (08) Sub-section (a) of the Thirteenth Amendment to the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby
summon the Provincial Council of the Uva Province to meet at 9.30 a.m. on the 13th day of January, 2015 in the Council
Chamber of the Provincial Council of Uva Province.
C. N
ANDA MA THEW,
 Governor of Uva Province.
At Badulla,
31st December, 2014.
