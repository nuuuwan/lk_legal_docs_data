FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
HORANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Horana Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the member
Nambige Rohan Piris, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that  Horana Pradeshiya Sabha
who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Abesinghege Don
Dasarath Somachandra being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Horana Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Abesinghege Don Dasarath Somachandra
elected as a member of the Horana Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Horana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kalutara,
03rd June, 2014.
