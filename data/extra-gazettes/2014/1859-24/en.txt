EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER  OF  THE
V A VUNIYA  SOUTH  SINHALA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL
AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which the
member Naghamighe Piyadasa belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Vanuniya South
Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
Now therefore, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Vanuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr. Weerasinge Dissanayage Achira
Kalum Sampath Dissanayaka  who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared
elected at the election held on 17th March of 2011 to Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha elected as  member of the
Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Vavuniya South Sinhala Pradeshiya Sabha
Elections Office,
Vavuniya,
01st  April, 2014.
