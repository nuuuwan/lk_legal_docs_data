Order No. 26
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader or distributor shall cause retail sale, expose or offer for retail sale, display for
retail sale in bags, packs or in any other form, the following varieties of rice beyond the Maximum Retail Prices given below ;
Maximum Retail Price
(Per Kg)
Samba - destoned and cleaned (Excluding Keeri Samba 77.00
and Suduru Samba)
White Nadu 68.00
Red Nadu 66.00
White Raw 66.00
Red Raw 60.00
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 05 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1593/28 dated 18th March, 2009 with regard to prices of rice.
                                                                       By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
Rumy Marzook,
Chairman.
Colombo,
09th April 2014.
