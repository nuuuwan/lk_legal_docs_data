THE  DIRECTION  PUBLISHED  IN  THE  EXTRAORDINARY  GAZETTE  No. 1681/3  DATED
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
Declaring the Gampola Ambuluwawa D.M. Jayarathna Biological Diversity Complex and the
Religious Centre as a Sacred Area
ACCORDING to the communication made by His Excellency the President of  the Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic in
the Sri Lanka Democratic Socialist Republic Extraordinary Gazette No. 1681/3 dated 22 nd Monday November 2010, and
according to the subject of  planning and developing the sacred places empowered, I Dr. Disanayaka Mudiyanselage
Jayarathna declare that the land area definitely described in the schedule herein is a sacred area forever and declare that as
‘Gampola  Ambuluwawa D.M. Jayarathna Biological Diversity Complex and Sacred Religious Centre’.
D
R. D.M. JAY ARATHNA,
Minister of Buddasasana and Religious Affairs.
Ministry of Buddasasana and Religious Affairs,
No. 135, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo 07.
18
thNovember, 2014.
