FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  BIYAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Biyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Death :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which the
member Iroshan Chamathkara  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Biyagama Pradeshiya
Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Abdul Carder Anifa
Mohamed Anver being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Biyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Abdul Carder Anifa Mohamed Anver elected as
a Member of the Biyagama Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Biyagama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
02nd May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  MAHARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being Elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which the
member M. W. D. Sahan Pradeep Vithana  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Mahara
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. K. T. Suraj Udayanga
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. K. T. Suraj Udayanga elected as a Member of the
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
28th April, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF CHAIRMAN OF THE  MAHARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being Elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance recognized political party  to which M. W. D.
Sahan Pradeep Vithana  Chairman belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that  Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman
within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Wanni Arachchige Susantha Siri to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Mahara  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Wanni Arachchige Susantha Siri elected as
Chairman of the Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Mahara Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
28th April, 2014.
