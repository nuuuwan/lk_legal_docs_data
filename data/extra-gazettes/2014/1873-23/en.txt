THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Re. No.: DSB/L/1/2/119. 
 
Ref. No. of Land Ministry: 4-3/8/2012/PC/302. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land in Extent about 0.0983 
Hectare, depicted in Sheet No. 5, Zonal 5 in C.M. No. 
620086 prepared by the Surv eyor General, situated at 
Pallewela Village, No. 256 C, Thumbagoda Grama 
Niladari Division, Balangod a Divisional Secretariat, 
Rathnapura District, and Sabaragamuwa Province.  
 
SCHEDULE 
 
01. Lot No. 203  
Name of Land: Pinmullekumbura, Gedaragawa arawa, 
Telikaragekumbura; Claimant: R. M. Rodrigo, No. 279, 
Halawata Road, Daluwakotuwa, Kochchikade; Extent: 
0.0983 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Balangoda Divisional Secretariat on 
the 10th day of September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 03rd day of September, 2014 at 10.00 
a.m. the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
