THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02) allotments of  land in total extent of about 0.427 Hectare,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. 2006/107 of 12.07.2006 prepared
by the Surveyor General of Badulla District. The Lots  A and B
called Sapumaltenna or  Wetahirewatta situated in the Village of Ella
Town  in the Minor Division of Kumbalwela Korale  in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Ella in Badulla District, Uva - Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Sapumaltenna  or  Wetahirewatta – Extent:
About 0.042 Hectare
North: Road (R); East: Lot No. 49 of T.S.P.P. 11; South: Drought
drain;West: Road (Pradesiya Saba).
Lot ‘B’ – Sapumaltenna or Wetahirewatta – Extent: About
0.385 Hectare
North: Road (Pradeshiya Saba) and Drought drain; East: Lot No.
49 of  T.S.P .P. 11 and Drought drain; South: Drought drain and Lot
Nos. 81 and 80 of  T.S.P.P.11;West: Lot Nos. 81, 80, 78, 77 of
T.S.P.P.11 and Road (Pradesiya Saba).
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
