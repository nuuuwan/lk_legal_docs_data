FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
JAFFNA  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Jaffna Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three consecutive
meetings without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognised political party namely Tamil United Liberation Front,  to which the
member,  Iramaiah Sangaiya belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Jaffna Municipal
Council  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Irasathurai Pushparasa
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Ponnudurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Jaffna Municipal Council, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Irasathurai Pushparasa  elected as a member of the Jaffna
Municipal Council.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Jaffna Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
14th  July, 2014.
