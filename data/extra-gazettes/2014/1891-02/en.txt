CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, Alisandaralage Senanayake,  Acting Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 01st  December, 2014, all
duties of  Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
shall be   paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1890/1  of
24th  November, 2014 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 07th  December,  2014.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
28th   November  2014.
