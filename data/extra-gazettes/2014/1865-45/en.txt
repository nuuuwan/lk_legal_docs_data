FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF   CHAIRMAN
OF  THE  V ALIKAMAM  EAST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISION)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman  in Valikamam East  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which Annalingam
Uthayakumar  the Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon
the member of that Valikamam  East Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Chairman
within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate Mr. Kanapathipillai Thuraisingam  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Valikamam  East  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kanapathipillai Thuraisingam elected as
Chairman of the Valikamam  East Pradeshiya Sabha.
P.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam  East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
05th  June, 2014.
