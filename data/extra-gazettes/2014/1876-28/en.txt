FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
BINGIRIYA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a  member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which Mr. M. P.
Hemachandra Kularathna  the former member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Appuhamy Lushan Reginold Rathnayakage to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Bingiriya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Appuhamy
Lushan Reginold Rathnayakage  elected as a member of the Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Bingiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
19th  August, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
UDUBADDAWA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which Mr. Balasooriya
Mudiyanselage Stanley Senarath Balasooriya the former member  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mrs. Arachchilage Geetha Kumuduni Wijesinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Udubaddawa
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs. Arachchilage
Geetha Kumuduni Wijesinghe  elected as a member of the Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
19th  August, 2014.
