PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
BINGIRIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of the  Bingiriya    Pradeshiya Sabha, do
hereby declare under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262)  that Mr. M. P.
Hemachandra Kularathna of the Bingiriya   Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of  member of that   Pradeshiya Sabha
as he  has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
T. A. C. N.  T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
 Bingiriya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
25th July, 2014.
