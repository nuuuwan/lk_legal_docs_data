THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Three (03)  allotments of land called Gapland Estate  in extent of
about 8.760 Hectares, depicted  as  Lot  Nos. 1, 2, 3  in  Advance
Tracing  No. MAHA/UDD/ACQ/95/556 dated 09.10.2002 prepared
by the Survey Superintendent of Kandy situated  in Loolwatta Village
Gandeka Korale Minor Division  in  Udadumbara Divisional
Secretary's Division  in  Kandy   District, Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01  – Gapland Estate  – Extent: About 6.817
Hectares
North: Dehigolla Estate;  East: Rosmil Estate; South : Stream;
West: Road (P. S.).
02. Lot No. 02  – Gapland Estate  – Extent: About 1.907
Hectares
North: Stream and Rosmil Estate; East: Rosmil Estate;South :
Stream and Gapland Estate; West: Road.
03. Lot No. 03  – Gapland Estate  – Extent:About 0.036 Hectare
North: Stream; East and South: Gapland Estate; West:  Stream
(P . S.).
