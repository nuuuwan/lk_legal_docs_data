EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE  PUTTALAM  URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS
AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member in Puttalam Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation :
And whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 08  to which M. M. Rasmi the member belonged, was
required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for
Election as a member of that Puttalam Urban Council who belongs to such Group who shall be declared elected as a member
within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Leader of the aforesaid Group  has nominated  Mr. M. A. R. Inamul Hasan being an eligible person
under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan ,  Returning Officer of  Puttalam Urban Council, acting in terms of Section
65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. A. R. Inamul Hasan   elected as a member of the  Puttalam Urban
Council.
P
AKKIY ARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
28th January, 2014.
