FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE
PANADURA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A  (1)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which
Wannakuwattha Waduge Neel Sunethra Lal Fernando the Chairman  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such Party who shall
be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days.
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has Communicated his decision to
nominate   Mr. Ifaz Muhammadu Nabuhan Muhammadu, to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ifaz Muhammadu Nabuhan Muhammadu elected
as Chairman of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara.
23rd May, 2014.
