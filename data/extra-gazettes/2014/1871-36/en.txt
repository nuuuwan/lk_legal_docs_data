THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
conservation of Knuckles and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
An allotment of land called Madoltenna bearing an extent of about
0.381 Hectare, shown as Lot ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/
MDD/2006/61 dated 21.04.2006, prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the Village of Kandekumbura in Minor Division of
Udasiyapattuwa South of Medadumbara Divisional Secretary’s
Division in Kandy District in Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Madoltenna – Extent: About 0.381 Hectare
North: Madoltenna claimed by Junul Abdeen, Chandra Ekanayaka
and others; East: Madoltenna claimed by Chandra Ekanayaka and
others; South: Madoltenna claimed by W. M. A. P. Loku Menika;
West: Madoltenna claimed by Jenul Abdeen.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
