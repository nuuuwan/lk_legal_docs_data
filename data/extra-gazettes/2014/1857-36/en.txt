No. 1857/36 – SATURDAY, APRIL 12, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (02) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0128 Hectare
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Maha/DOL/2012/683 and dated
26th February, 2013, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in
the Village of Medapitiya (CP 320026) in Kandukara Pahala Korale
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Doluwa in
Kandy District, Central Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Ambagasthenna  alias Liyowatta  alias
Oruwaketutennehena – Extent: About 0.0115 Hectare
North: Liyowatta claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board; East: Liyowatta claimed by National Water
Supply and Drainage Board and Ela; South: Ela and Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha); West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Liyowatta claimed by
National Water Supply and Drainage Board.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Ambagasthenna  alias Liyowatta  alias
Oruwaketutennehena – Extent: About 0.0013 Hectare
North: Ela and Liyowatta claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board; East: Liyowatta claimed by National Water Supply
and Drainage Board and Oruwaketutennehena claimed by G . R.
Seelawathie; South: Oruwaketutennehena claimed by G . R.
Seelawathie and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha) and Ela.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
