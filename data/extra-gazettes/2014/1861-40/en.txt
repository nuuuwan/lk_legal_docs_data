EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
KALUTARA  URBAN COUNCIL   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES
 (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of   a member in Kalutara Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member of the
Western Province Provincial Council ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the recognized political party   namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which the
member Pahalage Rohana Priyankara Abeygunawardana  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election  as a  member of
that Kalutara Urban Council  who  shall be declared  elected as  a member  ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has nominated Mr. Kahatapitiyage Nalin
Mahesh Perera being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Kalutara Urban Council, acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)   do hereby declare  Mr. Kahatapitiyage Nalin Mahesh Perera  elected as a member
of the Kalutara Urban Council.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Kalutara Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Kalutara.
7th  May, 2014.
