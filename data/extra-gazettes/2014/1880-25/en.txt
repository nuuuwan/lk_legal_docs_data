STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the  non placement of the 81 employees inclusive of Mr. P. V. Livinis and Mrs. R. D. Dhammika Kumari Francisco mentioned in the
schedule attached hereto, in permanent service when they were given employment once again at the Sri Lanka Transport Board after working
previously at Werahera Engineering Services  Company Limited is just, and if not so, to what reliefs each of them is entitled.

Accting Commissioner of Labour.

Colombo, this 11th day of September, 2014.
