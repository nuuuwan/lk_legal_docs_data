CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Jagath Premalal Wijeweera Director General of  Customs, determine that with effect  from 28th April  2014, all  duties of
Customs as well as other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the  Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1859/1 of
21st April, 2014  is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 04th May, 2014.
J
AGATH P. WIJEWEERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 01,
25th April, 2014.
