FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER SHIP OF  THE
IPALOGAMA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   member  in  Ipalogama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of
the Party;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the  recognized Political party   to which  Prasanna
Sugath  Herath, the former member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of   the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the   member of  that   Ipalogama  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as a
member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has Communicated his  decision to
nominate  Mr. Ilyas Muhajir  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of   Ipalogama Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ilyas Muhajir  elected as a
member of the Ipalogama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S.  M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Ipalogama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
14th  August,  2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER SHIP  OF  THE
RAMBEWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   member  in  Rambewa  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  ceased to be a member of
the Party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party  the  recognized political party   to which  Anarathnayaka
Mudiyanselage Chaminda Nalaka Saliya Rathna,  the former member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to  decide upon the   member of that   Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as a member within a  period of  thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated this decision to
nominate Mr. Abdeen Ibrahim Saibu   to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa, Returning Officer of  Rambewa  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Abdeen Ibrahim Saibu  elected
as a member of the Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S.  M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Rambewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
14th  August,  2014.
