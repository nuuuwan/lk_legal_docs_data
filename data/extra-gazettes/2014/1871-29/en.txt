THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been named as “ Premakeerthi De Alwis
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of the Western Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the
subject of Local Government under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall
be read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
J. M. B
ADRANI JAYAWARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
(1) Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
(2) District - Colombo
(3) Present name of the Street - A part of Independence Square Mawatha (The Common Access road between
the Sri Lanka Broadcasting Corporation and the Sri Lanka Rupawahini
Corporation)
(4) New Name of the Street - “PREMAKEERTHI   DE   ALWIS   MAW ATHA”,
(4) Road Description - This road starting  between  Assessment Number  08 and 15 of Baudhaloka
Mawatha (Cinnamon Garden Ward) and ends near Independence Square
Mawatha, (The Common road access between the Sri Lanka Broadcasting
Corporation and the Sri Lanka Rupawahini Corporation)
