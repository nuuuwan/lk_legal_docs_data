FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
MATHUGAMA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely People's Liberation Front  to which the
member,  Owitigala Hewage Rohana Chithral Priyantha belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. H. W. Piyasena  being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. H. W. Piyasena  elected as a member
of the Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
04th  July, 2014.
