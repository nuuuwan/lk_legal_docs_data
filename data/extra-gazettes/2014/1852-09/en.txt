Appointments  by the President
No. 190/ 1 of  2014
Promotion approved  His Excellency the President
P.S. No.: CSA/ 7/ 6/ 4
IT is hereby notified that Air Marshal Harsha Duminda Abeywickrama, RWP, RSP,VSV , USP, rcds, psc, qfi,  has been promoted
to the rank of Air Chief Marshal of the Sri Lanka  Air Force, with  effect from February 26, 2014.

LALITH WEERATUNGA,

No. 190/ 2 of  2014

PROMOTION AND APPOINTMENT APPROVED BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT
P.S. No.: CSA/ 7/ 6/ 4
