PART III – GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRA TIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA – 01.09.2010
1A
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two  allotments of  lands in total extent of about 51. 7976
Hectares, depicted in Lots A  to B in the Advance Tracing No.
Maha/PIK/2012/648 and dated on  20.05.2014, prepared  by the
Surveyor-General,  situated in the Miyanagolla Village of Gangaihala
Korale Minor Division in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Gangaihala Korale at Kandy District in Central Province and  bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galamuduna Watte – Extent: 48.3028 Hectares
North: Galamudunawatte belongs to Land Reforms Commission
and Road (RDA); East: Road (RDA), Road and Galamuduna watte
belongs to Kahawatte Plantation Company; South: Galamuduna watte
belongs to Kahawatte Plantation Company and Weladeniya
belongs to unidentified owner; West: Weladeniya belongs to
unidentified owner and Galamuduna watte belongs to Land Reforms
Commission.
2. Lot ‘B’ – Galamuduna Watte – Extent: 3.4948 Hectares
North: Road (RDA); East: Road (RDA), Road and Ma Oya;
South: Galamuduna watte belongs to Kahawatte Plantation Company
and Road; West: Road.
     Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No : 4/3/2/2011/FR/30.
