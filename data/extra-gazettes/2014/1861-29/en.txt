THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
The Gazette Notice No. 1856/19 published on the 01st of April 2014 is cancelled hereby and
this notice is valid from this day
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule hereunder has been re named as “ Bernad Soysa Mawatha” by
the Honourable Governor of the Western Province under Section 71(1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947
(Chapter 252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989.
J. M. B
HADRANI JAYAW ARDENA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
(1) Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
(2) District - Colombo
(3) Present name of the Street - Kirula Road
(4) New Name of the Street - “Bernad Soysa Mawatha”
(4) Road Description - This road starting from in between Assessment Numbers 7/14 and the
Assessment Number 10 of the Kirula Road adjacent to Thimbirigasyaya Road
within the Kirula Municipal Ward ends in between the Assessment Nos. 285/1
and 252.
