ORDER  PERTAINING  TO  ASSIGNING  THE  POWERS  OF  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC
SERVICE  UNDER  CLAUSE  32  OF  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ACT,  No.  42  OF  1987  AMENDED  BY
PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF  1990
I, Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province, as per powers entrusted in me under Clause No. 32 of
Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990, subjected to
enforcing by myself the powers related to appointment, transfer (including release from position  of officers in positions of
the Western Province Provincial Public  Service as described in schedule 01 below and appointment, transfer (including
release from position) of officers of the Western Province Provincial Public Service as described in schedule 02 below, shall
hereby assign;  having exempted the aforesaid powers  the other powers (to the extent of relevance) related to disciplinary
control and dismissal from service related to the said schedules of 01 and 02 including powers related to appointment,
transfer, dismissal from service and disciplinary control ; to the Western Province Provincial Public Service Commission with
effect from 31st of March 2014.
Further, it shall hereby be prescribed and declared that any party dissatisfied with the implementation of powers
assigned thus, by the Western Province Provincial Public Service Commission except in an occasion where  Western
Province Provincial Public Service Commission implements powers related to disciplinary control and dismissal from service
with regard to positions stated in schddule 02 below, has the right to appeal to the Governor of the Western Province.
Moreover, when implementing powers by the Western Province Provincial Public Service Commission according to the
powers stated under Sub-clause 32(2) (a) in the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of 1987 amended by Provincial Councils
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1990, unless any order is specifically made by me related to such powers, permission shall hereby
be granted to assign those powers to the Chief Secretary of the Werstern Province or certain officer as the Western Province
Provincial Public Service Commission decides.
In addition to that the order dated June 10 of 2013 pertaining to assigning of powers related to Western Province
Provincial Public Service as announced by me in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka under No. 1815/12 dated 20th June 2013 that has been enforced prior to assigning of these powers, shall hereby
be repealed without any prejudice to actions taken under the said order.
S. Alavi Mowlana,
Governor of the Western Province.
At the Governor’s Secretariat of the Western Province,
No. 98/4,
Havelock Road,
Colombo 05.
24th March, 2014.
