THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 1980.
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Minister-in-charge of the subject of
Defence and Urban Development, being of opinion that the lands specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for
widening of Akuregoda Pothuawawa Road under the Relocation Project of Tri Forces Head Quarters in the Akuregoda area do
by this order declare that the said lands are required for aforesaid purpose.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
07th July, 2014.
