The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
L.D.B.  4/ 2009.
THE ANIMALS  ACT, No. 29 OF 1958
Order  under Section 11 (C)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 11 (c) of the Animals Act, No. 29 of 1958, I Arumugan Thondaman, Minister
of  Livestock and Rural Community Development do by this order suspend the implementation of the regulation 22 in part
of the Regulations made under the aforesaid Act, and published in the  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1629/17  of
November  26, 2009,  for a period of two years.
A
RUMUGAN THONDAMAN,
Minister of Livestock and Rural
Community Development.
Colombo,
08th  December, 2014.
