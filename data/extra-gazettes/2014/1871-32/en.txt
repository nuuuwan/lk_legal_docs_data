PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
Uva  Province  Provincial Council
Notice under Section 10
NOTICE  is hereby given under Section 10 of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, (hereinafter referred to as the
Act) that elections are to be held for the purpose of electing members to the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
Districts situated within the Province, and specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto.
2. The number of candidates to be nominated for each such Administrative Districts as is specified in Column I, by a
recognized political party or an independent group, shall be as specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the
Schedule hereto.
3. Nomination papers will be received by the respective Returning Officers at the places mentioned in the corre-
sponding entry in Column III of the Schedule hereto, at any time during office hours during the “nomination period” which will
commence on 30
th July 2014 and expire at 12.00 noon on 06th August 2014.
4. The attention of any group of persons intending to contest as independent candidates is drawn to Section 14 of
the Act under which the group leader of each independent group or any person authorised by him must deposit with
Returning Officer between the date of publication of this notice and 12.00 noon on the day immediately preceding the end of
the nomination period specified above (i. e. 05th August 2014) a sum calculated at the rate of Two Thousand Rupees (Rs. 2,000)
per candidate.
5. Nomination Papers in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Returning Officer free of charge, before the
expiry of the nomination period.
6. Approved symbols for recognised political parties and approved symbols and distinguishing numbers for
independent groups will be allotted by the Returning Officers at each of the District Secretariat (Kachcheri), immediately after
12.00 noon on 06th August 2014.
MAHINDA DESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
16
th July, 2014.
