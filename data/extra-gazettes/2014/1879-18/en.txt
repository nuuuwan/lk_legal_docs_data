No. 1879/18 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Five (05) allotments of land called Delgahawatta and Delgahalanda
in extent of about 0.0158 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing
No. Gam/Kela/2013/236 dated 03.04.2014, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys of Gampaha District, situated in the
Village of Wedikanda in the Minor Division of Adhikari Pattuwa in
Kelaniya Divisional Secretary’s Division in Gampaha District,
Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Delgahawatta – Extent: About 0.0057 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 6 and 7 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894 and
Delgahawatta claimed by W. A. Pushpawathi; East: Delgahawatta
claimed by W. A. Pushpawathi and balance portion of same land;
South: Balance portion of same land, Delgahalanda claimed by W. G.
Dayawathie and Lot B; West: Lot B and Lot Nos. 7 and 8 in
Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Delgahalanda – Extent: About 0.0032 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 7 and 8 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894 and
Lot A; East: Lot A, Delgahawatta claimed by M. M. Sirisena and
balance portion of same land; South: Balance portion of same land,
Delgahalanda claimed by Neisamy Promo and Lot A; West: Lot C
and Lot Nos. 8 and 9 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Delgahalanda – Extent: About 0.0015 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 8 and 9 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894 and
Lot B; East: Lot B, Delgahalanda claimed by W. G. Dayawathie,
balance portion of same land, Delgahalanda claimed by A. E. M.
Saroja and Lot D; South: Delgahalanda claimed by A. E. M. Saroja,
Lot D and Lot No. 10 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894; West:
Lot No. 9 in Preliminary Plan No. 3894.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Delgahalanda – Extent: About 0.0043 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 9 and 10 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894 and
Lot C; East: Delgahalanda claimed by Neisamy Promo and balance
portion of same land; South: Balance portion of same land,
Delgahalanda claimed by C. M. M. Sarook and Lot E; West: Lot E
and Lot Nos. 10 and 11 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Delgahalanda – Extent: About 0.0011 Hectare
North: Lot Nos. 10 and 11 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894
and Lot D; East: Lot D, Delgahalanda claimed by A. E. M. Saroja and
balance portion of same land; South: Balance portion of same land;
West: Lot No. 11 in Preliminary Plan No. GAM/3894.
