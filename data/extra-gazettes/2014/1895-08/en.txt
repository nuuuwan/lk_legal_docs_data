THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. :    RDA/LA/COL/2013/06.
Land Ministry No. : 4/3/6/2013/HW/503.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 - 03 (Lots 03) allotments in total extent of about
0.0635 Hectare of  land in Primarary Plan No. Co: 9312 and dated
04.11.2014 Prepared by the Surveyor-Superintendent on behalf of
Surveyor - Colombo, General situated in the Village of Kelanimulla
in the Gramaniladhari Division Kelanimulla, Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Kolonnawa in Colombo District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 24
Name of land: Lansiya Kumbura (A part); Description: Marshy
Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Thero Kalugalle
Dhammarathana, Sri Vijayarathnaramaya, Kelanimulla; Extent:
0.0156 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 26
Name of land: Godaovita and Puwakgahawatta (A part);
Description: Marshy Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
L. R. D. Shiya Janaki, L. R. D. Vimalawathi, L. R. D. V . Padmini and
L. R. D. A. Rathnasiri, No. 135, Kelanimulla, Angoda; Extent: 0.0203
Hectare.
03. Lot No. 27
Name of land: Dombagaha watta, Thembili kotuwa and Godaovita
(A part); Description: Marshy Land; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: W . H. Perera, M. C. Perera, W. D. Perera and M. H.
Perera, No. 426, Kelanimulla, Angoda; Extent: 0.0276 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required to
appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Land Division, Road Development Authority, 3
rd floor,
Sethsiripaya on 12th January, 2015 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
31st day of December 2014, the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of
the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
