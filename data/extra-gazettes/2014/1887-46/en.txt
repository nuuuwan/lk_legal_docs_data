THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land depicted as Lot  A in Advance Tracing No.
KA/BRW/09/394  dated  09. 09.2009,  prepared by Senior Surveyor
General of Kalutara situated in the Village of  Hirigalgodalla,  in  the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Beruwala, in the  Kalutara  District,
Western  Province  and containing   in extent of about 3.506 Hectares,
and bounded as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’  – Digathurewatta  – Extent: 3.506 Hectares
North: Mahawelyaya claimant not known and Road; East:
Mahawelyaya claimant not known and Silwabelwatta claimed by  A.
I. C. Fernando; South: Silwabelwatta claimed by A. I. C. Fernando
and Mahawelyaya claimant not known; West: Mahawelyaya claimant
not known.
         Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Reference No.:LD/05/2008/VE/223.
