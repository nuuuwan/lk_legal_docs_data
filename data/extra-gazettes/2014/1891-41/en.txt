No. 1891/41 – FRIDAY, DECEMBER 05, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land, called following names and extents, depicted
in the Surveyor-General’s Advance Tracing No. Ma/MTL/2014/176
dated 09.07.2014, situated in the Villages of Thotagamuwa and
Aluvihare (Ward No. 01 Aluvihare within Municipal Council limit of
Matale) in the Minor Division of Gampahasiya Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Matale in Matale District, Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thotagamuwa Estate – Extent: 0.0431 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of this land and Road (R.D.A.); East:
Road (R.D.A.); South: Lot B and Dorakumbura Temple land claimed
by V en. Dunukewatte Gunarathana Thero; West: Remaining portion
of this land.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Dorakumbura Temple Land – Extent:
0.0151 Hectare
North: Thotagamuwa Estate claimed by E. M. M. Lal
Ekanayake and Lot A; East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.)
and Remaining portion of this land; West: Remaining portion of
this land.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Assmt. No. 1078, Trincomalee Street,
Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta – Extent:
0.0018 Hectare
North: Trincomalee Street and Remaining portion of this land;
East: Remaining portion of this land; South: Drainage; West:
Trincomalee Street.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Assmt. No. 1072, Trincomalee Street,
Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta – Extent:
0.0216 Hectare
North: Drainage; East: Remaining portion of this land; South:
Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta claimed by C. M.
K. Chandrarathne and Lot E; West: Trincomalee Street.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Assmt. No. 1072A, Trincomalee Street,
Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta – Extent:
0.0215 Hectare
North: Lot D and Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye
Watta claimed by M. C. Jayasinghe; East: Remaining portion of this
land; South: Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta claimed
by W. M. N. P. Senevirathna Banda and W. M. P . Senevirathna
Banda and Lot F; West: Trincomalee Street.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Assmt. No. 1070, Trincomalee Street,
Muththettuyaye Watta and Galedandayaye Watta – Extent:
