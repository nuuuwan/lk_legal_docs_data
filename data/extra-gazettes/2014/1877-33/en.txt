THE   STATE   INDUSTRIAL  CORPORATION  ACT,  No. 49  OF 1957 OF  CHAPTER  208
Order under Section 25
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me, by Section 25 of the State Industrial Act, No. 49 of 1957 of chapter 208, I, Nambukara
Helambage Rajitha Harischandra Senaratne, Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development, do hereby, with the
concurrences of the Minister of Finance and Planning and the Minister of Land and Land Development, transfer to the
Ceylon Fisheries Corporation, which was established by Incorporation Order made under Section 2 of the aforesaid Act and
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 14,186 of October 1st, 1964, the immovable property of the crown specified in the
Schedule hereto.
N
AMBUKARA HELAMBAGE RAJITHA HARISCHANDRA SENARATNE,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resource Development.
Colombo,
27th August, 2014.
