THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Subsection (1) of Section 27
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of Section 27 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, on the recommendation of
the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend  the Order made   under Section 21 of the said Act, in relation to the
Raja Rata University of Sri Lanka,  published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 896/2  of   November 7, 1995, as last amended by
Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1658/36 of  June 17, 2010 in the Schedule thereto by the substitution for the item
“Faculty of Agriculture”  appearing in Column I under the  heading  “Faculty” and its corresponding entries appearing in
column II under the heading “Department” of the following :—
“COLUMN  I COLUMN  II
F
ACULTY DEPARTMENT
Faculty of Agriculture 1. Department of Animal and Food Sciences
2. Department of Agricultural Engineering and Soil Science
3. Department of Agricultural Systems
4. Department of  Plant Sciences”.

ISSANAYAKE  MUDIY ANSELAGE  SUMANAWEERA  BANDA  DISSANAY AKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
