EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
RAJGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in  Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a
member of the Southern Province Provincial Council ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party, to which
Mr. Ariyawansha Sambu  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of  the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   decide upon the member of  that Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared  elected
as  Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  communicated   his decision to
nominate Mr. Nimal Thommadura, to fill such vacancy  ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning  Officer of  Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha,  acting in
terms of  Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of  Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Nimal Thommadura elected as  Vice Chairman of
the  Rajgama  Pradeshiya   Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SILV A,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
24th July, 2014.

PRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER OF  THE
MULATIYANA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT No. 30 of 2013
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Mulatiyana  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased  to be a
member of the party;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United National Party,  to which
the member Arachchige Munasingha Ranjith belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,
to   nominate within a period of  thirty  (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of  that
Mulatiyana  Pradeshiya  Sabha  who shall be declared  elected as  a  member ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party  has  nominated   Mr. Visenthige Pujitha,
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy  ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Mr. Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning  Officer of  Mulatiyana  Pradeshiya
Sabha,  acting in  terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of  V acancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare  Mr. Visenthige Pujitha elected as a
member  of  the  Mulatiyana Pradeshiya   Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAY A KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Mulatiyana Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matara,
24th July, 2014.
