FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
SIYAMBALANDUW A   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased of the
membership ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the former Member, Mr. Chandrasena Rathnayaka belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Arachchilage Gedara
Kapila Gunasekara being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of   Siyambalanduwa  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Arachchilage Gedara Kapila Gunasekara  elected
as a member of the Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Siyambalanduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Moneragala,
30th  August,  2014.
