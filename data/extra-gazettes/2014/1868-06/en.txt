FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
VERUGAL   PRADESHIYA  SABHA  UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)  AS
AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,
No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in  Verugal  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the  recognized political party to which Chandrapala
Vijayakan, the  Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the
member of that  V erugal Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as  Chairman within a
period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has  Communicated  his decision  to
nominate Mr. Chandrapala Vijayakan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Nalaka Rathnayake, Returning Officer of  Verugal  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of  V acancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Chandrapala Vijayakan, elected as Chairman of the
Verugal Pradeshiya Sabha.
N
ALAKA RATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
 Verugal  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Trincomalee,
20th June,  2014.
