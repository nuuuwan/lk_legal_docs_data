NOTICE    UNDER   SECTION  3(1)  OF   THE    REGISTRATION   OF    ELECTORS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)
  ACT,  No. 27  OF  2013
Supplementary list to insert the names of Sri Lankan citizens whose names have not been registered as electors
elsewhere in 2013 and who were internally displaced as result of actions of militant groups in Northern/Eastern
Province
ELECTORAL    DISTRICT    No. 10 - JAFFNA
IN terms of the Section 3(1) of the Registration of Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 27 of 2013, it is hereby notified that
the preparation of the supplementary list for displaced persons relevant to the afore mentioned electoral district has been
completed and it is now open for inspection by the general public during office hours at the District Elections Office Jaffna/
Kilinochchi
02. Any person who is eligible to be registered in the supplementary list of afore mentioned electoral district but
whose name does not appear in the list may appeal to the Registering Officer using the form set out in the afore
mentioned Registration of Elections Act,  in order to enter or retain his/her name in the register. Every application
regarding this matter should be submitted to the Registering Officer of District Elections Office, Jaffna/Killinochchi
within one week from the date on which this notice is published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka.
03. Any person whose name is included into the supplementary list of the afore mentioned electoral district, could
object to retain his/her own name or the name of any other person. These objections shall be in duplicate
substantially on Form “D” set out in the afore mentioned Act and be submitted to the Registering Officer of the
Elections Office, Jaffna/Kilinochchi every objection relating to the entering of a name in the register should be
submitted to the Registering Officer of District Elections Office, Jaffna/Kilinochchi within the one week from the
date on which this notice is published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
This notice will be exhibited at the Elections Office, Jaffna/Kilinochchi from 08th November 2014 to 14th November
2014 (including the said date as well)
