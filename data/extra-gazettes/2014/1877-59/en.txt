DECLARATION OF HAKMANA KABILIYAPOLA PURANA RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND
NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Hakmana Kabiliyapola Purana Maha Vihara sacred area", for its long-
term security.
 D
R. D. M. JAY ARATNE,
 Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF HAKMANA KABILIY APOLA PURANA MAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 2.7968 Hectares
situated in No.14 A Kambaliyapola grama Niladhari Division in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Hakmana of the
Administrative District of Matara in Southern Province depicted as Lot No.A in plan No.MR/HKM/2011/87 dated 12
th May
2011 of Surveyor General.
