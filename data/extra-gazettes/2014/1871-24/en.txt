THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  02  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980. I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the
subject of Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required for the
Department of  Police, the purpose of established the Police Station at Eheliyagoda under the Eheliyagoda Town  Development
Project do by this order, declare that the said land is required for the such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
7th July 2014.
SCHEDULE
An allotment of land depicted as lot 3 in FVP No. 691 dated 17.01.1950 prepared by Surveyor General containing in
extent of 0.1316 Ha. (00A.3R.12.00P) situated at the village of Eheliyagoda, Kandagamuwa Town, limit of Eheliyagoda
Pradeshiya Sabha within the Divisional Secretariat Division of Eheliyagoda in the District of Ratnapura, Sabaragamuwa
Province.
Boundaries of said allotment are as follows :
Part of Lot 3 Land called Gallindagewatta, Asst. No.2, 2A and 3
containing in extent of 0.1316 Ha. (00A.3R.12.00P)
North : Part of Kandagamuwa Ehelagama in Plan No. FVP 674 and Lot No. 2 ;
East : Part of Kandagamuwa Ehelagama in Plan No. FVP 674 and Lot No. 9 ;
South : Colombo Ratnapura Road, Lot No. 146 and No. 9 ;
West : Colombo Ratnapura Road, Lot No. 2.
