SOUTHERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Governor of the Southern Province
I, hereby announce that the following appointments have been approved by Hon. Neranjala Pushpa Kumari Balasooriya the
Governor of the Southern Province.
1. Mrs. W. K. K. Athukorala in special Grade of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service in addition to the post of Chief
Secretary of the Southern Province, as secretary to the subjects of finance and planning, engineering services coming under
the purview of Hon. Chief Minister of the Southern Province with effect from 04th April 2014.
2. Mr. Saman Darshana Pandikorala in Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry
of Law and Order, Local Government, Transport, Health and Indigenous Medicine Tourism of the Southern Provincial
Council with effect form 04th April 2014.
3. Mr. Wickramasiri  in Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Secretary to the Ministry of
Education, Lands and Land Development, Provincial Roads and Information  of Southern Provincial Council with effect form
04th April 2014.
4. Mr. Sumith Alahakoon  in Class I of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service as Acting Secretary to the Ministry of
Agriculture, Agrarain Development, Irrigation, Water Supply and Drainage, Food Supply and Distribution and Co-operative
Development of  the Southern Provincial Council with effect form 04th April 2014.
5. Mrs. D. S. Padmakulasuriya   in Special Grade of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service as  Secretary to the Ministry
of Fisheries, Animal Production and Development, Environment, Rural Industries, Electricity and Rural and Estate Infrastructure
facilities Development of  the Southern Provincial Council with effect form 04th April 2014.
6. Mr. Chinthake S. Lokuhetti   in Special Grade of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service as  Secretary to the Ministry
of Sports and Youth Affairs, Cultural and Art Affairs, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare Services, Women Affairs, and
Home Economic promotion, Housing and Construction and Man Power and Employment  of  the Southern Provincial Council
with effect form 07th April 2014.
