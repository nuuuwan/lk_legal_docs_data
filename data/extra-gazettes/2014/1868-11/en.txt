FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER
OF THE  CHA V AKACHCHERI    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending
meetings of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  to which the
member  Siriskantharasa Siriranjan  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Kugathasan Thanusan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kugathasan Thanusan elected as a member of
the Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
18th  June, 2014.
