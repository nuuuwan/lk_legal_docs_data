EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act,  No. 2 of
1980,  I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of  Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka  and  the Minister - in - charge of  the
subject of  Defence and Urban Development, being of  opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is  urgently
required  to public and religious activities do by this order, declare that the said lands are required for such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA.
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo ,
28th  July  2014.
SCHEDULE
One (01) allotment of  land depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No. 315 A dated 17.09.1992 prepared by Mr. P. C. Fernandopulle,
Licensed Surveyor and Leveller containing in extent of 00A. 1R. 18.12 P.  situated in Bloemendhal within the Municipal Council
limits of Colombo in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Colombo, in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
Boundaries of the said allotment of land is as follows :-
North - Access road from K. Cyril  C. Perera Mawatha;
East - Assmt. No. 478/ 6 A,  K. Cyril  C. Perera Mawatha;
South - Reservation for canal ;
West - The land belongs to Colombo Municipal Council.
