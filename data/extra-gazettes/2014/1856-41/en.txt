EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF A  MEMBER OF  THE  KATTANKUDY URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262)
AS  AMENDED BY LOCAL   AUTHORITIES.  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Member  in Kattankudy Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
And  whereas the Group Leader of the Independent Group No 1,  to which  Muhammathanifa Abdul Naseer,  the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate a person eligible under
the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kattankudy Urban Council  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as a member  within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Leader of the aforesaid  group  has nominated  Mr. Sahul Hameethu Firthous being an eligible
person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I, Abdul Majeedu Mahammadu Kabeer, Returning Officer of  Kattankudy Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of V acancies in Local. authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sahul Hameethu Firthous elected as member of the
Kattankudy Urban Council.
A. M. M. K
ABEER,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
21st March,  2014.
