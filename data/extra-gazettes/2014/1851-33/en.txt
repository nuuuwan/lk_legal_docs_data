NOTIFICATION
Appointment of a Chairman  and  Members of the Board for the approval of Credit Agencies
under the  Mortgage Act, No. 06 of 1949
It is hereby notified that the Hon. Minister of Industry and Commerce has by virtue of the powers vested in him, under
Section 114(1) of the Mortgage Act, No. 06 of 1949, has appointed Mr. R. Semasinghe to be the Chairman and Mr. Anura V .
Meddagoda and Mr. V . M. Weerasinghe to be the other members of the Board constituted for the purpose of the above Act,
for a period of 03 years effective from 13
th January 2014 to 12th January 2017.
It is further notified that the previous Board constituted in terms of Sri Lanka Government Gazette No. 1,602 of
15.05.2009 ceased to be in operation.
ANURA SIRIWARDENA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of Industry and Commerce,
Colombo 03,
06
th February 2014.
