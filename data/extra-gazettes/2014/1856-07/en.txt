EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
              My Reference No.: KDS/KUN/04/07/16/04/41. 
                       Lands and Land Development Ministry  
                           Reference   No.: 4/3/15/2012/VE/188. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land which is required for 
a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotement of land called "Kingstonwatta" in total 
extent of about 1.0488 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 01 
in the Preliminary Plan No. 6332, dated 19.02.2014, 
surveyed and prepared by the surveyor General, Situated 
in the Village of Gonawala in Udagampaha Korale minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretarys' Division of 
Kundasale in Kandy District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 1  
 
Name of Land: Kingstonwata; Extent: 1.0488 
Hectares. 
Boundaries 
 
North: Road (Pradesheey a Sabha) Kingstonwatta 
claimed by A. G. Sumanadasa and Kingstonwatta claimed 
by Gamini Senevirathne; East: Kingstonwatta claimed by 
Gamini Senevirathne and Kingtonwatta claimed by 
Senavirathne; South: Kingstonwatta claimed by 
Senevirathne and Road (Pradeshiya Sabaha ; West: Road 
(Pradesheeya Sabha) and Kingstonwatta claimed by A. G. 
Sumanadasa. 
 
Claimant and Address of the Claimant: N. D. S. 
Weerakkodi, No. 249/C, Maha Vihara Rd., Molligoda, 
Wadduwa. 
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands are  
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary's Office  at Kundasa le on the 19th day of May 
2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 12th day of May 
2014, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of the 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 L. A. D. G EETHA INDRANI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kundasale. 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Kundasale. 
19th March, 2014.
