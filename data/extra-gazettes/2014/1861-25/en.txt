Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Reference. No.: ෙකො/පාෙල්/ඉඅත්/3/75/UDA.  
I, hereby give  notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28  of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is  required for a Public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land / servitude  acquired :-  
 
 
Six (06) Allotments  of land depicted as Lot Nos. 1 to 
6 in extent of 1.1372 Ha. in P P Co. 9401 dated 
06.12.2013 prepared by Surveyor General situated in the 
village of Suduwella (Ward No. 24-Suduwella in the 
Municipal Council limits of Colombo) within Colombo 
Divisional Secretary's Divi sion in the District of 
Colombo, Western Province. 
 
 Lot No. 01  
 
Name of the land: Not known; Description: Garden 
and filled land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not 
known; Land  Extent: 0.4816 Hectare. 
 
 Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the land: Part of land bearing Asst. No. 320, 
D. R. Wijewardena Mawatha; Description: Garden; 
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not known; Land  
Extent: 0.0030 Hectare. 
 
 Lot No. 03

 Lot No. 04

 Lot No. 05

 Lot No. 06

All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Colombo 
Divisional Secretariat on the 17th day of June 2014 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of June, 
2014, the nature of their interests in the Land/Lands, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.
