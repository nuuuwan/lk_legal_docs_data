Provincial  Councils  Notifications
 UVA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
The Notification made under the Subsection 2(3)(b) of the Charter No. 02 of 2011 on power of
supervision and administration of Local Government of Uva Province Provincial Council
THE Notification published in the paragraph 03 of the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1793/6 - Wednesday January 16, 2013 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, suspending the functions of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District do
hereby repealed.
02. I, Shasheendra Rajapaksa,  the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion and Rural Infrastructure
Development and Construction, under the power vested in me, and under the power vested in the subsection 2(3)(b) of
Charter No. 02 of  2011 on the power of supervision and administration of the Local Government Institutions of Uva Province
Provincial Council, re-establish the Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha with effect from 16th June 2014.
03. Moreover the appointment of Mr. Rathnayake Mudiyanselage Jayawardene, Assistant Commissioner of Local
Government in Badulla District, in order to execute the powers and carry out the task of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha, made
through the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1822/21 - Wednesday August 07, 2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, is hereby repealed with effect from the 16th June 2014.
S
HASHEENDRA RAJAPAKSA,
The Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance
and Planning, Law and Order, Education, Local Government,
Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land, Irrigation, Economic Promotion
and Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction.
