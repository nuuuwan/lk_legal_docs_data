THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land  Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Five (05) allotments of below named lands depicted in Advance
Tracing No: GAM/DIVU/2013/361 and 31.10.2013, prepared by
the Surveyor General, situated in the Villages of  Diyagampola and
Koradaminna in the Minor Division of Hapitigama Korale in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Divulapitiya, in Gampaha District
in Western Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Diyagampolawatta – Extent: 0.0494 Hectare
North: Balance portion of same land, Canal and Lot F , (Canal);
East: Lot F (Canal), Lot E and Road (R.D.A); South: Lot E and Road
(R.D.A); West: Road (R.D.A) and Balance portion of same land.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Maussawawatta – Extent: 0.0646 Hectare
North: Balance portion of same land; East: Lot D and Road
(R.D.A); South: Lot D, Road (R.D.A) and Lot G (Canal); West: Lot
G (Canal), Canal and Balance portion of same land.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Maussawawatta – Extent: 0.0046 Hectare
North: Lot D, Road (R.D.A) and Balance portion of same land;
East: Balance portion of same land; South: Balance portion of same
land, Lot D and Road (R.D.A); West: Lot D and Road (R.D.A).
