THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER  203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport under Section 237 of the motor traffic act (Chapter 203) read with Section
128A  of the aforesaid Act,
KUMARA WELGAMA,
Minister of Transport.
Colombo,
23rd January, 2014.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as "Motor Traffic" (Public Service Driving Licence) Regulations No. 03 of the 2014
and shall come into force on 01.01.2015.
2. No holder of a licence for any class or classes of vehicles shall drive a motor coach as a public service vehicle for
carriage of passengers unless he is authorized in that behalf by a special endorsement by the Commissioner General under
Section 128A of the Act.
3. Any holder of a licence for any class or classes of vehicles who intends to drive any public service vehicle for
carriage of passengers shall make an application to the Commissioner General in the form as set out in the Schedule to these
regulation together with the payment of Rupees Two Thousand (Rs. 2000/-) as a levy for the issue of special endorsement by
the Commissioner General.
4. The following requirements and the qualifications shall by fulfilled by an applicant to obtain a special endorsement
to drive a motor coach as a public service vehicle and the applicant shall prove to the satisfaction of the Commissioner
General that he :-
(i) is a person of age between 23 and 65 years (both inclusive) ;
(ii) has obtained two years driving experience after obtained a regular driving license for a motor coach ;

"Act" means the Motor Traffic Act (Chapter 203) ;
"Commissioner General" means the Commissioner General of Motor Traffic appointed under the Act; and
"Public service vehicle" means any motor vehicle used or adapted to be used or kept for the carriage of
passengers for a fee or reward.
