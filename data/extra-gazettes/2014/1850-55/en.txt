EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   VICE   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
KATUWANA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(1)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262) AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   (SPECIAL
PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of a Vice Chairman in Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party  to which
Imbulahitiya Gamachchi Mahinda  the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide  upon the member of that Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such party  who shall be declared
elected as  a Vice Chairman within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Malavigey Jayantha to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathne,  Returning Officer of  Katuwana Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Malavigey Jayantha  elected as
Vice Chairman of the Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Katuwana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Hambantota,
10th  February, 2014.

PRINTED  AT  THE  DEPARTMENT  OF  GOVERNMENT  PRINTING,  SRI  LANKA.
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER   OF  THE
NAULA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA   UNDER    SECTION   65A(2)    OF   THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES
ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)  AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   (SPECIAL
PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in Naula Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of  the United National Party, the recognised political party  to which Disanayaka
Mudiyanselage Amarasooriya  the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Naula Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs to  such
party  who shall be declared  elected as  a member within a period of  thirty  (30) days;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognised political party  has nominated Mr. Makevitage Lionel Perera
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy  ;
Now therefore, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe,  Returning Officer of  Naula Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Makevitage Lionel Perera  elected as
a member of the Naula Pradeshiya Sabha.
S. D. S. A. R
UPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Naula Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matale,
07th  February, 2014.
