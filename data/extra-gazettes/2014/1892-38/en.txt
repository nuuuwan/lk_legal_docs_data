FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
LUNUGALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Lunugala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom   Alliance to which
the former Member, Arumuham Ganeshamoorthy  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within  a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Lunugala  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Manamendra Acharige
Palitha Ruwan Kithsiri Manamendra  being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of  Lunugala
Pradeshiya Sabha  acting in terms of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Manamendra
Acharige Palitha Ruwan Kithsiri Manamendra  elected as a member of the Lunugala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Lunugala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
09th December,  2014.
