THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LA W  No. 41 OF  1978  OF   THE  NATIONAL   STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Mahinda
Rajapaksa, Minister of Defence and Urban Development do by this order vest with the Urban Development Authority, with
effect from today the land described in the Schedule hereto belongs to the Galle Municipal Council.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
Minister of Defence and Urban Development.
Ministry of Defence and Urban Development,
No. 15/5, Baladaksha Mawatha,
Colombo - 03,
14th October 2014.

The land called bearing Asst. No. 5/1, Colombo Road containing extent of 0.0907 Ha. (0A., 0R. 35.8P.) depicted as Lot
No. 2  in Plan No. 2413 dated 10.12.2013 made by Mr. P . M. Sunil, Registered Licensed Surveyor situated at village of Kaluwella
in Divisional Secretariat Division of Galle Four Gravets within the Municipal Council Limits of Galle in District of Galle,
Southern Province.
The land bounded as follows :
North - Park ;
East - Road and Sea ;
South : Sea ;
West : Lot 1 herein.
