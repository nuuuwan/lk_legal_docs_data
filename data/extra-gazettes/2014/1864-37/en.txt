EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND  ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT)  
ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/60. 
 
Land Ministry's Reference No.: 4-3/15/2013/VE/153. 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section             
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 0f 1964,  that the 
Government intends to acquire following land servitude 
which is required for village widening. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land described in bellow in Plan No. Ma 
1618 Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of 
Surveys, situated in Owala village, Ikirigolla 
Gramaniladari Division in sub Division of Matale 
Pallesiya Pattuwa, in the Rattota Divisional Secretariat 
Division, Matale District, in Central Province. 
 
Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the land:  Wiharagamawatta; Description: 
Garden; Claiments: Wariyapola Group, Wariyapola, 
Matale; Extent: 4.0469 Hectares 
 
All the persons interested in the aforesaid land are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Rattota on the 27th day of August, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby required to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on on or before the 18th day of August 2014, 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensatio n for the requisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation, and details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 E. W. N. A.  EGODAWELA, 
  Divisional Secretary ( Acting), 
 Rattota. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Rattota.
