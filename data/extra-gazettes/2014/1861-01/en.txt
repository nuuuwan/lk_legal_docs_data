CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235),
I, Alisandaralage Senanayake, Acting Director General of  Customs, determine that with effect  from 05th May 2014, all  duties
of  Customs as well as other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the  Customs  Ordinance, (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1860/1 of
28th April, 2014  is hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 11th May, 2014.
A. S
ENANAY AKE,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
2nd May, 2014.
