FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF THE  MATARA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely People's Liberation Front to which the member
Mr. Damith Aththanayake  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within
a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Matara Pradeshiya Sabha
who shall be declared elected as a member within a period of thirty (30) days.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. M. Amal Prasanga being
an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Mr. Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. M. Amal Prasanga elected as a
Member of the Matara Pradeshiya Sabha.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Matara  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Matara,
7th April, 2014.

NOTICE  UNDER   SECTION  10A (1) (a)  OF  THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera, Returning Officer of the Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare
under Section 10A (1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  (Chapter 262) that Mr. Pechchimuththu Rajendran of
the Kolonna Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a
member of the United National Party.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Kolonna  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
