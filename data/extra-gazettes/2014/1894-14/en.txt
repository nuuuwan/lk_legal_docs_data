No. 1894/14 – MONDAY, DECEMBER 22, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of lands in extent of 0.0513 Hectare, depicted in
Advance Tracing No. KU/KTP/2009/30 dated 31.05.2010, prepared
by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Magulagama in
the Minor Division of Yatikaha Korala North in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Panduwasnuwara East in the District of
Kurunegala in North Western Province and bounded as follows:
1. Lot ‘A’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North and East: Mahawatta Kumbura claimed by L. E. M.
Jayathilaka; South: Mahawatta Kumbura claimed by L. E. M.
Jayathilaka and Lot C; West: Road.
2. Lot ‘B’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0010 Hectare
North and East: Road; South and West: Mahawatta claimed by
B. M. Ratnayaka.
3. Lot ‘C’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0028 Hectare
North: Lot A and Mahawatta Kumbura claimed by L. E. M.
Jayathilaka; East: Mahawatta and Mahawatta Kumbura claimed by
L. E. M. Jayathilaka; South and West: Road.
4. Lot ‘D’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0020 Hectare
North: Mahawatta claimed by L. E. M. Menikhami; East and
South: Road; West: Mahawatta claimed by C. L. E. M. Anulawathi.
5. Lot ‘E’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0007 Hectare
North: Road; East: Lot G and Mahawatta claimed by W. B. H. S.
K. Mendis and others; South: Mahawatta claimed by S. M. C. K.
Bandara; West: Mahawatta claimed by B. M. Ratnayaka.
6. Lot ‘F’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0145 Hectare
North: Mahawatta claimed by S. M. Wijekoon Bandara and others;
East: Mahawatta claimed by L. E. M. Menikhami and Lot J; South
and West: Road.
7. Lot ‘G’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0029 Hectare
North: Road; East: Lot H and Mahawatta claimed by S. M. C. K.
Bandara and others; South: Mahawatta claimed by W. B. H. S. K.
Mendis and others; West: Mahawatta claimed by S. M. C. K. Bandara
and Lot E.
8. Lot ‘H’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0200 Hectare
North: Road; East: Road of Pradeshiya Sabha and Mahawatta
claimed by S. M. C. K. Bandara and others; South: Mahawatta
claimed by S. M. C. K. Bandara and others; West: Mahawatta claimed
by W. B. H. S. K. Mendis and others and Lot G .
9. Lot ‘J’ – Mahawatta – Extent: 0.0067 Hectare
North: Mahawatta claimed by L. E. M. Menikhami; East: Road of
Pradeshiya Sabha and Road; South: Road; West: Lot F and Mahawatta
claimed by S. M. Wijekoon Bandara and others.
