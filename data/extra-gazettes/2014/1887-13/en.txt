No. 1887/13 – TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 04, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
the construction of the new bridge near bridge No. 29/8 on
Thiriwanaketiya-Agalawatta Road and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land described as Lot ‘C’ in total extent of
about 0.0215 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
R/SS/NVT/2013/516 and dated 17th June 2014, prepared by the
Surveyor-General  situated in the Village of Wewelkandura
(F.V .P. 245) Meda Pattuwa Minor Division, in Kukulu Korale  in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kalawana in Ratnapura District
in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘C’ – Ellagawa Kumbura – Extent: About
0.0215 Hectare
North and East: Balance portion of the same land; South and
West: Road (R.D.A.).
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
