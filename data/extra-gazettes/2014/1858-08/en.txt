EXTRAORDINARY
1A
Annual Higher Ordination of Rohana Sanga Sabhawa
IT is hereby informed that the 29th Higher Ordination Ceremony of the Sri Rohana Maha Sangha Sabhawa is to be held on 25th,
26th, 27th of April, 2014 at Sri Rohana Sangha Sabhawa of Siri Rathanajothi Piriwena, Urapola, Thihagoda and Mangala
Seemamalakaya of Randupatha Sri Rohana Uposathagara Maha Vihara, Kotuwegoda , Matara and that the pilgrims should
refrain from carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials and that the standing orders and regulations under Section
02 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175 - LDB 11/60) declared on 14th October 1991 and published in the Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, will be in force during the period in which the ceremony is held.
R
ANJITH YAPA.
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
11th April, 2014,
at Divisional Secretariat,
Matara.
