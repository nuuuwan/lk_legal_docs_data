 FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY UNDER PARAGRAPH 13 (b) OF  ARTICLE 99 OF THE CONSTITUTION
OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary - General of  Parliament has informed  me,  under Section 64(1) of the  Parliamentary  Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 - of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Seventh Parliament
by reason of resignation of Mr. Harin Fernando, who was declared elected as a Member of  Parliament for the Electoral District
No. 19 - Badulla under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act ;
And  whereas the Returning officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by me under
Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as  provided for under paragraph 13 (b) of  Article 99 of  the
Constitution ;
And  whereas the  said Returning officer, acting under Section 64 (2) of  the aforesaid Act,  has made a return in Form
''M" set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Karuppaiah Velayudam was declared elected as a
Member of  Parliament for the said electoral district ;
Now therefore,  I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of  the powers vested in me by
Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify  the general public that Mr. Karuppaiah Velayudam has been declared
elected as a member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District No.19 - Badulla to fill the vacancy caused by reason of
resignation of Mr. Harin Fernando.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections  Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
5th August, 2014.
