NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A(1)(A)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Nuwara   Eliya   Pradeshiya    Sabha
I,  Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando returning officer of the Nuwara Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Rajaratnam Ravikumar of
the Nuwara Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a
member of the Up-Country People's Front.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara Eliya,
11th  November, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
PANADURA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of failing to make and subscribe
an oath or affirmation in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance  to which
the former member, Mohan Lal Gunaransi  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Panadura
Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;

AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Hithanadura Sujith
Priyanga Silva  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Hithanadura Sujith Priyanga Silva elected as a
member of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
