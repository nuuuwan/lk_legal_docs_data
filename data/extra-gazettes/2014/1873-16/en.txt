Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of Medicine (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 2 of 2014 and
shall come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of Medicine Ordinance, No. 1 of 1980, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 83/7 of
April 10, 1980 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1317/17 of December 3, 2003 is hereby
further amended as follows :
(1) by the repeal of paragraph 6, and the substitution therefor of the following :-
“6 The officers of the Institute shall be the following :-
(a) the Director ;
(b) the Deputy Director ; and
(c) the holder of any other post declared by the Ordinance to be a post, the holder of which is an officer for the
purpose of this paragraph.”.
(2) in paragraph 7 thereof —
(i) by the repeal of subparagraphs (6) and (7) of that paragraph and the substitution therefor of the
following :-
“(6) If the Director, by reason of leave, illness, absence from Sri Lanka, or other cause is temporarily unable
to perform the duties of his office, the Deputy Director shall perform such duties. In the absence of both the
Director and the Deputy Director the Commission shall, within seven days of the occurrence of such
inability make such arrangements as it may think fit for carrying on the duties of the office of the Director.
Until such arrangements are made the Registrar of the University shall carry on the routine duties of the
office.
