NOTICE No : 41/0034 Puththalam District  
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadast ral Map No. 410009 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Dummaladeniya 
(Block 01) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Nilad-
hari Division of 489A - Dummaladeniya Central in the Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Wennappuwa in the District of Puththalam 
in the Province of North West should submit his claim to the 
undersigned before 11th June, 2014 In the event of failure to sub-
mit any claim before the above-mentioned date, action will be 
taken to hold an exparte inquiry to  determine title to the land and 
accordingly publish such determination in the Government Ga-
zette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 410009 refe rred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals’ office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
