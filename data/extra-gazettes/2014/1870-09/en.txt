No. 1870/9 –  MONDAY, JULY  07, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT,   (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
 
My Ref. No.: MIF/L/Acq/346. 
 
I, hereby give notice, that the Government proposes to 
acquire the following land for a public purpose under 
paragraph 7, Sub paragraph (1)  of Acquisition of Lands  
Act, No. 28  of 1964 (as amended.) 
 
Details of the proposed land :-  
 
Land in extent of  0.4241 hectare within the District of 
Colombo, Western Province, Maharagama  Provincial 
Secretary’s Region (within Maharagama  Provincial 
Council area) as shown in Plan No. 9457 prepared by 
Colombo District Director of Survey. 
 
Description of the Land as follows: 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Hemawimana Dawatagahalanda alias 
Dawatagahawatta; Land Usage: Garden, 4 permanent 
Buildings, well, Water tank; Owned by: Government; 
Original Ownership: P. Upali Weerawardena, No.113, 
Hemawimana Soratha Mawatha, Gangodawila, 
Nugegoda; Extent: 0.4241 Hectare. 
 
Parties claiming ownership or related parties 
individually or with letters of  authority are requested to 
meet me at 9.00 am on 04th August, 2014 at 
Maharagama, Provincial Secretariat. 
 
Further a document consisting of the nature of 
ownership and compensation for the land, how the 
compensation was worked out should be submitted to in 
duplicate before 21st July, 2014. 
 
Document and deeds  confirming ownership with 
extracts and other documents should be produced to me at 
the meeting. 
 
 B UDDI THARANGA KARUNASENA, 
 Provincial Secretary, 
 Maharagama. 
 
Provincial Secretariat, 
Maharagama. 
07th July, 2014.
