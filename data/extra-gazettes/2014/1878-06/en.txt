THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 7
                                                     My Reference No.: 3/2/3/10/253.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01-07 (Lots 07) allotments in extent of about 0.4221
Hectare, of land in Preliminery Plan No. Gam. 3891  and dated
19.02.2013 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village
of Nambadaluwa and Grama Niladhari Divisions of No. 348/H -
Horagollagama, No. 348/G - Nambadaluwa East and No. 348/E
Malwatta and Minor Division of Medapattuwa in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Attanagalla in Gampaha District in the Western
Province.
01. Lot No. 51
Name of Land: Horagollawatta; Description: Garden, contains
part of a permanent building; Claimant: Bandaranayake Foundation,
Kandy Road, Horagolla, Nittambuwa; Original Claimant: -; Extent:
0.0813 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 54
Name of Land: Horagollawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: Anura Bandaranayake, Kandy Road,
Horagolla, Nittambuwa; Extent: 0.0032 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 55

Name of Land: Horagollawatta; Description: Garden; Claimant:

05. Lot No. 225

06. Lot No. 226

Horagolla, Nittambuwa; Extent: 0.0021 Hectare.
07. Lot No. 228
Name of Land: Assmt. No. 251/B, Colombo Road; Description:

All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which  a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally,
or by agents duly authorised in writing, before me at 
Divisional
Secretariat, Attanagalla of the 12th September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing [in duplicate]
on or before the 05th September, 2014, the nature of their interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
