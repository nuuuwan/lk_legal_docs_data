EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Subsection (i) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that Government has intended 
to acquire the servitude of land given below for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of land to be acquired : 
 
The servitude of land called Irundathottam in extent of 
0.0031 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in P.P. No. P 4952 
prepared by and kept under custody of Superintendent of 
Surveys-Puttalam, situated at the Village of 
Munneswaram in the Munneswaram Pattu in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Chilaw in Puttalam District in the 
North Western Province, to be acquired. 
 
All the persons interested in the aforesaid land to be 
acquired, are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Chilaw 
Divisional Secretariat on 20th August, 2014 at 10.30 a.m. 
 
Further, it is requested to notify to me in writing in 
duplicate before 20th August, 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation, and details of the computation 
of such amount. 
 
 U. S. N. F ERNANDO, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Chilaw. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Chilaw. 
26th May, 2014.

LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 07 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Sub-section (i) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act that Government has 
intended to acquire land given below for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of Land to be Acquired : 
 
The land of Pushparama alias Viharawatte in extent of 
0.0127 Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 in P.P. No. P 4961 
prepared by and kept under custody of Superintendent of 
Surveys-Puttalam, situated at the Village of 
Munneswaram in the Munneswaram Pattu in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Chilaw in Puttalam District in the 
North Western Province, bounded as follows: 
 
North : Lot No. 08 in Preliminary Plan No. P 2795; 
East : Lot No. 08 in Preliminary Plan No. P 2795 and 
Pradeshiya Sabha Road; South : Pradeshiya Sabha Road 
and Lot No. 08 in Preliminary Plan No. P 2795; West : 
Lot No. 08 in Preliminary Plan No. P 2795. 
 
All the persons interested in the aforesaid land to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Chilaw 
Divisional Secretariat on 20th August, 2014 at 11.00 a.m. 
 
Further, it is requested to notify to me in writing in 
duplicate before 20th August, 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation, and details of the computation 
of such amount.
