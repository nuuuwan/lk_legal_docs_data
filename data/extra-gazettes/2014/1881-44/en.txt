THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Four (4) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.0373 Hectare
shown as Lots 'A' – 'D' in Advance Tracing No. MR/WLP/2014/147
of 28.08.2014 prepared by the Surveyor - General Situated in the
Village of Beraleliya Minor Division  of Weligam  Korale, Divisional
Secretary's Division of Welipitiya in Matara District, Southern
Province:
01. Lot "A" – Medakella Kumbura – Extent: 0.0139 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.), Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Lot C; East:

Way and Lot B; West: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.).

North: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot A; East: Lot A; South: Lot A and

(R.D.A).
