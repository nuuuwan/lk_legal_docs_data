FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
NUWARA-ELIYA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Nuwara- Eliya   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  Up- Country Peoples Front, to which  the
Former Member  Rajaratnam Ravikumar   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Nuwara-
Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. V elu Mylvaganam
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE   I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando,  Returning Officer of  Nuwara- Eliya    Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  V elu Mylvaganam elected as
a member of the  Nuwara- Eliya   Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
 Nuwara-Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Nuwara- Eliya,
24th  November  2014.
