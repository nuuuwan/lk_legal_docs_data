FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Unied National Party to which the member
Anuradha Wimalarathna  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within
a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Colombo Municipal Council
who shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Gurusamy Muruganadan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Colombo Municipal Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Gurusamy Muruganadan elected as a Member
of the Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo,
20th May, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely People's Liberation Front to which the member
Sunil Watagala  belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period
of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Colombo Municipal Council who
shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. R. W. Hemantha Kumara
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Colombo Municipal Council, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. R. W. Hemantha Kumara elected as a Member of
the Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo,
