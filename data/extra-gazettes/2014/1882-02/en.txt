THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.  2   OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of 1980.
I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President of Democratic  Socialist  Republic of Sri Lanka and the Minister-in-charge of the subject of
Defence and Urban Development, being of opinion that the land specified in the schedule hereto is urgently required  for the
purpose of Town Development Project under  Urban  Development Plan of Eheliyagoda do by this order declare that the said
lands is required for the such purpose.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Presdential Secretariat,
Colombo.,
19th September 2014.

An allotment of land depicted as Lot  A in Advanced Tracing No.R/EHL/2013/199 dated 30.08.2013  prepared by

Ihalagama Minor Division of Kuruwita Korale Palle Pattuwa limits of Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha within  the Divisional
Secretariat Division of Eheliyagoda, in the District of Ratnapura, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows :
Lot No.   A :
North : Railway reservation ;
East : Road (RDA) and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) ;
