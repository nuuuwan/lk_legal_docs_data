PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  the  Eastern  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Resignation ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognised political party to which the
member belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. K. Pushpa Kumar to fill
such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. K. Pushpa Kumar, elected as a member of the above
Provincial Council.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections  Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
06th June  2014.
