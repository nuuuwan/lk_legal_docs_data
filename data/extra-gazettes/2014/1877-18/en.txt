No. 1877/18 – TUESDAY, AUGUST 26, 2014
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Lands depicted as Lots 'A' to 'F' in Plan No. Co/MRT/2013/998
dated 03.12.2013 prepared by Surveyor  General, containing in
extent of 0.1095 Hectare, situated in the village of Egodauyana within
the Urban Council limits of Moratuwa in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Moratuwa in the District of Colombo, Western Province:
01. Lot  'A'  –  Thalgahawatta – Extent: 0.0301 Hectare
North: Lot No. 80 in Block No. 7 in Cadastral Plan No. 520217,
New Galle Road and Lot C;  East: New Galle Road and Lot C;
South: Lots D and B; West: Lot B and Railway reservation.
02. Lot  'B'  –  Thalgahawatta and Nugagahawatta – Extent:
0.0103 Hectare
North: Railway reservation, Lot No. 80 in Block No. 7 in  Cadastral
Plan No. 520217, Lots A, D, E and F;  East: Lots A, D, E, F and Lot
No. 176 in Block No. 7, Cadastral Plan No. 520217; South and
West:  Railway reservation.
03. Lot  'C'  –  Thalgahawatta – Extent: 0.0166 Hectare
North and East: New Galle Road; South and West:  Lots D and A.
04. Lot  'D'  –  Thalgahawatta – Extent: 0.0230 Hectare
North: Lots A, C and New Galle Road; East: Lot C and New
Galle Road; South and West:  Lots E and B.
05. Lot  'E'  –  Thalgahawatta – Extent: 0.0052 Hectare
North: Lot D and New Galle Road; East: New Galle Road and
Lot F; South: Lots F and B; West:  Lots B and D.
06. Lot  'F'  –  Nugagahawatta – Extent: 0.0243 Hectare
North: Lot E and New Galle Road; East: New Galle Road and Lot
No. 176, Block No. 7 in Cadastral Plan No. 520217; South: Lot No. 176
Block No. 7 in Cadastral Plan No. 520217 and Railway Reservation:
Lots B and E.
