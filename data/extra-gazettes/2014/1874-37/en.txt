No. 1874/37 –  THURSDAY, AUGUST  07, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
                                  Reference No.: 3/KATA/LA/85.   
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Three (03) allotments of land in total extent about 
0.1703 Hectares, shown in Am endment Preliminary Plan  
No. GAM 3856, prepared by Survey Superintendent of 
Gampaha, situated at Ambalanmulla village in Minor 
Division of Ragam Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Katana in Gamp aha District in the Western 
Province.  
 
 
1. Lot No. 10 
Name of the Land: Pilapitiyakumbura; Description: 
Waste Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: S. M. K. 
Ganeshan, No. 398, Lakecity, Jaela, Jaela; Extent: 0.0443 
Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 11 
Name of the Land: Pilapitiyakumbura; Description: 
Waste Land; Claimant: State;  Original Claimant: W. 
Camilus Fernando, No. 507, Negombo Road, Wattala; 
Extent: 0.1164 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 12 
Name of the Land: Pilapitiyakumbura; Description: 
Waste Land; Claimant: State;  Original Claimant Not 
Found; Extent: 0.0096 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Katana on the 10th day of September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (with 
2 copies) on or before the 27th day of August, 2014 the 
nature of their interests in the land, 
 
The particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land the amount of 
compensation and details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 K. G. H. H. R. K IRIELLA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Katana. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Katana. 
16th July, 2014.
