PART IV (A)  - GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA - 27.11.2014
2A
Be it enacted by the Provincial Council of the Northern Province as follows :
01. This Statute may be cited as the Transfer of Stamp Duty Statute of the Northern Province,
No. 02 of 2014.
02 (1) Where the Provincial Council has charged and collected, in terms of the Finance Statute of
the Northern Province, No. 1 of 2014, any stamp duty (hereinafter referred to as “the duty”) on any
instrument relating to a transfer of any immovable property situated within the administrative limits of any
Local Authority in the Northern Province and paid to the Provincial Fund of the Northern Province, the
Provincial Council may, on a written claim made in the form as provided in Annex 1 of this Statute by the
Administrative Officer of that Local Authority to the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance of the
Northern Province within a period of two years from the year in which the duty was collected as aforesaid,
transfer such amount of the duty, subject to the provisions of Sub-Section (3), to the Local Authority
within which administrative limits the immovable property in respect of whose transfer the duty was
collected is situated.
(2) Any duty not claimed by any Local Authority within the period as stipulated under Sub
Section (1) shall not be claimable thereafter by the said Local Authority. Provided however a Local Authority,
which had failed to make a claim within the stipulated period, may submit a late claim to the Minister and if
the Minister after having satisfied with the reasons for the delay in submitting the claim, direct the authority
to release such funds as provided under Sub Section (1).
(3) A sum equivalent to one per centum (01%) of the sum of duty to be transferred to any Local
Authority under Sub Section (1) shall be deducted by the Provincial Council and retained in the Provincial
Fund as being the expenditure of every description incurred by the Provincial Council in connection with
the collection and transfer of the duty.
03. No claim under Sub Sections (1) and (2) of Section 2 shall be valid unless accompanied by a
Schedule prepared in the manner as prescribed under Annex II of this Statute for the transfer of the duty
specified in that claim and certified by the Registrar General or any officer authorized by him in that behalf.
04. In this Statute, unless the context otherwise requires —
“Minister” means the Provincial Minister in charge for the subject of Finance in the Northern
Province ;
“Local Authorities” means any Municipal Council, Urban Council and Pradeshiya Sabha
and includes any Authority created and established by or under any law to exercise, perform
and discharge powers, duties and functions corresponding to or similar to the powers,
duties and functions exercised, performed and discharged by any such Councillor Sabha ;
“Instrument” has the same meaning as provided in the Finance Statute of the Northern
Province, No. 1 of 2014 ;
“Duty” means the stamp duty provided under Section 35(a) of Chapter XI of Part C of the
Finance Statute of the Northern Province, No. 1 of 2014 ;
“Administrative Officer” means :-
(a) In relation to a Municipal Council, the Municipal Commissioner of the Council ;
and
(b) In relation to an Urban Council or a Pradeshiya Sabha, the Secretary of the
respective Council or Sabha ;
