LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10A(1)(a)
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha
I,  Mr. Ponnuththurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Kirushnapillai Selvarasa of the Valikamam East Pradeshiya
Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the United People’s
Freedom Alliance.
M
R. P. KUGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East  Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Jaffna,
20th  May, 2014.
