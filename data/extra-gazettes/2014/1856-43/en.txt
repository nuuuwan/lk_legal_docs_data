THE HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29  OF 1971
Order  under  Section  10(1)(a) and 11(1)(a)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10(1)(a) and 11(1)(a)  of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, W. D. J.
Senevirathne, Minister of Public Administration and Home Affairs, do by this order, declare Tuesday, 15th April, 2014 a Public
Holiday on account of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year 2014.
