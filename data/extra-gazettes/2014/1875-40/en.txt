FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
PANADURA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which Ifaz
Muhammadhu Nabuhan Muhammadu the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has Communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Waduge Hemantha Fernando to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris, Returning Officer of   Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Waduge Hemantha Fernando elected as  Vice
Chairman of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Panadura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
13th August,  2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
WATTALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings
of the Council for a Period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the  recognized political party to which
Arachchige Nilantha Leshan Sanjeewa Senasingha Senarathna  the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me
under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that  Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has Communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Arachchige Nilantha Leshan Sanjeewa Senasingha Senarathna, to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning Officer of   Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Arachchige Nilantha Leshan
Sanjeewa Senasingha Senarathna, elected as  Vice Chairman of the Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
14th August,  2014.
