 NOTIFICATION
By virtue of the powers vested in Honourable Minister of Finance and Planning under the Section 4(3), 14 and 20 of the
Import and Export (Control) Act, No. 1` of 1969 as amended by Act, No. 48 of 1985 and Act, No. 28 of 1987, it is hereby notified
that the regulations published by the following Gazette Notifications were passed by the Parliament.
B. M. S. BATAGODA,
Deputy Secretary to the Treasury.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo 01,
30th of May 2014.
Gazette  Notification  passed  by  the Parliament
01. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1813/14 dated 05.06.2013 issued to publish a list of items subject to import
control was passed by the Parliament on 09.07.2013 ;
02. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1813/15 dated 05.06.2013 issued making it mandatory to comply with Sri Lanka
standard specifications for exported cinnamon  was passed by the Parliament on 09.07.2013 ;
03. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1819/32 dated 18.07.2013 issued to rescind the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1764/
9 dated 27.06.2012 issued to limit exportation of rice  was passed by the Parliament on 22.08.2013 ;
04. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1821/40 dated 01.08.2013 issued to control importation or exportation of Hydro
Chloro fluoro Carbons (HCFC)  was passed by the Parliament on 03.09.2013 ;
05. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1837/41 dated 21.11.2013 issued by including new HS Codes and repealing the
existing HS Codes in respect of the vehicles imported to Sri Lanka with the objective of transporting passengers
and goods was passed by the Parliament on 20.12.2013 ;
06. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1843/40 dated 02.01.2014 issued by amending the effective date of the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1813/15 dated 05.06.2013 issued making it mandatory to comply with Sri Lanka  Standard
Specifications for exported cinnamon  was passed by the Parliament on 22.01.2014 ;
07. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1844/49 dated 08.01.2014 issued making it mandatory to comply with Sri Lanka
Standard Specifications for 123 imported items  was passed by the Parliament on 22.01.2014 ;
08. The Gazette Extraordinary No. 1846/16 dated 21.01.2014 issued by including new HS Codes and repealing the
existing HS Codes in respect of  the vehicles imported to Sri Lanka with the objective of transporting   goods was
passed by the Parliament on 04.03.2014 ;
