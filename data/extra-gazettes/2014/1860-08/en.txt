THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHAS  ACT No. 15 OF 1987
Order
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987, I , A. L. M. Athaullah,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils, for the purpose of clarification do hereby amend the Order published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 453/19 of May 12, 1987 relating to the constitution of the Alayadivembu Pradeshiya Sabha by
the deletion of Part II of Schedule thereto and substitution of Schedule hereto.
A. L. M. A
THAULLAH,
Minister of Local Government and Provincial Councils.
Ministry of Local Government and
Provincial Councils,
25th April 2014.
