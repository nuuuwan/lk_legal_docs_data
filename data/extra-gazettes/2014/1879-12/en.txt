THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.9989 Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ma/MNN/2013/097 dated
27th February, 2014, prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys,
Vavuniya District, situated in the Village called Pallimunai, in the
Grama Niladhari Division No. MN/85 of Pallimunai West,  in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mannar, Mannar District in
Nothern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pallimunai – Extent: About 0.9989 Hectare
North: Path; East: Road (R.D.A.), Pallimunai Garden claimed by
Anthoni Queen Anthoni and others; South: Pallimunai Garden claimed
by Anthoni Queen Anthoni and others; West: Pallimunai Garden
claimed by S. Lucion Figurado and others.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
My Ref. No.: 4/3/4/2013/D/307.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: AO/MN/02/14/2013.
Ministry of Lands and Land Development,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
