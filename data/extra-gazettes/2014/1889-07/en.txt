FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
RIDIGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Ridigama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation:
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party  namely  United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the  Former member   Mr. S. D. Saman Bandula Weerarathna Malmeewala    belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days. a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election
as a member of that  Ridigama  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Bandula Rathnayake  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Ridigama
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Bandula Rathnayake elected as  a member of the  Ridigama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N.  T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Ridigama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
Distric Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
13th  November, 2014.
