Provincial  Councils  Notifications
 WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Notification
IT  is hereby announced under provisions of Section 289 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of
Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 that the regulations on payment of Travelling Expenses
to Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Councilors of Municipal Councils, formulated by me under powers vested by Section 289 of
Municipal Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of  Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 as
the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government of Western Province, and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette paper No. 1846/19 dated 21st January 2014 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka were
adopted by the Western Provincial Council at the Council meeting held on 07.10.2014.
P
RASANNA RANATUNGA,
Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning,  Law and Order,
Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Manpower and Employment, Engineering Services and Information
of the Western Province Provincial Council
Chief Ministry of the Western Provincial Council,
Shrawasthi Mandiraya,
Colombo 07.
03rd  November 2014.

Sub-section 191(g)(g) of Authority 255 in Urban Councils Ordinance read with Section 2 of  Provincial Councils (Consequential

Province, and published in the Extraordinary Gazette paper No. 1846/19 dated 21st January 2014 of the Democratic Socialist
