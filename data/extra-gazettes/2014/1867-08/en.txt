ANIMAL   DISEASES  ACT,  No. 59  OF  1992
Order  under Section 37 (5)
BY VIRTUE,  of the powers vested in me under section 37(5) of the Animal Diseases Act, No. 59 of 1992, I, Arumugan
Thondaman, Minister of Livestock and Rural Community Development do by this Order, declare that the animal diseases set
out in the Schedule hereto shall be deemed to be specified diseases for the purposes of the Act.
The Declaration published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1,457/21 of August 11, 2006 is hereby rescinded.
A
RUMUGAN T HONDAMAN,
Minister of Livestock and Rural Community Development.
Colombo,
09th June, 2014.
