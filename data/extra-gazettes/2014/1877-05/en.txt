FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KALMUNAI   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kalmunai Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending three
Consecutive Meetings without Permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Sri Lanka Muslim Congress to which the
fromer member Aboobucker Meerasaibu Mohamed Siraz  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Kalmunai Municipal Council who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Ahamed Lebbe
Athambawa Abdul Majeed being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of   Kalmunai Municipal Council  acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ahamed Lebbe Athambawa Abdul Majeed
elected as a member of the Kalmunai Municipal Council.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
22nd  August,  2014.
