NOTIFICATION
P. S. No. CSA/1/3/1/1.
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Patalee Champika Ranawaka, MP , who was appointed to the office of Minister of Technology
and Research by His Excellency the President, in the exercise of powers vested in him, by Article 44(1)(b) of the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in terms of Article 47(b) of the Constitution, resigned such office of
Minister, with effect from November 18, 2014.
                                                                                                     By order of His Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERATUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
