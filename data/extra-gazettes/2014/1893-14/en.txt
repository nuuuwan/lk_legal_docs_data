FINANCE   ACT,  No. 11  OF   1963
Order  under Section  72
BY   virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963 as amended from
time to time, read with Article 44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order, vest in the People's
Bank established under the People's Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 17th December Two Thousand and Fourteen,
the properties described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
The Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
17th December 2014.
SCHEDULE
1. All that divided and defined allotment of land marked as Lot 1 of one second share in Western side divided by the
Plan No. 2971 dated 27.08.1964 made by B. S. A. Kroon, Licensed Surveyor, of the land called “Putukaule Hena”
in extent of fifteen seers of Kurakkan,  i. e.  land in extent of four acres Two Roods and Twenty Six perches
(4A., 2R., 26P.) depicted in Plan No. 2974 dated 25.08.1964 made by K. Kumarasamy, Licensed Surveyor, situated
at Thanna, Kohonsiya Pattu in Southern Division in the District of Matale, Central Province and bounded on the
East by Lot 2 of this land depicted in said Plan No. 2971 on the South by Alawathuyaye Watta and Baron Singho's
Watta on the West by Katapita Watta on the North by Muwagala Watte containing in extent of Two Acres, one
Rood and Thirteen perches (2A., 1R., 13P.) together with trees, plantation and everything else standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio B 252/201 in the Land Registry, Matale.
