THE    UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section  27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, do by this order, on the recommendation of the University
Grants Commission, amend the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South Eastern University
of Sri Lanka and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996, as last amended by Order published in
Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1787/27 of December 05, 2012 by the addition immediately after item (b) in Column II under the
heading “Department of Study” which correspond to the entry “Faculty of Management and Commerce” in Column I under
the heading “Faculty” in the Schedule II to such order, of the following new item :-
                 “(c) Department of Management and Information Technology”
D
ISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA BANDA DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
27th October 2014.
