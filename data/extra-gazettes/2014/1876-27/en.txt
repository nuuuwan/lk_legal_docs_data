THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section   27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I,  Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, Minister of Higher Education, do by this Order,  amend
the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, on the recommendation of the   University  Grants Commission and
published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 420/25 of  September 26, 1986   in relation to the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, as
last  amended by order published in Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 1658/36 of June  17, 2010, in relation to the entries in
Column II under the heading '' Department'' which corresponds to the entry ''Faculty of Arts and Culture''  in Column I  under
the heading ''Faculty'' in  the Schedule to such Order by the addition immediately after item 9 in Column II of the following
New Item :-
''10.  Department of Hindu Civilization.''.
 D
ISSANAYAKE MUDIYANSELAGE SUMANAWEERA
BANDA DISSANAY AKE,
Minister of Higher Education.
Ministry of Higher Education,
Colombo,
14th August  2014.
