DECLARATION OF PAPILIYANA SUNETHRADEVI RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED
AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND
NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Papiliyana Sunethradevi Rajamaha Vihara sacred area", for its long-term
security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affair,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF PAPILIY ANA SUNETHRADEVI RAJAMAHA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 1.3749 Hectares
depicted as lot No.1 situated in Papiliyana village in Minor Division of Palle Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretariat Division
of Kesbewa of Colombo District in Western Province depicted in primary plan No.P.P .C.9122  dated 10
th November 2010 of
Surveyor General.
