NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION   10(A)(1)(a)   OF   THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Katana  Pradeshiya  Sabha
I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne, Returning Officer of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under
Section 10(A)(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Jeramiyes Lakshman Surendra Fernando
Pulle of the Katana Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that council as he has ceased to be a member of the
United National Party.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNE,
Returning Officer,
Katana Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
15th  February, 2014.
