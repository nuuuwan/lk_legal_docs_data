THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
  
Notice under Section 7 
 
                   Reference No.: GANA/4/5/Acquisition/81. 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/15/2012VE/284. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intended to acquire the following                   
land/servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:  
 
An allotment of land as described below shown in the 
Surveyor General’s plan No. FVP. 528 supplementary 
sheet No. 89-92, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division 
No. 219D, Aluthwatte in th e Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Gangaboda Pattuwa North, Nagoda in Galle 
District. 
 
Lot No. 1605  
 
Name of Land: Nakiyadeniya Estate; Present usage of 
Land: Home Garden with-Mixed Cultivation and with 5 
Buildings; Claimant: Manager, Watawela Plantation Co. 
Ltd., Colombo; Extent: 2.0234 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested to the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divi sional Secretariat, Nagoda 
on the 27th day of August 2014 at 10.30 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 31st day of July 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the compensation of such amount. 
 
 S. T HRIMANNA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Nagoda. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Nagoda.
