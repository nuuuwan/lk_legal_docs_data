EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Ref. No.: Mi/D.S/L/3/3481/31.    
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
 Description of the land to be acquired :  
 
An allotment of land mentioned as lot No. 31 in plan 
No. P.P. Gam 3481 prepared by Superintendent of 
Surveyor Gampaha, situated in the Village of Mirigama, 
in Udugahapattuwa, in divisional secretarial division at 
Mirigama, in the District of Gampaha and three (3) 
allotments had divided and shown as lot No. 54, 55, 56 
under same plan. 
 
01. Lot No. 54 
 
Name of Land: Illanwatta; Claimant: Government; 
Original Claimant: R.A.S.D. Ranasingha; Extent: 0.0060 
Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 55 
 
Name of Land: Illanwatta; Claimant: Government; 
Original Claimant: M.W. Ranasingha; Extent: 0.0025 
Hectare. 
03. Lot No. 56 
 
Name of Land: Illanwatta; Claimant: Government; 
Original Claimant: R.A.S.D. Ranasingha; Extent: 0.0087 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Office of Mirigama on 22nd August, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 15th August, 2014, the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 Y. I. M. S ILVA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Gampaha. 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Gampaha. 
08th  July, 2014.
