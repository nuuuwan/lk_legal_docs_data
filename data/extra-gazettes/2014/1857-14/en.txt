EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER OF THE  AKKARAIPATTU PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Under Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act No. 30 of 2013
WHEREAS the office of a member in Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of
the Council for a period of three months without permission;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely National Congress, to which the member
M. M. Mohamed Ali belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that  Akkaraipattu  Pradeshiya
Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. A. A. Mohamed Razeen
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
Now  therefore, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning  Officer of Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya  Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. A. A. Mohamed Razeen elected as  a member  of
the  Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya  Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Akkaraipattu Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Ampara.
02nd April, 2014.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER OF  THE   KATANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Under Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act No. 30 of 2013
WHEREAS the office of a member in  Katana  Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a member
of the party;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the member
Jeramiyes Lakshman Surendra Fernandopulle  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of  that  Katana
Pradeshiya  Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated Mr. Yakdehige Wickramapala
Samarathunga being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Patiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna, Returning  Officer of  Katana Pradeshiya  Sabha, acting in
terms of  Section 65A(2) of the Local  Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Yakdehige Wickramapala Samarathunga elected
as  a member  of   the  Katana  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
P. A. S. S
ENARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Katana Pradeshiya  Sabha.
Elections Office,
Gampaha,
04th  April, 2014.
