No. 1877/56 –  FRIDAY, AUGUST  29, 2014 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No. : අඹෙකෝ/3/1/අ/119. 
 
Reference No. of the Ministry of Land & Land 
Development : 4/3/9/2011/WS/244. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired:  
 
The allotment of land called “Dickoya Estate,  
Darawela Lower Division” in total extent of about 0.0127 
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 1 in the Preliminary Plan  
No. NU 2878 dated on 30.06.2014, surveyed and 
prepared by the Surveyor Gene ral, situated in the Village 
of Dickoya, in Ambagamuwa Korale Minor Division in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ambagamuwa 
Korale in Nuwara Eliya District, Central Province and 
bounded as follows:- 
 
Lot No. 1 – Name of the Land: Dickoya Estate, 
Darawela Lower Division; Extent of the Land: 0.0127 
Hectare. 
Boundries on the: 
 
North by: Dickoya Estate (T.P. 45507); East by: 
Dickoya Estate (T.P. 45507) and Road (RDA); South by: 
Road (RDA) & Dickoya Estate (T.P. 45507); West by: 
Dickoya Estate (T.P. 45507);  
 
Claimant and Address of the claimant : Watawala 
Plantations PLC, No. 60, Dharmapala Mawatha, 
Colombo 03.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office, at Ambagamuwa Korale on the 01st 
day of October 2014 at 11.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 24th day of September 2014, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
  
 H. M. C. K. H ERATH,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ambagamuwa Korale, 
 Ginigathhena. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary's Office, 
Ambagamuwa Korale, 
Ginigathhena.
