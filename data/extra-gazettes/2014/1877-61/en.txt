DECLARATION OF URAPOLA SIRI MEDHANKARARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that,, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as " Urapola Siri Medhankararama Vihara sacred area", for its long-term
security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF URAPOLA SIRI MEDHANKARARAMA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 8.3218 Hectares
depicted in the below mentioned Part I and Part II, Part III, Part IV , Part V , Part VI, Part VII, Part VIII, Part IX, Part X, Part XI
situated at Urapola Grama Niladari V asama in Minor Division of Gangabada Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretariat Division
of Kamburupitiya of theAdministrative District of Matara in Southern Province depicted in plan No.MR/KMB/2010/185,
Zone No. 01,  dated 14
th September 2010 of Surveyor General.
