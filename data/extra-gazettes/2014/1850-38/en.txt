I, hereby give notice under sub-section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the  land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following land located in the village of welegoda 
in the Welegoda-East Grama Niladhari Division in the 
Matara Divisional Secretariat Division, Matara District, 
Southern, Province, Depicted as lot 49 and 50 in plan                  
No. 820024 in sheet 1 of block 1 have been acquired for 
the expansion of Matara work shop and Ground water 
section.  
Schedule  
 
01. Lot No. 49  
 
Name of Land: Dingipadinchiwasitiyawatta alias 
Kurakkankanaththe Pitawagura; Description: Shrub; 
Original Claimant: A. T. Pushpa Priyanthi, T. Thusitha 
Priyananda, T. Dinendra, W. G. Sujitha Priyanga, W. G. 
Kavinda Priyanga and W. G. Dinusha Priyanga, Nupe, 
Matara; Extent : 0.00769 Hectares. 
 
01. Lot No. 50  
 
Name of Land: Dingipadinchiwasitiyawatta alias 
Kurakkankanaththe Pitawagura; Description: Shrub; 
Original Claimant: K. G. Thilak Jayasekara, No. 364/5, 
Nelum Road, Wava Road, Boralasgamuwa; Extent: 
0.0179 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at  Matara Divisional Secretariat on 
the 05th March, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 12th March, 2014 the nature of their interests 
in the land the particular s of their claims for 
Compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 RANJITH YAPA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Matara, 
February, 2014.
