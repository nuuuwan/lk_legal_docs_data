FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
MINUWANGODA   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Minuwangoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a member
of the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party  to which the member,
Wimalchandra Pushpakumar De Silva belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Minuwangoda
Urban Council  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Asurumunige Newton
Kulasooriya  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Minuwangoda Urban
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Asurumunige Newton
Kulasooriya  elected as a member of the Minuwangoda Urban Council.
P. A. S. SENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Minuwangoda Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
10th  July, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
PELIYAGODA   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Peliyagoda Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance  to which
the member,  Jayarathnage Chamila Sandaruwan belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Peliyagoda Urban Council  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Dilshan Malith
Dissanayake  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Peliyagoda Urban Council,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Dilshan Malith Dissanayake  elected
as a member of the Peliyagdoa Urban Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Peliyagoda Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
11th  July, 2014.
