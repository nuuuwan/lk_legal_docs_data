PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & C., by the Governors
1A
WESTERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
I, Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province as per powers assigned to me under clause 154 (f)(5) of the
13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby announce that the other
ministerial subjects assigned to Hon. Chief Minister of the Western Province Prasanna Ranathunga with effect from 14.07.2014
would be Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Manpower
and Employment, Engineering Services, Information and Trade.
It is notified further that the notification issued by me on 14.07.2014 in relation to the matter stated in the Extraordinary
Gazette Paper of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1876/30 dated 20.08.2014 would be annulled hereby.
S. A
LAVI MOWLANA,
Governor of the Western Province.
On the 27th day of the August 2014 at,
Governor's Secretariat (W. P.),
No. 98/4, Havelock Road,
Colombo 05.
