FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
THIRUKKOVIL    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Thirukkovil  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending meetings
of the Council for a Period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely IIankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi to which the
Former member Meharasa Chandirakumar, belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Thirukkovil
Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Kandhaiya
Amirthalingam being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of   Thirukkovil  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kandhaiya Amirthalingam  elected
as a member of the Thirukkovil  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Thirukkovil Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
31st  October,  2014.
