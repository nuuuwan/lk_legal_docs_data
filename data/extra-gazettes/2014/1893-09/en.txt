1A
NORTHERN   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made  by  the  Governor   of  the Northern  Province
GAMMANPILA Arachchige Chandrasiri, Governor of the Northern Province by virtue of  Power vested in me under the
Section 154 F (1), (4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka,
do hereby announce that I have made the following appointments.
Hon. Canagasabapathy Viswalingam Wigneswaran - Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Lands,Electricity, Housing and Construction, Industries
and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local Government, and
Provincial Administration of the Northern Province with
effect from 10th of December 2014.
Hon. Ponnudurai Ayngaranesan - Minister of Agriculture and Agrarian Services, Animal
Husbandry, Irrigation, Water Supply, Food Supply and
Distribution, Environment and Co-operative Development
of the Northern Province with effect from 10th of December
2014.
Hon. Pathmanathan Sathiyalingam - Minister of Health and Indigenous Medicine, Social
Services and Rahabilitation, Probation and Childcare
Services and Women's Af fairs of the Northern Province
with effect from 10 th of December 2014.
Hon. Balasubramaniam Deniswaran - Minsiter of  Fisheries, Transport, Trade and Commerce,
Rural Development, Road Development and Motor Traffic
of the Northern Province with effect from 10th of December
2014.
G.. A. C
HANDRASIRI,
Governor,
Northern Province.
10th December, 2014
