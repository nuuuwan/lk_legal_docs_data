DECLARATION OF BORELLA GOTHAMI VIHARA SACRED AREA AS SACRED AREA
ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF 22ND NOVEMBER
2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that,  the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as "Borella Gothami Vihara sacred area", for its long-term security.
DR. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF BORELLA GOTHAMI VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.7343 Hectares
depicted situated in Borella village in Gothamipura Grama Niladari Division ( within the boundaries of Colombo Municipal
Council, Divisional No.35 Borella South )in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Thibirigasyaya of the Administrative
District of Colombo in Western Province depicted in plan No.C.O./THI/2010/555 ( Primary Plan No.C:9137 ) dated 21
st October
2010 of Surveyor General.
