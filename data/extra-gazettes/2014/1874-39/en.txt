THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: DSB/LND/3/AQUIC/35-16. 
 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4/3/8/2012/NRD/153. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for Matara - Kataragama Railway project. 
 
Description of the land: 
 
Lot 910 and 911, depicted in the plan F.V.P. 308, 
prepared by the Surveyor Ge neral and situated in the 
village of Puwakdandawa in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Beliatta in Hambantota District. 
 
Lot No. 910  
 
Name of the Land: Korigewatta; Claimant:                      
G. Alahapperuma, No. 151/2, Matara Road, Beliatta; 
Extent: 0.0193 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 911  
 
Name of the Land: Mahawatta; Claimant: Nandadasa 
Abesooriya, No. 21/1, Mangala Road, Beliatta; Extent: 
0.0192 Hectare. 
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents, duly 
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
of Beliatta on the 18th September, 2014 at 10.00 a.m. and 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 11th September, 2014 the nature of their 
interests in the land, particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
All deeds and other documents in support of the 
claims should be produced at the inquiry. 
 
 J. H. S. P. J AYAMAHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Beliatta. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Beliatta. 
06th September, 2014.
