DECLARATION OF MAHANUW ARA SHRI VELUWANARAMA PURANA VIHARA SACRED AREA AS
SACRED AREA ORDER PUBLISHED IN EXTRAORDINARY GAZETTE NOTIFICATION No.1681/3 OF
22ND NOVEMBER 2010
I, Dr.Dissanayake Mudiyanselage Jayaratne as the Honourable Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs by According
to the subject matter of sacred area planning and development vested in me, as per the notification issued by His Excellency
the President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Mr.Mahinda Rajapaksa by the Extraordinary Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No.1681/3 of 22
nd November 2010, declared that, the land area depicted clearly in
the schedule as a sacred area and it shall be cited as " Mahanuwara Shri Veluwanarama Purana Vihara sacred area", for its
long-term security.
D
R. D. M. JAYARATNE,
Minister of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs.
On 20thAugust 2014,
Ministry of Buddhasasana and Religious Affairs,
No.135, Dharmapala Mawatha,
Colombo-07.
Schedule
COMPOUND AREA OF MAHANUWARA SHRI VELUW ANARAMA PURANA VIHARA SACRED AREA
All that divided and defined allotment of land which is bounded as follows, containing in extent 0.711 Hectares
depicted in the below mentioned Part A,B and C situated at Karamada Grama Niladari Wasama in Minor Division of Ganga
Palatha in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Udunuwara of the Administrative District of Kandy in Central  Province
depicted in plan No.Maha/UDN/2013/1682 dated 16
th September 2013 of Surveyor General.
