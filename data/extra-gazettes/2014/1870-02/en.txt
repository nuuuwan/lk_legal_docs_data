FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
SURIYAWEWA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Suriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being elected for
Southern Province Provincial  Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the member Peduru Arachchige Sujith Muthukumarana, belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Suriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Geeganage Sanjaya
Mahinda Weerasuriya being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathne Returning Officer of   Suriyawewa Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Geeganage Sanjaya Mahinda
Weerasuriya elected as a member of the Suriyawewa Pradeshiya Sabha.
U. D. D. D. C
HANDRARA THNE,
Returning Officer,
Suriyawewa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
03rd July,  2014.
