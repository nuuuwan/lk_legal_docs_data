THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
Order under Section 9
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 9(1) (a) of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Article
44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this Order designate the National Highways which were
declared as National Highways by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinay  No. 1846/15 of January 20, 2014 and
specified in the Schedule to this Order, to be Expressways.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Colombo.
