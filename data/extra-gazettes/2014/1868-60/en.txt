FINANCE  ACT, No. 11 OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963 as amended from
time to time, read with Article 44(2) of the Constitution  I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this order, vest in the People’s
Bank established under the People’s Bank Act, No. 29 of 1961, with effect from 26th June Two Thousand Fourteen, the
properties described in the Schedule hereto.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
The Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
26th June 2014.
SCHEDULE
1. All that undivided eleven upon sixty four share (11/64) of the extent of two paddy amuna of the land called
“Diyarathambale Chena and Pitimulla Kumbura” situated at Millawitiya, Uda Pattu North of Kuruwiti Korale in the
district of Rathnapura, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded on the North by Diggedigaha chena on the East by
the ditch in the land of Gampaha Korala, on the South by Palikada Mulle ovita, Kunda Manannalage ovita,
Pitamulla and Hiri Kumbura of Gampaha Korala, on the West by the boundry of the village of Millawitiya together
with rubber plantation registered under J 158 SR 86 at Rubber Controlling Office,
This land is registered in folio A 713/180 in the Land Registry, Rathnapura
2. All that divided and defined allotment of Lot A of Vidane Walauwe Watte of land marked as Lot A situated at
Pathegamgoda in Welitara, Walallawita Korale of Benthota in the District of Galle, Southern Province and bounded
on the North by Ampitiyawatta belonging to Dines De Zoysa Muhandiram, on the East by Lot B of the same land,
on the South by Lot A7 and Lot A2 of the same land, on the West by Mahadelathgedara watta containing in One
Rood (0A., 1R., 0P.) together with trees, fruits, cinnamon cultivation and everything else standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio B 363/202 in the Land Registry, Balapitiya
