FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBERSHIP  OF  THE
YATIYANTHOTA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Yatiyanthota Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United Peoples Freedom Alliance, to which
the member, R. M. Upul Sisira Kumara the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Yatiyanthota Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Kumbukgahamula
Gedara Kiriappulage Nimal Wijesooriya being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of  Yatiyanthota Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of V acancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kumbukgahamula Gedara Kiriappulage Nimal
Wijesooriya  elected as a member of theYatiyanthota Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALATHGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Yatiyanthota Pradeshiya Sabha.
Elections Office,
Kegalle,
03rd June,  2014.
