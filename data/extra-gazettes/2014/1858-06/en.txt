THE INDUSTRIAL DISPUTES ACT, CHAPTER 131 OF THE LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Mr. D.T. Leelaratne, Hellanda, Aluthwela, Hali-Ela of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Transport Board, Head Office, No. 200, Kirula Road, Colombo 05 of the other part.
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the punishing of Mr. D. T. Leelaratne who served in the post of Assistant Manager- running (covering) of the Bandarawela
Depot of the Sri Lanka Transport Board, after holding a disciplinary inquiry on the accusation of not providing fuel to the vehicle of a group
of mobile inspectors from the Badulla Regional Office that visited the Depot on 27.08.2008 is just, and if not so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
V . B. P. K. WEERASINGHE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 06th day of March, 2014.
