THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/02/11. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land which 
is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Lands to be acquired:  
 
Following allotments of land in Cadastral Map No. 
620291 situated in Batugedara Village in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division Rathnapu ra, in the District of 
Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 186 
Name of Land: Welihindakanaththewaththa; Detail: 
Open barren land; Claimant: Government; Extent: 0.0031 
Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 187 
Name of Land: Welihindakanaththewaththa; Detail: 
Open barren land; Claimant: D. V. Podinilame, Damsiri 
House, Maudalla, Dela, Rathnapura; Extent: 0.0504 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby requested to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before  
me at 10.00 a. m. on 14th October 2014 at Rathnapura 
Divisional Secretariat. 
 
Moreover, they are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing with two duplicates on or before the 07th day of 
October 2014 the nature of their interests in land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said Lands, the amount of 
Compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 C. M. K
OTTAWATHTHA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Rathnapura. 
 
At Rathnapura Divisional Secretariat, 
22nd August, 2014.
