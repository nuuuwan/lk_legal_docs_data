NOTICE BY THE DIRECTOR GENERAL OF CIVIL AVIATION UNDER THE AIR NA VIGATION REGULATION 196
MEMBERS of the public are hereby notified that this office has received on 22nd April, 2014 an application dated 10th March,
2014 from Fly Southern (Pvt) Ltd. Level 1, S. S. Tower, 92, Glennie Street, Colombo 2, seeking an Air Operator Certificate to
engage in Aerial Work Operation, Private Operation, Domestic Charter Operation and Regular Public Transport Operation,
International Charter Operations and International Cargo Operation on Schedule basis in compliance with Civil Aviation Act
and Regulations made thereunder. Business name of the Company is Fly Southern (Pvt) Ltd. with the trade name as “Fly
Southern”. The Directors of the Fly Southern (Pvt) Ltd. are declared as follows.
Mr. Samitha Abeygunawardana
Mrs. T. Dilanthi Suriyapathirana
Fly Southern (Pvt) Ltd. has nominated Mr. Samitha Abeygunawardana to be the Accountable Manager in charge of the
proposed operations of air services.
A copy of the aforementioned application is kept at the Technical Library, Ground Floor, of Head Quarters of the Civil
Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka, No. 04, Hunupitiya Road, Colombo 02, for the perusal of the members of the public, during
office hours of any working days.
Members of the public are hereby informed that this office would proceed with the certification process of this
application after 04th June, 2014 as per the established procedures to examine the degree of Applicant’s compliance with the
applicable Legislative provisions, Air Navigation Regulations, International Standards and Recommended practices speci-
fied by ICAO and associated local requirements adopted by this office and ascertain their level of competence for the safe,
secure and efficient operation of proposed air services, prior to granting them the requisite Air Operator Certificate and
together with other relevant approvals for the commencement of proposed operations.
