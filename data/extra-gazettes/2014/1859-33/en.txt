Proclamation  made by the Governor of the Western  Province
To all whom these presents shall come :
I, Seyed Alavi Mowlana, the Governor of the Western Province, in accordance with the powers vested in me by Paragraph
8(a) in Clause 154 ‘‘b’’ of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby summon the Western
Province Provincial Council to meet on 30th April 2014 at 11.15 a. m. in the Committee Room ‘A’ of Bandaranaike Memorial
International Conference Hall, Colombo 07.
S
EYED ALAVI MOWLANA,
Governor of the Western Province.
At the Secretariat of the Governor,
98/4, Havelock Road,
Colombo 05.
the 24 th day of April, 2014.
