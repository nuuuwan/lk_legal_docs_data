FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
V ALIKAMAM  EAST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
And  Whereas the Secretary of the recognised political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the member,  Asaipillai Vaseenthiran belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Valikamam
East Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And  Whereas  the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has nominated  Mr. Arulanantham Rajeevan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
Now Therefore  I,  Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Arulanantham Rajeevan  elected as a member of
the Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam East Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
15th  July, 2014.
