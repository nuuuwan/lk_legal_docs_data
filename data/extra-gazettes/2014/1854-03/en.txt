PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
EXCHANGE  CONTROL  ACT  (CHAPTER  423)
Order under Section 17
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Exchange Control Act (Chapter 423), read with paragraph (2) of
Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President, do by this
order grant permission to Licensed Commercial Banks appointed as Authorized Dealers to acquire, hold and transfer
International Sovereign Bonds issued outside Sri Lanka by the Government of Sri Lanka in year 2010, 2011and 2012, from the
secondary market, subject to the orders issued by me from time to time.
M
AHINDA RAJAPAKSA,
President.
Ministry of Finance and Planning,
Colombo,
17th March, 2014.
