FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE  MAHARAGAMA  URBAN  COUNCIL UNDER  SECTION
65A  (2)   OF  THE  LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Maharagama Urban  Council has fallen vacant by reason being elected as a member of
the Western Province Provincial Council :
And whereas the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which  the member
Rodney Rudolf Frazor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under Ordinance for election as a member of that  Maharagama Urban Council who
shall be declared elected as a member.
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Ranasingha Arachchige
Ajantha Ranjith being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy :
Now therefore I, Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardhana,  Returning Officer of  Maharagama Urban  Council,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ranasingha Arachchige Ajantha
Ranjith elected as a member of the Maharagama Urban Council.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDENA,
Returning Officer,
Maharagama Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Colombo District,
21st May, 2014.
