FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE
BERUWALA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Vice Chairman  in Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has
been appointed as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party  to which
Seema Hewage Kusman De Silva the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Manamendra Acharige Nevil Ranjith  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Manamendra Acharige Nevil Ranjith  elected as
Vice Chairman of the Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Beruwala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
11th  July, 2014.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF  THE
V ALIKAMAN   SOUTH   WEST  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Vice Chairman  in Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice
Chairman has been appointed as Chairman ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party  to which   Shanmugam
Sivakumaaran the Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Sinnathamby Mahenthiran  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sinnathamby Mahenthiran  elected
as Vice Chairman of the Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
10th  July, 2014.
