PROMOTION  AND  APPOINTMENT  APPROVED  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE  PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Rear Admiral Sirimevan Sarathchandra
Ranasinghe, WWV , RWP , USP,  ndc, psc, AOWC to the rank  of Vice Admiral with effect from October 26, 2017 and to
appoint him under the Article 61E, (1)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the
Head of the Sri Lanka Navy, with effect from that date.
                                 By His Excellency’s command,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
31st October, 2017.
