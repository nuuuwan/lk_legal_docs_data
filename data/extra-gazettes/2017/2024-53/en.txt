THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS  OF  CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by order made under Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (Revised
Edition 1956) as amended by Acts, No. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 37 of 1968 dated 22.09.2016 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No.
1987/14 dated 04.10.2016 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated
02.09.2016.
Mr. K. L .C. Harendra, No. 650/A/3, Arawwala, Pannipitiya. of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Broadcasting Corporation, Bauddhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 07. of the other part
was referred to Mr. S.M.S. Jayawardena for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said Order be revoked, I, W. D. J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour, Trade Union
Relations and Sabaragamu Development  do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings be taken upon the said
Order dated 22.09.2016.
W. D. J.  SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and
Sabaragamu Development.
Colombo,
20th June, 2017.

Order under Section (4) 1
WHEREAS an industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this
order exists between,
Mr. K.L.C. Harendra, No. 650/A/3, Arawwala, Pannipitiya. of the one part
and
Sri Lanka Broadcasting Corporation, Bauddhaloka Mawatha,  Colombo 07. of the other part
NOW THEREFORE I, Welatantirige Don John Seneviratne, Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamu Development
do by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 (1) of the Industrial Disputes Act. Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon
(1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Acts, Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions)
Act, No. 37 of 1968 hereby appoint,
Mr. S. Kariyawasam, No. 75, Melpitiya, Matale.to be arbitrator and refer the aforesaid dispute to him for settlement by
arbitration.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mr. K.L.C. Harendra who is working as the Acting Controller of the Sales Promotion Division of the Sri Lanka Broadcasting
Corporation has been caused injustice by not being confirmed in the post of controller from 18.02.2013of the Sports Division and if so, to
what reliefs he is entitled.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 18th day of May, 2017.
