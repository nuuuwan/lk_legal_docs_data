THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.1685 Hectare
depicted in Advance Tracing No. PU/MUN/2011/508 and dated
22.11.2011 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village
of Nallandaluwa in Puttalam pattu Minor Division, Mundel
Divisional Secratary's Division,Puttalam District and bounded as
follows:

0.1685 Hectare
North: Abdul Rasir Masuda claimed by Abdul Rasir Asmir of
