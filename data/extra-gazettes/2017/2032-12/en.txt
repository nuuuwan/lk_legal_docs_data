THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands  described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act Such Servitude is to be acquired from which lands are described
below.
1. Description of Servitude - For the construction at access to
Kumbukkana Intake of Monaragala - Buttala Integrated water Supply
Project.
2. Description of the land  - Three (03) allotments of land
containing in extent about 0.1220 Hectare  depicted as Lots  A, B and
C in the Advance Tracing No. MO/BTT/2015/725 and dated
24.06.2016 prepared by the Surveyor- General situated in the Buttala
Wedirata Korale Minor Division in the Village of Okkampitiya in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Buttala in Monaragala District
Uva Province and Bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Elamanaya – Extent: About 0.0619 Hectare
North: Elamanaya claimed by E.A. Anulawathi; East: Elamanaya
claimed by E.A. Anulawathi and canal; South: Canal, Elamanaya
claimed by E.A. Anulawathi and Kumbukkan Oya; West: Elamanaya
claimed by E.A. Anulawathi and Kumbukkan Oya.
  02. Lot ‘B’ – Elamanaya – Extent: About 0.0495 Hectare
North: Canal and Elamanaya claimed by E.A. Anulawathi; East:
Elamanaya claimed by E.A. Anulawathi and Road; South: Road and
Elamanaya claimed by E.A. Anulawathi; West: Elamanaya claimed
by E.A. Anulawathi and canal.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Elamanaya – Extent: About 0.0106 Hectare
North: Canal and Elamanaya Owners could not be found; East:
Elamaya owners could not be found and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road
(R.D.A.) and Elamanaya Owners could not be found; W est:
Elamanaya owners could not be found and canal.
