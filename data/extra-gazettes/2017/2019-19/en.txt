THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 07
My Reference No.:Anu/ 4/5 /2/7 /3/81/1.
Land  Ministry  Reference No.: 4/3/9/2014/WS/149.
I  hereby give notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 07 of
the Land Acquisition Act,  as Amended by  the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land required
for construction of Water Tank and Quarters under the
Anuradhapura North Water Supply Project.
  Description of the land to be acquired:-
An allotment of land called Pihimbiyagollawa Mookalana,
Kolongahawatta in total extent of  0.2023 Hectare depicted as
Lot No. 228 in Final Village Plan No. 580, prepared by the
surveyor - General in the village of Pihimbiyagollawa in Grama
Niladhari Division of   No.81 Pihimbiyagollawa , in Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Rambawa, Anuradhapura District
Northern Central Province and bounded as follows :-
Lot No. 228 - Name of the Land: Pihimbiyagollawa
Mookalana, Kolongahawatta;   Extent: 0.2023 Hectare.
North: Lot No. 227; East: Lot No. 230; South: Lot No. 230 and
Lot No. 229; West: Lot No. 229 and 227.
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands, on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat Rambawa,  on the 22nd day of June
2017 at 10.00 O'clock, and are hereby requested to notify  to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 22nd day of June
2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
 A. M. W. A. AMUNUGAMA,
 Divisional  Secretary,
   Rambawa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Rambawa.
17th May 2017.
