THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No.  41  OF  1978   ENACTED   BY  THE  NATIONAL
STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development
do hereby this Order vest with the Urban Development Authority with effect from today the lands morefully described in
the Schedule hereto being lands vested in the Municipal Council of Dehiwala Mount Lavinia.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M.P.),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,
17th and 18th Floor,
“Suhurupaya”,
Battaramulla,
12th June 2017.
SCHEDULE
Five allotments of lands shown as 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 in Plan No. 55 dated 11.12.1975 prepared by Municipal Surveyor
situated in Dehiwala village within the Municipal Council limits of Dehiwala Mt. Lavinia in the Divisional Secretariat
Division of Dehiwala in the Colombo District, Western Province and bounded on North by Station Road, East by
Asst. No. 21, Station Road, South by Asst. No. 22/4, Galle Road, West by Asst. Nos. 27 & 27/1 Station Road in extent of
