PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual Festival of  Suwa Sahanaya Catholic Church Weligama
IT is hereby notified under Section 02 of regulations framed under  Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the
Gazette No. 1993/ 23 of 17.11.2016 of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, That the Annual Festival of Suwa
Sahanaya Catholic Church of Matara  District in the Divisional Secretary’ s Division of  Weligama in the Matara District,
Will be conducted on 25th of November to 26th November 2017.
K. P. G. S
UMITH SHANTHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Weligama.
At the Divisional Secretariat,
Weligama,
10th November 2017.
