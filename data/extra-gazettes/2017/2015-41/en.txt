No. 448/1 of 2017
P.  S. No. : PS/HR/01/15/PS/Act
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe, Secretary to the Cabinet of Ministers to
act in the post of Secretary to the President with effect from 07th April 2017 until Mr. P. B. Abeykoon, Secretary to the
President returns to the Island.
                          By Order of  His Excellency,

OHANA KEERTHI D ISSANAYAKE,

(Human Resources Development and Agency Coordination),
