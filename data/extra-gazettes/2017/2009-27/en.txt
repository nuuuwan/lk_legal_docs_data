THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An  allotment  of land called Millagahawaththa in  extent of  about
0.1518 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. Gam/Gam/2015/
112 and dated 15th May, 2015, prepared by the Surveyor-General
in Madapaththuwa Minor Division in Gampaha District in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot A – Millagahawatta – Extent: About 0.1518 Hectare
North: Road, Divisional Secretariat street and the land of
Millagahawaththa claimed by Reginold Wicramarachchi; East: The
land of Millagahawaththa claimed by Reginold Wicramarachchi and
the land of Millagahawaththa claimed by Rani Wicramarachchi; South:
The land of Millagawaththa claimed by Rani Wicramarachchi and
the land of Millagahawaththa claimed by W. Piyasena; West: The
land of Millagahawaththa claimed by W. Piyasena and the Road.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
