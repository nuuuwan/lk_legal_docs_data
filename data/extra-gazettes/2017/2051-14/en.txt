Supplmentary Provision for Appropriation  Statute No. 12 of 2017
A statute to provide Supplementary Provisions to previously approved expenditure of the Nothern Provincial
Council for the financial year 2017 by the Honourable Governor on the 26th day of  December 2016 in accordance
to First Schedule to this statute and to make payments by way of advance out of the Northern Provinc ial
Council funds or monies to provide for the refund of such monies to the Nothern Provincial Council funds and
to make provisions for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council as follows.
1. This Statute may be cited as the Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statute No. 12 of 2017.
2. Without prejudice to any other Law which authorizes for any such expenditure, the expenditure of the
Northern Provincial Council during the financial year beginning from 1st January 2017 and ending on
31st December 2017 referred to as the "Financial year 2017" in this Statute re-allocation of Rupees
Twenty two million and five hundred thousand only (Rs. 22,500,000) from the expenditure the Honourable
Governor approved on the 26th December 2016 and to the limits stated in the First Schedule of this
Statute shall be met;
A) From the Nothern Provincial Council funds or monies, or
B) From the allocation of funds allocated in accordance to Article 154 R of the  Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and any other funds allotted or granted by the Government of
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
