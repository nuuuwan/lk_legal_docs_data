PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments   made by  the  Hon. Governor  of   North  Central  Province
BY virtue of the power vested in me under Article 4 and 5 of 154(f) of 13th amendment of Constitution of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, P. B. Dissanayaka, the Governor of the North Central Province do hereby notify that the
following appointments have been made with effect from the date mentioned below :
1. Hon. S. M. Peshala Jayarathna Bandara
As the Chief Minister of North Central Province and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local
Government, Rural Infrastructure Facilities, Special Projects, Tourism, Education, Information Technology,
Engineering Constructions and Cultrual Affairs of North Central Province with effect from 2017.05.17
Secretary to the Ministry - Mrs. W. T. A. Manel - SLAS Grade I (to perform duties)
2. Hon. M. Herath Banda
As the Minister of Provincial Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Child Care,
Environment and Provincial Council Affairs of North Central Province with effect from 2017.05.10
Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. S. M. S. Bandulasena - SLAS Special Grade
3. Hon. Susil Gunarathne
As the Minister of Cooperative, Trade and Food, Sports and Youth Affairs, Industrial Development of North
Central Province with effect from 2017.05.16
Secretary to the Ministry - Mr. R. A. S. Jayathilake - SLAS Special Grade
