Finance (Amendment) Statute of the Northern Province Provincial Council No. 04 of 2017
NOTICE is hereby given that the Northern Province, Provincial Council has adopted the Finance (Amendment) Statute of
the Northern Province Provincial Council No. 04 of 2017 on 25th May 2017 and that the Hon. Governor, Northern Province
has given his assent to it on 13th October 2017.

NORTHERN  PROVINCE   FINANCE  (AMENDMENT)  STATUTE  No.  04/2017
A STATUTE TO AMEND THE FINANCE STATUTE OF NORTHERN PROVINCE, NO. 01 OF 2014
Be it enacted by the Northern Province Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka as follows :
1. This Statute may be cited as the Finance (Amendment) Statute of Northern Province, No. 04
of 2017 and it shall come into operation on such date as the Governor of the Northern Province may give
his Assent thereto.
