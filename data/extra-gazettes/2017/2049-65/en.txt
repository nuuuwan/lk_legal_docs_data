CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
NOTICE  BY  THE  GOVERNOR   OF  THE  CENTRAL  PROVINCE
KNOW Ye that by virtue of powers vested by the Article 154 (b): 8 (b) of the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Niluka Ekanayake, Governor of the Central Province, do by this Notice prorogue
the fifth Session of the Sixth Provincial Council of the Central Province with effect from 08th December Two Thousand
Seventeen.
By virtue of powers vested in me by paragrph 8(a) of the said Article, I do hereby summon the said Council to meet at the
Chamber of the Central Province Provincial Council at Pallekelle, Kundasale on Wednesday the 07th February, Two Thousand
Eighteen at 10.00 a.m.
N
ILUKA  EKANAYAKE,
Governor,
Central Province.
Kandy.
