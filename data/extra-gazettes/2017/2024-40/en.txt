THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Declaration under Section 7 
 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2013/HW/561. 
My Reference No.: මහර/සං/2/මාෙකʣළ/උ/10/1/ඉ.අ. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the  following lands which are required for a 
public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
Lot No. 01 to 06 (Lots 06) allotments in total extent of 
about 0.0193 Hectare, of land in Preliminary Plan No. 
GAM 3984 and dated 26.07.2016, prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the Village of Kirillawala 
and  Minor Division of Adhikari Pattuwa in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mahara in Gampaha 
District in Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 01. 
Name of Land: Ketaliyakumbura; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: Stage; Original Claimant: R. A. 
Samarasena, No. 250, Udupila, Delgoda; Extent: 0.0003 
Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of Land: Ketaliyakumbura; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K. A. D. H. 
Kumarapeli, No. 22, Udupila, Delgoda; Extent: 0.0003 
Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 03 
Name of Land: Ilukpitiyepillewa; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: — ; Extent: 
0.0013 Hectare. 
4. Lot No. 04 
Name of Land: — ; Description: Ranthiye Canal; 
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: — ; Extent: 0.0037 
Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 05 
Name of Land: Bandara Kumbura; Description: 
Paddy Field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K. A. D. 
D. Kumarapeli and K. A. D. H. Kumarapeli, No. 22, 
Udupila, Delgoda; Extent: 0.0100 Hectare. 
 
 
6. Lot No. 06 
Name of Land: Bandara Kumbura; Description: 
Paddy Field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K. A. D. 
D. Kumarapeli and K. A. D. H. Kumarapeli, No. 22, 
Udupila, Delgoda; Extent: 0.0037 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat, at 
Mahara, on the 10th August 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 3rd August 2017, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
Servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.
