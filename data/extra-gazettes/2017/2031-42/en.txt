Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3986.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose.

in the Village of Parawahera in Kokawela Grama Niladhari
Division in Divisional Secretary's Division  of  Matara in
District, Southern Province, have been acquired under village
expansion project.
