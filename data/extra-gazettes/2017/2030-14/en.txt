APPOINTMENTS & C., BY THE GOVERNORS
UVA  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
BY virtue of  Powers vested to me by section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act. No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P . Jayasinghe, Governor
of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mr. H. P . A. G .  Anuruddha, Grade III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of the
Secretary to the Ministry of  Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Women's Affairs and Power
and Energy of the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his duties of permanent post, from the date 16.07.2017
till the return to the Island of  Mr. L. L. Anil Wijesiri, the Secretary to the Ministry of Health, Indigenous
Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Women's Affairs and Power and Energy of the Uva Provincial Council, Who
is abroad from the date 16.07.2017 to 24.07.2017.
2) Mr. R. M. Dayarathne, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, to perform the duties of the Deputy
Chief Secretary (Planning) of the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his duties of  permanent post, from the
date 16.07.2017 till the return to the Island of Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara, the Deputy Chief  Secretary
(Planning) of the Uva Provincial Council, Who is abroad from the date 16.07.2017 to 24.07.2017.
M. P. JAYASINGHE,
Governor of  Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
24th July , 2017.
