FOREIGN  EXCHANGE  ACT  No. 12  OF  2017
Order under Section 1
BY virtue  of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Foreign Exchange Act,  No. 12 of 2017, I , Ranil Wickramasinghe,
Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs, do by this Order appoint November 01, 2017 as the date on which the
aforesaid Act, shall come into operation.
The Order made under Section 1 of the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2036/32 of
September 14, 2017 is hereby revoked.
R
ANIL W ICKRAMASINGHE ,
Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs.
