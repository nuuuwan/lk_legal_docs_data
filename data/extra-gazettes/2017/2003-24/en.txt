EXTRAORDINARY
1A
THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been  named as “ Sri Wachissara Nahimi
Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge
of the Subject of Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter
252) which shall read with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of
1989.
V . K. A. A
NURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07.
Schedule
1. Name of the Local Authority - Colombo Municipal Council
2. District - Colombo
3. Present Name of the Street - The road has not been named
4. New Name of the Street - Sri  Wachissara  Nahimi  Mawatha
5. Road Description - This road is starting from in between the Assessment No. 64
and Assessment No. 68 of Sri Siddhartha Path belonging  to
the  Kirulapone Municipal Ward, ends from in between   the
Assessment No. 66/ 5 and to Assessment No. 68/ 51.
