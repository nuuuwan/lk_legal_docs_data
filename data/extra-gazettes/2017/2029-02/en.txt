No. 2029/2–  MONDAY JULY 24, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
 LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 
28 OF 1964  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  No.: 4/1/5/18. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4/3/4/2012 D/297. 
 
I  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following 
land/servitude which is required for a Public Purpose:  
     Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land called “Heraliyawala watta” in 
total extent of about 10.1175 Hectares, depicted as lots 01 
and 02 in the Preliminary Plan No. Ku 2352 dated 
26.04.2017, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Surveys Kurunegala District, situated in the Village 
called Heraliyawala in Grama Niladhari Division No. 817 
of Heraliyawala in Thiragandahaya Korale West in the 
Divisional Secretary’s  Divison Kurunegala in Kurunegala 
District of the North Western Province and bounded as 
follows. 
 
Schedule 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Heraliyawala watta; Description: 
Home Garden, a mix cultivation of 219 coconut trees of 
about 5-50 years old, 29 teak trees of about 08 -50 years 
old, 14 permanent buildings, a temporary building, a 
foundation, a toilet, 02 water tanks and a cement 
constructed well are available; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 2.7366 
Hectares. 
 
2. Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: Heraliyawala watta; Description: 
Home Garden, a mix cultivation of 723 coconut trees of 
about 5-50 years old, 41 teak trees of about 08 -50 years 
old, 09 jack trees of about 10 -60 years old, 04 permanent 
buildings,  a temporary building and a foundation are 
available; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Land 
Reform Commission; Extent: 7.3809 Hectares. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at  the Kurunegala Divisional 
Secretariat on 13 December 2017 at 9.30 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 30th November 2017 the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount.  
 
  E. M. M. S. EKANAYAKE, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Kurunegala. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kurunegala, 
18th July, 2017.
