No. 943/1  of 2017
P. S. No. : CSA/7/6/2
PROMOTION  AND   APPOINTMENT   APPROVED  BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Major General Nanediri Upendra
Mahesh Magiliyan Weeratunga Senanayake, RWP, RSP, USP, psc to the rank of Lieutenant General with effect from
June 27, 2017 and to appoint him under Article 61E(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka Army, with effect from that date.
                                              By His Excellency’s Command,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
09th August 2017.

PROMOTION  AND   APPOINTMENT   APPROVED  BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Lieutenant General Akuratiya Withanage
Jagath Crisanthe De Silva, RWP, VSV , USP, ndu, psc to the rank of General with effect from June 27, 2017 and to appoint
him as the Chief of Defence Staff in terms of Section 2 of the Chief of Defence Staff Act, No. 35 of 2009, with effect
from that date.
                                              By His Excellency’s Command,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
