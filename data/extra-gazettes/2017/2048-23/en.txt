THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Dinginaidegewatta in total extent of
0.0023 Hectare depicted in Advance Tracing No. GA/GFG/ 2016/
1058 and dated 31.12.2016 prepared by Surveyor General situated
in the Village of Madawalamulla North in the Divisional Secretary's
Division, Four Gravets, Galle in the District of Galle, Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dinginaidegewatta – Extent: 0.0023 Hectare
North: Dinginaidegewatta claimed by Somasiri Kodagoda; East:
Dinginaidegewatta claimed by Somasiri Kodagoda and Dharmaraja
Mawatha; South: Dharmaraja Mawatha and Dinginaidegewatta
claimed by K. A. S. Wickrama; West: Dinginaidegewatta claimed by
K. A. S. Wickrama and Dinginaidegewatta claimed by Somasiri
Kodagoda.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
