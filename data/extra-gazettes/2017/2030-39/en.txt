No. 2030/39 –  THURSDAY, AUGUST 03, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMEND) ACT 
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Reference No.: 3/ඉ/අƮපƮ/2. 
Land Min’s Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/490. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the  following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “Welikanda Mukalana” 
in total extent of about 0.0177 Hectare, depicted as Lot 
No. 145 in Final village plan No. 443, Suppliment No. 5, 
Sheet No. 7 dated 16.11.2016, surveyed and prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated at the village of 
Kiriwanapola, in Kiriwanapola Grama Niladhari Division 
of No. 983, in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Alawwa in Kurunegala District, North Western Province 
and bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 145  
 
Name of the land:  Welikanda Mukalan; Extent: 
0.0177 Hectare. 
Boundaries : 
North : Lot No. 146; East : Lot No. 146; South : Lot 
No. 78; West : Lot No. 39. 
Names and Addres of the claimants : 1. Mr. T. W. 
Senevirathna, No. 160/1, Wettewa Road, Kiriwanapola, 
Maharachchimulla. 2. Mrs. T. W. Chandrawathie, No. 
160/1, Wettewa Road, Kiriwanapola, Maharachchimulla. 
3. Mrs. T. W. Seelawathie Manike, No. 160/1, Wettewa 
Road, Kiriwanapola, Maharachchimulla. 4. Mr. T. W. 
Sanath Dayananda, No. 160/1, Wettewa Road, 
Kiriwanapola, Maharachchimulla. 5. Mrs. T. W. 
Kusumawathie, No. 160/1, Wettewa Road, Kiriwanapola, 
Maharachchimulla. 6. Mrs. T. W. Malani Hemalatha, No. 
160/1, Wettewa Road, Kiriwanapola, Maharachchimulla.   
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands  are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at the Alawwa Divisional Secretary’s 
Office on 06th September 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 12th September 2017 the nature of their 
Interest in the land, the Particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount.  
 
 P. SUSANTHA JAYATHILAKE,
