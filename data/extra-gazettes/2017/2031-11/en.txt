THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of land Containing in extent of about 0.4047
Hectare  as lots ‘A’, ‘C’ and ‘D’ depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
MR/AKR/2015/594 and dated 22.07.2016 Prepared by Senior
Superintendent of Serveys of Matara situated in the Village of Akuressa
in Weligam Korale, Minor Division in Divisional Secretary's Division
of Akuressa, in District of Matara in Southern Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Benwala and Naiwala – Extent : About 0.0006
Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A) and land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed
by L.R.C.; East: Land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed by
L.R.C., Canal and Lot  B; South:  Lot  B; West: Road (R.D.A).
02. Lot ‘C’ - Benwala and Naiwala – Extent : 0.0106 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A) and land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed
by L.R.C.; East: Lot  B; South:  Lot B, Canal and land called Benwala
and Naiwala claimed by L.R.C.; West: Land called Benwala and
Naiwala claimed by L. R. C. and Road (R.D.A).
03. Lot ‘D’ - Benwala and Naiwala – Extent : 0.3935 Hectare
North: Lot  B, Canal and land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed
by L.R.C.; East: Land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed by
L.R. C. ; South:  Land called Benwala and Naiwala claimed by L.R.C.
and Premises of Divisional Secretariat of Akuressa; West: Premises
of Divisional Secretariat of Akuressa, Canal and Lot B.
