PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Uva Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS  the Secretary of the Uva Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under Section 65(1)
of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Uva Province
Provincial Council by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political party, has nominated Mr. Kuda Bandara Medawela
to fill such vacancy;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Kuda Bandara Medawela, elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Moneragala in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
Mahinda Deshapriya - Chairman - Election Commission
N. J. Abeysekere - Member - Election Commission
S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole - Member - Election Commission
12th September, 2017,
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
