L.D.B 9/2011
FINANCE  ACT  No.  11  OF  1963
Order under Section 72
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 72 read with Section 71 of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 1963, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order, vest in the People’s Bank established under the People’s Bank Act,
No. 29 of 1961, with effect  from 31st January two thousand and seventeen, the properties described in the Schedule
hereto.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Colombo,
31st January 2016.
SCHEDULE
1. All that divided and defined allotment of land marked as Lot 3 called as “Udugamage Watta” depicted in Plan No.
1587 dated 29.07.1993 made by Licensed Surveyor D. Rathnayaka, situated at Bathambure in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Bathambure bearing No. 10A in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Rambukkana, in Medde Mediliya
Pattu in Kinigoda Korale in the District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded on the North by Lot
02 on the East by Panuambagahamula Watta and live fence, on The South by Lot 04 and on the West by Divungale
Watta claimed by D. M. Karunarathna and others and Lot 02 in extent of thiry three perches (00A., 0R., 33P.)
together with trees and everything else standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio B 399/48 in the Land Registry, Kegalle.
2. All that divided and defined allotment of land called as “Chempadu” situated at Uduppiddy in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Imayanan bearing No. J/359 in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Vadamarachchi South (Karaveddy)
in the District of Jaffna in Nothern Province and bounded on the North by the land of S. Sivachelvam and his wife,
East by the property of heirs’ of Chinnar Thambimuttu, South by the property of T. Shamugam and on the West by
road and containing in extent three Lachams Varagna Culture and four and quarter Kulees (3ims VC 4 1/4 KIs)
together with the buildings, plantations, well and everything else standing thereon.
This land is registered in folio A 310/75 in the Land Registry, Jaffna.
