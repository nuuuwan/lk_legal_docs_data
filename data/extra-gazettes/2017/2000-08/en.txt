EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,   No.   13 OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order amend Column III of the Schedule of the Order made under that Section and published
in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1999/23 dated 27.12.2016, by the substitution for the words and figures
“Rate specified for the corresponding category in the Gazette Notification No. 1968/19 dated 26.05.2016” of the words
and figures “Rate specified for the corresponding category in the Gazette Notification No. 1941/29 dated 20.11.2015.”
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
02nd January, 2017.
