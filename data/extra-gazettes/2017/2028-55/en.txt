1A  G  25948— 08  (07/2017)
SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25 OF  1973
Orders under Sections 32 and 33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera, the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect, the registration of the Sri Lanka Boxing Association and
the Bridge Federation of Sri Lanka of the National Association/Federation of Sri Lanka Boxing Association and the
Bridge Federation of Sri Lanka and as a interim arrangement, appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardene Tennakoon
Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports Development as competent
authority with effect from 20.07.2017 to continue its functions and further order that the election be convened and held
to elect new office - bearers to the Sri Lanka Boxing Association and the Bridge Federation of Sri Lanka for 2017.
D
AYASIRI JAYASEKERA (Attorney-at-Law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
20th July, 2017.
