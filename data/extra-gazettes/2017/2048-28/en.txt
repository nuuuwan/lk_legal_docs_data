SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Orders under Sections 32 and 33
I,  Dayasiri Jayasekera, the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect, the registration of the Sri Lanka Kabaddi Federation which
is the National Federation for Kabaddi and as an interim arrangement, appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardene
Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports Development as
competent authority with effect from 04.12.2017 to continue its functions and further order to conclude the election
which has already been convened nomination by Sri Lanka Kabaddi Federation in order to elect new office bearers to the
Sri Lanka Kabaddi Federation for 2017.
D
AYASIRI JAYASEKERA (Attorney at Law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
06th December, 2017.
