THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands do
hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The Allotments of land in extent of about 0.0398 Hectare depicted
as Lots  ‘A’ and ‘B’ in Advance Tracing No. H/ CGR/ BLA/ 2014/
601 dated 30.12.2014 prepared by the  Surveyor - General, situated
in the Village of Puwakdandawa in Beliatta Divisional Secretary’s
Division in Hambantota District and 
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Sannasigewaththa - Extent: 0.0084 Hectare
North:Land Acquired for Railway line and land claimed by
K. H. Banduwathi ; East: Lands claimed by  K. H. Banduwathi and
K. A. Piyadasa; South: Lands claimed by K. A. Piyadasa and
G . Alahapperuma and Lot B; West:Lot B and Land Acquired for Rail
way line.
02. Lot ‘B’ - Korigewaththa - Extent: 0.0314 Hectare
North:Land Acquired for Railway line and Lot A; East: Lot A and
Lands claimed by K. A. Piyadasa and G . Alahapperuma; South: Road
and Land claimed by G . Alahapperuma ; West: Land Acquired for
Railway line and Road.
