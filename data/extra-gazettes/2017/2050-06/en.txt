No. 2050/6 – MONDAY, DECEMBER 18, 2017
WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Order made under the Section 4 under the Licensing of Clubs Act
No. 17 of 1957
I, as the Minister in charge of Local Government of Western Province, hereby notify under the powers vested on me under
Section 04 of the Licensing of Clubs Act No. 15 of 1989, that it has been decided the fee to be charged for licenses issued with
regard to each Local authority under Licensing of Clubs Act No. 17 of 1975, related to clubs maintained in the administrative
limits of Municipal Council, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya Sabha of Western Province, shall be in the manner mentioned in
the following schedule.
ISURA DEV APRIY A,
Chief Minister and Minister in charge of
Finance and Planning, Engineering Services,
Law and Order, Local Government and
Provincial Administration,
Economic Development, Power and Energy,
Environmental Affairs, Water Supply and
Drainage and Tourist Affairs of
Western Province.
Chief Minister’s Office,
Western Province,
Srawasthi Mandiraya,
Colombo 07.
11th December, 2017.
