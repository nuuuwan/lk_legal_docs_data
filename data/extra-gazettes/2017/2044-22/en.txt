No. 2044/22  –  TUESDAY NOVEMBER 07, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: ATU/LAN/04/Do 12. 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/15/2015/VR/24. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intend to acquire the following Land/Servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired : 
 
The Land described below depicted in supplementary 
Plan No. 43 of F.V.P. No. 87 prepared by surveyor 
General of Matara situated at Uggashena Village in 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Athuraliya in 
Gangabadapaththu North the District of Matara. 
 
1. Lot No. 696  
Name of the land: Wilpitawaththa Kambi Adiya; 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission, P.O. Box 1526, No. 
G 82, Hector Kobbkaduwa Road, Colombo 07; 
Particulars of the land: Home Garden Road and Bush 
land; Extent 23.1977 Hectares. 
2. Lot No. 697  
Name of the land: Wilpitawaththa Kambi Adiya; 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission, P.O. Box 1526, No. 
G 82, Hector Kobbkaduwa Road, Colombo 07; 
Particulars of the land: Home Garden and mixed 
Cultivation; Extent 0.0568 Hectare. 
   
All Persons interest in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
to appear before me at the Athuraliya Divisional 
Secretary’s on the 05th day of December, 2017 at 10.00 
a.m. and  are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 27th day of November, 2017 the 
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details 
of the computation of such amount.  
 
 
  DEEPIKA K. GUNARATHNA, 
                      (Acting) Divisional Secretary, 
  Athuraliya. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Athuraliya, 
30th October 2017.
