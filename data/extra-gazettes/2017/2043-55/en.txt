REGISTRATION  OF   DEATHS   (TEMPORARY   PROVISION)   ACT,   No.   19   OF   2010
THE regulation made by the Minister under Sub-section (2) of Section 01 of  Registration of Deaths (Temporary Provision)
Act, No. 19 of 2010.
VAJIRA ABEYWARDENA,
Minister of  Home Affairs.
Colombo,
09th October 2017.
Regulation
The given effective period of  operation of the  provisions of registration of Deaths (Temporary Provision) Act,
No. 19 of 2010, will be extended by another two years from 09th of December 2017 to 09th December 2019.
