L.D.B 21/78(XV)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, in relation to the establishing of the Wayamba
University of Sri Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1093/8 of August 17, 1999 (as last amended by Order
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2037/17 of September 21, 2017), by the addition immediately after the entry
“Department of Insurance and Valuation” appearing in Column II,  which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Business
Studies and Finance” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry :-
“Department of English Language Teaching.”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
14th December 2017.
