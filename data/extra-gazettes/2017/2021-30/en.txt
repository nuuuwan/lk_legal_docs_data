No. 2021/30 –  WEDNESDAY, MAY,  31,  2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under  Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act,  as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964  that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
The following block of land which is located within 
the below mentioned boundaries, and this block of land 
constitute 0.0078 Hectares from a distinct portion of 
Moraketiyawaththa depicted as lot number 887 of main 
plan numbered as F.V.P. 299, Supplement No. 7, Sheet 
No. 7, drawn after having being surveyed by Survey 
General, and situated in Kanda Pahala Korale minor 
Division, Gurulupota Grama Niladhari Division, 
Gurulupota village which comes under the purview of 
Divisional Secretary’s Division Minipe, Kandy District in 
Central Province. 
To North : Lot Numbers 872 and 95; To East and 
South : Lot Number 95; To west : Lot Numbers 95 and 
872. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land on which 
a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at the Minipe Divisional Secretariat  on 30th 
August, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 03rd of 
July, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation  for the 
Acquisition of the said land/Servitude* the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
                            
 S. M. C. GUNARATHNA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Minipe.  
Minipe Divisional Secretariat, 
29th May, 2017.
