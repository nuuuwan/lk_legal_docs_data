SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from December 14, 2017, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2044/6 of 06.11.2017 in respect of  Sail Fish (Istiophorus platypterus) and
Marlin Fish (Makaira spp)  classifiable under the HS Code 0303.89.90 and Extraordinary Gazette Notification
No. 2044/25 of 07.11.2017 in respect of Thora Fish (Narrow - barred Spanish mackerel/Scomberomorus commerson)
classifiable under the HS Code 0303.89.90 are hereby resinded with effect from December 14, 2017.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
13th December, 2017.
