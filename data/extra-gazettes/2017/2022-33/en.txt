PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Hon. Governor - Central  Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 154E(5) of the 13th amendment of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, E. M. Niluka Sandaruwani Ekanayake, Governor, Central Province do hereby notified that I made
the following appointments with effect from 2nd May, 2017.
Hon. Thennekoon Mudiyanselage Thilina Bandara Thennekoon
As the Minister of Sports, Youth Affairs, Womens’ Affairs, Rural Development and Industries of the Central
Province.
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 154E(5) of the 13th amendement to the Constitution of
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, E. M. Niluka Sandaruwani Ekanayake, Governor, Central Province do hereby
notified that I made the following appointments with effect from 3rd May, 2017. (According to the Cabinet decisions on
03.05.2017 amendments made to the subjects of the Ministries of the Central Province)
Hon. Sarath Ekanayake
Chief Ministry of the Central Province, Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local Government
and Provincial Administration, Manpower, Education and Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Lands, Co-operative
Development, Trade and Commerce, Food Supplies and Distribution Affairs,  Environmental Affairs and
Investment Co-ordination and the subjects which are not taken under other Ministries.
Hon. M. Rameshwaran
Minister of Agriculture, Minor Irrigation, Animal Production and Development, Agrarian Development, Fisheries,
Hindu Cultural Affairs, Tamil Education (Except Muslim Schools) and Estate Infrastructure Facilities in the
Central Province.
E. M. N
ILUKA SANDARUWANI  EKANAYAKE,
Governor - Central Province.
