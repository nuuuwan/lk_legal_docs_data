FINANCE   BUSINESS   ACT,   No.   42   OF 2011
CANCELLATION of a licence to carry on finance business under Section 37 of the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of
2011.
DR. I NDRAJIT C OOMARASWAMY,
Chairman of the Monetary Board, and
Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
The public is hereby informed that the Monetary Board of Central Bank of Sri Lanka cancelled the licence granted to
Chilaw Finance PLC to carry on finance business under the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011 with effect from
17.04.2017, subsequent to the amalgamation of Chilaw Finance PLC with Richard Pieris Finance Ltd.
