THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed  for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An Allotment of land in total extent of about 1.0120 Hectare,
depicted as Lot 'A' in the Advance Tracing No.
AM/PAD/2015/21 dated 06.04.2015 prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of  Surveyrs - Ampara Distict, situated in the Village
called Padiyathalawa, in the Bintennapattu South Minor Division,
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Padiyathalawa of Ampara
District in Eastern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mahabedda Watta – Extent: 1.0120 Hectare

and Stream (Dry); South:  Stream (dry) and remaining portion of the
same land; West: Remaining portion of the same land and Road (R.
D. A).
