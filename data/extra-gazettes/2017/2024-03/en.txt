SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION OF SRI LANKA ACT, No. 36 OF 1987
Order under Section 14A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (1) of Section 14A of the Securities and Exchange Commission
of Sri Lanka Act, No.  36 of 1987, I Ranil Wickremesinghe, Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs, do
by this Order, specify in the Schedule hereto, the Rate of Cess to be charged and levied under Subsection (1) of
Section 14A of the above Act.
The Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1680/26 of November 19, 2010 pertaining to the Rate
of Cess to be charged and levied, is hereby rescinded.
RANIL  W ICKREMESINGHE,
Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs.
Colombo,
14th June, 2017.
