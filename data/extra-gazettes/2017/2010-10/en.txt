Order No. 43
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order remove the items of the White Sugar and Frozen or Chilled Brolier Chicken
Meat (Whole Chicken) with skin and without skin, from Order No. 40 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2003/44
dated 27.01.2017 and Order No. 39 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1975/68 dated 14.07.2016 respectively.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,

Chairman.
