No. 2025/19 –  TUESDAY, JUNE 27, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. No.: GAL/LAN/AQUIS/A13/19. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/377. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment)  Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land/Servitude 
described in the Schedule given below for a public 
purpose.  
 
 
Description of the Land/Servitude to be  acquired: 
 
 
Allotments of land in extent about 0.0569 Hectare 
depicted in Preliminary Plan No. Ke/3545 dated 
30.05.2017 prepared by Surveyor General, situated in the 
villages of Weragoda in Subdivision of Beligal Korale in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division  of Galigamuwa in the 
District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
Dates for Inquires: 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Lands to be 
acquires are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in written, before me at the 
Divisional Secretariat, Galigamuwa  on  19th July, 2017 
at 10.00 a.m.  
 
And are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(with duplicate) on or before the 12th July, 2017, the 
nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
  
 
 K. G. S. NISHANTHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Galigamuwa. 
 
22nd June, 2017, 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kegalle.
