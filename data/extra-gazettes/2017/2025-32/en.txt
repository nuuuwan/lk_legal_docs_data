No. 2025/32 – THURSDAY, JUNE 29, 2017
PART I : SECTION (I) - GENERAL
Government  Notifications
L. D. B. 21/78(xiv)
UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me  by section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs  of the Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said  Institute have failed
to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Jayantha Walihinda to be the Compe tent Authority with
effect from February 27, 2017 for the purpose of exercising, performing or Discharging in lieu of  the Director  of Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to
the said Director under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, by-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
LAKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Minister of  Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
03rd April, 2017.
