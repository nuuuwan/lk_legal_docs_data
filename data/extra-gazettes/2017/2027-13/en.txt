WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notice   published  under  section 2 (2)  of  the  Entertainment  Tax  Ordinance  No.: 12  of  1946
I, Isura Dewapriya, the Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Economic Promotion, Power and Energy, Environment Activities, Water Supply
and Drainage and Tourism of Western Province, do hereby announce that the decision, dated 16.08.2010 bearing No. 124 of the
Municipal Commissioner of the Colombo Municipal Council, arrived at for the percentage to be charged on the Entrance fee
for entertainment activities declared in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance bearing No. 12 of 1946 amended by the Entertainment
Tax (Amendment) Act bearing No. 37 of 1984, to be charged for the years 2017 and 2018 having imposed as described in the
following schedule as per the following provisions of the Section 2 (1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance, was approved by
me as per the powers vested in me in accordance with the provisions in the Section 2 (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance
bearing No. 12 of 1946 which shall be read with the Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act bearing
No. 12 of 1989.
I
SURA DEW APRIYA,
Chief Minister of Western Province and Provincial
Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineerng Services,
Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration
Economic Promotion, Eletricity and Power, Environment Affairs
Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism Affairs.
Shrawasthri Mandiraya,
