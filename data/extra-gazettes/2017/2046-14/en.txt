EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that rate of Excise duty specified in Column
III for the item No. 2 and 3 specified in Column I of the Schedule II in the Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated
09.11.2017 shall be amended as 30% of the payable duty with effect from 23.11.2017.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
22nd November 2017.
