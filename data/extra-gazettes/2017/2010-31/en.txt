THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under Paragraph (1) of
Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1933/13 of 21st September 2015 and amended from time to time, is further amended with effect from March 01,
2017 in the manner set out in the Schedule below :
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
14th March, 2017.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the Heading, “Minister of Industry and Commerce (No. 20)” of the said notification as follows:
(a) In Column I thereof, by the insertion immediately after item 10 thereof, of the following items ;
“11. Liaison work with Institute of Chartered Accountants ;
12. All other subjects that come under the purview of Organizations listed in Column II ;
13. Supervision of the Organizations listed in Column II”
(b) In Column III thereof, by the insertion immediately after the expression “Sri Lanka Institute of Textiles
and Apparels No. 33 of 1996” of the following item ;
“Institute of Chartered Accountants Act, No. 23 of 1959”
