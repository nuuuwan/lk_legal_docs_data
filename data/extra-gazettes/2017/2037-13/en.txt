NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification made by the Governor of North  Western Province
AMARA Piyaseeli Rathnayaka being the Governor of the North Western Province in terms of power vested in me under
Sub-section (5) of Section 154(f) of Amendment Act, No. 13 of the Constitution of the  Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, appointed the new cabinet of the North Western Province as follows. Accordingly I do hereby proclaim that
Hon. Dasanayake Gunarathne Mudiyanselage Dharmasiri Dasanayake and Hon. Piyasiri Ramanayake were sworn in/affirmed
before me at Colombo on this 15th September 2017 and Hon. R. P. D. Gunadasa Dehigama and Hon. W. J. Sumal Thisera
were sworn in/affirmed before me at Kurunegala on this 19th September, 2017 on the following posts in terms of Section
165 and Section 157 (a) 7 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
01. Hon. Minister Dasanayake Gunarathne
Basnayaka Mudiyanselage
Dharmasiri Dasanayake
02. Hon. Minister Piyasiri Ramanayake
03. Hon. Minister R. P. D. Gunadasa Dehigama
04.Hon. Minister W. J. Sumal Thisera
A
MARA P IYASEELI R A THNAY AKA,
 Governor of the North Western Province.
