THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT  is hereby  notified that the notification issued by me in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) of
Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1933/13 of 21st September, 2015 and amended from time to time, is further amended with effect
from August 29, 2017 in the manner set out in the Schedule below :
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
31st August, 2017.
SCHEDULE

notification, as follows :-
(a) In Column II thereof, by the insertion immediately after Item 17 thereof, of the following item;
18.  Engineering Council, Sri Lanka
(b) In Column III thereof, by the insertion immediately after the expression “Coast Conservation Act,
No. 57 of 1981” of the following Item;
“Engineering Council, Sri Lanka Act, No. 4 of 2017”
