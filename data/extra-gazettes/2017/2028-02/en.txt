PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
GENERAL Public is hereby notified that Annual Esala Festival of Seenigama Sri Devol Maha Devala in Grama Niladhari
Division, Seenigama, Divisional Secretariat Division, Hikkaduwa, Galle District of the Southern Province scheduled to be
held from 24th Thursday to 31st Thursday of August as per the Section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 175)  in
terms of the regulations laid down by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1965/39 dated 06.05.2016 of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
S. T. K
ODIKARA,
District Secretary/Government Agent,
Administrative District, Galle.
At the District Secretariat, Galle,
11th July 2017.
