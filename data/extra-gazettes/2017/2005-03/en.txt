PART 1: SECTION (1) - GENERAL
CIVIL  AVIATION  ACT,  No. 14  OF  2010
ORDER issued by me, as the Minister in charge of Civil Aviation by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 23
Sub-section 1 (a) of the Civil Aviation Act No. 14 of 2010.
NIMAL SIRIPALA DE SILVA (M.P.),
Minister of Transport & Civil Aviation.
Ministry of Transport and Civil Aviation,
Sethsiripaya - Stage II,
Battaramulla.
03rd February , 2017.
Order
(1) This order shall be cited as Batticaloa Airport Protected Area Order No. 01 of 2017.
(2) Each area of land within the meets and bounds specified in the schedule hereto is declared to be Protected Area under
section 23 sub-section 1(a) of the aforementioned Act.
(3) Any construction of a building or erection of any structure or making of any alternation to any existing, building or
other structure within the Protected Area shall conform to the requirements to be specified by the Director General of
Civil Aviation in terms of Section 23 (4)
