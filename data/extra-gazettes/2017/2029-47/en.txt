CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
BY virtue of the power vested in under Sections 171, 173 and 184 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987, read along
with the Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) No. 12 of 1989, it is hereby notified under
Section 184(3) of the said Pradeshiya Sabha Act that the order, approved by the Minister in charge of the subject Local
Government, which was published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
No. 1976/3 and dated 18th July 2016, is hereby seconded by the Central Provincial Council on 20.09.2016, under Sub-
section 184(2) of the said Act, read along with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Secretary,
