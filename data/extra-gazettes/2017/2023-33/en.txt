THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1454/37 of July 22, 2006 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2005/13 of February 08, 2017) which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139
of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after
item “9. Department of Sport Science and Physical Education” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds
to the entry “Faculty of Social Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the following new item :-
 “10. Department of International Studies”.
11. Department of Social Statistics
12. Department of Political Sciences”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
12th June, 2017.
