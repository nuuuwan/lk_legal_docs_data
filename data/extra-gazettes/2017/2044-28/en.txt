PART IV (A) – GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF S RI  LANKA – 08.11.2017
Uva Provincial Council Chater of Library Services Board (Amendment) Act, No. 04 of 2017
A charter design to amend the Uva Provincial Council charter of library services board
Imposed as below by the Uva Provincial Council of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
1. This charter is termed as Uva Provincial Council charter of library services board (Amendment) Act,
No. 04 of 2017. If it is not mentioned in any other way, then, it will be activate from the day Governor of the
Uva province approves it.
 2. Here onwards 2000 number 05 - Uva Provincial Council charter of library services board will be
recognized as the "Main Charter". The new sub paragraph below will be added after 3(A) Sub Paragraph of
Main charter. It will be imposed as the 3 (A) Sub Paragraph of Main charter.
3(A)  Library development and establishment of unity (oneness) within provincial library
services by processors such as Colletive planning to spread Library services, Centralized
and integrated database development, Preservation of library materials, Development of
regional collections and acquisition process, Inter library interchange and loan systems,
Establishment of mobile libraries, Establishement of information networks.
3.    The new sub paragraph below will be added after 6 (A) (I) (a) Sub Paragraph of Main charter. It will be
imposed as the 6 (A) (I) (a) Sub Paragraph of Main charter.
6 (A) (I) (a) Secretary of Provincial Ministry of Cultural Affairs
4. The new sub paragraph below will be added after  6 (A) (I) (a) Sub Paragraph of Main charter. It will
be imposed as the  6 (B) (I) and (II) Sub Paragraphs of Main charter.
6 (B) (I) Chairman who appointed by Education minister should be the in -charge of the
Director Board Meetings. He/she should be the chief Executive officer of the board. It is
his/her responsibility to direct the library services board according to government and
provincial charters.
6 (B) (II) Quorum of the Director Board Meetings should be five members.
 5. If there are any Inconsistencies within Sinhala and Tamil translations of this charter, then, Sinhala
charter wordings should be imposed.
