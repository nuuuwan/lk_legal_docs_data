SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification by the Governor, Sabaragamuwa Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 154C of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, I, Marshall Perera, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, under Sub-section (08) of Section 03 of the Road
Passenger Transport Authority Statute No. 01 of 1994 of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, do by this Order remove
the below mentioned officers from the office of the Chairman/Director of the Sabaragamuwa Province Road Passenger
Transport Authority constituted by the said Statute.
01. Mr. R. D. C. Jayawardene - Chairman
02. Mr. D. P. A. Palitha Wickramasinghe - Member of the Director Board
03. Mr. A. M. Alahakoon Bandara - Member of the Director Board
04. Mr. K. P. P. B. Shaman Pathirana - Member of the Director Board
05. Mr. H. S. Kariyawasam - Member of the Director Board
M
ARSHALL PERERA, PC,
Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
At the Governor's Office of Sabaragamuwa,
Rathnapura,
28th November, 2017.
