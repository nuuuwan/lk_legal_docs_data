PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
A Statute to provide for the transfer of Fines and Panalities imposed by Courts and collected under the Finance Statute of
the Northern Province, Provincial Council No. 01 of 2014 to the Local Authorities in the Northern Province.
Be enacted by the Northern Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
as follows :
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk
1A - PG  3632 — 27  (07/2017)
1. This Statute may be cited as the Tranfer of Court Fines and Penalties Statute of the Northern
Province Provincial Council No. 01 of 2017 and shall come into operation on such date as the
Governor of the Northern Province may give his assent thereto.
2. Where the Provincial Council has collected, in terms of the Finance Statute of the Northern
Province Provincial Council No. 01 of 2014, the fines and panalties imposed by courts situated in
the Northern Province the Provincial Council shall transfer the amount of such fines and penalties
to the relevant Local Authority on a claim, in writing, by the Chief Administrative Officer of such
Local Authority to the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance of the Province, made within
a period of five years from the thirty-first day of December of the year in which such fines and

3. No claim under Section 2 shall be valid unless accompanies with a Schedule of payment of such
fines and penalties specified in the claim specifying the date of such payments, the date of
remittance of such amount by the relevant court to the Provincial Council and the Ordinance or
the Act under which such fines and penalties have been imposed with particular reference to the
Schedule thereof and certified by the Registrar of the Court which imposed and charged such
fines and penalties or any other officer nominated by him on that behalf.
4. In this Statute, unless the context otherwise requires “Chief Administrative Officer” means
(a) In relation to a Municipal Council, the Municipal Commissioner ; and
(b) In relation to an Urban Council or a Pradeshiya Sabha, the Secretary thereof ;
“Local Authority” means any Municipal Council, Urban Council, or Pradeshiya Sabha and
includes any authority created and established by or under any law to exercise,
perform and discharge power, duties and function corresponding to or simillar to
the powers, duties and functions exercised, performed and/or discharged by any such
Council or Sabha ;
“Province” means the Northern Province of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka ;
“Provincial Council” means the Provincial Council of the Northern Province of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Tamil, Sinhala and English texts of this Statute, the
Tamil text shall prevail.
