LAND PARCELS 
 
Registration  of  Title Act, No.  21 of  1998 
(Section 12) 
 
NOTICE No :  42/0478 Kurunegala District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 420380 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Iluppugedara 
(Division No. 10) (Block 01) or any part thereof, situated within 
the Grama Niladhari Division of 838 - Iluppugedara in the Divi-
sional Secretariat Division of Kurunegala in the District of Ku-
runegala in the Province of North Western should submit his 
claim to the undersigned before 03rd  February, 2017 In the event 
of failure  to submit any claim before the above-mentioned date, 
action will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to 
the land and accordingly publish such determination in the Gov-
ernment Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act. 
 
 
The Cadastral Map No. 420380 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Settle-
ment, or the Surveyor - General‚Äôs office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
