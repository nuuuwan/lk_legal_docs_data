THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The Allotment  of land called  ‘Kongahamula watta’’ in total extent
of about 0.0334 Hectare, shown and described in the Advance Tracing
No. KU/MLL/2015/283 dated on 20.08.2015 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated at the Village of Theliyagonna
Mallawapitiya, Grama Niladhari Division in No. 711 in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Mallawapitiya, Kurunegala District, North
Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kongahamula Watta – Extent: About 0.0334
Hectare
  North:  Kongahamula Watta claimed by N. H. Haladeen; East: Ela;
South: Hijra Mawatha; West: Kongahamula Watta claimed by Noor
Sadhika and Kongahamula Watta claimed by Ummu Saleema.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
