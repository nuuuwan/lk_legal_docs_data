CONSULAR  FUNCTIONS  ACT,   No.  4  OF  1981
Notification under Section 8
I, Tilak Marapana, Minister of Foreign Affairs in terms of the powers vested in me under Section 8 of the Consular
Functions Act, No. 4 of 1981, do by this Notification specify that with effect from January 01, 2018 the fees set out in
Column II of the Schedule to this Notification shall be levied in respect of the Consular Functions specified in Column I
of the Schedule hereto, which are performed by the Consular Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Documents issued by the Consular Division Fees
1. Examination Certificates issued by the Department of Examination Rs. 500.00
2. Any Document issued by the Sri Lanka Government for a Foreign National Rs. 1,500.00
3. Any Export Document Rs. 6,000.00
4. Any Other Document Rs. 800.00
