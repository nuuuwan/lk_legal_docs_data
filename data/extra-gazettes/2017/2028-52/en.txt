No. 2028/52 – FRIDAY, JULY 21, 2017
CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Amendment in the Notification published in Gazette No.2001- 1,169 of the Central Provincial
Council by the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri  Lanka
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Developement, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and Construction of the
Central Provincial Council by virtue of powers vested under the paragraph 24 of year 1992 Statute No. 4 of the Passenger
Transport Authority Services, hereby announce that the paragraph 21(2) regarding the powers of the staff mentioned in the
year 2000 No. 01 passenger Transport Statute published in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Gazette on 26th
January 2001 has been amended as follows:–
"Until an approval for a separate Establishment Code and Financial Regulations is obtained for the Passenger Transport
Authority, the Establishment code and the Financial Regulations common to the public service at present will be relevant to all
institutional and financial activities of the Authority".
EDIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Road Developement, Transport, Power and,
Energy and Housing and Construction,
Central Province.
