SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from
02nd April 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
R
AV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
31st March, 2017.
