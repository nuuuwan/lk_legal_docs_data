THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 136)
Notification  under Section 6 (2)
I, W.D.J. Seneviratne, the Minister of  Labour and Trade Union Relations, acting under the provisions of Section 6 (1) of the Wages Board
Ordinance  (chapter 136) read with Section 6 (2) of the said Ordinance, do hereby make order applying  the  provisions of part II of the said
ordinance to the Trade of Marine Transport and Operational Services,  set out in the Schedule hereto.
The requirement under Section 7 of the said ordinance for the publication of the intention to make such order under Section 6(1 ), by
notification published in the Gazette, together with a notice calling for  objections, have been met  with by publishing the same in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1987/37 dated 06th October 2016.
W. D. J. SENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations,
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05.
04th May, 2017.
ORDER
