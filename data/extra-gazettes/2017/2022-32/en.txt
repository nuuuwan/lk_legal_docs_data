NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification of approval of Provincial Council
I do hereby notified that drafted amendments of by Laws indicated in Schedule below, made by the Minister in charge of the
subjects of the Local Government of North Central Province in terms of the Sub section (i) of Section 2 of Local Government
Institution (passed by Laws) Act, No. 06 of 1952 that should be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (incidental provision)
Act, No. 12 of 1989, and published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1960/35 and dated on 30.03.2016 of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, had been approved in the meeting of Provincial Council held on 29.11.2016, under Sub section
(3) of Local Government Institution (passed by Laws) Act, 06 of 1952 that should read with Section 2 of Provincial Council
(incidental provisons) Act, No. 12 of  1989.
Secretary,
Ministry of Local Government of
North Central Provincial Council.
At Chief Ministry,
26th April, 2017.
SCHEDULE
1. By Laws regarding maintenance, regularizing, supervision and control of rice boutique, canteen tea or coffee
shops within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya Sabha.
2. By Laws regarding regularizing, supervision and control of hotel maintenance within the authoritative limit of
Pradeshiya Sabha.
3. By Laws regarding regularizing, supervision and control of public playground within the authoritative limit of
Pradeshiya Sabha.
4. By Laws regarding regularizing, supervision and control of maintenance lodges within the authoritative limit of
Pradeshiya Sabha.
5. By Laws regarding Bakery within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya Sabha.
6. By Laws regarding advertisement boards within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya Sabha.
7. By Laws regarding parking vehicles and running vehicles within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya Sabha.
8. By Laws regarding selling foods and beverages by mobile trades within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya Sabha.
9. By Laws regarding regularizing, supervision and control of fish selling within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya
Sabha.
10. By Laws regarding regularizing, supervision and control of meat selling within the authoritative limit of Pradeshiya
Sabha.
