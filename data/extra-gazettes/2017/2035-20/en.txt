REGISTRATION   OF  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No.  44 OF  1980
IN the exercise of the powers vested by Section 8(1) of the Registration of Electors Act, No. 44 of 1980, the Election
Commission does hereby appoint for the Electoral Districts specified in the Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1967/3 dated 16
May 2016 issued by the Election Commission, following Officers mentioned by their designations in the corresponding
entry in Column III respectively, in addition to the Assistant Registering Officers appointed for each Electoral District as
mentioned in the said Gazette.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA, N. J. A BEYSEKERE, S. R A TNAJEEV AN H. H OOLE,
