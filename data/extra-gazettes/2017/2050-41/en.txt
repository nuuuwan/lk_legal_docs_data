WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Minute of the Western Provincial Public Management Assistants’ Service
Amendment No. 01
THE Minute of the Western Provincial Public Management Assistants’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1898/5 dated 19th January, 2015 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is amended in the following manner.
1. The Section 7.3.2.3 is removed and substituted by the following Section
7.3.2.3 Experience :
Shall have completed active and satisfactory service of at least 05 years in a permanent post in the Western
Provincial Public Service as at the prescribed date, which fact shall be certified by the Head of the Department.
(Clarifications under this section will remain unchanged)
2. The following Section is included as a serial number (iv) at the end of the Sub sections under the Section 7.3.2.5
7.3.2.5  Other
(iv) Shall have completed a satisfactory Service Period of five years immediately preceding the prescribed date.
3. The Section 8.3 is removed and substituted by the following Section
8.3 Authority for conducting Examination: the Commissioner General of Examinations or an institution authorized
by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission.
4. The Section (a) (iii) of the para 10.3.1.1 is removed and substituted by the following Section
(iii) shall be an officer who has obtained a degree from a University recognized by the University Grants Commission
and has completed an active and satisfactory service period of five years in the service.

5. The para 10.3.1.2 is removed and substituted by the following para 10.3.1.2 Method of Promotion
Appointments equivalent to 50% of the total vacancies in Supra Grade shall be made on the results of the limited
competitive examination. Candidates should obtain at least 40 marks for each subject relevant to the examination
for passing the limited competitive examination. The candidates who have satisfied the qualifications shall be
appointed after verifying their qualifications by an interview board appointed by the Western Provincial Public
Service Commission. The interview shall strictly be for examination of certificates by which the qualifications of the
candidates are proved and no marks shall be allocated in this regard.
(Syllabus and the marking scheme is given in annex 09).
6. The para 10.3.2.2 is removed and substituted by the following para 10.3.2.2 Method of Promotion
Appointments equivalent to 50% of the total vacancies in Supra Grade shall be made on the basis of merit. For this
purpose candidates should pass an Aptitude Test conducted by the Commissioner General of Examinations on
behalf of the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, obtaining at least 40 marks for each subject. Priority
shall be determined on the results of the said examination and on the order of the aggregate of marks allocated on
the basis of seniority and experience by the board appointed by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission.
Candidates shall appear for an interview held by a board appointed by the Western Provincial Public Service
Commission for verification of qualifications. No marks shall be allocated at that interview. (Annex 10)
7. The Note under the Annex 04 is replaced by the following part
Note 01
Officers who have satisfied the following requirements of the Computer Test relevant Efficiency Bar Examination of
Grade III of the Western Provincial Public Management Assistants’ Service shall be exempted.
01. Officers who have followed computer courses offered by the following institutions, of which the syllabus is
similar to the computer course indicated in the Service Minute.
a. Sri Lanka Institute of Development Administration
b. National Apprentice and Industrial Training Authority
c. V ocational Training Authority of Sri Lanka
d. Courses conducted by Technical Colleges and Technological Colleges which function under the Department
of Technical Education and Training.
e. Universities or other institutions recognized by the University Grants Commission
f. National Youth Council.
02. Officers who have obtained a degree in Computer Science from an university recognized by the University
Grants Commission or officers who have followed Computer Science as a main subject for the degree.
Note 02
Officers who have satisfied the qualifications indicated under the Note in the Annex 04 of the Minute of the
Western Provincial Public Management Assistants’ Service No. 1898/5 dated 19.01.2015 shall also be exempted
from the Computer Test for a period of two years from the date on which the Gazette containing these revisions is
published.
