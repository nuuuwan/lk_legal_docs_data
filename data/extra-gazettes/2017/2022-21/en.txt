LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Ref. No.: NCP/MG/L02/General/07-04. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2010/IR/201 dated 
12.06.2015. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the under-mentioned lands which are required for 
the purpose of construction of the Medirigiriya Kantale 
Road under the Moragahakanda, Kaluganga Development 
Project. 
 
Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
 The allotment of land described below as shown in the 
Plan in the Sheet No. 02 in the Zone No. 04 in the C.P. 
No. 120059 of the Surveyor General located in the 
Village of Etambaoya in the No. 98, Etambaoya Grama 
Niladhari’s Division in the Minor Division of Sinhala 
Pattuwa in the Medirigiriya Divisional Secretary’s 
Division in the District of Polonnaruwa in the North 
Central Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 91 
Name of land: Etambaoya Paddy field; Claimant: 
Government, (Encroacher, Puskala Mankada Gedara 
Rathnayaka Mudiyanselage Bala Menike of No. 324, 
Yaya 04, Stage II, New Town, Medirigiriya); Extent: 
0.0035 Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Medirigiriya 
Divisional Secretariat on the 07.07.2017 at 9.00 a.m. 
 
You are hereby requested to notify to me in writing in 
duplicate on or before 30.06.2017, the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of  such amount. 
 
 Divisional Secretary. 
 Medirigiriya. 
 
 
 
Medirigiriya Divisional Secretariat, 
01st June, 2017.
