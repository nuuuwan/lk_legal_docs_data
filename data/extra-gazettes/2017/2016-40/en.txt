CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,   No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
26th April 2017.
SCHEDULE
The land called Punchinadungolla situated in the Village of Weralugampola in Grama Niladari’s Division of 392A
Weralugampola in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Dompe, in the Gangaboda Pattu of Siyane Korale, in the
Administrative District of Gampaha, in the Province of Western of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 80 perches shown in
the rough sketch No. CEB/NTDND&EIP/P2/L1/L2 dated 07.03.2017 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board, for 20ft
wide access road bounded as follows :-
North : Land claimed by Saman Weerakkody, K. K. A. Ariyadasa and Padmini Lokubalasuriya ;
East : Land claimed by Padmini Lokubalasuriya and Ela ;
South : Land claimed by Padmini Lokubalasuriya, A. M. S. Gamini and Mahahena Papiliyawala Road ;
West : Land claimed by Padmini Lokubalasuriya, Aruna Ariyawansa and K. K. A. Ariyadasa.
Claimed by : Mrs. Padmini Lokubalasuriya, No. 910/10/J, Adikaram Watta, Ethul Kotte.
Mr. Aruna Ariyawansa, No. 254/1, Weralugampola, Pepiliyawala.
