THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act , that the lands described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An  allotment of land called Kurulugalawatta Name of the land
situated at Mathugobe Village (F.V .P . 169) of Morawak Korale Minor
Division in the Divisional Secretariat, Kotapola of Matara District
in the Southern Province, depicted as lot ' A' in Survey General's
Tracing No. MR/KTP/2009/133 and dated 30.06.2009, containing
in extent about 369.1898 Hectares and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kurulugalawatta or Punchi Kurulugalawatta or
Kosgulanahena – Extent:  369.1898 Hectares
North: Kahatagaha Dola and Boundary of Sabaragamuwa
Province; East:Boundary of Sabaragamuwa Province, Vihara Dola
Ensalwatta which is claiming over by Mathurata Plantation Company
Ltd; South: Vihara Dola and Kurulugalawatta which is claiming over
by Mathurata Plantaion Company Ltd. and others; West:
Kurulugalawatta which is claiming over by Mathurata Plantaion
Company Ltd. and others, Nana Dola and Kahatagaha Dola.
