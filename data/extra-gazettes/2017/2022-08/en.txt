SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby make an
order to waive off Rs. 25 per kg. from the applied Special Commodity Levy of 10% or Rs. 75 per kg. on the importation of fish,
classifiable under the HS Headings of 03.02 and 03.03, listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the Extraordinay
Gazette Notification No. 2018/2 of 08th May 2017.
In terms of this Order, a Special Commodity Levy of 10% or Rs. 50 per kg. on importation of fish shall be
collected for a period of 30 days effective from 07th June 2017.
M
ANGALA S AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
