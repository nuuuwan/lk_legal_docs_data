No. 2046/9 – TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 21, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘Halwathurawatta’ in extent of
about 1.234 Hectares depicted as Lot ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing
No. KA/BLT/01/208 and dated 28.05.2001, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Halwathura in the Minor
Division of Gangaboda Pattuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Bulathsinhala in Kalutara District in Western Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Halwathurawatta – Extent: About 1.234 Hectares
North: Halwathurawatta claimed by Horana Plantation Company;
East, South and West: Estate Road.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
