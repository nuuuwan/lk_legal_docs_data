No. 2004/25 – MONDAY, JANUARY 30, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘Narankaduwawatta’,  in total extent of
about 0.0539 Hectare depicted as Lot 'A' in Advance Tracing
No. Maha/PTH/2015/554 dated 15.03.2015 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Kadakula, in Minor Division of
Gamdahaya Korale in Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Pathahewaheta in the District of Kandy in Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Narankaduwa Watta – Extent: About 0.0539
Hectare
North: Narankaduwawatta claimed by L. A. D. K. Gunathilake
and Muthuwa Wedage Idama claimed by W. S. M. Rambanda; East:
Muthuwa Wedage Idama claimed by W. S. M. Rambanda; South:
Batapandure Hena claimed by Punchirala Wedarala and
Narankaduwawatta claimed by Pradeshiya Sabha, Pathahewaheta;
West: Narankaduwawatta claimed by Pradeshiya Sabha,
Pathahewaheta and Road.
John  A. E. Amarathunga,
