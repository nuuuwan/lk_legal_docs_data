THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: Rambu/551.
Ministry of Land Reference No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/07.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to    acquire the following land/ servitude* which is required for a
public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Under mentioned land shown in the surveyor General’s plan of
F. V . P . 141, Supplement No. 2, Sheet No. 2, Kawattiya situated in
the village of  No 3, Naththambura Grama Niladari Division,
Kinigoda Korale Minor Division, Rambukkana Divisional
Secretary’s Division in Kegalle District Sabaragamuwa Province.
1. Lot No. 68
Name of the Land: Ganimehena; Description: Garden;  Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Piyal Jayasooriya and others;
Extent: 0.0257 Hectares.
2. Lot No. 69
Name of the Land: Welikarehena; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: W. A. Podinilame;
Extent: 0.0864 Hectares.
3. Lot No. 70
Name of the Land: Ganimehena alise  Welikarehena;
Description: Garden; Claimant: Government; Original Claimant:
P . K. Wickramasingha; Extent: 0.1058 Hectares.
4. Lot No. 71
Name of the Land: Welikarehena; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Government; Original Claimant: P . D. N.
Jayasingha; Extent: 0.2195 Hectares.
5. Lot No. 72
Name of the Land: Welikarehena; Description: Garden;
Claimant: Government: Original Claimant: W. A.
Podinilame; Extent: 0.0101 Hectares.
All persons interested in aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Rambukkana Divisional Secretariat,
on the day of the 8th February , 2017, at 10.00 a.m. and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the 1st February, 2017 the nature of their
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for com-
putation for the said land/servitude the amount of com-
pensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
