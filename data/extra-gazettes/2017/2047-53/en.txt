SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments Made by the  Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Article154C of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, it is hereby notified that  I, Marshall Perera, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, under the
Section (3) of the part I of the Establishment of  the Development Construction and Machinery Authority  of the
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council  Statute No. 02 of  2009 of  the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council, appointed the
following persons as  Chairman/ Member of  the Director Board of  the  Sabaragamuwa Province  Development Construction
and Machinery Authority which is established by the said Statute, with effect from 30.11.2017.
01. Mr. P. Sunil Premachandra - Chairman
02. Mrs. Sriyani Padmalatha - Member of the Director Board
03. Mr. J. M. D. Wijayaraja - Member of the Director Board
04. Mr. A. M. Gnanasena - Member of the Director Board
05. Mr. A. D. K. A. W eerarathna - Member of the Director Board
06. Mr. Parakrama Piyasena - Member of the Director Board
07. Mr. P. Wijesighe - Member of the Director Board
M
ARSHALL PERERA, PC,
Governor,
Sabaragamuwa Province.
At the Governor’s Office,
Ratnapura,
30th November 2017.
