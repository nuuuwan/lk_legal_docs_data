I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section (7) of
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acqusition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The alloments of land depicted in the preliminary plan
No.K/3514 prepared by the  Surveyor General, within the
boundaries shown below situated in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. 9B  Yodagama in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Rambukkana  in  the  District  of  Kegalle in
Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Dodamgastenna; Land use; Estate; Claimant:
Government; First Claimant: N.S.J.S. Karunarathna, Y odagama,
Pinnawala and I. Prasanna Gunathillake, Wagolla,
Rambukkana; Extent: 0.6454 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in  writing , before me at Rambukkana Divisional Secretariat
on 26/07/2017, at 10.00 a.m.
Further, you are hereby requested to nitify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before 07/07/2017, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount
of compensation and details of computation of such amount.
