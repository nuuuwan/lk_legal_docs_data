Regulations
The Coast Conservation Regulations No. 1 of 1982 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 260/22 of September 02,
1983, as amended from time to time, are hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) by the substitution for regulation 7 thereof, of the following regulation :-
“7. Every application for a renewal of a permit under Section 18 of the Act shall be in “Form B” accompanied by
applicable fees set out in form  “D” in the Schedule hereto.” ;
(2) by the substitution for regulation 8 thereof, of the following regulation :-
“8. Every request for a transfer of a permit under Section 18 of this Act shall be submitted accompanied by
applicable fees set out in form “E” in the Schedule hereto.” ;
