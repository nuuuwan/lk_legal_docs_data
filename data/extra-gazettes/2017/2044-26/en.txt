SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at
the rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from November 09, 2017, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
The Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification  No. 2028/44 of 19.07.2017 in respect of Sprats classifiable under the HS Code
0305.59.20, Gazette Notification  No. 2039/2 of 02.10.2017 in respect of Dried Fish classifiable under  the HS Code
0305.59.90, Gazette Notification  No. 2032/24 of 16.08.2017 in respect of Potatoes, B’Onion and Lentils classifiable
under the HS Code 0701.90, 0703.10.20, 0713.40.11, 0713.40.12, 0713.40.21, 0713.40.22 and Gazette Notification
No. 2033/31 of 23.08.2017 in respect of Vegetable oils classifiable under the HS Headings 15.07, 15.11, 15.12 and
15.13 are rescinded with effect from November 09, 2017.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
08th November, 2017.
