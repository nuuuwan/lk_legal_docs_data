THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 
NO. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/MDB/4/2/1/64/15. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: L/03/J95/FR/191. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section   (1) of Section of 
the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, (Amendment) 
that the Government intend to acquire the following Land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Purpose of Acquisition: Knuckles Range Reservation : 
 
An allotment of lad called Angurupolawatta alias 
Angurukilowatta in total extent of about 0.1621 Hectares 
depicted as Lot  No. 01 in the preliminary  Plan No. Maha 
6719 Dated 29.09.2017 prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Survey’s - Kandy District, situated in 
the Village called  Mangoda in the Grama Niladhari 
Division  No 806 of Mangoda in the Udasiyapattuwa 
South Korale Minor Division in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Medadumbara in Kandy District 
of the Central Province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the land: Angurupolawatta alias Angurukolawatta; 
Claimant:W.R.M.Ukkuban da(Passed),Mangoda, 
Medhamahanuwara; Description: Scrub; Extent0.1621 Hectare. 
 
 All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/Servitude 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Medadumbara 
Divisional Secretariate on the 21st day of February, 2018 at 
02.00 p.m. and  are hereby requested notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13th day of 
February, 2018 the nature of their interest in the aforesaid 
land/Servitude the particulars of their claims of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
  A. M. WICKRAMARACHCHI, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
  Medadumbara. 
 
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Medadumbara, 
24th November, 2017.
