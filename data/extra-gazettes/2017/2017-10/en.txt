MICROFINANCE   ACT,   No.   6   OF 2016
Notice on deadline for an application  for a licence
THE Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka acting in terms of  section 33(b)(i) of the Microfinance Act,  No. 6 of
2016 hereby specifies that any person who has carried on microfinance business on 14th July 2016 i. e. the day preceding the
date on which the said Act came into operation and continues to carry on such business todate as permitted in section 33(a)
of the said Act shall comply with the provisions of the said Act and apply for a licence to continue to carry on the
microfinance business as a licensed microfinance company, on or before 30th September 2017.
