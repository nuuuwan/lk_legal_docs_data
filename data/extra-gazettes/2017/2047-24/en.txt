1A – G 26705 – 09 (2017/11)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilleke, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotments of land depicted in Plan No. Maha/UDN/2016/104
and dated 08.12.2016, prepared by the Surveyor - General, situated
in the Village of Bambaradeniya in the Minor  Division of
Medapalatha korale in the Udunuwara Divisional Secretary’s Division
in Kandy District in the Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Meegahakotuwawatte – Extent: 0.0053 Hectare
North: Meegahakotuwa watte claimed by J. M. C. Jayasundara
and Gedarakumbura claimed by A. B. Jayasundara; East:
Gedarakumbura claimed by A. B. Jayasundara and Gedarakumbura
vanatha claimed by T. B. Jayasundara; South: Gedarakumbura vanatha
(R.D.A.), Meegahakotuwawatte claimed by J. M. C. Jayasundara
and Road; West: Road (R.D.A.), Meegahakotuwawatte claimed by
J. M. C. Jayasooriya and Road (Rural road).
