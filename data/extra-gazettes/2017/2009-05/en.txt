PART 1: SECTION (1) - GENERAL
.
SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY ACT, No. 48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of list of the commodities
specified in Column 1 of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in
column 11 of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from 08th March 2017, subject to
provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1984/26 of 15.09.2016 in respect of Sugar (Other/Raw, HS Code Nos. 1701.12, 1701.13, 1701.14, 1701.91.10, 1701.91.90,
1701.99.30,1701.99.90) are rescinded with effect from 8th March, 2017.
RAVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 1,
