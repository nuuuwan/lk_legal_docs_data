L.D.B. 1/2012(III)
THE   FISHERIES   AND  AQUATIC   RESOURCES  ACT,  No.   2  OF  1996
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development under Section 61 read with
Section 29 and Section 61(1)(u) of the Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Act, No. 2 of 1996.
MAHINDA  A MARAWEERA,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic
Resources Development.
Colombo,
05th April, 2017.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as Prohibition of Catching Fish Species Thambuwa ( cephalopholis sonnerati )
Regulations 2017.
2. No person shall catch, possess, transport, purchase, exhibit for sale, sell or export the fish species Thambuwa,
cephalopholis sonnerati within Sri Lanka or Sri Lanka Waters.
