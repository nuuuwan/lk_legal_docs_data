COMMUNITY  BASED  CORRECTION  ACT,  No.  46  OF 1999
Order under the Sub Section (3) of Section 1
BY virtue of power vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 1 of Community Based Correction Act, No. 46 of 1999,
I. D. M. Swaminathan, Minister of Prison Reforms, Rehabilitation, Resettlement and Hindu Religious Affairs do hereby
order 22.05.2017 as the date on which the provisions of Part II of this Act with respect to the judicial divisions clearly
specified on following Schedule shall come into operation.
D. M. S
W AMINATHAN,
Minister of Prison Reforms, Rehabilitation,
Resettlement and Hindu Religious Affairs.
Colombo,
08th March, 2017.
