THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No. : UDU/2/2/10/71.
Land Ministry No. : 13/97/FR/65.
I hereby give Notice, under sub section (1) of section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No.28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/ servitude for public purpose
(the conservation of Dumbara mountain range.)
Description of the land  servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land called ''Koboneela Watta"bearing an
extent of about 21.2508 Hectares mentioned in F. V . P . 504 Sup.
9 Sheet 9 (lot number 192, 193,194,197), Supplement of the
surveyor Genaral, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Kobonilla, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province.
Name and address of Claimants
Mr. Niyomal Rajkumar,
No. 272,
Madawala,
Katugastota.
PRINTED A T THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/ servitude on
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 17th
May 2017 at 10.00 a.m and hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th May 2017 nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Udadumbara.
