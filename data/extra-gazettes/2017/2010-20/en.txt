Regulations Under Section 101 of The Customs Ordinance
(Chapter 235)
DISPOSAL OF MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENT IMPORTED UNDER EXEMPTION OF CUSTOMS IMPORT DUTY
ON CONDITIONAL BASIS BY ENTERPRISES IN THE BUSINESS OF MANUFACTURING AND EXPORTING
APPAREL
IN pursuance to the powers vested in me by Section 101 (1) (h) of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby promulgate regulations to
determine the Customs value for disposal of machinery or equipment, imported under an approved scheme of exemption of
Customs import duty on conditional basis by an enterprise, engaged in the business of manufacturing and exporting apparel.
2. The Customs value, for the disposal of Machinery or equipment, listed out in Coloumn IV and classifiable under the HS
Codes, listed out in Coloumn II of the Schedule I to these Regulations shall be determined as ''zero value'', if such machinery
or equipment are;
(i) imported by an enterprise, engaged in the business of manufacturing and exporting apparel under an a pproved
scheme of exemption of Customs import duty on conditional basis; and
(ii) used for more than ten (10) years from the date of importation of the same where the period of usage should be certified
by the approving authority.
3. The approving authority referred to in 2 (ii) above for the certification of the period of usage shall be the Director General
of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka in respect of an enterprise, established under Section 17 of the Board of Investment
Act No. 4 of 1978 as amended and engaged in the business of manugacturing and exporting apparel, and  the Director
General of Customs in respect of an apparel enterprise, established under the Temporary Imports for Export Purposes
(TIEP) Scheme and engaged in the business of manuracturing and exporting apparel.
4. The date of imporation, for the purpose of these regulations, shall be the date of the bill of entry (Date of the CUSDEC) of
the relevant machinery or equipment.
