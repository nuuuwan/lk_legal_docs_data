ANNUAL  PERAHARA  OF  BADULLA   MUTHIYANGANA    RAJAMAHA    VIHARAYA
IN accordance with the powers vested in me by Gazette Notifications No. 9902 of 10.09.1948, No. 10283 of 17.08.1951
and No. 14778 of 25.03.1955 published under Chapter 362 of the Pilgrimage Act, and under the powers conferred on
Divisional Secretaries by Act, No. 58 of 1992, and according to the constituion published in No. 1710 Gazette Notification
of 10.06.2011 on Annual Perahara of Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya, I, P. S. P. Abeywardhana, the Divisional
Secretary of Badulla do hereby make the following orders in connection with the Annual Perahara of Badulla Muthiyangana
Rajamaha Viharaya, 2017.
01. I do hereby notice that, the Annual Perahara of Badulla Muthiyangana shall begin with Inner Perahara on 09th of
May 2017 and proceed along the streets on 10th and 11th of May 2017, and end after having returned to the
Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
02. Route of the Perahara
Inner Perahara ceremony on 9th May 2017
The Perahara shall start from Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya premises on 10 th May 2017 and proceed
along Station Road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Lower Street, King’s Street, and downwards from Library
Mawatha and back to Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
The Perahara shall start from Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya premises on 11th May 2017 and proceed
along Station Road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Cocowatte Road, Udayaraja Mawatha, King’s Street, and
from Bazaar Street to Lower Street and back to Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.
03. Cooking and selling articles of trade are prohibited in and around Badulla Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya
compound.
04. Private vehicles are prohibited to park in the main roads during the Perahara Season. All types of vehicles,
including bicycles and carts are prohibited to enter the Vihara area without a permit issued by me or the Trustee
Chief incumbent of Muthiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya.

05. The permanent buildings and the buildings currently being constructed along the Perahara route under a formal
approval given by Badulla Municipal Council must conform to the instructions of the Health Department. At the
end of the Perahara, after having dismantled the temporary sheds, the lesses shall dispose of all polythene and
other garbage lying along the route of Perahara and Viharaya premises as instructed by the Public Health
Inspectors.
06. Construction of any temporary hut or building in and around the sacred area shall not be done without a permit
obtained from me thereto.
07. No person shall construct any temporary hut or building of any form, or effect any extensions to any existing
building on a street or on a land reserved for a street or in the sacred  area without a permit obtained from me or
from the Badulla Municipal Council.
08. Burning of crackers within the area is prohibited.
09. No person shall engage in selling tickets, flags or engage in any other task meant for collecting subscriptions,
within the camping site indicated in clause No. 03, without the prior approval of an Authorized Officer of
Divisional Secretary.
P. S. P . A
BEYWARDHANA,

Badulla.
