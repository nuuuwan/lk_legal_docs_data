PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
Filling of a Vacancy in the Central Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS  the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed the Election Commission under
Section 65(1) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occured in the membership of
the Central Province Provincial Council by reason of Death;
And whereas the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political Party to which the member
belonged was required by the Election Commission under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible
person to fill such vacancy;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized Political Party, has nominated Mr. Asoka Priyantha Bandara
Herath Herath Mudiyanselage to fill such vacancy;
The Election Commission, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of
1988, read with the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do
hereby declare Mr. Asoka Priyantha Bandara Herath Herath Mudiyanselage, elected as a member of the above Provincial
Council from the Administrative District of Nuwara-Eliya in respect of which such vacancy occured.
Mahinda Deshapriya - Chairman - Election Commission
N. J. Abeysekere - Member - Election Commission
S. Ratnajeevan, H. Hoole - Member - Election Commission
29th March, 2017,
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
