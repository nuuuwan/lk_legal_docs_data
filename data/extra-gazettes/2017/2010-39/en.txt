PROCLAMATION   BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY  THE
PRESIDENT  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA
WHEREAS, His Excellency the President has, by Notification published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2010/38 of 17th
March, 2017, been pleased to confer National Honours on the several persons named therein :
 Know ye that His Excellency the President will personally confer these Honours on the recipients at a Ceremony
of Investiture to be held at Nelum Pokuna Theatre on 20th March, 2017.
                                                                                   By His Excellency's Command,
P. B. ABEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
