Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Open University of Sri Lanka (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 02 of 2017 and
shall come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Open University of Sri Lanka Ordinance, No. 1 of 1990, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 591/18
of January 05, 1990 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1918/8 of June 09, 2015 is
hereby further amended in paragraph 16, of the Ordinance as follows :-
(a) in sub-paragraph (3) thereof, by the appeal of item (iv) and the substitution therefor of the following
items :-
“(iv) Mathematics ;
(v) Computer Science ; and
(b) by the renumbering of items (v), (vi) and (vii) in sub-paragraph (3) thereof as items (vi), (vii) and (viii) of
that sub-paragraph respectively.
