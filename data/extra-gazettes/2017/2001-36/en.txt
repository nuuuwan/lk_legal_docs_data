THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land in total extent of about 0.0377 Hectare  depicted
as Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. GAM/KATA/2016/161 and dated
18.06.2016, prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in village called
Kimbulapitiya in the Grama Niladhari  Division of  92C, Kimbulapitiya
Central in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Katana in Gampaha
District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pihimbiyagara – Extent: 0.0377 Hectare
North: Air Port of Sri Lanka,  name not known land claimed by Air
Port and Aviation Services Ltd. and  name not known land claimed by
Kelesta Fernando; East: Name not known land claimed by Kelesta
Fernando; South: Road and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.),
Air Port of Sri Lanka and name not known land claimed by Air Port
and Aviation Services Ltd.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2015/A V/206.
"Mihikatha Medura"
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
16th November, 2016.
