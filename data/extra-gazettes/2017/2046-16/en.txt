THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
No. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
                                        Ref. No. :  DEV/LND/ACQUI/04-8.
           Land Ministry Ref. No. :  4/3/15/2012/VE/158.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisiton
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude which is required for a
public purpose mentioned below.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotments of lands bearing Lot 01 (extent : 0.8215 Hec.)
depicted in the preliminary survey plan bearing No. MR/2586,
situated in the village of Naotunna, Grama Niladari Division of
Naotunna in Divisional Secretary's Division of Devinuwara,
Matara District, Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Kandagehena; Description: Home Garden, 7
coconut trees aged about 30-40 years, 1 mango tree aged
about 10-15 years, 3 teak trees aged about 10 years, 1 teak tree
aged about 15-20 years, 1 jak tree aged about 10-15 years and
cultivation of mixed crop; Extent: 0.8215 Hectare.
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid Land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat  of Devinuwara on the  15.12.2017 at
10.00 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 29.11.2017 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said  land/Servitude,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
RANJITH YAPA,
Divisional Secretary /Acquistion  Officer,
Devinuwara.
Divisional  Secretariat,
Devinuwara,
25th October, 2017.
