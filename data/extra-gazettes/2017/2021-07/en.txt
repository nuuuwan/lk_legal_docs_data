SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, a Special Commodity Levy at the
rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid from 31st May 2017 to 31st August 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of
Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
30th May, 2017.
