APPOINTMENT   MADE  BY  THE  PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that his Excellency the President has appointed Mrs. R. H. C. K. Ramasinghe, Grade III of the Sri
Lanka Planning Service, to the President’s Staff as an Assistant Director with effect from 12.06.2017 in terms of the
provisions of Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
                                          By order of His Excellency,
R
OHANA KEERTHI D ISSANAYAKE,
Senior Additional Secretary to the President,
(Human Resources Development),
For Secretary to the President.

Colombo 01,
October 09, 2017.
