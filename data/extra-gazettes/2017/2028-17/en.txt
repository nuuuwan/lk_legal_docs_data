ORDER  UNDER   SECTION   22(a)   OF
GRATER   COLOMBO   ECONOMIC   COMMISSION  LAW   (BOARD   OF  INVESTMENT  LAW)
No. 04  OF  1978  AS  AMENDED  BY  ACT  No.  43  OF  1980
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 22 (a) of Grater Colombo Economic Commission Law (Board of Investment
Law) No. 04 of 1978 as amended by Act, No. 43 of 1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, upon the recommendation of the Minister - in - Charge of the subject of development strategies and international
trade hereby declare areas described in the Schedule below to be a licensed zones for the purpose of  this Act.
                                              MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
                                             President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
17th of July 2017.
Schedule 1
All that allotment of divided and defined of lands depicted in as Lot Nos. 1, 2 and 3 of Plan No. P. P. Maha. 5345 dated 28th
June, 2002 made by Mr. K. Kathatharan Government Surveyor in respect of a land situated in the Village called Deiyannewela
in Word No. 17, Kotugodella within the Municipal Limits of Kandy, Minor Division of Gangawata Korale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in the District of Kandy in Central Province which lands are bounded
as follows and containing in extent of 5.0835 Hectares.
The said lands are bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 - Name of the land : Bogambara Prison land; Extent : 3.3674 Hectares.
 North: Hiragedara Mawatha and Mosque Road; East: Rajapihilla Mawatha and Road (RDA);  South: Road (RDA) and
Ehelepola Kumarihami Mawatha;  West: Keppettipola Road and Hiragedara Mawatha.
