ORDER  UNDER  SUB  -  SECTION  (4)  OF  SECTION  27  A  OF
THE  LAND  REFORM ACT  NO.  1  OF  1972  OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 A (4) of the Land Reform Act No. 1 of 1972 amended by the Land
Reform (Special provisions ) Act, No. 39 of 1981 of the National State Assembly, I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands, do hereby,
(a) Amend the Order by way of revocation of the order made on the portion of land in relation to the extent of land of the
land called  "Harepark Estate (Dehigolla Division)" depicted in the Second Schedule of the Order made under section
27 A (1) of the Land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972, Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 195/ 6 dated 31
st  May
1982 in respect of the Land called and mentioned as Harepark Estate (Dehigolla Division), which has been alienated
to Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation established under Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation  Act, No. 04 of
1958 and depicted comprehensively in the First Schedule of this order, due to not following the Order or conditions
of the agreement made for this alienation of the Land to Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation.
(b) It is hereby declared that the extent of Land depicted specifically in  Second Schedule of  this order is alienated again
to the Land Reform Commission as a result of this revocation.
John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
Ministry of Lands,
Land Secretariat,
‘‘Mihikatha Medura’’,
No. 1200/ 6, Rajamalwatta Avenue,
Battaramulla.
05th April, 2017.
