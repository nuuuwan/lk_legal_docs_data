THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)
ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
My  Reference No: 4/1/5/23.
Land Ministry No : 4-3/9/2015/WS/11.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
The allotment of land called "Dangahamula Kumbura (Now
Garden) Assessment No. 65/1, Pahala Gattuwana Road" in
total extent of about 0.0153 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 1 in
Preliminary Plan No. Ku 2348, dated 06.12.2016, surveyed and
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at the village of
Gattuwana, (Ward No. 7 within the Municipal Council Limit,
Kurunegala) in Kurunegala Town East Grama Niladhari
Division of No. 835 in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Kurunegala in Kurunegala District, North Western Province
and bounded as follows.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Dangahamula Kumbura (Now Garden)
Assessment No. 65/1 Pahala Gattuwana Road; Extent: 0.0153
Hectare.
Boundaries:
North: Paddy land claimed by Julian Appuhami; East: Road;
South: Garden claimed by Athugalapura Pathiranage Nimali
Senanayake Assessment No. 65/1 Pahala Gattuwana Road;
West: Garden claimed by Athugalapura Pathiranage Nimali
Senanayake Assessment No. 65/1 Pahala Gattuwana Road.
1. Mrs. Athugalapura Pathiranage Nimali Senanayake, No.
65/1, Pahala Gettuwana Road, Gettuwana, Kurunegala.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary's office, at
Kurunegala on the 03 day of May 2017 at 09.30 a.m. and are
hereby requested to norify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 26 day of April 2017, the nature of their interest in
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for
the acqusition of the said land, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
E. M. M. S. EKANAYAKE,
Divisional Secretary,
Kurunegala Division.
28th  February 2017
