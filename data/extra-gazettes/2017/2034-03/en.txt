I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as amemded by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to
acquire the land described below to develop water retaining area in
upper part of the Parliament Lake under Greater Colombo Flood
Control and Environment Improvement Project.
