L.D.B. 21/78(XVI)
THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 24A(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (1) of Section 24A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I,
Lakshman Kiriella, Minister of Higher Education and Highways, in consultation with the University Grants Commission,
do by this Order -
(1) establish with effect from 01.08.2017 the Postgraduate Institute of Indigenous Medicine as an Institute for
Higher Learning, for the purpose of providing, promoting and developing higher education ; and
(2) assign to such Institute the name and style of “the Postgraduate Institute of Indigenous Medicine, University of
Colombo”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and  Highways.
Colombo,
03rd August 2017.
