WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification published under Section 3 of Local Authorities (Standard by-laws) Act, No. 06 of 1952 which has to
be read with Section 2 of  Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
IT is hereby informed that, the draft by laws of Pradeshiya Sabha made by the Chief Minister and Minister in charge of
Local Government subject of Western Province under Section 2 of Local Authorities (Standard by Laws) Act, No. 06 of
1952 which has to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 and published
in Extraordinary Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1976/21 dated 20th of July 2016 have been
approved by the Provincial Council of Western Province on 17th of January 2017  in terms of the provision of Section 3 of
Local Authorities (Standard by Laws) Act, No. 06 of 1952 which has to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council
(Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 as mentioned in the following Schedule.
I
SURA DEV APRIY A,
Chief Minister of Western Province and
Minister of Finance and Planning, Engineering
Services, Law and Order, Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Economic Development,
Power and Energy, Environmental Affairs, Water Supply
and Drainage and Tourism.
Chief Ministry (Western Province),
Shrawasthi Mandiraya,
Colombo 07,
26th April, 2017.
