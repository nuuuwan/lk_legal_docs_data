THE  LAND  REFORM   LAW,   No.   1  OF  1972
Order under Subsection (4) of Section 27A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (4) of the Section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972 as
amended by the Land Reform (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981,  I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister
of Land, Tourism and Christian Affairs, do by this Order -
(a) amend the Order, made under subsection (I) of Section 27A of the Land Reform Law, No. 1 of 1972, and
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 456/4 of July 1, 1987 relating to the  land called and known as Hantane
Estate morefully described in the First Schedule to this Order and which had vested in the National Housing
Development Authority established by the National Housing Development Authority Act, No. 17 of 1979 as
last amended by National Housing Development Authority (Amendment) Act, No. 30 of 1999, by revoking, the
said Order as relates to the extent of land called Hantane Estate specified in the Second Schedule ; and
(b) State that upon revocation, such extent of land as specified in the Second Schedule to this Order, shall re-vest in
the Land Reform Commission.
