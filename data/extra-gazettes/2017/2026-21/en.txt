PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
SRI  SITHIRA  VELAYUTHA  SWAMI  TEMPLE  ANNUAL  FESTIVAL - 2017
DIVISIONAL SECRETARIAT OF THIRUKKOVIL DIVISION IN AMPARA DISTRICT
IT is hereby notified for the information of the pilgrims who attend the above festival and other concerned that the
above festival commences on 06.07.2017 with the schedule of execution of duties to the manes on 23.07.2017. Also
the festivals terminate on 25.07.2017 with the "Vairavar Pooja".
The attention of the pilgrims who attend the festival and others concerned is drawn to standing regulation
published in the  Government Gazette  No. 10247 dated 11th May 1951 which will be force during the above festival.
The camp area of the above festival has been enlarged as follows,
North :  Mahavidyalaya Road, Thambiluvil;
East :  Fisheries Road, Thirukkovil;
South :  Beach Road, Thirukkovil;
West :  Main Road, Thirukkovil.
S. J
AGARAJAN,
Divisional Secretary.
Divisional Secretariat,
Thirukkovil.
