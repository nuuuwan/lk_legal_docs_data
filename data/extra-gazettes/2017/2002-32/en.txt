THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways,  on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006 establishing
the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which Order is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid
Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1979/20 of August 09, 2016), by the addition immediately after item “Faculty of Science”  appearing in Column I
which corresponds to the entries in Column II of the  Schedule of the following entry :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology
Faculty of Nursing
