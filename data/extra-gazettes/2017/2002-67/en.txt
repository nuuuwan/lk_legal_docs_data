A Statute to amend the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of the 2015 as amended by Statute No. 02 of 2016
Appropriation Statute
2016
A Statute to amend the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of  2015, of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
1. This Statute may be cited as the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council Appropriation (Amendment) Statute No. 05
of the 2016, and shall come into operation of the date of receiving the assent of the Governor of the
Sabaragamuwa Province.
2. The expenditure of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council which is estimated Rupees Twenty Nine Billion, Six
Hundred and Fifty Million Two Hundred and Nineteen Thousand Five Hundred and Seventy One Rupees and
Thirty Cents only (Rs. 29,650,219,571.30) for the service of the period beginning on 1st of January, 2016 and
ending 31st of December, 2016 in this Statute referred to as the “Financial Year 2016” , which is specified  in
2nd paragraph of the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of 2015 as amended by Appropriation (Amendment) Statute
No. 02 of 2016, the first Schedule is hereby amended as the expenditure of the Sabaragramuwa Provincial
Council which is estimated Rupees Thirty Two Billion Two Hundred and Five Million, Six Hundred and Ninety
Thousand Nine Hundred and Ten Rupees and Thirty Cents only (Rs. 32,205,690,910.30) for the service of the
period beginning on 01st January 2016 and ending 31st of December 2016 in this statute referred to as the
“Financial Year 2016” as the First Schedule hereof.
3. The limits  mentioned in the Second Schedule of the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of 2015 as amended by
Appropriation (Amendment) Statute No. 02 of 2016, relevant to the limits of the Advanced Accounts is hereby
amended as per the second schedule hereof.
