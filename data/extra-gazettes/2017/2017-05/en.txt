THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land shown as Lot ‘B’ depicted in the
Advance Tracing No. MR/KTP/2017/34 and dated 28.02.2017,
prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of
Mathugobe (F.V .P. 169) in the Grama Niladhari Division of Tennipita
(244A) in the Minor Division of Morawakkorale, Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Kotapola in Matara District in Southern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘B’ – Handferd Division of Deniyaya Estate (Field
No. 08) – Extent: About 1.5524 Hectares
North: Handferd Hansavila Village claimed by Talawakele
Plantation Co. Ltd.; East: Handferd Hansavila Village claimed
by Talawakele Plantation Co. Ltd., Road (R.D.A) and Siri
Sadaham Vihara Bhoomi claimed by Talawakele Plantation
Co. Ltd.; South: Road (R.D.A) and Siri Sadaham Vihara
Bhoomi claimed by Talawakele Plantation Co. Ltd.;West: Siri Sadaham
Vihara Bhoomi claimed by Talawakele Plantation Co. Ltd. and
Lot A.
