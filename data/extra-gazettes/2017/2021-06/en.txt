STATUTE  (AMENDED)   FOR   PAYING   SALARIES   AND   ALLOWANCES   BEARING   No. 01   OF   2017   OF
THE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL   OF   UV A   PROVINCE
The name of the statute was called as “Act for paying salaries and allowances, bearing No. 2 of 1990 of the Uva
Provincial Council” would be called hereinafter as “The main statute” and amended as “statute for paying salaries and
allowances bearing No. 2 of 1990”
This Gazette Extraordinary  can be downloaded from  www.documents.gov.lk
1A PG  3541  — 17  (05/2017)
1. This statute would be called as “statute for paying salaries and allowances bearing No. 01 of
2017, and it would be effective from the date approved by the Hon. Governor of Uva Province.
2. This would be amended by removing the Section (b) of the sentence 2 of the main statute and
including the following sentence.
(b) A monthly salary and allowances to the deputy chairman of Uva Provincial Council, equal
to (2/3) two third of the monthly salary and other entire allowances paid to a deputy
minister of the parliament at present.
3. In the event of any inconsistency in between the Sinhala and Tamil text of the statute, Sinhala
shall prevail
