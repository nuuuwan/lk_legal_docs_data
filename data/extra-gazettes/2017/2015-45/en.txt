THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1482/26 of February 02, 2007 and
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1885/2 of October 20, 2014, which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the
University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be
a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the item “Faculty of Arts” appearing in
Column I of Schedule to the aforesaid Order, under the heading “Faculty” and in its corresponding entries appearing in
Column II  of such Schedule, under the heading “Department” of the following.
