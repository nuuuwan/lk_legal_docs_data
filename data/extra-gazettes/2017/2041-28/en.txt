 NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  3(1)  OF  THE  REGISTRATION  OF ELECTORS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No. 10  OF  2017
Supplementary Register of Electors - 2017
FOR  THE  SRI  LANKAN   CITIZENS   WHO   HA VE   BEEN   INTERNALLY   DISPLACED   AS   A   RESULT   OF
ANY   ACTIONS   OF   A   TERRORIST   MILITANT  OR  OTHER  GROUP   DURING   THE   RECENT   PAST  AND
WHOSE   NAMES   HA VE   NOT   BEEN   REGISTERED   AS  ELECTORS   ELSEWHERE   IN  THE   ISLAND
THE calling for applications in respect of the preparation of the Supplementary Register on the above matter has expired on
19.10.2017. Any person who is eligible under the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act to be registered in the
supplementary register but whose name does not appear in the said register, may appeal in writing to the respective Registering
Officer on the format set out in the aforesaid Registration of Electors Act, in order to have his/her name entered or retained
as the case may be in the register. Every appeal in this regard should be made so as to reach the Registering Officer of the
respective District Election Office within two weeks (02nd November 2017) from the date of this notice published in the
Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Any person whose name is included in the aforesaid supplementary register for the aforementioned electoral
district may object to the entry or retention of his/her own name or that of any other person in the register. Every objection
should be made in writing in duplicate and should be substantially in Form “C” set out in the aforesaid Act and be submitted
to the Registering Officer of the respective District Election Office within two weeks (02nd November 2017) from the
date of this notice published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Forms to make a claim, to enter or retain a name in the register or to submit objections to any claim to enter or
retain any name in the register may be obtained from the Election Office of the respective administrative district.
                                                              By Order of the Election Commission.
H. M. T. D. H
ERA TH,
Secretary,
Election Commission.
At the Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
On this 20th day of October 2017.

 NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  3(1)  OF  THE  REGISTRATION  OF ELECTORS  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)
ACT,  No. 10  OF  2017
Supplementary Register of Electors - 2017
FOR  THE  SRI  LANKAN   CITIZENS   WHO   HA VE   BEEN   INTERNALLY   DISPLACED   AS   A   RESULT   OF
ANY   ACTIONS   OF   A   TERRORIST   MILITANT  OR  OTHER  GROUP   DURING   THE   RECENT   PAST  AND
WHOSE   NAMES   HA VE   NOT   BEEN   REGISTERED   AS  ELECTORS   ELSEWHERE   IN  THE   ISLAND
NUMBER  AND  NAME  OF  THE  ELECTORAL  DISTRICT : 10 - JAFFNA
IN terms of Section 3(1) of the Registration of Electors (Special Provisions) Act, No. 10 of 2017, it is hereby notified that
the prepartion of the supplementary register for displced persons of the aforementioned electoral district has now been
completed and it is now open for inspection by the general public during office hours at Jaffna/Killinochchi District
Election Office.
2. Any person who is eligible under the aforesaid Special Provisions Act to be registered in the supplementary
register for the aforementioned electoral district but whose name does not appear in the said register, may appeal in writing
to the registering officer of Jaffna/Killinochchi District Election Office on the format set out in the aforesaid Registration
of Electors Act, in order to have his/her name entered or retained as the case may be in the register. Every appeal in this
regard should be made so as to reach the registering officer of Jaffna/Killinochchi District Election Office within two
weeks from the date of this notice published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
03. Any person whose name is included in the aforesaid supplementary register for the aforementioned electoral
district may object to the entry or retention of his/her own name or that of any other person in the register. Every objection
should be made in writing in duplicate and should be substatially in Form “C” set out in the aforesaid Act and be submitted
to the registering officer of Jaffna/Kilinochchi District Election Office within two weeks from the date of this notice
published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
This notice will be exhibited at the Jaffna/Killinochchi District Election Office from 20th October 2017 to
02nd November 2017 (including both days).
04. Forms to make a claim, to enter or retain a name in the register or to submit objections to any claim to enter
or retain any name in the register may be obtained from the aforesaid Election Office of the administrative district of
Jaffna/Kilinochchi .
T. A
KILAN,
Assistant Registering Officer,
Electoral District No. 10 - Jaffna.
At the Jaffna District Election Office,
On this 20th day of October 2017.
