THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
AS AMENDED BY LAND ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
D. S. F . No. : KIDS/LND/01/ACQ/07.
L. M. F . No.: 4/3/15/2013/VE/163.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by Land Acquisition
( Amended) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to    acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Lots 1140, 1141, 1142, 1143, 1144, 1145, 1146, 1147, 1148
(Total Extent of 5.1342 Hec.) in Sheet No. 45 of Supplementary
No. 41 Plan No. 218 prepared by Surveyor General situated in
the village of Yatipawwa, in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Yatipawwa in Meda Pattu of Kuruwiti Korale, Ratnapura
District, Sabaragamuwa Province.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kiriella, on  the
day of 14th of June 2017, at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby
required to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 31st of May, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation, and
the details of the computation of such amount.
I. R. M. PRADEEPIKA D. ILLUKKUMBURA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kiriella.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kiriella,
8th of May, 2017.
