THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land depicted in Advance Tracinged ,  No. MR/
MTR/2015/535 dated 16th March, 2016 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Thalpavila North in the Matara
Four Gravet Minor Division in the Divisional Secretaris Division of
Matara in Matara District in Southern Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ - Hillcrestwatta - Extent: 0.0555 Hectare
North: Road and Hillcreswatta claimed by M.B.Siriyawathie; East:
Silverdalewatta claimed by K.K.Ranjithlal and the land acquired for
proposed the Matara - Kataragama Railway Line; South: The land
Acquired for  proposed the Matara Katharagama Railway Line;
West: Hillcrestwatta claimed by Nandawathie Mahamullage and
Hillcrestwatta  claimant can not be found.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
