IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 19 of the
Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460) as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 to appoint the
under-mentioned Lawyer members and Valuer members of the Board of Review for the purpose of hearing appeals in the
manner therein provided, for a period of three years with effect from 23rd May 2017 and to appoint Mr. Upali de Soysa
Gunawardane and Mr. C. J. R. Anthony as Chairman and Vice Chairman respectively of the said Board of Review under
sub-section (3) of section 19 of the aforesaid Act :-
Lawyer Members:
1. Mr. Upali de Soysa Gunawardane
2. Mr. C. J. R. Anthony
3. Mr. Sarath S. Cooray
4. Mr. A. M. P. Rajakaruna
5. Mr. Priyantha Govinnage
6. Mr. K. A. T. Fernando
7. Mr. M. D. E. S. Thissera
8. Mrs. T. N. Geekiyanage

1. Mr. R. W. M. S. B. Rajapaksha
