THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
  An allotment of  land in extent of about 0.0537 hectare,  depicted
in Advance Tracing No. KA/BRW/2016/486 and  dated  06.09.2016,
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Payagala
in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Beruwala, in District of Kaluthara
in Western Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Samarappuwatta – Extent: 0.0537 Hectare
North: Samarappuwatta claimed by P . H. F. Fernando, St. Merry's

St. Merry's Fisheries Society, Railway and Samarappuwatta claimant
not known; South: Samarappuwatta claimant not known and coast;
West: Coast and Samarappuwatta claimed by P . H. F. Fernando.
