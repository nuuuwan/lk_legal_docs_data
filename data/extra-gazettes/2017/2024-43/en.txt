Order of Delegating Power
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Co-operative Societies Amendment Statute No. 01 of 2011 reading with
Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009, I, Susil Gunarathna, Provincial Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth
Affairs, Co-operative, Trade and Food, Industrial Development of North Central Provincial Council delegates all mentioned
in the Column III of the Schedule give below to the person mentioned in the Column I who has been appointed to perform
the post of the Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development (Departmental) to assist the North Central Provincial
Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar, accordance with the aforementioned Statute, to be effective
from the date mentioned in Column II.
S
USIL G UNARATHNA,
Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs,
Co-operative, Trade and Food, Industrial Development of
North Central Province.
