NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  AS AMENDED
BY THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)
NO. 28 OF 1964
Notice under Section 7
       My Ref. No.: BD/ST/LND/1/ACQUI/1/7/93.
Land Ministry Ref  No.: 4-3/8/2014/pc/26.
I hereby given notice, Sub-section 7 of the Land Acquisition
Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act,
No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends to acquire the
following land/ servitude which is required for a public
purpose.
     Description of the land/servitude to be acquired :
Portions of land described in the schedule attached,
situated in the Uda ellegama village in the Soranathota
Divisional Secretary's Division in Badulla District, in the
province of Uva, containing about 0.6359 hectares in extent
and depicted in the supplement No. 40 of the village Plan No.
88, prepared by the surveyor General.
01. Lot No. 431
Name of land: Rideepana Estate; Land Use: permanent
building with a garden; Owner: Land Reclamation
Commission; Extent: 0.5426 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 432
Name of land: Rideepana Estate; Land Use: permanent
building with a garden; Owner: Land Reclamation
Commission; Extent: 0.0755 Hectare.
0.3. Lot No. 433
Name of land: Rideepana Estate; Land Use: Access road;
Owner: Land Reclamation Commission; Extent: 0.0178 Hectare.
    All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired to appear personally or by agents
duly authorized in writing , before me at Soranathota Divisional
Secretary's Office on the 16th day of August  2017 at 2.00 p.m.
and are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate)
on or befor the 07th day of August 2017 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude
the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
H. M. NANDASENA,
Divisional Secretary,
Soranathota.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Soranathota,
28th June, 2017.
