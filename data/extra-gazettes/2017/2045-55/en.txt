LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
NOTIFICATION  UNDER  SECTION  27F
IT is hereby notified under Section 27F of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance read with 19 th amendment to the
constitution that the number of women candidates to be nominated in respect of each Local Authority specified in Column
II of the Schedule hereto situated within the administrative district specified in Column I thereof for the purpose of the
election held under the said Ordinance, is indicated in the corresponding note of the Column II of the said Schedule and that
the number of 25% women members to be returned is indicated in column IV of the said Schedule.
MAHINDA D ESHAPRIYA, N. J. ABEYESEKERE, S. RATNAJEEVAN H. HOOLE,
Chairman, Member, Member,
Election Commission. Election Commission. Election Commission.
Election Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
