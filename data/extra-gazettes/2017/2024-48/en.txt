CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Patha Dumbara Pradeshiya Sabha
IMPOSITION   OF  ENTERTAINMENT  TAX
BY virtue of the power vested in, under Sub section (1) of Section 2 of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 267), it
is hereby published the proposal resolved by the Secretary to the Patha Dumbara Pradeshiya Sabha is hereby approved by
the Minister in charge of the subject Local Government of the Central Provincial Council in the  Central Province, by virtue
of power vested by the Sub section (2) of the said Section.
P. B. W
IJAYARA TNA,
Secretary,
Chief Ministry, Education and Local Government Ministry,
Central Province.
Pallekele,
02nd December 2016.
PROPOSAL
By virtue of power vested in under Section 9(3) of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, it has been decided
to charge 10% of Entertainment Tax (except Entertainment Tax) on entry ticket charges for the imposition of Entertainment
Tax within the authority areas of Patha Dumbara Pradeshiya Sabha under Section 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance
No. 12 of 1946 and amended Entertainment Tax Act, No. 37 of 1984.
