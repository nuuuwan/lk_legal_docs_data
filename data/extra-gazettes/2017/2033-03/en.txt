ESALA   FESTIV AL   OF   MAHIYANGANA  -  2017
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Transfer of Powers (Divisional Secretaries)Act, No.58 of 1992, and according
to the regulations in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 362) publish in Government Gazette
No. 9902 of 10th September 1948, Gazette No. 10283 of 17th August 1951 Gazette No. 14778 of 25th March 1995 and
Gazette No. 14752 of 09th June l967, 1, Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Nimal Dissanayaka, the Divisional Secretary of
Mahiyangana, do hereby inform the following orders shall be in force with regard to the  camp of Mahiyangana Esala
Festival 2017.
1. It is announced the Mahiyangana Esala Festival will be commenced on the 22nd August 2017 in the closure of
the event will be followed by the “Diya Kepeema” Ceremony on the 06th September 2017.
2. The camp are for abode of pilgrims shall be the land demarcated by the 37th Mile Post of Mahiyangana-Bibila
main road up to the 1/39 culvert in front of Mahiyangana National School, and the land situated between the
Mahiyangana - Meegahakiula road the leading to the clock tower in front of Mahiyangana hospital and new
highway.
3. No person shall cook meals in “Vihara Maluwa”, “Dewala Boomiya”, “Palle Veediya” (The court yards of the
Vihara, Bodi and Dewala or within those premises.)
4. On a permit issues by me or by the officer incharge of the camp, Selling of flowers, Joss sticks, candles,
camphor, offering items and statutes for fulfillment of vows, is allowed in the strip of land opposite the “Sthupa”
at ”Mada Veediya” and in the strip of land issued to the left of the “Makara Thorana”
5. Within the camp area specified in above Section 2, selling and distribution of all food to pilgrims and selling of
other commodities and allowed in the 24 permanent building, or the Officer-in-charge of the camp. The area
beyond 20ft, from the drainage system of both sides of Palle Veediya, Mada Veediya, Dutugamunu Vishrama
Shala Mawatha (Pilgrims road) Ammodilla road, Badulla Mahiyangana main road, Bodimaluwa Mawatha and the
road in front of the “Sangawasa (Bikku abode), prescribed as the way of perahara procession by the “Basnayaka
Nilame”, is reserved for business purposes.

7. Establishing temporary stalls/mobile stalls and selling goods is entirely prohibited in the camp except as

8. Parking of private vehicles in the main street is hereby prohibited and no person shall enter any type of vehicle
in to the camp area including bicycles and bullock carts without a permit issues by me or the officer in charge
of the camp.
9. No person shall enter any motor vehicle in to the camp via the road reaching the sacred area by the side of the
“Wahalkada” (Vestibule) witch faces Mahiyangana Bibila main road, Mahiyangana Bibila old road, the main
access to the camp area, the road by the side of the clock tower located in front of the hospital, and the road by
the side of 1/39 culvert in front of Mahiyangana National School, unless written permission of obtained.
10. Publish notices will be made by me on the procedure of leasing the parcels of land for business purpose within
camp area.
11. All the temporary constructions in the leased out commercial lands shall comply with the specifications made
by the Department of Health. 01 or 02 sanitary toilets adjacent to each boutique will have to be made available
for the use of pilgrims, if ordered after the removal of the temporary building/sheds by the settlers of the
leased out land at the end or the festival, old refuse and garbage including polythene shall be collected and
disposed under the supervision of the Public Health Inspectors.
12. No person shall construct any building within camp area unless a license has been obtained in that behalf from
me.
13. No person shall without the previous sanction of the Divisional Secretary, construct any booth or any sort of
building or extend any building, so as to encroach on any land used or street for use as a street with in camp area
or on any land forming the banks of the river with in the camp area.
15th Regulation (Pilgrimages Ordinance)
(a) No person shall, without the previous of the Divisional Secretary, construct any booth or any sort of building
any extend any building, so as to encroach any land used or set apart for use as a street with in the camp area or
any land forming the bank of the river within the camp area.
(b) Firing of any sort of crackers is strictly prohibited within the camp area.
(c) No person shall make any collection of money with in the camp area on selling tickets, flags, or on any other
purpose unless permission is granted by the Divisional Secretary or other authorized officer.

MAHIYANGANA   ESALA   FESTIV AL  - 2017
According to the regulations in terms of the provisions of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chapter 362) publish in Government
Gazette No. 9902 of 10th September, 1948, Gazette No. 10283 of 17th August 1951, Gazette No. 14778 of 25th March
1995 and Gazette No. 14752 of 09th June 1967 and the transfer of powers Act, No. 58 of 1992, I, Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Nimal Dissanayaka, the Divisional Secretary of Mahiyangana, do hereby notify that  the following places
are allocated for the purpose of distributing and selling of food items among the pilgrims.
(a) All the permanent and temporary building in between the 37th k. m. 39/1 culvert opposite the National School,
along Mahiyangana Bibila road.
(b) Permanent and temporary building on both side Mahiyangana/Meegahakiula old road, up the clock tower in
front of the hospital.
(c) Temporary building construction on a permit issued by me, within the allocated ground for abode of pilgrims in
side the camp area.
No person shall distribute any sort of food item any place not appointed for the purpose, except specified in above.
