REGISTRATION   OF   PERSONS   ACT,   No. 32  OF  1968
REGISTRATION of  Persons regulations made by the Minister of  Internal Affairs, Vayamba development and Cultural
affairs under Section 52 of  the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968, published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 2021/ 28 dated 31st May 2017,  have been approved by the Parliament on 22nd August 2017. Therefore it is hereby
notified that aforesaid regulations are affected from 22nd August 2017, under Section 52 (3) of the Registration of
Persons Act.
