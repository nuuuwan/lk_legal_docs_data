THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
GA/AMB/01/355 and dated 26.06.2001, prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Thalgasgoda (within the boundary
of Urban Council, Ambalangoda Thalgasgoda, Division No. 11) in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ambalangoda in Galle District
in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 17 – Thalgasgoda Bedda, No Assessment Number,
Thalgasgoda Road – Extent: 1.093 Hectare
North: Lot No. 16 and Thalgasgoda Paddy field claimed by

P . Maduwage and Others; South: Ambalangoda Batapola Main Road;
West: Lot No. 16.
