THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : DS/GFG/L&L/02/2009/02.
Ministry of Land Reference No.: LD/05/2009/VT/98.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land Acquisition
Amendment Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to    acquire the following land/servitude* which is required
for a public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land in total extent of 0.0524 Hectare marked
as lot No. 01 in basic plan No. 4198 made by Surveyor General
situated in the village of Weliwatta in Grama Niladhari Division
of 97A Weliwatta, within Municipal Council Limits of
Galle, in Divisional Secretary's Division of Four Gravets Galle,
District of Galle, Southern Province. The description of the
aforesaid land is as follows :-
     1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Oroppuwatta Mawatha, Kebella,
Assessment No. 09, Matara Road; Description: Home Garden,
permanent building, and part of a dilapidated building;
Claimant: Cannot find; Extent: 0.0524 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Four Gravets, Galle on the 25th day
of January 2017 at 09.00 a.m and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 18th day of
January 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation and the datails of
the said land/ servitude, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
W. S. SATHYANANDA,
Divisional Secretary,
Four Gravets-Galle.
