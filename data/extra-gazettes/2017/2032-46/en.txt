THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20 (4) (b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
the Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Postgraduate
Institute of Archaeology have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed to restore
normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Prishantha Gunawardhana to be the Competent Authority with effect
from 24.07.2017 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging, in lieu of  Director of Postgraduate Institute
of Archaeology any power, duty or function which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director
under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By - Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and  Highways.
Colombo,
15th August 2017.
