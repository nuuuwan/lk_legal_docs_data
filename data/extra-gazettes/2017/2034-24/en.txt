THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake,  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land called "Rukattanagaha Watta" in extent of
about 0.1012 Hectare depicted as lot "A" in the Advance Tracing
No.MA/RTT/2016/448 and dated 28.01.2017 prepared by the
Surveyor General situated in the Ulpathapitiya Village, in Matale
Medasiya Pattuwa Minor Division,  Divisional Secretary's Division
of Rattota, Matale District in Central Province and bounded as
follows:
1A - G 26182 -  08(2017/08)
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rukattanagaha Watta – Extent: About  0.1012
Hectare
North: Rukattanagaha Watta belongs to National Water Supply
and Drainage Board and Road; East: Road and Rukattanagaha Watta
claimed by S.P.Wasantha Cokilam; South: Rukattanagaha Watta
claimed by S.P. Wasantha Cokilam and Rukattanagaha Watta Crown
Land (Suduganga Reservoir); West: Rukattanagaha Watta Crown Land
(Suduganga Reservoir) and Rukattanagaha Watta belongs to National
Water Supply and Drainage Board.
