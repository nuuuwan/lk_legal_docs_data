No. 2004/54 –  THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 02, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: UDU/2/5/2/56. 
Land Ministry’s  No.: 13/97/FR/151. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the  following land/
servitude for public purpose (the conservation of 
Dumbara mountain range.)  
 
 
Description of the Land/servitude to be  acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called “Winchfield Park Watta” 
bearing an extent of about 7.5025 Hectare mentioned in 
Maha 6627 (lot number 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 9, 10), 
Supplement of the surveyor General, situated in the 
Grama Niladhari Division of Karambakatiya, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Udadumbara of Kandy 
District, Central Province. 
 
Name and address of Claimants : Mrs. T. G. Pody 
Manike, No. 46, Gonawala, Rajawalla. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands/servitude 
on which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, at Udadumbara, on the 
03rd April 2017 at 2.00 p.m. and hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
27th March 2017, the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.   
 
 
 SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Udadumbara. 
 
18th January, 2017. 
At Udadumbara Divisional Secretariat.
