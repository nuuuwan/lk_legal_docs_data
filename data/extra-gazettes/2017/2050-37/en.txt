No. 2050/37 – THURSDAY, DECEMBER 21, 2017
L.D.B. 21/78 (XIV)
UNIVERSITIES  ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 20 (4) (b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 20(4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higer Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Postgraduate
Institute of Archealogy have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed to restore
normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Prishantha Gunawardhana to be the Competent Authority with effect
from October 25, 2017 for the purpose of exercising, performing and discharging in lieu of Director of Postgraduate Institute of
Archaelogy, any power, duty or function which has  been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Director under the
said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By-Law, Regulation or Rule made there under.
LAKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Colombo, Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
19th December, 2017.
