1A-  G  26183— 12 (08/2017)
SPORTS   ACT,  No.  25  OF 1973
Order under Sections 32 and 33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera, the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect, the registration of the Sri Lanka Judo Association which
is the National Association for Judo and as a interim arrangement, appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardene
Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports Development as
competent authority with effect from 30.08.2017 to continue its functions and further order that the election be convened
and held to elect new office bearers to the Sri Lanka Judo Association.
D
AYASIRI J AYASEKERA (Attorney-at-Law)(M.P.),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
30th August, 2017.

SPORTS   ACT,  No.  25  OF 1973
Order under Sections 32 and 33
I, Dayasiri Jayasekera, the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Act, No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend with immediate effect, the registration of the Sri Lanka Hand-ball Association
which is the National Association for Hand-ball and as a interim arrangement, appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake
Rajawardene Tennakoon Mudiyanselage Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports
Development as competent authority with effect from 30.08.2017 to continue its functions and further order that the
election be convened and held to elect new office bearers to the Sri Lanka Hand-ball Association for 2017.
D
AYASIRI J AYASEKERA (Attorney-at-Law)(M.P.),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
30th August, 2017.
