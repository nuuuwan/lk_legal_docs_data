No. 2010/02 – MONDAY, MARCH 13, 2017
ORDER  UNDER  SUB  SECTION (4), OF  SECTION  27 ‘A’ OF 1972  OF  THE
LAND  REFORM  ACT, NO. 01 OF  1972 OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE  ASSEMBLY
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27 a (4) of the Land Reform Act, 01 of 1972 amended by the Land Reform
(Special Provisions) Act No. 39 of 1981 of the National State Assembly I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amaratunga, Minister
of Lands do hereby,
a. Amend the Order by way of revocation of the Order made on the portion of land in relation to the extent of land
called Hutvil Waththa alias Haitiville Waththa speaifically depicted in the Second Schedule of the Order made
under Section 27 a (1) of the Land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972 published in the Gazette Extraordinary  no
195/6 dated 31
st May 1982 in respect of the land called Hutvil Waththa alias Haitiville Waththa, which has been
vested in Janatha Estates Development Board established under State Agricultural Corporation Act, No. 11 of
1972 and depicted comprehensively in first schedule of this Order, due to not following the order or conditions
of the Agreement made with regard to the vesting of the land to Janatha Estates Development Board.
b. It is hereby declared that the extent of land depicted specifically in Second Schedule of this Order is vested
again in the Land Reform Commission as a result of this revocation.
JOHN ANTHONY EMMANUEL AMARATUNGA
Minister of Lands
Ministry of Lands,
Land Secretariat,
Mihikatha Medura,
1200/6, Rajamalwatta Avenue,
Battaramulla.
