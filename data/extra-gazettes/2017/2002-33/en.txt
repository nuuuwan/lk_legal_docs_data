THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1482/26 of February 02, 2007 establishing
the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka which Order is deemed,  by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid,
Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1990/16 of October 26, 2016), by the substitution for the entry “8. Department of Production Engineering” appearing
in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of Engineering” in Column I of the Schedule of the
following entry :-
“8. Department of Manufacturing and Industiral Engineering”
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
31st December 2016.
