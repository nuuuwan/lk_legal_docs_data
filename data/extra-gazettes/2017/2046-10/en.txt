THE  INLAND  REVENUE  ACT,  No.  10  OF  2006
Notice under Section 34(8)(a)

Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do hereby declare that the “Lotus Hill Home for Disabled Children” organization
referred to in the Schedule given below be an approved charity for the purpose of that Section.

ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,

Colombo 01,
20th November 2017.
