                       Land Ministry No. : 4-3/10/2013/UDA/410.
My No.: KEG/LAN/AQUIS/A3/80.
The Gazettte Notice bearing No. 1966/45 issued on 13th of
May, 2016 under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the lands described the Schedule given below for
the Restoration of  Kegalle Bus Depot should be amended as
follows. Accordingly, I hereby give notice under sub section
(1) of  Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act.
Description of the lands to be acquired :
Twelve allotments of land described  in the Scheduled
attached  below depicted Lots 1-11 in the Preliminary Plan No.
3557 and Lot No. 08 in the Preliminary Plan No. 3488 prepared
by the Surveyor General situated within the boundary of
Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha of Waldeniya Grama Niladari
Division in Kegalle Divisional Secretary's Division of Kegalle
District of Sabaragamuva Province.
G 26417 - 17 (2017/10)
Dates of inquiries of P.P.K. 3557 are
Lot No. 01- 03 on the date of  06.11.2017 from 9.00 a.m.
Lot No. 04- 06 on the date of  07.11.2017 from 9.00 a.m.
Lot No. 07- 09 on the date of  08.11.2017 from 9.00 a.m.
Lot No. 10- 11 on the date of  08.11.2017 from 9.00 a.m.
Dates of inquiries of P.P.K. 3488 are
Lot No. 8 on the date of  09.11.2017 from 9.00 a.m.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be acquired
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly
authorized in writen, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kegalle
on the above mentioned dates and on the Scheduled time.
 And are hereby requested to notify me in writing with duplicate
of on or before the 25th October, 2017, the nature of their interest
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for
acquisition of the said land  the amount of  compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
H. T. R. NALIKA PIYASENA,
Divisional Secretary,
Land Acquisition Officer,
Kegalle.
11th September, 2017,
Divisional Secretariat,
Kegalle.
