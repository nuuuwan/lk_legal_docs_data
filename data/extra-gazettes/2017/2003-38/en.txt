No. 2003/38 –  FRIDAY, JANUARY 27, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: MUL/LND/2/ACQ/22. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: LD/5/2007/CA/196. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the  following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude  to be  acquired: 
 
Under mentioned allotments of land shown in the 
surveyor General’s plan of FVP 129, Supplement No. 13, 
Sheet No.18 Prepared by Matara Surveyour 
Superintendant, situated in the village of Mulatiyana, 
Neralampitiya Grama Niladari Division, Mulatiyana 
Divisional Secretarys Division in Matara District 
Southern Province: 
 
Lot No. 228;  
Name of the land: Ihalawatta; Description : Two 
Permanent buildings, a toilet, water tank and Garden; 
Claimants: Ariyalata Hemali Rathnayaka, N.B. 
Rathnayaka, Anurudda Dewapriya Rathnayaka, Dunidu 
Rathnayaka, Renuka Rathnayaka, Dammika Ramani 
Rathnayaka, Ranjani Rathnayaka, and Sudarmavo Jinami 
Rathnayaka on behalf of late Piyadasa Rathnayaka; 
Extent of the Land:  0.2702 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 229;  
Name of the land: Ilanganwatta alias Kirilapanewatta; 
Description: Garden, permanent building and Buddh 
image house; Claimants: L. H. Ranasinghe; Extent:  
0.0371 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land / lands on 
which a servitude is to the acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writting, before me at the Divisional Secretariat 
Mulatiyana on the 29th day of March, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of March 2017, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land/servitude the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount.   
 
 SUSANTHA ATTANAYAKA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Mulatiyana. 
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Mulatiyana, 
25th January, 2017.
