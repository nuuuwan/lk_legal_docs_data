THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 07
I hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose:
Description of the land to be acquired :
Three (03) allotments of land in extent of 0.0757 Hectare,
as Lot No. 1- 3 depicted in preliminary Plan No. CO 9689 and
dated 31.03.2017 prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated
in the village of Raththanapitiya in Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kesbewa in the District of Colombo in Western
Province more fully described as follows:
1. Lot No. 1
Name of land: A part of Assmt. No. 014 of Colombo Road;
Description: Garden, Contains a permanent building;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: K.D.S. Fernando, No. 01,
G .H. Perera Road, Raththanapitiya, Boralesgamuwa; Extent:
0.0035 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Dawatagaha Kumbura; Description: Garden,
Contains a mixed cultivation; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Encroached by Weresganga Rainwater Drainage &
Environment Improvement Project (Part of Lot 13 in PP CO.
9468); Extent: 0.0366 Hectare.
3. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Dawatagaha Kumbura; Description: Paddy
Field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Encroached by
Weresganga Rainwater Drainage & Environment
Improvement Project (Parts of Lots 15, 16, 17 & 18 in PP CO.
9468); Extent: 0.0356 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Kesbewa Divisional Secretariat on the 05th May,
2017 at 9.30 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before 28th April, 2017 the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars or their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
G. S. K. KANNANGARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Kesbewa,
04th April 2017.
