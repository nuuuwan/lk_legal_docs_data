L.D.B 21/78(xv)
 THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 22 of the aforesaid Act, in relation to the establishing of the
Vavuniya Campus of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 968/6 of March 26,
1997, by the addition immediately after the entry “Department of Economics and Management” appearing in Column 2,
which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Business Studies” appearing in Column 1 of the Schedule, of the following
new entry :-
“Department of English Language Teaching”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
13th November 2017.
