THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act:
An allotment of land depicted in Advanced Tracing No. R/EMB/
2014/294 dated on 22.07.2015, prepared by the Surveyor General
situated in the Village of Andoluwa (FVP 882)in Minor Division at
Diayapotagam Paththuwa in the Embilipitiya Division Secretary's
Division in Rathnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded
as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Karamatiye Yaya – Extent: 12.1497 Hectares
North: Lot Nos.889, 115 and 19; East: Lot Nos. 19, 122 and 889;
South: Lot Nos.122, 19 and 889; West: Lot Nos.889 and 115.
