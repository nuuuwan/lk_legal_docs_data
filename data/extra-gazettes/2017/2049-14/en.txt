REVISION  OF  SRI  LANKA  ENGINEERING  SERVICE  MINUTE  -  REVISION  No.  (04)
Section 20.1, 20.1.1 and 20.1.2 of Sri Lanka Engineering Service Minute, which has been published in the Extraordinary
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1836/6 dated 11.11.2013 and revised by the Gazettes Extraordinary,
No. 1887/21 dated 05.11.2014, No. 1905/07 dated 09.03.2015 and No. 2006/53 dated 17.02.2017, is hereby revised in
the following manner.
20.1 The interim period shall be effective from 01.01.2006 up to 31.12.2017. During the period the promotions of
officers who satisfy following qualifications and requirements shall be made as follows.
20.1.1 Promotion to Grade II of the service
(I) An officer who joined the service before 01.01.2008 and has been absorbed into Grade III of the service
shall be promoted to Grade II during the interim period to be effective from the date he/she completes all the qualifications
and requirements which are to be fulfilled in order to get promotions from grade II of class II to Grade I in class II as
mentioned in the minute of Sri Lanka Engineering Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 509/7 dated
07.06.1988 and relevant revisions.
20.1.2 Promotion to Grade I of the service
(II) Officer who joined the service before 01.01.2008 and have been absorbed into Grade II or have been
promoted to grade II during the interim period, shall be promoted in to Grade I, if they complete following qualifications
and requirements and every aspect during in the interim period to be effective from the date of completion.
(a) Shall have earned all salary increments in the immediately preceding 05 years ;
(b) Shall have not been subjected to any disciplinary punishment in immediately preceding 05 years ;
(c) Shall have maintained the status of charted engineer from the date of promotion to Grade II.
                                                                    On the order of Public Service Commission,
A. K
ULA THUNGA,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
