PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
Order No. 33
Unique No. Ka/14
ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  22(1)(f) OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  ACT,   N O.  1  OF 1972  OF  THE  NATIONAL STATE
ASSEMBLY
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under para (f) of Sub section (1) of Section 22, of land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972
of the National State Assembly, which has been amended  by Act No. 39 of 1975, Act  No. 14 of 1981, Act No. 39 of 1981,
Act  No. 14 of 1986 and Act No. 18 of 1986, I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amaratunge, the Minister of Lands do hereby this
Order declare that,
(a) the agricultural land indicated specifically in Column II of the Schedule, under this Order shall be alienated to
the persons whose names are indicated specifically in Column I of the Schedule ;
(b) The agricultural lands indicated specifically in the corresponding note of Column II of the Schedule, under this
Order were belonged to the persons, whose names appeared specifically in Column I of the Schedule, and the
said agricultural lands were included in the extent of lands which were considered to be belonged to the head of
the family, in which the above persons were also the members.
(c) The persons indicated in Column I of the Schedule, under this Order were below 18 years of  their age at the
time of imposing the ceiling, on agricultural lands in terms of the above Act.
(d) The agricultural lands indicated specifically in Column II of the Schedule, have not been alienated to any person
under Section 22 of the aforesaid Act.
(e) No compensation has been paid under Section 28 of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the above agricultural lands.
J
OHN ANTHONY  E MMANUEL  AMARA THUNGA,
Minister of Lands.
Ministry of Lands,
Land Secretariat,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwaththa Avenue,
Battaramulla,
