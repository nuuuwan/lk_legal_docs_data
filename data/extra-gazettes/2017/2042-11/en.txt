THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 7
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/28.
My Reference No.: Ja/2/2/5/1.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1 (Lot 01) allotment in total extent of about 0.0092
Hectare, of land in Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 4050 dated
26.04.2017, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of
Surveys, Gampaha District on behalf of the Surveyor General
situated in the village of Dandugama and Minor Division of
Ragam Pattuwa in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 194,
Dandugama in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Ja-ela in
Gampaha District in Western Province.
G 26472 - 17 (2017/10)
01. Lot No. 1
Name of Land: Dolewatta; Description: Open waste land;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: W. S. M. S. Fernando,
No. 72, Wijaya Road, Thudella, Ja-ela; Extent: 0.0092 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before
me at Divisional     Secretariat, Ja-ela of the 24.11.2017 at
9.30 a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
[in duplicate] on or before the 17.11.2017, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for com-
pensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
