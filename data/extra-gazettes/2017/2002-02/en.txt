UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 of 1978 
 
I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister of Higher Education and Highways by virtue of powers vested with me by the Sections 19 
and 21 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 hereby order in line with the Section 49(a) of the Land Acquisition Act, 
that the land mentioned in the Schedule below is required to acquire in order to establish the Faculty of Engineering of the 
University of Sri Jayewardenepura.  
 L
AKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
                Minister of Higher Education and Highways.   
Ministry of Higher Education and Highways, 
08th Floor, Maganeguma Mahamedura Building,
Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, 
Battaramulla,
10th January, 2017.

The land called Kekunagahawatta in extent of 15A., 01R., 32.9P. depicted as Lot No. 108 in Sheet No. 3 of Block

West in Homagama Divisional Secretary's Division in Colombo District of Western Province. 
North  : Block 3 of Cadastral Map No. 520009 in Kitulahena Village ; 
South  : Block No. 2 and Lot Nos. 2, 3, 4, 6 of Cadastral Map No. 520008 ; 
East  : Main Road of RDA (From Kottawa to Mattegoda); 
West  : Block No. 7 of Cadastral Map No. 520029 in Kitulahena Village.
