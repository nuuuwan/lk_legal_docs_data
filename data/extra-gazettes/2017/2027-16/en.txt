Reference No. : 3/7/12/7.
Land Minister Reference No.: LD/05/2007/PC/270.
I, hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to the
following land which is required for public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
The allotments of land called Liddesdale Estate plain total
Extent about 0.0843 shown as Lot Number 01 of P.P .N. 3087
situate in the Village of Liddeesdale Estate in 533 C
Rathnayakapura Division in Divisional Secretary Division of
Walapane in Nuwara Eliya District in Central Province and
described as follows.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Liddesdale Estate; Claimants: State;

interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for

amount.
