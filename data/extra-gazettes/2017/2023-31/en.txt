THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South Eastern
University of Sri Lanka, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996 (as last amended by Order
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1973/28 of June 29, 2016) by the addition immediately after item (e) appearing
in Column II under the heading “Department of Study” which corresponds to the entry “Factulty of Arts and Culture”
appearing in  Column I under the heading “Faculty” of the Schedule II to the such Order, of the following new item :-
 “(f) Department of English Language Teaching”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
12th June, 2017.
