NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of North Central Province
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 32(a) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Punchi Banda
Dissanayaka, the Governor of the North Central Province do hereby notify that the names of the officers, mention below
have been appointed as the relevant posts for North Central Provincial Services.
1. Mr. R. A. S. Jayathilaka, Officer in Special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Services the Secretary of the
North Central Province from 2016.04.11 to 2017.01.19
2. Mr. H. M. Thilakabandara, Officer in Special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Secretary of the
Public Service Commission of North Central Province with effect from 2017.01.04 until further the order is
made.
3. Mr. R. A. S. Jayathilaka, officer in Special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Secretary of the Ministry
of Cooperative, Trade and Food, Industrial Development, Transport, Sport and Youth Affairs of  North Central
Province with effect from 2017.01.20 until further  order is made.
4. Mr. H. L. S. Wijesinghe, Officer in Special Grade of Sri Lanka Administrative Service as the Secretary  of the
North Central Province with effect from 2017.01.20 until further order is made.
5. Mr. E. M. M. Ekanayaka, Officer in Grade I of Sri Lanka Administrative Service for acting as the Secretary to
the Ministry of Agriculture, Agri Product Marketing, Animal Production, Fisheries and Housing Affairs  of
North Central Province with effect from 2016.12.07 until further order is made.
6. Mrs. K. A. S. Maheshika, Officer in  Grade 1 of Sri Lanka Administrative Service  as the Commissioner of Local
Government of North Central Province with effect from 2016.06.06 until further order is made.
