No. 2046/5  –  MONDAY NOVEMBER 20, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
 
 Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/72. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/7/2015/WS/117. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land/
Servitude which is required for the Construction of 
Matale New intake and Pump - house of Visal Matale 
Water Supply Project. 
 
Description of the land  or Servitude to be acquired : 
 
Allotments of land described  in below in Plan  
No. Ma 1713, Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents 
of Surveys, Situated in Ulpathapitiya Village, 
Ulpathapitiya Grama Niladhari Division, in Sub Division 
of Matale Medasiya Pattuwa, in the Rattota Divisional 
Secretariat  Division, in Matale District, in Central 
Province. 
 Lot No. 1  
Name of the land: Rukaththana Gaha Watta; 
Description: Garden; Claimant: Mrs. S. P. Wasantha 
Kokilam, No. 2/103, Kiula, Matale; ; Extent 0.1012 
Hectare.   
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land is hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at   Divisional Secretariat Rattota on 
the 10th day of January, 2018, at 10.00 a.m. and  are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 02nd day of January, 2018, the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount.  
 
 
 
  H. M. N. DINIPRIYA, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
  Rattota. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Rattota, 
15th November, 2017.
