THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Kongahamula Watta’’ in total extent
of about 0.0379 Hectare depicted as Lot  A in Advance Tracing No.
KU/MLL/2015/259 - A and dated 23.11.2015 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village of  Idangapola in Narampola,
Grama Niladhari Division No. 725, in Mahagalboda Megoda Korale
(South) Minor Division in the Divisional Secretaries Division of
Mallawapitya in Kurunegala District, North Western Province and
bounded as follows.
01. Lot  ‘‘A’’ – Kongahamula Watta – Extent: 0.0379 Hectare
North: Road of Road Development Authority and Deduru Oya;
East: Deduru Oya and Land claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board; South: Land claimed by National Water Supply and
Drainage Board and Remaining portion of this land claimed by
Kristhogu Baduge Karunarathne; West: Remaining portion of this
land claimed by Kristhogu Baduge Karunarathne and road of Road
Development Authority.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
