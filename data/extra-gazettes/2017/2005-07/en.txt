No. 2005/7 –  TUESDAY, FEBRUARY,  07,  2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT,  
NO. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
My No.: LAN/ACQU/POLKA/D31/03. 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2015/LO/187. 
 
I hereby give notice, under  Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment)  
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
Two allotments of land in Total Extent of about 
2.9078 Hectare depicted in PPMADA 2841 Prepared by 
the Surveyor General Situated in the Village of 
Pattiyadichenai in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Koralaipattu in Batticaloa District, Eastern Province 
morefully described below: 
  
Lot No. 01 — Name of the Land: Pachchai -
madukadu and Muthiyanmadukadu; Description: 
Garden Contains 02 Coconut trees, 16 Palmyra trees, 
01 Mango tree and a masonry Well;  Claimant: 
Saravanamuthu Natkunam; Extent: 0.9594 Hectare. 
 
Boundaries (Lot 01): 
North by TP 169428 & Road; East: Road & TP 
169428; South:  TP 169428, TP 304539 & Lot 02; West : 
Lot 02 TP 304539 & TP 169428. 
Lot No. 02 — Name of the Land: Pachchai -
madukadu and Muthiyanmadukadu; Description: 
Garden Contains 12 Palmyra trees: Claimant: 
Natkunam Wimalenthiran; Extent: 1.9484 Hectare. 
 
 
Boundaries ( Lot 02): 
North by : TP 304539, TP 169428, Lot 01; East: Lot 
01, TP 169428 and  TP 304539; South: TP 304539; West: 
TP 304539. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Koralaipattu 
Secretary’s Office on 28th February, 2017 at 9.30 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                  
(in duplicate) on or before 21st February, 2017 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation and the details of the computation of 
such compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount. All deeds and documents in support of the 
claims should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
                            
 V. VASUTHOEVAN SLAS-1 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretariat, 
 Koralaipattu.  
 
06th February, 2017.
