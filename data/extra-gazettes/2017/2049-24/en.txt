THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake,  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotment of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. R/
ELP/2016/300 and dated 12.08.2016 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Village of Amuwala (FVP 81), in the Minor
Division of Pallepaththuwa in Divisional Secretary's Division of
Elapatha in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province:
01. Lot  ‘A’ –  Muruthagahahena – Extent: 0.0086 Hectare
North: Muruthagahena claimed by Akuretiya Gamage
Chandrasena; East: Muruthagahena claimed by Akuretiya Gamage
Chandrasena and Muruthagahahena and the Muruthagahahena where
Medical Office of Health; South: Muruthagahahena where Medical
Officer of Health office and Roads (P.S.); West: Road (P.S.) and
Muruthagahahena claimed by Akuretiya Gamage Chandrasena.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
