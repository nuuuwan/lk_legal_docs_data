LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT,  No.  28  OF
1964 (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
DIK/LAN/02/30/42.
I, hereby give notice, under Subsection (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public prupose.
Details of the land to be obtained :
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary plan No. 2701
of Surveyor General Matara and situated in village of
Pathegama Central Grama Niladhari Division of Pathegama
Central in Dickwella Divisional secretariat of Matara District.
01. Lot Number 01
Name of land: Godellahena; Description: Home Garden,
Mixed Cultivation is there; Claimed by: Malavi Arachchige
Wijesekara, Gegawawaththa, Rathmale; Extent: 0.0136 Hectare.
02. Lot Number 02
Name of land: Paranawaththa; Description: Home Garden,
Mixed Cultivation is there; Claimed by: Sirisena
Samarawickrama, Karunawathi Samarawickrama and Don
Hendrik Samarawickrama, Godellahena, Pathegama,
Kottagoda; Extent: 0.0121 Hectare.
03. Lot Number 05
Name of land: Palawaththa; Description: Home Garden,
Mixed Cultivation is there; Claimed by: Malavi Arachchige
Wijesekara, Gegawawaththa, Rathmale; Extent: 0.0747 Hectare.
04. Lot Number 06
Name of land: Palawaththa; Description: Home Garden and
Scrub; Claimed by: Malavi Arachchige Wijesekara,
Gegawawaththa, Rathmale; Extent: 0.0349 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing before me at the Dickwella Divisiona Secretary's Office
on 31.08.2017 at 10.30 a.m. are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 24.08.2017 the nature
of there interest in the land, the particulars of there claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount All deeds and documents in support of the claims
should be produced before me at the inquiry.
B. S. RANJITHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat ,
Dickwella.

I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
Act, No. 28 of 1964, (Amendment) that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
All that divided and defined allotments of land called
Kurunduwattehena in total Extent of 0.0125 Hectare depicted
as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary Plan Maha 6593 dated 24.05.2017
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village called
Uggahakumbura in the Galasiyapaththuwa Minor Division in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Poojapitiya in Kandy
District of the Central Province of which the boundaries are
given below.
Lot No. 01 – Name of the land: Kurunduwatthehena; Name
and addres of the Claimant: D. T. Kamalawathie, School
Lane, Marathugoda, Poojapitiya; Extent: 0.0126 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North: Canal; East : Kurunduwatthehena claimed by
Dombagammana Thalagahapele Gedara Kamalawathie; South:
Lot No. 1 in Priliminary Plan Maha/4673, Lot No. 2 of this plan
and Kurunduwatthehena Claimed by Dombagammana
Thalagahapele Gedara Kamalawathie; West: Lot No. 1 in
Priliminary Plan Maha/4673, Lot No. 2 of this plan and the
canal.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before me
at the Poojapitiya Divisional Secretariat on 13th of September,
2017 at 1.00 p.m. and are hereby requested to notify me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before 06th of September, 2017 the
nature of their interest in the land/servitude, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
M. W. M. M. M ADAHAPOLA,
Divisional Secretary,
Poojapitiya Division.
Divisional Secretariat,
Poojapitiya.
