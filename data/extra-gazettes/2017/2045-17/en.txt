THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35  of  May 28, 2009 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette  Extraordinary ,   No. 2028/34  of  July  19, 2017) which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is deemed, by virtue of the provisoins of
Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act as follows :
(1) by the addition immediately after the entry “12. Department of Anthropology” appearing in Column II of the
Schedule which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences” appearing in Column I of
the Schedule, of the following entry :-
“13. Department of Philosophy and Psychology” ;
(2) by the addition immediately after the entry “9. Department of Statistics” appearing in Column II of the Schedule
which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Applied Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule, of the
following entry :-
“10. Department of Sports Science”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
10th November 2017.
