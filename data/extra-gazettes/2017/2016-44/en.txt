THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No.  40  OF  2008
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Higher Education and Highways under Section 89(2)(c) of the National 
Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Section 13 of the aforesaid Act. 
   L AKSHMAN KIRIELLA, 
   Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo, 
28th April, 2017.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the National Thoroughfares (User Fee National Highways) (Special Provisions) 
Regulations No. 1 of 2017.
2. A driver who is driving a bus of any category shall be exempt from the payment of the User Fee as speciﬁ ed in -
 ( a) Regulation 3 of National Thoroughfares (Colombo-Katunayake User Fee National Highway) Regulations 
No. 1 of 2015 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1912/2 of April 27, 2015 in respect of the use 
of the Colombo-Katunayake User Fee National Highway ; 
 ( b) Regulation 3 of National Thoroughfares (User Fee National Highway) Regulations No. 2 of 2015 published        
in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1924/8 of July 20, 2015 in respect of the use of Southern User Fee National 
Highway, Galle-Matara User Fee National Highway and Kottawa-Kadawatha Section of Outer Circular 
User fee National Highway,
  from 12.00 midnight on April 30, 2017 to 12.00 midnight on May 1, 2017.
