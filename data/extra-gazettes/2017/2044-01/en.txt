No. 2044/1 – MONDAY, NOVEMBER 06, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land called ‘Hunnasgiriya Watta’ in extent of
5.6869 Hectares depicted as Lots ‘A’ and ‘B’ in the Advance Tracing
No. Ma/UKW/2010/65 and dated 30.04.2010, prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys, Matale and situated in the Village of
Imbulpitiya in the Minor Division of Matale Udasiya Pattuwa in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ukuwela in Matale District in
Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Hunnasgiriya Watta – Extent: 0.8424 Hectare
North: Bulathwatta Oya; East: Pradeshiya Sabha Road; South:
Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Hunnasgiriya Watta claimed by
R. M. S. Kumara; West: Hunnasgiriya Watta claimed by R. M. S.
Kumara, Road (R.D.A.) and Bulathwatta Oya.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Hunnasgiriya Watta – Extent: 4.8445 Hectares
North: Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Bulathwatta Oya; East:
Road; South: Road, Hunnasgiriya Watta claimed by State Plantation
Corporation and Channel; West: Channel and Pradeshiya
Sabha Road.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
