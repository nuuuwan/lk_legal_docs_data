L.D.B 21/78(xiv)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4) (b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
the Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute have failed
to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Jayantha Walihinda to be the Competent Authority with
effect from November 26, 2016 for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of Director of Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, any power, duty or function which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to
the said Director under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, by-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
31st December 2016.
