SPECIAL COMMODITY LEVY ACT, N O. 48 OF 2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, 
Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose 
in respect of list of the commodities speci ﬁ ed in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, 
at the rates speci ﬁ ed in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a 
period of 06 months eommencing from 21st May, 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of 
the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
