TOWN   AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  269)
Order under sections 6 (2) (b)  and 21 (2) (b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 6(2)(b) and section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development do by this Order:-
(a) declare that the area of land called the "Ganemulla Thibbotugoda Purana Sri Sugatharama Raja Maha Vihara sacred
Area" specified in the Schedule hereto shall be an urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance;
and
(b) direct than an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the urban development area declared to be the "Ganemulla
Thibbotugoda Purana Sri Sugatharama Raja Maha Vihara sacred Area";
PATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and
Western Development.
27th September, 2017,
Colombo.
