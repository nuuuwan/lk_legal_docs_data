THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in extent of  3.2287 Hectares depicted as lot
A in the Advance Tracing No. KE/WRK/2016/185 and dated
12.08.2016  prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at Bopitiya
Village in Beligal Korale Minor Division, in Warakapola Divisional
Secretary's Division in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot A  – Pallegama State (Batuwana Division) – Extent:
3.2287 Hectare
North: Degalasse Gal Athula; East: Degalasse Gal Athula and
Kiriwanawaththa; South: Kiriwanahena, Road (Pradeshiya Sabha),
Pallegama Waththa and Pansalwaththa; West: Kotuwewatha and
Degalasse Gal Athula.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
