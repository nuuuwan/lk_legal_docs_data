STRATEGIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS ACT, No. 14 OF 2008
Revocation of Notification under Section 3(4)
I, Malik Samarawickrama, being the Minister in charge of the subject of Development Strategies and International
Trade declare by this notification that the following Gazette Notification shall hereby be rescinded.
1. The Notification published under Sub-section (4) of the Section 3 of the Strategic Development Projects
Act, No. 14 of 2008 as amended, in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1847/35 dated 30.01.2014.
MALIK S AMARAWICKRAMA, MP,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
14th June, 2017.
