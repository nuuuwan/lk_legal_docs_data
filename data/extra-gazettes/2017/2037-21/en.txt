1A – G 26289 – 17 (2017/09)
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in Ananthapuri village in extent of 12.2677
Hectares depicted as Lot 'A' in Advance Tracing Plan No.: TRI/
TWG/2014/199A and dated 30.03.2015 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated within the Town and Gravets, Pradeshiya Saba
limits of Trincomalee situated in No.: 243L in the Puliyankulam
Grama Niladhari Division, in  Divisional Secretary's Division of
Town and Gravets, in Trincomalee District in Eastern Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A' – Panangkadu, Pulavutari, Tennanthotakadu,
Muthilinganthotakadu –  Extent: 12.2677 Hectares
North: Muthilinganthotakadu, Tennanthotakadu, Panangkadu,
Pulavutari and Path; East: Road(R.D.A.), Panangkadu, Pulavutari
and Path; South and West:  Tennanthotakadu, Muthilinganthotakadu
and Path.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
