THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
             My No.: NYGM/LAN/7-41.
Land Ministry's Ref. No.: 13/99/VE/33.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of theLand
Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends to acquire the
following Land/Servitude for a public purpose.
Description of the land/Servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of Land described below and depicted in Plan No.
FVP 592 Supplementary sheet No. 26 and 27 situated in No. 30 B,
Pitigala North Grama Niladhari Division of  Niyagama Divisional
Secretary's Division in Bentara Walallavita Korale South in Galle
District  Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 1028
Name of Land: Miriswathu Mookalana; Present Occupation:
Crown; Name and address of the Claiment: W. B. Thilakarathna
Peris (deceased) (spouse and two Children); Extent: 2.0462
Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/
servitude are hereby requested to appear personally, or
by agents duly authorised in writing, before me at
Niyagama Divisional   Secretariat, on 06th day of
November, 2017 at 10.30 a.m. and further requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
09.10.2017 the nature of their interest in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the land/servitude, the amount of
compensation and the details of computation of such
amount.
H. B. G . PRAGEETH RASIKA KUMARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Niyagama,
  Niyagama Division.
Divisional Secretariat,
Niyagama,
06th September, 2017.
