1A  - G  25970— 17  (07/2017)
THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  02 OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Repubic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of
the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development being of the opinion that the land specified in the Schedule
below is urgently required for the restoration of the Bus Stand under the Town Development Project of Eheliyagoda, do
by this order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
25th July, 2017.
SCHEDULE
All allotments of land depicted as Lot A of Advanced Tracing No. R/EHL/2015/994 made by Superintendent of
Survey on 17.06.2016, in respect of land situated in Kandegamuwa Village in Pallepattuwa Minor Division within the area
of authority of the Divisional Secretary’s Division, Eheliyagoda in the District of Ratnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province
containing in extent of about 01 acre, 00 roods and 12.01 perches of which boundaries are as follows :-
North : Eheliyagoda Pradheshiya Sabha premises and Pussellagehenewatta land where R/Eheliyagoda
Central College is located.
East : Pussellagehenewatta land where R/Eheliyagoda Central College is located and Lot No. B
South : Lot No. B, Pussellagehene deniya claimed by L. R. Dharmasiri and others, and roads (RDA) ;
West : Pussellagehene deniya claimed by L. R. Dharmasiri and others, Roads (RDA) and
Pussellagehenewatta land where Eheliyagoda Pradeshiya Sabha premises is located.
