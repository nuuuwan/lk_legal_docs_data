No. 2049/13  –  TUESDAY DECEMBER 12, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  Reference No.: 6/3/1/507. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the  
Government intends to acquire the following Lands 
which is required  for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or to be acquired : 
 
An allotments of land depicted as Lot No. 66 in  
Cadastral Map No. 530176 dated 26.05.2017 prepared by 
the Surveyor General  in the Village of Kumbuka in 
Kumbuka Pattu in the Divisional Secretaries, Division of 
Horana in  Kalutara District Western Province Containing 
in extent about 0.0459 Hectare. 
 
01. Lot No. 66 
Name of the land: Galgodakele; Description: Garden; 
Claimant of the Land: State; Extent 0.0459 Hectare. 
 
 
 
 All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  are 
hereby required to appear Personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the  Divisional 
Secretariat, Horana  on the 28 day  of December 2017 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd day of 
December 2017 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, and the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
 
   
 
  H. A. KAMAL PUSHPA KUMARA, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
  Horana. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Horana, 
06th  December,  2017.
