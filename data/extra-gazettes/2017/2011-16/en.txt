Northern  Provincial  Council
Supplementary Provision Appropriation  Statute No. 07 of 2016
A Statute to provide Supplementary Provisions to previously approved expenditure of the Northern  Provincial
Council for the financial year 2016 by the Honorable Governor on the 25th day of December 2015 in accordance
to First Schedule to this statute and to make payments by way of advance out of the Northern Provincial
Council funds or monies to provide for the refund of such monies to the Northern Provincial Council funds
and to make provisions for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council  as follows:–
1. This Statute may be cited as the Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statue No 07 of
2016.
