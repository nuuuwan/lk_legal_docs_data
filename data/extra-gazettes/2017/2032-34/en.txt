ANNUAL    PERAHERA     OF    BADULLA    KATHARAGAMA    DEVALAYA
IN accordance with the powers vested in me by Gazette notifications No. 9902 of 10.09.1948, No. 10283 of 17.08.1951 and
No. 14778 of 25.03.1955 published under Chapter 362 of the Pilgrimage Act, and under the powers conferred on Divisional
Secretaries by Act No. 58 of 1992 and according to the constitution published in No. 1622 Gazette Notification of 02.10.2009
on Annual Perahera of Badulla Katharagama Devalaya I, W. M. R. Ranjith, the Divisional Secretary of Badulla do hereby
make the following orders in connection with the Annual Perahera of Badulla Katharagama Devalaya, 2017:
01. I do hereby notice that, the Annual Perahera of Badulla Katharagama Devalaya shall begin with “Kap Situvima” on
23rd of August, 2017 and by having inner Perahera from 23rd August to 27th August, 2017 (5 days), by  having
Kumbal Perahera from 28th August to 01st September, 2017 (5 days), by having Randoli Perahera from 0 2nd
September to 06th September, 2017 (5 days), by having water cutting ceremony in the morning on 07th September,
2017 and end in the afternoon on 07th September, 2017 after having returned to the Devalaya;
02. Route of the Perahera :–
The Perahera shall start from Badulla Katharagama Devalaya premises and proceed along Badulla Lower Street,
Passara Road, (Muthiyangana Roundabout), Longden Road, Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Cocowatte Road,
Udayaraja Mawatha, King’s Street, Bazzar Street, Lower Street and back  to  Badulla Katharagama Devalaya;
03. Cooking and selling articles of trade are prohibited in and around Badulla Katharagama Devalaya Compound where
the throne of the Devalaya located;
04. Private vehicles are prohibited to park in the main roads during the Perahera Season. All types of vehicles, including
bicycles and carts are prohibited to enter the Devala area without a permit issued by me or the Nilame in-Charge of
the Devalaya;
