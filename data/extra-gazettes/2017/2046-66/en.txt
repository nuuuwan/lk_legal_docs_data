THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Lunupitiyawatta depicted as Lot ‘‘A’’ in
the Advanced Tracing No. PU/CHL/2016/419 dated 11.11.2016
prepared by Surveyor - General in situated in the village of
Munneshwaram in the Munneshwaram Pattuwa Minor Division, in
the Divisional Secretary's Division of Chilaw Puttalam District, North
Western Province,  and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Lunupitiyawatta –  Extent: 0.2240 Hectare
   North: Land of Lunupitiyawatta claimed by Kanagasabe Rajamani;
East: Road and Land of Lunupitiyawatta claimed byNational Water
Supply and Drainage Board; South: The Canal; West: Land of
Lunupitiyawatta claimed by Vellaiya Nagendradevi.
