CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sectioni 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy do by this Order approve the proposed acquisition of lands
required by the Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
29th March 2017.
SCHEDULE
Land depicted in the tracing No. CEB/MOGL/MHL/01 dated 18.04.2013 prepared by Ceylon Electricity Board
in total extent of 07 hectares, situated in, Galatha GN Division (No 1185) in the Divisional Secretary Division of Udapalatha,
in the Kandy District, in the Central Province, in the Island of Sri Lanka and bounded as follows :
North : Mahaweli River ;
East : Atabage Oya and Tail race of Kotmale Power House ;
West : Mahaweli River ;
South : Mahaweli River and Land belongs to Kotmale Power House.
Claimed by : Not identified.
