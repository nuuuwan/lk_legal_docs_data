EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order amend item No. 1(d) of the Schedule II of the Order
made under that Section and published by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1992/29 dated 10.11.2016, by
substitution of the following Schedule hereto.
This Order shall come into effect from 12.07.2017.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of  Finance and  Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
10th July, 2017.
SCHEDULE
Description Excise Duty
1 (d) Purchase of a locally manufactured motor vehicle or import of a 30% of the
motor vehicle by a Sri Lankan Diplomatic Officer who has served in payable duty
missions abroad, using a permit issued by the Ministry of Foreign
Affairs  under the Circular No. 210(III) dated 31.10.2016 and
subsequent  amendments.
