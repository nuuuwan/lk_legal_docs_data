STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether  the claim made by Mr. J. S. W. Bulathsinhala who was employed as a Wharf Executive of Pership Synergy (Pvt) Limited that, he
had been caused injustice by the non payment of Rs. 3997/- spent by him in the clearance of private container bearing No. 17345-3 while
carrying out Job No. PS/SI/26/09/14/10094, PS/CL entrusted on him by  the Company and  the deprival of entitlements and priviledges enjoyed
by  employees in posts similar to his, and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGA,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 18th day of November, 2016.
