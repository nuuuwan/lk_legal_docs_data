ORDER  MADE  UNDER  SECTION  10  OF  THE  ASSISTED  SCHOOLS  AND  TRAINING  COLLEGES
(SUPPLEMENTARY  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  08 OF 1961
Dambuwa Buddhist Sinhala Mixed School
I, Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, the Minister of Education, functioning in terms of the Powers vested on me under Section 10
of the Assisted Schools and Training Schools (Supplementary Provisions) Act, No. 08 of 1961, do hereby cancel the land
extent applicable only up to 02 Roods and 20 Perches specified in the Order of Transfer No. 1151 published in the
Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 12,998 dated 28.03.1962 of the Government of Sri Lanka with effect from 18th August
2017.

KILA VIRAJ KARIYAWASAM,
