1A  PG  3559 — 557  (06/2017)
EXCISE   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,   No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order amend item No. 1(c) of the Schedule II of the Order
made under that Section and published by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1992/29 dated 10.11.2016, by
substitution of the following Schedule hereto.
This Order shall come into effect from 10.06.2017.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
07th June 2017.
SCHEDULE
Description Excise Duty
Purchase of a locally manufactured motor vehicle or import of a motor
vehicle by a public officer using a permit issued under the Trade and
Investment Policy circular No. 01/2016 of 14.07.2016 and/or subsequent
amendments, with the Cost, Insurance, Freight (CIF) value of the vehicle
(i) Not more than USD 25,000 35% of the
payable duty
(ii) More than USD 25,000 and not more than USD 30,000 40% of the
payable duty
