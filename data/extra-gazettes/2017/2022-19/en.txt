of the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intends 
to acquire the under mentioned lands which are required 
for the purpose of construction of the Medirigiriya 
Kantale Road under the Medirigiriya, Moragahakanda, 
Kaluganga Development Project.

No. 96, Diggalpura Grama Niladhari’s Division in the 
Minor Division of Sinhala Pattuwa in the Medirigiriya 
Division Secretary’s Division in the District of 
Polonnaruwa in the North Central Province.
