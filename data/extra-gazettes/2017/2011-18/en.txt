EXTRAORDINARY
THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
IT  is hereby notified that the road, which has been described in the following Schedule, has been re-named as “P. B. Alwis
Perera Mawatha” by the Honourable Chief Minister and Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Local Government of the
Western Provincial Council under Section 71(1), Chapter 252 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance which shall be read
with the Section 02 of the Provincial Councils Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
V . K. A. A
NURA,
Municipal Commissioner,
Colombo Municipal Council.
Municipal Council,
Town Hall,
Colombo 07,
Schedule
01. Name of the Local Authority : Colombo Municipal Council
02. District : Colombo
03. Present Name of the Street : Muhandiram Road
04. New Name of the Street : P. B. Alwis Perera Mawatha
05. Description of the Road : This road, starting between Assessment No. 157 and Assessment
No. 159 near Kollupitiya Road, belonging to Kollupitiya Municipal
Ward and ends near Alwis Place between Assessment No. 25, and
Assessment No. 215.
