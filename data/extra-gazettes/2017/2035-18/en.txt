THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section, (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed  for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Padasalai Valavoo’’ in extent of  3.9354
Hectares, depicted as Lot A  in the Advance Tracing No.: TRI/TWG/
2015/316 and dated 31.03.2016, prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys -Trincomalee, situated within the Town
and Gravets, Kattukulam Patthu, Urban Council Limits of
Trincomalee situated in the Thirukkadaloor Grama Niladhari Division
No.: 243F, in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Town and Gravets,
Trincomalee District in Eastern Province and bounded as fallows.
