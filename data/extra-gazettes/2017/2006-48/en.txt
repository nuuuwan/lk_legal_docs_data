THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,
hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a public
purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land named as lot "A'' and in extent of about 0.7025
Hectare depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KE/GLG/2016/210 dated
10.08.2016, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village
of  Egalkanda  in the Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 65C - Nadeniya
in the Kandupita Pattuwa South minor division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Galigamuwa in Kegalle District, in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot A – Pindeniya Watta Boyagoda Kotasa – Extent:
0.7025 Hectare
North: Road, canal and Pindeniya Watta Boyagoda Kotasa ; East:
