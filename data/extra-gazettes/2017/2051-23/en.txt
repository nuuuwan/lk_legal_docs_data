PILGRIMAGES   ORDINANCE
GENERAL Public is hereby notified that Annual Sri Maha Bodhiraja Procession of Sri Shasanalankararama Ancient Temple in
Grama Niladhari Division, Udugama, Divisional Secretariat Division Nagoda, Galle District of the Southern Province scheduled
to be held from Saturday 17th to Tuesday 27th of February in 2018 as per the section 2 of the Pilgrimages Ordinance (Chap. 175)
in terms of the regulations laid down by the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1956/11 dated 01.03.2016 of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
S. T. KODIKARA,
District Secretary/Government Agent,
Administrative District, Galle.
