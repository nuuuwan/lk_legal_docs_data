No. 2006/51 –  FRIDAY, FEBRUARY  17,  2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT) ACT,  
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
DIK/LAN/02/30/42. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under  Subsection (1) of Section 7 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment)  
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Details of  the Land to be obtained : 
 
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary plan 
No. 2700 of surveyor General Matara and situated in 
village of Bambarenda Central Grama Niladhari Division 
of Rathmale in Dickwella Divisional secretariat of Matara 
District. 
  
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Welipanewatta; Description: Home 
garden, Mixed Cultivation is there, There is a grave;  
Claimed by: Malavi Arachchige Wijesekara, Gegawawaththa, 
Rathmale; Extent: 0.0288 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of Land: Senkundagekella; Description: Home 
garden, Mixed Cultivation is there;  Claimed by: Nambuhewa 
Thilakasiri, Senkundagekella, Rathmale; Extent: 0.0240 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Dickwella 
Divisional Secretary’s Office on 20.03.2017 at 10.30 a.m. 
are hereby requested I notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 13.03.2017 the nature of there 
interest in the land, the particulars of there claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. All deeds and documents in 
support of the claims should be produced before me at 
inquiry. 
 
                            
 B. S. RANJITHA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretariat, 
 Dickwella. 
  
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Dickwella, 
13th February, 2017.
