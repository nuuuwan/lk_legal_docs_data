EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
MY No.: UDU/2/2/10/61. 
Land Ministry No.: 13/96/FR/503. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land 
servitude for the public purpose (the conservation of 
Dumbara Mountain Range). 
  
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
 
 
An allotment of land called “Winchfield Park Watta” 
bearing an extent of about 0.4097 Hectare mentioned in 
Maha 1930 Supplement of the Surveyor General, situated 
in the Grama Niladhari Division of Karambakatiya, in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Udadumbara of Kandy 
District, Central Province. 
Name and address of Claimants: 
 
Mr. G. K. G. Piyasena, 
Wadawalakanda, 
Kobonilla. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on 
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara on the 15th 
November, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 8th 
November, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
  SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Udadumbara. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Udadumbara, 
11th September, 2017.
