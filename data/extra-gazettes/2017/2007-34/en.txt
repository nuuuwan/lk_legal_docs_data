STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the disciplinary order given on 23rd July 2014 in accordance with the disciplinary inquiry against  Mr. D. A. Perera who has  been
employed as the Assistant Accountant from  15.10.2001 at the Sri Jayawardanapura General Hospital, controlled under the Sri Jayawardanapura
General Hospital Board  established by Act, No. 54 of 1983. is just, and if not so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. Amarathunga,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo, this 18th day of November, 2016.
