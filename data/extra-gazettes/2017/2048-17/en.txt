THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Serapies Watta in total extent of about
2.0381 Hectares, depicted as lot ' A' in the Advanced Tracing No.KU/
PLG/2014/182 and dated 16th  August, 2014 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village called Panaliya in Udapola
Othota Korale Minor Division in Divisional Secretary's Division of
Polgahawela in Kurunegala District of the North Western Province
and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Serapies Watta – Extent: 2.0381 Hectares
North: Pradeshiya Sabha Road, Serapies Watta claimed by
P.A.Karunawathie and some others; East: Ma Oya; South: Ma Oya
and Road; West: Pradeshieya Sabha Road.
