THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthoney Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands,  hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the servitude over the lands  described below in the following
paragraph 1 are needed for a public purpose and will be servitude
under the provisions of that Act. It described in Paragraph 2 from
which land that the servitude will be acquried.
1. Description of the servitude  - Acquisition of servitude over
the land, for the laying of sewerage pipe lines of Greater Kurunegala
Water Supply and Sanitation Project.
2. Description of the land - Two(02) allotments of  land in total
extent of 0.0119 Hectare, depicted as Lots  'A' and  'B' in the Advance
Tracing No. KU/KRN/2015/668 and dated on 22.12.2015, prepared
by  the Surveyor General, situated at the Village of Madamegama,
(Ward No.11, within the Municipal Council Limits, Kurunegala) in
Kurunegala Town West Grama Niladhari Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Kurunegala, in Kururnegala District, North
Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dangaha Kumbura and Galagawa Kumbura –
Extent: 0.0017 Hectare
North: Aurvedic Hospital Land; East and  South: Lot B; West:
Aurvedic Hospital Land.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Dangaha Kumbura and Galagawa Kumbura –
Extent: 0.0102 Hectare
North: Aurvedic Hospital Land; East: Dangaha Kumbura and
Galagwa Kumbura claimed by T. J . P. Silva; South: Dangaha Kumbura
and Galagwa Kumbura claimed by T. J. P .Silva and Municipal
Council Road; West: Lot A.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
