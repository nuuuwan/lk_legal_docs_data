THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 2 OF 1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the
Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Megapolis and Western Development, being of the opinion that the land specified in
the Schedule I and II hereto is urgently required for the purpose of construction of Public Servants Apartments Complex
and for Urban Development Activities related other development activities related to the construction of a Science and
Technology City under the Megapolis and Western Development Project, do by this Order declare that the said lands is
required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
09th May 2017.
