No. 2050/35  – THURSDAY DECEMBER 21, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  Reference No.: Bala/2/1/4/11. 
Land Ministrie’s No.: LD/05/2001/PC/384. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the  
Government intends to acquire the following Lands/
Servitude*  which is required  for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or to be acquired : 
 
The Land called Maradanawatta extent about 0.0017 
Hectare depicted  as Lot 01 and 02 in the Survey 
General’s Plan of P.P.G.4292 of 14.11.2017 situated in 
88A, Kandegoda Grama Niladhari Division in Balapitiya  
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Galle  District.  
 
Boundaries as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 01 —  Maradanawatta —  0.0016 
Hectare. 
North : Maradanawatta claimed by L. A. P. K. De Silva. 
East   : Maradanawatta claimed by L. A. P. K. De Silva. 
South : Kandegoda Devata Road. 
West  :Kandegoda Devata Road. 
02. Lot No. 02 —  Maradanawatta —  0.0001 
Hectare. 
North  :  Kandegoda Devata Road. 
East    :  Kandegoda Devata Road. 
South :  Maradanawatta claimed by W. Janak Wasantha. 
West   :  Maradanawatta claimed by W. Janak Wasantha. 
 
 All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a Servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required to 
appear Personally or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at the  Divisional Secretariat, Balapitiya  on the 
21st day  of March 2018 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 14th day of March 2018 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
Servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.  
   
 
  B. P. S. ANURADHI, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
  Balapitiya. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Balapitiya, 
16th  December,  2017.
