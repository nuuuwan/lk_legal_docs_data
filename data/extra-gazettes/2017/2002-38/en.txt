Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No. of Land Ministry:LD/05/2005/PT/517. 
Reference No. of Divisional Secretary: Kal/6/Noro/
Thapal/Ath/692. 
 
 
I,  hereby give Notice under Sub-section 1 of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acqusution (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:-  
Description of the land to be acquired: Norochchole Sub 
Post Office. 
 
An allotment of land as depicted as Lot No. 198 in 
Plan of Surveyor General’s, No. FVP 3292 Supplement 
No.9, dated 04th May 2010 to the total extent 0.0869 
Hectare is situated in the Kalpitiya Divisional Secretary’s 
Division in Puthalam District. 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
Land Name: Kuravankudithottam; Description: Pilot of 
Land shown in the Plan No. FVP 3292, supplement 09, Lot 
No. 198, Dated 04.05.2010; Name of owner: Chairman, 
Land Reform Commission; Extent: 0.0869 Hectare 
 
 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which  a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at the office of Divisional Secretariat Kalpitiya, 
on the 26th February 2017 day of at 11.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 15th day of February 2017 the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of the compensation and the detail of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
  H. M. S. P. HERATH, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
                                        Kalpitiya. 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kalpitiya,
