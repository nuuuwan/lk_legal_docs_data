SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order
impose in respect of the list of commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the
rates specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 04 months commencing from 24th  August 2017, subject to provisions
of Sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
M
ANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of  Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
23rd August, 2017.
