AMENDMENT  TO   SECTION  3(1)(a)(i)  OF   THE   MINUTE  ON   THE   SRI  LANKA   AUDIT  SERVICE
THE Section 3(1)(a)(i) of the Minute on the Sri Lanka Audit Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 588/5
of 11th December 1989 and amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary No. 993/17 of 19th September 1997 and
No. 1056/23 of 04th December 1998 and No. 1904/31 of 04th March 2015 has been amended by the Public Service
Commission and the amended Section is published for information.
This amendment will take effect from the date on which it is published in the Gazette.

Amendment to Section 3(1)(a)(i) of Minute on the Sri Lanka Audit Service
The Section 3(1)(a)(i) of the Minute on the Sri Lanka Audit Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 588/5 of 11th December 1989 and amended by the Gazettes Extraordinary No. 993/17 of 19th September 1997 and
No. 1056/23 of 04th December 1998 and No. 1904/31 of 04th March 2015 has been amended by the Public Service
Commission and the amended Section is published for information.
3(1) Promotion of the Deputy Auditors General in Class I Grade I of the Sri Lanka Audit Service to Special
Grade.
(a) Qualifications to be fulfilled :
(i) A postgraduate degree in Accounting or Auditing (with a subject field or a subject) obtained from a
university/institution recognized by the University Grants Commission and a fellow membership of the
Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka or in England and Wales or the Chartered Institute of
Management Accountants, United Kingdom or the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, United
Kingdom.
