THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land in total extent of about 1.0117 Hectare depicted
as Lot A in the Advance Tracing No. KU/UDB/2015/45 dated
27.07.2015 prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the Village
called Ihala Weerakodiyana, in the Grama Niladhari Division, No. 1470
of Ihala Weerakodiyana, in the Yagampaththu Korale Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary' s Division of Udubaddawa in Kurunegala
District of North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Meegaha Kumbura –  Extent: 1.0117 Hectare
   North: Meegaha Kumbura claimant not known; East: Meegaha
Kumbura claimant not known and Ihala Weerakodiyana Kumbukgolla
Wewa; South: Ihala Weerakodiyana Kumbukgolla Wewa and
Kahatagaha Watta claimed by Chandrawathi and Others; West:
Kahatagahawatta claimed by Chandrawathi and Others and Meegaha
Kumbura claimant not known.
