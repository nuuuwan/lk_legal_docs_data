SRI   LANKA   INSTITUTE   OF   ARCHITECTS
The Correction of  the Publication in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2026/ 22 of 03rd July 2017 of the
Revision of  the Regulations Gazetted in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka ( Extraordinary) No. 1416/ 10 of  Tuesday, 25th October 2005
THE  notification relating to Regulations of  the  Sri Lanka Institute of  Architects published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2026/ 22 of 03rd July 2017 is hereby  corrected as follows :
As per the powers vested in the Council of the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects by Section 6 (1) and 6(2) read
with Sections 3 and 4 of the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects Law No. 1 of 1976 as amended by the  Sri Lanka Institute of
Architects  (Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 1996, a decision has been taken to repeal clauses 4.2.2.3 (4) and 4. 2. 2. 4 (4) of
the Regulations of the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1416/ 10 of   25th
October 2005 and to replace the said clauses with the following new clauses. The General Membership of the Sri Lanka
Institute of Architects at the Quarterly General Meeting held on 26th August 2016 has approved the said decision as
required under Section 6 (3) of Sri Lanka Institute of Architects Law No. 1 of 1976 as amended by the Sri Lanka Institute
of Architects (Amendment) Act,  No. 14 of 1996.
4.2.2.3. (4) A Graduate Member upon the expiration of five years from the  date of admission, shall cease to be
a Graduate Member unless on application, the  Council extends such Membership.
4.2.2.4. (4) A Student  Member upon the expiration of eight  years from the  date of admission, shall cease to be
a Student  Member unless on application, the Council extends such Membership.
