 THE  LAND   ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
       My Reference No.: Ambako/3/5/3/24.
Land Ministry Reference No.: LD/05/2006/A/149.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends to acquire the following Land/Servitude Which is
required for a public purpose.
     Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :
Lot No. 01 of Black Water Watta below name land in Total
Extent about 0.7583 Hectares, shown in P .P . No. 3076 and
dated 22.09.2016 prepared by the Senior Superintendent of
Surveyor Nuwara Eliya District, Situated in the village of Black
Water Watta in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Ambagamuwa, in the District of Nuwara Eliya.
Lot No. 01
Claimant's Name and Address: Land Reform Commission,
No. 475, Kaduwela Road, Ganehena, Battaramulla; Extent:
0.7583 Hectare.
Boundaries :
North: P .P . No. 1559 in Lot No. 7, T.P . No. 259510 and Road
(Pradesiya Saba); East: P .P . No. 1559 in Lot No. 9, 8 and P .P .
No. 3076 in Lot No. 2, 3; South: P. P . No. 1559 in Lot No. 8;
West: P .P . No. 1559 in Lot No. 7.
    All  persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisonal Secretariat, Ambagmuwa on the 14th Day of June,
2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before 07th day of June, 2017 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of Compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
R. T. P. SUMANASEKARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ambagamuwa Koralaya.
