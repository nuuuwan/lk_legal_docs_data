THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No.  41  OF  1978   ENACTED   BY  THE  NATIONAL
STATE  ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development
do hereby this Order vest in the Urban Development Authority with effect from today the lands morefully described in
the Schedule hereto being lands vested in the Urban Council of Maharagama.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M.P.),

Secretariat Division of Maharagama in the Colombo District, Western Province and bounded on North by Colombo
Avissawella High Level Road, East by Balance portion of the same land, South by Balance portion of the same land, West
