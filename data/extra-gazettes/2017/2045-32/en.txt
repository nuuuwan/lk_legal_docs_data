EXCISE (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 13 OF 1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I Mangala Samaraweera,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that:
01. The rate of Excise duty as at 09.11.2017 and specified in the Gazette Notification No. 1992/29 dated 10.11.2016 and its
subsequent amendments of Gazette Notification No. 2022/30 dated 09.06.2017, No.2024/1 dated 19.06.2017 and
No. 2027/7 dated 11.07.2017, shall be applicable for a motor vehicle classified under the HS Heading 87.03 imported
with a Letter of Credit (LC) which was opened by an individual in his/her name on or before 09.11.2017 and cleared
from Sri Lanka Customs on or before 30.04.2018.
02. The rate of excise duty specified in the Gazette Notification No. 2044/32 dated 09.11.2017 shall not be applicable for
the import of such Vehicles described in paragraph 01 above.
