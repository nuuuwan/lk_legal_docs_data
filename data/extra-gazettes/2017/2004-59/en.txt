 THE   SERVICE   MINUTE  OF  THE  SRI   LANKA  PRINCIPALS’   SERVICE
1st Amendment
THE Service Minute of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Number 1885/31 and dated 22.10.2014 will be hereby amended
By Order of the Public Service Commission
H. M. G AMINI SENEVIRATHNE,
Secretary,
Public Service Commission.
27th December 2016.
Section Sub Revision
No. Section
(a) The officers, who are in the Grade I Class 2 of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service as at
the effective date of the Service Minute and who have fulfilled qualifications to be
promoted to Class I of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service as at 01.07.2008 in accordance
with the prior Service Minute are absorbed to Class I of the Sri Lanka Principals’
Service.
and
(b) The officers in Grade I Class 2 of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service as at the effective
date of this Service Minute have not fulfilled qualifications to be promoted to Class
I of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service as at 01.07.2008 in accordance with the prior
Service Minute are absorbed to Class I of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service subjected
to the conditions mentioned below
I. the seniority of these officers should be placed below the seniority of all the
other officers absorbed to Class I with effect from 01.07.2008.

II. These Officers are entitled only to the salary of Class I of the Sri Lanka Principals’
Service.
III. The Officers would be qualified to be promoted to the Sri Lanka Education
Administrative/Teacher Educators’ Service under the merit/limited streams, which are
for the Principals of Class I of the Sri Lanka Principals’ Service, from the date of
fulfilling the other qualifications including the period of service required to be
promoted to Grade I under the Service Minute existed as at 30.06.2008.
Note : It is compulsory for the Officers to pass the Efficiency Bar Examination prescribed
for Grade I of Class 2 under the Service Minute existed as at 30.06.2008  or the
Second Efficiency Bar Examination mentioned in the new Service Minute in order to
obtain other benefits except the salary.
