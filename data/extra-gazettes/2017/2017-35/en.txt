PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual Festival of Mother’s National Church, Matara
IT is hereby notified under Section 02 of regulations framed under Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the
Gazette No. 687 of 01.11.1991 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that the Annual Festival of Mother’s
National Church of Matara in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Matara in Matara District, will be conducted on 31st
of August to 10th of September 2017.
D. D. K. W ICKRAMARACHCHI,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
At the Divisional Secretariat - Matara,
On 28th April 2017.
