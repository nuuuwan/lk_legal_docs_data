CODE  OF  CRIMINAL   PROCEDURE   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS) ACT,  No.  2 OF  2013
Order under Section 7(2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 7(2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 2 of 2013, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice do by this Order extend for a further period not exceeding
two years the operation of the provisions of the aforesaid Act, with effect from 2nd February, 2017.
