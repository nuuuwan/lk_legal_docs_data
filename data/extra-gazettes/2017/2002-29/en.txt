SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Lakshman Yapa
Abeywardana, Acting Minister of Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of list of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing from
19th  January 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,
No. 48 of   2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1996/50 of 09.12.2016  in respect of Vegetable Oils  (H.S Heading  Nos. 15.07, 15.11, 15.12, 15.13)  are rescinded with effect
from 19th January 2017.
L
AKSHMAN YAPA ABEYWARDANA,
Acting Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
18th January, 2017.
