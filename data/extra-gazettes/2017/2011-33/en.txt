 APPOINTMENT  OF  AUTHORIZED  OFFICERS   UNDER   THE  PRIV ATE   MEDICAL  INSTITUTIONS
(REGISTRATION)  ACT,  No.  21  OF  2006
Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub section 4 of the Section 14 of the Private Medical Institutions (Registration)
Act, No. 21 of 2006, I, Dr. Rajitha Senarathna, Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine do by this
Notification, appoint the following Officers mentioned in the Schedule hereto shall be the Authorized Officers for the
purpose of the said Act in respect of the area assigned to them in the Schedule and in any other area in respect to which
they may be appointed to act on the exigencies of services. They are appointed by virtue of their ex officio status and this
will cover the existing officers in the Schedule hereto who are holding such posts as well as officers who will be appointed
to such posts from time to time.
D
R. R AJITHA S ENARA THNA,
Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine.
Colombo,
01st March, 2017.
Schedule

Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine,
