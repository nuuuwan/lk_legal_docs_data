 DELEGATION   OF  AUTHORITY  OF  THE  DIRECTOR  GENERAL  OF  DEPARTMENT  OF
DIVINEGUMA    DEVELOPMENT  TO   DIVISIONAL   SECRETARIES
I, Neel Bandara Hapuhinna, who has been appointed as the Director General of the Department of Divineguma Development
by the Section 3(1) of the Divineguma Act, No. 1 of 2013, do hereby deligate powers, functions and duties of the Department
of Divineguma to all the Divisional Secretaries of the Island according to the Section 3(2) of the said Act for the purpose
of implementing the provisions of Divineguma Act within your Divisional Secretariat Division.
When implementing the duties of the Department of Divineguma Development as per this deligation of power,
you will be subjected to the general administration of the Additional Director General (District Secretary/Government
Agent) who has been appointed as per the Section 38 of the Divineguma Act of your Divisional Secretariat Division in
addition to my advices and orders.
N
EEL B ANDARA H APUHINNA,
Director General,
Department of Divineguma Development.
