THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, do hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands  described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of land in extent about 20.2347 Hectares,
depicted as Lots ‘A’ and ‘B’ in the Advanced Tracing No.
Maha/UDD/2013/104 and dated 12th June, 2015 prepared by the
Surveyor-General,  situated in the Village of Meemure in the Gandeka
Korale Minor Division in the Udadumbara Divisional Secretary's
Division in Kandy District and bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Welgala – Extent: 14.0805 Hectares
North: Welgala claimed by land reform commission; East: Welgala;
South: Welgala and Kakilawatte stream; West: Kakilawatte stream
and Welgala claimed by land reform commission.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Welgala – Extent: 6.1542 Hectares
North: Kakilawatte stream and cannel; East: Cannel and Welgala;
South: Welgala and Kakilawatte stream; West: Kakilawatte stream.

Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
