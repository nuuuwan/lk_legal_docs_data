109 E, Ambagahawila in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Akmeemana in Galle District of the Southern Province and
morefully described as follows.
01. Lot No. 1  - Name of  the land: Keselgashena;
Description: Home garden and scrub jungle; Original
claimant Chairman, Land Reforms Commissions, Colombo
7; Claimant: State; Total Extent: 2.1322 Hectares.

particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
