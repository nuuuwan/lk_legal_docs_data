THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act in respect of
the Sri Lanka Institute of Nanotechnology (Private) Limited, a Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 64697, do by this Order and subject to the conditions specified in
the Schedule I hereto, recognize the Sri Lanka Institute of Nanotechnology (Private) Limited as a Degree Awarding
Institution for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein, leading to the award of the Degrees specified in
Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviations are set out in the corresponding entries respectively in
Column II of the Schedule II hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS
Sri Lanka Institute of Nanotechnology (Private) Limited (hereinafter referred to as the “Institute”) shall adhere to the
minimum entry qualifications as prescribed in the Sri Lanka Qualification Framework (SLQF) as follows :-
i. A Bachelor’s Honours/Special degree of SLQF Level 6, in the relevant field ; or
ii. A Bachelor’s Honours degree of SLQF Level 6 in a related field and successful completion of a qualifying
examination ; or
iii. A qualification of SLQF level 7 or above in the relevant field.
