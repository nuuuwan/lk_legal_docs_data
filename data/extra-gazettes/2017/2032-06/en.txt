THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: CHI/LND.1/AC/5/64.
4/3/15/2014/VE/270.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Allotment of land in extent of 1.1114 Hectare described as
Lot No. 01 in Preliminary Plan No. P .P .Pu.5325 of 31.03.2017
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at the village of
Tiththakade in the Tiththakade Grama Niladari's Division of
No. 567 in the Munnessaram Pattu Minor Division in Divisional
Secretary's Division of Chilaw in Puttalam District in the North
Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Devalawatta; Description: 31 permanent
Building and contains Coconut cultivation 10-15 Years Old;
Claimant: Unable to find.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at the Divisional Secretariat, Chilaw on the 01st day
of November, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 15th day
of October, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of thier claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, and the details of the computation of such
amount.
D. M. A. K. DISANAYAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Chilaw .
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Chilaw .
09th August, 2017.
