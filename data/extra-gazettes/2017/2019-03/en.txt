No. 2019/3 –   MONDAY, MAY 15, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION NOTICE 
 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference  No.: 4/3/9/2013/WS/473. 
  
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The lands called Mahawatta, in extent of 0.1010 
Hectares stated in Lot No. 372 depicted in the F.V.P. 33 
Supplementary 18, prepared by the Surveyor -General, 
situated in the village of Moragaspitiya of Udugoda 
Udasiyapattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Pallepola in Matale District of 
Central Province, as bounded as followed. 
 
What is the Public Purpose: Construction of a 
Walmoruwa Water Tank for the Huge Matale Water 
Supply Project. 
Name of the Land: Mahawatta; Claiment: Mr. Konara 
Mudiyanselage Wijerathna Banda, No. 09, Kongahamula, 
Palapathwala.  
North: Lot No. 336 2/2 and Homapola (F.V.P. 42); 
East: Homapola (F.V.P. 42) and Lot No. 6; South: Lot 
No. 336 2/2 and 6; West: Lot No. 6 and 336 2/2. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Divisional Secretariat 
of Pallepola on the 21st day of June 2017 At 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in 
duplicated) on or before the 19th day of May, 2017 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 W. M. ANANDA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pallepola Division.  
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Pallepola, 
20th March, 2017.
