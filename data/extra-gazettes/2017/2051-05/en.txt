DEBT  RECOVERY  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT
Notification under Section 30
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of the powers vested in the Monetary Board established under the Monetary Law Act
(Chapter 422), in terms of Section 30 of the Debt Recovery (Special Provisions) Act, No. 02 of 1990, the Monetary
Board has, having regard to the current rates of bank interest, determined that with effect from 01st January 2018, the
Market Rate of Interest for the purpose of this Act, shall be nine point zero eight per cent (9.08%) per annum.
INDRAJIT  C OOMARASWAMY ,
Chairman of the Monetary Board and Governor,
Central Bank of Sri Lanka.

Colombo,
