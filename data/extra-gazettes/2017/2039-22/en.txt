THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms  hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land, depicted in Advanced Tracing No. GA/YKK/
2016/710 and dated 23.08.2016 prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the Village of Walpola in Minor Division of North Thalpe
Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Yakkalamulla in
Galle District in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pathiranage Watta, Koongasgoda Watta –
Extent: 0.0025 Hectare
   North: Katakanda Brook and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.);
South:Koongasgoda Watta, Pathiranage Watta claimed by H. K.
Leelawathi; West: Katakanda Brook and Koongasgoda Watta,
Pathiranage Watta claimed by H. K. Leelawathi.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kalukanda Watta –  Extent: 
0.0013 Hectare
   North: Road (R.D.A.) and Kalukanda Watta claimed by W. K.
Karunapala; East: Katakanda Brook and Kalukanda Watta claimed
by W. K. Karunapala; South:Katakanda Brook; West: Road (R.D.A.).
02. Lot ‘C’ – Pathiranage Watta –  Extent: 0.0090 Hectare
   North: Katakanda Brook and Pathiranage Watta claimed by
M. K. P . Kalyanawansa; East: Road (R.D.A.) and Pathiranage Watta
claimed by M. K. P. Kalyanawansa; South:Road (R.D.A.); West:
Road (R.D.A.) and Katakanda Brook.
