THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27 (1)
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister of
Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend the
Schedule to the Order made under section 21 of the aforesaid Act, in relation to the Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, published
in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 896/2 of November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 1908/23 of March 31, 2015) by the addition immediately after the entry "5. Department of Languages" appearing in Column
II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item "Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities" appearing in Column I of the
Schedule of the following entry:-
"6. Department of English Language Teaching".
L
AKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th June, 2017.
