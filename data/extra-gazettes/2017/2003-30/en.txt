THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: UDU/2/2/10/60. 
Land Ministry No.: 13/96/FR/502. 
 
I  hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose (the conservation of 
Dumbara mountain range) 
 
       Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called “WinchfieldPark Watta” 
bearing an extent of about 0.4072 Hectare mentioned  in 
P.P. Maha 1930 (Lot Number 11), Supplement of the 
surveyor General, Situated in the Grama Niladhari 
Divison of Karambakatiya, in the  Divisional Secretary’s 
Divison  of Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central 
Province. 
 
Name and Address of Claimants 
Mr. P. G. Piyadasa, Wadawala Kanda, Kobonilla. 
 
All Persons  interested in the aforesaid land/Servitude 
on which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat Office of Udadumbara, on 
the 01st day of March, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)  on or 
before  the 22nd day of February, 2017, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount. 
 
 
 
              SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA, 
                                    Divisional Secretary, 
           Udadumbara. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Udadumbara.
