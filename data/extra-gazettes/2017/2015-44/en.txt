THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act establishing the Uva Wellassa University
of Sri Lanka and published in  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1395/15 of June 01, 2005  by the addition immediately after
item “3. Faculty of Animal Science and Export Agriculture” appearing in Column I of the Schedule which corresponds to
the entries in Column II of the Schedule of the following item and entries.
“Column I Column II
 4. Faculty of Technological Studies 1. Department of Engineering Technology
2. Department of Biosystems Technology.”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
05th April, 2017.
