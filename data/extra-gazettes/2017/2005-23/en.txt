Notice under Section 7 
 
 
I  hereby inform under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the 
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which as required for a public purpose:-
