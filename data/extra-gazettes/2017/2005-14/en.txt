THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2002/32 of January 18, 2017), which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139
of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the substitution for the entry ”13.
Department of Psychological Medicine” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty
of Medicine” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entry :-
“13. Department of Psychiatry”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
25th January 2017.
