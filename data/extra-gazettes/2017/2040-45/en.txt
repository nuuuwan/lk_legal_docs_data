THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT

Notice  under  Section  7

I HEREBY give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land acquisition
(amended) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intend to
acquire the following land/ servitude which is required for a
public purpose.
Description of the land or Servitude to be acquired : Acquire
the land called Menthalawatta under the Village's expansion.
An  allotment, of land Lot No. 112 in extent about 6.0738Hect.
Depicted in supplementary No. 05 in F.V .P . 380 Situated at
Karadiyamulla village Karadiyamulla G . S. Division Kadawatha
Korale Imbulpe  Divisional Secretariat, Rathnapura District
and Sabaragamuwa Province.
G 26423 - 17 (2017/10)
Lot No. 112
Name of the land: Menthalawatta;Claimant: K. Kagnasinham,
Manipe South Manipe; Extent: 6.0738 Hectare.
All  person interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquire are hereby required to appear
personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Imbulpe Divisional  Secretariat on the 26th October 2017 at
9.30 a.m and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the  16th October 2017 the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for the
compensation for the acquisition of the said  land/servitude
the amount of compensation of such amount.
