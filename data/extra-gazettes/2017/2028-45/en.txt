1A  G  25940— 32 (07/2017)
THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby  notified that the notification issued by me in the  exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) of
Article 43 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and published  in the
Gazette  Extraordinary  No. 1933/13  of 21st September, 2015 and  amended from time to time,  is further  amended
with effect from  July 19, 2017  in the manner set out  in the  schedule below:
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
Presidential Secretariat, President.
Colombo 01,
19th July, 2017.
SCHEDULE
(1) With reference to the  Heading, Minister of National Integration & Reconciliation (No. 3) of the said  notification,
as follows :-
(a) In Column I thereof, by the insertion immediately after Item 10 thereof, of the following items ;
“ 11. All matters relating to the Office on Missing Persons (Establishment,   Administration and Discharge
of Functions) Act, No.14 of 2016 ;
12. All other subjects that come under the purview of Organizations listed in Column II ;
13. Supervision of the Organizations listed in Column II”
(b) in Column III thereof, by the addition immediately after “1. Office for National Unity and Reconciliation
(Entrusted with implementing the mandate of the Ministry)” thereof, of the following item;
“ 2.Office on Missing Persons”
(c) In Column III thereof, by the insertion of the  following Item ;
“Missing Persons (Establishment, Administration and Discharge of Functions) Act, No.14 of 2016”
