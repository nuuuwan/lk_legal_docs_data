No. 2032/8 –  TUESDAY AUGUST 15, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)ACT 
No. 28 OF 1964  (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) 
that the Government  intends to acquire the following 
land which is required for a for public purpose:- 
 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0115 
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 137 in Block No. 1 of 9 in 
Cadastral Map No. 510811 dated 24.11.2016 prepared by 
the Survey General situated in the village called 
Kendaliyaddapaluwa East in Adhikari Pattuwa Minor 
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mahara 
in Gampaha District of the Western province and more 
fully described as follows. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
Lot No. 137 
Name of the Land: Pepolgahawatta; Description: 
Home Garden; Claimant: Land Reform Commission; 
Extent: 0.0115 Hectare.  
 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at the Mahara Divisional Secretariat 
on the 24th day of October 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 17th day of October 2017 the nature of 
their interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount. 
 
 
 
          G. S. I. WIMALASENA, 
                                   Divisional Secretary, 
      Mahara. 
 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Mahara.
