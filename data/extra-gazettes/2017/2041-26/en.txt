No. 2041/26  –  THURSDAY OCTOBER 19, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Reference No.: 2/2/1/129. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4/3/9/2014/WS/226. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, That the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is reqiored for a public purpose: 
 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired: 
 
 
An allotment of land called Millagahawatte in an extent 
0.1537 Hectare depicted as preliminary plan  
No. Gam/3998 and dated 24.04.2017 prepared by the 
Senior Superintendent of Surveys Gampaha District on 
behalf of   the Surveys General situated at Galoluwa 
Village, No. 239A Rathupaswala Grama Niladhari 
Division in Gampaha Divisional Secretary Division in 
Gampaha District Western Province. 
 
North: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Delgahawatte 
Claimed by R. P. Wickramaarachchi 
 
East: Delgahawatta claimed by R. P. Wickramaarachchi. 
 
 
South: Lot No. 1 of P. P. Co 3231 ND Millagahawatte 
claimed by B. W. Piyasena. 
 
 
West: Millagahawatta claimed by B. W. Piyasena and  
Road (Pradeshiya sabha) 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at  Divisional Secretariat 
Gampaha on the 20th Day of December 2017, at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 06th day of December 
2017  the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of  the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
  C. P. W. GUNATHILAKE. 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
                                                     Gampaha. 
 
 
 
                            
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Gampaha, 
16th October, 2017.
