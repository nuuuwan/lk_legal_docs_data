UPPLEMENTARY PROVISION FOR APPROPRIATION  STATUTE NO. 08 OF 2017
A Statute to provide Supplementary Provisions to previously approved expenditure of the Northern  Provincial
Council for the financial year 2017 by the Honourable Governor on the 26th day of December 2016 in
accordance to First Schedule to this statute and to make payments by way of advance out of the Northern
Provincial Council funds or monies to provide for the refund of such monies to the Northern Provincial
Council funds and to make provisions for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council  as follows:–
1. This Statute may be cited as the Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statue No. 08 of
2017.
