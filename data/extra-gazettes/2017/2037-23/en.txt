THE  MOTOR  TRAFFIC  ACT  (CHAPTER  203)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Transport and Civil  Aviation under Section 237 of the  Motor Traffic Act, (Chapter
203) read with Sub-section (2) of  Section 5 and Section 19 of  the aforesaid Act.

Colombo,
21st  September, 2017.
Regulations
The Motor Traffic (Motor Tricycle) Regulations, No. 04 of 2017 published in the  Gazette Extraordinary No.
2035/ 51 of September 8, 2017 are hereby amended as  follows :-
(1) by the repeal of regulation 1 thereof  and substitution therefor of the following :-
‘‘1.These  regulations may be cited as the Motor Traffic (Motor Tricycle) Regulations, No. 04 of 2017 and shall
come into operation on January 1, 2018.’’;
(2) in regulation 2 (1) thereof, by the substitution for the words ‘‘transportation of persons for hire or other-
wise’’, of the words ‘‘transportation of persons for hire or otherwise’’; and
(3) in regulation 3 (a) thereof, by the substitution for the words and figures ‘‘at least 382 and 230 millimeters’’, of
the words and figures’’ at least 382 and 230 millimeters’’.
