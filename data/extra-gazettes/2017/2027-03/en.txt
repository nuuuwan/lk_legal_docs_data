CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I,  K. D. N. Ranjith Asoka,  Acting Director General of  Sri Lanka Customs, determine that with effect  from 10th  July, 2017, all
duties of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance  (Chapter 235),
shall be paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 2026/1 of
03rd  July,  2017 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 16th July, 2017.
K. D. N. R
ANJITH   ASOKA,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
07th   July,  2017.
