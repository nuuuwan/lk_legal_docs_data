THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My No.: UDU/2/2/10/98.
Land Ministry No.: 13/97/FR/323.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitude for public purpose (the
conservation of Dumbara mountain range.)
Description of the Land/servitude to be acquired :
An allotment of land called "Martin Watta" bearing an extent
of about 3.8717 Hectare mentioned in Maha 6628 Supplement
of the Surveyor General, situated in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Karambakatiya, in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Udadumbara of Kandy District, Central Province.
Name and Address of Claimants : Mrs. M. Somawathi,
Kumbukgolla, Meemure.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 20th
September, 2017 at 11.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 13th September,
2017 nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Udadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Udadumbara,
