THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare under Sub – section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Two (2) allotments of land in total extent of about 0.9496 Hectare,
depicted in Advanced Tracing No. Maha/PNW/2015/387 of
14.12.2015 prepared by the Surveyor - General  situated in the
Village of Hatale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Panwila
in Kandy District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  ‘A’ - Hatale Estate – Extent - 0.2104 Hectare
North: Hatale Estate claimed by Subramaniam Thiladaram and
Siridaran Thilakam;  East: Hatale Estate claimed by Subramaniam
Thiladaram and Siridaran Thilakam, Canal and Hatale Estate claimed
by the State;  South: Hatale Estate claimed by the State;  West:
Hatale Estate claimed by the State and Canal.
02. Lot  ‘B’ - Hatale Estate – Extent - 0.7392 Hectare
North: Hatale Estate claimed by Subramaniam Thiladaram and
Siridaran Thilakam;  East: Road;  South: Hatale Estate claimed by
the State;  West: Canal.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
                               Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
