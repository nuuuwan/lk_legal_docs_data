THE  FAUNA  AND  FLORA  PROTECTION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  469)
REGULATIONS made by the Minister of Sustainable Development and Wildlife under Section 71 of the Fauna and Flora
Protection Ordinance (Chapter 469) read with Section 44.
Minister of Sustainable Development and Wildlife.
Colombo,
09th March, 2017.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the Fauna and Flora Regulations (Amendment of Schedules) No.     0f 2017.
2. The Schedule VIII of the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance is hereby amended by the omission of item
referred by the scientific name “ Pericopsis mooniana ” under the family name Fabaceae.
