THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : 3/6/2016/10.
Ministry of Land Reference No.: 4-3/7/2016/HW/129.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government
intends  to    acquire the following land/servitude* which is
required for a public purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
Under mentioned land shown in the Surveyor General’s Plan
of F. V . P . 2343, situated in the Village of Parabewila in the
Grama Niladari Division of No. 893 Parabewila in the Minor
Division of Udapolamedalassa Korale in Polgahawela
Divisional Secretary’s Division in Kurunagala District of
North West Province.
1. Lot No. 136
Name of the Land: Katakalagahamulawaththa alias
Damunugahamulawaththa ; Description: Garden; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Dushmanthi Liyanagama;
Extent: 0.0409 Hectare.
2. Lot No. 137
Name of the Land: Katakalagahamulawaththa alias
Damunugahamulawaththa ; Description: Garden; Claimant:
Government; Original Claimant: Thanuja Priyadarshani
Liyanagama; Extent: 0.0102 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally , or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Polgahawela Divisional Secretariat, on the day of the 29th
of March 2017, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd of
March, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for  computation for the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
                 ANOMA DISSANAYAKA,
