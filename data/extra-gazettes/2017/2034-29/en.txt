THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
ORDINANCE made by the University Grants Commission under Section 18 read with Sub-section (2) of Section 24A of
the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978.
Secretary,
University Grants Commission.
Colombo,
28th August 2017.
Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of Science (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 04 of 2017 and
shall come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of Science Ordinance No. 1 of 1996, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 909/13 of February 09, 1996 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1775/22 of
September 14, 2012 is hereby further amended as follows :-
(1) In Paragraph 12 thereof–
(i) by the addition immediately after item (j) thereof, of the following new item :-
“(k) Biomedical Sciences.”.
(2) In Paragraph 22 thereof –
(i) by the substitution for the words “Statistics and Computer Science, or any other speciality” in the definition
of the expression “Speciality in Science” of the words “Statistics and Computer Science, Biomedical
Sciences or any other speciality”.
