TOWN  AND  COUNTRY  PLANNING  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 269)
Order under Section 6(2) (b) and Section 21(2)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 6(2) (b) and Section 21(2)(b) of the Town and Country Planning Ordinance
(Chapter 269), I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development, do by this Order :-
(a) declare that the area of land called the “Yudaganawa Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area” specified in the Schedule
hereto shall be an urban development area for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance; and
(b) direct that an outline physical plan shall be prepared for the “Yudaganawa Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area” which
is declared as above to be an urban development area.
PATALI CHAMPIKA RANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Colombo,
12th June, 2017.
SCHEDULE
The Area of Land Comprising the “Yudaganawa Raja Maha Vihara Sacred Area”
The area of land containing in an extent of 25.4840 Hectares (A-62, R-3, P-35.58) shown by the Surveyor General’s
Tracing No. M.O./BTT/2016/232 dated 08th June, 2016 (T.P. No. 100016) and situated in the village of Yudaganawa village
within the Minor Division of Buttala Korale within the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Buttala in the District of Monaragala
in the Uva Province and bounded as follows :
North : T. P. 100016
East : T. P. 100016
South : T. P. 100016
West : T. P. 100016
