UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Financial Statute No. 08 of 1990
NOTIFICATION   UNDER  ARTICLE  FIVE
I, Chamara Sampath Dassanayaka, Chief Minister of Provincial Council of Uva Province, Minister of Finance, Policy
Planning, Law and Order, Education, Local Government, Land, Power and Energy, Construction and Rural Infrastructure
Facilities, according to the powers assigned to me under the Article 5 of Financial Statute No. 08 of 1990, notify that the
turnover tax will be implemented from 01.07.2017 for the wholesale and retail businesses making a turnover more than
Rupees Five Hundred Thousand (Rs. 500,000) and less than Rupees Three Million (Rs. 3,000,000) per quarter.
C
HAMARA S AMPATH D ASSANAYAKA,
Chief Minister of Provincial Council of Uva Province,
Minister of Finance, Policy Planning, Law and Order, Education,
Local Government, Land, Power and Energy, Construction and
Rural Infrastructure Facilities
Office of the Chief Minister of Uva Province,
Badulla,
30th August 2017.
