L.D.B. 6/77(ii).
EXTRADITION LAW, No. 8 OF 1977
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3(1) of the Extradition Law, No. 8 of 1977, I, Maithreepala
Sirisena, President do by this Order declare that, since a Treaty was signed at Kyiv on the 25th day of June 2016
between the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and Ukraine, a foreign State, in order to establish a
bilateral legal mechanism governing the transfer of sentenced persons and to improve the efficiency of justice
which would develop co-operation between the State parties to the Treaty in the prevention of offences and inflict
of punishment in respect of such offences and that the parties to the Treaty undertake to extradite to each other,
upon request and subject to the provisions of the Treaty, the persons who are found within the territories of the
respective State parties for prosecution or carrying out sentences ; and that the provisions of such Treaty is in
conformity with the provisions of section 4 of the Extradition Law No. 8 of 1977 ; the provisions of the said
Law No. 8 of 1977 shall apply in respect of such foreign State, subject to such modification, limitation or
condition as specified in the Treaty.
            President.
Colombo,
01st June, 2017.
