RIGHT   TO  INFORMATION   ACT,   No. 12   OF   2016
Rules
IN terms of powers vested me under sub section 02 of the section 42 of the Right to Information Act, No. 12 of 2016, it
is hereby, declared approval of the rules, published in the Gazette Extraordinary of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka No 2004/66 – 03rd February 2017 with following amendment, made by the Right to Information Commission
under the powers of the Section 42 of the above Act.
 G AY ANTHA K ARUNA THILAKE,
Minister of Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media.
At the Ministry of Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media,
No.163, Kirulapone Mawatha,
Polhengoda,
Colombo 05,
6
th February 2017.

subsection

(21.59 cm x 35.56 cm) and up to A3 (29.7 cm x 42 cm)
