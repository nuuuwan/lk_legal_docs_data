No. 2046/18 – WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 22, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 4.0468 Hectares
depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/UDP/2016/380 and dated
30.12.2016 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village
of Senquarwatta in Grama Niladhari Division of Paradeka in
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Udapalatha, in Kandy District in
Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  'A'– Senquarwatta – Extent: 4.0468 Hectares
North: Bassrockwatta (C. M. 320196) and Werellapitiya (C. M.
320178); East: Werellapitiya (C. M. 320178) Road and Sanquarwatta
claimed by Land Reclamation Commission; South and
West: Sanquarwatta claimed by Land Reclamation Commission.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
