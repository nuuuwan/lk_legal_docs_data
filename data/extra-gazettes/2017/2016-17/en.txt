EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: MCUDP/LA/ABT/(W-05)/14/03. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/10/2014/LRB/292. 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire and own of the following 
blocks of Land for the construction of a Pumping station 
at left bank of Madiwela East Diversion Canal which is a                 
sub-project under the Metro Colombo Development 
Project.  
 
Description of  the Land :  
 
The undermentioned allotments of land situated in 
Udumulla Village belonging to Udumulla North Grama 
Niladhari Division in the Kolonnawa Divisional 
Secretary’s Division in the District of Colombo, Western 
Province made by the Surveyor General on 11.04.2017 
and depicted as Lot Nos. 1 and 2 in the Preliminary Plan 
No. ෙකʣ. 9685 containing in extent 0.8491.   
1. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Kurundugodalla Ovita alias 
Mahaduwa Pillewa; Land use: Home Garden; Claimant: 
N. Ariyarathne, No. 62, Ambathale, Mulleriyawa New 
Town; Extent: 0.8112  
 
2. Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: KurundugodallaOvita alias 
Mahaduwa Pillewa; Land use: Home Garden; Claimant: 
K. A. D. Nandasiri, No. 112, Lake Road; Udumulla, 
Mulleriyawa New Town; Extent: 0.0378  
All parsons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Land Acquisition Office of the Metro Colombo 
Urban Development Projects located in the Head Office 
of the  Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development 
Corporation, No. 03, Sri Jayawardhanapura Mawatha, 
Welikada,  Rajagiriya on 31st of May, 2017 at 9.30 a. m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                     
(in duplicate) on or before 23rd of May, 2017 the nature 
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land the amount of compensation and the details  of 
the  compensation of such amount. 
 
 C. W. ABESURIYA, 
 Land Acquisition Officer, 
 Metro Colombo Urban Development Project.  
 
 
Metro Colombo Urban Development Project. 
Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development  
   Corporation,
