THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Divisional Secretary Reference No.:DS/HAB/LAN/02/01/31.
Land's Ministry Reference No.: 4-3/6/2015/HW/309.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following Land which is required for a public
purpose:
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired:
An  allotment of Land depicted as Lot Nos. 01, 02 and 03 in
prliminary plan No. G/4220 situated in the village of Goviyapana
in No. 156,  Ahangama - East Grama Niladhari's Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Habaraduwa in Galle District
Southern Province and described as follows.
Lot No. 1
Name of the Land: Oruthotawatta; Description and Claimant
of the Land: Open waste land, H. W . M. A. Gunasekara, Matara
Road, Goviyapana, Ahangama; Extent: 0.0007 Hectare.
Lot No. 2
Name of the Land: Oruthotawatta; Description and Claimant
of the Land: Open waste land, H. U. P . Priyantha, Matara Road,
Goviyapana, Ahangama; Extent: 0.0046 Hectare.
Lot No. 3
Name of the Land: Oruthotawatta; Description and Claimant
of the Land: Open waste land, P . K. A. P . Pranando, No. 118,
Weewala, Jaela; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretary's office, Habaraduwa on the 07th day
of March, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 27th day of
February, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
All the Deeds Documents and extract which are relevant
prove the ownership should be forwarded at the inquiry.
THARANGA WIKRAMARATNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Habaraduwa.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Habaraduwa,
17th January, 2017.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 7
IN the Notice under Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act,
(Chapter 460) which was published by me in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1955/24 dated 26.02.2016 for the plots of
land depicted in the Surveyor General's Irrigation Plan No.
1/40/40 in the village of Higura in Kolonna Korale Minor
Division in Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province,

mentioned as 0.1092 Hectare.

0.0065 Hectare as per the amended plot details registered by
the Survey Department on 16.11.2016.
