EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No.: UDU/2/5/2/60. 
Land Ministry’s No.: 13/2000/FR/34. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude for public purpose (the conservation of 
Dumbara  Mountain Range). 
 
Description of the land/servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called “Karawugaha Madiththa” 
bearing an extent of about 16.2430 Hectare mentioned in 
P.P. Maha 6644, Supplement of the Surveyor General, 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Meemure, in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Udadumbara of 
Kandy District, Central Province. 
 
Name and address of Claimants 
 
1.  N. Amitha Nikapitiya, 
 164/2, Kaduwela Road, 
 Malabe. 
      2. B. Abeykoon, 
 No. 774/3, Millagahawaththa Road, 
 Thalangama North, 
 Malabe. 
 
       3.  Deepthi Naranpanawa, 
 940 E, Uda Watta Road, 
 Thalangama North, 
 Malabe. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude  
on which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorised in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 20th 
September 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify  to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
13th September 2017 nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
            SUJANI R. W IJETUNGA,
                            Divisional Secretary, 
                Udadumbara.
