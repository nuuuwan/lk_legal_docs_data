CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Amendment of the charges published on the Extraordinary Gazette 2014 and No. 1846/12 of the Central
Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and Construction of
the Central Provincial Council hereby announce that by virtue of powers vested by the Article No. 24 of year 1992 and No.
04 of the Passenger Transport Service Authority Statute.
The route permit rates published in the Extraordinary Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
on 21st January 2013 and 20th January 2014, minimum assessed taxonomic rates of A, B, C, D and E roads and the rates for
the bus permits given only to run “Sisusariya” Schoole service, the following amendments should have to be made respectively.
The creation of the amendments charges will be implemented from 01.01.2017.
Subject The previsous charges Amended charges
