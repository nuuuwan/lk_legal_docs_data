THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below are needed  for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the Provisions of that
act.
The  allotments of lands Lots 'A' to 'E'  in total extent of about
1.7104 Hectares, depicted in the Advanced Tracing No.
KE/GLG/2016/428 and dated  25.05. 2017 prepared by the Surveyor
General, situated in the Villages called Renapana in the Grama Niladari
Division of No: 68B, Renapana in the Kandupita Pattuwa North
Minor Division in the  Divisional Secretary's Division of Galigamuwa
in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa) –
Extent:  0.2050 Hectare
North: Dikkella Watta ;East: Dikkella Watta and Road; South:
Road; West: Road and Dikkella Watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa) –
Extent: 0.7794 Hectare
North: Dikkella Watta and Road;East: Road; South: Road and
Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa); West: Kudumeriyawatta
(Moragahakanda Kotasa) and Dikkella Watta.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa)  –
Extent: 0.6930 Hectare
North: Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa); East: Road
and Dikkella Watta; South and West: Road.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa) –
Extent: 0.0079 Hectare
North: Road; East: Dikkella Watta; South: Polkotuwe Kumbura;
West: Road.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Kudumeriyawatta (Moragahakanda Kotasa)  –
Extent: 0.0251 Hectare
North: Road and Polkotuwe Kumbura; East: Polkotuwe Kumbura;
South and West: Road.
