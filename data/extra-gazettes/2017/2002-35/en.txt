THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, amend the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid
Act, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 420/25
of September 26, 1986 in relation to the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, (as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1995/27 of December 1, 2016 as follows :-
(1) in sub-paragraph (e) of paragraph (iv) of that Order, by the substitution for the words “the Faculty of Health-
Care Science ; and” of the words “the faculty of Health-Care Science ;” ;
(2) by the addition immediately after sub-paragraph (e) of  paragraph (iv) of that Order, of the following sub-
paragraph (f) :-
“(f) the Faculty of Technology ; and” ;
(3) by the addition immediately after the entry “Faculty of Health-Care Sciences” appearing in Column I and its
corresponding entries in Column II in the Schedule to such Order, of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Technology 1. Department of Bio System Technology
2. Department of Multidisciplinary Studies.”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
31st December 2016.
