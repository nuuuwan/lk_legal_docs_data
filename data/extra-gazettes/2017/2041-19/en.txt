PROMOTION  AND   APPOINTMENT  APPROVED  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that his Excellency the President has been pleased to promote Rear Admiral Travis Jeremy Liyanduru
Sinniah, WWV , RWP, RSP and bar, USP ctf, ndu, psc to the rank of Vice Admiral with effect from August 22, 2017 and to
appoint him under Article 61E. (1)(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Head of the
Sri Lanka Navy, with effect from that date.
                                          By His Excellency’s  command,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
 October 16, 2017.
