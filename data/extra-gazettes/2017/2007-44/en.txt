SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 5
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Section 5 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake,  Minister of Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby make an Order to waive
off Rs. 10 per kg. from the applied Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 15 per kg. on the importation of rice, classifiable
under the HS Codes of 1006.30.10, 1006.30.20 and 1006.30.30 listed out in the Schedule of the Order published in the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 2000/85 of 06th January 2017.
In terms of this Order, a Special Commodity Levy of Rs. 05 per kg. on importation of rice shall be collected for
a period of 30 days effective from 27th February 2017.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
 Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
26th February, 2017.
