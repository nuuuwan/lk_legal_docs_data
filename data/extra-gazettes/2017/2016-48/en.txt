EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: BD/EL/LND/2/AQUSN/14. 
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands: 4-3/9/2015 
WS/107. 
 
I  hereby give Notice under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose:-  
     Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “ Pitawela Arawa alias 
Nethervil; Estate”  in total  extent of about 0.0998 
Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 291 in Final Villge Plan No. 
2A, Suppliment No. 13, Sheet No. 15, dated on 
30.01.2017, surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor 
General situated at the village of Gotuwela in 
Madhuragama Grama Niladhari Division, No. 68C, in 
Kumbalwela Korale Minor Division  in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Ella in Badulla District, Uva 
Province and bounded as follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 291 
Name of the Land: Pitawela Arawa alias Nethervil 
Estate; Extent: 0.0998 Hectare. 
Boundaries: 
North by : Lot No. 38; East by : Lot No; 38 and 40; 
South by: Lot No. 40 and 38; West by: Lot No. 38 
 
Name and Address of the claimants:  
1. Hapugastenna Plantation Company (P.L.C.),                      
No. 95A, Nambapana, Ingiriya. 
 
All Persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at  Divisional Secretary’s Office at, 
Ella on the 27th July 2017 and at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing duplicate to on or  
before 20th July 2017 the nature of their interest in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
  K. D. THANUKSHA N. ABEYWARDANA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Ella. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Ella,
