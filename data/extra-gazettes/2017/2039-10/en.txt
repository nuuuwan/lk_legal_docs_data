THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No. 2 OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of
the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of urban development being of the opinion that the land specified in the Schedule
below is urgently required for science, technology and associated urban development purposes in Stage II of Mahenawatte,
do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
29th September 2017.
SCHEDULE
All allotments of land depicted as Lots 199, 200, 201, 203 and 205 in Zone No. 22 out of 22 zones in the
cadastral map No. 520006 made by Survey General on 30.12.2008, in respect of part of land situated at Pitipana North in
Pitipana North Grama Niladhari Division No. 484 within the area of authority of the Divisional Secretary’s Division,
Homagama in the District of Colombo in Western Province containing in extent of about 51 acres 02 roods 01 perch of
which boundaries are as follows :
Lot No. 199, Mahenawatte - 02 acres 29.40 perches in extent
North : Lot No. 195 and road ;
East : Road ;
South : Lots No. 203 and 200 ;
West : Lots No. 203 and 200.
