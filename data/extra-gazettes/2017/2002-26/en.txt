THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order. revoke the Order made under Section 25A of the Universities
Act, No. 16 of 1978, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 536/15 of December 15, 1988, recognizing the Institute
of Technological Studies, a Private Limited Liability Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the provisions of the
Companies Act, No. 17 of 1982 as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing higher education leading to
the conferment of bachelor’s degrees in “Computer Science and in Computer System Design” without prejudice to anything
previously done thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
13th January 2017.
