THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)(b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1)(b) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, amend the Order made under Section 25A of the said Act
relating to the granting of Degree Awarding Status to the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) (Pvt)
Ltd, published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1721/19 of August 30, 2011 as amended by Order published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1829/36 dated September 26, 2013 by deleting paragraph (c) of item 4 and item 4A of the Schedule to
the aforesaid Order with effect from December 01, 2017.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
11th December, 2017.
