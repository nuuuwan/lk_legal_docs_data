LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDEMENT)  ACT  No.  28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: GAL/LAN/AQUIS/A13/43.
Ministry of land Ref  No.: 4-3/15/2016/VE/226.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire/servitude the Lands described in the schedule given
below.
Description of the lands to be acquired:
Allotments of land in extent about 2.8328 Hectares depicted
in Plan No. Ke 3550 dated 20.06.2017 prepared by Surveyor
General situated in the villages of Jayamalpura in 61B
Pothukoladeniya Grama Niladari Division in Kandupita Pattuwa
North minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division
Galigamuwa in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuva Province.
Allotment No. 01
Name of  the Land: Pindeniya Watta - Upper Division;
Claimants: 01. Managing Director, Kegalle Plantations,
No. 310, Highlevel Road, Navinna, Maharagama, 02. Chairman,
land reformation Commission, No. 82C, Hectorkobbekaduwa
Mawatha, Colombo 07; Extent: 2.8328  Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid Lands to be acquires
are hereby required to appear Personally or by agents duly
authorized in written before me on 23rd August, 2017 at 10.00
a.m. at Divisional Secretariat, Galigamuwa on the above
mentioned day and on the Schedule time.
And are here by requested to notify me in writing with
duplicate of on or before 16th August, 2017 the nature of their
interest in the land the particulars of their claims for
compensation for acquisition of the said land the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
K. G. S. NISHANTHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Land Acquisition Office,
Galigamuwa.
Divisional Secretariat,
Galigamuwa,
24th July , 2017.
