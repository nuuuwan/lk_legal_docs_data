Notice under Section 7
       Reference No.: Aku/4/1/1/33.
Reference Number of the Ministry of Land:
 4/3/8/2012/PC/305.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460), as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, was given by
me that the Government intends to acquire the following land
for a public purpose.
     Description of the land or servitude to be acquired :
The following land Lot in Total extend of Hectares 0.0394
depicted in the preliminary plan No. Maha/6352 prepared by
the Surveyor General situated at Pangollamada Grama
Niladhari Division at Udagampaha Korale Minor Division in
the Akurana Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy District
in Central Province.
Lot No. 03
Name of the land: Tannewatte;  Claimant: President,
Pangollamada Welfare Association, No. 253, Pangollamada,
Akurana; Extent: 0.0394 Hectare.
Boundaries :
North: Lot No. 01, Pradesiya Saba Road and Millagaha-
mullawatte claimed by S. H. L. M. Ahamed; East: Millagaha-
mullawatte claimed by S. H. L. M. Ahamed; South: Millagaha-
mullawatte claimed by S. H. L. M. Ahamed, Pradesiya Saba
Road and Tannewatte claimed by M. S. F. Shifana; West:
Pradesiya Saba Road, Tannewatte claimed by M. S. F. Shifana,
Lot No. 02 and 01.
    All  persons interested in the aforesaid land are required to
appear before me personally, or by agent duly authorized in
writing at Divisional Secretary's Office, Akurana on the 01st
June, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to me
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 15th June, 2017 the
nature of their interests in the land and the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the aforesaid
land the amount of compensation and the details of the
compensation of such amount.
MADAWA WARNAKULASOORIYA,
Divisional Secretary,
Akurana Division.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Akurana,
04th April, 2017.
