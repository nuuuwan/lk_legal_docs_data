PART 1: SECTION (1) - GENERAL
.
CUSTOMS  NOTIFICATION  (GENERAL)
Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, W.  A.  Chulananda Perera, Director General of Customs, determine that   with effect from 06th February, 2017 all duties of
Customs as well as other charges, penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Ch apter 235), shall be
paid and received at the rates of Exchane set out in the schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette No. 2004/01 of 30th January, 2017 is hereby
rescinded and the said rates of exchange are scheduled to be rescinded by a similar notification to be published on or before
12th February, 2017.
W.  A.  CHULANANDA PERERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
03rd February , 2017.
