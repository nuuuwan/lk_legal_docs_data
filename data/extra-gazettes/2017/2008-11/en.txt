 LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No: 6/3/1/370
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends  to   acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose:
Description of the Lands  to be acquired:
Nishshankawatta Godaparagahalanda Land in total extent
of 2.977 Hectares, as depicted in plan number PPK 3190 of
12.11.1997 prepared by the survey general, situated in the
village of Henegama, in divisional secretary's division of
Horana in the District of Kalutara in Western Province bounded
as follows.
North: Millagahawatta, sold part of Nishshankawatta Land,
Panadura- Horana High road, road which goes to paddy field
from high road and Gorokwatta; East: Road which goes to
paddy field from high road, Gorokwatta and bounded Kanewala
Village; South: Bounded Kanewala Village and Millagahahena;
West: Millagahahena, sold part of Nishshankawatta Land,
Panadura- Horana High road.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly auhtorized in
writing, before me at Horana Divisional Secretariat on the 07
April 2017 at 10.00 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing ( in duplicate) on or before 31st March 2017 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
    H. A. KAMAL PUSHPAKUMARA,
          Divisional Seceratry,
                    Horana.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Horana.
