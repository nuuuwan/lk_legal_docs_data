No. 2015/2 –  MONDAY, APRIL 17, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ministry No.: WAR/LAN/ACQUS/A2/5-49.  
Land Min.’s Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2014/WS/192. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the  following land/servitude 
which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
Lot No. 1 allotment in extent 0.1736 Hectares of land 
in Preliminary Plan No. Ke 3528 and dated 06.03.2017 
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys of 
Kegalle on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the 
Village of Thulhiriya in the Grama Niladhari Division of 
82A Thulhiriya in the minor division of BeligalKorale 
West in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Warakapola in Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa 
Province. 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Ovitewaththa; Description: Wood; 
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: A. A. M. P. 
Amarasinghe, Weyangoda, Essella, Naiwala, 1/60; 
Extent: 0.1736 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Warakapola 
on th 28th day of June, 2017 At 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 21st day of June, 2017, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computations of such amount. 
 
 LAXMENDRA DAMAYANTH KUMARA TENNEKOON, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Warakapola. 
 
06th April, 2017.  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Warakapola.
