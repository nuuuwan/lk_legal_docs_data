No. 1145/2 of  2017
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS   (AMENDMENT)   ACT,   No.  17  OF  2017
Order under Section 3A(1)
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka in terms of powers vested in me
under Sub-section 3A (1) of the Provincial Councils Elections (Amendment) Act No. 17 of 2017 do hereby appoint the
following as members of the Delimitation Committee for purposes of the aforesaid Act-
1. Mr. K. Thavalingam
2. Professor S. H. Hasbullah
3. Dr. (Mrs.) Anila Dias Bandaranaike
4. Mr. P. M. Sriwardena
5. Mr. Sangaran Vijayasanthiran
Furthermore, it is hereby notified that Mr. K. Thavalingam is appointed as Chairman of the Delimitation Committee.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
04th October 2017.
