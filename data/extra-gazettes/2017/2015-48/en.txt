SPORTS ACT No. 25 OF 1973
Orders under Sections 32 and 33 of the National Sports Association Regulations
I, Dayasiri Jayasekara the Minister of Sports by virtue of powers vested in me under Sections 32(b) and 33 of the Sports
Act No. 25 of 1973 do hereby suspend the registration of Sri Lanka Badminton Association, the National Association for
Badminton and as a interim arrangement appoint Mr. Samasundara Ekanayake Rajawardene Tennakoon Mudiyanselage
Saman Priyantha Bandara, Director General of the Department of Sports Development as its competent authority with
effect from 19.04.2017 to continue its functions and further order that the election be convened and held to elect new
office bearers to the Sri Lanka Badminton Association for 2017.
D
AYASIRI JAYASEKARA (Attorney-At-Law)
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
19th April, 2017.
