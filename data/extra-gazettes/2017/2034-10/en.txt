LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)  ACT  NO. 28
OF  1964 (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Land Mini's Ref.  No.: KDS/GIK/4/1/23.
My Ref.  No.: 4/3/2/2011/FR/30.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that the
Government intends to acquire the following land which is
required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Seven allotments of land in total extent of about 52.6718
Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 01 to 07 in the Preliminary Plan
No. Maha 6647 dated 15.05.2017 situated in the village called
Miyanagolla in the Grama Niladhari Division of Miyanagolla
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Ganga Ihala Korale in
Kandy District of the Central province and more fully described
as follows:
01. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Galamuduna Watta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 48.3028 Hectares.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Galamuduna Watta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 3.4948 Hectares.
03. Lot No. 03
Name of the Land: Galamuduna Watta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 0.4398 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 04
Name of the Land: Galamuduna W atta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 0.0880 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 05
Name of the Land: Galamuduna W atta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 0.0951 Hectare.
06. Lot No. 06
Name of the Land: Galamuduna W atta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 0.2211 Hectare.
07. Lot No. 07
Name of the Land: Galamuduna W atta; Claimant: Land Reform
Commission; Total Extent: 0.0302 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Ganga Ihala Korale Divisional
Secretariat on 25.10.2017 at 2.00 p.m. and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 11.10.2017
the nature of their interest in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount .
K. G. LANEROLLE,
Divisional Secretary,
Ganga Ihala Korale.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Ganga Ihala Korale,
Kurunduwatta New Town,
