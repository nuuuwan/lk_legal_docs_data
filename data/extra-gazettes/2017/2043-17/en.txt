CEYLON  ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith
Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable energy, do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition of the
lands required by the Ceylon Electricity Board, described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMPBALAPITIYA ,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
30th October 2017.
