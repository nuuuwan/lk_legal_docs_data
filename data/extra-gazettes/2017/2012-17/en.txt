SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of  Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of list
of  commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
This Order shall be valid for a period of 60 days from 01st April 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of
Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,  No. 48 of   2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2000/85 of 06.01.2017  in respect of Rice classifiable under H.S Code Nos. 1006.30.10, 1006.30.20 and
1006.30.30 is rescinded with effect from April 01st, 2017.
R
AV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
28th March, 2017.
