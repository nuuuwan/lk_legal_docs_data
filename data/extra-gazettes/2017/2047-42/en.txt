THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
                     Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4/3/2014/HW/405.
                         My Ref. No. : MLB/LND/LNDAQ/L1/39-6-i.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. (01) Lot  allotment of land in  Total Extent of about
0.0034 Hectare, in Preliminary Plan No. Mara. 2694  prepared
by the Senior - Superintendent of Surveyor of Matara District
on behalf of the Surveyor General, situated in the village of
Galpamuna in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Galpamuna(402B)  in the Minor Divisions of Gangabada
Paththuwa and Weligam Korale in  the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Malimbada  in Matara District in the Southern
Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Banwelkosiduwa; Description: Garden,
Contain 4 Nos. of Jack Trees - 5-15 years old; Claimant: State;
Original Claimant:  G .S.Leelasena, "Charitha", Kosinduwa,
Galpamuna, Palatuwa; Extent: 0.0034 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat  of Malimbada on
the 11.12.2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the  04.12.2017 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
                                                     Divisional Secretary
                                                     Malimbada.
22nd  November  2017,
Malimbada Divisional Secretary's Office.
