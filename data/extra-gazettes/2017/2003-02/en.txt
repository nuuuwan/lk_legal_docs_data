The land Acquisition for Construction of Reservor Dam, 
Power house and Switch Yard of Hydro Power Project of 
Moragolla, Gampola. 
 
I hereby give notice, under  Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of  the Land or servitude  to be acquired : 
 
 
The land allotments called  Atabagewatta, 
Sooriyagodawatta, Galathawatta in Total Extent of about 
4.1529 and 4.9296 Hectares, shown as Lot No. 129 -135 
and Lot 28 -34 in Cadastral Map No. 320088(3) 5 and 
320229(5) 2 depicted and prepared by Surveyor General 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of Kekulanda 
and Galatha in KandukaraIhalakorale in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Udapalatha in Kandy District, 
Central Province.
