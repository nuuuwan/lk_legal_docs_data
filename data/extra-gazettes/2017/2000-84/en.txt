WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
METAL  QUARRY  AND   METAL   CRUSHING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated
04.06.1971 that the Hon. Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations has been pleased to appoint the following persons
to be members of the Wages Board for the Metal Quarry and Metal Crushin Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the
Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of
three years commencing on 15th August 2016.
