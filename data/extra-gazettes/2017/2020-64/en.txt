Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3952. 
 
 
 
I hereby given notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
The following land located in the village of Polhena in 
Polhena Grama Niladhari Division in Matara Divisional 
Secretary’s Division, Matara District, Southern Province, 
depicted as paper No. 01 in region No. 03 in Cadastral 
Map No. 820031 will be acquired for the construction of 
one day fishery vessel berthing. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No. 515 
 
Name of the land: Polongunne Ambara, Assessment 
No. 61A, Beach Road; Claimant: Liyanamanage Barnis 
Appu, No. 56, Beach Road, Polhena, Matara and 
Thudawahewage Thanuja Nilanthi, Polhenagewatta, 
Udupila, Mirissa; Current land use: Garden with mix food 
Cultivation and tentative Building; Extent: 0.0402 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Matara on 22nd of 
June 2017 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 15th of 
June 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 D. D. K. WICKRAMARACHCHI,
