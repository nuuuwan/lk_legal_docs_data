LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 76A
BY virtue of the powers vested in Section 76A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by the Local
Authorities Elections (amended) Act, No. 22 of 2012 the Election Commission hereby directs that every reference in that
ordinance to an “Election Officer” or “Assistant Election Officer” of an administrative district, shall be deemed to be a
reference respectively to the “Returning Officer” or “Assistant Returning Officer” of that administrative district.
