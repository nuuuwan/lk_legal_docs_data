NOTICE No : 61/0034 Kegalle District 
 
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of 
lands reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 610010 made by the 
Surveyor-General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title 
Act, No. 21 of 1998 relating to the village of Siyambalapitiya
(Block 04) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama Nilad-
hari Division of 70 B - Yattogoda in the Divisional Secretariat 
Division of Galigamuwa in the District of Kegalle in the Province 
of Sabaragamu should submit his claim to the undersigned before 
03rd March, 2017 In the event of failure  to submit any claim 
before the above-mentioned date, action will be taken to hold an 
exparte inquiry to determine title to the land and accordingly pub-
lish such determination in the Government Gazette in terms of 
Section 14 of the above Act.  
 
The Cadastral Map No. 610010 referred to above may be perused 
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat, 
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title Set-
tlement, or the Surveyor - Generals’ office. 
 
Further information may be obtained from the Grama Niladhari, 
the Divisional Secretary, or the Commissioner of Land Title Set-
tlement.
