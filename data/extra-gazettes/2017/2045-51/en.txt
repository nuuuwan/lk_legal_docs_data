THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KE/
MWN/2016/442 and dated 23.05.2017 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the Village of Aththanagoda in No. 15 B
Aththanagoda Grama Niladhari Division in Thanipperu Paththu Minor
Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Mawanella in
Kegalle District in Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot A – Ambadeniyawaththa Welikanda Division
(97 section) – Extent: 1.0211 Hectare
North: Road and Lot A depicted in the advance tracing No. KE/
MWN/2016/270; East: Remaining portion of Ambadeniyawaththa;
South: Thuththiripitiyehena; West: Lot B, Remaining portion of
Ambadeniyawaththa.
02. Lot B – Extent: 0.0268 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of Ambadeniyawaththa and Lot A; East:
Lot A; South: Thuththiripitiyehena and Lot C; West: Lot C,
Thuththiripitiyehena and Stream.
03. Lot C – Ambadeniyawaththa Welikanda Division
(97 section) – Extent: 0.2146 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Lot B and Thuththiripitiyehena; South:
Thuththiripitiyehena; West: Thuththiripitiyehena and stream.
