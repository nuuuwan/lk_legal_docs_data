No. 2048/36 – THURSDAY, DECEMBER  07,  2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice Under Section 7 
 
My No.: 8/3/5/38(2014). 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4-3/8/2014/PC/339. 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire  the following land which 
is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of  the Land to be acquired : 
 
 
The allotment of land described below,  containing in 
extent 0.0304 Hectare,  depicted as lot  1 in Preliminary 
Plan No. Ma/1714, prepared by Surveyor General, 
situated at Wariyapola -Ward No. 10 Diyabubula Village 
(within the Municipal Council limit of Matale) in 
Kohonsiya Pattuwa Minor Division of Matale Divisioal 
Secretary’s Division, Matale District, Central Province.  
 
1. Lot No.1 -Wariyapola Estate – Original 
Ownership: Sity Naleema Mukthal 
North: Wariyapola Estate; Akkaramale Division 
claimed by S. H. Anverdeen and Others; East: 
Diyabubulariver Road, Diyabula River; South: 
Diyabubula Riaver and Wariyapola Estate -Akkaramale 
Division claimed by Ayoob Ansari and Others -West: 
Wariyapola Estate -Akkaramale Division claimed by 
Ayoob Ansari and Others and Road. 
 
All parsons interested in the aforesaid land on which a 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Matale Divisional Secretariat on the 27th day of 
December 2017 at 10.00 Clock a.m and hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
20th day of December 2017, the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
 K. P. K. L. P. MADHUWANTHI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matale.   
 
Divisional Secretariat,Matale, 
04th December 2017.
