PART  IV (A) — PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
Proclamations & C., by the Governors
NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Declaration Made   by the Governor of the North Central Province
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under paragraph 8(b) of Section 154(b) of the constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, P. B. Disanayake, Governor of the North Central Province declare hereby that the fifth session of
the six Provincial Council of the North Central Province came to end on mid-night of December 09th 2016,  and
By virtue of powers vested in me under Paragraph 8(a) of Section 154(b) of the aforesaid constitution, declare
hereby that the six session of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province shall meet at 10.00 a. m. on
January 31st 2017, at conference hall of the North Central Provincial Council.
P. B. D
ISANAYAKE,
Governor,
North Central Province.
Governor's Office,
North Central Province,
23rd January 2017.
