No. 2003/25 –   WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 25, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)  
ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Mini’s Ref.  No.: 4-3/8/2013/PC/555. 
 
My Ref.  No. : 5/1/2/20. 
  
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, of 
Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 (Amendment) that 
the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land depicted in Sheet No. 07, 
Suplement No. 9 of the Final Village Plan No. 294 dated 
17.12.2015, situated in the village called Kandanegedara 
in Medapattuwa West Minor Division in Medapattuwa 
Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Pannala 
in Kurunegala District of the North Western province and 
more fully described as follows: 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
1. Lot No. 52 
 
 Name of the land: Koongahamulahena alias 
Kahatagahamulahena; Description: Home garden, 112 
Coconut trees of about 30 - 40 Years old, 108 Coconut 
trees of about 01 - 05 years old, nine Jack trees, 32 teak 
trees, 1 Sooriyamara tree, 1 Halmilla tree, 2 permanent 
buildings, a foundation and a cement constructed well, 
available; Claimant: Mirissage Sujith Stanly Fernando, 
No. 178 C, Borella Road Wennappuwa; Extent: 1.1442 
Hectares.  
 
2. Lot No. 53 
 
 Name of the land: Koongahamulahena alias 
Kahatagahamulahena; Description: Road; Claimant: 
Mirissage Sujith Stanly Fernando, No. 178 C, Borella 
Road, Wennapuwa, W. A. Sisira Kumara Wijesinghe, 
Kandanegedara, Wadumunegedara, W. A. Sarath Kumara 
Wijesinghe, Kandanegedara, Wadumunegedara; Extent: 
0.0429 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Pannala Divisional 
Secretariat on 22nd March, 2017 at 10.30 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 15th March, 2017 the nature of their interest 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in Support of the Claims 
should be Produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
  R. M. S. WICKRAMARATHNE BANDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pannala. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Pannala.
