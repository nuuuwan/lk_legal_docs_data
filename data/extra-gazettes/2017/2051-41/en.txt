NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
North Central Province Provincial Council Finance Statute No. 13 of 1990
ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  77(1)( a)
I, Punchi Banda Disanayake Governor of North Central Province, by virtue of the power vested in me under Section 77(1)
of the Finance Statute No. 13 of 1990 of the North Central Provincial Council do hereby make the following orders with
regard to the payment of stamp duty on instruments referred to in Sections 37(a) read with Section 45 of that Statute.
P
UNCHI B ANDA D ISANAY AKE,
Governor,
North Central Province.
North Central Province Governor’s Office,
Anuradhapura,
ORDER
1) This order is cited as the order with regard to the payment of Stamp Duty under the Section 40(2) of the Finance
Statute No. 13 of 1990 as amended by No. 7 of 1991 Finance Statute (Amendment) of the North Central
Provincial Council and shall come into operation on 01st January 2018.
2) It is hereby required to use the certificate obtained by paying money to the bank in terms of Section 40(2) of
the Finance Statute No. 13 of 1990 of the North Central Provincial Council in place of using adhesive stamps.
3) The amount payable for stamp duty shall be paid to any People’s Bank in Sri Lanka to the Account Number
008-1-001-2-1724458 in accordance with the following forms.
a) Form I - Property situated in the North Central Province
b) Form II - Property situated outside the North Central Province
