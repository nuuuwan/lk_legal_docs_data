Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as the Postgraduate Institute of Medicine (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 1 of
2017 and shall come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Postgraduate Institute of Medicine Ordinance, No. 1 of 1980, published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 83/7 of April 10, 1980 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1873/16 of July 30,
2014 is hereby further amended as follows :
(I) In Paragraph 13 thereof -
(i) by the addition immediately after item (aj) of this paragraph, of the following new item :-
“(ak) Orthopaedic Surgery.”.
(2) In Paragraph 25 thereof -
(i) by the substitution for the words “Venereology or any other speciality” in the definition of the
expression “Speciality in Medicine” of the words “Venereology, Orthopaedic Surgery or any other
speciality”.
