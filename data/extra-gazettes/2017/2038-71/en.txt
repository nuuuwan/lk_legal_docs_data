 THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in depicted in Advance Tracing No.
KE/ARN/2016/383 and dated  23.02.2017, prepared by Surveyor -
General, situated  in  the Village of Kalugala, in the Grama Niladhari
Division of 47D, Kalugala, in the Ganhatha Palatha Minor Division
in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Aranayaka, in the District
of Kegalle, in Sabaragamuwa Province.
01. Lot 'A' – Debathgamawatta (Kalugala part ) – Extent:
0.4458 Hectare
North: Pilideniyahenyaya, Stream and Road; East: Stream; South:
Stream, Debathgama Watta(Kalugala Part); West: Debathgama
Watta(Kalugala Part) and Pilideniyahenyaya.
