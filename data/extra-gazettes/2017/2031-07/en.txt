THE   EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Orders issued by Honorable Minister of Finance under the Excise Ordinance
(Chapter 52) as amended from time to time have been passed by parliament.
1. Excise Notification No. 991 ( Gazette No. 1996/52 of 09.12.2016) order made Section 32, read with Section
26, 27 and 39, was passed by parliament on 07.04.2017.
2. Excise Notification No. 992 ( Gazette No. 1997/16 of 15.12.2016) order made Section 32, read with Section
25, was passed by parliament on 22.06.2017.
3. Excise Notification No. 994 ( Gazette No. 1998/7 of 20.12.2016) order made Section 32, read with Section
25, was passed by parliament on 22.06.2017.
R. H. S. S AMARATUNGA,
Secretary,
