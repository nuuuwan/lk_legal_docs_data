THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF  1971
Order  under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Wajira Abeywardane, Minister of
Home Affairs, do by this order,declare that, as regards the year 2018, each of  the days specified in the Schedule hereto shall
be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
WAJIRA ABEYWARDANE,
  Minister of  Home Affairs.

Colombo 07,
31st  March  2017.
