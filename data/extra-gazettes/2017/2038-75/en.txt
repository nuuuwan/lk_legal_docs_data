PART 1: SECTION (1) - GENERAL
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT.  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27 (I)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(I) of the Universities  Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister
of Higher Education and of Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend
the Schedule to the Order made under section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South Eastern University of Sri Lanka,
published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996 (as last amended by Order published  in Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 2023/31 of June 14, 2017) by the addition immediately after the entry "(f). Department of English Language Teaching"
appearing in Column II under the heading "Department of Study" which corresponds to the entry "Facul ty of Arts and
Culture" appearing in Column I under the heading "Faculty" of the Schedule II to such Order, of the following new item:-
"(g). Department of Sociology".
LAKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th  Sepetember 2017.
