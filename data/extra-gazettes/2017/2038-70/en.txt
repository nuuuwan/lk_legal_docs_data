No. 2038/70 –  FRIDAY SEPTEMBER 29, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference : YAK/LAN/01/03/06-1. 
Land Ministry Reference : LD/05/2008/PC/354. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a Public Purpose:- 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Allotment of land described in supplementary 23 and 24 
of FVP Plan No. 638 of Surveyor General and situated in 
No. 179A Nakiyadeniya Grama Niladhari Division in 
Yakkalamulla Divisional Secrerary’s Division in Galle 
District. 
 
Lot No. 857 
Name of the Land: Pahala Nakiyadeniya Kumbura, 
Nakiyadeniya Left bank; Discription: Filled up Paddy 
Land; Name of the Claimants: Ramya Gamage, Pathma 
Hardware, Nakiyadeniya and Nimal Gamage, Baddegama 
Road, Uluvitike; Extent: 0.2927 Hectare. 
 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land/Servitude 
are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Yakkalamulla 
Divisional Secretary’s office on the day of 8th November 
2017 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 17th day of 
October 2017 the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/Servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 CHANDIMA C. MUHANDIRAMGE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Yakkalamulla Division. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Yakkalamulla Division.
