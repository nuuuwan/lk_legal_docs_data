No. 2043/14  –  MONDAY OCTOBER 30, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: 3/7/14/2. 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 04-3/8/2012/PC/236. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act,  that the Government 
intends to  the following land which is required  for a 
public purpose: 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired : 
 
The allotments of land called Mulhalkele Estate plain 
total extent about 0.0169 shown as  Lots Number 01 of 
P.P.N. 3134 situated in the village of  Wathumulla in  
515 B, Walapane Division in Divisional Secretary 
Division of Walapane in Nuwara Eliya District in Central 
Province and described as follows: 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Mulhalkele Estate; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Land Use: 
Garden, Part of Permanent Building; Extent: 0.0169 
Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear,  personally,  or  by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office Walapane on the 20th day of 
December, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
06th day of December, 2017 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 
       I. M. SENANAYAKA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
                                                     Walapane. 
 
 
                            
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Walapane, 
15th October, 2017.
