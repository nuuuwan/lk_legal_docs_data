THE   CONSTITUTION  OF  THE   DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
I, hereby give notice by virtue of  Article 44(5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that
the subjects and functions and departments, statutory institutes and state corporations vested in the State Minister of
Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development appointed in terms of Article 44(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall be discharged in conformity with the Schedule hereto with effect from 22.02.2017.
M
AHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development.
Colombo,
22nd February 2017.
