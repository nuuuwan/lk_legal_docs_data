No. 2003/5 –   MONDAY, JANUARY 23, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Ref.  No.: HAK/LAN/1/5. 
  
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for the public purpose 
mentioned below. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Following allotment depicted in the Paper No. 12 of 
Final Village Plan 184 prepared by Survey Superintendent of 
Matare District situated in the village of Wepathaira South in 
Kandabada Pattu Minor Division in Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Hakmana, Matara District, Southern Province.  
 
Lot No. 259 —  Name Land: Balamahaththayage 
Watta; Name of Land owners: H. P. P. De Silva, H. M. 
Hilda Sriyani, H. P. Violet, H. P. Piyaseeli Violet 
Edirisuriya; Extent: 0.2092 Hectare. 
 
Boundary Details : 
 
North: Lot No. 100; East: Lot No. 100 and 150; 
South: Lot No. 150; West: Lot No. 100. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat of Hakmana on the 27th 
day of February, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 20th day of February, 2017 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
   
 KUSALAKA NANAYAKKARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Hakmana. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Hakmana, 
17th January, 2017.
