THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Twelve (12) allotments of lands, depicted in Advanced Tracing
No. KU/KPW/2016/635 and dated 07.03.2017, prepared by
Superintendent of  Surveys, situated in Poogalla (FVP 966) and
Narangalla (FVP 967) Villages in Narangalla Grama Niladhari Division
(No. 1135) of Kuliyapitiya – West Divisional Secretary's Division
in the District of Kurunegala in North Western Province,and  bounded
as follows :.
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Poogalla Estate – Extent: 3.0097 Hectares
North and East: Poogalla Estate claimed by National Livestock
Development Board; South: Koongahamulawaththa alias
Ambulbakmeegollahena claimed by D. M. S. Mahinda and Others,
Lots D, M and Balagollawatta claimed by P . N. J. Fonseka;West:
Balagollawaththa claimed by A. A. Somapala and Others, Lot C,
Balagollawatta claimant cannot be found, Balagollawaththa claimed
by H. S. K. Rosairo and Lot B.
(1A - G26251 - 22(2017/09)
