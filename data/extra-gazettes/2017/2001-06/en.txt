CEYLON   ELECTRICITY  BOARD  ACT,  No.  17  OF  1969
Order under Section 57
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 57 of the Ceylon Electricity Board Act, No. 17 of 1969, I, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, do by this Order, approve the proposed acquisition of lands required by the
Ceylon Electricity Board and described in the Schedule hereto.
R
ANJITH  S IYAMBALAPITIY A,
Minister of Power and Renewable Energy.
05th January 2017.

in the Province of Northern  of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 3.5761 hectares shown in the rough sketch dated
