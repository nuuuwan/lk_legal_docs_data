THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0320 Hectare depicted
in Advance Tracing No. MR/DKW/2015/276 and dated  26.08.2015
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of
Bambarenda Central in Grama Niladhari Division of
Wallabadapattuwa  Minor Divison, in  Divisional Secretary’s Division
of  Dickwella, in Matara District in Southern Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galketiyewatta – Extent: 0.0320 Hectare
North: The Land acquired by the proposed Matara- Kataragama
Rail way; East: The land Galketiyewatta which is declared the
ownership by Soma Kasthuriarachchi & Y .R.K.Nanayakkara; South:
The land Galketiyewatta which is declared the ownership by Soma
Kasthuriarachchi & Y .R.K. Nanayakkara; West: The Land acquired
by the proposed Matara- Kataragama Rail way.
