EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED  BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT,  NO. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice Under  Section  7 
 
My No.: WEDS/LND/9/ACQ/2-3. 
Ministry File No.: 4-3/4/2014/133. 
 
 
I hereby  give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended  by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No: 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intend to acquire the following land/ 
servitude  which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of land servitude to be acquired: 
 
    The  allotments of land in extent about 0.7081 He. 
depicted in Sheet No. 8 Plan Zonal 02 from Zonal 02 in 
C. M. No. 620142 prepared by Surveyor General, situated 
at Gangodagama Village, 219B, Gangodagama Grama 
Niladhari Division, Weligepola Divisional Secretariat 
Rathnapura District and Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
 
THE SCHEDULE 
 
 
1. Lot No. 169 
 Name of the Land: Welkeptiyawewaththa/
Godaparagahahena; Claimant: Land Reclamation 
Commission;  Extent :0.7081 Hectare. 
 
All  persons interested in the aforesaid  land/ lands  on  
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Weligepola Divisional Secretariat on the  
19th day of July,  2017  at  10.00 a. m. and are hereby 
requested to  notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the  12th day of July, 2017 the nature of their  
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ 
servitude, the amount of compensation of such amount. 
 
 H. M. H. .BANDARA, 
 Divisional  Secretary,  
 Weligepola. 
 
Weligepola Divisional Secretariat,  
14th June,  2017.
