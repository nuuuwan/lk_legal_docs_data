EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT, No. 13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Mangala
Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified
in the Columns I, II and III of the Schedule hereto, and the corresponding articles specified under the Schedule I of the
Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1992/29 dated 10.11.2016 and the Schedule of the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 1996/10 dated 06.12.2016, issued under the Section 3 of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified
in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from 18.08.2017.
The Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1996/10 of 06.12.2016 is hereby rescinded.
M
ANGALA  S AMARAWEERA ,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01.
17th August 2017.
