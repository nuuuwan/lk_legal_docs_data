I hereby give notice, under Sub -Section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, (Chapter 460) as Amended 
by the land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 
1964, was given by me that. The Government intends to 
acquire the following land for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land or Servitude  to be acquired: 
 
The Lot No.01 of the land called Thalahena in total 
extend  of Hectare 0.0379 depicted in the preliminary 
plan No. Maha/6729 and dated on 23rd August 2017, 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated at Dippitiya 
Village of Dippitiya Grama Niladhari Division at 
Udagampaha Korale Minor Division in the Akurana 
Divisional Secretary’s Division  of Kandy District in the 
Central Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: Thalahenawatte; Claimant: D. G. 
Piyasena, No. 600, Dippitiya, Alawathugoda; Extent: 
0.0379 Hectare. 
 
North: Road (Pradesiya Sabawa); East: Road (Pradesiya 
Sabawa) and Thalahena claimed D. G. Piyasena; South: 
Thalahena claimed D. G. Piyasena and Road (Pradesiya 
Sabawa); West: Road (Pradesiya Sabawa) 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are required 
to appear, before me personally, or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, at the Divisional Secretary’s Office 
Akuran on the 21st day of December 2017 at 10.00 a.m. 
and hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 21st day of January 2018 the 
nature of their interests in the land, and the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
afore said land the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
       MADAWA WARNAKULASOORIYA ,
