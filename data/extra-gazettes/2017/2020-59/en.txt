 PILGRIMAGES  ORDINANCE
Annual Feast of the National Shrine of St. Anthony at Wasalakotte
IT is hereby informed that, the annual feast of the National Shrine of St. Anthony at Wasalakotte, in the Divisional Secretariat
of Galewela, in the District of Matale, will be held on 17th and 18th of June 2017, under the terms and conditions of the
Gazette Notification, of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
M. U. N
ISHANTHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Galewela.
