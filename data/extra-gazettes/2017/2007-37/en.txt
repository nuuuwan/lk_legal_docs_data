L.D.B 9/73 (xii)
THE  FAUNA   AND  FLORA  PROTECTION  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  469)
Order under Subsection (2) of  Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Subsection (2) of Section 2 of the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance (Chapter
469), I, Gamini Jayawickrama Perera, Minister of Sustainable Development and Wildlife, do by this Order declare the
area of land described in the Schedule hereto to be a Sanctuary for the purposes of the aforesaid Ordinance and further
declare that such Sanctuary shall be called and known as the “Kotuaththawala Sanctuary”.
G
AMINI J AY AWICKRAMA P ERERA,
Minister of Sustainable Development and Wildlife.
Colombo,
02nd February, 2017.
SCHEDULE
Limits of the Kotuaththawala Sanctuary
All that area of land containing in extent 35 hectares situated within the Nikaweratiya Divisional Secretary’s
Division in the Administrative District of Kurunegala of North Western Province and bounded by the GPS coordinates
which are obtained from the SLD 99 coordinate system in accordance with the map ratio 1:50,000 of the Surveyor
Department as follows :–
North : Starting from the point of coordinates E 128078 N 288076 situated, towards the East up to the point
of coordinates E 128063 N 288198 situated ; from there towards the North-East up to the point of
coordinates E 127897 N 288242 situated ;
East : from the last mentioned point of the Northern boundary towards South-East up to the point of
coordinates E 127764 N 288270 situated ; from there towards South-West up to point of coordinates
E 127508 N 288352 situated ;
