LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  - 2018
Exhibition of Electoral Registers
THE public is hereby notified that the valid electoral register to be used for the forthcoming elections to Local Authorities
have been prepared by the relevant Returning Officers for each of the relevant Local Authorities based on the Electoral
Register certified on the 12th of October, 2017 for the year 2017 for the purpose of Parliamentary Elections. Such
registers have been made available for inspection by the relevant Returning Officers at the offices of the respective Local
Authorities. It is also further notified that stets have been taken to include the names of all electors whose names are in
the Electoral Register for 2017 in these registers.
If an elector finds that his/her name has not been included in the register of the relevant Local Authority or that
the name of an unqualified elector has been included in such a register, such information can be brought to the notice of
the Returning Officer of the relevant District Election Office, in writing, on or before 05th December, 2017. It is also
further notified that, the Returning Officers have taken measures to exhibit all relevant notices at the offices of all Local
Authorities.
