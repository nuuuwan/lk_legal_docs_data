ORDER   UNDER  SECTION 3
THE  JUDICATURE  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1978
By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, I Wijedasa Rajapaksha, Minister of
Justice in consultation with the chief Justice and the president of the Court of Appeal, do by this Order, amend the Order
published in the Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1679/40 of  November 10, 2010 as last amended Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1890/17 of November 26, 2014 as follows;
(1) by the substitution for the words "Thirty Three Judicial Zones" of the words "Thirty Four Judicial Zones";
(2) in the Schedule thereto :-
(i) by the substitution for the entry "Avissawella" appearing in Column I under the heading "Zone" and its corresponding
entry in Column IV under the heading of "Area of Jurisdiction" of the following entries respectively :-
"11. Avissawella The Judicial Zone of Avissawella shall comprise the Judicial Districts of
Avissawella and Ruwanwella.";
(ii) by the deletion of all the entries relating to the Judicial District, Judicial Division and Area of Jurisdiction "Homagama"
appearing in Column II, III and IV recpectively , appearing against corresponding Judicial Zone of "Avissawella" under
Column I;
(iii) by the substitution for the entry "Panadura" appearing in column I under the heading "Zone" and its corresponding
entry in Column IV under the heading of "Area of Jurisdiction" of the following entries respectively:-
"4. Panadura The Judicial Zone of Panadura shall comprise the Judicial Districts of  Panadura,
Horana and Moratuwa.";
(iv) by the deletion of all the entries relating to the Judicial District, Judicial Division and Area of Jurisdiction "Kesbewa"
appearing in Column II, III and IV recpectively, appearing against corresponding Judicial Zone of "Panadura" under
Column I;
