THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land shown as Lot ‘A’ depicted in the
Advance Tracing No. KE/WRK/2016/204 and dated 18.08.2016,
prepared by the Surveyor, situated in the Village of Ambakumbura
in the Grama Niladhari Division of Waddeniya (77) in the Minor
Division of Beligal Korale, Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Warakapola in Kegalla District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Orenggrow State (Kanuketiya division) –
Extent: 1.0951 Hectare
North: Road (Provincial Council) and Unapanduragawa Hena; East:
Unapanduragawa Hena, Ambagahamulahena Watta, Road and
Ambagahamulahena Watta; South: Ambagahamulahena Watta,
Orenggrow state (Kanuketiya division) and Kanuketiya;West:
Kanuketiya and Road (Provincial Council).
