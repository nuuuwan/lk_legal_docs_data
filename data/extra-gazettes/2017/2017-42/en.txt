CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Pradeshiya Sabhas
IT is hereby notified that the resolved By-laws, approved by the Minister of Local Government in the Central Provincial
Council, by virtue of power vested in him under sub Section (1) of Section 123 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987, read together with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, published
in the Part IV (A), Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 1955/7, dated 23.02.2016, has been
seconded by the Provincial Council of the Central Province on 21.02.2017, under Section 125 of the said Act, read together
with sub Section (3) of Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989.
Secretary,
Local Government and Chief Ministry,
