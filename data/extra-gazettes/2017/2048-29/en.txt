THE   CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that in terms of Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), His Excellency Maithripala
Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, further enlarged the time by 23 days up to 31st
December, 2017, having enlarged the said period by 06 weeks up to 08th December, 2017  by Notification published in
the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2042/9 dated 24th October 2017, and by Notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2029/15 dated 25th July, 2017 enlarged the period ending 27th April, 2017 by three months up to
27th July, 2017 and thereafter by another three months up to 27th October, 2017 for rendering of the report of the
Commission established under Section 2 of the said Act, on 27th January 2017 by Notification published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2003/41 dated 27th January, 2017 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate,
inquire into and report on the Issuance of Treasury Bonds during the period 01st February 2015 to 31st March, 2016 on
completion of the aforesaid tasks.
         By Order of His Excellency,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
December 05, 2017.
