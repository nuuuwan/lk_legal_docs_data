PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made by  the  Governor of Uva  Province
IT is hereby notified that  I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor of Uva Province, do hereby announce that Hon. Rathnayake
Mudiyanselage Kumarasiri Rathnayake has been removed from the post of Minister of Health and Indigenous Medicine,
Probation and Childcare, Women’s Affairs and Social Welfare of Uva Province, with effect from the date 10th August
2017 who was appointed by me as a Minister of Health and Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Women’s
Affairs and Social Welfare of Uva Province with effect from 22nd October 2015 by virtue of powers vested to me under
the Section 154F. (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
M. P. J
AY ASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
Badulla,
12th August 2017.
