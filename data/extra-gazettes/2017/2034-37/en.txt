L.D.B. 4/81(XII)
THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL   ACT,  No.  47  OF 1980
Order under Section 23 W
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 23 W of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 read with
Section 51of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Maithripala
Sirisena, President, do by this Order prohibit the use of all forms of polyethylene, polypropylene, polyethylene products or
polypropylene products as decoration in political, social,religious, national, cultural or any other event or occasion.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
30th August, 2017.
