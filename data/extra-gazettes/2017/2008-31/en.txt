 FISHERIES  AND  AQUATIC   RESOURCES  ACT,   No.  02  OF  1996
Regulations made by the Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development under Section 61 of the Fisheries
and Aquatic Resources Act, No. 2 of 1996 read with Section 28 of that Act.
MAHINDA AMARAWEERA,
Minister of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development.
Colombo,
27th February 2017.
Regulations
1. These regulations may be cited as Prohibition of Use of Spears in Fishing Operations Regulations
No. 1 of 2017.
2. No person shall use or possess, or have on board any local fishing boat, any spear gun.
3. No person shall engage in any fishing operation using spear guns or hand held spears within Sri Lanka
Waters.
4. In these Regulations -
“Spear gun” means a gun used to catch fish by shooting with pointed spike ;
“Sri Lanka Waters” shall have the same meaning as in the Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Act, No. 2 of
1996.
