Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: KDS/KGK/4/2/2015/2/1.
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands and
Parlimentary Reforms: 4-3/9/2015/WS/180.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following land which is required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired  :
The allotment of land called "Bo Gaha yata Pela,
Assessment No. 595/1, William Gopallawa Mawatha" in total
extent of about 0.0351 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 1 in
Preliminary Plan No. Maha 6727, dated on 30.10.2017, surveyed
and prepared by the Surveyor General, situated as the village
of Wel Ata Ward No. 1, Peradeniya (Withing the Kandy
Municipal Council Limit), in Mulgampola Grama Niladhari
Division (243) in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kandy
Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale in Kandy District, Central
Province and bounded as follows.
Lot No. 1 – Name of the Land: Bo Gaha yata pela,
Assessment No. 595/1, William Gopallawa Mawatha; Names
and Address of the claimants: 1. Mr. Mahinda Katugaha,
No. 39/4-1/3, Rajapihilla Mawatha, Kandy; Extent of the Land:
0.0351 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North by: Lot No. 2 in PP Maha 3859, Road and Walawe
watta alias Bo Gaha Yata Pelessa by S. K. B. Yatiwella; East
by: Walawe watta alias Bo Gaha Y ata Pelessa claimed by S. K.
B. Y atiwella and Bo Gaha Y ata Pela claimed by Chief Incumbent
of Thun Bo Maluwa Temple; South by: Bo Gaha Yata Pela
claimed by Chief Incumbent of Thun Bo Maluwa Temple and
Lot No. 14 in PP Maha 3859; West by: Lot No. 14, 15 and 2 in
PP Maha 3859.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized
in writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary's office, at
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawatta Korale on the 14th day
of December, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 07th day
of December, 2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said land, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
B. M. P. G. P. V. BANDARA,
Divisional Secretary,
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata
Korale Division.
Divisional Secretariat,
Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Division,
11th November, 2017.

Correction Slip
Notice under Section 7
This refers the Extra Ordinary Gazette  Notification
No. 521/22 dated 01st September in 1988.
The notice is hereby given under the Sub section (1) in the
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, (Amended) No. 28 of
1964 as the Government intends to acquire to below mentioned
land/servitude for a public purpose.
The Description of the land/servitude to be acquired  :
The land called as Kuruduwaththa Lot No. 1 of the Initial
Plan No. G/2198 located at the village, Meetiyagoda, Divisional
Tax Officer Division, Wellabada Pattuwa (North) in Galle
District.
Lot No. 1
Name of the allotment: Kuruduwaththa; Description: Home
Garden, consisted of 03 Jack trees age in between 10-25, 15
King Coconut trees age in between 10-15, 04 plastered
buildings covered above with local titles, a plastered water
tank, 04 Lavatories, a garage covered above with roofing
sheets and a plastered guard room; Extension: 0.0394 Hectares.
The all claimants and people who have ownership or other
relation to the aforementioned land/servitude themselves or
the people who are authorized by their documents should be
appeared before me at 9.00 a.m. on 08th February, 2018 at the
Divisional Secretariat-Ambalangoda.
Further, the nature of their ownership related to the
mentioned land, details on the facts for claiming
compensation regarding the acquiring that land, the amount
of compensation and the way it is calculated shall be  recived
to me by  duly completed copies prior to the 25.01.2018
Relevant deed of transfers and other particulars should be
brought on the date in which examination is due to be held in
order to confirm the own obligations or rights with regard to
the lands.
H. K. SARASI S. GUNASEKARA,
