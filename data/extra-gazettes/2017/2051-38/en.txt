THE  STRATEGIC  DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS   ACT,  No.  14  OF  2008
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-Section (4) of Section 3 of the Strategic  Development Project Act, No. 14 of 2008
as amended, I, Malik Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade, being the Minister in
Charge of the subject of Investment Promotion, further to the Order published under Sub section (4) of Section 3 under
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1867/5 dated 16th June, 2014 do by this Order declare that,
Clause (4) of the Order published under Gazette Extraordinary No. 1867/5 dated 16th June, 2014 is hereby
amended to read as follows in order to extend the time granted to implement the Project :-
“(4) The aforesaid Project shall commence commercial operations within ninety (90) months from the date stated in
the Project Agreement with the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka”.
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade.
Colombo,
29th December, 2017.
