THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in called Bibile Kumbura total extent of
about 0.0575 Hectare depicted in Advance Tracing No. KE/GLG/
2016/347 and dated  07.03.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General
situated in the Village of Bisowela in 69C Bisowela Grama Niladhari
Division in  Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Galigamuwa, in Kegalle
District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot 'A'– Bibile Kumbura – Extent: About 0.0575 Hectare
North: Bibile Kumbura and Pradeshiya Sabha Road; East:
Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Canal; South:Canal and Bibile Kumbura;
West: Bibile Kumbura.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
My. No.: 4-3/9/2016/WS/49.
Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
26th October, 2017.
