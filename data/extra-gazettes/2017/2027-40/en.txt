Northern Province Health Service (Amendment) Statute No. 10/2016
A  STATUTE  TO  AMEND  THE  HEALTH  SERVICE  STATUTE  OF
NORTHERN PROVINCE, No. 01 OF 2015
 BE it enacted by the Northern Province Provincial Council of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Statute may be cited as the Finance (Amendment) Statute of Northern Province,
No. 10 of 2016 and it shall come into operation on such date as the Governor of the Northern
Province may give his Assent thereto.
2. Section 8 of the Health Service Statute of the Northern Province  No  01 of 2015 is hereby
deleted, and the following new subsection is inserted.
‘‘The Provincial Director shall in the exercise, discharge and performance of the powers,
duties and functions by this Statute in matters relating to public health, act in consultation
with the Head of Local  Authority Commissioner/ Secretary within its limits and the Head  of
Local Authority Commissioner/Secretary shall assist and furnish the required information
to the Provincial Director''
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Tamil and Sinhala or English texts of this
Statute, the Tamil texts shall prevail.
