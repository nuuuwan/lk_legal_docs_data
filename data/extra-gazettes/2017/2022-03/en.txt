SRI  LANKA  SUSTAINABLE  ENERGY  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  35  OF  2007
Order under Sub section (1) of Section 12
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Sub section (1) of Section 12 of Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority Act,
No. 35 of 2007, I, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya, Minister of Power and Renewable Energy, in consultation with the Ministers
in charge of the subject of Mahaweli Development and Environmental, Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, Land, Public
Administration and Home Affairs, Irrigation and Water Management, Agriculture and Agrarian Services and the Ministers
of North Provincial Council to whom the subjects are assigned, do by this Order declare the areas of lands described in
the Schedule hereto as Energy Development Areas in the Jaffna District for the purpose of the aforesaid Act.
