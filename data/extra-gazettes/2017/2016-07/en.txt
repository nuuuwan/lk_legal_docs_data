TRUST  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “The Pastoral Fellowship Trust” namely -
1. Rev. Mary Joseph Gread Jabakumar
2. Rev. Kuppusamy Vijayan
3. Rev. Pattha Rasaretnam
4. Rev. Kanapathipillai Deiventhiran
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance
(Chapter 87) ;
NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Wijayadasa Rajapakshe,
Minister of Justice do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that -
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under
the said Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing No. 3932 of August 22, 2012 attested by  Michale
Fredrick Sproule Notary Public Colombo as amended by the Trust Deed No. 4108 of October 10, 2014
attested by Michale Fredrick Sproule Notary Public of Colombo shall be a body corporate with perpetual
succession and a common seal under the name and style of the “The Pastoral Fellowship Trust” ;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or
immovable, on behalf of the said Trust ; and
(c) the Trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and to be sued, to
answer and to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all
cases and actions arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be
necessary
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
19th April, 2017.
