FOREIGN  EXCHANGE  ACT,  No. 12  OF  2017
Order under Section 1
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 1 of the Foreign  Exchange Act, No. 12 of 2017, I, Ranil Wickramasinghe,
Minister of  National Policies and Economic Affairs, do by this  Order appoint  November 20, 2017 as the date on which
the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
The Order made under Section 1 of the aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 2040/ 43 of
October 13, 2017 is hereby revoked.
R
ANIL W ICKRAMASINGHE ,
Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs.
Colombo,
31st  October, 2017.
