No. 2003/37 – FRIDAY, JANUARY 27, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands, hereby
declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An Allotment of land called '' Zyston Seed Division Estate'', in
total extent of about 0.0632  Hectare, shown and described in the
Advance Tracing No. Maha/AKR/2015/449, dated 16.12.2015,
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village of Dippitiya
in Udagampaha Korale Minor Division in the  Divisional Secretary's
Division in Akurana in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot 'A' – Zyston Seed Division Estate  – Extent:
0.0632 Hectare
North: Zyston Seed Division Estate claimed by M. M. K.
Chaturanga Bandara and Zyston Seed Division Estate claimed by
S. M. S. Noorul Azmiya; East: Zyston Seed Division Estate claimed
by S. M. S. Noorul Azmiya; South: Zyston Seed Division Estate
claimed by S. M. S. Noorul Azmiya and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);
West: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Zyston Seed Division Estate
claimed by M. M. K. Chaturanga Bandara.
