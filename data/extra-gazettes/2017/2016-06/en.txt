THE   CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Delegation of Powers by the National Police Commission in terms of Article 155J
BY virtue of the powers vested in the National Police Commission in terms of Article 155J of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the said National Police Commission delegated some of its powers to the
Inspector General of Police and Police Officers as specified in the Government Gazette Extraordinary bearing No.
1965/37 dated May 06, 2016.
The  Schedule of the said Gazette Notification is replaced by the following Schedule with effect from January
26, 2017.
                                                                   By order of the National Police Commission,
P. H. M
ANATUNGA,
Chairman,
National Police Commission.
Office of the National Police Commission,
Block 09, BMICH,
Colombo 07,
26th January 2017.
