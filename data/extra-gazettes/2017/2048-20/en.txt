THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                   My No. :  09/03/01/2016/2,SR/Divu/2017/21.
         Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2016/HW/454.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot Nos. 90 - 97 (Lots 05) allotments in total extent of about
0.1863 Hectare, of land in Cadastral Map No. 510026, Block No.
05. Block 05 and Sheet No. 5 and dated 02.11.2017 prepared by the
Senior - Superintendent of Survey of Gampaha District on behalf of
Survey General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of
41/A, Parana Halpe East  in  the village of  Parana  Halpe  in  Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Divulapitiya in Gampaha District in the
Western Province.
01. Lot No. 90
Name of  land: Pothuvilakumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: N. A. Bandaranayaka, No. 73,
Kuruppu Road, Borella, (A part of the plot of Land No. 80,
to finalize the land acquisition); Extent: 0.0024 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 92
Name of  land: Pothuvilakumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: N. A. Bandaranayaka, No. 73,
Kuruppu Road, Borella, (A part of the plot of Land No. 80,
to finalize the land acquisition); Extent: 0.0119 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 93
Name of land: Pothuvilakumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Cannot be found, (A part of the
plot of  Land No. 81, to finalize the land acquisition); Extent: 0.0110
Hectare.
04. Lot No. 96
Name of land: Pothuvilakumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: Cannot be found; Original  Claimant:  Ranil  Livera, No.
81, Gregari Road, Borella, (A part of the plot of Land No. 85,
to finalize the land acquisition); Extent: 0.0842 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 97
Name of land: Pothuvilakumbura; Description: Paddy Field;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Gihan Livera, No. 81, Gregari
Road, Borella, (A part of the plot of Land No. 86, to finalize the land
acquisition); Extent: 0.0768 Hectare.
         All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat office of Divulapitiya on the from
22.12.2017 at 9.30 a.m  and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the  15.12.2017 the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation
for the acquisition of the said  lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
                                                            H. M. L. S. H ERATH,

                                                               Divulapitiya.
