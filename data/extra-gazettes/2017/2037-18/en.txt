THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1469/38 of November 03, 2006 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 2005/12 of   February  08, 2017), which specifies the Faculties
and the Departments of the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which is deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139
of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the  substitution for the item
“Faculty of Engineering’’  appearing in Column I of  the Schedule and  its corresponding entries appearing in Column II
thereto,  of the following :-
“Column I Column II
 Faculty of Engineering 1. Department of  Chemical and Process Engineering
2. Dapartment of  Civil Engineering
3. Department of Computer Science and Engineering
