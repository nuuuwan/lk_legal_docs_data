THE  WAGES   BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD   FOR   THE   FLOWERS,  ORNAMENTAL   PLANTS,   VEGETABLES   AND   FRUIT   GROWING
AND EXPORT   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Section 29(3) read with Section 33(3) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) the
determination relating to the minimum rate of wages in respect of workers engaged in Flowers, Ornamental Plants, Vegetables
and Fruits Growing and Export Trade specified in the Schedule hereto made by the Commissioner of Labour under Section
33(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance in pursuance of the authority conferred on him by an order made by Hon. Minister of
Labour, under Section 33(1)(a) of the Ordinance and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1916/12 of May 27, 2015
has been approved by the Hon. Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamuwa Development.
The determination shall come into force on the 01st August, 2017.
S. A. N. S
ARANATISSA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour, Trade Union Relations and
Sabaragamuwa Development.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
