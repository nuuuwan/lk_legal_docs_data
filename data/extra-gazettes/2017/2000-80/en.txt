THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
Ref. No. :  3/3/6/2/29.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following land/servitiude* which is required for
a public purpose.
Acquisition of a plot of land for the Construction of the
Udatenna New Water Treatment Plant of Greater Matale water
supply scheme.
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land called Rathwattawatta in extent of
about 0.0433 Hectare depicted as Lot 1 in the P .P .M 1687
prepared by Senior Superintendent of Survey, Matale and
situated at Warapitiya village in Udatenna Grama Niladhari in
Matale Udasiyapattuwa minor division in Ukuwela Divisional
Secretary's division in Matale District in Central Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Rathwattawatta; Claimant: R. R. U. Kuruppu,
No. 30/1, Udatenna, Matale.
         All  persons interested in the aforesaid/ lands on which a
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally or by agents  duly authorized in writing, before me
at Ukuwela Divisional Secretariat on the 17th day of January
2017 at 10.30 a.m., and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 09th day of January
2017 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of
their claims for compensation for the  acquisition of the said
land/servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
E. W. N. A. EGODAWELA,
Divisional Secretary,
Ukuwela.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Ukuwela,
23rd December, 2016.
