CONFERMENT  OF  POWERS
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2(4) of the Co-operative Societies Law No. 5 of 1972 as amended by the
Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of
1992 read in conjunction with provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Krishnapillai
Thurairajasingham, Minister of Agriculture Irrigation, Animal Production and Development, Fisheries, Co-operative
Development, Food and Distribution of Eastern Province do hereby confer on person named in Column I in the Schedule
hereto, who have been appointed to assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar of Co-operative
Societies of the Eastern Province, such powers of the Registrar under the Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 as
amended by Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act,
No. 11 of 1992 and the rules their under and which are more fully described in Column III of the said Schedule in respect
of their area with effect from the dates shown in Column II of the said Schedule, to be exercised by the aforesaid during
their tenure of office.
K
RISHNAPILLAI  T HURAIRAJASINGHAM ,
Minister of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Production
and Development, Fisheries, Co-operative Development,
Food and Distribution,
Eastern Province
