EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Declaration under Section 7 
 
Ref. No.: WA/18/84/2011. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/4/2011/D/142. 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition that the Government  intends to  
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
       Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The lands allotments of Five (5) in total extent of 
about 3.9135 Hectares, shown as Lot Nos. 1 -5 in the 
preliminary plan dated 02.12.2016 for Gam 3996 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the village 
of Nawammahara, the Regam Paththuwa Minor Divison 
in the Grama Niladhari Division of  No. 179 Elapitiwala, 
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wattala in the 
District of Gampaha, in  Western Province and bounded 
as follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of Land: — ; Description: Canal; Claimant: 
Unable to find; Extent: 0.6165 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 02 
Name of Land: — ; Description: Canal; Claimant: 
Sherman Sons Company, No. 194G, Nawala Road, 
Narahenpita, Colombo 05; Extent: 0.2849 Hectare.  
 
3. Lot No. 03 
Name of Land: Kurunduwatta; Description: Open 
marshy land; Claimant: Sherman Sons Company,          
No. 194G, Nawala Road, Narahenpita, Colombo 05; 
Extent: 2.9231 Hectares. 
 
4. Lot No. 04 
Name of Land: — ; Description: Canal; Claimant: 
Sherman Sons Company, No. 194G, Nawala Road, 
Narahenpita, Colombo 05; Extent: 0.0801 Hectare. 
 
5. Lot No. 05 
Name of Land: — ; Description: Road; Claimant: 
Sherman Sons Company, No. 194G, Nawala Road, 
Narahenpita, Colombo 05; Extent: 0.0089 Hectare.     
 
All the Persons  interested in the aforesaid lands on 
which are hereby required to appear personally or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretary’s Office of Wattala, on the 08th day of March 
2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me 
in writing (in duplicate)  on or before  the 08th day of 
March 2017, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 
              W. G. PREMALAL, 
                                    Divisional Secretary, 
           Wattala. 
 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Wattala, 
09th January, 2017.
