THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Reference No.: Anu/10/5/4/Kuru. 06/Chandrani 
Liyanage. 
Land and Land Development Ministry Reference No.: 
LD/4/2/5/(NC). 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act that the Government intend to 
acquire the  following land/Servitude* which is required 
for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of  the land in extent of about 0.0600 
Hectare shown in F.V.P. 1399 additional Paper No. 
prepared by the Survey Superintendent, situated in the 
Village of Allakattuwa in the sub Division of Nuwaragam 
Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Nuwaragam Palatha Central, in the  District of 
Anuradhapura. 
 
F.V.P. 1399  
 Lot No. A part of Lot No. 273 —  Name of  the 
land : Allakattuwatta; Claimant: Mrs. Chandrani 
Liyanage, Ven Vajira Dhammacharini Silmatha; 
Extent of land: 0.0600 Hectare. 
Boundary:  
North: Lot No. 266; East: A part of Lot No. 
273;South: Lot No. 274;West: Lot No. 272. 
 
All persons who have claims for the ownership or any 
other interest for the above land are requested to meet me 
on 28th day of June, 2017 at 9.30 a.m. at the Divisional 
Secretary office of Nuwaragam Palatha Central. In case 
of persons who need to make representations on behalf of 
them, are request to authorize their nominees with a letter 
of consent for such representations. 
 
In addition, all claimants are requested to submit their 
claims in duplicate on or before 21st day of June, 2017 
giving information on the nature of the claims, 
justification for the request required amount of 
compensation method of compensation calculation (how 
was the amount calculated) with plan number and lot 
number of the land. 
 
 G. A. KITHSIRI, 
 Divisional Secretary. 
 Nuwaragam Palatha Central.
