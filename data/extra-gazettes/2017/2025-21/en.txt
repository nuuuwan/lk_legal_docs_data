PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor for Uva Provincial Council
BY virtue of powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
1) Mr. G. K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Chief Secretary of
Uva Province, in addition to  his permanent post, from the date 20.01.2017 till the return to the Island of Mr.
Gamini Rajakaruna, Chief Secretary of Uva Province, who is abroad from the date 21.01.2017 to 01.02.2017.
2) Mr. G. K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Secretary to the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural Affairs and Textile and Small
Industries of Uva  Province, in addition to  his permanent post, from the date 14.01.2017 till the return to the
Island of Mr. W. M. M. G . Abeysinghe Banda, the Secretary to the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism,
Transport, Cultural Affairs and Textile and Small Industries of  Uva Province, who is abroad from the date 14.01.2017
to 13.02.2017.
3) Dr. Mrs. M. K. D. Senanayake, Retired Special Grade officer of the Senior Medical Administrative Service, has
been re-appointed on contract basis as Director to the Health Service of  Uva Province, from the date 10.01.2017
till the appointment of a permanent officer or up to 30.06.2017
4) Mr. S. K. Weerasundara, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Animal Product and Health Service, as Acting Provincial
Director at the Department of Animal Product and Health of  Uva Province, in addition to  his permanent post,
with effect from the date 21.02.2017.
5) Mrs. D. M. Vineetha Dissanayake, Class III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of
the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Power and
Energy, Construction, Rural Infrastructure Facilities and Land of Uva Province, in addition to her pemanent post,
from the date 27.02.2017, till the return to the Island of Mr. G . A. M. S. P. Ambanwela, the Secretary to the Ministry
of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Power and Energy, Construction, Rural
Infrastructure Facilities and Land of Uva Province, who is abroad from the date 27.02.2017 to 11.03.2017.
