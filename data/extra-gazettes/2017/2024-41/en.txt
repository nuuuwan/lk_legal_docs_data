REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT,  No.  32  OF  1968
Order under Section 34
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 34 of the Registration of Persons (Amendment) Act, No. 8 of 2016,
I,   S. B. Navinna, Minister of Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs do by this Order, alter the date
of expiration published in the Gazette No. 1979/40 of August 11, 2016 relating to the period of operation of Sections 6,
9, 14, 16 and 17 of the Registration of Persons Act, No. 32 of 1968 and every regulation made under the aforesaid Act
(other than the Registration of Persons Regulations 1990, published in the Gazette No. 637/6 of November, 20, 1990) to
be 30.06.2018, until the necessary infrastructure, arragements and technological methodologies are made to.
S. B. N
A VINNA,
Minister of Internal Affairs,
Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs.
Battaramulla,
20th June 2017.
