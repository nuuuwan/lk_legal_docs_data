No. 2029/19 –  TUESDAY, JULY 25, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMEND) ACT 
No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No.: KHDS/LND/04/ACQ/11. 
 
Land Min’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2009/VE/305. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7, 
of Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, (Amendement) 
that the Government intends to acquire the  following 
land which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude  to be  acquired: 
 
An Allotment of Land called “Naligewatta” in total 
extent of about 1.9141 Hectares depicted as Lot “A” in 
the Sheet No. 24, Supplement No. 24 of F. V. P. No. 701 
dated 19.01.2015, prepared by Surveyor General, situated 
in the village called Pannila in Pannilpattuwa Minor 
Division in the Grama Niladhari Division No.  226 of 
Pannila in the Divisional  Secretary’s Division Kahawatta 
in Rathnapura District of the Sabaragamuwa Province and 
more fully described as follows: 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
01. Lot No. 804  
 
Name of the land:  Naligewatta; Description: Home 
Gardem, a few permanent buildings and a mix cultivation 
of about 1 -2 years old are available; Original Claimant: 
Director, Land Reform Commission; Extent: 1.9141 
Hectares. 
  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at the Kahawatta Divisional 
Secretariat, on 06th September 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before 04th September 2017 the nature of their 
Interest in the land, the Particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount.  
 
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry.  
 
 
 B. T. GAYANI I. KARUNARATHNE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretary’s Division, 
 Kahawatta. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kahawatta 
19th July, 2017.
