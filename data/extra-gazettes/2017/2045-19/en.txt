No. 2045/19 – WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 15, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘Rahatungoda Watta’ in extent of
about 1.6187 Hectares, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. NU/
HKT/2016/128 and dated 08.09.2016, prepared by the Senior Survey
Superintendent (Nuwara Eliya), situated in the Village of
Kawatayamana in the Grama Niladhari Division of 501D –
Kawatayamana in the Minor Division of Gannewa Korale in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Hanguranketha in Nuwara Eliya
District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rahatungodawatta – Extent: About
1.6187 Hectares
North: Road (D.C.) and Rahatungoda Watta claimed by Janatha
Estate Development Board; East: Rahatungoda Watta claimed by
Janatha Estate Development Board; South: Rahatungoda Watta
claimed by Janatha Estate Development Board and Road (D.C.);
West: Road (D.C.).
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
