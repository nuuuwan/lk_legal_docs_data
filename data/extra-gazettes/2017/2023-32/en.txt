THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1518/15 of October 09, 2007 (as last
amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1960/9 of March 29, 2016), which specifies the Faculties and
the Departments of the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka which is deemed, by virtue of the provisions of Section 139  of the
aforesaid Act to be a University established under Section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after the entry
“12. Department of Pharmacology” appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item “Faculty of
Medicine” appearing in Column I of the Schedule of the following entries :-
 “13. Department of Microbiology
14. Department of Parasitology”.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
12th June, 2017.
