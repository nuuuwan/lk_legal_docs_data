LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AMENDED
BY  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDMENT)
ACT,  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: 4/1/b/w;am;a/79.
Reference No. of the Ministry of Lands:
4-3/9/2015/WS/30.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following land which is required for a public purpose.
Description of the Land to be acquired :
The allotment of land called "Kongahamulawatta" in total
extent of about 0.0334 Hectares, depicted as Lot No. 1 in
Preliminary Plan No. Ku 2374, dated 06.07.2017, Surveyed and
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated at the village of
Theliyagonna, in Mallawapitiya Grama Niladhari Division of
No. 711, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Mallawapitiya
in Kurunegala District, North Western Province and bounded
as follows.
Lot  No. 01 – Name of the Land: Kongahamulawatta; Names
and Address of the claimants: 1. Mrs. Rishana Maharoof,
No. 59, Udayar Mawatha, Theliyagonna; Extent of the Land:
0.0334 Hectare.
Boundaries:
North by: Kongahamulawatta claimed by N. H. Halaldeen;
East by : Ela; South by : Hijra Mawatha; West by :
Kongahamulawatta claimed by Noor Sadeeka and
Kongahamulawatta claimed by Ummu Saleema.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretary's Office, at
Mallawapitiya on the 16th day of October, 2017 at 10.00 a.m.
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in
duplicate) on or before the 9th day of October, 2017, the nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
R. M. P. RATHNAYAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Mallawapitiya.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Mallawapitiya,
15th August, 2017.
