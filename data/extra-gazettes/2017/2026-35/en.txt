1A  G  25851— 367  (07/2017)
CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that in the exercise of powers vested in me in terms of paragraph (1) of Article 43 of the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology Gazetted under the
Ministry of  Higher Education and Highways in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1933/13 dated September 21, 2015 which
was amended from time to time, will be removed from the Gazette Notification relating to Subjects and Functions of
Ministries and it will be deemed as not coming under the purview of any Ministry with effect from May 16, 2017.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
28th June 2017.
