THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No.  41  OF 1978  OF  THE  NATIONAL   STATE
ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly, I, Patali Champika Ranawaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development
do by this Order vest with the Urban Development Authority with effect from today, the land situated at Dematagoda in
Wanathamulla Ward within the limits of the Municipal Council of Colombo described morefully in the Schedule hereto,
being the land called Slaughter House belonging to the Colombo Municipal Council.
P
A TALI C HAMPIKA R ANAWAKA (M. P.),
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,

22nd February 2017.

Danister de Silva Mawatha in Dematagoda Ward No. 30, Wanathamulla within the Municipal Council limits of Colombo
in the District of Colombo, Western Province. Boundaries are as follows :
