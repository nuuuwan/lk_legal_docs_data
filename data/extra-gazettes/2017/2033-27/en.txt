THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunathilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act:
Four allotments of land described in below names and extents and
depicted as Lots 'A', 'B', 'C', and 'D' in Advance Tracing No.GAM/
DOM/2016/271 dated and10.08.2016 prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the Village of Radawana in Gangabada Paththuwa
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Dompe in
Gampaha District in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Alubogahakumbura – Extent: 0.4537 Hectare
North: Ela; East: Lots B and C; South: Lot D and Paddy field
called Alubogahakumbura claimed by L.G . Lalitha; West: Paddy field
called Alubogahakumbura claimed by L.G. Lalitha.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Alubogahakumbura – Extent: 0.0985 Hectare
North: Ela; East: Paddy field called  Alubogahakumbura claimed by
P.A. Sumanawathi and Others; South: Lot C; West: Lot A and Ela.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Alubogahakumbura – Extent: 0.1599 Hectare
North: Paddy field called Alubogahakumbura claimed by P .A.
Sumanawathi, Lots  A and B; East: Paddy field called Alubogahakumbura
claimed by P. A. Sumanawathi,  Lot B and Paddy field called
Alubogahakumbura claimant not known; South: Lot D and Paddy
field called Alubogahakumbura claimant not known; West: Lots D and
A.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Alubogahakumbura – Extent: 0.8068 Hectare
North: Lots A and D, Paddy field called Alubogahakumbura claimed

not known and Lot C; South:  Paddy field called Alubogahakumbura
claimant not known and Ela; West: Paddy field called
Alubogahakumbura claimed by L.G . Lalitha and Ela.
