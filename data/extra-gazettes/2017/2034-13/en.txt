and
Lanka Sugar Company (Pvt) Ltd, Personal Post Box, Pelwatta, Buttala. of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether the claim made by Mr. D. M. P. S. Jayasekara working at the Lanka Sugar Company (Pvt) Ltd. that he had been caused injustice
by the non payment of covering allowance for the duties carried out by him from 08.06.2011 to 30.11.2014 in the post of the shift in charge
(Covering) is just and if so,to what reliefs he is entitled.
A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour (Acting).
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, this 22nd day of July, 2017.
