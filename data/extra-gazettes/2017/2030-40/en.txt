THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)  AS
AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION
(AMENDEMENT)  ACT  No.  28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My Reference No.: RPDS/LND/09/ACQ/07/23.
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/15/2011/VE/166.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by Land Acquisition Act,
that the Government intends to acquire the following lands
which are required for a public purpose.
Following allotments of land called Falmgardenwaththa,
Jambugahaarawa, Galgodedeniya, Pedikumbura, Agalawaththa
Diganahenewaththa and Thennepitawaththa depicted as Lot
No. 353 in Extent 5.2633 Hectares in F.V .P .347 situated in
Weralupa Village of 153A New Town Grama Niladari Division
of Uda Paththuwa Sub Division in Kuruwita Koralaya,
Divisional Secretary's Division Rathnapura in the District of
Rathnapura in Sabaragamuwa Province.
Claimant: Balangoda Plantation, P .B. No. 06, Falmgarden,
Ratnapura.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands on which
servitude is to be acquired are here by requested to appear
personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, before me
at 10.30 a.m.on 23rd September, 2017 at Rathnapura Divisional
Secretariat.
Moreover, they are here by requested to notify to me in
writing with two duplicates on or before 16th September, 2017
the nature of their interest in land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands,
the amount of compensation and details of the computation
of such amount.
C. M. KOTTAWATHTHA,
Divisional Secretary,
Rathnapura.
Divisional Secretariat,
Rathnapura.
31st July , 2017.
