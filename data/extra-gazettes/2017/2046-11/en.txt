THE   URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   PROJECTS   (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)   ACT   No. 2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act No. 2 of 1980,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of the Minister-
in-Charge of the subject of Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development, being of the opinion that the lands specified
in the schedules I and II hereto are urgently required for the purposes of mix development Housing Project and resettlement
of underserved settlers respectively do by this order, declare that the said lands are required for such purposes.
MAITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
20th November, 2017.

prepared by the Surveyor General containing in extent of 12 A-0R-28.3P (4.9277 Ha.) situated in Suwarapola village within
the Divisional Secretariat Division of Kesbewa in the District of Colombo, Western Province.
Boundaries of the said allotment of land is as follows:–
North : Lot No. 39, 27, 26, 24, 23, 22 in Ward No. 04 of Cadastral Map No. 521201, Moratuwa Road (Lot No. 01 in Ward
No. 04 of Cadastral No. 521201), Lot No. 20, 21, 19, 16, 15, 46, 9 and 56 in Ward No. 04 of Cadastral Map No.
521201.
