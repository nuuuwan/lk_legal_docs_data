My No.: IR/COM/05/A/2015/180.
THE  INDUSTRIAL  DISPUTES  ACT, CHAPTER  131  OF  THE  LEGISLATIVE  ENACTMENTS OF CEYLON
( 1956 REVISED EDITION)
Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956) as amended by Acts., Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968 dated 14.03.2017 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka Extraordinary No. 2012/14 dated 28.03.2017 the Industrial Dispute in respect of the matter specified in
the statement of the Commissioner of Labour dated 22.02.2017.
Group of Employees including Mrs. S. P. Anoma Shyamali Kumari,Balagollagama, Balalla.  of the one part
AND
Central Cultural Fund, No. 212/1, Bauddhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 07.  of the other part
was referred to Mr. T.E. Santharajan for settlement by arbitration.
AND whereas it is now deemed expedient that the said order be revoked, I, W. D. J. Seneviratne, Minister of Labour, Trade
Union Relations and Sabaragamu Development do hereby revoke the said Order and further make Order that no proceedings
be taken upon the said Order dated 14.03.2017.
W. D. J. S
ENEVIRATNE,
Minister of Labour and Trade Union Relations,
and Sabaragamu Development.
Colombo.
09th November, 2017.

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
 Whether the group of employees including Mrs.S.P.Anoma Shyamali Kumari, who had worked in the  post of Excavation
Conservation Assistant, in the places of worship at Yapahuwa, Dambadeniya, Ridee Viharaya (Rideegama) and Nathagane
(Panduwasnuwara) of the Vayamba Square of the Central Cultural Fund have been caused injustice by termination of their
services from 02.11.2015 and if so to what reliefs they are entitled.
A. W
IMALAWEERA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, this 03rd day of October, 2017.
