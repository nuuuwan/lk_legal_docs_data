EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  No.: MOR/LAN/01/F1/186. 
Land Min.’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2009/VE/154. 
 
 
IN  the view of the Government decides to acquire the  
following land for public purpose, I hereby notice the 
decision under the Sub section (1) of the Section 7 of the 
Land Acquition Act.  
 
Details of the Land to be  acquired: 
 
Allotments containing 0.0347 Hectares, which 
mentioned as Lot Nos. 513 and 514 of the cadastral plan 
No. 520212/05/02 and dated 30.09.2016 of the Surveyor 
General and situated at Angulana (within the Moratuwa  
Urban Council area) in Moratuwa Divisional Secretariat 
area in the Colombo District of Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 513 
Name of the Land: Not mentioned ; Claimant: 
Government: First Claimant Weligamage Keerthipala 
Silva of No. 334, Beach Road, Angulana; Extent: 0.0122 
Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 514 
Name of the Land: Madangahawaththa, Gewatta; 
Claimant: Government; First Claimant ; Weligamage 
Keerthipala Silva of No. 334, Beach Road, Angulana; 
Extent: 0.0225 Hectare. 
 
All those who claim ownerships, entitlements or any 
other connectedness for the said land or persons having   
power of attorney delegated by them are requested to 
meet me at Moratuwa Divisional Secretariat, At 09.30 
a.m. on 28th June 2017. 
 
In addition, they should inform me in  written with 
two copies of nature of their entitlements, reasons of 
claiming compensation with regard to the acquisition of 
the land compensation  amount and how it has been 
calculated before 21st June 2017. Relevant deeds and 
documents should be presented in the interview in order 
to prove the entitlements. 
 
 H. R. H. KARUNARATHNE 
 Divisional Secretary,  and Land Acquiring Officer, 
                                           Moratuwa. 
 
19th April, 2017.  
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Moratuwa.
