No. 2050/11 – TUESDAY, DECEMBER 19, 2017
EXCISE  DUTY  ON  IMPORT  OF  NON - POTABLE  SPIRITS
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sections 2, 12, 22 and 25, read with section 32 of the Excise Ordinance
(Chapter 52), as amended from time to time, I, Mangala Samaraweera, Minister of Finance and Mass Media do by
this order amend the Rules set out in the Excise Notification No. 999 of the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2044/33
dated 09 November 2017 by inserting the following paragraph immediately after paragraph 5 and renumbering
paragraph 6 as paragraph 7.
“ 6. Any export oriented manufacturing company, which has entered into an agreement with the Board of
Investment of Sri Lanka and exports not less than 80% of total products manufactured using, as raw
materials the items in the schedule I of the Excise Notification No. 999 of the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 2044/33 dated 09 November 2017 and such export is certified by the Director General of the Board
of Investment of Sri Lanka, the rate of excise duty for import of such raw materials is 10% of the
payable duty specified in the said Excise Notification No. 999.”
MANGALA SAMARAWEERA,
Minister of Finance and Mass Media.
Ministry of Finance and Mass Media,
Colombo 01,
19 December 2017.
