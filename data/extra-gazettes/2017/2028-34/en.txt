THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, amend
the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1603/35 of May 28, 2009 and Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1711/24 of June 24, 2011 (as last amended by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary    No. 1995/24 of December 01,
2016) which specifies the Faculties and the Departments of the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, Sri Lanka, which is
deemed by virtue of the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be a University established under Section 21 of the
said Act, by the substitution for the item “Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences” appearing in Column I of the Schedule
to the aforesaid Order, under the heading “Faculty” and in its corresponding entries appearing in Column II of such Schedule
under the heading “Department” of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty Department
 Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences 1. Department of Economics
2. Department of Social Statistics
3. Department of Geography
4. Department of Sinhala and Mass Communication
5. Department of History and Archeology
6. Department of Pali and Buddhist Studies
7. Department of Languages and Cultural Studies
8. Department of English and Linguistics
9. Department of Political Science
10. Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice
11. Department of Sociology
12. Department of Anthropology”
L
AKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
14th July, 2017.
