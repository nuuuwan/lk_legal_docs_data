No. 2010/18 – TUESDAY , MARCH 14, 2017
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  FLOWERS,  ORNAMENTAL  PLANTS,  VEGETABLES  AND
FRUITS  GROWING  AND  EXPORT  TRADE
IN pursuance of the authority conferred on me by the Minister of Labour by an order made by him under Section 33 (1) (a)
of the Wages Boards Ordinance (chapter 136) and published in Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1916/12 of 27.05.2015,
I, M. D. Chandani Amaratunga, Commissioner of Labour hereby notify that I propose to determine under Section 33 (2) of
the aforesaid Ordinance the minimum rate of daily Wage for the workers in the Flowers, Ornamental Plants, Vegetables and
Fruits growing and export trade set out in the schedule below.
Objections to the proposed determination will be received by me until 12.00 noon on 07th April 2017.
Every such objection must be made in writing and must Contain a Statement of the grounds upon which such objection
is taken.
M. D. C. A
MARA TUNGA,
Commissioner General of Labour.
Department of Labour,
Colombo 05.
10th March, 2017.
