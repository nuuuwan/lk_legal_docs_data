No. 2035/4 –  MONDAY SEPTEMBER 04, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
 THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference  No.: 3/7/5/32. 
Land Minister Reference No.: 3/99/E/220. 
 
I  hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to  
the following land which is required for a public purpose:  
     Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land called Mulhalkele Estate, 
Perapitiya Paddy Field, Gallenagawa Hena plain Total 
extent about 0.2178, 0.3140 shown as Lots Number 61 
and 63 of F.V.P 615 situated in the Village of 
Wathumulla in 515 D Wathumulla Division  in Divisional 
Secretary Division of Walapane in Nuwara Eliya District 
in Central Province and described as follows: 
 
Lot No. 61 
Name of the Land: Mulhalkele Estate, Perapitiya 
Paddy Field, Gallenagawa Hena; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant: K. D. Thamara Nandane, K. D. 
Pathma Wijelatha and M. Karunawathe; Land Use: 
Garden, Contatins  20 -25 Years old Mixed Cultivation 
and Permanent two Buildings; Extent: 0.2178 Hectare. 
Lot No. 63 
Name of the Land: Mulhalkele Estate, Perapitiya 
paddy field, Gallenagawa Hena; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: D. M. Dissanayaka; Land Use: Garden, 
Contatins 20 -25 Years old Mixed Cultivation and 
Cemetery; Extent: 0.3140 Hectare. 
 
 
All Persons interest in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized 
in writing before me at  the Devisional Secretary’s office 
Walapane on 30th day of September 2017 at 10.00 a.m. 
and hereby requested to notify to me in writing                            
(in duplicate) on or before 21st September, 2017 the nature 
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the computation 
of such amount.  
 
 
  I. M. SENANAYAKA, 
                               Divisional Secretary, 
    Walapane. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Walapane, 
16th August, 2017.
