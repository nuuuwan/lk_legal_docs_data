Regulations
 1.  These regulations may be cited as the Medical Devices Pricing Regulations, No. 01 of 2017.
 2.  No manufacturer, importer, distributor, trader, pharmacist or a medical institution inclusive of a private medical 
institution or a person who is in possession of a medical device shall sell, offer for sale or charge for any medical 
device described by brand name or approved name and set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto, above the 
Maximum Retail Price (MRP) stipulated in the corresponding entry in Column IV thereof.
      3.  Every person or institution referred to in regulation 2, who sells or offer for sale or charges for any medical 
device which is described by its brand name or approved name and set out in the Schedule hereto, shall issue 
a receipt clearly indicating the brand name or the approved name and the price of such medical device.

 4.  The relevant Maximum Retail Price as set out in the Schedule, of each unit of item in the stock of such medical 
device manufactured or available for sale shall be printed or marked in their respective commercial package or 
label as expeditiously as practicable, prior to the expiry of a period of forty ﬁ ve days from the date of publication 
of these regulations.
 5.  Any manufacturer, importer, distributor, trader, pharmacist, or a medical institution inclusive of private medical 
institution or a person who is in possession of such medical devices who contravenes the provisions of these 
regulations commits an offence and shall be triable under Part IV of the National Medicines Regulatory Authority 
Act, No. 5 of 2015.
 6.  It shall be the duty of every manufacturer, distributor, trader, importer, pharmacist, a medical institution inclusive 
of private medical institution and a person who is in possession of such medical devices to display at every 
retail outlet the Maximum Retail Price of the medical devices set out in the Schedule hereto.
 7.  “Private Medical Institution” shall have the same meaning as in Section 20 of the Private Medical Institutions 
(Registration) Act, No. 21 of 2006.
