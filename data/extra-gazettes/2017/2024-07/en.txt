CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notifications made under the Paragraphs 24(2)(1)(xiii) and 24(3) of No. 04 of the year 1992 Road Passenger
Transport Statute and Paragraph 20 of  No. 01 of the year 2000 Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute
of the Central Province
I, Ediriweera Weerawardena, Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy, Housing and Construction of
the Central Provincial Council hereby announce under the powers vested in me by the Paragraph 24(2)(1)(xiii) of No. 04 of
year 1992 Road Passenger Transport Statute and Paragraph 20 to be read with the Paragraphs 19(n) and (t) of No. 01 of the
year 2000 Passenger Transport Services  Authority Statute, the amounts to be charged from the learners for driving lessons
conducted by the Driving School of the Central Province Passenger Transport Services Authority according to the below
mentioned vehicle categories.
The amounts are charged with effect from 22.05.2017.
Vehicle Category Relevant charge
Motor Bikes, Vans, Trishaws (A, B, B1) Rs. 14,000.00
Motor Bikes (A) RS. 3500.00
V ans (B) Rs. 10,000.00
Trishaws (B1) Rs. 4,000.00
Trishaws, Motor Bikes (B1, A) Rs. 7,000.00
Motor Bikes, Vans (A, B) Rs. 13,000.00
Vans, Trishaws (B, B1) Rs. 13,000.00
