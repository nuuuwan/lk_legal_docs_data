No. 2047/29 –  WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER,  29,  2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
                                                                                               
My Number: DSO/HOM/LAC/102. 
Land Ministry reference Number: 4-3/17/2017/e/142. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under sub-section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition(Amendment)Act,No.28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
The land under mentioned name and the extent which is 
depicted as lot No. 115 in sheet 06, Zone 01, in the 
Cadastral map  No. 520008 dated 25.10.2017 prepared by 
the Surveyor General, situated at Grama Niladhari 
Division No.587, Mattegoda West in Homagama 
Divisional Secretary’s  Division of Colombo District 
Western Province Boundaries. 
 
01. Lot No. 115 —  Kekunagahawaththa; Original 
claimant: Prime Lands Private Limited  of No. 75,          
D. S. Senanayake Mawatha, Colombo 08; Extent: 
4.0469 Hectare. (10 Acres) in extent. 
North: Zone 03 of Cadastral map 520029, lot No. 114 
and Block 01 of Cadastral map 520029 (Road, Road 
Development Authority), East: Lot No.114, Block 01 of 
Cadastral map 520029 (Road, Road Development 
Authority) and lot No.116. South: Lot No.116, 6  and 
Block 02; West: Block 02, block 07 of Cadastral map 
520029 and Block 03 of Cadastral map 520029. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land on which is 
to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at 
Homagama Divisional Secretary’s Office on the 19th day 
of February, 2018 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing in duplicate on or before the 
01st day of February, 2018 the  nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the compensation of such 
amount. 
 
 G. S. K. KANNANGARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Homagama Division. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Homagama, 
24th November, 2017.
