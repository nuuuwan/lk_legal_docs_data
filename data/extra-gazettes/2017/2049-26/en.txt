THE  PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Godapitiya Muslim Festival - 2017
IN terms of regulations “3” of the regulations formed under the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) and Public in the
Government Gazette No. 10630 of 8th January 1954. It is hereby notified that annual Pilgrimage of Godapitiya Poruwai
Mosque “Muhiyadeen Muslim Mosque” at Godapitiya, Akuressa in Athuraliya Divisional Secretariat in Matara District,
will commence on 20th December and terminate 31st December 2017.
The regulations relating this Pilgrimage Ordinance in Government Gazette No. 10630 of 8th January 1954, shall
in force within the camp above referred to in the regulations and described in the schedule thereto during the period of the
Pilgrimage referred to above.
D
EEPIKA K. G UNARATHNA,
Divisional Secretary,
Athuraliya.
Divisional Secretariat Athuraliya,
Thibbotuwawa,
Akuressa.
08th December 2017.
