No. 2031/48  –  FRIDAY AUGUST 11, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Ref.  No.: DEV/LND/ACQUI/03/04-14. 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude  which is required for the public purpose 
mentioned below: 
 
Description of the land  to be acquired: 
 
Following allotments of  lands bearing Lot 01 
(Extent : 2.3033 Hec.) depicted in the preliminary  survey 
plan bearing No. MR/2698, situated in the Village of 
Naotunna, Grama Niladhari Division of Naotunna in 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Devinuwara, Matara 
District, Southern Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Gorakapandurahena alias 
Gorakagahahena, Babiyalage Kurunduwatta and 
Welanketiya; Description: Home Garden, Mixed Crop 
Cultivation; Extent: 2.3033 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  on which 
servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at the Divisional Secretariat, Devinuwara, on the 14th 
September, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to 
notify  to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
30th August, 2017 nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/Servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
       RANJITH YAPA, 
            Acquisition Officer/Divisional Secretary, 
                                                Devinuwara. 
                            
Divisional Secretariat, 
Devinuwara, 
13th July, 2017.
