THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)  AS  AMENDED  BY  THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  (AMENDEMENT)
ACT  No. 28  OF  1964
Notice under Section 7
My  No.:  KEG/LAN/AQUIS/A3/99.
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act  as Amended by the Land Acquisition
(Amendment Act), No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends  to acquire the lands described the schedule given below for
the reconstruction of the bridge No. 11/4, Bulathkohupitiya-Karawanella Road in Kegalle.
     Description of the Lands to be acquired:
Four allotments of land described in the Scheduled attached below depicted in preliminary  Plan No: P. P. K. 3529 and Twenty
Eight Allotments of land described in the Scheduled attached below depicted in Preliminary Plan No: P . P .K. 3530 prepared by
the Surveyor Genaral situated in Hungampola and Godigamuwa Grama Niladhari Divisions (within the Kegalle Pradeshiya
Sabha boundaries) in the Divisional Secterary's Division in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuwa Province.
Dates for Inquires:
Lot No: 01 - 04 of  P . P . K. 3529 on the date of 15.05.2017 at  9.00 a.m.
Lot No: 01 - 07 of  P . P . K. 3530 on the date of 15.05.2017 at  10.00 a.m.
Lot No: 08 - 15 of  P . P . K. 3530 on the date of 16.05.2017 at  9.00 a.m.
Lot No: 16 - 28 of  P . P . K. 3529 on the date of 17.05.2017 at  9.00 a.m.
    All persons interested in the aforesaid lands to be acquired are hereby required to appear perso nally or by agents duly
authorized in written before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kegalle on the above mentioned days and on the scheduled time.
And are hereby requested to notify me in writing with duplicate of on or before 28h April 2017 the nature of their interest in
the land the Particulars of their  claims for compensation for acquisition of the Said Land the amount of Compensation and the
details of the Computation of such amount.
H. T. R. NALIKA PIYASENA,
Divisional Secretary,
Land Acquisition Officer, Kegalle.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Kegalle,
21th April, 2017.
