Amendment to Paragraph (a) of Appendix I of Minute on the Sri Lanka Audit Examiners’ Service
The last paragraph under Appendix I (a) of Minute on the Sri Lanka Audit Examiners’ Service published in the
Gazette Extraordinary No.380/61 of 20th December 1985 has been amended by the Public Service Commission and
the amended paragraph is published for information.
“Marks for the Accounting and Auditing question papers will be 150 each. Marks for other two question papers will
be 100 each. Applicants should obtain at least 40 per cent of the total marks for each question paper to be
considered for passing in the Efficiency Bar Examination.”
