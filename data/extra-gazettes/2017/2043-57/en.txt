PRADESHIYA  SABHAS   ACT,   No.   15  OF  1987
Order
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by sub-section(c) of Section 9A of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read
with Section 2 of the same, I, Faiszer Musthapha, Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government, hereby dissolve,
the Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha constituted by the Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 453/10 of the 12th
May of 1987 with effect from the 14th day of February, 2018, in order to constitute the new Pradeshiya Sabhas, which
shall be henceforth cited as the Maskeliya Pradeshiya Sabha, Norwood Pradeshiya Sabha and Ambagamuwa Pradeshiya
Sabha.
F
AISZER MUSTHAPHA,
Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government.
Colombo,
On the 01st day of November, 2017.
