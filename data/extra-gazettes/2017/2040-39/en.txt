SOUTHERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Pawn Brokers Statute No. 01 of 2013 of the Southern Provincial Council
BY virtue of the power vested in me under Section 6(3) of the Pawn Brokers Statute No. 01 of 2013 of the Southern
Provincial Council, I, Gusthinnawadu Shan Wijayalal De Silva, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Southern Provincial
Council do by the following orders with effect from the 31st October 2013.
ORDERS
Fee for the registration of the business of a pawn broker - Rs. 25,000.00
Fee for the annual renewal of a license for the business of a pawn broker - Rs. 1,000.00
G
USTHINNAW ADU SHAN WIJAYALAL DE SILV A,
