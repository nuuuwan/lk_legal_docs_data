PART  IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS
NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification Made by the Governor of the North Western Province
I, Amara Piyaseeli Rathnayaka being the Governor of the North Western Province in terms of power vested in me under
Sub-section 8(a) of Section 154(b) of Amendment Act, No. Thirteen of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby proclaim that the First Session of the Sixth Provincial Council in the North Western
Province is terminated with effect from Mid Night on 8th December 2017.
It is hereby further proclaimed that the Second Session of the Sixth Provincial Council in the North Western
Province in summoned to be held at the “Sasri” hall in the Provincial Council Office Complex, Kurunegala at 10.30 am on
16th January 2018.
Proclaimed at Kurunegala on this 8th December 2017.
A
MARA P IYASEELI R A THNAY AKA,
The Governor of  the North Western Province.
