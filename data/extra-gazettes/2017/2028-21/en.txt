EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Declaration under Section 7 
 
 
My No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/72. 
Land Min. Ref. No.: 4-3/5/2015/CEB/201. 
 
 
I hereby notify, under Sub -section 7, of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land Acquisition 
(Amendments) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the  following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude  to be  acquired: 
 
Allotments of the Land called Sambodhiwatta, 
situated at Kosmodara Village of Kosmodara Grama 
Niladhari’s Division of Kotapola Divisional Secretariat in 
Matara District  in the Southern Province, depicted as lot 
No. 2297, 2298,and 2299 of supplementary No. 89 in 
Sheet No. 117 in  FVP 45 made by Survey General, 
containing in extent about 0.0839 Hectares and 
boundaries of the land given below. 
 
01. Lot No. 2297 —  Name of the land 
Sambodhiwatta Containing in extent about —  Person 
who claiming over —  Sambodhi Trustee Board, No. 
09A, Closenburg Rd, Magalle, Galle  —  Hectare 
0.0155  
North: Lot No. 742 1/2, and Lot No. 331; East: Lot 
No. 742 1/2, and 2298, Lot No. 331; South: Lot No. 331; 
West: Lot No. 331. 
 
02. Lot No. 2298 Containing in extent about —  
Name of the land Sambodhiwatta —  Person who 
claiming over —  Sambodhi Trustee Board, No. 09A, 
Closenburg Rd, Magalle, Galle  —  Hectare 0.0066. 
North: Lot No. 742 1/2; East: Lot No. 742 1/2, and 
Lot No. 331; South: Lot No. 331; West: Lot No. 2297  
and Lot No. 742 1/2. 
 
03. Lot No. 2299 —  Name of the land 
Sambodhiwatta Containing in extent about —  Person

09A, Closenburg Rd, Magalle, Galle  —  Hectare 
0.0618. 
North: Lot No. 742 1/2 and 331; East: Lot No. 333 
and 744; South: Lot No. 744 and 331; West: Lot No. 331 
and 742 1/2. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land / servitude 
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally, 
or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me at  
Divisional Secretariat, Kotapola on 29th August, 2017 at 
10.00 a.m. Further, I hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 22nd August 2017, 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation  of the said land/servitude* 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
