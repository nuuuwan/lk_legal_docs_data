THE  LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice under Section 7
My  No.: UDU/2/2/10/42.
Land Ministry No.: L/03/J/95/FR/189.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964  that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude
for public purpose (the conservation of Dumbara mountain
range.)
     Description of the land / servitude to be acquired:
An allotment of land called ''Karapilahena Gatehena"
bearing an extent of  about 1.1410 Hectare mentioned in F.V .P
426 Sup. 1 Sheet 1 (lot number 269), Supplement of the
Surveyor General, situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of
Pallekanda, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Udadumbara of  Kandy District, Central Province.
Name  and  Address  of  Claimants : Mr. H. M. G. G.
Ranaweera Banda, No. 221, Welankatiya, Kalugala.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/servitude on
which is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara,  on the 16th  August
2017 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 9th August 2017 nature
of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
 SUJANI R. WIJETUNGA,
Divisional Secretary,
Udadumbara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Udadumbara.
14th June, 2017.
