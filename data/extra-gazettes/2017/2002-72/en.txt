UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Horizon College of Business and Technology (Pvt) Ltd, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under  the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 62877, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made
under said Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1975/47 of  July 14, 2016 by the addition immediately after
the entry ‘‘Bachelor of Science Honours in Information Technology’’ appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding entry
in Column II  thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE I
CONDITIONS
Horizon College of  Business and Technology (Pvt.) Ltd. shall, when admitting students for the degree programme
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in Networking  and
Mobile Computing, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications of 3 “S” passes in any subject stream in one and the same
sitting at GCE Advanced Level Examination or equivalent, as per UGC circular No. 995.
