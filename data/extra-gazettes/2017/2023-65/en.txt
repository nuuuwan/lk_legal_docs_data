THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the lands described below are needed for a public purpose
and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
Five (05) allotments of land in total extent of about 4.0469 Hectares
depicted as Lots ‘A ’ to ‘E’  in Advance Tracing No. KE/RMB/2016/
251 and dated 31.08.2016, prepared by the Surveyor situated in the
Village of  Walgama(F.V .P.149) in the Grama Niladhari Division No.04
of  Walgama in Walgampattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Rambukkana in the District of Kegalle in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Edella Estate , Panaliya division – Extent: 1.5475
Hectares.
North: Edella Estate, Dangahamula Watta, Kosgahamada Kumbura
and Kosgahamada Watta; East: Lot B; South: Lot B and Pradesheeya
Sabha Road; West: Pradesheeya Sabha Road and Edella Estate.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Extent: 0.0513 Hectare.
North: Lot A, Kosgahamada Watta and Road; East: Lots C,D and
E; South: Lot E and Pradesheeya Sabha Road; West: Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Edella Estate , Panaliya division – Extent: 0.0427
Hectare.
North: KongahagodaWatta; East: KongahagodaWatta, Lot No. 357
of F. V . P. 149, Road and Yapage Watta; South: Lot D and Road;
West: Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Extent: 0.0310 Hectare.
North: Lot C; East: Lot C, Road and Yapage Watta; South: Road,
Thimbirigahamada Hena and Lot E; West: Lots E and B.
5. Lot ‘E’ – Edella Estate , Panaliya division – Extent: 2.3744
Hectare.
North: Lots B and D; East: Thimbirigahamada Hena, Lot 357 of F.
V . P. 149, Ihala Thimbirigahamula Kumbura, Thimbirigahamula
Pillewa, Thimbirigahamade Watta, Lot 406 of F. V . P. 146 and
Thimbirigahamade Watta; South: Edella Estate and Pradesheeya Sabha
Road; West: Pradesheeya Sabha Road.
