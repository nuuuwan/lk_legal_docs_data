No. 2036/37 – FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 15, 2017
NORTH   CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
North Central Provincial Road Development Statute No. 02 of 2016
THE notice being made under the Sub section 01 of the North Central Provincial Road Development Statute No. 02 of 2016.
In terms of the authority and power vested in me being the Provincial Land, Irrigation, Road Development, Rural Development,
Housing and Women Affairs Minister by the Sub section 01 of the North Central Provincial Road Development Statute No. 02
of  2016 passed by the North Central Provincial Council. It is hereby notified that the  North Central Provincial Road Development
Department established under the said Statute will come in to effect from 1st of  September, 2017.
W.K. SARATH ILANGASINGHE,
North Central Provincial Minister of Land, Irrigation,
Road Development, Rural Development,
Housing and Women Affairs.
North Central Provincial Councils
Anuradhapura.
At Anuradhapura.
02nd of  September, 2017.
