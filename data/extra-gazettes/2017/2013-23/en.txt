PILGRIMAGE  ORDINANCE
Annual Higher Ordination of Rohana Sanga Sabhawa
IT is hereby informed that 32nd Higher Ordination Ceremony of the Sri Rohana Sanga Sabhawa is to be held on 28th, 29th,
30th April 2017 at Sri Rohana Sanga Sabhawa of Siri Rathanajothi Piriwena, Urapola Thihagoda and Mangala Seemamalakaya
of Randupatha Sri Rohana Upoosathagaraya Maha Vihara, Kotuwegoda, Matara and that the pilgrims should refrain from
carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials and that the standing orders and regulations under Section 02 of the
Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175 - LDB/11/60) declared on 14th October, 1991 and published in the Gazette of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka will be in force during the period in which the ceremony is held.
R
ANJITH YAPA,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
At Divisional Secretariat,
Matara,
31st March 2017.
