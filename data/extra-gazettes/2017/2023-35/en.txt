of the Central Province and more fully described as follows.
1. Lot No. 01
Name of the Land: Angurupota Mukalana; Description:Jungle;
Claimants: S. M. Tikiribanda, 27/11, Handaganawa, Morayaya;
Extent: 2.0701 Hectares.
2. Lot No. 02
Name of the Land: Angurupota Mukalana; Description:Jungle;
Claimants: S. M. Tikiribanda, 27/11, Handaganawa, Morayaya;
Extent: 0.4829 Hectare.

are hereby required to appear personally, or by agents
duly authorized in writing, before me at  Madadumbara
Divisional Secretariat, on the 13th day of September 2017at
10.00 am  and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 06th September,
2017, the nature of their interests in the aforesaid land/
servitude, the particulars of their claims for  compensation
the details of computation of  such amount.
