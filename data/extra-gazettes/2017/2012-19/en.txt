THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice  under  Section 7
                                                              My Ref. No :  5/3/4/3/4-1.
                    Land Ministry's Ref. No :  LD/05/2009/UDA/227.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acqusition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Purpose of acquisition  : Urban development activities cum
access road under the Greter
Dambulla Development Project
Description of the lands servitudes to be acquired :
Sixteen (16) allotments of land in extent of  0.7177 Hectare
depicted as Lot Nos. 1 to 16 in P P Ma. 1662 dated 12.02.2016
prepared by Surveyor General situated at Ward No. 2, Padeniya
No. E445 Gramasewa Division of Dambulla town, within the
Municipal Council limits of Dambulla in Divisional Secretary's
Division  of  Dambulla in the District of  Matale, Central
Province.
