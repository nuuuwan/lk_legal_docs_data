THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms do hereby declare under Sub – section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Land described below is needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
An allotment of land in total  extent of about 0.0183 Hectare and
depicted as Lot ‘A’ in the Advance Tracing Plan No.
MR/ATR/2016/295 and  dated 14.10.2016 (F.V.P. 86) prepared
by the Senior Superintendant of surveys of Matara  situated in
Talahagama Village in Weligam Korale North Minor Division in
Athuraliya Divisional Secretary’s Division in Matara District in
Southern Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot  ‘A’ - Parana Pansalwaththa – Extent:  0.0183 Hectare
North: Parana Pansalwaththa claimed by Sri Dharmarakshitha
Purana Viharaya, Liyanagewaththa claimed by Sumanawathi
Samarasinghe and Others;  East: Liyanagewaththa claimed by
Sumanawathi Samarasinghe and Others, Koggedarawaththa alias
Rukaththanagahakoratuwa claimed by T. L. Dharmadasa and
Others;  South: Koggedarawaththa alias Rukththanagahakoratuwa
claimed by T. L. Dharmadasa and Others Road (R.D.A.); West:
Parana Pansalwaththa claimed by Road (R.D.A.) and Chief
Incumbent Sri Dharmarakshitha Purana Viharaya.
