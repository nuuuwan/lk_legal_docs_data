THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  No. 40 OF  2008
Order under Section  86
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 86 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Lakshman
Kiriella, Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, restrict access by the public to or from the
Piliyandala Bypass which was declared as a national highway or any part of such highway other than from the access roads
specified in the Schedule to the Order made under Section 8 of the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1888/48 of November 14, 2014, for the reasons stated  below :
1) to prevent the reclamation of paddy fields and low lying lands alongside the Piliyandala Bypass ; and
2) to prevent the reduction of productivity of the Piliyandala Bypass due to unauthorized access connections.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Battaramulla,
02nd October 2017.
