KAMBURUPITIYA   SAPUGODA    ANNUAL    ASALA    MAHA    PERAHARA  -  2017
UNDER the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka  No. 308 dated 27 th June1984, it is hereby notified that annual Asala Maha Perehara of  Sapugoda  Ilangamgoda
Purana Raja Maha Vi hara (Sapugoda Vidyaniketha Pirivena) of  Kamburupitiya, Matara district, Southern Province for
the year 2017 will be held from 06 th July 2017 to 13 th July 2017.
N. H. M. W. W. N. HERATH,
Divisional Secretary,
Kamburupitiya.
At Divisional Secretariat of Kamburupitiya,
22nd  June 2017.
