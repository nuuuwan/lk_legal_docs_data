Direction No. 63
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders that they shall not manufacture,
import, distribute, store or sell or display for sale, expose for sale or offer for sale, wholesale or retail, products/items o f
operating voltage not exceeding 250V and listed below, unless the said products/items bear the SLS Product Certification
Mark issued by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution.
Product/Item SLS
(i) Rewirable and non-rewirable plugs SLS 734 : Part 1
(ii) Switched and un switched Socket outlets and extension SLS 734 : Part 2
cords/portable socket outlets
(iii) Adaptors and one to one conversion adaptors SLS 734 : Part 3
(iv) Fused connection units (switched and un switched) SLS 734 : Part 4
(v) Fused conversion plugs SLS 734 : Part 5
(vi) Cable reels fitted with plugs and socket outlets SLS 1532
           This Direction shall come into effect from 01st January, 2019
                                                                                            By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority
H
ASITHA TILLEKERATNE,
Chairman.
Colombo,
07th December, 2017.

Direction No. 64
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Direction under Section 12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all manufacturers, importers, distributors and traders that they shall not manufacture,
import, distribute, store or sell or display for sale, expose for sale or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any electrical
appliance of operating voltage not exceeding 250V , unless it carries an electrical plug conforming to SLS : 734 Part 1.
This Direction shall come into effect from 01st January, 2019
