THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake,  Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
The allotments of lands in extent of 0.1465 Hectare shown as Lots
A, B and C depicted in Advance Tracing No. Gam/Wat/2016/536 and
dated 28.02.2017 prepared by the Surveyor General of Gampaha
situated in the Village of Nawammahara, in 179 Elapitawala Grama
Niladhari Division, in Divisional Secretary's Division of Wattala, in
the District of Gampaha in Western Province and bounded as follows
:
01. Lot  ‘A’ –  Kurunduwatta – Extent: 0.0530 Hectare
North: Canal and Kurunduwatte claimed by Sri Lanka Navy; East
and South: Canal; West: Kurunduwatte claimed by Sri Lanka Navy.
02. Lot  'B' – Kajugahakumbura – Extent: 0.0855 Hectare
North and East: Kajugahakumbura claimed by Sri Lanka Navy;
South: Kajugahakumbura claimed by Sri Lanka Navy, Pradeshiya
Sabha Road and Lot C; West: Lot C and Kajugahakumbura claimed
by Sri Lanka Navy.
03. Lot  'C' – Kajugahakumbura– Extent: 0.0080 Hectare
North: Kajugahakumbura claimed by Sri Lanka Navy and Lot B;
East: Lot B and Kajugahakumbura claimed by Sri Lanka Navy; South:
Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Kajugahakumbura claimed by Sri
Lanka Navy.
Gayantha Karunatilake,
Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms.
