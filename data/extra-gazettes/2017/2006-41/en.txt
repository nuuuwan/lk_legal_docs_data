SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karnanayake, Minister of Finance,  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of
list of  the commodities specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in
the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing
from 17th  February 2017, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,
No. 48 of   2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 1981/46 of 24.08.2016  in respect of Potatoes (HS Code No. 0701.90), Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1980/37 of 19.08.2016 in respect of B’Onions (HS Code No. 0703.10.20) and Gazette Extraordinary No. 1986/55
of 29.09.2016 in respect of Lentils (HS Code No. 0713.40.11, 0713.40.12, 0713.40.21 and 0713.40.22) are rescinded
with effect from 17th February 2017.
R
AV I K ARNANAYAKE,
 Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
16th February, 2017.
