THE   CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC OF  SRI  LANKA
Notification
IT is hereby notified that in terms of Section 4 of the Commission of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393), His Excellency Maithripala
Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has further enlarged the period by 06 weeks up to
08th December, 2017 considering the current role of the Commission, having enlarged the period ending 27th April,
2017 by three months up to 27th July, 2017 by Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2029/15 dated
25th July, 2017 in the exercise of powers vested in him by Section 4 of the said Act and having again enlarged the said
period by three months up to 27th October, 2017 for rendition of the final report of the Commission established under
Section 2 thereof, on 27th January, 2017 by Notification published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2003/41 dated 27th
January 2017 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka to investigate, inquire into and report on the Issuance of
Treasury Bonds during the period 01st February 2015 to 31st March, 2016 on completion of the aforesaid tasks.
         By Order of His Excellency,
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
October 23, 2017.
