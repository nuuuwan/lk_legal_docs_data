THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land, called Dunkan Estate in total extent of about
0.0256 Hectare, depicted as Lot ‘A’  in the Plan No. MA/YTT/2008/267
dated 30th July, 2009 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys -
Matale situated in the village called Yelakkaraya in Asgiri
Pallesiyapattuwa Minor Division  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of  Yatawatta in Matale District of the Central Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Dunkan Estate - Extent: About 0.0256 Hectare
North: Dunkan Estate claimed by the Land Reform Commission
and Dunkan Estate claimed by E. M. P. A. C. Ekanayake; East: Dunkan
Estate claimed by E. M. P. A. C. Ekanayake and Dunkan Estate
claimed by the Land Reform Commission; South: Dunkan Estate
claimed by the Land Reform Commission and Road; West: Road and
Dunkan Estate claimed by the Land Reform Commission.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
    Minister of Lands.
My  No.: LD/05/2008/PT/289.
Ministry of Lands,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Land Secretariat,
1200/6, Rajamalwatta Road,
