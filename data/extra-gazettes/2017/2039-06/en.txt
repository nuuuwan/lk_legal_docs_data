THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF  1971
Order under Section  11(1)b

Minister of Home Affairs, do, by this order declare that the days specified in the schedule hereto shall be an additional
Bank Holidays for the purpose of the Act, in addition to the days for the time being specified in the Second Schedule to
that Act.
