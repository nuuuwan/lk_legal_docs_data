NORTHERN    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   (STANDARD   BY-LAWS)   ACT,   No.   6   OF 1952
Approved Standard By Laws for Local Authorities in Northern Province
Standard Draft By-Laws Chapter I to VI made by me, C. V . Wigneswaran, as Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and
Planning, Law and Order, Land, Social Service, Rehabilitation, Women's Affairs, Electricity, Housing and Construction,
Industries and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local Government and Provincial Administration of Northern Province
Provincial Council by virtue of powers vested in the Minister under whose purview the subject of Local Government of
Provincial Council fall under the Sub section I of Section 2 of Local Authorities (Standard  By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952
read with paragraph (a) of Sub section 1 of Section 2 of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of
1989 are published hereby.
1. Standard By Laws of Municipal Councils in the Northern Province the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1952/14 of
2016.02.02.
2. Standard By Laws of Urban Councils in the Northern Province the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1952/15 of
2016.02.02.
3. Standard By Laws of Pradeshiya Sabhas in the Northern Province the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1952/16 of 2016.02.02.
It is hereby published that the above Standard By Laws for Municipal Councils, Urban Councils and Pradeshiya Sabhas in
the Northern Province has been submitted at the Northern Provincial Council and was approved by the Northern Provincial
Council on 21.02.2017.
