L.D.B 21/1978(x).
 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine Ltd, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 64641, do by this Order, amend the Schedule to the Order made
under the said Act, and published in Gazatte Extraordinary, No. 1960/4 of March 28, 2016 by the addition immediately
after the entry “Bachelor of Science in Engineering in Civil Engineering” appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding
entry in Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto,
subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE   I
C
ONDITIONS
South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine Ltd shall, when admitting students for the degree programme
specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Science Honours Bio Systems, adhere to the minimum entry
qualifications of three (03) ‘S’ passes in the Bio Science stream or Bio System Technology stream in one and the same
sitting at the GCE Advanced Level Examination or equivalent.
SCHEDULE  II
“Column I Column II
Degree Abbreviation of the Degree
Bachelor of Science Honours in Bio Systems BScHons (Bio Systems)
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th November 2017.

 UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Colombo International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka
under the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 17745, do by this Order, amend the Schedule
to the Order made under said Act, and published in Gazatte Extraordinary , No. 1979/21 of August 09, 2016 by the
addition immediately after the entry “Bachelor of Artis in English” appearing in Column I and it’s corresponding entry in
Column II thereof, of the entries specified under Column I and Column II respectively of the Schedule II hereto, subject
to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto :-
SCHEDULE  I
C
ONDITIONS
Colombo International Nautical and Engineering College (Private) Limited, shall, when admitting students for the degree
programme specified in Schedule II hereto in respect of the Bachelor of Management (Honours) in Retail Marketing and
Branding, and Bachelor of Management (Honours) in Supply Chain Management, adhere to the minimum entry qualifications
of three (03) ‘S’ passes in any subject stream  in one and the same sitting at the GCE Advanced Level Examination or
equivalent.
SCHEDULE   II
