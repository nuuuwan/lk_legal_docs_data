APPOINTMENTS  MADE  BY  THE  HON. GOVERNOR  OF  THE  SABARAGAMUW A  PROVINCE
IT is hereby notified that, I, Marshall Perera, Governor of the Sabaragamuwa Province, by virtue of powers vested in me under
section 154 C of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby appoint the Revenue Commissioner
and the Deputy Revenue Commissioners of the Sabaragamuwa Province to the following posts with effect from 02.01.2018,
under section 6:1 of the Business Names Statute No. 08 of 1990.
