P. S. No. : PS/HR/01/15/PS/Act
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has appointed, Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe, Secretary to the Cabinet of
Ministers to act in the post of Secretary to the President with effect from November 04, 2017 until Mr. Austin Fernando,
Secretary to the President return to the Island in terms of the provisions of Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
