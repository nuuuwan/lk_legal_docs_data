Order No. 48
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY   ACT,   No. 09  OF  2003
Order  under Section 20 (5)
ACTING  under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of  the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders  that no manufacturer, importer, packer, distributor or trader shall sell, expose or offer
for sale, display for sale the items listed below, above the maximum retail prices given hereunder.
Product Maximum Retail Price
Mysore Dhal Rs.130/-  per kg
Dried Fish - Katta Rs.1,000/-  per kg
Dried Fish - Salaya Rs.425/-  per kg
Coconut Rs.75/-  per nut
This Order shall come into effect from 06th December 2017.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order remove Mysore Dhal from Order No. 40 published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 2003/44 on 27.01.2017 and Dried Fish - Katta and Dried Fish - Salaya from Order No. 39 published in
Gazette Extraordinary No. 1975/68 on 14.07.2016
                                                                By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
H
ASITHA TILLEKERATNE,
Chairman.
Colombo,
06th December 2017.
