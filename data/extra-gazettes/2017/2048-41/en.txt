THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below are  needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
Four (04) allotments of land depicted in Advance Tracing No.
Maha/MHN/2017/136 and dated 20.06.2017 prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the Village of Katukele, Ward No. 4
(Bahirawakanda) (within Kandy Urban Council Limit) in
Gangawata Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale in Kandy
District  of Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot “A” – Assmt. No. 187, Sirimavo Bandaranaike
Mawatha – Extent : 0.0115 Hectare
North: Sirimavo Bandaranaike Mawatha and canal;East:Canal;
South: Lot B, Assmt. No.274/4 at S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike Mawatha
claimed by C.K.J.S. Karunarathne and some others and Lot No.
189 at Sirimavo Bandaranaike Mawatha claimed by Ajith Dhammika
Abeywickrama; West: Lot No. 189 at Sirimavo Bandaranaike
Mawatha claimed by Ajith Dhammika Abeywickrama and Sirimavo
Bandaranaike Mawatha.
02. Lot “B” – Assmt. No. 274/4,  S.W.R.D Bandaranaike
Mawatha – Extent: 0.0060 Hectare
North: Lot A and canal; East: Canal and Lot C; South: Lot C,
cement drain and balance portion of this land; West:  Lot No. 189
at Sirimavo Bandaranaike Mawatha claimed by Ajith Dhammika
Abeywickrama and Lot A.
03. Lot “C” –  Extent : 0.0003 Hectare
North: Lot B and canal; East: Canal and Lot D; South :  Lot D
claimant cannot be found land and cement drain; West: Cement
drain, Assmt. No. 274/4 at S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike Mawatha
claimed by C.K.J.S. Karunarathne and Lot B.
04. Lot “D” – Name of the land cannot be found – Extent:
0.0034 Hectare
North: Lot C and canal; East: Canal and Kandy Bus stand
(Good shade); South: Kandy Bus stand (Good shade) and balance
portion of this land; West: Balance portion of this land, cement
drain and Lot C.
