STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether  Mr. D. M. Chandrasena, who is working in the Sri Lanka Transport Board had been caused injustice by a complaint made only
against him regarding a collective duty done in the period from 1st May 2004 to 30 th September 2007, during which he had served as the
Assistant Manager - Finance of the Mahiyangana Depot, the disciplinary inquiry held unilaterally without considering his objections and the
punishments given by the disciplinary inquiry, and if such injustice had been caused, to what reliefs he is entitled.
                                                                                                                                                                     A. WIMALAWEERA,
Commissioner  General of Labour(Acting).
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner General of Labour,
Colombo, this 23rd day of August  2017.
