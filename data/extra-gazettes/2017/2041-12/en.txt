THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land described as Lot ‘A’ in total extent of about
1.845 Hectares, depicted in the Advanced Tracing
No. R/KLW/97/74 and dated 6th July 1999,  prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of  Gangalagamuwa
(V .P. 948) Uda Pattuwa Minor Division,  in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Kalawana in Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Nawinnahena Tippalahena – Extent: About
1.845 Hectares
North: Lot No. 857 and Nawinnahena claimed by H. Gunadasa
Lot No. 869; East: Nawinnahena claimed by H. Gunadasa Lot Nos.
869 and 873 2/2;  South: Lot No. 873 2/2, Nawinnahena  claimed by
H. Gunadasa Lot No.869; West: Nawinnahena claimed by H.
Gunadasa, Lot Nos. 869 and 857.
