THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the aforesaid Act, in relation to the Raja Rata  University
of  Sri Lanka,  published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 896/2  of   November 07, 1995 (as last amended by Order
published in Gazette  Extraordinary ,   No. 2025/ 38  of  June  30, 2017) by the  addition immediately after the  entry
“Faculty of  Medical and Allied Sciences’’  appearing in Column I and its  corresponding entries in Column II  thereof,  of
the following :-
“Column I Column II
 Faculty of  Technology 1. Department of   Bio - Process Technology
2. Dapartment of  Electrical and Electronic Technology

Technology
