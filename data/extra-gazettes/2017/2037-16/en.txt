THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this
Order, amend the Schedule to the Order  made under Section 21 of  the said Act,  published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 281/7  of January 24, 1984  establishing  the University of  Ruhuna,  Sri Lanka (as last amended by Order  published
in Gazette Extraordinary,   No. 1995/ 26 of  December 01, 2016) by the  addition immediately after the  entry  “Faculty
of  Technology’’  appearing in Column I and its  corresponding entries in Column II  thereof,  of the following :
“Column I Column II
Faculty Department
 Faculty of  Allied Health Sciences 1. Department of  Nursing
2. Dapartment of  Pharmacy
3. Department of  Medical Laboratory Sciences’’.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
14th September  2017.
