L.D.B. 277/40
THE   ANTIQUITIES   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER   188)
Notice  under Section  19
I,  Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, Minister of Education do hereby give notice that I intend to make an Order under section 18 of the
Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) declaring the Monuments specified in the schedule to this Notice , to be ''protected
Mounuments'' for the purpose of the aforesaid Ordinance.
Objections to the making of the aforesaid Order shall be forwarded to the Director General of Archaelogy, COlombo on or
before 07.04.2017.
Every such objection shall be in writing and shall contain a statement of the grounds upon which such objection is made.
 AKILA VIRAJ KARIYAWASAM,
Minister of  Education.
Colombo,
08th March,  2017.
