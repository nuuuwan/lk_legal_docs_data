THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL   ACT,  No.  47  OF 1980
Order under Section 23 W
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 23 W of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 read with
Section 51 of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Order prohibit the activities specified in Schedule hereto.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
30th August, 2017.
SCHEDULE
Activities :-
(i) the manufacture of food wrappers from polythene as a raw material for in country use; and
(ii) the sale, offer for sale, offer free of charge, exhibition or use of food wrappers manufactured from polythene
as a raw material within the country.
For the purposes of this Order-
‘‘food wrappers’’ means lunch sheets.
‘‘Polythene” includes high density polyethylene, low density polyethylene and polypropylene.
