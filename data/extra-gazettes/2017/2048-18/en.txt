LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
IT is hereby notified that the name of Mr. Pulasthi Serasinghe Pathirana who was appointed as the Returning Officer of all
Local Authorities of the administrative district of Badulla by the Gazette Extraordinary No. 2046/86 dated Saturday the
25th November 2017 issued by the Election Commission under the provisions of Section 27(1) of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) is hereby deleted and the name of Mr. H. I. R. Hathurusinghe, the Assistant Commissioner
of Elections of the said district is substituted therefor.
