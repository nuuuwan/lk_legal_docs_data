LAND  REFORM  COMMISSION   ACT, No.  1  OF 1972 OF   THE   NATIONAL  STATE   ASSEMBLY
Order under Sub-section (4) of Section 27a
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amaratunga, the Minister of Lands, by this Order made in terms of powers vested in me by
Sub-section (4) of Section 27A of Land Reform Act, No. 1 of 1972 of the National State Assembly as amended by Land
Reforms (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 to be read together with Sub-article (2) of the Article 44 of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
(a) do hereby amend that rule by revoking them due to establishment of an Industrial and Technological Development
Super Zone in the part pertaining to the extent of land called Newchattle Watta specified in Schedule two
hereto, which is part of the land called and mentioned Newchattle Watta which has been transferred to State
Plantation Corporation by State Agricultural Corporation Act, No. 11 of 1972 and transferred under Sub-section
(1) of Section 27A of the said Land Reform Commission as published in the Gazette No. 815/10 on 21st April,
1994 relevant to the lands called and mentioned as Newchattle Watta as detailed in the first Schedule hereto.
(b) do hereby state that, following this revocation, the said extent of land explained in detail in the Second Schedule
hereto is conveyed back to Land Reforms Commission.
J
OHN ANTHONY  E MMANUEL  AMARATUNGA,
Minister of Lands.
Ministry of Lands,
Mihikatha Medura,
1200/6, Rajamalwaththa Road,
Battaramulla.
03rd May, 2017.
