EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification
IT is hereby notified that the following Order issued by Honourable Minister of Finance, by virtue of the power vested in
him by Section 32 read with Section 22 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as amended from time to time, and published
in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1981/87 of 26.08.2016 to impose an Excise duty per litre of imported foreign liquor
as follows with effect from 01.09.2016, was passed by Parliament on 10.12.2016.
(i) Foreign Malt Liquor Rs. 50.00
(ii) Foreign Wine Rs. 100.00
(iii) Any other Foreign Liquor Rs. 200.00
(which are not included in (i) and (ii) above)
R. H. S. S
AMARATUNGA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
07th March 2017.
RULES
1. A person who wishes to import liquor shall submit an application form set out in accordance with the Schedule
I to the Commissioner General of Excise.
2. If the Commissioner General of Excise is satisfied about the application forwarded by the applicant, the
approval shall be sent to the Controller of Imports and Exports with the form set out in accordance with the Schedule II.
3. If Commissioner General of Excise is not satisfied about the application, such Commissioner shall inform the
applicant, reason for rejection within five days of the receipt of the application.

4. After the receipt of foreign liquor by the Department of Customs, the Director General of Customs shall issue

Excise.
5. The Commissioner General of Excise shall issue a license for import and a pass for transport of foreign liquor
in the form set out in accordance with Schedule IV and V , upon payment of excise duty.
6. The import license issued by the Commissioner General of Excise shall be produced for inspection when
required by an Excise officer of the licensed premises.
