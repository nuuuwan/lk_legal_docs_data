No. 2046/2  –  MONDAY NOVEMBER 20, 2017 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My  Reference No.: KDS/PAN/04/04/02/10. 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 13/2000/FR/63. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub - section  (1) of Section  
7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intend to acquire the following Land/
Servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land  or Servitude to be acquired : 
 
Lot  01 of land called “Ketaloya Watta” in total extent 
of about 20.3065 Hectares, depicted in PP Plan No. 6682 
dated 24.05.2017 of prepared by the Surveyor General 
Situated in the Village Beddegama, Palispattu West 
Korale in the Divisionl Secretary’s Division of Panwila in 
Kandy District, Central Province and Bounded as follow: 
 
North: Ketaloya Watta titled by H. T. P. Gunathilaka 
 (H.P. 49972) and Peragolla Oya 
 
East: Peragolla Oya and Lot No. 01 of P.P. Maha 6137 
 
South: Lot No. 01 of P.P. Maha 6137 and Ketaloya 
 Watta titled by S. K. Gunathilaka (H.P. 49972) 
West:  Ketaloya Watta titled by S. K. Gunathilaka  
 (H. P.  49972) and Ketaloya Watta titled by  
 H. T. P. Gunatilake (H. P. 49972) 
 
Name and address of the Claimant : H. G. M. 
Gunatilake, No. 11, D. S. Senanayake Street, Kandy. 
 
  
All Persons interest in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at   Divisional Secretariat Panwila on the 22nd 
day of December, 2017 at 9.30 a.m. and  are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 6th day of December, 2017 the nature of their 
interest in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude, 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 
 
  J. P. U. K. JAYARATHNE, 
                      Divisional Secretary, 
  Panwila Division. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Panwila, 
15th November, 2017.
