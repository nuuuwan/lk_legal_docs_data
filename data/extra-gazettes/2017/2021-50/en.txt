No. 2021/50 – FRIDAY, JUNE 02, 2017
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, John Anthony Emmanuel Amarathunga, Minister of Lands hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition
Act, that the land described below is needed for a public purpose and
will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called “Dawatagahawatta” in total extent of
about 0.0489 Hectare depicted as Lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No.
Gam/Gam/2016/159 dated 17.08.2016, prepared by the Surveyor-
General situated in the Village of Bendiyamulla in Medapattuwa
Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Gampaha
in the District of Gampaha in Western Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dawatagahawatta – Extent: 0.0489 Hectare
North: Land claimed by the State of which the name of land could
not be traced; East: Land claimed by the State of which the name of
land could not be traced and Land claimed by Asoka Aberathne of
which the name of land could not be traced; South: Road; West: Land
claimed by the State of which the name of land could not be traced.
John A. E. Amarathunga,
Minister of Lands.
