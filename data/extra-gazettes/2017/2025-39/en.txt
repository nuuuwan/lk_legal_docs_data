THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me  by section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the Minister
of Higher Education and Highways,  on the Recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order, amend
the Schedule to the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1448/27 of June 09, 2006 (as last amended by Order
published in  Gazette Extraordinary No. 2005/14 of February 08, 2017), which specifies  the Faculties and the Departments
of the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, which is deemed,  by virtue of the provisions of section 139 of the  aforesaid Act, to
be a University established under section 21 of the said Act, by the addition immediately after the entry " 10. Department of
Buddhist Studies" appearing in Column II of the Schedule which corresponds to the item " Faculty of Arts" appearing in
Column I of the Schedule of the following entry:-
"11. Department of English Language Teaching".
LAKSHMAN  KIRIELLA,
Minister of  Higher Education and Highways.
Colombo,
28th  June, 2017.
