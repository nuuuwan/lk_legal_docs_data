EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: Bal 4/2/1. 
 
 
 
I hereby given notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired : 
 
Land called Walikkanwatta Extent about 01 R.04.0 P., 
depicted as Lot 01 in Surveyor General’s Advanced 
Tracing Number P.P.G. 1097 and 25.05.1975 situated in  
20-B Brakmanawatta Grama Niladaris Division in 
Balapitiya Divisional Secretary’s Division in Galle 
District. 
 
 
01. Lot No. 01 —  Walikkanwatta —  Extent 01 R.  
04.0 P.  
 
North: Road and P.P.A. 1664 Lot No. 01; East: 
Walikkanwatta claimed by G.O.T. De Silva and Main 
Road; South: Bogahawatta claimed by Dilman Abesekara 
and Walikkanwatta claimed by G.O.T. De Silva; West: 
Dachchigedara  Kuruduwatta claimed by L. Adlin and     
K. H. P. Sugathadasa. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Balapitiya on 
14th of September, 2017 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before 07th of September, 2017 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 B. P. S. ANURADHI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Balapitiya. 
 
 
At Divisional Secretariat, 
Balapitiaya, 
17th June, 2017.
