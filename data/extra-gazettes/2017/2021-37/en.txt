THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
Reference No.: Ma/San/03/229.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
     Description of the lands to be acquired:
Allotments of land in extent of about 0.0270 Hectare,
depicted as lot Nos. 01-03 in the dated 30.01.2017 in the
Preliminary Plan No. Gam. 3985 was prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the village of Ihala Karagahamuna, in the
Grama Niladari Division Ihala Karagahamuna North (248), in
the Minor Division of Adikari Paththuwa, in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Mahara, in the Gampaha District in
Western Province.
      01. Lot No. 1
Name of Land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden, 3
Coconut trees about 10 years old; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: P . V . J. Perera, No. 110, Mangala Mawatha, Ihala
Karagahamuna, Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0081 Hectare.
      02. Lot No. 2
Name of Land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Not known; Extent: 0.0099 Hectare.
      03. Lot No. 3
Name of Land: Millagahawatta; Description: Garden, 3
Coconut trees about 10 years old and One Mango tree;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: P . V . S. J. Perera, No. 106,
Mangala Mawatha, Ihala Karagahamuna, Kadawatha; Extent:
0.0090 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby required
to appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing,
before me at Divisional Secretary's Office of Mahara on the
05.07.2017 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to
me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 28.06.2017 the
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars or their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said land
the amount of compensation and the details of the computation
of such amount.
G. S. I. WIMALASENA,
Divisional Secretary,
Mahara.
Divisional Secretary's Office,
Mahara,
31st May, 2017.
