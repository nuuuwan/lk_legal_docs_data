THE  NATIONAL  ENVIRONMENTAL   ACT,  No.  47  OF 1980
Order under Section 23 W
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 23 W of the National Environmental Act, No. 47 of 1980 read with
Section 51of the Nineteenth Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Order prohibit the  activities specified in Schedule I hereto and exempt the items
specified in Schedule  II hereto.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Colombo,
30th August, 2017.
SCHEDULE   I
Activities :-
(i) manufacture of any bag of  high density  polyethylene as  a  raw material  for in  country use;  and
(ii) sale, offer  for sale, offer free of  charge, exhibition or use of  any bag manufactured from high density
polyethylene as a raw material within the country.
For the purposes of this Schedule bags  manufactured   from high density polyethylene as a  raw  material, means
bags of any dimension with or without handles for the purpose of carrying products or goods and includes grocery bags
generally referred to as ‘‘sili - sili bags’’.
