ANTIQUITIES   ORDINANCE
Order
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sub - section 39 ‘a’ of the Antiquities Ordinance (Chapter 188) as amended  by the Act
No. 24 of 1998, I, Minister of Education, do by this Order, appoint the members referred to in the Schedule here to the Advisory
Committee on Archaeology as effective for a period the 01st of December 2017 to 01st of December 2019.
AKILA VIRAJ KARIYAWASAM,
Minister of Education.
Ministry of Education,
01st December, 2017.
