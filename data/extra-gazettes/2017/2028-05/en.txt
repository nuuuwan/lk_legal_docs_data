Notification
 WAGES   BOARD   FOR   THE   PLUMBAGO TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour, Trade Union Relations and Sabaragamuwa Development has been pleased to appoint the
following persons to be members of the Wages Board for the Plumbago Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon.
