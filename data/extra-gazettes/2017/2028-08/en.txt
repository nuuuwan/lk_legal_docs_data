No. 867/1 of 2017
INDUSTRIAL   DISPUTES   ACT   (CHAPTER   131)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 22 of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative
Enactments of Ceylon (Revised Edition 1956) as amended by the Industrial Disputes (Amendments) Act, Nos. 14 and 62
of 1957 and No. 4 of 1962 [read with Industrial Disputes (Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1968] I, Maithripala Sirisena,
President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby–
(1) Appoint the persons specified in the Schedule hereto be members of the panel of which Industrial Courts shall
be constituted, and
(2) Specify the period of three years from 13th June, 2017 as the period of office of such members.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
17th July 2017.
