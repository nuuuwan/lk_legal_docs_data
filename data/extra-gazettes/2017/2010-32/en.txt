THE  URBAN   DEVELOPMENT  PROJECTS  (SPECIAL   PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  2  OF  1980
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Urban Development Projects (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2 of
1980, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Repubic of Sri Lanka upon the recommendation of
the Minister-in-Charge of the subject of Urban Development being of the opinion that the land specified in the Schedule
below is urgently required for the construction of the commercial complex under the Town Development Project of
Malabe, do by this order, declare that the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA S IRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
13th March, 2017.
SCHEDULE
All allotments of land called Dombagahawatta depicted as Lot B1 of Plan No. 2058 made by Anil Nawagamuwa,
Licensed Surveyor, in respect of land situated in Talangama North in Pallepattuwa Hewagam Korale within the area of
authority of Kaduwela Municipal Council in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kaduwela in the District of Colombo
in Western Province containing in extent of about 03 roods and 17.50 perches (0.3478 Hectares) of which boundaries are
as  follows :
North : Bus stand premises of Malabe ;
East : Bus stand premises of Malabe and land belonging to S and G Property Developers ;
South : Udawatte Road ;
West : Kaduwela Divisional Secretariat Building.
