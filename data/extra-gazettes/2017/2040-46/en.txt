THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Gayantha Karunatilake, Minister of Lands and Parliamentary
Reforms, hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for a
public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land shown as Lot 'A' depicted in  the Advanced
Tracing No. R/ KRW/2014/130 dated  27.04.2014 and  prepared by
the Senior Superintendent of Surveys Ratnapura for the Village of
Thembiliyana in the Uda Pattu South Minor Division of Kuruwita
Korale in the  Divisional Secretary's  Division of Kuruwita in
Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Paradise Estate – Extent:  1.5912 Hectares
North: Road and Mahadeniya; East: Mahadeniya and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); South: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); West: Road.
