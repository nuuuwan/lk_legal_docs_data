THE STATE AGRICULTURAL CORPORATIONS ACT, NO.11 OF 1972
Order under Section 2
I, Gardihewa Sarath Chandralal Fonseka, Minister of Regional Development do by this Order with the concurrence of the Minister to whom
the subject of Finance is assigned and the Minister to whom the subject of National Policies is assigned, amend the Order made by me under
Section 2 of  the State Agricultural Corporations Act, No. 11 of 1972 and published  in Gazette Extraordinary No. 302/12 of February 1,
1978 as follows:-
1. By the repeal of paragraph (5) and the substitution therefore of the following:-
"(5) state that the principal place of  business of the Authority shall be Colombo;"
2. By the repeal of paragraphs (7) and (8).
3. By the substitution for the words "FIRST SCHEDULE" of the word "Schedule";
4. In item (1) of the Schedule, by the substitution for the words " government or non- government agencies in such areas that may
be specified  by the Minister, from time to time;" of the words "government or non-government agencies;";
5. In item (5)  of the Schedule, by the substitution for the words and figure "specified in paragraph (1)." of the words and fi gure
"specified in paragraph (1);"
6. Immediately after item (5) of the Schedule, by the insertion of the following new items:-
"(6) to encourage, promote and assist both local and foreign agro -industry investments in commercial farming or any other
agricultural enterprise;
(7) to formulate and execute plans, deliver services and provide expertise to establish and manage such agro-industry
investments undertaken by non-government agencies, private sector of farmers either in partnership with the
Authority or without the Authority.".

8. In this Order-
(a) "agro - industry" means any enterprise or institution delivering material inputs to the farming sector
 and transforming,  distributing  or otherwise adding value to agricultural raw material targeting  both
 local and international market demands;
(b) "Commercial farming" includes crop farming, livestock farming and horticulture, floriculture  and sericulture
targeting the market demands; and
(c) "other agricultural enterprises" includes tissue culture, manufacturing of organic fertilizer, processing of food and
edible oil, pharmaceutical manufacturing using medicinal crops, agro tourism, manufacturing of agricultural
    machinery and other ancillary equipment and such other industries based on agriculture, horiculture and plantation
   crops.
