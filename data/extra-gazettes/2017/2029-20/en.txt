1A  G  25964— 08  (07/2017)
PENSION   MINUTE
Notification under the Section 51 of the Pension Minute dated 05th February, 1934, amended by the Pension
Minute (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1948
NOTIFICATION
THE interim Section 2 and 17 of the Pension Minute as amended from time to time  is hereby further amended by the
insertion immediately after aforesaid section of the following Sub section.
“Compulsory age limit of retirement of the officers in the Sri Lanka Engineering Service shall be extended up to
61 years.”
The amendment made to the Pension Minute by this Notification deemed as coming into force with effect from
07.02.2017.
