Government intends to acquire the following land/

Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Following Lots of Sheet No. 16 of F.V.P. 359 situated 
in the village of Millawitiya in North Uda Paththuwa in

Kuruwita of Rathnapura District in Sabaragamuwa 
Province.  
 
01. Lot No. 422  
Name of Land: Malebeddahena; Previous 
Claimant: T. R. Ranjan Keerthi; Present Claimant: 
Government; Extent: 0.0034 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 423  
Name of Land: Malebeddahena; Previous 
Claimant: Land Reforms Commission; Present 
Claimant: Government; Extent: 4.3900 Hectare.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Divisional Secretariat office of 
Kuruwita on the 06th day of July in 2015 at 9.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 29th day of June in 2015 of 
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 Divisional Secretary,
