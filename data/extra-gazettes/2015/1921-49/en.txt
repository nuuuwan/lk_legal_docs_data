AWARD
The Honourable Minister of Labour and Labour Relations
by virtue of the powers vested in him under Section 4(1) of
the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative
Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition) as amended by
Acts No. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial
Disputes (Special Provisions) Act No. 37 of 1968 appointed
me as the Arbitrator by his order dated 04.08.2014 and
referred the dispute between the aforesaid parties for
settlement by Arbitration.
Appearances:
Mr. H. A. G . Michael Trade Union Representative of Sri
Lanka Nidahas Jathika Pravahana Sewaka Sangamaya appears
for the Workman.

This matter was settled today (22.04.2015). The
Workman was paid the sum of Rs. 17448/- as cost of Living
due to him under the  Finance Division circular No. 01 of
2009 dated 10.04.2009. He accepted the cheque People’s
Bank Union Place, Colombo 2. cheque No. 005600 for the
amount Rs. 17448/- in full settlement of all claims in
respect of the dispute referred to me for arbitration. Both
Parties signed the case record.
I consider this settlement just and equitable and make
award accordingly.
