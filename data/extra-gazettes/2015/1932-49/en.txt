PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE
EASTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recongized political party,  to which the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill
such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Ibra Lebbe Mohamed
Mahir to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Ibra Lebbe Mohamed Mahir, elected as a
member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Ampara in respect of  which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
15th  September, 2015.
