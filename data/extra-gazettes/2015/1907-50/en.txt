MUNICIPAL  COUNCILS  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 10 (2) (b)
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Paragraph (b) of Sub-section (2) of the Section 10 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 252), I, Karu Jayasuriya, Minister of Public Administration, Local Government and Democratic Governance, do by
this Order extend the term of office of the members of each Municipal Council specified in the Schedule hereto, up to
15th May 2015.
KARU  JAYASURIY A,
Minister of Public Administration,
Local Government and Democratic Governance.
Colombo,
26th March 2015.

1. Dambulla Municipal Council
2. Bandarawela Municipal Council
