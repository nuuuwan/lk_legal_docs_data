  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  Uva   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Uva Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Uva
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Hewa Waduge Roy Kalinda
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Hewa Waduge Roy Kalinda, elected as a member of the
above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Moneragala  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09th September, 2015.

 PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  North Central  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated Mr.  Samarasingha Arachchilage
Janaka Priyantha Samarasingha  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Samarasingha Arachchilage Janaka Priyantha
Samarasingha, elected as a member of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Polonnaruwa  in
respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
09th September, 2015.
