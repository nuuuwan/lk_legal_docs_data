ORDER  UNDER  SECTION  10 (1) (a) AND 11 (1) (a)
OF  THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No.  29  OF  1971
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 10 (1) (a) and 11 (1) (a) of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, M. Joseph
Michael Perera, Minister of Home Affairs and Fisheries, do by this order declare Wednesday, 14th January, 2015 a special
Public and Bank Holiday of the State visit of H. H. Pope Francis to Sri Lanka.
M. J
OSEPH MICHAEL PERERA,
