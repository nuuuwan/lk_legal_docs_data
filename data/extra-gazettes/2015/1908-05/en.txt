EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
RATNAPURA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Ratnapura  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  People’s Freedom Alliance,  to which
the  former  member, Abeyratna Victor Kapil Wickramanath  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as
a member of  that Ratnapura   Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to  make a nomination within the
period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Matara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansha Perera, Returning Officer of  Ratnapura  Municipal
Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of V acancies in
Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Gangulwitiya Gamage Hemantha
Sudesh Gamage who has secured the  highest  number of  preferences next to the last of  the  member declared elected at the
election held on 08th of October  2011 to Ratnapura Municipal Council elected as a member of the Ratnapura Municipal
Council in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M. A. P. C.  P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Ratnapura  Municipal Council.
District   Elections Office,
Ratnapura
27th   March,  2015.
