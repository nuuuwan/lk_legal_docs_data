THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
Boundaries for the allotments of land containing in extent of 5.1345
Hectares depicted in Advance Tracing  No.R/ KRE/ 2013/ 375 dated
27.09.2013 prepared by Senior Superintendent of  Surveys for the
Village called Yatipauwa, in the Meda Pattu Minor Division of
Kuruwiti Korale in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Kiriella,
Ratnapura District, Sabaragamuwa Province:
01. Lot ‘A’ - Ellakanda Watta - Extent :  0.7181 Hectare
North: Road (P.S.) and Palletenna Dola; East: Palletenna Dola and
Lot C; South: Lot C and Road (P.S.); West: Road (P.S.) and Lot B.
02. Lot ‘B’ - Ellakanda Watta - Extent :  0.0245 Hectare
North and East: Lot A; South: Lot A and Road (P.S.); West: Road
(P .S.).
03. Lot ‘C’ -  Access Road - Extent :  0.1745 Hectare.
04. Lot ‘D’ - Ellakanda Watta - Extent :  0.4693 Hectare
North: Lot C and Palletenna Dola; East: Palletenna Dola and Lot
C; South and West:  Lot C.

and F.

North: Lot C; East: Lot C and Ellakanda Watta; South: Ellakanda
Watta and Road (P .S.); West: Road (P .S.) and Lot C.
