No. 1919/37 – TUESDAY, JUNE 16, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Allotments of below mentioned lands, depicted in the Tracing
No. Gam/Maha/ 2013/367 of 11.11.2013, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Udupila in the Minor Division of
Adhikari Pattuwa Siyane Korale (Western) in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Mahara in Gampaha District in Western
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kataliya Kumbura – Extent: 0.0099 Hectare
North: Balance portion of same land and Canal; East:
Balance portion of same land, Canal, Lot B (Canal), Canal and
Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and
balance portion of same land.
02. Lot ‘C’ – Kataliya Kumbura – Extent: 0.0016 Hectare
North: Canal and balance portion of same land; East: Balance
portion of same land and Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and
Canal; West: Road (R.D.A.), Canal, Lot B (Canal) and balance portion
of same land.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
