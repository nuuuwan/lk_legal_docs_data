EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460), 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT 
(AMENDMENT) No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
 Reference Number of the Ministry of Lands 
 and Land Development: LD/05/2009/E/30. 
 
 My Number: BD/BD/LND/03/05/12. 
 
I hereby give notice to the public under Sub-section (1) of 
the Section (7) of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended 
by the Land Acquisition Act (amendment) No. 28 of 1964 
the Land/servitude described below is required by the 
government and the Government intends to acquire the 
said land for public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land/servitude to be acquired : 
 
Two blocks of lands of 0.2889 Hectare in extent 
stated as block No. 03 and 04, in the preliminary plan of 
No. B/673 dated 10.02.2015, prepared by the Senior 
Superintendant of Survey, situated in Malwatte (within 
Badulla Municipality) coming under 78 J Badulupitiya 
Grama Niladhari Division, of Divisional Number 8, in 
Badulla Secretary Division of Badulla District. 
 
Acquiring of the Lands for Advance Institute of 
Technological Education Schedule of the Land

All those who claim ownership, interest and any other 
connection to aforesaid land, should appear personally, or 
by agents duly authorized in writing before me at 
Divisional Secretariat, Badulla on 12th June, 2015 at 2.30 
p.m. In addition, hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing with two copies of the nature of their interests in 
the land/lands, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation of such amount before 10th 
June, 2015.
