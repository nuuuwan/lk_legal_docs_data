FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
NEGOMBO   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Negombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of being elected as a
member of the Western Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to
which the former member, M. S. M. Sakawulla belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member
of that Negombo Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Jayasingha
Arachchige Don Sanath Anil Prasanna being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Negombo Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Jayasingha Arachchige
Don Sanath Anil Prasanna elected as a member of the Negombo Municipal Council.
P. A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Negombo Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Gampaha,
18th  November, 2015.
