THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0303 Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KE/KGL/2014/114 dated
25th September 2014, prepared by the Surveyor-General  situated
in the  Village of Pitihuma (Part) Ward No. 8, Pitihuma within
boundaries of  Kegalle Urban Council in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Kegalle in Kegalle  District in  Sabaragamuwa Province
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  – Radagolahala Kumbura  – Extent: 0.0303 Hectare
North: Radagolahala Kumbura; East: Radagolahala Watta and
Bandaranayaka Mawatha; South: Bandaranayaka Mawatha; West:
Radagolahala Kumbura.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.

THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Three (03) allotments of lands in total extent of about
0.0269 Hectare,  depicted  in the Advance Tracing No. Gam/Maha/
2015/104 dated 12.06.2015 prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Ihala Karagahamuna, Adikari Paththuwa
Minor Division in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Mahara in
the District of Gampah  in the Western  Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  – Millagahawatta  – Extent: About 0.0081 Hectare
North: Balance portion of this land and Mangala Mawatha; East:
Lot B (Road); South: Lot B (Road), Road and Millagahawatta
claimed by Road Development Authority; West: Balance portion of
same land.
02. Lot ‘B’  –  Extent,  About: 0.0099 Hectare
North: Lot A and  Mangala Mawatha; East: Lot C; South: Lot C,
Millagahawatta claimed by Road Development Authority and Road;
West: Millagahawatta claimed by Road Development Authority and
Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’  – Millagahawatta  – Extent: 0.0089 Hectare
North: Lot B and  Mangala Mawatha; East: Balance portion of
same land; South: Balance portion of same land and Millagahawatta
claimed by Road Development Authority; West: Road and Lot B
(Road).
