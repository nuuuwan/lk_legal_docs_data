THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My Reference No.: Anu/13/5/1/9/1. 
Land & Land Ministry Reference No. LD/05/2008/
HW/207. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land/servitude* which is required 
for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land  or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Land in extent of about 0.0311 Hectare, shown in 
Preliminary Plan and dated 08.12.2014 which appears as 
Final Village Plan No. 302, Supplement No. 13, Sheet 
No. 19 in the Preliminary Plan No. Anu/Me/4/RDA/TLW 
- Final Village Plan No. 302, Supplement No. 22, Sheet 
No. 28 and dated 19.10.2011, prepared by the 
Superintendent of Surveyor, situated in the villages  of 
Ihala Thalawa, Moragoda and Minor Division of 
Nuwaragama Palatha in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Thalawa, in Anuradhapura District.  
 
 
01. Lot No.: F.V.F. 302/900  
Name of the Land: Road Reservation; Claimant: 
State. 
 
02. Lot No.: F.V.F. 302/936  
Name of the Land: Road (Road Development 
Authority); Claimant: State. 
 
03. Lot No.: F.V.F. 302/15G  
Name of the Land: Road (Road Development 
Authority); Claimant: State. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands*  on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, Thalawa of 
the 27th April, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requiested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 20th April, 2015, the nature  of their interests 
in the land, the particul ars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the  details 
of the computation of such amount. 
 
 S ANDYA N. G. ABEYSEKARA,
