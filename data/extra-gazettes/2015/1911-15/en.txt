THE   UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Subsection (4) of   Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Subsection (4) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Sarath
Amunugama, Minister of Higher Education and Research, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
Trincomalee Campus of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously distrupted and that the authorities of the
Trincomalee Campus of the University have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order appoint Professor Ranjith
Premalal de Silva to be the Competent Authority with effect from April 15, 2015, for the purpose of exercising, performing or
discharging in lieu of Rector of Trincomalee Campus any powers, duties and functions respectively which have been
conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the said Rector of the Trincomalee Campus, Eastern University, Sri Lanka under the
said Act or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
S
ARATH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
21st  April, 2015.
