I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
Description of the Land  to be acquired: 
 
The allotment of land called “Keenalan watta” in total 
extent of about 0.2266 Hectare, depicted as Lot No. 312 
in Town Survey Preliminary Plan No. 11, Suppliment  
No. 22, Sheet No. 25, dated 28.11.2014, surveyed  and 
prepared by the Surveyor General, situated in the village 
of Ella (Town) in Kumbalwela Korale Minor Division in 
the Divisioal Secretary’s Division of Ella in Badulla 
District, Uva Province and bounded as follows. 
 
Lot No. 312 
Name of the Land: Keenalan watta; Extent of the 
Land: 0.2266 Hectare. 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North:Lot No. 310, 309 and Lot No.88; East:Lot 
No.88: South:Lot No. 88; West: Lot No. 88, 311 and Lot 
No. 310. 
 
Names and Address of the claimants: 1. Namunukula 
Plantation Company, No. 310, Highlevel Road, Navinna, 
Maharagama. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretary’s Office, at Ella on the 18th day of March 2015 
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of  March 
2015, the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
   
                                                            SAMANTHI RANASINGHE, 
                Divisional Secretary, 
                               Ella.
