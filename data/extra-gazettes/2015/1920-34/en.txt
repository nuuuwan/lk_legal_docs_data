THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, , Minister of Lands, hereby declare under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An Allotment of Land called Mahawatta in total extent of about
0.1034 Hectare, described in the Advance Tracing No.: Ma/PLL/
2013/360 and dated 24.04.2014, prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Moragaspitiya (F.V.P 33) in Udugoda
Udasiya Pattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Pallepola in Matale District, Central Province and
bounded as  follows:
01. Lot "A" –   Mahawatta  – Extent: About 0.1034 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of land and Homapola Village; East:
Homapola Village (F.V .P. 42); South: Remaining portion of land and
Road (P .S.);West:Road (P.S.) and remaining portion of land.
