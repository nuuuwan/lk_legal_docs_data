FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE OF   A   MEMBER
OF THE  PACHCHILAIPALLI    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   a member in  Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of   being ceased to be
a member of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front, the recognized political party   to which   the
Member Subramaniam Suren   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) as amended by the Local Authorities
Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2007, to nominate a person eligible under the ordinance to appoint as a member of  that
Pachchilaipalli of  Pradeshiya Sabha  within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Mariyampillai Jesuthasan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE   I,  P. Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha , acting in terms of
Section 65A(2) (b) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Mariyampillai Jesuthasan   elected as member
of  the  Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
30th  March, 2015.
