PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.  48   OF   2007
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Finance Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect of the commodity items
specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in
Column II of that Schedule. This order shall be valid for a period of 04 months commencing from 22nd January 2015, subject to
provisions of Sub-section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1891/8 of  02.12.2014 in respect of Rice (HS. Code No. 10.06) is rescinded with effect from 22nd January 2015.
R
AV I  KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of  Finance,
Colombo 01,
22nd January, 2015.
