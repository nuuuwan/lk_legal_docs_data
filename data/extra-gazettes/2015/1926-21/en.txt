THE   CUSTOMS   ORDINANCE
Customs Notification No. 1/2015 - Bonded Warehouses
IN pursuance of the power vested in me by Section 69 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), I, Ranasinghege
Semasinghe, Director General of Customs, do, with the approval of the Minister of Finance granted by virtue of the
powers vested in him by that Section, by this notice published in accordance with his direction approve and appoint
warehouses and places of security specified under Column 03 of the Schedule annexed hereto, as Private bonds, General
bonds, Duty Free shops, feeder bonds and Service bonds in which the goods specified under Column 06 of the said
Schedule imported by the owners of such warehouses and places of security may be warehoused, kept and secured without
payment of duty on the first entry thereof.
R. S
EMASINGHE,
Director General of Customs.
Industries and Services Directorate,
Sri Lanka Customs Department,
1st Floor, Customs House,
No. 40, Main Street,
Colombo 11,
3rd August, 2015.
