The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
L.D.B. 21/1978 (XII).
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 27(1)
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Kabir Hashim, Minister of
Highways, Higher Education and Investment Promotion, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants
Commission, amend the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act, establishing the South Eastern University of Sri
Lanka and published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 916/7 of March 27, 1996, as amended from time to time, by the addition
immediately after item (c) in Column II under the heading ‘‘Department of Study’’ which correspond to the entry ‘‘Faculty of
Arts and Culture’’ in Column I under the heading ‘‘Faculty’’ in the Schedule II to such Order, of the following new item :–
‘‘(d) Department of Political Science’’
KABIR  HASHIM,
Minister of Highways, Higher Education and Investment Promotion.
Colombo,
04th March, 2015.
