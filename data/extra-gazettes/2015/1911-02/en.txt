EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)    ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that, the Excise Duty on Hybrid Motor Cars specified in Column II of the
Schedule hereto, corresponding to the article No. 05 specified in Column II of the Schedule II of the Extraordinary Gazette
Notification No. 1903/39 dated 26.02.2015 issued under the Section 3 of the said Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in
the corresponding entry in Column III of the Schedule hereto with effect from 01.04.2015.
R
AV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
20th  April, 2015.
