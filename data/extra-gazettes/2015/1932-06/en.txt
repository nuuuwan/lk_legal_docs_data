PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  the Northern   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Northern  Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Northern   Province
Provincial Council by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recongized political party,  to which the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill
such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Kanapathippillai
Tharumalingam to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Council Elections Act, No. 02 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Kanapathippillai Tharumalingam, elected as a
member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Jaffna in respect of the which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
14th  September, 2015.

PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  the  Uva  Province Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Uva Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Uva Province Provincial
Council by reason of being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recongized political party,  to
which the member belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person
to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated Mr. Arthur Chamara
Sampath Dasanayake  Don to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 02 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Arthur Chamara Sampath Dasanayake Don,
elected as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Badulla in respect of the which
such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
