TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “Wish Foundation Trust” namely-
1. Jayantha Mihira Wickramarachchi,
2. Taniya Rasangi Wickramarachchi
3. Eranthi Lisa Wickramarachchi
4. Dhammika Kitulgoda
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87),
NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe,
Minister of Justice do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of  Trust bearing No. 264 of April 4, 2012 and attested by
R. W. M. P. Rajakaruna,  Notary Public of Colombo shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession
and a common seal under the name and style of “Wish Foundation Trust” ;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Trust  ; and
(c) the Trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and to be sued, to answer
and to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in  causes and
actions arising in relation to the said Trust.
The Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
06th November 2015.
