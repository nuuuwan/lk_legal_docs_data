THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of land  about 1.7230 Hectare, in  extent depicted
as Lots ‘A’ to ‘F’ in Advance Tracing No. KE/RMB/2011/188 and
dated 28.12.2011, prepared by the Surveyor-General, situated in the
Village of Diyasunnatha, in the Minor Division of Kinigoda Korale
in the   Divisional Secretary’s Division of Rambukkana in the District
of Kegalle, in Sabaragamuwa Province and boundaries of Lots 6 are
given below:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Damunukumbura – Extent: 0.0537 Hectare
North: Damunukumbura and Stream; East: Stream and Lot B;
South: Lot B; West: Lot B and Damunukumbura.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Damunukumbura – Extent:  0.8395 Hectare
North: Damunukumbura, Lot A and Stream; East: Stream and
Lot C; South: Lot C, Damunukumbura, Lots D and E; West: Lot E
and Fair Land.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Damunukumbura – Extent:  0.0557 Hectare
North: Lot B and Stream; East: Stream and Road; South:
Damunukumbura; West: Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Damunukumbura – Extent: 0.5513 Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Lot B and Damunukumbura; South:
Damunukumbura, Lots F and E; West: Lots E and B.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Damunukumbura – Extent: 0.2075 Hectare
North: Fair Land and Lot B; East: Lots B, D, F and
Damunukumbura; South:  Damunukumbura and Fair Land; West:
Fair Land.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Damunukumbura – Extent:  0.0153 Hectare
North: Lots E and D; East and South:  Damunukumbura; West:
Lot E.
