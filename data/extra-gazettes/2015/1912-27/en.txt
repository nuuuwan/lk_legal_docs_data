FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
KUNDASALE     PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United National Party,  to which the former
member,  Mr. Anura Senavirathne Kumburegedara belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Weerasekara
Mudiyanselage Harsha Kumara Weerasekara  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning Officer of  Kundasale
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Weerasekara
Mudiyanselage Harsha Kumara Weerasekara elected as a member of the Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kundasale Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
27th  April, 2015.
