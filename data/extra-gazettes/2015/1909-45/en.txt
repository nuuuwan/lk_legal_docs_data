THE   URBAN  DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY    LAW   No. 41 OF   1978   OF   THE   NATIONAL   STATE
ASSEMBLY
Cancellation of the Vesting Order under Section 15
BY virtue of the powers vested with the Minister of Defence and Urban Development under Section 15(1) of the Urban
Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978 the land described in the Schedule hereto was vested with the Urban Development
Authority by the Order under Section 15 published in the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1884/57 dated 16.10.2014.
I, Rauff Hakeem, Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage as the subject Minister of Urban
Development hereby cancel that aforesaid V esting Order since the subject property is not required for the Urban Development
Authority.
R
AUFF  HAKEEM M. P .,
Minister of Urban Development,
Water Supply and Drainage.
Ministry of Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage,

09th  April, 2015.
