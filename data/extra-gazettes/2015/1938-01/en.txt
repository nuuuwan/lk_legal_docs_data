CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, Alisandaralage Senanayake,  Acting  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 26th  October, 2015,
all  duties of Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter
235), shall be   paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1937/1 of
19th  October, 2015 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 01st November  2015.
A. S
ENANAYAKE,
Acting Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
23rd  October 2015.
