SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
01. Notification  under the section 184 of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)  and the Provincial Council (Consequential
Provisions) Act No.12 of 1989 .
02. I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government,
Health and indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment,
News, Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do hereby appoint a retired Judicial Officer, Hon. Saman
Wickrama Wickramarachchi (Retired High Court Judge), as a single person tribunal of inquiry  as per the powers vested in me
by the provisions of Section 184 (1A) of  the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)  to inquire and submit a report based on
the matters under section 184 (1)  of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255),  regarding the functions of the Chairman of
the Embilipitiya Urban Council,   Ven. Vewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero.
03.  I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government,
Health and indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment,
News, Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, do hereby temporally  suspend with immediate effect, Ven.
Vewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero, Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban Council of the Ratnapura  District, functioning as the
Chairman of the aforesaid Urban Council, as per the powers vested in me by the provisions of Section  184 (1B) (A) (i) of the
Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)
04. As the functions of Ven. Vewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero as the Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban Council has
been temporally suspended with immediate effect under the provisions of section 184 of the Urban Council Ordinance
(Chapter 255)  and the Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act No.12 of 1989, I do hereby order Mr. W.G. Lalith
Dharmakeerthi, Deputy Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban Council to execute the powers and carry out the tasks thereof,
under the section  184 (1B) (A) (i) of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255) and the Provincial Council (Consequential
Provisions) Act No.12 of 1989 .
.
M
AHEEPALA H ERATH
