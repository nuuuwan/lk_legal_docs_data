PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a  vacancy  in  the  Eastern  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Expulsion from the Party ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress, the recognized political party,  to which the   member
belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And Whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Abdhul Razzak Kulanthai
Maraikkar to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Abdhul Razzak Kulanthai Maraikkar, elected as a
member of the above Provincial Council  from the Administrative District of Ampara in respect of  which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
16th October, 2015.
