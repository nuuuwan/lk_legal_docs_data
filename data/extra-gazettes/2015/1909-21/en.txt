THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  of  1978
Order under Section 27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Sarath Amunugama,
Minister of Higher Education and Research, on the  recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order
amend the Order made under Section 21 of the aforesaid Act establishing the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka and published
in Gazette Extraordinary No. 281/7 of January 24, 1984, as amended from time to time, by the addition immediately after item
6 in Column II under the heading “Department” which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences”
in Column I under the heading “Faculty”, in the Schedule to such Order, of the following new items :-
“7. Department of Public Policy
  8. Department of English and Linguistics.”.
D
R. SARA TH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
01st  April, 2015.
