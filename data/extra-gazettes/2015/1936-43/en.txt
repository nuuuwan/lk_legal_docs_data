NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
North Western Provincial Council Notification made under Co-operative Employees Commission
Statute Number 05 of 1995 of  the North Western Province
I, Gunadasa Dehigama the Minister of the Provincial Ministry of Road, Transport, Co-operative Development, Trade, Housing
and Constructions, Industry and Rural Development of North Western Province by virtue of the powers vested to me by
Section 05 (01) of  the Co-operative Employees Commission of  North Western Province, Constituted under the Provisions
made by Section 05 of North Western Provincial Council's Co-operative Employees Commission Statute  No. 05 of 1995, do
hereby appoint following Persons as members of the  North Western Province Co-operative Employees Commission with
effect from 01.10.2015 to 01.10.2018.
Further it is hereby notified that under Section 07 of the Statute Mr.  R. P. Champika Lasitha Kumara Attorney at Law
has been appointed as the Chairman of  this Commission with effect from 01.10.2015 to 01.10.2018.
G
UNADASA  D EHIGAMA ,
 Minister of the Provincial Ministry of Road, Transport,
Co-operative Development, Trade, Housing and Constructions, Industry and
Rural Development of North Western Province.
9th October 2015.
