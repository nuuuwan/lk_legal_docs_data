THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 0.0254 Hectare depicted
as lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No. Gam/Meeri/2014/178 dated
11.08.2014 prepared by the Surveyor - General situated in the Village
of Bothale Pahalagama in Udugaha Pattuwa Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Meerigama in Gampaha District
of the Western Province and bounded as follows.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Galabodawatta – Extent: About 0.0254 Hectare
North: Road and Galabodawatta claimed by A. P . L. A. Ganga
Amarasinghe; East: Galabodawatta claimed by A. P. L. A. Ganga
Amarasinghe and Galabodawatta claimed by Kudavidanage; South:
Galabodawatta claimed by Kudavidanage; West: Galabodawatta
claimed by Kudavidanage and Road.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
