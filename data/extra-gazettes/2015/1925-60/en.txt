THE  WAGES   BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
I,  Dr. Amarajeewa Jinabandu Satharasinghe, Director General of Census and Statistics, being the Competent Authority
appointed under Section 20(2)(d) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136) by the Minister by notification published
in Gazette No. 9,327 of November 3, 1944, No. 9,963 of April 1, 1949, No. 10,541 of June 25 1953, No. 11,568 of
October 27 1958 and No. 13,179 of  June 22 1962 do hereby declare that the Cost of Living Index Number (Based on 2006/07)
for the month of July 2015, applicable to workers in each of the traders specified in the Schedule hereto is 182.8.

R. A. J. S ATHARASINGHE,
