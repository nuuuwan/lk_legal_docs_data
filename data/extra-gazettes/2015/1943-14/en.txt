THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don
Somadasa Gunawardana, Minister of Lands do  hereby
declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Land
Acquisition  Act, that the land described below are needed for a
Private Land Acquisition of Yala National Park  and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act.
Allotments  of  land in total extent of  about 203.8515 Hectares,
depicted in Advance Tracing No. MO/KTR/2013/346 and Dated
14.10.2013 prepared by  the  Monaragala District Superintendent of
Survey's  in situated in the Village of Katharagama in the Division
Secretary's Division of  Katharagama in  Monaragala District in Uva
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kumburukele – Extent:  179.7529 Hectares
North: Yaddalgamuwa Village of TOPP.P.P.26  and Lot No. 4618
of F.T.P. 25; East: Lot No. 4618 of F.T.P. 25 and Ethbendeniya Eliya
Ara; South: Ethbendeniya Eliya Ara, Ara and Menik Ganga; West:
Ara and Menik Ganga and Yaddalgamuwa Village of TOPP.P.P. 26.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Kumburukele – Extent:  24.0986 Hectare
North: Menik Ganga and Ara; East: Ara and Ethbendeniya Eliya
Ara; South: Ethbendeniya Eliya Ara, Ara and Menik Ganga; West:
Menik Ganga and Ara.
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
