Government  Notifications
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  The  North Central   Province   Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of Appointing the Governor of North Central Province Provincial Council  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was requested by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated  Mr. Abdul Rahim Abdul
Husain, to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare  Mr. Abdul Rahim Abdul Husain, elected as a member of
the above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Anuradhapura  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of  Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
20th February, 2015.
