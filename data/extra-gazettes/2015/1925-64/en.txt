THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
Three (03) allotments of land, containing in extent of 7.0747
Hectares, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. Ma/LGG/2011/123
dated 16.07.2011 of the Surveyor General, situated at Akarahediya
(ToPo PP 13) Village in Laggala Pallesiya Pattuwa Minor Division
of Laggala Pallegama Divisional Secretary’s Division in District of
Matale, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Alahakke Heneyaya Dachcheyaya,  Nikaatthe
Pelessa – Extent :  0.1338 Hectare                                     
North: Dachcheyaya forest claimant not known and Leloya;
East: Lot B; South: Lots C and D; West: Dachcheyaya forest
claimant not known.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Dachcheyaya –  Extent: 4.7938 Hectares
North: Leloya, Dachcheyaya Idama and field claimed by
E.M.Ekanayake and others; East : Digannawa reservation and Lot
C; South: Lot C; West: Lot C and Leloya.
03. Lot ‘F’ – Nikaatthe kumbura –  Extent:
2.1471 Hectares                                                             
North: Digannawa reservation and Lot D; East: Digannawa
reservation and  Diyamolliya  claimant not known and Biggediya
forest; South: Diyamolliya  claimant not known and  Biggepitiya
forest; West: Diyamolliya  claimant not known and  Biggediya
forest.
 Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,  
                          Minister of Lands and Land Development.
