THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An  allotment of land in total  extent of about 0.6197 Hectare, depicted
in Advance Tracing No. Ya/DLF/2014/122 dated 30th July, 2014
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of  Surveys - Jaffna District,
situated in the Village called Delft East  in the  Grama Niladhari Division
No. J/06 of Delft East in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Delft,
Jaffna  District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Parayantharai – Extent: About 0.6197 Hectare
North: Pararyantharai claimed by Kanapathipillai Kailayapillai
and others; East: Path; South: Pararyantharai claimed by Velupillai
Kanagarajah and others; West: Pararyantharai claimed by Velupillai
Kanagarajah Kanapathipillai and others.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
