NOTIFICATION
P.S No. CSA/1/4/1/1.
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Fiszer Musthapha, PC, MP, who was appointed to the office of Deputy Minister of Investment
Promotion by His Excellency the President, in the exercise of powers vested in him, by Article 46(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in terms of Article 47(b) of the Constitution, resigned such office of Deputy
Minister, with effect from December 31, 2014.
