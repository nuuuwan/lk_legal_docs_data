No. 1897/14  –  FRIDAY, JANUARY 16, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHEPTER 460) 
AS AMENDER BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
New No.: IMDS/LND/05/ACQ/20/2002. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
acquisition (amended) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intend to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose.  
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Acquire the land for the Karagasthalawa Primary 
School. 
 
Allotments of land : Lot No. 276 in Extent about 
1.2140 Hectares depicted in supplementary No. 13 in 
F.V.P. 591 situated at Karagasthalawa village 
Karagasthalawa G.S. Division Kadawatha Korale 
Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat, Rathnapura District and 
Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
All Person interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquire are here by required 
appear personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat on the 30th 
day of January, 2015 at 9.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 16th day of January, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for the 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation of such amount. 
 
 J. L. C. K. JAYASINGHA, 
                                                       Divisional Secretary,  
 Imbulpe. 
 
   
Divisional Secretariat, 
Imbulpe. 
12th January, 2015.
