EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PUTTALAM    URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262) AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Puttalam  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation;
And  whereas the Secretary of the recognized political part namely United National  Freedom Alliance to  which the
former Member, Mohamed Nizar Mohamed Nazmi belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to nominate  within a period of  thirty  (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Puttalam  Urban Council who shall be declared elected as a member ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political part  has  nominated  Mr. Mohamed Abubucker
Sadureen  being an eligble person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
Now therefore, I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan  Returning Officer  of   the Puttalam  Urban Council,  acting in  terms of
Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act,  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Mohamed Abubucker  Sadureen  elected as a member of
the  Puttalam  Urban Council.
P
AKKIY ARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
Elections Office,
Puttalam,
27
th  January  2015.
