No. 1909/19 – MONDAY, APRIL 06, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘Rambawa Kele’ in total extent of
about 0.4856 Hectare, described in the Advance Tracing No. Aa/
RMB/2013/819 and dated 19.12.2013, prepared by the Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of Rambewa (F.V .P. 157) in Kende
Korale West Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Rambewa in Anuradhapura District, North Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rambawa Kele – Extent: About 0.4856 Hectare
North: Land claimed by Thuduwage Elis Nona and Road
(Pradeshiya Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); South: Remaining
portion of land claimed by Thuduwage Jane Nona; West: Road
Reservation.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2012/WS/252.
Ministry of Lands,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
