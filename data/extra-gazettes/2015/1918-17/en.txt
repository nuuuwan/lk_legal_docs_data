Direction  No. : 58
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Direction under Section 10 (1) (a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10 (1) (a)  of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Private Healthcare Institutions that they shall include the following information/
details in the receipts/ bills issued to all patients who seek indoor treatment at their institutions.
* Details of all drugs and  vitamins administered on the patient, including their Generic/ brand name, quantity
used and the total cost  for each and every drug and/ or vitamin.
* Details of all devices used for treatment specifying the quantity and the cost for each and every such device
used.
This Direction shall come into effect from 01st of November, 2015.
By Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
1st  June, 2015.
