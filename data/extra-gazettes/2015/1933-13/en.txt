THE   CONSTITUTION   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF  SRI   LANKA
Notification
WHEREAS I have in the exercise of powers vested in me in terms of paragraph (1) of Article 43 of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined the number of Ministers of the Cabinet of Ministers and the Ministries
and the assignment of subjects and functions and Departments, Public Corporations & Statutory Institutions to the said
Ministers ;
It is now hereby notified that the subjects and functions and Departments, Public Corporations & Statutory
Institutions in the charge of various Ministers shall be as set out in the Schedule given below, from the date of this
Notification.
All such subjects and functions and Departments, Public Corporations & Statutory Institutions that are not specifically
assigned to any Minister will continue to remain in my charge as the President.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
September 21, 2015.
