Insertion of new paragraph 3(I) above the existing paragraph 3(I)
3(I) Promotion of the Deputy Auditors General in Class 1 Grade 1 of the Sri Lanka Audit Service to the Special Grade.
(a) Qualifications to be fulfilled
The promotion of the officers in Class 1 Grade 1 of the service who have  fulfilled the following requirements to the
vacancies in the Special Grade of the Service will be made only through promotion by the Public Service Commission.
(i) Has obtained a Postgraduate Degree in Accounts or Auditing (a field of study or as a subject) from a University/
Institute recognized by the University Grant Commission or has obtained the full membership of the Institute of
Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka or the Institute of Chartered Accountants of England and Wales or the
chartered Institute of Management Accountants of the United Kingdom or the Institute of Certified Chartered
Accountants of the United Kingdom.
(ii) Has completed an active and satisfactory service period of five (05) years in Class I Grade I of the Sri Lanka
Audit Service by the date of becoming eligible and earned five (05) salary increment after the promotion to
Class 1.
(iii) Has completed an active and satisfactory service period of not less than 18 years in the Executive Grade in the
qualifying  Sri Lanka Audit Service by the date of becoming eligible.
(This paragraph is not applicable to the direct recruits to Class I of the Sri Lanka Audit Service.)
(iv) Should have had a satisfactory or a higher level of performance for a period of five (05) years immediately
preceding the date of becoming eligible for promotion.
(v) Should have had a satisfactory service period of five (05) years in Class 1 and not being subjected to a
disciplinary punishment by the date of becoming eligible for promotion.
(b) Method of Promotion
The Promotion of the Deputy Auditor’s General of Class 1 Grade 1 of in Sri Lanka Audit Service to the Special Grade
only by promotion will be made by in Public Service Commission. The officers who fulfill all the qualifications stated
above will be promoted to the Special Grade of the Sri Lanka Audit Service according to the vacancies and on
seniority.
(c) Appointment to the posts of Additional Auditors General
Insertion of —
“The senior most officers who have served as Deputy Auditors General out of the officers in the Special Grade of the
Sri Lanka Audit Service will be appointed to the posts of Additional Auditor General according to the existing
vacancies by the Public Service Commission on the recommendation of a Board of Interview appointed by the
Public Service Commission.
As a new paragraph 3(I) has been added the existing paragraphs 3(I), (II) and (III) are amended as 3(II), (III) and (IV)
respectively.
W. P. C. W
ICKRAMARATNE,
Acting Auditor General.
