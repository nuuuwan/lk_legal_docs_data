 THE   WAGES  BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Order under Section 33(1)(a)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 33(1)(a) of the Wages Boards Ordinance, I, S. B. Nawinne, Minister of
Labour do by this order authorize the Commissioner of Labour to determine a general minimum time rate for workers in the
Beedi Manufacturing Trade and Cigar Manufacturing Trade.
S. B. N
AWINNE,
Minister of Labour.
Ministry of  Labour,
Labour Secretariat,
