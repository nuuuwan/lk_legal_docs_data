and the Chairman  and Hon, Justice Anil Goonaratne Esquire, Judge of the Supreme Court, a member of the said Commission
of Inquiry, have resigned from their positions;
AND WHEREAS, the need to fill the vacancies of the said positions have been identified;

NOW THEREFORE, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka reposing great
trust and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity, do, in pursuance of the provisions of Section 3 of the Commissions
of Inquiry Act, by these Presents, appoint Preethi Padman Surasena Esquire and Degiri Amendra Mendis Seneviratne Esquire,
Judges of the High Court, to be the Commissioners of the Commission of inquiry constituted in terms of the Warrant referred
to above.
AND, I do hereby appoint  the said Preethi Padman Surasena Esquire, Judge of the High Court to be the Chairman
of the said Commission.
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, on this 02nd day of April Two
Thousand and Fifteen.
                                                                                               By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
