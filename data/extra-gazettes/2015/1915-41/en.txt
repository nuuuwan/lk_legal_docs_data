EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
HAMBANTOTA   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Hambantota  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  National Party,  to which  the  former
member, Ayub Khan  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a
period of  of  thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a member of  that Hambantota  Municipal
Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Baba Juhara Cassim
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathna, Returning Officer of  Hambantota  Municipal
Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of V acancies in
Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Baba Juhara Cassim  elected as
a member of the Hambantota  Municipal Council.
D
EEPAL CHANDRARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Hambantota  Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Hambantota,
19th   May,  2015.
