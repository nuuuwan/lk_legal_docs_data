NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A(1)(a) OF THE LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Ella   Pradeshiya   Sabha
I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the  Ella  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of
the  (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance,  that   Mr. Basnayake Mudiyanselage Udayajeewa Bandara,  of the
Ella  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as he has ceased to be a member of the
United National Party.
SURANGA AMBAGAHA THENNE,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office, Ella P radeshiya Sabha.
Badulla,
12th May, 2015.
