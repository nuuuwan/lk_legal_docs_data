NATIONAL  WATER  SUPPLY  AND  DRAINAGE  BOARD  ACT,   No. 02 OF 1974
Order under Section 64(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 64(1) of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board  Act, No. 02 of 1974,
I, Rauf Hakeem, the Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage, do by this order, embody in the Schedule
hereto the provisions of the voluntary transfer scheme approved by me under Section 63(1) of the aforesaid Low, inrespect of
the Hakmana Water Supply undertakings on by the Pradeshiya Sabha Hakmana.
R
AUFF HAKEEM,
 Minister of  Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage.
Ministry of Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage,

Pelawatta, Battaramulla,
26th  June, 2015.
SCHEDULE
Contents of V oluntary Transfer Scheme Section 61 (1)
(a) The following movable and immovable property to be transferred to and vested in the National Water Supply
and Drainage Board to wit,
(i) All lands, buildings pipelines, all mechanical and electrical machinery and equipment fixtures and all spares
accessories and stores items required for the implementation and maintenance of all intakes refineries,
pressure and balancing tank and pumping station of the Hakmana Water Supply Scheme listed in schedule I.
(ii)Servitude over pipelines, spares accessories and stores items along with main pipelines and distribution
pipeline required for the operation and maintenance of water distribution network at the Hakmana Water
Supply Scheme within the area of authority of the Pradeshiya Sabha Hakmana.
