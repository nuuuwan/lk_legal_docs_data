PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.  48   OF   2007
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007,
I, Ravi  Karunanayake, Finance Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose
in respect of the commodity items specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This order shall be valid for a period of
06 months commencing from 2nd April 2015, subject to provisions of  Sub section (3) of   Section 2 of the Special
Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
R
AV I  KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
1st April, 2015.
