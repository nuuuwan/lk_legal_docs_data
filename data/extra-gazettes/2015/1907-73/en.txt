FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  ERA VUR   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman  in  Eravur Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member of
the Eastern Province Provincial Council  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which   Ali
Zahir Moulana Seyed  the former  Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to decide upon the member of that Eravur Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Mohamed Ismail Mohamed Thasleem, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Abdul Majeed Mahammadu Kabeer,  Returning Officer of  Eravur Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mohamed Ismail Mohamed Thasleem  elected as
Chairman of the  Eravur Urban Council.
A.M. M.  K
ABEER,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
27th March, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF   A  MEMBER  OF  THE
PUTTALAM    URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER 262) AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS the office of  a member  in  Puttalam  Urban Council   has fallen vacant by reason of  resignation;
AND  WHEREAS  the Group leader of  the Independent  Group   No. 08 to which the  Member, MR. S. R. M. M. Muhusi
belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2)  as amended by  the  Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act,
No. 16 of 2007, to   nominate  a person  eligible  under the  Ordinance  to appoint as   a member of that Puttalam  Urban Council
within a period of  thirty  (30) days  ;
AND  WHEREAS the Group leader of  the Independent  Group  has  nominated  Mr. Mohamed Rizwan Sabar   being
an eligble person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pakkiyarajah Ragunathan  Returning Officer  of   the Puttalam  Urban Council,  acting in  terms
of   Section 65A(2) (b) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance  as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act,  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Mohamed Rizwan Sabar  elected as a member of
the  Puttalam  Urban Council.
P
AKKIYARAJAH RAGUNATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Puttalam Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Puttalam,
27th  March   2015.
