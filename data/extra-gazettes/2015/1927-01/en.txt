No. 1927/1 – MONDAY, AUGUST 10, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
My  No.: KOT/LND/ACQVI/01/41. 
Land Min. Ref.No.: LD/05/2003/VEP/486. 
 
 
I hereby notify under Sub -section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, as amended by the land Acquisition 
(Amendments) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is 
required for a  public purpose :- 
 
Description of the land  or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of the land called Handfered estate alias 
Kekunahena situated at Mathugobe Village in the Grama 
Niladhari’s Division Tennipita (241B) of the Divisional 
Secretariat, Kotapola in Matara District in Southern 
Province, depicted as Lot No. 1406 of Supplementary 
Sheet No. 120, Sheet No. 102 of F. V. P. 169 made by the 
Surveyor General containing in extent about 0.865 
Hectare and boundaries of the land given below:- 
 
Owner of the land : Land reforms Commission; 
 
Leaseholder of the land: Thalawekele Plantation  
         Company Ltd. 
An allotment of the land called Handferdwatta alias 
Kekunahena Lot No. 1406, containing in extent about 
0.865 Hectare.   
North by : Road and Lot No. 87; East by : Road and 
Lot No. 87; South by: Lot No.87; West by: Road. 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land/servitude  
is to be acquired are hereby required to appear personally 
or by agents duly authorized in writing,  before me at the 
Divisional Secretariat of Kotapola on 21st day of  
September, 2015 at  10.00 a.m.. Further, I hereby 
requested to  notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 14th day of September, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in  the land, the particulars  of their claims for 
compensation of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of  the computation of such 
amount. 
 
                                               GANESHA AMARASINGHE, 
                                                     Divisional Secretary, 
   Kotapola. 
 
At the Divisional Secretariat, 
Kotapola. 
05th August, 2015.
