HAMBANTOTA   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
Programmed Budget - 2016
IT is hereby notified that under section 212 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) the programmed budget for the
year 2016 of Hambantota Municipal Council is open for public inspection at this office during working hours from 22nd
October to 30th October 2015 at the Hambantota Municipal Council Office (except on Sundays and Public Holidays)
E
RAJ RAVINDRA FERNANDO,
Mayor,
Hambantota Municipal Council.
