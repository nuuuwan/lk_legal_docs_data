THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)

Notice under Section 7 
 
 Reference No.:  4/3/1/119(IV)Additional 2. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to acquired: 
 
The following allotment of  land situated in the village 
of Nagoda in Grama Niladari division of 729 F Uswatta 
Divisional Secretary’s division of Kalutara in the district 
of Kalutara in Western province and marked in Surveyor 
General’s Plan No. P.P.K. 4511 and contains in extent 
0.0013 Hectares. 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Pitagoneliya; Description: Garden; 
Climants: Government Previous claimant E. P. 
Weerasekara; Extent: 0.0007 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 02  
Name of the Land: Pitagoneliya; Description: Garden; 
Climants: Government Previous claimant U. Munasinghe; 
Extent: 0.0006 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing 
before me at the Kalutara Divisional Secretariat on the 
12th day of October, 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 05th day of October, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. You should present 
relevant Deeds, Plans, and other documents to prove 
ownership to the land on the inquiry date. 
 
 WIMAL GUNATUNGA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kalutara. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Kalutara, 
22nd July, 2015.
