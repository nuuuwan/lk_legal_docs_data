No. 1903/11 –  WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY  25, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
  
My No.: WGSWD&EIP/ACQ/KB/06. 
 
Land Min’s Ref No.: 4/3/10/2012/LRB-22. 
 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the land described below 
to creating a water reserve under Weras Ganga Storm 
Water Drainage and Environment Improvement Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of 0.2297 Hectare 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary Plan No. Co. 
9469 dated 19.12.2014 prepared by the Surveyor General 
situated in the village called Bellanthara in the Grama 
Niladhari Division No. 535A of Bellanwila in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kesbewa in Colombo 
District of Western Province and described as follows: 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Kanuketiya Kumbura; Description: 
Marshy Land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:              
Mrs. Wickramasinghage Arachchige Sharmen Shiron 
Perera, No. 116/2C, Piriwena Road, Boralesgamuwa; 
Extent: 0.2297 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by Agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and 
Development Corporation, No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura 
Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on 08.04.2015 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 01.04.2015, the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 C. W. ABEYSURIYA, 
 Acquiring Officer,
