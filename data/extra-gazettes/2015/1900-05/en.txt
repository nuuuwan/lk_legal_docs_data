Notification
I, C. V . Wigneswaran, the Chief Minister and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Electricity, Housing
and Construction, Industries and Enterprise Promotion, Tourism, Local Government and Provincial Administration, of Northern
Province do hereby declare that I shall enforce the regulations relevant to Allowance Payments for Mayors, Deputy Mayors
and Members of Municipal Councils as per power assigned to me under Section 289 of Municipal Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 252) read with clause 2 of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, These Regulations
shall come into effect from 01.11.2014 and the regulations put into force from 30.10.2009 in relation to this matter as published
in the Gazette Paper of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1626 dated 30th October 2009 will be repealed with
effect from the date immediately previous to 01.11.2014.

2A
REGULATION
(1) These regulations may be cited as Regulations relating to payment of allowances to the Mayor, Deputy Mayor
and Member of Municipal Councils.
(2) Monthly allowance may be paid to the elected Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Member of Municipal Councils
according to the following details :
* Mayor - Rs. 30,000.00
* Deputy Mayor - Rs. 25,000.00
* Member - Rs. 20,000.00
(Entertainment allowance, too, is included in the above)
(3) Due to any one of the following reasons the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Member of a Municipal Council shall not
have the right for the aforesaid allowances.
(i) Non participation at any monthly Council meeting and committee meeting held in the month considered
for the payment of allowances, or
(ii) Being kept under detention or imprisoned due to any criminal allegation, or
(iii) Suspension of functioning of Municipal Council or of functions of a Mayor, Deputy Mayor or Member
under the provisions of Section 277(1B) (a) of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252)
3.1 However,
(i) on an occasion when the period under detention or being imprisoned, or on leave, or under suspension
of service can be counted as a part of a month, payment can be made in proportionate to the rest of the
month according to the occasion in participation the council meeting or committee meeting held in this
month.
(ii) This payment can be made, for absence on the approval of the council by submitting proper evidence due
to medical reasons.
(iii) This payment can be made, where a Mayor, Deputy Mayor or Member engage in official duty on formal
approval.
