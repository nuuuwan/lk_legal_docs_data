SRI   LANKA   ELECTRICITY  ACT,  No.  20  OF  2009
Order under Sub section (1) of the Section 46
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested under Sub section (1) of the Section 46 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009
(as amend), the Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka does hereby impose an annual levy in such amounts as are set out
in Column II of the Schedule hereto, to be recovered from those licensees holding such licenses as are specified in the
corresponding entry in Column I of that schedule. The previous Gazette Notification published in the Extraordinary Gazette
No. 1861/38 on Thursday 08th May, 2014 is hereby revoked.
Chairman,
Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka.
Level 06,
Bank of Ceylon Merchant Tower,
No. 28, St. Michael’s Road,
Colombo 03,
01st January, 2015.
