EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
SPECIAL   COMMODITY   LEVY   ACT,   No.  48   OF   2007
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of  the powers vested in me under section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Finance Minister of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,  do by this order impose in respect of the commodity items
specified in “Column I” of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries
in Column II of that Schedule. This order shall be valid for a period of 04 months commencing from 17th June 2015, subject to
provisions of Sub-section (3) of  Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1908/29 of  01.04.2015 in respect of  vegetable oil (HS Heding  Nos. 15.07, 15.11, 15.12, 15.13)  and  Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1903/4 of 24.02.2015 in respect of Massor dhal (red lentils) - Split (HS Cord Nos. 0713.40.12) and Yellow lentils - Split
(0713.40.22) are rescinded with effect from 17th June 2015.
R
AV I KARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of  Finance,
Colombo 01,
15th  June,  2015.
