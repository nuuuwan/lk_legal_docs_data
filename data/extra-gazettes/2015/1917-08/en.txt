PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
Appointments & C., by the Governors

My No. : 01/10/03/04.
UV A    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  by  the  Governor   of   Uva   Province
BY Virtue of  powers vested to  me by Section 32 (1) of  the  Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of  1987, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,
Governor of  Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
01. Mr. H. M. Jeewantha Herath, Grade  III   officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform, the Duties of
the  Secretary to the Ministry of  Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Co-operative,  Animal Production
and  Cultural Affairs for the  Uva Provincial Council, from  the date 22.05.2015 to 01.06.2015, till the return of  Mr.
W. M. M. G . Abesinghe Banda, to the  Island who is the Secretary to the Minister of  Road Development,
Housing, Water Supply, Co- operative, Animal Production and Cultural  Affairs for the  Uva Provincial Council.
02. Mr. H. P. A. G. Anuruddha, Grade  III   officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform, the Duties of  the
Secretary to the Ministry of  Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and  Childcare, Women's Affairs and  Power
and  Energy for the    Uva Provincial Council, from  the date 22.05.2015 to 01.06.2015, till the return of
Mr. L. L. Anil Wijesiri, to the  Island who is the Secretary to the  Minister of  Health,  Indigenous Medicine,
Probation and  Childcare, Women's   Affairs  and  Power and  Energy for the  Uva Provincial Council.
03. Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Grade  I   officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform, the Duties of
the  Secretary to the Ministry of  Sports,  Youth Affairs,  Textiles and Small  Industries  and  Estate Infrastructure
Development and  Estate Education  for the  Uva Provincial Council, from  the date 22.05.2015 to 01.06.2015, till
the return of  Mr. G. K. S. L. Rajadasa, to the  Island who is the Secretary to  the  Ministry of  Sports,  Youth Affairs,
Textiles and Small Industries  and  Estate Infrastructure  Development and Estate Education for the Uva Provincial
Council.
