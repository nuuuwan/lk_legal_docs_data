THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakarathne Bandara Ekanayake
the Minister of Lands (Acting) hereby declare, under Sub-section (1)
of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotments of below mentioned lands in total extent of about
0.1353 Hectare, depicted as Lots ‘A’ to ‘D’ in the Advance Tracing
No. MR/DKW/2014/232 dated 29th October, 2014 prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in Pathegama Central Village
Wallabadapattuwa East Minor Division, Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Dickwella in Matara District, Southern Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Godallahena – Extent: 0.0136 Hectare
North: Godallahena and Paranawatta acquired by the Railway
Department; East: Paranawatta acquired by the Railway Department,
Lot B, Paranawatta claimed by Wajira Priyantha Palihakkara and
Godallahena claimed by Sirisena Samarawickrama and others; South:
Godallahena claimed by Sirisena Samarawickrama and others; West:
Godallahena claimed by Sirisena Samarawickrama and others and
Godallahena acquired by the Railway Department.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Paranawatta – Extent: 0.0121 Hectare
North: Paranawatta and Palawatta acquired by the Railway
Department; East: Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department,
Lot C, Palawatta claimed by Kumari Thamara Ruwanpathirana
Palihakkara and others and Paranawatta claimed by Wajira Priyantha
Palihakkara; South: Paranawatta claimed by Wajira Priyantha
Palihakkara and Godallahena claimed by Sirisena Samarawickrama
and others; West: Lot A and Godallahena and Paranawatta acquired
by the Railway Department.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Palawatta – Extent: 0.0747 Hectare
North: Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department; East:
Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department, Lot D, Palawatta
claimed by P. Abesekara and others and Palawatta claimed by Kumari
Thamara Ruwanpathirana Palihakkara and others; South: Palawatta
claimed by Kumari Thamara Ruwanpathirana Palihakkara and others,
Paranawatta claimed by Wajira Priyantha Palihakkara; West:
Paranawatta claimed by Wajira Priyantha Palihakkara, Lot B,
Paranawatta and Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Palawatta – Extent: 0.0349 Hectare
North: Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department; East:
Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department, Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha) and Palawatta claimed by P . Abesekara and others; South:
Palawatta claimed by P. Abesekara and others, Palawatta claimed by
Kumari Thamara Ruwanpathira Palihakkara and others; West: Lot C
and Palawatta acquired by the Railway Department.
T. B. Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Actg.).
Ref. No.: 4-3/16/2013/NRD/377.
Ministry of Lands,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
Land Secretariat,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwatta Avenue,
Battaramulla.
