THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My No. : 3/3/2/33/4.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot No. 183 - 188 (Lots 3) allotmentsin total extent of about
0.0301 Hectare, of land in Cadastral Map No. 520024, Block
No. 01 and Sheet No. 5  and dated 18.08.2015 prepared by the
Sinior - Superintendent of  Survey of  Colombo  Dictric on
behalf  of  Survey General, situated in the Grama Niladhari
Division of 593A - Rilawala in the village of  Rilawala in
Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Homagama in Colombo
District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 183
Name of land: Kimbulawala or Ihala Ovita; Description:
Ela; Claimant:  State; Original Claimant:  Ranaweera  Arachchige
Lionel, No. 27/B,  Saranankara  Mawatha,  Rilawala,  Polgasovita
(Part of Lot No. 116); Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 185
Name of land: Kimbulawala or Ihala Ovita; Description:
Ela; Claimant:  State; Original Claimant:  Ranaweera  Arachchige
Lionel, No. 27/B,  Saranankara  Mawatha,  Rilawala,  Polgasovita
(Part of Lot No. 116); Extent: 0.0025 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 187
Name of land: Kimbulawala or Ihala Ovita and Ihatta Ovita;
Description: Open waste land; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant:  Ranaweera  Arachchige  Lionel, No. 27/B,
Saranankara  Mawatha,  Rilawala, Polgasovita (Part of  Lot
No. 68); Extent: 0.0273 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the afore said lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional  Secretariat office of  Homagama
on the  from 28.10.2015 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or  before the
21.10.2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their  claims for  compensation for the  acquisition of the
said lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Homagama.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
02nd October 2015.
