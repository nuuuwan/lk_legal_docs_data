LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 262)
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of  Elections by virtue of powers vested in me by Section 27 (1) of the Local Authorities
Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) as amended by Act, No. 24 of 1987, read with Section 4 (1) of  the said Act, do hereby appoint
the officer specified in Column III of  Schedule  hereto as Returning Officer of each Local Authority described in the corre-
sponding entry in Column II of the said Schedule, situated in the Administrative District described in the corresponding entry
in Column I of  the said Schedule, for the purpose of election of members of the prescribed Local Authorities in the Mullaitivu
Administrative District.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
