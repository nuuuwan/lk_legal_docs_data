Government  Notifications
SRI  LANKA  VOCATIONAL  TRAINING  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 12  OF  1995
Order under Section 27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 27(1), of the Sri Lanka Vocational Training Authority Act, No. 12 of 1995, read
with sub-sections (3) and (4) thereof I, Piyasena Gamage, Minister of Skills Development and V ocational Training, in
consultation with the Minister in charge of the subject of Lands and Minister in charge of the subject of Finance do by this
order declare that the allotments of Lands and the relevant properties described in the Schedule to this order on which the
training centre of the Sri Lanka V ocational Training Authority is carried on, be vested with the said Authority for the purpose
of the aforesaid Act.
P
IYASENA GAMAGE,
Minister of Skills Development and
V ocational Training.
Colombo,
07th August, 2015.
