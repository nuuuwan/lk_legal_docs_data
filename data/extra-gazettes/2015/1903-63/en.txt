THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Section 27 (1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  section 27 (1)  of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,    I,   Kabir Hashim, Minister
of  Higher Education, do by this Order,  on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, amend the Schedule
to the Order made under section 21 of the said Act in relation   to the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka published in
Gazette Extraordinary,  No. 896/2 of November 7, 1995   as last amended by order published in Gazette Extraordinary,
No. 1691/13 of  February 1, 2011, by the repeal of the item  “Faculty of Social Studies and Languages ” appearing in Column
I of such Schedule and its corresponding entries    appearing in Column II thereto, and the substitution therefore of the
following :-
''Column I Column II
Faculty Departments
Faculty of Social  Sciences and Languages 1. Department of  Social Sciences
2. Department of  Languages
3. Department of  English Language Teaching
4. Department of  Economic and Statistics
5. Department of  Geography and Environmental
Management''.
