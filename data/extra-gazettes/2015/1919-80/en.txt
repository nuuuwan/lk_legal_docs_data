FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH  WEST    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in  Valikamam South West  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  the recognized political party to which  Shanmugam
Sivakumaaran the former  Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected
as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Sinnathamby Mahenthiran to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj, Returning Officer of  Valikamam South West Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sinnathamby Mahenthiran
elected as Chairman of the Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RAVINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
V alikamam  South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
17th  June,  2015.
