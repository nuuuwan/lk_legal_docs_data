No. 170, Siyabalakote in the Minor Division of Negenahira Giruwa
Paththuwa of Divisional Secretary's Division of Ambalanthota in
Hambanthota District in the Southern Province.
 01. Lot No. 35
Name of land: Siyabalakotemahawewa; Description: Garden;

     02. Lot No. 36
Name of land: –; Description: Ela; Claimant: State; Original

     03. Lot No. 38

Original Claimant: Dhammika Rathnayaka, Siyabalakote,

to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat office of 07.12.2015 on 9.00 a.m. and are

said lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the
