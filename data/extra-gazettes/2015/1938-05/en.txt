THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands do  hereby declare, under Sub-
section (1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition  Act, that the land
described below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired
under the provisions of that Act.

and depicted as Lot  No. 01 in Priliminary  Plan No. KA/ING/07/142
of Superintendent of  Surveys, Kalutara,  situated in the Village
called Handapangoda in Raigam  Korale  Udugahapatthuwa Minor
Division of Divisonal Secretaries Division of Ingiriya in the District
of  Kalutara, Western Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 01 – Mahawattha alias Kotagalahena – Extent:
About  0.3950 Hectare

claimed by K. M. S. Bandara Kulathunga and others; West: Road .
