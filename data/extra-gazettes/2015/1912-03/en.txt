FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
MINIPE    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Minipe  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a member
of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the former
member  Bandaranayake Mudiyanselage Shantha Sisira Kumara Bandaranayake belonged, was required by me under Section
65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the ordinance for
election as a member of that Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Samarakoon
Mudiyanselage Manoj Sanjeewa Samarakoon being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of  Minipe  Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in
Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Samarakoon Mudiyanselage
Manoj Sanjeewa Samarakoon  elected as a member of the Minipe  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. Talangama,
Returning Officer,
Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
24th April,  2015.
