PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments  & C., by   the President
P.S. No.: PS/HR/01/82/PS/Act
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under Article 41(1) of the Constitution
of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe, Secretary to the  Cabinet  as  Acting
Secretary to  the President with effect from 19.10.2015  until Mr. P. B.  Abeykoon, Secretary to the President returns to the
Island.
                               By  Order  of  His  Excellency,
D. S. G

 (Human Resources Development),
for Secretary to the President.
