No. 1915/18 – WEDNESDAY, MAY  20, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
Servitude which is required for a public purpose . 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired : 
 
Under mention land is situated at Nerakkudah 
Kandam Koduvamadu Grama Niladhari Division in 
Eravur Pattu Divisional Secretariat Batticaloa in Eastern 
Province and is Preliminary Plan was perpared by Survey 
general and details in PP/BA/2591 and extend of 0.0840. 
 
Lot No. 01 — Name of Land: Nerakkudah 
Kandam; Usage at present: Chenai Cultivation; 
Claimant: Mrs. Jeyamalini Logitharajah; Extent: 
0.0840 Hectare. 
 
North: PP 99488 and Lot 3 in PP A515; East: Lots 3 
and 6 in PP A515; South: Lot 6 in PP A515 and Aru; 
West: TP 99488. 
 
All person interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before at Eravurpattu. On the 05th day of August 
on 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify  
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 06th day 
of July  2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
Particulars of their claims  for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land /servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 U. U
THAYASHRITHER, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Eravurpattu. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Eravur pattu, 
Chenkalady.
