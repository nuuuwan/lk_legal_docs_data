THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                    My No.:  Mahara/De/2/208.
                           Land Ministry No.: LD/05/2005/HW/275.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01- 02 (Lots 2) allotments of land in total extent of
about 0.0025 Hectare,  of  land in Preliminary Plan No. Gam.
3972 and dated 30.07.2015 prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the village of Rammuthugala  in the Minor Division
of  Adikari Paththuwa in  the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Mahara in Gampaha District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Godaporagahadeniya; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: M. A. W. Munasingha,
No. 589/4, Rammuthugala, Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0022 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 02
Name of land: Rammuthugalawatta; Description: Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Don Neeting Garment
Industries Limited, No. 588/2, Rammuthugala, Kadawatha;
Extent: 0.0003 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Mahara
on the 28.12. 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the
21.12.2015, the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation for the acquisition
of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the details
of the computation of such amount.
C. P. W. GUNATHILAKA,
Divisional Secretary,
Mahara.
Mahara Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Rammuthugala, Kadawatha,
16th November, 2015.
