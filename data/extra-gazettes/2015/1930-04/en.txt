FINANCE  BUSINESS   ACT,  No. 42  OF  2011
CANCELLATION of a licence to carry on finance business under Section 37 of the Finance Business  Act, No. 42 of
2011.
ARJUNA M AHENDRAN,
Chairman,
Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
The public is hereby informed that the Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka cancelled the licence
granted to Orient Finance PLC to carry on finance business under the Finance Business Act, No. 42 of  2011 with effect
from 16.07.2015, subsequent to the amalgamation with Bartleet Finance PLC. Further, please be informed that Bartleet
Finance PLC has changed its name to Orient Finance PLC with effect from 14.08.2015.
