MINUTE OF  THE  GOVERNMENT   LIBRARIAN  SERVICE -  REVISION  No. 02
THE Public Service Commission has ordered to revise the Minute of  the  Government Librarian Service published in the
Gazette  Extraordinary   of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1620/22 dated 24.09.2009 in the
following  manner.
I. To remove the provision mentioned within the bracket in Para. (a) (ii), Para. 10.3.1.1. of  Minute of the
Government Librarian Service No. 1620/22  dated 24.09.2009 (It is sufficient to have the Service as a
Librarian in Grade III even though Serving as a Librarian in Grade II has been specified).
K.V . P . M. J.  G
AMAGE,
