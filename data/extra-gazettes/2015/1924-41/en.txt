THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Two (2)  allotments of lands  as Lot Nos. 1 and 2  in extent of
about 0.1525 Hectare depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/dol/
2013/1817 of 04.12.2013, prepared by the Surveyor General of
Kandy situated in the  Village of, Kumbal Oluwe Wela (in Minor
Division)  in  the Divisonal  Secretary's Division of Doluwa, in
Kandy  District, Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot  No. 01 – Pinkanath Kumbura – Extent: 0.0250 Hectare
North: The Channel, Pinkanath Kumbura claimed by the Chief
Incumbant (Viharadhipathi) of Naranvita Viharaya; East: Channel
Pinkanath Kumbura claimed by the Chief  Incumbant (Viharadhipathi)
of Naranvita Viharaya and Lot No. 02; South: Lot No. 02; West:
Channel.
02. Lot  No. 02 – Miniyandeniya Kumbura – Extent: 0.1275
Hectare
North: The Channel, Lot No. 01  and Pinkanath Kumbura claimed
by the Chief  Incumbant (Viharadhipathi) of Naranvita Viharaya;
East: The Channel and  Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.); West:
The Channel and Lot No. 01.
