No. 1898/2 –  MONDAY, JANUARY  19, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISTION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3915. 
 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, As amended by the land 
Acquisition (amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose:- 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The below mentioned land depicted as lot 1 in the 
preliminary plan No. 2589 located in the Navimana South 
Grama Niladhari Division in the Matara Divisional 
Secretariat Division, Matara District, Southern Province, 
has been acquired for the construction of the Navimana 
South (No. 427) Grama Niladhari Office. 
 
(Schedule) 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
Name of the Land: Hungamgodagewatta; Particulars 
of Land: Garden, There are a Permanent building and a 
toilet; Original Owner of the land: Sanasa Society of 
Navimana  South; Extent: 0.0124 Hectares. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 28th 
day of January 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to me in writing (in -duplicate) on or before the 
22nd of January 2015 the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land/servitude* the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.  
 
  
 RANJITH YAPA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat,  
Matara.
