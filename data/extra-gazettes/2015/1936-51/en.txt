The Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
NOTIFICATION
BY virtue of the powers vested in me in terms of sub-paragraph (1) of Article 43 of the Constitution, I Maithripala Sirisena, the
President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka;
(a) having determined the number of Ministers of the Cabinet of Ministers in terms of the aforesaid Article;
(b) having assigned the subjects and functions to the said Ministers in the manner specified in the Notification
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1933/13 of  September 21, 2015;
(c) do hereby notify that the Schedule to the aforesaid Notification has been amended in the manner set out in
the Schedule below.
 MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
14th October, 2015.
Schedule
(1) With reference to the Heading: Minister of Transport (No. 7) of the said Notification, as follows:-
(a) The heading as “ (7) Minister of Transport & Civil Aviation”
(2) With reference to the Heading: Minister of Skills Development & V ocational Training (No. 18) of the said
Notification, as follows:-
(a) in Column II thereof, by the omission of items 9 and 13;
(b) in Column III thereof, by the omission of “Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education Act
No. 29 of 1995”;
