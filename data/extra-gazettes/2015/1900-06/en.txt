NORTH    CENTRAL    PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Removals made by the Hon. Governor of North Central  Province
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 154(f)4 of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, P. B. Dissanayake, Governor of the North Central Province hereby notify that the Hon. S. M.
Ranjith Samarakoon who was appointed to the office of the Chief Minister of the North Central Provincial Council with effect
from 24.09.2012, was removed from that office on 28.01.2015.
P. B. D
ISSANAYAKE,
Governor, North Central Province.
Governor's Office,
North Central Province,
28th January 2015.
