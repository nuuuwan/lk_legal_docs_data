 WAGES  BOARDS   ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES   BOARD  FOR THE   DOCK,   HARBOUR  AND   PORT  TRANSPORT   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under regulation 30 of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Mr. Chandana Wijayanama has been
appointed as Employer’s Representative and Mr. W. U. Priyasantha has been appointed employee’s representative of the
Wages Board  for the Dock, Harbour and Port Transport Trade in place of  Mr. Deshan Dewasagayam and Mr. P. D. Nimal
Karunathilake by the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 9 of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136). This appointment
is valid only up to 14.08.2016.
H
ERATH YAPA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
28th  April, 2015.
