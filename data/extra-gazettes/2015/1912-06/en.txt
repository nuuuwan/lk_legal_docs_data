THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 07 
of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose:-  
 
Description of  the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following four of land, situated in Divisional 
Secretariat Division of Walallawita in the District of 
Kalutara in Western Province show as Lot Nos. 2544, 
2545, 2547 and 2548 Supplementary No. 94 depicted in 
Final Village Plan No. 112 made by Surveyor General 
and situated in the Village of Pelawatta:— 
 
Lot No. 2544 
 
Name of the Land: Demodara Gan Kanda Pita 
Kattiya; Description: Road; Claimant: Kusumpala 
Walakulu-arachchi and D. C. Walakuluarachchi; Extent: 
0.0025 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 2545 
 
Name of the Land: Demodara Gan Kanda Pita 
Kattiya; Description : Road; Claimant: H. D. Gamini; 
Extent: 0.0120 Hectare. 
Lot No. 2547 
 
Name of the Land: Demodara Gan Kanda Pita 
Kattiya; Description: Home Garden; Claimant: D. C. 
Walakuluarachchi; Extent: 0.0077 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 2548 
 
Name of the Land: Demodara Gan Kanda Pita 
Kattiya; Description: Home Garden; Claimant: H. D. 
Gamini; Extent: 0.0011 Hectare.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired, are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at the Divisional Secretariat of  Walallawita  on 
the 10th June, 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby required 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
03rd June, 2015 describing the nature of the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquitsition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
               
 L. L. D.  T HILAKARATHNA,
