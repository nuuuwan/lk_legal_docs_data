PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE     EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE    DEMOCRATIC    SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI    LANKA - 11.07.2015
Greetings  !
WHEREAS,  in the exercise of powers vested in me by Article 33 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka a National Research Council was established by the Presidential Directive dated July 24, 2007 ;
Whereas, amendments  have been made to the said original directive from time to time ;
And whereas, Dr. Rohan Prasantha Perera Esquire, the Member of the said Council has resigned from the said
membership ;
And Whereas, the need to fill the vacancy of the said post has been identified ;
Now therefore, I,  Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
reposing great trust and confidence in the prudence, ability and fidelity of Dr. P. Nandalal Weerasinghe Esquire, Deputy
Governor of the Central Bank do hereby appoint the said Dr. P . Nandalal Weerasinghe Esquire as a member of the National
Research Council established by the said Presidential Directive :
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, on this 08th day of July, Two
Thousand and Fifteen.
                                                                                                          By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
