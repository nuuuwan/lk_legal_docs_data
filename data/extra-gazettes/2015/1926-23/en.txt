THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No. : VE/DS/AQ/R.D.A/164.
Land Ministry Ref. No. : 4-3/7/2014/HW/312.
I, hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 (Lot 01) allotments in total extent of about 0.0104
Hectare of land in Preliminary Plan No. Ya: 2820 and dated 15.07.2015
prepared by the Surveyor-Superintendent on behalf of Surveyor-
Jaffna, General situated in the Village of Saravanai in the
Gramaniladhari Divisions Saravanai West, J/21 in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Island South (Velanai) in Jaffna District in
the Northern Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Kaddu Nochchikuli and Nochchikuli; Description:
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Arumugam
Panchatcharam, Saravanai West, Velanai; Extent: 0.0104 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Island South (Velanai) on 27 th
day of August, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 20th day of August,
2015, the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the
amount of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
                                                             MANCHULADEVI  SATHEESAN,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat,
Island South Velanai.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Island South (Velanai).
03rd August, 2015.
