PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a  Vacancy  in  The  Northern   Province   Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Northern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Northern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Death  ;
And whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, fails to make a nomination within the specified
period, to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare elected,  Mr. Kandiah Sivanesan, who has secured the
highest number of preferences next to the last of the Members declared elected at the Northern Province Provincial Councils
Election which was held on 21st September 2013, as a member of the above Provincial Council from the Administrative
District of Mullaitivu  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of  Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
29th April, 2015.
