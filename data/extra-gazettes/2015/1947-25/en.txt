THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section  20(4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Section 20(4) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Eastern University, Sri
Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said University have failed to restore normal conditions,
do by this Order, appoint Professor Uma Coomaraswamy to be the Competent Authority with effect from January 1, 2016, for
the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of Vice-Chancellor any power, duty or function respectively
which has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to, the said Vice-Chancellor of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka under
the aforesaid Act or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN  KIRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education
and Highways.
Colombo,
23rd December 2015.
