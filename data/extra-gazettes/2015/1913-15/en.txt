THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice under Section 7
                                                                My No. : 3/3/2/83.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired :
Lot No. 153 - 181 (Lots 3) allotments in total  extent of
about 0.0222 Hectare, of land in Cadastral Map No. 520024,
Block No. 01 and Sheet No. 4  and dated 11.02.2015 prepared
by the  Senior - Superintendent of  Survey of  Colombo  District
on  behalf  of  Survey General, situated in  the Grama Niladhari
Division of 593A - Rilawala in the village of  Rilawala in
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Homagama in Colombo
District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 153
Name of  land: –; Description: Access Road; Claimant: Not
to be found;  Original Claimant: Not to be found; Extent: 0.0171
Hectare.
02. Lot No. 180
Name of  land: Godaparagahawatta; Description: Access
Road; Claimant: Not to be found;  Original Claimant: Not to be
found; Extent: 0.0026 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 181
Name of  land: Godaparagahawatta; Description: Garden;
Claimant:  Arambe  Gamaralalage  Sarath  Priyantha and
Chandralatha Hewa Palihakkara; Original Claimant: Arambe
Gamaralalage  Sarath  Priyantha and Chandralatha Hewa
Palihakkara, No. 95/3/1, Lokadarshana Mawatha, Higurala,
Puwakpitiya; Extent: 0.0025. Hectare.
 All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Homagama
on 03rd June, 2015 at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the  27th
May, 2015  the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars
of their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said
lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Homagama.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Homagama.
27th April, 2015.
