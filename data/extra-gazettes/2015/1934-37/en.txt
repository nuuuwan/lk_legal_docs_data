I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7, 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964 
(Amendment) that the Government intends to acquire the 
following land  which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Public purpose: 
 
Three allotments of land in total extent of 1.3130 
Hectares depicted as Lot No. 150, 151 and 152 in Sheet 
No. 7, Supplement No. 6 in the Final Village Plan No. 
193 prepared by the Superintendent of Surveys Ratnapura 
situated in the Grama Nila dhari Division No. 170 of 
Panawenna, Uda Pattuwa minor division in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Pelma dulla in Ratnapura District 
of Sabaragamuwa Province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
Schedule 
 
Lot No. 150 
 
Name of the Land: Neelagamawatta; Description: 
Home garden, 7 permanent buildings and a mix 
cultivation; Original Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation 
Company Ltd, No. 52, Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10;  
Extent: 0.6282 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 151 
 
Name of the Land: Neelagamawatta; Description: 
Home garden,  permanent buildings and a community 
building; Original Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation 
Company Ltd, No. 52, Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10;  
Extent: 0.5855 Hectare. 
Lot No. 152 
 
Name of the Land: Neelagamawatta; Description: 
Estate, a rubber plantation of 3 years old; Original 
Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation Company Ltd, No. 52, 
Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10;  Extent: 0.0993 Hectare. 
 
An allotment of land in total extent of 0750 Hectares 
depicted as Lot No. 583 in Sheet No. 17, Supplement  
No. 16 in the Final Village Plan No. 194 prepared by the 
Superintendent of Surveys Ratnapura situated in the 
Grama Niladhari Division No. 169 of Morathota, 
Nawadun Korale minor division in the Divisional 
Secretary's Division of Pelma dulla in Ratnapura District 
of Sabaragamuwa province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
Schedule 
 
Lot No.583 
 
Name of the land: Neelagama Tea Estate; Description: 
Estate, a rubber plantation of 10-15 years old; Original 
Claimant: Kahawatta Plantation Company Ltd, No. 52, 
Maligawatta Road, Colombo 10; Extent: 0.0750 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Pelmadulla 
Divisional Secretariat on 02nd  December, 2015 at 10.00  
a. m.  and are hereby reques ted to notify  me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 02nd December 2015,  the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
lands, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
