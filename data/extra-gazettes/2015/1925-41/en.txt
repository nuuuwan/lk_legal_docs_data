CHILA W   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
This  is  the Notice under  the  Article  of 24, 24(2) in Pradeshiya  Sabha  Act, No. 15th of  1987
IT is noticed that the minor mentioned in the Sabha will be declared as the minor roads belonged to Chilaw Pradeshiya
Sabha by Chilaw Pradeshiya Sabha of Puttalam District of North Western Province under the Article of 24 in Pradeshiya
Sabha Act, No. 15th of 1987.
If there are objections against this from the owners of the lands related to the measured and marked roads by the
Pradeshiya Sabha, it is noticed to Act according to 24(2) Article of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15th of 1987, to prove their
ownership within one month for the date of publishing Gazette.
