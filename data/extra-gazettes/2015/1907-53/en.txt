No. 1907/53 – FRIDAY, MARCH 27, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamylage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of  Lands, hereby declare under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land called ‘‘ Ethakada Kele’’ in total extent of
about 0.4047 Hectare, depicted as Lot  ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing
No. Aa/ MDW/ 2013/656  dated 20.09.2013, prepared by the
Surveyor General situated in the village  of  Ethakada (F.V .P. 627) in
Kadawath Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Medawachchiya in  Anuradhapura District in the North
Central Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  –  Ethakada Kele  –  Extent: About 0.4047  Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.) and Balance Portion of the Land claimed
by Chandrasekara Mudiyanselage Upali Chandrasekara, Kamala
Chandrasekara, Ukkubanda Chandrasekarage Nimal Chandrasekara,
Susila Chandrasekara, Malani Chandrasekara, Chandrasekarage
Sujatha Chandrasekara, Chandrasekarage Sarath Chandrasekara and
Others ; East, South and West : Balance Portion of the Land claimed
by Chandrasekara Mudiyanselage Upali Chandrasekara, Kamala
Chandrasekara, Ukkubanda Chandrasekarage Nimal Chandrasekara,
Susila Chandrasekara, Malani Chandrasekara, Chandrasekarage
Sujatha Chandrasekara, Chandrasekarage Sarath Chandrasekara and
Others.
