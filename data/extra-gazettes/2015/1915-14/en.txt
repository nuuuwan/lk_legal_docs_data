My No. 01/10/03/04.
BY virtue of powers vested to me  by Section 32 (1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of  1987, I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor
of Uva Province  do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
01. Mr. K. A. B. Senevirathne, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to act as Deputy Chief Secretary
(Engineering Service) for the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to his permanent post, with effect from the date
09.12.2014.
02. Mr. R. M. Piyadasa Rathnayake, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, As Director to
Education for the Uva Province, with effect from the date  01.07.2010.
03. Mr. M M. Wijenayake, Grade III Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Provincial Commissioner of
Local Government of Uva Province, on full time basis from the date  02.02.2015.
04. Mrs. M. E. Sumithra Silva, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Accountancy Service, to act as Deputy Chief Secretary
(Finance)  for the Uva Provincial Council, on full time basis with effect from the date 28.01.2015.
05. Mrs. R. M. S. D. Rathnayake, Grade I Officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the Duties of the
Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel & Training) for the  Uva Provincial Council, from the date 28.02.2015 to 15.03.2015,
till the return of Mrs. G . W. C. S. Priyabhashini, to the Island who is the, Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel & Training)
for the  Uva Provincial Council.
06. Mrs. Y . Kalai Arasi, Grade II Officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, to act as Additional Education
Director (Tamil Medium Education) in Tamil Medium Education Unit of the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs,
Textiles and Small Industries & Estate Infrastructure Development and Estate Education for the Uva Provincial
Council, on full time basis, with effect from the date  17.03.2015.
