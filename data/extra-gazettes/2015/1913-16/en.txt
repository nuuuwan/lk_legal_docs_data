NOTICE  UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(A)  OF   THE  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Colombo  Municipal  Council
I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of the Colombo Municipal Council, do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Nadil Dhushantha Malagoda of   the Colombo
Municipal Council has  vacated his office of member of that Council as he has ceased  to be a member of the United National
Party .
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
06th May, 2015.
