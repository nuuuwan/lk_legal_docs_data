NOTICE  No. : 11/0242 Anuradhapura District
It is hereby notified that any person having a claim to the owner-
ship or possession or interest to one or more of the parcels of lands
reflected in the Cadastral Map No. 110191 made by the Surveyor-
General under Section 11 of the Registration of Title Act No. 21 of
1998 relating to the Village of Ihalawewa. Yahalagama and Pahala
Galnewa (Block 12) or any part thereof, situated within the Grama
Niladhari Division of 276 - Kirikkulama in the Divisional Secretary's

failure to submit any claim before the above mentioned date, action
will be taken to hold an exparte inquiry to determine title to the
land and accordingly publish such determination in the Government
Gazette in terms of Section 14 of the above Act.
The Cadastral Map No. 110191 referred to above may be perused
at the relevant Grama Niladhari Office, Divisional Secretariat,
District Survey Office, Office of the Commissioner of Title
Settlement or the Surveyor - Generals' office.

the Divisional Secretary or the Commissioner of Title Settlement.
