THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  called ‘‘Rahatungoda Watta Rutland Division’’
in extent of about 0.0174  Hectare.  depicted in the  Advance  Tracing
No. NU/UDH/2013/400 dated 12.02.2014, prepared by the Surveyor
General  situated in the Village of Bulugahapitiya in Gannewa Korale
Minior Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Hanguranketha in Nuwara Eliya District of Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rahatungoda Watta Rutland Division – Extent:
About  0.0174 Hectare
North: Rahatungoda Watta Rutland Division claimed  by Janatha
Estate, Development Board, Road (RDA)  and Rahatungoda Watta
Rutland Division claimed  by Janatha Estate Development Board;
East and South: Rahatungoda Watta Rutland Division claimed  by
Janatha Estate Development Board; West: Rahatungoda Watta
Rutland Division claimed  by Janatha Estate Development Board
and Road  (RDA).
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My Reference No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/479.
Ministry of Lands,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
13th May, 2015.
