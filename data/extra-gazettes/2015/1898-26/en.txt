THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land

the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed

Road, Lot A and Metikandiya Watte claimed by the Government.

North: Metikandiya Watte claimed by Dept. of Sri Lanka Railway,
Metikandiya Watte claimed by Manjula Nisendra Gunasela

P. L. R. A. Jayawardena, Sahabanduwatte claimed by M. P. Lakshman
Rajaram and Lot E; South: Lot E and Galle Road; West: Galle Road
and Lot C.

North: Lot C, Wewkandiya Watte claimed by P. L. R. A.
Jayawardena and Sahabanduwatte claimed by M. P . Lakshman
Rajaram; East: Sahabanduwatte claimed by M. P . Lakshman Rajaram,
Sahabanduwatte claimed by Logal Perelta Alexander and Lot F; South:
