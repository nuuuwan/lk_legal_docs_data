THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land defined as ‘Lot A’ depicted in the Advance
Tracing No. KA/BND/2013/485 and dated 04.04.2014, prepared by
the Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Madupitiya in Minor

Bandaragama in Kalutara District, Western Province and bounded as
follows:

About 4.4490 Hectares

claimed by M. A. Perera and others.
