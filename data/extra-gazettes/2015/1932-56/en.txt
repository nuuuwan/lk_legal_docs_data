  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling of a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which the
member   belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Kariyawasam Pethangodage
Sisira Kumararathne  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Kariyawasam Pethangodage Sisira Kumararathne,
elected as a member of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Galle  in respect of which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
16th September, 2015.

PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling of a Vacancy in the Southern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which the
member   belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;

Chandana Krishantha  to fill such vacancy ;

Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Kankanamge Siribaddana Chandana Krishantha ,
elected as a member of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Matara  in respect of which such
vacancy occurred.
