NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order by virtue of the power vested in me under the Provisions of the Provincial Councils (Consequential
Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, read with the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 and
as amended under the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and
 Act,  No. 32 of 1983
R. P. D. Gunadasa Dehigama, Provincial Minister of Road, Transport, Co-operatives, Trade, Housing and Construction,
Rural Industries Development and Rural Development of North Western Provincial Council, do hereby delegate the
power vested with the Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western Provincial
Council, under the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended under the Provisions of the
Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 vof 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the rules framed thereunder,
thereafter, as per Column III and from the date as per Column II to the persons named as per Column I, to assist the
Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western Province.
R. P. D. G
UNADASA DEHIGAMA,
Provincial Minister of Road, Transport, Co-operatives,
Trade, Housing and Construction, Rural Industries Development
and Rural Development of North Western Province Provincial Council.
21st September 2015,
Kurunegala.
