FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
DIMBULAGALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of removal of member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, Sinhalage Jagath Samarawickrama belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of
that Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Wasala Mudiyanselage
Wala Gedara Janaka  Bandara being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Indika Hathurusingha,  Returning Officer of  Dimbulagaala Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Wasala Mudiyanselage  Wala Gedara  Janaka
Bandara elected as a member of the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA HATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Dimbulagala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
22nd April, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF    CHAIRMAN   OF   THE
LANKAPURA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman in Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of removal of chairman;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the   United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Athmadale Aluthwaththe Gedara Manju Sri Aluthwaththa the former chairman belonged was required by me under Section
65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs to such
party who shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Suraweera Arachchilage Bandula Samansiri to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Indika Hathurusinghe,  Returning Officer of  Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of
Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Suraweera Arachchilage Bandula Samansiri  elected as
a Chairman of the Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA HATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Lankapura  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
22nd April, 2015.

NOTICE   UNDER  SECTION  10A(1)(a)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
Thalawa  Pradeshiya Sabha
I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayake, Returning Officer of the Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the  Local Authorities   Elections  Ordinance (Chapter 262)  that Mr. Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Sarath Dissanayaka of the Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Pradeshiya
Sabha as he has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Thalawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Anuradhapura,
21st  of  April, 2015.
