CONTROL   OF    PESTICIDES   ACT,   No. 33  OF   1980
Order under Section 11
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 11 of  the Control of Pesticides Act, No. 33 of 1980, I, Gamage Anura
Wickramasiri Wijesekara, Registrar of Pesticides, do by this Order, in the interest of the public and on the advice of  the
Pesticides Technical and Advisory Committee, cancel every licence  issued in respect of Pesticides containing the active
ingredient bearing the Chemical Abstract Service Registry number specified in the Schedule hereto.
D
R. G. A. W. WIJESEKARA,
Registrar of Pesticides.
Peradeniya,
01st October, 2015.
