FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  DELFT   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Vice Chairman  in  Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Sebasthi Pethuru Arulappu  the former  Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Mariyasebastiyampillai Amaladorin, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Ponnuththurai Kuganathan,  Returning Officer of  Delft  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mariyasebastiyampillai Amaladorin  elected as Vice
Chairman of the  Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
P.  K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
27th  January, 2015.
