0.1211 Hectare.
02. Lot No. D29
Name of land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: (Part of the Lot No. B6154); Extent: 0.0020 Hectare.
03. Lot No. D30
Name of land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: (Part of the Lot Nos. B6152 and B6153); Extent:
0.1803 Hectare.
04. Lot No. D31
Name of land: –; Description: Road; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: (Part of the Lot No. B6150); Extent: 0.0376 Hectare.
05. Lot No. D32
Name of land: Gonnoruwa Bedda; Description: Garden and
Contains a permanent building; Claimant: State; Original
Claimant: Part of the Lot No. B6149 in possessed by Ganawathi
Ekanayaka, Gonnoruwa under Grant Licence No. Ham/1/Pra/
39735; Extent: 0.1125 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agents  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of
Hambantota on 04th day of December, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate)
on or before the  27th day of November, 2015, the nature of
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount
of compensation and the details of the computation of such
amount.
