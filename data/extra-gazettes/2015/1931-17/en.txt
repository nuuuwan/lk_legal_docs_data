THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land called ‘‘Pihille Ele Hena’’  in   total  extent
of  About 0.0624 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing No.
Maha/AKR/2014/560 dated 23.10.2014,  prepared by the Surveyor
General situated in the  Village  of  Dunuwila Viharagama in Sarasiya
Paththuwa  Korale Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Akurana in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded
as follows:
Lot ‘A’ – Phille Ele Hena – Extent: 0.0624 Hectare
North: Remaining portion of the said land and Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha); South: Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha) and remaining portion of the said land; West: Remaining
portion of the said land.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My Reference No.: 4-3/9/2014/WS/141.
Ministry of Lands
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
