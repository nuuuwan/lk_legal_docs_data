UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointment   made  by  the  Governor  of  Uva  Province
BY virtue of  powers vested to me under Section 154 F.   (4)  of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of  the Democratic
Socialist  Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, M. P. Jayasinghe,   Governor of the Uva  Province, do hereby announce that with effect
from the date 15th September, 2015, Hon. Arthur Chamara Sampath Dassanayake Don was appointed as  the Chief   Minister
of   the Provincial Council of  Uva Province.
M. P. J AYASINGHE,
Governor of  Uva Province.
Office of the Governor - Uva,
Badulla,
On this 15th day of September 2015.
