NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Declaration by the Hon. Governor of the North Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 8(b) of section 154(b) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Karunaratna Divulgane, Governor of the North Central Province declare hereby that the third session
of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province came to end on mid - night of November 10th 2014, and
By virtue of powers vested in me under paragraph 8(a) of section 154(b) of the aforesaid Constitution, declare
hereby that the fourth session of the sixth Provincial Council of the North Central Province shall meet at 10.00 a.m. on 09th
January, 2015, at the Conference Hall of the North Central Provincial Council.
K
ARUNARATNA DIVULGANE,
Governor,
North Central Province.
Governor’s Office,
North Central Province,
06th January, 2015.
