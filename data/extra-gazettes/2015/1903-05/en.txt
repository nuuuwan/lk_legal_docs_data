No. 57/1 of 2015
 P.S. No. CSA/7/6/2.
PROMOTION  AND  APPOINTMENT   APPROVED  BY   HIS   EXCELLENCY   THE   PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased promote Major General  A. W. J. C. de Silva RWP, USP,
ndu, psc to the rank of Lieutenant General and to appoint him under Article 61E(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of the Sri Lanka as the Head of Sri Lanka Army with effect from February 22, 2015.
                         By   His Excellency's command,
P.  B .  A
BEYKOON,
 Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
23rd February 2015.
