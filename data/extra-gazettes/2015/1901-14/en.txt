Notification
IT is hereby notified that I have in the exercise of powers vested in me under paragraph (1) (b) of Article 45 of the Constitution
of the  Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, determined that with effect from the date of this Notification, the
assignment of Duties and Functions, Departments, Statutory Institutions and Public Corporations to the Ministers appointed
under paragraph (1) (a) of Article 45, to be as indicated in the Schedule given below.
