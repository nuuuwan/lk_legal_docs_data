FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF    CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
PACHCHILAIPALLI    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in  Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Tamil United Liberation Front,  the recognized political party to which
Mr. Parnandu Dominic Anton the former  Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as a Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Mailvaganam Kumaravelu to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, R. C. Amalraaj, Returning Officer of  Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of
Section 65A(1) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of V acancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mailvaganam Kumaravelu  elected as Chairman of the
Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
R. C. A
MALRAAJ,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kilinochchi,
01 st  July,  2015.
