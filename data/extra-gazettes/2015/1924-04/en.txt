EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) AS 
AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
 
Reference No.: 10/3/1/3/54 
 
Land Ministry’s Reference No.: 4-3/9/2012/WS/306 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for the construction of the 
production tube wells and pump house of Udathenna 
water supply. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land described in below in Plan No. Ma 
1644. Approved by Matale Snr. Superintendents of 
Surveys, situated in Warapitiya village, Warapitiya 
Grama Niladari Division, in Sub Division of Matale 
Udasiya Pattuwa, in the Ratto ta Divisional Secretariat 
Division, in Matale District, in Central Province. 
Lot No. 1    
 
Name of Land: Thebilipola Hena or Thebilipolahena 
Watta; Description: Garden; Claiments: Mrs. Klera 
Munasinhe Siriwardana, Katudeniya, Warapitiya; Extent: 
0.1317 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid Land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Rattota on the 16th day of September, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 04th day of September, 2015. 
The nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensatio n for the requisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 B. M. G. B. W
IJEBANDARA, 
 Divisional Secretariat, 
 Rattota.
