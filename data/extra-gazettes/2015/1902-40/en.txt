 Notice under the Section 7 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7

 Lot No. 01

Commission; Extent: 0.1802 Hectares. 
 Lot No. 03  
Name of the land: Punakanda; Description: Home 
Garden, 55 permanent buildings; Original Claiment: 
Chairman Land Reforms Commission; Extent: 2.4433 
Hectares. 
  
 Lot No. 04  
Name of the land: Punakanda; Description: Home 
Garden, 01 permanent buildings; Original Claiment:

 All person interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents dully authorized in writing 
before me at Divisional Secretariat Akmeemana on the 
25th day of March, 2015 at 11.00 a.m. and are hereby

interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation and the details of the said land/servitude* 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
