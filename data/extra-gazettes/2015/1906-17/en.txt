MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE   IN  CRIMINAL   MATTERS  ACT,   No. 25  OF  2002
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters Act, No.
25 of 2002, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice and Labour Relations, do by this order declare that the provisions
of the aforesaid act shall apply in respect of the Agreement between the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka and the Government of the United Arab Emirates made at Abu Dhabi on the twenty seventh day of January Two
Thousand and Fourteen. This Agreement which was made between the Government of Sri Lanka and the Government of the
United Arab Emirates, which is a Non-Commonwealth Country, deals with mutual legal assistance in criminal matters and
concerns inter alia the improved effectiveness on the part of both parties to the Agreement through cooperation in relation
to the prevention, investigation, prosecution and suppression of crimes, including organized crimes within their respective
jurisdictions.
The Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1877/54 dated August 29, 2014 is hereby rescinded without
prejudice to  anything done thereunder.
W
IJEY ADASA RAJAPAKSHE,
 Minister of Justice and Labour Relations.
Colombo,
19th  February 2015.
