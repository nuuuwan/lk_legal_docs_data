NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments made by the Hon. Governor of the North Central Province
By virtue of powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, Karunaratna Divulgane,
Governor of the North Central Province, hereby notify that the officers named below were appointed to the respective posts
indicated in the North Central Provincial Public Service.
1. Mrs. S. M. S. Tennakoon – SLAS, Special Grade – as the Secretary to the Governor of the North Central
Province from 11.06.2014, until further notice.
2. Mr. R. M. Wanninayaka – SLAS, Special Grade – as the Secretary to the North Central Provincial Public
Service Commission, from 11.06.2014, until further notice.
3. Mr. R. G . A. Kularathna - SLAS, Special Grade – as the Secretary to the North Central Provincial Ministry of
Agriculture, Agro Product, Marketing, Animal Production, Animal Health, Fisheries, Environment and Provincial
Council Affairs, from 11.08.2014, until further notice.
4. Mr. L. J. M. G . C. Bandara - SLAS, Grade I – as the Secretary to the North Central Provincial Chief Ministry and
the Ministry of Provincial Law and order, Finance and Planning, Local Government Provincial Irrigation,
Provincial Road Development, Rural Intra – Structure Facilities, Sports and Youth Affairs, from 11.08.2014,
until further notice.
5. Mrs. D.H.M.N.P. Dasanayaka - SLAS, Grade I – to discharge the functions of the post of Secretary to the
North Central Provincial Ministry of Co-operative, Trade and Food, Small Industries Textile Industries, Rural
Development and Tourism, from 11.08.2014, until further notice.
6. Mr. P. K. Jayalath - SLAS, Grade I – as Deputy Chief Secretary (Administration) of the North Central Provincial
Chief Secretary Office from 14.08.2014, until further notice.
