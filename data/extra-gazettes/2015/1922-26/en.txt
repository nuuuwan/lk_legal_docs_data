THE   NATIONAL   THOROUGHFARES   ACT,   No. 40 OF   2008
Order under Section 11
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008, I, Kabir
Hashim, Minister of Highways and Investment Promotion, do by this Order designate the National Highway which was
designated as an “Expressway” by Order published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1920/36 of June 26, 2015 and
specified in the Schedule to this Order to be an “User Fee National Highway”.
The Order made under Section 11 of the aforesaid Act designating “Kottawa - Kothalawala Section of Outer
Circular National Highway (from 0.000 km to 9.900 km)” as an User Fee National Highway, and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary  No. 1850/33 of February 19, 2014 is hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
K
ABIR H ASHIM,
Minister of Highways & Investment Promotion.
Battaramulla,
07th July 2015.
SCHEDULE
AREA COMPRISING THE USER FEE NATIONAL HIGHW AY
The stretch of road being 19.245 kilometers long and 40.000 - 75.000 metres broad, situated between the
Kottawa interchange (0.000 km) and Kadawatha interchange (19.245 km) of the Expressway, designated as the Kottawa-
Kadawatha Section of Outer Circular National Highway.
