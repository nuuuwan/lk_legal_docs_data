PARLIAMENTARY GENERAL ELECTION - 2015
Facility for casting vote where there is a fear of a reasonable inability to cast vote at a given polling
station due to conditions prevailing in the area within which his polling station is situated
Paragraph 127 B of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981
PROVISIONS have been provided in paragraph 127 B of the Parliamentary Elections Act, No. 1 of 1981 as amended by the
Parliamentary Elections (Amendment) Act, No.15 of 1988 for any elector who reasonably fears that due to conditions
prevailing in the area within which his polling station is situated, that he is unable to cast his vote at such polling station, may
make an application requesting that he may be allowed to cast his vote at another polling station.
02. The relevant applications are available at all the District Returning Officers’ Offices, Divisional Secretariats and
all the Grama Niladharis’ Offices. Although the applications must be forwarded within one week of the notice of nomination
which was on 26
th June, 2015 to reach the Commissioner of Elections, Elections Secretariat, Sarana Mawatha, Rajagiriya, the
period has been extended till 10 th July, 2015. Therefore, the relevant applications shall be submitted to reach the aforesaid
address, on or before 10.07.2015.
03. The applicant by himself must quote his National Identity Card Number on the application and it must be
certified by the Grama Niladhari of the area, where he resides. Applications without the certification of the Grama Niladhari
will be rejected.
04. Applicants must furnish the relevant information based on the Electoral Register of the year 2014 and such
registers are available at all the District Secretariats/Kachcheris and District Election Offices.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
29th June, 2015,
Elections Secretariat, Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
