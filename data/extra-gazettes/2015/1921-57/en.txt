Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Reference  No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3961.  
  
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which is required for a public purpose. 
  
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired:— 
 
The following Land located in the Village of Kotuwegoda 
in Kotuwegoda South Grama Niladari Division (Division 
01) in Matara Divisional Secretariat Division, Matara 
District Southern Province, dipicted as Lot No. 01 in 
Preliminary Plan No. MR 2566, will be acquired for the 
construction of   quarters for the principle of St. 
Servaitius College matara. 
 
Schedule 
 
1. Lot No. 1-Name of the Land: Sinnakadirawatta; 
Current Land Use: Gard en, Two permanent 
buildings, one gate, one Water tank and Cement Well; 
Claimant: Government; Extent: 0.0739 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired  are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at the Matara Divisional Secretariat on 
the 16th day of July, 2015 at 10.30 a. m., and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing  (in duplicate) on or 
before the 09th day of July 2015  the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 R ANJITH YAPA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
 
19th June 2015, 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Matara.
