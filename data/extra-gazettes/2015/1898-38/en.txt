THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20(4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Prof. Rajiva Wijesinha,
the Minister of Higher Education, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Trincomalee Campus of
the Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously disrupted and that the Authorities of the Trincomalee Campus
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint with effect from January 15, 2015, Prof. Ranjith Premalal
De Silva to be the Competent Authority for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of Rector of
Trincomalee Campus any power, duty or function respectively which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to,
the said Rector of Trincomalee Campus under the aforesaid Act or any Order, Ordinance, Status, By-law, Regulation or
Rule made thereunder.
(P
ROFESSOR) RAJIV A WIJESINHA,
Minister of Higher Education.
Colombo,
21st January, 2015.
