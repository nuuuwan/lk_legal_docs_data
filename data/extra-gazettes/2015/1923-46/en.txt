No. 1923/46 – WEDNESDAY, JULY 15, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Dodamgashinnawatte in extent of
0.0633 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. Maha/PJP/ 2014/
612 and dated 05.12.2014, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Galhinna in the Minor Division of
Pallegampaha Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Poojapitiya in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Dodamgashinnawatte – Extent: About
0.0633 Hectare
North: Dodamgashinnawatte claimed by K. B. Ekanayaka; East:
Dodamgashinnawatte claimed by K. B. Ekanayaka and
Dodamgashinnawatte claimed by Muhammad Hussain Fausul Naima;
South: Dodamgashinnawatte claimed by Muhammad Hussain Fausul
Naima and Road (P .S.); West: Road (P .S.), Dodamgashinnawatte
claimed by State and Dodamgashinnawatte claimed by K. B.
Ekanayaka.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2014/WS/138.
Ministry of Lands,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
04th June, 2015.
