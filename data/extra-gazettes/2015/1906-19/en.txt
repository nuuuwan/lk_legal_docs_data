FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KEGALLE  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kegalle  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance to which
the former Member, Kudagoda Bopage Sarath Rupasena belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within  a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Kegalle  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Kaluarachchilage
Janaranjana Saman Kumara Kaluarchchi being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha, Returning Officer of  Kegalle  Pradeshiya Sabha  acting in terms
of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of V acancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kaluarachchilage Janaranjana Saman Kumara
Kaluarchchi elected as a member of the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.
G
AMALA THGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Kegalle  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kegalle,
17th March,  2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
DIMBULAGALA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Chairman  in Dimbulagala Pradeshiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  removal of
Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Sinhalage Jagath Samarawickrama the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Pushpa Kumara Ranasinghe to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Indika Hathurusingha, Returning Officer of  Dimbulagala  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Pushpa Kumara Ranasinghe  elected as
Chairman of the Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA HATHURUSINGHE,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office, Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha.
Polonnaruwa,
16th  March,  2015.
