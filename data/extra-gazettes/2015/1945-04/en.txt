FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  MAYOR OF   THE
KANDY   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  a Mayor in Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Mr. Thushantha Maheendra Rathwaththe of the former  Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Sena Dissanayaka to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning Officer of  Kandy
Municipal Council acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling
of vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Sena
Dissanayaka elected as  Mayor of the Kandy Municipal Council.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Kandy,
14th  December, 2015.
