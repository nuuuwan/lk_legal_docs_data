THE  FIRING  RANGE  AND   MILITARY   TRAINING   ACT, No. 24  OF 1951
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the power vested in me by Section 2 of the Firing Ranges and Military Training Act No. 24 of 1951 read with
Article 44(2) of the Constitution, I, Maithreepala Sirisena, Minister of Defence do by this Order, with the concurrence of
the Minister of Lands to establish a firing range specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, be known as 100 meters
Small Arms Firing  Range at Diyathalawa Air Force Training School. Further, the boundaries of the range shall be these
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule.
M
AITHREEPALA S IRISENA,
Minister of Defence.

before the commencement of firing at the 300 meters Small Arms Firing Range of Morawewa Air Force Camp, and at the
termination of firing, cause red flags to be  flown by day and all around red lights to be displayed by night at the following
points.
Butts and Stop Butts
Firing Points
At the turning point turning point junction to Morawewa Air Force Camp along Anuradhapura main road and at a
point ahed of the sub-guard room located in the road leading from Wewapara to Morawewa Air Force Camp.
05. During the period when read flags are flown or all around red lights are displayed at the 300 meters Small
Arms Firing Range at  Morawewa Air Force Camp, in accordance with regulations 3, no person other than the persons
