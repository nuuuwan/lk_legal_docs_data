BANK   OF  CEYLON   ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER 397)
Order under Subsection  (2) of Section 7
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by subsection (2) of section 7 of the Bank of Ceylon Ordinance (Chapter 397), I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order determine that the paid-up capital of the Bank of Ceylon shall be
increased from Rupees Five Billion to Rupees Ten Billion.
R
AV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Colombo,
22nd  July  2015.
