SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of  Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in respect
of the commodity items specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified in
the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 06 months commencing
from 21st November 2015, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act,
No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and  published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1931/7 of 07.09.2015 in respect of Potatoes (HS Code No. 0701.90), Gazette Extraordinary  No.
1935/45 of 09.10.2015 in respect of B’Onions (HS Code No. 0703.10.20), Gazette Extraordinary  No. 1934/32 of
01.10.2015 in respect of Fish (HS Heading No. 03.02 and 03.03), Gazette Extraordinary No. 1919/33 of 16.06.2015
and Gazette Extraordinary No. 1931/7 of 07.09.2015 in respect of Lentils (HS Code Nos. 0713.40.11, 0713.40.12,
0713.40.21, 0713.40.22) are rescinded with effect from 21st November 2015.
R
AV I K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
20th November, 2015.
