CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATIONS
The Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
ORDER   UNDER   SECTION   10 A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 10A of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235) as amended by Act,
No. 83 of 1988, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Finance Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka with the
approval of the Cabinet of Ministers, do by this order impose in respect of the commodity item specified in Column I of
the Shedule hereto a Surcharge in the interest of  the national economy, at the rates specified in the corresponding entries
in Column II of  that Schedule from 22nd September, 2015 till 08th October, 2015.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
21st September, 2015.
