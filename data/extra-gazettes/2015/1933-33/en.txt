WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
 Provincial  Road  Development  Authority  Statute  No. 04 of 1989  (Western  Province)
REMOV AL  FROM  OFFICE - CHAIRMAN, DIRECTOR  BOARD MEMBER OF  PROVINCIAL
ROAD  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  (WESTERN  PROVINCE)
ACCORDING to the powers entrusted in me by Section 3(8) in Part 01 of the Provincial Road Development Authority Statute
No. 04 of 1989 (Western Province), I, Kejitan Lalith Wanigarathne, Minister of Provincial Roads, Transport, Cooperative
Development and Trade, Fisheries, Housing and Constructions, Urban and Estate Sector Infrastructure Facilities, Industries
and Rural Development of the Western Province, do by this notice announce that Balage Don Vipula Rohana Kulasiri who
held office of Chairman in the Western Provincial Road Development Authority was removed with immediate effect from the
Chairmanship and membership of Western Provincial Road Development Authority, whereas, Don Mahinda Chithrasena
Ranaweera and Kahavitage Raja Sooriyakumara who held Director Board memberships in the said Authority were removed
with immediate effect from the Director Board membership of the said Authority.
K
EJITAN  LALITH  WANIGARATHNE,

Cooperative Development and Trade,
Fisheries, Housing and Construction,
