 SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY   ACT  No.  48  OF   2007
Order  under Section  2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I,
Ravi Karunanayake, Finance Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order.
(a) amend the Order made under aforesaid Act, and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1907/19 of March 25,
2015, (which Order has been valid for a period of over thirty days) by omitting from such order the commodity
bearing “H. S. Heading 10.06” and its corresponding entries appearing under the headings “H. S. Code”
“Description” and “Special Commodity Levy”
(b)  rescind the Order made under the aforesaid Act and published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1907/19 of March 25,
2015, (which Order has been valid for a period of over thirty days) with effect from 06th May 2015.
R
AVI KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of  Finance,
Colombo 01,
05th  May, 2015.
