 DECLARING  “SIR  ANAGARIKA  DHARMAPALA  YEAR”   INITIATING    FROM  17TH    SEPTEMBER  2014  AS
PER THE  CABINET   DECISION   No.    CP/14/0540/523/012
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by the cabinet decision No. CP/14/0540/523/012 dated 20th May 2014, I,  Joseph
Michael Perera, Minister of Home Affairs, do by this order declare the year commenced from 17th September 2014 as “Sir
Anagarika  Dharmapala Year”  in order to commemorate 150th Birth Anniversary of Sir Anagarika Dharmapala.
J
OSEPH MICHAEL PERERA,
 Minister of Home Affairs.
Ministry of Home Affairs,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07.
08th  of  May  2015.
