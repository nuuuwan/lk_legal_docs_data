THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land called ''Uduwara watta'' in total extent of
about 0.8628 Hectare described in the Advance Tracing
No. B/ELL/2014/765 dated 31st March 2015, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Halpe in Demodara
(68 - I) Grama Niladhari Division, in Kumbalwela Korale Minor
Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ella in Badulla
District, Uva Province  and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.0421 Hectare
North: Ela; East: Badulu Oya and Lot B; South: Badulu Oya, Lot
B and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and Ela.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.0375 Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.), Lot A and Badulu Oya; East: Badulu Oya
and Lot C; South: Lot C and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.)
and Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.0205 Hectare
North: Lot B and Badulu Oya; East: Badulu Oya and Lot D;
South: Lot D and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot B.
04. Lot ‘E’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.0288 Hectare
North: Lot D and Badulu Oya; East: Badulu Oya and Lot F;
South: Lot F and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot D.
05. Lot ‘F’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.1272 Hectare
North: Lot E and Badulu Oya; East: Badulu Oya and Lot G;
South: Lot G and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.) and Lot E.
06. Lot ‘G’ – Uduwara watta – Extent: About 0.3681 Hectare
North: Lot F and Badulu Oya; East: Badulu Oya and Lot H;
South: Badulu Oya, Lot H and Road (R.D.A.); West: Road (R.D.A.)
and Lot F.
