NORTHERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
 Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statute No. 04 of 2015
Be it enacted by the Northern Provincial Council as follows :
1. This Statute may be cited as the Supplementary Provision for Appropriation Statute
No. 04 of 2015.
2. Without prejudice to any other Law which authorizes for any such expenditure, the
expenditure of the Northern Provincial Council during the financial year begining
from 1st January 2015 and ending on 31st December 2015 referred to as the “Financial
year 2015” in this Statute re-allocation of Rupees One Hundred and Four Million
Nine Hundred and Fifty Seven Thousand Four Hundred and Eighty Eight and Seventy
Cents only (Rs. 104,957,488.70) from the expenditure the Honourable Governor
approved on the 22nd December 2014 and to the limits stated in the First Schedule
of this Statute shall be met :
A) From the Northern Provincial Council funds or monies, or
B) From the allocation of funds allocated in accordance to Article 154 (R) of the
Constitution of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and any other
funds allotted or granted by the  Government of Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka.
C. V . K
ANDIYA SIVAGNANAM, M. J EGU,
Chairman, Secretary,
Northern Provincial Council. Council Secretariat,
Northern Provincial Council.
