THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An  allotment  of land  called ‘Rajjammana Watta’ in  total extent
of about 0.1700 Hectare, depicted in the  Advance Tracing No. Ma/
MTL/2014/03 dated 26th June, 2014 prepared by the  Surveyor-
General, situated in the Village of  Rajjammana (F.V .P. 25) in
Gampahasiyapattuwa Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Matale  in Matale District, in Central Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Rajjammana Watta – Extent: About 0.1700
Hectare
North: Remaining portion of the same land, Road and Foot path;
East: Foot path and Ambanganga Reservation; South: Ambanganga
Reservation and  Remaining portion of the same land; West: Remaining
portion of the same land
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/425.
Ministry of Lands,
“Mihikatha Medura”,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
13th May, 2015.
