EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
IT is hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 
7 of the Land Acquition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
The land called Jangarakotuwawatta or Thalgaha-
mulawatta of about 0.1356 Hectare in extent. Depicted in 
the Surveyor General’s Prel iminary Plan No. M 1581 
situated in the Village of Dorakumbura of E 340 Dora-
kumbura Grama Niladhari Division, Minor Division of 
Asgiri Udasiyapattuwa at Di visional Secretary’s Division 
of Yatawatta of Matale District of Central Province. 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: —; Description: —; Claimant: 
Wijerathna Bandara Dorakumbura, No. 145/2 Lady Mc, 
Collum and Ekanayaka Mudiyanselage Chithra Padmini 
Kumari Dorakumbura, No. 11, College Place, Matale; 
Extent: 0.1356 Hectare. 
North: Jangarakotuwawatta or Thalgahamulawatta 
claiming by A. G. Ananda and Jangarakotuwawatta or 
Thalgahamulawatta claiming by Y. G. Loku Banda; East: 
Matale Attipola (Pradeshiya Sabha) Road; South: Janga-
rakotuwawatta or Thalgahamulawatta claiming by E. M. 
E. G. Senaviratna; West: Jangarakotuwawatta or Thal-
gahamulawatta claiming by A. G. Ananda. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in       
writing, before me at Yatawatta Divisional Secretariat,  
on the 26th day of January, 2015 at 10.00 o’ clock and are 
hereby requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) on 
or before the 12th  day of January, 2015 the nature of 
their interests in the land the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/             
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount.   
 
 A. M. J. K. N. A DIKARI,
