No. 1921/59 – FRIDAY, JULY 03, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 1.7455 Hectare
depicted as Lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No. Mu/MLL/2014/284 of
27.09.2014, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of Surveys-
Kilinochchi District, situated in the Village of Nayaru in the Grama
Niladhari Division No. MN/83 of Chemmalai East in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Maritimepattu in Mullaittivu District in
Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Nayarukani – Extent: 1.7455 Hectare
North: Nayarukani open waste land and Reservation for
Sea; East: Reservation for Sea and Nayaru Lagoon; South: Nayaru
Lagoon and Nayarukani Low Jungle; West: Nayarukani Low Jungle,
Path and Nayarukani open waste land.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My No.: 4-3/4/2013/D/216.
