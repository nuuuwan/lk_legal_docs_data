PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
REGULATION made by the Minister of Labour under Section 63 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136), and approved
by Parliament.
S. B. NAWINNE,
Minister of Labour.
Colombo,
06th May 2015.
Regulation
The Wages Boards Regulations 1971, published in the Ceylon Government Gazette No. 14961 of June 4, 1971 as last
amended by regulations published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1382/23 of March 4, 2005 is hereby further amended in
regulation 31 thereof as follows:-
1. in sub-paragraph (a) of that regulation by the substitution for the words "rupees four hundred" of the words
"rupees one thousand and five hundred"; and
2. in sub-paragraph (b) of that regulation by the substitution for the words "rupees five hundred" of the words
"rupees two thousand".
