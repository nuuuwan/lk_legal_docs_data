THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,   No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section  27(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, do by this Order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order establishing the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, which order is deemed, by virtue of
the provisions of Section 139 of the aforesaid Act, to be an Order made under Section 21 of the said Act, (as last amended
by Order published in Gazette Extraordinary , No. 1908/24 of March 31, 2015) by the addition immediately after the
entry “Faculty of Commerce and Management”appearing in Column I and its corresponding entries in Column II thereof,
of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty of Computing and Technology 1. Department of Software Engineering
2. Department of Computer Systems Engineering
3. Department of Applied Computing”.
L
AKSHMAN  K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education
and Highways.
