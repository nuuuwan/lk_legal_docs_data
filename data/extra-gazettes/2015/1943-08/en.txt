NOTIFICATION   UNDER   SECTION   16   SUB - SECTION  2 (D)  OF   THE
COAST  CONSERV ATION  AND  COASTAL  RESOURCE   MANAGEMENT  ACT No.  57  OF  1981
Proposed Colombo Port City Development Project
SUPPLEMENTARY   ENVIRONMENTAL   IMPACT   ASSESSMENT    REPORT
IT is hereby notified that the Report of  Supplementary Environmental Impact Assessment study on proposed Colombo
Port  City Development Project submitted by the Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development, 1st Stage, 3rd Floor,
Sethsiripaya, Battaramulla as required by the Section 16 (1) of  the Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Manage-
ment Act, No. 57 of 1981 will be available for inspection by the public at the following institutions from 8.30 a.m. to 4.15
p.m. for a period of 30 days from 01.12.2015. (except weekends and public holidays).
1. Ministry of Mahaweli Development and Environment, No. 500, T. B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10.
2. Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Department, New Secretariat Building, 4th Floor,
Maligawatte, Colombo 10.
3. Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resource, New Secretariat Building, 3rd Floor, Maligawatte,
Colombo10.
4. Central Environmental Authority, No. 104, “Parisara Piyasa”, Densil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
5. District Secretariat, Narahenpita, Colombo 05.
6. Divisional Secretariat (Colombo), Dam Street, Colombo 12.
7. Divisional  Secretariat (Thimbirigasyaya), Narahenpita, Colombo 05.
8. Divisional Secretariat, Wattala.
9. Colombo Municipal Council, Colombo.
10. Pradeshiya Sabhawa, Wattala.
11. Regional Office, Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Department, Custom Road,
Negambo.
