FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
HOMAGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Homagama   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of  the Party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  People's  Liberation Front, to which
the  Former  member  W. M. Ajith Premakumara  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member
of that  Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected  as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Susith
Samarawickrama Liyanage  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I,  Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardana ,  Returning Officer of  Homagama   Pradeshiya
Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Susith Samarawickrama
Liyanage  elected as  a member of the  Homagama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
 V
AJIRA G UNAWARDANA,
Returning Officer,
Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Colombo,
24th June  2015.
