FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  DEPUTY MAYOR
OF THE  MATARA   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Deputy Mayor in Matara Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Deputy Mayor has
been appointed as  Mayor ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to
which  Deegoda Gamage Yasarathna  the former  Deputy Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Matara Municipal  Council who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as  Deputy Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Kodippili Yakdehiyage Hareendra Ruwan Chinthaka, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE   I,  Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara,  Returning Officer of  Matara Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kodippili Yakdehiyage
Hareendra Ruwan Chinthaka  elected as Deputy Mayor of the  Matara Municipal  Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Matara,
21st  December, 2015.
