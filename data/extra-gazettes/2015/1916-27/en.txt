  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the North Western  Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Western   Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  North Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Appointing  the Member of  Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which the
member   belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  A. A. Nimal Karunathilake
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  A. A.  Nimal Karunathilake, elected as a member of the
above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Kurunagala  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
26th May, 2015.
