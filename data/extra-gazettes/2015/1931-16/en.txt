No. 1931/16 – WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 09, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Ref. : අƮ/කƍ/130/UDA. 
   
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for the public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land /Servitude to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of  Lands containing in extent of 
0.0683 Hectares depicted in preliminary Plan No. Co. 
9546 prepared by Superintendent of Surveyor of 
Colombo situated in Battaramulla village, within the 
Grama Niladari Division of 492/Sri Subothipura, 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kaduwela in the 
Colombo District, Western Province.  
 
Lot No. 01 and 02 in P P Co. 9546 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Polduwa; Present land use: 
Garden; Owner: State; Previous Owner: Thanthiriwattage 
Dona Nandani Wajirapani, No. 171, Moratuwa Road, 
Suwarapola, Pilyandala; Extent: 0.0682 Hectare. 
02. Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: Polduwa; Present land use: 
Garden; Owner: State; Previous Owner: Thanthiriwattage 
Dona Nandani Wajirapani, No. 171, Moratuwa Road, 
Suwarapola, Pilyandala; Extent: 0.0001 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land  are hereby 
required to appear personally, or agents duly authorized 
in writing, before me at the Kaduwela Divisional 
Secretariat on the 12th day of October, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before 05th day of October, 2015 the 
nature of them interested in the Land, the particular of 
their claimed for Compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 
 W.M. DAYAWATHI, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kaduwela. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Kaduwela, 
10th September, 2015.
