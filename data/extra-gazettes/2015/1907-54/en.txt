FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Colombo Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely Democratic Peoples Front to which the
former member, Thiagaraja Nirosh belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that Colombo Municipal
Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Aananthan Murugaiah
Thirubagara Kumar being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Colombo Municipal Council acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Aananthan Murugaiah Thirubagara Kumar
elected as a member of the Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
26th  March, 2015.
