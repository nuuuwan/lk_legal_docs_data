PRESIDENTIAL  ELECTIONS ACT, No. 15 OF 1981
Notice Under Section 64(2)
I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, do hereby notify under Section 64(2) of the Presidential Elections Act,
No. 15 of 1981, that, Mr. Maithripala Sirisena has been declared elected as the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, under Section 56 of the aforesaid Act.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
