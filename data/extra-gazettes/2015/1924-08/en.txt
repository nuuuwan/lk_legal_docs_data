THE  NATIONAL  THOROUGHFARES  ACT,  NO.  40  OF  2008
REGULATIONS made by Minister of Highways and Investment Promotion under section 89 (2) (c) of the Thoroughfares
Act, No. 40 of 2008 read with Sections 13 and 14 of the aforesaid Act.
 KABEER HASHIM,
Minister of Highways and Investment Promotion
Battaramulla,
17th July 2015.
Regulations
1. These Regulations may be cited as the National Thoroughfares (User Fee National Highway) Regulations
No. 2 of 2015.
2. These regulations shall apply to -
(a) the “Southern User Fee National Highway” declared by Order made under section 11 of the National
Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1731/2 of November, 8, 2011;
(b) the “Galle - Matara User Fee National Highway” declared by Order made under Section 11 of the National
Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1850/33 of February, 19, 2014;
and
(c) the “Kottawa – Kadawatha Section of Outer Circular User Fee National Highway” declared by Order
made under Section 11 of the National Thoroughfares Act, No. 40 of 2008 published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1922/26 of July, 8, 2015.
3. (1) There shall be levied from a driver of every vehicle being used on the aforesaid User Fee National Highways,
a fee in respect of the use of the User Fee National Highway by such driver, at such rates specified in Part A of the Schedule
to these regulations.
(2) The fee shall be charged in respect of the whole distance, or part thereof, of the User Fee National Highway.

4. (1) A driver of every vehicle who enters a User Fee National Highway from an interchange shall, prior to being
permitted to proceed along such highway, obtain a ticket from the Teller on duty, at such interchange.
(2) The driver of a vehicle which has been proceeding along the User Fee National Highway, when arriving at the
gate of an interchange for the purpose of exiting such User Fee National Highway shall surrender to the Teller the ticket
obtained at the interchange through which he entered.
(3)  The Teller shall inform the driver of the amount of the fee payable and accordingly the driver shall pay such
amount, prior to being permitted to pass through such interchange, and the teller shall issue a receipt in respect of such
payment.
5. (1) A driver of every vehicle shall keep the ticket referred to in regulation 4 (1), securely in his possession
until he reaches of the interchange through which he intends to exit such User Fee National Highway.
(2) Where the driver is unable to surrender the ticket to the teller, the driver shall be deemed to have travelled the
entire distance of the User Fee National Highway from the furthest possible point of entry to the actual point of exit and
shall accordingly be charged a fee at the rates specified in part A of the schedule to these regulations.
6. (1) The Road Development Authority, established under the Road Development Authority Act, No. 73 of 1981,
(hereinafter referred to as “the Authority”) shall ensure that, for the purpose of implementation of the provisions of these
regulations, sufficient number of gates are positioned at the required places along the User Fee National Highways.
(2) The gates so positioned along the User Fee National Highway shall be owned, operated and maintained by the
Authority, and each gate shall be manned by a Teller.
(3) The Teller shall be responsible for the collection of the user fee at every gate referred to in  paragraph (2), in
such sum as is specified in Part A of the Schedule to these regulations from every driver using such User Fee National
Highway :
Provided that the provisions of these regulations shall not apply to a driver who is driving a vehicle of a category
specified in Part B of the Schedule to these regulations, which is exempt from such payment.
7. The Teller shall at such times as are specified, hand over the day’s collection of the fees to the officer authorized
in that behalf by the Authority and obtain a receipt in respect of such amount.
8.  The money so collected as user fee shall be credited to the Fund of the Authority and be used for the purpose
of developing and maintaining the User Fee National Highway to which these regulations apply.
9. For the purpose of these regulations-
“Teller” means an officer who is manning the gates at an interchange;
“User Fee National Highways” includes the Southern User Fee National Highway from Kottawa (0.000km)
to Pinnaduwa (95.275km), Galle - Matara User Fee National Highway from Pinnaduwa(95.275km) to
Godagama (124.800km) and Kottawa - Kadawatha Section of  Outer Circular User fee National Highway
from Kottawa (0.000km) to Kadwatha (19.245km)
10.  The driver of a vehicle who contravenes the provisions of these regulations shall commit an offence and
shall on conviction after summary trial by a Magistrate be liable in the case of-
(a) a first offence, to a fine not exceeding five thousand rupees; and
(b) a second or subsequent offence, to a fine not exceeding fifty thousand rupees.
11. The National Thoroughfares (User Fee) Regulations No. 1 of 2014 published in the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1852/36 of March, 05, 2014 is hereby rescinded without prejudice to anything done thereunder.
