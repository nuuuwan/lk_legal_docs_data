CENTRAL   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Central Provincial Passenger Transport  Services  Authority Statute  No. 01 of 2000
REMOVAL OF MEMBERS APPOINTED
I,  Ediriweera Weerawardena, the Minister of the Ministry of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy and Housing
and Construction of the Central Province Provincial Council, hereby announce that the  following  member of  the board
of  directors  appointed through the  Extraordinary Gazette  No. 1833/ 3 on 21.10.2013 as per the powers vested on me
by the 6 (a) and 6 (e) clauses in the  Central Provincial Passenger Transport Services Authority Statute No. 01 of  2000 is
removed from the post of  Chairman/ Member of  The Board of   Directors of   Central Province Provincial Passenger
Transport Services Authority, as per the powers vested on me by the 12 th clause of the same statute.
E
DIRIWEERA WEERAW ARDENA,
Minister of Road Development, Transport,
Power and Energy and Housing and Construction of the
Central Province.
