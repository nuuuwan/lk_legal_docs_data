THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)           
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT No. 28 of 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
   
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands/Servitude* which are required 
for the public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be acquired: 
 
Six (6) allotments below name land shown in 
Preliminary Plan No. Ku. 2300 and 27.02.2014, prepared 
by the Surveyor General, situated in the village of 
Kuleepitiya and Minor Division of Udapola Othota East 
Korale in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 933 
Serapiecewatta in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Polgahawela, in the District of Kurunegala in North 
Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: — ; Description: Road; Claimant: 
State; Extent: 0.0003 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 2

State; Extent: 0.0085 Hectare. 
3. Lot No. 3 
Name of the Land: — ; Description: Road; Claimant:

4. Lot No. 4

5. Lot No. 5 
Name of the Land: — ; Description: Garden; 
Claimant: State; Extent: 0.0040 Hectare. 
 
6. Lot No. 6
