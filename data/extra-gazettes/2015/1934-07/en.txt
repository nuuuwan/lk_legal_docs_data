L.D.B.21/ 78 (xiv)
THE  UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under  Section 20 (4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20(4) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I,  Lakshman Kiriella, the Minister of University Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of
the affairs  of  the Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of  the  said
University have failed   to restore normal conditions, do by  this Order, appoint Prof.  Uma Coomarasamy to be the
Competent Authority with effect from October 1, 2015,  for the purpose  of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of
Vice- Chancellor any power, duty or function respectively which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to,
the  Said Vice- Chancellor of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka under the aforesaid Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute,
By law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of University  Education and Highways.
Colombo,
25th  September  2015.
