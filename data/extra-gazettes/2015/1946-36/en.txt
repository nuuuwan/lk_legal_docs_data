No. 1946/36 –   WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 23, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION (AMENDMENT)  
ACT No. 28 OF 1964 (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
            Ref. No.: DIK/LAN/2/30/29. 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, (amendment) 
that the Government intends to acquire the following land 
which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land to be  obtained: 
 
Following lands mentioned in the preliminary plan 
No. 2640 of surveyor General Matara and situated in 
village of Bambaranda Central and Bambaranda south, 
Grama Niladhari Division of Rathmale in Dickwella 
Divisional secretariat of Matara District. 
 
1. Lot No. 1  
Name of the Land: Kankanamboralassa; Description: 
Home Garden, There are 1 -5 year age teak tree, coconut 
tree and 5 -10 year age jack tree; Claimant: Dandeniya 
Arachchige Sunil, Boralassa, Rathmale, Matara; Extent: 
0.0356 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 2  
Name of the Land: Suraweeragemakeeriya; 
Description: Paddy field; Claimant: Thushil Sampath 
Kumara Kulathunga, Katukurundugahahena, Rathmale, 
Matara; Extent: 0.0710 Hectare. 
3. Lot No. 3  
Name of the Land: Geeganadedara; Description: Home 
Garden; Claimant: Pathma Kulathunga Rajapaksha, 
Katukurundugahahena, Rathmale, Matara; Extent: 0.0151 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Dickwella 
Divisional Secretary’s Office on 25th January, 2016 at 
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before 16th day of January, 
2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. All 
deeds and documents in support of the claims should be 
produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
 B. S. RANJITHA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretariat Dickwella. 
  
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Dickwella, 
16th December, 2015.
