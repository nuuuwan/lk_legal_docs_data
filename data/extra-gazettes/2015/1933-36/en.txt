THE   LAND   ACQUISITION   ACT   (CHAPTER   460)
Notice  under  Section  7
Land Ministry Reference No. : 4-3/07/2013/HW/104.
My Ref. No. : 4/3/1/3769/23.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire
the following lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 allotments in total extent of about 0.0008 Hectare
of land in Preliminary Plan No. Ka:4474 and Prepared by the
Kalutara District Surveyor - Superintendent on behalf of
Surveyor- General situated in the Village of  Kalutara North in
the No. 717 - Deshasthra of Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Kalutara in Kalutara District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Bulugahawatta; Description: Road; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: D. S. Weerakkodi, Assmt. No. 1, 3
and 5, Kalutara North, ; Extent: 0.0008 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Kalutara
on 14th day of October  2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before
the 07th day of October 2015, the nature of their interests in
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for
the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
