THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of lands in total  extent of about 5.894 Hectares,
depicted  in Lot Nos. 1 and 2 in Advance Tracing No. 96/251 and dated
22.10.1996 prepared by Surveyor General’s and situated in the Village
of Dodangoda West in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Dodangoda,
in the District of Kalutara in Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot No. 1 – Millakanda Pamula Kattiya–  Extent: 3.426
Hectares
North: Road and Millakanda Kele claimed by W. Kotalawala;
East: Millakanda Kele claimed by W. Kotalawala ; South: Millakanda
Kele claimed by W. Kotalawala and Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya
claimed by D.C. Wickramasinhe; West: Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya
claimed by D.C. Wickramasinhe, Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya
claimed by Siriwardanage Sisilin and others and Road.
02. Lot No. 2 – Millakanda Pamula Kattiya–  Extent: 2.468
Hectares
North: Road and Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya claimed by
Siriwardanage Sisilin and others and Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya
claimed by D.C. Wickramasinhe; East: Millakanda Pamula Pitakattiya
claimed by D.C. Wickramasinhe ; South: Millakanda Pamula
Pitakattiya claimed by D.C. Wickramasinhe and Diyaparagahamanana
Kumbura claimed by K. C. Martin; West: Diyaparagahamanana
Kumbura claimed by K. C. Martin, Keenagasmanana Millawa claimed
by T. Akmon and Road.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
Ref. No.: 13/ 96/ AS/ 93.
