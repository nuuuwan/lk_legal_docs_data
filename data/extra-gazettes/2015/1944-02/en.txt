UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70C of the aforesaid Act, in respect
of the Nagananda International Institute for Buddhist Studies (pvt) Ltd. a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under  the
Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 94125, do by this Order, recognize the Nagananda
International Institute for Buddhist Studies (pvt) Ltd. as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher
Education therein, leading to the award of the Degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the
abbreviations are specified in the corresponding entries  in Column II of the Schedule hereto.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degrees Abbreviations for Qualifications
Bachelor of Arts in Buddhist Studies BA (Buddhist Studies)
Bachelor of Arts (Special) in Buddhist Studies BA (Spl) (Buddhist Studies)
Post Graduate Diploma in Buddhist Studies PGDip (Buddhist Studies)
Master of Arts in Buddhist Studies MA  (Buddhist Studies)
Master of Philosophy in Buddhist Studies MPhil  (Buddhist Studies)
L
AKSHMAN K IRIELLA,
Minister of Higher Education and Highways.
