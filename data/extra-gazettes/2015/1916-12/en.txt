THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Order under Section 33 (1) (b)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me under Section 33 (1) (b) of  the Wages Boards Ordinance,  I, S. B. Nawinne, Minister of
Labour do by this order authorize  the Commissioner of  Labour to determine a general minimum  time rate for Workers in  the
Flowers, Ornamental Plants, Vegetables and Fruits growing and  Export Trade.
S. B. N
AWINNE
Minister of Labour.
Ministry of Labour,
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
 20th May 2015.
