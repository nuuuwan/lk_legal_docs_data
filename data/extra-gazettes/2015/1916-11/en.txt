THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  called ‘‘Pawalamalai Estate’’ in extent of
about 0.0090 Hectare, depicted as Lot A in the amended Advance
Tracing No. NU/AMB/2011/200 dated 26th November, 2013,
prepared by the Surveyor General  situated in the Village of
Ginigathhena in Ambagamuwa  Korale Minor Division in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Ambagamuwa Korale in Nuwara
Eliya District of Central Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pawalamalai Estate – Extent: About
0.0090 Hectare
North and East: Lot No. 29 in F. V . P . 106; South: Lot No. 29
in F. V . P. 106 and Road (Pradeshiya Saba); West: Road
(Pradeshiya Saba).
