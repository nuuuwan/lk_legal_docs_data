by Acts, No. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (Read
with Industrial Disputes) Special Provisions (Act, No. 37
of 1968) appointed me as the arbitrator and referred the
following  dispute for settlement by arbitration by his order
dated 10th June 2014.
The matter in dispute between the parties is : -
“Whether Mr. U. S. S. K. Rudrigo working as Leader
Fireman - Grade C-l, at the Fire and Beware
Division of the Ceylon Petroleum Storage
Terminals Ltd. has been caused injustice by being
given No pay leave when he applied for leave on
14.09.2011 and if so, to what relief he is entitled.”
Applicant was represented by Mr. Ananda Palitha, Branch
Secretary of the Jathika Sevaka Sangamaya. The respondent
organization was represented by Attorney at Law Miss
Melani Kudaliyanage. After brief discussion both parties
agree to reslove the matter in dispute on the following basis
a) The day in question, 14.09.2011, which day the
applicant requested casual leave, is approved ; and
b) Salary and other dues, arising out of the day in
question, will be restored.
Both parties sign the notes in my presence confirming
the agreement.
This is just and fair award.
P. N
A VARA TNE,
Arbitrator.
20th July 2015.
