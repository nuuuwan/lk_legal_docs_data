PARLIAMENTARY GENERAL ELECTION - 2015
Notice about the termination of accepting applications , on 14.07.2015 for obtaining the facility to cast the
votes of presons, when there is a fear of a reasonable inability to cast vote at  a given polling station due to
conditions prevailing in the area within which his/her polling station is situated
THE period for receiving of applications of the electors, who reasonably fears that he/she is unable to cast the vote at the
allotted polling station due to conditions prevailing in the area within which his/her polling station is situated and requesting
to cast their votes at another polling station, which was to be terminated on 10.07.2015 is extended till 14.07.2015 after
considering the requests made by the general public, civil organizations and political parties.
02. This last date will not be extended again under any circumstance. Therefore, the registered voters in the electoral
register, 2014, who have fulfilled the aforesaid qualification, but have not applied for the above facility by now, are kindly
requested to submit the duly prefect applications together with the certification of the Grama Niladhari of the area where
he/she resides to reach the Elections Secretariat or the nearest District Elections Office on or before 14.07.2015.
03. Applications that reached after 14.07.2015 either by hand or by post will not be accepted under any circumstance
and those applications will have to be rejected as delayed applications.
MAHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
11th July, 2015.
