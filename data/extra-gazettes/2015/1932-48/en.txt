NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification made by the Governor of North Western Province
Amara Piyaseeli Rathnayaka being the Governor of the North Western Province in terms of power vested in me under
Sub-section (4) and (5) of Section 154(f) of Amendment Act, No. 13 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, do hereby proclaim that New Mini Cabinet of the North Western Province was sworn in/affirmed before me at
Colombo on this 14th September, 2015 on the following posts in terms of Section 165 and Section 157(a)7 of the Constitution
of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
Proclaimed at Kurunegala on this Forteenth day in September of Two Thousand Fifteen.
A
MARA PIY ASEELI RATHNAYAKA,
 The Governor of the North Western Province.
