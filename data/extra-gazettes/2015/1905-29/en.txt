FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
PANNALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, S. A. Piyasena belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that Pannal Pradeshiya
Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Mohomed Samun
Mohomed Safwan being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama,  Returning Officer of  Pannala
Pradeshiya Sabha acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Mohomed
Samun Mohomed Safwan  elected as a member of the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
10th  March, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
ERA VUR    URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Eravur Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of appointed to Member of Eastern
Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance  to which
the former member,  Ali Zahir Moulana Seyed belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Eravur
Urban Council who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Meera Sahibu Mohamed
Nasir  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Abdul Majeed Mahammadu Kabeer,  Returning Officer of  Eravur Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Meera Sahibu Mohamed Nasir elected as a
member of the Eravur Urban Council.
A. M. M. K
ABEER,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
10th  March, 2015.
