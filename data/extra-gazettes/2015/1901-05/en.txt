No. 1901/5 – TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 10, 2015
THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotments of land Called Thannahena Estate in total extent of
about 8.3 Hectares, Shown as Lots A, B in Advance Tracing No.
MR/PTB/ 2014/ 190 dated 28th August, 2014, prepared by the
Surveyor-General, situated in the Village of Pitabeddara (F.V .P. 6)
Gramaniladari Division of Thannahena in Morawakorale Minor
Division in Pitabeddara Divisional Secretary's Division in Matara
District in Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Thannahena Estate – Extent: 4.0718 Hectares
North: Thannahena estate claimed by Namunukula plantation
limited and Galpoththa Dola; East: Canal, 16 Paddy field of
Thannahena claimed by G . K. Swarnapala, K. G . Nandasena and
Thannahena estate claimed by Namunukula plantation limited; South:
Road, canal, 16 paddy field of Thannahena claimed by G . K.
Swarnapala; West: Galpoththa Dola Thannahena estate claimed by
Namunukula plantation limited, Road and canal.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Thannahena Estate – Extent: 4.2282 Hectares
North: Road, Thannahena estate claimed by Namunukula plantation
limited, 16 paddy field of Thannahena claimed by W. somasiri and
T. G . Somadasa; East: Thannahena claimed by R. A. Gunapala and
Others; South: Thannahena estate claimed by Namunukula plantation
limited, Kathigana ela hena claimed by K. G. Nandasena, T. Jayathissa,
Road, Thannahena claimed by R. A. Gunapala and Others; West:
Thannahena Estate claimed by Namunukula plantation limited, 16
paddy field of Thannahena claimed by W. Somasiri and T. G .
Somadasa, K. G . Nanadasena and G . K. Swarnapala.
