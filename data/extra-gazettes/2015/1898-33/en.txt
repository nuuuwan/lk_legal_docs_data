THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is
needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions
of that Act.
An allotment of  land in total extent of about 5.6433 Hectares
depicted as Lot 'A' in the Advance Tracing No.  Mu/MLL/2014/285
and dated 27.09.2014, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of
Survey’s-Kilinochchi District situated in  the Grama Niladari Division
No. Mul/83 of Chemmalai East in the Village called Chemmalai  in
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Maritimepattu of Mullaithivu
District in Northern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ –  Kalliyadiyikadu – Extent: 5.6433 Hectares
North: Kalliyadiyikadu Low Jungle and Reservation for Sea; East:
Reservation for Sea, Kalliyadiyikadu Low Jungle; South:
Kalliyadiyikadu Low Jungle and Path; West: Path, Kalliyadiyikadu
Open waste land.
Janaka BandaraThennakoon
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
