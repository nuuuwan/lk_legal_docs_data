Notice under the Exchange Control Act (Chapter 423 of the CLE)
PERMISSION   IN   TERMS  OF   SECTION  7  OF  THE   EXCHANGE CONTROL    ACT
(1) NOTICE is hereby given under Section 7 of the Exchange Control Act read with Section 3 of the said Act that
permission is granted to persons resident in Sri Lanka who maintain Non Resident Foreign Currency Accounts (NRFC),
Resident Foreign Currency Accounts (RFC), Resident Non-Nationals’ Foreign Currency Accounts (RNNFC) and Foreign
Exchange Earners’ Accounts (FEEA) to make payments out of balances lying in above accounts to persons resident outside
Sri Lanka to:
(i) acquire securities permitted by the Minister of Finance by Order published in the Government Gazette
(Extraordinary) No. 1947/13 dated 29.12.2015 ; and
(ii) acquire any other asset of capital nature or for current account transactions as per the Operating Instructions
No. EC/06/94 and guidelines No. EC/D/GL/1994/2 dated 18.03.1994 issued to Authorised Dealers on removal
of Exchange Control restrictions on current (non-capital) transactions.
M
RS. T. M. J. Y . P . FERNANDO,
Controller of Exchange.
