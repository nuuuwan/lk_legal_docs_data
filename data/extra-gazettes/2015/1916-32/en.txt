EXTRAORDINARY 
THE  LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No. : KWDS/LND/01/ACO 02/2013. 
Land Ministry No.: 4-3/5/2013/HW/525. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Land Acquisition 
(Amendment) Act. No. 28 of 1964, that the Government 
intends to acquire the following land/servitude which is 
required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The Allotment of known as ELLAGAWA 
KUMBURA and shown as Lot 467 Sheet Number 18 and 
Supplement Number 16 (01 block) of land in total extent 
of about 0.0049 hectare F.V.P. 245 situated 
Wewelkandura Village and Wewelkandura Grama 
Niladhari Division in Medapattu of Kukulu Korale in 
Kalawana Divisional Secretariat Division of Ratnapura 
District, Sabaragamuwa Province.  
01. F.V.P. 245 - Sheet No. 18 and Supplement No. 16, 
Lot No. 467 
 
Name of Land: Ellagawa Kumbura; Claimant’s Name 
and Address: D. W. Pathmini Witharana, Wewelkandura, 
Kalawana; Extent: 0.0049 Hectare. 
 
“Land Acquisition for the conservation, of the New 
Bridge near Bridge No. 29 /8 on Thiriwanaketiya - 
Agalawatta Road” Ellagawa Kumbura in all persons 
interested in below aforesaid lands on which is servitude 
is to be Acquired are hereby required to appear personally 
or by agents dully authorized with documents in writing 
meet me on 24th, June 2015 at 10.00 a.m. at Kalawana 
Divisional Secretariat and are hereby required to notify to 
me in writing  (with 2 duplicates) on or before me on  
17th of June,2015 and nature  of their interests in the 
land, the particular of their claims for compensation for 
the Acquisition of the said lands servitude the amount of 
compensation and the details of the calculation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 G. D. L. U DAYA KUMARA,
