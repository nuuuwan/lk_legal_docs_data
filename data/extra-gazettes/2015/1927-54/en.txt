EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 of 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
  Reference No.: DS/VW/LA/AQ/1757. 
Ministry No. : 03J/82/E/336. 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land which 
is required for a  public purpose :- 
 
Description of the land be Acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan - Ya 1888 
Situation : Village - Chankanai Center 
GN Division  - Chankanai Center J/181 
DS Division  - Valikamam West, Chankanai 
District  - Jaffna 
Province  - Northern 
 
01. Lot No. 1  
Name of the land: Kommandarollai; Description: 
Open Land; Name of the Claimant: Sabapathipillai 
Ranganathan, Chankanai East, Chankanai;  Extent: 
0.6188 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid  land are 
hereby required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at Valikaman West, 
Chankanai DS Office on the day 04th day of November 
2015 at 10 a.m. and hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before  the day 20th day  of 
October 2015, the nature of their interest in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
Acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the Computation of such amount. 
 
All deeds and documents in support of claim should 
be produced before me at the inquiry. 
 
                                                    A. SOTHINATHAN, 
                                                     Divisional Secretary, 
                             Valikamam West, Chankanai. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Valikamam West, Chankanai,
