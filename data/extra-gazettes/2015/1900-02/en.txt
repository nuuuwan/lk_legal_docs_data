Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262)
Notice Under Section 38(3)
I, Nagalingam Vethanayagan, the Returning Officer, do by this Notice ;
Extend the date of poll of the under-mentioned Local Authorities fixed for 24.03.2012 by notice published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1744/8 of  08.02.2012 under Section 38(3) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), as
amended by the Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 01 of  2002, and appoint 28.02.2015 as the date for taking
of the poll of the said Local Authorities.
Puthukudiyiruppu Pradeshiya Sabha
Maritime Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha
N. V
ETHANAYAGAN,
Returning Officer,
Administrative District of Mullaitivu.
District Secretariat,
Mullaitivu District,
02 nd February, 2015.
