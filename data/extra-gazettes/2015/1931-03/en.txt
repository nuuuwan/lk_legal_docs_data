  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the Western  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Resignation ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the People's Liberation Front,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Rambukana Vidanapathirana
Sarath  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Rambukana Vidanapathirana , elected as a member of
the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Colombo  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
07th September, 2015.

 PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  Western   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Madampe Appuhamilage
Manjula Prasanna Sampath  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Madampe Appuhamilage Manjula Prasanna Sampath,
elected as a member of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Gampaha  in respect of which such
vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
