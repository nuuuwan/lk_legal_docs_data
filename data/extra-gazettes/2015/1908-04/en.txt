FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
PALINDANUW ARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, Ranaweera Patikirige Bandupema belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of
that Palindanuwara  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Galpayage Don Trimal
Sampath being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hennedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Galpayage Don Trimal Sampath
elected as a member of the Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
29th  March, 2015.
