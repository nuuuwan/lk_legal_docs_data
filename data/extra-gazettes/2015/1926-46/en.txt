MUTUAL  ASSISTANCE IN CRIMINAL  MATTERS ACT, No. 25 OF 2002
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Sub-section (3) of  Section 2 of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters
Act, No. 25 of 2002, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice do by this Order, declare that the provisions of the
aforesaid Act shall apply in respect of the Agreement between the Government of  the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka and the Government of  the Russian Federation made at St. Petersburg on the Twenty Eighth day of May
Two Thousand and Fifteen. This Agreement which was made between the Government of Sri Lanka and the  Government of
the Russian Federation, which is a Non- Commonwealth Country, deals with  mutual legal assistance in criminal matters
and concerns inter alia the improved effectiveness on the part of both parties to the Agreement through cooperation in
relation to combating crimes including terrorism within their respective Jurisdictions.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
30th  July, 2015.
