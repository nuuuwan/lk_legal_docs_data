THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  GARMENT  MANUFACTURING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under   regulation   30  of the Wages Boards Regulations, 1971  That  Mr. W. M. K. L. Weerasingha has
been appointed as Employer Representative  of  the Wages  Board for the Garment  Manufacturing  Trade in place of
Mr. R. L. P.  Peiris   by the   Hon. Minister of Labour Under Section 9 of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136). This
appointment is valid only up to 16 th February 2016.
H
ERA TH YAPA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
10th June  2015.
