TRUSTS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  87)
Order under Section 114
WHEREAS the Trustees of the “School for the Hearing Impaired Trust” namely-
1. Mrs. Ranjithamalar Balasingham,
2. Mr. Ajitkumar Charles,
3. Mrs. Dhushyanthy Lewis,
4. Mr. Seenithamby John Thevathas,
5. Mr. Sinthathurai Varathaseelan,
5. Mrs. Mary Matilda Christy
have applied to the Minister of Justice for their incorporation under Section 114 of the Trusts Ordinance (Chapter 87).
Now therefore, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the aforesaid Ordinance, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe,
Minister of Justice do by this Order, authorize the incorporation of the said Trustees and direct and declare that-
(a) the Trustees of the said Trust named above and their successors for the time being duly appointed under the
rules of the said Trust and the Declaration of the Trust bearing No. 2173 of April 30, 2014 attested by
K. Neelakandan,  Notary Public of Colombo shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a
common seal under the name and style of the “School for the Hearing Impaired Trust” ;
(b) the said Trustees may under the said name and style purchase, receive, hold, take on loan or otherwise
acquire, sell, exchange, mortgage, pledge, lease or otherwise dispose of any property movable or immovable,
on behalf of the said Tust  ; and
(c) the Trustees shall by the said name and style be able and competent in law to sue and be sued, to answer and
to be answered, to defend and to be defended, in any Court in Sri Lanka or elsewhere in all cases and
actions arising in relation to the said Trust.
This Order may be revoked, or from time to time be varied or amended in such manner as appear to me to be necessary.
W
IJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo,
06th November 2015.
