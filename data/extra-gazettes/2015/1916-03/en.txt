THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
W AGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  RUBBER (INCLUDING  TYRE  MANUFACTURING  AND  RE-BUILDING),  PLASTIC
AND PETROLEUM  RESIN  PRODUCTS  MANUFACTURING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under   regulation   30  of the Wages Boards Regulations, that Mr. Sarath Wijesinghe  has been appointed
as Employer's Representative  of  the Wages  Board for the Rubber (Including Tyre Manufacturing  and Re-building), Plastic
and Petroleum Resin Products   Manufacturing  Trade in place of  Mr. D. K. Sanjeewa  Serasinghe by the   Hon. Minister of
Labour Under Section 9 of the Wages Board Ordinance (Chapter 136). These appointment is valid only up to 15.02.2016.
H
ERA TH YAPA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour.
Labour Secretariat,
