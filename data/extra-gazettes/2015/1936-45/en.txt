THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Notice Under Section
My Reference No. : GAL/LAN/ACQUIS/A13/53.
Land Ministry Reference No. : 4/3/6/2012/HW/145.
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the
Government intends to acquire the following land/servitude*
which is required for a public purpose.
      Description of the land or servitude to be acquired  :
Location : Allotments below name land in total extent of
about 0.0532 Hectares, shown in Preliminary Plan No.
Ke 3460 dated 24.08.2015, prepared by the Surveyor General,
situated in the village of Holombuwa and Minor Division of
Beligal Korale (East) in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Galigamuwa, in the District of Kegalle.
Plan No : P .P .Ke. 3460
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Waramullaowita ; Claimant:  R.A.T.W.S.
Ranaweera and others ; Extent: 0.0130 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Waramullaowita ; Claimant:  R.A.T.W.S.
Ranaweera and others ; Extent: 0.0142 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Mahawalapallawatta ; Claimant:  P.A.
Nandawathi; Extent: 0.0097 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 192
Name of land: Mahawalapallawatta ; Claimant:  P.A.
Nandawathi; Extent: 0.0163 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on which
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Galigamuwa Divisional Secretariat of the 22.10.2015 at
10.00 a.m.  and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
[in duplicate] on or before the 15.10.2015, the nature of their
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude*,
the amount  of compensation and the details of the
computation of such amount.
