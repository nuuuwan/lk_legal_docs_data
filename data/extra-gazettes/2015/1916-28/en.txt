NORTH    CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Order  of  Delegating  Power
BY  virtue of powers vested in me under Co-operative Societies Amendment Statute No. 01 of 2011 reading with section 2.2
(5) of Co-operative Societies Statute No. 05 of 2009, I, Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Ranjith Samarakoon, Provincial Minister
of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs, Co-operative, Trade and Food, Industrial Development, of North Central Provincial
delegates all powers mentioned in the column III of the schedule given below to the person mentioned in the column I who
has been appointed to perform the functions of the post of the Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development
(Departmental) to assist the North Central Provincial Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar, in accordance
with the aforementioned Statute, to be effective from the date mentioned in column II.
S
AMARAKOON MUDIYANSELAGE RANJITH SAMARAKOON,
Provincial Minister of Transport, Sports and Youth Affairs, Co-operative,
Trade and Food, Industrial Development, of North Central Province.
    01st April, 2015.
