PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government   Notifications
NOTIFICATION   MADE  IN  TERMS   OF  THE   CABINET   DECISION   ON   DELEGATION  OF
FUNCTIONS  BY  CABINET   MINISTERS   TO  STATE  MINISTERS  AND  DEPUTY   MINISTERS,
AS PER  THE   PROVISIONS   IN   PARAGRAPH (2)  OF  ARTICLE 45  OF  THE   CONSTITUTION
OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
AS per decision taken by the Cabinet of  Ministers at its meeting held on 21.10.2015 based on the Cabinet Decision
dated 07.10.2015 on ‘‘Delegation of Functions to State Ministers and Deputy Ministers, I hereby notify as per the provi-
sions in Paragraph (2) of Article 45 of the Constitution that functions delegated to the Deputy Minister of Rural Eco-
nomic Affairs appointed in terms of Paragraph (1), Article 45 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka Should be implemented with effect from 16th November 2015 as indicated in the following Schedule.
P. H
ARRISON,
Minister of Rural Economic Affairs.
At the Ministry of Rural Economic Affairs,
16th November, 2015.
SCHEDULE
Duties and Functions :
1. Assisting the Hon. Minister in the formulation and implementation of national policies and strategies relat-
ing to the Subject of livestock development.
2. Assisting the Hon. Minister in the formulation of national and regional policies/ strategies and implementa-
tion of programs on rural economic development.
3. Assisting the Hon. Minister and the Ministry in coordination with the Ministry of Finance, and other Minis-
tries and the General  Treasury.
4. Supervision, regulation and evaluation of livestock development activities in the Eastern Province imple-
mented by the Ministry and assisting the Hon. Ministers each Activities.
5. Supervision, regulation and evaluation of rural livelihood development projects to be implemented in the
Batticaloa district.
6. Replying parliamentary questions raised by Hon. MPs and  assisting the Hon. Minister in that regard.
7. Acting on behalf of the Hon. Minister when he is on leave or out of the country.
8. Any other duties delegated by the Hon. Minister.
