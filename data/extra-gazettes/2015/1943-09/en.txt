No. 1943/9 –   WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 02, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
           My Reference No.: DST/LND/1/ACQ/76. 
 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following                           
land/servitude* which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be  acquired: 
 
Lot No. 150, 151 and 152 (Lots 03) allotments in total 
extent of about 0.0273 Hectare of lands name of 
Polkoratuwa and Ambalagewatta, Achariyawatte 
Kumbura alias Achariyawatta Kebella of land in Final 
Village Plan No. 313 and dated 04.09.2015 prepared by 
the Surveyor -Superintendent on behalf of Surveyor -
General situated in the Village of Polommaruwa the 
Gramaniladhari Divisions Polommaruwa North and 
Polommaruwa South in the Minor Division of Giruwa 
Pattuwa-Western of Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Tangalle in Hambantota District in the Southern Province 
and bounded as follows. 
 
01. Lot No. 150 —  Name of the land: Polkoratuwa 
and Ambalagewatta; Extent of Land: 0.0151 Hectare   
Boundary: 
 
North: Lot No. 64 1/4 and 64; East: Lot No. 64 1/4 and 
64; South: Lot No. 64 and 43; West: Lot No. 64 and 63. 
02. Lot No. 151 —  Name of the land: 
Achariyawatte Kumbura alias Achariyawatta 
Kebella; Extent of Land: 0.0099 Hectare   
Boundary: 
 
North: Lot No. 64; East: Lot No. 64 and 152; South: 
Lot No. 152 and 64; West: Lot No. 64 and 64. 
 
03. Lot No. 152 —  Name of the land: 
Achariyawatte Kumbura; Extent of Land: 0.0023 
Hectare   
Boundary: 
 
North: Lot No. 151 and 64; East: Lot No. 64; South: 
Lot No. 64; West: Lot No. 64 and 151.  
All persons interested in the aforesaid  lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
Office of Tangalle on 13th day of January, 2016 at 10.00 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 30th day of December, 
2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 N. W. YAPA,  
                                                       Divisional Secretary, 
 Divisional Secretariat, 
 Tangalle.  
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Tangalle, 
26th November, 2015.
