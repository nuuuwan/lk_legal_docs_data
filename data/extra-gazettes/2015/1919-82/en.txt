The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Proclamations & C., by the President
P. S. No. : CSA/7/8/15.
BY  HIS EXCELLENCY  MAITHRIPALA  SIRISENA
PRESIDENT OF THE  DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC OF SRI  LANKA
To :
1. Maxwell Parakrama Paranagama Esquire
2. Madam Dimingu Badathuruge Priyanthi Suranjana Vidyaratne
3. Madam Mano Ramanathan
Greetings  !
WHEREAS,  in terms of the provisions of Section 2 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) you were appointed by
Warrant dated 12th August 2013, as Commissioners of the Commission of Inquiry to inquire into whether any persons resident
in the Northern and Eastern Provinces in Sri Lanka have been abducted or have disappeared from their places of residence ;
WHEREAS, amendments and additions have been made to the said original directive from time to time ;
AND WHEREAS, I have identified that the number of Commissioners currently holding office is not adequate to more
efficiently and speedily execute the functions of the Commission which has been vested with an enhanced role :

NOW THEREFORE, I,  Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do in
pursuance of the provisions of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, by these presents, appoint Wijesekera Arachchige
Tillekeratne Ratnayake Esquire, retired Judge of the High Court and Hewahettige Sumanapala Esquire, retired Secretary to
Ministry, reposing great trust and confidence in their prudence, ability and fidelity, to be the Commissioners of the Commission
of Inquiry constituted in terms of the Warrant referred to above :
Given at Colombo, under the seal of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, on this 18th day of June, Two
Thousand and Fifteen.
                                                                                                                           By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
