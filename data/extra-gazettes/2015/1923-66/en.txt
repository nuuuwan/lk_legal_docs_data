No. 294/1 of 2015
PROMOTION AND APPOINTMENT APPROVED BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that H IS E XCELLENCY THE P RESIDENT has been pleased to promote Air Marshal Kolitha Aravinda
Gunatilleke, RWP, RSP, VSV , USP, ndc, psc, qfi to the rank of Air Chief Marshal with effect from June 16, 2015 and to
appoint him as the Chief of Defence Staff in terms of Section 2 of the Chief of Defence Staff Act, No. 35 of  2009, with effect
from that date.
                                                                By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
16th July,  2015.
P.S. No.: CSA/7/6/4.
No. 294/2 of 2015
PROMOTION AND APPOINTMENT APPROVED BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that H IS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT has been pleased to promote Air Vice Marshal Gagan Pulasthi
Bulathsinghala, RWP, RSP, USP, ndc, psc, to the rank of Air Marshal with effect from June 15, 2015 and to appoint him
under Article 61E(a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as the Head of the Sri Lanka
Air Force, with effect from that date.
                                                                 By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
16th July,  2015.
