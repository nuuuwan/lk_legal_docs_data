CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, R. Semasinghe,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 08th  June, 2015, all  duties of  Customs as well
as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be   paid and received
at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1917/2  of
01st   June, 2015 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 14th   June  2015.
R. S
EMASINGHE,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
05th   June   2015.
