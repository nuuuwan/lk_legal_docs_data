THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana Minister of Lands, hereby declare under Sub-section
(1) of  Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act:
The allotments of lands in total extent of about 9.4799 Hectares,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No.Ma/GLW/2014/392 prepared
by the Surveyor General of Mathale, situated in the Villages called
Hathadukkuwa and Beligamuwa, in the Grama Niladari Divisions of
431 B Hathadukkuwa  E 431 Beligamuwa  in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Galewela in Mathale District of Central
Province and bounded as follows:
1. Lot  ‘A’ – Mathayakotuwa – Extent: 0.1930 Hectare
North: Balance portion of the same land; East: Balance portion of
the same land, Mathayakotuwa claimed by H. D. Chandrasekara
Wilson Fernando and Lot B; South: Lot B; West: Mathayakotuwa
claimed by H. D. Chandrasekara Wilson Fernando and balance portion
of the same land.
2. Lot  ‘B’ – Mathayakotuwa – Extent: 0.1513 Hectare
North: Lot A and Beligamuwa Waththa claimed by National
Livestock Development Board; East: Balance portion of the same
land, Mathayakotuwa claimed by U. G . Upali Jayanandasooriya
and Lot C; South: Lot C and Paragaha Ela; West: Paragaha Ela and
balance portion of the same land.
3. Lot  ‘C’ – Mathayakotuwa – Extent: 0.1450 Hectare
North: Lot B and Mathayakotuwa claimed by H. D. Chandrasekara
Wilson Fernando; East: Balance portion of the same land,
Mathayakotuwa claimed by U. G . Sisilin and Lot D; South: Paragaha
Ela; West: Paragaha Ela and Lot B.
4. Lot  ‘D’ – Mathayakotuwa – Extent: 0.0729 Hectare
North: Lot C and Mathayakotuwa claimed by U. G . Upali
Jayanandasooriya; East: Balance portion of the same land; South:
Paragaha Ela; West: Lot C.
5. Lot  ‘E’ – Pinkumbura – Extent: 0.4335 Hectare
North: Paragaha Ela; East: Balance portion of the same land; South:
Lots F and G; West: Balance portion of the same land.
6. Lot  ‘F’ – Pinkumbura – Extent: 0.1248 Hectare
North: Lot E; East:Lot E and Balance portion of the same land;
South: Lot G; West: Lot E.
7. Lot  ‘G’ – Pinkumbura – Extent: 0.1175 Hectare
North: Lots E and F; East: Balance portion of the same land;
South: Balance portion of the same land and Lot H; West: Beligamuwa
Waththa claimed by National Livestock Development Board and
balance portion of the same land.
8. Lot  ‘H’ – Beligamuwa Waththa – Extent: 1.0913 Hectare
North: Lot G; East: Pinkumbura claimed by Hathadukkuwa
Nisolana Temple and Balance portion of the same land; South: Road

by Hathadukkuwa Nisolana Temple.
