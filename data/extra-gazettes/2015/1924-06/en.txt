Regulations
The Export of Cultured Marine Organisms on Artificial Substrates Regulations 2011 published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1730/14 of November 2, 2011 are hereby amended as follows :-
(1) In regulation 4 thereof by the substitution for the words “by a fee of rupees One Hundred (Rs. 100)” of the
words ”by a fee of rupees fifty (Rs. 50) for each substrate” ; and
(2) In Schedule I thereof by the substitution for the words “( This application shall be accompanied by fee of
rupees one hundred for each substrates )” of the words “( This application shall be accompanied by fee of
rupees fifty (Rs. 50/-) for each substrates ).”
