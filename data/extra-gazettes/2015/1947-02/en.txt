PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
 CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice by  the  Governor  of  the  Central Province  Provincial Council
KNOW ye that by virtue of powers vested by the Articles 154(b:(8):(a) of he Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Surangani Vishaka Ellawala, Governor of the Central Province, do by
this notice prorogue the Third Session of the Sixth Provincial Council of the Central Province with effect from 18th
December Two Thousand Fifteen.
(02) By virtue of powers vested in me by paragraph 8(a) of the said Article, I do hereby summon the said Council to
meet at the Chamber of the Central Province Provincial Council Complex at Pallekelle, Kundasale on Tuesday the 16th
February, Two Thousand Sixteen at 10.00 a. m.
S
URANGANI VISHAKA E LLAW ALA,
Governor of the Central Province.
Given at Kandy, this 15th day of December, Two Thousand Fifteen.
