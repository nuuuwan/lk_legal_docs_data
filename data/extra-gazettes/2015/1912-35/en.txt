FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   DEPUTY  MAYOR  OF   THE
RATNAPURA     MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Deputy Mayor in Ratnapura Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of  Appointed as
Mayor ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Godahena Kattadige Nilantha Roshan the former Deputy Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ratnapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Deputy Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Jayasinghe Mudiyanselage Chandradasa  Jayasinghe to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Matara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansha Perera,  Returning Officer of  Ratnapura Municipal
Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Jayasinghe Mudiyanselage
Chandradasa Jayasinghe elected as a Deputy Mayor of the Ratnapura Municipal Council.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Ratnapura Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
29th  April, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
MORATUW A  MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Moratuwa Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance,  to which
the former member, Thennakoon Pathiranage Dilip Kumara belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a
member of that Moratuwa Municipal Council who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Wedige Nimal Y asasiri
Peiris  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Moratuwa Municipal Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Wedige Nimal Yasasiri Peiris  elected as a
member of the Moratuwa Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Moratuwa Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
29th  April, 2015.
