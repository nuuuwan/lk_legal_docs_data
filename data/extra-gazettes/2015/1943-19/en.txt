PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
My No. : 01/10/03/04
UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of  the  Uva  Provincial Council
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1947, I, M. P . Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved by me.
01.  Mr. A. B. M. Wijeyathunga, Officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, to perform the duties of the Director of
Provincial Agriculture (Uva), from the date 22.08.2015 to 29.08.2015, till the return of Mr. R. M. Nandasiri, to the
Island who is the Director of Provincial Agriculture (Uva).
02.  Mr. G . A. Ariyasena Gurusinghe, Officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, to act as Deputy Director of
Agriculture (Training) of Bindunuwewa in-service Training Institute, on full time basis with effect from the date
01.08.2015.
03.  Mr. A. B. M. Wijeyathunga, Class I Officer of the Sri Lanka Agricultural Service, to act as Director to Agriculture
of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 01.10.2015.
04.  Dr. H. G. G . L. P . Ariyadasa, to perform the Duties of the Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala, in
addition to his permanent post, from the date 07.11.2015 to 22.11.2015, till the return of  Dr. P. D. K. Adhikari, to the
Island who is the Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala.
05. Mr. R. M. Ananda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Educational Administrative Service, to perform the duties of
the Director to Education of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, from the date 06.11.2015 to
18.11.2015, till the return of Mr. R. M. P . Rathnayake, to the Island who is the Director to Education of Uva
Province.
06. Mr. J. A. S. P . Jayasinghe, Class III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to the Co-
operative Service Commission of Uva Province, in addition to his permanent post, with effect from the date
18.11.2015.
