PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
HOMAGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
I, Rajapakshage Vajira Nishantha Gunawardhana, Returning Officer of the Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha ,  do hereby declare
under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262 ) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. W. M. Ajith Premakumara  of
the Homagama  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of   member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he  has ceased to be a member
of the People's Liberation Front.
V
AJIRA GUNAWARDHANA,
Returning Officer,
Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha .
District  Elections Office,
Colombo,
18th May, 2015.
