NORTH   WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notice made under the 5(I) of Paragraph II relevant to the General and Private Cemeteries
 Act, No. 57 of 1946
IT is hereby published that I, Jayasekara Pathirennehelage Dayasiri Pathmakumara Jayasekara, as the Chief Minister and
The Local Government Minister of the North Western Provincial Council, with accordance to the powers entrusted to me
from the provisions given by Section 5(I) of Paragraph (A) of the Sub section II of Section II of Section 2 of the Provincial
Council Act, No. 12 of 1989 (Consequential Regulations), the cemeteries which does cremations or inhumations, as
under mention Schedule I and II situated within the Udubaddawa Pradeshiya Sabha Limits of Kurunegala District of North
Western Province, as general cemeteries.
J. P. D
AYASIRI P A THMAKUMARA J AYASEKARA, Attorny-at-Law
Chief Minister of the North Western Provincial Council
and The Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Local
Government and Provincial Administration, Human Resources,
Educational and Cultural Affairs, Lands, Environment, Tourism,
Investments Coordinating, Co-operative Development
and Food Supply and Distribution Minister,
North Western Province.
Chief Minister's Office,
Kurunegala,
15th July 2015.
