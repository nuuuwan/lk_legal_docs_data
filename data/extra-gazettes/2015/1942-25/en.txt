EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
           My Reference No. 2/2/1/101/(11) 
 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose: 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be  acquired:  
Allotments of lands mentioned below in extents of 
0.0243 Hectare described in preliminary plan P. P. Gam. 
3954 which surveyed and arrange by Superintendent of 
Survey Gampaha on behalf of Surveyor -General situated 
in 230 C Galthotamulla village in Mada Ptthuwa, in 
Gampaha Division secretarial Division, in Gampaha 
District, in Western Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 1   
Name of land: Hadawakagahalanda; Description of the 
land: Home land, contained 03 Coconut trees age about 40 
years; Claimed by : Government First Claimed by                
Mr. Arachchi Appuhamilage Don Karunapala, 
Galthotumull, Yakkala; Extent: 0.0069 Hectare. 
 
2. Lot No. 2   
Name of the land: Hadawakagahalanda; Description of 
the land: Home land, contained 03 Coconut trees age about 
30 years, 01 coconut tree age about 03 years, 14 acrecanut 
trees age about 20 years,01 “ Kaluwara wood” tree age 
about 30 years and 02 Agar wood trees age about 10 
years.; Claimed by: Government; First Claimed by: Mr. 
Madurapperuma Arachchilage Jayarathna, No. 90, 
Galthotumull, Yakkala; Extent: 0.0135 Hectare. 
 
3. Lot No. 3   
Name of the land: Galakumbura; Description of the 
land: Paddy field; Claimed by: Government; First Claimed 
by: Not found; Extent: 0.0039 Hectare.  
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally, or agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Gampaha on the  20th 
day of January 2016, at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
13th Day of January, 2016 the nature of their  interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land/servitude*,  the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 C. P. W. GUNATHILAKA,  
                                                       Divisional Secretary, 
                                              Gampaha. 
 
  
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Gampaha, 
23rd  October, 2015.

Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the

An Allotment  of land in total extent of 0.767 Hectare

1. Lot No. 1
