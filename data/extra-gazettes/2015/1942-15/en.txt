L.D.B 21/78(XIV)
THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 20 (4)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 20 (4) of Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the
Minister of Higher Education and Highways, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the Gampaha
Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of the said Institute
have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order, appoint Professor Jayantha Walihinda to be the Competent
Authority with effect from November 26, 2015, for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of
Director of the Gampaha Wickramrachchi Ayurveda Institute, Sri Lanka any power, duty or function respectively which
has been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to, the said Director under the aforesaid Act or any Order, Oridnance,
Statute, By-law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
L
AKSHMAN KIRIELLA,
Minister of  Higher Education and
Highways.
Colombo,
24th November, 2015.
