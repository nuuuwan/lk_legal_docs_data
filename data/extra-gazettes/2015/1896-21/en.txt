I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government indents to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Preliminary plan: Ya 2777; Situation Village: 
Karanavai; GN Division: Karanavai, East. J/351; DS 
Division: Vadamaradchchi, South West; District: Jaffna; 
Province: Northern. 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Avalappulam; Description: Garden 
contains Seasonal Cropas; Name of the Claimant: K. 
Thavachelvam, No. 462/4, Segul Court, Hamton Lane, 
Wellawatta, Colombo 06;  Extent: 0.1434 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the Land: Avalappulam; Description: Way 
and Water Course; Name of the Claimant: Not Known;  
Extent: 0.0025 Hectare. 
 
Lot No. 03 
 
Name of the Land: Avalappulam; Description: Garden 
contains Seasonal Cropas; Name of the Claimant: K. 
Thavachelvam, No. 462/4, Segul Court, Hamton Lane, 
Wellawatta, Colombo 06;  Extent: 0.0216 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Vadamaradchchi, South West/ Karaveddy on the 18th 
Wednesday of February, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 5th of February, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particular of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land amount 
of compensation and the details of the computation of 
such amount.
