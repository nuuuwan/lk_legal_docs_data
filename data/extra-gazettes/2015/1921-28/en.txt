 SRI   LANKA   ACCOUNTING   AND  AUDITING    STANDARDS  ACT,   No. 15   OF   1995
Publication under Section 4(2)
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (hereinafter referred to as the
“Institute”), SLFRS 15 Revenue from Contracts with Customers was published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1875/44
dated Saturday, August 16, 2014, for the purpose of the Sri Lanka Accounting and Auditing Standards, Act, No. 15 of 1995.
SLFRS 15 Revenue from Contracts with Customers, which were to be effective for financial statements covering period
commencing on or after the first day of January Two Thousand Seventeen, shall be effective from the first day of January Two
Thousand Eighteen as decided by the Institute.
