No. 1906/3 –  MONDAY, MARCH  16, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS  AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, NO. 28 OF 1964  
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
My No. : ID/20/17/22/2003. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, As  Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Acquire the land for Nonperial R/Wiwakananda Tamil 
Vidyalaya. 
 
Allotments of lot No. 385, 386 : depicted in 
Supplementary No. 24, Topo 4A situated at Yakdewela 
village Yakdewela G. S. Division Kadawatha Korale 
Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat, Rathnapura District and 
Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
Lot No. 385  
Name of the Land: Pillumale Thalawa;  Claimant: W. 
A. S. De Wos; Extent: 0.3845 Hectare. 
Lot No. 386  
Name of the Land: Detaluketiye Mukalana, Batahire 
Mukalana, Maligatenna and Wangumullehena;  Claimant: 
Government; Extent: 0.4235 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a servitude is to be acquire are here by required 
appear personally, or by agent duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Imbulpe Divisional Secretariat on the 06th 
day of April 2015 At 9.30 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
26th day of March 2015. The nature of their interests in 
the land, the particulars of their claims for the   
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude the amount of compensation of such amount. 
 
 J. L. C. K. JAYASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Imbulpe. 
 Rathnapura District. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Imbulpe. 
11th  March, 2015.
