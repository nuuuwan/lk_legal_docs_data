Revocation of Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS by Order made under Section 4(1) of the  Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of
Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition), as amended by Act Nos. 14 and 62 of 1957, 14 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 read with Industrial Dispute s
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 37 of 1968  dated 24.06.2015 and published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

01. Mr. T. H. Dharmasena, 'Hewawasa', Mahagama (via Kalutara)
02. Mr. W. A. Wickramasinghe, No. 56, Talwatta, Gonawala (W.P.)
03. Mr. P. M. G. B. Senanayaka, Napana, Gunnepana

and
National Savings Bank, No. 255, Galle Road, Colombo 03 of the other part,

STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is
Whether the claim made by the below mentioned employees who have served the National Savings Bank as at 01.01.1988 as Grade IV
officers to be given Grade III Class III with effect from 01.01.1988, to be placed in corresponding grades and to be given the posts and grades
due thereafter is just, and if so, to what reliefs they are entitled.
01. Mr. T. H. Dharmasena
02. Mr. W. A. Wickramasinghe
03. Mr. P. M. G. B. Senanayaka
04. Mrs. A. W. W. Abesiriwardana
M. D. C. A
