EASTERN  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
I, Hearath Ekanayake Mudiyansalage Wanasekara Gethera Dissanayake, Acting Secretary to the Governor of the Eastern
Province by Order the Governor of Eastern Province do hereby declare and publish that the Board of Ministers of the Eastern
Provincial Council described in the scheduled hereto appointed by the Governor of the Eastern Province by virtue of Article
154 F (1), (4) and (5) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka resigned from their respective
portfolios as described in the schedule effective from 01.01.2015.
H. E. M. W. G. D
ISSANAYAKE,
Actg. Secretary to the Governor,
Eastern Province.
