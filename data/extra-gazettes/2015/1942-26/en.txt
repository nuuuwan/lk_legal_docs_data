THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is  needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  called Veerakulicholai Estate containing in
Extent  of 4.0090 Hectares depicted as Lot ‘A’  in the Advance
Tracing No. PU/VNT/2014/154 dated 22nd July 2014 prepared by
the  Puttalam District Superintendent of Surveys,  situated in the
Village of Karadipuwal in the Division of Pomparippu Pattu  in the
Divisional Secretary's Division of Wanathavillu,  District of Puttalam
and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Veerakulicholai – Extent:  4.0090  Hectares
North: Bullock Cart Track; East: FTP 22 Lot No. 1821; South:
FTP 22 Lot Nos. 1826, 1827 and 1828; West: FTP 22 Lot No. 1819.
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
Ref. No.: 4-3/2/2013/WR/602.
