UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No. 16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
By virtue of powers vested in me by section 25 A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978 I, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Sumanaweera Banda Dissanayake, the Minister of Higher Education, having obtained a report under section 70 C of the
aforesaid Act, in respect of the KAATSU- Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) a
Company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, No.7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 66705, do by
this Order and Subject to the conditions specified in Schedule I hereto, recognize the KAATSU-Highly Advanced Medical
Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher
Education therein, leading to the award of the degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule II hereto, of which the abbreviations
are set out in the corresponding entries respectively in Column II of the Schedule II.
 SCHEDULE   I
CONDITIONS
I. KAATSU- Highly Advanced Medical Technology Training Centre (Pvt) Limited (KIU) (hereinafter referred to
as the “Institute”) shall before expiry of two months from the date of granting the degree awarding status,
submit its Articles of Association duly amended as set out below to the Specified Authority :-
(a) to specify higher education as an object in the Articles of Association ;
(b) to undertake to inform the Specified Authority of voluntary winding up of the Company prior to such
winding up.
2. The Institute shall before the expiry of six months from the date of granting the degree awarding status,
submit the following details to Specified Authority:-
