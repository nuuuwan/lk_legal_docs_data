L.D.  B. 21/ 78 (xiv).
THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No.  16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection   (4) (b)  of Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  subsection (4) (b) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I,  Sarath Amunugama, Minister of Higher Education and Research, on being satisfied that the administration of  the affairs
of  the Gampaha  Wickramarachchi  Aryuveda Institute, Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of
the Gampaha Wickramarachchi  Aryuveda Institute  have failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order appoint
Professor Jayantha Welihinda to be the Competent Authority with effect from May 25, 2015, for the purpose of  exercising,
performing or discharging in lieu of   Director of   Gampaha  Wickramarachchi  Aryuveda Institute  any powers, duties and
functions respectively which have been conferred or imposed  on, or assigned to  the said Director of  the  Gampaha
Wickramarachchi  Aryuveda Institute,  Sri Lanka, under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, by law,  Regulation or
Rule made thereunder.
S
ARATH   AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
3rd  June  2015.
