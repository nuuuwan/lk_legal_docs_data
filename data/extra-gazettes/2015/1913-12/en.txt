FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
DOMPE   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, Abesinghe Mudiyanselage Upali Ranjith Siriwardana belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election
as a member of that Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Balasooriyage Samantha
Indika Dayasundara being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Balasooriyage Samantha  Indika
Dayasundara  elected as a member of the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
05th  May, 2015.
