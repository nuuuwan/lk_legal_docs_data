Mr. K. A. P. Chandralal Perera — No. 211/A, Temple

me at the Divisional Secretariat, Udadumbara, on the 05th 
August, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and hereby requested to notify 
to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 29th July, 
2015 nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of the 
said land, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of  such amount.
