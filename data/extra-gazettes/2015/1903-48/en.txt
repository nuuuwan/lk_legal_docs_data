FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
LUNUGAMWEHERA  PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Lunugamwehera  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  not attending
meetings of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the former
Member, Nawimana Urapolage Samitha Chathuranga  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within  a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Lunugamwehera  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Wije Kankanam
Pathiranage Upul Nishantha  being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Udaha Dasilige Deepal Darshana Chandrarathna, Returning Officer of  Lunugamwehera
Pradeshiya Sabha  acting in terms of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Wije Kankanam
Pathiranage Upul Nishantha  elected as a member of the Lunugamwehera  Pradeshiya Sabha.
D
EEPAL CHANDRARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Lunugamwehera  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Hambantota,
23rd February,  2015.
