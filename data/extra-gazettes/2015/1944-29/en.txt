PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
UV A  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments  made by  the  Governor  of  the  Uva  Province
BY virtue of  powers vested in me under Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P . Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province, do hereby announce that I have made the following appointments.
01.  Mr. Gunadasa Samarasinghe, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry
of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Product, Inland Fisheries Affairs of Uva Province with immediate effect from
the date 07.12.2015.
02.  Mr. G . K. S. L. Rajadasa, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry of
Road Development, Housing, Water Supply, Estate Infrastructure and Co-operative Affairs of Uva Province,
with immediate effect from the date 07.12.2015.
03.  Mr. Sumith Suraweera, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Secretary to the Ministry of
Sports and Youth Affairs, Tourism, Transport, Cultural Affairs and Textiles and Small Industries of Uva Province,
with immediate effect from the date 07.12.2015.
04.  Mr. W. M. M. G . Abeysinghe Banda, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Deputy Chief
Secretary (Personnel & Training) of Uva Province, with immediate effect from the date 07.12.2015.
05. Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya, Class I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to act as Secretary to the
Co-operative Service Commission of Uva Province in addition to his permanent post, with immediate effect
from the date 03.12.2015.
06. Dr. H. G . G. L. P. Ariyadasa, to perform the Duties of the Regional Director to the Health Service of  Monaragala,
in addition to his permanent post, from the date 04.12.2015 to 07.12.2015, till the return of Dr.  P. D. K.
Adhikari, to the Island who is the Regional Director to the Health Service of Monaragala.
M. P . J
AYASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
07th December 2015.
