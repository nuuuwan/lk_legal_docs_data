FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE OF  A   MEMBER OF  THE
V ALIKAMAM  EAST   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS   AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  V alikamam  East   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  IIankai  Tamil Arasu Kadchi  the   recognized  political party to which Kandiah
Thurairasa  the   member   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate
a   person,  eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Valikamam  East   Pradeshiya Sabha  who belongs
to such party  who  shall be declared elected  as a member within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Seeniyar Kunarathnam
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj,  Returning Officer of   Valikamam  East   Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Seeniyar  Kunarathnam  elected as
a member of the  V alikamam  East    Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C.  A
RAVINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam  East    Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Jaffna,
22nd  May  2015.
