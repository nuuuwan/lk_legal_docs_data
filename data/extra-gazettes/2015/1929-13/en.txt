PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Proclamations &c., by the President
PROCLAMATION  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE  PRESIDENT
KNOWE YE that by virtue of the powers vested in me by Article 70 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President, do by this Proclamation, summon Parliament to meet at the
Parliament Hall, Sri Jayawardenepura on the First day of September, Two Thousand and Fifteen, at 09.30 a.m.
At Colombo, this 26th day of August, Two Thousand and Fifteen.
                                                                                         By order of His Excellency,
P. B. ABEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
