THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An  allotment of land in total extent of about 0.8094 Hectare
depicted in Advance Tracing  No.Ya/DLF/2014/86 dated 30.07.2014
prepared by the Senior Superintendent of  Surveys -Jaffna District,
situated in the Village called Delft West, in the Grama Niladhari
Division No. J/01 of Delft West in the Divisional Secretary's Division
of Delft of Jaffna District in Northern Province and bounded
as follows:

North: Periyathurai Thombu claimed by State and Path; East:
Nochchi Thoddam claimant not known; South: Periyathurai Thombu
claimed by Kanagasabai and others; West: Sea Shore and Periyathurai
Thombu claimed by  State.
