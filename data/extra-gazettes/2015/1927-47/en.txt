No. 1927/47 – WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 12, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
My  Reference No.: DS/AK/5/1/393(vi). 
 
 
I hereby give notice under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following Land which 
is required for a  public purpose :- 
 
Description of the land  or servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called the name mentioned 
below in Total Extent of 0.0049 Hectare depicted in Final 
Village Plan No. 658 Supplement No. 66 as Lot 1605 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village 
of Ihalagoda in Grama Niladhari Division of 109 B 
Ihalagoda South in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Akmeemana in Galle District Southern Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 1605  
Name of the land: Kalugalmawatha Kanda, Nagaha 
Okanda Watta; Description: Part of distribution pipe line; 
Claimant: State; Previous Claimant: K. P. Dayawathi, 
No.389/1, Pinthaliya Road, Kadawatha; Extent: 0.0049 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land   is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally or by 
agents duly authorized in writing,  before me at the 
Divisional Secretariat of Akmeemana on 30th day of  
September, 2015 at  10.00 a. m. and are  hereby requested 
to  notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
23rd day of September, 2015 the nature of their interests 
in  the land, the particulars  of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said Land the 
amount of compensation and the details of  the 
computation of such amount. 
 
                                            PUSHPIKA THUSHARI JAGODA, 
                                                     Divisional Secretary, 
    Akmeemana. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Akmeemana. 
07th August, 2015.
