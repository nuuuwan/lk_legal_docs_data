  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS ACT, No. 2 OF 1988
 Filling of  a Vacancy in the Northern Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Northern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the  Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Northern  Province Provincial
Council  by reason of   Resignation.
And  whereas the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  the recognised Political  party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, has nominated  Mr. M. P. Nadaraj to fill such
vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. M. P. Nadaraj, elected as a member of the   above
Provincial Council   in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
01st  April, 2015.
