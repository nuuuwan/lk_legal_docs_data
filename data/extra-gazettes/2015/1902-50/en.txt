LOCAL    AUTHORITIES     ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE    (CHAPTER   262)
Notice under Section 38(1)
ELECTIONS OF CHAIRMAN, VICE CHAIRMAN  AND MEMBERS FOR THE MARITIME PATTU PRADESHIYA SABHA
THIS is to notify that the name of Mariampillai Antani Jeyanathan in the list of candidates of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi
published in the Schedule of Gazette Extraordinary No. 1691/35 of  01.02.2011 is removed from the list, since he has been
elected to the Northern Provincial Council and that he is ineligible to be considered as a candidate for the Maritime Pattu
Pradeshiya Sabha and candidacy or election of any other person in such nomination paper is not denied due to this removal.
R. S
ASEELAN,
Returning Officer,
Maritime Pattu Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Mullaitivu District,
20th February, 2015.
