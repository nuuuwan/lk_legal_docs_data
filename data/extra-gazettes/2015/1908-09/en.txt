Order No. 33
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no manufacturer, importer, packer, distributor or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display
for sale any 50 kg bag of Cement bearing the following generic names and sold under any Brand name, above the maximum
retail prices given below.
Maximum Retail Price
(per 50 kg bag)
Ordinary Portland Cement 870.00
Portland Limestone Cement 870.00
Masonry Cement 870.00
This Order shall come into effect from 31st  March  2015.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind  Order No. 30 Published in  Gazette Extraordinary No. 1902/ 47
dated 20th February 2015.
                                                                                         By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
R
UMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
31st  March  2015.
