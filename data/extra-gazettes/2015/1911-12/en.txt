  BIRTH  AND  DEATH  REGISTRATION ORDINANCE
Notice  under  section  05
I, M. Joseph Michael Perera Minister of Home Affairs by virtue of powers vested by me under Section 5 of  Birth and Death
registration ordinance (Chapter 110 ) do hereby amend the Notice of Puttalam District in North western Province which
published in the supplementary  Gazette No. 8146 on 20th September 1935.
By inserting list of Grama Niladhari Divisions in schedule “A” and “B” instead of the  Grama Niladhari Divisions
notified in connection with Othara North and Othara South Birth and Registration Division in Dankotuwa Divisional Secretary
Division under heading No. 13 and,
By inserting list of Grama Niladhari Divisions in schedule “C” and “D” instead of the  Grama Niladhari Divisions
notified in connection with  Naththandiya and Mudukatuwa Birth and  Death Registration Division in Naththandiya Divisional
Secretary  Division under heading No. 11 and 12,
By inserting list of Grama Niladhari Divisions in schedule “E” and “F” instead of the  Grama Niladhari Divisions
notified in connection with  Kammal Pattuwa Birth and  Death Registration Division in Wennappuwa Divisional Secretary
Division under heading No. 14,
I will be amended this reformation by this notice with effect from on the day of  08th, May of 2015
M. J
OSEPH MICHAEL PERERA,
Minister of Home Affairs.
07th April, 2015,
Colombo.
