THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The  allotments of  below mentioned lands in total extent of about
0.0339 Hectare, depicted in Tracing   No.  KU/MWT/2008/130 and
dated  26.11.2011, prepared by the  Senior  Superintendent of  Surveys
in Kurunegala  District,  situated in the village  of  Pilessa Ihalagama
in  the Minor  Division of  Gandahe Korale in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of  Mawathagama in  Kurunegala  District in the
North Western Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘B’  –  Pilikadawaththa  –  Extent: 0.0084  Hectare
North: Lot A; East: Lot No. 64 of  P . P . Ku. 2047 and

and Pilikadawaththa claimed by H. W. Saiman; West: Pilikadawaththa
claimed by H. W. Saiman.
02. Lot ‘C’  –  Pilikadawaththa  –  Extent: 0.0015  Hectare
North: Lot B; East: Lot B and Lot N 1083 of P. P. 2144;  South:
Lot C; West:  Lots  C, F and Pilikadawaththa claimed by H. W. Saiman.
03. Lot ‘D’  –  Pilikadawaththa  –  Extent: 0.0011  Hectare
North: Lots C and B; East: Lot N 1083 of P. P. Ku. 2144;  South:
Lot F; West:  Lot F and Pilikadawaththa claimed by H. W. Saiman.
04. Lot ‘F’  –  Pilikadawaththa  –  Extent: 0.0211  Hectare

05. Lot ‘G’  –  Pilikadawaththa  –  Extent: 0.0018  Hectare
North: Lot F;  East: Lot N 1083 of  P. P . Ku. 2144;  South and
West: Road.
