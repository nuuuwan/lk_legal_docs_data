CONSUMER  AFFAIRS   AUTHORITY ACT, No. 09 OF 2003
Direction under Section 10 (1)(a)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 10 (1)(a)  of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Manufactures and Traders that the following information shall be legibly marked on
the items or the wrapper of the items listed in the schedule below ;
1. Maximum Retail Price of the item
2. Name, Address and telephone number of the Manufacturer or importer
3. Date of Production
