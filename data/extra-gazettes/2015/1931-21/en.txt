THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is  needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An  allotment of  land  called ‘‘Pinkanda Estate’’ in total extent of
About 0.1000 Hectare, shown and depicted in the Advance Tracing
No. R/NVT/2013/561  dated 26.11.2013,  prepared  by the  Surveyor-
General  situated in the Village of Nivithigala (FVP 36)  in Nawadun
Koralaya, Meda Paththuwa Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Nivithigala in Rathnapura District in
Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pinkanda Estate  – Extent: About 0.1000 Hectare
North and East: Pinkanda Estate, Agalawatta Plantation Company;
South: Pinkanda Estate, Agalawatta Plantation Company and Road;
West: Road and Pinkanda Estate, Agalawatta Plantation Company.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
