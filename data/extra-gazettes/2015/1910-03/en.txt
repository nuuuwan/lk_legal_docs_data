I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act as Invented by the Land 
Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government

for public purpose:- 
 
Purpose of Acquisition — To Housing Purpose 
 
 Description of the land: 
 
A Portion of land called Meeriyakelle bearing in total 
Extent of 0.379 Hectare, Shown as Lot No. 270 of the 
Plan No. F.V.P.2A, Sheet 11 and 20.02.2000 dated, 
Surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor General of 
Badulla District, situated in Maduragama Grama 
Niladhari’s division of Gotuwela Village of Meeriyakelle 
in the minor Division of Kumbalwela Korle in the 
Divisional Secretariat division of Ella in Badulla District, 
Uva - Province and Bounded as Follows.  
 
Lot No.  270 - 0.379 HEC. 
 
North by: Lot No. 7a. Pattiyakumbura and Lot No. 46; 
East by: Lot No. 46, 202, 207, 208, 209 and 220; South 
by: Lot No. 208, 209, 220 and 46A; West by: Lot No. 7A 
Pattiyakumbura. 
All persons interested in above land on which a 
Servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Ella Divisional Secret ariat on the 29th of April, 
2015 at 10.00 a.m. 
 
Also hereby requested to notify to me in writing on or 
before the 29th of April, 2015 the nature of their interests 
in the land, the particul ars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land. The 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amounts.
