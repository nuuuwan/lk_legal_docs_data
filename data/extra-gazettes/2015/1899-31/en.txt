EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified in the Column II and III of the
Schedule hereto, corresponding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Headings mentioned in the Column I of
the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1885/42 dated 24.10.2014 issued under the Section 3 of this Act,
shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule hereto, with effect from
30.01.2015.
R
AV I KARUNANAYAKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
29th January, 2015.
