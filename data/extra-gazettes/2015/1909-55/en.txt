FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
DODANGODA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings
of the Council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, Nanthulage Cyril Fernando belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that
Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Wilpathage Don
Karunadhara being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Wilpathage Don Karunadhara  elected as a
member of the Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Dodangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
10th  April, 2015.
