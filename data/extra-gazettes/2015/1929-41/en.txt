THE  SOCIETIES   ORDINANCE
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 and 4  of  the  Societies  Ordinance  (Chapter 123),  I, Rishad
Bathiudeen Minister of  Industry and  Commerce, do by this notification :-
1. Authorize the purpose for which the Society known as “ Sri Saddhananda Daham Pasal Adi Shishya Sangamaya’’
situated at Sri Saddhananda Daham Pasal Adi Shishya Sangamaya Paramadhamma Visuddharamaya, Katuwawala,
Boralesgamuwa as a purpose to which the powers and facilities of that Ordinance ought to be extended ; and
2. Limit the application of that Ordinance to aforesaid society by exempting it from the provision of
Section   5 (4) of  that Ordinance.
R
ISHAD  B ATHIUDEEN,
Minister of  Industry and Commerce.
Ministry of   Industry and Commerce,
