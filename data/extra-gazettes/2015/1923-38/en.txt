PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
WHEREAS a Statutory Declaration under Section 18 of the Law has been made by Mrs. Dunuwila Sriyani Sujatha Ranasinghe
of No. 74, Rajapihilla Mawatha, Kandy, the Land Reform Commission by virtue of the powers vested in it under Section
19 of the Law, has made a statutory determination specifying the portion of agricultural land owned by the statutory lessee
which she shall be allowed to retain. The agricultural land so allowed to be retained by the statutory lessee is described in
the Schedule below.
T. A. S
UMANATISSA THAMBUGALA,
Chairman,
Land Reform Commission.
C 82, Hector Kobbekaduwa Mawatha,
Colombo 7,
09th July, 2015.
