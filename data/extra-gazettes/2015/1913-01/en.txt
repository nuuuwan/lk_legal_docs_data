CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, Jagath Premalal Wijeweera,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 04th  May, 2015, all  duties of
Customs as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be
paid and received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1912/1  of
27th   April, 2015 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar  notification
to be published on or before 10th   May  2015.
J
AGATH P. WIJEWEERA,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
30th  April   2015.
