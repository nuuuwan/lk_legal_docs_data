IT is hereby announced to the public that following Council Convention No. M.C 5(1) was  adopted at the Meeting of the
Council which was held on 14.07.2015 by the Municipal Council, Kurunegala as per the powers assigned by the Sub-
section (1) of Section 3 of Local Government Institutions (Standard By -laws) Act No.6 of 1952 , being the 261st authority.
A
NURADHA  G AMINEE  P ERAMUNAGE ,
 Mayor, Municipal Council,
 Kurunegala.
17th July 2015,
At the office of the Municipal Council,
Kurunegala.
Proposal
PROPOSAL   TO   EMBRACE   OF   STANDARD   BY-LAWS   OF   LOCAL   GOVERNMENT   INSTITUTIONS
THAT  WERE   ESTABLISHED   IN   THE   EXTRAORDINARY   GAZETTE   No. 541/17   AND   20.01.1989
I, propose to this Council to implement Standard By-laws depicted in the below mentioned annex mentioned in
the Standard By-laws series which were occurred to be published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No.541/17 and
20.01.1989 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka made by Minister in charge of the Local Government as
per the authority assigned under Sub section (1) of Section 2 of Local Government Institutions (Standard By-laws) No. 6
of 1952 which is the 261st authority to accept as Standard By-laws relevant to the Municipal Council Kurunegala from the
day that this proposal is published in the Gazette paper under the section 3 of (1).
Mayor,
Municipal Council,
 Kurunegala.
