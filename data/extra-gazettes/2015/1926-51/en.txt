ASSISTANCE  TO   AND  PROTECTION  OF  VICTIMS  OF   CRIME  AND   WITNESSES  ACT,  No. 4 OF  2015
Order under Section 1(2)
By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1(2) of the Assistance to and Protection of Victims of Crime and
Witnesses Act, No. 4 of  2015, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice do by this Order, appoint August 15, 2015,
to be the date on which the provisions of Parts I, II, III, IV and IX of the aforesaid Act shall come into operation.
 W
IJEY ADASA  R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
