 FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH 13(b) OF ARTICLE 99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION OF
THE DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF   SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary-General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eighth Parliament by
reason of Resignation of Mr. Don Arther Chamara Sampath Dasanayake, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament
for the Electoral District No. 19-Badulla under Section 62 of the aforsaid Act ;
AND WHEREAS the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed
by me under Section 64(1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of Article 99 of
the Constitution ;
AND WHEREAS the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return in
Form “M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. Lakshman Pinthu Jayathilake Senevirathne
was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the said electoral district ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested in
me by Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. Lakshman Pinthu Jayathilake
Senevirathne has been declared elected as a member of the Eighth Parliament for the Electoral District No. 19-Badulla to
fill the vacancy caused by reason of Resignation of Mr. Don Arther Chamara Sampath Dasanayake.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
16th  September, 2015.
