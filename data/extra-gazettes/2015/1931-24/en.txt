  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  Southern   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Southern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Southern
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United National Party,  the recognized political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Lokugamage Sanjeewa
Gemunu Edwin Karunathilaka  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Lokugamage Sanjeewa Gemunu Edwin Karunathilaka,
elected as a member of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Galle  in respect of which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
10th September, 2015.
