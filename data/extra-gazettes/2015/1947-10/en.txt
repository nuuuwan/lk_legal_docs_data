NOTIFICATION
P . S. No.CSA/1/24/1
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Piyankara Jayarathna, MP , who was appointed to the office of the State Minister of Law and
Order and Southern Development by His Excellency the President, in the exercise of powers vested in him, by  Article
44(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in terms of under Article 46(3)(b) of the
Constitution resigned such office of State Minister from  December 21, 2015.
                                                       By order of His Excellency,
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.

Colombo 01,
