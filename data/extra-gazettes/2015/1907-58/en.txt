SPORTS    ACT,   No.   25   OF  1973
Order under Sections 32, 33 and 34
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32, 33 and 34 of the Sports Act,  No. 25 of 1973, I, Navin Eranjan
Dissanayake, Minister of Tourism and  Sports, do hereby inform that the appointments of the office bearers of the Sri Lanka
Cricket are cancelled from 01st of April 2015 and the names of persons given below are appointed from 01st April 2015 to carry
out the functions of Sri Lanka Cricket until an election is held or until further notice.
1. Sidath W ettamuny - President
2. Prakash Schafter - Secretary
3. Lucille Wijewardena - Treasurer
4. Kushil Gunasekare - Vice President
5. Kapila Wijegunawardene - Vice President
 Members
1. Nuski Mohomed
2. Prassanna Jayawardene (PC)
3. Jayananda Warnaweera
4. Duminda Hulangamuwa.
N
A VIN ERANJAN DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
At Colombo,
On this 27th day of March 2015.
