THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section  7  
Land Ministry Reference No.: LD/05/2004/HW/03. 
My Ref. No. C/Ath/RDA/SP/04/08/28-1. 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be Acquired: 
 
Lot No. 01-03 (Lots 03) allotments in total extent of 
about  0.0192 Hectare, of land in Priliminary Plan No. 
CO. 9507 and dated 20.01.2015 prepared by the 
Surveyor-Superintendent on behalf of Surveyor - General 
situated in the Villages of Bambalapitiya (No. 47 
Division - Bambalapitiya, in Colombo Municipal 
Council) in the Gramaniladhari Divisions Bambalapitiya 
in the Minor Division of in the Divisional  Secretary’s 
Division of Thimbirigasyaya in Colombo District in the 
Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Asst. No. Not be found; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not be found;  
Extent: 0.0034 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of Land: Asst. No. Not be found; Description: 
Merinedrive Road; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: —;  
Extent: 0.0096 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 03 
 
Name of Land: Asst. No. Not be found; Description: 
Merinedrive Road; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Not 
be found;  Extent: 0.0062 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at  Land Division, Road 
Development Authority of Sethsiripaya, Baththaramulla 
of the 23rd April 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 18th day of March 2015, the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
