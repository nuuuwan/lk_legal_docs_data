EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
KALMUNAI  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Kalmunai  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Death ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  to which  the
former  member, Arulnayagam Amirthalingam  belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a
member of  that Kalmunai  Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Kunjuththampi
Ehamparam   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna, Returning Officer of  Kalmunai  Municipal  Council,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Kunjuththampi Ehamparam
elected as a member of the  Kalmunai  Municipal Council.
T
HILINA  W ICKRAMARATHNA ,
Returning Officer,
Kalmunai Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Ampara,
08th  October,  2015.
