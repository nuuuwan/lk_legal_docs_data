THE  URBAN  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY  LAW  No. 41 OF  1978 OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE
ASSEMBLY
Order under Section 15(1)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 15(1) of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978
enacted by the National State Assembly I, Patali Champika Ranwaka, Minister of Megapolis and Western Development do
by this Order, vest the permises of Bus Stand and Old Commercial Complex, belongs to the Weeraketiya Pradeshiya
Sabha as decribed in the Schedule below, with the Urban Develpment Authority, with effect from 02.11.2015.
P
ATALI C HAMPIKA  R ANAWAKA,
Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development,

The land called “Galabadihena (the premises of Walasmulla Bus Stand and Old Commercial Complex) containing
in extent of 0A., 3R., 15.8P depicted as Lot No. 1 in Plan No. 2008/040 dated 23.05.2008 made by T. H. Jeevananda,
Registered Licensed Surveyor and Court Commissioner, situated at village of Walasmulla (FVP 163) in Divisional
