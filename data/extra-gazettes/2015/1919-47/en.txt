No. 1919/47 – THURSDAY, JUNE 18, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Bibile Kumbura in total extent of
0.0154 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. KE/GLG/
2014/249 of 29.10.2014, prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Bisowela in the Minor Division of
Beligal Korale in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Galigamuwa
in Kegalle District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Bibile Kumbura (Currently used as high land)
– Extent: 0.0154 Hectare
North: Bibile Kumbura and Road (P .S.); East: Road (P .S.); South
and West: Bibile Kumbura.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My Ref. No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/688.
Divisional Secretary’s Ref. No.: GAL/LAN/ACQUI/A13/20.
