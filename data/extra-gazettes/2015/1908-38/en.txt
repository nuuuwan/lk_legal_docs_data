FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN OF THE
SORANATHOTA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman  in   Soranathota   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman
has been appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,   to which
Wijerathna  Gamage Neel Premathilaka   the  Former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Soranathota  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of   thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Balraman Vedasalam  to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka,  Returning Officer of    Soranathota
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.Balraman
Vedasalam  elected as Vice Chairman of the  Soranathota   Pradeshiya Sabha.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Soranathota  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
01st  April, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  A   MEMBER  OF  THE
CHA V AKACHCHERI    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE   LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS
ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely IIankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  to which the
Former member Vijayaratnam Edwin Daniel , belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified  ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Ponnuththurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of   Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Thankarasa Srivengadesvaran who had secured
the highest number of  preferences next to the last of the  member  declared at the election held on 23rd July, 2011 to
Chavakachcheri Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the Chavakachcheri elected Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of wich
such vacancy occured.
P.   K
UGANA THAN,
Returning Officer,
Chavakachcheri  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
31st  March 2015.
