FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
PASBAGE  KORALE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being Ceased to be
a member of the Party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Up Country Peoples Front to which the
former Member,  Mr. Dewamunige Asanka Prabath Silva  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within  a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that  Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Karuppiah Pathmanathan
being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayake, Returning Officer of Pasbage Korale
Pradeshiya Sabha  acting in terms of Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Karuppiah
Pathmanathan elected as a member of the  Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
AMAN SRI RATHNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
 Pasbage Korale  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
03rd of January,  2015.
