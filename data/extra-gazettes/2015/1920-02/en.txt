PILGRIMAGE ORDINANCE
Annual Festival - Mother’s National Church, Matara
It is hereby notified under Section 02 of regulations framed under Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175) published in the
Gazette No. 687 of 01.11.1991 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that the Annual Festival of Mother’s National
church of Matara in the Divisional Secretariat Division of Matara in the Matara District, will be conducted 03rd of September
to 13th of September 2015.
R
ANJITH YAPA,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
At Divisional Secretariat,
Matara.
17th  June, 2015
