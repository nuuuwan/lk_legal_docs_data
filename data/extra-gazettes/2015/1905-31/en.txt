EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: ව/18/CK/2013/01. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land which is required for a public 
purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land extent about 0.0177 Hectare, 
shown in Preliminary Plan No. GAM 3950, prepared by 
survey Superintendent of  Gampaha, situated at 
Telangapatha village in Grama Niladari Division of 175, 
Talangapatha in Minor Division of Adikari Pattuwa in the 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Wattala in Gampaha 
District in the Western Province. 
 
01.  Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Uluwala Kumbura; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Or iginal Claimant: Deepani 
Chintha Kulathunga, No. 14/7, Wisal Sewana, 
Pananwala, Delgoda; Extent: 0.0177 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in th e aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat,  
Wattala on the 23rd day of March, 2015 at 10.00 a. m. 
and are hereby requested to notify me in writing (with 2 
copies) on or before the 16th  day of March, 2015 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.   
 
 W. G. P REMALAL, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Wattala.    
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Wattala,  
02nd  March, 2015.
