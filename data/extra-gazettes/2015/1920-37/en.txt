THE   UNIVERSITIES   ACT, No.  16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection (4) of Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  Subsection (4) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I,  Sarath Amunugama, Minister of Higher Education and Research, on being satisfied that the administration of  the affairs
of  the Eastern University,  Sri Lanka have been seriously dislocated and that the authorities of  the said University have
failed to restore normal conditions, do by this Order appoint Professor Uma  Coomaraswamy to be the Competent
Authority with effect from July 1, 2015, for the purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of  Vice-Chancellor the
powers, duties and functions respectively which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the Vice- Chancellor of
the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, under the said Act or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By law, Regulation or Rule made
thereunder.
S
ARATH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
26th June  2015.
