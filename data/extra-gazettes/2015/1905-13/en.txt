NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments  made  by  the  Hon,  Governor  of  the  North  Central  Province
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Section 154(f)(4) and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, P. B. Dissanayaka, Governor of the North Central Province, hereby notify that the Following
appointments were made by me with effect from 05.02.2015.
01. Hon. S. M. Peshala Jayaratna Bandara
As North Central Provincial Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Provincial Road
Development, Rural Infrastructure Facilities and Special Projects, Tourism, Education, Information Technology,
Engineering Construction and Cultural affairs.
Ministry Secretary - Mr. L. J. M. G . C. Bandara - SLAS - Grade 1
02. Hon. K. H. Nandasena
As North Central Provincial Minister of Indigenous Medicine, Social Welfare, Probation and Childcare Services,
Environmental and Provincial Council Affairs.
Ministry Secretary - Mrs. D. M. C. K. Chandradasa - SLAS - Grade 1
03. Hon. Punchibanda Ratnayake
As North Central Provincial Minister of Agriculture, Agricultural Product Marketing, Animal Production, Animal
Health, Fisheries and Housing.
Ministry Secretary - Mr. Ananda Kularatna - SLAS - Special  Grade
04. Hon. H. B. Semasinghe
As North Central Provincial Minister of Land, Provincial Irrigation, Rural Development and Women’s Affairs.
Ministry Secretary - Mr. N. G . Panditharatna - SLAS - Grade 1
