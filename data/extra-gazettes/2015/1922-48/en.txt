No. 1922/48 –  FRIDAY, JULY 10, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460)  
Notice under Section 7 
 
My No. : Ds/KGK/4/9/94/143/LA. 
Land Ministry Reference No. 4/3/15/2011/VE/204. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land  which is required for a 
public purpose:- 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of Mobrey Estate bearing an extent of 
about 0.3248 Hectare within the total extent shown in the 
plan No. P.P. Maha 6427 and dated 09.06.2015 prepared 
by senior Superintendent of surveys (Kandy District) 
situated  in the village of Mobrey Estate in the Grama 
Niladari Division No. 267, Hindagala in the Minor 
Division of Gangawata Korale in the Divisional 
Secretarys Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in 
Kandy District Central Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 1— Name of the Land: Mobrey Estate; 
Original Claimant: Mobrey Hotel Company, Level 6, 
Hemas House, No. 75, Braybrook Place, Colombo 02, 
Extent 0.3248 Hectare.  
North: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3143; East: Lot No. 
01 in P. P. Maha 3143; South: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 
3143, ela (C) and Mobrey estate claimed by Government; 
West: ela (Canal), Mobrey Estate claimed by Government 
and Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3143. 
 
All persons interested  in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Kandy and Gangawata Korale on 17th day 
of September 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested 
to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before 10th 
day of September 2015, the nature of their interests in the 
land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for 
the acquisition of the said land, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 B. M. P. G. P. V. BANDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kandy and Gangawata Korale. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Kandy and Gangawata Korale. 
07th day of   July, 2015.
