LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
NUWARA   ELIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of the  Nuwara Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the  (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance,  that   Mr. Kathiravel Sivapragasam
Sachithananthan  of the Nuwara Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as
he has ceased to be a member of the Up - Country People’s front.
H. A. S. FERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office, N uwara Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Nuwara Eliya,
02nd February, 2015.
