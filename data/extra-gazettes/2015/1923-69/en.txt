FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
KANDY    MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Kandy Municipal Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party, to which the former
member  Mr. Neranjan Priyadarshana Wijayarathne belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of
that Kandy Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Mohomed Muththalib
Shiraj Hassan being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of  Kandy
Municipal Council, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mohomed
Muththalib Shiraj Hassan  elected as a member of the Kandy Municipal Council.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
14th of July,  2015.
