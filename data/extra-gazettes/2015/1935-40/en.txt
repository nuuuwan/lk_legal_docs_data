CONVENTION  ON  THE  CIVIL  ASPECTS  OF  INTERNATIONAL  CHILD
ABDUCTION  ACT,  No.  10  OF  2001
Order under Section  2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by section 2 of the Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction
Act, No. 10 of 2001, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice do by this Order specify -
(a) The country described  in the Schedule hereto, as a country in respect of which the provisions of this Act shall
apply ; and
(b) 5th September, 2015 as the date with effect from which the provisions of this Act shall apply, in respect of such
specified country.
WIJEYADASA R AJAPAKSHE,
Minister of Justice.
Colombo ,
05th  October, 2015.
