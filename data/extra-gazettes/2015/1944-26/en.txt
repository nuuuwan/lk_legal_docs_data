ARACHCHIKATTUWA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
Local Authorities (Standard By-Law) Act, No. 06 of 1952
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No.
06 of 1952, I, Asarappulige Fancis Paul Fernando, Secretary of the Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby inform
that Resolution on By-Law pertaining to the Development of Housing Property and Sale of Block Lands, taken under
Resolution No. 524 dated 09.11.2015 should be imposed within the area of authority of Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. F. P . F
ERNANDO,
Secretary,
Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
RESOLUTION
In terms of the powers vested in the Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government in the
Northwestern Province and under Sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act,
No. 06 of 1952 (Chapter 261) to be read with Para (a) of Subsection (1) of Section 2 of the Provincial Councils
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I do hereby determine under Sub section (3) of Section 9 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that By-law pertaining to the Development of Housing Property and Sale of
Block Lands which had been made by Minister in charge of the Subject of Local Government in the Northwestern
Province and had been published in the Part IV(A) of Extraordinary Gazette No. 1882/22 dated 01.10.2014 of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, and amended the said Gazette Notification and published in the Part
IV(A) of Extraordinary Gazette No. 1898/28 dated 20.01.2015 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Laka,
should be imposed within the area of authority of Arachchikattuwa Pradeshiya Sabha in accordance with provisions
of Section 3 of the Local Authorities (Standard  By-Laws) Act, No. 06 of 1952.
