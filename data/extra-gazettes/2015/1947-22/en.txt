FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  DEPUTY MAYOR  OF   THE
KANDY   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  a Deputy Mayor in Kandy Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of Deputy Mayor has
been appointed as Mayor ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which
Sena Dissanayaka of the former  Deputy Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Kandy Municipal Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Deputy Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Asanka Punyajith Bandara Dodamwala to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama,  Returning Officer of  Kandy
Municipal Council acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling
of vacancies in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Asanka
Punyajith Bandara Dodamwala elected as  Deputy Mayor of the Kandy Municipal Council.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Kandy,
29th  December, 2015.
