POLICE  ORDINANCE
NOTICE  on establishing a new Police Division in Sri Lanka Police by Inspector General of Police to investigate grievous
financial crimes under the Article 55 (Chapter 53) of the Police Ordinance being subject to the Cabinet decision and approval
of Minister in charge of the subject.
                          Inspector General of Police.
13th  February  2015,
at Colombo.
Establishment of a new Police Division in the Department of Police - Sri Lanka
01. It is hereby informed by this notice that the establishment of a new Police Division in terms of  the cabinet decision
made on 21.01.2015 and on 12.02.2015 in accordance with the approval of the Minister in charge of the subject of
Public Order.
02. In accordance with the Cabinet paper presented by the Hon. Minister on 21.01.2015 under the topic “Establisment
of Fraud and Corruption Investigation Division” the below mentioned crimes committed in any part of Sri Lanka
shall be investigated by this Division.
i. Grievous financial crimes, Corruption and massive unauthorized projects.
ii. Crimes against public funds and property.
iii. Grievous crimes against national security, public finance, health and environment.
iv. Unlawful enrichment and misuse of official powers
v. Investigating into the money laundering funding of terrorists and illegal transactions.
03. This Division shall function under a Deputy Inspector General of Police and a Director under the supervision of
the Inspector General of Police and, the Division shall have the powers to investigate within all the jurisdictions
established in Sri Lanka.
04. These complaints are forwarded to the Inspector General of Police for investigation by the Secretariat established
by the Cabinet Sub Committee under patronage of the Hon. Prime Minister set up in accordance with the Cabinet
decision and, in addition to that, the special complaints are forwarded by the Cabinet Sub Committee directly to the
Deputy Inspector General of Police in charge of the Division.
