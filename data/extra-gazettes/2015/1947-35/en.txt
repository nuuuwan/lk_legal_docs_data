THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Order Under Section 39(1)
By the Order No. 79 of 18th March, 2014 made under the Proviso
(a) to Section 38 of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460)  and
published in the Gasette Extraordinary No. 1856/13 dated 01.04.2014
was vested in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. But
the possession of the said land has not yet been taken for and on
behalf of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Therefore
I, Wejesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakararathne Bandara Ekanayake,
Acting Minister of Lands, by virtue of powers vested in me under
Section 39(1) of the Land Acquisition Act, do hereby revoke the
Order No. 79 of 2014 dated 18.03.2014 published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1856/13 dated 01.04.2014.
T. B. Ekanayake,
Minister of Lands (Acting).
