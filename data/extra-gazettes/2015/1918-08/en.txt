Ordinance
1. This Ordinance may be cited as Open University of Sri Lanka (Amendment) Ordinance, No. 02 of 2015 and shall
come into force upon publication in the Gazette.
2. The Open University of Sri Lanka Ordinance, No. 1 of 1990 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 591/18 of
January 05, 1990 as last amended by Ordinance published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1839/41 of December 05, 2013 is
hereby further amended in paragraph 16, of the Ordinance as follows :-
(1) by the repeal of sub-paragraph (1) of that paragraph and the substitution there for, of the following :–
‘‘(1) The Open University shall have the following Faculties :–
(a) Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences ;
(b) Faculty of Natural Sciences ;
(c) Faculty of Engineering Technology ;
(d) Faculty of Education ; and
(e) Faculty of Health Sciences. ’’;
