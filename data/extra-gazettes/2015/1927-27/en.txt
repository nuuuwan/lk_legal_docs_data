THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: 4/3/1/119/(IV)/Additional 3. 
   
I hereby give notice, under Sub - section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following lands / 
servitude which is required for a public purpose 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The following allotment of land situated in the Village 
of Nagoda in Grama Niladhari Division of 729 Nagoda 
South Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kalutara in the 
District of Kalutara in Western Province and marked in 
Surveyor General’s Plan No. P. P. K. 4512 and contains 
in extent of 0.0026 Hectare. 
 
01. Lot No. 1 
Name of the Land: Kobeihela; Description: Road; 
Claimant: Government. Previous claimant Jestin Silva; 
Extent: 0.0003 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 2 
Name of the Land: Kobeihela; Description: Road; 
Claimant: Government. Previous claimant U. S. 
Jayaneththi, R. Jayaneththi, S. Jayaneththi; Extent: 0.0007 
Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 3 
Name of the Land: Pulartanwatta; Description: Road; 
Claimant: Government. Previous claimant D. A. 
Mallikarachchi; Extent: 0.0016 Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Kalutara on the 21st 
day of September, 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 14th day of September, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/ 
servitude the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. You should present 
relevant deeds, plans and other documents to prove 
ownership to the land on the inquiry date. 
 
 WIMAL GUNATUNGA, 
 Divisional Secretary,
