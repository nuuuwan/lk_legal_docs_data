PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
EMBILIPITIY A   PRADESHIY A  SABHA
I, Mathara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansa Perera, Returning Officer of the Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha ,  do hereby
declare under     Section 10 A (1) (a) of the (chapter 262 ) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Matara Arachchige
Jayaweera  of the Embilipitiya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of   member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha as he  has ceased
to be a member of the United National Party.
P
ADMASIRI  CHANDRAW ANSA PERERA,
Returning Officer,
Embilipitiya Pradeshiya Sabha .
District  Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
12th May, 2015.
