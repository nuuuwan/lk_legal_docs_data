SPORTS    ACT,   No.   25   OF  1973
Order under Sections 32, 33 and 34
IN terms of the powers vested in me under Sections 32, 33 and 34 of the Sports Act,  No. 25 of 1973, I, Navin Eranjan
Dissanayake, Minister of Tourism and  Sports, do hereby inform that the appointments of the office bearers of the Sri Lanka
Judo Association  are cancelled from 15
th of  May 2015  and the names of  persons given below are  appointed from 15th of
May 2015 to 15 th of  July 2015 in  order to carry out the functions of  Sri Lanka Judo Association until an  an election is held
or until further notice.
1. E. S. Kithsiri De Silva - President
2. Dr.  Sampath P. Weerasinghe - Secretary
3. M. A. S. Nandana Wijesinghe - Treasurer
4. Lakshman Joshep Gunasekara - Vice President
 Members
1. K. B. Ranjith Dayananda
2. P. C. Pushpakumara
3. P. W. M. R. Lalith Bandara Angunawala
N
A VIN ERANJAN DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
At Colombo,
16th  May  2015.
