 PARLIAMENTARY  ELECTIONS  ACT. No. 1  OF  1981
 Filling of a Vacancy  under Section 64 (5)
WHEREAS the Secretary General of the Parliament has informed me under Section 64(5) of the  Parliamentary Elections
Act, No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Section 6 of  the Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 35 of 1988,  that a vacancy has
occurred in the membership of the Eighth Parliament by reason of the resignation of Mr. Sarath Chandrasiri Mayadunne
being a member nominated by the People's Liberation Front, a recognized political party, and declared elected as a Member
of Parliament under Article 99A of the Constitution ;
And  whereas the Secretary of that Political Party was required by me under the aforesaid Section to nominate an
eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid Political Party has nominated  Mr.  Bimal Niroshan Rathnayake Weerakoon
to fill such vacancy ;
Now therefore, I  Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the  powers conferrd on me by
Section 64 (5) of the Parliamentary Elections Act. No. 1 of 1981,  do hereby declare Mr.  Bimal Niroshan Rathnayake Weerakoon,
elected as a member of the Eighth Parliament to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of aforesaid Mr .Sarath Chandrasiri
Mayadunne.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
14th September, 2015.
