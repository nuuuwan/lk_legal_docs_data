Order under Section 39 (1)  of  the Sports  Act, No 25 of  1973 and Section 54 (3) of  the National
Sports Associations Regulations
I, Navin Eranjan Dissanayeke, Minister of Tourism and  Sports, in terms of   Order  No.1907/22 dated  25th  March   2015  under
Section  39  of the  Sports Act, No. 25 of   1973 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1907/ 22 dated 25th  March 2015 should
have called nominations  from all  National   Sports Association for  elections prior to  15 th of  May 2015.
But, it was not possible to hold elections. Therefore I, do hereby, under Section 39 (1) of  the Sports Act, No. 25 of
1973 inform all the under mentioned Sports Associations that I have appointed   Mr. K. D. S. Ruwanchandra, Director General
of  the Department of Sports Development as Competent Authorty for the smooth functioning of  Sports Associations that
failed to hold elections in lawful manner during the specified periods of  time till such elections are held.
Accordingly  in pursuance of   power vested in  me in respect of   the Sports   Association mentioned below, by order
published in Gazette Extraordinary No.1825/38  dated 30th August  2013 under  Section 41 of  the Sports Act, No.25 of 1973.
I, do hereby direct Mr. K. D. S. Ruwanchandra to convene and hold elections with respect to the following  National
Associations to elect office bearers.
1. Badminton
2. Hockey
3. Rugby  Football
4. Golf
5. Tennis
6. Bridge
7. Boxing
8. V olleyball
9. Rowing

AVIN ERANJAN DISSANAYEKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
