Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa

 Allotments of land (Lots A to E) in extent of about 0.0286 Hectare,

Division of Mirigama in Gampaha District in Western Province and

01. Lot ‘A’ – Jayabima – Extent: 0.0006 Hectare

(R.D.A.); West: Lot C, Jayabima claimed by W. M. Chandrajith
Bandara and balance portion of same land.

North: Road (R.D.A.); East:  Balance portion of same land;
South: Balance portion of same land and Canal; West: Canal and
Road (R.D.A.).
