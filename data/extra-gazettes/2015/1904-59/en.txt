FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  VICE   CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
NUWARA - ELIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in  Nuwara - Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to
be a member of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Up - Country Peoples Front,  the recognised political party to which
Mr. Kathiravel Sivapragasam Sachithananthan the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1)
of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party
who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Periyasamy Sivasamy Pathmanathan to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of  Nuwara - Eliya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Periyasamy
Sivasamy Pathmanathan  elected as Vice Chairman of the Nuwara - Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. Fernando,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara - Eliya,
03rd March,  2015.
