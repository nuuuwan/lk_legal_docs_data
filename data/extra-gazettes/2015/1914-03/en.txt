WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  PADDY  HULLING  TRADE
IT is hereby notified under Regulation 30 of the Wages Board  Regulations published in Gazette No. 14961 dated 04.06.1971
that the Hon. Minister of Labour has been pleased to appoint the following persons to be members of the Wages Board for the
Paddy Hulling Trade, by virtue of the powers vested in the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 09 of the Wages Boards
Ordinance (Chapter 136) No. 27 of 1941, for a period of three years commencing on 01st April 2015.
H
ERA TH YAPA,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
30th April 2015.
