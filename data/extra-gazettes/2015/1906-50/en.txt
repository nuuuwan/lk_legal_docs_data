Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a)
MINIPE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of the  Minipe  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the  (Chapter 262)  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance,  that  Mr. Bandaranayake Mudiyanselage
Shantha Sisira Kumara Bandaranayake of the Minipe Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of   member of that   Pradeshiya
Sabha as he  has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
 SAMAN SRI RA THNAY AKE,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office,  M inipe Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kandy,
20th March, 2015.
