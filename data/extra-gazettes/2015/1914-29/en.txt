FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
MAHAWA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a member
of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United National Party to which the former
member, Anura Bandara Imihami Mudiyanselage belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that Mahawa
Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Dissanayaka
Mudiyanselage Sarath Dissanayaka being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Karunasinghe Jayarathnage Sampath Madhawa,  Returning Officer of  Mahawa Pradeshiya
Sabha acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage
Sarath Dissanayaka elected as a member of the Mahawa Pradeshiya Sabha.
K. J. S. M
ADHAWA,
Returning Officer,
Mahawa  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kurunegala,
12th  May, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF   VICE CHAIRMAN   OF   THE
PALINDANUWARA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Vice Chairman in Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the   United People's Freedom Alliance the recognized political party to which
Ranaweera Patikirige Bandupema  the former vice chairman belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha who  belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as a Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to communicate his decision
within the period specified  ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of  Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Athula Priyantha Bellana  who had
secured the next highest number of preferences at the election held on 17th March 2011 to Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha
elected as Vice Chairman of the Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which such vacancy occured.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
12th  May, 2015.

NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A(1)(a)   OF    THE    LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS    ORDINANCE
Bandarawela   Pradeshiya   Sabha
I, Suranga Ambagahathenne, Returning Officer of the Bandarwela Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) of the Local Authorities Election Ordinance that Mr. Sachchidanandan Suren Kanna of the
Bandarawela Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as he has ceased to be a member
of the United National Party.
S
URANGA AMBAGAHATHENNE,
Returning Officer,
Bandarawela Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
12th  May, 2015.
