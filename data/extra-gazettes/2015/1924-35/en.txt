FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
DELFT   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Delft Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of failing to make and subscribe
an oath or affirmation in terms of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to
which the former member, Vayithilingam Sangarappillai belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member
of that Delft Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to communicate his decision
within the period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj,  Returning Officer of  Delft Pradeshiya Sabha acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Santhiyagappu Castona  elected as a
member of the Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Delft Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
05th  July, 2015.
