Order under Section 4(1)
WHEREAS AN industrial dispute in respect of the matter specified in the statement of the Commissioner of Labour which accompanies this

Mrs. R. A. D. N. Rupasinghe, No. 4 A,  Amarathunge Mawatha, Thalapathpitiya Road, Udahamulla, Nugegoda,  of the one part

vested in me by Section 4(1) of the Industrial Disputes Act, Chapter 131 of the Legislative Enactments of Ceylon (1956 Revised Edition),
as amended by Acts  Nos. 14 of 1957, 4 of 1962 and 39 of 1968 (read with Industrial Disputes - Special Provisions) Act No. 37 o f 1968

Hector Kobbekaduwa Agrarian Research and Training Institute, No. 114, Wijerama Mawatha, Colombo 07, of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is –
Whether Mrs. R. A. D. N. Rupasinghe, who had been  promoted to the post of Administration Officer (Programmes) – Grade II and worked
in that post from 20.12.2012 to  31.12.2013 has been caused injustice by the cancellation of the post and being placed in the post held earlier
from 01.01.2014, and if so, to what reliefs she is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 20th day of November, 2015.
