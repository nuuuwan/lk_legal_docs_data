BUDDHIST   TEMPORALITIES   ORDINANCE
Order  under Section 3
BY virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Buddhist Temporalities Ordinance (amended), I, Karu Jayasuriya,
Minister of Buddhasasana of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do here by this order, cancel the order of
Extraordinay Gazette Notification bearing the No. 1614/30 dated on 14.08.2009 on exempting Ruhunu Maha Katharagama
Devalaya from the operation of provisions of paragraph (cc) of Section 14 of that Ordinance and also cancel the order of
Extraordinary Gazette Notification bearing the No. 1801/39 dated on 15.03.2013 on exempting Ruhunu Maha Katharagama
Devalaya from the application of the provisions” so however that the aggregate of such period shall not exceed two years”
specified in paragraph (b) of the provision to selection 12 of that ordinance with effect from 17th of March 2015.
K
ARU JAYASURIYA,
Minister of Buddhasasana.
Colombo,
10th March 2015.
