FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE OF   VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  WATTALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Vice Chairman  in  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of   not attending
meetings  of  the  Council for a period of  three months without  permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Arachchige Nilantha Leshan Sanjeewa Senasingha Senarathna  the   former  Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me
under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs
to such party who shall be declared elected as Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has failed to communicate   his decision
within the period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE I,  Pathiraja  Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs.  Paranawithanalage Surani Manjula
who had secured the next highest number of preferences at the election held on 17th March 2011 to Wattala    Pradeshiya
Sabha elected as Vice Chairman of  the  Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
P. A. S.  S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
24th  February, 2015.
