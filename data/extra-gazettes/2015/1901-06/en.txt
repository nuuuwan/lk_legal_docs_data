No. 1901/6 –  TUESDAY, FEBRUARY  10, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
My No.: Sri Japura/EA/10/RD(01). 
 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the 1964 No. 28 of 
Government intends to acquire the following lands which 
are required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land  to be acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan No. —  Co. 9511. 
 
Position —  Colombo District Sri Jayawardhanapura 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Municipal Council 
Limits of Kotte, Village of Pagoda East, Lots from 1 in 
total extent of about 0.0007 Hectare, of land in 
Preliminary Plan No. Co:9511 and dated 26th December, 
2014 Surveyor Superintendent of Pagoda East, situated in 
the Western Province. 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Mirihana Road Asst. No. 30/1A 
Part of the Land; Description: Road; Claimant: State; 
Original Claimant’s Name Address: C. L. P. Daiyas 
Kostha; Extent: 0.0007 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agent duly authorized 
in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office           
Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte on the 20th April, 2015 at 
10.30 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 10th day of April, 
2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 AMAL J. S. S. EDIRISOORIYA,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Sri Jayawardhanapura, Kotte. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Sri Jayawardhanapura, Kotte,
