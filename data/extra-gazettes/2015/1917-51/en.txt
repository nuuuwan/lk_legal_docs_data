FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
NALLUR   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Nallur   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, to which  the
Former  member  Thambimuthu Thuraimani   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Nallur
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected  as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has  failed to make  a nomination within
the period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE,  I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj,  Returning Officer of  Nallur  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs.  Amirthanathan Niranchanathevi  who had
secured the highest number of perferences   next to  the last of  the member declared  elected at the election held on 23rd July
2011 to Nallur   Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the Nallur Pradeshiya Sabha in respect of which  such vacancy
occurred.
D. C. A
RAVINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Nallur   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Jaffna.
03rd  June  2015.
