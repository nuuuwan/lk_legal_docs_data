EXTRAORDINARY
The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
(Published by Authority)
PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government   Notifications
SPORTS ACT, No. 25 OF 1973
Order under Section No. 54 (3) of the National Sports Association Regulations
I, Dayasiri Jayasekara, Minister of Sports, by virtue of powers vested in me in terms of Section 54 (3) of  the Gazette
(Extraordinary) No. 1825/ 38 published on 30th of August 2013 under Section 41 of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973, do
hereby direct Kaludura Shantha Ruwanchandra de Silva, the Director General of  the Department of Sports Development
to convene and conduct the Election of Sri Lanka Cricket to elect new office bearers.
DAYASIRI JAYASEKARA (Attorney at Law),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
03rd  November, 2015.
