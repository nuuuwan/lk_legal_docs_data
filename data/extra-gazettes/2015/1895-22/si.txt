THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the
Land Acquisition Act,as Amended by the land Acquisition
(Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the Government intends
to acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
An allotment of land maked as lot No. 01 in total extent of
1.0670 Hectares depicited in B. P. G. 3721 of 26.07.2010 made
by Surveyor General situated in the Village of 134 A,
Panagamuwa in Divisional Secretary's division of Akmeemana,
District of Galle, Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 01
Name of land: Walahanduwawatta; Description: Home
Garden, 23 Permanent Buildings; Original Claimants: Chairman,
Land Reforms Commission; Extent: 1.0670 Hectares.
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on
which servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear
personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, before me
at Divisional Secretariat, Akmeemana on the 18th day of
February 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify
to me in writng (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of
February 2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the
particulars of their claims for compensation and the details of
the said land/servidude* the amount of compensation and
the details of the computation of such amount.
