CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Announcement
I, Sarath Ekanayake, as the Chief Minister of the Central Province, and as the Minister in charge of the subject of Local
Government, do hereby enforce following Regulations in respect of allowances payable to the Mayors, Deputy Mayors and
Members of the Municipal Councils, according  powers vested  in me under Section 289 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
Chapter 252 to be read  with the Section 02  of the Provincial Councils Act No. 12 of 1989 (Consequential Provisions). These
Regulations shall be effective from the 01st of November 2014 and  the Regulations published in the Gazette of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, bearing No.  1391/25  and dated 06th May 2005, in respect of this subject shall  hereby replaced
effective from the date preceding the said date.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKE,
The Chief Minister and the Minister in charge of the
subject of Local Government,
Central Province
The Chief Minister’s Office,
RULES
01. These Regulations shall be called and known as Regulations in respect of payment of allowances to Mayors,
Deputy Mayors  and Members of  the Municipal Councils in Central Province.
02. An allowance as mentioned below may be payable to the Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Members  elected      to the
Municipal Councils of the Central Province, effective from 01st of November, 2014.
Mayor – Rs. 30,000.00
Deputy Mayor – Rs. 25,000.00
Member – Rs. 20,000.00

03. 1. The Mayors, Deputy Mayors and Members  of the Municipal Councils shall not entitle for obtaining  these
allowances under following instances.
i. Failure to attend Council meetings  and committee meetings without  the approval of  the Council in respect of
the relevant month for which allowances are payable.
ii. Going abroad for private purpose, and outside the functions of the Councils.
iii. Being in custody or imprisonment in respect of any criminal charges.
03. 2. A Mayor, Deputy Mayor or a  Member shall not lose his right to claim allowances  for failure   to attend Council
meetings or committee meetings, when they are engaged  on any offficial function with the formal approval,
irrespective of whatever mentioned in Sub section (i) of Section 1 of this Regulation.
