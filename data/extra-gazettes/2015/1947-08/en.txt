WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Proclamations made by Governors by virtue of powers entrusted under paragraph 8(a) of clause 154(b) of the
Constitution
Proclamation
To all whom those presents shall come :
I, Kanapathipillai Cathiravelpillai Logeswaran, the Governor of the Western Province, in accrodance with the powers
vested in me by Paragraph 8(a) in clause 154“b” of the Constitution of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do
hereby summon the Western Province Provincial Council to meet on 31st December 2015 at 9.30 a. m. in order to
reconsider the Appropriation Statute No. 04 of 2015 for the Financial Statement for the year 2016 in the Council Chamber
of the Provincial Council Building Complex situated at No. 204, Denzil Kobbekaduwa Mawatha, Battaramulla.
K
ANAPATHIPILLAI C A THIRAVELPILLAI  L OGESWARAN,
Governor of the Western Province.
Secretariat of the Governor,
109, 5th Floor,
Rotunda Tower, Galle Road,
Colombo 03,
28th December 2015.
