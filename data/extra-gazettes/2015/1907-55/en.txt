NOTICE   UNDER   SECTION  10A(1)(a) OF   THE
LOCAL    AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
KIRINDA   PUHULWELLA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I,  Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of the Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby
declare under Section 10A(1)(a) of the (Chapter 262) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Wellappuli Arachchige
Shanaka Janapriya of the Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of member of that Pradeshiya Sabha as
he has ceased to be a member of the United National Party.
W. H. R. W
IJAYA KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Kirinda Puhulwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Matara,
23rd  March, 2015.
