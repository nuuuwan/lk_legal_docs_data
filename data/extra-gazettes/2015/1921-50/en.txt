SABARAGAMUWA    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification  under the Section 184 of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)  and the Provincial Council
(Consequential Provisions) Act, No.12 of 1989
I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Health and
indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment, News,
Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, In terms of the powers vested in me under section 184 (1A)  of the
Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255), do hereby notify that the duration of the Commission of Inquiry comprising retired
High Court Judge, Saman Wickrama Wickramaarachchi Esquire, appointed by the Extra Ordinary Gazette No. 1908/34 dated
the Thursday, Second  April, 2015 in the  Part IV (A) – Provincial Council Notifications  of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, to inquire whether  the matters mentioned under section 184 (1)  of the Urban Council Ordinance, were committed
by the Chairman, Ven. Vewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero, while holding his office as the Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban
Council  and to submit a report , is extended further 03 months.
I also notify that the provisions  mentioned in the section 2 and 3 of  the said gazette  will be operated further.
M
AHEEPALA HERATH
Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order,
Finance and Planning, Local Government,
Health and indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs,
Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and
Estate Welfare, Environment, News, Housing and
Construction  of the Sabaragamuwa Province.
Chief Ministry of the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
New Town, Ratnapura,
On this 30th of June, 2015.
