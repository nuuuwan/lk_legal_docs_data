PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Appointments  & C., by   the President
No. 376/ 1  of  2015
P.S. No.: CSA/1/2/4/1.
IT is hereby notified that HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT has been pleased, under paragraph (2) of Article 43 of the Constitution
of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, to appoint Hon. (Dr.) Sarath Leelananda Bandara Amunugama, Member of
Parliament, as the Minister of Special Assignment, with effect from October 23, 2015.

SUMITH ABEYSINGHE,
  Acting Secretary to the President.
