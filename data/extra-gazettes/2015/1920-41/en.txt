02. The Government Agent/ District Secretary of Jaffna will be the Returning Officer for Jaffna Electoral District
which comprises of Jaffna and Kilinochchi Administrative Districts while the Government Agent/District Secretary of Vavuniya
District will be the Returning Officer of Vanni Electoral District which comprises of Mannar, Vavuniya and Mullaitive
Administrative Districts. The Returning Officer of Digamadulla Electoral District will be the Government Agent/ Returning
Officer of Ampara Administrative District. The Returning Officers for the remaining Electoral Districts will be Government
Agent/ District Secretary of the respective Administrative Districts.
03. Deposits can be made during the period commencing from the day after 26.06.2015, the date on which the Notice
Under Section 10 of the Parliamentary Elections Act was published and ending at the time before which the nominations of
any Independent Group have to be delivered to the Returning Officer, on weekdays during office hours.
04.  Arrangements for receiving of deposits are made at the relevant District Secretariat or the  District Elections
Office.
