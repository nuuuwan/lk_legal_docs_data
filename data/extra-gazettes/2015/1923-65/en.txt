Government Notifications

Direction No. 59
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Direction  under Section  12(2)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 12(2) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority directs all Manufacturers, Importers, Distributors and Traders that they shall not manufacture,
import, distribute, store or sell or display for sale, expose for sale or offer for sale, wholesale or retail any item/s of
locally manufactured and/or imported protective helmets for vehicle users classified below, unless such helmets bear the
SLS product certification mark issued by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution, against the Sri Lanka Standard Specification
for protective helmets for vehicle users (SLS 517).
Type A - intended for competitive events and for use by wearers who demand an especially high degree of protection
Type B - intended for the ordinary motor cycle riders on public roads
This Direction shall come into effect from 1st of September 2016.
                                                                          By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority.
