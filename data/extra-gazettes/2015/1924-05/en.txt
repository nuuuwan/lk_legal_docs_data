THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below is needed for
a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of that
Act.
An allotment of land depicted  in Advance Tracing No. R/NVT/
2012/547 dated 30.11.2012,  prepared by the Surveyor-General,
situated in the Village of Karawita in Minor Division of Meda
Paththuwa in Nawadun Korale  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Nivitigala of Ratnapura District in Sabaragamuwa Province and
bounded as follow.
1. Lot ‘A’ –  Delgodagewathehena, Delgahawattehena,
Peelabadahena, Kiralagewattehena, Arambagewatta – Extent:
0.0506 Hectare
North: Delgodagewathehena, Delgahawattehena, Peelabadahena,
Kiralagewattehena, Arambagewatta Incumbent, Udakarawita  Sri
Isipathanaramaya; East : Delgodagewathehena, Delgahawattehena,
Peelabadahena, Kiralagewattehena, Arambagewatta Incumbent,
Udakarawita  Sri Isipathanaramaya and Road (Pradeshiya Saba);
South: Road (Pradeshiya Saba) and Delgodagewathehena,
Delgahawattehena, Peelabadahena, Kiralagewattehena,
Arambagewatta Incumbent, Udakarawita.  Sri Isipathanaramaya;
West: Delgodagewathehena, Delgahawattehena, Peelabadahena,
Kiralagewattehena, Arambagewatta Incumbent, Udakarawita  Sri
Isipathanaramaya.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
