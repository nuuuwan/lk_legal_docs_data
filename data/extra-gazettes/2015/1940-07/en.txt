SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973  OF  THE  NATIONAL  STATE  COUNCIL
REGULATIONS made under Section 41 of Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 to read with Section 31 thereof.
DAYASIRI JAYASEKARA,
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
09th  November, 2015.
Regulations
I do hereby amend the Sections 13 and 17 of regulations published on National Sports Associations No. 1 of 2013
published in the Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 1793/03 dated 15th January 2013 as follows :
(1) In Regulation 13(1) by substitution of words ‘‘any of the above positions’’ given after the words, “any person”
of the words ‘‘the same office’’.
(2) By insertion the following new proviso immediately after the proviso in Regulation 13(2),
‘‘Provided further that the aggregate period of four years should be computed from fifteenth (15th) January 2013,
the date the Regulations came into operation.’’
(3) In Regulation 17, by substitution of words ‘‘by secret vote’’ of words ‘‘by show of hands’’.
