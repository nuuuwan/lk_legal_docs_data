Order No. 36.
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority orders that no producer, trader or distributor shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display for
sale the following item, above the Maximum Retail Price specified hereunder :
Maximum Retail Price
Frozen or chilled Broiler Chicken Meat (whole chicken) without skin Rs. 480/- per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 10th December 2015.

PART I : SEC. (I) - GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY   OF   THE   DEMOCRATIC   SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC   OF   SRI   LANKA - 10.12.2015

Order No. 37.
CONSUMER   AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No.  09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the
Consumer Affairs Authority by Order No. 35 published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1942/14 dated 24th November
2015 the following  Maximum Retail Prices were introduced in respect of the items listed below :
Product Maximum Retail Price
Dried Sprats Rs. 410/- per kg (Un Packetted)
Dhal Rs. 169/- per kg (Un Packetted)
Dried Fish “Katta” Rs. 1,100/- per kg (Un Packetted)
Dried Fish “Salaya” Rs. 425/-  per kg (Un Packetted)
Chickpeas Rs. 169/- per kg (Un Packetted)
The Consumer Affairs Authority acting under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the said Act, orders that
manufacturers, importers, packers, distributors or traders of the above items may add a further sum not exceeding Rs. 5/-
per pack/packet irrespective of the weight, if the items listed above are offered for sale, exposed/displayed for sale in
packs/packets.
This Order shall come into effect from 10th December, 2015.
                                                             By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
H
ASITHA TILLEKARATNE,
Acting Chairman.
Colombo,
10th December, 2015.
