THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of  Lands, hereby declare under Sub–section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called ‘‘Galhenakanda Mukalana’’ in total
extent of about 0.0759 Hectare, shown and described in the Advance
Tracing No. KU/NRM/2013/788 dated 23.04.2014, prepared by the
Surveyor General, situated in the village of Handapangala in
Dambadeni Udukaha West Minor Division in the Divisional
Secretary's Division of Narammala in Kurunegala District, North-
Western` Province and bounded as follows. :
01. Lot ‘‘A’’ –  Galhenakanda Mukalana – Extent: About
0.0759 Hectare
North: Lot No. 32 in Final Village Plan No. 568; East: Lot No. 33
in Final Village Plan No. 568; South: Lot No. 33 in Final Village Plan
No. 568 and Road (Pradeshiya Sabha);West: Road (Pradeshiya
Sabha).
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands,
