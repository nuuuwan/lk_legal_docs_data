No. 1906/12 –  TUESDAY, MARCH  17, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No: ෙවන්/පෙද්/ඉඅත්/98/5 (11) 
Land Ministry Ref No: LD/05/2006/E/191. 
 
I, hereby give notice under sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the land Acquisition Act that the Government intends to 
acquire the following land for the CH/Wen/st. Rita 
(Thaldeka) Primary School. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land called Madangahawaththa in 
total extent of 0.2020 Hectar e depicted as lot No. 1 in 
Preliminary plan No: p.p.pu. 4950 prepared by the 
Surveyor General situated in the village of Thaldeka 
Kammal Paththawa Province Minor division in the 
Divisional Secreary's Division of Wennappuwa in 
Puttlam District North Western province. 
 
  Lot  No. 1 
  Name of the land: Madangahawaththa; Propose: for the 
CH/Wen/st. Rita (Thaldeka) Primary School; Claimant: 
H.A. D. M.M. Irangani; Extent: 0.2020 Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing. before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Wennappuwa on the 08th day of April, 2015 
at 10.30am and are heeby re quested to notify me in 
writing (in duplicates) on or before the 01st day of April, 
2015 the nature of their interests in the land the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
   U. S. N. F
ERNANDO, 
   Disisional Secretary, 
   Wennappuwa.  
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Wennappuwa. 
23rd February, 2015.
