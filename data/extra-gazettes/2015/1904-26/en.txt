CANCELLATION  OF   LICENCE   UNDER  THE  FINANCE   BUSINESS   ACT,   No. 42   OF   2011
THE public is hereby informed that the Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka, acting in terms of Section 37 of the
Finance Business Act, No. 42 of 2011, has cancelled the Finance Business licence issued to MCSL Financial Services Ltd., of
11th Floor, Bank of Ceylon Merchant Tower, 28, St. Michael's Road, Colombo 03, Sri Lanka with effect from 01.01.2015.
