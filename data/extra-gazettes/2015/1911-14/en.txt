UV A     PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL     COUNCIL
Announcement made under the Sub section 2(3)(A)(1) of 2011 No. 02, statute about powers to administrate and monitor Local
Government Institutions in Uva Provincial Council
02. According the powers assigned to me under the Sub Section 2(3)(A)(1) of 2011 No. 02 statute about powers to
administrate and monitor Local Government Institutions in Uva Provincial Council, Harin Fernando the Chief Minister of Uva
Province and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local Government, Social Welfare and Rural
Infrastructure Development and Construction, suspending the duties assigned to Mr. Rajapakse Mudiyansalage Abeysekara
as the Chief Executive (Chairman) of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District with effect from 22.04.2015.
03. As the duties of the Chief Executive of Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District has been suspended under
the Sub Section 2(3)(A)(1) of  2011 No. 02 Statute about powers to administrate and monitor Local Government Institution in
Uva Provincial Council the authority will be command to Mr. Herath Mudiyansalage Jayathilaka, the Deputy Chairman of
Welimada Pradeshiya Sabha in Badulla District to implement this authority and to fulfill the duties as per the powers assigned
in the above mentioned Statute.
H
ARIN FERNANDO,
Chief Minister of Uva Province
and the Minister of Finance and Planning,  Law and Peace,
Education, Local Government,  Social Welfare and
Rural Infrastructure Development and Construction
