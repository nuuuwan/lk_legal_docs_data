FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
WARAKAPOLA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in  Warakapola   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  United People's  Freedom  Alliance, to
which  the  Former  member  R.A. Kapila Senevirathne  belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of
that  Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected  as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Udahawiyannalage
Sanjaya Sanevirathna  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such  vacancy :
NOW THEREFORE I, Gamalathge Dilip Nishantha,  Returning Officer of  Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Udahawiyannalage  Sanjaya Sanevirathna
elected as  a member of the  Warakapola  Pradeshiya Sabha.
 G
AMALA THGE DILIP NISHANTHA,
Returning Officer,
Warakapola   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Kegalle,
14th May  2015.
