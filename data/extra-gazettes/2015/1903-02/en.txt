PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government  Notifications
SRI   LANKA   ELECTRICITY   ACT, No. 20  OF   2009
Order under Section 52
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me  by Section 52 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of 2009 and upon the recommen-
dations made by the Minister in charge of the subject of Power and Energy, I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka being of the opinion that :-
(a) the land specified in the Schedule hereto is urgently required for the construction of a Power Transmission
Project ; and
(b) the said Power Transmission Project being a public purpose would serve for the general welfare and benefit of
the public.
do by this order declare that  the said land is required for such purpose.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
20th  February   2015.
SCHEDULE
BOUNDARIES  OF  THE  LAND
The land depicted in the Lots 4 E and 4 D in the  Survey Plan No. 1040 dated 25.08.1992 surveyed by C. H. D.
Abeygunawardene, licensed Surveyor, situated in the village of  Siyambalagoda in Grama Niladari's Division of Siyambalagoda
North, in the  Divisional Secretary's Division of Homagama, in the Salpiti Korale, in the Administrative District of Colombo, in
the province of Western of Sri Lanka containing in extent of 5 Acres 2 Roods and 6.625 Perches and bounded as follows :-
North : Road from Polgasowita to Kottawa in Plan No. 1040 ;
East : Depa Ela in Plan No. 1040 ;
South : Depa Ela, Owita and Paddy field of D. M. Manana, Chandra Jayaweera and others and  Kenuwala Owita
of  D. S. Weerawardana and others in Plan No. 1040 ;
West : Kenuwala Owita of  D. S. Weerawardana and others, Kohilawala Kumbura of Leelawathie Wijesinghe
and  others, Land of P. Gunadasa and Road in Plan No. 1040
Area : 5 Acres 2 Roods and 6.625 Perches.
Claimed by :  A. M. Shanil Fernando.
