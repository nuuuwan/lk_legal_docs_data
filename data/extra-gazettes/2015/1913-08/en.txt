 NATIONAL    ZOOLOGICAL    GARDENS    ACT
ZOO  Development and Welfare Fund was established under Section 8 of National Zoological Gardens Act, No. 41 of 1982
and the entrance ticket fees of National Zoological Gardens are being credited to that fund. The rates for the entrance tickets
of Pinnawala Zoo newly opened for the public will be commenced from 01.05.2015 onwards as given below.
M. I. M. R
AFEEK,
Secretary,
Ministry of Tourism and Sports.
23rd  of  April  2015.
Orders
Entrance tickets fees to be charged from local and foreign visitors of Pinnawala Zoo will be commenced from
01.05.2015 onwards as follows :
No. Types of the ticket Ticket Fee (Rs.)
01. Local Adults 100/-
02. Local Children 30/-
03. Local School Groups (Per Child) 20/-
04. Foreign Adults 1,000/-
05. Foreign Children 500/-
06. Foreign Adults (of SAARC countries) 500/-
07. Foreign Children (of SAARC countries) 250/-
** Interpretation
Adults - Children above age of 12 years and adults
Children - Children above 3 years and below 12 years
