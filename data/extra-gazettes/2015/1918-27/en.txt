FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE    MEMBER OF  THE
THIRUKKOVIL   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262) AS AMENDED BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS, the office of  a member  in Thirukkovil    Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized  political party  namely  Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi, to which  the
Former  member  V eluppillei Mahenthiran   belonged  was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days  a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that  Thirukkovil
Pradeshiya Sabha  who shall be declared elected  as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has  nominated Mr. Somasundaram
Pakkiyarasa being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy;
NOW THEREFORE,  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Thirukkovil  Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Somasundaram Pakkiyarasa
elected as a member of the Thirukkovil  Pradeshiya Sabha.
 
THILINA WICKRAMARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Thirukkovil   Pradeshiya Sabha.
District  Elections Office,
Ampara.
10th   June  2015.

(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013  (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Nallathampi  Rasaraththinam   elected as a
