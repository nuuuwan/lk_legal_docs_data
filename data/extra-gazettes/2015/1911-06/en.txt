FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
MEDIRIGIRIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Chairman  in  Medirigiriya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  removal of Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance,  the recognised political party to which
Thebuwaththe Gamaralalage Ananda Sri Dharmasena the former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section
65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such
party who shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognised political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Widana Arachchilage Padmasiri Hemachandra to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Indika Hathurusingha, Returning Officer of  Medirigiriya  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms
of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Widana Arachchilage Padmasiri Hemachandra
elected as Chairman of the Medirigiriya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
I
NDIKA HATHURUSINGHA,
Returning Officer,
Medirigiriya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Polonnaruwa,
21st April,  2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
UDAPALATHA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Up - Country Peoples Front, to which the
former member  Mr. Periyasamy Mayilvaganam belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Balakrishnan Sridar
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thalangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Thalangama, Returning Officer of  Udapalatha
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Balakrishnan
Sridar  elected as a member of the Udapalatha  Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A. C. N. T
HALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Udapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kandy,
17th of April,  2015.
