DECLARATION   AS    PENSIONABLE    POSTS
‘‘  Home Guard Force’’ which was established under Section 52 of Manpower Mobilization and Supplementary Forces Act,
No. 40 of 1985, has been established as an new department under the name of ‘‘Department of Civil Security’’ as per the
Gazette Extraordinary of   Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated 13.09.2006 and it is hereby declared that the
following posts of  the said Department shall be pensionable posts as per  Section 2 of the Pensions Minute.
Post Department
(a) District Officer Department of  Civil Security
(b) Warden Department of  Civil Security
(c) Sub W arden Department of  Civil Security
(d) Civil Security Department of  Civil Security
