SPORTS    ACT,   No.   25   OF  1973
State Assembly
REGULATIONS made under Section 39(1) of the Sports Act No.25 of 1973 by Minister of Tourism and Sports.
NA VIN ERANJAN DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
On this 25th day of March 2015,
At Colombo.
I, Navin Eranjan Dissanayake, Minister of Tourism and  Sports ,in Pursuance of powers vested in me under Section
39(1) of the Sports Act, No.25 of 1973 do hereby extend the term of office of those who function as office bearers referred to
in the Schedule appended until the time of elections due to be held and office bearers are elected on 15
th of May 2015, enabling
them to continue the functions of their respective Associations. And in terms of Regulations made under the Sports Act,
No.25 of 1973 and published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.1793/3 dated 15
th January 2013 elections of all Sports bodies
which have completed two (2) years term of office shall hold elections by 31st March 2015.
Since there were suggestions and objections from National Association in this regard, Attorney General’s advice
was sought and legal advice was obtained on the complaints made to me, from those functioning, in the capacity of the
Ministry of Tourism and Sports. According to the advice “In the case of a person who has served a term of office as President,
Secretary, Treasurer, Vice President, Assistant Secretary, Assistant Treasurer of any National Association or Federation, his
total period of service shall not exceed 04 years if he is to be re-elected, and such a period shall be prior to the imposition of
Sports Regulations or thereafter. Such a person who has exceeded 4 years of service shall not be re-elected” as spelt out in
Section 13(1) and 13(2) of the relevant Gazette Extraordinary. Nominations for elections shall be accepted according to the
above advice.
All those who have not exceeded a total of 4 years inclusive of the 2 preceding years relevant to the post are hereby
kindly informed that they can submit their Nominations. I hereby revoke the approvals granted before under section 13(2) for
2015 elections by me.
