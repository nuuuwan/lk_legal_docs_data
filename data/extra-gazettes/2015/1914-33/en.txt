THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order under Subsection   (4) of Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by  subsection (4) of Section 20 the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978,
I,  Sarath Amunugama, Minister of Higher Education and Research, on being satisfied that the administration of  the affairs
of  the University of  Colombo, Sri Lanka have been seriously disrupted and that the authorities of  the  said  University have
failed   to restore normal conditions, do by   this Order appoint Professor  Lalitha  Mendis with effect from April 30, 2015 to
be the Competent Authority for the purpose  of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of  Vice- Chancellor any power,
duty and function respectively which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the Vice- Chancellor of the
University of Colombo, under the said Act, or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
S
ARATH   AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
13th  May  2015.
