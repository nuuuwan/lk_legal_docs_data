Notification
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under the Article 45(2) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka I, hereby declare that the following functions of the subjects under the purview of Ministry of Power & Renewable
Energy shall be assigned to the Deputy Minister of Power & Renewable Energy.
01. All the parliamentary affairs related to the Ministry of Power & Renewable Energy.
02. Labour relations of all the institutions under the purview of Ministry of Power & Renewable Energy.
03. Coordination of the activities related to the Sampur Coal Power Project.
04. Coordination of all the activities related to the Generation Plan of Ceylon Electricity Board.
