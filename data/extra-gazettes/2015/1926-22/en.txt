THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/KGK/3/2/2012/05 /LA. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following Lands which are required for a  
public parpose :- 
 
Description of the Land  to be acquired: 
 
02 allotments of land  in total extent of 0.0078 
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 -02 in preliminary plan 
No. P. P. Maha 6341 prepared by Surveyer  General 
situated in the Village of Katugastota and in the 
Divisional Secrtary’s Division of Kandy and Gangawata 
Korale in Kandy District Central Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 1    
Name of the Land: Assesment No. 19, Kurunegala 
Road; Claimant: State;   Extent: 0.0007 Hectare. 
 
North: Lot No’s 41, 40 and 39 in P. P. Maha 5779; 
East: Lot No. 39 in P. P. Maha 5779 Lot No. 2 in this 
Plan and Assesment No. 17 in Kurunegala Road Claimed 
by S. Ranaraja; South:Assesment No. 17 in Kurunegala 
Road Claimed by S. Ranaraja and Remaining Portion of 
this Land; West: Remaing Potion of this Land Road 
Claimant not Known and Lot No. 41 in P. P. Maha 5779. 
 
 
2. Lot No. 2    
Name of the Land: Assesment No. 17, Kurunegala 
Road; Claimant: State;   Extent: 0.0071 Hectare. 
 
North: Lot No’s 40, 39, 30  in P. P. Maha 5779 and 
Bridge; East: Bridge and Mahaweli Ganga; 
South:Mahaweli Ganga and Remaining of this Land 
Assesment No. 19 in Kurunegala Road Claimed by R. B. 
Ranaraja and Lot No. 01 in this Plan; West: Lot No. 01 in 
this Plan and Lot No. 40 in P. P. Maha 5779. 
 
Total : 0.0078 Hectare 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land  are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing  before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawatta 
Korale on the 05th day of August  2015 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to  notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 27th day of July 2015 the 
nature of their interests in  the Land the particulas  of 
their claims for compensation for the acquisition of  the 
said Land the amount of compensation and the details of  
the computation of such amount.
