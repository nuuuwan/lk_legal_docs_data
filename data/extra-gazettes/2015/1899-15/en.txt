No. 1899/15 –  WEDNESDAY, JANUARY  28, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7  
    
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2005/E/615. 
 
 
I, hereby  give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following lands which are required for 
purpose to B/Soraguna Maha Vidyalaya. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of lands situated in the Grama 
Niladhari Division of Madawela (157 -F), Village of 
Mahawela, in the Minor Division of Kandapalla Korale, 
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Haldummulla, Badulla 
District in Uva  Province depicted in FVP 45 
Supplementary No. 4 and Sheet No. 5 and dated 
31.12.2008 prepared by the Surveyor-General, containing 
in  Extent 0.2802 Hectare and described as follows: 
 
Lot No. 27 
 
Name of Land: Abagahawaththa Kumbura; 
Description: Open Bare Land; Claimant: State;Original 
Claimant: Soraguna Kuda Katharagama Devalaya, 
Haldummulla; Extent: 0.2802 Hectare. 
 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat, 
Haldummulla on  the 02nd March, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and 
are hereby requested to notify me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 23 rd February, 2015 and the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said land, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 E. M. S. B. JAYASUNDARA, 
 Divisional Secretary (Acting), 
 Haldummulla. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Haldummulla, 
26th January, 2015.
