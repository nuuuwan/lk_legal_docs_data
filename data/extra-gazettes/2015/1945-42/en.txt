EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
MATALE   MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Matale  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of not attending three
consecutive meetings without permission;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  People’s Freedom Alliance,  to
which  the  former  member, Arachchige Thusith Roshan Mirisse   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of
the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for
election as a member of  that Matale  Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Etipola Mapa
Gunathilaka Mudiyanselage Rohana Kumara   being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sudu Dewage Shanika Anuruddha Rupasinghe, Returning Officer of   Matale  Municipal
Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies
in Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Etipola Mapa Gunathilaka
Mudiyanselage Rohana Kumara  elected as a member of the Matale  Municipal Council.
S. D. S. A.  RUPASINGHE,
Returning Officer,
Matale  Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Matale,
18th   December,  2015.
