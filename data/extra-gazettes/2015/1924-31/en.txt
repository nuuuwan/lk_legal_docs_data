AND
Southern Province Road Development Authority, No. 19, Lower Dickson Road, Galle  of the other part
STATEMENT OF MATTER IN DISPUTE
The matter in dispute between the aforesaid parties is–
Whether  the claim made by Mr. M. K. S. M. Dias working at the Southern Province Road Development Authority to be placed in the post
of  Technical Officer - 5(II) from 01.06.1990, Technical Officer - 5(I) from 01.06.1992 and Engineer - Grade 4 from  01.06.1997 in accordance
with  the recruitment procedure of the Authority and to be paid all salary arrears in lieu of the higher grades of the Engineering Service in
respect of the period thereafter as well is just, and if so, to what reliefs he is entitled.
M. D. C. AMARATHUNGE,
Commissioner of Labour.
Dated at the Office of the Commissioner of Labour,
Colombo this 06th  day of July, 2015.
