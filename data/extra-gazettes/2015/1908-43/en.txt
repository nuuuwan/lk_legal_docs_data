FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN
OF THE  ERA VUR   URBAN  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
 LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY   LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   vice Chairman  in  Eravur Urban Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice Chairman has been
appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party   to which
Mohamed Ismail Mohamed Thasleem   the former  Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Eravur Urban Council who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as  Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision to
nominate   Mr. Meera Labbay Rebu Pasam, to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE   I,  Abdul Majeed Mahammadu Kabeer,  Returning Officer of  Eravur Urban Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Meera Labbay Rebu Pasam  elected as Vice
Chairman of the  Eravur Urban Council.
A.M. M.  K
ABEER,
Returning Officer,
Eravur Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
01st  April, 2015.
