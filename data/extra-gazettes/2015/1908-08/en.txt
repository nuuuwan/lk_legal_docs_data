THE  UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  of  1978
Order under Sub Section (4) of Section 20
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Sub Section (4) of Section 20 of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Sarath
Amunugama, Minister of Higher Education and Research, on being satisfied that the administration of the affairs of the
Eastern University, Sri Lanka have been seriously disrupted and that the authorities of the said University have failed to
restore normal conditions, do by this Order appoint Professor Uma Coomaraswamy to be the Competent Authority for the
purpose of exercising, performing or discharging in lieu of Vice-Chancellor the powers, duties and functions respectively
which have been conferred or imposed on, or assigned to the Vice-Chancellor of the Eastern University, Sri Lanka, under the
said Act or any Order, Ordinance, Statute, By Law, Regulation or Rule made thereunder.
S
ARA TH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
30th March, 2015.
