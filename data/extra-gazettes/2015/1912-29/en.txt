Notification
I, Dr. Amarajeewa Jinabandu Satharasinghe, Director-General of Census and Statistics, being the Competent Authority,  appointed under
Section 20 (2) (d) of the Wages Boards Ordinance (Chapter 136),  by the Minister by Notification published in Gazette No. 9,327 of

each of the traders specified in the Schedule hereto is 178.5.
