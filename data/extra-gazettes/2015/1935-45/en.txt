SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Lakshman
Yapa Abeywardana, Acting Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose
in respect of the commodity items specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates
specified in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This Order shall be valid for a period of 04 months
commencing from 9th  October 2015, subject to provisions of Sub section (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity
Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
Order made under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007 and published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1931/7 of 07.09.2015 in respect B’Onions (HS Code No. 0703.10.20) are rescinded with effect
from 9th October 2015.
L
AKSHMAN YAPA ABEYWARDANA,
Acting Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
09th October, 2015.
