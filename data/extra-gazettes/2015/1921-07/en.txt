SPECIAL  COMMODITY  LEVY  ACT,  No.  48  OF  2007
Order under Section 2
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007, I, Mahinda
Samarasinghe, Acting Minister of Finance of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do by this order impose in
respect of the commodity items specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto a Special Commodity Levy, at the rates specified
in the corresponding entries in Column II of that Schedule. This order shall be valid for a period of 04 months commencing
from 29th June, 2015, subject to provisions of Subsection (3) of Section 2 of the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007.
 M
AHINDA SAMARASINGHE,
Acting Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
29th  June 2015.
