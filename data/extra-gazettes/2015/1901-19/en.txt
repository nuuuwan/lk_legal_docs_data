EXCISE  ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 973
MINIMUM  MONTHLY  EXCISE  DUTY  PAY ABLE  BY  ARRACK  AND  BEER  MANUFACTURERS
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Section 28(a) read with Section 16(c) of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) as amended
from time to time, I, Ravi Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Notification order that all arrack and beer manufacturers
should pay a minimum excise duty of Rs. 200 million per month.
02. Liquor Manufacturing Licenses of all arrack and beer manufacturers who shall not fulfill the above condition with
respect to a certain month shall be cancelled with effect from the 01st of the subsequent month. This procedure shall be in effect
from 01.04.2015.
RAV I  KARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of Finance.
Ministry of  Finance,
Colombo,
13th February, 2015.
