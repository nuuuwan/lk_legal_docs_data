EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
KANDY  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT,  No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Kandy  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be a
member of the party  ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  National Party,  to which  the  former
member, Sinniah Sivagnanam   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a member of  that Kandy
Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Thumbane
Thribhuwana Keerthisinghe Dunuweera  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Talangama Appuhamilage Chaminda Namal Talangama, Returning Officer of   Kandy
Municipal  Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling
of V acancies in Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.  Thumbane
Thribhuwana Keerthisinghe Dunuweera   elected as a member of the Kandy  Municipal Council.
T. A. C. N. T
ALANGAMA,
Returning Officer,
Kandy  Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Kandy,
15th   December,  2015.
