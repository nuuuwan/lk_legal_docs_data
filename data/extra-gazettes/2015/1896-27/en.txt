NOTIFICATION
P.S. No. :  CSA/1/3/1/2.
IT  is hereby notified that His Excellency the President in the exercise of powers vested in him, under Article 47(a) of the
Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has removed Hon. Prime Minister, Dissanayake Mudiyanselage
Jayaratne, from the office of the Prime Minister, with effect from January 09, 2015.
By order of  His Excellency,
