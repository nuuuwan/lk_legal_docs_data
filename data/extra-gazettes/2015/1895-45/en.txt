NOTIFICATION
P.S. No. :  CSA/1/3/1/1.
IT  is hereby notified that the following Ministers, who were appointed by HIS EXCELLENCY THE PRESIDENT, in the exercise of
powers vested in him, by Article 44 (1) (b) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, has in terms
of under Article 47 (b) of the Constitution resigned such offices of Ministers :-
(1) Hon. Ibathul Rauff  Hakeem, MP, from office of  the Minister of  Justice, with effect from 28.12.2014 ;
(2) Hon. Basheer Segudawood, MP, from office of the Minister of  Productivity Promotion, with effect from  29.12.2014.
By order of  His Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERA TUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01,
30th  December, 2014.
