No. 1943/18 –   FRIDAY, DECEMBER 04, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDED) ACT No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
              My Reference No.: GODS/LND/05/ACQ/38. 
 
Land Ministry Reference No.: 4/3/9/2011/WS/232. 
 
 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land for the 
construction of intake and Pump House of Kahawatta 
Water Supply Scheme: 
 
Description of the Land or Servitude to be  Acquired: 
 
 
An allotments of land described below bearing Lot 
No. 254 depicted preliminary plan FVP 87 prepared on 
23.02.2015 by the Ratnapura District Superintendent of 
Surveys in total Extent of 0.0343 Hectare situated in 
232A Kotakethana Grama Niladhari Division in the 
village of Kotakethana in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division Godakawela in Ratnapura District. 
 
Name of the land: Thippolewatta, Wijehinnagewatta, 
Dombagaha Watta; Discription: Garden, constructed pump 
& Intake well; Claimants: Mrs. Dharsani ramyalatha, (care 
of Buwanaka) Pallegama Weedgoda, Mrs. L. Nansi Silva, 
Tempel Road Warapitiya, Kahawatta, Mrs. L. 
Wimalawathi, 13/A, Worapitiya Kahawatta, Mr. 
Daddumage Ajith Priyantha, Temple Road Kahawatta, 
Mrs. W. M. Podimanike, Temple Road Warapitiya, 
Kahawatta, Mrs. L. Kanthi Silwa, 50/G, Main Street, 
Kahawatta, Mr. P. M. Chandrasena, Kattange, Kahawatta, 
Mr. Wimaldharma and Mr. L. Vilsan Silwa, Jayasiri 
Home, Temple Road, Warapitiya, Kahawatta. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Godakawela on the 18th day of January, 2016 
at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 11th day of 
January, 2016 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 B. S. G. RUWAN SIRI,  
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Godakawela. 
 
  
Divisional Secretariat, 
Godakawela, 
26th November, 2015.
