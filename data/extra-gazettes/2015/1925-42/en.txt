FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE  OF    VICE CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
V ALIKAMAM   SOUTH   WEST   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE   (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of Vice Chairman  in  V alikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Vice
Chairman has been appointed as Chairman  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi,  the recognized political party to which
Sinnathamby Mahenthiran the former  Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that V alikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall
be declared elected as a Vice Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Arumugam Sinniah Kanesavel to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Thanabalasingam Akilan, Returning Officer of  Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of  the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Arumugam Sinniah Kanesavel
elected as Vice Chairman of the Valikamam South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
T. A
KILAN,
Returning Officer,
Valikamam South West  Pradeshiya  Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
27th  July,  2015.
