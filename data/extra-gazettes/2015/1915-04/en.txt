PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
LAND  REFORMS  ACT,  No. 71  OF  1972
Order under Sub Section (4) of  Section 27 a
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by the Sub section (4) of  Section 27 a of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 of the
Parliament amended by Land Reform (special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981, which should be read with Sub Aricle (2) of
Article 44 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka, I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage
Don Somadasa Gunawardana, Minister of Lands do hereby:-
(a) Amend the Order made under sub section (2) of section 27A of the aforesaid Act, and published in the Gazette
Extraordinary No. 815/10 dated 21 April 1994 relating to the land called and known as Walahanduwa Waththa
depicted in detailed in First Schedule of this order and alienated to Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation
established under Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation Act, No. 04 of 1958, by revoking on grounds of non-
compliance with such terms of conditions relating to consideration for the alienation to Sri Lanka  State
Plantation Corporation, such portion of the said order as relates to the extent of lands depicted in second
schedule hereto which is part of the land called and known as Walahanduwa Waththa.
(b) Further I do hereby declare that upon revocation, the extent of lands shall be re-alienated to Land Reform
Commission.
M. K. A. D. S. G
UNAWARDANA,
Minister of Lands.
Ministry of Lands,
Mihikatha Medura,
1200/6, Rajamalwaththa Road,
Battaramulla.
14 th May, 2015.
