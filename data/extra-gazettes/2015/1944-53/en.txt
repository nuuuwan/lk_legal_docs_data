THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of  Lands, hereby declare  under  Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed  for a public purpose and will be  acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  in total extent of about  1 Hectare depicted
as Lot ‘A’ in Advance Tracing No. R/PLM/2012/1006 of 22.04.2013
prepared by the Superintendent of  Surveys -Ratnapura, situated in
the Village of  Panawenna (F.V .P. 34) of Nawadun Korale Minor
Division,  in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of  Pelmadulla in
Ratnapura District of Sabaragamuwa Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot A  –  Pelmadulla Estate – Extent: About 1.00  Hectare
North and East: Balance portion of  Field No. 21 of Division 5;
South: Balance Portion of  Field No. 21 of Division 5 and Estate
Road; West: Field No. 9 of Division 05.
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
        Minister of Lands.
My No. : 4/3/8/2012/PC161.
Ministry of Lands,
"Mihikatha Medura",
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
25th September 2015.
