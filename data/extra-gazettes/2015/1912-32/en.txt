THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7    
My No.: 
 
 
I, hereby give notice under sub-Section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired:

Hectare, Final Village Plan No. 147, Supplement No. 25, 
Sheet No. 29, prepared by the Senior Superintendent of 
Survey of Kaluthara District on behalf of Survey General, 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of No. 822C 
Bothale, Gangoda in the village of Bothale in Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Bulathsinhala in Kaluthara 
District in the Western Province.
