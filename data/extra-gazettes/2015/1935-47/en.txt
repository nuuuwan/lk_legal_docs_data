No. 1935/47 – FRIDAY, OCTOBER 09, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
Notice under Section 7 
   
 My No.: KDS/HAR/4/2/1/35. 
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4/3/9/2013/WS/426. 
 
I hereby give public notice under Sub -section (1) of 
Section 7 of the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the 
Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that 
the Government intends to acquire the following lands/
Servitude which are required for  laying of distribution 
pipes from New Service Reservoir to Hedeniya under the 
Greater Kandy Water Supply  Project. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land in total Extent of about 0.0632 
Hectare and depicted in Preliminary Plan No.: 
Maha/6423, Surveyed and Prepared by the Senior 
Superintendent of Surveyor of Kandy District, situated in 
Village of Madawala, in Minor Division of Medasiya -
pathtuwa in Harispathtuwa Divisioal Secretariat Division 
in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as 
follows. 
 
1. Lot No. 01 
 
 
Name of Land: Galwetahena; Claimant: H. M. 
Somarathna Banda, No. 245, Henegama, Medawala, 
Harispathtuwa;  Extent: 0.0308 Hectare. 
2. Lot No. 02 
 
 
Name of Land: Galwetehena; Claimant: H. M. 
Somarathna Banda, No. 245, Henegama, Medawala, 
Harispathtuwa;  Extent: 0.0324 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat Galagedara on the 27th day of November, 
2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to 
me in writing (in duplicate)  on or before the 27th day of 
November 2015, the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land/servitude, the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount. 
 
 
 R. M. P. S. B. RATHNAYAKE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Harispaththuwa. 
 
 
At the Harispaththuwa,  
Divisional Secretariat, 
06th October, 2015.
