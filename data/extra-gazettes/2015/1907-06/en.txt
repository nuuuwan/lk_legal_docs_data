PILGRIMAGE   ORDINANCE
Annual Higher Ordination of Rohana Sanga Sabhawa
IT is hereby informed that the 30th Higher Ordination Ceremony of the Sri Rohana Maha Sangha Sabhawa is to be held on
08th, 09th and 10th of May, 2015 at Sri Rohana Sangha Sabhawa of Siri Rathanajothi Piriwena, Urapola, Thihagoda and
Mangala Seemamalakaya of Randupatha Sri Rohana Uposathagara Maha Vihara, Kotuwegoda, Matara and that the pilgrims
should refrain from carrying with them any plastic or polythene materials and that the standing orders and regulations under
Section 02 of the Pilgrimage Ordinance (Chapter 175 - LDB 11/60) declared on 14th October 1991 and published in the Gazette
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, will be in force during the period in which the ceremony is held.
R
ANJITH YAPA,
Divisional Secretary,
Matara.
Divisional Secretariat,
Matara,
18th  March 2015.
