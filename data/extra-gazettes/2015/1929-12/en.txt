CENTRAL   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment  made by  the  Hon.  Governor  of  the  Eastern  Provins   Provincial Council
I, Kalupage Austin Fernando, Governor of the Eastern Province by virtue of power vested in me under Section 154 F (1)
and (5) of  the Thirteenth Amendment to the Constitution of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby
Order publish the resignation tendered on 21.08.2015 by Hon.  Ibrahim Mohamed Mohamed Mansoor, Member of
Provincial Council of the Eastern Provincial Council and declare that he is no more a Minister of  the Board of Ministers
of the Eastern Provincial Council as appointed by order published by me in the Extra  Ordinary Gazette of  the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka No. 1905/ 05 dated 09.03.2015 and Hon. Ahamed Nazeer Zainulabdeen, Member and the
Chief Minister of the Eastern  Provincial Council shall temporarily cover the powers, functions and duties of that vacant
Ministry until a permanent arrangement is made.
A
USTIN F ERNANDO,
 Governor - Eastern  Province.
Governor's Secretariat,
Eastern  Province,
Orr's Hill,
Trincomalee,
25th August 2015.
