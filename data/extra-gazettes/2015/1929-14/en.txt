  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling  of   a  Vacancy  in  the  Central   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the  Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of  Nullification of  the office of a member of  the said Provincial  Council;
And  whereas the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance,  the recognized political party to which the
member   belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  AN. D. Nimal Premawansha
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  AN. D. Nimal Premawansha, elected as a member
of the above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Kandy  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya.
24th August, 2015.
