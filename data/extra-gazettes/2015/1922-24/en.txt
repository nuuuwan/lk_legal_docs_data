FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
VADAMARADCHI  SOUTH  WEST  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in V adamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi to which the
former member, Aalvaapillai Mathiyarasan belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that
Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Ramachandran
Sivachchanthiran being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Denicius Canute Aravintharaj,  Returning Officer of  V adamaradchi South West Pradeshiya
Sabha acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Ramachandran
Sivachchanthiran elected as a member of the Vadamaradchi South West Pradeshiya Sabha.
D. C. A
RA VINTHARAJ,
Returning Officer,
Vadamaradchi South West  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
06th July, 2015.
