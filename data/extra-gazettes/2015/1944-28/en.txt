WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Provincial Road Development Authority Statute No. 04 of 1989 (Western Province)
REMOV AL FROM OFFICE - CHAIRMAN, PROVINCIAL ROAD DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (WESTERN PROVINCE)
ACCORDING to the powers entrusted in me by Section 3(8) of the Part 01 in the Provincial Road Development Authority
Statute No. 04 of 1989 (Western Province), I, Kejitan Lalith Wanigarathne, Minister of Provincial Road, Transport,
Cooperative Development and Trade, Fisheries, Housing and Constructions, Urban and Estate Sector Infrastructure
Facilities, Industries and Rural Development of the Western Province, do by this notice announce that Mohamed Mulappar
Mohamed Amjad, who held the Chirman's Office in Western Province Provincial Road Development Authority was removed
with immediate effect from the Chairmanship and membership of the said Authority.
K
EJITAN L ALITH W ANIGARA THNE,
Minister of Provincial Roads, Transport, Cooperative Development
and Trade, Fisheries, Housing and Constructions, Urban and
Estate Sector Infrastructure Facilities, Industries and
Rural Development of the Western Province.
