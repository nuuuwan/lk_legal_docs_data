THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
Order under Article 98(8)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under paragraph (8) of Article 98 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, do by this Order certify that —
The number set out in Column II of the Schedule hereto be the number of members which each electoral district
specified in the corresponding entry in Column I of the Schedule hereto is entitled to return by virtue of the proclamation
under Article 97 and under Article 98.
M
AHINDA  DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
19th January, 2015.
