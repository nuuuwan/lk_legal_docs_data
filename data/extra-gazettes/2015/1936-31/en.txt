I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act,  as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intended to acquire Description of the land 
to be acquired. 
 
Discription of the Land to be acquired: - 
 
Allotments of land in total extent about 0.1173

P. P. Maha 6452, prepared by the Surveyor General 
situated in the Arabegama Village in Mada Palatha 
Korale Minor Division in Yatinuwara Divisional 
Secretary's Division in Kandy District and bounded as 
follows: 
 
Lot No. 01 - Name of Land: Alpitigodahena; 
Extent of the land: 0.1173 Hectare. 
 
Boundaries: 
 
North: Lot No. 1 & Road in PP/Maha 656 (Pradeshiya 
Sabha); East: Road (Pradeshiya Sabha) and Alpiti -
godahen Claimed by Sirisena Pathirana; South: 
Alpitigodahena Claimed by Sirisena Pathirana and Lot 
No. 1 in PP Maha 5206; West: Lot No. 01 in PP Maha 
5206 and Lot No. 01 in PP Maha. 
 
Name and Address of the claimant: K. B. Giragama, 
No. 14, Arabegama, Pilimatalawa. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at  
Divisional Secretariat  at on the 09th November, 2015 at 
2.00 p. m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 02nd day of 
November 2015. The  nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount.
