THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
   My No :  WEE/LND/AQUIR/44/(WEERA/2014/33).
  Land Ministry Ref. No :  4-3/6/2014/HW/294.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to
acquire the following lands which are required for a public
purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 740 - 741 (Lots 02) allotments in total extent of
about 0.5172 Hectare, of land in FVP No. 261, Supplement
No. 56 and Sheet No. 56 and dated 26.12.2014 prepared by
the Sinior - Superintendent of Survey of Hambanthota
District on behalf  of  Survey General, situated  in  the
Minor Division of Giruwa Paththuwa  North  in  Grama
Niladhari  Division of  Badigama South  in the village of
Badigama in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Weerakatiya
in Hambanthota District in the Southern Province.
01. Lot No. 740
Name of land: Raththagearahena, Gonhitiyawalawatta;
Description: Contains garden, 39 coconut plant trees about
1 - 3 years old, 57 coconut trees about 60 - 65 years old  and
04 palmyra trees; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: D. S. P.
Jagath, No. 385, Mahaweediya, Ambalangoda (Part of the Plot
of Land No. 10S); Extent: 0.4023 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 741
Name of land: Raththagearahena, Gonhitiyawalawatta;
Description: Paddy field; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
D. S. P. Jagath, No. 385, Mahaweediya, Ambalangoda (Part of
the Plot of Land No. 10S); Extent: 0.1149 Hectare.
All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby
required to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat office of
Weerakatiya on the  from 07.10.2015 at  9.30 a.m  and are
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or
before the 30.09.2015  the nature of their interests in the land,
the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said  lands, the amount of compensation
and the details of the computation of such amount.
                                                       C. P. W. W
ANNIARACHCHI,
                                      Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer
                                                            Weeraketiya.
08th September, 2015.
