FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
BUTTALA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL    AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being elected as a member
of Uva Province Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely United National Party to which the former
Member, Wijesingha Herath Mudiyanselage Dharmasena belonged  was required by me under Section 65A (2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within  a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of
that Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Sandanayaka
Mudiyanselage Pemadasa  being an eligible  person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Suranga Ambagahatenne, Returning Officer of  Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha  acting in terms of
Section  65A (2)  of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Sandanayaka Mudiyanselage Pemadasa  elected as a
member of the Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
S
URANGA  AMBAGAHATENNE,
Returning Officer,
Buttala  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Moneragala,
07th March,  2015.
