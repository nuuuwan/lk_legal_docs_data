Order under Section 22 (1) f
BY VIRTUE of the powers vested in me by paragraph (f) Subsection (1) of section 22 of Land Reform Law, No. 1 of  1972
of the National State Assembly as amended by No. 18 of  1986, No. 14 of  1986, No. 39 of  1981  No. 14 of 1981,  and No. 39
of 1975 I, The minister of Lands M. K. A. D. S.  Gunawardena, do by this Order declare that:-
(a)  the agricultural lands specified in Column II to this Order shall vest in the persons whose names are specified
in Column I of the Schedule to this Order;
(b) the agricultural lands specified in the corresponding entry in Column II of the Schedule to this Order were
owned by the persons whose names appear in Column I  of the Schedule to this order and that such agricul-
tural land  were included in the extent of land deemed to be owned by the head of the family of which such
persons were members;
(c) the persons whose names appear in Column I of the Schedule  to this Order were under eighteen years of age
at the  time of the imposition of the ceiling on agricultural land in terms of the aforesaid Law;
(d) the agricultural lands specified in   Column II of the Schedule here to have not been alienated to any person
under section 22 of the said Law; and
(e) no compensation has been paid in respect of  such agricultural  lands under Section 28 of the said law;
(f) It is declared that Government extra ordinary Gazette Notification No. 1568/15 and dated 23rd September 2008
in the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka is here by amended by the said Law.
M. K. A. D. S.  GUNAWARDENA,
 Minister of Lands.
Ministry of land,
Battaramulla,
29th  April,  2015.
