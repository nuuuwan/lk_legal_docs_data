PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  NOTIFICATIONS
NORTH  CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification  under  Section  185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 of
Provincial Council Act (Incidental Provision) of Number 12 of 1989 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
I have received the report prepared by retired judicial officer, K. Sunil Perera Appointed, in terms of the Section 185 (2) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987 to satisfy that Sinhalage Jagath Samarawickrama Chairman of Dimbulagala Pradeshiya
Sabha has committed one, more or all the offences as per the Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987 by
Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Ranjith Samarakoon Former Chief Minister, and Minister of Provincial Law and Order, Finance
and Planning, Local Government, Provincial Irrigation, Rural Infrastructure Facilities, Sport and Youth Affairs. In terms of the
said report it has been authenticated that Sinhalage Jagath Samarawickrama Chairman of Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha has
committed the offences as per the Section 185 (1) i and ii and after the confirmation of such offences, I do here by notify as
per the powers vested to me as The Minister of Portfolio of Local Governmnent, Sinhalage Jagath Samarawikrama has
removed from the post of Chairman and the Membership of Dimbulagala Pradeshiya Sabha with immediate effect.
S
UDASINGHE MUDIY ANSELAGE PESHALA JAYARATHNA BANDARA,
Chief Minister and Minister of Provincial Law and Order,
Finance and Planning, Local Government, Provincial
Road Development, Rural Infrastructure Facilities and Special
Project, Tourism, Education, Information Technology and Cultural Affairs.
On 18th February 2015,
Chief Ministry,
Office of the Chief Ministry.
