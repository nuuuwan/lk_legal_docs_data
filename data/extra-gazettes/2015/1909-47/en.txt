Conferment of Powers — Order
IN pursuance of the powers vested in me under the Section 2(2) of the Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read with Co-
operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992,
I, Mallawaarachchige Gamini Jayawickrama Perera, Minister of Food Security do hereby confer on the persons appointed to
assist the Commissioner of Co-operative Development and the Registrar of  Co-operative Societies and specified in the
Column I of the Schedule hereto, from  the date specified in Column II under Co-operative Societies Law, No. 5 of 1972 read
with Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of
1992 and under the rules made there under, the powers specified in Column III of the said schedule.
M
ALLAWAARACHCHIGE GAMINI JAYAWICKRAMA PERERA,
Minister of Food Security.
02nd April, 2015,
Colombo.
