UNIVERSITIES  ACT,  No.  16  OF  1978
Order under Section 25A
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 25A of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Lakshman Kiriella, the Minister
of Higher Education and Highways, having obtained a report under Section 70 C of the aforesaid Act, in respect of the
Horizon College of Business and Technology (Private) Limited, a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under  the Companies
Act, No. 7 of 2007 and bearing registration number PV 62877, do by this Order, recognize the Horizon College of Business and
Technology (Private) Limited, as a Degree Awarding Institute for the purpose of developing Higher Education therein,
leading to the award of the Degrees specified in Column I of the Schedule hereto, of which the abbreviations are specified in
the corresponding entries respectively in Column II of the Schedule hereto.
SCHEDULE
Column I Column II
Degrees Abbreviations to the Degrees
Master of Business Administration MBA
Bachelor of Management Honours in Marketing BM Hons (Marketing)
