No. 1901/16 – THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 12, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of below mentioned lands in total extent of about
0.6591 Hectare, depicted in Advance Tracing No. CO/DSO/2012/
1083 dated 01.02.2013 prepared by the Senior Surveyor-General of
Colombo District, situated in the Village of  Kothalawala - Kaduwela
in the Minor Division of Palle Paththuwa in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of  Kaduwela in Colombo District in Western Province and
bounded as follows:
1. Lot No. 1 – Assmt. No. 4, Avissawella Road (A Part) – Extent:
0.0005 Hectare
North: Assmt. Nos. 8 and 4, Avissawella Road; East: Assmt. Nos.
4 and 2, Avissawella Road; South: Lot No. 2 and Avissawella Road;
West: Avissawella Road.
2. Lot No. 2 – Assmt. No. 2, Avissawella Road (A Part) – Extent:
0.0150 Hectare
North: Lot No. 1 and Assmt. Nos. 4 and 2, Avissawella Road;
East: Assmt. No. 2, Avissawella Road, Land of Assmt. No not be
found claimed by Municipal Council of Kaduwela and Lot No. 3;
South and West: Avissawella Road.
3. Lot No. 3 – Assmt. No not be found, New Kandy Road
(A Part) – Extent: 0.0096 Hectare
North: Land of Assmt. No not be found claimed by Municipal
Council of Kaduwela; East: Kelani River; South: New Kandy Road;
West: Lot No. 2 and Assmt. No. 2, Avissawella Road.
4. Lot No. 4 – Assmt. No not be found, New Kandy Road
(A Part) – Extent: 0.634 Hectare
North: New Kandy Road; East: Kelani River; South:
Playground claimed by Municipal Council of Kaduwela; West:
Avissawella Road.
