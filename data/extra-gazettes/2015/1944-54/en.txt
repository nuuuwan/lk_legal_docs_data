SRI  LANKA   EXPORT   DEVELOPMENT   ACT,   No.  40  OF  1979
Order  under Section 14
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 14(1) of the Sri Lanka Export Development Act, No. 40 of 1979, I, Malik
Samarawickrama, Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade with the concurrence of the Minister of
Finance, do by this order declare that, a Cess shall be charged, levied and paid on import of any article specified in the
Column II and III of the Schedule hereto at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV in the Schedule
hereto, with effect from 14.12.2015, Accordingly, for every article specified under the H. S. Headings 0511.91.90,
1404.90.10, 2710.19.80, 3214.90.90, 3816.00, 3824.50, 3824.60, 3903.90.10, 3905.12, 3905.21, 3906.10.10,
3906.10.90, 3906.90.10, 4410.11.90, 5804.10.19, 6401.10, 6401.92, 6401.99, 6402.12, 6402.19, 6402.20, 6402.91,
6402.99, 6403.12, 6403.19, 6403.20, 6403.40, 6403.51, 6403.59, 6403.91, 6403.99, 6404.11, 6404.19, 6404.20,
6405.10, 6405.20, 6405.90, and 8418.91.10 mentioned in the Column I of the Schedule of the Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1941/33 dated 20.11.2015, the respective Cess rates mentioned in the corresponding entry in Column IV of that
Schedule are hereby repealed with effect from 13.12.2015.
M
ALIK  S AMARAWICKRAMA ,
Minister of Development Strategies
and International Trade.
Ministry of Development Strategies and International Trade,
Colombo 01.
11th December 2015.
