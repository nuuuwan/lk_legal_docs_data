NORTH   WESTERN    PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Municipal Council  Regulations
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 to be
read with Section 289 Chapter 252 of Municipal Council Ordinance, I, J. P. Dayasiri Pathmakumara Jayasekara, the Chief
Minister of the North Western Province and the Minister in Charge of the subject of Lcoal Government do hereby impose the
regulations relating to the payment of allowances for the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Members of Municipal Councils. These
regulations shall be effective from 01.11.2014.
J. P. D
AYASIRI PATHMAKUMARA JAYASEKARA,
  Chief Minister of the North Westren Province
and the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and
Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration,
Human Resources, Education  and Cultural Affairs,
Lands Environment and Food Supply and Distribution.
Chief Minister in the North Western Province,
Kurunegala,
22nd December 2014.
REGULA TIONS
1. These regulations are cited as the regulations relating to the disbursement of allowances for the Mayor, Deputy
Mayor and Members of Municipal Councils.
2. A monthly allowances per the below mentioned rates should be paid for the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and
Members of Municipal Councils.
Mayor 30,000.00
Deputy Mayor 25,000.00
Members 20,000.00

3. (a) At the following instances the Mayor, Deputy Mayor and Members of Municipal Councils shall not be
entitled for allowances.
i. Being absent at the General meeting and Committee meetings conducted during the month relevant
to the payment of allowances without obtaining the approval from the General Council.
ii. Being remanded or imprisoned on conviction of guilty for any criminal offence.
iii. Suspension of duties of the Members of any Municipal Council in terms of the Statute of Local
Government Authorities (powers of administration and supervision) of the North Western Province
No. 01 of 1991 of the North Western Provincial Council or Section 277 of  Municipal Council
Ordinance.
Provided that in case the period of remand custody, imprisonment, on leave or suspension of
service is a part of a month these allowances may be paid for the rest of the month on proportional
calculation.
(b) Whatsoever is mentioned in Sub Paragraph (1) of  Paragraph (b) of this order a Members' allowances shall
not be deprived in case a Member is absent at a Council meeting on a formal approval to perform an
official duty.
