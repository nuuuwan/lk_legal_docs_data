EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
GAMPAHA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Gampaha  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  National Party,  to which  the  former
member, Guruge Susantha Perera belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid  Ordinance,  to
nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a member of  that Gampaha
Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Mudali Maheepala
Appuhamillage Waruna Pradeep Maheepala  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne, Returning Officer of  Gampaha  Municipal  Council,
acting in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of V acancies in Local
authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Mudali Maheepala Appuhamillage
Waruna Pradeep Maheepala   elected as a member of the  Gampaha  Municipal Council.
P. A. S.  S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha  Municipal Council.
District    Elections Office,
Gampaha.
25th  May,  2015.
