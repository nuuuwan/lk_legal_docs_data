PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
Government Notifications
Direction  No. 53
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS    AUTHORITY   ACT,  No. 09 OF  2003
Direction  under Section 10 (1)  (a)
ACTING  under the powers vested in the Consumer Affairs Authority by Section 10 (1) (a) of  the Consumer Affairs Authority
Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer Affairs Authority directs all traders that they shall cause to be  fixed in some conspicuous
part or place of their shop/ outlet a weighing scale verified for its accuracy by the Department of Measurement Units
Standards and Services, for the  consumers to weigh any items prior to/ affer purchasing if they shall so require.
This Direction shall come into effect from 01st  of May, 2015.
By   Order of the Consumer Affairs Authority,
RUMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
Colombo,
17th  April  2015.
