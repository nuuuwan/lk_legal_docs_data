P.S. No. CSA/1/4/1/1
NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that Hon. Ekanayake Mudiyanselage Mahinda Nandimithra Ekanayake, MP, who was appointed to the
office of Deputy Minister of Higher Education by His Excellency the President, in the exercise of powers vested in him, by
Article 46(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has in terms of under Article 47(b) of the
Constitution resigned such office of Deputy Minister, with effect from January 01, 2015.
By Order of His Excellency,
L
ALITH WEERA TUNGA,
Secretary to the President.
07th January, 2015,
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo 01.
