EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
MORATUWA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in Moratuwa  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United  National Party,  to which  the  former
member, Yon Meregngnage Chaminda Kumara Silva belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of  of  thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a member
of  that Moratuwa   Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Athula Kernal Peiris
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of  Moratuwa  Municipal  Council, acting in
terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local  authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Athula Kernal Peiris  elected as a member of the
Moratuwa  Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Moratuwa  Municipal Council.
City  Elections Office,
Colombo.
23rd   March,  2015.
