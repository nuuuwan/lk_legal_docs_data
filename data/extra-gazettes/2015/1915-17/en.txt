PART IV (B) — LOCAL  GOVERNMENT
Notices under the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A (1) (a)
GAMPAHA   MUNICIPAL COUNCIL
I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathne, Returning Officer of the Gampaha  Municipal Council ,  do hereby declare under
Section 10 A (1) (a) of the (Chapter 262 ) Local Authorities Elections Ordinance that Mr. Guruge Susantha Perera  of the
Gampaha  Municipal Council   has vacated his office of   member of that  Council as he  has ceased to be a member of the United
National Party.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNE,
Returning Officer,
Gampaha  Municipal Council.
District  Elections Office,
Gampaha,
19th May, 2015.
