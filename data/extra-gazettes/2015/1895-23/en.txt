 DFCC  BANK (REPEAL  AND   CONSEQUENTIAL   PROVISIONS)    ACT,   No. 39   OF    2014
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 2 of the DFCC Bank (Repeal and Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 39 of
2014, read with Article 44(2) of the Constitution, I, Mahinda Rajapaksa, President/Minister of Finance and Planning, do by
this Order specify January 6, 2015, as the date from which the DFCC Bank Act (Chapter 165), be repealed.
M
AHINDA  RAJAPAKSA,
President/Minister  of Finance and Planning.
Colombo,
2nd  January, 2015.
