No. 1905/31 –  THURSDAY, MARCH  12, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
Reference No. : Maha/L/Ac/345. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following land which is required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Allotments of land in extent of about 0.0092 Hectare, 
depicted as Lot No. 01 in the Preliminary  Plan No. Co. 
9518 was prepared by the surveyor General of Colombo, 
District, situated in the village of Malapalla, In the Minor 
Division of Palle Paththuwa, in the Divisional Secretary’s 
Division of Maharagama, in the Colombo District in  
Western Province. 
 
Lot No. 01  
Name of the Land: Galabadawatta; Description: 
Garden; Claimant: State; Or iginal Claimant: H. P. 
Amarasena, No. 17/18, Ekwatta Road, Mirihana, 
Nugegoda; Extent: 0.0092 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land hereby 
required to appear personally, or by agents duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretary’s,  Office of Maharagama  on the 29th day of 
April , 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to 
notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or before the 
22nd  day of April, 2015 the nature of their interests in 
the land the particulars of their claims for compensation 
for the acquisition of the said land the amount of 
compensation and the details of the computation of such 
amount.   
 
 B UDDITHARANGA KARUNASENA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Maharagama. 
   
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Maharagama, 
12th  March, 2015.
