PUBLIC  EXAMINATIONS   ACT,  No.  25  OF  1968
Orders issued under the powers vested in the Minister-in-charge of the subject under the Section 22 of the Public
Examinations Act, No. 25 of 1968.
AKILA VIRAJ KARIYAW ASAM,
Minister of Education.
At Colombo,
12th August 2015.
ORDERS
01. These orders are identified as orders of the Public Examinations Act, No. 25 of 1968. This is an amendment
with respect to the order issued under Section 22 of the Public Examinations Act, No. 25 of 1968 published in
the Gazette No. 1816 dated 21.06.2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. This amendment is
not pertinent to the matters 02(ii) and 02(iii) of the aforesaid order.
02. The Commissioner General of Examinations conducts-
i. The examination to select students for popular schools and to award scholarships for students in grade 5
It is completely prohibited to organize supportive classes for the candidates of the said examination
during the total period from 3 days prior to the start to the final day of the examination, to direct such
classes, to conduct subject oriented lectures, seminars, workshops, to print question papers including
guessed question paper for such examination, to distribute them, to notify through posters, banners,
handouts, electronic or printed media that the questions in the question papers of the examination or
questions similar to them will be provided or to keep such documents in the possession. In case a certain
individual or an institution or any other party deemed to have neglected the said orders, such person, or
institution, or party shall be an offender under this act.

KILA VIRAJ KARIYAW ASAM,
