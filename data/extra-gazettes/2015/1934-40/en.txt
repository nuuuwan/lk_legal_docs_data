EXCISE   (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  13  OF  1989
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act, No. 13 of 1989, I, Ravi
Karunanayake, Minister of Finance, do by this Order declare that, the Excise duty on every article specified in the Columns
II and III of the Schedule hereto, corresponding to the every article specified under the following H. S. Heading mentioned
in the Column I of the Schedule I of the Extraordinary Gazette Notification No. 1903/39 dated 26.02.2015 issued under
the Section 3 of this Act, shall be payable at the rate specified in the corresponding entry in Column IV of the Schedule
hereto, with effect from 03.10.2015.
R
AVI K ARUNANAY AKE,
Minister of  Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
02nd October, 2015.
