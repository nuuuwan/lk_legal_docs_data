THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare under Sub–section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The  allotments of land called Palai Town in total extent of about
0.6264 Hectare, shown in the Advance Tracing No. Ki/PCH/2013/
053 dated 28.06.2013, surveyed and prepared by the Surveyor–
General, situated in the Village of Palai Town in the Grama Niladhari
Division of Palai Town, in the Divisional Secretary's Division of
Pachchilaipalli in Kilinochchi District, Northern Province and
bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  – Innasi Estate – Extent: 0.0631 Hectare
North: Innasi Estate; East: Innasi Estate and path; South: Path
and Innasi claimed by Sinnappu Rathinum; West: Innasi claimed by
Sinnappu Rathinum.
02. Lot ‘B’  – Innasi Estate – Extent: 0.1710 Hectare
North: Path and Innasi Estate; East: Innasi Estate and Lot C;
South: Lot C and Innasi claimed by Rajappu Arulanandam; West:
Innasi claimed by Rajappu Arulanandam and path.
03. Lot ‘C’  – Innasi Estate – Extent: 0.3923 Hectare
North: Lot B and Innasi Estate; East: Innasi Estate; South: Innasi
of which claimant not known; West: Innasi  claimant not known and
Lot B.

 Minister of Lands and Land Development.
