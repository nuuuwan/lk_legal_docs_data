CUSTOMS   NOTIFICATION   (GENERAL)
Customs  Ordinance (Chapter 235)
RATES  OF  EXCHANGE
IT is hereby notified that by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 17 of the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235)
I, R. Semasinghe,  Director General of Customs, determine that with effect  from 29th  June, 2015, all  duties of  Customs
as well as  other charges,  penalties and forfeitures incurred under the Customs Ordinance (Chapter 235), shall be   paid and
received at the Rates of Exchange set out in the Schedule.
The notification relating to the Rates of Exchange published in Gazette Extraordinary, No. 1920/1  of
22nd  June, 2015 is  hereby rescinded  and  the said  Rates of  Exchange are Scheduled to be rescinded  by a similar
notification to be published on or before 05th  July  2015.
R. S
EMASINGHE,
Director General of Customs.
Sri Lanka Customs,
Colombo 11,
26th   June   2015.
