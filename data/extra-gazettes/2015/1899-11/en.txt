EXTRAORDINARY
1A
PACHCHILAIPALLI   PRADESHIY A   SABHA
I, Ponnuthurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of the Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby declare under Section
10A(1)(a) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) that Mr. Parnandu Dominic Anton of the Pachchilaipalli
Pradeshiya Sabha has vacated his office of the member of that Council as he has ceased to be a member of the Tamil United
Liberation Front.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Pachchilaipalli Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
26th of January,  2015.
