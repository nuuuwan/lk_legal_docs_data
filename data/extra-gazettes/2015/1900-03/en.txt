CODE  OF   CRIMINAL   PROCEDURE   ( SPECIAL PROVISIONS)   ACT, No. 2  OF 2013
Order under Section 7 (2)
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 7 (2) of  the Code of Criminal Procedure (Special Provisions) Act, No. 2
of 2013, I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of  Justice and Labour Relations do by this Order extend for a further period not
exceeding two years the operation of  the provisions of  the aforesaid Act, with effect from 2nd February,  2015.
D
R. WIJEY ADASA RAJAPAKSHE, P. C.
Minister of Justice and Labour Relations.
Colombo,
2nd   February, 2015.
