FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
WATTALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to which
the former member, Arachchige Nilantha Leshan Sanjeewa Senasingha Senarathna belonged was required by me under
Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the
ordinance for election as a member of that Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Bope Arachchilage
Don Susil Nishantha being an eligible person under the aforesaid ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Bope Arachchilage Don Susil
Nishantha  elected as a member of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
18th  February, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
WATTALA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of not attending meetings of the
council for a period of three months without permission ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance  to which
the former member,  Pulukkutti Arachchige Don Stanley Norbert belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a
member of that Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Warnakula Suriyage
Sanjeewa Nishantha Fernando  being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Pathiraja Arachchillage Sunil Senarathna,  Returning Officer of  Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Warnakula Suriyage Sanjeewa
Nishantha Fernando elected as a member of the Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
P . A. S. S
ENARATHNA,
Returning Officer,
Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Gampaha,
18th  February, 2015.
