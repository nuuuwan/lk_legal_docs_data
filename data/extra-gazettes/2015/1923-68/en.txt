EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
My Reference No.: KDS/KGK/4/2(1)/2013/11/LA. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends 
to acquire the following Lands which are required for a 
public purpose 
 
Description of the Lands to be acquired: 
 
02. Allotmeats of Land in total extent of 0.0045 
Hectare depicted as Lot No. 01 -02 in preliminary plan 
No. P.P. Maha 6387 prepared by Surveyer General 
situated in the Village of Welata and in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Kandy and Gangawata Korale in 
Kandy District Central Province. 
 
Lot No. 01 —  Name of Land: Assesment No. 753, 
Willium Gopallawa Mawatha Moonamale Estate and 
Mattapela; Owner: Urban Development Authority; 
Extent: 0.0035 Hectare. 
 
Boundary Schedule in 
 
North: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3737 and Lot No. 01 
in P.P. Maha 3738; East: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3738; 
South: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3738 Lot No. 03 in P.P. 
Maha 3737 and Lot No. 02 in This Plan; West: Lot No. 02 
in this Plan and Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3737. 
Lot No. 02 —  Name of Land: Moonamale Estate 
and Mattapela; Owner: Kandy Municipal Council; 
Extent: 0.0010 Hectare.  
Boundary Schedule in 
 
North: Lot No. 01 in P.P. Maha 3737 and Lot No. 01 
in this Plan; East: Lot No. 01 in this Plan; South: Lot No. 
03 in P.P. Maha 3737 and Madabowala Lane; West: 
Madabowala Lane. 
 
Total: Extent: 0.0045 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested  in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Divisional 
Secretariat of Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale 
on the 30th day of July, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 20th day of July, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the Land the particulars of their claims for 
compensation of the acquisition of the said Land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 B. M. P. G. P. V. BANDARA,
