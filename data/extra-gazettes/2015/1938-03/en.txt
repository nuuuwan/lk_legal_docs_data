0.3823 Hectare
North: Road; East: Thennapitiyahena Estate claimed by E. W.
Chandra; South and West: Road.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Part of Sunderland Estate – Extent:
0.0653 Hectare

South: Lot D and Padmadevi Estate claimed by E. U. Senevirathna;
