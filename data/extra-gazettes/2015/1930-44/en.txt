EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (Chapter 460) As 
Amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 
No. 28 of 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
Reference No.: KRP/LAND/ACQVI/01/16. 
  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose 
 
Description of the Land  or Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
The allotments of land shown in the plan of F.V.P. 
Mara 2578 and No. of diagram 91/8(0/20 3B) situated in 
the Village of Dewalamulla, Grama Niladari Division in 
Puhulwella West, Kirinda-Puhulwella Divisional 
Secretary’s Division in Matara District. 
 
1. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Kebellagahawatta; Description: 
Jungle; Extent: 0.0253 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear or by agents duly authorized in writing, before 
me at Divisional Secretariat, Kirinda-Puhulwella of the 
28th  day of October, 2015 at 9.00 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 21st day of October, 2015 the nature of their
