FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER  OF  THE
NUWARA - ELIYA    PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  262)
AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in  Nuwara - Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  being ceased to be
a member of the party  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the recognized political party namely Up - Country Peoples Front, to which the
former member  Mr.  Ambaladevan Nagarajah belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance,
to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member of that
Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mr. Ponniah Ravichanthiran
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to  fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Hettiyakandage Arunapushpa Saumyasiri Fernando, Returning Officer of  Nuwara - Eliya
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by Filling of
Vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Ponniah
Ravichanthiran  elected as a member of the Nuwara - Eliya  Pradeshiya Sabha.
H. A. S. F
ERNANDO,
Returning Officer,
Nuwara - Eliya Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Nuwara - Eliya,
03rd March,  2015.
