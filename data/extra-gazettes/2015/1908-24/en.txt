L.D.B 21/1978(xii).
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order  under  Section 27(1)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Sarath Amunugama,
Minister of Higher Education and Research on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission, do by this Order
amend the Order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1454/37 of July 22, 2006, as amended from time to time, which
specifies the Faculties and Departments of the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, by the addition immediately after item 8 in
Column II which corresponds to the entry “Faculty of Social Sciences” in Column I, in the Schedule to such Order, of the
following new item :-
“9. Department of Sports Science and Physical Education.”
D
R. SARATH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
30th March 2015.
