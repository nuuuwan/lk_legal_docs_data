NOTIFICATION  UNDER  THE  BOTANIC  GARDENS  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  446)
BY virtue of power vested in the Section 2(2) of the Botanic Gardens Ordinance (Chapter 446) No. 31 of 1928 as amended by
the Act, No. 32 of 1973, I declare the Wet Zone Botanic Garden at Sitawaka, Awissawella which is described in the schedule,
as a National Botanic Garden with effect from 25th March, 2015.
N
A VIN DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
Ministry of Tourism and Sports,
No. 09, Philip Gunawardene Mawatha,
Colombo 07,
18th March 2015.
