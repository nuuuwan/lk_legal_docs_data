AS  PER  THE  CLAUSE  No.  42(2) K   OF THE    DIVINEGUMA   ACT,  No.   01   OF    2013
I,  Sajith Premadasa, the Minister of Housing and Samurdhi, as per the powers vested in me by the Clause No. 42(2)K of the
Divineguma Act, No. 01 of 2013, hereby order, to set-off the the total contribution along with the relevant interest on such
contribution to be paid by the officer to the Widow and Orphan Pension (W&OP) with effect from his first date of appointment
of the Authority concerned up to 03.01.2014, against the total contribution, i. e. 20% contribution jointly made to the
Employees' Provident Fund by the officer and the Government from the gross salary of the officers up to the date of
03.01.2014, considering the contribution made by the relevant Authority to the Employees' Provident Fund as per the Clause
No. 44 (e) ii of the Divineguma Act No. 01 of 2013, and pay the balance amount of money to the employee.
S
AJITH PREMADASA,
Minister of Housing and Samurdhi.
