Government  Notifications
MUNICIPAL   COUNCILS   ORDINANCE
Order under Section 10 (2) (b)
BY virtue of  powers vested in me by Paragraph (b) of Sub-section (2) of Section 10 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance
(Chapter 252), I, Faiszer Musthapha, Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government, do by this Order extend the
term of office of the members of Moratuwa Municipal Council up to 31st December, 2015.
F
AISZER M USTHAPHA ,
Minister of Provincial Councils and
Local Government.
Colombo,
16th October, 2015.
