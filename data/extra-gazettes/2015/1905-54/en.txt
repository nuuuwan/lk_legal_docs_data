WESTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointment   made  by  the  Governor  of  Western  Province
IT is hereby notified that in order to implement the powers assigned to the Western Province Provincial Council under the
13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the member of the Sixth Provincial
Council of Western Province, bearer of the name stated in the below schedule under Column I has been appointed as the
minister of the Ministry stated under Column II in the same schedule with effect from 09.02.2015.
