LOCAL  AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS   ORDINANCE
Notice under Section 10A(1)(a) of the Local  Authorities   Elections   Ordinance
UDAPALATHA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
I, Mudiyanselage Kumudu Saman Sri Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of the  Udapalatha  Pradeshiya Sabha, do hereby declare
under Section 10A(1)(a) of the  Local Authorities Elections Ordinance,  that   Mr. Periyasamy Mailvaganam  of the Udapalatha
Pradeshiya Sabha  has vacated his office of the member of that  Pradeshiya Sabha  as he has ceased to be a member of the Up
- Country People’s front.
SAMAN SRI RATHNAYAKE,
Returning Officer,
District Elections Office, U dapalatha Pradeshiya Sabha.
Kandy,
17th March, 2015.
