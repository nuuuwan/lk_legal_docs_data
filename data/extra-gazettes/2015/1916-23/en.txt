 NOTIFICTION   BY  MR.  SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on Wednesday, 03rd June, 2015 at 9.30 a.m.
I, Chamal Rajapaksa, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order No. 14 of the Standing
Orders of Parliament, I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Wednesday the 03rd of June 2015 at 9.30 a.m. having been
requested so to do by the Hon. Prime Minister.
C
HAMAL RAJAPAKSA,
Speaker.
Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte,
28th  May, 2015.
