THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
  
Reference No.: MTR/LND/ACQUI/L2/3921. 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude* which is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
The below mentioned land located in the Kekanadura 
East Grama Niladhari Division in Matara Divisional 
Secretariat Division, Matara District, Southern Province, 
depicted as lot 01 and 02 in t he prelimining plan                
No. 2588, has been acquired for the repairing of the main 
canal of the Kekanadura project where it has been 
damaged due to a landslide. 
 
SCHEDULE 
 
01. Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Kotigalkandawaththa; Particulars of 
Land: Open barrand land; Owner: Wedaththa Lanka Pvt. 
(Ltd.) No. 40, Gallface teres, Colombo 03; Extent: 0.0403 
Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 02 
 
Name of Land: Kotigalkandawaththa; Particulars of 
Land: Open barrand land; Owner: Wedaththa Lanka Pvt. 
(Ltd.) No. 40, Gallface teres, Colombo 03; Extent: 0.3040 
Hectare. 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by Agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Matara Divisional Secretariat on the 
10th of March, 2015 at 10.30 a.m. and are hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 03rd of March, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/
servitude*, the amount of compensation and the details of 
the computation of such amount. 
 
 R
ANJITH YAPA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Matara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Matara, 
18th February, 2015.
