MAKING  REVISIONS  TO  THE  MINUTE  OF  SRI  LANKA  ENGINEERING  SERVICE  -  REVISION  No.  02
THE Minute of Sri Lanka Engineering Service published in the Gazette Extraordinary of Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka No. 1836/6 dated 11.11.2013 is revised including the following section to Section 19 as “19.1.3 (b)”.
(b) Officers who are in Class I of Sri Lanka Engineering Service on supernumerary basis as at the effective date.
On the order of the Public Services Commission,
T. M. L. C. SENARATNE,
Secretary,
Public Services Commission.
