Appointments & C., by the Governors
APPOINTMENT MADE  BY THE GOVERNOR  OF  UV A PROVINCE
BY  virtue of powers vested to me under section 154F. (4) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce that with effect from
the date 14th January, 2015. Hon. Harin Fernando was appointed as the Chief Minister of the Provincial Council of Uva
Province.
C. NANDA MA THEW,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Governor - Uva,
Badulla,
14th January, 2015.
