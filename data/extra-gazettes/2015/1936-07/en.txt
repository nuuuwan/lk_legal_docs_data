THE  WAGES  BOARDS  ORDINANCE
Notification
WAGES  BOARD  FOR  THE  TEA  GROWING   AND  MANUFACTURING   TRADE
IT is hereby notified under  regulation  30  of the Wages Boards Regulations, that   Mr. U. C. Perera  and Mr.  R. G . Walpola   have
been appointed as Workers'  Representative  and  Employer's  Representative, respectively of  the Wages  Board for the Tea
Growing and Manufacturing  Trade by the Hon. Minister of Labour under Section 9 of  the Wages Boards Ordinance
(Chapter 136). This appointment is valid only up to 01.01.2018.
S. M. G
OTABHAYA J AYARATNE,
Secretary,
Ministry of Labour and Trade Union Relations.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
30th September   2015.
