MEDIATION   (SPECIAL    CATEGORIES   OF   DISPUTES)    ACT,   No.   21   OF   2003
Order under Section 2
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act, No. 21 of 2003,
I, Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice and Labour Relations, do by this Order determine that the category of disputes
of which the provisions of aforesaid Act shall apply in regard to the settlement through mediation, shall be disputes relating
to the possession or ownership of land.
D
R. WIJEYADASA RAJAPAKSHE, P. C.,
Minister of Justice and Labour Relations.
Colombo 01,
9th  February, 2015.
