THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
My Ref. No.: 3/Kata/I.Ath/89.
Land Ministry Ref. No.: 4-3/7/2014/HW/36.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 01 - 05 (Lots 5) allotments in total extent of about 0.2806
Hectare of land in Preliminary Plan No. Gam: 3938 and dated
29.12.2014 prepared by the Surveyor-Superintendent on behalf of
Surveyor- Puttalam, General situated in the Village of Manaweriya
in the Gramaniladhari Division 67 - A Manaweriya in the Minor
Division of Dunugaha paththuwa of Divisional Secretary’s Division
of Katana in Gampaha District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of land: Bridgeland watta; Description: Garden, Contains a
temporary building and Part of the temporary building; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: D. R. Erauwala, No. 65/1, Chilaw Road,
Manaweriya, Kochchikade;  Extent: 0.0348 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of land: Bridgeland watta; Description: Garden, Contains
No. of 3 part of the permanent building, Masonry well with and No.
of 8 Coconut trees about 30 years and No. of 4 Jack trees about 30
years; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: D. P . A. P. Jayawardhana,
No. 153, Chilaw Road, Manaweriya, Kochchikade;  Extent: 0.1045
Hectare.
03. Lot No. 3
Name of land: Bridgeland watta; Description: Garden, Contains A
Permanent building, A Part of the permanent building, A Part of the
temporary building with and 3 Coconut trees about 30 years and
One Jack tree about 30 years; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
K. V . Jayawardhana, Chilaw Road, Manaweriya, Kochchikade;
Extent: 0.0833 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 4
Name of land: Kosgaha watta; Description: Garden, Contains A
Permanent building with One Jack tree about 30 years, One Teak
tree about 30 years and One Coconut trees about 30 year; Claimant:
State; Original Claimant: G . Jude Fernando, No. 154, Chilaw Road,
Manaweriya, Kochchikade;  Extent: 0.0366 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 5
Name of land: Kosgaha watta; Description: Estate, Contains A
One Jack tree about 30 years; Claimant: State; Original Claimant:
Dushanthi Samarasingha, No. 105, Main Street, Kochchikade;  Extent:
0.0214 Hectare.
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat office of Katana on 04th day of March,
2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 25 th day of February, 2015,
the nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the amount of
compensation and the details of the computation of such amount.
                                                                  K. G. H. H. R. K
IRIELLA,
Divisional Secretary,
Divisional Secretariat,
Katana.
Divisional Secretary’s Office,
Katana.
02nd February, 2015.
