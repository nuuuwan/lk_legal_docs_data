NOTIFICATION  BY  MR.  SPEAKER
Convening of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
on Monday, 02nd February, 2015 at 2.00 p.m.
I, Chamal Rajapaksa, Speaker of Parliament do hereby notify that in pursuance of Standing Order No. 14 of the Parliament,
I have summoned the Parliament to meet on Monday the 02nd day of February 2015 at 2.00 p.m. having been requested so to
do by the Prime Minister.
CHAMAL  RAJAPAKSA,
Speaker.
Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte,
30th January, 2015.
