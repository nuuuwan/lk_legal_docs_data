FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13  (b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE
DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary General of Parliament has informed me, under Section 64 (1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Seventh Parliament
by reason of resignation of Mr. Pallewaththe Gamaralalage Maithripala Sirisena, who was declared elected as a Member of
Parliament for the Electoral District No. 18 - Polonnaruwa under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act.
 And whereas the Returning Officer, for the Electoral District which returned the said member was directed by me
unde Section 64 (1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under Paragraph 13(b) of Article 99 of the
Constitution ;
And whereas the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act has made a return in Form
"M" set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act to the effect that Mr. Jayasinghe Bandara was declared elected as a
Member of Parliament for the said Electoral District ;
Now therefore, I, Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested in me by
Section 64 (2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. Jayasinghe Bandara has been declared elected
as a Member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District No. 18 - Polonnaruwa to fill the vacancy caused by reason of
resignation of Mr. Pallewaththe Gamaralalage Maithripala Sirisena.
M
AHINDA DESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya,
16th January 2015.
