THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  in total  extent of 0.2023 Hectare, depicted
in the Advance Tracing No. Aa/RMB/2014/1025 dated 28.11.2014
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the  Village  of
Pihimbiyagollewa in the  Divisional Secretary's  Division of Rambewa
in Anuradhapura District and bounded  as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Kolongahakele – Extent: 0.2023 Hectare
North: State forest and land claimed by Meththananda; East: State
forest and Pihimbiyagollewa cemetery; South: Medawachchiya -
Horowpothana road and Pihimbiyagollewa cemetery; West:
Medawachchiya - Horowpothana road and land claimed by
Meththananda.
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
