PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
Filling  of  a  vacancy  in  the
Central   Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Central Province Provincial
Council by reason of being elected as a Member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance, the recongized political party,  to
which the   member belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person
to fill such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Kamal Ajith Kumara
Beligolla to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Kamal Ajith Kumara Beligolla, elected as a
member of the above Provincial Council  from the Administrative District of Kandy in respect of  which such vacancy
occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
16th  September, 2015.
