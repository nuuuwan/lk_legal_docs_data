EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF MAYOR  OF  THE
MATARA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of  Mayor  in  Matara Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party   to which
Hadunge Sosindra Nandana the former Mayor   belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  decide upon the member of that Matara Municipal Council who belongs to such party who shall be declared
elected as Mayor within a  period of   thirty  (30) days;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to
nominate  Mr. Deegoda Gamage Yasarathna to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Wewelwala Hewage Ravindra Wijaya Kumara, Returning Officer of  Matara  Municipal
Council, acting in  terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local  authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Deegoda Gamage
Yasarathna  elected as a Mayor of the Matara Municipal Council.
W. H. R. W
IJAY A KUMARA,
Returning Officer,
Matara Municipal Council.
District  Elections  Office,
Matara,
11th  November,  2015.
