UV A    PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Appointment made by the  Governor of Uva  Province
BY  virtue of powers vested to me under Section 154F(1)  and (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor of the Uva Province do hereby announce that I have made the
following appointment of the Provincial Council of Uva Province with effect from 13th February 2015.
01. Hon. Harin Fernando - Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Peace, Education, Local
Chief Minister of the Uva Provincial Government, Social Welfare, Rural Infrastructure Development and
Council Construction of the Uva Provincial Council.
M. P . J
AYASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
Badulla,
13th February 2015.
