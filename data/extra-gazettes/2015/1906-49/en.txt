THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Servitude of the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
This Acquisition is made for the purpose of providing access roads
which were lost under Matara - Kataragama Railway line Project.
Boundaries for the allotments of land containing in  extent  of 0.0088
Hectare depicted as Lot "A" in the Advance Tracing No. MR/DVN/
2013/788 dated 19.03.2014 made by Survey Superintendent of Matara
and situated in the village of Naotunna in Minor Division of Wellabada
Pattu West, Divisional Secretary's Division of Devinuwara, Matara
District, Southern Province.
01. Lot ‘A’ – Maddumaliyanagewatta – Extent: 0.0088 Hectare
North: Maddumaliyanagewatta acquired for the proposed Matara
- Kataragama railway line and Road; East: Road and
Maddumaliyanagewatta claimed by Kaliduwa Vidana Gamage
Piyadasa; South: Maddumaliyanagewatta claimed by Kaliduwa
Vidana Gamaage Piyadasa; West: Mahagedarawatta claimed by
Somasiri Madawala and Maddumaliyanagewatta acquired for the
proposed Matara - Kataragama railway line.
Janaka Bandara Thennakoon,
Minister of Lands and Land Development.
