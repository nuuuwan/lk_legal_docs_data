Order No. 32
CONSUMER  AFFAIRS  AUTHORITY  ACT,  No. 09  OF  2003
Order under Section 20(5)
ACTING under the powers vested in it by Section 20(5) of the Consumer Affairs Authority Act, No. 09 of 2003, the Consumer
Affairs Authority orders that no manufacturer, importer, packer, distributor or trader shall sell, expose or offer for sale, display
for sale the items listed below, above the maximum retail prices given hereunder.
 Product Maximum Retail Price
(Rs.)
White Sugar (unpacketted) 87/- per kg
Full Cream Milk Powder 325/- per 400g pack
810/- per 1kg  pack
Sustagen 1,500/- per 400g pack
Wheat Flour (unpacketted) 87/- per kg
Green Gram  (unpacketted) 265/- per kg
Dried Sprats (imported) (unpacketted) 385/- per kg
Canned Fish (imported) Rs. 140/- per Net weight of 425g,
Drained weight of 280g
Rs. 70/- per Net weight of 155g,
Drained weight of 105g.
Seeds of Coriander - Neither crushed nor ground (unpacketted) 350/- per kg
Black Gram (Ulundu) (unpacketted) 300/- per kg
Maldive Fish (unpacketted) 1,740/- per kg
Turmeric - Crushed or ground (unpacketted) 800/- per kg
Chillies - Crushed or ground (unpacketted) 400/- per kg
This Order shall come into effect from 19th March 2015.
The Consumer Affairs Authority do by this Order rescind Order No. 31 published in Gazette Extraordinary
No. 1902/55 dated 20th February 2015.
                                                                                            By order of the Consumer Affairs Authority
R
UMY MARZOOK,
Chairman.
