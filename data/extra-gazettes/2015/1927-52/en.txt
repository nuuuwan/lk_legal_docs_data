No. 1927/52 – THURSDAY, AUGUST 13, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Pansalatenna Estate in extent of
6.3062 Hectares, depicted as Lot ‘A ’ in the Advance Tracing No.
MA/UKW/2012/299 and dated 17.10.2012, prepared by the
Superintendent of Surveys, Matale District, situated in the Village of
Balakaduwa in the Minor Division of Kohonsiya Pattuwa in the
Divisional Secretary’s Division of Ukuwela in Matale District, Central
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Pansalatenna Estate – Extent: 6.3062 Hectares
North: Estate Road and Pansalatenna Estate  claimed by State
Plantation Corporation; East: Pansalatenna Estate claimed by State
Plantation Corporation and Pradeshiya Sabha Road; South:
Pradeshiya Sabha Road and Thittawelgolla Ela; West: Pansalatenna
Estate claimed by State Plantation Corporation and Estate Road.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
My No.: 3/3/6/2/22.
Ministry of Lands,
‘Mihikatha Medura’,
Rajamalwatta Road,
Battaramulla.
13th May, 2015.
