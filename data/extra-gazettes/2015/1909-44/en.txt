SPORTS  ACT,  No.  25  OF  1973
Direction under Section 54(3) of National Sports Association Regulations
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under  Section 54(3), Regulations of  National Sports Associations, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1825/38 of 30th August 2013, made under Section 41 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973, I, Navin Dissanayake,
Minister of Tourism and Sports do by this order direct that the Director General of the Department of Sports Development,
Ministry of Tourism and Sports to convene and conduct a meeting of the Sri Lanka Karate Do Federation to elect the new
office bearers.
N
AVIN DISSANAYAKE,
Minister of Tourism and Sports.
Colombo,
17th  March, 2015.
