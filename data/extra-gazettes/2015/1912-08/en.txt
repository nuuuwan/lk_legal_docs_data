FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF    CHAIRMAN OF   THE
MATHUGAMA    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman  in   Mathugama   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  Resignation  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,   to which
Liyana Arachchige Gunasena Liyanaarachchi    the  Former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of
the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Mathugama  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who
shall be declared elected as Chairman within a period of   thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his nominated
Mr. Don Roshan Manoj Priyantha Jayasingha  to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Hannedige Rasika Priyanga Peiris,  Returning Officer of    Mathugama Pradeshiya Sabha,
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities (Special Provisions) Act,  No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.Don Roshan Manoj Priyantha
Jayasingha  elected as  Chairman of the  Mathugama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
R
ASIKA PEIRIS,
Returning Officer,
Mathugama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Kalutara,
27th  April, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE   OF    CHAIRMAN   OF   THE
RAJGAMA     PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A (1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
  LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of   Chairman  in   Rajgama   Pradeshiya Sabha  has fallen vacant by reason of  death  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,   to which
Agampodi Manoj Pushpakumara Mendis    the  Former Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the
aforesaid Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be
declared elected as Chairman within a period of   thirty (30) days ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated  his decision  to
nominate  Mr. Nimal  Thommadura  to fill  such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I,  Anthony Bimal Indrajith De Silva,  Returning Officer of    Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha, acting
in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act,   No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr.Nimal  Thommadura  elected as  Chairman of the
Rajgama   Pradeshiya Sabha.
A. B. I. D
E SIL V A,
Returning Officer,
Rajgama  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Galle,
27th  April, 2015.
