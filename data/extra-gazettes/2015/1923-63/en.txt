EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land  or to be acquired: 
 
An allotment of land Situated in Kandebadda village 
(F.V.P. 171, H/OK/2012/756) in Kandebadda Grama 
Niladari division in Okewela divisional Secretary’s 
division in Hambantota District. 
 
Land No. 1675    
 
Name of Land: Bibulahena; Claimant: Gamage 
Pemawathi; Extent: 0.0535 Hectare. 
 
Land No. 1676     
Name of Land: Bibulahena; Claimant: Gamage 
Pemawathi; Extent: 0.0451 Hectare. 
Land No. 1677     
Name of Land: Bibulahena; Claimant: Gamage 
Pemawathi; Extent: 0.0617 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be acquired* are hereby required 
to appear personally, or by agents duly  authorized in 
writing, before me at Okewela Divisional Secretary’s  
Office on the 02nd September, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. and are 
hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) 
on or before the 26th August, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 K ANCHANA K. THALPAWILA, 
 Divisional Secretary - Okewela, 
 Hambantota District. 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office,  
Okewela, 
13th July, 2015.
