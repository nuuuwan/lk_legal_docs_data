PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
Filling  of  a  vacancy  in  the
Sabaragamuwa  Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Sabaragamuwa Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Sabaragamuwa
Province Provincial Council by reason of Nullification of the office of a member of the said Provincial Council ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recognized political party,  to which the   member
belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secreta ry of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Basuriyage
Ariyawansha to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Basuriyage Ariyawansha, elected as a member of
the above Provincial Council  from the Administrative District of Ratnapura in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
01st October, 2015.

PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
Filling  of  a  vacancy  in  the
North Central Province  Provincial  Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Central Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Central
Province Provincial Council by reason of being elected as Member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party, the recongized political party,  to which the   member
belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Anuradhanayaka K.
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Anuradhanayaka K., elected as a member of the
above Provincial Council  from the Administrative District of Anuradhapura in respect of  which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
01st October, 2015.
