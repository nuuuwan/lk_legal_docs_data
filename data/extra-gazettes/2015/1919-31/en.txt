   CENTRAL  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Appointments granted under the statute of  Co-operative Employee  No. 14 of  1990  the  Central Province
BY virtue of powers vested on me under the Section of 07 (1) and 04 (2)  of  the statute of Co- operative Employees
Commission No. 14 of 1990 in the Central Province, Ministry of  Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Local Government and
Provincial Administration, Human  Resource, Education and Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Land, Trade and Commerce, Food
supplies and Distribution Affairs and Investment Co-ordination, and Subjects which are  not taken under any others ministries
I Sarath Ekanayaka,
Central Province, hereby notified that Mr. Mahendra Karaputugala will be  appointed as a Member  and the
Chairman of  the  Central Provincial Co-operative Employees Commission with the effective date of 01.06.2015 and under the
Section 04 (2) of such statute,  following members will be appointed as other members of the aforesaid commission with the
effective date of  01.06.2015.
1. Lawyer Mr. Edirisinghe Gamini Samaratunga,
2. Mr. Wathuwala Gedara Chaminda Thushara Kamalarathne.
S
ARATH EKANAYAKA,
Chief Minister,
 Central Province Provincial Council.
