NOTIFICATION
IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and
Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces has been graciously pleased to confer the Vishista Sewa Vibhushanaya at an
Investiture to be held at the Presidential Secretariat on the eight day of October in the year Two Thousand and Fifteen on
the Officers named in the Schedule hereto in terms of Clause Four of the Presidential Warrant instituting the Vishista
Sewa Vibhushanaya published in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 156/5 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
issued on the first day of September in the year One Thousand Nine Hundred and Eighty one.
                                                                                                By His Excellency’s Command.
P. B. A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo,
08th October 2015.
