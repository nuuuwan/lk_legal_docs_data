NOW THEREFORE, I, Maithripala Sirisena,  President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka reposing
great trust and confidence in his prudence, ability and fidelity, do, in pursuance of the provisions of Section 3 of the Commissions
of Inquiry Act, by these Presents, appoint Pasdunkorale Arachchige Pematilaka Esquire, retired Auditor General  to be a

Thousand and Fifteen.
