  PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS   ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2  OF  1988
 Filling of a Vacancy in the Western Province Provincial Council
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of  being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the Democratic People’s Front,  the recognised political party to which the member
belonged was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such vacancy ;
And  whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.  Kurusami Kalankadi
Thevar  to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr.  Kurusami Kalankadi Thevar, elected as a member of the
above Provincial  Council from the Administrative District of  Colombo  in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIY A,
Commissioner of Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
10th September, 2015.
