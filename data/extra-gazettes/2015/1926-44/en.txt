THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Notice under Section 7
                                                             My No. : KEDS/ATH3/1/66.
I hereby give notice under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Land
Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to acquire the following
lands which are required for a public purpose.
Description of the land to be acquired:
Lot No. 1 - 5 (Lots 5) allotments in total  extent of  about 0.6015
Hectare, of land in Preliminary Plan No. Ko. 9490 and dated
10.06.2015 prepared by the Sinior - Superintendent of  Survey of
Colombo District on behalf  of  Survey General, situated in  the
Grama Niladhari Division of  597 - Jamburaliya in the village of
Jamburaliya in Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kesbewa in
Colombo District in the Western Province.
01. Lot No. 1
Name of  land:  Athurugahadeniyakumbura;  Description:  Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Karolis Perera, Madapatha,
Jamburaliya; Extent:  0.0105 Hectare.
02. Lot No. 2
Name of  land:  Athurugahadeniyakumbura;  Description:  Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: P . R. Dayawathi, Madapatha,
Jamburaliya; Extent: 0.0221 Hectare.
03. Lot No. 3
Name of  land:  Athurugahadeniyakumbura;  Description:  Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: P . R. Gunarathna and
P. R. Rathnawali,  Madapatha,  Jamburaliya; Extent: 0.0320 Hectare.
04. Lot No. 4
Name of  land:  Athurugahadeniyakumbura;  Description:  Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant: Mendis, Madapatha,
Jamburaliya; Extent: 0.0382 Hectare.
05. Lot No. 5
Name of  land:  Athurugahadeniyakumbura;  Description:  Garden;
Claimant: State; Original Claimant:  Diksan  Dalabandara  and  Gamini
Lokuge, Madapatha, Jamburaliya; Extent: 0.4987 Hectare.
 All  persons interested in the aforesaid lands are hereby required
to appear  personally or by agent  duly authorized in writing, before
me at Divisional Secretariat office of  Kesbewa on the  from 02.09.2015
at 10.00 a.m and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing
(in duplicate) on or before the 25.08.2015  the nature of  their  interests
in the land, the particulars of their claims for compensation for the
acquisition of the said lands, the amount of compensation and the
details of the computation of such amount.
Divisional Secretary,
Kesbewa.
Divisional  Secretary’s Office,
Kesbewa.
