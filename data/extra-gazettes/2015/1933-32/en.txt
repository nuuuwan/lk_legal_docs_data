No. 1933/32 –  WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER  23, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)  
 
As Amended by the Land  Acquisition (Amended) Act 
No. 28 of 1964  
(Notice Under Section)  
 
 
My Reference  No.: PUT/LND/01/01/06  
  
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land for the 
construction of pump House operator quarters of Puttalam 
Water Supply Scheme. 
 
Description of the land or servitude to be acquired: 
 
An allotments of land described below bearing Lot 
No. 2 depicted preliminary plan PU 5040 prepared on 
01.11.2012 by the Puttalam District Superintendent of 
surveys in total extent of 0.00 74 Hectare situated in 617B 
Puttalama east Grama Niladhari Division in the village of 
Manalgunduwa in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Puttalama in Puttalam District, bounded as follows.     
North: Lot No. 1 Preliminary Plan No. 5040 and 
Manalgunduwa Thottam land; East: Nedumkulam 
Thottam land claimed by jumma mosque;  South: 
Nedumkulam Thottam land claimed by jumma mosque 
and Urban council road; West: Urban council road 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Puttalam on the 11 th day of November 2015, 
at 10.00 a. m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 04th day of 
November, 2015 the nature of their interests in the land, 
the particulars or their clai ms for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 M. R. M. M ALIK, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Puttalam. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Puttalam. 
17th September, 2015.
