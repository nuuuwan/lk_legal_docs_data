CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
I, Sarath Ekenayeka, Minister of Chief and Local Government of Central Province, here with enact the rules concerning
members of Pradeshiya Sabha on the following allowances as per the powers bestowed to me by the 184th clause of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 which is to be read along with the 2nd clause of the Provincial Council Act, No. 12 of
1989. These rules are to be implemented from 16.01.2013 (for fuel allowance) and from 25.01.2012 (for telephone allowance).
Rules
01. These rules are known as rules for paying allowances for members of Pradeshiya Sabha.
02. This is in addition to the allowances declared by the Extraordinary Gazette No.  1391/25 of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated 06.05.2005.
Fuel Allowance
Payment of a monthly fuel allowance of Rs. 2,500/- for the members of Pradeshiya Sabha under Central Provincial
Council.
* This allowance is valid only for the members of Pradeshiya Sabha.
* If the Chairman/Deputy Chairman of Pradeshiya Sabha is not entitled to a fuel allowance so far, they can claim
this allowance. One person cannot claim two allowances concerning one matter.
* This is not relevant for the Urban Councils/Municipal Councils.
* Maximum amount paid for a member is Rs. 2,500/-. If one doesn’t own a bike/three wheeler or motor vehicle may
use any other vehicle and the relevant bills and documents should be produced ; running charts should be
acquired.
Details regarding running for monitoring of development work of Pradeshiya Sabha should be produced.

Payment of an allowance of Rs. 1,000/- for the members of Pradeshiya Sabha.
* Payment of an allowance of Rs. 1,000/- as telephone allowance for the members of Pradeshiya Sabha appointed
to the local government institutions under Central Provincial Council, on the basis that provisions are allocated

CENTRAL  PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
I, Sarath Ekenayeka, Chief and Local Government Minister of Central Province, here with enact the following regulations as
per the powers bestowed on me by the 188 (i) k.k. and the 289th clause of the Urban Council ordinance which is the 252nd
authority which is to be read along with the 2nd clause of the Provincial Council Act, No. 12 of 1989. This regulation is to be
implemented from the 17.02.2013.
Regulations
Paying of a special allowance for the Mayor/Deputy Mayor and members of Kandy Municipal Council, from the
Urban Council fund for participating at fixed committee meetings, co-committee meetings, advisory boards and special and
annual general meetings. This allowance is in addition to the regulations declared by the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1391/25
of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka dated on 06.05.2005.
For Annual General Meetings - Rs. 2,000.00
For a fixed meeting - Rs. 750.00
For sub Committees or advisory board - Rs. 500.00
S
ARA TH  EKENAYEKA,
Chief and Local Government Minister,
Central Province.
16th January, 2015,
at the Chief Minister’s Office.
