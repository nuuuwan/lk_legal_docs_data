FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER   OF   THE
SRI  JAYAWARDHANAPURA   KOTTE   MUNICIPAL   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a  member in Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of
resignation;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely United People's Freedom Alliance to
which the former member, Don Ranjan Ponnamperuma belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days, a person eligible under the ordinance for election as a member
of that Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council who shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Arachchige Rajika
Lalitha Kodithuwakku being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan,  Returning Officer of  Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies
in Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Arachchige Rajika
Lalitha Kodithuwakku  elected as a member of the Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Sri Jayawardhanapura Kotte Municipal Council.
City Elections Office,
Colombo,
13th  August, 2015.
