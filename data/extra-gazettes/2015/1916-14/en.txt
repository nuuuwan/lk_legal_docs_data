THE    EMPLOYEES'   PROVIDENT   FUND   ACT,   No.   15   OF   1958
Order under Section 23 A (6)
BY virtue of  the powers vested in me by Sub- Section (6) of  the Section 23A of the Employees' Provident Fund  Act, No. 15
of  1958, I,  Rajapakse  Mudiyanselage Seneviratne Banda Navinne,   Minister of  Labour do by this order appoint the 27th day
of  May 2015 as on the date on which the  provisions of  subparagraph (i) of  sub-section (1) of Section 23A of  the aforesaid
Act, shall come into operation.
S. B. N
A VINNE
Ministry of Labour, Minister of Labour.
Labour Secretariat,
Colombo 05,
26th  May 2015.
