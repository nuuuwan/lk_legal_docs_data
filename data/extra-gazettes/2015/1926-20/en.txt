URBAN   DEVELOPMENT   AUTHORITY   LAW   No. 41   OF  1978
Order under Section 3
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 3 of the Urban Development Authority Law No. 41 of 1978, I, Rauff
Hakeem, Minister of Urban Development, Water Supply and Drainage being of opinion that the area specified in Column
I of Schedule II hereto and having the metes and bounds specified in corresponding entity in Column II of Schedule II
hereto are suitable for  development and do hereby this order declare such areas for the purpose of the said section.
I do hereby declare that, the already declared areas set out in Schedule I together with the other areas declared as
aforesaid, shall be included within the limits of the expanded Kurunegala Urban Development Area set out in under
Schedule II hereto.
R
AUFF  H AKEEM,
Minister of Urban Development,
Water Supply and Drainage,
