THE  COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
IT is hereby notified that the road described in the Schedule  hereunder has been  named as “ Dr. Milina Sumathipala Mawatha”
by the Honourable Chief Minister of Western Province Provincial Council and Honourable Minister in charge of the Subject
of Local Government under Section 71 (1) of the Municipal Council Ordinance No. 29 of 1947 (Chapter 252) which shall read
with the Section 02 of the Provincial Council’s Act (Consequential Provisions) bearing No. 12 of 1989.
