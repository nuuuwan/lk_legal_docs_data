THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT)) ACT, No. 28 OF 1964 NOTICE 
UNDER SECTION 7 
 
My Ref. : RBK/LAN/AQURE/A3/27. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section 1 of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required  for a public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
The allotments of land in extent of 1.5466 Hectares 
depicted as Lot No. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 in the preliminary 
plan No. K/3491 prepared by the Surveyor General, 
within the boundaries shown below situated in the village 
of Kaudaulla in the minor division of Kinigoda, Korale in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Rambukkana in the 
District of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa Province.

All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents, duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Rambukkana 
Divisional Secretariat on the 30th July 2015 at 10.00  
a.m.  
 
Further, you are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before 29th July 2015, the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and details of
