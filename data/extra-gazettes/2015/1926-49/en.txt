THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of  land  in total extent of about 0.8094 Hectare,
depicted in the Advance Tracing No. B/HLE/2013/538 dated
14.10.2013 prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the  Village
of Wattepanguwa in Hapuwalakumbura Grama Niladari Division of
Bogoda Korale in Haliela Divisional Secretary’s  Division in Badulla
District and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Udoowara Watte – Extent: About  0.8094 Hectare
North: Valley, Road(R.D.A) and Estate Road; East: Road(R.D.A),
Estate Road, and Udoowara Estate claimed by Hapugastenne
Plantation; South: Udoowara Estate claimed by Hapugastenne
Plantation; West: Udoowara Estate claimed by Hapugastenne
Plantation and V alley.
M. K. A.D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
Reference No.: 4-3/9/2013/WS/110.
