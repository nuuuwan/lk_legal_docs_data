Direction  under  Section  54(3)  of  National  Sports  Association  Regulations
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 54(3), Regulations of National Sports Associations, published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1825/38 of 30th August 2013, made under Section 41 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973,
I, Dayasiri Jayasekara, Minister of Sports do by this order inform the Sri Lanka Judo Association that Mr. Kaludura De Silva
Shantha Ruwanchandra, Director General of the Department of Sports Development has been appointed as the Competent
Authority, in terms of the Section 39(1) of the Sports Act, No. 25 of 1973 for the smooth functioning of that Association and
direct that Mr. Kaludura De Silva Shantha Ruwanchandra, Director General of the Department of Sports Development, to
convene the election of the Sri Lanka Judo Association to elect new office bearers until an election is held lawfully, since the
term of the interim Committee of Sri Lanka Judo Association has expired on 15.07.2015.
D
AY ASIRI JAYASEKARA (ATTORNEY AT LAW),
Minister of Sports.
Colombo,
15th September 2015.
