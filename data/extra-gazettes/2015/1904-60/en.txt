FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  MAYOR   OF   THE
RATNAPURA   MUNICIPAL    COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of  Mayor in Ratnapura Municipal Council has fallen vacant by reason of death ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the   United People's Freedom Alliance  the recognized political party,  to which
Abeyratna Victor Kapil Wickramanath the former Mayor belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Ratnapura Municipal Council who belongs to such party, who shall be
declared elected as a Mayor within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has  communicated his decision to
nominate Mr. Godahena Kattadige Nilantha Roshan to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Matara Achchige Padmasiri Chandrawansha Perera,  Returning Officer of  Ratnapura Municipal
Council acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in
Local Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of  2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Godahena Kattadige Nilantha
Roshan elected as Mayor of the Ratnapura Municipal Council.
M. A. P. C. P
ERERA,
Returning Officer,
Ratnapura Municipal Council.
District Elections Office,
Ratnapura,
03rd  of March, 2015.
