REGISTRATION  OF  PERSONS  ACT,  No.  32  OF  1968
Notice
THIS is hereby notified that implementing and effective of the amendment of charges for following functions that are
carried out by the Department for Registration of Persons, had been published in Extraordinary Gazette Number 1922/25
dated 08.07.2015 are suspended until further notice.
(i) Registration of Persons and Issuing Identity Cards under Section 9,
(ii) Issuing Duplicate Identity Cards under Section 16,
(iii) Issuing Amended Duplicate of an Identity Card under Section 17.
