EXTRAORDINARY
1A
I, Ponnuthurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of the Poonakary  Pradeshiya Sabha, under paragraph (b) of  Sub section (1) of
Section 10A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), do hereby declare that Mr. Perampalam V eluppillai,
who contested at the Election for Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha, held on 23rd July 2011 as a candidate on the Nomination Paper
of the Tamil United Liberation Front, has forfeited his rights to have his name retained in the nomination paper of that
recognized political party, for filling any casual vacancy, by reason of him ceasing to be member of that recognized political
party.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
Returning Officer,
Poonakary Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Jaffna,
27th of January,  2015.

NOTICE UNDER SECTION 10A(1)(B)
I, Ponnuthurai Kuganathan, Returning Officer of the Poonakary  Pradeshiya Sabha, under paragraph (b) of  Sub section (1) of
Section 10A of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), do hereby declare that Mr. Kandiah Mahenthirarasa

recognized political party, for filling any casual vacancy, by reason of him ceasing to be member of that recognized political
party.
P. K
UGANATHAN,
