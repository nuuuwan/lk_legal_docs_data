FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  UNDER  PARAGRAPH  13  (b)  OF  ARTICLE  99  OF  THE  CONSTITUTION
 OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST   REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA
WHEREAS the Secretary - General of   Parliament has informed me, under Section 64(1) of the Parliamentary Elections Act,
No. 1 of 1981, as amended by Act, No. 15 of 1988, that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Seventh Parliament
by reason of death of Mr. Neranjan Wickramasinghe, who was declared elected as a Member of Parliament for the Electoral
District No. 15 - Kurunegala under Section 62 of the aforesaid Act.
And whereas  the Returning Officer, for the electoral district which returned the said member was directed by me
under Section 64(1) of the aforesaid Act to fill this vacancy as provided for under paragraph 13(b) of  Article 99 of the
Constitution ;
And whereas  the said Returning Officer acting under Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act has made  a return in Form
“M” set out in the First Schedule to the aforesaid Act, to the effect that Mr. R. D. Wimaladasa was declared elected as a
Member of Parliament for the said electoral district ;
Now therefore  I,  Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, in the exercise of the powers vested in me by
Section 64(2) of the aforesaid Act, do hereby notify the general public that Mr. R. D. Wimaladasa has been declared elected
as a member of the Seventh Parliament for the Electoral District No. 15 - Kurunegala to fill the vacancy caused by reason of
death of Mr. Neranjan Wickramasinghe.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of  Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Sarana Mawatha,
Rajagiriya.
