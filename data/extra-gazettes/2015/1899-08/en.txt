FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER
OF  THE  KATTANKUDY   URBAN   COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of  a member in Kattankudy Urban Council has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Group Leader of the Independent Group No. 1 to which the former Member Abdul Leththeef
Muhammathu Sabeel, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to nominate within a
period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that Kattankudy Urban Council who
shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Leader of the aforesaid group has nominated Mr. Muhammathu Abulhasan Muhammathu
Mihlar being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Abdul Majeedu Mahammadu Kabeer, Returning Officer of Kattankudy Urban Council, acting
in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local Authorities
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Muhammathu Abulhasan Muhammathu Mihlar
elected as a member of the Kattanduky Urban Council.
A. M. M. K
ABEER,
Returning Officer,
Kattankudy Urban Council.
District Elections Office,
Batticaloa,
23rd January,  2015.
