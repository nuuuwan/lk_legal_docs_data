PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS  NOTIFICATIONS
NORTH   CENTRAL   PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
Notification  under  Section  185 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 that should be read with Section 2 of
Provincial Council Act (Incidental Provision) of Number 12 of 1989 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
I have received the report prepared by retired judicial officer, K. Sunil Perera Appointed, in terms of the Section 185 (2) of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to satisfy that Athmadale Aluthwatthe Gedara Manju Sri Aluthwattha Chairman of
Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha has committed one, more or all the offences as per the Section 185(1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act
No. 15 of 1987 by Samarakoon Mudiyanselage Ranjith Samarakoon Former Chief Minister, and Minister of Provincial Law and
Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Provincial Irrigation, Rural Infrastructure Facilities, Sport and Youth Affairs.
In terms of the said report it has been authenticated that Athmadale Aluthwatthe Gedara Manju Sri Aluthwattha  Chairman of
Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha has committed the offences as per the Section 185 (1) i and after the confirmation of such
offences, I do here by notify as per the powers vested to me as The Minister of Portfolio of Local Government, Athmadale
Aluthwatthe Gedara Manju Sri Aluthwattha has removed from the post of Chairman of Lankapura Pradeshiya Sabha with
immediate effect.
S
UDASINGHE MUDIY ANSELAGE PESHALA JAYARATHNA BANDARA,
Chief Minister and Minister of Provincial Law and Order,
Finance and Planning, Local Government, Provincial
Road Development, Rural Infrastructure Facilities and Special
Project, Tourism, Education, Information Technology and Cultural Affairs.
Chief North Central Province,
Office of the Chief Ministry,
18th February 2015.
