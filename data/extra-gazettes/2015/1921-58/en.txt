No. 1921/58 – THURSDAY, JULY 02, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha  Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
Three (03) allotments of  land in total extent of about
0.0309 Hectare depicted in the Advance Tracing  No. Maha/PTD/
2014/188 dated 09th May, 2014, prepared by the Surveyor-General
situated in the Village of Girakaduwa in Pallegampaha Korale
West Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s Division of
Pathadumbara in Kandy District, Central Province and bounded as
follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Notharis Watta – Extent: About 0.0152 Hectare
North: Road (Pradesiya Sabha) and Notharis Watta claimed by
D. M. Balakumari Dasanayaka; East: Notharis Watta claimed by D.
M. Balakumari Dasanayaka; South: Notharis Watta claimed by D.
M. Balakumari Dasanayaka and Lot  B; West: Lot B, Werellahinna
claimed by A. M. Ranbanda and Notharis Watta claimed by D. M.
Balakumari Dasanayaka.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Werellahinna – Extent: About 0.0148 Hectare
North: Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Ranbanda, Notharis Watta
claimed by D. M. Balakumari Dasanayaka and Lot A; East: Lot A,
Nothariswatta claimed by D. M. Balakumari Dasanayaka, Lot C and
Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Senarath Bandara; South: Lot C,
Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Senarath Bandara and Werellahinna
claimed by A. M. Ranbanda; West: Werellahinna claimed by A. M.
Ranbanda.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Werellahinna – Extent: About 0.0009 Hectare
North: Lot B and Notharis Watta claimed by D. M. Balakumari
Dasanayaka; East: Notharis Watta claimed by D. M. Balakumari
Dasanayaka and Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Senarath Bandara;
South: Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Senarath Bandara and
Werellahinna claimed by A. M. Ranbanda; West: Lot B.
