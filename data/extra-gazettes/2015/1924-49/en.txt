PROMOTION   AND   APPOINTMENT  APPROVED  BY  HIS  EXCELLENCY  THE  PRESIDENT
IT is hereby notified that  His Excellency the President has been pleased to  promote Rear Admiral Ravindra  Chandrasiri
Wijegunaratne,  WV , RWP, USP ,  ndc,  psn to the rank of Vice Admiral and to appoint him as the Head of the Sri Lanka Navy
with effect from July11, 2015 under the Article 61 E (a) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka.
                                                By  Order  of  His  Excellency,
P.  B .  A
BEYKOON,
Secretary to the President.
