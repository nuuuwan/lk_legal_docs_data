LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
My No. : 4/3/1/(Appointments - Acquisitions.)
I, Munasinghe Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa Gunawardene, Minister of Lands shall order the below mentioned
regulation under the provisions of Section 63(2)a of the Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 460).
Regulation No. : 2015/05 - Appointments
The officers mentioned in schedule II, are hereby appointed for land acquisition duties in terms of the Land
Acquisition Act for the period described in the Schedule III in the areas referred to in Schedule I to expedite the land
acquisitions for Weras Ganga Storm Water Drainage and Environment Improvement Project implemented by the Sri Lanka
Land Reclamation and Development Corporation.
