No. 1914/38 – FRIDAY, MAY  15, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7  
 
 Ref. No.: AM/DS/D.E.V.7/E/1/165. 
4/3/9/2011/WS/162. 
 
I, do hereby inform that the Government expects to 
acquire under mentioned land for public usage according 
to the Sub -section (1) of Section 7 of the Land 
Acquisition Act, No. 28 of 1964. 
 
The details of land expected to acquire : 
 
02 land lots with extend of 0.4269 Hectare which 
described in 1st lot of AM/4278 surveyed by the Ampara 
District Senior Superintendent of Survey where situated 
at Paragakale West village of Wewgampaththu North of 
Divisional Secretariat, Ampara in the Ampara District of 
Eastern Province. 
 
01. Land Lot No. 01 - Paragakale Extent: 0.0324 
Hectare. 
 
North: PP/AM/245 Lot No. 35; East: PP/AM/245 Lot 
No. 35; South: Lot No. 02; West: PP/AM/245 Lot No. 36. 
 
02. Land Lot No. 02 - Paragakale Extent: 0.03945 
Hectare. 
 
North: PP/AM/245 Lot No. 01 and 45; East: PP/
AM/245 Lot No. 49; South: PP/AM/245 Lot No. 44; 
West: PP/AM/245 Lot No. 44, 43 and 36. 
 
If any person or authorized person by the document 
who has ownership or any other connection in the above 
land/acquisition can meet me at Divisional Secretariat at 
10.30 a.m. on 24.06.2015. 
And, it is necessary to forward me 02 copies of letter 
before 17.06.2015 containing states of ownership, reasons 
for compensation, compensation amount and method of 
amount calculation of the above land. 
 
 M. M. S. K. Bandara Mapa, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Ampara. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Ampara. 
27th April, 2015.
