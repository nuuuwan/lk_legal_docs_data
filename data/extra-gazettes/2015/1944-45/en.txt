THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands  hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is  needed for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An  allotment of  land  called ‘‘Govinnehena’’ in total extent of
About 0.0335 Hectare, described  in the Amended Advance Tracing
No. Maha/TMP/2014/627 dated 20th June, 2015,  prepared  by the
Surveyor-General,  situated at  the Village of Niyambepola in
Udapalatha Korale - East Minor Division in the Divisional Secretary’s
Division of Thumpane in Kandy District,  Central  Province and
bounded as follows :
01. Lot ‘A’ – Govinnehena – Extent: About 0.0335  Hectare
North and East: Remaining portion of the same land; South and
West: Remaining portion of the same land and Road.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
