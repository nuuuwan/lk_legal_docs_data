EXCISE   ORDINANCE
Excise Notification No. 985
BY Virtue of the powers vested in me by Sections 4 and 13 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), I, Ravi Karunanayake,
Minister of Finance do by this Order direct that, with effect from 01.01.2016
(1) The limit of sale of foreign liquor by retail, with respect to the whole island shall be 7.5 liters ; and
(2) The transport of maximum quantity of foreign liquor shall be 7.5 litres without a pass issued under the Ordinance
with respect to the whole island.
R
AVI K ARUNANAYAKE,
 Minister of Finance.
Ministry of Finance,
Colombo 01,
31st December 2015.
