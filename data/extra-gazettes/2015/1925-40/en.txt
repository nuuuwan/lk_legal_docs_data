SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL   COUNCIL
 Notification  under the Section 184 of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)  and the Provincial
Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989
 I, Maheepala Herath, Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning, Local Government, Health and
Indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs, Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment, News,
Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province, appointed a retired Judicial Officer, as a single person tribunal
of inquiry  as per the powers vested in me by the provisions of Section 184 (1) of  the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter
255)  to inquire and submit me a report whether he has committed an offence based on the matters under Section 184 (1)
of the Urban Council Ordinance (Chapter 255),  regarding the functions of the Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban Council
of the Ratnapura District,   Ven. V ewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero and now I have received the above mentioned inquiry
report.  Having deeply studying the allegations, evidences and documents against Ven. Vewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero,  I
am satisfied  that there are enough evidences to prove the said Ven. V ewelduwe Gnanaprabha Thero has committed one or
more offences  specified  under section 184 (1)  of the Urban Council Ordinance. Hence, I do hereby expel Ven. Vewelduwe
Gnanaprabha Thero, Chairman of the Embilipitiya Urban Council  from the post of Chairman  of the said Urban Council
and from its membership by virtue of powers vested in me under the provisions of Section  184 (1B) (B)   of the Urban
Council Ordinance (Chapter 255)
M
AHEEPALA H ERATH,
Chief Minister and Minister of Law and Order, Finance and Planning,
Local Government, Health and Indigenous Medicine, Women Affairs,
Co-operatives, Trade and Food, Transport and Estate Welfare, Environment,
News, Housing and Construction of the Sabaragamuwa Province.
Chief Ministry of the Sabararagamuwa Provincial Council,
New Town,
Ratnapura,
On this 28th   of July, 2015.
