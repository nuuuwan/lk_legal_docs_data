PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL COUNCILS
1A
 UVA   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointment  made by  the  Governor  of   Uva  Province
BY virtue of  powers vested to me under Section 154F. (5) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, I, M. P. Jayasinghe, Governor of Uva Province, do hereby announce that I have made the
following appointment for the Provincial Council of Uva Province, with effect from 07th December, 2015.
01. Hon. Upali Delton Samaraweera - Minister of Agriculture, Irrigation, Animal Product,
Member of the Uva Provincial Inland Fisheries Affairs of Uva Province.
Council
M. P . J
AYASINGHE,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Uva Governor,
Badulla,
07th December, 2015.
