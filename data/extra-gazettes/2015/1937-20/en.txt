 Pawnbrokers’ Statute  of  the  Western  Province
Provincial  Council  No. 01 of  2015
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 53 of the Pawnbrokers’ Statute No. 01 of 2015 of the Western Provincial
Council, I Edirisingha Arachchige Isura Dewapriya Dharmasena Perera, Chief Minister and Minister of Finance and Plan
Implementation, Engineering Services, Law and Order, Local Government and Provincial Administration, Economic Promotion,
Power and Energy, Environmental Affairs, Water Supply and Drainage and Tourism of the Western Province Provincial

effect from Gazette date.

REGULATIONS
1. These regulations may be cited as the regulations under Pawnbroker’s Statue of the Western Provincial Council No.
01 of 2015
2. The following fees and deposit should be charged on Pawn Broking Business under Sub section 7(3) and 9(2) of
this statute,
Description Fee Rs.
a. Registration of Pawn Broking Business 25,000/-
b. Annual License fee payable for Pawn Broking Business 5,000/-
c. Amount of security payable by Pawn Broking Business holder 100,000/-
3. Security deposit shall be credited only to a deposit account of People Bank, Bank of Ceylon and National Svings
Bank.
