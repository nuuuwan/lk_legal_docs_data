THE  HOLIDAYS  ACT,  No. 29 OF 1971
Order  under Section 8
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 8 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of 1971, I, Joseph Michael Perera, Minister of
Home Affairs and Fisheries, do by this order declare that, as regards the year 2016, each of  the days specified in the Schedule
hereto shall be Full Moon Poya Days for the purpose of this Act.
JOSEPH MICHAEL PERERA,
  Minister of  Home Affairs and Fisheries.
Ministry of  Home Affairs and Fisheries,
Independence Square,
Colombo 07,
16th  March  2015.
