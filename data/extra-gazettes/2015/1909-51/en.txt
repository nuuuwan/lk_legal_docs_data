THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Notice under Section 7  
 
My No.: GCF/Ath/KD/25/LR. 
 
Land Min’s Ref. No.: 4/3/10/2012/LRB/100. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that            
the Government indents to acquire the land described 
below to be utilized for the tanks constructed in Sri 
Jayawardhanapura area under the Greater Colombo Flood 
Control and Environment Improvement Project. 
 
 Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Two allotments of land in total extent of 1.5678 
Hectares depicted as Lot No. 01 and 02 in the Preliminary 
Plan No. Co. 9519 dated 20.02.2015 prepared by the 
Survey General situated in the village called Battaramulla 
(within the Municipal Council limit of Kaduwela) in the 
Grama Niladhari division No. 491/B of Kotuwegoda in 
the Divisional Secretary’s Division of Kaduwela in 
Colombo District of Western Province and described as 
follows.  
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of the Land: Land at Kotte Road of which the 
assessment number could not be traced; Present Land use: 
Diyawanna Oya; Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent: 
1.2213 Hectares. 
Lot No. 02 
 
Name of the Land: Land at Kotte Road of which the 
assessment number could not be traced; Present Land use: 
Home Garden; Claimant: Could not be traced; Extent: 
0.3465 Hectares. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands* on 
which a servitude is to be  acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Greater Colombo Flood Control Project Office 
located in the Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and 
Development Corporation, No. 03, Sri Jayawardenapura 
Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya on 15th May, 2015 at 9.30 
a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 8th May, 2015 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land/servitude* 
the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.
