Government  Notifications
PROVINCIAL   COUNCILS   ELECTIONS   ACT,   No.  2  OF  1988
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE
EASTERN   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the Secretary of the Eastern Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the Provincial
Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Eastern Province Provincial
Council by reason of Resignation ;
and whereas the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance, the recognized political party to which the
member belonged was requested by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill such
vacancy ;
and whereas the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party, fails to make a nomination within the specified
period, to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya, Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988, do hereby declare elected Mr. Ali Zahir Moulana Seyed, who has secured
the highest number of preferences next to the last of the Members declared elected at the Eastern Province Provincial
Councils Election which was held on 08th September 2012, as a member of the above Provincial Council for the Administrative
District of Batticaloa in respect of which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of  Elections.
Elections Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
20th January, 2015.
