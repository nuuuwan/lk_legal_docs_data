No. 1914/11 – WEDNESDAY, MAY  13, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
 
Notice under Section 7 
 
I hereby give notice under sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, that the Government intends to 
acquire the following lands which are required for a 
public purpose. 
 
Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
Below mentioned lands in total extent of about 0.0677 
Hectare, Preliminary Plan No . K 4509, prepared by the 
Senior Superintendent of Survey of Kaluthara District on 
behalf of Survey General, situated in the Grama Niladhari 
Division of 822 F, Kongasthanna in the village of 
Halwathura in Divisional Secretary’s Division of 
Bulathsinghala  in Kaluthara District in the Western 
Province.  
 
01. Lot No. 1 
 
Name of land: Elabodaovita or Mahaovita; 
Description: Open waste land; Claimant: State; Original 
Claimant: R. A. Subash, Halwathura, Bulathsinhala; 
Extent: 0.0015 Hectare. 
 
02. Lot No. 2 
 
Name of land: Elabodaovita or Mahaovita 
Kosgahagodalla; Description: Open waste land; Claimant: 
State; Original Claimant: R. A. D. Rathnasiri, Hal-
wathura, Bulathsinhala; Extent: 0.0338 Hectare. 
 
03. Lot No. 3 
 
Name of land: Kosgahaovita; Description: Open 
waste land; Claimant: State; Original Claimant: —; 
Extent: 0.0324 Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid lands are 
hereby required to appear personally or by agent duly 
authorized in writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat 
office of Bulathsinhala on 25th May, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. 
and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing (in 
duplicate) on or before the 25th May, 2015 the nature of 
their interests in the land, the particulars of their claims 
for compensation for the acquisition of the said lands, the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
 I RANGANIE WEERASINGHE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Bulathsinhala. 
 
 
Divisional Secretary’s Office, 
Bulathsinhala, 
25th  March, 2015.
