PART I : SECTION (I) — GENERAL
MEDIATION    (SPECIAL  CATEGORIES    OF    DISPUTES)  ACT,  No.  21  OF  2003
Order under Section 8(1)
By Virtue of powers vested in me by Section 3 of the Mediation (Special Categories of Disputes) Act, No. 21 of 2003, I,
Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Minister of Justice and Labour Relations, do by this Order, specify that the monetary value of the
subject matter within the Special Mediation Boards Area specified by order published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 1904/41
dated 04.03.2015 shall be less than Rs. 2,000,000 in respect of a dispute of the category of disputes determined by order
publsihed in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 1901/8 dated 10.02.2015 arising wholly or partly within such area.
D
R. WIJEYADASA RAJAPAKSHE PC,
Minister of Justice and Labour Relations.
Colombo,
19th  March, 2015.

2A
Regulation
1. This Regulation may be cited as the Mediation (Qualifications of Mediators) Regulations No........... of 2015.
2. A person eligible to be appointed as a Member of the Panel of Mediators in respect of the Special Mediation Boards
specified in the  Order published in Gazette No. 1904/41 of 04.03.2015  shall be between 35 to 70 years of age and ;
(a) has any professional qualification obtained from a recognized professional body established by or under any
written law ; or
(b) has been or is public officer.
