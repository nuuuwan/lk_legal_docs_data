THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT)ACT, No. 28 OF 1964  
 
(Notice under Section 7) 
 
My Reference No.: 3/2/3/10/249. 
 
 
I hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964 that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for the construction of Water Treatment Plant 
under the Gampaha Attanagalla, Minuvangoda Integrated 
Water Supply Project. 
 
Description of the Land  to be acquired: 
 
An allotments of land the name and extent mentioned 
below depicted to preliminary plan No. Gam. 3956 
prepared by the Surveyor General situated in the Village 
of Karasnagala in Grama Niladhari Division of No. 365 C 
Karasnagala (South) in the Divisional Secretaries 
Division of Attanagalla in Gampaha District Western 
Province. 
 
1. Lot No. 1   
 
Name of the Land: Dammullahena; Description: Open 
Waste Land with well;  Claimant: State; Previouse 
Claimant: Land Reform Commission; Extent: 1.2056 
Hectare. 
 
All persons interested in the  aforesaid land is to be 
acquired are hereby required to appear personally, or by 
agents duly authorized in writing, before me at Divisional 
Secretariat Attanagalla on the 26th day of August 2015 at 
10.00 a.m. and are hereby requested to notify to me in 
writing (in duplicate) on or before the 19th day of August 
2015 the nature of their interests in the land, the 
particulars of their claims for compensation for the 
acquisition of the said land, the amount of compensation 
and the details of the computation of such amount. 
 
 D. M. R ATHNAYAKE, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Attanagalla. 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Attanagalla. 
20th July, 2015.
