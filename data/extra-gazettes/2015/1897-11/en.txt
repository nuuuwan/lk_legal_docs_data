UV A  PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification
IT is hereby notified that I, C. Nanda Mathew, Governor of the Uva Province, do hereby announce that, Hon. Chief Minister
Shashindra Rajapaksha has been removed, from the post of Chief Minister of Uva Province, with effect from January 14, 2015,
who was appointed by me as a Chief Minister of Uva Province with effect from September 30, 2014 by virtue of powers vested
to me under Section 154F (4) of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
C. N
ANDA MA THEW,
Governor of Uva Province.
Office of the Governor - Uva,
Badulla,
14th January,  2015.
