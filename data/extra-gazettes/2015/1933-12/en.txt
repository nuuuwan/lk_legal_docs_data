EXTRAORDINARY
1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF  THE
COLOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 262)  AS  AMENDED BY
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)   ACT No. 30 OF 2013
WHEREAS  the office of a member  in  Colombo  Municipal  Council  has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely Democratic Peoples Front,  to which
the  former  member, Kurusamy Kalangandi Thevar belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the  aforesaid
Ordinance,  to  nominate within  a period of   thirty  (30) days  a person eligible under the  Ordinance for election as a
member of  that Colombo Municipal Council  who shall be declared  elected as a member ;
AND  WHEREAS the  Secretary of  the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated  Mrs. Manjula Perumal
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Sivasubramaniam Achchuthan, Returning Officer of  Colombo  Municipal  Council, acting
in  terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of Vacancies in Local
authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mrs. Manjula Perumal elected as a
member of the  Colombo Municipal Council.
S. A
CHCHUTHAN,
Returning Officer,
Colombo Municipal Council.
City    Elections Office,
Colombo,
18th  September,  2015.
