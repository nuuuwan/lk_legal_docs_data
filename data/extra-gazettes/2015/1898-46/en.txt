1A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  OFFICE  OF  CHAIRMAN  OF  THE
SORANATHOTA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of  Chairman in Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United People’s Freedom Alliance the recognized political party, to which
Manamendra Patabendige Leelasena the former Chairman, belonged was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to decide upon the member of that Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party, who shall be
declared elected as Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has failed to communicate his decision
within the period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of Soranathota
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. Wijerathna Gamage
Neel Premathilaka who had secured the next highest number of preferences at the election held on 17th March, 2011 to
Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha elected as Chairman of the Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha, in respect of which such vacancy
occured.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
20th of January,  2015.

2 A
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE  MEMBER  OF  THE
SORANATHOTA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)  OF  THE
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES    ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)  AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS  the office of  a member in Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of Resignation ;
AND  WHEREAS the Secretary of  the recognized political party namely United People’s Freedom Alliance, to which
the former member, Manamendra Patabendige Leelasena , belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid
Ordinance, to nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for Election as a member of that
Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha who shall be declared elected as a member ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid  recognized political party has failed to make a nomination within the
period specified ;
NOW THEREFORE, I, Rathnayaka Ralalage Asanka Sathis Kumara Rathnayaka, Returning Officer of Soranathota
Pradeshiya Sabha, acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of
vacancies in Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262) do hereby declare Mr. R. M. Kumarasekara
who had secured the highest number of preferences next to the last of the member declared elected at the election held on 17th
of March, 2011 to Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha elected as a member of the Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha, in respect of which
such vacancy occured.
A
SANKA RATHNAYAKA,
Returning Officer,
Soranathota Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Badulla,
20th of January,  2015.
