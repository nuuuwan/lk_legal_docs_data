THE LAND ACQUISITION AMENDMENT ACT 
NO. 28  OF 1964 ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Notice under Section 7 
 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: LD/05/2007/HA/19. 
 
My  Ref. No.: 3/1/14/45. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act No. 28 of 1964  
(Amendement) that the Government intends to acquire 
the following land which is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the land to be acquired: 
 
An alloment of land called  St. Bridget Estate in total 
extent of 0.3965 hectare depicted as Lot 01 in the 
Preliminary Plan No. Ku. 2307 situated in the Village 
called Gonawa in the Grama Niladhari Division No. 869 
Weerambugedara in the Divisional Secretary’s Division 
of Weerambugedara in Kurunegala District of the 
Northwestern Province and morefully described as 
follows: 
 
Schedule 
 
Name of the Land: St. Bridget Estate; Preliminary 
Plan No.: Ku. 2307; Description: Home garden with a 
mix cultivation. Comprising eight coconut trees of 45 -55 
years old, three jack trees of 10 -15 years old and four 
king coconut trees of 10 -15 years old, a two storied 
permanent building, six permanent buildings, a temporary 
building and a water tank; Claimant: Land Reform 
Commission; Total Extent: 0.3965 Hectare. 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land are hereby 
required to appear personally or by agents duly 
authorized in writing before me at the Weerambugedara 
Divisional Secretariat on 18th day of March, 2015 at 
10.00 and are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before 11th March 2015 the nature of 
their interests in the Land, the particulars of their                
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land, the amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount.  
 
All deeds and documents in support of the claims 
should be produced before me at the inquiry. 
  
 R. A. S.  JAYATHILAKA,  
 Divisional Secretary & Acquiring Officer. 
  
Divisional Secretary’s Office,  
Weerambugedara, 
12th January, 2015.
