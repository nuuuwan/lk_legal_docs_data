THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described
below are needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
The allotments of below mentioned lands in total extent of about
0.1157 Hectare, depicted in the Advance Tracing No. MR/HKM/
2014/422 and dated 20th November, 2014,  situated in the Village of
Denagama (FVP 178) in Minor Division of Kandabada Pattuwa East
in the Divisional Secretary’s Division, of Hakmana in Matara District,
Southern Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga –
Extent: 0.0005 Hectare
North: Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimant not
known; East: Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimant
not known, Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimed by
Madage Pemarathna and Lot B; South: Lot B and Road (R.D.A.);
West: Road (R.D.A.) and Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga
claimant not known.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga –
Extent: 0.0037 Hectare
North: Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimed by
Madage Pemarathna; East and South: Road (R.D.A.); West: Road
(R.D.A.), Lot A, Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimant
not known and Mulane Athmaga or Badaldeniya Athmaga claimed
by Madage Pemarathna.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Bogaha Koratuwa – Extent: 0.0053 Hectare
North and East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.), Lot D and
Bogaha Koratuwa claimant not known; West: Lot D and Bogaha
Koratuwa claimant not known.
PRINTED AT THE DEPARTMENT OF GOVERNMENT PRINTING , SRI LANKA.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Bogaha Koratuwa – Extent: 0.0266 Hectare
North: Denagama Wewa, Bogaha Koratuwa claimant not known
and Lot C; East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Road (R.D.A.) and Denagama
Wewa; West: Denagama Wewa.
05. Lot ‘E’ – Meemanagoda Madiththa – Extent: 0.0008
Hectare
North: Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.), Meemanagoda
Madiththa claimed by W. Garusingha; South: Meemanagoda
Madiththa claimed by W. Garusingha; West: Road (R.D.A.).
06. Lot ‘F’ – Uruwalahena Koratuwa Athmaga – Extent:
0.0674 Hectare
North: Denagama Wewa; East: Road (R.D.A.); South: Road
(R.D.A.) and Lot G; West: Lot G and Denagama Wewa.
07. Lot ‘G’ – Heenidukuttiya – Extent: 0.0114 Hectare
North: Heenidukuttiya claimed by H. S. P. Lamahami and others,
Denagama Wewa and Lot F; East: Lot F and Road (R.D.A.); South:
Road (R.D.A.); West: Heenidukuttiya claimed by H. S. P. Lamahami
and others.
