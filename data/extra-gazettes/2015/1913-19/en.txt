STATE   LANDS  ORDINANCE  CHAPTER  454
Revocation  of  Special  Grant  Made  Under  Section  6
WHEREAS by instrument dated 19th July, 2012 His Excellency  Mahinda Rajapaksa President of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka  alienated the land and premises described  therein to the Board of  Investment  of Sri Lanka of World
Trade Centre, Colombo 1. I, Maithripala  Sirisena President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka do hereby
revoke, annul and set aside the aforesaid Special Grant dated 19th July 2012, numbered 4/10/35833.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
07th  May 2015,
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo   01.
THE  BOARD  OF  INVESTMENT  OF  SRI  LANKA  LAW No. 4 OF  1978
Revocation  of  Order  Made  Under  Section  22A
I, Maithripala Sirisena, President of the Democratic  Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, do hereby revoke the Gazette Notification
declaring the Specified Zone for Heavy Industries, published in the Gazette (Extraordinary) No. 1758/26 dated 17.05.2012
made under Sub-section (2) of Section 22A of the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka Law No. 4 of 1978, as amended.
M
AITHRIPALA SIRISENA,
President of  the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
07th  May 2015.
Presidential Secretariat,
Colombo   01.
