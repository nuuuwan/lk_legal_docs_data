CENTRAL     PROVINCE     PROVINCIAL     COUNCIL
Removal of Board Members of the Central Provincial Road Development Authority under the Central Provincial  Road
Development Authority Charter No. 2 of 2003 of the Provincial Council of the Central Province
I, the Minister of Road Development, Transport, Power and Energy and Housing Construction of the Central Provincial
Council, hereby announce that the Board Members of the Central Provincial Road Development Authority appointed by me
through the Gazette No. 1847/4 of 27th January 2014, as per the Section No. 3(1) and (2) in the part I of the Central Provincial
Road Development Authority Charter No. 2 of 2003 of the Central Provincial Council, are removed from the Director Board of
the Central Provincial Road Development Authority with effect from the date mentioned before their names, as per the powers
bestowed on me by the Statement No. 3(8) in part I of the same Charter.
E
DIRIWEERA WEERAWARDENA,
Minister of Road Development, Transport,
Power and Energy and Housing Construction,
Central Provincial Council.
