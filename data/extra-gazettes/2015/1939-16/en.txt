PART III  — LANDS
Land  Reform  Commission  Notices
ORDER  UNDER  SUB  SECTION (4), SECTION  27‘a’  OF  THE  LAND  REFORM  ACT,  No. 01 OF   1972  OF  THE
PARLIAMENT
BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27a(4) of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 amended by the Land Reform
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 39 of 1981 of the Parliament, I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, do hereby,
(a) Amend the order by way of revocation of the order made on the portion of land in relation to the extent of land
of the land called Nalanda Waththa depicted in the Second Schedule of the order made under Section 27a(1)
of the Land Reform Act, No. 01 of 1972 in the Gazette Extraordinary No. 181/12 dated 27th February 1982
in respect of the land called and mentioned as Nalanda Waththa, which has been alienated to Sri Lanka State
Plantation Corporation established under Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation Act, No. 4 of 1958 and
depicted comprehensively in first Schedule of this order, due to not following the order or conditions of the
Agreement made of alienation of the land to Sri Lanka State Plantation Corporation.
(b) It is hereby declared that the extent of land depicted sepcifically in Second Schedule of this order is alienated
again to the Land Refrom Commission as a result of this revocation.
M. K. A. D. S. G
UNAW ARDANA,
Minister of Lands.
Ministry of Lands,
Land Secretariat,
Mihikatha Medura,
No. 1200/6, Rajamalwaththa Avenue,
Battaramulla
05th October, 2015.
