No. 1932/47 – TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 15, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460) 
AS AMENDED BY THE LAND ACQUISITION 
(AMENDMENT) ACT, No. 28 of 1964  
Notice under Section 7 
   
My Reference No. PLDS/LNDO4/ACQ/42.  
I hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of 
the Land Acquisition Act, as amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land/
servitude which are required for the public purpose. 
 
Description of the Land /Servitude to be Acquired: 
 
Lot No. 838, 839, 840 as depicted in the Sheet No. 36 
and  the Extra No. 34 in the Final Village Plan No. 208 
prepared by the Superintend of Surveyor Rathnapura, 
situated in the Grama Niladhari Division of 172 
Lellopitiya in Meda Paththuwa in the Divisional 
Secretary’s Division of Pelmadulla of Rathnapura District 
in Sabaragamuwa Province. 
 
Lot No. 838— Extent: 0.0216 Hectare 
North: Lot Nos. 746, 748, 747 and 841;  
East: Lot   Nos. 746, 747 and 841; South: Lot No. 839; 
West: Lot    Nos. 746 and 748. 
 
Lot No. 839— Extent: 0.2855 Hectare 
North: Lot Nos. 756, 750, 746, 749, 838, 841, 840 and 
842; East: Lot Nos. 840, 841 and 842; South: Lot                     
233 1/2, 233 and 235; West: Lot   Nos. 756, 750, 746 and 
749. 
Lot No. 840— Extent: 0.0107 Hectare 
North: Lot Nos. 840, 741 and 842; East: Lot                
Nos. 840, 741 and 842; South: Lot No. 839; West: Lot              
Nos. 839 and 840. 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands  on 
which a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in 
writing, before me at Divisional Secretariat Office of 
Pelmadulla on the 2nd November 2015 at 10.00 a. m. and 
are hereby requested to notify to me in writing                      
(in duplicate) on or before the  2nd November, 2015 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particulars of their  
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said  
land/servitude, the amount of compensation and the 
details of the computation of such amount. 
  
 A.  SUGATH SISIRA KUMARA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Pelmadulla. 
 
 
Divisional Secretariat, 
Pelmadulla. 
14th September, 2015.
