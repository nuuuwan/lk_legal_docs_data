IT is hereby notified that His Excellency the President has been pleased, under  Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, to appoint Mr. Sumith Abeysinghe,  Secretary to the Cabinet as Acting Secretary
to the President with effect from 15th February  2015 until Mr. P. B. Abeykoon, Secretary to President returns to the Island.
