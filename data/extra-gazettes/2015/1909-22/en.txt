THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)  
 
 Notice under Section 7  
 
Reference No.: ACQ/O/J/D/149. 
Ministry No.: 4-3/4/2014/D/149. 
 
I, hereby give notice, under Sub-section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act as Amended by the land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government indents to acquire the following land which 
is required for a public purpose. 
 
 Description of the lands to be acquired: 
 
Preliminary Plan - Ya 2789 
Situation Village - Valvedditturai 
GN Division - Valvedditturai South East, J/392 
DS Division - Vadamaradchchi North 
District - Jaffna 
Province - Northern 
 
Lot No. 1 
 
Name of the Land: Not Known; Description: Garden 
contains Permanent building (abandoned) and a masonry 
well; Name of the Claimed: Thavamani Shekspear No. 
376, Galle Road, Colombo 03; Extent: 0.1656 Hectare. 
 
 
 All persons interested in the aforesaid land/lands on 
which a servitude is to be  acquired are hereby required to 
appear personally, or by agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat, Vadamarachchi North/
Point Pedro on the 28th Tuesday of April 2015 at 10.00 
a.m. and  are hereby requested to notify to me in writing 
(in duplicate) on or before the 21st day of April 2015 the 
nature of their interests in the land, the particular of their 
claims for compensation for the acquisition of the said 
land amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 
  W ICKRAMA WARAGODA,

 District Secretariat,
