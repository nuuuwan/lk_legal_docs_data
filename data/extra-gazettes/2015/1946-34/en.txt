THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Wijesundara Mudiyanselage Thilakarathne Bandara Ekanayake,
Acting Minister of Lands, hereby declare under Sub-section (1) of
Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described below
is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land called Mappapulam  in total extent of about
0.3163 Hectare,  depicted  as lot ‘‘A’’ in the Advance Tracing No. Ya/
SND/2014/27 dated  06.05 2014, prepared by the Senior
Superintendent of Surveys' Jaffna   situated in the  Village of
Pandattarippu in  the Grama Niladhari Division No. J/146
Pandattarippu in the  Divisional Secretary’s Division of Valikamam
South West, in Jaffana  District in   Northern Province and bounded
as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’  – Mappapulam  – Extent: 0.3163 Hectare
North: Road (P .S) and Road (RDA); East: Mappapulam Claimed
by Parameswary wife of Navaratnam Nadarajah; South: Mappapulam
Claimed by Parameswary wife of Navaratnam Nadarajah and
Mappapulam claimed by Edward Navaratna and Others; West:
Mappapulam Claimed by Edward Navaratna,  Others and
Road (P .S).
