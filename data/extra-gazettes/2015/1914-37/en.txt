No. 1914/37 – FRIDAY, MAY 15, 2015
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT (CHAPTER 460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Munasingha Kariyawasam Appuhamilage Don Somadasa
Gunawardana, Minister of Lands, hereby declare, under Sub-section
(1) of Section 5 of the Land Acquisition Act, that the land described
below is needed for a public purpose and will be acquired under the
provisions of that Act.
An allotment of land in total extent of about 4.1758 Hectares
depicted as ‘Lot A’  in Advance Tracing No. Ma/MNN/2014/237 of
24.11.2014, prepared by the Senior Superintendant of Surveys -
Vavuniya, situated in the Village of Nadukkuda in the Divisional
Secretary’s Division of Mannar Town in Mannar District in Northern
Province and bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Selvariwatta – Extent: 4.1758 Hectares
North: Selvariwatta claimed by Land Reform Commission, Path
and Road (R.D.A.); East: Road (R.D.A.) and Selvariwatta claimed
by Kasan Mohideen and others; South: Selvariwatta claimed by
Kasan Mohideen and others and Selvariwatta claimed by M. K. M.
Saisa Umma and others; West: Selvariwatta claimed by M. K. M.
Saisa Umma and others, Selvariwatta claimed by Land Reform
Commission and Path.
M. K. A. D. S. Gunawardana,
Minister of Lands.
