L.D.B 21/78(xii).
THE UNIVERSITIES ACT, No. 16 OF 1978
Order  under  Section 27(1)
BY  virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27(1) of the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978, I, Dr. Sarath Amunugama,
Minister of Higher Education and Research, do by this order, on the recommendation of the University Grants Commission,
amend the Schedule to the Order made under Section 21 of the said Act in relation to the Rajarata University of Sri Lanka
published in Gazette Extraordinary No. 896/2 of November 7, 1995 and as last amended by Order published in Gazette
Extraordinary No. 1856/5 of March 31, 2014, by the subsitution for the item “Facultry of Social Sciences and Humanities”
appearing in Column I of such Schedule and its corresponding entries appearing in Column II thereto, and the substitution
therefore of the following :-
“Column I Column II
Faculty Departments
Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities 1. Department of Social Studies
2. Department of Humanities
3. Department of  Archeology and Heritage Management
4. Department of  Environmental Management
5. Department of  Languages.”.
D
R. SARATH AMUNUGAMA,
Minister of Higher Education and Research.
Colombo,
30th March 2015.
