PROVINCIAL  COUNCILS  ELECTIONS  ACT,  No. 2 OF 1988
FILLING  OF  A  V ACANCY  IN  THE
NORTH  WESTERN   PROVINCE  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
WHEREAS the Secretary of the North Western Province Provincial Council has informed me under Section 65(1) of the
Provincial Councils Elections Act, No. 2 of 1988 that a vacancy has occurred in the membership of the North Western
Province Provincial Council by reason of being elected as a member of the Parliament ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the United National Party , the recongized political party,  to which the
member belonged, was required by me under Section 65(2) of the aforesaid Act, to nominate an eligible person to fill
such vacancy ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr. Al. M. S. Sahabdeen
to fill such vacancy ;
I, Waduge Warunasiri Mahinda Deshapriya,  Commissioner of Elections, acting in terms of Section 65(2) of the
Provincial Council Elections Act, No. 02 of 1988, do hereby declare Mr. Al. M. S. Sahabdeen, elected as a member of the
above Provincial Council from the Administrative District of Kurunegala in respect of the which such vacancy occurred.
M
AHINDA D ESHAPRIYA,
Commissioner of Elections.
Election Secretariat,
Rajagiriya,
10th  September, 2015.
