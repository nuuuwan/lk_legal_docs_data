THE  LAND  ACQUISITION  ACT  (CHAPTER  460)
Declaration under Section 5
I, Janaka Bandara Thennakoon, Minister of Lands and Land
Development hereby declare, under Sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Land Acquisition Act, that the lands described below are needed
for a public purpose  and will be acquired under the provisions of
that Act.
The allotments of  land in extent of about 1.5466 Hectare, depicted
as Lots A to  F in Advanced  Tracing  No .  KE/RMB/2014/311 and
dated 24th December, 2014 prepared by Senior Syrvey Superinterdent
of Kegalle District and situated in the Village of Kavdaulla, in the
minor division of Ginigoda Korale, in the Divisional Secretary's
Division of Rambukkana in the Disitrict of Kegalle in Sabaragamuwa
Province and  bounded as follows:
01. Lot ‘A’ – Yommana Kumbura and Aswaduwama
Kumbura  – Extent: 0.0304 Hectare
North: Kanthapitiya Watta and Lot B; East: Lot B; South: Lot B
and Kanthapitiya Watta; West: Kanthapitiya Watta.
02. Lot ‘B’ – Yommana Kumbura and Aswadduma
Kumbura  – Extent: 0.3603 Hectare
North: Aswadduma Kumbura; East: Aswadduma Pillawa, Lots C
and D; South: Lot D  and Thimpirigahamula Kumbura; West:
Kanthapitiya Watta and Lot A.
03. Lot ‘C’ – Yommana Kumbura and Aswadduma
Kumbura  – Extent: 0.0411 Hectare
North: Lot B and Yommana Kumbura; East: Aswadduma pillawa,
Lot D; South: Lot D; West:  Lot B.
04. Lot ‘D’ – Yommana Kumbura and Aswadduma
Kumbura  – Extent: 0.4022 Hectare
North: Lot C and Aswadduma Pillawa; East: Road (Pradeshiya
Shaba) and Lot E; South: Lot E; West: Thimbirigahamula Kumbura
and Lot B.
05. Lot ‘E’   – Extent: 0.0284 Hectare
North: Lot D; East: Road (Pradeshiya Shaba) and Lot F; South:
Lot F and Stream; West: Gravel Road Thimbirigahamula Kumbura
and Lot D.
06. Lot ‘F’ – Yommana Kumbura and Aswadduma
Kumbura  – Extent: 0.6842 Hectare
North: Lot E and Road (Pradeshiya Shaba); East: Road (Pradeshiya
Shaba) and Aswadduma Kumbura; South: Aswadduma Kumbura;
West: Stream and Lot E.
