PART IV (A)  -  PROVINCIAL    COUNCILS

UV A   PROVINCE   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Appointments   made by  the  Hon.  Governor  of   Uva Province
BY virtue of the powers vested to me by Section 32(1) of the Provincial Council Act, No. 42 of 1987, I, M. P . Jayasinghe,
Governor of Uva Province do hereby notify that the following appointments have been approved  by me.
1. Mr. H. M. Jeewantha Herath, Grade III officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Commissioner
of Housing for Uva Province in addition to his permanent post, with effect from the date 01.06.2015.
2. Mr. W. M. M. G. Abesinghe Banda, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting
Commissioner of Co-operative Development and Registrar of Societies of Uva Province in addition to his
permanent post, with effect from the date 01.06.2015.
3. Mr. M. M. Ananda Wijitha Kumara, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Planning Service, as Acting Deputy Chief
Secretary (Planning) for the Uva Provincial Council, on full time basis with effect from the date 17.10.2014.
4. Mr. M. M. Wijenayake, Grade III officer of  the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of the
Provincial Commissioner of Local Government of Uva Province, from the date 01.07.2015.
5. Mrs. R. M. S. D. Rathnayake, Grade I officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, to perform the duties of
the Deputy Chief Secretary (Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council, from the date 27.06.2015
to 06.07.2015, till the return of Mr. Sumith Suraweera to the Island who is the Acting Deputy Chief Secretary
(Personnel and Training) for the Uva Provincial Council.
6. Mrs. R. A. H. A. Samarasinghe, Special Grade officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, as Acting Secretary
to the Uva Provincial Council Public Service Commission, from the date 27.06.2015 to 06.07.2015, till the
return of Mr. R. M. T. B. Hathiyaldeniya to the Island who is the Secretary to the Uva Provincial Council Public
Service Commission.
7. Mrs. S. K. Ekanayake, Grade III officer of the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, to perform the duties of the
Deputy Chief Secretary (Engineering Service) for the Uva Provincial Council, in addition to her permanent
post, from the date 27.06.2015 to 06.07.2015, till the return of Mr. A. A. P. S. K. Karunarathne, to the Island who is
the Acting Deputy Chief Secretary (Engineering  Service)
