NORTH    WESTERN    PROVINCE    PROVINCIAL    COUNCIL
Order by virtue of the power vested in me under the Provisions of the Provincial Councils (Conseqential Provisions) Act,
No. 12 of 1989, read with the Provisions of the Co-operative Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 and as amended under the
Provisions of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983
J. P. Dayasiri Pathmakumara Jayasekera, Attorney-at-Law, Chief Minister and the Minister-in-charge of the subject of Finance
an Plannng Law and Peace, Local Government and Manpower, Education and Cultural Affairs, Land, Environment, Tourism,
Investment Co-ordinate, Co-operative and Food Supply and Distribution of North Western Provincial Council, do hereby
delegate the power vested with the Commissioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western
Provincial Council, under the Provisions of the Co-operatives Societies Act, No. 05 of 1972 as amended under the Provisions
of the Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1992 and Act, No. 32 of 1983 and the rules framed thereunder,
thereafter, as per Column III and from the date as per Column II to the persons named as per Column I, to assist the Commis-
sioner and the Registrar of Co-operative Development of the North Western Province.
J. P . D
AYASIRI PATHMAKUMARA JAYASEKERA,
Attorney-at-Law, Chief Minister and the Minister-in-charge
of the subject of Finance an Plannng Law and Peace,
Local Government and Manpower, Education and Cultural Affairs,
Land, Environment, Tourism, Investment Co-ordinate, Co-operative
and Food Supply and Distribution of North Western Province Provincial Council.
At Kurunegala,
21st  April 2015.
