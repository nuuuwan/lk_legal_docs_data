FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   OFFICE OF  VICE  CHAIRMAN   OF   THE
NAVITHANVELI    PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(1)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a Vice Chairman in Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be
a member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  Illankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi the recognized political party  to which Amaradasha
Ananda the former Vice Chairman belonged, was required by me under Section 65A(1) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to decide
upon the member of that Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha who belongs to such party who shall be declared elected as Vice
Chairman within a period of thirty (30) days  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has communicated his decision to nominate
Mr. Arumugam Pillaiyanthamby  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(1) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare Mr. Arumugam Pillaiyanthamby elected
as a Vice Chairman of the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Navithanveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
07th  January, 2015.

FILLING  OF  A  VACANCY  IN  THE   MEMBER OF   THE
NA VITHANVELI   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
UNDER  SECTION  65A(2)   OF  THE
LOCAL   AUTHORITIES   ELECTIONS  ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER  262)   AS  AMENDED  BY
LOCAL  AUTHORITIES  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS)  ACT,  No.  30  OF  2013
WHEREAS the office of a member  in Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha has fallen vacant by reason of being ceased to be a
member of the party ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the  recognized political party namely Ilankai Tamil Arasu Kadchi  to which the
former member, Mr. Amaradasha Ananda belonged was required by me under Section 65A(2) of the aforesaid Ordinance, to
nominate within a period of thirty (30) days a person eligible under the Ordinance for election as a member of that Navithanveli
Pradeshiya Sabha who  shall be declared elected as a member  ;
AND WHEREAS the Secretary of the aforesaid recognized political party has nominated Mr.Thiyagarasa Puwasanthan
being an eligible person under the aforesaid Ordinance  to fill such vacancy ;
NOW THEREFORE  I, Don Thilina Ishan Wickramarathna,  Returning Officer of  Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha
acting in terms of Section 65A(2) of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance as amended by filling of vacancies in Local
Authorities   (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 (Chapter 262)  do hereby declare  Mr. Thiyagarasa Puwasanthan elected
as a member of the Navithanveli Pradeshiya Sabha.
T
HILINA WICKRAMARA THNA,
Returning Officer,
Navithanveli  Pradeshiya Sabha.
District Elections Office,
Ampara,
07th  January, 2015.
