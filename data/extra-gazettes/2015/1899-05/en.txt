No. 1899/5 –  MONDAY, JANUARY  26, 2015 
EXTRAORDINARY 
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 7 OF THE LAND 
ACQUISITION ACT, (CHAPTER 460)    
My Reference No.: 3/2/6/4/1/27. 
Land Ministry’s Ref. No.: 4/3/12/2012/CEB/103. 
 
 
I, hereby give Notice, under Sub -section (1) of Section 7 
of the Land Acquisition Act, as Amended by the Land 
Acquisition (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1964, that the 
Government intends to acquire the following land which 
is required for the establishment of the Centres of Alarm 
under Upper Kotmale Hydro Power Project. 
 
Description of the Land to be acquired: 
 
Following Allotment in the extent of 0.0101 Hectares, 
mentioned in the Preliminary Plan Mu. Pi. Nu. 2964 
dated 04.08.2014 prepared by the Surveyor General, 
situated in the village of Halpola in Udapane Korale in 
Kotmale Divisional Secretary’s Division of Nuwaraeliya 
District. 
 
Lot No. 01 
 
Name of Land: Halpola Polwaththa; Division  468H - 
Halpola Details: For the establishement of a Centre of 
Alarm under Upper Kotmale Hydro Power Project; 
Claimants: S. B. Wijesundara and H. B. Wijesundara; 
Extent: 0.0101 Hectare. 
 
 
All persons interested in the aforesaid  land on which 
a servitude is to be acquired are hereby required to appear 
personally, or by Agents duly authorized in writing, 
before me at Divisional Secretariat Office of Kotmale on 
13th March, 2015 at 9.00 a. m. Further It is hereby 
requested to notify to me in writing (in duplicate) on or 
before the 06th day of March, 2013 the nature of their 
interests in the land, the particulars of their claims for 
compensation for the acquisition of the said land the 
amount of compensation and the details of the 
computation of such amount. 
 I. M. SENANAYAKA, 
 Divisional Secretary, 
 Kotmale. 
 
Divisional Secretariat Office, 
Kotmale, 
22nd January, 2015.
