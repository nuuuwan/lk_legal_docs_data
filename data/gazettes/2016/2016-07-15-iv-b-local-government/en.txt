IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 05th August, 2016 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 22nd July,
2016.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

NEGOMBO  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Filling Vacancies in Western Provincial Public Service
APPLICATIONS are called only from the permanent residents with due qualifications living in the Western province for
recruiting to the post of Sanitary Labourer already fallen vacant with the Negombo Municipal Council.
Completed applications prepared as per the specimen herein by the applicants, to be sent under the registered cover
to reach me addressing "Urban Commissioner, Municipal Council, Negombo" before 15.08.2016.
Designation Grade No. of V acancies Salary Scheme Educational Qualifications
Health Labourer 111 06 PL - 1 - 2016 -(Rs.  24,250-10x250- Should pass Grade 8 (Year 9)
10x270-10x300-12x330- Rs. 36,410)
Physical Qualifications. – Every applicant must be physically and mentally fit do discharge duties related to the
post in the Western Province.
Other : (i) Must be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
(ii) Should be a permanent resident of Western Province for continued 03 years in advance to the
deadline.
(iii) Should possess unblemished character.
(iv) All qualifications needed for the qualifying to the post must be fulfilled as at date mentioned on the
application calling notice/Gazette.
Age : Minimum limit - Should not be less than 18 years as at deadline of accepting application.
Maximum limit - Should not be more than 45 years.
Status of post.– Permanent and pensionable.
· Language proficiency related to be acquired within five years of appointment as per Public Administration
Circular No. 01/2014.
· Contribution to the Widow/Widower and Orphan Pension Fund must be paid.
Recruitment Procedure :-
01. Health Labourer :- Vacancies will be filled on order of skills recurred by applicants at the structured interview.
Other conditions exceptional to general conditions given in Service Procedure Code of Public Services Commission,
Western Provincial Council :-
The recruits to be bound by conditions of Establishment Code of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,
financial regulations/regulations of the Western Provincial Council and regulations / orders issued from time to time by the
Hon. Governor or Public Services Commission of Western Province and order imposed by the Head of Department.
Definitions exceptional to definitions given in given in the Service Procedure Code of Public Services Commission,
Western Provincial Council :-
Definition exceptional to definitions if any given in the Service Procedure Code of Public Services Commission,
Western Provincial Council to be dealt in consultation with Public Service Commission of Western Province/Hon. Governor
of the Western Province.
Municipal Commissioner,
Negombo.
