The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 19th February, 2016 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 05th
February, 2016.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.
W. A. A. G. F
ONSEKA,

SRI  JAYA WARDANAPURA  KOTTE  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
A Draft Copy for levying tax subject to the undeveloped lands for the year 2016
TO levy taxes in subject to the lands undeveloped for the year 2016 by the Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council under Se ction
247(d) of the Municipal Council Ordinance (Chapter 252) in the Penal Code of Sri Lanka.
Where any land situated within the jurisdiction of Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council, which is suitable to construct
a building or cultivate daily or permanent, it can be developed for any purpose on a reasonable expense according to the opinio n of this
Council,
(a) When no constructed building in that land ; or
(b) If the relevant ratio enforced by the Council by an accepted resolution and when it is less than, the ratio between the
amount of real submerged land and the whole amount of land ; or
(c) Unless that land used for a permanent otherwise daily cultivation,
The following tax has imposed from the capital value of the lands of such lands :
1. When houses can construct up to 2,000 sq. ft. in the lands below 06 perches otherwise from the capital value of the land
- 0.5%
2. When houses can construct up to 3,000 sq. ft. in the lands below 06 to 10 perches otherwise from the capital value of the
land - 1%.
3. When houses can construct above 10 perches otherwise from the cpaitla value of the land - 1.5%.
In any case it is not covered by the above 1, 2, 3 then 2% tax will be charged from the owner of that land from the capital value.
It is hereby notified to the public that the Sri Jayawardanapura Kotte Municipal Council has been enforced tax subject to the
undeveloped lands as mentioned above under the order of Council Decision No. 06:01 (with amendments) of the Municipal Council f or
the year 2016 and to the relevant amendments done herein after, held on 28th August 2015, 17th December 2015.
