OPEN COMPETITIVE EXAMINATION FOR THE RECRUITMENT TO THE POSTS OF INSTRUCTOR (TEXTILE) CLASS III
AND INSTRUCTOR (WOOD WORK) CLASS III IN PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION OF NORTH CENTRAL PROVINCE -
2016
THE applications are invited from the permanent residents within the North Central Province, those who have following qualifications for the
recruitment to the posts of Instructor (Textile) class III and Instructor (Wood work) class III in the Public Service of North Central Province.
The closing date of receiving application will be 28.03.2016.
02. Public Service Commission of North Central Province has the authority to determine the number s to be recruited in accordance with
the vacancies existed at the time of recruitment.
03. Qualifications.—
I. Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka
II. Should have an excellent moral character and possess physical and mental fitness.
III. Should not less than 18 years and not more than 35 years of age as at the closing date of apppication (The age limit is not
applicable to the applicants who are now in the permanent post in the Government/Provincial Public Service)
IV. Should be a permanent resident within the territory of North Central Province for a period of 03 years prior to closing date  of
application.
01. Educational Qualification and experiences.—
1. For the post of Instructor (Textile) class III
 Should have passed G . C. E. (O/L) examination not more than two sittings with six subjects including Sinhala/Tamil languages
and mathematics
and
 Should have possessed the higher course in an institutes of esthetic studies or three years final certificate of textile
or
 Should have two years or three years certificates issued from Department of textile.
or
 Should have passed the subject of textile with credit pass in the G . C. E. (O/L) examination and 03 years experience as a
weaver in a reputed Government institute.
II.  For the post of Instructor (Wood Work) Class III
 Should have passed G . C. E. (O/L ) Examination in six subjects with three Credit passes for Sinhala/Tamil languages,
Mathematics at not more than two sittings.
and
 Should have followed one year carpentary training course in a Mechanical Carpentary Training Institute of Industrial Development
Department and have obtained the final certificate with all subjects.
and
 Should have followed one year carpentary higher course and passed all subject and have the final certificate.

Rs. 13,120  – 10 x145 – 11x170 – 10x240 – 10x320 = Rs. 22,040
03. Examinaiton fee .— Every candidate should pay the examination fee to North Central Provincial Council. Examination fee is
Rs. 500.00. Such examination fee should be paid to any Divisional Secretariats within North Central Province and the Receipt, NC 100 which
is issued thus, should be posted in the box prepaid in the application. The examination fee should be paid as crediting to the General Deposit
Account of North Central Provincial Council. The examination fee paid so, will not be refunded on any reason. (Securing a photocopy of the
receipt obtained by paying money and a copy of the application would be useful)
04. The procedure for applying and relevant details  .—
A.  Application should be prepared in both sides of A4 size papers (21x29cm) according to the specimen application contained in this
announcement, when preparing the application No. 01 to 08 should be included in to the first page, No. 09 to 12 should be contained in the
second page whereas the rest should be included in the third page as it appears in the specimen application. Further relevant details should be
furnished by own hand writing . The applications which are in compliance with specimen application or with incomplete details will be rejected
without any prior notice.
B. Duly completed applications should be sent under registered post to reach “Secretary, Provincial Public Service Commission,
Secretariat Building, Anuradhapura” before 28.03.2016 and should be mentioned as “competitve examination for the recruitment of Instructor
(Textile) class III or Instructor (Wood Work) Class III” or the top left corner of the envelope enclosing the application. If the applicant bears
any post in Public/Provincial Public Service. He/She should send his/her application through the head of the Department. We should not be held
responsible to the applicaiton lost or misplaced in post or delayed.
05. Examination Centers .— The examination will be conducted in the selected centers near Anuradhapura town.
06. Certifying the signature of Applicant .— This attestation should be signed by a principal of a Government School/Justice of peace/
Commissioner of oaths/Attorney-at-law/Commissioned officer of the threee Forces or and officer of the public/provincial public Service
holding a permanent position drawing and annual combined salary not less than Rs. 2,40,360.00.
07. Method of Recruitment .— Written test will be conducted by the Secretary to the Public Service Commission of North Central
Province.  Syllabus is as following :–
I. For the post of Intructor (Textile)
 Textile Mathematics
 Handloom Technology
 Textile Design
 General Knowledge on Textile
II. For the post of Instructor (Wood Work)
 Sketch for wood work
 Fundamental mathematics for wood work
 Tools and timber for wood work industry
 Wood work technology
100 marks each will be given to written and practical test. To qualify at least 40% has to be scored in each test.
The applicants who have passed written test and scored highest marks will be invited to practical test Action will be taken to recruit those
who have scored highest markes in both written and practical test after an interview. The final decision on filling vacancies shall be Public
Service Commission of North Central Province.
08. Terms of engaged in Service .— The post is permanent and pensionable. Appointees will be subjected to a probation period of three
years and they are bound to serve in any part of North Central Province.
