IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 23rd August, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 08th
August, 2013.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

GAMPAHA  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL
Recruitment to the Post of Fireman in Western Provincial Public Service – 2013
APPLICATIONS are hereby invited from permanent residents of the Western Province with under mentioned qualifications for the po st
of fireman to the fire service in Gampaha Municipal Council in the Western Provincial Council.
01. Salary Scale.– Rs. 14,280 - 145 x 7 - 180x10 - 240 x 17 - Rs. 21,175 - (Rs.-1-2006A).
02. Age Limit.– Applicants should be not less than 19 years and not more than 25 years of age. In first time the age limit of the
applicants who have completed fireman course conducted by the National Apprentice Authority is extended up to 35.
03. Educational and Other Qualification :
(a) Applicant should have a minimum of six passes at the G. C. E. (O/L) Examination in not more than two attempts
including Sinhala/Tamil, Mathematics and Science subject and among them should have passed in five subjects in one
sitting.
(b) Applicants should have an excellent character with good health, and also :
(i) Should be minimum of 5 feet and 4 inches of height,
(ii) Should be minimum of 125 Lb (55.5Kg.) of weight,
(iii) Applicants should be unmarried,
(iv) Should be a permanent resident within the Western Province minimum of 3 years.
04. Recruitment System. – A written exam and placement test will be conducted in terms of Public Administration Circular
No. 15/90 according to the results of examination those selected will be call for an interview to certify the qualification later they will be
absorbed into the service. The recruitment task will be carried out by Gampaha Municipal Commissioner.
Recruitment Examination :–
(a) Applicant should appear for a one hour question paper to examine general knowledge level,
(b) A qualifying test will be conducted in due course to examine the physical qualifications.
05. Conditions applicable to the service –
(i) This post is permanent and pensionable.
(ii) Applicants will be treated according to the provisions of the Establishment Code, Financial Regulations of the Western
Provincial Council and other condition issued time to time by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission and
also the orders of the said Local Authority. The service of this post is limited only to the Western Province.
06. Service Conditions. – Selected applicants should follow a one year training course. Appointing authority has the power to
exclude the trainees without any compensation who do not satisfactory work condition and behavior or expected professional stan dard.
Rs. 3,000 will be paid as an allowance during the training period.
Those who complete the training satisfactorily will appointed placing in the salary scale of fireman in Provincial Public Service.
Those completing the training should forward to bond signature values of Rs. 10,000 at the beginning of the training stating that
they will serve in the Western Provincial Public Service for period of five years.
All trainees should appear for medical examination before commencing their training.
Those who receiving appointment as fireman it is compulsory to reside within the local authority area.
