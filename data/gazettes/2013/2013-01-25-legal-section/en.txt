IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and
times of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. sho uld be
prepared by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, the reby
enabling those interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and  complaints
made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of  15th February, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 01st
February 2013.
P. H. L. V . DE SILV A,
Acting  Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2013.

01-669
AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  SERETSE  KHAMA
FOUNDATION TRUST
I, Buddhika Pathirana, do hereby give notice under the Standing
Order No. 48 of the Standing Orders of the Parliament of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I Propose to introduce
to Parliament a Bill entitled, " An Act to Incorporate the Seretse
Khama Foundation Trust".
The aim of the bill to Incorporate said Seretse Khama Foundation
Trust which has the following general objects.
(a) to prepetuate the memory of Sir Secretse Khama, the first
President of Botswana and spread his message of peace and
unity in Sri Lanka;
(b) to develop bilateral relations between Botswana and Sri Lanka
by promoting cultural, social and economic exchanges between
the two countries, etc.
B
UDDHIKA PATHIRANA,
Member of Paliament,
Matara District.
01-723
AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  SIVMUNI SE VEHERA
BUDDHIST FOUNDATION
I, Siriyani Wijewickrema, do hereby give notice under Standing Order
No. 48 of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I Propose the introduce to
Parliament a Bill entitled "An act to incorporate the Sivmuni Se
Vehera Buddhist Foundation".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Foundation which
has the following general objects.:-
1. to maintain the activities of the "Sivmuni Se Vehera" Buddhist
Centre at No.1, 8 th Avenue, Navagampura, Ampara;
2. to shape up the society according to the Buddhist Vision to
ensure righteous and religious society; and
3. to assist and make  donations to elders, preshool children and
students for  their education.
etc.
S
IRIYANI WIJEWICKREMA,
Member of Parliament.
