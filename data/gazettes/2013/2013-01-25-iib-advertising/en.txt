IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 15th February, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 01st
February, 2013.
P . H. L. V . DE SILV A,
Acting Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
01st January, 2013.

(GOSL funded Projects)
Ministry of Transport
EXTENSION OF DOUBLE RAILWAY LINE FROM
KALUTARA SOUTH TO PAYAGALA SOUTH
THE Chairperson, Ministry Procurement Committee, Ministry of
Transport, 01, D. R. Wijewardena Mawatha, Colombo 10, invites
sealed bids from you for the work that has to be carried out for the
Extension of Double Railway Line from Kalutara South to Payagala
South Widening the Embankment at Kalutara Lagoon Area, Contract
No. SRS/F.7018 as described below.
Work shall be programmed to carry out Railway Line from
Kalutara South to Payagala South Widening the Embankment at
Kalutara Lagoon Area. The Construction period is 150 days.
2. Bidding will be conducted through National Competitive
Bidding Procedure.
3. To be eligible for contract award, the successful bidder shall
not have been blacklisted and shall meet the following requirements.
The Bidders shall be registered in the Institution for Construction
Training and Development (ICTAD) for Grade C3 or above and
specialty for Coastal.
4. Qualification requirements to qualify for contract award
include :
(a) Average annual volume of construction work performed in
last five years shall be at least Rs. 450 Million.
(b) Experience in the renovation/construction of at least one
Contract of a nature and complexity equivalent to the works
over the last 10 years as the main contractor.
(c) The minimum amount of credit facilities, after setting apart
for the other contractual commitments and exclusive of
any advance payments which may be made under the
Contract, shall be not less than Rs. 25 Million. This should
be exclusive for the Project.
(d) Technical staff at site to be —
1. One Civil Engineer - BSc Eng. NDT with 5 years
experience.
2. One Technical Officer - NDT, HNDE, NDES, or
equivalent with 5 years experience.
3. One Work Supervisor - NCT or equivalent with 3
years experience.
5. Interested bidders may obtain further information from
Superintendent of Railway Stores, Railway Stores Department, Sri
Lanka Railways, Olcott Mawatha, Colombo 10. Telephone Nos.:
2438078, 2436818 and inspect the bidding documents at the address
given below from 0900 hrs. to 1500 hrs.
