IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of  03rd May, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 19th  April,
2013.

03. Recruitment procedure.–  Selections will be made to the number of vacancies to be filled checking qualifications by a
structured interview according to the applications received from applicants who have completed qualifications. If applications more than
the number of vacancies will be received from qualified applicants Selections will be made to the relevant number of vacancies checking
qualifications by a structured interview on the order of merits according to the results of a written examination conducted by the authority
of appointment or an institution delegated power by the authority of appointment only for the posts from number 01 to 06.
04. Structured Interview .– A structured interview will be held for the posts from number 01 to 06. These posts are directly
connected to behavioral control of children that should be functioned very sensitively. Qualified applicants from the applicants faced for
the written examination will be called for the structured  interview. Only the applicants who score total marks of higher level determined
adequate by the authority of appointment will be called for the structured interview. If there are a sufficient number of appli cants who
complete above qualifications, a two-fold as the number of vacancies will be called for the structured examination. The marks o btained
by the applicant in the written examination will not be given to the Board of structured interview. Selections will be made according to the
marks obtained by the examination and the marks given by asking specific questions on the capability of delivering duties relevant for this
post by a Board of expert not less than three persons with experience and profound knowledge on children’s rights, psychologica l
conditions and protection of children appointed by the authority of appointment.
05. Interview .– An interview will be held for the posts from number 07 to number 11. Selections will be made according to the
marks obtained by the examination and the marks given by an interview board comprising of not less than three persons appointed by the
authority of appointment asking specific questions from the applicant on his ability of delivering duties relevant for the post .
06. The examination fee is Rs. 350/-. The receipt obtained on payment of this examination fee as crediting to Provincial Revenue
Head 20-03-02-99 to the nearest Divisional Secretariat should be annexed and forwarded with the application form. Neither recei pts
obtained by paying the fee to other institutions nor money orders other than the receipts obtained by paying the fee in this me thod will

examination or remove the application due to whatever reason and the applicants who are revealed to be ineligible to sit for th e
