IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 10th May, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 26th  April,
2013.
P. H. L. V . DE SILV A,

PUJAPITIYA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
Post - Vacancies in the Primary Office Workers service
(unskilled) in the Local Government Institutions of the
Central Province Public Serivces
APPLICATIONS are called for recruitment of the following posts
in the Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha, from qualified persons, residing
within the administrative limits of Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
Post Number of Vacancies
1. Electrician 01
2. Work/Field Labourers 02
3. Library Attendants 03
4. Sanitary Labourers 04
5. Crematorium Operator 01
6. Crematorium Helper 01
These posts are permanent and pensionable. Contribution should
be made to Widow's/Widower's and Orphan's Pension Scheme. These
appointments will be subjected to a period of probation for 03
years.
02. Salary Scale.– Rs. 11,730 -10x120 - 10x130 -10x145 -12x160
- Rs. 17,600.
03. General Qualifications :
(i) All applicants should be citizens of Sri Lanka by descent
or by registration.
(ii) Age limit is not less than 18 years and not more than 45
years at the closing date of the application, should be
established. (Upper age limit is not applicable those who
are presently employed in the Central Province Local
Government Institutions).
(iii) Should establish a minimum period of 03 years residency
within the Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
(iv) All applicants should possess excellent moral character
and best physical health.
04. Educational Qualifications :
(i) Should have passed General Certificate of Education
(Ordinary Level) or National Certificate of General
Education (Ordinary Level) minimum 6 subjects
including Sinhala or Tamil Language, in not more than
two sittings.
(ii) Only the Sanitary Labourers completing the prescribed
syllabus for Grade 8 (Year 9) is sufficient.
05. Competencies/Efficiencies for Electricians :
(a) Should be obtained the certificate Level 02 (NVQ 2) or
Level 03 (NVQ 3) from the National V ocational
Qualification, related to the Electrician profession ; or
(b) Should be followed a course not less than one year and
obtained the certificate from the Department of Technical
Education and Training (DTET) relating the Electrician
profession ; or
(c) Should have certificate following a course related to the
Electrician profession not less than one year in the National
Youth Services Council (NYSC) ; or
(d) Should have a certificate following a course related to the
Electrician profession not less than one year in the
V ocational Training Authority (VTA) ; or
(e) Should have a certificate following a course related to the
Electrician profession not less than one year in the National
Apprentice Institute Training Authority (NAITA) ; or
(f) Should have a certificate from an institution registered
under Tertiary and V ocational Education Commission,
following a course not less than one year in the related
field.
(g) Having an A Grade driving license is considered as an
additional qualification.
06. Experience for Electricians and Crematorium Operators.– A
certificate of two years experience to the related post should be
forwarded.
07. Method of Recruitments :
(i) All applications possess minimum qualifications required
for the recruitments will be called for the interview.
(ii) The suitable will be selected through the recommendation
of the Board of Interview.
(iii) Preference will be given to those who serve under
temporary/casual/substitute/contract basis in the
Pujapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha.
08. Applications prepared according to the specimen given below
should be sent under Registered cover, to the "Secretary, Pujapitiya
Pradeshiya Sabha, Pujapitiya, on or before 22nd May, 2013.
The post applied for should clearly mentioned in the left hand
corner of the envelope. The copies of the following documents should
be attached with the application.
1. Certificate of Birth ;
2. Certificates of Educational Qualifications ;
