IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 08th March, 2013 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 22nd
February, 2013.
P. H. L. V . DE SIL V A,
Acting  Government Printer.
Department of Government Printing,
Colombo 08,
01st January, 2013

WESTERN  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification under Section 2(2) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946
BY virtue of powers vested upon me under Section 2(2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 read with Section 2 of the
Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 I, Prasanna Ranatunga, The Chief Minister and the Minister of
Finance and Planning, Law and Order, Land, Education, Local Government and Provincial Administration , Power and Energy, Manpower
and Employment, Economic Promotion and of the Western Province Provincial Council hereby announce that I have approved the
proposal passed by the Colombo Municipal Council on 2012.10.25 in terms of the provisions of 2(1) of the Entertainment Tax
Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 to impose and charge an Entertainment Tax of 10% on entrance fee for entertainment activities stated i n the
Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended by Entertainment Tax (Amendment) Act No. 37 of 1984.

GOVERNOR’S  SECRETARIAT - SABARAGAUWA  PROVINCE
All Island Service Vacancies in the Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council
APPLICATIONS are hereby invited from the Special Grade Officers of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service to fill the post of
Governor’s Secretary, Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council.
If there are no applications from the Special Grade Officers, applications from the first grade officers will be considered.
Therefore officers of the first grade are encouraged to apply.
Recruitment .– Applications receive regarding this notice will be submitted to the Honourable Governor. An eligible officer to
the post of Governor’s Secretary will be selected on the sole discretion of the Honourable Governor.
Final decision regarding the filing of vacancy of the Governor’s Secretary will be taken by the Honourable Governor.
Submitting the applications.–  Self prepared applications including the bio-data should be sent under registered post reach the,
Governor Secretary, Sabaragamuwa Provincial Governor’s Secretariat, Provincial Council Complex, New Town, Ratnapura, before
11.03.2013.
We have no liability whatsoever for any loss of applications in the mail.
B
ANDULA A. NAGASINGHE,
Hon. Governor’s Secretary,
Governor Secretariat,
Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
New Town,
Ratnapura.
