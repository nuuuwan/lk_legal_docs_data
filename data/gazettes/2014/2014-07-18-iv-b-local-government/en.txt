IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 08th  August, 2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 25th July,
2014.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

AKURANA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Vacancies in Central Provincial Government Service
APPLICATIONS are hereby called for recruitment to the following vacant posts existing in Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha, from the
permanent residents of the administrative area of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha.
01. Applicants should possess the following general qualifications in order to apply for the vacant posts existing in Akurana
Pradeshiya Sabha.
1.2 General Qualifications :
(a) The applicant should be a citizen of Sri Lanka and a permanent resident of Central Province. The applicant should
establish 03 years permanent residence within the administrative limit of Akurana Pradeshiya Sabha as at the closing
date of applications.
(b) The applicant should be not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years as at the closing date of applications. The
maximum age does not apply to those who are presently employed in a Local Government Institution.
(c) The applicant should possess an excellent character and a good physical and mental health. The successful applicant
should appear for a medical examination conducted by a qualified doctor within a period of one month after accepting
the appointment and if the applicant is found to be unfit appointment will be cancelled.
(d) The applicant should not have been convicted or punished by a Court of Law under the Penal Code.
(e) The applicant should not be a person who has been dismissed from the government service or Local Government
Service.

(v) Letters of experience ;
(vi) A copy of the driving license of those who apply for the post of heavy machine operator.
