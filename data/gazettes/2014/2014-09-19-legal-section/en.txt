IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-V acancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 10th October,  2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 26th
September, 2014.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter
shall be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order , by-law,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette .”

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  RAJITHA
SENARATHNA  FOUNDATION
I, Uduwatuwage Janath Priya Thilanga Sumathipala do hereby give
notice under standing Order No. 48 (1) of the Parliament of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I Propose to introduce
in Parliament a Bill entitled" and Act to incorporate the Rajitha
Senarathna Foundation :-
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Rajitha Senarathna
Foundation which has the following general objects :-
1. Establish the Kaluthara District resources Center to
decentralize urban concentrated resources to the rural level,
2. Identify the needs, issues, resources and potential market in
the Kaluthara District and build a small scale industrial
structure based on that, with the participation of the youth
of the area.
3. Initiate integrated economic development programme in the
Kalutara District with the collaboration of governmental,
non governmental organizations and the Private Sector, etc.
U
DUWATUWAGE JANATH PRIYA
THILANGA SUMATHIPALA,
Member of Parliament,
Colombo District.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  "INSTITUTION  OF
BUILDING  SERVICES  ENGINEERING  AND
TECHNOLOGY  SRI L ANKA"
I, Thilanga Sumathipala, do hereby gives notice under Standing order
No. 48 (1) of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of
Sri Lanka, that I proposes to introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled
"An Act to incorporate the Institution of Building Services Engineering
and Technology Sri Lanka".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Institution which has
the following general Objects :
1. To foster and promote Building Services Engineering and
Technology as a Profession in Sri Lanka safeguard the
professional status, interests, rights powers welfare and
privileges of Building Services Engineers and  technologists,
establish a path for the professional growth of members through
continuous Professional Development Programmes, introduce
general standards of Professional Conduct and Practice for
Building Services Engineers and Technologists and ensure the
maintenance of such standards in their Professional activities,
and promote the general advancement of Building Services as
Science, and as a Profession;
2. to create a forum locally for Building Services Engineers and
Technologists to express their opinion, share knowledge,
improve presentation skills and to promote fellowship and
facilitate the exchange of information and ideas on subjects of
Building Services, amongst the members of the Institution,
liaise with Institutions/Associations of Building Services
Engineers, and Technologists in other countries and discuss
with Institution, Associations, Manufacturers, Contractors and
other persons of engages on Building Services Engineering and
Technology on matters of common interest and to affiliate
with the Chartered Institution of Building Services Engineers
(CIBSE), UK and any other professional body in the world ;
and
3. to hold and to conduct, supervice and control the admission,
professional education and training of persons desiring to
quality as Professional Building Services Engineers and
Technologists, prescribe or approve courses of study for
qualifying examination for membership of the Institutions,
prescribe the qualifications for membership of the Institution
and set guidelines for those desiring to qualify as Building
Services Engineers and Technologists, liaise with Universities/
Institutions to conduct courses/programmes on "Building
Services Engineering and Technology" Awarding the
Membership and the status and maintaining a register of
Chartered Building Services Engineers including a Register of
disciplines of the members, and conduct examinations to qualify
personnel as building services professionals.
etc.
T
HILANGA SUMATHIPALA,
 MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT.
