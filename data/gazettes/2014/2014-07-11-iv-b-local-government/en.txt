The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 01st August, 2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 18th July,
2014.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

Posts – Vacant
HOMAGAMA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
APPLICATIONS are invited from permanent residents of the Western Province who have fulfilled the following qualifications to fi ll in
the vacant posts shown in the schedule below, in the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha of Provincial Public Service.
General Conditions of Recruitment :
1. Applicant should be a Sri Lankan citizen.
2. Applicant should establish a permanent residence of 3 years immediately prior to the closing date of applications within
the jurisdiction of the Western Province by producing an extract of a voters’ register or a certificate given by the
Divisional Secretary of the area to that effect.
3. Applicant should not be less than 18 years and be not more than 45 years as at the closing date of the applications.
However, the upper age limit is not applicable to those applicants who are permanently employed in the Public or
Provincial Public Service.
4. Applicant should not have been convicted by a Court of Law under the Penal Code.
5. Applicant should possess an excellent moral character and be in best physical and mental health.
6. Applicant should have compulsorily accomplished all the required qualifications as at the closing date of the applications.
7. Applicant is also obligated to follow the Establishments Code and Financial Regulations and to work according to the
rules, regulations and orders of the Western Provincial Council while performing the duties.
Application Procedure :
1. Applications self-prepared by the applicants on A4 papers using both sides as per the specimen application given below
shall be sent under registered cover to the address named “Secretary - Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha, Homagama” on or
before 28.07.2014. Applications received after the due date will be rejected.
2. The post applied should be clearly stated in full at the top left hand corner of the envelope in which the application is
enclosed.
3. Application should accompany photocopies of the following certificates :
(i) Birth Certificate ;
(ii) Educational Certificates ;
(iii) Certificate of the Divisional Secretary of the area to confirm residence ;
(iv) Two recently obtained character certificates (One of these should be from the Grama Niladhari of the area) ;
(v) Certificates related to service experience.

Method of Recruitment :
1. Selections for these posts will be made upon a formal interview from the applicants who satisfy the requirements of the
Posts advertised.
2. Only those applicants who have satisfied the requirements given for each Post will be called for the interview.
3. Special consideration will be given to those who are already employed at the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha at projects
level and to those applicants who are permanent residents of the Homagama Pradeshiya Sabha Administrative Area.
4. Those applicants who have applied for the Post of Driver are required to pass a driving competency test to be held before
a board consisting of an examiner from the Department of Motor Traffic and an officer from the Traffic Police in addition
to the formal interview. Ability of the applicants to drive tractors will be specially examined during this test. Those
applicants who have applied for the Post of Health Supervisor are required to appear for a written test also.
Following subjects will be tested in the written test :
(i) General Intelligence - 1 1/2 hours question paper
(ii) General Knowledge - 1 hour question paper
Those who score highest marks at the written test only will be called for the interview.
5. Incomplete applications or applications without photocopies of relevant certificates will be rejected.
