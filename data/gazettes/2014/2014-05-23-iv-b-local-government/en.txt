IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 13th June, 2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 30th May,
2014.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, notification or other matter shall
be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

02. Qualification required.– Every applicant should  :
(i) Not be less than 18 years and not more than 45 years on the closing date of applications.
(ii) Be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
(iii) Be a permanent resident in Southern Province for the three years immediately preceding the closing date of applications.
(iv) Be of excellent moral character and physically sound.
(v) Not be convicted under Panel Code in a Court of Law.
(vi) Special consideration will be given to employees who serve at present on permanent/causal/temporary/Substitute/
contract basis in Akmeemana Pradeshiya Sabha.
(vii) Every applicant should have the qualification corresponding to the relevant post.
(viii) The applicant should not be a person dismissed from the Public/Local Government/Co-operation Service.
03. Terms of engagement :
(i) The post is permanent and pensionable.
(ii) You should contribute to the Widows’ and Orphans’/Widowers and Orphans’ Pension Scheme.
(iii) The ap pointment will be on probation for a period of three years.
(iv) In addition to these terms of engagement every employee is liable to comply with regulation of the Establishment Code,
Financial Regulations, orders of the Hon. Governor of the Southern Province, Circulars, Regulations and orders issued
from time to time by the Central Government or Southern Provincial Public Service Commission.
04. Method of recruitment :
(i) Appointments will be made by calling applicants for an interview to check qualifications.
(ii) Applicant should attached to their applications the coppies of the following certificates and produce originals at the
interview.
(i) The National Identify Card.
(ii) The certificate of registration of births.
(iii) Educational Certificates.
(iv) A certificate obtained from Grama Niladhari to certify the permanent residential place counter signed by the
Divisional Secretary.
(v) Two character certificates recently obtained.
(vi) Certificate of vocational quailfication or experience (if any).
