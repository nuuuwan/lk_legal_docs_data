IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 25th April, 2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 11th April,
2014.

be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order, by-l aw,

Posts – Vacant
KANDY  FOUR  GRA VETS  AND GANGAWATA
KORALE   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
IN order to recruit to the vacant posts in Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, applications are hereby invited
from the qualified persons who possess the following qualifications.
Electrician (Street Lamps In Charger) - Class III (Primary - Semi-
skilled).
02. Salary Scale. – As per Public administration Circular
6/2006(IV), PL 1-2006-A.
Rs. 12,210 -18x 130 -Rs. 144,550  (Efficiency Bar Examination
before the 4th salary step).
03. General conditions of Recruitment :
3.1 All applicants should be citizens of Sri Lanka.
3.2 Applicants should be permanent residents of Kandy Four
Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha for a
period of 03 years immediately prior to the closing date
of application.
Applications shall be considered from adjoining Local
Government Institutions when sufficient applications
were not received to fill the vacancies.
3.3 The applicants’ age should not less than 18 years and not
more than 45 years. This age limit will not be applicable
for those who are already employed in Government/
Provincial Public Services.
3.4 Should be of excellent moral character and physically
sound to serve in any part of the Island.
3.5 Should not have been penalized by a Court of Law or
named unfit list in Government or Provincial Council
Service.
04. Qualifications for the Recruitment :
4.1 Educational Qualifications.– Should have passed with
minimum two credit passes in General Certificate of
Education (Ordinary Level) or National Certificate of
General Education (Ordinary Level) Minimum 6 subjects
including Sinhala or Tamil Language, in not more than two
sittings.
4.2 Professional Competencies/Efficiencies.– Should have the
Competencies/Efficiencies mentioned below related to the
post of Electricians.
4.2.1 Should be obtained the certificate Level 02 (NVQ
2) or Level 03 (NVQ 3) from the National V ocational
Qualification, related to the Electrician profession ;
or
4.2.2 Should be followed a course not less than one year
and obtained the certificate from the Department
of Technical Education and Training (DTET),
relating the Electrician profession ;
or
4.2.3 Should have a certificate by following a course
related to the Electrician profession, not less than
one year in the National Youth Services Council
(NYSC) ;
or
4.2.4 Should have a certificate by following a course
related to the Electrician profession, not less than
one year in the V ocational Training Authority
(VTA) ;
or
4.2.5 Should have a certificate following one year course
related to the Electrician profession in the National
Apprentice Institute Training Authority
(NAITA) ;
 or
4.2.6 Should have a certificate from an institute registered
under Tertiary and V ocational Education
Commission, following a course not less than one
year in the related field.
4.3 Experience.– Should possess at least two years experience
in the field of Electrician or Street Lamps in-charger
(Training period should be count minimum two years).
05. Terms of Engagement :
5.1 The posts are permanent and pensionable.
5.2 Contributions should be made to Widow’s/Widower’s
and Orphan’s Pension Scheme.
5.3 The appointees are subjected to serve a period of
probation for three years. Work, conduct and attendance
should be in a satisfactory manner to consider for making
appointments as permanent.
5.4 In addition to the medium language under which
recruitment has done, the appointees should obtain
proficiency in other official language. Relating this a pass
should be obtained within 05 years, in the test conducted
by the Official Language Department as per Circular No.
07/2007, dated 28.05.2007.

5.5 In addition to the terms and conditions or recruitment

5.6 The general conditions and other rules of Chapter II of

5.7 If the need is arise, residing within the Mahanuwara and
Kadawath Sathara Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha
administrative limits, may be laid.
06. Efficiency Bar.– Prior to attain the 4th step to Salary Scale
(Rs. 12,600) related to the recruitment, the Efficiency Bar Hurdle
has to be confronted. The Efficiency Bar Examination should be
succeeded within 03 years of the date of first appointment. It is
compulsroy to pass the Efficiency Bar Examination for the
permanency in the appointment.
07. Method of Recruitment. – All applicants, along those who
have minimum qualifications for the recruitment shall be called for a
structured interview. Only the qualified applicants will be called for
the interview and the date of interview will be notified in future. The
following documents, along the photo copies of them should be
produced at the interview :
(i) Original copy of Birth Certificate,
(ii) Original copies of Educational Qualifications,
(iii) School Leaving Certificate,
(iv) Recently issued Grama Niladhari Certificate,
(v) Recently issued two character Certificates,
(vi) Certificates relating professional competencies,
(vii) Service and experience certificates.
7.2 The most qualified applicants who possess highest
qualifications shall be selected for recruitment, among
those who faced the interview.
08. Forwarding Applications :
8.1 Appilcant can apply only for one post.
8.2 Applications should be prepared on an A4 sized paper,
using both sides, as per specimen of this notification and
sent under registered post to reach the “Secretary, Kandy
Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha,
Ampitiya” on or before 17.04.2014.
8.3 “Recruitment to the Post of Electrician” should be written
on the top left hand corner of the envelope, enclsoing
applications.
8.4 Incomplete and late applications will be rejected.
8.5 Applicants’ signatures should be attested by one of a
Principal of a Government School, Justice of the Peace,
Commissioner of Oaths, Notary Public, Commissioned
Officer of Armed Forces, Higher Officer of the Police
Service or an officer who draws Rs. 237,060 annual salary
in Government Service.
8.6 Officers already serving in the Government should forward
their application through their Heads of Institutions.
09. Number of recruits and the effective date of appointment
shall be confirmed by the Secretary to the Kandy Four Gravets and
Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha. The Secretary to the Council,
he himself reserves all such powers to delay or cancel or amend this
notification, after calling applications or during such process or come
to any final decision relating this matter.

IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  PUBLICATION   OF  GAZETTE
THE Weekly issue of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  is normally published on
Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public Holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately preceding
the Friday. Thus the last date specified for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also varies depending
on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.
The Schedule below shows the date of publication and the latest time by which notices should be received
for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times specified below will not be
published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return if publication
is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those desirous of ensuring
the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient time is allowed for postal
transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer does not accept payments of subscription for the Government Gazette.
Note.—Payments for inserting Notices in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
will be received by the Government Printer.
