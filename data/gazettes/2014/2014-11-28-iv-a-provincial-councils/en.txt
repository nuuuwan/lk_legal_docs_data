IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of  19th December, 2014 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
05th December, 2014.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter
shall be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order , by-law,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Municipal Council Orders
BY virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 2 of Local Government (Incidental Provisions) Act No. 12 of 1989 to be read with
Section 289 chapter 252 of Municipal Council Ordinance, I Dayasiri Jayasekara, the Chief Minister of the  North Western Provinc e and
the Minister in charge of the subject of Local Government do hereby impose the orders relating to payment of communication allowances
for the Members of Municipal councils.
ATTORNEY AT LAW J.P. DAY ASIRI PADMAKUMARA JAYASEKARA.
Chief Minister of the North Western Province and
 the Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Local Government and Provincial Administration, Human Resources,
Education and Cultural Affairs, Lands Environment and
 Food Supply and Distribution.
Kurunegala,
20th of October, 2014.
ORDERS
1. These orders are cited as the orders relating to the disbursement of communication allowances for the Members of Municipal
Councils.
2. Communication allowance could be paid for the Members of the Municipal Council subject to a maximum of Rs. 3,000 on
adoption of a formal resolution at the General Meeting.
3. These allowances shall be paid from the Council Fund and provisions should have been allocated by annual budget.
4. (a)   Members of Municipal Council shall not be entitled  for communication allowances at the following instances.
(i) Being absent at General Meeting and Committee meetings conducted during the month relevant to the payment of
allowances without obtaining the approval from the Council.
(ii) Being remanded of imprisoned on conviction of guilty for any criminal offence.
(iii) Suspension of duties of the Members of any Municipal Council in terms of the Statute of Local Government
Authorities (Powers of administration and supervision) of the North Western Province No. 01 of 1991 of the
North Western Provincial Council or Section 277 of Municipal Council Ordinance.
Provided that in case the period of remand custody, imprisonment on leave or suspension of service is a part of a
month these allowances may be paid for the rest of the month by proportionally calculation.
(b) Whatsoever is mentioned in Sub Paragraph (1) of Paragraph (a) of this order a Members' allowances shall not be deprived
in case a Member is absent at a council meeting on a formal approval to perform a official duty.
