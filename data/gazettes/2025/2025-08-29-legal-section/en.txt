IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION  is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of pub lication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.   
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of  19th September, 2025 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 04th September,

matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

PARLIAMENT
An Act  to Incorporate  the International Institute for Skills Development and Training
I, J. C. Alawathuwala, do hereby give notice under No. 53(1) of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka , that I propose to introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled “An Act to incorporate the International 
Institute for Skills Development and Training.”
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Institute which has the following General objects :-
(a) To facilitate the Sri Lankan community to follow in this country itself those courses of study that could be 
conducted in Sri Lanka from among those conducted by Higher Education Institutes overseas;
(b) To conduct vocational training under a proper procedure to suit the local and overseas job markets; and
(c) To conduct entrepreneurship training programme for the purpose of empowering the Sri Lankan youths as 
successful entrepreneurs.
etc.
  J. C. Alawathuwala,
  Member of Parliament,
  Kurunegala District.

PARLIAMENT
An Act  To Incorporate  The  Nethra  Pahasarana  Foundation
I, Hennayake Mudiyanselage Dinindu Saman Kumara, do hereby give notice under Standing Order No. 53 (1) of the Parliament 
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I propose to introduce in Parliament a Bill titled "An Act to Incorporate 
the Nethra Pahasarana Foundation."
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Nethra Pahasarana Foundation which has the following general objectives :-
1. To start, promote and maintain Computer Science and V ocational Training Courses.
2. Appreciation of Literature and providing opportunity for Amateur Creators.
3. Promote and encourage Sports and engaging in Social Public Welfare Activities.
  H. M. Dinindu  Saman,
  Member of Parliament,
  Badulla District.
