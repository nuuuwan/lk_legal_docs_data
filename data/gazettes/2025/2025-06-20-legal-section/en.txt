Attention  is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of pub lication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.

An  AcT   To  IncoRPoRATe  SuRAkImu  LAnkA
 
HARSHANA Rajakaruna, I hereby give notice that under standing order No. 53 (1) of the Parliament of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka I intend to present a Bill entitled An Act for the Incorporation of Surakimu Lanka Organization 
in Parliament.
The objectives of the following draft are to incorporate a single organization with the following common objectives.
I kindly request that the following draft be published in the Government Gazette in all three Languages.
1. Conducting educational leadership programs for Sports arts and arts students teachers youth and children in a religious 
environment to create a country form abuse and neglect.
2. Giving honorary titles to people who perform religious and Social services.
3. Conducting medical clinics and blood donation camps for people suffering from economic hardships, regardless of 
religion, race or caste.
4. To protect the country from extremist conspirators to destroy my country, my nation and the Sam Buddha Sasana, 
and to create a good virtuous, creative and righteous society by protecting the Lankan heritage, and to develop the 
Village and country by protecting the environment and making the lives of all people happy and harmonious.
And so on.
