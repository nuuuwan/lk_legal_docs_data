PARLIAmenT
A bill to established an Sovereignty enforcement council
Chanaka Madugoda, I hereby give notice that I intend to introduce a Bill in Parliament, entitled "A Bill to establish an 
Sovereignty Enforcement Council under standing order No. 53 (1) of the Parliament of the Domocratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka.
The objectives of Bill are to established a foundation with the following general objectives.
This institution operates with the primary objective of enforcing the sovereignty of the citizens of the Democratic Socialist 
of Sri Lanka through.
1. Protecting democracy.
2. Monitoring and advocating for free and fair elections.
3. Standing up for human rights.
4. Enhancing civic political literacy.
5. Providing legal awareness.
6. Advocating for citizen's rights.
7. Acting against injustices in the enforcement of the law.
8. Opposing corruption and misconduct in public service.
9. Safeguarding public sovereignty.
10. Encouraging greater citizen political participation.
 Chanaka Madugoda,
 Parliament Member of Galle District.
