ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 23rd June, 2017 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 09th June,
2017.

KEGALLE   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Calling for returns by virtue of powers under Section 139 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987
I, Secretary to the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha, by virtue of powers vested in me under Sub-section (3) of Section 9 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987, do hereby notify to the public that the owner and occupier of any house, building, land
or tenement is required to furnish returns of the rent or annual value or extent thereof as per the format given in order to enable
the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha to assess the annual value of such house, building, land or tenement liable to be rated or the
extent of any cultivated land for the purposes of the acreage tax within the limits of the Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha by virtue
of powers vested in Pradeshiya Sabha under Section 139 of the said Act.
Further, I notify that every person who fails or neglects to furnish returns on or before the date specified above or
knowingly makes it false or incorrect return shall be guilty of an offence as per Sub-section (3) of Section 139 of the
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 and shall be liable on conviction by court to a fine set out in the said Act.
S. A. S. M. W
ICKRAMASOORIYA,
Secretary,
Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha.

Pradeshiya Sabha on the 5th day in the month of April, 2016 under decision No. 4-39 that returns relating to every property
situated in the limits of Kegalle Pradeshiya Sabha should be obtained by virtue of powers vested in me under Section 139 of
Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 to be read with Section 9.3 of the said Act.
02. Accordingly, I hereby order to that the specimen attached to this notification should be completed with two
copies and delivered to reach the following address on or before 12.00 noon on ..............20.... by registered post or by hand
along with following documents :
1. Two certified copies of the deed of the land.
2. The original and a certified photocopy of folios of the land issued by the Additional District Register for a
period of last 30 years.
3. Two certified copies of the surveyor plan of the land (Two photocopies obtained as per the scale of the
original plan).
4. If any building is standing on the land, two copies of the plan of the building drawn as per the scale (In case
of a building constructed by obtained a construction license under the plans and building orders made
under Housing and Town Development Ordinance or Urban Development Authority Ordinance, two certified
copies obtained as per the scale of the approved plan).
5. Two certified copies of title reports prepared substantially as per the specimen attached to this notification
and certified by a Notary.
6. If any machinery is installed on the land or inside the building, two copies of a statement specifying the
details of each machine, date of purchase and value and two copies of receipts.
7. In case the above statements is made by any other person on bealf of the owner, two certified copies of the
registered letter of attorney of which power of attorney has been granted to the said person, should be
submitted along with two affidavits which ensure that the said letter of attorney is valid up to date and the
said attorney is still living.
03. Please read the instruction before completing the attached form.

hereto on or before the date specified above or who provides wrong or erroneous periodical return deliberately shall be a
guilty of an offence under Sub-section (3) of Section 139 of Pradeshiay Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987. it is further notified that
action will be taken to charge at the Magistrate's Court against any person who is guilty of an offence under the said Sub-
section and that if found guilty by the Magistrate's Court, he/she shall be liable to pay a fine not exceeding Rupees One
Thousand.

IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  PUBLICATION   OF  GAZETTE
THE Weekly issue of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  is normally published on
Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public Holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately preceding
the Friday. Thus the last date specified for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also varies depending
on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.
The Schedule below shows the date of publication and the latest time by which notices should be received
for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times specified below will not be
published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return if publication
is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those desirous of ensuring
the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient time is allowed for postal
transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer does not accept payments of subscription for the Government Gazette.
Note.—Payments for inserting N otices in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
will be received by the Government Printer.
