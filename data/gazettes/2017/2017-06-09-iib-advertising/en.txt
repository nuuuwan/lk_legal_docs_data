IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notiﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertain ing to notiﬁ cation 
will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. 
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 30th June, 2017 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 16th June, 2017.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of  2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satis ﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or 
other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”
 GANGANI LIYANAGE,
 Government Printer (Acting).

SRI  LANKA  RAILWAYS
Request for expressions of Interest
for Investigation, preliminary design & 
preparation of BOQ, Cost estimate and bidding 
document for construction of Railway double 
Line from Polgahawela to Kurunegala
THE Sri Lanka Railways intends to prepare the bidding 
document for construction of double line from Polgahawela 
to Kurunegala which will be approximately 22 kilometres 
in length. The proposed double line is travelling along with 
existing Polgahawela - Kurunegala Railway line. There 
are one crossing station and three substation in between 
Polgahawela and Kurunegala main stations. 08 Nos. 
bridges and 79 Nos. culverts are to be constructed parallel 
to existing bridges and culverts. There are 19 Nos. Level 
crossing protection systems are to be provided for all level 
Crossings. The Consultant should provide the Investigation 
& preliminary design details, BOQ, cost estimate and the 
bidding document to the Sri Lanka Railways.
Double tracking from Polgahawela to Kurunegala will 
help to enhance traf ﬁ c handling capacity of Northern & 
Eastern lines. Effective Railway lines would encourage 
road users to actively use railway; which is expected to 
see an overall reduction in fuel usage, less emission of 
toxic gases polluting the environment, saving of time from 
trafﬁ c jams, noise pollution etc.
The ministry Consultancy Procurement Committee, 
Ministry of Transport & Civil Aviation intends to invite 
Expressions of Interest (EOIs) from eligible consultants 
(ﬁ rms) that are willing to undertake this assignment. 
EOI must include requested information as speci ﬁ ed in 
the ‘‘EOI Information Form’’ along with documentry 
evidence in support of Core business and years in business, 
Technical, Financial, Business and Administrative    
strength / standing, qualiﬁ cations in the ﬁ eld of assignment, 
Experience in carrying out assignments of a similar nature 
and complexity, Technical and managerial organization 
of the ﬁ rm, Technical capabilities and appropriate skills 
of key staff, Available equipment of the organization and 
other relevant information.
The ‘‘Description of the Project’’ and ‘‘EOI Information 
Form’’ documents are now available with Superindent 
of Railway Stores, Railway Stores Department, P.O.Box 
1347, Olcott Mawatha, Colombo - 10, Sri Lanka for 
further information. Interested parties may download these 
documents from the website : www.transport.gov.lk and 
www.railway.gov.lk.
Consultants (ﬁ rms) will be selected in accordance with 
the procedures set out in the Government Procurement 
Guidelines : Selection & Employment of Consultants. All 
interested parties are informed that all documentations 
shall be in English Language.
Expressions of Interest including all requested 
information must be hand delivered to the address; The 
Secretary, C/o Chief Accountant’s of ﬁ ce, Ministry of 
Transport and Civil Aviation, Seventh Floor, Stage 11, 
‘Sethsiripaya’ Battaramulla on or before 19th June, 2017.
Envelope Containing the expressions of Interest 
should be, marked ‘‘EOI for investigaion, preliminary 
design and preparation of BOQ, Cost estimate and the 
bidding document for construction of Railway double 
line from Polgahawela to Kurunegala’’.

 Ministry Consultancy Procurement Committee.
