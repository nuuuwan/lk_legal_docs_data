IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 17th November, 2017 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 2nd November, 2017.
 Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
 “Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, noti ﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other 
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

TO  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC  SERVICE - 2017

qualiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in this notiﬁ cation to ﬁ ll the vacancies in Technological Service (Civil/ Draughtsman) Grade III and 
Training Grade of Central Provincial Public Service.
01. General conditions :
1.1 Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
1.2 Shall be of excellent character.
1.3 Should possess a continues permanent residence for a period of not less than 03 years within Central Province 
by the closing date of application  (permanent residence of spouse will be considered when the applicant 
having married a person holding more than 03 years of permanent residence within Central Province).
1.4 Shall be not less than 18 years and not more than 30 years of age on the closing date of applications (the 
maximum age limit is inapplicable to those already in Central Provincial Public Service).
1.5 Should possess the adequate physical and mental ﬁ tness required to serve in any area of Central Province 
and discharge duties of the post. 
1.6 No person who is ordained in any religious order shall become eligible to sit the examination

03. Salary scale :     The posts of Technological Service (Civil/ Draughtsman) Grade III and Training Grade  of Central 
Provincial Public Service are entitled for the salary scale of MN 3 -2016 which is  Rs. 31,040 - 10 x445- 11 x 660- 10 
x 730- 10x 750- Rs. 57,500/- as per Public Administration Circular No. 03 /2106. Salary will be paid as per provisions 
indicated in Schedule II of the said circular. 
 Allowances for Training Grade - as per provisions indicated in Schedule V-19 of P.A. Circular No. 03/2016
 1 st year - Rs. 18, 728.00
04. Terms of Engagement :
Terms of Engagement determined by the relevant scheme of recruitment approved by the Central Provincial 
Public Service Commission.
05. Mode of recruitment :
Vacancies shall be ﬁ lled on district population ratio as per P.A. Circular 15/90. Central Provincial Public 
Service Commission shall conduct a competitive examination and the candidates obtaining highest marks 
will be subjected to an interview for selection of suitable candidates. No marks will be assigned at the 
interview. Recruitment will be made under the merit of marks obtained in the examination by the candidates. 
A minimum of 40% marks should be obtained for each question paper.
06. Conditions of recruitment :
6.1.  This post is permanent and pensionable.
6.2. Appointment is subject to the provisions in the Public P.A. circular 01/2014 issued on Implementation of 
Ofﬁ cial Language Policy
6.3. Should serve minimum of 05 years within Central Province once you call upon appointment, not eligible to 
get transfer and should have serve Minimum of 03 years in the institution of your ﬁ rst appointment.
07.  Method of application :
 7.1 A non-refundable examination fee of Rs. 600/- should be credited to the Central Provincial Chief Secretary’s 
revenue Head 20-03-02-13. This fee could be paid to any Divisional Secretary ofﬁ ce in Central Province and 
the receipt issued should be ﬁ rmly afﬁ xed to the application. The application without receipt will be rejected. 
The paid examination fee will not be refunded under any circumstances. 
 7.2 Applications should be prepared using both side of a A4 size paper, in accordance with the specimen application 
form at the end of this noti ﬁ cation. Duly perfected applications should be posted under registered cover, to 
reach the Secretary, Central Provincial Public Service Commission, No. 244, Katugastota Road, Kandy, on or 
before 25.11.2017. The caption “Recruitment to the posts in Technological Service to Central Provincial 
Public Service - 2017” and the Code number of administration District and post apply  should be indicated 
on the top left hand corner of the envelope enclosing the application. Applications not conform to specimen, 
incomplete and for which Examination fee is not paid by the due date will be rejected without any notice. No 
complain will be taken into consideration in relation to loosing an application in posting or being late.
08. Examination Medium :
The examination will be held in Sinhala, Tamil and English medium and not allowed to change the medium later
09. Examination Center :
Central Provincial Public Service Commission will notify in the admission card.

10. Syllabus: Open competitive examination consists of two question papers.
01. Intelligence Test – (Time 01 hour, Total marks - 100)
This paper consists of objective questions to assess the candidate’s, power of logical reasoning 
and analysis and ability to draw sound inferences.
02. Technological question paper (Time 03 hours, Total marks - 100)
Consists of multiple Choice Questions and Structural Questions applicable to the post
a) For the post of Technological Service (Civil) Grade III/  Training Grade to Central Provincial Public Service
This paper is designed for candidates applied for civil posts consists of questions to test the level of 
technological knowledge, a civil ofﬁ cer should possess in the ﬁ eld of Building Construction & maintenance, 
Road construction & Maintenance, and Water Supply & Drainage 
b) For the post of Technological Service (Draughtsman) Grade III to Central Provincial Public Service
This paper is designed for candidates applied for Draughtsman posts consists of questions to test 
the level of technological knowledge, a Draughtsman should possess.
11. Instruction on Application :
A specimen of the application for this examination is appended to the end of this notiﬁ cation. The application should 
accordingly be prepared only on A4 size paper using both sides of the paper, so as paragraphs 01 to 05 in the ﬁ rst 
page, and the rest in other pages of the application. Applicant should refer following codes for ﬁ lling the application
11.1. Code numbers of District apply where applicant permanently reside 
Kandy  - 01
Matale  - 02
Nuwara Eliya - 03
11.2. Code numbers relevant to the post
Grade III and Training Grade  Civil   : 01- C
Draughtsman  : 01- D
11.3. The signature of the applicant should be attested by a Principal of a Government School, Justice of the peace, 
Commissioner of Oaths, Notary Public, Attorney-at-Law or an ofﬁ cer who holds tertiary or senior level as 
per P.A. Circular No. 03/2016 in a permanent post in Government or Provincial Public Service. 
11.4. Any particulars mention in the application send by you found to be false or inaccurate before the recruitment, 
your candidature will be cancelled. If it is revealed that any of the particulars, a candidate has furnished 
is false and has been furnished deliberately knowing it is false, or else he has deliberately suppressed any 
important fact, he/she may be liable to be dismissed from service.
11.5. Applicants who are already in public service or in the Provincial Public Service should forward the applications 
through the Head of Department.
12. The Central Provincial Public Service Commission will make the ﬁ nal decision of any matter not covered by these 
regulations and decision on ﬁ lling the vacancies, leave them vacant or ﬁ lling a portion of vacancies.
