 (i) The age limits is not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years at the closing date of applications. (Upper

Government institution).
 (ii) Applicants should be citizens of Sri Lanka by descent or by registration. 
 (iii) applicants should have an excellent moral character and physically sound, should face a medical examination 
conducted by a Government medical personnel within a month of appointment. if the physical condition is 
found not suitable, the appointment will be cancelled. 
 (iv) Should not have been convicted by a Court of Law under Penal Code. 
 (v) Should establish most immediate 03 years of permanent residency within the authority areas of Pujapitiya 
Pradeshiya Sabha to the closing date the applications are accepted. (residency should be proved by the V oter's 
Register or the certificate issued by the Divisional Secretary). 
 (vi) Should not be a person dismissed from the Government or Provincial Government Service. 
 (vii) applicants already serving in Government or Provincial Government Service have not been subject to any 
inquiry on disciplinary ground (except admonishment) and should be earned all salary increments for 05 
consecutive years prior to the closing date of applications. if selected, further contributions should be made 
to the Widower’s and Orphans Pension Fund Scheme.
