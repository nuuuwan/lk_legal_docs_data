IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices for
publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. All
Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in respect
of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time both from the
date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such notices to
actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to noti fication will not be
entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. Notices
for publication in the weekly Gazette of  26th May, 2017  should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 12th May,  2017.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter shall  be
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.

APPLICATIONS are called from both male and female candidates who residing within Central Province to fill the vacancies
in Grade III of Sports Officers in Central Provincial Sports Department.
01. General conditions .-
1.1 Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
1.2 Shall possess a continuous permanent residence for a period of not less than 03 years within Central Province
by the closing date of application (30.05.2017).
1.3 Shall be of good character and sound physical health .
1.4 Shall not be a person who is ordained in any religious order .
1.5 Applicants those already in Public/ Provincial Public service shall have submitted a certificate from Head
of Department proving that they possess satisfactory period of service by the closing date of application.
1.6 Shall be not less than 18 years and not more than 30 years of age as at closing date of applications
(30.05.2017).
02. Educational Qualifications :
pass in G . C. E. (O/L) Examination in 06 (six) subjects with credit passes for Sinhala/ Tamil/ English Language,
Mathematics and two more subject, at one sitting.
03. Professional Qualifications:
3.1 Shall have successfully completed not less than one year Diploma course on Sports Science conducted by
National Institute of Sports Science;
and
3.2 Shall have obtained 01
st, 02nd or 03 rd  place in an international game (Asian Games, Asian Championship,
Commonwealth Games, world Championships or Olympic)  representing Sri Lanka ;
or
3.3 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02nd or 03rd  place in National Sports Festival conducted by Ministry of Sports;
or
3.4 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02 nd or 03 rd place in an individual event in Provincial Level (Inter District
Competition) conducted as a 3 rd phase of the National Sports Festival;
or
3.5 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02nd or 03rd place in a group event in Provincial Level (Inter District Competition)
conducted as a 3 rd phase of the National Sports Festival;
or
3.6 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02nd or 03rd place in an individual event in District Level (Inter Divisional Secretariat
sports competition) conducted as a 2 nd phase of the National Sports Festival.
or
3.7 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02nd or 03 rd place in a group event in District Level (Inter Divisional Secretariat
sports competition)  conducted as a 2 nd phase of the National Sports Festival;
or
3.8 Shall have obtained 01 st, 02nd or 03rd place in an Annual National Level Games conducted by National Sports
Associations.

04. Physical Qualifications :
Each candidate shall be physically and mentally fit to serve in any part of Sri Lanka and to discharge duties of the
post. Provincial Department of Sports, National Institute of Sports Science or Sports Medical Unit will test the
physical qualifications of reaction velocity, flexibility, abdominal reaction velocity and its flexibility, abdominal
strength, strength of both hands, agility, standing long jump, speed, endurance etc. (shall not be physical or mantel
disability condition)
Shall have completed all the educational qualifications and professional qualifications as at 30.05.2017
05. Salary :
5.1 Payment of salary and other matters will be done under the provisions in Public Administrative Circular No.
03/2016
5.2 Salary scale .– Rs. 29,840 - 10 x300- 11 x 350 -  10x 560- 10x 660- Rs. 48,890/- (salary code of MT 1-
2006)
06. Terms of Engagement :
Terms of Engagement determined by the scheme of recruitment of Sports Officers approved by the Central
Provincial Public Service Commission.
07. Mode of recruitment :
7.1 An open examination will be conducted for all the applicants who have fulfilled basic qualifications,
candidates passed in the examination will be subjected to an interview and a priority list will be prepared
based on the order of merit of the total marks obtained at the written examination and the interview. candidates
equal to the number of vacancies will be called for a practical test to examine their physical qualifications
and Recruitment will be made under the merit of marks obtain in the examination for the candidates who
pass the practical test. If any candidate fails in the practical test, the next candidate in the priority list will be
called for the practical test and if he/she qualified in the test, appointment will be awarded.
7.2 Priority for recruitment will be made for following field of sports
1. Wushu

9. Triathlon
10. Gymnastic
08. Conditions of recruitment :
8.1  This post is permanent and pensionable.
8.2 Appointment is subject to the provisions in the public P.A. circular 01/2014 issued on Implementation
of Official Language Policy.
8.3 Should serve minimum of 05 years within Central Province once you call upon appointment, not eligible
to get transfer and no request on release to some other posts will be considered.

09.  Method of application :
9.1 A non-refundable examination fee of Rs. 600 should be credited to the Central Provincial Chief Secretary’s
Revenue Head 20-03-02-13. This fee could be paid to any Divisional Secretary office in Central Province
and the receipt issued should be firmly affixed to the application. The application without receipt will be
rejected. The paid examination fee will not be refunded under any circumstances.
9.2 Applications should be prepared using both side of a A4 size paper, in accordance with the specimen
application form at the end of this notification. Duly perfected applications should be posted under registered
cover, to reach the Secretary, Central Provincial Public Service Commission, No. 244, Katugastota Road,
Kandy, on or before 30.05.2017. The caption “ Recruitment to the post of Sports Officer Class III to
Central Provincial Public Service - 2017” and the Code number of administrative District should be
indicated on the top left hand corner of the envelope enclosing the application. Applications not conform to
specimen, incomplete and for which Examination fee is not paid by the due date will be rejected without any
notice. No complain will be taken into consideration in relation to loosing an application in posting or being
late.
10. Examination medium :
The examination will be held in Sinhala, Tamil and English medium and not allowed to change the medium later.
Medium apply shall be clearly indicated in the application.
11. Examination Center :
Central Provincial Public Service Commission will notify in the admission card.
12. Syllabus: Open competitive :
I. Aptitude Test – (Time 01 hour, Total marks - 100)
The question paper shall consist of subject related questions designed to test the candidate’s ability of
expression, comprehension, spellings, use of simple grammar, language and essay, drafting a given letter,
preparing graphs and tables based on the given data, summarizing passages, expressing the idea of several
given sentences in one sentence, power of logical reasoning and analysis and ability to draw sound inferences.
II. General Intelligence – (Time 01 hour, Total marks - 100)
The question paper shall consist of questions to gauge knowledge on History of Sri Lanka, Geographically,
Socially and Economically important information and Local and International information on sports.
13. Instruction on Application :
A specimen of the application for this examination is appended to the end of this notification. The application should
accordingly be prepared only on A4 size paper using both sides of the paper, so as paragraphs 01 to 05 in the first
page, and the rest in other pages of the application. Applicant should refer following codes for filling the application.
13.1 Code numbers of District apply where applicant permanently reside
Kandy - 01
Matale - 02
Nuwara Eliya - 03
13.2 Use the Code numbers relevant to the field of sports given in section 7.2 of this notification.
13.3 The signature of the applicant should be attested by a Principal of a Government School, a Justice of the
peace, a Commissioner of Oaths, an Attorney-at-Law, a Notary Public, or a staff grade officer who holds a
permanent post in Government or Provincial Public Service with an annual salary of Rs. 240,360/= or
more.

IMPORTANT  NOTICE  †REGARDING   PUBLICATION   OF  GAZETTE
THE Weekly issue of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  is normally published
on Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public Holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately
preceding the Friday. Thus the last date specified for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also
varies depending on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.
The Schedule below shows the date of publication and the latest time by which notices should be
received for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times specified below
will not be published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return
if publication is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those
desirous of ensuring the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient
time is allowed for postal  transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer does not accept payments of subscription for the Government
Gazette .
Note.—Payments for inserting Notices in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka will be received by the Government Printer.
