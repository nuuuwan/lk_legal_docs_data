1. General conditions for recruitment :
 (i) The candidate shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
 (ii) The candidate shall be a permanent resident of Western Province for a period of three (03) years immediately 
preceding the closing date of applications.
 (iii) More attention will be paid to the candidates who are the permanent residents of the area of Urban Council, 
Kalutara.
 (iv) Shall not be less than 18 years and not more than 45 years of age. (The maximum age limit will not be 
applicable to those who are already holding a permanent post in Public Service.)
 (v) More attention will be paid to the employees serving at present in Urban Council, Kalutara  on casual, 
substitute or contract basis.
 (vi) The candidates shall be of sound moral character and they shall be of sound health to perform the duties of 
the post.
 (vii) Shall not have been punished by a court under Penal Code.

through the respective Head of Department.
