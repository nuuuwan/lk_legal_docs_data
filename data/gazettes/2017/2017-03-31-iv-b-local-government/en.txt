Posts – Vacant
WILGAmuWA  PrADeShIyA  SABhA
aPPLicaTiONS are hereby invited from among those persons who are permanent residents of the Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya 
Sabha, in order to recruit to the vacant posts in the Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha. 
1. Those who apply to the posts vacant in the Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya Sabha, should possess the following general 
qualifications. 
General Qualifications to the Recruitment : 
 (i) Applicants should be citizens of Sri Lanka and permanent residents of Central Province. Should establish 
a period of minimum 03 years permanent residency within the authority areas of Wilgamuwa Pradeshiya 
Sabha. 
 (ii) The age limits is not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years at the closing date of applications. Upper 
age limit will not applicable to the applicants those who are presently employed in a Local Government 
institution. 
 (iii) applicants should have an excellent moral character and physically sound. Should face a medical examination 
conducted by a qualified medical personnel within a month of appointment. If the physical condition is 
found not suitable, the appointment will be cancelled. 
 (iv) Should not have been convicted by a court of Law under Penal code. 
 (v) Should not be a person dismissed from the Government/Provincial Government Service. 
02. Posts in Vacant :
