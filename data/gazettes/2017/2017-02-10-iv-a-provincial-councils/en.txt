IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices for
publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. All
Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in respect
of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time both from the
date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such notices to
actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to noti fication will not be
entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. Notices
for publication in the weekly Gazette of  3rd March, 2017  should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 17th  February, 2017.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or Enactment provides that any proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter shall  be
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”.
G
ANGANI  LIYANAGE,
 Government Printer (Acting).
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
01st January,  2017.

CALLING  APPLICATIONS  FOR  RECRUITMENT  OF  COMMISSIONER  OF  AYURVEDA IN
WESTERN  PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
APPLICATIONS are invited from suitably qualified persons to fill the below mentioned Post which will be vacant
from 20.02.2017 in the Western Provincial Council.
1. Duties and qualifications of the relevant post :
Post Service Qualifications No. of Vacancies
Provincial Commissioner Sri Lanka Ayurvedic An officer of Super      01
of  Ayurveda Medical Service Grade of Sri Lanka
Supra Grade  A yurvedic Medical
Service
2. Other :
Applicants;
 Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
 Should be of good character.
 Should have completed all the qualifications required for the post on or before the closing date.
 Should be confirm in the relevant service/ class and grade.
 Should have not been charged for any disciplinary actions within last five years and Should have
earned all the salary increments during this period.
 Should be mentally and physically sound enough to serve in his or her post.
3. Method of Recruitment :
The qualifications of the applicants for the above mentioned post will be examined by a structured interview
board appointed by the Governor, Western Province. The candidate who would obtain highest marks at the
structured interview will be appointed to the vacancy. Marking scheme of the interview is attached as annex
01.
The decision of the Honorable Governor shall be final regarding this recruitment.
4. Conditions of the Employment and Service:
This appointment shall be subjected to general rules set out in the new Procedural rules of the Public Service
Commission, Establishment Code, Financial Regulations and the relevant service minute and the conditions
mentioned in the letter of appointment issued at the time of appointment to the relevant post and shall be
subjected to regulations of the Provincial Council/ Statutes/ Financial Regulations issued by the Honorable
Governor from time to time.
Selected candidates should be agreeable to serve in the Western Provincial Council for a period of 03 years.

5. Submission of Application:
Duly and accurately completed applications should be sent under registered post to reach “Secretary to the
Governor (Western Province), Secretariat of the Governor, 5th Floor, No. 109, Rotunda Building , Galle
Road, Colombo 03” with the recommendation of the Head of the Department on or before 07.03.2017. The
post applied should be indicated clearly on the top left hand corner of the envelop. Complaints of delays and
misplacement of applications will not be considered. The application should be in accordance with the
specimen form attached as annex 02 and also can be downloaded from the website www.secgov.wpc.gov.lk.
Note:– The originals and the certified copies of all the certificates should be presented at the interview.
National Identity Card issued by the Department for Registration of Persons or a valid passport is accepted
at the interview.
6. Providing False Information:
The candidature will be revoked if any detail in the application was found false or incorrect before the recruitment.
If such information is revealed after the recruitment he/ she will be dismissed after taking appropriate actions.
