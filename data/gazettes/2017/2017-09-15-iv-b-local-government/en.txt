Amendment to the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist republic of Sri Lanka 
no. 2032, Published on the 11th of August, 2017 related to the Vacancies existing in 
the Panwila Pradeshiya Sabha of the central Province Public Service
THE Amendment to the notification, under the above caption, the Educational Qualifications in 04.1, are hereby mentioned 
below and the closing date of the application notified in 08.2 is hereby amended to 30.09.2017.
04.1 Educational Qualifications.– According to the service Statute of the Primary Service (unskilled) in the Local 
Government Institutions in the Central Provincial Public Service, should have passed in the General Certificate of Education 
Ordinary Level Examination, minimum six subjects with two credit passes, out of that five subjects in one and same sitting. 
As such,

qualifications required to the procedure shall be reduced to Grade 5 for the recruitment process.
