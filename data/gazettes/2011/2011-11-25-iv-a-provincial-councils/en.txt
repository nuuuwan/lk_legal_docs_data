03. The following conditions 3.2 to 3.4 should be fulfilled by the

3.1 Approved societies :
(a) Sammurdhi Balakayas
(b) Multi purpose Co-operative Societies
(c) Rural Development Societies
(d) Registered Farmer Organizations.

authority of the societies and a letter from the
Divisional Secretaries should be submitted to prove
this.
(b) (i) Rural Development Societies and Sammurdhi
Balakayas should submit a certificate of financial
position and certificate of eligibility of supplying
cooked provision, to the hospitals obtained from
the Divisional Secretaries.
(ii) Registered farmer organizations should submit the
certificate of registration obtained from the
Assistant Commissioner of Agrarian Services.
3.3 Multi-purpose Co-operative Societies can only submit
tenders for supply foods to the hospitals situated within
their area. They should submit a letter issue from the
relevant bank, regarding the financial status (The account
name should be the society's name)
3.4 All societies should submit their registration certificate
along with the Bid documents.
3.5 Above mentioned societies can get special benefit if the
hospital is situated in the relevant area. If not they will be
considered as a normal applicants.
04. These approved societies will be allowed a preference up to
5% over the lowest Bids.
05. Issuing for Bid forms .– The Bid document for hospitals
stipulated under Schedule 1, 2 and 3 will be issued on 15th December,
2011 until 2.30 p. m. at Provincial Director of Health Services Office,
Kandy.

10.(a) Quantities.– Approximate quantities of supplies required
are given in 6th column of the Schedule 1, 2, 3 and 5th column of the
Schedule 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 annexed herewith. (Price and rate should be

11. Forwarding Bids.– All tenders should be forwarded in two
copies each copy should be enclosed in separate sealed envelopes
and send both the copies in one cover to the Provincial Director of
Health Services, Office of the Provincial Director of Health Services,
P. O. Box 121, Sangaraja Mawatha, Kandy. The cover enclosing the
tender should bare at the top left corner the nature of the services of
diet contract "Tender to supply cooked provisions for the .............
Hospital in .......... District" at the bottom left corner the name and
the address of the tenderer should be written clearly. The cover
enclosing the sealed Tender may be sent by registered post or
deposited in the Bids box at office of the Ministry of Health Services
Kandy. Any responsibility will not be taken regarding postal delays.
12. Accepting of Bids. – All Bids with the date stamp from the
office of Ministry of Health Services would be accepted on 16th
December, 2011 until 10.30 a. m. Bid applications will be opened at
11 a. m. on the same date.
13. Bidders can be presented on the time of opening of the tender.
14. Decision of Bidders.– The total cost of the services arrived at
on the rates quoted for item in Schedule "A" diets in the Bids form
will be the basis for a decision on the Bids received. The tender
should however quote for items in Schedule "C" in the tender form.
But the prices to be paid shall be rates quoted in the tender or
prevailing market rate or rates submitted by the Divisional Secretary.
The lowest price of those will be selected. If such items have control
price ; payments will be made in accordance with the control price.
Bids procedure of supply of cooked provision will be subjected to
amend regulation tax and various statutes, which are issued by the
Government from time to time. Final Bids decision will be taken by
the secretary of the Ministry of Health Central Province/Provincial
Director of Health Services, Central Province/the Director Board of
procurement of the relevant District Health Services.
15. L. P. Gas should be used for cooking meals at Nawalapitiya
District General Hospital and Digana Rehabilitation Hospital in
Kandy District, Matale District General Hospital in Matale District,
District Base Hospital Dickoya and Divisional Hospital Maskeliya
in Nuwara Eliya District. Therefore using gas for cooking should be
taken into consideration when submitting rates for a diet.
16. Validity of Bid Rates.– The rates quoted by the Bids should be
valid for the period of contract mentioned in paragraph 1 above and
under no circumstances will the rates be permitted to be varied
during such period, unless specifically provided for in the agreement.
17. Security Deposits.– The selected tenders will be required to
sign agreement. In addition to that a performance bond (issued by a
reputed bank which is accepted by Central Bank) should be submitted
for 10% of full tender amount. Contractors who did not work under
the relevant agreement in first month will not be pay any payment
and would like to inform that their names will be entered in the black
list.
18. Storage Accommodation.– Successful Bidders will not be
provided the facilities in the nature of store room, or in the institution
or within its premises.
19. Bids Conditions.– Prospective tenders should study the Bids
conditions on the reverse of the Bids forms and comply with
requirements therein. Bidders should also note the conditions of the
agreement in the Bids form.
