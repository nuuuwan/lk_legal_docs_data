IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such Notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 18th March, 2011 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
04th March, 2011.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2011.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  JAYARATNE  FOUNDATION
I , Arundika Fernando do hereby give notice under standing Order No. 48 of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the Democrat ic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I propose to introduce in Parliament a Bill entitled “An Act to incorporate the Jayaratne Foundation”.
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Foundation which has the following General objects :–
1. To constitute children’s organizations, elders organizations,  and to commence programmes to distribute  housing loans, lands  to
commence water schemes, electricity schemes, roads, Health, Education and other infrastructures.
2. To establish religious and National harmony and organize programmes, to create and obtain their cooperation for the Development
of the aforesaid objects.
etc.
ARUNDIKA FERNANDO,
Member of Parliament,
Puttalam District.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  ASOKA  SELLAHEWA  SCHOLARSHIP   FOUNDATION
I, Member of Parliament Janaka Wakkumbura do hereby give notice under standing Order No. 48 of the Standing Orders of the Parliament
of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to introduce in Parliament a Bill entitled  “An Act to incorp orate the
Asoka Sellahewa (Incorporation).”
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Asoka Sellahewa (Incorporation) which has the following General objects :–
(a) Award of Scholarships providing financial assistance to improve the skills in respect of educational, social, cultural, spiritu al,
sports, and art to students qualified in accordance with criteria decided by the Corporation,out of those presently receiving
their education at R/Eheliyagoda M. M. V . (National School)
(b) Commence and implement projects and practical programmes for the students presently receiving their educatiion at Eheliyagoda
M. M. V . for the improvement of skills of education, social, cultural, spiritual, sports and art.
(c) Implementation of the programmes envisaged under the above (a) and (b) for the students receiving their education at
R/Eheliyagoda M. M. V ., suburban provincial schools and any other schools in the island as decided by the Corporation.
Janaka Wakkumbura,
Member of Parliament,
Rathnapura  District.
