IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 05th August, 2011 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 22nd July,
2011.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2011.

KULIYAPITIYA  URBAN  COUNCIL
APPLICATIONS are invited from qualified candidates for recruitment of following vacant posts of primary level non-technical category of
service of the North Western Provincial Public Service in the Kuliyapitiya Urban Council.
01. Primary Level Non-Technical Category of Service :
 Designation Number of Posts Monthly salary scale
Health Labourer 03 Rs. 11,730 - 10 x 120 - 10 x 130 - 10 x 145 - 12 x 160 -
Road Labourer 01 Rs. 17,600
1.2 Educatinal Qualifications :
(a) Should have passed at least two subjects at the G . C. E. (O/L) Examination.
(b) Minimum educational qualification for recruitment of Health Labourer is having passed Grade 8 (Year 9) in a school
approved by the Government.
(c) Educational qualifications specified in the scheme of recruitment for the time being are applied to the employees personally
who have been recruited on the basis of casual/substitute/contract.
N. B.– The designation for this category of service is merely mentioned for delegation of duties according to circumstances and the
appointing Authority/Head of the Department/Head of the Institute may give any designation mentioned in the Schedule
of designations and delegate any duty to any employee. Whatever designations given to employees, all of them belong to
the same category of service.
1.3 Age.– Should be not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years of age to the closing date of application. This upper age limit
will not be applicable to the applicants who are already employed in a permanent post in the Kuliyapitiya Urban Council.
02. Common Conditions :
1. Should be a permanent resident within the jurisdiction of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council within a period not less than 3 years
immediately prior to the closing date of application. Certificate of residence issued by the Grama Niladhari should have
been countersigned by the Divisional Secretary.
2. Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
3. Should have an excellent character and physically in good health.
4. If the applicant already employed in the Provincial Public Service, he should not have been subject to punishment other
than warning within the imemdiate 5 years and he should earn all salary increments within that immediate 5 years.
5. Educational qualifications specified in the scheme of recruitment for the time being are applied to the employees personally
who have been recruited on the basis of casual/substitute/contract in the Kuliyapitiya Urban Council.
6. Candidates will be accepted for the examination for recruitment to these posts if the required qualifications mentioned
above have been fulfilled to the closing date of application.
7. This post is permanent and pensionable. Contribution should be made to the Widows' and Orphans' Pension Fund.
8. Selected candidates are subjected to 3 years probationary period.
9. Preference will be given to those who are employed on basis of casual/substitute/contract in this Urban Council in the
related posts at present.
03. Method of recruitment and method of application :
1. Candidates are recrutied on the result of an structural interview after calling applications for Non-Technical Posts.
2. Copies of following certificates should be sent along with the application :–
(a) Birth certificate ;
(b) Educational certificates ;
(c) Certificate of residency of Divisional Secretary ;
(d) Two character certificates issued recently ;
(e) Certificates of Professional/experience.
