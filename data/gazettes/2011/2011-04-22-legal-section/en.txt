IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such Notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 29th April, 2011 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
15th April, 2011.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEW ARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2011.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  JAYAWICKREME
FOUNDATION
I,  Nishantha Muthuhettigama hereby give notice that I intend to
present a bill titled, Jayawickreme Foundation (Incorporation) Bill
in Parliament under the Standing Order No. 48 of Standing Orders
of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
The objective of the draft bill is the incorporation of the said
Foundation with the following objectives:
(a) To improve the economic strength of the members of the
Foundation;
(b) To cater to cultural needs of the members of the Foundation;
(c) To develop motivation towards mutual assistance among
the members of the Foundation;
 etc.
N
ISHANTHA MUTHUHETTIGAMA ,
Member of Parliament,
Galle District.

AN ACT TO INCORPORATE  THE  STUDY  CENTRE
FOR  THE  ADV ANCEMENT  OF  TECHNOLOGY  AND
SOCIAL  WELFARE
I,  Kanaka Herath do hereby give notice under the Standing Order
No. 48 of the Standing Orders of the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to introduce in
Parliament a Bill entitled “An Act to incorporate the Study Centre
for the Advancement of Technology and Social Welfare.
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Centre which has
the following General objects:-
(1) Providing Forum for the presentation or original
Communications and discussions thereon.
(2) Establishing and maintaining data repositories
 etc.
K
ANAKA HERATH,
Member of Parliament,
Kegalle District.
