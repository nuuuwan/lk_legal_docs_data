IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such Notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three month s from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 29th July, 2011  should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
15th July, 2011.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEW ARDENA,
Government Printer.

Miscellaneous Notices
AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE ASSOCIATION OF
COMMUNITY PHARMACISTS OF SRI LANKA
I, Sunil Handunnetthi hereby give notice that I intend present a bill
titled, Assiciation of Community Pharmacists of Sri Lanka
(Incorporation) Bill in Parliament under the Standing Order No. 48
of the Standing Orders of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka.
The Objective of the draft bill is the incorporation of the said
Association with the following objectives.
(a) To maintain, upgrade and protect the goodwill of Pharmacists;
(b) To provide assistance to members in solving their professional
problems;
(c) To take action against injustices caused to the amateur
Pharmacists
etc.
S
UNIL HANDUNNETTHI,
Member of Parliament,
Colombo District.

AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE PHILIP
GUNAWARDENA COMMEMORATIVE SOCIETY
I, Sriyani Wijewickrama do hereby give notice under Standing Order
No. 48 of the Standing Orders of the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I propose to intoduce in
Parliament a Bill entitled “ An Act to Incorporate the “Philip
Gunawardena Commemorative Society”.
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Association which has
the following general objects:-
(1) to enroll only those who accept and agree to follow the
philosophy of the late Mr. Philip Gunawardena and to
prepare and implement a code of ethics to perform their
activities on the philosophy of the late Mr. Philip
Gunawardena and to explore collect and conserve that
philosophy  in order to render maximum service to the
country through them and to research and codify them
systematically;
(2) to carry out memorial activities in memory of late Mr. Philip
Gunawardena; and
(3) to foster and promote education by establishing and
maintaining schools and educational institutions and by
scholarships,
etc.
S
RIY ANI WIJEWICKRAMA,
Member of Parliament,
Digamadulla District.
