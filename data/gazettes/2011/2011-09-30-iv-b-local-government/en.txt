IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 07th October, 2011 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 23rd
September, 2011.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2011.
N.B.— Tamil version of this Gazette is printed separately.

Posts – Vacant
AMBALANGODA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
APPLICATIONS are invited to fill the vacancy of Road labourer of Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha from the persons who permanently
residing in the area of Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha and possessing the relevant qualifications for the post.
2. Qualifications :
Post Number of vacancies Salary Scale Minimum qualifications
Road Labourer 01 Rs. 11,730 - 10x120-10x130 - 10x145 – Applicant should at least passed 5 th standard
12x 160- Rs. 17,600 as 06/2006 (iv) from a school.
PL-1 2006A
Required other qualifications and general conditions.
(i) Applicants should be between the age group 18-45 by the closing date (However this maximum age limit is not
applicable to the applicants who are already engaged in Government Service or  Local Government Permanently Service.
(ii) Applicants should be a citizen of Sri Lanka by birth or registration.
(iii) Applicants should be a person who permanently resided in the limit of Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha at least 3 years
before the closing date of the application.
(iv) Applicants should contain a good moral character and as well in good health condition.
(v)  Applicants should not be a person who punished by a court of law for any offence.
(vi) Preference will be given to those who possess the experience for relevant field.
(vii) Applicants should be completed minimum qualifications mentioned each post.
3. Conditions for Engagement in service  :
(i) This post is permanent and entitled for pension and W and OP contribution to the above funds are mandatory by
employee.
(ii) This appointment is subjected for probationary period of 3 years.
(iii) The persons who recruited should strictly adhered and abide by all rules and regulations imposed by the government, the
rules and regulations imposed by Public Service Commission of Southern Province in time to time all financial and
administrative regulations stipulated in the Establishment code, all circulars instructions issued by Departments in
frequent intervals and also all amendments and instructions issued by Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha.
4. Procedure of Recruitment :
(i) All applicants will be interviewed and recruitment for service will be effected after reviewing the qualifications.
(ii) All applicants should attach the photo copies of  the following certificates with applications and should present the
original certificates and the national identity card at the interview.
(a) Birth Certificate.
(b) Certificates of educational qualifications
(c) A copy of electoral roll for proof of residency in the area and certificate from the Grama Niladhari recently signed
by provincial secretariat.
(d) Two character certificates obtained recently.
(e) Professional certificates if any.
5. Only the applicants who completed the basic qualifications are called for the interview.
6. Procedure of Application.–  Applications which are processed according to this notice should be addressed to ‘The Chairman,
Ambalangoda Pradeshiya Sabha, Batapola" and be sent by registered post mentioning the post applied for on the left hand corner of the
envelope on or before 07 October, 2011.
(Applicants already engaged in Government Service or Local Government Service should forward their applications through the
relevant Department Heads)
(Applications should be written in A4 size papers. Applications which against with the instructions and uncompleted are
rejected without any notice)
