Local Authorities (Standard by-laws) Act, no. 06 of 1952
BY virtue of power vested upon me under subsection (1) of Section 3 of Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 
of 1952 (Chapter 261), I hereby notify that the following proposal was passed by the udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha at the 
General Meeting held on 06.04.2022.
 T. M. G. Tennakoon,
 Chairman,
 udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha.
At the udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha,
On 19th May, 2022.
PROPOSAL
By virtue of power vested under the Subsection (1) of Section 3 of Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952, 
the udunuwara Pradeshiya Sabha hereby proposes to adopt and implement, from the date this proposal is published in the 
Gazette, the standard by-laws denoted by Section Nos. 01-29 of standard by-laws/standard by-laws of Municipal Councils/
urban Councils/Pradeshiya Sabhas declared by the Gazette Notice published in Section IV (b) of the Extraordinary Gazette 
No. 1816/42 dated 28.06.2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that were framed by the Minister in-
charge of the subject of Local Government of the Central Provincial Council by virtue of power vested upon the Minister 
in charge of the subject of Local Government of the Central Provincial Council under Sub-section (1) of Section 2 of Local 
Authorities (Standard by-laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952, (Chapter 261) read with paragraph (a) of Sub section (1) of Section 2 
of Provincial Councils (Consequential Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, seconded by the Central Provincial Council and 
published in Section IV (a) of the Extraordinary Gazette No. 1816/42 dated 28.06.2013 of the Democratic Socialist Republic 
of Sri Lanka.
