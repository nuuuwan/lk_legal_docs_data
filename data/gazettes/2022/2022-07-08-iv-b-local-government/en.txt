atteNtioN  is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 29th July, 2022 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 15th July, 2022.

Publish as a Developed Area
the general public are hereby informed that the Dickwella Pradeshiya Sabha was passed the following proposal under 
decision No. 5:1:7 at the meeting of Sabha held on 22 February 2022.
 Kithsara Muthukumarana,
 Chairman,
18th April 2022, Dickwella Pradeshiya Sabhawa.
Dickwella Pradeshiya Sabha.
PROPOSAL
as per Section (1) of 134 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 proposed that the area from A to G described below of 
Dickwella Pradeshiya Sabha to publish as a developed area.
Area A -
From The Walasgala Kumara Stores junction up to Radampala junction in Matara direction 4.2 km in distance. The land 
within 100m in East and West from the middle line of Matara Main Road through the Dickwella Radampala Road to 
published as a developed area and
Area B -
From the Bathigama hospital junction up to Beliwattha Seethagalla (Pradeshiya Sabha limit) in Matara direction 6.940 km 
in distance. The land within 100m in South and North from the middle line of Dickwella Matara Main Road to published as 
a developed area and
Area C -
From Dodampahala Lahiru Ricemill junction (Pradeshiya Sabha limit) up to Nilwella junction in Tangalle direction 3.01 km 
in distance. The land within 100m in North and South from the middle line of Tangalle Main Road through the Dickwella 
Mahawela Road to published as a developed area and
Area D -
From Nilwella ‘Orchid Vila’ tourist resort up to Siyambalagaha junction in Nilwella direction 1.550 km in distance. The 
land within 100m in North West and South east from the middle line of Nilwella Road to published as a developed area and
Area E - 
From the border of assessment area (near Buddha statue) of Welihitiya Sirisumana Mawatha up to Radampala junction 
in Aluthgoda direction 1 km in distance. The land within 100m in North and  South from the middle line of Welihitiya 
Aluthgoda Road to published as a developed area and
Area F - 
From the MR/Dickwella Methodist school junction up to sea beach direction 750m in distance. The land within 100m in east 
and West from the middle line of Dickwella Hiriketiya Road to published as a developed area and 
Area G -
From the Pehembiya Road and ‘vers’ tourist resort up to sea beach 1.120 km in distance. The land within 100m in North east 
and South west from the middle line of Dickwella Pehembiya Road to published as a developed area.
