ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 28th October, 2022 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 14th October 2022.

Department of Transport
Approving of Qualified Garages for the Issuance of Fitness Certificates Year - 2023
APPLICATIONS are invited from garage owners and depots belonging to the Sri Lanka Transport Board who are registered 
as a business and who can present evidence of relevant tax payments for the purpose of issuing certificates of fitness for the 
year 2023 for  commercial vehicles registered in the Central Province. Only garages located in the Central Province will be 
considered for this approval.
01. Applicants must meet the following Qualifications.
 (i) facilities
 (a) A 20 feet long, 2 1/2 feet wide, 4  1/2 feet deep vehicle inspection pit or an inspection platform or lift providing 
similar facilities.
 (b) Brake testing equipment
 (c) Headlamp testing equipment
 (d) Mechanical tools such as wrenches, spanners, hammers etc., are used to check the undercarriage of the vehicle, 
steering joints, brakes, cables etc. and worn parts.
 (ii) Staff
  As certification officers, persons with the following qualifications should be employed in the garage.
 (a) Possessing five(5) years of experience in the field of Motor Vehicle Engineering with special specialization in 
Motor Vehicle Engineering or National Technical Diploma Certificate in Mechanical Engineering or Junior 
Industrial Officer Certificate.
 (b) Having passed the four (4) year course in Motor Mechanics at Lanka German Institute of Technical Training 
Moratuwa or having eight (8) years of experience in the field or motor vehicle engineering with other equivalent 
qualifications.
02. The following documents should be submitted along with the application form.
 (a) Attested photocopy of certificates showing qualification of certifying officers.
 (b) A bank receipt received on crediting a non-refundable fee of Rs. 1000 to the A/c no. 33965 (bank of ceylon) 
to the name "Commissioner of Transport, Central Province".
 (c) A rough  map showing the exact location of the garage and the route from the city center to the location (briefly 
detailed) as accessible to the inspecting officer. (Summery of the road map)
The relevant applications are available from this office on working days of the week.
The cover containing the application should be marked "Registration of Garages for Issuance of Fitness Certificates 2023" 
on the top left corner of the envelope and send by Registered post addressed to the Commissioner of Transport, Department 
of Transport, Central Province, yatinuwara Street, Kandy to be received on or before 15th october, 2022.

THE Weekly issue of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is normally published on 
Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public Holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately 
preceding the Friday. Thus the last date specified for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also 
varies depending on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.
The Schedule below shows the date of publication and the latest time by which notices should be 
received for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times specified below 
will not be published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return 
if publication is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those 
desirous of ensuring the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient time is 
allowed for postal  transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer accept payments of subscription for the Government Gazette.                
Note.—Payments for inserting Notices in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka 
will be received by the Government Printer.
