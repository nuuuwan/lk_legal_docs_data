IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 07th September, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 24th
August, 2012.
B. K. S. R A VINDRA,
Acting  Government Printer.
Department of Government Printing,
Colombo 08,
June 23, 2012.

Provincial  Council  Notifications
UVA   PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL
Notification on Establishing a Tourist Zone Covering Bandarawela, Badulla and Mahiyangana area in Uva Provincial
Council Limits
I, Shasheendra Kumara Rajapaksha, The Chief Minister of Uva Provincial Council, according to the powers vested to me by the 13t h
amendment of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, hereby declare that a Tourist Zone will be established
Encompassing Bandarawela, Badulla and Mahiyangana within the area of Uva Provincial Council. It is noticed that the consent of the Uva
Provincial Council or the authorized officer on behalf of the Uva Provincial Council or the authorized Head of the Department should be
obtained to implement any project or any other activity having an indirect or direct impact on the industry of tourism, within the Divisional
Secretariat Divisions given in Column (I) of the Schedule, the Grama Niladhari Divisions given in Column (II) of the Schedule, the places
mentioned in Column (III) possessing tourism importance, or within any other area pertaining to the limits of the said Provincial Council.
It is hereby further declared that the Uva Provincial Council has the Authority to Implement Development Projects that will impact tourism
and development of places of tourists interests stated in Column (III) of the Schedule.
SHASHEENDRA KUMARA RAJAPAKSHA,
The Chief Minister of Uva Provincial Council and
The Provincial Minister of Finance and Planning, Law and Order,
Education, Local Government, Cultural Affairs, Transport, Land
Irrigation, Economic Promotion, Rural Infrastructure Facilities
Development and Construction.
