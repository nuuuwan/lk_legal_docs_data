IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 23rd March, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 09th
March, 2012.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,

Posts – Vacant
WELIGAMA   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Vacancies in Southern Provincial Public Service
APPLICATIONS are invited from qualified permanent residents in the area of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha for the recruitment for th e
following vacancy currently prevailing at Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
01. Title of Post No. of Posts Educational Qualifications
Office Employee 01 Should pass Grade 08 at least
02. Salary Scale.– Rs. 11,730 - 10 x 120 - 10 x 130 - 10 x 145 - 12 x 160 - Rs. 17,600.
03. General Qualifications :
(i) Applicant should be a citizen of Sri Lanka and a permanent resident within the area of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha for
a period of 03 years. (Grama Niladari's certificate has to be counter signed by the Divisional Secretary) ;
(ii) Age should not be less than 18 years and not more than 45 years of age as at the closing date of applications. (This age
limit is not relevant to those who are already in the service) ;
(iii) Applicant should bear an excellent moral character and good health ;
(iv) Applicant should have not been punished by any court of law under Penal Code ;
(v) Prority will be given to those who are currently employed in such post on the basis of casual/temporary/contract/
services at Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha ;
(vi) Applicants should possess above mentioned qualifications.
04. Service conditions :
(i) Persons qualified for the post will be selected through an interview on the need of service ;
(ii) The post is permanent and pensionable ;
(iii) Deductions from salary will be made for the Widow/Widower and Orphanage Pension Scheme ;
(iv) Probation period will be 03 years from the date of appointment ;
(v) In addition, you are bound to act according to conditions of Establishment Code of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka, conditions and regulations that will be enact from time to time by Southern Provincial Council or Public Service
Commission, provisions of Ordinance/Acts relevant to Local Government Institutions, orders that are issued by
Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
05. Method of application .– Applicants those who are already employed in Public/Provincial Public Service should forward
their applications through their Head of Department and late applications will be rejected.
Applications should be prepared as per the specimen form set out below and duly completed applications should be sent under
registered post to "Chairman" Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha, Weligama on or before 20.03.2012. The post applied for should be clear ly
mentioned on the top left hand corner of the envelope in which the application is enclosed. Incomplete and late applications wi ll be
rejected.
Chairman of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha possesses the powers of delay or alteration or making amendments or cancellation of
this notification during or after the invitation of applications.
Photocopies of following certificates should be attached to the application and originals should be forwarded at the interview :–
1. Certificate of birth ;
2. Educational certificates ;
3. Grama Niladhari's certificate to prove the residence (Counter signed by Divisional Secretary) ;
4. Two recent testimonials (One certificate has to be obtained from Grama Niladari) ;
5. Certificate regarding profession or experience ;
6. If already employed in this Sabha in the post concerned on casual/temporary/substitution/contract/service basis such
letter or certificates of appointment.
