The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of  Sri Lanka
IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 22nd June, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 08th June,
2012.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEW ARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Government Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2012.
N.B.— Part I-III of the Gazette No. 1,762 of 08.06.2012 was not published.

WARIYAPOLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Proposal for marking of building limits along the roads of Pradeshiya Sabha Limits
IT is hereby proposed that constructions of any building, a fence, a parapet wall or a gate within building limits (50 feets from the centre
point of all owned and controlled by Department of Road Development – North West and Road Development Authority) which are
situated within Pradeshiya Sabha limits, shall not be legal in terms of provisions of Sub-section (a) of Section 49 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987 and that it is suitable to consider 25 feet form the centre point of roads other than above mentioned roads and 20 feet form
the centre point of roads run through the blocked lands which are situated within Pradeshiya Sabha limits, as the building limi t in terms
of Sub-section (1) of Section 21 of Act 'B'.
Further it is proposed that the adopted resolution on 27th December, 2011 at general meeting for acceptance of construction of
building within above said limits without prior approval to be not legal should be published in Part IV(B) of the gazette of Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the resolution to be implemented form the date on which it is published in the gazette.
A
NANDA JAY ALA TH,
Chairman,
Pradeshiya Sabha – Wariyapola.
At Pradeshiya Sabha – Wariyapola,
28th December, 2011.

WARIYAPOLA  PRADESHIYA  SABHA
Notice on Pradeshiya Sabha Owned Roads
ALL roads of Sabha limits other than roads owned and controlled by Road Development Authority and Department of Road Development
shall be considered as Pradeshiya Sabha owned roads in terms of provisions of Sections 21, 22 and 23 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, N o. 15
of 1987.
It is hereby notified that the Pradeshiya Sabha Wariyapola in the District of Kurunegala, North Western Province declares that
the roads set out in Schedule below to be owned by Pradeshiya Sabha – Wariyapola in terms of Section 24(1) of said Act.
Further it is notified that the Sabha has decided under decision No. 4 taken at general meeting held on 27.12.2011 that if any
party who claims to the roads which were surveyed, marked and then traced by the Pradeshiya Sabha opposes in this regard shall act in
terms of Section 24(2) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987 within 03 months of this notice is published in the gazette.
ANANDA JA Y ALA TH,
Chairman,
Pradeshiya Sabha – Wariyapola.
At Pradeshiya Sabha – Wariyapola,
30th December, 2011.
