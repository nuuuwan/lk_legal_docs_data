IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 01st June, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 18th May,
2012.

WELIGAMA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
Vacancies in Southern Provincial Public Service
APPLICATIONS are invited from qualified permanent residents in
the area of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha for the recruitment for the
following vacancy currently prevailing at Weligama Pradeshiya
Sabha.
01. Title of Post No. of Educational
Posts Qualifications
Health Labourer 02 Should pass Grade 05
at least
02. Salary Scale .– Rs. 11,730 - 10x120 - 10x130 - 10x145 -
12x160 - Rs. 17,600.
03. General Qualifications :
(i) Applicant should be a citizen of Sri Lanka and a
permanent resident within the area of Weligama
Pradeshiya Sabha for a period of 03 years. (Grama
Niladari's certificate has to be countersigned by the
Divisional Secretary).
(ii) Age should not be less than 18 years and not more than
45 years of age as at the closing date of applications.
(This age limit is not applicable to those who are already
in the service).
(iii) Applicant should bear an excellent moral character and
good health.
(iv) Applicant should not have been convicted and punished
by any Court of Law under Penal Code.
(v) Priority will be given to those who are currently
employed in such post on the basis of casual/temporary/
contract/services at Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
(vi) Applicants should possess above mentioned
qualifications.
04. Service Conditions :
(i) Persons qualified for the post will be selected through an
interview on the need of service.
(ii) The post is permanent and pensionable.
(iii) Deductions from salary will be made for the Widow/
Widower and Orphanage Pension Scheme.
(iv) Probation period will be 03 years from the date of
appointment.
(v) In addition, you are bound to act according to conditions
of Establishments Code of Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, conditions and regulations that will be enact
from time to time by Southern Provincial Council or
Public Service Commission, Provisions of Ordinance/
Acts relevant to Local Government Institutions, Orders
that are issued by Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
05. Method of Application .– Applicants those who are already
employed in Public/Provincial Public Service should forward their
applications through their Heads of Departments and late
applications will be rejected.
Applications should be prepared as per the specimen form set
out below and duly completed applications should be sent under
registered post to "Chairman", Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha, Weligama,
on or before 20.06.2012. The post applied for should be clearly
mentioned on the top left hand corner of the envelope in which the
application is enclosed. Incomplete and late applications will be
rejected.
Chairman of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha possesses the powers
of delay or alteration or making amendments or cancellation of this
notification during or after the invitation of applications.
Photo copies of following certificates should be attached to the
application and originals should be forwarded at the interview :-
1. Certificate of birth ;
2. Educational certificate ;
3. Grama Niladari's certificate to prove the residence
(Countersigned by Divisional Secretary) ;
4. Two recent testimonials (One certificate has to be obtained
from Grama Niladari) ;
5. Certificate regarding profession or experience ;
6. If already employed in this Sabha in the post concerned
on casual/temporary/substitution/contract/service basis
such letter or certificates of appointment.
06. Only those who have fulfilled basic qualifications will be
called for the interview.
P
USHPA KUMARA BATTAGE,
Chairman,
Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha.
Office of Weligama Pradeshiya Sabha,
Weligama,
27th day of April, 2012.

By-Laws
PRADESHIYA  SABHA - WARIYAPOLA
By – Law  on Declaration  of
Public Market “Public  Market Area”
IT is proposed that  the  By – Law prepared by the  Village Council,
Wariyapola of the District of Kurunegala under Section 42 of Village
Council Ordinance (Cap 257) and  approved by the  Minister of
Public Administration and Home Affairs in terms of powers vested
in him by the above said Section and then declared by a notice
published  in the  Gazette No. 127 of 30.08.1974 by the Secretary
of the said Ministry which  mentions that the market area should be
a circle of 02 miles’ radii starting from the market should be further
accepted by Pradeshiya Saba Wariyapola subject to the  following
amendments. The said  amended By – Law  notice  has been  accepted
by general meeting held  on 30.10.2003 and it is further  notified
that  the said  resolution should be implemented  considering it is
valid from the date on which the same  was published in the Gazette.
1. The land area bounded by the  Chilaw Road and Puttalam
Road which  are beginning from the centre of Wariyapola town and
the  Adikari Mawatha from which the either side of the said roads
are linked shall be considered as the market of Wariyapola. The
circular area  with  02 miles’ radii starting from the centre point  of
market premises shall be the  market area.
1.1 Opening or maintenance of a meat/fish stall, a market or
carrying  out  businesses by using a mobile vehicle holding
or maintenance of a trade fair  shall not be done  within
this  market area without a valid licence issued by the
Chairman of Pradeshiya  Sabha, Wariyapola or an officer
authorized  by him.
 2. Carrying  out businesses or publishing in that regard shall
not be done  in a place other  than a place shown by the  Chairman
or the  officer authorized by him, despite a person  possesses a
valid  licence.
3. Pradeshiya Sabha, Wariyapola  shall be the authority of
opening and maintenance of meat/fish stalls, weekly fairs, daily
fairs and  day and night fairs within the said  market area.
4. The resolution in respect of charges and rental for  using  a
block of land  or a shop apartment in the  market area shall be
adopted at general meeting  and then annually imposed.
“Authorized Person” shall mean an officer authorized for
aforesaid purpose on a  written notice issued by the  Chairman and
“Sabha” shall mean the  Pradeshiya Sabha, Wariyapola.
A
NANDA JAY ALATH,
Chairman,
Pradeshiya Sabha Wariyapola.
Pradeshiya Sabha of Wariyapola,
22nd December, 2011.
