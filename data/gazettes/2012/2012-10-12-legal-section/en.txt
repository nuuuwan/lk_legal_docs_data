IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such Notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 02nd November, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
19th October, 2012.
B. K. S. R A VINDRA,
Acting Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
June 23, 2012.
N.B.— Parts III and IV(A) of the Gazette No. 1,779 of  05.10.2012 were not published.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  M. K. A. D. S.
GUNA WARDANA  FOUNDATION
I, Arundika Fernando, do hereby give notice under Standing Order
No. 48 of the Standing order of the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I Propose to introduce to
Parliament a Bill entitled "An act to incorporate the M. K. A. D. S.
Gunawardana Foundation"
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Foundation which
has the following general objects:-
1. to promote peace, goodwill, understanding and cooperation
among all communities and people of Sri Lanka ; and
2. to promote and protect Human rights, Human values,
fundamental freedoms democracy and pluralism in Sri Lanka.
etc.
Arundika Fernando,
Member of Parliament.
10-643
AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE CHAMPIKA
PREMADASA COMMUNITY
DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION
I, Gayantha Karunatileka, do hereby give notice under Standing
Order No. 48 of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to
introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled "An Act to incorporate the
Champika Premadasa Community Development Foundation".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Foundation which
has the following general objects :-
1. to assist in the development of educational, religious,
cultural, aesthetic and sports facilities without any
reference to race, religion or caste and to conduct various
festivals and competitions ; and
2. to promote sustainable development in rural and backward
areas by utilizing the natural resources that is available in
those areas.
etc.
Gayantha Karunatileka,
Member of Parliament.

EDUCATIONAL RELIEF FOUNDATION
I, Ramesh Pathirana do hereby give notice under Standing Order
