IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and
times of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. sho uld be
prepared by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, the reby
enabling those interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and  complaints
made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of  05th  October, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 21st
September, 2012.

AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  DAWULDENA  SIRI  GNANISSARA  SASANA  WARDHANA
FOUNDATION
I, Dr. Sudharshani Fernandopulle Member of Parliament do hereby give notice under standing Order No.48 of the Standing Order of  the
Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka that I propose to introduce in Parliament a Bill entitled" an act to incorporate
the Dawuldena Siri Gnanissara Sasana Wardhana Foundation.”
The aim of the Bill is to Incorporate the said Dawuldena Siri Gnanissara Sasana Wardhana Foundation which has the following
General Objects :-
1. To Propagate the teachings of Lord Buddha among the people of all wolks of life spread all over the world and in that behalf
afford all facilities.
2. To establish and maintain training institutions for educating children learning in higher education centres and schools about
the Doctrime of  Buddhism so as to Create an awareness in them of the life style of a true Buddhist.
3. To establish and maintain welfare centres for the benefit of needy and/or Physical handicapped or displaced people
irrespective of cast greed religion or other differences whatever.
4.  To work in close collaboration with international bodies having similar objects to and in that regard sponsor and organize
international seminars and cultural shows
etc.
Dr. (Mrs.) SUDARSHINI  FERNANDOPULLE,
Member of Parliament,
Gampaha District.

AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE SRI LANKA WRITERS ORGANIZATION
I, Alhaj A. H. M. Azwer Member of Parliament, do hereby give notice under Standing Order No.48 (1) of the  Parliament of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I purpose to introduce in Parliament a Bill entitled  “An Act to incorporate the Sri Lanka
Writers Organization”.
The aim of the Bill is to Incorporate the said Sri Lanka Writers Organization  which has the following General Objects :-
1. to function as an organization of Sri Lankan writers who are engaged in the writing activities in the Main Languages used in
Sri Lanka.
2. to establish a writers centre to enable the writers representing the Sinhala, Tamil and Muslim ethnic groups mainly and all
other ethnic groups in Sri Lanka to assemble, conduct seminars, conduct studies and provide accomadation when necessary.
3. to build relationships between the writers Organization in the SAARC and Commonwealth Countries in Particular and with
The Writers Organization in the other countries in commonly and take steps to promote Sri Lankan Literature and Sri
Lankan Litterateurs, etc.
Alhaj A. H. M. A
ZWER,
Member of Parliment.
