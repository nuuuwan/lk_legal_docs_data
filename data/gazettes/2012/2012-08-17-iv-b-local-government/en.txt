IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 07th September, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 24th
August, 2012.

KANDY  FOUR  GRA VETS   AND  GANGAWATA   KORALE   PRADESHIYA  SABHA
IN order to recruit to the vacant primary posts (unskilled) in Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha, applic ations
are hereby invited from the qualified persons who possess the following qualifications and from among those persons who are res idents
of the Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha.
1. Sanitary Labourers – Class III
2. Watcher – Class III
02. Salary Scale. – As per Public Administration Circular 6/2006(IV), PL 1-2006-A, the salary scale to the above posts.
Rs. 11,730- 10x120 - 10x130 - 10x145 - 12x160 - Rs. 17,600. (Efficiency Bar Examination before the 4th salary step).
03. General conditions of recruitment :
3.1 All applicants should be citizens of Sri Lanka ;
3.2 Applicants should be permanent residents of Kandy Four Gravets and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha for a
period of 03 years imediately prior to the closing date of application.
Applications shall be considered from adjoining Local Government Institutions where sufficient applications were not
received to fill the vacancies.
3.3 The applicants’ age should not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years. This age limit will not be applicable for
those who are already employed in Government/Provincial Public Services.
3.4 Should be of excellent moral character and physically sound.
04. Terms of Engagement :
4.1 These posts are permanent and pensionable.
4.2 Contribution should be made to widow’s/widower’s and orphan’s pension scheme.
4.3 The appointees are subjected to serve a period of probation for three years. Work, conduct and attendance should be
in a satisfactory manner to consider for making appointments as permanent.
4.4 In addition to the medium language under which recruitment has done, the appointees should obtain proficiency in
other official language. Relating this, an oral test, conducted by the Secretary to the Sabha, should be passed within 05
years of the recruitment as per Circular No. 07/2007, dated 28.05.2007.
4.5 In addition to the terms and conditions of recruitment mentioned herein, for primary office assistants’ (unskilled)
services appointees should comply with regulations of Establishment Code, Finance Regulations, orders that may be
laid down from time to time by Central Provincial Council and the Secretary to the Sabha.
05. Qualifications for the recruitment :
5.1 Sanitary Labourers – Class III
Should be completed the aproved syllabus for Grade 8 (Year 9) in a school approved by the Director General of
Education.
5.2 Watcher – Class III
Should have passed the General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) or National Certificate of General Education
(Ordinary Level), minimum 6 subjects including Sinhala or Tamil Language, in not more than two sittings.
06. Efficiency Bar.– Prior to attain the 4th step to the Salary Scale related to the recruitment, the Efficiency Bar Hurdle has to
be confronted.
07. Method of Recruitment :
7.1 All applicants, along those who have minimum qualifications for the recruitment shall be called for a structured
interview :
Only the qualified applicants will be called for the interview and the date of interview will be notified in future. The
following documents, along the photo copies of them should be produced at the interview :
