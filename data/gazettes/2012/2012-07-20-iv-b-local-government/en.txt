IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 10th August, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 27th July,
2012.
B. K. S. R A VINDRA,
 Acting Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
June 23, 2012.

Local Government Notifications
KUCHCHA VELI   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952
THE following decision taken by the Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha by virtue of the power vested in North East Provincial Council under
Section 2 of the Local Authorities (Standard By-Laws) Act, No. 6 of 1952 is hereby notified.
THE DECISION
The Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha do hereby decides under Sections 122, 126 with Section 221( a) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No. 15 of 1987, to adopt and approve to come in force in the area of Authority of Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha with effect from the date
on which this decision is published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the By-Laws Part I and Section No.
01 – 42 of Part II of the said Standard By-Laws, framed by the Honourable Minister of Local Government, Housing and Construction and
published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 520/7 of 23rd August, 1988, Part IV – Local Government and decided and approved by the
Honourable Governor, Eastern Province on which the decision published in Part IV of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic
of Sri Lanka, No. 03 of 30.05.2012.
A. MUBARAK
Chairman,
Kuchchaveli Pradeshiya Sabha,
Nilaveli.
