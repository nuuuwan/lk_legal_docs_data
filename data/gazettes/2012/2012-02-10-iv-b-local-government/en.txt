IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months  from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazette should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 17th February, 2012 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 03rd
February, 2012.

SEETHAWAKA   PRADESHIYA   SABHA
Public Service Vacancies in Western Province
APPLICATIONS are called for recruitment to following post in the Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha from qualified application residen t in
the Western Province. Preference will be given to those living within Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha area and those who are present serving
as casual or refief employes.
Designation Number of Designation Salary scale Educational qualification
Health Labourer 02 Rs. 11,730-10x120-10x130-10x145- Educational qualification is immaterial
12x160 - Rs. 17,600
Other Qualification Required :
(1) Applicant should be Sri Lankan citizen ;
(2) Should be permanent residence of Western Province within the last 3 years to the last date of receiving application ;
(3) Should not be below 18 years and over 45 years on the date 10.02.2012. (This age limited will not be applicable to
applicants who are already in Government/Provincial Government service. This concession is eligible only to those who
are in permanent Government/Provincial Government Service) ;
(4) Should not have be convicted by Court of Law for any offence under the Penal Code ;
(5) Should possess excellent characters and best physical health.
Conditions of Employment :
(1) This post is permanent and pensionable ;
(2) This appointment will be subjected to a period of probation for three years ;
(3) In addition to the conditions of recruitment the applicant should undertake to comply with the Establishment Code,
Financial Regulations or conditions that will be made by Western Provincial Council from time to time, the laws and
ordinance relating to Provincial Administration and orders made by from time to time by, Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha.
Method of Application .– Application prepared according to the specimen application form shown here under should be send
under register cover to "The Secretary, Seethawaka Pradeshiya Sabha, Hanwella" to reach him before 05.03.2012. The envelope enclosing
the application should on its left hand top be written "The application for the Post of Health Labourer".
Those who are employed in Government/Provincial Government service should submit there applications through their Heads
of institutions. Applications received after the closing date of applications will be rejected.
The applications should be enclose with the copies of following documents :–
(i) Certificate birth ;
(ii) Certificate of educational qualifications ;
(iii) Certificate of residence ;
(iv) Certificates of other qualifications and related matters.
K. A. C
HANDANA PADMASIRI,
