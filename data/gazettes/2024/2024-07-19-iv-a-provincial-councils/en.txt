Recruitment to Post of bio medical engineer Grade III of executive Service category in the 
Department of health of Western Provincial Public Service – 2024 (open)
APPLICATIONS are called from the qualified applicants who are permanent residents of the Western Province to fill the 
vacancies in the post of Bio Medical Engineer Grade III of Executive Service Category in the Department of Health of 
Western Provincial Public Service.
02.  Salary Scale
Salary code number for this post is SL-1-2016 and the monthly salary scale entitled to this post as per P.A.C. 03/2016  
is ,
Rs. 47,615 - 10 x 1,335 – 8 x 1,630 – 17 x 2,170 - Rs.110,895 /-  ( 01
st salary step)
03.  Service conditions :
        (i) This post is permanent. Pensionable. You are subjected to the principle decisions taken by the government 
regarding the pension scheme you are entitled for.
       (ii) Eligible candidates to be recruited to this post will be appointed subject to a probation period of three years 
from the date of appointment.
       (iii) After being recruited to the post, the candidates should pass the efficiency bar examinations and official 
language proficiency examination as specified in the Scheme of Recruitment and Promotion for the post of 
Biomedical Engineer.
 (iv)  Should adhere to the Establishments Code of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Procedural 
rules of the Western Provincial Council Public Service, Financial regulations of the Western Provincial 
Council, regulations or orders imposed by the Honorable Governor of the Western Provincial Council, other 
regulations or orders imposed from time to time by the Western Provincial Public Service Commission and 
ministerial and department orders.
04. fields of existing vacancies :
 There are 02 vacancies in the post of Bio Medical Officer. Western Provincial Public Service Commission has the 
final decision regarding the recruitment for the vacancies of this post. 
4.1   Closing date of applications is 20.08.2024. All the qualifications relevant to the applied post should be 
completed on before 20.08.2024.
05. Qualifications :
5.1 Educational Qualifications
    Should have obtained a degree in Electronic/ Electrical/ Mechanical Engineering/ Bio Medical   
 Engineering from a University accepted by the University Grants Commission. 
 5.2   experience
    Having served as a Bio Medical Engineer, Clinical Engineer or Medical Scientist in the Public Service,  
 Provincial Public Service, a Statutory Board or in the private sector is considered as a special qualification.

08. method of application
8.1    Application should be prepared in accordance with the specimen application given with this notification by using the 
both sides of an A4 size paper and should be filled by the applicant in his/her own handwriting . Paragraphs from 
No. 01 to 04 should be on the first page and paragraphs from No. 04 onwards should be on other pages. Applications 
that are not prepared in accordance with the specimen application and that are inaccurate and incomplete will be 
rejected without any prior notice. Applicants should bear the loss of sending incomplete applications. It would be 
useful to keep a copy of the application with you.
 8.2 Completed application should be sent to “Secretary, Western Provincial Public Service Commission, No.628, 10
th 
Floor, Jana Jaya City Building, Nawala Road, Rajagiriya” so as to reach on or before 20.08.2024. by Registered 
Post. It should be mentioned as “Recruitment to Bio Medical Engineer Grade III of the Western Provincial Public 
Service -2024 (Open)” on the upper left corner of the envelop which contains the application. Receipt of the 
applications will not be acknowledged and applications that are received after the given date, ones which have not 
fulfilled the required qualifications or incomplete ones will be rejected without any notice. 
8.3    As soon as the admissions are issued to the applicants an announcement will be made about it in the official website 
of the Western Provincial Public Service Commission, i.e., www.psc.wp.gov.lk . If the admission was not received 
even after 7 days of the publication of the notice, the applicant should inform the Western Provincial Public Service 
Commission as stated in the notification. The applicant should make his/her inquiry from the Examinations Unit 
of the Western Provincial Public Service Commission while keeping a copy of the application, payment receipt, 
application registered receipt ready in his/her hand and giving his/her full name, address, National Identity Card 
number and name in the application calling notification correctly. 
 8.4 The signature of the applicant should be attested by an authorized officer to do so. The signature of the applicant 
should be attested by any of Justice of the Peace, a Commissioner of Oaths, a lawyer, a Notary Public, a principal 
of a government school (a permanent staff officer  in public or provincial public service drawing an annual salary 
of Rs. 498,960/- or over), a Chief incumbent or a Head of a Buddhist or any other religion in charge of a place of 
worship or holding a position of importance, A commissioned officer in Tri-Forces, an officer of police service 
holding a gazzetted post. Officers who are already in public or provincial public service should get their signatures 
attested by the head of the department or an authorized officer to do so for him.

10. furnishing false information:
 If it was revealed that an applicant has not any fulfilled the required qualifications, his/her candidacy will be cancelled
