01. Qualifications
1.1.  General Qualifications
 1.1.1. Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka
 1.1.2. Should possess a continues permanent residence for a period of not less than 03 years within the Kandy 
Municipal authority area as at the closing date of applications 
 1.1.3. Shall be of excellent character
 1.1.4. Should possess the adequate physical and mental fitness required to serve in any area of Central Province 
and discharge duties of the post 
 1.1.5. Age should be not less than 21 years and not more than 35 years as at 31.12.2023. (Accordingly, only those 
whose date of birth falls on or before 31.12.2002, on or after 31.12.1988 are eligible to apply for this)

  Shall have obtained a following degree from a university recognized by the University Grants Commission or a 
degree awarding institution recognized by the University Grants Commission or a higher education institution 
recognized as a degree awarding institution by the Ministry in charge of higher education
 (a) B.Sc. (Special) Degree with Chemistry
    or
 (b) B.Sc. (General) Degree with Chemistry
    or
 (c)  M.Sc. Degree with Chemistry
Note:  Shall have completed all the qualifications including degree relevant to the post on or before 31.12.2023.
