Attention  is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of  12th July, 2024 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 28th June, 2024.

TENDERS  are hereby invited by the Divisional Secretary of Kurunegala, till 10.30 a.m. on 23rd August 2024 for the 
purchase of the exclusive privilege of the selling fermented toddy, by the retail at the toddy taverns referred to in the  
Schedule below during the period from 01st January, 2025 to 31st December, 2025. Subjects the sales conditions published 
by the Excise Commissioner in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, No. 207 of 20th August, 1982 
and the conditions application to all Exercise Licenses for the time being in force, and to the following conditions.
Duly perfected tenders in the prescribed forms by the persons who enter for to purchase the rents of toddy must be 
accompanied by a receipt issued by any shroff counter in any Divisional Secretariat acknowledging the fixed tender deposit 
indicated in the schedule here under together with a certificate of assets which is obtained in terms of the sale conditions 
of toddy, published in the above Gazette notification and enclosed in the sealed envelope and must be clearly written the 
number and name of the toddy tavern on which tender is made by oneself in the top left side corner and should send it 
to reach the Divisional Secretary of Kurunegala by registered post or be placed in the tender box keeps in the Divisional 
Secretariat for this purpose before the time prescribed in the schedule for closure of tenders. Due tender forms are obtainable 
from any Divisional Secretariat.
All alternation made in the tender form must be authenticated by the person who obtain the tender by placing his 
signature. Tenders of those which do not comply with these requirements, will be rejected.
All persons who obtain tender should be present at this Divisional Secretariat at 10.30 a.m. on 23rd August, 2024 
which date is the closing of tenders.
The Divisional Secretary, Kurunegala reserved him self the right of rejection of a tender form or all the tender forms 
without assigning any reasons therefore.
On being declared the purchase of the privilege successful person who obtained tender should not later than 2.00 
p.m. and the date of which he is declared to the purchaser, pay to the Divisional Secretary, Kurunegala a sum fixed by him 
as security of deposits and sign the sales conditions.
We cannot assure to give the same places where the toddy taverns are held for the year 2025 the buildings where 
the toddy taverns are running at present cannot be purchased for the year 2025 and instead, the toddy taverns have to be 
started in a new place, the newly selected place should obtain the approval of the Divisional Secretary of Kurunegala by the 
successful person within the 30 days from the date of grant of the tender. 
If the rent is not sold on 23rd August, 2024 went of satisfactory bids, the re-sale of rent will be held on 20th 
September, 2024, 10.30 a.m. at this office.
Further particulars, if required can be obtained from the Divisional Secretariat, Kurunegala.
