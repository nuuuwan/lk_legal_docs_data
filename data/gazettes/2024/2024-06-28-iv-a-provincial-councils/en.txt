open competitive examination for the Recruitment of Graduates on District - basis to the class 3 
Grade I (A) of Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service for the existing vacancies in the Provincial council Schools of 
the north Western Province - 2024
ONLINE applications are hereby invited from the eligible graduates as per this announcement and the Service Minute of Sri 
Lanka Teacher’s Service of the North Western Provincial Council Public Service, for recruitment of teachers on a district 
basis to the existing vacancies of Class 3 Grade I (A) of Sri Lanka Teacher’s Service in the Provincial Council Schools of 
the North Western Province.
01.  This recruitment is done in Sinhala, Tamil and English Medium Subjects and a candidate can apply for a maximum 
of 03 subjects mentioned in the schedule. Even if a candidate has not applied for the primary subject, he/she will be 
considered for a primary appointment with his/her consent if he/she is qualified for primary but not selected for any 
of his/her choices.
02.  I. Residents of Kurunegala district  should apply for subject vacancies in Kurunegala district and residents of 
Puttalam district should apply for subject vacancies in Puttalam District. 
  II. Residents of the respective district will be given priority in recruitment. 
  III. Only in cases where there are not enough qualified candidates to fill the vacancies in one district, the vacancies 
will be filled from the qualified candidates for the same subjects in the other district.
03.  A list of subjects having vacancies in Sinhala, Tamil, and English language media in each district is given in the schedule. 
The number of vacancies will be determined by the Public Service Commission of the North Western Provincial Council.
04. The Salary Scale:
   The salary scale applicable to this post is Rs. 31,490 - 6x445 - 7x525 - 2x600 - Rs. 39,035/- monthly combined 
salary scale in Segment G.E.1-2016-A of the Public Administration Circular No. 03/2016(IV) dated 05.01.2022. 
Salaries will be paid placing on the salary step of Rs. 37,835/-, the fourteenth step of this salary scale.
05. Educational and Other Qualifications:
 5.1  Educational Qualifications:
 I. Having a degree related to the subjects mentioned in the Schedule from a university or Degree Awarding 
Institute recognized by the University Grants Commission, which falls under specialized subject areas 
recognized for teaching purposes, or having completed the four-year Higher National Diploma in 
Accountancy (HNDA) offered through the Sri Lanka Technical College or Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced 
Technological Education (SLIATE) as per the Pub. Ad. Circular 46/90. 
       and
 
 II.  Having passed Sinhala Language or Tamil Language along with Mathematics as a subject in the G.C.E. 
Ordinary Level Examination. 
       Very Important: The following should be considered when filling out the online applications.

  ●  Candidates should pay attention to the subjects they can apply for based on their degree

  ● Only one online application can be submitted under one National Identity Card number. So before applying, check

  5.2 Physical and Mental Fitness:
   Every applicant should be physically and mentally fit enough to perform duties as a teacher. 
 5.3  Age:
   Applicant’s age as of the closing date of applications should be at least 18 years and not exceed 40 years. 
 5.4  Other Qualifications:
 I. The applicant must be a citizen of Sri Lanka and a permanent resident of the North Western Province.
 II. The applicant should have excellent moral character and physical fitness.
 III. Should be able to serve in any area of North Western Province.
 IV . Residency should be proved at the interview by a DS 04 certificate issued by Grama Niladhari to be a permanent 
resident in the North-Western Province for at least 03 consecutive years by the closing date of applications. 
Applicants who have less than 03 consecutive years of permanent residence in the north-Western Province 
by the closing date of applications must also confirm their period of residence through a DS 04 Grama Niladhari 
Certificate.
 V . In cases where the permanent residence of the applicant is less than 03 consecutive years in the North Western 
Province by the closing date of applications, those applicants will also be considered as per Section IV above, 
if the applicant’s spouse has got permanent residence in the North Western Province for at least 03 previous 
consecutive years. The spouse’s residential certificate (DS 04) along with the marriage certificate should be 
produced at the time of the interview. 
06. Conditions of Service  :
 I. Selected graduate applicants will be appointed to the post of Class 3 Grade I (A) of the Sri Lanka Teachers’ 
Service subject to the general conditions which govern the appointments to the public/provincial public service, 
conditions stipulated by the Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service Minute in North Western Provincial Public Service and 
to the amendments to same which have been made already or will be made in future. 
 II. This post is permanent. Appointees will be subjected to a policy decision taken by the Government about the 
pension scheme entitled for this post. 
 III. Appointees will be subjected to a probation period of 03 years.

07. Recruitment:
  7.1 Method of Recruitment :
   The recruitment is done under the provisions in the Service Minute of Sri Lanka Teacher’s Service of the North 
Western Provincial Council Public Service and the approval of the Hon. Governor of the North Western Province. 
  7.2 Written Examination : This written examination consists of two (02) papers.
Sub. No. Subject Max. marks Pass Marks
01 Aptitude Test (duration 01 hour) 100 40
02 General Knowledge (duration 01 hour) 100 40
   Syllabus
   Subject No.  01 - Aptitude Test
   This question paper will be prepared to test the skills and logical intelligence of the applicants. This paper will 
comprise 50 multiple-choice and short-answer questions. (02 marks for each question and the total marks is 100)
   Subject No. 02 - General Knowledge
   This paper will comprise 50 multiple-choice questions to test the general knowledge in social, economic, and 
political areas. (02 marks for each question and the total marks is 100)
 7.3  Examination Procedure:
 I. y ou can apply for this exam in Sinhala, Tamil, or English medium.
 II. Every applicant must appear for both (02) papers.
 III. Every applicant should appear in all the papers of this examination in one language medium. That language 
medium should be the language medium in which the applicant intends to get his/her appointment.
 IV . Applicants applying for English subject must sit for the examination in English medium.
 V . An applicant will not be permitted to change the medium of language indicated in his/her application at a later 
stage.
