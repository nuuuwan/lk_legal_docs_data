calling applications for the recruitment of bachelor of education graduates to Grade II of class 2 in Sri Lanka 
Teaching Service for Sinhala, Tamil and english medium Teacher Vacancies in Provincial council Schools of 
Western Province - 2024
FOR the vacancies in the Sri Lanka Teaching Service in the Provincial Council schools of Western Province, applications 
are invited from the Bachelor of Education graduates of the Western Province who are permanent residents of the Western 
Province and who meet the qualifications for recruitment to Grade II of Class 2 in Sri Lanka Teaching Service in accordance 
with the minutes of Sri Lanka Teaching Service of the Western Provincial Public Service.
1. This recruitment is made for teacher vacancies for Sinhala, Tamil and English media subjects and the number of recruits 
will be determined based on the available provisions and number of vacancies. The candidates who qualify from the 
interview will be called for the practical test and the recruitment will be done based on the order of total merit of the 
candidates who have scored the highest marks.
2. Salary system
According to the salary segment of G-E-2-2016A stated in the Public Administration Circular No. 03/2016(iV) dated 
05.01.2022, the monthly salary scale related to this post is Rs.39,175 – 10x825 – 7x1,335 – 20x1,630 – Rs. 89,370. you will 
be placed in the salary scale of Rs.40,825/- which is step 03 of this salary scale. Apart from this, you are also entitled to other 
allowances paid by the government to public service officials from time to time.
3. Educational and other qualifications
3.1. Educational qualifications
i. Should possess a bachelor of education degree from a university recognized by the University Grants 
Commission or a degree awarding institution recognized by the University Grants Commission, in specialized 
fields recognized for teaching work. This degree should be a valid degree on the date of consideration of 
qualifications (31.07.2024). 
And
ii. Pass in six (06) subjects with three credits with passes in Mother Tongue and Mathematics in not more than two 
sittings of the General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) Examination. (Must have passed at least five 
(05) subjects at one time.)
And
iii. Must have passed in three (03) subjects at the same time in the General Certificate of Education (Advanced 
Level) Examination.
Very Important : (To be noted while completing the application form.)
 Applicants should be careful about the subject and medium they can apply for according to their degree. 
(Schedule 01 and 02)
 Applicants should apply for the respective teacher vacancies only according to the subject of expertise.

Age should be not less than 18 years and not more than 35 years as on the date of consideration of eligibility

3.4. Other Qualifications :
•	 Applicant must be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
•	 Applicant must be of excellent character.
•	 The applicant or his/her spouse applying for the vacancies must be permanent and continuous residents of 
the Western Province for a period of not less than 03 years preceding the last date of receipt of applications. 
(Residence certificate (DS04) issued by Grama Niladhari should prove residence at the time of interview.
•	 Should be able to work in any part of the Western Province.
3.5. Last date for consideration of eligibility – 31.07.2024
All the qualifications mentioned above should be completed on or before 31.07.2024.
4. Terms of service
 i.   Graduate candidates who are selected subject to the general conditions governing State/Provincial Council public 
Service Appointments, the conditions laid down in the Minutes of Sri Lanka Teacher Service of the Western 
Provincial Council Public Service, and the amendments made or hereafter made to the said minute, the selected 
graduate candidates will be appointed to Grade II in Class 2 of Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service.
 II.  This position is permanent and pensionable. The appointees should be subject to a policy decision taken by the 
government in the future regarding the pension scheme for this position.
 iii.  Those appointed to this post will be subject to a probationary period of 03 years.
 IV .  Promotions are made according to the Minutes of Sri Lanka Teacher Service of the Western Provincial Council 
Public Service.
 V .  Proficiency in the second official language should be obtained as prescribed by P.A.C.18/2020 dated 16.10.2020 
and its amendments from time to time.

5. Recruiting
 5.1.  Recruitment Method :
  The recruitment will be made according to Section 7.2.3.5 of the Minutes of Sri Lanka Teacher Service of the 
Western Provincial Council Public Service effective from 01.07.2008.
  A general interview and practical test will be conducted for selection.
  Candidates who qualify the general interview will be referred to the practical test. (Clause 7.2.4.3. (a) of the 
Minutes of Sri Lanka Teacher Service shall apply.)
  Based on the merit of the total marks obtained in the practical test, the candidates who have secured the highest 
marks will be selected for the vacancies.
 5.2.  General interview :
  Check whether the qualifications specified in the Gazette notification published in accordance with the service 
minute and check for physical qualifications.
 5.3.  Practical Test :
