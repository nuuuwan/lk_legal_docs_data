Attention is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notification will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.

P ARLIAmenT
An Act to incorporate the united human Rights 
Protection organization
I,  Major Pradeep undugoda, do hereby give notice under 
No. 53(1) of the Standing order of the Parliament of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to 
introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled "An act to incorporate 
the united human Rights Protection organization".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said organization 
which has the following general objects :-
(a) to develop a rapid - response system for immediate 
relief during crises, including the distribution of 
essential supplies, temporary shelter, and medical 
assistance ;
(b) to advocate for human rights through awareness 
campaigns, and provide legal aid and support 
for individuals facing human rights violations. 
This includes partnering with legal experts and 
organizations ;
 
and
(c) to conduct widespread awareness campaigns on 
human rights issues, promoting a culture of respect, 
tolerance, and equality.
etc.
 Major
 PradeeP UndUgoda,
 Member of Parliament.

P ARLIAmenT
An Act to incorporate the International human 
Rights council
I, Major Pradeep undugoda, do hereby give notice under 
No. 53(1) of the Standing order of the Parliament of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to 
introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled "An act to incorporate 
the International human Rights Council".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Council which 
has the following general objects :-
(a) to conduct educational programs to raise awareness 
about sustainable living practices, conservation, 
and the importance of preserving Sri Lanka's 
Biodiversity ;
(b) to organize cultural festivals, art exhibitions, and 
exchange programs to showcase Sri Lankan heritage 
globally. This promotes cultural under standing and 
appreciation ;
 
and
(c) to establish partnerships with international 
cultural organizations to facilitate joint initiatives, 
exhibitions, and projects that promote cross cultural 
collaboration.
etc.
 Major
 PradeeP UndUgoda,
 Member of Parliament.
