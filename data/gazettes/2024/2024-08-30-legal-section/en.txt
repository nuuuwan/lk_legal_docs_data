respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time

PARLIAmenT
An Act to Incorporate the malkara Institute Trust
I, Sudarshini Fernandopulle do hereby give notice under No. 53(1) of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, that I propose to introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled "An Act to incorporate the Malkara 
Institute Trust".
The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Trust which has the following general objects :-
Etc.
(a) To advance for the benefit of children with special needs, education by establishing, maintaining, carrying on, managing 
and developing a school offering a specialized curriculum suitable for such children with special needs and focusing on 
how to best assist a child's development and grouping children based on their ability level;
(b) Working together with children on the autism spectrum and their circle of support including families, therapists, allies 
and supporters in order to create a comprehensive, tailored learning and support system that addresses the specific 
learning priorities of each child as well as fostering string partnerships with families, providing them with resources, 
support and a sense of belonging with a community that understands and shares their aspirations for their children;
 and
(c) To promote for the benefit of children who have needs by reason of their age, infirmity, disability, financial hardship or 
social economic circumstances, the provision of facilities for recreation or other leisure time activities in the interest of 
social welfare and with the object of improving the condition of life of the said children.
 Sudarshini Fernandopulle,
 Member of Parliament.
