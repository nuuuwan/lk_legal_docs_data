By 	virtue	of	powers	vested	by	Chapter	252	of	Legislative	Enactment	Code	of	Ceylon	and	as	per	Section	2	of	“Sub	Statues	
on	Vehicle	Parking	and	Traffic	Control,	which	was	made	by	the	Chief	Minister	of	Southern	Province	and	Published	in	the
