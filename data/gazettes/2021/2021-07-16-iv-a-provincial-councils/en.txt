ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of  06th August, 2021 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 22nd July, 2021.

OPEN / LIMITED COMPETITIVE EXAMINATION FOR RECRUITMENT TO THE 
POST OF REVENUE
INSPECTORS FOR GRADE III OF THE REVENUE INSPECTION SERVICE IN LOCAL GOVERNMENT 
INSTITUTIONS OF THE CENTRAL PROVINCIAL PUBLIC SERVICE - 2021
APPLICATIONS are called from both male and female candidates who are residing within Central Province to ﬁll the 
vacancies in Grade III of the Revenue Inspection Service in Local Government Institutions of the Central Province.
Examination .- The examination for selection of suitable candidates is organized by the Central Provincial Public Service 
Commission or conducted by the Commissioner General of Examinations on behalf of the Commission. This examination 
is expected to be held in Kandy.
01. Number of persons to be appointed and the date on which the appointment will take effect will be determined by the  
Commissioner of Local Government.
02. Terms of service -
 i. Selected Candidates will be appointed to Grade III of the Revenue Inspection Service subject to the conditions 
imposed in the scheme of recruitment and promotion to the Revenue Inspection Service of the Department of 
Local Government Central Province and the amendments made to it or to be made here in after.
 ii. This post is permanent and pensionable.
 iii. Eligible candidates for the post will be appointed to this service subject to a three year probationary period from 
the date of appointment.
 iv. Failure to be con ﬁrmed on the post at the end of the probation period will result in termination of his / her 
service.
 v. According to the provisions of Pub. Ad. Circular 18/2020, all of ﬁcers shall acquire proﬁciency in other ofﬁcial 
languages within 03 years of joining the  service in addition to the of ﬁcial language in which they have joined 
the service. (Level of Proﬁciency to be obtained - Category 03) Salary increments of ofﬁcers who do not obtain 
the required Second Language Pro ﬁciency within 03 years from the date of appointment will be suspended
until they qualify. 
 vi. The selected of ﬁcers will be subjected to the condition of serving in any part of the Central Province and any 
request for transfer will not be considered until the end of the three year probation period.
 vii.  This appointment is subject to the Establishments Code, Financial Regulations, Provincial Council Financial 
Rules, Departmental Orders and orders issued by the Government and the Central Provincial Council from
time to time.
 viii.  Complete uniform must be worn when performing ﬁeld duties.
  
 ix.  If required by the Head of Department / Head of the Institution, a motorcycle should be kept for travel.
 Should work both day and night to collect /supervise revenue on occasions, festivals, as directed  by the Head of 
Department/Institution.
03. Salary Scale. - The posts of Grade  III of the Revenue Inspection Service are entitled for the salary scale of                   
 MN 01-2016 as per Public Administration Circular No. 03 /2106, which is
  Rs. 27,140- 10X300 -11 X350 -10X 495 - 10X 660 - Rs. 45,540/-

04.  Qualiﬁcations.-
1. Basic qualiﬁcations -
 (a)  Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka
 (b)  Age as at 31.07.2021 shal not be less than18 years and not more than 30 years. The maximum age limit does not 
apply to ofﬁcers already serving in a permanent post in the public service.
 (c)  Shall be of excellent character and good physical health.
 (d) No person who is ordained in any religious order shall become eligible to sit the examination.
 (e) Applicants should be permanent residents In the Central Province. (Proof of residence within the period of 03 
years prior to the closing date of applications should be con ﬁrmed at the interview by a document signed and 
issued by the relevant Divisional Secretary on the certiﬁcate issued by the Grama Niladhari)
 (f) Applicants must have not been convicted under the Penal Code or by a court.
Note:   Candidates appearing for this examination should have completed all the quali ﬁcations mentioned in this Gazette 
notiﬁcation by the last date of receipt of applications . All relevant certi ﬁcates and documents should be kept 
ready for submission as requested by the Commissioner of Local Government. Cannot apply in anticipation of 
exam results.
05. Educational and Professional Qualiﬁcations.- 
 Recruitment under Open Stream:-
 i. A pass in G. C. E. (O/L) Examination in 06 (six) subjects with credit  passes for Sinhala/Tamil/ English Language, 
Mathematics and two more subjects, at one sitting ;
    and
 ii. Should have passed at least one subject (01) (except common General Test and Ordinary English) in the GCE 
(Advanced Level) Examination.
Recruitment under Limited Stream :-
 i. A pass in G. C. E. (O/L) Examination in 06(six) subjects including Sinhala/Tamil/English Language and Mathematics 
with atleast two credit passes in not more than two sittings ;
    and
 ii. Should have completed more than 10 years of active, satisfactory and permanent service in a non-technical, semi-
technical or technical grade relevant to the primary service category of the Local Government Service in the 
Central Province.
A Satisfactory service period is a period of service having shown a moderate level of performance, all salary increments 
been earned in the preceding 05 years as scheduled without any suspension, reduction, stoppage or deferment and that is 
not subject to any other form of disciplinary punishment other than a warning.

06.  Examination fee .- Examination fee is Rs. 600. The amount should be credited to the Revenue Head of the Chief 
Secretary of the Central Province on 20-03-02-13 on or before the last day of receipt of applications. This fee 
could be paid to any Divisional Secretary Ofﬁce in Central Province and the receipt issued to your name should be 
ﬁrmly afﬁxed to the application. The fee paid for the examination will not be refunded in full or in part  under any 
circumstances (it may be useful to have a photocopy of the receipt of payment). No stamps or cash orders will be 
accepted for the examination fee.
07.  Method of application:-
 (a) The application form should be prepared on A4 size paper using both sides of the paper in accordance with the 
sample format attached to this announcement and the candidate should ﬁll it clearly in his own handwriting. 
The application should be prepared in such a way that 01 to 02 on the ﬁrst page, 03 to 05.ii on the second page, 
05.iii to 10 on the third page and 11 to 13 on the fourth page. Non-compliant applications and applications with 
incomplete information will be rejected without notice (it will be useful to keep a photocopy of the completed 
application).
 (b)  Applications for the Open / Limited Competitive Examination should be sent by registered post to reach the 
Commissioner of Local Government, Department of Local Government, Pallekele, Kundasale on or before 
16.08.2021. The caption “Open / Limited Competitive Examination for Recruitment to Grade III of Revenue 
Inspector Service- 2021” should be clearly written on the top left corner of the envelope. No applications received 
after this date will be accepted. Applicants employed in the Public Service should submit their applications 
through their Heads of Departments / Heads of Institutions.
 (c)  Incomplete applications in all respects will be rejected. This department is not responsible for the loss or delay of 
an application during mailing and will not consider any complaints in this regard. Further, it does not constitute a 
valid admission for his / her examination.
 (d) The application should clearly state whether you are applying on open basis or limited  basis.
 (e) The code of the district in which the applicant is permanently residing should be mentioned as the district of 
application.
  Kandy - 01 Matale -02 Nuwara Eliya - 03
 (f) The Signature of the applicant should be certi ﬁed by a person authorized to certify the signature on the examination 
application form and the admission form of the examination.The signatures of the of ﬁcers already employed in 
the Government or the Provincial Public Service should be certi ﬁed by the Head of the relevant Department 
or an of ﬁcer authorized to do so on his behalf and the signatures of other candidates should be certi ﬁed by a 
Justice of Peace,Commissioner of Oaths, Notary Public, Principal of a Government School, a  permanent Staff 
Grade Ofﬁcer of Public or Provincial Public Service with an annual combined salary of Rs.240,360 / - or more, 
Authorized Ofﬁcer of the Armed Forces an ofﬁcer holding a gazetted post in the Police Service or by any one of 
these.
Note - No document or copy other than the receipt of the payment of examination fee should be 
attached with the application form.
08.  Appearing for the examination .-
 (a) Admissions will be issued by the Central Provincial Public Service Commission/ Commissioner General of 
Examinations to all the candidates who have duly submitted their applications. Candidates appearing for the 
examination are required to appear for the examination at the examination center of their choice and a candidate 
who does not present his/her certiﬁed admission card on the ﬁrst day of the examination is not allowed to appear 
for the examination.

10. Providing false information
  If a candidate is found to be ineligible, his/her candidacy may be cancelled before or during the examination or 
at any time after the examination. He/she may be dismissed from public service if it is revealed that he/she has 
knowingly presented any false information, or if he/she has deliberately suppressed any important information.

12.  Method of selection for appointment -
Seventy percent (70%) of the existing vacancies will be ﬁ lled on the basis of open competitive examination results 
and thirty percent (30%) on the basis of limited competitive examination results. Vacancies will be ﬁ lled in accordance with 
Government policies and regulations regarding recruitment after con ﬁ rmation of eligibility through an interview called on 
the basis of marks obtained in the competitive examination. In the event that there are several candidates who are equal 
to the ﬁ nal marks in the awarding of appointments, the person with the highest quali ﬁ cations according to the educational 
qualiﬁ cations and professional qualiﬁ cations relevant to the ﬁ eld will be selected at the interview. Recruitments are made on 
the basis of district population ratio as per the provisions of Public Administration Circular 15/90.
