OPEN / LIMITED COMPETITIVE EXAMINATION FOR RECRUITMENT TO THE POST 
OF TECHNOLOGICAL SERVICE (CIVIL/ MECHANIC/ ELECTRIC/ DRAUGHTSMAN) TO 
CENTRAL PROVINCIAL PUBLIC SERVICE - 2021
APPLICATIONS are called from both male and female candidates who are residing within Central Province and any parts of 
Sri Lanka; possess required qualiﬁcations speciﬁed in this notiﬁcation to ﬁll the vacancies in Technological Service (Civil/ 
Mechanic/ Electric/ Draughtsman) Grade III and Training Grade of Central Provincial Public Service.
01. General conditions :
1.1 Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka
1.2 Shall be of excellent character
1.3 Applicants residing any part of the Island shall apply. Applicants who have completed all the quali ﬁcations as 
at the date mentioned in the notiﬁcation and possess a continues permanent residence for a period of not less 
than 03 years within Central Province or having married a person holding more than 03 years of permanent 
residence within Central Province are given priority to ﬁll the vacancies at ﬁrst and then applicants residing 
other parts of Island will be considered for the recruitment based on their marks priority on the condition of 
compulsory service for 05 years.
1.4 Applicants applying on open basis shall be not less than 18 years and not more than 35 years of age on the 
closing date of applications 
1.5 Should possess the adequate physical and mental ﬁtness required to serve in any area of Central Province 
and discharge duties of the post 
1.6 No person who is ordained in any religious order shall become eligible to sit the examination
1.7 Shall have completed all the quali ﬁcations and conditions prescribed in the noti ﬁcation as at the date 
mentioned in the recruitment notiﬁcation

03. Salary scale :
The posts of Technological Service (Civil/ Mechanical/ Electrical/ Draughtsman) Grade III of Central Provincial 
Public Service are entitled for the salary scale of MN 3 -2016 as per Public Administration Circular No. 03 /2106, 
which is 
 Rs. 31,040 - 10 x445 - 11 x 660 - 10 x 730 - 10x 750- Rs. 57,550/- (MN 3 -2016)
*Allowances for Training Grade - as per provisions indicated in Schedule V-18 of P.A. Circular No. 03/2016 with 
effect from 01.01.2020
1 year   – Rs. 27,140.00 
2 years – Rs. 27,440.00
04. Terms of Engagement :
• Terms of Engagement determined by the  service minute of the Central Provincial Technical Service
• 70% of the existing vacancies will be recruited under open stream and the remaining 30% will be ﬁlled under 
limited basis
• If the number of vacancies is less than 03, recruitment will be done only under open stream.
• The number of vacancies allocated under the open stream will be recruited at ﬁrst by the applicants who are 
eligible under above table 2.1.1 and then applicants will be admitted to the training grades under 2.1.2 and 
2.1.3 only if the number is not sufﬁcient to ﬁll the vacancy number.
• If there are not enough internal quali ﬁed candidates for the percentage to be recruited from the limited 
competitive examination, the balance will be ﬁlled by the external candidates selected by the open competitive 
examination itself.
05. Mode of recruitment : 
Central Provincial Public Service Commission shall conduct a competitive examination and the candidates 
obtaining higher marks based on the marks priority will be subjected to an interview for selection of 
suitable candidates. No marks will be assigned at the interview. Recruitment will be made under the merit 
of marks obtained in the examination by the candidates. A minimum of 40% marks should be obtained 
for each question paper. Recruitment for the vacancies will be done based on the merit shown in the 
examination.
06. Conditions of recruitment :
6.1.  This post is permanent and pensionable. You shall be subjected to a policy decision taken by the government 
in future in respect of the pension scheme entitled to you.
6.2. Appointment is subject to the provisions in the public P.A. circular 18/2020 issued on Implementation of 
Ofﬁcial Language Policy
6.3. Should serve minimum of 05 years within Central Province once you call upon appointment and any 
request made for transfer or release to another post outside the Central Province during that period will not 
be considered.

07. Method of application :
7.1. Examination fee is Rs. 600/-
7.2. Candidates residing within the Central Province should adhere to the following procedure in paying their 
examination fees :
•      A non-refundable examination fee of Rs. 600/- should be credited to the Central Provincial Chief 
Secretary’s Revenue Head 20-03-02-13. This fee could be paid to any Divisional Secretary of ﬁce in 
Central Province and the receipt issued should be ﬁrmly afﬁxed to the application. The application 
without receipt will be rejected. The paid examination fee will not be refunded under any circumstances. 
7.3. Candidates possess permanent residents outside the Central Province should adhere to the following 
procedure in paying their examination fees :
• Examination fee should be paid at any People’s Bank branch drawn in favour of Secretary of the 
Central Provincial Public Service Commission, to the Account No. 003-1-001-0-000-6004 maintained 
by the People’s Bank, Kandy and the relevant receipt issued by the Bank should be ﬁrmly afﬁxed to 
the application.
• When ﬁlling the bank receipt, the name of the account holder should be mentioned as “Secretary, 
Central Provincial Public Service Commission” and the applicant’s name, address, telephone number, 
National identity card number and reason as “Recruitment to the post of Grade III Technological 
Service (Civil/ Mechanical/ Electrical/ Draughtsman) to Central Provincial Public Service - 2021 
/ Recruitment to the post of Training Grade Technological Service (Civil/ Mechanical/ Electrical/ 
Draughtsman) – 2021”. It is mandatory to ﬁll the bank receipt.
7.4. Applications should be prepared using both side of a A4 size paper, in accordance with the specimen 
application form at the end of this notiﬁcation. Duly perfected applications should be posted under registered 
cover, to reach the Secretary, Central Provincial Public Service Commission, No. 244, Katugastota Road, 
Kandy, on or before 03.05.2021. The caption “Recruitment to the post of Grade III Technological Service 
(Civil/ Mechanic/ Electric/ Draughtsman) to Central Provincial Public Service - 2021 / Recruitment to the 
post of Training Grade Technological Service (Civil/ Mechanic/ Electric/ Draughtsman) – 2021”  and the 
permanent residential district should be indicated on the top left hand corner of the envelope enclosing the 
application. Applications not conform to specimen, incomplete and for which Examination fee is not paid 
by the due date will be rejected without any notice. No complain will be taken into consideration in relation 
to loosing an application in posting or being late.
08. Examination Medium :
The examination will be held in Sinhala, Tamil and English medium and not allowed to change the medium later.
09. Examination Center :
Central Provincial Public Service Commission will notify in the admission card.
10. Syllabus: Open competitive examination consists of two question papers.
01. Intelligence Test – (Time 01 hour, Total marks - 100)
This paper consists of objective questions to assess the candidate’s power of logical reasoning 
and analysis and ability to draw sound inferences.
