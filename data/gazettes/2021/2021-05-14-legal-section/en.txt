An  AcT  To  IncoRPoRATe  The  mAboTuWAnA  SRI  PIyARAThAnA  nAyAkA  TheRo   
SuWASevAnA  founDATIon 
I, Asanka Navarathna, do hereby give notice under No. 53(1) of the Standing Order of the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka, that I  propose to introduce to Parliament a Bill entitled “An Act to incorporate the Mabotuwana Sri 
Piyarathana Nayaka Thero Suwasevana Foundation”.
   The aim of the Bill is to incorporate the said Trust which has the following general objects :
 (a) to give temporary residential facilities to patients from various areas of Sri Lanka, suffering from dreadful 
non-communicable diseases to the Karapitiya National Hospital for treatment ;
 (b) to operate a day care centre for disabled and backward children ;
 (c) to operate a library for making available library facilities for the mental comfort of residents.
etc.
  
AsAnkA nAvArAthnA,
  Member of Parliament.
