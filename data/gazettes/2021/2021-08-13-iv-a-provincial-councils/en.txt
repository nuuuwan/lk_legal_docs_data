1.   This by -law shall be enacted in accordance with the powers vested in the Ayagama Pradeshiya 
Sabha under Section 126 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act No. 15 of 1987.
2.   This by-law shall be called the by-law of the maintenance of weekly fairs in the area of Ayagama 
Pradeshiya Sabha.
3.   This By-laws shall be enacted for the Regulation, Controlling, Administration and Supervision of 
Weekly Fair in the Ayagama Pradeshiya Sabha
4.  All the buildings of the Weekly  fair shall be constructed of brick, stone or permanent construction 
material and shall be constructed at least six inches above ground level with concrete on the ground.
5.   The roof must be constructed of some permanent roo ﬁng material. Also, there should be a permanent 
system for drainage of rainwater collected from each roof.
6.  The minimum distance between two rows of market stalls within the building shall be at least six 
feet wide, except in any reasonable case.
7.   The pavement shall be constructed of permanent construction material suitable for the roaming of 
the customers and rain water shall be provided so as not to disturb the passenger roaming.
8.   Every trade table built within the weekly fair shall be made of brick, cement, or cobble stone with 
a concrete slab or a stainless steel plate.
9.  Howerver, when ﬁnished with a concrete plate, the layer must be plastered, polished, cemented, or 
ceramic.
10.  Whenever there is no need to create trade table, the stripes that separate each part should be marked 
in white.
