RECRuITMENT ON OPEN BASIS FOR ThE POSTS OF SPORTS OFFICER (GRADE III OF SERVICE CATEGORy 
OF MANAGEMENT ASSISTANTS TEChNICAL SEGMENT 02)
APPLICATIONS are invited from Sri Lankan citizens with the following qualifications for the Post of Sports Officer in the 
Provincial Department of Sports, Southern Province.
01.  Method of recruitment : 
Applicants who possess the qualifications in the notifications will be subjected to a vocational examination conducted 
by Provincial Department of Sports and Sports Medical unit of the Southern Province and the applicants who pass the 
vocational examination will be subjected to the aptitude appraisal interview before an interview board appointed by 
the Secretary to the Provincial Ministry of Sports, Southern Province and vacancies will be filled in order of the marks 
obtained from the that aptitude appraisal interview. The manner in which marks should be obtained in each of these 
tests in given under No. 06 of this Notification.
02.  Qualification required :
 I.  Educational and V ocational Qualifications :
 (a) Should have passed the G. C. E.(Ordinary Level) Examination in Six subjects at One sitting with credit 
passes in Sinhala/Tamil/English Language, Mathematics and Two (02) other Subjects
    and
 II. Should have passed G.C.E. (Advanced Level) Examination new syllabus in all subjects (excluding General 
test); or should have passed G.C.E. (Advanced Level) Examination old syllabus in three subjects
    and
  have won 1st or 2nd places in competitions held at Provincial level (between Districts) in the National Sports 
Festival held by the Ministry of Sports.
    or 
  have won 1st, 2nd or 3rd places at the National Sports Festival.
    or
  have won 1st, 2nd or 3rd places at National Championships held by National Sports Associations registered 
under the Sports Act.
    or
  (b)
 I. Should have passed the G. C. E. (Ordinary Level) Examination in Six subjects at One sitting with credit 
passes in Sinhala/Tamil/English language, Mathematics and two (02) other subjects.
    and
 II. Should have represented Sri Lanka in South Asian Games, Asian Games, Commonwealth Games, Olympic 
Games or World Championships and won medals for 1st, 2nd and 3rd places.

 *  It is compulsory for the Officers recruited under the above qualifications to obtain a technical diploma relevant to 
the field of sports which is the National V ocational Qualification (NVQ) Level Six (6) recognized by the Tertiary 
and vocational Education Commission within three years to confirm the service. Officers who do not obtain a 
Technical Diploma being a National V ocational Qualification (NVQ) level Six (06) relevant to the field of Sports 
within first three years will not be confirmed in the service at the end of the probationary period and their salary 
increments will be suspended. If an officer who has been subjected to such salary increment suspension fails to 
obtain Technical Diploma being a National V ocational qualification (NVQ) level Six (06) relevant to the field of 
Sports, the Secretary to the Provincial Ministry of Sports, Southern Province must terminate his appointment on 
the approval of the Provincial Public Service Commission of the Southern Province on inefficiency.
03. Experience :
 Experience in the relevant field is considered to be an additional qualification.
04.  Every candidate is physically and mentally fit to serve in any part of Sri Lanka and to perform the duties of the Post.
05.  Other qualifications:
 Every applicant
 I. Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
 II. Should be of excellent character.
 III. Should be a permanent resident in Southern Province and should provide a certificate issued by the Grama 
Niladhari to the effect that the applicant has a continuous permanent residence in the Southern Province for a 
period of three years immediately preceding the closing date of applications.
 IV . Should have completed all the qualifications from 02 to 06 in every respect on the date of calling for applications.
06. Age limit :
 Minimum limit : 18 years
 Maximum limit : 35 years
07. Salary Scale :
The Salary Scale of this post is Rs. 30,140-10x350 - 11x370 - 10x560 - 10x660 - Rs. 49,910 (MT 02-03-2016) 
in accordance with schedule I of the Public Administration Circular 03/2016 of 25.02.25. you are paid salaries in 
accordance with the provisions of that circular as stated in Schedule II therein. (E.g. Basic salary of Rs. 30,140 and 
a living allowance of Rs. 7,800 is relevant to the year from 01.01.2021 to 31.12.2021.)
08. The following making procedure is used for the interview.
 i.  V ocational Examination :
V ocational examination for physical fitness consists of eight component: reaction speed, flexibility, abdominal 
strength, arm strength, posture, sudden jump, speed and endurance. Applicants must obtain a minimum of 30 
marks for each test and an average of 40% or more in all subjects. This does not apply to sports that are not 
related to sports such as carom, chess, weightlifting, archery, etc.

Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately 
preceding the Friday. Thus the last date specified for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also 
varies depending on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.

received for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times specified below 
will not be published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return 
if publication is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those 
desirous of ensuring the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient time 
is allowed for postal  transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer accept payments of subscription for the Government Gazette.
