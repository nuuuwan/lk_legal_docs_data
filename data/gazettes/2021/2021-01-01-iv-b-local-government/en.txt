PROPOSAL
By virtue of powers vested in local government authorities by Section 2 of Entertainment Tax Ordinance (Chapter 
267), in the event of any entertainment purpose for functions in the entertainment ordinance aforesaid, Beliatta Pradeshiya 
Sabha proposes to impose and recover an entertainment tax of Seven percent (7%) of total income received from entrants 
when it is a film show and Ten percent (10%) when it is another event of entertainment. It is further proposed that the said 
entertainment tax should be paid to Beliatta Pradeshiya Sabha prior to day of holding such event of entertainment.
