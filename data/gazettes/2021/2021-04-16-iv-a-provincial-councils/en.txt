IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of  07th May, 2021 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 23rd, April 2021
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
 “Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, noti ﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other

PART IV (A) – GAZETTE  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA – 16.04.2021
PROVINCIAL  COUNCIL  PUBLIC  SERVICE  COMMISSION – NORTH  WESTERN  PROVINCE
Recruitment to the Post of Bio Medical Engineer (Executive Ofﬁcers’ Category)  -Grade III of the Department of 
Health Services ( North Western Province) -2021 – (Open )
APPLICATIONS are invited from the males and females who have the following qualiﬁcations to be recruited to the Post 
of Bio Medical Engineer(Executive Ofﬁcers’ Category)  -Grade III of the Department of Health ( North Western Province) 
in North Western Provincial Council Public Service
01. Educational Qualiﬁcations /Professional Qualiﬁcations.-
 1.1  Educational Quali ﬁcations:-
  Candidate should possess a Degree on Electronic/ Electrical/Mechanical Engineering/Bio medical   
  Engineering from a university recognized by the University Grant Commission
  Note: - Priority will be given to the candidates who possess a degree for Bio Medical Engineering   
  Science
 1.2   Experience:-
  Priority will be given to the candidates who have two ( 2) year  experience as a Bio Medical Engineer in  
  a government or private institute.
 (This should be proved by a service certiﬁcate)
02.  Physical Fitness .-
   Every applicant should be mentally and physically ﬁt to serve in any part of North Western Province  
  and to discharge the duties assigned to the post. 
 03.  Other Qualiﬁcations .-
 I.  Age should be not less than 21 years and not more than 45 years as at closing date of   application. 
 II.  Applicants should have excellent moral character and should be in good health condition.
 III.  All educational, professional, and other requirements shall be completed by the closing date of   application 
and the certiﬁcates should have been obtained on or before that date. 
 
 IV . Any person who bears priesthood in any Religious Sect is ineligible to apply for this post. Applications are 
called island-wide. Priority will be given to the permanent residents of North Western Province, and if there are 
no eligible candidates in North Western Province, the candidates from the outside provinces will be considered. 
04.  Salary Scale.-
 As per SL 1-2016 in  the Public  Administration Circular No. 03/2016 dated 25-02-2016, the monthly    
 salary scale applicable to this post is Rs. 47,615 – 10x 1335 – 8x 1630 – 17x 2170 –  Rs. 110,895.
05.  Conditions of the Service .-
 I.  This post is permanent. The pension scheme applicable to this post will be subject to a policy decision taken by 
the government in the future.
 II. The appointees to this post will  subject to probation period of (three) 03 years and will be con ﬁrmed at the 
end of the probation period, if his/her work and conduct is satisfactory, and if the appointee has passed the ﬁrst 
Efﬁciency Bar, and if he/she has satisﬁed the other requirements needed to be conﬁrmed in the service.
 III  The appointees shall acquire the competency in the other of ﬁcial language/ languages other than the language 
that qualiﬁed for this post, during 03 years from the date of appointment, in terms of Public Administration 
Circular No. 18/2020.
