IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 06th March, 2020 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 20th February,  2020.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, noti ﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other 
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

Proclamations & c., by the Governors
NORTH WESTERN PROVINCIAL COUNCIL
Municipal Council Orders
1. Ahamed Jamaldeen Mohamed Muzammil, who exercises the powers of the Governor of North Western Provincial Council 
and the minister in charge of the Provincial Council subject to Section 289 of the Municipal Council Ordinance, which is the 
252 authority read with Section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Subsidiary Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989 of the Municipal 
Council Ordinance. Amendments relating to the payment of communications and transport allowances to members of 
Municipal Councils in accordance with the powers vested in them under is imposed. 
 A
HAMED JAMALDEEN MOHAMED MUZAMMIL,
 Governor of North Western Province.
In Kurunegala, 
24th December, 2019.
 1)  The Regulations are referred to as the Communication and Transportation Allowances for Members of Municipal 
Councils.
 2)  Either maximum amount of communication allowance of Rs. 10,500.00 for mayor of the municipal councils by a 
formal resolution of the council Rs. 8,500.00 for the Deputy Mayor, Rs. 7,000.00 for other members of the council 
or maximum amount of the telephone bills can be paid monthly under that limits.
 3)  For members of municipal councils by a formal resolution of the council Transport allowance can be paid subject 
to a maximum of Rs. 7,000.00.
 4)  These allowances should be paid out of the council fund and if the council is convinced that there is suf ﬁ cient 
income for it, the annual budget should have provided for it.
 5)  Municipal Councilors are not entitled to Communication and Transportation allowance in the following cases:
 I.  Attending the General Meeting and Committee Meetings of the month of the payment of allowances 
without the approval of the General Assembly,
 II. Detention or imprisonment for any criminal charge,
 III.  The functions and functions of the members of any Municipal Council have been suspended in terms of 
the Local Authorities (Administration and Supervisory Powers) Statute of the North Western Provincial 
Council Act, No. 01 of 1991 or Section 277 of the Municipal Council Ordinance.
 However, in the event of a period of detention, imprisonment or leave or suspension of service, these allowances  
 may be calculated in proportion to the remainder of the month.
 6)  Notwithstanding paragraph (a) of paragraph (1) of this Order, Members shall not be deprived of their allowance for 
non-attendance at a meeting of the Council in the event of a formal approval of a duty.
The Municipal Council Regulations published in the Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 1891 dated 28.11.2014 imposed by the Minister 
in-charge before the payment of communication allowances to Members of Municipal Councils has been canceled.
In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala, Tamil or English texts of this order, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
