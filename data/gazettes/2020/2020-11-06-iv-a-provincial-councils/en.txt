IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 27th November, 2020 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 13th November,  2020.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
 “Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, noti ﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other 
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

APPROVING OF QUALIFIED GARAGES FOR THE ISSUANCE OF FITNESS CERTIFICATES YEAR 2021
APPLICATIONS will be received from owners of Garages who have been registered as a business and having evidence 
of making relevant tax payments and from the Sri Lanka Transport Board Depots, to entrust the issuance of ﬁ tness 
certiﬁ cates to commercial vehicles registered within the Central Province,for the year 2021,Granting of such approval 
will only be considered for garages situated within the Central Province.
1  Applications should have fulﬁ lled the following qualiﬁ cations :
 i. Facilities
 
 a. Should contain either an inspection pit of 20 feet long 2 1/2 feet wide and 4 1/2 feet high in size or 
an inspection platform or a hoist with equal facilities
 b. Brake testing equipment
 c. Head lamps testing equipment
 d. Mechanical tools such as screw drivers, spanners, hammers etc, required for checking of under carriage, 
steering wheel functions , brake cables, etc and rusted parts.
ii. 
Staff
Persons having the following quali ﬁ cations should be employed in the garage 
as certifying ofﬁ cer
 a. Specially mastered the auto engineering subject or National Technical Diploma on motor mechanism 
or obtained Junior Technical ofﬁ cer certiﬁ cate with 05 years experience in the auto engineering ﬁ eld
 b. Followed and passed the four year course on motor mechanism at the Ceylon German Technical 
Training Institute (CGTTI) or any other equivalent qualiﬁ cations along with 08 years experience in the 
Auto engineering ﬁ eld.
2.  Following documents should be submitted with the applications. 
 a. Certi ﬁ ed copies of the relevant qualiﬁ cation certiﬁ cates of the certifying ofﬁ cers
 b. A bank receipt received on crediting of a non - refundable fee of Rs. 1,000/- to the A/C No.33965 of the 
Commissioner of Transport, Central Province.
 c. A sketched map depicting the speci ﬁ c situation of the garage for easy reaching by the inspection ofﬁ cer 
and the access road reachable from the town centre (summary of the road map)

IMPORTANT  NOTICE   REGARDING   PUBLICATION   OF  GAZETTE
THE Weekly issue of the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka  is normally published on 
Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public Holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately 
preceding the Friday. Thus the last date speci ﬁ ed for the receipt of notices for publication in the Gazette also 
varies depending on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.
The Schedule below shows the date of publication and the latest time by which notices should be 
received for publication in the respective weekly Gazette. All notices received out of times speci ﬁ ed below 
will not be published. Such notices will be returned to the sender by post for necessary amendment and return 
if publication is desired in a subsequent issue of the Gazette. It will be in the interest of all concerned if those 
desirous of ensuring the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that suf ﬁ cient time 
is allowed for postal  transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
The Government Printer accept payments of subscription for the Government Gazette.                
Note.—Payments for inserting Notices in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka 
will be received by the Government Printer.
