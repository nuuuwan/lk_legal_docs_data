constitution for Independent Group no. 01 
01ST  ChAPTER
1. The name of the party and power 
area
Should be renamed Bandarawela Municipal Council Independent Group No. 1 
Bandarawela Urban Council area is the power area.
2. Symbol and the Colour The symbol of that independent group is the clock and the colour is yellow. 
But the symbol and colour can be changed when needed.
3. Objective To submit nominations for the Bandarawela Municipal Council for the local 
government elections in the Bandarawela Municipal Council area, after gaining 
power, take action to educate the people living in the area, manage the health 
conditions, upgrade, uphold universal suffrage and equal rights for all by 
recognizing the fundamental rights of the people and carrying out all ancillary 
activities related to the implementation of that objective.
4. On the date of enactment Effective from 18th December 2018
5. Party membership Candidates contesting from the Independent Group No. 01 of the Bandarawela 
Municipal Council and any other Member coming into competitive power are 
recognized as members under this constitution and it must be acknowledged 
that every such member has accepted this Act and will act in accordance with it. 
5.1 Terms of membership Anyone who accepts membership, namely a candidate and/or member, must 
agree to the following :
(a) Recognition of the party's principles, objectives and code of conduct. 
(b) Supporting all the candidates nominated by the party and not supporting 
other candidates who are contesting against each candidate. 
(c) If elected as a member, follow the legitimate orders of the team leader, 
Obedience is following and following the decisions made by the team 
leader. 
(d) Treating the so-called independent group 01 as a party. 
(e) Failure to support various other political parties and/or non-activism 
support to any political party that opposes the above agreements. 
(f) Do not publicity criticize the team leader and/or an elected member, make 
the necessary criticisms at the group meeting and do not criticize in a 
way that breaks the concept of going outside.
(g) If a member is elected as a member he/she must agree to the decisions of 
the group leader in the house or at the meetings and not abstain from 
voting on the proposals of an opposing party or voting in a manner that 
would discredit the Independent Group.

6. Disciplinary powers (a) Any member who abuses, insults, threatens or assaults another member/
group leader or publicly criticizes group policies or supports another 
political party or issues statements that bring and/or defame group policy 
and collective results Do not engage in any activities that cause public 
disrepute. 
(b) Independent Group Management Committee or its members should not be 
abused, threatened or insulted.
(c) do not give political support to a member or member of another party or 
independent group and if the group gains power in the Bandarawela 
Municipal Council, support another party so as not to lose that power. 
(d) Every member should behave in an exemplary and dignified manner and 
always act in a manner that preserves the dignity of the Municipal Council. 
7. Management Committee Independent group and implementation of its policies a committee called the 
Management Committee should be set up to implement the policy formulation, 
implementation of regulations and advising and that committee should be 
composed of eleven members. 
8. Composition of the Management 
Committee
Of the Management Committee it should consist of a teacher, a lawyer, a business 
professional, a retired civil servant and six other ordinary people and the group 
leader should be the head of the management committee. 
The committee is also the disciplinary authority. 
9. Management Committee 
Decisions
The decision of the management committee is final and at a critical juncture the 
team leader must have a decisive vote in addition to his own. 
10. Powers of the Management 
Committee
I. Preparation of nominations required for an election.
II. Arranging to contest an election.
III. Amendments to the Constitution, Imposition of Rules, Compilation of 
Standing Orders. 
IV . Taking Disciplinary action of the Members.
V . Carry out all activities to implement the objectives of the party/independent 
group. 
11. Quota The quota of the management committee should not be less than 1/4 of the 
number of members. 
12. Leader of the group The Group leader is the chief of the group, The decision he makes legally 
becomes final, subject to the approval of the committee. 
13. Funds There should be not funding for Independent Group No. 01 and no member 
has the right to, raise funds. But a fund called the Members' Forum Fund 
can be started from the salaries of the elected members and deposited in the 
recognized bank. From that fund, money can be spent and invested according to 
the agreement of the group and it should be done in a very transparent manner. 
The group leader and one other member are authorized to activate the account.

The group leader should be the mayor and the appointment of the Vice mayor

In the event of death, resignation, or due to another action, the Vice Mayor

16. Membership and Loss of 
membership
Groups that act in a manner that is intentional to the detriment of the objectives 
of the constitution, act contrary to the decision of the group leader and act outside 
the group spirit, approving a motion by another party or another 
Disciplinary action should be taken against those who make statements and 
statements that harm the existence of an independent group by party during a

provided for in this Constitution, the Management Committee may, by means of 
the law recognized in the country, formulate the necessary rules, which shall be 
passed by a majority vote of the members of the Management Committee. Since 
every member is bound to accept any such rule as part of this Constitution, no 
member shall have the right to question it.
