IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 03rd December, 2010 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 19th
November, 2010.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.

WITHIN  THE  SABARAGAMUWA   PROVINCE
BY virtue of powers vested in me under Sub section (3) of Section 52 of the Amendment Act, No. 02 of 1978 of the Children and Young
Persons Ordinance No. 48 of 1939 and under the Provisions of Section 2(1)“a ” of Provincial Council (Consequential Provisions) Act,
No. 12 of 1989, I, Gonagala Manannalage Karunapala, Minister of Probation and Child Care Services of Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council,
do hereby notify that a Remand Home was establish at Buduherassagama Village, Muruththettuwa in Batangala Grama Niladhari Division
of No. 123 in Dehiowita Divisional Secretariat Division in Kegalle District of Sabaragamuwa Province and it is maintained as a Remand
Home with effect from 22nd day of July, 2010.

Notices Calling for Tenders
MINISTRY OF HEALTH, INDIGENOUS MEDICINE, SOCIAL WELFARE,
PROBATION AND CHILD CARE  SERVICES
Department of Health Services
WASHING DIRTY CLOTHS IN HOSPITALS AT KANDY , MATALE, NUWARA ELIYA  DISTRICT
HEALTH SERVICES DIRECTORS DIVISION - 2011
QUALIFICATIONS for Bidders .- Sealed bids are received by Department  Health Services Central Province from Sri Lankans and Sri
Lankan firms for washing dirty clothes for the institutions, stipulated in the Column 1 of the Schedule 1, 2, 3, annexed herewi th during
the period of 01st of January 2011 to  31st of  December 2011.  (including these two days)
(i) Bid Form Charges and the Deposit :-
Relevant bid document could be obtained from the Office of the Provincial Director of Health Services, No. 163, Sangaraja
Mawatha, Kandy on payment  of deposit mentioned in Column 2 of Sub-schedule 1, 2, 3 for approved Rural Development
Societies, Registered Co-operative Societies and other approved societies could be obtained without the deposit fees. Such
societies should prove their registration.
Approved  Societies :-
1. Samurdhi Balakaya,
2. Multi Purpose Co-operative Societies,
3. Rural Development Societies,
4. Registered Farmers Organization.
(ii) Bid Documents :-
Relevant bid document could be obtained till 2.30 p.m., 22nd of December 2010. When apply for bid forms they should
have submit with the receipt of deposit fee.
2. Minimum Number of pieces should have washed for one month is annexed herewith in Column 5 of attached Sub-schedule.
Price should be mentioned for large (more than 2 meters), Medium (in between 1 meter to 2 meter), small (less than one meter) c lothes
separately. Should wash cloths at least 3 times per month.
3. Forwarding Bids.– All tenders should be  forwarded in two copies, each copy should be enclosed in separate sealed envelopes
and send both the copies in one cover to the Provincial Director of Health Services, P. O. Box 163, Sangaraja Mawatha, Kandy. The cover
enclosing  the tender should bare at the top left corner "Washing  Dirty Cloths - 2011 and the name of  the Institution" at the  bottom left
corner the name and the address of the tenderer should be written clearly.
4. Closing Date for Accepting Bids.– All tenderes would be accepted 23rd of December 2010 at 10.30 a.m. Tender application
will be opened 11.00 a.m. on the same date.
5. Opening of Bids :- Bidder or an authorized representative could be present when opening tenders.
Validity period of the prize is 31st of December  2011. Prizes could not be changed.
Security Deposit .- The selected tenders will be required to sign an agreement  after deposit the security fee in Column 3 of
Sub-schedule.
8. Tender Conditions .- Prospective tenderers should study the tender conditions on the reverse of the tender forms and comply
with requirement therein. Bidders should note the agreement in the tender form.
9. Other Details :-
(i) Delivery charges for transporting dirty clothes to remove germ. Any charge should not be paid if transporting dirty
clothes before washing for remove germs. If you will wash clothes inside the hospital premises, contractor should have to
pay for storage facilities, water and electricity, Medical Officer in - Charge of that hospital will be asses the charges.
(ii) Bidders could be applying for a part or full of the service in a hospital.
