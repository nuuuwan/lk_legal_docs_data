IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
Notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 27th August, 2010 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 13th
August, 2010.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.

Other requirements  :
(i) Applicant, should have obtained the certificate of competence for all vehicles issued by the commissioner of Motor

(ii) Should have 3 years driving experience after obtaining the driving license. A good experience for driving tractors (with the

(iii) Should be at least 5 feet in height ;
(iv) Should be of sound physical condition and good eye sight to serve both during day and night ; and

Terms of the Engagement :
1. This post is permanent and pensionable.
2. This appointment is subject to 3 years probationary period.

Code, Financial regulations of Republic of Sri Lanka, Departmental orders and the orders which may be, from time to
time issued by the Western Provincial Council and Peliyagoda Urban Council.
Other requirements  :
1. Applicant should be a citizen of Sri Lanka either by descant or by registration.
2. Should be of excellent moral character and sound physical condition.
3. Residence within the Western Province for a period of immediate 03 years as at the closing date of the application should
be established.
4. Age should be not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years as at the closing date of the application. This upper age
limit will not be applicable to those who are already employed in the Public or Provincial Public Service.
5. Should not be a person convicted in a count under the Penal Code or dismissed from the Public or Provincial Public
Service.
6. Secretary of the Peliyagoda Urban Council reserves right to delay, change this recruitment or cancel or amend this notice
after calling or while such applications are being called.
