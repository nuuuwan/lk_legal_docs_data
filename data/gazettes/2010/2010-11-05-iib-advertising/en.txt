IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette of 12th November, 2010 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 29th
October, 2010.
LAKSHMAN  GOONEWARDENA,
Government Printer.
Department of Govt. Printing,
Colombo 08,
January 01, 2010.

INVITATION  FOR  BIDS
Ministry of Economic Development
Department of National Botanical Garden
FUNCTIONING OF  THE CAFETARIA OF THE ROYAL
BOTANIC GARDENS, PERADENIYA 2010-2011
 Sealed Bids are Invited from the qualified bidders to select a
suitable person to functioning the cafeteria situated in the Royal
Botanical Garden which belongs to the Department of National
Botanical Garden for a period of one year from 2010-2011.
2. Sealed Bids will be received up to 10.00 a.m. on 26th
November 2010 and bids will be opened as soon as it is closed.
Bidders or his authorized reprentative is permitted to be present at
the time of the opening of the tenders.
3. The bidding documents and other particulars can be obtianed
from the Head Office of Department of National Botanical Garden
No. 1094, Sirimavo Bandaranayake Mawatha, Peradeniya during
office hours up to 24th November, 2010. The renderers should
deposit a non refundable fee of Rs.5000 for this purpose and submit
the receipt general 172 for it.
4. Two copies of the bidding document will be issued to the
tenderness to submit their tenders. The forms should be duly filled
and marked original and copy and  submit in two separate envelopes.
The envelopes should be sealed in place where is can be opened or
place the rubber stamp compulsorily the words “function of the
cafeteria” should be written on the top left hand corner of the
envelop.
5. The sealed bids should be send by registered post to reach the
Chairman, Procurement Committee (Major), Department of National
Botanic Gardens, P. O.Box 14, Peradeniya or be deposited in the
Tender box provided at this Department. The tenders which are not
received in time will be rejected.
6. A Bid Security Rs,200,000  valid for 90 days should be obtained
from a reputed bank or an Insurance Institute and submit it with the
bidding documents.
7. The Procurement Committee (Major) of the Department
reserves to themselves the right of selecting the suitable tender.
Intervening the selection of the tender will be treated as a
disqualification. The Director General of the Department of Botanic
Gardens reserves the right of taking the final decision regarding a
reason which is not included in the above notification or in the
tenders. If required further details may be obtained from the Director
(Finance) of the Department over the telephone No. 081-2388654.
Chairman,
