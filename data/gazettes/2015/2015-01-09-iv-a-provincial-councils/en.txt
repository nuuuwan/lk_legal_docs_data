publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of  30th January 2015 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on

By - Law on parking of Three Wheelers in Kolonnawa Urban Council
1. These By - Laws may be cited as By - Laws of the Kolonnawa Urban Council relating to parking of Three -
Wheelers.
2. These By - Laws shall be enacted for the purpose of regulating, controlling and levying parking charges of
Three Wheelers, within the jurisdiction of the Urban council.
3. (1) there shall be a committee that shall be herein after referred to as “Control Committee” consisting of the
persons referred in the schedule one in order to control the Three - Wheelers.
(2) A meeting of the control committee shall be held at least for every 06 months and the special meetings
of control committee shall be held at any occasion, deemed appropriate by the chairman.
(3) Making necessary actions to receive the calling notification for its members prior to 04 days of the
control committee meeting shall be the duty of the chairman.
(4) Reports shall be maintained on the decisions taken in control committee meetings and the approval
should be taken for the recommendations of the said committee.
(5) The Quorum of the control committee shall be one third of its members.
(6) The role mentioned in the first schedule, part II shall be the role of the control committee.
(7) The chair of the control committee meetings shall be presided by the chairman of the Urban Council.
(8) The secretary of the Urban Council shall be the secretary ofthe control committee.
4. (1) Allowing the places mentioned in the column I of the second schedule of this By - Law, herein after
referred to as “Parking Places” to be used as three wheeler parks and allowing to park a number of Three
Wheelers at the relevant parking place similar to the maximum number of Three Wheelers mentioned in
the column II of the said schedule entitled to be parked at each parking place shall be legal.
(2) Subject to the recommendations of the control committee, parking places mentioned in the column I and
the maximum number of Three Wheelers mentioned in the column II in second schedule shall be determined
by the Council time to time by a resolution passed by the Council.
(3) If an amendment is needed for the By - Law, it may be done by a resolution adopted on the
recommendations of the control Committee. Accordingly, such changes, amendments and removals shall
come in to operation from the date of publication of them in the Gazette or from a date specified therein.
(4) Displaying the maximum number of Three Wheelers that can be parked at the respective park on a notice
board clearly in all three languages shall be the duty of the secretary of Council.
(5) The procedure of parking Three Wheelers in each place shall be decided from time to time by the control
committee. Reserving the space which shows the parking procedure and marking them with 4 inches
wide white lines shall be the duty of the secretary of the Council.
5. (1) No Three Wheeler shall be parked at any parking place without a valid license issued by the chairman of
the council or an Officer authorized by him.
(2) No Three Wheeler shall be parked in any other parking place other than a parking place prescribed in the
permit issued for the Three Wheeler.
(3) No Three Wheeler shall be parked at a public place awaiting hires other than in the relevant parking
place.
(4) No vehicle other than a motor vehicle shall be parked at any parking place.
