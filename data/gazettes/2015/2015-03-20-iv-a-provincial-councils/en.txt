IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notification appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of accept ance
of Notices for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes  shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of
publication. All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times
of applications in respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be  prepared
by giving adequate time both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enab ling those
interested in the contents of such notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints mad e in respect
of corrections pertaining to notification will not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e.
notices for publication in the weekly Gazette  of 10th April, 2015 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on
27th March, 2015.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
“Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order , by-law, notification or other matter
shall be published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisfied if such rule, regulation, order , by-law,
notification or other matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”
W. A. A. G. FONSEKA,

RECRUITMENT  FOR  THE  POST  OF ANALYST  TO  CENTRAL  PROVINCIAL  PUBLIC  SERVICE
(KANDY  MUNICIPAL  COUNCIL)
01. Applications are invited from the qualified candidates to fill the vacancies in the post of Analyst to Central Provincial Pu blic
Service (Kandy Municipal Council).
02. Applications should be prepared in accordance with the specimen application form given at the end of this notification. Duly
perfected applications should be posted under registered post, to reach the Secretary, Central Provincial Public Service Commission,
No. 244, Katugastota Road, Kandy, on or before 16.04.2015. The caption “Recruitment for the post of analyst to Central
Provincial Public Service (Kandy Municipal Council)” should be indicated on the top left hand corner of the envelope enclosing
the application.
03. Terms of Employment
(i) This post is permanent and Pensionable and subject to 03 years of probation period.
(ii) The appointment shall be subject to the General conditions applicable to the appointments to the posts in Central
Provincial Public Service and to the terms and conditions of the scheme of recruitment and Promotion of Central
Provincial Analysts (Kandy Municipal Council) and any amendments hereinafter made thereto and provisions indicated
in Establishments Code and Financial Regulations.
04. Salary Scale
In terms of Public Administration Circular 06/2006, Salary Code is SL 1-2006 A
Salary Scale: Rs. 22,935- 10 x 645 - 8x790, Rs. 35,705/-
05. General conditions
(i) Shall be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
(ii) Shall possess a continues permanent residence for a period of not less than 03 years within Central Province by
the closing date of application.
(iii) Shall be not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years of age on the closing date of applications (The
maximum age limit shall not be applicable for the candidates who are already in Public or Provincial Public
Service.).
06. Educational and Professional Qualifications
(i) A First Class or Second Class Honors Degree for Chemistry from a recognized University
or
(ii) A member of the Royal Institute of Chemistry in Great Britain and Ireland
or
(iii) Higher academic or professional qualifications in Chemistry
07. Method of application
(a) Applications should be prepared using both side of a A4 size paper, in accordance with the specimen application
form given at the end of this notification. The application can be downloaded from the Central Provincial Public
Service Commission’s website (
www.psc.cp.gov.lk).
(b) The signature of the applicant should be attested by a Principal of a Government School, Justice of Peace, Commissioner
of Oaths, an Attorney-at-Law, a Notary Public, Commissioned Officer of the Army, Navy or Air Force, Gazetted
Officer of the Police, or an officer who holds a permanent post in Government or Provincial Public Service with an
annual salary of Rs. 240,360/= or more.
(c) Applicants who are already in public service or in the Provincial Public Service should forward the applications
through the Head of Department. If failed to do, their applications will be rejected.
(d) Applications not conform to specimen and incomplete will be rejected without any notice. No complain will be taken
into consideration in relation to loosing an application in posting or being late.

08. Mode of recruitment
Recruitment will be made under the merit of marks obtained in the interview held to ascertain the qualifications of the candidates.
09. Penalty for Furnishing False Information
Care should be taken to furnish correct information in filling the application form. If a candidate is found to be ineligible, his
candidature is liable to be cancelled at any stage prior to during or after the examination. If it is revealed that any of the particulars
a candidate has furnished is false and has been furnished deliberately knowing it is false, or else he has deliberately suppres sed
any important fact, he/she may be liable to be dismissed from service.
10. The Public Service Commission will make the final decision of any matter not covered by these regulations and decision on fi lling
the vacancies, leave them vacant or filling a portion of vacancies.
