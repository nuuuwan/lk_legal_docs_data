04.  Qualifications Required :
 04.01. Required qualifications for the Post of Driver 
Basic Qualifications :
 (i) Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
 (ii) Should establish most immediate 03 years of permanent residency within the authority areas of Mahanuwara 
Kadawath Sathara and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha to the closing date the applications are 
accepted. (if sufficient applications not found in authority areas, only the adjoining Local Government 
Institution residents will be considered) 
 (iii) The age limit is not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years at the closing date of applications. 
(Upper age limit will not applicable to the applicants those who are presently employed permanently in 
Government institutions).
 (iv) Should have an excellent moral character.
 (v) Should possess a sound knowledge in Highway Code.
 (vi) All the qualifications required to the recruitment should be completed at the closing date of application. 
Educational Qualifications :
 (i) Should have passed minimum six subjects, in not more than two sittings in the General Certificate of 
Education Ordinary Level Examinations, with minimum 02 Credit passes. Out of that, 05 subjects should 
be passed in same sitting.
Professional Qualifications :
 (i) Should possess Grade D driving license and G 1 driving license issued by the Commissioner General of 
Motor Traffic. (Possessing J Grade driving license is treated as additional qualifications)
Physical Qualifications :
 (i) Should have an excellent eye sight and physically sound.
 (ii) Minimum height 05 feet and 02 ½ inches.
Experience :
 (i) Shall count minimum three years experience after obtaining driving license. (Experience should be proven 
by certificates)
 04.02. Qualifications for Sanitary Labourers and Work/ Field Labourers
Basic Qualifications :
 (i) Should be a citizen of Sri Lanka with excellent moral character.
 (ii) Should establish most immediate 03 years of permanent residency within the authority areas of Mahanuwara 
Kadawath Sathara and Gangawata Korale Pradeshiya Sabha to the closing date the applicants are accepted. 
(if sufficient applications not found in authority areas, only the adjoining Local Government Institution 
residents will be considered) 
 (iii) The age limits is not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years at the closing date of applications. 
(Upper age limit will not applicable to the applicants those who are presently employed permanently in 
Government institutions).

05. Method of Recruitment :
 05.01. The applicants, who possess all the qualifications required for recruitment, will be called for a structural 
interview. The date and time of the interview will be notified later. Applicants should be kept ready with 
following certificates to forward in the interview, with photo copies.
 (i) First copy of Birth Certificate
 (ii) National Identity Card 
 (iii) Driving License for the post of Driver
 (iv) First copies of Educational Qualifications
 (v) School Leaving Certificates
 (vi) A recent certificate from the Grama Niladhari
 (vii) Two recent character certificates
 (viii) Certificate to prove the Professional competencies
 (ix) Service certificates and Lettes
 05.02. The most qualified for the post Sanitary/ Labourers and Work/ Field Labourers the highest scorer of 
merit from the structured interview shall be selected and enlisted.
 05.03. All the applicants who faced the structural interview for the post of driver, shall be subjected to a 
written test. The highest scorers from the written examination shall subjected to a trade test, and the 
most qualified in the trade test shall be selected under merit and enlisted.
