Recruitment for the Post of Labourer in the Textiles Department 
appLICatIONS are hereby called from suitable candidates for recruitment for the post of Labourer in the textiles department, 
Central province. 
01. General Terms :
 1.1 Applicant must be a citizens of Sri Lanka, 
 1.2 applicant must be a resident within the Central province,
 1.3 must have permanent and continuous residence within the Central province for a minimum period of 
three years, during the immediate preceding 03 years, before the date of meeting qualifications, 
 1.4 applicant must be of excellent character, 
 1.5 Must have met the qualifications required for the recruitment for the post as at the closing date of 
applications, which is 30.09.2018.
02. Age limit.- must be between the ages form 18 to 45 as at 30.09.2018.
03. Educational Qualifications.- must have passed six subjects with minimum of 02 credits, at the G. C. e. Ordinary 
Level examination. (as per the letter No. eSt-2/ReCRU/02/0365, dating 31.12.2013 of the director General-establishment).
04. Physical Qualifications.- every applicant must be adequately suitable both physically and mentally to serve in 
any part within the Central province and to hold the responsibilities of the position. 
05. Method of Recruitment.- Suitable candidates shall be chosen through a structured interview. Recruitments shall 
be made based on the number of vacancies, from the candidates who have obtained highest marks at the interview. Candidates 
who are already integrated to the service in the department will be given preference due to the nature of the job. 
06. Number of vacancies.- Number of Vacancies  -12. Recruitments intended during the first phase - 06
Recrutimet intended during the second phase - 06.
07. Salary scale assigned for the position.- pL 1-2016 salary scale, as per in pa Circular 03/2016. Rs. 24,250 -10x250 
-10x270 -10x300 -12x330 - Rs. 36,410.
08. Conditions of employment.- this position is permanent and pensionable. Other terms and conditions shall be 
determined according to the service minute relating to the post. 
09. Nature of employment.- dying and laundering of cloths, including duties at dying centers. 
10. Method of application :
10.1 applications must be prepared on a4 size paper using both sides of it, in accordance with the specimen 
application, where items 1 to 6 should appear on page No. 1 and items No. 7 to 11 should appear on page No. 2. 
duly completed applications must be forwarded to the address of "Secretary, ministry of Sports and Industries, 
p. O. box 41, Getambe, peradeniya" before 04.10.2018, in registered post. must write, "Recruitment of the 
post of Labourer in the textiles department, Central province" on top left hand side corner of the envelope. 
(applicants whose are already employed in the textiles department must submit their applications through 
their department Head).
10.2 Prospect for the appointment will be cancelled in the event of any applicant found not to have fulfilled the
