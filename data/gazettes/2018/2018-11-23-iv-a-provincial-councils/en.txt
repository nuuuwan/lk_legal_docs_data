aPPLICaTIoNS are hereby invited from the permanent residents of the North Western Province (male / Female) who have 
completed a Higher National Diploma Course in English Language (Full time / Part time) offered by the Sri Lanka Institute of 
Advanced Technical Education (SLIATE) for recruitment to the posts of the Grade 3-1 (C) of the Service Minute of Sri Lanka 
Teachers’ Service to fill the English language teaching vacancies existing in the North Western Provincial Council Schools. 
01. The number of recruitments will be decided on the basis of service requirements and available financial allocations. 
02. eligibility:
 1.1  General Qualifications:
 I. Applicant should be a Citizen of Sri Lanka. 
 II. applicant should possess an excellent moral character and he / she should be in good health condition. 
 III. age should be not less than 18 years and not more than 40 years as at closing date of applications.  
 Iv. applicant should be a permanent resident of the territory of the North Western Provincial Council for a period 
not less than three (03) years preceding to the closing date of applications. (Residency should be proved by 
a Residence Certificate issued by the Grama Niladhari and counter signed by the Divisional Secretary) 
 V . Every applicant should be mentally and physically fit to serve in any area of the North Western Province. 
 VI. All qualifications for recruitment are required to be completed by the closing date of applications i.e. 
31.12.2018.
       2:2   Educational Qualifications :
     The qualifications mentioned below (1) (2) and (3) should have been obtained. 
  
 (1) A Higher National Diploma in English (Full time / Part time) offered by the Sri Lanka Institute of 
advanced Technical education (SLIaTe). 
 (2) One of the qualifications mentioned below I,II,III should have been obtained.  
   I. Should have passed three (03) subjects in one sitting at the G.C.e. (advanced Level) exam-
ination including english Language. 
       or
   II.  Should have passed three (03) subjects in one sitting at the G.C.e. (advanced Level) exam-
ination and an ordinary Pass for english Literature and a Credit Pass for english Language 
at the G.C.e. (ordinary Level) examination.  
       or
   III. Should have passed three (03) subjects in one sitting at the G.C.e. (advanced Level) exam-
ination and a distinction for english Language at the G.C.e. (ordinary Level) examination.

03.    Service conditions :
Selected applicants will be appointed to the posts of Grade 3-1 (C) of the Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service of the North 
Western Provincial Council public service subject to the general regulations governing the appointments to the public 
/provincial public service, conditions stipulated by the North Western Provincial Teachers’ Service minute and the 
amendments to same which have been made already or will be made in future. 
3.1    Salary Scale: 
Salary scale applicable to this post according to Ge 1-2016 of the Schedule I of the Public administration 
Circular No. 03/2016 dated 25.02.2016 is Rs. 27,740 – 6x300 – 7x380 – 2x445 – 33,090/-. (Salary will be paid 
according to the Schedule II until 01.01.2020)  
1.2 This appointment is permanent. The pension scheme applicable to this post will be subject to a policy decision 
taken by the Government in future. 
1.3 Promotions will be done according to the provisions of the Sri Lanka Teachers’     Service Minute of the North 
Western Provincial Council Public Service. 
1.4 appointees should serve at least six (06) years in the district of initial appointment. Further appointees are 
bound to serve in a very difficult/ difficult school at least for five (05) years within their total term of office, in 
case his/ her school of first appointment is not a very difficult/ difficult school.
1.5 The Second Language competency level applicable to the post according to the Public administration circular 
No. 01/2014 dated 21.01.2014 and its amendments, should be obtained within five (05) years from the date of 
appointment. 
1.6 In addition to the Scheme of Recruitment of this post, appointees should abide by the regulations of the estab-
lishment Code of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Financial Regulations, Financial Regulations 
Code of the North Western Provincial Council, disciplinary Code of the North Western Provincial Council 
Financial, Procedural Rules of the North Western Provincial Council, departmental orders and other regulations 
imposed by the Government and North Western Provincial Council.  
04. Recruitment :
a. Method of Recruitment :
 I. Qualified English Diploma holders will be recruited to fill the English teaching vacancies existing in the 
North Western Provincial Council Schools.  
 II. Recruitment will be done on district basis. applicants from the Kurunegala district will be selected for the 
english language teaching vacancies existing in the Provincial council schools in the Kurunegala district and 
applicants from the Puttlam district will be selected for the english language teaching vacancies existing in 
the Provincial council schools in the Puttlam district.

06.  method of Selection:
 (a)    applicants equal to triple the number of english teaching vacancies and who have passed both subjects of the 
written examination, permanent residents of each district will be called for the interview in order of merit.
(B)  Qualified applicants of two times the number of English language teaching vacancies in order of merit according 
to the total marks obtained at the written examination and the interview, will be called for the practical test. 
(C)     Priority lists for each district will be prepared according to the total marks obtained at the written test, interview 
and the practical test, and highest qualified applicants will be selected for appointments. 
(D)  Although all the qualified applicants of a particular district have been selected and still the vacancies are 
available, appointments will be given from the priority list of the other district. 
   
07. examination fees :-
a sum of Rs. 600/- being the examination fee should be paid to the divisional Secretariat of the area of permanent 
residence to be credited to Revenue Head 2003-02-13 and the receipt NWPC /2 obtained from the divisional 
Secretariat should be affixed to the application. Receipts other than the receipts obtained from the Divisional 
Secretariat or Money Orders will not be accepted. (please keep a photocopy of the receipt for future reference). It 
should be noted that the examination fee is non-refundable under any circumstance and it will not be allowed to 
transfer the examination fees paid to any other examination.  
08.  method of Application:- 
 I. applications should be prepared using a4 (21x29 cm) papers according to the specimen given at the end 
of this notification.  
 II. applications that do not comply with the specimen application form, which are incomplete, applications 
of the candidates who have not fulfilled the required qualifications will be rejected without notice. Further, 
the applicants should check whether the application form complies with the specimen application form.  
It would be useful to keep a copy of the completed application with the applicant. Receipt of applications 
will not be acknowledged.
