APPLICATIONS are hereby invited from among those persons who are permanent residents of the Y atinuwara Pradeshiya

mentioned in the Gazette notification. 
Age.– Age limits is not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years.

skills in a trade test, among those who scored highest marks in the written examination, who possess all qualifications 
required.
