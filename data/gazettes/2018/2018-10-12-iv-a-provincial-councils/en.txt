by - laws relating to crematorium
Name of the by law 1 the by-laws relating to regularize, control and charge fees on the crematorium.
Preamble 2 these by laws consist of provisions relating to regularize, control and charge fees on the 
crematorium for protection of convenience and welfare and all facilities of the people of 
the administrative limit.
Objective 3 the objective of the enactment of these by laws is to regularize, control and administration 
of all relevant conditions including convenience and welfare of the people in the area.
Legal provisions for 
drafting the by law
4  by virtue of powers vested in with the Pradeshiya Sabha under section 122(1) of the 
Pradeshiya Sabha act, No. 15 of 1987, these by-law have been drafted.
Functions to be 
discharged
5
a
The charges for the cremation of a dead body shall be levied under the classification set 
out in the first schedule of this part.
b While it is possible to determine by resolution approved by the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya 
Sabha the charges for the cremation of a dead body, it shall be amended from time to time 
on such approved of a resolution.
c While charges levied or amended from time to time under paragraph (a) above shall be 
published in the Gazette, it shall be prominently displayed at the place where applications 
are received for booking the crematorium and at the crematorium.
6 anybody expecting to obtain a licence mentioned  in bylaw (hereinafter referred to as 
''the applicant'') shall forward an application substantially prepared in accordance with 
the second schedule to this part together with the documents mentioned below to the 
Chairman or the Authorized Officer.
a   I.   the identity card of the applicant,
 II.   the birth certificate of the deceased (if available) or identity card,
III.   the certificate issued by the Grama Niladhari of the Division where the deceased
       was living.
       in order to facilitate the establishment of the relationship between the deceased and
       the applicant.

b crematorium shall be reserved only for the deaths submitted with following forms 
certified by the Inquirers - into - Sudden Deaths or Registrars of birth and Deaths under 
the regulations of the Registration of births and deaths Ordinance in respect of the death 
of the deceased.
  I.   B18 - Certificate of the Inquirer - into-Sudden Deaths (Section 39 (1),, 41(1)g)

 V .    B2 - The death certificate of the deceased.
7 In the event of accepting any dead body for cremation in accordance with the application 
forwarded, the applicant shall be issued with the licence by the chairman or the authorized 
Officer after charging the fees levied according to schedule No. 1. The time and the date 
on which the dead body due to be cremated according to the licence should be handed 
over to the Crematorium Keeper shall be clearly specified in the relevant licence.
8 In the event of a dead being not accepted for cremation due to some reason, the chairman 
or the Authorized Officer shall take steps to immediately inform the applicant in writing 
about it, specifying the reasons for such non-acceptance.
9 dead bodies shall be accepted for cremation and cremations shall be done between the 
period 7.00 a.m. to 7.00 p.m. on all days of the week.
10 In the event of many applications being forwarded for cremation on a same day, if a dead 
body is delivered to the crematorium after the time specified, action shall be taken to 
cremate first a dead body which hand over to the crematorium on time.
11 the dead body shall be handed over to the crematorium Keeper on or before the time 
specified in the licence issued. The Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha, the Chairman or the 
Authorized Officer shall not be subjected to responsibility in respect of any inconvenience 
or loss caused to the applicant or anybody else having rights over the deceased due to the 
failure to handover the dead body on time.
12 the crematorium Keeper shall maintain a Register substantially prepared in accordance 
with the third Schedule to this part, in regard to each dead body cremated in any 
crematorium.
13
a
The applicant or any authorized representative of him shall take possession of the ashes 
of the body before the elapse of seventy two hours after the cremation of any dead body.
b However, on a written request made by the applicant the chairman or the authorized 
Officer shall extend the period during which the ashes are kept with the Crematorium 
Keeper.
c The Period of extension for the ashes to be kept in the custody of the Crematorium Keeper 
under paragraph (b) shall not be more than two weeks from the date of the cremation.
d The Chairman or the Authorized Officer or the Crematorium Keeper shall have the power 
to dispose of the ashes that had not been removed even after the expiry of such period 
allowed for the removal of ashes. 
Functions not to be 
discharged
14
a
No dead body shall be cremated in any crematorium owned by the council without a 
license issued by the Chairman or an Authorized Officer.

in the 7th column of the Death Certificate of a deceased under ''cause of death and the 
place of the burial or cremation the Chairman or the Authorized Officer shall not accept 
the dead body for cremation or issue a license to cremate it.
15
a
Nobody other than the crematorium Keeper or his assistant shall enter in to covered 
section of the chamber of the crematorium after the closure of the security door of the 
chamber on introducing the dead body in the chamber of any crematorium.
b Nobody shall enter in to the premises of any crematorium without permission of the 
Chairman, Authorized Officer or the Crematorium Keeper.
c Notwithstanding anything stated in paragraph (b) :-
I.    any other person or persons who participate in the handing over of a dead body for 
cremation with the person who had obtained a license to cremate it or with his 
authorized representative; and,
II.    any other person or persons who arrive at the crematorium on the specified date or 
earlier to received ashes, with the person who had obtained the licence or with his 
authorized representative,
shall be considered as persons who have been allowed to enter the premises of the 
crematorium.
16 Within the premises of any crematorium nobody shall, :-
(a)  act in a manner disrupting peace ;
(b)  damage or try to damage property within the premises of the crematorium belonging 
to the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha ;
(c)  obstruct the official functions of the Crematorium Keeper or any of his Assistants;
(d)  decorate or paste notices within the premises of the crematorium.
(e)  If any damage is caused to any property, building of equipment by a person who 
reserve the crematorium, such person shall be agreed to pay the assessment value 
of the said damage ordered by the chairman.
17 No fee shall be charged for the cremation of a dead body of any chairman, Vice chairman, 
Member or employee who serves presently at the Sabha or any chairman, Vice chairman 
or Member who had served previously at the Sabha. 
Functions of the Local 
Government Institute
18 In the event of a decision being arrived at to issue a license on an application made for 
permission to cremate a dead body, it shall be the duty of the chairman or the authorized 
Officer to register the death in the sequence such applications are received in a Register 
Substantially prepared according to the Fourth  Schedule to this part. this Register shall 
be maintained at the Officer of the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya Sabha accepting applications.
19 In the event of a dead body being not accepted for cremation due to some reason the 
Chairman or the Authorized Officer of the Ruwanwella  Pradeshiya  Sabha shall take 
steps to immediately inform the applicant in writing about it, specifying the reason for 
such non-acceptance.
20 It Shall be the duty of the Chairman or the Authorized Officer to make arrangement to bury 
the ashes not removed by any applicant or any authorized representative at the premises 
of the crematorium or in any cemetery.

21 It shall be the duty of the chairman to maintain and operate the crematoriums in conditions 
suitable for cremation of dead bodies.
22 In the event of the closer of any crematorium for the purpose of repairs or maintenance 
activities, it shall  be the duty of the chairman to display prominently a notice on it in all 
the three languages at the Office issuing licenses for cremation of dead bodies and  at the 
entrance to the crematorium premises.
23 even though a dead body has been delivered to the crematorium for cremation, the 
chairman has the power to refer the dead body to another crematorium for burial or 
cremation, if the dead  body shall be unable to cremate before or during of cremation on 
electric failure or technical faults of the machineries of the crematorium.
delegation of Power 24 It shall be lawful to delegate in writing the powers vested with the Pradeshiya Sabha in 
respect of regularize, supervise and control of the matters relating to these by laws to any 
officer of the Sabha by the chairman of it.
Investigation 
procedure and 
supervision
25 The authorized officer of the investigation shall be the Chairman or any Authorized 
Officer of the Sabha.
complaints and 
Remedial mechanism
26 In any instance of violation or breach of these by laws, any citizen can report or complaint 
about it to the Chairman or the relavant Officers in writing or verbally at any such instance. 
an investigation in respect of every such complaint shall be made by the chairman or 
the authorized officer of the Sabha.
Supervision 27 The Chairman or any officer authorized by him shall have the powers of implementation, 
examination and supervision of the provisions of these by-laws.
Violation of the by 
law
28 Violation of any provision or several provisions or all provisions of these by-laws shall 
be an offence.
Fines and penalty 29 Violation or noncompliance of any section of these by-laws shall be an offence and any 
person who violates any section in these by laws shall be guilty of an offence and on being 
convicted by a court of proper jurisdiction shall be liable to a fine set out in the Section 
122(2) of the Pradeshiya Sabha act., No. 15 of 1987 and in case of continuing the violation 
by any person even after being convicted by a court and being notified in writing by the 
Chairman or Secretary of the Pradeshiya Sabha or any other Officer authorized by the 
Chairman considering the violation, such person sall be liable to an extra fine set out in 
the Section 122(2) of the Pradeshiya Sabha act, No. 15 of 1987 for each day of violation.
Interpretations and 
definitions
30 In by - laws of this part, unless any other meaning is sought, -
"cremation chamber" means the chamber where the dead body is kept and the outer 
chamber constructed outside the main chamber ;
"crematorium premise" means the whole crematorium and the land on which it is located ;
"crematorium Keeper" means any employee appointed by the Ruwanwella Pradeshiya 
Sabha to be in-charge of the crematorium and its functions ;
"ashes" means any matter left behind after cremating a dead body ;
"dead body" means the entire dead body of a deceased person or the dead body of a still 
born person or any part of a dead body.
31 In the event of any inconsistency between the tamil and english texts of this by Law, 
the Sinhala text shall prevail.
