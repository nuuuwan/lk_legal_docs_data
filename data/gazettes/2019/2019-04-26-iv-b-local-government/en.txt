02. Age Limit.– Age of applicant should be not less than 18 years and not more than 45 years for the closing date of 
applications (Upper age limit is not applicable for the applicants currently work in the public or provincial public service).
03. General Qualifications :
 (i) Applicants should be Sri Lankan citizens either by decent or by registration.
 (ii) Applicants should be permanent residents of the Southern Province at least 03 consecutive years 
immediately before the closing date of applications. (It should be confirmed by voter registry or a certificate 
issued recently by Divisional Secretary confirming the residence).
 (iii) Applicant should be in moral character and should be in good health.
 (iv) Applicant should not have been convicted by any court under penal code. (should submit a Police 
Certificate to prove that the applicant has not been convicted for a criminal offence).
 (v) All qualifications required should have been completed as at the closing date prescribed in the application. 
(Priority will be given for those who reside within the jurisdiction of the Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha).
04. General Conditions :
 (i) The power to amend or cancel this notice or change or delay recruitment after or during the period of 
calling applications is vested in Hon. Chairman of the Habaraduwa Pradeshiya Sabha.
 (ii) Selected candidates should undergo government medical examination within 03 months of the recruitment. 
Appointment will be cancelled, if it is proved by the medical examination that the candidate is not 
physically and mentally fit for the appointed position. 
05. Conditions of Employment :
 (i) This is a permanent and pensionable position. Pension scheme, pertaining to the above position is subject 
to State Policy Decisions to be taken by the state in due course. Contributions to the W and OP fund should 
be made. 
 (ii) Selected candidates will be subjected a probationary period of three (03) years. Service will be confirmed 
at the end of the probationary period by considering the work, attendance and behavior within the said 
period.
06. Conditions of Recruitment :
 (i) In addition to the terms and conditions of the employment, you are abide by the rules and regulations of 
the Establishment Code and Financial Regulations of Sri Lanka, Departmental orders, Regulations and 
orders issued by the Southern Provincial Council and Southern Provincial Public Services Commission 
time to time.
