IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 08th March, 2019 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 22nd February, 2019.

OPEN COMPETITIVE EXAMINATION FOR RECRUITMENT OF DIPLOMA HOLDERS 
TO THE GRADE 3 – 1 (C) OF THE SRI LANKA TEACHERS’ SERVICE TO FILL THE             
V ACANCIES OF TEACHERS IN ENGLISH LANGUAGE OF THE PROVINCIAL 
COUNCIL SCHOOLS OF THE NORTH WESTERN PROVINCIAL COUNCIL ON 
DISTRICT BASIS - 2019
APPLICATIONS are invited from the males and females who are not less than 40 years of age and not more than 43 years 
of age, and  permanent residents of the North Western Province and who have successfully completed Higher National             
Diploma in  English ( full time/part time ) offered by Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE), 
to be recruited to the  posts of 3 – 1 (c ) of  Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service Minutes of  North Western Provincial Council Public 
Service,  to ﬁ ll the existing vacancies for  English Teachers  in the schools of North Western Provincial Council.
Note :- 
 The applicants who have already submitted their applications according to the  gazette notiﬁ cation for the recruitment 
for the above stated post published in Part 1V (a) in Gazette No. 2099 of 23.11.2018 shall not require to re-apply.
01. The number of recruitments will be decided on the basis of service requirements and available ﬁ nancial allocations. 
02. Eligibility:
 
 1.1  General Qualiﬁ cations :
 I. Applicant should be a Citizen of Sri Lanka. 
 II. Applicant should possess an excellent moral character and he / she should be in good health condition. 
 III. Applicant should be a permanent resident of the territory of the North Western Provincial Council for a 
period not less than three (03) years preceding to the closing date of applications. (Residency should be 
proved by a Residence Certi ﬁ cate issued by the Grama Niladhari and counter signed by the Divisional 
Secretary) 
 IV . Every applicant should be mentally and physically ﬁ t to serve in any area of the North Western Province. 
 V . All the quali ﬁ cations required to be recruited shall have completed by 31.12.2018.
       2:2   Educational Qualiﬁ cations:-
  The quali ﬁ cations mentioned below (1) (2) and (3) should have been obtained. 
  
 A Higher National Diploma in English (Full time / Part time) offered by the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced 
Technical Education (SLIATE)
 One of the qualiﬁ cations mentioned below I,II,III should have been obtained.

including English Language;

G.C.E. (Ordinary Level) Examination;

03.  Service Conditions:-
Selected applicants will be appointed to the posts of Grade 3-1 (C) of the Sri Lanka Teachers’ Service of the 
North Western Provincial Council public service subject to the general regulations governing the appointments 
to the public / provincial public service, conditions stipulated by the North Western Provincial Teachers’ Service 
Minute and the amendments to same which have been made already or will be made in future. 
3.1   Salary Scale: 
Salary scale applicable to this post according to GE 1-2016 of the Schedule I of the Public Administration 
Circular No. 03/2016 dated 25.02.2016 is Rs. 27,740 – 6x300 – 7x380 – 2x445 – Rs.33,090/-. (Salary will be 
paid according to the Schedule II until 01.01.2020) 
 This appointment is permanent. The pension scheme applicable to this post will be subject to a policy decision 
taken by the Government in future. 
 Promotions will be done according to the provisions of the Sri Lanka Teachers’   Service Minute of the North 
Western Provincial Council Public Service. 
 Appointees should serve at least six (06) years in the District of initial appointment. Further appointees are 
bound to serve in a very difﬁ cult/ difﬁ cult school at least for ﬁ ve (05) years within their total term of ofﬁ ce, in 
case his/ her school of ﬁ rst appointment is not a very difﬁ cult/ difﬁ cult school.
 The Second Language competency level applicable to the post according to the Public Administration circular 
No. 01/2014 dated 21.01.2014 and its amendments, should be obtained within ﬁ ve (05) years from the date of 
appointment. 
 In addition to the Scheme of Recruitment of this post, appointees should abide by the regulations of the                           
Establishment Code of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Financial Regulations, Financial Reg-
ulations Code of the North Western Provincial Council, Disciplinary Code of the North Western Provincial 
Council Financial, Procedural Rules of the North Western Provincial Council, Departmental Orders and other 
regulations imposed by the Government and North Western Provincial Council. 
 Recruitment :
a.
Method of Recruitment :
  Qualiﬁ ed English Diploma holders will be recruited to ﬁ ll the English Teaching vacancies existing in 
the North Western Provincial Council Schools. 
  Recruitment will be done on District basis. Applicants from the Kurunegala District will be selected for 
the English language teaching vacancies existing in the Provincial council schools in the Kurunegala 
District and applicants from the Puttlam District will be selected for the English language teaching                
vacancies existing in the Provincial Council Schools in the Puttlam District.

 4.4   Practical Test (15 Marks) 
The ability to expression of ideas and communication skills of the applicants in relation to teaching and learning 
process which are important for teaching profession will be especially considered.  Applicants should make a                            
presentation of not more than ten (10) minutes in English medium on a topic selected by the applicant. 
 Method of Selection :
 (A)    Applicants equal to triple the number of English teaching vacancies and who have passed both subjects 
of the written examination, permanent residents of each district will be called for the interview in order of 
merit.
 (B) Quali ﬁ ed applicants of two times the number of English language teaching vacancies in order of merit 
according to the total marks obtained at the written examination and the interview, will be called for the 
practical test. 
 (C)    Priority lists for each district will be prepared according to the total marks obtained at the written test, 
interview and the practical test, and highest qualiﬁ ed applicants will be selected for appointments. 
 (D) Although all the quali ﬁ ed applicants of a particular district have been selected and still the vacancies are 
available, appointments will be given from the priority list of the other district. 
 Examination Fees :
A sum of Rs. 600/- being the examination fee should be paid to the Divisional Secretariat of the area of per-
manent residence to be credited to Revenue Head 2003-02-13 and the receipt NWPC  uq /2 obtained from the                                         
Divisional   Secretariat should be af ﬁ xed to the application. Receipts other than the receipts obtained from the 
Divisional Secretariat or Money Orders will not be accepted. (please keep a photocopy of the receipt for future 
reference). It should be noted that the examination fee is non-refundable under any circumstance and it will not be 
allowed to transfer the examination fees paid to any other examination.  
07.  Method of Application :
  Applications should be prepared using A4 (21x29 cm) papers according to the specimen given at the end 
of this notiﬁ cation. 
  Applications that do not comply with the specimen application form, which are incomplete, applications 
of the candidates who have not fulﬁ lled the required qualiﬁ cations will be rejected without notice. Further, 
the applicants should check whether the application form complies with the specimen application form.  
It would be useful to keep a copy of the completed application with the applicant. Receipt of applications 
will not be acknowledged. 
  Attestation of the Signature of the Applicant:
The Signature of the applicant in the application form should be attested by a Principal of a Government 
School / a Justice of Peace / Commissioner of Oaths / Attorney-at-Law / Notary Public / Commissioned 
Ofﬁ cer in the Armed Forces / an ofﬁ cer holding a gazetted post in the Police Service or an ofﬁ cer holding 
a permanent staff grade post in the Public / Provincial Public Service whose initial basic salary is above 
Rs. 32,960/-.
