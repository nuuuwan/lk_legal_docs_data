IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 14th June, 2019 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 31st May, 2019.
Electronic Transactions Act, No. 19 of 2006 - Section 9
 “Where any Act or enactment provides that any Proclamation, rule, regulation, order, by-law, noti ﬁ cation or other matter shall be 
published in the Gazette, then such requirement shall be deemed to have been satisﬁ ed if such rule, regulation, order, by-law, notiﬁ cation or other 
matter is published in an electronic form of the Gazette.”

Recruitment to the Post of Provincial Commissioner of Revenue of the Provincial Public Service in the 
North Western Provincial Council - 2019
(Executive Service Category Grade I)
APPLICATIONS are called from the eligible Male/Female Sri Lankans for the post of Provincial Commissioner of Revenue 
for the Provincial Public Service in the North Western Provincial Council by order of the Hon. Governor of the North 
Western Province.
01. 
 Name of the Post    :    Provincial Commissioner of Revenue (Executive Service Category Grade I)
02.  Service Conditions  :  I This Post is Permanent and  Pensionable.
 II The post must be subjected to general conditions of recruitment of the Government               
Service Post stipulated in Section II “A” in the Part I of the Gazette Notiﬁ cation of the 
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
03. Salary Category and Salary Scale : SL 1 - 2016
  Rs. 47,615 – 10 x 1335 – 8 x 1630 – 17 x 2170 – 110895
  However, Salary will be paid in terms of provisions of Schedule II of Public Administration 
Circular No. 3/2016
04. Qualiﬁ cations :
The applicant should have completed 20 years service period in Executive Service category date of calling 
applications and, should have completed minimum 03 years satisfactory service period in the Executive Service  
Category (Grade I) and a suitable Ofﬁ cers who have completed preceding 05 years satisfactory active service period 
as at closing date shall be appointed for Provincial Revenue Commissioner. 
05. 
Method of Recruitment :
A suitable person shall be appointed on the recommendation of an interview board appointed by the Governor of 
the North Western Province.
06. Marking Scheme of Interview :
