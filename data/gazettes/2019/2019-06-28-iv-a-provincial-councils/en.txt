IMPORTANT  NOTICE  REGARDING  ACCEPTANCE  OF  NOTICES 
FOR  PUBLICATION  IN  THE  WEEKLY  “GAZETTE”
ATTENTION is drawn to the Notiﬁ cation appearing in the 1st week of every month, regarding the latest dates and times of acceptance of Notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazettes, at the end of every weekly Gazette of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
 All Notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes shall close at 12.00 noon of each Friday, two weeks before the date of publication. 
All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Noti ﬁ cations ﬁ xing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-Vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving  adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such 
notices to actively and positively participate please note that inquiries and complaints made in respect of corrections pertaining to notiﬁ cation will 
not be entertained after three months from the date of publication.
 All notices to be published in the weekly Gazettes should reach Government Press two weeks before the date of publication i.e. notices 
for publication in the weekly Gazette of 19th July, 2019 should reach Government Press on or before 12.00 noon on 05th July, 2019.

By Law on Management of Areas for the training purpose of windsurﬁ ng and Kite surﬁ ng
 1.   This by law is imposed for making provisos in respect of the control and management of lagoon and coastal areas 
(hereinafter known as areas) around that lagoon which have been named as suitable areas for conducting training 
services of windsurﬁ ng and kite surﬁ ng by the Divisional Secretary within the area of authority of Kalpitiya under 
the control and management of Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya.
 2.  This By law is enacted under Section 122(1) to be read with Section 126 (vi) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 
1987.
 
 3.  This By law is cited as the By law on management of areas for the purpose of training of windsur ﬁ ng and kite surﬁ ng 
of the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya.
 4. (i)  No person or an institute shall engage in windsur ﬁ ng and kite surﬁ ng training at the areas relevant to this By law 
unless possessing a permit substantially issued by the Chairman under Schedule I of this By Law.
  (ii) The maximum number of permits that could be issued for a person in respect of a certain place maintained for 
the purpose of training shall be decided upon the extent of length meters of the place of relevant coastal belt.
  (iii) No person shall engage in training of trainees exceeding the permitted number of trainees for himself.
  (iv) In case trainers are engaged in the training activities every trainee in each institute shall wear different colored 
coats as advised by the Pradeshiya Sabha so as to recognize each training institute.
 5. (i)  Every person who applies for a permit under the aforesaid Section 4 shall apply for such permit by obtaining an 
application substantially prepared under Schedule II of this by law on payment of a fee to the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Kalpitiya.
  (ii) Prescribed fees to be paid for an application under this Section shall be decided and declared upon an adoption 
of resolution moved from time to time by the Pradeshiya Sabha.
  (iii) Prescribed fees under this Section shall be decided by the Sabha on the basis of an annual permit and considering 
the number of trainees for each permit.

 6.  Every permit issued under Section 4 of this By law, shall be ended on 31 st December of the particular year in which 
the permit is issued unless it is cancelled earlier on account of violation of conditions of this By law.
 7.  Every person who applies for a permit under this By Law shall have completed the following conditions.
  (i)  Should have been registered for the said purpose at the Sri Lanka Tourist Board.
  (ii)  The training should be conducted under quali ﬁ ed trainers who have obtained international training licenses for 
windsurﬁ ng and kite surﬁ ng and that should be veriﬁ ed by a valid training license.
  (iii)  In case a training center is maintained at the lagoon and coastal area, the relevant constructions shall be constructe d 
in accordance with a plan approved by the Department of Coastal Conservation and a copy of the approved plan 
should be annexed.
  (iv)  A report recommended by the Medical Ofﬁ cer of Health to the effect that sanitary facilities including First Aid facilities 
for the trainees have been properly maintained shall be submitted together with the relevant request.
 8.  The maximum number of permit to be issued in respect of identifying a coastal and a lagoon belt or for an identi ﬁ ed 
coastal and lagoon belt within this area of authority for training purposes shall be decided by a committee consisting 
Divisional Secretary, Secretary to the Pradeshiya Sabha, Manager in charge of the Division of Kalpitiya of the 
Department of Coastal Conservation, Assistant Director Fisheries, Regional Of ﬁ cer of the Tourism Development 
Authority and Deputy Director of Urban Development Authority upon the consent of District Lagoon Management 
Committee.
 9.  The duration of training under a permit shall be conducted from 9.00 to 06.00 hours and no person shall engage in 
training activities excluding such hours.
 10.  In case coastal line and lagoon line are utilized for the training purpose the coastal area speci ﬁ cally allocated for 
ﬁ sheries activities shall not be used.
 11.  Pradeshiya Sabha shall provide facilities for training activities conducted under this bylaw.
12.   Issue of a temporary permit substantially with the Schedule III of this by law by the Chairman for a time period decided 
by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya in respect of engaging in windsurﬁ ng and kite surﬁ ng at a certain part of Lagoon 
belt maintained by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya, under the agreement entered into between the Divisional Secretary 
Kalpitiya and the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya, shall be lawful and in order to obtain such permit an application prepared 
substantially with the Schedule IV of this By Law obtained by the Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya shall be forwarded.
13.  Fees to be levied for a permit under Section 12 shall be decided by the Pradeshiya Sabha and subsequently shall be 
published in the 
Gazette paper.
14.  Any person who resided within the area of authority of Pradeshiya Sabha Kalpitiya engages in windsur ﬁ ng and kite 
surﬁ ng at the coastal belt for entertainment shall not be relevant for the purpose under this by law.
15.  Violation of any provision of this by law shall be an offense and a maximum ﬁ ne may be imposed in case a person 
is found guilty of that offense by a proper judicial court and if such offense is further continued by the said person 
notwithstanding the handing over of a written noti ﬁ cation by the Chairman or any authorized of ﬁ cer stating the 
offense, an additional punishment shall be imposed for each day of violation and the maximum of ﬁ ne or maximum 
of additional punishment shall be imposed under Sub Section (2) of Section 122 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No, 15 of 
1987.
 16.  In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala, Tamil and English text in this By law the Sinhala text shall 
prevail.
