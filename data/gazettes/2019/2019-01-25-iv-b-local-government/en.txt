examination conducted by a Government medical personnel within a month of appointment. if the 
physical condition is found not suitable, the appointment will be cancelled. 
 7. Should not have been convicted by a Court of Law under penal code.
 8. Should not be dismissed by the Government of Provincial Public Services or should not a pensioner, 
retired under 44/90 Public Administration Circular.
