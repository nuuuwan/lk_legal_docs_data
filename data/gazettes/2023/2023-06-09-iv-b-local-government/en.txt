Imposition of entertainment tax
notice under Section 2 (2) of entertainment tax ordinance no. 12 of 1946
By virtue of power vested on me in terms of Section 2 (2) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, which to 
be read with Section 2 of provincial council Act (Incidental Provisions) No. 12 of 1989; It is notified that approval was 
granted by me; Wasantha Karannagoda, Admiral of the fleet, the Governor of North Western Province for resolution number 
06.7 of General Meeting of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council held on 13.12.2022 as per Section 2 (1) of Entertainment Tax 
Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, to assign a Tax equal to Ten percentage (10%) from Entertainment Activities out of entrance fee 
of Entertainment Activities described in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, as amended by the Entertainment 
Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 1984.
  Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Karannagoda, 
  Governor,
  North Western Province.
On this 11 of May, 2023,
At Governor's Office – Kurunegala.
resolution
By virtue of power vested under Section 2 (1) of Entertainment Ordinance No. 12 of 1946, Kuliyapitiya Urban 
Council has proposed to impose and levy and entertainment Tax equal to ten percentage (10%) of entrance fee of Entertainment 
Activities described in the ditto ordinance and conducted within the Jurisdiction of Kuliyapitiya Urban Council and it is 
suggested to publish in the Gazette with approval of Hon. Governor in terms of Section 2 (2) of Entertainment Tax Ordinance 
No. 12 of 1946, which to be read with Section 2 of Provincial Council Act (Incidental Provisions) No. 12 of 1989.
And it will be effect from the first day of following month after the resolution published in the Gazette.
