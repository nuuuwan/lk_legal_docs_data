Imposing entertainment tax
Notification made under Section 2(2) of Entertainment Tax No. 12 of 1946
by virtue of powers vested under Sub section 2 (2) of the Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 to be read with 
Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) Act, No. 12 of 1989, I, Wasantha Karannagoda, the Hon. Governor 
in the North Western Province, do hereby notify that I have approved the following resolution to levy an Entertainment tax of 
10% of the entry fee charged for Entertainment activities which has been adopted under Resolution No. 06/2021/43/05/04 at 
the General Council held on 02nd June, 2021 by the Pradeshiya Sabha Maho by virtue of the powers vested in the Pradeshiya 
Sabha under Sub Section (1) of Section 2 of Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 amended by the Entertainment 
(Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 1984.
 Admiral of the Fleet Wasantha Karannagoda,
 Hon. Governor in the North Western Province,
 Governor’s Office,
 North Western Province.
On 16th January, 2023,
At the Hon. Governor’s Office in the North Western Province.
RESOLuTION
“By virtue of powers vested under Sub section (1) of Section 2 of the Entertainment tax Ordinance No. 12 of 
1946, Pradeshiya Sabha Maho proposes that a tax equivalent to ten percent (10%) from the total amount received should 
be imposed and levied in respect of entering into any entertaining activity specified in the Entertainment Tax Ordinance 
within the areas of administrative limits of Pradeshiya Sabha Maho and this adoption of resolution should be published in 
the Gazette paper under the approval of the Hon. Governor in the North Western Province under Sub-section 2 (2) of the 
Entertainment Tax Ordinance No. 12 of 1946 to be read with Section 2 of the Provincial Council (Incidental Provisions) 
Act, No. 12 of 1989 and executed from the first of the ensuing month immediate after the month in which this adoption of 
resolution is published in the aforesaid Gazette.”
