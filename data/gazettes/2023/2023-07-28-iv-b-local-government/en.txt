AS per the Extra Ordinary Gazette Notification No. 2061/4 and dated 05.03.2018, the Gampola Urban Council Members' 
tenure was scheduled to be end on the 19.03.2022, incidentally it was extended through the Extra Ordinary Gazette 
Notification No. 2262/8 and dated 10.01.2022, being terminated from 19.03.2023, hence I, U. G. Jayarathna, performing 
the duties of the Secretary at the office of the Gampola Urban Council, do hereby notify to the General Public to implement 
under mentioned Proposal under the Resolution No. 2023/465 of the Resolution book on 05.07.2023, under Section 184 a 
of the Chapter 255 of the Urban Council Ordinance.
 U. G. Jayarathna,
 Secretary, 
 Gampola Urban council.

PROPOSAL
By virtue of power vested in, under the provisions of By Laws relating to the Solid Waste Management, approved 
and complied by the Minister in charge of Local Government, Central Province, under sub Section 1 of section 3 (Standard 
By Laws) of the Local authorities Act, No. 6 of 1952, and published in the Extra Ordinary Gazette No. 1816/42 and dated 
28.06.2013, subsequent to the publication of such Standard by Laws in the Gazette No. 2146, dated Friday, 18.10.2019, it 
was accepted and to enact Within the authority areas of the Gampola Urban Council and by virtue of power vested in under 
sub Sections  (IV) and (V) of Section 07, by the Resolution No. 04:01, adopted on the 18th day of October 2019, I have 
decided to charge a Litter Garbage Tax on non de - composing solid waste, at the rate of Rupees 25.00 per kg for the year 
2023, on garbage collected from the trading centers within the authority areas of Gampola Urban Council, under section 184 
a of Chapter 255 of Urban Councils Ordinance.
