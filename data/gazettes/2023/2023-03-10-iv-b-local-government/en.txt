APPLICATIONS  are invited from the permanent  residents of the area of authority of Pradeshiya Sabha Nawagaththegama

selection of suitable candidates for the following post on Service Agreement basis.
01. Post :
 i. Name of the post :  Watchman
 ii. Number of Posts : 02
 iii. Monthly Salary Scale  : 1/30th of the daily wage from the starting step of the PL -1 2016 salary system 
as  per Public Administration Circular No. 3/2016 and dated 25.02.2016
 iv. Educational Qualification  : Should have passed at least 2 subjects in the G. C. E. O/L, Examination. (Except 
for optional subjects) (Educational qualification specified in the then recruiment 
procedure are personally applicable only to the employees who are currently 
recruited on casual, substitute, contract basis).
02. Other Qualifications required :
 1. Should not be less than 18 years of age and not more than 45 of age as on the closing date of applications
 2. The applicant must be a Sri Lanka Citizen by descent or registration
 3. Applicant must be a permanent resident for not less than three years in the area of authority of Pradeshiya 
Sabha Nawagaththegama. (Should have proofed by a certificate issued by the Grama Niladhari of the Grama 
Niladhari Division and countersigned by the Divisional Secretary)
 4. The candidate should have an excellent character and should be in good health
 5. Should not have been convicted before a court of law under penal code.
 6. Applicants should fulfill the minimum qualifications mentioned before the post.
03. Terms of Employment :
 i. Recruitment for this post is on daily wage basis
 ii. You shall be subject to the policy decisions of the Public Service Provident Fund

Regulations, Department Orders of the Republic of Sri Lanka and other regulations and orders issued by the 
Government or Provincial Council from time to time.

 01. Qualified Candidates will be selected based on the basis of results of a structured interview as per the requirement.

should be submitted at the interview.
