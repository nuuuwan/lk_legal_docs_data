APPROviNG  OF  QuALiFiED  GARAGES  FOR  thE  iSSuANCE  OF  FitNESS  CERtiFiCAtES  yEAR - 2024
APPLICATIONS are invited from garage owners and depots belonging to the Sri Lanka transport Board who are registered 
as a business and who can present evidence of relevant tax payments for the purpose of issuing certificates of fitness for the 
year 2024 for Commercial Vehicles registered in the Central Province. Only garages located in the Central Province will be 
considered for approval.
01.  Applicants must meet the following qualifications :
(i) Facilities :
 (a) 20 feet long, 2 1/2 feet wide, 4 1/2 feet deep vehicle inspection pit or an inspection platform or lift providing 
similar facilities.
 (b) Brake testing equipment
 (c) Headlamp testing equipment
 (d) mechanical tools such as wrenches, spanners, hammers etc. are used to check the undercarriage of the 
vehicle, steering joints, brakes, cables etc., and worn parts.
(ii) Staff :
 As certification officers, persons with the following qualifications should be employed in the garage
 (a) Possessing five (5) years of experience in the field of motor vehicle Engineering with special specialization 
in motor vehicle Engineering or National technical Diploma Certificate in mechanical Engineering or Junior 
industrial Officer Certificate.
 (b) having passed the four (4) year Course in motor mechanics at Lanka German institute of techical training 
Moratuwa or having eight (8) years of experience in the Field of motor vehicle engineering with other 
equivalent qualifications.
02.  The following documents should be submitted along with the application form
 (a) Attested photocopy of certificated showing qualification of certifying officers.
 (b) A bank receipt received on crediting a non-refundable fee of Rs. 2,000 to the A/C No. 33965 (Bank of 
Ceylon) to the name of “Commissioner of Transport, Central Province”.
 (c) A rough map showing the exact location of the garage and the route from the city center to the location 
(briefly detailed) as accessible to the inspecting officer. (Summary of the road map).
03. While issuing permits a smart phone is required to upload the details and photos of the tests conducted through the 
application software for the purpose of issuing revenue licenses through the eRL 2.0 program. Therefore, from year 
2024, having a smart phone will be considered a mandatory part of garage registration for issuance of certificate of 
fitness.

qualifications according to this notification to be recruited to Grade 1 (a) in Class 3 of Sri Lanka teachers’ Service complying 
to Sri Lanka teachers’ Service minute of the Public Service of Western Province for the vacancies in Sri Lanka teachers’

recruitments will be decided based on the existing provisions and the service requirement. This examination is valid 
for vacancies exist until 31.12.2025, subject to the further approvals for recruitments. Recruitments will be made 
on the priority order of total marks obtained by the applicants at the competitive examination and the practical 
examination.

03. Educational and Other Qualifications :
3.1 Educational Qualifications:
 
 i. Should have obtained a degree related to the subjects specified in the schedule 01 awarded from a university 
recognized by the university Grants Commission or from a degree-awarding institution recognized by the 
university Grants Commission in a field specializing in and recognized for the purpose of teaching. this 
degree should be valid prior to the closing date of the applications.                                                          
      and                                                          
 ii. Should have passed six (06) subjects with three Credit Passes including passes for the subjects of Sinhala 
Language and mathematics in not more than two attempts at the General Certificate of Education (Ordinary 
Level) Examination. (Shall have passed in at least 05 subjects in one sitting.)                                                           
and                                                          
 iii. Should have passed the General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level) Examination in 03 subjects at 
one sitting.
Very Important (Should be considered when filling the applications)                                                         
• Applicants should be aware of the subjects they can apply for as per their degrees.
• Can apply for the relevant teacher vacancies based on the main subject/ subjects studied for the degree
• Can apply for only three subjects
• Will not be allowed to change the applied subjects and medium due to any reason later
3.2  Physical and mental Eligibility:
 Every candidate should be mentally and physically fit to perform duties as a teacher.
3.3  Age:
 Should not be less than 18 years and more than 35 years of age as at the closing date of applications.
3.4 Other Qualifications:
 i. the applicant should be a citizen of Sri Lanka,
 ii. The applicant should be of excellent character,
 iii. Should be able to serve in any area of the Western Province,
 iv.  Applicant or the spouse should be permanent residents of Western Province for a period of not less than 
03 years preceding immediately to the closing date of applications. (Should confirm at the interviews by 
the Certificate on Residence [DS 04 format] issued by the Grama Niladhari).
04. Service Conditions :
 i. Selected Graduate candidates will be appointed to Grade 1(a) in Class 3 of the Sri Lanka teachers’ Service 
subjected to the general conditions governing Public/ Provincial Public Service appointments, the conditions 
imposed by the Sri Lanka teachers’ Service minute of the Western Provincial Council Public Service and any 
amendments already made to such minutes or will be made thereafter.
 ii. this post is permanent and pensionable. Appointees should comply with the policy decisions taken by the 
Government in future regarding the pension scheme.
 iii. Candidates appointed to this post will be subjected to a probationary period of 03 years.

05. Recruitment :
5.1 method of Recruitment:
 Recruitments will be made according to Section 7.2.4 of Sri Lanka teachers’ Service minute of Western 
Provincial Council Public Service which is effective from 01.07.2008.
 A written examination will be held for the selection. 
 According to the order of highest marks received at the written examination, a three-fold number of applicants 
compared to the vacancies existing for each subject within the province will be called for the general interview. 
Applicants who qualify from the general interview will be called for the practical test later. 
 Applicants, who obtained the highest total marks at the written examination and practical test will be selected 
for the existing vacancies as enforced by Section 7.2.4.3.1 of teachers’ Service minute.
 Recruitment period based on the results of this examination according to this Gazette notification will be valid 
for the vacancies existing as at 30.06.2023.

Syllabus :
Subject No. 01 – Aptitude Test
 this question paper is designed to measure the candidate’s skills relating to the teaching profession and knowledge 
of logical intelligence. this consists of 50 multiple choice and short answer type questions. All of those questions 
should be answered.
 Subject No. 02 – General Knowledge
 
 this paper consists of multiple choice and short answer type questions that test general knowledge on field of teaching

 i. This examination will be conducted in Sinhala, Tamil and english mediums.

be the same language medium for the appointment.
