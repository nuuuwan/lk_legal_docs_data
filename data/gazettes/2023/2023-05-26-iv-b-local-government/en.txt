* Imposition of three-wheeler Parking Permit fees for the year 2023
In the Tenth (10) line of the proposal for he imposition of three-wheeler parking permit fees for the year 2023, published in 
the Gazette Notification of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka bearing No. 2312 dated 23.12.2022 under Serial 
No. 12-484/6, page No. 6165, it is mentioned as “in the by-laws regarding Advertisements”. I do hereby announce that this 
Should be amended as “in the by-laws to be read with section 9 of the by-laws on the parking of three-wheelers.”
* charges for crematoriums for the year 2023.
I do hereby announce that “No. 178/22” in the Sixth (06) line of the proposal to charge  crematorium fees for the year 2023 
published in the Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka bearing No. 2312 dated 23.12.2022 under Serial 
No. 12-484/9 on the Page No. 6167, should be amended as “No. 1978/22” and the Tenth (10) line of the same proposal 
should be amended to read as “Crematoriums By-law” instead of “By-laws on Advertisements”
* Levying charges on Services for the year 2023.
In the sixth (6) line of the proposal for the imposition of Services and Charges for the year 2023, published in the Gazette 
Notification of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka bearing No. 2312 dated 23.12.2022 under Serial No. 12-
484/11, page No. 6169, it is mentioned as “No. 1978/22”. I do hereby announce that this should be amended as “No. 
1978/22”.

iN my capacity as the Secretary of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha and Officer implementing the powers, functions and 
duties of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha, in terms of the powers vested in me by the Section 9.3 of the Pradeshiya Sabha 
Act  bearing No. 15 of 1987, I have decided for the following amendment to be made to the effect that the prescription that 
“A fee of Rs. 1000 per application mentioned in the proposal by which license fees are imposed upon Advertisements/visual 
environment for the year 2023 published on the page No. 6164 under Serial No. 12-484/5 of the Gazette of the Democratic 
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka bearing No. 2312 dated 23.12.2022, to be amended as “A fee of Rs. 100 per application” 
regarding the imposition of license fees upon Advertisements/visual environment by the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha for the 
year 2023.
Resolution
By-law on Advertisements of Standard By-laws published in the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1947/6 dated 
28.12.2015, corrected by the Extraordinary Gazette Paper No. 1978/22 dated 03.08.2016 having adopted by the Provincial

by the Gazette No. 2159 dated 17.01.2020, has been adopted by Pradeshiya Sabha of Panadura by the Gazette Paper No. 
1995 dated 25.11.2016 and in accordance with the powers assigned to the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha by Section 8 of the 
said by-laws on Advertisements, a fee mentioned in the following Schedule shall be charged for the year 2023 for displaying 
of an advertisement in any form or appearance within the boundaries of the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha and in accordance
