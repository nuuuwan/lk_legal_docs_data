the decisions taken in the meeting regarding long distance bus stand held on 08.12.2021 at 03.00p.m, with the participation 
of 15 representatives including Hon.Mayor, Commissioner of Jaffna municipal Council, Chief engineer, Director of UDA 
Northern Province, OIC of traffic and President of Northern regional transport authority and president of private bus 
institution and representative of archeological department are as follow :-
1. It is decided to conduct 3 services (Local services, out district services and night service buses to Colombo) from 
private bus stand since 15.12.2021.
2. It is decided that direction sign boards will be fixed by municipal Council. The traffic police should implement the 
rules regarding roads strictly.
3. It is accepted that the private local service buses to stay at current place for 10 minutes, after the departure from 
long distance bus stand. All local service buses have to drop passengers at long distance bus stand.
4. It is decided that the buses start their journey from CTB bus stand will reach saththira junction (color signal) 
through Hospital road and go to out district through k.k.s road and Main Street and the buses coming from out 
district will also have to use this way. The decision will be implemented immediately and the cooperation to it 
would be given by traffic police.
5. It is decided that the long distance buses, Start from CTB bus stand will not be allowed to travel through hospital 
road to reach vempadi junction.

Fridays. If a Friday happens to be a Public holiday the Gazette is published on the working day immediately

varies depending on the incidence of public holidays in the week concerned.

desirous of ensuring the timely publication of notices in the Gazette  make it a point to see that sufficient time is 
allowed for postal  transmission of  notices to the Government Press.
