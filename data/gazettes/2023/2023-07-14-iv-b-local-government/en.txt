All Government Departments, Corporations, Boards, etc. are hereby advised that Notifications fixing closing dates and times of applications in 
respect of Post-vacancies, Examinations, Tender Notices and dates and times of Auction Sales, etc. should be prepared by giving adequate time 
both from the date of despatch of notices to Govt. Press and from the date of publication, thereby enabling those interested in the contents of such

Declare as a Developed area
it is hereby notified to the General Public that approval was give to declare the areas up to 70th post of Thirappane Town, 
Aluth Punchikulama, in term of powers vested in Galkulama Town and Nachchaduwa Town (areas described in the attached 
maps) as developed areas by L. H. V . Iroshani who functions as Asst. Commissioner of Local Government of Anuradhapura 
District, North Central Province in term of powers vested in Local Government Assistant Commissioner by Se. 134 (1) of 
Pradeshiya Saba Act, 15 of 1987 by virtue of resolution passed under resolution No. 22 of General Meeting of Thirappane 
Pradeshiya Sabha held in 19.11.2018 and resolution No. 04.01.2023 of General Meeting held on 24.01.2023 in terms of 
powers vested in Pradeshiya Sabha under Sect. 134 (1) of Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of 1987.
