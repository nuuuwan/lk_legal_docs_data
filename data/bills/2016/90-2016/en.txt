STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 :  This clause amends section 431 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15
of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect of the
section as amended is to—
(a) make provision to reduce the period granted for a person to establish his claim for
seized property taken under section 29 or stolen; and
(b) increase the value of the property for which notification is required under that
section.
Clause 3 :  This clause amends section 432 of the principal enactment and provides for a
consequential amendment to the marginal note of that section.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
ACT, NO. 15 OF 1979
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Code of Criminal
Procedure (Amendment) Act, No.   of 2016.
2. Section 431 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act,
No. 15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal
enactment”) is hereby amended as follows:—
(a) in subsection (2) of that section,  by the substitution
for the words “within six months” of the words
“within two months”; and
(b) in subsection (3) of that section,  by the substitution
for the words “two thousand five hundred rupees or
more.” of the words “twenty five thousand rupees
or more.”.
3. Section 432 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended by the repeal of the marginal note to that section
and the substitution therefor of the following:—
“Procedure where no claimant appears within two
months.”.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
