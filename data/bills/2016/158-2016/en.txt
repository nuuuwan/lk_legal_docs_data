AN ACT TO AMEND THE FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES
ACT, NO. 2 OF 1996
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Fisheries and Aquatic
Resources (Amendment) Act, No.    of 2016.
2. The following new section is hereby inserted
immediately after section 28 of the Fisheries and Aquatic
Resources Act, No. 2 of 1996 (hereinafter referred to as the
“principal enactment”) and shall have effect as section
28
A of that enactment.
28A. (1) No person shall engage in or cause
any other person to engage in fishing
operations carried out by Trawl Nets, including
those using nets without two wings but using
vertical poles at the two free ends of the net.
(2) No person shall use, posses, import,
transport, purchase or sell Trawl Nets for the
purpose of carrying out trawling operations in
Sri Lanka waters.
(3) No license shall be issued under Part II
of the principal enactment for fishing
operations carried out by Trawl Nets in
Sri Lanka waters.
(4) Any license, which has already been
issued under the principal enactment for
fishing operations carried out by Trawl Nets in
Sri Lanka waters, shall not be renewed, and

49 (7). Any person who contravenes or fails

term not exceeding two years or to a fine not
less than fifty thousand rupees.”.
4. Section 66 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended by the insertion, immediately after the definition
of the expression “Sri Lanka Waters”, of the following new
definitions:—
“Trawl Net” means towing nets having a cone
shaped body and a bag or cod end (madiya)
connected to the apex with or without two
wings connected to the other end.
“Trawling” means any fishing operation carried out
by dragging a Trawl Net mechanically along
the bed or in mid water.
5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
