AN ACT TO AMEND THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF INTERIOR DESIGNERS
(INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 2 OF 1998
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Institute of
Interior Designers (Incorporation)  (Amendment)  Act,
No.    of 2016.
2. The aim of the Bill is to amend the section No. 4 of
the Act under the general powers of Corporation, to include
the following:—
(1) membership in the Corporation is a requirement for
all Practitioners in the filed of Interior Design and
Recognition of the Professional Status and Licence
for practice of an Interior Designer, Interior
Architect, Interior Technologist and Interior
Decorater will be for those who have obtained
approval from the Corporation fulfilling the
requirements and abiding by the Rules &
Regulations and Ethical Code of Practice;
(2) the Corporation will be the responsible body to
award Charter Status (Chartered Interior Designer)
to those who specialize and qualify for such
recognition in the field of Interior Design;
(3) the Corporation will provide a Quality Assurance
Certificate for Market Acceptance on such Interior
Products of good Designer value and production
quality;
(4) Recommendation by the Corporation will be
necessary for the awarding of Certificates for
competency in Technical skills in the filed of
Interior Decoration and Interior Technology;
Short title.
