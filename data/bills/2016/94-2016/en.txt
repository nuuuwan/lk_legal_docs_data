Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Value Added Tax
(Amendment) Act, No.    of 2016 and shall be deemed to
have come into operation on January 1, 2016 unless the
dates on which certain provisions thereof shall come into
operation are specified in such sections.
