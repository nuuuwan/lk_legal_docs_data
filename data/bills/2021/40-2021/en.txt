1National Minimum Wage of Workers
(Amendment)
Short title
L.D.—O. 25/2019
AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL MINIMUM WAGE OF WORKERS
ACT, NO. 3 OF 2016
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the National Minimum
Wage of Workers (Amendment) Act, No.       of  2021.
2. Section 3 of the National Minimum Wage of Workers
Act, No. 3 of 2016 is hereby amended in subsection (1) thereof
by the substitution for the words, “shall be ten thousand
rupees and the national minimum daily wage of a worker
shall be four hundred rupees” of the words, “shall be twelve
thousand five hundred rupees and the national minimum
daily wage of a worker shall be five hundred rupees.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
