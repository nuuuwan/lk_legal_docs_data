1Tobacco Tax (Amendment)
Short title.
AN ACT TO AMEND THE TOBACCO TAX  ACT, NO. 08 OF 1999
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Tobacco Tax
(Amendment) Act, No.       of 2021.
2. Section 15 of the Principal Act is hereby amended by
repealing sub-section 15 (1) of the said Act and by the
substitution of the following paragraph:-
“15. (1) Every person is guilty of an offence under
this Act shall on conviction after summary trial
before a Magistrate be liable to a fine of three
times of the value of the goods forfeited by the
Department of Customs or the Excise
Department under this Act or imprisonment of
either description for a term not exceeding five
years.”
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
