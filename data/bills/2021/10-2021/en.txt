Muslim Marriage and Divorce (Repeal) 1
5
AN ACT TO REPEAL THE MUSLIM MARRIAGE AND DIVORCE
ACT, NO. 13 OF 1951
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1.  This Act may be cited as the Muslim Marriage and
Divorce (Repeal) Act, No.    of 2021.
2. The Muslim Marriage and Divorce Act, No. 13 of
1951 is hereby repealed.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
