STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause  2 : This clause amends section 3 of the Fiscal Management (Responsibility)
Act, No. 3 of 2003 by amending paragraphs ( e) and ( f) thereof and the legal effect of this
section as amended is to revise the objectives underlying responsible fiscal management–
(a) by increasing the guarantee limit specified in paragraph ( e) up to 15 percent;
and
(b) by moving the established debt to gross domestic product target, to year 2030.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE FISCAL MANAGEMENT (RESPONSIBILITY)

1. This Act may be cited as the Fiscal Management

Act, No. 3 of 2003 is hereby amended as follows:-
(a) in paragraph (e) of that section, by the substitution,
for the words “ten per centum;” of the words “fifteen
per centum;”; and
(b) in paragraph ( f) of that section, by the substitution,
for the words and figures “January 1, 2020” of the
words and figures “ January 1, 2030”.

and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
