and  Exchange   Commission  of   Sri   Lanka  Act,  No. 36  of  1987  and  for

SRI LANKA; TO REGULATE MARKET INSTITUTIONS, CERTAIN PUBLIC
OFFERS OF SECURITIES, MARKET INTERMEDIARIES; TO PROTECT
INVESTORS AND TO PROVIDE FOR ENFORCEMENT MEASURES; TO DEAL
WITH MARKET MISCONDUCT; AND TO OVERCOME THE CHALLENGES
ENCOUNTERED BY SECURITIES MARKET REGULATORS AND TO REPEAL
THE SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION OF SRI LANKA ACT,
NO. 36 OF 1987 AND FOR MATTERS CONNECTED  THEREWITH OR
INCIDENTAL THERETO.
