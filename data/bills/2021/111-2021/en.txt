STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends paragraph ( b) of subsection (1) of section 2 of the
Appropriation Act, No. 7 of 2020, and the legal effect of section as amended is to increase
the borrowing limit of rupees two thousand nine hundred ninety seven billion to rupees
three thousand three hundred ninety seven billion.
