STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause  2 : This clause amends section 195 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No.
15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) and is consequential to the
amendment made by clause 3.
Clause  3 : This clause amends the principal enactment by inserting new section 195
A
in that enactment, and the legal effect of the amendment is to make provision to hold
pre-trial conferences at the High Court in order to conclude trials expeditiously.
