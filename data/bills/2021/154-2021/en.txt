AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE INSTITUTE OF SPORT FOR PEACE,
EDUCATION, EMPOWERMENT AND DEVELOPMENT (SPEED)
WHEREAS an Institute called and known as the
“Institute of Sport for Peace, Education, Empowerment and
Development (SPEED) ” has heretofore been established in
Sri Lanka for the purpose of effectually carrying out its
objects and transacting all  matters connected with the said
Institute according to the rules agreed to by its members:
AND WHEREAS the said Institute has heretofore
successfully carried out and transacted the several objects
and matters for which it was established and has applied to
be incorporated and it will be for the public advantage to
grant the said application:
BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Institute of Sport for
Peace, Education, Empowerment and Development
(Incorporation) Act, No.    of 2021.
2. (1) From and after the date of commencement of this
Act, such and so many persons as now are members of the
Institute of Sport for Peace, Education, Empowerment and
Development (hereinafter referred to as the  “Institute”) or
shall hereafter be admitted as members of the Corporation
hereby constituted shall be a body corporate with perpetual
succession, under the name and style of the “Institute of
Sport for Peace, Education, Empowerment and
Development” (hereinafter referred to as “the Corporation”),
and by that name may sue and be sued with full power and
authority to have, and use a common seal and to alter the
same at its pleasure.

(2) The Corporation shall be deemed to be a voluntary
social service organization within the meaning, and for the

(Registration and Supervision) Act, No. 31 of 1980 and the
provisions of that Act shall apply to and in relation to the

3. (1) The general objects for which the Corporation is
constituted are hereby declared to be:—
(a) to  increase performance of Sri Lanka and South
Asian nations in International sporting events by
establishing a platform for the upholding of
principles and values of Olympism and to support
Olympic movement;
(b) to optimize the potential of sport for contributing
to national and regional peace building and conflict
prevention and to achieve internationally agreed
development goals by using sport as a tool for peace
education and social integration under Sport for
Development and Peace;
(c) to promote professional, traditional and recreational
sport in Sri Lanka through creating access to
resources, services and facilities;
(d) to promote gender equality and the empowerment
of women through suporting their career and
advancement in sport, in particular their progressive
high performance in international sporting events;
(e) to advance the use of sport as a vehicle to promote
health and prevent drug abuse through physical
education, foster the inclusion and well-being of
persons with disabilities and facilitate social
inclusion of marginalized groups;

15. (1) The Seal of the Corporation shall not be affixed

16. (1) If upon the dissolution of the Corporation there
remains after the satisfaction of all its debts and liabilities
any property whatsoever, such property shall not be
distributed among the members of the Corporation, but shall
be given or transferred to any other institution having objects
similar to those of the Corporation, and which is by the rules
thereof, prohibited from distributing any income or property
among its members.
(2) For the purposes of subsection (1) the appropriate
institution shall be determined by the members of
the Corporation immediately before the dissolution at
a general meeting by the majority of votes of the
members present.
17. Nothing in this Act contained shall prejudice or
affect the rights of the Republic or of any body politic or
corporate.
18. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall
prevail.
