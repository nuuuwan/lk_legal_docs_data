AN  ACT  TO AMEND THE SRI LANKA BAPTIST SANGAMAYA
(INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 54 OF 1944
WHEREAS the members of the Sri Lanka Baptist Sangamaya
formally known as Ceylon Baptist Council incorporated
under Ceylon Baptist Council Ordinance No. 54 of 1944 as
amended by the Sri Lanka Baptist Sangamaya (Incorporation)
(Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 1991 have passed a resolution
to amend the name of the Sangamaya:
AND WHEREAS the said Sangamaya, for the purpose of
giving effect to such resolution has applied to change the
name of such Sangamaya and it will be expedient to grant
such application:
NOW THEREFORE, be it enacted by the Parliament of the
Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Baptist
Sangamaya (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act, No.    of 2022.
2. In every context in which the Sri Lanka Baptist
Sangamaya is mentioned (whether by that name or by the
abbreviation of “the Sangamaya”) in the Sri Lanka Baptist
Sangamaya Act, No. 14 of 1991 (Incorporation) (Amendment)
or in any notice or other document issued under that Act,
there shall, unless the context otherwise requires, be
substituted for the expressions “the Sri Lanka Baptist
Sangamaya” or “the Sangamaya”, the expressions “Sri Lanka
Baptist Church or “the Church”, as the case may be.
3. Every contract, agreement or other instrument or
document whatsoever made, issued or executed prior to the
date of commencement of that Act, by or in favour “the Sri
Lanka Baptist Sangamaya.” shall be deemed on and after the
date of commencement of that Act to be and to have been
made, issued or executed by or in favour of “Sri Lanka Baptist
Church”.
