STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 8 of the Sri Lanka Electricity Act, No. 20 of
2009 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect of the
section as amended is to allow any person to participate in a bidding process for the
generation of electricity.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 9 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of the section as amended is -
(a) to allow any person to apply for the issue of a generation licence to generate
electricity; and
(b) to take away the restriction imposed on a person to be eligible to apply for the
issue of a generation licence to generate electricity over and above the
generation capacity of 25 M.W. and also to allow any person to apply for the
same without any demarcation on the generation capacity.
Clause 4 : This clause amends section 43 of the principal enactment and the legal
effect of the section as amended is -
(a) the repeal of subsection (3) thereof as an consequential amendment made in
relation to the amendment made to subsection (1) of section 9; and
(b) to expand the application of the provisions of the proviso to any existing
generation plant that is to be developed and also to rectify an omission in that
subsection.
