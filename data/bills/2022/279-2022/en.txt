STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause  2 : This clause amends section 2 of the Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”), and the legal effect of that section as
amended is to increase the VAT rate–
(i) from 8% to 12% with effect from June 1, 2022 to August 31, 2022; and
(ii) from 12% to 15% with effect from September 1, 2022,
on the value of goods or services supplied, or goods imported.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 10 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of that section as amended is to decrease the threshold of registration for VAT from
Rs. 300 million to Rs. 80 million per annum  with effect from October 1, 2022.
Clause 4  : This clause amends Part II of the First Schedule to the principal enactment in
order to remove the VAT exemption on condominium residential apartments with effect
from October 1, 2022.
