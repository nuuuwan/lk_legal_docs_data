STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 :  This clause amends section 3 of the Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight
Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers and Container Operators Act, No.
10 of 1972 and the legal effect of that section as amended is to provide for the Minister to
make regulations with respect of regulating the procedure for imposing and collecting
charge or levy imposed by the relevant service providers.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist

1. This Act may be cited as the Licensing of Shipping
Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common
Carriers and Container Operators (Amendment) Act,
No. …… of 2022.
2. Section 3 of the Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight
Forwarders, Non- Vessel Operating Common Carriers and
Container Operators Act, No. 10 of 1972 is hereby amended
in subsection (5) thereof as follows:-
(1) in paragraph ( e) thereof, by the substitution for the
words “contravention of such standards.”  of the
words “contravention of such standards; and”;
(2) by the addition  at the end of the paragraph ( e) of
that subsection, of the  following new paragraph:-
“(f) a charge, levy and tariff imposed  by the
relevant service providers of  shipping agents,
freight forwarders, non-vessel operating
common carriers and container operators for
the services rendered by them,  and regulating
the procedure of imposing and collecting such
charge, levy and tariff .”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
