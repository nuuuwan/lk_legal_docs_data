STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This clause amends the section 18 B of the Shop and Office Employees
(Regulation of Employment and Remuneration) Act (Chapter 129) and the legal effect of
the section as amended is to provide for the entitlement of seventy days leave for the
confinement of a delivery of live a child and twenty eight days leave for the confinement
of delivery of a dead child for female worker.
