STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 30 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) (hereinafter
referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect as amended is to expand the
Categories of persons required to keep instruments.
Clause 3 :  This clause amends section 32 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
as amended is to empower the Minister to make rules for the issue of security features to be
affixed on an excisable article and to levy charges for the provision of such security features,
with a view to eliminate any revenue loss from duties on an excisable article.

L.D.—O. 21/2018
AN ACT TO AMEND THE EXCISE ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 52)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Excise (Amendment)
Act, No.    of 2018.
2. Section 30 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52)
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended as follows:—
(1) in subsection (1) by the substitution for the words
“Every person who manufactures or sells excisable
article under a licence” of the words “Every person
who imports, exports, transports, manufactures,
sells, possess or stores any excisable article under a
licence, permit or pass;
(2) in the marginal note to that section for the words
“certain licensees” of the words “holder of a licence,
permit or pass”.
3. Section 32 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended by the insertion immediately after paragraph (j) of
the following new paragraphs:—
“(ja) prescribing the types of security features to be
affixed on an excisable article when such excisable
article is imported, exported, transported,
manufactured, sold, possessed or stored;
(jb) levying charges for  issuing of the security features
referred to in paragraph ( ja) to be affixed on an
excisable article when such excisable article is
imported, exported, transported, manufactured,
sold, possessed or stored;”.
Short  title.
