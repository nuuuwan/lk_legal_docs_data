STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause amends section 4 of the Prescription (Special Provisions) Act, No. 5
of 2016 in order to extend the time period within which a disadvantaged person is entitled to avail
himself of the benefits of the Act.

Short title.
L.D.—O. 39/2017
AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRESCRIPTION (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)
ACT, NO. 5 OF 2016
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Prescription (Special
Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No.    of 2018.
2. Section 4 of the Prescription (Special Provisions) Act,
No. 5 of 2016 is hereby amended as follows:—
(1) by the substitution for the words “two years after
the coming into operation of this Act.” of the words
“four years from the date of coming into  operation
of this Act.”;
(2) by the substitution for the words “two years after
the date of operation of this Act.”, in the marginal
note to that section, of the words “four years from
the date of coming into  operation of this Act.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
