STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This clause amends section 3 of the Land (Restrictions on Alienation)
Act, No. 38 of 2014 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal
effect of the section as amended is to extend the application of the exemption granted by
that section to certain properties.
Clause 3:  This clause amends section 5
A of the principal enactment, and the legal effect
of the section as amended is to amend the effective date of that section.
