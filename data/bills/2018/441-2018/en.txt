STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause inserts new sections 24 A, 24B, 24C, 24D, and 24 E to the Commissions
of Inquiry Act (Chapter 393) (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the
legal effect of the sections as inserted is to provide for the Commission to Investigate
Allegations of Bribery  or Corruption to consider material collected in the course of an
investigation or an inquiry by a Commission of Inquiry and direct the Director General for
the Prevention of Bribery or Corruption to institute Crimianl Proceedings.
Clause 3:  This clause amends section 27 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of that section as amended is to insert definitions for the expressions “Bribery Act”,
“Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption”, “Declaration of Assets
and Liabilities Law”, “Director General”, “law enforcement authority”, “Penal Code” and
“Prevention of Money Laundering Act”.
