PART III
AMENDMENTS  TO  THE  PRADESHIYA  SABHA  ACT
12. Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987 (hereinafter in this Part referred to as the “principal
enactment”)  is hereby repealed and the following section is
subtituted therefore:—
4.  (1) A Pradeshiya Sabha constituted by
an Order under subsection (1) of section 2,
shall consist of:—
(a)  such number of elected members as
determined by the Minister by Order
made under section 3
C  of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance
( Cap. 262); and
(b) such number of other members
not exceeding thirty per centum of the
total number of elected members as
determined under paragraph (a), to be
returned as members under the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Cap.
262), to represent those electors who
have not secured any representation
in the Sabha, at an election held for
the election of members .
(2) Where the number constituting thirty
per centum referred to in paragraph (b) of
subsection (1) is an integer and fraction, the
integer shall be deemed to be the number
which shall constitute such thirty per centum,
for the purpose of that subsection.”.
