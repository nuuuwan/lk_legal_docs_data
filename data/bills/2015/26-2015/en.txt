STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause amends section 135 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15
of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) by including section 291c of
the Penal Code in paragraph ( e) of subsection (1)  thereof, and the legal effect of the section
as amended is to make the Attorney General’s sanction a pre-requisite to take cognizance of
an offence punishable under section 291 C.
Clause 3 :  This clause amends the First Schedule  to the principal enactment and makes
an amendment consequential to the amendment made by clause 2.
