STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause inserts a new section 35 A in the Immigrants and Emigrants Act
(Chapter 351) and the legal effect of the section is to make provision for the Authority
prescribed to issue or renew passports to take finger printing from an applicant for a
passport or the renewal of a passport to be inserted in the passport.

35
