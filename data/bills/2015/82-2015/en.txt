AN ACT TO AMEND THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA
Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Nineteenth
Amendment to the Constitution.
(2) The provisions of sections 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 11 (other
than paragraph (1) of Article 46), 17, 19, 20, 21, 23, 33, 34,
35, 36, 37 and 38 of this Act shall come into force on April

relates to paragraph (1) of Article 46 of the Constitution of
the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka shall come
into force upon the conclusion of the general election held
immediately after the date on which this Act comes into
operation.
