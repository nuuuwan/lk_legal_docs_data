STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This clause inserts new section 291c in the Penal Code (Chapter 19) and the
legal effect of the section is to make provision to convict and punish persons who cause or
instigate acts of violence or hostility which lead to religious, racial or communal disharmony
between different racial or religious groups.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE PENAL CODE (CHAPTER 19)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Penal Code
(Amendment) Act, No.   of 2015.
2. The following section is hereby inserted immediately
after section 291B of the Penal Code (Chapter 19) and shall
have effect as section  291 C of that Code:—
291C. Whoever, by the use of words spoken,
written or intended to be read, or by signs, or
by visible representation, or otherwise, intends
to cause or attempts to cause or instigates or
attempts to instigate, acts of violence or
religious, racial or communal disharmony, or
feelings of ill-will or hostility, between
communities or different classes of persons or
different racial or religious groups, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either
description for a term which may extend to
two years.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
