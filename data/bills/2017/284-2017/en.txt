AN  ACT TO AMEND THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
ACT, NO. 36 OF 2003
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Intellectual Property
Act, No.    of  2017.
2. Section 161 of the Intellectual Property Act, No. 36 of
2003 is hereby amended by the insertion immediately after
subsection (4) thereof, the following new subsection:—
“(4A) The Minister may prescribe any geographical
indication in respect of any goods or products for
the purpose of this Act.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
