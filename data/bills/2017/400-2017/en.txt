STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 5 of the Commercial Mediation Centre of
Sri Lanka Act, No. 44 of 2000 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”), and the
legal effect of the section as amended is to increase the number of members of the Board of
Management of the Commercial Mediation Centre with a view to ensuring better co-ordination
among the line agencies.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 6 of the principal enactment and the legal effect of
the section as amended is to enable the Minister to appoint the Chairman of the Board of
Management and to specify the manner by which the Chairman can be removed from office.
