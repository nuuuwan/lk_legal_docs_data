STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 15 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), and the
legal effect of the section as amended is to include the requirement of obtaining a license for
tapping any palmyrah tree and coconut tree which produces toddy.

L.D.—O. 63/2016
AN ACT TO AMEND THE EXCISE ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 52)
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Excise (Amendment)
Act, No.    of 2017 and shall come into operation on
January 1, 2018.
2. Section 15 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) is
hereby amended by the repeal of paragraphs ( b) and (c) of
that section, and the substitution therefor of the following:—
“(b) no tree producing toddy, other than kithul tree shall
be tapped;
(c) no toddy shall be drawn or lowered from any tree
other than the kithul tree.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
