STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 2 of the Economic Service Charge Act, No. 13 of
2006 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect of the section
as amended is to charge the service charge in advance for certain imports.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 3 of the principal enactment and the legal effect of
the section as amended is to set off the service charge against the actual liability.
Clause 4 : This clause amends Schedule III of the principal enactment and is consequential
to section 2 of this Act.
Clause 5 : This clause inserts a new Schedule to the principal enactment and the legal
effect will be to charge the service charge on retail sales of petrol, diesel, and kerosene.
