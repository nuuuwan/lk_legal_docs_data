1Provincial Councils Elections (Amendment)
Short title.
L.D.—O. 40/2017
AN ACT TO AMEND THE PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ELECTIONS
ACT, NO. 2 OF 1988
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Provincial Councils
Elections  (Amendment) Act, No.       of 2017.
2. Section 10 of the Provincial Councils Elections Act,
No. 2 of 1988 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that
section, by the substitution for the words and figures from
"Within one week" to "an election to such  Council.", of the
words and figures  "Within one week from the date specified
in terms of Article 154DD of the Constitution, the
Commissioner shall publish a notice of his intention to hold
an election to all Provincial Councils.".
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
