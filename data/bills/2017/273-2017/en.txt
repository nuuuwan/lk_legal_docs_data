STATEMENT OF LEGAL  EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 19 of the Pradeshiya Sabhas Act, No. 15 of 1987
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect of this section
as amended is to enable Pradeshiya Sabhas to spend any part of the Pradeshiya Sabha Fund
for development activities in estate settlements.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 33 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of this section as amended is to enable Pradeshiya Sabhas to construct and maintain roads
and other common amenities in the plantation regions upon adoption of a special resolution
and in concurrence with the administrative authority of the relevant estate.
