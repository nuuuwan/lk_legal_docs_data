STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause amends section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 and the legal
effect of the section as amended is to increase the number of judges of the High Court.

Judicature (Amendment) 1
L.D.—O. 32/2017
AN ACT TO AMEND THE JUDICA TURE ACT, NO. 2 OF 1978
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka  as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment)
Act, No.    of 2017.
2. Section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 is hereby
amended by the repeal of paragraph ( a) of that section and
the substitution therefor of the following paragraph:—
“(a)  not less than ten and not more than eighty five judges,
each of whom shall be known as a “Judge of the
High Court;”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
