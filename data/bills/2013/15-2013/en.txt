STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 2 of the Economic Service Charge Act, No. 13 of
2006 and the legal effect of the section as amended is to exclude—
(a) funds voted by Parliament from the Consolidated Fund or any loan arranged by
the Goverment of Sri Lanka, for the provision of any service, free of charge by
any public corporation; and
(b) proceeds from the sale of any organic fertilizer by the manufacturer of such
product,
as turnover for the purposes of Economic Service Charge.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 13 of the Economic Service Charge Act, No. 13 of
2006 and the legal effect of the section as amended is to exclude the Central Bank from the
Economic Service Charge.
