STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Defence Services Command and Staff College (Amendment) Bill
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 7 of the Defence Services Command and Staff
College Act, No. 5 of 2008 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the
legal effect of the section as amended is to include the Vice-Chancellor of the General
Sir John Kotelawala Defence University in the Board of Management of the Staff College
instead of the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Kelaniya.
Clause 3 :  This clause amends section 15 of the principal enactment and the legel effect
of the section as amended is consequential to the amendment made by clause 2.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE DEFENCE SERVICES COMMAND AND STAFF
COLLEGE ACT, NO. 05 OF 2008
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Defence
Services Command and Staff College (Amendment) Act,
No.            of 2013.
2. Section 7 of the Defence Services Command and Staff
College Act, No. 05 of 2008 (hereinafter referred to as “the
principal enactment”) is hereby amended in subsection (1)
of that section by the repeal of paragraph (f) of that subsection
and the substitution therefor, of the following paragraph:—
“(f) the Vice-Chancellor of the General Sir John
Kotelawala Defence University;”.
3. Section 15 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended in subsection (1) of that section by the repeal of
paragraph (b) of that subsection and the substitution therefor,
of the following paragraph:—
“(b) a representative nominated by the Vice-Chancellor
of the General Sir John Kotelawala Defence
University;”.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
