STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This clause amends paragraph ( c) of section 7 of the Institute of Policy Studies
of Sri Lanka Act, No. 53 of 1988, and the legal effect of the section as amended is to enable
the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka to nominate a representative to the Board of
Governors of the Institute.

“(c) the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka or an
