STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2  : This clause amends section 28 of the Mines and Minerals Act, No. 33 of
1992 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”)  and the legal effect of the
section as amended is to provide for the Minister to prescribe the minerals that are required
for construction purposes which requires a licence for transportation of the same.
Clause 3  : This clause amends section 33 of the principal enactment  and the legal
effect of the section as amended is to prescribe reserved Minerals which requires the
approval of the Minister, to obtain a licence.
Clause 4  : This clause amends section 64 of the principal enactment  and the legal
effect of the section as amended is to add two new paragraphs in respect of which the
Minister may make regulations.
