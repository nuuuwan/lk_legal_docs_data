AN ACT TO AMEND THE INSTITUTE OF QUANTITY SURVEYORS,
SRI LANKA (INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 20 OF 2007
WHEREAS it has become necessary, inter alia, to alter the
Corporate Membership categories and associated names of
Members of the Institute of Quantity Surveyors, Sri Lanka:
AND WHEREAS the Institute of Quantity Surveyors,
Sri Lanka has requested making of necessary amendments
to the Institute of Quantity Surveyors, Sri Lanka
(Incorporation) Act to provide for above requirements and
matters incidental thereto and it is expendient to affect such
amendment:
NOW BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Institute of Quantity
Surveyors, Sri Lanka (Incorporation) (Amendment)
Act, No.      of 2024.
2. Section 13 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended as follows:-
(i) by renumbering the existing subsections (2), (3)
and (4) of that section as (3), (4) and (5), respectively,
and insertion of the following as subsection (2) of
that section, the words, “Every member of the
Corporation who is registered as a Honorary Life
Fellow of the Corporation shall be entitled to use
the abbreviated designation ‘Hony. LFIQS.SL’ after
his or her name”;
