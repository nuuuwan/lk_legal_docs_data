AN ACT TO AMEND THE MATERNITY BENEFITS
ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 140)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -
1.  This Act may be cited as the Maternity Benefits
(Amendment) Act, No.      of 2024.
2.  Section 3 of the Maternity Benefits Ordinance
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended by the insertion of subsection (1) (c) and (1) (d) of
that section, as follows:-
''(c) seven days from the date of miscarriage, if the
miscarriage occurs in the first twenty eight weeks
of pregnancy; and
(d) seven days from the date of termination of
pregnancy under medical grounds''.
3.   In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
