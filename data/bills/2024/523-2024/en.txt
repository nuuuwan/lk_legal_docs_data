L.D.- O. 60/2024
 AN ACT TO  AMEND THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI  LANKA
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1.  This Act may be cited as the Twenty Second
Amendment to the Constitution.
2. Article 83 of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka is hereby amended in paragraph ( b)
thereof, by the substitution for the words “to over six years,”,
of the words “to over five years,”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
