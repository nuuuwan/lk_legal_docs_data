STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2  : This clause amends section 3 of the of the National Minimum Wage of
Workers  Act, No. 3 of 2016 and the legal effect of that section as amended is to increase
the national monthly and daily wages of workers.

L. D.- O. 29/2024
AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL MINIMUM WAGE OF WORKERS
ACT, NO. 3 OF 2016
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-
1. This Act may be cited as the National Minimum Wage
of Workers  (Amendment) Act, No.   of  2024.
2. Section 3 of the  National Minimum Wage of Workers
Act, No. 3 of 2016 is hereby amended in subsection (1)
thereof, by the substitution for the words “twelve thousand
five hundred rupees and the national minimum daily wage
of a worker shall be five hundred rupees”, of the words
“seventeen thousand five hundred rupees and the national
minimum daily wage of a worker shall be seven hundred
rupees”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
