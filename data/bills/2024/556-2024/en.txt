STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends certain sections of the Penal Code (Chapter 19) which
appear in the Schedule to the Bill, and the legal effect of the sections as amended is to
increase the fines imposed in respect of the relevant offences.

L.D.—O. 45/2019
AN ACT TO AMEND THE PENAL CODE ( CHAPTER 19 )
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Penal Code (Amendment)
Act, No.    of 2024.
2. The sections of the Penal Code (Chapter 19) specified
in Column I of the Schedule hereto, are hereby amended by
the substitution for the words specified in the corresponding
entry in column III, of the words specified in the
corresponding entry in Column IV of that Schedule for the
purpose of increasing penalties imposed in respect of the
offences specified in Column II thereof.
3. For the avoidance of doubt, it is hereby declared that
the provisions of section 2 shall not apply in respect of an
offence referred to in the Schedule, where such offence was
committed prior to the date of coming into operation of this
Act.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
