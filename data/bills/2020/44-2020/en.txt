STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause  2 : This clause amends section 10 of the Shop and Office Employees (Regulation
of Employment and Remuneration) Act (Chapter 129) and the legal effect of the section
as amended is to increase the employable age from fourteen years to sixteen years.
