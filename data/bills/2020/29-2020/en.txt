STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

legal effect of the section as amended is to terminate the levying of Debt Repayment Levy
with effect from January 1, 2020.

L.D.—O. 3/2020
AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 35 OF 2018
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment)
Act, No.    of  2020.
2. Section 36 of the Finance Act, No. 35 of 2018 is hereby
amended in subsection (1) thereof, by the substitution for the
words and figures “ending on December 31, 2021,” of the
words and figures “ending on December 31, 2019,”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
