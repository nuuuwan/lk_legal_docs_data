1Marriages (General) (Amendment)
AN  ACT  TO AMEND  THE  MARRIAGES (GENERAL)
ORDINANCE  NO. 19 OF  1907
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Marriages (General)
(Amendment) Act, No.    of  2020.
2. The Preamble of the Principle enactment is hereby
amended by deletion of the words “other than the marriages
of Muslims”.
3. Notwithstanding anything contrary to the provisions
of the Principle enactment or the provisions of any other law,
the Marriages (General) Ordinance No. 19 of 1907 shall
prevail.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
