AN ACT TO AMEND THE MARRIAGE REGISTRATION ORDINANCE
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1.  This Act may be cited as the Marriage Registration
(Amendment) Act, No.    of 2020.
2. The long title of the Marriage Registration Ordinance
No. 19 of 1907 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal
enactment”) is amended to remove the words “OTHER
THAN THE MARRIAGES OF MUSLIMS AND” therein.
3. In the Section 64 substitute the interpretation of
“marriage” with the following :- “marriage” means, “any
marriage contracted under this ordinance between a man
and a woman who have reached the age of 18 and above”.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
