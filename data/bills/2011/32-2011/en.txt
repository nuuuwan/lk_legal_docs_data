STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends the Long Title to the Coast Conservation Act, No. 57 of
1981, (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the Long Title as amended
indicates the new area of Coastal Resource Management which is brought within the ambit
of the principal enactment.
Clause 3 : This clause makes a general amendment to the principal enactment whereby
the there is substituted for the words “Coast Conservation Act”, “Director of Coast
Conservation”, “Director” and “coast conservation” appearing therein, the words “Coast
Conservation and Coastal Resource Management”, “Director-General of Coast
Conservation”, “Director-General” and “coast conservation and coastal resource
management” respectively.
Clause 4 :  This clause amends section 3 of the principal enactment and the section as
amended provides for the appointment of Directors, Deputy Directors and Assistant
Directors.
Clause 5 :  This clause amends section 4 of the principal enactment and the section as
amended widens the scope of the powers to include-
(a) the co-ordination of activities of other agencies, within the coastal zone ;
(b) the preparation and implementation of coastal zone management plans ;
(c) the dissemination of  information on coast conservation ;
(d) the conduct of research and the implementation of the coast conservation
management programmes.
Clause 6 :  This clause replaces section 5 of the principal enactment and the section as
replaced provides for a wider delegation of powers by the Director-General.
