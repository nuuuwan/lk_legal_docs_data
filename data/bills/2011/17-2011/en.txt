2011 BUDGET  ; AND  TO  PROVIDE  FOR  MATTERS  CONNECTED  THEREWITH
OR  INCIDENTAL  THERETO .
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Pensions (Consequential
Provisions) Act, No.     of 2011.
2. For the avoidance of doubts it is hereby declared
that the provisions of the Acts specified in the First Schedule
to this Act shall be deemed to have no application in respect
of the enactments specified in the Second Schedule to this
Act, and specifically in relation to the provisions dealing
with administration of the Acts and its application,
membership, contributions and mode of collection of
contributions.
3. (1) Parliament may by Resolution amend the First
Schedule and the Second Schedule to this Act, by adding
thereto or omitting therefrom any Act from any of the
aforesaid Schedules in order to give effect to the changes
which may be brought about in the policy of the Government
in relation to the payment of pensions.
(2) Upon the Resolution seeking to amend the First
Schedule and the Second Schedule being approved by
Parliament in accordance with the Standing Orders of
Parliament, the Secretary to the Ministry of the Minister
shall publish a Notification in the Gazette setting out the
content of the Resolution in such manner as indicates the
amendment made to the Schedule.
