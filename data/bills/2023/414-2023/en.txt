STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 :  This clause amends section 10 of the principal enactment and the legal effect of the 
section as amended is to employ before 6.00 a.m or after 6.00 p.m any female employee who has 
attained the age of eighteen in any institution which is engaged in business, based on information 
technology and knowledge process outsourcing or business process outsourcing or engaged in 
administration of accounts or related work in a Back Office for an institute situated abroad.
Clause 3 : This clause amends section 66 of the principal enactment to enable the Minister to 
make regulations specifying the terms and conditions relating to welfare of such females including 
their security, transport facilities and facilities to rest to be followed by an employer who employs 
such female after 10.00 p.m. and before 6.00 a.m. 
Clause 4 : This clause amends section 68 of the principal enactment to expand the definition of 
the expression “office” and is consequential  to the  amendment made to  Clause 2.
