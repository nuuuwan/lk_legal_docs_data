STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause  2 : This clause amends section 205 of the Civil Procedure Code (Chapter 101)
and the legal effect of this section as amended is to provide for-
(a) the issuance of one certified copy of the judgement or final decree of an action
to each party free of charge, where the respective parties to such action apply
for the same; and
(b) the recongnition of a document issued or transmitted by a court electronically,
as signed and sealed documents and the electronic signature of the Registrar,
court and other authorized officers.
