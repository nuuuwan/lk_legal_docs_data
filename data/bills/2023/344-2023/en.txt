STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
This Bill amends section 2 A of the Principal Enactment so as to ensure that any Order
made by the Minister thereunder will come into force only upon the approval thereof by
Parliament.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE VALUE ADDED TAX  ACT, NO. 14 OF 2002
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Value Added Tax
(Amendment) Act, No.     of 2023.
2. Section 2 A of the Value Added Tax Act, No.14 of
2002 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is
hereby amended by the deletion of subsections (2), (3), (4)
and (5) thereof, and the substitution therefor of the following
new subsections which shall take effect as subsections (2)
and (3) thereof:-
“(2) Every such Order shall be published in the Gazette,
and shall as soon as convenient thereafter be placed
before Parliament for its approval.
(3) Such Order shall only come into force upon its
approval by Parliament.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
