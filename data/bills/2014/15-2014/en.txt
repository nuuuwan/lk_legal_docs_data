STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 2 of the Local Authorities Filling of Vacancies
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013, by the repeal of the proviso to that section and it
is consequential to the amendments made to subsection (4) of section 66
B of the Local
Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) and the legal effect due to such repeal will be
to revert back to the procedure followed by the Municipal Council, Urban Council or
Pradeshiya Sabha, prior to such amendment when re-submitting a budget or supplementary
budget to the Municipal Council, Urban Council or Pradeshiys Sabha, as the case may be.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Local Authorities Filling
of Vacancies (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No.   of

amended by the repeal of the proviso to that section.
