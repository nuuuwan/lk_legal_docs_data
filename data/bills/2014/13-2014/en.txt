STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause repeals the proviso to section 169 of Pradeshiya Sabha Act,
No.15 of 1987 and the legal effect due to such repeal will be to revert back to the procedure
followed by the Pradeshiya Sabha when re-submitting a budget or supplementary budget to
the  Pradeshiya Sabha for further consideration.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRADESHIYA SABHA ACT, NO. 15 OF 1987
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Pradeshiya Sabha
(Amendment) Act, No.   of 2014 and shall come into operation
on such date as the Minister may appoint by Order Published
in the Gazette.
2. Section 169 of the Pradeshiya Sabha Act, No. 15 of
1987 as amended by Local Authorities (Special Provisions)
Act, No. 21 of 2012 is hereby further amended by the repeal
of the Proviso to that section.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
