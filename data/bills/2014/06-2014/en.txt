STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This Clause amends section 3 of the Value Added Tax Act, No.14 of 2002
(hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) and the legal effect of that section as
amended is to reduce the threshold of the liability of the wholesale and retail trade from
Rs. 500 Million to Rs. 250 Million and related matters.
Clause 3:  This Clause amends section 8 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of that section as amended is to limit the exemption granted to the wholesale or retail
business subject to certain conditions.
Clause 4:  This Clause amends section 10 of the principal enactment and is consequential
to the amendment made to section 3 of the principal enactment.
Clause 5:  This section amends section 20 of the principal enactment and the legal
effect of that section as amended is to provide for the avoidance of the issue of tax invoices
on deemed liability referred to in section 8 of the principal enactment.
Clause 6:  This Clause amends section 22 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of that section as amended is to provide for matters in the case where long term and general
insurance businesses are segregated by the persons carrying on both long term insurance
business and general insurance business,and to allow any bank or finance company to
claim the unabsorbed input credit where any acquisition or merger of such bank or
finance company takes place.
Clause 7:  This Clause amends section 25C of the principal enactment and  the legal
effect of that section as amended is to adopt only the profit calculation method referred to
in that section in calculating the VAT of Financial services.
Clause 8: This Clause amends section 25D of the principal enactment and is consequential
to the amendment made to section 25C of the principal amendment.
Clause 9:  This Clause amends section 40 of the principal enactment and the legal effect
of that section as amended is to expand the present preferential claims of default tax from
one year to five years.
Clause 10:  This Clause amends section 71 of the principal enactment and the legal
effect of that section as amended is to reduce the contribution to the VAT refund fund by
the Director- General of Customs on importation of goods from 10% to 6%.
Clause 11:  This Clause amends section 83 of the principal enactment and the legal
effect of that section as amended is to expand the definition of “international transportation”.
Clause 12:  This Clause amends PART II of the First Schedule to the principal enactment
in order to add new exemptions and to restrict exemptions granted to certain items.
Clause 13:   This Clause specifies the change of certain designations in keeping with the
changes made to such designations in the Service Minute of the Inland Revenue Service.
