STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
 Clause 2: This clause amends section 3 of the National Minimum Wage of Workers Act, No. 3 
of 2016 and the legal effect of that section as amended to increase the national minimum monthly 
wage by Rs. 9,500 and national minimum daily wage by Rs. 380 with effect from April 1, 2025 to 
December 31, 2025 and further to increase the national minimum monthly wage by Rs. 3000 and 
national minimum daily wage by Rs. 120 with effect from January 1, 2026.
 Clause 3: This clause is to consolidate the budgetary relief allowances as part of the wage of 
the workers.
