AN  ACT  TO  REPEAL THE ONLINE SAFETY 
ACT, NO. 9 OF 2024
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Online Safety (Repeal) 
Act, No.    of 2025.
2. The Online Safety Act, No. 9 of 2024 is hereby 
repealed.
3. Any regulations made under the Online Safety Act, 
No. 9 of 2024, after its enactment, shall be repealed from the 
date of enactment of this Act.
4.  In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala 
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
