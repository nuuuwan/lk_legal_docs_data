Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers (Amendment)

STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
  Clause 2:   This clause amends section 3 of the Budgetary Relief Allowance of  Workers Act, 
No. 4 of 2016 and the legal effect of that section as amended is to cease the payment of Budgetary 
Relief Allowance effective from March 31, 2025.

Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers (Amendment)  
L.D.-O 19/2025
An Act to amend the Budgetary Relief Allowance of 
Workers Act, No. 4 of 2016
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -
 1.  (1) This Act may be cited as the Budgetary Relief 
Allowance of Workers (Amendment) Act, No.       of  2025.
 (2) The provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have 
come into operation on April 1, 2025.
 2.  Section 3 of the Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers 
Act, No. 4 of 2016 is hereby amended as follows: -
(1)  in subsection (1) thereof, by the substitution for 
the words and figures "With effect from May 1, 
2015,", of the words and figures "For the period 
commencing on May 1, 2015 and ending on 
March 31, 2025, ''; and
(2)  in subsection (2) thereof, by the substitution 
for the words "commencing from January 1, 
2016", of the words "for the period commencing 
on January 1, 2016 and ending on March 31, 
2025,".
 3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala 
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
