1Land Development (Amendment)
Short title
AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAND DEVELOPMENT ORDINANCE 
(CHAPTER 464)
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Land Development  
(Amendment) Act, No.       of 2025.
2. Section 48 A of the principal enactment is hereby 
amended by the repeal of the words “or upon his or her 
marriage” in subsection 48
A 2(c).
3. Section 48 B of the principal enactment is hereby 
amended by the repeal of subsection 48B 1(a).
4. Section 68 of the principal enactment is hereby amended 
in subsection (1) of that section by the substitution for the 
words “fails to succeed” of the words “fails without reasonable 
cause to succeed”.
5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala 
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
