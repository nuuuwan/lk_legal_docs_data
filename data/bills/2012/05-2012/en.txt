AN ACT TO AMEND THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DEMOCRATIC
SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA.
WHEREAS the Constitution of the Democrtic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka has guaranteed and assured to protect
and foster the Buddha Sasana and the rights of all other
religions:
WHEREAS it has become a timely need to enact provisions
into the Constitution for the purpose of maintenance and
preservation of religious dignity and holiness with a view to
achieving of unity and concord of the Nation:
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Nineteenth Amendment
to the Constitution.
2.  The Article 91 of the Constitution be amended by
inserting the following:—
“91(1) (d) (xiii) a priest of any religion”.
3. This amendment shall come into operation soon after
the expiry or the dissolution of the seventh Parliament.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
