STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause amends section 3 of the Land (Restrictions on Alienation) Act, No. 38 of
2014 and the legal effect of the section as amended is to extend the application of the exemption
granted for land transactions under paragraph (i) of that section, to relevant companies which have
acquired lands prior to April 1, 2018.

L.D.—O. 34/2019
AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAND (RESTRICTIONS ON ALIENATION)
ACT, NO. 38 OF 2014
Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Land (Restrictions on
Alienation) (Amendment) Act, No.    of 2019.
2. Section 3 of the Land (Restrictions on Alienation)
Act, No. 38 of 2014 is hereby amended in subsection (1) by
the repeal of paragraph ( i) and the substitution therefor of
the following paragraph:—
“(i) any land, the title of which is transferred, to a
company referred to in paragraph (b) of subsection
(1) of section 2, listed in the Colombo Stock
Exchange.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall
prevail.
