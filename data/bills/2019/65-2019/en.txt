STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2 : This clause amends section 5 of the Prescription Ordinance (Chapter 68), and
the legal effect of the section as amended is to make provisions so that an action for the
recovery of any sum due upon any mortgage of any property or any bond for the payment
of money shall be prescribed after ten years from the date of giving notice to the debtor.
