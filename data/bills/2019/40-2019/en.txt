Short title.
AN ACT TO AMEND THE HOLIDAYS ACT, NO. 29 OF 1971
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Holidays (Amendment)
Act, No.    of 2019.
2. The following new section is hereby inserted
immediately after section 14 of the Holidays Act, No. 29 of
1971 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”)
and shall have effect as section 14
A of that enactment:—
“14A. No person shall on any Full Moon
Poya Day or any Sunday till 3.00 p.m. conduct
or caused to be conducted or facilitate for any
tuition class for students attending schools at
any premises or any location.”.
3. Section 17 of this Act is hereby amended by deleting
the words “One thousand rupees” and by substitution
therefor of the following words “Ten thousand rupees”.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
