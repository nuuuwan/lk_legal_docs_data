STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2:  This clause amends paragraph ( b) of subsection (1) of section 2 of the
Appropriation Act, No. 6 of 2019, and the legal effect of section as amended is to increase
the borrowing limit of rupees two thousand seventy nine billion to rupees two thousand
two hundred and seventy nine billion.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE APPROPRIATION ACT, NO. 6 OF 2019
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1.   This Act may be cited as the Appropriation
(Amendment) Act, No.    of 2019.
2. Section 2 of the Appropriation Act, No. 6 of 2019 is
hereby amended in paragraph ( b) of subsection (1) thereof,
by the substitution for the words “rupees two thousand
seventy nine billion” of the words “rupees two thousand
two hundred and seventy nine billion”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
