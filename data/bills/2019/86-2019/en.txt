STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT
Clause 2: This clause inserts the new section 68 A in the Mortgage Act (Chapter 89), and the
legal effect of that section is to limit the applicability of Part III of the Mortgage Act in relation to
movable property upon coming into operation of the Secured Transactions Act, No.   of 2019.

L.D.—O. 65/2017
AN ACT TO AMEND THE MORTGAGE ACT (CHAPTER 89)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Mortgage (Amendment)
Act, No.       of 2019 and shall come into operation on such
date as the Minister may appoint by Order published in
the Gazette.
2. The following new section is hereby inserted
immediately after section 68 of the Mortgage Act
(Chapter 89) and shall have effect as section 68
A of that
enactment:—
68A. The provisions of Part III of this Act
shall only apply to any movable property in so
far as they are not inconsistent with the
provisions of the Secured Transactions Act,
No.    of 2019, from and after the date of coming
into operation of the said Secured Transactions
Act.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
