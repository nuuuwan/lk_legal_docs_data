A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  WIDOWS ’ AND  ORPHANS ’ PENSION  SCHEME
(ARMED  FORCES ) ACT , NO . 18 OF  1970
BE enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Widows’ and Orphans’
Pension Scheme (Armed Forces) (Amendment) Act,  No. 29
of 2009.
2. The Widows’ and Orphans’ Pension Scheme (Armed
Forces) Act, No. 18 of 1970 (hereinafter referred to as the
“principal enactment”) is hereby amended by the insertion
immediately after section 3
A  thereof, of the following new
section:—
3AA . For the purposes of sections 3, 3A  and
3B  of this Act, “a member of the armed forces”
means any person—
(a) who, being a male, is an officer or soldier
of the Regular Force of the Sri Lanka
Army; or
(b) who, being a male is an officer or seaman
of the Regular Naval Force of the Royal
Ceylon Navy or Sri Lanka Navy as the
case may be; or
(c) who, being a male, is an officer or airman
of the Regular Air Force of the Royal
Ceylon Air Force or Sri Lanka Air Force
as the case may be.”.
3. The following new sections are hereby inserted
immediately after section 3
