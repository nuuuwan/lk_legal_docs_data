An Act tO AmenD the SAmurDhi Act, nO. 1 Of 2013
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -
1. This Act may be cited as the Samurdhi (Amendment) 
Act, No. 15 of 2025. 
2. The following new section is hereby inserted 
immediately after section 28 of the Samurdhi Act, No. 1 of 
2013 (in this Act referred to as the “principal enactment”) 
and shall have effect as section 28A of that enactment:–
 
 28A. The provisions of Article 154 of the 
Constitution relating to audit of accounts of 
public corporations and the provisions of 
the National Audit Act, No. 19 of 2018 shall 
apply to the audit of the accounts of samurdhi 
community based banks.”.
3. The following new section is hereby inserted 
immediately after section 35 of the principal enactment and 
shall have effect as section 35A of that enactment: –
 35A. The provisions of Article 154 of the 
Constitution relating to audit of accounts of 
public corporations and the provisions of 
the National Audit Act, No. 19 of 2018 shall 
apply to the audit of the accounts of samurdhi 
community based banking societies.”.
4. In the event of any inconsistency between Sinhala and 
Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
