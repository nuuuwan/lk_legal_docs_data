A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  EXCISE  (SPECIAL  PROVISIONS )
A CT , NO . 13 OF  1989
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :–
1. This Act may be cited as the Excise (Special
Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 46  of 2007.
2. Section 5 of the Excise (Special Provisions)  Act,
No. 13 of 1989 is hereby amended, in sub-paragraph (ii)
of paragraph (b) of subsection (2) of that section by the
substitution for the words “at the rate of ten per centum”
of the words “at the rate of fifteen per centum”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall
prevail.
