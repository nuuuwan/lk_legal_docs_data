A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  JUDICATURE  ACT , NO . 2 OF  1978
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment)
Act, No. 31 of 2007 and shall come into operation on such
date as the Minister may appoint by Order published in the
Gazette.
2. Section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 is
hereby amended by the substitution for the words “not more
than forty Judges.” of the words “not more than sixty Judges.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
