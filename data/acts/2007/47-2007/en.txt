A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE   REGIONAL   INFRASTRUCTURE   DEVELOPMENT
LEVY   ACT , NO . 51 OF  2006
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Regional Infrastructure
Development Levy (Amendment) Act,  No. 47  of 2007.
2.  Section 2 of the Regional Infrastructure Development
Levy Act, No. 51of 2006 is hereby amended, by the
substitution    for  the  words “ at   the  rate  of  two  and  half
per centum” of the words “at the rate of two and half per
centum in respect of a motor vehicle of a cylinder capacity
not exceeding 1600, five per centum in respect of a motor
vehicle of a cylinder capacity between 1600 and 2000 and
seven and half per centum in respect of a motor vehicle of a
cylinder capacity exceeding 2000”.
3. In the event of  any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
