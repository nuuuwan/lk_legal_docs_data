AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRESCRIPTION ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 68)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Prescription
(Amendment) Act, No. 26 of 2014.
2. Section 15 of the Prescription Ordinance, is hereby
repealed and the following section is substituted therefor:—
15. Nothing herein contained shall  in any
way—
(a) affect the alienable or inalienable
rights of the State; or
(b) apply to any proceedings in any
action for divorce, or to any case in
which special provision has been or
may hereafter be made for regulating
and determining the period within
which actions may be commenced
against any public officer or other
person.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
