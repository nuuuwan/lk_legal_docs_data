1 Corporal Punishment (Repeal)
Act, No. 23 of 2005
[Certified on 02nd August, 2005]
L.D.—O. 55/2004.
A N   ACT  TO  REPEAL  THE  CORPORAL  PUNISHMENT  ORDINANCE
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Corporal Punishment
(Repeal) Act, No.  23  of 2005.
2. The Corporal Punishment Ordinance (Chapter 21) is
hereby repealed.
3.    Wherever in any written law, there is included as part
of a sentence to be imposed by Court in respect of  any offence,
a sentence of whipping, such law shall from and after the date
of the coming into operation of this  Act, be deemed to be
amended by the  omission therefrom of such part of such
sentence as relates to whipping.
4.    In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this  Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
