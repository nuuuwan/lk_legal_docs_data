L.D.—O. 46/2004.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  PRISONS  ORDINANCE
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Prisons  (Amendment)
Act, No. 22  of 2005.
2. Section 79 of the Prisons Ordinance (hereinafter
referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended
as follows :—
(i) by the repeal of paragraph (g) of that section;
(ii) by the re-lettering of paragraph (h) of that section as
paragraph (g).
3.   Section 81 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended as follows :—
(1) in subsection (4) of that section —
(a) by the repeal of paragraph (d) thereof ; and
(b) by   the  relettering   of   paragraph   (e)  thereof
as  paragraph (d);
(2) by the repeal of subsection (5) and (6) of that
section ; and
(3) by the renumbering of subsections (7) and (8) as
subsections (5) and (6) of that section.
4.  Section 86 of  the principal enactment is hereby
repealed.
5.   In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
