A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  SOCIETIES ORDINANCE
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Societies Ordinance
(Amendment) Act, No. 11  of 2005
2. Section 9 of the Societies Ordinance (Chapter 123)
(hereinafter referred to as “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended as follows :—
(1) in subsection (3) of that section, by the substitution
for all the words from “recoverable as such” to the
end of that subsection, of the words “recoverable in
an action filed in the District Court”;
(2) in subsection (4) of that section, by the substitution
for the words “two thousand five hundred” of the
words “five hundred thousand”;
(3) in subsection (5) of that section, by the substitution
for the words “two thousand five hundred” of the
words “five hundred thousand”; and
(4) in subsection (8) of that section, by the substitution
for the words “A person under the age of twenty-one
but above the age of sixteen” of the words” A person
under the age of eighteen years, but above the age
of sixteen.”.
3. Section 17 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended by the substitution for the words “receive more
than two thousand rupees by way of gross sum, together with
any bonuses or additions declared up on assurances not
