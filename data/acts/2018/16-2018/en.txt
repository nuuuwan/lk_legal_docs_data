National Defence Fund (Amendment)

[Certified on 22nd of June, 2018]
L.D.—O. 34/2017
AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL DEFENCE FUND
ACT, NO. 9 OF 1985
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka  as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the National Defence Fund
(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2018.
2. Section 3 of National Defence Fund Act, No. 9 of 1985
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended as follows:—
(1) by the repeal of paragraphs ( a) and (b) of subsection
(2) of that section and substitution therefor of the
following paragraph:—
“(a) the following ex-officio members:—
(i) the Secretary to the President of the
Republic;
(ii) the Secretary to the Ministry of the
Minister assigned the subject of
Defence; and” ;
(2) by relettering the paragraph ( c) of subsection (2) of
that section as paragraph (b); and
(3) by the substitution in subsections (3) and (4), for
the words and figures “paragraph (c) of subsection
(2)”, of the words and figures “paragraph ( b) of
subsection (2)”.
Short title.
Amendment of
section 3 of Act,
No. 9 of 1985.
