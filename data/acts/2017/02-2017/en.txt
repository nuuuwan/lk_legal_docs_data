AN ACT TO AMEND THE DIVINEGUMA
ACT, NO. 1 OF 2013
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Divineguma
(Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2017.
2. The Long title to the Divineguma Act,  No. 1 of 2013
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended as follows:—
(1) by the substitution for the expression “DEPARTMENT
OF DIVINEGUMA DEVELOPMENT” of the
expression “DEPARTMENT OF SAMURDHI
DEVELOPMENT”; and
(2) by the substitution for the word “DIVINEGUMA”
of the word “SAMURDHI”.
3. The second paragraph of the Preamble of the principal
enactment is hereby amended by the substitution for the
words “AND WHEREAS, Divineguma intends” of the words
“AND WHEREAS, Samurdhi intends”.
4. (1) In the principal enactment and any other written
law there shall be substituted—.
(a) for the expression “Department of Divineguma
Development” of the expression “Department of
Samurdhi Development”;
