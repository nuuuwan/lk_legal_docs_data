AN ACT TO AMEND THE IMMIGRANTS AND EMIGRANTS
ACT (CHAPTER 351)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Immigrants and Emigrants
(Amendment) Act, No. 7 of  2015.
2. The Immigrants and Emigrants Act (Chapter 351) is
hereby amended by the insertion, immediately after section
35 thereof, of the following new section:—
35
A. The Authority prescribed to issue or
renew passports may take finger printing for
the inclusion in the passport, from a person
applying for a passport or the renewal of a
passport.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
