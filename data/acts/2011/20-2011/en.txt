1Excise (Amendment) Act, No. 20 of 2011
[Certified on 31st March, 2011]
L.D.—O. 3/2011.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  EXCISE  ORDINANCE  (CHAPTER  52)
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Excise (Amendment)
Act, No. 20 of 2011.
(2) The provisions of this Act shall for all purposes be
deemed to have come into operation on November 19, 2010.
2. Section 15 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) is
hereby amended by the repeal of paragraphs (b) and (c) of
that section, and the substitution therefor of the
following :—
“(b) no tree producing toddy, other than the kithul tree
and the palmyrah tree, shall be tapped ; and
(c) no toddy shall be drawn from any tree, other than
the kithul tree and the palmayrah tree.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
