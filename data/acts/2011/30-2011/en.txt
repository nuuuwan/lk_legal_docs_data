A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  PRADESHIYA  SANWARDANA  BANK
A CT , NO . 41 OF  2008
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Pradeshiya Sanwardana
Bank (Amendment) Act, No. 30 of 2011.
2. Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sanwardana Bank Act,
No. 41 of 2008 is hereby repealed and the following Section
substituted therefor :—
4. The head office of the Bank may be
established in any location in Sri Lanka,
outside the Administrative District of
Colombo, as may be determined by the
Board.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
