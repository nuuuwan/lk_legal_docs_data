Short title.
[Certified on 30th October, 2010]
L.D.—O. 35/2008
A
N ACT TO AMEND THE PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ACT, NO. 42    OF 1987
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—
1. This Act may be cited as the Provincial Councils
(Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 2010.
2. Section 25 of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of
1987 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that section by
the substitution for the words “three months before the
commencement of such financial year”, of the words “one
month before the commencement of such financial year”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
