Merchant Shipping (Change of Designation)
Act, No. 3 of 2006
1
[Certified on 13th February , 2006]
L.D.—O. 35/2005.
A N  ACT  TO  CHANGE  THE  DESIGNA TION OF  THE  DIRECTOR  OF  MERCHANT
SHIPPING  AND  TO  MAKE  SUCH   AMENDMENTS   IN  WRITTEN   LAW   AS   ARE
 CONSEQUENTIAL .
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Merchant Shipping
(Change of Designation)  Act, No. 3  of 2006.
2. With effect from the date of commencement of this
Act, in every context in which “Director of Merchant
shipping” appears in—
(a) subsection (1) of section 3 and elsewhere in
the Merchant Shipping Act, No. 52 of 1971
and in any other written law; and
(b) any notice, communication, or other
document issued, made, required or
authorized by or under any written law,
there shall be substituted for the words “Director of Merchant
Shipping”,  the words “Director-General of Merchant
Shipping”.
3.   In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
Short title.
Amendment to
section 3 of the
Merchant
Shipping Act, No.
52 of 1971.
Sinhala text to
prevail in case of
inconsistency.
