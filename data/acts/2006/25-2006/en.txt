A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  SRI LANKA  INSTITUTE  OF  ADV ANCED
TECHNICAL  EDUCATION  ACT , NO . 29 OF  1995
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-
1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Institute of
Advanced Technical Education (Amendment)  Act, No. 25
of 2006.
2. The Long Title to the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced
Technical Education Act, No. 29 of 1995 (hereinafter referred
to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended, by the
substitution for the words “AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE
ESTABLISHMENT OF THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF
ADV ANCED TECHNICAL EDUCATION;” of the words
“AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE, ESTABLISHMENT
OF THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF ADV ANCED
TECHNOLOGICAL  EDUCATION;”.
3. (1) In the principal enactment and in any other written
law, there shall be substituted—
(a) for the words “Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced
Technical Education” of the words “Sri Lanka
Institute of Advanced Technological Education”;
(b) for the words “Advanced Technical Institute” or
“Advanced Technical Institutes” of the words
“Advanced Technological Institute” or “Advanced
Technological Institutes” as the case may be.
(2) Every reference to the “Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced
Technical Education” and “Advanced Technical Institute”or
“Advanced Technical Institutes” in any notice, notification
contract, communication or other document shall be read
and construed as a reference respectively to the “Sri Lanka
