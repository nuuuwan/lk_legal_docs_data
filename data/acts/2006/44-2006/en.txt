[Certified on 06th November, 2006]
L.D.—O. 50/2004.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  SRI LANKA  TEA  BOARD  LAW ,
N O . 14 OF  1975
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Tea Board
(Amendment)  Act, No. 44 of 2006
2. Section 6 of the Sri Lanka Tea Board Law, No. 14 of
1975,  is hereby  amended in paragraph (b) of subsection (1)
of section 6 by the repeal of sub-paragraph (iii) of that
paragraph and the substitution therefor of the following sub-
paragraphs :—
“(iii) a representative of the Ministry of the Minster in
charge of the subject of Plantation Industries,
nominated by that Minister ;”,
(iiia) one person nominated by the Minister in charge of
the subject of Plantation Industries.”.
3.   In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
Short title.
Amendment of
section 6 of Law,
No. 14 of 1975.
Sinhala text to
prevail in case of
inconsistency.
