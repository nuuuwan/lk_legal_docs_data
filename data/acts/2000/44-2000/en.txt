COMMERCIAL MEDIATION CENTRE OF SRI LANKA 
2. (I) There shall be established a Centre to be called
the Commercial Mediation Centre of Sri Lanka (hereinafter 
referred to as "the Centre"). 
(2) The Centre shall. by the name assigned to it by
subsection (I ), be a body corporate with perpetual succession 
and a common seal, and may sue and be sued in it's corporate 
name. 
(3) The head office of the Centre shall be in Colombo. The
Board of Management of the Centre may establish such branch 
offices of the Centre as the Board may consider necesssary in 
places in Sri Lanka, outside Colombo. 
3. The functions of the Centre shall be -
(a) to promote the wider acceptance of mediation and
conciliation for the resolution and setllement of
commercial disputes ;
(b) to encourage parties to resolve commercial disputes
by mediation and conciliation ;
