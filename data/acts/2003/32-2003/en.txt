A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  NATIONAL  HOUSING  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY
A CT , NO . 17 OF  1979
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the National Housing
Development Authority (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 2003.
2. Section 10 of the National Housing Development
Authority Act, No. 17 of 1979 is hereby repealed.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
