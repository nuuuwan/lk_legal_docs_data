Navy (Amendment) Act, No. 53 of 1999
[Certified on 14th December, 1999]
L.D.––O. 55/98.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  NAV Y ACT
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :––
1. This Act may be cited as the Navy (Amendment) Act,
No. 53 of 1999.
2. Section 28 of the Navy Act, as amended by Act, No. 11
of 1993 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that section, by
the substitution, for the words “the rank of Lieutenant” of the
words “the rank of Lieutenant Commander”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act,the Sinhala text shall prevail.
Short title.
Amendment of
section 28 of
Chapter 358.
Sinhala text to
prevail in case of
inconsistency.
