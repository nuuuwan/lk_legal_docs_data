L.D. - O. 75/96.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE   JUDICA TURE  ACT , NO .2 OF  1978
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-
1. This Act may be cited as the Judicature
(Amendment) Act, No.27 of  1999.
2. Section 48 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, is
hereby amended, by the repeal of the proviso to that section
and the substitution therefor, of the following proviso :-
“Provided that where any criminal prosecution,
 proceeding or matter (except on an inquiry preliminary to
committal for trial) is continued before the successor of
any such judge, the accused may demand that the
witnesses be resummoned and reheard.”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala Text shall
prevail
