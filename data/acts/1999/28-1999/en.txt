Offences Against Public Property (Amendment)
Act, No. 28 of 1999
[Certified on 17th September, 1999]
L.D.––O. 36/97.
A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  OFFENCES  AGAINST  PUBLIC
PROPERTY  ACT , NO . 12 OF  1982
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :––
1. This Act may be cited as the Offences Against Public
Property (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1999.
2. Section 8 of the Offences Against Public Property Act,
No. 12 of 1982 is hereby amended in the proviso to subsection
(1) if that section, by the substitution, for the words “exceeds
five thousand rupees” of the words “exceeds twenty five
thousand rupees”.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act,the Sinhala text shall prevail.
Short title.
Amendment of
section 8 of Act
No. 12 of 1982.
Sinhala text to
prevail in case of
inconsistency.
