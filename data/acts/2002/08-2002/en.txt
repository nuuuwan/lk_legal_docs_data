1Save  the Nation Contribution  (Abolition)
Act, No. 8 of 2002
L.D.—O. 56/2001.
A N  ACT  TO  ABOLISH  THE  CHARGING  OF  SAV E THE  N ATION
C ONTRIBUTION , UNDER  THE  SAV E THE  NA TION CONTRIBUTION
A CT , NO . 5 OF  1996, FROM  PERSONS  TO  WHOM  THAT  ACT  APPLIES ,
WITH  EFFECT  FROM  OCTOBER  1, 2001
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the Save the Nation
Contribution (Abolition) Act, No. 8 of 2002.
2. Notwithstanding anything in the Save the Nation
Contribution Act, No. 5 of 1996—
(a) the Save the Nation Contribution shall not be charged
and recovered from any person to whom that Act
applies, for any quarter of any year ; and
(b) the employer of any person to whom that Act applies
shall not be required to deduct any amount that such
employer is required to deduct from the emoluments
payable to such person, under section 4 of that Act,
for any month,
        commencing on or after October 1, 2001.
3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
