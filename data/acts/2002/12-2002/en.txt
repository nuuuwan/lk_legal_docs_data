A N  ACT  TO  AMEND  THE  PENAL  CODE
BE it hereby enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1. This Act may be cited as the  Penal Code (Amendment)
Act, No. 12 of 2002.
PART I
2. The Penal Code (hereinafter referred to as  the
“principal enactment”) is hereby amended by the repeal of
section 118.
3. Chapter XIX of the principal enactment is hereby
repealed.
PART II
4. The Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15 of 1979
is amended by the repeal of paragraph (f) of subsection (1) of
section 135.
5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and the Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
Sinhala text to
prevail in case of
inconsistency.
